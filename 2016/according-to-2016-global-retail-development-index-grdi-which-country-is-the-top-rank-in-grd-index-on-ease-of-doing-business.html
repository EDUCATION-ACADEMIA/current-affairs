According to 2016 Global Retail Development Index (GRDI) which country is the top rank in GRD index on ease of doing business

(A) India

(B) ** China

(C) Russia

(D) US

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is India’s rank in the Global Retail Development Index(GRDI)? Notes: India ranked No. 2 in the Global Retail Development Index (GRDI) in 2019.

Concept note-2: -The GRDI ranks 35 developing countries on a scale of zero to 100-the higher the ranking, the more urgency to enter a country. Since the GRDI’s inception in 2002, the countries ranked on the Index have tracked closely as the top destinations for retailers in subsequent years.

Concept note-3: -According to the global consulting firm Deloitte’s report titled “Global Powers of Retailing 2022: Resilience Despite Challenges”, The Indian brand, Reliance Retail, ranked 56th on the Top 250 list based on the retail revenue growth for FY2020.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is First. The Global Retail Development Index (GRDI) has ranked 1st to India in 2017.
