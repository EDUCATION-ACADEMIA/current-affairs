According to NITI Aayog’s index, which of the following state has the most farmer friendly state in the country

(A) Gujarat

(B) Rajasthan

(C) ** Maharashtra

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Maharashtra. In NITI Aayog Maharashtra achieved the first rank. NITI Aayog launched Agricultural Marketing and Farmer Friendly Reforms Index (AMFFRI).

Concept note-2: -The top 10 agriculture-producing States in terms of cash receipts in calendar year 2021 were (in descending order): California, Iowa, Nebraska, Texas, Minnesota, Illinois, Kansas, Indiana, North Carolina, and Wisconsin.

Concept note-3: -Maharashtra ranks first. NITI Aayog launches the first ever Agricultural Marketing & Farm Friendly Reforms Index. Maharashtra ranks first. The low levels of productivity, growth and incomes ailing the farm sector in India and causing immense agrarian distress is well known.
