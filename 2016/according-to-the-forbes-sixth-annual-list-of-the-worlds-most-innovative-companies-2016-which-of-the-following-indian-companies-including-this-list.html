According to the Forbes sixth annual list of The World’s Most Innovative Companies 2016, which of the following indian companies including this list

(A) Hindustan Unilever

(B) Tata Consultancy Services

(C) Asian Paints

(D) ** All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Five major Indian companies including Asian Paints, Hindustan Unilever, Tata Consultancy Services, Sun Pharma and Larsen & Toubro have been ranked among Forbes’s list of ‘The World’s Most Innovative Companies’.

Concept note-2: -TATA Consultancy Services The Tata Group established TCS in 1968 as Tata Computer Systems. It is the highest-ranking Indian firm, rating 64th in Forbes World’s Most Innovative Companies.

Concept note-3: -New York, NY (September 5, 2012) – Salesforce.com tops Forbes’ list of The World’s Most Innovative Companies for the second year in a row (“The Nifty 50, ” p. 90), followed by Alexion Pharmaceuticals (No. 2) and Amazon.com (No. 3).
