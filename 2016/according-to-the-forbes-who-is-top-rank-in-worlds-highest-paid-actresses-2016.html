(C) Deepika Padukone

Concept note-3: -Forbes is doing its part today, unveiling its annual list of the highest-paid actresses in the world. For the second straight year, Jennifer Lawrence is the reigning queen of high earners, making an estimated $46 million in June 2015 to June 2016.
