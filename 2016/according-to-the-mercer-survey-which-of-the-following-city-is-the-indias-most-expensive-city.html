According to the Mercer survey, Which of the following city is the India’s most expensive city

(A) ** Mumbai

(B) Delhi

(C) Hyderabad

(D) Bangalore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai is the most expensive city in India for expatriates living and working overseas: Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living Survey. Mumbai, June 29, 2022: According to Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living survey, Mumbai (127) is the most costly city in India in terms of both living expenses and accommodation costs.

Concept note-2: -1. Mumbai. Mumbai is the richest Indian city and it also ranks among the most expensive cities in the world. The cost of living is highest in Mumbai in comparison to all the other cities of India.

Concept note-3: -Douglas Broom. New York and Singapore have been named the most expensive cities in the world in 2022 by the Economist Intelligence Unit. Prices in global cities are over 8% higher than in 2021, the sharpest rise for 20 years.

Concept note-4: -Mumbai is the most expensive city in India in terms of both living expenses and lodging costs, according to Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living assessment. Hyderabad is the most expensive Indian city in the ranking, followed by New Delhi, Chennai, Bengaluru, and Hyderabad.
