According to the most powerful women based outside the US by Fortune, what is the rank of SBI Chairperson Arundhati Bhattacharya

(A) 1st

(B) ** 2nd

(C) 3rd

(D) 4th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -She is also currently chairperson of SWIFT India, part of a global payments network. Bhattacharya tendered her resignation as an independent director of CRISIL (a global analytical company providing ratings, research, and risk and policy advisory services) effective 15 April.

Concept note-2: -Arundhati Bhattacharya is the first woman to be the Chairperson of the State Bank of India (7 October 2013 – 6 October 2017). She was succeeded by Rajnish Kumar. In 2016, she was listed as the 25th most powerful woman in the world by Forbes.

Concept note-3: -Arundhati was awarded several titles like the 24th most powerful woman in Asia-Pacific by Fortune magazine (2014), 25th(2016), and 30th (2015)most powerful woman by Forbes. In 2018, she was titled “the business leader of the year” at the Asian awards, among other accolades.

Concept note-4: -Arundhati Bhattacharya, Chairperson of State Bank of India (SBI), the country’s largest public-sector bank, gets a pittance for salary compared to her peers in private banks. Bhattacharya’s annual salary is Rs 28.95 lakh while ICICI Bank CEO Chanda Kochchar is paid Rs 6.09 crore annual salary.
