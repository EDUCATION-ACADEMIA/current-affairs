(A) 1st

(B) 2nd

(C) ** 3rd

(D) 4th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Team India Ranking 2022 India has moved up to second position in the ICC Test Team Rankings. India has achieved this rank by playing around 29 matches. In this way, according to the ICC Team India Ranking 2022, India has reached the second position with 114 ratings.

Concept note-2: -As per the latest Odi rankings released by ICC on 2 Feb 2023, India with 114 rating is placed at the top of the points table. Australia with 112 rating is on the second spot. New Zealand with 111 rating occupies the third place.

Concept note-3: -ICC Test Rankings-Updated on 18 Jan 2023 As per the latest Test rankings released by ICC on 18 Jan 2023, Australia with 126 rating is placed at the top of the points table. India with 115 rating is on the second spot. England with 107 rating occupies the third place.
