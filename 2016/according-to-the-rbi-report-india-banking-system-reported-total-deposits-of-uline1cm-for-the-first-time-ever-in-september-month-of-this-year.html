(A) ** Rs 100 lakh crore

(B) Rs 1000 lakh crore

(C) Rs 10000 crore

(D) Rs 100000 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the basis of an assessment of the current and evolving macroeconomic situation, the Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) at its meeting today (September 30, 2022) decided to: Increase the policy repo rate under the liquidity adjustment facility (LAF) by 50 basis points to 5.90 per cent with immediate effect.

Concept note-2: -The RBI has followed a July-June financial year since 1940 when it moved away from a January-December year. RBI will start following April-March financial year after a nine-month year ending March 31, 2021.
