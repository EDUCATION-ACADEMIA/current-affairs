According to the State of the World’s Children 2016 Report, How many children may die before reaching their fifth birthdays

(A) 50 million

(B) 60 million

(C) ** 70 million

(D) 80 million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under-five mortality In 2021, 5.0 million children under 5 years of age died. Globally, infectious diseases, including pneumonia, diarrhoea and malaria, remain a leading cause of under-five deaths, along with preterm birth and intrapartum-related complications.

Concept note-2: -Brief Definition: The under-five mortality rate refers to the probability of dying. before age 5 years per 1, 000 newborns.

Concept note-3: -Under-five mortality rate as defined here is strictly speaking not a rate (i.e. the number of deaths divided by the number of population at risk during a certain period of time) but a probability of death derived from a life table and expressed as rate per 1000 live births.

Concept note-4: -Nutrition-related factors contribute to about 45% of deaths in children under-5 years of age.
