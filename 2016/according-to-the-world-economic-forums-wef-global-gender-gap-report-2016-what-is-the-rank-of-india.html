According to the World Economic Forum’s (WEF) Global Gender Gap Report 2016, what is the rank of India

(A) 34

(B) 110

(C) 98

(D) ** 87

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India ranked 135 th out of 146 countries in gender parity, according to the latest Global Gender Gap Report by the World Economic Forum. This is of concern considering India ranked somewhere in the middle, bagging the 87 th position worldwide just six years ago, in 2016.

Concept note-2: -India ranks 135 out of 146 in Global Gender Gap Index.

Concept note-3: -Global Gender Gap Report 2022: India Ranks 135 Out Of 146, Slips Drastically In Health And Survival. The Global Gender Gap Report 2022 places India at 135 out of 146 countries. In comparison to the year before, where we ranked 140 out of 156, India has slightly improved its position.

Concept note-4: -Proving that is the World Economic Forum’s Gender Gap Report 2022, which ranks India 135 out of 146 countries.

Concept note-5: -Iceland has retained its position as the world’s most gender-equal country, among 146 nations on the index. Finland, Norway, New Zealand and Sweden are the top five countries on the list respectively.
