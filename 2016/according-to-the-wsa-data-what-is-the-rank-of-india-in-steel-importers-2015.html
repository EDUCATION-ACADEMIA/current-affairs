According to the WSA data, What is the rank of India in steel importers 2015

(A) 3rd

(B) 8th

(C) 12th

(D) ** 10th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: India, the world’s third largest steel producer, was among the top 10 importers of the alloy last year, according to the latest data by the global industry body World Steel Association (WSA).

Concept note-2: -In 2021, the United States, the leading steel importer, imported 29.7 million metric tons. In the same year, the previous year’s leader China ranked second with 27.8 million metric tons, and the European Union leader, Germany, ended up in the third place with 23.3 million metric tons of steel imports.

Concept note-3: -Data show that between April and October, imports served 10% of India’s total steel demand.

Concept note-4: -Imports exceeded export of finished steel by 262, 000 tonnes and rose 92 per cent on y-o-y basis.

Concept note-5: -In April-July 2022, the largest share in the total import of steel products was hot-rolled non-alloyed steel – 35%, or 0.307 million tons.
