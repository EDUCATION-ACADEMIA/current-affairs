(A) London

(B) Paris

(C) Moscow

(D) ** New York

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living survey, Mumbai is the most expensive city to live in India.

Concept note-2: -New York and Singapore are the most expensive cities to live in: Survey-Hindustan Times.

Concept note-3: -You should plan to spend around ₨4, 614 ($57) per day on your vacation in Mumbai, which is the average daily price based on the expenses of other visitors. Past travelers have spent, on average, ₨1, 719 ($21) on meals for one day and ₨519 ($6.37) on local transportation.
