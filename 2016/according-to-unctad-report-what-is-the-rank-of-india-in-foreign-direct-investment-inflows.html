According to UNCTAD report, what is the rank of India in Foreign Direct Investment inflows

(A) 5th

(B) 10th

(C) ** 10th

(D) 12th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It may be noted that as per the UNCTAD World Investment Report (WIR) 2022, in its analysis of the global trends in FDI inflows, India has improved one position to 7th rank among the top 20 host economies for 2021.

Concept note-2: -Key Findings of the WIR 2022. UNCTAD World Investment Report (WIR) 2022 has ranked India at 7th rank among the top 20 host economies for 2021, in terms of FDI.

Concept note-3: -According to the United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD), India jumped one position to 7th among the top recipients of foreign direct investment (FDI) 2021.

Concept note-4: -The United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD) expects India’s economy to grow 5.7% in 2022 and 4.7% in 2023. India’s gross domestic product (GDP) grew 8.7% in FY22.
