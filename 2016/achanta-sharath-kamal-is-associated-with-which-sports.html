Achanta Sharath Kamal is associated with which sports

(A) ** Table Tennis

(B) Snooker

(C) Cricket

(D) Hockey

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2003, Sharath became the National Champion for the first time at the National Table Tennis Championships. He again won the nationals in 2004. From 2006 to 2010, Kamal won the senior nationals five times consecutively. In 2007 nationals, Sharath made a clean sweep of 4 golds.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Achanta Sharath Kamal. Achanta Sharath Kamal was the first Indian table tennis player ever to become nine times senior national champion as of January 2021.

Concept note-3: -Achanta Sharath Kamal, a prominent Indian paddler, is the first Indian to be chosen to the International Table Tennis Federation’s Athletes’ Commission (ITTF). Eight athletes, including four men and four women, were chosen for the Athletes’ Commission from Asia, Africa, the Americas, Europe, and Oceania.

Concept note-4: -Today he has a career decorated with a number of international awards. In the Beijing Olympics, he was the only table tens player from India. Sharath Kamal Achanta has a world ranking of 32.
