Affordable Plotted Housing Policy 2016 is associated with which of the following Yojana

(A) ** Deen Dayal Jan Awas Yojana

(B) Samajwadi Awas Yojana

(C) Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana

(D) Indira Awaas Yojana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Deen Dayal Jan Awas Yojna as a policy introduced by the Government of Haryana is primarily focused upon 2 lakh affordable housing units by the end of 2022 by focusing upon developing residential townships spreading over a land area covering a minimum of 5 Acres of land and a maximum of 15 acres of land.

Concept note-2: -Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban)-PMAY (U) The Mission provides Central Assistance to the implementing agencies through States/Union Territories (UTs) and Central Nodal Agencies (CNAs) for providing houses to all eligible families/ beneficiaries against the validated demand for houses for about 1.12 cr.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) – Urban : Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana aims to provide housing for all by 2022. It offers an interest subsidy for home loans for first-time homebuyers in urban areas. It is applicable in all states and UTs.
