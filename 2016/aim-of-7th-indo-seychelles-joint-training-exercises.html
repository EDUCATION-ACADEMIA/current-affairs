Aim of 7th Indo Seychelles joint Training Exercises

(A) punctuality

(B) ** Interoperability

(C) Enhancing military cooperation

(D) Build and promote positive military relations

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Garuda is a joint exercise between India and France Air Force that will provide a platform for both countries to enhance operational capability and interoperability, while also sharing best practices.

Concept note-2: -Exercise KazInd 2022 is the 6th edition of the Indo-Kazakhstan Joint exercise and is aimed at building trust and understanding of each other in the field of national security.

Concept note-3: -Exercise “SURYA KIRAN” is conducted annually between India and Nepal with the aim to enhance interoperability in jungle warfare & counter terrorism operations in mountainous terrain and HADR under UN mandate.

Concept note-4: -Ex Dharma Guardian – 2022 is a joint military exercise between India and Japan, is being conducted at the Foreign Training Node in Belagavi.
