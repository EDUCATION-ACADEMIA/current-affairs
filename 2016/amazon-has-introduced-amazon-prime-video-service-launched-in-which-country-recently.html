Amazon has introduced Amazon Prime Video Service launched in which country recently

(A) Myanmar

(B) Vietnam

(C) ** India

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Video is available worldwide (except for Mainland China, Iran, North Korea, Russia and Syria). Initially it was available only to residents of the United States, United Kingdom, Japan, Germany and Austria.

Concept note-2: -Original Programming. The service debuted on September 7, 2006 as Amazon Unbox in the United States. On September 4, 2008, the service was renamed Amazon Video on Demand.

Concept note-3: -Amazon launched the prime video services in 2011 where global player like Netflix had already ventured. Still Amazon entered the Indian market with services like Prime Video.
