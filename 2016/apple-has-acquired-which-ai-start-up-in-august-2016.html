(A) Turin

(B) Pascal

(C) Byte

(D) ** Turi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple has recently acquired two AI startups, Xnor.ai, a company specialising in low-power, edge-based AI tools, especially for image recognition, and Voysis, a voice interaction AI platform.

Concept note-2: -Siri is Apple’s virtual assistant for iOS, macOS, tvOS and watchOS devices that uses voice recognition and is powered by artificial intelligence (AI).

Concept note-3: -Apple Inc. acquired a startup called AI Music that uses artificial intelligence to generate tailor-made music, according to a person with knowledge of the matter, adding technology that could be used across its slate of audio offerings.

Concept note-4: -The work is innovative. The experience is magic. The people working here in machine learning and AI are building amazing experiences into every Apple product, allowing millions to do what they never imagined.
