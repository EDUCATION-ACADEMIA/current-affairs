Apple Inc. has opened a new Development Office in which city

(A) ** Hyderabad

(B) Chennai

(C) Bengaluru

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple India will operate out of the executive centre Safina Towers office in Bengaluru and will pay a rent of ₹1.42 crore per month, the sales deed mentioned. “There will be an escalation of 5% in November 2023 and after that every 12 months, ” the registered document mentioned.

Concept note-2: -In 2021, Apple signed over a 4 lakh sqft office deal with Prestige Group in Bangalore to cater to the global and Indian markets and open a large retail centre in the city. Additionally, the biggest unit manufacturing Apple iPhone in India is coming up near Hosur in Bengaluru.

Concept note-3: -Apple Inc. One Apple Park Way; Cupertino, CA 95014, U.S.A.
