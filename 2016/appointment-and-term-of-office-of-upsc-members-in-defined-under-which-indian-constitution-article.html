Appointment and term of office of UPSC members in defined under which Indian Constitution Article

(A) Articles 315

(B) ** Articles 316

(C) Articles 320

(D) Articles 325

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Article-316. Appointment and term of office of members.

Concept note-2: -Articles 315 to 323 in Part XIV of the Constitution of India provides for the establishment of Public Service Commission for the Union and a Public Service Commission for each State. Each state has its own Public Service Commission with functions similar to the Union Public Service Commission.

Concept note-3: -Article-318. Power to make regulations as to conditions of service of members and staff of the Commission. Article-319. Prohibition as to the holding of offices by members of Commission on ceasing to be such members.

Concept note-4: -Article 312 of the Indian Constitution empowers the State Legislatures to request the Indian Parliament for the creation of an All India Service. 2. The Indian Forest Service was set up by the Indian Parliament as an All India Service after independence.
