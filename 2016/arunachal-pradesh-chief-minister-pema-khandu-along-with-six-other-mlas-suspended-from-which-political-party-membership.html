Arunachal Pradesh Chief Minister Pema Khandu along with six other MLAs suspended from which political party membership

(A) Bharatiya Janata Party

(B) Indian National Congress

(C) ** People’s Party of Arunachal

(D) Bahujan Samaj Party

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Peoples Party of Arunachal, a regional political party in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh. It was founded in September 1977 by Bakin Pertin, Oken Lego and L. Wanglat as president, Vice President and General Secretary of the party.

Concept note-2: -The key political players in Arunachal Pradesh state in north-east India are the ruling Bharatiya Janata Party, Indian National Congress, Janata Dal (United) and People’s Party of Arunachal.

Concept note-3: -A model PPA (Power Purchase Agreement) to be executed between RPG and DISCOMs has been prepared by MNRE and attached at Annexure-I.
