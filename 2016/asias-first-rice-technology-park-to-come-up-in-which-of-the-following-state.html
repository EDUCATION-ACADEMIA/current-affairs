Asia’s first Rice Technology Park to come up in which of the following state

(A) West Bengal

(B) ** Karnataka

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Karnataka state government recently decided to establish Asia’s first-ever Rice Technology Park at Gangavati.

Concept note-2: -Karnataka Government decides to set up Asia’s first Rice Technology Park in Gangavati. Karnataka Government has decided to go ahead with the establishment of Asia’s first Rice Technology Park at Gangavati in Koppal district.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Ranibennur. Maize Technology park will be established at Ranibennur in Haveri, Karnataka.
