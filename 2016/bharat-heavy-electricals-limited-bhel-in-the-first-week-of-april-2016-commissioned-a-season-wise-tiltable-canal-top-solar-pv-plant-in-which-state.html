Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) in the first week of April 2016 commissioned a season wise tiltable canal top solar PV plant in which state

(A) Gujarat

(B) ** Andhra Pradesh

(C) Odisha

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Established in 1956, BHEL is based in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -BHEL: One Stop solution for Solar BHEL’s solar portfolio of more than 1225 MW comprises of commissioned (more than 830 MW) and under execution plants located across the country ranging from ground mounted, roof top, canal top to floating solar PV plants.

Concept note-3: -Government has approved the grant of Maharatna status to Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) and Gas Authority of India Ltd. (GAIL). This decision was communicated on 1st February, 2013.

Concept note-4: -India’s largest floating solar power project in Telangana India’s largest floating Solar Power Project is now fully operational. NTPC declared Commercial Operation of the final part capacity of 20 MW out of 100 MW Ramagundam Floating Solar PV Project at Ramagundam, Telangana with effect from 1 July.
