BRICS summit 2016 held in which country

(A) China

(B) ** India

(C) South Africa

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2016 BRICS summit will be the eighth annual BRICS summit, an international relations conference attended by the heads of state or heads of government of the five member states Brazil, Russia, India, China and South Africa. The summit will be held in Panaji, Goa in India, from 15th to 16th October 2016.

Concept note-2: -In October 2016, the BRICS leaders convened the 8th BRICS Summit in Goa, India, under the theme of “Building Responsive, Inclusive, and Collective Solutions".

Concept note-3: -The Seventh Summit of the BRICS (Brazil, the Russian Federation, India, China and South Africa) will be held from 9-10 July 2015, in Ufa, the Russian Federation.

Concept note-4: -South Africa’s Durban is set to host the 15th BRICS Summit in August this year. South Africa took over the presidency of the five-nation bloc from China on January 1 this year. This is the third time South Africa takes the reins of the presidency of BRICS for a year.
