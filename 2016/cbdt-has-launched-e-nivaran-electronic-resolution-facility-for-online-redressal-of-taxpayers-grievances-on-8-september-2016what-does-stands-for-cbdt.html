[SOURCES]

CBDT has launched e-nivaran (electronic resolution) facility for online redressal of taxpayers’ grievances on 8 September 2016.What does stands for CBDT

(A) ** Central Board of Direct Taxes

(B) Common Board of Direct Taxes

(C) Central Board of Division Tax

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Income Tax department to promote the paperless environment launched ‘e-nivaran’, a special electronic system in order to fast-track taxpayer grievances and ensure early resolution or redressal of their complaints.

Concept note-2: -Central Board of Direct Taxation.

Concept note-3: -e-Nivaran portal facilitates the taxpayers to track the status of their complaint and also get updates on them. To ensure a timely solution for the problems, an assessment officer monitors the complaints that have been registered on the platform.
