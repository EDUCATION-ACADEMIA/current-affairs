Commonwealth Judo Championships 2016 will be held in which country

(A) India

(B) France

(C) ** South Africa

(D) New York

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2016 Commonwealth Judo Championships, Port Elizabeth, South Africa. The 2016 Commonwealth Judo Championships will take place in Port Elizabeth, South Africa, from 23-28 April 2016.

Concept note-2: -Coventry Arena will host both wrestling and judo during the Birmingham 2022 Commonwealth Games.

Concept note-3: -Judo is now a core sport in the Commonwealth Games programme. It was previously excluded in the 2018 edition in the Gold Coast but has now been reinstated as a core sport. Birmingham 2022 will be the fourth time it has been contested following appearances at Auckland 1990, Manchester 2002 and Glasgow 2014.

Concept note-4: -The published list of sports for the Victoria 2026 Commonwealth Games unfortunately does not include judo. Judo made a return to the Commonwealth Games programme this summer, being announced as a core sport following the exclusion of judo in the Games programme for the 2018 Commonwealth edition in the Gold Coast.
