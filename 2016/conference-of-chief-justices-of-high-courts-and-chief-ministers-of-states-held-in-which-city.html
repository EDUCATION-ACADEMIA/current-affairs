Conference of Chief Justices of High Courts and Chief Ministers of States held in which city

(A) Hyderabad

(B) Mumbai

(C) ** New Delhi

(D) Bangalore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi will participate in the inaugural session of the Joint Conference of Chief Ministers of States and Chief Justices of High Courts at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi on 30th April, 2022 at 10 AM.

Concept note-2: -Which is the venue of the Conference of High Court Chief Justices 2022? Notes: Chief Justice of India NV Ramana chaired the 39th conference of Chief Justices of various High Courts of the country, at the premises of the Supreme Court, New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Speaking on the occasion, Union Minister for Law and Justice Shri Kiren Rijiju said that this Joint Conference of Chief Ministers of States and Chief Justices of High Courts is aimed to deliberate on ways to deliver timely, affordable and quality justice to the citizens of this country.

Concept note-4: -The first Chief Justices’ conference was held in November 1953 and till date 38 such conferences have been organised. The last Conference was held in the year 2016.

Concept note-5: -CJI N V Ramana to chair 39th conference of chief justices today.
