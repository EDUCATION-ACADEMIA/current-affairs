Doha Bank will open next branch in which of the following state

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) West Bengal

(D) ** Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Doha Bank Q.P.S.C., Qatar opened its first branch in India at Mumbai, on June 10, 2014 and currently have 3 branches located at Sakhar Bhavan, Nariman Point, Mumbai, Chennai and Kochi.

Concept note-2: -The Insurance activities segment includes effecting contracts of insurance, carrying out contracts of insurance, arranging deals in investments and advising on investments. The company was founded in 1978 and is headquartered in Doha, Qatar.

Concept note-3: -Raghavan Seetharaman (born 1959) was the Former CEO of Doha Bank (DSM:DHBK). He resigned in March 2022 after 15 years as CEO. Set up in 1978, Doha Bank is one of the largest commercial banks in Qatar.

Concept note-4: -Doha Bank is the largest private commercial bank in the State of Qatar. It was incorporated in 1978 and commenced its domestic and international banking services in Doha, Qatar on March 15, 1979.
