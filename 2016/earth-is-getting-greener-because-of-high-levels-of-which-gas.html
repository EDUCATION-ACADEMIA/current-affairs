Earth is getting greener because of high levels of which gas

(A) Hydrogen

(B) Carbon monoxide

(C) Methane

(D) ** Carbon dioxide

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A quarter to half of Earth’s vegetated lands has shown significant greening over the last 35 years largely due to rising levels of atmospheric carbon dioxide, according to a new study published in the journal Nature Climate Change on April 25.

Concept note-2: -A CO₂-richer world. Plants need CO₂ to grow through photosynthesis. We found that the biggest factor in driving the global greening trend is the fertilisation effect of rising atmospheric CO₂ due to human activity (atmospheric concentration grew by 46 parts per million during the period studied).

Concept note-3: -Carbon dioxide (CO2) is an important heat-trapping gas, or greenhouse gas, that comes from the extraction and burning of fossil fuels (such as coal, oil, and natural gas), from wildfires, and from natural processes like volcanic eruptions.

Concept note-4: -The Greening of Planet Earth is a half-hour-long documentary advocating climate change denialism.
