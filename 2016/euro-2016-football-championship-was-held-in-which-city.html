Euro 2016 football championship was held in which city

(A) Coimbra

(B) ** Paris

(C) London

(D) Melbourne

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The final was held at the Stade de France in Saint-Denis, a suburb of Paris.

Concept note-2: -France was chosen as the host nation on 28 May 2010, after a bidding process in which they beat Italy and Turkey for the right to host the 2016 finals.

Concept note-3: -Where was EURO 2016 held? France staged EURO 2016, having previously hosted the 1960 and 1984 EUROs and the 1998 FIFA World Cup (winning the latter two competitions).

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. Euro Cup 2016 was the 15th UEFA European Championship, the quadrennial international men’s football championship of Europe organised by UEFA.
