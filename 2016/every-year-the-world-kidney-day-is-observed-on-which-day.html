Every year the world kidney day is observed on which day

(A) ** 2nd Thursday in March

(B) 4th Tuesday in March

(C) 3rd Wednesday in March

(D) 1st Thursday in March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Kidney Day started in 2006 and has not stopped growing ever since. Every year, the campaign highlights a particular theme.

Concept note-2: -World Kidney Day is a global campaign that aims to raise awareness of the importance of our kidneys to our overall health and to reduce the frequency and impact of kidney disease and its associated health problems worldwide. WKD is celebrated every second Thursday in March.

Concept note-3: -This year, it is being observed on March 10. World Kidney day is celebrated on the second Thursday of March every year. This year, it is being observed on March 10.
