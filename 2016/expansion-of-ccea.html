Expansion of CCEA

(A) Cabinet Committee Economic Affairs

(B) Cabinet Committee Economic on Affairs

(C) Cabinet on Committee Economic Affairs

(D) ** Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CCEA in administrative circles is abbreviated as Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs. Q. Who heads the Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs?

Concept note-2: -Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA)

Concept note-3: -CCEA has the mandate to review economic trends on a continuous basis, as also the problems and prospects, with a view to evolving a consistent and integrated economic policy framework for the country.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. Cabinet approves Expansion of Beti Bachao Beti Padhao: for a Pan India coverage in all the 640 districts.

Concept note-5: -The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs chaired by Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has approved development of Multipurpose Cargo (other than Container/Liquid) Berth Off Tuna Tekra at Gulf of Kutch at Kandla on Build, Operate and Transfer (BOT) basis under Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode.
