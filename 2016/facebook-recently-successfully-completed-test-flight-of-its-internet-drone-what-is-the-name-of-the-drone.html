Facebook recently successfully completed test flight of its Internet drone. What is the name of the drone?

(A) Loon Internet

(B) Aquira

(C) ** Aquila

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Facebook called the landing ‘absolutely perfect, ’ but admitted that Aquila suffered a ‘few minor, easily repairable dings’ from its gravel landing pad. Facebook’s Aquila internet drone recently completed its second full-scale test flight, and this time the aircraft had a smooth landing.

Concept note-2: -Operating Requirements Keep your drone within sight. If you use First Person View or similar technology, you must have a visual observer always keep your drone within unaided sight (for example, no binoculars). You cannot be a pilot or visual observer for more than one drone operation at a time.

Concept note-3: -For travelers or hobbyists who want some cool shots from unique perspectives, and the art of photography is secondary, a drone is the way to go. They provide flight assistance and smart features to help you get some great shots that you would never be able to get with a standard point-and-shoot consumer camera.
