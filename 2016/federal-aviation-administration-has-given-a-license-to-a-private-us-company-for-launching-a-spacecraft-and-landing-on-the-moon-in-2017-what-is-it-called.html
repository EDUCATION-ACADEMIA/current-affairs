Federal Aviation Administration has given a license to a private US company for launching a spacecraft and landing on the moon in 2017. What is it called

(A) Moon Exploration

(B) Moon Trip

(C) ** Moon Express

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SpaceShipOne (SS1), the first private crewed space vehicle, which flew past the boundary of space (100, 000 metres, or 328, 000 feet) over the United States in 2004 in competition for the Ansari X Prize.

Concept note-2: -Launch is scheduled no earlier than May 2024. Artemis 3 (2025) is the planned first crewed lunar landing. The mission depends on a support mission to place a Human Landing System (HLS) in place in a near-rectilinear halo orbit (NRHO) of the Moon prior to the launch of SLS/Orion.

Concept note-3: -On July 11, 2021, Richard Branson and Virgin Galactic made the first successful flight to space. On July 20, 2021, Jeff Bezos and Blue Origin also made a successful flight to space.
