EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ficci flo HIGHLIGHTS An all India forum for women, FLO has its headquarters in New Delhi, with 18 chapters covering different geographical regions of India.

Concept note-2: -FICCI Ladies Organization (FLO) is the women wing of the Federation of Indian Chamber of Commerce & Industry (FICCI). An all India forum for women, FLO has its headquarters in New Delhi, with 16 chapters covering different geographical regions of India. FLO represents over 6800 women entrepreneurs and professionals.

Concept note-3: -About us. Established in 1983, FICCI Ladies Organisation, currently led by Ms. Jahnabi Phookan, National President FLO, 20-21, is an all India forum for women with headquarters in New Delhi. The organization has 16 chapters set-up across India, representing over 6800 women entrepreneurs and professionals.
