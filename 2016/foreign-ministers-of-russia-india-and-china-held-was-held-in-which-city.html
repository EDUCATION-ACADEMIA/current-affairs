Foreign Ministers of Russia, India and China held was held in which city

(A) ** Moscow

(B) Mumbai

(C) Beijing

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Foreign Ministers of the Russian Federation, the Republic of India and the People’s Republic of China held their 14th Meeting in Moscow, Russian Federation, on 18 April 2016. 2.

Concept note-2: -On the evening of May 19, 2022, State Councilor and Foreign Minister Wang Yi chaired the virtual meeting of BRICS Ministers of Foreign Affairs/International Relations in Beijing.

Concept note-3: -BRIC is a grouping acronym referring to the developing countries of Brazil, Russia, India, and China, which are identified as rising economic powers.

Concept note-4: -Sergey Viktorovich Lavrov (Russian: , pronounced [sʲɪrˈɡʲej ˈvʲiktərəvʲɪtɕ lɐˈvrof]; born 21 March 1950) is a Russian diplomat and politician who has served as the Foreign Minister of Russia since 2004.
