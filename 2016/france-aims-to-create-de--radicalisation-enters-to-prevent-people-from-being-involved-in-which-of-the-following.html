France aims to create de- radicalisation enters to prevent people from being involved in which of the following

(A) Jihadist Groups

(B) ISIS

(C) Terrorism

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Radicalisation is defined as the process by which people come to support terrorism and extremism and, in some cases, to then participate in terrorist groups.

Concept note-2: -Prevent is one of the four elements of the Government’s Counter-Terrorism Strategy, Contest. There are 4 elements of this strategy: Prepare, Protect, Pursue and Prevent.

Concept note-3: -Radicalisation is a safeguarding issue and refers to the process by which a person comes to support terrorism and other forms of extremism leading to terrorism.
