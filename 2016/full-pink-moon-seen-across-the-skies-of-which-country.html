Full Pink Moon seen across the skies of which country

(A) Canada

(B) Europe

(C) England

(D) ** North America

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pink full moon is seen behind a stork in the village of Rzanicino, near Skopje in North Macedonia.

Concept note-2: -JAKARTA-Pink full moon or pink moon occurs in several countries, including Indonesia. The appearance of the face or disk of the moon looks so close because it is at its closest point to Earth. The nickname Pink Moon appeared around the 1930s after the American Indian tribal name for April.

Concept note-3: -This weekend will see the rising of the “Pink Moon, ” always one of the year’s most popular astronomical spectacles. It will look its best as it appears low on the eastern horizon on Saturday, April 16, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Will the pink Moon be visible in India? Yes, this year, India will be able to see the bright Moon illuminating the sky.

Concept note-5: - How To See the Pink Supermoon The moon will be visible anywhere on Earth on a clear night with an unobstructed sky. To see the most dazzling version, go to a dark place with as little ambient light as possible.
