German home appliances major BSH has opened the technology centre in which city

(A) Chennai

(B) Chandigarh

(C) Ranchi

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Besides, the German firm will open the first experience centre of its luxury built-in appliances brand Gaggenau in Mumbai this year.

Concept note-2: -The group stemmed from a joint venture set up in May 1967 between Robert Bosch GmbH (Stuttgart) and Siemens AG (Munich), and it posted annual sales of 15.6 billion euros in the year 2021. BSH is an abbreviation for Bosch und Siemens Hausgeräte.

Concept note-3: -BSH Hausgeräte GmbH makes Bosch and Siemens products, and is Europe’s biggest home appliance maker.

Concept note-4: -Ninety-four percent of the share capital of Robert Bosch GmbH is held by Robert Bosch Stiftung GmbH, a charitable foundation. The remaining shares are held by Robert Bosch GmbH and by a corporation owned by the Bosch family.

Concept note-5: -It includes the principal brand names Bosch and Siemens, Gaggenau, Balay, Neff, Thermador, Constructa, Viva and Ufesa brands, and further six regional brands.
