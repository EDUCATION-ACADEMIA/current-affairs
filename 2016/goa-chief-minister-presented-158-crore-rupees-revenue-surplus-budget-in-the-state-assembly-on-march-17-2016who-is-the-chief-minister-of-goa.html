Goa chief minister presented 158 crore rupees revenue surplus budget in the state assembly on March 17 2016.Who is the chief minister of Goa

(A) Manohar Parrikar

(B) Raghubar Das

(C) ** Laxmikant Parsekar

(D) Pawan Kumar Chamling

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pramod Sawant, presented the Budget for the state for the financial year 2022-23 on March 30, 2022. The Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) of Goa for 2022-23 (at current prices) is projected to be Rs 91, 417 crore. This is a growth of 2.2% over the revised estimate of GSDP for 2021-22 (Rs 89, 422 crore).

Concept note-2: -Manohar Parrikar was born to a Gaud Saraswat Brahmin family in Mapusa, Goa. He studied at Loyola High School, Margao.

Concept note-3: -She served as the Chief Minister of Goa, Daman and Diu on two occasions, and also was the President of the Maharashtrawadi Gomantak Party. She is the first (and as of 2023, the only) woman to serve as the Chief Minister of Goa, Daman and Diu. Kakodkar in 2011.
