GoI has notified pay revisions as per 7th Pay Commission providing a hike of how many times to government employees

(A) 2.34

(B) 2.76

(C) ** 2.57

(D) 2.49

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 7th Pay Commission’s new “Fitment factor” will stand at a 3% annual increment for all employees.

Concept note-2: -Latest updates on 7th pay commission Over 23 Lakh retired Pensioners to get benefit of up to Rs. 18, 000 under 7th Pay commission. 3 Pending Dearness Allowance (DA) installments to be restored from 01 July, 2021. Modi Govt Extended the LTC for the period of next two years till September 25, 2022 as a Diwali Gift.

Concept note-3: -Important Recommendations By The 7th Pay Commission The minimum pay for a newly recruited Class I Officer shall be INR 56, 100 per month. The maximum pay for government employees has been recommended to an increased INR 2.5 lakhs for the top scale of employees like Cabinet Secretary and equivalent.
