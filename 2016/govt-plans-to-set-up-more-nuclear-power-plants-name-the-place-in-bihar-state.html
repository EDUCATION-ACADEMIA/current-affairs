Govt plans to set up more nuclear power plants. Name the place in Bihar state

(A) Bulandshahr

(B) ** Razauli

(C) Patiala

(D) Patana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nuclear Power Corporation of India Ltd (NPCIL) is planning to undertake the Rajauli Nuclear Power Plant project in Bihar, India. The project involves the construction of a 2, 800MW nuclear power plant with four units of 700MW each.

Concept note-2: -Jaitapur Nuclear Power Project is a proposed nuclear power plant in India.

Concept note-3: -Rajasthan Atomic Power Station, located in Rawatbhata in the state of Rajasthan. The plant now operates 4 IPHWR reactors and one CANDU reactor.
