Govt to decide ST status of communities in which state

(A) ** Assam

(B) Haryana

(C) Kerala

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Andhra Pradesh, Arunanchal Pradsh, Assam, Bihar, Gujarat, Goa, Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Orissa, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Tripura And West Bengal.

Concept note-2: -What is the Process of Inclusion in the ST List? The process to include tribes in the ST list begins with the recommendation from the respective State governments, which are then sent to the Tribal Affairs Ministry, which reviews and sends them to the Registrar General of India for approval.

Concept note-3: -Madhya Pradesh has the highest tribal population in India as per 2011 Census.
