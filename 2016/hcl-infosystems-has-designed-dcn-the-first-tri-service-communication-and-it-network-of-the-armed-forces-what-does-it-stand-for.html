HCL Infosystems has designed DCN, the first tri-service communication and IT network of the armed forces. What does it stand for

(A) Defence Combat Network

(B) ** Defence Communication Network

(C) Defence Combatant Network

(D) Defence Council Network

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is a Defence Communication Network? Ans. A strategic, highly secure and scalable system that has a pan-India reach and enable the army, air force, navy and the Special Forces Command to share situational awareness for a faster decision-making process is called the Defence communication Network.

Concept note-2: -AFNet is the first network of its kind for Indian Defence Institutions, which interlinks major installations throughout the country on a high bandwidth network.

Concept note-3: -India’s first Integrated Defence Communication Network (DCN) was dedicated to the nation by the Defence Minister on 1 July 2016. DCN is a state-of-the-art strategic, exclusive, secure communication network. It is imperative for augmenting Network-Centric Warfare (NCW).
