Herbalife International India, a global premier nutrition company, has entered into a two-year exclusive partnership with which organization

(A) Board of Control for Cricket in India

(B) Indian Hockey Federation

(C) ** Indian Olympic Association

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Herbalife Nutrition Ltd. Los Angeles, California, U.S.

Concept note-2: -Mark Hughes (1956–2000) founded Herbalife Nutrition in 1980, with the goal of changing the nutritional habits of the world, one person at a time.

Concept note-3: -Herbalife’s FSSAI license falls into the central FSSAI license as its turnover exceeds more than 20 crores.

Concept note-4: -Herbalife International, Inc. markets and distributes a broad spectrum of more than 150 herb-and botanicals-based weight management and dieting products, cosmetics, and general health and nutrition products, through a worldwide network of more than one million independent distributors in 50 countries.
