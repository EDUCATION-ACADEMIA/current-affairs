How many countries are signed a landmark declaration to rid the world of drug-resistant infections or Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) or superbugs.

(A) ** 193

(B) 169

(C) 156

(D) 97

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 193 countries of the United Nations have agreed a landmark declaration to rid the world of drug-resistant infections or “superbugs".

Concept note-2: -There are three types of public health antimicrobials: sterilizers, disinfectants, and sanitizers.

Concept note-3: -The current list includes COVID-19, Crimean-Cong haemorrhagic fever, Ebola virus disease and Marburg virus disease, Lassa fever, Middle East respiratory syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS), Nipah and henipaviral diseases, Rift Valley fever, Zika and Disease X.
