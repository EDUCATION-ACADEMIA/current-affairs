How many Deputy Governor in Reserve Bank of India (RBI)

(A) 1

(B) 2

(C) 3

(D) ** 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At present there are 4 deputy governors of the RBI.

Concept note-2: -Besides the governor, the RBI has four deputy governors. The three other deputy governors of the bank are M K Jain, M Rajeshwar Rao and T Rabi Sankar.

Concept note-3: -Chintaman Dwarkanath Deshmukh, a member of the Indian Civil Service, was the first Indian Governor of the Bank. His association with the Bank commenced in 1939, when he was appointed Government’s liason officer. He later served as Secretary and thereafter in 1941 as Deputy Governor of the Bank.
