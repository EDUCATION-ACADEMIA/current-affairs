How many Indians received the Magsaysay Award 2016

(A) ** 2

(B) 4

(C) 5

(D) 1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Two Indians, human rights activist Bezwada Wilson and south Indian classical musician TM Krishna, have been conferred the Ramon Magsaysay Award for 2016.

Concept note-2: -Karnataka-born Bezwada Wilson, a prolific campaigner for eradication of manual scavenging in India, and Carnatic singer T M Krishna from Chennai, were today chosen for the prestigious Ramon Magsaysay Award for 2016.

Concept note-3: -The first Indian to win the Ramon Magsaysay Award is Vinoba Bhave in 1958 under the category of Community Leadership.

Concept note-4: -The 2022 Ramon Magsaysay Awardees are Sotheara Chhim (Cambodia), Bernadette Madrid (Philippines), Tadashi Hattori (Japan) and Gary Bencheghib (Indonesia).

Concept note-5: -First Indian woman who won Ramon Magsaysay Award-Mother Teresa.
