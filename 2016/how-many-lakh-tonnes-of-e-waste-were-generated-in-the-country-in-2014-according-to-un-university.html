How many lakh tonnes of e-waste were generated in the country in 2014 according to UN University

(A) 14

(B) ** 17

(C) 20

(D) 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"As per the United Nations University report, 17 lakh tonnes of e-waste was generated in the country in 2014, ” Environment Minister Anil Madhav Dave said in a written reply in Rajya Sabha.

Concept note-2: -Many hazardous substances in e-waste are extremely dangerous to human health and the environment. In 2020-2021, India processed 3.4 lakh tonnes of e-waste.

Concept note-3: -Globally, consumers generated 44.7 million metric tons of e-waste in 2016, according to a new report from the International Solid Waste Association, the United Nations University and the International Telecommunication Union.

Concept note-4: -The United States topped the list of the 184 countries analyzed for the total volume of e-waste generated each year, at 9.4 million tons in 2012; followed by China, with 7.2 million tons.

Concept note-5: -The largest producer of electronic waste is China with a total of 10.1 million tons.
