How many Ministers (Ministers of State) were inducted in the Union Council of Ministers in a major expansion

(A) 17

(B) ** 19

(C) 23

(D) 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the Constitution of India, the total number of ministers in the council of ministers must not exceed 15% of the total number of members of the Lok Sabha.

Concept note-2: -of article 75. “(1A) The total number of Ministers, including the Prime Minister, in the Council of Ministers shall not exceed fifteen per cent. of the total number of members of the House of the People.
