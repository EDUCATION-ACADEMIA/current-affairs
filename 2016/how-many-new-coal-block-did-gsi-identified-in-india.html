Concept note-1: -51 mineral blocks including lithium and gold handed over to 11 state governments: Centre. Based on the priorities set by the government of India and the importance and urgency of proposals presented by the members and stakeholders, the annual programme of GSI is given a final shape.

Concept note-2: -With its headquarters at Kolkata, GSI has six Regional offices at Lucknow, Jaipur, Nagpur, Hyderabad, Shillong and Kolkata and offices in almost all States of the country.

Concept note-3: -Deucha Panchami (-) coal mine or Deaucha Panchami coal block is a coal mine situated in Deucha and Panchamati area under Mohamadbazar community Development Block of Birbhum district, West Bengal, India. This coal mine or block belongs to Birbhum coalfield area.

Concept note-4: -Coal deposits are primarily found in eastern and south-central India. Jharkhand, Odisha, and Chhattisgarh accounted for almost 70% of the total known coal reserves in India.
