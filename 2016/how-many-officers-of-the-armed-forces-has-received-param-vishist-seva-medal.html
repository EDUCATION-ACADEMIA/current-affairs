How many officers of the armed forces has received Param Vishist Seva Medal

(A) ** 13

(B) 4

(C) 7

(D) 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Param Vishisht Seva Medal: Olympian Neeraj Chopra honoured.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Neeraj Chopra. Neeraj Chopra has been honored with Param Vishisht Seva Medal. Neeraj Chopra, a javelin thrower, became only the second Indian to win an Olympic gold medal. At the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, he earned a gold medal in the Javelin Throw event.

Concept note-3: -Conditions of Eligibility: The medal is awarded for distinguished service of the most exceptional order.
