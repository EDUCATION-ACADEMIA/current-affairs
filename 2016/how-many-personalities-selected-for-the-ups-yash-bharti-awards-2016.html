How many personalities selected for the UP’s Yash Bharti Awards 2016

(A) 23

(B) ** 54

(C) 47

(D) 35

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Uttar Pradesh government on 23 October 2016 declared the list of Yash Bharati awardees 2016. 54 personalities were selected for the coveted award for the year 2016-17.

Concept note-2: -Instituted in 1994, it is awarded to those personalities whose contribution is remarkable in the field of literature, social work, medicine, film, science, journalism, handicrafts, culture, education, music, drama, sports, industry and astrology.

Concept note-3: -Yash Bharti award is the highest award of the State Government of Uttar Pradesh. It was instituted in the year 1994.

Concept note-4: -The Uttar Pradesh Government has instituted several awards in different fields such as Vishwa Bharti Award, Maharishi Valmiki Award, Maharishi Vyas Award, Maharishi Narada Award, Ved Pandit Award, Vishesh Award, Vividh Award and Namit Award.
