How many states have already signed the ’24x7 Power For All’ Documents

(A) 18

(B) 20

(C) ** 22

(D) 24

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Villages in just six states receive round-the-clock or 24-hour electricity supply, according to the data from the Power Ministry. Rural areas in Gujarat, Himachal Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, West Bengal and Telangana receive 24 hours of power supply.

Concept note-2: -The Government of India is committed to improve the quality of life of its citizens through higher electricity consumption. Our aim is to provide each household access to electricity, round the clock. The ‘Power for All’ programme is a major step in this direction.
