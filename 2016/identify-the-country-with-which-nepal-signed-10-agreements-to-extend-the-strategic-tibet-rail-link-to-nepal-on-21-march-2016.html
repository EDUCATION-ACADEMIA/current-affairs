Identify the country with which Nepal signed 10 agreements to extend the strategic Tibet rail link to Nepal on 21 March 2016

(A) Russia

(B) ** China

(C) India

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nepal seals agreement on transit rights through China.

Concept note-2: -In January 1856, a representative group of Tibet came to Kathmandu for discussion of the treaty. After a long discussion, the representative group ultimately signed in Thapathali a treaty on 24 March. 24 March 1856 (Chaitra 3, 1912 B.S.)

Concept note-3: -The Trade and Payments Agreement between Nepal and China has been in operation since 1981. The agreement allowed use of number of trading points for transport of goods between the two countries.

Concept note-4: -India–Nepal Jaynagar–Bardibas railway line is a semi-operational line between India and Nepal. DEMU passenger service is operation on this route.

Concept note-5: -China plans to extend this railway up to Lake Paiku/Gyirong, which is around 60 km from the Rasuwa border crossing. Construction of the railway from Shigatse to Gyirong is expected to be complete by 2022.
