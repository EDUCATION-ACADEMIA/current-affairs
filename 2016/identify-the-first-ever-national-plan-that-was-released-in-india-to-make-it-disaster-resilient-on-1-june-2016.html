Identify the first ever national plan that was released in India to make it disaster resilient on 1 June 2016

(A) Five Year Plan

(B) National Plan on Climate Change

(C) NITI Aayog Plan

(D) ** National Disaster Management Plan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Disaster Management Plan (NDMP) was first released in 2016. The primary aim of the plan is to make India disaster resilient and drastically lessen the damage caused during and in the aftermath of disasters, natural and man-made.

Concept note-2: -National Disaster Management Plan (NDMP), 2016 is the first-ever national plan prepared in the country for disaster management. With National Disaster Management Plan 2016 India has aligned our National Plan with the Sendai Framework for Disaster Risk Reduction 2015-2030, to which India is a signatory.

Concept note-3: -In June 2016, India launched its first National Disaster Management Plan (NDMP) with the aim of improving the country’s resilience to disasters and reducing the loss of lives and assets. This issue highlights the importance of the NDMP and its significance to resilience building in India.
