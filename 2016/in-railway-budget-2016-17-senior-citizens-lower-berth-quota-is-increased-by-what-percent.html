In Railway Budget 2016-17, senior citizen’s lower berth quota is increased by what percent

(A) 100 %

(B) ** 50 %

(C) 60 %

(D) 80 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -5. Quota for senior citizens for lower berths in each coach to be increased by 50% thereby adding nearly 120 lower berths per train for then.

Concept note-2: -Railways are preparing a Plan size of Rs. 1, 21, 000 crore in 2016-17.

Concept note-3: -According to the rules, in all trains having reserved sleeping accommodation, a combined quota of six lower berths per coach in Sleeper class and 3 lower berths per coach each in AC 3 tier and AC-2 tier classes has been earmarked for Senior Citizens. Female passengers of 45 years of age and above and pregnant women.

Concept note-4: -The capital outlay for 2016-17 was set at ₹1.21 lakh crore (US$15 billion), a 20% increase from last year. The revenue from traffic was projected at ₹1.85 lakh crore (US$23 billion).

Concept note-5: -* Lower berth quota(SS quota) is allotted only to the single lady passenger of age 45 and above travelling alone or senior citizen passenger travelling alone. So, more than 1 ticket cannot be booked AT A TIME in LOWER BERTH QUOTA, even if all the passengers are severally and jointly eligible for the same.
