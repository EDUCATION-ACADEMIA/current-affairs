In which bank has recently tied up with Ferrari for co-branded credit card

(A) YES Bank

(B) ** ICICI Bank

(C) Axis Bank

(D) Kotak Mahindra Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ferrari Credit Cards, which will be available in two variants-Ferrari Platinum Credit Card and Ferrari Signature Credit Card-will be offered to elite customers, who are enthusiasts of the iconic luxury brand.

Concept note-2: -The ICICI Bank Ferrari Signature Credit Card is a co-branded credit card issued by the bank in partnership with Ferrari.

Concept note-3: -Mumbai, 29 August 2022: ICICI Bank today announced its partnership with National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) to launch a range of credit cards on RuPay, the indigenous payments network.

Concept note-4: -Amazon Pay ICICI Credit Card is a co-branded credit card that offers benefits on online shopping. This card is specifically designed to avail discount on your purchases at Amazon. However, only Amazon Prime members can earn the maximum benefits via this credit card.

Concept note-5: -Central Bank of India was the first Bank to introduce credit cards in India.
