In Which of the following railway station Ministry of Railways has launched high speed Wi-Fi hotspot on May 5, 2016

(A) ** Vijayawada, Kachiguda and Raipur

(B) Vijayawada, Kachiguda and Bhubaneshwar

(C) Vijayawada, Kachiguda and Kochi

(D) Adilabad, Ahmednagar and Kochi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The railways provided Wi-Fi facility first at the Mumbai Railway station in 2016, reaching its 6000th station, at Hazaribagh town of Jharkhand .

Concept note-2: -A: Few of the trains do have wifi services. You can connect with the high-speed wifi connection if you are traveling via Shatabdi specific, Rajdhani, and Duronto trains. Aside from these trains, Gatimaan and Tejas express also provide these facilities for their passengers.
