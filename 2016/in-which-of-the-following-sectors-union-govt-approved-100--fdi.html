[SOURCES]

In which of the following sectors, Union Govt approved 100 % FDI

(A) Stock Exchange

(B) Pharmaceutical

(C) Defence

(D) ** Both A & B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -FDI up to 100%, under the automatic route is permitted for manufacturing of medical devices.

Concept note-2: -100% FDI is allowed in Chemical sector under automatic route.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution The correct answer is Private Security Agencies.

Concept note-4: -1.1 As per extant policy, FDI, up to 100%, under the automatic route, is permitted in the power sector (except atomic energy). This includes generation, transmission and distribution of electricity, as well as power trading, subject to the provisions of the Electricity Act, 2003.
