In which Place imposed ban on sale of Army Uniforms in the state

(A) Haryana

(B) ** Punjab

(C) Uttra Pradesh

(D) Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The new Digital Pattern Combat Uniform for Indian Army soldiers was unveiled on 15 January 2022 (Army Day). The improved uniform has a contemporary look and functional design. The fabric has been made lighter, stronger, breathable, quick drying and easier to maintain.

Concept note-2: -What is Indian army uniform called? Ans. The General Duty Uniform of the Indian army is known as 5SD.

Concept note-3: -All Soldiers are required to wear an AGSU. The Army Service Uniform (ASU) will become an optional, formal and ceremonial uniform.

Concept note-4: -What Is the Purpose of AR 670-1? AR 670-1 prescribes uniform and grooming standards for Army personnel. According to Paragraph 1-1, “The Army is a profession. A soldier’s appearance measures part of their professionalism.”
