In Which State Introduce Free Bus Pass Scheme

(A) Kerala

(B) ** Tamil Nadu

(C) West Bengal

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In May 2021, the Tamil Nadu government launched a scheme that made public city and town buses (with certain caveats) free for women. While the rides are completely free (with no restrictions on the number of trips per day or month), the scheme is applicable only on ‘white board’ buses.

Concept note-2: -Students studying from 1st Std to 12 th Std. in the Schools recognized by Government and students studying in the Government ITI, Government Colleges and Government Polytechnics are eligible to avail this free bus pass facility.

Concept note-3: -CHENNAI: Tamil Nadu continues to charge the lowest bus fare in the country across all sectors like ordinary buses, express and semi deluxe buses and air conditioned buses.
