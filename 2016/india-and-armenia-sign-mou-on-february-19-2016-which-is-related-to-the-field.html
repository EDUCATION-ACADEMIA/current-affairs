India and Armenia Sign MoU on February 19, 2016, Which is related to the field

(A) Brodcast

(B) ** Agriculture

(C) Medicine

(D) exchange food and drink

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India-Armenia sign MoU on High Impact Community Development Projects in Armenia. The eight-session of the India-Armenia Intergovernmental Commission on Trade, Economic, Scientific and Technological, Cultural and Educational Cooperation was held in Yerevan yesterday.

Concept note-2: -Armenia and India maintain active political ties. Effective cooperation exists between the two nations within international bodies. After Armenia’s independence in 1991, Armenian-Indian relations were reestablished. Diplomatic relations were established between the Republic of Armenia and India in 1992.

Concept note-3: -India on Friday agreed to double its high-impact community development project in Sri Lanka with a new bilateral agreement signed during the much-anticipated visit of External Affairs Minister S Jaishankar.

Concept note-4: -International relations between Armenia and India have been described as amicable. In 2022, it was reported that the two nations were exploring the possible of long-term military cooperation.
