India and which country has signed 6 agreements/Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) to boost bilateral cooperation.

(A) Qatar

(B) USA

(C) Bangladesh

(D) ** Kyrgyzstan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India and United Arab Emirates have signed an MoU to establish a framework to facilitate and enhance bilateral cooperation on climate action.

Concept note-2: -India has symbiotic relationship with Bhutan. Today, both countries signed a MoU which will open new vistas of bilateral co-operation in the areas of Climate Change, waste management etc.

Concept note-3: -India and Panama have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to enhance cooperation in the training of diplomats.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Australia. India and Australia have signed an MoU to promote tourism. The Union Minister of Commerce and Industry Piyush Goyal and Australian Minister of Trade, Tourism, and Investment, Dan Tehan signed the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) on 11 Feb 2022.
