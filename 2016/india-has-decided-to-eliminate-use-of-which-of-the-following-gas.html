[SOURCES]

India has decided to eliminate use of which of the following gas

(A) CFC-12

(B) HR2D

(C) ** HFC-23

(D) SF6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hydrofluorocarbons (HFCs) are greenhouse gases (GHGs) commonly used by federal agencies in a wide variety of applications, including refrigeration, air-conditioning (AC), building insulation, fire extinguishing systems, and aerosols.

Concept note-2: -India emits about 3 gigatonnes (Gt) CO2eq of greenhouse gases each year; about two and a half tons per person, which is less than the world average. The country emits 7% of global emissions, despite having 17% of the world population.

Concept note-3: -Kigali Agreement and India According to the treaty, India should start phase down by 2028 and cut HFC emission by 15% of 2024-26 levels by the year 2047.
