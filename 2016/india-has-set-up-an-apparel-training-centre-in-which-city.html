India has set up an apparel training centre in which city

(A) Abuja

(B) ** Kaduna

(C) Calabar

(D) Oshogbo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Raipur. With the help of the Apparel Export Promotion Council, three Apparel Training & Designing Centres (ATDC) have been set up in Chhattisgarh at Bilaspur, Raipur, Bhilai, and Rajnandgaon.

Concept note-2: -Due to easy availability of raw material, humid climate for spinning and weaving, Ahmedabad city in Gujarat has emerged as a prominent cotton textile industrial centre which is also referred to as the “Manchester of India."

Concept note-3: -The main centre of the textile industry in Gujarat is Ahmedabad. It is referred to as the Manchester of India. Mumbai, the capital city of Maharashtra was the leading cotton textile producing centre in India during British rule. Ahmedabad at present is considered to be the Manchester of India.

Concept note-4: -Indore and Ludhiana are best recognized for producing top quality ready-made garments and Bareily is also known for its excellent zari works which are one of the bests in the world.

Concept note-5: -Mumbai is the largest center for the cotton textile industry in India.
