India has signed government-to-government deal with which country to buy 145 M-777 Ultra-Light Howitzers worth $750 million under the Foreign Military Sales programme.

(A) Japan

(B) Russia

(C) China

(D) ** USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has contracted 145 M777 guns from the BAE Systems (British Aerospace) and over half of them have been inducted.

Concept note-2: -Ending a three-decade-old howitzer drought, India today signed a contract with the US for buying 145 M777 ultralight howitzers for $737 million (Rs 5000 crore). A senior MoD official today formally signed the Letter of Offer and Acceptance (LOA) with the US government for the seven regiments of howitzers.

Concept note-3: -The estimated cost is $114 million.
