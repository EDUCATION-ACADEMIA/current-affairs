India Post on 3 June 2016 released the "My Stamp" with which ecommerce company

(A) Caratlane

(B) ShopClues

(C) Jabong

(D) ** Amazon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The postage stamp, which is the legally valid receipt for postage paid, can be released by either the Minister for Communications or Minister of State for Communications.

Concept note-2: -The First Stamp of Independent India was issued on 21 November 1947. It depicts the Indian Flag with the patriots’ slogan, Jai Hind (Long Live India), on the top right hand corner.

Concept note-3: -India Post is one of the prime carriers that Amazon India uses as a delivery channel.
