India ranked first in FDI Destination.Which country replaced by India

(A) ** China

(B) Indonesia

(C) Mexico

(D) Brazil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Singapore is ranked one FDI provider to India. The small island nation has invested $15.9 billion in FY22 which is accounted for 27 per cent of India’s total FDI received. USA is India’s second biggest investor with FDI of $10.5 billion, with 18 per cent of total FDI.

Concept note-2: -Sector-wise FDI Equity Inflows during April-September 2022 During the first half of this fiscal, Singapore emerged as the top investor. It was followed by Mauritius, the U.A.E., the U.S.A., the Netherlands and Japan.

Concept note-3: -Singapore (27.01%), USA (17.94%), Mauritius (15.98%), Netherland (7.86%) and Switzerland (7.31%) emerge as top 5 countries for FDI equity inflows into India FY 2021-22.

Concept note-4: -The United States is the top destination of inward foreign direct investment. Source: IMF Data, Coordinated Direct Investment Survey. Note: Chart shows inward FDI positions.
