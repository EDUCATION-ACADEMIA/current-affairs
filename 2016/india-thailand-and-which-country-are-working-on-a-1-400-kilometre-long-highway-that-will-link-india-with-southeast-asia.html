India, Thailand and which country are working on a 1, 400-kilometre long highway that will link India with Southeast Asia

(A) ** Myanmar

(B) Bangladesh

(C) Bhutan

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under India’s Look East policy, the Myawaddy-Thinggan Nyenaung-Kawkareik section of the India–Myanmar–Thailand Trilateral Highway that connects India and Thailand via Myanmar has been opened to tourists, making for a splendid road trip.

Concept note-2: -Q. 1 Is India Myanmar Thailand’s trilateral Highway completed? Ans. 1 No, it is under construction and expected to be completed by 2023.

Concept note-3: -How far is it from India to Thailand? The distance between India and Thailand is 2475 km. The road distance is 4158.4 km.

Concept note-4: -India’s Andaman and Nicobar Islands share a maritime border with Thailand, Myanmar and Indonesia.
