Indian Navy has commissioned INS Karna, an independent base of Marine Commandos (MARCOS) at which place

(A) ** Visakhapatnam

(B) Kochi

(C) Gandhinagar

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Marine Special Force (IMSF) was located there in 1987. On 12 July 2016, the naval base INS Karna was commissioned near Visakhapatnam as the garrison & permanent base for the unit.

Concept note-2: -Indian Naval Ship (INS) Karna is the dedicated base for the Indian Special Operations Forces, MARCOS or Marine Commandos, raised in February 1987 as the Indian Marine Special Force (IMSF). It is located in the home port of the Navy’s Eastern Command, Vishakapatnam.

Concept note-3: -Marine commandoes or Marcos are special forces of the Indian Navy comprising the toughest soldiers who undergo rigorous training to conduct swift and stealthy response in denied territories.

Concept note-4: -Any Indian navy officer can apply for Marcos but their age not be more then 20 Year. So if applicant those wants to apply for Marcos must be remember check their age before applying.
