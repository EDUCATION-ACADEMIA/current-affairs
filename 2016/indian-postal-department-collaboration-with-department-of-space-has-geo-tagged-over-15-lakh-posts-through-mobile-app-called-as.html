Indian Postal Department collaboration with Department of Space has geo-tagged over 1.5 Lakh posts through Mobile App called as

(A) Rhuvan

(B) ** Bhuvan

(C) Shuvan

(D) Ghuvan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bhuvan IndiaPost App is a user-friendly mobile application which enables to collect and report for geo-tagged of Post Office information on various parameters such as type of post office, name, services offered, delivery status, PIN-code and address.

Concept note-2: -Indian postal network is the largest in the world.

Concept note-3: -The new 10, 000 post offices will be opened during the current financial year, which will take the total number of post offices in India to around 1.7 lakh.

Concept note-4: -The IPPB has enabled more than 136, 000 post offices, including 110, 000 in rural India, to provide a complete suite of banking services.
