Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) has joined hands with which bank to chalk out a detailed plan for promotion of rail ticketing activities

(A) ICICI Bank

(B) Axis Bank

(C) ** SBI

(D) Punjab National Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is an Indian public sector undertaking that provides ticketing, catering, and tourism services for the Indian Railways.

Concept note-2: -IRCTC arranges for full train charters, coaches as well as reserved berth programmes through regular trains for tourism purposes. During this short period we have been able to provide such facilities to over 50, 000 persons.

Concept note-3: -One97 Communications Limited (OCL) that owns the brand Paytm, country’s leading payments and financial services company has empowered its users with a seamless train ticket booking experience as the authorised partner of Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC).

Concept note-4: -Indian Railways plays a significant role in the growth and development of domestic as well as foreign tourism. It runs many tourism specific trains to promote tourism in India. It provide various promotional schemes and tour packages to attract domestic and foreign passengers.
