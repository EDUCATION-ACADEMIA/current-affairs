India’s first eco-friendly liquefied natural gas (LNG)-driven bus was launched in which state

(A) New Delhi

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) ** Kerala

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tata Motors has unveiled India’s first liquefied natural gas (LNG) powered bus at an event in Kerala.

Concept note-2: -The first private CNG bus in Kerala-Kochi City bus KL-07-AM-8390 working a Vyttila circular service.

Concept note-3: -KOCHI: In a positive step towards curbing air pollution, India’s first commercial Liquified Compressed natural gas (LNG) bus was launched in Kochi. Created by Petronet, the bus fitted with a 180 kg cryogenic tank, the bus can travel up to 900 km in a single filling.

Concept note-4: -Tata Starbus LP 909 CNG: 40 Seater bus price starts from Rs 28.03 Lakh* Onwards. The 2023 price of Tata Starbus LP 909 CNG: 40 Seater bus can vary slightly from state to state and city to city.
