India’s most famous Delhi Race Course Road renamed as _________

(A) APJ Abdul Kalam Road

(B) Aurangzeb Road

(C) Atal Bihari Vajpayee Road

(D) ** Lok Kalyan Marg

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -7, Lok Kalyan Marg, formerly 7, Race Course Road, is the official residence and principal workplace of the Prime Minister of India. Situated on Lok Kalyan Marg, New Delhi, the official name of the Prime Minister’s residence complex is Panchavati.

Concept note-2: -Pincode of PRIME MINISTER RESIDENCE 7 RACE COURSE ROAD NIRMAN BHAWAN NEW DELHI CHANAKYA PURI 110011 Pincode.net.in.

Concept note-3: -Pincode of 7 Lok Kalyan Marg New Delhi, Delhi is 110021.
