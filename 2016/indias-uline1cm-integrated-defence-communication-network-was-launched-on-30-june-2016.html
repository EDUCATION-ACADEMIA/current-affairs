India’s _________ integrated defence communication network was launched on 30 June 2016

(A) ** 1st

(B) 2nd

(C) 3rd

(D) 4th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first Integrated Defence Communication Network (DCN) was dedicated to the nation by the Defence Minister on 1 July 2016. DCN is a state-of-the-art strategic, exclusive, secure communication network. It is imperative for augmenting Network-Centric Warfare (NCW).

Concept note-2: -What is a Defence Communication Network? Ans. A strategic, highly secure and scalable system that has a pan-India reach and enable the army, air force, navy and the Special Forces Command to share situational awareness for a faster decision-making process is called the Defence communication Network.

Concept note-3: -Headquarters Integrated Defence Staff, popularly known as HQ IDS, was raised on 01 October 2001.

Concept note-4: -Chief of Integrated Defence Staff to the Chairman, Chiefs of Staff Committee (CISC) Air Marshal BR Krishna paying homage to the fallen heroes at the National War Memorial in New Delhi on the occasion of 7th Armed Forces Veterans Day on January 14, 2023.
