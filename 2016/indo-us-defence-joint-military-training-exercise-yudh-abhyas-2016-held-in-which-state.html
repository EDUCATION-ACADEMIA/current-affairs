Indo-US defence joint military training exercise ’Yudh Abhyas 2016’ held in which state

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) Rajasthan

(C) Assam

(D) ** Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India and the US have successfully concluded the 18th edition of joint military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas’ in the Auli district of Uttarakhand on Saturday.

Concept note-2: -Notably, the Indo-US military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas 2022’ commenced in Uttarakhand’s Auli on November 19. The joint military exercise is conducted annually between India and US with the aim of exchanging best practices.

Concept note-3: -India-US Yudh Abhyas 2022: Why China Is Rattled As New Delhi Hosts Joint Military Exercise Near LAC In Auli, Uttarakhand.

Concept note-4: -The 16th edition of the Yudh Abhyas exercise took place between February 8 and February 21, 2021, at Mahajan Field Firing Range in Rajasthan.

Concept note-5: -Exercise “SURYA KIRAN” is conducted annually between India and Nepal with the aim to enhance interoperability in jungle warfare & counter terrorism operations in mountainous terrain and HADR under UN mandate.
