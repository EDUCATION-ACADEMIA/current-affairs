Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board sets up two advisory committees.Which of the following has the head of advisory committee on corporate insolvency and liquidation

(A) ** Uday Kotak

(B) Mohandas Pai

(C) M V Nair

(D) J Ranganayakulu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Committee was constituted to examine issues arising from the implementation of the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code. The Code provides a time-bound 180-day process to resolve insolvency of companies and individuals.

Concept note-2: -2022: Disciplinary Committee 1: Shri Ravi Mital, Chairperson. Disciplinary Committee 2: Shri Sudhaker Shukla, Whole-time Member. Disciplinary Committee 3: Shri Jayanti Prasad, Whole-time Member.
