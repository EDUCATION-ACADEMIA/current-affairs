International Day of Families was observed on which date

(A) ** May 15

(B) May 16

(C) May 17

(D) May 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 1993, the General Assembly decided in a resolution (A/RES/47/237) that 15 May of every year should be observed as The International Day of Families.

Concept note-2: -The International Day of Families is observed every year on 15 May to reflect the importance that the international community attaches to families.

Concept note-3: -Theme: Families and Urbanization Urbanization is one of the most important megatrends shaping our world and the life and wellbeing of families worldwide.
