International woman wrestler Geeta Phogat has appointed as the Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP) of which state

(A) ** Haryana

(B) Odisha

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CHANDIGARH: Haryana government appointed international woman wrestler Geeta Phogat as a Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP) in Haryana Police . A proposal of the home department was approved at a meeting of the state cabinet on Tuesday presided over by chief minister Manohar Lal Khattar .

Concept note-2: -Phogat is a graduate and a sportsperson of international repute. She is also a gold medallist of the 2010 Commonwealth Games in Delhi. After 9 months of working with the Haryana Police, Geeta Phogat shared her first picture in her uniform.

Concept note-3: -Geeta Phogat, the star wrestler from Haryana, turns 34 on December 15. She is India’s first freestyle wrestler who won India’s first gold medal in wrestling at the Commonwealth Games in 2010.

Concept note-4: -Who is Vinesh Phogat? Vinesh Phogat is an Indian wrestler belonging to the famed Phogat family of Haryana. Vinesh, like her sisters Geeta and Babita, was trained by her father, Mahavir Singh Phogat, to become a wrestler.
