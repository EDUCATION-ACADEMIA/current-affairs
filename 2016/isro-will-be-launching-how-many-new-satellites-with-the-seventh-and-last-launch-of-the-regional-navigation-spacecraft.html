ISRO will be launching how many new satellites with the seventh and last launch of the regional navigation spacecraft

(A) 20

(B) ** 21

(C) 22

(D) 23

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IRNSS-1G is the seventh of the seven satellites constituting the space segment of the Indian Regional Navigation Satellite System .

Concept note-2: -Recently, PSLV-C54 was successfully launched EOS-06 satellite on 26th November 2022 along with Eight Nano-satellites including INDIA-BHUTAN SAT (INS-2B).

Concept note-3: -ISRO’s work horse PSLV-C54 has successfully launched EOS-06 satellite along with Eight Nano-satellites into two different SSPOs. The mission was accomplished from Satish Dhawan Space Centre SHAR on 26th November 2022.

Concept note-4: -2022 has witnessed the maximum number of orbital and sub-orbital launches. 149 out of 155 launches were successful in 2022. According to United Nations Office for Outer Space Affairs (UNOOSA), 8261 individual satellites are orbiting the Earth now, out of which 4, 852 satellites are active.
