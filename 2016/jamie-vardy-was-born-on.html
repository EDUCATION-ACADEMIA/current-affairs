Jamie Vardy was born on

(A) ** 11 January 1987

(B) April 17, 1989

(C) October 11, 1982

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jamie Richard Vardy (né Gill; born 11 January 1987) is an English professional footballer who plays as a striker for Premier League club Leicester City.

Concept note-2: -Jamie Vardy signed a 2 year / £14, 560, 000 contract with the Leicester City, including an annual average salary of £7, 280, 000. In 2022, Vardy will earn a base salary of £7, 280, 000, while carrying a cap hit of £7, 280, 000.

Concept note-3: -He started his footballing career at the age of sixteen, playing for Stockbridge Park Steels youth system. On his debut for Halifax Town in 2010, Vardy scored the winning goal against Buxton. He was their top scorer for the season, with 25 goals in 37 appearances.
