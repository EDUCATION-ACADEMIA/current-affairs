Japanese PM Shinzo Abe has won public support for his ruling coalition in what is the world’s _________ largest economy:

(A) First

(B) Second

(C) ** Third

(D) Fourth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abe proposed a three-pronged economic strategy-quickly dubbed “Abenomics” by pundits-featuring aggressive monetary easing, flexible fiscal policy to bolster growth while containing debt, and deregulation and other structural reforms. Abenomics made sense as a strategy but largely failed in implementation.

Concept note-2: -As Japan’s longest-serving prime minister, Mr Abe had shared a special bond with India. He was given the Padma Vibhushan–India’s second-highest civilian award-in 2021.
