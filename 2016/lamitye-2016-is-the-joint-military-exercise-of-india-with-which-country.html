LAMITYE 2016, is the joint military exercise of India with which country

(A) Indonesia

(B) Japan

(C) ** Seychelles

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: ‘Lamitye’ is a joint military exercise between India and Seychelles. In the local dialect ‘Creole’ it means friendship. Its been conducted since 2001 with the aim of enhancing military cooperation and interoperability.

Concept note-2: -India-Seychelles Joint Military Exercise The Lamitye military alliance was organised a couple of times between the Indian army and Seychelles army with the key motive of improving military collaboration and interoperability among the two nations. “Lamitye” is commonly defined as friendship.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Seychelles. The 9th Joint Military Exercise LAMITIYE-2022 between the Indian Army and Seychelles Defence Forces (SDF) is being conducted from 22-31 March 2022. It is being conducted at Seychelles Defence Academy (SDA), Seychelles.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Thailand.

Concept note-5: -Exercise Yudh Abhyas is conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, Tactics, Techniques and Procedures between the Armies of the two nations. The previous edition of the exercise was conducted at Joint Base Elmendorf Richardson, Alaska (USA) in October 2021.
