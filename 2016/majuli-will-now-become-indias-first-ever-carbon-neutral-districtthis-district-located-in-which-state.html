Majuli will now become India’s first ever Carbon neutral district.This district located in which state

(A) Jammu and Kashmir

(B) Punjab

(C) ** Assam

(D) Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is a major step towards achieving the Glasgow goal, says PM Modi. Palli village in Jammu’s Samba district has become the country’s first panchayat to become carbon neutral, fully powered by solar energy and with all its records digitised and saturation of benefits of all the Central schemes.

Concept note-2: -Kerala Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan on Saturday declared a seed farm, located in Aluva here, as the first carbon neutral farm in the country.

Concept note-3: -Kerala Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan declared a seed farm, located in Aluva, as the first carbon neutral farm in the country.

Concept note-4: -Action for Climate Resilient Development in Majuli’ (SACReD, Majuli) to make river island Majuli country’s first ever Carbon Neutral district by 2020.
