Mondelez has inaugurated seventh plant in which country

(A) US

(B) China

(C) ** India

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mondelçz India, part of Mondelçz International Inc which makes Cadbury chocolates, inaugurated its plant today at Sri City in Andhra Pradesh. This is its seventh plant in the country. The investment for phase 1, which was inaugurated today, was $190 million.

Concept note-2: -DEERFIELD, Ill., Dec. 2, 2013 /PRNewswire/–Mondelez International announced plans to invest $190 million in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh to establish the company’s largest manufacturing plant in Asia Pacific.

Concept note-3: -Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister N. Chandrababu Naidu (right), Mondelez India MD Chandramouli Venkatesan (left) and Mondelez International executive vice-president and Asia Pacific president Maurizio Brusadelli during the inauguration of its largest manufacturing plant in Asia Pacific at Sri City in AP on Monday.

Concept note-4: -Mondelez India first entered the Indian biscuits category in 2011 with the launch of Oreo. Its turnover for 2021-22, as per Business Standard, was Rs 9, 296 crore in 2021-22.
