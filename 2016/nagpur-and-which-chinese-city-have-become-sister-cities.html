Nagpur and which Chinese city have become sister cities

(A) Bozhou

(B) ** Jinan

(C) Hangzhou

(D) Inner Mongolia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nagpur: City and China’s Jinan city have become sister cities for exchange of ideas and technology.

Concept note-2: -A sister city or a twin town relationship is a form of legal or social agreement between two geographically and politically distinct localities for the purpose of promoting cultural and commercial ties.

Concept note-3: -The ‘Sister City Square’ will comprise of an open area installed with a globe model marked with the 15 sister cities of Mumbai, i.e. Los Angeles, St Petersburg (Russia), Stuttgart, Yokohama, Honolulu, New York City, Zagreb, Barcelona, Bussan, Port of Odessa, Jakarta, Nadi, Antananarivo and Shanghai.

Concept note-4: -Sister City In diplomacy, this concept is seen as a way to enhance strategic ties between the two nations, both cultural as well as economic. In 2013, India signed the sister city agreements with China–the pairs were Delhi-Beijing, Bengaluru-Chengdu and Kolkata-Kunming.

Concept note-5: -Hyderabad-Secunderabad is called twin cities. Hyderabad is the capital of Telangana. Hyderabad is known as “City of Pearls".
