Name of the web portal which is launched by Union Tourism Ministry

(A) NOPAS

(B) NOASP

(C) ** NOAPS

(D) NAPSO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Utsav Portal website, a digital initiative launched by the Ministry of Tourism, aims to showcase all the events, festivals and live darshans across India to promote different regions of the country as popular tourist destinations worldwide.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Swadesh Darshan. It is the flagship scheme of the Ministry of Tourism for the development of tourism infrastructure in the country.

Concept note-3: -Hon’ble Minister of Tourism, Culture & DoNER Shri G Kishan Reddy briefed the media on the first day of the International…

Concept note-4: -Additional Information Union Minister of Tourism G. Kishan Reddy launched the E-marketplace platform on 8 March 2022. It was launched in an event organised at Indian Institute of Tourism and Travel Management (IITTM), Gwalior.

Concept note-5: -Swadesh Darshan Scheme is a Central Sector scheme launched in 2014-15 by the Ministry of Tourism and Culture, Government of India for the integrated development of theme-based tourist circuits. The scheme aims to promote, develop and harness the potential of tourism in India.
