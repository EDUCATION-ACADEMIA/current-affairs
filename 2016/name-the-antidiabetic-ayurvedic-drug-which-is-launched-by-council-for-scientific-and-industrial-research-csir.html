Name the antidiabetic ayurvedic drug which is launched by Council for Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR)

(A) BGR-31

(B) BGR-32

(C) BGR-33

(D) ** BGR-34

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Council for Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) launched the Blood Glucose Regulator (BGR)-34 which is India’s first anti-diabetic ayurvedic drug for Diabetes.

Concept note-2: -Neem and Madhunadhini/Gudmar: They are wonderful, bitter herbs that help reduce sugar levels. Ashwagandha: It helps reduce stress, fatigue, improve immunity, and insulin sensitivity at the same time.

Concept note-3: -Ayurvedic medicine is effective in blood sugar control in patients with type 2 diabetes, study finds. A new study, led by experts at the University of Nottingham, has found that several traditional medicines commonly used in South Asia, are effective in maintaining blood sugar levels in patients with type 2 diabetes.
