Name the campaign, that can send their Diwali greetings and messages to the soldiers to boost their morale.

(A) Diwali @ Soldiers

(B) ** #Sandesh2Soldiers

(C) #happy2Soldiers

(D) wishtoSoldiers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: On October 22, Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched a public campaign, Sandesh2Soldiers, urging the people to be a part of it. The prime minister prodded people to express their gratitude to soldiers guarding the borders through the campaign by sending them personalised messages on the occasion of Diwali.

Concept note-2: -Your Bravery is incomparable nor matchable, we wish you happy Diwali and pray for you safety home return. Wishing a Happy Deepavali and Prosperous New Year to our soldiers. Thanks for sacrificing your happiness and comforts for our safety – From Our Nation, I wish You & Salute on this Diwali.

Concept note-3: -Earlier on Monday, Modi landed in Kargil to celebrate Diwali with jawans. Addressing soldiers, he said: “For me, all of you have been my family for years now. It is a privilege to spend Diwali with our brave jawans in Kargil."

Concept note-4: -My Dear Sir, on the occasion of Deepavali, I wish you all your wishes come true and you live all your dreams in the coming year – Wishing you and your family a wonderful and cheerful Diwali. Dearest Boss, Here is wishing this Diwali illuminate your life with lots of joy, happiness and success!
