Name the co founder of housing.com who quit on March 10, 2016

(A) Rahul Yadav

(B) Ashok Sharma

(C) ** Advitiya Sharma

(D) Rahul Varman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Advitiya Sharma’s resignation came in March 2016. He was CMO at Housing. During his exit, he wrote in a mail to employees, “the toughest phase in our history was the last 9 months.” But a month later, he bounced back and launched an edtech startup GeniusTeacher to reimagine how education is delivered and consumed.

Concept note-2: -“A 100x company needs 100x team!” Rahul Yadav, who co-founded real estate portal Housing.com, posted on Facebook in December, announcing an investment in his second start-up, Intelligent Interfaces, by Indian cricketer Yuvraj Singh.

Concept note-3: -About Us | Housing. Founded in 2012 and acquired by REA India in 2017, Housing.com is India’s most innovative real estate advertising platform for homeowners, landlords, developers, and real estate brokers.
