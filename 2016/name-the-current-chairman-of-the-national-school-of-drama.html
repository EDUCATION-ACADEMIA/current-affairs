Name the current chairman of the National School of Drama

(A) Waman Kendre

(B) Girish Karnad

(C) Mahesh Dattani

(D) ** Ratan Thiyam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ramesh Chandra Gaur is presently Director, National School of Drama (NSD), an autonomous institute under Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India. He has assumed his office in NSD w.e.f. 15.03. 2022 (A/N).

Concept note-2: -National School of Drama (NSD), educational institution in New Delhi founded in 1959 for the study of theatre and providing training in acting, stagecraft, and related subjects. It is considered the foremost school of its kind in India.

Concept note-3: -Now at present, NSD is running 4 centers under consolidation of outreach program, which are in Gangtok, Bengaluru, Agartala and Varanasi, in addition to Delhi campus.

Concept note-4: -National Public School (or NPS-RNR or NPS-R) is a school located in Bangalore, India, established in 1959 by K. P. Gopalkrishna. The campus, which consists of four buildings, is located on Chord Road, 5th Block, Rajajinagar.
