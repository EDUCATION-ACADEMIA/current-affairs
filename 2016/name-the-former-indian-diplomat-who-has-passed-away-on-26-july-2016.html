Name the Former Indian diplomat who has passed away on 26 July 2016

(A) Neelabh Ashk

(B) David Goodall

(C) Bijoya Chaudhuri

(D) ** Arundhati Ghose

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Arundhati Ghose (25 November 1939 – 25 July 2016) was an Indian diplomat. She was Permanent Representative of India to the UN Offices in Geneva and was head of the Indian delegation that participated in the Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty (CTBT) negotiations at the Conference on Disarmament in Geneva in 1996.

Concept note-2: -Vinay Mohan Kwatra is the 34th and the current Foreign Secretary.

Concept note-3: -India’s global footprint and size of IFS According to the Ministry of External Affairs, India’s sanctioned IFS cadre strength is 850, as against 6, 500 for the IAS and 4, 843 for the IPS, as of 2017.
