Name the Gujarat CM, who has submitted her resignation recently

(A) Ganpatbhai Vasava

(B) Nitinbhai Patel

(C) ** Anandiben Patel

(D) Bhupendrasinh Chudasama

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current chief minister is Bhupendrabhai Patel of the BJP. He was elected for the post following the resignation of then incumbent Vijay Rupani, who was in the office since 7 August 2016.

Concept note-2: -Shri Bhupendrabhai Patel was sworn-in as the Chief Minister of Gujarat for the first time on 13th September, 2021. Born on July 15, 1962, in Ahmedabad, Shri Patel holds a Diploma in Civil Engineering from Government Polytechnic, Ahmedabad.

Concept note-3: -In the 2022 Gujarat Legislative Assembly Elections, the Bharatiya Janata Party won a record-breaking 156 of the total 182 seats, with the party forming the state government for the 7th consecutive time. On 12 December 2022, Patel took oath as the Chief Minister of Gujarat, for the second time with this massive win.
