Name the head of Sant Nirankari Mission who has passed away in a road accident in Canada

(A) Karpal Singh

(B) ** Baba Hardev Singh

(C) Gobind Singh

(D) W Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Baba Hardev Singh : This is how Nirankari sect guru died in car crash in Canada (Crash pic inside)

Concept note-2: -Sant Nirankari Mission is a Nirankari spiritual organisation based in Delhi, India. It was founded in 1929 by Buta Singh. Mata Sudiksha, the daughter of Satguru Baba Hardev Singh, is the sixth spiritual head of the Mission since 17 July 2018.

Concept note-3: -He was 62. According to reports, Baba Hardev Singh was travelling with his two son in law Sandeep Khinda and Avneet Setya in Cadillac SUV from New York to Canada at around 5:00 AM Indian Standard Time (IST) when their car crashed.

Concept note-4: -Some Lesser Known Facts About Mata Savinder Hardev Though she was enrolled in Daulat Ram College, Delhi for higher education, she left her studies midway and got married to Baba Hardev Singh on the occasion of 28th Annual Nirankari Sant Samagam in Delhi on 14 November 1975.

Concept note-5: -Today, it has nearly 2, 000 centres and lakhs of followers across the world. In 1980, then sect chief Satguru Gurbachan Singh was assassinated by a Sikh extremist at the Foundation’s headquarters in Delhi. After his death, Singh’s son Hardev Singh was nominated as the fourth satguru.
