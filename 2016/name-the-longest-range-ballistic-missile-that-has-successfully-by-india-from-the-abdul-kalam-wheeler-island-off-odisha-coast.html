Name the Longest Range Ballistic Missile, that has successfully by India from the Abdul Kalam Wheeler Island off Odisha coast.

(A) Prithv-2

(B) Agni-4

(C) ** Agni-5

(D) Prithv-6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 19 April 2012 at 08.05 am, the Agni V was successfully test-fired by DRDO from Wheeler Island off the coast of Orissa.

Concept note-2: -Abdul Kalam Island, formerly known as Wheeler Island, is an island off the coast of Odisha, India, approximately 150 kilometres (93 mi) east of the state capital Bhubaneswar.

Concept note-3: -Agni-V. The Agni-V is an Indian intercontinental ballistic missile (ICBM) with a range of over 5, 000 km.

Concept note-4: -The 20-metre-long, two-stage ballistic missile has a strike range of 2, 000 km To 3000 km. It has a launch weight of 17 tonnes and can carry a payload of 1, 000 kg.
