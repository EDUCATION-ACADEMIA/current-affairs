Name the Marathi critic and litterateur who has died on 27 May 2016

(A) Mangesh Padgaonkar

(B) ** R G Jadhav

(C) Shanta Shelke

(D) Arun Kolatkar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vishnu Vaman Shirwadkar, popularly known by his pen name, Kusumagraj, was an eminent Marathi poet, playwright, novelist and short-story writer, apart from being a humanist, who wrote of freedom, justice, and emancipation of the deprived.

Concept note-2: -Upon being adopted somewhat late in life in 1930s, his name was changed to Vishnu Waman Shirwadkar. He later adopted the sobriquet ‘Kusumagraj’. He pursued his primary education in Pimpalgaon and high school education in the New English School of Nashik, which is now called J.S.

Concept note-3: -Vishnu Vaman Shirwadkar, popularly known by his pen name ‘Kusumagraj‘, was an eminent Marathi poet, playwright, novelist, short story writer and a freedom writer.
