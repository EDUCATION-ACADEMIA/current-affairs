Name the newly elected MLA in Tamil Nadu who passed away on 25 May 2016

(A) Senthur Pandian

(B) Kader Basha

(C) ** S.M.Seenivel

(D) P. Krishna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -S. M. Seenivel (died 25 May 2016) was an Indian politician of the All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (AIADMK) party.

Concept note-2: -AIADMK under J. Jayalalithaa won the elections and became the first ruling party to be re-elected in Tamil Nadu since 1984, though with a simple majority.

Concept note-3: -The Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly is the unicameral legislature of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It has a strength of 234 members of whom are democratically elected using the First-past-the-post system.
