Name the Novel, that is written by Lakshmi Saravana Kumar and he won the Sahitya Akademi’s Yuva Puraskar award 2016 for this novel

(A) One Indian Girl

(B) Uppu Naaikal

(C) ** Kaanakan

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -J-K’s Shaista Khan wins ‘Sahitya Akademi Yuva Puraskar’ in 2022, becomes 9th Kashmiri to bag award.

Concept note-2: -Suravaram Pratap Reddy was the first winner of this award.

Concept note-3: -Mumbai: The ‘Sahitya Akademi Award’ for 2022 will be given to Anuradha Roy for English Literature, Badri Narayan for Hindi Literature and Praveen Bandekar for Marathi Literature.

Concept note-4: -Yuva Gaurav Award is a literary honour conferred by Gujarat Sahitya Akademi, Government of Gujarat. Established in 2007, the award recognizes and promotes young Gujarati authors. The award comprises a certificate, shawl and a cash prize of ₹ 50, 000.
