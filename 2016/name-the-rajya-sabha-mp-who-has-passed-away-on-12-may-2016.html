Name the Rajya Sabha MP who has passed away on 12 May 2016

(A) ** Praveen Rashtrapal

(B) Khekiho Zhimomi

(C) Balram Jakhar

(D) Sheila Kaul

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Praveen Rashtrapal, a politician from Indian National Congress party, was a Member of the Parliament of India representing Gujarat in the Rajya Sabha, the upper house of the Parliament. He died on 12 May 2016.

Concept note-2: -A Member of Parliament in the Rajya Sabha (abbreviated: MP) is the representative of the Indian states to the one of the two houses of the Parliament of India (Rajya Sabha).

Concept note-3: -The Rajya Sabha or Council of States is the Upper House of the Parliament of India. Membership is limited to 250 members, and the present Rajya Sabha has 245 members.

Concept note-4: -Ajay Sancheti (born 22 October 1965) is Member of Rajya Sabha belongs to Bharatiya Janata Party. He is a Member of Parliament, representing Maharashtra in the Rajya Sabha the upper house of Indian Parliament.

Concept note-5: -The Rajya Sabha (Council of States) is the upper house of the Parliament of India. As of February 2020, 252 BJP leaders have been a member of the Rajya Sabha, out of which 82 are sitting members.
