Name the renowned Educationalist who has passed away on 18 June 2016

(A) James Franco

(B) ** Jeppiaar

(C) Chris Cooper

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jeppiaar (11 June 1931 – 18 June 2016), also known as J. P. R., was an Indian politician, educationist, and industrialist. He was born in Muttom near Nagercoil, Travancore Cochin Presidency, now the Kanyakumari District, Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-2: -Later entered in the politics and got the trust from former Chief Minister M.G. Ramachandran. He is considered as the man of success with wide ideology. JEPPIAAR’s father was Jesuadimai and mother Panimalar Ammal.

Concept note-3: -Dr. Jeppiaar, the Founder Chancellor of Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology is a self-made man.
