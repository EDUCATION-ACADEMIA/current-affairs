Name the Renowned Pink Panther star who has passed away on 24 May 2016

(A) Martin Miller

(B) Roger Rees

(C) ** Burt Kwouk

(D) Peter Sellers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The franchise began with the release of the classic film The Pink Panther in 1963. The role of Clouseau was originated by and is most closely associated with Peter Sellers.

Concept note-2: -Burt Kwouk(1930-2016) Burt Kwouk was a British actor, who was best known for his role as Cato in the Pink Panther films, and for playing Mr Ling in the third James Bond film, Goldfinger.

Concept note-3: -He and the animator Friz Freleng created the cool feline character he called “the James Bond of the animation world.” The character’s first cartoon won an Oscar.
