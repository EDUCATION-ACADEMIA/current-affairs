Name the South Africa Bowler banned from International Cricket Council on 25 February 2016

(A) Smith

(B) Jacques Kallis

(C) ** Aaron Phangiso

(D) David morkel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 1970, the ICC banned South Africa from participating in internationally recognised cricket.

Concept note-2: -South Africa was suspended by the ICC in 1970 following a resolution against the country’s government’s apartheid policy. The country returned to international cricket after a 21-year gap on November 10, 1991, playing versus India at Calcutta.

Concept note-3: -Dale Willem Steyn (/ˈsteɪn/; born 27 June 1983) is a South African former professional cricketer who played for the South African Cricket Team. He is often regarded as one of the greatest fast bowlers of all time and the greatest Test bowler of his generation.

Concept note-4: -Rilee Roscoe Rossouw (born 9 October 1989) is a South African cricketer who played for South Africa between 2014 and 2016, before making a return to international cricket in July 2022.
