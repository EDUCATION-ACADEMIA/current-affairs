Name the Tennis player who on 20 March 2016 won the women’s singles title at the BNP Paribas Open Tennis titles 2016

(A) V.Azarenka

(B) Serena Williams

(C) ** Victoria Azarenka

(D) Petra Kvitova

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Simona Halep was the defending champion, but lost in the quarterfinals to Serena Williams. Victoria Azarenka won the title, defeating World No. 1 Williams in the final, 6–4, 6–4.

Concept note-2: -Two-time defending champion Novak Djokovic defeated Milos Raonic in the final, 6–2, 6–0 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2016 Indian Wells Masters. It was his record-equaling fifth Indian Wells title.

Concept note-3: -Angelique Kerber defeated the defending champion Serena Williams in the final, 6–4, 3–6, 6–4 to win the women’s singles tennis title at the 2016 Australian Open. It was her first major title.

Concept note-4: -The BNP Paribas Open is the largest two-week combined event outside of the four Grand Slams and the most-attended WTA 1000 and ATP World Tour Masters 1000 tennis tournament in the world.
