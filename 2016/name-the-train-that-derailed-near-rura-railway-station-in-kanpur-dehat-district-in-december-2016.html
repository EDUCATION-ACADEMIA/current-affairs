Name the train that derailed near Rura railway station in Kanpur Dehat district in December 2016

(A) Rajdhani Express

(B) Garib Rath Express

(C) Howrah gaya Express

(D) ** Sealdah Ajmer Express

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -13 coaches of the Ajmer-Sealdah express train derailed near Rura railway station in Kanpur’s Dehat district. The accident occurred at around 6 am near Rura railway station.

Concept note-2: -A) Kanpur Dehat district doesn’t have a railway station of its own, however, the nearest station to Kanpur Dehat district is the Chaunrah station with station code CNH.

Concept note-3: -There are 15 weekly trains running from Kanpur Central. There are no trains originating, 14 trains passing and 1 trains terminating at Kanpur Central Railway station.

Concept note-4: -Kanpur Central (formerly known as Cawnpore North Barracks, station code: CNB) is a central and junction railway station in the city of Kanpur and is one of the five central Indian railway stations.
