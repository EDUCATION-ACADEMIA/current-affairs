Name the web portal that is an online system for redressal of service related grievance of serving and former Railway employees

(A) Pavan

(B) Serving Employees

(C) ** Nivaran

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nivaran Portal is the Platform for Resolution of Service Related Grievances of Serving & Former Railway Employees.

Concept note-2: -Nivaran portal which was launched on 30.6. 2016 by Hon’ble MR has been steadily stabilising and around 400 grievances have been lodged on it by serving and former Railway employees.

Concept note-3: -’NIVARAN–the brainchild of Shri Suresh Prabhakar Prabhu was born two months ago when the Minister tweeted about his directions to the Railway Administration to develop an online portal for grievance redressal.

Concept note-4: -The passengers can also file their complaints and suggestions on the web page http://www.coms.indianrailways.gov.in/. The passengers can also SMS their complaints on mobile number 9717630982.

Concept note-5: -Procedure for Online Complaint Registration Step 1: The applicant needs to access the E-Filing website of Income Tax Department. Step 2: Click on “e-Nivaran” tab, which is present on the homepage of the portal. Step 3: Select “Submit Grievance”, the following radio buttons are displayed.
