Name the website that track flights, register complaints to offer people a convenient and hassle-free air travel experience.

(A) AirService

(B) AirQuery

(C) ** AirSewa

(D) AirCustomer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -AirSewa enables travelers to submit grievances and seek information on air travel in India. It is an initiative by MoCA, Govt. of India. Airport Information. All the services and facilities including assistance to disabled or unaccompanied minor etc available at various airports in India.

Concept note-2: -You can file your grievance online with the Central Government Ministries, Departments and various State Government Departments. This service is provided by the Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances.

Concept note-3: -Digi Yatra is a voluntary facility for passengers that provides a seamless and hassle-free experience at the airport. This policy eliminates the need for central storage of passenger’s Personally Identifiable Information (PII) data, as all data is encrypted and stored in the passenger’s smartphone.

Concept note-4: -Currently available for domestic departures, DigiYatra uses facial recognition technology to simplify the terminal entry and security clearance at the airport. It is a decentralised mobile-based ID storage platform where you can digitally save your ID and travel documents.
