NATO launched Anaconda-16 exercise in which country

(A) Russia

(B) US

(C) ** Poland

(D) Argentina

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) members and partners on 6 June 2016 launched Anaconda-16 Exercise, the biggest ever joint military exercise in Poland.

Concept note-2: -AN16 is a Polish national exercise that seeks to train, exercise and integrate Polish national command and force structures into an allied, joint and multinational environment.

Concept note-3: -More than 30, 000 soldiers from NATO and partner nations took part in exercise Cold Response in Norway in the winter of 2022.

Concept note-4: -India and Japan conducted a joint military exercise titled ‘Dharma Guardian 2022’ at Maratha Light Infantry Regimental Centre in Belgaum, Karnataka. It was held from 27th February to 10th March 2022. It covers platoon-level joint training on operations in the jungle and semi-urban and urban terrain.

Concept note-5: -The exercise ‘Veer Guardian-2023’ involving the Indian Air Force (IAF) and Japan Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) will be held at Japan’s Hyakuri air base. The IAF said on Saturday that its deployment at the exercise will include four Su-30 MKI jets, two C-17 aircraft and one IL-78 plane.
