NATO Summit 2016 concluded in which country

(A) ** Poland

(B) Brazil

(C) Canada

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On July 8-9, 2016, President Obama and the heads of state and government of our 27 NATO allies, plus new observer state Montenegro, will gather in Warsaw, Poland, for a pivotal NATO Summit, the 27th since the signing of the Washington Treaty that established the Alliance in 1949.

Concept note-2: -Lithuania is a vital NATO Ally. As host to a multinational battlegroup, Lithuania helps to provide strong deterrence and defence for the whole Alliance. I look forward to a successful Summit in Vilnius in July 2023.

Concept note-3: -At present, NATO has 30 members. In 1949, there were 12 founding members of the Alliance: Belgium, Canada, Denmark, France, Iceland, Italy, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, the United Kingdom and the United States.

Concept note-4: -Leaders also agreed to address the security consequences of climate change and develop NATO’s next Strategic Concept in time for the next summit in 2022. Three principal issues: reinforcing the Alliance’s deterrence and defence, stepping up the fight against terrorism and fairer burden-sharing between NATO members.
