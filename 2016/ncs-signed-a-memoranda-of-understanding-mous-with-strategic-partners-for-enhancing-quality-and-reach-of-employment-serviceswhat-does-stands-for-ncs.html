NCS signed a Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) with strategic partners for enhancing quality and reach of employment services.What does stands for NCS

(A) ** National Career Service

(B) National Correct Service

(C) National Career Supervising

(D) National Course Service

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NCS|About National Career Service-About National Career Service Scheme, its Key benefits and services. A. A.

Concept note-2: -Whenever, any employer shortlists your profile against a particular job posting, you will get job alerts through SMS on the mobile number with which you have registered on the portal. Alternatively, you may contact us on NCS Toll Free Helpline 1800-425-1514 and we will connect you with the employer.

Concept note-3: -Free registration of skill providers. Access to large candidate database from all over the country. Customized alerts for the registered users through emails/sms. Availability of skill training seekers and employers.

Concept note-4: -You’ll learn how to be a better communicator and your leadership and interpersonal skills will improve. All of these are vital in the workplace. And when employers ask you what makes you stand out, then you always have NCS to fall back on. After all, not everybody does something like NCS.
