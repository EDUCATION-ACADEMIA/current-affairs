Nepal is all set to hold its first-ever military exercise with which country

(A) India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Russia

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 16th edition of Indo-Nepal joint training exercise “Surya Kiran-XVI” between India and Nepal will be conducted at Nepal Army Battle School, Saljhandi (Nepal), from December 16–29, 2022, read the Ministry of Defence press release.

Concept note-2: -Exercise “SURYA KIRAN” is conducted annually between India and Nepal with the aim to enhance interoperability in jungle warfare & counter terrorism operations in mountainous terrain and HADR under UN mandate.

Concept note-3: -Last year, the 15th India-Nepal combined battalion-level military training exercise ‘Surya Kiran’ took place in Pithoragarh, Uttarakhand.

Concept note-4: -“SURYA KIRAN-XVI” will be conducted from 16th to 29th December 2022. Exercise “SURYA KIRAN” is conducted annually between India and Nepal to enhance interoperability in jungle warfare & counter-terrorism operations in mountainous terrain and HADR under UN mandate.

Concept note-5: -The ‘Surya Kiran’ exercise is conducted annually in Nepal and India. The 15th edition of the joint exercise was held in Pithoragarh, India.
