NMCC Stand for

(A) National Mortgage Complaint Center

(B) National Moot Court Competition

(C) New Millenium Cyanide Christ

(D) ** National Manufacturing Competitiveness Council

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Manufacturing Competitiveness Council (NMCC) has been set up by the Government to provide a continuing forum for policy dialogue to energise and sustain the growth of manufacturing industries in India.

Concept note-2: -National Manufacturing Competitiveness Council The main objective of NMCC is to provide a continuing forum for policy dialogue and to energize and sustain the growth of manufacturing industries.

Concept note-3: -National Manufacturing Competitiveness Council’ (NMCC) had been instituted for proper policy interventions by the government to enhance productivity and augment the rate of growth in the manufacturing industry. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-4: -The NMCC was established in 2004 as a part of the Common Minimum Programme (CMP) of erstwhile government to provide a continuing forum for policy dialogue to energize and sustain the growth of manufacturing industry.
