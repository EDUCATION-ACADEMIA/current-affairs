North East Democratic Alliance in news was formed by which of the following political party

(A) Indian National Congress (INC)

(B) Communist Party of India (CPI)

(C) Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP)

(D) ** Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The North-East Democratic Alliance or NEDA is a political coalition that was formed on May 24, 2016, by Bharatiya Janata Party.

Concept note-2: -The National Democratic Alliance (NDA) (IAST: Rāṣṭrīya Loktāntrik Gaṭhabandhan) is a centre-right to right-wing conservative Indian political alliance led by the right-wing Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).

Concept note-3: -In May 2016, after the Bharatiya Janata Party led National Democratic Alliance formed its first government in Assam, and formed a new alliance called the North-East Democratic Alliance (NEDA) with Himanta Biswa Sarma as its convener.

Concept note-4: -In Meghalaya total 13 thirteen Political Parties including 04 of National status are contesting in the election battleground of 60 member State Assembly.
