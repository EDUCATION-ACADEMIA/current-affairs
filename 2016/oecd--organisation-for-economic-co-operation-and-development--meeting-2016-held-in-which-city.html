OECD ( Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development ) meeting 2016 held in which city

(A) Singapore

(B) Geneva

(C) ** Paris

(D) New York

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Final Declaration of the OECD Meeting of Agriculture Ministers held in Paris on 7-8 April 2016-OECD.

Concept note-2: -The OECD is represented outside of Paris by Centres in Berlin, Mexico City, Tokyo, and Washington. The Centres serve as regional contacts for the full range of OECD activities, from the sales of publications, to inquiries from the media, to liaison with governments, parliaments, business, labour and civil society.

Concept note-3: -The OECD is represented outside of Paris by Centres.

Concept note-4: -1 The Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) was established on Dec. 14, 1960, by 18 European nations, plus the United States and Canada. 3 The organization is headquartered in the Chateau de la Muette in Paris, France.

Concept note-5: -The Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) is a unique forum where the governments of 37 democracies with market-based economies collaborate to develop policy standards to promote sustainable economic growth.
