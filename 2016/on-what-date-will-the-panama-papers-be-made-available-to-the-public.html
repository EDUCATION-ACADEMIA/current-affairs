On what date will the Panama papers be made available to the public

(A) ** May 9

(B) May 6

(C) May 4

(D) May 1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: The “Panama Papers” trove of leaked documents revealing the vast extent of global tax evasion will be made available to the public on May 9, the ICIJ confirmed.

Concept note-2: -The Panama Papers were released on April 13, 2016 from an unknown location in Panama.

Concept note-3: -Whistleblower John Doe, who leaked the data that gave rise to the Panama Papers, comes out of a six-year silence to lift the veil on his motivations. He warns against the proliferation of shell companies and their use by authoritarian regimes.

Concept note-4: -The documents were released on April 3, 2016, by the German newspaper Süddeutsche Zeitung (SZ), dubbing them the “Panama Papers.” The document exposed the network of more than 214, 000 tax havens involving people and entities from 200 different nations.

Concept note-5: -Background. On 20 October 2017, an anonymous Reddit user hinted at the existence of the Paradise Papers. Later that month, the International Consortium of Investigative Journalists (ICIJ) approached the offshore law firm Appleby with allegations of wrongdoing.
