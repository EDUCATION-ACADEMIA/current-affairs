Pakistan’s HBL has received a license to open a branch in which country

(A) India

(B) Sri Lanka

(C) ** China

(D) Nepal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HBL has created history by being the first and only bank from Pakistan to have a branch in Beijing and one of the three banks from South Asia and MENA region to offer end-to-end RMB intermediation in China.

Concept note-2: -2021 HBL became the first Pakistani bank to open a branch and serve clients in Beijing, China.

Concept note-3: -Internationally, HBL has a presence in 25 countries, through its branch operations, subsidiaries in Hong Kong and the UK, affiliates in Nepal, Kenya and Kyrgyzstan and representative offices in Iran and China.

Concept note-4: -HBL had been operating its New York branch since 1978, and was one of only three Pakistani banks to have a presence in the United States (the other two being the National Bank of Pakistan and the United Bank Ltd).
