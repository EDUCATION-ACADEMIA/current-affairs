Panagarh Air Force Base is renamed as

(A) ** Air Force Station Arjan Singh

(B) Dilbagh Singh Air Force Station

(C) Air Force Station Ronald Ivelaw-Chapman

(D) Subroto Mukerjee Air Force Station

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Air Force Station Arjan Singh (ICAO: VEPH), formerly Panagarh Airport, is an airport serving the city of Panagarh, in the state of West Bengal in India.

Concept note-2: -To commemorate his contribution to the IAF, Air Force Station Panagarh was renamed as Air Force Station Arjan Singh in 2016.

Concept note-3: -Kalaikunda Air Force Station (ICAO: VEDX) is an Indian Air Force Base in Kharagpur, located in the West Midnapur district of the state of West Bengal. It was the home of No. 18 Squadron IAF, the Flying Bullets.

Concept note-4: -Panagarh Air Force Station is a midsized airport in West Bengal, India. The airport has one runway: 15/33. The ICAO designator of this field is VEPH. Nearby other airfields are Durgapur Steel Plant Airport, Kazi Nazrul Islam Airport, Burnpur Airport, Surichua Air Base and Sido Kanhu Airport.
