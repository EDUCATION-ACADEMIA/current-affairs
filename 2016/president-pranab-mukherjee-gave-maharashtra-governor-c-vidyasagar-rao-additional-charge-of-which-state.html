(A) ** Tamil Nadu

(B) Karnataka

(C) Kerala

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chennamaneni Vidyasagar Rao (born 12 February 1941) is an Indian politician from Telangana, associated with Bharatiya Janata Party. He served as Governor of Maharashtra from 2014 to 2019.

Concept note-2: -Complete answer: Kandukuri Veeresalingam was known as the ‘South India Vidyasagar’.

Concept note-3: -Bhagat Singh Koshyari took oath as the Governor of Maharashtra on 5th September 2019.

Concept note-4: -Bhagat Singh Koshyari is the governor of Maharashtra since September 5, 2019.
