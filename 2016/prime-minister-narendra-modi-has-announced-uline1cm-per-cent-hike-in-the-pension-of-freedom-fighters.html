Prime Minister Narendra Modi has announced _________ per cent hike in the pension of freedom fighters.

(A) 10

(B) ** 20

(C) 30

(D) 40

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Atal Pension Yojana (APY) is very beneficial for people who are working in private sector or organizations where there is no provision of retirement benefits like pension.

Concept note-2: -Freedom fighters also get Rs 26, 000 pension from the Union government under the Swatantrata Sainik Samman Yojana (SSSY).

Concept note-3: -It includes an ex-INA or ex-military person who died while fighting against the British. After death of freedom fighter pensioner, eligible dependents for family pension are spouse/unmarried & unemployed daughters/mother or father.
