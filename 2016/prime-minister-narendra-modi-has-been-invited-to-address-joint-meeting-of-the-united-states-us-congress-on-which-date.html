[SOURCES]

Prime Minister Narendra Modi has been invited to address joint meeting of the United States (US) Congress on which date

(A) June 3

(B) May 23

(C) May 18

(D) ** June 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Narendra Modi, parliamentary leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party, started the first tenure of his prime ministership, after his swearing-in as the 14th Prime Minister of India on 26 May 2014.

Concept note-2: -On January 6, 2021, during the Joint Session of Congress convened to count the electoral votes for the 2020 presidential election victory of Joe Biden, a mob of rioters supporting outgoing President Donald Trump stormed and vandalized the Capitol building.

Concept note-3: -The Speaker of the House of Representatives usually presides over Joint Sessions and Joint Meetings; however, the President of the Senate presides over Joint Sessions where the electoral votes are counted, as required by the Constitution (Article I, section 1; 12th Amendment).

Concept note-4: -The purpose of a Joint Meeting has usually been for Congress to hear an address from an important figure-generally a visiting foreign leader. This practice became a standard part of foreign leaders’ state visits to the United States after 1945.
