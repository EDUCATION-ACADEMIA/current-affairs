Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated a newly built integrated international terminal in which of the following civil airport recently

(A) Bhuj Airport

(B) Pasighat Airport

(C) Silchar Airport

(D) ** Vadodara Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday inaugurated Mopa International Airport in Goa, named after former chief minister and defence minister Manohar Parrikar. The airport’s foundation stone was laid by PM Modi in November 2016. This will be the second airport in Goa, the first one being located at Dabolim.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the newly-designed Terminal-2 of the Kempegowda International Airport (KIA) in Bengaluru on Friday. The terminal has been nicknamed as ‘Terminal in a garden’ due to extensive use of bamboo. The eco-friendly terminal has been built at an estimated cost of Rs 5, 000 crore.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday inaugurated new greenfield international airport in Mopa, Goa. Chief minister Pramod Sawant announced the new airport will be named after former chief minister Manohar Parrikar. Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday inaugurated the Mopa International Airport in Goa.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the newly integrated terminal building of the Maharaja Bir Bikram(MBB) Airport in Agartala today. Tripura will have its first integrated terminal building of MBB showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the state.
