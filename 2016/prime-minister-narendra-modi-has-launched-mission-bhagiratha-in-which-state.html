Prime Minister Narendra Modi has launched Mission Bhagiratha in which state

(A) Andhra Pradesh

(B) Karnataka

(C) Haryana

(D) ** Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bhagiratha is a safe drinking water project for every village in Telangana State, with a budget of ₹43, 791 crores.

Concept note-2: -Mission Bhagiratha is a flagship program of the newly constituted state of Telangana.

Concept note-3: -Telangana government has launched its own packaged drinking water ‘Mission Bhagiratha’. The bottle contains regular piped drinking water and is supplied to all government offices at free of cost.
