Prime Minister Narendra Modi has presented the Ramnath Goenka Awards for Excellence in which field

(A) Science

(B) Cinema/Films

(C) ** Journalism

(D) Architecture

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ramnath Goenka Excellence in Journalism Awards (RNG Awards) are one of the awards in India in the field of journalism.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Zishaan A Latif. Zishaan A Latif has been named the winner of the Ramnath Goenka Award in the Photo Journalism category.

Concept note-3: -The India chapter of the International Press Institute, IPI-India, on Friday conferred the Award for Excellence in Journalism 2022 jointly to The Print and NDTV correspondent Saurabh Shukla.

Concept note-4: -Pulitzer Prizes They honor “excellence in journalism” and “prize original reporting, good writing and, where appropriate, visual storytelling."
