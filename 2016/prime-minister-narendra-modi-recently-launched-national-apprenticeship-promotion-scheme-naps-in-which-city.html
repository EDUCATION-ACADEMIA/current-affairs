Prime Minister Narendra Modi recently launched National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) in which city

(A) Chandigarh

(B) Ranchi

(C) Lucknow

(D) ** Kanpur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When did the National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme come into effect? The National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme was implemented from 1st October 2016. The National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme was implemented from 1st October 2016.

Concept note-2: -Apprenticeship melas are hosted in the country every month, wherein selected individuals receive a monthly stipend in accordance with government criteria for gaining new skills. Apprenticeship is considered the most sustainable model of skill development, and it has been getting a big boost under Skill India Mission.

Concept note-3: -The event will witness the participation from many companies representing various sectors. The participating organizations will have the chance to meet potential apprentices on a single platform and choose applicants on the spot, providing them with an opportunity to strengthen their livelihood and learn new skills.

Concept note-4: -The National Apprenticeship Training Scheme in India is a one-year program for all students. It gives technical qualifications to the youth of the country. The program gives practical knowledge and skills that are required in their field of work.
