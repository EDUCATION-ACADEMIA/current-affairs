PSLV C34 is carrying how many satellites into orbit, marking a first in ISRO’s history

(A) 24

(B) ** 22

(C) 21

(D) 26

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PSLV-C34 was the 36th mission of the PSLV (Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle ) program and 14th mission of PSLV in XL configuration. The PSLV-C34 successfully carried and deployed 20 satellites in the Sun-synchronous orbit.

Concept note-2: -Recently, PSLV-C54 was successfully launched EOS-06 satellite on 26th November 2022 along with Eight Nano-satellites including INDIA-BHUTAN SAT (INS-2B).

Concept note-3: -It has been 44 years, and ISRO has successfully launched almost 150 satellites. After its first satellite launch, ISRO had also made its launch vehicle, through which it launched Rohini in 1979, although this satellite had failed to achieve orbit.
