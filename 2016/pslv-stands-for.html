PSLV stands for

(A) Polar survey landing Vehicle

(B) Polarised source laser viewing

(C) ** Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle

(D) Precise source locating vision

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -GSLV-Mk II is the largest launch vehicle developed by India.

Concept note-2: -The Indian Space Research Organization, or ISRO, developed the PSLV (Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle) and GSLV (Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle) as rocket launch systems for placing satellites into orbit.

Concept note-3: -With this launch Indian Space Research Organisation marked its 100 space missions, with 62 satellites, 37 launch vehicles and 1 Space Capsule Recovery Experiment.

Concept note-4: -The full form of PSLV is Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle. It is an operational launcher from India.
