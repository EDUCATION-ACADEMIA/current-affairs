Puttingal temple is located in which state of India

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Kerala

(C) Uttarakhand

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Srirangam Temple is often listed as the largest functioning Hindu temple in the world. The temple, located in Tamil Nadu, occupies an area of 156 acres (631, 000 m²) with a perimeter of 4, 116m (10, 710 feet), making it the largest temple in India and one of the largest religious complexes in the world.

Concept note-2: -A temple in Hinduism is called a mandir and is a special place where Hindus worship.

Concept note-3: -In the early modern period, Fiji, Guyana, Kenya, Malaysia, Mauritius, Réunion, Myanmar, Seychelles, Singapore, South Africa, Suriname, Tanzania, Trinidad and Tobago and Uganda, have seen many temples being built, as the Indian Diaspora settled across these areas over the past 250–300 years.
