Raghunathpur thermal plant is located in which state of India

(A) Jharkhand

(B) ** West Bengal

(C) Bihar

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Valley Corporation (DVC ) is implementing 2X600 MW Raghunathpur Thermal Power Project, Phase – I, at Purulia District in the state of West Bengal.

Concept note-2: -Raghunathpur is a city and a municipality in Purulia district in the state of West Bengal, India. It is the headquarters of the Raghunathpur subdivision.

Concept note-3: -Hussain Sagar Thermal Power Station is the historic thermal power plant that was located in Hyderabad, Telangana on the banks of Hussain Sagar. It was India’s first thermal power station, opened in 1920 by the erstwhile seventh Nizam of Hyderabad.

Concept note-4: -Vindhyachal Thermal Power Station, Madhya Pradesh. The Vindhyachal Thermal Power Station in the Singrauli district of Madhya Pradesh, with an installed capacity of 4, 760MW, is currently the biggest thermal power plant in India. It is a coal-based power plant owned and operated by NTPC.
