Rani Laxmi Bai award is given by which state

(A) Haryana

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Odisha

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rani Laxmi Bai Award is conducted by the Department of Sports and Youth Affairs under the Government of Haryana. The award is to honor the lifetime contribution of sportswomen in Haryana. Sportswomen of National/ International recognition will be awarded a cash prize of INR 2, 00, 000 and other rewards.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Sports. The award is given to a woman for outstanding sports contribution, which has promoted lifetime sports. There is a provision of giving two lakh rupees in this.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is option 4. Rani Laxmi Bai award is not given by Madhya Pradesh Government.

Concept note-4: -The Laxman award is awarded by the Uttar Pradesh Government. Under this scheme, sports players that are honored with this award are given a citation, a bronze statue of Rani Laxmi Bai/ Laxman, and a cash prize of Rs. 3, 11, 000.

Concept note-5: -Lakshmi Bai was rani (queen) of Jhansi. During the Indian Mutiny of 1857–58, she rapidly organized her troops and assumed charge of the rebels in the Bundelkhand region.
