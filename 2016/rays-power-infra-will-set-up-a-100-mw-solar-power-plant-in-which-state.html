Rays Power Infra will set up a 100 mw solar power plant in which state

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) Odisha

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) ** Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: Solar power service provider Rays Power Infra Pvt Ltd today announced the launch of its 100 MW project in Uttarakhand.

Concept note-2: -Rajasthan has the highest solar power generation potential of any state in the country. The state recently surpassed Karnataka as the leading state in solar installations. As of December 2022, Rajasthan’s operational solar power projects produced roughly 16, 060 MW of solar energy.

Concept note-3: -Hence, the largest solar power plant in India is located at Kamuthi in Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-4: -India’s first solar power plant was developed by Godawari Green Energy Limited in Rajasthan. The 50-MW solar thermal power plant was set up in Naukh village located in the Jaisalmer district of Rajasthan.

Concept note-5: -Solar Power Plant, For Commercial, Capacity: 1-100Mw at Rs 32970/kilowatt in Ahmedabad.
