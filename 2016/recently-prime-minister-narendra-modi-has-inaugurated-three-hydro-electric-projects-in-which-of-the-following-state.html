Recently, Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated three hydro-electric projects in which of the following state

(A) ** Himachal Pradesh

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Karnataka

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The country’s top five hydroelectric power plants hail from the states of Uttarakhand, Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh and Gujarat.

Concept note-2: -Which is the largest hydroelectric power plant in India? The Koyna Hydroelectric Project is the largest hydroelectric power plant in India. It is a complex project with four dams including the largest dam on the Koyna River, Maharashtra hence the name Koyna Hydroelectric Project.

Concept note-3: -Arunachal Pradesh Turns Power Surplus As PM Modi Inaugurated 600 MW Kameng Hydro Power Project. Border state of Arunachal Pradesh has turned power surplus after Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated 600 MW Kameng Hydro Power Station on Saturday (19 November).
