CURRENT AFFAIRS-2016
[SOURCES]

Recently, the Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) was recommended imposition of 3050 crore rupees penalty in which of the following telecom operator

(A) Bharti Airtel

(B) Vodafone

(C) Idea Cellular

(D) ** All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In October 2016, Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (Trai) had recommended imposing a total penalty of ₹3, 050 crore on Airtel, Vodafone and Idea (now merged) for allegedly denying inter-connectivity to Reliance Jio.

Concept note-2: -While TRAI was a regulator and a dispute settlement body, DoT continued making policies for the sector. Expectedly, this led to a conflict between TRAI and the Ministry. There were also concerns about TRAI being able to execute its functions in an independent and impartial manner.

Concept note-3: -Bharti Airtel chief Sunil Mittal said in October the company will opt for the four-year moratorium on spectrum and AGR payments, which will help it conserve a cash outgo of Rs 35, 000-40, 000 crore that would be used for network and other capital expansion programmes.
