Recently which institution has been awarded the EFMD Quality Improvement System (EQUIS) Accreditation by EFMD

(A) ** Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta

(B) Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad

(C) Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad

(D) Indian Institute of Management Indore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’IIM Kozhikode is delighted to receive EQUIS Accreditation, which promises rigorous quality control and international benchmarking.

Concept note-2: -EQUIS is a comprehensive quality review system for business and management schools. Its rigorous peer-review process signals the school’s overall quality, viability and self-improvement commitment to students, employers and academic partners worldwide.

Concept note-3: -EFMD stands for European Foundation for Management Development. It is an international, non-profit organization of both business schools and corporations with nearly 900 member organizations in 86 countries.

Concept note-4: -The MPCA uses the Environmental Quality Information System (EQuIS) to store and manage monitoring data and associated laboratory results from streams, lakes, groundwater, ambient air, soil, sediment, and gas, collected through MPCA programs and partnerships.

Concept note-5: -What does AACSB accreditation mean? Founded in 1916, The Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business is known worldwide as the longest standing and most recognised professional accreditation body in the field of business education at the Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Doctoral level.
