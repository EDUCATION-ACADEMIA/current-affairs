Recently, which of the following has launched by the Union Ministry of Human Resource Development (HRD)

(A) Digital India Hackathon 2015

(B) Make India Hackathon 2017

(C) ** Smart India Hackathon 2017

(D) Development India Hackathon 2016

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is option 3, i.e. National Testing Agency (NTA). Union Ministry of Human Resource Development (HRD) has announced that two of India’s most popular competitive exams, Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) and National Eligibility and Entrance Test (NEET) will be conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA).

Concept note-2: -In 1985, the Rajiv Gandhi government changed its name to the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), and with the public announcement of the newly drafted “National Education Policy 2020” by the Narendra Modi government, the Ministry of Human Resource Development was renamed back to the Ministry of Education.

Concept note-3: -1. University education; Central Universities; Rural Higher Education, Foreign Aid Programme relating to Higher Education, Technical Education Planning and Development of School Education. 2. Institutions of higher learning (other than Universities).

Concept note-4: -The Union Minister of Human Resource Development Ramesh Pokhriyal has launched a Bharat Padhe Online campaign for crowd sourcing of ideas to improve online education ecosystem of India.
