[SOURCES]

Recently which of the following state has redrawn its map by creating 21 new districts.

(A) ** Telangana

(B) Odisha

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -21 new districts were created on 11 October 2016, which lead to 31 districts in Telangana. All districts were divided into minimum 2 to maximum 5 except Hyderabad district district which was untouched. Two new districts, Mulugu and Narayanpet were created on 17 February 2019, taking the total number of districts to 33.

Concept note-2: -It was double bonanza for the people of Telangana as the 29th State not only celebrated Dasara in a grand manner but 21 new districts also became a reality taking the total number to 31.

Concept note-3: -The state of Andhra Pradesh has 26 districts spread across three regions-Uttarandhra, Coastal Andhra and Rayalaseema.
