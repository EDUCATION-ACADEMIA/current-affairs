Recently, which state has announced the retirement age of doctors raised to 65 years

(A) ** Haryana

(B) Odisha

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -LUCKNOW A seven-member committee has been asked to prepare a report on the proposal to increase the retirement age of government doctors in Uttar Pradesh. While the state government wants to increase retirement age of government doctors to 65 years from the present 62 years, the doctors are opposing the idea.

Concept note-2: -The Assam government has raised the retirement age of doctors in government medical colleges of the State from 65 to 70 years.

Concept note-3: -New Delhi: Andhra Pradesh government has good news for its employees. In fact, the state employees have two reasons to celebrate as per the latest 7th pay commission news. The state has decided to increase the age of retirement from 60 to 62 years. Along with that, the salary will also be hiked by 23.39 per cent.
