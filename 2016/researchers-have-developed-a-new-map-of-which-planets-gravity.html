Researchers have developed a new map of which planet’s gravity

(A) Saturn

(B) Venus

(C) Neptune

(D) ** Mars

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A new map of Mars’ gravity made with three NASA spacecraft is the most detailed to date, providing a revealing glimpse into the hidden interior of the Red Planet.

Concept note-2: -Among all the planets in our solar system, Jupiter is the only planet whose gravity is higher than all planets.

Concept note-3: -Gravity anomaly maps (see globe below) show how much the Earth’s actual gravity field differs from the gravity field of a uniform, featureless Earth surface. The anomalies highlight variations in the strength of the gravitational force over the surface of the Earth.

Concept note-4: -The gravity on Jupiter is greater than the gravity on Earth because Jupiter is more massive.
