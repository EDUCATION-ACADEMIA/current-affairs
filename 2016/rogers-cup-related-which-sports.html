Rogers Cup related which sports

(A) Badminton

(B) ** Tennis

(C) Kabaddi

(D) Cricket

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Tennis. Rogers Cup is associated with Tennis.

Concept note-2: -Financial terms of the partnership weren’t released A new chapter begins. Rogers Cup is now the National Bank Open.

Concept note-3: -Ezra Cup. It is a well-known Polo Tournament conducted annually in India. It is also the first-ever polo trophy and the Calcutta Polo Club runs it. This cup was initiated in 1880.

Concept note-4: -The tennis tournament will change its name to accommodate its new lead sponsor. Beginning this year, the tournament will now be titled the National Bank Open presented by Rogers. The sponsorship is set to last for the next decade, the longest in Canadian tennis history.
