Russian consortium led energy giant Rosneft Oil Company has agreed to acquire which indian oil Company for $13 billion

(A) Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd

(B) Hindustan Oil Exploration Company Ltd

(C) Gujarat Gas Co Ltd

(D) ** Essar Oil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai: The nearly $13-billion acquisition of Essar Oil Ltd by a consortium led by Russia’s Rosneft PJSC was completed on Sunday, two people aware of the matter said.

Concept note-2: -Essar, built by brothers Shashi and Ravi Ruia, sold Essar Oil to a group backed by Russian oil major Rosneft for about $13 billion in 2017 as part of efforts to raise funds to settle $25 billion which it owed to Indian banks.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI, Sept 10 (Reuters)-India’s second-biggest private refiner Nayara Energy, part-owned by Russian oil major Rosneft (ROSN.MM), has appointed Prasad K. Panicker as chairman, replacing Tony Fountain, the company said in a statement.

Concept note-4: -The 400, 000-barrel per day (bpd) Omsk plant, 1, 600 km east of Moscow, started operations in 1955 and is Russia’s largest oil refinery.
