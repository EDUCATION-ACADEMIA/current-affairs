Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana launched in which year

(A) 2005

(B) ** 2014

(C) 2015

(D) 2016

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) was launched on 11th October, 2014 with the aim to translate the comprehensive vision of Mahatma Gandhi about an ideal Indian village into a reality, keeping in view the present context.

Concept note-2: -The programme was launched by the Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi on the birth anniversary of Jayaprakash Narayan, on 11 October 2014. The distinct feature of this Yojana is that it is (a) demand driven (b) inspired by society (c) based on people’s participation.

Concept note-3: -The main objectives of SAGY are: To trigger processes which lead to holistic development of the identified Gram Panchayats. To substantially improve the standard of living and quality of life of all sections of the population through. Improved basic amenities.

Concept note-4: -Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY) was formally launched in 18 B.B village in Sri Ganganagar district of Rajasthan on 23 July 2010.

Concept note-5: -SAGY | Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India.
