SAPCC has been launched by Uttarakhand government on 20th June. What does SAPCC stand for

(A) ** State Action Plan of Climate Change

(B) State Action Plan of Change Climate

(C) State Action Planning of Climate Change

(D) Set Action Plan of Climate Change

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Key Successes: Development of institutions and partnerships at both national and international levels – E.g. India-led International Solar Alliance. National Mission on Sustainable Habitat-Adoption of Energy Conservation Building Code, Programs like AMRUT, Smart Cities etc. to promote sustainable urbanization.

Concept note-2: -The National Action Plan on climate change (NAPCC) encompasses eight missions in the specific areas of Solar Energy, Enhanced Energy Efficiency, Sustainable Habitat, Water, Sustaining the Himalayan Eco-system, Strategic Knowledge for Climate Change, Green India, and Sustainable Agriculture.

Concept note-3: -The Government of India launched National Action Plan on Climate Change (NAPCC) on 30thJune, 2008 outlining eight National Missions on climate change. These include: National Solar Mission.
