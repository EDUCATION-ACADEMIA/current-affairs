SBI Card launches premium credit card _________

(A) ** Elite

(B) Plite

(C) Klite

(D) Mlite

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SBI Aurum Credit Card is an invite-only super premium credit card. The card offers all-rounder benefits around rewards, travel, movies, concierge service, etc. Additionally you get to avail memberships from top Entertainment, Dining and Lifestyle brands.

Concept note-2: -In order to be eligible for the premium credit cards, you need to have a relatively high annual income which is above Rs. 50, 000 for most such cards. Will I get a concierge service with premium credit cards?

Concept note-3: -Features. Issuance Charges : Nil. Annual Maintenance Charges : Rs 350/-plus GST. Card Replacement Charges : Rs 300/-plus GST. Get instant SMS alerts for all Cash withdrawal/Purchase transactions/Online transactions made on your SBI Premium.

Concept note-4: -SBI Platinum Card Fees and Charges One-time joining fee for the SBI Platinum Card is Rs. 2, 999. Cardholders are charged an annual fee of Rs. 2, 999 for every membership year.
