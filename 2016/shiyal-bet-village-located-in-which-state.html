Shiyal Bet village located in which state

(A) ** Gujarat

(B) Odisha

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shiyalbet, Jafrabad | District Amreli, Government of Gujarat | India.

Concept note-2: -The Shiyalbet Village located in Jafrabad Taluka, Shiyalbet population in 2023 is estimated to be 6, 217, According to last census in 2011, 5096 People are living in this Village, out of these 2667 are males and 2429 are females. Population of Shiyalbet is 5, 708 inhabitants in 2022.

Concept note-3: -Jafrabad is well known for the fight between Marathas and British at Asseye in 1803, where a small monument carving the name of British Officer Colonel Stevenson, who died during the war, was erected.

Concept note-4: -Jafarabad, or Jafrabad State, was a tributary princely state in India during the British Raj. It was a dependency of the Nawab of Janjira State and located in the Kathiawar Peninsula on the Gujarat coast. The state had formerly been part of the Baroda Agency and later of the Kathiawar Agency of the Bombay Presidency.

Concept note-5: -Jafrabad Amreli Pin Code is 365540. Pin Code is also known as Zip Code or Postal Code. Jafrabad Amreli is located in district Amreli, Gujarat, INDIA.
