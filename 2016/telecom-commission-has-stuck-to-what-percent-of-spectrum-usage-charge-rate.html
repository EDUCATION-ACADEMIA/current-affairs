Telecom Commission has stuck to what percent of spectrum usage charge rate

(A) 4.5 %

(B) 5 %

(C) ** 3 %

(D) 6.3 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Spectrum usage charges given by each operator are computed as a specific percentage of the adjusted gross revenue earned by operators. At present, operators give 3 per cent of their revenues every year as spectrum usage charges.

Concept note-2: -Spectrum Usage Charges (SUC)-An Overview. Spectrum Usage Charges (SUC)are payable by Licensees, in four quarterly instalments during each financial year. The quarterly instalments of SUC dues for the first three quarters of a financial year, are payable within 15 days of the completion of the relevant quarter.
