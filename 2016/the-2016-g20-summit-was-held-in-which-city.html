The 2016 G20 Summit was held in which city

(A) ** Hangzhou

(B) Moscow

(C) London

(D) Washington

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The G20 summit 2016 was held in Hangzhou, China. The theme of the 2016 G20 Summit was ‘Toward an Innovative, Invigorated, Interconnected and Inclusive World Economy. It was the 11th summit since the inception of G20 in 1999. The chair of the summit was Xi Jinping, President of China.

Concept note-2: -The 2016 G20 Hangzhou summit was the eleventh meeting of the Group of Twenty (G20). It was held on 4–5 September 2016 in the city of Hangzhou, Zhejiang. It was the first ever G20 summit to be hosted in China and the second in an Asian country after 2010 G20 Seoul summit was hosted in South Korea.

Concept note-3: -The theme of the 2016 Summit is China, ‘Towards an innovative, invigorated, interconnected and inclusive world economy’.

Concept note-4: -“Indonesia will hold the G20 Presidency in DEC 2022 while India will hold the Presidency a year after, ” said Retno. Indonesia’s presidency commenced on 1 December 2021, following the transfer of presidency from Italian Prime Minister Mario Draghi to Indonesian president Joko Widodo at the closing of the Rome summit.
