The 2016 India International Science festival (IISF) has started in which city

(A) Bangalore

(B) Ahmedabad

(C) Mumbai

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India International Science Festival (IISF-2016) inaugurated in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -The IISF 2022 is the eighth edition since its inception in 2015. The first and second IISF were held in New Delhi, the third in Chennai, the fourth one in Lucknow, fifth in Kolkata, sixth through virtual mode and the last one IISF was held in Goa which witnessed a footfall from over 5 lakh people worldwide.

Concept note-3: -MP: 8th India International Science Festival to be held in Bhopal from Saturday. Bhopal, Jan 20 (PTI) The 8th India International Science Festival-2022 (IISF) will be held from January 21 to 24 at the Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT) in Bhopal in Madhya Pradesh, an official said on Friday.

Concept note-4: -The journey of IISF was started with its inaugural event at Delhi in 2015. Seven editions of IISF have been organized since then in northern, southern and the eastern regions of the country.

Concept note-5: -The First India International Science Festival (IISF) is being organized at IIT Delhi from 4th to 8th December 2015. The event is jointly being organized by the Ministries of Science & Technology and Earth Sciences, Govt.
