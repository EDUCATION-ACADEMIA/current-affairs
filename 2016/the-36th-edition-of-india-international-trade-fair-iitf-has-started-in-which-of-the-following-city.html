The 36th edition of India International Trade Fair (IITF) has started in which of the following city

(A) Chennai

(B) Banglore

(C) Chandigarh

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Over one million general visitors visited the fair. The 36th edition of the popular India International Trade Fair (IITF) was held from 14 to 27 November 2016 at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi. The theme of this fair was Digital India.

Concept note-2: -"IITF offers an ideal platform to showcase Indian products under the ‘Vocal for Local’ campaign, and infuse renewed confidence and vigour in the Indian economy, ” it said. Some 12 countries including Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Bahrain, Belarus, Iran, Nepal, Thailand, Turkey, UAE, and the UK will participate in the fair.

Concept note-3: -i. The partner states of IITF 2022 are Bihar, Jharkhand, and Maharashtra, whereas the focus states are Uttar Pradesh and Kerala.

Concept note-4: -Since its inception in 1980, the Trade Fair Delhi has become a major global event for global business communities, clients, and tourists. Every year, it attracts millions of visitors and thousands of exhibitors.

Concept note-5: -Entry to the fair at Pragati Maidan will close at 2pm. Notice: Complimentary entry to school children for IITF 2022 ongoing at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi will not be allowed on 26.11. 2022 and 27.11. 2022.
