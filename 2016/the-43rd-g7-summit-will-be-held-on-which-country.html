The 43rd G7 Summit will be held on which country

(A) Canada

(B) ** Italy

(C) France

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As the G7 Presidency in 2023, Japan has decided to host the G7 Trade Ministers’ Meeting in Osaka-Sakai, on 28 and 29 October 2023 after hosting the first online meeting on 4 April 2023. In October, the meeting and related events will be held in the host cities of Osaka and Sakai.

Concept note-2: -The 48th G7 summit was held from 26 to 28 June 2022 in Schloss Elmau, Krün, Bavarian Alps, Germany.

Concept note-3: -The G7 is an informal grouping of seven of the world’s advanced economies, including Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the United Kingdom, and the United States, as well as the European Union.
