The 4th Nuclear Security Summit 2016 held in which city

(A) Seoul

(B) The Hague

(C) New York

(D) ** Washington

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Summary: The fourth and final Nuclear Security Summit will be held March 31-April 1, 2016, at the Walter E. Washington Convention Center in Washington, DC. Last year in Berlin, the President formally announced his plan to host a fourth and final Nuclear Security Summit in 2016.

Concept note-2: -On April 1, 2016 local time, the fourth Nuclear Security Summit was held in Washington D.C., capital of the US. President Xi Jinping attended the summit and delivered an important speech entitled “Strengthen Global Nuclear Security Architecture and Promote Global Nuclear Security Governance".

Concept note-3: -This tenth nuclear security summit at Georgetown will cover various topics from historical events to future nuclear security issues.

Concept note-4: -The Hague in the Netherlands provided the setting on March 24th and 25th for the third Nuclear Security Summit (NSS).
