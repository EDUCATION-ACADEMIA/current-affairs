The 7th Clean Energy Ministerial (CEM7) and Mission Innovation (MI) was held at which city

(A) ** San Francisco

(B) New York

(C) Moscow

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This meeting of ministers from the 20 MI countries and the European Union coincided with the seventh Clean Energy Ministerial (CEM7).

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is India. India is hosting the Clean Energy Ministerial (CEM) senior officials meeting from 6-8 April 2022. It will review the work of various workstreams in Clean Energy Policies.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which country has launched the Mission Innovation Clean-Tech Exchange at the Net Zero Summit hosted by Chile? Notes: India has launched the Mission Innovation Clean-Tech Exchange at the Net Zero Summit hosted by Chile.
