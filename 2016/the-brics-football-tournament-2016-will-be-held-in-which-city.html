The BRICS Football Tournament 2016 will be held in which City

(A) Rio de Janeiro

(B) ** Goa

(C) Moscow

(D) Durban

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The summit was held from 15 to 16 October 2016 at the Taj Exotica hotel in Benaulim, Goa, India. India holds the chair of the BRICS from February 2016 to December 2016.

Concept note-2: -In October 2016, the BRICS leaders convened the 8th BRICS Summit in Goa, India, under the theme of “Building Responsive, Inclusive, and Collective Solutions".

Concept note-3: -China, South Africa, Brazil and Russia express their sincere gratitude to the Government and people of India for hosting the Eighth BRICS Summit in Goa.

Concept note-4: -The headquarters of the BRICS Bank is in Shanghai.
