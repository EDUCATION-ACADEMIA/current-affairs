The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA) has approved the setting up of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) in how many state

(A) ** 62

(B) 45

(C) 37

(D) 98

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Commissioner Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (2-5 Four persons to be nominated by the Chairman of the Society from amongst outstanding educationists). Dr. Abu Saleh Shariff, Professor National Council of Applied Research (NCAER) & Member Secretary of Justice Sachar Committee.

Concept note-2: -Three decades after introduction of the awards-winning Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) across the country, Tamil Nadu (pop. 72.1 million) remains the only state that does not host a single one.

Concept note-3: -Brief History of JAWAHAR NAVODAYA VIDYALAYA In 1985, the Government of India has decided to set up co-educational institutions known as Model Schools, one in each district, essentially to provide good quality modern education to the talented children predominantly from rural areas.
