The Centre has announced an investment of over Rs 5, 000 crore to construct a 12-km-long elevated railway corridor inside _________ under Smart City Project

(A) Chandigarh

(B) Panaji

(C) Ranchi

(D) ** Guwahati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the Union Budget 2017, an investment of over Rs 5, 000 crores to construct a 12-km-long elevated railway corridor inside Guwahati was announced. In 2018, the Centre announced the same and immediately the NFR claimed that the location survey had started and would be completed soon.

Concept note-2: -Ghum railway station of the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway is the highest railway station in India. It is situated at an altitude of 2, 258 metres (7, 407 ft). The place is the home of the Ghum Monastery and the Batasia Loop, a bend of the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.

Concept note-3: -Current status Bridge over Chandrabhaga river is being laid. Also a bridge over Alaknanda river in Tehri is under construction.
