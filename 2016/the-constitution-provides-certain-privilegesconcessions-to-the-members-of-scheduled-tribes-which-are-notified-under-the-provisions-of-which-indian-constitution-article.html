The Constitution provides certain privileges/concessions to the members of Scheduled Tribes which are notified under the provisions of which Indian Constitution Article

(A) Article 244

(B) Article 46

(C) Article 338 A

(D) ** Article 342

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Article 335 “The claims of the members of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes shall be taken into consideration, consistently with the maintenane of efficiency of administration, in the making of appointments to services and posts in connection with • the affairs of the Union or of a State."

Concept note-2: -Article 342 provides for specification of tribes or tribal communities or parts of or groups within tribes or tribal communities which are deemed to be for the purposes of the Constitution the Scheduled Tribes in relation to that State or Union Territory.

Concept note-3: -Article 332 of the Constitution of India provides for reservation of seats for the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes in the Legislative Assemblies of the States.

Concept note-4: -Article 350. Language to be used in representations for redress of grievances-Every person shall be entitled to submit a representation for the redress of any grievance to any officer or authority of the Union or a State in any of the languages used in the Union or in the State, as the case may be.
