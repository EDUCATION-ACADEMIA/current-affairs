The Defence and Research Development Organization (DRDO) has successfully flight tested was conducted from which Air Force station

(A) Thiruvananthapuram

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) Bangalore

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Acting in the same direction, DRDO along with Indian Army has successfully completed six flight-tests of ‘Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile (QRSAM)’ system from Integrated Test Range (ITR) Chandipur off the Odisha coast.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of Defence said that Defence Research and Development Organisation, DRDO, successfully carried out a training launch of a Short-Range Ballistic Missile, Prithvi-II from the Integrated Test Range, Chandipur off the Odisha coast.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution The correct answer is Astra. ASTRA is a Beyond Visual Range (BVR) class of Air-to-Air Missile (AAM) system designed to be mounted on fighter aircraft. The missile is designed to engage and destroy highly maneuvering supersonic aircraft.

Concept note-4: -DRDO successfully test-fires Agni-Prime missile.
