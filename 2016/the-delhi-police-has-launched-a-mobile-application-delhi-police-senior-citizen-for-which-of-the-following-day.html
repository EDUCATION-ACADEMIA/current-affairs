[SOURCES]
The Delhi Police has launched a mobile application “Delhi Police Senior Citizen” for which of the following day

(A) International Day of Non-violence

(B) ** International Day of Older Persons

(C) Independence Day

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the occasion of the International Day of Older Persons, the app ‘Delhi Police Senior Citizen’ was launched by Delhi Lt Governor Najeeb Jung and Police Commissioner Alok Kumar Verma in the presence of senior police officials.

Concept note-2: -They can also register themselves by contacting on telephone numbers 20819034, 23746100 Extn. No. 69975 or through Senior Citizen Mobile App or Helpline No. 1291 or through Delhi Police website https://delhipolice.gov.in or through SHO/ACP/DCP office of their area.

Concept note-3: -You can apply by calling “14567” which is a National Helpline for the Elders and they should be able to facilitate you to get a Senior Citizen Certifiate or Identity Card.

Concept note-4: -Delhi Police comes under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), Government of India.
