The eight nations composing the SAIEVAC recently.What is the full form of SAIEVAC

(A) ** South Asia Initiative to End Violence Against Children

(B) South Africa Initiative to End Violence Against Children

(C) South America Initiative to End Violence Against Child

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -South Asia Initiative to End Violence against Children (SAIEVAC)

Concept note-2: -SAARC is an economic and political regional organisation of countries in South Asia set up in 1985. It aims to accelerate the process of economic and social development in its member states through increased intra-regional cooperation.

Concept note-3: -Sri Lanka maintains resident missions in 6 South Asian countries i.e. Afghanistan, Bangladesh, India, the Maldives, Nepal and Pakistan, while having one Deputy High Commission in Chennai and two Consulates General in Mumbai and Karachchi.

Concept note-4: -The South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation (SAARC) is the regional intergovernmental organization and geopolitical union of states in South Asia. Its member states are Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Bhutan, India, Maldives, Nepal, Pakistan, and Sri Lanka.
