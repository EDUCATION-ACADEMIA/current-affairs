The eleventh edition of ASEM summit 2016 began in which country

(A) Italy

(B) Belgium

(C) ** Mongolia

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At the eleventh Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM11) held in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia in July 2016 under the main theme “20 Years of ASEM: Partnership for the Future through Connectivity” and issued the Statement of ASEM Leaders on International Terrorism and the Ulaanbaatar Declaration.

Concept note-2: -The theme of the two-day summit is Strengthening Multilateralism for Shared Growth. Vice President Venkaiah Naidu is leading the Indian delegation. This Summit will also mark the 25th anniversary of the ASEM process. It is being hosted by Cambodia as ASEM Chair.

Concept note-3: -2 Association of Southeast Asian Nations: Brunei Darussalam, Cambodia, Indonesia, Lao PDR, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand and Vietnam.

Concept note-4: -The 13th ASEM Summit (ASEM13) in Phnom Penh, Cambodia.
