The Environment Pollution (Prevention and Control) Authority (EPCA) on 5 July 2016 recommended a green cess of _________ percent on diesel vehicles

(A) 14-15

(B) 17-20

(C) ** 20-22

(D) 7-12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The government has decreed commercial vehicle owners to pay green tax at the rate of 10-25% of their road tax liabilities. For instance, if their road tax is ₹6000, the maximum green tax applicable would be ₹1500.

Concept note-2: -The Supreme Court-appointed Environment Pollution (Prevention and Control) Authority (Epca) on Thursday welcomed the ordinance to put in place a new anti-pollution agency that replaces the 22-year-old monitoring body.

Concept note-3: -In a nutshell, Bharat Stage (BS) norms are defined for the engine, fuel and permissible emission norms to help reduce pollution. Between a BS4 and a BS6 diesel car, the former comply with much stricter emission standards. In addition, BS6 fuels are much cleaner as they contain five times lower sulphur content.
