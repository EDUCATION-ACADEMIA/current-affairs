The first meeting of the BRICS Working Group on Energy Saving and Energy Efficiency began in which city

(A) ** Visakhapatnam

(B) New Delhi

(C) Kolkata

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Shanghai. BRICS(Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa) headquarter is located in Shanghai, China. BRICS Started in 2001 as BRIC, an acronym coined by Goldman Sachs for Brazil, Russia, India, and China. South Africa was added in 2010.

Concept note-2: -South Africa to host 15th BRICS summit at Durban in late August 2023.

Concept note-3: -In 2009, Brazil, Russia, India, and China came together to form BRIC. It works towards the betterment of the economy and development of the nations.
