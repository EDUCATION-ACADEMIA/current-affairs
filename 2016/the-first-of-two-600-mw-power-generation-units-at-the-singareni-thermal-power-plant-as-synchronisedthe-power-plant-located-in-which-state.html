The first of two 600 MW power generation units at the Singareni Thermal Power Plant as synchronised.The power plant located in which state

(A) Andhra Pradesh

(B) Odisha

(C) Karnataka

(D) ** Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Hussain Sagar Thermal Power Station is the historic thermal power plant that was located in Hyderabad, Telangana on the banks of Hussain Sagar. It was India’s first thermal power station, opened in 1920 by the erstwhile seventh Nizam of Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -a) Dhuvaran is the first thermal power station in Gujarat. Thus, Option (a) is the correct answer.

Concept note-3: -The Singareni Thermal Power Plant (STPP) is a coal-fired power station in Pegadapalli, a village in Mancheriyal District of Telangana, India. The power plant has an installed capacity of 1200 MW, consisting of two 600 MW units, and is operated by the Singareni Collieries Company.
