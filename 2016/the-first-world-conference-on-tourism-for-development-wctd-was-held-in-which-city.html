The first World Conference on Tourism for Development (WCTD) was held in which city

(A) New York

(B) Moscow

(C) ** Beijing

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Summit on Sustainable Tourism held in Vitoria on 26 and 27 November was the scene of the unanimous adoption and proclamation by His Excellency Don Iñigo Urkullu, President of the Basque Government, of the World Charter for Sustainable Tourism +20.

Concept note-2: -It was at its third session (Torremolinos, Spain, September 1979), that the UNWTO General Assembly decided to institute World Tourism Day, commencing in the year 1980.

Concept note-3: -Secretariat: UNWTO headquarters are based in Madrid, Spain.
