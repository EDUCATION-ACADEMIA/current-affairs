The fourth edition of joint military exercise Garuda Shakti between India and which country

(A) Sri Lanka

(B) China

(C) ** Indonesia

(D) Nepal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ‘Garuda Shakti’ exercise aims at strengthening understanding, cooperation and interoperability between the special forces of India and Indonesia.

Concept note-2: -Garuda Shakti is a bilateral exercise between special forces of armies of India and Indonesia.

Concept note-3: -INDIAN ARMY SPECIAL FORCES COMMENCE JOINT EXERCISE GARUDA SHAKTI WITH INDONESIAN SPECIAL FORCES AT SANGGA BUANA TRAINING AREA, KARAWANG, INDONESIA.

Concept note-4: -The exercise ‘Veer Guardian-2023’ involving the Indian Air Force (IAF) and Japan Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) will be held at Japan’s Hyakuri air base. The IAF said on Saturday that its deployment at the exercise will include four Su-30 MKI jets, two C-17 aircraft and one IL-78 plane.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Indonesian Army. Garuda Shakti is a bilateral exercise between special forces of armies of India(Para Special Force) and Indonesia. It had started off in 2012, in India. It is conducted as part of military diplomacy between the Indian and Indonesian Army alternatively in India and Indonesia.
