The Global Day of Parents was proclaimed by the UN General Assembly in which year

(A) 1998

(B) 2001

(C) 1987

(D) ** 2012

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global Day of Parents is commemorated on June 1 every year. ‘Appreciate all parents throughout the world’ is the theme for Global Day of Parents, 2022.

Concept note-2: -The United Nations adopted a resolution in the fall of 2012 to observe Global Day of Parents. The first observance took place on June 1, 2013.

Concept note-3: -In 1993, the General Assembly decided in a resolution (A/RES/47/237) that 15 May of every year should be observed as The International Day of Families.

Concept note-4: -Global Parents’ Day Significance The day aims to promote awareness of the importance of parenthood for positive development in children. Parents, children, teachers, family, and government organisations unitedly celebrate this day to promote effective parenting.
