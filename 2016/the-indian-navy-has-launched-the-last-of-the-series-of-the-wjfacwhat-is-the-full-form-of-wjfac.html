The Indian Navy has launched the last of the series of the WJFAC.What is the full form of WJFAC

(A) ** Water Jet Fast Attack Craft

(B) Water Jet Fasted Attack Craft

(C) Watered Jet Fast Attack Craft

(D) Water Jet Free Attack Craft

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -INS Tarasa (T94) is a patrol vessel of the Car Nicobar-class of Indian Navy and the last ship in the series of four Water Jet Fast Attack Craft (WJFAC).

Concept note-2: -INS Tihayu (T93) is a patrol vessel of the Car Nicobar-class of Indian Navy and the third ship in the series of four Water Jet Fast Attack Craft (WJFAC).

Concept note-3: -On 02 October 1934, this Service was renamed Royal Indian Navy (RIN), with its headquarters at Bombay.

Concept note-4: -The new naval crest has a traditional naval clear anchor below the Ashoka lion head with ‘Sham No Varunah’ inscribed below it, which has been taken from Vedas and means ‘may the ocean God be auspicious unto us’.
