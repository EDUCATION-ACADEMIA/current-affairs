The Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is set to launch the facility of travel insurance for passengers at premium of Rs _________

(A) 10 Lakhs

(B) ** 1 Rupee

(C) 15 Lakhs

(D) 100 Rupee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The premium w.e.f 01-Nov-2021 is Re. 35 paise per passenger inclusive of all taxes. 5. Customer shall receive the policy information through SMS and on their registered email IDs directly from Insurance Companies along with the link for filling nomination details.

Concept note-2: -The Bharat Gaurav trains were launched by the Railways Ministry to be operated in places having touristic importance and promote theme-based tourism.

Concept note-3: -The travel insurance is only available if you purchase train tickets from the official IRCTC web portal or mobile app. At the time of making payment for your tickets, you can tick the travel insurance box for purchasing insurance.

Concept note-4: -The present tagline is “Lifeline of the nation”. The IRCTC Logo depicts pan India connectivity of railway networks.
