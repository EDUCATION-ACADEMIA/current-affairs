(A) SMART-1

(B) Chang’e 1

(C) LADEE

(D) ** Chandrayaan-2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With a more robust lunar rover onboard, the Indian Space Research Organisation planned to launch its third mission to the moon, Chandrayaan-3, in June of next year. The move will help the organisation in its future interplanetary explorations.

Concept note-2: -ISRO’s vision is stated as “Harness space technology for national development while pursuing space science research and planetary exploration.”

Concept note-3: -Chandrayaan-1 was India’s first lunar probe. It was launched by the Indian Space Research Organisation on 22nd October 2008. Q. What is the name of the first mission to the Moon?
