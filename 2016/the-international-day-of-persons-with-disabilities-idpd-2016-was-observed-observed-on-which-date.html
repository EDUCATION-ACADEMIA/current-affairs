The International Day of Persons with Disabilities (IDPD) 2016 was observed observed on which date

(A) 8 May

(B) 23 April

(C) 19 July

(D) ** 3 December

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International Day of Persons with Disabilities (IDPD), 3 December 2016 | United Nations Enable.

Concept note-2: -The annual observance of the International Day of Disabled Persons was proclaimed in 1992, by the United Nations General Assembly resolution 47/3.

Concept note-3: -December 3 is International Day of Persons with Disabilities, and on this Day WHO joins other partners around the world to call for innovation and transformative solutions to achieve Health for ALL.

Concept note-4: -The theme this year is “Transformative solutions for inclusive development: the role of innovation in fuelling an accessible and equitable world“.

Concept note-5: -The History of IDPD International Day of Persons with Disabilities (IDPD) is a day of observance, marked annually. On this day, organizations, individuals and government work for increasing awareness about the challenges that the disabled community faces daily. This day was proclaimed by the United Nations in 1992.
