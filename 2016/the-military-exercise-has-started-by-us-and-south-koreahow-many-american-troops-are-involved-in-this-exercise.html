The Military exercise has started by US and South Korea.How many American troops are involved in this exercise

(A) ** 17, 000

(B) 15, 000

(C) 30, 000

(D) 23, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Some 800 troops from both countries who were deployed there in November last year have been taking part in the exercises which kicked off on Jan.

Concept note-2: -With 28, 500 U.S. soldiers, sailors, airmen, and marines in South Korea, U.S. forces in South Korea are a major presence in the region and a key manifestation of the U.S. government’s aim to rebalance toward the Asia-Pacific.

Concept note-3: -Foal Eagle (Korean: ) is a combined field training exercise (FTX) conducted annually by the Republic of Korea Armed Forces and the United States Armed Forces under the auspices of the Combined Forces Command. It is one of the largest military exercises conducted annually in the world.

Concept note-4: -The South Korean military also recently completed the 12-day “Hoguk” military field exercises that are held annually and involve US troops.
