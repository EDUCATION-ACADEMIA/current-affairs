The National Summit on Family Planning concluded in which state of India

(A) Gujarat

(B) Bihar

(C) ** New Delhi

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Theme: National Family Planning Summit 2022 theme was “Sustaining efforts, Steering Partnerships, Shaping Vision in Family Planning – Sabka Saath, Sabka Vishwas, Sabka Prayas & Sabka Vikas”. Organization: National Family Planning Summit 2022 was organized by Union Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.

Concept note-2: -India was the first country in the world to have launched a National Programme for Family Planning in 1952. With its historic initiation in 1952, the Family Planning Programme has undergone transformation in terms of policy and actual programme implementation.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: Tamil Nadu is the first Indian State to recognize the basic relevance of family planning to national planning.

Concept note-4: -Assertion (A) – Reproductive and Child Health (RCH) program evolved from the family planning program. Reason (R) – Its only aim is to foresee health of the children.
