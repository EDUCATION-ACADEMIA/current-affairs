The President to transfer a judge from one high court to other high court under which Indian Constitution article

(A) ** Article 222

(B) Article 56

(C) Article 123

(D) Article 236 A

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Article 222 of the Constitution makes provision for the transfer of a Judge (including Chief Justice) from one High Court to any other High Court.

Concept note-2: -(c) the office of a Judge shall be vacated by his being appointed by the President to be a Judge of the Supreme Court or by his being transferred by the President to any other High Court within the territory of India.

Concept note-3: -(1) The president may, after consultation with the Chief Justice of India, transfer a Judge from one High Court to any other High Court.

Concept note-4: -Overview of Article 217 (1): (a) a Judge may, by writing under his hand addressed to the President, resign his office. (b) a Judge may be removed from his office by the President in the manner provided in clause ( 4 ) of Article 124 for the removal of a Judge of the Supreme Court.
