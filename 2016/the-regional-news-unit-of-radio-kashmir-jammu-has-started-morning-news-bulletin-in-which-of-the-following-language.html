The regional news Unit of Radio Kashmir Jammu has started morning news bulletin in which of the following language

(A) Telungu

(B) Malayalam

(C) ** Gojri

(D) Kannada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The RNU of AIR, Jammu is regularly broadcasting PM Modi’s “Mann Ki Baat” and also important feature programs in Dogri language “Azadi Ka Mahotsav” with highest listenership and appreciation.

Concept note-2: -The ISBS broadcasted its first news bulletin on January 19th 1936. On June 8th, 1936, the ISBS changed its name to All India Radio.

Concept note-3: -Formerly known as Radio Kashmir Srinagar, it was renamed as AIR Srinagar following the Jammu and Kashmir Reorganisation Act, 2019. It is one of public stations in the union territory alongside AIR Jammu and AIR Leh.

Concept note-4: -Out of these, 89 bulletins are broadcast daily from Delhi in the Home Service in English, Hindi and other Indian languages. The 44 Regional News Units (RNUs) putout 355 daily news bulletins in 67 languages. This includes news bulletins mounted exclusively on FM ‘Gold’ channel from 22 AIR Stations.

Concept note-5: -On June 8, 1936, the Indian State Broadcasting Service became All India Radio. The Central News Organisation (CNO) came into existence in August, 1937.
