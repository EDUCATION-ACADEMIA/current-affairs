The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) Governor has launched _________portal to check illegal money collection

(A) IllegalMoney

(B) ChechMon

(C) ** Sache

(D) Cache

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -RBI launches ‘Sachet’ portal to check illegal money collection. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) on Thursday launched a portal to curb illegal collection of money by companies.

Concept note-2: -In case you have any complaint against any entity which has defaulted in repayment of money/deposits collected from you illegally under any scheme, you can file a complaint against the entity on Sachet portal.

Concept note-3: -Check whether or not a particular entity seeking to accept public deposits is registered with any regulator and whether the entity is permitted to accept deposits.

Concept note-4: -Launch of Complaint Management System by RBI Members of public can access the CMS portal at RBI’s website to lodge their complaints against any of the entities regulated by RBI.
