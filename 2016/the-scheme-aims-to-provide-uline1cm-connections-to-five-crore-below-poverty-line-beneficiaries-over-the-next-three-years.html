The scheme aims to provide _________ connections to five crore below-poverty-line beneficiaries over the next three years

(A) Education

(B) ** Cooking Gas

(C) Kerosin

(D) None of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana will provide 5 crore LPG connections to BPL families, with the financial support of Rs 1600.

Concept note-2: -Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) aims to safeguard the health of women & children by providing them with a clean cooking fuel – LPG, so that they don’t have to compromise their health in smoky kitchens or wander in unsafe areas collecting firewood.

Concept note-3: -Ujjwala 2.0: Additional allocation of 1.6 Crore LPG Connections under PMUY Scheme with special facility to migrant households. The scheme was launched in Mahoba, Uttar Pradesh by Hon’ble Prime Minister of India, Shri. Narendra Modi.

Concept note-4: -The Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) is a government scheme launched in 2016 by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The scheme originally envisaged the distribution of 50 million LPG connections to women below the poverty line. Later, it aimed to provide LPG connections to eight crore women by March 2020.

Concept note-5: -The Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana 2022-23 provides ₹1600 financial assistance to BPL and APL ration card holders. Through this project, the government aims to provide LPG connections to poor APL and BPL families in the country.
