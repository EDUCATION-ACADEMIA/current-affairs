The seventh meeting of South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation (SAARC) Immigration Authorities was held in which city

(A) Dhaka

(B) New Delhi

(C) ** Islamabad

(D) Kathmandu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ranasinghe Premadasa met at the Seventh Summit of the South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation (SAARC) at Dhaka, Bangladesh on 10-11 April, 1993.

Concept note-2: -Secretariat: Kathmandu (Nepal).

Concept note-3: -The first summit was held in Dhaka, Bangladesh on 7–8 December 1985 and was attended by the Government representative and president of Bangladesh, Maldives, Pakistan and Sri Lanka, the kings of Bhutan and Nepal, and the Prime Minister of India.

Concept note-4: -SAARC was founded in Dhaka on 8 December 1985. Its secretariat is based in Kathmandu, Nepal.
