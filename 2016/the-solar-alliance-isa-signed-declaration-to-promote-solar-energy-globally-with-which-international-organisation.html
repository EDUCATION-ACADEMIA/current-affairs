[SOURCES]

The Solar Alliance (ISA) signed declaration to promote solar energy globally with which international organisation

(A) IMF

(B) ADB

(C) ** World bank

(D) IBRD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Solar Alliance (ISA) is an alliance of 123 signatory countries, most being sunshine countries, which lie either completely or partly between the Tropic of Cancer and the Tropic of Capricorn.

Concept note-2: -The Journey of the ISA (2019-2020) We thank New Zealand for signing the Framework Agreement of the International Solar Alliance. Hon NanaiaMahuta, Minister of Foreign Affairs, New Zealand handed over signed ISA Framework Agreement to DrSJaishankar, External Affairs Minister of India.

Concept note-3: -The establishment of ISA was announced at the 21st session of United Nations Climate Change Conference of the Parties (COP-21) in Paris, France held on November 30, 2015.

Concept note-4: -Talking to reporters in New Delhi yesterday, ISA Director General Ajay Mathur said, Union Power and New and Renewable Energy Minister R K Singh has been re-elected as the President of International Solar Alliance.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Germany.
