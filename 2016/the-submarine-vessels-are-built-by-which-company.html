The submarine vessels are built by which company

(A) Chittagong Marine Dockyard

(B) FMC Dockyard

(C) TaraTari Shipyard

(D) ** Mazagon Dock Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The new submarine was handed over to the Indian Navy on 20 December. Indian shipbuilder Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders (MDL) in Mumbai, Maharashtra, in close coordination with French company Naval Group, is constructing the vessels of the class.

Concept note-2: -HSL (Hindustan Shipyard Limited), A Government of India Undertaking, Department of Defence Production (DDP), Ministry of Defence (MoD), India.

Concept note-3: -Ministry of Defence signed a contract with Larsen & Toubro for acquisition of Two Multi-Purpose Vessels (MPVs) for Indian Navy at cost of Rs.887 Crore on 25 March 2022.
