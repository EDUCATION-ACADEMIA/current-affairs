The Supreme Court has banned the sale of firecrackers in which city recently

(A) Ahmedabad

(B) Bangalore

(C) Chennai

(D) ** Delhi-NCR

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -a) No firecrackers other than green crackers bearing QR Code would be sold in the Bazi-Bazar which is proposed to be held in Kolkata on and from 18.10. 2022.

Concept note-2: -4. West Bengal: Green fireworks are allowed during Kali Puja on October 24, 2022. The Calcutta High Court has announced no fireworks except green crackers that have QR codes in them. These green crackers will be imported and sold only during the festival.

Concept note-3: -The top court had last year clarified there is no blanket ban on the use of firecrackers and only those fireworks which contain barium salts are prohibited.

Concept note-4: -There is no strict ban on crackers in Uttar Pradesh, but the rules are strict. Chief minister Yogi Adityanath has instructed to keep firecracker shops away from the population for Diwali. He said the purchase and sale of firecrackers that are sensitive to the environment and health should be discouraged.
