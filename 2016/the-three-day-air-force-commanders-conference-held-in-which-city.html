The three-day Air Force Commanders’ Conference held in which city

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Kochi

(C) Ahmedabad

(D) Visakhapatnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Hon’ble Raksha Mantri (RM), Shri Rajnath Singh, inaugurated the Air Force Commanders’ Conference (AFCC) at Air Headquarters in New Delhi today.

Concept note-2: -Indian Air Force Day 2022: Major announcements made during 90th anniversary celebrations. The Indian Air Force on Saturday (September 8) celebrated its 90th year anniversary. The theme for this year’s anniversary celebrations is IAF: Transforming for the Future .

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Rajnath Singh.

Concept note-4: -The Motto of the Indian Air Force (Touch the sky with Glory) has been taken from the eleventh chapter of the Gita, the Discourse given by Lord Krishna to Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra during the Great War of Mahabharata.

Concept note-5: -The Indian Air Force Day is celebrated on October 8th every year, to mark the date of establishment of the Bharatiya Vayu Sena. 8th October 2021 was India’s 89th Air Force Day. IAF and Indian Air Force Day both constitute an important part of the UPSC Syllabus.
