The Tourism Ministry has sanctioned 25 projects worth Rs 2, 048 crore for 21 states and Union Territories under which scheme

(A) Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

(B) Kisan Vikas Patra

(C) Udaan Scheme

(D) ** Swadesh Darshan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1337.63 crore. “Close to 25% of SWADESH Darshan Projects have been sanctioned to North East states. Under PRASHAD Scheme, the Ministry has sanctioned approximately 200 crores for various projects in the region”, Union Minister added.

Concept note-2: -A total of 76 projects have been sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan Scheme.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Swadesh Darshan. It is the flagship scheme of the Ministry of Tourism for the development of tourism infrastructure in the country.

Concept note-4: -15 thematic circuits have been identified under the Swadesh Darshan for development.
