The _________ edition of Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) summit concluded on 20 November 2016 in Lima

(A) 32nd

(B) 19th

(C) ** 24th

(D) 27th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United States congratulates Thailand on hosting a successful APEC year in spite of ongoing economic challenges brought on by the COVID-19 pandemic and Russia’s war against Ukraine.

Concept note-2: -To revive growth, APEC 2022 will focus on restoring connectivity by resuming safe and seamless cross-border travel, reinvigorating tourism and the services sector, facilitating business mobility as well as increasing investment in health security.

Concept note-3: -India never joined APEC despite initial enthusiasm when it was launched in 1989. India was not seen as an open economy in 1989, but after India’s positive role in the EAS, Indonesia, the Chair of APEC in 2013, attempted to invite India, but was dissuaded by China.

Concept note-4: -The Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) is a group primarily focused on trade and economic growth for the 21 members of the economic forum and their people. In addition to trade and economic goals, APEC focuses on improving living standards, inclusivity, and sustainability.
