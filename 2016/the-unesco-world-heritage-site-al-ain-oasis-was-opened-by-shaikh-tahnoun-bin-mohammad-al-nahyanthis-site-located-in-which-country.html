The Unesco World Heritage site Al Ain Oasis was opened by Shaikh Tahnoun Bin Mohammad Al Nahyan.This site located in which country

(A) Saudi Arabia

(B) Afghanistan

(C) Indonesia

(D) ** Abu Dhabi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Al Ain is the fourth largest city in the UAE and is part of the emirate of Abu Dhabi.

Concept note-2: -A central element of the UNESCO Cultural Sites of Al Ain, Al Ain Oasis is fed by a traditional falaj irrigation system and is located in the middle of the city. Al Ain Oasis preserves a culture and a way of life going back thousands of years.

Concept note-3: -Al Ain: The Great Oasis of the United Arab Emirates. This green oasis in the middle of the Rub al Khali desert is a 4, 000 year old city and UNESCO World Heritage Site. In Al Ain city, the Al Ain Oasis has been opened as the UAE’s first curated UNESCO World Heritage Site visitor experience.

Concept note-4: -Al Ain Oases Dating back to over 4000 years ago, the city of Al Ain in the United Arab Emirates is home to many oases and is a treasured UNESCO World Heritage Site.
