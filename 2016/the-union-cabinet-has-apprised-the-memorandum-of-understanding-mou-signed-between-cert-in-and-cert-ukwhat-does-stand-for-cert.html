[SOURCES]

The Union Cabinet has apprised the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed between CERT-In and CERT-UK.What does stand for CERT

(A) ** Computer Emergency Response Team

(B) Common Emergency Response Team

(C) Computer Emergency Responsible Team

(D) Computer Emergency Resource Team

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-IN or ICERT) is an office within the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology of the Government of India. It is the nodal agency to deal with cyber security threats like hacking and phishing.

Concept note-2: -Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT)

Concept note-3: -The MoU was signed in February 2022. The MoU will provide a mechanism and help in creating and ecosystem that will promote innovation and entrepreneurship in both countries leading to new technology creation, manpower training, IP generation through collaboration.

Concept note-4: -CERT-In has been designated to serve as the national agency to perform the following functions in the area of cyber security: Collection, analysis and dissemination of information on cyber incidents. Forecast and alerts of cyber security incidents. Emergency measures for handling cyber security incidents.
