The Union Cabinet has given its approval to Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with which country to support Ease of Doing Business in India

(A) Japan

(B) USA

(C) Russia

(D) ** UK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union Cabinet chaired by the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has approved the signing of Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) in the field of Judicial Cooperation between India and Judicial Service Commission of the Republic of Maldives.

Concept note-2: -India and Panama have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to enhance cooperation in the training of diplomats.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Japan.
