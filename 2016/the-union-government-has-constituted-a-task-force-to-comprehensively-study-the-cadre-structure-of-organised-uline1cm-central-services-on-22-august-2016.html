[SOURCES]

The union government has constituted a Task Force to comprehensively study the cadre structure of organised _________ Central Services on 22 August 2016.

(A) ** Group A

(B) Group B

(C) Group C

(D) Group D

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An Organised Group ‘A’ Service is one which is constituted consciously as such by the Cadre Controlling Authorities and such a service can be constituted only through the established procedures.

Concept note-2: -Organized sectors also comply with Indian laws and regulations, such as the Minimum Wages Act, Factories Act, the Gratuity Payment Act, the Shops and Establishments Act, etc. There is no compliance in the unorganised sector with Indian laws and regulations. Examples: Government-sector jobs, industries, etc.
