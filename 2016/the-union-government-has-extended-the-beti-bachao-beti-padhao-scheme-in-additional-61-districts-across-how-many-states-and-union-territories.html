[SOURCES]

The Union Government has extended the Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme in additional 61 districts across how many States and Union Territories

(A) ** 11

(B) 29

(C) 8

(D) 17

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Phase II: In Phase II, 61 districts from 11 states were selected.

Concept note-2: -The scheme provides parents with income tax rebate benefits and an attractive interest rate of 7.6% on the deposits. It offers parents the option to open a savings account per girl child (under the age of 10 years) and allows parents to make deposits in the account for a period of 15 years.

Concept note-3: -Vijayapura has become the only district in Karnataka and one among the 100 districts in the country to have been chosen for the implementation of the ‘Beti Bachao Beti Padhao’ (BBBP) scheme.

Concept note-4: -Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) was launched by the Prime Minister on 22nd January, 2015 at Panipat, Haryana. BBBP addresses the declining Child Sex Ratio (CSR) and related issues of women empowerment over a life-cycle continuum.
