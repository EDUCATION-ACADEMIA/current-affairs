[SOURCES]

The Union Government has inaugurated Hunar Haat (Skill Haat) exhibition at India International Trade Fair (2016) in which city

(A) Pune

(B) Kochi

(C) Banglore

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Minister for Minority Affairs Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi inaugurated the 41st edition of Hunar Haat in Agra on May 19.

Concept note-2: -Union Minister for Information & Broadcasting and Youth Affairs & Sports, Shri Anurag Thakur inaugurated the 40th edition of the ‘Hunar Haat’ in the presence of Union Minority Affairs Minister Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi, in Mumbai today.

Concept note-3: -“Vishwakarma Vatika”, traditional circus, laser shows, musical and spectacular cultural programmes of renowned and emerging artists, selfie points etc are major attraction of this “Hunar Haat”. The visitors at Agra “Hunar Haat” will enjoy musical and cultural programmes every evening.

Concept note-4: -The next “Hunar Haat” will be organised at JLN Stadium, New Delhi from 22 December 2021 to 2 January 2022.

Concept note-5: -The 41st edition of the India International Trade Fair (IITF) is currently going on at the Pragati Maidan in New Delhi.
