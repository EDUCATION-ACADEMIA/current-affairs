The Union Ministry of Urban Development has approved what amount under HRIDAY scheme for improving infrastructure facilities around core heritage sites in five cities.

(A) Rs.110 crore

(B) Rs.105 crore

(C) Rs.200 crore

(D) ** Rs.114 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HRIDAY scheme aims in the conservation of heritage, urban planning and in increasing the economic growth of the heritage cities. The National Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana emphasize on the accessibility, security, safety, livelihood, cleanliness and faster service delivery in the heritage cities.

Concept note-2: -The scheme does not include the Madurai city.

Concept note-3: -HRIDAY’s programmes and advocacy efforts have initiated setting up of ‘Tobacco-Free Schools, Tobacco-Free Homes, Tobacco-Free Communities’ as well as Tobacco-Free jurisdictions across the country.
