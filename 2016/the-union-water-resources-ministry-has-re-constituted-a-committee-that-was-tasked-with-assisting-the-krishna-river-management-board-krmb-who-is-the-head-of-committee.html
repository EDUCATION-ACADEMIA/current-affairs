The Union Water Resources Ministry has re-constituted a committee that was tasked with assisting the Krishna River Management Board (KRMB). Who is the head of committee

(A) R.P. Pandey

(B) Gopala Krishnan

(C) Pradeep Kumar Shukla

(D) ** A.K. Bajaj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The headquarters of the Krishna River Management Board shall be at Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -Krishna River Management Board (KRMB) is an autonomous body established as per Andhra Pradesh Reorganization Act, 2014 under the administrative control of Ministry of Jal Shakti to manage and regulate the waters in Krishna Basin in the states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

Concept note-3: -The Headquarter of GRMB is located at Hyderabad.
