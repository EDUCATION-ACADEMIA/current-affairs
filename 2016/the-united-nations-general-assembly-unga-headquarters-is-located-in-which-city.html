The United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) Headquarters is located in which city

(A) Paris

(B) Chicago

(C) ** New York

(D) California

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Each year, in September, the full UN membership meets in the General Assembly Hall in New York for the annual General Assembly session, and general debate, which many heads of state attend and address.

Concept note-2: -The General debate is held in the United Nations General Assembly Hall at the United Nations Headquarters in New York.

Concept note-3: -The United Nations Information Center in Washington, DC engages with U.S. government officials, Congress, civil society, media, academia, and young people, serving as a resource to enhance Americans’ understanding of the United Nations. The United Nations founded in 1945, now has 193 Member States.

Concept note-4: -The United Nations General Assembly Building is part of the headquarters of the United Nations in the Turtle Bay neighborhood of Manhattan in New York City.
