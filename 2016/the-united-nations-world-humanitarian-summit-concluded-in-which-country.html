The United Nations World Humanitarian Summit concluded in which country

(A) Canada

(B) Argentina

(C) France

(D) ** Turkey

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When is World Humanitarian Day 2022, and what is the theme? World Humanitarian Day 2022 takes place on Friday 19th August. In 2022, the theme for World Humanitarian Day is #ItTakesAVillage. Whenever and wherever people are in crisis, there are others who help them.

Concept note-2: -World Humanitarian Day 2023 theme: ‘It takes a village’. A theme is decided every year to observe WHD that aims to bring partners from across the humanitarian system together and work collectively for survival, well-being and dignity of people affected by crises.

Concept note-3: -Their goal: to seek a better global response to the challenges faced by humanitarian actors, and to place humanitarian action on a better footing for the future.

Concept note-4: -World Humanitarian Day is commemorated every year on 19 August to pay tribute to humanitarian workers killed and injured in the course of their work, and to honour all aid and health workers who continue, despite the odds, to provide life-saving support and protection to people most in need.
