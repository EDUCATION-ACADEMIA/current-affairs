The United States has signed 38 billion dollars deal with which country to provide the country with military assistance over a 10-year period, from 2019 to 2028

(A) India

(B) Iraq

(C) Oman

(D) ** Israel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Starting 10 years after its founding, Israel adopted an official humanitarian aid agenda, providing vital relief to more than 140 countries.

Concept note-2: -According to USAID Data Services as of December 2021, in constant 2021 U.S. dollars (inflation-adjusted), total U.S. aid to Israel obligated from 1946-2021 is $247 billion.
