The Uttar Pradesh (UP) Government has approved the Detailed Project Report (DPR) of which city

(A) Allahabad

(B) Jhansi

(C) Faizabad

(D) ** Varanasi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The firm will get 90 years instead of 40 to develop the project. The Uttar Pradesh cabinet on Thursday approved the revised detailed project report (DPR) of the Film City to be developed on 1000 acres of land in Sector 21 along the Yamuna Expressway, close to the Jewar airport site.

Concept note-2: -The Film City is a gateway to Noida’s twin brother, Greater Noida, which has developed as an ultra-modern industrial township, evincing interest from large globally established multinational corporations and other corporate houses promising international standards of living.

Concept note-3: -In September 2020, the Yogi government had announced to establish a film city in Greater Noida with an investment of Rs 10, 000 crore.
