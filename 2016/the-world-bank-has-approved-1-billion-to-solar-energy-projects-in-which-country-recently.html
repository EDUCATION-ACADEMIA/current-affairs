The World Bank has approved $1 billion to solar energy projects in which country recently

(A) ** India

(B) China

(C) Russia

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chinese investment in clean energy is the highest worldwide. In 2019, China pumped some 83.4 billion U.S. dollars into clean energy research and development.

Concept note-2: -The EU, the United States, Vietnam, and Japan are ranked as top solar producers. A gigawatt (GW) is a unit of measurement of electrical power. Photovoltaic (PV) technology converts sunlight into electrical energy.

Concept note-3: -The Bhadla Solar Park is the World’s largest Solar Power Plant. It is based in Bhadla village, in India’s Rajasthan’s Jodhpur district – a region known for its solar-friendly high temperatures.

Concept note-4: -Iberdrola already operates Europe’s largest solar power plant. Iberdrola already owns the largest photovoltaic park on the European continent. The 590 MW Francisco Pizarro facility in Extremadura is in western Spain. It was commissioned in August.
