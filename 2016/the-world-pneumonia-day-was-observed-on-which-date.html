The World Pneumonia Day was observed on which date

(A) May 5

(B) 26 December

(C) 7 July

(D) ** November 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By India Today Web Desk: World Pneumonia Day is dedicated to raising awareness about the disease named Pneumonia. It is observed every year on November 12. Pneumonia is a lung-based infection caused by bacteria, viruses, and fungi.

Concept note-2: -WORLD PNEUMONIA DAY 2022: HISTORY AND SIGNIFICANCE The day first came into effect on November 12, 2009, under the initiative named ‘Stop Pneumonia’. This was a global coalition against child pneumonia.

Concept note-3: -World Pneumonia Day was first observed on 12 November 2009 under ‘Stop Pneumonia’, an initiative of the Global Coalition Against Child Pneumonia. After that, WHO and UNICEF launched an integrated global action plan to prevent and control pneumonia.

Concept note-4: -Pneumonia is the single largest infectious killer of adults and children which claimed over 2.5 million lives in 2019 alone. World Pneumonia Day is observed annually on November 12 with an aim to highlight and raise awareness about the complications and issues related to pneumonia.

Concept note-5: -The specific theme of World Pneumonia Day 2022 is ‘Pneumonia Affects Everyone. ‘ How important is it to improve the visibility of this disease and the people it affects?
