The World’s first Bollywood theme park opened in which country

(A) ** Dubai

(B) Singapore

(C) Malaysia

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World’s First Bollywood Theme Park in Dubai.

Concept note-2: -BOLLYWOOD PARKS™ Dubai is the first park of its kind in the world. In six fascinating zones, you can experience different environments inspired by the greatest Bollywood blockbusters.

Concept note-3: -DXB Entertainments is the owner of Dubai Parks and Resorts. The business is organised into two operational business units: Theme Parks and Retail and Hospitality.

Concept note-4: -The ticket prices for Bollywood Parks Dubai ranges from INR 1, 860 to INR 11, 200. Now that you know the entry fee for Bollywood Parks Dubai in Dubai, you can book your visit anytime as per your plan.
