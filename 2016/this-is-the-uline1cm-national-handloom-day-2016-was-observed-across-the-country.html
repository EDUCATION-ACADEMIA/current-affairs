[SOURCES]

This is the _________ National Handloom Day 2016 was observed across the country

(A) 1st

(B) ** 2nd

(C) 3rd

(D) 4th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The date, 7th August, was chosen as the Swadeshi Movement was launched on the same date in the year 1905, which was based on Bal Gangadhar Tilak’s ideology of swadeshi.

Concept note-2: -The first National Handloom Day was inaugurated on 7 August 2015 by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in Chennai. On this day, we honour our handloom-weaving community and highlight the contribution of this sector in the socio-economic development of our country.

Concept note-3: -2022 marks the 8th National Handloom Day. This year theme of National Handloom Day 2022 is ”Handloom, an Indian legacy”.

Concept note-4: -The 7th August was chosen as National Handloom Day to commemorate the Swadeshi Movement which was launched on this day in 1905. The National Handloom Day is observed annually to honour our handloom weavers and provide impetus to the handloom industry.

Concept note-5: -Note – The theme of National Handloom Day 2021 is “Handloom, an Indian Legacy”.
