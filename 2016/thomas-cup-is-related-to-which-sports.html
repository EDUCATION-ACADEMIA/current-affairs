Thomas Cup is related to which sports

(A) ** Badminton

(B) Cricket

(C) Hockey

(D) Football

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thomas Cup, trophy signifying world supremacy in the sport of badminton. The cup was donated in 1939 by Sir George Thomas for a series of men’s international team competitions to be managed by the International Badminton Federation (IBF), of which Thomas was then president.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Thomas cup is related to the game of Badminton.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Badminton.

Concept note-4: -Its name honours Sir George Thomas, legendary Founder-President of the International Badminton Federation (now BWF), who was keen that badminton should have its own version of the Davis Cup in tennis.
