UIDAI has ban sharing of Aadhaar details.What does stands for UIDAI

(A) ** Unique Identification Authority of India

(B) Unique Identity Authority Information

(C) Unique Identity Agency of India

(D) Unique Information Authority of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is the 4 digit code that one provides for their offline paperless e KYC on the UIDAI website just before downloading the file.

Concept note-2: -What is Virtual ID (VID)? VID is a temporary, revocable 16-digit random number mapped with the Aadhaar number. VID can be used in lieu of Aadhaar number whenever authentication or e-KYC services are performed. Authentication may be performed using VID in a manner similar to using Aadhaar number.

Concept note-3: -Reasons for the Deactivation of Aadhaar As per these guidelines, a person’s Aadhaar can be deactivated by the regional UIDAI authority on following grounds: If there are multiple holders having same demographic and biometric details, all Aadhaar numbers are likely to be deactivated.
