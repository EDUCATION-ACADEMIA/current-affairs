(A) Afghanistan

(C) Canada

Concept note-1: -Presently, there are several laws which deal with specific forms of trafficking For instance, the Immoral Traffic (Prevention) Act, 1986 covers trafficking for commercial sexual exploitation while the Bonded Labour System (Abolition) Act, 1976 deals with punishment for employment of bonded labour.
