US President Barack Obama has signed National Bison Legacy Act into law officially making the American bison the country’s first national mammal.What is the name of mammal

(A) American Bald Eagle

(B) ** American Bison

(C) Marsupial

(D) Rhinoceros

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The American bison was named the national mammal of the United States on May 9, 2016.

Concept note-2: -The Founding Fathers made an appropriate choice when they selected the bald eagle as the emblem of the nation. The fierce beauty and proud independence of this great bird aptly symbolizes the strength and freedom of America.

Concept note-3: -"The National Bison Legacy Act recognizes the cultural and economic importance of the bison in North Dakota and across our nation. The noble creatures truly reflect the frontier spirit and rugged strength of our nation.” More than 40 million bison once roamed across most of North America.

Concept note-4: -Joining the bald eagle as a national symbol, the American bison recently became the United States’ first national mammal.
