Venkaiah Naidu associated which of the following Union Ministry

(A) Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs

(B) ** Ministry of Information & Broadcasting

(C) Ministry of Human Resource Development

(D) Ministry of Law & Justice

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Muppavarapu Venkaiah Naidu (born 1 July 1949) is an Indian politician who served as the 13th vice president of India from 2017 to 2022.

Concept note-2: -Venkaiah Naidu won the election and took oath as 13th Vice-President of India on 11 August 2017 at Darbar Hall, Rashtrapati Bhawan, New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -On 11 August 2022, Jagdeep Dhankhar took office as the 14th vice president.

Concept note-4: -The office of vice president is the second-highest constitutional office after the president and ranks second in the order of precedence and first in the line of succession to the presidency. The vice president is also a member of the Parliament of India as the ex officio chairman of the Rajya Sabha.
