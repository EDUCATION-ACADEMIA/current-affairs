Videocon d2h ties up with which of the following company

(A) Reliance

(B) ** Vodafone m-pesa

(C) Aircel

(D) Bharti Airtel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dish TV Videocon will continue to operate services under the 3 distinct brand identities of Dish TV, Zing and Videocon D2H.

Concept note-2: -Jawahar Goel, CMD, Dish TV India Limited, said, “We are extremely pleased to announce that the D-Day is finally here. Today, Videocon d2h Limited and Dish TV India Limited have become one entity.

Concept note-3: -Announcing the merger, DishTV India’s chairman and managing director Jawahar Goel said that Videocon d2h and DishTV India had become one entity.

Concept note-4: -But now it’s marketed by Dish Infra Services Pvt Ltd (Dish TV). So videocon d2h may remain as seperate brand under Dish infra Services[Dish TV].

Concept note-5: -“Today, Videocon D2h Ltd and Dish TV India Ltd have become one entity. This amalgamation positions the new entity for exceptional future growth and profitability and puts on us the responsibility to lead the DTH industry in India to the next level, ” Dish TV India CMD Jawahar Goel said.
