Vyas Samman Award award is given in which field

(A) ** literary

(B) Music

(C) Education

(D) Sports

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Vyas Samman is a literary award in India, first awarded in 1991. It is awarded annually by the K.K. Birla Foundation and includes a cash payout of Rs 4, 00, 000 (as of 2019).

Concept note-2: -The Vyas Samman is given by K K Birla Foundation for outstanding literary work in Hindi authored by an Indian citizen published during the last 10 years. It carries an award of Rs 4 lakh along with a citation and a plaque. It was initiated in 1991.

Concept note-3: -The Vyas Samman is given by K K Birla Foundation for an outstanding literary work in Hindi authored by an Indian citizen published during the last 10 years. It carries an award of four lakh rupees along with a citation and plaque.

Concept note-4: -The Saraswati Samman is an annual award for outstanding prose or poetry literary works in any of the 22 languages of India listed in Schedule VIII of the Constitution of India.

Concept note-5: -The KK Birla Foundation instituted the Vyas Samman award in 1991. It is given annually to an outstanding work of Hindi literature by an Indian citizen published in the last 10 years. The award includes a cash prize of Rs 3.50 lakh.
