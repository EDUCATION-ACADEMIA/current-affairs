WEF 126 countries energy security report released. What is India’s rank in this report

(A) ** 90

(B) 100

(C) 35

(D) 72

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is 87th. India has been ranked at the 87th position among 115 countries in the Energy Transition Index (ETI) that tracks nations on the current performance of their energy systems across various aspects, according to a World Economic Forum (WEF) report released.

Concept note-2: -India ranks 3rd in renewable energy country attractive index in 2021. The country has set an ambitious target to achieve a capacity of 175 GW worth of renewable energy by the end of 2022, which expands to 500 GW by 2030. This is the world’s largest expansion plan is in renewable energy.

Concept note-3: -Sweden leads the global rankings, followed by Norway and Denmark.
