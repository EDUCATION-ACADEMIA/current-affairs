What does NIMZ stand for, with regard to the Delhi Mumbai Industrial Corridor

(A) National Indian Manufacturing Zone

(B) ** National Investment and Manufacturing Zones

(C) National Incentive and Manufacturing Zone

(D) None of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Investment & Manufacturing Zones (NIMZs) are one of the important instruments of the policy. Government has already granted final approval to the NIMZ in Sangareddy district, Telangana.

Concept note-2: -The National Investment and Manufacturing Zone (NIMZ) in Zaheerabad, Telangana, India, is a strategically located zone that offers a wide range of advantages for businesses looking to set up operations in southern India. One of the key advantages of the NIMZ is its proximity to key markets.

Concept note-3: -To enable the NIMZ to function as a self governing and autonomous body, it will be declared by the State Government as an Industrial Township under Art 243 Q(c) of the Constitution.
