What is Expansion of NEET?

(A) ** National Eligibility Entrance Test

(B) Nation Entrance Eligibility Test

(C) Both A

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEET stands for National Eligibility cum Entrance Test.

Concept note-2: -NEET has almost become synonymous with MBBS. It is a flagship course that medicine offers. The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test assesses students’ knowledge for their entrance to medical institutes across the country.

Concept note-3: -It is mandatory to give NEET to become a doctor. You must qualify NEET exam even for private medical colleges. Since Health Ministry has scrapped all other exams like AIIMS and JIPMER as well, not NEET is the only entrance exam and you need atleast qualifying marks to get admission in MBBS anywhere in India.

Concept note-4: -The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (Undergraduate) or NEET (UG), formerly the All India Pre-Medical Test (AIPMT), is an all India pre-medical entrance test for students who wish to pursue undergraduate medical (MBBS), dental (BDS) and AYUSH (BAMS, BUMS, BHMS, etc.)

Concept note-5: -NEET PG: NEET Postgraduate Examination, which is also known as the Common Entrance Test (CET), and is intended for admission to post-graduate medical courses offered by various medical institutes or colleges in India.
