What is the Full form of RDEL

(A) ** Reliance Defence and Engineering Ltd

(B) Reliance Direct Exchange Line

(C) Reliance Departmental Expenditure Limit

(D) Reliance Data Evaluation Laboratory

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliance Defence And Engineering Ltd Live Stock Price, Rdel Live Share Price, 533107 | BSE. BSE BEAM. BSE SSE.

Concept note-2: -Capital departmental expenditure limit (CDEL) The Department of Health (DH) sets a capital departmental expenditure limit (CDEL), which covers the capital spend of NHS trusts and is used by DH and HMT to monitor and manage capital expenditure within the sector.

Concept note-3: -Resource Spending is the spending on day-to-day running costs of government programmes and administration.

Concept note-4: -As per the spending guidelines, ministries and departments are required to make monthly or quarterly expenditure plans and cap it at 25% of Budget Estimates in each of the first three quarters.
