What is the India’s rank in female literacy as per International Commission on Financing Global Education Opportunity

(A) 41

(B) 67

(C) 23

(D) ** 38

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Of the 77.7% Indian literacy rate in 2022, male literacy rate stands at 84.7% and female literacy rate stands at 70.3% as compared to global average female literacy rate of 79% (as per UNESCO).

Concept note-2: -Literacy Rate of Women in India According to a report published by the National Statistical Office (NSO), the female literacy rate in India is approximately 70.3 per cent.

Concept note-3: -The results of 2011 census reveal that there has been an increase in literacy in the country. The literacy rate in the country is 74.04 per cent, 82.14 for males and 65.46 for females.

Concept note-4: -68.9% Hint:Literacy rate is defined as the percentage of population in any particular age group who can read and write.
