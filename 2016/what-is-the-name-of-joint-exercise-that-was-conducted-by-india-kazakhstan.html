What is the name of Joint Exercise, that was conducted by India, Kazakhstan

(A) ** PRABAL DOSTYK-16

(B) SARGN-15

(C) YRTEW -56

(D) KERT-19

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Exercise KazInd 2022 is the 6th edition of the Indo-Kazakhstan Joint exercise and is aimed at building trust and understanding of each other in the field of national security.

Concept note-2: -KazInd 2022: India and Kazakhstan carry out joint military drill in Meghalaya. To strengthen positive military relations between India and Kazakhstan, the nations conducted their 6th edition of Army Joint Exercise KazInd 2022 in Meghalaya’s Umroi.

Concept note-3: -The exercise ‘Veer Guardian-2023’ involving the Indian Air Force (IAF) and Japan Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) will be held at Japan’s Hyakuri air base. The IAF said on Saturday that its deployment at the exercise will include four Su-30 MKI jets, two C-17 aircraft and one IL-78 plane.

Concept note-4: -Garuda is a joint exercise between India and France Air Force that will provide a platform for both countries to enhance operational capability and interoperability, while also sharing best practices.

Concept note-5: -Ex KazInd, an annual Exercise between Indian Army and Kazakhstan Army was conducted at Foreign Training Node, Umroi.
