What is the rank of India in 2016 Networked Readiness Index (NRI) by the Geneva-based World Economic Forum (WEF)

(A) 41st

(B) 72nd

(C) 59th

(D) ** 91st

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has improved its position by six slots and is now placed at 61st rank as per the Network Readiness Index 2022 (NRI 2022) report released recently.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 Network Readiness Index (NRI), which reveals the most network-ready economies in the world, will be launched on November 15, 2022. The NRI is the worldwide guide to most tech enabled countries and reveals which country is at the top of the table and which ones are among fastest movers in the ‘new digital era’.

Concept note-3: -Oxford Insights has published its AI Readiness Index 2022. India is ranked at 32nd position among 181 nations in the report. The country has improved its previous year’s ranking by 19 places.

Concept note-4: -NRI Institute of Technology is ranked number 23817 in the world for its impact it produces.
