What is the rank of India in Global Competitiveness Report (GCR) 2016-2017

(A) 16

(B) ** 39

(C) 56

(D) 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India was ranked 58th with a score of 62.0 in the latest Global Competitiveness Report. “This is the largest gain among all G20 economies, ” WEF said. Agencies India remained the “South Asia’s main driving force".

Concept note-2: -The World Economic Forum’s (WEF) annual Global Competitiveness Index saw India jumping 16 places in 2016-17-the highest leap by any country. It now ranks 39 among the 138 economies that were surveyed.

Concept note-3: -India has come up on 37th rank in 2022 from 43rd in 2021 in the World Competitive Index.

Concept note-4: -Economic performance: It has improved from 37th in 2021 to 28th in 2022. Government efficiency: It has improved from 46th in 2021 to 45th in 2022. Business efficiency: It saw a huge improvement from 32nd rank in 2021 to 23rd in 2022. Infrastructure: It on the other hand, saw no change at 49.

Concept note-5: -India 54th On Global Travel Development Index, Top-Ranked In South Asia.
