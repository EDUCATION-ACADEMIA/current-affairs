What is the theme for International Day of Yoga 2016

(A) ** Connect the Youth

(B) Connect the People

(C) Connect the Heart

(D) Connect the World

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IDY-2016. The second International Day of Yoga was held around the theme ‘ Yoga for the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals. ‘ Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi led a mass Yoga demonstration at Chandigarh, where more than 30, 000 people participated.

Concept note-2: -The theme for 2017 was “Yoga for Health".

Concept note-3: -The theme for International Day of Yoga 2022 is “Yoga for humanity”.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi is leading the International Day of Yoga celebrations from the heritage city of Mysuru in Karnataka. More than 15, 000 people are participate in the Yoga celebrations along with the Prime Minister at the Mysore Palace grounds.

Concept note-5: -The theme of International Yoga Day 2023 is “Humanity.” Past themes include “Yoga for Heart, ” “Yoga for Peace, ” and “Yoga at Home… Yoga with Family.”
