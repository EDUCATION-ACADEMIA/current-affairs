What is the theme for the 2016 International Anti-Corruption Day

(A) Break the Corruption Chain

(B) ** United against corruption for development, peace and security.

(C) Corruption: An impediment to the Sustainable Development Goals

(D) ACT - Against Corruption Today

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of the 2017 International Anti-Corruption Day was ‘United against corruption for development, peace and security’. The theme of 2016 International Anti-Corruption Day was “Solidarity on Corruption Against Development, Peace and Security".

Concept note-2: -The theme for International Anti-Corruption Day 2022 is “Uniting the world against corruption.” This year marks the 20th anniversary of the United Nations Convention Against Corruption (UNCAC).

Concept note-3: -International Anti-Corruption Day 2022: Stop kleptocrats and protect the common good.

Concept note-4: -The Assembly also designated 9 December as International Anti-Corruption Day, to raise awareness of corruption and of the role of the Convention in combating and preventing it. The Convention entered into force in December 2005.

Concept note-5: -1-Which United Nations organization organized International Anti-Corruption Day? The United Nations Development Program organizes it.
