What is the theme for the Global Media and Information Literacy Week 2016

(A) Celebrating Connectivity across Cultures

(B) ** New Paradigms for Intercultural Dialogue

(C) Media and Information Literacy for the Sustainable Development Goals

(D) Make Media and Information Literacy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Media and Information Literacy: New Paradigms for Intercultural Dialogue.

Concept note-2: -Theme: “Nurturing trust: A Media and Information Literacy Imperative".

Concept note-3: -The theme of Global MIL Week 2022 is “Nurturing trust – A Media and Information Literacy Imperative”.

Concept note-4: -Media and information literacy empowers people to think critically about information and use of digital tools. It helps people make informed choices about how they participate in peace building, equality, freedom of expression, dialogue, access to information, and sustainable development.

Concept note-5: -Global Media and Information Literacy (MIL) Week 2022 is observed from October 24 to October 31. The Global MIL Week 2022 focuses on trust and solidarity as it relates to people, media, digital platforms, governments, the private sector, and non-governmental organizations.
