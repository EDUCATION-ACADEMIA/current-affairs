What is the theme for United Nations Day 2016

(A) One Humanity, Shared Responsibility

(B) Strong UN. Better World

(C) ** Freedom First

(D) Solutions for a Prosperous World

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of the 2016 International Youth Day is “The Road to 2030: Eradicating Poverty and Achieving Sustainable Consumption and Production”. This year’s Day is about achieving the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

Concept note-2: -Nourishing Peace, UNA-USA’s theme for UN Day 2022, aims to address how food security and peace are intertwined on the international scale.

Concept note-3: -The themes of international days are always linked to the main fields of action of the United Nations, namely the maintenance of international peace and security, the promotion of sustainable development, the protection of human rights, and the guarantee of international law and humanitarian action.

Concept note-4: -UN Day marks the anniversary of the entry into force in 1945 of the UN Charter. With the ratification of this founding document by the majority of its signatories, including the five permanent members of the Security Council, the United Nations officially came into being.

Concept note-5: -Peace, dignity and equality on a healthy planet.
