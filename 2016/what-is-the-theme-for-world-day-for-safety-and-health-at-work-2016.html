What is the theme for World Day for Safety and Health at Work 2016

(A) Safety and health in the use of chemicals at work

(B) The prevention of occupational diseases

(C) Join in building an OSH preventive culture

(D) ** Workplace Stress: a collective challenge

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year, “Workplace Stress: a collective challenge” is the theme of the campaign of the World Day for Safety and Health at Work.

Concept note-2: -The World Day for Safety and Health at Work 2022 focuses on enhancing social dialogue towards a culture of safety and health.

Concept note-3: -It is in this context that ‘Medication safety’ has been selected as the theme for World Patient Safety Day 2022, with the slogan ‘Medication without harm’.

Concept note-4: -The annual World Day for Safety and Health at Work on 28 April promotes the prevention of occupational accidents and diseases globally.

Concept note-5: -The World Day for Safety and Health at Work is celebrated annually on 28 April to promote the prevention of occupational accidents and diseases globally.
