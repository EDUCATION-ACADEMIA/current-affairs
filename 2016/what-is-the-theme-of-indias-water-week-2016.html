What is the theme of India’s Water Week 2016

(A) Water for All: Striving Together

(B) Water Management for Sustainable Development

(C) ** Water for all: Striving together

(D) Efficient Water management: Challenges and Opportunities

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for IWW-2022 is “Water Security for Sustainable Development with Equity” focusing on various aspects of water security and management and related challenges for equitable development.

Concept note-2: -THEME FOR IWW 2022: “WATER SECURITY FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT AND EQUITY”

Concept note-3: -The President of India, Smt. Droupadi Murmu inaugurated the 7th India Water Week today at the India Expo Centre in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, in august presence of Governor, Uttar Pradesh, Smt.

Concept note-4: -India Water Week to Be Celebrated from 1st-5th November, 2022. Aim: To raise awareness, conserve and use water resources in an integrated manner, Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation, is organizing the 7th edition of IWW. Theme: “Water Security for Sustainable Development and Equity”.
