What is the theme of International Mother Earth Day 2016

(A) It’s Our Turn to Lead

(B) Green Cities because in the present world

(C) Tomorrow’s World

(D) ** Trees for the Earth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year the selected subject are the trees. Under the slogan “Trees for the Earth, ” Earth Day Network, organizer of the international event, wants to highlight the importance of trees for the survival of all living beings on the planet.

Concept note-2: -Happy Earth Day 2017! International Mother Earth Day is celebrated on April 22 every year, this year’s theme focusing on ‘Environmental & Climate Literacy’.

Concept note-3: -Earth Day 2015 To recognize the 45th anniversary of Earth Day, the 2015 global theme was termed “It’s Our Turn to Lead."

Concept note-4: -The Earth Day 2022 Theme is Invest In Our Planet.

Concept note-5: -WASHINGTON – The global theme for Earth Day 2013 is “The Face of Climate Change, ” Earth Day Network announced today.
