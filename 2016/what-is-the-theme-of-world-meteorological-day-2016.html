What is the theme of World Meteorological Day 2016

(A) Watching the Weather to Protect Life and Property

(B) ** Hotter, Drier, Wetter - Face the Future

(C) Weather and climate: engaging youth

(D) Weather, climate and the air we breathe

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Each year, on 23 March, Singapore joins the other members of WMO to celebrate World Meteorological Day around a chosen theme. The theme for 2016 is: “Hotter, Drier, Wetter. Face the Future.”

Concept note-2: -Understanding Clouds is the theme of World Meteorological Day 2017 to highlight the enormous importance of clouds for weather climate and water.

Concept note-3: -I am therefore very happy that the theme of World Meteorological Day 2022 is “Early Warning and Early Action.” It celebrates the great achievements of national meteorological and hydrological services in improved early warning systems.

Concept note-4: -The day is celebrated to commemorate the coming into existence of the World Meteorological Organization on March 23, 1950. Since 1961, the day has been celebrated with a different theme each year. For 2022, the theme is “early warning and early action.”

Concept note-5: -What is the theme of World Meteorological Day 2023? The theme of World Meteorological Day 2023 is “The Future of Weather, Climate and Water across Generations”.
