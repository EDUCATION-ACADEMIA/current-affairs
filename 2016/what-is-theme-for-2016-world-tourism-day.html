What is theme for 2016 World Tourism Day

(A) Tourism Linking Cultures

(B) ** Tourism for All - promoting universal accessibility.

(C) One billion tourist’s one billion opportunities

(D) Tourism and Energetic Sustainability

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For this year’s World Tourism Day theme “Tourism for All – Promoting Universal Accessibility”, it is a conscious reminder to us all around the world to push for integration in order to improve each other’s potential in tourism and to promote a mutual understanding of different cultures and traditions.

Concept note-2: -World Tourism Day 2022: The theme of this year is “ Rethinking Tourism". The aim is to rethink tourism for development, including through education and jobs, and tourism’s impact on the planet and opportunities to grow more sustainably.

Concept note-3: -World Tourism Day 2022: Theme World Tourism Day 2022’s theme is “Rethinking Tourism.” In order to capitalize on this chance to rethink how we do tourism in order to create a more sustainable, inclusive, and resilient industry, Indonesia will be the host nation for this year’s official celebration.

Concept note-4: -’Rethinking Tourism” is the theme for World Tourism Day 2022.
