What is theme for the the World Habitat Day 2016

(A) Voices from Slums

(B) Shelter is My Right

(C) Cities without Slums

(D) ** Housing at the Centre

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Habitat Day is celebrated on the first Monday of October every year. The event focuses on the state of human settlements and on the basic right of all to adequate shelter. The theme of World Habitat Day 2016 was ‘Housing at the Centre’.

Concept note-2: -In 2022, World Habitat Day (WHD 2022) under the theme “Mind the Gap. Leave No One and Place Behind” looks at the problem of growing inequality and challenges in cities and human settlements.

Concept note-3: -World Habitat Day 2012 was held on 1 October 2012, under the theme – Changing Cities, Building Opportunities.

Concept note-4: -The topic of World Habitat Day 2023 will be linked to resilient urban economies: cities as drivers of growth and recovery and, in 2024, to engaging youth to create a better urban future.
