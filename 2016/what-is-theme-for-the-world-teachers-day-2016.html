What is theme for the World Teachers’ Day 2016

(A) ** Valuing Teachers, Improving their Status.

(B) Empowering Teachers, building sustainable societies

(C) The new generation will be created by teachers

(D) Nation building passes through teachers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme this year focuses on “Valuing teachers, improving their status”.

Concept note-2: -World Teachers Day 2015: Empowering teachers, building sustainable societies. The Malta Union of Teachers is commemorating World Teachers Day 2015, held annually on 5th October, with the theme “Empowering teachers, building sustainable societies”.

Concept note-3: -In 2022, the celebrations of World Teachers’ Day will focus on the theme “The transformation of education begins with teachers".

Concept note-4: -The theme of National Teachers’ Day 2022 is “Teachers: leading in crisis, reimagining the future”. The theme of National Teacher’s Day 2022 highlights that during crisis Teachers always lead and reimagine the future.

Concept note-5: -For this year’s celebration, the United Nations Educational, Scientific, and Cultural Organization and Education International came up with the theme: Recovery begins with teachers, placing teachers in the lead to recovery from various global crises.
