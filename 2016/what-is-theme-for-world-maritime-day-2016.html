What is theme for World Maritime Day 2016

(A) Maritime education and training

(B) ** shipping: indispensable to the world

(C) IMO conventions: effective implementation

(D) Sustainable development: IMO’s contribution beyond Rio+20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thank you for celebrating World Maritime Day 2022 with us. Our theme for the year has been “New technologies for greener shipping".

Concept note-2: -With the theme “Your voyage – then and now, share your adventure, ” the Day of the Seafarers campaign for 2022 will examine seafarer voyages, including what they entail, how they have changed over time, and what has remained central to seafarers’ reality.

Concept note-3: -World Maritime Day was celebrated for the first time on March 17, 1978.

Concept note-4: -Since 1964, National Maritime Day has been celebrated in India on April 5. Indian National Maritime Day is celebrated on April 5 because, on this date in 1919, SS Loyalty, the first Indian flag merchant ship, sailed from Mumbai to London. Scindia Steam Navigation Company Ltd was the owner of SS Loyalty.
