What percentage of India’s coffee export in 2015-2016

(A) 13.38 %

(B) ** 13.39 %

(C) 13.37 %

(D) 13.36 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The country exports over 70% of its production. In 2021-22, the total exports recorded a 42% rise to US$ 1.04 billion from the previous year. In March 2022, exports of coffee were valued at US$ 114.7 million, a 22% growth from February 2022.

Concept note-2: -Coffee consumption and growth in India It is projected to grow to Rs 32, 000 crore (approximately US$ 4.05 billion) in 2027. Indian consumers are embracing international coffee cafe culture with cafe chains growing rapidly.

Concept note-3: -Beans are often kept and transported in coarse hessian bags. Beans are also shipped in bulk using bulk containers with plastic liners. On arrival in the destination country, the shipments are sent to warehouses or direct to the roaster.
