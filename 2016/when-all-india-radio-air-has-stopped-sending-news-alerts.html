When All India Radio (AIR) has stopped sending news alerts

(A) ** April 5

(B) April 6

(C) April 7

(D) April 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Out of these, 89 bulletins are broadcast daily from Delhi in the Home Service in English, Hindi and other Indian languages. The 44 Regional News Units (RNUs) putout 355 daily news bulletins in 67 languages. This includes news bulletins mounted exclusively on FM ‘Gold’ channel from 22 AIR Stations.

Concept note-2: -Director General (News) All India Radio Dr. Vasudha Gupta has been given additional charge of Director General of AIR. A 1989-batch Indian Information Service Officer, Dr. Gupta has served in various capacities in the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting in an over 32 year long career.
