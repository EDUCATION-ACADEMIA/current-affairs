When is the Day of Russia celebrated

(A) June 10

(B) June 11

(C) ** June 12

(D) June 13

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It has been celebrated annually on 12 June since 1992. The day commemorates the adoption of the Declaration of State Sovereignty of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic (RSFSR) on 12 June 1990.

Concept note-2: -Holidays Today in Russia There are no holidays in Russia today.

Concept note-3: -Day of Conception (also known as Procreation Day) is a Russian Holiday made popular by the region of Ulyanovsk, birthplace of Vladimir Lenin. The day itself takes place on September 12th, and couples who then have a child on June 12th are rewarded by the regional government.

Concept note-4: -June 12, Independence Day – The adoption of The Declaration of Sovereignty of the Russian Federation in 1991 is celebrated today.
