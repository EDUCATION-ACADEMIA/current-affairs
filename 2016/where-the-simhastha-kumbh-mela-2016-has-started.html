Where the Simhastha Kumbh Mela 2016 has started

(A) Nasik

(B) Haridwar

(C) Prayag

(D) ** Ujjain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Simhastha Kumbh is a celebration like no other in India. It is celebrated when Jupiter ascends into sun sign Leo’s quarter or the Simha constellation of the zodiac, which is why it is called Simhastha.

Concept note-2: -Prayag in modern-day Allahabad is believed to be the most important pilgrimage centre for Hindus.

Concept note-3: -2023 is going to be the year of Magh Kumbh. Magh Kumbh is believed by the Hindus to be the origin of the Universe and is, therefore, an auspicious occasion. In 2023, Magh Kumbh Mela has been organized at Prayag. It will be held for the usual 45 days this year, from 6 January to 18 February.
