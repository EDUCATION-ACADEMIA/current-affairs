Which bank has has launched cheaper home loan schemes for defence and other government employees under the 7th Pay Commission

(A) Axis Bank

(B) ** SBI Bank

(C) ICICI Bank

(D) HDFC Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1. Which bank takes the lowest interest rate on home loans? HSBC provides people with the lowest interest rate on home loans which range from 6.85% to 7.5 % p.a.

Concept note-2: -State Bank of India (SBI) offers Shaurya Home Loan scheme to the army and other defense personnel. The interest rates of SBI Shaurya Home Loan start from 8.60% p.a. for tenures of up to 30 years.

Concept note-3: -For central government and state government employees, the House Building Advance (HBA) scheme provides a home loan for the construction of a new home. Previously, government employees were entitled to take an HBA of up to INR 7.5 lacs. However, due to recent policy changes, you can now get a loan of up to INR 25 lacs.
