Which bank will become the India’s first Small Finance Bank (SFB)

(A) Au Financiers Ltd

(B) ** Capital Local Area Bank

(C) ESAF Microfinance and Investments Private Ltd

(D) Disha Microfin Private Ltd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is option 3, i.e. Capital Small Finance Bank.

Concept note-2: -Capital Small Finance Bank (erstwhile Capital Local Area Bank) is the India’s first small finance bank founded in April 2016 as a microfinance lender, with its headquarters in Jalandhar, India.

Concept note-3: -Equitas Small Finance Bank is a New Age Bank that gives children, youth, families and business people across India a new way to bank.

Concept note-4: -Equitas Small Finance Bank The company has a market capitalization of ₹6205 cr and has made a profit of ₹281 cr in 2022. It has a P/E Ratio (TTM ) of 15.83 and an EPS (TTM) of 3.05.
