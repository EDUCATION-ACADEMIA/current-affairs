Which city became the first Indian city to host the 2017 FIFA U-17 World Cup

(A) New Delhi

(B) ** Kochi

(C) Bhubaneshwar

(D) Ahmedabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The matches were played in six stadiums in six host cities around the country, with the final taking place at the Salt Lake Stadium in Kolkata.

Concept note-2: -In October 2017, England were crowned world champions at Under-17 level after beating Spain 5-2 in the final. Players like Jadon Sancho and Phil Foden were arguably the standout performers of that England U17 team and have since become stars in their own right.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Goa. Goa has been provisionally selected as a venue for the FIFA under 17 World Cup 2017.

Concept note-4: -Get group standings and semi-finals, third-place playoff and final scorecards. The FIFA U-17 Women’s World Cup 2022 was hosted in India from October 11 to 30. The seventh edition of the FIFA U-17 Women’s World Cup was initially scheduled for 2020 but was then postponed due to COVID-19.
