Which city is planning to build tower taller than Burjkhalifa

(A) Singapore

(B) Malaysia

(C) ** Dubai

(D) New York

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Located on the north side of Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, it is planned to be the first 1 km (3, 281 ft) tall building, and the centrepiece and first phase of a development and tourist attraction known as Jeddah Economic City.

Concept note-2: -The Jeddah Tower: Saudi Arabia aims for the sky With a structure that evokes a bundle of leaves shooting up from the ground, the Kingdom Jeddah Tower will surpass by nearly 200 metres the Burj Khalifa, which has held the title as the world’s tallest free-standing structure since it opened in Dubai in 2010.

Concept note-3: -The second tallest building in India presently is World View. Both of these buildings are located in Mumbai, and they have 76 floors.
