Which city will host the 2016 Indian Super League (ISL) Opening Ceremony

(A) Delhi

(B) Margao

(C) Kochi

(D) ** Guwahati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISL 2016 opening ceremony will be held on October 1 in Guwahati, home venue for NorthEast United FC . The 2016 season of the Indian Super League (ISL) is all set to commence.

Concept note-2: -At the end of the season, Atlético de Kolkata came out as champions after defeating the Kerala Blasters in a penalty shootout, 4–3, during the final.

Concept note-3: -ISL 2022-23 Schedule: Indian Super League to start with Kerala Blasters hosting East Bengal FC-Sportstar.

Concept note-4: -ISL Golden Boot winner 2016 – Marcelinho Though his team was knocked out of the semi-finals through penalties, Marcelinho emerged as the ISL top scorer for the season with 10 goals from 15 matches.

Concept note-5: -Champions in 2021-22, Hyderabad FC are the latest addition in the ISL winners list. The eighth edition of the Indian Super League (ISL) concluded with Hyderabad FC beating Kerala Blasters in the final to clinch the ISL 2021-22 title.
