Which city will host the two day national conference on labour

(A) Hyderabad

(B) Jaipur

(C) New Delhi

(D) ** Bhubaneswar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The two-day Conference is being organised by the Union Ministry of Labour & Employment on 25-26 August, 2022 at Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Which city is the host of ‘National Conference of Labour Ministers’ in 2022? Notes: The ‘National Conference of Labour Ministers’ is being organised by the Union Ministry of Labour & Employment at Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Labour Conference (ILC) is the apex level tripartite consultative committee in the Ministry of Labour & Employment to advise the Government on the issues concerning working class of the country.

Concept note-4: -The first meeting of the Indian Labour Conference (then called Tripartite National Labour Conference) was held in 1942 and so far a total of 46 Sessions have been held.
