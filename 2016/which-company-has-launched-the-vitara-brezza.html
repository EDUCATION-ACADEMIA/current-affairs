Which company has launched the Vitara Brezza

(A) Mahindra and Mahindra

(B) Tata Motors

(C) ** Maruti Suzuki

(D) Hyundai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maruti Suzuki, the country’s largest carmaker, on Thursday unveiled the all-new Brezza priced in the range of ₹7.99 lakh-₹ 13.96 lakh (ex-showroom).

Concept note-2: -Maruti Suzuki introduced its first sub-four metre offering, the Vitara Brezza, in the Indian market in 2016. The brand had carried out the entire research and development process of the SUV in the country.

Concept note-3: -The Vitara Brezza is Maruti Suzuki’s sub-compact SUV, which is under 4m in length and competes with Mahindra TUV 300 and Ford Eco Sport. This car is more of a crossover than an actual SUV.

Concept note-4: -2022 Maruti Suzuki Brezza Pricing The new Maruti Suzuki Brezza starts at Rs 7, 99 lakh onwards, while the automatic variants start at Rs 10.96 lakh onwards. Also, the new Brezza will be available on subscription basis as well, priced at Rs 18, 300 per month.

Concept note-5: -If you remember, Toyota also sells Brezza under the name Urban Cruiser. Toyota has not launched the updated or facelift version of Urban Cruiser in the market yet. There are reports that Toyota will not be launching a 2022 version of Urban Cruiser.
