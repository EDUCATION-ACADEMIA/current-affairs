Which company is associated with ’My Stamp’ released by India Post

(A) BHEL

(B) ** HAL

(C) Air India Limited

(D) Biecco Lawrie

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’My Stamp’ is the brand name for personalized sheets of Postage Stamps of India Post.

Concept note-2: -The postage stamp, which is the legally valid receipt for postage paid, can be released by either the Minister for Communications or Minister of State for Communications.

Concept note-3: -The stamp features a profile of Queen Victoria. distance. February 1901.

Concept note-4: -A philatelic cover is an envelope or post card prepared with a stamp(s) and address and sent through the mail delivery system for the purpose of creating a collectible item.
