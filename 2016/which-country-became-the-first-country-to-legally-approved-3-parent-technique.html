Which country became the first country to legally approved 3-parent technique

(A) USA

(B) France

(C) Germany

(D) ** UK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Britain became the first country in the world to allow the creation of so-called “three-parent” babies as MPs voted overwhelmingly in favour of the controversial technique of mitochondrial donation.

Concept note-2: -LONDON: Britain today created history by becoming the first country to legalise children conceived with DNA from three parents after lawmakers voted in favour of the controversial procedure that sparked a fierce ethical debate with senior Church figures.

Concept note-3: -Britain’s fertility regulator on Thursday granted doctors the first UK license to create babies using a three-parent IVF technique designed to prevent inherited genetic diseases.

Concept note-4: -Britain has become the first country in the world to permit the use of “three-person IVF” to prevent incurable genetic diseases.

Concept note-5: -three-parent baby, human offspring produced from the genetic material of one man and two women through the use of assisted reproductive technologies, specifically mitochondrial replacement therapy (MRT) and three-person in vitro fertilization (IVF).
