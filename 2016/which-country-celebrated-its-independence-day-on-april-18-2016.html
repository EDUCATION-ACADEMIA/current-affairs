Which country celebrated its Independence Day on April 18, 2016

(A) ** Zimbabwe

(B) Ireland

(C) Israel

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Zimbabwean independence is celebrated on 18 April each year and Robert Mugabe has been at the helm since 1980.

Concept note-2: -Latvia Independence Day is on November 18. On this day in 1918, Latvia declared its independence from Russian occupation after World War I. But who knew that the country would need to do it all over again 72 years later?
