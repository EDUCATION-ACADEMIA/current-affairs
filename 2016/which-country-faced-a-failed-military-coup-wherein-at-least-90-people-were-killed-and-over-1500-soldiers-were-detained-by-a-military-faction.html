Which country faced a failed military coup wherein at least 90 people were killed and over 1500 soldiers were detained by a military faction

(A) ** Turkey

(B) Afghanistan

(C) Iraq

(D) Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A coup d’état was attempted in Guinea-Bissau on 1 February 2022. A few hours later, president Umaro Sissoco Embaló declared the coup over, he said that “many” members of the security forces had been killed in a “failed attack against democracy."

Concept note-2: -Failed coup. No regime change, such as when a leader is illegally shuffled out of power without changing the identity of the group in power or the rules for governing. Replacement of incumbent with another dictatorship.
