Which country has been named the most peaceful country in the World

(A) Switzerland

(B) Denmark

(C) Austria

(D) ** Iceland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the Global Peace Index 2022, Iceland was the most peaceful country in the world with an index value of 1.1.

Concept note-2: -Japan has consistently followed the path of a peace-loving nation since the end of World War II, and has been achieving peace and stability in the Asia-Pacific region and the international community. Japan’s security policy is grounded on this path.

Concept note-3: -The country ranks high in social stability, equality, democracy, and more. Even the electricity energy mix is almost entirely renewable. One factor that puts it so high on the Peace Index is that it is without a standing army. Iceland has no army, navy, or air force.
