Which country has commemorated the 30th anniversary of the nuclear disaster at Chernobyl on April 26, 2016

(A) France

(B) Germany

(C) Japan

(D) ** Ukraine

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ukraine has commemorated the 30th anniversary of the Chernobyl nuclear disaster.

Concept note-2: -On 8 December 2016 the United Nations General Assembly adopted a resolution designating 26 April as International Chernobyl Disaster Remembrance Day.

Concept note-3: -Between March 1990 and June 1991, 200 experts from the Soviet Union and 24 other countries conducted 50 field missions to examine the area. A special task force-under which the Chernobyl fund would be established-was created in 1992 to coordinate international efforts. Don’t miss what matters.

Concept note-4: -1 – Sweden actually sent the first alert After the explosion, the first westerners to know were Swedish Nuclear plant workers whose sensor’s read high levels of radiation. Sweden sent out the first alert that something was happening.
