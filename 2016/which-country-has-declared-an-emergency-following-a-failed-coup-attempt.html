Which country has declared an emergency following a failed coup attempt

(A) Indonesia

(B) ** Turkey

(C) Pakistan

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Many government buildings, including the Turkish Parliament and the Presidential Palace, were bombed from the air. Mass arrests followed, with at least 40, 000 detained, including at least 10, 000 soldiers and 2, 745 judges, for being affiliated with the coup attempt.

Concept note-2: -Failed coup. No regime change, such as when a leader is illegally shuffled out of power without changing the identity of the group in power or the rules for governing. Replacement of incumbent with another dictatorship.

Concept note-3: -A constitutional referendum was held in Turkey on 16 April 2017 on whether to approve 18 proposed amendments to the Turkish constitution that were brought forward by the governing Justice and Development Party (AKP) and the Nationalist Movement Party (MHP).
