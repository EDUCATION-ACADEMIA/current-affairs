Which country has given highest civilian award to Prime Minister Narendra Modi

(A) Nigeria

(B) Myanmar

(C) Bangladesh

(D) ** Saudi Arabia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PM Narendra Modi Honoured With Bhutan’s Highest Civilian Award Ngadag Pel gi Khorlo.

Concept note-2: -’Bharat Ratna’, the highest civilian Award of the country, was instituted in the year 1954. Any person without distinction of race, occupation, position or sex is eligible for these awards.

Concept note-3: -In the order of rank, Padma Shri is the fourth highest civilian award. This award is conferred to people for achievements in any field including the service rendered by the Government employees.
