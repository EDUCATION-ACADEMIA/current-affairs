Which country has invited Indian businesses to invest in the Central Asian country

(A) Kazakhstan

(B) Uzbekistan

(C) Tajikistan

(D) ** Kyrgyzstan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Subsequently, there have been exchanges at high-levels at bilateral and multilateral forums. The inception of the India-Central Asia Dialogue at Foreign Ministers’ level, the 3rd meeting of which was held in New Delhi from 18-20 December 2021, has provided an impetus to India-Central Asia relations.

Concept note-2: -Central Asia is of great strategic importance to India. There is enormous scope for pragmatic and profitable engagement between the two. The focus of relationship between the Central Asian Republics (CARs) and India can be defined by the importance of Central Asia for peace and stability in our region.
