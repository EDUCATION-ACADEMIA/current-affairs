Which country has launched first hack proof Quantum Communication Satellite on May 2016

(A) ** China

(B) US

(C) Japan

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -China has successfully launched the world’s first quantum-enabled satellite, state media said. It was carried on a rocket which blasted off from the Jiuquan Satellite Launch Centre in China’s north west early on Tuesday.

Concept note-2: -China says it has launched the world’s first quantum satellite, a project Beijing hopes will enable it to build a coveted “hack-proof” communications system with potentially significant military and commercial applications.

Concept note-3: -It is the world’s first quantum communications satellite, launched by China in 2016. The satellite serves as the source of pairs of entangled photons. Entangled photons are twinned light particles whose properties remain intertwined no matter how far apart they are.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 12. China has launched the first 6G satellite into space.

Concept note-5: -1988. Yoshihisa Yamamoto and K. Igeta propose the first physical realization of a quantum computer, including Feynman’s CNOT gate. Their approach uses atoms and photons and is the progenitor of modern quantum computing and networking protocols using photons to transmit qubits and atoms to perform two-qubit operations.
