Which country has recently signed a MoU with India on Renewable Energy Cooperation

(A) US

(B) China

(C) Russia

(D) ** UAE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union Cabinet, chaired by the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi was apprised of the signing of Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between India and French Republic in the field of renewable energy cooperation. The MoU was signed in January 2021.

Concept note-2: -India and Panama have signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) to encourage cooperation in the training of diplomats. The MoU between India and Panama was signed by the External Affairs Minister of India Dr. Subramanyam Jaishankar and the Foreign Minister of Panama Janaina Tewaney Mencomon.

Concept note-3: -As a follow up of Letter of Intent, an MoU between DoWR, RD & GR, Government of India and Ministry of Environment. Government of Denmark was signed on 12.09. 2022 during visit of Hon’ble Minister of Jal Shakti to Denmark.

Concept note-4: -HPCL signed MoU with Solar Energy Corporation of India.
