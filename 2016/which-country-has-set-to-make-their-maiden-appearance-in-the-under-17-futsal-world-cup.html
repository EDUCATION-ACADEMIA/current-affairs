Which country has set to make their maiden appearance in the Under 17 Futsal World Cup

(A) ** India

(B) Brazil

(C) Canada

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India will make its maiden appearance in the Under-17 Futsal World Cup in Paraguay after being invited by the sport’s governing body AMF through wild card.

Concept note-2: -The FIFA U-17 Women’s World Cup will be held in three different states in 2022. The designated venues are Kalinga Stadium in Bhubaneswar, Fatorda Stadium in Goa, and DY Patil Stadium in Navi Mumbai.

Concept note-3: -Kia has been an official sponsor of FIFA on numerous events, including the FIFA World Cup, since the beginning of the partnership in 2007. The FIFA U-17 Women’s World Cup 2022 will be held in India from October 11-30 with matches to be held in Navi Mumbai, Bhubaneshwar and Goa.

Concept note-4: -Rhian Brewster-FW Brewster won the Golden Boot award at the U17 World Cup as he scored eight goals in six matches, including a goal in the final.

Concept note-5: -16, 2022 4:52 p.m. Rabat-Morocco’s national futsal team today defeated Iran in the final of the 2022 Continental Futsal Championship, clinching a 3-4 victory in Bangkok, Thailand.
