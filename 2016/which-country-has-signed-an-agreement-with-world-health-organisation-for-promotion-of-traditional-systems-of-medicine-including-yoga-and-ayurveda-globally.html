[SOURCES]

Which country has signed an agreement with World Health Organisation for promotion of traditional systems of medicine, including Yoga and Ayurveda, globally.

(A) ** India

(B) US

(C) Russia

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Health Organization (WHO) and the Government of India today signed an agreement to establish the WHO Global Centre for Traditional Medicine.

Concept note-2: -Ayurveda is recognized in Nepal, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Sri-Lanka, UAE, Colombia, Malaysia, Switzerland, South Africa, Cuba, Tanzania. Romania, Hungary, Latvia, Serbia and Slovenia are 5 countries of European Union (EU), where Ayurvedic treatment is regulated.

Concept note-3: -The Prime Minister and Government of India are supporting the establishment of the WHO Global Centre for Traditional Medicine in Jamnagar, Gujarat, India, as a global good and in the spirit of Vasudhaiva Kudumbakam: the world is one family.

Concept note-4: -The World Health Organisation (WHO) has recognised Ayurveda as a traditional medicine system along with conventional medicine systems of other countries. The organisation realised the importance and role of the traditional medicinal system in the healthcare sector.

Concept note-5: -Nepal, Bhutan Nepal is the first country to execute a National Policy on Ayurveda.
