Which country has successfully launched pair two high-resolution remote sensing satellites SuperView-1 01 and 02 to get commercial images.

(A) Russia

(B) Japan

(C) USA

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is China. The pair of SuperView-1 remote sensing satellites were launched by China. SuperView-1 or GaoJing-1 constellation consists of four Chinese commercial remote sensing satellites operated by Beijing Space View Tech Co Ltd.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is China.

Concept note-3: -High spatial resolution remote sensing is an area of considerable current interest and builds on developments in object-based image analysis, commercial high-resolution satellite sensors, and UAVs. It captures more details through high and very high resolution images (10 to 100 cm/pixel).

Concept note-4: -30cm is the best spatial resolution option available today for remote sensing using high-resolution commercial satellites.

Concept note-5: -China launches new batch of three remote sensing satellites.
