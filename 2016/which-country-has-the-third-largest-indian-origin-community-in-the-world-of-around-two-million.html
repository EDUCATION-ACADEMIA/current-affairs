Which country has the third largest Indian-origin community in the world of around two million

(A) Saudi Arabia

(B) United States

(C) ** Malaysia

(D) Nepal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Almost 2 million more live in the U.S., making up the country’s third-largest immigrant group. Among Indian Americans, nearly nine-in-ten were born in India. As a whole, Indian Americans are among the highest educated and have some of the highest income among racial and ethnic groups in the U.S.

Concept note-2: -India’s large diaspora is distributed across the United Arab Emirates (3.5 million), the United States of America (2.7 million) and Saudi Arabia (2.5 million).
