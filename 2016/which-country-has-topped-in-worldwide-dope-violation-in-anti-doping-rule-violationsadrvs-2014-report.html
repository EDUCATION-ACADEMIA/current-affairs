Which country has topped in worldwide dope violation in Anti-Doping Rule Violations(ADRVs) 2014 report

(A) Australia

(B) China

(C) Brazil

(D) ** Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: India has been ranked 3rd for the third year in a row in the recently published Anti-Doping Rule Violations (ADRVs) report for year 2015. The report is compiled by the World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA).

Concept note-2: -The World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA; French: Agence mondiale antidopage, AMA) is a foundation initiated by the International Olympic Committee based in Canada to promote, coordinate, and monitor the fight against drugs in sports.

Concept note-3: -The term doping is widely used by organizations that regulate sporting competitions. The use of drugs to enhance performance is considered unethical, and is prohibited by most international sports organizations, including the International Olympic Committee.
