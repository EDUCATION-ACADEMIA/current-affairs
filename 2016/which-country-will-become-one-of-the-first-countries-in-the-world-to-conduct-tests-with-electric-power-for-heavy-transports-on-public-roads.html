Which country will become one of the first countries in the world to conduct tests with electric power for heavy transports on public roads

(A) ** Sweden

(B) Norway

(C) Switzerland

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With this, Sweden has become the first country in the world to have electric road .

Concept note-2: -The road was opened in June 2016 in Sandviken municipality near Gävle in central Sweden.

Concept note-3: -Delhi-Jaipur Expressway to turn India’s 1st ‘Electric highway’, trial run of e-vehicles begin.

Concept note-4: -The Atal Harit Vidyut Rashtriya Mahamarg (AHVRM) is named after former Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee. The total length of AHVRM will be nearly 500 kilometres. Currently, the world’s largest EV highway is situated in Berlin, Germany. This highway is 109 kilometres long.
