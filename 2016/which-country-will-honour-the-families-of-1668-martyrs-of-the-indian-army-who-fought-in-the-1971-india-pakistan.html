Which country will honour the families of 1668 martyrs of the Indian Army who fought in the 1971 India-Pakistan

(A) Nepal

(B) Bhutan

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) ** Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The USSR had played a critical role in supporting India during the 1971 India-Pakistan war. The United States, on the other hand, had backed Pakistan at the time and opposed the liberation of Bangladesh.

Concept note-2: -Bhairon Singh Rathore served in the Battle of Longewala in the 1971 India-Pakistan War and became a popular war hero.

Concept note-3: -Vijay Diwas or Victory Day is commemorated on December 16, marking the end of the 1971 Indo-Pak War and the liberation of Bangladesh. India declared victory on this day 51 years ago after Pakistan signed the Instrument of Surrender.
