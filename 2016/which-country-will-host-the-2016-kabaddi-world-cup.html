Which country will host the 2016 Kabaddi World Cup

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Thailand

(D) Mexico

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2016 Kabaddi World Cup was the third edition of the standard-style Kabaddi World Cup. It was organised by the International Kabaddi Federation and contested from 7 to 22 October 2016 in Ahmedabad, India. Twelve countries competed in the tournament. India won their 3rd World Cup by defeating Iran 38–29 in the final.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is India. The winners of the 2016 Kabaddi World Cup (standard style) were India. India has won all six Kabaddi world cups played till now. The 2016 Kabaddi World Cup (standard style) was held in Ahmedabad, India from 7 to 22 October 2016.

Concept note-3: -The 2016 World Kabaddi Cup was the sixth edition of the circle style World Kabaddi Cup, held from 4 November to 17 November 2016 with the Opening Ceremony on 3 November 2016 at the Nehru stadium Roopnagar. The tournament took place in Punjab, India.

Concept note-4: -The Kabaddi World Cup will be held in the West Midlands region of the United Kingdom. The Kabaddi World Cup 2025 will be hosted out of Asia for the first time announced by the World Kabbadi Federation (WKF).
