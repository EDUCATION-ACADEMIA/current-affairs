Which country will host the first BRICS film festival

(A) ** India

(B) Brazil

(C) China

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BRICS Film Festival was first organised by India in New Delhi from 2-6 September 2016, wherein twenty films (4 each from BRICS countries) were screened.

Concept note-2: -KOLKATA: ‘Aparajito’, the Anik Dutta film that is inspired by the story behind the making of Satyajit Ray’s ‘Pather Panchali’, has won the special jury award at the 2022 BRICS Film Festival.

Concept note-3: -The Jury made the selection for the awards under the five categories after examining the twenty films which were showcased for the Sixth BRICS Film Festival held alongside 52nd IFFI, Goa.

Concept note-4: -The BRICS countries, namely Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa, are considered the five foremost emerging economies in the world.

Concept note-5: -Brazil, Russia, India, China and South Africa, originally an informal group of the leading emerging economies of the early 2000s, have since experienced very different growth paths. Their significance in the global economy at a time of considerable geopolitical uncertainty remains an open question.
