Which country will hosting the third "Our Ocean" conference in September 2015-16

(A) Canada

(B) Australia

(C) Chile

(D) ** US

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In an effort to mobilise the international community and take tangible action to mitigate such pressures on the ocean, the President of the French Republic has decided to organise a One Planet Summit dedicated to the ocean, scheduled to take place in Brest on 9-11 February 2022.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 UN Ocean Conference, co-hosted by the Governments of Kenya and Portugal, will take place on 27 June – 1 July 2022, in Lisbon, Portugal. The overall theme of the Conference is: “Scaling up ocean action based on science and innovation for the implementation of Goal 14: stocktaking, partnerships and solutions.”

Concept note-3: -Since its inception in 2014, Our Ocean Conference has produced more than 1, 800 commitments worth nearly USD 108 billion.
