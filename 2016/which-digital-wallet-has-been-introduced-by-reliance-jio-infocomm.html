Which digital wallet has been introduced by Reliance Jio Infocomm

(A) ** JioMoney

(B) JioWallet

(C) JioIndia

(D) JioInfoWallet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Click on Finance tab and select the SIM associated with JioMoney wallet and initiate device binding.

Concept note-2: -JioMoney will also be accepted at thousands of offline stores across India.

Concept note-3: -Paytm becomes India’s first platform to accept international remittances directly into digital wallet. Ria’s network supports more than 3.6 billion bank accounts and 410 million mobile and virtual accounts.

Concept note-4: -JioMoney is an Indian mobile wallet. Users load money via their credit/debit card or bank account. Once loaded with credit, JioMoney can be used to make purchases online or in-store as well as send money to friends and family.

Concept note-5: -Reliance Jio Infocomm Ltd (RJIO), a subsidiary of Reliance Industries Limited, is a telecommunication service provider and mobile network operator. The company offers products such as sim cards, mobile devices and internet dongles.
