Which former London mayor was the favourite PM candidate and bowed out on June 1

(A) Sadiq Khan

(B) Tony Blair

(C) ** Boris Johnson

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current mayor is Sadiq Khan, who took office on 9 May 2016. The position was held by Ken Livingstone from the creation of the role on 4 May 2000 until he was defeated in May 2008 by Boris Johnson, who then also served two terms before being succeeded by Khan.

Concept note-2: -Sadiq Khan is the current Mayor.

Concept note-3: -Boris Johnson served as mayor of London from 1 May 2008 until 5 May 2016, being elected in 2008 and reelected in 2012. During his mayoralty, Johnson oversaw the preparations and hosting of the 2012 Summer Olympics and oversaw the cycle hire scheme.

Concept note-4: -London has one Mayor and 25 Assembly Members – who are elected by you. They work with London’s councils, central government and many others on different aspects of London life.
