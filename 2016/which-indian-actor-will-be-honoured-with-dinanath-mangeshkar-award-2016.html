Which Indian actor will be honoured with Dinanath Mangeshkar Award 2016

(A) ** Ranveer Singh

(B) Johnny levor

(C) Raj babbar

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ranveer Kapoor received the Deenanath Mangeshkar Award for 2016.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi will be honoured with the inaugural Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Award which is instituted in the memory of the veteran singer Lata Mangeskar.

Concept note-3: -2022: PM receives Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Award at a ceremony in Mumbai. The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi attended the Master Deenanath Mangeshkar Awards Ceremony in Mumbai today. On this occasion, Prime Minister was conferred 1st Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Award.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi was conferred with the first Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Award in Mumbai today.

Concept note-5: -Lata Mangeshkar was bestowed with the Dadasaheb Phalke Award in 1989. In 2001, she was awarded the Bharat Ratna, India’s highest civilian honour. The government of France conferred on her its highest civilian award (Officer of the Legion of Honour) in 2007.
