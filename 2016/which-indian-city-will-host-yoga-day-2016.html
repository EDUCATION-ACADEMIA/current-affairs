Which Indian city will host Yoga Day 2016

(A) Mumbai

(B) New Delhi

(C) ** Chandigarh

(D) Ranchi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IDY-2016. The second International Day of Yoga was held around the theme ‘ Yoga for the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals. ‘ Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi led a mass Yoga demonstration at Chandigarh, where more than 30, 000 people participated.

Concept note-2: -World Yoga Day 2017: PM Narendra Modi performs yoga with participants in Lucknow. It was a rainy start to the third edition of International Yoga Day.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi is leading the International Day of Yoga celebrations from the heritage city of Mysuru in Karnataka. More than 15, 000 people are participate in the Yoga celebrations along with the Prime Minister at the Mysore Palace grounds.

Concept note-4: -The theme of International Yoga Day 2015 was ‘Yoga for Harmony and Peace.
