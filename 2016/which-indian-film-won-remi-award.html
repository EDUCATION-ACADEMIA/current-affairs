Which Indian film won REMI Award

(A) Kathakali

(B) ** Kanavu variyam

(C) Thanga magan

(D) Vilambu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian American actress and filmmaker Lakshmi Devy has won the prestigious Gold Remy Award for Direction at the 54th World fest Houston International Film Festival. She was awarded for her film “When the Music Changes”.

Concept note-2: -The 51st International Film Festival of India has conferred Biswajit Chatterjee, veteran actor, producer of Hindi and Bengali cinema, with the Indian Personality of the Year Award. This award will be presented to him, when the National Film Awards are presented in March 2021.

Concept note-3: -The Dadasaheb Phalke Award is India’s highest award in the field of cinema. It is presented annually at the National Film Awards ceremony by the Directorate of Film Festivals, an organisation set up by the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting.

Concept note-4: -Mega Star Chiranjeevi receives Indian Film Personality of the Year Award for 2022 at the closing ceremony of 53rd edition of IFFI.
