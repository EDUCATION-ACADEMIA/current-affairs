Which is the highest Gallantry Wartime award in Indian Army

(A) ** Param Vir Chakra

(B) Ashoka Chakra

(C) Param Vishisht Seva Medal

(D) Maha Vir Chakra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Param Vir Chakra-Highest-military award in India. Awarded for national bravery in the presence of the enemy.

Concept note-2: -The Param Vir Chakra (PVC) is India’s highest military decoration, awarded for displaying distinguished acts of valour during wartime.

Concept note-3: -The Maha Vir Chakra is the second-highest military honorary award in India, after the Param Vir Chakra, and is awarded for acts of conspicuous gallantry in the presence of the enemy, whether on land, at sea, or in the air.

Concept note-4: -The Param Vir Chakra, The Maha Vir Chakra & The Vir Chakra.

Concept note-5: -1. The Param Vir Chakra (PVC) is India’s highest military decoration awarded for the highest degree of valour or self-sacrifice in the presence of the enemy, similar to the British Victoria Cross, US Medal of Honor, or French Legion of Honor or Russian Cross of St. George.
