Which is the National Day of Luxembourg

(A) June 22

(B) ** June 23

(C) June 24

(D) June 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The festivities all over the Grand-Duchy start on the eve of National day, June 22nd. Luxembourg City starts the celebrations with a solemn changing of the guard at the Grand-Ducal palace around 4:00 pm. In the evening a torchlight procession takes place throughout the capital city.

Concept note-2: -On 23 June, Luxembourg celebrates the Grand Duke’s birthday, which is also commonly referred to as the National Day. This celebration includes more formal and traditional acts, such as a military parade and a religious service called Te Deum.

Concept note-3: -National days on Fri Jun 23rd, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s National Take Your Dog to Work Day, National Porridge Day, National Let It Go Day, International Women in Engineering Day, International Widows Day… and much more!

Concept note-4: -Henri (French: Henri Albert Gabriel Félix Marie Guillaume, pronounced [ɑ̃ʀi]; German: Heinrich; born 16 April 1955) is the Grand Duke of Luxembourg. He has reigned since 7 October 2000.

Concept note-5: -The fireworks will take place at pont Adolphe and will light up the sky from 11pm for 17 minutes. Some of the best spots to watch the firework include place de la Constitution, the Viaduc or the Hollerich or Gare areas.
