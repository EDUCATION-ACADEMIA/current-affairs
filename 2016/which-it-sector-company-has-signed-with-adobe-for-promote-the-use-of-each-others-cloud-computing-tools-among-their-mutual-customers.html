Which IT Sector company has signed with Adobe for promote the use of each other’s cloud-computing tools among their mutual customers.

(A) Apple

(B) Google

(C) Intel

(D) ** Microsoft

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tech giants Microsoft and Adobe announced a major partnership to promote the use of each other’s cloud-computing tools among their mutual customers. Satya Nadella, Microsoft’s chief executive, called the deal a “massive mileston, ” in an appearance here with Shantanu Narayen, his Adobe counterpart.

Concept note-2: -Adobe on the AWS cloud allows for virtualizing and scaling those tools across campus environments.

Concept note-3: -By the 1990s, huge numbers of personal computers were being connected as the technology became more affordable. Until finally, in 1999, Salesforce became the first company to offer applications over the internet, heralding the arrival of Software as a Service.

Concept note-4: -On-Demand Self Service – A cloud computing service model by which a customer can provision additional cloud resources on-demand, without involving the service provider. Resources are typically provisioned through an online control panel.

Concept note-5: -Azure provides Adobe with a trusted, global cloud and a powerful data platform for intelligent services, including comprehensive machine learning and cognitive capabilities in Microsoft Cortana Intelligence Suite and SQL Server.
