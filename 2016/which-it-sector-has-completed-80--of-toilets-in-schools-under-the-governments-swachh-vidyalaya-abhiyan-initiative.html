Which IT sector has completed 80 % of toilets in schools under the government’s Swachh Vidyalaya Abhiyan initiative

(A) Wipro

(B) Infosys

(C) ** TCS

(D) CTS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2014, Swachh Bharat Swachh Vidyalaya (SBSV) initiative was launched to ensure that all schools in India have access to separate functional toilets for boys and girls. The initiative lays emphasis on promoting safe and appropriate hygiene practices in schools and behaviour among children.

Concept note-2: -The objectives of Swachh Bharat include eliminating open defecation through the construction of household-owned and community-owned toilets and establishing an accountable mechanism of monitoring toilet use.

Concept note-3: -According to the Swachh Bharat Mission dashboard (SBM), there are 15.13 crore households with toilets.
