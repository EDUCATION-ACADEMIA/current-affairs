Which of the film director J.C. Daniel Award for his overall contribution to the Malayalam film industry on 6 September 2016.

(A) Mani Ratnam

(B) Saji Paravoor

(C) Bharath Gopi

(D) ** K.G. George

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -George won the prestigious J.C. Daniel Award 2016. Renowned film maker and veteran award-winning film director K.G. George won the prestigious J.C. Daniel Award for his overall contribution to the Malayalam film industry.

Concept note-2: -The most recent winner is Malayalam new wave auteur K P Kumaran who was honoured on the 3rd of August 2022.

Concept note-3: -30 September 2022, 08:34 PM IST New Delhi: The 68th National Film Awards ceremony held here on Friday marked a proud moment for Keralites as singer Nanchamma, actors Aparna Balamurali and Biju Menon received the awards.

Concept note-4: -Kerala State Film Awards 2022 Best Director Dileesh Pothan won the Kerala State Film Awards 2022 for Best Director for Joji. Umesh Moor won the Best Character Actor for Kala. Unnimaya Prasad won the Best Character Actress for Joji. Hridayam won the Best Film with Popular Appeal and Aesthetic Value.
