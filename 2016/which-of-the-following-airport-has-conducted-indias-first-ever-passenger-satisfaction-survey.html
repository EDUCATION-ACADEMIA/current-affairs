Which of the following Airport has conducted India’s first ever Passenger Satisfaction Survey

(A) Indira Gandhi International Airport

(B) ** Chatrapati Shivaji International Airport

(C) Trivandrum International Airport

(D) Vir Savarkar International Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Swami Vivekananda Airport, Raipur, Chhattisgarh, has topped the charts in the Customer Satisfaction Index Survey among 49 airports in India.

Concept note-2: -Raipur Airport Ranked First in Customer Satisfaction Index Survey – Punekar News.

Concept note-3: -Which is the first international airport in India? Ans. Cochin International Airport, Kerala is the first international airport in India developed under PPP-Public-Private Partnership Model.

Concept note-4: -Raipur, In another proud moment for Chhattisgarh, Swami Vivekananda Airport (SVA) of Raipur has been adjudged 2nd rank as best airport in India. The ranking was based on the recently concluded Airport Service Quality (ASQ) survey by Airport Council International (ACI), Montreal (Canada) for the second quarter of 2022.

Concept note-5: -Delhi’s IGI Airport ‘Cleanest Airport’ In India, South Asia.
