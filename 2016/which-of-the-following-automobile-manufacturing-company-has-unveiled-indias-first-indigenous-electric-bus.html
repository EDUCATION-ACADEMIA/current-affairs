Which of the following automobile manufacturing company has unveiled India’s first indigenous electric bus

(A) Toyota

(B) Tata Motors

(C) Audi

(D) ** Ashok Leyland Ltd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Ashok Leyland. India’s first indigenous electric bus has been manufactured by Ashok Leyland. Ashok Leyland Ltd on 17 October 2016 unveiled its Circuit Series electric bus.

Concept note-2: -Union Transport Minister, Nitin Gadkari has launched India’s first electric double-decker bus at YB Centre in south Mumbai. The name of the bus is “Switch EiV 22”, the double-decker bus will be run by the Mumbai civic transport body from September.

Concept note-3: -BYD Motors Inc., Solaris Bus & Coach sp. z o.o., Volvo Group, Yutong Bus Co., Ltd., VDL Bus & Coach BV, and Hyundai Motor Company are leading players in the global electric bus market.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is option 2 i.e. Himachal Pradesh.

Concept note-5: -India’s first Double Decker AC electric bus unveiled in Mumbai-‘Switch EiV 22’ | The Economic Times.
