Which of the following bank has entered into corporate agency agreements with Cigna TTK Company Ltd, and Reliance General Insurance Company Ltd for providing health and general insurance respectively

(A) HDFC Bank

(B) IDBI Bank

(C) DCB Bank

(D) ** Andhra Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HYDERABAD: Andhra Bank has entered into corporate agency agreements with Cigna TTK Company Ltd, and Reliance General Insurance Company Ltd for providing health and general insurance respectively, to its customers.

Concept note-2: -Cigna TTK Health Insurance, a joint venture of the US-based Cigna Corporation and TTK Group, is planning to expand its insurance partnership with Andhra Bank.

Concept note-3: -Reliance General Insurance Company Limited is an India-based private insurance company and is part of Reliance Anil Dhirubhai Ambani Group, wholly owned through Reliance Capital.
