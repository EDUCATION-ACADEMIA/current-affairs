Which of the following bank has launched the first set of newly issued 500 rupee notes

(A) Indian Overseas Bank

(B) ICICI Bank

(C) Canara Bank

(D) ** State Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In a first, a State Bank of India branch in Bhopal on Sunday rolled out the first set of the newly issued Rs. 500 notes.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank of India has announced the issue of new Rs 500 denomination bank notes which are the Mahatma Gandhi Series that would have the insert letter “E” in both its number panels.

Concept note-3: -The Rs. 500 note was introduced in October 1987 with the portrait of Mahatma Gandhi.

Concept note-4: -The new ₹500 notes in the Mahatma Gandhi (New) Series are different from the present series in colour, size, theme, location of security features and design elements. The size of the new note is 66mm x 150mm. The colour of the notes is stone grey and the predominant new theme is Indian heritage site-Red Fort.
