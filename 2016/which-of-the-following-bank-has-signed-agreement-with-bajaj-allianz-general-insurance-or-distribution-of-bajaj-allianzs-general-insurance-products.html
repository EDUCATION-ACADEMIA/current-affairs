Which of the following bank has signed agreement with Bajaj Allianz General Insurance or distribution of Bajaj Allianz’s general insurance products

(A) ** Canara Bank

(B) Indian Bank

(C) SBI

(D) Punjab National Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under this strategic agreement, Bajaj Allianz General Insurance will offer its industry leading general insurance products to the customers of Canara Bank.

Concept note-2: -NEW DELHI: Punjab & Sind Bank (PSB) and Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance have announced a partnership for distribution of the latter’s life goals-based product suite to the bank’s customers.

Concept note-3: -Punjab & Sind Bank (PSB), a public sector bank, and Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance Company (BALIC) have entered into a Corporate Agency Partnership.

Concept note-4: -As part of the alliance, IPPB will strive to make available affordable insurance products through its robust network of 650 branches and over 136, 000 banking access points to citizens at large.

Concept note-5: -HDFC Bank has finalised a new bancassurance partner in Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company, sources in the bank said.
