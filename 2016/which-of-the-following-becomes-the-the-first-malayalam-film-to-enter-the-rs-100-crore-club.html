Which of the following becomes the the first Malayalam film to enter the Rs 100 crore club.

(A) Oppam

(B) Welcome to Central Jail

(C) Kasaba

(D) ** Pulimurugan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2016, Mohanlal starring Pulimurugan became the first Malayalam film to enter the club. First Kannada movie to enter 100 Crore club was KGF directed by Prashanth Neel, released in 2018, starring Yash and Srinidhi Shetty crossing 153 crores in 11 days.

Concept note-2: -The 2005 film Rajamanikyam was the first Malayalam film to cross ₹25 crore. The 2013 film Drishyam was the first Malayalam film to cross ₹50 crore and ₹75 crore. The 2016 film Pulimurugan was the first Malayalam film to cross ₹100 crore and 150 crores mark.

Concept note-3: -Thalapathy Vijay leads the list with 5 movies grossing over 100 crore worldwide. This is also the highest for any South Indian actor.

Concept note-4: -Shah Rukh Khan-starrer Pathaan has continued to create new box office records as it became the fastest film to enter Rs. 300 crore club within four days.
