Which of the following bollywood actor has topped the 2016 Forbes India Celebrity 100 List.

(A) Shah Rukh Khan

(B) Akshay Kumar

(C) Hrithik Roshan

(D) ** Salman Khan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MUMBAI: Bollywood superstar Salman Khan has unseated his actor-friend Shah Rukh Khan to become the top earning celebrity of 2016 in the Forbes India Celebrity 100 List with an estimated income of Rs 270.33 crore.

Concept note-2: -Again, Salman tops Forbes India Celeb 100 list with Rs 232 cr; SRK, Virat Kohli follow. The ‘Tiger Zinda Hai’ actor had toppled SRK to become the top earning celebrity in 2016.

Concept note-3: -Virat Kohli The cricket star occupies top spot on the 2019 Forbes India Celebrity list, with a prolific run scoring spree and a glut of endorsements.

Concept note-4: -Amitabh Bachchan, Akshay Kumar, Shahrukh Khan, Alia Bhatt, Ranveer Singh, and Hritik Roshan are among the Indian celebrities featuring in the list.
