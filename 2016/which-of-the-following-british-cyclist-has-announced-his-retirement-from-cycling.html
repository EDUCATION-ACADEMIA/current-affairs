(A) Tom Barras

(B) Peter Bissell

(C) Bruce Bursford

(D) ** Bradley Wiggins

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sir Jason Kenny : Great Britain’s most decorated Olympian announces retirement from cycling to take up coaching. S ir Jason Kenny, Great Britain’s most decorated Olympian of all-time, has announced his retirement from competitive cycling.

Concept note-2: -Chris Hoy, in full Sir Christopher Andrew Hoy, (born March 23, 1976, Edinburgh, Scotland), British cyclist and race-car driver whose six career Olympic gold medals are among the most won by any Briton. Hoy took up cycling at age seven.

Concept note-3: -On 18 April 2013 Hoy announced his retirement from competitive cycling. He said he was very proud to have taken part in the transformation of the sport.

Concept note-4: -Britain’s most successful Olympian Sir Jason Kenny has announced his retirement from track cycling, six months after winning his seventh gold medal.

Concept note-5: -About Laura Laura Kenny began cycling when her mother decided to take up the sport to lose weight and went on to be the first female athlete in history to win five Olympic gold medals for Team GB.
