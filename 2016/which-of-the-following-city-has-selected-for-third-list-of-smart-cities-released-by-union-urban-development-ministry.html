Which of the following city has selected for third list of Smart Cities released by Union Urban Development Ministry

(A) Vellore

(B) Mangalore

(C) Kota

(D) ** All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -How many smart cities are there in India? The Government has selected 100 smart cities as part of the Smart Cities Mission. These include cities from across the states, selected in the proportion of the urban population.

Concept note-2: -In the five rounds, 100 cities are selected countrywide. These cities will be upgraded as per the area development plan. All the states of India are participating in the programme, leaving West Bengal.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is 10. The no. of cities in U.P. which are covered under the ‘Smart City’ Scheme are 10.
