Which of the following committee was constituted for Advertisements issued by Delhi government violated Supreme Court guidelines

(A) Nachiket Mor Committee

(B) Pratyush Sinha

(C) ** BB Tandon Committee

(D) V V Daga Committee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The complaints may be filed before the committee on its address: The Member Secretary, Committee on Content Regulation in Government Advertising (CCRGA), Room No.

Concept note-2: -At present in India, there is no central statutory agency or uniform legislation regulating the advertising industry. The Indian advertising market as a whole is regulated and controlled by a non-statutory body, the Advertising Standards Council of India (ASCI).
