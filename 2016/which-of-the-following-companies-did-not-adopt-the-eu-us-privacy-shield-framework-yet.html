Which of the following companies did not adopt the EU-US Privacy Shield framework yet

(A) Microsoft

(B) Google

(C) Salesforce

(D) ** Intel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The EU–US Privacy Shield was a legal framework for regulating transatlantic exchanges of personal data for commercial purposes between the European Union and the United States.

Concept note-2: -The GDPR applies to: a company or entity which processes personal data as part of the activities of one of its branches established in the EU, regardless of where the data is processed; or.

Concept note-3: -As a result of the Schrems II decision, the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework is no longer a valid mechanism to comply with EU data protection requirements when transferring personal data from the European Union to the United States.

Concept note-4: -As a consequence, transferring data from the EU to the US solely under the Privacy Shield principles is now not compliant with the GDPR, and a supervisory authority (data protection authority) could fine you for GDPR infringement.
