Which of the following company has been named the company of the year by Fortune

(A) Nike

(B) Apple

(C) Microsoft

(D) ** Alphabet Inc

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW YORK | MUMBAI, February 03, 2023: Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) (BSE: 532540, NSE: TCS) has been named to FORTUNE® magazine’s list of the World’s Most Admired Companies.

Concept note-2: -Walmart was the top-ranking company on the list for the sixth consecutive year. Country Garden Holdings posted the greatest jump in rank – rising 176 positions on the 2019 list. Walmart reported the largest number of employees (2, 200, 000), followed by China National Petroleum (1, 382, 401) and China Post Group (935, 191).

Concept note-3: -Wipro ranks 35th in prestigious Computerworld Top 500 2022 ranking.

Concept note-4: -Indian Oil Corporation topped the inaugural Fortune India 500 rankings, and Reliance Industries was listed second. The next three places were taken by State Bank of India, Bharat Petroleum and Hindustan Petroleum respectively.

Concept note-5: -There are a total of nine Indian companies in the Fortune Global 500 list 2022, including four private sector companies and five public sector companies.
