Concept note-2: -Sir Alexander Cunningham was the first Director-General of the Archaeological Survey of India. He excavated several ancient archaeological sites mentioned in the Buddhist texts.

Concept note-3: -It was founded in 1861 by a British Army engineer who took a keen interest in archaeology in India, James Cunningham. Archaeological pursuits started much earlier, in the 18th century, when Sir William Jones formed the Asiatic Society in 1784 together with a group of antiquarians.
