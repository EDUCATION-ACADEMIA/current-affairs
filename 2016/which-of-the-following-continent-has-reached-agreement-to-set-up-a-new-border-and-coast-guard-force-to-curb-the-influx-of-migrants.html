Which of the following continent has reached agreement to set up a new Border and Coast Guard Force to curb the influx of migrants.

(A) North America

(B) Asia

(C) ** European Union

(D) Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Agency carries out border controls, border surveillance and return activities and carries out those tasks in cooperation with Member States’ authorities and non-EU countries. It also performs ‘vulnerability assessments’ to detect and mitigate weaknesses in the EU border protection system.

Concept note-2: -With the exception of Bulgaria, all the other Eastern European EU countries had received less than 0.04 per cent. However, these countries have received a large number of Ukrainian refugees in 2022. In Western Europe, it is Portugal that has received the fewest refugees, at 0.03 per cent.

Concept note-3: -Overall situation at end of 2021 27.1 million refugees and. 53.2 million internally displaced persons (due to conflict and violence).
