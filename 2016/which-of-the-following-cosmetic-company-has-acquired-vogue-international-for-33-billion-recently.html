Which of the following cosmetic company has acquired Vogue International for $3.3 billion recently

(A) Bobbi Brown

(B) Estee Lauder

(C) Mary Kay

(D) ** Johnson & Johnson

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Matt and Hallie Kane, owners of Vogue Beauty College & Salon and Downtown Barber School. The doors of Hollywood Beauty College opened in 1959. Concept note-2: -Vogue is the owner of hair care brands OGX and Maui Moisture.

Concept note-3: -Johnson & Johnson Consumer Inc. acquires Vogue International LLC, the developer of OGX® hair and personal care products.
