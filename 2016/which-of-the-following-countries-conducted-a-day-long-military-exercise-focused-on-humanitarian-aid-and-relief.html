Which of the following countries conducted a day-long military exercise focused on humanitarian aid and relief.

(A) ** India, China

(B) India, Russia

(C) US, China

(D) Pakistan, China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 18th edition of Indo-US joint training exercise “YUDH ABHYAS 22” is scheduled to be conducted in Uttarakhand this month. Exercise Yudh Abhyas is conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, Tactics, Techniques and Procedures between the Armies of the two nations.

Concept note-2: -India, Kazakhstan hold joint military exercise in Meghalaya | North East India News, The Indian Express.

Concept note-3: -The exercise ‘Veer Guardian-2023’ involving the Indian Air Force (IAF) and Japan Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) will be held at Japan’s Hyakuri air base. The IAF said on Saturday that its deployment at the exercise will include four Su-30 MKI jets, two C-17 aircraft and one IL-78 plane.

Concept note-4: -What is the Garuda exercise? Ans. Garuda is a joint exercise between India and France Air Force that will provide a platform for both countries to enhance operational capability and interoperability, while also sharing best practices.
