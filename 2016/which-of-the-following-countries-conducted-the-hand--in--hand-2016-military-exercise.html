Which of the following countries conducted the Hand- in- Hand 2016 military exercise

(A) Bangladesh and China

(B) Pakistan and USA

(C) Russia and India

(D) ** India and China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hand in Hand exercise is India-China joint military exercise. The exercise takes place between the armies of both countries. The aim of Hand in Hand exercise is to enhance interoperability between the two armies to counter-terrorism.

Concept note-2: -Hand-in-Hand is an annual exercise that takes place as part of India’s and China’s military diplomacy and interaction. The exercise will include tactical operations in an UN-mandated multinational counterinsurgency/counterterrorism environment.

Concept note-3: -India, Kazakhstan hold joint military exercise in Meghalaya | North East India News, The Indian Express.

Concept note-4: -Bongo Sagar is the naval exercise between Bangladesh and India.

Concept note-5: -The exercise ‘Veer Guardian-2023’ involving the Indian Air Force (IAF) and Japan Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) will be held at Japan’s Hyakuri air base. The IAF said on Saturday that its deployment at the exercise will include four Su-30 MKI jets, two C-17 aircraft and one IL-78 plane.
