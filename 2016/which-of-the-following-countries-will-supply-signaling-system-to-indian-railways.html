Concept note-2: -(1) When a Driver finds an Automatic Stop signal with an ‘A’ marker at ‘On’, he shall bring his train to a stop in the rear of the signal. After bringing his train to a stop in the rear of the signal, the Driver shall wait there for one minute by day and two minutes by night.
