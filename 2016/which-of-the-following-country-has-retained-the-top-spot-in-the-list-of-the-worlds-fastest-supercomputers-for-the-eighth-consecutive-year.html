Which of the following country has retained the top spot in the list of the world’s fastest supercomputers for the eighth consecutive year

(A) Switzerland

(B) US

(C) Japan

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The US has retaken the top spot in the race to build the world’s fastest supercomputer. ‘Frontier ’ is capable of more than a billion, billion operations a second, making it the first exascale supercomputer.

Concept note-2: -1. Fugaku (Japan) Japan’s Fugaku, which was jointly developed by RIKEN and Fujitsu, is now the world’s fastest supercomputer.

Concept note-3: -The 60th TOP500 was published in November 2022. Since June 2022, USA’s Frontier is the most powerful supercomputer on TOP500, reaching 1102 petaFlops (1.102 exaFlops) on the LINPACK benchmarks.
