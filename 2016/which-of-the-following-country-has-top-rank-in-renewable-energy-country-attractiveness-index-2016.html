Which of the following country has top rank in Renewable energy country attractiveness index 2016

(A) ** US

(B) China

(C) India

(D) Chile

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The US continues its top ranking on RECAI.

Concept note-2: -US has retained the top spot, followed by Mainland China on the second position. According to EY’s 58th edition of ‘Renewable Energy Country Attractiveness Index’ (RECAI), India has retained the 3rd position.

Concept note-3: -Spain remained the most attractive PPA market in 2022, with Germany overtaking the United States for second place. The United Kingdom, Australia and France completed the top five. EY also introduced a new renewables attractiveness ranking, normalized to countries’ gross domestic product.
