(A) Iraq

(B) Qatar

(C) Syria

(D) ** Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Citizen Charter concept was first articulated and implemented in the United Kingdom in 1991 by the then P.M. John Major. Q. Which of the following was the first satellite launched by India?

Concept note-2: -Lots of other countries also have Bills or Charters of Human Rights, including: India, Brazil, Hong Kong, Kazakhstan, Iraq, East Timor, Turkey and Argentina.

Concept note-3: -Known today as the Cyrus Cylinder, this ancient record has now been recognized as the world’s first charter of human rights. It is translated into all six official languages of the United Nations and its provisions parallel the first four Articles of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights.

Concept note-4: -The concept of a citizen’s charter was initiated by former British Prime Minister John Major in the year 1991. It was started as a national programme intended to improve the quality of public services.
