Which of the following country has warned India that it will take appropriate action if India violates the Indus Water Treaty (IWT) unilaterally.

(A) Afghanistan

(B) China

(C) ** Pakistan

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As per the IWT, Pakistan bombing / destroying dams, barrages, power stations, etc. located in Indian part of the Indus system of rivers is violation of the IWT which can lead to abrogation of IWT.

Concept note-2: -Indus Waters Treaty, treaty, signed on September 19, 1960, between India and Pakistan and brokered by the World Bank. The treaty fixed and delimited the rights and obligations of both countries concerning the use of the waters of the Indus River system.

Concept note-3: -According to the treaty, India can use the water from the western rivers for domestic, non-consumptive needs such as storage, irrigation, and also the generation of electricity. The treaty gives India 20% of the water from the Indus River System and the rest 80% to Pakistan.

Concept note-4: -The Treaty was signed at Karachi by Field Marshal Mohammad Ayub Khan, the then President of Pakistan, Shri Jawaharlal Nehru, the then Indian Prime Minister and Mr. W.A.B. Illif of the World Bank on 19th September, 1960.
