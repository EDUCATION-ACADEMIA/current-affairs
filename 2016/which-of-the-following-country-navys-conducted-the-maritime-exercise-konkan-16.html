Which of the following country Navy’s conducted the maritime exercise ’Konkan 16 ’

(A) Bangladesh, Sri Lanka

(B) Pakistan, China

(C) Russia, China

(D) ** India, UK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -KONKAN 16 is an annual bilateral maritime exercise conducted between the Indian Navy and the Royal Navy.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is the India-United Kingdom. Konkan naval exercise is conducted between the India-United Kingdom.

Concept note-3: -21st EDITION OF INDIA FRANCE BILATERAL NAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the Western Seaboard today, 16 Jan 23.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Qatar. The bilateral maritime exercise Za’ir-Al-Bahr (Roar of the Sea) is conducted between India and Qatar.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Russia. The Navies of India and Russia conducted a passing exercise in the Arabian Sea on 14 Jan’22. Indian Navy’s indigenously designed and built guided-missile destroyer INS Kochi exercised with Russian Federation Navy’s destroyer Admiral Tributs.
