Which of the following court has became the fourth state to launch e-stamp facility

(A) ** Rajasthan High Court

(B) Maharashtra High Court

(C) Madhya Pradesh High Court

(D) Odisha High Court

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The High Court of Rajasthan has recently launched the e-stamp facility in its Jodhpur and Jaipur benches simultaneously.

Concept note-2: -Delhi has become the first state in the country to issue e-Stamp papers in all denominations.

Concept note-3: -When was the e stamp paper launched in India? The Indian Government launched the e-stamping facility in July 2013. This initiative aimed to minimize fraud and mistakes during the payment of stamp duty.

Concept note-4: -The Punjab government on Wednesday decided to abolish the physical stamp papers in an attempt to bring efficiency and check the pilferage of State revenue.
