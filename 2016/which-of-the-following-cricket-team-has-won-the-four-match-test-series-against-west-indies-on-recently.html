Which of the following cricket team has won the four-match test series against West Indies on recently

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Australia

(D) South Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When the West Indies won its first Test series against England in 1950, it was celebrated as a national achievement, as a way of demonstrating that West Indians were the equals of white Englishmen.

Concept note-2: -As of December 2022, West Indies played 565 Test matches resulting in 181 victories, 204 defeats, 179 draws and 1 tie for an overall winning percentage of 32.03.

Concept note-3: -Virat Kohli became the first captain of India to win two Tests in the West Indies.
