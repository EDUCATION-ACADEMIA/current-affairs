Which of the following cricket team won the three-match test series against New Zealand

(A) Australia

(B) South Africa

(C) West Indies

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India registered a 168-run victory against New Zealand in Ahmedabad, clinching the three-match series by a 2-1 margin. The 168-run victory was India’s biggest-ever win (by margin) in T20Is.

Concept note-2: -Overall, Australia have won 26 of the 45 Test matches played for the Trans-Tasman Trophy, with New Zealand winning 6.

Concept note-3: -Of the 35 Test matches played between these two nations, 22 have been won by India, New Zealand has managed to win only 13 games and no match was abandoned.

Concept note-4: -Team wins, losses, draws and ties As of February 2023, New Zealand have played 460 Test matches resulting in 109 victories, 181 defeats and 170 draws for an overall winning percentage of 23.69.
