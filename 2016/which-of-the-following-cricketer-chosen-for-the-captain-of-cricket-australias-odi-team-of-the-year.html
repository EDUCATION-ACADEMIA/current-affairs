(A) David Warner

(B) Quinton de Kock

(C) Steve Smith

(D) ** Virat Kohli

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is option 4, i.e. MS Dhoni.

Concept note-3: -Cummins will become Australia’s 27th ODI captain, with the right-arm quick to lead the country in 50-over cricket ahead of next year’s 2023 ICC Men’s Cricket World Cup in India.
