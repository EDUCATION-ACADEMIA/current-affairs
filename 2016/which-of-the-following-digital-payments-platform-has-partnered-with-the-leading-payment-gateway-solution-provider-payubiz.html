Which of the following digital payments platform has partnered with the leading payment gateway solution provider PayUbiz

(A) Dwolla

(B) Tilt

(C) ** Freecharge

(D) Square Cash

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PayU is a regulated financial institution. It holds licenses from national banks and local regulators. PayU’s products include a PCI-DSS certified payment gateway, an anti-fraud system and an online Visa/MasterCard acquirer.

Concept note-2: -Comparison of PayUbiz vs PayU In terms of Features PayUbiz includes features like Mobile Support, Online Payment, Invoicing and Multi Currency. PayU is known for functionalities like Online Payment, Payment Gateway Integration, Compatibility with Different Payment Gateways and Highest Success Rate.

Concept note-3: -PayU is both a real-time and offline bank transfer payment service. It allows shoppers to make payments directly from their bank account. For online real-time bank transfer purchases, the shopper selects their preferred online bank and logs into their account to confirm the payment.
