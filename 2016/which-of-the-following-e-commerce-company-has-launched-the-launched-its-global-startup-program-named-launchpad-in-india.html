Which of the following e-commerce company has launched the launched its global startup program named Launchpad in India

(A) Flipkart

(B) Snapdeal

(C) Alibaba

(D) ** Amazon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Amazon Launchpad, which was first launched back in mid-2015, is a medium and catalyst for providing startups with a global e-commerce platform to foster their brand’s reach.

Concept note-2: -Amazon Launchpad is designed to help products get discovered faster. Increased exposure for your brand in Amazon’s store through Unique Finds storefront placements and category-focused email campaigns. Waived fees for one Lightning Deal or one 7-day Deal per week.

Concept note-3: -Amazon Launchpad is an internal marketing program designed by Amazon to help new startups and small businesses reach their potential customers through Amazon. Their goal is to help create brand awareness for these startups.

Concept note-4: -Amazon Launchpad is an exclusive program that showcases innovative products from startups and emerging brands like yours to millions of Amazon customers. We make it easy for brands to launch new products on Amazon and get them discovered.
