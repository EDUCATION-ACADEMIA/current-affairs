Which of the following e-commerce firm to set up first Indian office at Bandra-Kurla Complex (BKC) in Mumbai

(A) eBay

(B) Amazon

(C) Netflix

(D) ** Alibaba

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Alibaba’s office will be housed at the Platina building owned by the The Wadhwa Group in the Bandra-Kurla Complex.

Concept note-2: -A prominent business centre of Mumbai, Bandra Kurla Complex is known for its commercial and business prowess across the world. Known as the growth centre of Mumbai, it houses numerous offices and commercial centres. Over 60, 000 people work at BKC anytime.

Concept note-3: -Godrej BKC (Bandra-Kurla Complex) is a project by Godrej Properties Limited developed in partnership with Jet Airways located in Mumbai, India. The architectural partner is SOM (Skidmore, Owings and Merrill) and the construction was undertaken by L&T. 1, 300, 000 sq. ft.

Concept note-4: -The culmination of a plan conceived by the Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority (MMRDA), the Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC) now houses the whole gamut of service-sector multinationals, as well as foreign consulates.
