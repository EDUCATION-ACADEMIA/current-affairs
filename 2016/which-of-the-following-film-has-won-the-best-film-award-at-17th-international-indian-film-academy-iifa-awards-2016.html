Which of the following film has won the Best Film award at 17th International Indian Film Academy (IIFA) Awards 2016

(A) ** Bajirao Mastani

(B) Piku

(C) Bajrangi Bhaijaan

(D) Masaan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Bollywood titans of 2015–Bajrangi Bhaijaan and Bajirao Mastani–emerged victorious at the International Indian Film Academy (IIFA) Awards 2016 with the Salman Khan-starrer winning best picture while Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s magnum opus taking home best director gong.

Concept note-2: -The IIFA Award for Best Film is chosen via a worldwide poll and the winner is announced at the ceremony. UTV Motion Pictures won 3 awards each, followed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali & Yash Chopra with 2 awards each.

Concept note-3: -The nominations were announced on 26 May 2016. Bajirao Mastani led the ceremony with 18 nominations, and won a leading 13 awards, including Best Director (for Sanjay Leela Bhansali), Best Actor (for Ranveer Singh) and Best Supporting Actress (for Priyanka Chopra).

Concept note-4: -The 22nd International Indian Film Academy Awards (IIFA) was held from 3-4 June at the Etihad Arena, Abu Dhabi. This year the IIFA 2022 Awards were hosted by Salman Khan, Maniesh Paul and Riteish Deshmukh. The film Shershah has won awards in the maximum five categories this year.
