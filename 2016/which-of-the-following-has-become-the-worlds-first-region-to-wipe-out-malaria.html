Which of the following has become the world’s first region to wipe out Malaria

(A) ** Europe

(B) Africa

(C) US

(D) Asia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Today we celebrate this historical achievement of having El Salvador certified malaria free.”

Concept note-2: -Kerala had been the first State in the country to achieve the eradication of malaria in 1965.

Concept note-3: -By the end of 1949, more than 4, 650, 000 house spray applications had been made. It also included drainage, removal of mosquito breeding sites, and spraying (occasionally from aircrafts) of insecticides. Total elimination of transmission was slowly achieved.

Concept note-4: -Five countries-Argentina, the Kyrgyz Republic, Paraguay, Sri Lanka, and Uzbekistan-recently achieved three consecutive years of zero local transmission. All but Uzbekistan have initiated the WHO process for malaria-free certification.
