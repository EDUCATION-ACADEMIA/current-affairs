Which of the following has the first Indian company to join EP100 campaign

(A) Tata Motors Ltd

(B) Hero MotoCorp Ltd

(C) ** Mahindra & Mahindra

(D) Bajaj Auto Ltd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mahindra became the first Indian company to join the EP100 campaign on 22 April 2016. By signing this campaign, the company committed to double its energy productivity by 2030.

Concept note-2: -Indian automobile giant Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd. has become the first Indian company to sign up for “EP100”, a global energy productivity campaign.

Concept note-3: -The EP100 Cooling Challenge, led by Climate Group and the Alliance the Save Energy, was launched in September 2019.

Concept note-4: -India’s 100-year-old consumer goods company Godrej expands its footprint in the digital world and grows market both domestically and overseas.

Concept note-5: -EP100 is a corporate commitment platform through which companies pledge to improve their energy productivity by deploying energy efficient technologies and practices.
