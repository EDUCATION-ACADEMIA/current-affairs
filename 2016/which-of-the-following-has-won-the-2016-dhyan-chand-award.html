Which of the following has won the 2016 Dhyan Chand Award

(A) Sylvanus Dung Dung

(B) Rajendra Pralhad Shelke

(C) Satti Geetha

(D) ** All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The Major Dhyan Chand award recipient 2016 was Rajendra Prasad Shelley for Rowing, Sylvanus Dung Dung for hockey and Sathi Geeta for athletics. Amit Kumar was odd one in this list. SSC CGL Tier I 2022 Results released on 9th February 2023.

Concept note-2: -Key highlights: Table Tennis player Sharath Kamal is the sole recipient of the prestigious Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award this year.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is P.V. Sindhu. PV Sindhu, one of India’s medalists at the 2016 Rio Olympics, was presented with the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award, India’s highest sporting honour, by former President Pranab Mukherjee.
