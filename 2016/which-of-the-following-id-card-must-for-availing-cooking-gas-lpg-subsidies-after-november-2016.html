Which of the following id card must for availing cooking gas (LPG) subsidies after November 2016.

(A) Voter Id

(B) ** Aadhaar card

(C) PAN Card

(D) Driving Licence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Yes, an Aadhaar card is mandatory to avail LPG subsidy.

Concept note-2: -As per the Ujjwala scheme, beneficiaries are eligible to receive Rs. 200 for a cylinder as subsidy. Subsidy is being provided for a maximum of 12 bottles per year. The main aim of the subsidy is to help ease the burden due to the increase in cooking gas prices.

Concept note-3: -Also, the re-introduction of LPG subsidy under the Ujjwala Yojana, which is meant for low-income people at the rate of Rs 200/cylinder, in May 2022 is seen to cost Rs 6, 100 crore in FY23.

Concept note-4: -The PAHAL scheme was first launched under the UPA government in 2013, and was then revised and relaunched after the Modi government came to power in 2014.

Concept note-5: -All LPG customers, even Ujjwala Yojana recipients, purchased LPG cylinders at market prices after the subsidy was removed in June 2020. At current market rates in the country, a 14.2 kilogramme LPG cylinder costs Rs 1, 003.
