Which of the following Indian Article has guarantees that no person shall be deprived of his life or personal liberty except according to the procedure established by law

(A) Article 20

(B) ** Article 21

(C) Article 22

(D) Article 23

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Article 22 in The Constitution Of India 1949.

Concept note-2: -According to Article 21: “Protection of Life and Personal Liberty: No person shall be deprived of his life or personal liberty except according to procedure established by law.” This fundamental right is available to every person, citizens and foreigners alike.

Concept note-3: -“Article 21 of the Constitution guarantees the life and personal liberty to all persons. It guarantees the right of persons to life with human dignity. Therein are included, all the aspects of life which go to make a person’s life meaningful, complete and worth living.

Concept note-4: -Article 21 – Protection of life and personal liberty, no person shall be deprived of his personal liberty except according to the procedures established by law.

Concept note-5: -EQUALITY RIGHTS (ARTICLES 14 – 18) 1.1 Article 14 of the Constitution of India reads as under: “The State shall not deny to any person equality before the law or the equal protection of the laws within the territory of India.”
