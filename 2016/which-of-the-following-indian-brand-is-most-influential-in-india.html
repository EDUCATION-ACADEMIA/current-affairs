Which of the following Indian brand is most influential in India

(A) ** Flipkart

(B) SBI

(C) Airtel

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Truly Made in India: Origin of Famous Indian Brands Prominent among these are Tata Group (1868), Dabur (1884), Godrej (1897), Vadilal (1907), and Parle G (1929).

Concept note-2: -Indian Brand Names like Infosys, SBI, and TCS (Tata Consultancy Services), to HDFC, Bajaj Finance, Reliance Industries, Bharti Airtel, and Hindustan Unilever are very popular.

Concept note-3: -After several years at the top as India’s most valuable brand, HDFC Bank has ceded the numero uno position to TCS. In Kantar Brandz India’s 2022 Rankings, the homegrown IT giant came out tops with a brand value of $45.52 billion. TCS’ brand value rose 212 per cent between 2020 and 2022.
