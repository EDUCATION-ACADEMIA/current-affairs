Which of the following Indian cricketer has has been made an honorary life member of the Marylebone Cricket Club (MCC).

(A) ** Zaheer Khan

(B) Gautam Gambhir

(C) Ishant Sharma

(D) Lakshmipathy Balaji

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -VVS Laxman, the former batsman, has been awarded the honorary life membership of Marylebone Cricket Club. With this, he joined fellow Indian icons Sachin Tendulkar, Rahul Dravid and Sourav Ganguly on the prestigious list of MCC Honorary Life Members.

Concept note-2: -Anjum Chopra is the first Indian woman cricketer to be awarded an honorary life membership of the Marylebone Cricket Club.

Concept note-3: -Clare Connor Becomes First Female MCC President In Club’s 234-year-old History. Clare Connor’s nomination was announced by her predecessor and Sri Lankan legend Kumar Sangakkara at the Annual General Meeting last year.

Concept note-4: -Former England captain Clare Connor on Friday took charge as the MCC (Marylebone Cricket Club) president, becoming the first woman to hold the post in the club’s 234-year-old history.
