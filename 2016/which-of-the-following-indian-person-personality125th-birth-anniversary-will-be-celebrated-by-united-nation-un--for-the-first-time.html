(A) Jawaharlal Nehru

(B) Ram Mohan Roy

(C) ** Dr. B.R. Ambedkar

(D) Bhagat Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has contributed more than 180, 000 troops, the largest number from any country, participated in more than 43 missions and 156 Indian peacekeepers have made the supreme sacrifice while serving in UN missions. India has also provided and continues to provide eminent Force Commanders for UN Missions.

Concept note-2: -India’s status as a founding member of the Non-Aligned Movement and the Group of 77 cemented its position within the UN system as a leading advocate of the concerns and aspirations of developing countries and the creation of a more equitable international economic and political order.

Concept note-3: -Babasaheb Bhim Rao Ambedkar, a renowned social reformer and a Dalit icon, was born on April 14 in 1891. He died on December 6 in 1956.
