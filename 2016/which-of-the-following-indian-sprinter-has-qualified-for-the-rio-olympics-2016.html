(A) Soma Biswas

(B) ** Dutee Chand

(C) Rahi Sarnobat

(D) Geeta Zutshi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -But finally on 25 June 2016, Dutee broke the same National record twice in one day after clocking 11.24 at the XXVI International Meeting G Kosanov Memorial at Almaty, Kazakhstan, thereby qualifying for the Olympic Games.

Concept note-2: -The 120 athletes include 32 members of Indian men’s and women’s hockey team. Apart from them, 36 athletes will represent India in Athletics (track and field events) category alone. Indian will be competing across 15 events at Rio Olympics.

Concept note-3: -The 2016 Olympic Games at Rio was a watershed one for India as for the first time in Olympic history, their medal haul was enriched only by its female athletes with PV Sindhu and Sakshi Malik claiming historic podiums on their Games debut.

Concept note-4: -Proud moment for Dutee Chand at Rio 2016 The last time she appeared at the Games, she made waves by becoming the first Indian female athlete in 36 years to qualify for the 100m in the Olympics at Rio 2016.
