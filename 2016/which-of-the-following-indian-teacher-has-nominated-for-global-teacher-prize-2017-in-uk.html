Which of the following Indian teacher has nominated for Global Teacher Prize 2017 in UK

(A) Ragavi Devi

(B) Jaya Lakshmi

(C) ** Kavita Sanghvi

(D) Kavya Menon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kavita Sanghvi has been recognised for her experiential method of teaching physics as a principal of MET Rishikul Vidyalaya in Mumbai by The Global Teacher Prize 2017.

Concept note-2: -Maggie MacDonnell from the Canada received her Global Teacher Prize‬ award from Sunny Varkey of the Varkey Foundation, His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the Vice President and Prime Minister of the United Arab Emirates and Ruler of Dubai.

Concept note-3: -Ranjitsinh Disale-Global Teacher Prize Winner from India Ranjitsinh Disale won price money of $1 million, which he decided to share with his fellow finalists. Furthermore, this is the first time that the winner has decided to share the prize money in this prize ceremony.

Concept note-4: -Mr Sahu has been selected for the most prestigious Global Teacher Award 2022 on the basis of nomination and his merit and received the award among other award winners from nearly 110 countries. Global Teacher Award recognises and celebrates excellence in education on a global scale.

Concept note-5: -Satyam Mishra, a mathematics teacher from Bhagalpur in Bihar, and Meghana Musunuri, social studies, English and math teacher from Hyderabad have made it to the top 50 shortlist for this year’s USD 1-million Global Teacher Prize announced on Thursday.
