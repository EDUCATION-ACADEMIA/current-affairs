(B) Narsingh Yadav

(C) Hardeep Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -India won its first-ever silver medal in individual event at the Olympics when its ace shooter Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore struck it on the fourth day at the 28th Olympic Games in Athens bringing good news to millions of sports fans back home.
