Which of the following india’s language ranked 10th in the top 10 most powerful languages on the planet

(A) Marathi

(B) Tamil

(C) Bengali

(D) ** Hindi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hindi is considered as 10th most powerful language in the ‘World Power Language Index’ published by World Economic Forum on december 2, 2016.

Concept note-2: -United States of America The United States is the world’s most powerful country, with a dominant military, economic strength, and cultural influence.

Concept note-3: -As per the 2011 Census of India, languages by highest number of speakers are as follows: Hindi, Bengali, Marathi, Telugu, Tamil, Gujarati, Urdu, Kannada, Odia, Malayalam.

Concept note-4: -Currently, these are the 10 most spoken languages in the world in 2023, sorted by number of mother language speakers: Mandarin Chinese, Spanish, English, Hindi, Bengali, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, Yue chinese, Vietnamese.
