Which of the following Institute has won the 2015-16 Maulana Abul Kalam Azad (MAKA) Trophy

(A) Banaras Hindu University

(B) Aligarh University

(C) ** Punjabi University

(D) Calcutta University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Panjab University.

Concept note-2: -Guru Nanak Dev University in Amritsar has won the trophy 23 times, most recently in the year 2022. Panjab University in Chandigarh has won the trophy 15 times, most recently in the year 2020, while Delhi University based in Delhi has won the trophy 14 times, most recently in the year 2000–2001.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar won the prestigious Maulana Abul Kalam Azad (MAKA) Trophy for 2017-18.

Concept note-4: -The MAKA Trophy instituted in year 1956-57. It is a sports honor of the Republic of India. The award is named after Abul Kalam Azad commonly referred to as Maulana Azad, one of the senior leaders of the Indian independence movement and the first Minister of Education in independent India.
