Which of the following is not a Reserve Bank of India (RBI) zonal

(A) Chennai

(B) Kolkata

(C) Mumbai

(D) ** Bangalore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are four zonal offices of RBI at Mumbai, Kolkata, Delhi and Chennai.

Concept note-2: -State Bank of Sikkim only operates in Sikkim. It was established in 1968, 5 years before Sikkim acceded to India. State Bank of Sikkim is owned by the state government.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank of India has offices at 31 locations. To see the Profile of the office, click on Profile below and choose the city in the map.

Concept note-4: -Advancing loans to the public and accepting deposits from the public is not the function of the Reserve Bank of India.
