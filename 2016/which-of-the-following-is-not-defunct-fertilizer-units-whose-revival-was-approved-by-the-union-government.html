Which of the following is not defunct Fertilizer Units whose revival was approved by the Union Government

(A) Gorakhpur

(B) Sindri

(C) ** Kantipur

(D) Barauni

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Domestic fertilizer companies are required by law to “Neem coat” at least 75% of their urea production which can even go up to 100 percent. There used to be a 35 percent cap on this. Additionally, the government has permitted businesses to increase the price of urea that has been coated with neem by just 5%.

Concept note-2: -Fertilizer Corporation of India Ltd. (FCIL) has five units at Sindri (Jharkhand). Talcher (Odisha), Ramagundam (Telangana), Gorakhpur (U.P.) & Korba (Chhattisgarh).

Concept note-3: -Shri Atul B. Patil has a rich experience of over 32 years in Marketing and Personnel Management in the Fertilizers Industry. He has been on the Board of NFL since April 2022.

Concept note-4: -Sindri Unit is located about 20 kms away from the district headquarters of Dhanbad district of Jharkhand State. This is the first fertilizer unit in India.
