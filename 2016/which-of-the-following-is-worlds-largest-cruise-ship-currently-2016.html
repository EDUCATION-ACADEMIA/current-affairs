Which of the following is world’s largest cruise ship currently 2016

(A) ** Harmony of the Seas

(B) Regal Princess

(C) Anthem of the Seas

(D) Costa Diadema

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With 18 decks, capacity for 6, 780 passengers and 2, 100 members of the crew the Symphony of the Seas is now the biggest cruise ship in the world.

Concept note-2: -The biggest ship in the world, Wonder of the Seas℠ brings even bigger adventures to the award-winning Oasis Class.

Concept note-3: -Weighing more than 230, 000 tons with a total capacity of nearly 7, 000 passengers, Wonder of the Seas is currently the world’s largest cruise ship. Strategic Account Manager Joanne Cotterman says all guests are now welcome – a big change from when cruising restarted a year ago during the pandemic.

Concept note-4: -Royal Caribbean’s Wonder of the Seas was the largest cruise ship worldwide as of February 2022, with a gross tonnage of 236.86 thousand.
