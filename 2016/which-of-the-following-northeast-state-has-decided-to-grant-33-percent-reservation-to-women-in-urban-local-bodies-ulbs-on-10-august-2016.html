Which of the following northeast state has decided to grant 33 percent reservation to women in Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) on 10 August 2016.

(A) Mizoram

(B) Manipur

(C) Meghalaya

(D) ** Nagaland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -33% reservation is provided to women in local administration in India.

Concept note-2: -The 73rd and 74th constitutional amendments facilitated the large-scale participation of women in political institutions at the local and regional levels by introducing 33% reservation in rural and urban local bodies. Currently, around 1.4 million women are elected representatives in India.

Concept note-3: -Whereas, the Urban Local Bodies (ULBS) in Nagaland comprising of 3 (three) Municipal Councils and 16 (sixteen) Towns Councils (listed below) have self-declared respective ULBS as Open Defecation-Free (ODF) following the protocols issued by Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs, Govt.

Concept note-4: -As per provisions contained in Article 243 D of the Constitution, 1/3rd of the Seats of Panchayati Raj Institutions and 1/3rd offices of the Chairperson at all level of Panchayati Raj Institutions covered by Part IX of the Constitution are reserved for women.
