Which of the following Organization has imposed interim ban on Manja

(A) Environmentalist Foundation of India

(B) ** National Green Tribunal

(C) India Green Conservation

(D) Poovulagin Nanbargal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MUMBAI: The police have banned the use, sale and storage of nylon manja or Chinese manja used to fly kites as it is likely to cause grievous injury to birds and humans ahead of Makar Sankranti on Saturday.

Concept note-2: -The list is endless. Such incidents are reported from all over India. But did you know that the Chinese manjha is actually banned by the National Green Tribunal (NCT)? Yes, Chinese manjha remains a contraband item in the country since 2017.

Concept note-3: -According to Indian Express, ‘Chinese manjha’ is made of monofilament fishing lines that are melted and mixed with polymers. After the strings are formed, they are coated with glass. The National Green Tribunal (NGT) banned the sale of these strings across the country in 2017.
