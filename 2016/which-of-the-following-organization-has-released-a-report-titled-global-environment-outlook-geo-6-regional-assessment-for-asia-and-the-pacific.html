Which of the following organization has released a report titled Global Environment Outlook (GEO-6): Regional assessment for Asia and the Pacific

(A) WNO

(B) GEF

(C) ** UNEP

(D) IUCN

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) publishes the Global Environment Outlook (GEO) report as its flagship environment assessment.

Concept note-2: -Nairobi, 06 December 2022 – Over 300 experts from around the world met between September and October 2022 to officially launch the production of the 7th edition of the Global Environment Outlook (GEO-7), the flagship report of the UN Environment Programme (UNEP).

Concept note-3: -GEO-6: Healthy Planet, Healthy People At present, the world is not on track to meet the Sustainable Development Goals. UNEP’s GEO-6 calls for policy interventions that address entire systems in order to lead to healthy lives for all people.

Concept note-4: -The sixth edition of the Global Environment Outlook (GEO-6) provides a clear assessment of the current state of the environment, the challenges that we face and how well we have dealt with them, with due consideration given to gender, indigenous knowledge and cultural dimensions.
