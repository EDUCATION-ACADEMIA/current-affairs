Which of the following organization has released the State of the World’s Children 2016 report on 28 June 2016

(A) UU-UNO

(B) ** UNICEF

(C) UNDCP

(D) UNHCR

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The State of the World’s Children 2016 | UNICEF.

Concept note-2: -The State of the World’s Children 2021.

Concept note-3: -UNICEF’s flagship report – the most comprehensive analysis of global trends affecting children.

Concept note-4: -The theme of World Children’s Day 2022 is ‘Inclusion, for every child’.

Concept note-5: -Our full name was the United Nations International Children’s Emergency Fund. In 1950, the UNICEF mandate was broadened to address the long-term needs of children and women in developing countries everywhere.
