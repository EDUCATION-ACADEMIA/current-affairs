Which of the following Pakistani cricketer has has received the prestigious ICC Test Championship mace

(A) Shahid Afridi

(B) Inzamam-ul-Haq

(C) ** Misbah-ul-Haq

(D) Muhammad Imran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pakistan has the distinct achievement of having won each of the major ICC international cricket tournaments: ICC Cricket World Cup (1992), ICC T20 World Cup (2009) and ICC Champions Trophy (2017); as well as becoming the fourth team to lift the mace of the ICC Test Championship in 2016.

Concept note-2: -Australia are the most successful team, having held the mace a record eight times. South Africa had it for three years from 2013-2015. Prior to South Africa’s time at the top of the ICC Test Championship, England held the mace for a solitary year in 2012. New Zealand are the current holders of the ICC Test mace.

Concept note-3: -New Zealand are the winners of the ICC World Test Championship (WTC) Final and will take home the ICC Test Championship Mace along with a cool purse of $1.6 million.
