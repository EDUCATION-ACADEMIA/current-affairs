Which of the following political parties has won the Tamil Nadu Election 2016

(A) DMDK

(B) PMK

(C) DMK

(D) ** AIADMK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -AIADMK under J. Jayalalithaa won the elections and became the first ruling party to be re-elected in Tamil Nadu since 1984, though with a simple majority.

Concept note-2: -All India Trinamool Congress under Mamata Banerjee won 211 seats, and thus was reelected with an enhanced majority.

Concept note-3: -The Indian state of Tamil Nadu has 38 districts after several splits of the original 13 districts at the formation of the state on 1 November 1956.

Concept note-4: -The votes were counted on 2 May 2021; the SPA amassed 159 seats, with the DMK alone winning in 133 constituencies, securing an absolute majority for the first time in 25 years. The NDA won 75 seats, out of which 66 were of the AIADMK.
