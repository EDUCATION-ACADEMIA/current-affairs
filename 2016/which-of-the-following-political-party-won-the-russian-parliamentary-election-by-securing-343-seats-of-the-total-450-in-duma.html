Which of the following political party won the Russian parliamentary election by securing 343 seats of the total 450 in Duma

(A) Communist Party of the Russian Federation

(B) ** United Russia party

(C) Political party LDPR

(D) Patriots of Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Members of four political parties became the State Duma deputies: United Russia (343), the Communist Party of the Russian Federation (42), LDPR-the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia (39), and A Just Russia (23).

Concept note-2: -The members of the State Duma are elected by the citizens of the Russian Federation on the basis of universal equal and direct suffrage by secret ballot. A total number of elected members is 450.

Concept note-3: -Status and powers, composition and Regulations of the State Duma. The State Duma is one of the chambers of the Russian parliament, the Federal Assembly. It is a legislative authority that consists of 450 members elected for five years.

Concept note-4: -Electoral system The State Duma is elected on a single election day for a term of five years, with parallel voting that was used between 1993 and 2003. Out of 450 seats, 225 are elected by proportional representation from party lists with a 5% electoral threshold, the whole country forming a single constituency.
