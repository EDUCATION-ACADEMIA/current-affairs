Which of the following private sector bank has acquired BSS Microfinance in an all-cash deal for Rs 139.2 crore.

(A) ICICI Bank

(B) Yes Bank

(C) Axis Bank

(D) ** Kotak Mahindra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The cost of acquisition or the price at which Kotak Mahindra Bank has acquired shares of BSS Microfinance is Rs 139.2 crore.

Concept note-2: -DEVARAJAN KANNAN His SBU encompasses Agri Finance, Commercial Vehicles, Construction Equipment, Tractor Finance, and Microfinance.

Concept note-3: -5.3 The beginning of the micro finance movement in India could be traced to the self-help group (SHG)-bank linkage programme (SBLP) started as a pilot project in 1992 by National Bank for Agricultural and Rural Development (NABARD).

Concept note-4: -The average Bss Microfinance salary ranges from approximately ₹1.4 Lakhs per year for a Center Manager to ₹10.2 Lakhs per year for a Assistant Manager Operations.

Concept note-5: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) shall regulate the micro finance sector; it may set an upper limit on the lending rate and margins of Micro Finance Institutions (MFIs).
