Which of the following rocket can be used for Gaofen-3 Satellite

(A) EPOS

(B) ALV X-1

(C) ** Long March 4C

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The satellite has been named Gaofen-3 03. It was from the Jiuquan Satellite Launch Centre with the use of a Long March-4C rocket.

Concept note-2: -China has launched a new Earth observation satellite on 7th April 2022. This satellite will become a part of the country’s land-sea radar satellite constellation. It will capture images to help China safeguard its maritime interests.

Concept note-3: -Gaofen (Chinese: ; pinyin: Gāofēn; lit. ‘high resolution’) is a series of Chinese high-resolution Earth imaging satellites launched as part of the China High-resolution Earth Observation System (CHEOS) program.

Concept note-4: -China launches third group of Yaogan-35 remote sensing satellites-CGTN.

Concept note-5: -GF3 has a spatial resolution ranging from one meter through 500 m, and its largest viewing swath is 650 km. GF3 has the following acquisition modes: Spotlight. Stripmap.
