Which of the following scheme can get their photo or logo printed inside the postage stamp of individual or a corporate entity by India Post

(A) My Individual Stamp

(B) My India Stamp

(C) ** My Stamp

(D) Own Stamp

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: An individual or a corporate entity can now get their photo or logo printed inside the postage stamp for Rs 12 lakh, the Department of Posts said today. “We have opened up customised printing of ‘My Stamp’ for individuals and corporates.

Concept note-2: -Phad of Shri Devnarayan: These are famous cloth paintings by Shree lal Joshi. They depict the local legend of Devnarayan who is a local folk deity in particular parts of Rajasthan. This painting is painted in five colours-orange, red, grey, green, and yellow.

Concept note-3: -’My Stamp’ is the brand name for personalized sheets of Postage Stamps of India Post.

Concept note-4: -The most commonly known method of planographic printing is lithography. This type of printing is based on the principle that oil and water do not mix. Ink used to print stamps is oil based. The printing surface is treated to hold water in the areas that are not to be inked.

Concept note-5: -The First Stamp of Independent India was issued on 21 November 1947. It depicts the Indian Flag with the patriots’ slogan, Jai Hind (Long Live India), on the top right hand corner. It was valued at three and one-half annas.
