Which of the following Sri Lanka’s cricketer was inducted into the ICC Cricket Hall of Fame

(A) Kumar Sangakkara

(B) ** Muttiah Muralitharan

(C) Tillakaratne Dilshan

(D) Mahela Jayawardene

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Three greats of the game have become the latest people to be inducted into the ICC Cricket Hall of Fame. The ICC announced a trio of additions to the ICC Hall of Fame on Tuesday, with Pakistan legend Abdul Qadir, England trailblazer Charlotte Edwards and West Indies great Shivnarine Chanderpaul the latest inductees.

Concept note-2: -ICC Hall of Fame: Shivnarine Chanderpaul, Charlotte Edwards and Abdul Qadir inducted. The International Cricket Council (ICC) has announced the latest legends who join the prestigious list of cricketing greats that make up the ICC Hall of Fame.

Concept note-3: -Kapil Dev, who led India to the emphatic World Cup victory in 1983, is considered one of the India’s greatest all-rounders.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Vinoo Mankad. He is the lone Indian Cricketer included in the ICC Hall of Fame special inductions to mark the inaugural ICC World Test Championship Final.
