Which of the following state became India’s first state to implement Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) in Kerosene on 1 October 2016.

(A) Bihar

(B) Rajasthan

(C) Assam

(D) ** Jharkhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jharkhand is the first state in India to launch this scheme in kerosene.

Concept note-2: -Government of India launched the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) Program on 1st Jan 2013 to directly transfer the benefits to the under-privileged population covered under 34 central schemes.

Concept note-3: -PAHAL has since entered into Guinness book of World Record being largest Direct Benefit Transfer Scheme. Applicable subsidy is directly transferred into the bank accounts of the beneficiaries.
