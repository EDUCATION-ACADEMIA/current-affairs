Which of the following state became the first state to distribute 2 crore LED bulbs under Unnat Jyoti by Affordable LEDs for All (UJALA) scheme.

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) Karnataka

(C) Jharkhand

(D) ** Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gujarat becomes first state to distribute 2 crore LED bulbs under UJALA scheme: All about it-India Today.

Concept note-2: -Unnat Jyoti by Affordable LEDs for All (UJALA) was a project to distribute efficient LED lighting, launched by Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi on 5 Jan 2015.

Concept note-3: -Govt distributes 36.78 cr LEDs under UJALA scheme.

Concept note-4: -“Gram Ujala Scheme has been implemented by Convergence Energy Services Limited (CESL). Under Gram UJALA Scheme, 1 crore LED bulbs have been distributed in rural areas of Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, and Telangana as a Pilot Project.

Concept note-5: -UJALA Scheme-Full Form, Objectives & Implementation. UJALA scheme was launched by PM Narendra Modi on 1st May 2015 under the government of India.
