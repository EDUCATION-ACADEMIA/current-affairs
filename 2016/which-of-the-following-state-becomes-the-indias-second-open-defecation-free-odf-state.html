Which of the following state becomes the India’s second Open Defecation Free (ODF) State

(A) Sikkim

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) ** Himachal Pradesh

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: Sixty percent of all open defecation in the world takes place in India and 60 to 70 per cent of the women of the country defecate in the open. Himachal Pradesh became the second such state along with Kerala.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Mizoram. South Maubuang in Aibawk block of Aizawl district in Mizoram has been declared a model ODF (open defecation free) Plus village.

Concept note-3: -Taking a step ahead towards the Swachh Bharat Mission, South Maubuang village, located in the Aibawk block of Aizawal district in Mizoram, has become the first Model ODF (Open-Defecation Free) Plus village of the state.

Concept note-4: -BHUBANESWAR: Odisha is among the top five performing states where maximum number of villages have been declared open defecation free (ODF)-Plus. Other top performing states are Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Uttar Pradesh and Himachal Pradesh.

Concept note-5: -Goa has also become the first state to provide tap water to every household under the “Har Ghar Jal Mission”. Apart from that, Goa achieved 100 per cent target to provide free ration to the poor and needy.
