Which of the following state government has launched open government data portal

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Assam

(C) ** Sikkim

(D) Tripura

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sikkim has become the first Indian state to launch open government data website to promote transparency.

Concept note-2: -A platform for supporting Open Data initiative of Government of India. It facilitates Government Organizations to publish their datasets in open formats for free public use. OGD is also available as Software as a Service (SaaS).

Concept note-3: -Open Government Data (OGD) Platform India – data.gov.in – is a platform for supporting Open Data initiative of Government of India.

Concept note-4: -India.gov.in is the Indian government’s web portal for citizens. It presents information resources and online services from government sources, accessible from a single point. It is also known as the National Portal of India.
