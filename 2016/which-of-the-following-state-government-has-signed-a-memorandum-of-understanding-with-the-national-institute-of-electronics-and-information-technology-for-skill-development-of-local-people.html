Which of the following state government has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with the National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology for skill development of local people.

(A) Assam

(B) Tripura

(C) Sikkim

(D) ** Arunachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For the first time since India’s independence, a Ministry for Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE) has been formed to focus on enhancing employability of the youth through skill development.

Concept note-2: -The National Policy on Skill Development is an umbrella framework devised by the Government of India to develop employable skills among the youth of the country through learning and producing workers adequately skilled to meet the requirements of industry.

Concept note-3: -UK has entered into a partnership with India under skill India programme.

Concept note-4: -Skill India is an initiative launched by the government in 2015 to train over 40 crore Indians in different industry-related jobs. The vision is to create an empowered workforce by 2022 with the help of various schemes and training courses.
