Which of the following state governor has promulgated Official Language (Amendment) Ordinance, 2016 to facilitate use of a language in transaction of official businesses of the Government

(A) ** Odisha

(B) Arunachal Pradesh

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Odisha Official Language (Amendment) Ordinance, 2016 was promulgated which will facilitate use of Odia language in transaction of official businesses of the Government.

Concept note-2: -The Department Official Language is the Government of India’s department responsible for the implementation of the provisions of the Constitution relating to official languages and the provisions of the Official Languages Act, 1963.

Concept note-3: -Rule 12 Responsibility for compliance-Page 4 (1) It shall be the responsibility of the administrative head of each Central Government office:-( i) to ensure that the provisions of the Act and these rules are properly complied with; and (ii) to devise suitable and effective check points for this purpose.

Concept note-4: -However, article 343(1) of the Indian constitution specifically mentions that “The official language of the Union shall be Hindi in Devanagari script.

Concept note-5: -An Act to amend the Official Languages Act, 1963. Act, 1967.
