Which of the following state has given approval for implementation of the Rs 50-crore National Hydrology Project to be set up in the state

(A) Odisha

(B) Bihar

(C) Jharkhand

(D) ** Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Hydrology Project (NHP) was started in the year 2016 as a Central Sector Scheme with 100% grant to Implementing agencies on pan India basis with a budget outlay of Rs 3680 Crore to be spent over a period of 8 years.

Concept note-2: -Budget Outlay: about Rs3, 680 Crore, with World Bank Assistance to the tune USD 160 million.

Concept note-3: -It was started in 2016 as a Central Sector Scheme with 100% grant to implementing agencies on pan India basis. It is an initiative of the Ministry of Jal Shakti and is supported by the World Bank.
