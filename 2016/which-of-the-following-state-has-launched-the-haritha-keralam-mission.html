Which of the following state has launched the Haritha Keralam Mission

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) Maharashtra

(D) ** Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kerala is blessed with copious rainfall, rivers, canals, backwaters and ponds. Mission Haritha Keralam aims to ensure that no havoc is wrecked to our Water sources, Earth, Air, Soil and Water.

Concept note-2: -The Chief Minister of Kerala Pinarayi Vijayan on 8 December 2016 launched the Haritha Keralam Mission in Thiruvananthapuram.

Concept note-3: -The Launch of the Haritha Keralam Mission The most ambitious program Haritha Keralam was recently launched by the chief minister of the state Kerala Mr. Pinarayi Vijayan.

Concept note-4: -Thiruvananthapuram: The authorities of Haritha Keralam Mission said that 47, 736 kilometre of rivulets in the state has been restored through the project ‘Ini Njan Ozhukatte’, which was commenced for the rejuvenation of natural water sources.
