Which of the following Tamil film will be India’s official entry in the Foreign Language film category at the Oscar Award 2017

(A) Kaaka Muttai

(B) ** Visaranai

(C) Kuttram Kadithal

(D) Appuchi Gramam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tamil film ‘Visaaranai ’ is India’s official entry to Academy Awards 2017 in the Foreign Language Film category. Featuring Dinesh, Anandhi and Aadukalam Murugadoss in the lead roles, the film focuses on corruption and police brutality.

Concept note-2: -Newton is a Hindi feature film that has been selected as India’s official entry for the International Feature Film category at the 90th Academy Awards 2017.

Concept note-3: -Marathi film Court has been selected as India’s official entry to the best foreign-language category of the 88th Academy or Oscar Awards 2016. Queen gets the best film fare award in 2015.

Concept note-4: -SS Rajamouli’s ‘RRR’ featured on the Academy’s list of productions eligible for Oscar nominations at the 95th Academy Awards.
