Which of the following team won the 2016 Davis Cup

(A) Croatia

(B) Australia

(C) USA

(D) ** Argentina

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Argentina, led by Juan Martin Del Potro, became only the 15th nation in 117 years to win the Davis Cup title by defeating Croatia 3-2 in the Final in Zagreb on 25-27 November.

Concept note-2: -See a complete list of Davis Cup Champions. The 2021/22 Davis Cup Champions is Canada. They won the title by defeating Australia 2-0 in the finals.
