Which of the following telugu actor has adopted Burripalem village in Andhra Pradesh and launched several development works worth about Rs 2.14 crore on 8 May 2016

(A) Allu Arjun

(B) Ravi Teja

(C) Nagarjuna

(D) ** Mahesh Babu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Babu himself has adopted his native village, Burripalem.

Concept note-2: -Naresh who is also a politician is actor Mahesh Babu’s elder brother.

Concept note-3: -The first village he adopted was in his ancestral place Under the Smart Village-Smart Ward program launched by the state government, Babu adopted Burripalem, his ancestral (father’s side) village near Tenali.

Concept note-4: -Mahesh Babu has several properties including his lavish house in Hyderabad worth Rs. 28 crores. The actor resides with his actress wife Namrata Shirodkar and two kids, a son named Gautam and a daughter named Sitara.
