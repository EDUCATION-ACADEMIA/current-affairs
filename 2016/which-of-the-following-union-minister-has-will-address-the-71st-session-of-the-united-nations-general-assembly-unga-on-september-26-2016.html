Which of the following union minister has will address the 71st session of the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) on September 26, 2016

(A) Arun Jaitley

(B) Manohar Parrikar

(C) Suresh Prabhu

(D) ** Sushma Swaraj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 71st ordinary session opened on 13 September 2016, at 3 p.m. His Excellency Peter Thomson of Fiji was the President of the 71st session of the General Assembly. The theme of the 71st session was “The Sustainable Development Goals: A Universal Push to Transform our World".

Concept note-2: -The 72nd ordinary session opened on 12 September 2017 and closed on 17 September 2018. His Excellency Miroslav Lajčák of Slovakia was the President of the 72nd session of the General Assembly.

Concept note-3: -Csaba Korosi is a Hungarian diplomat currently serving as president of the 77th United Nations General Assembly.

Concept note-4: -The first session of the assembly convened on Jan. 10, 1946, in London, with 51 countries represented. As of 2006 there were 192 members of the General Assembly.
