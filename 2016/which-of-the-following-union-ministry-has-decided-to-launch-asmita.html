Which of the following Union Ministry has decided to launch ASMITA

(A) ** Union Ministry of Human Resource Development

(B) Union Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

(C) Union Minister of Defence

(D) Union Ministry of Earth Sciences

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IAS Exam Latest Updates. The Ministry of Human Resource and Development had decided to launch the ASMITA scheme in June 2016 for the benefit of the government-operated schools by keeping a track of individual students. The ASMITA scheme is a tracking system for each individual related to the governmental school.

Concept note-2: -ASMITA is an acronym for All School Monitoring Individual Tracing Analysis and launched under Shala Asmita Yojana (SAY). SAY aims to track the educational journey of school students from Class I to Class XII across the 15 lakhs private and government schools in the country.

Concept note-3: -The Asmita Yojana is designed and operated by women self-help organisations with the goal of ensuring access to and availability of affordable sanitary pads in rural Maharashtra.
