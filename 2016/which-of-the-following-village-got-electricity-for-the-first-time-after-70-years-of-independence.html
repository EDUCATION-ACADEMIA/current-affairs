Which of the following village got electricity for the first time, after 70 years of Independence

(A) Patiya

(B) Tikri

(C) ** Shiyal Bet

(D) Kepran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first electric street light was lit in Bangalore on 5 august 1905 .

Concept note-2: -In 2006, Gujarat became the first state to fully electrify all its villages followed by Andhra Pradesh in 2016 and Kerala in 2017.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Gujarat. Gujarat is the first State in India to achieve 100% electrification of households.

Concept note-4: -The power plants at Darjeeling and Shimsha were established in 1898 and 1902 respectively and is one of the first in Asia.
