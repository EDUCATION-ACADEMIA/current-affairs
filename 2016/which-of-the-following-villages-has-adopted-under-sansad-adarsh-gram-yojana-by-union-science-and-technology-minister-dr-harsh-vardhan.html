Which of the following villages has adopted under Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana by Union Science and Technology Minister Dr. Harsh Vardhan

(A) Ghoga

(B) Dheerpur

(C) ** Both of the above

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The plan. Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana was initiated to bring the member of parliament of all the political parties under the same umbrella while taking the responsibility of developing physical and institutional infrastructure in villages and turn them into model villages.

Concept note-2: -Hiware Bazar is a village in the Ahmednagar District of Maharashtra, India.

Concept note-3: -The ‘Adarsh Grams’ are to become schools of local development and governance, inspiring other Gram Panchayats. By involving villagers and leveraging scientific tools, a village development plan is prepared under the leadership of Member of Parliament. The distinct feature of this Yojana is that it is: Demand Driven.
