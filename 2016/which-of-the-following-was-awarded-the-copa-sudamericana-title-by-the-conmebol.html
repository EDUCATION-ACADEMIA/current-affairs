Which of the following was awarded the Copa Sudamericana title by the CONMEBOL

(A) Philippine

(B) Argebtina

(C) Japan

(D) ** Chapecoense

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current champion is Independiente del Valle, who defeated São Paulo in the 2022 edition.

Concept note-2: -The CONMEBOL Sudamericana, named as Copa Sudamericana (Spanish pronunciation: [ˈkopa suðameɾiˈkana]; Portuguese: Copa Sul-Americana [ˈkɔpɐ ˈsulɐmeɾiˈkɐnɐ]), is an annual international club football competition organized by CONMEBOL since 2002.

Concept note-3: -CONMEBOL. /ˈkɑːn.mɪ.bɑːl/ uk. /ˈkɒn.mɪ.bɒl/ the organization for soccer in South America, also known as the Confederación Sudamericana de Fútbol.

Concept note-4: -Independiente of Argentina is the most successful club in the cup’s history, having won the tournament seven times. Argentine clubs have accumulated the most victories with 25 wins, while Brazil has the largest number of winning teams, with 10 clubs having won the title.

Concept note-5: -The oldest continental confederation in the world, its headquarters are located in Luque, Paraguay, near Asunción. CONMEBOL is responsible for the organization and governance of South American football’s major international tournaments.
