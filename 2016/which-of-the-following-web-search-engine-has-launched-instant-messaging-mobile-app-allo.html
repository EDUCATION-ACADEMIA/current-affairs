(B) Bing

(C) AOL.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Use Google Chat to message a person or group: On Google Chat for web. On the desktop app. On the Android mobile app.

Concept note-4: -Instant messaging was pioneered in the early Internet era; the IRC protocol was the earliest to achieve wide adoption.
