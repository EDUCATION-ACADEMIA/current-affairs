Which organization has released the World Development Indicators 2016

(A) ** World Bank Group

(B) UNESCO

(C) Td Bank Group

(D) WTO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Global Monitoring Report 2015/2016, produced by the World Bank Group in partnership with the International Monetary Fund, comes at an inflection point in both the setting of global development goals and the demographic trends affecting those goals.

Concept note-2: -World Development Indicators (WDI), comprehensive set of data and statistics published annually by the World Bank that allows for the evaluation of the development of most countries in the world.

Concept note-3: -The World Bank publishes the World Development Report (WDR) annually. It has been published since 1978. The latest world bank report is the World Development Report 2022 which was released by the World Bank on February 15th.

Concept note-4: -World Development Indicators (WDI) is the World Bank’s premier compilation of cross-country comparable data on development.

Concept note-5: -The World Bank’s World Development Report, published annually since 1978, is an invaluable guide to the economic, social, and environmental state of the world today.
