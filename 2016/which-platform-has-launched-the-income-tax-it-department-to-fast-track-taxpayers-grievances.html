Which platform has launched the Income Tax (IT) department to fast track taxpayers grievances

(A) e-ITDept

(B) ** e-nivaran

(C) e-fasttrack

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Income Tax department to promote the paperless environment launched ‘e-nivaran’, a special electronic system in order to fast-track taxpayer grievances and ensure early resolution or redressal of their complaints.

Concept note-2: -The Grievance Cell receives the grievance petitions of the public and redresses the same. The petitions are received in the following manner: The assessees file the grievance petitions at the main Information and Facilitation Counter. The assessees send their petitions by post to the Grievance Cell.

Concept note-3: -“Pratidhwani”, which translates to ‘resonance’, it denotes the movement, reflection and multiplication of sounds, ideas and communication.

Concept note-4: -Tax Information Network (TIN) is an initiative by Income Tax Department of India (ITD) for the modernization of the current system for collection, processing, monitoring and accounting of direct taxes using information technology.
