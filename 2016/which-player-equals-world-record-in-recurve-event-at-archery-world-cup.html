Which player equals world record in recurve event at Archery World Cup

(A) Lee Seung yun

(B) ** Deepika Kumari

(C) Xu Jing

(D) Sara Lopez

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The farthest archery target hit (male) is 330 m (1, 083 ft), and was achieved by Tyler Toney (USA) on the set of Dude Perfect in Sadler, Texas, USA, on 2 September 2022.

Concept note-2: -1. Deepika Kumari. Deepika Kumari, widely regarded as the best archer, has received numerous medals and honours. She made headlines when she won two gold medals in individual and team events at the Commonwealth Games at the age of 16.

Concept note-3: -Parth Salunke, in fact, was part of all three of India’s medals in recurve archery events. Parth teamed up with Mrinal Chauhan and Akash to win the men’s recurve team gold medal.

Concept note-4: -India began the day with the men’s compound team dishing out a superb fightback to prevail over France 232-230 to win the gold medal.
