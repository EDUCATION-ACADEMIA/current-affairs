Which poem has been selected for Bihari Puraskar award 2015

(A) Jagah Jaisi Jagah

(B) Muanjodaro

(C) Dhooni Tape Teer

(D) ** Katha Sun Aawe Hai Sabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -32nd Bihari Award 2022: K.K. The 32nd Bihari Award for the year 2022 will be given to Dr. Madhav Harda by the Birla Foundation for his literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chol Pahar Sakhi Ri’. K.K. Birla Foundation made this announcement in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Bihari Puraskar (2021-22): Noted writers Madhu Kankariya and Dr Madhav Hada were awarded the 31st and 32nd Bihari Puraskar, respectively. Kankariya was awarded the Puraskar for her 2018 novel ‘Hum Yahan The’ while Hada was awarded for his 2015 literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chola Pahar Sakhi Ri’.

Concept note-3: -Noted Hindi writer Dr Madhav Harda has been selected for the 32nd Bihari Puraskar 2022 for his literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chola Pahar Sakhi Ri’. This was announced by the K K Birla Foundation on 29 September 2022.

Concept note-4: -The Bihari Puraskar is one of the three literary awards instituted by KK Birla Foundation in 1991. Named after the famous poet Bihari, the award for Rajasthani authors carries a cash prize of ₹2.5 lakh, a plaque and a citation. Writers Dr Madhav Hada and Madhu Kankariya were awarded the Bihari Puraskar on Wednesday.
