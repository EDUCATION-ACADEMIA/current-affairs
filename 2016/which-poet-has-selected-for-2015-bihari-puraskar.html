Which poet has selected for 2015 Bihari Puraskar

(A) Om Thanvi

(B) ** Bhagwati Lal Vyas

(C) Suryakanta Devi

(D) Chandra Prakash Deval

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Madhav Hada will be awarded the 32nd Bihari Puraskar for his 2015 literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chola Pahar Sakhi Ri’, the KK Birla Foundation announced on Thursday. The Bihari Puraskar is one of the three literary awards instituted by the foundation in 1991.

Concept note-2: -32nd Bihari Award 2022: K.K. The 32nd Bihari Award for the year 2022 will be given to Dr. Madhav Harda by the Birla Foundation for his literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chol Pahar Sakhi Ri’. K.K. Birla Foundation made this announcement in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Writers Dr Madhav Hada and Madhu Kankariya were awarded the Bihari Puraskar on Wednesday. Noted writers Madhu Kankariya and Dr Madhav Hada on Wednesday were awarded the 31st and 32nd Bihari Puraskar, respectively. The Bihari Puraskar is one of the three literary awards instituted by KK Birla Foundation in 1991.

Concept note-4: -Author Madhu Kankariya has been chosen for the 31st Bihari Puraskar for her 2018 novel ‘Hum Yahan The’. The award carries a cash prize of ₹2.5 lakh, a plaque and a citation.

Concept note-5: -Madhu Kankariya won 2021 Bihari Puraskar for her book ‘Hum Yahan The’.
