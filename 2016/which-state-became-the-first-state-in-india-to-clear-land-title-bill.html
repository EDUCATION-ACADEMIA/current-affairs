Which state became the first state in India to clear land title bill

(A) ** Rajasthan

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Haryana

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajasthan becomes the first state in India to clear land title Bill.

Concept note-2: -Till the Rajasthan law was passed, India did not guarantee titles.

Concept note-3: -An Act to provide for survey and maintenance of record of urban lands and certification of titles thereof and matters connected therewith and incidental thereto. 1. Short title, extent and commencement.-(1) This Act may be called the Rajasthan Urban Land (Certification of Titles) Act, 2016.

Concept note-4: -The existing land acquisition act was framed in 1894, during British rule, and authorises individual states to acquire land in the “general interest".

Concept note-5: -The Bill provides for the registration of all immovable property to establish a system of conclusive, electronically recorded titles. It also provides for a mechanism to invite objections and for the resolution of disputes through special tribunals.
