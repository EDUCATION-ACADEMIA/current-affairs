Which state government has directed all the district collectors to extend Madhu Babu Pension Scheme to three lakh more beneficiaries from August 15

(A) ** Odisha

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Karnataka

(D) Maharastra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Odisha CM orders to provide pension to beneficiaries under Madhu Babu Pension Scheme within 15 days.

Concept note-2: -All the beneficiaries being covered under State Old Age Pension Scheme and the Odisha Disability Pension Scheme in the State at present will be treated as beneficiaries under the Madhu Babu Pension Yojana from the date it come into force.

Concept note-3: -In January 2008, State Government merged two old schemes, namely, Revised Old Age Pension Rules, 1989 Et Disability Pension Rules, 1985 and introduced the Madhu Babu Pension Yojana Rules, 2008.

Concept note-4: -Eligibility Criteria : The age of the beneficiaries should be more than 60 years. He/She must be a permanent resident/domicile of Odisha. He/She should not be involved in criminal activities.
