Which state government has launched the Biju Kanya Ratna Yojana (BKRY) for the development of girls in three districts of the state.

(A) Haryana

(B) ** Odisha

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Odisha government on Saturday launched the Biju Kanya Ratna Yojana (BKRY) for the development of girls in three districts of the state.

Concept note-2: -In order to address this issue holistically the state government launched the Biju Kanya Ratna Yojana (Ama Kanya Ama Ratna) on 3rd September 2016 in three districts of Odisha-Ganjam, Dhenkanal and Angul.

Concept note-3: -The name of this particular program is Biju Kanya Ratan Yojana. BKRS was launched in Orissa by the state government. The scheme is similar to the other schemes supporting girl child and their education, Mazi Kanya Bhagyashree Yojana and Beti Bachao Beti padhao scheme.
