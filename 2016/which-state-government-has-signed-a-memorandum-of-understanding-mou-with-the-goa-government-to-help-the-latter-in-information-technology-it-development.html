Which state government has signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with the Goa government to help the latter in information technology (IT) development.

(A) Karnataka

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) ** Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In this regard, BSE has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the government of Goa, the exchange said in a statement.

Concept note-2: -The Maharashtra government has signed a MoU with the British Council to strengthen educational and cultural cooperation.

Concept note-3: -India and Panama have signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) to encourage cooperation in the training of diplomats. The MoU between India and Panama was signed by the External Affairs Minister of India Dr. Subramanyam Jaishankar and the Foreign Minister of Panama Janaina Tewaney Mencomon.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The Goa government signed an MoU with the Software Technology Parks of India (STPI) for setting up an information technology (IT) park at Dona Paula.

Concept note-5: -The Comptroller & Auditor General of India and the Comptroller General of the Republic of Chile signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) to strengthen institutional and professional capacities and learn from each other’s best practices.
