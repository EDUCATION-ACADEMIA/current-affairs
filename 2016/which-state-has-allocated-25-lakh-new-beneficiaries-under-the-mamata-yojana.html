Which state has allocated 25 lakh new beneficiaries under the Mamata Yojana

(A) Haryana

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About Odisha Mamata Yojana 2022 Through this scheme, the government is going to provide monetary incentives so that they can improve their nutrition and promote health-seeking behavior. The financial incentives that will be offered under Odisha Mamata Yojana will be Rs 5000.

Concept note-2: -Objectives Of Odisha Mamata Yojana Rupees 5000 will be provided to each and every beneficiary under this scheme and they will be able to get the benefit of this scheme without doing the rounds of Government offices every now and then. The amount will be provided in two instalments of Rupees 3000 and Rupees 2000.
