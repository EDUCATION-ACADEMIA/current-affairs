Which state has constructed 10 lakh pucca houses under various rural housing schemes in past two years

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Bihar

(C) Punjab

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The houses in Gujarat are made of bricks and cement in cities. ‘Pucca’ houses made of cement and bricks are found both in villages and cities of Gujarat. There are many multi-storeyed buildings in the cities.

Concept note-2: -To provide shelter to the homeless people, the Karnataka government has planned to develop one lakh homes for the economically weaker section of society under CM’s One Lakh Housing Scheme. These homes will be built on government land in phases.

Concept note-3: -Basava Vasati Yojana scheme 2023: Benefits The Karnataka government choses beneficiaries for the RGRHCL scheme, belonging to the financially and socially poor section. It provides homes at prices lower that the market price, with the support of the central government.

Concept note-4: -INTRODUCTION. The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) has been devised in line with Government’s commitment to provide ‘Housing for All’ by 2022 in the rural areas. The scheme aims at providing a pucca house with basic amenities to all houseless householder living in kutcha and dilapidated houses by 2022.
