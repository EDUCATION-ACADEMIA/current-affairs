Which state has inaugurated 100 piped water supply projects for rural areas on 28 April 2016

(A) ** Odisha

(B) Bihar

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Gujarat achieves 100% household tap water connections under Jal Jeevan Mission’ | Latest News India-Hindustan Times.

Concept note-2: -26.57 lakh households have been provided with tap water connection since the launch of the Mission. 33 districts, 247 blocks, 13, 927 Gram Panchayats and 18, 187 villages have become ‘Har Ghar Jal’ in Gujarat. Karangadh village: from no tap connection to 100% Tap Water connection in Just One Year!

Concept note-3: -Goa, Telangana and Haryana have achieved 100% tap connectivity to all households. Union Territories like Puducherry, the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Dadra and Nagar Haveli, and Daman and Diu, have also provided 100% of their households with tap water connections.

Concept note-4: -Seven states, UTs achieve ‘100% piped water connection’ goal Goa, Telangana, Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Dadra and Nagar Haveli, Daman and Diu, Puducherry and Haryana are the states that can now boast of 100 per cent household water connections.
