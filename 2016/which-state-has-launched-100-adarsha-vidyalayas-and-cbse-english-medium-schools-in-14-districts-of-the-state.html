Which state has launched 100 Adarsha Vidyalayas and CBSE English medium schools in 14 districts of the state

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Odisha

(C) Bihar

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Odisha government has launched 100 Adarsha Vidyalayas and CBSE-affiliated English medium schools in 14 districts of the state.

Concept note-2: -So far, Adarsha Vidyalayas have become functional in 74 educationally backward blocks of Karnataka from 2010-11 onwards.

Concept note-3: -The Cuttack Zilla school, Odisha’s first modern school up to matriculation level, was established in 1866. It was extended to become a Collegiate school in 1868, which provided first and second year college education to Odia students.
