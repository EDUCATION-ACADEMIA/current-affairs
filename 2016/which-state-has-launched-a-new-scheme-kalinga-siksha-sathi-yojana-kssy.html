Which state has launched a new scheme Kalinga Siksha Sathi Yojana (KSSY)

(A) ** Odisha

(B) Haryana

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Odisha Government has come up with a new scheme named as Kalinga Sikhya Sathi Yojana (KSSY) for the aspiring students at only 1% interest.

Concept note-2: -Under Arivu Education Loan Scheme 2022 (KMDC), religious minority students who are desirous of pursuing professional courses like Medical, Engineering, and Dental, are provided financial assistance ranging from Rs. 20, 000/-to Rs. 30, 000/-per year till the completion of the course.

Concept note-3: -Banks Offering Education Loan in Bihar 6.90% to 8.90% p.a. 6.75% to 9.50% p.a.

Concept note-4: -Collateral Required for Education Loan 10 lakh for programs in India and Rs. 20 lakh for programs abroad. According to the rules, collateral security is not needed for loans up to Rs. 4 lakh.
