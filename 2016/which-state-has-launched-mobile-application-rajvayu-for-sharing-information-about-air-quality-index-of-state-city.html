Which state has launched mobile application RajVayu for sharing information about air quality index of state city

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Goa

(C) ** Rajasthan

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SAFAR or the System of Air Quality and Weather Forecasting and Research is a project by the Indian government’s Ministry of Earth Sciences. The app is available on Android and iOS. The website is also available.

Concept note-2: -India’s first air quality checking Mobile App, SAFAR-Air was launched on 17 February 2015 at the Indian Institute of Tropical Meteorology in Pune, Maharashtra. SAFAR is an acronym for System of Air Quality Weather Forecasting and Research which was first launched in Delhi in 2010 during the Commonwealth Games.

Concept note-3: -The National Air Quality Quality (AQI) India was launched on 17 September 2014 in New Delhi under the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan by Environment Minister Shri PrakashJavadekar.

Concept note-4: -Users can also choose between LAQI (Local AQI) or BAQI (Breezometer AQI) data for more accurate information. Another nice app you can try out is IQAir+.
