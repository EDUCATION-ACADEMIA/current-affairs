Which state has launched the schemes to distribute Energy Efficient Agriculture Pumps and Fans

(A) Telangana

(B) ** Andhra Pradesh

(C) Karnataka

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Synopsis. The pilot fan distribution programme will be launched by EESL in Andhra Pradesh with a two year installment scheme.

Concept note-2: -In order to make country more energy efficient, the National Energy Efficient Agriculture Pumps Programme has been launched by the Ministry of Power.

Concept note-3: -A consumer can buy the fans by paying Rs 60 a month per fan on EMI basis. The EMI amount will be added to the consumers’ electricity bills for two years. These energy-efficient ceiling fans will cost Rs 1440/-to a consumer who uses the installment route.

Concept note-4: -One of the most simple, but often overlooked, measures to save energy is to eliminate unnecessary use. Pumping system efficiency measures include shutting down unnecessary pumps and using pressure switches to control the number of pumps in service when flow-rate requirements vary.
