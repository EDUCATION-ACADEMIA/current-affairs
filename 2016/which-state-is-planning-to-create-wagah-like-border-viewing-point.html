Which state is planning to create Wagah-like border viewing point

(A) ** Gujarat

(B) Jammu and Kashmir

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It has been built as a tourist attraction on the lines of the Wagah-Atari border in Punjab. Union Home Minister Amit Shah on Sunday inaugurated the India-Pakistan border viewing point in Nada Bet in Banaskantha district of north Gujarat .

Concept note-2: -Located in the Rann of Kutch region, this attraction, also known as the ‘Wagah of Gujarat’, is connected by a narrow bitumen road cutting across mudflats that get inundated during high-tide.

Concept note-3: -Wagah Border | District Amritsar, Government of Punjab | India.

Concept note-4: -Map of the India–Pakistan border along the Rann of Kutch between Gujarat, India and Sindh, Pakistan. A tourist in front of the Wagah border post, on the Pakistani side, looking towards Attari on the Indian side.
