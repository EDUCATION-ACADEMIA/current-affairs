Which state will host the 2022 National Games

(A) Assam

(B) Haryana

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Meghalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2022 National Games of India, also known as the 36th National Games of India and informally as Gujarat 2022, was the 36th edition of the National Games of India and was held in Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Surat, Vadodara, Rajkot and Bhavnagar in the state of Gujarat between 29 September and 12 October 2022.

Concept note-2: -Meghalaya had signed the Host City Contract with the Indian Olympic Association (IOA) for hosting the 39th National Games in 2022 on January 3, 2018.

Concept note-3: -About 7500 players from 36 states and union territories participated in 36 types of sports competitions held in six cities of the state. Goa will host the 37th National Games in October next year. The Goa delegation received the IOA flag at the Closing Ceremony of the 36th National Games in Surat yesterday.
