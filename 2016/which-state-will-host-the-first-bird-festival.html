Which state will host the first “Bird Festival”

(A) ** Goa

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Kerala

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -KOLKATA: The first-ever Bird Festival will take place in the Sunderbans in February with the aim to create a platform for knowledge-sharing and discussion on the future of conservation of the Sunderban birds. The festival is scheduled to be held from February 7 to 10, 2023.

Concept note-2: -GIDHWA-PARSADA BIRD FESTIVAL 2021 • (31st January-2nd February) • 1ST EVER BIRD FESTIVAL OF CHHATTISGARH • Chhattisgarh hosted it’s first ever bird festival of 3 days from 31st of January-2nd of February to boost our natural environment, intended to support conservation measures and to observe wildlife.

Concept note-3: -Notes: Bihar state is set to organise the first ever migratory bird festival this year. This three-day long festival is aimed to enhance the awareness about migratory birds among common public and to check cases of poaching in the region.

Concept note-4: -First Bird Festival of Chhattisgarh, District-Bemetara: The first bird festival of Chhattisgarh will be organized in the villages of Gidhwa and Parsada, which give shelter to birds of different species of the country and the world. It will run for three days from 31 January to 2 February.

Concept note-5: -Bihar CM Nitish Kumar inaugurated Bihar’s first state bird festival ‘Kalrav’ on 16 January 2021.
