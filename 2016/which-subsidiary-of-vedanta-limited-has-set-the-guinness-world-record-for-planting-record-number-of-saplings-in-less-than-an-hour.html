Which subsidiary of Vedanta Limited has set the Guinness World Record for planting record number of saplings in less than an hour

(A) Tata companies

(B) Aditya Birla Group

(C) ** Talwandi Sabo Power Limited

(D) DHFL Venture Capital Fund

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MANSA: Talwandi Sabo Power Limited (TSPL), a subsidiary of Vedanta Limited, has officially set the Guinness World Record for simultaneously planting 2, 08, 751 saplings at a single location in less than an hour.

Concept note-2: -All Guinness World Records record titles must fulfill key criteria including: Measurable-Is it the fastest / longest / heaviest / most? Breakable-Can the record be broken or repeated by someone else? All our record titles must be open to being challenged.

Concept note-3: -Speaking on the closing ceremony in Prayagraj, chief minister Yogi Adityanath said the first record was made for planting 5 crore plants in the first hour i.e. from 9 am to 10 am. The second record was made for planting 1.1 lakh saplings in Kasganj in presence of governor Anandi Ben Patel.
