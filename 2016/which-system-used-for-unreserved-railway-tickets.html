Which system used for Unreserved Railway Tickets

(A) Paperless Unreserved Ticketing System

(B) Passenger Service System

(C) ** Bar coding System

(D) Ticketing Services

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Unreserved Ticketing System facilitates the ticketing needs of Unreserved journey passengers.

Concept note-2: -With the unreserved ticket booking app, passengers can board the train 3 hours after the booking. To book platform tickets, you need to be within a radius of 2 km of the station or 15 metres away from the railway track. Passengers can book seasonal tickets for 3 months, 6 months, or a year.

Concept note-3: -Passenger Reservation System (PRS) & Unreserved Ticketing System (UTS)

Concept note-4: -The UTS app promotes paperless economy. This mobile ticketing app enables booking unreserved paperless journey ticket, issue/renew season ticket and platform ticket. This application is available for download on google play store and windows play store for free.

Concept note-5: -Indian Railways offers the facility to purchase unreserved tickets through mobile phone at Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata and Secunderabad.
