Which team won Ranji Trophy 2016

(A) Australia

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Bangalore

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gujarat beat Mumbai in the final by 5 wickets to win their first title. In September 2016, the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) announced the dates, groups and fixtures for the competition.

Concept note-2: -Gujarat have overpowered the heavyweights-Mumbai-to be the Ranji Champions. The battle of the west goes to Gujarat. The Ranji Trophy belongs to Gujarat. After almost 26 years Mumbai have been outclassed and defeated in the finale.

Concept note-3: -The final was contested by Saurashtra and Mumbai, a repeat of the 2012–13 final. Mumbai won the match by an innings and 21 runs to claim their 41st title.

Concept note-4: -The 2017–18 Ranji Trophy was the 84th season of the Ranji Trophy, the premier first-class cricket tournament that took place in India between October 2017 and January 2018. Vidarbha won the tournament, beating Delhi by 9 wickets in the final, to win their first Ranji Trophy title.

Concept note-5: -The 1934–35 Ranji Trophy was the inaugural season of the Ranji Trophy. It was contested between 15 teams in four zones in a knockout format. Bombay defeated Northern India in the final.
