Which Telecom company is going to buy the videocon telecom spectrum

(A) Aircel

(B) Vodafone

(C) Idea

(D) ** Bharti Airtel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s largest telecom operator by subcriber base Bharti Airtel has acquired Videocon Telecom for Rs 4, 428 crore. India’s largest telecom operator by subcriber base, Bharti Airtel, said on Thursday that it has acquired Videocon Telecom for Rs 4, 428 crore.

Concept note-2: -Bharti Airtel has paid Rs 8312.4 crore to the Department of Telecommunications (DoT) towards dues for spectrum acquired in the recently concluded 5G auctions. Airtel has taken up the option to pay more upfront amount as against 20 equal annual instalments, paying four years of the 2022 spectrum dues upfront.

Concept note-3: -Airtel in the lead Its strategy towards spectrum acquisition has been smart and deliberate over the years, and today, it has the largest pool of low and mid band spectrum (Sub GHz/ 1800/ 2100/ 2300 bands) which can provide the best 5G coverage.
