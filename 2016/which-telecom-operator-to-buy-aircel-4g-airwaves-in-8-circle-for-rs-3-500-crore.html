Which telecom operator to buy aircel 4G airwaves in 8 circle for Rs 3, 500 crore

(A) Vodafone

(B) Idea

(C) BSNL

(D) ** Airtel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Telecom Ministry on Sunday cleared the 4G spectrum deal worth Rs 3500 crore between Bharti Airtel and Aircel. As per the agreement, Airtel will acquire Aircel’s rights to use 4G spectrum in eight telecom circles for Rs 3500 crore.

Concept note-2: -Airtel acquires Aircel’s 4G spectrum for ₹3, 500 crore The deal comes ahead of the mega launch of telecom services by Mukesh Ambani’s Reliance Jio, which until now was the only player with pan-Indian 4G airwaves.

Concept note-3: -Bharti Airtel Limited, commonly known as (d/b/a) Airtel, is an Indian multinational telecommunications services company based in New Delhi. It operates in 18 countries across South Asia and Africa, as well as the Channel Islands.
