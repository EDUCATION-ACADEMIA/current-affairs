Which tropical fish species is best known for splitting pressurised water jets to shoot prey out of the air and is a “smart” animal, according to research

(A) ** Archefish

(B) Angel fish

(C) Goldfish

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Archerfish boast an unusual talent: They hunt by spitting powerful streams of water out of their mouths at unsuspecting above-water insects.

Concept note-2: -Archerfish are famous for shooting mouthfuls of water at insects to dislodge them from vegetation above the water.

Concept note-3: -The archerfish (spinner fish or archer fish) form a monotypic family, Toxotidae, of fish known for their habit of preying on land-based insects and other small animals by shooting them down with water droplets from their specialized mouths. The family is small, consisting of ten species in a single genus, Toxotes.

Concept note-4: -In the wild: Archerfish are essentially carnivorous. Their diet is mainly insects, small aquatic crustaceans, insect larvae and zooplankton.
