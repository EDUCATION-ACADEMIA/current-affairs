Which UN agency has adopted two new recommendations on education and training

(A) FAO

(B) ** UNESCO

(C) ILO

(D) WHO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) was born on 16 November 1945. UNESCO has 195 Members and 8 Associate Members and is governed by the General Conference and the Executive Board.

Concept note-2: -Hint: The United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organisation(UNESCO) is a part of the United Nations which is responsible for global unity and world peace through education and cultural means. The United Nations Children Emergency Fund(UNICEF) is a humanitarian service provided to children worldwide.

Concept note-3: -UNESCO works with countries to increase the supply of qualified, motivated teachers by improving policies relating to them and supporting quality training which meets learners’ needs. Special focus is placed on Africa, inclusion, gender sensitivity and digital technologies.

Concept note-4: -UNESCO is the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization. It contributes to peace and security by promoting international cooperation in education, sciences, culture, communication and information.
