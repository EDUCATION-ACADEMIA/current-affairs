Which Union Ministry launched the Startup India portal and mobile app on March 31, 2016

(A) Minister of Civil Aviation

(B) Minister of Science and Technology

(C) Minister of Human Resource Development

(D) ** Ministry of Commerce and Industry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The campaign was first announced by Indian Prime Minister, Narendra Modi during his speech on 15 August 2015.

Concept note-2: -List of Government Mobile Apps: 1) Aarogya Setu App. 2) Aaykar Setu App. 3) BHIM UPI App.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sports Ministry. Fit India Movement: The Fit India Movement was launched on 29th August 2019 by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi.

Concept note-4: -Startup India is a flagship initiative launched by the Government of India on 16th January, 2016 to build a strong eco-system for nurturing innovation and startups in the country which will drive economic growth and generate large scale employment opportunities.
