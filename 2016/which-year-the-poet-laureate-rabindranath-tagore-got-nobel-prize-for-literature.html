Which year the poet laureate Rabindranath Tagore got Nobel Prize for Literature

(A) 1893

(B) 1925

(C) ** 1913

(D) 1909

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Poet Rabindranath Tagore won the Nobel Prize for Literature in 1913 for his collection Gitanjali published in London in 1912. The prize gained even more significance by being given to an Indian for the first time. This honour established Tagore’s literary reputation worldwide.

Concept note-2: -Rabindranath Tagore became the first non-European to win a Nobel Prize in Literature on this day in 1913. On November 13, 1913, Tagore was announced as the first non-European Literature Laureate, reveals a post shared by the Nobel Prize Organisation.
