Who among the following film actor has been selected by France for its prestigious Chevalier de L’Ordre Arts et Lettres.

(A) Rajinikanth

(B) Shahrukh Khan

(C) ** Kamal Haasan

(D) Amitabh Bachchan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Kamal Hassan. Kamal Hassan had won the Henri Langlois Award for his contribution to cinema in France as a child artist in his debut movie Kalathoor Kannamma in 1959. In 2016, He was awarded the Knight Of the Order of Arts and Letters for his huge contribution to cinema.

Concept note-2: -In 2016, Actor Kamal Haasan was awarded the Chevalier de L’Ordre Arts et Lettres (The Knight of the Order of Arts and Letters) by the French government. The French government honoured Kamal Haasan by knighting him for his outstanding contribution to Indian cinema.

Concept note-3: -Sir Michael LaPlante, received the Legion of Honour in 2016, from the French Government and title of “Chevalier” (Knight); the highest French Order for military and civil merits.

Concept note-4: -Carnatic vocalist Aruna Sairam will be conferred the Chevalier de l’Ordre National du Mérite, the Knight of the Order of the Merit, by the French government. She has been selected for her singing talent and contribution to the development of Indo-France relations.

Concept note-5: -French service-members For active-duty commissioned officers, the Legion of Honour award for service is achieved after 20 years of meritorious service, having been awarded the rank of Chevalier of the Ordre National du Mérite.
