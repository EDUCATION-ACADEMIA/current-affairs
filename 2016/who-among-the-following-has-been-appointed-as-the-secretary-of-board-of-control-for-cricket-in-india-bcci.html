Who among the following has been appointed as the secretary of Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI)

(A) Anurag Thakur

(B) ** Ajay Shirke

(C) Amitabh Choudhary

(D) M L Nehru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of 18 October 2022, Jay Shah, the son of the Home Minister of India Amit Shah, is BCCI’s secretary. Rajiv Shukla of the Congress party who is associated with the BCCI from more than a decade is its vice-president and Ashish Shelar of BJP is its treasurer.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Ravi Shastri.

Concept note-3: -The selected All-India Women’s Selection Committee comprises: Neetu David, Chairman. Arati Vaidya. Renu Margrate.

Concept note-4: -It is the governing body of Indian cricket. The BCCI headquarters is at ‘Wankhede Stadium’ in Mumbai. The first president of BCCI was ‘Grant Govan’, while the secretary was ‘Anthony De Mello’.
