Who among the following Sri Lankan Cricketer has been appointed as the Past Player representative in ICC cricket committee

(A) Tillakaratne Dilshan

(B) Sanath Jayasuriya

(C) Kumar Sangakkara

(D) ** Mahela Jayawardena

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Daniel Vettori and VVS Laxman have also been appointed to the ICC Men’s Cricket Committee as current player representatives, while Roger Harper has been appointed as the second past player representative, joining Sri Lanka’s Mahela Jayawardena, ” stated ICC in an official statement.

Concept note-2: -Greg Barclay has been unanimously re-elected as chairman of the International Cricket Council (ICC) for a second two-year term.

Concept note-3: -July 2022-Prabath Jayasuriya (Sri Lanka) Sri Lanka’s newest spin sensation Prabath Jayasuriya was crowned ahead of rival contenders England’s Jonny Bairstow-who won the Men’s award back in June-and French youngster Gustav McKeon.

Concept note-4: -ICC Awards: 23-year-old Harry Brook fended off stiff competition from Babar Azam and Travis Head to win the ICC Men’s Player of the Month Award for December 2022.
