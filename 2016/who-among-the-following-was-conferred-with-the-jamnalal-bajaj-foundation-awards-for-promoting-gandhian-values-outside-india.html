Who among the following was conferred with the Jamnalal Bajaj Foundation Awards for promoting Gandhian values outside India

(A) Theresa May

(B) Barack Obama

(C) ** Sheikh Rached Ghannouchi

(D) Haider al-Abadi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International Award for Promoting Gandhian Values outside India: Dr Ogirat Younan and Dr Walid Slyabi of Lebanon was awarded the Jamnalal Bajaj award for Promoting Gandhian Values outside India.

Concept note-2: -He founded the Bajaj Group of companies in the 1920s, and the group now has 24 companies, including six that are listed on the bourses. He was also a close and beloved associate of Mahatma Gandhi, who is known to have often declared that Jamnalal was his fifth son.

Concept note-3: -Jamnalal Bajaj was born on 4 November 1889, in a village in Rajasthan. At the age of 5, he was adopted by a wealthy businessman from Wardha, Bachraj Seth.

Concept note-4: -Humanitarian, freedom fighter, philanthropist, social reformer and a devoted Mahatma Gandhi follower, Jamnalalji was adopted as the fifth son by Gandhiji. He was the alter ego of the Mahatma in the Gandhian constructive movement, who took active part in the Indian freedom struggle.

Concept note-5: -Jamnalal Bajaj Award is an Indian award, for promoting Gandhian values, community service and social development.
