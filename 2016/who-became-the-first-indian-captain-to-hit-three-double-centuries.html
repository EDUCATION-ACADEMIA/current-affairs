Who became the first Indian captain to hit three double centuries

(A) Ajinkya Rahane

(B) ** Virat Kohli

(C) Suresh Raina

(D) Mahendra Singh Dhoni

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rohit Sharma becomes the first Indian captain to score centuries in all 3 formats of international cricket. There were a lot of questions surrounding Rohit Sharma’s form who had struggled to hit his stride in the past one year.

Concept note-2: -Kohli 1st Indian captain to score 200 overseas. Virat Kohli became the first Indian captain to score a double century outside India when he reached 200 against West Indies on Friday.

Concept note-3: -in the 2015 Cricket World Cup. Rohit Sharma is the only player with three ODI double centuries having scored 209, 264 and 208*.

Concept note-4: -Polly Umrigar, in 1955, scored the first ever Test double century by an Indian batsman. Virat Kohli tops the double century list among the Indian cricketers. Virat scored his 7th double ton in the match against South Africa on October 2019 at Pune.

Concept note-5: -Virat Kohli is the only Captain who scored 3 consecutive centuries both in Tests and ODIs.
