Who became the first Indian to clinch two gold medals at the Paralympics 2016

(A) Varun Singh Bhati

(B) Deepa Malik

(C) Mariyappan Thangavelu

(D) ** Devendra Jhajharia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Devendra Jhajharia (born 10 June 1981) is an Indian Paralympic javelin thrower competing in F46 events. He is the first Indian Paralympics player to win two gold medals at the Paralympics.

Concept note-2: -Mariyappan Thangavelu is the first gold medallist for India in 2016 Paralympics.

Concept note-3: -Avani Lekhara became the first Indian to win a gold medal at the Paralympic Games. She went on to clinch a bronze at a later event thus becoming the first Indian woman to win multiple Paralympic medals.

Concept note-4: -SPORTS. Rio 2016 featured 22 sports, two more than London 2012, with canoe and triathlon making their Paralympic debut.
