Who became the first Thai golfer to win the Women’s British Open

(A) Ariya Campanero

(B) Lee Mirim

(C) ** Ariya Jutanugam

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ariya Jutanagarn made history this year when she became the first Thai golfer, male or female, to win a major golf championship, taking first place at the Women’s British Open in July 2016. This record-breaking golfer also won the 2016 LPGA Player of the Year Award last November.

Concept note-2: -The reigning champion is Ashleigh Buhai, who won in a playoff at Muirfield in 2022.

Concept note-3: -Ariya has an older sister Moriya, who is also a professional golfer. She has won two times on the LPGA Tour. 3. Their parents are father Somboon and mother Narumon and they have four older half-siblings through their father.
