Who becomes first Indian to win Mister World title

(A) Nicklas Pedersen

(B) ** Rohit Khandelwal

(C) Francisco Escobar

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rohit Khandelwal (born 19 August 1989), is an Indian model, actor, television personality, winner of Mr India 2015 and the first ever Indian to be crowned Mister World in the 2016 contest.

Concept note-2: -The first Mister India called Graviera Adonis Man of the Year contest was held in 1995. Bikram Saluja of Punjab was named the first ever Mister India and represented India at Mister World, where he was placed among top 10 finalists.

Concept note-3: -The current Mr. India’s world is Vishnu Raj Menon from Bengaluru. He was declared as Peter England Mr. India 2016 on 24th November 2016.
