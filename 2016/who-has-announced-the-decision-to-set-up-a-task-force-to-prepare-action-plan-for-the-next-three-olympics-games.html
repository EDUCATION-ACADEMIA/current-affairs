Who has announced the decision to set up a task force to prepare action Plan for the next three Olympics Games

(A) Pranab Mukherjee

(B) Rajnath Singh

(C) ** Narendra Modi

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Where will the 2024 Olympics be held? The 2024 Olympics will be held in Paris, France. This means that the 2024 Paris Olympics will be the sixth time that France has hosted the Olympic Games in total, having hosted two summer games in Paris previously in 1900 and 1924.

Concept note-2: -The first celebration of the modern Olympic Games took place in its ancient birthplace-Greece. The Games attracted athletes from 14 nations, with the largest delegations coming from Greece, Germany, France and Great Britain.

Concept note-3: -The Olympic Charter specifies that: “The goal of Olympism is to place sport at the service of the harmonious development of humankind, with a view to promoting a peaceful society concerned with the preservation of human dignity.”

Concept note-4: -The Olympic Movement is the concerted, organised, universal and permanent action, carried out under the supreme authority of the IOC, of all individuals and entities who are inspired by the values of Olympism. It covers the five continents.
