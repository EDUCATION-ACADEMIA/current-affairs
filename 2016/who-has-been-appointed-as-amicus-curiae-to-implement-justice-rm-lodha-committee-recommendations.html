Who has been appointed as amicus curiae to implement Justice RM Lodha committee recommendations

(A) Gopal Pillai

(B) Gopala Krishnan

(C) ** Gopal Subramanium

(D) Gopal Srinivasan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Supreme Court on Thursday appointed senior advocate Maninder Singh as an amicus curiae to assist it in the matter related to the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) and fixed the hearing on the plea of the cricket body for amending its constitution concerning tenure of office bearers on July 28.

Concept note-2: -Hence an Amicus Curiae was appointed by the court who was a lawyer practicing on the criminal side.

Concept note-3: -In general, Amicus Curiae is someone who is not a party to the litigation, but who believes that the court’s decision may affect its interest. The person who is usually allowed by the India courts, to act as amicus curiae, are people who represent the unbiased will and opinion of the society.

Concept note-4: -amicus curiae, (Latin: “friend of the court”), one who assists the court by furnishing information or advice regarding questions of law or fact.
