Who has been appointed as India’s Ambassador to Jordan

(A) R. Masakui

(B) Anil Trigunayat

(C) ** Shubhdarshini Tripathi

(D) K. Nandini Singla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -01 Aug 2019-Ambassador Anwar Haleem (IFS:1991), assumed charge as Ambassador of India to the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. 19 December 2022-India Jordan Business Meet.

Concept note-2: -Q. Who was recently appointed as the Ambassador of India to the United States? Notes: Veteran diplomat Taranjit Singh Sandhu has been recently appointed as the next Indian Ambassador to the United States. He replaces Harsh Vardhan Shringla, who was the previous Indian envoy to the US.

Concept note-3: -Ambassador Taranjit Singh Sandhu is one of the most experienced Indian diplomats on US affairs, having served in the Indian Mission in Washington DC twice earlier.

Concept note-4: -The President of India is responsible for appointing ambassadors to the foreign countries. The appointment of ambassadors to foreign countries as the Prime Minister give name list to The President. The Ministry of External Affairs nominates an Individual name and The Prime Minister approves it.
