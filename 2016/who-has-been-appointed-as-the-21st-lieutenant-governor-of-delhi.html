Who has been appointed as the 21st Lieutenant Governor of Delhi.

(A) Kiran Bedi

(B) Praful Khoda Patel

(C) Farooq Khan

(D) ** Anil Baijal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Vinai Kumar Saxena took over as the 22nd Lieutenant Governor of Delhi on 26th May 2022.

Concept note-2: -Prior to his appointment as the LG of Delhi, Shri Saxena served as the Chairman of Khadi & Village Industries Commission (KVIC) from October 2015 to May 2022.

Concept note-3: -The lieutenant governor’s name is Devendra Kumar Joshi, who took office on 8 November 2017. Devendra Kumar Singh was appointed by Ram Nath Kovind and has been the President of India since 25 July 2017. Delhi, which is the capital of India, is also a union territory, but unlike the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

Concept note-4: -Vinai Kumar Saxena (born 23 March 1958) is the Lieutenant Governor of Delhi since 26 May 2022, appointed by BJP led Union Government. On 25 October 2015 he was appointed as the Chairman of Khadi and Village Industries Commission, an Organization under the Ministry of Medium, Small and Micro Enterprise.
