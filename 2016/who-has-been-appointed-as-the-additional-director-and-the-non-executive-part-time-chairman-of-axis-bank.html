Who has been appointed as the Additional Director and the Non-Executive (Part-Time) Chairman of Axis bank

(A) Som Mittal

(B) Prasad R Menon

(C) ** Sanjiv Misra

(D) Rakesh Makhija

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Independent Director & Non Executive (Part time) Chairman-Axis Bank. Rakesh Makhija is an Independent Director on the Board of Axis Bank Limited since 27th October 2015.

Concept note-2: -RBI approves Rakesh Makhija’s reappointment as Axis Bank’s Non-Executive Chairman.

Concept note-3: -Amitabh Chaudhry reappointed Axis Bank CEO.

Concept note-4: -Rajiv Anand-Deputy Managing Director and Board of Directors-Axis Bank.

Concept note-5: -Rajiv joined Axis AMC in 2009 and was the founding Managing Director & CEO of Axis Asset Management Co. Ltd. He was appointed as the President (Retail Banking) of Axis Bank in May 2013, to spearhead the business with his sharp acumen which led to the achievement of several key milestones.
