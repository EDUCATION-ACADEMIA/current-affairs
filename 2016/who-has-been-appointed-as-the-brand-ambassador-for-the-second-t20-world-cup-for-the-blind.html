Concept note-2: -Bengaluru, January 6th 2023: Indian Women’s cricket team captain Harmanpreet Kaur is the Brand Ambassador for the Women’s Blind Cricket.

Concept note-3: -Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru, Karnataka on 17 December 2022. It was the third title for India having won it in 2012 and 2017. All the three T20 World Cup for Blind has been hosted by India.
