Who has been appointed as the Central Bank governor of Bangladesh

(A) ** Fazle Kabir

(B) Atiur Rahman

(C) Salahuddin Ahmed

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fazle Kabir (born 4 July 1955) is a Bangladeshi bureaucrat, economist and central banker.

Concept note-2: -Abdur Rouf Talukder, new Bangladesh Bank governor.

Concept note-3: -For all latest news, follow The Daily Star’s Google News channel. Talukder is the 12th governor of the central bank.
