Who has been appointed as the CEO of Suzlon group

(A) ** JP Chalasani

(B) V. Raghuraman

(C) V. Subramanain

(D) Girish R. Tanti

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vinod Tanti-Chairman and Managing Director, Suzlon Group.

Concept note-2: -Ashwani Kumar is the Chief Executive Officer of The Suzlon Group, India’s leading renewable energy company. With over three decades of experience across India’s leading Infrastructure and Power companies, he is a name to reckon with on the Indian business landscape.

Concept note-3: -This order is a repeat order from Adani Green Energy Ltd. and is in addition to the existing order of 226.8 MW as announced on 13th August 2021. Suzlon will execute the project with a scope of supply, installation, and commissioning. Additionally, Suzlon will offer post-commissioning operation and maintenance services.

Concept note-4: -Suzlon Energy on Friday (7 October 2022) announced that its board has approved the appointment of Vinod R. Tanti as the chairman & managing director of the company, with immediate effect, for a period of 3 years up to 6 October 2025.
