Who has been appointed as the Chairman and Managing director (MD) of Bank of Maharashtra recently

(A) Ateesh Singh

(B) G Sreekumar

(C) Sushil Muhnot

(D) ** Ravindra Marathe

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri A.B. Vijayakumar joined as Executive Director of Bank of Maharashtra on 10th March, 2021.

Concept note-2: -Vijayakumar A. has assumed charge as Executive Director of Bank of Maharashtra w.e.f. 10.03. 2021. Prior to assuming the position, he was Chief Vigilance Officer at Indian Overseas Bank since 01.04. 2020.

Concept note-3: -The bank was founded by V.G. Kale and D. K. Sathe in Pune. The bank was registered on 16 September 1935 with an authorized capital of US$1 million and became operational on 8 February 1936.

Concept note-4: -General Manager, Mumbai Region, Shri M. G. Sanghvi, Executive Director of the Bank, Shri A. S. Bhattacharya, Chairman & Managing Director of the Bank and Shri Sanjay Arya, General Manager, Mumbai Region.
