(B) ** K M Hanumantharayappa

(C) Chandra Mouli Bajpeyi

(D) Narpat Raj Singhvi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About K M Hanumantharayappa KM Hanumantharayappa by profession is a sericulturist and a weaver.

Concept note-2: -Hanumantharayappa as Chairman of the Central Silk Board, Bangalore.
