(A) Cyrus Mistry

(B) Nusli Wadia

(C) Koushik Chatterjee

(D) ** O.P. Bhatt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr. T V Narendran is the CEO & Managing Director of Tata Steel Limited.

Concept note-2: -Mr Natarajan Chandrasekaran, Executive Chairman.

Concept note-3: -The Tata Steel board on 29 March 2022 approved the appointment of Noel Naval Tata as the vice-chairman of the company. Noel Tata is also the chairman of Tata International Limited, Voltas Limited, Tata Investment Corporation Limited and the vice-chairman of Titan Company.

Concept note-4: -Chairman N Chandrasekaran: Happy New Year 2022 | Tata group.

Concept note-5: -MUMBAI: Noel Tata, the half-brother of Tata Group patriarch Ratan Tata, has been named vice-chairman of Tata Steel, his first such role in a leading entity of the 154-year-old, $109-billion conglomerate.
