Who has been appointed as the chairperson of the Joint Coordination Committee for Development of Parliamentary Affairs, according to the ruling National League for Democracy

(A) Win Myint

(B) ** Aung San Suu Kyi

(C) U Htin Kyaw

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Democratic Party (Burmese: , pronounced [dìmòkəɹɛʔtɪʔ pàtì]; abbreviated DP) is a political party in Myanmar (Burma), founded in 1988. It was formally registered in May 2010, with its headquarters in Pazundaung Township, Yangon.

Concept note-2: -Aung San Suu Kyi, the former State Counsellor of Myanmar, serves as its chairperson.

Concept note-3: -Aung San Suu Kyi fled the scene with the help of her driver, Kyaw Soe Lin, but was arrested upon reaching Ye-U. The government imprisoned her at Insein Prison in Rangoon. After she underwent a hysterectomy in September 2003, the government again placed her under house arrest in Rangoon.
