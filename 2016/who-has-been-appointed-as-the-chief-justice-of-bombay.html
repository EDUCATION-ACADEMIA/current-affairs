Who has been appointed as the chief justice of Bombay

(A) ** Manjula Chellur

(B) G Rohini

(C) Susila Devi

(D) Narmada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Justice Datta, son of the late Justice Salil Kumar Datta, a former judge of the Calcutta High Court, was born on February 9, 1965.

Concept note-2: -Dhananjaya Y. Chandrachud has been appointed as the new Chief Justice of India on 9th November 2022. Justice Chandrachud is the 50th CJI and has taken over the post from Justice U.U Lalit who served a brief term as India’s 49th Chief Justice.

Concept note-3: -Sir Mathew Richard Sausse, was the first Chief Justice of Bombay High Court .

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is the President of India. The Chief Justice of India (CJI) is appointed by the President of India under clause (2) of Article 124 of the Constitution.
