Who has been appointed as the Executive Secretary of United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC)

(A) Fatma Samoura

(B) ** Patricia Espinosa Cantellano

(C) Deepa Kumar

(D) Christiana Leucas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Simon Stiell named as UN Climate Change Executive Secretary.

Concept note-2: -António Guterres (UN Secretary-General) at the Climate Implementation Summit of COP 27 | UN Web TV.

Concept note-3: -What is the purpose of the secretariat? The UNFCCC secretariat (UN Climate Change) is the United Nations entity tasked with supporting the global response to the threat of climate change. UNFCCC stands for United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change.

Concept note-4: -The UN Climate Change Conference in Sharm el-Sheik, Egypt, should shift the world towards implementation of previously agreed plans to tackle humanity’s greatest challenge, Simon Stiell, the new Executive Secretary of the UN Climate Convention (UNFCCC), said on Sunday at the opening of COP27.

Concept note-5: -António Guterres, the ninth Secretary-General of the United Nations, took office on 1st January 2017.
