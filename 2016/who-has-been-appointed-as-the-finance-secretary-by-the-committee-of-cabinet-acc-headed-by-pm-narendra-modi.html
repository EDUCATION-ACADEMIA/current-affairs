(A) Hasmukh Adhia

(B) Prashant M. Wadnere

(C) Ratan Watal

(D) ** Ashok Lavasa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Malhotra takes over as the top bureaucrat in the revenue department at a time when the government is preparing for the Union Budget 2023-24 to be tabled in Parliament on February 1. He would be looking into various tax-related proposals for the Budget and would also be the ex-officio Secretary to the GST Council.

Concept note-2: -T. V. Somanathan is the incumbent Finance Secretary. Two years, term can be extended.

Concept note-3: -He is a 1990-batch IAS officer of THE Rajasthan cadre and is an IIT-Kanpur alumnus. Malhotra has a Masters degree in public policy from the Princeton University. Senior bureaucrat Sanjay Malhotra on Thursday took charge as the secretary in Department of Revenue, Ministry of Finance.
