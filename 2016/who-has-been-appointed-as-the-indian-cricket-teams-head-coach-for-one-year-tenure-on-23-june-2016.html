Who has been appointed as the Indian cricket team’s head coach for one-year tenure on 23 June 2016

(A) Sachin Tendulkar

(B) VVS Laxman

(C) ** Anil Kumble

(D) Sourav Ganguly

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The NCA chief VVS Laxman has been appointed as the interim head coach of the Indian cricket team for the upcoming ACC Asia Cup 2022 on Wednesday.

Concept note-2: -Hrishikesh Kanitkar Appointed Head Coach Of Indian Women’s Cricket Team by BCCI. The BCCI appoints Hrishikesh Kanitkar as head coach of the Indian women’s cricket team and Ramesh Powar to join NCA.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Ravi Shastri. Ravi Shastri was appointed the head coach of the Indian Cricket Team by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) after Anil Kumble’s resignation in 2017. He is 24th number of Coach of Indian Cricket Team. His contract is from 2017 to 2021.
