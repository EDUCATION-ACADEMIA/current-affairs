(A) Deepa Gopalan Wadhwa

(B) ** T. Suresh Babu

(C) Vijay Gokhale

(D) Arun Kumar Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ambassador Richard L. Buangan-U.S. Embassy in Mongolia.
