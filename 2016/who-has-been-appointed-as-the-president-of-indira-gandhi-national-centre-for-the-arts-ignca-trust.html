Who has been appointed as the president of Indira Gandhi National Centre for the Arts (IGNCA) Trust

(A) Chinmaya Gharekhan

(B) Chandraprakash Dwivedi

(C) Prasoon Joshi

(D) ** Ram Bahadur Rai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Government today reconstituted the board of Indira Gandhi National Centre for the Arts (IGNCA), appointing senior journalist Ram Bahadur Rai as the president of the trust.

Concept note-2: -Panaji: Dattaguru Amonkar has been appointed as the regional director of the Indira Gandhi National Centre for the Arts (IGNCA), Goa regional centre.

Concept note-3: -The founder trustees of IGNCA were Rajiv Gandhi, R. Venkataraman, P.V. Narasimha Rao, Pupul Jayakar, H. Y. Sharada Prasad and Kapila Vatsyayan.

Concept note-4: -IGNCA is an autonomous institution under the Ministry of Culture. Its headquarters is at New Delhi.

Concept note-5: -An international jury, including British architect James Sterling and Japanese architect Fumihiko Maki, along with India’s AP Kanvinde and BV Doshi, chose American architect Ralph Lerner’s design, out of 190 entries from 37 countries.
