(A) ** Anant Maheshwari

(B) Bhaskar Pramanik

(C) Satya Nadella

(D) Teri List-Stoll

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As President of Microsoft India, Anant Maheshwari is responsible for Microsoft’s overall business and presence in India, leading engagement with policy makers, customers, business partners, and the industry ecosystem.

Concept note-2: -Rajiv Kumar is the Managing Director, Microsoft India Development Center (IDC) and Corporate Vice President of Microsoft’s Experiences and Devices (E+D) India Group. Rajiv moved to IDC 15 years ago to start the Dynamics CRM and Office product development team.
