Who has been appointed as the Prime Minister of Laos

(A) ** Thongloun Sisoulith

(B) Bounnhang Vorachit

(C) Chuommaly Sayasone

(D) Thongsing Thammavong

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The highest position in the government, they direct the country’s executive branch. The prime minister is accountable to the president, the National Assembly and the country’s only legal party: the Lao People’s Revolutionary Party (LPRP ). The current prime minister is Sonexay Siphandone, who was elected in 2022.

Concept note-2: -The only legal political party is the Lao People’s Revolutionary Party (LPRP). The head of state is President Thongloun Sisoulith, who is also the LPRP general secretary, making him the supreme leader of Laos.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister of Laos Khamtay Siphandone and wife Madam Thongvanh being greeted by Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong and Mrs Goh (both with back to camera) on arrival at Istana for ceremonial welcome.

Concept note-4: -The current prime minister Phạm Minh Chính has served since 2021.

Concept note-5: -The monarchy lasted until 2 December 1975, when the last king Sisavang Vatthana abdicated the throne to the Pathet Lao, who abolished the kingdom and proclaimed Laos as a communist state.
