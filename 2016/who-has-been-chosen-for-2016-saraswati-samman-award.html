Who has been chosen for 2016 Saraswati Samman award

(A) ** Padma Sachdev

(B) Veerappa Moily

(C) Govind Mishra

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Recently, Hindi writer “Prof. Ramdarsh Mishra” for his poetry collection ‘Mai to yaha hoon’ was awarded the Saraswati Samman for 2021 on April 7, 2022.

Concept note-2: -I. Sahitya Akademi Award prizes the most outstanding books of literary merit published in any of the major Indian languages recognized by the Akademi. II. Shashi Tharoor is one of the recipients of this award.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI: Delhi’s lieutenant governor Najeeb Jung presented on Monday the 25th Saraswati Samman for 2015 to eminent Dogri author Padma Sachdev.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Vasdev Mohi. Renowned Sindhi writer Vasdev Mohi will be conferred with 29th Saraswati Samman for his short stories collection-Chequebook. Mr Mohi has authored 25 books of poetry, stories and translations.
