Who has been chosen for the 2016 Bihari Puraskar

(A) Bala Subramanian

(B) Rajesh Khanna

(C) Ajay Singh

(D) ** Satya Narayan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr Satya Narayan, an esteemed Rajasthani litterateur has been chosen to award the 26th Bihari Puraskar 2016 by the K K Birla Foundation. He was been chosen for his Hindi book titled Yeh Ek Dunia published in 2010.

Concept note-2: -Bihari Puraskar (2021-22): Noted writers Madhu Kankariya and Dr Madhav Hada were awarded the 31st and 32nd Bihari Puraskar, respectively. Kankariya was awarded the Puraskar for her 2018 novel ‘Hum Yahan The’ while Hada was awarded for his 2015 literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chola Pahar Sakhi Ri’.

Concept note-3: -Author Madhu Kankariya has been chosen for the 31st Bihari Puraskar for her 2018 novel ‘Hum Yahan The’. The award carries a cash prize of ₹2.5 lakh, a plaque and a citation.

Concept note-4: -Named after the famous poet Bihari, the award carries a cash prize of ₹2.5 lakh, a plaque and a citation. Mohankrishna Bohara will be awarded the 30th Bihari Puraskar Award for his critical work ‘Taslima: Sangharsh aur Sahitya’, the KK Birla Foundation said in a statement on Thursday.
