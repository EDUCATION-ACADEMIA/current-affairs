Who has been chosen for the 23rd Rajiv Gandhi National Sadbhavana Award

(A) Muzaffar Ali

(B) Amjad Ali Khan

(C) ** Shubha Mudgal

(D) D. R. Mehta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Congress party announced that the the 24th Rajiv Gandhi National Sadbhavana Award will go to former West Bengal Governor Gopalkrishna Gandhi “for his outstanding contribution towards the promotion of communal harmony, peace and goodwill”.

Concept note-2: -Sadbhavana Diwas 2022 Theme Every year Sadbhavana Diwas is celebrated on August 20 and a specific theme is allotted. The Sadbhvana Diwas 2022 Theme is to “promote National Integration and Communal Harmony among people of all religions, languages, and regions".
