who has been chosen for UNESCO or Guillermo Cano World Press Freedom Prize 2016

(A) Mazen Darwish

(B) ** Khadija Ismayilova

(C) Paula Broadwell

(D) Christiane Amanpour

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Kyo Soe and Wa Lone (Myanmar).

Concept note-2: -The UNESCO/ Guillermo Cano World Press Freedom Prize was established in 1997 by UNESCO’s Executive Board in Memory of Guillermo Cano, a Colombian journalist who died in the exercise of his profession.

Concept note-3: -Created in 1997, the annual UNESCO/Guillermo Cano World Press Freedom Prize honours a person, organization or institution that has made an outstanding contribution to the defence and, or promotion of press freedom anywhere in the world, and especially when this has been achieved in the face of danger.
