Who has been conferred with the Mohun Bagan Ratna

(A) Stephen Constantine

(B) Sanjay Dhyani

(C) ** Syed Nayeemuddin

(D) Gigy George

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shyam Thapa, Indian football legend, conferred the Mohun Bagan Ratna-Sportstar.

Concept note-2: -Syed Naeemuddin was conferred the Mohun Bagan Ratna in 2016. Nayeemuddin was chosen for the prestigious award for his overall contribution to the club and the country both as a player and as a coach.

Concept note-3: -Arindam Bhattacharya (Goalkeeper) Having bagged the Golden Glove in the 2020-21 season, ATK Mohun Bagan’s all-time XI is incomplete without this man.

Concept note-4: -About Mohun Bagan Ratna Mohun Bagan AC is one of the oldest football clubs in Asia, having been established on 15 August 1889. The Mohun Bagan Ratna is awarded annually to great football players. The award is presented annually on 29 July i.e.. on Mohun Bagan Day to the winners.
