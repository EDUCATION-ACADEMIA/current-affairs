Who has been elected as Deputy Speaker of Kerala Assembly

(A) O Rajagopal

(B) C Balakrishnan

(C) Sreeramakrishnan

(D) ** V Sasi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Deputy Speaker is Chittayam Gopakumar of CPI. The leader of the Assembly is Pinarayi Vijayan from CPI(M ).

Concept note-2: -Unsourced material may be challenged and removed. K. O. Aysha Bai (25 October 1926 – 28 October 2005), also spelled Ayesha Bai, was a Communist politician from south India. She was the first Deputy Speaker of the Kerala Legislative Assembly (6 May 1957 – 31 July 1959).

Concept note-3: -The Deputy Speaker is elected in the first meeting of the Lok Sabha after the General elections for a term of five years from amongst the members of the Lok Sabha.

Concept note-4: -A. N. Shamseer is the incumbent speaker of the Kerala Legislative Assembly.
