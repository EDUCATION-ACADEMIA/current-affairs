Who has been elected as the National President Of All India Manufacturers’ Association (AIMO)

(A) Suresh Deora

(B) Harish L Metha

(C) ** K E Raghunathan

(D) Ajay Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All India Manufacturers’ Organisation.

Concept note-2: -About AIMO (TNSB) The Organisation has established itself as a body committed to Industrial progress with the motto “Prosperity through Industry". It has been recognized by Government as an apex Organisation of industry and given representation on most of the policy making bodies of the Central and State Governments.

Concept note-3: -All India Manufacturer’s Organisation. The purpose of AIMO is to encourage dialogue and partnership among various stakeholders of the MSME ecosystem for promoting innovation and knowledge sharing on MSME related issues.
