Who has been elected as the president of Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FICCI)

(A) Sanjay Bhatia

(B) R Narayan

(C) A Didar Singh

(D) ** Pankaj Patel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr. Subhrakant [1], Managing Director, Indian Metals & Ferro Alloys Ltd has today taken over as FICCI President for 2022-2023, from Mr Sanjiv Mehta at the apex chamber’s 95th Annual Convention held in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Press Release. NEW DELHI, 02 November 2022: The Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI) today announced Mr Subhrakant Panda as its President-Elect.

Concept note-3: -Mr Subhrakant Panda takes over as FICCI President for 2022-23, Dec 17, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Established in 1927, FICCI is the largest and oldest apex business organisation in India. Its history is closely interwoven with India’s struggle for independence, its industrialization, and its emergence as one of the most rapidly growing global economies.

Concept note-5: -Media executive Uday Shankar has taken over as FICCI President for 2020-2021, the industry body said on Monday. Currently, Shankar is President, The Walt Disney Company APAC & Chairman, Star & Disney India. He has taken over from Sangita Reddy, Joint Managing Director, Apollo Hospitals Group.
