Who has been elected as the Prime Minister of Lebanon

(A) Michel Aoun

(B) Sethrida Geagea

(C) Michel Suleiman

(D) ** Saad Hariri

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Incumbent The current prime minister is Najib Mikati, having taken office on 10 September 2021.

Concept note-2: -On the evening of December 9, 2022 local time, President Xi Jinping met with Lebanese Prime Minister Najib Mikati in Riyadh.

Concept note-3: -2022) France takes note of Najib Mikati’s designation as prime minister following consultations in the new Parliament.

Concept note-4: -Under article 49 of the Lebanese Constitution, a qualified majority of two-thirds of the members of the Lebanese Parliament is required to elect the president in the first round. The quorum also amounts to two-thirds of the Parliament. In the second round, a President can be elected by a simple majority of 65 deputies.
