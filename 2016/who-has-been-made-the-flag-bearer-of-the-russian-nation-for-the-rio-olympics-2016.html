(A) Elena Isinayeva

(B) Darya Klishina

(C) ** Sergei Tetyukhin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Highlights. After two weeks and 306 events, the 2016 Rio Olympics finally comes to an end. Sakshi created history as she won the first medal in the 2016 Rio Olympics.
