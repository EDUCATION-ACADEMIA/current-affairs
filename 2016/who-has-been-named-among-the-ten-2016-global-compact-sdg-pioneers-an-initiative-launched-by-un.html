Who has been named among the ten ’2016 Global Compact SDG Pioneers’ an initiative launched by UN

(A) ** Zubaida Bai

(B) Shiza Shahid

(C) Bunker Roy

(D) Scott Harrison

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ramkrishna Mukkavilli becomes first Indian to be recognised as Global SDG pioneer by UNGC.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which Indian has been selected as Global SDG Pioneer by the UN Global Compact Network in 2022? Notes: Ramkrishna Mukkavilli, the head of Hyderabad-based startup Maithri Aquatech, has been selected as Global SDG Pioneer by the UN Global Compact Network (UNGC).

Concept note-3: -To date, Maithri Aquatech has generated over 100 million litres of fresh water from air and saved an estimated 200 million litres of crucial groundwater resources from exploitation. “I am extremely humbled to have been recognised by the United Nations Global Compact.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Ramkrishna Mukkavilli. Ramkrishna Mukkavilli has become the first Indian to be recognised as the Global Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) Pioneer for water stewardship by the United Nations Global Compact (UNGC).

Concept note-5: -Each year the United Nations Global Compact celebrates a group of SDG Pioneers-business leaders who are doing an exceptional job to advance the Global Goals through the implementation of our Ten Principles on human rights, labour, environment and anti-corruption.
