Who has been nominated as the 2016 AIFF Player of the Year

(A) ** Jeje Lalpekhlua

(B) Robin Singh

(C) Arnab Mondal

(D) Sunil Chhetri

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Jeje Lalpekhlua has been nominated as the ‘2016 AIFF Player of the Year. ‘ The announcement was made by Mr. Praful Patel, President, AIFF at the AIFF Annual General Body Meeting held at the AIFF Football House in New Delhi on Wednesday (December 21, 2016).

Concept note-2: -2021-22 AIFF Women’s Footballer of the Year: Manisha Kalyan. 2021-22 Men’s Footballer of the Year: Sunil Chhetri.

Concept note-3: -Sunil Chhetri named AIFF Men’s Footballer of the Year for 2021-22-know all winners.

Concept note-4: -AIFF Player of the Year Award winners for 2021-22: AIFF Men’s Footballer of the Year 2021-22: Sunil Chhetri. AIFF Women’s Footballer of the Year 2021-22: Manisha Kalyan.

Concept note-5: -Sunil Chhetri has received the award a record seven times: in 2007, 2011, 2013, 2014, 2017, 2018−19 and most recently, 2021−22. Jo Paul Ancheri and Bhaichung Bhutia are the other players who have won this award more than once.
