Who has been selected the prestigious 25th Vyas Samman 2015

(A) Vijay Kumar Roy

(B) Safdar Hashmi

(C) ** Sunita Jain

(D) Rangeya Raghav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Renowned Hindi Writer Sunita Jain bags the 25th Vyas Sammam award 2015. Well-known Hindi scholar and writer Sunita Jain’s poetry collection Kshama has been selected for the prestigious Vyas Samman 2015 for her poetry collection “Kshama”. The Vyas Samman is a literary award in India, first awarded in 1991.

Concept note-2: -The 31st Vyas Samman was conferred on to well-known Hindi writer Dr. Asghar Wajahat at a function in New Delhi today. Dr. Wajahat has been chosen for the prestigious award for his play Mahabali.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Sharad Pagare. Well-known Hindi writer Prof. Sharad Pagare was conferred with the prestigious Vyas Samman-2020 for his novel ‘Patliputru Ki Samragi. ‘

Concept note-4: -The 31st Vyas Samman was conferred on to well-known Hindi writer Dr Asghar Wajahat at a function in New Delhi. He has been chosen for the prestigious award for his play Mahabali.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Mamta Kalia. Mamta Kalia is the author of the book “DukkhamSukkham” which won the Vyas Samman 2017. This was Mamta Kalia’s 27th Vyas Samman award and was selected by a committee headed by author Vishwanath Prasad Tiwari.
