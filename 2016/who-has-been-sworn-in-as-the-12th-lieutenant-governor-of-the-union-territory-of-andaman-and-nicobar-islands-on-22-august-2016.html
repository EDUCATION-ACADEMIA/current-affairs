Who has been sworn in as the 12th Lieutenant Governor of the Union Territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands on 22 August 2016.

(A) Najma Heptulla

(B) VP Singh

(C) ** Jagdish Mukhi

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current Lieutenant Governor is Devendra Kumar Joshi.

Concept note-2: -Lieutenant Governor :Admiral D K Joshi, PVSM, AVSM, YSM, NM, VSM Retd.

Concept note-3: -Admiral D K Joshi, PVSM, AVSM, YSM, NM, VSM (Retd.)

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is option 3 i.e. Arcot Doraiswamy Loganadan. On March 23, 1942, Japan occupied Andaman and Nicobar islands, with the help of the Indian National Army.

Concept note-5: -Mukhi gained a B.Com. from Raj Rishi College in Alwar, Rajasthan in 1965, followed by a M. Com. from the university of Delhi in 1967. Until entering politics he was a professor at Shaheed Bhagat Singh College, Delhi University. He was awarded a PhD in finance from Kurukshetra University in October 1995.
