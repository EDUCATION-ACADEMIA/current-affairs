Who has been sworn in as the first mayor of Imphal

(A) Ramesh Kumar

(B) Akhilesh Kumar

(C) ** Soram Sunil

(D) Bala Chandran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first mayor of the corporation was Soram Sunil and was elected in 2016. The present mayor of Imphal Municipal Corporation is Laisangbam Lokeshwar and was elected to the position in 2017.

Concept note-2: -It seems there were congregations or plenty of houses or gathering of houses or beautiful houses in the present Imphal, the name Imphal is perhaps derived from compounding two words namely, “Yum” and “Phal” where yum means house and phal means plenty or beautiful.

Concept note-3: -ABOUT DISTRICT Imphal East District came into being with effect from 18th June, 1996 by bifurcating the erstwhile Imphal district into two district namely Imphal East District and Imphal West District occupying the eastern part of Imphal district with its head quarters at Porompat .

Concept note-4: -What is the capital of Meghalaya? Shillong is the capital of Meghalaya.
