Who has been sworn in as West Bengal Chief Minister for a second term

(A) Amit Mitra

(B) ** Mamata Banerjee

(C) Adhir Ranjan Chowdhury

(D) Surya Kanta Mishra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sworn in on 20 May 2011, Trinamool leader Mamata Banerjee is West Bengal’s incumbent chief minister, the first woman to hold the office. She was subsequently voted to power in 2016 and 2021 assembly elections.

Concept note-2: -Mamata Banerjee (Bengali pronunciation: [mɔmot̪a bɔnd̪ːopad̪d̪ʱae̯]; born 5 January 1955) is an Indian politician who is serving as the eighth and current chief minister of the Indian state of West Bengal since 20 May 2011, the first woman to hold the office.

Concept note-3: -Mamata Banerjee-Chief Minister of West Bengal.
