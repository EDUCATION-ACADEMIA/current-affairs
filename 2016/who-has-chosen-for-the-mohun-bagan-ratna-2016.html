Who has chosen for the Mohun Bagan Ratna 2016

(A) Stephen Constantine

(B) ** Syed Nayeemuddin

(C) Rahim

(D) Peter Thangaraj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Syed Naeemuddin was conferred the Mohun Bagan Ratna in 2016. Nayeemuddin was chosen for the prestigious award for his overall contribution to the club and the country both as a player and as a coach. He became the 16th recipient of the prestigious award.

Concept note-2: -Shyam Thapa, Indian football legend, conferred the Mohun Bagan Ratna-Sportstar.

Concept note-3: -About Mohun Bagan Ratna Mohun Bagan AC is one of the oldest football clubs in Asia, having been established on 15 August 1889. The Mohun Bagan Ratna is awarded annually to great football players. The award is presented annually on 29 July i.e.. on Mohun Bagan Day to the winners.

Concept note-4: -Former India goalkeeper Shibaji Banerjee, who stood out against the Pele-led Cosmos Club in an exhibition match in Kolkata in 1977, will be conferred with Mohun Bagan Ratna posthumously, the club announced on Wednesday.
