Who has inaugurated the South India’s first Doppler weather radar

(A) Ratan Kumar Sinha

(B) ** A S Kiran Kumar

(C) Sekhar Basu

(D) Shailesh Nayak

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 1964, scientists added a three-centimeter wavelength research Doppler radar to NSSL’s list of assets-a move that would revolutionize weather observation. This new radar allowed scientists to measure motion inside storms for the very first time, providing clues into the development of severe weather.

Concept note-2: -From the first description of Doppler phenomenon, to the 21st century renaissance era of cardiac imaging, little could anyone imagine the great impact of Christian Doppler’s observation would have had on clinical echocardiography and ultrasonography in general.

Concept note-3: -The IMD has augmented the Doppler Weather Radar network in Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Ladakh, and Jammu & Kashmir.
