(A) Pawan Munjal

(B) Pritam Singh

(C) Ravi Nath

(D) ** Sunil Kant Munjal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pawan Munjal, Chairman and CEO at Hero Moto Corp spoke exclusively to Business Today TV’s Chetan Bhutani and said that the company is focused on getting the right EV product in the market and is also readying new conventional motorcycles.

Concept note-3: -Pawan Munjal’s net worth is $3.6 Billion (Rupees – 26, 880 Crores) according to the data, which is making him one of the Top 100 Richest men in India.

Concept note-4: -Chairman/CEO, Hero Motocorp Ltd.
