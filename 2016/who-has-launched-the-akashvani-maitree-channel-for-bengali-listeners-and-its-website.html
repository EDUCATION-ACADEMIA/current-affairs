Who has launched the Akashvani Maitree channel for Bengali listeners and its website.

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) Arun Jaitley

(C) Rajnath Singh

(D) ** Pranab Mukherjee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Radio Maitree, a live radio program, was launched during the pandemic lockdowns in 2020. The platform was designed to reach out to TCS associates and their families as they tuned in to listen to their favorite songs, share personal stories, and stay involved despite the isolation on account of lockdowns.

Concept note-2: -Listen online to All India Radio Akashvani Maitree 594 MW in Kolkata (West Bengal) in quality 96 Kbps. Broadcasting was opened in New Delhi (India) in 2011.

Concept note-3: -Listen to AIR Bangla Live broadcast from West Bengal online via Radio India Live | www.radioindialive.com.

Concept note-4: -When the Indian State Broadcasting Service (ISBS) was renamed All India Radio, Rabindranath Tagore rechristened it as Akashvani, the voice that comes over from the skies, through a poem penned for the inauguration of Calcutta’s shortwave service. ‘Akashvani’ was also used in the context of radio by M.
