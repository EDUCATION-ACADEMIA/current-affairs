Who has sworn in as Minister for Electricity, Prohibition and Excise of Tamil Nadu

(A) S. P. Velumani

(B) Natham R.Viswanathan

(C) ** Thangamani

(D) Thiru R.Vaithilingam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hon’ble Minister for Electricity, Prohibition & Excise Thiru V. Senthilbalaji and District Collector Thiru. Kranthi Kumar Pati I.A.S., Garlanded to the Statue of Uzhavar Perunthalaivar Thiru. Narayanasamy Naidu’s Birth Day Anniversary at Vaiyampalayam in Sarkar Samakulam Panchayat Union on 06.02.

Concept note-2: -Muthuvel Karunanidhi Stalin (/stɑːlɪn, -lin/, born 1 March 1953), often referred to by his initials as MK Stalin, is an Indian Tamil politician serving as the 8th and current Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-3: -Udhayanidhi Stalin (born 27 November 1977) is an Indian film producer, actor, and politician who prominently works in the Tamil cinema. He was appointed as Minister for Youth Welfare and Sports Development of Tamil Nadu in the ministry led by his father the chief minister of Tamil Nadu, M. K. Stalin.
