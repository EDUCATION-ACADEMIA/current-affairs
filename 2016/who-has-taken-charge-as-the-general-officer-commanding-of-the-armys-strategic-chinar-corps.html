Who has taken charge as the General officer Commanding of the Army’s strategic Chinar Corps

(A) AS singh

(B) Surinder Singh

(C) Praveen Bakshi

(D) ** J S Sandhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Amardeep Singh Aujla. Lieutenant General Amardeep Singh Aujla has taken over as General officer Commanding of the strategic Chinar Corps of the Indian Army from Lt Gen D P Pandey.

Concept note-2: -Chinar Corps commander Lt Gen DP Pandey explains new breed of terrorists in Kashmir; shames ‘white-collar terrorists’

Concept note-3: -XV Corps, or 15 Corps, also known as Chinar Corps, is a Corps of the Indian Army which is presently located in Srinagar and responsible for military operations in the Kashmir Valley. It has participated in all military conflicts with Pakistan and China till date.

Concept note-4: -LT GEN AMARDEEP SINGH BHINDER, AVSM, VSM, GOC-IN-C, SOUTH WESTERN COMMAND. Lieutenant General Amardeep Singh Bhinder, AVSM, VSM took over the command of South Western Command on 1st Apr 2021.

Concept note-5: -Lt Gen Upendra Dwivedi, AVSM, GOC-in-C, Northern Command.
