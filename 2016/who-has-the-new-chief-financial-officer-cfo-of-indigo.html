Who has the new Chief Financial Officer (CFO) of IndiGo

(A) ** Rohit Philip

(B) Rohini Bhatia

(C) Aditya Ghosh

(D) Anupam Khanna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -InterGlobe Aviation Ltd, which operates India’s biggest carrier IndiGo has appoined Gaurav Negi as Chief Financial Officer effective April 01, 2022. Negi is IndiGo’s fourth finance chief in six years.

Concept note-2: -IndiGo appoints Gaurav Negi as CFO replacing Jiten Chopra-BusinessToday.

Concept note-3: -Gaurav Negi, currently IndiGo’s senior vice president and head of governance, risk and compliance will assume the position of CFO at IndiGo. At GE, where he worked before joining IndiGo in December 2021, Negi had held positions across multiple countries and industries.

Concept note-4: -Abhijit DasGupta. Mr. Abhijit DasGupta is an experienced veteran in the aviation industry with professional experience of over 20 years. He has functional expertise in Long Term Network and Fleet Strategy, Network Planning and Analysis, Schedule Management, Airline Partnerships and Strategic Regulatory Affairs.
