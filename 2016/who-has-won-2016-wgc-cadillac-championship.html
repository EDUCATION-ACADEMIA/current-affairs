(A) Dustin Johnson

(B) Jordan Spieth

(C) ** Adam Scott

(D) Rickie Fowler

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The tournament was won by Adam Scott. Doral, Florida, U.S.

Concept note-2: -Collin Morikawa won the 2021 event by three strokes ahead of Billy Horschel, Viktor Hovland and Brooks Koepka. In August 2021, the PGA Tour released its schedule for the 2021–22 season.

Concept note-3: -Tiger Woods has the record number of wins with three. The winner receives a Wedgwood trophy named the Walter Hagen Cup.

Concept note-4: -Austin, Texas, U.S. The tournament was won by 2021 runner-up Scottie Scheffler, who defeated Kevin Kisner 4 and 3 in the final. With the win, Scheffler’s third in his last five starts, he rose to number one in the Official World Golf Ranking for the first time.
