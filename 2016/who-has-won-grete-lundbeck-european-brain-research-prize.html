Who has won Grete Lundbeck European Brain Research Prize

(A) ** Richard Morris

(B) Maiken Nedergaard

(C) Sydney Brenner

(D) Charles Kao

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Professors Silvia Arber, Ole Kiehn and Martyn Goulding have been awarded The Brain Prize 2022, worth 10 million Danish kroner, for having revolutionized our understanding of the neuronal cell types and circuits underlying movement.

Concept note-2: -In 2022 The Brain Prize, the world’s largest prize for neuroscience, was awarded to Silvia Arber (Switzerland), Martyn Goulding (USA) and Ole Kiehn (Denmark) for their pioneering work on the neural cell types and circuits that control movement.

Concept note-3: -Prize winners 2022 Professor Silvia Arber, Martyn Goulding and Ole Kiehn have been awarded The Brain Prize for having revolutionized our understanding of the neuronal cell types and circuits that control movement.

Concept note-4: -Winners of the The Brain Prize are chosen from the pool of nominated candidates by a selection committee consisting of at least 8, and no more than 12 members. In terms of scientific merit, standing and knowledge, the members are outstanding and are highly respected by the international scientific community.
