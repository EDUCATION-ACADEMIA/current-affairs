Who has won the 2016 Hungarian Grand Prix title of Formula One

(A) ** Lewis Hamilton

(B) Nico Rosberg

(C) Daniel Ricardo

(D) Sebastian Vettel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nico Rosberg won his only World Drivers’ Championship title in the final race of the season. With nine wins and seven other podiums, Rosberg beat teammate and defending World Champion Lewis Hamilton by five points, ending Hamilton’s 2 year dominance.

Concept note-2: -Winner: Lewis Hamilton It was his fifth successive podium – which is the longest active streak in F1 currently – and 11th rostrum in Hungary. He’s now just 12 points behind his team mate George Russell.

Concept note-3: -The race was won by Max Verstappen from the 10th position on the starting grid, while Mercedes drivers Lewis Hamilton and George Russell finished second and third respectively, with Russell having started from pole position.

Concept note-4: -Max Verstappen has clinched his second Formula 1 world championship in confusing circumstances after winning a rain-shortened Japanese GP.
