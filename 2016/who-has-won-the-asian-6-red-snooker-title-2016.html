Who has won the Asian 6-Red Snooker title 2016

(A) Aditya Mehta

(B) Kamal Chawla

(C) Manan Chandra

(D) ** Pankaj Advani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ACBS Asian 6 Reds Snooker Championship: Indian Pankaj Advani claimed the 5th edition ACBS Asian 6 Reds Snooker Championship on in Abu Dhabi.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The National Snooker Championship 2016 was won by Aditya Mehta, by defeating Manan Chandra by 6-3 in the men’s final round of the National Snooker Championship 2016. SSC CHSL 2022-23 Admit Card for Tier I will be out soon. SSC CHSL Exam Dates Out.

Concept note-3: -This is the first silver Advani has won this season, the rest all gold medals–at the National Championships of Billiards, 6-red Snooker, 15-red Snooker and the Asian Billiards Championship. The defeat in the final meant that Advani could not complete a career Grand Slam in cue sport.

Concept note-4: -Pankaj Advani is the only cueist to have won the Asian and World Championships in all formats of Billiards and Snooker. He has the maximum IBSF world championships in billiards and snooker. He won the Asian Snooker Tour title 2018 held in China. He defeated Joe Seat of China.

Concept note-5: -India’s Pankaj Advani won the Asian Snooker Championship 2021 by defeating Amir Sarkhosh.
