Who has won the Best player at women’s International Hockey Federation (FIH) 2016

(A) Maria Granatto

(B) ** Carla Rebecchi

(C) Joyce Sombroek

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Felice Albers of Netherlands is women’s Player of the Year.

Concept note-2: -Australia won the 2016 Champions Trophy Men’s Hockey tournament defeating India in the finals in London. The tournament was held between 10 to 17 June 2016 in London, United Kingdom. Australia holds the record by winning fourteenth time after defeating India 3 – 1 in the final on a penalty shoot-out after a 0 – 0 draw.

Concept note-3: -FIH Player of the Year 2021-22: India’s Harmanpreet Singh wins men’s award. The Indian hockey star also won the award last year. Harmanpreet Singh holds the record for the most goals scored in an FIH Pro League season.

Concept note-4: -Teun de Nooijer (Netherlands) This Dutch hockey great has played for Uttar Pradesh in the Hockey India League. Teun de Nooijer is widely considered one of the greatest field hockey players of modern times.
