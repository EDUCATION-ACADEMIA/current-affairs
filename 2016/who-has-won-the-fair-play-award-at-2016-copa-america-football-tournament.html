Who has won the Fair Play Award at 2016 Copa America Football tournament

(A) Chile

(B) ** Argentina

(C) Brazil

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -These were the footballers who made the list of top scorers in the CONMEBOL Copa América 2016, in fact the top 4 in goal numbers. Alexis Sánchez was the golden ball of the tournament, Eduardo Vargas won golden boot, Claudio Bravo won the golden glove award while Argentina won the fair play award.

Concept note-2: -Arsenal forward Alexis Sanchez was named player of the tournament as Copa America Centenario champions Chile also swept the individual awards.
