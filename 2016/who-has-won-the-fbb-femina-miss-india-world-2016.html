Who has won the FBB Femina Miss India World 2016

(A) Roshmitha Harimurthy

(B) ** Priyadarshini Chatterjee

(C) Pankhuri Gidwani

(D) Sushruthi Krishna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Twenty-one contestants competed for the titles of Femina Miss India 2016. Aditi Arya crowned Priyadarshini Chatterjee as her successor. She represented India at Miss World 2016, Sushruthi Krishna was crowned as the 1st Runner Up by Aafreen Vaz, and Pankhuri Gidwani was crowned as the 2nd Runner Up by Vartika Singh.

Concept note-2: -Top 5 finalists of fbb Femina Miss India 2016, Navpreet Kaur, Pankhuri Gidwani, Priyadarshini Chatterjee, Roshmitha Harimurthy and Sushruthi Krishna clicked with Varun Dhawan.

Concept note-3: -Priyadarshini Chatterjee (born 5 September 1996) is an Indian model and beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Femina Miss India World in 2016. She represented India at the Miss World 2016 pageant. She is the first northeast Indian to represent India at Miss World.

Concept note-4: -Enhancing the world of beauty and glamour, fbb Femina Miss India 2016 proudly announced and crowned their winners at a glittering and scintillating ceremony at Yash Raj Studios on April 9th.

Concept note-5: -Miss Rajasthan 2016 winner GEETANNJLI is selected as one of the Rajasthan finalist in FBB Femina Miss India.
