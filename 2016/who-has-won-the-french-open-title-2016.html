Who has won the French Open title 2016

(A) Andy Murray

(B) ** Novak Djokovic

(C) Rafael Nadal David

(D) Ferrer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Novak Djokovic defeated Andy Murray in the final, 3–6, 6–1, 6–2, 6–4 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2016 French Open.

Concept note-2: -Garbiñe Muguruza won her first Grand Slam title by beating defending champion Serena Williams. At his fourth attempt in the final, Novak Djokovic claimed his first-ever Roland-Garros title.

Concept note-3: -Garbiñe Muguruza defeated the defending champion Serena Williams in the final, 7–5, 6–4 to win the women’s singles tennis title at the 2016 French Open.

Concept note-4: -The statistic ranks male professional tennis players (active and retired) by the number of French Open titles won since the beginning of the Open Era in 1968. Rafael Nadal has won the French Open a record 14 times, including most recently in June, 2022.

Concept note-5: -He later won the 2019 U.S. Open singles title, which was his 19th career Grand Slam championship, the second most for men behind Federer’s 20 titles. The following year he tied Federer when he won his 13th French Open title. (Their record was equaled in July 2021 by Djokovic.)
