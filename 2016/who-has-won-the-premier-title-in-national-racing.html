Who has won the premier title in National Racing

(A) Karthik Tharani

(B) Raghul Rangasamy

(C) Anindith Reddy

(D) ** Vikash Anand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Giacomo Agostini, with 15 victories, has won the most world championships. Ángel Nieto is second with 13 world championships and Valentino Rossi, Mike Hailwood and Carlo Ubbiali are third with 9 world championships.

Concept note-2: -Technical regulations. The MotoGP world championship is raced using 1000cc prototypes. Riders have an allocation of 7 engines for the whole season. Since 2017, all the teams use the same electronic management system, provided by the championship promoter.

Concept note-3: -While the chassis are ‘free’, the major feature of the Moto2 regulations is that all the riders use the same engine, meaning a rider’s individual talent is showcased. After nine consecutive years, the Honda 600cc engine was replaced by a three cylinder 765cc Triumph engine.
