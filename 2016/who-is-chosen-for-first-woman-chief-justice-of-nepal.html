Who is chosen for first woman chief Justice of Nepal

(A) Anita Sapkota Chapagain

(B) ** Sushila Karki

(C) Anita Kumar

(D) G. Rohini

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sushila Karki (Nepali: ) (born 7 June 1952 in Biratnagar) is a Nepalese jurist. She is the former Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of Nepal and the only woman to have held the post.

Concept note-2: -Justice Fathima M. Beevi was the first woman judge of the Supreme Court of India. She was appointed to the apex Court in 1989.

Concept note-3: -The first female Chief Justice of the supreme court in India was Fatima Beevi, appointed in 1989.

Concept note-4: -Career/Experience: Lady Chief Justice The Right Honourable Dame Siobhan Keegan was appointed as a High Court Judge in October 2015. She was one of the first two women to be appointed to the High Court along with Madam Justice McBride.
