(A) M.S Swaminathan

(B) Dr. Hiralal Chaudhari

(C) ** Dr Verghese Kurien

(D) Norman Borlaug

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Padma Vibhushan Dr Verghese Kurien, ‘Father of White Revolution in India’, left for his heavenly abode on 9th September 2012 at the age of 90.

Concept note-2: -Verghese Kurien is known as the Father of the White Revolution. He is so called because of his initiatives and immense contribution to the dairy sector. He worked a lot in increasing milk production through cooperative societies to fulfill the needs of our country.
