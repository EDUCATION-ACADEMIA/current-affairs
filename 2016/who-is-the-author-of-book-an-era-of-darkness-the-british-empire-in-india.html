Who is the author of book ‘An Era of Darkness: The British Empire in India’

(A) Arun Shourie

(B) Vikram Seth

(C) Abul Fazal

(D) ** Shashi Tharoor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In this explosive book, bestselling author Shashi Tharoor reveals with acuity, impeccable research, and trademark wit, just how disastrous British rule was for India.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Shashi Tharoor. Shashi Tharoor won the Sahitya Akademi Award 2019 for his book ‘An Era of Darkness: The British Empire in India’ on December 18, 2019. This book was published in 2016.

Concept note-3: -An Area of Darkness is a book written by V. S. Naipaul in 1964. It is a travelogue detailing Naipaul’s trip through India in the early sixties. It was the first of Naipaul’s acclaimed Indian trilogy that includes India: A Wounded Civilization (1977) and India: A Million Mutinies Now (1990).

Concept note-4: -Tharoor shows that the Raj was an era of darkness for India, where rapacious economic exploitation of India was committed on an unprecedented scale.
