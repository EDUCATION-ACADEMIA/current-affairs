Who is the author of book "Conflicts of Interest: My Journey through India’s Green Movement "

(A) Vikram Seth

(B) Arundhati Roy

(C) Ruskin Bond

(D) ** Sunita Narain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Green Movement is the term used to describe peasant resistance to the Bolshevik government during the Russian Civil War. The first rebellions against the Bolshevik government began in 1918 and increased with frequency and intensity through the civil war period.

Concept note-2: -The Green Movement aims at creating a holistic and ecological view of the world. It makes people conscious of stopping further degradation and deterioration of nature and its resources. It started in 1972. The world’s first nationwide Green Party was founded in New Zealand.
