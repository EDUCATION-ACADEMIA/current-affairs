Who is the author of book His Bloody Project

(A) ** Graeme Macrae Burnet

(B) Jhumpa Lahiri

(C) Rosalyn D’Mello

(D) Neel Mukherjee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Graeme Macrae Burnet has established a reputation for smart and literary mystery writing with his highly praised novel, His Bloody Project, which was a finalist for the Man Booker Prize and the Los Angeles Times Book Award.

Concept note-2: -Graeme Macrae Burnet is the author of the ‘fiendishly readable’ His Bloody Project, which was shortlisted for the 2016 Booker Prize and the LA Times Book Awards.

Concept note-3: -The story of what led to the deaths and the trial of Roddy Macrae is told in vivid detail by Graeme Macrae Burnet in his Man Booker Prize shortlisted novel His Bloody Project. The village of Culduie is real and so are many of the characters in the novel but the tale told by Burnet is a fiction.

Concept note-4: -His Bloody Project, by Graeme Macrae Burnet, tells the story of three brutal murders in a remote community of the Scottish Highlands in 1869. A young man by the name of Roderick Macrae is arrested and stands trial for the crimes. He readily admits that he carried out the attacks but shows no remorse.

Concept note-5: -Graeme Macrae Burnet’s novel, His Bloody Project, is a tale of murder in the year 1869. Set in the small Scottish hamlet of Culduie, Scottish crofter Roderick Macrae committed a triple homicide of Flora, Donnie, and Lachlan Broad Mackenzie within their home.
