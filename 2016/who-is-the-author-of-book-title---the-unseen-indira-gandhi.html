(B) Pupul Jayakar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indira Gandhi is the author of the book “My Truth". Indira Gandhi was the first female Prime Minister of India, serving for three consecutive terms (1966-77) and a fourth term from 1980 until she was assassinated in 1984.

Concept note-2: -Indira Gandhi was born Indira Nehru, into a Kashmiri Pandit family on 19 November 1917 in Allahabad.
