Who is the chief minister of Chhattisgarh 2016

(A) Punnu Lal Mohalle

(B) Ajit Jogi Mahasamund

(C) Brij Mohan Agarwal

(D) ** Raman Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bhupesh Baghel (born 23 August 1960) is an Indian politician serving as the third and current Chief Minister of Chhattisgarh. He was president of Chhattisgarh Pradesh Congress.

Concept note-2: -The first was Ajit Jogi of the Indian National Congress. He was succeeded in 2003 by Raman Singh of the Bharatiya Janata Party who served three consecutive five-year terms. His successor, and current incumbent, is the Indian National Congress leader Bhupesh Baghel who was elected in 2018.
