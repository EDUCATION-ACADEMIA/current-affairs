Who is the first Asian to win Golden Reel Awards

(A) Rajat Kamal

(B) ** Resul Pookutty

(C) A.R.Rahman

(D) Ilaya Raja

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Resul Pookutty becomes first Asian to win Golden Reel Award.

Concept note-2: -The 69th Golden Reel Awards, were held on March 13, 2022, by the Motion Picture Sound Editors (M.P.S.E.) to honor the best in sound editing for film, television, computer entertainment and student productions in 2021.

Concept note-3: -The Golden Reel Award for Outstanding Achievement in Sound Editing – Musical for Feature Film is an annual award given by the Motion Picture Sound Editors. It honors music editors whose work has warranted merit in the field of cinema; in this case, their work in musical feature films.

Concept note-4: -The Honorary Filmmaker’s Award is bestowed upon an individual outside the sound community whose career has demonstrated a great appreciation and understanding of the impact of sound editing. MPSE members receive a discount for awards applications.
