Who is the first president of the Paralympic Committee of India

(A) ** Rathan Singh

(B) Vishwanath

(C) S.P. Sanghwan

(D) S. Chandrashekar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Its first President was Rathan Singh.

Concept note-2: -India’s first medal in Paralympics came in 1974 Games, with Murlikant Petkar winning a gold medal in swimming. India’s best finish yet has been in the 2020 Games, at 24th place with a medal haul of 19 medals (5 gold, 8 silver and 6 bronze).

Concept note-3: -The Paralympic Committee of India (PCI) was founded in the year 1994.
