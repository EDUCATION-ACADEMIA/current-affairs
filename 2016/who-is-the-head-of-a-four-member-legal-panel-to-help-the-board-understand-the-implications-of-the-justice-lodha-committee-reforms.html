Who is the head of a four-member legal panel to help the Board understand the implications of the Justice Lodha Committee reforms

(A) Adarsh Kumar Goel

(B) V. R. Krishna Iyer

(C) ** Markandey Katju

(D) S. H. Kapadia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The BCCI yesterday appointed former Supreme Court judge Markandey Katju as the head of a four-member legal panel to help the board understand the implications of the Justice Lodha Committee reforms.

Concept note-2: -Lodha – Chairman Mr. G Mahalingam, Whole Time Member, SEBI-Member Mr. Anand Rajeshwar Baiwar Executive Director, SEBI-Member Further, Shri. Anindya Kumar Das, General Manager, SEBI is the Nodal Officer cum Secretary to the Committee.

Concept note-3: -R.M. Lodha, former Chief Justice of India, and former Supreme Court judges, Mr. Justice (Retd.) Ashok Bhan and Mr. Justice (Retd.)

Concept note-4: -Following the Justice (retired) Mukul Mudgal probe into the 2013 scandal, the Supreme Court appointed the Lodha Committee to suggest structural changes that would provide checks and balance in the smooth running of cricket in the country.

Concept note-5: -Lodha committee was formed by Supreme Court of India in January 2015 under Justice RM Lodha after the wake of the Justice Mukul Mudgal Committee report that called for reforms within the BCCI.
