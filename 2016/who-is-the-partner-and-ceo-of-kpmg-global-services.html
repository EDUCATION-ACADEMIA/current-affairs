Who is the Partner and CEO of KPMG Global Services

(A) John B.Veihmeyer

(B) ** Sameer Chadha

(C) Ms. Doughtie

(D) Manish Verma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A seasoned business leader, Sameer is the Chief Executive Officer of KPMG Global Services (KGS).

Concept note-2: -Yezdi Nagporewalla has been appointed as the new CEO of KPMG in India for a term commencing February 7, 2022 and ending on December 31, 2026. He was nominated by the KPMG India Board and approved by the India Partners. The firm executed a well-defined governance process for the selection of the CEO.

Concept note-3: -KPMG Global Services (KGS) is a strategic global delivery organisation that engages with KPMG member firms to provide innovative, scalable and customised business services.

Concept note-4: -KPMG in India has appointed Anindya Basu as National Managing Partner of the firm. He will be based out of Gurugram. KPMG in India has appointed Anindya Basu as National Managing Partner of the firm.
