Who is the top rank in Fortune’s 51 Most Powerful Women list.

(A) ** Mary Barra

(B) Indra Nooyi

(C) Marillyn Hewson

(D) Ginni Rometty

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Revathi Advaithi and Bela Bajaria, two Indian American executives, have made it into Fortune’s list of the most powerful women in business for 2022, which is once again headed by Karen Lynch, president and CEO of CVS Health.

Concept note-2: -Atop the 2022 MPW list is Karen Lynch, President and CEO of CVS Health, the highest-ranking Fortune 500TM and Fortune Global 500TM company ever to be led by a woman.

Concept note-3: -(Clockwise from top left) Karen Lynch, Carol Tomé, Jessica Tan, Jane Fraser, Julie Sweet and Mary Barra topped Fortune’s Most Powerful Women in Business list for 2022. The competition to be listed among Fortune’s Most Powerful Women has gotten a little stiffer this year.

Concept note-4: -In 2022, Ursula von der Leyen became one of the West’s staunchest supporters of Ukraine amid Russia’s unprovoked invasion. Ursula von der Leyen is the most powerful woman in the world.
