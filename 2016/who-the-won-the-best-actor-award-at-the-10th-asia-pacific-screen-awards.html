Who the won the Best Actor Award at the 10th Asia Pacific Screen Awards

(A) Ajay Kumar

(B) Aamir Khan

(C) Manoochehr Mohammadi

(D) ** Manoj Bajpayee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Manoj Bajpayee, Best Actor winner. Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Best Actor special mention. Youn Yuh-jung, Best Actress Jury Grand Prize winner.

Concept note-2: -Brillante Mendoza is the first Filipino director to be honored by France with the title Chevalier dans l’Ordre des Arts et Lettres. His films Kanatay (2009) and Taklub (2015) have won awards at the Cannes Film Festival, and Thy Womb saw him win Achievement in Direction at the 2012 Asia Pacific Screen Awards.

Concept note-3: -Rajkummar Rao won the 2017 Asia Pacific Screen Awards, in the best actor category for Newton. The movie was produced by Drishyam Films and directed by Masurkar.
