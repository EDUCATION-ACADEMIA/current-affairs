Who took over as Minister of State in the Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers on 11th July 2016

(A) Dr. Mahendra Nath Pandey

(B) Ajay Tamta

(C) Arjun Ram Meghwal

(D) ** Mansukh L. Mandaviya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -English: Shri Mansukh L. Mandaviya takes charge as the Minister of State for Chemicals & Fertilizers, in New Delhi on July 11, 2016.

Concept note-2: -Dr. Mansukh Mandaviya, Minister of Health and Family Welfare and Minister of Chemicals and Fertilizers.

Concept note-3: -Mansukh Laxmanbhai Mandaviya (born 1 June 1972) is an Indian politician currently serving as the Minister of Health and Family Welfare and Chemicals and Fertilizers of India. He is also a Rajya Sabha member from Gujarat. M.A., Ph.D.
