Who was awarded the prestigious Mother Teresa International Award by the Mumbai-based NGO Harmony Foundation

(A) Harakchand Savla

(B) Narayanan Krishnan

(C) Dr Sangthankima

(D) ** Shaikh Abdullah Bin Zayed Al Nahyan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Laxmi Agarwal. For being a prolific campaigner and an active voice for the victims of acid attack.

Concept note-2: -Sudhanshu Shekhar Singh founder and CEO of Humanitarian Aid International received the award. SPARK was awarded in recognition of their tremendous work of equipping refugee youth and women with relevant skills to make them resourceful and productive with the ability to transform their war-torn nations.

Concept note-3: -In 1979 she received the Nobel Peace Prize for her humanitarian work, and the following year the Indian government conferred on her the Bharat Ratna, the country’s highest civilian honour.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Social Justice. Environmentalist Dr Anil Prakash Joshi, who was conferred upon the Padma Bhushan this year, has been awarded the Mother Teresa Memorial Award for Social Justice 2021.
