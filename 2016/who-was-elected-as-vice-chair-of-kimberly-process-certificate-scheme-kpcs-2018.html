Who was elected as Vice Chair of Kimberly Process Certificate Scheme (KPCS) 2018

(A) Russia

(B) China

(C) Dubai

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ahmed Bin Sulayem, Executive Chairman and CEO, DMCC.

Concept note-2: -The participants include all major rough diamond producing, exporting and importing countries. The diamond industry, through the World Diamond Council, and civil society groups are also integral parts of the KP.

Concept note-3: -The Kimberly Process promotes supervised and legal diamond mining and trade. The purpose of the Kimberly Process is to: Improve the follow-up of the traceability of rough diamonds of mines to the National Permanent Secretary of the Kimberly Process. Create a more transparent and better controlled diamond trade.

Concept note-4: -The Kimberley Process (KP) is an international, multi-stakeholder initiative created to increase transparency and oversight in the diamond industry in order to eliminate trade in conflict diamonds, or rough diamonds sold by rebel groups or their allies to fund conflict against legitimate governments.
