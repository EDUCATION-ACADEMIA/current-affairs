Who was recently appointed as the Prime Minister of Armenia

(A) ** Serzh Sargsyan

(B) Karen Karapetyan

(C) Hovik Abrahamyan

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nikol Vovayi Pashinyan (Armenian: , pronounced [nikɔl pʰɑʃinˈjɑn]; born 1 June 1975) is an Armenian politician serving as the prime minister of Armenia since 8 May 2018. A journalist by profession, Pashinyan founded his own newspaper in 1998, which was shut down a year later for libel.

Concept note-2: -Hovhannes Kajaznuni or Katchaznouni (Armenian: ; 14 February 1868 – 15 January 1938) was an Armenian architect and politician who served as the first Prime Minister of the First Republic of Armenia from 6 June 1918 to 7 August 1919. He was a member of the Armenian Revolutionary Federation.

Concept note-3: -The prime minister of Azerbaijan is the head of government of Azerbaijan. The current prime minister is Ali Asadov on 8 October 2019 after the removal of his predecessor, Novruz Mammadov.
