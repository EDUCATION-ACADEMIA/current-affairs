Who was sworn in as Chief Justice of Kerala High Court recently

(A) Vinoth Kumar

(B) ** Mohan M Shantanagoudar

(C) Girish Chandra Gupta

(D) Bijan Kumar Mukherjea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Newly appointed Additional High Court Judge Shoba Annama Eapen being sworn in by Chief Justice S. Manikumar on Wednesday.

Concept note-2: -Manikumar (born 24 April 1961) is an Indian judge who is presently serving as the Chief Justice of the High Court of Kerala ( headquartered at Ernakulam, Kochi). The High Court of Kerala is the highest court in the Indian state of Kerala and in the Union Territory of Lakshadweep.

Concept note-3: -Justice Dhananjaya Yashwant Chandrachud was sworn in as the 50th Chief Justice of India on November 9, 2022. He will serve as the CJI for two years.

Concept note-4: -Justice K. Vinod Chandran is the senior most Judge of the Kerala High Court with wide experience.
