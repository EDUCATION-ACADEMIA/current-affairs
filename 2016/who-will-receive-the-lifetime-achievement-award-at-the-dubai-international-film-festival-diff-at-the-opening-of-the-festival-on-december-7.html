Who will receive the Lifetime Achievement Award at the Dubai International Film Festival (DIFF) at the opening of the festival on December 7.

(A) Jackie Chan

(B) Tom Hanks

(C) Leonardo DiCaprio

(D) ** Samuel L Jackson

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Rekha. Indian actress Rekha will be honored by the Dubai International Film Festival (DIFF) with a Lifetime Achievement Award.

Concept note-2: -Italian cinematographer Vittorio Storaro will be honoured with the Lifetime Achievement Award at the 51st International Film Festival of India. He is best known for his work in the films like “The Conformist” (1970), “Last Tango in Paris” (1972), etc.

Concept note-3: -Noted Indian playback singer Asha Bhosle was honoured with the Lifetime Achievement Award at the 11th Dubai International Film Festival (DIFF).

Concept note-4: -Celebrated Spanish film Director Carlos Saura has been honoured with the prestigious Satyajit Ray Lifetime Achievement award at the gala opening ceremony of 53rd edition of the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) in Goa today, 20 November, 2022.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answe is Rekha. Rekha was honoured with Lifetime Achievement Award at the 2016 Dubai International film Festival (DIFF).
