Who won the 200 metres gold for the third straight Games in 19.78 seconds at Rio Olympics 2016

(A) Andre de Grasse

(B) Christophe Lemaitre

(C) ** Usain Bolt

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jamaican sprinting superstar Usain Bolt bowed out of his last-ever individual race at the Olympic Games in a blaze of glory, taking gold in the 200m in 19.78 seconds. Having already secured the triple over 100m, he repeated the feat over his preferred distance.

Concept note-2: -The men’s 200-meter final from Rio de Janeiro saw Usain Bolt take home the 2016 Olympic gold medal with a dazzling time of 19.79 seconds.

Concept note-3: -The event was won by Derek Drouin of Canada, the nation’s first victory in the men’s high jump since 1932 (and second overall). Drouin was the ninth man to win multiple medals in the high jump, after his 2012 bronze.
