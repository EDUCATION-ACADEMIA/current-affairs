Who won the 2016 Australian Open Squash women’s singles title

(A) Mayar Hany

(B) ** Dipika Pallikal

(C) Laura Massaro

(D) Joshna Chinappa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Angelique Kerber of Germany won her maiden Australian Open Women’s Singles title beating Serena Williams of USA-6-4, 3-6, 6-4. Q. Who won the 2018 women’s singles title in the Australian Open 2018?

Concept note-2: -Angelique Kerber defeated the defending champion Serena Williams in the final, 6–4, 3–6, 6–4 to win the women’s singles tennis title at the 2016 Australian Open.

Concept note-3: -As of 2023, Serena Williams had won the most women’s Australian Open titles, with a total of seven. In the same year, Aryna Sabalenka, from Belarus, won her first Australian Open, beating Elena Rybakina in the final.

Concept note-4: -Christine O’Neil has the distinction of being the last Australian female to win the singles title at the Australian Open. In 1978, she beat No. 7 Betsy Nagelsen at Kooyong, 6-3, 7-6.

Concept note-5: -Serena Williams defeated Maria Sharapova in the final, 6–3, 7–6 to win the women’s singles tennis title at the 2015 Australian Open. It was her sixth Australian Open singles title and her 19th major singles title overall. Li Na was the reigning champion, but she retired from professional tennis in September 2014.
