Who won the 2016 Bharat Gaurav Award, that has announced by Kolkata’s football club East Bengal

(A) Mohun Bagan

(B) ** Milkha Singh

(C) Tej P. Singh

(D) S K Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Milkha Singh-recipient of the Bharat Gourav award in 2016.

Concept note-2: -Bharat Gaurav 2022 awardee Mrs Nivedita Saha who is artist based in Dubai & working with a vision & mission of women empowerment.

Concept note-3: -Filmmaker Madhur Bhandarkar to receive Bharat Gaurav Award by NRI community of US.

Concept note-4: -Talking about the accolade in an exclusive conversation with ETimes, Paramita Bhattacharyya shared, “The award ‘Bharat Gaurav Samman’ is given to someone who has worked tirelessly to make a significant contribution to their field of work, benefited society, and worked for the nation throughout their service.”
