Who won the 2016 China Open Men’s Singles title

(A) Grigor Dimitrov

(B) Novak Djokovic

(C) ** Andy Murray

(D) Rafael Nadal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rafael Nadal defeated Nick Kyrgios in the final, 6–2, 6–1 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2017 China Open.

Concept note-2: -Rafael Nadal defeated Kevin Anderson in the final, 6–3, 6–3, 6–4. It was his third US Open title and 16th major title overall. It was also his first hard court title since January 2014.

Concept note-3: -Defending champion Novak Djokovic defeated Andy Murray in a rematch of the previous year’s final, 6–1, 7–5, 7–6 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2016 Australian Open.

Concept note-4: -Novak Djokovic defeated Roger Federer in the final, 6–4, 5–7, 6–4, 6–4 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2015 US Open. It was his second US Open title and tenth major title overall.
