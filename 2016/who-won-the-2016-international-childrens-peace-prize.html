Who won the 2016 International Children’s Peace Prize

(A) Abraham M Keita

(B) Neha Gupta

(C) Malala Yousafzai

(D) ** Kehkashan Basu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The KidsRights Expert Committee selected Rena Kawasaki as a winner out of 175 candidates from 46 countries.

Concept note-2: -Meet Vihan and Nav, Indian brothers who won International Children’s Peace Prize for waste project. Vihaan (17) and Nav Agarwal (14) have developed the ‘One Step Greener’ initiative segregating recyclables and organising pickups for trash from thousands of homes.

Concept note-3: -Rena Kawasaki, ICPP winner, featured in the EARCOS Triannual Journal. In honor of Rena’s win of the International Children Peace Prize 2022, Jennifer, the educator that nominated Rena for ICPP, wrote the lead article…

Concept note-4: -By awarding the Nobel Peace Prize for 2022 to Ales Bialiatski, Memorial and the Center for Civil Liberties, the Norwegian Nobel Committee wishes to honour three outstanding champions of human rights, democracy and peaceful co-existence in the neighbour countries Belarus, Russia and Ukraine.
