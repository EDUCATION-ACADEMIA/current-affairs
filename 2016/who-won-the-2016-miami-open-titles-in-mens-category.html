Who won the 2016 Miami Open titles in men’s category

(A) Kei Nishikori

(B) ** Novak Djokovic

(C) Andy Murray

(D) Rafael Nadal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Two-time defending champion Novak Djokovic defeated Kei Nishikori in the final, 6–3 6–3 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2016 Miami Open.

Concept note-2: -Roger Federer defeated Rafael Nadal in the final, 6–3, 6–4 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2017 Miami Open. It was Federer’s third Miami Open title, and he claimed his third Sunshine Double with the win.

Concept note-3: -Defending champion Novak Djokovic defeated Andy Murray in a rematch of the previous year’s final, 6–1, 7–5, 7–6 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2016 Australian Open.

Concept note-4: -Carlos Alcaraz defeated Casper Ruud in the final, 7–5, 6–4 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2022 Miami Open.
