Who won the 2016 Prakash Rakesh Kardaley Memorial Award for Civic Journalism

(A) Ashwaq Masoodi

(B) Aroon Purie

(C) ** Christin Mathew Philip

(D) Jaideep Bose

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Zishaan A Latif. Zishaan A Latif has been named the winner of the Ramnath Goenka Award in the Photo Journalism category.

Concept note-2: -The India chapter of the International Press Institute, IPI-India, on Friday conferred the Award for Excellence in Journalism 2022 jointly to The Print and NDTV correspondent Saurabh Shukla.

Concept note-3: -Conversation. The winner of the Ramnath Goenka Award in the Photo Journalism category is Zishaan A Latif, who won the award for his photo essay, ‘The arduous struggle for inclusion in the #NRC, ’ which was published in The Caravan in October 2019.
