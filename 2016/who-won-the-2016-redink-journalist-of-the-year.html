Who won the 2016 RedInk Journalist of the Year

(A) ** Ravish Kumar

(B) Shikha Shalini

(C) Dilasha Seth

(D) Rajdeep Sardesai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"This award belongs to the Indian Express, the entire team, ” said Raj Kamal Jha, the Chief Editor of Indian Express, who received the prestigious ‘Journalist of the year’ for the overall coverage of ‘’The Panama papers’.

Concept note-2: -Thayil Jacob Sony George, a Padma Bhushan awardee, won the lifetime achievement award and Dainik Bhaskar Group’s national editor Om Gaur won the journalist of the year award.

Concept note-3: -TNIE-Kerala chief photographer T P Sooraj wins Redink Award for ‘The Big Picture’ Instituted by the Mumbai Press Club, the Redink Awards honour excellence in Indian journalism.

Concept note-4: -Pippa Crerar was one of the major winners of the night at the British Journalism Awards 2022, taking home the Journalist of the Year, Politics Journalism and Women in Journalism’s Woman of the Year prizes.

Concept note-5: -The winning entries in each category will be awarded a total cash prize of Rs. 1 Lakh, a trophy, and a citation.
