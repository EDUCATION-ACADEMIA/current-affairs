Who won the 2016 United States Grand Prix

(A) Nico Rosberg

(B) Mario Andretti

(C) ** Lewis Hamilton

(D) Anti Rinson

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hamilton went on to win his 5th race in the United States, with Nico Rosberg in second and Daniel Ricciardo rounding out the podium spots.

Concept note-2: -Hamilton started the race from pole and went on to win the race, while Rosberg finished second, securing his maiden World Drivers’ Championship title. Sebastian Vettel completed the podium in third. A week after the Grand Prix, Rosberg announced his retirement from Formula One, making this Grand Prix his last.

Concept note-3: -The 2016 Constructors’ Champions are Mercedes, having dominated the season for the third consecutive year. Having won all but two races, they wrapped up the title in Japan. The 2016 Drivers’ Champion is Nico Rosberg, winning his first title with nine race wins compared to defending champion Lewis Hamilton’s ten.
