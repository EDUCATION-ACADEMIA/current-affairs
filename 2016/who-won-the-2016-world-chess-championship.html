Who won the 2016 World Chess Championship

(A) Sergey Karjakin

(B) Fabiano Caruana

(C) Viswanathan Anand

(D) ** Magnus Carlsen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Chess World Cup 2015 was a 128-player single-elimination chess tournament held in Baku, Azerbaijan, from 10 September to 5 October 2015. 2015 FIDE World Cup winner Sergey Karjakin.

Concept note-2: -Indian Grandmaster Viswanathan Anand was the champion of World Rapid Chess Championship 2017. He won the title on 28 December 2017 by beating Russia’s Vladimir Fedoseev by 2-0 in a two-game tie-break in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

Concept note-3: -The 2016 World Chess Championship title was won by Magnus Carlsen.
