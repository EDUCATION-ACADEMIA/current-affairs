(A) Priyanka Chopra

(B) ** Radhika Apte

(D) Kangna Ranaut

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tribeca has announced the Audience Award winners for their 2022 festival, naming Our Father, The Devil, by Ellie Foumbi ‘17, Best Narrative Feature.
