Who won the Durand Cup football title 2016

(A) Neroca

(B) DSK Shivajians

(C) Aizawal FC

(D) ** Army Green

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Army Green won the tournament for the first time by defeating NEROCA on penalties 6–5.

Concept note-2: -Since the post-independence era from 1950, the two Kolkata rivals Mohun Bagan and East Bengal have gone on to become the most successful teams having won the Durand Cup 16 times each.

Concept note-3: -The Durand Cup is annually organized by the Durand Cup Football Tournament in association with the All India Football Federation (AIFF). The winner of the tournament is facilitated with three trophies, the Durand Cup, the President’s Cup, and the Shimla Trophy.

Concept note-4: -Hyderabad City Police were the first champions of the Durand Cup in the post-Independence era, beating Mohun Bagan 1-0 in a final replay in 1950.

Concept note-5: -With six minutes of time added on, Mumbai City made constant efforts for an equaliser and Stewart came close to score in the final seconds with a free kick which ultimately went wide, and the final culminated with Bengaluru winning their first-ever Durand Cup.
