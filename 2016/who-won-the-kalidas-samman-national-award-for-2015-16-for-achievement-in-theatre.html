Who won the ’Kalidas Samman’ national award for 2015-16 for achievement in theatre.

(A) N. Rajam

(B) Saroja Vaidyanathan

(C) Gopi Kalamandalam

(D) ** Raj Bisaria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -LUCKNOW: Renowned theatre personality and Padma Shri, Prof Raj Bisaria is all set to weave another feather in his cap with the Madhya Pradesh government honouring him with its prestigious ‘Kalidas Samman’ national award for 2015-16 for achievement in theatre.

Concept note-2: -Puru Dadhich (Kathak dance), Vasudev Kamath (painting) and theater artist and famous actor Rajiv Varma were honored with the prestigious Rashtriya Kalidas Samman’ of the state government. For the first time these four honors were given in Kalidas ceremony.

Concept note-3: -The award is named after the ancient classical Sanskrit writer Kalidasa. First awarded in 1980. First recipients: Semmangudi Srinivasa Iyer and Mallikarjun Mansur.

Concept note-4: -Notes: The award is decided by the jury members consisting of five member committee of Sangeet Natak Academy’, which consists of famous performer, sculpture detractor and administrator, honor those promising writer. The cash prize awarded in 2 Lakh Indian rupee.
