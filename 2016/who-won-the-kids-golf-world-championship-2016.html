Who won the Kids Golf World Championship 2016

(A) Pinehurst Resort

(B) Karl Vilips

(C) ** Arjun Bhati

(D) Ajay Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Past boys’ champions include Notah Begay III, Ernie Els, Phil Mickelson, Corey Pavin, Nick Price, Craig Stadler, David Toms, Jason Day and Tiger Woods.

Concept note-2: -The American Junior Golf Association (AJGA) has been the leader and most competitive junior golf tournament series for the past 20 years. With alumni like Tiger Woods, Phil Mickelson, Michelle Wie and Paula Creamer, it’s easy to see why the AJGA is known for producing strong players.

Concept note-3: -Founded in 1968, the IMG Junior World Championships attracts over 1, 200 participants from almost 60 countries and over 40 States. Qualifying tournaments are held around the country. This is also known as the Junior World Golf Championship San Diego.
