Who won the lifetime achievement award at the Dinanath Mangeshkar Awards 2016

(A) Anil Kapoor

(B) Shashi Kapoor

(C) Amitabh Bachchan

(D) ** Jeetendra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jeetendra, 74, received the Lifetime Achievement Award while Mr Bhansali and Ranveer received Special Awards for direction and acting for their film Bajirao Mastani. Mr Bhansali, 52, said that he considers the Mangeshkar family a huge inspiration in his life.

Concept note-2: -Noted music director and violinist Uttam Singh has won the 2016 Lata Mangeshkar Award for Lifetime Achievement.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi was conferred with the first Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Award in Mumbai today.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is PM Narendra Modi. PM Narendra Modi on 24 April 2022 received the first Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Award. He was awarded for his selfless service to the country and society at the 80th annual Master Deenanath Mangeshkar Awards ceremony held in Mumbai.

Concept note-5: -2022: PM receives Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Award at a ceremony in Mumbai. The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi attended the Master Deenanath Mangeshkar Awards Ceremony in Mumbai today. On this occasion, Prime Minister was conferred 1st Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Award.
