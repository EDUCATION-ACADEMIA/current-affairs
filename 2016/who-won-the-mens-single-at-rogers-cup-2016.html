Who won the men’s single at Rogers Cup 2016

(A) Kei Nishikori

(B) ** Novak Djokovic

(C) Ivan Dodig

(D) Jamie Murray

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Novak Djokovic defeated Kei Nishikori in the final, 6–3, 7–5 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2016 Rogers Cup. It was his record 30th Masters 1000 title and his record 43rd Masters 1000 final. He did not lose a single set in the entire tournament.

Concept note-2: -Defending champion Novak Djokovic defeated Andy Murray in a rematch of the previous year’s final, 6–1, 7–5, 7–6 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2016 Australian Open.
