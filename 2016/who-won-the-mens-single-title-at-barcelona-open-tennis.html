Who won the men’s single title at Barcelona Open (Tennis)

(A) Kei Nishikori

(B) ** Rafael Nadal

(C) Novak Djokovic

(D) Roger Federer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rafael Nadal won the men’s singles tennis title in the 2021 Barcelona Open tournament.

Concept note-2: -Carlos Alcaraz defeated Pablo Carreño Busta in the final, 6–3, 6–2 to win the singles tennis title at the 2022 Barcelona Open.

Concept note-3: -Rafa Nadal has a very special bond with the Barcelona Open Banc Sabadell: he has won the title 12 times, has won 66 matches, the centre court bears his name and he has been a member of the club since his time as a child.

Concept note-4: -The Barcelona Ladies Open was a tennis tournament for women held in Barcelona in Catalonia (Spain) and was played on outdoor clay courts. It was an International-level tournament on the WTA Tour with total prize money of $220, 000.
