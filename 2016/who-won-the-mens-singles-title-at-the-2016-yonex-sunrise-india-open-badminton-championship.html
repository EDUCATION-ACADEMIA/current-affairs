Who won the men’s singles title at the 2016 Yonex-Sunrise India Open badminton championship

(A) ** Kento Momota

(B) Viktor Axelsen

(C) Gideon Markus Fernaldi

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Lakshya Sen. Lakshya Sen clinched his first Super 500 title in the final of the men’s singles final at the Yonex-Sunrise India Open, in New Delhi on 16 January 2022.

Concept note-2: -India shuttler Lakshya Sen won the men’s singles final of the ongoing India Open 2022 at the KD Jadhav Stadium on Sunday. Lakshya Sen defeated Singapore’s Loh Kean Yew 24-22, 21-17 in the summit clash that lasted for 54 minutes.
