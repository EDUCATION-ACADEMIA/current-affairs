Who won the Men’s T20 World cup 2016

(A) New Zealand

(B) ** West Indies

(C) England

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sri Lanka won the match by six wickets, its first World Twenty20 victory, after being runners-up twice at 2009 and 2012. Sri Lanka became the 5th team to win this title after India, Pakistan, England, West Indies This was the third time where both the finalists were Asian teams.

Concept note-2: -Team India has won the T20 World Cup only one time in 2007. Indian cricket team beat Pakistan in the final by 5 runs to win the very first World T20 trophy in 2007.
