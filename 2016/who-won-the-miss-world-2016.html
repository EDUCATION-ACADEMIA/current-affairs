Who won the Miss World 2016

(A) Priyadarshini Chatterjee

(B) Natasha Mannuela

(C) Catriona Elisa Gray

(D) ** Puerto Rico’s Stephanie Del Valle

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Priyadarshini Chatterjee (born 5 September 1996) is an Indian model and beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Femina Miss India World in 2016. She represented India at the Miss World 2016 pageant. She is the first northeast Indian to represent India at Miss World.

Concept note-2: -Mireia Lalaguna of Spain crowned her successor Stephanie Del Valle of Puerto Rico at the end of the event. It was the second time that the Miss World pageant held in the United States. It was the second time where Puerto Rico won Miss World, after Wilnelia Merced in 1975. Miss World 2016, Stephanie Del Valle.
