Who won the National title in 6-red Snooker

(A) Aditya Mehta

(B) Ishpreet Chadha

(C) Chitra Magimairaj

(D) ** Pankaj Advani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ishpreet Singh wins 6-Red Snooker Nationals, beats Pankaj Advani in final.

Concept note-2: -Ishpreet Singh Chadha of India was named the winner of the 2nd Sheikh Kamal SAARC Snooker Championships 2022 last Thursday in Dhaka. In the final, Chadha defeated Brijesh Damani, another Indian.

Concept note-3: -KUALA LUMPUR: S. Shrikrishna of India became the new World 6-red snooker champion when he defeated Habib Sabah of Bahrain 51-4, 0-39, 63-0, 39-0, 45-7, 43-2 in the final here on Tuesday. The 22-year-old completely outplayed Habib in a one-sided best-of-nine frames final.

Concept note-4: -Judd Trump beat Mark Williams 10-8 in Sunday’s final to win the Cazoo Masters at Alexandra Palace. Trump trailed 8-7 but won three frames in a row to clinch the first prize of £250, 000, wrapping up victory with a clearance of 126.
