Who won the prestigious Saraswati Samman for the year 2015

(A) Veerappa Moily

(B) Govind Mishra

(C) Sugathakumari

(D) ** Padma Sachdev

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Delhi’s lieutenant governor Najeeb Jung presented on Monday the 25th Saraswati Samman for 2015 to eminent Dogri author Padma Sachdev.

Concept note-2: -Recently, Hindi writer “Prof. Ramdarsh Mishra” for his poetry collection ‘Mai to yaha hoon’ was awarded the Saraswati Samman for 2021 on April 7, 2022.

Concept note-3: -Amrita Pritam, who wrote in Punjabi and Hindi, was an Indian novelist, essayist and poet.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. Mahabaleshwar Sail, a famous Konkani writer won the Saraswati Samman 2016 for his novel “Hawthan”, which written in the Konkani language. It portrays the life of the potter community-based in Goa.
