Who won the special award at the 74th Dinanath Mangeshkar Awards

(A) Jeetendra

(B) ** Ranveer Singh

(C) Priyanka Chopra

(D) Salman Khan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is PM Narendra Modi. PM Narendra Modi on 24 April 2022 received the first Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Award. He was awarded for his selfless service to the country and society at the 80th annual Master Deenanath Mangeshkar Awards ceremony held in Mumbai.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Narendra Modi. PM Narendra Modi has been honoured with the first-ever Lata Deenanath Mangeshkar Award. The award has been instituted in the memory of the veteran singer who passed away in February 2022. The award ceremony was held on 24 April 2022 in Mumbai.

Concept note-3: -Lata Mangeshkar was bestowed with the Dadasaheb Phalke Award in 1989. In 2001, she was awarded the Bharat Ratna, India’s highest civilian honour. The government of France conferred on her its highest civilian award (Officer of the Legion of Honour) in 2007.

Concept note-4: -Lata Mangeshkar Awards Lata Mangeshkar has won 10 awards-Filmfare Award in 1959, Filmfare Award in 1963, Filmfare Award in 1966, Filmfare Award in 1970, Filmfare Award in 1994, Filmfare Award in 1995, Filmfare Award in 2005, National Award in 1972, National Award in 1990 and National Award in 2006.
