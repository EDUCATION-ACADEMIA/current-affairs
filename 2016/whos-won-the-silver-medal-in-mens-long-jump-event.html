Who’s won the silver medal in men’s long jump event

(A) ** Prem kumar

(B) Anand

(C) Viswanathan Anand

(D) Johann zukerfort

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Commonwealth Games 2022 athletics: Murali Sreeshankar wins historic silver medal in long jump.

Concept note-2: -India’s Jeswin Aldrin won the long jump event at the Golden Fly Series Liechtenstein 2022 athletics meet in Schaan with an 8.12m-jump on Sunday. Jeswin Aldrin was the only athlete to leap over eight metres on the day. Jeswin Aldrin was followed by Czech Republic’s Radek Juska, who registered his best jump of 7.70m.

Concept note-3: -Long jump national record Murali Sreeshankar holds the national record in the event with a jump of 8.36m, which he achieved at the Federation Cup 2022. Anju Bobby George holds the record, among women, with a jump of 6.83m, achieved at the Athens Olympics in 2004.
