Whose 141st Anniversary was celebrated on October 31, 2016?

(A) Jawarhalal Nehru

(B) Indira Gandhi

(C) ** Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nation salutes Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, on his 141st Birth Anniversary on 31st October, 2016 which is also celebrated as Rashtriya Ekta Diwas or National Unity Day.

Concept note-2: -Rashtriya Ekta Diwas or National Unity Day is celebrated annually on October 31 to pay tribute to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, an Indian freedom fighter and independent India’s first deputy prime minister and home minister.
