Whose death anniversary is observed as International Day of Charity

(A) ** Mother Teresa

(B) Kuriakose Elias Chavara

(C) Euphrasia

(D) Alphonsa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 5 September. The International Day of Charity is observed annually on 5 September to commemorate the death anniversary of Mother Teresa .

Concept note-2: -In the ‘2030 Agenda on Sustainable Development’ which was adopted in 2015, the United Nations observed that eliminating poverty is one of the greatest global challenges and is required for sustainable development.

Concept note-3: -International Day of Charity was chosen to be celebrated on September 5th in order to commemorate the anniversary of the passing away of Mother Teresa. She received the Nobel Peace Prize in 1979 “for work undertaken in the struggle to overcome poverty and distress, which also constitute a threat to peace."

Concept note-4: -The International Day of Charity is observed on September 5. On this day, philanthropic and humanitarian efforts of any kind are honoured. September 5 was chosen to celebrate the day because it is Mother Teresa’s death anniversary. She devoted her life for charity and helping people in need.

Concept note-5: -Not just people, but many organizations and stakeholders are also urged to help others through volunteer and philanthropic activities. September 5 was chosen to be observed as the International Day of Charity as it marks the anniversary of passing away of Mother Teresa.
