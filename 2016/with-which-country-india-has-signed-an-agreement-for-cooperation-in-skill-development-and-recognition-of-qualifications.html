With which country India has signed an agreement for cooperation in Skill Development and Recognition of Qualifications

(A) US

(B) Canada

(C) Oman

(D) ** Qatar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -of UAE and MSDE: In 2016, MSDE signed a Memorandum of Understanding with National Qualifications Authority (NQA), Govt. of UAE, to enhance skill development and mutual recognition of qualifications.

Concept note-2: -UK has entered into a partnership with India under skill India programme.

Concept note-3: -National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) is a not-for-profit public limited company incorporated on July 31, 2008 under section 25 of the Companies Act, 1956 (corresponding to section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013). NSDC was set up by Ministry of Finance as Public Private Partnership (PPP) model.

Concept note-4: -The Union Minister for Education, Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Shri Dharmendra Pradhan addressing at the launch of Digital Skilling initiative of AICTE, in New Delhi on June 06, 2022.
