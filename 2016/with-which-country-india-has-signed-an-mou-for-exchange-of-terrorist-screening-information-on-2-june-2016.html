With which country India has signed an MoU for exchange of terrorist screening information on 2 June 2016

(A) China

(B) Russia

(C) Japan

(D) ** US

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2016. Security, an Arrangement was signed by Home Secretary and the Ambassador of the United States of America to India for exchange of terrorist screening information.

Concept note-2: -U.S.-India defense trade cooperation continues to expand with the Logistics Exchange Memorandum of Agreement (LEMOA), Communications, Compatibility and Security Agreement (COMCASA), and the Industrial Security Agreement (ISA) now in place.

Concept note-3: -In India, the Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Amendment Act is the primary anti-terrorism law. To gather information related to security, the National Intelligence Grid (NATGRID) has been established. An operational hub has been created for the National Security Guard to ensure a rapid response to terrorist attacks.
