World Environment Day (WED) was observed on which date

(A) June 2

(B) June 8

(C) June 19

(D) ** June 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -First held on 5 June 1973, World Environment Day has grown into a global platform for raising environmental awareness and spurring environmental action.

Concept note-2: -In 1972, the UN General Assembly designated 5 June as World Environment Day (WED).

Concept note-3: -First held on 5 June 1973, World Environment Day has grown into a global platform for raising environmental awareness and spurring environmental action.

Concept note-4: -World Environment Day was established in 1972 by the United Nations at the Stockholm Conference on the Human Environment ( 5–16 June 1972), that had resulted from discussions on the integration of human interactions and the environment.

Concept note-5: -World environment day is celebrated on June 5 every year and this day is commemorated to raise awareness about environmental issues, the increasingly deteriorating conditions, climate change etc.
