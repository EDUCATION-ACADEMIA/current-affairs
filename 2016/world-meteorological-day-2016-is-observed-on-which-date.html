World Meteorological Day 2016 is observed on which date

(A) March 21

(B) March 22

(C) ** March 23

(D) March 24

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The day also recognises the work of meteorologists from across the globe. Each year, on 23 March, Singapore joins the other members of WMO to celebrate World Meteorological Day around a chosen theme.

Concept note-2: -World Meteorological Day takes place every year on 23 March and commemorates the coming into force on 23 March 1950 of the Convention establishing the World Meteorological Organization.

Concept note-3: -I am therefore very happy that the theme of World Meteorological Day 2022 is “Early Warning and Early Action.” It celebrates the great achievements of national meteorological and hydrological services in improved early warning systems.

Concept note-4: -What is the theme of World Meteorological Day 2023? The theme of World Meteorological Day 2023 is “The Future of Weather, Climate and Water across Generations”.

Concept note-5: -The day is celebrated to commemorate the coming into existence of the World Meteorological Organization on March 23, 1950. Since 1961, the day has been celebrated with a different theme each year.
