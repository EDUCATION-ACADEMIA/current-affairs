[SOURCES]

World Sleep Day 2016 was recently observed on

(A) ** March 19

(B) March 14

(C) March 16

(D) March 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Quality Sleep, Sound Mind, Happy World’ is the theme for World Sleep Day this year to raise awareness about the impact of sleep on the mind and body.

Concept note-2: -World Sleep Day timeline We celebrate the first ever World Sleep Day on March 14, 2008.

Concept note-3: -World Sleep Day has been celebrated every year since 2008 before the Spring Equinox. It was started by a group of medical professionals and its main aim is to raise awareness about the importance of good sleeping habits. These medical professionals have banded together to form The World Sleep Society.

Concept note-4: -The day spread awareness about the importance of regular sleep for achieving an ideal quality of life and improving health globally. Every year, World Sleep Day is observed on Friday before Spring Vernal Equinox.
