World Youth Skills Day 2016 observed on which date

(A) 14 July

(B) ** 15 July

(C) 16 July

(D) 17 July

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Youth Skills Day 2022 is observed annually on July 15. It focuses on the strategic importance of equipping young people with skills for employment, decent work and entrepreneurship. This day is to equip young people around the world with essential skills for employment, work, and entrepreneurship.

Concept note-2: -The United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) proclaimed this day in November 2014 to highlight the importance of investing in youth skills development. World Youth Skills Day aims to raise awareness about the need to equip young people with skills for employability, entrepreneurship, and self-employment.

Concept note-3: -The theme of World Youth Skills Day 2022 is ‘Transforming youth skills for the future’. For the year 2021, the theme was ‘Reimagining youth skills post-pandemic which aimed at helping the youth recover from the devastation caused by the Covid-19 pandemic, both on a skill basis and an emotional basis.

Concept note-4: -WORLD YOUTH SKILLS DAY HISTORY In 2014, the UN General Assembly adopted a resolution that proclaimed July 15th World Youth Skills Day. The first World Youth Skills Day was held on July 15, 2015.
