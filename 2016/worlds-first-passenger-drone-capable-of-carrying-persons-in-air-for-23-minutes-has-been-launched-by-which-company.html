World’s first passenger drone capable of carrying persons in air for 23 minutes has been launched by which company

(A) Reliance Defence

(B) ** Ehang

(C) Honeywell

(D) TASL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The drone, manufactured by Sagar Defense Engineering Company, is capable of a safe landing even after a technical snag in the air.

Concept note-2: -Varuna is the product of Pune-based Sagar Defence Engineering. (Image credit: Screengrab from video tweeted by ANI) India’s first passenger drone, built by Pune-based Sagar Defence Engineering, will soon be inducted into the Indian Navy.

Concept note-3: -Varuna: India’s first passenger drone.

Concept note-4: -More specifically, Ehang, a technology company based in Guangzhou, China, has developed the Ehang 184, the world’s first passenger drone.
