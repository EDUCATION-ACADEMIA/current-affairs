Yoga guru Ramdev-promoted Patanjali Ayurveda launched its mega food park in which city

(A) ** Nagpur

(B) Pune

(C) New Delhi

(D) Bangaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gadkari, who was instrumental in getting the yoga guru to invest in the region, claimed that Patanjali would be a boon to farmers of Vidarbha region, infamous for farmer suicides, and it would transform Nagpur.

Concept note-2: -On 5 January 2010, the world’s largest food park opened 20km from the holy city of Haridwar, Uttarakhand, India. The Rs. 5bn Patanjali Food and Herbal Park is spread across an area of around 95 acres.

Concept note-3: -COVERING AN AREA OF 100, 000 SQM IN BOLOGNA, FICO EMBODIES THE WONDERS OF ITALIAN BIODIVERSITY.

Concept note-4: -Patanjali Ayurved is an Indian multinational conglomerate holding company, based in Haridwar, India. It was founded by Ramdev and Balkrishna in 2006. Its office is in Delhi, with manufacturing units and headquarters in the industrial area of Haridwar.
