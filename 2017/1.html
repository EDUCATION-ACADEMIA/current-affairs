Which player broke the 300m World Record at Golden Spike Meet recently?

(A) Cristiano Ronaldo

(B) David Beckham Jaco

(C) ** Van Niekerk

(D) Rory McIlroy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The male world record for the 300m sprint is 30.81 seconds, set by Wayde van Niekerk in 2017. For women, Marita Koch ran 34.14 seconds in 1985.

Concept note-2: -Until, that is, Jamaican legend Usain Bolt set the current world record in August 2009-almost 13 years ago. Bolt’s time of 9.58 seconds saw him reach an astonishing 44.72km/h when he hit full stride in the 100m final of the Berlin 2009 World Athletics Championships.
