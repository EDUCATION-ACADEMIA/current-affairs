2017 International Kite festival was started in which city

(A) Mumbai

(B) ** Ahmedabad

(C) New Delhi

(D) Ranchi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -KITE 2017, Telangana International Kite Festival is a two-day family fun festival celebrating Sankranthi on 14–15 January 2017, aiming to support the tourism profile and rich cultural heritage of Hyderabad as a hidden jewel of India.

Concept note-2: -The International Kite Festival, Uttarayan, is celebrated in many cities of Gujarat, Telangana and Rajasthan including Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur, Surat, Vadodara, Rajkot, Hyderabad, Nadiad, and Dakor.

Concept note-3: -The International Kite festival has many names, it is also known as Uttarayan or Makar Sankranti in Gujarat. This festival has been celebrated every year on January 14 since 1989. The kite flying is led from Ahmedabad. It is regarded as one of the biggest festivals of Gujarat.

Concept note-4: -Q. Which Indian city is the host of the International Kite Festival 2023? Notes: The International Kite Festival 2023 recently commenced in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Governor Acharya Devvrat inaugurated the festival at Sabarmati Riverfront.
