Aadhaar card is now mandatory for entry into pubs in

(A) New Delhi

(B) Chennai

(C) ** Hyderabad

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aadhar is mandatory for many admission applications such as NEET, university admissions, etc. Aadhaar is compulsory for PDS schemes to avail subsidy on ration and kerosene. Aadhaar is mandatory for Pensioners. Aadhar is mandatory for opening savings bank accounts.

Concept note-2: -The prohibition and excise department of Telangana has made it mandatory for pub-goers to show an identity card, particularly Aadhaar, for entry. The step is an attempt to check entry of minors at pubs where they are often served alcohol.
