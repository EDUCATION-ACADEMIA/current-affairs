According to doping violation report published by World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA), India has been ranked at _________, for 2015 with 117 athletes from the country being punished after testing positive for banned substances.

(A) Fifth

(B) Fourth

(C) Second

(D) ** Third

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the latest report published by the World Anti-Doping Agency, 152 (17% of the world total) Anti-doping Rule Violations (ADRVs) were reported in India in 2019, leaving it third in the list with the maximum dope offenders coming from bodybuilding (57).

Concept note-2: -Q. What is the India’s rank in the WADA’s Anti-Doping Rule Violations (ADRVs) report for year 2015? Notes: India has been ranked 3rd for the third year in a row in the recently published Anti-Doping Rule Violations (ADRVs) report for year 2015. The report is compiled by the World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA).

Concept note-3: -The report has revealed the latest report published by the World Anti-Doping Agency (Wada), which has put India into the top-three of the world’s biggest violators behind Russia (167) and Italy (157). Brazil (78) is at fourth and Iran (70) fifth positions.
