According to the 2016 Annual Status Education Report (ASER), which state has highest ratings in reading and maths

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Kerala

(C) Bihar

(D) ** Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Annual Status of Education Report (ASER) 2022: The Annual Status of Education Report (ASER) released today revealed that almost all (98.4%) students in the age bracket of 6-14 years are now enrolled in schools. This national survey sheds light on learning outcomes in schools and is led by the Pratham Foundation.

Concept note-2: -Annual Status of Education Report (ASER) is biennially published by the NGO Pratham, since 2005.

Concept note-3: -Recently, the 17th Annual Status of Education Report (ASER) 2022 was released by NGO Pratham, which highlights the impact of the pandemic on education. The report unveils high enrolment of children in schools which is a good performance indicator for government programmes like Nipun Bharat Mission.
