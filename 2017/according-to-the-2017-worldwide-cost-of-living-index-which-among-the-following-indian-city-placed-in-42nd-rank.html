According to the 2017 Worldwide Cost of Living Index, which among the following indian city placed in 42nd rank

(A) Chennai

(B) New Delhi

(C) Hyderabad

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living survey, Mumbai is the most expensive city to live in India.

Concept note-2: -It should be noted that India along with Turkiye (37th) entered the top 40 for the first time. It stood at 46th in 2021 GII.

Concept note-3: -The Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU) has recently published its Worldwide Cost of Living Index for 2022. In this edition, New York and Singapore jointly top the rankings as the world’s most expensive cities to live in, while last year’s number one, Tel Aviv, now ranks in third place.
