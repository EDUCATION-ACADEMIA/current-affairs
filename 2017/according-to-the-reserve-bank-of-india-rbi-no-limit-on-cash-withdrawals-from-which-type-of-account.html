According to the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), no limit on cash withdrawals from which type of account

(A) Salary Account

(B) Current Account

(C) ** Savings Account

(D) Checking Accounts

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A cash withdrawal refers to taking money out of a bank account, usually a checking account, in the form of cash.

Concept note-2: -The current account holders (applicable to Current Accounts which are operational for last three months or more) are allowed to withdraw up to ₹ 50000 in a week.

Concept note-3: -A savings account in a bank allows you to deposit and withdraw money any time you want.

Concept note-4: -In current account, amount can be deposited and withdrawn at any time without giving any notice.
