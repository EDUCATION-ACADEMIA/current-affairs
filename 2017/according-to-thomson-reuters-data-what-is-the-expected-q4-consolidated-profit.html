According to thomson reuters data, what is the expected Q4 consolidated profit

(A) ** 35.67 billion rupees

(B) 25.8 billion rupees

(C) 19.5 billion rupees

(D) 16.8 billion rupees

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Revenues increased 5% (6% organic) to $701 million. Recurring revenues grew 6% (94% of total, all organic), driven by strong performances from Practical Law, Westlaw, HighQ and the Government business. Transactions revenues decreased 10% (6% of total, decreased 7% organic).

Concept note-2: -Revenues increased 4% (5% organic) to $704 million. Recurring revenues grew 5% (94% of total, 6% organic) primarily driven by Westlaw, Practical Law and HighQ.

Concept note-3: -Profit margin can be defined as the percentage of revenue that a company retains as income after the deduction of expenses. Thomson Reuters Corp net profit margin as of September 30, 2022 is 14.38%.

Concept note-4: -Thomson Reuters “provides the intelligence, technology and human expertise you need to find trusted answers.” But, what does that mean exactly? We provide trusted data and information to professionals across 3 different industries: Legal; Tax and Accounting; and News & Media.
