Among these Who is nominated for Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna 2017 for Hockey recently?

(A) ** Shri Sardar Singh

(B) Shri Naresh Iyer

(C) Shri Dhanraj Pillay

(D) Shri Gurubaj Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sardara Singh was awarded Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award 2017 for Hockey. The award is the highest sporting honour of India.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Rani Rampal. About Rani Rampal: Rani Rampal is an Indian Hockey player.

Concept note-3: -Sharath Kamal has been conferred with India’s highest sporting honour, the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna award. He won three gold medals and a silver at the Commonwealth Games 2022 earlier this year. The 40-year-old Sharath Kamal will be the sole recipient of the Khel Ratna award this year.
