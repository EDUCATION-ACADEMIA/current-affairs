As per NIRF rankings, Which institute has been named as the best higher education institute in India?

(A) ** Bengaluru’s Indian Institute of Science

(B) Jamia Hamdard

(C) Indian Institute of Technology-Madras

(D) Jawaharlal Nehru University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Madras, has again topped the NIRF Ranking 2022.

Concept note-2: -The top five position as per NIRF 2022 Ranking has been grabbed by Miranda House of Delhi University, Hindu College, Presidency College, Loyola College and Lady Shri Ram College for Women.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Institute of Technology, or IIT for short, in the southern city of Chennai, had the highest ranking among India’s leading educational institutions in 2022, followed by the Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore.
