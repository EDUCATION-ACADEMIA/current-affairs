Asia’s cleanest village Mawlynnong becomes role model for cleanliness. Mawlynnong located in which state

(A) Assam

(B) Manipur

(C) Tripura

(D) ** Meghalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mawlynnong is a village in the East Khasi Hills district of the Meghalaya state in North East India. It is notable for its cleanliness and also was chosen by Discover India magazine as Asia’s cleanest village.

Concept note-2: -Everything You Need To Know About Mawlynnong, Meghalaya-Cleanest Village In Asia. Mawlynnong, located in Meghalaya, is called “God’s own Garden” and has enough reasons for that. It was awarded the title “cleanest village in Asia” by Discover India in 2003.

Concept note-3: -Mawlynnong Village is the cleanest village in Asia. Discover India magazine declared the village as cleanest in Asia in 2003 and cleanest in India in 2005. The village is located in the East Khasi Hills of Meghalaya. The village is located at a distance of 90 km from Shillong, capital of Meghalaya.

Concept note-4: -The cleanest village in India, Mawlynnong Village is also known as God’s Own Garden and for all the right reasons. In 2003, Mawlynnong was awarded the title of the cleanest Village in Asia by Discover India.

Concept note-5: -SHILLONG: Mawlynnong village in East Khasi Hills district of Meghalaya has bagged the silver award as the “Best Eco Tourism Destination” in India. Mawlynnong village in Meghalaya is popularly known as the cleanest village in entire Asia.
