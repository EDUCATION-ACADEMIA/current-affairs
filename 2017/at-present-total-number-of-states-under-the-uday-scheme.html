(A) 24

(B) ** 26

(C) 21

(D) 22

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -15 states have joined UDAY scheme: Piyush Goyal .

Concept note-2: -Only operational (10 states, 1 UT): Gujarat, Uttarakhand, Goa, Karnataka, Puducherry, Manipur, Sikkim, Arunachal Pradesh, Kerala, Tripura & Mizoram.

Concept note-3: -Till now 31 states/UT have joined this scheme Mizoram being the 27th state to join.

Concept note-4: -The scheme envisages: Operational improvement. Reduction of cost of generation of power. Development of Renewable Energy. Energy efficiency & conservation.

Concept note-5: -Under the Union Budget 2020-21, the Indian government launched ‘UDAY 2.0’ scheme, with an aim to install smart prepaid metres, prompt payments by DISCOMs, ensure short-term availability of coal and revive gas-based plants.
