Between Which countries India approved the Memorandum of Cooperation recently?

(A) ** BRICS Countries

(B) Australia

(C) Iran

(D) Albania

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cabinet approves signing of a MoU between India and Denmark on cooperation in the field of Water Resources Development and Management.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is France. India and France have signed an MoU with scope for cooperation in health research. The MoU was signed on 25 January 2022 between the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, CSIR and Institut Pasteur, France.

Concept note-3: -Space cooperation MoU Signed between India and Tunisia.
