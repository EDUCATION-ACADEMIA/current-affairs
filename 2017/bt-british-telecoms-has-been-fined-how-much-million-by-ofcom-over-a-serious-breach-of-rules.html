BT (British Telecoms) has been fined how much million by Ofcom over a ‘serious breach’ of rules?

(A) £52 million

(B) ** £42 million

(C) £62 million

(D) £72 million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As a result of these findings, Ofcom has imposed a penalty of £6, 300, 000 on BT.

Concept note-2: -Ofcom publishes a range of policy and research documents across the telecoms, broadcasting and postal services markets.

Concept note-3: -Ofcom is the regulator for the communications services that we use and rely on each day. We make sure people get the best from their broadband, home phone and mobile services, as well as keeping an eye on TV and radio.

Concept note-4: -If your problem is with BT Basic or Kingston Communications’ social access package, complain to your provider. If this does not resolve the problem, contact Ofcom’s Consumer Contact Team on 0300 123 3333 or 020 7981 3040.
