Central Government launched the “Deen Dayal SPARSH Yojana”, an annual scholarship for students collecting stamps. Only students of which Classes will be eligible for the scholarship?

(A) Class 6 to Class 10

(B) Class 3 to Class 5

(C) Class 5 to Class 8

(D) ** Class 6 to Class 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About the scheme: Students of class VI to IX having good academic record and pursuing philately as a hobby will be awarded scholarship on the basis of Philately Quiz & Philately Project to be conducted by Regional Offices. The amount of Scholarship per awardee is Rs. 6000/-per annum (for one year only) .

Concept note-2: -Deen Dayal SPARSH Yojana The objective of the scholarship is to “Promote Philately among children at a young age in a sustainable manner that can reinforce and supplement the academic curriculum in addition to providing a hobby that can help them relax and de-stress".

Concept note-3: -A scheme of providing Scholarships for promotion of aptitude & research in Stamps as a Hobby, for the students of Class 6th to 9th standards, was started by the Department of Posts in 2017-18.

Concept note-4: -The amount of Scholarship is to be Rs. 6000/-per annum Rs. 500/-per month. Selections to the scholarship would be for one year and there would be no bar on an already selected student applying for the scholarship next year provided he/she fulfils other criterion.

Concept note-5: -Scholarship for Promotion of Aptitude & Research in Stamps as a Hobby (SPARSH) is a pan-India scholarship program launched as Deen Dayal SPARSH Yojana by Ministry of Communications on 3 November 2017. The program aims to increase the reach of the hobby of collection and study of Postage stamps known as Philately.
