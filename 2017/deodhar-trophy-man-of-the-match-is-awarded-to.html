Deodhar Trophy ‘Man of the match’ is awarded to

(A) Murugan Ashwin

(B) ** Dinesh Karthik

(C) Antony Das

(D) Vijay Shankar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Cricket. The Deodhar Trophy is a List A cricket competition in Indian domestic cricket. It is a 50-over tournament which is played annually between 3 teams.

Concept note-2: -The Deodhar Trophy, also known as Mastercard Devdhar trophydue to sponsorship reasons (also spelled Devdhar Trophy), is a List A cricket domestic cricket tournament of India.

Concept note-3: -Deodhar Trophy Winners The team had won the DB Deodhar Cup 13 times. At present, India B-a robust Deodhar Trophy 2021 squad holds the title of the cup.

Concept note-4: -The Duleep Trophy, also known as Mastercard Duleep trophy due to sponsorship reasons, is a domestic first-class cricket competition played in India.

Concept note-5: -The series was announced in 1934, and the initial fixtures took place in 1934-35. Maharaja Bhupinder Singh of Patiala donated the trophy.
