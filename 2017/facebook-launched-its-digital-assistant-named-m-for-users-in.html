Facebook launched its digital assistant named "M" for users in

(A) China

(B) India

(C) UK

(D) ** US

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -M assists users by providing suggestions and helping them find things more easily. The digital assistant provides Messenger users with in-chat suggestions and services. Unlike some digital assistants, M is not voice interactive but pops up in users’ text chats.

Concept note-2: -The first modern digital virtual assistant installed on a smartphone was Siri, which was introduced as a feature of the iPhone 4S on 4 October 2011.

Concept note-3: -Once your Facebook Virtual Assistant has created a list of Facebook Groups that are relevant to your business, your Virtual Assistant will then need to: Request to join these Groups as your Facebook Business Page.

Concept note-4: -Facebook, Inc. If a user made a request for M, it uses algorithms to determine what the user wants. If M did not understand, a human takes over the conversation, unbeknownst to the user. This allows M to learn.
