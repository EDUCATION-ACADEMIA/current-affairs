Facebook’s programme named ’She Means Business’ for training women entrepreneurs was recently launched in which Indian state?

(A) Gujarat

(B) ** Odisha

(C) Kerala

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik launched the ‘She Means Business’ programme on Facebook for women entrepreneurs in Odisha.

Concept note-2: -Facebook Pragati is a CSR initiative of Facebook powered by The/Nudge Centre for Social Innovation to incubate and accelerate early stage women-led non-profits that are working in the areas of women entrepreneurship and to drive awareness and adoption of technology among women in India.

Concept note-3: -Kalpana Saroj, the Chief Executive Officer of Kamani Tubes is the first ever businesswoman of India.

Concept note-4: -Vani Kola is an Indian Venture Capitalist and a famous female entrepreneur. In 2014, Fortune India listed her as one of the most powerful women in the Indian business.

Concept note-5: -Meta had created the #SheMeansBusiness programme in 2016 to be a space for entrepreneurial women to make valuable connections, share advice and move forward together.
