For Aadhaar linked IRCTC accounts, Indian Railways has increased the monthly cap on tickets booked from six to

(A) 10

(B) 17

(C) 15

(D) ** 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IRCTC changes rules for user IDs linked to Aadhaar, allows booking of 24 tickets a month.

Concept note-2: -The Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) has allowed passengers to book 24 tickets a month. Passengers can book the tickets only if they link their Aadhar card with IRCTC. Those who haven’t linked their Ids can book 12 tickets in a month.

Concept note-3: -An individual user can book a maximum of six tickets in a calendar month.

Concept note-4: -Can I book more than 6 passengers on IRCTC at once? A. No. At the moment IRCTC (Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation) only allows booking of max 6 passengers in quotas other than tatkal.

Concept note-5: -Increasing the number of tickets booked in a month from 12 to 24 by a user ID that is linked to Aadhaar and that one of the passengers in the ticket to be booked is verifiable through Aadhaar.
