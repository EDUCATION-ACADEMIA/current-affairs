For which Odisha Chief Minister unveils logo and mascot recently?

(A) Cricket

(B) Football

(C) ** Hockey

(D) Volleyball

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"It’s an honour and delight for me to unveil the logo of the FIH Hockey Men’s World Cup for the second consecutive time, ” expressed Naveen Patnaik after unveiling the official logo.

Concept note-2: -Bhubaneswar, India, April 18, 2022: Hon’ble Chief Minister of Odisha Shri Naveen Patnaik unveiled the official logo of the FIH Odisha Hockey Men’s World Cup 2023 Bhubaneswar-Rourkela at the Kalinga Hockey Stadium on Thursday, April 14.

Concept note-3: -He was the founder and longtime president of the Biju Janata Dal (BJD; Biju People’s Party), a regional political party focused on Odisha, and he also served as chief minister (head of government) of the state (2000– ). Patnaik was born in Cuttack, in what is now Odisha.

Concept note-4: -On 5 January 2023, Hockey India launched the hockey anthem, Jai Ho Hindustan Ki for the world cup.
