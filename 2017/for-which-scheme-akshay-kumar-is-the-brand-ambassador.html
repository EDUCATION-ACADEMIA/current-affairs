For which Scheme Akshay Kumar is the Brand Ambassador?

(A) UDAN Scheme

(B) Atal Pension Yojana

(C) Central Government Scheme

(D) ** Swachh Bharat Mission

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Actor Akshay Kumar has been appointed as the brand ambassador of Uttarakhand on 7 February 2022. CM Dhami presented Akshay with a traditional cap of the state, a shawl, and a painting of all the gates of the four pilgrimage sites–Badrinath, Kedarnath, Gangotri, and Yamunotri.

Concept note-2: -10 Crore, according to a MensXP report. Moreover, Alia Bhatt charges between Rs 1 Crore to Rs 3 Crore while Akshay Kumar gets Rs 8 Crore to Rs 10 Crore per ad.

Concept note-3: -Akshay Kumar was the top celebrity to endorse a maximum number of categories, as he featured in ads of 36 brands. In India, the best way to sell a product is to make a celebrity endorse it. Even toilet products.

Concept note-4: -Daljit Singh, a dedicated social worker of Sahnewal town, has been appointed brand ambassador for Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban) and Swachh Survekshan 2023 for Sahnewal.
