[SOURCES]

From April 1, 2017, Interest rates on small savings deposits including PPF cut by

(A) 0.4 %

(B) 0.2 %

(C) ** 0.1 %

(D) 0.5 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Public Provident Fund (PPF) Interest Rate 2023: The current interest rate on PPF deposits is 7.1%. No change in PPF account interest rate was announced on December 30. This means, the same 7.1% interest rate would be applicable to PPF deposits made in the first quarter of New Year 2023.

Concept note-2: -According to analysts, the changes were mostly prompted by the government’s inability to control the spiralling budget imbalance. The savings deposit interest rate was reduced from 4% to 3.50%. The PPF rate was lowered from 7.1 to 6.4 percent. Answer.
