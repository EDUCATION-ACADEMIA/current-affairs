Google India has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with which state for supporting the digitization efforts of the state

(A) ** Telangana

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Google Signs MoU with Assam Govt to Promote Digital Growth & Development. Google on Monday signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Assam government to support and accelerate the mission to promote digital growth and development in the state.

Concept note-2: -For the same, Google has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Government of Karnataka to support their vision to promote innovation and spur the growth of start-ups across the state. The Indian start-up ecosystem is the world’s third largest such ecosystem, with Karnataka being the start-up hub of India.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Telangana. Google has signed an MoU with the Telangana government to bring benefits of digital economy to youth and women entrepreneurs.
