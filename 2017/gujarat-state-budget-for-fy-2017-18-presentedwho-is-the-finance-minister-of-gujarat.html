Gujarat State Budget for FY 2017-18 presented.Who is the finance minister of Gujarat

(A) Vijaykumar Ramniklal Rupani

(B) Bhupendrasinh Manubha Chudasama

(C) ** Nitinbhai Patel

(D) Babubhai B. Bokhiriya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nitin Patel has presented the Budget for the financial year 2017-18 on 21 February, 2017. The Annual Budget outlay for the year is proposed at Rs. 1, 72, 179.24 crore.

Concept note-2: -Expenditure (excluding debt repayment) in 2022-23 is targeted at Rs 2, 18, 408 crore. This is an increase of 13% over the revised estimate of 2021-22 (Rs 1, 92, 541 crore). This expenditure is proposed to be met through receipts (excluding borrowings) of Rs 1, 82, 295 crore and net borrowings of Rs 28, 976 crore.

Concept note-3: -Per Capita GSDP: Per capita GSDP in 2016-17 was Rs 1, 51, 362 crore.

Concept note-4: -Gujarat Budget Highlights of 2022-23 PDF Budget Present with a ₹2, 43, 965-crore annual budget, with the largest outlay for education, a number of populist announcements for farmers, tribals, and fishermen, and provisions for cow protection as well as tackling the menace of stray cattle.
