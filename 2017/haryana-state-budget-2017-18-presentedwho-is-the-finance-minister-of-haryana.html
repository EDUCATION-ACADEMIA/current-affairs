Haryana State Budget 2017-18 presented.who is the finance minister of Haryana

(A) Manohar Lal Khattar

(B) Ram Bilas Sharma

(C) ** Abhimanyu

(D) Om Prakash Dhankar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Finance Minister of Haryana, Capt. Abhimanyu presented the state budget for the financial year 2017-18 on 6 March 2017. The proposed budget for the year 2017-18 was Rs. 1, 02, 329.35 crore.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi: Union Budget 2023 will be presented by Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman today.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is option 3 i.e. 1, 2, and 3. The Union Budget for 2017-18 was announced by Late Mr Arun Jaitley on February 1, 2017. Theme for Budget 2017-18 was: “Transform, Energise and Clean India” – TEC.

Concept note-4: -Manufacturing, which contributes 30% to the GSDP grew by 6.1%. Agriculture with a share of 18% grew by 7% in 2016-17. Per capita income: The per capita GSDP of Haryana in 2016-17 (at current prices) was Rs 1, 99, 612. This is 11% higher than 2015-16, when the per capita GSDP was Rs 1, 79, 432.
