How many ATM does the ICICI bank commissioned to change as Solar powered ATM?

(A) 100

(B) ** 200

(C) 500

(D) 350

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This development finance institution has a network of 5, 275 branches and 15, 589 ATMs across India and has a presence in 17 countries.

Concept note-2: -Solar powered ATM is a machine in banking industry that recreates the banking services in areas with shortage of electricity. Solar ATM consists of components, which include solar plates, battery, and ATM machine.

Concept note-3: -ICICI Bank cash withdrawal limit from branch According to the bank’s website, ICICI Bank has set a daily cash withdrawal cap of 1, 00, 000 for transactions made at ATMs using their platinum identity chip debit card.

Concept note-4: -After this, the bank will charge Rs 20 per financial transaction and Rs 8.50 per non-financial transaction. These charges are for using both ICICI Bank ATMs and non-ICICI Bank ATMs. The number of free transactions offered at its ATMs is five and at non-Axis Bank ATMs is three (in metro locations).
