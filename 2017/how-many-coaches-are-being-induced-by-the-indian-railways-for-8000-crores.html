How many coaches are being induced by the Indian Railways for 8000 crores?

(A) 25000 coaches

(B) ** 40, 000 coaches

(C) 15, 000 coaches

(D) 10, 000 coaches

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A conventional coach costs Rs 80 lakh to Rs 1.5 crore and . LHB coach costs Rs 1.6 crore to Rs 2 crore.”

Concept note-2: -An average coach length will be around 25 meters, hence in 650 meters 24 coaches and an engine can be accommodated comfortably. Hence passenger trains can have maximum 24 coaches. It varies from 12 to 24 coaches based on the route and train type.

Concept note-3: -Till August 2022, 31, 519 LHB coaches were manufactured (RCF-9, 923 coaches, ICF 12, 602 coaches, and MCF 8, 994 coaches).
