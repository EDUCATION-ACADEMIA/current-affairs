How many crores did the GMR Airports loan from Axis Bank for developing Mopa airport?

(A) 500 crores

(B) 750 crores

(C) 650 crores

(D) ** 1330 crores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Panaji/Hyderabad: The GMR Group, which has bagged the contract to build a greenfield airport at Mopa in north Goa, on Monday secured funding worth Rs1, 330 crore from Axis Bank for developing the Rs3, 000-crore new international airport in the tourist state.

Concept note-2: -GMR Goa International Airport Limited, India GMR Goa International Airport Limited (GGIAL), a subsidiary of GMR Airports Limited (GAL), developed New Goa Manohar International Airport at Mopa in North Goa.

Concept note-3: -The configuration of the airside facilities and terminal facilities is done based on traffic projections. Phase I of the construction will be completed by December 2022, enabling the airport to cater to 44 lakh passengers per year. Further, expansion will be as per requirements.

Concept note-4: -Current Status Goa’s Mopa Airport was inaugurated and opened in December 2022 by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Commercial operations will start in early 2023.

Concept note-5: -Presently, the group owns and operates Delhi International Airport, Hyderabad International Airport, and New Goa Manohar International Airport.
