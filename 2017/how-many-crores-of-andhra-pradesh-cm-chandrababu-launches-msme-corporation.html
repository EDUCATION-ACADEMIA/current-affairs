How many crores of Andhra Pradesh CM Chandrababu launches MSME Corporation?

(A) ** 100 crores

(B) 120 crores

(C) 50 crores

(D) 40 crores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is imperative to highlight that MSMEs are the backbone of the Indian economy contributing nearly 30% of GDP. Andhra Pradesh accounting 25 lakh MSME enterprise employed about 70 lakh in the state contributes 40% of exports across various sectors.

Concept note-2: -As per data from the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises, as of November 25 2022, the Udyam Registration portal registered 12, 201, 448 MSMEs, replacing the former process of filing for a Udyog Aadhaar Memorandum (UAM).

Concept note-3: -India has a large 7.9 million registered Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) base. The MSME Industry segment contributes 33% of the Country’s GDP and generates over 120 million jobs across industries and regions in the country contributing towards wealth creation at the grassroot level.

Concept note-4: -MSMEs can avail discounts on interest rates on overdraft facilities from banks as well as under the Credit Guarantee Trust Fund Scheme.
