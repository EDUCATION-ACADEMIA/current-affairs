How many crores spend on every year currency management operations?

(A) 2000

(B) 5000

(C) ** 21, 000

(D) 15000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Currency management essentially relates to issue of notes and coins and retrieval of unfit notes from circulation. This work is performed through 18 issue offices of the Reserve Bank and a wide network of 4195 currency chests, 488 repositories and 3562 small coin depots managed by banks and Government treasuries.

Concept note-2: -India Reserve Money: Currency in Circulation data was reported at 32, 787, 129.662 INR mn in 20 Jan 2023. This records a decrease from the previous number of 32, 816, 596.662 INR mn for 13 Jan 2023.

Concept note-3: -Presently, the Reserve Bank issues banknotes in denominations of ₹2, ₹5, ₹10, ₹20, ₹50, ₹100, ₹200, ₹500 and ₹2000. Coins in circulation comprise 50 paise and ₹1, ₹2, ₹5, ₹10 and ₹20 denominations. VIII.
