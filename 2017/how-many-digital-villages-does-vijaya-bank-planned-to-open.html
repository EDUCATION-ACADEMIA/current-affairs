How many digital villages does Vijaya Bank planned to open?

(A) ** 100

(B) 50

(C) 80

(D) 500

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vijaya Bank is going to develop as many as 100 digital villages in various States of the country as part of its initiative to promote digital banking among the rural population.

Concept note-2: -Akodara village of Gujarat situated in Sabarkantha district became the first digital village of India.

Concept note-3: -Vijaya Bank became a scheduled bank in 1958. During the year 1963–68 is grew into all India bank by merger of nine smaller banks into Vijaya Bank. It got nationalised in year 1980.

Concept note-4: -In 1958, it was promoted and became a scheduled bank. From 1963 to 68, the Vijaya bank grew both in terms of size and stature-9 small banks had merged with it, thus contributing to its growth and advancement. On April 15, 1980, the bank was nationalized.
