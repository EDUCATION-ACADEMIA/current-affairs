How many gold medals have been clenched by Indian Athletes on Day 2 (yesterday) of 22nd Asian Athletics Championships?

(A) Two

(B) Three

(C) ** Four

(D) One

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India topped the Asian Youth Athletics Championships 2022 medals table with 24 medals, six golds, 11 silver and seven bronze.

Concept note-2: -The 23rd edition of the Asian Athletics Championship was held in Doha, Qatar in 2019 in which 595 athletes from 43 nations participated. It was a 4-day long event. India secured 4th position in the medals tally (3 Gold, 7 Silver, and 7 Bronze) which was topped by Bahrain followed by China and Japan.

Concept note-3: -India participated and hosted the 1951 Asian Games held in the capital city, New Delhi from 4 to 11 March 1951. India was ranked second with 15 gold medals in this edition of the Asiad.
