(A) ** 2

(B) 3

(C) 5

(D) 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the Fortune India list, the Top 5 Most Powerful Women in India are Nirmala Sitharaman, Nita Ambani, Soumya Swaminathan, Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw, and Suchitra Ella.

Concept note-2: -Nirmala Sitharaman among 6 Indians on Forbes list of world’s most powerful women. Forbes on December 7 released its annual list of the 100 Most Powerful Women in the World, 2022, in which six women from India feature.

Concept note-3: -Nirmala Sitharaman, 5 other Indians in Forbes’ World’s 100 Most Powerful Women 2022 list. Ranked at number 36, FM Sitharaman has made it to the Forbes list for the fourth time in a row.

Concept note-4: -(Clockwise from top left) Karen Lynch, Carol Tomé, Jessica Tan, Jane Fraser, Julie Sweet and Mary Barra topped Fortune’s Most Powerful Women in Business list for 2022.
