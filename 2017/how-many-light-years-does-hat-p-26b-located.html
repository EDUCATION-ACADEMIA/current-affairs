How many light years does HAT-P-26b located?

(A) 230

(B) ** 437

(C) 365

(D) 298

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Located about 437 light years away, HAT-P-26b orbits a star roughly twice as old as the sun.

Concept note-2: -Researchers have now discovered a hot super-Earth in orbit around a nearby red dwarf star Gliese 486-only 26 light years away from the Sun. The planet, named Gliese 486b, has a mass 2.8 times that of the Earth, and is only 30 per cent bigger.

Concept note-3: -Extremes from Earth’s viewpoint On 31 March 2022, K2-2016-BLG-0005Lb was reported to be the most distant exoplanet discovered by Kepler telescope, at 17, 000 ly away.

Concept note-4: -Correct Answer: B) Neptune – 2.688 billion miles away from Earth!
