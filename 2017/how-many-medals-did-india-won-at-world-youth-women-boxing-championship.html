How many medals did India won at World youth women boxing championship?

(A) ** 5

(B) 3

(C) 2

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year’s championships witnessed participation from close to 600 boxers from 73 countries. India’s eight medals in the women’s section is also the highest for any country.

Concept note-2: -Reigning Asian Youth Champion Ravina showcased splendid tenacity to emerge victorious in her finals bout and added another gold to India’s total medal tally of 11 at the IBA Youth Men’s and Women’s World Boxing Championships 2022 in La Nucia, Spain.

Concept note-3: -Shiva Thapa and Mohammad Hussamuddin won gold medals at the Men’s National Boxing Championships 2022 in Hisar on Friday. Record six-time Asian medal-winning Indian boxer Shiva Thapa easily defeated the 2021 world youth championships bronze medallist Ankit Narwal by unanimous decision in the 63.5kg final.

Concept note-4: -Babu Lall, in men’s bantamweight (54kg), registered India’s first Olympic victory in boxing as he beat Allan Monteiro of Pakistan in the Round of 32.
