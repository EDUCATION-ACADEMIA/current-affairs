(A) ** 7

(B) 5

(C) 3

(D) 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 15th BRICS summit will take place this year in late August in South Africa’s Durban. The announcement was apparently made by Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov at a joint news conference with Foreign Minister of the State of Eritrea Osman Saleh in Massawa, Eritrea on January 26, as reported by news agency ANI.

Concept note-2: -South Africa to host 15th BRICS summit at Durban in late August 2023.

Concept note-3: -Since 2009, they have increasingly formed into a more cohesive geopolitical bloc, with their governments meeting annually at formal summits and coordinating multilateral policies; China hosted the most recent 14th BRICS summit on 24 July 2022.
