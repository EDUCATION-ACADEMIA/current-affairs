How many member does the country Montenegro joined North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO)?

(A) 35

(B) ** 29

(C) 54

(D) 64

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At present, NATO has 30 members. In 1949, there were 12 founding members of the Alliance: Belgium, Canada, Denmark, France, Iceland, Italy, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, the United Kingdom and the United States.

Concept note-2: -Today, NATO has 30 members, who are stronger and safer together.

Concept note-3: -Of the 30 members of NATO, two are North American countries and 28 are European countries.
