How many Memorandum Of Understanding (MOU)are signed in Indian Transport summit recently?

(A) ** 34

(B) 21

(C) 15

(D) 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The summit was organized by the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways and conducted on a virtual platform from 2ndto 4thMarch, 2021. 486 MoUs were signed for Maritime India Summit 2021 by 22 Maritime Ports/Agencies/Authorities for INR 3.39 lakh crore or USD 47.02 Bn across different subsectors.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Australia. India and Australia have signed an MoU to promote tourism. The Union Minister of Commerce and Industry Piyush Goyal and Australian Minister of Trade, Tourism, and Investment, Dan Tehan signed the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) on 11 Feb 2022.

Concept note-3: -The road, railway, and shipping ministries recently signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to develop Multi Modal Logistics Parks (MMLP).

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is France. India and France have signed an MoU with scope for cooperation in health research. The MoU was signed on 25 January 2022 between the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, CSIR and Institut Pasteur, France.
