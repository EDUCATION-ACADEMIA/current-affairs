How many millions did the Bangalore Metro Rail Project Phase II Got recently?

(A) ** 300

(B) 250

(C) 150

(D) 400

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Namma Metro Phase 2 will be completed before 2024: Karnataka CM Bommai. Bommai added that work on the Rs 26, 000-crore Phase 3 will be launched immediately after approval is received from the union government.

Concept note-2: -Namma Metro Phase 2A (Blue Line) This line would run along the Outer Ring Road and is proposed to have 13 stations – Silk Board, HSR Layout, Agara, Ibbalur, Bellandur, Kadubeesanahalli, Kodibisanahalli, Marathahalli, ISRO, Doddanekundi, DRDO Sports Complex, Sarasvathi Nagara (previously Mahadevapura) and KR Puram.

Concept note-3: -It also passes through Hebbal and Yelahanka. The under construction line is expected to open in 2 phases (Phase-2A in June-2025 and Phase-2B in December-2025). In June 2022, BMRCL launched the first ever U Girder span on ORR-Airport metro line.

Concept note-4: -Two lines of Bangalore Metro will be available to the public by mid-2023: the extended Purple Line from Baiyappanahalli to Whitefield (15 km) and the new Yellow Line from R V Road to Bommasandra (19 km). The extended Purple Line from Baiyappanahalli to Whitefield will be inaugurated in two stages.
