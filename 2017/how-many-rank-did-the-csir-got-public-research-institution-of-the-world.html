How many rank did the CSIR got Public Research Institution of the World?

(A) 7

(B) 5

(C) ** 9

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CSIR is ranked 37th among 1587 government institutions worldwide and is the only Indian organization among the top 100 global government institutions, according to the Scimago Institutions Ranking World Report 2021.

Concept note-2: -CSIR holds the 17th rank in Asia and leads the country at the first position.

Concept note-3: -As of April 2022, there are 38 research laboratories under CSIR in entire India.

Concept note-4: -Be it, better machines or improved techniques for processing of cereals and grains; high-value products from spices and so on, CSIR is there. In the food-processing sector alone, CSIR technologies contribute about Rs. 800 crore worth production every year.
