How many runs did the India’s Mithali Raj reached in indian Women’s ODI?

(A) ** 6000

(B) 2500

(C) 4500

(D) 3800

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Mithali Raj. Former Indian Women’s Cricket team captain Mithali Raj became the first female cricketer in history to score 7, 000 ODI runs. She achieved this landmark in her 213th ODI on 15 March 2021.

Concept note-2: -Former Indian captain Mithali Raj has scored the most number of runs (7805) in Women’s ODIs cricket. She has amassed runs with an impressive average of 50.68.

Concept note-3: -Former England captain Charlotte Edwards was the first one to breach the 10, 000-run barrier in women’s international cricket.
