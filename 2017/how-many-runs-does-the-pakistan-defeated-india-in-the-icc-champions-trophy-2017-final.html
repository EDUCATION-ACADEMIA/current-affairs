How many runs does the Pakistan defeated India in the (ICC) Champions Trophy 2017 final?

(A) ** 180

(B) 90

(C) 60

(D) 50

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India, the defending champions, came into the final by defeating Bangladesh with ease by 9 wickets in the second semi-final at Birmingham on 15 June, to reach their fourth Champions Trophy final, a record. Pakistan won by 180 runs, which was the largest margin of victory in the final of an ICC ODI tournament.
