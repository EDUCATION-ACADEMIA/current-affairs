How many sportspersons received 2017 Padma Shri Award including Virat Kohli?

(A) ** 8

(B) 9

(C) 5

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Besides Kohli, the seven other sportspersons named for the award are Rio Olympics bronze medallist wrestler Sakshi Malik, Paralympians Mariyappan Thangavelu and Deepa Malik, gymnast Dipa Karmakar, Indian hockey captain PR Sreejesh, discus thrower Vikas Gowda and blind cricket team captain Shekar Naik.

Concept note-2: -Sanjeev Kapoor was awarded Padma Shri 2017 for his culinary contributions.

Concept note-3: -Hockey star Vandana Katariya receives Padma Shri.

Concept note-4: -Kohli was in Kanpur earlier this year for a T20 International against England when the news was announced. “It’s a huge honour for me to win the Padma Shri award, the fourth highest civilian award in this country.

Concept note-5: -Virat Kohli (2017) He won the Padma Shri award in 2017.
