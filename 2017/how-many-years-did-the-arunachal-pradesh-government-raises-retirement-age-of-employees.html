How many years did the Arunachal Pradesh Government raises retirement age of employees?

(A) ** 60

(B) 58

(C) 75

(D) 65

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Itanagar: The Arunachal Pradesh government has decided to increase the retirement age of regular state government employees to 60 years with effect from January 1 next year.

Concept note-2: -Superannuation. A superannuation pension shall be granted to a Government servant who is retired on his attaining the age of 58/60/62 years as the case maybe.

Concept note-3: -To ensure work continuity in government offices under the present circumstances, the government is increasing the retirement age from 62 to 63 years.

Concept note-4: -The retirement age for central government employees was last revised in May 1998, when it was moved up from 58 to 60 years. The corresponding age in the private sector has largely been in the 58 to 62 year-band.

Concept note-5: -stated to have been issued by the Finance Department, Andhra Pradesh government, mentioning that the age of retirement of government employees is raised from 62 years to 65 years is under circulation.
