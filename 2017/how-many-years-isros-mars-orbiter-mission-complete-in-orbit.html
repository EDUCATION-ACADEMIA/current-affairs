How many years ISRO’s Mars Orbiter Mission complete in Orbit?

(A) ** 3 years

(B) 5 years

(C) 4 years

(D) 2 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISRO has become the fourth space agency to successfully send a spacecraft to Mars orbit. Though the designed mission life is 6 months, MOM completed 7 years in its orbit on Sept 24, 2021.

Concept note-2: -After a 298-day transit to Mars, it was put into Mars orbit on 24 September 2014. The mission was a “technology demonstrator” project to develop the technologies for designing, planning, management, and operations of an interplanetary mission. It carried five scientific instruments.

Concept note-3: -In Short. By India Today Web Desk: Over a decade after it was launched, India’s maiden mission to Mars-Mangalyaan-has completed its journey. The Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM) has, reportedly, run out of propellant, making it difficult to be revived in the Red Planet’s orbit.

Concept note-4: -ISRO’s Mangalyaan completed 3 years in Martian orbit on September 24, 2017. The Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM), also called Mangalyaan is a space probe orbiting Mars since 24 September 2014. It is India’s first interplanetary mission.
