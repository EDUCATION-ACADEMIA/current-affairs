How many years of Birth anniversary for Rabindranath Tagore going to celebrated in Egypt?

(A) ** 156th

(B) 168th

(C) 175th

(D) 159 th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On which dates India organized the Cultural Festival in Egypt to mark the 156th birth anniversary of Rabindranath Tagore?

Concept note-2: -According to the Bengali calender, Rabindranath Tagore Jayanti is celebrated on 25th day of Boisakh and as per the English calender, it is being celebrated on 7th May 2022. This year it is 161st birth anniversary of Rabindranath Tagore. Rabindranath Tagore was born on 7th May 1861 in Jorasanko Thakurbai in Kolkata.

Concept note-3: -May 7, 2023 Rabindra Jayanti is annually celebrated in remembrance of the birth anniversary of writer, novelist and musician, Rabindranath Tagore. He was born in Calcutta on May 7th 1861 and is well known for shaping and influencing music and literature.

Concept note-4: -Rabindra Jayanti celebrates the birth anniversary of the great Bengali poet, scholar, novelist, playwright, humanist, philosopher, and Nobel laureate-Rabindranath Tagore. The day is celebrated in May, on the 9th day of the Bengali calendar month-Boishakh.

Concept note-5: -It is celebrated in early May, on the 25th day of the Bengali month of Boishakh ( ), since Tagore was born on this day of the year 1268 (1861 AD) ( , ) of the Bengali calendar.
