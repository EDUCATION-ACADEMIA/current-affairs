How much percent Wholesale Inflation falls in the month of March

(A) 2.50 %

(B) ** 5.70 %

(C) 3.80 %

(D) 6.50 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Index Numbers of Wholesale Price in India for the month of March, 2022 (Base Year: 2011-12) The annual rate of inflation is 14.55% (Provisional) for the month of March, 2022 (over March, 2021) as compared to 7.89% in March, 2021.

Concept note-2: -The annual rate of inflation based on all India Wholesale Price Index (WPI) falls to 4.95% (Provisional) for the month of December, 2022 (over December, 2021) against 5.85% recorded in November, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The annual rate of inflation based on all India Wholesale Price Index (WPI) number is 4.95% (Provisional) for the month of December, 2022 (over December, 2021) against 5.85% recorded in. November, 2022.

Concept note-4: -As per official updates, this wholesale inflation has declined to 4.95 percent hitting another low as compared to previous figures. The WPI inflation data comes now, after retail inflation data was released, which also showed an ease to 5.72 per cent.
