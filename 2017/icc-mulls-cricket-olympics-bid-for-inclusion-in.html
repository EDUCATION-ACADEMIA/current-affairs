ICC mulls cricket Olympics bid for inclusion in

(A) 2018

(B) ** 2024

(C) 2020

(D) 2028

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ICC has convened a Working Group to lead the bid on behalf of the sport which will be focused on cricket becoming part of the Olympic family for LA 2028, Brisbane 2032 and beyond.

Concept note-2: -The International Olympics Committee (IOC) has conveyed the same to the International Council and now, the global cricketing body has to make a fresh pitch for the 2032 Brisbane Olympics.

Concept note-3: -ICC proposes six-team T20 events for both men and women at 2028 Olympics. The ICC has recommended six-team T20 events for both men and women to the 2028 Los Angeles Olympic Games organising committee (LA28), as it continues its push for cricket to be included in the biggest global sports events.

Concept note-4: -Earlier, India shared an inclination to host the 2032 Olympics but now is now focused on hosting the 2036 Summer Olympics. The news regarding the hosting is not yet confirmed by the IOC. Although, India will be the host for the IOC session in 2023.

Concept note-5: -Cricket was included in the Olympics in 1896 Athens Olympics. With not enough participants, it was shelved.
