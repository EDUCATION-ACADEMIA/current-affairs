In how many years Union Government Rs 2, 600 Crores package for leather industry; generates 3.24 lakh jobs?

(A) ** 3

(B) 5

(C) 6

(D) 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Government has approved a 2, 600 crore rupees (approximately 390 million US dollars) special package to generate employment in the country’s leather and footwear industry which aims to create 3.24 lakh jobs in the next three years.

Concept note-2: -During 2021-22, India exported total leather and leather products of value US$ 4.87 billion, a 32% increase from the previous year. In August 2022, the total leather exports stood at US$ 473.87 million.

Concept note-3: -It gives a more employment opportunity. Especially women employees are high. It plays a unique position in Indian Economy but the industries run profit motive and earn more profit through the leather product clusters like leather finished goods, leather garments leather footwear, saddler and harness.

Concept note-4: -Tamil Nadu is the biggest leather producing state in India. Around 40 per cent of the total leather produced in the country comes from Tamil Nadu itself. Some major leather producing areas of Tamil Nadu are Chennai, Vaniyambadi, Dindigul, Vellore, and Trichy.

Concept note-5: -India is the second-largest exporter of leather garments, third-largest exporter of Saddlery & Harnesses, and 4th largest exporter of Leather Goods in the world. The export of footwear, leather and leather products from India was to the tune of US $ 3.68 billion during 2020-21.
