In which film festival Baahubali The Conclusion film to be screened?

(A) ** International Film Festival Goa

(B) Chennai Film Festival

(C) Jaipur Film Festival

(D) International Film Festival Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Russian Embassy recollects what SS Rajamouli said when Baahubali: The Conclusion was screened at the Moscow Film Festival.

Concept note-2: -Kamal Haasan attended a success party for Vikram along with Vijay Sethupathi. The film earned Rs 150 crore in Tamil Nadu, breaking Baahubali 2’s record.

Concept note-3: -Baahubali 2 grossed ₹1.526 billion in India on the first day of its release. In 3 days, the film grossed ₹3.91 billion, setting the record for the highest opening weekend gross in India. It became the highest-grossing film in India grossing ₹5.65 billion in 5 days. Baahubali 2: The Conclusion crossed ₹10 billion nett.

Concept note-4: -Most of the climax scenes of the film were shot in Kullu, Manali, Himachal Pradesh, which are must-visit hill stations.
