India and which country has approved a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) in the field of cyber security

(A) Qatar

(B) USA

(C) Canada

(D) ** Vietnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The Union Cabinet, approved an MoU on the cooperation in the area of Cyber Security between India and Finland.

Concept note-2: -The MoU was signed in February 2022. The MoU will provide a mechanism and help in creating and ecosystem that will promote innovation and entrepreneurship in both countries leading to new technology creation, manpower training, IP generation through collaboration.

Concept note-3: -The Information Technology Act (2000) (the IT Act) 6 contains provisions for the protection of electronic data. The IT Act penalises ‘cyber contraventions’ (Section 43(a)–(h)), which attract civil prosecution, and ‘cyber offences’ (Sections 63–74), which attract criminal action.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Japan.
