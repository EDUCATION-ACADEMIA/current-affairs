India and which country has signed a agreement for the bilateral cooperation in the field of agriculture and allied sectors.

(A) Cyprus

(B) France

(C) Guinea

(D) ** Kenya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Tomar spoke of strengthening cooperation with Nepal in all fields including agriculture and informed that India has made great progress in the field of research and development in agriculture and allied sectors.

Concept note-2: -Based on Israel’s unique expertise in Agriculture, India and Israel have signed the Agreement for Agricultural Cooperation in 2006.

Concept note-3: -India and Panama have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to enhance cooperation in the training of diplomats.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution India and Fiji have signed an MoU for cooperation in the field of agriculture and allied sectors.

Concept note-5: -Indo-Israeli Agricultural Cooperation Project started in 2008 following the signing of a three-year Action Plan based on a Government to Government Agreement. Both started an agricultural fund worth $50 million that focused on dairy, farming technology and micro-irrigation.
