India and which country has signed a protocol to amend the existing bilateral taxation treaty for Avoidance of Double Taxation and the Prevention of Fiscal Evasion.

(A) Russia

(B) Vietnam

(C) Malaysia

(D) ** Austria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Governments of India and the People’s Republic of China, on 26 November 2018, signed a Protocol amending the Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (tax treaty) between India and China. India notified1 this Protocol on 17 July 2019.

Concept note-2: -As per the DTAA agreement between India and the USA, the same income is not taxable in both countries. Thus, if you have paid tax on such income in USA, you can claim the credit of such tax paid by filing Form 67.
