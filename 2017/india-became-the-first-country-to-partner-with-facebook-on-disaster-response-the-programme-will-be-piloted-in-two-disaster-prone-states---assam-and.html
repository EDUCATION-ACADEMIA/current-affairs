(A) Bihar

(B) ** Uttarakhand

(C) Odisha

(D) Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In June 2016, India launched its first National Disaster Management Plan (NDMP) with the aim of improving the country’s resilience to disasters and reducing the loss of lives and assets.

Concept note-2: -Ans – D ( Narendra Modi) On June 1, 2016, Narendra Modi unveiled India’s first disaster management plan.

Concept note-3: -First responders are the first people to spring into action when a disaster hits. They include police officers, firefighters and emergency medical technicians.

Concept note-4: -On 1 June 2016, Narendra Modi, the Prime Minister of India, launched the Disaster Management Plan of India, which seeks to provide a frame work and direction to government agencies for prevention, mitigation and management of disasters.
