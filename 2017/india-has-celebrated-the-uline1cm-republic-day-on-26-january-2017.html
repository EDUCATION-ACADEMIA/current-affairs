India has celebrated the _________ Republic Day on 26 January 2017

(A) 66th

(B) 67th

(C) ** 68th

(D) 69th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s 74th Republic Day 2023: Parade, Flag hoisting time, all you need to know about. India marks January 26 as its Republic Day consistently.In 2023, the nation is praising its 73rd Republic Day.

Concept note-2: -Every year Republic Day is celebrated in India on 26th January with grandeur. Magnificent parades at Janpath, New Delhi, comprising the Indian National Army and national flag hoisting in various parts of the country are common practices followed on this day. In year 2023, it will mark India’s 74th Republic Day.

Concept note-3: -However, you might be wondering how the nation celebrated its first Republic Day in 1950. Republic Day commemorates the implementation of the Constitution on January 26, 1950. India attained independence from British rule on August 15, 1947, and it wasn’t until January 26, 1950, that the Constitution came into effect.
