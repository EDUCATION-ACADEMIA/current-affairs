India won the three-match One-Day International series against which team recently

(A) Australia

(B) ** England

(C) South Africa

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India clean sweeps ODI series against Sri Lanka with 317-run massive win in third match.

Concept note-2: -Australia has won the most matches in ODI cricket history, with 592 wins.

Concept note-3: -India vs New Zealand, 3rd ODI Highlights: India thrashed New Zealand by 90 runs in the third and final ODI to complete a clean sweep of the three-match series. Batting first, India rode on centuries from openers Rohit Sharma and Shubman Gill to post a formidable 385 for nine.

Concept note-4: -Since the time that these two powerhouses first clashed against each other in 1947, there have been 102 matches played between them with Australia coming out on top with 43 wins while India have 30 victories.

Concept note-5: -Incredibly, since 2009, in 27 home bilateral series that India have played, the team has won 24 and lost only 3.
