Indian Coast Guard Ship ICGS AYUSH was commissioned at which city

(A) ** Kochi

(B) Chennai

(C) Ahmedabad

(D) Visakhapatnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The fast patrol vessel (FPV) of the Indian Coast Guard ICGS Arnvesh was commissioned at Visakhapatnam by Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief Eastern Naval Command Vice-Admiral H.C.S.

Concept note-2: -Defence Secretary of India, Dr Ajay Kumar has commissioned the Indian Coast Guard Ship (ICGS) Saksham. The fifth in the series of 105-metre Offshore Patrol Vessels (OPVs) class, at Goa.

Concept note-3: -Indian Coast Guard ship Kamla Devi, the last in series of five fast-patrol vessels, was commissioned in Kolkata on Thursday.

Concept note-4: -ICGS Samarth: The Indian Coast Guard’s latest and largest Offshore Patrol Vessel (OPV). Samarth is first in the series of six 105m offshore patrol vessels and has been built by Goa Shipyard Limited.

Concept note-5: -Indian Coast Guard’s fifth offshore patrol vessel Saksham commissioned in Goa.
