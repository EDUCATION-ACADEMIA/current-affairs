Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has successfully launched a record 104 satellites including which of the following Indian satellite

(A) Astrosat

(B) Resourcesat-1

(C) IRNSS-1A

(D) ** Cartosat-2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PSLV-C37 Successfully Launches 104 Satellites in a Single Flight.

Concept note-2: -PSLV-C37 successfully carried and deployed a record 104 satellites. It was launched on 15 February 2017 by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) from the Space center at Sriharikota at Andrapradesh.

Concept note-3: -Recently, PSLV-C54 was successfully launched EOS-06 satellite on 26th November 2022 along with Eight Nano-satellites including INDIA-BHUTAN SAT (INS-2B).

Concept note-4: -ISRO has successfully launched almost 150 satellites till the current day since 1975.

Concept note-5: -SSLV-D2 lifted off from the first launch pad at Satish Dhawan Space Centre-SHAR, Sriharikota at 9:18 a.m. This is the first satellite launch in 2023. “The SSLV-D2-EOS-07 mission is successfully accomplished, ” ISRO mentioned on its Twitter handle, a few minutes after the vehicle took off.
