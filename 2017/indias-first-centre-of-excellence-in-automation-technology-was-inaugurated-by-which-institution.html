[SOURCES]

India’s first Centre of Excellence in Automation Technology was inaugurated by which institution

(A) Anna University

(B) IIT Bombay

(C) IIM Ahmedabad

(D) ** Gujarat Technological University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first ‘Centre of Excellence for Skill Development’ in Power Sector has been established in collaboration with which country? Notes: A Centre of Excellence for Skill Development in Power Sector has been established in Gurugram Haryana. It is instituted at the National Institute of Solar Energy campus.

Concept note-2: -Our partnership with Government of France for instituting the Centre of Excellence is another shining example of cooperation between the countries to achieve their long-terms energy goals.”

Concept note-3: -Centre of Excellence in skilling ecosystem is envisioned to be one stop resource centre, established/working in partnership with industry to raise training standards, boost productivity, address emerging skill gaps and align training & research with industry needs.

Concept note-4: -Underscoring Prime Minister’s vision of making India the Skill Capital of the World, Shri Dharmendra Pradhan, Union Minister of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship and Minister of Education, today launched India’s first Skill India International Centre at SDI Bhubaneshwar.
