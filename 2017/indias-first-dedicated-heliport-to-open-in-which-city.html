(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Chennai

(C) Bangalore

(D) Ahmedabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Civil Aviation Minister Ashok Gajapathi Raju inaugurated the state-of-the-art heliport at Rohini in north Delhi, saying this is the first such integrated facility in South Asia.

Concept note-2: -Rohini Heliport is a Heliport located at Sector 36, Rohini in Delhi, India. It is the first Heliport in India. Union Civil Aviation Minister Ashok Gajapathi Raju inaugurated the new facility on 28 February 2017.

Concept note-3: -Pawan Hans is a joint venture by the government of India and the state-owned ONGC which holds a 49 per cent stake in the company.
