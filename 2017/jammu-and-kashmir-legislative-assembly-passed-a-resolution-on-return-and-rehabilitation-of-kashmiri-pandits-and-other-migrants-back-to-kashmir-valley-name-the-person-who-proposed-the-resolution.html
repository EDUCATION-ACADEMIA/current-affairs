Jammu and Kashmir Legislative Assembly passed a resolution on return and rehabilitation of Kashmiri Pandits and other migrants back to Kashmir valley. Name the person who proposed the resolution.

(A) Kavinder Gupta

(B) ** Omar Abdullah

(C) Abdul Rehman Veeri

(D) Mehbooba Mufti Sayeed

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Jammu and Kashmir Liberation Front (JKLF), an organization that had generally secular antecedents and the predominant goal of political independence, led the uprising but did not abjure violence.

Concept note-2: -The Govt. of India has approved a proposal for providing relief to the migrants of the hilly areas of Jammu division at par with the Kashmiri migrants i.e. cash relief of Rs. 2500 per head per month (maximum Rs. 10000 per family per month) plus relief for ration/fodder at the existing rate (i.e. 9 Kg.

Concept note-3: -Some Hindus across India tried to help the Pandits. Bal Thackeray from Maharashtra got seats reserved in engineering colleges for the children of these Pandits. He was one of the first persons to help them after which Punjab also followed suit.

Concept note-4: -Members of the Legislative Assembly were elected for a six-year term up to 2019 and five-year term thereafter. The seats are filled by direct election from single member constituencies using the first past the post method.
