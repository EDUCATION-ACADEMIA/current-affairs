[SOURCES]

JEE Main, NEET likely to be conducted twice a year from

(A) 2021

(B) 2020

(C) 2018

(D) ** 2019

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEET To Be Conducted Twice By National Testing Agency (NTA) from 2019 Onwards.

Concept note-2: -Will NEET UG 2023 be conducted twice? NTA conducts NEET exam annually for granting medical, dental, nursing, AYUSH admission. As per the latest update, NTA has not confirmed conducting NEET, twice a year from 2023.

Concept note-3: -No policy decision to conduct NEET 2022 twice a year: NTA in RTI reply.

Concept note-4: -The Ministry of Education and Ministry of Health will soon resume discussions of holding the medical entrance exam-NEET-twice a year.

Concept note-5: -Recently, the National Testing Agency (NTA) announced the NEET Exam Date for the undergraduate course of session 2023-2024. The National Eligibility Cum Entrance Test (NEET) UG 2023 is scheduled to be conducted on 7th May 2023 at a national level.
