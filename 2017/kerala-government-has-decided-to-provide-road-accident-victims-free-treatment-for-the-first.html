CURRENT AFFAIRS-2017
[SOURCES]

Kerala government has decided to provide road accident victims free treatment for the first

(A) ** 48 hours

(B) 24 hours

(C) 15 hours

(D) 12 hours

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Many deaths and impact of injuries can be prevented with First Aid if causalities are treated immediately. First aid is the initial care given to an injured person. This timely care prior to the arrival of the medical help means the difference between life and death.

Concept note-2: -Family members of victims of ‘hit-and-run’ cases will get a compensation of Rs 2 lakh for death from current cap of Ra 25, 000. As per latest notification by Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, any person grievously injured in a road crash will get Rs 50, 000 solatium, up from the current Rs 12, 500.
