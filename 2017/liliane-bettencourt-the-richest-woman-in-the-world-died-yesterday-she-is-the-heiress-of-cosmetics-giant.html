Liliane Bettencourt, the Richest Woman in the World died yesterday. She is the heiress of cosmetics giant

(A) Johnson & Johnson

(B) ** L’Oreal

(C) Unilever

(D) Avon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Paris: Liliane Bettencourt, heiress to the L’Oreal cosmetics empire and the world’s richest woman, has died. She was 94. Her death was announced in a statement from Jean-Paul Agon, chief executive officer at L’Oreal Group.

Concept note-2: -Francoise Bettencourt is the richest woman in the world. She is the heiress to the L’Oréal beauty empire and her fortune is said to amount to around 70 billion dollars. At 68 years old, she leads a discreet life, away from the spotlight.

Concept note-3: -She is a French author and a board member of the company L’Oréal. She also serves as the president of her family foundation, Bettencourt Schuller Foundation. As of February 2023, Françoise Bettencourt Meyers’ net worth is estimated to be $90 Billion, making her one of the richest women in the world.
