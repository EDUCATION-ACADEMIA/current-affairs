Monash University Institute of Railway Technology (IRT) has entered into an agreement with the Indian Railways to support future research and technology development.This institute located in which country

(A) ** Australia

(B) Africa

(C) Canada

(D) England

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answers. Union Cabinet has been apprised of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between Ministry of Railways, Government of India and the Federal Department of the Environment, Transport, Energy and Communications of Swiss Confederation on Technical Cooperation in Rail Sector.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Pachpadra. Indian Railways Research and Training Centre is situated in Pachpadra. Pachpadra is in the Barmer district in the Indian state of Rajasthan.

Concept note-3: -Integral Coach Factory (ICF) is a manufacturer of rail coaches situated in Perambur, Chennai, India. It was founded in 1955 and is inhabited and governed by the Indian Railways.

Concept note-4: -A Bengaluru-based defence PSU, Bharat Electronics Ltd, has now commissioned the Real Times Train Information System for Indian Railways (RTIS). The RTIS is now in the first phase and will provide real-time train information to Indian Railways.
