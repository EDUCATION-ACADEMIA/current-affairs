Name the mobile application, that was launched by CBSE to help students

(A) Help Student

(B) Gather Info

(C) Student Info

(D) ** Exam Locator

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is CBSE Dost for Life.

Concept note-2: -CBSE App: Download BYJU’S CBSE Learning App Today.

Concept note-3: -The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has launched a new mobile application–’Dost for Life’–for the psychosocial well-being of students during the COVID-19 pandemic. The app has been designed for students of Classes 9 to 12 and can be used for counselling sessions from May 10 onwards.

Concept note-4: -The app goes beyond the annual exam and post result counselling route, with features such as counselling via chat box, tips on mental health and well-being, CBSE rap songs, audio-visuals messages, Covid protocols and a compendium of courses after +2.
