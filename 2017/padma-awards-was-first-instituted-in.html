Padma Awards was first instituted in

(A) 1956

(B) 1948

(C) ** 1954

(D) 1949

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Padma Awards, which were instituted in the year 1954, is announced every year on the occasion of Republic Day except for brief interruption(s) during the years 1978 and 1979 and 1993 to 1997.

Concept note-2: -Rukmini Devi Arundale (awarded in 1956) was a theosophist and Bharata Natyam dancer.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Satyendra Nath Bose.
