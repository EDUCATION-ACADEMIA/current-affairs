Physicists from Austria have created new form of chemical element

(A) Lithium

(B) Nitrogen

(C) ** Hydrogen

(D) Helium

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Physicists from Austria have created new form of Hydrogen i.e. negatively charged hydrogen clusters.

Concept note-2: -Otto Robert Frisch, (born October 1, 1904, Vienna, Austria-died September 22, 1979, Cambridge, Cambridgeshire, England), physicist who, with his aunt Lise Meitner, described the division of neutron-bombarded uranium into lighter elements and named the process fission (1939).

Concept note-3: -Lise Meitner helped discover the radioactive element protactinium, and was the first to describe and coin the term “nuclear fission” in a scientific paper. She was the first woman physics professor in Germany.
