Prime Minister Narendra Modi is all set to connect the 100th anniversary of Champaran Satyagraha with

(A) Bal swachta mission

(B) ** Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

(C) Make in India

(D) Digital India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajendra Prasad, Mazhar-ul-Haq, Narahari Parekh, JB Kriplani Mahadev Desai, Brajkishore Prasad, Anugrah Narayan Sinha, Ramnavmi Prasad, Shambhusharan Varma were important leaders associated with Champaran Satyagraha.

Concept note-2: -The Champaran Satyagraha of 1917, in the Champaran district of Bihar was the first act of Satyagraha by Gandhiji against the British in India and was considered as the first act of Civil disobedience movement. It recently completed 100 years in April 2017.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. Sardar Patel is NOT associated with Champaran Satyagraha. During the British Raj, the Champaran Satyagraha started in 1917 in the district of Bihar. It was launched by Mahatma Gandhi.
