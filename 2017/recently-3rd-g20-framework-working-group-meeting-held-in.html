[SOURCES]

Recently, 3rd G20 Framework Working Group meeting held in

(A) Hyderabad

(B) Mumbai

(C) ** Varanasi

(D) Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Today, Thursday 18 March, G20 Framework Working Group’s members met virtually, for their third official meeting under the Italian G20 Presidency, which offered an opportunity to make progress on some of the most pressing G20 agenda items.

Concept note-2: -The last G20 Summit was held in November 2022 in Indonesia. Presently, the Prime of India, Shri Narendra Modi, is the chairman of G20. The chairman of the G20 cooperation changes every year.

Concept note-3: -The G20 Summit 2023 will be held in India this year. It will be held in New Delhi at Pragati Maidan.

Concept note-4: -India holds the Presidency of the G20 from December 1, 2022 to November 30, 2023. The 43 Heads of Delegations-the largest ever in G20-will be participating in the final New Delhi Summit in September next year.

Concept note-5: -Australia and Brazil co-chairs of G-20 IWG along with India. The first G20 Infrastructure Working Group (IWG) meeting under G20 India Presidency will take place in Pune, Maharashtra on16th-17th January 2023.
