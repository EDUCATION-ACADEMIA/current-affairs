Recently, Facebook launched food delivery service in

(A) China

(B) India

(C) Saudi Arabia

(D) ** United States

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Dabbawalas (1890) The dabbawalas delivered meals directly to people at their place of work. This intricate meal delivery system is now more modern, and users take advantage of the service using text messaging and email.

Concept note-2: -DoorDash DoorDash is the company that’s currently raking in more monthly sales than any other food delivery service in the U.S. Whether you’re craving fast food, Thai, vegan food, or something from your local convenience store, you can use DoorDash to order in over 800 North American cities.

Concept note-3: -DoorDash can deliver throughout the night, depending on your location. In most cities and towns where DoorDash operates, you won’t have any problem getting DoorDash deliveries until around 9-10 PM, or even a bit later.
