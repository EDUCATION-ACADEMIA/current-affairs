EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajkiran Rai G is new MD of National Bank for Financing Infrastructure and Development. Announced in Budget 2021, NaBFID is a special development financial institution for infrastructure financing.

Concept note-2: -Who has been appointed as the Managing Director (MD) of NaBFID? Notes: Rajkiran Rai G has been appointed as the Managing Director (MD) of National Bank for Financing Infrastructure and Development (NaBFID) has appointed Rajkiran Rai G as its managing director (MD) for nearly five years.

Concept note-3: -The managing director and chief executive officer of India’s National Bank for Financing Infrastructure Development (NaBFID), Rajkiran Rai G, said on Wednesday that the institution may begin disbursing loans from the current financial year (FY23).
