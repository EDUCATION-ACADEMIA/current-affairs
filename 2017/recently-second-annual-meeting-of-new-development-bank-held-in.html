Recently, Second Annual meeting of New development bank held in

(A) Bhubaneswar

(B) Hyderabad

(C) ** Delhi

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On June 23, 2022, Mr. Marcos Troyjo, President of the New Development Bank (NDB), reported on the Bank’s achievements to the Leaders of Brazil, Russia, India, China and South Africa at the 14th BRICS Summit, held in a virtual format under the chairmanship of the Government of the People’s Republic of China.

Concept note-2: -Union Minister of Finance & Corporate Affairs and India’s Governor for the New Development Bank (NDB), Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman chaired the 7th Annual Meeting of Board of Governors of NDB through video-conference in New Delhi today.

Concept note-3: -It was launched in 2015 by Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa (BRICS), a group of major emerging economies. The current president of the NDB is Marcos Prado Troyjo from Brazil. The NDB headquarters are situated in Shanghai, China. K.V.
