Recently, which country unveils statue of AK-47 assault rifle inventor “Mikhail Kalashnikov”?

(A) ** Russia

(B) Germany

(C) China

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It’s one of Russia’s symbols. Alas, for life to continue, for lovely children to grow up, for beautiful women in Russia, there must be a weapon, ” the monument’s sculptor, Salavat Shcherbakov, told reporters.

Concept note-2: -Russian Mikhail Kalashnikov invented the weapon that bears his name in the middle of the 20th century. Born on Nov. 10, 1919, Kalashnikov was a tank mechanic in the Soviet military during the Second World War. He was wounded during the German invasion of the USSR in 1941.

Concept note-3: -Carried by American enemies and allies alike since 1947, it is the standard infantry weapon for 106 countries. There are an estimated 100 million AK-47s of a number of variations round the world.

Concept note-4: -In 1953, the legendary AK-47 assault rifle was a state secret. In April 1953, the Central Intelligence Agency received its first report of what would become the most recognized weapon of the 20th century: the AK-47 assault rifle.
