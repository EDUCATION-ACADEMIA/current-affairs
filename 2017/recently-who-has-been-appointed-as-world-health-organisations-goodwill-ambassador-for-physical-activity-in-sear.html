Recently, who has been appointed as World Health Organisation’s Goodwill Ambassador for physical activity in SEAR?

(A) ** Milkha Singh

(B) Amitabh Bachchan

(C) Jet Li

(D) Christine Kaseba-Sata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Milkha Singh promotes the WHO South-East Asia Region’s NCD (Non-communicable Diseases) Prevention and Control Action Plan to reduce the effects of NCDs with the help of physical activities.

Concept note-2: -Professor Keizo Takemi, WHO Goodwill Ambassador for Universal Health Coverage. In October 2022 Professor Keizo Takemi stepped down from his post as Goodwill Ambassador for universal health coverage. Professor Takemi is an internationally renowned advocate on global health and development.

Concept note-3: -"Emami made this choice for a definite resonation between Milkha Singh’s fitness and endurance, more widely celebrated with his recently released inspirational biopic ‘Bhaag Milkha Bhaag’, and the health benefits imparted from Zandu Kesari Jivan, ” Emami said in a statement on Tuesday.
