Scientists named 3 diseases that could cause next global health emergency. Which of the following is not part of these three diseases

(A) Lassa fever

(B) ** Zika virus

(C) Nipah virus

(D) MERS-CoV

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Heart Disease. Cancer. Chronic Lower Respiratory Diseases (asthma, emphysema, and chronic bronchitis)

Concept note-2: -So, the correct answer is ‘Tuberculosis’.

Concept note-3: -Non-infectious diseases, such as heart disease or cancer, cannot be eradicated. Without an effective treatment against a disease there is no possibility of eradicating it.

Concept note-4: -To date, the World Health Organization (WHO) has declared only 2 diseases officially eradicated: smallpox caused by variola virus (VARV) and rinderpest caused by the rinderpest virus (RPV).
