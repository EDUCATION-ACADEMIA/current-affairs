(A) Karunanithi

(B) O Panneerselvam

(C) VK Sasikala

(D) ** Edappadi K. Palaniswami

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Palaniswami (born 12 May 1954), also known as E.P.S., is an Indian politician and the leader of opposition in the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly. He served as the 7th chief minister of Tamil Nadu, from 2017 to 2021., Palaniswami served as the Joint Coordinator of the AIADMK from 2017 to 2022.

Concept note-2: -Per capita GSDP: The per capita GSDP of Tamil Nadu in 2017-18 (at current prices) was Rs 1, 86, 125. This is 11% higher than the figure for 2016-17 (Rs 1, 67, 240).
