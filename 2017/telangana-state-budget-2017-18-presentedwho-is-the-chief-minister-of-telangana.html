Telangana State Budget 2017-18 presented.who is the chief minister of Telangana

(A) Chandrababu Naidu

(B) Naveen Patnaik

(C) ** Chandrashekar Rao

(D) Vijay Rupani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Total expenditure for 2017-18 is estimated to be Rs 1, 49, 646 crore, a 33.4% increase over the revised estimate of 2016-17. In 2016-17, there was a decrease of Rs 18, 225 crore (14%) in the revised estimate over the budget estimate.

Concept note-2: -Harish Rao said the Union government’s decision to consider off-budget borrowings to corporations as budget borrowings of the states from 2021-22 had come as a shock.

Concept note-3: -T. Harish Rao, presented the Budget for the state for the financial year 2022-23 on March 7, 2022. The Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) of the state for 2022-23 (at current prices) is projected to be Rs 13.04 lakh crore. This is a growth of 13% over the revised estimate of GSDP for 2021-22 (Rs 11.54 lakh crore).

Concept note-4: -Telangana Finance Minister T Harish Rao today introduced the state Budget for the Financial year 2023-24 in the assembly with an expenditure outlay of 2 lakh 90 thousand 396 crore rupees. This includes 37 thousand 525 crore rupees in capital expenditure and 2 lakh 11 thousand 685 crore rupees of Revenue expenditure.
