The 19th Bharat Rang Mahotsav was held in which city

(A) Mumbai

(B) Banglore

(C) Ahmedabad

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Previously, the NSD had organised Bharat Rang Mahotsav in July 2022 under the “Azadi Segment” of the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, to commemorate and pay its tribute to the martyrs of the country.

Concept note-2: -As part of the 22nd Bharat Rang Mahotsav, 2022 (Azadi Segment), 30 plays will be showcased in Delhi, Bhubaneshwar, Varanasi, Amritsar, Bengaluru and Mumbai from 16th July to 14th August, 2022.

Concept note-3: -Notes: Maharashtra Governor Bhagat Singh Koshyari inaugurated the 22nd ‘Bharat Rang Mahotsav’ in Mumbai.
