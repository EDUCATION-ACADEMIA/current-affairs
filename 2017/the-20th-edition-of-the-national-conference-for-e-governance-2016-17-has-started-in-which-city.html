(A) Kochi

(B) Ahmedabad

(C) Ranchi

(D) ** Visakhapatnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About the 25th NCeG 25th National Conference on e-Governance (NCeG) will be held in Katra – a city in the union territory of Jammu and Kashmir.

Concept note-2: -Award was presented by Hon’ble Union Minster Dr Jitendra Singh on 7th Jan 2022 during 2 days 24th National Conference on eGovernance held at Hyderabad International Convention Centre, Hyderabad.
