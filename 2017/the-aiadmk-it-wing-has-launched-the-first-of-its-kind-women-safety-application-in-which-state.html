CURRENT AFFAIRS-2017
[SOURCES]

The AIADMK IT Wing has launched the first-of-its-kind women safety application in which state

(A) ** Tamil Nadu

(B) Kerala

(C) Bihar

(D) Jharkhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1. 112 India. The Central Government of India has launched 112 India, an all-in-one women’s security app that can be used to issue an SOS alert with only a single tap in any situation. The service is available on both Android and iOS devices in 23 states and Union Territories.

Concept note-2: -Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu is undoubtedly the safest city for women in India in 2022. The rate of crime in the city in 2019 was only 7.9. Crime rate is the number of crimes recorded per one lakh of the population.

Concept note-3: -Jai Ram Thakur, the Chief Minister of Himachal Pradesh, has launched the ‘Shakti’ app to ensure safety and protection of women in the state.

Concept note-4: -People residing in Tamil Nadu, especially, Women and elderly people can use the Kavalan – SOS app whenever they feel vulnerable or threatened. Through this mobile app, help from Police can reach them at the earliest no matter where they are.
