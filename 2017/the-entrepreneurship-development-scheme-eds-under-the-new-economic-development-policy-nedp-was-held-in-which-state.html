The Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (EDS) under the New Economic Development Policy (NEDP) was held in which state

(A) Haryana

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) ** Mizoram

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEDP is the economic policy rolled out by the Mizoram state government in 2016 to boost the agriculture in the state. Under this scheme EDS was launched in 2017 on January 19 in Aizawl. SSC CGL Tier I 2022 Results released on 9th February 2023.

Concept note-2: -New Land Use Policy (NLUP)/ New Economic Development Policy (NEDP: The State Government of Mizoram has launched State flagship programme-New Land Use Policy on 14th January 2011 (this day of 14th January was since then observed as State’s Labour day every year).

Concept note-3: -Entrepreneurship Development Programme (EDP) is a programme which helps in developing entrepreneurial abilities. The skills that are required to run a business successfully is developed among the students through this programme. Sometimes, students may have skills but it requires polishing and incubation.

Concept note-4: -Their responsibilities are to provide a stimulating business environment and development support to SMEs, by stimulating legislation, improving institutional capacities, rendering adequate measures of economic policy, and establishing the necessary infrastructure.

Concept note-5: -Entrepreneurship Development Institute of india. Institute for Rural Management and Adminstration. National Institute for Entrepreneurship and Small Business Development (NIESBUD) National Alliance of young entrepreneurs (NAYA)
