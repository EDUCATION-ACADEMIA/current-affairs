The first World Theatre Day International Message was written by

(A) Stephen King

(B) Dan Brown

(C) John Grisham

(D) ** Jean Cocteau

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first World Theatre Day Message was written by Jean Cocteau in 1962.

Concept note-2: -WORLD THEATRE DAY 2022: SIGNIFICANCE The significance of this day is to raise awareness regarding the importance of theatre and to know the importance of theatre around the world. The day also provides opportunities for theatrical communities to put forward their work on a large scale.

Concept note-3: -The theme for World theatre Day 2022 is ‘Theatre and a Culture of Peace’ and it remains the same every year.

Concept note-4: -The first World Theatre Day International Message was written by French poet and playwright Jean Cocteau in 1962.
