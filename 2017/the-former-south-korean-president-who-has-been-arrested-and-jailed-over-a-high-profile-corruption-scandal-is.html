The former South Korean president, who has been arrested and jailed over a high profile corruption scandal is

(A) Lee Myung-bak

(B) ** Park Geun-hye

(C) Roh Moo-hyun

(D) Kim Dae-jung

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Park Geun-hye (Korean: ; RR: Bak Geun(-)hye; IPA: [pak‿k͈ɯn. hje]; often in English /ˈpɑːrk ˌɡʊn ˈheɪ/; born 2 February 1952) is a South Korean politician who served as the 11th president of South Korea from 2013 to 2017, until she was impeached and convicted on related corruption charges.

Concept note-2: -Chun Doo-hwan (Korean: ; Hanja: ; Korean pronunciation: [tɕʌnduɦwɐn] or [tɕʌn] [tuɦwɐn]; 18 January 1931 – 23 November 2021) was a South Korean army general and military dictator who ruled as an unelected strongman from 1979 to 1980 before replacing Choi Kyu-hah as president of South Korea from 1980 to 1988.

Concept note-3: -The 2016 South Korean political scandal (Korean: · , Park Geun-hye–Choi Soon-sil gate) involves the influence of Choi Soon-sil, the daughter of shaman-esque cult leader Choi Tae-min, over President Park Geun-hye of South Korea.
