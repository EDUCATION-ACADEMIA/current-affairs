(A) Deutsche Bank

(B) Standard Chartered

(C) ** Bombay Mercantile Co-operative Bank Ltd

(D) Punjab National Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has, by an order dated July 7, 2022, imposed a monetary penalty of ₹70 lakh (Rupees Seventy lakh only) on Bank of India.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) on Monday informed that it has imposed monetary penalties on nine cooperative banks from six states for non-compliance with various provisions.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has imposed a monetary penalty of ₹1, 76, 00, 000 (Rupees One Crore and Seventy Six Lakh only) on Vakrangee Limited (the entity) for non-compliance with certain provisions of the White Label ATM (WLA) directions.
