The State Bank of India has launched a new wealth management product named “SBI Exclusif” in which city

(A) Mumbai

(B) Ahmedabad

(C) New Delhi

(D) ** Kochi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SBI Exclusif, a wealth management product, was introduced by State Bank of India in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -SBI Wealth Management provides customized financial planning for small businesses and individuals. We offer independent financial guidance focused on our clients’ unique needs and priorities. Being able to offer a range of services allows us to customize each financial plan.

Concept note-3: -Wealth Savings Account is a Premium Account offered only for Wealth Clients with add-on benefits viz. Free VISA Signature Debit Card, Fee Waivers, 25% Concession on Locker Rentals and many more. Sl.No.

Concept note-4: -State Bank of India (SBI) a Fortune 500 company, is an Indian Multinational, Public Sector Banking and Financial services statutory body headquartered in Mumbai.
