The Steel Ministry has released new draft National Steel Policy of 2017 envisaging to double India’s domestic steel production capacity to 300 million tonnes by which year

(A) 2020-25

(B) 2017-30

(C) 2017-20

(D) ** 2030-31

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The policy projects crude steel capacity of 300 million tonnes (MT), production of 255 MT and a robust finished steel per capita consumption of 158 Kgs by 2030-31, as against the current consumption of 61 Kgs.

Concept note-2: -The National Steel Policy, 2017 aspires to achieve 300 MT of steelmaking capacity by 2030.

Concept note-3: -The contribution of steel to India’s economy is around 2%.
