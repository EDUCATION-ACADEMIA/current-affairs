The _________ Annual Grammy Awards were announced by the Recording Academy announced at the Staples Center in Los Angeles

(A) 70th

(B) 89th

(C) 23rd

(D) ** 59th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first Grammy Awards ceremony was held in Los Angeles in 1959, and 28 awards were presented.

Concept note-2: -The Recording Academy will reveal the nominations for the 2022 GRAMMYs Awards show via the second annual virtual livestream event taking place Tuesday, Nov. 23 and available to view on live.grammy.com.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Pandit Ravi Shankar. Sitar maestro Pandit Ravi Shankar will be honoured with a Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award posthumously, organizers of the Recording Academy announced, the first Indian to get the prestigious award.
