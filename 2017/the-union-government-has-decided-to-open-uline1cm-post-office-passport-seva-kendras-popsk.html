The Union Government has decided to open _________ Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSK).

(A) 47

(B) 100

(C) ** 56

(D) 79

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Passport Offices (PO) are vested with the authority to issue passports, besides revocation and impounding and exercise control over the PSK(s) under them. They handle all back-end functions required for processing all passport applications.

Concept note-2: -Step 4: After submission, visit the nearby post office which has Passport Seva Kendra along with your application print receipt and original documents. Step 5: Upon successful verification by the officials, your passport will be issued within 7 to 14 working days.

Concept note-3: -Generally, a passport application processing will take 2-3 hours at Passport Seva Kendra for capturing Photos, Biometrics and other processes.

Concept note-4: -It means your application is under scrutiny……. you got to give yourself atleast 7 working days after your application has been submitted with all supporting documents for your status to change on your application tracker status…. If it does not change then you need to contact your RPO office…
