The Union Government is planning to set up a Defence Procurement Organisation (DPO).This agency will be established based on the recommendation of which committee

(A) Rajendran Committee

(B) ** Pritam Singh Committee

(C) Ajay Singh Committee

(D) Ravi Kumar Committee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The procurement proposals were cleared by the Defence Acquisition Council (DAC) headed by Defence Minister Rajnath Singh.

Concept note-2: -The Department of Defence is mandated with Defence of India and every part thereof including defence policy.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of Defence consists of five departments; Department of Defence (DoD), Department of Military Affairs (DMA), Department of Defence Production (DDP), Department of Defence Research and Development (DRDO), and Department of Ex-Servicemen Welfare (DESW).

Concept note-4: -The Defence Procurement Procedure (DPP) was first initiated in 2002 to streamline the procurement of military hardware for the Armed Forces in a systematic and time-bound manner.
