[SOURCES]

The Union Ministry of Railways has signed an MoU with which country for technical cooperation in rail sector on February 2, 2017 with focus on safety operations.

(A) Indonesia

(B) Japan

(C) China

(D) ** Italy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: The railway ministry and Italy’s Ferrovie Dello Stato Italiane Group (FS group) have signed an Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) for technical cooperation in rail sector especially in the area of safety and modernisation. The FS group that manages majority of rail operations in Italy.

Concept note-2: -Answers. Union Cabinet has been apprised of the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between Ministry of Railways, Government of India and the Federal Department of the Environment, Transport, Energy and Communications of Swiss Confederation on Technical Cooperation in Rail Sector.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Centre for Development of Telematics. The Ministry of Railways has signed an MoU with the Centre for Development of Telematics (C-DOT). It has been signed for the modernization of telecommunication facilities related to operations and safety.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-5: -In supersession of Board’s letter under reference, the revised format for entering into Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between Railways and CRIS for execution of IT projects is enclosed herewith.
