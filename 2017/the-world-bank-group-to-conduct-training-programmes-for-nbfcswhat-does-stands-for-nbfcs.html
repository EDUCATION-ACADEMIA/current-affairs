Concept note-3: -What is a NBFC? A Non-Banking Financial Corporation is a company that is registered under the Companies Act, 1956 of the Companies Act, 2013 and is involved in the lending business, hire-purchase, leasing, insurance business, receiving deposits in some cases, chit funds, stocks, and shares acquisition, etc.
