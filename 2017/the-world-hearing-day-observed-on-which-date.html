The World Hearing Day observed on which date

(A) 17 May

(B) 31 October

(C) 29 April

(D) ** 3 March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Hearing Day is held on 3 March each year to raise awareness on how to prevent deafness and hearing loss and promote ear and hearing care across the world.

Concept note-2: -The WHO, on March 3 in 2007, observed World Hearing Day for the first time. In 2016, they decided to declare this day as World Hearing Day. It was known as International Ear Care Day before that.

Concept note-3: -The World Hearing Day 2022 with the theme “To hear for life, listen with care” will focus on the importance and means of hearing loss prevention through safe listening, with the following key messages: It is possible to have good hearing across the life course through ear and hearing care

Concept note-4: -3 March is World Hearing Day World Hearing Day is held on 3 March each year to raise awareness on how to prevent deafness and hearing loss and promote ear and hearing care across the world.

Concept note-5: -According to WHO, the day was created in 2007 in a bid to raise awareness about deafness. It was then called International Ear Day, but the name was changed to World Hearing Day in 2016. The WHO has taken “To hear for life, listen with care” as the theme for this year’s World Hearing Day.
