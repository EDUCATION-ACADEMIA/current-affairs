To boost growth in India’s iOS developer community, recently, Apple has announced the opening of its new App Accelerator in

(A) Kolkata

(B) Delhi

(C) Hyderabad

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has one of the most vibrant and exciting iOS developer communities in the world and already tens of thousands of developers in India make apps for iOS. The App Accelerator Bengaluru will help to further enrich the iOS app ecosystem.

Concept note-2: -Yes, an iOS developer is a good career option in 2022. iOS development is a fast-growing field with a high demand for iOS developers across the world. iOS developers are also among the top earners in the tech industry.

Concept note-3: -In 2023, the iOS app developers should focus on Core Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and the Internet of Things. Among the latest technologies are also iBeacon and Augmented Reality.

Concept note-4: -As more and more companies look to grow their online presence, the demand for iOS app developers is rising significantly. Apple makes up a significant portion of that demand. According to the US Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), the demand for iOS developers is expected to rise 25 percent between 2021-2031.
