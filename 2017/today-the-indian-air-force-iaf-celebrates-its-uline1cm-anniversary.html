Today the Indian Air Force (IAF) celebrates its _________ anniversary.

(A) 60th

(B) 75th

(C) 80th

(D) ** 85th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Air Force was officially established on October 8, 1932 and this year marked as the 90th-anniversary celebration of the IAF.

Concept note-2: -Indian Air Force Day 2022: IAF Marks 90 Years With Celebration In Chandigarh. Indians proudly celebrate October 8 as the day to pay tribute to the air warriors of the Indian Air Force (IAF) who tirelessly defend the nation by securing the Indian airspace and conducting rescue operations during natural calamities.

Concept note-3: -Indian Air Force Day is celebrated on October 8 every year. Every year, the Air Force Day is celebrated at Hindon base in presence of IAF chief and senior officials of the three armed forces. These celebrations include an air display where the most crucial and vintage aircraft put up a magnificent show.

Concept note-4: -It is also the 8th largest in the world. INDIAN AIR FORCE DAY HISTORY: The Indian Air Force was established in the country on October 8, 1932, by the British Empire.

Concept note-5: -The theme for IAF Day 2022 is ‘Transforming for the Future’. The event will be attended by President Draupadi Murmu and Defence Minister Rajnath Singh along with top officials.
