Truecaller partners with Google to improve Video Calls. What is that video calling app, which will be integrated within the Truecaller app?

(A) ** Google Duo

(B) Google Voice

(C) Google Allo

(D) Google Fiber

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -How Does Google Duo Work? The new feature will now make high-quality video calling available to our 250 million users globally. This update enables users to launch a Duo video call with a single tap within the Truecaller app and can switch between WiFi and cellular data for uninterrupted conversation on-the-go.

Concept note-2: -Eyecon. The next alternative to Truecaller is Eyecon. If you are searching for it on Google Play Store, then you can search it as Eyecon Callerid.

Concept note-3: -Join from your phone Use the Google Meet app to join a video call, or join audio-only by calling the dial-in number in the meeting invite.

Concept note-4: -Yes, we provide predefined Video Caller IDs which can be used for your Video Caller ID.

Concept note-5: -Google Meet has been updated to include both video calling and meeting capabilities. You can now use Google Meet to make 1:1 video calls, group calls, or voice calls. On your phone, you can also create, schedule, or join meetings.
