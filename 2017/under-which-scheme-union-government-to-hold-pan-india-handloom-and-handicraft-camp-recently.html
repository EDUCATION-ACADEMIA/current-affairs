Under which Scheme Union Government to hold Pan India Handloom and Handicraft Camp recently?

(A) Atal Pension Yojana Swarna

(B) ** Swarozgar Yojana Hastkala sahyog Shivir Scheme

(C) Deen Dayal Upadhyaya

(D) Gram Jyoti Yojana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Culture and Ministry of Textiles are organising a programme called “Jharokha-Compendium of Indian handicraft/ handloom, art and culture” to celebrate the traditional Indian handicrafts.

Concept note-2: -Ambedkar Hastshilp Vikas Yojana: To support artisans with their infrastructure, technology and human resource development needs. The objective of mobilising artisans into self-help groups and societies with the agenda of facilitating bulk production and economies in procurement of raw materials.

Concept note-3: -Overview: It is a PAN India celebration and will be held in 13 states and UTs at 16 locations. Under this programme, the first event has been organized in Bhopal starting from 8th March 2022 on the occasion of International Women’s Day. The location of the first event was Rani Kamlapati Railway Station in Bhopal.

Concept note-4: -Margin money assistance to a maximum of Rs. 10, 000 per weaver and credit guarantee for a period of 3 years is also provided.
