U.S. approves sale of Rs. 98, 000 crore THAAD missiles to

(A) Iran

(B) ** Saudi Arabia

(C) South Korea

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The U.S. Army operates seven THAAD batteries, each with its own AN/TPY-2 radar.

Concept note-2: -THAAD has been deployed in the United Arab Emirates, Israel, Romania, and South Korea. On 17 January 2022, THAAD made its first operational interception, of an incoming medium-range ballistic missile in the UAE.

Concept note-3: -MDA-Terminal High Altitude Area Defense (THAAD)

Concept note-4: -Deployed on the grounds of a former golf course south of South Korea’s capital, Seoul, THAAD is designed to intercept incoming ballistic missiles. The weapon carries no warhead and destroys missiles through impact. The system is considered one of the best defenses available.
