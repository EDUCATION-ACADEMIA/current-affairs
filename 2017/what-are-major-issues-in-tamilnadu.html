What are major issues in tamilnadu?

(A) Jallikattu issue

(B) ** Cauvery water and Methane, hydrocarbon and nuterino projects

(C) Dam for irrigation purposes

(D) Flyower construction

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Social inequality The Brahmins captured priesthood in rich temples. Temples, formal education, high offices and property rights were denied to the hardworking tillers and artisans who were categorised as Sudras and Panchamas. Thus, the masses of Tamil Nadu were deprived of rights.

Concept note-2: -Chennai has major safety issues. Crime rates have been observed rising in the city. Major cause of concern is that many shameful crimes have took place in broad daylight where people don’t even bother to help the victim. Violent crimes like armed robbery and assault have been observed on an increase.

Concept note-3: -CHENNAI: Tamil Nadu has the highest death rate due to lifestyle diseases such diabetes and kidney disease. Its death rate for ischemic heart diseases and Alzheimer’s is also higher than the national average, according to a report, ‘India: Health of the Nation’s States’.
