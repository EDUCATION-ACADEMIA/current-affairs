What is the name of the app which was introduced by the Google recently for businesses?

(A) ** Hire

(B) Linked In

(C) Naukiri app

(D) Times job

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Google My Business is now Google Business Profile Manager And here’s something that’s new: in addition to accessing your profile from business.google.com, you’ll also be able to manage it from the Google Maps App or in Google Search.

Concept note-2: -Google Workspace. Business Apps & Collaboration Tools.

Concept note-3: -The Google My Business dashboard will be transitioned to support businesses with multiple listings, and Google would like businesses to manage their Business Profile directly from Search or Maps. To manage your listing from Google Search, all you have to do is search for your business.

Concept note-4: -Google Business Profile is an easy-to-use tool for businesses and organizations to manage their online presence across Google. To help customers find your business, you can verify your business and edit your business information.
