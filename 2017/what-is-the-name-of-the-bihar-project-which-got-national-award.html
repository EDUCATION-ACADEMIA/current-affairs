What is the name of the Bihar Project which got National Award?

(A) Government Infrastructure Project

(B) ** Project Jal Sanchay

(C) Clean Ganga Project

(D) Smart Cities Project

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -East Champaran was awarded for rejuvenating the 80km stretch of Moti Jheel and Dhanaulti river under state’s Jal-Jeevan-Hariyali mission by roping in labourers during the Covid pandemic to provide them jobs.

Concept note-2: -Padma awards Bihar 2022 The Padma Award, the country’s most prestigious civilian honour, is awarded in three categories.

Concept note-3: -These two teachers of Bihar who were awarded the National Teacher Award-2022 include Nishi Kumari from Mahadev Higher Secondary School Khushrupur, Patna and Saurav Suman from Lalit Narayan Laxmi Narayan Project Girls’ High School Triveniganj, Supaul.

Concept note-4: -These awards are further divided into four types: Bharat Ratna. Padma Vibhushan. Padma Bhushan.

Concept note-5: -Pride of Indian Education Awards in Bihar may be bestowed upon India’s Top Universities, Colleges, Institutes, Coaching Classes, Education Professionals who have made a real difference in the Education sector in Bihar. The sixth edition of Pride of Indian Education Awards are planned to be held in July, 2023.
