What is the name of the missile from a mobile launcher at Integrated Test Range at Chandipur in Odisha?

(A) Brahmos - I

(B) PSLV C - 11

(C) ** Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile (QR-SAM)

(D) Agni -III

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Integrated Test Range (ITR) is a missile testing facility composed of two complexes-Launch Complex-IV (LC-IV) located on Abdul Kalam Island and Launch Complex-III (LC-III) located at Chandipur.

Concept note-2: -India successfully test fires Agni Prime ballistic missile off Odisha coast | India News, The Indian Express.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is option 1 i.e Subsonic Cruise missile.
