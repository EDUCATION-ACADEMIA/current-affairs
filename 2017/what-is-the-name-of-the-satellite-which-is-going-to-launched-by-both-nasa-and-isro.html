What is the name of the satellite which is going to launched by both NASA and ISRO?

(A) Brahmos - II

(B) Agni -II

(C) ** NISAR

(D) Atlas V

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISRO-NASA ‘NISAR’ satellite to be launched from India in September. NASA and ISRO, jointly developed an Earth-observation satellite, called NISAR (NASA-ISRO Synthetic Aperture Radar), got a send-off ceremony at the American space agency’s Jet Propulsion Laboratory .

Concept note-2: -SSLV-D2 lifted off from the first launch pad at Satish Dhawan Space Centre-SHAR, Sriharikota at 9:18 a.m. This is the first satellite launch in 2023. “The SSLV-D2-EOS-07 mission is successfully accomplished, ” ISRO mentioned on its Twitter handle, a few minutes after the vehicle took off.

Concept note-3: -On Sept. 30, 2014, NASA and ISRO signed a partnership to collaborate on and launch NISAR. The mission is targeted to launch in 2024. NASA is providing the mission’s L-band synthetic aperture radar, a high-rate communication subsystem for science data, GPS receivers, a solid-state recorder and payload data subsystem.

Concept note-4: -Conclusion. NISAR is a three-year mission that is expected to be launched in 2024 from Satish Dhawan Space Centre.

Concept note-5: -PSLV-C53/DS-EO Mission. ISRO undertakes PSLV-C53/DS-EO mission on June 30, 2022. The launch is scheduled at 18:00 hours IST from the Second Launch Pad at Satish Dhawan Space Centre, Sriharikota.
