What is the name of the satellite which is the third brightest object in the world?

(A) Cartosat

(B) Brahmos - II

(C) Agni - III

(D) ** Mayak satellite

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mayak satellite may be third brightest object in sky after Sun and Moon.

Concept note-2: -“Venus ranks as the third-brightest celestial object to light up the heavens, after the sun and moon, respectively, ” EarthSky reports.

Concept note-3: -4. Mars. The red planet comes at number four on this list. There are times however when Mars appears brighter than Jupiter.

Concept note-4: -The second brightest object is the planet Venus due to its highly reflective atmospheric content. The third brightest object in the night sky is the largest planet in the Solar System, Jupiter.
