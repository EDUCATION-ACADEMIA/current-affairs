[SOURCES]

What is the name of the scheme which was approved by the Union Cabinet recently?

(A) Atal Pension Yojana

(B) Jan Dhan Yojana

(C) ** Umbrella scheme of Modernisation of Police Forces

(D) Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The PM-DevINE Scheme will have an outlay of Rs. 6, 600 crore for the four year period from 2022-23 to 2025-26 (remaining years of 15th Finance Commission period).

Concept note-2: -The Ministry’s new scheme “Prime Minister’s Development initiative for the north eastern region-PM-Devine” (with an outlay of Rs. 6, 600 crore), has been approved earlier in october-2022 separately, under which larger and high impact proposals under infrastructure, social development and livelihood sectors are taken.

Concept note-3: -HRIDAY scheme was launched on 21st January 2015 for the development of heritage cities. This scheme aimed to preserve and revitalise heritage cities of India to reflect their unique character.

Concept note-4: -A new scheme, Prime Minister’s Development Initiative for North-East Region (PM-DevINE) was announced in the Union Budget 2022-23. An initial allocation of Rs 1, 500 crore was made along with an initial list of projects (Annexure-A).
