What is the name of the ship which is Circumnavigating the Globe on an Indian-Built SailBoat INSV Tarini by Women Naval Officers?

(A) ** Navika Sagar Parikrama

(B) USS Carl Vinson

(C) Surface Combalant

(D) USS Jimmy Carter

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Navika Sagar Parikrama is the name of expedition for circumnavigation the globe on INSV Tarini by Indian Navy’s Women Naval Officers.

Concept note-2: -INSV Tarini is the sister vessel of INSV Mhadei. The project is considered essential towards promoting Ocean Sailing activities in the Navy while depicting Government of India’s thrust for ‘Nari Shakti’.

Concept note-3: -Navika Sagar Parikrama ( lit. ‘Sailors’ Sea Circumambulation’) was a circumnavigation of the globe by female officers of the Indian Navy. The six-member all-woman team circumnavigated and managed the whole operation in their first-ever global journey, on INSV Tarini.

Concept note-4: -Dilip Donde became the first Indian to complete such a journey.

Concept note-5: -The expedition, known as the Navika Sagar Parikrama, was the first-ever Indian circumnavigation of the globe by an all-women crew.
