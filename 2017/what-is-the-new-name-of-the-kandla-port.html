What is the new name of the Kandla Port?

(A) ** Deendayal Port

(B) Chennai Port

(C) Kochi Port

(D) Kamaraj Port

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kandla Port was renamed as Deendayal Port in 2017 under the Indian Ports Act, 1908.

Concept note-2: -The Union Ministry of Shipping has renamed Kandla Port Trust as Deendayal Port Trust, by exercising powers conferred on it under Indian Ports Act, 1908. The name was changed as part of yearlong centenary celebrations of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay, who had stood for uplift of poor and weaker sections of society.

Concept note-3: -Deendayal Port Trust, Paradip Port Trust, Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust, Mumbai Port Trust, Visakhapatnam Port Trust, Chennai Port Trust, Kolkata Port Trust, New Mangaluru Port Trust, V. O. Chidambaranar Port Trust, Kamarajar Port Limited, Kochi Port Trust, Mormugao Port Trust.

Concept note-4: -Mumbai port is one of India’s tax-free port. Kandla Port Trust or Deendayal Port is seaport in Kutch District of Gujarat near the city of Gandhidham.

Concept note-5: -It is Located on Coromandel Coast about 24 km north of Chennai Port, Chennai. Deendayal (Kandla) Port handled the highest volume of traffic i.e. 84.91 Million tonnes (16.37% share).
