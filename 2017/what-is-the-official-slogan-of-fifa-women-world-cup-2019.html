What is the official Slogan of FIFA Women World Cup 2019?

(A) All in One Rhythm

(B) To A Greater Goal

(C) ** Dare To Shine

(D) The Beautiful Side Of 20Eleven

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BEAUTIFUL!! “Dare to shine” ==> such a beautiful slogan.

Concept note-2: -What is the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 slogan? ‘Now is All, ’ says the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 motto. It represents living your dream, realising your destiny, and owning the moment.

Concept note-3: -The official slogan is “All in One Rhythm” (Portuguese: “Juntos num só ritmo") which is a registered Trademark.

Concept note-4: -Allez, dis-moi. Come on, tell me. Interestingly, as simple and obvious as the slogan Allez les Bleus ! is, for years it was trademarked by an individual who earned hundreds of thousands of euros from it before the Comité national olympique et sportif français (CNOSF) reacquired the rights in December 2014.
