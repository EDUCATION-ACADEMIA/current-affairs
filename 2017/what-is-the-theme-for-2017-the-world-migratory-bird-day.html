What is the theme for 2017 the World Migratory Bird Day?

(A) ** Their Future is Our Future - A Healthy Planet for Migratory Birds and People

(B) Life is a silent and observant

(C) Life is a history and various issues being established.

(D) Liberation of Life

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With the 2017 theme “Their Future is our Future-A healthy planet for migratory birds and people", World Migratory Bird Day has thrown light on the topic of “Sustainable Development for Wildlife and People".

Concept note-2: -WMBD 2022 is addressing the growing issue of light pollution on these birds and actions on a global scale to help migrate these birds safely.

Concept note-3: -A key goal of WMBD 2022 is to raise awareness of the issue of light pollution and its negative impacts on migratory birds. The campaign will be used to highlight actions that can be taken by a wider range of actors including individuals to reduce light pollution.

Concept note-4: -Migratory birds are those that fly out to different parts of the world in the winter to escape the cold. The Siberian Crane and Flamingoes are examples of migratory birds that flock to India during the winter.

Concept note-5: -Birds mainly migrate for food and the formation of nests to grow their young ones for their proper feeding. Example: In the winter season Siberian cranes migrate from Siberia to India as they cannot survive in the extreme condition of winter in Siberia.
