What is the theme for 2017 World Meteorological Day

(A) Hotter, drier, wetter. Face the Future

(B) ** Understanding Clouds

(C) Climate Knowledge for Climate Action

(D) Weather and Climate: Engaging Youth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Understanding Clouds is the theme of World Meteorological Day 2017 to highlight the enormous importance of clouds for weather climate and water.

Concept note-2: -I am therefore very happy that the theme of World Meteorological Day 2022 is “Early Warning and Early Action.” It celebrates the great achievements of national meteorological and hydrological services in improved early warning systems.

Concept note-3: -What is the theme of World Meteorological Day 2023? The theme of World Meteorological Day 2023 is “The Future of Weather, Climate and Water across Generations”.

Concept note-4: -The day is celebrated to commemorate the coming into existence of the World Meteorological Organization on March 23, 1950. Since 1961, the day has been celebrated with a different theme each year. For 2022, the theme is “early warning and early action.”

Concept note-5: -World Meteorological Day takes place every year on 23 March and commemorates the creation of the World Meteorological Organization and the essential contribution of National Meteorological and Hydrological Services to the safety and wellbeing of society The theme for 2022 is “Early Warning and Early Action".
