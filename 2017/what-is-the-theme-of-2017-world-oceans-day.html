What is the theme of 2017 World Oceans Day?

(A) ** Our Oceans, Our Future.

(B) "Healthy oceans, healthy planet"

(C) Everywhere Water Present

(D) Raise the issue water

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for World Oceans Day 2017 is Our Oceans, Our Future with a conservation action focus on encouraging solutions to plastic pollution and preventing marine litter for a healthier ocean and a better future.

Concept note-2: -"Revitalization: collective action for the ocean” is the theme for World Oceans Day 2022, a year framed by the UN Decade of Ocean Science and the celebration of the United Nations Ocean Conference, two years after being cancelled because of the pandemic.

Concept note-3: -The theme for the UN’s observance of World Oceans Day 2015 was “Healthy Oceans, Healthy Planet.” The celebration of World Oceans Day 2015 coincided with the first day of the twenty-fifth meeting of the Meeting of States Parties to the United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea.

Concept note-4: -The theme for World Oceans Day 2023 is “Revitalization: Collective Action for the Ocean", and this year’s event will be hosted at the UN New York Headquarters, with the celebrations broadcasted online, meaning that no matter where you are in the world, you can join in.

Concept note-5: -World Oceans Day Themes 2012 theme-“Youth: The Next Wave for Change.” World Oceans Day 2013 theme-“Ocean and People.” 2014 theme-“Ocean Sustainability: Together We Have the Power to Protect the Ocean.”
