What is the theme of the year 2017 International Day for Biological Diversity (IDB)?

(A) ** Biodiversity and Sustainable Tourism

(B) Biodiversity and Climate Change

(C) Water and Biodiversity

(D) Invasive Alien Species

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2017 International Day for Biological Diversity (IDB) will be celebrated under the theme, ‘Biodiversity and Sustainable Tourism’.

Concept note-2: -The theme in 2022 is “Building a shared future for all life”.

Concept note-3: -The Secretariat of the Convention on Biological Diversity (CBD) is pleased to announce the Biodiversity Day 2022 slogan: “Building a shared future for all life”.

Concept note-4: -International Day for Biological Diversity-22 May In December 2000, the UN General Assembly adopted 22 May as IDB, to commemorate the adoption of the text of the Convention on 22 May 1992 by the Nairobi Final Act of the Conference for the Adoption of the Agreed Text of the Convention on Biological Diversity.

Concept note-5: -The United Nations declared 2010 the International Year of Biodiversity (IYB) to raise awareness about the crucial importance of biodiversity, to communicate the human costs of biodiversity loss, and to engage people, particularly youth, throughout the world in the fight to protect all life on Earth.
