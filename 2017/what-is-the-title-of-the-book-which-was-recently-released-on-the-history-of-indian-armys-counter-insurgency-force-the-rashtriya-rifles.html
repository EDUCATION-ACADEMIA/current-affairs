What is the title of the book which was recently released on the history of Indian Army’s Counter Insurgency Force, the Rashtriya Rifles?

(A) Brave of Indian Army

(B) India the Land of Brave

(C) India’s Brave Military

(D) ** Home of the Brave

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Rashtriya Rifles (RR; transl. National Rifles) is a counter-insurgency force in India, formed in 1990, to specifically serve in the Jammu and Kashmir region.

Concept note-2: -Rashtriya Rifles comprises 65 battalions. Known RR battalion affiliations include: 1 RR – Mahar Regiment. 2 RR – Sikh Light Infantry.

Concept note-3: -Home of the Brave tells the story of a very brave and optimistic eleven-year-old named Kek, who uses poetry to narrate his experience fleeing war-torn Sudan and settling into a very new and previously unimaginable life in the United States.
