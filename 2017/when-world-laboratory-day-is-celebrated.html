When World laboratory day is celebrated

(A) April 11th

(B) May 1st

(C) March 3

(D) ** April 24th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Two reasons to celebrate laboratories and the people who work there at 23 April, World Laboratory Day.

Concept note-2: -We celebrate World Laboratory Day every year on April 23 to honor the unique workspaces that provide controlled conditions and enable scientific research, experiments, and measurement.

Concept note-3: -We celebrate this day to cachet the unique workspaces that provide meticulous conditions and qualify scientific research, experiments and measurements. Medical laboratory professionals are vital members of the medical community. Their work assists doctors in diagnosing and treating illnesses.
