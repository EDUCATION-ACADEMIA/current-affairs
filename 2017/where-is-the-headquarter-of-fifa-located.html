Where is the headquarter of FIFA located?

(A) Zurich, Switzerland

(B) ** Dubai, UAE

(C) Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

(D) New York, USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The headquarters of FIFA (German: FIFA-Hauptquartier) is a distinctive complex in Zürich. The complex has served as the official headquarters of FIFA since its completion in 2006. It is located on the Zürichberg, a wooded hill in District 7.

Concept note-2: -The headquarters of FIFA (German: FIFA-Hauptquartier) is a distinctive complex in Zürich. The complex has served as the official headquarters of FIFA since its completion in 2006. It is located on the Zürichberg, a wooded hill in District 7.

Concept note-3: -FIFA was established in Paris in 1904 but moved to Zurich in 1932 because of Switzerland’s location in the center of Europe, its political neutrality and because “it was accessible by train, ” according to a timeline on FIFA’s website.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Zürich. The headquarters of the Federation Internationale de Football Association (FIFA) is located in Zurich, Switzerland. It was founded in Paris, France on 21 May 1904. It organizes various major football tournaments which include the FIFA World Cup.

Concept note-5: -FIFA, also known as FIFA Football and to be rebranded as EA Sports FC from 2023, is a series of association football video games developed and released annually by Electronic Arts under the EA Sports label. As of 2011, the FIFA franchise has been localised into 18 languages and available in 51 countries.
