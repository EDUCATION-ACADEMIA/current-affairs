Where the G7 summit held?

(A) ** Italy

(B) Switzerland

(C) Washington

(D) England

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 48th G7 summit was held from 26 to 28 June 2022 in Schloss Elmau, Krün, Bavarian Alps, Germany.

Concept note-2: -As the G7 Presidency in 2023, Japan has decided to host the G7 Trade Ministers’ Meeting in Osaka-Sakai, on 28 and 29 October 2023 after hosting the first online meeting on 4 April 2023. In October, the meeting and related events will be held in the host cities of Osaka and Sakai.

Concept note-3: -The role as host, also known as the G7 presidency, rotates annually among member countries in the following order: France, United States, United Kingdom, Germany, Japan, Italy and Canada.
