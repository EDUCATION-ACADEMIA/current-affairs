Which Airline wins “2018 Airline of the Year” award?

(A) ** Air New Zealand

(B) Qantas Airways

(C) Singapore Airlines

(D) Etihad Airways

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Singapore Airlines is honoured to be named ‘World’s Best Airline’ in the Skytrax awards, and I dedicate it to the 26, 000 SIA Group employees who focus every day on delivering the world’s best travel experience to our customers, ” said SIA CEO Mr Goh Choon Phong, who accepted the top award at a ceremony in London today.

Concept note-2: -Qatar Airways has been voted the World’s Best Airline for a record seventh time at the 2022 World Airline Awards, winning the prestigious Airline of the Year 2022 title.

Concept note-3: -1 airline of 2022: Qatar Airways. Qatar Airways ranked at the top of Skytrax’s list. The carrier’s chief executive, His Excellency Mr. Akbar Al Baker, stated in a press release that Qatar Airways’ goal when it was created in 1993 was always to be named the best airline.

Concept note-4: -Qatar Airways also took home three additional awards from Skytrax including World’s Best Business Class, World’s Best Business Class Lounge Dining, and Best Airline in the Middle East.

Concept note-5: -Akbar Al Baker, said: “To be named as the World’s Best Airline was always a goal when Qatar Airways was created, but to win it for the seventh time and pick up three additional awards is a testament to all the hard work of our incredible employees.
