Which app is launched by the Union Minister Piyush Goyal recently to monitor the coal dispatch?

(A) ** SEVA app

(B) My MSME app

(C) My BSNL app

(D) My UPI app

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Minister of State for Power, Coal, New and Renewable Energy and Mines Piyush Goyal recently launched the Saral Eindhan Vitaran Application (SEVA) aiming to facilitate the industry’s connection with the coal companies by proving information on coal dispatch.

Concept note-2: -UTTAM stands for – Unlocking Transparency by Third Party Assessment of Mined Coal (uttam.coalindia.in). The app is developed by the Ministry of Coal and Coal India Limited (CIL).

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution URJA MITRA is the name of the app launched by the Union Ministry of Power to provide real-time information sharing on the power supply.

Concept note-4: -UPSC Mains Q. Which Union Ministry launched ‘MyCGHS’ Mobile Application? Notes: Union Health Minister Mansukh Mandaviya launched the revamped ‘Central Government Health Scheme’ CGHS website and “MyCGHS” mobile application. The website shall benefit over 40 lakh beneficiaries with real-time information.

Concept note-5: -Minister of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution (2020 – present)
