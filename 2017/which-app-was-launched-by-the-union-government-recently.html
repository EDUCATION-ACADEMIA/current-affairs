Which app was launched by the Union Government recently?

(A) ** Mobile app “Celebrating Yoga”

(B) Swachh Bharat App

(C) BHIM app

(D) My BSNL App

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is To track and trace missing children. The Union Government has launched a mobile application Reunite for To track and trace missing children.

Concept note-2: -List of Government Mobile Apps: 1) Aarogya Setu App. 2) Aaykar Setu App. 3) BHIM UPI App.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bhasha Sangam.

Concept note-4: -MyGov is Government of India’s innovative citizen engagement platform for direct citizen participation in governance by providing an avenue for channelizing their ideas, comments and creative suggestions to Central Ministries and associated organizations.
