Which award did Sangram ISSF World Cup Final recently?

(A) ** Silver

(B) Gold

(C) Bronze

(D) Certificate

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISSF Rifle/Pistol World Championship 2022: India finish with 34 medals-list of all medallists. With 12 golds, nine silvers and 13 bronze, India finished second on the medals table behind China at the Cairo meet. Junior shooters won 25 of these medals.

Concept note-2: -In Shooting, two-time Olympian Mairaj Ahmad Khan created history by becoming the first Indian shooter to win the gold medal in the men’s skeet event at the ISSF World Cup in Changwon, South Korea, yesterday. Mairaj Ahmad Khan recorded 37 hits out of a possible 40 in the final to win gold.

Concept note-3: -Saurabh Choudhary won the first gold medal for India in the ISSF (International Shooting Sports Federation) Rifle/Pistol world cup in Cairo, Egypt on 1 March 2022. The 19-year-old Indian defeated Michael Schwald of Germany in the gold medal match of the Men’s 10m Air Pistol event.

Concept note-4: -Arjun Babuta, 23, won the men’s 10m air rifle gold medal at the ISSF World Cup in Changwon, South Korea, on Monday.It was the first senior international medal for Arjun.
