Which bank has can offer gold loans up to Rs 2 lakh by RBI

(A) Privat Bank

(B) NABARD

(C) Cooperative Bank

(D) ** Regional Rural Banks

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A circular issued by the RBI relaxed the lending norms for gold loans by increasing the LTV from 75% to 90%. The RBI guidelines for gold loans allow lenders, whether banks or NBFCs, to lend up to 90% of the gold jewellery value.

Concept note-2: -Loan financing limit 2 crore. Minimum limits to the gold loan amount usually depend on borrower requirements, with financing available up to as low as Rs. 5, 000.
