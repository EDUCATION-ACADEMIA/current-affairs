Which bank launches ‘YES GST’ Program to assist MSMEs recently?

(A) Dena Bank

(B) Canara bank

(C) ** Yes Bank

(D) Bank of Baroda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -YES BANK launches YES MSME initiative to enable access to funding.

Concept note-2: -YES Bank, on Monday, rolled out ‘YES MSME’, whereby the bank will support micro, small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) through solutions across lending, deposits, insurance, customised and segmented digital solutions for retail, manufacturing, wholesale, trade and service providers.

Concept note-3: -GST Number of YES BANK LIMITED is 27AAACY2068D1ZG in Maharashtra.

Concept note-4: -GST Number of YES BANK LIMITED is 24AAACY2068D1ZM in Gujarat.

Concept note-5: -YES BANK stands for Youth Enterprise Scheme Bank. The founder, CEO, and managing director of the Yes Bank is Rana Kapoor.
