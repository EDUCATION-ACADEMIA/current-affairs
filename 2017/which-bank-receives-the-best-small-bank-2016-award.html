Which bank receives the ’best small bank 2016’ award?

(A) Bandhan Bank

(B) Dhanlaxmi Bank

(C) City Union Bank

(D) ** Karur Vysya Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -South Indian Bank . South Indian Bank has won the ‘Best Bank Award for Digital Banking among Small Banks’ and ‘Best Bank Award for High Performance IT Ecosystem among Small Banks’ in the thirteenth edition of the IDRBT Banking Technology Excellence Awards 2016-17.

Concept note-2: -Tamilnad Mercantile Bank (TMB) has bagged ‘Best Small Bank Award’ in the ‘Best Banks Survey for the year 2022’ done by Business Today-KPMG (BT-KPMG Best banks survey). The bank has won this award under the category of banks with the book size of less than ₹1 lakh crore.

Concept note-3: -Capital Small Finance Bank (erstwhile Capital Local Area Bank) is the India’s first small finance bank founded in April 2016 as a microfinance lender, with its headquarters in Jalandhar, India.

Concept note-4: -Equitas Small Finance Bank The company has a market capitalization of ₹6205 cr and has made a profit of ₹281 cr in 2022.
