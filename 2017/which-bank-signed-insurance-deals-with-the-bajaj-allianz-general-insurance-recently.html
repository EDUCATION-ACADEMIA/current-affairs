Which bank signed Insurance Deals with the Bajaj Allianz General Insurance recently?

(A) ** Union Bank of India

(B) Dena Bank

(C) Canara Bank

(D) Karnataka Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) is offering the product in partnership with Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance Company Ltd. (BALIC).

Concept note-2: -Union Bank of India having the most diversified network of over 4200 branches has entered into Corporate Agency tie up with Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Co Ltd to offer its customers; quality general insurance products at competitive rates.

Concept note-3: -Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company Limited is a joint venture between Allianz SE, the world’s leading insurer, and Bajaj Finserv Limited. The Company received the certificate of registration from IRDA on 2nd May 2001 to conduct general insurance business in India.

Concept note-4: -HDFC Bank has finalised a new bancassurance partner in Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company, sources in the bank said.
