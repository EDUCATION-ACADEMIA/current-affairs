Which bank signs deal with Intellect Design for Digital Transaction Banking Platform?

(A) Bank of Baroda

(B) ** Bangkok Bank

(C) United Bank of Commerce

(D) Union Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A digital transaction involves the collaboration of several parties including large financial firms and a number of sectors within the economy. Examples include swiping a debit card at a store, paying for a purchase online, or transferring money from an app to your bank account.

Concept note-2: -#1 Nubank. Launched in 2013 as a direct reaction to the undeserving, over-charging incumbent banks of Brazil, Nubank was founded by David Velez, Cristina Junqueira and Edward Wible, having since grown to become the most successful digital bank to date.

Concept note-3: -A digital-only bank provides banking facilities exclusively through digital platforms, such as mobile, tablets, and the Internet. It offers basic services in the most simplified manner, with the help of electronic documentation, real-time data, and automated processes.
