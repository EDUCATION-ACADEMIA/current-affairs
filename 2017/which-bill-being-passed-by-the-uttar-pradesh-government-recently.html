Which bill being passed by the Uttar Pradesh Government recently?

(A) ** Good and Service tax bill(GST)

(B) The National Commission for Backward Classes (Repeal) Bill

(C) The Collection of Statistics (Amendment) Bill

(D) The Repealing and Amending Bill

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A new analysis from nonpartisan legislative staff shows that 94.48 percent of bills that passed the legislature in 2022 were bipartisan. Of the 308 total bills that passed both chambers in 2022, 291 had the ‘yes’ vote of at least one Republican senator or representative.

Concept note-2: -Abstract. Recently, the Government of Uttar Pradesh (UP) unveiled a draft bill of the UP Population Policy (2021-2030) on World Population Day to bring down the gross fertility rate and stabilization of the population.

Concept note-3: -A bill is proposed legislation under consideration by a legislature. A bill does not become law until it is passed by the legislature as well as, in most cases, approved by the executive. Once a bill has been enacted into law, it is called an act of the legislature, or a statute.

Concept note-4: -In passing an ordinary bill, a simple majority of members present and voting is necessary. But in the case of a bill to amend the Constitution, a majority of the total membership of the house and a majority of not less than two-thirds of the members present and voting is required in each house of Parliament.
