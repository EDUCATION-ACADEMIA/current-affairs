Which board banned bans Stapler Pins in tea bags from January 2018?

(A) National Dairy Development Board (NDDB)

(B) National Oilseeds and Vegetable Oils Development Board (NOVOD)

(C) ** Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI)

(D) National Horticulture Board (NHB)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) on Monday said it is banning the use of stapler pins in tea bags effective January 1, 2018.

Concept note-2: -FSSAI’s Ban The use of staple pins in tea bags is a hazard to consumers, since any loose staple pin consumed inadvertently with tea may cause serious health issues.

Concept note-3: -There is absolutely no evidence to suggest that exposing staples to microwaves will cause any toxic or harmful chemicals to be released. Microwaves will be reflected by the metal, not absorbed.
