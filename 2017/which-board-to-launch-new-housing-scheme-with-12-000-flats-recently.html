Which board to launch new housing scheme with 12, 000 flats recently?

(A) ** Delhi Development Authority (DDA)

(B) Tamil Nadu Housing board

(C) Kerala Public Service Commission

(D) Karnataka State Commission board

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As against the prior deadline of March 31, 2022, the urban scheme has now been extended till December 31, 2024, because only 61.77 lakh houses have been completed so far, as against the total of 12.26 million houses sanctioned under the scheme.

Concept note-2: -The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Gramin (PMAY-G) has been extended upto the year 2024.

Concept note-3: -Starting from September 12, 2022, the DDA Housing Scheme 2022 is an online running scheme for the Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) and Lower Income Group (LIG) categories. Under DDA Housing Scheme 2022, over 8, 500 flats are available for sale in the Narela Sub City of Delhi.

Concept note-4: -When to apply for PMAY? The PMAY scheme is being implemented in three phases, with the first two phases having ended. Currently, the last phase is on; it began on April 1, 2019, and will end on March 31, 2022.
