(A) ** Mary Kom

(B) Kavita Gupta

(C) Sarita Devi

(D) Rani Suresh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Asian Boxing Championships 2022: Lovlina, Parveen, Saweety and Alfiya strike gold.

Concept note-2: -This is Lovlina‘s first gold medal on the Asian stage.

Concept note-3: -Q. Mary Kom was the first Indian female boxer to win an Olympic medal.
