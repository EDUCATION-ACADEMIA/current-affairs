Which cabinet minister is Smt Maneka Gandhi

(A) Minister of urban development

(B) Minister of railways

(C) Minister of External affairs

(D) ** Minister of the Woman and Child Development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 2nd July 1977, the Regional Passport Office (New Delhi) ordered her to surrender her passport. The petitioner was also not given any reason for this arbitrary and unilateral decision of the External Affairs Ministry, citing public interest.

Concept note-2: -She founded People For Animals, which runs 36 free veterinary hospitals. She is a prolific author, and her “animal” radio and TV shows are famous. Previously when she led a different Ministry, she took action on wildlife, animal testing and zoos and set up the Animal Welfare Board of India.

Concept note-3: -Menaka (Sanskrit: , romanized: Menakā, lit. ‘incomparable’) is an Apsara (heavenly nymph) in Hindu literature.
