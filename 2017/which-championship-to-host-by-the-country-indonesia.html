Which championship to host by the Country Indonesia?

(A) ** AFC U-19 Championship

(B) All India Tennis Championship

(C) ICC Champions Trophy

(D) All India Chess Championship

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Esports Federation (IESF) has confirmed that Bali, Indonesia will be the host of the 2022 WE Championships Finals.

Concept note-2: -After the 2021 tournament was cancelled, it was decided on 24 December 2020 to award the 2023 hosting rights to Indonesia.

Concept note-3: -Sports in Indonesia are popular from both the participation and spectating aspect. Some popular sports in Indonesia are badminton, football, volleyball, basketball and the native Indonesian martial art pencak silat. Badminton is one of Indonesia’s most successful sport.

Concept note-4: -Home favourite Jonatan Christie claimed his first title in 10 months at the 2023 Indonesia Masters on Sunday (29 January). In an all-Indonesian final, he defeated Chico Aura Dwi Wardoyo 21-15, 21-13 in 44 minutes for his maiden BWF Badminton Tour title on home soil inside Jakarta’s Gelora Bung Karno Sports Palace.
