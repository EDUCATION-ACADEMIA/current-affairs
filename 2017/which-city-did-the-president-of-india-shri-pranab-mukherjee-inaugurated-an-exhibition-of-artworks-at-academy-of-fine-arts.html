Which city did the President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee inaugurated an exhibition of artworks at Academy of Fine Arts?

(A) ** Kolkata

(B) Chennai

(C) Mumbai

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pranab Mukherjee BR (11 December 1935 – 31 August 2020) was an Indian politician and statesman who served as the 13th president of India from 2012 until 2017.

Concept note-2: -Electoral votes. The election’s Returning Officer V. K. Agnihotri announced: “I declare that Shri Pranab Mukherjee has been duly elected to the office of President of India.” Mukherjee gained 373, 116 MP votes and 340, 647 MLA votes for a total of 713, 763 votes to win the election.

Concept note-3: -Mukherjee became President on 25 July 2012. He succeeded Pratibha Patil (served 2007–12), India’s first woman president.
