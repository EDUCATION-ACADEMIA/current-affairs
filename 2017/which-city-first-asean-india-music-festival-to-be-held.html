Which city First ASEAN India Music Festival to be held?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Chennai

(C) Kolkata

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Its first edition was held in 2017, and saw much of Delhi and around coming together in celebration. The music festival marks 30 years of India’s association with ASEAN and would be held on the grounds of Delhi’s famous Purana Quila on November 18, 19 & 20.

Concept note-2: -It is a three-day festival held at Purana Qila, Delhi. It is aimed to celebrate the musical diversity of Southeast Asia. 10 bands from ASEAN countries and five acts from India are participating in the festival.

Concept note-3: -The music festival will also take place in Shillong, Meghalaya, a gateway to ASEAN, to mark 2022 as the ASEAN-India Friendship Year and emphasise India’s Act East Policy.” Tags: ASEAN.

Concept note-4: -ASEAN India Music Festival 2022 is back in Delhi with a bang and is scheduled to be held from November 18 to 20 at Purana Quila, Delhi.

Concept note-5: -The up-gradation of the relationship into a Strategic Partnership in 2012 was a natural progression to the ground covered since India became a Sectoral Partner of the ASEAN in 1992, Dialogue Partner in 1996 and Summit Level Partner in 2002.
