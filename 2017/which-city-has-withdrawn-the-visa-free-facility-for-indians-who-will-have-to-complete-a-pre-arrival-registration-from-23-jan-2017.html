Which city has withdrawn the visa-free facility for Indians who will have to complete a pre-arrival registration from 23 Jan 2017.

(A) London

(B) New York

(C) Washington

(D) ** Hong Kong

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In a major setback to Indian travellers, Hong Kong has now withdrawn the visa-free facility for Indians. The Hong Kong Immigration Department announced, “The Pre-arrival Registration for Indian Nationals will be implemented on January 23, 2017.

Concept note-2: -The vital thing to note is that approximately 170 nationalities are visa exempt, and India is one of them. This means that no visa is required for Hong Kong as an Indian passport holder.

Concept note-3: -Serbia is the only European country that Indians can go to without a visa. There is no need to get a prior visa before entering Serbia as a tourist.
