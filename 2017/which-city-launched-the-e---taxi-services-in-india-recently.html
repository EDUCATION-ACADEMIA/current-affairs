Which city launched the e - Taxi services in India recently?

(A) ** Hyderabad

(B) Chennai

(C) Bihar

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The e-taxi is one of the ambitious projects of the Union Government and forms as a part of its United Nations Development Program (UNDP) millennium goals to tackle pollution. The project will be undertaken by Nagpur Municipal Corporation and is the first project of its kind in India.

Concept note-2: -Under the new initiative, the objective is to link the existing autorickshaw-taxi networks in Kerala with an aim to ensure safe and dispute-free travel for the public at affordable rates prevailing in the state.

Concept note-3: -The story so far: Kerala has soft launched ‘Kerala Savari’, the country’s first online taxi service owned by a State government, to ensure fair and decent service to passengers along with fair remuneration to auto-taxi workers.

Concept note-4: -KOCHI: Kochi-based GMT Technologies will launch ‘PikMe’, an online taxi and auto-booking app, on Tuesday.
