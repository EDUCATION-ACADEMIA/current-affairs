Which city the Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) shifted recently?

(A) ** Chennai, Tamil Nadu

(B) Bengaluru, Karnataka

(C) Raipur Chhattisgarh

(D) Patna, Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is New Delhi. The Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) is India’s largest Central Armed Police Force.

Concept note-2: -Jammu & Kashmir Zone J & K Zone, CRPF, Jammu raised on 22/09/2009 with operational jurisdiction of the Force deployed in four states, three UTs and NCT viz. Himachal Pradesh, Punjab, Rajasthan, Haryana, J&K, Ladakh, Chandigarh and Delhi.

Concept note-3: -CRPF Recruitment 2023-Important Dates The online computer-based examination for CRPF recruitment 2023 exam for Constable Ministerial and ASI posts is scheduled to be conducted from 22nd to 28th February 2023. Check the complete schedule for CRPF Recruitment 2023 here.

Concept note-4: -The Central Reserve Police Force came into existence as Crown Representative’s Police on 27th July 1939. It became the Central Reserve Police Force on enactment of the CRPF Act on 28th December 1949. It has completed 83 years of glorious history.
