Which city the India and ASEAN Youth Summit began recently?

(A) ** Bhopal

(B) Mumbai

(C) Chennai

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ASEAN – India commemorative summit is being held in Cambodia from 11th November to 13th November 2022.

Concept note-2: -They agreed that this affinity constituted a special asset for the further development of their relations. convened the First ASEAN-India Summit in Cambodia in accordance with the decision of the ASEAN Leaders at the 7th ASEAN Summit in Brunei Darussalam in November 2001.

Concept note-3: -With the theme “Strengthening ASEAN-India Partnership in the Indo-Pacific”, the summit aims to provide a platform for youth leaders of both India and ASEAN to engage with each other and to develop a resilient and symbiotic relationship between countries.

Concept note-4: -The up-gradation of the relationship into a Strategic Partnership in 2012 was a natural progression to the ground covered since India became a Sectoral Partner of the ASEAN in 1992, Dialogue Partner in 1996 and Summit Level Partner in 2002.
