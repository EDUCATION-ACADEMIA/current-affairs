Which city the Union Government launched Jiyo Parsi Publicity Phase-2 recently?

(A) Chennai

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Kolkata

(D) Jaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Parzor Foundation and Madison BMB with Bombay Parsi Punchayat, TISS, Mumbai and Federation of Zoroastrian Anjumans of India launch the Jiyo Parsi Campaign-Phase-II. The Hon’ble Minister for Minority Affairs Shri. Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi presided over a gathering of eminent personalities and released the campaign.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of Minority Affairs launched the ‘Jiyo Parsi’ scheme in November 2013 with the intention to balance the population of the Parsi community and increase the total fertility rate, for which a budget of Rs 4 to 5 crore is provided every year.

Concept note-3: -Jiyo Parsi is a Central sector scheme for Containing Population Decline of Parsis in India.
