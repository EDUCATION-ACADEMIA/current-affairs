Which city the Union Government to organise Global Ayurveda meet?

(A) ** Kochi

(B) Mumbai

(C) Chennai

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Where is the Global Ayurveda Festival always held? The Global Ayurveda Summit is held annually in Kerala.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The Fourth Global Ayurveda Festival (GAF) will be held at Kochi in Kerala from 16th to 20th May 2020. A curtain raiser event was organized in New Delhi on 5 March 2020 in this regard. The theme for the five-day event is Ayurveda Medical Tourism: Actualizing India’s credibility.
