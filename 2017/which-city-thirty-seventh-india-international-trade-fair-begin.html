Which city Thirty Seventh India International Trade Fair Begin?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Jaipur

(C) Raipur

(D) Jodhpur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains Notes: India Trade Promotion Organisation (ITPO) is set to conduct the 40th edition of India International Trade Fair (IITF) at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi from November 14 to 27.

Concept note-2: -Union Minister Piyush Goyal, Ministry of Commerce and Industry (MoCI), inaugurated the 41st edition of the India International Trade Fair (IITF) at Pragati Maidan in New Delhi, Delhi, on November 14, 2022. The theme of the IITF 2022 is “Vocal for Local, Local to Global” and was held from November 14 – 27, 2022.

Concept note-3: -Since its inception in 1980, the Trade Fair Delhi has become a major global event for global business communities, clients, and tourists. Every year, it attracts millions of visitors and thousands of exhibitors.

Concept note-4: -Among the exhibitions of 24 states, the pavilion of Bihar state won the gold medal.
