Which city to host 3-day Indian Mobile Congress meet in September?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Chennai

(C) Bengaluru

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India Mobile Congress 2017 was held from 27 September 2017 to 29 September 2017 at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -The theme of the IMC-2022, is ‘New Digital Universe’ and has the objectives of promoting local manufacturing, fostering international regional cooperation, inspiring inclusive & sustainable development, promoting entrepreneurship and innovation, driving foreign and local investments, amongst others.

Concept note-3: -MWC Barcelona (formerly but still commonly referred to as Mobile World Congress) is an annual trade show organised by GSMA, dedicated primarily to the mobile communications industry. The event is held in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain at the Fira de Barcelona Gran Via, usually in February or early-March.

Concept note-4: -Ramakrishna P-Chief Executive Officer-India Mobile Congress | LinkedIn.

Concept note-5: -Mobile World Congress (MWC) is an international industry event organised by the GSMA, a trade body for the technology communications and connectivity sector. MWC Barcelona is the flagship event, with the next taking place on 27 February – 2 March 2023, while MWC events also take place in Las Vegas, Shanghai and Africa.
