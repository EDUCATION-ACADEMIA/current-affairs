(C) ** Raw Sugar

(D) Onions

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What Is an Anti-Dumping Duty? An anti-dumping duty is a protectionist tariff that a domestic government imposes on foreign imports that it believes are priced below fair market value.

Concept note-2: -tariff, also called customs duty, tax levied upon goods as they cross national boundaries, usually by the government of the importing country. The words tariff, duty, and customs can be used interchangeably.
