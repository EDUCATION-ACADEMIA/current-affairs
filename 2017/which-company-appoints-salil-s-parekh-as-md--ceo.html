Which company Appoints Salil S Parekh as MD & CEO?

(A) ** Infosys

(B) Tech Mahindra

(C) Cognizant

(D) Mind Tree

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IT giant Infosys has announced that Salil Parekh has been reappointed as Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and Managing Director (MD) by the Board of Directors. His new term will be effective from July 1, 2022 to March 31, 2027. The reappointment is subject to approval by shareholders.

Concept note-2: -Parekh has more than thirty years of global experience in the IT services industry with a track record of driving digital transformation for enterprises.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Salil Parekh.

Concept note-4: -He was first appointed CEO on January 2, 2018.

Concept note-5: -Infosys said its chief executive Salil Parekh has been reappointed for a further term of five years, subject to shareholder approval. The 57-year old will extend his run as the longest serving non-founder CEO of India’s second-largest software exporter, which clocked $16.3 billion in revenue for financial year 2022.
