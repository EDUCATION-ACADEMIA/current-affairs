Which company Conferred with CII Industrial Innovation Award recently?

(A) ** L & T Technology Limited

(B) Ashok Leyland

(C) Tech Mahindra

(D) Ford Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kris Gopalakrishnan, Past President, CII and Chairman, CII Industrial Innovation Awards 2021 along with Dr S Chandrashekhar, Secretary-DST, Government of India presented the award to Tata Steel.

Concept note-2: -Top 3 Most Innovative Institute 2022 under the category of Innovation Quotient goes to IIT Roorkee, International Institute of Information Technology and Northern India Textile Research Association.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee has been awarded “The Most Innovative Research Institute of the Year” by the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII).

Concept note-4: -Gujar Mal Modi Award for Science & Technology This award is to acknowledge and reward excellence in research and development and outstanding contribution to Innovative Science & Technology in India. The award consists of a cash prize of Rs 2.01 Lakh, a scroll of honour and a silver shield.

Concept note-5: -The Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) works to create and sustain an environment conducive to the growth of industry in India, partnering industry and government alike through advisory and consultative processes.
