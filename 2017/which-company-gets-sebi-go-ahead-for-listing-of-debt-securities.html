Which company Gets SEBI go-ahead for Listing of Debt Securities?

(A) Tech Mahindra

(B) ** India INX

(C) Federal

(D) Ashok Leyland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All Government securities and Treasury bills are deemed to be listed automatically as and when they are issued. Other securities, issued publicly or placed privately, could be listed or admitted for trading, if eligible, as per rules of the Exchange by following prescribed procedure.

Concept note-2: -Listed Debt Securities means Debt Securities of the Company that have been admitted to trading on a Regulated Market.

Concept note-3: -Reasons for listing debt securities Listing debt securities on a stock exchange is usually carried out to gain access to a wider group of investors. The target investors for most types of debt securities are institutional or professional investors.
