Which company launches Android Oreo Go edition for low-end phones?

(A) ** Google

(B) Samsung

(C) Apple

(D) Yahoo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Galaxy J2 Core is the Samsung-made Android Go phone we recommend you get, with its biggest perk being a large amount of expandable storage. Similar to most Android Go devices, the Galaxy J2 Core only has 8GB of onboard storage.

Concept note-2: -For more information on the recent development additions, see Android 13 (Go edition): Easier to update and tailored just for you).

Concept note-3: -The truth is 1GB of RAM hasn’t been enough for even basic usage for a long time, and if a phone in 2022 came with 1GB of RAM, you should avoid it like the plague, even if it runs Android’s Go edition. It’s not a good experience.
