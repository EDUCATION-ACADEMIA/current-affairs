Which company offers 1, 30, 000 scholarships for developers and students for Skill India mission?

(A) ** Google

(B) Yahoo

(C) Snapchat

(D) Microsoft

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The JN Tata Endowment – Higher Education of Indians This government scholarship for students is applicable if you are seeking higher education in international universities across the world. The scholarship amount can range from INR 1, 00, 000 to INR 10, 00, 000, depending upon the eligibility criteria met by the student.

Concept note-2: -An amount of INR 50, 000 per annum is given to the scholarship winners for every year. Since its inception in the year 2014-15, thousands of girl students have been benefitted from the Pragati Scholarship scheme.

Concept note-3: -Pragati Scholarship Eligibility Criteria The family’s total income should not exceed more than six lakhs per annum. One girl in a family is allowed to apply for the scholarship. It can be extended up to two girls if the family income is less than Rs. 8 Lakhs per annum.
