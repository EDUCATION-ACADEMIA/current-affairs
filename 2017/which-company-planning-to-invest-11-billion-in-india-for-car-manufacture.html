Concept note-3: -1. Maruti Suzuki. Maruti Suzuki or Maruti Suzuki India Limited was founded back in 1981 and is the leader in manufacturing cars in India. In 2020, Maruti Suzuki had a market share of 54.16 percent, however, due to the pandemic, that market share dropped to 45.61 percent in March 2021.
