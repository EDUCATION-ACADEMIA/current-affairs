Which company received World’s Second Highest Green Rating?

(A) ** SIERRA ODC Coimbatore

(B) World Green Building council

(C) HongKong Green Building

(D) Green MaG

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 6 Star Rating represents ‘World Leadership’ in environmentally sustainable building practices.

Concept note-2: -1. Suzlon One Earth, Pune. This significantly unique office is designed by Christopher Charles, Pune based architect and has received LEED Platinum rating in 2010. To ignite our eyes, he and his partner came up with the concept “Office in the garden”.

Concept note-3: -CII-Sohrabji Godrej Green Business Centre, Hyderabad The center was the first building in India and the first outside the United States to acquire a LEED Platinum certification.

Concept note-4: -Project Info. SIERRA’s eFACiLiTY® building is rated the world’s 2nd and India’s highest ranking Green Building under LEED NC v2009 scoring about 103 points out of the possible 110. SIERRA’s eFACiLiTY® building is a model building portraying the green features and smart concepts.
