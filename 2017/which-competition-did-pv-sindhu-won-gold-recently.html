(A) ** Korea Open Superseries Competition

(D) World Senior Championships

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Former world champion PV Sindhu added one more gold medal to her cabinet by winning the women’s singles title at the Commonwealth Games 2022 in Birmingham, UK, on Monday (8 August).

Concept note-2: -The men’s singles was won by Arunachal’s Laa Tukum over Hemam Malemnganba of Manipur 21-18, 21-9. The women’s singles was an all-Assam affair, with Isharani Baruah defeating Disha Sarma 21-3, 21-11.

Concept note-3: -Commonwealth Games 2022 badminton: India’s Lakshya Sen clinches maiden gold. Lakshya Sen beat Ng Tze Yong 19-21, 21-9, 21-16 in the final to win his first Commonwealth Games gold medal. India’s Lakshya Sen won the men’s singles badminton gold medal at the Commonwealth Games 2022 in Birmingham, UK on Monday.
