(A) ** China

(B) Pakistan

(C) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The opening ceremony for the International Army Games 2022 (Army Games 2022) took place solemly at Patriot Park on the outskirts of Moscow, Russia on August 15. This year event drew the participation of 264 teams from 34 nations to compete in 34 events.

Concept note-2: - “Russia holds its first International Army Games". Washington Post. Archived from the original on 2018-08-02. Retrieved 2018-08-01.

Concept note-3: -The Military World Games is a multi-sport event for military sportspeople, organized by the International Military Sports Council (CISM). They have been held since 1995, although championships for separate sports had been held for some years.

Concept note-4: -The CISM Military World Summer Games are a multisports event organised every four years, one year before the year the Olympic Games are organised. It is an event that is based on the CISM and Olympic spirit, without political, religious and racial consideration nor discrimination.
