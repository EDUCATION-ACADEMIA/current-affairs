Which country athlete won the Delhi Half Marathon recently?

(A) ** Ethiopia

(B) Zimbabwe

(C) South Sudan

(D) Malawi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Avinash Sable eked out a narrow win in the Indian elite race at the Delhi Half Marathon 2022 on Sunday after edging out Kartik Kumar by a margin of 0.04 seconds.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi, October 16: Ethiopia’s Chala Regasa and Kenya’s Irine Cheptai took the honours at the Vedanta Delhi Half Marathon 2022, a World Athletics Elite Label Race, when they crossed the line in 60:30 and 66.42 respectively on Sunday.

Concept note-3: -Sanjivani Jadhav won the Indian women’s race. Long-distance runner Abhishek Pal bettered his personal best to win the Indian men’s elite race at the Kolkata 25K 2022 on Sunday.
