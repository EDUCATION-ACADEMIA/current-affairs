Which country became the first to partner with Facebook on disaster response?

(A) Singapore

(B) Japan

(C) ** India

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India became the first country to partner with Facebook on disaster management response. Facebook rolled out disaster maps for India in bid to help communities recover faster form a disaster.

Concept note-2: -Who released the first Disaster Management Plan of India? Explanation: Narendra Modi released the first Disaster Management Plan of India on 1 June 2016. This plan seeks to provide a framework and direction to government agencies for prevention, mitigation and management of disasters. 10.

Concept note-3: -In June 2016, India launched its first National Disaster Management Plan (NDMP) with the aim of improving the country’s resilience to disasters and reducing the loss of lives and assets.

Concept note-4: -On 1 June 2016, Narendra Modi, the Prime Minister of India, launched the Disaster Management Plan of India, which seeks to provide a frame work and direction to government agencies for prevention, mitigation and management of disasters.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Uttarakhand. The first state in the country to establish the Ministry of Disaster Management is Uttarakhand.
