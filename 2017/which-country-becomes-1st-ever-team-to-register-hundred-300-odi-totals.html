Which country Becomes 1st Ever Team to Register Hundred 300+ ODI Totals?

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) South Africa

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sunday, January 15, 2023. Thiruvananthapuram : Team India became the first-ever team in ODI cricket to register a win by 300 runs or more, becoming the team with the biggest victory margin in terms of runs in the history of 50-over format on Sunday.

Concept note-2: -England is the first team to score a 300+ score in a One-day International game. It was against India on June 7th at Lord’s, when the home team scored 334/4 in the first game of 1975 Prudential World Cup.

Concept note-3: -only rohit sharma can score 300 runs in odi, it has ability to achieve this target.
