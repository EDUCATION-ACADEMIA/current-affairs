Which country has introduced military draft for both men and women

(A) Russia

(B) Canada

(C) Switzerland

(D) ** Sweden

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Most countries that use conscription systems only conscript men; a few countries also conscript women. For example, Norway, Sweden, North Korea, Israel, and Eritrea conscript both men and women.

Concept note-2: -South Korea In this country, all able-bodied Korean males between the age of 18-28 are required to serve in the country’s military for about two years.

Concept note-3: -While women officers and enlisted personnel serve with distinction in the U.S. Armed Forces, women have never been subject to Selective Service registration or a military draft in America.

Concept note-4: -A draft is the mandatory enrollment of individuals into the armed forces. The United States military has been all-volunteer since 1973. But an act of Congress could still reinstate the draft in case of a national emergency.
