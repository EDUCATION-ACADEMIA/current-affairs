Which country has suspended the process of issuing visa for travellers from Pakistan, Iraq, Iran, Syria and Afghanistan

(A) Qatar

(B) Oman

(C) Singapore

(D) ** Kuwait

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Can I Obtain My Visa for Pakistan on Arrival? No. Pakistan does not offer a visa on arrival for US citizens, so you must obtain your visa for travel to Pakistan before you depart via the Embassy of Pakistan in Washington, DC.

Concept note-2: -The Government of India has decided to restore all valid regular paper /e-visa [including long term 10 years tourist regular paper visa and 5 years e-visa] irrespective of its issue date, for the nationals of USA since 16 March 2022.

Concept note-3: -UAE consul-general in Karachi calls rumours circulating online “fake news", confirms Pakistanis can apply for and are being granted UAE visa.

Concept note-4: -countries affected by the visa ban include Uganda, Ghana, Sierra Leone, Sudan, Cameroon, Nigeria, Liberia, Burundi, Republic of Guinea, Gambia, Togo, Democratic Republic of Congo, Senegal, Benin, Ivory Coast, Congo, Rwanda, Burkina Faso, Guinea Bissau, Comoros, and the Dominican Republic.
