Which country hosts FIFA World Cup 2018?

(A) Qatar

(B) Germany

(C) Mexico

(D) ** Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -France’s win was their second World Cup title, following their victory in 1998, which they hosted. Griezmann was named the man of the match, while Croatia’s Luka Modrić was awarded the Golden Ball as FIFA’s outstanding player of the tournament. The final was the highest-scoring World Cup final since 1966.

Concept note-2: -Seventeen countries have hosted the World Cup, most recently Qatar, who hosted the 2022 edition. The 2026 tournament will be jointly hosted by Canada, the United States and Mexico, which will give Mexico the distinction of being the first country to host games in three World Cups.

Concept note-3: -Brazil has won the most World Cups with five, and they have hosted the tournament twice.

Concept note-4: -It took place in Qatar from 20 November to 18 December 2022, making it the first World Cup held in the Arab world and Muslim world, and the second held entirely in Asia after the 2002 tournament in South Korea and Japan.
