Which country is 3rd Highest Number of Family-Owned Businesses?

(A) ** India

(B) China

(C) USA

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The report also states that India has 108 publicly-listed, family-owned businesses, making it the third-highest in the world behind China at 167 and the United States at 121 (as of 2015).

Concept note-2: -Only a third of family businesses in this study made it through the second generation, and only 13% made it through the third.

Concept note-3: -Family businesses in India represent 70 percent of publicly traded companies and 85 percent of private firms.
