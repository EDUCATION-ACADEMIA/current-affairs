Which country is second place in the LPG importer?

(A) India

(B) ** China

(C) Pakistan

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"Rapid increase in population combined with LPG penetration in rural areas has resulted in an average growth of 8.4 per cent in LPG consumption, making India the second largest consumer of LPG in the world at 22.5 million tonnes.

Concept note-2: -"With estimated imports of above 12 million metric tonnes in the financial year 2018-19, India stands as world’s second largest importer of LPG, after China.
