Which country is the focus nation in the 31st Surajkund International Crafts Mela

(A) ** Egypt

(B) Sri Lanka

(C) Lebanon

(D) Thailand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jammu and Kashmir is the theme State for it. The fair will showcase the richness and diversity of handicrafts, handloom, and the cultural heritage of India. More than 30 countries will participate in the Surajkund fair and the partner nation is Uzbekistan.

Concept note-2: -The Surajkund Mela attracts over a million tourists from India and throughout the world. This year’s theme state is the North Eastern area, and the partner country is the Shanghai Cooperation Organization.

Concept note-3: -Uzbekistan, the Partner Nation of the Mela. Over 100 crafts persons and many cultural troupes have come to participate in the mela and will bring the true color of Uzbekistan to the people of India.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Kyrgyzstan.
