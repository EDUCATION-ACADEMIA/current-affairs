Which country launched its first satellite recently

(A) Syria

(B) China

(C) Libya

(D) ** Mongolia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On October 4, 1957, the Soviet Union launched the earth’s first artificial satellite, Sputnik I.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is China. China successfully launched a new Earth observation satellite on 7 April 2022.

Concept note-3: -India’s new SSLV rocket successfully delivered three satellites to orbit on Thursday night (Feb. 9), bouncing back from an August 2022 failure.

Concept note-4: -A SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket launches with the Surface Water and Ocean Topography (SWOT) spacecraft onboard, Friday, Dec. 16, 2022, from Space Launch Complex 4E at Vandenberg Space Force Base in California.
