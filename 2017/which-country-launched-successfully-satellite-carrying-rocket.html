Which country launched successfully satellite-carrying rocket?

(A) ** Iran

(B) India

(C) USA

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Iran has announced the successful test flight of a rocket capable of propelling satellites into space, three months after launching a satellite with the help of Russia.

Concept note-2: -Launched on 15 February 2017 from the Satish Dhawan Space Centre at Sriharikota, Andhra Pradesh, the rocket successfully carried and deployed a record number of 104 satellites in sun-synchronous orbits in a single mission, breaking the earlier record of launching 37 satellites by a Russian Dnepr rocket on 19 June 2014.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is China. China successfully launched a new Earth observation satellite on 7 April 2022.

Concept note-4: -October, 1957: Soviets launch first artificial satellite into Earth orbit. Fifty years ago, on October 4, 1957, the Soviet Union launched Sputnik, the first man-made satellite, shocking the American public and beginning the Space Age.
