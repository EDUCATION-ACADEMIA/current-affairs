Which country newly elected President is Khaltmaa Battulga?

(A) Nigeria

(B) Australia

(C) Denmark

(D) ** Mongolia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He was the Democratic Party’s candidate in the 2017 presidential election and was elected President with 50.6% in the run-off, the first-ever run-off election in modern Mongolian history. Mongolians are sharply divided about his role in the 2019 Mongolian constitutional crisis.

Concept note-2: -Ukhnaagiin Khürelsükh (Mongolian: , romanized: Uhnágín Hürelszüh, [oˈnaɟiŋ ˈxuɾɘ̆ɬsux]; born 14 June 1968), also referred to as Khürelsükh Ukhnaa, is the 6th and current president of Mongolia, beginning his term on June 25, 2021 after winning the 2021 Mongolian presidential election.

Concept note-3: -Mongolian Prime Minister Luvsannamsrai Oyun-Erdene Meets with Wang Yi. On August 7, 2022 local time, Mongolian Prime Minister and Chairman of the Mongolian People’s Party Luvsannamsrai Oyun-Erdene met with State Councilor and Foreign Minister Wang Yi in Ulaanbaatar.

Concept note-4: -The incumbent prime minister is Luvsannamsrain Oyun-Erdene, who has served since 27 January 2021.
