Which country Overtakes US to Become Second Largest Smartphone Market: Report?

(A) ** India

(B) Iraq

(C) Bulgaria

(D) New York

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India–which saw a shipment of 40.4 million units during the third quarter–was second to China where 100.6 million smartphones were shipped, the data showed.

Concept note-2: -Apple market share for Q4 2022 was 23% followed by Samsung market share of 19%. The 2022 global smartphone shipments declined by 12% to 1.2 billion units, the lowest since 2013.

Concept note-3: -The country with the most smartphone users is China. More than 950 million people use smartphones in China. This means that the Chinese market is larger than that of India, the US, and Indonesia combined.

Concept note-4: -Samsung was named the world’s largest smartphone brand in 2022. Apple and Samsung took the first and second spots in the fourth quarter of 2022. Xiaomi, Vivo and OPPO took third, fourth, and fifth place, respectively.
