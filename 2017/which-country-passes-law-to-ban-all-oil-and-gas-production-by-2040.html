Which country Passes Law to Ban all Oil and Gas Production by 2040?

(A) ** France

(B) Australia

(C) Paris

(D) South Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -France has become the first European country to ban adverts for fossil fuels under a new climate law. Announced on 22 August, the legislation prohibits advertising for all energy products related to fossil fuels such as petrol products, energy from the combustion of coal mining and hydrogen-containing carbons.

Concept note-2: -France has recently enacted a law banning advertisements of petroleum energy products, energy from the combustion of coal mining, and hydrogen-containing carbons. In doing so, France became the first European country to enforce this unique pledge.

Concept note-3: -Crude Oil Production in France averaged 23.58 BBL/D/1K from 1994 until 2022, reaching an all time high of 58.00 BBL/D/1K in February of 1994 and a record low of 10.00 BBL/D/1K in April of 2020.
