Which country Prime minister won the confidence vote in Parliament?

(A) ** United Kingdom

(B) Japan

(C) Iran

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A vote of no confidence in the British Labour government of James Callaghan occurred on 28 March 1979. The vote was brought by Opposition leader Margaret Thatcher and was lost by the Labour Government by one vote (311 votes to 310), which was announced at 10:19 pm.

Concept note-2: -Israel. The constructive vote of no confidence has been in place since the direct election of the Prime Minister of Israel was abolished in 2001.
