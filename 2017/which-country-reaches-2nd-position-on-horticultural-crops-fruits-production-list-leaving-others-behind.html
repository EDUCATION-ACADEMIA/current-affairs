Which country reaches 2nd position on horticultural crops, fruits production list leaving others behind?

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) South Africa

(D) Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fresh Fruits & Vegetables. India’s diverse climate ensures the availability of all varieties of fresh fruits & vegetables. It ranks second in fruits and vegetable production in the world, after China.

Concept note-2: -Therein that year, China was the top producer with nearly 549 million metric tons, followed by India with approximately 128 million metric tonnes of fresh vegetables.

Concept note-3: -This statistic shows the world’s leading 10 producers of fresh vegetables in 2021. In that year, China was the leading producer with a production volume of over 600 million metric tons, followed by India with approximately 138 million metric tons of fresh vegetables.

Concept note-4: -(2ndAdv. Est.) Total Horticulture production in 2021-22 is estimated to be 341.63 Million Tonnes, an increase of about 7.03 Million Tonne (increase of 2.10%) over 2020-21 (Final).

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is 2. It ranks second in fruits and vegetable production in the world, after China.
