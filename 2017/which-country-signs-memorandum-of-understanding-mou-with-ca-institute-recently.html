Which country signs Memorandum of understanding (MoU) with CA Institute recently?

(A) ** Nepal

(B) Australia

(C) China

(D) Netherlands

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Share this page: The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) signed the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Kuwait Accountants and Auditors Association (KAAA) in Kuwait on November 21, 2019.

Concept note-2: -CPA Australia has a Mutual Recognition Agreement (MRA) with ICAI.

Concept note-3: -ICAI does not have a Mutual Recognition Agreement with the JICPA (Japanese Institute of Certified Public Accountants). The Japan CPA exams are all in Japanese.

Concept note-4: -The MoU applies to members in good standing who have gained membership in ICAI or one of the Canadian Provincial CPA bodies by meeting the education, experience and practical experience requirements of the ICAI or Canadian Provincial CPA bodies.
