Which country Slips to 8th Rank in Valuable Nation Brand List?

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Nepal

(D) Bhutan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United States was the world’s most valuable nation brand by a distance in 2022, with a value of 26.47 trillion U.S. dollars.

Concept note-2: -India is world’s 7th most valuable nation brand The paradox of India’s performance globally relative to the size of its population, economy and its rich heritage continues, and has been accentuated during the pandemic.

Concept note-3: -Tata Consultancy Services was the most valued brand across India in 2022. This means it had the highest reputation as a company, and was valued at over 45 billion U.S. dollars that year. HDFC followed the private financial institution with over 32 billion dollars-worth in brand value.

Concept note-4: -Brand India has shown its strength in Brand Finance’s Global Soft Power Index 2022 study retaining its 7th rank.
