Which country the 22nd World Petroleum Congress (WPC) International Conference held recently?

(A) Paris, France

(B) Bern, Switzerland

(C) ** Istanbul, Turkey

(D) Tripoli, Libya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 23rd World Petroleum Congress was held in Houston, Texas, marking the first time since 1987 that the summit took place in the US. More than 5, 000 attendees from over 70 countries were present at the summit, while more than 75 top energy companies were showcased on the exhibition floor.

Concept note-2: -Where is World Petroleum located? World Petroleum’s headquarters are located at PO Box 291197, Fort Lauderdale, Florida, 33329, United States What is World Petroleum’s phone number? World Petroleum’s phone number is (954) 327-0724 What is World Petroleum’s official website?

Concept note-3: -Every three years, the World Petroleum Council organizes the World Petroleum Congress as the principal meeting place for a global discussion of oil and gas issues.

Concept note-4: -World Petroleum Council (WPC) was established in 1933 to promote the management of the world’s petroleum resources for the benefit of mankind. Today, it is the premier global oil and gas forum and the only international organization to represent all aspects of the petroleum sector.
