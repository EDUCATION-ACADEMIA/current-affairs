Which country the Two-day Indian Ocean Conference to be held?

(A) China

(B) ** Sri Lanka

(C) Pakistan

(D) North Korea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -External Affairs Minister Dr S Jaishankar addressed the fifth Indian Ocean Conference in Abu Dhabi on 4 December 2021. The theme of the Indian Ocean Conference 2021 is “Indian Ocean: Ecology, Economy, Epidemic".

Concept note-2: -Q1# Which city hosted the Indian Ocean Conference 2021? Indian Ocean Conference 2021 was hosted in Abu Dhabi.

Concept note-3: -Dr Jitendra Singh will deliver the Keynote address at the UN Conference on the theme “Scaling up Ocean Action based on Science and Innovation for the implementation of Goal 14: Stocktaking, Partnerships and Solutions”.

Concept note-4: -Foreign Secretary Mr. Bharat Raj Paudyal addressed the 5th Indian Ocean Conference held in Abu Dhabi, the United Arab Emirates, today.

Concept note-5: -For more than 30 years, France has been a member of the Indian Ocean Commission (IOC), alongside the Comoros, Madagascar, Mauritius and Seychelles. The IOC is an international organization with a Francophone, island identity. Its five Member States share the same ambition to galvanize the organization.
