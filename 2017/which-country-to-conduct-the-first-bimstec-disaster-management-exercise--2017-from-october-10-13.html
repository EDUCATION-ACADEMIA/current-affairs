Which country to conduct the First ’BIMSTEC Disaster Management Exercise- 2017’ from October 10-13?

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) England

(D) New Zealand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India to conduct the First ‘BIMSTEC Disaster Management Exercise-2017’ from October 10-13.

Concept note-2: -BIMSTEC Disaster Management Exercise DMEx-2017 The National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) is the primary agency for the exercise, which took place in Delhi and the National Capital Region (NCR) from 10 to 13 October 2017.

Concept note-3: -The 1st BIMSTEC, Disaster Management Exercise was carried out in Nepal in 2017.

Concept note-4: -The National Disaster Management Plan (NDMP) was first released in 2016. The primary aim of the plan is to make India disaster resilient and drastically lessen the damage caused during and in the aftermath of disasters, natural and man-made. The plan was updated in 2018.

Concept note-5: -Correct answer is Home Ministry. NDRF stands for the abbrevation of National Disaster Response Force. It comes under the Home Ministry which was estabilished in the year 2006.
