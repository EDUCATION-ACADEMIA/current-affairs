Which country to Help Mauritius on Digital Locker?

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Afghanistan

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government of India will assist Mauritius to develop and set up digital locker services, a top official said on Thursday.

Concept note-2: -DigiLocker is a flagship initiative of Ministry of Electronics & IT (MeitY) under Digital India programme.

Concept note-3: -DigiLocker ties into Digital India’s vision areas of providing citizens a secure document access platform on a public cloud. Targeted at the idea of paperless governance, DigiLocker is a platform for issuance and verification of documents & certificates in a digital way, thus eliminating the use of physical documents.

Concept note-4: -DigiLocker is a secure cloud based platform for storage, sharing and verification of documents & certificates.

Concept note-5: -The beta version of the service was rolled out in February 2015, and launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on 1 July 2015.
