Which country to hold Major Tri-Service ’Indra’ Combat Exercise in October with India?

(A) ** Russia

(B) France

(C) Iran

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Russia and India are co-hosting a large-scale Army Exercise named INDRA – 2021. The military exercise INDRA is a joint Exercise between the Russian Army and the Indian Army.

Concept note-2: -The Royal Air Force of the United Kingdom and the Indian Air Force conduct a joint air force exercise known as Exercise Indradhanush. The exercise aims to improve mutual operational understanding between the two air forces through close interaction. The exercise began in the year 2006 and has since had five editions.

Concept note-3: -Exercise Yudh Abhyas is conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, Tactics, Techniques and Procedures between the Armies of the two nations.

Concept note-4: -INDO OMAN JOINT MILITARY EXERCISE AL NAJAF IV CONCLUDES This platoon level 13 days exercise had commenced on 01 August 2022. The aim of the exercise was to achieve inter-operability and to acquaint each other with operational procedures and combat drills in a Counter Terrorism environment under United Nations mandate.

Concept note-5: -The exercise ‘Veer Guardian-2023’ involving the Indian Air Force (IAF) and Japan Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) will be held at Japan’s Hyakuri air base. The IAF said on Saturday that its deployment at the exercise will include four Su-30 MKI jets, two C-17 aircraft and one IL-78 plane.
