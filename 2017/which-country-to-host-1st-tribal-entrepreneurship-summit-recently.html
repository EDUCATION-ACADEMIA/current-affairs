Which country to host 1st Tribal Entrepreneurship Summit recently?

(A) ** India

(B) China

(C) Japan

(D) Nepal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first Tribal Entrepreneurship Summit was held at Dantewada district of Chhattisgarh in November 2017. It was organized by NITI Aayog in partnership with Government of United States of America.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains Notes: The United States is set to host the first tribal nations’ summit since 2016. President Joe Biden will announce steps to improve public safety and justice for Native Americans during the summit. Leaders from more than 570 tribes in the United States are expected to participate in the two-day event.

Concept note-3: -It means that tribal citizens themselves, instead of assuming that economic development is the government’s job, see entrepreneurship as a way of rebuilding communities and families and participating in restoring the nation’s economy.
