Which country to host BRICS film festival in June

(A) India

(B) Iran

(C) ** China

(D) Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bangladesh will be this edition’s Country in Focus. The Country in Focus is a special segment that recognizes the cinematic excellence and contributions of the country.

Concept note-2: -Hosted by China Film Administration and undertaken by the Shanghai International Film and TV Festival Center, the 2022 BRICS Film Festival was held several days ago. This year, the festival is composed of three programs, that is film competition, themed forums and film screenings.

Concept note-3: -KOLKATA: ‘Aparajito’, the Anik Dutta film that is inspired by the story behind the making of Satyajit Ray’s ‘Pather Panchali’, has won the special jury award at the 2022 BRICS Film Festival.

Concept note-4: -The 6th edition of the BRICS Film Festival was held alongside 52nd India International Film Festival (IFFI) from 20-28 November at Goa.
