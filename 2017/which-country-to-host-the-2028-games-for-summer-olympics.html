Which country to host the 2028 games for Summer Olympics?

(A) ** Los Angeles

(B) United States of America

(C) South Africa

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which country will host the 2028 Summer Olympic Games? Notes: The United States of America will host the 2028 Summer Olympic Games.

Concept note-2: -Official websites of Paris 2024, Milano Cortina 2026, Los Angeles 2028 et Brisbane 2032.

Concept note-3: -Four cities have been chosen by the IOC to host upcoming Olympic Games: Paris for the 2024 Summer Olympics, Milan–Cortina d’Ampezzo for the 2026 Winter Olympics, Los Angeles for the 2028 Summer Olympics, and Brisbane for the 2032 Summer Olympics.
