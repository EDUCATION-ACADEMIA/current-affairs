Which country won Azlan Shah Cup 2017

(A) Australia

(B) Canada

(C) Newwyork

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Great Britain enthralled fans to defeat world champions Australia 4-3 in the final of the 26th Sultan Azlan Shah Cup to lift the title for the first time, becoming only the second British team to win the competition following England’s triumph way back in 1994.

Concept note-2: -Great Britain defeated Australia 4–3 in the final to win the cup.

Concept note-3: -The 2016 Sultan Azlan Shah Cup was the 25th edition of the Sultan Azlan Shah Cup. It was held in Ipoh, Perak, Malaysia from 6–16 April 2016. Australia won the title for the ninth time after defeating India 4–0 in final.

Concept note-4: -Australia emerged as Champions of the 2017 Azlan Shah Cup by defeating England 2-1 in the final. It was the 10th title for the Aussies who have made 15 finals since the inaugural tournament in 1983. England end the campaign with Silver and Argentina win Bronze.
