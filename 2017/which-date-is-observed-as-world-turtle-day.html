Which date is observed as World Turtle day?

(A) May 10

(B) ** May 23

(C) April 26

(D) June 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is 23 May. World Turtle Day is an annual event that has been taking place since 2000 on 23 May. It was founded by American Tortoise Rescue. The purpose of World Turtle Day is to educate people about the things that they can do to protect the habitats of turtles and tortoises.

Concept note-2: -Turtles and tortoises are common sightings in parables, stories, and pop culture and we celebrate World Turtle Day on May 23 to honor these resilient animals. Turtles and tortoises are associated with wisdom and perseverance. They are reptiles that are found in many environments all around the world.

Concept note-3: -World Turtle Day is observed on May 23 every year by American Tortoise Rescue, a nonprofit organization. The day is celebrated to raise awareness among people to protect turtles and tortoises and their disappearing habitats around the world.

Concept note-4: -World Turtle Day 2022: This year’s theme for World Turtle Day is “Shellebrate". The theme asks “everyone to Love and Save Turtles". World Turtle Day 2022: World Turtle Day is celebrated on May 23. Tortoises and turtles play an important role in the ecological design of Earth.

Concept note-5: -American Tortoise Rescue, a nonprofit organization established in 1990 for the protection of all species of tortoise and turtle, is sponsoring our annual World Turtle Day® on May 23.
