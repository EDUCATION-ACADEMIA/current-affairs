Which day is celebrated across the country on September 14 every year?

(A) ** Hindi Day

(B) National Youth Day

(C) National Handlooms Day

(D) National Teachers Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hindi Day (Hindi: , romanized: hindī divas) is celebrated in India to commemorate the date 14 September 1949 on which a compromise was reached-during the drafting of the Constitution of India-on the languages that were to have official status in the Republic of India.

Concept note-2: -Hindi Diwas September 14, 2022: As everyone knows Hindi is our mother tongue, and every year India celebrates Hindi Diwas on September 14. This day shows the importance of the Hindi Language and inspires younger generations to adopt the language more and more.

Concept note-3: -14th September: Hindi Diwas Hindi Diwas is marked on 14th September in India and is one of the most special days in September. In 1949, during this day, the Indian Constitution community assumed Hindi as the Indian authorized or official language.

Concept note-4: -It was on September 14, 1950 when Hindi became the official language of India. “The official language of the Union shall be Hindi in the Devanagari script, ” according to Article 343 of the Constitution. Since then, September 14 is observed as Hindi Diwas in India.

Concept note-5: -World Hindi Day or Vishwa Hindi Diwas is observed on January 10. The day is observed to promote the language. January 10 also marks the anniversary of the language being spoken for the first time in the United Nations General Assembly. Vishwa Hindi Diwas is different from Hindi Diwas, which is observed on September 14.
