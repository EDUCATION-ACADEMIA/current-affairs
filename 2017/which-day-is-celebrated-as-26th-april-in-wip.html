Which day is celebrated as 26th April in WIP?

(A) ** World Intellectual Property Day

(B) World Health Day

(C) World Peace Day

(D) World Earth Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) “Why celebrate World Intellectual Property Day ?”, “What kind of events can I organize?” are examples of frequent questions that are asked about World Intellectual Property Day.

Concept note-2: -National days on Wed Apr 26th, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s World Stationery Day, International Guide Dog Day, Get Organized Day, National Help A Horse Day, Alien Day… and much more!

Concept note-3: -The theme of World Intellectual Property Day 2022 is ‘IP and Youth Innovating for a Better Future’. Events organized to mark the Day seek to inform about the role of intellectual property rights in encouraging innovation and creativity.

Concept note-4: -April 26 was chosen as the date for World Intellectual Property Day because it coincides with the date on which the Convention Establishing the World Intellectual Property Organization entered into force in 1970. World Intellectual Property Day is WIPO’s largest intellectual property (IP) public outreach campaign.

Concept note-5: -WIPO was formally created by the Convention Establishing the World Intellectual Property Organization, which entered into force on 26 April 1970.
