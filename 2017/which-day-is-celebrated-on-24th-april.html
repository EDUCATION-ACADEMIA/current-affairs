Which day is celebrated on 24th April?

(A) ** National Panchayati Raj Diwas

(B) National Service day

(C) National Sacrifice day.

(D) National Green day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Panchayati Raj Day is celebrated every year on 24 April in India. On this day Constitution came into force with effect on 24 April 1993. In 2010 the first National Panchayati Raj Day was celebrated.

Concept note-2: -1970 – The Gambia becomes a republic within the Commonwealth of Nations, with Dawda Jawara as its first President. 1980 – Eight U.S. servicemen die in Operation Eagle Claw as they attempt to end the Iran hostage crisis. 1990 – STS-31: The Hubble Space Telescope is launched from the Space Shuttle Discovery.

Concept note-3: -24 April: National Panchayati Raj Day Panchayati Raj came into existence after the passing of the 73rd Constitution Amendment Act, 1992 which came into force with effect on 24 April 1993.
