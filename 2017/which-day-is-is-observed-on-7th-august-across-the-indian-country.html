Which day is is observed on 7th August across the Indian Country?

(A) National Health Day

(B) International Youth Day

(C) ** National Handloom Day

(D) World Youth Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Swadeshi Movement which was launched on 7th August, 1905 had encouraged indigenous industries and in particular handloom weavers. In 2015, the Government of India decided to designate the 7th August every year, as the National Handloom Day.

Concept note-2: -National Handloom Day (7th August) National Handloom Day is one of the special days in August. It celebrates the Swadeshi Movement, which began in Bengal, to emphasise using Indian-made goods.

Concept note-4: -The National Handloom Day is being observed every year on 7 August to honour the handloom weavers in the country.
