Which day is observed as 21st October?

(A) ** National Police Commemoration Day

(B) National Teacher Day

(C) National Health Day

(D) National History Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National days on Sat Oct 21st, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s International Sloth Day, National Sweetest Day, International Day of the Nacho, National Apple Day, Count Your Buttons Day… and much more!

Concept note-2: -To honour the ever lasting memory of these gallant Policemen, who have made the supreme sacrifice in the line of duty, October 21 every year is observed as ‘Commemoration Day’ by the Police forces all over India.

Concept note-3: -Police Commemoration Day in India is celebrated on October 21. It honours the memory of Indian police personnel killed by Chinese soldiers in 1959. The Remembrance Day was officially instituted in 1960 and has been observed at the national level since 2012.

Concept note-4: -October 21, 2022 is Vaishnava Rama Ekadashi, Rama Ekadashi and Govatsa Dwadashi.

Concept note-5: -On this day in 1520, explorer Ferdinand Magellan and three Spanish ships entered the strait later named for him, sailing between the mainland tip of South America and the island of Tierra del Fuego toward the Pacific Ocean.
