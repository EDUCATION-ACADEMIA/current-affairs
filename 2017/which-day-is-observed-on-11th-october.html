Which day is observed on 11th October?

(A) ** International Day of the Girl Child

(B) International Day for Women

(C) World Heart Day

(D) World Peace Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On December 19, 2011, United Nations General Assembly adopted Resolution 66/170 to declare October 11 as the International Day of the Girl Child, to recognize girls’ rights and the unique challenges girls face around the world.

Concept note-2: -"International Day of the Girl Child October 11".

Concept note-3: -International Day of the Girl Child is observed on 11 October to raise voices for girls and stand up for their rights. International Day for Natural Disaster Reduction is observed annually on 13 October to raise awareness about the risk of disaster reduction.

Concept note-4: -The subject or theme of the International Day of the Girl Child for 2022 is “Our time is now-our rights, our future.” According to the website of the UN, female children constantly face enormous problems in terms of their physical health, education, and mental wellness.

Concept note-5: -National Girl Child Day 2023: It is observed on 24 January to spread awareness about the inequalities faced by girls in Indian society, and also to promote awareness about the rights of the girl child and the importance of female education, health, and nutrition.
