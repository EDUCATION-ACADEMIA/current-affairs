Which day is observed on August 13th across the world?

(A) World Heart Day

(B) World Earth Day

(C) ** World Organ donation day

(D) World Youth Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Organ Donation Day is observed on 13 August to spread awareness about the importance of organ donation.

Concept note-2: -National days on Sun Aug 13th, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s National Prosecco Day, International Lefthanders Day, National Filet Mignon Day, Afternoon Tea Week… and much more!

Concept note-3: -On August 13, we celebrate World Organ Donation Day to raise awareness of the value of organ donation. Every year on August 14, Youm-e-Azadi, or Pakistan Independence Day, is observed.

Concept note-4: -13 August in Indian and World History is celebrated, observed, and remembered for various reasons. 13 August is the birth anniversary of Ramesh Chandra Dutt, Gangaprasad Verma, Vyjayanthimala, and Sridevi. 13 August is also observed as the death anniversary of Maharani Ahilyabai Holkar, and Bhikaji Rustom Cama.
