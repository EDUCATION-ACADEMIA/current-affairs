Which day is observed on May 23 across the world?

(A) World Health Day

(B) World Environment day

(C) World Earth Day

(D) ** World Turtle Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This day honors two animals associated with wisdom and perseverance-turtles and tortoises.

Concept note-2: -May 23 is the United Nations’ (UN) International Day to End Obstetric Fistula, which promotes action towards treating and preventing obstetric fistula, a condition that affects many girls and women in developing countries.

Concept note-3: -1929 Australia began a new gold rush in the Southwest part of the continent. 1934 Bank Robbers Bonnie Parker and Clyde Barrow are killed by federal agents. 1945 Nazi Heinrich Himmler committed suicide after being taken into Allied custody.

Concept note-4: -2022 Daily Holidays that fall on May 23, include: International Day to End Obstetric Fistula. International Turtle Day. Lucky Penny Day. National Taffy Day.
