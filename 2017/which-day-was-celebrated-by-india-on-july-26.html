Which day was celebrated by India on July 26?

(A) ** Kargil Vijay Diwas day

(B) National Voters Day

(C) National Youth day

(D) National Science Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India observes Kargil Vijay Diwas on 26th July every year to mark the anniversary of the day we achieved victory over Pakistani infiltrators in the 1999 Kargil War.

Concept note-2: -The Kargil war was ended on 26 July which continued approx 60 days. This day is celebrated to honor the Kargil War Heroes. National Refreshment Day is observed annually on the fourth Thursday in July which falls on 28 July in 2022.

Concept note-3: -2022 Daily Holidays that fall on July 26, include: Americans With Disabilities Day. Armed Forces Unification Day.

Concept note-4: -26 July Special Day in India is celebrated as Kargil Vijay Diwas every year to mark the successful victory of Operation Vijay which lasted for 3 months along the Line of Control. Nearly, 500 Indian army officials, soldiers & Jawans were martyred during the battle.
