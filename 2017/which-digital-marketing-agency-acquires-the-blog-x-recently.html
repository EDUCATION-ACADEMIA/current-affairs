Which digital marketing agency acquires the Blog X recently?

(A) E advisors India Pvt Limited

(B) Windchimes Communication

(C) ** Techmagnate

(D) Blue digital

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Techmagnate, India’s leading Digital Marketing Agency acquired BlogX, India’s largest blog conference where bloggers and content managers connect.

Concept note-2: -The “father of digital marketing, ” Philip Kotler, is often referred to as such. He is an American professor who is credited with founding marketing as an academic discipline and has produced over 60 marketing books.

Concept note-3: -Blog Marketing is a content marketing strategy. The marketing strategy uses blogs to address potential customers directly and individually, thus achieving customer loyalty for their own brand. This type of marketing uses viral marketing methods in which an interest group is built based on a blog.
