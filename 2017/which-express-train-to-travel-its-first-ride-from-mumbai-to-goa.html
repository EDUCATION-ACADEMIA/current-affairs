Which express train to travel its first ride from Mumbai to Goa?

(A) Mumbai Express

(B) ** Tejas Express

(C) Bhopal Shatabdi Express

(D) Rajdhani Express

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first train from Mumbai to Goa is 01471 Pune Dnr Spl. This train takes about 11hr 55min to reach Goa. It leaves Mumbai CSTM at 00:20:00 and reaches Goa MAO at 12:15:00 and operates on Tues.

Concept note-2: -To enjoy the most luxurious train journey between Mumbai and Goa, you have to take a ride on the ‘Mumbai CST-Karmali Tejas Express’.

Concept note-3: -The Konkan belt offers a stunning view of waterfalls, rivers, valleys, tunnels, lush green fields and creeks. The Jan Shatabdi Express on the Mumbai-Madgaon route presently has 1 Vistadome Coach, 3 AC Chair Car, 10 Second Class Jan Shatabdi seating coaches, 1 SLR Coach & 1 Generator Car.

Concept note-4: -Everything About Tejas Express Mumbai to Goa The Tejas Express starts its journey at 5:50 AM and reaches its destination by 2:00 PM on the same day. It is to be noted that Tejas Express for this route runs on all the days of the week except for Mondays and Thursdays.
