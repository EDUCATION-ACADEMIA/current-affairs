Which film is awarded as the Best film in International Indian Film Academy Awards 2017?

(A) ** Neerja

(B) Talvar

(C) Tanu Weds Manu

(D) Returns Mary Kom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Neerja . Neerja film won the Best Film award at the IIFA Awards ceremony in 2017.

Concept note-2: -Seeta was a 1933 Indian talkie Bengali film, directed by Debaki Bose and produced by the East India Film Company. It won an honorary diploma in the 2nd Venice International Film Festival in 1934, becoming the first Indian talkie to be shown at an international film festival.

Concept note-3: -Filmfare: This award honors artistic and technical excellence in the Hindi-language film industry of India. IIFA (International Indian Film Academy Awards): This awards presented to honor both artistic and technical excellence of professionals in Indian cinema, (includes all Indian language film industry).

Concept note-4: -The International Indian Film Academy Awards, popularly known as IIFA, is an annual awards ceremony for Bollywood. Produced by Wizcraft International Entertainment Pvt.
