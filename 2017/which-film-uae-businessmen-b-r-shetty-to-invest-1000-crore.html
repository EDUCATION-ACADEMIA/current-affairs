Which film UAE businessmen B R shetty to invest 1000 crore

(A) ** The Mahabharata’

(B) The Ramanyana

(C) Karna

(D) Bhagavat Geetha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -B R Shetty to invest Rs 1, 000 crore to make Mahabharata film.

Concept note-2: -B.R Shetty (born 1 August 1942) is an Indian-born businessman who is the founder and acquirer of a number of companies based in the United Arab Emirates, including Abu Dhabi-based NMC Health, Neopharma, BRS Ventures, and Finablr.

Concept note-3: -History. NMC Health was founded by B. R. Shetty in 1974 as the New Medical Centre in Abu Dhabi. In 1981, a new marketing & distribution division, NMC Trading was launched, which distributed medicines to pharmacies in the UAE.

Concept note-4: -It was in March that Finablr went into administration, and the de-listing will speed up the re-launch of operations of ex-Finablr businesses acquired by Wizz Financial.

Concept note-5: -Dr Bavaguthu Raghuram Shetty, Founder & Chairman, is one of the leading business icons in the Middle East. He has worked extensively in many sectors including pharmaceuticals, healthcare, financial services, FMCG and hospitality, and is a passionate advocate .
