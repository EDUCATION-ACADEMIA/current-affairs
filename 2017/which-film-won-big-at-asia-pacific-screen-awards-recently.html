Which film won big at Asia Pacific Screen Awards recently?

(A) ** Newton

(B) Bahubaali

(C) Lagaan

(D) Sholay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The best new performer award also went to a Korean woman, Park Ji-min in “Return to Seoul, ” the only film to win multiple awards on the night.

Concept note-2: -Rajkummar Rao won the 2017 Asia Pacific Screen Awards, in the best actor category for Newton. The movie was produced by Drishyam Films and directed by Masurkar.

Concept note-3: -Brillante Mendoza is the first Filipino director to be honored by France with the title Chevalier dans l’Ordre des Arts et Lettres. His films Kanatay (2009) and Taklub (2015) have won awards at the Cannes Film Festival, and Thy Womb saw him win Achievement in Direction at the 2012 Asia Pacific Screen Awards.

Concept note-4: -Manoj Bajpayee, Best Actor winner.
