Which government launches two online portals Maha DBT, MahaVASTU?

(A) ** Maharashtra

(B) Gujarat

(C) Kerala

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maharashtra Chief Minister Devendra Fadnavis launched MahaDBT and MahaVASTU online portals at an event here.

Concept note-2: -Maharashtra Government on 3 August 2017 launched MahaDBT and MahaVASTU, two online portals to streamline the process of Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).

Concept note-3: -Aaple Sarkar DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) or MahaDBT is a scholarship portal launched by the Government of Maharashtra. Mahadbt portal provides the benefits of many social welfare schemes like e-Scholarships to the students of Maharashtra.
