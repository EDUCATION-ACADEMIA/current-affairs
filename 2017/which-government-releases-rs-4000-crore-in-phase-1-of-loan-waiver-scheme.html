Which government releases Rs 4000 crore in phase 1 of Loan Waiver Scheme?

(A) Bihar

(B) Punjab

(C) New Delhi

(D) ** Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Farmers of These 19 Districts Will Be Benefitted From UP Govt’s Decision. This is not the first time, the UP government has decided to waive off farm loans.

Concept note-2: -The first nationwide farm-loan waiver in independent India was implemented in 1990 by the VP Singh-led government. It cost the exchequer Rs 10, 000 crore. In 2008, the Agricultural Debt Waiver and Debt Relief Scheme, implemented by the UPA government, involved an outgo of Rs 71, 680 crore.

Concept note-3: -In a farm loan waiver scheme, the Centre or the state Government repays the loan to the banks on behalf of the farmers, simply by using public money collected in the form of taxes. When there is a poor monsoon or natural calamity, farmers cannot repay their loans.

Concept note-4: -EXTENSION OF PERIOD OF PAYMENT OF 75% SHARE BY “OTHER FARMERS” from 01.07. 09 TO 31.12. 09.
