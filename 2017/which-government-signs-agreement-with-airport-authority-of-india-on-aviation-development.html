Which Government signs agreement with Airport Authority of India on aviation development?

(A) Kerala

(B) ** Uttarkhand

(C) West Bengal

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -AAI signs MoU with Sweden to facilitate smart and sustainable aviation technology collaboration.

Concept note-2: -UP government, AAI sign lease pact for Ayodhya International airport | The Financial Express.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Himachal Pradesh.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Assam. Assam has signed a pact with the Airport Authority of India (AAI) to develop villages in Kamrup.

Concept note-5: -2.3 Airports Authority Act (the “AAI Act") was enacted by the Central Government in 1994, which stated that all government airports are to be developed, financed, operated and maintained by Airport Authority of India ("AAI").
