Which gun are being introduce in India Army recently?

(A) A K 47

(B) ** M777

(C) G 629

(D) 38 T

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first batch of AK 203 assault rifles have been produced by the small arms factory in Amethi, marking the start of a ten year contract during which a complete transfer of technology will take place for the modern rifles to be used by Indian security forces.

Concept note-2: -The Army plans to field in 2023 a rifle and light machine combination that will replace the standard issue M4 carbine and the M249 Squad Automatic Weapon for its close combat force.
