Which Indian Businessman topped Forbes List of Asia’s Richest Families recently?

(A) ** Mukesh Ambani

(B) Gautam Adani

(C) Adi Godrej

(D) N R Narayana Moorthy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mukesh Ambani the chairman and largest shareholder of Reliance Industries, has been the richest Indian for 13 consecutive years. He is currently world’s 10th richest person in the world according to Forbes. Savitri Jindal is currently India’s richest woman topping the list at 7th position.

Concept note-2: -The biggest gainer by far is Gautam Adani of India, whose Adani Group encompasses a network of publicly traded companies active in power generation, cement, real estate and more. In early February 2022, Adani became the richest person in Asia and the tenth richest person in the world worth $90.1 billion.

Concept note-3: -India’s Gautam Adani overtook Mukesh Ambani to become the richest person in Asia in February 2022. His fortune soared $55 billion last year, and he quickly rose even higher up the ranks. In late January he was the third richest person in the world.
