Which Institute develops new technology to manufacture biofuel?

(A) Anna University

(B) Kerala University

(C) ** Indian Institute of technology, Kharagpur

(D) Nagaland University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru, announced the development of new technology to produce green hydrogen from biomass.

Concept note-2: -“It is soil-to-soil technology, an integrated process where we are using natural resources to extract gaseous and liquid biofuel and then converting the wastes into bio fertilizer.

Concept note-3: -Sources of Biofuel The common organic materials used to create biofuel include: Animal fat. Sugarcane. Rice.

Concept note-4: -India retains an aspirational biodiesel blend goal (on-road use) of B5 by 2030. In 2022, the national average blend rate is estimated at 0.07 percent.
