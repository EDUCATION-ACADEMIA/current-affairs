Which institute got the Prime Minister Yoga Award recently?

(A) Krishnamacharya Yoga Mandiram

(B) ** Ramamani Iyengar Memorial Yoga Institute, Pune

(C) Ashtanga Institute, Mysore

(D) Bihar School of Yoga, Munger

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ramamani Iyengar Memorial Yoga Institutes, Pune has bagged the first PM’s Yoga award for outstanding contribution to promotion and development of Yoga. The Prime Minister of India instituted the award at the 2nd International Day of Yoga celebrations at Chandigarh.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister’s Yoga Award for Outstanding Contribution towards the Development and Promotion of Yoga at National and International Levels-2022. Prime Minister’s Yoga Award for Outstanding Contribution towards the Development and Promotion of Yoga at National and International Levels-2022.

Concept note-3: -The Prime Minister’s Award for Outstanding Contribution towards the Development and Promotion of Yoga, one of the most coveted honours in the field of Yoga, has been announced for the year 2021.

Concept note-4: -Interested individuals and institutions can access the PMYA page at https://innovateindia.mygov.in/pm-yoga-awards-2022/ to understand the nominations process and participate. The nomination process for this year started on 28th March, 2022 and the last date for the submission of the entries is 27 April 2022.
