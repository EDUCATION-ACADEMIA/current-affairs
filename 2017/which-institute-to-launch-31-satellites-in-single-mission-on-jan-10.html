Which institute to launch 31 satellites in single mission on Jan 10?

(A) ** ISRO

(B) NASA

(C) SPARTAN

(D) SPIC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PSLV-C38 Successfully Launches 31 Satellites in a Single Flight. ISRO’s Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle PSLV-C38 successfully launched the 712 kg Cartosat-2 Series Satellite along with 30 co-passenger satellites today (June 23, 2017) from Satish Dhawan Space Centre SHAR, Sriharikota.

Concept note-2: -Recently, PSLV-C54 was successfully launched EOS-06 satellite on 26th November 2022 along with Eight Nano-satellites including INDIA-BHUTAN SAT (INS-2B).

Concept note-3: -The Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) is the national space research agency of the Republic of India.

Concept note-4: -SSLV-D2 lifted off from the first launch pad at Satish Dhawan Space Centre-SHAR, Sriharikota at 9:18 a.m. This is the first satellite launch in 2023. “The SSLV-D2-EOS-07 mission is successfully accomplished, ” ISRO mentioned on its Twitter handle, a few minutes after the vehicle took off.

Concept note-5: -NSIL’s 1st Demand Driven Satellite Mission Post Space Sector Reforms. GSAT-24 is a 24 Ku band communication satellite meant for meeting the Direct-To-Home (DTH) communication needs of the country. Satellite weighed 4180 kg at lift-off and has mission life of 15 years.
