Which institute to launch Indian Regional Navigation Satellite System (IRNSS-1H)?

(A) Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA)

(B) Russian Federal Space Agency (RFSA)

(C) National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA)

(D) ** Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Space Research Organisation. https://www.isro.gov.in/Spacecraft/irnss-1i. Retrieved October 19, 2019. “PSLV-C41/IRNSS-1I".

Concept note-2: -PSLV-C41 Successfully Launches IRNSS-1I Navigation Satellite.

Concept note-3: -IRNSS is an independent regional navigation satellite system being developed by India. It is designed to provide accurate position information service to users in India as well as the region extending up to 1500 km from its boundary, which is its primary service area.

Concept note-4: -IRNSS-1H was the eighth in the Indian Regional Navigational Satellite System (IRNSS) series of satellites, after IRNSS-1A, IRNSS-1B, IRNSS-1C, IRNSS-1D, IRNSS-1E, IRNSS-1F and IRNSS-1G.
