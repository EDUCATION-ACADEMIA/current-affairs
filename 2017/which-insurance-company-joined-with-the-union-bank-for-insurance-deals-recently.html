Which insurance Company joined with the Union Bank for Insurance Deals recently?

(A) ** Bajaj Allianz General Insurance

(B) HDFC General Insurance

(C) SBI General Insurance

(D) Kotak Mahindra Life Insurance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Insurer: From 01.06. 2022 onwards all the PMSBY enrollments and claims will go to United India Insurance Company Limited.

Concept note-2: -Union Bank of India having the most diversified network of over 4200 branches has entered into Corporate Agency tie up with Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Co Ltd to offer its customers; quality general insurance products at competitive rates.

Concept note-3: -Bank of Baroda to acquire 21% stake of Union Bank of India in IndiaFirst Life Insurance.

Concept note-4: -Recently, Andhra Bank and Corporation Bank were amalgamated into Union Bank of India with effect from 01.04. 2020. Today, it has a network of 8, 700+ domestic branches, 10, 900+ ATMs, 16, 200+ BC Points serving over 153+ million customers with 76, 000+ employees .

Concept note-5: -Founded in 2009, Star Union Dai-ichi Life Insurance (SUD Life) is a joint venture between two of India’s leading public sector banks, Bank of India and Union Bank of India, and Dai-ichi Life Holdings, a leading life insurance company in Japan.
