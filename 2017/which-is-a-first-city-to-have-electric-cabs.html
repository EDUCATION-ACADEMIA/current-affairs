Which is a first city to have electric cabs?

(A) Chennai

(B) Kolkata

(C) ** Nagpur

(D) Patna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kolkata: City’s first e-cab fleet was formally flagged off on Friday by West Bengal Transport Corporation managing director Rajanvir Singh Kapur.

Concept note-2: -ETO Motors deploys 50 3W EVs in Kevadia; India’s first Electric Vehicle City.

Concept note-3: -The e-taxi is one of the ambitious projects of the Union Government and forms as a part of its United Nations Development Program (UNDP) millennium goals to tackle pollution. The project will be undertaken by Nagpur Municipal Corporation and is the first project of its kind in India.

Concept note-4: -Reva is India’s first electric car. At just 3.7 lakh rupees, it is also the cheapest Electric car in the country. The company launched the Reva i model in 2008.

Concept note-5: -Kevadia in Gujarat will soon transform into the country’s first electric-vehicle-only city.
