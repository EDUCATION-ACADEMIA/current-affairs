Which IT company has ranked the top brand among Global 500 brands

(A) Apple

(B) Amazon

(C) Intel

(D) ** Google

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to brand valuation consultancy Brand Finance’s report, “Global 500 2023”, while Amazon is back at No 1, its brand value has fallen by over $50 billion this year, with its rating slipping from AAA+ to AAA.

Concept note-2: -Infosys (NSE, BSE, NYSE: INFY), a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting, today announced that it has been recognized as the top 3 most valuable IT services brands globally, according to Brand Finance, the world’s leading brand valuation firm, in its Brand Finance Global 500 2023 report.

Concept note-3: -Tata Group’s IT behemoth TCS, founded in 1968, rounds out the top ten list of Fortune 500 India for 2022. Last year, Tata Consultancy Services more than doubled its brand value to $45.5 billion to become India’s most valuable brand.

Concept note-4: -In 2022, Apple was the most valuable brand in the world with an estimated brand value of about 355.1 billion U.S. dollars.
