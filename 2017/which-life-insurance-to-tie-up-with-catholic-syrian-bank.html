Which life insurance to tie up with catholic Syrian bank?

(A) ** HDFC life

(B) LIC life insurance

(C) The New India assurance limited

(D) SBI life

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CSB Bank has tied up with Edelweiss Tokio Life to offer life insurance solutions through its branch network.

Concept note-2: -HDFC Life distributes its products through a multi-channel network consisting of Insurance agents, Bancassurance partners (HDFC Bank, Saraswat Bank, RBL Bank), a Direct channel, Insurance Brokers, MFIs (Micro Finance Institutions), SFBs (Small Finance Banks), etc.

Concept note-3: -The Minimum Average Monthly Balance to be maintained is only Rs 2, 500 in Rural/Semi-Urban & Rs 5000 in Urban/Metro.

Concept note-4: -We have implemented strategic changes in business model as a full service new age private sector bank backed by our globally reputed promoter – FIH Mauritius Investments Ltd, a Company wholly owned by Fairfax India Holdings Corporation( Fairfax India), part of Fairfax Group.
