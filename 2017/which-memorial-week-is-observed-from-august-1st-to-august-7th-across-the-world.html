Which memorial week is observed from August 1st to August 7th across the world?

(A) ** World Breastfeeding Week

(B) World Antibiotic Awareness week

(C) World Creativity and Innovative week

(D) Prevention of Blindness Week

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International Beer Day is observed on the first Friday of August. Originally it was started in Santa Cruz, California in 2007. It is observed on 7 August every year to honour the handloom weavers in the country.

Concept note-2: -2022 Daily Holidays that fall on August 1, include: Colorado Day-(Local observance in Colorado) DOGust Universal Birthday for Shelter Dogs. Homemade Pie Day. International Can-It Forward Day-(Mason Jars)

Concept note-3: -The important days in August 2023 are World Wide Web Day (1 Aug), Hiroshima Day (6 Aug), National Handloom Day (7 Aug), Quit India Day (9 Aug), Independence Day (15 Aug), Teej (19 Aug), National Sports Day (29 Aug), Onam (29 Aug), and Raksha Bandhan (30 Aug).
