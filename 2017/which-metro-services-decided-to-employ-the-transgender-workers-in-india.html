Which metro services decided to employ the transgender workers in India?

(A) Mumbai metro services

(B) ** Kochi metro services

(C) Chennai Metro Services

(D) Delhi Metro services

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Noida Metro Rail Corporation (NMRC) on Tuesday officially renamed one of its stations here as “Pride Station” dedicated to the transgender community, a first for Metro services in northern India.

Concept note-2: -The Delhi Metro Rail is the largest and busiest metro network in India. It is managed and executed by the Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC). Delhi Metro serves the National Capital and the NCR cities of Gurugram, Faridabad, Noida, Ghaziabad, Bahadurgarh and Ballabhgarh.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Metro rail service in India has become the second largest operational metro rail network in the country? Notes: The Chief Minister of Telangana recently flagged off the stretch of Hyderabad Metro between Hyderabad and its sister city Secunderabad.
