Which ministry approved delegation of powers to Border Roads Organisation (BRO)?

(A) Ministry of Agriculture

(B) Ministry of External Affairs

(C) ** Ministry of Defence

(D) Ministry of Finance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Defence Ministry approves delegation of powers to BRO.

Concept note-2: -Border Roads Organization (BRO) | Ministry of Road Transport & Highways, Government of India.

Concept note-3: -The Border Roads Organisation functions under the control of the Ministry of Defence (since 2015). This body is responsible for maintaining the road networks in the border areas of India.

Concept note-4: -The BRDB was re-constituted in 1985 with the Defence Minister as the chairman. Currently, the Minister of State for Defence is the chairman of the Board.
