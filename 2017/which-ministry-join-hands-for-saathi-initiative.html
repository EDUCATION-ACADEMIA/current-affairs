Which ministry join hands for SAATHI initiative?

(A) Ministry of External Affairs

(B) ** Ministry of Power & Textiles

(C) Ministry of Finance

(D) Ministry of Women and Child Development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union Ministry of Power and Textiles have jointly launched an initiative called SAATHI (Sustainable and Accelerated Adoption of efficient Textile technologies to Help small Industries).

Concept note-2: -SAATHI scheme is jointly implemented by the Union Ministry of Textiles and the Union Ministry of Power under its PSU Energy Efficiency Services Limited (EESL).

Concept note-3: -Such S&T infrastructure will be known as Sophisticated Analytical & Technical Help Institute (SATHI). These Centres will be equipped with major analytical instrument and advanced manufacturing facility, which is usually not available at Institutes / Organisations.

Concept note-4: -Ministries of Power and Textiles have now joined hands under a new initiative SAATHI (Sustainable and Accelerated Adoption of efficient Textile technologies to Help small Industries).
