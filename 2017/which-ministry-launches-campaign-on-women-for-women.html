Which ministry launches campaign on ’women for women’?

(A) Ministry of Ayush

(B) Ministry of defence

(C) Ministry of External Affairs

(D) ** Ministry of Ministry of Women and Child Development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Nai Chetna’ Gender Campaign Launched by Ministry of Rural Development-Key Points. The Union Minister described that the ‘Nai Chetna’ campaign focuses on women.

Concept note-2: -Organisation: The Ministry of Women and Child Development is headed by Hon’ble Minister Smt. Smriti Zubin Irani, Minister of State Dr.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of Urban Development recently launched “Nai Chetna-Pahal Badlav Ki, ” a community-led national campaign against gender discrimination. The campaign was also launched in Kerala under the auspices of the Kudumbashree Mission.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Mahila e Haat. Mahila E-Haat online marketing platform is launched on 7th March 2016 by Smt. Maneka Sanjay Gandhi, Hon’ble Minister for Women and Child Development.

Concept note-5: -NCW launched it in collaboration with CyberPeace Foundation and Meta, said by the Ministry of Women and Child Development.
