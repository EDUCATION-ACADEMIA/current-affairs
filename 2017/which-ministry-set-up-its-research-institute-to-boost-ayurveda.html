Which ministry set up its research institute to boost Ayurveda?

(A) Ministry of Finance

(B) Ministry of External Affairs

(C) ** Ministry of AYUSH

(D) Ministry of Telecommunication

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under Central Sector Scheme for promotion of International Cooperation, (IC Scheme), the Ministry of AYUSH undertakes various steps to promote and publicize AYUSH systems of medicine including Ayurveda across the globe.

Concept note-2: -AYUSH is an acronym for Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homeopathy and are the six Indian systems of medicine prevalent and practiced in India and some of the neighboring Asian countries with very few exceptions in some of the developed countries.
