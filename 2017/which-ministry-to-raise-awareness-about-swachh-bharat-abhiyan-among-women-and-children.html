Which ministry to raise awareness about Swachh Bharat Abhiyan among women and children

(A) ** Ministry of Women & Child Development

(B) Ministry of Urban Development

(C) Ministry of Finance

(D) Ministry of Home Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The campaign Bal Swachhta Abhiyan was launched by the Minister of Women and Child Development Menka Gandhi in New Delhi and is being carried out by the Education and Health Department in India.

Concept note-2: -Swachh Bharat Mission-Gramin, Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation.

Concept note-3: -Swachhta Pakhwada (SP) The Ministry of Tourism (MoT), with the aim of maintaining cleanliness at tourist destinations across the country has observed awareness campaign on cleanliness by undertaking a fortnightly awareness drive called “Swachhta Pakhwada”.

Concept note-4: -The Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) launched on 2nd October, 2014, by modifying the erstwhile Nirmal Bharat Abhiyan (NBA), is a community-led and people-oriented programme aimed at universalizing safe sanitation.

Concept note-5: -’Swachhta’ should be instilled in children as one of the core values: Shri Arun Jaitley.
