Which ministry won Bronze Medal at the India International Trade Fair 2017?

(A) Ministry of Corporate Affairs

(B) Ministry of External Affairs

(C) Ministry of Telecommunication

(D) ** Ministry of Health Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kerala Pavilion won gold medal at the India International Trade Fair (IITF)organized in New Delhi. The 6000 square feet pavilion underlining the concept of ‘Vocal for Local, Local to Global’ won the award in the State & Union Territory Pavilion category.

Concept note-2: -The theme of the IITF 2022 is “Vocal for Local, Local to Global” and was held from November 14 – 27, 2022. It was organized by the India Trade Promotion Organization (ITPO), a division of MoCI.

Concept note-3: -"IITF offers an ideal platform to showcase Indian products under the ‘Vocal for Local’ campaign, and infuse renewed confidence and vigour in the Indian economy, ” it said. Some 12 countries including Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Bahrain, Belarus, Iran, Nepal, Thailand, Turkey, UAE, and the UK will participate in the fair.

Concept note-4: -PATNA: The Bihar Pavilion was awarded a gold medal in ‘Best Pavilion’ category during the closing ceremony of the 40th India International Trade Fair (IITF) held at Pragti Maidan in New Delhi on Saturday.
