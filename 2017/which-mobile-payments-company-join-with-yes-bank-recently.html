Which mobile payments company join with Yes Bank recently?

(A) Paytm

(B) Freecharge

(C) ** TerraPay

(D) Quickr

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Electrum Fintech Solutions provides an integrated Payments and Spend Management platform-“ZRO” in association with Yes Bank. This is an innovative solution for issuance and management of cards along with a Web Portal for corporates and mobile app for the users.

Concept note-2: -PhonePe, which earlier partnered with Yes Bank, is now working with private sector lender ICICI Bank for UPI transactions.

Concept note-3: -PhonePe’s founder Sameer Nigam was appointed as the CEO of the company. In August 2016, the company partnered with Yes Bank to launch a UPI-based mobile payment app, based on the government-backed UPI platform.

Concept note-4: -ICICI Bank, Housing Development Finance Corp Ltd, Axis Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank, Bandhan Bank, Federal Bank and IDFC First have also joined the SBI-led consortium and invested in Yes Bank.
