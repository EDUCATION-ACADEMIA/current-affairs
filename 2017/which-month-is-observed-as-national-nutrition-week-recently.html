Which month is observed as National Nutrition Week recently?

(A) ** September

(B) November

(C) October

(D) January

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Nutrition Week is observed every year from September 1 to September 7 in a bid to raise awareness about the importance of nutrition for the human body and how we can take adequate and timely measures to eradicate hunger and malnourishment.

Concept note-2: -The theme of National Nutrition week 2022 is ‘Celebrate a World of Flavours’. The government introduces a special theme every year that primarily focuses on that year’s theme. Last year, the theme for nutrition week was ‘feeding smart right from the start.

Concept note-3: -National Nutrition Week 2022: 5 Must-to Add Nutrients In Women’s Diet Regimen For Overall Health And Well-being. National Nutrition Week (NNW) is observed from September 1-7 every year to educate the populace about the significance of nutrition.

Concept note-4: -The basic themes of the National Nutrition Month 2022 are women and health, child and education, nutrition and education, water conservation, management with gender sensitivity, he said and added traditional food and drink have been prescribed for women and children in tribal areas.

Concept note-5: -National Nutrition Month is an annual nutrition education and information program created by the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics. In India, National Nutrition Week is observed from September 1 to September 7 to raise public awareness about nutrition and healthy eating habits.
