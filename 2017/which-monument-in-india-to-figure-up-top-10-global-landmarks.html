Which monument in India to figure up top 10 global landmarks?

(A) Qutab Minar of Delhi

(B) Red fort

(C) Jantar mantar

(D) ** Taj Mahal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Travellers’ choice: Taj Mahal only Indian monument to figure in top 10 global landmarks.

Concept note-2: -1. The Statue of Liberty, New York, USA.

Concept note-3: -Taj Mahal is the most famous monument in India. Built by Shahjahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal, it is a symbol of undying love. It is also the second best UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Concept note-4: -Humayun’s Tomb, Delhi A masterwork of Persian architects, this tomb is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and a must-visit part of India’s Golden Triangle.
