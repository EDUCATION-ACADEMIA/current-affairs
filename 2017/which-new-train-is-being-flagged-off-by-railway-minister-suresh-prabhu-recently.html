(B) High-speed rail.

(C) ** Vistadome coach train

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi, Feb 27 (IBNS): Union Railways Minister Suresh Prabhu flagged off the Husafar and Antyodaya express trains on Monday through video conferencing from Rail Bhavan, New Delhi. He also launched a new Partnership in Parcel Business with the Department of Posts (India Post) and a new Catering Policy.

Concept note-2: -Currently a Member of Rajya Sabha from Andhra Pradesh, he has been a Member of Lok Sabha in the 11th, 12th, 13th and 14th Lok Sabha between 1996 and 2009. His constituency comprised the district of Sindhudurg and parts of Ratnagiri district in the state of Maharashtra on the western coast of India.

Concept note-3: -Minister of Railways Ashwini Vaishnaw stressed bringing transformation to Railways. Minister of Railways Ashwini Vaishnaw stressed bringing transformation in Railways for its better future and helping the economy to grow further.
