Which of the following bank account not remove all limits on cash withdrawals from ATM by RBI

(A) ** Savings Bank Accounts

(B) Current Account

(C) Cash Credit Accounts

(D) Overdraft Accounts

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is a savings account? With savings accounts, funds are less accessible, since these accounts are made to store money for financial goals. Checks can’t be written against them, and you’re generally limited to six free withdrawals or transfers a month from the account.

Concept note-2: -Monthly Transactions The permissible limit without attracting any charge is usually anywhere between 3 to 5 transactions per month (financial and non-financial). Current accounts: Do not have any limit on the maximum number of transactions which one can carry out.

Concept note-3: -However, non-cash withdrawal transactions (such as balance enquiry, cheque book request, payment of taxes, funds transfer, etc.), on own bank ATMs are not to be part of the number of free ATM transactions.

Concept note-4: -In current account, amount can be deposited and withdrawn at any time without giving any notice.
