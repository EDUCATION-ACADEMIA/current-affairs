Which of the following countries has conducted the joint military exercise ’Al Nagah-II 2017’

(A) Qatar-India

(B) China-Russia

(C) ** Indo-Oman

(D) Srilanka-Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is India and Oman. In an effort to build bilateral military-to-military relations and skills, a 14-day joint defence exercise ‘Al-Nagah-II’ between the armies of India and Oman was conducted in the Dhauladhar Ranges at Bakloh in Himachal Pradesh from March 6, 2017.

Concept note-2: -India, Kazakhstan hold joint military exercise in Meghalaya | North East India News, The Indian Express.

Concept note-3: -21st EDITION OF INDIA FRANCE BILATERAL NAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the Western Seaboard today, 16 Jan 23.

Concept note-4: -Joint Military Exercises with the UAE: With the UAE, India has In-UAE BILAT (bilateral naval exercise) as well as Desert Eagle-II (bilateral air force exercise).
