Which of the following country will become the newest member of NATO?

(A) Croatia

(B) Luxembourg

(C) Albania

(D) ** Montenegro

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -North Macedonia is the newest member of NATO; it joined the alliance in 2020. In May 2022 Sweden and Finland announced their intention to join NATO in response to Russia’s unprovoked invasion of Ukraine.

Concept note-2: -The most recent member states to join NATO were Montenegro on 5 June 2017 and North Macedonia on 27 March 2020. In July 2022, NATO invited Finland and Sweden to join the organization, and the ratification process for the two countries is in progress. In September 2022, Ukraine applied for NATO membership.
