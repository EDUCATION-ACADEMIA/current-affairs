Which of the following freedom fighter 120th birth anniversary celebrated on 23 January 2017

(A) Bhagat Singh

(B) ** Subhash Chandra Bose

(C) Mahatma Gandhi

(D) Bal Gangadhar Tilak

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A grateful nation remembers the great freedom fighter Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose on his birth anniversary today, January 23.

Concept note-2: -January 23 marks the birth anniversary of Subhas Chandra Bose, popularly known as “Netaji", who was one of the most prominent Indian freedom fighters. The day is celebrated as Parakram Diwas, or the “day of valour", in India.

Concept note-3: -Parakram Diwas (also called Parakram Divas) is celebrated on January 23 every year. It is an Indian national holiday marking the birthday of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose (popularly called Netaji), a prominent nationalist, politician, and freedom fighter.

Concept note-4: -Parakram Day Bravery Day, also known as Parakram Diwas, is now a recognised national day in India and is observed on January 23. In 2021, just before Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose’s 125th birthday, the Indian government unveiled it.

Concept note-5: -Subhas Chandra Bose, also known as Netaji, was born in Cuttack, Odisha, on 23 January 1897. He was one of the most important figures in India’s struggle for independence. Netaji was also behind the revival of the Indian National Army or the Azad Hind Fauj.
