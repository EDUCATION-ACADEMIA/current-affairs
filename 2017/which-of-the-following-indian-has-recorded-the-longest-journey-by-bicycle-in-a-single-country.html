Which of the following Indian has recorded the longest journey by bicycle in a single country

(A) Bikram Singh

(B) Amrit Singh

(C) Sreedhar Savanur

(D) ** Prasad Erande

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prasad Erande from Pune travelled 14, 576 kilometres on a cycle across India, establishing a Guinness World Record for the ‘longest journey by bicycle in a single country’. He covered the distance between June 22-November 9 in 2014.

Concept note-2: -6, 275 Lives Have Been Saved With Monthly Donations From 2, 29, 365 Donors. Save Countless Lives By Giving Monthly. Help others that need urgent medical care. Need help:-Solo Cycle journey of 20, 000 km to make a new Guinness Record for the Longest Cycle journey in India.

Concept note-3: -Two cycles. One world record. Two Bengalureans, 23-year-old Dhanush Manjunath and 24-year-old Hemanth YB now hold the Guinness World Record for the longest journey by bicycle in a single country (team). .

Concept note-4: -Tommy Godwin rode 2, 084 miles (3, 354 km) between 16 and 22 July 1939. Bruce Berkeley rode 2, 825 kilometres (1, 755 mi) between 23 and 29 June 2014.

Concept note-5: -The longest continuous cycling path is 80.6 km (50.1 miles) and was achieved by Roads & Transport Authority (United Arab Emirates), in Dubai, United Arab Emirates on 22 November 2022. RTA attempted this record title to promote the Al Qudra Cycling as one of the best and longest continuous cycle track in the world.
