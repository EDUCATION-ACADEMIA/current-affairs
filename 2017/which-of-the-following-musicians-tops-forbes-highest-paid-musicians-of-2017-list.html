Which of the following Musicians tops Forbes Highest Paid Musicians of 2017 List?

(A) Justin Bieber

(B) Beyonce

(C) Drake

(D) ** Diddy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Diddy Did It: Sean Combs is this year’s highest-paid musician.

Concept note-2: -This may come as a bit of a surprise, but Bruce Springsteen made it to the top of Forbes’ list of highest-earning musicians in 2021 at an incredible $435 million.

Concept note-3: -LOS ANGELES (Reuters)-Beyonce’s hit album “Lemonade” and world tour made her the highest-paid woman in music in 2017, according to an annual Forbes list on Monday, edging out Britain’s Adele and pop star Taylor Swift.

Concept note-4: -Taylor Swift ($825 million) Not old enough to buy a beer at the decade’s outset, Swift became a stadium-packing superstar-and music’s highest-paid woman of the 2010s.
