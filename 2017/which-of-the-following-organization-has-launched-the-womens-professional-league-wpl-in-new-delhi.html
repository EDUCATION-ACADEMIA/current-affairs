Which of the following Organization has launched the Women’s Professional League (WPL) in New Delhi.

(A) Indian Boxing Federation

(B) Netball Federation of India

(C) ** All India Football Federation

(D) Futsal Association of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The new tournament was informally known as the Women’s Indian Premier League; however, on 25 January 2023 the BCCI officially named it the Women’s Premier League.. The WPL auction has been scheduled for 13 February 2023, beginning at 14:30 IST.

Concept note-2: -Each of the five WPL teams-Mumbai Indians, Delhi Capitals, Gujarat Giants, Royal Challengers Bangalore and UP Warriorz-will come to the table equipped with a contingent of professionals, coaches and scouts, all armed with copious amounts of data and several mock auction rounds under their belt.
