Which of the following state has recently have two Deputy Chief Minister

(A) Punjab

(B) Haryana

(C) Odisha

(D) ** Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Currently, 11 out of the 28 states and 1 out of the 8 union territories have deputy chief ministers. Out of these 10 states and union territories, Andhra Pradesh has five deputy chief ministers and Uttar Pradesh have two deputy chief ministers.

Concept note-2: -Manish Sisodia, Deputy Chief Minister of Delhi is a senior leader of the Aam Aadmi Party and a member of the Political Affairs Committee.

Concept note-3: -The first was Vallabhbhai Patel of the Indian National Congress party, who was sworn in on 15 August 1947, when India gained independence from the British Raj.

Concept note-4: -He is the second highest ranking executive authority of the state’s council of ministers. The current Deputy Chief of Bihar is Tejashwi Yadav, serving in office since 10 August 2022.

Concept note-5: -Shri Manish Sisodia ( Deputy Chief Minister, Delhi )
