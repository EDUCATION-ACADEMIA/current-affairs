Which of the following was included as a member of the governing body of the Sports Authority of India (SAI).

(A) Chetan Anand

(B) Saina Nehwal

(C) ** Jwala Gutta

(D) Lee Chong Wei

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -14 Regular schools with 11 disciplines namely Athletics, Basketball, Football, Gymnastics, Hockey, Kho-Kho, Kabaddi, Swimming, Table Tennis, Volleyball and Wrestling.

Concept note-2: -Sports Authority of India | Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Sports India. Former Union Ministers of Youth Affairs and Sports Rajyavardhan Rathore announced that the national sports body, Sports Authority of India (SAI), will now be known as ‘Sports India’.

Concept note-4: -To establish, run, manage and administer the institutions to produce high caliber coaches, sports scientists and physical education teachers. To plan, construct, acquire, develop, take over, mange, maintain and utilize sports infrastructure and facilities in the country.
