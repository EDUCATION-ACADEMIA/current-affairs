Which place 14th South Asian Association for Regional Co-operation in Law Conference begins?

(A) ** Colombo

(B) New York

(C) Nagaland

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A declaration adopted at the end of the summit also covered a variety of other subjects, aimed at deepening ties within the 22-year-old regional grouping. government of India, Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Bhutan, Maldives, Nepal, Pakistan and Sri Lanka met for the 14th SAARC summit in New Delhi on April 3-4.

Concept note-2: -The first summit was held in Dhaka, Bangladesh on 7–8 December 1985. The first summit agreed to establish a SAARC Secretariat and adopted an official SAARC emblem. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -Its secretariat is based in Kathmandu, Nepal.

Concept note-4: -Afghanistan joined as the 8th member of SAARC in 2007. To date, 18th Summits have been held and Nepal’s former Foreign Secretary is the current Secretary General of SAARC. The 19th Summit will be hosted by Pakistan in 2016.
