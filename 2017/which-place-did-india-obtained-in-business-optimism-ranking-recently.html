Which place did India obtained in Business Optimism Ranking recently?

(A) ** 7th

(B) 9th

(C) 15th

(D) 28th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India ranks fourth in terms of business optimism globally: Grant Thornton.

Concept note-2: –The United States takes the top ranking for the 10th consecutive year. Germany advances to the second position as Canada falls one position to third.

Concept note-3: -India jumps 79 positions from 142nd (2014) to 63rd (2019) in ‘World Bank’s Ease of Doing Business Ranking 2020’. To further enhance the ease of doing business in the country more than 39, 000 compliances have been reduced and more than 3, 400 legal provisions have been decriminalized.

Concept note-4: -In the recent years, India has emerged as one of the most attractive destinations, not only for investments, but also for doing business. The country has risen significantly in the Ease of Doing Business (EoDB) rankings, making it one of the top performers on a global platform.
