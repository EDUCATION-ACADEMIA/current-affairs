Which place India Opens First Partition Museum recently?

(A) ** Amritsar

(B) New Delhi

(C) Chennai

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World’s first Partition Museum to be inaugurated in Amritsar, Gulzar’s book to be launched.

Concept note-2: -On 24 October 2016, with the support of the Punjab Heritage and Tourism Promotion Board, Government of Punjab, TAACHT opened the doors of the world’s first Partition Museum at the Town Hall in Amritsar with a curtain raiser exhibition in four galleries.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Punjab. Amritsar was the location for the museum dedicated to Partition. It focuses on stories of survivors.

Concept note-4: -Partition Museum, India 1947 Refugee Artefacts, Oral Stories, Archival Materials.

Concept note-5: -The Partition Museum, initiated by The Arts and Cultural Heritage Trust (TAACHT), has been set up as a People’s Museum to remember all those millions who lost their homes or loved ones at that time.
