Which place Indian Railways installs Asia’s largest Solid State Interlocking (SSI)?

(A) ** Kharagpur

(B) Nagpur

(C) Jaipur

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Railways installs Asia’s largest Solid State Interlocking system in West Bengal. Indian Railways has installed Asia’s largest Solid State Interlocking (SSI) system at Kharagpur in West Bengal.

Concept note-2: -With the commissioning of 3rd line between Bandel – Magra, India’s largest state-of-the-art Electronic Interlocking System (EI) has been commissioned at Bandel station. The new Electronic Interlocking System at Bandel Junction having provision of 1002 routes is, in fact, Asia’s largest Electronic Interlocking System.

Concept note-3: -Bandel Jn. Station in West Bengal now has the world’s largest Electronic Interlocking System.

Concept note-4: -An SSI interlocking cubicle comprises three Interlocking Processors or Multi Processor Modules (MPMs), two Panel Processors and a Diagnostics Processor (DMPM). An SSI system can operate on two MPMs in the event of the failure of one.

Concept note-5: -BANDEL STATION GOT WORLD’S BIGGEST INTERLOCKING SYSTEM INSTALLED BY INDIAN RAILWAYS | WORLD RECORDS.
