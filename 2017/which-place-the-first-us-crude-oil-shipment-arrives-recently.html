Which place the First US Crude Oil Shipment Arrives recently?

(A) ** Odisha

(B) Vizag

(C) Kerala

(D) Kanyakumari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MT New Prosperity arrived at Paradip port in Odisha. This is one of the first shipments to India since the US stopped oil exports in 1975.

Concept note-2: -Crude oil moves from wellhead to refinery using barges, tankers, over land, pipelines, trucks, and railroads. Natural gas is transported by pipelines and liquefied natural gas (LNG) tankers.

Concept note-3: -Answer: (a) The world’s first oil well was drilled in Pennsylvania, USA, in 1859.
