Which plane got Commercial Flight Nod recently?

(A) ** Plane Dornier 228

(B) Mirage 2000

(C) MiG-29

(D) Sukhoi Su-30

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The DGCA (Directorate General of Civil Aviation ) on Friday granted air operator’s permit to Jet Airways .

Concept note-2: -Watch: Made-in-India Dornier aircraft makes its maiden ‘commercial’ flight. The state-run Alliance Air became the country’s first commercial airline to fly an Indian-made aircraft for civil operations.

Concept note-3: -Air India will get 250 Airbus planes–210 single-aisle A320neos and 40 widebody A350s. It plans to get 220 Boeing aircraft, –190 of its 737 MAX narrowbody jets, 20 787 wide-bodies and 10 777Xs. As per the report, Airbus and Air India signed the agreement on February 10.

Concept note-4: -Air India has finalised an order for around 250 aircraft with Airbus and the deal is expected to be announced next week, a source said on Thursday. The source in the know also said the airline has signed a deal with Boeing for about 200 planes.
