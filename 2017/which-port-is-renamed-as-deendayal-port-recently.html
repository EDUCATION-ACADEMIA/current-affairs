Which port is renamed as Deendayal Port recently?

(A) ** Kandla Port

(B) Visakhapatnam Port

(C) Chennai Port

(D) Jawaharlal Nehru Port

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kandla, now officially Deendayal Port Authority, is a seaport and town in Kutch district of Gujarat state in Western India, near the city of Gandhidham. Located on the Gulf of Kutch, it is one of India’s major ports on the west coast.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. Kandla Port in India has been renamed as Deendayal Port. It is situated in the Kutch district of Gujarat. It has been named after Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Gujarat. The Kandla port (Gujrat): One of the top 12 major ports in the country, had been rechristened as Deendayal Port in the name of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya.

Concept note-4: -Haldia Port, also known as the Haldia Dock Complex (HDC), is a major seaport located along the Hooglhy River in West Bengal, India. The port is the larger of two dock facilities operated by the Kolkata Port Trust, the smaller being the Kolkata Dock System, located about 100km upstream.

Concept note-5: -The Union Ministry of Shipping has renamed Kandla Port Trust as Deendayal Port Trust, by exercising powers conferred on it under Indian Ports Act, 1908. The name was changed as part of yearlong centenary celebrations of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay, who had stood for uplift of poor and weaker sections of society.
