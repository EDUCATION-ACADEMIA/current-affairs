Which program launched by the Meghalaya CM?

(A) Inauguration of Land Scheme

(B) ** “Livelihood Intervention and Facilitation of Entrepreneurship” (LIFE)

(C) Inauguration of Hospital

(D) Inauguration Three Day MODI Fest

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Meghalaya Chief Minister Conrad Sangma launched the shared school bus system, prime tourism vehicles and agriculture response vehicle scheme.

Concept note-2: -Meghalaya CM launches “Citizen Engagement and Communication Programme” at Tura, West Garo Hills district. In Meghalaya, the “Citizen Engagement and Communication Programme” was launched by Chief Minister Conrad K Sangma today at Tura, West Garo Hills district.

Concept note-3: -The Citizen Engagement and Reform Communication Program (CERC) built understanding and trust between citizens and police by establishing platforms for communication, engagement, and collaboration through libraries and other civic platforms.

Concept note-4: -FOCUS+ is a scheme devised by the Meghalaya government where farmers who are part of a group of producers will get Rs 5000 each.
