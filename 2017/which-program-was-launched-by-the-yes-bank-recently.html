Which program was launched by the YES Bank recently?

(A) Launch of a new app

(B) ** YES GST Program

(C) Inaugurate of a new Branch

(D) E services across the world.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Called Yes Bank Agri Infinity, a select cohort of startups will not only receive mentoring by veteran bankers for experiential co-development but also get access to the lender’s digital banking infrastructure and network, collaborative opportunities to pilot new solutions and fundraising advisory.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Yes Bank. Yes Bank has announced the launch of an annual startup enabler program, YES BANK Agri Infinity. It seeks to co-develop digital financial solutions for the food and agriculture ecosystem by mentoring entrepreneurial ventures in the field.

Concept note-3: -Reaffirming that the sale of assets to JC Flowers Asset Reconstruction Company (ARC) is on track, Prashant Kumar, MD & CEO, YES Bank, said the deal should conclude soon.

Concept note-4: -Enterprise Resource Planning(ERP)-YES BANK.
