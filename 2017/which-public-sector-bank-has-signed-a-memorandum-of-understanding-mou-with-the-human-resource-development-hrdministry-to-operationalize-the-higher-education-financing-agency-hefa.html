Which public sector bank has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Human Resource Development (HRD)Ministry to operationalize the Higher Education Financing Agency (HEFA)

(A) Bank of India

(B) UCO Bank

(C) Bank of Baroda

(D) ** Canara Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Higher Education Financing Agency (HEFA) is a joint venture company of Canara Bank and Ministry of Education GoI. HEFA provides financial assistance for creation of educational infrastructure and R&D in India’s premier educational Institutions.

Concept note-2: -The primary objective of HEFA is to leverage funds from the market and provide low-interest loans to universities for infrastructure and R&D.

Concept note-3: -ii. The Minister of Human Resource Development heads the Ministry of HRD. For purposes of the Departments of Higher Education and School Education & Literacy, he is presently assisted by two Ministers of State, HRD.
