Which railway services planned to develop 21 new stations?

(A) Chennai Metro Rail Systems

(B) ** Konkan Metro Rail Systems

(C) Kerala Metro Rail Systems

(D) Mumbai Metro Rail Systems

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union Cabinet on 28 September approved the Indian Railways’ proposal for the re-development of three major railway stations–New Delhi, Ahmedabad and Mumbai’s CSMT. The project involves an investment of approximately ₹10, 000 crore.

Concept note-2: -"The goal under Vision 2047 is to increase the port handling capacity to 10, 000 MTPA. Contours of the plan will soon be spelled out. There will be avenues for private participation that are being worked upon, ” the official said. India has 12 major ports and 78 operational non-major ports.

Concept note-3: -South Coast Railway zone is the newest zone in Indian Railways.
