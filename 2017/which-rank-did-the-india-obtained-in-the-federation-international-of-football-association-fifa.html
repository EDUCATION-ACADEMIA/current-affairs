Which rank did the India obtained in the Federation International of Football Association (FIFA)?

(A) 85

(B) ** 96

(C) 58

(D) 76

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India is ranked 104th in the FIFA Football Rankings.

Concept note-2: -India is in the 106th spot on the FIFA Ranking 2022 list. Indian men’s football team has a score of 1192 points.

Concept note-3: -This is Full List of FIFA World Men’s Ranking as of 22 December 2022. As per the FIFA World Rankings 2022, Brazil stands in the first position with 1, 841 points. Belgium and Argentina rank as the second and third in FIFA rankings with 1, 817 and 1, 774 points respectively.

Concept note-4: -The FIFA Men’s World Ranking is a ranking system for men’s national teams in association football, led by Brazil as of 2022.
