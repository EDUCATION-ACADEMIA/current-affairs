Which river bridge the Indian Railways constructed main arch recently?

(A) ** Chenab River Bridge

(B) Mahatma Gandhi Setu

(C) Jawahar Setu

(D) Godavari Arch Bridge

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In April 2021, the Chenab Rail Bridge’s arch was completed and the overall bridge was completed in August 2022. It is expected to open to rail traffic in December 2022. Key technical data of the bridge include: Deck height (height above river bed): 359 m (1, 178 ft), (height above river surface): 322 m (1, 056 ft)

Concept note-2: -Constructed over the Chenab river in the Kouri region of the Reasi district, the bridge stands at over 1177 feet above the river bed. It features 93 deck segments and is a part of the much-anticipated 272-km-long railway line from Udhampur to Baramulla.

Concept note-3: -Vembanad Rail Bridge: 4.62 Km, Kerala Edappally and Vallarpadam are connected by the Vembanad Rail Bridge in Kochi, Kerala. It is the longest railway bridge in India, measuring a total of 4, 620 metres.

Concept note-4: -[8] The bridge was fully completed and was inaugurated in August 2022. After the photos were shared on Twitter, there was instant applause at the engineering marvel.

Concept note-5: -It is being constructed by AFCONS Construction Company, having 17 spans, of which the main steel arch portion across the Chenab river is 476 meters in length and its height is 359 meters above the river bed. More than 1, 300 workers and 300 engineers have been working round-the-clock to complete the bridge in time.
