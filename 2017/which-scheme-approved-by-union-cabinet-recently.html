Which scheme approved by Union Cabinet recently?

(A) ** Special Infrastructure Development Scheme

(B) Atal pension Yojana

(C) National Service Scheme

(D) Integrated child development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry’s new scheme “Prime Minister’s Development initiative for the north eastern region – PM-Devine” (with an outlay of Rs. 6, 600 crore), has been approved earlier in october-2022 separately, under which larger and high impact proposals under infrastructure, social development and livelihood sectors are taken.

Concept note-2: -In the 2022 Union budget of India, there are 740 central sector (CS) schemes.

Concept note-3: -PM-DevINE will lead to creation of infrastructure, support industries, social development projects and create livelihood activities for youth and women, thus leading to employment generation. PM-DevINE will be implemented by Ministry of DoNER through North Eastern Council or Central Ministries/ agencies.

Concept note-4: -What are the schemes launched by the Modi government in 2022? There are various schemes launched by prime minister Narendra Modi sum the core schemes launched by the Modi government are swachh Bharat Abhiyan, Pradhanmantri Jan Dhan Yojana, Digital India Pradhanmantri ujjwala plan, Namami gange Yojana and many more.
