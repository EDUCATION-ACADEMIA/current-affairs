Which scheme introduced by Union Cabinet recently?

(A) ** Pradhan Mantri Mahila Shakti Kendra

(B) Atal Pension Yojana

(C) Suraksha Savings Scheme

(D) Senior Citizen Savings Scheme (SCSS)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry’s new scheme “Prime Minister’s Development initiative for the north eastern region – PM-Devine” (with an outlay of Rs. 6, 600 crore), has been approved earlier in october-2022 separately, under which larger and high impact proposals under infrastructure, social development and livelihood sectors are taken.

Concept note-2: -HRIDAY scheme was launched on 21st January 2015 for the development of heritage cities. This scheme aimed to preserve and revitalise heritage cities of India to reflect their unique character.

Concept note-3: -What are the schemes launched by the Modi government in 2022? There are various schemes launched by prime minister Narendra Modi sum the core schemes launched by the Modi government are swachh Bharat Abhiyan, Pradhanmantri Jan Dhan Yojana, Digital India Pradhanmantri ujjwala plan, Namami gange Yojana and many more.
