Which scheme is being launched by the Union Minister Shri Piyush Goyal?

(A) Live Stock Insurance Scheme

(B) Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment

(C) ** Energy Conservation Building Code 2017

(D) National Literacy Mission Programme

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government has rolled out a number of new initiatives like Soil Health Card Scheme, Neem Coated Urea, Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY), Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY), National Agriculture Market (e-NAM), Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) and Interest Subvention Scheme.

Concept note-2: -Shri Piyush Goyal (57)is the Minister of Commerce & Industry (2019-present), Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution (2020-present), Textiles (2021-present) and Leader of the Rajya Sabha (2021-present).
