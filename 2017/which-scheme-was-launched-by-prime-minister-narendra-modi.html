Which scheme was launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi?

(A) ** Intensified Mission Indradhanush

(B) National Rural Health Mission

(C) National Skill Development Mission

(D) Green India Mission

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The PM-DevINE Scheme will have an outlay of Rs. 6, 600 crore for the four year period from 2022-23 to 2025-26 (remaining years of 15th Finance Commission period). Efforts will be made to complete the PM-DevINE projects by 2025-26 so that there are no committed liabilities beyond this year.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi introduced schemes like PM Jan Dhan Yojana, Ujjwala Yojana, Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana and others since assuming office in 2014.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi will inaugurate a two-day event titled “PM Kisan Samman Sammelan 2022” on 17th October 2022 at Mela Ground, IARI, Pusa, New Delhi. On this occasion, Shri Modi will release Rs. 16, 000 Crores under the PM-KISAN flagship scheme as 12th instalment through Direct Benefit Transfer.

Concept note-4: -Atal Pension Yojana (APY) Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) PMVVY or Prime Minister Vaya Vandana Yojana.
