Which service is open between North Korea and Russia recently?

(A) Train service

(B) ** Ferry service

(C) Flight Services

(D) Tram services

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Khasan-Tumangang Crossing This is the only land crossing between Russia and North Korea, connecting the village of Khasan in Primorskiy Krai in the Russian Far East with the town of Tumangang in the DPRK’s Rason Special Economic Zone.

Concept note-2: -In September 2022, Russia reportedly purchased millions of shells and rockets from North Korea to aid in its invasion of Ukraine. Relations between the two countries continued after the dissolution of the Soviet Union. The relationship regained importance after Vladimir Putin was elected President of Russia in 2000.

Concept note-3: -demilitarized zone (DMZ), region on the Korean peninsula that demarcates North Korea from South Korea. It roughly follows latitude 38° N (the 38th parallel), the original demarcation line between North Korea and South Korea at the end of World War II.

Concept note-4: -The UK supports the US assessment that North Korea has completed an arms delivery to Russia for use by the Wagner Group, which paid for this equipment and has thousands of troops in Ukraine. This is a clear breach of UN Security Council Resolutions.
