Which shuttler Won Silver at World Badminton Championship 2017?

(A) Jwallla Kutta

(B) Ajay Jayaram

(C) Chetan Anand

(D) ** P V Sindhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2017 world championships was the first time that two Indian shuttlers were on the podium in a single edition. With PV Sindhu claiming silver, Saina Nehwal won bronze after reaching the semi-finals.

Concept note-2: -PV Sindhu has won the silver medal in the 2016 Rio Olympics.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Kidambi Srikanth. Kidambi Srikanth clinched a silver medal in the World Badminton Championships, Spain on 19 December 2021. He has become the first male shuttler to win silver at BWF Championships.
