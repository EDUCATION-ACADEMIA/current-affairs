Which social network launches Express Wifi in India?

(A) Twitter

(B) ** Facebook

(C) Snapchat

(D) Instagram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation : Facebook launched its “Express Wi-Fi” in India to help more people get online. It means a Free Basics platform, ties up with entrepreneurs to help them set up public WiFi hotspots and helping them provide internet to a lot of citizens in poor or no connectivity areas.

Concept note-2: -Express Wi-Fi is a division within Facebook Connectivity, a group of global internet connectivity initiatives by Meta.

Concept note-3: -Express Wi-Fi is a carrier-provided connectivity service bringing fast and affordable internet to neighborhoods around the world.
