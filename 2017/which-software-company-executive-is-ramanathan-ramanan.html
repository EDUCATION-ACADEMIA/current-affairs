Which software company Executive is Ramanathan Ramanan?

(A) Infosys

(B) ** Tata Consultancy Services

(C) Cognizant

(D) Wipro

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -R Ramanan is the Senior Vice President of Tata Consultancy Services, prior to this he was the Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer and a member of the Board of Directors of CMC Ltd ., a subsidiary of the globally acclaimed Tata Consultancy Services (TCS).

Concept note-2: -Ramanan is the Mission Director of the Atal Innovation Mission (AIM)-a flagship national Innovation initiative of the Prime Minister and the Government of India of India under the NITI Aayog (National Institution for Transforming India).

Concept note-3: -Chintan Vaishnav. Dr. Chintan Vaishnav is a socio-technologist, an engineer trained to design and build large-scale systems that possess both human as well as technological complexities.

Concept note-4: -The Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) was set up by NITI Aayog with an objective to create and encourage an environment of innovation and entrepreneurship across schools, educational organisations, research institutes and industries including MSMEs.
