Which space Organization tested the indigenously developed cryogenic engine for the upper stage ‘GSLV Mk III’ rocket.

(A) CNSA

(B) NASA

(C) ROSCOSMOS

(D) ** ISRO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The GSLV Mk3 rocket or LVM3 is a three-stage heavy lift launch vehicle developed by ISRO. The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) successfully tested the country’s existing CE-20 Cryogenic engine at a higher thrust level of 21.9 tonnes.

Concept note-2: -GSLV Mk III is a three-stage heavy lift launch vehicle developed by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).

Concept note-3: -The Launch Vehicle Mark-3 (LVM 3), previously referred as the Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle Mark III (GSLV Mk3), is a three-stage medium-lift launch vehicle developed by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).

Concept note-4: -In Short. By India Today Web Desk: The Indian Space Research Organisation (Isro) has successfully tested the indigenously developed cryogenic engine that will power its heaviest rocket, the LVM-3. The Launch Vehicle Mark-III went through the hot test at an uprated thrust level of 21.8 tonnes for the first time.
