(A) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjee on Monday announced that the state has won the International Travel Award 2023 for ‘Best Destination for Culture’.

Concept note-2: -Bengal Takes Home Prestigious International Tourism Award For Best Destination For Culture. Adjudged as the ‘Best Destination for Culture’ by a global organisation of travel writers Pacific Area Travel Writers Association (PATWA), Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee announced the news on September 5.

Concept note-3: -West Bengal has been accredited the International Travel Award 2023 for Best Destination for Culture by the Pacific Area Travel Writers Association (PATWA), an affiliate member of the United Nations World Tourism Organization (UNWTO).
