Which state became the first state in India to link its police posts in various districts with CCTNS.

(A) Odisha

(B) Haryana

(C) Bihar

(D) ** Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Crime and Criminal Tracking Network System is a project which was launched in the state of Himachal Pradesh in 2015. The aim is to connect 177 sites and 114 police stations in the state. Himachal Pradesh is the first state to link its police posts with Crime and Criminal Tracking Network and Systems.

Concept note-2: -Lord Cornwallis setup Police department in country to establish law and order.

Concept note-3: -Hence, Crime and Criminal Tracking Network and Systems (CCTNS) project, was started in 2009, with a total approved outlay of Rs 2000 Crore.
