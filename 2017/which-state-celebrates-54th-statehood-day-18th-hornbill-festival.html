Which state celebrates 54th Statehood Day, 18th Hornbill festival?

(A) ** Nagaland

(B) West Bengal

(C) Kerala

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rio said that the Hornbill festival is a symbol of Brand Naga land and the emerging Naga Soft Power.

Concept note-2: -The Festival is celebrated in Nagaland to encourage inter-tribal interaction and to preserve, protect and revive the uniqueness and richness of the Naga heritage. It is organized by State Tourism and Art & Culture Departments and also supported by Union Government.

Concept note-3: -Novaturient Managements presented Hornbill Fashion Night under the theme ‘Lieyie, fall/ winter 2022’ at The Heritage, Kohima on Tuesday. The word “Lieyie” is derived from the Angami Dialect meaning harvesting accolades.

Concept note-4: -10 (EMN): The 23rd edition of Hornbill Festival 2022 came to a close on Saturday after 10 days of activities with all the cultural troupes that participated in this year’s edition taking part in unity dance around a bonfire here at the Naga Heritage Village, Kisama.
