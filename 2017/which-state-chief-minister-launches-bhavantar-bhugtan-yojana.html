Which state Chief Minister Launches ’Bhavantar Bhugtan Yojana’?

(A) ** Madhya Pradesh

(B) Kerala

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The scheme was launched by the chief minister Shivraj Singh on 16 October 2017.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Uttar Pradesh. The state government has decided to set up at least one Farmer Producer Organization (FPO) in every block of Uttar Pradesh. Under this scheme, a total of 2, 725 will be formed.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Bhavantar Bharpayee Yojana. Key Points. Bhavantar Compensation Scheme-This scheme is a unique scheme of Haryana Government. The farmers of the state who sell their crops like vegetables, fruits etc.

Concept note-4: -The Bhavantar Bhugtan Yojana (BBY) was launched by the BJP government in October 2017 in the wake of unrest among farmers over falling prices. It was conceived after five protesting farmers died in police firing and one in custody in June 2017 near Mandsaur. Bhavantar literally means difference in rate.

Concept note-5: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which state government has launched Bhavantar Bhugtan Yojna (BBY) for farmers? Notes: Shivraj Singh Chouhan, the Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh, has launched the ambitious ‘Bhavantar Bhugtan Yojna’ for the farmers of the state at the Krishi Upaj Mandi in Khurai, Sagar.
