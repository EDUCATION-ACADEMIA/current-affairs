Which state did the Naveen Patnaik opened the longest bridge?

(A) Kerala

(B) Karnataka

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Cuttack. Odisha Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik on 20 December 2021 inaugurated the state’s longest bridge over Mahanadi river at Gopinathpur in Cuttack district.

Concept note-2: -Longest Bridge in Odisha-Mahanadi Barrage.

Concept note-3: -The Second Mahanadi Rail Bridge is a rail bridge over the Mahanadi near Cuttack in the Indian state of Odisha.

Concept note-4: -Longest bridge of cuttack-Mahanadi Barrage.

Concept note-5: -Dhola Sadiya Bridge, also known as Dr. Bhupen Hazarika Bridge, is the longest bridge in India that crosses a body of water. The Lohit River connects the Assamese villages of Dhola and Sadiya by this bridge, which is located in the state of Assam.
