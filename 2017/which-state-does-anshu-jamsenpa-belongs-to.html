Which state does Anshu Jamsenpa belongs to?

(A) Kerala

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) ** Arunachal Pradesh

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -She is from Bomdila, headquarters of West Kameng district, Arunachal Pradesh-the state that holds most north-eastern position of India. She was awarded India’s fourth highest civilian award the Padma Shri in 2021.

Concept note-2: -Daughter of an Indo Tibetan Border Police officer, Jamsenpa’s interest in mountaineering only grew post her marriage to Tsering Wange, one of the officials of Arunachal Pradesh Mountaineering and Adventure Sports Association, and the birth of her two daughters.

Concept note-3: -Famous Indian mountaineer Santosh Yadav achieved the feat to be the first woman in the world to climb Mount Everest twice and the first woman to successfully climb Mt. Everest from Kangshung Face.

Concept note-4: -Mount Everest is a peak in the Himalaya mountain range. It is located between Nepal and Tibet, an autonomous region of China. At 8, 849 meters (29, 032 feet), it is considered the tallest point on Earth.
