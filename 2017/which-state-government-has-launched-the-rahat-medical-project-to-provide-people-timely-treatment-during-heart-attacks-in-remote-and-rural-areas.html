Which state government has launched the RAHAT medical project to provide people timely treatment during heart attacks in remote and rural areas

(A) ** Rajasthan

(B) Odisha

(C) Bihar

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Rajasthan Heart Attack Treatment Program (RAHAT) was inaugurated on 25 February 2017 by Vasundhara Raje, the former Chief Minister of Rajasthan.

Concept note-2: -Begin CPR if the person is unconscious. Push hard and fast on the center of the person’s chest in a fairly rapid rhythm-about 100 to 120 compressions a minute. If an automated external defibrillator (AED) is immediately available and the person is unconscious, follow the device instructions for using it.

Concept note-3: -Key Facts. RAHAT project was launched by Eternal Heart Care Centre Hospital, a private hospital based in capital city Jaipur. It will provide emergency ambulance service and telemedicine service to the patients facing heart attack through the hospitals.

Concept note-4: -A heart attack is a medical emergency. Call 911 or the local emergency number if you think you or someone else is having a heart attack. The average person waits 3 hours before seeking help for symptoms of a heart attack. Many heart attack patients die before they reach a hospital.
