Which state government has signed a MoU with technology giant Microsoft to utilise Microsoft Azure Stack for its state data centre.

(A) ** Andhra Pradesh

(B) Odisha

(C) Bihar

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Microsoft To Set Up Rs 15, 000 Crore Data Centre In Hyderabad; Company’S Largest, Fourth In India.

Concept note-2: -Azure Government Cloud is Microsoft’s solution for government entities and those that work with them to protect information and maintain compliance. It offers all the features of Azure, plus enhanced security and data governance protocols to protect the sensitive data you work with.

Concept note-3: -KT Rama Rao, Minister Municipal Administration & Urban Development, Industries & Commerce, and Information Technology, Government of Telangana, said, “I am extremely delighted that Microsoft chose Hyderabad as the destination for its largest datacenter investment in India.

Concept note-4: -Azure Government is the mission-critical cloud, delivering breakthrough innovation to US government customers and their partners. Only US federal, state, local, and tribal governments and their partners have access to this dedicated instance, with operations controlled by screened US citizens.
