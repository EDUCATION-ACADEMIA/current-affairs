Which state Government launches Safe City Surveillance scheme for Women Safety?

(A) ** Bihar

(B) Kerala

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) West bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which state government has launched “Safe City Surveillance” scheme for checking crime against women at public places? Notes: The Bihar government has launched “Safe City Surveillance” scheme for checking crime against women at public places across the state.

Concept note-2: -The Bihar government has launched Safe City Surveillance Scheme for checking crime against women.

Concept note-3: -Under the Mukhya Mantri Kanya Suraksha Yojana, Government of Bihar contributes Rs. 2000/-for every Girl Child belonging to the BPL category and born on or after November 22, 2007. The benefit of the Yojana is limited to two girls per family falling below the poverty line.

Concept note-4: -The Safe City project aims to create a safe, secure and empowering environment for women in public places, to enable them to pursue all opportunities without the threat of gender-based violence and/or harassment.

Concept note-5: -In 2022, the Bihar state government will implement various social welfare initiatives, including the Bihar Student Credit Card Scheme and the Kushal Yuva Program, as part of the 7 Nischay of Bihar project.
