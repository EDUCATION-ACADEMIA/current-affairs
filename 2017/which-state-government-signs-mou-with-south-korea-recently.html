Which state Government Signs MoU with South Korea recently?

(A) ** Uttar Pradesh

(B) Bihar

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Uttar Pradesh. Uttar Pradesh has signed MoU with South Korea to enhance tourism cooperation. The Uttar Pradesh government signed a memorandum of understanding with South Korea to enhance cooperation in the field of tourism, skill development, culture and farming.

Concept note-2: -The Maharashtra government has signed a MoU with the British Council to strengthen educational and cultural cooperation.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is France. India and France have signed an MoU with scope for cooperation in health research. The MoU was signed on 25 January 2022 between the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, CSIR and Institut Pasteur, France.
