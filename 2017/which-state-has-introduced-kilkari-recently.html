Which state has introduced kilkari recently?

(A) Kerala

(B) Goa

(C) ** Haryana

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: The National Health Mission (NHM) in Haryana has recently launched a mobile-based application “Kilkari” to create awareness among pregnant women, parents and health workers on the importance of ante and post natal care, institutional delivery and immunisation.

Concept note-2: -Designed and launched originally in Bihar in 2013, Kilkari was adopted by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India, in 2014, and scaled up nationally.

Concept note-3: -Kilkari is a mobile health education service that provides pregnant women, new mothers, and their families with timely, accessible, accurate and relevant information about Reproductive, Maternal, Neonatal and Child health. It aims to improve families’ knowledge and uptake of life-saving preventative health practices.
