Which state has launched a new scheme Adarsh Gram Yojana for rural Poor

(A) Bihar

(B) Assam

(C) Odisha

(D) ** Arunachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY) was formally launched in 18 B.B village in Sri Ganganagar district of Rajasthan on 23 July 2010.

Concept note-2: -Under the scheme, Narendra Modi has adopted Jayapur village his constituency Varanasi in Uttar Pradesh. PM Modi has adopted 6 villages as of now.

Concept note-3: -To enable an area based development approach, a new scheme called the Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY) was launched on a Pilot basis during 2009-10, following the Finance Minister’s declaration in his Budget Speech delivered on 6.7. 2009.

Concept note-4: -’ Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) was launched on 11th October, 2014 with the aim to translate the comprehensive vision of Mahatma Gandhi about an ideal Indian village into a reality, keeping in view the present context.
