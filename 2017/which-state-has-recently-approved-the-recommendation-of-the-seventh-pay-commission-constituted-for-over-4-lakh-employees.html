Which state has recently approved the recommendation of the Seventh Pay Commission constituted for over 4 lakh employees.

(A) Haryana

(B) Odisha

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) ** Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The salary scale structure for Pay Level 4 starts at Rs 25, 500 and ends at Rs 81, 100 The salary scale structure for Pay Level 5 starts at Rs 29, 200 and ends at Rs 92, 300.

Concept note-2: -Recommended maximum pay for government employees: The Seventh Pay Commission also recommends to increase the maximum pay for government employees to Rs. 2.25 lakhs per month for Apex Scale and Rs. 2.5 lakhs per month for Cabinet Secretary and others working at the same level.

Concept note-3: -Govt of Karnataka Salary FAQs The average Govt of Karnataka salary ranges from approximately ₹1.5 Lakhs per year for a Computer Operator to ₹7.4 Lakhs per year for a Medical Officer. Salary estimates are based on 358 Govt of Karnataka salaries received from various employees of Govt of Karnataka.
