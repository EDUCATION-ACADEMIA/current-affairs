Which state has signed an agreement with Invest India to promote start-ups and MSME sector of the state

(A) Sikkim

(B) Punjab

(C) Goa

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SIDBI ties up with Bihar govt to promote MSME sector in state.

Concept note-2: -On behalf of the Odisha government, micro, small and medium enterprises (MSME) secretary LN Gupta and managing director of Invest India Deepak Bagla signed the deal to take forward the Odisha Startup and MSME initiatives.

Concept note-3: -The most awarded investment promotion agency in the world, Invest India is the advisor, guide, and facilitator to every investor looking to make a home in India.

Concept note-4: -Invest India is responsible for promoting and facilitating Investments to India. The shareholding currently is 51% FICCI and 49% DIPP. Subsequently DIPP will dilute its equity to include all State Governments.
