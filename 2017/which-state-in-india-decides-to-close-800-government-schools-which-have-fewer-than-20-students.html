Which state in India decides to close 800 government schools which have fewer than 20 students?

(A) ** Punjab

(B) Haryana

(C) Telangana

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Punjab government is focusing on closing down only those 800 primary schools that witnesses less than 20 students on the daily basis.

Concept note-2: -Nearly 20, 000 schools closed in India in 2021-22 due to Covid, staff strength also dropped: Ministry of Education report-The Hindu BusinessLine.

Concept note-3: -World School Rankings 2022 Delhi School A total of 5 Delhi state Government Schools are in the top 10 lists of the Education World School Rankings 2022 India. The top position has been won by the Delhi State government-run Rajkiya Pratibha Vikas Vidyalaya in Sector 10, Dwarka.
