Which state is Underwater ‘ghost nets’ posing threat to marine ecosystem?

(A) Karnataka

(B) ** Kerala

(C) Bihar

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The underwater Ghost nets are posing threats to marine ecosystem of Kerala coast.

Concept note-2: -Ghost nets often sink to the sea floor and can be found in sensitive habitats, such as coral reefs.

Concept note-3: -Ghost nets don’t only catch fish; they also entangle sea turtles, dolphins and porpoises, birds, sharks, seals, and more. These animals swim into nets, often unable to detect them by sight or sonar. The nets keep animals from moving freely, cause injuries, and keep mammals and birds from rising to the surface for air.

Concept note-4: -A ghost net is a fishing net that’s been lost or abandoned in the ocean . They are one particularly appalling part of the global ghost fishing problem, which includes fishing gear abandoned in the water. Any net or line left in the ocean can pose a threat to marine life.
