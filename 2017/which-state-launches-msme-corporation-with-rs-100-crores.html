(A) Karnataka

(B) Kerala

(C) ** Andhra Pradesh

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Today there are more than 8.5 lakh Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in Karnataka which employ over 55 lakh people.

Concept note-2: -MSME in Tamil Nadu There are 12.94 Lakh registered number of units in the State, providing an employment opportunities to about 80.81 Lakh persons.

Concept note-3: -As per data from the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises, as of November 25 2022, the Udyam Registration portal registered 12, 201, 448 MSMEs, replacing the former process of filing for a Udyog Aadhaar Memorandum (UAM).

Concept note-4: -MSMEs in Bihar consistently feed the domestic and the international value chain as manufacturers, suppliers, distributors, retailers, contractors and service provider by accounting for a substantial segment of our industrial units.
