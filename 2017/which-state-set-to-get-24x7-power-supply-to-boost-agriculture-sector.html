Which State set to get 24x7 power supply to boost agriculture sector?

(A) ** Telangana

(B) Chennai

(C) Jaipur

(D) Raipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This joint initiative of Government of India and Government of Gujarat aims to enhance the satisfaction levels of the consumers and improve the quality of life of people through 24x7 power supply at affordable cost.

Concept note-2: -Villages in just six states receive round-the-clock or 24-hour electricity supply, according to the data from the Power Ministry. Rural areas in Gujarat, Himachal Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, West Bengal and Telangana receive 24 hours of power supply.

Concept note-3: -Free power to agriculture is vogue in states like Andhra Pradesh (AP), Tamil Nadu, Telangana and Punjab.

Concept note-4: -Raja Sekhara Reddy. He is the only leader to provide free electricity to the agriculture sector. Green Andhra Pradesh is his vision.

Concept note-5: -Punjab is the most fertile state on earth. It is best to produce wheat, sugarcane, rice, vegetables, and fruits in Punjab. Punjab’s other name is the Granary of India and India’s breadbasket. Around 93% of the total productive land used to produce food grain.
