Which state signs MoU with South Korea and welcomes the investors?

(A) ** Andhra Pradesh

(B) Kerala

(C) Goa

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Uttar Pradesh. Uttar Pradesh has signed MoU with South Korea to enhance tourism cooperation.

Concept note-2: -Adani Group and South Korea’s POSCO have signed a MoU to establish a steel mill at Mundra in Gujarat. “POSCO and the Adani Group have agreed to explore business cooperation opportunities, including the establishment of a green, environment-friendly Integrated Steel Mill at Mundra, Gujarat, as well as other businesses.

Concept note-3: -On March 1, 1969, the Ministry of Unification was established as a government body responsible for all issues pertaining to inter-Korean relations and unification.

Concept note-4: -The Adani group is all set to try its fortunes in the steel sector. The company has tied up with South Korean steel major POSCO to explore opportunities, including setting up a steel mill in Gujarat.
