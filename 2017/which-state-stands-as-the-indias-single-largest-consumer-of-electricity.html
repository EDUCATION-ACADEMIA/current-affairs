Which state stands as the Indias single largest consumer of electricity?

(A) Kerala

(B) Goa

(C) ** Maharashtra

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -West Bengal is the top region by peak electricity demand in India. As of last month, peak electricity demand in West Bengal was 66, 739 megawatts that accounts for 25.74% of India’s peak electricity demand. The top 5 regions (others are Maharashtra, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, and Madhya Pradesh) account for 56.24% of it.

Concept note-2: -In India, power is generated from conventional (Thermal, Nuclear & Hydro) and renewable sources (Wind, Solar, Biomass etc.). However, Major production of Electricity is achieved through coal a thermal power plant which is around 75% of the total power generation.

Concept note-3: -The latest annual per capita consumption of electricity in Bihar is the lowest. It is 85% lower than the average Indian consumption. The highest per capita consumption of electricity is in Dadra and Nagar Haveli.

Concept note-4: -Dadra & Nagar Haveli (UT) is the top region by electricity consumption per capita in India.
