Which state to get Mega Coastal Economic Zone in India?

(A) ** Maharashtra

(B) Gujarat

(C) Karnataka

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Maharashtra. The Union Government has given go-ahead for setting up India’s first mega coastal economic zone (CEZ) at Jawaharlal Nehru Port (JNPT) in Maharashtra.

Concept note-2: -Tamil Nadu has the highest number of operational SEZs (40), followed by Karnataka (31) and Maharashtra (30). In this zone, industries get some tax advantages.

Concept note-3: -As of 31st January 2021; 265 SEZs are operational in the country. About 64% of the SEZs are located in five states – Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh and Maharashtra.

Concept note-4: -In November 2017, Union Government approved the establishment of country’s first mega coastal economic zone (CEZ) consists of 14 CEZs at Jawaharlal Nehru Port (JNPT) in Maharashtra. It will be the first of its kind initiative stretching along north Konkan region spread across Mumbai, Thane, Pune, Nashik, and Raigad.
