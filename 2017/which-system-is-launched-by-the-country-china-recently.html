Which system is launched by the country China recently?

(A) ** Floating solar farm

(B) Launch of Space Vehicle

(C) China National Space Administration (CNSA)

(D) Energy Storage Facility

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The China Aerospace Science and Technology Corporation (CASC), the country’s main space contractor and maker of the Long March rocket series, will again aim for more than 50 launches this year, according to an announcement from an early January meeting. China launched 64 times in 2022.

Concept note-2: -China on November 4, 2022 outlined plans to further expand the global reach of its home-grown Beidou satellite navigation system, billed as its alternative to America’s Global Positioning System (GPS).

Concept note-3: -China sends second Shiyan-10 test satellite sent into orbit with its final launch of 2022. HELSINKI-China has conducted its 64th and final launch of the year, sending the Shiyan-10 (02) into orbit from Xichang spaceport.

Concept note-4: -Of the 4, 852 active artificial satellites orbiting the Earth as of January 1, 2022, 2, 944 belong to the United States. This is by far the largest number of any single country, with their nearest competitor, China, accounting for only 499.
