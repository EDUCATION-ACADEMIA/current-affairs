Which team defeated did South Korea defeat and got 2017 Sudirman Cup?

(A) German

(B) Singapore

(C) Indonesia

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -South Korea shocked badminton giants China on Sunday in the final of the 2017 Sudirman Cup, ending the team’s run of six straight titles with a 3-2 win.

Concept note-2: -Korea defeated China 3–2 to win the tournament, which became the first Sudirman Cup title for Korea after 2003.

Concept note-3: -The Sudirman Cup is the World Mixed Team Championships which takes place every two years. The Sudirman Cup is a test of the all-round strength of a team. There are five matches in every tie: Men’s Singles, Women’s Singles, Men’s Doubles, Women’s Doubles and Mixed Doubles.

Concept note-4: -The Sudirman Cup is the World Team Championships, held every two years, and one of the most prestigious titles in the sport.
