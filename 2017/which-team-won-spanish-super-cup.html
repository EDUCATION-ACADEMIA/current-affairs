Which team won Spanish Super Cup?

(A) Zaragoza

(B) Sevilla

(C) Valencia

(D) ** Real Madrid

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Barcelona wins Spanish Super Cup after beating Real Madrid 3-1 in final hosted by Saudi Arabia. Barcelona players celebrate on the podium after winning the Spanish Super Cup final on January 15. Barcelona produced an impressive performance to beat Real Madrid 3-1 and lift the Spanish Super Cup on Sunday.

Concept note-2: -Barcelona have now won the Super Cup a record 14 times. Madrid have 12 to their name.

Concept note-3: -Real Madrid have won the trophy 13 times, in 1988, 1989, 1990, 1993, 1997, 2001, 2003, 2008, 2012, 2017, 2020 and 2022, while Barcelona have now won it 15 times.

Concept note-4: -Barcelona’s 13 Spanish Super Cup victories: 1983, 1991, 1992, 1994, 1996, 2005, 2006, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2013, 2016, 2018.

Concept note-5: -How many Spanish Super Cup titles do Real Madrid and Barcelona have? Barcelona are the most prolific Spanish Super Cup winner with 13 titles, closely followed by Real Madrid with 12.
