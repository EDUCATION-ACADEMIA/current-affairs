(A) ** Rohan Bopanna

(B) Vijay Amritraj

(C) Ankita Raina

(D) N.Shriram Balaji

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Krawczyk/Salisbury Win Roland Garros Mixed Doubles Title".

Concept note-2: -Marcelo Arévalo and Jean-Julien Rojer defeated Ivan Dodig and Austin Krajicek in the final, 6–7, 7–6, 6–3 to win the men’s doubles tennis title at the 2022 French Open. They saved three championship points en route to winning their first major title as a team and their third overall title together.

Concept note-3: -The first champions have been crowned at Wimbledon 2022, with Neal Skupski and Desirae Krawczyk defeating Matthew Ebden and Samantha Stosur 6-4, 6-3 to claim the mixed doubles title.

Concept note-4: -Two tennis greats-Gustavo Kuerten and Martina Navratilova-will present the trophies to the winners of the men’s and women’s singles finals of the French Open next weekend.
