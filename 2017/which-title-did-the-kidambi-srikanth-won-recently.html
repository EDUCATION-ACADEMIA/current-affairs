Which title did the Kidambi Srikanth won recently?

(A) ** Australia Open Super Series Title

(B) French open tennis title

(C) IBF World Champions

(D) England open Tennis Badminton

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kidambi reached the final of the men’s singles at the 2021 badminton world championships and is also the only Indian to win the men’s title in a Super Series Premier event on the BWF Tour.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Kidambi Srikanth. Kidambi Srikanth clinched a silver medal in the World Badminton Championships, Spain on 19 December 2021. He has become the first male shuttler to win silver at BWF Championships.

Concept note-3: -Saina Nehwal won a first-ever silver at the 2015 Championships, and then a bronze in 2017. P. V. Sindhu won silver in consecutive editions in 2017 and 2018. Sindhu then went on to win the gold at the 2019 BWF World Championships and become the first Indian to ever finish on top of the podium.
