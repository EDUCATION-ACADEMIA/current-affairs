(C) FIFA cup title

Concept note-1: -Paes received the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna award, India’s highest sporting honor, in 1996–97; the Arjuna Award in 1990; the Padma Shri award in 2001; and India’s third-highest civilian award, the Padma Bhushan prize in January 2014, for his outstanding contributions tennis.

Concept note-2: -Leander Paes won the bronze medal. Ranked 126 in the world at the time, he had entered the Atlanta Olympics as a wild card. In 1996, he was the only medalist for India, a country that finished tied 71st on the leaderboard. Paes was the first Indian, and Asian, tennis player to win an Olympic medal.

Concept note-3: -Nicknamed “Indian Express", tennis legends Mahesh Bhupathi and Leander Paes had a fallout in 2000. But, they never spoke clearly about what went wrong between them.
