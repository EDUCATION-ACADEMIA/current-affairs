Which title was won by the Tennis Player Rafael Nadal recently?

(A) Chile Open

(B) Brasil Open

(C) Queen’s Club Championships

(D) ** 2017 US Open title

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As such, Nadal broke his tie with Roger Federer and Novak Djokovic, and became the first man in history to win a total 21 (after winning the 2022 Australian Open), and 22 (after winning the 2022 French Open) Grand Slam singles titles.

Concept note-2: -This is the most for a single Grand Slam by any player in the history of the sport. Apart from his 14 French Open titles, Nadal has won the US Open four times, Wimbledon twice, and the Australian Open twice in 2009 and 2022.

Concept note-3: -French Open 2022 | Rafael Nadal beats Casper Ruud to win 14th French Open title; takes 22nd Grand Slam-The Hindu.

Concept note-4: -Wimbledon 2022: Rafael Nadal withdraws from tournament due to abdominal tear ahead of semifinal match. Rafael Nadal has withdrawn from Wimbledon ahead of his semifinal match against Nick Kyrgios, informing All England Club officials Thursday that he will not be able to play due to a seven-millimeter abdominal tear.
