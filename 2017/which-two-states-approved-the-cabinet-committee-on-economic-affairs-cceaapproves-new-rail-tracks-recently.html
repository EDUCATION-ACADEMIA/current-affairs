Which two states approved The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA)approves new rail tracks recently?

(A) ** Tamil nadu and Kerala

(B) Maharashtra and West Bengal

(C) Bihar and Odisha

(D) New Delhi and Arunachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Doubling of 160 km long Ramna – Singarauli railway line has been approved at a cost Rs. 2675.64 crore and is likely to be completed by 2019-20. The project will cover the districts of Garhwa in Jharkhand, Singrauli in Madhya Pradesh and Sonbhadra in Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA) is headed by Prime Minister of India. CCEA reviews economic conditions periodically. It takes important economic decisions on price control, FDI, Public Sector Undertakings etc.

Concept note-3: -3. Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs is chaired by the Prime Minister of India.

Concept note-4: -The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs chaired by Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has approved development of Multipurpose Cargo (other than Container/Liquid) Berth Off Tuna Tekra at Gulf of Kutch at Kandla on Build, Operate and Transfer (BOT) basis under Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode.
