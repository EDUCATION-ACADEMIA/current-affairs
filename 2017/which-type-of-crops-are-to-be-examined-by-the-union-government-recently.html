Which type of crops are to be examined by the Union Government recently?

(A) Horticulture

(B) Plant propagation

(C) Vertical Farming

(D) ** Genetically Modified

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution The Correct Answer is Minimum support price. The Minimum Support Price (MSP) for wheat was first declared in 1966-67 by the Government of India. It is a sort of guarantee of a minimum price for Indian agricultural produce.

Concept note-2: -For example, crop of wheat means that all the plants grown in a field are that of wheat. You already know that crops are of different types like cereals, vegetables and fruits. These can be classified on the basis of the season in which they grow.
