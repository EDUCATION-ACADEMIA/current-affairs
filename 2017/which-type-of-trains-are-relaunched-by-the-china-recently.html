Which type of trains are relaunched by the China recently?

(A) Short-distance trains

(B) Long Distance Trains

(C) ** Bullets Train

(D) Goods Train

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shanghai maglev is the world’s first commercial high-speed maglev, it has a cruising speed of 431 km/h (268 mph). It is the fastest commercial electric train and also the fastest overall operational rail network in the world .

Concept note-2: -Swiss supertrain. A late debut, which entered service in December 2022, Switzerland’s Golden Pass Express, or GPX as it’s known, connects Montreux, Gstaad and Interlaken, with special panoramic trains that change height and wheel gauge to slip between the varying lines.

Concept note-3: -China already has a running service for Maglev train in Shanghai that connects the main city with the airport. China has unveiled world’s fastest train maglev this is capable to attain the top speed to 600 km per hour.

Concept note-4: -As of August 2022, the fastest train on Earth, based on its record speed, is the Japanese L0 Series Maglev with a record speed of 603 kilometers per hour.
