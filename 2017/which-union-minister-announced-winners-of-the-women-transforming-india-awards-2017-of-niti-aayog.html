Which union minister announced Winners of the Women Transforming India Awards 2017 of NITI Aayog?

(A) Smt Sushma Swaraj

(B) ** Smt Smriti Irani

(C) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(D) Shri Naresh Iyer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. Aparna Hegde was recognised by the Women Entrepreneurship Platform and NITI Aayog for her exemplary work in maternal and child health in the 5th edition of the Women Transforming India Awards, 2021.

Concept note-2: -This year, as part of the Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav to celebrate 75 years of India’s independence, the WTI Awards will be conferred on 75 women achievers to celebrate their contribution towards a ‘Sashakt Aur Samarth Bharat’.

Concept note-3: -NITI Aayog’s Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP) organized the fifth edition of the Women Transforming India Awards (WTI). The WTI Awards 2021 were conferred upon 75 women achievers to celebrate their contribution towards a ‘Sashakt Aur Samarth Bharat’.
