Which Union Minister attends G-20 Finance Ministers Meeting recently?

(A) ** Shri Arun Jaitley

(B) Shri Prakash Javadekar

(C) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(D) Shri Naresh Iyer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -FM Nirmala Sitharaman participates in first G20 Finance Ministers, Central Bank Governors’ meeting under G20 Presidency of Indonesia.

Concept note-2: -The current Finance Minister of India is Nirmala Sitharaman.

Concept note-3: -The Fourth Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governor Meeting (FMCBG) was held on 12-13 October 2022, back-to-back with the 2022 Annual Meetings of the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank Group.

Concept note-4: -at #FICCINECM. Union Minister of Finance and Corporate Affairs Smt Nirmala Sitharaman, MoS Dr Bhagwat Kishanrao Karad, MoS Shri Pankaj Chaudhary and senior officials of the Ministry of Finance called on President Droupadi Murmu at Rashtrapati Bhavan before presenting the Union Budget 2023-24.

Concept note-5: -Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman has launched Union Budget Mobile App.
