CURRENT AFFAIRS-2017
[SOURCES]

Which Union Minister confirmed that 13, 872 villages electrified under rural scheme DDUGJY?

(A) Shri Suresh menon

(B) Shri Vivek Sharma

(C) Shri Nitin Gadkari

(D) ** Shri Piyush Goyal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“The erstwhile Rajiv Gandhi Grameen Vidyutikaran Yojana (RGGVY) scheme for village electrification and providing electricity distribution infrastructure in the rural areas has been subsumed in the DDUGJY scheme. Rural Electrification Corporation is the Nodal Agency for implementation of DDUGJY.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is option 4 i.e., Deendayal Upadhyay Gram Jyoti Yojana. Rajiv Gandhi Grameen Vidyutikaran Yojana has been replaced by “Deendayal Upadhyay Gram Jyoti Yojana".

Concept note-3: -Objectives. The Ministry of Power, Government of India has launched Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Gram Jyoti Yojana for rural areas with the following objectives: To provide electrification to all villages. Feeder separation to ensure sufficient power to farmers and regular supply to other consumers.
