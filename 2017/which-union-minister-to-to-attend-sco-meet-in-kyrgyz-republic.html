Which Union Minister to to attend SCO meet in Kyrgyz Republic?

(A) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(B) Shri Naresh Iyer

(C) Shri Narayana Swamy

(D) ** Shri Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi [India], November 18 (ANI): India has launched the official website of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) as it will host the next SCO summit as a chairman of organisation in 2023. The website highlights the events that will be undertaken under the chairmanship of India next year.

Concept note-2: -The SCO Secretary-General is elected to a three-year term. The current Secretary-General is Zhang Ming of China, who assumed his office on 1 January 2022.

Concept note-3: -Importance of SCO for India SCO allows India to deepen its strategic reach in Central Asia. India already has substantial soft power potential in Central Asia. India’s membership of SCO can boost energy security by providing access to mineral and energy resources of the Central Asian countries.
