Which union ministry Launched Two New Contraceptives Antara and Chhaya?

(A) Ministry of Home Affairs

(B) ** Ministry of Health and Family Affair

(C) Ministry of Finance

(D) Ministry of Defence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nadda, the Minister for Health and Family Welfare of the Government of India is the Vice Chair of the PPD Board of Directors. The two contraceptives Antara and Chhaya have been launched in the 10 states of Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Karnataka, Haryana, West Bengal, Odisha, Delhi and Goa.

Concept note-2: -Antara is injectable contraceptive, effective for three months drug whereas Chayya is contraceptive pill, effective for one week.

Concept note-3: -Chhaya is an oral contraceptive pill which does not contain any hormone. It is available in the market in some places as ‘Saheli’ tablet. It has been introduced in the public health system in the name of ‘Chhaya’ to benefit more women at no cost.

Concept note-4: -Assertion (A) – Reproductive and Child Health (RCH) program evolved from the family planning program. Reason (R) – Its only aim is to foresee health of the children.

Concept note-5: -Saheli is world’s first and only oral non steroidal contraceptive pill. ‘Saheli’ aka Centchroman (ormeloxifene 30mg) is the only non–steroidal pill with zero side effects available in the world.
