Which University in India decided to give gold medal only to the students who are vegetarian and non drinkers?

(A) ** Savitribai Phule Pune University

(B) Jawaharlal Nehru University

(C) Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

(D) Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -No matter how meritorius you are and no matter your how excellent your academic performance, you are not going to get a gold medal unless you are a vegetarian and a teetotaller. At least not in the Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU).

Concept note-2: -One Gold Medal shall be awarded to the best student among all students of all four faculties in the University at postgraduate level. 4. One Silver medal shall be awarded to the second best student among all the students of all the faculties at graduate as well as postgraduate level in the University. 5.

Concept note-3: -(a) A Gold Medal will be awarded to a student who has passed all the prescribed examination in first attempt forwarded that he/she has the highest marks but not less than 70% of the aggregate marks.

Concept note-4: -The conferral of a gold medal is considered one of the highest honors that a university can bestow on a student. The medal is typically made of gold or sterling silver and carries a monetary award with it.

Concept note-5: -A gold medal may be awarded to a student who has: Secured the highest CGPA in a degree program along with, qualifying following three conditions: Passed examination in each course of study in first attempt (i.e. students who improve D grades and repeat the courses of F grade will not be awarded with the medal).
