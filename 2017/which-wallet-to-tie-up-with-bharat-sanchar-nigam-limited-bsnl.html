Which wallet to tie up with Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL)?

(A) Paytm

(B) Freecharge

(C) ** MobiKwik

(D) MSwipe

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In partnership with MobiKwik, BSNL unveils its mobile wallet to enable one tap bill payments for its subscribers.

Concept note-2: -It’s easy! You just need to click on ‘Add Money’ button from “Available Balance” drop down menu. When enter the amount, you will be redirected to the ‘Payment Options’ page from where you can select various payment methods. How can I transfer my wallet balance of MobiKwik wallet to BSNL wallet ?

Concept note-3: -Most banks have their e-wallets and some private companies. e.g. Paytm, Freecharge, Mobikwik, Oxigen, mRuppee, Airtel Money, Jio Money, SBI Buddy, itz Cash, Citrus Pay, Vodafone M-Pesa, Axis Bank Lime, ICICI Pockets, SpeedPay etc.

Concept note-4: -Tech Mahindra launched India’s first contactless digital payment system named MoboMoney. It is a prepaid wallet which uses NFC technology. Q. Which bank has launched the country’s first contactless debit and credit cards?
