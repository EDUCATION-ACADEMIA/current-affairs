Who appoints Commission to examine sub-categorisation of Other Backward Classes?

(A) ** President Ram Nath Govind

(B) Prime Minister Narendra Modi

(C) Shri Arun Jaitley

(D) Shri Vijay Prakash

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(3) The Chairperson, Vice-Chairperson and other Members of the Commission shall be appointed by the President by warrant under his hand and seal.

Concept note-2: -Shri. Hansraj Gangaram Ahir assumed charge as Chairperson, National Commission for Backward Classes here today.

Concept note-3: -Complete answer: The second backward class committee was appointed on 1st Jan 1979 by the government of India. It was established by the Janata Party government under Prime Minister Morarji Desai with a mandate to identify the socially or educationally backward classes of India.

Concept note-4: -National Commission for Backward Classes (NCBC) was initially constituted by the Central Govt by the National Commission for Backward Classes Act, 1993 (27 of 1993) dated 2.4. 1993 and so far the Commission had been reconstituted 7 times up to 2016.
