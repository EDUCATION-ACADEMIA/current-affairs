Who appoints Deputy Governor of the Reserve Bank of India?

(A) ** Government of India

(B) Reserve Bank Governor

(C) President of India

(D) Prime Minister of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Members appointed by the Central Government.

Concept note-2: -Who appointed the RBI governor? RBI governor appointed Under Section 8 of the RBI Act 1934, the central government has the power to appoint Governors and Deputy Governors to the Reserve Bank of India.

Concept note-3: -The Governor and the Deputy Governors of the Reserve Bank of India are appointed by the Central Government.

Concept note-4: -The six-member Monetary Policy Committee is headed by RBI Governor Shaktikanta Das. Besides the governor, the RBI has four deputy governors. The three other deputy governors of the bank are M K Jain, M Rajeshwar Rao and T Rabi Sankar.
