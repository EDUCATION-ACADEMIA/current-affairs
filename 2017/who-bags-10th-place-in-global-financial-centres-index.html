(A) ** Gujarat International Finance Tec-City (GIFT City)

(B) London International Finance Tec-City

(C) Mumbai International Finance Tec-City

(D) Singapore International Finance Tec-City

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which city topped the Global Financial Centres Index (GFCI) in 2022? Notes: New York topped latest rankings from the Global Financial Centres Index (GFCI) and remained as the world’s most favoured financial centre for fourth year.

Concept note-2: -Hong Kong, Shanghai, Beijing and Shenzhen are among the top 10 performers. Guangzhou, Chengdu and Qingdao are among the top 50 global financial centres. In India, New Delhi and Mumbai are ranked 68th and 70th ranks respectively.

Concept note-3: -Leading financial centers worldwide 2022 New York had a rating of with 759 points during that time, followed by London, with 726 points.
