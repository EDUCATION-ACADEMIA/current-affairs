Who became the fastest cricketer to score 1000 runs as ODI captain

(A) AB de Villiers

(B) Kane Williamson

(C) ** Virat Kohli

(D) Alastair Cook

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pakistan skipper Babar Azam, on Wednesday, became the fastest skipper in the history of ODI cricket to notch up 1000 runs. Babar completed the record against West Indies in the opening match at Multan Cricket Stadium.

Concept note-2: -Babar Azam surpassed the long-standing record of Virat Kohli, becoming the fastest to score 1, 000 runs as captain in ODIs.

Concept note-3: -Shubman Gill on Wednesday surpasses records set by Virat Kohli and Shikhar Dhawan to become the fastest Indian batsman to score 1000 runs. 23-year-old Gill, who made his ODI debut in January 2019, had completed 1000 runs in 19 innings.
