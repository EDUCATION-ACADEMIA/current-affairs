Who became the first ever Swimmer to win three gold medals in one night at worlds Championship?

(A) Mark Spitz

(B) Matt Biondi

(C) Michael Phelps

(D) ** Caeleb Dressel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mark Spitz, considered the swiftest swimmer of all time, made his big splash during the 1972 Olympics, becoming the first athlete to win seven gold medals in an Olympiad. His performances were even more remarkable considering world records were set in all seven events.

Concept note-2: -Caeleb Dressel is a two-time Olympian, 7x Olympic champion, and thirteen-time World Champion in competitive swimming. At the 2017 World Championships Dressel won seven gold medals to tie Michael Phelps for the most won at a World Championship meet.

Concept note-3: -At the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Dressel won his first Olympic medal, a gold medal, in the 4×100 meter freestyle relay.
