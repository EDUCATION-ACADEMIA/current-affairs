Who became the first Indian to be promoted by the International Basketball Federation (FIBA) as the Technical Delegate (TD)

(A) Geethu Anna Jose

(B) Jagdeep Singh

(C) Ram Kumar

(D) ** Norman Isaac

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Norman Isaac has become the first Indian ever to be promoted by the International Basketball Federation (FIBA) as Technical Delegate (TD).

Concept note-2: -Basketball was brought to India in 1893–4 by Canadian T. Duncan Patton, who later returned to India as the Acting General Secretary of the Calcutta YMCA from 1903–5. The game was initially most popular among women, although slow to spread.

Concept note-3: -The abbreviation FIBA, was originally derived from the french term Federation Internationale de Basketball Amateur.

Concept note-4: -The Basketball Federation of India (BFI) is the governing and controlling body of basketball in India. It is responsible for the development and promotion of the sports authority of India at all levels.
