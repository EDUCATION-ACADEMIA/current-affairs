Who became the second asian to play 1000 matches across all forms of Cricket

(A) Shahid Afridi

(B) Rahul Dravid

(C) Shakib Al Hasan

(D) ** Kumar Sangakkara

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian cricket team, with 1000 matches, holds the record for most ODIs played by a team. They achieved the milestone in the first ODI match against the West Indies at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad on February 6, 2022. On the momentous occasion, India beat West Indies by six wickets.

Concept note-2: -Cricket Legend Virat Kohli has become the first Indian and just the second player overall in international cricket history to have played 100 matches each in all three formats of the game.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is India. The Indian cricket team created history on February 06 by becoming the first side in the world to complete the milestone of 1, 000 ODIs. The team has two ODI World Cups and a T20 World Cup to their name.

Concept note-4: -India on Sunday became the first team to play 1000 One-Day Internationals. The side achieved the feat in the ongoing first ODI against West Indies here at the Narendra Modi Stadium. In the 999 ODIs played earlier, India accounts for 518 wins and 431 losses.
