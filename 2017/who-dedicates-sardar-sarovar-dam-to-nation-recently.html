Who dedicates Sardar Sarovar Dam to nation recently?

(A) Shri Arun Jaitley

(B) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(C) Shri Naresh Iyer

(D) ** Shri Narendra Modi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PM Modi dedicates Sardar Sarovar dam to nation on his birthday.

Concept note-2: -Modi dedicated the Sardar Sarovar Dam to the people of India on Sunday. The project was an “engineering miracle” and a “symbol of India’s new and emerging power”, he said. The dam’s inauguration coincided with Modi’s 67th birthday.

Concept note-3: -The project took form in 1979 as part of a development scheme funded by the World Bank through their International Bank for Reconstruction and Development, to increase irrigation and produce hydroelectricity, using a loan of US$200 million.

Concept note-4: -Current Reservoir Level: Western: Gujarat: Sardar Sarovar data is updated daily, averaging 121.130 m from Apr 2015 to 09 Feb 2023, with 409 observations. The data reached an all-time high of 138.680 m in 15 Sep 2022 and a record low of 104.410 m in 03 May 2018.
