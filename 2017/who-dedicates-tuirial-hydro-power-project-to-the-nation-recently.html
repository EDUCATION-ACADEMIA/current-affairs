Who Dedicates Tuirial Hydro Power Project to the Nation recently?

(A) ** Prime Minister Narendra Modi

(B) Shri Desmi Khan

(C) Shri Ramesh krishnan

(D) Shri Vignesh Sharan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tuirial Hydro Power Station is medium head storage scheme to harness the hydro power of Tuirial River and located at Saipum, Kolasib District of Mizoram. The installed capacity of the Station is 60 MW with a catchment area of 1861 Sq.

Concept note-2: -The first hydroelectric power plant in India was installed in Darjeeling, West Bengal. A project with a capacity of 130 kW installed at Sidrapong (Darjeeling) in the year 1897. This hydroelectric power plant was also the first hydroelectric power plant in Asia.

Concept note-3: -The Koyna Hydroelectric Project is the largest hydroelectric power plant in India. It is a complex project with four dams including the largest dam on the Koyna River, Maharashtra hence the name Koyna Hydroelectric Project.

Concept note-4: -India’s first hydroelectric power station with 2 × 65 kW capacity was commissioned on 10 November 1897 by Sir C. C. Stevens, the Acting Lieutenant Governor of Bengal.
