Who develops Humraaz app for soldiers to track Promotions and Postings recently?

(A) ** Indian Army

(B) Indian Navy

(C) Indian Police

(D) Indian Airforce

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This App has been developed exclusively for serving soldiers of the Indian Army by the technical team of Army Soldiers (Adjutant Generals Branch (MP-8)) for communication of their service and pay-related information to them on their mobile phones. It was launched in August 2017.

Concept note-2: -Developed by the Indian Army, the original ARMAAN app is used for information decimation, messaging, military engineering services related complaints, army rest house related services, and many others. It also has a feature that allows army members to send messages to the office of the Chief of Army Staff (COAS).

Concept note-3: -The Indian Army has launched a contemporary messaging application named ASIGMA (Army Secure IndiGeneous Messaging Application) .

Concept note-4: -About ASIGMA App ASIGMA app has been sent out on Army-owned hardware. It will cater to the army from this time forward and lending itself to lifetime support with future upgrades. It is designed to meet all futuristic requirements for Indian army and boasts of an enhanced user experience.

Concept note-5: -To install Hamraaz App in your mobile, you need to click on the downloaded Hamraaz APK File. After Click on Hamraaz APK file, a new page opens with option of Install and Cancel Buttons. Now you need to click on Install Button to Install the App in your Mobile Phone.
