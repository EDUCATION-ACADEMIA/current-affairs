(A) ** President Donald Trump

(B) Prime Minister Narendra Modi

(C) Shri Arun Jailtey

(D) John Williams

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first Medal of Honor presentation for Vietnam was to Captain Roger Donlon for actions on 6 July 1964 as commanding officer of the U.S. Army Special Forces Detachment defending Camp Nam Dong against a Viet Cong attack.

Concept note-2: -5th Class Clarence Sasser was a part-time college student when he was drafted into the Army as a medic. He was sent to Vietnam at the height of the war and did his job so fearlessly during battle that he earned the Medal of Honor.

Concept note-3: -This Special Forces Medic Earned the Medal of Honor by Saving 60 Lives in Vietnam. When Gary Rose was 19, he joined the Army, but it was the Army that chose Gary Rose for Special Forces training.
