Who got the 50th International World Fest award from India

(A) ** Shabana Azmi

(B) Nazim begam

(C) Vidya balan

(D) Nithya ramkumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thespian Rajinikanth was conferred the ICON of Golden Jubilee of IFFI award.

Concept note-2: -John Bailey will chair the International Jury of 50th International Film Festival of India.

Concept note-3: -The 53rd edition of International Film Festival of India (IFFI) held its coveted award ceremony on the closing day of the festival on Monday in Goa. While Spanish coming-of-age drama I Have Electric Dreams won the Golden Peacock award, Vahid Mobasheri and Daniela Marin Navarro took home the top acting honours.

Concept note-4: -Here are the winners of the film festival: No End-Nader Saeivar, Best Director. Best Male Actor-Vahid Mobasheri, No End. Best Female Actress-Daniela Marin Navarro, I Have Electric Dreams. Behind the Haystacks by Asimina Proedrou, is the Best Debut Feature Film by a new Director.
