Who got the highest wicket taker in Women’s Cricket ODI?

(A) Anjum Chopra

(B) Amita Sharma

(C) ** Jhulan Goswami

(D) Gouher Sultana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Who has taken the most wickets in women’s ODI? Jhulan Goswami has taken the most wickets in the women’s ODI (255). Who is the only bowler to take 200 wickets in women’s ODI? Jhulan Goswami is the only player to take 200 wickets in women’s ODI.

Concept note-2: -Jhulan Goswami is the leading wicket-taker in WODIs.

Concept note-3: -As of January 2023, the leading wicket-taker of all time in One Day Internationals is Sri Lanka’s Muttiah Muralitharan. The right-arm spin bowler took 534 wickets in an ODI career spanning from 1993 to 2011.

Concept note-4: -Leading female ODI cricket run-scorers 2023 As of September 2022, Mithali Raj tops the list of all-time run scorers in women’s ODIs, having amassed 7, 805 in her career between 1999 and 2022.
