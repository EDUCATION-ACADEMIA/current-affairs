Who hails Real-time Governance module of ChandraBabu Naidu?

(A) ** Kiran Mazumdar Shaw

(B) Naresh Iyer

(C) Vignesh Sharath

(D) Selvaraman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Real Time Governance Society (RTGS) is an Indian e-governance initiative of chief minister N. Chandrababu Naidu from Andhra Pradesh, India. It was formed on 6 September 2017. The department reports to the chief minister.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Prajavani. Prajavani e-governance initiative in Andhra Pradesh is a public grievance redressal system. Prajavani is a unique public-private partnership program, which gives citizens an opportunity to interact with the government without coming to any government office.
