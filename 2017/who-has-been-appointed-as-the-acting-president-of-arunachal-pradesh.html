Who has been appointed as the acting president of Arunachal Pradesh

(A) Najma Heptulla

(B) E. S. L. Narasimhan

(C) P. Sathasivam

(D) ** P B Acharya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -B. D. Mishra is a former brigadier of the Indian Army. He is the present governor of Arunachal Pradesh. and also has additional charge of Governor of Meghalaya.

Concept note-2: -The governor of Arunachal Pradesh is a nominal head and representative of the president of India in the state of Arunachal Pradesh. The governor is appointed by the president for a term of five years. The current governor is Kaiwalya Trivikram Parnaik.

Concept note-3: -Brigadier B. D. Mishra (born 20 July 1939) is an Indian politician and former brigadier of the Indian Army. In October 2017, Governor of Arunachal Pradesh.
