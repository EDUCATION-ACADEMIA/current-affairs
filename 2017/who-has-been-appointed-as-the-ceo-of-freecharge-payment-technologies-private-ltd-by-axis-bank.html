Who has been appointed as the CEO of Freecharge Payment Technologies Private Ltd, by Axis Bank?

(A) Shikha Sharma

(B) Rajiv Anand

(C) ** Sangram Singh

(D) Sanjiv Misra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Axis Bank Completes Acquisition Of Digital Payments Platform Freecharge, Names Sangram Singh As CEO.

Concept note-2: -We would like to expand merchant network to almost 10 million in 3 years: Siddharth Mehta, MD & CEO, Freecharge.

Concept note-3: -Axis Bank acquired Freecharge from Snapdeal in a Rs 385 crore ($60 million) deal in July 2017. Before that, Freecharge’s original founders Kunal Shah and Sandeep Tandon sold the wallet platform to Snapdeal for about Rs 3, 000 crore ($400 million) in April 2015.

Concept note-4: -Axis Bank acquired an 100% shareholding of Freecharge for Rs. 385 crores outside its main line of business with an expectation to help the bank take a leap towards digital distribution of financial products to provide its customers in a more efficient manner.

Concept note-5: -FreeCharge, a wholly owned subsidiary of Axis Bank Limited, is India’s No. 1 payments app. Customers across the country use FreeCharge to make prepaid, postpaid, DTH, metro recharge and utility bill payments for numerous service providers.
