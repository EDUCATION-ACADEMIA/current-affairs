Who has been appointed as the Chairman of Vodafone-Idea merged entity

(A) Tarjani Vakil

(B) Vittorio Colao

(C) Gerard Kleisterlee

(D) ** Kumar Mangalam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Idea-vodafone merger: Kumar Mangalam Birla to head India’s largest telecom company.

Concept note-2: -Ravinder Takkar takes over as Chairman of Vodafone Idea.

Concept note-3: -Akshaya Moondra to be next Vodafone Idea CEO; Ravinder Takkar becomes non-executive director.

Concept note-4: -"By combining our businesses, Vodafone UK and Three UK will gain the necessary scale to be able to accelerate the rollout of full 5G in the UK and expand broadband connectivity to rural communities and small businesses, ” Vodafone said in a statement at the time.

Concept note-5: -Ravinder Takkar, the managing director (MD) and chief executive officer (CEO) of telecom operator Vodafone Idea Limited (Vi), will replace Himanshu Kapania as the telco’s new chairman.
