Who has been appointed as the full-time interim Director General (IDG) of the International Solar Alliance (ISA).

(A) Ajay Singh

(B) Ram Varma

(C) Rajesh Kumar

(D) ** Upendra Tripathy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: Former New and Renewable Energy Secretary Upendra Tripathy has been appointed as the full-time interim Director General of International Solar Alliance.

Concept note-2: -Talking to reporters in New Delhi yesterday, ISA Director General Ajay Mathur said, Union Power and New and Renewable Energy Minister R K Singh has been re-elected as the President of International Solar Alliance.

Concept note-3: -The Fifth Assembly of the International Solar Alliance was inaugurated today by Shri Raj Kumar Singh, Minister of Power and New & Renewable Energy, India in his capacity as the President, International Solar Alliance.

Concept note-4: -Antigua and Barbuda joined ISA as the 102nd member 2022.
