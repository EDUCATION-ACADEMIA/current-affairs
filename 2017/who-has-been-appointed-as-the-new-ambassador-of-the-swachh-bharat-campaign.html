Who has been appointed as the new ambassador of the Swachh Bharat Campaign.

(A) Priyanka Chopra

(B) Aishwarya Rai

(C) Deepika Padukone

(D) ** Anushka Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -From later dates the following public icons & celebrities were nominated as National Brand Ambassadors by Prime Minister Modi to join and support the Swachh Bharat Mission: Shilpa Shetty (Actress), from February 2017. Raveena Tandon (Actress), from February 2017. Sanjay Dutt (Actor), from 2018.

Concept note-2: -Daljit Singh, a dedicated social worker of Sahnewal town, has been appointed brand ambassador for Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban) and Swachh Survekshan 2023 for Sahnewal. He has been entrusted with this responsibility for his contribution towards society.

Concept note-3: -Shilpa Shetty was appointed as the new Brand Ambassador for Swachh Bharat Mission in 2017.

Concept note-4: -The ‘#MyCleanIndia’ was also launched simultaneously as a part of the Swachh Bharat drive to highlight the cleanliness work carried out by citizens across the nation. Swachh Bharat Abhiyan has become a ‘Jan Andolan’ receiving tremendous support from the people.
