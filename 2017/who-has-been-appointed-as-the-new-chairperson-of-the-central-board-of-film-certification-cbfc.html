Who has been appointed as the new Chairperson of the Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC)?

(A) Shravan Kumar

(B) Anurag Srivastava

(C) Pahlaj Nihalani

(D) ** Prasoon Joshi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prasoon Joshi chairs the board; Joshi became its 28th chairperson on 11 August 2017, after Pahlaj Nihalani was fired.

Concept note-2: -Censor Board Chairman Pahlaj Nihalani on Friday welcomed the Information and Broadcasting Ministry’s decision to form a panel, to be headed by veteran filmmaker Shyam Benegal, for revamping of the board.

Concept note-3: -In 1952, it was reconstituted as the Central Board of Film Censors. In 1983, the name was changed to Central Board of Film Certification.

Concept note-4: -Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) is a statutory body under Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, regulating the public exhibition of films under the provisions of the Cinematograph Act 1952.
