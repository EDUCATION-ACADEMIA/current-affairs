Who has been appointed by United Nations to head the World Food Programme (WFP), recently?

(A) James T. Morris

(B) James Ingram

(C) ** David Beasley

(D) Josette Sheeran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -David Beasley is new head of WFP.

Concept note-2: -WFP has been working in India since 1963, with work transitioning from food distribution to technical assistance since the country achieved self-sufficiency in cereal production.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Odisha. The United Nations World Food Programme (WFP) and the Odisha government have joined hands. They will improve the food security of small and marginal farmers by strengthening their resilience to climate change.

Concept note-4: -WFP has more than 14, 000 staff worldwide of whom over 90 percent are based in the countries where the agency provides assistance.
