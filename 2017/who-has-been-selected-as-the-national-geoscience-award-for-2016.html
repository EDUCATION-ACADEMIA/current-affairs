Who has been selected as the National Geoscience Award for 2016

(A) Ashok Kumar

(B) Muduru Lachhana Dora

(C) Biswajeet Lenka

(D) ** Keshav Krishna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rath is a recipient of the prestigious National Geosciences Award 2016 from the President of India. He has co-authored a book titled ‘Underground Storage Technologies’ and has published several technical papers and participated in conferences in India and overseas.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of Mines invites nominations for the National Geoscience Awards-2022 for contributions in the field of fundamental/applied geosciences, mining, and allied areas. The awards, instituted in 1966 and given annually, are an initiative of the Ministry to encourage geoscientists for striving toward excellence.
