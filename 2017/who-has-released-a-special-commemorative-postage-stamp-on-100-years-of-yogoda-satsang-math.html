Who has released a special commemorative postage stamp on 100 years of Yogoda Satsang Math.

(A) ** Narendra Modi

(B) Pranab Mukherjee

(C) Rajnath Singh

(D) Arun Jaitley

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The occasion was the release of a postage stamp by the Government of India commemorating the 100th anniversary of Yogoda Satsanga Society. The date was chosen to honour the anniversary of Paramahansaji’s mahasamadhi, which took place on March 7, 1952.

Concept note-2: -Minister of Communications and Information Technology approves issue of Commemorative Postage Stamps by the Department of Posts, Govt. of India on the recommendations of the Philatelic Advisory Committee (PAC)/Sub-Committee of the PAC.

Concept note-3: -The First Stamp of Independent India was issued on 21 November 1947. It depicts the Indian Flag with the patriots’ slogan, Jai Hind (Long Live India), on the top right hand corner. It was valued at three and one-half annas. A memorial to Mahatma Gandhi was issued 15 August 1948 on the first anniversary of Independence.

Concept note-4: -The stamp features a profile of Queen Victoria. distance. February 1901.

Concept note-5: -In 1893, the first U.S. commemorative stamps, honoring that year’s World Columbian Exposition in Chicago, were issued. The subject-Columbus’s voyages to the New World-and size of the stamps were innovative.
