Who has taken oath as the Lieutenant Governor of Delhi

(A) Farooq Khan

(B) Kiran Bedi

(C) Mridula Sinha

(D) ** Anil Baijal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prior to his appointment as the LG of Delhi, Shri Saxena served as the Chairman of Khadi & Village Industries Commission (KVIC) from October 2015 to May 2022.

Concept note-2: -A 1995 batch IAS officer of AGMUT cadre Chandra Bhushan Kumar has been appointed as Principal Secretary to Lieutenant Governor of Delhi, Vinai Kumar Saxena. Surendra Singh, a 2005 batch UP cadre officer has been appointed as secretary to Lt Governor.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is option 4 i.e. Aditya Nath Jha. Aditya Nath Jha was of the Indian Civil Service(ICS) from 1936 batch.
