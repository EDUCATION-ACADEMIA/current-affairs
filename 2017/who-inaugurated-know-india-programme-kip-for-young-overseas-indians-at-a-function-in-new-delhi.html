Who inaugurated Know India Programme (KIP) for young overseas Indians at a function in New Delhi?

(A) Smt Nithya Menon

(B) Smt Smriti Irani

(C) ** Smt Sushma Swaraj

(D) Smt Vidya Shree

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Know India Programme (KIP) is a flagship initiative for Diaspora engagement which familiarizes Indian-origin youth (18-30 years) with their Indian roots and contemporary India, through a three-week orientation programme organised by the Ministry.

Concept note-2: -The Know India Programme (KIP) is a kind of an Orientation Programme expected to provide the participants exposure to various facets of the Indian way of life, culture, spirituality, adventure and sports, creativity and composite character of India and interaction with youth from different parts of the country.

Concept note-3: -The Know India Programme (KIP) is a flagship initiative of Government of India organised by the Ministry of External Affairs, which facilitates travel of youth in the Indian diaspora (between 18 – 30years) to India, that includes a 25-day orientation programme in various cities in India.
