Who inaugurated Patanjali institute in haridwar?

(A) ** PM Narendra Modi

(B) Mr Suresh Prabhu

(C) Mr Kamal dev

(D) Mr Suresh Cheran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi, who is on a visit to Uttarakhand today, inaugurated Baba Ramdev’s Patanjali Research Institute in Haridwar too.

Concept note-2: -Patanjali Yogpeeth, which was inaugurated in 2006 is located in Haridwar, North India. It was founded by Swami Ramdev and Acharya Balkrishna. The trust aims to increase the propagation of yoga and Ayurveda as methods of health promotion and healing.

Concept note-3: -Patanjali Yogpeeth is a yoga institute located in Haridwar, Uttarakhand, India. Founded in 2006 and named after the Rishi Patanjali, the purpose of the institute is to practice, research, and develop yoga and ayurveda. The institute is the flagship project of the yoga teacher and entrepreneur Ramdev.

Concept note-4: -Swami Shankar Dev is a spiritual personality. He is guru of world known Yogrishi Swami Ramdev Ji Maharaj. Swami Ramdev ji Maharaj took sanyas diksha from Swami ji in 1995.

Concept note-5: -Acharyakulam is a Co-Ed school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) . It is managed by Patanjali Yogpeeth Trust.
