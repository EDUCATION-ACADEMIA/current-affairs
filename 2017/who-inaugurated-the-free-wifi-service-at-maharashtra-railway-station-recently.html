Who inaugurated the free wifi service at Maharashtra railway station recently?

(A) Shri Arun Jaitley

(B) Shri Shweta Menon

(C) ** Shri Suresh Prabhu

(D) Shri Kannan Devan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai, May 22: Giving a major fillip to government’s Digital India Initiative, Union Railway Minister Suresh Prabhu on Sunday inaugurated free Wi-Fi facility at 28 railway stations on Konkan Railway .

Concept note-2: -This initiative first began in the year 2016 at the Mumbai Railway station.

Concept note-3: -This will help all the rail passengers and also promote digital literacy in the country. Here’s how you can access Wi-Fi at various railway stations for free: Step 1: Open the Wi-Fi settings in your smartphone and select RailWire network. Step 2: Open the internet browser and type railwire.co.in in the web address bar.

Concept note-4: -The Railways began the service of providing free WiFi at its stations from January 2016 from Mumbai Central station, and in a span of 46 months RailTel has extended the service to 5, 500 stations across the country. “The mission is to provide WiFi at all railway stations (except the halt ones).
