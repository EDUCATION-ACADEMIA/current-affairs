Who introduced Aadhar two policies recently?

(A) ** LIC

(B) The New India Assurance

(C) Birla Sun life Insurance

(D) United India Insurance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Bill, 2016 was introduced by Minister of Finance, Mr. Arun Jaitley, in Lok Sabha on March 3, 2016.

Concept note-2: -2009–2013. The UIDAI was established on 28 January 2009 after the Planning Commission issued a notification. On 23 June Nandan Nilekani, the co-founder of Infosys, was appointed by the then-government, UPA, to head the project.

Concept note-3: -Earlier in October 2022, the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) urged the citizens of the country to update their identification and residence proof documents, if they were issued the unique ID more than 10 years back, however, have not updated their details since then.

Concept note-4: -Aadhaar Card Update 2022: Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) has issued new guidelines to update the Aadhaar card. Now you no longer need supporting documents to update your Aadhaar card. Aadhaar is a 12-digit unique identity number that serves as the primary identification system in India.
