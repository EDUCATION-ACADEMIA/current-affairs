Who is appointed as new National Investigation Agency?

(A) Shri Ramesh Kumar

(B) Shri Rajesh Sharma

(C) Shri Naresh Singh

(D) ** Shri YC Modi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) appointed former Punjab Director General of Police (DGP) Dinkar Gupta as the Director General of the National Investigation Agency (NIA).

Concept note-2: -Former Punjab DGP Dinkar Gupta was on Thursday appointed as the new Director General of the National Investigation Agency (NIA), an anti-terror probe agency formed after the 26/11 attack in Mumbai.

Concept note-3: -The first director of NIA was Radha Vinod Raju. NIA is the National Investigation Agency that was formed in 2008 by the Parliament of India on 31 December 2008, with Radha Vinod Raju being its Director General who served until 31 January 2010.

Concept note-4: -National Investigation Agency Government of India-NIA.
