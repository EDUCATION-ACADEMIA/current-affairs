Who is appointed as the Deputy Chief of Integrated Defence Staff (IDS)?

(A) ** Shri Air Marshal PN Pradhan

(B) Shri Narayan Swamy

(C) Shri Ramesh gupta

(D) Shri Kamlesh Nathan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -VICE ADMIRAL SANJAY J SINGH, AVSM, NM.

Concept note-2: -AIR MARSHAL BR KRISHNA PVSM AVSM SC ADC CHIEF OF INTEGRATED DEFENCE STAFF TO THE CHAIRMAN, COSC.

Concept note-3: -The branches are headed by Deputy Chiefs of Integrated Defence staff (DCIDS) who are the rank of Lt Gen equivalence, the Division are headed by Assistant Chiefs of integrated Defence Staff (ACIDS) who are of Maj Gen Equivalence and Dtes are headed by Deputy Assistant Chief of Integrated Defence Staff (DACIDS).

Concept note-4: -✅ Integrated Defence Staff Educational Qualifications: (1) Bachelor of Arts (BA) or Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) with Mathematics and Statistics as main subject. (2) 03 years experience of collection, compilation, analysis and interpretation of statistical data. ✅ Selection Process: Interview.
