Who is appointed as the Joint Secretary in Prime Minister Office?

(A) ** Gopal Baglay

(B) Suresh Yadav

(C) Kamal Singh

(D) Vijay yadav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The PMO is headed by the Principal Secretary, currently Pramod Kumar Misra.

Concept note-2: -Joint secretary is the overall in charge with the necessary measure of independent functioning and responsibility of the wing of the department allocated and entrusted to him.

Concept note-3: -Hardik Satishchandra Shah is an Indian civil servant as a member of Indian Administrative Service of Gujarat Cadre and is currently serving as the Private Secretary to the Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi.
