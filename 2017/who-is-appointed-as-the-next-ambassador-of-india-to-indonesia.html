Who is appointed as the next Ambassador of India to Indonesia?

(A) ** Pradeep Kumar Rawat

(B) Suresh Kumar

(C) Harish Singh

(D) Santhosh Raj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Manoj Kumar Bharti appointed as next Ambassador of India to the Republic of Indonesia. He is presently Special Secretary in the Ministry of External Affairs.

Concept note-2: -Shri Bharti had his schooling from Netarhat Residential School, Jharkhand. He is B. Tech in Electrical Engineering from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur.

Concept note-3: -The President approves the appointments of Ambassadors/High Commissioners based on the recommendations of the Prime Minister and the External Affairs Minister and the appointment is also subject to the agre‘ment of the receiving State.

Concept note-4: -Who was recently appointed as the Ambassador of India to the United States? Notes: Veteran diplomat Taranjit Singh Sandhu has been recently appointed as the next Indian Ambassador to the United States. He replaces Harsh Vardhan Shringla, who was the previous Indian envoy to the US.

Concept note-5: -Ambassador Pankaj Sharma, a career diplomat, belongs to the 1991 Batch of the Indian Foreign Service. He took charge as the Ambassador of India to Mexico on 9th February, 2022.
