Who is appointed as the next Ambassador of India to the Holy See?

(A) ** Sibi George

(B) Naresh Khan

(C) Suresh Kannan

(D) Rajesh Khanna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current ambassador is Joe Donnelly, who presented his credentials to Pope Francis on April 11, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Diplomatic relations between India and the Holy See were established soon after India’s independence in 1948. India’s Ambassador in Berne, Switzerland, has traditionally been accredited to the Holy See which maintains a Nunciature (Embassy) in New Delhi, presently headed by a Nuncio (Ambassador).
