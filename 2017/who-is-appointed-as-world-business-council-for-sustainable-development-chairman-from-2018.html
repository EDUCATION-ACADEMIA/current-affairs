Who is appointed as World Business Council for Sustainable Development Chairman from 2018?

(A) ** Sunny Verghese

(B) John Willaims

(C) Peter James

(D) Leo Haltman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sunny Verghese, the Singapore-based Indian-origin businessman, was appointed as the Chairman of the Geneva-based World Business Council for Sustainable Development (WBCSD). He succeeded Paul Polman, the CEO of Unilever, on January 1 and will serve a two-year term.

Concept note-2: -Sunny Verghese, Singapore-based Indian-origin businessman, has been appointed as the Chairman of World Business Council for Sustainable Development (WBCSD). Sunny Verghese is 57 years old.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sunny Verghese.

Concept note-4: -WBCSD is the premier global, CEO-led community of over 200 of the world’s leading sustainable businesses working collectively to accelerate the system transformations needed for a net zero, nature positive, and more equitable future.
