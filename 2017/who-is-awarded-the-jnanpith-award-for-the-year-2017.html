Who is awarded the Jnanpith Award for the year 2017?

(A) Pratibha Ray

(B) Shankha Ghosh

(C) ** Krishna Sobti

(D) Raghuveer Chaudhari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Krishna Sobti was an Indian Hindi-language writer. She was born in Gujrat, India. She writes under the pseudonym Hashmat. Krishna Sobti awarded the 53rd Jnanpith Award 2017.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Damodar Mauzo. Key Points Damodar Mauzo: He is a Goa-based poet and recipient of 57th Jnapith Award. . He won the Sahitya Akademi Award in 1983 for his novel Karmelin.

Concept note-3: -Krishna Sobti Her pen name is Hashmat. She was awarded the 53rd Jnanpith Award in 2017. She was honoured with the Sahitya Akademi Award in 1980.

Concept note-4: -The most recent recipients of the award are Assamese poet Nilmani Phookan and Konkani writer Damodar Mauzo, awarded for the years of 2021 and 2022 respectively.
