Who is elected as Chairman of Press Trust of India recently?

(A) Shri Harish Raghavendra

(B) Shri Vijay Prakash

(C) ** Shri Viveck Goenka

(D) Shri Sunil Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Its current chairman is Aveek Sarkar.

Concept note-2: -Vijay Joshi-CEO & Editor-in-Chief-Press Trust of India (PTI) | LinkedIn.

Concept note-3: -Press Trust of India (PTI), news agency cooperatively owned by Indian newspapers, which joined together to take over the management of the Associated Press of India and the Indian outlets of the Reuters news agency of Great Britain. It began operating in February 1949 and is headquartered in Mumbai.

Concept note-4: -PTI Head Office, New Delhi PTI Building, 4, Parliament Street, New Delhi-110 001.

Concept note-5: -THE Press Trust OF India Limited is a Non-govt company, incorporated on 27 Aug, 1947. It’s a public unlisted company and is classified as’company limited by shares’. Company’s authorized capital stands at Rs 25.0 lakhs and has 21.664% paid-up capital which is Rs 5.42 lakhs.
