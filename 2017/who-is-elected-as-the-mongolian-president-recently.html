Who is elected as the Mongolian President recently?

(A) Nambaryn Enkhbaya

(B) Jalkhanz Khutagt Sodnomyn Damdinbazar

(C) Dogsomyn Bodoo

(D) ** Khaltmaa Battulga

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current president is Ukhnaagiin Khürelsükh . Political parties with representation in the State Great Khural nominate candidates. The president was originally limited to two four-year terms, but this was changed to a non-renewable six-year term starting with the 2021 presidential election.

Concept note-2: -08 July 2022 His Excellency Oyun-Erdene Luvsannamsrai, Prime Minister of Mongolia, received a ceremonial welcome at the Istana this morning.

Concept note-3: -Electoral system Following 2019 constitutional amendments, the President is elected using the two-round system for a six-year term, and may only serve one term. Previously, the term was of four years, renewable once Political parties with representation in the State Great Khural are allowed to nominate candidates.

Concept note-4: -Ukhnaagiin Khürelsükh (Mongolian: , romanized: Uhnaagiin Hürelsüh, [oˈnaɟiŋ ˈxuɾɘ̆ɬsux]; born 14 June 1968), also referred to as Khürelsükh Ukhnaa, is the 6th and current president of Mongolia, beginning his term on June 25, 2021 after winning the 2021 Mongolian presidential election.
