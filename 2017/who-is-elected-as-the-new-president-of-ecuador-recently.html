Who is elected as the new president of Ecuador, recently?

(A) Guillermo Lasso

(B) Andres Paez

(C) ** Lenin Moreno

(D) Jorge Glass

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current President of Ecuador is Guillermo Lasso, who succeeded Lenín Moreno on 24 May 2021.

Concept note-2: -Lasso became the country’s first center-right president since Gustavo Noboa, who served from 2000 to 2003. Lasso vowed that in his first days in office, he would increase COVID vaccination numbers and promote oil production, mining and privatizations in the country.

Concept note-3: -José María Velasco Ibarra (19 March 1893 – 30 March 1979) was an Ecuadorian politician. He became president of Ecuador five times, in 1934–1935, 1944–1947, 1952–1956, 1960–1961, and 1968–1972, and only in 1952–1956 he completed a full term.

Concept note-4: -Luis Alfredo Palacio González (born 22 January 1939) is an Ecuadorian cardiologist and former politician who served as President of Ecuador from 20 April 2005 to 15 January 2007.

Concept note-5: -Alfredo Enrique Borrero Vega (born 19 October 1955) is an Ecuadorian neurosurgeon and politician. He is the 52nd vice president of Ecuador since 24 May 2021. He was elected to the vice presidency in the 2021 election.
