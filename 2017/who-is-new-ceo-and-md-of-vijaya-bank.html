Who is new CEO and MD of Vijaya Bank?

(A) ** Sankara Narayanan

(B) Sunil Kumar

(C) Suresh menon

(D) Ramesh Iyer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Correct Answer is Option 1 i.e Bangalore. Vijaya Bank was founded on 23rd October 1931 by late Shri A.B. Shetty and other enterprising farmers in Mangaluru, Karnataka.

Concept note-2: -Location and Overview: Established in the year 1931, Vijaya Bank Now Bank Of Baroda (Head Office) in M G Road, Bangalore is a top player in the category Corporate Companies in the Bangalore.

Concept note-3: -Vijaya Bank-(Amalgamated) Vijaya Bank became a scheduled bank in 1958. During the year 1963–68 is grew into all India bank by merger of nine smaller banks into Vijaya Bank. It got nationalised in year 1980.
