Who is represented for the Little Miss Universe?

(A) ** Padmalaya Nanda

(B) Sheela Ramakrishnan

(C) Nandini Varma

(D) Sudha Raghunathan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -12-year-old Padmalaya Nanda from Odisha Wins Little Miss Universe 2017 Contest in USA. The Indian coastal state of Odisha is in international spotlight today. All thanks to 12-year-old Padmalaya Nanda, the “Little Miss Universe 2017” title winner!

Concept note-2: -Divita Rai representing India at Miss Universe 2022: Know everything about her-Times of India.

Concept note-3: -163 or 5’4” was the height of the shortest Miss Universe title-holders. While there is no height requirement in Miss Universe, countries are obssessed in sending their tallest beauty queens to the pageant. The last Miss Universe winner with a height below 5’6” was 2012 Miss USA Olivia Culpo, who stands 5’5".

Concept note-4: -Harnaaz Sandhu of India crowned R’Bonney Gabriel of the United States as Miss Universe 2022. It is the United States’ first victory in 10 years, and the ninth victory of the country-the most by any country in the pageant’s history.

Concept note-5: -All contestants were carefully monitored during their time and judges paid special attention to manners and conversational skills. Eight-year-old Kempton Park resident, Gabriella Bryant, was crowned Little Miss Universe 2022 in Turkey on October 20.
