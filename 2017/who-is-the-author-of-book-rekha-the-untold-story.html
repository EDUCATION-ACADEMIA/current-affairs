Who is the author of book "Rekha: The Untold Story"

(A) Sheikh Mohammed

(B) Amir Vardhan

(C) Siva Balan

(D) ** Yasser Usman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rekha: The Untold Story is a 2016 Indian biographical book by Yasser Usman, detailing the life of Indian film actress Rekha.

Concept note-2: -Yasser Usman is the author of the book “Rekha: The untold story”.

Concept note-3: -Apart from parallel cinema, Rekha took on other increasingly serious, even adventurous roles; she was among the early actresses to play lead roles in heroine-oriented revenge films, the first of which was Khoon Bhari Maang in 1988. She won her second Filmfare Award for her performance in the film.
