Who is the Chairman of NITI Aayog?

(A) Arun Jaitley

(B) Balachandran

(C) ** Narendra Modi

(D) Bibek Debroy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr Parameswaran Iyer is the Chief Executive Officer of NITI Aayog. He is a member of the Indian Administrative Service, Uttar Pradesh Cadre, 1981 batch.

Concept note-2: -Shri Suman Bery is currently Vice Chairperson, NITI Aayog, in the rank and status of a Cabinet Minister. An experienced policy economist and research administrator, Mr Bery took over as NITI Aayog Vice Chairperson from 1 May 2022.

Concept note-3: -VC is appointed by the Prime Minister of India, who also chairs the NITI Aayog.
