Who Is the chief Guest of the Civil service day function

(A) Arun Jaitley

(B) ** Rajnath Singh

(C) Naveen Patnak

(D) Suresh Prabhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Civil Service Day theme 2022 is “Vision India-Bringing Citizens and Government Closer.” This theme indicates that a dedicated day such as National Civil Service Day brings the bureaucracy and citizens closer together by highlighting the efforts of the civil servants.

Concept note-2: -In India, the highest-ranking civil servant is the Cabinet Secretary. He/she is ex-officio Chairman of the Civil Services Board; the chief of the Indian Administrative Service and head of all civil services.

Concept note-3: -The first such function was held in Vigyan Bhawan on 21 April 2006.

Concept note-4: -The Government of India celebrates April 21 every year as ‘Civil Services day’ as an occasion for the civil servants to rededicate themselves to the cause of citizen and renew their commitments to public service and excellence in work.
