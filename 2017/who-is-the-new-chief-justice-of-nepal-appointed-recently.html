Who is the new Chief Justice of Nepal appointed recently?

(A) Shri Hari Prasad Pradhan

(B) Shri Suresh Yadav

(C) Shri Surendra Prasad Singh

(D) ** Shri Gopal Prasad Parajuli

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current Chief Justice is Cholendra Shumsher Rana, who took over the role on 2 January 2019.

Concept note-2: -The Chief Justice is appointed by the President on the recommendation of the Constitutional Council. The Chief Justice must have a least three years of sercice as a Supreme Court justice. The administrative head of the Supreme Court is the Chief Registrar.

Concept note-3: -The current and 50th Chief Justice is Justice Dhananjaya Y. Chandrachud, who entered office on 9 November 2022.

Concept note-4: -Chief Justice Cholendra Shamsher Rana retired on Tuesday.
