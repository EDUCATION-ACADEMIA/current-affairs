Who is the new defence Secretary appointed by Union Cabinet?

(A) ** Sanjay Mitra

(B) Kishore Singh

(C) Vishal kapoor

(D) Ramesh Yadav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Defence Secretary (IAST: Rakṣā Saciva) is the administrative head of the Ministry of Defence. This post is held by a senior Indian Administrative Service of the rank of secretary to the Government of India . The current Defence Secretary is Shri. Giridhar Aramane, I.A.S.

Concept note-2: -Shri Giridhar Aramane assumes the office of Defence Secretary.

Concept note-3: -Austin III. Lloyd James Austin III was sworn in as the 28th Secretary of Defense on January 22, 2021.

Concept note-4: -Rajiv Gauba (born 15 August 1959; IAST: Rājīva Gaubā) is an Indian civil servant serving as the current Cabinet Secretary of India since 2019.
