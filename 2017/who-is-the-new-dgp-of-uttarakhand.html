Who is the new DGP of Uttarakhand?

(A) ** Anil Kumar Raturi

(B) Suresh kumar

(C) Nikhil Raj

(D) Vijay Raj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ashok Kumar ( ; born 9 November) is a 1989 batch Indian Police Service officer of the Uttarakhand cadre, who is currently serving as the 11th Director General of Uttarakhand Police.

Concept note-2: -The Director-General of Police (DGP) is the greatest policeman of Indian states/union territories, with a three-star rank. The Commissioner of Police may hold either DGP or IGP. However, it is more likely that he will hold the level of DIGP.

Concept note-3: -All the States have a Director General of Police (DGP). Commissioner of Police is not a rank, but it is a posting. The senior-most police officer in a commissionerate is the Commissioner of Police. Director-General of Police is not a posting but the Highest Rank in the State Police Hierarchy.
