Who is the title sponsor of IPL 2016

(A) Sun TV network

(B) ** VIVO electronics

(C) Flipkart

(D) Sony pictures

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chinese smartphone manufacturer Vivo was awarded the title sponsorship for the 2016 and 2017 seasons.

Concept note-2: -Sponsors over the years After Pepsi ended its contract due to spot-fixing allegations between 2013 and 2015 within the league, Vivo stepped in as the league’s primary sponsor.

Concept note-3: -Tata Group took over as the IPL 2022 sponsor till the end of the 2023 edition. In 2018, VIVO agreed to a five-year partnership worth Rs 440 crore each year till 2022. However, due to border tensions, the Chinese mobile manufacturer’s contract was paused for a year in 2020.

Concept note-4: -The BCCI this year will earn little over Rs 1, 000 crore from the Indian Premier League (IPL 2022) central sponsorships, Inside Sport reported. This will be record sponsorship revenue generated by BCCI till date in 15 seasons of IPL.

Concept note-5: -So in this way, Pepsi just paid 238.2 crores to BCCI for 3 years and served as the title sponsor of IPL for IPL 2013, 2014, and 2015.
