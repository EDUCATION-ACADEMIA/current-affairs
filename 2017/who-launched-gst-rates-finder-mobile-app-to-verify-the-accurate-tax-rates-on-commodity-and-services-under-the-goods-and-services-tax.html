Who launched "GST Rates Finder" mobile app to verify the accurate tax rates on commodity and services under the Goods and Services Tax?

(A) Urjit Patel

(B) Narendra Modi

(C) Pranab Mukherjee

(D) ** Arun Jaitley

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi, July 7: Ministry of Finance launched an app – GST Rates Finder – on Friday to allow consumers verify the accurate tax rate on commodity and services under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) regime.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The rate of GST on IT services is 18%.

Concept note-3: -When SGST & CGST or IGST is Applied? As mentioned, 18% GST has to be paid by the customer on the purchase of mobile phones. If the customer is buying a phone from the dealer within state premises then that 18% will equally be divided into SGST (State GST) and CGST (Central GST).

Concept note-4: -GST rates applicable on mobile phones are 18% and the mobile HSN code is 8517.

Concept note-5: -Rate of GST on essential commodities is 0%.
