Who launched mobile App MyFASTag and FASTag Partner?

(A) ** The National Highways Authority of India

(B) Indian Institute of Technology

(C) Anna University

(D) Income tax Department

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. National Highways Authority of India launched two mobile Apps – MyFASTag and FASTag Partner.

Concept note-2: -It is affixed on a vehicle and enables automatic toll payments directly from the customer account that is linked to FASTag. FASTag has been developed by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) as part of its National Electronic Toll Collection (NETC) program to offer a nationwide electronic toll solution.

Concept note-3: -FASTag is an electronic toll collection system in India, operated by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI). It employs Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology for making toll payments directly from the prepaid or savings account linked to it or directly toll owner.

Concept note-4: -Myfastag app In addition to SBI Fastag, there are other apps for availing Fastag related services in India. Likewise, one of the best apps you can have on your phone is the Myfastag app. You can get it both for android and iOS phones.

Concept note-5: -Conversation. Hon’ble Minister, Shri Nitin Gadkari addressing the audience at the launch of the TV awareness campaign for FASTag in the presence of Shri Akshay Kumar, Brand Ambassador for Road Safety Campaign, today, in New Delhi.
