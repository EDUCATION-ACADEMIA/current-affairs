Who launches ‘National Sports Talent Search Portal’?

(A) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(B) Shri Naveen Patnaik

(C) Shri Rajesh Sharma

(D) ** Shri Venkaiah Naidu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Sports Talent Search Scheme (NSTSS) has been formulated for talent identification in the age group of 8-12 years and nurturing of identified talented sports persons. The scheme is being implemented by Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, Government of India.

Concept note-2: -The scheme of Rural Sports Programme, launched in the year 1970-71 with a view to broadbasing games and sports and tapping the hidden talent available in rural areas, was being operated by the Department of Youth Affairs & Sports, Government of India.

Concept note-3: -What is the Sports Code? The Sports Code, or National Sports Development Code of India to be precise, was introduced in 2011 by the Central government, which wanted good governance practices in the management of sports at the national level without interfering in the autonomy of the national sports bodies.

Concept note-4: -The Madhya Pradesh State Government has launched a comprehensive Talent Search Campaign in the field of sports.

Concept note-5: -The scheme was started on August 28, 2017, at the Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium in New Delhi, by the nation’s Vice President, the honorable M. Naidu, and the Sports Minister, Vijay Goel. 2Q. What are the eligibility criteria for the National Sports Talent Search Scheme (NSTSS)?
