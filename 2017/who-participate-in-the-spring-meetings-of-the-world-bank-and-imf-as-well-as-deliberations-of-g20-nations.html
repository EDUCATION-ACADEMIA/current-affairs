Who participate in the Spring Meetings of the World Bank and IMF as well as deliberations of G20 nations?

(A) Shri Rajnath singh

(B) ** Shri Arun Jaitley

(C) Shri L.K Advani

(D) Shri. Suresh Prabhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Board of Governors is the IMF’s highest decision-making body. It consists of one governor and one alternate governor for each member country. The governor is appointed by the member country and is usually the minister of finance or the head of the central bank.

Concept note-2: -The World Bank participates as observer in IMFC’s meetings.

Concept note-3: -The 2030 development agenda. In 2015, under the 2030 Global Development Agenda, the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) replaced the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs). The IMF and the World Bank work together to support member countries reach their SDGs. There’s more information in this video on SDGs.

Concept note-4: -The Annual Meetings of the Boards of Governors of the IMF and World Bank Group take place in October in Washington, DC.
