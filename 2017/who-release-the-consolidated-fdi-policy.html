Who release the consolidated FDI policy?

(A) ** The Department of Industrial Policy & Promotion (DIPP)

(B) Department of Agriculture

(C) Department of Forest

(D) Department of Tax and Excise Duty

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -FDI Policy | Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade | MoCI | GoI.

Concept note-2: -Proposals for raising FDI beyond 49% from such companies will require Government approval. Licence applications will be considered by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, Ministry of Commerce & Industry, in consultation with Ministry of Defence and Ministry of External Affairs.

Concept note-3: -The government began liberalising FDI during 1980-91 with the Industrial Policy Statements of 1980 and 1982 followed by the Technology Policy Statement in 1983.
