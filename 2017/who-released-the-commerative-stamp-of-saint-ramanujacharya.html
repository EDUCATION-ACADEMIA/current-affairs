Who released the commerative stamp of Saint Ramanujacharya?

(A) ** Prime Minister Narendra Modi

(B) Mrs Aruna Nair

(C) Mr.Suresh Prabhu

(D) Mr Arun Jaitley

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Issued for : Department of Posts is pleased to release a Commemorative Postage Stamp on Ramanujacharya on the occasion of his 1000th birth anniversary.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Odisha.

Concept note-3: -Answer: Nagaland Nagaland became the 16th state of India on 1 December 1963.

Concept note-4: -He provided an intellectual basis for the practice of bhakti (devotional worship) in three major commentaries: the Vedartha-samgraha (on the Vedas, the earliest scriptures of Hinduism), the Shri-bhashya (on the Brahma-sutras), and the Bhagavadgita-bhashya (on the Bhagavadgita).

Concept note-5: -1017 CE – 1137 CE; IAST: Rāmānuja; [ɽaːmaːnʊdʑɐ]), also known as Ramanujacharya, was an Indian Hindu philosopher, guru and a social reformer. He is noted to be one of the most important exponents of the Sri Vaishnavism tradition within Hinduism.
