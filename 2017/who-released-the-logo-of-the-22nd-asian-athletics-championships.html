Who released the logo of the 22nd Asian Athletics Championships?

(A) Shri Arun Jaitley

(B) Shri Kumar Mishra

(C) ** Shri Naveen Patnaik

(D) Shri Suresh Prabhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Asian Athletics Championship was held in the Kalinga Stadium in Bhubneswar. 560 athletes participated in this championship. 41 countries participated in this championship. The mascot of this championship was Olive Ridley Sea Turtle.

Concept note-2: -The President of the Asian Athletics Association and IAAF Council Member Mr. Suresh Kalmadi has been re-elected for the second consecutive term announced the Athletics Federation of India (AFI) in a Press Release today.

Concept note-3: -The Asian Athletics Association is the continental governing body for the sport of athletics in Asia. It is headquartered in Singapore.
