Who represents India in World Masters Game going to held at Auckland

(A) ** Mann Kaur

(B) Wilma Ramsay

(C) Carol Wayne

(D) Mary Kom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Masters Games are an international multi-sport athletic competition held every four years by the International Masters Games Association (IMGA) for athletes over the age of 30 and into their middle age and senior years. The games are held in the year following the Olympics.

Concept note-2: -This is the first time the IMGA has visited to inspect the progress of planning for the games since Taipei City and New Taipei City won the right to host the World Masters Games 2025 Taipei & New Taipei City (WMG 2025 Taipei & New Taipei City).

Concept note-3: -The World Masters Games 2021 Kansai will be held at more than 80 venues, mainly in the Kansai region, for 17 days from Friday, 13th-Sunday, 29th May, 2022 featuring a total of 35 sports and 59 events. ※The following schedule was created based on the information that is current as of 21st September, 2021.

Concept note-4: -IMGA World Masters Games, Taipei 2025 The Games, which is officially the world’s largest multi-sport participatory event, will have its eleventh edition in 2025 after previously visiting Canada, Italy, Australia, Denmark and New Zealand.
