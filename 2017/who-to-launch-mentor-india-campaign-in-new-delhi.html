Who to launch Mentor India Campaign in New Delhi?

(A) ** Shri Amitabh Kant

(B) Shri Rajesh Sharma

(C) Shri Naresh Singh

(D) Shri Vijay Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Institute for Transforming India (NITI Aayog) had launched ‘Mentor India’ Campaign as part of Atal Innovation Mission, with the objective of building a huge network of Mentors across India, in order to build future Innovators.

Concept note-2: -Mentor India is a strategic nation-building program to engage mentors who can lead the way for students of Atal Tinkering Labs across India. We call them “Mentors of Change” who volunteer for mentorship and help students practice future skills such as design thinking.

Concept note-3: -2 lakh per year (for a period of 5 years).

Concept note-4: -Please go to www.niti.gov.in and download the Guidelines for Setting Up Atal Tinkering Laboratories under Atal Innovation Mission. Please read the guidelines carefully before applying.
