Who to soon introduce innovative techniques to eliminate rail accidents?

(A) Indian Airways

(B) Indian Jetways

(C) ** Indian Railways

(D) Indian Army

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first railway on Indian sub-continent ran over a stretch of 21 miles from Bombay to Thane. The idea of a railway to connect Bombay with Thane, Kalyan and with the Thal and Bhore Ghats inclines first occurred to Mr. George Clark, the Chief Engineer of the Bombay Government, during a visit to Bhandup in 1843.

Concept note-2: -Kavach is a signficant initiative in this mission to make and keep Indian Railways accident free. So, what is Kavach? Kavach is an indigenously developed Automatic Train Protection system developed by the Research Design and Standards Organisation (RDSO) in collaboration with the Indian industry.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of Railways had appointed a High Level Safety Review Committee under the chairmanship of Dr. Anil Kakodkar to review the safety of the Indian Railways and recommend improvements. The Committee recently presented its report.

Concept note-4: -Kavach ( lit. ‘Armour’) is an automatic train protection (ATP) system indigenously developed by Indian Railways through Research Designs & Standards Organisation (RDSO). Initial development of Kavach started in 2012 under the name Train Collision Avoidance System (TCAS).
