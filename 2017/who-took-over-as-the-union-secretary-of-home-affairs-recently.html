Who took over as the Union Secretary of Home Affairs recently?

(A) Shri Rajesh Sharma

(B) Shri Vijay Sekhar

(C) Shri Ram Kumar

(D) ** Shri Rajiv Gauba

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NAME : AJAY KUMAR BHALLA Phil (Social Sciences), Punjab University. WORK EXPERIENCE: Presently Union Home Secretary since 23.8.

Concept note-2: -This post is held by a senior IAS officer of the rank of Secretary to Government of India. The current Home Secretary is Ajay Kumar Bhalla.

Concept note-3: -Rajiv Gauba (born 15 August 1959; IAST: Rājīva Gaubā) is an Indian civil servant serving as the current Cabinet Secretary of India since 2019.

Concept note-4: -In the Hierarchy of a Ministry in the Government of India, the position of Secretary is below Minister of State (MoS) and above Additional Secretary. In the Hierarchy of a Ministry in the Government of India, the position of Joint Secretary is below Additional Secretary and above Directors and Deputy Secretaries.
