Who was elected as the Mayor of Mumbai

(A) ** Vishwanath Mahadeshwar

(B) Snehal Ambekar

(C) Sunil Prabhu

(D) Shraddha Jadhav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Suhas Wadkar is an Indian politician served as a Deputy Mayor of Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation, which is India’s richest municipal corporation. He was elected as Deputy Mayor of Mumbai without any opposition.

Concept note-2: -Clause 23 of the Municipal Law provides that the Mayor/the Municipal Chairperson/the Municipal President, as the case may be, shall be elected by the elected Councillors from among themselves.

Concept note-3: -Snehal Ambekar (born 31 July 1972) is Shiv Sena Politician from Mumbai. She was the former Mayor of Mumbai. She first Dalit woman to hold the position.
