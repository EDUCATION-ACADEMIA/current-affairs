Who was honoured with the Kala Ratan Award 2017?

(A) ** Anupam Kher

(B) Pradeep Anand Naik

(C) Ranbir Kapoor

(D) Akshay Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Veteran actor Anupam Kher honoured with the Kala Ratan Award by Vice President. He was awarded for his contribution in Bollywood, he worked in more than 400 films.

Concept note-2: -Notes: Veteran actor Anupam Kher will be honoured with the Kala Ratan Award by Vice President Hamid Ansari.

Concept note-3: -Lok Kala Ratna Award 2022, is an Annual International Indian Folk Art competition and exhibition for all Indian Folk Artists and presented by IIFAG Australia. The competition is open to all citizens of the world.

Concept note-4: -This award is conferred to artists from the field of Dancing, Singing, Music, Folk/Tribal arts /Theatre, Painting, Sculpture, Puppetry, Photography, Anchoring, etc. These awards are bestowed upon individuals with the aim of recognizing their outstanding work in the field of art.

Concept note-5: -Padma Awards-one of the highest civilian Awards of the country, are conferred in three categories, namely, Padma Vibhushan, Padma Bhushan and Padma Shri.
