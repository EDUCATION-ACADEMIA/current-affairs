Who was Sworn in as AIADMK Legislature Party Leader

(A) Edappadi K.Palaniswami

(B) K.Pandiarajan

(C) O Panneerselvam

(D) ** Sasikala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Before that, she appointed Edappadi K. Palaniswami as legislative party leader (Chief Minister). She also appointed her nephew and former treasurer of the party, T. T. V. Dhinakaran, as the deputy general secretary of the AIADMK party.

Concept note-2: -The DMK was founded on 17 September 1949 by the former chief minister of Tamil Nadu C. N. Annadurai (Anna) as a breakaway faction from the Dravidar Kazhagam headed by E. V. Ramasami (Periyar). DMK was headed by Annadurai as the general secretary from 1949 until his death on 4 February 1969.

Concept note-3: -The AIADMK joined the National Democratic Alliance (NDA) of the Bharatiya Janata Party, the ruling party of the Union Government of India, with Palanisami as its Chief Ministerial candidate.
