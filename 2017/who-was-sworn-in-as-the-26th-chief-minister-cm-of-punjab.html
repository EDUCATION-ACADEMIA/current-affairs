Who was sworn in as the 26th Chief Minister (CM) of Punjab.

(A) ** Amarinder Singh

(B) Navjot Singh Sidhu

(C) Brahm Mohindra

(D) Manpreet Badal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Amarinder Singh was sworn in as the 26th Chief Minister of Punjab on 16 March 2017 at Punjab Raj Bhavan, Chandigarh. The oath of office was administered by the Punjab governor, V.P. Singh Badnore.

Concept note-2: -Bhagwant Singh Mann (born 17 October 1973) is an Indian politician serving as the 17th & Current Chief Minister of Punjab. He leads the cabinet ministry of Punjab and is a member of the Aam Aadmi Party. He is also a former actor, comedian and satirist.

Concept note-3: -Rajinder Kaur Bhattal (born 30 September 1945) is an Indian politician and member of Congress who served as the 14th Chief Minister of Punjab from 1996 to 1997 and 2nd Deputy Chief Minister of Punjab from 2004 to 2007.
