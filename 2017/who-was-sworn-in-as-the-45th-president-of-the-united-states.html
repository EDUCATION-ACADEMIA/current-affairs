Who was sworn-in as the 45th President of the United States

(A) Barack Obama

(B) Hillary Clinton

(C) ** Donald Trump

(D) Mike Pence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The inauguration of Donald Trump as the 45th president of the United States marked the commencement of Donald Trump’s term as president and Mike Pence ’s term as vice president.

Concept note-2: -Donald John Trump (born June 14, 1946) is an American politician, media personality, and businessman who served as the 45th president of the United States from 2017 to 2021. Queens, New York City, U.S.

Concept note-3: -With the 2021 inauguration of Joe Biden, the presidential oath has been taken 76 different times by 45 persons.

Concept note-4: -When is Inauguration Day? Inauguration Day occurs every four years on January 20 (or January 21 if January 20 falls on a Sunday) at the U.S. Capitol building in Washington, DC. The next presidential inauguration is scheduled to be on January 20, 2025.
