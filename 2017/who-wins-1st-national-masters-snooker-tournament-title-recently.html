Who wins 1st National Masters Snooker Tournament Title recently?

(A) Judd Trump

(B) Neil Robertson

(C) ** I H Manudev

(D) Ronnie O’Sullivan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pherwani was his opponent in the final once again and this time Chawla won 6-3 to be crowned champion. “To be honest, I never thought I could win the title here, ” Chawla told this daily.

Concept note-2: -Who won the Masters snooker last year? Neil Robertson landed that winner’s windfall in 2022, beating Barry Hawkins 10-4 to win the tournament for the second time. After defeating O’Sullivan in the quarterfinals, Robertson won by his narrowest margin when he edged past Mark Williams 6-5 in the semifinals.

Concept note-3: -Masters snooker final 2023: Judd Trump beats Mark Williams to claim second title. Judd Trump said his Masters win over Mark Williams was his “best ever” after claiming the title for a second time.

Concept note-4: -Chris Wakelin dashed the dream of Belgian teenager Julien Leclercq to clinch victory in the one-frame BetVictor Snooker Shoot Out in Leicester.
