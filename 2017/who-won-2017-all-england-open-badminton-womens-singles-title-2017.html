Who won 2017 All England Open Badminton Women’s singles title 2017

(A) Wang Shixian

(B) Ratchanok Intanon

(C) Carolina Marin

(D) ** Tai Tzu-ying

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World No. 1 Tai Tzu-ying from Taiwan clinched the women’s singles title at the 2017 Yonex All England Open Badminton Championships by defeating Thailand’s Ratchanok Intanon March 12 in Birmingham, becoming the first Taiwan athlete to win the title and taking home US$45, 000 in prize money.

Concept note-2: -All England Open Badminton Championships: Japan’s Akane Yamaguchi Claims Women’s Singles Crown.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Prakash Padukone. On March 23, 1980, Prakash Padukone became the first Indian to win the All England Badminton Championships. Padukone clashed with Indonesia’s Liem Swie King in the summit clash at the Wembley Stadium. Padukone defeated his opponent 15-3, 15-10.
