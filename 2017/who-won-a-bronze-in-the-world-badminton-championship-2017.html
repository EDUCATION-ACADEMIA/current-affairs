Who won a bronze in the World Badminton Championship 2017?

(A) Ajay Jayaram

(B) Chetan Anand

(C) Arvind Bhatt

(D) ** Saina Nehwal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2017 world championships was the first time that two Indian shuttlers were on the podium in a single edition. With PV Sindhu claiming silver, Saina Nehwal won bronze after reaching the semi-finals.

Concept note-2: -BWF World Championships 2022: India’s Chirag-Satwik seal historic bronze medal in men’s doubles.

Concept note-3: -Pusarla Venkata Sindhu, known as PV Sindhu, is an Indian professional badminton player and the first Indian woman to win an Olympic silver medal. Born on July 5, 1995, in Hyderabad, Sindhu is also one of the two Indian badminton players to ever win an Olympic medal, the other being Saina Nehwal.

Concept note-4: -Sindhu became the first Indian to win Badminton World Championships gold by defeating Nozomi Okuhara. Nozomi Okuhara is from Japan.
