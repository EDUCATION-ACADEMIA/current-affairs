Who won Ballon d’Or, Equals Lionel Messi’s Record?

(A) ** Cristiano Ronaldo

(B) Denmis Christania

(C) John Williams

(D) Williams Peter

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Argentinian footballer Lionel Messi, widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, has received seven Ballon d’Or awards, the most for any football player, as well as the 2009 FIFA World Player of the Year and 2019 The Best FIFA Men’s Player.

Concept note-2: -Messi went on to win the Ballon d’Or for the next three seasons in a row, including in 2012, fresh from scoring a record 73 goals in all competitions across the previous season.

Concept note-3: -It was first awarded in 1956. The most recent winner was Real Madrid’s Karim Benzemais in 2022. Messi has won the men’s Ballon d’Or award a record seven times, Cristiano Ronaldo has won the award five times.

Concept note-4: -At the club level, Messi holds the record for most goals in La Liga with 474. In total, including clubs and country, Messi has scored 793 goals in 1, 003 appearances. He also has 350 assists to his name. On the other hand, Ronaldo has scored 819 goals in 1, 145 appearances for both club and country.
