Who won bronze in 69 kgs women youth category?

(A) ** Nirupama Devi

(B) Shreeja

(C) Nirmala Singh

(D) Anitha Rao

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Lovlina Borgohain. Indian boxer Lovlina Borgohain (69 kg ) won a bronze medal in the Olympic Games.

Concept note-2: -Indian boxer Lovlina Borgohain won the gold at the Asian Boxing Championship in the 75kg category.

Concept note-3: -Mary Kom was the first Indian female boxer to win an Olympic medal. Q. Who is the first Indian woman to win a medal in Olympics?

Concept note-4: -Lovlina Borgohain is an Indian boxer who plays in the 69Kg category. She is 23 years old and hails from the Golaghat district of Assam. She won a bronze medal in the women’s welterweight category at Tokyo Olympics 2021.
