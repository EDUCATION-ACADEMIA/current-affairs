Who won gold in the Asian Blitz Chess Championship?

(A) Keerthi Menon

(B) Parvathi

(C) ** Vaishali

(D) Tania Sachdev

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PANJIM: Another of Goa’s promising chess players earned international honours to bring glory to the State and country.

Concept note-2: -Magnus Carlsen and Bibisara Assaubayeva win the World Blitz Chess Championships. The World Blitz Chess Championship ended in Almaty (Kazakhstan) with the victory of Magnus Carlsen in the Open section and Bibisara Assaubayeva in the Women’s.

Concept note-3: -New Delhi (04 Nov 2022):-Top seed Grandmaster Praggnanandhaa R and Women Grandmaster Nandhidhaa P V became the Asian Champions in the Open and Women’s sections respectively in the Asian Continental Chess Championship which concluded here at Leela Ambience Convention Hotel on Thursday.

Concept note-4: -The men’s section was won by Magnus Carlsen. He became the first player in chess history to hold the Classical chess world championship, Rapid world championship and the Blitz world Championship at the same time.

Concept note-5: -Asian Continental Chess Championships (Open and Women’s Championships) 25 October – 04 November 2022, New Delhi, – All India Chess Federation.
