Who won gold medal in Asia Archery Championship?

(A) ** Shri Abhishek Verma

(B) Shri Suresh Singh

(C) Shri Vivek Chopra

(D) Shri Naresh Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The men’s team of Akash Mrinal Chauhan and Parth Salunkhe won the gold, defeating Korea. Tisha Punia and Salunkhe settled for silver after losing to Kai-Han Yang and Szu-Min Su of Chinese Taipei in the recurve mixed team event.

Concept note-2: -The Indians managed just one 10 in the second end as the French duo reduced the deficit to one-point. The third was a tie, while in the deciding fourth end, Abhishek and Jyothi held their nerves to edge out their opponents by two points to annexe the title.

Concept note-3: -Notes: India has won 3 gold medals in Archery Championships.
