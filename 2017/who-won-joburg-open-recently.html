Who won Joburg Open recently?

(A) ** Shubhankar Sharma

(B) Vijaya Sekhar

(C) Nithish Sharma

(D) Naresh Iyer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dan Bradbury wins Joburg Open for first European tour title.

Concept note-2: -Englishman Dan Bradbury has won his maiden DP World Tour title after carding a four-under 67 in the final round at the Joburg Open.

Concept note-3: -The purse of the Joburg Open is ZAR 17, 500, 000, which is equal to approximately 1 million USD. Like any other European Tour event, a cut is made after the first 36 holes, and only the golfers advancing to the final rounds and successfully finishing them can earn their share of the total purse.

Concept note-4: -In 2023 all players competing for a total prize money of 17, 500, 000 ZAR (ca. 1, 000, 000€). The course for the tournament at Houghton GC plays at Par 72.
