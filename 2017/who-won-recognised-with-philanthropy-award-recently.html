Concept note-1: -2022 Philanthropist of the Year We are proud to announce Cecilia “Cec” Sanchez de Ortiz as the Philanthropist of the Year.

Concept note-2: -THE OUTSTANDING PHILANTHROPIST AWARD is presented to an individual or family with a record of exceptional generosity who, through direct financial support, has demonstrated outstanding civic and charitable responsibility, and who encourages others to take philanthropic leadership roles in their communities.
