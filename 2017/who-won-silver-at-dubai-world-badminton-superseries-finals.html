Who won Silver at Dubai World Badminton Superseries Finals?

(A) ** PV Sindhu

(B) Asha Agarwal

(C) Geeta Phogat

(D) Krishna Poonia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -P.V. Sindhu became the first Indian to win Badminton World Championships gold by defeating Nozomi Okuhara.

Concept note-2: -World No. 3 Sindhu’s journey to the semis has been a cakewalk as the shuttler has dominated her opponents. However, she will aim to end the year on a good note. In 2017, the lanky Indian shuttler has won two Super Series events and a silver in the BWF World Championships.

Concept note-3: -The tournaments are divided into four levels – Super 1000 (four tournaments), Super 750 (six tournaments), Super 500 (nine tournaments) and Super 300 (eleven tournaments). Each of these tournaments offers different ranking points and prize money.
