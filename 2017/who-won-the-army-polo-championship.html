Who won the Army Polo Championship

(A) Army Service Corps

(B) Remount

(C) Veterinary Corps

(D) ** 61 CAVALRY

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ASC Team and Armoured Corps-Artillery Team were declared Joint Winners of the Army Commander’s Polo Cup 2022. Lt. Col. Vishal Chauhan, Naveen Singh played really well.

Concept note-2: -The official further clarified that it was in fact Spain that won the XII FIP Polo World Championship. The United States Polo Association, which organised the tournament, had also posted on its official website that Spain defeated the United States 11-10 in overtime and took home the trophy.

Concept note-3: -Zone A teams were Argentina, Mexico, Pakistan and Spain. Zone B teams were Australia, Italy, Uruguay and USA. According to the official page, Spain was crowned XII FIP World Polo Champions, and the USA earned the silver medal victory.

Concept note-4: -Considered to be the most prestigious polo tournament in the United States, the U.S. Open is the apex of the 26-goal tournaments held during the Florida high-goal winter polo season that attracts fans and polo enthusiasts from across the United States and around the world.
