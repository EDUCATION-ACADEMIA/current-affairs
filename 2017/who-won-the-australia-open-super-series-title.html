Who won the Australia Open Super Series Title?

(A) P.V. Sindhu

(B) Chetan Anand

(C) Aparna Phopat

(D) ** Kidambi Srikanth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ashleigh Barty defeated Danielle Collins in the final, 6–3, 7–6 to win the women’s singles tennis title at the 2022 Australian Open. She became the first home player to win an Australian Open singles title since Chris O’Neil in 1978.

Concept note-2: -SYDNEY-China’s Shi Yuqi bagged the men’s singles title at the Australian Badminton Open 2022 on Sunday with a 2-1 victory over compatriot Lu Guangzu in the final.

Concept note-3: -Rafael Nadal defeated Daniil Medvedev in the final, 2–6, 6–7, 6–4, 6–4, 7–5 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2022 Australian Open.

Concept note-4: -PV Sindhu creates history, clinches World Tour Finals title PV Sindhu became the first Indian to win the season-ending tournament.
