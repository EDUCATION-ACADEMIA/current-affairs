Who won the Indonesia Open Super Series Premier Title recently?

(A) P.V Sindhu

(B) Saina Nehwal

(C) ** Kidambi Srikanth

(D) Ashok Misra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kidambi Srikanth clinched the Indonesia Super Series Premier title in style on Sunday as the World No 22 outclassed Japan’s Kazumasa Sakai 21-11, 21-19 in a match that lasted just 37 minutes.

Concept note-2: -Home favourite Jonatan Christie claimed his first title in 10 months at the 2023 Indonesia Masters on Sunday (29 January). In an all-Indonesian final, he defeated Chico Aura Dwi Wardoyo 21-15, 21-13 in 44 minutes for his maiden BWF Badminton Tour title on home soil inside Jakarta’s Gelora Bung Karno Sports Palace.

Concept note-3: -The 2023 Indonesia Open is an international badminton tournament that will take place at the Istora Senayan in Jakarta between 13 and 18 June 2023. It is part of the BWF World Tour Super 1000 series on the 2023 BWF World Tour, with a total prize pool of US$1, 250, 000.
