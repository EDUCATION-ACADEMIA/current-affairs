Who won the ninth Halle Open title recently?

(A) Andy Murray

(B) Venus Williams

(C) ** Roger Federer

(D) Serena Williams

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HALLE, Germany (AP)-Roger Federer defeated Germany’s Alexander Zverev 6-1, 6-3 to win the Gerry Weber Open for a record ninth time on Sunday. Playing in his 140th career final, Federer saved the only break point he faced and converted four of his eight opportunities to clinch his 92nd career title in 53 minutes.

Concept note-2: -Located in Halle, Germany, the Halle Open is an annual ATP 500 tennis tournament in June that takes place at the Owl Arena, previously known as the Gerry Weber Stadion. For 2022, the total prize money for the event is €2, 134, 520, which converts to $2, 223, 209.

Concept note-3: -A History Of Roger Federer’s 10 Halle Titles | ATP Tour | Tennis.

Concept note-4: -Novak wins on debut in Halle! Exhausted Roger Federer withdraws! – Novak Djokovic.

Concept note-5: -The 2022 Halle Open (known for sponsorship reasons as the Terra Wortmann Open) was a tennis tournament played on outdoor grass courts. It was the 29th edition of the Halle Open and part of the ATP Tour 500 series of the 2022 ATP Tour.
