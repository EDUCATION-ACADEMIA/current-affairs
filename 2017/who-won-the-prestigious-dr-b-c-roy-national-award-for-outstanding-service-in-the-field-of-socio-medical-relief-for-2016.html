Who won the prestigious Dr B C Roy national award for ’outstanding service in the field of socio medical relief’ for 2016.

(A) Suresh Krishna

(B) Aravind Kumar

(C) Ravi Shankar

(D) ** Raghu Ram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr P Raghu Ram, President, Association of Breast Surgeons of India, Director, KIMS-USHALAKSHMI Centre for Breast Diseases & CEO, Ushalakshmi Breast Cancer Foundation has been selected for the prestigious Dr B C Roy National award for ‘Outstanding service in the field of Socio Medical Relief’ for the year 2016.

Concept note-2: -Eminent Medical Teacher: Rajesh Malhotra, New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -B.C. Roy National Award Fund registered under the Societies Registration Act, XXI of 1860, has decided to give award in recognition of the outstanding service in the field of Socio-Medical Relief and in the establishment of the Medical organisations and Medical institutions.

Concept note-4: -B.C. Roy Trophy is associated with Football. Additional InformationHockey: It is a team game. It is played between two teams of 11 players each infield.

Concept note-5: -B. C. Roy Award, the highest Indian award in the medical category. The award was instituted in 1976 by the Government of India under the aegis of the Medical Council of India. The award is named after Bidhan Chandra Roy, a medical doctor and a former chief minister of the Indian state of West Bengal.
