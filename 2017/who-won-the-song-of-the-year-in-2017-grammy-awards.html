Who won the Song of the year in 2017 Grammy Awards

(A) Lemonade

(B) David Bowie

(C) Hotline Bling

(D) ** Adele

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Grammys 2017: Adele Wins Album of the Year, Says It Should Have Been Beyoncé Adele has won Album of the Year at the 59th Annual Grammy Awards. 25 took the victory over records by Beyoncé, Justin Bieber, Drake, and Sturgill Simpson.

Concept note-2: -Instead, the Grammys divided the general categories in a typical voting split. Bonnie Raitt won the song of the year prize for “Just Like That.” Lizzo took record of the year for “About Damn Time.” Album of the year went to Harry Styles for his album Harry’s House.

Concept note-3: -For the second year in a row, BTS lost the Grammy for pop duo/group performance.
