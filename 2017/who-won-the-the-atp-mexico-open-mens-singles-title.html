Who won the the ATP Mexico Open Men’s Singles title

(A) ** Sam Querrey

(B) Dominic Thiem

(C) Roger Federer

(D) Rafael Nadal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Rafael Nadal. Australian Open champion Rafael Nadal captured his fourth title in Acapulco on 26 Feb 2022. He won with a 6-4 6-4 after defeating Cameron Norrie in the final of the ATP 500 event.

Concept note-2: -The tournament’s winner will earn $125, 040, while the runner-up will take home a respectable $72, 395.
