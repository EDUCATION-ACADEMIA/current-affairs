Who won the the gruelling men’s 50-kilometre event of the National Race Walking Championships 2017

(A) Gurmeet Singh

(B) Manish Singh

(C) Basanta Bahadur Rana

(D) ** Sandeep Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sandeep Kumar clocked three hours 55 minutes and 59.05 seconds in a brilliant performance to win the race. Sandeep Kumar of Haryana smashed his own national record en route to winning the gold medal in the gruelling men’s 50km event of the National Race Walking Championships here today.

Concept note-2: -Despite having never raced beyond 35km before, China’s Liang Rui upstaged the world gold and silver medallists to win the women’s 50km race walk in a world record* of 4:04:36 at the IAAF World Race Walking Team Championships Taicang 2018.
