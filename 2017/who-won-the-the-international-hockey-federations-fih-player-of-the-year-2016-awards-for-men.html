Who won the the International Hockey Federation’s (FIH) Player of the Year (2016) awards for men

(A) Danny Kerry

(B) Karen Brown

(C) Laurine Delforge

(D) ** John-John Dohmen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Harmanpreet Singh becomes just the fourth player to win the Player of the Year award (men’s category) in consecutive years.

Concept note-2: -FIH Player of the Year 2021-22: India’s Harmanpreet Singh wins men’s award. The Indian hockey star also won the award last year. Harmanpreet Singh holds the record for the most goals scored in an FIH Pro League season.

Concept note-3: -The Netherlands’ Felice Albers and India’s Harmanpreet Singh have been named the International Hockey Federation (FIH) Player of the Year for the 2021-2022 season in the women’s and men’s competitions respectively.

Concept note-4: -The International Hockey Federation (FIH) can reveal that Felice Albers of Netherlands and Harmanpreet Singh of India have been named the FIH Players of the Year 2021-22 in the women’s and men’s category respectively.

Concept note-5: -Harmanpreet Singh of India and Felice Albers of Netherlands have been named the FIH Players of the Year 2021-22 in the women’s and men’s category respectively, the International Hockey Federation (FIH) announced on Friday.
