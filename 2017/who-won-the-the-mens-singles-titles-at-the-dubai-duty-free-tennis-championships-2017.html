Who won the the men’s singles titles at the Dubai Duty Free Tennis Championships 2017.

(A) Fernando Verdaso

(B) Rafael Nadal

(C) Roger Federer

(D) ** Andy Murray

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Andy Murray defeated Fernando Verdasco in the final, 6–3, 6–2 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2017 Dubai Tennis Championships.

Concept note-2: -Belinda Bencic World No 9 Bencic lifted the trophy in Dubai in 2019 after defeating defending champion Petra Kvitova in the final. Since then, the Swiss has taken her career record to seven singles titles-including a gold medal at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics-and two wins in doubles.

Concept note-3: -Andrey Rublev defeated Jiří Veselý in the final, 6–3, 6–4 to win the men’s singles title at the 2022 Dubai Tennis Championships. It was Rublev’s tenth career singles title.

Concept note-4: -Murray joins the likes of Novak Djokovic, Daniil Medvedev, Alexander Zverev and 2022 champion Andrey Rublev among others competing at this year’s event. “We are delighted to welcome Andy back to Dubai, ” said Colm McLoughlin, the executive vice chairman and CEO of Dubai Duty Free.
