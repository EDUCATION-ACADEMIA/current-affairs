Whose memorial was inaugurated by the Prime Minister Narendra Modi recently?

(A) Dr. Ambedkar memorial

(B) Dr. Bashveshwara Memorial

(C) ** Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam Memorial

(D) Dr Sardar Vallabhai patel Memorial

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The new addition to the list is the recently inaugurated Statue of Prosperity which is a tribute to Bengaluru’s founder Nadaprabhu Kempegowda. Read about some more. The 182-metre-tall statue is a tribute to the Iron Man of India, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.

Concept note-2: -The Pradhanmantri Sangrahalaya (the Prime Ministers’ Museum) is readying a gallery on Prime Minister Narendra Modi, the Ministry of Culture said Thursday. A ministry statement said the gallery is scheduled to open for public viewing in January 2023.
