With Which country India begin ’Yudh Abhyas 2017’ military exercise recently?

(A) Pakistan

(B) ** United States of America

(C) China

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Exercise Yudh Abhyas, conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, tactics, techniques and procedures between the Armies of the two nations.

Concept note-2: -Notably, the Indo-US military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas 2022’ commenced in Uttarakhand’s Auli on November 19. The joint military exercise is conducted annually between India and US with the aim of exchanging best practices.

Concept note-3: -It started in 2002 and is undertaken by the armies of both countries to promote defence cooperation. The latest edition (17th edition) of the Yudh Abhyas Exercise is currently underway at Joint Base Elmendorf Richardson in Alaska, USA.

Concept note-4: -The 18th edition of Indo-US joint training exercise “YUDH ABHYAS 22” is scheduled to be conducted in Uttarakhand this month. Exercise Yudh Abhyas is conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, Tactics, Techniques and Procedures between the Armies of the two nations.

Concept note-5: -Yudh Abhyas 20: India-US joint military exercise commenced in Rajasthan. As part of defence cooperation, soldiers from India and the United States participated in a joint military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas 20.
