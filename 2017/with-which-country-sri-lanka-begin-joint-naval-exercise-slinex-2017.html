With Which country Sri Lanka Begin Joint Naval Exercise SLINEX 2017?

(A) ** India

(B) Nepal

(C) Pakistan

(D) Bhutan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SLINEX exemplifies the deep maritime engagement between India and Sri Lanka and has grown in scope over the years to strengthen mutual cooperation, in consonance with India’s policy of ‘Neighbourhood First’ and Hon’ble PMs vision of ‘Security and Growth for All in the Region (SAGAR)’.

Concept note-2: -SLINEX (Sri Lanka–India Naval Exercise) is being conducted in two phases.

Concept note-3: -The seventh edition of India-Sri Lanka joint maritime naval exercise SLINEX 2017 was held near Vishakhapatnam (Andhra Pradesh) in Bay of Bengal. It will be conducted from 7th to 14th September 2017.

Concept note-4: -The 9th edition of India – Sri Lanka Bilateral Maritime Exercise named SLINEX (Sri Lanka–India Naval Exercise) is held at Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-5: -SIMBEX-2022 is being conducted in two phases – Harbour Phase at Visakhapatnam from 26 to 27 October 2022 followed by the Sea Phase in Bay of Bengal from 28 to 30 October 2022.
