With Which India Joint Exercise Ekuverin Concludes recently?

(A) ** Maldives

(B) Singapore

(C) Russia

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Curtain raiser Indo Maldives joint Ex Ekuverin – 19 at Aundh Mil Stn, Pune from 07 to 20 Oct 19. Tenth edition of the Joint Military Exercise Ekuverin between the Indian Army and the Maldives National Defence Force will be organised from 07 to 20 October 2019 at Aundh Military Station in Pune, Maharashtra.
