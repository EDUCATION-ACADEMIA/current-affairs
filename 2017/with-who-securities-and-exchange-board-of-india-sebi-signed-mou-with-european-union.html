With, Who Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) signed MoU with European Union?

(A) ** Iran

(B) Pakistan

(C) Bahrain

(D) Afghanistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Securities and Exchange Board of India was constituted as a non-statutory body on April 12, 1988 through a resolution of the Government of India.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is the Narasimham Committee. The SEBI was given statutory recognition in 1992 on the recommendation of the Narasimham Committee. The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), was initially constituted on April 12, 1988.
