With Whom MobiKwik signed to enable one-tap payment solution for e-wallet users?

(A) ** Samsung Pay

(B) Airtel Pay

(C) Pay check

(D) Vodafone Pay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cash Deposit: Mobikwik and ICICI bank have tied up to enable customers to easily add money to Mobikwik wallet by using the deposit at bank feature.

Concept note-2: -Founded in 2009 by Bipin Preet Singh and Upasana Taku, we commenced operations as a mobile wallet to make digital payments convenient for our users.

Concept note-3: -Bipin Preet Singh is the Managing Director, Co-Founder, and CEO of our Group. He founded MobiKwik with a vision to transform the digital payments landscape in India, which was close to nil when we started in 2009.

Concept note-4: -On 8 November 2017, IDFC Bank entered into a strategic partnership with digital payments solution company MobiKwik to launch a co-branded virtual Visa prepaid card for customers of Mobikwik.

Concept note-5: -The Company is in the business of issuance of Semi-Closed prepaid Mobikwik Wallet and is authorized by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) to operate a Payment System.
