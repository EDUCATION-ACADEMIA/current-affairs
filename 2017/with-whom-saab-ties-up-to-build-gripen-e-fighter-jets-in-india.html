With Whom SAAB Ties up to Build Gripen E fighter jets in India?

(A) ** Adani Group

(B) Reliance Group

(C) Ashok Leyland Group

(D) Birla group

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Saab Aeronáutica Montagens, a new manufacturing plant located near Sao Paulo, has begun producing Gripen E/F aerostructures, including the tail cone and front fuselage for the single-seat Gripen E version of the jet.

Concept note-2: -The Advanced Medium Combat Aircraft (AMCA), under development by the Bengaluru-based Aeronautical Development Agency (ADA) of the DRDO (Defence Research and Development Organisation) for close to two decades, is a fifth-generation, medium-weight, multirole, and twin-engine aircraft.

Concept note-3: -The HAL Twin Engine Deck Based Fighter (TEDBF) is a canard delta wing, twin-engine, carrier-based, multirole combat aircraft currently under development for the Indian Navy.
