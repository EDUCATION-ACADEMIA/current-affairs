[SOURCES]

World Bank has agreed to provide loan to develop internal transmission arrangements of the upcoming Rewa Ultra Mega Solar (RUMS) project in which state

(A) Odisha

(B) Bihar

(C) Haryana

(D) ** Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -750 MW Rewa Ultra Mega Solar Power Project (Rewa UMSPP) is one of the largest single-site solar power plants in the world, which spread over an area of 1590 hectares in Rewa district of Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -The Welspun Solar MP project is a 151 megawatt (MW) photovoltaic power station built in Bhagwanpura village, just south of Diken, Jawad tehsil of Neemuch district, Madhya Pradesh state, in central India. Operational since February 2014, it is one of the largest solar power projects in India.

Concept note-3: -The solar parks are: (i) Rewa Ultra Mega Solar Park (750 MW); (ii) Neemuch–Mandsaur Solar Park (500 MW); (iii) Agar-Shajapur Solar Park (500 MW); (iv) Chhattarpur Solar Park (500 MW); and (v) Rajgarh–Morena Solar Park (500 MW).

Concept note-4: -Rewa Ultra Mega Solar Limited (RUMSL) was formed in 2015 and is a Joint Venture Company of Madhya Pradesh UrjaVikas Nigam Limited (MPUVN), and Solar Energy Corporation of India (SECI).
