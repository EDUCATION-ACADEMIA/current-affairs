“World Food India 2017”, a three-day global food fair is going to be held on

(A) ** November 3 - 5

(B) December 3 - 5

(C) August 3 - 5

(D) September 3 - 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Food India will be organized at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi from 3-5 November, 2017. A global event geared to transform the food economy, it will feature 40, 000 square metres of exhibitions, sector-focused seminars, sessions by various states and countries, an exciting Food Street, and more.

Concept note-2: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, will inaugurate World Food India 2017 on 3rd November 2017 from Vigyan Bhavan New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Market Overview 2023-2028: The India food processing market size reached INR 25, 455 Billion in 2022. Looking forward, IMARC Group expects the market to reach INR 45, 345 Billion by 2028, exhibiting a growth rate (CAGR) of 9.5% during 2023-2028.

Concept note-4: -According to UNO-India, there are nearly 195 million undernourished people in India, which is a quarter of the world’s hunger burden. Also, roughly 43% of children in India are chronically undernourished. India ranks 68 out of 113 major countries in terms of food security index 2022.
