World’s First Dividing Retractable Football pitch will be built in Tottenham’s new stadium at

(A) ** London

(B) China

(C) Russia

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Premier League club boast the only purpose-built NFL venue outside America with its state-of-the-art retractable pitch one of the key features. The incredible designs sees the real grass pitch split into three sections before rolling away into a storage area beneath the South Stand.

Concept note-2: -Tottenham Hotspur Stadium is north-east of Central London, outside of the Congestion Charge zone. The A10/Tottenham High Road runs north-to-south past the stadium, all the way to City/Commercial Road; which runs east-to-west through Central London, past King’s Cross and Euston stations.

Concept note-3: -Spurs moved into their state-of-the-art stadium – currently simply named Tottenham Hotspur Stadium – back in 2019 after the £1 billion ground was built on the site of the old White Hart Lane near Seven Sisters in London.

Concept note-4: -Other stadiums that incorporate retractable playing surfaces include the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium in the UK, Sapporo Dome in Japan, the University of Phoenix Stadium in the USA, the Gelredome in the Netherlands and the Veltins-Arena in Germany.
