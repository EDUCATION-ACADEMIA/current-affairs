(A) Oracle

(B) ** Microsoft

(C) Apple

(D) IBM

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Stratoluanch Roc completed a successful test flight over the Southern California desert on Thursday morning, according to Space.

Concept note-2: -Stratolaunch Roc, the world’s biggest airplane, completed its ninth test flight over Mojave Desert. Made to carry rockets and smaller vehicles, the Roc is also called a mothership aircraft.

Concept note-3: -The A380 is the largest passenger jet ever built by passenger capacity, volume, weight, and wingspan. The Boeing 747-8 beats it for length, however, as will the upcoming Boeing 777-9 (technically built but not yet in service).

Concept note-4: -The Scaled Composites Model 351 Stratolaunch or Roc is an aircraft built by Scaled Composites for Stratolaunch Systems to carry air-launch-to-orbit (ALTO) rockets. It was announced in December 2011, rolled out in May 2017, and flew the first time on April 13, 2019.
