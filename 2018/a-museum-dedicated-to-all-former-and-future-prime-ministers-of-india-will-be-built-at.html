A museum dedicated to all former and future Prime Ministers of India will be built at

(A) Vidhana Soudha, Bengaluru

(B) Ripon Building, Chennai

(C) ** Teen Murti Bhavan, Delhi

(D) Raj Bhavan, Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The museum is located at the Teen Murti Avenue earlier known as Nehru Memorial Museum and Library with renovated buildings and newly redeveloped new galleries. The Museum has two main buildings and each building has a reception at entrance.

Concept note-2: -The Nehru Memorial Museum, a legacy of the Nehru-Gandhi family, has been expanded as ‘Pradhan Mantri Sangrahalaya’ to showcase the life and times of not only the first PM, Jawaharlal Nehru, but all his successors. Built at Teen Murti Estate, PM Museum is brainchild of the current incumbent, PM Narendra Modi.

Concept note-3: -It was inaugurated by the then Prime Minister (and Nehru’s daughter), Indira Gandhi, on 6 February 1984.

Concept note-4: -Inaugurated on April 14 by Prime Minister Narendra Modi and its doors thrown open to the public a week later, the PMs’ Museum is located at the Nehru Memorial Museum and Library complex at Teen Murti Estate.
