According to the study by Microsoft, what is the cost inccured to the large-sized organisation in India due to cyberattacks?

(A) $5.1 million

(B) ** $10.3 million

(C) $17.8 million

(D) $16.5 million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It may come as no surprise that as more organizations evolve and scale their digital business models, the median cost of an attack has surged - from $10,000 last year to $18,000 in 2022. The US is bearing the brunt of generally higher cyberattack costs, with 40% of attack victims incurring costs of $25,000 or higher.

Concept note-2: -To further advance its security solutions, Microsoft raised its yearly investment to $4 billion as it announced a cumulative investment of $20 billion over the next five years in 2021.

Concept note-3: -In 2022, the average cost of a data breach has reached a record high of US$4.35 million, according to the 2022 cost of a data breach report by IBM and the Ponemon institute.

Concept note-4: -The global cost of cybercrime was estimated at some 8.4 trillion U.S. dollars in 2022.
