’Adopt a Heritage’ Scheme is being implemented by the Ministry of

(A) Agriculture and Farmers Welfare

(B) Railways & Transport

(C) ** Tourism & Culture

(D) Commerce and Industry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The President of India launched the scheme on World Tourism Day, i.e., 27 September 2017. The Adopt a Heritage Scheme allowed public and private sector enterprises to acquire top heritage areas of India and enhance tourism activities there.

Concept note-2: -When was the ‘Adopt a Heritage’ project launched? The project Adopt a Heritage: ‘Apni Dharohar Apni Pehchaan’ was launched by the Hon’ble President of India on 27th September 2017 on the occasion of World Tourism Day.
