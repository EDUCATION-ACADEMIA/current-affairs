After Delhi Assembly passed the resolution for full statehood, which of the following Union Territory demanded full statehood, recently?

(A) Chandigarh

(B) Daman and Diu

(C) Lakshadweep

(D) ** Puducherry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Goa, along with Daman and Diu was a Union Territory of India until 30 May 1987.

Concept note-2: -Option 3 is correct:The Union territory of Arunachal Pradesh was created in 1972. In 1987, it was conferred with statehood along with Mizoram and Goa.

Concept note-3: -Lack of better water supply to the region, lack of job opportunities and subsequent youth migration have been cited as major reasons for the demand of statehood.
