Air passengers may be allowed to make mobile calls and surf the internet during flights in the Indian airspace, possibly from _________

(A) April 2019

(B) March 2019

(C) February 2019

(D) ** January 2019

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to current FAA regulations, handheld electronic devices can be used during all phases of a flight. However, specific airlines may have different rules regarding phone use during takeoff or landing.

Concept note-2: -In addition, it is also very useful when your battery capacity on the device is running low. Now, using the flight mode on your device essentially means that you can no longer use its basic features. However, you can still use your phone’s wifi to access internet in flight mode.

Concept note-3: -Though technology and aviation have evolved greatly over the decades, you’re still not allowed to make in-flight calls.
