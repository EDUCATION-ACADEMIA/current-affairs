Air pollution will cause

(A) Infertility

(B) ** Chronic asthma

(C) Thyroid

(D) Kidney disease

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prolonged exposure can cause serious medical conditions, such as cancer, heart attacks and strokes. In fact, one in three deaths from strokes, lung cancer and chronic respiratory disease globally are caused by air pollution.

Concept note-2: -Air pollution and the release of gasses into the atmosphere can have many negative effects on the environment like global warming, damage of the ozone layer and acid rain. It can also make people sick. It can make it difficult to breathe and it. causes diseases such as lung cancer, respiratory infections, and heart.
