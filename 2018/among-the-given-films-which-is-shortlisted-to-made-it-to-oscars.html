Among the given films which is shortlisted to Made It To Oscars?

(A) ** Period, End of Sentence’

(B) Sanju

(C) Padmavati

(D) Race 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -RRR, Chhello Show, All That Breathes, and The Elephant Whisperers are the four Indian films nominated for Academy Awards in 2023. The Oscar nominations announcement will be live-streamed on YouTube, Oscar.com, Oscars.org, and other Academy social media platforms.

Concept note-2: -The song Naatu Naatu from the hit Telugu-language film RRR has won a best original song Oscar nomination. It’s the first Indian feature film to be nominated for anything other than best international film at the Academy Awards, which take place in March.

Concept note-3: -Being shortlisted means that the entry is one step away from making it to the final nominations for the award which will be held on March 12, 2023. The last shortlist announced by the Academy Awards was on December 22 when it put this out for 10 categories.
