As per Global Real Estate Transparency Index (GRETI) 2018, Indian real estate transparency ranked _________ globally.

(A) 22nd

(B) 50th

(C) ** 35th

(D) 40th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UK and US continue to hold the top two positions in terms of real estate market transparency worldwide. JLL’s 2022 Global Real Estate Transparency Index shows that transparency is continuing to improve across most countries and territories.

Concept note-2: -Ranked 54, JLL is the only International Property Consultant (IPC) that made it to the list of Top 100 Best Companies to Work For in 2022.

Concept note-3: -The JLL Global Real Estate Transparency Index is based on a combination of quantitative market data and information gathered through a survey of the global business network of JLL and LaSalle across 94 countries and 156 city markets.
