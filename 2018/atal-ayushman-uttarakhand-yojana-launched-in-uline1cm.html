Atal Ayushman Uttarakhand Yojana launched in _________?

(A) ** Uttarakhand

(B) Chennai

(C) Pune

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Date of implementation: 23.09. It was launched in September 2018 by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare. That ministry later established the National Health Authority as an organization to administer the program.

Concept note-2: -There is no restriction on family size, age or gender under AAUY Scheme. All members of eligible families as present in SECC 2011 data are automatically covered under this Ayushman Bharat Yojana in Uttarakhand. In case of hospitalization, poor people need not pay any money as this treatment is absolutely free.
