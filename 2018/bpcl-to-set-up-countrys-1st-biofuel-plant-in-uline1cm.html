BPCL To Set Up Country’s 1st Biofuel Plant in _________?

(A) ** Odisha

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Kerala

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BPCL is setting up an integrated 2G and 1G Bio Ethanol Refinery at Bargarh, Odisha. The Bio-ethanol Refinery, which will increase the production capacity to about 6 Cr lit per annum of ethanol.

Concept note-2: -Kinchlingi, a 16 household village in the state of Orissa in India, has been producing its own biodiesel since November 2004.

Concept note-3: -A total of 5 biofuel production plants have been installed in Rajasthan.
