BSE’s iconic building Phiroze Jeejeebhoy Towers secured trademark recently. Where is the Headquarter of BSE located?

(A) Pune

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Hyderabad

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) has secured image trademark for its iconic building, ‘Phiroze Jeejeebhoy Towers’ located on Dalal Street in Mumbai, Maharashtra under Trade Marks Act, 1999.

Concept note-2: -The Bombay Stock Exchange is located on Dalal Street in downtown Mumbai, India. In the 1850s, stockbrokers would conduct business under a banyan tree in front of the Mumbai town hall.

Concept note-3: -BSE secured a trademark for its iconic building Phiroze Jeejeebhoy Towers at Dalal Street in Mumbai in the year 2018. BSE was founded in the year 1875. It was started as a small group of brokers who worked under a banyan tree.

Concept note-4: -The BSE is the 6th largest stock exchange with an overall market capitalisation in the world with more than ₹276.713 Lakh Crore or $3.56 Trillion USD, as of January 2022. BSE Ltd. The corporate headquarters of BSE on Dalal Street.
