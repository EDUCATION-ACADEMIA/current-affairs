Cabinet approves “3 Year Action Plan of Agricultural Education Division and ICAR Institutes” with an outlay of

(A) Rs.2325.46 crore

(B) ** Rs.2225.46 crore

(C) Rs.2425.46 crore

(D) Rs.2525.46 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to ICAR standards, ICAR certifies ear tags, RFIDs, milk meters and genetic laboratories. ICAR also provides its members with the Certificate of Quality for specific field of actions and species. ICAR acts in five major field of certifications: Recording devices and milk meters certified by ICAR.

Concept note-2: -Agricultural Education uses a three-circle model of instruction. These are classroom and laboratory instruction, leadership development, and experiential learning.

Concept note-3: -As the three circle model of Agricultural Education suggests, there are three important aspects to a total, successful secondary Agricultural program: classroom/laboratory instruction, FFA, and Supervised Agricultural Experiences.

Concept note-4: -Formerly known as Imperial Council of Agricultural Research, it was established on 16 July 1929 as a registered society under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 in pursuance of the report of the Royal Commission on Agriculture. The ICAR has its headquarters at New Delhi.
