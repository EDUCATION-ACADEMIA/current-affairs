Conference on E-Mobility in Indian Railways held in _________?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Hyderabad

(C) Chennai

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution. On 4th September 2018, Conference on E-Mobility in Indian Railways was held in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -4. CSWI, HQ. Office, New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -The first electric train ran in India with the inauguration of services between Bombay VT and Kurla Harbour on 3rd Feb 1925 on ex-GIP Railway system. The section was electrified on 1500 Volt DC.

Concept note-4: -West Central Railway has become the first zone on Indian Railways whose entire route is lectrified. Electrification has been done for 3012 kms.

Concept note-5: -In this process electrification of 1, 973 route km has been achieved during 2022-23, which is 41 per cent higher as compared to corresponding period of 2021-22. In addition, 1, 161 km and 296 km of electrification of double lines and sidings respectively have also been completed till date.
