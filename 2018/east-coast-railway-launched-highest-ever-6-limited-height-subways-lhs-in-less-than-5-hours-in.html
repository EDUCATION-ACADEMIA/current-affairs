East Coast Railway launched highest ever 6 ’Limited Height Subways (LHS)’ in less than 5 hours, in

(A) Cuttack Division

(B) Brahmapur Division

(C) Vizianagaram Division

(D) ** Sambalpur Division

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Launching of 6 Limited Height Subways in Sambalpur Division is a first of its kind not only in East Coast Railway, but also in the entire Indian Railways.

Concept note-2: -As per Railway Board guide lines the height is 2.5 m to 3.6 m and width is 4 m.

Concept note-3: -Ref.: Railway Board’s letter No. 2006/CE-I/MISC-2(RUBS) dt. 01.11. 2006 to General Managers, All Indian Railway (copy enclosed).
