First ever dedicated dog park was constructed in _________?

(A) Chennai

(B) ** Hyderabad

(C) New Delhi

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The country’s first ever dog park is inaugurated in Hyderabad this week and is all set to welcome the pooch lovers. This newly opened dog park in Hyderabad is spread across an area of 1.3 acres of land and is a perfect place to take your dogs on outing.

Concept note-2: -Billed as India’s first, an exclusive and certified ‘Dog Park’ boasting of facilities such as a walking track, off-leash area, and a clinic is all set for inauguration in Kondapur, Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: -The first dog park in the United States was the Ohlone Dog Park, which was founded by Martha Scott Benedict and Doris Richards in 1979 in Berkeley, Calif. Since then, dog parks have become standard amenities in developing city and suburban neighborhoods across the country, but are they actually good for dogs?

Concept note-4: -Few people know that when they are at play in a dog park, there was one woman, Doris Richard, who played a major role in the development of places for canines to play in America. In fact, Doris was the founder of what is considered the first city dog park – Ohlone Dog Park in Berkeley, CA.
