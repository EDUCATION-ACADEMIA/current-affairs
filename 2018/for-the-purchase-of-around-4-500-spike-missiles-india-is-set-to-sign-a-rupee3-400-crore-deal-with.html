For the purchase of around 4, 500 Spike missiles, India is set to sign a Rupee.3, 400-crore deal with

(A) Russia

(B) ** Israel

(C) France

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Total 85 Spike-LR/ER and 110 Spike-NLOS missiles.

Concept note-2: -Spike NLOS is a multi-purpose, electro-optical/infrared missile system. Its advanced rocket motor provides capability to reach ranges up to 32 kilometers.

Concept note-3: -The name Spike would be familiar to people in India: It is a family of Israeli anti-tank missiles, with the variants primarily differing in range. The Indian Army has inducted a man-portable variant of the Spike, which can destroy tanks and bunkers up to four kilometres away.

Concept note-4: -Javelin’s outstanding performance comes at a cost. According to the U.S. Army’s 2023 budget for missile procurement, the cost for a single Javelin all-up round – that is, one missile – is $197, 884. That’s only slightly cheaper than a new 2022 Ferrari Roma, and more than enough for older models.
