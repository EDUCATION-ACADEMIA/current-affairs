For which organisation did the Hindustan Shipyard Limited build India’s first ever missile tracking ship?

(A) ** National Technical Research Organisation

(B) Indian Navy

(C) Indian-Ocean Rim Organisation

(D) Hindustan Shipping and Research Organisation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -INS Dhruv is India’s only navy vessel suitable for long surveillance of nuclear missiles.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is INS Dhruv. India has launched its first ballistic missile and satellite tracking ship INS Dhruv. It is a 10, 000-tonne vessel and was commissioned from the southern Indian port of Visakhapatnam. It has been built by Hindustan Shipyard along with DRDO and NTRO.

Concept note-3: -INS Dhruv, the latest addition to the Indian Navy’s arsenal, was built by the Hindustan Shipyard in collaboration with the DRDO and NTRO.
