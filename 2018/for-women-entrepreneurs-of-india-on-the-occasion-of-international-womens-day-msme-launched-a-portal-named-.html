For Women Entrepreneurs of India, on the occasion of International Women’s Day, MSME launched a portal named:-

(A) e-Samvad

(B) ** Udyam Sakhi

(C) Shakthi

(D) Mathru Poorna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International Women’s Day: MSME Ministry launches ‘SAMARTH’ to promote women entrepreneurship.

Concept note-2: -What is Udyam Portal? About: It was launched on 1st July, 2020. It’s an online system for registering MSMEs, launched by the Union MSME ministry.

Concept note-3: -Women entrepreneurs in India play a significant role in advancing the economic growth of the country. They collectively contribute 3.09% of industrial output and employ 10% of the total workers engaged in different economic activities in the country, according to ifc.org.

Concept note-4: -Mudra Yojana Scheme Mudra Yojana is a scheme that might benefit women seeking to start or expand their small business.

Concept note-5: -NITI Aayog has launched a Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP) for providing an ecosystem for budding & existing women entrepreneurs across the country.
