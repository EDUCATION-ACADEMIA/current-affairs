Former India hockey skipper, who recently announced his retirement from international hockey is _________?

(A) P. R. Sreejesh

(B) Birendra Lakra

(C) Manpreet Singh

(D) ** Sardar Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Harmanpreet Singh has been named captain of the Indian team, while Amit Rohidas will be his deputy for the marquee event. India, the Tokyo Olympics bronze medallist, will look to end its 47-year drought for the World Cup. It last won the trophy in 1975.

Concept note-2: -A storied sports career that saw him captain the India men’s national hockey team led to Sandeep Singh’s beginning in politics in 2019 as he won the Haryana Assembly election from Pehowa and became the state sports minister.

Concept note-3: -At the quadrennial competition, drag flicker Harmanpreet Singh will serve as the team’s captain, with Amit Rohidas serving as vice-captain. 33 players were put through a two-day trial at the SAI Centre in Bengaluru before the final selection was chosen.

Concept note-4: -Indian Hockey Squad Captain 2023 One of the Goalkeepers of the Indian Female Hockey team, Savita Punia, is the captain of the Indian Female Hockey Team 2023. Defender Deep Grace Ekka is the vice-captain of the Indian Female Hockey Team 2023.
