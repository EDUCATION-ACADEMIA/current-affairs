(A) ** October 20, 2018

(B) November 5th, 2018

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first known music festival was the Pythian Games, a precursor of the Olympics, which was held from the late sixth century BC at the sanctuary of Apollo at Delphi.

Concept note-2: -THE BRIEF HISTORY OF MUSIC FESTIVALS Prior to becoming the mainstream commodity so adored today ( Learn more about the 32 million people attending festivals in the United States alone), festival origins can be traced back as far as 6th century B.C. with ancient Greek gatherings music and competition.

Concept note-3: -Dhanteras, Naraka Chaturdasi, Lakshmi Puja, Govardhan Puja, and Bhai Dooj are the five days of Diwali.

Concept note-4: -The word “festival” was originally used as an adjective from the late fourteenth century, deriving from Latin via Old French. In Middle English, a “festival dai” was a religious holiday. Its first recorded used as a noun was in 1589 (as “Festifall").
