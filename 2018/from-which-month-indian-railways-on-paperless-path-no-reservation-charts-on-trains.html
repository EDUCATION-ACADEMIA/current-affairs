From Which month Indian Railways on paperless path No reservation charts on trains?

(A) ** March

(B) June

(C) May

(D) August

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Railways Going Paperless, No Reservation Charts On Trains From March 1.

Concept note-2: -The first chart for any train is prepared by the authorities about 4 hours before the scheduled departure time of the train from the originating station.

Concept note-3: -The final chart is made 30min before departure of train. Berth and coach number is assigned to all passengers having final status as confirmed or RAC.

Concept note-4: -Whenever a passenger enters a PNR number in the online portal, he/she might get the message “Chart is Not Prepared Yet”. This means the final chart is not prepared after considering all the factors like quota seat placement, RAC adjustment, tatkal seat confirmation, etc.

Concept note-5: -Passengers can also check for the train reservation chart online on the IRCTC website. The IRCTC reservation charts offer a complete visual picture of the train layout, berth status, occupancy and more.
