EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -September 2, 2022 Results of G20 Digital Economy Ministerial Meeting. On September 1, 2022, the G20 Digital Economy Ministerial Meeting was held in Bali, Indonesia, and Mr. TSUGE Yoshifumi, State Minister for Internal Affairs and Communications, attended the meeting.

Concept note-2: -The 2023 G20 Delhi summit (Hindi: 2023 G20 ) is the upcoming eighteenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), a summit scheduled to take place in Pragati Maidan, New Delhi in 2023.

Concept note-3: -The Group of Twenty, or G20, is the premier forum for international cooperation on the most important aspects of the international economic and financial agenda. It brings together the world’s major advanced and emerging economies.

Concept note-4: -The Riyadh Initiative on the Future of the WTO (the Riyadh Initiative) was launched by the Saudi G20 Presidency (the Presidency), building on the commitment from our Leaders, to provide an additional opportunity for members to work constructively towards the necessary reform of the WTO.
