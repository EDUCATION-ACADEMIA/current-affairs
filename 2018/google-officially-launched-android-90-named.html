Google officially launched Android 9.0 named

(A) KitKat

(B) Froyo

(C) ** Pie

(D) Donut

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Android Pie (codenamed Android P during development), also known as Android 9 (API 28) is the ninth major release and the 16th version of the Android mobile operating system. It was first released as a developer preview on March 7, 2018, and was released publicly on August 6, 2018.

Concept note-2: -Succeeded by the Android 11 and preceded by the Android 9.0 “Pie, ” the Android 10 was earlier named the Android Q. It came with an adaptive battery setting (upgraded one) and also dark mode. Thus, the battery life gets much longer as compared to the precursors.

Concept note-3: -Android 9 (Pie) or Android 8 (Oreo) Every year in August, Google unveils its new android version which plays with the minds of technology geeks. Expectations with Google’s operating system are so high, not only the experts but every user looks at what new Google is delivering this time.
