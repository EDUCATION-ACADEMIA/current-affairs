How many agreements did India signed with Palestine recently?

(A) ** 6

(B) 5

(C) 4

(D) 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Three pacts – on building schools, on Information Technology and imparting vocational training – were signed between the two side after the talks. Indian Prime Minister Manmohan Singh said: “Support for the Palestinian cause has been a cornerstone of India’s foreign policy.

Concept note-2: -In 1974, India became the first Non-Arab State to recognize Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) as the sole and legitimate representative of the Palestinian people. In 1988, India became one of the first countries to recognize the Palestinian State.

Concept note-3: -The two countries have an extensive and comprehensive economic, military, and political relationship. They are both in I2U2 Group. The Indian and Israeli governments have stated they have deep ties rooted in mutual problems and a desire to address them, especially in regards to Israeli techs applications for India.
