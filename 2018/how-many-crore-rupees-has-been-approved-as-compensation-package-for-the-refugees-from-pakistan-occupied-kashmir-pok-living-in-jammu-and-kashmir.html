How many crore rupees has been approved as compensation package for the refugees from Pakistan-occupied Kashmir (PoK) living in Jammu and Kashmir?

(A) Rs 5, 000-crore

(B) Rs 4, 000-crore

(C) ** Rs 2, 000-crore

(D) Rs 6, 000-crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The territory shares a border to the north with Gilgit-Baltistan, together with which it is referred to by the United Nations and other international organizations as “Pakistani-administered Kashmir".

Concept note-2: -Pakistan-Occupied-Kashmir is the full form of POK. In 1947, Pakistan actually invaded Pakistan Occupied Kashmir (POK). POK has been split into two parts: Azad Kashmir and Gilgit-Baltistan.
