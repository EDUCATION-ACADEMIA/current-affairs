How many crores did DAC Approves Proposals Worth?

(A) ** 15, 935 Crores

(B) 17, 000 Crores

(C) 18, 956 Crores

(D) 20, 000 Crores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DAC approves capital procurement proposals worth ₹4, 276 crore.

Concept note-2: -DAC is the highest decision-making body in the Defence Ministry for deciding on new policies and capital acquisitions for the three services – Army, Navy and Air Force, and the Indian Coast Guard. The Defence Acquisition Council is headed by the Defence Minister.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Armed Forces are the military forces of the Republic of India. It consists of three professional uniformed services: the Indian Army, Indian Navy, and Indian Air Force.
