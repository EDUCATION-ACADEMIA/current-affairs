How many crores did PGCIL Ink Pact With Power Ministry recently?

(A) ** 25, 000

(B) 35, 000

(C) 45, 000

(D) 65, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Power Grid Corporation of India Limited (POWERGRID), a Schedule ‘A’, ‘Maharatna ‘ Company operating under Ministry of Power, is engaged in bulk transmission of power through its EHVAC (upto 765 kV level) and ±800/±500kV HVDC transmission network.

Concept note-2: -The national electric grid in India has an installed capacity of 410.3 GW as of 31 December 2022. Renewable power plants, which also include large hydroelectric power plants, constitute 40.7% of the total installed capacity.

Concept note-3: -Power Grid Corporation of India Limited(POWERGRID), is a Schedule ‘A’, ‘Maharatna’ Public Sector Enterprise of Govt. of India which was incorporated on 23rd Oct 1989 under the Company Act, 1956.
