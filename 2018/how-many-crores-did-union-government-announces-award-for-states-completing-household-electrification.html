How many crores did Union Government announces award for states completing Household Electrification?

(A) ** 100

(B) 90

(C) 85

(D) 250

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first DISCOM/Power Departments to complete 100% household electrification will be felicitated with cash award of Rs. 50 Lakh for the employees and Rs. 100 crore grant to be spent for distribution infrastructure.

Concept note-2: -Gujarat is the first State in India to achieve 100% electrification of households. All 18,065 villages of Gujarat state have now access to a three-phase uninterrupted electric supply. Jyotigram Yojana is an initiative of the Gujarat government throughout the state.

Concept note-3: -In 2006, Gujarat became the first state to fully electrify all its villages followed by Andhra Pradesh in 2016 and Kerala in 2017.

Concept note-4: -Andhra Pradesh becomes second state to achieve 100% electrification.

Concept note-5: -Hence, State C has the maximum percentage of electrified villages.
