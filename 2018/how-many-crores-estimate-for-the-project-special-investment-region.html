How many crores estimate for the project “Special Investment Region”?

(A) ** 2, 420 crores

(B) 1, 550 crores

(C) 4, 500 crores

(D) 2, 800 crores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dholera Special Investment Region (DSIR) is a Greenfield industrial planned city in Gujarat’s Ahmedabad district, around 100 kilometers to the south-west. A legal framework known as the Special Investment Region Act 2009 was made by the Government of Gujarat.

Concept note-2: -Out of 24 industrial nodes identified on Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor, 6 nodes are located in the State of Gujarat.

Concept note-3: -Emerging as the world’s largest auto hub, Mandal Becharaji Special Investment Region (MBSIR) is a hotbed for Japanese automobile and auto component manufacturing industries. Companies like Suzuki Motor Corp, Honda Motorcycles and Scooter India and Toyota Tsusho India have established their units within MBSIR.
