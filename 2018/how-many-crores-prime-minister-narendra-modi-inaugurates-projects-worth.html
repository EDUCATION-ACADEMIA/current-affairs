EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Prime Minister and other ministers of his cabinet get salary under Article 75 of the Indian Constitution. Prime Minister Narendra Modi gets a salary of more than one lakh 60 thousand rupees every month. Apart from this, the Prime Minister also gets many facilities.

Concept note-2: -Government releases Rs 22 crore per year for the upkeep of Rashtrapati Bhavan and other expenditures. He gets Rs 50, 000 as basic pay, Rs 3, 000 as expenditure-related allowance, Rs 2 thousand per day that amounts to Rs 60, 000 per month as other allowances and Rs 45, 000 per month as parliamentary constituency allowance.

Concept note-3: -Shri Narendra Modi was sworn-in as India’s Prime Minister on 30th May 2019, marking the start of his second term in office.

Concept note-4: -Social welfare In June 2015, Modi launched the “Housing for All By 2022” project, which intends to eliminate slums in India by building about 20 million affordable homes for India’s urban poor.
