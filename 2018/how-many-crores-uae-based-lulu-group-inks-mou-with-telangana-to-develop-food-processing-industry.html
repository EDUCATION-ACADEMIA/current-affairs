How many crores UAE based Lulu group inks MoU with Telangana to develop food processing industry?

(A) ** 2500

(B) 1500

(C) 1850

(D) 1950

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Lulu Group has said it would invest ₹500 crore to set up a food processing factory in Telangana.

Concept note-2: -As per the MoU, Lulu will set up shopping malls and hypermarkets in Noida, Varanasi, Gorakhpur, Ayodhya, Kanpur, and Prayagraj with a total investment of Rs 4, 500 crore.

Concept note-3: -Yusuff Ali Musaliam Veettil Abdul Kader, popularly known as M. A. Yusuff Ali (born 15 November 1955) is a UAE-based Indian businessman and billionaire. He is the Chairman and Managing Director of LuLu Group International, which owns the LuLu Hypermarket chain worldwide and LuLu International Shopping Mall.
