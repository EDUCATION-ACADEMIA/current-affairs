How many health centers Ayushman Bharat to spread its roots with an aim to set up recently?

(A) ** 150000

(B) 25000

(C) 45000

(D) 86000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fulfilling the principles of “Antyodaya”- leaving no one behind, 150,000 Ayushman Bharat Health and Wellness Centres (AB-HWCs) are transforming the delivery of comprehensive primary health care services which are universally free and cater to all age groups from birth to death, through a continuum of care approach.

Concept note-2: -This step seeks to provide healthcare assurance to the migrant workers, who have lost their jobs and sources of livelihood during the lockdown due to Covid-19.

Concept note-3: -Notes: Karnataka has been ranked first in establishing Health and Wellness Centres under Ayushman Bharat Program in 2020-21.

Concept note-4: -It has two goals, one, creating a network of health and wellness infrastructure across the nation to deliver comprehensive primary healthcare services, and another is to provide insurance cover to at least 40 per cent of India’s population which is majorly deprived of secondary and tertiary care services.

Concept note-5: -Karnataka Health Minister Dr K Sudhakar Saturday said the state has ranked first in the registration of health professionals and number two in the registration of health facilities under the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission.
