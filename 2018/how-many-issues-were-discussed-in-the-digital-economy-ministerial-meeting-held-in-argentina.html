Concept note-1: -Argentina has selected the theme, ‘Building consensus for fair and sustainable development, ’ for the 2018 G20 Leaders’ Summit, and has identified three key issues for the agenda: the future of work, infrastructure for development, and a sustainable food future.

Concept note-2: -September 2, 2022 Results of G20 Digital Economy Ministerial Meeting. On September 1, 2022, the G20 Digital Economy Ministerial Meeting was held in Bali, Indonesia, and Mr. TSUGE Yoshifumi, State Minister for Internal Affairs and Communications, attended the meeting.

Concept note-3: -Digital competitiveness rankings by country worldwide 2022 As of 2022, Denmark ranked as the most digitally competitive country in the world.

Concept note-4: -India’s digital economy grew 2.4 times faster than the economy between 2014 and 2019 generating around 62.4 million jobs, said a paper published in the latest bulletin of the Reserve Bank of India. In absolute terms, the size of the digital economy has grown from $107.7 billion in 2014 to $222.5 billion in 2019.
