How many lakhs Gramin Dak Sevaks to be benefitted by the wage revision?

(A) ** 3.07 lakh

(B) 5 lakhs

(C) 6.5 lakhs

(D) 8 lakhs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -3.07 lakh Gramin Dak Sevaks will be benefitted by this wage revision.

Concept note-2: -2022. It has, therefore, been decided that the Dearness Allowance payable to the Gramin Dak Sevaks shall be at the same rates as payable to Central Government Employees ie 38% (percent) with effect from the 1st July, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The minimum TRCA for 4 hours/level 1 in the TRCA slab has been increased to INR 12000/-per month and maximum to INR 29, 380/-.

Concept note-4: -The GDS Salary is known as Time Related Continuity Allowance (TRCA).

Concept note-5: -The salary for GDS posts is referred to as Time Related Continuity Allowance (TRCA).
