How many lakhs Monetary Limit For Filing Cases In DRT Doubled?

(A) ** 20

(B) 56

(C) 35

(D) 45

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Act is applicable to cases where the amount of debt due to any bank or financial institution defined under the Act or a consortium of banks or financial institutions is Rs. 20 lakh or more.

Concept note-2: -Rs. 1, 000 for every one lakh, subject to a maximum of Rs. 1, 50, 000.

Concept note-3: -The jurisdiction of the Permanent Lok Adalats is upto Rs. Ten Lakhs.

Concept note-4: -What is the minimum due amount to file a case in DRT? The minimum amount owed to financial institutions (FIs) and banks should be more than 20 lac rupees.

Concept note-5: -100 crores or more are eligible NBFCs that are covered under the SARFAESI Act to enforce security interest on debts amounting to at least Rs. 50 lacs.
