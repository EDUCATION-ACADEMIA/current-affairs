Concept note-1: -HD maps are often captured using an array of sensors, such as LiDARs, radars, digital cameras, and GPS. HD maps can also be constructed using aerial imagery. High-definition maps for self-driving cars usually include map elements such as road shape, road marking, traffic signs, and barriers.
