How many new features are added In E-NAM Platform by agriculture ministry?

(A) ** 6

(B) 10

(C) 15

(D) 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The new features of the e-NAM platform allow the remote bidding and mobile-based payment for traders which will strengthen the agriculture marketing. The added features of the e-NAM platform promote the inter-State trade by providing online transport facilities for distant buyers.

Concept note-2: -The Centre on Tuesday informed Parliament that it has achieved integration of 1,260 wholesale mandis, located in 22 states and three union territories, with the electronic-National Agriculture Market (e-NAM).

Concept note-3: -Transparent online trading with enhanced accessibility to the market. Real time price discovery for better & stable price realization for producers. Reduced transaction cost for buyers. Availability of information on e-Nam mobile app about commodity prices.

Concept note-4: -i. Integration of additional 415 mandis with e-NAM, taking the total number of mandis to 1000 across 18 states and 3 UTs, thus bringing more markets access to farmers to sell their agriculture produce. ii.
