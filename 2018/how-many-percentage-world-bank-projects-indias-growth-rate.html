Concept note-1: -The World Bank has revised India’s 2022-23 GDP growth forecast upward to 6.9 per cent from the earlier estimate of 6.5 per cent, due to robust economic activities according to Dhruv Sharma, Senior Economist at the World Bank.

Concept note-2: -"Growth in India is projected to slow from 8.7% in FY2021/22 to 6.9% in FY2022/23, the latter revised 0.6 percentage point lower since June,” the report said. It further said that India is expected to be the fastest growing economy of the seven largest emerging markets and developing economies (EMDEs).
