How many places has been taken under Phase II of Swachh Bharat Mission?

(A) 12

(B) 5

(C) ** 10

(D) 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So far 32, 791 individual soak pits and 1, 331 community soak pits have been constructed.

Concept note-2: -ODF Plus activities under Phase II of Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) will reinforce ODF behaviours and focus on providing interventions for the safe management of solid and liquid waste in villages.

Concept note-3: -It is a restructured version of the Nirmal Bharat Abhiyan launched in 2009 that failed to achieve its intended targets. One step towards cleanliness. Phase 1 of the Swachh Bharat Mission lasted till October 2019. Phase 2 is being implemented between 2020–21 and 2024–25 to help cement the work of Phase 1.

Concept note-4: -13, 239.89 crore. And, the Mission allocation for SBM-U 2.0 (2021-2026) is Rs. 30, 980.20 crore.

Concept note-5: -On February 19, the cabinet approved the second phase of the Swachh Bharat Mission, Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s pet project focussed on sustainability of ODF and management of solid and liquid waste.
