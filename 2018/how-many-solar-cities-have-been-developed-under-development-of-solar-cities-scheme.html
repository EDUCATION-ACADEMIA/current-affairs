How many solar cities have been developed under Development of Solar Cities scheme?

(A) ** 60

(B) 50

(C) 45

(D) 35

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Option 3 is correct: The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy under its scheme “Development of Solar Cities” has approved/sanctioned 60 Cities including 13 Pilot and 5 Model Cities up to 12th Five-year Plan period. Minister of State (Independent charge) for Power & New and Renewable Energy is Shri Raj Kumar Singh.

Concept note-2: -Abstract: The Ministry has launched a Scheme on “Development of Solar Cities”: under which a total of 60 cities/towns are proposed to be supported for development as “Solar/ Green Cities”. At least one city in each State to a maximum of five cities in a State may be supported by the Ministry.
