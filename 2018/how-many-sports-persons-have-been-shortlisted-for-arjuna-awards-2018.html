How many sports persons have been shortlisted for Arjuna Awards 2018?

(A) 8

(B) 10

(C) 15

(D) ** 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -20 sportspersons are picked for this year’s Arjuna Awards.

Concept note-2: -Hockey player Manpreet Singh received the Arjuna Award 2018 from President Kovind. He won Bronze in the Asian Games 2018, Gold in Asia Cup 2017, Silver in CWG 2014 and Gold in Asian Games 2014 as a part of the Men’s team.

Concept note-3: -To date, 57 cricketers have been honoured with the Arjuna Award, including 12 women. Some of the notable names to have received the Arjuna Award for cricket include former captains like Mansoor Ali Khan Pataudi, Sunil Gavaskar, Kapil Dev, Sachin Tendulkar and Virat Kohli.

Concept note-4: -Apart from Sharath, 25 sportspersons were presented with the Arjuna Award, given out for consistent performance over a period of four years.

Concept note-5: -Three awards went to Kabaddi’s way this year after the national sporting honors were announced by the Government of India.
