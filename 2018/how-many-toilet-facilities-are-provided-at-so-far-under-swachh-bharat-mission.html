How many Toilet facilities are provided at so far under Swachh Bharat Mission?

(A) ** 192

(B) 150

(C) 180

(D) 230

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About 10.9 crore individual household latrines (IHHLs) have been constructed in the country under SBM (G).

Concept note-2: -Since October 2014, over 9.5 crore toilets have been built all over the country and 564, 658 villages have been declared Open Defecation Free (ODF). As on 14 June 2019, 30 States/UTs are 100 per cent covered with Individual household Latrine (IHHL). SBM has significantly improved health outcomes.

Concept note-3: -4, 000 per household toilet for each identified beneficiary household, in states and UTs. However, for North eastern and Hilly states, the Central government incentive will be Rs 10, 800 per unit.

Concept note-4: -They mobilise communities towards toilet use as well as help develop their abilities to build the toilets themselves.
