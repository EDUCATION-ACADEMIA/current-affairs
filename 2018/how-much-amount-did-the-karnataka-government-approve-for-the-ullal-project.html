(B) Rs.200 crore

(C) Rs.197 crore

(D) Rs.195 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -₹18.85 lakh crore (US$236 billion) (2022–23 est.)

This is a growth of 9.6% over the revised estimate of GSDP for 2021-22 (Rs 17, 21, 336 crore).
