How much did the Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) Board approve for the Phase IV proposal?

(A) Rs.42, 000 crore

(B) ** Rs.46, 000 crore

(C) Rs.52, 000 crore

(D) Rs.58, 000 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DDA grants Rs 130 crore to DMRC for Delhi metro phase 4 project.

Concept note-2: -Delhi Metro Phase 4’s Priority Corridors are expected to be completed and opened in June 2026 much later than its deadline of December 31, 2024.

Concept note-3: -The 29-km line will connect the Janakpuri West Metro station on the Magenta line and the RK Ashram station on the Blue line. The Janakpuri-RK Ashram stretch will run through Haiderpur, Sadar Bazar, Azadpur, Bhalswa, Pushpanjali Enclave, Mangolpuri and Paschim Vihar.

Concept note-4: -A senior official said the total budgetary outlay for year 2023-24 for all metro projects stands at Rs 19, 518 crore. The allocation in 2022-23 was Rs 19, 130 crore. However, the revised budget estimates for the same for 2022-23 was 15, 628 crore, according to budget documents.
