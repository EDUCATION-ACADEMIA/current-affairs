(A) ** $10 million

(B) $55 million

(C) $100 million

(D) $5 million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Exim Bank of India has extended a line of credit of USD 100 million (Rs 730 crore) on behalf of the Indian government to the Africa Finance Corporation (AFC) to develop infrastructure in the continent and boost economic revival of countries in the region.

Concept note-2: -Line of Credit: Under this scheme, the EXIM bank offers credit to foreign governments and their agencies and overseas financial institutions to finance import of eligible goods from India.
