How much revenue loss was caused to KRSTC due to Kerala flood?

(A) Rs.23 lakh

(B) Rs.25 lakh

(C) ** Rs.27 lakh

(D) Rs.29 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -KSRTC’s debt service commitments were about 4% of total income during FY21-FY22. And it is likely to be at about INR1,180 million during FY23-FY24.

Concept note-2: -KSRTC’s income per day is Rs 4.39 crore but the expenditure amounts to Rs 8.76 crore. These costs include pensions and salaries. The daily loss of KSRTC is Rs 4.37 crore.

Concept note-3: -Bengaluru: The Karnataka State Road Transport (KSRTC), which suffered losses previous year, has made a turnaround and recorded a profit of ‘10.29 crore.

Concept note-4: -Karnataka RTC, which was once in loss, is now marching towards profit due to key tweaks in the way of its operations. Also, Karnataka RTC is the top revenue grabber public transportation service provider among the south Indian states. In fact, the town-to-town services are the major contributors to the revenue.
