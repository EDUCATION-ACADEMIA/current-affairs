IAMAI Forms AR/VR Committee To Promote Technologies in _________?

(A) ** India

(B) Saudi Arabia

(C) Denmark

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IAMAI forms AR/VR committee to promote the technologies in India.

Concept note-2: -AR is intended to enhance the virtual world and the real world. VR replaces the real world with a fictional reality, which is primarily intended to enhance games.

Concept note-3: -The AI algorithm can be used to collect more data that’s detailed to multiple complement AR sensors, such as gyroscopes and GPS. Furthermore, AI can be used with AR to offer better mobile experiences to users through interactive and engaging apps.

Concept note-4: -VR and AR can be used to make simulations where training can be safe and free of consequences. Employers can create an exact simulation of their operations with the equipment they use. New employees can then gain expertise through the simulation and experiment to their heart’s content.

Concept note-5: -In recent years, we have tried to perfect AI algorithms to act more human-like such as being able to make decisions. In addition, mobile technology is being developed with AI algorithms. Virtual reality is a form of interaction between humans and computers in which a real or imaginary environment is simulated.
