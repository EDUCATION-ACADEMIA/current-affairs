In India, the Kargil conflict against Pakistan is also referred to as

(A) ** Operation Vijay

(B) Operation Polo

(C) Operation Sarp Vinash

(D) Operation Meghdoot

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In India, the conflict is also referred to as Operation Vijay (Hindi: , lit. ‘Victory’), which was the codename of the Indian military operation in the region.

Concept note-2: -Kargil War, conflict in May–July 1999 between Pakistan and India in Kargil, a sector of the disputed Kashmir region located along the line of control that demarcates the Pakistan-and India-administered portions of Kashmir.

Concept note-3: -The Kargil War was an armed conflict that took place between India and Pakistan from May to July 1999. It is also referred to as “Operation Vijay", which was the name of the Indian operation to clear the Kargil sector.

Concept note-4: -The code name of India-Pakistan kargil war was Operation Vijay.

Concept note-5: -A war took place between India and Pakistan in the Kargil region over the Kashmir issue, in the year 1999. This war came to be called the Kargil War or the Kargil conflict.
