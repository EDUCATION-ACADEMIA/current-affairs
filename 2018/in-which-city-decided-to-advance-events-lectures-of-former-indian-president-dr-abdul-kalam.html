In which city decided to advance events, lectures of former Indian President Dr Abdul Kalam?

(A) ** Singapore

(B) Sydney

(C) New york

(D) Malaysia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, the former President of India, is known as the ‘Missile Man of India’ for his contribution to developing India’s missile projects, Prithvi and Agni missiles. He also played a significant role in developing the first indigenous satellite launch vehicle.

Concept note-2: -Abdul Kalam who was the 11th president of India. Kalam was born and brought up in Rameswaram and became a scientist and worked for DRDO and ISRO. After his sudden demise at Shillong on 27th July 2015, he was laid to rest at Pei Karumbu, Rameswaram on 30th July 2015.

Concept note-3: -Before becoming the President of India, he worked with the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) as an aerospace engineer.

Concept note-4: -He collapsed on the stage while delivering a speech at the Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Shillong. The nation was in shock when the news went out that Dr Kalam, former Indian President, was in hospital and passed away soon after.
