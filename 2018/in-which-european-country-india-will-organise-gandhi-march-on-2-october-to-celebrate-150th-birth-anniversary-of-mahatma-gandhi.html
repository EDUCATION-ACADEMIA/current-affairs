In which European country, India will organise ’Gandhi March’ on 2 October to celebrate 150th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi?

(A) Germany

(B) South Africa

(C) ** Netherlands

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Embassy organized various events to commemorate the 150th Birth Anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi in Spain.

Concept note-2: -2. “It is our social responsibility as citizens of India to help fulfill Gandhiji’s vision of Clean India, by his 150th birth anniversary in 2019.”

Concept note-3: -Every year on October 2, Gandhi Jayanti is observed to commemorate the birth anniversary of Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, also known as Mahatma Gandhi. Mahatma Gandhi was one of the most instrumental leaders in the Indian freedom struggle, and has been regarded as the ‘Father of the Nation".

Concept note-4: -Gandhi Jayanti is being observed today, on October 2, 2022. Mahatma Gandhi was born on October 2, 1869 in Porbandar city of Gujrat.

Concept note-5: -The Nation is gearing up to offer Karyanjali as a Shraddhanjali (Tribute) to Mahatma Gandhi and Kasturba Gandhi on their 150th Birth Anniversary. Gandhiji’s freedom struggle culminated in India’s Independence on 15th August, 1947.
