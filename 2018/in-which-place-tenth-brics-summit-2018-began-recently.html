In Which place Tenth BRICS Summit 2018 began recently?

(A) ** Johannesburg

(B) Australia

(C) New york

(D) Denmark

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 10th BRICS Summit will convene in South Africa. The theme for the 10th BRICS Summit is, ‘BRICS in Africa: Collaboration for Inclusive Growth and Shared Prosperity in the 4th Industrial Revolution’.

Concept note-2: -Lt Gen Prakash Katoch (27 March 2021).

Concept note-3: -South Africa’s Durban is set to host the 15th BRICS Summit in August this year. South Africa took over the presidency of the five-nation bloc from China on January 1 this year. This is the third time South Africa takes the reins of the presidency of BRICS for a year.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Shanghai. BRICS(Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa) headquarter is located in Shanghai, China.
