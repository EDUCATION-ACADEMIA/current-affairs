In which state is Mirzapur located?

(A) Andhra Pradesh

(B) Punjab

(C) ** Uttar Pradesh

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mirzapur is a city in Uttar Pradesh, India, roughly 650 km from both Delhi and Kolkata, almost 87 kms from Allahabad and 67 kms from Varanasi.

Concept note-2: -It is known for its carpets and brassware industries, and the folk tradition of kajri and birha music. The city is surrounded by several hills of the Maikal range and is the headquarters of Mirzapur district.

Concept note-3: -Most of the city was established by British officers, but the starting development was founded by the most famous officer of British East India Company “Lord Marquess Wellesley”.
