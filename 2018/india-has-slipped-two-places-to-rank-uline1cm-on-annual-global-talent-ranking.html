India has slipped two places to rank _________ on annual ’Global Talent Ranking’.

(A) 55th

(B) ** 53rd

(C) 52nd

(D) 51st

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International Institute for Management Development (IMD) World Competitiveness Center’s “World Talent Ranking Report 2021” has been released. India ranks 56th in the report. Europe continues to dominate the rankings.

Concept note-2: -The annual World Competitiveness Index is released by the Institute of Management Development (IMD). India is on 37th as per World Competitiveness Yearbook (WCY). Among all the Asian Economies, India has witnessed the sharpest rise with the six-position jump from 43rd to 37th rank.

Concept note-3: -The 2022 ranking of the Swiss institute showed that Switzerland remains the leader among 63 economies in attracting and retaining talent, followed by Sweden and Iceland.

Concept note-4: -Global Talent Competitive Index: The index is released by INSEAD (Graduate Business School) in partnership with Adecco Group (Human Resource Group) and Google. GTCI was first launched in 2013. At present, 132 countries and 155 cities are ranked in this Global Talent Competitiveness Index.
