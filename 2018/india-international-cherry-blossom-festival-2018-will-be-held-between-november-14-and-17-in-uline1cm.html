India International Cherry Blossom Festival 2018 will be held between November 14 and 17 in _________

(A) ** Meghalaya

(B) Jammu and Kashmir

(C) Nagaland

(D) Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India International Cherry Blossom festival is scheduled to be held from November 14 to 17 in Shillong, Meghalaya. This festival is the world’s only autumn cherry blossom festival.

Concept note-2: -The annual Shillong Cherry Blossom Festival 2022 took a break in 2020 due to the pandemic and did a slow restart last year in 2021. But this year, the festival is planning to come back with a bang. This year it will be a four-day event, from November 23 to 26.

Concept note-3: -The cherry blossom festival in India is celebrated in Meghalaya’s capital city, Shillong, to cherish nature’s bounty. According to the Times of India, the cherry blossom festival was celebrated from 25th to 27th November 2021 in Shillong.

Concept note-4: -Toward the close summer time of year, pre-monsoon showers are common, specifically in Karnataka, and another state of India. They assist within the first ripening of mangoes and afterward frequently said as Cherry Blossom.

Concept note-5: -India is endowed with nature’s beauty in abundance, and Cherry Blossoms, too, add to it. From November till March, they bloom in full glory across many states. In North East’s Khasi Hills, cherry blossoms start flowering as soon as the spring season falls.
