India is going to celebrate the _________ Independence Day on August 15, 2018.

(A) 70th

(B) 73rd

(C) 71st

(D) ** 72nd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: India is all decked up to celebrate its 76th Independence Day on August 15. This day commemorates India’s independence from colonial rule after two centuries of oppression and suppression at the hands of the British.

Concept note-2: -The official journey of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav commenced on March 12, 2021, which started a 75-week countdown to the 75th anniversary of India’s independence and will end post a year on August 15, 2023.

Concept note-3: -India Celebrates 75 Years of Independence with the Theme “Nation First, Always First” The Republic of India will celebrate the platinum anniversary of its independence in its remembrance to the declaration of Indian independence from Britain on 15 August 1947.

Concept note-4: -India will complete the 75 years of Independence on 15th August 2022 but India will celebrate the 76th Independence day.
