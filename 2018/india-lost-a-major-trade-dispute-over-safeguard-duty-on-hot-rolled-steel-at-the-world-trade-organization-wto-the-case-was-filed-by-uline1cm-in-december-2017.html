India lost a major trade dispute over safeguard duty on hot-rolled steel at the World Trade Organization (WTO). The case was filed by _________ in December 2017.

(A) China

(B) ** Japan

(C) Germany

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The General Council convenes as the Dispute Settlement Body (DSB) to deal with disputes between WTO members.

Concept note-2: -Ideally disputes are resolved through negotiations. If this is not possible, WTO Members can request the establishment of a panel to settle the dispute. The panel will issue a report, which can subsequently be appealed before the WTO’s Appellate Body on questions of law.
