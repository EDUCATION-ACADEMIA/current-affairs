India was ranked _________th in the Global Gender Gap Index 2018, same as it was ranked in 2017.

(A) 109

(B) ** 108

(C) 104

(D) 106

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global Gender Gap Index (GGGI) published by the World Economic Forum in 2018 ranks 149 countries on their status of gender equality through various parameters. On this index, India ranked 108th on its performance on gender equality.

Concept note-2: -India ranks 135 out of 146 in Global Gender Gap Index.

Concept note-3: -India ranked 135 th out of 146 countries in gender parity, according to the latest Global Gender Gap Report by the World Economic Forum. This is of concern considering India ranked somewhere in the middle, bagging the 87 th position worldwide just six years ago, in 2016.

Concept note-4: -India has been ranked 108th out of 149 countries in the Global Gender Gap Index 2018, the same as 2017.

Concept note-5: -India ranks 135 among a total of 146 countries in the Global Gender Gap Index, 2022, released by the World Economic Forum. The country is the worst performer in the world in the “health and survival” sub-index in which it is ranked 146.
