India will become 3rd largest aviation market in world by

(A) ** 2020

(B) 2021

(C) 2022

(D) 2019

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India will become one of the top aviation hubs by 2040. The passenger traffic is expected to grow six-fold to around 1.1 billion.

Concept note-2: -India has become the world’s 3rd largest aviation market: PM Modi at inauguration of greenfield airport in Mopa, Goa.

Concept note-3: -India has evolved as the third-largest aviation market globally, Prime Minister Narendra Modi said on Sunday. With the country emerging as a tourist hub and there being an upsurge in demand for tourism post-Covid, it was time to push for “ease of travel, ” he said.

Concept note-4: -The Geneva-based International Air Transport Association (IATA) in its latest 20-Year Air Passenger Forecast has also projected India to be the third largest aviation market by 2026, replacing the UK.

Concept note-5: -India will displace the UK for third place in 2026, while Indonesia enters the top ten at the expense of Italy, ” IATA, which represents some 265 airlines comprising 83% of global air traffic, said. India’s passenger traffic will touch 442 million by 2035, IATA said.
