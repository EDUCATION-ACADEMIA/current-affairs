India won how many medals in World Junior Wushu Championships?

(A) Ten

(B) Six

(C) ** Nine

(D) Three

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India wins eight medals in junior wushu world championship .

Concept note-2: -Aniyan Midhun, popularly known as “Son of the Arabian Sea” (born 18 June 1992), is an Indian Wushu practitioner. He holds the World record for being the first South Indian to represent India in International Wushu fighting and win Gold.

Concept note-3: -Indian Praveen Kumar has emerged victorious at the World Wushu Championships held in Shanghai, China.

Concept note-4: -The World Junior Wushu Championships (WJWC) is an international wushu competition organized by the International Wushu Federation (IWUF) for competitors below 18 years of age.

Concept note-5: -Praveen Kumar has scripted the history by becoming the first Indian man to win a gold medal at Wushu World Championships.
