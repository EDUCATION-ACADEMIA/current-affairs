[SOURCES]

India’s First project of Swadesh Darshan was launched in

(A) West Bengal

(B) Assam

(C) ** Manipur

(D) Tripura

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri K J Alphons inaugurates the First Swadesh Darshan project in Sikkim.

Concept note-2: -Swadesh Darshan Scheme is a Central Sector scheme launched in 2014-15 by the Ministry of Tourism and Culture, Government of India for the integrated development of theme-based tourist circuits. The scheme aims to promote, develop and harness the potential of tourism in India.

Concept note-3: -Swadesh Darshan was first launched by the central government in 2015 under which 75 sites were developed. In July 2022, the Ministry of Tourism revamped its Swadesh Darshan scheme as Swadesh Darshan 2.0 (SD2.

Concept note-4: -Jashpur, marked for tribal tourism circuit under ‘Swadesh Darshan Yojana’, has been developed as an ethnic tourism village.

Concept note-5: -Union Ministry of Tourism has inaugurated Indias 1st tribal circuit project connecting 13 tourism sites in Chhattisgarh under Swadesh Darshan Scheme. Tourism Ministry has sanctioned 74 projects worth ₹ 5997.47 cr. to 31 states & UTs under this scheme.
