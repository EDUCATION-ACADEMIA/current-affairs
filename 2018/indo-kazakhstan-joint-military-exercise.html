Indo-Kazakhstan joint military exercise:

(A) KHANJAR

(B) SAMPRITI

(C) MAITREE

(D) ** KAZIND

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Exercise KazInd 2022 is the 6th edition of the Indo-Kazakhstan Joint exercise and is aimed at building trust and understanding of each other in the field of national security.

Concept note-2: -Indo-Kazakhstan Joint Military Exercise “Kazind – 2022” to Commence at Umroi (Meghalaya) The 6th Edition of Indo-Kazakhstan joint training exercise “KAZIND-22” is scheduled to be conducted at Umroi (Meghalaya) from 15 to 28 December 2022.

Concept note-3: -Ex KazInd, an annual Exercise between Indian Army and Kazakhstan Army was conducted at Foreign Training Node, Umroi.

Concept note-4: -The military exercise between Russia and India is known as Indira. The INDRA series of exercises began in 2003 and was conducted as a bilateral naval exercise alternately between the two countries.
