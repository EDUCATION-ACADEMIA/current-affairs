Concept note-1: -Initially, with the launch of the app in 2018, it was pulled out from the play store. But it later got relaunched in 2021 to get a high-quality Instagram experience that doesn’t rely on the network or device used.

Concept note-2: -Instagram Lite makes it easier to bring you closer to the people and things you love. The Instagram Lite app is small, fast and works on all networks. Enjoy the best features of INSTA like connecting with friends, sharing what you’re up to, or seeing what’s new from others all over the world.

Concept note-3: -Lite is the 0% fat version of Instagram. A “light” version of the app for pics (and videos, stories, reels, etc.) that takes up less space than the standard version. This Instagram format could be helpful if you use Instagram every day and your mobile is running low on storage space.
