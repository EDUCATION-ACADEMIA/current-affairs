(A) Jerry Yang and David Filo

(B) Dustin Moskovitz and Eduardo Saverin

(C) Evan Williams and Jan Koum

(D) ** Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger

Concept note-2: -The co-founders of photo-sharing giant Instagram, Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger, are both leaving the firm. Mr Systrom, chief executive, said they were departing to “explore our curiosity and creativity again". Instagram, purchased by Facebook in 2012 for $1bn (£760m) in cash and stock, has more than a billion users.
