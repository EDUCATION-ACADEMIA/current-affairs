International Cricket Council ropes in Royal Stag as official sponsor for _________?

(A) ** 5 years

(B) 4 years

(C) 2 years

(D) 6 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Cricket Council (ICC) has roped in Indian alcobev brand Royal Stag as official sponsor for the next five years. The partnership, which runs through 2023, will cover international events, including the upcoming big-ticket calendar events – the ICC Cricket World Cup, ICC T20 World Cup and.

Concept note-2: -In this association, the Indian brand has been appointed as the official sponsor of the West Indies Men’s team for their ongoing tour of India. The logo of Seagram’s Royal Stag will be featured on the front of the team’s playing shirts throughout the six matches of the bilateral series, three ODIs and three T20Is.

Concept note-3: -The ICC is the global governing body for cricket. Representing 108 members, the ICC governs and administrates the game and works with our members to grow the sport. The ICC is also responsible for the staging of all ICC Events.

Concept note-4: -The ICC is responsible for the organisation and governance of cricket’s major international tournaments, most notably the Cricket World Cup and the T20 World Cup. It also appoints the umpires and referees that officiate at all sanctioned Test matches, One Day Internationals and Twenty20 Internationals.
