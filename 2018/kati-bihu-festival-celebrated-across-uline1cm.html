Kati Bihu festival celebrated across _________?

(A) ** Assam

(B) Meghalaya

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kati Bihu 2022: The people of Assam are celebrating the auspicious harvest festival of Kati Bihu today. It is marked annually on the first day of the Kati month in the Assamese calendar and usually falls during mid-October. This year, it is on Tuesday, October 18.

Concept note-2: -Kati Bihu will be celebrated throughout Assam on Wednesday 18 October 2023.

Concept note-3: -The date for Magh Bihu 2023 festival is January 15 as per the traditional calendar of Assam. The date of Magh Bihu can vary from year to year based on the lunar calendar. The festival has its roots in the agricultural traditions of the region and is celebrated in the Assamese month of Magh, which falls in January.
