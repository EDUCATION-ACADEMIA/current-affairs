Konkan is the naval exercise between India and _________

(A) Australia

(B) USA

(C) ** UK

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is the India-United Kingdom. Konkan naval exercise is conducted between the India-United Kingdom.

Concept note-2: -Initial Planning Conference for Exercise Konkan 23 between Indian Navy and Royal Navy was held on 01 December 2022 at Mumbai . Personnel from Indian Navy and Royal Navy worked out operational and administrative aspects related to conduct of exercise.

Concept note-3: -The maritime component of the exercise, to be conducted off the west coast of India, is scheduled to be held in two phases. The harbour phase is planned at Mumbai from 21-23 October 2021, while at sea, the exercises will be conducted from 24-27 October 2021.

Concept note-4: -The Royal Air Force of the United Kingdom and the Indian Air Force conduct a joint air force exercise known as Exercise Indradhanush. The exercise aims to improve mutual operational understanding between the two air forces through close interaction.

Concept note-5: -The eighth edition of Exercise Garuda Shakti focuses on enhancing understanding, cooperation and interoperability between the Special Forces of both armies.
