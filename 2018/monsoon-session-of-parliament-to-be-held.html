Monsoon Session of Parliament to be held

(A) ** from July 18 to August 10

(B) from July 15 to August 10

(C) from July 18 to August 15

(D) from July 10 to August 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Monsoon Session, 2022 of the Parliament which commenced on Monday, the 18th of July, 2022 has been adjourned sine die on Monday the 8th of August, 2022.

Concept note-2: -The Monsoon Session 2022 of Parliament was held from Monday the 18 of July, 2022 to Monday the 8 of August, 2022. The session provided for 16sittings spreading over a period of 22 days. During the Session, 6 Bills (6 in Lok Sabha) were introduced.

Concept note-3: -India does not have a fixed parliamentary calendar. By convention, Parliament meets for three sessions in a year. Budget Session: The longest, the Budget Session, starts towards the end of January and concludes by the end of April or the first week of May.
