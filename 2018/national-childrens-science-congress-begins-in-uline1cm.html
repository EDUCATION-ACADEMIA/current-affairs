National Children’s Science Congress Begins in _________?

(A) ** Chennai

(B) Bhubaneswar

(C) Bihar

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Genesis The National Children’s Science Congress (NCSC) began as an experiment in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh in the early nineties, involving children in open-ended scientific projects of relevance to the society. It became a nationwide activity since 1993.

Concept note-2: -The National Children’s Science Congress at the district and state levels is the flagship programme of National Council for Science and Technology Communication under the ambit of DST. It is a platform for children to carry out small research activities at micro-level.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Science and Technology: Rural Development. Theme: Science and Technology: Rural Development. Indian Science Congress is India’s oldest, biggest, and one of the most prestigious scientific events.

Concept note-4: -National Children’s Science Congress (NCSC) is a nationwide Science Communication programme started in the year 1993. It is a programme of National Council for Science and Technology Communication (NCSTC), Department of Science and Technology, Government of India.
