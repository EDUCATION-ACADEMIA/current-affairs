National Energy Conservation Day is celebrated every year on _________th December in India.

(A) ** 14

(B) 12

(C) 13

(D) 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Energy Conservation Day – December 14, 2023. Every year, India observes National Energy Conservation on December 14.

Concept note-2: -National Energy Conservation Day is celebrated on December 14 in India to raise awareness among people about global warming, climate change and promote ways of save energy resources.

Concept note-3: -In order to ensure that energy is conserved for future generations, it is necessary to use it efficiently. Since 1991, National Energy Conservation Day has been celebrated on December 14.

Concept note-4: -The National Energy Conservation Day is celebrated every year on 14th December 2022. The purpose is to showcase the achievements of the nation in energy efficiency and conservation.

Concept note-5: -14 December-National Energy Conservation Day It is observed on 14 December to raise awareness about the need for energy and its conservation in daily life. Since 1991, it is celebrated every year on 14 December by the Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE), under the Ministry of Power.
