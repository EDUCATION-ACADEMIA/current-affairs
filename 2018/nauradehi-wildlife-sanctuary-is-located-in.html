Nauradehi Wildlife Sanctuary is located in

(A) ** Madhya Pradesh

(B) Bihar

(C) Assam

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nauradehi Wildlife Sanctuary is the largest wildlife sanctuary in Madhya Pradesh. The sanctuary covers nearly 1197 km² area across three districts i.e. Sagar, Damoh and Narsinghpur, of Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Nearest railway station for visiting Nauradehi wildlife sanctuary are Sagar railway station (SGO), Narsingpur railway station, Jabalpur railway station (90kms).

Concept note-3: -It was established in December, 2005 following the recommendations of Tiger Task Force.

Concept note-4: -Jim Corbett National Park. Q. India has wildlife sanctuaries.
