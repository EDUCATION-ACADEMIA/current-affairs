[SOURCES]
Nepal will take part in a 12-day-long ’Sagarmatha Friendship-2’ military exercise with

(A) Pakistan

(B) Bangladesh

(C) Japan

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This comes at a time when Nepal has agreed to participate in a 12-day long military exercise, Sagarmatha Friendship-2, with China from September 17-28 in Chengdu, as reported by The Times Of India.

Concept note-2: -Exercise “SURYA KIRAN” is conducted annually between India and Nepal with the aim to enhance interoperability in jungle warfare & counter terrorism operations in mountainous terrain and HADR under UN mandate.

Concept note-3: -Facts about Surya Kiran Exercise for UPSC India and Nepal participate in the Surya Kiran exercise with their troops of armies.

Concept note-4: -Bongo Sagar is the naval exercise between Bangladesh and India.

Concept note-5: -The ‘Surya Kiran’ exercise is conducted annually in Nepal and India. The 15th edition of the joint exercise was held in Pithoragarh, India.
