NHAI to start Pilot Project named “Pay As You Use” tolling system on which of the following National Highways?

(A) ** Delhi to Mumbai

(B) Hajira to Kolkata

(C) Varanasi to Kanyakumari

(D) Cuttack to Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The pilot project involves implementing a satellite based electronic toll collection system running on GPS/GSM technology for around 500 commercial vehicles on the Delhi Mumbai national highway.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has recently updated its mobile application “My FASTag” with a new feature to check balance status.

Concept note-3: -FASTag is an electronic toll collection system in India, operated by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI). It employs Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology for making toll payments directly from the prepaid or savings account linked to it or directly toll owner.

Concept note-4: -The National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) was constituted by an Act of Parliament, the National Highways Authority of India Act, 1988.

Concept note-5: -National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) was constituted by an Act of Parliament in 1988 under the administrative control of the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways NHAI has been set up as a Central Authority to develop, maintain and manage the National Highways entrusted to it by the Government of India.
