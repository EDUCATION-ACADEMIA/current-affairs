On Behalf of Which day Rapid Reporting System For SAG Launched?

(A) National youth day

(B) National Health Day

(C) National Voters day

(D) ** National Girl Child Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rakesh Srivastava, Secretary of Union Ministry of Women and Child Development (MoWCD), had launched the Phase-1 i.e. the beneficiary module of the Rapid Reporting System for the Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) in New Delhi on January 24, 2018.

Concept note-2: -The scheme aims at motivating out of school girls to go back to formal schooling or vocational /skill training under non-nutrition component of the scheme. Platform. The scheme will be implemented through existing Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) under Integrated Child Development Scheme (ICDS).

Concept note-3: -The Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) was devised in 2010 as a special intervention for adolescent girls of the age group 11 – 14 years to break the intergenerational life-cycle of nutritional and gender disadvantage and offer a supportive environment for the self-development of adolescent girls in the country.

Concept note-4: -What is RRS Portal? RRS portal full form is Rapid Reporting System. The Portal has been launched by The Union Ministry of Women and Child Development (WCD). The Portal is a web-based monitoring system specially designed for the monitoring of the Scheme for Adolescent Girls.
