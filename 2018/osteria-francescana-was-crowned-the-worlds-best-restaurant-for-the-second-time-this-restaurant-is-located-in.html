“Osteria Francescana” was crowned the world’s best restaurant for the second time. This restaurant is located in

(A) France

(B) ** Italy

(C) Spain

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Osteria Francescana-Massimo Bottura-Modena, Italy.

Concept note-2: -Osteria Francescana is rated with three stars by the Michelin Guide and holds the first position on the Italian food guide l’Espresso-Ristoranti d’Italia with a score of 20/20.

Concept note-3: -Share this story At Francescana in the ancient city of Modena, Bottura takes traditional Italian dishes and turns them on their head with modern and innovative techniques. The restaurant claims three Michelin stars and was No. 1 on the World’s 50 Best Restaurants list in 2016.

Concept note-4: -Massimo Bottura (Italian pronunciation: [ˈmassimo botˈtuːra]; born 30 September 1962) is an Italian restaurateur and the chef patron of Osteria Francescana, a three-Michelin-star restaurant based in Modena, Italy ranked No. 1 on The World’s 50 Best Restaurants list twice and currently part of the Best of the Best list.
