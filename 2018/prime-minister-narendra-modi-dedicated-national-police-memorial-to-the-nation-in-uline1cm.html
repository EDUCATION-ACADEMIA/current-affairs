Prime Minister Narendra Modi Dedicated National Police Memorial To The Nation in _________?

(A) New Delhi

(B) Hyderabad

(C) ** Chennai

(D) Pune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The renovated and refurbished memorial and museum was inaugurated by the prime minister, Narendra Modi, on 21 October 2018, which is also the Police Commemoration Day (Police Martyr’s Day) in India.

Concept note-2: -The National Police Memorial (NPM) site is located at the crest of Shantipath in Chanakyapuri, New Delhi and the NPM campus has a tiered landscape having capacity to accommodate over 750 persons.

Concept note-3: -October 21st is celebrated every year as the Police Commemoration Day. The day marks the sacrifice of 10 CRPF men who lost their lives to a Chinese attack in 1959.

Concept note-4: -India will have its first-ever national police museum as the Central government has decided to establish the unique facility in Lutyen’s Delhi.

Concept note-5: -Every year, India celebrates Police Commemoration Day on October 21 to honour the police personnel who sacrificed their lives while discharging their duties. The day remembers the sacrifices made by the 10 policemen who were martyred fighting for the country during a Chinese firing in 1959.
