(A) ** Cartoonist

(B) Musician

(C) Politician

(D) Scientist

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi released ‘Timeless Laxman’, a Collection of Works of well-known cartoonist R.K. Laxman at Raj Bhavan, Mumbai on Tuesday (18th Dec).

Concept note-2: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today released the coffee table book “Timeless Laxman”, based on the famous cartoonist RK Laxman.

Concept note-3: -The main of the book is to help with exam stress and anxiety. Prime Minister Narendra Modi has updated his book, “Exam Warriors” ahead of the Pariksha Pe Charcha 2023.
