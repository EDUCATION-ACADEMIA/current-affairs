[SOURCES]

Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the Bogibeel Bridge, India’s longest rail-cum-road bridge, falling in the eastern part of _________ and Arunachal Pradesh on December 25.

(A) ** Assam

(B) Nagaland

(C) Meghalaya

(D) Manipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sikang Bridge is another name for the Dibang River. The Mahatma Gandhi Setu and Bhupen Hazarika Setus are the longest road bridges in India, respectively, and both span the Dibang River in Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -First, HD Deve Gowda inaugurated it, then Atal Bihari Vajpayee started the construction work under his reign, and finally, Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the bridge in 2019. It took the Bogibeel bridge a total of 200 months to get completed.

Concept note-3: -Bhupen Hazarika Setu also known as Dhola Sadiya Bridge is the longest bridge of 9150 meters constructed over Lohit river in Tinsukia (Assam).
