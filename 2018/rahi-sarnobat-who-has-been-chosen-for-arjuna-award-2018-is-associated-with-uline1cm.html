Rahi Sarnobat, who has been chosen for Arjuna Award 2018, is associated with _________

(A) ** Shooting

(B) Hockey

(C) Table Tennis

(D) Athletics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rahi Sarnobat is a record-breaking 25m pistol shooter from India. The two-time Olympian was the first Indian pistol shooter to win an ISSF World Cup gold and the first Indian woman shooter to win gold at the Asian Games.

Concept note-2: -Sarnobat became India’s first pistol shooter to win a gold medal in the World Cup when she won the 25 metre pistol event in the ISSF World Cup in Changwon. In the 2014 Commonwealth Games at Glasgow, she won gold in the women’s 25 metre pistol event.
