Recently, Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) announced its exit from the proposed light metro project for two cities in

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) ** Kerala

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Silver Line (Line 10) is an under construction line of Delhi Metro which is expected to be completed by September 2025. This 23.622 km line consists of 15 stations (4 Elevated & 11 Underground) from Tughlakabad to Delhi Aerocity. This Line is a part of Phase 4 of Delhi Metro.

Concept note-2: -The 29-km line will connect the Janakpuri West Metro station on the Magenta line and the RK Ashram station on the Blue line. The Janakpuri-RK Ashram stretch will run through Haiderpur, Sadar Bazar, Azadpur, Bhalswa, Pushpanjali Enclave, Mangolpuri and Paschim Vihar.

Concept note-3: -It was finally completed on 18 September 2021 with the opening of the Grey Line extension from Najafgarh to Dhansa Bus Stand. Driverless operations on the 38 km (24 mi) long Magenta line began on 28 December 2021, making it the first driverless metro line of Delhi Metro and India’s first driverless metro.
