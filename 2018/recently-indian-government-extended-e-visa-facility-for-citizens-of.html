[SOURCES]

Recently, Indian Government extended e-visa facility for citizens of

(A) 150 countries

(B) ** 165 countries

(C) 160 countries

(D) 155 countries

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There will be no limit of stay for 30 days. Citizens of 156 Eligible Countries for Indian e-Visa now can apply for 1-year and 5-year e-tourist visas from 16 March 2022 and stay in India for a longer period.

Concept note-2: -Indian visa extension In case of urgency, the foreigner can visit the FRRO/FRO office directly to process their visa extension. Please note that electronic visas cannot be extended. Once you enter India, it is valid for the indicated duration. If your e-visa expires you will have to apply for a new visa.

Concept note-3: -’E-visa’-64 News Result(s) India has restored all currently valid five year e-tourist visa given to citizens of 156 countries and regular paper visa to nationals of all countries with immediate effect, two years after their suspension follow the COVID-19 outbreak.

Concept note-4: -Currently, travelers from more than 160 countries can apply for a tourist e-visa, e-business visa, and e-medical attendant visas.
