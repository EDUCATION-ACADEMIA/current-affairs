[SOURCES]
Researchers found that diabetic women are at greater risk of

(A) lymphatic system problem

(B) Infertility

(C) ** Cancer

(D) AIDS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Diabetes increases the risk of heart disease (the most common diabetes complication) by about four times in women but only about two times in men, and women have worse outcomes after a heart attack. Women are also at higher risk of other diabetes-related complications such as blindness, kidney disease, and depression.

Concept note-2: -In general, women tend to have higher SHBG levels than men and low SHBG concentrations may be associated with even higher diabetes risk in women compared with men.

Concept note-3: -Diabetes is associated with an increased risk of heart disease, stroke, high blood pressure and narrowing of blood vessels (atherosclerosis). Nerve damage (neuropathy) in limbs.
