Sathyasri Sharmila has become the first transgender lawyer in India. She belongs to which state?

(A) Telangana

(B) Karnataka

(C) Kerala

(D) ** Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sathyasri Sharmila becomes India’s first transgender lawyer from Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-2: -Srishti Chakraborty. A landmark moment for India’s transgender community occurred this Saturday. In a ceremony held at the Madras High Court, Sathyasri Sharmila was enroled with the Bar Council of Tamil Nadu and Puducherry, making her India’s first transgender lawyer.

Concept note-3: -Sharmila, who hails from Tamil Nadu, did not have an easy journey, as she was subject to torture and abuse on account of her gender. “Today, I enrolled my name in the Bar Council Of Tamil Nadu And Puducherry and became the first transgender lawyer in India.

Concept note-4: -Sathyasri Sharmila becomes first transgender lawyer to enrol with TN Bar Council.

Concept note-5: -The reportedly first transgender lawyer in the Nilgiris, Sowmya Sasu, said that she was extremely happy that she will get to practice law in the district where she has felt “safest” and most at home.
