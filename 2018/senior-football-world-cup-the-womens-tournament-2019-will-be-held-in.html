Senior Football World Cup: the women’s tournament 2019 will be held in

(A) United States

(B) Qatar

(C) Russia

(D) ** France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In March 2015, FIFA awarded France the right to host the 2019 FIFA Women’s World Cup over South Korea. In the 2019 edition, which was held in France, the United States won the tournament for the fourth time.

Concept note-2: -The opening match will be contested between New Zealand and Norway at Eden Park, Auckland on 20 July 2023. The final will take place on 20 August 2023 at Sydney Olympic Stadium in Australia. The United States are the defending champions, having won the previous two tournaments.

Concept note-3: -The FIFA Women’s World Cup 2023™ is jointly hosted by two countries, Australia and Aotearoa New Zealand, and held for the first time in the Southern Hemisphere.
