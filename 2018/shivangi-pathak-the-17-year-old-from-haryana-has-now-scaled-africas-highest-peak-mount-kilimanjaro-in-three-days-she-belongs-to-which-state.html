Shivangi Pathak, the 17-year-old from Haryana, has now scaled Africa’s highest peak, Mount Kilimanjaro, in three days. She belongs to which state?

(A) West Bengal

(B) Assam

(C) Karnataka

(D) ** Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Previously, this teenager from Haryana became the youngest Indian woman to reach the peak of Mount Everest, and now by scaling Africa’s Highest peak, Mount Kilimanjaro, Shivangi Pathak has added another feather to her cap. This 17-year-old teen, who is from Haryana’s Hisar city has scaled the peak in three days.
