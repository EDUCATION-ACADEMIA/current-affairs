Smart City in which of the following state will have 250 smart poles to charge electric vehicles?

(A) ** Karnataka

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Gujarat

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which city has the most electric charging stations? Los Angeles leads with double the number of charge stations than the next city on the list.

Concept note-2: -California has the highest number of EV charging stations at 15,182, making up an impressive 29% of all charging stations in America. In fact, the Golden State has nearly double the chargers of the following three states, New York (3,085), Florida (2,858), and Texas (2,419) combined.

Concept note-3: -Electric (EV) Charging Stations Looking for an electric vehicle charging station near you? There are 605 EV charging stations in India spread across 78 cities. Of these, Maharashtra has the highest number with 243 charging stations while Jammu and Kashmir has the least with 1 charging stations.

Concept note-4: -Gurugram: India’s largest electric vehicle (EV) charging station with a capacity of 121 charging points for 4 wheelers, has been opened here in Sector 86. With this EV station, the city has now two of the largest electric vehicle charging stations in the country.
