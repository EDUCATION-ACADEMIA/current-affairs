SpaceX launched world’s heaviest communications satellite - ‘Telstar 19 Vantage’ satellite, which weighs over

(A) 6, 500 kg

(B) 8, 000 kg

(C) 5, 000 kg

(D) ** 7, 000 kg

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Coming in at 15, 600 lbs. (7067 kilograms), its payload, Telstar 19V, was the heaviest commercial communications satellite ever built.

Concept note-2: -The FCC previously authorized SpaceX to launch and operate up to 12, 000 Starlink satellites, including roughly 4, 400 first-generation Ka-band and Ku-band Starlink spacecraft that SpaceX has been launching since 2019.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is USA. SpaceX created yet another record-launch of most number of satellites on a single rocket-when its first smallsat rideshare mission called Transporter-1 successfully launched 143 spacecraft into orbit.

Concept note-4: -Elon Musk’s company SpaceX has successfully launched the Transporter-1 mission, breaking the record for the most number of satellites ever flown on a single rocket.
