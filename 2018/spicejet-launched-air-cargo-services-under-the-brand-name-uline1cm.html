SpiceJet launched air cargo services under the brand name _________

(A) SpiceFreights

(B) SpiceServs

(C) SpiceCars

(D) ** SpiceXpress

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In January 2021 Company provided the much-needed logistical support in terms of transport storage and distribution. During 2020-21it introduced wide-body cargo planes for long-haul operations to Europe Africa and the CIS countries while also launching scheduledfreighter services to Bangkok and Singapore.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Nagaland.

Concept note-3: -SpiceJet, the country’s favourite airline, introduced SpiceMax in 2014, a product that offers a lot more comfort and convenience for customers willing to pay a modest fee. SpiceJet customers opting for SpiceMax seats can enjoy best-in-class leg room, with at least 6 inches more leg room compared to standard seats.

Concept note-4: -When looking at the various types of cargo transported by air, they fall into two main groups: General Cargo and Special Cargo. Special Cargo is then divided up into smaller specialized sub-groups.
