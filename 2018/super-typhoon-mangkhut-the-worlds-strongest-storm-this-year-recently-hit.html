Super Typhoon Mangkhut, the world’s strongest storm this year, recently hit

(A) ** Philippines

(B) Indonesia

(C) Japan

(D) Maldives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The right-hand eyewall - the worst part of the storm - made a direct hit over Hong Kong. Winds clocked in at 109 mph in the city. Storm surge up to 11 feet set record water levels in most spots; in Hong Kong proper, the ocean climbed to its highest level since at least 1904.

Concept note-2: -Super Typhoon Hinnamnor, the strongest global storm of 2022, is sitting just south of Japan’s Okinawa Island with sustained winds of 241 kilometers per hour. According to Japan Meteorological Agency, the Hinmnamnor storm will further move northward to approach the island over the weekend.

Concept note-3: -1 – TROPICAL CYCLONE NALGAE, Philippines – 4 NOVEMBER 2022.

Concept note-4: -On 22 September 2022, the center of Tropical Storm “KARDING” was estimated at 1,320 km East of Northern Luzon (17.9°N, 134.2°E). “KARDING” intensified into a Severe Tropical Storm on 24 September 2022, estimated at 475 km East of Casiguran, Aurora or 520 km East of Baler, Aurora (15.6°N, 126.5°E).
