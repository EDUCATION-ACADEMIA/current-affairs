The 10-nation ICC women’s World T20 is scheduled to take place in

(A) Australia

(B) New Zealand

(C) ** West Indies

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The upcoming T20 event was originally scheduled to initiate at the end of 2022. However, ICC postponed the event to February 2023 due to the schedule colliding with FIFA World Cup 2022. Three different stadiums in South Africa were selected as the hosting venue for this year’s T20 World Cup.

Concept note-2: -We take a look at ten players who have what it takes to make a serious splash at the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2023 in South Africa.

Concept note-3: -Momentum builds up for ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2023 in South Africa.

Concept note-4: -Which teams are participating in the 2023 Women’s T20 World Cup? The 2023 Women’s T20 World Cup edition will have 10 teams including host South Africa, New Zealand, England, Australia, India, West Indies, Pakistan, Ireland, Sri Lanka and Bangladesh.
