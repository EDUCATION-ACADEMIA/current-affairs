The Bahrain Economic Development Board (EDB) signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with which of the following Indian state Government to promote FinTech?

(A) Karnataka

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Bank of Maharashtra.

Concept note-2: -The Bahrain Economic Development Board (EDB) is an investment promotion agency with overall responsibility for attracting investment into the Kingdom and supporting initiatives that enhance the investment climate.

Concept note-3: -His Royal Highness Prince Salman bin Hamad Al Khalifa, the Crown Prince, Prime Minister and Chairman of the Economic Development Board (EDB), today issued Edict (1) of 2022 on restructuring the Board of Directors for the Bahrain Mumtalakat Holding Company (Mumtalakat).
