The BCCI recently announced that a cricketer guilty of age fraud will be barred from all its recognised tournaments for _________ years.

(A) Four

(B) One

(C) Three

(D) ** Two

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"Former Zimbabwe captain Brendan Taylor has been banned from all cricket for three and a half years after he accepted breaching four charges of the ICC Anti-Corruption Code and, separately, one charge of the ICC Anti-Doping Code, ” the ICC said.

Concept note-2: -These 5 players can be banned Mumbai’s Tanush Kotian, Rohan Kunumal (Kerala), Apoorva Wankhede (Vidarbha), Chirag Gandhi (Gujarat) and Ramakrishna Ghosh (Maharashtra) are the five players.

Concept note-3: -Age fraud in sports is commonly witnessed. A few participants who crossed the age for participating in the ‘under 18’ category, in the district level or the state level competitions, produce fake age certificates.
