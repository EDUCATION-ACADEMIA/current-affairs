[SOURCES]

The first India-U.S. tri-services exercise that is to happen in 2019 will focus on _________

(A) ** UN based scenario

(B) WHO based scenario

(C) Defence based scenario

(D) SAARC based scenario

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tiger Triumph is the bilateral tri-service amphibious military exercise involving the armed forces of India and the United States. It is the first tri-service military exercise between the two countries. India has previously only held tri-service exercises with Russia.

Concept note-2: -India and Japan conducted a joint military exercise titled ‘Dharma Guardian 2022’ at Maratha Light Infantry Regimental Centre in Belgaum, Karnataka. It was held from 27th February to 10th March 2022. It covers platoon-level joint training on operations in the jungle and semi-urban and urban terrain.

Concept note-3: -The 2022 exercise also was the 30th anniversary of Malabar, which began as a bilateral exercise between Indian and US navies. Japan has been a permanent partner of the Malabar since 2015 and Australia has been participating since 2020.

Concept note-4: -Vajra Prahar’ is a Special Forces joint training exercise conducted alternately in India and the US to share the best practices and experiences in areas such as joint mission planning and operational tactics as also to improve interoperability between the Special Forces of both nations.
