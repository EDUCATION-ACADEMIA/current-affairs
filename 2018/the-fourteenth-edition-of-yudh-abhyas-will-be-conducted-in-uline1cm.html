The fourteenth edition of Yudh Abhyas will be conducted in _________

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Uttarakhand

(C) Rajasthan

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Yudh Abhyas military exercise started in the year 2002. Since then both the countries are participating in this exercise annually. The latest edition of this exercise will take place from 14 to 31 October 2022 in Uttarakhand.

Concept note-2: -Notably, the Indo-US military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas 2022’ commenced in Uttarakhand’s Auli on November 19.

Concept note-3: -Exercise Yudh Abhyas, conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, tactics, techniques and procedures between the Armies of the two nations. The previous edition of the exercise was conducted at Joint Base Elmendorf Richardson, Alaska (USA) in October 2021.

Concept note-4: -Yudh Abhyas 2022: Soldiers Of Indian And US Army Take Part In Joint Military Exercise In Uttarakhand. Militaries from India and the U.S. are taking part in a high-altitude training exercise in a cold, mountainous terrain close to India’s disputed border with China. The training exercise began two weeks ago.

Concept note-5: -The 18th edition of Indo-US joint training exercise “YUDH ABHYAS 22” is scheduled to be conducted in Uttarakhand this month. Exercise Yudh Abhyas is conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, Tactics, Techniques and Procedures between the Armies of the two nations.
