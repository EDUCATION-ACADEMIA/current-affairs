The government hiked the minimum support price (MSP) for paddy by a record

(A) Rs 150 per quintal

(B) ** Rs 200 per quintal

(C) Rs 180 per quintal

(D) Rs 230 per quintal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The MSP for Paddy which was Rs. 1310/-per quintal in 2013-14 has increased to Rs. 2040/-per quintal in 2022-23, an increase of almost 56%.

Concept note-2: -Government has increased the MSP of Rabi Crops for Marketing Season 2023-24, to ensure remunerative prices to the growers for their produce. The absolute highest increase in MSP has been approved for lentil (Masur) at Rs. 500/-per quintal followed by rapeseed and mustard at Rs. 400/-per quintal.
