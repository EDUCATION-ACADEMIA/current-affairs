The Indian Navy will be conducting its flagship Theatre Level Operational Readiness Exercise, (TROPEX), from _________

(A) March 2019

(B) ** January 2019

(C) April 2019

(D) February 2019

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This operational level exercise is conducted biennially and witnesses participation not only by all Indian Navy units but also of Indian Army, Indian Airforce and Coast Guard assets. TROPEX 23 is being conducted over a duration of three months from January-March 2023.

Concept note-2: -TROPEX 2021 It is the navy’s largest war game. Drills and exercises including SEA VIGIL and AMPHEX-21 were part TROPEX.

Concept note-3: -21st EDITION OF INDIA FRANCE BILATERAL NAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the Western Seaboard today, 16 Jan 23.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Prasthan. Prasthan Exercise: Conducted every six months, this exercise is an important element of ensuring offshore security.
