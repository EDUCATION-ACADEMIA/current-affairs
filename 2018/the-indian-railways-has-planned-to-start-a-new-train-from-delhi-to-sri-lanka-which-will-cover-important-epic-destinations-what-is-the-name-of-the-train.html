The Indian Railways has planned to start a new train from Delhi to Sri Lanka which will cover important epic destinations. What is the name of the train?

(A) Sharavathi Express

(B) Maitree Express

(C) ** Shri Ramayana Express

(D) Wainganga Express

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Yes, You’ll Also Be Exploring Sri Lanka. Taking yet another step to flaunt the beauty of Indian culture, IRCTC is introducing a new route following different locations mentioned in Ramayana, the Great Hindu Epic. Sri Ramayana Express, as the route is called, is expected to get on the track FROM 14th November 2018.

Concept note-2: -What are the Fares for the Journey? The Ramayana Express Train Ticket price would be INR 82, 950 per person for 2 AC and INR 1, 02, 095 for 1AC class. The prices may be subject to change. For Madurai, IRCTC offers sleeper class coach facilities for tourists to make the journey budget friendly.

Concept note-3: -The train will cover four important destinations associated with the life of Lord Ram in a 16-days tour package spread over India and Sri Lanka. Named as Shri Ramayana Yatra-Sri Lanka, the circuit will have two travel components–one each in India and Sri Lanka.

Concept note-4: -Railways to introduce Sri Ramayana Express Nov 14 from Delhi to Rameshwaram. Indian Railways is planning to introduce a unique pilgrim train on the Ramayana circuit to cover the major destinations related to the Hindu epic.
