The Indian women’s hockey team defeated three-time champions _________ 1-0 in the semi-final to reach their first final at Asian Games since 1998.

(A) ** China

(B) Malaysia

(C) Japan

(D) Thailand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian women’s hockey team will play Spain in the final on Saturday. The Indian hockey team won a tense semi-final shootout against Ireland in Valencia, Spain on Friday to make the Women’s FIH Nations Cup 2022 final. Udita (44’) equalised for India after Carroll Naomi (14’) put Ireland ahead.

Concept note-2: -Asia Cup Hockey 2022: India wins Bronze medal, beats Japan 1-0.

Concept note-3: -Women’s Hockey Asia Cup 2022: India Beat China 2-0 For Third-Place Finish.

Concept note-4: -In that edition of the Games, women’s hockey made its debut in the Olympics and the sport was played in a round-robin format with top two teams qualifying for the final. The Rani Rampal-led side will play Argentina in the semifinal on Wednesday.
