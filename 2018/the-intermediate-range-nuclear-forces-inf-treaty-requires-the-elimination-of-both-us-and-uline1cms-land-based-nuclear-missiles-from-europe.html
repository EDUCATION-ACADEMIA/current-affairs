The Intermediate-range Nuclear Forces (INF) Treaty requires the elimination of both US’ and _________’s land-based nuclear missiles from Europe.

(A) Japan

(B) ** Russia

(C) Germany

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 1987 Intermediate-Range Nuclear Forces (INF) Treaty required the United States and the Soviet Union to eliminate and permanently forswear all of their nuclear and conventional ground-launched ballistic and cruise missiles with ranges of 500 to 5, 500 kilometers.

Concept note-2: -The Intermediate-Range Nuclear Forces Treaty (INF Treaty, formally Treaty Between the United States of America and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics on the Elimination of Their Intermediate-Range and Shorter-Range Missiles) is a 1987 arms control agreement between the United States and the Soviet Union.

Concept note-3: -President Ronald Reagan and Soviet Union General Secretary Mikhail Gorbachev sign the Intermediate-Range Nuclear Forces Treaty in the East Room of the White House, Dec. 8, 1987. Esper said that despite U.S. objections, the Russians have continued to violate the treaty, and this is why the U.S. withdrew.
