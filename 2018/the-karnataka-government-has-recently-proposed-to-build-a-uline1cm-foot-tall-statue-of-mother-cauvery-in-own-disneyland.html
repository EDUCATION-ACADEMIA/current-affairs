The Karnataka government has recently proposed to build a _________ foot tall statue of Mother Cauvery in own Disneyland.

(A) ** 125

(B) 150

(C) 200

(D) 250

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Gommateshwara statue is a 57-foot (17 m) high monolithic statue on Vindhyagiri Hill in the town of Shravanbelagola in the Indian state of Karnataka. Carved of a single block of granite, it is one of the tallest monolithic statues in the world, second only to the Great Sphinx of Giza.

Concept note-2: -Mother Cauvery is the goddess named after river Cauvery. Not just this, the government has also proposed to build a museum complex, two glass towers measuring 360 feet providing a bird’s eye view of the KRS reservoir, a band stand, an indoor stadium and a replica of historical monuments too boost tourism.

Concept note-3: -Kaveri River, Kaveri also spelled Cauvery, sacred river of southern India.
