The maximum speed of the India’s first indigenously developed engineless train "Train 18" is _________

(A) ** 220 Km/hr

(B) 250 Km/hr

(C) 180 Km/hr

(D) 270 Km/hr

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. India’s first indigenously built engineless semi-high speed train, Train 18, has been renamed ‘Vande Bharat Express’. The train is set to run between Delhi and Varanasi at a maximum speed of 160 kmph. It is regarded as a successor to the Shatabdi Express.

Concept note-2: -Manufacturing first set. The manufacturing of “Train-2018” was completed in October 2018. During the test runs, the train achieved an operational speed of 180 km/h. According to speed certificates for LHB coach trains on the RDSO website, India ran its first test run at this speed with an LHB coach train in 2000.

Concept note-3: -Train 18 is the first engine-less and made-in-India train: The train was completed in Integral Coach Factory, Chennai, and all done within 18 months. 2.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. Acknowledging its made-in-India status, the Indian Railways has named the indigenously manufactured Train 18 as Vande Bharat Express. Vande Bharat Express is set to run between Delhi and Varanasi at a maximum speed of 160kmph.

Concept note-5: -"Train 18 will now be known as Vande Bharat Express. It’s a train built completely in India by Indian engineers, in a span of 18 months. It’ll ply from Delhi to Varanasi.
