The new 10 Rupees note will reportedly feature the picture of

(A) ** Rani-Ki-Vav

(B) Ajanta Caves

(C) Fatehpur Sikri

(D) Sun Temple

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The new ₹10 denomination banknotes in the Mahatma Gandhi (New) Series bear signature of the Governor, Reserve Bank of India. The note has motif of ‘Sun Temple, Konark’ on the reverse, depicting the country’s cultural heritage.

Concept note-2: -Photo of Father of Nation – Mahatma Gandhi. In some Rs 10 notes, a photo of Sun Temple, Konark can be seen. In some Rs 10 notes, a photo of an elephant and tiger can be seen. One can observe 10 rupees written in 15 different languages.

Concept note-3: -Konark chakra symbol is printed on the new Rs 10 note to be issued by the RBI. The Reserve Bank will soon bring into circulation new Rs 10 denomination banknotes in chocolate brown colour, which will have India’s cultural heritage Konark Sun Temple as a motif on the reverse side.

Concept note-4: -Contemporary Currency notes have 15 languages on the panel which appear on the reverse of the note.
