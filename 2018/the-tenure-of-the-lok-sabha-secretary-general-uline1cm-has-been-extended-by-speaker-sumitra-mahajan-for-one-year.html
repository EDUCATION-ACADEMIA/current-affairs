The tenure of the Lok Sabha Secretary General, _________, has been extended by Speaker Sumitra Mahajan for one year.

(A) Sanjay Mitra

(B) Pradeep Kumar Sinha

(C) Nripendra Misra

(D) ** Snehlata Shrivastava

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The longest-serving speaker of the Lok Sabha is Balram Jakhar. He was a senior leader of the Congress party, a prominent figure in national politics, and a distinguished parliamentarian. He was elected to Loksabha in 1980 and 1984 from Ferozpur and Sikar.

Concept note-2: -The Lok Sabha, unless sooner dissolved, continues to operate for five years for time being from the date appointed for its first meeting. However, while a proclamation of emergency is in operation, this period may be extended by Parliament by law or decree.

Concept note-3: -Lok Sabha members are elected for a term of 5 years. Usually, the Parliament has 545 members. Q. The members of the Lok Sabha are elected for a term of years.

Concept note-4: -Om Birla (born 23 November 1962) is an Indian politician, agriculturist and social worker who is the 17th and current Speaker of the Lok Sabha. He serves as a Member of Parliament from Kota-Bundi constituency in Rajasthan. Prior to parliament, he was elected thrice to the assembly of Rajasthan.
