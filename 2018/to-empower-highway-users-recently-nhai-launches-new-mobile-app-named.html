To empower highway users, recently NHAI launches new mobile app named

(A) ** Sukhad Yatra App

(B) Kruzr

(C) HumSafar Road Safety

(D) One Touch Response

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sukhad Yatra mobile app has been developed by National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) to empower the highway users, it said adding the key feature of the app includes provision for the user to enter road quality-related information or to report any accident, pothole on the highway.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of Road Transport & Highways has launched the Sukhad Yatra App and Toll-free Emergency number for the benefit of the Highway users. The mobile application has been prepared by National Highways Authority of India (NHAI).

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. Shri Nitin Gadkari launched a mobile App Sukahd Yatra and a Toll-free number.

Concept note-4: -National Highways Authority of India launched ‘Harit Path’, a mobile app, in 2020. The application is to monitor the location, growth, species details, maintenance activities, targets and achievements of each of its field units for each and every plant under all plantation projects.

Concept note-5: -The app will be promoted extensively through schools and colleges across the country.
