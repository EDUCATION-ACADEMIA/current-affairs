Two Indians who received this year’s Ramon Magsaysay Award are:

(A) Gethsie Shanmugam and Abdon Nababan

(B) Sanjiv Chaturvedi and Anshu Gupta

(C) TM Krishna and Bezwada Wilson

(D) ** Bharat Vatwani and Sonam Wangchuk

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2022 Ramon Magsaysay Awardees are Sotheara Chhim (Cambodia), Bernadette Madrid (Philippines), Tadashi Hattori (Japan) and Gary Bencheghib (Indonesia).

Concept note-2: -Indians who got Ramon Magsaysay The famous Indians among the sixty people are Vinoba Bhave, Mother Teresa, Varghese Curien, Jaiprakash Narayan, Satyajit Ray, M.S. Subbulakshmi, Kiran Bedi, Arvind Kejriwal, Sonam Wangchuk, and Ravish Kumar.

Concept note-3: -Vinoba Bhave was the first Indian who won the Ramon Magsaysay award. Human rights activist Vinoba Bhave was among the first five individuals to receive the Ramon Magsaysay Award in its year of inception in 1958.
