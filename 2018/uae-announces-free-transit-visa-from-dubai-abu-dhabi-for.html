UAE announces free transit visa from Dubai, Abu Dhabi for

(A) 1 day

(B) ** 2 days

(C) 3 days

(D) 5 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Transit visas for 48 hours are issued free of charge to passengers transiting through the UAE’s airports. You need to apply for the visa in advance through a UAE-based airline. This visa is not extendable, nor renewable.

Concept note-2: -You will not require a UAE transit visa if you are staying in the airport for a period of four hours or less.

Concept note-3: -The transit visa is valid for 14 days, starting from your arrival in the UAE. If you are transiting on the third destination on your trip itinerary, you will need to have proof of a confirmed onward flight (i.e. an air ticket). Your passport must be valid for a minimum of 6 months.

Concept note-4: -The Dubai transit visa for 48 hours is issued free of charge to passengers transiting through the UAE’s airports. Eligible applicants need to apply for the 48 hours transit visa in advance through a UAE-based airline. The visa is non extendable and non-renewable.
