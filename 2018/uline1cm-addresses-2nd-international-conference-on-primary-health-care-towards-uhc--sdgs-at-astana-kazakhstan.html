_________ addresses 2nd International Conference on Primary Health Care towards UHC & SDGs at Astana, Kazakhstan?

(A) ** Shri J P Nadda

(B) Shri Prakash Javadekar

(C) Shri Arun Jaitley

(D) Shri Suresh Prabhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We will strive to avoid or mitigate conflicts that undermine health systems and roll back health gains. We must use coherent and inclusive approaches to expand PHC as a pillar of UHC in emergencies, ensuring the continuum of care and the provision of essential health services in line with humanitarian principles.

Concept note-2: -The Conference was co-hosted by the Government of Kazakhstan, the United Nations Children’s Fund (UNICEF), and the World Health Organization (WHO). The 1978 Declaration of Alma-Ata founded the movement for primary health care.

Concept note-3: -The Astana Declaration acknowledged the Alma Ata Declaration of 19782, 3 commitment to the fundamental role of strong primary health care for population health.
