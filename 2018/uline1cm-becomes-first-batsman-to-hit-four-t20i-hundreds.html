_________ becomes first batsman to hit four T20I hundreds?

(A) ** Rohit Sharma

(B) Suresh Raina

(C) MS Dhoni

(D) Dinesh Karthik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rohit Sharma is the only player in the world to have hit four centuries in T20 internationals and is considered one of the best T20 players in the world.

Concept note-2: -India’s Rohit Sharma leads the list with four T20I centuries, followed by Czech Republic’s Sabawoon Davizi, India’s Suryakumar Yadav, Australia’s Glenn Maxwell and New Zealand’s Colin Munro with three each.

Concept note-3: -The first century of the T20 International format was scored by Chris Gayle against South Africa at the inaugural ICC World Twenty20 in 2007. Aaron Finch of Australia has scored the highest individual score of 172 runs in the T20 format.

Concept note-4: -Rohit Sharma, current Indian team captain, has scored the most centuries in T20I cricket with the highest score of 118.
