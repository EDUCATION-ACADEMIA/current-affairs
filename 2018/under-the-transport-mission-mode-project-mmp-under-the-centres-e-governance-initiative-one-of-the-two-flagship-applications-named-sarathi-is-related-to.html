(B) ** Driving licence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A mission mode project (MMP) is an individual project within the National e-Governance Plan (NeGP) that focuses on one aspect of electronic governance, such as banking, land records or commercial taxes etc.

Concept note-2: -The Union Government approved the National e-Governance Plan (NeGP), comprising of 27 Mission Mode Projects (MMPs) and 10 components on May 18, 2006.

Concept note-3: -In vertebrates, there are 28 different types of MMPs [1,2,8,9,11,12,13,16,17], at least 23 are expressed in human tissue [1]. MMPs can be subdivided according to bioinformatic analysis, in 5 types [23]: Non-furin regulated MMPs (MMP-1, -3, -7, -8, -10, -12, -13, -20, and -27);
