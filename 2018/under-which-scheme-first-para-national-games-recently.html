Under which scheme First Para-National Games recently?

(A) ** Khelo India Scheme

(B) Jan Dhan Yojana

(C) Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana

(D) Suraksha Scheme

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -First National Para Games 2022 under Khelo India Program-PM Jan Dhan Yojana.

Concept note-2: -The Khelo India Youth Games 2022 is all set to kick off on January 30, 2023, with a Grand Opening ceremony at the TT Nagar Stadium in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. Prime Minister Narendra Modi is scheduled to be present during the opening ceremony, ahead of the tournament.

Concept note-3: -On 27 February 2019, PM Narendra Modi launched the Khelo India App at the Youth Indian Parliament in Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi to promote sports and fitness.

Concept note-4: -KIYG 2023 is being held at 11 venues across eight cities in Madhya Pradesh. Over 5, 000 athletes will compete in 27 sports for 1, 936 medals. Get full schedule. The Khelo India Youth Games 2023 will be held in Madhya Pradesh from January 30 to February 11, 2023.

Concept note-5: -Khelo India – National Programme for Development of Sports.
