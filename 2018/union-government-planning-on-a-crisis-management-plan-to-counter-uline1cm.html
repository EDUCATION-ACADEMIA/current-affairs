Union Government planning on a Crisis Management Plan to counter _________?

(A) ** Cyber Attacks

(B) Terrorism

(C) Health Affairs

(D) Water Management

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -These four critical elements – that are often missing in company crisis management teams and plans – are: Clearly identified team roles and responsibilities. A formal incident assessment team and process. Effective Incident Action Planning (IAP) skills.

Concept note-2: -A crisis management plan outlines how your business will react if a crisis occurs. The plan should identify who will take action and what their roles will be. The goal of a crisis management plan is to minimize damage and restore business operations as quickly as possible.

Concept note-3: -Prevention, mitigation, preparedness, response and recovery are the five steps of Emergency Management.
