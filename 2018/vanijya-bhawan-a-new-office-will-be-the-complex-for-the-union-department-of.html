Concept note-1: -Vanijya Bhawan, the new premises of Ministry of Commerce and Industry will be unveiled by Prime Minister Tomorrow. Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the new premises of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry Vanijya Bhawan on Thursday at 10:30 AM.

Concept note-3: -The Bhawan is built on an area of 4.33 Acres near iconic India Gate at a cost of Rs. 226 Cr.
