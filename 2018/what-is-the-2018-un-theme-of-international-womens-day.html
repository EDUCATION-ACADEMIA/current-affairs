What is the 2018 UN Theme of "International Women’s Day"?

(A) Investing in Women and Girls

(B) Equality for Women is Progress for All

(C) Planet 50-50 by 2030: Step It Up for Gender Equality

(D) ** Time is Now: Rural and urban activists transforming women’s lives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year, the UN theme for International Women’s Day, is “Time is Now: Rural and urban activists transforming women’s lives, ” echoing the priority theme of the upcoming 62nd session of the UN Commission on the Status of Women.

Concept note-2: -The theme for International Women’s Day, 8 March 2023 (IWD 2023) is, “DigitALL: Innovation and technology for gender equality”.

Concept note-3: -8 March is International Women’s Day – devoted to celebrating the achievements of women and seeking gender equality. The campaign theme this year is #EmbraceEquity – while the United Nations’ theme is ‘DigitALL: Innovation and technology for gender equality’.

Concept note-4: -The 2017 theme for International Women’s Day, 8 March, focuses on “Women in the Changing World of Work: Planet 50-50 by 2030”.
