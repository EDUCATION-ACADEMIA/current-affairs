What is the aim of the Rajasthan government to sign MoU with Microsoft?

(A) To provide employment to the youth

(B) To improve the standards of digital literacy in Rajasthan

(C) Only B

(D) ** Both A and B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Microsoft. The objective is to develop technical education at government colleges, increase digital literacy and improve digital education in Rajasthan.

Concept note-2: -Ayushman Bharat Mahatma Gandhi Rajasthan Swasthya Bima Yojana (AB-MGRSBY) Mukhyamantri Nishulk Dava Yojna | Chief Minister’s Free Medicine Scheme. Mukhyamantri Nishulk Janch Yojna. Nishulk Sanitary Napkins Distribution Scheme.

Concept note-3: -DoITC aims to leverage Information & Communication Technology (ICT) for improving governance, employment opportunities, bridging the socio-economic inequalities and improving the quality of life of citizen of the state.
