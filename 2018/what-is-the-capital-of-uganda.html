What is the Capital of Uganda?

(A) Kigali

(B) Amman

(C) ** Kampala

(D) Paramaribo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kampala, capital and largest city of Uganda. It occupies a series of hills at an elevation of about 3, 900 feet (1, 190 metres) and is situated in the southern part of the country, just north of Lake Victoria.

Concept note-2: -Uganda’s outstanding features are the largest freshwater lake on the continent, the source of the longest river, the strongest waterfall, the largest number of primates, and the highest number of mountain gorillas worldwide.

Concept note-3: -Uganda is currently ranked among the 15th poorest countries in the world. Latest rankings by the International Monetary Fund show that with a per capital income of 711 dollars, Uganda is the 14th poorest country while the poorest is South Sudan followed by Yemen.
