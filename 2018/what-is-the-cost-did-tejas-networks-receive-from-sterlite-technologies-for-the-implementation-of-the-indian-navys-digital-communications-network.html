What is the cost did Tejas Networks receive from Sterlite Technologies for the implementation of the Indian Navy’s digital communications network?

(A) Rs.10 crore

(B) Rs.101 crore

(C) ** Rs.111 crore

(D) Rs.150 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tejas Networks Obtains Rs. 111 crore Order from Sterlite Technologies.

Concept note-2: -Tejas Networks | Business | Tata group.

Concept note-3: -Tejas Networks is an optical, broadband and data networking products company based in India. The company designs develops and sells its products to telecom service providers, internet service providers, utilities, security and government entities in 75 countries.

Concept note-4: -Tejas products enable high-speed internet services for homes and enterprises, 4G/5G mobile backhaul, wholesale bandwidth services, and mission-critical networks in India and worldwide. Tejas Networks is an India-based optical and data networking products company.

Concept note-5: -The previous watershed moment for 22-year-old Tejas Networks-which had already listed on the bourses in 2017-came in July 2021. That month, Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group, acquired a majority stake in the firm.
