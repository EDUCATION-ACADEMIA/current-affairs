What is the full form of NMCG?

(A) ** National Mission for Clean Ganga

(B) National Mission for Compact Ganga

(C) National Movement for Clean Ganga

(D) National Mission for Clear Ganga

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG)

Concept note-2: -The Secretary to the Government of India, MoWR, RD&GR is the current chairman of the Governing Council of NMCG.

Concept note-3: -National Mission for Clean Ganga | NMCG. 2.To maintain minimum ecological flows in the river Ganga with the aim of ensuring water quality and environmentally sustainable development.

Concept note-4: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi chaired the National Ganga Council meet held on 30th December 2022 via video conferencing in Kolkata.

Concept note-5: -Asok Kumar G has made significant and diverse contribution to the Water and Sanitation Sector. Presently he is the Mission Director, National Water Mission (NWM) and till recently held additional charge of the post of Executive Director (Projects) in the National Mission to Clean Ganga (NMCG) also.
