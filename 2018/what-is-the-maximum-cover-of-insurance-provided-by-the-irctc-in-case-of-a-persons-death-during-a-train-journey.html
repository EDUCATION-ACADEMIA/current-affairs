What is the maximum cover of insurance provided by the IRCTC, in case of a person’s death during a train journey?

(A) ** Rs. 10 lakh

(B) Rs. 7.5 lakh

(C) Rs. 2 lakh

(D) Rs. 5 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The travel insurance that passengers can purchase at 49 paise while purchasing their train tickets is standard travel insurance. It offers a coverage of up to Rs. 10 lakhs and covers events such as train accidents leading to death or permanent disability of the passenger and even robbery, loot, and other hazards.

Concept note-2: -The premium w.e.f 01-Nov-2021 is Re. 35 paise per passenger inclusive of all taxes. 5. Customer shall receive the policy information through SMS and on their registered email IDs directly from Insurance Companies along with the link for filling nomination details.

Concept note-3: -The coverage for the policy shall be for each passenger under the PNR in case of Death, Permanent Total Disability, Permanent Partial Disability, and Hospitalization Expenses for Injury and Transportation of mortal remains following Rail Accident or untoward incident.

Concept note-4: -Moreover, this complimentary travel insurance also includes an exclusive coverage of Rs. 1 lakh against household theft / robbery during the travel period of the passengers.

Concept note-5: -If a confirmed ticket is cancelled more than 48 hrs before the scheduled departure of the train, flat cancellation charges shall be deducted Rs. 240/- for AC First Class/Executive Class, Rs. 200/- for AC 2 Tier/First Class, Rs. 180 for AC 3 Tier/AC Chair car/ AC 3 Economy, Rs.
