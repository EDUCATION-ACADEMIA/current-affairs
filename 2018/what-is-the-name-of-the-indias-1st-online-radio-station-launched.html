What is the name of the India’s 1st Online Radio Station Launched?

(A) Radio Bharat

(B) ** Radio Umang

(C) Mann Ki Baat

(D) All India Radio

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s 1st Internet Radio “Radio Aksh” For Visually Challenged Launched In Nagpur.

Concept note-2: -Radio Umang: India’s first online radio station launched.

Concept note-3: -If you were to set about researching the history of internet radio you would learn the world’s first ever internet radio station was launched by Carl Malmud way back in 1993.

Concept note-4: -Deccan Radio (Nizam Radio 1932), the first radio station in Hyderabad State (now Hyderabad, India), went live on air on 3 February 1935. It was launched by Mir Osman Ali Khan the seventh Nizam of Hyderabad with a transmitting power of 200 Watts.

Concept note-5: -The country’s first-ever radio channel for the visually impaired, named ‘Radio Aksh’ has been launched in Nagpur.
