What is the name of the mobile app that the Indian Railways (IR) recently launched to help the passengers register their complaints through mobile phone/web?

(A) IR Madraid

(B) IR Serv

(C) ** IR E-aD

(D) Rail Madad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rail Madad application has been developed to enable Railway passenger to lodge a complaint or give suggestion through UMANG app and facility to track live status of complaints. The objective of this portal is to enhance experience of Railways passengers with swift and satisfactory resolution of complaints.

Concept note-2: -With RailMadad service, the Indian Railways will be able to assist passengers 24*7, said the ministry. To assist passengers with integrated solutions, the Ministry of Railways has launched the RailMadad service in the country.

Concept note-3: -The passengers can also file their complaints and suggestions on the web page http://www.coms.indianrailways.gov.in/. The passengers can also SMS their complaints on mobile number 9717630982.

Concept note-4: -Install the newly launched “IRCTC RAIL CONNECT” android app and book a railway ticket anywhere in India at your fingertips. Experience the latest features in addition to existing train ticketing services: :: New users register and activate from App directly.

Concept note-5: -Specially Crafted App for Differently Abled to Smoothen Their Train Journey. Coming soon Android.
