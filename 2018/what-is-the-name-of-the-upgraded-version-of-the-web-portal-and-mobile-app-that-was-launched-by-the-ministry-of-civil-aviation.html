What is the name of the upgraded version of the web portal and mobile app that was launched by the Ministry of Civil Aviation?

(A) Airservice 2.0

(B) ** AirSewa 2.0

(C) Digiyatra 2.0

(D) Digisewa 2.0

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is E-Sampada. Hardeep S Puri launched E-Sampada, a web portal and mobile app on Good Governance Day in December 2020.

Concept note-2: -AirSewa app and website provides you information about flights operating from various airports in India. All the services and facilities including assistance to disabled or unaccompanied minor etc available at various airports in India.

Concept note-3: -The government on Thursday launched DigiYatra which will provide a seamless entry and embarkation process for air passengers at the airports in the national capital, Bangalore and Varanasi.
