What is the new national record in the women’s 100m race?

(A) 11.30 seconds

(B) ** 11.29 seconds

(C) 11.31 seconds

(D) 11.28 seconds

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dutee Chand Dutee Chand held the 100m National Record of 11.17 seconds recorded at the Indian Grand Prix held last year in Patiala.

Concept note-2: -After all, Amlan Borgohain is the fastest Indian sprinter to date, with both the men’s 100m and 200m national records under his belt.

Concept note-3: -The current record is 10.49 seconds set by Florence Griffith-Joyner in 1988.
