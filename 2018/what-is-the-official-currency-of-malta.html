What is the official Currency of Malta?

(A) Dinar

(B) Leu

(C) Dollar

(D) ** Euro

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The currency of Malta is the Euro.

Concept note-2: -Malta joined the European Union in 2004 and adopted the euro on 1 January 2008.

Concept note-3: -MTL-Maltese Lira The currency code for Maltese Liri is MTL.

Concept note-4: -One EUR is equivalent to 0.429300 MTL. Enter the amount to be converted in the box to the left of Indian Rupee. Use “Swap currencies” to make Maltese Lira the default currency.
