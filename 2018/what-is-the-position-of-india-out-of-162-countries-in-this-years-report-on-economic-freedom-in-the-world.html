What is the position of India out of 162 countries, in this year’s report on Economic Freedom in the World?

(A) ** 96

(B) 98

(C) 92

(D) 94

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India ranks 111 out of 162 countries on personal freedom index.

Concept note-2: -The rankings of some other major countries are Japan (12th), Canada (14th), Germany (25th), Italy (44th), France (54th), Mexico (64th), India (89th), Russia (94th), Brazil (114th), and China (116th).

Concept note-3: -Published: Thursday 08 September 2022 India ranked 132 out of 191 countries in the United Nations Human Development Index 2021-22 released September 8, 2022.

Concept note-4: -India has slipped eight positions below its last year’s 142nd rank out of 180 countries in the World Press Freedom Index. India has slipped to 150th position in 2022 World Press Freedom Index. Norway has bagged the first post in the report by Reporters Without Borders. North Korea remains at the bottom.

Concept note-5: -India slipped to the 119th position in the Human Freedom Index Report 2021 of 165 countries.
