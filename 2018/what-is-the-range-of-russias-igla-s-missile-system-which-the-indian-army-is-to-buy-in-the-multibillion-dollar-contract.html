What is the range of Russia’s Igla-S missile system which the Indian Army is to buy in the multibillion-dollar contract?

(A) Long Range Air Defence (LNGRAD)

(B) Medium Range Air Defence (MEDRAD)

(C) Short Range Air Defence (SHORAD)

(D) ** Very Short Range Air Defence (VSHORAD)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As per requirements, the VSHORAD should have a maximum range of 6 km, altitude of 3 km along with all-weather capability and will replace the existing Igla in service which is in urgent need of replacement.

Concept note-2: -India has signed a deal worth over Rs 35,000 crore to acquire five squadrons of the S-400 air defence missiles from Russia over three years and deliveries of all units are expected to get over by end of the next financial year.

Concept note-3: -"Igla-S” man-portable air defense system is designed to defeat all types of visible fixed- and rotary-winged aircraft on head-on and in pursuit courses as well as small aerial targets like cruise missiles around the clock under induced thermal and background noise.
