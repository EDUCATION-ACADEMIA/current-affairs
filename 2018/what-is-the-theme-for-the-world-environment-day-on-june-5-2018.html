What is the theme for the World Environment Day on June 5, 2018?

(A) Connecting People to Nature - in the city and on the land, from the poles to the equator

(B) Go wild for life

(C) ** Beat Plastic Pollution

(D) Green Economy: Does it include you?

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2018. India hosted the 45th celebration of World Environment Day under the theme “Beat Plastic Pollution.” Over 6, 000 people gathered at Versova Beach in Mumbai to join UN Environment Champion of the Earth Afroz Shah in a beach clean-up, where they collected over 90, 000 kg of plastic.

Concept note-2: -The theme for 2018 is beating plastic pollution. The Host Every World Environment Day has a different global host country, where the official celebrations take place.

Concept note-3: -India will serve as the global host of the 2018 World Environment Day, which will convene under the theme, ‘Beat Plastic Pollution’.

Concept note-4: -World Environment Day-theme 2022 World Environment Day is celebrated 5th June worldwide, and this 2022 year theme is entitled as “Only One Earth”.

Concept note-5: -The theme for 2017 is “connecting people to nature". The theme for World Environment Day 2017, implores us to get outdoors and into nature, to appreciate its beauty and its importance, and to take forward the call to protect the Earth that we share.
