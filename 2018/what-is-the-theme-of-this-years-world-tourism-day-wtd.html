What is the theme of this year’s World Tourism Day (WTD)?

(A) Sustainable Tourism - a Tool for Development

(B) Tourism for all - promoting universal accessibility

(C) Tourism and Community Development

(D) ** Tourism and the Digital Transformation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of World Tourism Day 2021 is “Tourism for Inclusive Growth.” The day focuses on Tourism for Inclusive Growth.

Concept note-2: -The inaugural report is titled “Rethinking Tourism: From Crisis to Transformation”, reflecting the timely relevance of the 2022 theme as well as the unprecedented crisis that hit the sector in 2020.

Concept note-3: -For World Tourism Day 2023, hosted in the Middle East, the following theme is proposed: “Tourism and Green Investments”. Sustainable investments are at the centre of tourism’s contribution to the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

Concept note-4: -World Tourism Day 2022: The theme of this year is “ Rethinking Tourism". The aim is to rethink tourism for development, including through education and jobs, and tourism’s impact on the planet and opportunities to grow more sustainably.

Concept note-5: -The theme of World Tourism Day 2022 is ‘Rethinking Tourism’. Everyone will focus on understanding the growth of the tourism sector and reviewing and redeveloping tourism after the COVID-19 pandemic.
