What policy did the Union Cabinet approve recently with an aim to increase India’s exports to $60 billion by 2022?

(A) Agriculture Import Policy

(B) ** Agriculture Export Policy

(C) Fertilizers Export Policy

(D) Fertilizers Import Policy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The present policy aims to keep the percentage of exports limited to 12% for the fiscal year 2022-23. This will help create a moderate impact on foreign investors in the Indian market. The Ministry of Commerce has attempted to simplify export techniques and reduce export duties.

Concept note-2: -To promote novel, indigenous, organic, ethnic, traditional and non-traditional Agri products exports. To provide an institutional mechanism for pursuing market access, tackling barriers and deal with sanitary and phytosanitary issues.

Concept note-3: -New Delhi, Feb 26 (KNN) The Government of Maharashtra launched the state’s Agriculture Export Policy (AEP) which will focus on export promotion of 21 agricultural commodities.
