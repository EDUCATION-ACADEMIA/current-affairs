What type missiles does India plan to procure from Israel?

(A) SAM missiles

(B) ** Spike missiles

(C) Tomahawk missiles

(D) ATA missiles

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indo-Israeli joint venture Kalyani Rafael Advanced Systems(KRAS) rolled out the 100th kit of the made-in-India Medium Range Surface to Air Missile (MRSAM ), for delivery to the Indian Armed Forces .

Concept note-2: -India as a destination for Israeli arms Between 1997 and 2000, 15 percent of all Israeli arms exports travelled to India. By the mid-2000s, this had increased to 27 percent, with India broadening its range of purchases, such as surveillance equipment, drones and surface-to-air-missiles.

Concept note-3: -The AD-1 is a long-range interceptor missile designed for both low exo-atmospheric and endo-atmospheric interception of long-range ballistic missiles as well as aircraft.

Concept note-4: -is a high response, quick reaction, vertically launched supersonic missile, designed to neutralize enemy aerial threats – missiles, aircraft, guided bombs, helicopters. Used by Army, Navy and Air Force as different variants.
