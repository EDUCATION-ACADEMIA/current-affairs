When does India celebrate the Air Force Day?

(A) 5th October

(B) 6th October

(C) 7th October

(D) ** 8th October

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indians proudly celebrate October 8 as the day to pay tribute to the air warriors of Indian Air Force (IAF) who tirelessly defend the nation by securing the Indian airspace and conduct rescue operations during natural calamities. The President of India is the Commander-in-chief of the IAF.

Concept note-2: -Indians proudly celebrate October 8 as the day to pay tribute to the air warriors of Indian Air Force (IAF) who tirelessly defend the nation by securing the Indian airspace and conduct rescue operations during natural calamities. The President of India is the Commander-in-chief of the IAF.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Air Force Day is celebrated on October 8th every year, to mark the date of establishment of the Bharatiya Vayu Sena. 8th October 2021 was India’s 89th Air Force Day. IAF and Indian Air Force Day both constitute an important part of the UPSC Syllabus.

Concept note-4: -Indian Air Force Day is celebrated on October 8 every year. Every year, the Air Force Day is celebrated at Hindon base in presence of IAF chief and senior officials of the three armed forces. These celebrations include an air display where the most crucial and vintage aircraft put up a magnificent show.

Concept note-5: -Indian Air Force Day (8th October) This day is celebrated to commemorate the efforts of the Indian Air Force. It is considered one of the most important days in October in India.
