When does National Sports Day celebrated every year?

(A) July 26

(B) September 15

(C) ** August 29

(D) November 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India celebrates National Sports Day on 29th August to commemorate the birth anniversary of hockey legend, Major Dhyan Chand.

Concept note-2: -National Sports Day is celebrated on 29th August on the birth anniversary of Major Dhyan Chand. Know about the life of Dhyan Chand, and awards given on the National Sports Day in this article.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The National Sports Day in India is celebrated on August 29. Question: When was National Sports Day first celebrated in India? Answer: National Sports Day was included in the list of celebratory days in India for the first time in 2012. Question: Why do we celebrate National Sports Day?

Concept note-4: -The Department of Legal Affairs celebrated National Sports Day on 29th August 2022 at Shastri Bhawan, New Delhi, to commemorate the birth anniversary of Hockey Legend Major Dhyan Chand Ji.
