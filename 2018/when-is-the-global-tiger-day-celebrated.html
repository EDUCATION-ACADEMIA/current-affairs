When is the Global Tiger Day celebrated?

(A) July 27

(B) July 28

(C) ** July 29

(D) July 30

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Tiger Day is observed every year on July 29 across the world in order to raise awareness for tiger conservation.

Concept note-2: -International Tiger Day: History International Tiger Day was introduced in 2010 after it was discovered that 97 per cent of tigers had disappeared in the past century, with only about 3, 000 remaining.

Concept note-3: -International Tiger Day was founded in 2010 at the Saint Petersburg Tiger Summit in Russia to raise awareness about the loss of the tiger population globally. On this day, the 13 tiger range countries came together to create Tx2, the global goal to double the number of wild tigers by the year 2022.
