When is the Indian Navy Day celebrated?

(A) 1st December

(B) 2nd December

(C) 3rd December

(D) ** 4th December

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India celebrates December 04 as Navy Day, every year, to acknowledge the role of the Indian Navy and commemorate its achievements in ‘Operation Trident’ during the 1971 Indo-Pak War.

Concept note-2: -Every year on 4th December, the Indian Navy commemorates the commencement of Operation Trident against Pakistan during the Indo-Pak War. The Indian Navy organised the night strike on December 4-5, 1971. The day is observed to acknowledge and appreciate the Indian Navy’s achievements and involvement in the country.
