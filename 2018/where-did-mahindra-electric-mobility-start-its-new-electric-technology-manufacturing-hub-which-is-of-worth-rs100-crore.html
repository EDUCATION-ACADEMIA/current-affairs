Where did Mahindra Electric Mobility start its new electric technology manufacturing hub which is of worth Rs.100 crore?

(A) Pune

(B) ** Bengaluru

(C) Ahmedabad

(D) Kolkatta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mahindra has announced an electric vehicle manufacturing facility in Pune in the Indian state of Maharashtra and plans to invest the equivalent of Rs. 1.14 billion for the purpose over a period of seven to eight years.

Concept note-2: -The company’s first vehicle was the REVAi electric car, available in 26 countries with more than 4, 000 of its different versions sold worldwide by mid-March 2011. Reva was acquired by Indian conglomerate Mahindra & Mahindra in May 2010. After the acquisition, the company launched the electric hatchback e2o in 2013.

Concept note-3: -The Mahindra Verito now comes with an electric variant-eVerito India’s first electric sedan that is silent, smooth and suave. Learn more about the eVerito specifications, reviews of Mahindra Verito electric car, the price of eVerito and mileage (range).

Concept note-4: -There are 2 Mahindra Electric cars currently available for sale at starting price Rs 9.13 Lakh Lakh. The most popular Mahindra Electric cars are Mahindra XUV400 EV (Rs. 15.99-18.99 Lakh), Mahindra E Verito (Rs. 9.13-9.46 Lakh).
