Where does the 73rd United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) happen?

(A) ** US

(B) UK

(C) Germany

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Regular sessions. The General Assembly meets annually in a regular session that opens on the third Tuesday of September, and runs until the following September. Sessions are held at United Nations Headquarters in New York unless changed by the General Assembly by a majority vote.

Concept note-2: -The 77th session of the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA 77) opened on September 13, 2022, with High-Level Week taking place September 19-23 in New York.

Concept note-3: -The first session of the assembly convened on Jan. 10, 1946, in London, with 51 countries represented. As of 2006 there were 192 members of the General Assembly.
