Where is the first and second AIIB annual meetings held?

(A) Seria, Brunei and Dhaka, Bangladesh

(B) Suva, Fiji and Jakarta Indonesia

(C) ** Beijing, China and Jeju, South Korea

(D) Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia and Kathmandu, Nepal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Minister of Finance & Corporate Affairs Nirmala Sitharaman attended the 7th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) via video conference from New Delhi on 26 October 2022.

Concept note-2: -AIIB and the Government of India jointly hosted the third Annual Meeting of the AIIB Board of Governors in Mumbai, India.

Concept note-3: -The Board of Governors of the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) announced that its eighth Annual Meeting will be held in Sharm el-Sheikh, Egypt, on Sept. 25-26, 2023.

Concept note-4: -The theme of the 2022 AIIB Annual Meeting is “Sustainable Infrastructure Toward a Connected World”, which aims to reaffirm infrastructure’s crucial role in supporting recovery, growth and connectivity.
