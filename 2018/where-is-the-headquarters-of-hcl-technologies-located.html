Where is the Headquarters of HCL Technologies located?

(A) ** Noida

(B) Kolkata

(C) Bengaluru

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HCL Technologies Limited, d/b/a HCLTech (formerly Hindustan Computers Limited), is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company headquartered in Noida.

Concept note-2: -India: Bengaluru, Chennai, Coimbatore, Gurugram, Hubli, Hyderabad, Kochi, Kolkata, Lucknow, Madurai, Mumbai, Nagpur, Noida, Pune, Vijaywada.

Concept note-3: -About HCL IT City The Bhoomi Pujan and foundation stone for HCL Lucknow was laid in mid October 2014. Based in the heart of the IT City SEZ Zone at Check Gajaria, HCL campus is spread over 100 acres and is the largest campus of HCL across India. It can accommodate more than 6, 000 employees when fully operational.

Concept note-4: -HCL is a multinational IT company started in India. The company head office is located in Noida. HCL was established in 1976 as one of India’s original IT start-ups.
