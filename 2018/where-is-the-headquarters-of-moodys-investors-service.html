Where is the headquarters of Moody’s Investors Service?

(A) ** New York

(B) Spain

(C) Denmark

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MOODY’S SHARED SERVICES INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED is a hospital & health care company based out of 3rd Floor, Electric Mansion, Appasaheb Marathe Marg, , Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

Concept note-2: -Moody’s Investors Service provides investors with credit ratings, risk analysis, and research for stocks, bonds, and government entities. Moody’s Analytics develops software and tools to help capital markets with risk management, credit analysis, and economic research.

Concept note-3: -History of Moody’s Initially called Moody’s Manual of Industrial and Miscellaneous Securities, it was later superseded by Moody’s Manual of Railroads and Corporation Securities, then by Moody’s Analyses of Investments.

Concept note-4: -Moody’s is headquartered in New York, NY and has 10 office and retail locations located throughout the US.
