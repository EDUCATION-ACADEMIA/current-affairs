Where is the headquarters of U.N. Food and Agricultural Organization (FAO)?

(A) Geneva, Switzerland

(B) London, UK

(C) ** Vienna, Austria

(D) Rome, Italy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Headquartered in Rome, Italy, the FAO maintains offices throughout the world. The organization, which has more than 180 members, is governed by the biennial FAO conference, in which each member country, as well as the European Union, is represented.

Concept note-2: -The FAO Building (Italian: Palazzo FAO, literally “FAO Palace") is the international headquarters of the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO), located in the San Saba rione of Rome, Italy.

Concept note-3: -The Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) is a specialized agency of the United Nations leading international efforts to defeat hunger. FAO’s goal is to achieve food and security for all and make sure that people have regular access to enough high-quality food and lead active, healthy lives.

Concept note-4: -The Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) is a specialized agency of the United Nations that leads international efforts to defeat hunger.
