Where is the Mestripalya lake located?

(A) Mumbai

(B) Hyderabad

(C) ** Bengaluru

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dating back to 1537, Ulsoor Lake is one of the oldest and largest lakes in Bangalore.

Concept note-2: -Bangalore was once a city of 1,000 lakes. The earliest record of the lakes dates back to the 16th century, when the founder of Bangalore-a chieftain known as Kempe Gowda-began constructing artificial tanks for irrigation, fishing and drinking water.

Concept note-3: -KSPCB monitors 278 lakes in Karnataka, 106 of which are in Bengaluru.
