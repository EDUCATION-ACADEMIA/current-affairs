Where will the 2018 G20 summit happen?

(A) ** Buenos Aires, Argentina

(B) Geneva, Switzerlan

(C) Vienna, Austria

(D) London, UK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2018 meeting of the G20 Leaders’ Summit will take place in Buenos Aires, Argentina, 10 years after the first G20 Leaders’ Summit took place in Washington, DC.

Concept note-2: -The 2018 G20 Buenos Aires summit was the thirteenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), which was held on 30 November and 1 December 2018 in the city of Buenos Aires, Argentina. It was the first G20 summit to be hosted in South America.

Concept note-3: -The Group of G20 (G20) comprises Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Italy, Japan, Republic of Korea, Mexico, Russia, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, Turkey, United Kingdom, and the United States along with the European Union.

Concept note-4: -The G20 Summit 2023 will be going to organized in India. As per the reports, India has the presidency this time and will host the G20 meetings in New Delhi.

Concept note-5: -India holds the Presidency of the G20 from December 1, 2022 to November 30, 2023. The 43 Heads of Delegations-the largest ever in G20-will be participating in the final New Delhi Summit in September next year.
