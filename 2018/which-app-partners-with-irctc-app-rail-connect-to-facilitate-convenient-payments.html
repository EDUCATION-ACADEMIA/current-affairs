Which app partners with IRCTC app Rail connect to facilitate convenient payments?

(A) ** PhonePe

(B) Oxigen

(C) Freecharge

(D) Mobikwik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s AI-driven financial wellness platform CASHe said that it has partnered with Indian Railways Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) to provide a Travel Now Pay Later (TNPL) payment option on its travel app IRCTC Rail Connect.

Concept note-2: -iPay is an IRCTC owned Payment Gateway. With the launch of IRCTC iPay, the passengers will not need any 3rd party platforms as the IRCTC iPay provides payment options like Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, Net Banking, iMudra Wallet.

Concept note-3: -IRCTC Rail Connect : Railway’s Official Mobile App. IRCTC Connect is the latest FREE Android IRCTC official mobile app that allows you to perform Train Ticket Booking, Book Your meal via Mobile, Air Ticket booking and IRCTC Tourism etc via your mobile phone. Indian Railways Catering and Tourism Corp. Ltd.

Concept note-4: -Install the newly launched “IRCTC RAIL CONNECT” android app and book a railway ticket anywhere in India at your fingertips.

Concept note-5: -IRCTC Indian Railway Rail Connect App: The Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) enables travellers to book tickets through its IRCTC application. The travellers can easily book their ticket via IRCTC app and make payments using IRCTC’s e-wallet.
