Which bank First to merge in Share Swap Deal recently?

(A) ** IDFC Bank

(B) HDFC Bank

(C) SBI Bank

(D) Dena Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The boards also approved a share swap ratio of 139 shares of IDFC Bank for 10 shares of Capital First, a debt financing firm for small entrepreneurs. The merger will also result in V Vaidya-nathan, currently chairman and managing director of Capital First, succeeding Ra-jiv Lallas MD and CEO of the merged entity.

Concept note-2: -The merger, expected to conclude in the second quarter of 2023, will give HDFC access to 16.7 trillion rupees ($202 billion) of funds comprising so-called low-cost current and savings account deposits and time deposits of the bank.
