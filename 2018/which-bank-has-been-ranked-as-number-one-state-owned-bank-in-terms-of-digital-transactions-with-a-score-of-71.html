(B) Allahabad Bank

(C) Andhra Bank

Concept note-1: -"Based on the recent findings of DFS, PNB is ranked number one PSU bank in digital transactions in India. The bank is fully committed to the Digital India initiative,” it said. The bank is rated as ‘Good’ by the government with a score of 71 which is the highest category of performance, it said.
