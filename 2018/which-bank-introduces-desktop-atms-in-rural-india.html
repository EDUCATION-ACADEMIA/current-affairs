Which Bank Introduces Desktop ATMs in Rural India?

(A) ** Andhra Pradesh Grameena Vikas Bank

(B) Saptagiri Grameena Bank

(C) Chaitanya Godavari Grameena Bank

(D) Andhra Pragathi Grameena Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Andhra Pradesh Grameena Vikas Bank introduces ‘Desktop ATMs’ in rural India - The Hindu BusinessLine.

Concept note-2: -Background. The first ATM in India was set up in 1987 by HSBC in Mumbai.

Concept note-3: -The first ATM in India was set up in 1987 by HSBC in Mumbai. Q. Which bank first introduced credit card in India?

Concept note-4: -India’s largest private sector bank, ICICI launched the “ATM on Wheels” country first Mobile ATM, in Mumbai. It was connected to the bank through the GPRS technology.

Concept note-5: -There are over 650,000 villages in India, but only one ATM for every ten villages.
