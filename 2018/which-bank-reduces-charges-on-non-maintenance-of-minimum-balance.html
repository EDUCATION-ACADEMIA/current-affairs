Which bank Reduces Charges On Non-Maintenance Of Minimum Balance?

(A) ** State Bank of India

(B) RBL Bank

(C) Axis Bank

(D) Federal Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -State Bank of India has cut penalty charges for non-maintenance of average monthly balance (AMB) by up to 75%. State Bank of India (SBI) penalty for not maintaining minimum balance is much less than private banks.

Concept note-2: -SBI, India’s largest bank, does not ask its customers to maintain a minimum balance. What is the minimum average daily balance?

Concept note-3: -It is worth noting that while banks are allowed to charge non-maintenance of minimum balance penalty to the customers, there is no cap on the charges. The penal charges have to be approved by the bank’s board. Banks charge different charges based on the location of the account.

Concept note-4: -Kotak 811 Zero Balance Account (Kotak Mahindra Bank-811 Digital Bank Account): Kotak 811 is a zero balance savings account that has zero charges and maintenance fees.
