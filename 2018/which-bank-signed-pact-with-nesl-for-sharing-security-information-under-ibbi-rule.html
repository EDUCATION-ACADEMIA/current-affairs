Which bank signed pact with NeSL for sharing security information under IBBI rule?

(A) ** Corporation Bank

(B) State Bank of India

(C) Dena Bank

(D) Yes Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Overseas Bank (IOB) has signed Information Utility pact with NeSl.

Concept note-2: -The primary role of NeSL is to serve as a repository of legal evidence holding the information pertaining to any debt/claim, as submitted by the financial or operational creditor and verified and authenticated by the parties to the debt.

Concept note-3: -National E-Governance Services Limited (NeSL) is the first Information Utility (IU) registered with The Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India (IBBI) as per the Information Utility Regulations, 2017.

Concept note-4: -Rs. 25/-per loan account as a one-time charge.

Concept note-5: -Ans. Yes. All the parties connected to Debt need to electronically authenticate the information with an IU, in order to store the information.
