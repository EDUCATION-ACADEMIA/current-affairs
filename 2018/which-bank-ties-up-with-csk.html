Which bank Ties Up With CSK?

(A) Bank of Baroda

(B) Canara Bank

(C) Dena Bank

(D) ** Equitas Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -March 23, 2022. Mumbai: ICICI Bank today announced that it has launched a co-branded credit card in partnership with Chennai Super Kings (CSK), one of the most successful cricket teams in India.

Concept note-2: -The makers of TVS Eurogrip (the principal sponsor of CSK), TVS Srichakra Limited, are headquartered in Madurai, Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-3: -The team has also signed a chest sponsorship deal with Gulf Oil. For nearly 11 years, the company has been associated with the franchise. CSK has also named SNJ 10000, Jio, Astral Pipes, British Empire, Equitas Small Finance Bank and Nippon Paints as principal partners for IPL 2022.

Concept note-4: -Reliance Jio. The Mumbai-based Indian telecom giant has maintained a sponsorship deal with all eight teams across IPL since the 2018 season. For the CSK, Jio’s logo can be seen on the left of the team’s matchday cap and helmet.

Concept note-5: -The Chennai Super Kings ICICI Bank Credit Card has recently been launched by the ICICI Bank in partnership with the IPL team, CSK. It is targeted at individuals who are big fans of the team and have a dream of meeting the team players or getting their autographs.
