Which book was released on the occasion National Cancer Awareness Day?

(A) ** Book on Mouth Cancer

(B) The God of Small Things

(C) Train to Pakistan

(D) A Suitable Boy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The country is observing ‘Cancer Awareness Day’ today. On the occasion, a book entitled “Fifty years of cancer control in India” was released by Shri Shatrughan Sinha, Minister for Health & Family Welfare.

Concept note-2: -This year’s theme is “close the care gap” which is about understanding the inequities in cancer care and taking actions to make the necessary progress to address them.

Concept note-3: -National Cancer Awareness Day: History Therefore in 2014, the day was celebrated for the first time and focuses on the early detection and cure of cancer.

Concept note-4: -National Cancer Awareness Day is observed on November 7 in India. The day highlights the significance of increased awareness about cancer prevention and early detection for the general public.

Concept note-5: -National Cancer Awareness Day is observed on November 7 in India. Every year, this day is observed to draw attention to the importance of the condition and to raise awareness about its early detection, treatment, and diagnosis.
