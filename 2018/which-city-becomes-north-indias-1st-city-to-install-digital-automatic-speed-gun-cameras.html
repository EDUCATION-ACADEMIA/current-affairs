Which city Becomes North India’s 1st City To Install Digital Automatic Speed Gun Cameras?

(A) ** Chandigarh

(B) Guwahati

(C) Ahmedabad

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chandigarh Becomes North India’s 1st City to Install Digital Automatic Speed Gun Cameras [Video]

Concept note-2: -Of the 155 cameras, 30 are RLVD systems while 125 are OSVD cameras.

Concept note-3: -Most of the major cities of India - Mumbai, NCR, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Chennai - have installed cameras with the ANPR system on their roads. ANPR systems consist of fixed or mobile speed cameras that time vehicles over a known fixed distance and then calculate the motorist’s average speed for that journey.

Concept note-4: -"These cameras have capability of zoom in - zoom out facilities and it would work even in night and foggy weather. It can detect traffic violators of particular violations and straight away recommend control room for challans,” Ojha said.
