Which City in India has been ranked third among a list of 15 best cities in the world by Travel + Leisure?

(A) Gangtok

(B) Varanasi

(C) Mysore

(D) ** Udaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The list of Top 15 Best Cities in Asia has also been released by Travel + Leisure Magazine. In the list of top cities, Jaipur has been ranked third and Udaipur has got the fifth position. The top city in the list is Ubud (Indonesia), Chiang (Thailand) at the second place and Osaka (Japan) at the fourth place.

Concept note-2: -Additionally, in the list of top 15 Best Cities in Asia, Jaipur has been ranked third and Udaipur fifth. The top city on the list is Ubud in Indonesia, Chiang in Thailand ranked the second and Osaka in Japan at the fourth place. Check out the full list here.

Concept note-3: -NYC has found itself on this annual ranking for the past seven years, and this year it takes the number three spot!

Concept note-4: -UDAIPUR rated the ‘Top City in Asia’ and ranked #3 in the ‘World’s Top 15 Cities’.
