Which city ranked as India’s Most Congested City according to NBER?

(A) ** Bangalore

(B) Chennai

(C) Pune

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The research conducted by University of California, Berkley and 3 other universities studies the current status of mobility in urban India, using Google maps. Bengaluru tops the list as the most congested city followed by Mumbai in the second place and Pune in the seventh place.

Concept note-2: -World’s Most Congested Cities: Mumbai, India’s maximum city has been ranked world’s third most congested traffic as it loses 121 hours annually to traffic with congestion level standing at 53%.

Concept note-3: -Mumbai is the most congested city in India in 2021 as per the report, followed by Bengaluru and Delhi. The congestion level in Delhi fell by 14 percent in 2021 in comparison to 2019, while in Mumbai, it dropped by 18 percent.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Mumbai. Mumbai has been ranked as the fifth most congested city in the world in 2021, as per TomTom’s 2021 Traffic Index released on February 9, 2022.
