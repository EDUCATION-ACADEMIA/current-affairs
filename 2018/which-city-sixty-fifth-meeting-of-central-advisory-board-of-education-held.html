Which city Sixty Fifth meeting of Central Advisory Board of Education held?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Bihar

(C) Chennai

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -65th meeting of Central Advisory Board of Education held in New Delhi. The 65th Meeting of the Central Advisory Board of Education (CABE) was held under Chairmanship of Union Human Resource Development Minister Prakash Javadekar in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Union Human Resource Development Minister Shri Prakash Javadekar chairs the 65th Central Advisory Board of Education (CABE) The 65th Meeting of the Central Advisory Board of Education (CABE) was held under the Chairmanship of Union Human Resource Development Minister Shri Prakash Javadekar here today.

Concept note-3: -Union Human Resource Minister, Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’ chaired the Special Meeting of the Central Advisory Board of Education (CABE) held in New Delhi today.

Concept note-4: -The Central Advisory Board of Education, the oldest and the most important advisory body of the Government of India in education was first established in 1920 and dissolved in 1923 as a measure of economy. It was revived in 1935 and has been in existence ever since. 2.
