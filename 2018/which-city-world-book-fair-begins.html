Which city World Book Fair Begins?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Kolkata

(C) Chennai

(D) Patna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 31st New Delhi World Book Fair (NDWBF) will now take place in the newly constructed Halls 2-5 GF in Pragati Maidan from February 25 to March 5, according to the National Book Trust of India.

Concept note-2: -The fair will be open to visitors between January 31 and February 12. Spain is the focal country of Book Fair 2023, which will also include the 9th Kolkata Literature Festival from February 9 to 11.

Concept note-3: -The New Delhi World Book Fair, hosted at Pragati Maidan in New Delhi, is India’s second oldest book fair after the Kolkata Book Fair. The first New Delhi World Book Fair was held from March 18 to April 4, 1972 in roughly 6790 m2 area with 200 participants. It was inaugurated by V. V. Giri, then President of India.
