Which committee Released SATH-E Roadmaps recently?

(A) ** NITI Aayog

(B) Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana

(C) Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana

(D) Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NITI Aayog launched Project SATH-Education : Sustainable Action for Transforming Human capital in Education, not only to help States tackle these problems, but also to demonstrate the possibilities and challenges in making these systemic shifts for other States to follow.

Concept note-2: -Project SATH-E, ‘Sustainable Action for Transforming Human Capital-Education’, was launched in 2017 to identify and build three ‘role model’ States for the school education sector. After an elaborate selection process, Jharkhand, Odisha and Madhya Pradesh were chosen.

Concept note-3: -With the help of the State Governments, NITI Aayog has started the Sustainable Action for Transforming Human Capital (SATH) programme. The program’s goal is to start the transformation of the health and education industries.

Concept note-4: -With a vision to initiate transformation in the education and health sectors and to address the need expressed by many states for technical support from NITI Ayog, SATH – a programme for ” Sustainable Action for Transforming Human Capital” with the state government was launched by NITI Ayog.
