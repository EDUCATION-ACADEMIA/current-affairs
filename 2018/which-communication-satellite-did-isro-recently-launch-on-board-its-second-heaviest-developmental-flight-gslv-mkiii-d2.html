Which communication satellite did ISRO recently launch on board its second heaviest developmental flight GSLV-MkIII D2?

(A) ** GSAT-29

(B) GSAT-39

(C) GSAT-49

(D) GSAT-69

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -GSAT-29 is a multiband, multi-beam communication satellite, intended to serve as test bed for several new and critical technologies.

Concept note-2: -The Launch Vehicle Mark-3 (LVM 3), previously referred as the Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle Mark III (GSLV Mk3), is a three-stage medium-lift launch vehicle developed by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).

Concept note-3: -GSLV Mk-III (LVM3) is the new heavy lift launch vehicle of ISRO for achieving a 4000 kg spacecraft launching capability to GTO (Geosynchronous Transfer Orbit) in a cost effective manner.
