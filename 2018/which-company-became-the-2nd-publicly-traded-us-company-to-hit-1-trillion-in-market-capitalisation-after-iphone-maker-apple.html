Which company became the 2nd publicly traded US company to hit $1 trillion in market capitalisation after iPhone maker Apple?

(A) Walt Disney

(B) Microsoft

(C) Facebook

(D) ** Amazon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple became the first publicly traded U.S. company to hit a $1 trillion market cap during intraday trading on Aug.

Concept note-2: -Apple became the world’s first $3 trillion company for a brief time during intraday trading on Jan. 3, 2022. The iPhone maker also holds the distinction of being the world’s first company to reach $1 trillion and $2 trillion. According to analysts, Apple stock still has room to grow in the future.

Concept note-3: -Microsoft Corporation on Tuesday became the second US public company to hit $2 trillion in market value, briefly joining the league of Apple Inc.

Concept note-4: -Apple becomes first company to hit $3 trillion market value, then slips.
