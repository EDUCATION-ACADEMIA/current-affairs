Which company emerged as the most profitable Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) in 2017-18?

(A) Power Grid Corporation

(B) National Thermal Power Corporation Limited

(C) ** Indian Oil Corporation

(D) Oil and Natural Gas Corporation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Oil Corporation, Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) and NTPC emerged as the top three most profitable Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) in 2017-18. The companies contributed 13.37%, 12.49% and 6.48% respectively to the total profit earned by Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) during the period.

Concept note-2: -The top five CPSEs with the highest net profits were ONGC, Indian Oil Corporation, Power Grid Corporation of India, NTPC and Steel Authority of India Ltd (SAIL), the survey said.

Concept note-3: -At the top of the list is state-run Oil & Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) with a net profit of Rs 40,306 crore.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi: The total profit of 177 public sector companies (PSUs), out of 255 such operational enterprises, rose by 37.53 percent to Rs 1,89,320 crore in the 2020-21 fiscal year, the government has said.
