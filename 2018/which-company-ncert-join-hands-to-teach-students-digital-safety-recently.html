Which company NCERT Join Hands To Teach Students Digital Safety recently?

(A) ** Google

(B) Samsung

(C) Apple

(D) Yahoo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) has signed a pact with Google to integrate a course on Digital Citizenship and Safety in information and communication technology curriculum.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The National Council of Educational Research and Training-NCERT and Rotary India have signed an MoU. They will provide e-learning telecast content for class one to 12th overall NCERT TV channels.

Concept note-3: -ePathshala is a website and mobile application developed by NCERT to display and disseminate a variety of study resources to learners across the country. A number of e-resources like digital textbooks, videos, periodicals, etc are available through the website and mobile app.

Concept note-4: -It encourages teachers to be ICT literate for official and personal use to build comfort and later creativity in educational work. NCERT provides training to pre-service teachers through the Regional Institutes of Education (RIEs) to evaluate and integrate available materials into the learning process.

Concept note-5: -DIKSHA, e-Pathshala, NROER, ICT in Education curriculum are the ICT initiatives of NCERT.
