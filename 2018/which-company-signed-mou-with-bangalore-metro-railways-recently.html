Which company signed MoU with Bangalore metro railways recently?

(A) Cognizant

(B) ** Infosys

(C) Tata consultancy services

(D) Virtusa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A memorandum of understanding (MoU) has been signed between Bengaluru’s Namma Metro and ITPL (Information Technology Park Limited) for direct access to the metro line from the tech park located near Whitefield, announced the metro department.

Concept note-2: -Bengaluru Metro Rail Corporation Limited (BMRCL), a joint venture of Government of India and the State Government of Karnataka, is the agency for building, operating and expanding the Namma Metro network. Services operate daily between 05:00 and 24:00 running with a headway varying between 2–4 minutes.

Concept note-3: -Phase 2’s Contractors Ayesa Ingenieria Y Arguitectura S.A.U.

Concept note-4: -Two lines of Bangalore Metro will be available to the public by mid-2023: the extended Purple Line from Baiyappanahalli to Whitefield (15 km) and the new Yellow Line from R V Road to Bommasandra (19 km). The extended Purple Line from Baiyappanahalli to Whitefield will be inaugurated in two stages.

Concept note-5: -The Bangalore Metro Rail Corporation Ltd (BMRCL) has accepted China-based CRRC’s request to manufacture a total of 216 metro train coaches in partnership with Titagarh Wagons in Kolkata.
