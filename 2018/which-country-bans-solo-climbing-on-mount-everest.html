Which country Bans Solo Climbing on Mount Everest?

(A) ** Nepal

(B) China

(C) India

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Recently Nepal government made a decision to ban solo climbers, blind people and double amputees to climb the mountain in Nepal including the worlds highest peak-Mount Everest.

Concept note-2: -Foreign solo climbers will need to be accompanied by a guide while climbing Mount Everest. “The mountaineering regulation has been amended to improve safety of the climbers and has delegated more power to the Department of Tourism to function independently, ” Tourism Secretary Maheshwar Neupane told the Kathmandu Post.

Concept note-3: -Air pressure/density and oxygen levels The top of Mount Everest is one-third of the sea level’s atmospheric pressure. This level of air pressure is not convenient for helicopters to handle. The oxygen levels at the Everest base camp itself are at a 50% drop. The further up you go, the oxygen levels keep decreasing.
