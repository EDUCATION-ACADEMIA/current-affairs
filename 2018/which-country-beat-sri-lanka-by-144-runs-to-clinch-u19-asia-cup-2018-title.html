Which country beat Sri Lanka by 144 runs to clinch U19 Asia Cup 2018 title?

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) South Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India defeated Sri Lanka by 144 runs in the final to win the tournament for the 6th time.

Concept note-2: -Rohit’s solitary century in the tournament, an unbeaten 111, came against Pakistan in the 2018 edition in Dubai. Rohit had also led India to its seventh Asia Cup title in 2018, in the absence of then ODI skipper Virat Kohli.

Concept note-3: -India has won the Asia Cup the most number of times. The country registered itself on the Asia Cup Winners List seven times, in the years 1984, 1988, 1991, 1995, 2010, 2016, and 2018. After India, Sri Lanka won the Asia Cup six times in 1986, 1997, 2004, 2008, 2014, and 2022.

Concept note-4: -Navjot Singh Sidhu (IND) & Arjuna Ranatunga (SL) has won the maximum “Man of the Tournament” awards in the Asia Cup.
