Which country Calls Upon All Nations To Adopt Comprehensive Approach In Combating Terrorism?

(A) ** Vietnam

(B) Indonesia

(C) Australia

(D) Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of 2023, the list consists of Cuba, Iran, North Korea, and Syria. Countries formerly on the list were Iraq, Libya, South Yemen, and Sudan.

Concept note-2: -The Comprehensive Convention on International Terrorism (CCIT) is a proposed treaty which intends to criminalize all forms of international terrorism and deny terrorists, their financiers and supporters access to funds, arms, and safe havens.

Concept note-3: -The UN Global Counter-Terrorism Strategy (A/RES/60/288) is a unique global instrument to enhance national, regional and international efforts to counter terrorism. The General Assembly reviews the Strategy every two years, making it a living document attuned to Member States’ counter-terrorism priorities.
