Which country celebrated 70th Independence Day?

(A) ** Sri Lanka

(B) Pakistan

(C) India

(D) Nigeria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Consulate General of India, Birmingham celebrated India’s 70th Independence Day on August 15 with traditional fervour and enthusiasm at the Chancery premises.

Concept note-2: -India will complete the 75 years of Independence on 15th August 2022 but India will celebrate the 76th Independence day.

Concept note-3: -Photos: Pakistan celebrates 75th Independence Day.

Concept note-4: -Celebration of 70th Independence day of India. Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s government is likely to celebrate this memorable occasion with the theme “Yaad Karo Kurbani” (remember all the sacrifices).

Concept note-5: -This is the reason why August 15, 2022, is being celebrated as the 76th Independence Day. Hence, in totality, the correct way to say is that India is celebrating the 75th anniversary of its independence but it’s still the 76th Independence Day.
