Which country defeat Thailand by 66 runs in Women’s T-20 Asia Cup Cricket?

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Ireland

(D) South Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thailand Women won the toss and elected to field.

Concept note-2: -Indian women’s team beat Pakistan consecutively for the second time to lift the Women’s Twenty 20 Asia Cup 2016. It was their sixth title in as many editions with a 17-run win over Pakistan in Bangkok. It was Mithali Raj who set up the win, scoring an unbeaten 73 after India opted to bat and taking them to 121 for 5.

Concept note-3: -Matches were played at the Asian Institute of Technology Ground and the Terdthai Cricket Ground (both located in Bangkok). India were undefeated during the tournament, and defeated Pakistan by 17 runs in the final.
