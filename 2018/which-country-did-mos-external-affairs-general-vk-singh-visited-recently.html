Which country did MoS External Affairs General VK Singh visited recently?

(A) ** North Korea

(B) South Africa

(C) Indonesia

(D) Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He is the current Minister of State in the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways and Ministry of Civil Aviation in the Second Modi ministry.

Concept note-2: -General Bikram Singh, PVSM, UYSM, AVSM, SM, VSM, ADC (born 19 July 1952) is a retired Indian army officer who served as the 24th Chief of Army Staff (COAS) of the Indian Army. Previously the General Officer Commanding-in-Chief of the army’s Eastern Command, he succeeded General V. K. Singh as COAS on May 31, 2012.

Concept note-3: -General (Dr.) Vijay Kumar Singh (Retd.), is currently the Hon’ble Union Minister of State for Civil Aviation, and also for Road Transport and Highways. General Singh was previously the Union Minister of State for External Affairs.

Concept note-4: -Vishwanath Pratap Singh (25 June 1931 – 27 November 2008), shortened to V. P. Singh, was an Indian politician who was the 7th Prime Minister of India from 1989 to 1990 and the 41st Raja Bahadur of Manda. He is India’s only prime minister to have been former royalty.
