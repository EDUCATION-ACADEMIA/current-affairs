(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Saudi Arabia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Defence Planning Committee is a senior decision-making organisation created on 19 April 2018 by the Government of India.

Concept note-2: -It is a constitutional body. 2. The council is chaired by the Prime-Minister. 3.

Concept note-3: -National Security Council (NSC). The CDS is the highest military representative, who commands and coordinates the activities of three services through the Vice CDS and the three service chiefs.

Concept note-4: -The defence planning process attempts to match the budgetary resources likely to be made available for the requirement to establish the defence capability necessary to face the threats and challenges.

Concept note-5: -Spending public resources in a planned, responsible and strategic manner allows for an effective and efficient defence capability. Planning also allows for an optimal resource-distribution, essential for the democratic and economic development of a country.
