Which country has most Outward Migrants In 2017: ADB Report

(A) ** India

(B) Saudi Arabia

(C) Indonesia

(D) Albania

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2017, India had the most outmigrants with 17 million, followed by the People’s Republic of China (PRC) with 10 million.

Concept note-2: -Top 10 Countries with the Highest Number of Emigrants (Former Residents living Internationally)-United Nations 2020: India-17.9 million. Mexico-11.1 million. Russia-10.8 million.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Maharashtra. Maharashtra with 2.3 Million (23 lakh) net in-migrants receives the maximum number of immigrants followed by Delhi, Gujarat, and Haryana.

Concept note-4: -Mexico is the top origin country of the U.S. immigrant population.

Concept note-5: -Based on statistical evidence, Guyana was the recipient of 238, 909 East Indian immigrants up to the termination of the system in 1917; Trinidad 143, 939; Jamaica 36, 412; Grenada 3, 033; St. Vincent 2, 472; St. Lucia 4, 354; and St. Kitts 337.
