Which country is the current chairperson of Bay of Bengal Initiative for Multi-Sectoral Technical and Economic Cooperation (BIMSTEC)?

(A) India

(B) Pakistan

(C) ** Nepal

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which country was the host of the BIMSTEC summit in 2022? Ans. In 2022, Sri Lanka served as the host country for the Bay of Bengal Initiative for Multi-Sectoral Technical and Economic Cooperation (BIMSTEC) Summit.

Concept note-2: -Having assumed the chairmanship of the Bay of Bengal Initiative for Multi-Sectoral Technical and Economic Cooperation (Bimstec) this year, Thailand has pledged to make Bimstec a more tangible grouping than ever before.

Concept note-3: -Taking to Twitter, the official spokesperson of the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Arindam Bagchi said, “A warm welcome to BIMSTEC Secretary General Tenzin Lekphell on his arrival in New Delhi today.”

Concept note-4: -7 countries are members of Bay of Bengal Initiative for Multi-Sectoral Technical and Economic Cooperation (BIMSTEC). The member countries are India, Bhutan, Bangladesh, Myanmar, Thailand, Nepal, Sri Lanka.
