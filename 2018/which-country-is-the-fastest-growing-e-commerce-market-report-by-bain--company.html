Which country is the fastest growing e-commerce market: Report by Bain & Company?

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Saudi Arabia

(D) Indonesia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple.com was the fastest growing e-commerce website in 2022. The global domain of the big tech company saw its average annual traffic grow by over 262 percent, outdoing even the fashion marketplace shein.com at about 75 percent.

Concept note-2: -Presumably in 2022, China will generate the largest ecommerce revenue. It will be equal to $1.5 trillion.

Concept note-3: -China will remain the world’s most penetrated ecommerce market in 2022, with 46.3% of the country’s retail sales taking place online. Rounding out the top three are the UK and South Korea, where ecommerce orders will make up 36.3% and 32.2% of retail purchases, respectively.
