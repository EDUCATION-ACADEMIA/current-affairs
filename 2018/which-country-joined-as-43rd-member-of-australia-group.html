Which country joined as 43rd Member of Australia Group?

(A) ** India

(B) Indonesia

(C) Albania

(D) South Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India became the Australia Group’s 43rd member on Jan. 19, following a consensus decision at the group’s June 19 plenary. The Australia Group is dedicated to preventing the proliferation of biological and chemical weapons through voluntary export controls.

Concept note-2: -On 19 January, India became the 43rd member of the Australia Group.

Concept note-3: -With the incorporation of India on January 19, 2018, it now has 43 members, including Australia, New Zealand, the European Commission, all 27 member states of the European Union, United Kingdom, the United States, Canada, India, Ukraine, and Argentina. The name comes from Australia’s initiative to create the group.
