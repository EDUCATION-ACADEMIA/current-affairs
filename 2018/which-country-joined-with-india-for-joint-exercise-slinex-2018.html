Which country joined with India for Joint Exercise SLINEX-2018?

(A) ** Sri Lanka

(B) Pakistan

(C) Indonesia

(D) Saudi Arabia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SLINEX (Sri Lanka India Naval Exercise) are a series of naval exercises between the Indian Navy and the Sri Lanka Navy.

Concept note-2: -SLINEX (Sri Lanka–India Naval Exercise) is being conducted in two phases.

Concept note-3: -The Sri Lanka Navy will be represented by SLNS Sayurala, an advanced offshore patrol vessel and the Indian Navy by INS Kirch, a guided missile corvette.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Navy is hosting the 29th edition of the Singapore-India Maritime Bilateral Exercise (SIMBEX) from 26 to 30 October, 2022 at Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-5: -21st EDITION OF INDIA FRANCE BILATERAL NAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the Western Seaboard today, 16 Jan 23.
