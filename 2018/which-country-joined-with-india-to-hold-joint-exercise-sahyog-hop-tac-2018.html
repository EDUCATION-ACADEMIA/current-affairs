Which country joined with India to Hold Joint Exercise ’Sahyog HOP TAC-2018’?

(A) ** Vietnam

(B) Pakistan

(C) Denmark

(D) New york

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The maiden joint exercise of the Coast Guards of India and Vietnam “Sahyog HOP TAC-2018” was held in the Bay of Bengal, off the Chennai coast, Tamil Nadu. It was aimed at strengthening the working level relationship between the coast guards of both navies.

Concept note-2: -What is Sahyog Kaijin? Under an agreement between both countries in 2006, Sahyog Kaijin is the significant military exchange between Japan and India.

Concept note-3: -The exercise, Sahyog Kaijin, is between the Indian and Japanese Coast Guard as agreed in 2006. This activity proceeds with personnel of both sides coming together on one of the vessels of either nation and further practising the exercise together.
