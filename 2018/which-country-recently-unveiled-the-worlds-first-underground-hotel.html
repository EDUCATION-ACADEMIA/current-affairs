Which country recently unveiled the world’s first underground hotel?

(A) Japan

(B) Kuwait

(C) Singapore

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One of the last examples of these is the world first underground luxury hotel in Shanghai. We are familiar with “luxury skyscraper hotels", but this is something unique: Groundscraper Underground Luxury Hotel in China!

Concept note-2: -Grand Canyon Caverns / Arizona The Grand Canyon Caverns Suite is famously billed as “the world’s largest, deepest, darkest motel room, ” the underground hotel room is almost 220 feet below ground, and surrounded by history.

Concept note-3: -Wonderland is the first hotel in the world to be built underground, though it’s not a subterranean dungeon as you might expect. It’s built into the cliffs of a formerly abandoned quarry about 30 miles from Shanghai’s city center, descending nearly 290 feet down to a lake at the bottom of the pit.

Concept note-4: -The Wonderland Hotel was a hotel built in 1911 at Elkmont, Sevier County, Tennessee by the Wonderland Club Company and was dismantled and partially preserved in 2005 by the National Park Service. It burned down in early 2016. U.S.
