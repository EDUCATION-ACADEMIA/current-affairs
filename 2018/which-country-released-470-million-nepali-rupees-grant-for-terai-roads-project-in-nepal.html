Which country released 470 Million Nepali Rupees Grant For Terai Roads Project In Nepal?

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Saudi Arabia

(D) Indonesia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Government of India has released a grant of 470 million Nepalese rupees for the Terai road project in Nepal.

Concept note-2: -Nepal’s first paved road was built with aid from India in the early 1950s. It connected Kathmandu with Raxaul on the Indian border. As of 1997, additional roads were being built, primarily with the cooperation of India but also the United States, including an East-West Highway through southern portions of the country.

Concept note-3: -The new Karnali road will link Khulalu, Simikot and Hilsa and will be 286km long. The first phase of the Kaligandaki highway will link Gaindakot, Mirmi, Balubang and Phalebas in Parbat and will be 283km long, while its second phase will link Kusma, Phalebas, Setibeni and Mirmi and will be 98km long.
