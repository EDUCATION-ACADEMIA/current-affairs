Which country To Host 1st International Solar Alliance In March 2018?

(A) Pakistan

(B) Indonesia

(C) Iran

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is New Delhi. The first summit of the International Solar Alliance(ISA) was held in New Delhi. It was held on 11 March 2018 in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Report of the First Assembly of the International Solar Alliance (ISA) held on 3rd October, 2018 at Greater Noida, India.

Concept note-3: -India and France launched the International Solar Alliance (ISA) to boost solar energy in developing countries. It was launched at the United Nations Climate Change Conference in Paris in November 2015 by the Indian Prime Minister and French President. Its secretariat is located in Gurugram, India.

Concept note-4: -Meanwhile, Denmark becomes the first country to ratify International Solar Alliance Framework Agreement (ISA FA) after amendments to it entered into force on January 8, 2021. ISA is an alliance of over 120 countries initiated by India.
