Which country Unveils ICC-Approved T20 League?

(A) ** Australia

(B) Canada

(C) Denmark

(D) Finland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -T20 Blast is a professional Twenty20 cricket league held in England and Wales for English and Welsh first-class counties. The competition was launched by the England and Wales Cricket Board in 2003 as the first professional Twenty20 league in the world.

Concept note-2: -IPL can easily be rated as the #1 T20 franchise cricket league in the world. IPL has the most # of teams participating as well as the most number of editions (15 in total) that have been organized so far.

Concept note-3: -SA 20 is a Twenty20 franchise cricket tournament to be played in South Africa in early 2023. The first edition is scheduled to feature six teams based in South African cities. In August 2022, the league was formally named the SA 20. Graeme Smith is the commisioner of this championship.

Concept note-4: -Indian Premier League (IPL), Indian professional Twenty20 (T20) cricket league established in 2008. The league, which is based on a round-robin group and knockout format, has teams in major Indian cities.
