Which country withdraws from United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO)

(A) ** Israel

(B) Iran

(C) Pakistan

(D) South Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -United States of America and Israel have withdrawn from UNESCO, accusing the cultural body of ‘anti-Israel bias’. The full form of UNESCO is the United Nations Educational, Scientific, and Cultural Organization. Its headquarters is in Paris, France. It was established in 1945.

Concept note-2: -The United States and Israel left the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) last December. The two countries opposed several anti-Israel resolutions passed by the UN-organization in previous years.

Concept note-3: -The US and Israel had quit the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organisation (UNESCO) with the close of 2018.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is option 3 i.e United States of America and Israel both.

Concept note-5: -The United States joined UNESCO at its founding but later withdrew in 1984 because of a growing disparity between U.S. foreign policy and UNESCO goals. After an almost twenty-year absence from the organization, the United States rejoined the organization in October 2003.
