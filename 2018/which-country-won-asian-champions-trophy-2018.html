Which country Won Asian Champions Trophy 2018?

(A) ** South Africa

(B) Pakistan

(C) Indonesia

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Wow. What a game of cricket and what a performance from Pakistan! A brilliant turnaround from the shellacking they received in their opening fixture against India, they’ve completely turned things round to beat their fiercest rival comprehensively and win their first ever ICC Champions Trophy.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is India. India women’s hockey team won the Asian Champions Trophy after beating China 2-1 in the final.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is India. The 2016 Asian Hockey Champions Trophy tournament held in Kuantan, Malaysia. India stormed into the final of the fourth Asian Champions Trophy Hockey tournament, beating South Korea.
