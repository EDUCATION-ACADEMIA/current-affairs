Which country won “Hockey Champions Trophy 2018” title?

(A) Argentina

(B) ** Australia

(C) India

(D) Netherlands

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Australia won their 15th title by defeating India in the final after penalties.

Concept note-2: -The 2018 Men’s Hockey World Cup was the 14th edition of the Hockey World Cup, the quadrennial world championship for men’s national field hockey teams organized by the FIH. It was held from 28 November to 16 December 2018, at the Kalinga Stadium in Bhubaneswar, India.

Concept note-3: -Germany are the reigning men’s FIH World Cup champions, having clinched the 2023 edition held in Odisha, India.

Concept note-4: -Pakistan won the first FIH Hockey World Cup, beating hosts Spain 1-0 in the final.
