Which country won the U-16 West Asian Football Federation Cup in Amman, Jordan?

(A) ** India

(B) Pakistan

(C) Indonesia

(D) Saudi Arabia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The winner of Asia Cup 2022 title is Sri Lanka. Q5.

Concept note-2: -The AFC Asian Cup is an association football competition established in 1956. It is contested by the men’s national teams of the members of the Asian Football Confederation (AFC), the sport’s Asian governing body, and takes place generally every four years.

Concept note-3: -South Asian Football Federation (SAFF), founded in 1997, is an association of the football playing nations in South Asia and is part of the larger Asian Football Confederation. The members of the association are India, Bangladesh, Bhutan, Maldives, Nepal, Pakistan and Sri Lanka.

Concept note-4: -The South Asian Football Federation (SAFF) is the unity of South Asian football-playing countries.
