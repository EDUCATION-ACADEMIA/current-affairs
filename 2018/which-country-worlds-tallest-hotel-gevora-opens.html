Which country World’s Tallest Hotel ’Gevora’ Opens?

(A) ** Dubai

(B) North America

(C) South Africa

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are many attractions to discover within close proximity to Gevora Hotel - The Tallest Hotel in the World. Pose in front of The Dubai Fountain located 1.9 km away, and you can always show your friends the time you went to Dubai.

Concept note-2: -The world’s tallest hotel, Gevora hotel Dubai is known for its unparalleled views, amazing dining options and wellness facilities. Admire city skyline and Burj Khalifa views from the pool and rooftop restaurant.

Concept note-3: -Gevora Hotel - The Tallest Hotel in the World is located in World Trade Centre DIFC area, which is famous for attractions such as Emirates Towers, Art Space and Ficus Gardens.

Concept note-4: -The Gevora Hotel consists of a main hotel building and a parking garage building. The main building has an architectural height of 1,169.1 ft (356.33 m), making it the tallest hotel building according to Guinness World Records.
