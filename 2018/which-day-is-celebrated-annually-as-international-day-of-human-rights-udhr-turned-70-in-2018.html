Which day is celebrated annually as International Day of Human Rights; UDHR turned 70 in 2018?

(A) ** December 10

(B) November 6th

(C) March 7th

(D) April 9th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Human Rights Day is observed every year on 10 December – the day the United Nations General Assembly adopted, in 1948, the Universal Declaration of Human Rights.

Concept note-2: -December 10, 2021 marks the 73rd anniversary of the UN Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR). This year also marks the 5th anniversary of the UN Declaration on the Right to Peace (DRP).

Concept note-3: -The 2017 theme is ‘Let’s stand up for equality, justice and human dignity’.

Concept note-4: -The 2022 Theme of Human Rights Day is Dignity, Freedom, and Justice for All.

Concept note-5: -The Universal Declaration of Human Rights articulates fundamental rights and freedoms for all. The General Assembly of the United Nations adopted the Declaration on 10 December 1948.
