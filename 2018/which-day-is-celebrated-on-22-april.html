Which day is celebrated on 22 April?

(A) World Heart Day

(B) ** World Earth Day

(C) World AIDS Day

(D) World Diabetes Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Earth Day is an annual celebration that honors the achievements of the environmental movement and raises awareness of the need to protect Earths natural resources for future generations. The first Earth Day was held April 22, 1970.

Concept note-2: -World Book Day is also celebrated in India on 23rd April every year. It is commemorated in many parts of India to encourage & aware of people for reading & publishing books.

Concept note-3: -The April 22 date was selected in part to maximize the number of students that could be reached on university campuses as this date it fell between colleges’ spring break and final exams.

Concept note-4: -The first Earth Day – April 22, 1970 – is sometimes said to have marked the beginning of the modern environmental movement.

Concept note-5: -Earth Day 2022 Date Earth day, also known as International Mother Earth day, is celebrated each year on April 22. The main aim is to raise awareness about the growing concerns of overpopulation, loss of biodiversity, and depleting quality of the environment. This year Earth day will happen on Fri, April 22, 2022.
