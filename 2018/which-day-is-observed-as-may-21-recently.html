Which day is observed as May 21 recently?

(A) ** Anti Terrorism Day

(B) World War Orphan Day.

(C) Army day.

(D) World Laughter Day.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Anti-Terrorism Day is observed every year on 21 May to spread awareness about the violence caused by the terrorists and also in the memory of former Indian PM Rajiv Gandhi who passed on this day.

Concept note-2: -National days on Sun May 21st, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s World Meditation Day, International Tea Day, I Need A Patch For That Day, National Memo Day, Rapture Party Day… and much more!

Concept note-3: -21st May: Anti-terrorism day This day commemorates the death anniversary of one of the former Prime Ministers of India, Late Shri Rajiv Gandhi (20 August 1944 – 21 May 1991).

Concept note-4: -Labour Day (1st May) Labour Day is one of the most important days in May and it is celebrated across the world. Antarrashtriya Shramik Diwas or Kamgar Din is the name given to Labour Day in India.

Concept note-5: -On 21st March, World Forestry Day or International Day of Forests is celebrated every year to raise public awareness about the values, significance, and contributions of the forests to balance the life cycle on the earth.
