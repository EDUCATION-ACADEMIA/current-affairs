Which day is observed on 12 February?

(A) ** National Productivity Day

(B) Sanremo Music festival

(C) Agni Utsav

(D) Chinese lantern Festival

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -12 February is celebrated every year as Darwin Day to commemorate the birth anniversary of the father of evolutionary biology, Charles Darwin in 1809. This day highlights Darwin’s contribution to evolutionary and plant science.

Concept note-2: -February 12 is celebrated as hug day under Valentine Week 2023 Schedule.

Concept note-3: -Valentine’s week is celebrated from 07 February to 14 February. Valentine’s Day is celebrated on 14 February every year. If you’re in a relationship or want to be, you’ll understand what we’re talking about.

Concept note-4: -The seven days of Valentine’s Week are the days to express love in different ways and make your partner feel special. It starts from 7 February, which is celebrated till 14 February.
