Which day is observed on october 9, 2018?

(A) ** World Ozone Day

(B) World Fathers Day

(C) World Post Day

(D) World Post Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Post Day is celebrated each year on 9 October, the anniversary of the establishment of the Universal Postal Union in 1874 in the Swiss Capital, Bern. It was declared World Post Day by the UPU Congress held in Tokyo, Japan in 1969.

Concept note-2: -October 9 was chosen because it is the anniversary of the day that the ship Restauration arrived in New York from Stavanger, Norway on October 9, 1825. This was the start of organized immigration from Scandinavia to the USA.

Concept note-3: -World Postal Day is celebrated on 9 October every year to raise awareness among people about the role of the postal sector for people and businesses every day.

Concept note-4: -National Nanotechnology Day-October 9, 2022 (On or around October 9) National Pro-Life Cupcake Day. National Sneakers Day.
