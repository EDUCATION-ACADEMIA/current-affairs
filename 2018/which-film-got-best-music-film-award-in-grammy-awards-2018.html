Which film got Best Music Film award in Grammy Awards 2018?

(A) ** The Defiant Ones

(B) The Baby Driver

(C) All Eyes on Me

(D) Patti cakes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Allen Hughes ’ “The Defiant Ones” Wins Best Music Film | 2018 GRAMMY. The team behind The Defiant Ones celebrated a big win for Best Music Film at the 60th GRAMMY Awards.

Concept note-2: -Bruno Mars won Album Of The Year for 24K Magic at the 60th GRAMMY Awards.

Concept note-3: -It’s official: Beyoncé has made GRAMMY history. Her win for Best Dance/Electronic Album for ‘RENAISSANCE’ put her GRAMMY total at 32, the most of all time. After winning the award for Best Dance/Electronic Music Album, Beyoncé now holds the all-time record for most GRAMMY wins.

Concept note-4: -Jon Batiste won Best Music Video for “Freedom, ” off his album We Are, at the 2022 GRAMMYs.
