Which Indian actor was honoured with special Icon Award at the London Indian Film Festival?

(A) Amitabh Bachchan

(B) ** Irrfan Khan

(C) Ranbir Kapoor

(D) Akshay Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ICON Award for Outstanding Contribution to Indian and World Cinema was presented to Indian actor Manoj Bajpayee.

Concept note-2: -Mega Star of Tollywood, Padma Bhushan awardee Shree Konidela Siva Sankara Vara Prasad popularly known as Chiranjeevi has been presented with Indian Film Personality of the Year Award for 2022, at the closing ceremony of 53rd edition of International Film Festival of India (IFFI) in Goa today.

Concept note-3: -IFFI 2022 full list of winners: I have Electric Dreams wins Best Film, Chiranjeevi receives award.

Concept note-4: -Megastar Chiranjeevi receives Indian Film Personality of the Year Award for 2022 at IFFI | Hindi Movie News - Bollywood - Times of India.
