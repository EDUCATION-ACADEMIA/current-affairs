Which Institue from Pune launched an e-library of ancient religious and historical works?

(A) SMEF’S Brick Group of Research Institutes

(B) Arihant Institute of Research Studies (AIRS)

(C) ** Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute (BORI)

(D) Suryadatta Group of Research Institutes (SGRI)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute (BORI), which houses one of South Asia’s largest and most invaluable agglomeration of rare manuscripts, opened its treasure vault digitally by launching an e-library of ancient religious and historical works on Wednesday.

Concept note-2: -Founded in 1917 by Ramakrishna Gopal Bhandarkar, this is a pioneering research institute in the field of Orientology and also houses a large collection of manuscripts and rare books.

Concept note-3: -The Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute is working for the last 100 years in Pune.
