Which institute develops aircraft that can fold wings mid-flight?

(A) ** NASA

(B) SPIC

(C) RBI

(D) ISIR

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NASA has successfully applied a new technology in flight that allows aircraft to fold their wings to different angles while in the air. The recent flight series, which took place at NASA’s Armstrong Flight Research Center in California, was part of the Spanwise Adaptive Wing project, or SAW.

Concept note-2: -All aircraft wings take forces that would cause bending; this is the physics of flight as the wings support the fuselage’s weight. But with the 787, the wing structure bends more under this force.

Concept note-3: -An elliptical planform is the most efficient aerodynamic shape for an untwisted wing, leading to the lowest amount of induced drag.
