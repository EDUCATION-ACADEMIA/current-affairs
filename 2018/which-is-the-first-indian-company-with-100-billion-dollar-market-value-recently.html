(A) Infosys

(B) Wipro

(C) ** Tata consultancy Services

(D) Cognizant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliance Becomes First Indian Company To Hit $100 Billion Revenue In A Year.

Concept note-2: -On Friday, Mukesh Ambani-led Reliance Industries Limited became the first Indian company to surpass a gross revenue of over $100 billion or about Rs 792, 756 crores in a year.

Concept note-3: -This week India just witnessed bootstrapped startup Zoho cross the $1Bn revenue mark and become India’s first billion dollar product company!

Concept note-4: -A tech, financial, or fintech companies worth more than $ 100 billion is called Hectocorn. Or there is another name for the company, this corporation is “Super Unicorn".

Concept note-5: -Gautam Adani, the chairman of Adani Group The centibillionaires club has a new member: Gautam Adani. The Indian tycoon, whose empire includes ports, mines and green energy, is the latest whose fortune reached the $100 billion mark.
