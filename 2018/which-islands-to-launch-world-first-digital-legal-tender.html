Which islands to launch world-first digital legal tender?

(A) ** Marshall Islands

(B) Barrel Island

(C) Neil Island

(D) Munroe Island

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jamaica Launches “Jam-Dex”, Becomes First Nation To Legalise Digital Currency.

Concept note-2: -The Republic of the Marshall Islands (RMI) will become the first sovereign nation to issue a cryptocurrency that will be legal tender. The new currency is called Sovereign with the symbol SOV. The SOV will be distributed to the public via an Initial Currency Offering (ICO).

Concept note-3: -In September last year, El Salvador became the first country in the world to introduce Bitcoin as legal tender. According to a government statement, President Faustin Archange Touadera validated the law Wednesday.

Concept note-4: -At present, El Salvador and the Central African Republic (CAR) are the only two countries in the world where Bitcoin functions as a legal currency.
