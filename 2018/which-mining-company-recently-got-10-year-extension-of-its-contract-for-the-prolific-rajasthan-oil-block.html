Which mining company recently got 10-year extension of its contract for the prolific Rajasthan oil block?

(A) Essar Steel Ltd

(B) ** Vedanta Ltd.

(C) TATA Steel Ltd

(D) Arch Steel Ltd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Billionaire Anil Agarwal’s Vedanta Ltd has got a 10-year extension of the licence for its prolific Rajasthan oil block till May 14, 2030, the firm said in a stock exchange filing on Thursday.

Concept note-2: -Vedanta Ltd on February 21 announced it has made an oil discovery in its exploratory well in Rajasthan’s Barmer district.

Concept note-3: -Assam is the highest natural gas-producing state with a production of 8.3 MMSMCD.

Concept note-4: -The Mangala Area sits in Barmer Basin and is thought to contain oil in place volumes of 3.6 billion barrels (570, 000, 000 m3), of which 1 billion barrels (160, 000, 000 m3) are thought to be recoverable. Cairn India is the operator of the field, a subsidiary of Vedanta Group.
