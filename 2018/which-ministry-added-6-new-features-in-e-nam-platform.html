Which ministry added 6 New Features In E-NAM Platform?

(A) Ministry of Sports Affair

(B) ** Ministry of Agriculture

(C) Ministry of Telecommunication

(D) Ministry of Finance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The e-NAM electronic trading platform has been created with an investment by the Government of India (through the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers’ Welfare).

Concept note-2: -The new features of the e-NAM platform allow the remote bidding and mobile-based payment for traders which will strengthen the agriculture marketing. The added features of the e-NAM platform promote the inter-State trade by providing online transport facilities for distant buyers.

Concept note-3: -National Agriculture Market (NAM) is a pan-India electronic trading portal launched on 14th April, 2016 completely funded by Central Government and implemented by Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium (SFAC).

Concept note-4: -Since 31st March 2018, 415 new mandis have been integrated on National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) platform. As on 31st December 2021, 1000 mandis of 18 States and 03 Union Territories have been integrated with e-NAM platform.
