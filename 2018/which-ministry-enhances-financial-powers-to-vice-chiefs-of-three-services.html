Which ministry enhances financial powers to Vice Chiefs of three Services?

(A) ** Defence Ministry

(B) Finance Ministry

(C) External Affairs Ministry

(D) Internal Affairs Ministry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The defence ministry said the delegated financial powers of vice chiefs of the services have been increased by 10 percent subject to an overall ceiling of Rs 500 crore.

Concept note-2: -A new book titled ‘India’s Most Fearless 3: New Military Stories of Unimaginable Courage and Sacrifice’ was launched by the three service chiefs-Air Chief Marshal VR Chaudhari, Admiral R Hari Kumar, and General Manoj Pande-at an event in New Delhi on January 17, 2023.

Concept note-3: -The CDS has a responsibility for coordination among the three services in equipment and arms procurement, training and staffing. The CDS acts the principal military advisor to the Defence Minister on all matters related to the tri-services and military advisor to the Nuclear Command Authority.

Concept note-4: -The President of India is the supreme commander of all the three divisions. He or she exercises control over all the three divisions. Q. is the supreme commander of the defence forces in India.
