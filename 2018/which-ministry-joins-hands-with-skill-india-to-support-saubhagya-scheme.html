Which ministry Joins Hands With Skill India To Support Saubhagya Scheme?

(A) Ministry of Defence

(B) Ministry of Telecommunications

(C) Ministry of External Affairs

(D) ** Ministry of Power

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Power would be the implementing authority of the scheme. Power connection will be provided in both rural and urban areas of the country.

Concept note-2: -The Hon’ble Prime Minister launched Saubhagya scheme on 25th September 2017 to achieve universal household electrification covering every village and every district in the country.

Concept note-3: -Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship.

Concept note-4: -Skill Hubs: Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship in consultation with Ministry of Education (MoE) has planned to implement the ‘Skill Hub Initiative’ under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana 3.0 (PMKVY 3.0).
