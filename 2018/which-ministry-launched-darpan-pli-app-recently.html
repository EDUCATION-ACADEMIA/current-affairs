Which ministry launched DARPAN-PLI app recently?

(A) ** Ministry of Communication

(B) Ministry of External Affairs

(C) Ministry of Finance

(D) Ministry of Defence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union Ministry of Communications has launched DARPAN-PLI application for seamless collection of premium for Postal Life Insurance (PLI) and Rural Postal Life Insurance (RPLI) policies.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. On 21 December 2017, Digital Advancement of Rural Post-Office for a New India (DARPAN) was launched by the Ministry of Communications.

Concept note-3: -The Minister of Communications Shri Manoj Sinha today launched DARPAN – “Digital Advancement of Rural Post Office for A New India” Project to improve the quality of service, add value to services and achieve “financial inclusion” of the unbanked rural population.
