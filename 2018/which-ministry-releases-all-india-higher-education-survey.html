Which ministry releases All India Higher Education Survey?

(A) Ministry of Telecommunication

(B) Ministry of Sport affairs

(C) Ministry of External Affairs

(D) ** Ministry of Human resource department

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Recently, All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE) 2020-2021 was released by the Ministry of Education, Government of India.

Concept note-2: -To portray the status of higher education in the country, the Ministry of Education has endeavored to conduct an annual web-based AISHE since 2010-11.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of Education, Government of India has released All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE) 2020-2021.

Concept note-4: -All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE) was initiated in 2011 during which data for the year 2010-11 was collected.

Concept note-5: -Thus, UGC regulates Higher Education. The MHRD, is responsible for the overall development of the basic infrastructure of the Higher Education sector, both in terms of policy and planning.
