Which mobile network launched VOIP Based WINGS Service?

(A) ** Idea

(B) Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL)

(C) Vodafone

(D) Bharti Airtel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BSNL has launched the VOIP based telecom service, WINGS, in August 2018.

Concept note-2: -BSNL Wings is an Internet Telephony (VOIP) service that allows users to make and receive calls from anywhere across the country as well as abroad. Users can call directly to a mobile number from their contacts list using the Wings app.

Concept note-3: -Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP), is a technology that allows you to make voice calls using a broadband Internet connection instead of a regular (or analog) phone line.

Concept note-4: -Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) is planning to include WhatsApp to its voice over internet protocol (VoIP) app, Wings. According to BSNL, short message service (SMS) will soon be integrated and WhatsApp configured to the Wings app.

Concept note-5: -FreJun is the #1 VoIP service provider in India and secures a top spot in this list. It comes with a vast bundle of smart features such as call scheduling, Autodialer, call recording, and click-to-call, to name a few.
