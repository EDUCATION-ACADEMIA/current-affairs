Which Money Receives Sebi Nod As Registered Investment Adviser?

(A) ** Paytm Money

(B) Ola Money

(C) Make Money

(D) Amazon Seller.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Investment Advisor salary in India with less than 1 year of experience to 10 years ranges from ₹ 1.7 Lakhs to ₹ 16.2 Lakhs with an average annual salary of ₹ 3.7 Lakhs based on 542 latest salaries.

Concept note-2: -Only SEBI Registered Advisors are allowed to offer financial advice to clients. The advantage is that the are client-centric, conflict-free, unbiased when they engage with their clients. Through the decades, investors had no credible source to turn to for unbiased advice.

Concept note-3: -Applicants are required to submit the net worth certificate by a chartered accountant and it shall include the membership number of the chartered accountant in the certificate. The Networth certificate should not be more than 6 months old at the time of filing of application.

Concept note-4: -Finzoom Investment Advisors Private Limited is the holding Company, registered with Securities Exchange Board of India as an investment advisor under SEBI (Investment Advisors) Regulations, 2013 vide registration no.
