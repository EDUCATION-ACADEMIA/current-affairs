Which month Tamil Nadu: Unique Nilgiri Mountain Railway to run special train services?

(A) ** June

(B) May

(C) April

(D) March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A summer special service runs in April and May, starting from Mettupalayam at 09:30 am and from Udhagamandalam at 12:15 pm. Between Coonoor and Udagamandalam, four daily trains run each way.

Concept note-2: -The Nilgiri Mountain Railway is a meter-gauge single-track railway in Tamil Nadu State, 46km long. Its construction was first proposed in 1854, but due to the difficulty of the mountainous location, the work only started in 1891 being completed in 1908.

Concept note-3: -Bookings for extended trips of above special trains on special charges will open on 05.01. 2023 at all computerised reservation centres and on website http://www.irctc.co.in.

Concept note-4: -Nilgiri Mountain Railway resumed operations w. e. f. 6th September 2021. The train No. 06136, left Mettupalayam Junction at 07.10 am on Monday with 105 passengers.
