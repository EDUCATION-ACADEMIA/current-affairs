Which of the airlines did the AAI serve a notice asking it to pay Rs.20 crore by 30th November to partially clear its dues?

(A) IndiGo

(B) Jet airways

(C) ** SpiceJet

(D) Frontier

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Rajasthan. Airports Authority of India (AAI) successfully conducted a flight trial using GAGAN (GPS Aided GEO Augmented Navigation) based LPV Approach Procedures at Kishangarh Airport, Rajasthan. India is the first country in Asia Pacific Region to achieve such a landmark.

Concept note-2: -Sanjeev Kumar, a Maharashtra-cadre IAS officer of the 1993 batch, took over as the chairman of the Airports Authority of India (AAI) on 7 April 2021.

Concept note-3: -AAI has been constituted under an Act of Parliament, namely, the Airports Authority of India Act, 1994 and has been constituted as a Statutory Body.

Concept note-4: -R K Srivastava, IAS-Chairman. M. Sathiyavathy, Director General of Civil Aviation-Ex-Officio Member.
