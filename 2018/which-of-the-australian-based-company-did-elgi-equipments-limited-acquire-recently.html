Which of the Australian based company did Elgi Equipments Limited acquire recently?

(A) Harvey Norman

(B) Macquarie Group

(C) Orbital Corporation

(D) ** Pulford Air and Gas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Air-compressor maker Elgi Equipments today announced that it has acquired a 100 per cent shareholding of Sydney-headquartered F R Pulford and Son Pty Ltd for 11.04 million (Australian Dollars), about Rs 56 crore, in an “all cash deal.”

Concept note-2: -Today, Elgi Equipments Limited is a global air compressor manufacturer with a broad line of innovative and technologically superior compressed air systems. For nearly 30 years, Dr. Varadaraj has led and guided the organisation’s business interests and serves as the Managing Director of Elgi Equipments Ltd.

Concept note-3: -Dr. Varadaraj is a third-generation entrepreneur from the illustrious business family of LRG Naidu. For nearly 30 years, Dr. Varadaraj has led and guided the organisation’s business interests in textiles, polymers, mechanical machines and consumer appliances.

Concept note-4: -Elgi Equipments Limited (EEL) is engaged in manufacture trading of air compressors and providing after sales services. The Company has manufacturing plants in different locations in India.
