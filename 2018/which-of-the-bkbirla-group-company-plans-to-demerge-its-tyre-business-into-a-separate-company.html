Which of the B.K.Birla group company plans to demerge its tyre business into a separate company?

(A) Mangalam Cement Ltd.

(B) Century Textile and Industries

(C) Ultratech Cement

(D) ** Kesoram Industries Ltd.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Synopsis. The B K Birla group company decided to demerge its tyre vertical into a separate entity-Birla Tyres Ltd-to create and unlock value for both the businesses.

Concept note-2: -The proposed demerger scheme will require sanction from NCLT-Kolkata and other authorities. The demerger will be in the ratio of 1:1; that is, for each share of Kesoram held, shareholders will get one fully paid-up equity share of ₹10 each of Birla Tyres Ltd.

Concept note-3: -Over time, the group’s main companies were Kesoram Industries, Century Textiles and Industries, Century Enka, Mangalam Cement, and ECE Industries. His close team of executives cite multiple examples of his business acumen.

Concept note-4: -JK Tyre completes acquisition of BK Birla’s tyre business at ₹2, 200 cr.

Concept note-5: -History. Birla Tyres was first established in 1991, as part of Kesoram Industries Limited.
