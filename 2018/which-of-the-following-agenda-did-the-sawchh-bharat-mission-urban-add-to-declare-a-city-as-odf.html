Which of the following agenda did the Sawchh Bharat Mission (Urban) add to declare a city as ODF+?

(A) It must be free of open defecation

(B) ** It must be free of public urination

(C) It must be free of e-waste

(D) It must be free of debt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under the mission, all villages, Gram Panchayats, Districts, States and Union Territories in India declared themselves “open-defecation free” (ODF) by 2 October 2019, the 150th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi, by constructing over 100 million toilets in rural India.

Concept note-2: -The SBM(G) was launched on 2nd October 2014 to ensure cleanliness in India and make it Open Defecation Free (ODF) in Five Years. It seeks to improve the levels of cleanliness in rural areas through Solid and Liquid Waste Management activities and making Gram Panchayats Open Defecation Free (ODF), clean and sanitised.

Concept note-3: -Swachh Bharat Mission Grameen (SBM-G), the flagship programme of the Government of India, today recorded another milestone – of 101462 villages declaring themselves as ODF (open defecation free) Plus.

Concept note-4: -The Swachh Bharat Abhiyan for Urban Areas will mainly focus on the cleanliness in Urban Areas and will try to eliminate all these problems completely. The Swachh Bharat Abhiyan or the Swachh Bharat Mission for Urban Areas is implemented by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (M/o HUA).

Concept note-5: -The goal under the programme is that all the villages achieve ODF (3/Model) status at the earliest. Declaration of a villages of any of the three stages of ODF Plus will be done by the Gram Panchayat through a resolution passed by the Gram Sabha.
