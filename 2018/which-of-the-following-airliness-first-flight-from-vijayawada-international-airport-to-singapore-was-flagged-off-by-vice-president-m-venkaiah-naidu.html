Which of the following airlines’s first flight from Vijayawada international airport to Singapore was flagged off by Vice-President M. Venkaiah Naidu?

(A) Jet Airways

(B) Spice Jet

(C) Air India

(D) ** IndiGo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NTR Amaravati International Airport (IATA: VGA, ICAO: VOBZ) is a public international airport serving Andhra Pradesh Capital Region. The airport is located at Gannavaram 25 KM from Vijayawada, where National Highway 16 connecting Chennai to Kolkata pass through.

Concept note-2: -With three international airports - Visakhapatnam, Vijayawada (Gannavaram) and Tirupati - Andhra Pradesh is well connected to all major cities in India and to some foreign destinations also.

Concept note-3: -Andhra Pradesh: COVID preventive measures will be taken at Gannavaram airport, say Airport Director M Lakshmikanth Reddy.
