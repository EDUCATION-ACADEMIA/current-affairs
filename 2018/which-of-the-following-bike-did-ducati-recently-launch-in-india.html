Which of the following bike did Ducati recently launch in India?

(A) ** Panigale V4 R

(B) 1299 Panigale

(C) Hyperstrada

(D) Diavel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The new model launches include the Panigale V4 R, Monster SP, Diavel V4, Streetfighter V4 SP2, Multistrada V4 Rally, Scrambler Icon 2G, Scrambler Full Throttle 2G, Scrambler Nightshift 2G and finally, the Streetfighter V4 Lamborghini. Now that, is a tasty, delectable slew of motorcycles right there!

Concept note-2: -The new generation Scrambler mixes iconic identity with even more modern design and components to explode the concept of fast-forward heritage. Through the rich offer of customization, it ties in with the personality of the rider, who can choose from different colors and a wide range of accessories.

Concept note-3: -There are 18 bike models available from Ducati in India. Most expensive Ducati model is. 40.99 Lakh.

Concept note-4: -With sales of 10, 716 units, the Multistrada V4 range was Ducati’s bestseller.

Concept note-5: -Ducati bike price starts from Rs. 9.39 Lakh for Scrambler 800 and goes upto Rs. 69.99 Lakh for Panigale V4.
