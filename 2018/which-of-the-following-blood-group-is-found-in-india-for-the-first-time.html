Which of the following blood group is found in India for the first time?

(A) P(2) phenotype

(B) P1(k) phenotype

(C) P2(k) phenotype

(D) ** P null phenotype

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A 65-year-old man from Gujarat who is a heart patient has been identified with EMM negative blood group, making it India’s first and world’s tenth case of unique blood group which cannot be categorized into the existing groups of ‘A’, ‘B’, ‘O’ or ‘AB’.

Concept note-2: -EMM Negative Blood Group is the first distinct blood type in India. Only 9 persons in the entire globe have the rarest blood type as of today, but a 65-year-old man from Rajkot, Gujarat, has been found to have it.

Concept note-3: -The most common blood group among the Indian population is O+ that is present in approximately 35% of the population.

Concept note-4: -Jka was the first antigen to be discovered by Kidd blood group system, subsequently, two other antigens Jkb and Jk3 were found. Agarwal et al.

Concept note-5: -In molecular history, type A appears to be the ‘oldest’ blood type, in the sense that the mutations that gave rise to types O and B appear to stem from it. Geneticists call this the wild-type or ancestral allele.
