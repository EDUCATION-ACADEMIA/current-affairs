Which of the following company won the Innovation Award that is held at Automechanika, Germany?

(A) ** Manatec

(B) Waysure Technologies

(C) iHorse Technologies

(D) Zeuxine

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The award is a coveted award for manufacturers and suppliers of motorhomes, vans, caravans or accessories. Initiated and supported by the editors of Europe’s leading caravanning countries, the European Innovation Award stands for innovation in an industry dedicated to the quality of leisure of millions of people.

Concept note-2: -The CES Innovation Awards program is an annual competition honoring outstanding design and engineering in consumer technology products. The program recognizes honorees in a multitude of consumer technology product categories and distinguishes the highest rated in each.
