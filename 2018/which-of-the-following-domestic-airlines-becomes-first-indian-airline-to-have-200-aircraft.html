Which of the following domestic airlines becomes first Indian airline to have 200 aircraft?

(A) ** IndiGo

(B) SpiceJet

(C) GoAir

(D) Vistara Airlines

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IndiGo becomes first Indian airline to have 200 planes-Times of India.

Concept note-2: -Facts and Figures. Market share of 55.7% as of November, 2022. Fleet of 300 aircraft including 160 new generation A320 NEOs, 23 A320 CEOs, 39 ATRs and 78 A321 NEO.

Concept note-3: -The airline offers 180 seats on its A320 CEO fleet while its A320 NEO planes are 180 and 186 seaters. The seating capacity on the A321 fleet is 222/232 and ATR fleet offers 78 seats, IndiGo said in a statement. “Our fleet size now stands at 300 aircraft, giving wings to our ambitions.

Concept note-4: -Tata group-owned Air India dethroned another Tata airline Vistara to become the second biggest domestic airline in December 2022, the data shows.
