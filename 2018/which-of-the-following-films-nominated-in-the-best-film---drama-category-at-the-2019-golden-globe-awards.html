Which of the following films nominated in the ’Best Film - Drama’ category at the 2019 Golden Globe Awards?

(A) Mary Poppins Returns

(B) Green Book

(C) Crazy Rich Asians

(D) ** Black Panther

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 1917. In Golden Globe Awards 2019 1917 movie won the award in the category Best Motion Picture - Drama. Sam Mendes’ 1917 won the best picture, drama trophy at the Golden Globes.

Concept note-2: -Universal’s The Fabelmans (DP Janusz Kaminiski) took Best Motion Picture Drama and Searchlight’s The Banshees of Inisherin (DP Ben Davis BSC) won for Musical or Comedy at the 80th annual Golden Globe Awards.

Concept note-3: -The nominees in the Best Television Series (Drama) category include AMC’s ‘Better Call Saul’, Netflix’s ‘The Crown’, Apple TV Plus’s ‘Severance’, Netflix’s ‘Ozark’ and HBO’s ‘House of the Dragon’. ‘Severance’ is being touted as the strongest contender to win in this category.
