Which of the following firm is planning to provide optical fibre network and to open 12, 000 new mobile sites across Tamil Nadu?

(A) Reliance Jio

(B) ** Bharti Airtel

(C) Tata DoCoMo

(D) BSNL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -₹499 Airtel Xstream Fiber Plan The other benefits users get in this pack are free access to the Airtel Xstream app, Wynk Music, and Shaw Academy. In addition, the user can also bundle up this plan with an Xstream DTH box, with one month HD pack.

Concept note-2: -Airtel ‘All-in-One’ Xstream Fiber broadband plans In terms of difference, ₹699 offers 40 Mbps speed and one OTT platform whereas, ₹1099 offers 200 Mbps speed and it comes with Amazon Prime and Disney+ Hotstar. The ₹1599 plan gives you 300 Mbps along with Disney+ Hotstar, Netflix and Amazon Prime access.

Concept note-3: -India’s Optical Fibre Cable Network Expands To Over 35 Lakh Route Kilometre. The government has informed the Parliament that 35.5 lakh kilometers of optical fiber cable (OFC) have been laid across the country as of 30 September 2022.
