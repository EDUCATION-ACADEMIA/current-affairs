Which of the following firm makes history by becoming first US company to reach $1 trillion market value?

(A) ** Apple

(B) Walmart

(C) Amazon

(D) Microsoft

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple became the first publicly traded U.S. company to hit a $1 trillion market cap during intraday trading on Aug.

Concept note-2: -Apple became the world’s first $3 trillion company for a brief time during intraday trading on Jan. 3, 2022. The iPhone maker also holds the distinction of being the world’s first company to reach $1 trillion and $2 trillion.

Concept note-3: -Apple becomes first company to hit $3 trillion market value, then slips.

Concept note-4: -In August 2018, Apple became the first American company ever to be worth $1 trillion, an achievement that took 42 years. It surged past $2 trillion two years later. Its next trillion took just 16 months and 15 days.
