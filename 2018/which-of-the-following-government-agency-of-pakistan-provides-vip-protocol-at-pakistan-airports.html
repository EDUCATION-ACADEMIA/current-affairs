Which of the following government agency of Pakistan provides VIP protocol at Pakistan airports?

(A) National Anti-profiteering Authority

(B) ** Federal Investigation Agency

(C) Airport Authority of Pakistan

(D) Authority for Advance Rulings

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Islamabad International Airport (IATA: ISB, ICAO: OPIS) is the international airport serving Islamabad, the capital city of Pakistan.

Concept note-2: -There are an estimated 151 airfields in Pakistan. Major international airports are situated in Karachi, Islamabad and Lahore. Other international airports are situated in Peshawar, Multan, Sialkot, Faisalabad, Quetta, Rahim Yar Khan, Turbat, Gwadar, D.G.Khan, Tharparkar, Sukkur and Skardu .

Concept note-3: -The New Gwadar International Airport (NGIA) is being built and will be country’s biggest when completed in September 2023, although a soft opening and a test flight is scheduled for 23 March 2023 when it is expected to be inaugurated by the Chinese President Xi.

Concept note-4: -The Jinnah International Airport Karachi is also one of the most hustled airports in the country. It was established in 1925 and the airport was named after the founder of Pakistan Mr. Jinnah. It is considered to be the largest Airport of Pakistan.
