Which of the following Indian city ranked world’s 2nd best location to launch tech startups?

(A) Mumbai

(B) Kolkata

(C) Hyderabad

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Traditionally, India’s startup ecosystem has been concentrated in the Tier 1 cities of the country, with Bengaluru and Delhi NCR being the two largest startup hubs of the country.

Concept note-2: -Silicon Valley/Bay Area, California There is perhaps no more well-known startup city in the US than Silicon Valley. The Bay Area is the global epicenter for tech innovation and has been for decades. This area is home to some of the most iconic tech companies in the world, including Apple, Google, and Facebook.

Concept note-3: -Bangalore has been known as the silicon city for years now, as it is the hub for IT Companies in the country. Currently, the city is called the Startup capital of India. With Prime Minister Narendra Modi emphasizing on his “Make in India” project, the city has grown a few folds by housing 7000+ Startups in the country.

Concept note-4: -Some best cities for startups in India are Bengaluru, Delhi, Mumbai, Gurugram, Hyderabad, Noida, and Pune.
