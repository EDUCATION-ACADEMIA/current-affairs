Which of the following Indian PSU has been featured on the world’s top 100 arms producers in 2017?

(A) Indian Ordnance Factories

(B) Hindustan Aeronautics

(C) Bharat Electronics

(D) ** Bharat Dynamics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2017, a fourth defence PSU Bharat Dynamics Limited broke into the top-100 with arms sales of $ 880 million.

Concept note-2: -The Stockholm International Peace Research Institute has noted that three Indian companies that rank among the top-100 defence companies, viz., Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, AVANI and Bharat Electronics Limited, account for 1.2% of the defence exports of the top-100 total.

Concept note-3: -PSU stands for Public Sector Undertakings in India and these are government-owned corporations. These Public sector companies represents the ‘Group of Companies’, owned by the union government of India, or one of the many states or territorial governments, or both.
