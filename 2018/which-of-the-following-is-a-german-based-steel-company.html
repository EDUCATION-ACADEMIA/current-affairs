Which of the following is a German based steel company?

(A) Kobe Steel

(B) Baoshan Iron & Steel

(C) Arcelor Mittal

(D) ** ThyssenKrupp

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Biggest companies in the Iron & Steel Manufacturing industry in Germany. IBIS World covers 4 companies in the Iron & Steel Manufacturing in Germany industry, including Thyssenkrupp Steel Europe AG, ArcelorMittal S. A. (Deutschland), Salzgitter AG and Hüttenwerke Krupp Mannesmann GmbH.

Concept note-2: -Thyssenkrupp is Germany’s largest steelmaker, and the company’s Duisburg steel production site alone accounts currently for 2% of Germany’s CO2 emissions.

Concept note-3: -Germany is one of the world’s leading manufacturers of steel, with production concentrated in the Ruhr region; however, since the peak output of the early 1970s, a number of plants have closed.

Concept note-4: -According to World Steel, Germany is one of the ten largest steel producers in the world. In 2021, the country increased output of steel by 12.3% compared to 2020 – up to 40.1 million tons, ranking 8th in the world ranking of producers.
