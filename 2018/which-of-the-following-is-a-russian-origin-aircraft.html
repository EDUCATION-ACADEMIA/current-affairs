Which of the following is a Russian-origin aircraft?

(A) Su-30MKI

(B) ** MiG-29

(C) HAL Tejas

(D) Mirage 2000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Russian aircraft industry offers a portfolio of internationally competitive military aircraft such as MiG-29 and Su-27, while new projects such as the Sukhoi Superjet 100 are hoped to revive the fortunes of the civilian aircraft segment.

Concept note-2: -For Russian aircraft, the initial letters reflect the agency that designed the aircraft, such as the Sukhoi Aviation Corporation or the former Mikoyan and Gurevich Design Bureau, now Russian Aircraft Corporation MiG; both bureaus are well known for their fighter aircraft.

Concept note-3: -Mozhaysky’s airplane (Russian: ) was an experimental Russian airplane designed and built by a naval officer Alexander Mozhaysky (also transliterated as Mozhayskiy, Mozhayski, Mozhaiski and Mozhaisky).
