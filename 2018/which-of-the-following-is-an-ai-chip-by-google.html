Which of the following is an AI chip by Google?

(A) Nervana Chip

(B) ** Edge TPU Chip

(C) ACM Chip

(D) Pringles chip

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is the AI chip by Google, and what does it do? Google’s new AI chip is called the TPU (Tensor Processing Unit). It is specifically designed for machine learning tasks. The TPU can handle trillions of operations per second, which is significantly faster than traditional CPUs and GPUs.

Concept note-2: -As explained above, Google uses RankBrain, neural matching, and BERT in most queries you enter into Google Search, but Google also has core updates.

Concept note-3: -An AI chip is a special type of semiconductor designed specifically for artificial intelligence applications. AI chips are different from traditional semiconductors in several ways: AI chips are designed to handle the large amounts of data required for training and inference in AI applications.

Concept note-4: -Google Tensor is a series of ARM64-based system-on-chip (SoC) processors designed by Google for its Pixel devices. The first-generation chip debuted on the Pixel 6 smartphone series in 2021, and were succeeded by the second-generation chip on the Pixel 7 and Pixel 7 Pro smartphone series in 2022.

Concept note-5: -An energy-efficient chip called NeuRRAM fixes an old design flaw to run large-scale AI algorithms on smaller devices, reaching the same accuracy as wasteful digital computers.
