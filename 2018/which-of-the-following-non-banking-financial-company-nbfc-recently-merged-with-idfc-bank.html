Which of the following Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) recently merged with IDFC Bank?

(A) IndoStar Capital Finance Limited

(B) PNB Gilts Limited

(C) HDB Financial Services Limited

(D) ** Capital First Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -and Capital First Ltd. today announced the completion of merger following the receipt of all requisite shareholder and regulatory approvals and the merged entity will be called IDFC First Bank, subject to shareholders’ approval.

Concept note-2: -The boards also approved a share swap ratio of 139 shares of IDFC Bank for 10 shares of Capital First, a debt financing firm for small entrepreneurs. The merger will also result in V Vaidya-nathan, currently chairman and managing director of Capital First, succeeding Ra-jiv Lallas MD and CEO of the merged entity.

Concept note-3: -In 2010, IDFC become the first NBFC to be classified as Infrastructure Finance Company (IFC) by RBI. In 2013, the company applied for Banking License from RBI and obtained in principle approval in 2014 to set up a bank for a period of 18 months.

Concept note-4: -The reverse merger of IDFC with IDFC First Bank is aimed at unlocking value for the former’s shareholders. As such, the exercise offers little benefit to the bank. In fact, the transaction needs to take care of the extent of dilution faced by existing shareholders of the bank.

Concept note-5: -IDFC and IDFC First Bank Merger details: In a regulatory filing on December 30, 2021, IDFC First Bank had given approval for the merger of IDFC and IDFC First Bank.
