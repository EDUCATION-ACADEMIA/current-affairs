Concept note-1: -UN ramps up humanitarian appeal for life-saving assistance to 3.4 million Sri Lankans. Concept note-2: -Moving forward, the UN strives to support the Government of Sri Lanka to achieve sustainable and inclusive economic growth, with equitable access to quality social services, strengthened human capabilities and reconciliation for long-lasting peace.
