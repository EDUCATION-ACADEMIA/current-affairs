Which of the following place is taken under Phase II of Swachh Bharat Mission?

(A) The Taj Mahal, Agra

(B) Meenakshi Temple, Madurai

(C) Golden Temple, Amritsar

(D) ** Charminar, Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ODF Plus activities under Phase II of Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) will reinforce ODF behaviours and focus on providing interventions for the safe management of solid and liquid waste in villages.

Concept note-2: -SBM(G) Phase-II: It emphasizes the sustainability of achievements under phase I and to provide adequate facilities for Solid/Liquid & plastic Waste Management (SLWM) in rural India.

Concept note-3: -On February 19, the cabinet approved the second phase of the Swachh Bharat Mission, Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s pet project focussed on sustainability of ODF and management of solid and liquid waste.

Concept note-4: -The Swachh Bharat Mission – Gramin (SBM-G) programme, under the Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation, is a flagship programme of the Government of India.
