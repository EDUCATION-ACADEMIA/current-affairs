(A) Yodhin Punja

(B) Gurdeep Singh

(C) Mujeeb Ur Rahman

(D) ** Rohit Kumar Paudel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sachin Tendulkar is the youngest debutant at the age of 16 years and 238 days and Farokh Engineer is the oldest debutant at the age of 36 years and 138 days.

Concept note-2: -Shubman Gill becomes the youngest player in history to register a century in all the 3 formats-Crictoday.

Concept note-3: -Youngest Debutants in Test Cricket When Pakistan’s Hasan Raza made his test debut at the tender age of 14 years and 227 days, he became the youngest Test debutant breaking the record of his countryman Mushtaq Mohammad.
