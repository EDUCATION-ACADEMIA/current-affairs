Which of the following players will jointly be conferred with Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award 2018, India’s highest sporting honour?

(A) Virat Kohli and Manika Batra

(B) ** Virat Kohli and Mirabai Chanu

(C) Neeraj Chopra and Hima Das

(D) Jinson Johnson and Smriti Mandhana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. India men’s cricket team captain Virat Kohli and weightlifter Mirabai Chanu were conferred with the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award by President Ram Nath Kovind at the Rashtrapati Bhavan in 2018.

Concept note-2: -Shri Sharath Kamal Achanta has won the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 in the Table Tennis discipline.

Concept note-3: -Viswanathan Anand, who became India’s first chess grandmaster in 1988, was the first recipient of the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award, in 1991-1992. Q.
