Which of the following police station bagged the top spot in the List of Top 10 Performing Police Stations 2018 across India?

(A) Andaman and Nicobar’s Campbell Bay

(B) Tamil Nadu’s Periyakulam

(C) Puducherry’s Nettapakkam

(D) ** Kalu police station in Rajasthan’s Bikaner

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Andhra Pradesh is the best state in India for law and order situations. According to the IPF Smart Police Index 2021, the states with the finest policing are Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Assam, Kerala, and Sikkim. The lowest five states are Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, and Punjab.

Concept note-2: -BHUBANESWAR: Union home minister Amit Shah Friday awarded Aska police station in Odisha’s Ganjam district as the number one police station in the country in the annual ranking of police stations for 2022, an official release said.

Concept note-3: -Union Home Minister Amit Shah awarded the Aska Police Station of Ganjam, district of Odisha as the number one police station in the country. Aska Police station was awarded in the annual ranking of Police station for 2022.

Concept note-4: -Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik in a tweet has congratulated Aska police station as it has been selected as best police station in the country. Aska has also been declared as the best police station in CCTNS dashboard for 2022.
