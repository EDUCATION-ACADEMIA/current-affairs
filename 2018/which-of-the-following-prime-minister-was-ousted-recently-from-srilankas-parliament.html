Which of the following Prime Minister was ousted recently from SriLanka’s Parliament?

(A) Maithripala Sirisena

(B) ** Ranil Wickremesinghe

(C) Mahinda Rajapaksa

(D) Chandrika Kumaratunga

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2022 Sri Lankan economic and political crisis On 12 May 2022, President Gotabaya Rajapaksa appointed veteran politician Ranil Wickremesinghe as Prime Minister of Sri Lanka. On 9 July 2022, protestors stormed the Presidential secretariat, Presidential house and set fire to Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe house.

Concept note-2: -Mahinda Rajapaksa is the only prime minister who was suspended from his duties by the Supreme Court, becoming the first de facto prime minister of Sri Lanka in 2018.
