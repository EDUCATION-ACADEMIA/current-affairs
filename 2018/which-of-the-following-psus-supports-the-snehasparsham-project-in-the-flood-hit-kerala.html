Which of the following PSUs supports the Snehasparsham project in the flood-hit Kerala?

(A) Ignite the world

(B) Kerala Service Pvt Ltd

(C) ** Kerala Feeds

(D) Save farmers Organization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ans-Kerala was taken by surprise by the floods because the state had been getting considerably less rainfall over the past few years. The unexpected rain battered the state as though there was no respite at all.

Concept note-2: -In 2018, Kerala saw record rainfall, leading to widespread disaster. Although similar rainfall patterns were seen in 2022, improved dam management allowed the State prevent another disaster.

Concept note-3: -But development such as roads, buildings and rock quarries in sensitive areas of the state have helped cause natural disasters, said Dr Abhilash. Any infrastructural activity on the hill slopes can adversely impact the region’s environment, with regular downpours causing soil erosion and landslides, he said.
