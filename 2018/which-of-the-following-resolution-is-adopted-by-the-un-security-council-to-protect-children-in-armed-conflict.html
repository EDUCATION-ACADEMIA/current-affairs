Which of the following resolution is adopted by the UN Security Council to protect children in armed conflict?

(A) ** 2427

(B) 2422

(C) 2417

(D) 2413

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -United Nations Security Council resolution 2535 (2020) on youth, peace and security was unanimously adopted 14 July 2020. As the third resolution on youth, peace and security, it underscores the critical role of youth in the prevention and resolution of conflicts.

Concept note-2: -UN Security Council Resolution 2601 (2021)-the protection of education in armed conflict.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains Q. Which institution adopted a resolution on the protection of education in armed conflict (Resolution 2601)? Notes: The UN Security Council adopted a resolution on the protection of education in armed conflict named ‘Resolution 2601’. The resolution won the unanimous support of the 15-member council.
