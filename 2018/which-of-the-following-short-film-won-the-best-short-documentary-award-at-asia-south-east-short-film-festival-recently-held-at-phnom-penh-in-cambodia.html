Which of the following short film won the best short documentary award at Asia South East Short Film Festival recently held at Phnom Penh in Cambodia?

(A) Sprout

(B) Suddenly Last Summer

(C) ** Finding Beauty in Garbage

(D) Night Fishing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Victor Gabriel’s “Hallelujah” won the Grand Prix Best Short, Seemab Gul’s “Sandstorm” (“Mulaqat”) won the Best Live Action and Joseph Pierce’s “Scale” won the Best Animation.

Concept note-2: -The Best short film was awarded to The Water Murmurs, directed by Jianying Chen. A special mention was given to Lori, by Abinash Bikram Shah.

Concept note-3: -The Short Film Palme d’Or (French: Palme d’Or du court métrage) is the highest prize given to a short film at the Cannes Film Festival. Since the creation of the Cinéfondation section in 1998, a common Official Jury awards the Short Film Palme d’Or as well as the prizes for the three best films of the Cinéfondation.

Concept note-4: -In the legal drama, Diop chronicles the trial of a Franco Senegalese mother who committed infanticide. The award for best short film went to Snow in September from Mongolian director Lkhagvadulam Purev-Ochir.
