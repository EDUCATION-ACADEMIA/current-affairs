Which of the following state government barred CBI from entering states without approval?

(A) ** Andhra Pradesh

(B) Telangana

(C) Karnataka

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first state to withdraw general consent to the CBI was Mizoram in 2015, then ruled by the Congress.

Concept note-2: -Jitendra Singh: Nine states have withdrawn general consent to CBI: Union Minister Jitendra Singh-The Economic Times.

Concept note-3: -New Delhi: Nine states, including Telangana and Meghalaya, have withdrawn general consent given to the Central Bureau of Investigation for probing certain offences, Union Minister Jitendra Singh said on Wednesday.

Concept note-4: -THE MAHARASHTRA government has reversed the previous Maha Vikas Aghadi (MVA) government’s decision of withdrawing general consent to the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI). The decision was taken in a meeting of the state cabinet on Thursday.

Concept note-5: -The Telangana government has withdrawn general consent given to the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) in the state.
