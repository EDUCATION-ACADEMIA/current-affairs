Which of the following State has approved NCERT syllabus for the State-run and aided schools?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Karnataka

(C) ** Tripura

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -These states and union territories are Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Arunachal Pradesh, Chandigarh, Jharkhand, Delhi, Goa, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Bihar, Jammu and Kashmir, Sikkim and Uttarakhand, a NCERT official said.

Concept note-2: -These includes states of Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Uttarakhand, Goa, Tripura, Delhi, Gujarat, Haryana and Mizoram that have implemented NCERT books right from class 1 onwards.

Concept note-3: -Textbooks published by NCERT are prescribed by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) from classes I to XII, with exceptions for a few subjects, especially for the Class 10 and 12 Board Examination. Around 19 school boards from 14 states have adopted or adapted the books.
