Which of the following State’s team won the 24th Senior Women’s National football championships?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Odisha

(D) ** Manipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Manipur.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 All-Ireland Senior Ladies’ Football Championship was the 49th edition of the Ladies’ Gaelic Football Association’s premier inter-county ladies’ Gaelic Football tournament. It took place in summer 2022 in Ireland. Meath retained the title they won in 2021.

Concept note-3: -Founded in 1970, it has been named the WFA Cup, FA Women’s Cup and now Women’s FA Cup (Vitality Women’s FA Cup for sponsorship reasons). Designed as an equivalent to the FA Cup in men’s football, the competition began in 1970–71 as the Mitre Challenge Trophy, organised by the Women’s Football Association (WFA).
