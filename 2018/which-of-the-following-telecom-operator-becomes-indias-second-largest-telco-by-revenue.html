Which of the following telecom operator becomes India’s second largest telco by revenue?

(A) Idea Cellular

(B) Vodafone India

(C) ** Reliance Jio

(D) Bharti Airtel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Airtel and Reliance Jio both launched their 5G Network Operations recently. Both networks are good on their own in various aspects. Airtel has been providing its services to its users all across the country for decades. Meanwhile, Reliance Jio has great track data of providing its services at lower prices.

Concept note-2: -Jio added 7, 24, 790 subscribers and Airtel added 4, 12, 767 subscribers. With this, Jio now has 36.66% market share, followed by Airtel at 31.80% and Vi at 21.75% share. Further, 90.21% market share is captured by private players and 9.79% share is grabbed by PSUs.

Concept note-3: -Speaking of the overall market share, Reliance Jio is once again a dominant player with a whopping 52.33% share followed by Bharti Airtel’s 27.39% market share. Vodafone Idea clocked 15.35% of the total market share followed by BSNL with 3.12%.
