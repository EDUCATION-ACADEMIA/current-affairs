Which of the following telecommunications company bagged world’s first big 5G deal worth $3.5 billion?

(A) Verizon

(B) Huawei

(C) Ericsson

(D) ** Nokia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bharti Airtel’s 5G service will be available in select cities from Saturday (October 1), the company’s chairman Sunil Bharti Mittal said at the sixth edition of India Mobile Congress (IMC 2022). With this, Bharti Airtel has become the first company to launch 5G services in the country.

Concept note-2: -Ericsson (NASDAQ: ERIC) and T-Mobile (NASDAQ: TMUS) today announced a multi-year, $3.5 billion contract to support T-Mobile’s nationwide 5G network deployment.

Concept note-3: -T-Mobile takes the cake for 5G with unparalleled cellular coverage and good speeds. Finally, Verizon boasts the best 4G LTE network overall and the fastest-but not widely available-5G availability.
