Which payments bank ties up with Bharti Axa to Offer Jeevan Jyoti Bima?

(A) ** Airtel Payments bank

(B) Paytm Payments bank

(C) Fino PayTech Payments bank

(D) Vodafone M-Pesa Payments Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Airtel Payments Bank and Bharti AXA Life Insurance announce first of its kind alliance to offer ‘Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana’

Concept note-2: -Bharti AXA Life Insurance Forges Bancassurance partnership with Utkarsh Small Finance Bank.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana(PMJJBY) Department of Financial Services. Ministry of Finance. Government of India.

Concept note-4: -Airtel Payments Bank ties up with ICICI Lombard to offer smartphone insurance.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bharti AXA Life Insurance. Bharti AXA Life Insurance has entered into a bancassurance partnership with Shivalik Small Finance Bank.
