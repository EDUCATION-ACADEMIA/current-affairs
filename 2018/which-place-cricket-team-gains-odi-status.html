Which place Cricket Team Gains ODI Status?

(A) ** Nepal

(B) Pakistan

(C) India

(D) Indonesia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Cricket Council (ICC) regularly releases a points-based ranking system for international teams based on their most recent performances. Currently at the top of the list for ODI nations is India, with a rating of 114 and 5, 010 points as of January 2023.

Concept note-2: -As per the latest Odi rankings released by ICC on 2 Feb 2023, India with 114 rating is placed at the top of the points table. Australia with 112 rating is on the second spot. New Zealand with 111 rating occupies the third place.

Concept note-3: -One Day Internationals, also known as ODIs, are a pacier format which started in 1971 but gained in popularity from the 1980s. These are one-innings matches of 50 overs per side, in which teams with a blend of technique, speed and skill are expected to do well.
