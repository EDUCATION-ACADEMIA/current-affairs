Which place Haryana’s First Cyber Police Station Opened?

(A) ** Gurugram

(B) Hyderabad

(C) Kolkata

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answers. Haryana’s first cyber police station was inaugurated in Gurugram, to facilitate registration of cyber offence related cases.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Gurugram. On March 7th, 2018, the cyber police station was inaugurated in Gurugram, Haryana for facilitating the registration of cybercrime cases. This is the first cyber police station in Haryana.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Bengaluru. India’s first police station for cybercrimes inaugurated at Bengaluru in Karnataka. India’s first police station for cybercrimes inaugurated in the year 2001.

Concept note-4: -Till August this year, Singh says, as many as 36,996 complaints of cyber crime were received on cyber crime helpline number 1930, portal cybercrime.gov.in, 309 cyber-desks and 29 cyber police stations in the state.

Concept note-5: -In India, one of the very first cases of cyber crime was that of Yahoo v. Akash Arora. This case occurredin 1999. In this case, the defendant Akash Arora was accused of using the trademark or domain name ‘yahooindia.com’ and a decree of permanent injunction was sought.
