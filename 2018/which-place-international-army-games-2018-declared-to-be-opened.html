Which place International Army Games 2018 declared to be opened?

(A) ** Moscow

(B) Ireland

(C) Australia

(D) New Zealand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2018 International Army Games-Was held from July 28 to August 11, 2018. The competitions were held in Armenia and Iran for the first time.

Concept note-2: -The International Army Games, also known as the “Military Olympics, ” are being co-hosted by twelve nations this year. Involved are more than 270 teams from 37 different nations and regions. The Russian Ministry of Defense is the host nation for these international military sports competitions.

Concept note-3: -This year, the Chinese military sent nine teams abroad to participate in competitions in Russia, Iran, Kazakhstan, Algeria, and Uzbekistan. The International Army Games, initiated by the Russian Defense Ministry, have served as an international platform for militaries to exchange experience and strengthen cooperation.

Concept note-4: -The opening ceremony for the International Army Games 2022 (Army Games 2022) took place solemly at Patriot Park on the outskirts of Moscow, Russia on August 15. This year event drew the participation of 264 teams from 34 nations to compete in 34 events.

Concept note-5: -Vietnamese and Russian teams win gold medals of “Emergency Area” contest. 18/08/2022 After three competition days, the Vietnamese and Russian teams excellently bagged gold medals of the “Emergency Area” contest within the Army Games 2022, announced the organizing panel on August 17.
