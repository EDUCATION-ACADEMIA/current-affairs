Which place joins hand to incorporate UDAY scheme providing electricity to people?

(A) ** Andaman and nicobar islands

(B) Lakshadweep islands

(C) Goa

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lakshadweep joins “UDAY” scheme; would derive an overall net benefit of Rs 8 crore through “UDAY”

Concept note-2: -Only operational (10 states, 1 UT): Gujarat, Uttarakhand, Goa, Karnataka, Puducherry, Manipur, Sikkim, Arunachal Pradesh, Kerala, Tripura & Mizoram.

Concept note-3: -Till now 31 states/UT have joined this scheme Mizoram being the 27th state to join.

Concept note-4: -The states which have agreed to join the scheme are Andhra Pradesh, Rajasthan, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Himachal Pradesh, Punjab, Jammu & Kashmir, Haryana, Gujarat, Chhattisgarh, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Odisha and Maharashtra.

Concept note-5: -UDAY (Ujwal DISCOM Assurance Yojana), a Scheme for the Financial Turnaround of Power Distribution Companies (DISCOMS), has been approved by the Government of India with an objective to improve the operational and financial efficiency of the State DISCOMS.
