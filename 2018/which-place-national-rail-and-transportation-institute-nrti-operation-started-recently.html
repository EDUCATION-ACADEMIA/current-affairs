Which place National Rail and Transportation Institute (NRTI) operation started recently?

(A) ** Vadodara

(B) Mumbai

(C) Chennai

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Vadodara. The National Rail and Transportation Institute (NRTI) is India’s first and only civil service training institute located in Vadodara, Gujarat, It is named as the National Rail and Transportation Institution (NRTI).

Concept note-2: -Gati Shakti Vishwavidyalaya (GSV; formerly National Rail and Transportation Institute) is India’s first transport University located in Vadodara, Gujarat, India.

Concept note-3: -CUET PG 2023 exam will be conducted from June 1 to June 10, 2023. CUET PG 2023 registrations are expected to begin in the month of March 2023. National Rail and Transportation Institute (NRTI) was established in 2018 in Vadodara, Gujarat. It is focused on transport-related education.

Concept note-4: -On 15 December 2018, Union Railways Minister Piyush Goyal has dedicated India’s first railway university to the Nation in Vadodara.

Concept note-5: -Established as a Deemed to be University under De Novo category in 2018, it has now been converted to a Central University and rechristened as Gati Shakti Vishwavidyalaya (GSV) under the Ministry of Railways, Govt.
