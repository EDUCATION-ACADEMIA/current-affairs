Which program launched in Chhattisgarh recently?

(A) National Ayush Mission (NAM)

(B) Janani Suraksha Yojana.

(C) ** Startup India

(D) Janani Shishu Suraksha Karyakaram.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -[Apply] CG Kaushalya Matritva Yojana ( ) Online Application / Registration Form: Chhattisgarh government has launched a new CG Kaushalya Maternity Scheme in state Budget 2023. In this Kaushalya Matritva Yojana, govt. will incentivize mothers on the birth of second girl child.

Concept note-2: -Kishori Shakti Yojana was launched by the Chhattisgarh government for the benefit of adolescent girls. The program is run under the auspices of the Ministry of Women & Child Development, Government of India.

Concept note-3: -The state has two ongoing schemes for the promotion of organic farming-PKVY and Jaivik Kheti Mission (state scheme). In organic farming, many farmers use the principles and practices of Natural Farming, such as Jivamrit, Beejamrit, and botanical pesticide, etc.
