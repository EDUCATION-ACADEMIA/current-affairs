Which rank did Forbes Billionaires List 2018: Amazon’s Bezos Richest, Mukesh Ambani?

(A) ** 19

(B) 21

(C) 25

(D) 36

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliance Industries Chairman Mukesh Ambani, who is the richest man in India, moved up to the 19th global rank on the list of billionaires worldwide. In 2017, he ranked 33rd with $23.2 billion. 1. Amazon chief Jeff Bezos tops the Forbes list of the World Billionaire.

Concept note-2: -In 2018, Amazon founder Jeff Bezos was ranked at the top for the first time and became the first centibillionaire included in the ranking, surpassing Microsoft founder Bill Gates, who had topped the list 18 of the previous 24 years. In 2022, after topping the list for four years, Bezos was surpassed by Elon Musk.

Concept note-3: -Amazon founder Jeff Bezos is the now the third wealthiest person, while the top two of Bernard Arnault (Louis Vitton) and Elon Musk (Tesla, SpaceX, Twitter) remained unchanged on January 24.
