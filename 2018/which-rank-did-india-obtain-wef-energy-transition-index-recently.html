Which rank did India obtain WEF Energy Transition Index recently?

(A) ** 78

(B) 85

(C) 96

(D) 47

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is 87th. India has been ranked at the 87th position among 115 countries in the Energy Transition Index (ETI) that tracks nations on the current performance of their energy systems across various aspects, according to a World Economic Forum (WEF) report released.

Concept note-2: -The Global Energy Transition Index (ETI) is published by World Economic Forum (WEF) to quantify the global change to renewable energy.

Concept note-3: -India ranked 87 out of 110 countries in the Global Energy Transition Index (ETI), a benchmark by the World Economic Forum.
