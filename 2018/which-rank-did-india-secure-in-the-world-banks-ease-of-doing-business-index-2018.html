Which rank did India secure in the World Bank’s Ease of Doing Business Index 2018?

(A) 103rd

(B) ** 77th

(C) 54th

(D) 89th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s rank in Ease of Doing Business has moved to 63 in 2019 from 77 in 2018.

Concept note-2: -India’s “rank in ease of doing business according to World Bank report has improved from 142 in 2014 to 63 in 2022 and reduction in compliances is now a major focus.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI, October 31, 2018 – Sustained business reforms over the past several years helped India jump 23 places to move to 77th position in this year’s global ease of Doing Business rankings.
