Which startup has announced launch plan for its first 10-kg nanosatellite Centauri I with ISRO’s PSLV?

(A) Deep Space Industries

(B) Arianespace

(C) ** Fleet Space Technologies

(D) Team Indus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISRO’s work horse PSLV-C54 has successfully launched EOS-06 satellite along with Eight Nano-satellites into two different SSPOs. The mission was accomplished from Satish Dhawan Space Centre SHAR on 26th November 2022.

Concept note-2: -The LVM3 is the next generation launch vehicle capable of launching 4 tonne class of communication satellites and 10 tonne class of payloads to LEOs. The vehicle was developed with completely indigenized technologies including the C25 cryo stage.

Concept note-3: -PSLV-C5 was launched at 04:52 hours Coordinated Universal Time (10:22 hours Indian Standard Time) on 17 October 2003 from Satish Dhawan Space Centre.
