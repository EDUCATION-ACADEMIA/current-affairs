Which state adjudged ‘best state’; Himachal pavilion gets ‘silver medal’ at India International Trade Fair, New Delhi?

(A) ** Uttarakhand

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Kerala

(D) Saudi Arabia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Odisha pavilion won the prestigious ‘Special Appreciation Certificate for excellence in display’ in the category of ‘State Government and Union Territory’ on Sunday at the 41st edition of India International Trade Fair(IITF), 2022.

Concept note-2: -Kerala Pavilion won gold medal at the India International Trade Fair (IITF)organized in New Delhi. The 6000 square feet pavilion underlining the concept of ‘Vocal for Local, Local to Global’ won the award in the State & Union Territory Pavilion category.

Concept note-3: -The Lavi Fair or Lavi Mela officially known as International Lavi Fair is fundamentally a trade fair held in Rampur Bushahr, Shimla.

Concept note-4: -PATNA: The Bihar Pavilion was awarded a gold medal in ‘Best Pavilion’ category during the closing ceremony of the 40th India International Trade Fair (IITF) held at Pragti Maidan in New Delhi on Saturday.
