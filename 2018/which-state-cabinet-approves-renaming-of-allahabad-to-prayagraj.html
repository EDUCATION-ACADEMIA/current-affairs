Which state Cabinet approves Renaming Of Allahabad To Prayagraj?

(A) Bihar

(B) Himachal Pradesh

(C) ** Uttar Pradesh

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1575 AD - Emperor Akbar founded the city in by the name of “ILLAHABAS” which later became ALLAHABAD meant “The City of Allaha” impressed with the strategic importance of the SANGAM.

Concept note-2: -Allahabad HC Dismisses Plea To Rename High Court as Prayagraj HC [Read Order] Recently, the Allahabad High Court dismissed a plea to rename itself either as Prayagraj High Court or the Uttar Pradesh High Court.

Concept note-3: -The city was known as Prayag before 16th-century Mughal emperor Akbar built a fort near the confluence of the Ganga and the Yamuna and named it “Ilahabad”. His grandson Shah Jahan renamed the entire city “Allahabad”.

Concept note-4: -Prayagraj, also called Prayag, formerly Allahabad or Ilāhābād, city, southern Uttar Pradesh state, northern India.
