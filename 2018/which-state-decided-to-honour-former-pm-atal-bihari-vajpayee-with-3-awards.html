Which state decided to honour former PM Atal Bihari Vajpayee with 3 awards?

(A) ** Madhya Pradesh

(B) Arunachal pradesh

(C) Bihar

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Atal Bihari Vajpayee (25 December 1924 – 16 August 2018) was an Indian politician. He served as the 10th Prime Minister of India for six years from 1998 through 2004. He was also serving as Prime Minister from May 1996 through June 1996. He founded the Bharatiya Janata Party.

Concept note-2: -Atal Bihari Vajpayee was an Indian politician who served twice as Prime Minister of India, first from 16 May to 1 June 1996, and then from 19 March 1998 to 22 May 2004. A member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), Vajpayee was the tenth Prime Minister.
