Which state emerges as first champion of Khelo India School Games with 38 gold medals?

(A) ** Haryana

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Kerala

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Haryana claimed the first runners-up trophy with 128 medals-41 golds, 32 silvers and 55 bronze. Hosts Madhya Pradesh managed 39 golds, 30 silvers and 27 bronze medals to finish third on the KIYG 2022 medals table.

Concept note-2: -Haryana has topped the overall medal tally at the first-ever Khelo India School Games (KISG-2018) in New Delhi after winning the 102 medals which include 38 gold and 26 silver. Maharashtra has acquired the 2nd position with 111 medals while Delhi with 94 medals came third.

Concept note-3: -Maharashtra lead the KIYG 2023 medal table with 53 gold medals, 53 silvers and 47 bronze after Day 12. Haryana are second with 40 golds, 27 silvers and 49 bronze.

Concept note-4: -First Khelo India Youth Games was held in New Delhi in 2018. The Second was held at Pune and the third edition was held in 2020 in Guwahati. Maharashtra defended its title as they topped the medal tally with 78 Gold medals.
