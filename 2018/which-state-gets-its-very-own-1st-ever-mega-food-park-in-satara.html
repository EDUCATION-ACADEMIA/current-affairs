Which state gets its very own 1st ever Mega Food Park in Satara?

(A) Tamil nadu

(B) Kerala

(C) ** Maharashtra

(D) Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Maharashtra’s first Mega Food Park named as Satara Mega Food Park Pvt. Ltd. was inaugurated at Degaon Village in Satara district by Minister of Food Processing Industries Harsimrat Kaur Badal.

Concept note-2: -The Satara Mega Food Park Pvt Ltd, located at Degaon village in the Satara district, was inaugurated on March 1st by Harsimrat Kaur Badal, the Union Minister for Food Processing. The chief minister of Maharashtra, Devendra Fadnavis, was present on the occasion.

Concept note-3: -1. Srini Mega Food Park, Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-4: -Mega Food Parks Scheme launched by the government in 2008 provides financial assistance up to 50 crores to set up modern infrastructure facilities for food processing called Mega Food Parks.

Concept note-5: -The India Food Park (IFP), an integrated food processing facility located in the Tumkur district in the Indian state of Karnataka, was inaugurated by the Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi in September 2014.
