Which state government appoints A.R. Rahman as its Brand Ambassador?

(A) Telangana

(B) Bihar

(C) ** Sikkim

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Oscar-winning music composer AR Rahman has been appointed as the Brand Ambassador of Sikkim government, said an official notification issued by Chief Secretary AK Srivastava.

Concept note-2: -A R Rahman, the renowned music composer, has been appointed as the Brand Ambassador of the Sikkim government. He will promote and project the states achievements nationally and globally.

Concept note-3: -Uttarakhand Government appoints Prasoon Joshi as its Brand Ambassador. CEO & CCO of McCann Worldgroup India, Prasoon Joshi has been appointed by Uttarakhand Government as the state’s brand ambassador.

Concept note-4: -Priyanka Chopra appointed brand ambassador, will promote ‘Awesome Assam’ The Assam government had previously been in talks with Sachin Tendulkar, and Deepika Padukone.
