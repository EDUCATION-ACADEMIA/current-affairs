Which state government launched subsidised power scheme named ‘Sambal’ for labourers and poor families?

(A) Assam

(B) Punjab

(C) Haryana

(D) ** Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Madhya Pradesh state government has launched a subsidized power scheme called ‘Sambal’ for laborers and poor families. It is also a power bill waiver scheme. Under the Sambal scheme, the families that belong Below Poverty Line will be provided electricity at a low cost.

Concept note-2: -Launched by the chief minister of Madhya Pradesh Shivraj Singh Chouhan. The ultimate goal of this scheme is to provide power facilities to all households in the state. The below poverty line families and registered labourers of the unorganised sector are the beneficiaries of this scheme.

Concept note-3: -Mukhyamantri Jan Kalyan Sambal Yojana has been established to better provide workers in unorganized sectors benefits of the various government initiatives. As a part of this Yojana social security is offered to all workers in unorganized industries who fall below the poverty level under the MP Naya Savera Scheme 2023.
