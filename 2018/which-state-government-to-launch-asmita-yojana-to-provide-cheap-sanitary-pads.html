(A) Kerala

(B) Karnataka

(C) West bengal

(D) ** Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Maharashtra government launched cheap sanitary pad scheme Asmita Yojana on the International Women’s Day for rural women.

Concept note-2: -The Sikkim government is set to announce a scheme (Bahini) to install vending machines to provide free sanitary pads. This is the first time that a state government has taken a decision to cover all girls studying in Classes 9-12.
