Which state India’s best ’State of the Year’ award?

(A) ** Chhattisgarh

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Bihar

(D) Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Kerala.

Concept note-2: -Maharashtra is India’s best performer, state rankings released by a domestic credit rating company showed, highlighting its robust position on finances and social indicators.

Concept note-3: -For the year 2022, Assam scored 1, 185.3 out of a score of 2, 000 points followed by Andhra Pradesh at 1, 165.2 points and Madhya Pradesh at 1, 165.2 points.

Concept note-4: -Sikkim has retained its position as the best-governed small state in India. The 7th edition of Public Affairs Index, 2022, prepared by Bengaluru-based not-for-profit think tank Public Affairs Center (PAC), was released on Friday.
