Which state launches 24 hour helpline for students recently?

(A) ** Tamil Nadu

(B) Kerala

(C) West Bengal

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A 24-hour control room has been set up in Delhi and the helpline numbers are +911123012113, +911123914104, +911123017905 and 1800118797. The Indian Embassy in Kyiv also issued an advisory to Indian nationals stuck in Ukraine.

Concept note-2: -It was launched by Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu-K Palaniswami. The Education Guidance Centre was set up at the Secretariat that also offers information relating to school and other academic institutes owned by the government.

Concept note-3: -Emergency Response Support System (ERSS) is a Pan-India single number (112) based emergency response system for citizens in emergencies.
