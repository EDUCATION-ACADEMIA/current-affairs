Which state launches digital platform for police force recently?

(A) ** Gujarat

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Kerala

(D) Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which state government has launched the digital platform “Pocket Cop Project” for police force? Notes: Gujarat Chief Minister Vijay Rupani has launched the digital platform ‘Pocket Cop Project’ in order to ensure that the police force of the state is equipped with modern day technology.

Concept note-2: -Union Home Minister Amit Shah awarded the Aska Police Station of Ganjam, district of Odisha as the number one police station in the country. Aska Police station was awarded in the annual ranking of Police station for 2022.

Concept note-3: -Andhra Pradesh is the best state in India for law and order situations. According to the IPF Smart Police Index 2021, the states with the finest policing are Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Assam, Kerala, and Sikkim. The lowest five states are Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, and Punjab.
