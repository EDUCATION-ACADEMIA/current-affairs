Which state opens door for medical colleges availing best medical facilities?

(A) Madhya pradesh

(B) ** Arunachal Pradesh

(C) Kerala

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian states with the most medical colleges include Karnataka, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, and Uttar Pradesh. States with the fewest include Manipur, Tripura, Chandigarh, Goa, and Sikkim.

Concept note-2: -Bengaluru: Karnataka has the highest number of medical colleges, and consequently, medical seats in the country at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. Karnataka is home to 53 medical colleges, of which 18 are government colleges.

Concept note-3: -Medical colleges are spread throughout the nation in different states and cities. There are more than 2000 Medical colleges in India. The highest number of colleges are located in Tamil Nadu (227), Karnataka (210), Uttar Pradesh (181), Maharashtra (179), and Delhi NCR (165).

Concept note-4: -AIIMS (All India Institute of Medical Sciences), New Delhi The highest and the most popular dream institution of medical science is the All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi.
