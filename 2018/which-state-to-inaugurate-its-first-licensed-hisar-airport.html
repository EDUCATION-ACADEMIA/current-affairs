Which state to inaugurate its first licensed Hisar Airport?

(A) ** Haryana

(B) Madhya pradesh

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hisar Airport is located on the outskirts of the city. In August 2012, the DGCA approved the Haryana state government’s plan to develop the airport to operate domestic passenger services.

Concept note-2: -With the construction of the Maharaja Agrasen International Airport in Hisar, new avenues for development will emerge, and both the city’s residents and the state will benefit enormously from it, Chautala said. In a statement issued here, he said the runway work of the airport will be completed by March next year.

Concept note-3: -Maharaja Agrasen Airport’s expansion project is under construction in Hisar, Haryana by Airports Authority of India (AAI). Currently, Phase 2 is under construction with a December 2022 deadline. The airport will be upgraded to an international airport by 2030.
