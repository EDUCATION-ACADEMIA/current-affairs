Which state To Observe ’Rhino Day’ On September 22?

(A) ** Assam

(B) Himachal Pradesh

(C) Kerala

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Home to India’s largest population of one-horned Rhinos, Assam observes World Rhino Day on September 22.

Concept note-2: -Every year on September 22, World Rhino Day is observed to spread awareness about the different Rhinoceros species and the dangers they face.

Concept note-3: -World Rhino Day 2022-This Year’S Theme Is ‘Five Rhino Species Forever’

Concept note-4: -World Rhino Day is observed on 22nd September to spread awareness about the different Rhinoceros species and the dangers they face. This day also celebrates all five rhino species namely the Sumatran, Black, Greater One-horned, Javan, and White rhino species.

Concept note-5: -Kaziranga national park is located in Assam. 2. The park is a home to two-thirds of the world’s great one-horned rhinoceroses.
