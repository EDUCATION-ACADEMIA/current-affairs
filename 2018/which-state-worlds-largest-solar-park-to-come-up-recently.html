Which state World’s Largest Solar Park To Come Up recently?

(A) ** Gujarat

(B) Tamil nadu

(C) West Bengal

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bhadla Solar Park is the largest solar park in the world as of 2022 and is spread over a total area of 5, 700 hectares (14, 000 acres) in Bhadla, Phalodi tehsil, Jodhpur district, Rajasthan, India.

Concept note-2: -Which is World’s Largest Solar Power Plant and where is it located? The Bhadla Solar Park is the World’s largest Solar Power Plant. It is based in Bhadla village, in India’s Rajasthan’s Jodhpur district – a region known for its solar-friendly high temperatures.

Concept note-3: -Bhadla Solar Park, India (2, 245 MW) Sprawling over an area of 14, 000 acres, the Bhadla Solar Park in India has the position of the biggest solar plant in world. It is situated in Rajasthan in the Bhadla village of Jodhpur District.

Concept note-4: -The Karnataka government has launched the worlds largest solar park Shakti Sthalaon at Pavagada in Tumakuru district, about 180km from Bengaluru.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Madhya Pradesh. The world’s largest floating solar energy project of 600 MW is started in Khandwa district of Madhya Pradesh. Will begin power generation by the year 2022-23.
