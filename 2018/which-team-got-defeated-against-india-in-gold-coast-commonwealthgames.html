Which team got defeated against India in Gold Coast CommonwealthGames?

(A) ** Malaysia

(B) Australia

(C) North America

(D) South Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hockey: India settle for silver after 7-0 defeat in men’s gold medal match. The Australian men’s hockey team made history once again at the Commonwealth Games by winning their seventh-consecutive title. They beat India 7-0 in the gold medal match and remain the only team in men’s hockey to win a CWG gold.

Concept note-2: -The unavailability of a decent shooting range in the British West Midlands region, and the organiser’s unwillingness to invest in a new facility had led to shooting being dropped from the roster.

Concept note-3: -Pistol shooter Jaspal Rana is the most successful Indian athlete at the Commonwealth Games, with 15 medals – nine golds, four silvers and two bronze. He dominated the shooting circuit in the 1990s and early 2000s.
