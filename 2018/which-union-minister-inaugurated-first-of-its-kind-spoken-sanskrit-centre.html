Which union minister Inaugurated First Of Its Kind Spoken Sanskrit Centre?

(A) ** Shri Rajnath Singh

(B) Shri Arun jaitley

(C) Shri Suresh menon

(D) Shri Vijay prakash

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The state of Uttarakhand has become the first state in India to declare Sanskrit as an official language.

Concept note-2: -A large part of the population in India, including Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, and Punjab speaks and understands Urdu, but only 2, 4821 people in the country speak Sanskrit.

Concept note-3: -But Sanskrit is now spoken by less than 1% of Indians and is mostly used by Hindu priests during religious ceremonies. It’s one of the official languages in only one Indian state, Uttarakhand in the north, which is dotted with historical Hindu temple towns.
