Which Union minister Launches ’Khelo India’ Logo?

(A) Shri Arun Jaitley

(B) Shri Vijay Prakash

(C) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(D) ** Shri Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Sports Minister Anurag Singh Thakur launched the Khelo India’s new Dashboard in New Delhi, which has all the statistical data related to the Khelo India Scheme and the Khelo India events.

Concept note-2: -Union Minister of Youth Affairs and Sports Anurag Thakur and the LG of Jammu and Kashmir Manoj Sinha on Saturday launched the third Khelo India Winter Games mascot, theme song and jersey. The Winter Games are scheduled to be held from the 10th to the 14th of this month.

Concept note-3: -BHOPAL: The mascots of Khelo India Youth Games (KIYG)-2022, Asha and Mowgli, and its smart torch ‘Amarkantak’ were released along with the theme song on Saturday by Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan and Union Minister Nisith Pramanik.
