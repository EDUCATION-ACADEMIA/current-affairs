Which University has been ranked A+ in the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) review 2018?

(A) Jawaharlal Nehru University

(B) IGNOU

(C) Banaras Hindu University

(D) ** Delhi University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Content Curator | Updated On-Jan 1, 2023. National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) is an autonomous body that accredits higher education institutions after assessing their performance based on framed parameters. The council was established by the University Grants Commission (UGC) in 1994.

Concept note-2: -MUMBAI: A deemed university in Bhubaneswar has pipped India’s top-ranked university of 2022, the Institute of Science, in its NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) score. The university, ranked outside the top 15, has scored 3.88, the highest ever awarded in the country, compared to IISc’s 3.67.
