Who approves Procurement Of Equipment Worth Rs 9, 100 Crores?

(A) ** Defence Acquisition Council (DAC),

(B) Bharat Heavy Electrical Limited BHEL

(C) National Petroleum Corporation Limited

(D) Oil And Natural Gas Foundation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The procurement proposals were cleared by the Defence Acquisition Council (DAC) headed by Defence Minister Rajnath Singh.

Concept note-2: -The Defence Acquisition Council (DAC), which met on Tuesday under the chairmanship of Defence Minister Rajnath Singh, accorded Acceptance of Necessity (AoN) for capital acquisition proposals of armed forces worth ₹8,357 crore.

Concept note-3: -A meeting of Defence Acquisition Council (DAC), held on January 10, 2023 under the chairmanship of Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh, accorded Acceptance of Necessity (AoN) for three capital acquisition proposals, amounting to Rs 4,276 crore.

Concept note-4: -DAC is the highest decision-making body in the Defence Ministry for deciding on new policies and capital acquisitions for the three services – Army, Navy and Air Force, and the Indian Coast Guard. The Defence Acquisition Council is headed by the Defence Minister.
