Who are the only Indians to have qualified for the season-ending BWF World Tour Finals 2018?

(A) Ashwini Ponnappa and Kidambi Srikanth

(B) ** PV Sindhu and Sameer Verma

(C) Saina Nehwal and K. Srikanth

(D) Sai Uttejitha Rao and HS Prannoy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HS Prannoy, the only Indian at the BWF World Tour Finals, looks to make a mark on debut.

Concept note-2: -The top eight based on 26 world tour tournaments get to feature in the Finals. Players who take part in more tournaments have higher percentage of qualifying – even if they don’t win regularly.

Concept note-3: -Sindhu became the first Indian to win Badminton World Championships gold by defeating Nozomi Okuhara. Nozomi Okuhara is from Japan. It was the BWF World Championships of 2019.

Concept note-4: -17:27-Viktor Axelsen shatters Anthony Sinisuka Ginting Axelsen finished 2022 with a 51-3 record and eight titles including his second world championship in Tokyo in August. He was, naturally, named the tour’s top men’s player.
