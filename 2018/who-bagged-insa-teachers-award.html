Who bagged INSA Teachers Award?

(A) ** Tezpur University

(B) Professor University Of Oxford

(C) Harvard university

(D) University of Chicago

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bikram Phookum receives INSA Best Teacher Award.

Concept note-2: -a) The award shall be made to outstanding teachers currently working or worked in India, with at least 15 years of teaching experience at undergraduate or postgraduate level in any India College/University/Institution. b) Maximum age limit is 55 years as on December 31 of the year preceding the year of the award.

Concept note-3: -KARIMNAGAR: Mr Lalit Mohan Sahu, the senior faculty and Project Based Learning resource person from Paramita Heritage School of Karimnagar town has been honoured with AKS Global Teacher Award for the academic year 2022-23 by AKS global Academy.

Concept note-4: -Shefali Arya, P.G.T Home Science, DAV Public School, Pushpanjali Enclave, Pitampura has been honored by the Delhi Government. She is recipient of Delhi State Teachers’ Award 2022 under big school category.
