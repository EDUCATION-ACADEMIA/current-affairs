Who bags IPI-India Award for excellence in journalism 2018?

(A) ** Namrata Ahuja

(B) Vikram Chandra

(C) Rajat Sharma

(D) Shweta Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -THE WEEK journalist bags IPI India Award for Excellence in Journalism 2018. The prestigious IPI India Award for Excellence in Journalism 2018 has been given to Namrata Biji Ahuja of THE WEEK magazine this year.

Concept note-2: -The award comprises a cash prize of ₹1 lakh, a trophy and a citation, to each team. The Print, a news portal, and Saurabh Shukla of the NDTV have been selected for the International Press Institute (IPI) India Award for Excellence in Journalism, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The India chapter of the International Press Institute, IPI-India, on Friday conferred the Award for Excellence in Journalism 2022 jointly to The Print and NDTV correspondent Saurabh Shukla.

Concept note-4: -The Ramnath Goenka Excellence in Journalism Awards (RNG Awards) are one of the awards in India in the field of journalism.
