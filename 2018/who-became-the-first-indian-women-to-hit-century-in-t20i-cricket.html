Who became the first Indian women to hit century in T20I cricket?

(A) ** Harmanpreet Kaur

(B) Jhulan Goswami

(C) Mithali Raj

(D) Smriti Mandhana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Harmanpreet Kaur is the first Indian woman cricketer to hit a century in T20 International cricket by scoring 103 runs in 51 balls.

Concept note-2: -1st Indian Woman Cricketer To Score 100 Harmanpreet is the first Indian woman Cricketer to have played 100 T20 Internationals and also to have scored a century. She scored 103 runs in 51 balls.

Concept note-3: -Former cricketer Suresh Raina was the first Indian to score a century in T20 Internationals. The flamboyant southpaw registered India’s maiden ton when he scored 101 runs off just 60 balls against South Africa during an ICC T20 World Cup encounter in May 2010.

Concept note-4: -Suzie Bates of New Zealand has scored the most runs in Women’s T20 International (3683). Who is the highest run-scorer in Women’s T20 cricket in India? Harmanpreet Kaur is India’s highest run-scorer in Women’s T20 cricket (2887).
