Who became the youngest Indian shooter to win a medal at the Asian Games?

(A) Sanjeev Rajput

(B) ** Shardul Vihan

(C) Deepak Kumar

(D) Ravi Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shotgun shooter Shardul Vihan became the youngest Indian to win a medal at the Asian Games after clinching the men’s double trap silver medal at the 2018 continental meet. Shardul was just 15 years, 7 months and 12 days at the time of his win.

Concept note-2: -Asian Games: How Saurabh Chaudhary became India’s youngest gold medallist.

Concept note-3: -Sachin Nag won the 100m freestyle swimming event to record India’s first gold medal at the Asian Games. The Mr Asia of 1951, a men’s physique competition, was also held as a non-medal event during the First Asian Games.
