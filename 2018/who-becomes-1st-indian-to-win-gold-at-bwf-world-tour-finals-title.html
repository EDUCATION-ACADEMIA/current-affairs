Who becomes 1st Indian to win gold at BWF World Tour Finals title?

(A) HS Prannoy

(B) Kidambi Srikanth

(C) Saina Nehwal

(D) ** PV Sindhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ten Indian badminton players have won 13 medals at the BWF World Championships over the years, with only PV Sindhu going on to become a world champion.

Concept note-2: -First Indian to Win Olympic Medal in Badminton Saina Nehwal has acquired 24 international titles containing 11 tiles in superseries. She is also the first Indian to reach the finals of the BWF World Championship, achieving the title of the only Indian to win the BWF World Championship.

Concept note-3: -BWF World Tour Finals winners: PV Sindhu only Indian-full list. Only the top eight players and pairs on the World Tour rankings are invited to participate in the BWF World Tour Finals every year.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. Prakash Padukone, (born June 10, 1955, Bangalore, India), Indian badminton champion who dominated the national badminton scene for almost a decade (1971–80) and put India on the sport’s international map.
