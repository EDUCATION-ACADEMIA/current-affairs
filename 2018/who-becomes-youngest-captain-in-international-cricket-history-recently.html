Who Becomes Youngest Captain In International Cricket History recently?

(A) ** Rashid Khan

(B) Tatenda Taibu

(C) Javed Miandad

(D) GC Grant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tatenda Taibu (20 years 358 days) The young Zimbabwean broke the record of Mansur Ali Khan Pataudi’s to become the youngest Test captain in cricket history. He led his team in 10 Tests, losing 9 and drawing 1, which shows that Taibu was not comfortable in this role.

Concept note-2: -In the Test format, Mansoor Ali Khan Pataudi, at 21 years and 77 days of age, became the youngest captain to lead India against West Indies, in 1962 at the Kensington Oval.
