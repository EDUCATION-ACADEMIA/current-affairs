Who broke his own junior world record to win the 10m Air Pistol Junior Men event at the ISSF World Championship?

(A) ** Saurabh Chaudhary

(B) Arjun Singh Cheema

(C) Om Prakash Mitharwal

(D) Shahzar Rizvi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: A 16-year-old shooter from India once again made each of his seven other challengers feel old on Sunday by winning a World Cup gold in his first senior ISSF final; the world record in a men’s 10m air pistol final and the Olympic quota place that came along for Saurabh Chaudhary was the icing on the cake.

Concept note-2: -China topped the ISSF Rifle/Pistol World Championships 2022 medals tally with 58 medals, 27 hold, 16 silver and 15 bronze.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 4. India has taken first place in the medal standing in International Shooting Sport Federation, ISSF World Cup 2022 in Cairo. The Indian team finished on the top of the medals tally with a total of seven medals comprising of four gold, two silver, and one bronze.

Concept note-4: -Q. Which is the venue of the 2022 ISSF World Cup? Notes: This year’s first ISSF World Cup is being held in the city of Cairo, Egypt.

Concept note-5: -The ISSF World Cup Rifle/Pistol/Shotgun is taking place in Changwon, Korea from July 9th to 21st.
