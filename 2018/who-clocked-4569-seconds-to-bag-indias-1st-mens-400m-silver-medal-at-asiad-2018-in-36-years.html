Who clocked 45.69 seconds to bag India’s 1st men’s 400m silver medal at Asiad 2018 in 36 years?

(A) Rajiv Arokia

(B) Manjit Singh

(C) ** Muhammed Anas Yahiya

(D) Jinson Johnson

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Muhammed Anas bagged silver in men’s 400m after clocking 45.69 seconds. By India Today Web Desk: India’s Muhammed Anas bagged silver in men’s 400m on Sunday at the Asian Games 2018. Anas clocked 45.69 seconds to finish second behind Qatar’s Hassan Abdalelah, who won the gold medal.
