Who gets independent charge of I & B Ministry recently?

(A) ** Shri Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore

(B) Shri Arun Jaitley

(C) Shri Naresh Iyer

(D) Shri Suresh Krishnan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Minister of State with independent charge is a minister without an overseeing Cabinet Minister in the State or Union Government of India. He himself is in charge of his ministry, unlike Minister of State who is also a Minister but assists a cabinet minister.

Concept note-2: -Honble Union Minister Prakash Javdekar to launch an e-compendium of articles on Constitution, Fundamental Rights, and Duties.

Concept note-3: -Sh Anurag Singh Thakur is the Union Minister of Information & Broadcasting and Youth Affairs & Sports. He is a 4th term Member Parliament (Lok Sabha/Lower House) from Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh.
