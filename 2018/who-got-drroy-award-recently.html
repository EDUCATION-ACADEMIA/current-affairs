Who got Dr.Roy Award recently?

(A) ** Misra

(B) Naresh Kumar

(C) Vivek Kumar

(D) Suresh Yadav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -B.C Roy award is given for excellence in medical sciences. It was instituted in 1962 by medical council of India in memory of Dr. B.C. Roy.

Concept note-2: -B. C. Roy Award, the highest Indian award in the medical category. The award was instituted in 1976 by the Government of India under the aegis of the Medical Council of India. The award is named after Bidhan Chandra Roy, a medical doctor and a former chief minister of the Indian state of West Bengal.

Concept note-3: -The value of the award is Rs. 15, 000/-and a Medal. It has further been decided that services rendered towards leprosy, Blindness, Physically handicapped, relief during natural calamities, Community Health, Malaria, Family Planning and Nutrition will also fall under the category of Socio-Medical Relief.
