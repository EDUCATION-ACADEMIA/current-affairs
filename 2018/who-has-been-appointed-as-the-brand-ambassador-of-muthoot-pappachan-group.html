Who has been appointed as the brand ambassador of Muthoot Pappachan Group?

(A) Madhuri Dixit

(B) ** Vidya Balan

(C) Kareena Kapoor

(D) Alia Bhatt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Muthoot Pappachan Group appoints Vidya Balan as its brand ambassador | Mint.

Concept note-2: -Muthoot Finance-India’s No. 1 Most Trusted Financial Services Brand as per The Brand Trust Report 2022, and the country’s leading Gold Loan NBFC, has signed on the widely loved, multi-talented, highly awarded actress, Madhuri Dixit, as its brand ambassador.

Concept note-3: -Muthoot FinCorp Limited (MFL) is the flagship company of Muthoot Pappachan Group (popularly known as Muthoot Blue), a systemically important non-deposit taking non-banking financial company.

Concept note-4: -Driven with the commitment to society, the Group set up a Public Charitable Trust – Muthoot Pappachan Foundation, that has touched thousands of lives through its diverse programs. The CSR initiatives of Muthoot Blue revolve around the theme HEEL, covering Health, Education, Environment and Livelihood.
