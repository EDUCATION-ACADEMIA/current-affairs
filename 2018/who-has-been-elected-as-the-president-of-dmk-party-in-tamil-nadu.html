Who has been elected as the President of DMK party in Tamil Nadu?

(A) K.Anbazhagan

(B) Duraimurugan

(C) ** M.K.Stalin

(D) M.K.Alagiri

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Honorable Thiru R.N. Ravi, Governor of Tamil Nadu is the Constitutional Head of the state. Hon’ble Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu Thiru M.K.Stalin is the Head of the elected Government and heads the Council of Ministers.

Concept note-2: -The AIADMK swept to power in 1977, defeating the DMK in the assembly election. M.G.R. was sworn in as the 3rd chief minister of Tamil Nadu on 30 June 1977 and he remained in power until his death on 24 December 1987, winning consecutive assembly elections held in 1980 and 1984.
