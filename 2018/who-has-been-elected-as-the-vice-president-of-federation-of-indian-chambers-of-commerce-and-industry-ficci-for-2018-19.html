Who has been elected as the Vice President of Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI) for 2018-19?

(A) James Murdoch

(B) Sandip Somany

(C) Sangita Reddy

(D) ** Uday Shankar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Star India’s Chairman and CEO Uday Shankar has been elected Vice President of Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI) for 2018-19. He was recently appointed Star and Disney India’s Chairman and President of The Walt Disney Company Asia Pacific.

Concept note-2: -Mr Harsha Vardhan Agarwal, Vice Chairman and Managing Director, Emami Limited has joined FICCI leadership as Vice President.

Concept note-3: -Mr. Subhrakant [1], Managing Director, Indian Metals & Ferro Alloys Ltd has today taken over as FICCI President for 2022-2023, from Mr Sanjiv Mehta at the apex chamber’s 95th Annual Convention held in New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -Mr. Arun Chawla is Director General of the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FICCI), India’s apex business chamber representing industry and trade. Mr. Chawla is a post-graduate with a degree in Law from the University of Delhi.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Uday Shankar.
