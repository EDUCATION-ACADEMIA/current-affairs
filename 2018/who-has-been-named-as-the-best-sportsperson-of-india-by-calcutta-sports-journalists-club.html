Who has been named as the ‘Best Sportsperson of India’ by Calcutta Sports Journalists’ Club?

(A) ** Sunil Chhetri

(B) Suprajit Rakshit

(C) Koustav Chatterjee

(D) Ankit Mondal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sunil Chhetri.

Concept note-2: -Among the pioneers of sports journalism in India mention must be made of Kishore Bhimani17, K. Sundar Rajan18, Moti andi19, V. M. Balachandran (popularly known as Vimsy)20 and T. D. Partha.

Concept note-3: -Sports journalism focuses on reporting amateur and professional sporting news and events. Sports journalists work in all media, including print, television broadcasting and the internet.
