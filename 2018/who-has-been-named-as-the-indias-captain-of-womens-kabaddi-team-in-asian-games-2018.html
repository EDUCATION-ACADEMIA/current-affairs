Who has been named as the India’s captain of Women’s kabaddi team in Asian Games 2018?

(A) ** Payel Chowdhury

(B) Sakshi Kumari

(C) Shalini Pathak

(D) Ritu Negi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is option 4) Payel Chowdury. Payel chowdury was the Captain of the India n Women’s Kabbadi team. India women lost 24-27 to Iran in the final of women’s kabaddi in Jakarta. This is the first time since 1990 that India have not won a gold medal in kabaddi at the Asian Games.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution: Payel Chowdhury was the captain of the Indian women’s team that won silver at the 2018 Asian Games. She becomes the first Bengali to captain the Indian Women Kabaddi team.

Concept note-3: -Iran won the gold medal in Men’s Kabaddi at 2018 Asian game. It was hosted by Jakarta, Indonesia. In semi-finals, Iran defeated India with 27-18 and defeated South Korea in finals with 26-16.
