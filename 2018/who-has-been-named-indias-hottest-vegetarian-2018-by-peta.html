Who has been named India’s Hottest Vegetarian 2018 by PETA?

(A) Ranbir Kapoor

(B) ** Anushka Sharma

(C) Rajkummar Rao

(D) Alia Bhatt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s Hottest Vegetarians 2018: Kartik Aaryan and Anushka Sharma win in this year’s poll hosted by PETA India. Just like every year, People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA) have announced the India’s Hottest Vegetarians for the year of 2018.

Concept note-2: -Akshay Kumar and Bhumi Pednekar Are PETA India’s 2021 Most Beautiful Vegetarians. As 2021 is rapidly coming to a close, it’s time for PETA India to name its Most Beautiful Vegetarian Celebrities of the year – and actors Akshay Kumar and Bhumi Pednekar have won the coveted title.

Concept note-3: -For singing the praises of vegan eating, keeping animal skins out of her handbag line for Rheson, and taking many other actions for animals, actor Sonam Kapoor has been named PETA India’s Person of the Year for 2018.

Concept note-4: -Over the years, PETA has honored many inspiring stars with this award, including Billie Eilish, Sadie Sink, Chris Paul, Lizzo, Colin Kaepernick, Jessica Chastain, Benedict Cumberbatch, Alex Morgan, and Jhené Aiko.

Concept note-5: -Anushka Sharma won the PETA ‘Person of the Year’ award in 2017. India cricket team captain Virat Kohli was named People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA) India’s ‘Person of the Year for 2019.
