Who has been newly appointed as CEO of International Association of Athletics Federations (IAAF)?

(A) Olivier Gers

(B) ** Jon Ridgeon

(C) Sebastian Coe

(D) Svein Arne Hansen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sebastian Coe re-elected as IAAF chief During the 52nd IAAF Congress at Doha, Sebastian Coe from Britain has been re-elected as president of the International Association of Athletics Federations (IAAF) for a second term for 4 years. Coe is the former Olympic and world champion middle-distance runner.
