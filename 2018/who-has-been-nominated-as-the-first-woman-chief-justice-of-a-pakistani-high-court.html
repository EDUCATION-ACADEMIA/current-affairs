Who has been nominated as the first woman chief justice of a Pakistani high court?

(A) Rabia Qari

(B) Hina Jilani

(C) Asma Jahangir

(D) ** Tahira Safdar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ayesha A. Malik is the first female Supreme Court justice of Pakistan, marking a milestone in the country’s 74-year history. She was sworn in January 24.

Concept note-2: -DESK BRIEF: Justice Leila Seth was the first woman to be Chief Justice of a High Court. Her autobiography tell us this was no small feat. Justice Leila Seth was sitting in the Delhi High Court one day, when a large group of people flooded her courtroom.

Concept note-3: -Mrs. Justice Ayesha A. Malik, before her elevation to the Supreme Court, was serving as a Justice of the Lahore High Court since March 2012. Born in June 1966, her education was completed from schools in Paris, New York and London.
