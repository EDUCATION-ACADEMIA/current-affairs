Who has been officially nominated to be Pakistan’s PM?

(A) Sherry Rehman

(B) ** Imran Khan

(C) Shehbaz Sharif

(D) Nadeem Nusrat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 11th April 2022, Shehbaz Sharif was elected as the country’s 23rd Prime Minister. He won by majority 174 votes in the National Assembly of Pakistan.

Concept note-2: -Shahbaz Sharif was elected Prime Minister of Pakistan after the successful no-confidence motion against Imran Khan, his nomination was supported by all joint opposition parties who voted to remove the previous prime minister from office, while the recently ousted government party boycotted the elections.

Concept note-3: -In Pakistan, Shehbaz Sharif has become the 23rd prime minister of the country after taking an oath. He was administered the oath by Senate Chairman Sadiq Sanjarani. Earlier today, parliament elected the Opposition’s joint candidate Sharif as the country’s new prime minister.
