Who has been recently appointed as New MD and CEO of ‘Central Bank of India’?

(A) Mrutyunjay Mahapatra

(B) Padmaja Chandru

(C) Karnam Shekhar

(D) ** Pallav Mohapatra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr. Matam Venkata Rao is a Post Graduate in Agriculture from Sri Venkateshwara Agriculture College, Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Godwin Emefiele is the Governor of the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN).

Concept note-3: -Central government has announced the appointment of K Satyanarayana Raju as Managing Director and CEO of Canara Bank. His appointment is effective from February 7. Earlier, Raju served Canara Bank as executive director from March 2021. He replaced L V Prabhakar who demitted office on December 31, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Pradip Pranlal Khimani has been appointed as part-time non-official director on the Board of Central Bank of India for a period of three years from the date of notification i.e. December 21, 2021, or until further orders, whichever is earlier.
