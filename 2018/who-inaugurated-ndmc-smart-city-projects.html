Who inaugurated NDMC Smart City Projects?

(A) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(B) Shri Arun Jaitley

(C) Shri Vivek Sharma

(D) ** Shri Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PHOTO GALLERY-Inaugration of NDMC Smart City projects by Shri Rajnath Singh.

Concept note-2: -Project Implementation Status The NDMC constituted an SPV in July 2016 for the implementation of the smart city proposal and to ensure timely completion of projects.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched the Smart Cities Mission in India on June 25, 2015. The project aims to improve the quality of life for people living in India and drive economic growth.

Concept note-4: -The Smart City Delhi mission of NDMC is to “be the global benchmark for a capital city". Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi announced the commencement of the Smart City Delhi Mission in 2015, along with many other cities.

Concept note-5: -Indore will create history by implementing a fully digital addressing system, making it the first city in India to do so.
