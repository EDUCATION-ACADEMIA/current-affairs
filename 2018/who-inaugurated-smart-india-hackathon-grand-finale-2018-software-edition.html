Who inaugurated Smart India Hackathon Grand Finale 2018-Software Edition?

(A) ** Shri Prakash Javadekar

(B) Shri Arun jaitley

(C) Shri Ramesh Kumar

(D) Shri Suresh Gupta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The year 2017 saw the first edition of the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) being launched. Introduced by the Ministry of Education (MoE), SIH gives an opportunity to students (especially engineering students) to come up with innovative ideas and solutions to problems posed by corporates, PSUs, and NGOs.

Concept note-2: -After much deliberation, the judges are pleased to announce that BNY Mellon and Exactpro are this year’s Swift Hackathon 2022 winners.

Concept note-3: -Team Agrocon, comprising of Atul, Mahak Saxena, Bhuvan Sharma, Shubham, Preeti Lakshmi and team leader Yogendra Singh, along with mentor Ankita Sharma, won the first prize at the SIH’s grand finale held at the Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering, Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-4: -Heartiest Congratulations to Master Devansh Gaikwad for becoming one of the “Grand Finale Winners of SMART INDIA HACKATHON JUNIOR 2022” in the Robotics and Drones category.
