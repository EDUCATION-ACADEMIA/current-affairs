Who integrates its Mobile App DND 2.0 & MyCall With UMANG Platform?

(A) ** Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI)

(B) Income Tax Department

(C) Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL)

(D) National Highways Authority of India (NHAI)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) has integrated its Mobile Apps namely DND 2.0 & MyCall with UMANG Platform with the objective of protecting consumer’s interests. About: UMANG stands for “Unified Mobile Application for New-age Governance”.

Concept note-2: -UMANG (Unified Mobile Application for New-age Governance) is developed by Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) and National e-Governance Division (NeGD) to drive Mobile Governance in India.

Concept note-3: -UMANG (Unified Mobile Application for New-Age Governance) is one of the key initiatives under the Digital India program to develop a common, unified platform and mobile app to facilitate a single point of access to all government services.

Concept note-4: -Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) has launched new mobile apps such as TRAI MyCall App, TRAI MySpeed App, Do Not Disturb (DND 2.0) & new updated TRAI website. The TRAI MyCall is an Android application for Crowd sourced Voice Call Quality Monitoring.

Concept note-5: -UMANG App can be used to book cylinders online, request for refills, subsidies, mechanic services, surrender connections, etc. These services can be availed on HP Gas, Bharat Gas and Indane Gas. Pensioners can avail services offered by the Department of Pension & Pensioner’s Welfare.
