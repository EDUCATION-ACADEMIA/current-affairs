Who is appointed as as CEO Of Ayushman Bharat Mission?

(A) ** Shri Indu Bhushan

(B) Shri Ramesh Kumar

(C) Shri Suresh Kumar

(D) Shri Naresh Raj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indu Bhushan serves as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) and the National Health Authority (NHA), Government of India.

Concept note-2: -Dr Sharma was the first Director General of the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI). He was also appointed as the chairman of TRAI in 2015, where he served till 2020.

Concept note-3: -The CEO of the NHA functions as the Member Secretary of the Governing Board. The Governing Board comprises Chairperson and 11 members. Succeeding Indu Bhushan on 1 February 2021, Former TRAI chairman Ram Sevak Sharma is the current CEO of the NHA.
