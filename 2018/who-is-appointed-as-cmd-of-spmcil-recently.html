Who is appointed as CMD Of SPMCIL recently?

(A) ** Shri S Selvakumar

(B) Shri Naresh kumar

(C) Shri Ramesh Kumar

(D) Shri Vivek Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The government today appointed senior IAS officer S Selvakumar as Chairman and Managing Director (CMD) of Security Printing and Minting Corporation of India Ltd (SPMCIL). The state-owned SPMCIL produces banknotes, coins, postage stamps, non-judicial stamps and other official documents.

Concept note-2: -Ms. Tripti P. Ghosh is a Post Graduate in Economics from Delhi University. She belongs to 1994 batch of ICAS.

Concept note-3: -Important takeaways for all competitive exams: SPMCIL Chairman and Managing Director: Tripti Patra Ghosh; SPMCIL Founded: 10 February 2006.

Concept note-4: -The Key Managerial Personnel (KMP) at Security Printing And Minting Corporation Of India Limited India are Ajay Agarwal as Cfo(Kmp) and Sachin Agarwal as Company Secretary. Sunil Kumar Sinha, Tripti Patra Ghosh, Ram Kumar Chilukuri and 8 other members are presently associated as directors. Company No.
