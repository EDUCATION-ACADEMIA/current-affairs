Who is appointed as DG Of Sports Authority Of India?

(A) ** Neelam Kapur

(B) Siddhartha Upadhyay

(C) Jwala Gutta

(D) Nandini Varma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Sports Authority of India Director General Sandip Pradhan was on Thursday granted a tenure extension of more than two years in his present capacity by the Government of India.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Sports India. Former Union Ministers of Youth Affairs and Sports Rajyavardhan Rathore announced that the national sports body, Sports Authority of India (SAI), will now be known as ‘Sports India’.

Concept note-3: -Sandip Pradhan - Director General - Govt. of India | LinkedIn.

Concept note-4: -(a) General Body of Sports Authority of India (SAI), an autonomous organisation under Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports is constituted as per Memorandum of Association and Rules of SAI. The General Body comprises of 42 members out of which 26 are nominated for a period of three years.
