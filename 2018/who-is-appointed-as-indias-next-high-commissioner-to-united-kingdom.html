Who is appointed as India’s next High Commissioner to United Kingdom?

(A) ** Ruchi Ghanashyam

(B) Subrata bhattacharjee

(C) Sandeep Arya

(D) R. Ravindra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vikram Doraiswami appointed as next High Commissioner of India to UK.

Concept note-2: -Verma, 57, succeeds Acting High Commissioner Anshuman Gaur. Among his other postings, Verma has served in Indian missions in Hong Kong, China, Vietnam and Turkey. He has also served as the Consul General of India in Milan, Italy. Verma graduated from Patna University and went to IIT Delhi for Masters in Physics.

Concept note-3: -The building was conceived by Sir Atul Chatterjee, the first High Commissioner for India in London, and Herbert Baker.

Concept note-4: -These representatives are officers of the Indian Foreign Service (IFS), who are responsible for the execution of India’s foreign policy. In some nations, they are called ambassadors while in others they are known as high commissioners.
