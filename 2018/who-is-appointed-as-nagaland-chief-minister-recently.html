Who is appointed as Nagaland Chief Minister recently?

(A) ** Neiphiu Rio

(B) Hokishe Sema

(C) Vizol Angami

(D) John Bosco Jasokie

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Neiphiu Rio (born November 11, 1950) is an Indian politician.

Concept note-2: -The current incumbent is Neiphiu Rio of the Nationalist Democratic Progressive Party, in office since 8 March 2018.

Concept note-3: -The Democratic Alliance of Nagaland is a state level coalition of political parties in Nagaland. It headed the Nagaland government with the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).

Concept note-4: -Yanthungo Patton, also known as Y. Patton, is an Indian politician from Nagaland and the current Deputy Chief Minister (as a Home Minister) of the Indian state of Nagaland serving in the NDPP-BJP administration. He is the BJP MLA from Tyüi Assembly Constituency and was Home Minister in the NPF-led government.
