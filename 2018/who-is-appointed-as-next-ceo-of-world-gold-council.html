Who is appointed as next CEO of World Gold Council?

(A) ** David Tait

(B) Suresh Kumar

(C) Naresh Kumar

(D) Ankit Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -David Tait | CEO | World Gold Council.

Concept note-2: -The World Gold Council or the WGC is a nonprofit association of the world’s leading gold producers. A market development organization for the gold industry, the World Gold Council includes 33 members and many members are gold mining companies.

Concept note-3: -According to the latest report by the World Gold Council (WGC), India, which is the world’s largest consumer of gold, has the 11th largest gold reserve, with the current holding pegged at 607 tonnes.

Concept note-4: -The World Gold Council’s 34 Members are some of the world’s most forward-thinking gold mining companies.
