(B) P.K.Gupta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Billionaire Mukesh Ambani’s Reliance Industries Ltd (RIL) on Friday, November 4, said its board of directors has appointed K.V. Kamath as an independent director of the company for a term of five years with effect from the date he assumes office.

Concept note-2: -Reliance Industries has appointed K V Kamath as the Independent Director of the company for a period of five years.

Concept note-3: -Mukesh D. Ambani-Chairman and Managing Director-Reliance Industries Limited.

Concept note-4: -Manoj Modi is a Director at Reliance Jio and Reliance Retail. He is known as the right hand of business tycoon Mukesh Ambani.

Concept note-5: -Reliance Retail Limited is majorly in Manufacturing (Textiles) business from last 25 years and currently, company operations are amalgamated.
