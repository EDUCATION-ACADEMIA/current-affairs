He was appointed as the banks’s MD and CEO since October 15, 2018.

Concept note-3: -Sandeep Bakhshi is the Managing Director and CEO of ICICI Bank since October 15, 2018. Prior to his appointment as MD & CEO, he was a Wholetime Director and the Chief Operating Officer (COO) of the Bank.
