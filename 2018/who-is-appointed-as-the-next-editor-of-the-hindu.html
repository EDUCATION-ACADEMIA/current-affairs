Who is appointed as the next Editor of ‘The Hindu’?

(A) ** Suresh Nambath

(B) Naresh Kumar

(C) Dinesh Karthik

(D) Nithish Kant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Hindu’s new Group Digital Editor Sriram Srinivasan. The Hindu Group Publishing Pvt. Ltd. (THGPPL) publisher of The Hindu, The Hindu businessline, Frontline and Sportstar has appointed Sriram Srinivasan as Group Digital Editor effective 17 October 2022.

Concept note-2: -Frontline is a fortnightly English language magazine published by The Hindu Group of publications headquartered in Chennai, India. Vaishna Roy is the editor of the magazine.

Concept note-3: -Vaishna Roy. The Hindu Group is pleased to announce the appointment of Vaishna Roy as the Editor, Frontline, with effect from May 1, 2022.
