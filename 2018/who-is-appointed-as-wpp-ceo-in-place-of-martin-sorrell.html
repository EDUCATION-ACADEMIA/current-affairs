Who is appointed as WPP CEO In Place Of Martin Sorrell?

(A) ** Mark Read

(B) Williams John

(C) J. Walter Thompson,

(D) Millward Brown

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mark Read appointed WPP CEO in place of Martin Sorrell.

Concept note-2: -Any officer of the company may be appointed/ designated as CEO of the Company. Further, the CEO who is not a director may be appointed by the Board of Directors. He need not be appointed by the Shareholders of the Company nor his appointment is subject to shareholders’ approval, unless he is a Director of the Company.

Concept note-3: -Mark Daniel Read (born in Worcester Park, London on 7 November 1978) is an English singer/songwriter, best known as member of the boy band A1 from 1998 to 2002, and 2009 to the present.
