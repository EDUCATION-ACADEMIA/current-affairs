Who is awarded as the UEFA Player of the Year (2017-2018)?

(A) ** Luka Modric

(B) Cristiano Ronaldo

(C) Franck Ribery

(D) Lionel Messi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Luka Modrić has won the 2017/18 UEFA Men’s Player of the Year award.

Concept note-2: -The Best FIFA Men’s Player The 24 candidates were announced on 17 August. The three finalists were announced on 22 September 2017. Cristiano Ronaldo won the award with over 43% of the vote.

Concept note-3: -Real Madrid and France forward Karim Benzema has been named 2021/22 UEFA Men’s Player of the Year.

Concept note-4: -Karim Benzema (2021-22) Benzema won the UEFA men’s player of the year award in 2022, becoming the second French player to win it after Ribery.
