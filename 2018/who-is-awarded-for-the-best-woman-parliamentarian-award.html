(A) ** Smt.Kanimozhi

(B) Smt.Basava Rajeswari

(C) Smt. Maneka Sanjay

(D) Smt. Mayawati Kumari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Outstanding Parliamentarian Award is an award given by the Indian Parliamentary Group to an outstanding sitting Member of the Indian Parliament for overall contribution in the Indian Parliament. It was instituted in 1995 by Shivraj Patil, who was the Speaker of Lok Sabha from 1991 to 1996.
