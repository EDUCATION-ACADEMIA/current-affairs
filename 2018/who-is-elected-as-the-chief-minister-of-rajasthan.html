Who is elected as the Chief Minister of Rajasthan?

(A) Vasundhara Raje

(B) ** Ashok Gehlot

(C) Bhairon Singh Shekhawat

(D) Hira Lal Devpura

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ashok Gehlot (born 3 May 1951) is an Indian politician serving as the current Chief Minister of Rajasthan.

Concept note-2: -Incumbent After securing majority in 2018 assembly election, Ashok Gehlot of the Indian National Congress assumed office on 17 December 2018.

Concept note-3: -Shri Ashok Gehlot, Chief Minister, Rajasthan.

Concept note-4: -The Chief Minister is appointed by the Governor who also appoints other ministers on the advice of the Chief Minister. The Council of Ministers is collectively responsible to legislative assembly of the State.
