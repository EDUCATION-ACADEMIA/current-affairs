(A) Anil Kapoor

(B) Shahid Kapoor

(C) Salman Khan

(D) ** Shah Rukh Khan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shah Rukh Khan honoured with Crystal Award at World Economic Forum.

Concept note-2: -Deepika Padukone became the only Indian actress to receive WEF’s Crystal Award.

Concept note-3: -Lin receives the 2023 Crystal Award for her extraordinary creative talent in combining science, art and architecture and her exemplary leadership in the promotion of nature and environment.
