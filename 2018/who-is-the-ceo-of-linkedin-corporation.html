Who is the CEO of LinkedIn Corporation?

(A) Stan Meresman

(B) ** Jeffrey Weiner

(C) Michael Moritz

(D) Leslie Kilgore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ryan Roslansky is the CEO of LinkedIn, the world’s largest and most powerful network of professionals. Ryan joined the company in May 2009 and has since held leadership roles in every part of LinkedIn’s business.

Concept note-2: -LinkedIn Corp. Chief Executive Officer Jeff Weiner is stepping down after 11 years in the role and will become executive chairman. Ryan Roslansky, currently senior vice president of product, will become CEO effective June 1, the Microsoft Corp. company said Wednesday in a statement.

Concept note-3: -In 2016 LinkedIn was acquired by Microsoft for approximately $26 billion. The following year it reported more than 500 million members in about 200 countries.

Concept note-4: -LinkedIn is worth more than $26 billion: Initial returns are most important for any acquisition.
