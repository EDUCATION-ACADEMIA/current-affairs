Who is the Chairman of TRAI?

(A) Sunil Bajpai

(B) ** R.S.Sharma

(C) Sunil Kumar Gupta

(D) U.K.Srivastava

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Officers are selected from the Indian Telecommunications Service and the Indian Administrative Service (IAS). The current chairperson of TRAI is PD Vaghela, an IAS officer of the Gujarat cadre, batch of 1986.

Concept note-2: -The chairman of Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI), PD Vaghela, on Tuesday (December 13) said that India will be the first country in the world to auction spectrum for satellite communications.

Concept note-3: -Mr. Mats Granryd, Director General, GSMA alongwith his team visited TRAI and held discussions with TRAI delegation led by Dr. P.D. Vaghela, Chairman-TRAI.
