Who is the current president of International Kabaddi Federation?

(A) Vinod Tiwari

(B) ** Janardan Singh Gehlot

(C) Yeong Hak Yoon

(D) Manjeet Chillar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The founder and current president is Ashish Pachori from India.

Concept note-2: -Vinod Kumar Tiwari has been elected as the new President of the International Kabaddi Federation (IKF).

Concept note-3: -The International Kabaddi Federation was founded in 2004. It is headquartered in Jaipur.

Concept note-4: -Posted by : Super User Mridul Gehlot and Dinesh Patel were today elected unanimously president and general secretary of Amateur Kabaddi Federation of India (AKFI).

Concept note-5: -Team Delhi won the Kabaddi League 2022. They beat 3-time winners Patna by a score line of 37-36 in a closely-fought final.
