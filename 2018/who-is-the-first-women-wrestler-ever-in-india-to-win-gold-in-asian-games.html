Who is the first women wrestler ever in India to win gold in Asian games?

(A) Yuki Irie

(B) ** Vinesh Phogat

(C) Pooja Dhanda

(D) Sakshi Malik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vinesh Phogat (born 25 August 1994) is an Indian wrestler. She became the first Indian woman wrestler to win gold in both Commonwealth and Asian Games. She is the only Indian woman wrestler to win multiple medals at the World Wrestling Championships.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Kamaljit Sandhu. Kamaljeet Sandhu is a female Indian athlete who won a gold medal at the 1970 Bangkok Asian Games in the 400 meter race. She ran the distance in 57.3 seconds. She is the first Indian woman athlete to win an individual gold medal at the Asian Games.

Concept note-3: -Vinesh Phogat scripts history; first Indian woman wrestler to win two medals at the World Championships.
