Who is the highest-paid actress in the world in 2018, according to the Forbes magazine list?

(A) ** Scarlett Johansson

(B) Angelina Jolie

(C) Jennifer Aniston

(D) Priyanka Chopra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Helping to save the world with The Avengers has helped Scarlett Johansson become the best-paid actress in the world, according to Forbes magazine. Johansson, who plays Black Widow in the Avengers films, earned $40.5m (£31.9m) in the past year, according to the magazine’s annual estimates.

Concept note-2: -This statistic displays a ranking of Hollywood’s highest-paid TV actresses between June 2017 and June 2018. In that period, Sofia Vergara, known for her role on ‘Modern Family’, was the highest earner, with an annual income of 42.5 million U.S. dollars.

Concept note-3: -1. Sofía Vergara is once again the highest-paid actress: She made her $43 million from endorsements, Modern Family and as a new $10-million-per-season judge on America’s Got Talent.

Concept note-4: -Dwayne Johnson, also known as the Rock, tops the Forbes list of the world’s ten highest-paid actors, collecting $89.4 million between June 1, 2018, and June 1, 2019. “It has to be audience first.

Concept note-5: -The best-paid actress worldwide was Sofia Vergara in 2020, with an annual income of 43 million U.S. dollars. Angelina Jolie came second. She earned roughly 35.5 million U.S. dollars with for example her leading role in “The Eternals".
