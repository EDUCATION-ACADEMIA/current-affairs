Who is the Lieutenant Governor of Puducherry?

(A) Ram Naik

(B) Tathagata Roy

(C) ** Kiran Bedi

(D) Banwarilal Purohit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(Smt.) Tamilisai Soundararajan took charge as Governor of Telangana on 8th September 2019 and happens to be the 1st woman to hold this Office. She assumes the office of the Lt. Governor of Puducherry on 18th February 2021.

Concept note-2: -Incumbent Since 1963, Puducherry has had 10 chief ministers. The longest-serving and current chief minister, N. Rangasamy from All India N.R.

Concept note-3: -The lieutenant governor is appointed by the President of India for a term of five years, and holds office at the President’s pleasure.
