Who is the Minister of Shipping and Water resources?

(A) Rajnath Singh

(B) ** Nitin Gadkari

(C) Arun Jaitley

(D) Ravi Shankar Prasad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Today, Hon’ble Union Minister MoPSW Shri sarbanandsonwal will inaugurate ‘Bharat Pravah’ in New Delhi. It will showcase the socio-cultural & economic significance of rivers & ports in everyday life and also highlight the importance of Ports, Shipping & Waterways in the nation.

Concept note-2: -There are two Department viz. Department of Shipping and Department of Road Transport & Highways. Further, in 2020 the Ministry of Shipping was renamed as Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways.

Concept note-3: -Union Minister for Ports, Shipping and Waterways and Ayush Shri Sarbananda Sonowal inaugurated The National Logistics Portal (marine) in New Delhi today.

Concept note-4: -Hon’ble Union Minister of Ayush Sarbananda Sonowal visited All India Institute of Ayurveda.
