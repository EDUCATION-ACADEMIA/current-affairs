Who is the most followed world leader on Twitter?

(A) Theresa May

(B) Narendra Modi

(C) ** Donald Trump

(D) Pope Francis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1. Barack Obama (133.2m followers)

Concept note-2: -Twitter accounts with the most followers worldwide 2023. As of January 2023, former U.S. President Barack Obama was the most followed person on Twitter. His account was followed by over 133 million people. Second-ranked was Twitter CEO Elon Musk with over 127 million followers.

Concept note-3: -Who has the most followers on Twitter in India? Narendra Modi with 86.24 M has the most Twitter followers for India.
