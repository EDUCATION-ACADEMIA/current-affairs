Who is the present governor of Reserve Bank of India?

(A) Shri Rajesh Kumar

(B) Shri Suresh Rajan

(C) Shri Vijay Kumar

(D) ** Shri Urjit Patel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Das is a retired 1980 batch Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer of Tamil Nadu cadre. Delhi University (BA, MA); Utkal University (D.Litt.)

Concept note-2: -Shaktikanta Das is the present RBI Governor, appointed for a period of three years.

Concept note-3: -Shri Shaktikanta Das, IAS Retd., former Secretary, Department of Revenue and Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance, Government of India assumed charge as the 25th Governor of the Reserve Bank of India effective December 12, 2018.

Concept note-4: -Recently, Shaktikanta Das has been appointed the Governor of RBI for a period of three years. The Governor of the Reserve Bank of India is the Chief Executive Officer of the Central Bank of India and the Ex-officio Chairman of its Central Board of Directors.
