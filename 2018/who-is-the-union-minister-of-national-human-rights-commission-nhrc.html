Who is the union minister of National Human Rights Commission (NHRC)?

(A) ** Shri Jagat Prakash Nadda.

(B) Shri Naresh iyer

(C) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(D) Shri Vignesh Rajan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr. Devendra Kumar Singh joined the National Human Rights Commission, NHRC, India as its new Secretary General on 2nd May, 2022. He is a Kerala Cadre Indian Administrative Service, IAS officer, of 1989 batch. Prior to joining the Commission, he was holding the charge of Secretary, Union Ministry of Cooperation.

Concept note-2: -NHRC Chairperson, Justice Shri A.K Mishra address at 30th National Human Rights Commission (NHRC) Foundation Day (12.10. 2022) programme | National Human Rights Commission India.

Concept note-3: -Description: NHRC comprises a chairman and four members. The chairman is always the retired chief justice of India. Justice H.L. Dattu is the current chairman of the NHRC (National Human rights commission).

Concept note-4: -Answer: The National Human Rights Commission (NHRC) was formed to protect human rights at the national level. The NHRC was formed in 1993 under the ‘Protection of Human Rights Act. ‘ The commission has five members, including a chairman who is a retired Chief Justice of India.
