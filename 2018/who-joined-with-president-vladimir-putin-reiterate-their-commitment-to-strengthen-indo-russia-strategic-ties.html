Who joined with President Vladimir Putin reiterate their commitment to strengthen Indo-Russia strategic ties?

(A) ** Shri Narendra Modi

(B) Shri Arun Jaitley

(C) Shri Vivek Chopra

(D) Shri Anand Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Traditionally, the Indo-Russian strategic partnership has been built on five major components: politics, defence, civil nuclear energy, anti-terrorism co-operation, as well as the advancement of and exploration of outer space travel.

Concept note-2: -Bilateral ties with Russia are a key pillar of India’s foreign policy. India sees Russia as a longstanding and time-tested friend that has played a significant role in its economic development and security.

Concept note-3: -During the Cold War, India and the Soviet Union had a strong strategic, military, economic and diplomatic relationship. After the Dissolution of the Soviet Union, Russia inherited its close relationship with India which resulted in both nations sharing a Special Strategic Relation.
