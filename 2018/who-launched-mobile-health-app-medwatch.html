Who launched Mobile Health App ‘MedWatch’?

(A) ** Indian Air Force

(B) Indian Navy

(C) Indian Army

(D) Indian Police

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The MedWatch mobile health app is an Indian Air Force initiative and a small contribution to our citizen.

Concept note-2: -The ‘MedWatch’ was launched on 8 October on the occasion of IAF’s 86th anniversary and was conceived by the doctors of IAF and developed in-house by Directorate of Information Technology (DIT) “with Zero financial outlay".

Concept note-3: -’MedWatch’, the first mobile health app in the three Armed Services, was launched by Air Chief Marshal BS Dhanoa PVSM AVSM YSM VM ADC, Chief of the Air Staff, during the Air Force Commanders’ Conference at New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution: Recently Indian Air Force launched an innovative mobile health App named MedWatch.
