Who Launched Three Key Projects In Arunachal Pradesh?

(A) ** Shri Narendra Modi

(B) Shri Prakash Javadekar

(C) Shri Prakash kumar

(D) Shri Jitin singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Arunachal Pradesh became a full-fledged State on February 20, 1987. Till 1972, it was known as the North-East Frontier Agency (NEFA).

Concept note-2: -The North-East Frontier Agency was renamed Arunachal Pradesh by Bibhabasu Das Shastri, the Director of Research and K.A.A. Raja, the Chief Commissioner of Arunachal Pradesh on 20 January 1972, and it became a union territory. Arunachal Pradesh became a state on 20 February 1987.

Concept note-3: -Formerly known as the North East Frontier Agency (from the British colonial era), the area was part of Assam until it was made the Indian union territory of Arunachal Pradesh in 1972, and in 1987 it became an Indian state.
