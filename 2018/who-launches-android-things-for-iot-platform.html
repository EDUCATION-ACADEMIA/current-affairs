Who launches Android Things for IoT platform?

(A) ** Google

(B) Samsung

(C) Apple

(D) Verizon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Google has launched Android Things, a new comprehensive Internet of Things (IoT) platform to make it quicker and easier to build IoT products using Android APIs.

Concept note-2: -Android Things is a deprecated Android-based embedded operating system platform by Google, announced at Google I/O 2015, and launched in 2018.

Concept note-3: -Android Things is a ‘managed Operating system ( OS)’ , developed for IoT devices like smart locks, smart thermostats & more. It provides the necessary software and hardware frameworks, required to develop IoT enabled products at scale.

Concept note-4: -IoT devices are among the devices being built on the Android OS. The Android environment acts as the front end for IoT devices. One reason IoT developers prefer Android is that it is inexpensive.
