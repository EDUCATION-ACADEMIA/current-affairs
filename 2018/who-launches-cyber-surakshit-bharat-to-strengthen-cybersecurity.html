Who Launches Cyber Surakshit Bharat to Strengthen Cybersecurity?

(A) ** MEITY

(B) Ministry of External Affairs

(C) Ministry of Defence

(D) Ministry of Finance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It was launched in 2018 by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY).

Concept note-2: -The cyber Surakshit Bharat initiative launched took place on the 19th of January 2018 in Delhi.

Concept note-3: -The objective of the programme is to educate & enable the Chief Information Security Officers (CISO) & broader IT community to address the challenges of cyber security: Create awareness on the emerging landscape of cyber threats.

Concept note-4: -NeGD, MeitY organises 30th Batch of Chief Information Security Officers’ (CISOs) Deep Dive Training Programme under Cyber Surakshit Bharat Initiative.

Concept note-5: -More specifically, the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) defends US infrastructure against cyber threats. As a part of the Department of Homeland Security, CISA is responsible for protecting federal networks and critical infrastructure from attacks.
