Who makes her way in the semi final of All England Championship?

(A) ** PV Sindhu

(B) Sania Mirza

(C) Saina Nehwal

(D) Dinesh karthik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An Seyoung defeats Tai Tzu Ying at the All England semi-final. An Seyoung is into the final of the 2022 All England Open after a 21-19, 21-13 victory over World No. 1 Tai Tzu Ying. An will playing for her first All England title in Birmingham as Tai bows out.

Concept note-2: -Japan’s Yamaguchi Akane is All England women’s singles champion after a 21-15, 21-15 victory over South Korea’s An Seyoung in 43 minutes.

Concept note-3: -All-England Open 2022 Schedule: Semi-final-matches will start at 3:30 PM IST on March 19. The final match will start at 3:30 PM IST on March 20.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Prakash Padukone. On March 23, 1980, Prakash Padukone became the first Indian to win the All England Badminton Championships. Padukone clashed with Indonesia’s Liem Swie King in the summit clash at the Wembley Stadium.
