Who presents the Golden Globes awards?

(A) The Society for Cinema and Media Studies

(B) ** The Hollywood Foreign Press Association

(C) The International Federation of Film Critics

(D) Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Golden Globe Award, any of the awards presented annually by the Hollywood Foreign Press Association (HFPA) in recognition of outstanding achievement in motion pictures and television during the previous year.

Concept note-2: -Golden Globes 2023 Presenters Include Jenna Ortega, Jennifer Coolidge, Ana de Armas, and More. The beleaguered awards show will return to NBC on January 11, with Jerrod Carmichael hosting.

Concept note-3: -The biggest Golden Globe acting winners present a well-disputed ranking. Meryl Streep is on top of the TV and movie stars list and has already won eight times and been nominated 32, with movies like “The Devil Wears Prada” and “The Iron Lady".

Concept note-4: -No longer a nonprofit group of foreign entertainment journalists, the Golden Globes Assn. is now under the Eldridge umbrella, and the 96 members (including those with emeritus status) of the newly reconstituted for-profit are its paid employees.
