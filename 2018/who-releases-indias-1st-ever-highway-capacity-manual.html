Who Releases India’s 1st Ever Highway Capacity Manual?

(A) Shri Arun jaitley

(B) ** Shri Nitin Gadkari

(C) Shri Vignesh Gupta

(D) Shri Naresh Gupta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nitin Gadkari releases India’s first ever Highway Capacity Manual.

Concept note-2: -Roads have been existing in India for the last 5000 years. In early stages of Indian history, Ashoka and Chandragupta made efforts to construct roads. But the real progress was made during the Mughal period.

Concept note-3: -The first serious attempt was made in 1943. The First Road Development Plan (Nagpur Plan) was formulated in that year. This plan could not be implemented due to lack of coordination among the princely states and British India.

Concept note-4: -Chandragupta Maurya, the founder of the ancient Indian Maurya Empire, built this highway along an ancient route called Uttarapatha in the 3rd century BCE, extending it from the mouth of the Ganges to the north-western frontier of the Empire.
