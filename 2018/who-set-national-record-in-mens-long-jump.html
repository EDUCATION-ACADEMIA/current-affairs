Who set national record in men’s long jump?

(A) Ankit Sharma

(B) ** Sreeshankar

(C) Muralikumar Gavit

(D) Abhishek Pal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There was Jesse Owens’ eight-meter jump in Ann Arbor in 1935. That world record-and to be precise, it was 8.13 meters (26 feet, 8 1/16 inches)-lasted 25 years, until Ralph Boston broke it, and then broke it, and then broke it again.

Concept note-2: -Murali Sreeshankar (born 27 March 1999) is an Indian athlete who competes in the long jump event. He holds the national record of 8.36 metres set in 2022. Government Victoria College (B. Sc.)
