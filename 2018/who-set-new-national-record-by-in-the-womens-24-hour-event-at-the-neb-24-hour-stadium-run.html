Who set new national record by in the women’s 24-hour event at the NEB 24 Hour Stadium Run?

(A) Meenal Kotak

(B) Hema Saini

(C) Aparna Choudhary

(D) ** Apoorva Chaudhary

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The marathon will comprise of four independent races, including the full marathon of 42.2 kilometres, which will begin at 4:00 am; the half marathon of 21.09 kilometres, which will begin at 6:15 am, a special 10 kilometres run for a cause, which will begin at 07:45 am and the 5 kilometre, which will begin at 08.15 am.

Concept note-2: -The Chennai Marathon is an annual marathon race held in Chennai, India. The race is organised by the Chennai Runners Association and is typically held in late November or early December. The race has both a full marathon and a half marathon distance.

Concept note-3: -For the Full and Half Marathon, the minimum age to participate is 18 years. For the 5K Fun Run, all minors must be accompanied by adults.
