Who signed agreement MoU for Development of Logistics Sector recently?

(A) ** CII

(B) NIA

(C) CBI

(D) NTPC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The road, railway, and shipping ministries recently signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to develop Multi Modal Logistics Parks (MMLP). The agreement was signed under Bharatmala Pariyojana.

Concept note-2: -MMLP is officially defined as a freight-handling facility with a minimum area of 100 acres (40.5 hectares), with various modes of transport access, mechanized warehouses, specialized storage solutions such as cold storage, facilities for mechanized material handling and inter-modal transfer container terminals.
