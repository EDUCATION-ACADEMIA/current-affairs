Who Smashes National Record In High Jump in India recently?

(A) Anil Kumar Prakash

(B) Dharambir Singh

(C) Surendra Singh

(D) ** Tejaswin Shankar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Out to become a cricketer, Tejaswin Shankar has emerged as India’s best high jumper. He holds the national record in India and is now a Commonwealth Games medal winner.

Concept note-2: -Jeswin Aldrin won the men’s long jump gold medal at the National Games 2022 with a meet record leap of 8.26m, which also helped him qualify for the 2023 World Athletics Championships in Budapest, Hungary.

Concept note-3: -The world record of 2.45 metres (8.04 ft) by Cuban Javier Sotomayor in 1993 has never been surpassed.
