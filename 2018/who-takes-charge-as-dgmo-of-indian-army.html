Who takes charge as DGMO Of Indian Army?

(A) ** Anil Chauhan

(B) Suresh Yadav

(C) Kumar Sinha

(D) Vijay Chopra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi (20.04. 2022): Lt Gen Manoj Kumar Katiyar will be taking over as the next Director-General of Military Operations (DGMO) of the Indian Army from May 1, 2022. He is currently the General Officer Commanding of 1 Corps, a strike formation responsible for offensive operations against Eastern and Western theatres.

Concept note-2: -DIRECTOR GENERAL MEDICAL SERVICES (ARMY) & SR COL COMDT Lt Gen Daljit Singh, VSM, DGMS (Army) has been appointed as Sr Colonel Commandant of Army Medical Corps on 26 Nov 2021.

Concept note-3: -Lt General Manoj Kumar Katiyar has been appointed as the next Director-General of Military Operations (DGMO). He will be taking over the new office on May 1, 2022. He is presently the General Officer Commanding of 1 Corps, a strike formation responsible for offensive operations against both Pakistan and China.
