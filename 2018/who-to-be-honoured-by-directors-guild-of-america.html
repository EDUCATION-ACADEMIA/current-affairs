Who to be Honoured By Directors Guild Of America?

(A) ** Ang Lee

(B) Antonio Banderas

(C) Benicio Del Toro

(D) Bruce Lee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Joining the DGA The DGA does not provide applications for membership upon request. Your membership in the DGA is subject to approval by your category Council. Applications for membership are submitted to the appropriate Council upon submission of a completed application form and payment of the requisite initiation fee.

Concept note-2: -The DGA membership is represented by six councils of elected officials. Councils are designated by their specific categories: Directors; Assistant Directors and Unit Production Managers; Associate Directors, Stage Managers and Production Associates; and by geographic area.
