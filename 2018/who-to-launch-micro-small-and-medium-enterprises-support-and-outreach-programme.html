Who to launch Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Support and Outreach Programme?

(A) ** Shri Narendra Modi

(B) Shri Ramesh Sharma

(C) Shri Dinesh Karthick

(D) Shri Arun Jaitley

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today launched a historic support and outreach programme for the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) sector. As part of this programme, the Prime Minister unveiled 12 key initiatives which will help the growth, expansion and facilitation of MSMEs across the country.

Concept note-2: -Union Minister for MSME Shri Narayan Rane today launched the MSME Innovative Scheme (Incubation, Design and IPR) along with the MSME IDEA HACKATHON 2022.

Concept note-3: -National Small Industries Corporation Limited (NSIC) (NSIC) is an ISO 9001-2015 certified Government of India Enterprise under Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME). NSIC has been working to promote aid and foster the growth of micro, small and medium enterprises in the country.

Concept note-4: -MSME was first introduced by the MSMED (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises Development) Act of 2006.
