Who was reinstated as Prime MInister of Sri Lanka?

(A) Maithripala Sirisena

(B) ** Ranil Wickremesinghe

(C) Mahinda Rajapaksa

(D) Karu Jayasuriya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mahinda Rajapaksa Appointed by Gotabaya Rajapaksa, following the resignation of Ranil Wickremesinghe after the 2019 Sri Lankan presidential election and was re-appointed following the 2020 Sri Lankan parliamentary election.

Concept note-2: -The SLPP, led by Mahinda, registered a landslide victory in the general election, securing two-thirds majority in Parliament needed to amend the Constitution to further consolidate the powerful Rajapaksa family’s grip on power.

Concept note-3: -Sirimavo Ratwatte Dias Bandaranaike (17 April 1916 – 10 October 2000) was a Sri Lankan politician. She served as prime minister three times and was the leader of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party. She was the first female to be elected head of government in the world.
