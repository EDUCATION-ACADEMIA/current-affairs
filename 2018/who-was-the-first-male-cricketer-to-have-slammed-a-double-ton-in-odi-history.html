Who was the first male cricketer to have slammed a double ton in ODI history?

(A) ** Sachin Tendulkar

(B) Chris Gayle

(C) Martin Guptill

(D) Virender Sehwag

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first man in ODI history to score a double century, Sachin Tendulkar smashed an unforgettable ODI double against South Africa in Gwalior.

Concept note-2: -Out of total 10 double hundreds scored in international ODI men’s cricket, seven double centuries are scored by Indian batsmen. Sachin Tendulkar: India’s master blaster Sachin Tendulkar was the first men to hit the double century in an ODI against South Africa in Gwalior in 2010. He scored 200* runs in 147 balls.

Concept note-3: -From Sachin Tendulkar, Chris Gayle to Shubman Gill, here we list the cricketers who’ve scored a double century in the ODI format.

Concept note-4: -In November 2014, India’s Rohit Sharma broke the record for the highest individual score in a One Day International cricket match. The opener scored an astonishing 264 runs of 173 balls against Sri Lanka in Kolkata.
