Who will be India’s Republic Day chief guest in 2019?

(A) Sudan President Omar al-Bashir

(B) Mauritius President Ameenah Gurib

(C) Egypt President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi

(D) ** South Africa president Cyril Ramaphosa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Egypt has also been invited as a ‘Guest Country’ during India’s Presidency of G-20 in 2022-23. AIR correspondent reports that President Sisi will be accorded a ceremonial welcome at the Rashtrapati Bhavan on 25th January.

Concept note-2: -Republic Day 2023 Chief Guest This year, Egyptian President Abdel-Fattah el-Sisi is the chief guest of Republic Day celebrations.

Concept note-3: -The 73rd republic day 2022 parades in Delhi were held on January 26, 2022. The republic day 2022 chief guest for this event was the President of India Ram Nath Kovind and the Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi. The 73rd Republic day 2022 parades have been a part of the national celebrations in India since 1950.
