Who wins “Sportsperson Of The Year award for 2017" by the Sports Illustrated India magazine?

(A) Yuki Bhambri

(B) Manav Vikas Thakkar

(C) ** Kidambi Srikanth

(D) Virat Kohli

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Kidambi Srikanth, the 2018 Gold Coast Commonwealth Games gold medalist shuttler, has been chosen as Sports Illustrated’s Sportsperson of the Year award for 2017 in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -At the Turf 2022 and India Sports Awards of Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FICCI), former Ranji cricketer Sarkar Talwar was honoured with the Lifetime Achievement of the Year award.

Concept note-3: -Curry, who also shared the award with the 2017–18 Warriors, joins LeBron James, Tom Brady and Tiger Woods as the only multiple-time winners. We salute him this year not just for what he did, but for how he did it.
