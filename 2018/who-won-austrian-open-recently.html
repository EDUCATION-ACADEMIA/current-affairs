(A) ** Parupalli Kashyap

(B) Anthony Wilding

(C) Norman Brooke

(D) Marin Cilic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -American Serena Williams is a seven-time champion, which is an Open Era record. Naomi Osaka is a two-time champion. Ashleigh Barty won the title in 2022, becoming the first Australian woman to win the title in 44 years.

Concept note-2: -2022 Australian of the Year and disability advocate Dylan Alcott was the last Australian to win the men’s quad singles, taking out the title seven times in a row between 2015-2021.

Concept note-3: -As of 2023, Serbian tennis legend Novak Djokovic had won the most Australian Open men’s singles titles, with a total of 10. Meanwhile, Roger Federer, who retired in 2022, won six Australian Opens during his career.
