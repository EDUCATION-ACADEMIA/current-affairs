Who won Best Actress Award in Indian International Film Awards (IIFA), held in Bangkok?

(A) Zaira Wasim

(B) Alia Bhatt

(C) Vidya

(D) ** Sridevi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Best Actress: Sridevi (Mom) Best Film: Tumhari Sulu. Best Director: Saket Chaudhary (Hindi Medium) Best Debut Director: Konkona Sensrama (A Death in the Gunj)

Concept note-2: -Vicky Kaushal and Kriti Sanon won the Best Actor and Best Actress awards for their role in Sardar Udham and Mimi, respectively.

Concept note-3: -Aparna Balamurali has been adjudged Best Actress for her role alongside Suriya in Soorarai Pottru, while Biju Menon has bagged the Best Supporting Actor award for Malayalam film Ayyappanum Koshiyam.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Vicky Kaushal. The 22nd International Indian Film Academy Awards, also known as the IIFA, was held on 3–4 June 2022.

Concept note-5: -GUESTS AT IIFA 2022 Mithun Chakraborty, Boney Kapoor, Madhuri Dixit, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, Lara Dutta, Tamannaah Bhatia, Nargis Fakhri, Bobby Deol, Urvashi Rautela, Arjun Rampal, Sanya Malhotra and others, will attend IIFA 2022.
