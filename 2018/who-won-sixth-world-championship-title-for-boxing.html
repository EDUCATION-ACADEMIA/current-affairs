Who won Sixth World Championship Title for Boxing?

(A) ** Mary Kom

(B) Katie Taylor

(C) Yazmin Rivas

(D) Maribel Ramirez

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A remarkable achievement for the mother of three, whose last appearance in a final at the Worlds was in 2010 – a bout she won.

Concept note-2: -India’s six-time gold medallist Mary Kom pulls out of 2023 World Boxing Championships with injury. India’s Mary Kom, the most successful boxer in the history of the Women’s World Championships with six titles, has pulled out of this year’s event due to injury.

Concept note-3: -Manny Pacquiao has also won world titles in six different categories. On November 13, 2010, Pacquiao won his eighth boxing championship by defeating Antonio Margarito, making him the only professional boxer in history to win eight titles in different weight classes.

Concept note-4: -Manny Pacquiao is the first boxer to win world titles in seven different weight divisions.

Concept note-5: -Mary Kom was the only Indian to win six gold medals in world boxing championships.
