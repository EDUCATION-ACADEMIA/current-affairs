Who won the Japanese Formula One Grand Prix?

(A) ** Lewis Hamilton

(B) V. Bottas

(C) M. Verstappen

(D) K. Raikkonen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Max Verstappen wins Japanese GP and world title after Leclerc handed penalty – as it happened. Verstappen wins F1 title! Verstappen wins the Japanese GP!

Concept note-2: -Max Verstappen claimed victory in the Japanese Grand Prix, and took the 2022 world title with it, after a dramatic late penalty for Charles Leclerc put the championship beyond doubt.

Concept note-3: -In the entire history of Formula 1, no Japanese driver has ever won a World Championship Grand Prix.

Concept note-4: -Did the real Niki Lauda quit the 1976 Japanese Grand Prix because of the rain? Yes, but it wasn’t just because he felt that the rain-soaked track was unsafe. To complicate things, Lauda’s tear ducts had been damaged by fire during the horrific crash at the German Grand Prix earlier in the season.

Concept note-5: -The win meant that Verstappen took the Championship title, leading Pérez by 113 points, with 112 points still available.
