Whom to launch Amma Two-wheeler Scheme in Chennai?

(A) ** Shri Narendra Modi

(B) Shri Ramesh Kumar

(C) Shri Arun Jaitley

(D) Shri Dinesh Karthik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Amma Two-wheeler is a scheme that will empower the working women in Tamil Nadu State. Also, it will enable them to purchase women-friendly two-wheelers to go to their enterprises, places of employment, engagements, etc.

Concept note-2: -Benefits of Amma Two-Wheeler Scheme It will cover 50% of the cost of the scooter or ₹25, 000, whichever is lower. The scheme is available to all women in the Tamil Nadu state (rural or urban). The 50% subsidy is directly credited into the beneficiary’s bank account.
