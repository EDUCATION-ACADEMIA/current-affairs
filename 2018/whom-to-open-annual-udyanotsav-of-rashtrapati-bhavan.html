Whom to open annual Udyanotsav of Rashtrapati Bhavan?

(A) ** Shri Ram Nath Kovind

(B) Shri Vijay prakash

(C) Shri Arun Jaitley

(D) Shri Deepak Misra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Ram Nath Kovind . President Ram Nath Kovind opened the annual Udyanotsav of Rashtrapati Bhavan on 12 February 2021.

Concept note-2: -Rashtrapati Bhavan, home to the President of the world’s largest democracy will be open for the people from December 1, 2022. It will be open for public viewing for five days in a week.

Concept note-3: -What is the construction cost of the Rashtrapati Bhavan? The Rashtrapati Bhavan was constructed at a cost of Rs 14 million.

Concept note-4: -Visiting days of Rashtrapati Bhavan Museum Complex (Circuit No. 2) are all days except Mondays and Gazetted holidays.
