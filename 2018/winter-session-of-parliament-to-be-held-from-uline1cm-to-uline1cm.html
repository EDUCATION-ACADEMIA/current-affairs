[SOURCES]

Winter Session of Parliament to be held from _________ to _________

(A) December 15 to January 10

(B) December 5 to January 3

(C) December 9 to January 5

(D) ** December 11 to January 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The winter session of Parliament is held in mid-November to mid-December every year.

Concept note-2: -Speaking about the Winter Session, Union Minister for Parliamentary Affairs, Coal and Mines, Shri Prahlad Joshi stated that the Winter Session, 2022 provided 13 sittings spread over a period of 17 days.

Concept note-3: -At present, the Central Hall is used for holding joint sittings of both the houses of parliament and also used for address by the president at the commencement of the first session after each general election.

Concept note-4: -But, three sessions of Lok Sabha are held in a year: Budget session: February to May. Monsoon session: July to September. Winter session: November to mid-December.

Concept note-5: -2022. Earlier, the Constitution (Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes) Order (Second Amendment) Bill, 2022 was passed unanimously by the Rajya Sabha during the ongoing Winter Session of the Parliament on 14.12.
