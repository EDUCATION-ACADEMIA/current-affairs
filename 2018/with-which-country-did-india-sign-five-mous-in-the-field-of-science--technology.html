With which country did India sign five MoUs in the field of Science & Technology?

(A) U.S.

(B) ** South Korea

(C) China

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The MoU between the two countries was signed on Wednesday during the inaugural session of the India-Singapore Technology Summit 2022, which was held virtually and co-organised by Singapore’s Ministry of Trade and Industry, India’s Department of Science and Technology and the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII).

Concept note-2: -India occupies 3rd rank in terms of number of PhDs awarded in Science and Engineering (S&E) (nearly 25,000) after the USA and China. India ranks 3rd globally in terms of number of Startups (77,000) and in terms of no of UNICORNs (107) in the world.

Concept note-3: -It may be recalled that according to the Global Innovation Index (GII) 2022 brought out by World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO), India’s GII ranking has also improved significantly from 81st in 2014 to 40th position in 2022.

Concept note-4: -India is among the top countries globally in the field of scientific research, positioned as one of the top five nations in the field for space exploration. The country has regularly undertaken space missions, including missions to the moon and the famed Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV).

Concept note-5: -The Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh Shri Shivraj Singh Chauhan and Union Minister for Science & Technology, Dr Jitendra Singh today jointly inaugurated India International Science Festival, IISF-2022 in Bhopal with the theme, “Marching towards Amrit Kaal with Science Technology and Innovation”.
