With Whom IRCTC Ties Up recently?

(A) ** Ola

(B) Fastrack

(C) Uber

(D) Meru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -AI-driven financial wellness platform, CASHe, announced that it has partnered with the Indian Railways Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC), to provide a “travel now pay later” (TNPL) payment option on its travel app, IRCTC Rail Connect.

Concept note-2: -It was initially wholly owned by the Government of India and operated under the administrative control of the Ministry of Railways but has been listed on the National Stock Exchange since 2019, with the Government continuing to hold majority ownership.

Concept note-3: -After the Finance Ministry asked Indian Railways to merge the Centre for Railway Information and Systems (CRIS) and RailTel with IRCTC, the Railway Board has asked the FM to reconsider the merge.

Concept note-4: -Govt to sell up to 5% stake in IRCTC via OFS; floor price set at Rs 680 per share.
