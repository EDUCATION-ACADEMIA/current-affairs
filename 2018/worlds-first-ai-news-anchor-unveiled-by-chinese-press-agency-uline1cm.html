World’s first AI news anchor unveiled by Chinese press agency _________

(A) China News Service

(B) ** Xinhua

(C) Global Times

(D) China News Digest

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The artificial intelligence robot named “Xin Xiaomeng” sported a short haircut and wore a pink blouse and earrings in a one-minute video presentation by Xinhua. Xin Xiaomeng is modeled after real life Xinhua news anchor Qu Meng and was developed by Xinhua and tech firm Sogou Inc.

Concept note-2: -China’s state-run news agency just unveiled the AI-powered anchor at China’s World Internet Conference. The news anchor is modeled on a real presenter named Zhang Zhow.

Concept note-3: -Xiaoyi is known to be the first robot in the world which makes history by passing the China’s National Medical Licensing Examination, the first assessment of its kind for any would-be doctor practicing in the country.

Concept note-4: -In China, the first artificial intelligence (AI) news anchor made her debut. An AI anchor has been added to the state-run Xinhua News Agency of China. Zhang Zhao, a regular news presenter, served as the model for the AI anchor.
