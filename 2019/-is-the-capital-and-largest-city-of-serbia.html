____ is the capital and largest city of Serbia.

(A) Sofia

(B) George town

(C) ** Belgrade

(D) Tashkent

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Belgrade, Serbo-Croatian Beograd (“White Fortress”), city, capital of Serbia. It lies at the confluence of the Danube and Sava rivers in the north-central part of the country.

Concept note-2: -Population: 1.6 Million. Belgrade is Serbia’s capital and largest city. It is located at the confluence of the Sava and Danube rivers.

Concept note-3: -Since 1992 Belgrade has been the capital of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, consisting of the Republic of Serbia and the Republic of Montenegro. As of February 2003, Belgrade has been the capital of the newly formed State Union of Serbia and Montenegro.

Concept note-4: -Novi Sad is the second largest city of Serbia, and the capital of the autonomous province of Vojvodina and the administrative center of the South Bačka District. Judging by archaeological findings, Novi Sad has been a good place for living for five thousand years already.
