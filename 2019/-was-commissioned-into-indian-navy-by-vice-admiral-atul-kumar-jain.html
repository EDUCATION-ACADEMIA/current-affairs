____ was commissioned into Indian Navy by Vice-Admiral Atul Kumar Jain.

(A) MK II LCU

(B) ** LCU L-56

(C) Mk. IV LCU

(D) LCU L-32

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vice Admiral Atul Kumar Jain was commissioned in the Indian Navy on 1 July 1982. He is an alumnus of Sainik School Rewa, National Defence Academy (Pune), the Defence Services Staff College (Wellington), the College of Naval Warfare (Mumbai) and the National Defence College (Pretoria, South Africa).

Concept note-2: -Vice Admiral Ajendra Bahadur Singh, Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Western Naval Command, accompanied by Mrs Charu Singh, heading NWWA (WR) called on the Hon’ble President of India Smt Droupadi Murmu, at Rashtrapati Bhawan, New Delhi on Thursday (September 08, 2022).

Concept note-3: -The current VCNS is Vice Admiral Satish Namdeo Ghormade who took over from Vice Admiral G. Ashok Kumar on 31 July 2021.

Concept note-4: -Ans. There are total 110 Rear Admirals in the navy.

Concept note-5: -Admiral Adhar Kumar Chatterji was the first Indian officer to hold the rank of full admiral.
