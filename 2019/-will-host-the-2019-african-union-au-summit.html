____ will host the 2019 African Union (AU) Summit?

(A) Seychelles

(B) Maldives

(C) ** Niger

(D) Indonesia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Niger to host African Union Summit on Industrialization and Economic Diversification, 20-25 November 2022 | UNIDO.

Concept note-2: -African Union Summit on Industrialization and Economic Diversification. Industrialising Africa: Renewed commitment towards an Inclusive and Sustainable Industrialization and Economic Diversification. 20 – 25 November 2022.

Concept note-3: -Comoros will assume the chairmanship of the African Union in 2023, the first time the island nation is being entrusted with the assignment. Comoros was handed the responsibility after Kenya withdrew its bid. President Azali Assoumani and his Kenyan counterpart William Ruto had been separately lobbying for the top post.
