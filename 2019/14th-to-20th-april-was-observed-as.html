[SOURCES]

14th to 20th April was observed as;

(A) ** National Fire Service Week

(B) World Immunization Week

(C) National Non-Smoking Week

(D) World Orphan Week

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fire Service Week is observed from 14-20th April every year to sensitize public on Fire Safety and Fire Prevention and there by promote national prosperity.

Concept note-2: -14 April- B.R. Ambedkar Remembrance Day Ambedkar Remembrance Day is also known as Ambedkar Jayanti or Bhim Jayanti which is observed on 14 April to commemorate the memory of B.R.

Concept note-3: -National days on Thu Apr 20th, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s National Cheddar Fries Day, National Pineapple Upside-Down Cake Day, Get to Know Your Customers Day, National High Five Day, Volunteer Recognition Day… and much more!

Concept note-4: -20 April is the birth anniversary of Gopinath Mohanty, Chandrabali Singh, Nara Chandrababu Naidu, and Mamta Kulkarni. 20 April is also observed as the death anniversary of Pannalal Ghosh, Gaurishankar Hirachand Ojha, and Shakeel Badayuni.
