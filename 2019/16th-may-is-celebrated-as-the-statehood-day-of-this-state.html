16th May is celebrated as the Statehood Day of this state.

(A) Kerala

(B) Goa

(C) ** Sikkim

(D) Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sikkim celebrated its 47th statehood day on 16 May 2022. It was on this day in 1975 that Sikkim had become the 22nd state of the Union of India following the 36th amendment of the Constitution. In September 1974, the Chogyals called for a referendum.

Concept note-2: -The government of India granted their statehood on January 21, 1972, under the North Eastern Region (Reorganization) Act, 1971. Background During British rule, there were 565 princely states in India. They were not part of British India but were tied ..

Concept note-3: -Sikkim State Day is celebrated on May 16, to commemorate the formation of Sikkim as the 22nd state of India Union, in 1975. The day the state of Sikkim was formed by the Thirty-sixth Amendment of the Constitution of India in 1975.

Concept note-4: -Himachal Pradesh is celebrating its 53rd Statehood Day with full enthusiasm across the state on Wednesday(January 25). It was on this day in 1971 that Himachal Pradesh became the 18th state of India.

Concept note-5: -Sikkim celebrates its statehood day on 16 May every year. It marks the day in 1975 when Sikkim transitioned to become the 22nd state of India from being a monarchy earlier. The Indo-Sikkimese treaty was signed in the year 1950. With this, Sikkim became a protectorate of India.
