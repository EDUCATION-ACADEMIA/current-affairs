“281 and Beyond” is an autobiography of ____

(A) Sachin Tendulkar

(B) Saurav Ganguly

(C) ** V.V.S. Laxman

(D) Rahul Dravid

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cricketer VVS Laxman’s autobiography “281 and Beyond” (Co Author: R Kaushik) is amazing. One of the best sports-based books I have ever read.

Concept note-2: -Q. The book “281 and Beyond” is the autobiography of which Indian cricketer? Notes: The book “281 and Beyond” is the autobiography of veteran cricketer VVS Laxman, which will be launched by Westland Sport in November 2018.

Concept note-3: -A Century is Not Enough: My Roller-coaster Ride to Success (2018) is an English autobiography written by former Indian cricketer and captain Sourav Ganguly with Gautam Bhattacharya. The book was first published by Juggernaut on 24 February 2018.

Concept note-4: -Vangipurapu Venkata Sai Laxman ( pronunciation (help·info); born 1 November 1974) is a former Indian international cricketer and a former cricket commentator and pundit. A right-hand batsman known for his elegant stroke play, Laxman played as a middle-order batsman in Test cricket.
