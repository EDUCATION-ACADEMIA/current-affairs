30th January 2019, is celebrated as World Leprosy Eradication Day. The theme for 2019 is;

(A) To live is to help to live

(B) Leprosy defeats, Live transform

(C) ** Ending discrimination, stigma, and prejudice

(D) Zero Child Leprosy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Leprosy Day was observed on 27 January 2019 (Sunday) with the theme “ending discrimination, stigma and prejudice".

Concept note-2: -This year’s World Leprosy Day theme is ending discrimination, stigma, and prejudice.

Concept note-3: -In 2023, World Leprosy Day is Sunday 29 January. The theme of World Leprosy Day 2023 is “Act Now.

Concept note-4: -Theme 2023: Act Now, End Leprosy. History: World Leprosy Day was established in 1954 by French philanthropist Raoul Follereau.

Concept note-5: -The theme of the World Leprosy Eradication Day 2022 is “United for Dignity”.
