A famous player Koneru Humpy is related to which of the following game?

(A) Badminton

(B) ** Chess

(C) Swimming

(D) Polo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Chess. Koneru Humpy is a grandmaster of Indian chess and is from Gudivada, Andhra Pradesh.
