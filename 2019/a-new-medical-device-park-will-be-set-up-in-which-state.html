(A) Haryana

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Assam

(D) ** Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Central government given approval for the promotion of medical device park in UP, Himachal, Tamil Nadu and MP. Government of India working hard and putting their efforts to create world class Infrastructure and reduce cost of production.

Concept note-2: -California (228 businesses), Massachusetts (79 businesses) and Florida (78 businesses) are the States with the most number of Medical Device Manufacturing businesses in the US.

Concept note-3: -Upcoming medical devices park in Rajkot, Gujarat will encourage production and reduce import dependency on the global market. The proposed medical device park would be a geographic cluster of surgical & medical device manufacturing, research and R&D testing laboratories.

Concept note-5: -Medical Devices Park, Hyderabad is a medical devices industrial estate located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The largest such Park in India spread over 250 acres. The dedicated park’s ecosystem supports medical technology innovation and manufacturing.
