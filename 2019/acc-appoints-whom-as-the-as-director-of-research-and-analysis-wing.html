ACC appoints whom as the as Director of Research and Analysis Wing?

(A) ** Samant Goel

(B) S.M. Narvane

(C) Mahendra Pratap

(D) Vinaytosh Mishra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Samant Goel is appointed as the director of Research and Analysis Wing (RAW).

Concept note-2: -Samant Kumar Goel is an Indian civil servant working as an intelligence officer and is of the IPS cadre. He is the current Chief of the Research and Analysis Wing, India’s foreign intelligence agency.

Concept note-3: -Samant Kumar Goel’s contract as secretary of the intelligence agency’s Research and Analysis Wing (RAW).
