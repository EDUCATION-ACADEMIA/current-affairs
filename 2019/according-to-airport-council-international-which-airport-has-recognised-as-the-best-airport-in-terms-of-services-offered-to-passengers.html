According to Airport Council International, which airport has recognised as the best airport in terms of services offered to passengers?

(A) Pune International Airport

(B) Vadodara International Airport

(C) ** Cochin International Airport Limited

(D) Coimbatore International Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Delhi Airport awarded as Best Airport in ACI-ASQ for 2021 Delhi Airport has been awarded the 2021 Airport Service Quality (ASQ) Award for Best Airport by Size and Region (over 40 million passengers per year in Asia-Pacific).

Concept note-2: -The Cochin International Airport Limited (CIAL) has been awarded the Airport Service Quality (ASQ) award 2022 by the Airport Council International (ACI). The award is considered to be the highest honour in the global aviation sector.

Concept note-3: -At the World Travel Awards 2022 held in Paris last month, Indira Gandhi International Airport was awarded ‘the best’ and ‘the cleanest’ airport in India/South Asia, and Rajiv Gandhi International Airport bagged the title of the ‘Best Airport Staff in India & South Asia’.

Concept note-4: -Kochi: Cochin International Airport (Cial) has won the Airport Service Quality Award-2022, instituted by the Airport Council International (ACI) for ‘Mission Safeguarding’, a programme implemented in 2021-22 which ensured seamless traffic and reinforced passenger satisfaction in the aftermath of the pandemic.
