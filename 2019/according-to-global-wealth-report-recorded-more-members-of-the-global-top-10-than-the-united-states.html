According to Global Wealth Report recorded more members of the global top 10% than the United States?

(A) ** China

(B) India

(C) Australia

(D) Saudi Arabia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -People with the top 1% of net worth (opens in new tab) in the U.S. in 2022 had $10,815,000 in net worth. The top 2% had a net worth of $2,472,000. The top 5% had $1,030,000. The top 10% had $854,900.

Concept note-2: -Broken down even more, the top 1% now holds about a third of all wealth - up from a little less than a quarter in 1989.

Concept note-3: -top 10% of adults hold 85%, while the bottom 90% hold the remaining 15% of the world’s total wealth, top 30% of adults hold 97% of the total wealth.

Concept note-4: -Ultra high net worth individuals: population of global 1 percent 2021, by country. Over 22 million individuals residing in the United States belonged to the global top one percent of ultra high net worth individuals worldwide in 2021.
