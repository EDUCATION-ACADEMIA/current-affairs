According to Moody’s, Indian economy is expected to grow at ______ percent in 2019-2020.

(A) ** 7.3

(B) 7.2

(C) 7.4

(D) 7.6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Moody’s Cuts India’s 2022 GDP Growth Forecast to 7%, Sees 2023 Growth at 4.8%

Concept note-2: -"For India, the 2022 real GDP growth projections have been lowered to 7 per cent from 7.7 per cent. The downward revision assumes higher inflation, high-interest rates and slowing global growth will dampen economic momentum by more than we had previously expected,” the agency said in its Global Macro Outlook 2023-24.

Concept note-3: -Moody’s on Friday slashed India’s GDP growth projection for 2022 to 7 per cent on expectation that global slowdown and high domestic interest rates would dampen economic momentum.

Concept note-4: -Moody’s Investors Service on Friday lowered India’s economic growth projection for 2022 to 7% from 7.7% earlier.
