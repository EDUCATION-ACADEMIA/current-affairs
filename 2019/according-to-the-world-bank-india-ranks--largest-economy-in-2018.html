According to the World Bank, India ranks ____ largest economy in 2018.

(A) 10

(B) 8

(C) 6

(D) ** 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India Falls to 7th Spot in Global GDP Rankings: World Bank Report. According to the report, India’s GDP value for 2018 was $ 2,726,322.62 million. New Delhi: A new World Bank report shows that India has slipped to seventh spot when it comes to 2018 global GDP (gross domestic product) rankings.

Concept note-2: -It is the world’s fifth-largest economy by nominal GDP and the third-largest by purchasing power parity (PPP). According to the International Monetary Fund (IMF), on a per capita income basis, India ranked 142nd by GDP (nominal) and 125th by GDP (PPP).

Concept note-3: -The world’s top four economies in the World Bank list in 2018 were the United States, with a GDP of $20.5 trillion, followed by China ($13.6 trillion), Japan ($4.9 trillion) and Germany ($3.9 trillion).

Concept note-4: -India’s economy is forecast to grow by 7% this year, making it the 5th largest in the world, the International Monetary Fund (IMF) says.
