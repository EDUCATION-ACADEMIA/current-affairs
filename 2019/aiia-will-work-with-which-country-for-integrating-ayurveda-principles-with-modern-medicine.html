[SOURCES]

AIIA will work with which country for integrating Ayurveda principles with modern medicine?

(A) Russia

(B) Japan

(C) Israel

(D) ** Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All India Institute of Ayurveda (AIIA) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with Western Sydney University, Australia at New Delhi. The MoU will promote the collaboration in research and developing guidelines for integrating Ayurveda principles with modern medicine.

Concept note-2: -The All India Institute of Ayurveda (AIIA), the apex institute of Ayurveda in India under the Ministry of AYUSH, and the National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (NIAIST), Japan have signed an MoU for academic establishment.

Concept note-3: -Ayurveda, a natural system of medicine, originated in India more than 3,000 years ago. The term Ayurveda is derived from the Sanskrit words ayur (life) and veda (science or knowledge).

Concept note-4: -Ayurveda is the system of medicine that evolved in India with a rationale logical foundation and it has survived as a distinct entity from remote antiquity to the present day. The fundamentals on which the Ayurvedic system is based are essentially true for all times and do not change from are to age.

Concept note-5: -India has already signed agreements with countries like Malaysia and Bangladesh to promote Ayurveda abroad.
