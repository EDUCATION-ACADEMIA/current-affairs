Airports Authority of India (AAI) plan to test remote Air Traffic Control (ATC) tower system for which of the following state?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Haryana

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) ** Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Current surveillance is either based on voice position reporting or based on radar (primary surveillance radar (PSR) or secondary surveillance radar (SSR)) which measures range and azimuth of aircraft from the ground station.

Concept note-2: -AAI manages a total of 137 airports which include 24 International airports (3 Civil Enclaves), 10 Custom Airports (4 Civil Enclaves) and 103 Domestic airports (23 Civil Enclaves).

Concept note-3: -According to a report by The Hindu, a government official states that to comply with the DGCA’s regulatory requirement properly, the country needs 40% more ATC personnel in 2022 than its present strength of 3,163.
