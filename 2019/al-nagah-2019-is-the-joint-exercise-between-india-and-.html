“Al Nagah 2019” is the joint exercise between India and ____

(A) ** Oman

(B) UAE

(C) Iraq

(D) Brazil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Table of Content. The Indo-Oman Joint Military Exercise AL NAGAH 2019, the third rendition of a combined military operation between the Indian Army and Royal Oman Army , concluded at Jabel AI Akhdar planning centre, Oman, on March 25, 2019.

Concept note-2: -What do we Know about Exercise Al NAJAH? About: It’s the fourth edition of the exercise. The scope of the exercise includes professional interaction, mutual understanding of drills & procedures, establishment of joint command & control structures and elimination of terrorist threats.

Concept note-3: -The 4th Edition of India-Oman Joint Military Exercise ‘AL NAJAH-IV’ begins in Rajasthan at the Foreign Training Node of Mahajan Field Firing Ranges. The exercise takes place between contingents of the Indian Army and the Royal Army of Oman from August 01 to 13, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Al Najah: 4th edition of India-Oman joint military exercise concludes in Rajasthan. The fourth edition of Indo-Oman joint military exercise ‘Al Najah’ concluded on august 13 at Mahajan Field Firing Range in Bikaner. Parts of the Indian Army and the Royal Army of Oman participated in it.

Concept note-5: -There are four major domestic military exercises of India namely Gandiv Vijay, Paschim Lehar, Vayu Shakti and Vijay Prahar.
