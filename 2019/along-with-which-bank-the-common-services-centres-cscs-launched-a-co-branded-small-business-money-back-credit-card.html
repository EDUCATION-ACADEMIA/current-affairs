Along with which bank, the Common Services Centres (CSCs) launched a co-branded ‘Small Business Money-Back Credit Card’?

(A) Axis Bank

(B) ICICI Bank

(C) SBI Bank

(D) ** HDFC Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The HDFC Bank CSC Small Business Moneyback Credit Card is enabled for contactless payments, facilitating fast, convenient and secure payments at retail outlets.

Concept note-2: -Common Services Centers (CSCs) Scheme is one of key enablers of the Digital India program. CSCs are the access points for e-delivery of G2C, education, healthcare, agriculture and financial services to villages in India, thereby contributing to a digitally and financially inclusive society.

Concept note-3: -To pay your bills use the IFSC code, “HDFC0000128” to make NEFT payments towards your HDFC Bank credit cards.

Concept note-4: -You will enter your net banking page. Choose the “Register New Card” link. You will have to provide details such as Card Number, PIN Number and Expiry Date. Click on the Submit button after entering these details.
