Apurvi Chandela bags ____ medal in 10m Air Rifle in ISSF World Cup.

(A) ** Gold

(B) Silver

(C) Bronze

(D) None of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISSF Rifle/Pistol World Championship 2022: India finish with 34 medals - list of all medallists. With 12 golds, nine silvers and 13 bronze, India finished second on the medals table behind China at the Cairo meet. Junior shooters won 25 of these medals.

Concept note-2: -Abhinav Bindra collects the gold medal for India in the men’s 10m air rifle shooting competition. Bindra captures India’s first individual gold medal.

Concept note-3: -Deaflympics: Vedika Sharma wins bronze in women’s 10m air pistol.

Concept note-4: -Rahul Jakhar bagged his third medal at the event, winning silver with Deepender Singh, Anurodh at the P5 Mixed Team 10m SH1 event. Deepender also captured silver at P5 Mixed 10m SH1 event. “2022 World Shooting Para Sports World Cup, Munich Update Day 4, 2 more Silver for India.
