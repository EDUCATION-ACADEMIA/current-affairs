Archana Kamath is associated to which of the following sports?

(A) Judo

(B) ** Table Tennis

(C) Badminton

(D) Chess

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Archana Kamath is associated with Table Tennis. She is the first player in Karnataka to hold that distinction in TT history. In 2014, She added to India’s Table Tennis team.

Concept note-2: -Archana Kamath, Table Tennis Player, Moves To Court After Commonwealth Games 2022 Exclusion. Archana Kamath was initially picked in the Indian team for the Commonwealth Games 2022 in Birmingham. But was suddenly dropped after CoA took charge of TTFI.

Concept note-3: -Archana is the daughter of ophthalmologists Anuradha and Girish Kamath.

Concept note-4: -Archana Kamath hails from Bangalore and has been famed in the game of Table Tennis. She has been ranked as number 1 from the state of Karnataka in four different categories and is the only player from Karnataka to hold the distinction.
