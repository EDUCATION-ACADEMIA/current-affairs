Arjuna Award winner Raghbir Singh Bhola passed away recently. He belongs to which of the following sport?

(A) Cricket

(B) ** Hockey

(C) Tennis

(D) Basket Ball

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BHUBANESWAR: Ace hockey player Deep Grace Ekka has been selected for the prestigious Arjuna Award 2022 for her contribution to women’s hockey. She is among the 24 other sportspersons selected for the award by the ministry of youth affairs and sports, which was announced in New Delhi on Monday.

Concept note-2: -Raghbir Singh Bhola was a hockey player who competed for India at the 1956 and 1960 Olympic Games, winning a gold and silver medal, respectively. He also toured with the Indian squad in Singapore and Malaysia in 1954 and on a 1959 European Tour.
