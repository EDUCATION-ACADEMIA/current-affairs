As per Global Prime Office Occupancy Costs survey, what is the position of New Delhi’s Connaught Place?

(A) 6

(B) 15

(C) ** 9

(D) 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Delhi’s Connaught Place 9th most expensive office location in World: CBRE . As per property consultant CPRE’s annual Global Prime Office Occupancy Costs Survey, New Delhi’s Connaught Place (CP) is 9th most expensive office location in the world with an annual rent of nearly $144 per sq. ft.

Concept note-2: -Delhi’s Connaught Place Among the Most Expensive Office Places in the World.

Concept note-3: -New Delhi’s Connaught Place ranks improved to 17th in the most expensive office market in the world from 25th position in 2020, with an annual average occupancy cost of USD 109 square feet, according to property consultant JLL.

Concept note-4: -Connaught Place of New Delhi is one of the largest commercial, financial, and business centres of the country. The rentals of this place are Rs. 850 per square feet in a year.

Concept note-5: -The average capital value of properties in Jor Bagh is Rs 47,800 per sq ft, according to Delhi NCR Insite Report Oct-Dec 2021.
