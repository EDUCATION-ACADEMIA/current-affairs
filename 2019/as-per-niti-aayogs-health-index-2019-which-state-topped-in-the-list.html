As per NITI Aayog’s health index 2019, which state topped in the list?

(A) ** Kerala

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On overall ranking based on the composite index score in 2019–20, the top-ranking states were Kerala and Tamil Nadu among the ‘Larger States’, Mizoram and Tripura among the ‘Smaller States’, and DH&DD and Chandigarh among the UTs. A robust and acceptable mechanism is used for measuring performance.

Concept note-2: -UP last again in NITI Aayog Health Index 2019-20, Kerala tops ranking | Business Standard News.

Concept note-3: -Karnataka tops NITI Aayog innovation list.

Concept note-4: -NITI Aayog India Innovation Index 2022 Karnataka Top Rank: Karnataka has topped the NITI Aayog’s India Innovation Index, 2022, which ranks sub-national innovation capacities and ecosystems. The state has ranked first in the Major States category in all three editions of the Index.

Concept note-5: -With the highest per capita health expenditure of Rs 9,871 in 2021-22, more than thrice the national average of just over Rs 3,000, and a well-structured healthcare network, Kerala has emerged as the best performing big state in health for the third consecutive year.
