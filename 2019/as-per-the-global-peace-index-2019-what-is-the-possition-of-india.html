As per the Global Peace Index 2019, what is the possition of India?

(A) 92

(B) 63

(C) 122

(D) ** 141

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s is ranked 141 among 163 countries on the Global Peace Index 2019. The ranking of India in 2018 was the 136th.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Iceland. This is the thirteenth edition of the Global Peace Index (GPI), which ranks 163 independent states and territories according to their level of peacefulness.

Concept note-3: -Global Peace Index (GPI) is a report produced by the Institute for Economics & Peace (IEP) which measures the relative position of nations’ and regions’ peacefulness.
