[SOURCES]

As per the report of CISCO, what is the position of India in General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Index?

(A) 8th

(B) ** 6th

(C) 12th

(D) 5th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India ranks 6th in GDPR readiness index: Cisco Report.

Concept note-2: -Now India is set to legislate a Personal Data Protection Bill (PDPB) or Act (PDPA), which would control the collection, processing, storage, usage, transfer, protection, and disclosure of personal data on Indian residents. PDPB is an important development for global managers.

Concept note-3: -General data protection laws India is not a party to any convention on protection of personal data which is equivalent to the GDPR or the Data Protection Directive.

Concept note-4: -At its core, GDPR Compliance means an organization that falls within the scope of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) meets the requirements for properly handling personal data as defined in the law. The GDPR outlines certain obligations organizations must follow which limit how personal data can be used.

Concept note-5: -The GDPR is a new regulation created by the European Union. It has been four years in the making and was finally approved on April 14, 2016. It will replace its predecessor, the Data Protection Directive 95/46/EC, which was adopted in 1995.
