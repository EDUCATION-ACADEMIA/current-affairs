As the governments move of merger of 10 PSBs into 4, the banks Oriental Bank of Commerce and United Bank of India will be absorbed by which bank?

(A) State Bank of India

(B) Bank of Baroda

(C) ** Punjab National Bank

(D) Canara Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1) Oriental Bank of Commerce (OBC) and United Bank of India will be merged into Punjab National Bank (PNB). After the merger, these together will form the second-largest public sector bank in the country, after State Bank of India (SBI).

Concept note-2: -After the massive merger, the total number of Public Sector Banks (PSBs) in India has come down from 27 banks in 2017 to 12 in 2021.
