Ashok Leyland with which bank has recently signed an MOU for vehicle financing?

(A) Bank of Baroda

(B) Indian Bank

(C) ** ICICI Bank

(D) HDFC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is ICICI Bank.

Concept note-2: -Ashok Leyland has announced the merger of non-banking financial company in the business of financing commercial and personal vehicles – Hinduja Leyland Finance (HLF) – with NXT DIGITAL.
