At which place PM Narendra MOdi will perform Yoga on International Yoga Day 2019?

(A) Jabalpur

(B) ** Ranchi

(C) Gandhinagar

(D) Chandigarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India celebrates 5th International Day of Yoga 2019 - PM Modi during a mass yoga event in Ranchi | The Economic Times.

Concept note-2: -35,985 people, including PM Modi and dignitaries from 84 nations, performed 21 asanas (yoga postures) for 35 minutes at Rajpath in New Delhi, becoming the largest yoga class ever held, and with the largest number-84-of participating nations.

Concept note-3: -IDY- 2019. The 5th International Day of Yoga was held in Ranchi, Jharkhand. Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi led a crowd of over 40,000 people. The theme of the 5th IDY was ‘Yoga for Heart’.

Concept note-4: -This year the theme for the celebration of International Yoga Day is “Yoga for humanity” Prime Minister Narendra Modi is leading the International Day of Yoga celebrations from the heritage city of Mysuru in Karnataka.

Concept note-5: -Changlang, June 21, 2022: The International Yoga Day 2022 was celebrated in Changlang district along with rest of the country. Since 2015, the International Yoga Day has been celebrating on June 21 every year.
