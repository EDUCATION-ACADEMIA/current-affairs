Bharat Vandana Park, has been inaugurated in which city?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Pune

(C) Patna

(D) Nashik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DDA officials said it will be a one-of-its-kind park. The construction work of the Bharat Vandana Park project, the Delhi Development Authority (DDA)’s flagship project in Dwarka, which started in December 2019, is 40% complete, officials said.

Concept note-2: -Bharat Vandana Park, the upcoming biggest theme park of India which will be based on mini India theme will be completed by March 2022. The Bharat Vandana Park will be spread over 200 acres in Dwarka sector 20 in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Rather, the project is worth Rs. 530 crore and aims to develop the park as one of the main tourist attractions in the national capital. Moreover, the work on Bharat Vandana Park has started, and the project will be ready by November 2022.
