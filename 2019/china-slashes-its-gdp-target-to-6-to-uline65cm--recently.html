China slashes its GDP target to 6 to ______ % recently.

(A) ** 6.5

(B) 7.4

(C) 6.3

(D) 6.2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -China’s economy is in bad shape because of three years of Covid lockdowns and a persistent property market slump. Economists had generally expected growth to slump to a rate between 2.7% and 3.3% for 2022, lower than the government’s target of 5.5%.

Concept note-2: -According to preliminary estimates, the gross domestic product (GDP) was 121,020.7 billion yuan in 2022, an increase of 3.0 percent over last year at constant prices.

Concept note-3: -For the full year of 2022, the economy grew by 3.0%, missing the official target of around 5.5% and marking the second slowest pace since 1976 amid the impact of Beijing’s zero-COVID policy.
