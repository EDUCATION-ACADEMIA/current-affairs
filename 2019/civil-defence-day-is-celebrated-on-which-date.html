Civil Defence Day is celebrated on which date?

(A) 12th December

(B) ** 6th December

(C) 14th December

(D) 2nd December

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Civil Defence Day is celebrated on 6th December every year to make aware of the citizens and highlight the importance of Civil Defence. The civil Defence provides protection to the civilian population during crisis.

Concept note-2: -In India, National Defence Day is celebrated on March 3.

Concept note-3: -In 1990, the International Civil Defense Organization (ICDO) declared this day a global holiday. Civil Defense Day is a time to celebrate the significance of civil defense and to commemorate those who have sacrificed for it.

Concept note-4: -In 2022, the Indian Army’s theme for the event was “In Stride with the Future”. It was seen as an acknowledgement of the “increasingly critical role played by niche and disruptive technologies in modern warfare”.

Concept note-5: -THE CIVIL DEFENCE ACT, 1968 ACT NO. 27 OF 1968 [24th May, 1968.] An Act to make provision for civil defence and for matters connected therewith. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Nineteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:- CHAPTER I PRELIMINARY 1.
