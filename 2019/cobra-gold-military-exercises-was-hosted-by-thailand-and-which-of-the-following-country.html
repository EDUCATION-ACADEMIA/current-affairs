Cobra Gold Military Exercises was hosted by Thailand and which of the following country?

(A) Indonesia

(B) ** United States

(C) Malaysia

(D) South Korea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first Cobra Gold exercise was held in 1982. The exercise is between Thailand, the USA with allied and partner nations. It is the largest Asia-Pacific military exercise held every year.

Concept note-2: -One of the Asia-Pacific region’s largest multinational military exercises, Cobra Gold began in Thailand with around 10,000 personnel and 29 countries as participants. It is aimed at enhancing interoperability, strengthening relationships among participating nations.

Concept note-3: -The exercise ‘Veer Guardian-2023’ involving the Indian Air Force (IAF) and Japan Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) will be held at Japan’s Hyakuri air base. The IAF said on Saturday that its deployment at the exercise will include four Su-30 MKI jets, two C-17 aircraft and one IL-78 plane.

Concept note-4: -Frequently Asked Questions about Maitree Exercise It is an annual event between the troops of the armies of both countries. It is also called Indo-Thailand Joint Exercise.
