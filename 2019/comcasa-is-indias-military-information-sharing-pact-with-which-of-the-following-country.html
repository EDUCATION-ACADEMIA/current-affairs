[SOURCES]

COMCASA is India’s military-information sharing pact with which of the following country?

(A) Japan

(B) Russia

(C) China

(D) ** United States

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India and Cyprus have signed a number of agreements in various areas including on Defence and Military Cooperation between the two countries.

Concept note-2: -The Industrial Security Agreement (ISA) summit between India and the United States was held between September 27to October 01, 2021 at New Delhi. The summit was organised to develop protocol for the exchange of classified information between the defence industries of both the nations.

Concept note-3: -U.S.-India defense trade cooperation continues to expand with the Logistics Exchange Memorandum of Agreement (LEMOA), Communications, Compatibility and Security Agreement (COMCASA), and the Industrial Security Agreement (ISA) now in place.

Concept note-4: -These are “routine” agreements and include: General Security of Military Information Agreement (GSOMIA); Logistic Support Agreement (LSA); Communications and Information Security Memorandum of Agreement (CISMOA) and the Basic Exchange and Cooperation Agreement for Geospatial Intelligence (BECA).
