Cricket Association of this state granted full membership in the BCCI.

(A) ** Uttarakhand

(B) Karnataka

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“The Members approved the recommendation of the affiliation committee of BCCI to grant full member status to Chhattisgarh State Cricket Sangh . They will be a part of the Central Zone for all the BCCI tournaments,” BCCI stated in a release.

Concept note-2: -The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) is the national governing body of cricket in India. Its headquarters are situated at Cricket centre, Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai.

Concept note-3: -General Secretary: Mahim Verma. Joint Secretary: Avinish Verma. Treasurer: Prithvi Singh Negi. Member (Apex Council): Deepak Mehra.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Cricketers’ Association is a non-profit company incorporated on July 5, 2019, recognized by the Board of Control for Cricket in India as the official association for ex-cricketers in India.
