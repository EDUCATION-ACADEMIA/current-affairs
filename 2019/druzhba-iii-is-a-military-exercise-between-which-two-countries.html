Druzhba-III is a military exercise between which two countries?

(A) India-Russia

(B) ** Pakistan-Russia

(C) India-Oman

(D) India-Maldives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pakistan-Russia Joint Training Exercise Druzhba-III.

Concept note-2: -Exercise Yudh Abhyas is conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, Tactics, Techniques and Procedures between the Armies of the two nations. The previous edition of the exercise was conducted at Joint Base Elmendorf Richardson, Alaska (USA) in October 2021.

Concept note-3: -The 2022 exercise also was the 30th anniversary of Malabar, which began as a bilateral exercise between Indian and US navies. Japan has been a permanent partner of the Malabar since 2015 and Australia has been participating since 2020.

Concept note-4: -Notably, the Indo-US military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas 2022’ commenced in Uttarakhand’s Auli on November 19. The joint military exercise is conducted annually between India and US with the aim of exchanging best practices.
