Essel World launched an interactive bird park in ______, Maharashtra.

(A) ** Gorai

(B) Borivali

(C) Powai

(D) Chembur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: In Mumbai, EsselWorld launched India’s first foreign interactive and exotic world park at the Essel World Bird Park in Gorai. The rain forest themed park is spread across 1.5 acres and is home to over 500 birds belonging to 60 different exotic species.

Concept note-2: -Mumbaikars can now spend quality time in the company of Dudu- the Sulphur-crested Cockatoo, Rio the blue and yellow macaw, Hulk the Eclectus parrot and several other such beautiful exotic birds at the EsselWorld Bird Park, which is India’s first interactive bird park located at Gorai village, Borivali.

Concept note-3: -EsselWorld Bird Park, India’s first exotic and interactive bird park, has been launched at Gorai village, Borivali in Mumbai.

Concept note-4: -You Pay ₹390.00. Spread over close to two acres, Esselworld Bird Park has over 150 species of plants such as shrubs, bushes, aquatic plants and creepers as a habitat for over 400 birds.
