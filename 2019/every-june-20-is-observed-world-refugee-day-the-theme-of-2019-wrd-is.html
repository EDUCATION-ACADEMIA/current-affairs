CURRENT AFFAIRS-2019
[SOURCES]

Every June 20 is observed World Refugee Day. The theme of 2019 WRD is;

(A) Embracing Refugees to celebrate our Common Humanity

(B) Take 1 minute to support a family forced to flee

(C) ** StepWithRefugees-Take A Step on World Refugee Day

(D) Now More Than Ever, We Need to Stand with Refugees

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Theme of World Refugee Day 2022 is Whoever, Whatever, Whenever. The theme of World Refugee Day 2022 underlines that every refugee has to be treated respectfully. On this occasion, the main aim focuses that every refugee wherever they belong should feel secure and comfortable.

Concept note-2: -The theme for Refugee Week 2023 is ‘Healing’, and hopes to help imagine a world where healing replaces harm, and care becomes the world’s shared currency. This year, it will take place from 20th to 26th June, coinciding with World Refugee Day (20th).

Concept note-3: -Each year on June 20, the world celebrates World Refugee Day. In 2022, the focus was on the right to seek safety.

Concept note-4: -The World Refugee Day 2022 theme will be ‘Together we heal, learn, and shine’. Supporting, helping, and ensuring the safety of refugees should be an all-time goal and not just limited to the Refugee Day.
