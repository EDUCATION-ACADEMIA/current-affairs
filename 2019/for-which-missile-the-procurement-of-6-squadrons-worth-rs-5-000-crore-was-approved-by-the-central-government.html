For which missile, the procurement of 6 squadrons worth Rs 5, 000 crore was approved by the central government?

(A) Prithvi air defence missile

(B) ** Akash air defense missile

(C) Barak 8 air defense missile

(D) Trishul air defense missile

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Akash Missile is in operation with the Indian Air Force and the Indian Army. This missile was launched into service in the year 2009 in India.

Concept note-2: -The Akash missile system can target aircraft up to 50 km (31 mi) away. It has the capability to neutralise aerial targets like fighter jets, cruise missiles and air-to-surface missiles as well as ballistic missiles. It is in operational service with the Indian Army and the Indian Air Force.

Concept note-3: -Cabinet Approves Export of Akash Missile System and Creates a Committee for faster Approval of Exports. Under the Atma Nirbhar Bharat, India is growing in its capabilities of manufacturing wide variety of Defence platforms and missiles.
