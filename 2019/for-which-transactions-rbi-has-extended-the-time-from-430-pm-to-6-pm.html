[SOURCES]

For which transactions RBI has extended the time from 4:30 pm to 6 pm?

(A) National Electronic Fund transfer (NEFT)

(B) ** Real Time Gross Settlement System (RTGS)

(C) Unstructured Supplementary Service Data (USSD)

(D) Immediate Payment Service (IMPS)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Transactions through National Electronic Funds Transfer (NEFT) and Real Time Gross Settlement (RTGS) System will continue upto 2400 hours as hitherto on March 31, 2022. 4.

Concept note-2: -RTGS is available 24x7x365 with effect from December 14, 2020. 6.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has decided to extend the timeline for banks and other institutions to migrate to the framework for processing of e-mandates on recurring online transactions by six months to September 30. The earlier deadline to migrate to the Additional Factor of Authentication (AFA) ended on March 31.
