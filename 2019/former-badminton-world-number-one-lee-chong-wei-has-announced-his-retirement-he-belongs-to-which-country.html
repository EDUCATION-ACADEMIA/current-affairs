Former badminton world number one Lee Chong Wei has announced his retirement. He belongs to which country?

(A) Brazil

(B) Germany

(C) ** Malaysia

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Datuk Lee Chong Wei DB, DCSM, PJN, DSPN, AMN, JP, PhD(SptsSc) (h.c.) (Chinese: ; Pe̍h-ōe-jī: Lí Chong-úi; born 21 October 1982) is a Malaysian former badminton player. As a singles player, Lee was ranked first worldwide for 349 weeks, including a 199-week streak from 21 August 2008 to 14 June 2012.

Concept note-2: -Lee, who was diagnosed with early stage nose cancer last year, said he opted to retire after being told by doctors that his body could no longer take the intense training needed for high-level competition.
