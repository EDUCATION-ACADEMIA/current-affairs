Golden Chariot service is associated with which railways?

(A) Pakistan Railways

(B) Myanmar Railways

(C) ** Indian Railways

(D) China Railways

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Golden Chariot luxury train, operated by the Indian Railways subsidiary IRCTC and Karnataka State Tourism Development Corporation Limited (KSTDC), is all set to resume its operations.

Concept note-2: -The Golden Chariot is a luxury tourist train that connects the important tourist spots in the Indian states of Karnataka, Goa, Kerala & Tamil Nadu as well as Pondicherry, depending on the selected itinerary. It is named after the Stone Chariot in the Vitthala Temple at Hampi.

Concept note-3: -Golden Chariot, the luxury train owned by Karnataka Tourism and operated by IRCTC, will operate two intra-Karnataka itineraries this month.

Concept note-4: -While the Golden Chariot journey is centered around South India, the train routes differ according to the itinerary. All 3 journeys start & end at Bengaluru but cover different destinations across Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Goa & Kerala.

Concept note-5: -The train leaves station from Bengaluru and after 6 nights and 7 days journey, it loopbacks at Bengaluru, Yaswantpur station. The Golden Chariot tour starts in Bengaluru and takes passengers to Bandipur for jungle safari, next it stops in Mysor to enchant passengers with royalty and its forts.
