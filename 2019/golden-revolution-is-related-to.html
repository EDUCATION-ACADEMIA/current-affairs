‘Golden Revolution’ is related to?

(A) Food grain Production

(B) Fruit Production

(C) ** Honey and Horticulture Production

(D) Fish Production

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Golden revolution is related to the production of honey and horticulture. It is a part of the important agricultural revolutions of India. Nirpakh Tutej is considered to be the Father of The Golden Revolution in India.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Horticulture and Honey. The Golden Revolution is related to Horticulture and Honey.

Concept note-3: -Golden Revolution: The period between 1991 to 2003 is known as the period of the Golden Revolution. This made India a world leader in the production of bananas, mangoes, etc. and provided sustainable livelihood and nutrition options.

Concept note-4: -Brown revolution is related to Leather/Cocoa production.
