Guillermo Cano Press Freedom Prize is an initiative of ______

(A) UNEP

(B) UNSC

(C) UNDP

(D) ** UNESCO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Belarusian Association of Journalists (BAJ) has been named as the laureate of the 2022 UNESCO/Guillermo Cano World Press Freedom Prize, following the recommendation of the International Jury of media professionals.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Kyo Soe and Wa Lone (Myanmar).

Concept note-3: -The UNESCO/ Guillermo Cano World Press Freedom Prize was established in 1997 by UNESCO’s Executive Board in Memory of Guillermo Cano, a Colombian journalist who died in the exercise of his profession.
