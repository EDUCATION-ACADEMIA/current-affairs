EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -First IFFI The 1st edition of IFFI was organized by the Films Division, Government of India, with the patronage of the first Prime Minister of India. Held in Mumbai from 24 January to 1 February 1952, the Festival was subsequently taken to Madras, Delhi, Calcutta and Trivandrum.

Concept note-2: -Directed by Costa Rican director Valentina Maurel, Spanish film ‘I Have Electric Dreams’ won The Golden Peacock which includes a cash award of Rs 40 lakh.

Concept note-3: -Founded in 1952, the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) is one of Asia’s most prominent film festivals. Since 2004, it has moved to its permanent venue at Goa, where it is jointly organized by DFF and the State Government of Goa, as per the International Film Festival guidelines and protocols.
