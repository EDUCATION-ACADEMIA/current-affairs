Hari Prasad Chaurasia is a renowned player of

(A) Sitar

(B) ** Flute

(C) Sarod

(D) Tabla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One of the most lovable musicians of our times, Padma Vibhushan Pt Hariprasad Chaurasia has not only taken the flute to the hitherto uncharted territory but is also a renowned composer, having created unforgettable immortal songs for several hit films.

Concept note-2: -Hariprasad Chaurasia (born 1 July 1938) is an Indian music director and classical flautist, who plays the bansuri, in the Hindustani classical tradition.

Concept note-3: -1)Pandit Hariprasad Chaurasia: He is a renowned Indian classical flutist who was born on 1st July, 1938 in Uttar Pradesh, India. Chaurasia is known internationally and is also the greatest master of the Bansuri, or flute.
