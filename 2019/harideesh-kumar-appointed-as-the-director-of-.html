Harideesh Kumar appointed as the Director of ____

(A) NIRJAFT

(B) ICAR-NBFGR

(C) NASSCOM

(D) ** IBPS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -B Harideesh Kumar has been appointed as director of Institute of Banking Personnel Selection for a period of three years. Earlier, he was the Executive Director of Canara Bank.

Concept note-2: -The managing and administering body for IBPS is the RBI, Finance Ministry of India, and NIBM.

Concept note-3: -IBPS functions as an autonomous body that is engaged in conducting competitive exams to recruit clerks and officers in the banking sector.

Concept note-4: -Full Form of IBPS – Institute of Banking Personnel Selection. The Full form of IBPS is the Institute of Banking Personnel Selection.
