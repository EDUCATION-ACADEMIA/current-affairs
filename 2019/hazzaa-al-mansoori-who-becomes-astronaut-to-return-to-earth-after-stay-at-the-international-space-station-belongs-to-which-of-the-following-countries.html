Hazzaa al-Mansoori who becomes astronaut to return to earth after stay at the International Space Station belongs to which of the following countries?

(A) USA

(B) Israel

(C) China

(D) ** UAE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UAE astronaut Hazza Al Mansouri said his historic mission was “only the beginning” for the country’s space programme. The first Emirati in space was speaking at a press conference shortly after returning to Earth, following his eight-day stay at the International Space Station (ISS).

Concept note-2: -When did Hazza Al Mansoori go to space? Hazza Al Mansoori’s mission launched him aboard the Soyuz MS-15 spacecraft on 25 September 2019 to the International Space Station. After eight days, he returned safely back to earth by landing in Kazakhstan on 3 October 2019, aboard Soyuz MS-12.

Concept note-3: -Al Mansouri made headlines worldwide, as he became the first Emirati in space and the first Arab on the International Space Station (ISS) on September 25, 2019. “We have reached safely, thanks to Allah.

Concept note-4: -Emirati astronaut Hazza Al Mansoori will be the first Arab and Emirati astronaut to travel to ISS on an eight-day space mission on 25 September 2019.
