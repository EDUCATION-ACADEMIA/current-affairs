How many Committees formulated for Union Territory related issues of J-K?

(A) ** 3

(B) 2

(C) 6

(D) 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The act consists of 103 clauses, extends 106 central laws to the UTs, repeals 153 state laws, and abolishes the Jammu and Kashmir Legislative Council among other things.

Concept note-2: -The act re-constituted the former state of Jammu and Kashmir into two union territories, one being Jammu and Kashmir and the other being Ladakh, with effect from 31 October 2019.

Concept note-3: -The head of state of Jammu and Kashmir is a Lieutenant Governor, appointed by the President of India on the advice of the central government. His or her post is largely ceremonial. The Chief Minister, is the head of government and chairs a council of ministers.
