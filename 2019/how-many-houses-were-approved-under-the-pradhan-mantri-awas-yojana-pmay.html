How many houses were approved under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)?

(A) ** 81 Lakh

(B) 83 Lakh

(C) 85 Lakh

(D) 88 Lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Launched on June 25, 2015, the PMAY Urban mission aims to end housing shortage in India’s urban areas, by ensuring a pucca house to all eligible households by 2022, when India completes 75 years of its independence. All in all, the government plans to build 20 million homes under the PMAY-U mission.

Concept note-2: -To get more information about PM Awas Yojana Gramin List check the official website www..pmayg.gov.in . So that You can get complete information about PM Awas Yojana Gramin List.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) is an initiative by the Government of India in which affordable housing will be provided to the urban poor with a target of building 2 crore (20 million) affordable houses by 31 March 2022.
