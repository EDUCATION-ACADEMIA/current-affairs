How many IAF officers are in the part of Mi-17 V5?

(A) 2

(B) 4

(C) 5

(D) ** 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2008, India had signed a contract with Rosoboronexport for the delivery of 80 Mi-17V-5, which was completed in 2011-2013. In 2012-2013, three additional deals were signed to supply a total of 71 Mi-17V-5 for the needs of the Air Force, the Home Ministry and the Secretariat of the PM of India, Rostec said.

Concept note-2: -* In 2019, an Mi-17V5 chopper carrying six IAF personnel was brought down by friendly fire from the Spyder air defence missile system. * According to latest data, the IAF currently has over 200 of these helicopters in service.

Concept note-3: -The Mi-17V5 is considered one of the “safest” and “most modern” military transport helicopters, a retired IAF officer said.
