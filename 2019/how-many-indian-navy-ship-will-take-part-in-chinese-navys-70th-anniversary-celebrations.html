(B) ** 2

(C) 5

(D) 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -The People’s Liberation Army Navy (PLAN) surpassed the US Navy in fleet size sometime around 2020 and now has around 340 warships, according to the Pentagon’s 2022 China Military Power Report, released in November.
