How many Indians have been appointed as the members of technical committees of Asian Gymnastic Union?

(A) 4

(B) ** 5

(C) 8

(D) 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rameshwar Dayal were the First founder President and Secretary of Gymnastics Federation of India (GFI) who worked hard to raise the standard of this mother sports in the country and remained till 1956.

Concept note-2: -For the first time in the history of the Games, India won over 100 medals in total. For the first time ever India won a medal in gymnastics, with Ashish Kumar winning a silver and a bronze.

Concept note-3: -Learn about the equipment used for rhythmic gymnastics at the Tokyo Olympics. In rhythmic gymnastics, there are five apparatus used for individual competition: rope, hoop, ball, clubs, and ribbon.

Concept note-4: -The first Olympic Games to feature rhythmic gymnastics was in 1984 in Los Angeles, USA, where Canadian Lori Fung was the first gymnast in the history of the sport to take an Olympic title (Los Angeles).
