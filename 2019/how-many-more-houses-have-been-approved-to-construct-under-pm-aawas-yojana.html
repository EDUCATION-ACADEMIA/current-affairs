How many more houses have been approved to construct under PM Aawas Yojana?

(A) 351, 000

(B) 400, 000

(C) ** 331, 000

(D) 471, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Is the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana active or not? The central government has extended the validity of both the verticals of the PMAY programme. The PMAY-Gramin programme is valid till March 31, 2024. The PMAY-Urban scheme is valid till December 2024.

Concept note-2: -PMAY scheme targets constructing about 20 million houses at an affordable price by 31 March 2022. The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Gramin (PMAY-G) has been extended upto the year 2024. The total target of pucca houses has also been revised to 2.95 crore houses.
