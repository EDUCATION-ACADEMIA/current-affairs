How much amount BSNL got from Bharat Broadband Nigam to clear Bharat Net vendor dues?

(A) Rs. 450 crores

(B) Rs. 580 crores

(C) Rs. 620 crores

(D) ** Rs. 770 crores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BSNL gets Rs 770 crore from BBNL to clear BharatNet vendor dues - The Economic Times.

Concept note-2: -Agreement was signed in April 2020 between Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) and M/s Bharat Sanchar Nigam Ltd. (BSNL). USOF funded the Capital Expenditure incurred by BSNL for the work allotted under the project as per the tender discovered rates of Rs 36.3856 Crores in the form of subsidy.

Concept note-3: -BharatNet Scheme has been launched by the government of India under the Digital India program in order to provide high-speed digital connectivity of the internet in rural areas at a very affordable price. This high-speed digital connectivity will be provided through optical Fibre.

Concept note-4: -In April 2022, it was announced that India’s flagship rural broadband project: Bharat Broadband Network Ltd (BBNL), the special purpose vehicle set up to implement BharatNet would merge with Bharat Sanchar Nigam Ltd (BSNL), the national telecom service provider.

Concept note-5: -The Bharat Net project proposes broadband connectivity to households under village Panchayats and even to government institutions at the district level. It intends to cover all 2.5 lakh Gram Panchayats for the provision of E-governance, E-healthcare, E-Commerce, E-Education, and Public Interest Access services.
