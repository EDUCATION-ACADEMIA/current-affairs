How much of people has enrolled under PM Shram Yogi Maandhan Scheme?

(A) 10 Lakh Person

(B) 18 Lakh Person

(C) 22 Lakh Person

(D) ** 30 Lakh persons

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Over 49 lakh beneficiaries have enrolled themselves in the country under Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan Yojana: Rameswar Teli.

Concept note-2: -E Shram Card 3000 Pension Yojana 2023 Overview Workers in the country’s unorganized sector can apply for a pension. Will get a monthly pension of 3000 rupees and will have immediate access to other government programs. Apply with the assistance of Jan Seva Kendra.

Concept note-3: -Grievance Redressal: To address any grievances related to the scheme, subscriber can contact at customer care number 1800 267 6888 which will be available on 24*7 basis(to be effective from 15 Febraury 2019).

Concept note-4: -Government of India has allocated Rs. 350 Crore (BE) for Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan (PM-SYM) in Union Budget 2022-23.

Concept note-5: -The scheme is meant to benefit workers in the unorganized sector and this includes street vendors, rickshaw pullers, agricultural workers, mid-day meal workers, construction workers, or workers in similar other occupations. There are an estimated 42 crore, such unorganized workers, in the country.
