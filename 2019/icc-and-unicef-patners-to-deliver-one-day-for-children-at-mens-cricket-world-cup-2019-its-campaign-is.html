ICC and UNICEF patners to deliver One Day for Children at Mens Cricket World Cup 2019. Its campaign is;

(A) #OneDay4Cricket

(B) #OneDay4Match

(C) #OneDay4Player

(D) ** #OneDay4Children

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Cricket Council (ICC) and UNICEF have launched a global partnership to empower women and girls and promote inclusion and gender equality through cricket.

Concept note-2: -The International Cricket Council (ICC) has inked a new sponsorship extension with the car manufacturing company, Nissan Motor India. With this new deal, Nissan will prolong to be the official sponsor of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022.

Concept note-3: -Also, UNICEF’s partnership with GAVI, Unilever and IKEA Foundation continues to achieve results for children, adolescents and women in India.

Concept note-4: -The 2019 World Cup featured 10 teams, a decrease from previous World Cups in 2011 and 2015, which each featured 14 teams.
