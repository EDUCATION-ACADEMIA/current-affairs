In which country the 45th G7 Summit for the year 2019 was held?

(A) Germany

(B) USA

(C) ** France

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Biarritz. The 45th G7 summit was held in Biarritz, France. It was held in Biarritz on 24-26 August 2019. ‘Fighting Inequality’ is the theme of the 45th G7 summit.

Concept note-2: -The UK has also invited leaders from Australia, India, South Africa, and South Korea to attend the Leaders’ Summit as guest countries. The US held the G7 Presidency in 2020 but did not convene a Leaders’ Summit due to the global pandemic. The 2019 G7 Leaders’ Summit took place in France in August 2019.

Concept note-3: -The 2019 G7 Summit, presided over by France, will focus on fighting inequality.

Concept note-4: -The Biarritz Summit (G7 Summit) will be held on 24–26 August 2019 in Biarritz, France.
