In which of the following cities India International Cooperative Trade Fair has been scheduled to be held?

(A) Patna

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Amritsar

(D) Gurugram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which city is set to play host to the 40th edition of India International Trade Fair (IITF)? Notes: India Trade Promotion Organisation (ITPO) is set to conduct the 40th edition of India International Trade Fair (IITF) at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi from November 14 to 27.
