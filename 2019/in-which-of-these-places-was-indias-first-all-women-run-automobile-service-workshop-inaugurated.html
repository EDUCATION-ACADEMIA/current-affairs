In which of these places was India’s first all-women-run automobile service workshop inaugurated?

(A) ** Jaipur

(B) Chennai

(C) Mumbai

(D) Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mahindra & Mahindra has announced the one-year completion of India’s first all-women-run automobile service workshop in Jaipur.

Concept note-2: -The Compact Qwik (CQ) auto outlet is a part of Mahindra’s initiative named ‘Pink Collars’ that promotes recruitment of women in core productive roles across the company’s automobile workshops.

Concept note-3: -The country’s automobile industry has humble roots. No, it was not the legendary Padmini! The first car to be made in India was the Hindustan 10 motor car which later came to be called as Hindustan Ambassador. It was a rebadged version of the Morris 10 series M through a technical collaboration with Morris Motors, UK.
