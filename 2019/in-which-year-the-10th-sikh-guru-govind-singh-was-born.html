In which year, the 10th Sikh guru Govind Singh was born?

(A) 1660

(B) ** 1666

(C) 1659

(D) 1668

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Guru Gobind Singh, original name Gobind Rāi, (born 1666, Patna, Bihar, India-died October 7, 1708, Nanded, Maharashtra), 10th and last of the personal Sikh Gurus (1675–1708), known chiefly for his creation of the Khalsa (Punjabi: “the Pure”), the military brotherhood of the Sikhs.

Concept note-2: -Guru Gobind Singh, the 10th guru of Sikhism, coined the sacred Khalsa Vani, often spoken by the Sikhs, “Waheguru ji ka Khalsa, Waheguru ji ki Fateh". He also founded the five principles of Khalsa. Guru Gobind Singh, the 10th guru of Sikhs, was born on December 22, 1666 in Patna, Bihar, according to the Julian calendar.

Concept note-3: -On December 22, 1666, Guru Gobind Singh Ji was born in Patna, and the day is celebrated as Guru Gobind Singh Jayanti. He is an inspiration for millions of people worldwide, and they pay tribute to the brave Sikh leader on this day.

Concept note-4: -Guru Govind singh jayanti 2022 celebrated on 29 December. This festival is also known as Prakash parv.

Concept note-5: -Guru Gobind Singh birth anniversary is being celebrated on December 29, 2022. The place where Guru Gobind Singh was born is now Takhat Shri Harimander Ji Patna Sahib.In 1670, his family again moved to Punjab.In March 1672, his family moved to a place called Chak Nanaki, located in the Shivalik Hills of the Himalayas.
