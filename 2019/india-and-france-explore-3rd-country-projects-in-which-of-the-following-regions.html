India and France explore 3rd country projects in which of the following regions?

(A) ** Western Indian Ocean region

(B) Central India region

(C) Northeast India region

(D) South Central Region

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India, France explore 3rd country projects in Western Indian Ocean region.

Concept note-2: -Space. ISRO and CNES (French National Space Agency) have an umbrella agreement, operating successfully since 1993, under which joint missions like Megha-Tropiques and SARAL have been successfully developed.

Concept note-3: -2. The areas of Defence & Security cooperation, Space cooperation and Civil nuclear cooperation constitute the principal pillars of our Strategic Partnership with France.

Concept note-4: -Réunion (/riːˈjuːnjən/; French: La Réunion, [la ʁe.y.njɔ̃] ( listen); previously Île Bourbon; Reunionese Creole: La Rényon) is an island in the Indian Ocean that is an overseas department and region of France.

Concept note-5: -Which satellite was developed by India and France? Note : Megha-Tropiques satellite was developed by India and France. It was launched on 12 October, 2011 by PSLV-C18.
