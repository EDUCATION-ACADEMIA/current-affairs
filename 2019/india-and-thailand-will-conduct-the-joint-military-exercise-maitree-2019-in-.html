India and Thailand will conduct the Joint Military Exercise MAITREE-2019 in ____

(A) Odisha

(B) ** Meghalaya

(C) Andra Pradesh

(D) Chhattisgarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This is the 12th edition of Indo-Thailand joint exercise – MAITREE-2019 being held as per the United Nations mandate. A joint military exercise between the Indian Army and Royal Thailand Army commenced at the Joint Training Node, Umroi military station in Meghalaya’s Ri Bhoi district on Monday.

Concept note-2: -What is the Maitree Exercise? Exercise Maitree is a bilateral exercise between the Indian Army and the Royal Thai Army, started off in 2006. It is an annual event between the troops of the armies of both countries. It is also called Indo-Thailand Joint Exercise.

Concept note-3: -Ex Dharma Guardian – 2022 is a joint military exercise between India and Japan, is being conducted at the Foreign Training Node in Belagavi.

Concept note-4: -Notably, the Indo-US military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas 2022’ commenced in Uttarakhand’s Auli on November 19.
