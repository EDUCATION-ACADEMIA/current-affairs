Concept note-1: -What is Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA)? The Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement or DTAA is a tax treaty signed between India and another country ( or any two/multiple countries) so that taxpayers can avoid paying double taxes on their income earned from the source country as well as the residence country.
