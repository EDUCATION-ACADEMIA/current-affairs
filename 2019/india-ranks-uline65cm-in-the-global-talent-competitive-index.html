India ranks ______ in the global talent competitive index.

(A) ** 80

(B) 72

(C) 65

(D) 44

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Among Asian countries studied, Singapore ranked 2nd, while Vietnam came in 74th; Thailand 75th; Indonesia 82nd; the Philippines ranked 80th, and India ranked 101st. The Global Talent Competitiveness Index (GTCI) 2022, released on 3 November, saw Malaysia taking the 45th spot among 133 countries ranked around the world.

Concept note-2: -International Institute for Management Development (IMD) World Competitiveness Center’s “World Talent Ranking Report 2021” has been released. India ranks 56th in the report. Europe continues to dominate the rankings.

Concept note-3: -Economic performance: It has improved from 37th in 2021 to 28th in 2022. Government efficiency: It has improved from 46th in 2021 to 45th in 2022. Business efficiency: It saw a huge improvement from 32nd rank in 2021 to 23rd in 2022. Infrastructure: It on the other hand, saw no change at 49.
