Indian Badminton Tournament was held in ____

(A) Pune

(B) Mumbai

(C) ** New Delhi

(D) Surat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian National Badminton Championships is a tournament organized to crown the best badminton players in India since 1934.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 India Open was the first tournament of the 2022 BWF World Tour and was part of the India Open championships, which had been held since 1973. The tournament was organized by the Badminton Association of India with sanction from the Badminton World Federation.

Concept note-3: -Men’s singles player Lakshya Sen and the men’s doubles pair of Chirag Shetty and Satwiksairaj Ranklireddy entered the tournament as the reigning champions, having won their respective events in the 2022 edition.

Concept note-4: -The 2022 Malaysia Open (officially known as the Petronas Malaysia Open 2022 for sponsorship reasons) was a badminton tournament that took place at the Axiata Arena, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, from 28 June to 3 July 2022 and had a total prize of US$675,000.
