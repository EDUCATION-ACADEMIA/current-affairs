India’s Asian champion Gopi Thonakal was qualified for World Athletics Championships 2019. He is associated with ______

(A) ** Marathon

(B) High jump

(C) Long jump

(D) Hammer throw

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is fourth. India finished fourth in the tournament, with 17 medals (3 Gold, 7 silver and 7 bronze). The 23rd Asian Athletics Championships were held at Khalifa International Stadium, Doha, Qatar in 2019.

Concept note-2: -India won its first ever medal in the World Championships in Athletics (now known as World Athletics Championships) when Anju Bobby George made her historical leap, clearing 6.7 m and securing the bronze medal in long jump, in France in 2003.

Concept note-3: -US wins most Gold, India places 33rd at the 2022 World Athletics Championships. The World Athletics Championships were held in America for the first time.
