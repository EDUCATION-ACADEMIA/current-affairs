India’s first private-sector Howitzer gun-manufacturing plant has come up in which of the following cities?

(A) Hyderabad

(B) Kochi

(C) Dehradun

(D) ** Surat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first private sector howitzer gun-making unit is located in Hazira, Gujarat. It is a transshipment port and a suburb in the Surat district of Gujarat. The contract was won by Larsen and Toubro to supply 100 self-propelled gun systems in the army.

Concept note-2: -Upgrade. Advanced Weapons and Equipment India upgraded 155 mm/45 calibre into 155 mm/52 calibre gun which can now fire upto 42 km. Dhanush weighs less than 14 tons, and is considered the lightest howitzer in the 155 mm category. The newly upgraded gun has double baffle muzzle brake and retractable barrel.

Concept note-3: -The Advanced Towed Artillery Gun System (ATAGS) is a towed 155 mm/52 calibre howitzer that is being developed for the Indian Army by Armament Research and Development Establishment (ARDE), Tata Advanced Systems and Kalyani Strategic Systems.

Concept note-4: -: a short cannon used to fire projectiles at medium muzzle velocities and with relatively high trajectories.
