India’s Vidisha Baliyan was crowned the Miss Deaf World 2019 title. She belongs to which state?

(A) Karnataka

(B) Andra Pradesh

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) ** Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vidisha Baliyan is an Indian female deaf model and a retired tennis player. She wins the Miss Deaf World title in 2019. She is from Uttar Pradesh’s Muzzafarnagar city.

Concept note-2: -Miss World 2019, the 69th edition of the Miss World pageant, was held on 14 December 2019 at the ExCeL London in London, United Kingdom. Vanessa Ponce of Mexico crowned her successor Toni-Ann Singh of Jamaica at the end of the event.

Concept note-3: -Suman Rao was crowned Miss World India 2019 on Saturday 15 June at a star-studded ceremony held at Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Indoor Stadium in Mumbai.

Concept note-4: -India won its first title in 1966 (Rieta Faria) & last in 2017 (Manushi Chillar).
