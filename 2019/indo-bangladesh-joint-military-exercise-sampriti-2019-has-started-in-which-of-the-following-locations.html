Indo-Bangladesh joint military exercise ’Sampriti-2019’ has started in which of the following locations?

(A) ** Tangail

(B) Hooghly

(C) Barguna

(D) Sylhet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The eighth edition of Exercise Sampriti, commenced at the Bongobondhu Cantonment, Tangail Bangladesh with an impressive opening ceremony on 03 March 2019.

Concept note-2: -India-Bangladesh joint military exercise Sampriti culminates in Jashore. The 12-day long joint military exercise between India and Bangladesh ‘Sampriti’ culminated after a validation exercise at Jashore in Bangladesh.

Concept note-3: -As part of the ongoing India Bangladesh bilateral defence cooperation, a joint military training exercise Ex SAMPRITI-X is being conducted at Jashore Military Station in Bangladesh from 05 June to 16 June 2022.

Concept note-4: -Exercise SAMPRITI is an important bilateral defence cooperation endeavour between India and Bangladesh which is hosted alternately by both countries. The exercise was conducted from June 5 to June 16, 2022.

Concept note-5: -Exercise “SURYA KIRAN” is conducted annually between India and Nepal with the aim to enhance interoperability in jungle warfare & counter terrorism operations in mountainous terrain and HADR under UN mandate.
