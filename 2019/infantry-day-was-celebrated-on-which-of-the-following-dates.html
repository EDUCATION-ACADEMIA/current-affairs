Infantry Day was celebrated on which of the following dates?

(A) ** October 27

(B) October 26

(C) October 28

(D) October 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Infantry Day is commemorated on 27 October every year to acknowledge the contributions of Infantry, the largest fighting arm of the Indian Army.

Concept note-2: -National days on Fri Oct 27th, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s National American Beer Day, Frankenstein Friday, National Black Cat Day, National Bandana Day, National Mentoring Day… and much more!

Concept note-3: -India celebrates 75th Army Day today India celebrates Army Day on January 15 every year to commemorate the achievements of the first Indian Commander in Chief of the Indian Army - General (later Field Marshal) K.M. Cariappa.

Concept note-4: -Every year the Infantry Day is celebrated on October 27 to honour, respect and pay tribute to the courageous Indian infantry soldiers who fought and were martyred while saving the nation and its people when the Pakistan Army invaded Kashmir at the time of Independence.

Concept note-5: -On 15 January 2022, India celebrated its 75th Indian Army Day in New Delhi. Army Day marks a day to salute the valiant soldiers who sacrificed their lives to protect the country and its citizens.
