International Day of world’s Indigenous people is celebrated on ____

(A) 7th August

(B) 8th August

(C) ** 9th August

(D) 10th August

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In order to raise awareness of the needs of these population groups, including their health needs, every 9 August commemorates the International Day of the World’s Indigenous Peoples, chosen in recognition of the first meeting of the UN Working Group on Indigenous Populations held in Geneva in 1982.

Concept note-2: -In order to raise awareness of the needs of these population groups, including their health needs, every 9 August commemorates the International Day of the World’s Indigenous Peoples, chosen in recognition of the first meeting of the UN Working Group on Indigenous Populations held in Geneva in 1982.

Concept note-3: -The Department of Economic and Social Affairs (DESA) is organizing a virtual commemoration of the International Day from 9 am to 11am (EST) on Tuesday, 9 August 2022, focusing on this year’s theme: “The Role of Indigenous Women in the Preservation and Transmission of Traditional Knowledge”.

Concept note-4: -International Day of the World’s Indigenous Peoples is celebrated as an all nations community celebration with artists, speakers, poets, performers, and a wide range of vendors and community service booths in Kingston, Ontario, by Ollin.

Concept note-5: -The day marked the first meeting in 1982 of the UN Working Group on indigenous populations. The UN General Assembly also declared 1995-2004 as the International Decade of World’s Indigenous Peoples.
