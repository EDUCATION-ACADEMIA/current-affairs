International Internet Day was observed on which date?

(A) ** 29 October

(B) 30 October

(C) 28 October

(D) 31 October

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is 29 October. International Internet Day is observed every year on 29 October. The day commemorates the anniversary of the first message that was sent between two computers on 29th October 1969 through ARPANET, the predecessor of the Internet.

Concept note-2: -National days on Sun Oct 29th, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s National Cat Day, National Internet Day, World Stroke Day, National Oatmeal Day, National Hermit Day… and much more!

Concept note-3: -The theme of safer internet day 2023 is ‘Want to talk about it? according to UK Safer Internet Centre. Safer Internet Day 2022 theme is “Together for a better internet”. In 2021, the theme was “Together for a better internet”.

Concept note-4: -International Internet Day is celebrated on October 29 every year across the world to celebrate the usage of the internet for the first time. The day marks the sending of the first electronic message which was transferred from one computer to another in 1969.
