ISO 31000:2018 is a standard related to which of the following tasks?

(A) Information technology

(B) Ships and marine technology

(C) Human resource management

(D) ** Risk management

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISO 31000:2018 provides guidelines on managing risk faced by organizations. The application of these guidelines can be customized to any organization and its context. ISO 31000:2018 provides a common approach to managing any type of risk and is not industry or sector specific.

Concept note-2: -ISO 31000, Risk management – Guidelines, provides principles, a framework and a process for managing risk. It can be used by any organization regardless of its size, activity or sector.
