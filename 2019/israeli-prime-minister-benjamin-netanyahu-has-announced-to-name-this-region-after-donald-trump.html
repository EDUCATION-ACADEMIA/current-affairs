Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has announced to name this region after Donald Trump.

(A) Kursi Beach

(B) Mount Bnei Rasan

(C) Jan Dhan Accoun

(D) ** Golan Heights

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Trump Heights (Hebrew: , Ramat Trump, [ʁaˈmat ˈtʁamp]) is a planned Israeli settlement in the Israeli-occupied Golan Heights named after and in honor of the 45th President of the United States, Donald Trump.

Concept note-2: -The Golan Heights is a rocky plateau in south-western Syria, about 60km (40 miles) south-west of Damascus and covers about 1,000 sq km. It has a political and strategic significance which belies its size.

Concept note-3: -On March 25, 2019, the United States officially recognized Jerusalem as the capital city of Israel.
