(B) Rajasthan

(C) ** Kerala

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Saxena made his first class debut for Madhya Pradesh on 17 December 2005 in the 2005-06 Ranji Trophy . He made his Twenty20 debut for Madhya Pradesh on 21 October 2009 in the 2009-10 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy.
