Jammu and Kashmir Reorganisation Bill 2019 provides ____ reservation for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) among the general category.

(A) 20%

(B) 14%

(C) 12%

(D) ** 10%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eligibility Criteria for EWS Certificate He shall not bear any caste certificate under his name. EWS reservation is only for the General category candidates. Family Income : The annual family income of the candidate must be less than Rs. 8 Lakhs per annum.

Concept note-2: -Eligibility[edit] Family income below 8 lakhs INR or as per state norms. Family owned agricultural land less than 5 acres. Residential flat owned by family which is less than 1000 sq. ft.

Concept note-3: -The validity of the EWS certificate remains for up to 1 year.

Concept note-4: -The Jammu and Kashmir Reorganisation Act, 2019 is an act of the parliament of India containing provisions to reconstitute the Indian-administered state of Jammu and Kashmir into two Indian-administered union territories (UTs) called Jammu and Kashmir, and Ladakh, and becoming effective on 31 October 2019.
