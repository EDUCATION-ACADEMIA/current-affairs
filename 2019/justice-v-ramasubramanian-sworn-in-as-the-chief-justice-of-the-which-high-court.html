Justice V Ramasubramanian sworn in as the Chief Justice of the which high court?

(A) Patna High Court

(B) Meghalaya High Court

(C) Jharkhand High Court

(D) ** Himachal Pradesh High Court

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ramasubramanian (born 30 June 1958) is a judge of Supreme Court of India. He is former chief justice of Himachal Pradesh High Court.

Concept note-2: -Authoring the judgment, Justice Ramasubramanian reasoned that the RBI’s circular imposed disproportionate restrictions, observing that it had failed to demonstrate that virtual currency trading causes harm to banks and other types of financial institutions. In effect, the judgment opens up India to crypto-currencies.

Concept note-3: -The new Chief Justice of India is Justice D.Y. Chandarchud who took his oath in presence of the President of India, Droupadi Murmu on 8th November 2022.

Concept note-4: -Justice Dhananjaya Yashwant Chandrachud was sworn in as the 50th Chief Justice of India on November 9, 2022. He will serve as the CJI for two years.

Concept note-5: -President Appoints Justice Manindra Mohan Shrivastava As Acting Chief Justice Of Rajasthan High Court.
