Ladu Kishore Swain, who passed away recently, was the current MP from which Lok Sabha constituency of Odisha?

(A) ** Aska

(B) Keonjhar

(C) Jagatsinghpur

(D) Sundargarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ladu Kishore Swain (8 July 1947 – 5 February 2019) was an Indian politician who was the Member of Parliament in the Lok Sabha from Aska in the Indian state of Odisha.

Concept note-2: -21 Lok Sabha members are elected.

Concept note-3: -Pratap Chandra Sarangi (born 4 January 1955), was a Minister of State in the Government of India for Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries and Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises. He is a Politician from Balasore, Odisha and serves as a National Executive member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).
