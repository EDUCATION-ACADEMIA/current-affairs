Losar Festival is celebrated in which state/UT?

(A) Manipur

(B) Nagaland

(C) Mizoram

(D) ** Ladakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ladakh celebrated Losar Festival to mark the Ladakhi New Year. Losar Festival is celebrated in Ladakh on 24th December 2022. Ladakh celebrated Losar Festival to mark the Ladakhi New Year.

Concept note-2: -Ladakh celebrates ‘Losar’ the traditional new year with symbolic performances in a low key affair. Ladakh is celebrating, Losar, the traditional new year today. Losar is being celebrated by Buddhist Community in the region with elaborated symbolic festivities in the name of God and Goddess.

Concept note-3: -Marking the start of spring and the first day of the lunar calendar, Losar is a popular festival of Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-4: -Losar (Tibetan: , Wylie: lo-gsar; “new year") also known as Tibetan New Year, is a festival in Tibetan Buddhism. The holiday is celebrated on various dates depending on location (Tibet, Bhutan, Nepal, India) tradition.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. Losar Festival is celebrated in the Ladakh region. It is celebrated to mark the beginning of the Ladakhi New Year.
