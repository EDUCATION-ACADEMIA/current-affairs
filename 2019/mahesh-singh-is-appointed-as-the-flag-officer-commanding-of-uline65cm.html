Mahesh Singh is appointed as the Flag Officer Commanding of ______

(A) West Bengal Naval Area

(B) ** Karnataka Naval Area

(C) Tamil Nadu Naval Area

(D) Orissa Naval Area

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -REAR ADMIRAL AN PRAMOD ASSUMES CHARGE AS FLAG OFFICER COMMANDING MAHARASTRA NAVAL AREA (FOMA) Rear Admiral Sandeep Mehta, VSM, handed over the baton of Flag Officer Commanding Maharashtra Naval Area to Rear Admiral AN Pramod on 28 November 2022 at an elegant ceremonial parade held at INS Kunjali Parade ground.

Concept note-2: -Vice Admiral Biswajit Dasgupta, PVSM, AVSM, YSM, VSM Vice Admiral Biswajit Dasgupta assumed charge as the Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Eastern Naval Command (ENC) on 01 December 2021.

Concept note-3: -Vice Admiral Biswajit Dasgupta is the current FOC-in-C ENC, who took over on 30 November 2021.
