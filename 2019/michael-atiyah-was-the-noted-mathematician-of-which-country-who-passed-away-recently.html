Michael Atiyah was the noted mathematician of which country, who passed away recently?

(A) Germany

(B) ** United Kingdom

(C) France

(D) United States

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Michael Atiyah worked in Topology and Geometry and was best known for his work on K-theory and the Atiyah-Singer Index Theorem. He was awarded a Fields Medal in 1966.

Concept note-2: -Terence Chi-Shen Tao FAA FRS (Chinese: ; born 17 July 1975) is an Australian and American mathematician. He is a professor of mathematics at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), where he holds the James and Carol Collins chair.

Concept note-3: -Pythagoras (570 BCE -500/490 BCE) Much of his work is lost – enshrined in the confidentiality of the secret society he led. However, his teachings remain influential and some have called him the first pure mathematician.
