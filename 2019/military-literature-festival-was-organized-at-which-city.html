Military Literature Festival was organized at which city?

(A) ** Chandigarh

(B) Indore

(C) Aizawl

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In December 2017 for the first time, a military literature festival was held at the lake club in Chandigarh. The ambience of the lake club complementing the spirit of the literature festival with its lake and wooded campus, set in the lap of nature.

Concept note-2: -The Jaipur Literature Festival, or JLF, is an annual literary festival which takes place in the Indian city of Jaipur each year in the month of January.

Concept note-3: -The Jaipur Literature Festival 2023 will begin on Thursday at Hotel Clarks Amer in the city.

Concept note-4: -The lit fest will happen from January 19 to 23 at Hotel Clarks Amer, Jaipur.

Concept note-5: -Namita Gokhale and William Dalrymple are the festival directors and Teamwork Arts’ Sanjoy Roy produces it. Surina Narula is the founding sponsor and advisor of the Jaipur Literature Festival. The festival goes on for five days, where people engage in discussions and sessions on different topics.
