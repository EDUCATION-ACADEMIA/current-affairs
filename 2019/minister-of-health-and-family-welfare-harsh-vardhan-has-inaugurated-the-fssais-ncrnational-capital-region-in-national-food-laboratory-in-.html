CURRENT AFFAIRS-2019
[SOURCES]

Minister of Health and Family Welfare, Harsh Vardhan has inaugurated the FSSAI’s NCR(National Capital Region) in National Food Laboratory in ____

(A) ** Delhi

(B) Lucknow

(C) Surat

(D) Jaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Food Laboratory(NFL), Delhi NCR at Ghaziabad was inaugurated by Dr. Harsh Vardhan, Hon’ble Union Minister of Health and Family Welfare in the August presence of Shri Ashwini Kumar Choubey, Hon’ble Minister of State for Health & Family Welfare, Smt.

Concept note-2: -These are apart from more than 200 NABL accredited FSSAI notified laboratories that form the national food testing network, the statement said.

Concept note-3: -The National Food Laboratory (NFL) Ghaziabad is an apex Food Laboratory of India dealing in Quality testing of food under FSS Act. 2006. The laboratory has end to end analytical capability to test any food samples for compliance as per FSS Act and Rules/Regulations.

Concept note-4: -The theme for this day was ‘Healthy Diets for a Zero Hunger World’. To mark the occasion, Dr Harsh Vardhan also launched the ‘Food Safety Mitra (FSM)’ scheme, along with the ‘Eat Right Jacket’, and ‘Eat Right Jhola’ to strengthen food safety administration and scale up the ‘Eat Right India’ movement.
