[SOURCES]

Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports recognises Kudo International Federation India as;

(A) Kudo Sports Federation

(B) ** National Sport Federation

(C) Kudo Association

(D) Indian Association of Kudo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports recognises Kudo International Federation India as National Sport Federation.

Concept note-2: -LIST OF RECOGNISED NATIONAL SPORTS FEDERATIONS FOR THE YEAR 2020-2021 | Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports | Government of India.

Concept note-3: -The Kudo revolution was started in India by Soshihan Mehul Vora president of KIFI Association, on the eve of the 3rd Akshay Kumar International Invitational Karate-Do Tournament, this was the first time ever a six member all Japan champion team gave demonstrations and taught students and instructors in India.

Concept note-4: -The Department of Sports & Youth Welfare function is to create sports infrastructure, maintenance of the infrastructure, development of playfields, create human resource for sports and physical education. All round development of the youth of the state through activities framed under the Sports & Youth Welfare policy.
