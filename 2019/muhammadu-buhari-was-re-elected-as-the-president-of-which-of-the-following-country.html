Muhammadu Buhari was re-elected as the president of which of the following country?

(A) Morocco

(B) Sudan

(C) ** Nigeria

(D) Libya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Muhammadu Buhari ( listen) GCFR (born 17 December 1942) is a Nigerian politician, who has served as president of Nigeria since 2015.

Concept note-2: -Personal Life. President Buhari was born on 17thDecember, 1942 in Daura, Katsina State to Adamu and Zulaiha Buhari. President Buhari was raised by his mother, following the death of his father when he was about four years old.

Concept note-3: -The first ceremonial president, who served during the first republic was Nnamdi Azikiwe, while the first executive president of Nigeria was Shehu Shagari, he was also the first president to be elected to the position.

Concept note-4: -The president is directly elected in national elections to a four-year term, along with the vice president. Muhammadu Buhari is the 15th and current president of Nigeria, having assumed office on 29 May 2015.
