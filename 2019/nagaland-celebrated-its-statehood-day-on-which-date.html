Nagaland celebrated its statehood day on which date?

(A) 30th November

(B) ** 1st December

(C) 2nd December

(D) 3rd December

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nagaland became the 16th state of India on December 1, 1963. It is home to a rich variety of natural, cultural, and environmental resources.

Concept note-2: -Recently, Nagaland has celebrated its 60th Statehood Day on December 1st 2022. Nagaland statehood day also marks the beginning of Hornbill festival in Nagaland.

Concept note-3: -Nagaland is celebrating its 60th statehood day today. It became the 16th State of the Union of India on 1st December 1963.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Nagaland. Nagaland’s celebrated its 59th Statehood Day on December 1, 2021. On this day in 1963, it became the 16th state of India.

Concept note-5: -The State, on 1st December 2020, celebrated its 58th Statehood Day at Nagaland Civil Secretariat Plaza, Kohima. The event began with the Review of Parade by DGP Nagaland John Longkumer IPS. Chief Minister Neiphiu Rio undertook the General Salute and delivered the Statehood day speech.
