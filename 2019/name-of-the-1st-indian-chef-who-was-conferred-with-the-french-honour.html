Name of the 1st Indian chef who was conferred with the French honour.

(A) Saransh Goila

(B) Vikas Khanna

(C) ** Priyam Chatterjee

(D) Sanjeev Kapoor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Priyam Chatterjee . Priyam Chatterjee is the first Indian chef to receive this French honour Chevalier de l’Ordre du Mérite Agricole, Given by the French government in the Order of Agricultural Merit.

Concept note-2: -Chef Bhatia, who is also the first Indian chef to be awarded a Michelin star for his restaurant Zaika in London, is the only Indian in the culinary world to receive two Michelin stars.

Concept note-3: -Cooking legend Julia Child introduced French cuisine to American cooks in 1963 with WGBH’s pioneering television series, The French Chef. She was passionate about food and she changed the way Americans cook and eat. WGBH is proud to look back at her accomplishments and how we helped to make her a household name.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Chef Atul Kochhar is the first Indian to be awarded a Michelin star.

Concept note-5: -About Auguste Escoffier Beginning his career at 13 years of age, Auguste Escoffier was quickly consumed with ambition to become the best and moved steadily upward until he was known as the King of Chefs, Chef of Kings.
