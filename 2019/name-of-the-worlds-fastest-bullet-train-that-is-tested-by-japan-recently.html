Name of the world’s fastest bullet train that is tested by Japan recently.

(A) ** ALFA-X

(B) Renfe AVE

(C) Korail KTX

(D) Maglev

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of August 2022, the fastest train on Earth, based on its record speed, is the Japanese L0 Series Maglev with a record speed of 603 kilometers per hour.

Concept note-2: -1: Shanghai Maglev - 460 kph/286 mph (China) The world’s fastest public train is also unique – it’s the only link in the world currently carrying passengers using magnetic levitation (Maglev) rather than conventional steel wheels on steel rails.

Concept note-3: -China has unveiled world’s fastest train maglev this is capable to attain the top speed to 600 km per hour. China’s train maker CRRC released the high-speed train at a railway industry trade fair, in Berlin, Germany.

Concept note-4: -The bullet train, or “Shinkansen”, is a type of passenger train which operates on Japan’s high-speed railway network. Capable of reaching a maximum speed of 320kms per hour, the bullet train offers riders an exceptionally unique and efficient travel experience.
