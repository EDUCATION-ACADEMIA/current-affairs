Name the Bank which became the second largest public sector undertaking (PSU) bank.

(A) Axis Bank

(B) ** Bank of Baroda

(C) Union Bank of India

(D) Dena Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank of Baroda (BOB or BoB) is an Indian public sector bank headquartered in Vadodara, Gujarat. It is the second largest public sector bank in India after State Bank of India, with 132 million customers, a total business of US$218 billion, and a global presence of 100 overseas offices.

Concept note-2: -Punjab National Bank The bank was established in 1894. PNB bank is merged with OBC Bank and United Bank. The new bank will become the second-largest Public Sector Bank of India with 18 Lakh crore rupees and the second largest in terms of branch network across the country.

Concept note-3: -#3 Bank of Baroda Third on our list is Bank of Baroda (BOB).

Concept note-4: -PSBs in India: On the basis of the assets held by the bank, SBI tops the list of top 10 PSBs in India. It is followed by PNB and Bank of Baroda. SBI holds total assets worth a whopping Rs 45.34 lakh crores.

Concept note-5: -Motilal Oswal tracks top 7 PSU banks - State Bank of India, Punjab National Bank, Bank of India, Bank of Baroda, Central Bank, Union Bank and Indian Bank and from the list its top picks are SBI, BoB and Central Bank.
