Name the bank which has received CCI Nod for Merger with Gruh Finance.

(A) RBL Bank

(B) Kotak Mahindra Bank

(C) ** Bandhan Bank

(D) IndusInd Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gruh Finance, the affordable housing finance arm of HDFC Ltd, was taken over in January by Bandhan Bank in a share-swap deal.

Concept note-2: -Subject to regulatory approvals, the merger shall take effect from January 1, 2019. The share exchange ratio is decided at 568:1000, which means shareholders of GRUH Finance will receive 568 shares of Bandhan Bank for every 1000 shares that they hold of GRUH Finance.

Concept note-3: -HDFC acquires 9.89% stake in Bandhan Bank As many as 15.93 crore shares of the bank was transferred to HDFC, Bandhan Bank said.

Concept note-4: -Mr. Ghosh has over 30 years of experience in microfinance and development terrain. He founded Bandhan in 2001 as a not-for-profit enterprise for financial inclusion and women empowerment.

Concept note-5: -HDFC, a public shareholder in Bandhan Bank, held a 9.89 per cent stake in the private lender at the end of the December quarter, shareholding data with the exchange showed.
