Name the city which is choosed by Facebook as base for WhatsApp Pay as the centre for rolling WhatsApp Pay globally.

(A) Dubai

(B) New York

(C) ** London

(D) Berlin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Facebook has chosen London as the centre for the global roll-out of WhatsApp Pay, before the social media giant launches its digital payments service on the mobile messaging platform in India, according to a media report.

Concept note-2: -Yes, WhatsApp Payment features are only available in India, Brazil, and select users in the US for now. India is actually the fastest-growing digital payment market, which means the competition is fierce.

Concept note-3: -The payment gateway can be operated through Facebook, Instagram, Messenger and WhatsApp.

Concept note-4: -WhatsApp Pay isn’t available for all users in India. WhatsApp beta users will most likely have the feature on their app. To start using the service, a WhatsApp Pay user needs to initiate a payment to a contact. Once the request is received, the user can set up their UPI account on WhatsApp.

Concept note-5: -WhatsApp Pay: In-Chat Payment. The WhatsApp In-Chat Payment feature will allow users to make payments to anyone from their WhatsApp contact list. The new option will enable WhatsApp users to both send and receive money.
