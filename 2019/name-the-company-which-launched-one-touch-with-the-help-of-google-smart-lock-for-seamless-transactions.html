Name the company which launched One Touch with the help of Google Smart Lock for seamless transactions?

(A) Mobikwik

(B) Paytm

(C) Free charge

(D) ** PayPal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PayPal connects a global network of buyers and sellers on its open payment’s platform.

Concept note-2: -Security that just works: with Smart Lock turned on, your Android phone stays unlocked when it’s connected to a trusted device - like your smartwatch - or when it’s in a familiar spot - like your pocket. So it’s ready to use when you need it.

Concept note-3: -Google Smart Lock is an unlocking feature for Android devices and Chromebooks. In Android, the system allows you to keep your phone unlocked in certain preapproved, known-to-be-safe circumstances. In ChromeOS, it empowers your Android phone to keep your computer unlocked.

Concept note-4: -Google Smart Lock allows you to keep all your passwords in one place, synced to your Google account, so you can access them across devices.
