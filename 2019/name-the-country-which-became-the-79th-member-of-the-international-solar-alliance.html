Name the country which became the 79th member of the International Solar Alliance.

(A) ** St. Vincent and Grenadines

(B) Jamaica

(C) Dominican Republic

(D) Cuba

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-3: -International Solar Alliance. The following 112 countries have signed the ISA Framework Agreement: Commonwealth of Australia. Peoples Republic of Bangladesh.
