Name the country which has test-fired nuclear-capable surface-to-surface ballistic missile Ghaznavi?

(A) Israel

(B) ** Pakistan

(C) North Korea

(D) Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pakistan earlier successfully test-fired nuclear-capable surface-to-surface ballistic missile Ghaznavi.

Concept note-2: -Pakistan successfully test-fires nuclear-capable surface-to-surface ballistic missile. Pakistan on Thursday successfully test-fired a nuclear-capable surface-to-surface ballistic missile which can strike targets up to 290 kilometres, the army said.

Concept note-3: -On 3 November 2022, North Korea reportedly fired at least one ballistic missile off its east coast, including one, believed to be a long-range missile, that flew over and past Japan.

Concept note-4: -India successfully test fired nuclear-capable Agni-V ballistic missile having a range of over 5,000 km, marking a significant boost to the country’s strategic deterrence.
