Name the country which won the T20 Physical Disability (PD) World Cricket Series 2019.

(A) ** India

(B) England

(C) Bangladesh

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which country’s team has won the 2019 T20 Physical Disability Cricket World Series? Notes: Indian cricket team has won the T20 Physical Disability World Cricket Series in England . In the final, India defeated England by 36 runs at the Blackfinch New Road ground in Worcester.

Concept note-2: -New Zealand finished with 15 runs, the Super Over tied, but England’s superior boundary count (26 to New Zealand’s 17) meant they won the World Cup title for the first time in four final appearances.

Concept note-3: -Team India has won the T20 World Cup only one time in 2007. Indian cricket team beat Pakistan in the final by 5 runs to win the very first World T20 trophy in 2007.

Concept note-4: -C Shivakumar, fondly called Sachin Shiva, from Madurai has become the first player from Tamil Nadu to be selected as the captain of the Indian physically challenged cricket team.

Concept note-5: -In 2022, a new disability cricket league was launched called the Disability Premier League. This competition will see the best disabled players compete against one another at a star-studded event over three weekends late in the summer.
