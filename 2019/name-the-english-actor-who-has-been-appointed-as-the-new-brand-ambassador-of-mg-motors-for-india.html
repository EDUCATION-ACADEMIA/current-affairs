Name the English actor who has been appointed as the new brand ambassador of MG Motors for India.

(A) Mark Ruffalo

(B) ** Benedict Cumberbatch

(C) Tom Hiddleston

(D) Robert Downey, Jr

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Marque carmaker MG (Morris Garages) India has announced its association with British actor Benedict Cumberbatch as its brand ambassador. The actor, known for his stellar roles in various popular TV shows and movies, will be the face of MG India.

Concept note-2: -His social and philanthropic work, as well as his support for inclusivity beyond gender is also in sync with our core brand ideology of nurturing diversity. We are glad to have Benedict Cumberbatch as our brand ambassador to represent MG.”

Concept note-3: -MG India announces Benedict Cumberbatch as brand ambassador for the Hector. -British actor Benedict Cumberbatch is the brand ambassador for MG Motor India. -The MG Hector will be the first car from the British carmaker in the country. -The Hector will come with class-leading features and internet capabilities.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi, October 23, 2019: MG (Morris Garages) Motor India today announced Benedict Cumberbatch as the face and ambassador for their upcoming Electric Car “MG ZS EV” in India. The announcement comes as a further extension of the ongoing association between two British icons.

Concept note-5: -Rajeev Chaba is the President and Managing Director of MG Motor India, the India unit of the iconic Morris Garages British brand. He has over three decades of industry experience.
