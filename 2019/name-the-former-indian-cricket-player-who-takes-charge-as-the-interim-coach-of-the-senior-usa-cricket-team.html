(A) Manoj Prabhakar

(B) ** Kiran More

(C) Dilip Vengsarkar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Ravi Shastri. He is 24th number of Coach of Indian Cricket Team. His contract is from 2017 to 2021.
