Name the India delegation, who lead the 2nd meeting of China-India high-level mechanism on cultural and people-to-people exchanges held in Beijing, China .

(A) ** Jai Shankar

(B) Amit Shah

(C) Nirmala Sitharaman

(D) Ramesh Pokhriyal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Wang Yi and Subrahmanyam Jaishankar , Minister of External Affairs of India , Co-chair the Meeting.

Concept note-2: -The main purpose of the MoU is flood control and disaster mitigation in downstream areas. Thus, a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with People’s Republic of China was signed on April, 2005 upon provision of hydrological information of the River Sutlej/Langqen Zangbo in Flood Season by China to India.

Concept note-3: -How are India’s Trade Relations with China? One of the Biggest Partners: China is India’s second biggest trading partner after the US. In 2021-22, Indo-China bilateral trade was USD 115.83 billion - 11.2% of India’s total merchandise trade of USD 1,035 billion (Indo-US trade - 11.54%).

Concept note-4: -There are regular Party-to-Party exchanges between the Communist Party of China and political parties in India. In order to facilitate exchanges between Indian states and Chinese provinces, the two sides have also established a States/Provincial Leaders Forum.

Concept note-5: -ndia and China signed Education Exchange Programme (EEP) in 2006, which is an umbrella agreement for educational cooperation between the two countries. Under this agreement, government scholarships are awarded to the students , by both the sides, in recognized institutions of higher learning in each other’s country.
