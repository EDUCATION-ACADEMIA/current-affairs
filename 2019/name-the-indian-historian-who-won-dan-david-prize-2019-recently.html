Name the Indian historian who won Dan David Prize 2019, recently.

(A) M. Chidananda Murthy

(B) George Menachery

(C) Dipesh Chakrabarty

(D) ** Sanjay Subrahmanyam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Noted Indian historian, Sanjay Subrahmanyam won Israel’s prestigious Dan David Prize for 2019. He was awarded for his work on inter-cultural encounters between Asians, Europeans, and people of North and South America during the early modern era.
