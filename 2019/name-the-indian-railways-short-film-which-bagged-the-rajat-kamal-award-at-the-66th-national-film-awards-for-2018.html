Name the Indian Railway’s Short film which bagged the ‘Rajat-Kamal’ award at the 66th National Film Awards for 2018.

(A) Paroksh

(B) ** Madhubani-The Station of Colours

(C) The God Must Be Crazy

(D) Half Ticket 2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Badhai Ho’ bagged the award for Best Popular Film Providing Wholesome Entertainment. Hindi movie ‘Padman’ was declared Best Film on Social Issues.

Concept note-2: -Director: Addinath Kothare Rajat Kamal and Rs. 1,50,000/- (each) The film traces the story of a dry and parched village that comes together to generate their most valuable resource – Water.

Concept note-3: -Gujarati film ‘Hellaro’ was declared Best Feature Film winner while ‘Badhai Ho’ bagged the award for Best Popular Film Providing Wholesome Entertainment.

Concept note-4: -Hellaro has been awarded as the Best Feature Film in the 66th National Film Awards, 2019. Bulbul Can Sing won the award for the Best Assamese Film. Badhaai Ho won the Best Popular Film award. Andhadhun won the award for the Best Hindi Film.
