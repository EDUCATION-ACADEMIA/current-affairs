Name the India’s largest bank which has signed an MoU with the Bank of China to boost business opportunities.

(A) Canara Bank

(B) ** State Bank of India

(C) ICICI Bank

(D) Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s largest bank State Bank of India (SBI) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the world’s third-largest lender by capital size, Bank of China , to enhance business synergies between them, reports Press Trust of India (PTI).

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The SBI’s Shanghai branch is now connected to China’s National Advance Payment System (CNAPS), becoming the first Indian Bank to operationalise it.

Concept note-3: -State Bank of India (SBI) is the oldest bank in India. State Bank of India is also the largest bank in India.
