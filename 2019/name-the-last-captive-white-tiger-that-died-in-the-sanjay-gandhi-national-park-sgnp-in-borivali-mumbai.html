Name the last captive white tiger, that died in the Sanjay Gandhi National Park (SGNP) in Borivali, Mumbai.

(A) Sidharth

(B) Renuk

(C) ** Bajirao

(D) Avni

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai: Bajirao, the last captive white tiger at the Sanjay Gandhi National Park (SGNP) in Borivali, died at the age of 18.

Concept note-2: -In May 2019, the last captive White tiger in Mumbai died. The name of the tiger is Bajirao. The last captive white tiger inside Sanjay Gandhi National Park (SGNP) died of age-related complications. The animal was 18 years old.

Concept note-3: -to hunt for the white tiger you need not go any further but to this place.. in fact they have 2 of them.

Concept note-4: -There are 4 tigers that are kept semi-confined in a 20 ha (49.42 acres) fenced area that is toured by the buses.

Concept note-5: -Mammals. Small herds of Spotted Deer, a solitary Sambhar, a darting Barking Deer or being surprised by a Black-naped Hare running across your path are just some of the pleasant surprises of the National Park, but nothing can really match the awe, fear and goose-pimply feeling when one is confronted by a large Leopard.
