Name the Maritime Exercise between Singapore and India that held in Singapore recently.

(A) ** SIMBEX

(B) Varuna

(C) INDRA NAV

(D) SLINEX

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Singapore India Maritime Bilateral Exercise (SIMBEX) is an annual bilateral naval exercise conducted by the Indian Navy and the Republic of Singapore Navy (RSN).

Concept note-2: -The Singapore Army’s Singapore Light Weight Howitzers (SLWH) firing off during a joint live-firing with the Indian Army. The Singapore Army and the Indian Army successfully concluded Exercise Agni Warrior (XAW) 2022, in Devlali, India, from 13 November to 30 November 2022.

Concept note-3: -India Navy conducts maritime exercise with Singapore at Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-4: -Answer- Visakhapatnam is the headquarters of the Eastern Naval Command. MILAN 2022 required a large infrastructure and hence this biennial multilateral naval exercise was conducted in Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-5: -21st EDITION OF INDIA FRANCE BILATERAL NAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the Western Seaboard today, 16 Jan 23.
