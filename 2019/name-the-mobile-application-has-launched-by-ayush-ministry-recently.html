Name the mobile application has launched by AYUSH Ministry, recently?

(A) Yoga Router

(B) ** Yoga locator

(C) Yoga trace

(D) Yoga map

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In a glittering ceremony that reflected the whole government approach of PM Modi and saw the participation of his six cabinet colleagues, Union Minister of Ayush Sarbanand Sonowal launched the ‘Y-Break’ mobile application at Vigyan Bhawan on Wednesday.

Concept note-2: -A mobile application, “Namaste Yoga” was also launched during the event. The event also marked the beginning of a series on Common Yoga Protocol to be telecast on DD India channel. The series is produced by produced Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga (MDNIY). International Day of Yoga is celebrated on June 21.

Concept note-3: -Maintain health by enhancing immunity. The Ministry of AYUSH presents the “Ayush Sanjivani” mobile application for understanding the measures adopted by public for enhancing immunity and keeping themselves healthy in the difficult COVID-19 situation.

Concept note-4: -Hon’ble Union Minister for Health and Family Welfare digitally launched the revamped CGHS (Central Government Health Scheme) website (www.cghs.gov.in) and “MyCGHS” mobile app.

Concept note-5: -This year, the Sixth Siddha Day celebration on the theme “Siddha Diet and Nutrition for Healthy Life” was jointly organized by Central Council for Research in Siddha (CCRS, Ministry of Ayush, Govt. of India), National Institute of Siddha (NIS, Ministry of Ayush, Govt.
