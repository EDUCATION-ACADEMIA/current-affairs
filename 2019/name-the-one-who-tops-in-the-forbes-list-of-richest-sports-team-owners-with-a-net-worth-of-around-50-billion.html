Name the one who tops in the Forbes list of richest sports team owners with a net worth of around $50 billion.

(A) Mateschitz

(B) Steve Ballmer

(C) ** Mukesh Ambani

(D) Hasso Plattner

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of March, when Forbes locked in net worths for the 2022 World’s Billionaires list, Los Angeles Clippers owner Steve Ballmer had reclaimed his title as the richest sports team owner on earth.

Concept note-2: -According to Forbes, Los Angeles Clippers owner, Steve Ballmer, who has a net worth of $83 billion is the richest sports team owner in 2022. Rob Walton, who recently bought the Denver Broncos, came in at no. 2 with an estimated net worth of $56.7 billion. His purchasing power was nearly impossible to match.

Concept note-3: -A new study from Sidelines.io revealed the Dallas Cowboys as the richest sports team in America in 2022. According to the website, out of the 152 teams in the NBA, NFL, MLB, MLS, and NHL that were analyzed, the Dallas Cowboys ranked first, followed by the New England Patriots.

Concept note-4: -Based on available data, with a net worth of approximately $91 billion, Mukesh Ambani, who owns the Indian Premier League cricket club Mumbai Indians, is the richest sports team owner in the world, followed by Carlos Slim (net worth $82 billion); and Steve Ballmer (net worth $80 billion).
