[SOURCES]

Name the Organisation, which has recently released the 2019 Global Food Policy Report (GFPR).

(A) ** International Food Policy Research Institute (IFPRI)

(B) Food and Agriculture Organization(FAO)

(C) International Crops Research Institute for the Semi-Arid Tropics

(D) World Food Programme

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2019 Global food policy report | IFPRI : International Food Policy Research Institute.

Concept note-2: -Why in News? Recently, the International Food Policy Research Institute (IFPRI) has released Global Food Policy Report: Climate Change & Food Systems, showing India’s risk for hunger could increase 23% by 2030 due to Climate Change.

Concept note-3: -The Global Hunger Index (GHI) is a tool designed to comprehensively measure and track hunger at the global, regional, and country levels. The International Food Policy Research Institute (IFPRI) calculates GHI scores each year to assess progress, or the lack thereof, in combating hunger.

Concept note-4: -The International Food Policy Research Institute (IFPRI) was established in 1975 in recognition of the need for an independent research institute that would deal with socioeconomic policies for agricultural development.
