Name the person who has been included recently as the member of the Advisory Council to the 15th Finance Commission headed by N.K. Singh.

(A) Arvind Panagariya

(B) Amit Mitra

(C) Abhijit Banerjee

(D) ** Krishnamurthy Subramanian

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nand Kishore Singh was appointed as the commission’s chairman, with its full-time members being Shaktikanta Das and Anoop Singh and its part-time members being Ramesh Chand and Ashok Lahiri. The commission held its first meeting on 2017-12-04.

Concept note-2: -The Fifteenth Finance Commission was constituted in November 2017. The commission’s chairman is N. K. Singh, with its full-time members being Shaktikanta Das and Anoop Singh. In addition, the commission also has two part-time members in Ramesh Chand and Ashok Lahiri.

Concept note-3: -The most recent Finance Commission was constituted in 2017 and is chaired by N. K. Singh, a former member of the Planning Commission.

Concept note-4: -The 15th Finance Commission recommendations will cover a period of five years from 2021-22 to 2025-26 and is headed by NK Singh, a senior member of the BJP. The commission was set up to recommend the devolution of taxes and other fiscal matters for five fiscal years commencing 1 April 2020.

Concept note-5: -The 15th Finance Commission was constituted by the President of India in November 2017, under the chairmanship of NK Singh. Its recommendations will cover a period of five years from the year 2021-22 to 2025-26.
