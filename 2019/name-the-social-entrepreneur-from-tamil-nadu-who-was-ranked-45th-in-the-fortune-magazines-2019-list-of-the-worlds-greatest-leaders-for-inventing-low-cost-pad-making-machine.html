Name the Social entrepreneur from Tamil Nadu, who was ranked 45th in the Fortune magazine’s 2019 list of the world’s greatest leaders for inventing low cost pad making machine.

(A) Naveen Krishna

(B) Sandeep Ahuja

(C) ** Arunachalam Muruganantham

(D) Smita Ram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Arunachalam Muruganantham, inventor of a low-cost sanitary pad-making machine and grassroots mechanism has been featured in Fortune magazine’s 2019 list of world’s 100 greatest leaders. He is a social entrepreneur from Tamil Nadu. He ranked 45th in the list released by the American publication.
