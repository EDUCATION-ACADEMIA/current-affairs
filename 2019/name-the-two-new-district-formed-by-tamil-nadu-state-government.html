Name the two new district formed by Tamil Nadu state government.

(A) Erode and Thiruvarur

(B) ** Tenkasi and Chengalpattu

(C) Sivagangai and Dindigul

(D) Tiruppur and Thiruvarur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Two divisions – Tenkasi and Sankarankovil (new) – and eight taluks – Tenkasi, Shencottai, Kadayanallur, Sivagiri, Veerakeralampudur, Sankarankovil, Thiruvengadam and Alangulam – constitute Tenkasi district. Vellore district was trifurcated and Tirupattur and Ranipet districts were created.

Concept note-2: -George on Thursday. On Thursday, Chief Minister Edappadi K Palaniswami announced that Vellore district would be trifurcated with two new districts being created with Ranipet and Tirupattur as headquarters.

Concept note-3: -The last of the five new districts of Tamil Nadu was inaugurated by state Chief Minister Edappadi K Palaniswamy. Chengalpattu became Tamil Nadu’s 37th district on Friday.
