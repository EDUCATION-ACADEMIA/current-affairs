Name the world famous handcrafted footwear, which gets a Geographic Indication (GI) tag from the Controller General of Patents, Designs and Trade Mark .

(A) ** Kolhapuri

(B) Dharwadi

(C) Solapuri

(D) Satari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kolhapuris , the sturdy leather chappal that rose from its humble rural origins to occupy the high table of fashion globally, now has a Geographical Indication (GI) tag.

Concept note-2: -Till date, Maharashtra has been prized with 33 Geographical Indication (GI) Tags, the latest being Alphonso, Sangli Turmeric, and Kolhapuri Chappal.

Concept note-3: -The Controller General of Patents, Designs and Trade Marks (CGPDTM) has granted Geographical Indication (GI) tag for Kolhapuris, the ethnic leather chappal (footwear) to four districts in each state of Maharashtra and Karnataka.

Concept note-4: -Kolhapuri chappals are Indian decorative hand-crafted and braided leather slippers that are locally tanned using vegetable dyes.

Concept note-5: -Their devotion and hardwork made this handicraft recognised by whole world and each Chappal produced is the replica of their mind and culture. These are durable, smooth to use and sober in colours. Available in three colours, oil, natural and polish. So smart, so light, so trim that you will want to wear them anywhere.
