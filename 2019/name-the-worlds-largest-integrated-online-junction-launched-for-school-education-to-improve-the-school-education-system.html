Name the world’s largest Integrated Online Junction launched for school education to improve the school education system.

(A) Education

(B) ** Shagun

(C) Shala

(D) Gunvatta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Context. Union HRD Ministry has launched one of world’s largest Integrated Online Junction for – School Education ‘Shagun’.

Concept note-2: -It is an initiative to improve school education system by creating a junction for all online portals and websites relating to various activities of the Department of School Education and Literacy in the Government of India and all States and Union Territories.

Concept note-3: -Shala Gunvatta (Shagun) Portal is a twin track approach to monitor progress of implementation of the various components while also capturing and sharing of best practices from States and UTs.

Concept note-4: -NEW DELHI, August 29: With the aim to showcase innovations in the school elementary education and continuous monitoring of the Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA), MHRD has launched the ‘ShaGun’, an online monitoring website.

Concept note-5: -The ShaGun initiative started by the Ministry of Human Resource Development involves the creation of platforms for both Central and State education portals for the Department of School Education and Literacy.
