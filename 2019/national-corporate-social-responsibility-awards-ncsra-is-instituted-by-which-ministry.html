National Corporate Social Responsibility Awards (NCSRA) is instituted by which ministry?

(A) ** Ministry of Corporate Affairs

(B) Ministry of Human Resource and Development

(C) Ministry of Minority Affairs

(D) Ministry of Commerce & Industry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India, instituted the annual National Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Awards to recognize companies that have made positive impact on the society through their innovative & sustainable CSR initiatives.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India has instituted National Corporate Responsibility (CSR) Awards to recognize companies that have made a positive impact on the society through their innovative & sustainable CSR initiatives. This award is the apex recognition of CSR initiatives in India.

Concept note-3: -Ministry of Corporate Affairs had issued ‘Voluntary Guidelines on Corporate Social Responsibility, 2009’ as a first step towards mainstreaming the concept of Business Responsibilities.
