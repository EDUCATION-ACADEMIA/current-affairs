[SOURCES]

National Deworming Day (NDD-2019) was observed on;

(A) February 12

(B) February 11

(C) ** February 10

(D) February 09

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Deworming Day is conducted in all states/UTs on February 10 every year, with a mop-up day on February 15. Some states/UTs also conduct a bi-annual round on 10 August, depending on worm prevalence in their state/UT.

Concept note-2: -National Deworming Day 2022: From symptoms to prevention, know all about worm infection in children.

Concept note-3: -National Deworming Day (NDD) was launched in 2015 and is one of the largest public programmes implemented on a single day reaching crores of children and adolescents via two NDD rounds every year.

Concept note-4: -The National Deworming Day is an initiative of Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India to make every child in the country worm free. This is one of the largest public health programs reaching large number of children during a short period.
