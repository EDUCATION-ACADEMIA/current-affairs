National Doctor’s Day is observed on July 1st of every year. The theme of National Doctor’s Day 2019;

(A) Don’t attack doctors, Doctors are genius

(B) ** Zero tolerance to violence against doctors

(C) Say no to violence against doctors

(D) Stop violence against doctors

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year, the theme of National Doctor’s day 2019 is “Zero tolerance to violence against doctors and clinical establishment". The theme is decided and announced by the Indian Medical Association .

Concept note-2: -Bidhan Chandra Roy. I agree to the terms and conditions and privacy policy. This time the National doctor’s Day 2019 was celebrated with the theme “Zero tolerance to violence against doctors and clinical establishments”.

Concept note-3: -The theme for National doctors day 2022 is ‘Family Doctors on the Front Line’. This theme highlights doctors’ contribution and shows them gratitude as they care 24x7 for the whole of humanity or a community as their family.

Concept note-4: -In India, it marks the birth and death anniversary of the legendary physician and second Chief Minister of West Bengal, Dr. Bidhan Chandra Roy. It was his belief that only a healthy person who is strong in mind and body can ever dream of achieving India’s freedom i.e. Swaraj.

Concept note-5: -The theme for National Doctors’ Day 2022 is “Family Doctors on the Front Line.” Being a doctor brings you many new challenges every day and one such challenge is to keep fighting for the health of your patients. Warm wishes on Doctor’s Day.
