National Epilepsy Day is being observed on which date?

(A) ** November 17

(B) November 16

(C) November 15

(D) November 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Each year, on November 17, National Epilepsy Day is marked in India to raise awareness of the condition. National Epilepsy Day 2022 is observed to raise public awareness of epilepsy. What is epilepsy?

Concept note-2: -The 26th March is Purple Day, a time to get people talking about epilepsy, raise awareness of the condition and raise vital funds.

Concept note-3: -National Epilepsy Day is observed annually on November 17th in an effort to raise awareness of epilepsy and reduce the stigma and fear surrounding it.

Concept note-4: -Theme of National Epilepsy Awareness Day 2022 According to the Epilepsy Foundation, the theme of National Epilepsy Awareness Month (NEAM) 2022 is “There is no NEAM without ME”.
