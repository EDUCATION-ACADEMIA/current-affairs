National Sports Day in India is celebrated on the 29th of August every year. It is celebrated in the memory of ____

(A) Milkha singh

(B) Mansoor Ali Khan Pataudi

(C) ** Major Dhyan chand

(D) Dara Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Sports Day is celebrated on Dhyan Chand’s birthday on August 29 to honour the hockey legend.

Concept note-2: -National Sports Day is celebrated on 29th August on the birth anniversary of Major Dhyan Chand. Know about the life of Dhyan Chand, and awards given on the National Sports Day in this article.

Concept note-3: -This day marks the birthday of Major Dhyan Chand Singh, the hockey player who won gold medals in Olympics for India in the years 1928, 1932 and 1936. He scored 570 goals in his career, from 1926 - 1949 (according to his autobiography, Goals).

Concept note-4: -India celebrates National Sports Day on August 29, the birth anniversary of hockey hero Major Dhyan Chand. In 2012, the day was declared India’s National Sports Day.

Concept note-5: -The Department of Legal Affairs celebrated National Sports Day on 29th August 2022 at Shastri Bhawan, New Delhi, to commemorate the birth anniversary of Hockey Legend Major Dhyan Chand Ji. Sporting events like cricket, Table-Tennis and badminton were organized.
