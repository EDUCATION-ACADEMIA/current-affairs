National Sports Day is celebrated in India every year on ____

(A) 26th August

(B) 27th August

(C) 28th August

(D) ** 29th August

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India celebrates National Sports Day on 29th August to commemorate the birth anniversary of hockey legend, Major Dhyan Chand. The day is also dedicated to the nations’ sports heroes and champions, honoring their contribution and dedication towards bringing laurels to the country.

Concept note-2: -National Sports Day is celebrated on 29 August every year to commemorate the birthday of Indian hockey legend Major Dhyan Chand.

Concept note-3: -India celebrates National Sports Day on August 29, the birth anniversary of hockey hero Major Dhyan Chand. In 2012, the day was declared India’s National Sports Day.

Concept note-4: -The National Sports Day in India is celebrated every year on August 29th to commemorate the birth anniversary of hockey legend Major Dhyan Chand.
