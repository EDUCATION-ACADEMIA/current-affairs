National Unity Day will be celebrated on which date?

(A) 25th October

(B) 27th October

(C) 29th October

(D) ** 31st October

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Unity Day 2022 - Rashtriya Ekta Diwas or National Unity Day is celebrated on October 31st every year to commemorate the birth anniversary of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel , who was India’s first Deputy Prime Minister of India and first Home Minister of India from 1947 to 1950.

Concept note-2: -National Unity Day 2022 - Rashtriya Ekta Diwas or National Unity Day is celebrated on October 31st every year to commemorate the birth anniversary of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, who was India’s first Deputy Prime Minister of India and first Home Minister of India from 1947 to 1950.

Concept note-3: -A pledge-taking ceremony is held in government offices and PSUs on National Unity Day. The speech that is read out is as follows: “I solemnly pledge that I dedicate myself to preserving the unity, integrity, and security of the nation and also strive hard to spread this message among my fellow countrymen.

Concept note-4: -The birth anniversary of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, the first Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister of India is celebrated on 31 October, that is why we celebrate this day as National Unity Day in India.

Concept note-5: -Every year on October 31, the Rashtriya Ekta Diwas, or National Unity Day, is observed to mark the birth anniversary of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.
