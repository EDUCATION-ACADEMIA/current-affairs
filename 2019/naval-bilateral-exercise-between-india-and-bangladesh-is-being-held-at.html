Naval bilateral exercise between India and Bangladesh is being held at?

(A) Andaman sea

(B) ** Visakhapatnam

(C) Babina military station

(D) Bay of Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why in News? Recently, the third edition of Exercise Bongosagar, bilateral naval exercise between India and Bangladesh, commenced at Port Mongla, Bangladesh.

Concept note-2: -Bongo Sagar is the naval exercise between Bangladesh and India.

Concept note-3: -Sampriti Exercise is a bilateral defence cooperation exercise between the armies of India and Bangladesh. It is seen as a part of Indo-Bangladesh defence cooperation.

Concept note-4: -SIMBEX-2022 is being conducted in two phases – Harbour Phase at Visakhapatnam from 26 to 27 October 2022 followed by the Sea Phase in Bay of Bengal from 28 to 30 October 2022.

Concept note-5: -Indian Navy’s multilateral exercise Milan-2022 was organized from February 25, 2022, in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. It was conducted over a duration of nine days in two phases with the harbour phase scheduled from February 25 and the Sea Phase from March 1 to March 4.
