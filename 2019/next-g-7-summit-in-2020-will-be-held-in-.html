Next G-7 summit in 2020 will be held in ____

(A) ** USA

(B) India

(C) China

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Event: G7 Leaders’ Summit 2022 | SDG Knowledge Hub | IISD. The G7 Leaders’ Summit 2022 is expected to take place in June 2022 in the Bavarian Alps. Germany will take over the G7 Presidency on 1 January 2022, and will host the Summit. The seven G7 countries are Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the UK and the US.

Concept note-2: -The 46th G7 summit of the leaders of the Group of Seven was originally scheduled for June 10–12, 2020, at Camp David, United States. However, the summit was canceled due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Concept note-3: -As the G7 Presidency in 2023, Japan has decided to host the G7 Trade Ministers’ Meeting in Osaka-Sakai, on 28 and 29 October 2023 after hosting the first online meeting on 4 April 2023. In October, the meeting and related events will be held in the host cities of Osaka and Sakai.

Concept note-4: -G7 summit, Schloss Elmau, 26-28 June 2022.

Concept note-5: -Germany has been hosting the G7 Summit. It has held the G7 Presidency since January 2022. Federal Chancellor Olaf Scholz has invited the heads of state and government to Schloss Elmau in Upper Bavaria.
