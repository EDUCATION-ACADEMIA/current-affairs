[SOURCES]

NITI Aayog recently launched the fourth edition of Women Transforming India Awards in ____

(A) Lucknow

(B) Kolkata

(C) Mumbai

(D) ** Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -75 Winners Felicitated as Part of Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav. NITI Aayog organized the fifth edition of the Women Transforming India (WTI) Awards on 21 March 2022.

Concept note-2: -NITI Aayog’s Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP) organized the fifth edition of the Women Transforming India Awards (WTI). The WTI Awards 2021 were conferred upon 75 women achievers to celebrate their contribution towards a ‘Sashakt Aur Samarth Bharat’.

Concept note-3: -In line with this call, the NITI Aayog created the ‘Strategy for New India 75’released in 2018. This strategy is all the more essential to transform the nation in the post-pandemic world.

Concept note-4: -Dr. Aparna Hegde was recognised by the Women Entrepreneurship Platform and NITI Aayog for her exemplary work in maternal and child health in the 5th edition of the Women Transforming India Awards, 2021.
