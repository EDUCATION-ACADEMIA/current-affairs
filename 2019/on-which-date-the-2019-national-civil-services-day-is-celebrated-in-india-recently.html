On which date, the 2019 National Civil Services Day is celebrated in India recently?

(A) ** April 21

(B) April 20

(C) April 22

(D) April 23

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government of India celebrates April 21 every year as ‘Civil Services day’ as an occasion for the civil servants to rededicate themselves to the cause of citizen and renew their commitments to public service and excellence in work.

Concept note-2: -The Government of India observes the 21st of April every year as ‘Civil Services Day’. This is an occasion for the civil servants to rededicate themselves to the cause of citizens and renew their commitment to public service and excellence in work. The first such function was held in Vigyan Bhawan on 21 April 2006.

Concept note-3: -Why is 21st April celebrated as Civil Service Day in India? National Civil Service Day is celebrated on 21st April to commemorate the date on which Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel addressed the probationers of Administrative Services Officers and referred to civil servants as the ‘steel frame of India’.

Concept note-4: -The theme of Civil Services Day 2022 is “Vision India2047 – Bringing Citizens and Government closer”.
