On which of the following dates is World Day against the Death Penalty is observed?

(A) ** 10 October

(B) 11 October

(C) 12 October

(D) 13 October

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Death Penalty: A Road Paved with Torture: World Day against the Death Penalty 2022 is dedicated to reflecting on the relationship between the use of the death penalty and torture or other cruel punishment.

Concept note-2: -Following its independence, the Republic of India adopted various colonial-era laws, including the Indian Penal Code, 1860, and the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898. The IPC imposed six punishments, including the death penalty.
