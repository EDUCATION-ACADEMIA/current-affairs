On which of the following dates World Post Day is observed?

(A) October 8

(B) ** October 9

(C) October 11

(D) October 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Post Day is celebrated each year on 9 October, the anniversary of the establishment of the Universal Postal Union in 1874 in the Swiss Capital, Bern. It was declared World Post Day by the UPU Congress held in Tokyo, Japan in 1969. Since then, countries across the world participate annually in the celebrations.

Concept note-2: -The theme for 2022 World Post Day is “Post for Planet.” There are so many elements of daily business that Posts are already making more sustainable to ensure a greener planet for future generations.

Concept note-3: -The theme for World Post Day 2022 is ‘Post for Planet’. The Post is the world’s largest logistics network. Every year, more than 150 countries celebrate World Post Day in a variety of ways. In certain countries, World Post Day is observed as a working holiday.

Concept note-4: -Established in 1874, the Universal Postal Union (UPU), with its headquarters in the Swiss capital Berne, is the second oldest international organization worldwide. With its 192 member countries, the UPU is the primary forum for cooperation between postal sector players.
