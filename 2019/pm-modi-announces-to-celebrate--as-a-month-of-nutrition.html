[SOURCES]
PM Modi announces to celebrate ____ as a month of nutrition.

(A) November

(B) October

(C) ** September

(D) December

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of National Nutrition week 2022 is ‘Celebrate a World of Flavours’. The government introduces a special theme every year that primarily focuses on that year’s theme. Last year, the theme for nutrition week was ‘feeding smart right from the start.

Concept note-2: -Since 2018, India celebrates the month of September as Nutrition Month. It was heartening to note that the Indian Prime Minister spoke recently on the significance of observing September as Nutrition Month. He said, we celebrate the Poshan Maah (Nutrition Month) every year from the 1st to the 30th of September.

Concept note-3: -National Nutrition Week 2022: Essential nutrients for women at every age.

Concept note-4: -With the focus on inculcating good nutrition habits and positive behaviors that help pregnant women, lactating mothers and adolescent girls combat malnutrition, the Ministry of Women and Child Development is celebrating the 5th Rashtriya Poshan Maah 2022 from 1st to 30th September 2022.
