PM Modi will inaugurate India’s second Multi-Modal Terminal (MMT) in which city?

(A) Prayagraj

(B) ** Sahibganj

(C) Gaya

(D) Cuttack

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate India’s second multi-modal terminal built at Sahibganj in Jharkhand on Thursday, a government statement said. This is the second of the three Multi Modal Terminals (MMTs) being constructed on river Ganga under Jal Marg Vikas Project (JMVP).

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated India’s first Inland Multi-Modal Terminal Port on river Ganga at Ramnagar in Varanasi and received first container cargo belonging to PepsiCo.

Concept note-3: -Varanasi Multi-Modal Terminal or Varanasi Port is an Inland river port situated in the city of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. The port is located on the Ganges river.

Concept note-4: -PM Narendra Modi inaugurates first multi-modal terminal constructed on River Ganga - The Economic Times.
