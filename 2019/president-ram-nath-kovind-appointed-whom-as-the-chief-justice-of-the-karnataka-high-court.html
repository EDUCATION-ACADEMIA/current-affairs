President Ram Nath Kovind appointed whom as the Chief Justice of the Karnataka High Court?

(A) Mohammad Rafiq

(B) ** Abhay Shreeniwas Oka

(C) Raghvendra Singh Chauhan

(D) Vijay Kumar Bist

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Justice N V Ramana sworn in as 48th Chief Justice of India by President.

Concept note-2: -Justice D.Y. Chandrachud will take oath as the the 50th Chief Justice of India on November 9, 2022. President Droupadi Murmu appointed Justice D.Y. Chandrachud as the 50th Chief Justice of India (CJI), Union Law Minister Kiren Rijiju said on October 17.

Concept note-3: -Aravind Kumar (born 14 July 1962) is an Indian Judge. Presently, he is Chief Justice of Gujarat High Court. He is former Judge of Karnataka High Court.
