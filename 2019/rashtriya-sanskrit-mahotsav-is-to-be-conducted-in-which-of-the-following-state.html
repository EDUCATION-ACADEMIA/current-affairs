Rashtriya Sanskrit Mahotsav is to be conducted in which of the following state?

(A) ** Madhya Pradesh

(B) West Bengal

(C) Bihar

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, the Mega festival, Rashtriya Sanskriti Mahotsav 2022 was inaugurated in Arts college Ground, RAJAMAHENDRAVARAM, Andhra Pradesh by Governor, Andhra Pradesh Shri Biswabhusan Harichandan in the presence of Union Minister for Culture , Tourism and DoNER Shri G. K Reddy.

Concept note-2: -Q. ‘Rashtriya Sanskriti Mahotsav 2022’ was inaugurated in which Indian state/UT? Notes: Under the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, Rashtriya Sanskriti Mahotsav 2022 was inaugurated in Andhra Pradesh by the Governor Biswabhusan Harichandan.

Concept note-3: -7th Rashtriya Sanskriti Mahotsav 2018 held in Karnataka 7th edition of Rashtriya Sanskriti Mahotsav (RSM) 2018 under the Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat programme was held in Bengaluru, Karnataka from January 14-20, 2018.

Concept note-4: -11th edition of ‘Rashtriya Sanskriti Mahotsav’ on February 14, 2021. Ten editions were organized in Delhi, Bengaluru, Varanasi, Tawang, Karnataka, Gujarat, Tehri, and Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-5: -Notes: In Madhya Pradesh, the 10th edition of the Rashtriya Sanskriti Mahotsav-National Cultural Festival is being organized by the Union Ministry of Culture from 14th to 21st of October under the ‘Ek Bharat, Shreshtha Bharat’ campaign in Jabalpur, Sagar and Rewa.
