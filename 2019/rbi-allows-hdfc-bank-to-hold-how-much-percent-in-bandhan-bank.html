RBI allows HDFC Bank to hold how much percent in Bandhan Bank?

(A) ** 9.9

(B) 8.4

(C) 12

(D) 18.6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -RBI Allows HDFC To Hold Only 9.9% In Bandhan Bank.

Concept note-2: -(2) Save as provided in sub-section (1), no banking company shall hold shares in any company, whether as pledgee, mortgagee or absolute owner, of an amount exceeding thirty per cent.

Concept note-3: -Kolkata, November 1, 2021: The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has authorised Bandhan Bank as an Agency Bank of the RBI for undertaking Government business.

Concept note-4: -HDFC received these shares from Bandhan on account of the merger of GRUH Finance with Bandhan and in line with the share exchange ratio mentioned in the scheme of merger. However, post the block deal, HDFC now holds 4.95% of the paid-up share capital of Bandhan.
