Recently, DRDO Launched Long Range Surface to Air Missile (LR-SAM). In which coast it was launched?

(A) ** Odisha Coast

(B) Karnataka coast

(C) Tamil Nadu coast

(D) Kerala cost

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DRDO and Indian Navy successfully flight tested VL-SRSAM from the ITR,Chandipur off the coast of Odisha on 23 August 2022.

Concept note-2: -The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and the Indian Navy on Tuesday successfully flight tested the indigenously developed Vertical Launch Short Range Surface-to-Air Missile (VL-SRSAM) from the Integrated Test Range (ITR), Chandipur off the coast of Odisha.

Concept note-3: -Barak 8 LRSAM has been jointly developed by DRDO and IAI, Israel to be launched from ship. It possesses high degree of manoeuvrability at target interception range. It has maximum speed of Mach 2 with maximum operational range of 70 km (which has been increased to 100 km).

Concept note-4: -Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Army have successfully completed six flight-tests of Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile (QRSAM) system from Integrated Test Range (ITR) Chandipur off the Odisha coast.
