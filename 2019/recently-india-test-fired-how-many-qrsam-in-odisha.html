Recently, India test fired how many QRSAM in Odisha?

(A) 3

(B) ** 2

(C) 4

(D) 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DRDO & Indian Army successfully conduct six flight-tests of Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile system off Odisha coast.

Concept note-2: -Recently, India conducted six successful flight-tests of the Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile (QRSAM) system from Integrated Test Range (ITR) Chandipur off the Odisha coast. The test was jointly conducted by the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Army.

Concept note-3: -Range & Altitude : 05 to 30 Km & 6 Km.
