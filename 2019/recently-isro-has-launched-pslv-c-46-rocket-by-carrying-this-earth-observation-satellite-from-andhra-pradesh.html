Recently, ISRO has launched PSLV C-46 rocket by carrying this earth observation satellite from Andhra Pradesh.

(A) TecSAR

(B) ANUSAT

(C) RISAT-1

(D) ** RISAT-2B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Recently, PSLV-C54 was successfully launched EOS-06 satellite on 26th November 2022 along with Eight Nano-satellites including INDIA-BHUTAN SAT (INS-2B).

Concept note-2: -All the satellites have been injected into their intended orbits. ISRO’s work horse PSLV-C54 has successfully launched EOS-06 satellite along with Eight Nano-satellites into two different SSPOs. The mission was accomplished from Satish Dhawan Space Centre SHAR on 26th November 2022.

Concept note-3: -Recently, ISRO’s PSLV-C54 successfully placed nine satellites which includes an Earth Observation Satellite (EOS-06) in multiple orbits. This is the 56th flight of the Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) and the 24th flight of the PSLV-XL version.

Concept note-4: -NanoMx is a multispectral optical imaging payload. The Nov. 26 launch was ISRO’s fifth and final mission for 2022.
