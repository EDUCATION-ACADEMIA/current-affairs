Recently, Kummanam Rajasekharan has step down from his position. He was the Governor of;

(A) ** Mizoram

(B) Assam

(C) Manipur

(D) Nagaland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -From 2015 to 2018, he was the state president of Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) in Kerala. He is the first BJP leader from Kerala to become governor. He currently serves as the administrative committee member of the Sree Padmanabha Swamy Temple in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala.

Concept note-2: -Governors At Home on the occasion of Independence Day 2022 at Raj Bhavan, Aizawl. Hon’ble Governor and Lady Governor with Smt Droupadi Murmu, Hon’ble President of India at Raj Bhavan, Mizoram. Hon’ble Governor releases “Exam Warriors” a book authored by Hon’ble Prime Minister.
