Recently, Mohamed Ould Ghazouani elected as the president of ____?

(A) Sudan

(B) Seychelles

(C) Kyrgyzstan

(D) ** Mauritania

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 22 June 2019, he became Mauritania’s elected president after a presidential election against five candidates in a race to the “gray palace".

Concept note-2: -Mohamed Limam has been vice president (VP) and head of country of bp Mauritania since January 2018. He has a longstanding experience in the energy sector in the African continent.

Concept note-3: -Ans. According to Article 58 of the Constitution, no person shall be eligible for election as President unless he is a citizen of India, has completed the age of thirty-five years and is qualified for election as a member of the House of the People.

Concept note-4: -The President of Pakistan is indirectly elected by the Electoral College of Pakistan – a joint sitting of the Senate, National Assembly and Provincial Assemblies.
