Recently, Scientists discover 8th continent under Europe. It’s named as;

(A) ** Greater Adria

(B) Mathilde

(C) Cruithne

(D) Davida

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A lost 8th continent is hidden nearly 1,000 miles under Europe, new research shows. Scientists named it ‘Greater Adria. ‘ Hundreds of millions of years ago, Earth had one giant supercontinent named Pangea.

Concept note-2: -The continent is called Greater Adria. It’s the size of Greenland and it broke off from North Africa, only to be buried under Southern Europe about 140 million years ago. And chances are, you’ve been there without even knowing it.

Concept note-3: -Zealandia (pronounced /ziːˈlændiə/), also known as Te Riu-a-Māui (Māori) or Tasmantis, is an almost entirely submerged mass of continental crust that subsided after breaking away from Gondwanaland 83–79 million years ago.

Concept note-4: -Know More About the 8th Continent Zealandia The continent, called Zealandia, is supposedly about 3500 feet deep inside the southern area of the Pacific Ocean.

Concept note-5: -Only recently recognized by scientists, Zealandia is the most submerged, thinnest, and youngest continent yet found.
