Recently, the 9th Session of the India-Kenya Joint Trade Committee (JTC) was held in ____

(A) Allahabad

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Kolkata

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -President Moi visited India for a bilateral visit in 1981 and for the NAM Summit in 1983. JTC: The India-Kenya Joint Trade Committee (JTC) was set up at Ministerial level in 1983. The ninth session of JTC at CIM level was held in New Delhi in August 2019.

Concept note-2: -India is Kenya’s sixth largest trading partner and the largest exporter to Kenya. Indian exports to Kenya include pharmaceuticals, steel, machinery and automobiles while Kenyan exports to India are largely primary commodities such as soda ash, vegetables and tea.

Concept note-3: -Top Products which India Exports to Kenya are - Unsaturated Acyclic Monocarboxylic Acids, Cyclic Monocarboxylic Acids, Motorcycles, and Other Articles Of Plastics And Articles Of Other Materials etc. Export process from India to Kenya and other countries is almost same worldwide.

Concept note-4: -Kenya is an east African country that lies on the coastline of the Indian Ocean. Despite being separated by the Indian Ocean, both the countries have nurtured a warm and strong relationship through the ages.
