Recently which anniversary of Guru Nanak Dev Ji was celebrated?

(A) 250th

(B) 350th

(C) 400th

(D) ** 550th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Guru Nanak Jayanti or Gurpurab is on November 8, 2022. It will be observed as the 553rd birth anniversary of Guru Nanak Dev Ji.

Concept note-2: -Guru Nanak Jayanti 2022: It will be celebrated on 8th November 2022. It is one of the sacred festivals of Sikhs and is celebrated with utmost love and enthusiasm. Guru Nanak Jayanti is also known as Guru Nanak’s Prakash Utsav and Guru Nanak Gurupurab.

Concept note-3: -The Sikh community considers the day of Guru Nanak Jayanti as very special for their religion because on this day in 1469, on November 8, Guru Nanak Dev was born, when Guru Nanak was born, that day was Kartik Purnima. People also call Guru Nanak ji by the name of Gurupurab.

Concept note-4: -On 22 September 1539, Guru Nanak, the founder of Sikhism died aged 70 at Kartarpur in present-day Pakistan. Guru Nanak Dev is one of the most revered Gurus because of his noble teachings.
