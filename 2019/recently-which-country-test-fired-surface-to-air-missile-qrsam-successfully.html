Recently, Which country test fired surface-to-air missile "QRSAM" successfully?

(A) ** India

(B) South Korea

(C) USA

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India successfully test-fires Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile System (QRSAM)

Concept note-2: -The Strategic Forces Command (SFC) which operates the Agni-5, carried out the successful test of the missile from APJ Abdul Kalam Island off the coast of Odisha. The nuclear capable missile which uses a three-stage solid fuelled engine, has been developed by the Defence Research and Development organisation (DRDO).

Concept note-3: -Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO) conducted a successful maiden flight-test of Phase-II Ballistic Missile Defence (BMD) interceptor AD-1 missile with large kill altitude bracket from APJ Abdul Kalam Island off the coast of Odisha on November 02, 2022.

Concept note-4: -DRDO conducts successful test flight of VSHORADS missile | India News - Times of India.

Concept note-5: -Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Army have successfully completed six flight-tests of Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile (QRSAM) system from Integrated Test Range (ITR) Chandipur off the Odisha coast. The flight tests were conducted as part of evaluation trials by the Indian Army.
