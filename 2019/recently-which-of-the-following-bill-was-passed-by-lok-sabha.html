Recently, Which of the following bill was passed by Lok Sabha?

(A) The Higher Education Act 2018

(B) The Education Act 2019

(C) Indian Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act 2009

(D) ** Reservation in Teachers’ Cadre Bill 2019

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Amendment) Bill, 2021. The Airports Economic Regulatory Authority of India (Amendment) Bill, 2021. The Government of National Capital Territory of Delhi (Amendment) Bill, 2021. The Finance Bill, 2021.

Concept note-2: -2022. Following this, in relation to the state of Karnataka, the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Fourth Amendment) Bill, 2022, was also passed unanimously in Rajya Sabha on 22.12.

Concept note-3: -Money Bills can be introduced only in Lok Sabha. Rajya Sabha cannot make amendments in a Money Bill passed by Lok Sabha and transmitted to it. It can, however, recommend amendments in a Money Bill, but must return all Money Bills to Lok Sabha within fourteen days from the date of their receipt.

Concept note-4: -Nine Bills introduced in Lok Sabha; 7 Bills passed by Lok Sabha and 9 Bills passed by Rajya Sabha: Shri Pralhad Joshi, Union Minister of Parliamentary Affairs, Coal & Mines.
