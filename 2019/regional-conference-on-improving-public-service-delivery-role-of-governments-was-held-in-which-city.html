Regional Conference on ‘Improving Public Service Delivery-Role of Governments’ was held in which city?

(A) New Delhi

(B) ** Nagpur

(C) Dehradun

(D) Indore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It conducts examinations for the appointment of the services in the state. It is consulted on the following decisions related to personnel management: Methods of recruitment and selection to the state civil service and civil posts.

Concept note-2: -Public Service is a service which is provided by government to people: either directly (through the public sector). or by financing provision of services. Some key Public Services are: Health care, Education, Social services for the poor and marginalized.

Concept note-3: -Delivery of public services is organized through assigning responsibilities of performing certain tasks to the bureaucracy and their ability to perform the tasks and motivation decides how efficiently the services will be delivered.

Concept note-4: -Central Services: People under this category are directly under the rules and regulations of the central government. State Services: People under this category are governed by the state government.
