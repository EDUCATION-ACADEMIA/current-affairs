Reliance Jio and ____ has partnered to offer cloud infra for businesses.

(A) ** Microsoft

(B) Infosys

(C) Wipro

(D) Google

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliance Jio has partnered with some of the world’s leading technology giants such as Meta, Google, Microsoft and Intel to roll out 5G services by Diwali in key metro cities of India.

Concept note-2: -Ambani also said that Reliance Jio will “leverage the advanced capabilities of Google Cloud to offer Jio’s Private 5G stack and other 5G-enabled solutions to both domestic and global users at scale.” Reliance Jio is working with Intel on “technologies used in Jio’s cloud-scale data centres and 5G Edge locations”.

Concept note-3: -Xiaomi India, a Chinese technology giant has announced its collaboration with Reliance Jio to offer 5G experience to their customers.
