Researchers from which of the following institute developed an app to detect fake currency?

(A) IIT Bombay

(B) IIT Madras

(C) ** IIT Kharagpur

(D) IIT Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Researchers at IIT Kharagpur have built a smartphone-based application to detect fake currency and also provided solutions for industrial operations ranging from thermal power plants to nuclear radiation tracking, an official told IANS.

Concept note-2: -With the free Chkfake app, you can now check for common signs that help identify a fake currency note.

Concept note-3: -UV rays discovered by Johann Ritter. It is used for detecting fake and forged currency.

Concept note-4: -Ascending font size One of the easiest ways to spot a counterfeit is to look for any anomalies in the number panel at the top left and the bottom right side of the note. An authentic currency note will display these numbers in an ascending pattern from left to right.
