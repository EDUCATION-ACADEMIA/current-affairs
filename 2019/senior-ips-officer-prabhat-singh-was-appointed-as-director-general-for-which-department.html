Senior IPS Officer Prabhat Singh was appointed as Director General for which Department?

(A) ** NHRC

(B) NCDRC

(C) NCPCR

(D) NITI Aayog

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Appointment of Shri Prabhat Singh, IPS (AGMUT:85), Special Director, CRPF as Director General (Investigation), NHRC. Ministry of Home Affairs.

Concept note-2: -Kuldiep Singh (IPS) is new Director CRPF & MA Ganapathy (IPS) is new DG NSG. New Delhi: The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the proposal of the Ministry of Home Affairs for the appointment of Kuldiep Singh (IPS) as Director-General of the Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF).
