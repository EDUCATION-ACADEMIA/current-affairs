SITMEX is the trilateral exercise of Indian Navy, Singapore Navy and ____

(A) Vietnam Navy

(B) ** Thailand Navy

(C) Taiwan Navy

(D) Philippines Navy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The trilateral exercise will focus on tactical naval manoeuvres and surface warfare drills.

Concept note-2: -INDIAN NAVY PARTICIPATES IN INDIA, SINGAPORE AND THAILAND TRILATERAL MARITIME EXERCISE ‘SITMEX’

Concept note-3: -It includes a number of tactical training drills such as naval manoeuvres and surface warfare exercises. It aims to strengthen mutual confidence and develop common understanding and procedures towards enhancing the overall maritime security in the Indo-Pacific region.

Concept note-4: -The Singapore-India-Thailand Maritime Exercise (SITMEX) is the maiden trilateral exercise involving the Republic of Singapore Navy (RSN), Indian Navy and the Royal Thai Navy.

Concept note-5: -SIMBEX-2022 is being conducted in two phases – Harbour Phase at Visakhapatnam from 26 to 27 October 2022 followed by the Sea Phase in Bay of Bengal from 28 to 30 October 2022.
