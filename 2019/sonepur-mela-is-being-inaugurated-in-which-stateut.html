Sonepur Mela is being inaugurated in which state/UT?

(A) ** Bihar

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) New Delhi

(D) Ladakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sonepur Cattle Fair (Hindi: ) is held on Kartik Poornima (the full moon day) over the months of November and December in Sonepur, Bihar, on the confluence of river Ganges(Gandak). It is also known as Harihar Kshetra Mela.

Concept note-2: -The famous Sonepur Cattle Fair 2022, which is a very popular fair of Bihar, is called Sonepur in the Harihar region, a short distance from Bihar’s capital Patna, which is Asia’s largest fair.

Concept note-3: -Sonepur Mela is held every year in Bihar in Sonepur which is located at the confluence of River Ganga and Gandak. It is celebrated in the months of November and December for 15 to 30 days. When is Sonepur Mela 2022 going to be held? Sonepur Mela 2022 is going to be held from 20th November to 5th of December in Bihar.

Concept note-4: -Sonepur Mela is one of Asia’s largest cattle fairs, organized at the confluence of two mighty rivers, the Ganga and Gandak. Popular since ancient eras for the trading of livestock, this month-long event takes place on the auspicious occasion of Kartik Purnima in the month of November.

Concept note-5: -Sonepur Mela 2022 - When and Where is it Celebrated? The Sonepur Cattle Fair or Sonepur Mela held in Northern Bihar during the month of Kartik or November is one of the largest in Asia. It is also regarded as a very auspicious time for Hindus as it is held on Kartik Poornima or the full moon night.
