Special Olympics World Games 2019 is held at ______

(A) ** Abu Dhabi

(B) London

(C) Beijing

(D) Tokyo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For the first time, Special Olympics World Games will be held in the Middle East! Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates is making plans to host the Special Olympics World Summer Games March 14-21 2019.

Concept note-2: -Special Olympics, international program to provide individuals with intellectual disabilities who are eight years of age or older with year-round sports training and athletic competition in more than 20 Olympic-type summer and winter sports.

Concept note-3: -The new Olympic motto now reads in Latin “Citius, Altius, Fortius - Communiter” and “Faster, Higher, Stronger - Together” in English.

Concept note-4: -Then, one day in July 1968, the world began to change. The first Special Olympics competition was held in Chicago’s Soldier Field for young people with intellectual disabilities.
