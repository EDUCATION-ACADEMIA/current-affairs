Suman Rao won Femina Miss India World 2019 Title. She hails from which Indian state?

(A) Odisha

(B) Andra Pradesh

(C) ** Rajasthan

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hailing from a small village in Aaidana, Rajasthan, Suman Rao ensured that every step of her journey would be empowering. Today, as a role model to thousands of women in her hometown, she helped them with employment opportunities and so continues to reign in their hearts.

Concept note-2: -Suman Ratan Singh Rao (born 23 November 1998) is an Indian model and beauty pageant title holder who was crowned Femina Miss India 2019. She represented India at the Miss World 2019 pageant in ExCeL London in United Kingdom, and was crowned as the 2nd runner-up and as Miss World Asia.

Concept note-3: -Shivani Jadhav is an Indian model and beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Femina Miss Grand India 2019 at the grand finale of Femina Miss India 2019 by the outgoing titleholder Meenakshi Chaudhary.

Concept note-4: -It was held at Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Indoor Stadium, Mumbai on June 15, 2019, and was hosted by Karan Johar, Manish Paul, and Miss World 2017, Manushi Chhillar. At the end of the event, Anukreethy Vas of Tamil Nadu crowned Suman Rao of Mumbai as her successor.

Concept note-5: -The title of Femina miss Haryana 2019 was won by Sonal Sharma.
