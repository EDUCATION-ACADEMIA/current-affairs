Sydney and Brisbane chosen to host $15 million 24 team ATP Cup in which year?

(A) 2048

(B) 2030

(C) 2024

(D) ** 2020

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World No. 1 Carlos Alcaraz and coach Juan Carlos Ferrero combine for three honours in the 2022 ATP Awards, following the 19-year-old’s historic season on the ATP Tour. Andy Murray, Rafael Nadal, Holger Rune and Casper Ruud have also been named among the winners.

Concept note-2: -The tournament was part of the 2022 ATP Tour. Due to the uncertainties with the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, it was held at the Ken Rosewall Arena and the Sydney Super Dome in Sydney, from 1 to 9 January 2022 with 16 teams. Canada won the tournament, defeating Spain 2–0 in the final.

Concept note-3: -For The Love Of Country Team Serbia won the inaugural edition in 2020, Team Russia lifted the trophy in 2021 and Team Canada triumphed in 2022. In 2022 ATP Cup, 16 countries competed in Sydney.
