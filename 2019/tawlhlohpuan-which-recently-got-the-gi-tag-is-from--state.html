Tawlhlohpuan, which recently got the GI Tag, is from ____ state.

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Assam

(C) Manipur

(D) ** Mizoram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tawlhlohpuan, a woven fabric from Mizoram, was also accorded the Geographical Indication status. Mizo Puanchei is a colourful Mizo shawl considered essential by most women, and was also accorded the Geographical indication status for the State of Mizoram.

Concept note-2: -06/7Kaji Nemu. Kaji Nemu of Assam got the GI tag in 2022. It is full of nutrients, which include increasing vitamin C levels, decreasing blood cholesterol, boosting liver function, and boosting metabolism.

Concept note-3: -The famous Tandur red gram of Telangana has got geographical indication (GI) tag. The GI application had been filed by the Yalal Farmers Producers Company Limited on September 24. The GI registration process was facilitated by Prof. Jayashankar Telangana State Agricultural University.

Concept note-4: -Pochampally Ikat was the first product from the state to receive the GI tag in 2005.
