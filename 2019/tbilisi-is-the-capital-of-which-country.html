Tbilisi is the capital of which country?

(A) Ethiopia

(B) ** Georgia

(C) Lebanon

(D) Portugal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The capital of Georgia is Tbilisi (Tiflis).

Concept note-2: -Location. Tbilisi is located in the South Caucasus at 41° 43’ North and 44° 47’ East. The city lies in Eastern Georgia on both banks of the Kura River (locally known as Mtkvari).

Concept note-3: -Tbilisi, formerly Tiflis, capital of the republic of Georgia, on the Mtkvari (Kura) River at its dissection of the Trialeti (Trialetsky) and Kartli (Kartliysky, or Kartalinian) ranges.
