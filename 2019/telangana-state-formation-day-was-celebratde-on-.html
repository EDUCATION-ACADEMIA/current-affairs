Telangana State Formation Day was celebratde on ____

(A) 1st June

(B) ** 2nd June

(C) 3rd June

(D) 4th June

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The state of Telangana was officially formed on 2 June 2014. The day marks significance in the state’s history for the sustained Telangana movement through the years.

Concept note-2: -Telangana Day or Telangana Formation Day is celebrated on 2 June every year. This day commemorates the formation of the state of Telangana. On this day, the people of Telangana get a state public holiday. The state of Telangana was officially established on 2 June 2014 from the state of Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -Telangana, as a geographical and political entity was born on June 2, 2014 as the 29th and the youngest state in Union of India. However, as an economic, social, cultural and historical entity it has a glorious history of at least two thousand five hundred years or more.

Concept note-4: -Telangana Integration Day: Holiday declared for all educational & govt institutions. September 17 this year will mark the completion of 74 years since Hyderabad state’s annexation to India, for which the state government has planned year-long celebrations.

Concept note-5: -Every year on November 1, Karnataka Formation Day is commemorated as ‘Rajyotsava. ‘ This festival celebrates the formation of the Karnataka state on November 1, 1956, as a result of the union of Kannada-speaking territories in South India.
