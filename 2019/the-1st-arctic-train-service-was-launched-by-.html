The 1st Arctic Train Service was launched by ____

(A) ** Russia

(B) Japan

(C) China

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Russia has introduced the first tourist train service that carries passengers through its Arctic region, heading for Norway in Northern Europe. “The Arctic fascinates everybody,” said Nurlan Mukash, executive director of the German Lernidee rlebnisreisen tour group behind the project.

Concept note-2: -Which country has recently launched Arctic train service? Notes: In Russia, the first tourist train service traveling through Russia’s Arctic region and on to Norway set off from St. Petersburg station with 91 passengers aboard for the inaugural trial journey on June 5.

Concept note-3: -Russia has launched first Arctic train service running from St. Petersburg station headed to Norway. The inaugural trial journey of first tourist train traveling through Russia’s Arctic region and on to Norway will be a 11-day trip with 91 passengers aboard.
