The 2nd edition of India-Russia Strategic Economic Dialogue 2019 held in ____

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Mumbai

(C) Kolkata

(D) Pune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The second IRSED was held in New Delhi on 10 July 2019. The Indian side was led by NITI Aayog Vice Chairman Dr Rajiv Kumar and the Russian side by MED Minister Maxim Oreshkin.

Concept note-2: -A Joint Statement titled “India-Russia Partnership for Peace, Progress and Prosperity” was also adopted. The two leaders had most recently met on the sidelines of the SCO Summit on September 16, 2022 in Samarkand, Uzbekistan. 3. The two leaders maintain regular contact through telephone conversations.
