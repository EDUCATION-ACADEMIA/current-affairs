The 6th Indian Ocean Dialogue was organized in whichcity?

(A) Nagpur

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Visakhapatnam

(D) Ludhiana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Ocean Dialogue (IOD) is a flagship initiative of the Indian Ocean Rim Association (IORA), with its origins in the 13th Council of Ministers meeting, held in November 2013 in Perth, Australia.

Concept note-2: -The Coordinating Secretariat of IORA is located at Ebene, Mauritius.

Concept note-3: -The dialogue was jointly hosted by External Affairs Ministry and Indian Council of World Affairs. Dialogue was hosted under the theme- “Leveraging Digital Technologies for Health, Education, Development, and Trade in Indian Ocean Rim Association (IORA) Member States”.

Concept note-4: -The IORA Secretariat is based in Mauritius.
