The Army Commander Conference was held in which of the following cities?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Lucknow

(C) Agra

(D) Gurugram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The Ministry of Defence announced that the Army Commanders’ Conference has been inaugurated in New Delhi. It aims to brainstorm on current and emerging security and administrative aspects and chart the future course for the Indian Army.

Concept note-3: -Kodandera Madappa Cariappa, byname Kipper, (born January 28, 1899, Shanivarsanthe, Coorg district, Mysore [now Kodagu district, Karnataka state], India-died May 15, 1993, Bangalore), Indian military officer and the first chief of staff of the Indian army after India became independent of Great Britain.

Concept note-4: -Answer: General Manoj Mukund Naravane, who assumed the position on December 31st, 2019, is the present chief of the military.
