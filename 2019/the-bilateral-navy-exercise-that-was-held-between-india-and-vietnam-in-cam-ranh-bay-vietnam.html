The Bilateral Navy exercise that was held between India and Vietnam in Cam Ranh Bay, Vietnam.

(A) ** IN-VPN BILAT EX

(B) IND-VTNM BILAT NAVY EX

(C) IND-VTNM EX

(D) IND-VTNM BILAT EX

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The 2nd edition of ‘Indian Navy-Vietnam Peoples’ Navy Bilateral Exercise’ (IN – VPN BILAT EX) was held off Cam Ranh Bay , Vietnam from 13-16 April 2019. The exercise was undertaken as a part of the ongoing Overseas Deployment of Eastern Fleet ships to South East Asian countries.

Concept note-2: -The next edition of VINBAX will be hosted in Vietnam in 2023.

Concept note-3: -The 3rd edition of Vietnam-India Bilateral Army Exercise “Ex VINBAX 2022” is conducted at Chandimandir, Haryana from August 1 to 20, 2022. The theme of Ex VINBAX 2022 is the “Employment and deployment of an Engineer Company and a Medical Team as part of United Nations Contingent for Peace Keeping Operations”.

Concept note-4: -The 3rd Edition of Vietnam India Bilateral Army Exercise “Ex VINBAX 2022” is scheduled to be conducted at Chandimandir from 01 to 20 Aug 2022.

Concept note-5: -SIMBEX-2022 is being conducted in two phases – Harbour Phase at Visakhapatnam from 26 to 27 October 2022 followed by the Sea Phase in Bay of Bengal from 28 to 30 October 2022.
