The BJP won how many seats in Maharashtra assembly elections?

(A) ** 105

(B) 120

(C) 115

(D) 95

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Presently, 288 members of the Legislative Assembly are directly elected from the single-seat constituencies. The members of the upper house, the Maharashtra Vidhan Parishad (the legislative council) are indirectly elected through an electoral college.

Concept note-2: -Since its inception in 1960, and also of predecessor states such as Bombay, the politics of Maharashtra has been dominated by the Indian National Congress party for a long period of time.

Concept note-3: -19 Rajya Sabha members and 48 Lok Sabha members are elected.
