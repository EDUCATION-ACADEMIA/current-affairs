The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs, chaired by Prime Minister Narendra Modi approved a scheme for Farmer’s welfare. It was named as;

(A) PMKSY

(B) ** KUSUM

(C) NMSA

(D) NCU

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ‘Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi’ (PM-KISAN) scheme was introduced by the government of India in its interim Budget 2019.

Concept note-2: -Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN), a flagship scheme of Government of India, is an outcome of the continued commitment of Prime Minister of India, Sri Narendra Modi to initiate policy actions and implement public programs for an inclusive and productive agricultural sector.

Concept note-3: -16,000 crores under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) through Direct Benefit Transfer. Under the scheme, eligible farmer families are provided a benefit of Rs. 6000 per year in three equal instalments of Rs. 2000 each.
