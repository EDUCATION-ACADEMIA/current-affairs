The Capital city of Mali is

(A) Nouakchott

(B) ** Bamako

(C) Rabat

(D) Port Louis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mali is divided into eight regions (régions) and one capital district. The regions are named after their region capitals: Gao, Kayes, Kidal, Koulikoro, Mopti, Ségou, Sikasso, Tomboctou (Timbuktu), and Bamako the capital district.

Concept note-2: -Notable Cities: The capital of Mali is Bamako, which is in the district of Bamako. The largest city in Mali is Bamako, with an urban population of 2,009,109.

Concept note-3: -Mali (Mālī): The capital province, for which the empire gets its name. Al-’Umari reports that the capital itself, located in the province of Mali, was called byty.

Concept note-4: -Mali is a landlocked country in West Africa, located southwest of Algeria.
