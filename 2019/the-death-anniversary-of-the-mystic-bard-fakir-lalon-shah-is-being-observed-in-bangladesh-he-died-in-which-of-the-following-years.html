The death anniversary of the mystic bard Fakir Lalon Shah is being observed in Bangladesh. He died in which of the following years?

(A) ** 1890

(B) 1891

(C) 1892

(D) 1893

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He is believed to have died on the first day of the Bangla month of Kartik in 1890.

Concept note-2: -Lalon (Bengali: ; 14 October 1772 – 17 October 1890), also known as Lalon Shah, Lalon Fakir, Shahji and titled Fakir, Shah, was a prominent Bengali spiritual leader, philosopher, mystic poet and social reformer.

Concept note-3: -Lalon’s philosophy of humanity rejects all distinctions of caste, class, and creed and takes stand against religious conflicts and racism. It denies all worldly affairs in search of the soul and embodied the socially transformative role of sub-continental Bhakti and Sufism.
