The DRDO test fired the Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile (QRSAM) successfully In which state?

(A) ** Odisha

(B) Karnataka

(C) Kerala

(D) Tamilnadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Army have successfully completed six flight-tests of Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile (QRSAM) system from Integrated Test Range (ITR) Chandipur off the Odisha coast.

Concept note-2: -DRDO conducts successful test flight of VSHORADS missile | India News - Times of India.

Concept note-3: -It is a short-range surface-to-air missile (SAM) system, primarily designed and developed by DRDO to provide a protective shield to moving armoured columns of the Army from enemy aerial attacks.

Concept note-4: -QUICK REACTION SURFACE TO AIR MISSILE (QRSAM) is a quick reaction Surface to Air Missile capable of Search on Move, Track on Move and Fire on Short halts.

Concept note-5: -The Strategic Forces Command (SFC) which operates the Agni-5, carried out the successful test of the missile from APJ Abdul Kalam Island off the coast of Odisha. The nuclear capable missile which uses a three-stage solid fuelled engine, has been developed by the Defence Research and Development organisation (DRDO).
