The first Asian team which is to win a Test series in South Africa.

(A) Singapore

(B) Bangladesh

(C) Nepal

(D) ** Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sri Lanka becomes the first Asian team to win a Test series in South Africa .

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is India. India has become the first Asian team to win a Test match at Centurion after beating South Africa by 113-run.

Concept note-3: -Sri Lanka cricket team became the first Asian side to win a Test series in South Africa after winning the series by 2-0. This was Sri Lanka’s sixth series in South Africa and their first win. Kusal Perera was given the Man of the Series award.

Concept note-4: -This was Pakistan’s first ever ODI series win over South Africa and became the first Asia team to beat the Proteas in an away series.

Concept note-5: -India 1st Asian team to win a Test series in Australia. After clinching the four-match Test series against Australia 2-1, India became the first Asian team to win a Test series on Australian soil. This was India’s 12th Test series in Australia, having earlier lost eight and drawn three.
