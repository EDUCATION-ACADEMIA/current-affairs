The first ever indigenously developed robot restaurant of the eastern India region has been launched in which state?

(A) Jharkhand

(B) ** Odisha

(C) Bihar

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Odisha’s first robot restaurant opens in Bhubaneswar.

Concept note-2: -With the launch of The Yellow House - Robot Restaurant - in Sector 104, Noida, Delhi-NCR has got its first restaurant with robotic waiters. It is a multi-cuisine restaurant chain which had earlier successfully established three restaurants in Jaipur, before expanding to Noida.

Concept note-3: -Delhi-NCR Gets Its First Restaurant With Robots Instead Of Waiters In Noida. Imagine sitting in a restaurant and waiting for waiters to help you out with your hunger needs and instead of human waiters, robots walk in as waiters. How amusing would that be! Well, you can experience that now.

Concept note-4: -Owned by Shayne Hayashi, the Two Panda Deli in Pasadena, California, USA, became the first restaurant to “employ” robot waiting staff when it opened in 1983.
