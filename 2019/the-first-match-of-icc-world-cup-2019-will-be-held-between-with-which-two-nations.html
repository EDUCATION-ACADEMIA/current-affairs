The first match of ICC World Cup 2019 will be held between with which two nations?

(A) ** England vs South Africa

(B) South Africa vs Australia

(C) Pakistan vs England

(D) Bangladesh vs Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hosts England will kick-start the World Cup as they lock horns with South Africa in the first match of the mega-event. India will play their first match against Proteas on June 5 at The Rose Bowl in Southampton.

Concept note-2: -The 2019 Cricket World Cup started on 30 May and was hosted by England and Wales.

Concept note-3: -The first match of ICC Cricket World Cup 2019 was played between hosts England and South Africa on May 30.

Concept note-4: -The opening ceremony took place on The Mall in central London during the evening of 29 May 2019, a day before the start of the World Cup.

Concept note-5: -ICC World Cup 2023 host will be the 13th edition of the men’s Cricket World Cup inthe country India and This is the first time that the entire tournament of ICC World Cup 2023 host will be held in India. India already has hosted the World Cup thrice in the past along with other countries in 1987,1996 and 2011.
