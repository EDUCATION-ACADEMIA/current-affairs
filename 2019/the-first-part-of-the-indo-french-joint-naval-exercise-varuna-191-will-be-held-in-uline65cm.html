The first part of the Indo-French joint naval exercise, Varuna 19.1 will be held in ______

(A) Vizag

(B) ** Goa

(C) Chennai

(D) Kochi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Varuna 2023: India-France conclude joint naval exercise in Goa.

Concept note-2: -21st EDITION OF INDIA FRANCE BILATERAL NAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the Western Seaboard today, 16 Jan 23.

Concept note-3: -According to the Ministry of Defence, the 21st edition of “Varuna” the bilateral naval exercise between India and France commenced on the western seaboard.

Concept note-4: -The bilateral exercise between the navies of India and France was started in the year 1993, it was named ‘Varuna’ in the year 2001. Garuda – Exercise Garuda VII between the Indian Air Force (IAF) and the French Air and Space Force (FASF) was held in November 2022 at Air Force Station Jodhpur.
