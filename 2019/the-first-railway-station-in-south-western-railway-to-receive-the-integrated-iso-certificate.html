The first railway station in South Western Railway to receive the Integrated ISO certificate.

(A) Srirangapatna

(B) ** Mysuru

(C) Channapatna

(D) Whitefield

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On June 24, Quest Certification Ltd., a Chennai-based international accredited agency, presented IMS Certificate to Divisional Railway Manager (DRM), Mysuru. With this Mysuru Railway Station has become the first Station over South Western Railway (SWR) and second in India to receive IMS Certification.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The Guwahati Railway Station has become the first ever railway station in the Indian Railways to get an ISO certification from the National Green Tribunal (NGT) for providing passenger amenities in a clean and green environment.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is the Habibganj railway station. Habibganj railway station is a railway station in Bhopal City and part of the West Central Railway.

Concept note-4: -ISO 50001 is a company level certification based on a standard published by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO). The specification requires use of an energy management system with a main purpose of using energy more efficiently.

Concept note-5: -Kathgodam railway station - Wikipedia.
