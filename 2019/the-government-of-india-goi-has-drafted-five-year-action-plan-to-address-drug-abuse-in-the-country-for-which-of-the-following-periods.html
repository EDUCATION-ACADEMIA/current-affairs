The Government of India (GoI) has drafted five-year action plan to address drug abuse in the country for which of the following periods?

(A) 2019 to 2024

(B) 2017 to 2022

(C) ** 2018 to 2023

(D) 2016 to 2021

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under the NDPS Act, it is illegal for a person to produce/manufacture/cultivate, possess, sell, purchase, transport, store, and/or consume any narcotic drug or psychotropic substance. Under one of the provisions of the act, the Narcotics Control Bureau was set up with effect from March 1986.

Concept note-2: -Preventive education. Awareness generation. Identification, counselling, treatment and rehabilitation of drug dependent persons. Training and capacity building of service providers through collaborative efforts of government and NGOs.
