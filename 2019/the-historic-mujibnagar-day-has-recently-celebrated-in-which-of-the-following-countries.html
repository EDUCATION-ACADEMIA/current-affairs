The historic Mujibnagar day has recently celebrated in which of the following countries?

(A) Myanmar

(B) ** Bangladesh

(C) Nepal

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ‘Mujibnagar Government ’ was instituted on April 17, 1971, when members of Bangladesh’s founded assembly took the constitutionally mandated, logical, and possible step toward realizing their aspiration of becoming an independent country.

Concept note-2: -Mujibnagar Day is celebrated in Bangladesh on April 17 every year. This day is particularly associated with the emergence of Bangladesh as an independent territory.

Concept note-3: -The Provisional Government of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh, popularly known as the Mujibnagar Government, was established following the declaration of independence of East Pakistan as Bangladesh on 10 April 1971.
