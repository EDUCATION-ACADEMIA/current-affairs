The International Olympic Committee has opened its new headquarters in ____

(A) Russia

(B) United States

(C) Germany

(D) ** Switzerland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why does the International Olympic Committee have its headquarters in Lausanne, Switzerland? The IOC headquarters have been in Lausanne, on the shores of Lake Geneva, since 1915.

Concept note-2: -Current IOC headquarters in Lausanne, Switzerland.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Switzerland. The International Olympic Committee inaugurated the new headquarters in Switzerland to mark its 125th anniversary in June 2019.
