The Internet of Things (IoT) India Congress 2019 is scheduled to be held in which of the following cities?

(A) Dehradun

(B) Indore

(C) ** Bengaluru

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The fourth edition of India’s largest conclave on IoT, the IoT India Congress, 2019, was set in motion today at the Hotel LaLiT Ashok in Bengaluru. The two-day event is India’s largest Platform of platforms for the Internet of Things.

Concept note-2: -IoT is used in various applications like smart home, smart parking, smart water level monitoring, smart healthcare, smart traffic lighting, smart waste management, smart solar panels, etc.

Concept note-3: -What is IoT? The Internet of Things (IoT) describes the network of physical objects-“things”-that are embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies for the purpose of connecting and exchanging data with other devices and systems over the internet.

Concept note-4: -There will be 22 billion connected devices in the world by 2025. IoT technology can be used in sectors like Manufacturing, Agriculture, Healthcare, Transportation, Media/Advertising, Retail, Water and Waste Management, Power Distribution, etc.
