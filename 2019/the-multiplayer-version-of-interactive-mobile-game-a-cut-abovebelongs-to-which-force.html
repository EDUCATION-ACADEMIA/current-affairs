The multiplayer version of interactive mobile game ‘A Cut Above’belongs to which force?

(A) Indian Navy

(B) ** Indian Air Force

(C) Indian Army

(D) CISF

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Cut Above is a follow-up to the 2014 game called Guardians of the Skies which was also produced by the IAF. Guardians of the Skies had won various awards such as “Best mobile application (game) in 2014 from Microsoft India” and was also awarded by Unity3D.

Concept note-2: -DISHA, located in Delhi, is an acronym for the Directorate of Induction and Selection in Holistic Approach. DISHA Cell was set up by the Indian Air Force at Air Headquarters in 2005, under the aegis of the Air Officer-in-Charge Personnel, to propagate officers’ induction publicity.

Concept note-3: -The Motto of the Indian Air Force (Touch the sky with Glory) has been taken from the eleventh chapter of the Gita, the Discourse given by Lord Krishna to Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra during the Great War of Mahabharata.

Concept note-4: -Indian AIR Force salary in India ranges between ₹ 2.6 Lakhs to ₹ 12.0 Lakhs with an average annual salary of ₹ 8.0 Lakhs. Salary estimates are based on 85 latest salaries received from Indian AIR Forces.
