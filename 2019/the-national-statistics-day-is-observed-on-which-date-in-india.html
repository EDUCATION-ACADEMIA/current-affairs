The National Statistics Day is observed on which date in India?

(A) 26th June

(B) 27th June

(C) 28th June

(D) ** 29th June

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Statistics Day of India is observed on the 29th of June every year by arranging seminars, programmes, and campaigns across the country. The first National Statistics Day was commemorated in 2007. Following that event, a proclamation was released in the Indian Gazette on June 5, 2007.

Concept note-2: -The theme of this year is ‘Data for Sustainable Development’.

Concept note-3: -National days on Thu Jun 29th, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s National Handshake Day, National Camera Day, World Industrial Design Day, National Bomb Pop Day, International Mud Day… and much more!

Concept note-4: -Background. At its 41st Session in February 2010, the United Nations Statistical Commission proposed celebrating 20 October 2010 as World Statistics Day (Decision 41/109).
