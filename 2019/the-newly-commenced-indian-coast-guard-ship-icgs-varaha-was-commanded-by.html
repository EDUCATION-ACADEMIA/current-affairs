The newly commenced Indian Coast Guard Ship (ICGS) Varaha was commanded by;

(A) Anil Kumar Chawla

(B) Atul Kumar Jain

(C) Ajit Kumar

(D) ** Dushyant Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The previous series was also built by GSL - ICGS Samarth, ICGS Shoor, ICGS Sarathi, ICGS Shaunak, ICGS Shaurya and ICGS Sujay. The latest addition includes ICGS Sachet, ICGS Sujeet, ICGS Sajag, ICGS Sarthak and ICGS Saksham.

Concept note-2: -To improve the coastal security system, the Indian Coast Guard (ICG) added Patrol Vessel Samarth to its fleet.

Concept note-3: -India has gifted an offshore patrol vessel named Suraksha to Sri Lanka. Suraksha is the first offshore patrol vessel of Sri Lankan coast guard.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is the Indian Coast Guard ship Vajra.
