The Prime Minister, Narendra Modi unveiled development projects worth Rs. 3350 crores for which of the following city?

(A) Udaipur

(B) Kanpur

(C) ** Varanasi

(D) Rishikesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This was followed by the full terms of Atal Bihari Vajpayee, Manmohan Singh, and Narendra Modi. Modi is the 14th and current prime minister of India, serving since 26 May 2014.

Concept note-2: -The economic policies of Modi’s government focused on privatization and liberalization of the economy, based on a neoliberal framework. Modi liberalized India’s foreign direct investment policies, allowing more foreign investment in several industries, including in defense and the railways.

Concept note-3: -Narendra Modi, parliamentary leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party, started his tenure after his swearing-in as the 16th Prime Minister of India on 30 May 2019. Several other ministers were also sworn in along with Modi.
