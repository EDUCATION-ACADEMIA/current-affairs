The traditional dance of which state sets a New Guinness World Record, recently?

(A) ** Nagaland

(B) Odisha

(C) Kerala

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Mexican state set a new Guinness World Record recently by assembling more than 900 dancers to perform a folk dance in Michoacan.

Concept note-2: -Boosie is a Moton Park dancer. As of June 2022 she holds the Guinness World Record for “Longest heel-toe and park girl marathon by an individual", for which she danced for 126 hours continuously. Besides dancing, she appeared in a Clipse music video “Grindin".

Concept note-3: -Ans : The Konyak dance is the largest traditional dance in the world and the title was achieved on April 5, 2019, when the dance, with 4,687 participants, was performed by the Konyak Union at Mon, Nagaland.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Ladakh. Ladakhi Shondol the royal dance of Ladakh creates history by entering into the Guinness book of world records as the largest Ladakhi dance. Shondol dance, which is best known as the royal dance used to be performed by artists for King of Ladakh.

Concept note-5: -The record for performing Bharatanatyam dance non-stop for maximum duration was set by J P Sansithaa (born on June 12, 2012), of Salem, Tamil Nadu. She performed Bharatanatyam dance non-stop for 39 minutes, 38 seconds and 2 milliseconds, at the age of 9 years, 8 months and 13 days, as confirmed on February 15, 2022.
