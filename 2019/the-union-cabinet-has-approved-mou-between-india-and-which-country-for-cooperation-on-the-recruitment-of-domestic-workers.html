The Union Cabinet has approved MoU between India and which country for cooperation on the recruitment of domestic workers?

(A) Japan

(B) Iran

(C) ** Kuwait

(D) Saudi Arabia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Japan.

Concept note-2: -The Union Cabinet chaired by the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has approved the signing of Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) in the field of Judicial Cooperation between India and Judicial Service Commission of the Republic of Maldives.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Q. Union Cabinet has approved a MoU with which country in the field of geology and mineral resources? Notes: The Union Cabinet, headed by Prime Minister Narendra Modi has approved a MoU with Finland in the field of geology.

Concept note-4: -Cabinet approves MoU between India and Finland for cooperation in the field of Geology and Mineral Resources.
