CURRENT AFFAIRS-2019
[SOURCES]

The Union Cabinet has approved the amendment to which article to strengthen northeast autonomous councils?

(A) Article 282

(B) Article 284

(C) Article 281

(D) ** Article 280

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are six (6) Statutory Autonomous Councils namely, Rabha Hasong Autonomous Council. Mising Autonomous Council. Tiwa Autonomous Council.

Concept note-2: -The Sixth Schedule has provision for the creation of Autonomous District Councils, and Regional Councils endowed with certain legislative, executive, judicial and financial powers. However, the administrative powers and functions of these District Council and Regional Council differ from State to State.

Concept note-3: -Presently, 10 Autonomous Councils in Assam, Meghalaya, Mizoram and Tripura are formed by virtue of the Sixth Schedule with the rest being formed as a result of other legislation.
