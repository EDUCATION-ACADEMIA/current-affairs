[SOURCES]

The United Nations (UN) has decided to hold its 2020 ocean conference in which of the following cities?

(A) New York

(B) Paris

(C) New Delhi

(D) ** Lisbon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The forthcoming United Nations Ocean Conference for the conservation and sustainable use of oceans, seas, and marine resources will be held in Lisbon, Portugal in 2020. It will be co-hosted by Governments of Kenya and Portugal.

Concept note-2: -The United Nations Conference on Sustainable Development - or Rio+20 - took place in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil on 20-22 June 2012.

Concept note-3: -On-site registration in Lisbon: 23 June to 1 July 2022 The UN Ocean Conference (27 June to 1 July), co-hosted by Portugal and Kenya, is a unique opportunity to boost collective efforts and find science-based solutions to effectively address the challenges that the ocean is now facing.
