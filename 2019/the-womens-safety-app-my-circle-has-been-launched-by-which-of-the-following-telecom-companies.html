The women’s safety app "My Circle" has been launched by which of the following telecom companies?

(A) Reliance

(B) ** Bharti Airtel

(C) BSNL

(D) Vodafone"

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. The women’s safety app “My Circle” has been launched by which of the following telecom companies? Notes: Telecom operator Bharti Airtel in collaboration with FICCI Ladies Organisation (FLO) launched a women safety app – My Circle - to help women in case they face any problem or a panic situation.

Concept note-2: -Shake2Safety. Shake2Safety has been designed to allow users to notify emergency contacts in case of an emergency via message, location, photo, and audio. The app can also be used without WiFi when the user’s phone is locked or triggered by shaking the phone or clicking the unlock button four times.

Concept note-3: -Indore: Aiming to ensure safety of female passengers, Government Railway Police (GRP) have introduced a new feature in its official mobile application called GRP MP Help, to allow train passengers to raise an alarm in case of an emergency or crime incident during their journey.
