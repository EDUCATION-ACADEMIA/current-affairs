[SOURCES]
This government issued Pink tickets to women passengers for free travel in DTC and cluster buses.

(A) Maharashtra Government

(B) Kerala Government

(C) ** Delhi Government

(D) Andra Pradesh Government

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For women to travel free, they have to take a single colour pass of pink color. This pass will be obtained from the conductor only. For this, there will be no payment to women rides. All DTC (AC, Non AC), Cluster bus running in Delhi-NCR, women can travel free.

Concept note-2: -The ‘pink ticket’ allows women to travel for free on public buses.

Concept note-3: -At least 76% of over two million bus journeys clocked in the past five months in the national capital were using “pink tickets”, a Delhi government initiative which allows women to travel for free in public buses, data accessed by HT from the Delhi Transport Corporation (DTC) showed.

Concept note-4: -Construction workers can register for free passes on the DTC website or at one of the 34 registration booths set up by the construction workers’ welfare board.
