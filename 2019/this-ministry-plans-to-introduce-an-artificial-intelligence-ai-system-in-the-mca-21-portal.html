CURRENT AFFAIRS-2019
[SOURCES]

This ministry plans to introduce an Artificial Intelligence (AI) system in the MCA 21 portal.

(A) Ministry of External Affairs

(B) ** Ministry of Corporate affairs

(C) Ministry of Communications

(D) Ministry of Information and Broadcasting

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MCA (AI & ML) is a 2- year program designed to impart theoretical and practical knowledge in the specialized area of Artificial Intelligence. The curriculum is planned in consultation with industry experts and academic stalwarts.

Concept note-2: -INDIAai is the National AI Portal of India - a central hub for everything AI in India and beyond. A joint initiative of MeitY, NeGD and NASSCOM, the website aims to be the trusted content powerhouse in the backdrop of India’s journey to global prominence in Artificial Intelligence.

Concept note-3: -Candidates having MCA with specialization in AI & ML have great employment opportunities in top recruiting companies and organization as Data Scientist, ML Engineer, Robotics scientist, AI engineer, BI developer, and Big data Analyst. Students can also enroll in a doctorate programme and make their career in research.

Concept note-4: -Specialized MCA in AI and ML allow students to acquire knowledge of mathematics and statistics. The program develops a foundation of different programming languages and technologies. Recruiters look for such a focused skillset in candidates who can harness the strength of AI and ML.
