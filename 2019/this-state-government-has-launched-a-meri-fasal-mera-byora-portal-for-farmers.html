This state government has launched a ‘Meri Fasal Mera Byora’ portal for farmers.

(A) ** Haryana

(B) Punjab

(C) Gujarat

(D) Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Meri Fasal Mera Byora 2022 portal was launched by the Haryana government to provide all agricultural and land information for the state of Haryana online. By uploading information on their crops, farmers in Haryana can benefit from several government programs through this platform.

Concept note-2: -“Meri Fasal Mera Byora” (MFMB) scheme was launched by the Hon’ble CM, Haryana on 4th July 2019 with an objective to digitize the crop records of the cultivators/ farmers. It facilitated the crop-cultivators of the state to register their personal details of land, crops, banks, payment options etc.

Concept note-3: -Meri Fasal Mera Byora is an online portal launched by the Haryana Government to provide the farmers of the state with multiple services on a single portal. The farmers can register into the portal to access all the online services offered by the Haryana Government.

Concept note-4: -Publish Date: March 15, 2022. MFMB portal developed by NIC Haryana for farmers to self-report crop sown information along with land & bank account details.
