This state tops in the Khelo India Youth Games with 91 medals?

(A) ** Maharashtra

(B) Haryana

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Maharashtra. Maharashtra topped the Khelo India Youth Games of the year 2020 with a total of 256 medals.

Concept note-2: -Maharashtra topped the medal tally with 83 medals including 28 gold, 30 silver and 25 bronze medals. Haryana and Madhya Pradesh are at the second and third positions with 23-23 gold medals respectively. Rajasthan is currently in fourth and Odisha is in fifth position.

Concept note-3: -Khelo India Youth Games: Haryana maintain top slot, clinch 49 medals including 22 gold medals. More sports News - Times of India.

Concept note-4: -Hockey Madhya Pradesh clinches Khelo India Youth Games 2022 Men’s U-18 title. In Hockey, Madhya Pradesh defeated Odisha 6-5 to clinch Khelo India Youth Games 2022 Men’s Under-18 Qualifiers title in Bhubaneswar yesterday, December 30.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The first Khelo India School Games was held in New Delhi where Haryana topped the overall medal tally with 102 medals- 38 gold and 26 silver.
