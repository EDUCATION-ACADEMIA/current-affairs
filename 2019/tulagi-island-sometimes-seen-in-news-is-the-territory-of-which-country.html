Tulagi Island, sometimes seen in news, is the territory of which country?

(A) Kiribati

(B) Tuvalu

(C) Nauru

(D) ** Solomon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tulagi, less commonly known as Tulaghi, is a small island-5.5 by 1 kilometre (3.42 by 0.62 mi), area 2.08 square kilometres (0.80 sq mi)-in Solomon Islands, just off the south coast of Ngella Sule.

Concept note-2: -Tulagi, also spelled Tulaghi, town and island in the Solomon Islands, southwestern Pacific Ocean, north of Guadalcanal. The island has a circumference of 3 miles (5 km).

Concept note-3: -Tulagi was the capital of the British Solomon Islands Protectorate between 1897 and 1942. The British withdrawal from the island during the Pacific War, its capture by the Japanese and the American reconquest left the island’s facilities damaged beyond repair.
