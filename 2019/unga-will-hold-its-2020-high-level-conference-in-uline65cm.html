UNGA will hold its 2020 high-level conference in ______

(A) Athens

(B) London

(C) Paris

(D) ** Lisbon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -From 21 to 23 September 2022, the SDG Action Zone will take place during the High-level Week of the UNGA. It will champion the activists and allies accelerating progress towards the SDGs, through candid, honest and powerful conversations.

Concept note-2: -High-level events are dialogues and discussions on most relevant topics of global importance with participation of top UN management as well as government officials, scientific, business, civil society leaders etc.

Concept note-3: -This year marked the 76th session of the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA 76), opening in New York on September 14, 2021.

Concept note-4: -The 77th session of the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA 77) opened on September 13, 2022, with High-Level Week taking place September 19-23 in New York.
