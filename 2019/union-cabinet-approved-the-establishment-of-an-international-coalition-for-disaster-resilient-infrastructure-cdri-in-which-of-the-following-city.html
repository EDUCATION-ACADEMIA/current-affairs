Union Cabinet approved the Establishment of an International Coalition for Disaster Resilient Infrastructure (CDRI) in which of the following city?

(A) Ahmedabad

(B) Bhopal

(C) Mumbai

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union Cabinet has given retrospective approval for the establishment of an International Coalition for Disaster Resilient Infrastructure (CDRI) in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -As an ‘International Organization,’ CDRI will facilitate capacity development and enable mechanisms for supporting disaster-resilient infrastructure both in India and globally. Launched by Hon. Prime Minister of India, Shri.

Concept note-3: -Where is the Coalition for disaster resilient infrastructure located? The CDRI’s Secretariat is located in New Delhi, India.

Concept note-4: -The Coalition for Disaster Resilient Infrastructure (CDRI) and the Government of India (GoI) signed a headquarters agreement on Monday in New Delhi. The Coalition for Disaster Resilient Infrastructure (CDRI) and the Government of India (GoI) signed a headquarters agreement on Monday in New Delhi.
