Union Minister for Tourism K.J. Alphons, has inaugurated the first project under the Swadesh Darshan Project in ______

(A) Uttarkhand

(B) Andra Pradesh

(C) ** Sikkim

(D) Manipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Swadesh Darshan Scheme is a Central Sector scheme launched in 2014-15 by the Ministry of Tourism and Culture,Government of India for the integrated development of theme-based tourist circuits. The scheme aims to promote, develop and harness the potential of tourism in India.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of Tourism under its Swadesh Darshan Scheme (SDS) has sanctioned 76 projects for Rs. 5399.15 crores for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. These sanctioned projects include Tribal and Rural tourism projects.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Swadesh Darshan. It is the flagship scheme of the Ministry of Tourism for the development of tourism infrastructure in the country.
