Ursula von der Leyen has been nominated to lead EU Commission. She belongs to which nation?

(A) France

(B) Italy

(C) ** Germany

(D) Portugal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Commission President (currently Ursula von der Leyen) is proposed by the European Council (the 27 heads of state/governments) and elected by the European Parliament.

Concept note-2: -The President of the European Commission is Ursula von der Leyen, from Germany. The Commission is represented in each EU country by a local office, called a “representation".
