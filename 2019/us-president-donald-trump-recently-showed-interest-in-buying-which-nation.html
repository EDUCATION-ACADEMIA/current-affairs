Concept note-2: -The island is rich in, among others, gold, diamonds, uranium, iron, zinc, crude oil and natural gas. Moreover, there is even a quarter of the world’s reserves of the so-called “rare earth elements” (REEs), which are crucial for the development of advanced technologies.

Concept note-3: -Home to 56,000 people, Greenland has its own extensive local government, but it is also part of the Realm of Denmark. Despite the distance between Greenland and Denmark - about 3532 km between their capitals - Greenland has been associated with Denmark politically and culturally for a millennium.
