Vande Bharat Express runs between Delhi and which of the following states?

(A) Lucknow

(B) Amritsar

(C) Kolkata

(D) ** Katra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The train is covering the route from New Delhi, Haryana, Chandigarh, Punjab, and Himachal Pradesh. The train operates 6 days a week. The fifth train was the Vande Bharat Express launched on November 10, 2022.

Concept note-2: -The new Vande Bharat Express trains will start from the Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CSMT). While one will go on the Mumbai-Pune-Solapur route, the other will take the Mumbai-Nashik-Sainagar Shirdi route.

Concept note-3: -Vande Bharat Express trains list: There are a total of 7 advanced high-speed Vande Bharat Express Trains that are operational in the country till January 12. The 8th Vande Bharat Express between Secunderabad to Visakhapatnam is flagged by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on January 15.

Concept note-4: -The much-awaited Vande Bharat Express (VBE) will finally run between Visakhapatnam and Secunderabad via Vijayawada after it is officially launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi at the Secunderabad Railway Station.

Concept note-5: -Vande Bharat Express train: Varanasi – New Delhi (22435)/ New Delhi – Varanasi (22436) On February 18, 2019, the first Vande Bharat Express train was inaugurated, and its very first route connecting the capital city of Delhi to the holy city of Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh).
