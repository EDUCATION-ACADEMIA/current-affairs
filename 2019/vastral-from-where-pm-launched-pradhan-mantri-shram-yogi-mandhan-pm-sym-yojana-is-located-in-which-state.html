Vastral, from where PM launched ’Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Mandhan (PM-SYM) Yojana’ is located in which state?

(A) Andhra Pradesh

(B) ** Gujarat

(C) Odisha

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: In February 2019, Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan (PM-SYM) under the Ministry of Labour and Employment. It was launched in Vastral, Gujarat.

Concept note-2: -Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan (PM-SYM) was launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi under the Ministry of Labour and Employment in February 2019. It was launched at Vastral in Gujarat.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan is a social welfare scheme launched by the Ministry of Labour and Employment of the Government of India in February 2019 for poor labourers in the unorganised sector from minimum 18 years of age to maximum 40 years.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Tuesday launched a pension scheme ‘Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan for unorganized sector workers in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. This mega pension scheme ensures old age protection for unorganized Workers, whose monthly income is Rs 15,000/ per month or less.

Concept note-5: -Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan is a voluntary and contributory Pension Scheme for Unorganized Workers for entry age of 18 to 40 years with monthly income of Rs. 15000 or less.
