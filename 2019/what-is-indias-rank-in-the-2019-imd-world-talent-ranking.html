What is India’s rank in the 2019 IMD World Talent Ranking?

(A) 50th

(B) ** 59th

(C) 42nd

(D) 50th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the latest IMD World Talent Ranking- 2019, India has slipped 6 places from 53rd in 2018 to 59th rank this year on a global annual list of 63 countries. International Institute for Management Development (IMD) is a business education school based in Switzerland.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 56th.

Concept note-3: -India secures 1st rank in AI talent concentration and overall 61st according to the recently released Network Readiness Index 2022. India’s position has improved by six slots in the latest version of 2022.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is USA. USA ranks 1st in the IMD World Digital Competitiveness Ranking 2019.
