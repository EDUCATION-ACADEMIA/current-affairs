What is the chronological number of Uddhav Thackeray as the CM of Maharashtra?

(A) 18th

(B) 16th

(C) ** 19th

(D) 20th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Uddhav Balasaheb Thackeray (Marathi pronunciation: [ud̪ʱːəʋ ʈʰaːkɾeː], born 27 July 1960) is an Indian politician who served as the 19th Chief Minister of Maharashtra from 2019 to 2022.

Concept note-2: -So far, President’s rule has been imposed thrice in the state: first from February to June 1980 and again from September to October 2014. It was again imposed on 12 November 2019. The current incumbent is Eknath Shinde of the Balasahebanchi Shiv Sena since 30 June 2022.

Concept note-3: -Having been sworn in at the age of 44, he’s the second-youngest Chief Minister in Maharashtra’s history after Sharad Pawar.

Concept note-4: -Sharad Govindrao Pawar (Marathi pronunciation: [ʃəɾəd̪ pəʋaːɾ], born 12 December 1940) is an Indian politician. He has served as the Chief Minister of Maharashtra for four ministries.
