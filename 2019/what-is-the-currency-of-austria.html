What is the currency of Austria?

(A) Peso

(B) Dollar

(C) ** Euro

(D) Pound

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Adoption of the euro The euro banknotes and coins were introduced in Austria on 1 January 2002, after a transitional period of three years when the euro was the official currency but only existed as ‘book money’.

Concept note-2: -The currency of Austria is the Euro.

Concept note-3: -The schilling (German: Schilling) was the currency of Austria from 1925 to 1938 and from 1945 to 1999, and the circulating currency until 2002. The euro was introduced at a fixed parity of €1 = 13.7603 schilling to replace it. The schilling was divided into 100 groschen.

Concept note-4: -The currency in Austria is the euro.
