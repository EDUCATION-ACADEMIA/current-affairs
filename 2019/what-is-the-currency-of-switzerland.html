What is the currency of Switzerland?

(A) Euro

(B) Sterling

(C) ** Swiss franc

(D) Yen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Introduced in 1850, the Swiss franc (CHF) is the official currency of Switzerland, Liechtenstein and the Italian exclave of Campione d’Italia, and is the only franc remaining in Europe.

Concept note-2: -While the Swiss Franc is accepted everywhere, you can also use the Euro in most of the places. If you are on a Switzerland trip, it would be ideal for you to use francs only.

Concept note-3: -1 CHF = 89.664515 INR Feb 09, 2023 16:20 UTC The currency converter below is easy to use and the currency rates are updated frequently.

Concept note-4: -All major department stores, many shops, businesses, hotels, restaurants and souvenir shops accept euros as payment. However, your change will be given in Swiss francs. We advise you to withdraw Swiss francs at a bank ATM or to pay with an EC card or credit card.
