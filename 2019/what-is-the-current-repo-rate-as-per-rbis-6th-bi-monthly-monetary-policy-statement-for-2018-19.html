(A) 6.0 %

(B) 6.75 %

(C) 6.50 %

(D) ** 6.25 %

Concept note-1: -liquidity adjustment facility (LAF) by 25 basis points from 6.5 per cent to 6.25 per cent with immediate effect. Consequently, the reverse repo rate under the LAF stands adjusted to 6.0 per cent, and the marginal standing facility (MSF) rate and the Bank Rate to 6.5 per cent.
