What is the full form of news network “NDTV”?

(A) News Department Television Network

(B) ** New Delhi Television Network

(C) News Daily Television Network

(D) News Direct Television Network

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi Television (NDTV) was founded in 1988. It started its journey by live election coverage in India .

Concept note-2: -New Delhi Television is, and has been for more than a quarter century, a pioneer in India’s news television and digital journalism. Founded in 1988 by Radhika Roy and Prannoy Roy, NDTV is today the most watched, credible and respected news network in India and a leader in Internet.

Concept note-3: -NDTV India (styled as NDTV ) is a Hindi news channel in India owned by New Delhi Television Limited. In June 2016, NDTV decided to launch two separate channels called NDTV India and NDTV Spice in the United Kingdom.

Concept note-4: -Post the open offer, the Adani Group holds 37.5 per cent in NDTV while the Roys jointly own 32.3 per cent.
