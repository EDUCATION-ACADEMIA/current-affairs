What is the India’s rank at the 2019 US Chambers International IP Index?

(A) 52nd

(B) 14th

(C) ** 36th

(D) 43rd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. According to the US Chamber of Commerce, India ranked 44th of 50 countries in the Intellectual Property Index 2018. The US tops the list, followed by the United Kingdom and Sweden. India ranked 36th of 50 countries in the Intellectual Property Index 2019.

Concept note-2: -Overall, India is ranked 43 out of 55 countries in the International IP Index.

Concept note-3: -Union Minister for Commerce and Industry, Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution and Textiles, Shri Piyush Goyal said that India had come a long way in the Global Innovation Index (GII) from the 81st spot in 2015 to the 40th spot in 2022 today.

Concept note-4: -As of 2022, the leading country for the best intellectual property environment was the United States with an overall score of 95.48 points.

Concept note-5: -Switzerland tops the GII for the ninth consecutive year. Its solid strong performance translates to excellent innovation outcomes including patent applications, IP receipts and high-tech manufacturing products.
