What is the length of recently launched pipeline for transportation of natural gas from Siberia to northeast China?

(A) 2000 kms

(B) 2500 kms

(C) ** 3000 kms

(D) 3500 kms

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By the two 1,139 m (3,737 ft) tunnels under the Amur River, it is connected to the 3,371 km (2,095 mi) Heihe–Shanghai pipeline in China. Together they form the eastern route for gas supplies from Siberia to China.

Concept note-2: -That pipeline runs for 3,000 kilometres (1864.11 miles)through Siberia and into northeastern China’s Heilongjiang province.

Concept note-3: -What is Nord Stream 1 and how much gas does it supply? The undersea pipeline stretches 1,200km (745 miles) under the Baltic Sea from the Russian coast near St Petersburg to north-eastern Germany.

Concept note-4: -Nord Stream 2 (German-English mixed expression; German: Nord and English: Stream 2, literally ‘North Stream 2’; Russian: - 2) is a 1,234-kilometre-long (767 mi) natural gas pipeline from Russia to Germany running through the Baltic Sea, financed by Gazprom and several European energy companies.
