What is the limit of restructured loan for farmers to be eligible under Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Farmer Loan Waiver Scheme?

(A) ** 2 Lakh

(B) 5 Lakh

(C) 7Lakh

(D) 3 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under this scheme, the loan of up to Rs 2 lakh of the farmers of the state will be waive off by the Government of Maharashtra.

Concept note-2: -In a farm loan waiver scheme, the Centre or the state Government repays the loan to the banks on behalf of the farmers, simply by using public money collected in the form of taxes. When there is a poor monsoon or natural calamity, farmers cannot repay their loans.

Concept note-3: -“Loan waivers destroy the credit culture which may harm the farmers’ interest in the medium to long term and also squeeze the fiscal space of governments to increase productive investment in agriculture infrastructure,” the report concluded.

Concept note-4: -A loan waive-off is a benefit offered to borrowers by the government where the borrower is no longer under the burden of paying back the loan amount to the lender as a result of a genuine change in their financial circumstances. Examples of loan waivers are the Agricultural Debt Waiver and Debt Relief Scheme in India.
