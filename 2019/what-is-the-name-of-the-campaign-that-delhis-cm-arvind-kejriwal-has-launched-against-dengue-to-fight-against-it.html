What is the name of the campaign that Delhi’s CM Arvind Kejriwal has launched against dengue to fight against it?

(A) War Against Dengue

(B) Dengue War

(C) ** Champions

(D) Warriors

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 4S campaign stands for “Search and Destroy” mosquito breeding places, “Secure Self Protection” from mosquito bite, “Seek Early Consultation” when signs and symptoms of dengue occur, and “Say Yes to Fogging” as a last resort when there is an impending outbreak.
