What is the name of the event where the Indian flag will be hoisted in J & K in 73rd Independence Day of India for the first time?

(A) Jakas-Azad

(B) Jashn-ul-Fitr

(C) ** Jashn-e-Azad

(D) J & K-e-Fitr

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Bharat Jodo Yatra Completed of “30 January 2023” after unfurled National Tricolour by Rahul Gandhi in Sher-E-Kashmir Stadium,Srinagar.

Concept note-2: -It is the Prime Minister who raises the national flag up and hoists it with the aim to honour the historical event of the day. Whereas, when the flag is unfurled on Republic Day, it is closed and tied at the top of the pole. The President unfurls it without pulling the flag up as it takes place on Independence Day.

Concept note-3: -Every year on Independence Day, the tricolour is hoisted by the Prime Minister of India at the Red Fort. The national flag is tied at the lower part of the flagpole and it is raised by the Prime Minister.

Concept note-4: -Every year, the Prime Minister hoists the National Flag at Delhi’s Red Fort and addresses the nation, which is followed by a military parade. Q. The Andaman and Nicobar Islands administration has celebrated the 74th anniversary of First Flag Hoisting Day on which date?

Concept note-5: -On Republic Day 2023, the flag-hoisting ceremony is scheduled to start at 8 am. At 9:30 am, the parade will begin.
