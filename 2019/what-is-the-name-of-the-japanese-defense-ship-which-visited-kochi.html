What is the name of the Japanese defense ship which visited Kochi?

(A) USS Susquehanna

(B) ** JS Sazanami

(C) INS Arihant

(D) USS Missouri

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -JMSDF Destroyer Suzutsuki made a port call at Kochi from 18 to 19 October 2022. The ship was accorded a warm welcome.

Concept note-2: -INS: Indian Navy Ship The Royal navy is the precursor of the Indian Navy, so until today, Indian navy follows some customs of the royal navy. At present (2019) there are a total of seventeen Active Indian Navy Ships which are as follows: INS Arighat (S74): It is an advanced nuclear-powered Ballistic Missile Submarine.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Navy’s present force level comprises about 150 ships and submarines.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Navy is a multi-dimensional force that has been organized to safeguard India’s maritime territorial integrity and other maritime interests. It is headed by the Chief of the Naval Staff or the CNS. He is of the rank of Admiral and has his headquarters at New Delhi.
