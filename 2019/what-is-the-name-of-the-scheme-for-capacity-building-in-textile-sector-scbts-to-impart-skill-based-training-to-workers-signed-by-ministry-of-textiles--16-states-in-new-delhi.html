What is the name of the Scheme for Capacity Building In Textile Sector (SCBTS) to impart skill based training to workers signed by Ministry of textiles & 16 states in New Delhi?

(A) Aushadhi

(B) Upadhyaya

(C) Samridhi

(D) ** Samarth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: The Textile Ministry decided to impart skill training under Samarth – Textile Sector Capacity Building Scheme (SCBTS).

Concept note-2: -SAMARTH is a placement-oriented programme targeting skill development of 10 lakh youth in the entire value chain of textiles, excluding Spinning & Weaving in the organized sector. The Government launched the Scheme for Capacity Building in Textile Sectorii (SCBTS) and named it Samarth Scheme.

Concept note-3: -Samarth (Scheme for Capacity Building In Textile Sector) is a flagship skill development scheme approved in continuation to the Integrated Skill Development Scheme for 12th Five Year Plan (FYP), Cabinet Committee of Economic Affairs.

Concept note-4: -The ‘Scheme for Capacity Building in Textile Sector (SCBTS)’ shall be known by the name “ (Samarth)”, signifying the broad objective of the scheme to skill the youth for gainful and sustainable employment in the textile sector.

Concept note-5: -Ministry of Textiles has launched many schemes over the past few years for the promotion of the Textile Industry in India and improving the livelihood of the people associated with it. Schemes like Project SURE, Scheme For Integrated Textile Park (SITP), Silk Samagra, Powertex India, and Samarth were launched.
