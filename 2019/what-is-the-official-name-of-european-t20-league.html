What is the official name of European T20 league?

(A) ** Euro T20 Slam

(B) Euro T20

(C) Euro T20 league

(D) European T20 Slam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Euro T20 Slam is a planned professional Twenty20 cricket league. The first edition of the tournament was initially scheduled to start in August 2019, with matches to be held in Ireland, Scotland and the Netherlands. Its launch has been postponed on several occasions due to the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Concept note-2: -Indian Premier League (IPL), Indian professional Twenty20 (T20) cricket league established in 2008. The league, which is based on a round-robin group and knockout format, has teams in major Indian cities.

Concept note-3: -The T20 Blast, currently named the Vitality Blast for sponsorship reasons, is a professional Twenty20 cricket competition for English and Welsh first-class counties.

Concept note-4: -The International League T20 (ILT20), (also endorsed as DPW ILT20 for sponsorship reasons) is a ongoing 20-20 cricket tournament being played in the United Arab Emirates. It is sanctioned by Emirates Cricket Board.

Concept note-5: -The European Cricket League (ECL), is a professional Ten10 cricket league contested by the cricket clubs of European countries.
