What is the position of Hyderabad’s Rajiv Gandhi International Airport among the top ten best airports in the world?

(A) 7

(B) ** 8

(C) 5

(D) 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to AirHelp’s survey, RGIA was given a score of 8.27 out of 10, taking a number of factors into consideration like ‘On-Time Performance’, ‘Service Quality’, and ‘Food and Shops’ and stands in the eighth position. Yeyyy!

Concept note-2: -The airport has also progressed in its overall ranking, moving from 64th position in 2021 to 63rd position in 2022 World’s Top 100 Airport league.

Concept note-3: -Hyderabad is also the third best airport in South Asia after Delhi and Bangalore. Delhi’s Indira Gandhi International Airport secured the 37th rank and Bangalore’s Kempegowda International Airport was listed 61st.

Concept note-4: -Among the separate global rankings for ‘Large Airports’, Hyderabad’s Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, Bengaluru’s Kempegowda International Airport, Chennai’s Chennai Airport and Kolkata’s Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose Airport stood at 11th, 15th, 16th and 18th respectively.

Concept note-5: -1. Istanbul Airport (IST) For centuries, Istanbul has sat at the crossroads of international trade and travel, so it’s only natural that the Turkish city has finally stepped into its position as a global transport hub in the age of air travel as well.
