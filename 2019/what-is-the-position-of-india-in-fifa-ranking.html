What is the position of India in FIFA ranking?

(A) 75

(B) 86

(C) 98

(D) ** 104

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India is in the 106th spot on the FIFA Ranking 2022 list. Indian men’s football team has a score of 1192 points.

Concept note-2: -FIFA Football Ranking 2022 The Indian team, which is at the 104th position, is just above the New Zealand team. According to the FIFA Football Ranking 2022, New Zealand was defeated by Costa Rica, due to which it withdrew from making its place in the World Cup.

Concept note-3: -Indian men’s football team is ranked 106th in the world while the women’s team is ranked 61, Minister for Sports and Youth Affairs Anurag Thakur told the Lok Sabha.

Concept note-4: -The FIFA Men’s World Ranking is a ranking system for men’s national teams in association football, led by Brazil as of 2022.

Concept note-5: -AGRA: India has not qualified for Fifa World Cup 2022 but the biggest football extravaganza, this time being held in Qatar, has a direct connection with the country and especially the city of Taj.
