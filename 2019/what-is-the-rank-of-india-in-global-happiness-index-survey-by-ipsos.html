What is the rank of India in Global Happiness Index survey by Ipsos?

(A) 5th

(B) 8th

(C) ** 9th

(D) 15th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India ranked 136th in the World Happiness Report 2022. 139th in 2021 and 144th in World Happiness Report 2020.

Concept note-2: -With an average life evaluation of 3.819, India now holds the 136th place among the nations in the World Happiness Index 2023, which will be released in 2023.

Concept note-3: -Finland is in the top position in the world happiness report in 2022. Followed by Denmark and Iceland in second and third place. Switzerland, Netherlands, Luxembourg, Sweden, Norway, Israel and New Zealand, were among the top 10 ‘happiest’ countries in the world [1].

Concept note-4: -The World Happiness Report 2022 was published by the United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network. Finland is the happiest country and India is among the 10 nations with the largest drop.
