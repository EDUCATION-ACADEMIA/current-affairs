What is the revised interest rate of Employee’s Provident Fund (EPF) for 2018-19?

(A) 8.45 %

(B) 8.55 %

(C) 8.35 %

(D) ** 8.65 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Earlier in the year 2016-17 and 2018-19, the EPFO had given an 8.65% rate of interest to the subscribers.

Concept note-2: -It should be noted that the current proposed PF Interest Rate for FY 2021-22 at 8.1% is the lowest of the interest rates paid by EPFO since 1977-78, i.e. at 8.5% (FY 2019-20 and 2020-21), 8.65% (FY 2018-19), 8.55% (FY 2017-18), 8.65% (FY 2016-17), and so on.

Concept note-3: -However, the PPF interest rate has been falling consistently since 2013. While the PPF investment limit was increased from Rs 1 lakh/year to Rs 1.5 lakh/year in 2014, the interest has been reduced by 1.7% from 8.8% to 7.1% between 2013 and 2022, as per official data.
