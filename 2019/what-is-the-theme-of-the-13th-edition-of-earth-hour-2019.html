What is the theme of the 13th edition of Earth Hour 2019?

(A) ** Connect2Earth

(B) Save Earth

(C) NatureCallingYou

(D) GiveBack

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Theme was “Reduce, Rethink and Change the way we live”. This was the 13th edition of Earth hour celebrated in 2019. This year more than 180 countries participated in support of the Earth Hour 2019.

Concept note-2: -Earth Hour 2019 theme is “Reduce, Reuse, Change the Way We Live”. Earth Hour is the only movement in the support of earth in which the major landmarks of the world from Eiffel tower to Sydney Opera House and from Golden Gate to Birmingham Palace go dark in support of Earth Hour.

Concept note-3: -’Shape Our Future’ was The Earth Hour 2022 theme. Every year, Earth Hour is observed worldwide on the last Saturday of March to support the struggle against climate transformation and responsibility toward a better planet. Earth Hour in 2022 is being commemorated on March 26, 2022.

Concept note-4: -In 2016, in recognition of the interconnected world we live in today thanks to social media and the crosscutting nature of climate change itself, Earth Hour aimed to move the movement for climate action from skylines to timelines.
