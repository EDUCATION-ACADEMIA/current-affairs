What is the theme of the 2019 edition of UN Global Road Safety Week?

(A) Road Security a Goal, Not an Intermission

(B) ** Leadership for road safety

(C) Young road users, including young drivers

(D) Walk for Road Security

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Requested by the United Nations General Assembly, the Fifth United Nations Global Road Safety Week was held from 6-12 May 2019. This Week focused on leadership for road safety. It is widely acknowledged that stronger leadership for road safety is needed to achieve the Global Goals.

Concept note-2: -The theme of the 33rd Road Safety Week 2022 is ‘Sadak Suraksha Jeevan Raksha’. Road Safety Week is celebrated from January 11 to January 17 every year.

Concept note-3: -The theme of Road Safety Week 2022 (9-15 May) is ROAD SAFETY HEROES, celebrating the heroic work of road safety professionals, and explaining how we can all play a part in making journeys safer for everyone.

Concept note-4: -Road Safety Week Themes Theme for Road Safety Week 2016 – “Road Safety-Time for action”.

Concept note-5: -The theme for road safety week 2017 was “Your safety, secures your family-Be cautious on Roads”.
