What is the theme of the 2019 National Safe Motherhood Day (NSMD)?

(A) End Maternal Mortality

(B) For safe childbirth, let’s visit health centres

(C) Respectful Maternity Care

(D) ** Midwives for Mothers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Midwives for Mothers’ was the theme for 2019.

Concept note-2: -National Safe Motherhood Day (NSMD) 2022: Theme The goal of these campaigns is to seek every woman has the right to live and they could survive during pregnancy and childbirth.

Concept note-3: -National Safe Motherhood Day is observed every year on April 11. It is aimed at raising awareness about the care that a pregnant woman needs before, during, and after childbirth.

Concept note-4: -Q. ‘National Safe Motherhood Day’ is celebrated in India on April 11, the birth anniversary of which personality? Notes: The Government of India in the year 2003 designated 11 April as National Safe Motherhood Day. The day also marks the birth anniversary of Kasturba Gandhi.

Concept note-5: -In the year of 2003, the Indian government recognised April 11th as National Safe Motherhood Day at the proposal of WRAI, an association of 1800 organisations. Kasturba Gandhi’s birthday is also celebrated on this day.
