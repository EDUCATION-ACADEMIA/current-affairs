What is the theme of the 2019 World Tuberculosis Day (WTD)?

(A) Gear up to end TB

(B) Unite to End TB

(C) Wanted: Leaders for a TB-free world

(D) ** It’s time

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This World TB Day, WHO calls on governments, affected communities, civil society organizations, health-care providers, and national/international partners to unite forces under the banner “Find. Treat. All. #EndTB” to ensure no one is left behind.

Concept note-2: -The theme of the 2022 World TB Day was: Invest to End TB. Save Lives.

Concept note-3: -What is the theme of World TB Day 2023? The theme of World Tuberculosis Day was ‘Invest to End TB.

Concept note-4: -’Unite to end TB’ is the global theme for World Tuberculosis Day and it resonates as a reminder that we can overcome the challenge, we can end TB, provided we stand together.

Concept note-5: -Each year, we commemorate World Tuberculosis (TB) Day on March 24 to raise public awareness about the devastating health, social and economic consequences of TB, and to step up efforts to end the global TB epidemic.
