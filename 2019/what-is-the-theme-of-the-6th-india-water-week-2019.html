What is the theme of the 6th India Water Week 2019?

(A) Water Cooperation-Building Partnerships

(B) Water is the Key to Socio-economic Development and Quality of Life

(C) Minimising Harmful Fluxes From Land to Water

(D) ** Water cooperation:Coping with 21st Century Challenges

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Sixth edition of India Water Week-2019 with the Theme “Water Cooperation – Coping with 21st century challenges” was organized during 24th - 28th September, 2019 at New Delhi, India with Japan and European Union as International partners.

Concept note-2: -THEME FOR IWW 2022: “WATER SECURITY FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT AND EQUITY”

Concept note-3: -Explanation: The 6th India Water Week-2019 (IWW-2019) is to be held at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi from 24 to 28 September 2019. It is organised by the Ministry of Jal-Shakti, Department of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation, Government of India.

Concept note-4: -The theme of the 7th India Water Impact Summit (IWIS 2022) is ‘Restoration and Conservation of Small Rivers in a Large Basin’ with emphasis on the select aspects of ‘Mapping and Convergence of 5Ps’ - People, Policy, Plan, Programme and Project….
