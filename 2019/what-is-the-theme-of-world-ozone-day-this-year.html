What is the theme of World Ozone Day this year?

(A) Control the gas.

(B) ** 32 Years and Healing.

(C) Stop Carbon Emission.

(D) Carbon free home.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of this year’s World Ozone Day is Montreal Protocol35: global cooperation protecting life on earth. The theme not only encourages sustainable development but also recognises the wider impact of the Montreal Protocol.

Concept note-2: -The theme of World Ozone Day 2022 is “Montreal Protocol35: global cooperation protecting life on earth”.

Concept note-3: -International Day for the Preservation of the Ozone Layer 2022 marks 35 years of signing the original Montreal Protocol with the slogan of ‘Ozone for life’, a message that ozone must be protected for future generations as well.

Concept note-4: -In this NASA false-color image, the blue and purple shows the hole in Earth’s protective ozone layer over Antarctica on Oct. 5, 2022. Earth’s protective ozone layer is slowly but noticeably healing at a pace that would fully mend the hole over Antarctica in about 43 years, a new United Nations report says.
