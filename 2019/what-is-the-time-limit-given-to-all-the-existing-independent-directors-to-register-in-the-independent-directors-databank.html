What is the time limit given to all the existing independent directors to register in the Independent Director’s Databank?

(A) ** 3 months

(B) 6 months

(C) 9 months

(D) 10 months

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Response: Individuals not exempted are required to pass the online proficiency self-assessment test within 2 years of their inclusion of their name in the databank.

Concept note-2: -As per Section 149(10), independent director shall hold office for a term up to five consecutive years on the Board of a company but shall be eligible for reappointment on passing of a special resolution by the company and disclosure of such appointment in the Board’s report.

Concept note-3: -According to section 149(10), an independent director can be appointed for a term up to 5 consecutive years. This was clarified by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs via its General Circular 14/ 2014, stating that the appointment of an independent director for the term of 5 years or less is permissible.

Concept note-4: -Process in MCA Portal Go to “ID Databank Registration” section under “MCA Services” and click on “Individual Registration. Enter DIN/PAN/Passport details and click on “Submit”. Enter email ID and mobile no. (if not pre-populated).

Concept note-5: -An Independent Director should be minimum of 21 years. A Non-executive director should not be more than age of 75 years.
