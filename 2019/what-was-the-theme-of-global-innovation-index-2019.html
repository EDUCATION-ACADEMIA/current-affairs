What was the theme of ‘Global Innovation Index’ 2019?

(A) Family planning is a human right

(B) Innovation is the way to success

(C) Information Technology for Science Education

(D) ** Creating Healthy Lives-The Future of Medical Innovation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Theme: Creating Healthy Lives - The Future of Medical Innovation. The GII 2019 looks at the medical innovation landscape, exploring the role and dynamics of medical innovation as it shapes the future of healthcare, and analyzing the potential influence this may have on economic growth.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 edition of the Global Innovation Index (GII) tracks the most recent global innovation trends against the background of an ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, slowing productivity growth and other evolving challenges.

Concept note-3: -India increased the most in three pillars: Institutions (61, from 77 in 2019), business sophistication (55, from 65 in 2019), and creative outputs (64, from 78 in 2019) India has moved four places on the Global Innovation Index (GII) 2020 to rank at 48 since 2019.

Concept note-4: -Theme for 2022: “What is the future of innovation-driven growth?”.

Concept note-5: -India is ranked 40th, six places above the previous year’s rank.
