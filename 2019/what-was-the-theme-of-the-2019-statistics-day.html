What was the theme of the 2019 Statistics Day?

(A) Protecting Civilians, Protecting Peace

(B) ** Sustainable Development Goals

(C) Families and Climate Action:Focus on SDG 13

(D) Bridging the standardisation gap

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of Statistics Day, 2019 is “Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)”. The theme has been chosen for intensive and focused discussions towards filling the data gaps and improvement of timelines/quality in SDGs.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sustainable Development Goals. The theme of Statistics Day, 2019 was Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Concept note-3: -The theme of Statistics Day, 2022 is “Data for Sustainable Development”. On this occasion, MoSPI also recognizes the outstanding contribution through high-quality research in the field of applied and theoretical statistics benefiting the official statistical system through awards instituted for this purpose.

Concept note-4: -The day also brings our attention to the work done by statisticians, who provide us with reliable data and numbers for future reference. The theme for World Statistics Day 2022 is “Data for Sustainable Development.”

Concept note-5: -The theme of Statistics Day, 2022 is ‘Data for Sustainable Development’.
