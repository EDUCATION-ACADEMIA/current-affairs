What was the theme of the International Albinism Awareness Day 2019?

(A) ** Still Standing Strong

(B) Social and Human Sciences

(C) Advancing with renewed hope

(D) Shining our light to the world

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2022 Theme - United in making our voice heard.

Concept note-2: -They are often denied the chance to go to school, get a job, have a family and face discrimination for their entire lives, simply because of their appearance. Albinism is a genetic condition that causes a deficit in the body’s biosynthesis of melanin, a pigment that colors the skin, hair and eyes.

Concept note-3: -Several genes give instructions for making one of several proteins involved in producing melanin. Melanin is made by cells called melanocytes that are found in your skin, hair and eyes. Albinism is caused by a change in one of these genes.

Concept note-4: -A slogan for a cause The theme for this year’s International Albinism Awareness Day will be “Still standing strong”.
