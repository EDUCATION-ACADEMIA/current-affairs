What was the theme of the World Pharmacists Day 2019?

(A) Pharmacists:Your Partner in health

(B) Pharmacists-simplifying your medicines use, no matter how complex

(C) Pharmacists:Your medicines experts

(D) ** Safe and effective medicines for all

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of World Pharmacist Day 2019 was “Safe and effective medicines for all". It focuses on the safety of patients by improving the use of medicine and reducing the errors related to medicine. Therefore, the role of a pharmacist is very important.

Concept note-2: -“Pharmacy united in action for a healthier world” was the theme of World Pharmacists Day on 25 September 2022. The theme highlighted pharmacy’s positive impact on health around the world and aimed to further strengthen solidarity among the profession.

Concept note-3: -WORLD PHARMACY DAY 2022: THEME This year’s theme, “Pharmacy united in action for a healthier world,” aims to promote unity and highlight the sound effects of pharmacy on health.

Concept note-4: -World Pharmacy Day started in 2009 in Istanbul during the FIP Council. The aim of this exercise is to promote and advocate for the role of pharmacists in delivering safe effective healthcare. September 25th was the date on which FIP was found and hence was chosen as World Pharmacy Day. What is FIP?

Concept note-5: -Mahadeva Lal Schroff: father of Indian pharmacy education.
