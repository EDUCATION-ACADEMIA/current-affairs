When did National Space Day 2019 observed?

(A) May 2

(B) ** May 3

(C) May 4

(D) May 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, National Space Day is celebrated on first Friday of May and in 2019 it is celebrated on 3 May, 2019. This day educate , encourage the youth of our nation to take science as subject and study about space exploration etc.

Concept note-2: -National Space Day began in 1997 as just plain National Space Day by the Lockheed Martin Corporation. The day was designed to allow people to take in the myriad wonders of the vast, uncharted space that our planet and countless others float in.

Concept note-3: -The World Space Week 2022 theme is “Space and Sustainability” focusing on achieving sustainability in space and achieving sustainability from space. The theme is inspired by how sustainability in space relates to how humanity uses space, most pressingly, the orbital area surrounding Earth.

Concept note-4: -May5 Fun Holiday – Space Day Space Day is celebrated annually on the first Friday of May. An unofficial educational holiday created in 1997 by Lockheed Martin, Space Day aims to promote the STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics) fields among young people. Learn more about outer space on Space Day.

Concept note-5: -Many scientific organizations celebrate this day with demonstrations, gatherings, and educational programs. You can be sure that your local science museum or observatory is doing something or other on this day, and it’s an excellent chance for you or your kids to learn about the wonders of the galaxy and beyond.
