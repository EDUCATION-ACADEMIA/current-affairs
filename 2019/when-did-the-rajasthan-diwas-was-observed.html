When did the Rajasthan Diwas was observed?

(A) 29th March

(B) 1st April

(C) ** 30th March

(D) 31st Mmarch

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajasthan Day 2022: March 30 is celebrated across the state to mark the formation of the state of Rajasthan. On this day in 1949, the four states, namely Jodhpur, Jaipur, Bikaner, and Jaisalmer, joined with the United State of Rajasthan, and the region came to be known as Greater Rajasthan.

Concept note-2: -The state was formed on March 30th, 1949 when Rajputana was merged into the Dominion of India. Jaipur is the largest city was declared as the capital of the state. Rajasthan Diwas is observed on March 30th every year to commemorate the formation of the state.

Concept note-3: -Rajasthan Foundation Day is observed on March 30th to commemorate the formation of the state. Jaipur: Rajasthan was formed on March 30, 1949, when Rajputana the name adopted by the British Raj for its dependencies in the region was merged into the Dominion of India.

Concept note-4: -On March 30, 1949 Rajputana was merged into the Dominion of India. Jaipur was declared the capital of the state as it was the largest city. March 30 is observed as Rajasthan Diwas or Rajasthan Statehood Day as the state was formed on on this day in 1949 when Rajputana was merged into the Dominion of India.

Concept note-5: -Rajasthan was formed on March 30, 1949 when Rajputana was merged into the Indian Union. Rajasthan Day 2022 celebrates the 73rd foundation day of the state.
