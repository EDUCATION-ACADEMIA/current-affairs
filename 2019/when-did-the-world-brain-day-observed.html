When did the World Brain Day observed?

(A) July 21

(B) ** July 22

(C) July 23

(D) July 24

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Brain Day is observed on July 22 every year to create awareness on brain health.

Concept note-2: -Each year, the World Federation of Neurology-a non-governmental organization-celebrates World Brain Day on July 22.

Concept note-3: -History of World Brain Day The World Federation of Neurology was the first to establish World Brain Day as a worldwide commemoration. This organization was founded in 1957 in Belgium and is now one of the most prominent promoters of neurology and brain health research in the world.

Concept note-4: -Fostering quality neurology and brain health worldwide World Brain Day (WBD) 2022 was dedicated to the theme “Brain Health for all” as our brains continue to be challenged by pandemics, wars, climate change and the myriad of disorders impacting human existence globally.

Concept note-5: -Each year, the day has a theme to concentrate efforts on the most pressing issues in brain health. The theme for 2022 World Brain Day is “Brain Health for all”.
