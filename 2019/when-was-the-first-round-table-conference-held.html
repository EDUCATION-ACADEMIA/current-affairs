When was the first round table conference held?

(A) 12 Nov 1928

(B) 12 Nov 1929

(C) ** 12 Nov 1930

(D) 12 Nov 1931

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -First Round Table Conference (November 1930 – January 1931) The Round Table Conference officially inaugurated by His Majesty George V on November 12, 1930 in Royal Gallery House of Lords at London and chaired by the Prime Minister.

Concept note-2: -About: In response to the inadequacy of the Simon Report, the Labour Government, which had come to power under Ramsay MacDonald in 1929, decided to hold a series of Round Table Conferences in London. The first Round Table Conference convened from 12 November 1930 to 19 January 1931.

Concept note-3: -The First Round Table Conference was held between November 1930 and January 1931 in London.

Concept note-4: -First Round Table Conference (November 1930 – January 1931)
