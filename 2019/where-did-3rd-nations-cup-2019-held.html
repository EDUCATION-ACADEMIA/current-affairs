Where did 3rd Nation’s Cup 2019 held?

(A) China

(B) ** Serbia

(C) Malaysia

(D) Thailand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The match was held at the Cairo International Stadium in Cairo, Egypt, on 19 July 2019 and was contested by Senegal and Algeria. Algeria won the final 1–0 for their second Africa Cup of Nations title, and their first since 1990.

Concept note-2: -The teams in each group played a single round robin. After the group stage, the top two teams and the four best third-placed teams advanced to the round of 16. The winners advanced to the quarter-finals.
