Where did India’s first natural Ice Cafe located?

(A) Himchal Pradesh

(B) ** J & K

(C) Sikkim

(D) Arunachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This latest tourist attraction at a height of around 14,000 feet is located in Gya, a village in Leh (Manali-Leh Highway). This cafe was created by a natural process by the Border Road Organisation officials and some locals during winters.

Concept note-2: -Kangsing, India’s First Ice Cafe In Chilling, Leh | So Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Located in the Ski resort of Gulmarg, the café has been attracting a major crowd and is started by the Kolahoi Green Group of Hotels and Resorts. The first-ever Igloo café in India is a one-of-a-kind experience and the ice restaurant is winning the hearts of the visitors.

Concept note-4: -In a unique and first-of-its-kind initiative, the Department of Science & Technology (DST), Govt of India, has undertaken to set up India’s first-ever “Night Sky Sanctuary” in Ladakh which will be completed within next three months.
