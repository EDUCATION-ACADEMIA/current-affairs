Where did the First BRICS Sherpa Meeting held?

(A) Moscow

(B) Beijing

(C) New Delhi

(D) ** Curitiba

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Sherpas’ meeting is the first official event held in China after it took over the BRICS chairmanship, and it also served to start the political preparations for the BRICS summit.

Concept note-2: -China 2022 From 18 to 19 January 2022, China’s BRICS Sherpa and Vice Foreign Minister Ma Zhaoxu hosted the First BRICS Sherpas’ Meeting this year, which was attended by the BRICS Sherpas of Russia, India, Brazil and South Africa as well as representatives of relevant line agencies of China.

Concept note-3: -First Summit, Yekaterinburg, Russia, June 2009 Prime Minister, Dr Manmohan Singh, attended the first BRIC Summit in Yekaterinburg on 16 June 2009. The Summit adopted Joint Statement of BRIC Leaders and a Joint Statement on Global Food Security.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is China. The first BRICS Sherpas meeting of 2022 was held virtually on January 18-19, 2022. China has taken on the rotating chairmanship of BRICS in 2022.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The first BRICS Summit, after the inclusion of South Africa, was held at Sanya, China in April 2011.
