Where was the country’s biggest sculpture, stucco figurine from buddhist site excavated by Indian archaeologists recently?

(A) Belegali in Hubli, Karnataka

(B) ** Phanigiri in Suryapet, Telangana

(C) Maniyoor in Kannur, Kerala

(D) Chandur in Belgaum, Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Archaeologists in Telangana have recently unearthed a rare life-sized stucco sculpture from a Buddhist site at Phanigiri in Suryapet. It is considered to be the biggest stucco sculpture found in the excavations in the country till date.

Concept note-2: -Indian archaeologists recently unearthed a rare life-sized stucco sculpture from a Buddhist site at Phanigiri in Suryapet, Telangana.

Concept note-3: -Stucco sculptures are made as decorative coatings on the walls and ceilings of a structure. Use. It is a material made from aggregates, binder, and water. It is applied when it is wet and then it hardens in to a very dense solid.
