Where was the G20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors Meeting held?

(A) ** Fukuoka, Japan

(B) New Delhi, India

(C) Beijing, China

(D) Washington, D.C., U.S.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India hosts the First G20 Finance and Central Bank Deputies Meeting under the G20 India Presidency in Bengaluru, Karnataka.

Concept note-2: -The G20 Finance Track, led by Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors of G20 countries, focuses on economic and financial issues. It provides an effective forum for global economic discourse and policy coordination.

Concept note-3: -The first G20 Infrastructure Working Group (IWG) meeting under G20 India Presidency will take place in Pune, Maharashtra on16th -17th January 2023.

Concept note-4: -India’s G -20 Presidency India holds the Presidency of the G20 from December 1, 2022 to November 30, 2023. The 43 Heads of Delegations- the largest ever in G20-will be participating in the final New Delhi Summit in September next year.

Concept note-5: -Story highlights. India Budget 2023: Country’s finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman said that New Delhi’s G20 presidency presents a ‘unique opportunity’ in a larger global spotlight as it strengthens the country’s role in the world economic order when countries across the globe are facing various challenges.
