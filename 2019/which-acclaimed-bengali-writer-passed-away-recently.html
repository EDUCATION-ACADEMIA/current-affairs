Which acclaimed Bengali writer passed away recently?

(A) Shakti Chattopadhyay

(B) Satyajit Ray

(C) Nabarun Bhattacharya

(D) ** Nabanita Deb Sen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eminent Bengali writer Buddhadeb Guha, author of many notable works such as ‘Madhukari’ (Honey Gatherer), died on Sunday, August 29, 2021. He was 85. Guha, died of post-covid complications at a private hospital in Kolkata on Sunday at 11:25 pm after a massive cardiac arrest, his family said.

Concept note-2: -Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee who was personally monitoring Debnath’s health since he was admitted to the hospital in the first week of January said in her message, “I am deeply saddened by the demise of eminent children’s writer and cartoonist Narayan Debnath.

Concept note-3: -Buddhadeb Guha (29 June 1936 – 29 August 2021) was an Indian writer of fiction in Bengali language. Apart from writing, he was a trained singer and a painter too.

Concept note-4: -Rabindranath Tagore and Kazi Nazrul Islam are the most well-known prolific writers of Bengal in 20th century.
