Which Airline has banned the usage of single-use plastic on the flight of its subsidiaries from October 2, 2019?

(A) SpiceJet

(B) GoAir

(C) ** Air India

(D) IndiGo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Air India is all set to impose a ban on 1st use plastic products like bags, cups and straws from 2nd October 2019. For the first phase, the ban will be implemented on all flights of Air India Express and Alliance air flights.

Concept note-2: -Air India, India’s national airline, will ban the usage of single-use plastic aboard its flights from October 2, 2019.

Concept note-3: -In the second phase, the single-use plastic will be banned in all Air India flights, it said. In his Independence Day speech, Prime Minister Narendra Modi had urged people to shun single-use plastic and encouraged usage of jute and cloth bags to protect the environment.

Concept note-4: -The government has banned the manufacture, sale and use of identified single-use plastic items like plates, cups, straws, trays, and polystyrene from July 1, 2022.

Concept note-5: -To phase out select SUP items by 2022, the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC), Government of India notified the Plastic Waste Management Amendment Rules, 2021, on 12 August 2021.
