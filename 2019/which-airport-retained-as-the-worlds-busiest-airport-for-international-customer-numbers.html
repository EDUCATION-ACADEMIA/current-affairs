Which Airport retained as the world’s busiest airport for international customer numbers?

(A) Beijing Capital International Airport

(B) Los Angeles International Airport

(C) Tokyo Haneda Airport

(D) ** Dubai International Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dubai International retains title of the world’s busiest international airport.

Concept note-2: -Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta regained the top position in 2021. Alternatively, London has the world’s busiest city airport system by passenger count. As of 2021, the United States accounts for the top seven busiest airports in the world, and 10 of the top 12 positions.

Concept note-3: -Dubai International Airport (DXB) retained its position as the world’s busiest international hub, toppling the UK’s London Heathrow with over 2.1 million passengers registered in October, according to the aviation consultancy OAG.
