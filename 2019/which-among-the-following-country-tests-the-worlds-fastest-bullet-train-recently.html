Which among the following country tests the World’s fastest bullet train, recently?

(A) US

(B) China

(C) ** Japan

(D) Philippines

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Besides Maglev, China already has a network of 37,900 km of high-speed rail (HSR or bullet trains). It plan to almost double the network in the next 10 years. Chinese researchers are working on maglev technology on cars as well.

Concept note-2: -In fact, you are floating! This dreamlike experience is will soon be a reality thanks to Japan’s famous Maglev bullet trains, the fastest train in the world. Japan is already well known for its extensive Shinkansen train system, which has been in operation since 1964.

Concept note-3: -As of August 2022, the fastest train on Earth, based on its record speed, is the Japanese L0 Series Maglev with a record speed of 603 kilometers per hour.

Concept note-4: -Fastest Train in India: The Vande Bharat Express is the fastest train in India and has the fastest top design and can reach the speed of 180 km/h (110 mph).
