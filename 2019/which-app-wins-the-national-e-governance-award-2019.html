Which app wins the National e-Governance Award 2019?

(A) mPassport Seva

(B) UMANG

(C) BHIM

(D) ** IRCTC Rail Connect

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UMANG, IRCTC App, iSTART Among The Winners Of e-Governance Awards 2019.

Concept note-2: -MUMBAI: A team of researchers at IIT Bombay was recently awarded the 25th National E-Governance Gold Award 2022 in Jammu and Kashmir, by union minister of state for science and technology Jitendra Singh.

Concept note-3: -The National Awards for e-Governance (NAeG) will be presented under 5 categories of the NAeG Scheme - 2022 to 18 e-Governance initiatives at Central, State and District levels, Academic & Research Institutions and Public Sector Undertaking. This includes 9 Gold and 9 Silver Awards.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution EPFO won the e-governance gold award under the category Innovative use of technology in e-governance. EPFO won this award for its Universal Account Number Programme (UAN programme). The silver award in this category was awarded to Delhi Police for their ‘Lost Report Application’.

Concept note-5: -e-Panchayat Mission Mode Project (eGramSwaraj and AuditOnline) of Ministry of Panchayati Raj has won the GOLD AWARD under the category “Excellence in Government Process Re-engineering for Digital Transformation” of the National Awards for e-Governance.
