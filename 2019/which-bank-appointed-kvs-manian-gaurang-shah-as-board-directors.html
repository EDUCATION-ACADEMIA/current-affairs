Which bank appointed KVS Manian, Gaurang Shah as board directors?

(A) IDBI Bank

(B) Yes bank

(C) ** Kotak mahindra Bank

(D) Dena Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The bank also announced the reappointment of KVS Manian as whole time director for a period of three years w.e.f. November 2022 or from the date of regulatory approvals, whichever is later. Kotak Mahindra Bank also accepted Gaurang Shah’s request to not continue as the Whole Time Director and Director of the bank.

Concept note-2: -Directors of Kotak Mahindra Prime Limited are Vyomesh Dineshchandra Kapasi, Shahrukh Homi Todiwala, Amit Mohan, Uday Suresh Kotak, Ravi Venkatraman, . He is the Managing Director & CEO of the Bank and also its promoter.
