Which city has topped the Mercer’s index of most liveable cities for the 10th year?

(A) Luxembourg

(B) ** Vienna

(C) London

(D) New York

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2022 Monocle Survey determined the world’s most livable city was Copenhagen, followed by Zurich, Lisbon and Helsinki.

Concept note-2: -Summing Up: Most Livable Cities in India In the 2022 list, Bangalore secured first position, followed by Pune, Ahmedabad, Chennai, Surat, Navi Mumbai, Coimbatore, Vadodara, Indore and Greater Mumbai.

Concept note-3: -For the third time in the past five years, Vienna captured the first spot in the 2022 Global Liveability Index, the annual report published by The Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU).

Concept note-4: -Delhi has been ranked the best liveable city in India, followed by Mumbai, Chennai, Ahmedabad, and Bengaluru.
