Which city is organizing the 2019 World Trade Organisation (WTO) ministerial meeting of developing countries?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Pune

(C) Raipur

(D) Bhopal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -WTO | Ministerial conferences - Twelfth WTO Ministerial Conference - Geneva Switzerland.

Concept note-2: -The First WTO Ministerial Conference was held in Singapore between 9 and 13 December 1996.

Concept note-3: -The WTO’s 12th Ministerial Conference was held in Geneva, Switzerland, from 12 to 17 June 2022. Initially scheduled to end on 15 June, the ministerial gathering was extended by two days to allow more time for negotiations and reaching agreements.

Concept note-4: -WTO members secure unprecedented package of trade outcomes at MC12. WTO members successfully concluded the 12th Ministerial Conference (MC12) in Geneva on 17 June, securing multilaterally negotiated outcomes on a series of key trade initiatives.

Concept note-5: -The last Ministerial Conference took place in Geneva, in June 2022.
