Which city is the venue of the 2019 Indian Panorama Film Festival (IPFF)?

(A) Lucknow

(B) Kolkata

(C) Pune

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -66th National Film Awards and Indian Panorama, 2019 Film Festival (February 12, 2020 – March 2, 2020), a 21 day film festival was inaugurated on February 12, 2020, Siri Fort Auditorium II, New Delhi, by Secretary I&B, Shri.

Concept note-2: -First IFFI The 1st edition of IFFI was organized by the Films Division, Government of India, with the patronage of the first Prime Minister of India. Held in Mumbai from 24 January to 1 February 1952, the Festival was subsequently taken to Madras, Delhi, Calcutta and Trivandrum.

Concept note-3: -The 51st International Film Festival of India was held from 16 to 24 January 2021 in Goa. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic the festival went hybrid, there was physical and virtual screening of 50 films out of 224 films across various categories.

Concept note-4: -The 53rd International Film Festival of India was an event held from 20 - 28 November 2022 with Alma & Oskar by Dieter Berner as the Opening feature film, and Perfect Number by Krzysztof Zanussi as the Closing feature film.
