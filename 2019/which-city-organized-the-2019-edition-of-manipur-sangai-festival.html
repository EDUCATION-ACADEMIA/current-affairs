Which city organized the 2019 edition of Manipur Sangai Festival?

(A) Chandel

(B) Kamjong

(C) ** Imphal

(D) Bishnupur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Unlike the previous editions of the festival, the 2019 edition of the Manipur Sangai Festival will be organized at different locations in Imphal, Bishnupur and Senapati District from 24th to 30th November 2019.

Concept note-2: -Labelled as the grandest festival in the state, the Manipur Sangai Festival helps in promoting Manipur as a world-class tourism destination. The festival is named after the state animal, Sangai, the brow-antlered deer found only in Manipur.

Concept note-3: -The 11th edition of the Sangai Festival 2022, a 10-day long annual cultural-cum-tourism festival, started on November 21.

Concept note-4: -Manipur Sangai Festival is being organized from November 21 to 30. What is Manipur Sangai Festival? Why is the festival celebrated? How is the 2022 edition of the Manipur Sangai Festival celebrated?

Concept note-5: -About sangai festival 2022 program list. - KHAUBOYS (rockyandmayur) November 21, 2022 Handloom and Handicrafts, Tug-of-War, Arambai Show, Mukuna competition, Yubi Lakpi, Sagol Lamjen, Tunnel Light, Half Marathon show, Rack Garden and more at Ibudhon Marjing, Heinhang, and Mao, Senapati.
