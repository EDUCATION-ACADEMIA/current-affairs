Which company launched first BS-VI motorcycle of India?

(A) Honda Motorbike

(B) Bajaj Auto

(C) ** Hero MotoCorp

(D) Royal Enfield

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hero MotoCorp today launched India’s first BS-VI compliant motorcycle – the new Splendor iSmart with a starting price of Rs 64,900 (ex-showroom, Delhi). Hero MotoCorp became the first two-wheeler manufacturer in the country to receive the BS-VI certificate from iCAT.

Concept note-2: -The Hero Splendour iSmart is India’s first BS6 bike in India. This bike which had been carburated for so many years now finally gets fuel injection.

Concept note-3: -The Type Approval Certificate was issued to the Company’s Splendor iSmart motorcycle that has been developed and manufactured indigenously by Hero MotoCorp. Last year ICAT issued the country’s first approval for BS-VI norms for Heavy Commercial Vehicle (HCV) segment also.

Concept note-4: -Royal Enfield Bullet was the first first two wheeler in India being introduced but it was used by the military at first.

Concept note-5: -19 February 2016: Hero Splendor iSmart is the first motorcycle to feature the iSmart technology. It is fitted with a 97.2cc four stroke engine that powers Splendor bikes since inception but as it houses iSmart technology it returns 102.5kmpl.
