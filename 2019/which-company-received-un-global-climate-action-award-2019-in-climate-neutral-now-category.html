Which company received UN global climate action award 2019 in ‘climate neutral now’ category?

(A) TCS

(B) Wipro

(C) ** Infosys

(D) CTS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Infosys won the award for its carbon neutral program, which delivers scalable, innovative and practical climate actions that address climate change and help drive progress on many other sustainable development goals, such as gender equality, health and well-being and economic opportunity.

Concept note-2: -Commending our efforts in climate action, the UN awarded Infosys with the prestigious United Nations Global Climate Action Award in the ‘Climate Neutral Now’ category.

Concept note-3: -UN Global Climate Action Awards: Climate Neutral Now Climate neutrality is a three step process, which requires individuals, companies and governments to: Measure their climate footprint; Reduce their emissions as much as possible; Offset what they cannot reduce with UN certified emission reductions.

Concept note-4: -Also, many commitments were made by countries, cities, businesses and international coalitions. For example, the Climate Ambitious Coalition contains now “73 countries committed to net zero emissions by 2050, as well as a further 1214 actors (regions, cities, businesses, investors) who have pledged the same goal".
