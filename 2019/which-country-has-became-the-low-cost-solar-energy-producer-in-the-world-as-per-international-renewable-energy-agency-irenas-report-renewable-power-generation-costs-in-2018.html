Which country has became the low cost solar energy producer in the world as per International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA)’s report “Renewable Power Generation Costs in 2018”?

(A) China

(B) ** India

(C) UK

(D) US

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -China leads the world as the top producer of solar energy, installing more than 48 GW of photovoltaic (PV) capacity in 2020. The EU, the United States, Vietnam, and Japan are ranked as top solar producers. A gigawatt (GW) is a unit of measurement of electrical power.

Concept note-2: -In the United States, Astronergy offers one of the cheapest solar panels in 2022 at $250 per panel or about $0.70/W. American company Heliene also has a $250 panel, which works out to about $0.80/W.
