Which country has been named as the host of 2019 Africa Cup of Nations?

(A) Cameroon

(B) ** Egypt

(C) Morocco

(D) Ivory Coast

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The match was held at the Cairo International Stadium in Cairo, Egypt, on 19 July 2019 and was contested by Senegal and Algeria. Algeria won the final 1–0 for their second Africa Cup of Nations title, and their first since 1990.

Concept note-2: -The 2021 Africa Cup of Nations tournament, hosted by Cameroon, and was postponed to 2022 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Concept note-3: -The last time Nigeria co-hosted the AFCON was 22 years ago - alongside Ghana. The Super Eagles lost the final of that tournament on penalties to Cameroon. Prior to that tournament, the country had hosted the 1980 AFCON, defeating Algeria in the final.

Concept note-4: -The last AFCON competition was hosted by Egypt in 2019 and it was Algeria that lifted the title.
