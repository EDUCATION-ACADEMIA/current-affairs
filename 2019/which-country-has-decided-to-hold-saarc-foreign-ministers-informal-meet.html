Which country has decided to hold SAARC Foreign Ministers’ informal meet?

(A) ** Nepal

(B) Bhutan

(C) Bangladesh

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Secretariat: Kathmandu (Nepal).

Concept note-2: -The last SAARC summit, which is supposed to be a biennial event, was hosted by Nepal in 2014.

Concept note-3: -The first summit was held in Dhaka, Bangladesh on 7–8 December, 1985.

Concept note-4: -The 19th SAARC summit was a scheduled diplomatic conference which was originally planned to be held in Islamabad, Pakistan, on 15–19 November 2016, but got cancelled after an attack on an Indian army camp in Kashmir.
