Which country has successfully launched second generation data relay Tianlian II-01 satellite?

(A) ** China

(B) Japan

(C) South Korea

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tianlian (Simplified Chinese: , Traditional Chinese: , English: Sky Link) also known as CTDRS, is a Chinese data relay communication satellite constellation. The constellation serves to relay data from ground stations to spacecraft and rockets, most significantly China’s crewed spaceflight program.

Concept note-2: -China sends second Shiyan-10 test satellite sent into orbit with its final launch of 2022. HELSINKI - China has conducted its 64th and final launch of the year, sending the Shiyan-10 (02) into orbit from Xichang spaceport.

Concept note-3: -Sun Jiadong was responsible for the Dongfanghong 1 technology. In 1967, Dang Hongxin chose a copper antenna membrane that resolved the difficulties of broadcasting on an ultra-short wave antenna between 100 °C and −100 °C. Engineers installed a music player playing “The East is Red” on the satellite.
