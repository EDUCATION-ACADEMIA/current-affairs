Which country is organizing the 34th edition of Aahar 2019 - the International Food and Hospitality Fair?

(A) Nepal

(B) Japan

(C) ** India

(D) South Korea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India Trade Promotion Organisation (ITPO) is organizing its annual flagship B2B expo, “AAHAR-The International Food and Hospitality Fair", at Pragati Maidan (New Delhi) in April 2022.

Concept note-2: -“AAHAR – The International Food & Hospitality Fair” is one of Asia’s best known brands in Food & Hospitality shows & is in its 33rd year. It is organised by India Trade Promotion Organisation (the premier trade promotion body of the Government of India) in New Delhi, India.

Concept note-3: -AAHAR – The International Food & Hospitality Fair (B2B) is a major event organized by India Trade Promotion Organisation, the country’s leading trade promotion organization. The 36th edition will be held in New Delhi at Pragati Maidan.
