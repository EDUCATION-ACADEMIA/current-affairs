Which country is planning to build its first space-based solar power station, orbiting 36, 000 kilometres above ground?

(A) France

(B) Russia

(C) ** China

(D) USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -China, as a part of its ambitions project, is eyeing to come up with a solar power plant in space by 2028. The target year for launching a solar power plant in space is two years ahead of its scheduled time.

Concept note-2: -The energy can be used to power the moving satellites in orbit. By India Today Web Desk: After successfully returning samples from the Moon, landing, and roving on Mars in their first attempt, China is set to launch the first solar-powered plant in space.

Concept note-3: -Bhadla Solar Park is the largest solar park in the world as of 2022 and is spread over a total area of 5,700 hectares (14,000 acres) in Bhadla, Phalodi tehsil, Jodhpur district, Rajasthan, India.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Rajasthan. India’s first solar power plant was developed by Godawari Green Energy Limited in Rajasthan. The 50-MW solar thermal power plant was set up in Naukh village located in the Jaisalmer district of Rajasthan.
