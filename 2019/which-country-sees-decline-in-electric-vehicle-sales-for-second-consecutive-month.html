Which country Sees Decline In Electric Vehicle Sales For Second Consecutive Month?

(A) Denmark

(B) ** China

(C) Indonesia

(D) USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Norway, Iceland and Sweden also rank the highest when it comes to having the most EVs on the road per capita.

Concept note-2: -Revenue in the Electric Vehicles market is projected to reach US$457.60bn in 2023. Revenue is expected to show an annual growth rate (CAGR 2023-2027) of 17.02%, resulting in a projected market volume of US$858.00bn by 2027. Electric Vehicles market unit sales are expected to reach 16,206.9K vehicles in 2027.

Concept note-3: -In comparison to 2021, there was a 141 per cent increase in EV sales in 2022. Just over 10 lakh EVs were sold in the country in 2022, making 4 per cent of total vehicles sold during the year.

Concept note-4: -Making electric cars creates more emissions The raw materials for making the car have to be mined, and the process of mining creates a lot of greenhouse gases. Then the raw materials have to be refined before they can be used, which again emits more greenhouse gas.
