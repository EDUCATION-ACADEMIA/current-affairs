Which country topped in the FIFA World Rankings?

(A) England

(B) ** Belgium

(C) Spain

(D) Denmark

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Brazil extended its lead over second-placed Belgium in the latest FIFA rankings released on 27 October 2023 to take the field as the top-ranked team at this year’s World Cup in Qatar, starting 20 November 2022.

Concept note-2: -The FIFA Men’s World Ranking is a ranking system for men’s national teams in association football, led by Brazil as of 2022.

Concept note-3: -If we talk about the world ranking under FIFA Football Ranking 2022, then Brazil is at the top and after Brazil, Belgium, Argentina, France, England, Spain, Italy, Netherlands, Portugal and Denmark are making their own rankings.

Concept note-4: -While Brazil and Argentina, the World Cup 2022 winners, are first and second, respectively. The Blue Tigers have been ranked 106th for quite sometime now.
