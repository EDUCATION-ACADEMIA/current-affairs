Which Country topped the medals tally in the ISSF Junior World Cup 2019?

(A) China

(B) ** India

(C) Canada

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India emerged as the most successful nation in the series, topping medal table in all Rifle-Pistol World Cups and Finals.

Concept note-2: -ISSF Rifle/Pistol World Championship 2022: India finish second with 34 medals - get winners list.

Concept note-3: -In the ISSF Shooting World Cup, India topped the medal tally at Changwon in South Korea. India finished the tournament with eight medals including three golds, four silvers and one bronze and was way ahead of hosts Korea and Serbia in the medal tally.

Concept note-4: -Indian shooters concluded their campaign on top with a total of 33 medals at the ISSF Junior World Cup 2022 in Suhl, Germany this week. India’s tally of 33 medals - 13 golds, 15 silvers and five bronzes - was the most by any country at the 2022 edition.

Concept note-5: -Which country topped the medal tally in the ISSF World Cup 2022? Notes: India topped the medal tally in the ISSF World Cup 2022 in Cairo, Egypt with a total of seven, four gold, two silver and one bronze, medals. Norway was at the second place India with six medals.
