Which country tops in the global energy transition index?

(A) Italy

(B) Switzerland

(C) Norway

(D) ** Sweden

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Sweden. Sweden is the top-ranking country in the World Economic Forum’s 74th Global Energy Transition Index.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is 87th. India has been ranked at the 87th position among 115 countries in the Energy Transition Index (ETI) that tracks nations on the current performance of their energy systems across various aspects, according to a World Economic Forum (WEF) report released.

Concept note-3: -The Global Energy Transition Index (ETI) is published by World Economic Forum (WEF) to quantify the global change to renewable energy. It provides a numerical value for how well a country is doing in their transition to renewable energy and also helps in developing energy transition roadmaps.

Concept note-4: -India ranked 87 out of 110 countries in the Global Energy Transition Index (ETI), a benchmark by the World Economic Forum.

Concept note-5: -The World Economic Forum’s Energy Transitions Index (ETI) is one example. The ETI measures countries’ energy system performance and transition readiness.
