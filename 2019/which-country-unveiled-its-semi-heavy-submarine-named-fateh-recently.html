Which country unveiled its semi-heavy submarine named "Fateh" recently?

(A) ** Iran

(B) Israel

(C) Russia

(D) Brazil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The new submarine, named Fateh, is Iran’s first in the semi-heavy category.

Concept note-2: -Named the Fateh, the new submarine is Iran’s first in the semi-heavy category. The near 600-tonne underwater vessel is equipped with torpedoes and naval mines in addition to cruise missiles.

Concept note-3: -INS Arihant is India’s first indigenous nuclear powered ballistic missile capable submarine.

Concept note-4: -SLBMs have been developed by seven other countries, including the United States, Russia, China, Britain, France, India, and North Korea. All of those countries also have arsenals of nuclear weapons, which have typically been used to arm SLBMs.

Concept note-5: -INS Arihant carried out a successful launch of a Submarine Launched Ballistic Missile (SLBM) on October 14, 2022. The missile was tested to a predetermined range and impacted the target area in the Bay of Bengal with very high accuracy.
