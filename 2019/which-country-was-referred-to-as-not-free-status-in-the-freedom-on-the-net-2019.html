Which country was referred to as ‘Not Free’ status in the Freedom on the Net-2019?

(A) ** China

(B) India

(C) Vietnam

(D) Iceland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Iceland. In Iceland, internet access is widespread. There are no digital divides or differences in penetration rates across geography, gender, or education levels. The country boasts very few restrictions to online content, with zero public records of government takedown requests during the reporting period.

Concept note-2: -Russia was rated “partly free” in Freedom on the Net by Freedom House in 2009 (score 49), 2011 (score 52), 2012 (score 52), 2013 (score 54), and 2014 (score 60) and as “not free” in 2015 (score 62), 2016 (score 65), 2017 (score 66), and 2018 (score 67) where scores range from 0 (most free) to 100 (least free).
