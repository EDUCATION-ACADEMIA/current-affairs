Which Country will host the 2020 ICC Women’s T20 World Cup?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) India

(C) NewZealand

(D) ** Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Momentum builds up for ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2023 in South Africa.

Concept note-2: -The 2023 ICC Under-19 Women’s T20 World Cup was the first edition of the ICC Women’s Under-19 Cricket World Cup, hosted by South Africa in 2023. The tournament was moved from its original slot at the end of 2021 to January 2023 because of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Concept note-3: -ICC World Cup 2023 host will be the 13th edition of the men’s Cricket World Cup inthe country India and This is the first time that the entire tournament of ICC World Cup 2023 host will be held in India.

Concept note-4: -The 2023 ICC Women’s T20 World Cup is the 8th edition of the tournament. This time the showpiece event is being played in South Africa.
