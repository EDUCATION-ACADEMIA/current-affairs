Which country’s parliament has recently passed the Uighur bill?

(A) Canada

(B) ** USA

(C) England

(D) South Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -6256) is a United States federal law that would change U.S. policy on China’s Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region (XUAR, or Xinjiang) with the goal of ensuring that American entities are not funding forced labor among ethnic minorities in the region.

Concept note-2: -“The Uyghur Policy Act equips the U.S. with tools needed to support the basic human rights and distinct identities of Uyghurs and other ethnic minorities in the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region subject to the Chinese government’s inhumane treatment.

Concept note-3: -More than one million Uighur and other Muslim minorities are forcibly held in mass detention camps in the Xinjian province where they face countless human rights abuses from forced labour, coerced sterilisation, and destruction of their culture and religious identity.

Concept note-4: -Uyghurs have suffered from state sponsored discrimination for decades in terms of restrictions on religious freedom, language rights, cultural rights and freedom of movement.

Concept note-5: -Over 1 million people, mainly Uyghurs, have been arbitrarily detained in “re-education” or “de-extremification” facilities. There are reports of widespread rape, sexual abuse and torture of ethnic minorities in these facilities.
