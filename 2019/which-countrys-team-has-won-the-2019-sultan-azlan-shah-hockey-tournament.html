Which country’s team has won the 2019 Sultan Azlan Shah Hockey tournament?

(A) Poland

(B) ** South Korea

(C) India

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -South Korea won the tournament for the third time after defeating India 4–2 on penalties, after the match ended in a draw at 1–1, in the final.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is option 1 i.e Australia. Australia has won the 27th Sultan Azlan Shah Cup.

Concept note-3: -All matches will be played at the Azlan Shah Stadium in the city of Ipoh in Malaysia, which is the permanent venue of the Sultan Azlan Shah Cup. The Sultan Azlan Shah Cup was first played in 1983. It became an annual event in 2003.

Concept note-4: -Australia has won Sultan Azlan Shah Hockey Cup titles with 10th times & India have won the title 5th times with 1 co-champion South Korea in 2010. Previous champions 2019 Sultan Azlan Shah Hockey Cup Tournament Winner South Korea beats 5-times champions India 4 - 2 on penalties in the final match.
