Which edition of the India International Trade Fair has begun in Delhi?

(A) 27th

(B) ** 39th

(C) 43rd

(D) 32nd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 41st edition of the India International Trade Fair (IITF) has started at Pragati Maidan in New Delhi today. Commerce and Industry Minister Piyush Goyal inaugurated the fair.

Concept note-2: -Since its inception in 1980, the Trade Fair Delhi has become a major global event for global business communities, clients, and tourists. Every year, it attracts millions of visitors and thousands of exhibitors.

Concept note-3: -Union Minister Piyush Goyal, Ministry of Commerce and Industry (MoCI), inaugurated the 41st edition of the India International Trade Fair (IITF) at Pragati Maidan in New Delhi, Delhi, on November 14, 2022.

Concept note-4: -14th Nov, 2022 to 27th Nov, 2022 at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi 110001 India, New Delhi, India IITF - India International Trade Fair 2019 is being a multi-product exhibition has on display a wide range of products and services.

Concept note-5: -India Trade Promotion Organisation will organize the 26th edition of Delhi Book Fair (in Halls 3 and 4) and the 22st Stationery Fair/6th Office Automation and 6th Corporate Gift Fair (in Halls 3 and 4) during 22-26 December, 2022 at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi.
