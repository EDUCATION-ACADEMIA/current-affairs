Which edition of the Nagaland Hornbill Festival recently began at Kisama?

(A) 18th

(B) 19th

(C) ** 20th

(D) 21st

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -23rd Hornbill Festival 2022 begins at Naga Heritage Village Kisama in Nagaland. Nagaland’s famous Hornbill Festival is scheduled from 1st December to 10th December 2022. Vice President Jagdeep Dhankhar graced the inaugural function of the festival as the Chief Guest.

Concept note-2: -10 (EMN): The 23rd edition of Hornbill Festival 2022 came to a close on Saturday after 10 days of activities with all the cultural troupes that participated in this year’s edition taking part in unity dance around a bonfire here at the Naga Heritage Village, Kisama.

Concept note-3: -The 23rd Hornbill Festival, which began at the Naga Heritage Village Kisama in Nagaland on December 1, has been entertaining and enrapturing audiences for roughly a week now.

Concept note-4: -VP Jagdeep Dhankhar to inaugurate 23rd edition of Hornbill Festival of Nagaland. The much awaited 23rd edition of the Hornbill Festival of Nagaland will start today, December 1, at Naga Heritage Village, Kisama.

Concept note-5: -Govt. of Nagaland organizes Hornbill Festival every year to encourage inter-tribal interaction and to promote cultural heritage of Nagaland.
