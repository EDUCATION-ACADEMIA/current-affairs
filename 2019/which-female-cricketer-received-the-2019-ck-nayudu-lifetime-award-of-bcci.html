Which female cricketer received the 2019 CK Nayudu Lifetime Award of BCCI?

(A) ** Anjum Chopra

(B) Jhulan Goswami

(C) Mithali Raj

(D) HamandeepKaur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Former India women’s captain Anjum Chopra is the co-recipient of the lifetime achievement award for the year 2019.

Concept note-2: -K Srikanth won the BCCI CK Nayudu Lifetime Achievement Award for the year 2019. Krishnamachari Srikkanth is a former Indian captain of the Indian Cricket Team. He represented India between 1981 and 1992. He has played 43 Tests, scoring 2062 runs with two hundred and 12 half-centuries.

Concept note-3: -Rangaswamy 1st woman to get BCCI lifetime achievement award.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. Shantha Rangaswamy, former Indian women’s cricket team captain, received the first ‘CK Nayudu lifetime achievement award’ by BCCI in 2017. India had recorded its first-ever Test win in November 1976 against West Indies under Shantha’s captaincy at Moin-ul-Haq stadium in Patna.

Concept note-5: -New Delhi, 27 November 2022: At the TURF2022 & India Sports Awards of FICCI, former Ranji cricketer Sarkar Talwar was honoured with the Lifetime Achievement of the Year award on Saturday.
