Which film won the Best Film award in the 20th edition of NEXA’s IIFA Awards for 2019?

(A) Padmaavat

(B) ** Raazi

(C) Andhadhun

(D) Sanju

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Andhadhun won 6 awards, including Best Director (for Sriram Raghavan), thus becoming the most-awarded film at the ceremony.

Concept note-2: -Uttarakhand CM congratulates Jubin Nautiyal for winning ‘Best Playback Singer Male’ at IIFA 2022.

Concept note-3: -The 22nd IIFA Award ceremony was held in Abu Dhabi, UAE. Actor Sidharth Malhotra led the ceremony and his film Shershah got 21 nominations and became the most awarded film at IIFA 2022, winning in six categories, including best film and best director.
