Which hockey player has been nominated for the 2019 Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award?

(A) Chinglensana Singh Kangujam

(B) ** P R Sreejesh

(C) Akashdeep Singh

(D) Romesh Pathania

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hockey India on Saturday announced the nomination of Indian Men’s team goalkeeper PR Sreejesh and former Women’s team player Deepika for the prestigious Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award while Harmanpreet Singh, Vandana Katariya and Navjot Kaur have been nominated for the Arjuna Award .

Concept note-2: -The Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award 2019 was awarded to Bajrang Punia for wrestling and Deepa Malik for athletics.

Concept note-3: -Rani Rampal is an Indian Hockey player. She was born on 4th December 1994 in Shahbad Markanda Village in Kurukshetra district in Haryana. She conferred with the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna 2020 and the first woman hockey player to get this award. She trained under Baldev Singh former recipient of the Dronacharya Award.
