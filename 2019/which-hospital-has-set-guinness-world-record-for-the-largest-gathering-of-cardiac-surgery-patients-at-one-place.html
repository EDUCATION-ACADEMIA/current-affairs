Which hospital has set Guinness World Record for the largest gathering of cardiac surgery patients at one place?

(A) ** Ramesh Hospitals, Vijayawada

(B) Star Hospitals, Hyderabad

(C) Max Healthcare, Gurgaon

(D) Fortis Escorts Hospital, Jaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -City’s Ramesh Hospitals has set a Guinness World Record by having 1,020 cardiac surgery patients at once at a function hall near the city on Thursday as part of an initiative to create awareness and give hope to people suffering from heart ailments.

Concept note-2: -The heart institute of Apollo Hospital is one of the biggest groups in cardiology. This hospital is famous for housing one of the best heart surgeons in the world for cardiothoracic surgery. Well, the scorecard of this hospital shows an unmatched record of more than 152,000 surgeries.
