Which IIT institute topped in the latest QS(Quacquarelli Symonds) Graduate Employability Rankings 2020?

(A) IIT Madras

(B) IIT Mumbai

(C) IIT Kanpur

(D) ** IIT Bombay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IIT-Bombay bagged the first position among the Indian institutes in the 2020 edition of the London-based Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) World Graduate Employability Rankings.

Concept note-2: -IIT Bombay Tops Among Indian Universities in QS Graduate Employability Rankings 2022. Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) has moved up in the range from 111-120 last rankings year (2020) to 101-110 this year in the Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) Graduate Employability Rankings 2022.

Concept note-3: -The country’s Universities that have ranked the highest are: Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Bombay – 177th Rank. Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Delhi – 185th Rank.
