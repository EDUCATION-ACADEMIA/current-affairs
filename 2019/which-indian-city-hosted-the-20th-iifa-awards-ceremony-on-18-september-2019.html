Which Indian city hosted the 20th IIFA awards ceremony on 18 September 2019?

(A) Kolkata

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Hyderabad

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 20th IIFA Awards (International Indian Film Academy Awards) were held on 18 September 2019. For the first time in the Awards’ history, the awards were held in India, Mumbai. The nominees were announced on 2019.

Concept note-2: -IIFA 2023 Hosts The International India Film Academy Awards (IIFA) 2023 will be hosted by Bollywood actor Farhaan Akhtar and Abhishek Bachchan, along with actor-TV presenter Manish Paul.

Concept note-3: -The post reads, “The biggest celebration of Indian cinema, the International Indian Film Academy and Awards (IIFA) is rescheduled for its 23rd edition in Yas Island, Abu Dhabi, and will NOW be held on the 26th and 27th of May, 2023.” The two-day IIFA awards will now take place on May 26 and May 27, 2023.

Concept note-4: -The Awards, which gave Indian cinema a platform on a global scale, were held on June 3 and 4, 2022, at Etihad Arena. -On June 25, 2022, at 8:00 p.m. IST, Colors Tv will broadcast the star-studded event.
