(A) Sujatha Singh

(B) Nirupama Rao

(C) ** Shyam Saran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Former foreign secretary Shyam Saran was conferred with Japan’s second-highest national award called ‘The Order of the Rising Sun, Gold and Silver Star’ for his contributions to strengthening the strategic ties and enhancing mutual understanding between India and Japan.
