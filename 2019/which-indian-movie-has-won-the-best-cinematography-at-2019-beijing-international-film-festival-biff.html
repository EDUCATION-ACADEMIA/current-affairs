Which Indian movie has won the best cinematography at 2019 Beijing International Film Festival (BIFF)?

(A) Kayamkulam Kochunni

(B) Odiyan

(C) ** Bhayanakam

(D) Eeda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following Indian Movie won best cinematography at 2019 Beijing International Film Festival? Malayalam film Bhayanakam (Fear) directed by Jayaraj won the best cinematography award at the Beijing International Film Festival. The cinematography was done by Nikihil S Praveen.

Concept note-2: -The 50th International Film Festival of India, 2019 announces the selection of Indian Panorama. Indian Panorama is a flagship section of IFFI, which showcases the best of contemporary Indian Feature and Non-Feature Films of the years. The Feature Film Jury has selected 21 Feature films from the received entries .

Concept note-3: -66th National Film Awards and Indian Panorama, 2019 Film Festival (February 12, 2020 – March 2, 2020), a 21 day film festival was inaugurated on February 12, 2020, Siri Fort Auditorium II, New Delhi, by Secretary I&B, Shri.

Concept note-4: -Main idea of a film festival is not to present a single vision of cinema, but to bring a variety of options, said Nadav Lapid, Israeli director, writer and Chairperson of International Competition Jury at the 53rd International Film Festival of India.
