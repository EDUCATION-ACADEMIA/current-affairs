Which Indian pavilion has won the ’Exhibitor of the Year Award’ at 106th Indian Science Congress (ISC-2019)?

(A) ** DRDO

(B) ISRO

(C) BARC

(D) VSSC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) pavilion received ‘Exhibitor of the Year Award’ at 106th Indian Science Congress (ISC) held at Lovely Professional University (LPU) in Punjab.

Concept note-2: -The 106th Indian Science Congress (ISC-2019) will be held at Lovely Professional University (LPU) in Jalandhar, Punjab from 3rd to 7th January 2019.

Concept note-3: -Indian Science Congress Association(ISCA) is a premier scientific organisation of India with headquarters at Kolkata, West Bengal. The association started in the year 1914 in Kolkata and it meets annually in the first week of January.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated the 107th Indian Science Congress (ISC) on January 3 at the University of Agricultural Sciences, Bengaluru; The main focus of the latest edition of the prestigious science event was “rural development”

Concept note-5: -The 107 Indian Science Congress 2022 was held at the University of Agricultural Sciences, GKVK Campus, Bangalore, Karnataka in January 2022.
