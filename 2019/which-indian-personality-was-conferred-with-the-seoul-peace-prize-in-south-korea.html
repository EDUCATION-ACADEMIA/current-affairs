(A) Smt Sushma Swaraj

(B) Shri Venkaiah Naidu

(C) Shri Ram Nath Kovind

(D) ** Shri Narendra Modi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year’s prestigious award was given to Ales Bialiatski, the Memorial and the Center for Civil Liberties.

Concept note-2: -The Seoul Peace Prize Cultural Foundation, headed by Yeom Jae-ho, made the announcement on Wednesday.

Concept note-3: -The Seoul Peace Prize was established in 1990 to commemorate the success of the 24th Olympic Games held in Seoul. It has its genesis in the Korean people’s yearning for peace in the Korean peninsula.
