Which Indian Singer has been honoured with ‘Magnificent Performing Arts Award’ at the 21st Century Icon Awards in London, United Kingdom (UK)?

(A) Arijit Singh

(B) ** Sonu Nigam

(C) Shreya Ghoshal

(D) Kumar Sanu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Sonu Nigam.

Concept note-2: -Sonu Nigam has won 10 awards - Filmfare Award in 2003, Filmfare Award in 2004, Filmfare Award - Kannada in 2008, Filmfare Award - Kannada in 2009, IIFA in 2002, IIFA in 2004, IIFA in 2013, Mirchi Music Award in 2021, Mirchi Music Award in 2021 and National Award in 2003.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which Indian singer has won the Magnificent Performing Arts Award at 21st Century Icon Awards in London? Notes: Popular Bollywood playback singer Sonu Nigam has been honoured with the Magnificent Performing Arts Award at the annual 21st Century Icon Awards in London.

Concept note-4: -She has been honored by the US state of Ohio, where the governor Ted Strickland declared 26 June 2010 as “Shreya Ghoshal Day". In April 2013, she was awarded with the highest honour in London by the selected members of House of Commons of the United Kingdom.
