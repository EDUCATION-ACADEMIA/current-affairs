Which institute has been ranked as the ’Best Educational Institution’ in the 2019 National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF)?

(A) IIT-Bombay

(B) ** IIT-Madras

(C) IIT-Roorkee

(D) IIT-Kharagpur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is IIT Madras.

Concept note-2: -Therefore, IIT, Madras secured the first rank in the overall category of NIRF ranking 2019. GSET result are out to download from 21st January 2023. GSET Answer Key released on 7th November 2022. This is the provisional answer key, and any objections against the same can be submitted till 19th November 2022.

Concept note-3: -In the overall university ranking, the University of Delhi is ranked at the thirteenth position. NIRF ranking 2019 was announced by the President Ram Nath Kovind at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi on Monday (April 8). Hindu College’s ranking has jumped to 2nd from 4th position.

Concept note-4: -NIRF. National Assessment and Accreditation Council, (NAAC) was founded by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of India in the year 1994 for assessing and giving accreditation to the Institutions for Higher Education in the country.
