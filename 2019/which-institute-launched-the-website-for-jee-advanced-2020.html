Which institute launched the website for JEE Advanced 2020?

(A) ** IIT Delhi

(B) IIT Roorkee

(C) IIT Hyderabad

(D) IIT Madras

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IIT Delhi has launched the website for JEE (Advanced) 2020 examination. The website was launched by Chairman, JAB-2020, Prof. V Ramgopal Rao (Director, IIT Delhi). IIT Delhi is the organising institute for JEE (Advanced) 2020.

Concept note-2: -However, IIT Guwahati is in charge of conducting the JEE Advanced in 2023. Also, the revised JEE Advanced 2023 syllabus is out.

Concept note-3: -The National Testing Agency (NTA) organises JEE (Main) while one of the IITs organises JEE (Advanced) under the guidance of IIT JAB. In 2019, IIT Roorkee was the organising institute for JEE (Advanced) in which a total number of 161,319 candidates had appeared in both papers 1 and 2.

Concept note-4: -The JEE Mains Phase I examination will be conducted by the JEE Apex board from June 20 to 29, 2022. At the same time, the session 2 exams will be held from 21 to 30 July 2022. JEE Advanced exam will be conducted on 28th August 2022.
