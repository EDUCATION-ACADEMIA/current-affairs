Which international organisation is organising the 4th Global Digital Health Partnership Summit 2019?

(A) ** WHO

(B) FAO

(C) UNESCO

(D) UNICEF

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ‘4th Global Digital Health Partnership Summit’ was inaugurated in New Delhi on 25 February 2019. It was hosted by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare in collaboration with the World Health Organisation and the Global Digital Health Partnership.

Concept note-2: -The 4th Global Digital Health Partnership Summit being held at New Delhi was inaugurated by the Union Health Minister J P Nadda in the presence of Shri Ravi Shankar Prasad Union Minister of Law & Justice and Electronics & Information Technology.
