Which is the first Indian state to issue the highest number of golden cards under Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY)?

(A) Odisha

(B) West Bengal

(C) ** Jammu and Kashmir

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which state has become the first Indian state to issue highest number of golden cards under Ayushman Bharat scheme? Notes: Jammu and Kashmir (J&K) has become the first state in India to issue highest number of golden cards under Ayushman Bharat-Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY).

Concept note-2: -Ayushman Bharat Golden Card is a card through which poor people of the country can get their treatment for up to 5 lakh rupees in government and private hospitals for free.

Concept note-3: -States/UTs Covered Under the Ayushman Bharat Scheme A total of 33 states/UTs in India have implemented the PMJAY scheme. The scheme is completely government-funded, and the costs are shared among the Central and State governments as per the ratio decided by the Ministry of Finance.

Concept note-4: -This synergy between Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB PM-JAY)and Employees State Insurance Scheme (ESIS) will create an ecosystem wherein ESIC beneficiaries will be able to access services at ABPM-JAY empanelled hospitals and vice versa.

Concept note-5: -Ayushman Bharat –Pradhan Mantri Jan AarogyaYojana (AB-PMJAY) to be launched by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi in Ranchi, Jharkahnd on September 23, 2018.
