Which is the only country having hypersonic weapons?

(A) ** Russia

(B) India

(C) USA

(D) Israel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -France, Australia, India, Germany, Japan, South Korea and North Korea and Iran also have hypersonic weapon research programs. Australia and the US have begun joint development of air-launched hypersonic missiles, as announced by a Pentagon statement on 30 November 2020.

Concept note-2: -Russia has deployed its own hypersonic Kinzhal missiles in Ukraine, marking perhaps the first time such weapons have been used in war. And during a test last year, a Chinese hypersonic missile flew around the world before hitting its target. “China and Russia are the driver,” said Wolfe.

Concept note-3: -Who has hypersonic weapons? China, the U.S., and Russia have the most advanced capabilities, and several other countries are investigating the technology, including India, Japan, Australia, France, Germany and North Korea, which claims to have tested a hypersonic missile.

Concept note-4: -Russia & China are the only two countries that possess them. Department of Defense US feels Washington is lagging behind. What are hypersonic missiles?
