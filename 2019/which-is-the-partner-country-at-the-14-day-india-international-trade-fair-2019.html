Which is the partner country at the 14-day India International Trade Fair 2019?

(A) Nepal

(B) ** Afghanistan

(C) Kazakhstan

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In its latest edition, the 14-day long fair is being attended by several countries, including Australia, Iran, UK, Vietnam, Bahrain, Bangladesh, Bhutan, China, Egypt, Hong Kong and Indonesia. This year, Afghanistan is the partner country while Bihar and Jharkhand were focus states.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The 39th edition of India International Trade Fair (IITF-2019) has started at Pragati Maidan in New Delhi with theme ‘Ease of Doing Business’. In the 14-day Trade Fair, the status of ‘Partner Country’ has been accorded to Afghanistan and South Korea is the Focus Country.

Concept note-3: -Some 12 countries including Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Bahrain, Belarus, Iran, Nepal, Thailand, Turkey, UAE, and the UK will participate in the fair.

Concept note-4: -International Trade Fair 2022: Check Ticket Prices, Entry Timings, Traffic Advisory, Other Details Here. Foreign countries are also participating in the trade fair. Countries like Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Bahrain, Belarus, Nepal, Thailand, Iran, Turkey, UAE and UK are actively participating in the event.
