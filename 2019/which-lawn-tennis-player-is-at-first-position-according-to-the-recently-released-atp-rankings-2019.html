Which Lawn Tennis player is at first position according to the recently released ATP Rankings-2019?

(A) Roger Federer.

(B) Novak Djokovic

(C) ** Rafael Nadal

(D) Rod Laver.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which Lawn Tennis player is at first position according to the recently released ATP Rankings-2019? Rod Laver.

Concept note-2: -Novak Djokovic, the current men’s singles world No. 1.

Concept note-3: -Djokovic, who has spent a record 373 weeks as world No. 1, returns to the top spot for the first time since last June.

Concept note-4: -#2. Federer revolutionized the sport and holds the record for the longest consecutive reign as the world number 1 tennis player for 237 weeks from Feb 2, 2004 to Aug 17, 2008. He retired in 2022 with a record of being the world’s number 1 tennis player for a total of 310 weeks, according to the ATP ranking.
