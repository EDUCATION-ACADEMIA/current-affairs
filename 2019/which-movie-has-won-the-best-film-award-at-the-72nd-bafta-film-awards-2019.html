Which movie has won the best film award at the 72nd Bafta Film Awards 2019?

(A) The Favourite

(B) A Star Is Born

(C) ** Roma

(D) Bohemian Rhapsody

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The epic science fiction film Dune received the most nominations with eleven; The Power of the Dog and Belfast followed with eight and six, respectively. The former ultimately won two-Best Film and Best Director (Jane Campion); Dune received the most wins, with five, but did not take home any major category awards.

Concept note-2: -A.R. Rehman is the first Indian won the BAFTA film award. Rahman received awards for best film song and best soundtrack, both compositions for the Oscar - winning film Slumdog Millionaire. Concept note-3: -BAFTA Awards 2022 Winners: The Power of the Dog Takes Best Picture, Director. Big moments for Paul Thomas Anderson, CODA, and more tease shake-ups in the Oscar race, but the front-runner is still holding strong.
