Which novel has been adjudged as the best novel of 2017 at the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Awards?

(A) Naalukettu

(B) Aarachaar

(C) Thakshankunnu Swaroopam

(D) ** Nireeswaran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -James’s Nireeswaran adjudged best novel of 2017. The Kerala Sahitya Akademi Awards for 2017 have been announced. Nireeswaran by V.J. James has been selected the best novel.

Concept note-2: -The Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award for Novel is an award given every year by the Kerala Sahitya Akademi (Kerala Literary Academy) to Malayalam writers for writing a novel of literary merit. It is one of the twelve categories of the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award.

Concept note-3: -The Sahitya Akademi Award first winner was R.K. Narayan, who was given the award for his novel ‘The Guide’ in 1960. Who runs the Sahitya Akademi?
