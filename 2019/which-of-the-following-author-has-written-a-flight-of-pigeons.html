Which of the following author has written ‘A Flight of Pigeons’?

(A) William Wordsworth

(B) ** Ruskin Bond

(C) Leo Tolstoy

(D) William Shakespeare

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Flight of Pigeons is a novella by Indian author Ruskin Bond .

Concept note-2: -Set in Shahjahanpur during the revolt of 1857, A Flight of Pigeons is Ruskin Bond’s classic novella about the twists of fate, history and the human heart.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Ruskin Bond.

Concept note-4: -The story begins with the death of Ruth’s father in front of her own eyes and the burning of their house. They take shelter in Lala Ramjilal’s house for few weeks after which they were forcefully taken away by a Pathan leader named Javed Khan to his own house after having spared their lives.

Concept note-5: -In 1977/78 film-maker Shyam Benegal wrote to Ruskin Bond about the latter’s novella ‘A Flight of Pigeons’ that had been recommended to him by Ismat Chugtai. And by 1979, Benegal had made ‘Junoon’, based on the book, starring Shashi Kapoor, Shabana Azmi, Jennifer Kendal and Naseeruddin Shah.
