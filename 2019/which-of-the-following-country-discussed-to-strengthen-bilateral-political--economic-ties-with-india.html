Concept note-2: -Today, India and Japan share a global vision of peace, stability and shared prosperity, based on sustainable development. Concept note-3: -Japan’s bilateral trade with India, totaled US$ 17.63 billion in FY 2018-19. Exports from Japan to India during this period were US$ 12.77 billion and imports were US$ 4.86 billion.
