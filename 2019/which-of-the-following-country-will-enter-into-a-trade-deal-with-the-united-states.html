(A) Taiwan

(B) Turkey

(C) ** China

(D) Brazil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United States has free trade agreements in force with 20 countries. These are: Australia. Bahrain.

Concept note-2: -The United States has agreements in force with 20 countries: Australia, Bahrain, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Dominican Republic, El Salvador, Guatemala, Honduras, Israel, Jordan, Mexico, Morocco, Nicaragua, Oman, Panama, Peru, Singapore, and South Korea.

Concept note-3: -The United States has free trade agreements (FTAs) in effect with 20 countries. These FTAs build on the foundation of the WTO Agreement, with more comprehensive and stronger disciplines than the WTO Agreement. Many of our FTAs are bilateral agreements between two governments.

Concept note-4: -The United States is party to 14 Free Trade Agreements (FTAs) with 20 countries.
