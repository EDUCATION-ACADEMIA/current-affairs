Which of the following has signed an agreement with Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd (BHEL) for cooperation in the development of solar power projects?

(A) SAIL

(B) ONGC

(C) ** GAIL

(D) Coal India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -State-owned gas utility GAIL India Ltd has signed an agreement with Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd (BHEL) for cooperation in the development of solar power projects.

Concept note-2: -BHEL is involved in engineering, manufacturing, construction, servicing and evaluation of various products from key economic sectors such as transportation like Railways, energy, water, oil and gas, defence & aerospace and transmission, in addition to power plant service.

Concept note-3: -Government has approved the grant of Maharatna status to Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) and Gas Authority of India Ltd. (GAIL). This decision was communicated on 1st February, 2013.

Concept note-4: -Tenders : BHEL (Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited) is a public sector undertaking, working under Ministry of heavy industries, Government of India.
