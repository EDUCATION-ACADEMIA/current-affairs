Which of the following Indian player has won Dutch Open Badminton 2019?

(A) Harshit Chaudhary

(B) Arpit Gupta

(C) Vivek Joshi

(D) ** Lakshya Sen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sen clinched his first BWF Tour title by winning the Dutch Open men’s singles title after beating Yusuke Onodera of Japan. The Dutch Open is a BWF Tour Super 100 tournament. In November 2019, he won the SaarLorLux Open which is a BWF Tour Super 100 tournament held in Saarbrücken, Germany.

Concept note-2: -Besides Lakshya and Chirag, their father Dhirendra Sen, mother Nirmala Sen and national coach Vimal Kumar U have been named in the FIR.

Concept note-3: -The Indian challenge in men’s singles category at the India Open 2023 ended on Thursday as ace shuttler Lakshya Sen crashed out in the second round to Denmark’s Rasmus Gemke in a three-game thriller.

Concept note-4: -A PV Sindhu fan, Unnati Hooda is the youngest Indian badminton player to win a BWF Super 100 World Tour title. She won the Odisha Open 2022 at the age of 14.

Concept note-5: -Lakshya is a Kumaoni boy who is born and brought up in Almora, Uttarakhand. His family includes his father Mr D.K Sen, his mother Mrs. Nirmal Sen and one brother Chirag Sen.
