Which of the following is set to be awarded Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award, 2019?

(A) Virat Kohli

(B) ** Bajrang Punia

(C) Hima Das

(D) Mary Kom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award 2019 was awarded to Bajrang Punia for wrestling and Deepa Malik for athletics.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 2. Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award is given for the spectacular and outstanding performance in the field of sports by a sportsperson over a period of four years. For Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award: Indian wrestler Bajrang Punia and Rio Paralympics silver medallist Deepa Malik.

Concept note-3: -Deepa Malik was awarded for Para Athletics. Other National Sports Awards include Dronacharya Awards, Arjuna Awards, and Dhyan Chand Award.

Concept note-4: -Shri Sharath Kamal Achanta has won the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 in the Table Tennis discipline. The Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 ( 2022) is given by the Indian government and the Department of Sport to those sportspersons who have excelled in their sports.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sachin Tendulkar. The first Cricket player to be awarded Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award is Sachin Tendulkar. The Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award is the highest sporting honour given in India in the field of sports and games.
