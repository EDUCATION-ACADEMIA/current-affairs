Which of the following months is celebrated as the Breast Cancer Awareness Month?

(A) November

(B) ** October

(C) September

(D) January

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Every year, October is observed as Breast Cancer Awareness month with the objective to increase awareness about the disease and its prevention. Breast cancer is the most common cancer affecting women across the globe and in India.

Concept note-2: -October is Breast Cancer Awareness Month, an annual campaign to raise awareness about the impact of breast cancer.

Concept note-3: -WHEREAS, there is a national #LightUpMBC campaign on October 13th every year to illuminate 225 landmarks in the metastatic colors of teal, pink and green, throughout the world, bringing awareness to the disease and to honor the number of lives lost to MBC.

Concept note-4: -For nearly four decades now, the country has recognized October as National Breast Cancer Awareness Month. It is a time annually devoted to educating everyone about breast cancer - including metastatic breast cancer (MBC) - and the importance of early detection and access to timely, high-quality care.

Concept note-5: -What is Breast Cancer Awareness Month? October 13 is nationally recognized in the United States as Metastatic Breast Cancer Awareness Day.
