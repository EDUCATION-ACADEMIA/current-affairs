Which of the following movement was launched on 8th August, 1942?

(A) ** Quit India Movement

(B) Dandi March

(C) Non Cooperation Movement

(D) Swadeshi Movement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 8 August 1942 at the All-India Congress Committee session in Bombay, Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi launched the ‘Quit India’ movement. The next day, Gandhi, Nehru and many other leaders of the Indian National Congress were arrested by the British Government.

Concept note-2: -The All-India Congress Commitee met at Bombay on August 8, 1942 and passed the Quit India Resolution adopted at Wardha in July 1942.

Concept note-3: -The Quit India movement was started in August 1942 during the Second World War against the British rule.

Concept note-4: -After the failure of the Cripps Mission to secure Indian support for the British war effort, Gandhi made a call to Do or Die in his Quit India movement delivered in Bombay on 8 August 1942 at the Gowalia Tank Maidan.

Concept note-5: -The Quit India Movement was a movement started by Mahatma Gandhi on 8 August 1942 during the World War II asking for an end to British rule in India. The movement was started in a speech in Bombay where Mahatma Gandhi asked Indians to Do or Die.
