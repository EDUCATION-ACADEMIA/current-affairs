Which of the following railway station has received Gold Rating by the Indian Green Building Council?

(A) Vadodara Station

(B) Itarsi Junction

(C) Allahabad Junction

(D) ** Vijayawada railway station

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Visakhapatnam is one of the few railway stations to get this prestigious certificate. Visakhapatnam secured 82 out of 100 points in six environmental categories. The Environment Directorate of Indian Railways, with the support of the IGBC, has developed the Green Railway Station rating system.

Concept note-2: -In 2021, Central Railways’ Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus Railway Station (CSMT) was awarded IGBC Gold Certification.

Concept note-3: -Indian Railways’ CSMT station in Mumbai has been awarded IGBC Gold certification for implementing various green initiatives and smart passenger amenities.

Concept note-4: -IGBC Green Railway Stations rating system is a voluntary and consensus based programme. IGBC Green Railway Stations rating system is the first of its kind holistic rating in India to address environmental sustainability in Indian railway stations.

Concept note-5: -The Udhna Junction has the distinction of being the first “Green Railway Station” to be listed in the India Book of Records for working towards the restoration of ecosystem and climate. This railway station is located in Gujarat’s Surat district.
