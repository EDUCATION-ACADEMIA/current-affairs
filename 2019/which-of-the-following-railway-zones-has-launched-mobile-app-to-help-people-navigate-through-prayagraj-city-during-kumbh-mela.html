Which of the following railway zones has launched mobile app to help people navigate through Prayagraj city during Kumbh Mela?

(A) North Eastern Railway

(B) Northeast Frontier Railway

(C) North Western Railway

(D) ** North Central Railway

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The North Central Railway (NCR) launched a mobile application to help people navigate through Prayagraj city during Kumbh Mela starting from 15 January 2019. The name of the app is ‘Rail Kumbh Seva Mobile App’.

Concept note-2: -North Central Railways / Indian Railways Portal.

Concept note-3: -The new app introduced by NCR will add as a digital aid for helping tourists in the city. Here’s all you need to know about the ‘Rail Kumbh Seva Mobile App’

Concept note-4: -Northern Railway now comprises of 5 Divisions-Ambala, Delhi, Ferozpur, Lucknow and Moradabad.

Concept note-5: -West Central Railway is using 776 electric locomotives.
