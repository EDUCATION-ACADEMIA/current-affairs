Which of the following regiment will be incorporated in the Indian Army for the first time?

(A) ** Dhanush regiment

(B) Kamaan regiment

(C) Commando regiment

(D) Kerala Scouts

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Madras Regiment is the oldest infantry regiment of the Indian Army, originating in the 1750s. The regiment took part in numerous campaigns with both the British Indian Army and the post-independence Indian Army. “Veer Madrassi, Adi Kollu, Adi Kollu, Adi Kollu !”

Concept note-2: -This series is going to introduce you all to one of the most decorated and battle-hardened regiments in the world-the 3rd Gorkha Rifles of the Indian Army.

Concept note-3: -Kodandera Madappa Cariappa, byname Kipper, (born January 28, 1899, Shanivarsanthe, Coorg district, Mysore [now Kodagu district, Karnataka state], India-died May 15, 1993, Bangalore), Indian military officer and the first chief of staff of the Indian army after India became independent of Great Britain.
