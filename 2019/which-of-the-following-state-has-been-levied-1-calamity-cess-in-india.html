Which of the following state has been levied 1% calamity cess in India?

(A) Odisha

(B) Gujarat

(C) ** Kerala

(D) Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With this, Kerala has become the first state to levy calamity cess.

Concept note-2: -Article 270 of the Indian Constitution exempts cess from the divisible pool of revenues that the Union government must distribute to the states. This means, while the cess/surcharge boosts the Union government’s revenues, it reduces the divisible pool of resources and thus the states’ share of the revenue.

Concept note-3: -(For example, Kerala Flood Cess 1%.)

Concept note-4: -Kerala Flood Cess is imposed 1% on the value of supply of goods or services or both coming under Schedule II, III & IV of SRO. No. 360/2017 Dt. 30.06.

Concept note-5: -The cess is levied on goods such as automobiles and air conditioners that attract the highest 28% GST and on so-called sin goods such as aerated drinks, coal, pan masala and cigarettes.
