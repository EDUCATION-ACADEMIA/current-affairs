Which of the following states is not under Inner Line Permit?

(A) ** Tripura

(B) Manipur

(C) Nagaland

(D) Mizoram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ILP is currently in force in 4 States of N-E - Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Mizorom, Manipur. Not in Meghalaya.

Concept note-2: -Inner Line Permit System is not applicable in Sikkim.

Concept note-3: -There are four North-Eastern states that have ILP: Arunachal Pradesh. Nagaland. Mizoram.

Concept note-4: -The ILP system is in force in four Northeastern states - Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Mizoram and now Manipur - and no Indian citizen can visit any of these states without permit unless he or she belongs to that state, nor can he or she overstay beyond the period specified in the ILP.

Concept note-5: -Four states in the North-Eastern belt have an inner line permit. They are Nagaland, Manipur, Arunachal Pradesh, Mizoram, and Lakshadweep. In addition, the inner line permit is required to enter the islands of Lakshadweep.
