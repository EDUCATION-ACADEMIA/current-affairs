Which of the following team wins the Champions League for the sixth time?

(A) Bayern Munich

(B) Tottenham Hotspur

(C) ** Liverpool

(D) Barcelona

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Italian club AC Milan are next with seven. German team Bayern Munich and England’s Liverpool have won the annual showpiece six times each.

Concept note-2: -There have been three Premier League winners of the UEFA Champions League since it was formed, Manchester United (twice; in 1998/99 and 2007/08), Liverpool (twice; 2004/05 and 2018/19) and Chelsea (twice; 2011/12 and 2020/21). Those five wins have produced plenty of drama.

Concept note-3: -Bayern Munich won the final 1–0 thanks to a 59th-minute goal scored by former Paris Saint-Germain player Kingsley Coman, who was later selected as man of the match.

Concept note-4: -Real Madrid hold the record for the most victories, having won the competition 14 times, including the inaugural competition in 1956, and the latest in 2022.

Concept note-5: -Liverpool won the final 2–0, with a penalty which was scored after 106 seconds by Mohamed Salah and a goal by substitute Divock Origi after 87 minutes.
