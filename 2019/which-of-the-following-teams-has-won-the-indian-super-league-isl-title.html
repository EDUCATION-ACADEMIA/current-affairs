Which of the following teams has won the Indian Super League (ISL) title?

(A) Mumbai City FC

(B) FC Goa

(C) ** Bengaluru FC

(D) Kerala Blasters

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Since the league’s inaugural season, a total of five clubs have been crowned as the Champions: ATK (3), Chennaiyin (2), Bengaluru (1), Mumbai City (1), and Hyderabad (1). Since its introduction of League Winners Shield in 2019–20 season, Goa , Mumbai City and Jamshedpur have won the League Winners Shield once.

Concept note-2: -2021-22 ISL winner: Hyderabad FC Hyderabad FC made steady progress since debuting in the Indian Super League in 2019, going from a last place finish in their maiden appearance to winning the title in 2022.

Concept note-3: -In the previous eight editions, erstwhile ATK have been the most dominant team, having secured the title three times. Hyderabad FC, Chennaiyin FC and Bengaluru FC are the other three sides, which complete the ISL winners list.

Concept note-4: -ISL Winners & Runners List. The Indian Super League (ISL) has been entertaining football fans in the country since it’s inception in 2014. The premier domestic football league saw ATK Kolkata - now ATK Mohun Bagan - beating Kerala Blasters FC in the final of the inaugural season to be crowned the champions.

Concept note-5: -Mumbai City won the championship, having defeated ATK Mohun Bagan in the final, and also won the League Winners Shield for league premiers.
