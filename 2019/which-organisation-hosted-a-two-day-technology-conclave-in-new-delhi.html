Which organisation hosted a two-day Technology Conclave in New Delhi?

(A) HCL

(B) TCS

(C) ** NIC

(D) Wipro

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Communications and IT Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw inaugurated the 3rd edition of the NIC Tech Conclave in New Delhi on 3 March 2022. The two-day event being is organized by the National Informatics Centre (NIC).

Concept note-2: -The event was attended by Secretary and Additional Secretary, MeitY, along with Director General NIC, and other dignitaries from Centre and State Governments. Tech Conclave 2022 focused on Emerging Technologies applicable especially in e-Governance, with the theme –Next Gen Technologies for Digital Government.

Concept note-3: -Minister of State for Electronics and IT, Shri Rajeev Chandrasekhar inaugurates NIC Tech Conclave 2022.

Concept note-4: -The tech conclave is being organized by the National Informatics Centre(NIC) on 3rd and 4th March 2022 with focus on emerging technologies applicable especially in e-Governance. The Theme for Tech Conclave this year is “Next Gen Technologies for Digital Government”.

Concept note-5: -Union Minister for Electronics & Information Technology, Railways and Communications, Shri Ashwini Vaishnaw will inaugurate the 3rd edition of ‘NIC Tech Conclave 2022’, a two-day event being organized by the National Informatics Centre (NIC), at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi on 3rd& 4th March 2022.
