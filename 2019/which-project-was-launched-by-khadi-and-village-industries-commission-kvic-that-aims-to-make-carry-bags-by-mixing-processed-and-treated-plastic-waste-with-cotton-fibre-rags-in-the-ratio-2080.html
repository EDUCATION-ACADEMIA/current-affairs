Which project was launched by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) that aims to make carry bags by mixing processed and treated plastic waste with cotton fibre rags in the ratio 20:80?

(A) ** REPLAN

(B) RECYCLE

(C) REUSE

(D) RENDER

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -REPLAN stands for REducing PLastic in Nature. In this project, the waste plastic is collected, cleaned, chopped, beaten and treated for softness. It aims to make carry bags by mixing processed and treated plastic waste with cotton fibre rags in the ratio of 20:80.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) The scheme is implemented by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) functioning as the nodal agency at the national level.

Concept note-3: -The Khadii and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) is a statutory body formed in April 1957 by the Government of India, under the Act of Parliament, ‘Khadi and Village Industries Commission Act of 1956’.

Concept note-4: -The KVIC is charged with the planning, promotion, organisation and implementation of programs for the development of Khadi and other village industries in the rural areas in coordination with other agencies engaged in rural development wherever necessary.

Concept note-5: -It aids in creating common service facilities that help in processing of raw materials. It aids the marketing of KVI products through artisans and other avenues. It creates linkages with multiple marketing agencies for the promotion and sale of KVI products.
