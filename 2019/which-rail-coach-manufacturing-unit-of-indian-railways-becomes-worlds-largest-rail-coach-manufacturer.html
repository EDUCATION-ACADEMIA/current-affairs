Which rail coach manufacturing unit of Indian Railways becomes world’s largest rail coach manufacturer?

(A) Chittaranjan Locomotive Works, Asansol

(B) Modern Coach Factory, Raebareli

(C) Rail Coach Factory, Kapurthala

(D) ** Integral Coach Factory, Chennai.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Integral Coach Factory (ICF ) Chennai has overtaken China’s largest coach manufacturing unit to become the largest rail coach maker in the world.

Concept note-2: -This makes ICF the largest rail coach manufacturer in the world.

Concept note-3: -The Integral Coach Factory (ICF), in Chennai, has beaten its last year’s coach production target. The coach manufacturing factory of Indian Railways has recently beaten its last year’s outturn of 3,262 coaches in just a period of 9 months and 13 days.

Concept note-4: -Integral Coach Factory (ICF) is a manufacturer of rail coaches situated in Perambur, Chennai, India. It was founded in 1955 and is inhabited and governed by the Indian Railways. Hence, this is the correct answer.

Concept note-5: -The Diesel Locomotive Works (DLW) in Varanasi, India, is a production unit owned by Indian Railways, that manufactures diesel-electric locomotives and its spare parts. Founded in 1961, the DLW rolled out its first locomotive three years later, on 3 January 1964.
