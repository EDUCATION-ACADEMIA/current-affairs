Which railway station has been certified as country’s first ‘Eat Right Station’ by FSSAI for food safety and hygiene?

(A) ** Mumbai

(B) Bhopal

(C) Hyderabad

(D) Amravati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Railways’ Bhopal Railway Station has been awarded a 4- star ‘Eat Right Station’ certification for providing high-quality, nutritious food to passengers.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is the Habibganj railway station. Habibganj railway station is a railway station in Bhopal City and part of the West Central Railway.

Concept note-3: -The ‘Eat Right Station’ certification is awarded by FSSAI to railway stations that set benchmarks in providing safe and wholesome food to passengers. The station is awarded a certificate upon a conclusion of an FSSAI-empanelled third-party audit agency with ratings from 1 to 5.

Concept note-4: -Bhopal Express is India’s first ISO 9000-2001 certified train.

Concept note-5: -Indian Railways’ Varanasi Cantt Railway Station has been awarded a 5- star ‘Eat Right Station’ certification for providing high-quality, nutritious food to passengers.
