Which rank was secured by India at the newly released Women, Peace, and Security Index?

(A) ** 133

(B) 134

(C) 123

(D) 101

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India ranks 148 out of 170 countries in the ‘Women, Peace And Security Index 2021’.

Concept note-2: -India ranks 133 out of 167 countries in Women, Peace and Security Index.

Concept note-3: -In the Women Peace and Security Index 2021/2022, Norway takes the top rank, scoring high across all dimensions, including educational and financial inclusion and anti-discrimination legislation.

Concept note-4: -India ranks 135 among a total of 146 countries in the Global Gender Gap Index 2022 and is the worst performer in the world in the “health and survival” sub-index where it is ranked 146.
