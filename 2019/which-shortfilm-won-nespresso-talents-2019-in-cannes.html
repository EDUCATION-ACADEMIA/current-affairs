Which shortfilm won Nespresso Talents 2019 in Cannes?

(A) The Good Girl

(B) Test Drive

(C) ** Seed Mother

(D) Ankul

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nespresso Talents Awards 2019: New Zealand filmmaker triumphs at Cannes with short film about Bali.

Concept note-2: -CANNES: An Indian filmmaker, Achyutanand Dwivedi, made amends for his nation’s disappointing no-show in the 72nd Cannes Film Festival. His three-minute film, ‘Seed Mother’, won the third prize in the international section of Nespresso Talents 2019 in Cannes on Friday night.

Concept note-3: -At the official Cannes award ceremony, before the feature prizes are handed out, there is another Palme d’Or announced: for the best short film. The 2019 recipient was The Distance Between Us and the Sky, directed by Vasilis Kekatos.

Concept note-4: -The 72nd annual Cannes Film Festival took place from 14 to 25 May 2019. Mexican filmmaker Alejandro González Iñárritu served as jury president. The Palme d’Or went to the South Korean film Parasite, directed by Bong Joon-ho; Bong became the first Korean director to win the award.

Concept note-5: -The Crow: An AI Film that Won Cannes Short Film Festival.
