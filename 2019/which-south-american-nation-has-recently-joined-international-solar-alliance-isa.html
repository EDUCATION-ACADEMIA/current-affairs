Which South American nation has recently joined International Solar Alliance (ISA)?

(A) Ecuador

(B) Colombia

(C) Uruguay

(D) ** Bolivia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Republic of Panama signed the Framework Agreement of the International Solar Alliance.

Concept note-2: -This move will make Bolivia, which is known to have one-fourth of the world’s lithium reserves, one of the major provider of metal for India’s e-mobility and e-storage needs.

Concept note-3: -The External Affairs Ministry said Ambassador of Republic of Congo, Raymond Serge Bale signed the International Solar Alliance Framework Agreement in the presence of Joint Secretary. India has welcomed Congo to International Solar Alliance.

Concept note-4: -Recently, Israel, United States, Greece join International Solar Alliance, membership rises to 102, Recently joined by Antigua & Barbados on 7 Jan 2022. Syria joined in February 2022 to become the 103rd member.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Italy.
