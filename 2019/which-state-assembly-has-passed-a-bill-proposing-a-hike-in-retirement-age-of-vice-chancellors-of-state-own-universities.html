Which state assembly has passed a bill proposing a hike in retirement age of Vice-chancellors of State own Universities?

(A) ** West Bengal

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Himachal Pradesh

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The West Bengal Assembly today passed a bill endorsing hiking of retirement age of Vice-chancellors of State own Universities from 65 years to 70 years replacing an ordinance on the matter.

Concept note-2: -of 70 years, whichever is earlier. Committee.

Concept note-3: -With their role limited to presiding over convocations, Chancellors in central universities are titular heads, who are appointed by the President in his capacity as Visitor.

Concept note-4: -Hon’ble Governor is ex-officio Chancellor of the State Universities. As per the provisions of the Act of the concerned University, the Governor appoints the Vice Chancellor on the advice/ in consultation with the State Government.

Concept note-5: -The governor of the state, appointed as the union’s representative of state by the president, is the honorary chancellor of all State owned universities. The de facto head of any government university is the vice-chancellor.
