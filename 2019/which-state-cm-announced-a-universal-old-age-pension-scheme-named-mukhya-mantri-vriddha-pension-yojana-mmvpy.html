Which state CM announced a Universal Old Age Pension scheme, named Mukhya Mantri Vriddha Pension Yojana (MMVPY)?

(A) ** Bihar

(B) Andra Pradesh

(C) TamilNadu

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bihar Chief Minister Vridhjan Pension Yojana 2023 Elderly people between the age 60 to 79 years will receive a pension of ₹400 per month, while elderly people above age 80 years will receive ₹500 per month.

Concept note-2: -IGNOAPS – Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme The IGNOAPS is one such pension plan in India. It was introduced in 2007 by the Ministry of Rural Development, and it is popularly known as NSAP (National Social Assistance Programme).

Concept note-3: -Rajasthan Old Age Pension Scheme: Benefits Through the old age pension 2022 scheme, Rs 750-1000 will be given as a monthly pension to the male citizens above the age of 58 and to female citizens above the age of 55 as financial aid. All the senior citizens of the Rajasthan state will be able to live an easier life.

Concept note-4: -Varishtha Pension Bima Yojana is a central-guaranteed pension scheme for elderly Indian citizens aged 60 years and above. The pension scheme, under the Ministry of Finance, is operated by the Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC).
