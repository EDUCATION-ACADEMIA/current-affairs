Which state CM introduced KALIA Chhatra Bruti for the children of farmers?

(A) Andra Pradesh

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) ** Odisha

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Odisha government launches KALIA Chhatra Bruti scholarship for Farmers Children. The government of Odisha has launched the KALIA Chhatra Bruti scholarship for the children of farmers. The Scholarship is under the KALIA, Krushak Assistance for Livelihood and Income Augmentation, a scheme for financing farmers in Odisha.

Concept note-2: -Odisha KALIA Scholarship Under the Kalia Scholarship Scheme, the Government of Odisha is going to provide financial assistance to the children of the beneficiaries of Kalia Yojana so that they can pursue their professional or technical courses.

Concept note-3: -KALIA scheme launched by the Odisha government is related to Farmers. KALIA - “Krushak Assistance for Livelihood and Income Augmentation”. The aim of the scheme is to accelerate agricultural prosperity and reduce poverty in the State payments to encourage cultivation and associated activities.

Concept note-4: -The two major mission of the KALIA Scheme is to relieve debt-ridden farmers and to provide financial assistance to agricultural households, landless agricultural laborers, and marginal cultivators in Odisha.

Concept note-5: -Explanation: Odisha government distributed Rs 869 crore to 41.85 state farmers under the Krushak Assistance for Livelihood and Income Augmentation (KALIA) scheme and assured them of additional aid for crop losses due to floods in the state.
