Which State government conferred with Platinum award in Web Ratna under the category State/UT by Digital India Awards?

(A) ** Tripura

(B) Kerala

(C) Maharashtra

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Central Board of Secondary Education has won Digital India Awards 2022- Gold. The award was conferred by President of India, Droupadi Murmu to the Board. CBSE won the award in the Data Sharing and Use for Socio Economic Development Category.

Concept note-2: -Digital India Awards (DIA) has been instituted by MeitY, under the aegis of National Portal of India to encourage and honour innovative digital solutions/ exemplary initiatives by various government entities in the realm of Digital Governance.

Concept note-3: -At Plenary Hall, Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi NIC UP received Digital India Award ‘Platinum’ icon in ‘Exemplary Online Services’ category for Project ‘VYAS - VarnijYakar Automation System’ . The ceremony was held on 19th December, 2016 at Plenary Hall, Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi-110001.

Concept note-4: -Excellence in Digital Governance - STATE/UT The Award acknowledges the State/UT of India that displays exemplary initiative in establishing comprehensive digital presence in sectors like health, labour, finance, social justice and environment leading to the accomplishment of sustainable development goals.
