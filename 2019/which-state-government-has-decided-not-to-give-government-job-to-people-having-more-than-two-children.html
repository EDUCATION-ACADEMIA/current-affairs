Which state government has decided not to give government job to people having more than two children?

(A) ** Assam

(B) Bihar

(C) Haryana

(D) Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -No government jobs for those with more than 2 kids, decides Assam cabinet.

Concept note-2: -The Indian population is increasing exponentially and will continue to multiply in the coming years. This is the higher ratio of people of married age who will give birth to children. The state of Assam and Uttar Pradesh in India has adopted the two-child policy to control the rampantly increasing population.

Concept note-3: -Enacted in 1992, the Odisha Zilla Parishad Act also prohibits people with more than two children from holding any post in panchayats and urban local bodies.

Concept note-4: -India does not have a national child policy as of July 2021. Many local laws in India apply penalties for having more than two children. Local two-child laws in India have been criticized for being unnecessary, violating women’s rights, and discriminating against Muslims.
