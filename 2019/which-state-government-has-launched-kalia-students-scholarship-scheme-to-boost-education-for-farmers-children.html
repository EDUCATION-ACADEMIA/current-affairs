Which state government has launched Kalia Students’ Scholarship Scheme to boost education for farmers children?

(A) Chhattisgarh

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Assam

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Kalia Scholarship Program would enable the children of Kalia Yojana recipients to receive financial support from the state of Odisha in order to enroll in professional or technical programmer.

Concept note-2: -Odisha KALIA Scholarship Under the Kalia Scholarship Scheme, the Government of Odisha is going to provide financial assistance to the children of the beneficiaries of Kalia Yojana so that they can pursue their professional or technical courses.

Concept note-3: -KALIA scheme launched by the Odisha government is related to Farmers. KALIA - “Krushak Assistance for Livelihood and Income Augmentation”. The aim of the scheme is to accelerate agricultural prosperity and reduce poverty in the State payments to encourage cultivation and associated activities.

Concept note-4: -KALIA stands for “Krushak Assistance for Livelihood and Income Augmentation”. This scheme has been launched by Odisha Government to accelerate Agricultural Prosperity and reduce poverty in the State.

Concept note-5: -KALIA Scheme - Important Points for UPSC. The Odisha government took an initiative in January 2019 for the small farmers, cultivators and landless agricultural labourers through a scheme called Krushak Assistance for Livelihood and Income Augmentation (KALIA Scheme.)
