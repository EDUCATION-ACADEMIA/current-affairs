Which state government plans to set up Swachh Bharat World University to mark Mahatma Gandhi’s 150th birth anniversary?

(A) Haryana

(B) Gujarat

(C) Andra Pradesh

(D) ** Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government of Maharashtra has decided to set up ‘Swachh Bharat World University’ in Wardha to mark Mahatma Gandhi’s 150th birth anniversary.

Concept note-2: -“A clean India would be the best tribute India could pay to Mahatma Gandhi on his 150 birth anniversary in 2019,” said Shri Narendra Modi as he launched the Swachh Bharat Mission at Rajpath in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -On 2nd October 2014, the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan was officially launched at Rajpath in New Delhi by the honourable Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Complete answer: The day on which the campaign was officially launched i.e. 2nd October 2014, was the 145th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi.

Concept note-4: -The Nation is gearing up to offer Karyanjali as a Shraddhanjali (Tribute) to Mahatma Gandhi and Kasturba Gandhi on their 150th Birth Anniversary. Gandhiji’s freedom struggle culminated in India’s Independence on 15th August, 1947.

Concept note-5: -Swachh Bharat Abhiyan was launched on the occasion of Mahatma Gandhis birth. anniversary.
