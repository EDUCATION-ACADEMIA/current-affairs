Which state government sets up a Skill University with the cost of 850 crore?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) ** Assam

(C) Andra Pradesh

(D) TamilNadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Assam government will set up a Skill University at the cost of 850 crore rupees at Darrang district. This will be perhaps the first skill university of the country with a capacity of 10 thousand seats.

Concept note-2: -The Government of Haryana, understanding the emphasis of the Government of India towards skill and entrepreneurship development launched India’s first Skill University in 2016. The University is being set up at village Dudhola in district Palwal.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Rajasthan. Rajasthan: The Rajasthan government opened the country’s first government-run Skills University.

Concept note-4: -C.V. Raman University Bihar (CVRU Bihar) was established in 2018 as Bihar’s first Skill University with an aim an aim to curb the migration problem by offering affordable quality education and skill-based training to the local youth.

Concept note-5: -GUWAHATI: Chief minister Sarbananda Sonowal on Monday laid the foundation of the Assam Skill University (ASU) at Mangaldoi in Darrang district - a first-of-its-kind institute in the northeast.
