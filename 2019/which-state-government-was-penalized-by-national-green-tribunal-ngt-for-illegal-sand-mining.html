Which state government was penalized by National Green Tribunal (NGT) for illegal sand mining?

(A) Arunachal Pradesh

(B) ** Andhra Pradesh

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2021, titled “illegal sand mining in Prakasham and Kadapa District, Andhra Pradesh”. S.No Particulars Page Nos.

Concept note-2: -A bench, headed by NGT Chairperson Justice Adarsh Kumar Goel, directed the chief secretary of Andhra Pradesh to forthwith prohibit all unregulated sand mining.

Concept note-3: -The NGT directed the Chief Secretary, Odisha to initiate cases against illegal sand miners and also pass appropriate orders under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002 against the offenders/illegal miners.

Concept note-4: -Bihar had banned sand mining from July 1 to September 30 last year following a National Green Tribunal (NGT) order.
