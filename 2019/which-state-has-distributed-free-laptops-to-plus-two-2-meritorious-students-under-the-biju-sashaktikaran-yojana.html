Which state has distributed free laptops to Plus Two (+2) meritorious students under the ‘Biju Sashaktikaran Yojana’?

(A) Jharkhand

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) ** Odisha

(D) Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Odisha government has taken an initiative to distribute free laptops for the students of classes 11 and 12. This way, students will be able to learn more and be more knowledgeable about technology. Biju Yuva Sashaktikaran Yojana helps students to learn online and study by themselves.

Concept note-2: -The Odisha government has decided to distribute free laptops to the students who have passed their 12th from any of the Odia District Schools. It is said that approximately 15000 laptops will be distributed to the students. But only those who have pursued their education in Odisha State are eligible.

Concept note-3: -The Odisha government will distribute free laptops under the Biju Yuva Sashaktikaran Yojana 2023. Under this scheme, free laptops will be distributed to the meritorious students of class 12th. Eligible students can visit the official website at dhe.odisha.gov.in for Odisha Free Laptop scheme online registration.

Concept note-4: -Only the students of Odisha state can apply for the laptop scheme. Students must be studying in class 12th of any Odisha government school. To be eligible for the free laptop, students must score good marks. Only those students will be selected who are successful in securing 70% or more marks in the final examination.

Concept note-5: -Tamil Nadu Free Laptop Scheme 2023 has been launched by Chief Minister Edappadi K Palaniswami for students who recently passed the 10th or 12th standard examination. The online application form filling procedure will commence very soon. Applicants have to fill out the application form before the last date.
