Which state has launched the Arundhati Gold Scheme?

(A) Arunachal Pradesh

(B) ** Assam

(C) Nagaland

(D) Sikkim

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Many parts of our country are plagued with social evils surrounding the girl child, like child marriage, female foeticide, dowry, etc. But the Assamese society, from time immemorial, is fortunate to be free from such evils.

Concept note-2: -Arundhati Gold Scheme application has been developed to facilitate the parents of the girl child who are economically not very sound, but like all parents desire to give some gold which is considered auspicious to gift daughters on her marriage.

Concept note-3: -Assam govt invites Arundhati Gold Scheme online application form 2023 at official website revenueassam.nic.in/arundhati/. Under this Arundhati Swarna Yojana, the state govt. will provide Rs. 30,000 to newlywed brides at the time of their wedding.

Concept note-4: -DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR APPLICATION FOR THE SCHEME: HSLC/CBSE or Equivalent pass certificate as proof of age. Minimum Legal age of Bride and Groom must be 18 and 21 years. b. Tea Tribe including Adivasi Communities Birth Certificate of both Bride and Groom / Any Academic Certificate as proof of Age.
