Which state has topped ACI’s Ease Of Doing Business (EDB) Index 2018?

(A) Gujarat

(B) ** Andhra Pradesh

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the last ranking released in July 2018, Andhra Pradesh topped the chart, followed by Telangana and Haryana (3rd).

Concept note-2: -It ranks Maharashtra at number one amongst the states and UTs in India, with a score of 3.33 out of 5, followed by Gujarat, Chandigarh, Haryana and Himachal Pradesh, he said.

Concept note-3: -Karnataka business rankings: Karnataka emerges at top in Ease of Doing Business rankings, along with six states - The Economic Times.

Concept note-4: -Haryana has been included in the top achievers category in the fifth edition of ‘State Ease of Doing Business’ released by the Ministry of Commerce and Industry on June 30, 2022. Apart from Haryana, the top achievers category includes Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Karnataka, Punjab, Tamil Nadu and Telangana.

Concept note-5: -India’s “rank in ease of doing business according to World Bank report has improved from 142 in 2014 to 63 in 2022 and reduction in compliances is now a major focus.
