Which state tops in organizing the Fit India Week?

(A) Karnataka

(B) Bihar

(C) UP

(D) ** Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -More than 21,000 schools have received Fit India flag. Currently, the Fit India Flag has been obtained by 21,344 schools. Andhra Pradesh also leads this movement with a whopping 8,117 - the highest number of Fit India-ranked schools across India.

Concept note-2: -Andhra Pradesh tops in organizing Fit India School Week, more than 21,000 schools receive FIT India flag – ScooNews. Get Published - Write for Us!

Concept note-3: -FIT INDIA Movement was launched on 29th August, 2019 by Hon’ble Prime Minister with a view to make fitness an integral part of our daily lives.

Concept note-4: -Hon’ble Prime Minister of India has launched the Fit India Movement on 29 Aug 2019 with a view to make Physical Fitness a way of life. Fit India Movement aims at behavioural changes – from sedentary lifestyle to physically active way of day-to-day living.

Concept note-5: -The launch of Fit India Freedom Run 3.0 took place at the Major Dhyanchand National Stadium in New Delhi on Sunday morning on the occasion of Gandhi Jayanti today.
