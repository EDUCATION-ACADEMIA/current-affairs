(B) Scotland

(D) ** Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kok Leong makes history with World Snooker Championships 2022 win. National snooker player Lim Kok Leong has created history after emerging as the first Malaysian player to win the IBSF World Championship in Antalya, Turkey, yesterday.

Concept note-2: -The IBSF & Turkish Billiards Federation are hosting the 2022 World Snooker Championships for the Men, Women and Masters categories. These championships shall be played in Antalya, Turkiye from 4th November till 17th November.
