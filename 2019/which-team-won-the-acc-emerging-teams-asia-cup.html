Which team won the ACC Emerging Teams Asia Cup?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) ** Pakistan

(C) India

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICC U19 Cricket World Cup 2022: India Beat England By 4 Wickets In Final To Win Record-Extending 5th Title.

Concept note-2: -Under his captaincy, India won the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2011 Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy, the most by any captain. He also led India to victory in the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cup. Additionally, under his leadership, India won the 2010 and 2011 ICC Test Mace and 2013 ICC ODI Championship.

Concept note-3: -This year six teams or countries participated in Asia Cup 2022. Sri Lanka won the Asia Cup 2022 title. The first Asia cup was played in 1984 in UAE and India was the winner of this tournament and Sri Lanka was the runner-up.

Concept note-4: -India, with seven titles (six ODI and one T20I), is the most successful team in the tournament. Sri Lanka is the second most successful team, with six. Sri Lanka has played the most Asia Cups (14) followed by India, Pakistan and Bangladesh who have played 13 each. Sri Lanka won the Asia Cup 2022.
