Which team won the first ever Premier Badminton League?

(A) Ahmedabad Smash Masters

(B) ** Bengaluru Raptors

(C) Delhi Dashers

(D) Pune 7 Aces

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hyderabad Hotshots was the first winner of Premier Badminton League in the year 2013 with Awadhe warriors. Hyderabad Hotshots changed the team name to Hyderabad Hunters in 2016. Hyderabad Hunters was the winner of the year 2017-2018 against Bengaluru Blasters .

Concept note-2: -First Season (Season 1) The four teams to proceed to the semi-finals were Delhi Dashers, Chennai Smashers, Awadhe Warriors and Mumbai Rockets. Delhi beat Chennai 4-3 and Mumbai Rockets beat Awadhe Warriors 3-1 to proceed to the final. Delhi Dashers won the tournament after beating Mumbai Rockets 4-3.

Concept note-3: -Premier Badminton League 2022 to start on December 17 Bengaluru Raptors won the last edition held in February 2020 before the COVID-19 pandemic forced a halt. The latest edition will be the sixth season of the tournament.

Concept note-4: -The Premier Badminton League (PBL) is one of the top badminton leagues in the world. Its playing field features the top Indian and international badminton talent in the world, including Olympic and World Championship Medal Winners.
