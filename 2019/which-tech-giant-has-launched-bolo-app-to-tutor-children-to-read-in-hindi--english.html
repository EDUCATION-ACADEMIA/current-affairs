Which tech giant has launched ’Bolo’ app to tutor children to read in Hindi & English?

(A) ** Google

(B) Microsoft

(C) Facebook

(D) Infosys

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Google launches ‘Bolo’ to tutor children to read Hindi, English.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Google. Google unveiled a new app ‘Bolo’ on 5 March 2019 that aims to help children in primary school learn to read in Hindi and English. The free app, which is being launched in India first, uses Google’s speech recognition and text-to-speech technology.

Concept note-3: -Read Along (formerly Bolo) is a free and fun speech based reading tutor app designed for children aged 5 and above. It helps children improve their reading skills in English and many other languages by encouraging them to read aloud interesting stories together with “Diya”, the friendly in app assistant.
