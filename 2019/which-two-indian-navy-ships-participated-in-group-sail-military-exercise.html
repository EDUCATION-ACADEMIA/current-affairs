Which two Indian Navy Ships participated in Group Sail military exercise?

(A) Pratap and Charag

(B) Sukanya and Sharada

(C) Kirpan and Kora

(D) ** Kolkata and Shakti

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is INS Talwar. INS Talwar participated in the Exercise Cutlass Express 2021.

Concept note-2: -This edition also marked the 30th anniversary of the exercise and was hosted by the JMSDF. The Indian Navy was represented by Eastern Fleet ships Shivalik and Kamorta led by Rear Admiral Sanjay Bhalla, Flag Officer Commanding Eastern Fleet.

Concept note-3: -Arnala class of ships will replace the Abhay class ASW Ships of the Indian Navy and are designed to undertake anti-submarine operations in coastal waters and Low Intensity Maritime Operations including subsurface surveillance in littoral waters.

Concept note-4: -Latest News Separately, the Navy also conducted the biennial tri-services amphibious exercise (AMPHEX) 2023 at Kakinada in Andhra Pradesh from January 17 to 22.
