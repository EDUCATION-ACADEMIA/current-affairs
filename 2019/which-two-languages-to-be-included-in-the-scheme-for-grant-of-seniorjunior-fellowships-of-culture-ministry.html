Which two languages to be included in the Scheme for grant of Senior/Junior Fellowships of Culture Ministry?

(A) Gujarati and Hindustani

(B) Sindhi and Gujarati

(C) Hindustani and Maithili

(D) ** Nepali and Santhali

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Junior Fellowship (25-40 years) & Senior Fellowship (Above 40 years) The CCRT is implementing partially the scheme titled “The Fellowship Scheme for the Award of Fellowship to outstanding persons in the Field of Culture”. Under this Scheme 200 each of Junior and Senior Fellows are selected every year.

Concept note-2: -The Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) scheme of the University Grants Commission (UGC) is open to candidates who qualify in the National Eligibility Testing (NET) of the UGC and the UGC-Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) joint test.
