Which union Minister flags off the bus named “Jaldoot” in Pune, Maharashtra which showcases the importance of water conservation?

(A) Ravi Shankar Prasad

(B) Harsh Vardhan

(C) Piyush Goyal

(D) ** Prakash Javadekar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’JALDOOT’ will encourage people’s participation for Water Conservation – Prakash Javadekar . Concept note-2: -Devendra Fadnavis is Current Deputy Chief Minister of Maharashtra And Minister of Water Resources. Government of Maharashtra since 14 August 2022.
