Which union ministry has launched ’RDP INDIA 2019 app’ for Republic Day parade?

(A) Ministry of Home Affairs

(B) ** Ministry of Defence

(C) Ministry of Information and Broadcasting

(D) Ministry of Women and Child Development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -People can watch the highlights of this year’s Republic Day parade at the Rajpath on a mobile application which has been launched by the Defence Ministry . The ‘RDP INDIA 2019’ app was launched on Saturday on the occasion of the 70th Republic day.

Concept note-2: -Republic Day 2023 Chief Guest This year, Egyptian President Abdel-Fattah el-Sisi is the chief guest of Republic Day celebrations.

Concept note-3: -Egyptian President Abdel Fattah Al Sisi is the chief guest for Republic Day this year. He arrived in New Delhi on Tuesday. This is the first time that any Egyptian president has been invited as a chief guest for Republic Day. Egypt is among the nine countries invited to the G20 Summit under India’s presidency in 2023.

Concept note-4: -The Republic Day parade began with the President taking the salute. During the march past, Indian Army was represented by a Mounted Column of 61 Cavalry, nine Mechanised Columns and six marching contingents.
