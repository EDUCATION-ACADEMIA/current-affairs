Which university in the world secured top spot in the World University Rankings 2020, published by Times Higher Education?

(A) Standford University

(B) ** Oxford University

(C) MIT

(D) Yale University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The University of Oxford tops the ranking for the seventh consecutive year.

Concept note-2: -For the fourth year in a row, the University of Oxford leads the rankings in first place, while the University of Cambridge falls to third. The California Institute of Technology rises three places to second, while Stanford, Yale, Harvard and Imperial College London all appear in the top ten.

Concept note-3: -The University of Oxford tops the ranking for the sixth consecutive year, while mainland China has two institutions in the top 20 for the first time: Peking University and Tsinghua University share 16th place.

Concept note-4: -This year’s ranking of the world’s top 1,000 universities sees relatively little movement at the very top. Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), Stanford University and Harvard University are an unchanged top three, while the University of Oxford continues to be the best university in the UK.

Concept note-5: -With 75 institutes included in the Times Higher Education (THE) World University Rankings 2023, India is the fifth most represented country on the list. In 2020, 56 universities took part, up from 31 in 2017.
