Who among the following accepted Punjab CM Amarinder Singh’s invite to attend the inaugural event of Kartarpur corridor in Pakistan?

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) Ram Nath Kovind

(C) ** Manmohan Singh

(D) Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All Indian passport holders can visit Kartarpur. However, foreigners are not allowed. Indians belonging to any religion can use the Corridor and enter the Gurdwara. It’s not limited to only Sikhs.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Option 4: Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the Kartarpur Corridor from India and from Pakistan by his counterpart, Imran khan. It is a 4.5 km long highway that connects Dera Baba Nanak shrine in India with Shri Darbar Sahib Kartarpur in Pakistan.

Concept note-3: -The Kartarpur Sahib Corridor links Gurudwara Darbar Sahib in Pakistan, which is the final resting place of Guru Nanak Dev, to Dera Baba Nanak shrine in Punjab’s Gurdaspur district. This announcment comes at an important time for Sikhs as it is Guru Nanak Dev’s birthday anniversary on November 19.
