Who among the following has been appointed as Lokpal of Punjab?

(A) ** Vinod Kumar Sharma

(B) Satish Kumar Mittal

(C) Dilip B Bhosale

(D) Pradip Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About Lokpal The Act came into force on 16th January, 2014 and has been amended once in 2016 since its notification. Vide notification dated 26th May 2022, Shri Justice Pradip Kumar Mohanty, Member (Judicial) has been authorised to act as the Chairperson, Lokpal with effect from 28th May,2022.

Concept note-2: -The present Act is called “Punjab Lokpal Act, 1996” which has been enacted to secure proper investigation of inquiry against public men and thereby ensuring eradication of corruption, adherence to the rule of law by observing rules of natural justice.
