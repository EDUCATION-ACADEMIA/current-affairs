Who among the following has been appointed as the interim finance and corporate affairs minister by the President of India?

(A) ** Shri Piyush Goyal

(B) Shri Naresh Iyer

(C) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(D) Shri Vikram Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Arun Jaitley will be designated as a minister without a portfolio during his period of indisposition.

Concept note-2: -Currently the minister for railways and coal, Goyal will also be interim minister for corporate affairs, the government said. Jaitley will remain a minister without portfolio.

Concept note-3: -Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman was born on 18th August 1959 in the temple town of Madurai, Tamil Nadu. She did her schooling and her graduation in Economics, from Seethalakshmi Ramaswamy College in Tiruchirapalli. She went on to do her masters in Economics from Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi.
