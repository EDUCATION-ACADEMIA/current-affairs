Who among the following has been nominated for the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award by Hockey India?

(A) Rupinder Pal Singh

(B) S. V. Sunil

(C) ** P.R. Sreejesh

(D) Sardar Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is PR Sreejesh and Deepika.

Concept note-2: -Rani Rampal is an Indian Hockey player. She was born on 4th December 1994 in Shahbad Markanda Village in Kurukshetra district in Haryana. She conferred with the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna 2020 and the first woman hockey player to get this award. She trained under Baldev Singh former recipient of the Dronacharya Award.

Concept note-3: -Viswanathan Anand, who became India’s first chess grandmaster in 1988, was the first recipient of the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award, in 1991-1992.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. Sardara Singh was awarded Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award 2017 for Hockey.
