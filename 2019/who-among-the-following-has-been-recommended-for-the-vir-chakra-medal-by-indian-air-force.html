Who among the following has been recommended for the Vir Chakra medal by Indian Air Force?

(A) Birender Singh Dhanoa

(B) ** Abhinandan Varthaman

(C) Anil Khosla

(D) Pradeep Padmakar Bapat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The India Air Force is recommending Wing Commander Abhinandan Varthaman for the wartime gallantry medal Vir Chakra for shooting down an F-16 in aerial conflict with Pakistan.

Concept note-2: -Instituted on 26 Jan 1950 and awarded for acts of Gallantry in the presence of the enemy. Medal: Circular in shape and is made of standard silver, and is embossed on the obverse a five pointed heraldic star with the points of the star just touching the rim.

Concept note-3: -Abhinandan Varthaman was born on 21 June 1983 in Tamil Jain Family. Varthaman’s family is from Thirupanamoor, a village about 19 km (12 miles) from Kanchipuram. His father is a retired Indian Air Force (IAF) Air Marshal (OF-8) and his mother is a doctor. Varthaman was educated at Sainik School, Amaravathinagar.
