Who among the following has been selected for the 2018 Saraswati Samman?

(A) Jeet Thayil

(B) Arundhathi Subramaniam

(C) Jayanta Mahapatra

(D) ** K Siva Reddy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prestigious Saraswati Samman, 2018 was conferred to Telugu poet K Siva Reddy, for his collection of poems titled Pakkaki Ottigilite (Turning Aside While Lying Down).

Concept note-2: -Telugu poet K Siva Reddy is to be conferred the prestigious Saraswati Samman 2018, for his collection of poems titled Pakkaki Ottigilite (Turning Aside While Lying Down).

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sharankumar Limbale. Dr Sharan Kumar Limbale has bagged the Saraswati Samman, the country’s highest literary award, for year 2020 for his Marathi novel ‘Sanaatan’ published in 2018.

Concept note-4: -On 7th April 2022,Prof Ramdarash Mishra was awarded by Saraswati Samman 2021 for his hindi poem “Mein to Yahan Hun”.

Concept note-5: -Gujarati poet, playwright and academic Sitanshu Yashaschandra has been selected for the 2017 Saraswati Samman for his collection of verses titled Vakhar.
