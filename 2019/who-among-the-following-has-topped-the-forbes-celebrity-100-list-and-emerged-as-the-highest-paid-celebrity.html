Who among the following has topped the Forbes’ Celebrity 100 list and emerged as the highest-paid celebrity?

(A) Ed Sheeran

(B) Lionel Messi

(C) ** Taylor Swift

(D) Kylie Jenner

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Virat Kohli The cricket star occupies top spot on the 2019 Forbes India Celebrity list, with a prolific run scoring spree and a glut of endorsements.

Concept note-2: -Amitabh Bachchan, Akshay Kumar, Shahrukh Khan, Alia Bhatt, Ranveer Singh, and Hritik Roshan are among the Indian celebrities featuring in the list.

Concept note-3: -The world’s celebrities may be well known for the media they produce, but the bulk of their earnings are made through business dealings. First on the list is New Zealand director Peter Jackson, best known for directing, producing, and writing the screenplays for the Lord of the Rings and Hobbit trilogies.

Concept note-4: -Salman Khan topples Shah Rukh Khan to lead Forbes India Celebrity 100 list with Rs 270.33 cr. However, the 50-year-old Salman is ranked at No 2 in terms of fame, which is topped by ace cricketer Kohli.
