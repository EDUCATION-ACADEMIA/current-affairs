Who among the following is selected for 2019 Dada Sahab Phalke award?

(A) ** Amitabh Bachchan

(B) Rajkumar Hirani

(C) Lata Mangeshkar

(D) Sanjay Leela Bhansali

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Amitabh Bachchan. Amitabh Bachchan to be honoured with Dada Saheb Phalke Award 2019. The Dadasaheb Phalke Award is India’s highest award in cinema.

Concept note-2: -Rajinikanth won the Dadasaheb Phalke Award in 2019 and got awarded in 2021, whereas Asha Parekh won this award in 2020 but will receive it in 2022 due to the COVID pandemic.

Concept note-3: -Reddy (1974) and B. Nagi Reddy (1986); Raj Kapoor (1987) and Shashi Kapoor (2014); Lata Mangeshkar (1989) and Asha Bhosle (2000); B. R. Chopra (1998) and Yash Chopra (2001). Veteran actor Asha Parekh was honoured in the 68th National Film Awards for the year 2020 in a function held in New Delhi, on 30 September 2022.

Concept note-4: -At the Dada Saheb Phalke Film Festival in 2022, Farhan Akhtar won Best Actor for the film Toofan. Asha Parekh, a veteran actress, who arrived wearing a blue saree, was awarded the Outstanding Contribution To The Film Industry.
