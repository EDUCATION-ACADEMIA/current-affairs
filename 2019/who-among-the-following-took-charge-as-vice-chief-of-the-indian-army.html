Who among the following took charge as Vice Chief of the Indian Army?

(A) Lt. Gen. Devraj Anbu

(B) Lt. Gen. Satinder Kumar Saini

(C) Lt. Gen. Abhay Krishna

(D) ** Lt. Gen. Mukund Naravane

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current VCOAS is Lt. General B. S. Raju who assumed the office on 1 May 2022.

Concept note-2: -Notes: Lieutenant General SK Saini has recently been appointed as the new Vice Chief of Army Staff, after the then Army Vice Chief Lt Gen MM Naravane was appointed as the Chief of Army Staff.

Concept note-3: -Lt Gen MV Suchindra Kumar, AVSM, YSM**, VSM, DCOAS (Strat) Lieutenant General MV Suchindra Kumar, Ati Vishisht Seva Medal, Yudh Seva Medal**, Vishisht Seva Medal assumed the appointment of Deputy Chief of Army Staff (Strategy), Integrated Headquarters of Ministry of Defence (Army) on 01 July 2022.

Concept note-4: -Lt Gen Manoj Pande, PVSM, AVSM, VSM took over as the Vice Chief of the Army Staff on 01 February 2022. An alumnus of National Defence Academy, Lt Gen Manoj Pande was commissioned in December 1982 to the Corps of Engineers (The Bombay Sappers).

Concept note-5: -General Manoj Pande is the current Chief of Army Staff and he took over the office on April 30, 2022.
