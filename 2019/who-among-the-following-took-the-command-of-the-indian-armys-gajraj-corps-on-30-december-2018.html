Who among the following took the command of the Indian Army’s Gajraj Corps on 30 December 2018?

(A) Lt Gen Gurpal Singh Sangha

(B) ** Lt Gen Manoj Pande

(C) Lt Gen Ranbir Singh

(D) Lt Gen Manoj Mukund Naravane

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -LT GEN AMARDEEP SINGH BHINDER, AVSM, VSM, GOC-IN-C, SOUTH WESTERN COMMAND. Lieutenant General Amardeep Singh Bhinder, AVSM, VSM took over the command of South Western Command on 1st Apr 2021.

Concept note-2: -Lt General Manoj Kumar Katiyar has been appointed as the next Director-General of Military Operations (DGMO). He will be taking over the new office on May 1, 2022. He is presently the General Officer Commanding of 1 Corps, a strike formation responsible for offensive operations against both Pakistan and China.

Concept note-3: -Lieutenant General Ajai Kumar Singh, AVSM, YSM, SM, VSM, an alumnus of St Gabriel’s Academy Roorkee, National Defence Academy and Indian Military Academy was commissioned into the Seventh Battalion of the Eleventh Gorkha Rifles in December 1984.
