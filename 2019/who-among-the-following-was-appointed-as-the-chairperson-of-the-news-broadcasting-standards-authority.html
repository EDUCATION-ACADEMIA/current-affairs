Who among the following was appointed as the chairperson of the News Broadcasting Standards Authority?

(A) Sharad Arvind Bobde

(B) ** Arjan Kumar Sikri

(C) Madan Lokur

(D) Ranjan Gogoi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution: The correct answer is option 2, i.e. Justice A K Sikri. Justice AK Sikri was recently appointed as the chairperson of the news broadcasting standards authority(NBSA) by the news broadcasters Association(NBA).

Concept note-2: -News Broadcasting Standards Authority is an independent body set up by the News Broadcasters Association. Its task is to consider and adjudicate upon complaints about broadcasts.

Concept note-3: -The News Broadcasting & Digital Standards Authority (NBDSA) has fined a Hindi TV channel for turning a news debate into a “communal issue” and not adhering to guidelines.
