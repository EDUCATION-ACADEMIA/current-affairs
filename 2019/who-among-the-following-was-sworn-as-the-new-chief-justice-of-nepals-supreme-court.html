Who among the following was sworn as the new Chief Justice of Nepal’s Supreme Court?

(A) Justice Deepak Raj Joshee

(B) Justice Gopal Prasad Parajuli

(C) ** Justice Cholendra Shamsher J B Rana

(D) Justice Om Prakash Mishra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hon’ble Chief Justice Mr. Khil Raj Regmi.

Concept note-2: -The Supreme Court is composed of the Chief Justice, twenty Justices. The Supreme Court was formed on 2013 BS (1956 AD). The first Chief Justice of Nepal was Hari Prasad Pradhan. Justices of the Supreme Court hold their office till the age of sixty-five.

Concept note-3: -Justice Dhananjaya Yashwant Chandrachud was sworn in as the 50th Chief Justice of India on November 9, 2022. He will serve as the CJI for two years.

Concept note-4: -Sushila Karki (Nepali: ) (born 7 June 1952 in Biratnagar) is a Nepalese jurist. She is the former Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of Nepal and the only woman to have held the post. Karki became Chief Justice on 11 July 2016.
