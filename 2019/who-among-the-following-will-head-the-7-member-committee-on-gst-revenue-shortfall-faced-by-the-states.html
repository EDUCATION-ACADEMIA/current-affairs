Who among the following will head the 7-member committee on GST Revenue Shortfall faced by the states?

(A) Ram Vilas Paswan

(B) Upendra Kushwaha

(C) Nitish Kumar

(D) ** Sushil Kumar Modi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The GST Council is headed by the Finance Minister.

Concept note-2: -The chairman of the GST Council of India is the Union Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman. The GST Council is a joint forum of the Center and the States, established by the President in accordance with Article 279A(1). The governing body of GST is the GST Council.

Concept note-3: -The GST Council is an apex member committee establishing or modifying any regulation or rule about GST in India.

Concept note-4: -The GST council is headed by the finance minister. The current head of the GST council is Nirmala Sitharaman.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. Late Arun Jaitley was the first chairman of the Goods and Services Tax (GST) Council.
