Who became the first Indian to be crowned Mr. Universe 2019 at the 11th WBPF Championship?

(A) Varinder Ghuman

(B) ** Chitharesh Natesan

(C) Thakur Anoop Singh

(D) Sangram Chougule

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chitharesh Natesan became the first Indian to win ‘Mr. Universe (Pro) 2019’ in 90kg category at the 11th World Bodybuilding and Physique Sports Championship(WBPF) in South Korea.

Concept note-2: -Chitharesh Natesan aka The Indian Monster, (Malayalam: ; born 7 May 1986) is an Indian professional bodybuilder and Mr. Universe of 2019 at the 11th World Bodybuilding and Physique Sports Championship organised by World Bodybuilding And Physique Sports Federation held in South Korea.

Concept note-3: -Monotosh Roy: He won Eastern Indian Bodybuilding Championship in 1939. He obtained the All India Bodybuilding Championship in 1947. Roy moved to the UK in 1951, and took part in the Mr. Universe series.

Concept note-4: -"Consuming the right nutrients post-workout is as important as what you eat before,” said Vikram Jadhav, Indian fitness model and coach, who won the title of Mr India in bodybuilding in the year 2019.

Concept note-5: -The victory makes Chitharesh the first Indian and Malayali to have clinched the title. The muscleman already has quite a few titles to his credit and winning international titles has become a habit.
