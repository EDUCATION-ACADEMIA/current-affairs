Who became the second player in the world to play 100 T20 internationals?

(A) Mihali Raj

(B) Suresh Raina

(C) Jasprit Bumra

(D) ** Rohit Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shoaib Malik (111 games) from Pakistan is the only player to have played more than 100 T20 Internationals. Rohit is currently the top-scorer in this format with 2452 runs with regular skipper Virat Kohli second in the list with an aggregate of 2450 runs.

Concept note-2: -Harmanpreet Kaur, the Indian Women Cricket team Captain has become the 1st Indian cricket player to play 100 T20 International matches. She is the first Indian to achieved this. Harmanpreet achieved this at the final T20 International against South Africa at Surat, India. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -If a player scores 100 runs in a single inning, it is known as a century in a cricket match. Scoring a century in T20I is way too difficult because of the limited-overs format. The first century of the T20 International format was scored by Chris Gayle against South Africa at the inaugural ICC World Twenty20 in 2007.

Concept note-4: -2. Rohit Sharma. Rohit Sharma became the second Indian to hit a T20I century when he smashed a ton against South Africa in Dharamsala. Rohit scored 106 runs to help India post 199 runs on the board, which the Proteas eventually chased thanks to a brilliant innings by J.P. Duminy.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. Stand-in captain Rohit Sharma became the first Indian male cricketer and second in world cricket to complete 100 T20 Internationals.
