Who becomes first batsman to score 20, 000 international runs in a decade?

(A) K. L. Rahul

(B) Rohit Sharma

(C) MS Dhoni

(D) ** Virat Kohli

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kohli becomes first batsman to score 20,000 international runs in a decade.

Concept note-2: -The announcement of the ICC World XI Teams, along with the winners of the individual ICC awards, was made on 28 December 2020. Virat Kohli won Men’s Cricketer of the Decade.

Concept note-3: -Virat Kohli becomes the fastest cricketer to score 20,000 international runs.

Concept note-4: -Indian captain Virat Kohli has become the first batsman to score 20,000 international runs in a single decade, the incredible stat providing another reflection of his greatness. Kohli has aggregated 20,502 runs across three formats out of which he has scored 20,018 in the ongoing decade.
