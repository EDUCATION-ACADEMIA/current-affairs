Who clinched the 2019 men’s singles title at the Barcelona Open Tennis tournament?

(A) ** Dominic Thiem

(B) Rafael Nadal

(C) Alexander Zverev

(D) Daniil Medvedev

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dominic Thiem defeated Daniil Medvedev in the final, 6–4, 6–0, to win the singles title at the 2019 Barcelona Open.

Concept note-2: -Thiem broke the Big Three’s stranglehold on Grand Slam titles when he won the 2020 U.S. Open but a wrist injury in 2021 sidelined him for months and he has struggled to get back to the same level since.

Concept note-3: -All the winners from the 2019 tournament at Roland-Garros. The incredible Rafael Nadal extended his record-breaking reign at Roland-Garros by winning a 12th men’s singles title with a 6-3, 5-7, 6-1, 6-1 victory over Dominic Thiem in the final.

Concept note-4: -Carlos Alcaraz defeated Pablo Carreño Busta in the final, 6–3, 6–2 to win the singles tennis title at the 2022 Barcelona Open.
