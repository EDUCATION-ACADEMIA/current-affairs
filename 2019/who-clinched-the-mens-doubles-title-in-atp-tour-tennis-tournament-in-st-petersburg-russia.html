Who clinched the men’s doubles title in ATP Tour tennis tournament in St. Petersburg, Russia?

(A) ** Divij Sharan

(B) Matteo Berrettini

(C) Luke Bambridge

(D) Artem Sitak

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nadiia Kichinok and Raluca Olaru took home the doubles title.

Concept note-2: -Novak Djokovic defeated Casper Ruud in the final, 7–5, 6–3 to win the singles tennis title at the 2022 ATP Finals.

Concept note-3: -The champion in 2022 will receive $108,000, more than double the amount Daria Kasatkina received last year (+111.8%). The runner up gets a similar pay bump with a cheque for $108,000, a 106.2% increase.

Concept note-4: -The total prize money on offer in St Petersburg for 2021 is $863,705, a drop of -30.6% compared to 2020. In 2020 the event tasted homegrown success with Andrey Rublev taking down the title, following in the footsteps of previous Russian winners Yevgeny Kafelnikov, Marat Safin and Daniil Medvedev.
