Who has become India’s 65th Grand Master?

(A) ** Raunak Sadhwani

(B) V. Anand

(C) Vladimir Kramnik

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Raunak Sadhwani becomes India’s 65th Grandmaster .

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. Raunak Sadhwani has become the 65th Indian Grandmaster at the age of 13 years. Sadhwani is from Nagpur and an International master.

Concept note-3: -Congratulations Harshit Raja – 69th Grandmaster of the country – All India Chess Federation.

Concept note-4: -15-year-old Prithu Gupta becomes India’s 64th Grandmaster – All India Chess Federation.

Concept note-5: -Koustav Chatterjee becomes India’s 78th Grandmaster - ChessBase India.
