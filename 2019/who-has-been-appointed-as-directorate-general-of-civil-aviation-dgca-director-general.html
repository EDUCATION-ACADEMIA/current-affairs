Who has been appointed as Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) director general?

(A) ** Arun Kumar

(B) BL Santosh

(C) S. Raveendran

(D) Amitabh Kant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved the appointment of Vikram Dev Dutt as the next director general in the Directorate of General of Civil Aviation (DGCA). He will take over as the head of the aviation regulator on February 28, 2023. He will be succeeding the incumbent DGCA chief Arun Kumar.

Concept note-2: -The Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) is the regulatory body in the field of Civil Aviation, primarily dealing with safety issues. It is responsible for regulation of air transport services to/from/within India and for enforcement of civil air regulations, air safety, and airworthiness standards.
