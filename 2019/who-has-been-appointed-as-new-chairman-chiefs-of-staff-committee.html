Who has been appointed as new Chairman, Chiefs of Staff Committee?

(A) ** General Bipin Rawat

(B) B S Dhanoa

(C) V.K. Singh

(D) Arup Raha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current holder of the office is General Sahir Shamshad Mirza appointed in 2022.

Concept note-2: -Chairman, Joint Chiefs of Staff General Mark A. Milley is the 20th Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, the nation’s highest-ranking military officer, and the principal military advisor to the President, Secretary of Defense, and National Security Council.

Concept note-3: -The appointment comes more than nine months after the post fell vacant following the death of General Bipin Rawat. The Government on Wednesday, September 28, 2022, appointed former Eastern Army Commander Lt. General Anil Chauhan as the next Chief of Defence Staff (CDS).
