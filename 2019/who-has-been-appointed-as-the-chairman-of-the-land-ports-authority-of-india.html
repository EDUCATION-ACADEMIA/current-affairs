Who has been appointed as the chairman of the Land Ports Authority of India?

(A) Amit Puri

(B) M Vishnu Vardhan Rao

(C) ** Aditya Mishra

(D) Rakesh kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Yashwant Kumar Jain He is holding the charge of Under Secretary (General Administration), Land Port Authority of India.

Concept note-2: -Senior IPS officer Aditya Mishra was appointed as the chairman of the Land Ports Authority of India (LPAI). He is a 1989 batch IPS officer of Uttar Pradesh cadre. He has been appointed for a period of 5 years from the date of assumption of the charge.

Concept note-3: -A 1989 batch IPS officer of UP cadre, Aditya Mishra has been appointed as Chairman, Land Ports Authority of India (LPAI).

Concept note-4: -The Land Ports Authority of India (LPAI) celebrated its 9th Foundation Day today at its Headquarter in New Delhi.
