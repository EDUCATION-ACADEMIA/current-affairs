Who has been conferred with the prestigious Global Teacher Prize for 2019?

(A) Yasodai Selvakumaran

(B) Swaroop Rawal

(C) ** Peter Tabichi

(D) Martin Salvetti

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Peter Tabichi, the 2019 Winner of the Global Teacher Prize from the Varkey Foundation, is a Franciscan Brother who works as a science teacher at Keriko Mixed Day Secondary School, a public school in a remote part of Kenya’s Rift Valley.

Concept note-2: -Global Teacher Prize 2022, Apply for getting prize money of $1 million at globalteacherprize.org. The Varkey Foundation Global Teacher Prize is a $1 million award for an exceptional teacher who has made an outstanding contribution to the profession.

Concept note-3: -Siddhartha Ghosh, Head, Training & Placements and Professor of CSE, received the best teacher’s award 2019 by Indian Servers and Lions Club of Vijayawada on 2nd Oct 2019.

Concept note-4: -KARIMNAGAR: Mr Lalit Mohan Sahu, the senior faculty and Project Based Learning resource person from Paramita Heritage School of Karimnagar town has been honoured with AKS Global Teacher Award for the academic year 2022-23 by AKS global Academy.
