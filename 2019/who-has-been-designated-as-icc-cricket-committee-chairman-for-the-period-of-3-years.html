Who has been designated as ICC Cricket Committee Chairman for the period of 3 years?

(A) Sachin Tendulkar

(B) VVS Laxman

(C) Sourav Ganguly

(D) ** Anil Kumble

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Anil Kumble.

Concept note-2: -Greg Barclay gets second term as ICC chairman, Jay Shah to head F&CA committee. New Zealand’s Greg Barclay was on Saturday unanimously re-elected as chairman of the International Cricket Council (ICC) for a second two-year term.

Concept note-3: -Commenting on his re-appointment Greg Barclay said: “It is an honour to be re-elected as the Chair of the International Cricket Council and I would like to thank my fellow ICC Directors for their support.

Concept note-4: -Greg Barclay has been re-elected as ICC chairman for another two-year term after his rival, Zimbabwe Cricket (ZC) chairman Tavengwa Mukuhlani, pulled out of the contest late on Friday. Barclay, who served his first stint as ICC chair between 2022 and this November, will now be in the position until 2024.
