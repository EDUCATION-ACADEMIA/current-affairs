EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Boxing Association (AIBA) is a non-profit international organization that succeeded the former Fédération Internationale de Boxe Amateur in 1946.

Concept note-2: -On December 13, 2020, AIBA adopted a new constitution. In 2021, Olympic champion, two-time World champion István Kovács was appointed General Secretary of AIBA. Later that year, AIBA appointed Professor Ulrich Haas to lead the AIBA’s Independent Governance Reform Group.
