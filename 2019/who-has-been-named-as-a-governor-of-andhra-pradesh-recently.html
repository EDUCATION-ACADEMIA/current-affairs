Who has been named as a Governor of Andhra Pradesh, recently?

(A) Satyadev Narayan Arya

(B) ** Biswa Bhusan Harichandan

(C) Lalji Tandon

(D) OP Kohli

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hon’ble Governor Sri Biswabhusan Harichandan has congratulated the NSS Programme Officers and NSS Volunteers, for winning awards at the national-level and for participating in the Republic Day Parade held in New Delhi on January 26.

Concept note-2: -The official residence of the governor is the Raj Bhavan, situated in Vijayawada. E. S. L. Narasimhan is the longest serving governor. Biswabhusan Harichandan is the current serving governor.
