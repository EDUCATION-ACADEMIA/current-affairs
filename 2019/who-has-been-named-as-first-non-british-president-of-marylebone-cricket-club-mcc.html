Who has been named as first non-British President of Marylebone Cricket Club (MCC)?

(A) Varun Chopra

(B) Mahela Jayawardene

(C) ** Kumar Sangakkara

(D) David Byas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Former Sri Lanka batsman Kumar Sangakkara became the first non-British president of the prestigious Marylebone Cricket Club.

Concept note-2: -In the 21st century there have been MCC Presidents who as players were wholly professional: Tom Graveney, Derek Underwood, Mike Gatting and Matthew Fleming. The 2018–19 President, Anthony Wreford, nominated Kumar Sangakkara as his successor in May 2019; Sangakkara became MCC’s first non-British President.

Concept note-3: -Former England batter Clare Connor has officially succeeded Sri Lanka legend Kumar Sangakkara as the new president of the MCC.

Concept note-4: -Clare Connor becomes the first female to take office as President of MCC in its 234-year history - Female Cricket.

Concept note-5: -MCC Presidents The current President of MCC is Stephen Fry.
