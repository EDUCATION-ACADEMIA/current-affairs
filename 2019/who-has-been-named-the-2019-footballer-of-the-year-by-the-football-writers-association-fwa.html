Concept note-1: -Liverpool star Mo Salah was voted Footballer of the Year for the second time, having first won the award in 2018. The Egyptian received 48% of the vote beating Kevin De Bruyne (Manchester City) and Declan Rice (West Ham) to first place. He said: “This feels great.
