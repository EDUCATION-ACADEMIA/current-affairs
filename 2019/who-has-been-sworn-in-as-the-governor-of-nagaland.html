Who has been sworn in as the Governor of Nagaland?

(A) ** R.N. Ravi

(B) Ramesh Bais

(C) Anandiben Patel

(D) Keshari Nath Tripathi.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prof Jagdish Mukhi is the 20th Governor of Nagaland and he assumed his office on 17th September 2021, as additional charge. Prof Jagdish Mukhi is the 30th Governor of Assam and he assumed his office on 10th October 2017.

Concept note-2: -The Correct Answer is Vishnu Sahay. The First Governor of Nagaland was Vishnu Sahay. Tenure Period - 1 December 1963 to 16 April 1968.

Concept note-3: -Governor of Assam and Nagaland Prof. Jagdish Mukhi has called on the Indian Red Cross Society Nagaland State branch to play its part in eliminating TB by 2025 as envisaged by the government.

Concept note-4: -Lt. Gen. Gurmit Singh, PVSM, UYSM, AVSM, VSM (Retd.)
