WHO has declared Ebola outbreak in which among the following countries as a global health emergency?

(A) Libya

(B) Nigeria

(C) Egypt

(D) ** DR Congo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ebola virus was first described in 1976 near the Ebola River in what is now the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Since then, the virus has emerged periodically and infected people in several African countries.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Liberia. According to the world health organization, the most affected country by Ebola in Liberia. The largest Ebola outbreak in history was first reported in March 2014 and declared over by the World Health Organization (WHO) on 10 June 2016. Ebola virus disease was first described in 1976.
