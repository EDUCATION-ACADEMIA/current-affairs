Who has launched a Fiscal Performance Index (FPI) to assess state and central budgets?

(A) ** CII

(B) FICCI

(C) IFC

(D) AIMA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) has launched a Fiscal Performance Index (FPI) to assess state and central budgets.

Concept note-2: -The Fiscal Performance Index (FPI) is a composite index that presents the relative performance of state finance. Fiscal performance consists of composite fiscal indicators of the respective states.

Concept note-3: -For 2022-23, the fiscal deficit of the government is estimated to be Rs 16.61 lakh crore or 6.4 per cent of the GDP.

Concept note-4: -The government has budgeted fiscal deficit to be Rs 16.61 lakh crore or 6.4 per cent of the GDP in the current year ending March 2023. The deficit is funded by market borrowings.
