Who has launched smart garbage bins for smart waste management?

(A) ** SAIL

(B) BHEL

(C) ONGC

(D) NMDC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The smart garbage monitoring system is being implemented in local bodies across the State by the Haritha Keralam Mission in association with the Suchitwa Mission, Clean Kerala Company, and the Local Self-Government department (LSGD), as part of introducing digitalisation in waste management.

Concept note-2: -AdvanceTech India Pvt Ltd is delighted to introduce our new product in the Smart Environmental Market. We introduce the SMART BIN that has excellent features that will be fit for Smart Environment Solution.

Concept note-3: -What is a smart waste bin? They are containers created with an intelligent system that uses sensors, image recognition and artificial intelligence to obtain information on the amount of waste it receives, the types of waste and send this data.

Concept note-4: -Steel Authority of India Ltd (SAIL) has launched smart garbage bins made of stainless steel which integrates technology for smart waste management.

Concept note-5: -Fill-level sensors designed specifically for sensing waste levels, like our TrashSonar line, are being used to sense the level of trash bins in order to determine if a can needs to be emptied or not. That means there could be less time on the road for trucks, less need for human resources as well as fuel emissions.
