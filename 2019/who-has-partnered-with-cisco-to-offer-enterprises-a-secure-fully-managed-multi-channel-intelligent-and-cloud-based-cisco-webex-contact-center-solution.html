Who has partnered with Cisco to offer enterprises a secure, fully-managed, multi-channel, intelligent and cloud-based Cisco Webex Contact Center solution?

(A) Mind Tree

(B) Tech Mahindra

(C) Reliance Communications

(D) ** Tata Communications

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cisco kicks off WebexOne 2022 with innovations in the Webex Suite to reimagine workspaces and enable flexible workstyles. Cisco partners with Microsoft to give customers the option to run Microsoft Teams on world-class Cisco collaboration devices.

Concept note-2: -Webex® Contact Center gives you the tools you need to deliver AI-powered, connected customer experiences. Create tailored customer experiences informed by customer experience management. Let customers connect when and how they want – via chat, text, social, email, or call.

Concept note-3: -Webex Edge Connect Edge Connect directly and securely connects your network to the cisco Webex cloud. The dedicated connection to the Webex backbone delivers greater performance, reliability, and security with significant cost savings. Explore benefits.

Concept note-4: -The Webex Setup Assist team accesses the organization and starts configuration of Webex services. Post-configuration, the customer can audit and modify the setup of their services through Control Hub. The Webex Setup Assist team can review configuration changes made by the customer.
