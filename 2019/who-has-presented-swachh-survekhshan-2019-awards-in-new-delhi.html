Who has presented Swachh Survekhshan-2019 Awards in New Delhi?

(A) Shri Rajnath Singh

(B) Shri Narendra Modi

(C) ** Shri Ram Nath Kovind

(D) Shri Venkaiah Naidu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -President Ram Nath Kovind presented Swachh Survekhshan-2019 Awards in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -For the fifth consecutive year, Indore was awarded the title of India’s Cleanest City under Swachh Survekshan, while Surat and Vijayawada bagged the second and third spots respectively in the ‘more than 1 lakh population’ category.

Concept note-3: -Indore has been awarded the cleanest city in the country in the Swachh Survekshan 2019 (SS 2019) awards while Bhopal has been declared as the cleanest capital. Ujjain has bagged the award for being the cleanest city in the population category of 3 lakh to 10 lakh.

Concept note-4: -The Secretary, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), Shri Manoj Joshi has launched the eighth edition of Swachh Survekshan (SS) – SS 2023 under Swachh Bharat Mission Urban 2.0, in a virtual event here today.

Concept note-5: -The Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation had commissioned “Swachh Survekshan Grameen-2019” (SSG 2019) through an independent survey agency to develop ranking of all districts of India on the basis of quantitative and qualitative sanitation (swachhata) parameters.
