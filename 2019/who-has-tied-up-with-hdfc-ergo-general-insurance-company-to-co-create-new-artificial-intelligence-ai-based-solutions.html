Who has tied-up with HDFC ERGO General Insurance Company to co-create new Artificial Intelligence (AI)-based solutions?

(A) ** IBM India

(B) Accenture

(C) TCS

(D) Microsoft

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HDFC ERGO General Insurance, one of India’s leading insurance companies, announced it is partnering with Google Cloud to create a technology platform that will help digitize insurance purchasing and servicing in the country.

Concept note-2: -Notes: India’s third largest non-life private insurer, HDFC ERGO General Insurance has launched the Artificial Intelligence (AI) based tool called IDEAS (Intelligent Damage detection Estimation and Assessment Solution) motor insurance claims.

Concept note-3: -Currently, about 75% of the physical inspections in the company are assisted through the IDEAS tool and have settled more than 20,000 motor claims in the utmost accuracy level.
