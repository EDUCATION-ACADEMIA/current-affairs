Who has won the ACJ Award for Investigative Journalism?

(A) Veena George

(B) ** Nileena M S

(C) Nidhi Razdan

(D) Shereen Bhan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We commend Shyamlal Yadav, Sandeep Singh and The Indian Express for this outstanding work,” it added.

Concept note-2: -ACJ Award for Investigative Journalism This award is to act as a catalyst and inspire more journalists, editors and newsrooms to pursue investigative work and, in the process, raise the standards of journalism and contribute to the public interest.

Concept note-3: -The Pulitzer Prize for Investigative Reporting has been awarded since 1953, under one name or another, for a distinguished example of investigative reporting by an individual or team, presented as a single article or series in a U.S. news publication.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Zishaan A Latif. Zishaan A Latif has been named the winner of the Ramnath Goenka Award in the Photo Journalism category. He documented the plight of people who were dropped from the National Register of Citizens (NRC).

Concept note-5: -Pulitzer Prizes They honor “excellence in journalism” and “prize original reporting, good writing and, where appropriate, visual storytelling."
