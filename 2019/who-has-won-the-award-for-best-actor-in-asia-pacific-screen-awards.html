Who has won the award for Best Actor in ASIA PACIFIC SCREEN AWARDS?

(A) ** Manoj Bajpayee

(B) Ayushman Khurrana

(C) Vicky Kaushal

(D) Amitabh Bachan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajkummar Rao won the 2017 Asia Pacific Screen Awards, in the best actor category for Newton. The movie was produced by Drishyam Films and directed by Masurkar.

Concept note-2: -Brillante Mendoza is the first Filipino director to be honored by France with the title Chevalier dans l’Ordre des Arts et Lettres. His films Kanatay (2009) and Taklub (2015) have won awards at the Cannes Film Festival, and Thy Womb saw him win Achievement in Direction at the 2012 Asia Pacific Screen Awards.

Concept note-3: -Manoj Bajpayee, Best Actor winner.

Concept note-4: -Ganesan was the first Indian actor to win a “Best Actor” award in an International film festival, the Afro-Asian Film Festival held in Cairo, Egypt in 1960.
