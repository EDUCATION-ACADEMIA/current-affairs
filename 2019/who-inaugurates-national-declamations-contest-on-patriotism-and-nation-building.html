Who inaugurates National Declamations Contest on Patriotism and nation building?

(A) Shri Prakash Javadekar

(B) Shri Vikram Sharma

(C) ** Shri Rajyavardhan Rathore

(D) Shri Nitin Gadkari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajyavardhan Rathore has said that patriotism and nation building are two sides of a coin and the nation building is the collaborative effort between the government and citizens.

Concept note-2: -The topic of Declamation Contest has helped a great deal in creating awareness about the Modern Indian thinkers among audiences and about the Prime Minister’s Financial and Social Inclusion Schemes, and denouncing all kinds of social evils as well as promotes the spirit of Patriotism.

Concept note-3: -CONTRIBUTION OF YOUTH IN NATION BUILDING Patriotism means love for country but at the same time it doesn’t mean hatred for others. Now, lets talk about nation building, it refers to the process of engaging all the citizens in the development of nation.

Concept note-4: -Patriotism is the love and pride for one’s nation to an extent of sacrificing even one’s life for the betterment of it. A great sense of patriotism was shown by our freedom fighters during their struggle for independence.

Concept note-5: -People should be concerned about their country’s progress and prosperity. A true patriot always gets respect, love, and affection from other citizens of the country as well. Thank you for being a part of and continue promoting patriotism.
