Who is appointed as New Chairman of ITC?

(A) ** Sanjiv Puri

(B) Vishal Puri

(C) Suresh Rajan

(D) Vikram Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sanjiv Puri (60) is the Chairman & Managing Director of ITC Limited.

Concept note-2: -In a wide-ranging interview ITC chairman and managing director, Sanjiv Puri, tells Ishita Ayan Dutt that the company aspires to be India’s largest FMCG company and hopes to expand margins by 100 bps each year.

Concept note-3: -ITC Ltd to introduce millets into most of its packaged food products, says chairman Sanjiv Puri.

Concept note-4: -Limited in 1974. In recognition of the ITC’s multi-business portfolio encompassing a wide range of businesses, the full stops in the Company’s name were removed effective September 18, 2001. The Company now stands rechristened ‘ITC Limited,’ where ‘ITC’ is today no longer an acronym or an initialised form.

Concept note-5: -While the gross remuneration drawn by ITC Chairman & Managing Director Sanjiv Puri in FY22 was up 5.35 per cent to ₹ 12.59 crore, which includes a consolidated salary of ₹ 2.64 crore, perquisites /other benefits of ₹ 49.63 lakh and a performance bonus of ₹ 7.52 crore.
