Who is elected as PM of Belgium?

(A) Oliver Barrow

(B) Juan Evo Morales

(C) Charles Michel

(D) ** Sophie Wilmes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Usually, it is the formateur of the federal government who then becomes Prime Minister. It usually takes several months of negotiations before the formateur is ready to accept a formal royal invitation to form a government. Per the Constitution, the King’s acts are only valid with the countersignature of a minister.

Concept note-2: -Charles Michel was born in Namur, Wallonia, on 21 December 1975. He is the son of Louis Michel, a former European Commissioner, and Martine Pierre.
