Who is the author of “Mind Master:Winning Lessons from a Champion’s Life”?

(A) Magnus Carlsen

(B) ** Viswanathan Anand

(C) Anish Giri

(D) Fabiano Caruana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vishy Anand on his 50th birthday had the launch of his autobiography, Mind Master - Winning Lessons from a Champion’s Life. It is not just any other autobiography. It is a must read autobiography for everyone by the seven-time World Champion and the flag bearer of the country.

Concept note-2: -It is written by Anand with author-journalist Susan Ninan. Ahead of the 44th FIDE Chess Olympiad, Hachette India on Tuesday announced the expanded paperback edition of five-time world chess champion Viswanathan Anand’s widely acclaimed memoir ‘Mind Master: Winning Lessons from a Champion’s Life’.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Viswanathan Anand. Mind Master is the autobiography of chess legend Viswanathan Anand which is co-authored by sports journalist Susan Ninan. It was released on 8 December 2019 and published by Hachette India.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Mind Master. Mind Master is the name of the autobiography of Viswanathan Anand. Mind Master was co-authored by sports journalist Susan Ninan.

Concept note-5: -Viswanathan Anand, (born December 11, 1969, Madras [now Chennai], India), Indian chess master who won the Fédération Internationale des Échecs (FIDE; international chess federation) world championship in 2000, 2007, 2008, 2010, and 2012. Anand learned to play chess from his mother when he was 6 years old.
