Who is the author of the famous book “Geetanjali”?

(A) Mahatama Gandhi

(B) ** Rabindranath Tagore

(C) Pt. Jawahar Lal Nehru

(D) Indira Gandhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gītāñjali, a collection of poetry, the most famous work by Rabindranath Tagore, published in India in 1910. Tagore then translated it into prose poems in English, as Gitanjali: Song Offerings, and it was published in 1912 with an introduction by William Butler Yeats.

Concept note-2: -Her first story, “Bel Patra” (1987), was published in the literary magazine Hans and was followed by a collection of short stories Anugoonj (1991). The English translation of her novel Mai catapulted her to fame.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Bengali. The Gitanjali was written by a world-famous poet of India Rabindranath Tagore. It is a collection of Bengali poems which is mainly written in the Bengali language which was published on 14th August 1910.
