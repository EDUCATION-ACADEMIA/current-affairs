Who is the author of the novel "Nireeshwaran" which won the best novel award in the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award 2017, announced on 23rd January 2019?

(A) Mariam Dunstan

(B) ** VJ James

(C) Sarika Devi

(D) Parvathy Nair

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Name the author of the novel “Nireeshwaran” that won the best novel award in the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award 2017, announced on 23rd January 2019? Explanation: On 23rd January 2019,Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award 2017 was announced, Malayalam Novelist VJ James’s, Novel Nireeshwaran won the best novel award.

Concept note-2: -The Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award for Novel is an award given every year by the Kerala Sahitya Akademi (Kerala Literary Academy) to Malayalam writers for writing a novel of literary merit. It is one of the twelve categories of the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award.
