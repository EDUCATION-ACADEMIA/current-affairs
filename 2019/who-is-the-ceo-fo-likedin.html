Who is the CEO fo LikedIn?

(A) ** Jeff Weiner

(B) Evan Spiegel

(C) Jan Koum

(D) Kevin Systrom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chief Executive Officer. Ryan Roslansky is the CEO of LinkedIn, the world’s largest and most powerful network of professionals. Ryan joined the company in May 2009 and has since held leadership roles in every part of LinkedIn’s business.

Concept note-2: -In May 2020, the company had around 20,500 employees. LinkedIn’s CEO is Ryan Roslansky.

Concept note-3: -In an email to LinkedIn employees, LinkedIn CEO Jeff Weiner explained why he decided to sell the company to Microsoft.

Concept note-4: -Weiner played an instrumental role in LinkedIn’s acquisition by Microsoft for $26 billion in June 2016. Currently, he is the Executive chairman of Linkedin as in 2022.

Concept note-5: -Who are we? LinkedIn began in co-founder Reid Hoffman’s living room in 2002 and was officially launched on May 5, 2003. Today, LinkedIn leads a diversified business with revenues from membership subscriptions, advertising sales and recruitment solutions under the leadership of Ryan Roslansky.
