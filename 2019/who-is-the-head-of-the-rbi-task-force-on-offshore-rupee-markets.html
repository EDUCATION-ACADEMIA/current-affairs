Who is the head of the RBI task force on offshore rupee markets?

(A) ** Usha Thorat

(B) N S Vishwanathan

(C) Mahesh Kumar Jain

(D) Viral Acharya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Accordingly, RBI has constituted the Task Force on Offshore Rupee Markets. The Task Force will have the following composition: Smt. Usha Thorat, former Deputy Governor, Reserve Bank of India - Chairperson.

Concept note-2: -When currencies are sold within the local market, it is called an onshore market. The onshore market is regulated and monitored by market regulators like RBI and SEBI. But when foreign currencies are exchanged in the overseas market, it’s called offshore market.

Concept note-3: -Mr. Tom Donald is the assign Foreign Agent (foreigner) to monitor your transfer upon the crediting of your account.

Concept note-4: -The report of the Task Force has been published on the RBI website today.
