Who is to be appointed as the Chief of the Air Staff of the Indian Air Force?

(A) Air Chief Marshal Fali Homi Major

(B) Air Chief Marshal Pradeep Vasant Naik

(C) ** Air Marshal Rakesh Kumar Singh Bhadauria

(D) Air Chief Marshal Arup Raha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Appointments to the office of Chief of the Air Staff are made by the Appointments Committee of the Cabinet, which is chaired by the Prime Minister of India. The term length of the Chief of the Air Staff is three years or until the age of 62 of the holder, whichever is earlier.

Concept note-2: -Appointments to the office are made by the Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC). The CAS reaches superannuation upon three years in the office or at the age of 62, whichever is earlier.

Concept note-3: -On 21 September 2021, the Government of India appointed Chaudhari as the next Chief of the Air Staff after the retirement of Air Chief Marshal Rakesh Kumar Singh Bhadauria on 30 September 2021.
