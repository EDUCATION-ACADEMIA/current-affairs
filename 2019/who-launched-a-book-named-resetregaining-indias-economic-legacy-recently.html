(A) Nirmala Sitharaman

(B) Ram Nath Kovind

(C) ** Pranab Mukherjee

(D) Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr Subramanian Swamy is an economist with a PhD from Harvard University, with Nobel Laureate Simon Kuznets and a jointly published research with Nobel Laureate Paul A. Samuelson.

Concept note-2: -Reset by Kurt Andersen: 9781588369642 | PenguinRandomHouse.com: Books.

Concept note-3: -R.C. Dutt is the Author of the Book “Economic History of India."

Concept note-4: -The Indian GDP rose from $266 billion in 1991 (inflation adjusted) to $3 trillion in 2019 while its purchasing power parity rose from $1 trillion in 1991 to $12 trillion in 2019. India continues to face significant challenges, including poverty, malnutrition and unemployment.

Concept note-5: -This is first of the two volumes of the Economic History of India, by ROMESH CHUNDER DUTT who undoubtedly was one of the great figures of his generation in India.
