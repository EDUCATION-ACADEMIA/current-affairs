Who launched a Young Scientist Programme ’YUVIKA’ for Schoolkids?

(A) Microsoft

(B) ** ISRO

(C) DRDO

(D) BARC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Space Research Organisation is organising a special programme for School Children called “Young Scientist Programme” “YUva VIgyani KAryakram” , YUVIKA, to impart basic knowledge on Space Technology, Space Science and Space Applications to the younger students with a preference to rural areas.

Concept note-2: -Indian Space Research Organization, ISRO is going to organise the Young Scientist Programme 2023 or Yuva Vigyani Karyakram (YUVIKA) 2023 this year also. The programme was organized last year to encourage scientific curiosity in the school children to which it received an overwhelming response.

Concept note-3: -YUVIKA 2022: As per the recent updates, the residential training programme, ‘YUva VIgyani KAryakram’ (YUVIKA) organized by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has received over one lakh applications from students from across the country.
