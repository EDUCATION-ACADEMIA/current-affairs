Who of the following has become the wicket-keeper captain with the most number of catches in a match?

(A) Kamran Akmal

(B) Rashid Latif

(C) MS Dhoni

(D) ** Sarfaraz Ahmed

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MS Dhoni holds the record for most dismissals (91) in T20 internationals, with 57 catches and 34 stumpings.

Concept note-2: -Mark Boucher leads with 532 catches as wicket keepers and Rahul Dravid leads with 210 catches as a fielder.

Concept note-3: -MS Dhoni is the best wicketkeeper India has had in Test cricket. He has 294 dismissals in 90 Test matches – 96 more dismissals than the next best. Out of those dismissals, 256 were catches. Known as arguably the best keeper against spinners, MS Dhoni has 38 stumpings to his name in the longer format.
