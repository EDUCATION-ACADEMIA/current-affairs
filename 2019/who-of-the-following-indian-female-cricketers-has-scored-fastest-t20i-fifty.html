Who of the following Indian female cricketers has scored fastest T20I fifty?

(A) Harmanpreet Kaur

(B) ** Smriti Mandhana

(C) Ekta Bisht

(D) Nagarajan Niranjana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This modal can be closed by pressing the Escape key or activating the close button. The record for the fastest fifty in women’s T20Is is held by Sophie Devine, who made an 18-ball half-century against India in 2015.

Concept note-2: -#1 Yuvraj Singh - 12 balls (India vs England; Durban, T20 World Cup 2007) Arguably rated as one of the greatest T20I innings of all time, the dynamic left-handed batter’s record 12-ball fifty included a special feat as well.

Concept note-3: -Among Indians, Smriti Mandhana scored the fastest fifty in women’s T20 internationals. She smashed a 23-ball fifty against England at Commonwealth Games 2022. New Zealand captain Sophie Devine leads the list for the fastest fifty in women’s T20 internationals.

Concept note-4: -Fastest fifty in T20 cricket: Yuvraj Singh’s iconic knock against England still holds strong. Yuvraj Singh scored his half-century in just 12 balls in an ICC T20 World Cup match in 2007. He hit Stuart Broad for six sixes in an over during the knock.
