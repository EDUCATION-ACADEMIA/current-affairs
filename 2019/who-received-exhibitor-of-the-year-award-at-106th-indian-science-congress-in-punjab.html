Who received Exhibitor of the Year Award at 106th Indian Science Congress in Punjab?

(A) BARC

(B) ISRO

(C) HAL

(D) ** DRDO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) pavilion received ‘Exhibitor of the Year Award’ at 106th Indian Science Congress (ISC) held at Lovely Professional University (LPU) in Punjab.

Concept note-2: -Indian Science Congress Association(ISCA) is a premier scientific organisation of India with headquarters at Kolkata, West Bengal. The association started in the year 1914 in Kolkata and it meets annually in the first week of January.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Future India: Science and Technology. The theme of the 106th Indian Science Congress is Future India: Science and Technology.

Concept note-4: -India Science Award is one of the highest and the most prestigious national recognition by the Government of India for outstanding contribution to science. The primary and essential criterion for the award is demonstrated and widely accepted excellence in science.

Concept note-5: -SHANTI SWARUP BHATNAGAR PRIZE FOR SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY It is the highest award for science and technology in India.
