Who received the SASTRA-Ramanujan Award-2019?

(A) Eevin Kalis

(B) ** Adam Harper

(C) Radha Tyagi

(D) Wess Forges

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The SASTRA Ramanujan prize for 2019 was awarded to mathematician Dr. Adam Harper, Assistant Professor with the University of Warwick, England.

Concept note-2: -About SASTRA Ramanujan Prize The award recognizes individuals aging 32 and below who have made significant contributions in the field of mathematics that is broadly influenced by Srinivasa Ramanjuan. The first to receive this award was Manjul Bhargava and Kannan Soundararajan.

Concept note-3: -Yunqing Tang will receive the SASTRA Ramanujan Prize for 2022. Tang is an assistant professor at the University of California, Berkeley, U.S.A. The award is presented annually to a person aged 32 and below for outstanding contributions in the field of mathematics, influenced by Srinivasa Ramanujan in a broad way.

Concept note-4: -The SASTRA Ramanujan Prize for 2022 will be awarded to Yunqing Tang Assistant Professor at the University of California, Berkeley, USA. The award includes a cash prize of $10,000. The SASTRA Ramanujan Prize for 2022 will be awarded to Yunqing Tang Assistant Professor at the University of California, Berkeley, USA.
