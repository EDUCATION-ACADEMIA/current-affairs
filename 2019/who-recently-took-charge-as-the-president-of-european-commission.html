Who recently took charge as the President of European Commission?

(A) Donald Tusk

(B) Jean-Claude Juncker

(C) ** Ursula von der Leyen

(D) Guy Verhofstadt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In July 2019, the European Council nominated Ursula von der Leyen to succeed Jean-Claude Juncker, and she was elected the 13th President of the European Commission by the European Parliament on 16 July.

Concept note-2: -European Commission president – Ursula von der Leyen.

Concept note-3: -The Swedish presidency of the Council of the EU: 1 January - 30 June 2023. During the first half of 2023, Sweden holds the presidency of the Council of the EU. Sweden has identified four priorities for the work under its third presidency of the Council: security - unity.

Concept note-4: -Charles Michel, President of the European Council - Consilium.
