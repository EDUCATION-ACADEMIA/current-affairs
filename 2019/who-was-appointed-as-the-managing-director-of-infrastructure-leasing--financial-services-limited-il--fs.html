Who was appointed as the managing director of Infrastructure Leasing & Financial Services Limited (IL & FS)?

(A) Srinivasan Natarajan

(B) Vineet Nayyar

(C) ** Chandra Shekhar Rajan

(D) Malini Shankar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dileep Vishwakarma. CS Rajan The term of Uday Kotak, who was appointed as chairman of crisis-hit Infrastructure Leasing and Finance Ltd (IL&FS) is ending this week and the Union government has appointed CS Rajan, who is managing director (MD), as the new chairman for six months.

Concept note-2: -LIC is already the largest shareholder in IL&FS with a 25% stake, which it may raise. Apart from LIC, India’s largest public sector bank State Bank of India is also likely to participate in the rights issue. “The IL&FS Group is involved in many infrastructure projects including through equity and debt financing.

Concept note-3: -In pursuant to the acquisition of IL&FS Mutual Fund by UTI Mutual Fund, all the schemes of IL&FS mutual fund will be transferred to UTI mutual fund with effect from July 05, 2004.
