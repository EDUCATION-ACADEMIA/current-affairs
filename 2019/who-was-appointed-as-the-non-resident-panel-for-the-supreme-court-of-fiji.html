Who was appointed as the non-resident panel for the Supreme Court of Fiji?

(A) Uday Lalit

(B) Kurian Joseph

(C) Jasti Chelameswar

(D) ** Madan Bhimrao Lokur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lokur - a former judge of the Supreme Court of India - as a judge of the non-resident panel of the Supreme Court of Fiji.

Concept note-2: -Former Judge of Supreme Court of India Justice Madan B Lokur has been re-appointed as a judge of the non-resident panel of the Supreme Court of Fiji.

Concept note-3: -Justice Madan Bhimarao Lokur (born 31 December 1953) is an Indian jurist. He is a Judge of Supreme Court of Fiji.

Concept note-4: -Parbati Kumar Goswami (born 1 January 1913-1992) was an Indian Jurist, acting Governor of Assam and Nagaland and former Judge of the Supreme Court of India. He was the first Assamese Justice in Supreme Court of India.

Concept note-5: -Current Chief Justice Kamal Kumar is the acting Chief Justice.
