(A) Ben Stokes

(B) Kane Williamson

(C) ** Eoin Morgan

(D) Jason Roy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first team to win the T20 World Cup, led by MS Dhoni was India. India won the first T20 World Cup in 2007 by defeating Pakistan in the final match. India only lost one game during the tournament, against New Zealand in the Super 8s. Shahid Afridi of Pakistan was named Player of the Series in this tournament.
