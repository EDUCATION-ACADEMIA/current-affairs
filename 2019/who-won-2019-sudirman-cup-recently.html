Who won 2019 Sudirman Cup, recently?

(A) Russia

(B) Brazil

(C) Japan

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The tournament was played in Nanning, China , between 19 and 26 May 2019. Korea is the defending champion. China defeated Japan 3–0 in the final to win the tournament.

Concept note-2: -India made badminton history, wins first-ever Thomas Cup title 2022.

Concept note-3: -Bangkok was named as the host in November 2018 during BWF Council meeting at Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, where the BWF also announced the host for 18 major events, including Thomas and Uber Cup, Sudirman Cup, BWF World Championships, BWF World Junior Championships, and BWF World Senior Championships from 2019 to 2025.

Concept note-4: -The overall win was sealed for South Korea when Sung Ji Hyun overcame Ratchanok Intanon 21-13, 21-17 in the fourth match. Sung won 42 rallies during the match, according to the tournament’s official website.
