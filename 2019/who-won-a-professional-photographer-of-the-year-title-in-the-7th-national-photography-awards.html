(A) Shri V. Ravi Kumar

(B) Shri Gurdeep Dhiman

(C) ** Shri SL Shanth Kumar

(D) Shri Manish Jaisi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Professional Photographer of the Year: Shri SL Shanth Kumar. Amateur Photographer of the Year: Shri Gurdeep Dhiman.

Concept note-2: -It was inaugurated in 1942 and replaced by two photojournalism prizes in 1968: the Pulitzer Prize for Feature Photography and “Pulitzer Prize for Spot News Photography", which was later renamed Pulitzer Prize for Breaking News Photography in 2000.
