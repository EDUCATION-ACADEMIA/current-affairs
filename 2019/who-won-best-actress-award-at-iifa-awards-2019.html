Who won Best Actress Award at IIFA Awards 2019?

(A) Deepika Padukone

(B) ** Alia Bhatt

(C) Katrina Kaif

(D) Sara Ali Khan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IIFA Awards 2019: Ranveer Singh wins best actor for Padmaavat, Alia Bhatt bags best actress for Raazi.

Concept note-2: -The 20th edition of the International Indian Film Academy (IIFA) Awards was attended by scores of Bollywood celebrities. While Ranveer Singh walked away with the best actor trophy for Padmaavat, Alia Bhatt won the best actress for her role in Raazi.

Concept note-3: -Some of the biggest winners of the night were Ranveer Singh (Best Actor for Padmaavat), Alia Bhatt (Best Actress for Raazi), Sriram Raghavan (Best Director for Andhadhun) and Raazi (Best Film).

Concept note-4: -Uttarakhand CM congratulates Jubin Nautiyal for winning ‘Best Playback Singer Male’ at IIFA 2022.
