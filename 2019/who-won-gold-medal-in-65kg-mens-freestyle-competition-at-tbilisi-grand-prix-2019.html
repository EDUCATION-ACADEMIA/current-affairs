Who won gold medal in 65kg men’s freestyle competition at Tbilisi Grand Prix 2019?

(A) Saurav Gurjar

(B) Sushil Kumar

(C) Yogeshwar Dutt

(D) ** Bajrang Punia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bajrang, who had won a gold at the same event last year as well, prevailed 2-0 over Iran’s Peiman Bibyani in the 65kg final of the men’s freestyle competition in the capital city of Georgia.

Concept note-2: -India’s Bajrang Punia clinches gold in men’s 65kg wrestling, completes hat-trick of Commonwealth Games medals.

Concept note-3: -Indian wrestler Bajrang Punia won the gold medal at Tbilisi Grand Prix of V. Balavadze and G. Kartozia in Georgia with a 9-3 win over Iran’s Mehran Akbar Nasiriafrachali in the 65kg category final.

Concept note-4: -India’s Bajrang Punia won a bronze medal at the World Wrestling Championships 2022. Get full list of Indian medal winners in Belgrade.
