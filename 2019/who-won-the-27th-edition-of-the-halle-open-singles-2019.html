Who won the 27th edition of the Halle Open Singles 2019?

(A) David Goffin

(B) ** Roger Federer

(C) Karen Khachanov

(D) Andreas Seppi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Roger Federer won a record-extending tenth singles title in Halle, defeating David Goffin in the final, 7–6, 6–1.

Concept note-2: -Located in Halle, Germany, the Halle Open is an annual ATP 500 tennis tournament in June that takes place at the Owl Arena, previously known as the Gerry Weber Stadion. For 2022, the total prize money for the event is €2,134,520, which converts to $2,223,209.

Concept note-3: -The Halle Open is a men’s tennis tournament held in Halle, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. Held since 1993, the event is played on four outdoor grass courts and is a part of the ATP Tour 500 series on the ATP Tour schedule. Between 1993 and 2018 it was sponsored by Gerry Weber.

Concept note-4: -The 2022 Halle Open (known for sponsorship reasons as the Terra Wortmann Open) was a tennis tournament played on outdoor grass courts. It was the 29th edition of the Halle Open and part of the ATP Tour 500 series of the 2022 ATP Tour.
