Who won the 37th Athens Classic Marathon?

(A) Felicien Muhitira

(B) ** John Kipkorir Komen

(C) Costas Gelaouzos

(D) Daniel Muindi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Charalampos Pitsolis wins the Athens Marathon That broke not only his own record but also that of his countryman, Konstantinos Gkelaouzos. Congratulations to Xaralampos Pitsolis for winning the 39th #AthensMarathon!

Concept note-2: -A 8 hours time limit has been set for the Marathon Race participants to reach the finish line. In case a participant has not reached the finish line by that time (see Time Limit section below), he/she needs to stop and will be taken to the Finish by vehicles of the Organizing Committee.

Concept note-3: -More than 60,000 runners from 120 countries will join this unique event that celebrates sports, culture and human values, drawing the world attention to Athens.

Concept note-4: -It is perhaps the most difficult major marathon race: the course is uphill from the 10 km mark to the 31 km mark – the toughest uphill climb of any major marathon. The course begins in the town of Marathon, where it passes the tomb of the Athenian soldiers, and it traces a path near the coast through Nea Makri.
