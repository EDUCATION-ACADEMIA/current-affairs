Who won the Crystal Award for raising mental health awareness?

(A) Kareena Kapoor

(B) ** Deepika Padukone

(C) Kangana Ranaut

(D) Kriti Sanon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which Indian actress won the Crystal Award for raising mental health awareness? Notes: Actress Deepika Padukone has been honoured by the 26th Annual Crystal Award for raising mental health awareness.

Concept note-2: -Deepika Padukone became the only Indian actress to receive WEF’s Crystal Award.

Concept note-3: -Shahrukh received the award along side Elton John and Hollywood actress Cate Blanchett. ‘’Honoured to receive the World Economic Forum’s 24th Crystal Award, together with Elton John and Cate Blanchett.

Concept note-4: -The Crystal Award is presented at Davos each year by Hilde Schwab, Chairwoman and Co-Founder of the World Economic Forum’s World Arts Forum.

Concept note-5: -The Women in Film Crystal + Lucy Awards-first presented in 1977 by the now–Los Angeles chapter of the Women in Film organization-were presented to honor women in communications and media.
