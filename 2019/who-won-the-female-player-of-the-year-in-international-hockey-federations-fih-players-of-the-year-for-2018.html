Who won the Female Player of the Year in International Hockey Federation’s (FIH) Players of the Year for 2018?

(A) ** Eva de Goede

(B) Hilary Knight

(C) Meghan Duggan

(D) Caroline Ouellette

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2018 edition of the FIH Hockey World Cup was won by Belgium. Netherlands and Australia followed in the second and third places.

Concept note-2: -The International Hockey Federation (FIH) can reveal that Felice Albers of Netherlands and Harmanpreet Singh of India have been named the FIH Players of the Year 2021-22 in the women’s and men’s category respectively.

Concept note-3: -Felice Albers of Netherlands is women’s Player of the Year In women’s category, Felice Albers of Netherlands was named the FIH Player of the Year.

Concept note-4: -FIH Player of the Year 2021-22: India’s Harmanpreet Singh wins men’s award. The Indian hockey star also won the award last year. Harmanpreet Singh holds the record for the most goals scored in an FIH Pro League season.
