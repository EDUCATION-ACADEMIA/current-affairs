Who won the FICCI CSR Award for Women Empowerment?

(A) Adani Group

(B) Vedanta Limited

(C) L & T

(D) ** JSPL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ambuja Cements wins FICCI CSR Award in Women Empowerment Category. Ambuja Cements has won the ‘Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FICCI) CSR Award 2020-2021’ in the ‘Women Empowerment Category.

Concept note-2: -The aim of the award is to identify and recognise the efforts of companies in integrating and internalising Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR). Over the years, a number of companies have been recognised by the FICCI CSR Award for their exemplary work in the area of CSR. needs to be submitted by 11 March 2022.

Concept note-3: -The ‘FICCI Corporate Social Responsibility Awards’ has the following categories: Category 1: FICCI CSR Award for Women Empowerment. Category 2: FICCI CSR Award for Skill Development and Livelihood. Category 3: FICCI CSR Award for Education.

Concept note-4: -About Us. Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India has instituted National Corporate Responsibility (CSR) Awards to recognize companies that have made a positive impact on the society through their innovative & sustainable CSR initiatives. This award is the apex recognition of CSR initiatives in India.
