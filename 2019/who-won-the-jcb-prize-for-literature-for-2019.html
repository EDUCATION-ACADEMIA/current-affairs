Who won the JCB Prize for Literature for 2019?

(A) Chandrahas Choudhury

(B) Nayantara Sahgal

(C) Rana Dasgupta

(D) ** Madhuri Vijay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Madhuri Vijay. U.S.-based-Indian author Madhuri Vijay’s debut novel, “The Far Field” bagged this year’s JCB prize for Literature, the most expensive Indian award for writing. She also received Rs 25 lakh as the prize money and a sculpture.
