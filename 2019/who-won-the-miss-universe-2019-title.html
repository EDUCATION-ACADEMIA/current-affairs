Who won the Miss Universe 2019 title?

(A) Demi-Leigh

(B) Iris Mittenaere

(C) ** Zozibini Tunzi

(D) Catriona Gray

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At the end of the event, Catriona Gray of the Philippines crowned Zozibini Tunzi of South Africa as Miss Universe 2019.

Concept note-2: -South Africa wins Miss Universe 2019 crown; India’s Vartika Singh in top 20.

Concept note-3: -Apart from the crown, DNA reported that the new Miss Universe will receive an annual salary of $250,000 to carry out her responsibilities.

Concept note-4: -Punjab-born Harnaaz Sandhu was crowned Miss Universe 2021 in Israel. She became the third Indian after Lara Dutta and Sushmita Sen to win the major title.

Concept note-5: -Vartika Brij Nath Singh is an Indian model and beauty pageant titleholder who was appointed as Miss Universe India 2019 and represented India at 68th edition of the Miss Universe pageant.
