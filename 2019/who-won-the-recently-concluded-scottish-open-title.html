Who won the recently concluded Scottish Open Title?

(A) Anand Pawar

(B) Kiran George

(C) Luka Wraber

(D) ** Lakshya Sen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gary Wilson clinched the first title of his career at the age of 37 when beating Joe O’Connor in the final of the Scottish Open at the Meadowbank Sports Centre.

Concept note-2: -The reigning champion is Cameron Smith, who won the 2022 Open at St Andrews with a score of 268.

Concept note-3: -Gary Wilson won his maiden ranking title when claiming victory at the Scottish Open, beating Joe O’Connor 9-2 in a one-sided final in Edinburgh.

Concept note-4: -Gary Wilson won the 2022 BetVictor Scottish Open, beating Joe O’Connor 9-2 in the final. For the match report click here. The world ranking event was staged at the Meadowbank Sports Centre for the first time, and it was the first professional snooker event in Edinburgh since 2003.

Concept note-5: -The total purse for the 2022 Genesis Scottish Open was $8 million. The winner’s payout for the 2022 Genesis Scottish Open was $1.4 million. Xander Schauffele was the champion.
