Who won the World Sportsman of the Year award at the 2019 Laureus World Sports Honours?

(A) M S Dhoni

(B) Tiger Woods

(C) Virat Kohli

(D) ** Novak Djokovic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -02. 2019. World No. 1 Novak Djokovic was named the Laureus World Sportsman of the Year for the fourth time in a ceremony in Monaco on Monday.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 award was won by Dutch racing driver Max Verstappen.

Concept note-3: -Sachin Tendulkar won the ‘Laureus Sporting Moment 2000-2020’ award for the images of him being carried around by his team-mates after India won the ICC World Cup 2011.

Concept note-4: -Yuwa, a Jharkhand-based NGO which works for girl empowerment won the Laureus Sport for Good Award, becoming the third Indian entry to bag the honour.
