Who won Women’s Singles title in YONEX All England Open Badminton Championships 2019?

(A) ** CHEN Yufei

(B) Tai Tzu-ying

(C) Jia Yifan

(D) Wakana Nagahara

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Who won the women’s singles title at the recently concluded All England Badminton Championships? Answer. The answer is Akane Yamaguchi.

Concept note-2: -It was a part of the All England Open championships, which had been held since 1899. In the women’s category, Tai Tzu-Ying won the championship. Viktor Axelsen is a Danish badminton player.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Prakash Padukone. On March 23, 1980, Prakash Padukone became the first Indian to win the All England Badminton Championships. Padukone clashed with Indonesia’s Liem Swie King in the summit clash at the Wembley Stadium. Padukone defeated his opponent 15-3, 15-10.

Concept note-4: -Japan’s Yamaguchi Akane is All England women’s singles champion after a 21-15, 21-15 victory over South Korea’s An Seyoung in 43 minutes.
