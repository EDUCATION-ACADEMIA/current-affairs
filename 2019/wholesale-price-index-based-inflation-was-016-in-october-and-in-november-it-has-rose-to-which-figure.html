Wholesale price index-based inflation was 0.16% in October and in November it has rose to which figure?

(A) 0.32%

(B) 0.49%

(C) ** 0.58%

(D) 0.64%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The rate of inflation based on WPI Food Index decreased from 6.48% in October, 2022 to 2.17% in November, 2022.

Concept note-2: -(Current Price / Base Period Price) × 100 is the formula used to calculate the Wholesale Price Index (WPI).

Concept note-3: -A wholesale price index (WPI) measures overall change in producer prices over time. It is a measure of inflation based on the prices of goods before they reach consumers.

Concept note-4: -Definition: Wholesale Price Index (WPI) represents the price of goods at a wholesale stage i.e. goods that are sold in bulk and traded between organizations instead of consumers. WPI is used as a measure of inflation in some economies. Description: WPI is used as an important measure of inflation in India.
