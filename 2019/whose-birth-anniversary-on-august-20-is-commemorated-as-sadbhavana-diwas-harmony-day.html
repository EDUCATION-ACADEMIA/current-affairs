Whose birth anniversary on August 20 is commemorated as Sadbhavana Diwas (harmony day)?

(A) Morarji Desai

(B) Lal Bahadur Shastri

(C) Indira Gandhi

(D) ** Rajiv Gandhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The birth anniversary of former Prime Minister Late Shri Rajiv Gandhi is observed as ‘Sadbhavana Diwas’ on 20th August every year. The theme of Sadbhavana is to promote National Integration and Communal Harmony among people of all religions, languages and regions.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is ‘Sadbhavana Divas. ‘ 20th August is the birth anniversary of former Prime Minister ‘Rajiv Gandhi’. Sadbhavana Diwas is celebrated on 20th August to appreciate his efforts in Indian development.

Concept note-3: -Sadbhavana Diwas 2022 Theme Every year Sadbhavana Diwas is celebrated on August 20 and a specific theme is allotted. The Sadbhvana Diwas 2022 Theme is to “promote National Integration and Communal Harmony among people of all religions, languages, and regions".

Concept note-4: -It is given on 20 August, the birth anniversary of Rajiv Gandhi, which is celebrated as Sadbhavna Diwas (Harmony Day).
