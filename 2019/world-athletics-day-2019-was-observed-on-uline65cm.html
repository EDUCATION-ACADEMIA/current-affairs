World Athletics Day 2019 was observed on ______

(A) May 4

(B) May 5

(C) May 6

(D) ** May 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Every year on May 7, World Athletics Day is commemorated. On this day, the International Association of Athletics Federations (IAAF), formerly the International Amateur Athletic Federation, was created as a social responsibility project called ‘Athletics for a Better World’.

Concept note-2: -7 May – World Athletics Day In 1996, the then-President of the International Amateur Athletic Federation (IAAF), Primo Nebiolo, introduced the World Athletics Day to promote athletics amongst youngsters and encourage the new generations to pursue their passion for sports.

Concept note-3: -World athletics day is celebrated on the 7th of May every year.

Concept note-4: -World Athletics Day 2022: Objectives To establish a firm link between the youth, sport, and environmental conservation.
