World Bee Day was observed on ______

(A) ** 20th May

(B) 19th May

(C) 18th May

(D) 17th May

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of this year’s World Bee Day event is “Bee Engaged: Celebrating the diversity of bees and beekeeping systems".

Concept note-2: -World Bee Day was first inaugurated on 20th May 2018 and is celebrated every year ever since. The date for World Bee Day was picked as 20th May because, on this day, an apiculture pioneer Anton Janša was born hailing from a beekeepers family in Slovenia.

Concept note-3: -The birth anniversary of Slovenian beekeeper Anton Jansa (1734-1773), the forefather of modern beekeeping, is celebrated on May 20 as World Bee Day.

Concept note-4: -To raise awareness of the importance of pollinators, the threats they face and their contribution to sustainable development, the UN designated 20 May as World Bee Day.
