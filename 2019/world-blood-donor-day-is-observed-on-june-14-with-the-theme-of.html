World Blood Donor Day is observed on June 14 with the theme of;

(A) Safe Blood For All

(B) ** Blood Donation and Universal Access to Safe Blood Transfusion

(C) Give Blood. Give Now. Give Often

(D) Blood connects us all

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The event serves to raise awareness of the need for safe blood and blood products and to thank voluntary, unpaid blood donors for their life-saving gifts of blood.

Concept note-2: -For 2022, the World Blood Donor Day slogan is “Donating blood is an act of solidarity. Join the effort and save lives” to draw attention to the roles that voluntary blood donations play in saving lives and enhancing solidarity within communities.

Concept note-3: -The World Health Organization initiated and declared June 14, the birth anniversary of Karl Landsteiner, as World Blood Donor Day in 2004.

Concept note-4: -HOST FOR WORLD BLOOD DONOR DAY 2022 EVENTS Mexico will host World Blood Donor Day 2022 through its National Blood Centre. The global event will be held in Mexico City on 14 June 2022.

Concept note-5: -World Blood Donor Day is celebrated every year on June 14. For 2021, the World Blood Donor Day slogan will be “Give blood and keep the world beating”.
