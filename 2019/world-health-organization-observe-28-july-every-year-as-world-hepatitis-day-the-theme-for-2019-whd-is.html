World Health Organization observe 28 July every year as “World Hepatitis Day”. The theme for 2019 WHD is;

(A) Test. Treat. Hepatitis

(B) ** Invest in eliminating hepatitis

(C) Hepatitis:Think Again

(D) Eliminate Hepatities

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2019: Invest in eliminating hepatitis. 2020: Hepatitis Free Future. 2021: Hepatitis can’t wait. 2022: Bringing Hepatitis care closer to you.

Concept note-2: -During World Hepatitis Day 2019 campaign, WHO is urging all countries and partners to promote the theme “Invest in eliminating hepatitis”. WHO will release new estimates for additional investments needed to achieve globally agreed hepatitis elimination goals by 2030, in the context of the universal health coverage.

Concept note-3: -This year’s theme, “Bringing hepatitis care closer to you”, aims to raise awareness about the need to simplify and bring hepatitis care to primary health facilities, community-based venues and locations beyond hospital sites, so that care is closer to communities and people wherever they are.

Concept note-4: -World Hepatitis Day (WHD) is recognized annually on July 28th, the birthday of Dr. Baruch Blumberg (1925–2011). Dr. Blumberg discovered the hepatitis B virus in 1967, and 2 years later he developed the first hepatitis B vaccine.
