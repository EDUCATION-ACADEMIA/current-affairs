CURRENT AFFAIRS-2019
[SOURCES]

World Youth Skills Day was observed on July 15 with the theme of;

(A) ** Learning to learn for life and work

(B) Improving the image of TVET

(C) Youth skills for work and life

(D) SkillsChangeLives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2014, the United Nations General Assembly declared 15 July as World Youth Skills Day, to celebrate the strategic importance of equipping young people with skills for employment, decent work and entrepreneurship.

Concept note-2: -The celebrations of World Youth Skills Day 2022 highlighted the ongoing focus on the Transforming Education Summit (September 2022), and contributed to the work being done under its Thematic Action Track “Learning and skills for life, work, and sustainable development”.

Concept note-3: -Intergenerational Solidarity: Creating a World for All Ages. the objective of International Youth Day 2022 is to amplify the message that action is needed across all generations to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and leave no one behind.

Concept note-4: -World Youth Skills Day 2023 Theme Every year, World Youth Skills Day holiday is observed with a certain theme set by the United Nations. The theme of World Youth Skills Day 2022 was ‘Transforming youth skills for the future’.

Concept note-5: -2020: Skills for a Resilient Youth. 2019: Youth Skills for Sustainability and Innovation. 2018: Innovation and Sustainability. 2017: Skills for the Future of Work.
