According to APEDA, how many states have finalised their action plan to boost agricultural exports?

(A) 5

(B) 6

(C) 7

(D) ** 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -8 states finalise action plan for agriculture export policy: Government.

Concept note-2: -Sixteen States viz. Maharashtra, U.P., Kerala, Nagaland, Tamil Nadu, Assam, Punjab, Karnataka, Gujarat, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Manipur, Sikkim & Uttarakhand and 1 Union Territory viz. Ladakh have finalized the State/UT specific Action Plan.

Concept note-3: -The Government of Maharashtra launched the state’s Agriculture Export Policy (AEP) on Friday, which will focus on export promotion of 21 agricultural commodities.

Concept note-4: -The Agriculture Export Policy is framed with a focus on agriculture export oriented production, export promotion, better farmer realization and synchronization within policies and programmes of Government of India.
