(A) 6th

(B) ** 8th

(C) 7th

(D) 5th

Concept note-1: -According to The International Farm Comparison Network (IFCN) Dairy Processors Report 2020 (Biennial), Amul (Anand Milk Union Limited) a brand of dairy cooperative major Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation (GCMMF) emerged as the 8th Largest Milk Processor globally in the list of Top 20. i.

Amul spurred India’s White Revolution, which made the country the world’s largest producer of milk and milk products.
