According to Status of the Leopards in India, which state has recorded the most number of Leopards in 2020?

(A) ** Madhya Pradesh

(B) Karnataka

(C) Maharashtra

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Of the 12, 852 leopards, Madhya Pradesh tops the list with 3, 421 leopards and is followed by Karnataka with 1, 783 leopards and Maharashtra with 1, 690.

Concept note-2: -The South Indian states of Karnataka, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu are home to nearly 44% of the elephants, 35% of the tigers and 31% of the leopards in India. The state of Karnataka alone is home to 22% of the elephants, 18% of the tigers and 14% of the leopards in India.
