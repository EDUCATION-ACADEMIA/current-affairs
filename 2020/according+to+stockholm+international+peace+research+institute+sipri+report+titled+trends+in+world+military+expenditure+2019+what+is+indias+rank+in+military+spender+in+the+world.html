According to Stockholm International Peace Research Institute (SIPRI) report titled “Trends in World Military Expenditure, 2019”, what is India’s rank in military spender in the world?

(A) 1

(B) 6

(C) ** 3

(D) 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United States was the top nation in terms of military expenditure, spending $801 billion to make up almost 38% of global military spending in 2021. America has been the top military spending nation since SIPRI began tracking in 1949, making up more than 30% of the world’s military spending for the last two decades.
