According to Google, it had blocked and removed how many ‘bad advertisements’ globally in 2019?

(A) 1.7 billion

(B) 3.7 billion

(C) 6.7 billion

(D) ** 2.7 billion

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2019, we blocked and removed 2.7 billion bad ads-that’s more than 5, 000 bad ads per minute.

Concept note-2: -Google also tripled the number of account-level suspensions for advertisers in the last year. 3.4 billion ads blocked or removed. The top three categories of bad ads were abusing the ad network (652 million); adult content (287 million); and trademark (137 million).

Concept note-3: -On Google. To block an ad on Google service, like Search and YouTube: On the ad, select More . Select Block ad .

Concept note-4: -Click the search icon in the taskbar and type Notifications. Click Notifications & action settings. Under Notifications from apps and other senders, find Google Chrome and turn off the toggle next to it to stop the ads.
