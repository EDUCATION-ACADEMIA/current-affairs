According to the State Bank of India’s Ecowrap report India’s GDP is estimated to grow at what percent in the last quarter of FY20?

(A) 2.8%

(B) 3.2%

(C) 1.8%

(D) ** 1.2%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s output will expand 7% in fiscal year 2022-2023 and 6% in next fiscal year, ” S&P Global Ratings Asia-Pacific chief economist Louis Kuijs said. Meanwhile, SBI Research expects FY23 economic growth at 6.8% as against the RBI’s projection of 7%. In addition, it estimated Q2FY23 growth at 5.8%.

Concept note-2: -The Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) has downgraded India’s GDP growth forecast to 6.6% for FY 2022-23 from 6.9% projected earlier on moderation in exports and domestic demand and inflation crimping private consumption.

Concept note-3: -India’s real GDP is expected to grow by seven per cent in 2022-23 and by 6.4 per cent in 2023-24, as per Morgan Stanley.
