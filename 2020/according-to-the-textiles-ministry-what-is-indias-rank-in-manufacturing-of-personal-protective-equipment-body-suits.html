According to the Textiles Ministry what is india’s rank in manufacturing of Personal Protective Equipment body suits?

(A) 3

(B) ** 2

(C) 5

(D) 1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India is the World’s 2nd Largest PPE Manufacturer in the World in Just 60 Days.

Concept note-2: -1. Honeywell International, Inc. Founded 115 years ago by Mark C. Honeywell, the company is a leading provider of safety and productivity solutions (SPS), aerospace, performance materials & technologies (PMT), and building technologies.

Concept note-3: -India became the second largest country to export PPEs to the world, punching a production of around 4 lac PPEs per day. With increased demand from both the domestic and overseas markets, the industry grew to cross a Rs. 7000 Cr mark and is anticipated to grow steadily.

Concept note-4: -Mallcom (India) Ltd. is India’s leading Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) brand. Having a presence in more than 50 countries in 6 continents, Mallcom has established itself as the integrated manufacturer and distributor for head to toe protection and is a valued partner for major importers in their brand name.

Concept note-5: -Personal protective equipment (PPE) refers to specialized garments or accessories such as clothing, helmets, gloves and so forth that fulfil a protective function against various hazards, mechanical or chemical, when worn by workers.
