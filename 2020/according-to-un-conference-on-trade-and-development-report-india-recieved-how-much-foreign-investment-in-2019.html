According to UN Conference on Trade and Development report India recieved how much foreign investment in 2019?

(A) USD 71 billion

(B) ** USD 51 billion

(C) USD 61 billion

(D) USD 41 billion

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India received USD 51 billion in foreign investment in 2019 and was the world’s 9th largest recipient of foreign direct investments (FDI) in 2019, according to a report by the UN’s trade body.

Concept note-2: -Singapore (27.01%), USA (17.94%), Mauritius (15.98%), Netherland (7.86%) and Switzerland (7.31%) emerge as top 5 countries for FDI equity inflows into India FY 2021-22.

Concept note-3: -India foreign direct investment for 2019 was $50.61B, a 20.17% increase from 2018. India foreign direct investment for 2018 was $42.12B, a 5.38% increase from 2017. India foreign direct investment for 2017 was $39.97B, a 10.1% decline from 2016.

Concept note-4: -India has so far received healthy foreign direct investment (FDI) in 2022. As per the latest figures of the government, India has registered its highest-ever total FDI inflows of $84.84 billion in 2021-22.
