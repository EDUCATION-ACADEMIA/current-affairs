Actor Sir Sean Connery died at 90, he is the famous character of which movie?

(A) Iron Man

(B) Harry Potter

(C) ** James Bond

(D) Batman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sir Sean Connery (born Thomas Connery; 25 August 1930 – 31 October 2020) was a Scottish actor. He was the first actor to portray fictional British secret agent James Bond on film, starring in seven Bond films between 1962 and 1983.

Concept note-2: -Mr. Connery’s last movie was one of his lesser ones: “The League of Extraordinary Gentlemen” (2003), an unsuccessful screen adaptation of a clever comic-book series about a group of Victorian heroes.

Concept note-3: -He died on Oct. 31 at around 1 a.m. at his home in the Bahamas. “He died peacefully in his sleep surrounded by family, ” Connery’s rep told Fox News on behalf of his wife, Micheline Roquebrune, and his two sons, Jason and Stephane, at the time of his death.

Concept note-4: -Sean Connery, in full Sir Sean Connery, original name Thomas Connery, (born August 25, 1930, Edinburgh, Scotland-died October 31, 2020, Nassau, Bahamas), Scottish-born actor whose popularity in James Bond spy thrillers led to a successful decades-long film career.

Concept note-5: -The actor had passed away in Bahamas on October 31 at the age of 90. AP Sean Connery died of respiratory failure due to pneumonia as well as atrial fibrillation.
