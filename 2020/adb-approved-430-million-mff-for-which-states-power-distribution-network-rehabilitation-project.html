ADB approved $430 million MFF for which state’s Power Distribution Network Rehabilitation Project?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) ** Uttar Pradesh

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ADB Approves $430 Million to Help Rehabilitate Power Distribution Networks in Uttar Pradesh, India.

Concept note-2: -ADB supports projects in developing member countries that create economic and development impact, delivered through both public and private sector operations, advisory services, and knowledge support.

Concept note-3: -It also provides financing to certain private sector projects as well as public-private partnerships through grants, loans, technical assistance, and equity investments to promote development. The ADB is controlled by member countries, with the U.S. and Japan having the largest stake.

Concept note-4: -ADB obtains its funding by issuing bonds on the world’s capital markets. It also relies on the contributions of member countries, retained earnings from lending operations, and the repayment of loans.

Concept note-5: -A founding member of ADB in 1966, India is now ADB’s fourth largest shareholder and its top borrower since 2010.
