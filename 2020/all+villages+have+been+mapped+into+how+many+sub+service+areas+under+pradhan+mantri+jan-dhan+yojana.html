All villages have been mapped into how many sub service areas under Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan Yojana?

(A) 1.99 lakh

(B) ** 1.59 lakh

(C) 2.59 lakh

(D) 3.00 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Banking Service Points: PMJDY aimed at providing banking service points throughout rural India by mapping over 6 lakh villages into 1.6 lakh Sub Service Areas (SSAs). Each SSA typically comprised of 1, 000-1, 500 households.

Concept note-2: -The six pillars-providing bank accounts, credit, insurance, pension, financial literacy, and increasing the penetration of banking agents-were designed to address the prior challenges and to accelerate financial inclusion in India.

Concept note-3: -About 5.4 crore PMJDY account holders received direct benefit transfer (DBT) from the Government under various schemes in June, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) is National Mission for Financial Inclusion to ensure access to financial services, namely, Banking/ Savings & Deposit Accounts, Remittance, Credit, Insurance, Pension in an affordable manner.
