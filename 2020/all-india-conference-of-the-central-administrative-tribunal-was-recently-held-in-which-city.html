All India Conference of the Central Administrative Tribunal was recently held in which city?

(A) Bengaluru

(B) Panaji

(C) ** New Delhi

(D) Ranchi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All India Conference of Central Administrative Tribunal 2020 held in New Delhi. The annual All India Conference of the Central Administrative Tribunal (CAT) was held in New Delhi, today.

Concept note-2: -Recently, the annual All India Conference of the Central Administrative Tribunal was held in New Delhi. Key points: The conference had two technical sessions that discussed issues related to the working of the CAT.

Concept note-3: -Currently, the tribunal has 63 Benches.

Concept note-4: -In addition the Central Administrative Tribunal, Principal Bench is dealing with the matters of Govt. of National Capital Territory of Delhi.

Concept note-5: -The Central Administrative Tribunal (CAT) was established in 1985 through the Administrative Tribunals Act. The CAT is responsible for adjudication or trial of matters connected with recruitment and conditions of service of personnel in public service in India.
