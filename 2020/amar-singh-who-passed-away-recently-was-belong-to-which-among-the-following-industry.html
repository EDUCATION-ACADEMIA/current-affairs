Amar Singh who passed away recently was belong to which among the following industry?

(A) Singer

(B) Actor

(C) ** Politician

(D) News Editor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maharana Amar Singh I, the Maharana of Mewar (March 16, 1559 – January 26, 1620), was the eldest son and successor of Maharana Pratap of Mewar. He was the 16th Rana of Mewar dynasty of Sisodia Rajputs and ruler of Mewar from January 19, 1597 till his death on January 26, 1620. His capital was Udaipur.

Concept note-2: -Amar Singh died on 1 August 2020 at the age of 64 in Singapore due to kidney failure.

Concept note-3: -Having arrived to perform in Mehsampur, Punjab, both Chamkila and Amarjot were gunned down as they exited their vehicle on 8 March 1988 at approximately 2PM. A gang of motorcyclists fired several rounds, fatally wounding the couple and other members of the entourage.
