Ammunition Factory Khadki, Pune Handed Over 40 mm Under Barrel Grenade Launcher (UBGL) to Which force?

(A) CRPF

(B) ** BSF

(C) IAF

(D) ITBP

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pune: The Ammunition Factory at Khadki has made a 40 mm Under Barrel Grenade Launcher (UBGL) for the Border Security Force . All components for this UBGL were secured from Indian industries making it completely Atma Nirbhar. This ammunition is fired from a launcher fitted under the barrel of 5.56 mm Rifle (INSAS).

Concept note-2: -The ammunition fired by the UBGL is similar to the Milkor MGL used by the Indian Army, allowing for standardisation. The UBGL’s can be equipped with Air Burst rounds for enhanced lethality. These rounds uses programmable electronic fuse for detonation and has self destructive capabilities in case of missing the target.

Concept note-3: -2) Range is 400 Meters in place of 30 Meters of hand grenade. 3) It can be fired by same equipment by the Soldier. 4) It is also a very safe ammunition for carrying by Soldiers.

Concept note-4: -40×51 mm MV (40 mm medium velocity) The propellant has medium pressure and gives the projectile an average velocity of 100 m/s (328 ft/s) depending on the ammunition type. It has a maximum range of 800 meters, exceeding conventional extended range low-velocity variants by up to 375 meters.

Concept note-5: -40 x 46 mm Rounds RLV-HEF, RLV-HEFJ and RLV-P for grenade launchers UBGL-M family, M 203 type. The 40x46 mm Round RLV-HEF, RLV-HEFJ (Jump), are intended to destroy enemy’s personnel, transport means and light armoured materiels at distance up to 400 m.
