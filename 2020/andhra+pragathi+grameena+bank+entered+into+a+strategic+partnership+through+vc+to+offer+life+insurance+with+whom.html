Andhra Pragathi Grameena Bank entered into a strategic partnership through VC to Offer Life Insurance with whom?

(A) ** Canara HSBC OBC Life Insurance

(B) Bajaj Alliance

(C) Oriental Bank Life Insurance

(D) LIC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Canara HSBC OBC Life Insurance & Andhra Pragathi Grameena Bank Entered into a Strategic Partnership through VC to Offer Life Insurance.

Concept note-2: -Brief Information: Andhra Pragathi Grameena Bank came into existence from 01.06. 2006 by amalgamating Rayalaseema Grameena Bank, Sri Anantha Grameena Bank and Pinakini Grameena Bank consequent upon the Government of India Notification dt. 01.06.

Concept note-3: -Due to the merger of SBI Associate Banks with State Bank of India, the bifurcation process of APGVB was interrupted. However, now the process of amalgamation with SBI has completed.

Concept note-4: -Andhra Pragathi Grameena Bank is an Indian Regional Rural Bank based in Andhra Pradesh, India. It was founded in 2006 as a Scheduled Commercial Bank under the Regional Rural Banks Act of 1976 to provide banking services in Rayalaseema and Coastal Andhra. It is owned by the Ministry of Finance, Government of India.
