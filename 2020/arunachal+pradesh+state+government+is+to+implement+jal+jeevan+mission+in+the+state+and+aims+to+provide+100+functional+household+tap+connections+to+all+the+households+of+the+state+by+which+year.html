Arunachal Pradesh state government is to implement Jal Jeevan Mission in the State and aims to provide 100% Functional Household Tap Connections to all the households of the State by which year?

(A) ** 2023

(B) 2021

(C) 2022

(D) 2025

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"Yet another achievement on the auspicious occasion of #NewYear Gujarat declared as 100% #HarGharJal state.

Concept note-2: -Tamil Nadu has emerged as the only state in India which has achieved the target for 2022 Q1 and Q2 for Jal Jeevan Mission, with 69.57 lakh households provided with tap connections.

Concept note-3: -On 15th August 2019, when Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi announced Jal Jeevan Mission from the ramparts of Red Fort, only 3.23 Crore (17%) rural households out of a total of 19.35 Crore rural households had access to tap water supply in their households.
