As per the report of Deep Knowledge Group’s safest country in the world from covid-19 what is India’s rank?

(A) 26th

(B) 36th

(C) ** 56th

(D) 46th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -While Switzerland is at the top position with a score of 752, India ranked 56th in the comprehensive report. The study ranked 200 countries into four tiers of Coronavirus safety. As per the 250-page report, Switzerland is followed by Israel and Germany, while South Sudan is the most dangerous of all.

Concept note-2: -As per the GPI report for the year 2022, India holds the rank of 135th in the list of the safest countries in the world.

Concept note-3: -India ranked 60th of 121 countries in the Gallup Law and Order Index for 2021, scoring 80 on an index that ranges from 1 to 100, with a higher score indicating that more people in a country feel secure. Singapore ranked the highest with a score of 96, while Afghanistan was at the bottom of the list with 51.

Concept note-4: -Is India Safe to Travel Right Now? Yes, you can travel safely to major tourist cities, such as New Delhi, Agra, and Goa. But northern borders or conflict areas like Kashmir and Jammu are currently not recommended due to occasional terrorist attacks [Nov. 2022].
