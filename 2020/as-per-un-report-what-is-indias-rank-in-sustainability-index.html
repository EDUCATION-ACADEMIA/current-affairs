As per UN report, what is India’s rank in Sustainability Index?

(A) 48

(B) 62

(C) ** 77

(D) 86

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India was ranked 121 out of the 163 countries. It was ranked 117 in 2020 and 120 in 2021.

Concept note-2: -India’s rank in the global Sustainable Development Report, 2022 has slipped for the third consecutive year. India has been ranked 121 in the index with an SDG index score of 60.3. It ranked 117 in 2020 and 120 in 2021.

Concept note-3: -The 2022 SDG Index is topped by three Nordic countries – Finland, Denmark and Sweden – and all top 10 countries are European countries.

Concept note-4: -The Sustainable Competitiveness Index 2022 State of The World 2022: The average sustainable competitiveness score is 44.1, less than 50% of the possible best score. The higest score is 60.7, achieved by Sweden: Even the leading nations are far from being truly sustainable & competitive.
