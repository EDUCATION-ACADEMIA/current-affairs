BharatPe launches how many Cricket Stars as Brand Ambassadors?

(A) 5

(B) 8

(C) 9

(D) ** 11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BharatPe has onboarded 11 Indian national level cricket players as its Brand Ambassadors.

Concept note-2: -This campaign gives offline merchants of BharatPe an opportunity to create their own video advertisement- featuring India’s cricketers, Rohit Sharma or KL Rahul.

Concept note-3: -Let’s have a look at the Indian cricket personalities, who have enhanced their brand endorsement portfolio with some lucrative deals. Sourav Ganguly, the former Indian skipper and the President of the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI), has topped the list as Dada remained a brand favourite in 2022.

Concept note-4: -Ashneer Grover’s Bharat Pe once hired Salman Khan as brand ambassador.

Concept note-5: -Owing to his high stature in the field of cricket, Rohit Sharma has famous brands lined up for him at his doorstep. Sharma has a colossal brand value of $25 million. He has 22 official brand contracts currently ongoing.
