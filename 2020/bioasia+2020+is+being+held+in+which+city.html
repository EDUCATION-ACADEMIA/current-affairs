BioAsia 2020 is being held in which city?

(A) Lucknow

(B) ** Hyderabad

(C) New Delhi

(D) Kochi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 17th edition of BioAsia 2020 was recently held in Hyderabad. The three-day global forum at HICC, Hyderabad was inaugurated by KT Rama Rao, Minister for Industries and commerce & IT, Government of Telangana.

Concept note-2: -Bio Asia Summit, the largest biotechnology, and life sciences forum of Central Asia, was held in Hyderabad, Telangana from 17-19 February 2020.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is ‘Today for Tomorrow’. The theme of BioAsia 2020 is: ‘Today for Tomorrow’. BioAsia 2020 explores the capabilities that Life sciences companies should invest in Today to thrive and create value Tomorrow.

Concept note-4: -In its venture to strengthen links with India, which has developed into a global player in the life sciences industry, the UK has partnered with the 20th edition of BioAsia - the marquee healthcare and Life Sciences event, organized by the Government of Telangana.

Concept note-5: -Switzerland is the country partner for the 17th edition of BioAsia 2020, the global bio business forum, which would be held from February 17 to 19 in the city. This was announced at a press conference here on Tuesday.
