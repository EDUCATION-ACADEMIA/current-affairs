BOB Financial Solution Limited partnered with which company to launch four new variants of BOB Credit Cards?

(A) American Express

(B) Rupay

(C) ** MasterCard

(D) Visa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank of Baroda (BoB) subsidiary BOB Financial Solutions Limited (BFSL) and Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation Ltd (IRCTC) have partnered up to launch the IRCTC BoB RuPay Contactless Credit Card.

Concept note-2: -To that end, RBIH is launching the inaugural edition of Swanari TechSprint, which Bank of Baroda is supporting as a scale-up partner, to create digital solutions to close the gender gap.

Concept note-3: -New Delhi, August 24, 2022: Bank of Baroda, one of India’s leading public sector banks, today announced the launch of the bob World Yoddha Debit Card on the RuPay platform, especially for the Indian Armed Forces.

Concept note-4: -BOB Financial Solutions Limited was established in the year 1994. A Non-Banking Financial Company and wholly owned subsidiary of Bank of Baroda, it has established itself as a pioneer in the space of credit cards.

Concept note-5: -Which financial services company has launched the ‘ConQR’- QR code embedded credit card, along with BOB Financial? Notes: BOB Financial, the credit card arm of Bank of Baroda and Mastercard have jointly launched small business-focussed QR code embedded credit card.
