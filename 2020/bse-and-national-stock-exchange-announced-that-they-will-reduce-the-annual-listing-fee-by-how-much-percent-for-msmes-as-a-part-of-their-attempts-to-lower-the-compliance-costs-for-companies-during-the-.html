BSE and National Stock Exchange announced that they will reduce the annual listing fee by how much percent for MSMEs, as a part of their attempts to lower the compliance costs for companies during the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic?

(A) 15%

(B) 35%

(C) 20%

(D) ** 25%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Annual listing fee payable by an Issuer is limited to a maximum of Rs. 5.50 lacs. The above fees shall be applicable on the incremental issues to be listed on and after April 01, 2022, and also to the existing Capital. The Annual Fees is Rs 25, 000/- or 0.01% of full market capitalization, whichever is higher.

Concept note-2: -Eligibility Criteria: The minimum market capitalization of the Company shall be Rs. 25 crore (market capitalization shall be calculated by multiplying the post-issue paid-up number of equity shares with the issue price).
