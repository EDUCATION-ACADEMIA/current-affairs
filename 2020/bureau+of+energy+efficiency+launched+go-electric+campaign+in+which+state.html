Bureau of energy efficiency launched Go-Electric campaign in which state?

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) Kerala

(C) ** Andhra Pradesh

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is a collaboration with the Andhra Pradesh State Energy Conservation Mission, the Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE). They both launched the “Go Electric” campaign. It is launched to encourage the use of electric vehicles.

Concept note-2: -The objective of this campaign is to create awareness among masses about benefits of switching over to Electric Vehicles (EVs), including various initiatives taken by Centre & State Governments to enhance acceptability of Electric Vehicles, and Electrical Cooking.

Concept note-3: -What is the name of the campaign launched by Government of India to boost the adoption of electric vehicles and appliances? Notes: The Government of India has recently launched a campaign named “Go Electric campaign”.

Concept note-4: -The Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is a significant cleanliness campaign started by the Government of India.

Concept note-5: -Ministry of Power along with Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, Ministry of Heavy Industries and NITI Aayog has launched a nationwide “Go Electric” Campaign on 19.02. 2021 to educate the general public on the benefits of electric vehicles.
