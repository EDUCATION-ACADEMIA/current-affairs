Captain Carlton Chapman passed away, he belonged to which sports?

(A) Cricket

(B) Hockey

(C) ** Football

(D) Kabaddi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As captain of the Indian football team, Chapman led the country to the South Asian Football Federation (SAFF) Gold Cup title in 1997. He came through the ranks at Jamshedpur’s renowned Tata Football Academy in the early 1990s before joining Kolkata’s legendary football club East Bengal in 1993.
