CURRENT AFFAIRS-2020
[SOURCES]

CBSE announced integration of Artificial Intelligence in the high school curriculum for the 2020-2021 academic year. The curriculum was developed in collaboration with which compnay?

(A) Microsoft

(B) ** IBM

(C) Google

(D) Apple

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CBSE partners with IBM to integrate AI curriculum in 200 schools in India. The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) on Thursday announced the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) in the high school curriculum for classes XI and XII for the current academic year (2020-21).

Concept note-2: -Which company collaborated with CBSE and Ministry of Education for “AI For All” initiative? Notes: Intel, CBSE and Ministry of Education announced the launch of the “AI For All” initiative on July 29, 2021.

Concept note-3: -Artificial Intelligence (AI) is an overarching discipline that covers a broad range of domains and applications, and is expected to impact every field in the coming future. This curriculum focuses on building AI readiness in young minds.

Concept note-4: -In sync with the larger goal of enabling and empowering the next generation with AI skills, CBSE introduced AI as a subject in 2019 from class VIII and has since then extended it from class VI – XII.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Intel. Intel has collaborated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) to launch the ‘AI For All’ initiative. It is aimed at creating a basic understanding of artificial intelligence (AI) for people in India.
