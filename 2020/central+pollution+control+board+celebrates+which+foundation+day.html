Central Pollution Control Board celebrates which Foundation Day?

(A) 40th

(B) 44th

(C) ** 46th

(D) 51st

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The CBCB was established on September 23, 1974 under The Water (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 as a technical arm of Central Government for environmental research, monitoring, regulation and enforcement in the country.

Concept note-2: -The Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB), statutory organisation, was constituted in September, 1974 under the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974. Further, CPCB was entrusted with the powers and functions under the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.

Concept note-3: -Unsourced material may be challenged and removed. The Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) of India is a statutory organization under the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (Mo. E.F.C.C.). It was established in 1974 under the Water (Prevention and Control of pollution) Act, 1974.
