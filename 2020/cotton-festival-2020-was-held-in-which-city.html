Cotton Festival 2020 was held in which city?

(A) Kathmandu

(B) Assam

(C) ** Dhaka

(D) Guwahati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cotton Fest 2020 will take place at the Old Park Station in Newtown, Johannesburg.

Concept note-2: -With around 100 South African artists confirmed to perform over 3 stages, this action packed festival returns to The Station in Newtown for TWO DAYS on 23 & 24 April 2022.

Concept note-3: -Cotton Fest celebrates South African talent by bringing together a high-octane lineup of local artists, musicians, rappers, and DJs. Returning to its home at The Station, a disused Edwardian-era train station in Newtown, Cotton Fest is back for a fourth edition.

Concept note-4: -JOHANNESBURG – The urban music and fashion explosion known as Cotton Fest will be back for the fourth edition in Johannesburg on 4 February. The festival is a legacy of the late rapper and fashionista Riky Rick that was created in 2019.

Concept note-5: -Cotton Fest founder, the late rapper Riky Rick, launched the festival in 2019 to bring together established and upcoming South African talent. The annual festival was put on hold in 2021, due to Covid-19 restrictions, returning this year.
