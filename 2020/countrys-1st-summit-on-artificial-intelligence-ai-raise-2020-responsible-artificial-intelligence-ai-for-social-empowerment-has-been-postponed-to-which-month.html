(A) September

(B) August

(C) July

(D) ** October

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Responsible AI for Social Empowerment (RAISE 2020) virtual summit will be a Global Artificial Intelligence summit to be organized by the Government of India in partnership with Industry & Academia. It will be inaugurated by the Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi on October 5, 2020 at 7 PM.

Concept note-2: -With the advent of the new decade, the Government of Telangana officially declared 2020 as the year of AI (Artificial Intelligence).

Concept note-3: -RAISE (Responsible AI for Social Empowerment and Education) is a new MIT-wide initiative headquartered in the MIT Media Lab and in collaboration with the MIT Schwarzman College of Computing and MIT Open Learning.
