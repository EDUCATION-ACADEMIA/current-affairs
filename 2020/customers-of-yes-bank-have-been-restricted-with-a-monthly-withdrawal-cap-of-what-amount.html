Customers of Yes Bank have been restricted with a monthly withdrawal cap of what amount?

(A) Rs 20, 000

(B) ** Rs 50, 000

(C) Rs 70, 000

(D) Rs 100, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Yes Bank withdrawal limit capped at Rs 50, 000; RBI supersedes board.

Concept note-2: -Daily domestic cash withdrawal limit of INR 25, 000.

Concept note-3: -The daily domestic and International ATM withdrawal limit on the card is Rs. 25, 000. The daily domestic purchase limit is Rs. 25, 000.

Concept note-4: -Yes Bank withdrawal limited to ₹50, 000, RBI says don’t panic: 10 points | Mint.

Concept note-5: -Daily Domestic & International ATM Withdrawal Limit of INR 1, 00, 000. Daily Domestic Purchase Limit at POS (Point-of-Sale) and Ecommerce of INR 3, 00, 000.
