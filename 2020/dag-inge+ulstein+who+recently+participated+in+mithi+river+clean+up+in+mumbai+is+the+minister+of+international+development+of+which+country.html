Dag-Inge Ulstein, who recently participated in Mithi river clean up in Mumbai is the Minister of International Development of which country?

(A) Finland

(B) Sweden

(C) ** Norway

(D) Denmark

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Mithi River (Pronunciation: [miʈʰiː]) is a river on Salsette Island, the island of the city of Mumbai, India. It is a confluence of tail-water discharges of the Powai and Vihar lakes. The river is seasonal and rises during the monsoons.

Concept note-2: -Representational image. Mumbai: The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) has decided to clean up the highly polluted Mithi river by stopping the sewage discharge to the water body. The civic authorities have floated two tenders worth nearly Rs 2100 crore to improve water quality in the river.

Concept note-3: -Despite tidal influence of west coast, the river remains as a drain with very poor water quality.
