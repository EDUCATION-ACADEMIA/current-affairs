Day of International Criminal Justice is annually observed on whcih day?

(A) 14 July

(B) ** 17 July

(C) 18 July

(D) 16 July

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In June 2010, at the Review Conference of the Rome Statute in Uganda’s Kampala town, it was decided that July 17 would be observed as the Day of International Criminal Justice.

Concept note-2: -Sumit Arora Published On July 18th, 2022. World Day for International Justice is observed on July 17 to commemorate the organisations that work to bring justice to the victims of international criminal acts. It also commemorates the establishment of modern court systems in the world.

Concept note-3: -World Day for International Justice is celebrated worldwide on July 17 every year to mark an emerging modern system of justice against international criminal acts. It is also known as International Criminal Justice Day or International Justice Day.

Concept note-4: -17 July is the Day of International Criminal Justice.

Concept note-5: -National Justice Day, celebrated on May 3, seeks to honor all who answer to the name ‘Justice’ and inform us about the popularity and variations of the name. ‘Justice’ is one of those words everyone has heard of but is confused for something else. It is an English name that means ‘just, ’ ‘righteous, ’ or ‘upright.
