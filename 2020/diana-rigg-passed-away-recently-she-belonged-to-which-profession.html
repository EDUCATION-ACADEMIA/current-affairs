Diana Rigg passed away recently, she belonged to which profession?

(A) Sportsperson

(B) ** Actress

(C) Singer

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Diana Rigg, in full Dame Enid Diana Elizabeth Rigg, (born July 20, 1938, Doncaster, Yorkshire, England-died September 10, 2020, London), English actress who gained worldwide fame during the 1960s for her portrayal of Emma Peel on the television series The Avengers.

Concept note-2: -Her daughter, Rachael Stirling, said that the cause of death was cancer, which had been diagnosed in March.

Concept note-3: -In a will signed days before she died, Rigg left the bulk of her estate, which included property in France and America, to Rachael Stirling, her 43-year-old daughter by her second husband, Archibald Stirling, who was a theatre director and former officer in the Scots Guards.

Concept note-4: -She was appointed Dame Commander of the Order of the British Empire (DBE) at the 1994 Queen’s Birthday Honours for her services to drama. Dame Diana Rigg died of lung cancer on September 10, 2020, she was 82 years old.
