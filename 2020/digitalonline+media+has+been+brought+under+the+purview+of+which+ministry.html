Digital/Online media has been brought under the purview of which ministry?

(A) Ministry of Home Affairs

(B) Ministry of Finance

(C) ** Ministry of Information and Broadcasting

(D) Ministry of Communication

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The government has brought video streaming over-the-top (OTT) platforms under the ambit of the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting. These platforms were so far under the purview of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology.

Concept note-2: -Digital Media Guidelines and Policies | Ministry of Information and Broadcasting | Government of India.

Concept note-3: -Home | Ministry of Information and Broadcasting | Government of India.

Concept note-4: -Content Regulation | Ministry of Information and Broadcasting | Government of India.

Concept note-5: -Ministry of Information and Broadcasting (Ministry of I&B) is a ministerial level agency of the Government of India responsible for the formulation and administration of rules, regulations and laws in the areas of information, broadcasting, the press and the Cinema of India. 4, 012 (2016 est.)
