DP World signed up as global logistics partner of which IPL team?

(A) Kolkata Knight Riders

(B) Chennai Super Kings

(C) Delhi Capitals

(D) ** Royal Challengers Banglore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As RCB’s partner, DP World will leverage our global logistics expertise and network to support the team with their complex logistical requirements as we develop our relationships and operations on the sub-continent.

Concept note-2: -The team has DP World as Global Logistics Partner, Exide and Jio as principal sponsors for this season. Associate Partners: Following that, RCB have PUMA, MPL, boAt, Meesho, Chola MS, and Nippon Paint in the associate partners category.

Concept note-3: -StockGro signs up as the official sponsor of Royal Challengers Bangalore. During this season of Indian Premier League, social and gaming investment platform StockGro has been on-boarded by Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) as its official sponsor. This brand sponsorship deal will run throughout the 2022 season of IPL.

Concept note-4: -The owner of RCB is United Spirits Limited, which is the largest Indian alcoholic beverage company. United Spirits is a subsidiary of Diageo which is based in London.
