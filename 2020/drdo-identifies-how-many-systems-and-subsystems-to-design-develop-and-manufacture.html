DRDO identifies how many systems and subsystems to design, develop and manufacture?

(A) 58

(B) ** 108

(C) 86

(D) 113

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To this effect, DRDO has identified following 108 systems and subsystems which will be designed, developed and manufactured by industry only and will not be taken up by DRDO itself for development.

Concept note-2: -Its mission also includes providing technical solutions to the services for combat effectiveness optimization and encouraging the welfare of troops. Developing infrastructure, quality manpower and indigenous technological base for the defence systems and services of the country.

Concept note-3: -Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) is a defence R&D hub which develops defence technologies, systems/products that are required for Indian Armed Forces. DRDO develops defence technologies in the project mode.
