Dr.Ram Manohar Lohia’s birth anniversary was observed on which date?

(A) ** March 23

(B) March 20

(C) March 22

(D) March 21

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ram Manohar Lohia was born on 23 March 1910 at Akbarpur, modern-day Uttar Pradesh in a Bania family.

Concept note-2: -Ram Manohar Lohia, (born March 23, 1910, Akbarpur, India-died October 12, 1967, New Delhi), Indian politician and activist who was a prominent figure in socialist politics and in the movement toward Indian independence.

Concept note-3: -The original Socialist Party had its roots in the Congress Socialist Party (CSP), the socialist caucus of the Indian National Congress, which fused in 1948 with the Bolshevik-Leninist Party of India, Ceylon and Burma (BLPI). Hector Abhayavardhana of the BLPI became General Secretary of the new party.
