Eat Right India movement was launched by which organisation?

(A) FAO

(B) UN

(C) WTO

(D) ** FSSAI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Therefore, FSSAI has embarked on a large-scale effort to transform the country’s food system in order to ensure safe, healthy and sustainable food for all Indians through the ‘Eat Right India’ movement. The tagline ‘Sahi Bhojan. Behtar Jeevan’, thus, forms the foundation of this movement.

Concept note-2: -The ‘Eat Right India’ movement initiated by FSSAI under the aegis of Ministry of Health & Family Welfare has been creating awareness amongst the people about safe, healthy and sustainable dietary habits.

Concept note-3: -The ‘Eat Right India’ movement is designed to be a collaborative effort, which builds on the collective action of all stakeholders consumers, food businesses, community organizations, experts and professionals, and the government, bringing them together in a Jan Andolan towards a healthier, happier India.

Concept note-4: -FSSAI launches the Eat Right Movement with Sh. Rajkummar Rao as its Brand Ambassador - YouTube.
