Eddie Van Halen, who was passed away was a renowned person of which field

(A) Environmentalist

(B) ** Musician

(C) Choreographer

(D) Editor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eddie Van Halen was the co-founder of the popular American rock band Van Halen and is considered one of the greatest guitar players in rock history. He is known for his two-handed fret-board tapping technique on the guitar, which gave his heavy metal rock group a unique sound.

Concept note-2: -Surprisingly, considering how Van Halen was never one to use sparseness or restraint, Van Halen’s number one influence appears to have been British six-string master Eric Clapton. “Clapton was it.

Concept note-3: -Eddie Van Halen’s final words to Valerie Bertinelli and their son, Wolfgang, were “I love you.” In her new memoir, “Enough Already: Learning to Love the Way I Am Today, ” Bertinelli recalled her final moments with the rocker, who lost his battle to throat cancer in October 2020.

Concept note-4: -Eddie’s most commonly cited early guitar hero is Eric Clapton; in interviews he always cited EC as his great early inspiration, but he was also a great fan of Jimmy Page, Ritchie Blackmore and indeed Allan Holdsworth. As any fan of Eddie will know, the backbone of his vocabulary lies in a fiery blues-rock vocabulary.
