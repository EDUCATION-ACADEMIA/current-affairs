FIFA world Cup will be held in which city?

(A) Spain

(B) ** Qatar

(C) Japan

(D) USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The tournament will be jointly hosted by 16 cities in three North American countries: Canada, Mexico, and the United States. The United States will host 60 matches, including every match from the quarterfinals onward, while neighboring Canada and Mexico will each host 10 matches, making a total of 80 matches.

Concept note-2: -The final match of the 2026 World Cup will be played at the MetLife Stadium in Rutherford, New Jersey, just 8 km west of New York City.

Concept note-3: -The trio of nations hosting the 2026 World Cup - the USA, Canada and Mexico - will divide games up between them with certain group-stage matches based in one nation to reduce travel.
