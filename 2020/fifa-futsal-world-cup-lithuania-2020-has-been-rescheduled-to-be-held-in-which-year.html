FIFA Futsal World Cup Lithuania 2020 has been rescheduled to be held in which year?

(A) ** 2021

(B) 2022

(C) 2024

(D) 2023

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 12 September 2021, the Futsal World Cup will kick off in Lithuania, marking the first FIFA event to be held in the country. The tournament was originally scheduled to take place in September 2020, but had to be postponed for a year due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Concept note-2: -16, 2022 4:52 p.m. Rabat - Morocco’s national futsal team today defeated Iran in the final of the 2022 Continental Futsal Championship, clinching a 3-4 victory in Bangkok, Thailand.

Concept note-3: -ABOUT | IFA Futsal World Championship 2022 Spain | US Futsal.

Concept note-4: -Taking place quadrennially, the FIFA Futsal World Cup™ sees 24 nations compete on the international stage. With continental qualification pathways leading to an exciting finals event, the competition has seen four past champions; Brazil, Spain, Argentina and Portugal.

Concept note-5: -Futsal is the FIFA-recognised form of small-sided indoor football (the word is a contraction of the Spanish ‘fútbol sala’). It is played between two teams who each have five players on the pitch at any one time, with rolling substitutes and a smaller ball than soccer that is harder and less bouncy.
