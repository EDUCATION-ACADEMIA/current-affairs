GAIL has planned to invest over ____ to expand the National Gas Pipeline Grid?

(A) Rs.10, 000 crores

(B) Rs.22, 000 crores

(C) Rs.30, 000 crores

(D) ** Rs.45, 000 crores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -GAIL is the first company in India to own and operate pipelines for LPG transmission. It has 2038 km LPG pipeline network 1, 415 km of which connects the western and northern parts of India and 623 km of networks is in the southern part of the country connecting Eastern Coast.

Concept note-2: -About GAIL It owns and operates a network of around 14617 km of natural gas pipelines spread across the length and breadth of country. It is also working concurrently on execution of multiple pipeline projects to further enhance the spread.

Concept note-3: -1. Hajira-Bijapur-Jagdishpur (HBJ) Gas Pipeline is the world’s largest and the longest underground pipeline. 2. The Salaya-Mathura Pipeline (SMPL) and Paradip-Haldia-Barauni Pipeline are the crude oil pipelines in India.
