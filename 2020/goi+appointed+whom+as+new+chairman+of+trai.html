GoI appointed whom as new chairman of TRAI?

(A) ** P.D. Vaghela

(B) Deepak Chahar

(C) RS Sharma

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is PD Vaghela.

Concept note-2: -Officers are selected from the Indian Telecommunications Service and the Indian Administrative Service (IAS). The current chairperson of TRAI is PD Vaghela, an IAS officer of the Gujarat cadre, batch of 1986.

Concept note-3: -The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet has approved the appointment of PD Vaghela as the new Chairman of Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI).

Concept note-4: -Mr. Mats Granryd, Director General, GSMA alongwith his team visited TRAI and held discussions with TRAI delegation led by Dr. P.D. Vaghela, Chairman-TRAI.
