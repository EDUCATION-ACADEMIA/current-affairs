Google is to open its second cloud region in which city?

(A) Bengaluru

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Hyderabad

(D) Noida

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Google is set to launch its second cloud region in which Indian city? Notes: Technology Major Google recently announced that it would open a Delhi cloud region. It will be the company’s second region in India after the Mumbai cloud region which was launched in 2017.

Concept note-2: -The Delhi-NCR region is the second Google Cloud region in India.

Concept note-3: -Google Cloud Platform (GCP), the cloud computing service of Alphabet Inc, provides compute, storage, and networking services through its data centers in over 20 countries and 35 locations around the world.

Concept note-4: -Alphabet Inc’s Google will set up its first cloud region in Greece, the company, giving a boost to the country’s efforts to become a world cloud computing hub. The deal is estimated to contribute some 2.2 billion euros ($2.13 billion) to Greece’s economic output and create some 20, 000 jobs by 2030.

Concept note-5: -Google has announced a new Cloud Region in Delhi NCR. This new Google Cloud Region has been set up to serve customers and the public sector better, Google says. The new Cloud Region has been designed to serve both Indian and global companies and can enable them to build accessible applications for their customers.
