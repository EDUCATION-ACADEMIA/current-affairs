(A) Rs.1500

(B) Rs.750

(C) ** Rs.500

(D) Rs.1000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Women Jan Dhan bank account holders will start getting the second installment of Rs 500 from Monday in line with the announcement made by Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman in March.

Concept note-2: -Government will start sending the amount of 500 rupees to the women account holders of Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) for the month of June from today. The amount is the third installment under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package.

Concept note-3: -Benefits under PMJDY Rupay Debit card is provided to PMJDY account holder. Accident Insurance Cover of Rs.1 lakh (enhanced to Rs. 2 lakh to new PMJDY accounts opened after 28.8.2018) is available with RuPay card issued to the PMJDY account holders.
