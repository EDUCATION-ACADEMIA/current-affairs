Govt Set Up High-level Committee headed by whom to commemorate 400th birth anniversary of Shri Guru Tegh Bahadur?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) ** Narendra Modi

(C) Ramnath Kovind

(D) Arvind Kejriwal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At that time, Guru Tegh Bahadur Ji, Mata Nanki Ji, their mother and Mata Gujri Ji, their wife had been staying in Bakala Sahib for 26 years. Guru Sahib did meditation for all those years in a basement room. It was Bhai Makhan Shah Lubana who discovered Guru Sahib and told the world that he had found the Guru.

Concept note-2: -Guru Tegh Bahadur Martyrdom Day 2022, Guru Tegh Bahadur Shaheedi Diwas 2022: Guru Tegh Bahadur Martyrdom Day is observed every year on November 24, commemorating Shaheedi Diwas of Guru Tegh Bahadur.

Concept note-3: -Guru Tegh Bahadur was born at Guru Ke Mahal, Amritsar on April 1, 1621, to Guru Hargobind, the sixth Guru of the Sikhs and Mata Nanaki.

Concept note-4: -Guru Tegh Bahādur, (born 1621?, Amritsar, Punjab, India-died November 11, 1675, Delhi), ninth Sikh Guru (1664–75) and second Sikh martyr.
