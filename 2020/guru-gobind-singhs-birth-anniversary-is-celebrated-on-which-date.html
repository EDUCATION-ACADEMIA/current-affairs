Guru Gobind Singh’s birth anniversary is celebrated on which date?

(A) 1st January

(B) ** 2nd January

(C) 3rd January

(D) 4th January

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The tenth guru of the Sikhs, Guru Gobind Singh Ji’s birth anniversary is celebrated on December 22 every year.

Concept note-2: -Guru Govind singh jayanti 2022 celebrated on 29 December. This festival is also known as Prakash parv.

Concept note-3: -Guru Gobind Singh birth anniversary is being celebrated on December 29, 2022. The place where Guru Gobind Singh was born is now Takhat Shri Harimander Ji Patna Sahib.In 1670, his family again moved to Punjab.In March 1672, his family moved to a place called Chak Nanaki, located in the Shivalik Hills of the Himalayas.

Concept note-4: -Guru Gobind Singh Jayanti or Prakash Parv 2022: It is celebrated as the birth anniversary of Guru Gobind Singh and this year it falls on 29 December. He was the tenth Guru out of total of ten Sikh Gurus. On 22 December 1666, he was born in Patna, Bihar as per the Julian calendar.

Concept note-5: -Guru Gobind Singh Jayanti was celebrated twice last year on 9th of January and 29th of December. Since, the day of Guru Gobind Singh Jayanti is calculated according to the Vikram Samvat Calendar, every few years this happens and Guru Gobind Singh Jayanti is celebrated twice a year.
