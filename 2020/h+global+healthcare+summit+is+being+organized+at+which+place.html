h Global Healthcare Summit is being organized at which place?

(A) Kochi

(B) Chennai

(C) ** Visakhapatnam

(D) Madurai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -VISAKHAPATNAM: Around 100 eminent Indian-origin doctors serving in the US have landed in the port city for the Global Health Summit which begins on Friday.

Concept note-2: -In 2022, the World Health Summit Regional Meeting took place from June 15 to 17 in Rome, Italy and was organized by Sapienza University of Rome. The World Health Summit Regional Meeting 2023 will take place on April 13 in Washington DC, USA and online.

Concept note-3: -Finding Solutions for Global Health WHS 2022 strengthened exchange, stimulated innovative solutions to health challenges, fostered global health as a key political issue and promoted the global health debate in the spirit of the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Concept note-4: -The World Health Summit strengthens collaboration and open dialogue guided by science, fosters global health as a key political issue and promotes the global health debate in the spirit of the UN Sustainable Development Goals.
