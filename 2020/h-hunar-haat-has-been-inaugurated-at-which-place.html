h Hunar Haat has been inaugurated at which place?

(A) Lucknow

(B) Kanpur

(C) ** Indore

(D) Ahmedabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Speaking about Hunar Haat which is an initiative of Ministry of Minority Affairs, Union Minister Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi said that one will get to witness ‘Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat’ and experience the essence of ‘Unity in Diversity’ at the Hunar Haat in Mumbai.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution Handmade products from over 28 states and 8 Union territories as well as traditional dishes are available in the 300 stalls in the 34th ‘Hunar Haat’, with more than 600 artisans and craftsmen participating. The next “Hunar Haat” will be organized at Jawahar Lal Nehru (JLN) Stadium in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -“Vishwakarma Vatika”, traditional circus, laser shows, musical and spectacular cultural programmes of renowned and emerging artists, selfie points etc are major attraction of this “Hunar Haat”. The visitors at Agra “Hunar Haat” will enjoy musical and cultural programmes every evening.

Concept note-4: -39th edition of ‘Hunar Haat’ begins in Chandigarh.

Concept note-5: -“Removing the barriers of caste, community, region and religion, “Hunar Haat” has given employment and self-employment opportunities to about 10.5 lakh artisans and craftsmen from all sections of the society in the past 6 years.
