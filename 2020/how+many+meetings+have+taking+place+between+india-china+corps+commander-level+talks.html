How many meetings have taking place between India-china corps commander-level talks?

(A) 2

(B) 4

(C) ** 5

(D) 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 17th round of India- China Corps Commander Level Meeting was held at Chushul-Moldo border meeting point on the Chinese side on 20th December 2022.

Concept note-2: -India, China hold 17th round of Corps Commander talks | Mint.

Concept note-3: -India-China are attempting to revive the stalled process of disengagement and de-escalation in eastern Ladakh. India and China held the 16th round of Corps Commander level talks on Sunday for over 12 hours as the two sides attempted to revive the stalled process of disengagement and de-escalation in Eastern Ladakh.

Concept note-4: -Ladakh standoff: India-China hold 17th round of talks, agree to maintain stability in Western Sector - BusinessToday.

Concept note-5: -Chushul is a village in the Leh district of Ladakh, India. It is located in the Durbuk tehsil, in the area known as “Chushul Valley", south of the Pangong Lake and west of the Spanggur Lake. The Line of Actual Control with China runs about 5 miles east of Chushul, across the Chushul Valley.
