How many members committee formed by central government to oversee construction of new Parliament building?

(A) ** 5

(B) 7

(C) 10

(D) 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which is the head of the 5 member panel constituted by the Centre to oversee timely implementation of various projects under the Central Vista Redevelopment Plan? Explanation: The committee will be headed by former finance secretary Ratan P Watal.

Concept note-2: -Timeline. Sep 2019: The master plan of ‘Redevelopment of Central Vista Avenue’ is conceived by the Government of India. Sep 2020: Tata Projects Ltd won the contract for construction of the new parliament building for ₹862 crores by the CPWD.

Concept note-3: -The Estimates Committee is the largest committee of the parliament. It consists of 30 members, all are appointed from the Lok Sabha.

Concept note-4: -The Lok Sabha will be three times bigger with 888 seats, with ease of sitting for MPs. It is based on Peacock theme, the National Bird.
