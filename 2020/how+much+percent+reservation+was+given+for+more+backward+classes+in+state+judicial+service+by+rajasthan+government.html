How much percent reservation was given for More Backward Classes in state judicial service by Rajasthan government?

(A) 21%

(B) 7%

(C) ** 5%

(D) 3%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The more backward classes include Gurjars, Raika-Rabari, Gadiya-Lohars, Banjara and Gadariya. The Rajasthan government has approved five per cent reservation to the ‘more backward classes’, including Gurjars, in the state judicial service, according to a statement.

Concept note-2: -In Rajasthan, including 5% reservation for most backward classes (MBCs) and 10% for economically weaker sections (EWS), the total reservation is 64%.

Concept note-3: -Andhra Pradesh state percentage of reservation is around 50%. Including the overall 1/3 reservation for women, 66.66% of seats are reserved in Andhra Pradesh in Education and Government jobs. Andhra Pradesh BC quota has sub-quotas for castes, classified in A, B, C and D.

Concept note-4: -Currently, Karnataka provides 32% reservation for OBCs, 15% for SCs and 3% for STs, totaling 50% as capped by the Supreme Court.
