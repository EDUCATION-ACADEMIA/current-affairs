How many agreements have been signed between India and Portugal in areas including defence, culture and yoga?

(A) 8

(B) 10

(C) 12

(D) ** 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 1974, India and Portugal signed a treaty recognising India’s sovereignty over Goa, Daman & Diu, Dadra & Nagar Haveli and related matters. With the signing of this treaty in New Delhi on December 31, 1974, diplomatic relations were re-established and an era of friendly bilateral relations began.

Concept note-2: -Indians make around less than 1% of the Portugal population & approx population of Indians in Portugal as of 2022 is 85 thousand.

Concept note-3: -The Portuguese invaded Goa in 1510, defeating the Bijapur Sultanate. The Portuguese rule lasted for about 450 years, and heavily influenced Goan culture, cuisine, and architecture. In 1961, India annexed Goa after a 36-hour battle and integrated it with the Indian Territory.
