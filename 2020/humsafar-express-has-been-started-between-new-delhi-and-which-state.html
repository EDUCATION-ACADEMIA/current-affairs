Humsafar Express has been started between New Delhi and which state?

(A) Bihar

(B) ** Jharkhand

(C) Chhattisgarh

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"Humsafar” is a Urdu word meaning “life partner” or “soulmate” The first service was inaugurated on 16 December 2016 between Gorakhpur and Anand Vihar Terminal, New Delhi. The fares of Humsafar Express at the time of launch are around 15–20% higher compared to normal AC-3 tier and Sleeper class fares.

Concept note-2: -IRCTC has now brought out Humsafar Express, which was introduced by Railway Minister Piyush Goyal recently. This Indian Railways train is going to ply between Patna and Bengaluru. The weekly special service between these two cities is the latest from the IRCTC, and is Maharashtra’s pride.
