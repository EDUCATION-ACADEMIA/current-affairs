IBM collaborated for digital services with which company?

(A) ** Indian Oil Corporation

(B) Reliance

(C) Hindustan Petroleum

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Infosys BPM, the business process management arm of Infosys (NSE, BSE, NYSE: INFY), today launched the Center of AI and Automation, in collaboration with IBM, at the Infosys Business Experience Lounge in Poland, to bring digital excellence to enterprises globally.

Concept note-2: -Samsung | IBM IBM and Samsung have collaborated for decades.

Concept note-3: -IBM Business Partner Tata Consultancy Services Ltd. helps companies accelerate development by helping them implement the IBM Cloud (formerly known as IBM Bluemix) platform as a service.

Concept note-4: -IBM provides consulting and managed security services to align AWS native and enterprise threat management.

Concept note-5: -Infosys is a Premier Partner of IBM and has a 360° relationship with it covering all levels of engagement like collaboration on training and certifications, Beta program participations, Solution and project consulting and additionally, access to partner channels and events.
