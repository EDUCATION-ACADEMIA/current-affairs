ICMR has approved a total of how many laboratories across India to conduct the tests for COVID-19?

(A) 224 laboratories

(B) 324 laboratories

(C) 424 laboratories

(D) ** 624 laboratories

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICMR’s 26 national institutes address themselves to research on specific health topics like tuberculosis, leprosy, cholera and diarrhoeal diseases, viral diseases including AIDS, malaria, kala-azar, vector control, nutrition, food & drug toxicology, reproduction, immuno-haematology, oncology, medical statistics, etc.

Concept note-2: -INDIAN COUNCIL OF MEDICAL RESEARCH.

Concept note-3: -DDRC SRL Diagnostics at Panampilly Nagar, Ernakulam is the first ICMR & Government Of India approved private COVID-19 testing lab in Kerala. We offer qualitative RT-PCR and Antigen tests for the COVID-19 suspects.

Concept note-4: -Gunjan Lab LLP, Bharuch - ICMR COVID-19 Testing Laboratories.
