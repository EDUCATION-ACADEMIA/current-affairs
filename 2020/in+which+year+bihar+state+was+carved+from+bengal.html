In which year Bihar state was carved from Bengal?

(A) ** 1912

(B) 1914

(C) 2012

(D) 1956

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bihar and Orissa was separated from Bengal on 22 March 1912, with Patna as capital.

Concept note-2: -Bihar formed a part of the Bengal Presidency under the British until 1912, when the province of Bihar and Orissa was formed; in 1936 the two became separate provinces of British-ruled India.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is 1912. Explanation: On 22 March 1912, both Bihar and Orissa were separated from Bengal and became Bihar and Orissa Province.

Concept note-4: -In 1874, Assam was detached from the administrative control of Bengal and was made into a separate Chief Commissionership. As a consequent of the partition of Bengal in 1905, Assam was tagged with Eastern Bengal to form the province of Eastern Bengal and Assam under a Lieutenant Governor.

Concept note-5: -Purushottam Prasad Sharma and Akhaury Basudeva Narayan Sinha. The Beharee continued its crusade for separation of Bihar from Bengal. Committee’15 was formed in 1908.
