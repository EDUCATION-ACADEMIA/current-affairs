In which state Indian Coast Guard Ship C-449 Commissioned?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Chennai

(C) UP

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chennai: The Indian Coast Guard (ICG) Ship C-449 was commissioned on Thursday by chief secretary K Shanmugam in Chennai, which would further strengthen the maritime and coastal security of Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The Indian Coast Guard has recently commissioned its ‘C-448’ high-speed boat at Mangaluru, Karnataka.

Concept note-3: -ICGS Samarth: The Indian Coast Guard’s latest and largest Offshore Patrol Vessel (OPV). Samarth is first in the series of six 105m offshore patrol vessels and has been built by Goa Shipyard Limited.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is ICGS Saksham. Defence Secretary Dr Ajay Kumar commissioned the Indian Coast Guard Ship (ICGS) Saksham at Goa on March 16, 2022.

Concept note-5: -The Indian Coast Guard has commissioned the fifth warship in the series of 105-meter long offshore patrol vessels (OPVs) ICGS Saksham at Goa Shipyard.
