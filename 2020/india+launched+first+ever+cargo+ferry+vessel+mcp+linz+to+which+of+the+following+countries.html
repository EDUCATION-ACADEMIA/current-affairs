India launched first ever Cargo Ferry vessel ‘MCP linz’ to which of the following countries?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) Nepal

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) ** Maldives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Cargo Ferry Vessel MCP Linz operated by the Shipping Corporation of India (SCI) will connect Tuticorin and Cochin ports in India with Kulhudhuhfushi and Male ports in the Maldives.

Concept note-2: -India is set to launch a direct ferry service to which neighbouring country? Notes: India is set to launch a new direct cargo ferry service to the Maldives, in the third week of September, as per the Indian high commission in the Maldives.

Concept note-3: -Kochi to The Maldives – A 700 KM Ferry Ride If you love the travel that gives you the sight of the mighty sea, involves the adventure water sports and serenity of nature then we have good news for you!

Concept note-4: -The cargo ferry was a transport manufactured by Kiara Cargo Corporation. It was a freighter with six engines. The ferry could carry cargo both internally and externally in attached containers, or externally carry captured spacecraft.
