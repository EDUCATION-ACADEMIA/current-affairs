India ranked at which place in the list of countries with most Publications on AI from 2015-2019?

(A) 1st

(B) 2nd

(C) ** 3rd

(D) 4th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India ranks 3rd in list of Countries with most Publications on AI from 2015-2019, China tops list : Nature Index 2020 Artificial Intelligence.

Concept note-2: -Jumping 19 places from last year, India has ranked 32nd among 181 nations in the AI Readiness Index 2022.

Concept note-3: -Oxford Insights has published its AI Readiness Index 2022. India is ranked at 32nd position among 181 nations in the report. The country has improved its previous year’s ranking by 19 places.

Concept note-4: -However, some countries are known for their significant contributions to the field of AI, including the United States, China, Canada, and several European countries, such as the United Kingdom, France, and Germany.
