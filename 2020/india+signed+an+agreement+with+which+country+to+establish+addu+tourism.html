India signed an agreement with which country to establish Addu Tourism?

(A) Sri Lanka

(B) ** Maldives

(C) Nepal

(D) Malta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. India recently signed agreements with which country to establish a Tourism Zone in its Addu islands? Notes: India and Maldives signed 5 agreements for establishing ‘Addu Tourism zone’ in five islands of Addu atoll. An atoll is a ring-shaped chain of islands formed of coral.
