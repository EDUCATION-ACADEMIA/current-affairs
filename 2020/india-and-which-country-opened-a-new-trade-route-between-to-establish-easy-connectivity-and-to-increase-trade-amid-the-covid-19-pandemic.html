India and which country opened a new trade route between to establish easy connectivity and to increase trade amid the COVID-19 pandemic?

(A) Nepal

(B) Bangladesh

(C) ** Bhutan

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Alexander’s invasion opened up trade routes and enabled Indian traders to trade on the Silk Route.

Concept note-2: -Option 1 is correct i.e. Bhutan. India and Bhutan have opened a new trade route between Jaigaon in West Bengal and Pasakha in Bhutan.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Ocean trade routes connected Southeast Asia, India, Arabia, and East Africa, beginning at least as early as the third century BCE. This vast international web of routes linked all of those areas as well as East Asia (particularly China).
