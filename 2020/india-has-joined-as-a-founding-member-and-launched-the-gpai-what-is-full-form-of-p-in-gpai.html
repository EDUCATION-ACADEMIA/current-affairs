India has joined as a founding member and launched the GPAI. What is full form of P in GPAI?

(A) Programme

(B) ** Partnership

(C) Plan

(D) Purposed

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has urged member states of the Global Partnership on Artificial Intelligence (GPAI) to work together on evolving a common framework of rules and regulations to prevent user harm from Artificial Intelligence (AI).

Concept note-2: -Share. The Global Partnership on Artificial Intelligence (GPAI) is a multi-stakeholder initiative which aims to bridge the gap between theory and practice on AI by supporting cutting-edge research and applied activities on AI-related priorities.

Concept note-3: -India joined the GPAI in 2020 as a founding member. Artificial Intelligence is expected to add 967 billion US dollars to the Indian economy by 2035.

Concept note-4: -The founding members are: Australia, Canada, France, Germany, India, Italy, Japan, Mexico, New Zealand, the Republic of Korea, Singapore, Slovenia, the UK, the US, and the EU. The G7 countries in GPAI are Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the UK, and the US.

Concept note-5: -India joined the GPAI in 2020 as a founding member.
