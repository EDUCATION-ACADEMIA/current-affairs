India received 2nd batch of which Aircraft?

(A) MiG 21

(B) Sukhoi

(C) ** Rafale

(D) Mirage

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Rafale is a 4.5-generation aircraft and has helped India regain its supremacy over Indian subconstituent skies with long-range air-to-air and air-to-ground along with advanced radar and electronic warfare capabilities.

Concept note-2: -India has already procured 36 Rafale fighter jets for the IAF from Dassault Aviation at a cost of nearly Rs 60, 000 crore. Anirban Bhaumik, DHNS, New Delhi, Jan 05 2023, 21:59 ist. updated: Jan 05 2023, 22:08 ist.

Concept note-3: -F-21: For India. The F-21 addresses the Indian Air Force’s unique requirements and integrates India into the world’s largest fighter aircraft ecosystem with the world’s pre-eminent defence company. Lockheed Martin and Tata would produce the F-21 in India, for India.

Concept note-4: -In 2019, Sweden and Italy joined the Tempest project. During the same year, India and Japan were also invited to join the project. On 1 April 2020, the Japanese F-X program was announced.
