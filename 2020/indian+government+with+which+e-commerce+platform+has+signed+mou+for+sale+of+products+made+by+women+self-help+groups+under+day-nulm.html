CURRENT AFFAIRS-2020
[SOURCES]

Indian government with which e-commerce platform has signed MoU for sale of products made by women self-help groups under DAY-NULM?

(A) Alibaba

(B) ** Flipkart

(C) Amazon

(D) Snapdeal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) which functions under Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs recently signed MoU with e-commerce major Amazon. The agreement is for e-marketing of products made by Self Help Groups (SHGs) across the country in urban areas.

Concept note-2: -Flipkart signs an MoU with Rural Development Ministry for the DAY-NRLM program.

Concept note-3: -MoU is a part of the Flipkart Samarth program. It was signed with the aim of providing skilled & underserved communities of weavers, craftsmen, and artisans with national market access using the Flipkart marketplace. It will also provide them with dedicated support for knowledge and training.
