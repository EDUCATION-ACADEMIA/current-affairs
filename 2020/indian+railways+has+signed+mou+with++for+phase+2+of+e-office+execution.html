Indian Railways has signed MoU with ____ for Phase 2 of e-Office execution?

(A) RITES

(B) Rail Vikas Nigam

(C) ** RailTel

(D) WAPCOS Ltd.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Railways signs MoU with RailTel for Phase 2 of e-Office Execution. After the successful completion of the Phase 1 execution of NIC e office for 50000 users in 58 units, Indian Railways has signed MoU with RailTel, a miniratna PSU under Ministry of Railways, for the phase 2 of the project.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The MoU between the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, India and the Federal Ministry of Transport, Innovation and Technology, Austria was approved by the Cabinet.

Concept note-3: -In supersession of Board’s letter under reference, the revised format for entering into Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between Railways and CRIS for execution of IT projects is enclosed herewith.

Concept note-4: -In a bid to expand the green cover, the Maharashtra government recently signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Indian Railways, under which trees will be planted on the land adjacent to the railway tracks in the state.

Concept note-5: -What is e-office? The e-Office system is an integrated file and records management system that allows employees to manage content, search for data internally and collaborate. The file system also enables the electronic movement and the tracking of files, and the archival and retrieval of data. place?
