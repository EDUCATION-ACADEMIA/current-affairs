Indian Army and police of which state conduct joint anti-terror exercise in Pune?

(A) Kerala

(B) ** Maharashtra

(C) Punjab

(D) J & K

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The joint exercise was held at Lullanagar in Pune on Tuesday and saw participation of Counter Terrorism Task Force (CTTF) of the Indian Army, Anti-Terrorism Squad (ATS) of Maharashtra Police alongwith Quick Reaction Teams (QRTs), dog squads and bomb disposal teams of both agencies.

Concept note-2: -The multilateral joint anti-terror exercise “Manesar Anti-Terror 2022, ” hosted by India under the framework of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO), concluded on Thursday. The multilateral Joint anti-terror exercise hosted by India under the SCO concluded.

Concept note-3: -Ex Dharma Guardian – 2022 is a joint military exercise between India and Japan, is being conducted at the Foreign Training Node in Belagavi.

Concept note-4: -UPSC Mains Q. ‘Suraksha Kavach 2’ is a Joint Security Exercise organised by Indian Army and which state? Notes: Suraksha Kavach 2, a Joint Security Exercise was organised between Indian Army and Maharashtra Police at Lullanagar, Pune.
