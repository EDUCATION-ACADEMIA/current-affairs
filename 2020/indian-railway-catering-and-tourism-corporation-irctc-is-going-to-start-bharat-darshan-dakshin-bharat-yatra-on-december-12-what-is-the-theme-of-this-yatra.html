Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is going to start ‘Bharat Darshan-Dakshin Bharat Yatra’ on December 12. What is the Theme of this Yatra?

(A) Show India for Tourism

(B) Show Dakshin Bharat

(C) ** Show India to Indians

(D) Show Dakshin Bharat to Indians

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of the yatra, which will cover Sri Rangam (Ranganathaswamy temple), Brihadeswara temple at Thanjavur, Rameswaram, Madurai Meenakshi and Kanyakumari, is to ‘Show India to Indians’.

Concept note-2: -Based on the theme of Unity in Diversity, the park showcases renowned replicas of Indian monuments. In the evening time, Light and Sound Show is organized that is very entertaining.

Concept note-3: -State of Art Bharat Gaurav Deluxe AC Tourist Train with 1st AC & 2nd AC Class will be operated for 8 days all inclusive tour. Tourist Train consists of 4 first AC coaches, 2 second AC Coaches, one well equipped pantry car and two rail restaurants. It can accommodate 156 tourists onboard.

Concept note-4: -The Bharat Gaurav trains were launched by the Railways Ministry to be operated in places having touristic importance and promote theme-based tourism.

Concept note-5: -Train Itinerary : Delhi Safdarjung – Nasik Road – Hospate – Rameshwaram – Bhadrachalam Road – Nagpur – Delhi Safdarjung.. Boarding / Deboarding : Delhi, Mathura, Agra, Gwalior, Virangana Lakshmibai, Lalitpur, Bina, Bhopal. No. seats : 600 (Comfort – 60, Superior – 270 & Standard – 270)
