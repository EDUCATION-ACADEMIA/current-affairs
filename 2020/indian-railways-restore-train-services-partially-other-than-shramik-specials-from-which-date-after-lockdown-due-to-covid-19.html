Indian Railways restore train services partially (Other than Shramik specials) from which date after lockdown due to COVID-19?

(A) May 13

(B) May 11

(C) ** May 12

(D) May 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cancellation Charges for Partial Cancellation of Train In case of partial train cancellation, train diverted, train short terminated etc., TDR is required to be filed online up to Seventy Two Hours of the Scheduled Departure of the Train at Passenger’s Boarding Station.

Concept note-2: -Refund on Partial Waitlisted tickets All refunds on partially waitlisted tickets will be entertained only if ‘non-travel’ certificate from the TTE of the train to the effect that the partially wait-listed passengers have not travelled is obtained and submitted along with refund application.

Concept note-3: -Get only once chance Indian Railways says that the transfer of tickets can be done only once, that is, if the passenger has transferred his ticket once to another person, then he cannot change it, that is, now this ticket cannot be transferred to anyone else.
