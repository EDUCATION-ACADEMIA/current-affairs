India’s 1st CoE for the Skill Development in Power Sector inaugurated in which city?

(A) Shillong

(B) Lucknow

(C) Shillong

(D) ** Gurugram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Centre has been established in the campus of National Institute of Solar Energy, Gurugram, Haryana.

Concept note-2: -Odisha CM Naveen Patnaik inaugurated India’s first World Skill Centre (WSC) at Mancheswar Industrial Area of Bhubaneswar on 5 March 2021.

Concept note-3: -Q. India’s first ‘Centre of Excellence for Skill Development’ in Power Sector has been established in collaboration with which country? Notes: A Centre of Excellence for Skill Development in Power Sector has been established in Gurugram Haryana. It is instituted at the National Institute of Solar Energy campus.

Concept note-4: -Underscoring Prime Minister’s vision of making India the Skill Capital of the World, Shri Dharmendra Pradhan, Union Minister of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship and Minister of Education, today launched India’s first Skill India International Centre at SDI Bhubaneshwar.

Concept note-5: -Our partnership with Government of France for instituting the Centre of Excellence is another shining example of cooperation between the countries to achieve their long-terms energy goals.”
