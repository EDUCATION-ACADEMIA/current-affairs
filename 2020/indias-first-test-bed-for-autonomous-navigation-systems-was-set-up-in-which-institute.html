India’s first Test bed for Autonomous Navigation Systems was set up in which Institute?

(A) IIT Kanpur

(B) IIT Bombay

(C) IIT Madras

(D) ** IIT Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first autonomous navigation facility “TiHAN” launched at IIT Hyderabad . India’s first Autonomous Navigation facility, TiHAN inaugurated by Union Minister of State for Science & Technology, Jitendra Singh at the IIT Hyderabad campus.

Concept note-2: -TiHAN-IITH testbed on Autonomous Navigations (Aerial & Terrestrial) will help in accurate testing of next generation autonomous navigation technologies.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Q. What is the name of ‘India’s first Autonomous Navigation facility’ inaugurated recently? Notes: India’s first Autonomous Navigation facility, TiHAN was inaugurated by Jitendra Singh, Union Minister of State for Science & Technology at the IIT Hyderabad campus.

Concept note-4: -The Minister inaugurates the TiHAN Testbed for Autonomous Navigation at IIT Hyderabad, a unique, first of its kind, state of the art, futuristic “autonomous navigation” facility to develop unmanned ground and aerial vehicles.

Concept note-5: -TiHAN: India’s first technology innovation hub on autonomous navigation facility launched at IIT Hyderabad.
