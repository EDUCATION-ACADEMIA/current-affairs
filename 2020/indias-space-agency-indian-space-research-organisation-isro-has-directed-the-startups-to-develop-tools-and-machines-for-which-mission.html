India’s space agency Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has directed the startups to develop tools and machines for which mission?

(A) Outer Space Mission

(B) Space exploration

(C) ** Gaganyaan-1 mission

(D) Mars mission

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISRO’s vision is stated as “Harness space technology for national development while pursuing space science research and planetary exploration.”

Concept note-2: -Isro is planning to launch the Aditya Sun mission in the early months of 2023. The spacecraft will be placed in a halo orbit around the Lagrange point 1 (L1) of the Sun-Earth system, which is about 1.5 million km from our planet.

Concept note-3: -Space: Fact or Fiction? ISRO’s first satellite, Aryabhata, was launched by the Soviet Union on April 19, 1975.

Concept note-4: -Gaganyaan Mission was launched by the ISRO, and it aims to send a three-person crew to space by 2022. The Prime Minister of India announced this mission on Independence Day in 2018. In addition to this manned Gaganyaan Mission, ISRO also plans to launch two unmanned missions ahead of it.
