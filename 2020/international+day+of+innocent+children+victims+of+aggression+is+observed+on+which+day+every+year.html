International Day of Innocent Children Victims of Aggression is observed on which day every year?

(A) 1 June

(B) ** 4 June

(C) 2 June

(D) 3 June

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The purpose of the day is to acknowledge the pain suffered by children throughout the world who are the victims of physical, mental and emotional abuse. This day affirms the UN’s commitment to protect the rights of children.

Concept note-2: -International Day of Innocent Children Victims of Aggression – June 4, 2023. International Day of Innocent Children Victims of Aggression is an annual observance that happens on June 4 every year to acknowledge the pain suffered by children throughout the world who are victims of physical, mental, and emotional abuse.
