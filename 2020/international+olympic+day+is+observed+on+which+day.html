International Olympic Day is observed on which day?

(A) ** 23 June

(B) 20 June

(C) 22 June

(D) 18 June

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International Olympic Day is celebrated on June 23 each year and was first observed in 1948. The Olympics is the world’s largest international multi-sport event held every four years. Thousands of athletes and sportspersons from all across the world take part in various games and sports.

Concept note-2: -Each year June 23 is a day when athletes and people worldwide congregate and organise various sporting events. Nations across the world have their respective Olympic committees that overlook the celebrations that take place on International Olympic Day.

Concept note-3: -Theme of International Olympic Day 2022 The theme this year is ‘Together, For a Peaceful World’, which will be accompanied by the social media hashtags #MoveForPeace and #OlympicDay. The World Olympic Day 2022 emphasises the power that sport has to build a better world and brings people together in peace.

Concept note-4: -Olympic Day commemorates the founding of the International Olympic Committee on 23 June 1894 by Baron Pierre de Coubertin.
