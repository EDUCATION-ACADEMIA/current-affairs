International Day of Cooperatives was celebrated on which day this year?

(A) 2 July

(B) 1 July

(C) 3 July

(D) ** 4 July

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2022 International Day of Cooperatives: Cooperatives Build a Better World.

Concept note-2: -Tools. International Day of Cooperatives is celebrated on the first Saturday of July every year. It was first celebrated under the auspices of the United Nations in 2005, though its history as a day celebrated by the international cooperative movement and the International Co-operative Alliance goes back 1923.

Concept note-3: -2022 Coopsday theme A decade on from the UN International Year of Cooperatives, which showcased the unique contribution of cooperatives to making the world a better place, this year’s #CoopsDay slogan –“Cooperatives Build a Better World”– echoes the theme of the International Year.

Concept note-4: -The theme of 100th International Cooperative Day is “Cooperatives Build a Better World”.
