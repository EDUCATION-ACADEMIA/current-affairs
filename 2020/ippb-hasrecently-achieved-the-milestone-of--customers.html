IPPB hasrecently achieved the milestone of ____ customers?

(A) 1 crore

(B) ** 2 crore

(C) 3 crore

(D) 4 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India Post Payments Bank (IPPB), a digital fist bank, said it achieved the milestone of onboarding 5 customers in just 3 years of inception.

Concept note-2: -Opened in 2018, as of January 2022, the bank has more than 5 crore customers.

Concept note-3: -A. You can avail yourself of services such as easy bill payments, Doorstep Banking services, instant money transfer through IMPS and other remittance services. In addition, you will get the benefit of Virtual Debit card, QR card, phone banking, SMS banking, free quarterly email statements and SMS alerts.

Concept note-4: -IPPB uses DoP’s giant network of 189, 000 GDS to deliver financial products and services at the convenience of the customers’ doorstep. Further, the trusted network of GDS offers handholding support to customers to bring low- and moderate-income (LMI) people into the fold of digital payments.
