Janaushadhi week is celebrated in India from which date?

(A) 22-28 February

(B) ** 1-7 March

(C) 8-14 March

(D) 15-21 March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In news– The 4th Janaushadhi Diwas 2022 was observed from 1st March to 7th March.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers will organize the Janaushadhi Diwas from 1st March to 7th March 2022. Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers will organize the Janaushadhi Diwas from 1st March to 7th March 2022.

Concept note-3: -The 4th Janaushadhi Diwas 2022 celebrations commenced today. It will be a weeklong celebration starting today ie from 1st March to 7th March 2022. Jan Aushadhi kendras today conducted Jan Aushadhi Sankalp Padyatra at 75 locations, across the country.

Concept note-4: -The Jan Aushadhi Diwas is celebrated on 7 March 2022 by the Pharmaceuticals & Medical Devices Bureau of India (PMBI). The day is observed to spread awareness about the usages of generic medicines and the benefits of Jan Aushadhi Pariyojana. The theme of the 4 Janaushadhi Diwas is “Jan Aushadhi-Jan Upyogi”.
