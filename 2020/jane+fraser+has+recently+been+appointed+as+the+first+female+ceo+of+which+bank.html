Jane Fraser has recently been appointed as the first female CEO of which bank?

(A) ** Citigroup

(B) Bank of America

(C) RBI

(D) Standard Chartered Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -She is the first female CEO in the firm’s history. Jane has deep experience across Citi’s consumer and institutional businesses and, in many ways, she helped shape Citi into the company it is today.

Concept note-2: -Chair of the Board and Chief Executive Officer. Brian Moynihan leads a team of more than 210, 000 employees dedicated to making financial lives better for people, companies of every size, and institutional investors across the United States and around the world.

Concept note-3: -Institutional Investors own CitiGroup. The top shareholders of the company include: The Vanguard Group, Inc (8.27%) SSga Funds Management, Inc (4.8%) Berkshire Hathaway (2.84%)
