Khelo India University Games 2020 recently concluded in which city?

(A) Guwahati

(B) ** Bhubaneswar

(C) Kolkata

(D) Raipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -History. On 22 February 2020, Prime Minister Narendra Modi, inaugurated the first edition of the Khelo India University Games in Cuttack.

Concept note-2: -KIIT University in Bhubaneswar, which is located in the state of Odisha, is slated to host the first Khelo India University Games in 2022.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bhubaneshwar. The first edition of the 2020 Khelo India University Games was held from February 22 to March 1, 2020, at KIIT University. It was organized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports.

Concept note-4: -Khelo India University Games 2022: Jain University Clinches KIUG Crown; Swimmer Siva Sridhar Reigns Supreme. Lovely Professional University finished second while defending champions Punjab University had to be satisfied with the third spot.

Concept note-5: -Athletics, wrestling, boxing, shooting, kayaking-canoeing, rowing, volleyball, judo and swimming are to be held in Bhopal in the Khelo India Youth Games.
