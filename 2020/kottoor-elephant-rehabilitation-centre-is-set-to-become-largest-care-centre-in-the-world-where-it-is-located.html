Kottoor Elephant Rehabilitation Centre is set to become largest care centre in the World, where it is located?

(A) Delhi

(B) Odisha

(C) Rajasthan

(D) ** Thiruvananthapuram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kottur Elephant Sanctuary and Rehabilitation Centre located near Kappukadu in Thiruvananthapuram district of Kerala is an elephant sanctuary and rehabilitation centre.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Thiruvananthapuram. The Elephant Rehabilitation centre at Kottoor in Thiruvananthapuram is set to become the largest care centre and cure centre for elephants in the world. The project is being implemented at a cost of Rs 108 crore with funding from Kerala Infrastructure Investment Board.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The Kerala government is to set up the country’s first elephant rehabilitation centre in Kottoor, an eco-tourism village. This scheme is started by CM of Kerala, 105 crore Rs. has been allocated for it.

Concept note-4: -The Kerala government is working to set up the country’s first elephant rehabilitation centre in Kottoor, an ecotourism village near the state capital Thiruvananthapuram. The first phase of the Rs 105 crore-project was launched last month by chief minister Pinarayi Vijayan.
