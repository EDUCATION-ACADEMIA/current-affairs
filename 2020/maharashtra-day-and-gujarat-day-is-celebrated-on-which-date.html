Maharashtra Day and Gujarat Day is celebrated on which date?

(A) 2 May

(B) 30 April

(C) 28 April

(D) ** 1 May

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 1 May. May 1 is celebrated as ‘Maharashtra Day’ and ‘Gujarat Day’ every year. On this day, 57 years ago, the existing state of Maharashtra was formed.

Concept note-2: -Maharashtra and Gujarat celebrated their statehood day on 1st May 2022. On May 1st, 1960, the bilingual state of Bombay was divided into two separate states by the Bombay Reorganisation Act, 1960: Maharashtra for Marathi speaking people and Gujarat for Gujarati speaking people.

Concept note-3: -Gujarat Day is observed on May 1 to mark the creation of Gujarat as a separate state. In 1960, two states - Gujarat and Maharashtra - were carved out of Bombay State. Since then, May 1 is celebrated in both the states with great zeal and enthusiasm.

Concept note-4: -Gujarat was carved out of the Bombay state on May 1, 1960 after the Parliament passed the Bombay Reorganisation Act. Gujarat Day is being celebrated on May 1 to mark the creation of a newly carved state. The Gujarati-speaking state was carved out of Maharashtra in 1960.

Concept note-5: -Gujarat Day: For Gujarat Day celebrations this year, a grand event will take place in Patan town, for which elaborate preparations have been made. The Gujarat state came into existence on May 1, 1960.
