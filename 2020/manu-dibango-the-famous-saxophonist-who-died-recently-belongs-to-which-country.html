Manu Dibango, the famous saxophonist who died recently belongs to which country?

(A) Republic of Congo

(B) ** Republic of Cameroon

(C) Republic of Macedonia

(D) Republic of China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Emmanuel N’Djoké Dibango was born on Dec. 12, 1933, in Douala, the largest city in Cameroon. His father was a civil servant; his mother was a dressmaker. He grew up listening to Protestant church music, local traditional music and Westernized pop.

Concept note-2: -He was celebrated for his unique tunes that blended American funk and traditional jazz with local Cameroonian rhythms to form a unique genre, with fans remembering him as a man whose hearty laughter filled his conversations.
