Concept note-1: -Mary Higgins Clark, née Mary Theresa Eleanor Higgins, (born December 24, 1927, Bronx, New York, U.S.-died January 31, 2020, Naples, Florida), American mystery and suspense writer who for more than four decades was a fixture on best-seller lists. Higgins began writing poetry at the age of six.
