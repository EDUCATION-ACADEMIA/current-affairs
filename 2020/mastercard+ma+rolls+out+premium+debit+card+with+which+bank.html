Mastercard (MA) rolls out premium Debit Card with which bank?

(A) SBI

(B) PNB

(C) ** SBM

(D) HDFC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai: YES BANK, in partnership with Mastercard, announced the launch of YES Private Debit Card for the Bank’s ultra-high net worth individual (UHNI) customers.

Concept note-2: -Major banks like BDO, Metrobank, Security Bank, RCBC, BPI and PNB issues Debit Mastercard.

Concept note-3: -Perfect for the leisure traveller, the Platinum Debit Mastercard delivers a mix of travel benefits, privileges and experiences. So whether it’s airport concierge services or securing the best restaurant in town, get the most out from this card. Get a Mastercard. MASTERCARD BENEFIT INQUIRIES.

Concept note-4: -Debit and credit cards allow cardholders to withdraw cash and make purchases. Credit cards are debt instruments but debit cards are not. Debit card users can only spend the money available in their bank account unless they have overdraft protection.

Concept note-5: -If you use a debit card or credit card, you will see the logo for either Visa or Mastercard® in the corner of it.
