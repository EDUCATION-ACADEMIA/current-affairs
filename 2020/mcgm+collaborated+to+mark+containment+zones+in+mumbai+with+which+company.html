MCGM collaborated to mark Containment Zones in Mumbai with which company?

(A) Microsoft

(B) ** Google

(C) Flipkart

(D) Amazon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Amazon India provides special Delivery Services in containment zones in Mumbai in collaboration with BMC.

Concept note-2: -BMC Elections 2022: Mumbai has been divided into 24 administrative wards that are ordered alphabetically from Ward A to Ward T. BMC ELECTIONS 2022: The capital of Maharashtra, Mumbai, is administered by the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) or Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai (MCGM) which is a civic body.

Concept note-3: -The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC; IAST: bṛhanmuṃbaī mahānagarapālikā), also known as the Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai (MCGM), is the governing civic body of Mumbai, the capital city of Maharashtra.

Concept note-4: -The city is divided is into six zones and further divided into 24 wards for administrative convenience. Each ward is headed by an Assistant Commissioner. Further, the Corporation appoints Heads of Departments of the numerous departments that work with a specific mandate such as Water, Health, Sanitation.
