Men’s T20 World cup, 2020 will hold in which country?

(A) ** Australia

(B) New Zealand

(C) India

(D) West Indies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 28 June 2021, BCCI President Sourav Ganguly confirmed that due to the COVID-19 situation in the country the board has officially communicated to the ICC about their decision to move the event from India to the UAE. Some of the preliminary round matches of the event were also set to be held in Oman.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Australia. Australia hosted the 2020 ICC Women’s T20 World Cup.

Concept note-3: -The ICC Men’s Cricket T20 World Cup 2022, the 8th T20 Cricket World Cup, has been scheduled between October 16 and November 13, 2022, in Australia.

Concept note-4: -The T20 World Cup 2022 will be held in Australia on October 16, 2022, and this is the first time that australia has been given a chance to host such a big event as cricket’s Twenty20 competitions. The ICC Men’s cricket T20 World Cup 2022 will be held between October 16, 2022 and November 13, 2022 in Australia.
