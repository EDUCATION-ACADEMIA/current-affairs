Mount Merapi spewed ash and hot gas in a massive column as high as 6km into the sky. Mount Merapi is located in which country?

(A) ** Indonesia

(B) Singapore

(C) Thailand

(D) Vietnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The frequently active Merapi is located immediately north of the major city of Yogyakarta in central Java, Indonesia. Volcanism has included lava flows, pyroclastic flows, lahars, explosions with heavy ashfall, incandescent block avalanches, block-and-ash flows, and cycles of dome growth and destruction.

Concept note-2: -Indonesia, Volcanic Eruption Mount Merapi in Yogyakarta and Central Java (9 Mar 2022)

Concept note-3: -Merapi volcano is situated above the subduction zone between the Eurasian and Indo-Australian tectonic plates. This subduction extends for about 2000 km from Sumatra to the Lesser Sunda Islands.

Concept note-4: -Mount Merapi is the most active of more than 120 active volcanoes in Indonesia and has repeatedly erupted with lava and gas clouds recently.
