NABARD approved ____ to boost infrastructure in Jammu and Kashmir?

(A) Rs.100 crore

(B) Rs.200 crore

(C) Rs.300 crore

(D) ** Rs.400 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -JAMMU: Aligning with its vision and mission for fostering rural prosperity, the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has enhanced support to various sectors of rural economy for sustainable growth and development of Union Territories of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh during 2021-22.

Concept note-2: -Rural Infrastructure Development Fund (RIDF) A major policy initiative for rural infrastructure development has been the setting up of RIDF in NABARD in 1995 for financing rural infrastructure projects. RIDF was set up to fund infrastructure projects in States, which are incomplete due to lack of financial resources.
