Name the Indian state which approves 5% Reservation in Judicial Service for MBC.

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Gujarat

(C) ** Rajasthan

(D) Andra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With the passage of this bill, five castes including Gurjar, Gadiya, Luhar, Banjara, Rebari, and Raika which were previously classified as OBC, have now been classified as MBC (Most Backward Classes).

Concept note-2: -Andhra Pradesh state percentage of reservation is around 50%. Including the overall 1/3 reservation for women, 66.66% of seats are reserved in Andhra Pradesh in Education and Government jobs. Andhra Pradesh BC quota has sub-quotas for castes, classified in A, B, C and D.

Concept note-3: -Hi Candidate, MBC category refers to the backward class Christians and backward class Muslims of Tamilnadu.

Concept note-4: -The Rajasthan government has also increased the annual income limit for the creamy layer in the more backward classes (MBC) and backward classes (BC) to ₹8 lakh from an earlier limit of ₹4.30 lakh.
