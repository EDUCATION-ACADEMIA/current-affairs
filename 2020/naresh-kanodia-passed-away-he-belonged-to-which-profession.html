Naresh Kanodia passed away, he belonged to which profession?

(A) Dancer

(B) Singer

(C) ** Actor

(D) Politician

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Naresh Kanodia (20 August 1943 – 27 October 2020) was an Indian Gujarati film actor, singer, musician and politician from Gujarat.

Concept note-2: -Hitu Kanodia is an Indian politician and served as MLA from Idar constituency, Gujarat. He was born on 21 February 1970 to actor-politician Naresh Kanodia and Ratan Kanodia. Kanodia started his film career as a child artist and acted in over 100 Gujarati films till date.

Concept note-3: -Naresh Kanodia(1943-2020) Naresh Kanodia was born on 20 August 1943 in Kanoda, India. He was an actor, known for Jagya Tyathi Sawaar (1981), Kanku Ni Kimat (1983) and Aanganiya Sajavo Raaj (1999). He was previously married to Rima Kanodia.
