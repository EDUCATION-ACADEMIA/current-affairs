Nation observed the Antyodaya Divas on which date?

(A) 24 September

(B) ** 25 September

(C) 26 September

(D) 27 September

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Every year, Antyodaya Diwas is celebrated in India on September 25 to mark the birth anniversary of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya and remember his life and legacy. In 2014, Prime Minister Narendra Modi dedicated the day in honour of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya.

Concept note-2: -Every year on September 25, World Pharmacist Day is commemorated with the goal of highlighting and advocating the pharmacist’s contribution to health improvement.

Concept note-3: -Antyodaya Diwas 2022: Theme The importance of this day lies in “reaching out to the last person”.

Concept note-4: -Antyodaya means “uplifting the poorest of the poor” or “rise of the last person". The day was declared by the Government of India on September 25, 2014 and is being officially celebrated since the year 2015.

Concept note-5: -Antyodaya Diwas is celebrated annually on 25th September in India. It marks the birth anniversary of Indian leader Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya and is celebrated in his honour to remember his life and legacy.
