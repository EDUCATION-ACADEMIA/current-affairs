NATIONAL CAPPUCCINO DAY is being observed annually on which date?

(A) 7 November

(B) ** 8 November

(C) 9 November

(D) 10 November

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Cappuccino Day (November 8th) | Days Of The Year.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi: National Cappuccino Day is celebrated on November 8 every year. The day reminds us to allow loved ones to take a respite from their daily lives and take a sip of cappuccino, an Italian coffee drink that is traditionally prepared with equal parts double espresso, steamed milk, and steamed milk foam.

Concept note-3: -Did you know that November 8th is National Cappuccino Day? Every year on November 8th, millions of coffee fiends (including myself) in the US celebrate by enjoying a cup of rich espresso and steamed milk with a smooth layer of foam on top. Cappuccinos are my favorite coffee drink of all time.

Concept note-4: -National days on Thu Nov 16th, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s National Fast Food Day, National Button Day, Clarinet Day, Social Enterprise Day, Use Less Stuff Day… and much more!

Concept note-5: -9th November- National Legal Services Day In India, the 9th of November is observed as National Legal Services Day to raise awareness among people in areas where legal literacy is lacking. The Legal Services Authorities Act was enacted in 1995, and people have been aware of the lack of legal literacy since then.
