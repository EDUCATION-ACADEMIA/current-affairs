National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro-Sciences (NIMHANS) is in which Indian city?

(A) Indore

(B) Patna

(C) ** Bangalore

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences (NIMHANS), situated in the heart of the garden city, is a familiar landmark. The NIMHANS campus is located on Hosur Road-an arterial road that emanates from the centre of Bengaluru and leads to Electronics City (and stretches up to Hosur in Tamil Nadu).

Concept note-2: -NIMHANS has five campuses in the city, spread over an area of 174 acres of urban establishments, which includes 30 acres of an under-construction Bangalore North campus.

Concept note-3: -The Delhi Shahdra Junction station is the nearest one to National Institute Of Mental Health &amp; Neuro Sciences (Nimhans) in Delhi.

Concept note-4: -The National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences Bangalore is a government institution located in Bangalore, Karnataka.
