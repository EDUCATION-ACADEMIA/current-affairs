National Law Day is celebrated on which date?

(A) November 22

(B) November 25

(C) ** November 26

(D) November 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Law Day - also known as Samvidhan Divas - is celebrated in India on November 26 every year to commemorate the adoption of the Constitution of India.

Concept note-2: -Constitution Day also known as ‘Samvidhan Divas’, is celebrated in our country on 26th November every year to commemorate the adoption of the Constitution of India. On 26th November 1949, the Constituent Assembly of India adopted the Constitution of India, which came into effect from 26th January 1950.

Concept note-3: -26 November 2022 will be a bank holiday on the second Saturday. 27 November 2022 will be a weekly Sunday holiday.

Concept note-4: -Constitution Day (IAST: Samvidhāna Divasa), also known as “National Law Day”, is celebrated in India on 26 November every year to commemorate the adoption of the Constitution of India.

Concept note-5: -L.M. Singhvi, the then President of Supreme Court Bar Assocation – an association of practicing lawyers of Supreme Court - proposed that 26th November be celebrated as Law Day. Though there had been no official declaration, the day was observed as ‘Law Day’ since 1979.
