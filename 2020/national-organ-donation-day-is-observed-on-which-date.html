National Organ Donation Day is observed on which date?

(A) 23 November

(B) ** 27 November

(C) 15 August

(D) 26 November

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In India, National Organ Donation Day is observed on November 27 from 2010. Donating organs means you are giving someone a new lease of life.

Concept note-2: -August 13th is celebrated as World Organ Donation day. India celebrates its Organ Donation day on 27th November across the country. The aim of this day is to motivate normal human beings to pledge to donate organs after death, and to spread awareness about the importance of organ donation.

Concept note-3: -Complete answer: 1) Let’s share our life with others. 2) Come ahead and take part in saving lives. 3) No caste – No bar – save lives – donate organs. 4) Giving a second change of life is in your hands.

Concept note-4: -World Organ Donation Day 2022: Theme This year’s theme for World Organ Donation Day 2022 is “let’s pledge to donate organs and save lives”. The Day is celebrated to raise awareness of the need for donating organs and to thank donors for their life-saving contributions.

Concept note-5: -First district hospital State Health Commissioner Randeep D. told The Hindu that Chikkamagaluru has become the first district hospital in the State to identify a potential organ donor. With this, Karnataka has recorded the second highest organ donations among Southern States after Telangana this year, he said.
