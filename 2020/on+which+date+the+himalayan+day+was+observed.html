On which date the Himalayan Day was observed?

(A) 7 September

(B) 8 September

(C) ** 9 September

(D) 10 September

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Himalayan Day is celebrated on 9 September every year. The Himalayas plays an important role to save and sustain nature and protect the country from adverse weather conditions. The Himalayan range is also responsible for rain besides being a rich repository of floral and faunal biodiversity.

Concept note-2: -Himalaya Day is celebrated every year on 9 September with an aim to preserve the Himalayan ecosystem and region.

Concept note-3: -’Himalaya Diwas’ or ‘Himalaya Day’ is celebrated on September 9 in Uttarakhand to raise awareness about the conservation of the rich flora and fauna of the state. In 2014, the then CM Harish Rawat declared September 9 as ‘Himalaya Diwas’ to propagate the message of conservation of the Himalayan ecosystems.

Concept note-4: -In Uttarakhand, Himalaya Day is observed on September 9 each year.

Concept note-5: -Himalayan Diwas 2022: Theme Himalaya Day 2022 was observed under the theme ‘Himalayas will be safe only when the interests of its residents are protected.
