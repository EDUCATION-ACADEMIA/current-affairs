On which agenda 8th edition of the North East Festival was held in Assam?

(A) Atmanirbhar Bharat

(B) Make in North East

(C) Mission COVID-19

(D) ** Growth Agenda for North East Post Covid-19

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Guwahati. Assam Governor Jagdish Mukhi inaugurated the 9th edition of the North East Festival (NEF) at a program held in Guwahati on 7 January 2022.

Concept note-2: -to December 26th, 2022 at Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium, New Delhi. year ahead!

Concept note-3: -MAJULI FESTIVAL, ASSAM The Majuli Festival, held in Garamur on the banks of the river Luit every year in November, is a four-day event that celebrates the Majuli region’s unique Neo-Vaishnavite history, as well as the culture of Assam as a whole.
