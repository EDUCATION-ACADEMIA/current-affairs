One of Bollywood’s most beloved stars Irrfan Khan passed away on 29 April 2020 due to which of the following diseases?

(A) Skin cancer

(B) ** Neuroendocrine tumour

(C) Leukemia

(D) Prostate cancer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of 2017, his films had grossed $3.643 billion (₹237 billion) at the worldwide box office. Khan was diagnosed with neuroendocrine cancer in March 2018, and died on 29 April 2020, at 53 years old, from a colon infection caused by the disease.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi: On this day, last year, actor Irrfan Khan died of cancer in Mumbai.

Concept note-3: -He was 53. His death was confirmed by a spokesman. Mr. Khan had cancer and was admitted to a hospital in Mumbai for a colon infection last week, the spokesman said.

Concept note-4: -Paan Singh Tomar (2010) Directed by Tigmanshu Dhulia, the film won a National Award in the best film category and also bagged Irrfan the National Award for Best Actor in 2013. He also received the Filmfare Award for Best Actor (Critics) for his performance in the film.
