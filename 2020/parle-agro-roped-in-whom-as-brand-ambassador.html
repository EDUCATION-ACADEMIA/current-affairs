Parle agro roped in whom as brand ambassador?

(A) Katrina Kaif

(B) Alia Bhatt

(C) Riya Chakarvati

(D) ** Priyanka Chopra Jonas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian beverage company, Parle Agro has signed actor Kriti Sanon as the brand ambassador for the country’s beverage brand, Appy Fizz . The brand refreshed its look and unveiled its new packaging, adding a new dimension to the brand and broadening its bold and edgy appeal, the company stated in the press release.

Concept note-2: -Charan joins a list of stars as brand ambassador of Parle Agro’s different brands, including Priyanka Chopra for Appy Fizz, Arjun Kapoor for B Fizz and Varun Dhawan for flavoured milk Smoodh.

Concept note-3: -Beverage major Parle Agro has signed Ram Charan as brand ambassador for the iconic Frooti brand along with Alia Bhatt.

Concept note-4: -Parle Agro has unveiled a campaign ‘Feel the Fizz’, for its fruit drink, Appy Fizz. Conceptualised by &Walsh, the TVC features the brand’s ambassador, actor Priyanka Chopra.

Concept note-5: -Since its inception in 1984, Parle Agro started its operations with Prakash Chauhan in command with his two daughters, Schauna and Nadia- joining the business too.
