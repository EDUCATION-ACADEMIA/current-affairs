PM Narendra Modi unveiled Rs 100 commemorative coin and postal stamp to mark foundation day of which university?

(A) Delhi University

(B) Calcutta University

(C) ** Lucknow University

(D) Patliputra University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Wednesday unveiled a commemorative coin of the centennial foundation day of the University of Lucknow through a video conference.

Concept note-2: -The First Stamp of Independent India was issued on 21 November 1947. It depicts the Indian Flag with the patriots’ slogan, Jai Hind (Long Live India), on the top right hand corner. It was valued at three and one-half annas.

Concept note-3: -The new system of prepaid postage was introduced in the reign of the then Governor-General, Lord Dalhousie.

Concept note-4: -The first postal stamp in India was introduced on 1 July 1852 in the Scinde district. In 1854 the introduction of uniform postage rates led to the development of the first postage stamps valid for use throughout India.

Concept note-5: -This stamp, known as the Penny Black, was the world’s first postage stamp. Before the postal reforms of 1840 sending a letter was expensive. The charge was for each sheet of paper that a letter comprised, and for the distance covered.
