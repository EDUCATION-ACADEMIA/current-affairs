Pradhan Mantri Jan-Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) completed how many years of successful implementation?

(A) Four

(B) Five

(C) ** Six

(D) Ten

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) - National Mission for Financial Inclusion, completes eight years of successful implementation.

Concept note-2: -PMJDY completes eight years;Accounts grew three-fold from 14.72 crore in March 2015 to 46.25 crore in Aug 2022. Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana PMJDY, the National Mission for Financial Inclusion completed eight years of successful implementation today, August 28th.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY), the biggest financial inclusion initiative in the world, was announced by the Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi on 15thAugust 2014 from the ramparts of the Red Fort and launched by him on 28th August 2014 across the country.

Concept note-4: -Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana Achivements With more than 46 billion bank accounts opened since August 28, 2014, and a deposit balance of Rs 1.74 lakh crore, the PMJDY has increased its reach to 67% of rural or semi-urban areas and 56% of women who have Jan Dhan accounts.

Concept note-5: -Meghalaya became the first state in India to achieve 100 percent success in implementing the Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana.
