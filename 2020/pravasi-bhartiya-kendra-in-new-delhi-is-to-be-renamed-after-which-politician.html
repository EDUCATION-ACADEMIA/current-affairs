Pravasi Bhartiya Kendra in New Delhi is to be renamed after which politician?

(A) Arun Jaitley

(B) Digvijay Singh

(C) ** Sushma Swaraj

(D) Atal Bihari Vajpayee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The MEA said, it is solemn tribute to the invaluable contribution of Mrs. Swaraj to Indian diplomacy, the cause of the Indian diaspora and the ethos of public service. During her four decade long career, Sushma Swaraj was elected to the Lok Sabha four times and thrice to Rajya Sabha.

Concept note-2: -In a solemn tribute to late external affairs minister Sushma Swaraj, the government has renamed Pravasi Bharatiya Kendra as Sushma Swaraj Bhawan, and Foreign Service Institute (Delhi) as Sushma Swaraj Institute of Foreign Service, on the eve of her 68th birth anniversary on Friday.

Concept note-3: -Sushma Swaraj Institute of Foreign Service.

Concept note-4: -Incumbent Narasimha Rao and I. K. Gujral. The current Minister of External Affairs is Subrahmanyam Jaishankar, succeeding Sushma Swaraj of the Bharatiya Janata Party on 30 May 2019.
