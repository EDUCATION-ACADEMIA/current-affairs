PravasiBharatiya Divas is celebrated on which date?

(A) 7th January

(B) 8th January

(C) ** 9th January

(D) 10th January

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated the 17th Pravasi Bharatiya Divas Convention in Indore, Madhya Pradesh today.

Concept note-2: -Embassy of India, Hague, Netherlands : The 17th Pravasi Bharatiya Divas (PBD) Convention - Indore, January 8-10, 2023.

Concept note-3: -The website for the 17th Pravasi Bharatiya Divas (PBD) Convention was jointly launched on 13 October 2022 by the External Affairs Minister, Dr. S. Jaishankar and the Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh, Shri Shivraj Singh Chouhan.

Concept note-4: -The 17th Pravasi Bharatiya Divas Convention is being organized in partnership with the Madhya Pradesh Government from 08-10 January 2023 in Indore.
