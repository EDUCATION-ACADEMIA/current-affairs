Qaboos bin Said Al Said, who recently died at 79 was the Sultan of which country?

(A) Saudi Arabia

(B) ** Oman

(C) Qatar

(D) Kuwait

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Qaboos bin Said Al Said (Arabic: , IPA: [qaː.buːs bin sa.ʕiːd ʔaːl sa.ʕiːd]; 18 November 1940 – 10 January 2020) was Sultan of Oman from 23 July 1970 until his death in 2020.

Concept note-2: -Omani Sultan Qaboos bin Said, seen during a 2010 ceremony in Muscat, died at the age of 79 after suffering for years from an undisclosed illness.

Concept note-3: -"Sultan Qaboos was the architect of the special ties between India and Oman. He had studied in India and always maintained a special relationship with India.

Concept note-4: -The Sultan Qaboos bin Said Al Said, who owned Wargrave Manor, died in January at the age of 79. The Sultan, who hosted a banquet and reception for 250 people on Mill Green in 1985, was commemorated by two organisations he helped in the community.
