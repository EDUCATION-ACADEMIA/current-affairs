Rashtriya Raksha University Bill passed in Lok Sabha is for which state?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) ** Gujarat

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To address the above issue, it is proposed to establish the Rashtriya Raksha University by upgrading the Raksha Shakti University, Gandhinagar, Gujarat, established under the State Act, that is, the Raksha Shakti University Act, 2009 by the Government of Gujarat as an institution of national importance.

Concept note-2: -Unsourced material may be challenged and removed. Rashtriya Raksha University (RRU), formerly Raksha Shakti University (RSU), is a central university and an Institute of National Importance located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India. It has a specialization in national and internal security.

Concept note-3: -Establishment of the University: The Bill establishes the Raksha Shakti University, Gujarat (established under the Raksha Shakti University Act, 2009) as a University called the Rashtriya Raksha University in Gujarat.

Concept note-4: -Minimum eligibility Criteria: Candidates must possess 50% marks in the qualifying examinations for the admission in any programme offered by the respective Schools of the University. For female, SC, ST, and PH candidates, the minimum eligibility criteria will be 45%.
