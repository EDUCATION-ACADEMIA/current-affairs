Recently Rajya Sabha passed a bill giving national importance tag to which of the following?

(A) AIMS

(B) Rajasthani Forts

(C) Kerala’s temples

(D) ** IIITs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajya Sabha passes IIIT Amendment Bill, ‘National Importance’ tag for 5 new IIITs.

Concept note-2: -2022. Earlier, the Constitution (Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes) Order (Second Amendment) Bill, 2022 was passed unanimously by the Rajya Sabha during the ongoing Winter Session of the Parliament on 14.12.

Concept note-3: -NIPER, MOHALI In 1998, Parliament enacted National Institute of Pharmaceutical Education and Research Act, 1998. NIPER was declared as an “Institute of National Importance” under the Act of Parliament on 26th June 1998. NIPER is a member of Association of Indian Universities.

Concept note-4: -How many institutes of national importance are there in India? There are 130 INIs in India, as of 2021. This list includes 7 AIIMS, 23 IITs, 20 IIMs, 25 IIITs, 29 NITs, 7 IISERs, 3 NIDs, 1 NIPER, etc.
