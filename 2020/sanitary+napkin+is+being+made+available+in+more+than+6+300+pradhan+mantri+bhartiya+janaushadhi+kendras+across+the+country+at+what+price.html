Sanitary Napkin is being made available in more than 6, 300 Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras across the country at what price?

(A) Rs 15/-per pad

(B) ** Rs 1/-per pad

(C) Rs 5/-per pad

(D) Rs 10/-per pad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jan Aushadhi sanitary napkins at Rs 4/packet | Sanitary Napkins in Gajraula | ID: 23174002988.

Concept note-2: -The market price of the similar Sanitary Napkins is around Rs. 3/- to Rs. 8/- per pad. Since inception (4 June 2018) to 10th June, 2020 over 4.61 crore sanitary Napkins was sold at Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras.

Concept note-3: -The cost of such machine is about 75 lakhs to 2.5 crores. These machines are generally run by multinational companies only. Now the quality napkins can be manufactured at home by using the newly invented sanitary napkin manufacturing machine at the cost of Rs. 75000 approx.

Concept note-4: -Central government has now started Janaushadhi Suvidha Sanitary Napkins Scheme 2023 to promote menstrual hygiene among women. In this scheme, Indian govt. is providing Suvidha Sanitary Pads at just Rs. 1 (earlier Rs. 2.5) through Jan Aushadhi Kendras.
