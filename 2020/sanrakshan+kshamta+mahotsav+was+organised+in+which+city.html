Sanrakshan Kshamta Mahotsav was organised in which city?

(A) Chandigarh

(B) Kurukshetra

(C) Sonipat

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A mass Awareness programme was organized on Fuel and Energy Conservation and the protection of the environment at Sarvajanin High School of Subhachuk, Dibrugarh on 29th April ‘22 near to BCPL Lepetkata Plant.

Concept note-2: -SAKSHAM 2022, A flagship public awareness campaign by the Petroleum Conservation Research Association (PCRA), under the aegis of the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas (MoPNG), to spread the message of Oil & Gas conservation took off at Chennai Petroleum Corporation Limited (CPCL), with full enthusiasm.

Concept note-3: -This year, ( ) (SAKSHAM CHAMTHA MAHOTSAV) 2022 (SAKSHAM) will be conducted from April 11 to April 30, 2022 with the theme of “Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav through Green & Clean Energy” all over the country.
