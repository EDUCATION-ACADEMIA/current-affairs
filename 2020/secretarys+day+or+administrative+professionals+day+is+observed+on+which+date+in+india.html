Secretary’s day or Administrative Professionals Day is observed on which date in India?

(A) 12 April

(B) 20 April

(C) ** 21 April

(D) 22 April

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When is Administrative Professionals Day 2023? Administrative Professionals Day is observed annually on the Wednesday of the last full week in April.

Concept note-2: -Wednesday, June 4, 1952, was designated the first National Secretaries Day. In 1955, the National Secretaries Association changed the dates to the current celebration during the last full week of April.

Concept note-3: -Every year, this day of recognition falls on the Wednesday of the last full week of April. Mark these dates on your calendar: 2022: April 27. 2023: April 26.

Concept note-4: -Administrative Professionals Day (also known as Secretaries Day or Admin Day) is a day observed yearly in a small number of countries. It is not a public holiday in any of them. In some countries, it falls within Administrative Professionals Week (the last full week of April in the United States).

Concept note-5: -Administrative Professionals Day, on the Wednesday of the last full week of April, is a time to celebrate these professionals who work as the glue keeping a business together.
