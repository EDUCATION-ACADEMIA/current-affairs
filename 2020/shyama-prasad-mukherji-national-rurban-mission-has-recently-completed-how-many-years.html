Shyama Prasad Mukherji National Rurban Mission has recently completed how many years?

(A) 1 year

(B) 2 years

(C) 3 years

(D) ** 4 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Rurban Mission which is wide known as National Rurban Mission is Initiated on September 2015 by Ministry of Rural Development, India. As per mission norms Rurban Clusters had been identified by state.

Concept note-2: -The objective of the Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) is to stimulate local economic development, enhance basic services, and create well planned Rurban clusters.

Concept note-3: -A ‘Rurban cluster’, would be a cluster of geographically contiguous villages with a population of about 25000 to 50000 in plain and coastal areas and with a population of 5000 to 15000 in desert, hilly or tribal areas.

Concept note-4: -SPMRM | Ministry of Rural Development | GOI.
