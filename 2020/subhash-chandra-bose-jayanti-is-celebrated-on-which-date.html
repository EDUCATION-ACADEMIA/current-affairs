Subhash Chandra Bose Jayanti is celebrated on which date?

(A) 21st January

(B) 22nd January

(C) ** 23rd January

(D) 24th January

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Jayanti 2023 (23rd Jan) – 126th Birth Anniversary. Subhash Chandra Bose Jayanti or Netaji Jayanti is celebrated as a national event in India to commemorate the birthday of Netaji Subash Chandra Bose on 23rd January every year.

Concept note-2: -Parakram Day Bravery Day, also known as Parakram Diwas, is now a recognised national day in India and is observed on January 23. In 2021, just before Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose’s 125th birthday, the Indian government unveiled it.

Concept note-3: -Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose Jayanti 2023: The birth anniversary of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose on January 23 is being observed as Parakram Day, as per the notification issued by the central government (2021).

Concept note-4: -Subhas Chandra Bose was born to Prabhavati Bose (née Dutt) and Janakinath Bose on 23 January 1897 in Cuttack-in what is today the state of Odisha in India, but was then the Orissa Division of Bengal Province in British India.
