Tablighi Jamaat belongs to which religion?

(A) ** Muslim

(B) Hindu

(C) Christian

(D) Jain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By some measures this made Tablighi Jamaat the largest Muslim movement in the World. The majority of the followers of the Tablighi Jamaat live in South Asia.

Concept note-2: -Within Sunni, we have Deobandi which has Tablighi jammat as its sub-sect. In addition Sunni branch also harbours Salafism which now a days considerably overlap with Wahhabism.

Concept note-3: -Tabligh an arabic word that means to reach out, to make known or to let people be informed. To make Islam’s message known to people is called tabligh. Tabligh Jamat (proselytizing or conveying group) is a Muslim missionary and revival movement and they put forward the slogan ‘O Muslim, Be Muslim’.
