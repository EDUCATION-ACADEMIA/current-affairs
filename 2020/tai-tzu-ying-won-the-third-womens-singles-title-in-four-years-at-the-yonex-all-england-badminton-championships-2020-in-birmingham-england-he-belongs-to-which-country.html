Tai Tzu Ying won the third women’s singles title in four years at the Yonex All England Badminton Championships 2020 in Birmingham, England. He belongs to which country?

(A) South Korea

(B) ** Taiwan

(C) China

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Olympic champion Viktor Axelsen has overpowered India’s Lakshya Sen to win the All England Open men’s singles title 21-10, 21-15 in 53 minutes. Axelsen didn’t drop a single game on his way to victory here in Birmingham and in his fourth consecutive final he dominated the unseeded Sen.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Prakash Padukone. On March 23, 1980, Prakash Padukone became the first Indian to win the All England Badminton Championships. Padukone clashed with Indonesia’s Liem Swie King in the summit clash at the Wembley Stadium. Padukone defeated his opponent 15-3, 15-10.
