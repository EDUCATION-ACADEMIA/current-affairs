The 11th National KVK Conference 2020 was held in which city?

(A) Kolkata

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Nashik

(D) Bhubaneshwar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Tomar was addressing the 11th National KVK Conference organized by the Indian Council of Agricultural Research at the National Agricultural Science Centre Complex, New Delhi here today.

Concept note-2: -KVK is an integral part of the National Agricultural Research System (NARS). The first KVK was established in 1974 at Puducherry.

Concept note-3: -There are 731 Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs) in the country. State/UT wise number of KVKs is given in Table below.

Concept note-4: -Mohan Singh Mehta appointed by ICAR in 1973 the idea of establishment of Farm Science Centre (Krishi Vigyan Kendra) was developed. The first KVK, on a pilot basis, was established in 1974 at Pondicherry under the administrative control of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural University, Coimbatore.
