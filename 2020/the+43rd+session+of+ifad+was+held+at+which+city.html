The 43rd session of IFAD was held at which city?

(A) Brussels

(B) London

(C) ** Rome

(D) Oslo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) 43rd Governing Council meeting was held in Rome, Italy.

Concept note-2: -IFAD headquarters is located in Rome, Italy.

Concept note-3: -An international response to global food shortages The conference was organized by the United Nations in response to the food crises of the early 1970s, when global food shortages were causing widespread famine and malnutrition, primarily in the Sahelian countries of Africa.

Concept note-4: -abbreviation for. International Fund for Agricultural Development.

Concept note-5: -International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD)
