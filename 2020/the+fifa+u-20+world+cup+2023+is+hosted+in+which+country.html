The FIFA U-20 World Cup 2023 is hosted in which country?

(A) ** Indonesia

(B) Peru

(C) Colombia

(D) Poland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A special event was held today at the iconic Gelora Bung Karno Stadium in Jakarta to celebrate the major milestone of 100 days to go until the start of the FIFA U-20 World Cup Indonesia 2023™.

Concept note-2: -The opening match will be contested between New Zealand and Norway at Eden Park, Auckland on 20 July 2023. The final will take place on 20 August 2023 at Sydney Olympic Stadium in Australia. The United States are the defending champions, having won the previous two tournaments.

Concept note-3: -It will be hosted by Indonesia, which will be the first FIFA tournament hosted by the country. It will also be only the second U-20 World Cup to be held in Southeast Asia, first since 1997 in Malaysia, and the first FIFA tournament in the region since the 2012 FIFA Futsal World Cup in Thailand.

Concept note-4: -FIFA U-20 World Cup 2023™ Official Emblem The Official Emblem of the FIFA U-20 World Cup Indonesia 2023™ has been unveiled on FIFA+.The Official Emblem of the FIFA U-20 World Cup Indonesia 2023™ has been unveiled on FIFA+.

Concept note-5: -The FIFA U-20 World Cup is the biennial football world championship tournament for FIFA members’ men’s national teams with players under the age of 20.
