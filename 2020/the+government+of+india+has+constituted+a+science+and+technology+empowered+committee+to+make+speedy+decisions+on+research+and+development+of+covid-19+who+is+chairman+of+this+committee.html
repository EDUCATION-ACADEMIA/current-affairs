(A) T. S. Thakur

(B) ** Vinod Paul

(C) Harsimrat Kaur Badal

(D) Prakash Javadekar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Department of Science and Technology (India) supports open access to scientific knowledge, originated from the public-funded research in India. In December 2014, the DST and the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Government of India had jointly adopted their Open Access Policy.
