The Indian Railway Stations Development Corporation Limited is developing a 1st of its kind “Form Based Codes” with the support from which foundation?

(A) The World Energy Foundation

(B) ** Shakti Sustainable Energy Foundation

(C) Good Energies Foundation

(D) Amercian Energy Foundation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IRSDC, with support from Shakti Sustainable Energy Foundation, is developing Form Based Codes for designing and approval of layout plans as well as building plans for station areas that ensure a compact, pedestrian-friendly, market responsive, transit oriented, sustainable development.

Concept note-2: -India’s first railway opened in 1853, a 32 km line between Bombay and Thane.

Concept note-3: -The first railway on Indian sub-continent ran over a stretch of 21 miles from Bombay to Thane. The idea of a railway to connect Bombay with Thane, Kalyan and with the Thal and Bhore Ghats inclines first occurred to Mr. George Clark, the Chief Engineer of the Bombay Government, during a visit to Bhandup in 1843.

Concept note-4: -Indian Railway Stations Development Corporation Limited (IRSDC) was a special purpose vehicle owned by the Rail Land Development Authority (RLDA), Ircon International and RITES. All three companies are owned by the Indian Ministry of Railways. IRSDC was incorporated on 12 April 2012.

Concept note-5: -The State Railway Establishment Code, Vol. I was originally published in September, 1940. Subsequently four editions were brought out as Indian Railway Establishment Code, Volume I in 1945, 1951, 1959 and 1971 respectively.
