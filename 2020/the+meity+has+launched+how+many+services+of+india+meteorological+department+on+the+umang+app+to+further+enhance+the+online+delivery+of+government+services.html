[SOURCES]

The MeitY has launched how many services of India Meteorological Department on the UMANG App to further enhance the online delivery of Government services?

(A) 3

(B) 4

(C) ** 7

(D) 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Single app for all services: The best part about the Umang app is that it a single platform for over 100 government services.

Concept note-2: -UMANG App can be used to book cylinders online, request for refills, subsidies, mechanic services, surrender connections, etc. These services can be availed on HP Gas, Bharat Gas and Indane Gas. Pensioners can avail services offered by the Department of Pension & Pensioner’s Welfare.

Concept note-3: -For the convenience of administrative and technical control, there are 6 Regional Meteorological Centres, each under a Deputy Director General with headquarters at Mumbai, Chennai, New Delhi, Calcutta, Nagpur and Guwahati.

Concept note-4: -Recently, the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has added the Indian Meteorological Department (IMD) weather services to the Umang Mobile App.
