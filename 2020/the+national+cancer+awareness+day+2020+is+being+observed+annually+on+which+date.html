The National Cancer Awareness Day 2020 is being observed annually on which date?

(A) 6 November

(B) ** 7 November

(C) 8 November

(D) 9 November

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In India, nearly 1.1 million new cases of cancer are reported annually. National Cancer Awareness Day is observed on 7th November for the last 8 years coinciding with the birth anniversary of the Nobel-prize-winning scientist Madame Curie.

Concept note-2: -National Cancer Awareness Day is observed on November 7 in India. The day highlights the significance of increased awareness about cancer prevention and early detection for the general public.

Concept note-3: -November 7 has been observed as National Cancer Awareness Day in the country for the last 8 years now. It was first initiated by Dr. Harsh Vardhan, the Union Minister of Health and Family Welfare in India, in September 2014.

Concept note-4: -National Cancer Awareness Day 2022: It is observed on 7 November in India to educate people about cancer, its treatment, and its symptoms.

Concept note-5: -The multi-year theme for World Cancer Day 2022-2024 is ‘Close the Care Gap. ‘It focuses more on exposure, engagement, and opportunities to meet the global awareness of Cancer Day.
