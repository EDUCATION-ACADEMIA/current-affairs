The World Hunger Day was observed across the world on which day?

(A) May 24, 2020

(B) ** May 28, 2020

(C) May 26, 2020

(D) May 27, 2020

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Hunger Day is observed every year on 28 May. The day was created by The Hunger Project to bring awareness to the more than 690 million people living in chronic hunger during an unprecedented global crisis.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. World Hunger Day is observed on May 28 every year. The day was started by The Hunger Project in 2011.

Concept note-3: -The theme for 2022 is Leave NO ONE behind. World Food Day 2022 is being marked in a year with multiple global challenges, including the COVID-19 pandemic, conflict, climate change, rising prices and international tensions.

Concept note-4: -September is Hunger Action Month.
