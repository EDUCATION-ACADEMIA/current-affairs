The 10th National Science Film Festival 2020 being hosted in which state?

(A) ** Tripura

(B) Bengaluru

(C) Assam

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The national science film festival 2020 is being jointly by organized Vigyan Prasar, and the Tripura State Council of Science and Technology, Govt. of Tripura.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains Notes: The tenth edition of the National Science film festival has been inaugurated in the state of Tripura. The festival will be held in virtual mode. VigyanPrasar, an autonomous agency under the Department of Science and Technology, has been organizing this festival.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Goa. 51st International Film Festival of India IFFI will be held in Goa, from November 20th to 28th, 2020.

Concept note-4: -The 51st International Film Festival of India was held from 16 to 24 January 2021 in Goa. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic the festival went hybrid, there was physical and virtual screening of 50 films out of 224 films across various categories.
