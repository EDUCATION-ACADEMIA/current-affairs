The 4th Edition of the Women Transforming India Awards are to be conferred by which minister?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) Narendra Modi

(C) Ram Nath Kovind

(D) ** Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NITI Aayog organised the Fourth Edition of the Women Transforming India Awards. It was done in the presence of Shri Rajnath Singh, Raksha Mantri.

Concept note-2: -The 75 awardees represent different regions and sectors and have been selected through an elaborate process spanning several months.

Concept note-3: -What is the WTI Award? About: The WTI Awards is NITI Aayog’s annual initiative to highlight the commendable and ground-breaking endeavours of India’s women leaders and change-makers.
