The Asian Paralympic Committee announced that the 4th Asian Youth Para Games will take place in which country?

(A) ** Bahrain

(B) Taiwan

(C) Mongolia

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2021 Asian Youth Para Games (Arabic: 2021, romanized: al-ʼAl’ab al-Baralimibia al-Asīawīah Lilshabab 2021), also known as the 4th Asian Youth Para Games is the 4th edition of multi-sport event for Asian athletes with different abilities. This event will be held in Manama, Bahrain.

Concept note-2: -Japan will host the 5th edition of Asian Para Games in 2026 in Nagoya. The 4th Asian Para Games 2022 will be hosted by Hangzhou, China.

Concept note-3: -The 5th edition of the Asian Youth Para Games 2025 will be hosted by Tashkent, the capital of Uzbekistan, and the approval given by the Asian Paralympic Committee’s (APC) Executive Board.
