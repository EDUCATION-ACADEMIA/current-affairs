The Carlyle Group, an United States(US) based private equity firm acquired how much stake in SeQuent Scientific Limited, the largest animal healthcare company from India?

(A) 64%

(B) 54%

(C) 44%

(D) ** 74%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Carlyle to take over SeQuent Scientific in its biggest India control deal. Private equity firm Carlyle has agreed to acquire up to 74% of animal healthcare company SeQuent Scientific Ltd for Rs 1, 580 crore (about $210 million) in what would be its biggest control-oriented deal in India.

Concept note-2: -About. The Carlyle Group has made 297 investments. Their most recent investment was on Jan 18, 2023, when Pathalys Pharma raised $150M . The Carlyle Group has made 7 diversity investments.

Concept note-3: -Carlyle was founded in 1987 by its three founders – Bill Conway, Dan D’Aniello, and David Rubenstein. The Carlyle Group manages $373 billion in assets under management investing in Global Private Equity, Global Credit and Global Investment Solutions as of December 31, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Founded in 1987, the Carlyle Group is a global alternative asset manager investing in corporate private equity, real assets, global market strategies and investment solutions. Carlyle dropped to sixth in this year’s PEI 300, from fourth in 2021.
