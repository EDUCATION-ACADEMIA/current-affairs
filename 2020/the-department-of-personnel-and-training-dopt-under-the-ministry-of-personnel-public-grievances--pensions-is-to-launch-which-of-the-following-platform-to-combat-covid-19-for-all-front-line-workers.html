The Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT), under the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, is to launch which of the following platform to combat COVID-19 for all front-line workers?

(A) Covid platform

(B) Corona platform

(C) Safety platform

(D) ** iGOT platform

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A delegation of Bharatiya Postal Employees federation calls on Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) for M/o Personnel, Public Grievances, Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh in New Delhi today, hails the employee-friendly budget presented by the Modi Govt.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions is the coordinating agency of the Central Government in personnel matters specially issues concerning recruitment, training, career development, staff welfare as well as the post retirement dispensation.

Concept note-3: -The Minister of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions is the cabinet minister in charge of Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions. The position is generally held by the prime minister, but sometimes it has been held by other senior members of the cabinet, such as Minister of Home Affairs.
