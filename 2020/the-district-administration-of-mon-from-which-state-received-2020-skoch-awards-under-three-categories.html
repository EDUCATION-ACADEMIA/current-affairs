(A) ** Nagaland

(B) Assam

(C) Sikkim

(D) Tripura

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Haryana has been awarded the SKOCH Award for ‘Best State in Health’.

Concept note-2: -KOLKATA: The Bengal government’s department of women and child development and social welfare was given the Skoch Award for the Lakshmir Bhandar schem.

Concept note-3: -The award was instituted in 2003 by the SKOCH Group, which is a consulting firm founded in 1997. The Bengal government had received four SKOCH awards for its schemes under the Ease of Doing Business initiative last year too.

Concept note-4: -West Bengal has bagged the prestigious SKOCH award in the women and child development category for its popular ‘Lakshmir Bhandar’ scheme, Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee said on Saturday.
