(A) Delhi

(B) Goa

(D) Uttar Pradesh

Concept note-2: -The Pasteur Institute of India developed influenza vaccine in 1957 and an BPL inactivated rabies vaccine in 1970. This institute developed and produced, for the first time in India, indigenous trivalent oral polio vaccine (OPV) in 197030.
