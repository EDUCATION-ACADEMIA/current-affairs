The fourth East Asia Summit Conference on Maritime Security Cooperation is to be organized in which city?

(A) Kochi

(B) Vishakhapatnam

(C) Kolkata

(D) ** Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -4th ASEAN Summit, Singapore, 27-29 January 1992 - ASEAN Main Portal.

Concept note-2: -The 10th EAS Economic Ministers’ Meeting was held in Siem Reap on 17 September 2022, chaired by Cambodia. EAS Economic Ministers acknowledged ASEAN’s centrality in regional post-pandemic recovery.

Concept note-3: -ASEAN’s 10 member countries are Brunei, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, the Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, and Vietnam. The United States and the Russian Federation joined at the 6th East Asia Summit in 2011.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi [India], November 22 (ANI): The fifth East Asia Summit (EAS) conference on Maritime Security Cooperation will be held in Kolkata on November 23-24, the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) said in a statement on Monday.
