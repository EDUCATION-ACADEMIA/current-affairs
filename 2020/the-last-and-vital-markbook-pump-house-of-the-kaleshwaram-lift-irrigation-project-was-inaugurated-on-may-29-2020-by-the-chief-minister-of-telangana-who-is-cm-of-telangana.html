The last and vital Markbook pump house of the Kaleshwaram Lift Irrigation Project, was inaugurated on May 29 2020 by the Chief Minister of Telangana. Who is CM of Telangana?

(A) Ashwathnarayan C N

(B) ** K Chandrasekhar Rao

(C) Pinarayi Vijayan

(D) Biplab Kumar Deb

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Engineer-in-Chief (Ramagundam) of the Kaleshwaram project, the first pump was run at 6.30 p.m. and the second one at 7.30 p.m. He said both the pumps had lifted water as per the designed discharge. Both the pump had discharge designed capacity of 2, 200 cusecs of water each.

Concept note-2: -Telangana is home to the world’s largest lift irrigation project - The Kaleshwaram Lift Irrigation System.

Concept note-3: -Kaleshwaram Project - Important Facts. The Kaleshwaram Lift Irrigation Project is a multi-purpose irrigation project on the Godavari River in Kaleshwaram, Bhupalpally District, Telangana, India. The 3 main purposes of this project are irrigation, transport, and power.

Concept note-4: -The project was first conceived in 2012, when the BJP was in power and two years later, the Congress government revised the project cost to Rs 12, 912.36 crore. And, now in 2022, the project cost is increased to Rs. 23, 251 crore.
