The ministry of coal is planning to link the mining plan portal with which website?

(A) PARIVAR

(B) ** PARIVESH

(C) GAEVAAD

(D) DHARWAD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In India, the operations in Coalmines are regulated by the Mines Act, 1952 Mine Rules – 1955, Coal Mine Regulation-2017and several other statutes framed thereunder. Directorate-General of Mines Safety (DGMS) under the Union Ministry of Labour & Employment (MOL&E) is entrusted to administer these statutes.

Concept note-2: - Ministry of Coal and its subsidiaries and Neyveli Lignite corporation India Limited (NLCIL).

Concept note-3: -Bharat Coking Coal Limited (BCCL), Central Coalfields Limited (CCL), Eastern Coalfields Limited (ECL), Western Coalfields Limited (WCL), South Eastern Coalfields Limited (SECL), Northern Coalfields Limited (NCL), Mahanadi Coalfields Limited (MCL) and Central Mine Planning and Design Institute (CMPDI).

Concept note-4: -Important areas include Assam, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Arunachal Pradesh, Jammu and Kashmir, Himalayan foothills of Darjeeling in West Bengal, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, and Kerala.
