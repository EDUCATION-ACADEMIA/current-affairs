The Union Ministry of Labour and Employment in partnership with which company offers free online career skills training through the National Career Service project?

(A) Wipro

(B) ** TCS iON

(C) Infosys

(D) Tech Mahindra Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Career Service (NCS) Portal now offers “Career Skills” Training by TCS iON free of cost for its registered Jobseekers.

Concept note-2: -On this occasion, Dr Shashank Goel, Additional Secretary/Director General of Employment, Ministry of Labour and Employment launched an e-Book touching upon the journey of NCS so far, on 07.03. 2022 at Shram Shakti Bhawan, New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Bringing Talent Closer to Opportunities… A National ICT based portal is developed primarily to connect the opportunities with the aspirations of youth. This portal facilitates registration of job seekers, job providers, skill providers, career counsellors, etc.

Concept note-4: -DGE&T Objective – NCS Portal A National ICT based portal to connect the opportunities with the aspirations of youth and facilitate registration of job seekers, job providers, skill providers, career counsellors, etc. The portal should provide job matching services in a highly transparent and user friendly manner.

Concept note-5: -1. How can I register as an Employer on NCS Portal? You can register directly on the portal or visit the nearest Model Career Center to get registered. You can also reach out to the Toll Free Helpline 1800-425-1514 (Tuesday to Sunday, 8.00 AM to 8.00 PM).
