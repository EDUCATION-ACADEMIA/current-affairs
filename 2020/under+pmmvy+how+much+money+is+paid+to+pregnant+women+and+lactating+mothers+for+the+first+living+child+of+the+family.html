Under PMMVY, how much money is paid to pregnant women and lactating mothers for the first living child of the family?

(A) Rs 2, 000

(B) ** Rs 5, 000

(C) Rs 7, 500

(D) Rs 10, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under PMMVY, a cash incentive of ‘ 5000/- is provided directly to the Bank / Post Office Account of Pregnant Women and Lactating Mothers (PW&LM) for first living child of the family subject to fulfilling specific conditions relating to Maternal and Child Health.

Concept note-2: -Apply for 2nd/3rd Instalment Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana is a maternity benefit program run by the government of India. It is a conditional cash transfer scheme for pregnant and lactating women of 19 years of age or above for the first live birth.

Concept note-3: -To provide compensation for the wage loss in terms of cash incentives so that the woman can take adequate rest before and after delivery of the first living child. It is a partial compensation which is a part of a plan to provide a total sum of Rs. 6, 000 on an average to the woman.

Concept note-4: -To register under the PMMVY offline, the expectant mothers can get in touch with Anganwadi workers i.e. ASHA workers. They will guide the potential beneficiaries with the process of registration and documents through the three installments of the maternity benefit scheme.
