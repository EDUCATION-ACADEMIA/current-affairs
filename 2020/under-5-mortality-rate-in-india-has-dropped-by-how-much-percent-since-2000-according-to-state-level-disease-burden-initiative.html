Under-5 Mortality Rate in India has dropped by how much percent since 2000 according to State-Level Disease Burden Initiative?

(A) 42%

(B) 44%

(C) 40%

(D) ** 49%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Under-5 Mortality Rate in India has dropped by 49 per cent since 2000, but there is a six-fold variation in the rate between states and an 11-fold variation between various districts.

Concept note-2: -Brief Definition: The under-five mortality rate refers to the probability of dying. before age 5 years per 1, 000 newborns.

Concept note-3: -The neonatal mortality rate fell to 18 (17, 19) deaths per 1, 000 live births in 2018 from 37 (36, 38) in 1990 and 31 (30, 31) in 2000 – a 52 (47, 53) per cent and 42 (37, 45) per cent decline, respectively.
