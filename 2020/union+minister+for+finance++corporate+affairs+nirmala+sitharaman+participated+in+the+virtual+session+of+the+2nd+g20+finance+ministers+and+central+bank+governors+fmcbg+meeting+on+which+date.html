Union Minister for Finance & Corporate Affairs Nirmala Sitharaman participated in the virtual session of the 2nd G20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors (FMCBG) meeting on which date?

(A) 10 April

(B) 11 April

(C) ** 15 April

(D) 12 April

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Story highlights. India Budget 2023: Country’s finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman said that New Delhi’s G20 presidency presents a ‘unique opportunity’ in a larger global spotlight as it strengthens the country’s role in the world economic order when countries across the globe are facing various challenges.

Concept note-2: -The G20 Finance Track, led by Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors of G20 countries, focuses on economic and financial issues. It provides an effective forum for global economic discourse and policy coordination.

Concept note-3: -Sitharaman’s budget for the fiscal year starting April 2023 is her fifth straight since 2019. Other ministers who have presented five straight annual financial statements include Arun Jaitley, P Chidambaram, Yashwant Sinha, Manmohan Singh and Morarji Desai.

Concept note-4: -The first finance minister of India was R. K. Shanmukham and delivered the first budget of India.
