(A) Amazon

(B) ** Flipkart

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -The Maharashtra government has signed a MoU with the British Council to strengthen educational and cultural cooperation.

Concept note-3: -Khadi and Village Industries Board CEO Avinash Krishna Singh signed the MoU on behalf of the Uttar Pradesh Government, Seller Services Director and GM Gopal Pillai signed on behalf of Amazon India.
