Union HRD Minister launches which platform for inviting e-learning Content contributions in both school and higher education?

(A) SabPadhe 2.0

(B) VidyaGyan 2.0

(C) PdaiDaan 2.0

(D) ** VidyaDaan 2.0

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union HRD Minister launches national program VidyaDaan 2.0 for inviting e-learning Content contributions in New Delhi. The Union Human Resource Development Minister Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal’Nishank’ e-launched VidyaDaan 2.0 program for inviting e-learning content contributions in New Delhi today .

Concept note-2: -HRD Minister Dr. Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank launches VidyaDaan 2.0 program for inviting e-learning contributions from individuals & organizations across the country.

Concept note-3: -E-Pathshala: This initiative focuses not only on students but also teachers and parents. E-Pathshala can be accessed through a web portal or mobile app for the E-textbooks with 3500+ curriculum content in English, Sanskrit, Urdu, and Hindi.

Concept note-4: -SWAYAM is a free learning platform but there is a little price for certification. SWAYAM has been created cooperatively by MHRD (Ministry of Human Resource Development), and AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education) with the help of Microsoft.

Concept note-5: -Profile of Hon’ble Minister Dharmendra Pradhan is the Cabinet Minister for Education and Skill Development and Entrepreneurship in the Government of India.
