Union Minister of Finance & Corporate Affairs Nirmala Sitharaman participated in the 5th Annual Meeting of Board of Governors of New Development Bank through video-conference. Where is the Headquarters of NDB?

(A) ** Shanghai

(B) Delhi

(C) Rio de Janeiro

(D) Volgograd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Board of Governors agreed that its Eighth Annual Meeting will be held in Shanghai, China. The Board of Governors approved the 2021 Financial Accounts for the Ordinary Operations of the New Development Bank as well as the 2021 Financial Accounts of the Project Preparation Fund.

Concept note-2: -NDB has so far approved 21 projects of India for an amount of $7.1 billion. Union Minister of Finance & Corporate Affairs and India’s Governor for the New Development Bank (NDB), Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman chaired the 7th Annual Meeting of Board of Governors of NDB through video-conference in New Delhi today.

Concept note-3: -Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman has launched Union Budget Mobile App.
