United Nations General Assembly is based in which city?

(A) Durban

(B) ** New York

(C) Sydney

(D) Paris

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The General debate is held in the United Nations General Assembly Hall at the United Nations Headquarters in New York.

Concept note-2: -Each year, in September, the full UN membership meets in the General Assembly Hall in New York for the annual General Assembly session, and general debate, which many heads of state attend and address.

Concept note-3: -The first General Assembly of the United Nations, comprising 51 nations, convenes at Westminster Central Hall in London, England.

Concept note-4: -The United Nations General Assembly Building is part of the headquarters of the United Nations in the Turtle Bay neighborhood of Manhattan in New York City.

Concept note-5: -Much of the UN Secretariat is located in New York City, USA. The UN also has three major Offices outside of its Headquarters and five Regional Economic Commissions.
