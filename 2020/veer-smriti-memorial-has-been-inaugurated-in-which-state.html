Veer Smriti Memorial has been inaugurated in which state?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Assam

(C) ** Nagaland

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Earlier, he also inaugurated Smriti Van Memorial in Bhuj district. Addressing the gathering, the Prime Minister said that Smriti Van Memorial in Bhuj and Veer Bal Smarak at Anjar are the symbols of shared pain of Kutch, Gujarat and the entire country.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Mokokchung. In Nagaland, a ‘veer smiriti’ memorial has been set up, which is dedicated to the 357 army and Assam Rifles personnel killed while fighting an insurgency. The memorial is situated in the Mokokchung.

Concept note-3: -The Assam Rifles has constructed a combined war memorial in Nagaland for the 357 Army and Assam Rifles personnel killed while fighting insurgency in the northeastern state. The memorial has been built at Mokokchung, which is also the cultural and intellectual centre of Nagaland.
