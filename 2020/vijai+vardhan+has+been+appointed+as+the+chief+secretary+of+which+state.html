Concept note-1: -Bengaluru: 1986-batch senior IAS officer Vandita Sharma took charge as the 39th Chief Secretary of Karnataka on Tuesday. She replaces P Ravi Kumar, who retired from service. Vandita is the fourth woman chief secretary after Teresa Bhattacharya (2000), Malathi Das (2006) and K Ratna Prabha (2017).
