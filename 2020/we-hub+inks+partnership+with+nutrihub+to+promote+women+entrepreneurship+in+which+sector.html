WE-Hub inks partnership with NutriHub to promote women entrepreneurship in which sector?

(A) Private Sector

(B) Public Sector

(C) ** Agricultural Sector

(D) Industrial Sector

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -WE HUB, NutriHub partner to aid women entrepreneurs in agri business - The Hindu BusinessLine.

Concept note-2: -NITI Aayog launched the Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP) initiative to provide a platform to women entrepreneurs across the country. Under this initiative, the Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) has collaborated with NITI Aayog to provide financial assistance to women entrepreneurs.

Concept note-3: -Women Empowerment Schemes Stree Shakti Yojana. Dena Shakti Scheme. Bhartiya Mahila Bank Business Loan. Mahila Udyam Nidhi Yojana.

Concept note-4: -Self Employed Women’s Association (SEWA) It helps women entrepreneurs through its micro financial products at Sewa Bank, and Sewa Trade Facilitation Centre (STFC). Over the age of 15, any woman who is self-employed can become a member of SEWA.
