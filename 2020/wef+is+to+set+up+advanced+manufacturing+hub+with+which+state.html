WEF is to set up advanced manufacturing hub with which state?

(A) Odisha

(B) ** Tamil Nadu

(C) Karnataka

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. In which state, India’s first advanced manufacturing hub (AMHUB) is to be established? Notes: India’s first advanced manufacturing hub (AMHUB) is to be established in the state of Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-2: -Guidance in partnership with the World Economic Forum to set up India’s first advanced manufacturing hub in Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-3: -“We are thrilled to welcome the United States Centre for Advanced Manufacturing to Michigan, where our century-long auto manufacturing heritage, innovative businesses and hardworking people continue to shape and drive the future of manufacturing in the US and around the world, ” said Michigan Governor Gretchen Whitmer.

Concept note-4: -“The centre in Telangana will play an important role in transforming the regional, national and global healthcare sector, and improving patient access and outcomes.” C4IR Telangana is the 18th centre to join the World Economic Forum’s Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR) Network, and the second centre in India.
