What amount of insurance cover has been allowed for sanitation workers, ASHA workers, doctors, nurses and paramedics who are on the frontlines of the coronavirus fight?

(A) Rs 70 lakh

(B) ** Rs 50 lakh

(C) Rs 10 lakh

(D) Rs 2 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ayushman Bharat - National Health Protection Mission will have a defined benefit cover of Rs. 5 lakh per family per year.

Concept note-2: -PM-JAY is the world’s largest health insurance/ assurance scheme fully financed by the government. It provides a cover of Rs. 5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization across public and private empanelled hospitals in India.

Concept note-3: -Chiranjeevi Health Insurance Scheme: Rajasthan is a government scheme that provides cashless medical insurance to the permanent residents of Rajasthan, for up to Rs. 5 Lakh. The scheme was made available to all the families of Rajasthan from May 1, 2021.
