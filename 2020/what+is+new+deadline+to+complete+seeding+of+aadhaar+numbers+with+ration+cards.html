What is new deadline to complete seeding of Aadhaar numbers with ration cards?

(A) July 30

(B) ** September 30

(C) May 30

(D) June 30

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The earlier deadline of December 31, 2021 was similarly extended till March 31, 2022 and now, it is extended to June 30, 2022. Don’t miss out on ET Prime stories!

Concept note-2: -From April 1, 2022, accounts where Pan-Aadhaar seeding has not taken place will not be able to carry out any transaction. Further, such accounts could also track penalties under the Income-Tax Act.

Concept note-3: -Deadline for linking ration card with Aadhaar card extended till June 30 now. Offering relief to ration cardholders, the administration has now extended the deadline of linking Aadhaar card with ration cards across India. Notably, this is not the first time that this deadline has been extended by the government.

Concept note-4: -Since ration cards have been one of the oldest proof of residence in the country, it is important to link them with Aadhaar so that the benefits received by the holder can be streamlined and the cases of fraud can be prevented.

Concept note-5: -What is the last date for linking the Aadhaar card with Ration Card in West Bengal? The last date for linking the Aadhaar card with Ration Card in West Bengal is December 31, 2021.
