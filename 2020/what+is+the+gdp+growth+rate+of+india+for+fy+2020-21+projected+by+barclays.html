What is the GDP growth rate of India for FY 2020-21 projected by Barclays?

(A) -9.4

(B) -10.5

(C) ** -6.4

(D) -8.5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Barclays has projected India’s real GDP growth at seven per cent in 2022-23.

Concept note-2: -Giving more details, the Minister stated that as per provisional estimates released by National Statistical Office (NSO), Indian economy in 2021-22 has fully recovered the pre-pandemic real GDP level of 2019-20.
