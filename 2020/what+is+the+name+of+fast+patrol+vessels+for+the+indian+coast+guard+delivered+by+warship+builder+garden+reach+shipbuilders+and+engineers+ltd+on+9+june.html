What is the name of Fast Patrol Vessels for the Indian Coast Guard delivered by Warship builder Garden Reach Shipbuilders and Engineers Ltd on 9 June?

(A) ICGS Ameya Amogh

(B) ** ICGS Kanaklata Barua

(C) ICGS Achook

(D) ICGS Atulya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Coast Guard has commissioned the fast patrol vessel “ICGS Kanaklata Barua” that was built by the Garden Reach Shipbuilders and Engineers Limited (GRSE).

Concept note-2: -Indian Coast Guard ship Kamla Devi is officially the fifth and last vessel of the series of FPVs designed and built by GRSE as per the specifications of the Indian Coast Guard.

Concept note-3: -Indian Coast Guard ship ‘Vajra’ commissioned formally into service on March 24th 2021. It is the 6th offshore patrol vessel to enhance coastal security. ICGS Vajra is the sixth in the series of seven Offshore Patrol Vessels (OPVs).
