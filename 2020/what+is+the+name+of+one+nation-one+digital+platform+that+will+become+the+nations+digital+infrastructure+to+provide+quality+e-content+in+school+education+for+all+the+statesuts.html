[SOURCES]

What is the name of one nation-one digital platform that will become the nation’s digital infrastructure to provide quality e-content in school education for all the states/UTs?

(A) ** DIKSHA

(B) SHIKSHA

(C) EDUTECH

(D) DIGISHIKSHA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PM eVIDYA will also be called a one Nation digital platform. Other than that a TV channel called one class channel will also be launched for students who are studying in classes 1st to 12th. For visually and hearing impaired students the government will also do radio podcasts.

Concept note-2: -Pradhan Mantri eVIDYA One Nation One Digital Platform Objective. The purpose to launch the eVidya program was to give proper education to all students even during the lockdown period.

Concept note-3: -E-Pathshala: This initiative focuses not only on students but also teachers and parents. E-Pathshala can be accessed through a web portal or mobile app for the E-textbooks with 3500+ curriculum content in English, Sanskrit, Urdu, and Hindi.

Concept note-4: -A digital learning platform can be a learning management system (LMS) or learning content management systems (LCMS). It can also be a virtual classroom tool or a virtual learning environment (VLE).

Concept note-5: -As part of PM eVidya announced under the Atma Nirbhar Bharat programme, DIKSHA is the ‘one nation; one digital platform’ for school education in India. DIKSHA is available for grades from 1 to 12. DIKSHA can be accessed through mobile application and web portal.
