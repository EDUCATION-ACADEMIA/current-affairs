What is the theme of Global Technology Summit (GTS) 2020?

(A) ** Geopolitics of Technology

(B) India’s growth and prosperity

(C) Technology and Society:Getting the Balance Right

(D) The Future of Data

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for this year’s Summit is ‘Geopolitics of Technology.

Concept note-2: -About the Global Technology Summit 2022 The theme for the 2022 edition of GTS is “Geopolitics of Technology”.

Concept note-3: -2022 Global Technology Summit The seventh iteration of the Global Technology Summit explored the present climate in the shapeshifting world of international alliances, emerging technologies, and geopolitics.
