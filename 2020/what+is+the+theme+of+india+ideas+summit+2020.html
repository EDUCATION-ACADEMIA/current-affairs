What is the theme of India Ideas Summit 2020?

(A) ** Building a Better Future

(B) Business and Environment

(C) Business in post-COVID world

(D) Sustainable Business

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for this year’s Summit is ‘Building a Better Future’. Prime Minister congratulated USIBC on its 45th anniversary this year. He thanked the USIBC leadership for their commitment to advancing India-US economic partnership.

Concept note-2: -The theme of the event was “Maximising the Next 75 years of U.S. – India Prosperity.” CEOs and top management of American companies were present at the occasion.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution The correct answer is 1 only. It was organised by the US - India Business Council virtually on 22nd July 2020. Hence, statement 1 is correct. The Summit witnessed discussions on areas including India-US cooperation and the future of the relation between the two counties in a post-pandemic world.
