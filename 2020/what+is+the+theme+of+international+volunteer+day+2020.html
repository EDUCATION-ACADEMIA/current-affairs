What is the theme of International Volunteer Day 2020?

(A) Global Applause-give volunteers a hand

(B) ** Together We Can Through Volunteering

(C) Your world is changing. Are you? Volunteer!

(D) Celebrate volunteering

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for this IVD is solidarity though volunteering - “Together, act now". The value of volunteers will be celebrated and recognized. While the world grapples with humanitarian and development challenges - amid wars and climate change, we need volunteers to work together for the common good.

Concept note-2: -International Volunteer Day (IVD) 2022 celebrates the theme of solidarity through volunteering. This campaign highlights the power of our collective humanity to drive positive change through volunteerism.

Concept note-3: -On International Volunteer Day (IVD) 2022, we celebrate our theme of solidarity through volunteering. On International Volunteer Day (IVD) 2022, we celebrate our theme of solidarity through volunteering.

Concept note-4: -The Red ‘V’, which has become an international symbol for volunteering, was commissioned by Margaret Bell, former President of Volunteering Australia and given to Volunteering Australia and the International Association for Volunteer Effort (IAVE) to be used universally as a symbol of volunteering.

Concept note-5: -Volunteering can provide a healthy boost to your self-confidence, self-esteem, and life satisfaction. You are doing good for others and the community, which provides a natural sense of accomplishment. Your role as a volunteer can also give you a sense of pride and identity.
