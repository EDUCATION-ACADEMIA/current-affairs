What is the theme of Pravasi Bharatiya Divas 2021?

(A) Role of Indian Diaspora in building New India

(B) Redefining engagement with the Indian diaspora

(C) ** Contributing to Atmanirbhar Bharat

(D) Apna Bharat, Apna Gaurav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 17th Pravasi Bharatiya Divas Convention is being organised in partnership with the Madhya Pradesh Government from 08-10 January 2023 in Indore. The theme of this PBD Convention is “Diaspora: Reliable partners for India’s progress in Amrit Kaal”.

Concept note-2: -The three-day convention took place from January 8 to 10 in the cleanest city of India, Indore, Madhya Pradesh. The theme of 17th Pravasi Bharatiya Divas is ‘Diaspora: Reliable partners for India’s progress in Amrit Kaal.’ The convention is being held with the joint partnership of the Madhya Pradesh government.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi met President of the Cooperative Republic of Guyana, H.E. Dr. Mohamed Irfaan Ali, on the sidelines of 17th Pravasi Bhartiya Divas (PBD) in Indore today. President Irfaan Ali is on an official visit to India from 8-14 January 2023 and is the Chief Guest at the 17th PBD.

Concept note-4: -17th Pravasi Bharatiya Diwas Convention.

Concept note-5: -16 Pravasi Bharatiya Divas Conventions have been organised till date. 16th PBD was conducted in a virtual setting with the theme “Contributing to Aatmanirbhar Bharat” in 2021 during the pandemic.
