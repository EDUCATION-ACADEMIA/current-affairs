What was the theme of International Day of Yoga 2020?

(A) Yoga for Everyone-Yoga at Home

(B) Yoga for Health-Yoga in Life

(C) ** Yoga for Health-Yoga at Home

(D) Yoga for Corona-Yoga in Life

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The resolution for International Day of Yoga was proposed by India and endorsed by a record 175 member states through UN resolution 69/131. This year the theme of International Yoga Day (IDY) is ‘Yoga for Health - Yoga at Home’.

Concept note-2: -International Yoga Day 2021: This year the theme is ‘Yoga For Wellness’ and for the practicing yoga for physical and mental well-being.

Concept note-3: -The 6th International Yoga Day was celebrated at The Glocal University on Sunday, June 21, 2020. In view of the COVID-19 pandemic, this year International Yoga Day was celebrated on digital media platforms and there were no mass gatherings at the campus.

Concept note-4: -Recognizing its universal appeal, on 11 December 2014, the United Nations proclaimed 21 June as the International Day of Yoga by resolution 69/131.

Concept note-5: -The theme for the sixth International Yoga Day was Yoga for Health – Yoga at Home which takes into account the social distancing measures announced by most countries due to COVID-19. The Sports committee of MMCOE celebrated the 6th International Day of Yoga on 21st June 2020.
