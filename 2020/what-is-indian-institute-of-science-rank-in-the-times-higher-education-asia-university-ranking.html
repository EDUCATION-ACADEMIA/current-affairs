What is Indian Institute of Science rank in the Times Higher Education Asia University Ranking?

(A) ** 36th

(B) 46th

(C) 26th

(D) 16th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Times Higher Education Rankings 2023 has been announced. This year, the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore has secured the top place among Indian universities. Five Indian universities made it into the world’s top 500 varsities. IISc has been placed under the 251-300 bracket.

Concept note-2: -IISc has been placed in the 251-300 bracket, jumping one ranking band from last year. MGU, which was tied at the third spot, jumped two ranking bands, from 601-800 in the 2022 ranking to 401-500 in the latest tally. In total, 22 Indian universities made it below rank 800 - three of which were debutant entries.

Concept note-3: -While China bags the second place with 97 ranked institutions, India follows as the third most represented country, with 71 of its universities making it to the rankings. In terms of the top 10 institutions, China leads the way with several entries along with smaller geos such as South Korea, Hong Kong, etc.

Concept note-4: -THE Asia University Rankings 2022: With 42nd rank, the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore is the only institute to make the top-50 list.
