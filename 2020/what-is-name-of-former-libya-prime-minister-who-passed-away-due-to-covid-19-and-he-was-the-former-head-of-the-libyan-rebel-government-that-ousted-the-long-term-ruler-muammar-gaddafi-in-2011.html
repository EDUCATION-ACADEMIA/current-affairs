What is name of Former Libya Prime Minister who passed away due to COVID-19 and he was the former head of the Libyan rebel government that ousted the long term ruler Muammar Gaddafi in 2011?

(A) Khalifa al-Ghawil

(B) ** Mahmoud Jibril

(C) Shukri Mohammed Ghanem

(D) Baghdadi Ali Mahmudi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Muammar Gaddafi Prime Minister. Simultaneously served as the Chairman of the Revolutionary Command Council (RCC).

Concept note-2: -On March 6, 2014, Prime Minister Ali Zeidan of Libya met with Ambassador Wu Sike, the Special Envoy of China on the Middle East Issue, who was in Rome, Italy attending the Ministerial Conference on International Support to Libya.

Concept note-3: -The fall of the last remaining sites in Sirte under pro-Gaddafi control on 20 October 2011, followed by the subsequent killing of Gaddafi, marked the end of the Libyan Arab Jamahiriya.
