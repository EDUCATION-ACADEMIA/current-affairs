What is the name of a National Program (Artificial Intelligence)for the youth launched by Union Minister for Electronics and Information Technology Ravi Shankar Prasad?

(A) Responsible AI for India

(B) ** Responsible AI for Youth

(C) Responsible AI for Bharat

(D) Responsible AI for All

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ravi Shankar Prasad launched India’s national Artificial Intelligence Portal & launched “Responsible AI for Youth”

Concept note-2: -The National AI Portal is a joint initiative by the Ministry of Electronics and IT (MeitY), National e-Governance Division (NeGD) and NASSCOM.

Concept note-3: -The country’s first Artificial Intelligence & Robotics Technology Park (ARTPARK) was launched in Bengaluru yesterday. It is promoted by a not-for-profit foundation set up by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bengaluru, with a seed capital of Rs. 230 crore from the State and Central governments.

Concept note-4: -Union Minister for Electronics & Information Technology, Railways and Communications, Shri Ashwini Vaishnaw will inaugurate the 3rd edition of ‘NIC Tech Conclave 2022’, a two-day event being organized by the National Informatics Centre (NIC), at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi on 3rd& 4th March 2022.
