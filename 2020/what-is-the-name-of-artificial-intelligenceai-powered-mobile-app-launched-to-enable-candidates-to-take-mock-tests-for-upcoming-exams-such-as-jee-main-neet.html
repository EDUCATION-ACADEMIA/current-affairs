What is the name of Artificial Intelligence(AI)-powered mobile app launched to enable candidates to take mock tests for upcoming exams such as JEE main, NEET?

(A) ** National Test Abhyas

(B) National Test Practice

(C) Sarva Test Abhyas

(D) Sarva Test exercise

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Test Abhyas App is an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-powered smartphone app launched by the National Testing Agency (NTA) which conducts competitive exams like JEE and NEET.

Concept note-2: -National Test Abhyas app is designed to give students exposure to Computer Based Tests (CBT) being conducted by India’s National Testing Agency (NTA). With the help of National Test Abhyas app, you can experience Computer Based Mock Tests for JEE MAIN and NEET on your mobile or tablet, for free!

Concept note-3: -Both Allen And Aakash Test series for NEET are very good and are recommended for checking the level of your preparation.

Concept note-4: -Aakash NEET Mock tests are available free of cost for the NEET 2023 aspirants. The NEET Mock Tests have been designed as per the latest exam pattern and syllabus released by the NTA.
