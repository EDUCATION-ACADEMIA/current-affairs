What is the name of e-Governance platform developed by MHRD to provide quality education to students across all Universities & Higher Education Institutions?

(A) SAFAL

(B) SAHARA

(C) SARTHAK

(D) ** SAMARTH

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The aim of the e-Governance platform, SAMARTH, is to provide quality education to students across all Universities & Higher Education Institutions (HEIs).

Concept note-2: -What is Samarth E-Gov Suite? The Samarth E-Gov Suite is a purpose built platform for Universities and HEIs to deploy a digital framework of good governance. Samarth increases the efficiency of Institutions by enabling them to : (1) leverage digital technologies to define and govern administrative processes.

Concept note-3: -e - Governance in Education Sector. Electronic Governance (e-Governance) is the use of Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) for the planning, implementation, and monitoring of government programs, projects, and activities.

Concept note-4: -The pilot project SAKSHAT: A One Stop Education Portal launched on October 30, 2006 by His Excellency, the then President of India to facilitate lifelong learning for students, teachers and those in employment or in pursuit of knowledge free of cost to them.

Concept note-5: -The Uttarakhand education department launched an e-governance portal “Samarth”.
