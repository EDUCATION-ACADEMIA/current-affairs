What is the name of mental health rehabilitation helpline launched by Government of India?

(A) VIRAT

(B) KOMAL

(C) ** KIRAN

(D) KESHAV

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment’s 24x7 Toll-Free Mental Health Rehabilitation Helpline Kiran (1800-599-0019) to provide relief and support to persons with Mental Illness.

Concept note-2: -National Mental Health Programme (NMHP) | Ministry of Health and Family Welfare | GOI.

Concept note-3: -To address the huge burden of mental disorders and shortage of qualified professionals in the field of mental health, Government of India has been implementing National Mental Health Program (NMHP) since 1982. The district Mental Health Program was added to the Program in 1996.

Concept note-4: -Early detection & treatment. Training: imparting short term training to general physicians for diagnosis and treatment of common mental illnesses with limited number of drugs under guidance of specialist. The Health workers are being trained in identifying mentally ill persons. IEC: Public awareness generation.

Concept note-5: -The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has launched a 24/7 toll-free helpline ‘Kiran’ to provide support to people facing anxiety, stress, depression, suicidal thoughts and other mental health concerns.
