What is the new deadline for qualification period of Tokyo Olympics?

(A) June 1, 2021

(B) ** June 29, 2021

(C) July 29, 2021

(D) July 1, 2021

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IOC Set June 29, 2021 As New Deadline For Olympic Qualification Period.

Concept note-2: -Some federations allow very young athletes to participate in the Olympics, while others have higher age minimums. For example, alpine skiers must be 17 or older, while biathlon competitors must turn 22 in the same year as the Olympic Games. Generally speaking, 15 years old seems to be one of the more common minimums.

Concept note-3: -There is no specific age limit for taking part in the Olympic Games.
