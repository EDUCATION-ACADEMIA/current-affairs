What is the theme of the 18th Edition of Dhaka International Film Festival?

(A) Gender Equality in Cinema

(B) ** Better Film, Better Audience and Better Society

(C) Oriental and Occidental aesthetics

(D) West Meets East

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of the festival is ‘Better Film, Better Audience and Better Society’.

Concept note-2: -Bangladesh assumed the position of the IORA Chairship at the 21st IORA COM Meeting in Dhaka held on 17 November 2021 and adopted the theme of “Harnessing the opportunities of the Indian Ocean sustainably for inclusive development” for its Chairship from 2021 – 2023.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 20th. The 20th Dhaka International Film Festival (DIFF) took place between January 15-23, 2022 at multiple venues in Dhaka. The festival showcased 225 films from 70 countries under 10 categories.

Concept note-4: -Q. An international Film Festival with the theme of ‘Better Film, Better Audience and Better Society’, was recently inaugurated in which city? Notes: The 18th edition of the famous Dhaka International Film Festival was recently inaugurated at Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh.

Concept note-5: -UPSC Mains Notes: ‘Koozhangal’ from India won the best film award in the Asian Film Competition section at the 20th Dhaka International Film Festival. It was directed by P S Vinothraj from Tamil Nadu.
