What was the theme of 36th Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) Summit?

(A) Corona Responsive ASEAN

(B) ** Cohesive and Responsive ASEAN

(C) Active and Responsible ASEAN

(D) Active and Responsive ASEAN

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for the Summit was “Cohesive and Responsive ASEAN".

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Vietnam. The 36th (ASEAN) Association of Southeast Asian Nations summit held in Vietnam under Vietnamese PM Nguyen Xuan Phuc. Cohesive and Responsive ASEAN was the theme of this summit.

Concept note-3: -This year’s Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) Summit hosted by Cambodia from 10-13 November 2022, in Phnom Penh was the first in-person meeting held after the outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020.

Concept note-4: -The 1st ASEAN Summit was held in February 1976 in Bali, Indonesia. At this summit, ASEAN expressed its readiness to “develop fruitful relations” and mutually beneficial co-operation with other countries of the region. The ASEAN leaders signed the Treaty of Amity and Cooperation in Southeast Asia.

Concept note-5: -“Indonesia is honored to be the Chair of ASEAN in 2023. The chairmanship of Indonesia will adopt the theme ‘ASEAN Matters: Epicentrum of Growth, ” President Jokowi said at Sokha Hotel, Phnom Penh, Sunday (11/13).
