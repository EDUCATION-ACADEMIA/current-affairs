When did the Sadbhavan Diwas celebrated?

(A) 15 August

(B) 17 August

(C) 19 August

(D) ** 20 August

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The award was instituted by All India Congress Committee of the Indian National Congress Party (INC), in 1992 to commemorate the lasting contribution made by the former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi, carries a citation and a cash award of Rs. ten lakhs.

Concept note-2: -The birth anniversary of former Prime Minister Late Shri Rajiv Gandhi is observed as ‘Sadbhavana Diwas’ on 20th August every year. The theme of Sadbhavana is to promote National Integration and Communal Harmony among people of all religions, languages and regions.

Concept note-3: -Our nation will celebrate Sadbhavana Diwas 2023 to commemorate Rajiv Gandhi’s 78th birth anniversary this year. As a mark of respect to the late former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi, India observes Sadbhavana Diwas on August 20 every year.

Concept note-4: -Sadbhavana Diwas 2022 Theme The Sadbhvana Diwas 2022 Theme is to “promote National Integration and Communal Harmony among people of all religions, languages, and regions".
