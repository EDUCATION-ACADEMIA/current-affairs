When was 73rd foundation day of Himachal Pradesh celebrated?

(A) April 10

(B) ** April 15

(C) April 11

(D) April 16

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 15 April 2021, Himachal Pradesh celebrated its 73rd Foundation day.

Concept note-2: -To commemorate the foundation of the state, April 15 is being celebrated every year as ‘Himachal Day’. Himachal Pradesh was originally classified as a Union territory in 1956. It was created as the 18th state of India in 1971.

Concept note-3: -Himachal Pradesh is celebrating its 53rd Statehood Day with full enthusiasm across the state on Wednesday(January 25). It was on this day in 1971 that Himachal Pradesh became the 18th state of India.

Concept note-4: -Himachal Day is observed every year on 15 April in Himachal Pradesh. The state of Himachal Pradesh came into existence on this day in 1948 when. Post Independence, 28 minor princely states were integrated into one province of Himachal Pradesh. Himachal Day 2023 is an official holiday in the state of Himachal Pradesh.
