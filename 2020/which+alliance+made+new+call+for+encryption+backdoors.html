Which alliance made new call for encryption backdoors?

(A) ISRO

(B) ** Five Eyes Government

(C) DRDO

(D) All of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is the Five Eyes alliance? The Five Eyes nations include Canada, Australia, New Zealand, the United Kingdom, and the United States. The genesis of the idea was born from the UKUSA Agreement, in which the above countries agreed to cooperate for signals intelligence at the start of the Cold War.

Concept note-2: -Any encryption backdoor is a vulnerability by definition. It is impossible to build a backdoor that only the ‘good guys’ can access. If the FBI can decrypt your emails or get access to your computer’s hard drive, so can criminals, terrorists, and other governments.

Concept note-3: -The central dilemma is that we can’t just break encryption or insert a backdoor in a way that only the authorities can take advantage of – doing so would weaken the entire system, making it possible for attackers to hijack the backdoor, allowing them to access everyone’s communications.

Concept note-4: -The need for government access to encrypted communications. In any investigation and prosecution, information and evidence play a key role. Criminals would thrive to protect their information relating to the crime or their activities from the authorities enforcing the law.
