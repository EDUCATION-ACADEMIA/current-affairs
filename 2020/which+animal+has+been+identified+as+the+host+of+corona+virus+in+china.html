Which animal has been identified as the host of Corona Virus in China?

(A) ** Pangolin

(B) Tapir

(C) Slow Loris

(D) Masked palm civet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses. Some coronaviruses cause cold-like illnesses in people, while others cause illness in certain types of animals, such as cattle, camels, and bats. Some coronaviruses, such as canine and feline coronaviruses, infect only animals and do not infect people.
