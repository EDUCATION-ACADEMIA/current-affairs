Which app has been launched by RBI for visually challenged persons to identify new currency notes?

(A) VISMONEY

(B) MONEY

(C) ** MANI

(D) BLIND

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The MANI application is free and can be downloaded from the Android Play Store and iOS App Store without any charges/payment.

Concept note-2: -The MANI App was launched on January 01, 2020 for aiding visually impaired persons to identify the denomination of Indian Banknotes.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has recently updated its Mobile Aided Note Identifier (MANI) app that helps the visually impaired people to identify banknotes to include 11 more Indian languages.

Concept note-4: -"DAKSH is a web-based end-to-end workflow application through which RBI shall monitor compliance requirements in a more focused manner with the objective of further improving the compliance culture in Supervised Entities (SEs) like Banks, NBFCs, etc, ” PTI quoted it as saying.

Concept note-5: -MANI (Mobile Aided Note Identifier)
