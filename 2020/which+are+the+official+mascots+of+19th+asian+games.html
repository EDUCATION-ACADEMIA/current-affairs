Which are the official mascots of 19th Asian Games?

(A) Chenchen

(B) Congcong

(C) Lianlian

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -They stand for not only the joy of sports but the culmination of cultural and economic development, so much so that they agree with the character of Hangzhou and proudly join the ranks of the emblem and slogan of the 19th Asian Games Hangzhou 2022. The three mascots are named Chenchen, Congcong and Lianlian.

Concept note-2: -Mascot of Asian Games is agiler with another year to grow The 19th Asian Games Hangzhou 2022.

Concept note-3: -Smart Triplets is the collective name of official mascots of the 19th Asian Games, Hangzhou in 2022. Individually, they are called-Congcong, Lianlian and Chenchen. The names are the UNESCO World Heritage sites of Hangzhou in eastern China. Hence Kinchen is not the official mascot.
