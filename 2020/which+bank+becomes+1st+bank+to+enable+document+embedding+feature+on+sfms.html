Which bank becomes 1st bank to enable document embedding feature on SFMS?

(A) RBI

(B) ** IDBI

(C) SBI

(D) PNB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SFMS. Structured Financial Messaging Solution – This is akin to SWIFT. SFMS allows intra / inter bank message transfer. This also provided for transfer of file attached in a secured mode.

Concept note-2: -IDBI Bank Ltd. has inherited a rich legacy from its predecessor entity - Industrial Development Bank of India – which was an apex Development Financial Institution (DFI) in the realm of industry from July 1, 1964 to September 30, 2004.

Concept note-3: -The objective of the IDBI includes: Coordinating, supervising, and controlling the activities of Finacial Institutions like ICICI, LIC, etc. The Collection of resources for other financial institutions and providing financial assistance. Planning and promoting key industries to enhance industrial growth.
