Which bank launched contactless RuPay National Common Mobility Card?

(A) SBI

(B) ** Karnataka Bank

(C) Bank of Mysore

(D) Bank of Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HDFC Bank is the first bank to issue cards to NMMT commuters. Customers can get these contactless cards over the counters of NMMT bus depot. Navi cards can be seamlessly used at retail outlets having POS terminals, E-commerce and ATMs.

Concept note-2: -Bank of Baroda subsidiary launches co-branded RuPay contactless credit card for Indian Army | Mint.

Concept note-3: -One Nation One Card Yojana:- Prime Minister Modi on 28th Dec has launched its ambitious and innovative plan known as National Common Mobility Card. The facility is now obtainable for some metro Airport Express Line.

Concept note-4: -National Common Mobility Card RuPay Contactless is a debit card which allows you to make payments in fraction of seconds, just by tapping the card on the card reader (supporting contactless transactions). You don’t need to enter the PIN for completing contactless payments below INR 5000.
