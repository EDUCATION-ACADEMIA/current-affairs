Which city has introduced India’s first ever fully contactless airport car parking with contact less toll transaction for their passengers?

(A) Delhi

(B) Bangalore

(C) ** Hyderabad

(D) Ranchi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first fully contact-less car parking at GMR’s Hyderabad International Airport.

Concept note-2: -Customers use their phones to locate a car park, book space and pay. Digital wallets such as Google Wallet, Apple Pay, etc., provide the convenience of ticketless parking. Car park operators have started using apps for their car parks, and consumers can use their e-wallets to make transactions.

Concept note-3: -Park+ FASTag is a single RFID tag for multiple needs and helps you de-clutter your car windshield. Park+ FASTag helps you with no stopping drive-thru at NHAI Toll plazas, HPCL petrol pumps, society & corporate gates, and select parking lots across the country. Using Park+ you can buy and recharge FASTag online.

Concept note-4: -Rajiv Gandhi International Airport Parking and Charges Quality parking service is assured from car park operator and efficient integrated parking automation system is implemented for the quick entry and quick exit from the parking points.

Concept note-5: -Arrival - Private cars/Government vehicle for a 90-second duration (free parking). No Minibus/ Bus/ Heavy vehicle/ yellow plate Taxi/ two-wheeler allowed on to the arrival ramp. Departure - All cars/ Taxi allowed for dropping the passengers/ visitors but no pick up from departure.
