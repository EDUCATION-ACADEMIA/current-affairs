Which city’s police has joined hands with Uber to launch Himmat Plus app for safety of people travelling in cabs?

(A) Bengaluru

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Gandhinagar

(D) Bhopal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Speaking on the occasion, Delhi Police commissioner Amulya Patnaik said that the department’s core mission is to ensure the safety of the public. Over 1, 000 Himmat QR verification cards were distributed among drivers.

Concept note-2: -The Delhi Police has integrated its “Himmat Plus App” with the app of ride hailing company “Uber” to ensure safety and security of people travelling in cabs.

Concept note-3: -The revamped app ‘Himmat Plus’ was launched by Delhi Lt Governor (LG) Anil Baijal on Tuesday.
