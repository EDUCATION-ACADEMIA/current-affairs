Which city’s police has started 3rd edition of ‘Sukanya’ project for girls’ self-defense training?

(A) New Delhi

(B) ** Kolkata

(C) Chennai

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The third edition of “Sukanya” project has been started by Kolkata Police. The batch of “Sukanya” started at 100 city-based colleges and schools situated in Kolkata Police jurisdiction.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Karnataka. Karnataka government has launched a self-defense training program ‘Obavva Art of Self Defence Training’ for 50, 000 girl students in schools and colleges.

Concept note-3: -Project Sukanya is the dream project of the Hon’ble Chief Minister of West Bengal, Dr. Mamata Banerjee in order to provide self defence training to girl students of schools and colleges. It was initiated in 2014 by the Kolkata Police in collaboration with the Women Development and Social Welfare Department.

Concept note-4: -Project Sukanya is a platform for skilled women to promote handicrafts and homemade products and in turn earn a living – this is how Project Sukanya generates employment for women.
