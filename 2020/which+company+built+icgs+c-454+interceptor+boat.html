Which company built ICGS C-454 Interceptor Boat?

(A) Mazagon Dock Limited

(B) Naval Dockyard

(C) Cochin Shipyard Limited

(D) ** Larsen & Toubro

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The interceptor boat C-454, built by Larsen and Toubro at its Hazira plant, will operate from Gujarat under the control of the Commander Coast Guard Region (North-West) of the ICG, a defence release said. The interceptor boat will ensure maritime security of Gujarat’s 1, 600-km coastline.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which firm built the Interceptor Boat ‘ICGS C-454’ that was launched into the service of the Coast Guard? Notes: Interceptor Boat ‘ICGS C-454’, that has been built by Larsen and Toubro, was recently launched into the service of the Coast Guard.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Coast Guard Ship (ICG) named C-453 was commissioned into services in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. This was the 17th interceptor boats out of the 18 which are being indigenously built by Larsen & Toubro Ltd.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Coast Guard Ship (ICGS) Vajra is the sixth vessel in the series of seven OPVs being built under a Ministry of Defence contract signed in 2015. It was commissioned to the Indian Coast Guard in 2021.
