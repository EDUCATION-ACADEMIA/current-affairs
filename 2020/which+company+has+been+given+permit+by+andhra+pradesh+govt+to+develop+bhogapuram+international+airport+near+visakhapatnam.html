Which company has been given permit by Andhra Pradesh govt. to develop Bhogapuram International Airport near Visakhapatnam?

(A) Adani Group

(B) GVK

(C) ** GMR Group

(D) Jaypee Group

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -GMR Visakhapatnam International Airport Limited (GVIAL), a subsidiary of GMR Airports Limited (GAL), is developing a Greenfield International Airport at Bhogapuram in Vizianagaram district of Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -No Objection Certificate (NOC) for Bhogapuram International Airport has been received. The State government plans to invite PM Modi to lay the foundation stone in November 2022. The airport’s construction is expected to start by the end of 2022, and the completion deadline is slated as December 2024.

Concept note-3: -The project would be taken up in the public–private partnership (PPP) mode with the state holding a stake in the form of land holdings. The proposed airport is 45 km away from Visakhapatnam, 25 km away from Vizianagaram and 63 km away from Srikakulam cities.

Concept note-4: -With three international airports - Visakhapatnam, Vijayawada (Gannavaram) and Tirupati - Andhra Pradesh is well connected to all major cities in India and to some foreign destinations also.
