Which company launched ‘The Anywhere School’ online learning?

(A) Microsoft

(B) ** Google

(C) Amazon

(D) Flipkart

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Google launches ‘The Anywhere School’ with over 50 new features.

Concept note-2: -E-Pathshala: This initiative focuses not only on students but also teachers and parents. E-Pathshala can be accessed through a web portal or mobile app for the E-textbooks with 3500+ curriculum content in English, Sanskrit, Urdu, and Hindi.

Concept note-3: -Google Classroom is a free blended learning platform developed by Google for educational institutions that aims to simplify creating, distributing, and grading assignments. The primary purpose of Google Classroom is to streamline the process of sharing files between teachers and students.

Concept note-4: -Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA), ICT in Schools, Centrally Sponsored Scheme on Teacher Education (CSSTE), Shaala Siddhi, Rashtriya Avishkar Abhiyan are being implemented to improve the quality of secondary education.
