Which country became the 25th country who was granted observer status in the World Trade Organization?

(A) Uzbekistan

(B) Pakistan

(C) ** Turkmenistan

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Turkmenistan became 25th country with Observer Status in World Trade Organization.

Concept note-2: -Currently, 164 states are members of the WTO. The WTO also has 24 observer states. Turkmenistan became the 25th observer country.

Concept note-3: -The other is Iran, which is an observer state and begun negotiations in 1996.

Concept note-4: -WTO members consist of 164 distinct nations. That is in addition to 24 observer governments (countries moving through the process of becoming members).

Concept note-5: -Vietnam became the 150th member of the World Trade Organization (WTO) on January 11, 2007.
