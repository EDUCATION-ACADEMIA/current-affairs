Which country has awarded Legion of Merit:Military honour to Narendra Modi?

(A) Russia

(B) Sri Lanka

(C) ** USA

(D) Bhutan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Following the invasion of North Africa in November 1942, a number of United States officers were awarded the Legion of Merit in the degree of Officer. One of the recipients was future Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff Lyman Lemnitzer.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Narendra Modi.

Concept note-3: -The Legion of Merit, also known as LOM is a military award that is given for exceptionally meritorious conduct in the performance of one’s duty. The decoration is issued to members of uniformed services of the United States as well as military and political figures of foreign governments.

Concept note-4: -The Legion of Merit with a “V” device is awarded to members of the United Nations Armed Forces for combat actions in the performance of outstanding service to the United States.
