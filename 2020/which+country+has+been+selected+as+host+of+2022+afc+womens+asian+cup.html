Which country has been selected as host of 2022 AFC Women’s Asian Cup?

(A) Indonesia

(B) ** India

(C) Nepal

(D) Malaysia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Host India were the most affected, with as many as 12 players testing positive for COVID-19, rendering them unable to name 13 players for their match against Chinese Taipei, as required. India was forced to withdraw due to tournament regulations.

Concept note-2: -India to host AFC Women’s Asian Cup 2022 finals. NEW DELHI: The Asian Football Confederation has granted All India Football Federation the rights to host the AFC Women’s Asian Cup 2022 finals.

Concept note-3: -Qatar will become the first country to host three Asian Cups, after 1988 and 2011. Due to the high summer temperatures in the Gulf region and Qatar’s participation in the 2023 CONCACAF Gold Cup, the tournament will probably be postponed from mid-2023 to early 2024.

Concept note-4: -Here’s everything you need to know about the upcoming AFC Women’s Asian Cup 2022. The AFC Women’s Asian Cup returns to India for the first time since 1979 as the best of Asia bids to stake its claim for the continent’s top honour. The tournament will be played in Maharashtra between January 20 and February 6.
