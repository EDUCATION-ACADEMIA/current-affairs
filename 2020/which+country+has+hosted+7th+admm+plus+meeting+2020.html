Which country has hosted 7th ADMM plus meeting 2020?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) India

(C) ** Vietnam

(D) Bangkok

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ADMM-Plus is the only official framework of Defense Minister’s meetings in the Asia-Pacific Region. In addition to the ten countries of ASEAN, eight countries such as Japan, the US and China participate in this framework to promote more practical defense cooperation.

Concept note-2: -Glimpses of ASEAN Defence Ministers Meeting (ADMM) plus attended by Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh in Siem Reap, Cambodia on November 23, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The ADMM Plus is an annual meeting of Defence Ministers of ten ASEAN countries and its eight dialogue partner countries, viz., India, USA, Russia, China, Australia, Japan, New Zealand and South Korea. The year 2022 also marks the 30th Anniversary of India-ASEAN relations.
