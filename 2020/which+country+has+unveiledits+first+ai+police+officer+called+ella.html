Which country has unveiledits first AI police officer called Ella?

(A) USA

(B) ** New Zealand

(C) France

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which country has unveiled its first Artificial Intelligence (AI) Police officer Ella? Notes: The New Zealand Police have recently unveiled their first Artificial Intelligence (AI) Police officer Ella.

Concept note-2: -New Zealand Police unveiled its first Artificial intelligence (AI) officer Ella on 12 February 2020. Ella is a digital person that is powered by AI and uses real-time animation to emulate face-to-face interactions.

Concept note-3: -Her name is ‘Ella’. Ella stands out from the crowd because she is a life-like virtual assistant who has been programmed to use real-time animation to emulate face-to-face interaction with others.

Concept note-4: -In recent years, artificial intelligence in law enforcement has become an important aspect of police work globally. As AI- based police technology becomes increasingly essential to law enforcement, areas like crime prevention and prediction are going through major changes.
