Which country is the world’s happiest nation in UN’s World Happiness Report of 156 countries?

(A) France

(B) ** Finland

(C) Norway

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Finland is in the top position in the world happiness report in 2022. Followed by Denmark and Iceland in second and third place. Switzerland, Netherlands, Luxembourg, Sweden, Norway, Israel and New Zealand, were among the top 10 ‘happiest’ countries in the world [1].

Concept note-2: -Finland was deemed the happiest country. India ranked 136th on the World Happiness Report compiled and released by the United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network on Saturday. It has ranked a total of 150 countries.

Concept note-3: -For five years in a row, Finland has ranked No. 1 as the happiest country in the world, according to the World Happiness Report.
