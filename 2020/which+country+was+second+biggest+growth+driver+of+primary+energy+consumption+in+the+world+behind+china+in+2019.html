Which country was second biggest growth driver of primary energy consumption in the world, behind China, in 2019?

(A) Brazil

(B) ** India

(C) Germany

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: India was second biggest growth driver of primary energy consumption in the world, behind China, in 2019 even though it witnessed fall in demand in oil and coal, according to BP Statistical Review released on Wednesday.

Concept note-2: -In 2019, approximately 7.5 trillion kilowatt hours of power were consumed in China. This figure represents an increase of nearly five percent in comparison to the previous year.

Concept note-3: -China is the top country by primary energy production in the world. As of 2021, primary energy production in China was 134.96 quadrillion btu that accounts for 22.25% of the world’s primary energy production.
