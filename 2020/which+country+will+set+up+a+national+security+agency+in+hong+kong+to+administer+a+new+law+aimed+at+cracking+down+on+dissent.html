(B) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Hong Kong national security law, officially the Law of the People’s Republic of China on Safeguarding National Security in the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, is a piece of national security legislation concerning Hong Kong.

Concept note-2: -Hong Kong was transferred to China on 1 July 1997, after 156 years of British rule.

Concept note-3: -subversion - undermining the power or authority of the central government. terrorism - using violence or intimidation against people.
