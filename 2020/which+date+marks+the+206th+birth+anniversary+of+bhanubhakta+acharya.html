Which date marks the 206th Birth Anniversary of Bhanubhakta Acharya?

(A) July 11

(B) ** July 13

(C) July 12

(D) July 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Early life and education. Bhanubhakta Acharya was born on 13 July 1814 (29 Ashadh 1871 BS) in the village of Chundi Ramgha, Tanahun District, Nepal to Dhananjaya Acharya, and Dharmawati Acharya.

Concept note-2: -by / 16 Jul 2020 12:39 PM IST / 0 Comment(s) / 938. 13th July is celebrated as the 206th Birth Anniversary of Bhanubhakta Acharya. He was a Nepali poet, translator and writer. He was the first writer to translate the great epic Ramayana from Sanskrit to Nepali.

Concept note-3: -Singtam: The 208th birth anniversary of the great poet of Nepali literature Bhanubhakta Acharya was observed on Tuesday at Singtam in East Sikkim. While July 13 is observed as Bhanu Jayanti, an event will accordingly be celebrated in Gangtok on Wednesday with great pomp and gaiety among the Nepali community.

Concept note-4: -Bhanubhakta’s ‘Ramayan’ is known as the first major literary work in Nepali, the country’s national language.
