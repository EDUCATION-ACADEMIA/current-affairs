Which district bagged Union Jal Shakti ministry’ award 2019 for water conservation efforts?

(A) Mahe

(B) Kutch

(C) Karaikal

(D) ** Kadapa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the Best State Category Uttar Pradesh Awarded First Prize, Rajasthan Second and Tamil Nadu Third.

Concept note-2: -Uttar Pradesh topped among all Indian states in terms of water conservation. On 29 March 2022, President Ram Nath Kovind awarded the ‘best state’ award at the National Water Awards 2022.

Concept note-3: -On March 29, 2022, President Ram Nath Kovind presented the first prize to Uttar Pradesh in the ‘Best State’ category for its best performance towards water conservation at the 3rd National Water Awards distribution ceremony at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -Launch of Jal shakti Abhiyan-Catch the Rain by Hon’ble Prime Minister of India. District Kamareddy, Government of Telangana | India.
