Which feature introduced by Government in Aarogya Setu to Help People, Businesses & Economy to return to normalcy?

(A) ** Open API

(B) KYC

(C) Net banking

(D) none of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Open API Service of Aarogya Setu, addresses the fear/risk of Covid-19 infections and help the people, businesses and the economy to return to normalcy.

Concept note-2: -The App uses Bluetooth based contact tracing to identify potential users who may have come in close proximity (during the past couple of weeks) with a person who has tested positive for COVID-19.

Concept note-3: -vi. All passengers shall be advised to download Aarogya Setu app on their mobile devices. vii.

Concept note-4: -On taking the self-assessment on Aarogya Setu, those users who have taken at least one dose of COVID19 Vaccine will get the tab of “partially vaccination/vaccinated (unverified)“ on the home screen of Aarogya Setu. This is based on the declaration of vaccination status given by the user during the self-assessment.
