Which Heritage Park has received the Award of Excellence 2020, for the Cultural Heritage Conservation was released by UNESCO?

(A) ** Sunder Nursery

(B) Lodhi Garden

(C) Coupons Hacker

(D) Glass House

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UNESCO New Delhi is pleased to share that Delhi’s Heritage Park, Sunder Nursery received the Award of Excellence for their successful efforts towards ecological and heritage restoration.

Concept note-2: -Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya (CSMVS) was conferred with the ‘Award of Excellence’ at the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organisation (UNESCO) Asia-Pacific Awards for Cultural Heritage Conservation-2022.

Concept note-3: -Award of Excellence: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya, Mumbai. Award of Distinction: Stepwells of Golconda, Hyderabad. Award of Merit: Domakonda Fort, Telangana, and Byculla Station, Mumbai.

Concept note-4: -There are 40 UNESCO World Heritage Sites in India. Dholavira and Ramappa Temple are the latest addition to the list under the ‘Cultural’ category. ‘Ramappa Temple’, Telangana and ‘Dholavira’, Gujarat which is included in the list of UNESCO World Heritage sites in 2021.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Amar Singh College of Srinagar. The Amar Singh College of Srinagar was recognized with the ‘Award of Merit’ in the 2020 UNESCO Asia-Pacific Awards for Cultural Heritage Conservation.
