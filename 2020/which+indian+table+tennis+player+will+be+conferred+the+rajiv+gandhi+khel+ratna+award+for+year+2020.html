Which Indian Table Tennis player will be conferred the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award for year 2020?

(A) Mouma Das

(B) Madhurika Patkar

(C) ** Manika Batra

(D) Sharath Kamal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rohit Sharma: Batsman Rohit Sharma was among the five athletes who had received the country’s highest sporting honour, the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award, for 2020.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Manika Batra.

Concept note-3: -Shri Sharath Kamal Achanta has won the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 in the Table Tennis discipline. The Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 ( 2022) is given by the Indian government and the Department of Sport to those sportspersons who have excelled in their sports.

Concept note-4: -On August 29, 2020, the Sports Ministry handed over a total of 74 National Sports Awards, including 5 Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Awards and 27 Arjuna Awards.
