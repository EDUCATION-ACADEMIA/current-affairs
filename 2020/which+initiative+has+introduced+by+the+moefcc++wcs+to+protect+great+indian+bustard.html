Which initiative has introduced by the MoEFCC & WCS to protect Great Indian Bustard?

(A) ** Firefly Bird Diverter

(B) Flak-like Diverter

(C) Fire Flak Diverter

(D) Flak Bird Diverter

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -d. The Ministry of Environment Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC) along with the Wildlife Conservation Society (WCS) India has come up with a unique initiative called “Firefly Bird Diverter” for overhead power lines.

Concept note-2: -With an objective of conservation of the remaining population of critically endangered Great Indian Bustard Ardeotis nigriceps, locally called Godawan, an ambitious conservation program namely, Project Great Indian Bustard, has was launched by Honorable Chief Minister, Ashok Gehlot on 5thof June 2013.

Concept note-3: -About: Firefly bird diverters are flaps installed on power lines. They work as reflectors for bird species like the GIB. Birds can spot them from a distance of about 50 meters and change their path of flight to avoid collision with power lines.

Concept note-4: -The objective of ‘Habitat Improvement and Conservation Breeding of Great Indian Bustard-an integrated approach’ is to build the captive population of the GIBs and to release the chicks in the wild. The initiative is likely to take 20 to 25 years.
