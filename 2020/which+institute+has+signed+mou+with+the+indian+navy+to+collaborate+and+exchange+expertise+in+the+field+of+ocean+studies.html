Which institute has signed MoU with The Indian Navy to collaborate and exchange expertise in the field of Ocean Studies?

(A) ** CSIR NIO

(B) CSIR-NIO

(C) CAS

(D) CASMB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MOU BETWEEN INDIAN NAVY AND SPACE APPLICATIONS CENTRE (ISRO) FOR COOPERATION ON SATELLITE BASED NAVAL APPLICATIONS IN OCEANOLOGY AND METEOROLOGY.

Concept note-2: -Amity University Uttar Pradesh signed an MoU with the Indian Navy to form a long-term relationship for Academic cooperation. The MoU will enhance the educational qualifications.

Concept note-3: -The National Institute of Oceanography (NIO)with its headquarters at Dona Paula, Goa, and regional centers at Kochi, Mumbai and Visakhapatnam, is one of the 38 constituent laboratories of the Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CSIR), New Delhi.
