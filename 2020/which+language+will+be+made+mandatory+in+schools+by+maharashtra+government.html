Which language will be made mandatory in schools by Maharashtra government?

(A) Hindi

(B) ** Marathi

(C) English

(D) Sanskrit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For the academic year 2021-22, the students in 2nd and 7th standard will be mandated to study Marathi followed by 3rd and 8th stranded in 2022-23, 4th and 9th stranded in 2023-24 and 5th and 10th standard students will be mandated to study in 2024-25.

Concept note-2: -As per the GR, Marathi will be a compulsory subject taught in all schools including the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE), Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE) as well as applicable to IB and NIOS too.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI: The Maharashtra education department has made it mandatory for schools affiliated to the state board, Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), Council of Indian School Certificate Examination (CISCE) and other boards to teach Marathi language as a compulsory subject.

Concept note-4: -Maharashtra culture minister Subhash Desai who introduced the bill in the assembly on Thursday said the Maharashtra Official Languages Act, 1964 did not make the use of Marathi mandatory for the local authorities. Desai said Marathi will be mandatory for communicating with the people of the state.
