Which minister held 5th review meeting on CAPEX in this financial year?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) ** Nirmala Sitharaman

(C) Subrahmanyam Jaishankar

(D) Narendra Modi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Finance Minister Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman holds 5th review meeting on CAPEX of CPSEs to boost expenditure in economy. Union Minister for Finance & Corporate Affairs Smt.

Concept note-2: -Economic growth and spending promises In her 2022 budget, Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman said that India’s economic growth in the current fiscal year (to the end of March 2023) was “estimated to be 9.2%, [the] highest among all large economies".

Concept note-3: -Sitharaman’s budget for the fiscal year starting April 2023 is her fifth straight since 2019. Other ministers who have presented five straight annual financial statements include Arun Jaitley, P Chidambaram, Yashwant Sinha, Manmohan Singh and Morarji Desai.
