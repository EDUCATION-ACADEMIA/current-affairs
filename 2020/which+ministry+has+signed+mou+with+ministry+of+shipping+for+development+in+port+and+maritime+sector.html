Which ministry has signed MoU with Ministry of shipping for development in port and maritime sector?

(A) Ministry of Culture

(B) ** Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship

(C) Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises

(D) Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways (MoPSW) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) to jointly undertake the skill development project and harness the innate capabilities in Ports and Maritime sector.

Concept note-2: -Today, Hon’ble Union Minister MoPSW Shri sarbanandsonwal will inaugurate ‘Bharat Pravah’ in New Delhi. It will showcase the socio-cultural & economic significance of rivers & ports in everyday life and also highlight the importance of Ports, Shipping & Waterways in the nation.

Concept note-3: -Union Minister of State (I/C) for Port, Shipping and Waterways chaired the 18th meeting of the Maritime State Development Council (MSDC) through a video conference organized by the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday said the Ministry of Shipping is being expanded and renamed as the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways.
