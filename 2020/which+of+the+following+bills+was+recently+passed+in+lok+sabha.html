Which of the following bills was recently passed in Lok Sabha?

(A) Education bill

(B) Reservation for minorities bill

(C) ** Bilateral Netting of qualified financial contracts bill

(D) None of the above.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Amendment) Bill, 2021. The Airports Economic Regulatory Authority of India (Amendment) Bill, 2021. The Government of National Capital Territory of Delhi (Amendment) Bill, 2021. The Finance Bill, 2021.

Concept note-2: -2022. Following this, in relation to the state of Karnataka, the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Fourth Amendment) Bill, 2022, was also passed unanimously in Rajya Sabha on 22.12.

Concept note-3: -A money bill is deemed to have passed both houses with any recommended amendments the Lok Sabha chooses to accept, and without any that it chooses to decline. The definition of “Money Bill” is given in Article 110 of The Constitution of India.

Concept note-4: -Money Bills can be introduced only in the Lok Sabha. IV. Rajya Sabha is fully empowered to reject or amend a Financial Bill as it does in the case of ordinary bill .
