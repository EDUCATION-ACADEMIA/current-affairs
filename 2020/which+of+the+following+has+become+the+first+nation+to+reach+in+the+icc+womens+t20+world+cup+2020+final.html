Which of the following has become the first nation to reach in the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2020 Final?

(A) South Africa

(B) ** India

(C) England

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Packed with proven performers, Australia have lifted the trophy five times in seven editions. They have hardly been challenged in the format since winning the last T20 World Cup in 2020.

Concept note-2: -Harmanpreet also became the first Indian woman to score a century in T20I. She is now the third player in world cricket to score a T20I International century. Coming in to bat at number five, she stitched a record 134-run partnership with Jemimah Rodrigues to put India in command.

Concept note-3: -New Zealand defeated Australia by four runs in the final, winning their first and only title.
