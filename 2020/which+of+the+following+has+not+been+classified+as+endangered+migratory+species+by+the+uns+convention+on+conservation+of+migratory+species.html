Which of the following has not been classified as “endangered migratory species” by the UN’s Convention on Conservation of Migratory Species?

(A) Great Indian Bustard

(B) Asian Elephant

(C) ** Striped Hyena

(D) Bengal Florican

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Convention on Migratory Species (CMS), also known as the Bonn Convention, is an environmental treaty of the United Nations that provides a global platform for the conservation and sustainable use of terrestrial, aquatic and avian migratory animals and their habitats.

Concept note-2: -Migratory species means a species of animal that has the whole or a part of its population emigrate regularly, periodically or seasonally from a geographical area to another one.

Concept note-3: -The Convention on the Conservation of Migratory Species of Wild Animals (also known as CMS or the Bonn Convention) was signed in Bonn, Germany, on 23 June 1979. It aims to conserve terrestrial, aquatic, and avian migratory species throughout their range.

Concept note-4: -This treaty of the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) aims to facilitate close cooperation on the conservation of migratory species between the countries through which these animals travel on their annual journeys.
