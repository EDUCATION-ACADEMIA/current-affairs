Which of the following police stations awarded with ISO 9001:2015 certification for quality management system?

(A) Kerala Police Station, Alappuzha

(B) ** North Police Station in Kohima

(C) New Delhi Police Station

(D) Assam Police, Guwahati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bhubaneswar becomes 1st Indian city to get ISO 9001:2015 certification - What is FSSM services?

Concept note-2: -Chennai: Flower Bazaar police station was awarded with ISO 9001:2015 and WASH (Workplace Assessment for Safety and Hygiene) standard certificates after an autonomous body carried out an independent assessment on Wednesday.

Concept note-3: -Key Points: i. 29 private cesspool vehicles along with BMC cesspool vehicles provided FSSM services in all the 67 wards of the Bhubaneswar.

Concept note-4: -ISO 9001 sets out the criteria for a quality management system and is the only standard in the family that can be certified to (although this is not a requirement). It can be used by any organization, large or small, regardless of its field of activity.
