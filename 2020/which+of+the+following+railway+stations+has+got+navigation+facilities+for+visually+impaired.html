Which of the following railway stations has got navigation facilities for visually impaired?

(A) Ludhiana Junctionrailway station

(B) ** Chandigarh railway station

(C) Amritsar Junction railway station

(D) Jalandhar city railway station

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ot Staff. To make railway journeys friendlier and safer, the Southern Railway has installed Braille navigation maps at the Puratchi Thalaivar Dr M.G. Ramachandran Central Railway Station and Egmore station.

Concept note-2: -Two Braille master boards installed Coimbatore also earns the distinction of being the fourth station under the Railways to become visually challenged-friendly following Mysuru, Bengaluru stations in Karnataka and Borivali station in Mumbai, Maharashtra.
