Concept note-1: -What led to Russia being ‘banned’ from Tokyo Olympics 2020? In December 2019, the World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA) banned Russia for four years from competing in international events, including the Tokyo Olympics and the FIFA World Cup in 2022.

Concept note-2: -Technically, Russia is banned from competing in the Olympics. But like the 2020 Summer Olympics in Tokyo, Russian athletes are still participating at the 2022 Winter Olympics.

Concept note-3: -2016 and 2020 Olympics After investigations, the IOC barred Russia’s entire team from participating in any Olympic competition.

Concept note-4: -Russia was officially banned in 2019 from competing in international sporting events for four years after it was caught running a state-sponsored doping program designed to boost its medal haul at international sporting events.
