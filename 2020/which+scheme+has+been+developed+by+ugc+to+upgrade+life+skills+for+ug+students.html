Which scheme has been developed by UGC to upgrade life skills for UG students?

(A) ** Jeevan Kaushal

(B) Ajivika Abhiyan

(C) Kaushal Jeevan

(D) Abhiyan Sanchalika

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The WHO has identified ten core life skills that include Self-Awareness, Empathy, Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking, Decision-making, Problem-solving, Interpersonal Skills, Effective Communication, Coping with Stress, and Managing Emotions (WHO, 1997).

Concept note-2: -Curriculum for Life Skills (Jeevan Kaushal) is a University Grants Commission (UGC) initiative for providing Life Skills (including soft skills) to students enrolled in Institutions of Higher Education especially at under-graduate level.

Concept note-3: -Youth focused Life Skills Education and Counseling Services (YLSECS) program, trained teachers/National Service Scheme (NSS) officers to deliver Life Skills Education (LSE) and counseling services to college going youth in the state of Karnataka in India.
