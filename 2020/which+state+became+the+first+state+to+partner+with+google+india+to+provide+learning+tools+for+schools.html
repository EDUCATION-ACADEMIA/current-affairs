Which state Became the First State to Partner with Google India to Provide Learning Tools for Schools?

(A) ** Maharashtra

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Assam

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maharashtra Became the First State to Partner with Google India to Provide Learning Tools for Schools.

Concept note-2: -Kerala has become the first state in the country that has made public education completely digital. Kerala achieved this milestone within two years of the launch of the initiative. The total expenditure on the initiative was Rs 42.36 crores.

Concept note-3: -Google Devices Google conquered the classroom, or started to, in 2014 as it has been marketing Chromebooks to schools since that time (De Vynck & Bergen, 2020).

Concept note-4: -Classroom was the brainchild of Mr. Rochelle, who started Google’s education apps group, and Zach Yeskel, a Google product manager and former high school math teacher.

Concept note-5: -Google Workspace for Education Fundamentals-Gives you tools to aid teaching and learning, such as Classroom, Google Meet, Google Docs, Google Forms, and Google Chat. Google Workspace for Education Standard-Same tools as Education Fundamentals but with advanced security features and enhanced administration controls.
