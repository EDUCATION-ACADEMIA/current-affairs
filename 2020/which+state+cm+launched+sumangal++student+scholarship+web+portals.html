Which state CM launched ‘Sumangal’ & Student Scholarship web portals?

(A) Sikkim

(B) Assam

(C) Kerala

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Odisha Chief Minister, Naveen Patnaik has launched two web portals named ‘Odisha State Scholarship Portal’ and ‘Sumangal Portal’.

Concept note-2: -Odisha CM Naveen Patnaik sanctions scholarship worth Rs 101 crore for SC, ST students.

Concept note-3: -The rate of the scholarship for students at the graduation level is INR 10, 000 per annum for the 1st three years of college and university courses. The rate of the scholarship for students at the postgraduation level is INR 20, 000 per annum.

Concept note-4: -This scheme is opened for General, SC, ST, OBC/SEBC, EBC students to apply. The last date for the submission of application is 31 May 2023.

Concept note-5: -Eligible students can apply for the scheme through the Odisha State Scholarship Portal on or before the deadline, which is April 10th. Under this initiative, selected beneficiaries will receive a free laptop or a cash prize of Rs. 30000.
