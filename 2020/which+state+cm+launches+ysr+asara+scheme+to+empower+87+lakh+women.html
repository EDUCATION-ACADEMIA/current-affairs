Which state CM launches YSR Asara scheme to empower 87 lakh women?

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) ** Andhra Pradesh

(C) Telangana

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -YSR Aasara is a program launched by the Government of Andhra Pradesh to improve the productivity of Self Help Groups(SHGs) by reimbursing the outstanding loans and improve the lifestyle of urban and rural women.

Concept note-2: -YSR Asara scheme will increase the women empowerment rate. The Government of Andhra Pradesh is going to give SHG loan to women who belong to the minority community. Now the females of Andhra Pradesh will not have to take a loan on a higher rate of interest.

Concept note-3: -Through the YSR Cheyutha scheme, 23.14 lakh women belonging to 45 to 60 years of age will get the benefit for 4 years. For the implementation of this scheme special arrangements will also be made at Rythu Bharosa Kendra in every village so that each and every beneficiary of the state can get the benefit of this scheme.

Concept note-4: -The objective of the scheme is to give money to the women so that they can continue their life without any problem. Given below are the details regarding the objective benefits and the step-by-step procedure through which you will be able to apply for the YSR Asara Pathakam 2021.

Concept note-5: -Under AP YSR Asara Scheme, loans will be provided to the women of self-help groups in 4 installments. The amount will be directly transferred to the women of self-help groups by the Andhra Pradesh government in their bank accounts.
