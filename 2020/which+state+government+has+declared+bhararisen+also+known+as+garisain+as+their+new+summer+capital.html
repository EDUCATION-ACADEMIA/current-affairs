Which state Government has declared Bhararisen, also known as Garisain, as their new summer capital?

(A) Himachal Pradesh

(B) ** Uttarakhand

(C) Odisha

(D) Meghalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is being considered as the future Permanent capital of Uttarakhand. Gairsain was envisaged as the state capital during the statehood agitation. However, after the formation of the state on 9 November 2000, Dehradun was made the temporary capital of the state.

Concept note-2: -The correct option is D Shimla Q. is known as the summer capital of India.
