Which state government has launched Orunodoi scheme?

(A) ** Assam

(B) Sikkim

(C) Meghalaya

(D) Tripura

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Turning Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s vision of direct benefit transfer into reality, the Himanta Biswa Sarma -led Assam government has launched a special scheme, Orunodoi 2.0, under which 10.54 lakh beneficiaries will be given Rs 1, 250 every month, officials said.

Concept note-2: -The Assam Orunodoi Scheme was launch by the Assam government on December 1, 2020. Beneficiaries of the Orunodoi Scheme in Assam will receive Rs 830 per month to purchase essentials like sugar, medicines, and pulses.

Concept note-3: -The largest direct benefit transfer programme, known as Assam Arunodoi Scheme 2023 or Orunodoi, was introduced on December 1, 2020. It is a brand-new initiative to support economically disadvantaged families and empower women.

Concept note-4: -Orunodoi, the poverty alleviation scheme, launched on December 1, 2020, entails a monthly grant of ₹ 1, 250 to each beneficiary. It is one of 18 flagship schemes of the Government of Assam in operation.
