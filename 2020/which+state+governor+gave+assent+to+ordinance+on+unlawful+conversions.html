Which state Governor gave assent to ordinance on Unlawful Conversions?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Chhattisgarh

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Odisha was the first State to enact anti-conversion legislation, the Orissa Freedom of Religion Act, 1967. Madhya Pradesh enacted the same the following year.

Concept note-2: -Such laws, already in force in five of India’s states (Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Odisha, Gujarat and Himachal Pradesh) and being proposed in a sixth (Maharashtra), are used disproportionately against minorities.
