Which state Govt Launched ‘Karma Sathi Prakalpa’ scheme to Provide Soft Loans, Subsidies to Youth?

(A) Odisha

(B) ** West Bengal

(C) Haryana

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -During International Youth Day, West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee introduced the Karma Sathi Prakalpa Scheme. This initiative will provide soft loans and subsidies to around 1 lakh unemployed youngsters in West Bengal, allowing them to become self-sufficient.

Concept note-2: -Under the Yuvashree scheme, an unemployment allowance of Rs 1500 per month will be given to the eligible youth. Youth eligible for Yuvashree Unemployment Allowance can avail the benefit of the scheme by applying on the Employment Bank Portal.

Concept note-3: -About Karma Sathi Prakalpa Under the scheme, the state government has set a target of providing loans to one lakh youths every year. Each beneficiary can avail of a loan up to Rs 2 lakh under the scheme. Apart from loans, the state government will also provide subsidies for taking up new projects.

Concept note-4: -The main objective of WB Sathi prakalpa scheme 2022 is to provide loans to unemployed youths of West Bengal State so that they can get some kind of employment by starting some new business.
