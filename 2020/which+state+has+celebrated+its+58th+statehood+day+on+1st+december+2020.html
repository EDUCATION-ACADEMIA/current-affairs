Which state has celebrated its 58th Statehood day on 1st December 2020?

(A) ** Nagaland

(B) Haryana

(C) Jharkhand

(D) Sikkim

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The State, on 1st December 2020, celebrated its 58th Statehood Day at Nagaland Civil Secretariat Plaza, Kohima.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Nagaland. Nagaland celebrated its 58th Statehood Day on 1 December 2020. It became the 16th State of the Union of India on 1st December 1963.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Nagaland. Nagaland’s celebrated its 59th Statehood Day on December 1, 2021. On this day in 1963, it became the 16th state of India.
