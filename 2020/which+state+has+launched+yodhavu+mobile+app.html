Which state has launched ‘Yodhavu’ mobile app?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Karnataka

(C) ** Kerala

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kerala CM launches ‘’Yodhavu” mobile app to combat drug menace | Deccan Herald.

Concept note-2: -X. Photo: Mathrubhumi. Thiruvananthapuram: Kerala Police launched a project called ‘Yodhav’ (Warrior) to eliminate the production, use and trafficking of drugs in the state. The project will have the support of Education, Health, Excise, Social Justice and Local Self Government departments.

Concept note-3: -’Yodhavu’ (Warrior), a mobile app through which the public can inform police about drug abuse and its distribution. People can reach out to the number through photos, voice messages or videos if they come across anyone selling or using drugs.

Concept note-4: -Users have to use WhatsApp in the normal manner for using the app. Information can be shared in the form of audio, video, image, or text just like sending any other WhatsApp message to a designated number. Information can be passed on to the number 99959-66666.
