Which state is to provide free laptops to first-year-degree students in government colleges from January 12?

(A) Telangana

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) ** Karnataka

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Through this scheme, free laptops will be provide to students who have secure minimum 65% marks in class 10th and 12th. A budget of 1800 crores has been set by the government for the UP Free Laptop Yojana 2023.

Concept note-2: -Features Of The Laptop Scheme Over 1.50 lakh state students who belong to ST and SC categories will be benefited from the scheme. The scheme is aimed to help students belonging to ST/SC category. Laptops of around Rs 32, 000 to Rs 35, 000 will be provided to the students.

Concept note-3: -Features of the UP Muft laptop Yojana A merit list will be generated, based on which students who applied will be given the laptops. For this scheme, the government is providing a budget allocation of INR 1800 crore. The academic performance of the student will decide the securing of the laptop.

Concept note-4: - 2023 First step for registration of UP Free Laptop Scheme is visit upcmo.up.nic.in. Secondly, click on the Free Laptop Yojana for Students link in the main menu. After that download the Application Form and fill the complete details.
