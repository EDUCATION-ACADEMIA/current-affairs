Which state launched Mukhya Mantri Kisan Sahay Yojana replacing PMFBY to compensate crop loss of farmers for FY21?

(A) ** Gujarat

(B) Punjab

(C) Haryana

(D) Chattisgarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which state government has launched ‘Mukhya Mantri Kisan Sahay Yojana’ replacing PM Fasal Bima Yojana? Notes: The Chief Minister of Gujarat Vijay Rupani has recently launched a State-funded scheme named Mukhya Mantri Kisan Sahay Yojana.

Concept note-2: -Notes: Goa government has formally launched a state-sponsored pilgrimage scheme for people named ‘Mukhyamantri Devdarshan Yatra Yojana’. Under the scheme, people over the age of 50 years are eligible to apply and the state government funds the meals and boarding of pilgrims during the tours.

Concept note-3: -The focus areas with regards to implementation of PMFBY are as follows: Spreading awareness through publicity campaigns. Educating farmers about the benefits of the scheme to persuade them to get coverage under PMFBY. Wide coverage of non-loanee farmers under the scheme.
