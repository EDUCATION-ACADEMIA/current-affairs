Which state launches 3 schemes to generate 25 crore man-days for MGNREGA to help returnee migrant workers?

(A) Telangana

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Bihar

(D) ** Jharkhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the new wage rates notified on Tuesday, Sikkim has the highest daily payout at Rs 333 under the MGNREGA for 2022-23. The amount was Rs 318 in 2021-22.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Uttarakhand. Uttarakhand state has raised the working days under MNREGA from 100 to 150 days.
