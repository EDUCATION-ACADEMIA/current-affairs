Which state police department launched ‘CybHER’, a month-long virtual awareness campaign to make cyberspace safe for women and children?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Haryana

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“CybHER- Making Cyberspace Safe for Women and Children”, a month-long online awareness campaign launched by Women Safety Wing, Telangana State Police in association with the Legal Aid Centre, Symbiosis Law School, Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -Bharosa App Bharosa is now being replicated in all districts of Telangana State.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which state/UT Police launched the ‘Cyber Congress initiative’? Notes: The Women Safety Wing of Telangana Police recently launched the ‘Cyber Congress initiative’. It aims to train, empower and equip the young generation the knowledge and skills required to protect themselves and their communities from cyber crimes.

Concept note-4: -Akancha Srivastava, is the founder of Akancha Srivastava Foundation. The Foundation was formed in 2017. It is an 80G certified, non-profit organization in India that works for the education and empowerment of people by imparting the knowledge of cyber safety via its initiative Akancha Against Harassment .
