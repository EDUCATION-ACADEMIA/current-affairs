Which state ranked top in terms of performance in Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)-II as per union minister Jitendra Singh?

(A) Chandigarh

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) ** Himachal Pradesh

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Himachal Pradesh’s Mandi district has bagged the top position in successful implementation of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY).

Concept note-2: -Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY) was formally launched in 18 B.B village in Sri Ganganagar district of Rajasthan on 23 July 2010.

Concept note-3: -This Centrally Sponsored Scheme was introduced in 2000 by the then-prime minister of India Atal Bihari Vajpayee.
