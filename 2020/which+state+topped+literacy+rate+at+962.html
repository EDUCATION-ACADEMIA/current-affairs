Which state topped literacy rate at 96.2%?

(A) Karnataka

(B) ** Kerala

(C) Gujarat

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At 96.2 per cent, Kerala emerged as the most literate state in India once again. In Kerala, the female literacy rate has been pegged at 95.2 per cent in contrast to the male literacy rate at 97.4 per cent.

Concept note-2: -As per the Indian Census, amongst the 28 states of India, Kerala has the highest literacy rank whereas Bihar stands at the lowest. The most Literate Union Territory is Lakshadweep with an approximately 91.8% literacy rate. The least in Jammu and Kashmir with a 68.7% literacy rate.

Concept note-3: -Among the states in India, Kerala has the highest literacy rate.

Concept note-4: -Kerala is the most literate state in India, with the highest literacy rate of 94 percent. Which state in India has the lowest literacy rate? According to the National Statistical Office (NSO) survey, Andhra Pradesh has the lowest literacy rate at 66.4 per cent.

Concept note-5: -Andhra Pradesh’s literacy rate of 66.4% is the lowest among all states in India, and Kerala ranked highest in the list with 96.2%. Delhi stood second with 88.7%.
