Which state won the men’s title at the senior National volleyball championships?

(A) ** Tamil Nadu

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Odisha

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Volleyball Championship: Rajasthan emerges champion in men’s category, Kerala lifts women’s title.

Concept note-2: -Haryana team defeated the Indian Railway 3-0, to win the Men’s title in Senior National Volleyball Championship 2021-22. Similarly, in the women’s category, the Kerala team defeated the Indian Railway 3-1, to lift the trophy.

Concept note-3: -Senior National Volleyball Tournament From February 2 To 9 In Guwahati. GUWAHATI: The 71st Senior National Volleyball tournament will be held from February 2 to 9 in Guwahati.

Concept note-4: -In terms of competition results, Gujarat captured the men’s team gold, with Haryana, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh in respective second, third and fourth places. The women’s team event was won by West Bengal.

Concept note-5: -Bhubaneswar: Haryana Men’s Team and Kerala Women’s Team became champions of 70th Senior National Volleyball Championship held at the Biju Patnaik Indoor Stadium, KIIT Deemed to be University by following COVID guidelines. The Haryana Men’s Team became the champion after defeating the Indian Railway 3-0.
