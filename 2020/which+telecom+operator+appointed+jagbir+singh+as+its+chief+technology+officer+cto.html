Which telecom operator appointed Jagbir Singh as its Chief Technology Officer (CTO)?

(A) ** Vodafone Idea

(B) Reliance Jio

(C) Airtel

(D) BSNL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vodafone Idea appoints Jagbir Singh as CTO.

Concept note-2: -Jagbir Singh, Vodafone Idea Ltd: Profile and Biography - Bloomberg Markets.

Concept note-3: -A chief technology officer (CTO) is an executive who is responsible for the management of an organization’s research and development (R&D) as well as its technological needs.

Concept note-4: -Organization. CTOs typically manage several departments, ranging from network infrastructure to help desk support to security, ensuring that each department is staffed with the right expertise and works cohesively with one another.
