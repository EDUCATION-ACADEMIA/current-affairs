Which TV show creates world record for Highest Viewed Entertainment Programme Globally?

(A) Jai Hanuman

(B) Jai Shri Krishna

(C) ** Ramayan

(D) Mahabharat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -#RAMAYAN - WORLD RECORD! Highest Viewed Entertainment Program Globally, another tweet read. Written, produced and directed by Ramanand Sagar, Ramayan, first aired on Doordarshan in 1987 and acquired a cult status over the years.

Concept note-2: -One Piece is the most-watched show in the world (according to TV Time) As for One Piece, the pirate-themed anime adventure is one of the biggest anime series in history, adapted from one of the biggest manga series in history.

Concept note-3: -Ramanand Sagar’s Ramayan returned to TV on March 28 and smashed viewership records. It recorded 77 million impressions on April 16, making it the most-watched entertainment show in the world, according to BARC India.

Concept note-4: -The most popular TV show and one of the most famous Hindi serials as per the data is “Kundali Bhagya”. It was a show started by Zee TV and is categorized as the highest trp show.
