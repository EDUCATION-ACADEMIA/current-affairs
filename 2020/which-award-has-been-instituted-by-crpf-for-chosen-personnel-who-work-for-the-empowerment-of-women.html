Which award has been instituted by CRPF for chosen personnel who work for the empowerment of women?

(A) Saksham

(B) ** Shakti

(C) Nari

(D) Nirbhaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The CRPF has established a special “Shakti Award” that will be granted yearly to a selected member of staff who works to advance women’s rights.

Concept note-2: – Provide legal aid and guidance to women. - Cater to the primary need for food, shelter, clothing, and health of women. - Create a positive environment for women with access to basic healthcare, education, employment, etc. - Provide these opportunities at the block and district level in the country.

Concept note-3: -Women empowerment helps boost women’s status through literacy, education, training and awareness creation. Furthermore, women’s empowerment refers to women’s ability to make strategic life choices that were previously denied them.

Concept note-4: -The National Policy on Empowerment of Women adopted in 2001 states that “ All forms of violence against women, physical and mental, whether at domestic or societal levels, including those arising from customs, traditions or accepted practices shall be dealt with effectively with a view to eliminate its incidence.

Concept note-5: -Nari Shakti Puraskar is given by the Government of India to deserving individuals as well as institutions that work for the empowerment of women. The Nari Shakti Puraskar is awarded annually on 8th March, i.e., on the occasion of International Women’s Day.
