Which Bank has introduced a first-of-its-kind facility for customers to open current accounts through its mobile app?

(A) Federal Bank

(B) Dhanlaxmi Bank

(C) Axis Bank

(D) ** IndusInd Bank Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IndusInd Bank has recently launched a mobile application-based facility for opening a current account, thereby becoming the first bank in the country to do so.

Concept note-2: -Now IndusInd is the first bank in India to introduce this assisted mobile application based facility.

Concept note-3: -ICICI Bank was the first bank to introduce internet banking facility in the country in order to enable individuals and corporates to transact online without visiting the branch. It was also the first bank to start mobile banking in the country.

Concept note-4: -Best Current Accounts in 2023 IndusInd Bank: Free fund transfer benefit via NEFT, RTGS, and IMPS. HDFC Bank: Pre-approved loans on current account deposits. Kotak Mahindra Bank: Convert current account balance to term deposits. SBI: Surplus funds in the current account are transferred to the term deposit account.
