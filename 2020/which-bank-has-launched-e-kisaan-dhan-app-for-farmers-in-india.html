Which Bank has launched ‘e-Kisaan Dhan’ App for Farmers in India?

(A) UCO Bank

(B) ** HDFC Bank

(C) ICICI Bank

(D) IDBI Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Our e-Kisaan Dhan app will allow us to do just that taking HDFC Bank’s entire range of products and services across the length and breadth of the country, particularly to rural India.

Concept note-2: -HDFC Bank has launched the e-Kisaan Dhan app across India to access a bouquet of agricultural and banking services on their mobile phones for farmers. The app will provide value-added services such as mandi prices, weather forecast, latest farming news, information on seed varieties, e-pashuhaat.

Concept note-3: -In the statement, Axis Bank claimed it is “first new generation private sector bank to launch an electronic Kisan Card on the RuPay platform."
