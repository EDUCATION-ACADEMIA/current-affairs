Which bank has published its Ecowrap report?

(A) ** SBI

(B) SVC

(C) HDFC

(D) ICICI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -FY22 GDP growth seen at 8.2-8.5%, for Q4 at 2.7%: SBI Ecowrap report.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains Q. ‘Ecowrap’ is the flagship economic research report of which financial institution of India? Notes: India’s largest lender State Bank of India (SBI) has recently released the recent edition of its flagship economic research report ‘Ecowrap’.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains Q. The economic report named “Ecowrap” is released by which organization? Notes: The State Bank of India in its recent Ecowrap report has lowered India’s GDP contraction for FY 21 from earlier 10.9% to 7.4%.

Concept note-4: -The State Bank of India published its Ecowrap report on Income Inequality in India.
