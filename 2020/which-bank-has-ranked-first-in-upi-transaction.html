Which bank has ranked first in UPI transaction?

(A) ** SBI

(B) Yes Bank

(C) Bank of Baroda

(D) HDFC Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Paytm Payments Bank, which powers Paytm UPI, continues to lead UPI payments as the largest beneficiary bank and a leading remitter bank.

Concept note-2: -PhonePe is the best UPI app in India and it is a revolutionary app that trusts Indian people for making online mobile payments. It is not only for UPI payment but also used for recharge, making online bill payments, ordering food, shopping, etc.

Concept note-3: -According to the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI), a person can transfer up to ₹ 1 lakh through UPI in a single day. Google Pay, Paytm, and PhonePe are some of the commonly used applications to make UPI transactions.

Concept note-4: -PhonePe and Google Pay command over 85% of the UPI market share, even as over a dozen players also compete in the space.
