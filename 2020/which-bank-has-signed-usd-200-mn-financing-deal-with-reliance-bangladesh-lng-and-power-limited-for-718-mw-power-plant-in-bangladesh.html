Which bank has signed USD 200 mn financing deal with Reliance Bangladesh LNG and Power Limited for 718 mw power plant in Bangladesh?

(A) WHO

(B) SIDBI

(C) IMF

(D) ** ADB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The government of Bangladesh on Sunday signed an agreement with Reliance Power of India to set up 750 MW gas-based power plant in Meghnahat near Dhaka.

Concept note-2: -The project is still under construction. As of the end of December 2021, an overall progress of 74.3% had been achieved by the EPC Contractor, Samsung C&T Corporation, which is 2.5% behind the planned progress of 76.8% as per the schedule of the Project.

Concept note-3: -The largest steam turbine power plant based on natural gas is situated at Ghorasal, Narsingdi. There are six numbers of units (per units 55 and 210 MW) having capacity (55x2+210x4) 950 MW. Installation of 200-300 MW capacity duel fuel plants at the existing Ghorasal premises is under process.
