Which bank is to acquire shares in CSC e-Governance for Rs 36 crore?

(A) ICICI

(B) YES Bank

(C) ** AXIS Bank

(D) HDFC Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Axis Bank to acquire shares in CSC e-Governance for Rs 36 crore.

Concept note-2: -CSC - Common Service Centres, has been set up by the Ministry of Electronics & IT under the Companies Act, 1956 to oversee implementation of the CSC scheme.

Concept note-3: -It is a pan-India network catering to regional, geographic, linguistic and cultural diversity of the country, thus enabling the Government’s mandate of a socially, financially and digitally inclusive society.

Concept note-4: -GST Number of CSC E GOVERNANCE SERVICES INDIA LIMITED is 04AADCC8991Q1ZW in Chandigarh.
