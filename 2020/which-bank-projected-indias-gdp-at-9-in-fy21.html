Concept note-1: -The World Bank on Tuesday revised India’s 2022-23 GDP growth forecast upward to 6.9% from 6.5% estimated in October after factoring in “a strong outturn” in the second quarter of the current financial year.

Concept note-2: -GD GROWTH & OUTLOOK: Q2FY23 4.7% in 2021 to 8.8% in 2022 but will decline to 6.5% in 2023 and to 4.1% by 2024.
