Which bird is the official mascot for the Goa 2020 National Games?

(A) Crow

(B) Penguin

(C) ** Rubigula

(D) Crane

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which is the state bird of Goa, that was recently named as the mascot of the National Games, 2020? Notes: Rubigula, the Flame-throated Bulbul is the official state bird of Goa. The bird, also known as Ruby-throated yellow bulbul, has been named as the official mascot for the 2020 National Games.

Concept note-2: -’Rubigula’, Flame-Throated Bulbul, has been unveiled as the official mascot for the Goa 2020 National Games in Panaji, Goa on 1 Feb 2020. Goa will be hosting the 36th edition of the National Games from 20th October to 4th November 2020.

Concept note-3: -Remarking on the official mascot for the National Games 2022 is Savaj, the Asiatic lion, the Prime Minister said that the mascot reflects the mood of India’s youth, a passion for fearless entry into the sporting arena. This is also a symbol of rising India in a global scenario, he said.

Concept note-4: -Moscot: Jaya the blackbuck and Vijay the tiger are the mascots for Khelo India Youth Games.

Concept note-5: -Flame Throated Bulbul is the state bird of Goa.
