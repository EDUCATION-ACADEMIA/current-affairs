Which company has launched World’s 1st Satellite-based Narrowband-IoT Network?

(A) Vodafone-IDEA

(B) Bharti Airtel

(C) Reliance Jio

(D) ** BSNL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited Chairman & MD: Pravin Kumar Purwar.

Concept note-2: -BSNL has partnered with US-based Skylo to launch satellite-based internet of things (IoT) device service. The state-run telecom operator has launched the world’s first satellite-based narrowband IoT network. The satellite-based IoT network is first of its kind.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which Indian telecom company has rolled out world’s first satellite-based narrowband-IoT network? Notes: Indian state-owned telecommunications company BSNL in partnership with machine connectivity solutions company Skylotech India, to launch a satellite-based narrowband IoT network in India.

Concept note-4: -The concept of internet everywhere has been promoted as nextgen technology arriving in 2021. Advancements in narrowband Internet of Things (NB-IoT) and satellite NB-IoT are moving closer to that goal. Basic narrow-band connectivity allows for users in remote locations such as farmers to connect with the internet.

Concept note-5: -Skylo. Upgrades your existing hardware and provides ubiquitous satellite connectivity. No extra hardware - get affordable, reliable connectivity with a simple firmware upgrade, and never be out of coverage again.
