Which company has manufactured India’s first 100 Octane Petrol?

(A) BPCL

(B) ** IOCL

(C) Castrol India

(D) ONGC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IndianOil in Media Indian Oil Corporation (IOC) on Tuesday launched the nation’s first 100 Octane petrol, helping India join a select league of nations globally that have such superior quality fuel.

Concept note-2: -Notes: Indian Oil Corporation (IOC) has recently launched the nation’s first 100 Octane petrol. Union Oil Minister Dharmendra Pradhan launched the petrol. The 100-octane petrol also called as XP100 premium petrol will be initially available at select outlet of IOC in 10 cities.

Concept note-3: -poWer100, an ultra-premium branded petrol, marketed by HPCL has an octane rating of 100 and is currently one of the highest-octane rated petrol available in India. Higher the octane number, more is the ability of the fuel to withstand compression without detonating.

Concept note-4: -Indian Oil plans to roll-out XP100 premium grade petrol in 15 identified cities across the country in two phases. In the first phase, it has been made available at select ROs w.e.f December 1, 2020, in Delhi, Gurugram, Noida, Agra, Jaipur, Chandigarh, Ludhiana, Mumbai, Pune and Ahmadabad.
