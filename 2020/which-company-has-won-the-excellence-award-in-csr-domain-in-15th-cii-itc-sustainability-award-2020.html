Which company has won the Excellence Award in CSR Domain in 15th CII-ITC sustainability Award 2020?

(A) ** NTPC

(B) Maruti Suzuki India

(C) Hindustan Zinc

(D) RELIANCE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The award was presented to Hindustan Zinc at the 15th CII-ITC Sustainability Awards in a virtual ceremony held in the presence of Anurag Singh Thakur, Minister of State for Finance and Corporate Affairs.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is National Thermal Power Corporation Limited (NTPC). The country’s largest power producer, National Thermal Power Corporation Limited (NTPC) has been conferred “Excellence” in the prestigious CII-ITC Sustainability Awards 2020 in Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Domain.

Concept note-3: -These awards are the apex national level recognition by the Government of India. The CSR awards seek to infuse excellence in CSR interventions undertaken by corporates in pursuance of Section 135 of the Companies Act, 2013.

Concept note-4: -Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India has instituted National Corporate Responsibility (CSR) Awards to recognize companies that have made a positive impact on the society through their innovative & sustainable CSR initiatives. This award is the apex recognition of CSR initiatives in India.

Concept note-5: -NTPC received “Company with Best CSR Practice Award” in 8th Asia Business Responsibility Summit organized by Asian Centre for Corporate Governance and Sustainability on 26th Nov 2021.
