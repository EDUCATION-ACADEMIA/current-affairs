Which company launched Make Small Strong campaign?

(A) Microsoft

(B) ** Google India

(C) Facebook

(D) Twitter

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Start a conversation around supporting small businesses with the Make Small Strong Vote initiative. Just google ‘Make Small Strong Vote’, then choose from the options that help a small business grow and show your support on social media.

Concept note-2: -Notes: Google India has recently announced the launch of its nationwide campaign named ‘Make Small Strong’. It aims to help support small businesses and enhance demand through customer support.

Concept note-3: -Notes: Technology Major Facebook has recently announced that it has launched an offline to online small and medium businesses (SMB) guide. The company has also launched other new resources to help small and medium businesses.

Concept note-4: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which technology company has launched a platform for small enterprises in India, to enter into digital ecosystem? Notes: Google India announced a platform for small enterprises in India, to enter into digital ecosystem, named ‘Grow with Google Small Business hub’.
