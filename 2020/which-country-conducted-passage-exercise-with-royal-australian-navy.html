Which country conducted Passage Exercise with Royal Australian Navy?

(A) USA

(B) Russia

(C) ** India

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Navy concludes Maritime Partnership Exercise with the Australian Naval Ship. The Maritime Partnership Exercise (PASSEX ) between the Indian Navy and the Royal Australian Navy concluded on 3 November 2022 in Bay of Bengal.

Concept note-2: -In September, Indian naval warship INS Satpura and a P8 I Maritime Patrol Aircraft were in Darwin for participation in the multinational Exercise Kakadu, 2022.

Concept note-3: -Indian Navy’s indigenously designed and built guided missile destroyer, INS Kochi, exercised with Russian Federation Navy’s RFS Admiral Tributs on 14 January 2022 in the Arabian Sea.

Concept note-4: -The exercises included tactical manoeuvres, helicopter landings and amphibious operations, which signified the high degree of interoperability between IN and RAN. RAN ships HMAS Adelaide and HMAS Anzac visited Visakhapatnam from 30 Oct to 01 Nov 2022. This was part of Australia’s Indo-Pacific Endeavour 2022 (IPE 22).

Concept note-5: -SIMBEX-2022 is being conducted in two phases – Harbour Phase at Visakhapatnam from 26 to 27 October 2022 followed by the Sea Phase in Bay of Bengal from 28 to 30 October 2022.
