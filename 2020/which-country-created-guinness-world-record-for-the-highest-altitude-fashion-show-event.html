Which country created Guinness World Record for the highest altitude fashion show event?

(A) India

(B) China

(C) ** Nepal

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which country recently set a Guinness World Record for the highest altitude fashion show event ever held? Notes: Nepal recently organised the highest altitude fashion show event at 5340 metres height at Kala Patthar, near the Everest base camp.

Concept note-2: -Mount Everest Fashion Runway is a fashion show held at an altitude of 5340 m at Kala Patthar, near the Everest Base Camp in Nepal.

Concept note-3: -The most models in a fashion show is 430, and was achieved by You Night Events, Positive Image Modeling, and Chic Nouvelle Worldwide Model Management (all USA), in New Orleans, Louisiana, USA, on 1 October 2022.

Concept note-4: -The Guinness World Records has named Dor Bahadur Khapangi from Nepal as the shortest teenager living (male). Born November 14, 2004, Dor is 17 years old and has an average height of 73.43 cm (2 ft 4.9 in). The measurement took place on March 23, 2022, in Nepal’s Kathmandu.

Concept note-5: -35+ Nepalese In Guinness World Record[2021]
