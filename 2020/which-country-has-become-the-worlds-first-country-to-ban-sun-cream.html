Which country has become the world’s first country to ban sun cream?

(A) Marshall Islands

(B) Tuvalu

(C) Guam

(D) ** Palau

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Pacific nation of Palau has become the first country to ban sun cream that is harmful to corals and sea life.

Concept note-2: -Palau Sunscreen Ban Palau is the first country that banned sunscreen nationwide.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. Thailand has banned sunscreens containing chemicals that damage coral from all of its marine national parks. Lotions tourists use for sun protection are harming slow-growing corals.

Concept note-4: -Palau, a small island nation in the West Pacific Ocean imposed a ban on sun screens that contain chemicals affecting the coral reefs and other aquatic species. The step is also taken to conserve its Rock Islands Southern Lagoon, which is a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Concept note-5: -Why are sunblocks banned? Studies have shown that these chemicals are harmful to coral reefs. They have been shown to destroy coral larvae, obstruct the coral reproductive systems and be a significant cause of coral bleaching.
