Which country is the first country in the world to control desert locusts using drones?

(A) China

(B) USA

(C) Pakistan

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India is the first country to control locusts through drones.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Israel. Israel has become the first-ever country to allow drones in civilian airspace.

Concept note-3: -The UN body Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) has appreciated India to be the first country in the world that is using drone for locust control, it added.

Concept note-4: -In 2000, the Japanese company Yamaha introduced the world’s first agricultural drone, the R-50, which was designed for crop mapping and field analysis.

Concept note-5: -On March 16, 2022, Rajasthan Agriculture Minister Lalchand Kataria released the book ‘Locust Control in Rajasthan’ at Pant Krishi Bhawan.
