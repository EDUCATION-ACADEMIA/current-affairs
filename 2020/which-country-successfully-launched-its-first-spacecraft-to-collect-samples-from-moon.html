Which country successfully launched its first spacecraft to collect samples from moon?

(A) Japan

(B) India

(C) Russia

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -China Successfully Launches Its First Spacecraft To Moon To Collect Samples, Return To Earth.

Concept note-2: -’Unmanned Spacecraft’ - 77 News Result(s) Chang’e 5, the unmanned Chinese spacecraft carrying rocks and soil from the Moon, returned safely to Earth in the first mission in four decades to collect lunar samples, the Xinhua news agency said.

Concept note-3: -Apollo 11, U.S. spaceflight during which commander Neil Armstrong and lunar module pilot Edwin (“Buzz”) Aldrin, Jr., on July 20, 1969, became the first people to land on the Moon and walk the lunar surface.

Concept note-4: -As part of human exploration of the Moon, numerous space missions have been undertaken to study Earth’s natural satellite. Of the Moon landings, Luna 2 of the Soviet Union was the first spacecraft to reach its surface successfully, intentionally impacting the Moon on 13 September 1959.

Concept note-5: -China’s Chang’e-4 spacecraft successfully landed on the far side of the moon this morning Beijing time, accomplishing a worldwide first in lunar exploration.
