Which country successfully test-fired enhanced version of PINAKA rocket system?

(A) ** India

(B) China

(C) USA

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A new version of the Pinaka rocket system has been successfully flight-tested by the DRDO and the Indian Army at the Pokhran firing ranges, the defence ministry said on Saturday.

Concept note-2: -The Enhanced Pinaka Mk-I Rocket System (EPRS) was successfully test fired in the Pokhran range in Rajasthan.

Concept note-3: -Pinaka (from Sanskrit: , see Pinaka) is a multiple rocket launcher produced in India and developed by the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) for the Indian Army.

Concept note-4: -The Pinaka extended range rocket trials developed by the Defence Research and Development Organization (DRDO) were carried out in firing ranges of Pokhran, Rajasthan.

Concept note-5: -The Pinaka rocket system has been developed by Armament Research and Development Establishment, Pune supported by High Energy Materials Research Laboratory, another Pune-based laboratory of DRDO. The EPRS is the upgraded version of Pinaka variant which has been in service with the Indian Army for the last decade.
