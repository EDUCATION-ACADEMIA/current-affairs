Which country will be the guest of honour at the Paris Book Fair 2020?

(A) USA

(B) Japan

(C) ** India

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has been designated as the Guest of Honour at Paris Livre (Paris Book Fair ) in 2020.

Concept note-2: -After being cancelled for two years in a row owing to the Covid pandemic, the Paris Book Festival, France’s main literary event, was held in Paris – and welcomed India as its guest of honour country.

Concept note-3: -Visit the India Pavilion at the Grand Palais Ephémère, Paris, France from Thursday, April 21 to Sunday, April 24, 2022 from 10:00am to 8:00pm.

Concept note-4: -The 46th edition of Chennai Book Fair organised by the BookSellers and Publishers Association of South India (BAPASI) in association with the Tamil Nadu government is set to begin from January 6 at YMCA Grounds near Nandanam in Chennai.

Concept note-5: -The fair will remain open from 11 am to 8.30 pm every day. It is expected that many writers and publishers from other countries, who were not able to visit the fair in the last two years due to Covid-19 restrictions, will turn up in big numbers this year.
