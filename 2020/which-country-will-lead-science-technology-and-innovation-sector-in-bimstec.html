Which country will lead science, technology and innovation sector in BIMSTEC?

(A) India

(B) Myanmar

(C) ** Sri Lanka

(D) Thailand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -security, including energy (India); agriculture and. food security (Myanmar); people-to-people contacts (Nepal); science, technology and innovation (Sri Lanka), and.

Concept note-2: -The seven sectors are trade and investment, environment and climate change, disaster management and energy, agriculture and fishery, people-to-people contact, culture and tourism, science, technology and public health and connectivity.

Concept note-3: -Which country was the host of the BIMSTEC summit in 2022? Ans. In 2022, Sri Lanka served as the host country for the Bay of Bengal Initiative for Multi-Sectoral Technical and Economic Cooperation (BIMSTEC) Summit.

Concept note-4: -Notes: The Union Cabinet has approved a memorandum of association (MoA) by India for the establishment of BIMSTEC Technology Transfer Centre in Colombo.

Concept note-5: -BIMSTEC has a coordinating body called the BIMSTEC Working Group, which has a rotating chairman based on which member country chairs the organisation (the current Chair of BIMSTEC is Sri Lanka). Under this, meetings are to be held monthly at the Dhaka secretariat to review the progress of the regional grouping.
