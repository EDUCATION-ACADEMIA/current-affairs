Which edition of annual meeting of the board of governors of Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) held recently?

(A) 3rd

(B) 2nd

(C) ** 5th

(D) 4th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -26-28, 2021. The Board of Governors of the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) announced that its sixth Annual Meeting will be held in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (UAE) on Oct. 27-28, 2021.

Concept note-2: -Union Minister of Finance & Corporate Affairs Nirmala Sitharaman attended the 7th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) via video conference from New Delhi on 26 October 2022.

Concept note-3: -During the 2022 Annual Meeting on Oct. 26-27, 2022, AIIB’s Board of Governors approved the resolution confirming that the eighth Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors will be convened on September 25-26, in Sharm el-Sheikh, Egypt.

Concept note-4: -The Board of Governors of the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) announced that its eighth Annual Meeting will be held in Sharm el-Sheikh, Egypt, on Sept. 25-26, 2023.

Concept note-5: -The theme of the 2022 AIIB Annual Meeting is “Sustainable Infrastructure Toward a Connected World”, which aims to reaffirm infrastructure’s crucial role in supporting recovery, growth and connectivity.
