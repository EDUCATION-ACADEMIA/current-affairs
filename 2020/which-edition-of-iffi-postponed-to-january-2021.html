Which edition of IFFI postponed to January 2021?

(A) 49th

(B) 50th

(C) ** 51st

(D) 52nd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“The 51st Edition of the International Film Festival of India scheduled to be held at Goa from 20th November to 28th November, 2020 has been postponed to 16th to 24th January, 2021, ” the I&B Ministry said in a statement.

Concept note-2: -The 53rd International Film Festival of India was an event held from 20 - 28 November 2022 with Alma & Oskar by Dieter Berner as the Opening feature film, and Perfect Number by Krzysztof Zanussi as the Closing feature film.

Concept note-3: -The 52nd International Film Festival of India opened on 20 November 2021 with The King of All the World (El Rey de Todo El Mundo) by Carlos Saura in Goa. Like 51st edition this edition was also held in hybrid format, that combined online and face-to-face participation.

Concept note-4: -24 Feature Films and 20 Non-Feature Films To Be Screened During IFFI. International Film Festival of India announces the selection of films for the Indian Panorama section to be screened during its 52nd edition in Goa.

Concept note-5: -The Film Bazaar is a five-day event from Nov 20th to Nov 24, 2022, in the 53rd edition of the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) that is to be held in Goa on November 20-28. The bazaar will provide a platform for connecting all the selected film projects with the delegates from the industry.
