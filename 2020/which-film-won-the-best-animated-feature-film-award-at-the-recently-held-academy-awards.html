Which film won the Best Animated Feature Film Award at the recently held Academy Awards?

(A) ** Toy Story 4

(B) The Lion King

(C) Small Fry

(D) Cars 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nick Park won in 2006 for Wallace & Gromit: The Curse of the Were-Rabbit. George Miller won in 2007 for Happy Feet. Pete Docter holds the record for most wins in this category, winning three times for Up in 2010, Inside Out in 2016, and Soul in 2021. Lee Unkrich won in 2011 for Toy Story 3 and again in 2018 for Coco.

Concept note-2: -Disney’s ‘Encanto’ wins best animated feature at 2022 Oscars; Lin-Manuel Miranda misses out on EGOT. The team behind ‘Encanto’ accepts the Oscar for best animated feature film at the 94th Academy Awards on Sunday.

Concept note-3: -With more and more of these films achieving huge commercial success and recognition, the Academy finally added the award for Best Animated Feature in 2002 at the 74th ceremony. After years of Disney Animation dominating, it was DreamWorks who won the inaugural award, for “Shrek.”

Concept note-4: -The first film to win in this category was Flowers and Trees by Walt Disney, who has since held the category’s record for most nominations (39) and most wins (12). MGM’s Tom and Jerry (1940–67) is the category’s most lauded animated series over all, being nominated for a total of 13 Oscars and winning 7.
