Which Government has launched electric vehicles forum?

(A) Bihar

(B) Gujarat

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) ** Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Delhi Government felicitated leaders of the EV ecosystem through ‘Switch Delhi EV Awards’ while commemorating the 2-year implementation of the Delhi EV policy during the fourth Delhi EV Forum.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is NITI Aayog.

Concept note-3: -Top EV Stocks in India #1 – Mahindra Electric Mahindra is the pioneer of EV in the Indian space. Being the first major EV manufacturer it launched Mahindra Reva, its first EV as early as 2001. The Mahindra Reva was India’s first electric car.

Concept note-4: -Chandigarh aims to phase out 100% registration of fuel bikes in two years and 50% registration of fuel cars within five years to promote electric vehicles, as per its new EV policy.

Concept note-5: -The Electric Vehicles Initiative (EVI) is a multi-government policy forum dedicated to accelerating the introduction and adoption of electric vehicles worldwide. The IEA acts as Coordinator to support the EVI member governments in this activity.
