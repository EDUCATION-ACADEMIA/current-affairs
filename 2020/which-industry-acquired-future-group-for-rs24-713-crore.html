Which Industry acquired Future Group for Rs.24, 713 crore?

(A) TATA

(B) ** Reliance

(C) Mahindra

(D) Suzuki

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliance Retail has acquired future group’s retail, wholesale, warehouse and logistics businesses for Rs. 24, 713 crore.

Concept note-2: -Billionaire Mukesh Ambani’s Reliance Industries Ltd has taken over the operations of at least 200 stores of Future Retail and has offered jobs to its employees after the Kishore Biyani-led group failed to make lease payments to landlords, sources said on Saturday.

Concept note-3: -Since February, Reliance Retail has taken control of 947 stores of the Future group after the latter’s lease agreement expired. Reliance later rebranded some of the stores as “Smart Bazaar” – a grocery retail chain of the Mukesh Ambani-owned company.

Concept note-4: -Reliance became the main tenant under the store leases at some point and then sub-leased the stores to Future. Once Future missed rental payments, it was entitled to terminate the sub-leases. The takeover of stores in February, explains Avanti T.

Concept note-5: -Reliance took control of over 200 Big Bazaar stores, 34 Central stores and 78 Brand Factory outlets after Future Group defaulted on payments for renewing leases in April.
