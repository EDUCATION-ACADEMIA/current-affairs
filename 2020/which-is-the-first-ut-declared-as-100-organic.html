Which is the first UT declared as 100% Organic?

(A) Ladakh

(B) Jammu and Kashmir

(C) Delhi

(D) ** Lakshadweep

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution: The correct answer is Lakshadweep. Lakshadweep is the first Union Territory in India to be declared 100% organic by the Ministry of Agriculture in 2020.

Concept note-2: -The Lakshadweep is the Second Union Territory / State to be declared fully organic after Sikkim.

Concept note-3: -Sikkim had a resolution in 2003, to shift towards Organic Farming with an aim to stop usage of chemicals and pesticides in farming.

Concept note-4: -Lakshadweep is the correct answer.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sikkim (India). In 2016, Sikkim declared itself as fully organic, the first and only state in India. Sikkim’s transition to becoming a 100 percent organic state was not easy.
