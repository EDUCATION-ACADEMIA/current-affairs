Which milk diary became 1st Indian dairy company to enter Rabobank’s Global Top 20, 2020 list?

(A) Mother Diary

(B) ** Amul

(C) Gopalji

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Amul (Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation) has become the first Indian dairy company to enter Rabobank’s list of Global Top 20 Dairy Companies. Amul was ranked 16th with an annual turnover of $5.5 billion. “This is a matter of pride for 36 lakh milk producers of Gujarat, ” Amul said.

Concept note-2: -Amul was placed at 16th spot in 2020. Amul has achieved an annual turnover of $5.3 billion. French-based dairy company Lactalis has topped the list as the world’s largest dairy company with a turnover of US $23.0 billion.

Concept note-3: -Gujarat based dairy cooperative giant GCMMF which sells its products under the brand name of Amul, has secured the 2nd position in the world top 300 cooperatives.

Concept note-4: -Mother Dairy is a wholly owned subsidiary of the National Dairy Development Board which is a statutory body under the ownership of Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying of the Government of India that manufactures, markets and sells milk, dairy products, edible oil and fruits & vegetables.

Concept note-5: -Amul is also ranked as No 1 dairy brand, ahead of the leading food and dairy brands in the Asia-Pacific region like Kraft, Dutch Lady, Dumex, Walls, Anchor, Magnolia and Everyday, an Amul release said here.
