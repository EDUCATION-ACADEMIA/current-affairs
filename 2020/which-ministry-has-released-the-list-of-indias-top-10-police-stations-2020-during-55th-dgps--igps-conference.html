Which ministry has released the list of India’s top 10 police stations 2020 during 55th DGPs & IGPs conference?

(A) ** Ministry of Home Affairs

(B) Ministry of Prime Minister’s Office

(C) Ministry of External Affairs

(D) Ministry of Defence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Delhi’s Sadar Bazar police station was announced as the best police station across the country this year by the Ministry of Home Affairs.

Concept note-2: -Union Home Minister Amit Shah awarded the Aska Police Station of Ganjam, district of Odisha as the number one police station in the country. Aska Police station was awarded in the annual ranking of Police station for 2022.

Concept note-3: -Berhampur: Union home minister Amit Shah on Friday awarded Aska police station in Ganjam district as the best police station in the country for 2022. Prasant Kumar Sahoo, inspector in-charge, Aska police station, received the trophy from Shah at the annual conference of DGPs-IGPs in New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -BHUBANESWAR: Union home minister Amit Shah Friday awarded Aska police station in Odisha’s Ganjam district as the number one police station in the country in the annual ranking of police stations for 2022, an official release said.
