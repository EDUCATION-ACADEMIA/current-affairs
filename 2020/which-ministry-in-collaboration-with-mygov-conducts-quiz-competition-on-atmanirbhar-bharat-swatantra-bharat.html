Which ministry in collaboration with MyGov conducts quiz competition on ‘Atmanirbhar Bharat-Swatantra Bharat’?

(A) Health and family welfare ministry

(B) ** Defence ministry

(C) Chemicals and Fertilizers ministry

(D) Skill Development and Entrepreneurship ministry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As a part of Independence Day Celebrations 2020, an online quiz competition on “Aatmanirbhar Bharat – Swatantra Bharat” will be conducted by Ministry of Defence in coordination with MyGov during 29th July – 10th August 2020 in order to create patriotic feeling among youth and masses.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Defence with MyGov congratulate and thank all the participants for their contribution and encourages them to continue taking part in such future activities.

Concept note-3: -Quiz Whizz is a virtual contest that will take place entirely online.

Concept note-4: -Register on mygov.in to participate. You will be asked for personal details such as name, email id etc. You will also be required to mention the kind of skills you have & the issues on which you may like to provide inputs.

Concept note-5: -quiz, a contest in which participants test what they know by answering questions on one or more topics.
