Which ministry introduced Foreign Contribution(Regulation) Amendment Act, 2020 in parliament that has come into force with effect from Sept 29, 2020?

(A) ** Ministry of Home Affairs

(B) Ministry of Corporate Affair

(C) Ministry of Finance

(D) Ministry of Health and Family Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Foreign Contribution Regulation Act (FCRA) | Ministry of Home Affairs.

Concept note-2: -This was stated by the Minister of State for Home Affairs, Shri Nityanand Rai in a written reply to a question in the Lok Sabha today. The Central Government has notified the Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Amendment Rules, 2022 vide GSR No.

Concept note-3: -Foreign Contribution Regulation Act (FCRA) | Ministry of Home Affairs.

Concept note-4: -The Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act was amended in 2020 to discourage expenditure by non- government organisations (NGOs) on “unproductive items” such as “inflated staff salaries, posh buildings and office and luxurious vehicles, ” the Ministry of Home Affairs has said in its 2021-22 annual report.
