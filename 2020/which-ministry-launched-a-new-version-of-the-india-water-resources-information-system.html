Which Ministry launched a New Version of the India Water Resources Information System?

(A) ** Ministry of Jal Shakti

(B) Ministry of Neer

(C) Ministry of water supply

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Jal Shakti has launched a new version of the India Water Resources Information System (India-WRIS) with new functionalities and features.

Concept note-2: -The Union Minister of Jal Shakti, Shri Gajendra Singh Shekhawat today released the Dynamic Ground Water Resource Assessment Report for the entire country for the year 2022.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of Jal Shakti, a new ministry under the Government of India was formed in May 2019. It was formed by merging two existing ministries - The Ministry of Water Resources, River Development & Ganga Rejuvenation and The Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation.

Concept note-4: -’water power/strength/capabilities’) is a ministry under Government of India which was formed in May 2019 under the second Modi ministry. This was formed by merging of two ministries; Ministry of Water Resources, River Development & Ganga Rejuvenation and Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation.
