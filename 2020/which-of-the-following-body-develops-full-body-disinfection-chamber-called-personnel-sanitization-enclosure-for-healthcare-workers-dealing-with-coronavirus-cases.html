Which of the following body develops full-body disinfection chamber called ‘Personnel Sanitization Enclosure’ for healthcare workers dealing with coronavirus cases?

(A) BARC

(B) ** DRDO’s VRDE

(C) ICMR

(D) ISRO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sanitizing – removes bacteria from surfaces. Disinfecting – kills harmful bacteria and viruses from surfaces. Sterilizing – kills all microorganisms from surfaces.

Concept note-2: -Disinfection may be defined as the process of destruction or inactivation of harmful micro-organism in water either by physical process or chemical process. Physical process includes heating, ultraviolet radiation etc. and chemical process includes addition of disinfectants such as chemicals.
