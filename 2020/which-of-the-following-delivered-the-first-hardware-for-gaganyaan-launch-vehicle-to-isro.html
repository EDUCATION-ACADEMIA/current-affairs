Which of the following delivered the first hardware for Gaganyaan Launch Vehicle to ISRO?

(A) International Aerospace Manufacturing

(B) Hindustan Aeronautics Limited

(C) ** Larsen & Toubro

(D) Godrej Aerospace

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Larsen & Toubro Ltd. Larsen & Toubro Ltd. (L&T) has delivered the first hardware, a booster segment, for Gaganyaan Launch Vehicle to ISRO.

Concept note-2: -Gaganyaan project envisages demonstration of human spaceflight capability by launching crew of 3 members to an orbit of 400 km for a 3 days mission and bring them back safely to earth, by landing in Indian sea waters.

Concept note-3: -According to Union Minister of State for Space Jitendra Singh, India’s first human space flight mission under the ‘Gaganyaan program’ is scheduled to launch in the fourth quarter of 2024. (21 December). Gaganyaan, India’s first human space mission, is set to launch in 2024.

Concept note-4: -During the interaction, he announced that ‘Gaganyaan’ is all set to be launched in 2024. While speaking to the media, the Minister highlighted India’s achievements in the field of Science and Technology and that’s why India’s first self-sufficient Gaganyaan will enter space in 2024.

Concept note-5: -ISRO chairman S. Somanath announced on 30 June 2022 that the first crewed mission will not happen until at least 2024 because of safety concerns.
