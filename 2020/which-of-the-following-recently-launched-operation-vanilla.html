Which of the following recently launched Operation Vanilla?

(A) Indian Army

(B) ** Indian Navy

(C) Indian Air Force

(D) Naval Guard

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Disaster Management. The Indian Navy has launched ‘Operation Vanilla’ to assist the population of Madagascar affected by Cyclone Diane.

Concept note-2: -What is Operation Vanilla? Operation Vanilla was an operation undertaken by the Indian government to help Madagascar, which was badly hit by a cyclone. The Indian government sent INS Airavat carrying resources to help.

Concept note-3: -In response to Madagascar’s national disaster, the Indian Navy launched ‘Operation Vanilla’ on January 28, 2020. This resulted in diverting a major amphibious ship to assist the affected people.

Concept note-4: -Operation Vanilla: Indian Navy on Tuesday launched Operation Vanilla in its effort to support the people of flood-hit Madagascar. Indian Navy said, ‘As the first respondent, Indian Navy Ship Airavat, which was en-route to Seychelles has been diverted to Madagascar for rendering assistance.
