Which of the following recently recruited female officers for the first time in its Helicopter stream?

(A) Indian Army

(B) Indian Air Force

(C) ** Indian Navy

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is the Indian Navy. For the first time, two women officers have been selected to join as ‘Observers” (Airborne Tacticians) in the helicopter stream in the Indian Navy.

Concept note-2: -All those who want to know how to join the Indian Army after 12th for females must know that three academies are; Indian Military Academy, Indian Naval Academy, Indian Air Force Academy, and Indian Naval Academy. Women are eligible for officers training academy only.

Concept note-3: -Since 1992, women were first inducted in to various branches of Indian Army only in short service commission. In 2008, women were first inducted as permanent commissioned officers in Legal and Education corps, in 2020 they were first inducted in 8 more corps as permanent commissioned officers.

Concept note-4: -SSCW (JAG) This is a very appropriate job for women, along with other prestigious Government Jobs. This scheme is specifically for law graduates, and every year, the Indian Army selects eight female candidates via the JAG entrance process.

Concept note-5: -The Indian Armed Forces comprise of three divisions – Indian Army, Indian Navy, and the Indian Air Force.
