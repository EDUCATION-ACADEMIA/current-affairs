Which of the following won Deloitte Football Money League in 2018-19?

(A) Real Madrid

(B) ** Barcelona

(C) Juventus

(D) Chelsea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Manchester United retained top spot for a second consecutive year, generating total revenue of €676m (£581m).

Concept note-2: -Manchester City remain top of the list, compiled by the accountancy firm Deloitte, with revenues of £619m from the 2021-22 season – just ahead of Real Madrid (£604m) in second.

Concept note-3: -Leading soccer clubs worldwide 2021-2022, by revenue In the 2021/22 season, Manchester City had the highest revenue out of any soccer club worldwide, generating around 731 million euros. Real Madrid placed second, with a total revenue of 713.8 million euros.

Concept note-4: -The table was led by Manchester City - the Premier League champions at the end of the 2021/22 season - with £619.1m in total revenue followed by Real Madrid on £604.5m.

Concept note-5: -Champions Manchester City retain top spot, making 731m euros (£619.1m), ahead of Real Madrid (713.8m euros).
