Which of the followings has been renamed as Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Port?

(A) Visakhapatnam Port

(B) ** Kolkata Port Trust

(C) New Mangalore Port

(D) Mumbai Port Trust

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Kolkata Port . Kolkata Port is renamed Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Port.

Concept note-2: -SYAMA PRASAD MOOKERJEE PORT, KOLKATA.

Concept note-3: -Haldia Port, also known as the Haldia Dock Complex (HDC), is a major seaport located along the Hooglhy River in West Bengal, India. The port is the larger of two dock facilities operated by the Kolkata Port Trust, the smaller being the Kolkata Dock System, located about 100km upstream.

Concept note-4: -1.1 The Port has two Docks viz. Kidderpore Dock (KPD) and Netaji Subhas Dock (NSD).

Concept note-5: -Haldia port or Haldia Dock Complex has been built at the meeting place of the Haldi River and Hooghly river. Kolkata Port Trust has been created in this port as the port’s partner. So it is not a port. It is an official dock complex.
