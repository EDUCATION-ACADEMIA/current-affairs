Which one portal has been started to support foreign Tourist in India?

(A) Stand in India

(B) ** Stranded in India

(C) Projected in India

(D) Diversified in India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Titled “Stranded in India” portal aims to act as a support network for foreign tourists stuck in various parts of the country.

Concept note-2: -The development of the Indian tourism industry began in the 19th century when the Sir John Sargent Committee was established in 1945 to promote and develop Indian tourism.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The main reason why India is famous for tourism means all type of tourist destinations in one place. India is the right place for wild safaris, desert safaris, nature seeking places, honeymoon spot, adventurous activities, spiritual places, and historical places of India.

Concept note-4: -Maneka Gandhi, the union minister of Women and Child Development (WCD), inaugurated an online portal NARI in New Delhi for the empowerment of women.

Concept note-5: -"Swadesh’ and Prasad’ schemes were launched by the Ministry of Tourism of the government of India to encourage visits to 95 pilgrimage and spiritual centres in India.
