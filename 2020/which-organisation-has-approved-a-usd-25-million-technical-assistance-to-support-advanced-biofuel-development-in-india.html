Which organisation has approved a USD 2.5 million technical assistance to support advanced BioFuel development in India?

(A) World Bank

(B) Bank Central Asia

(C) DBS

(D) ** Asian Development Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Manila-based multilateral lending agency Asian Development Bank (ADB) has approved a USD 2.5 million (about Rs 18 crore) technical assistance to support advanced biofuel development in India.

Concept note-3: -The Aemetis Kakinada plant is one of the largest biodiesel production facilities in India and is capable of processing a variety of vegetable oils, animal oils, and waste oil feedstocks into biodiesel that meet international product standards.
