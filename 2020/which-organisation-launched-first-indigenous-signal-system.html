Which organisation launched first indigenous signal system?

(A) Indian Railways

(B) ** Delhi Metro

(C) Transport Ministry

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) on Tuesday took a major step towards development of an indigenously-built communication-based train control with the launch of i-ATS - the first Made-in-India signalling system.

Concept note-2: -Delhi Metro on December 24 last year had entered into the 20th year of its operations and marked the occasion by commencing a field trial of the i-ATS technology. ATS is a computer-based system, which manages train operations.

Concept note-3: -The Indigenous Automatic Train Supervision System (i-ATS) is a first in India and has been developed by the combined efforts of DMRC and Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL).

Concept note-4: -The earliest form of railroad signal was simply a flag by day or a lamp at night. The first movable signal was a revolving board, introduced in the 1830s, followed in 1841 by the semaphore signal.
