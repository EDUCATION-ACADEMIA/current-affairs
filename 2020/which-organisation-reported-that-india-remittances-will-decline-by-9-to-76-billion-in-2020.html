Which organisation reported that India remittances will decline by 9% to $76 billion in 2020?

(A) IMF

(B) WHO

(C) ** World Bank

(D) RBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Third, notwithstanding sharp decline in remittances to Maharashtra in 2020- 21, it has emerged as the top recipient state along with Delhi apart from the traditional states of Kerala and Tamil Nadu which have witnessed gradual decline in recent years.

Concept note-2: -GCC’s price support policies kept inflation low in 2022, and higher oil prices increased demand for labor, enabling Indian migrants to increase remittances and counter the impact of India’s record-high inflation on the real incomes of their families.

Concept note-3: -Data from the RBI Remittances Survey, 2021 shows that the four leading countries in sending remittances to India are the US, the United Arab Emirates (UAE), the UK and Singapore. Together, the four account for as much as 54 percent of India’s remittance inflows.
