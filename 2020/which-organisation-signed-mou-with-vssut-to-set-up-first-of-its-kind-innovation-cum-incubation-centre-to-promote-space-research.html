Which organisation signed MoU with VSSUT to Set up first of its kind innovation cum incubation centre to promote space research?

(A) DRDO

(B) ** ISRO

(C) Government of India

(D) NASA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) signed an MoU with Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology (VSSUT) Burla, Odisha.

Concept note-2: -Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) and Microsoft signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to help Indian Space tech start-ups with technology tools, go-to-market support, and mentoring to help them scale and become business ready.

Concept note-3: -National Institute of Technology Rourkela, is the Space Technology Incubation center serving the eastern region. The Indian Space Research Organisation signed MoU with the National Institute of technology of Rourkela and tied up with it to establish the Space Technology Incubation Center in March 18 2021.
