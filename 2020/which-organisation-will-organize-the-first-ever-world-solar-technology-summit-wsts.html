Which organisation will organize the first-ever World Solar Technology Summit (WSTS)?

(A) ** ISA

(B) WSTS

(C) ISRO

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why in News. Recently, the first-ever World Solar Technology Summit (WSTS) was organised by the International Solar Alliance (ISA) and Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI).

Concept note-2: -The first summit of the International Solar Alliance(ISA) was held in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -It is an Indian initiative, which was launched jointly by Prime Minister of India Mr. Narendra Modi and H.E. François Hollande, President of France on 30 November 2015 in Paris, France on the side-lines of the 21st Conference of Parties (CoP 21) to the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change.

Concept note-4: -The International Solar Alliance (ISA) plans to launch the World Solar Bank (WSB) at the United Nations Climate Change Conference in Glasgow in November 2021. The ISA is the first treaty-based international government organization headquartered in India.

Concept note-5: -International Solar Alliance (ISA): Importance, Objectives & Initiatives | ISA UPSC. The International Solar Alliance is a collaborative platform for increased deployment of solar energy technology that serves as the vehicle to assess energy, ensure energy security, and facilitate the energy transition for its members.
