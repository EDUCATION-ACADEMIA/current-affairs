Which portal has been launched by the Union Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME)to assist Indian MSMEs to march into big league as National and Global Champions?

(A) STRENGTH

(B) ** CHAMPIONS

(C) POWER

(D) TORCHBEARER

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) has launched CHAMPIONS - Creation and Harmonious Application of Modern Processes for Increasing the Output and National Strength portal.

Concept note-2: -RAMP Portal launched; to scale up MSME coverage.

Concept note-3: -MSME-Champions is a technology-driven centralized control room, grievance management, and information platform launched by the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) under Government of India. The Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi launched the Champions portal on 9 May 2020.

Concept note-4: -MSME Ministry introduces AI & ML for strengthening its single window system portal ‘Champions’. MSME Ministry introduces Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) strengthening its Single Window System Portal ‘Champions’ to assist MSMEs of the country.

Concept note-5: -The Government of India’s Ministry of Micro Small and Medium Enterprises has introduced the CHAMPIONS portal, www.Champions.gov.in, a technology driven control room cum management information system, as part of a significant effort.
