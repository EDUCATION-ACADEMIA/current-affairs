Which State Assembly has approved a bill on caste validity certificate?

(A) ** Maharastra

(B) Jharkhand

(C) Bihar

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maharashtra Assembly gives nod to bill on caste validity certificate.

Concept note-2: -OBC certificate has to be renewed each year. You can renew the certificate by applying for it at the district office of one’s city or districts.

Concept note-3: -No, you cannot claim benefits in Maharashtra based on caste certificate in Karnataka. Also, you cannot convert Caste certificate in Karnataka to Maharashtra, as caste certificate in Maharashtra is only issued to residents of Maharashtra.

Concept note-4: -Purpose of a Caste validity certificate It provides a reservation quota for government jobs and their application process. The caste validity certificate can be used by the students who belong to the SC/ST for educational purposes like applying for scholarships and admission to schools and colleges.
