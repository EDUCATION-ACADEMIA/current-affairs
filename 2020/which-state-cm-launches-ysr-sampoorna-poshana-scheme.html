Which state CM launches ‘YSR Sampoorna Poshana’ scheme?

(A) Karnataka

(B) Telangana

(C) ** Andhra Pradesh

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chief Minister YS Jagan Mohan Reddy has on Monday launched YSR Sampoorna Poshana and YSR Sampoorna Poshana Plus schemes to provide nutritious food to pregnant women, lactating mothers and children.

Concept note-2: -YSR Sampoorna Poshana was launched by Chief minister of Andhra Pradesh Y. S.

Concept note-3: -Benefits Of YSR Sampoorna Poshana Plus Scheme In this plan, the AP government will spend Rs. 850 every month for pregnant ladies and new mothers’ eating regimens in plain territories, Rs. 350 for newborn children, and Rs. 412 for youngsters.

Concept note-4: -YSR Sampoorna Poshana will cover the urban and rural areas lying in plain lands while YSR Sampoorna Poshana Plus is intended to supply nutritious food exclusively in 77 Tribal areas covering 8320 Anganwadi Centres. Around 30.16 lakh women/mothers and children will benefit from these two schemes.

Concept note-5: -Y.S. Jagan Mohan Reddy Gaaru, Participating in KIA Grand Opening Ceremony.
