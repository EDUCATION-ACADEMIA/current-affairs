Which state government has come up with YSR Cheyutha Scheme 2020 to create own employment opportunity for women?

(A) Orissa

(B) ** Andhra Pradesh

(C) Kerala

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -YSR Cheyutha is a program launched by the Government of Andhra Pradesh to assist women aged 45 to 60 from weaker socio-economic background by providing financial benefit of ₹75000 over the period of four years.

Concept note-2: -On Wednesday, August 12th, Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister YS Jagan Mohan Reddy will inaugurate the YSR Cheyutha Scheme. This initiative would offer ₹75000 in cash assistance to women over the age of four who are between the ages of 45 and 60.

Concept note-3: -Through the YSR Cheyutha scheme, 23.14 lakh women belonging to 45 to 60 years of age will get the benefit for 4 years. For the implementation of this scheme special arrangements will also be made at Rythu Bharosa Kendra in every village so that each and every beneficiary of the state can get the benefit of this scheme.

Concept note-4: -This scheme aims at equipping women of SC/ST/OBC/minority castes. The Financial benefit of Rs. 75000 is to be provided over a period of four years to those women who come under 45 to 60 years of age.
