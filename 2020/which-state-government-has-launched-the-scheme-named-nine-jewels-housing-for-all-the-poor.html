Which state government has launched the scheme named ‘Nine jewels-housing for all the poor’?

(A) ** Andhra Pradesh

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Goa

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -YSR EWS Housing scheme is a state government initiative for Economically weaker section category Citizens of Andhra Pradesh. State Government is going to provide pucca house to all the beneficiaries and for this purpose, Rs. 1280 crore has allocated under the state Budget.

Concept note-2: -Navaratnalu are a set of nine welfare schemes introduced by Y. S. Jagan Mohan Reddy, the Chief minister of Andhra Pradesh, as promised during Praja Sankalpa Yatra (political campaign tour on foot) before 2019 assembly elections.

Concept note-3: -Cherukuvada Sri Ranganadha Raju is the Minister of Housing in Y. S. Jagan Mohan Reddy’s government of Andhra Pradesh, India. He was elected to the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly from Achanta constituency in the 2019 assembly election.
