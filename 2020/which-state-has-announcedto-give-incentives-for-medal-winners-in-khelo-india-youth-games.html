Which state has announcedto give incentives for medal winners in Khelo India Youth Games?

(A) Haryana

(B) ** Madhya Pradesh

(C) Assam

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hockey Madhya Pradesh clinches Khelo India Youth Games 2022 Men’s U-18 title. In Hockey, Madhya Pradesh defeated Odisha 6-5 to clinch Khelo India Youth Games 2022 Men’s Under-18 Qualifiers title in Bhubaneswar yesterday, December 30.

Concept note-2: -Maharashtra topped the medal tally with 83 medals including 28 gold, 30 silver and 25 bronze medals. Haryana and Madhya Pradesh are at the second and third positions with 23-23 gold medals respectively. Rajasthan is currently in fourth and Odisha is in fifth position.

Concept note-3: -KIYG 2023 is being held at 11 venues across eight cities in Madhya Pradesh. Over 5, 000 athletes will compete in 27 sports for 1, 936 medals. Watch live. The Khelo India Youth Games 2023 will be held in Madhya Pradesh from January 30 to February 11, 2023.

Concept note-4: -The 4th Khelo India Youth Games are scheduled to be held at Dr. Karni Singh Shooting Ranges, New Delhi from 3rd to 9th February 2022.
