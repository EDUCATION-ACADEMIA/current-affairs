Which state Observed its Patriots’ day 2020 on August 13?

(A) Sikkim

(B) Maharashtra

(C) ** Manipur

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In Manipur, the Patriots’ Day was observed on 13 August 2020 commemorating the 1891 Anglo-Manipuri war. During the war, many Manipuris sacrificed their lives to safeguard the independence of the State.

Concept note-2: -Patriots’ Day is observed every year on August 13 in memory of brave patriots, who laid down their lives in the Anglo-Manipuri War of 1891.

Concept note-3: -National Prosecco Day, National Filet Mignon Day and National Left Handers Day are all celebrated on August 13th.

Concept note-4: -When is Patriot’s Day in 2023? Patriot’s Day in 2023 is on the Sunday, 13th of Aug (8/13/2023).

Concept note-5: -August 13 is considered a red-letter day in the history of Manipur as on this fateful day in 1891 the prince of Manipur Bir Tikendrajit and General Thangal were hanged at Pheidapung in Imphal in broad daylight by the British before thousands of Manipuris for revolting against them.
