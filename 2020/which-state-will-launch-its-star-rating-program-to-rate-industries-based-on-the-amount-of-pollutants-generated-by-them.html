Which state will launch its “Star Rating Program” to rate industries based on the amount of pollutants generated by them?

(A) ** Jharkhand

(B) Bihar

(C) Kerala

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Star Rating Program-a first-of-its-kind initiative in India to rate large industrial units based on their emissions-has come a long way since it was launched in Maharashtra in June 2017.

Concept note-2: -Along with informing residents and industries, the program aims to strengthen the Odisha State Pollution Control Board’s (SPCB Odisha) regulatory efforts to reduce pollution.

Concept note-3: -The star rating program uses the CEMS data to display information about industrial emissions in the public domain-the most polluting industries are given a 1-star rating, and the best performing industries are given a 5-Star rating.

Concept note-4: -The star rating index is an easy and accessible way to inform residents of Maharashtra about the industry emissions in their state. “MPCB’s program is path-breaking in providing the public with critical information and rigorously testing its impact on pollution emissions.
