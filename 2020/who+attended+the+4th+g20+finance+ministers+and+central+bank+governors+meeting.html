Who attended the 4th G20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors meeting?

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) Smriti Irani

(C) Nitin Gadkari

(D) ** Nirmala Sitharaman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 19 countries are Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, Germany, France, India, Indonesia, Italy, Japan, Mexico, the Russian Federation, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, South Korea, Turkey, the UK, and the US.

Concept note-2: -In this 4th meeting, Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors of G20, Minister Sri Mulyani Indrawati, and Governor Perry Warjiyo co-chaired the sessions.

Concept note-3: -The G20 has issued a meeting summary of the Fourth G20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors meeting held in Washington DC on 12–13 October 2022.

Concept note-4: -The First G20 Finance and Central Bank Deputies Meeting, under the Indian Presidency, was held in Bengaluru, Karnataka, during December 13-14, 2022, under the co-chairship of Secretary, Economic Affairs, Shri Ajay Seth and Deputy Governor, Reserve Bank of India, Dr. Michael Patra.

Concept note-5: -The G20 Finance Track, led by Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors of G20 countries, focuses on economic and financial issues. It provides an effective forum for global economic discourse and policy coordination.
