Who became first bowler to scalp 500 wickets in T20I?

(A) Jasprit Bumrah

(B) James Anderson

(C) Sunil Narine

(D) ** Dwayne Bravo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dwayne Bravo becomes 1st bowler to scalp 500 wickets in T20 cricket.

Concept note-2: -Walsh became the first bowler to reach 500 wickets in the history of Test cricket. He achieved the feat against South Africa in 2001, getting Jacques Kallis leg before wicket in Port-of-Spain, Trinidad.

Concept note-3: -As of January 2023, the leading wicket-taker of all time in international Twenty20s was New Zealand’s Tim Southee. The right-arm medium-fast bowler has taken 134 wickets in 107 matches between 2008 and 2022.

Concept note-4: -Yuzvendra Chahal Over the years, he has been an important member of the Indian T20 side with his accurate witty leg-spin bowling. For India, Chahal has taken 91 wickets in 75 matches at a decent average of 24.68. which makes him the player with the most T20 wickets for India.
