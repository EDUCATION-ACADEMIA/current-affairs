Who has become No.1 all-rounder in latest ICC test ranking?

(A) Jason Holder

(B) Ravindra Jadeja

(C) ** Ben Stokes

(D) Pat Cummins

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2022, Shakib Al Hasan is the best all rounder in the world in ODI cricket. He has 372 rating points in the ODI all rounder rankings.

Concept note-2: -England Test captain Ben Stokes was rewarded for the dramatic turnaround in his team’s fortunes as the all-rounder was named Test player of 2022 by the International Cricket Council. Stokes’ record as an all-rounder last year was impressive, scoring 870 runs and taking 26 wickets from 15 matches.

Concept note-3: -As per the latest Test rankings released by ICC on 18 Jan 2023, Australia with 126 rating is placed at the top of the points table. India with 115 rating is on the second spot. England with 107 rating occupies the third place.

Concept note-4: -Who is the number 1 all-rounder in Test cricket? With 385 points, India’s Ravindra Jadeja is the number 1 all-rounder in the ICC Test cricket rankings.
