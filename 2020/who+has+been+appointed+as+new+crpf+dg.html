Who has been appointed as new CRPF DG?

(A) S S Deswal

(B) Krishna Kumar

(C) ** Anand Prakash Maheshwari

(D) Om Singh Tiwari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union government has announced to give additional charge as Border Security Force (BSF) DG to senior Madhya Pradesh cadre IPS officer and CRPF Director General (DG) Sujoy Lal Thaosen a day ahead of retirement of incumbent Pankaj Kumar Singh on December 31.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi (16.03. 21): Senior IPS officer Kuldiep Singh (IPS:1986:WB) was on Tuesday appointed as the new Director-General of Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) for a period ending 30.09. 2022 i.e. date of his superannuation. He replaces Dr AP Maheshwari (IPS:1984:UP) who retired last month.

Concept note-3: -CRPF Director General (DG) Sujoy Lal Thaosen has been given the additional charge of the post of the Border Security Force (BSF) DG following the retirement of incumbent Pankaj Kumar Singh on December 31.
