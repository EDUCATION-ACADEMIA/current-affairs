Who has been awarded with bharati award 2020?

(A) Prem Gopal

(B) Jaganmohan Reddy

(C) ** Seeni Viswanathan

(D) Yash Chaudhari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Subramanyam Bharti Award (Devanagari: ) is a literary honour in India which Kendriya Hindi Sansthan, (Central Hindi Organization), Ministry of Human Resource Development, annually confers on writers of outstanding works in Hindi literature.

Concept note-2: -The Uttar Pradesh Government has instituted several awards in different fields such as Vishwa Bharti Award, Maharishi Valmiki Award, Maharishi Vyas Award, Maharishi Narada Award, Ved Pandit Award, Vishesh Award, Vividh Award and Namit Award.

Concept note-3: -Yash Bharati Award is the highest civilian award of the Government of Uttar Pradesh.
