Who has been named EY World Entrepreneur of the year 2020?

(A) Mallika Srinivasan

(B) Rahul Yadav

(C) Venugopal Dhoot

(D) ** Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw, Executive Chairperson of India-based Biocon Limited, was this evening named EY World Entrepreneur Of The Year™ 2020 at a ground-breaking, virtual award ceremony. Kiran was picked from among 46 award winners from 41 countries and territories vying for the world title.

Concept note-2: -Ernst & Young LLP (EY US) today announced that Holly Thaggard, Founder, and Amanda Baldwin, CEO, of Supergoop! have been named the Entrepreneur Of The Year® 2022 National Overall Award winners.

Concept note-3: -Watch the stellar stories of EOY India 2021 Winners on This webisode features the entrepreneurial journeys of Falguni Nayar, Founder & CEO, FSN E-Commerce Ventures (Nykaa) and AM Naik, Group Chairman, Larsen & Toubro (L&T).

Concept note-4: -EY World Entrepreneur Of The YearTM celebrates the vision, insight and determination of entrepreneurs from around the globe. The winner of this ultimate global competition for entrepreneurs is chosen by a diverse panel of independent judges.
