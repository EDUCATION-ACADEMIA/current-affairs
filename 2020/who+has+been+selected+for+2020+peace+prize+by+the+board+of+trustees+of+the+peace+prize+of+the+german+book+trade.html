Who has been selected for 2020 Peace Prize by the Board of Trustees of the Peace Prize of the German Book Trade?

(A) Jagdish Bhagwati

(B) ** Amartya Kumar Sen

(C) Avinash Dixit

(D) Surjit Bhalla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Peace Prize of the German Book Trade is considered one of the world’s most prestigious literary awards. The 2020 Peace Prize will be awarded to Amartya Sen, the Indian economist, philosopher and Nobel laureate.

Concept note-2: -Max Tau was forced to flee Germany in 1938. But in his Norwegian exile he stands up for reconciliation and peace, also with the idea to found a peace library. He was the first to receive the Peace Prize in 1950.

Concept note-3: -Friedenpreis is an international peace prize awarded annually by the German Publishers and Booksellers Association, which runs the Frankfurt Book Fair. The prize has been awarded since 1950. The recipient is remunerated with €25, 000.

Concept note-4: -The German Book Prize is worth a total of 37, 500 euros. The winner receives 25, 000 euros, the other five shortlisted authors receive 2, 500 euros each.
