Who has inaugurated Unique “Urban Forest” at the Office of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India(CAG) in New Delhi?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) ** Prakash Javadekar

(C) Nirmala Sitharaman

(D) Nitin Gadkari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Urban forest - Inaugurated by Shri Prakash Javadekar, Minister of Environment and Climate | Comptroller and Auditor General of India.

Concept note-2: -For applying to CAG Recruitment 2022, candidates must have a relevant Bachelor’s Degree from a recognised institute or university, as well as fluency in the language of the state from which they are applying.

Concept note-3: -CAG of India, Sh. Girish Chandra Murmu (centre) along with the Heads of delegation from Niger, Philippines, Brazil, Egypt, Qatar and Kenya (left to right) at the 14th KSC SC Meeting held in Cairo, Egypt from 12th to 14th September, 2022.

Concept note-4: -An urban forest encompasses the trees and shrubs in an urban area, 1 including trees in yards, along streets and utility corridors, in protected areas, and in watersheds. This includes individual trees, street trees, green spaces with trees, and even the associated vegetation 2 and the soil beneath the trees.
