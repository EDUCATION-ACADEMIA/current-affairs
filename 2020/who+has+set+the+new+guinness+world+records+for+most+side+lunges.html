Who has set the new Guinness World Records for most side lunges?

(A) ** Sai Deepak

(B) Devesh Choudhary

(C) Arjun Miglani

(D) Irfan Mehsood

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. B Sai Deepak set a Guinness World Records for most side lunges in 60 seconds.

Concept note-2: -B Sai Deepak has set a Guinness World Records for most side lunges in 60 seconds and dedicated the rare feat to women and girl safety.

Concept note-3: -Ashrita Furman (born Keith Furman, September 16, 1954) is a Guinness World Records record-breaker. As of 2017, Furman has set more than 600 official Guinness Records and currently holds 530 records, thus holding the Guinness world record for the most Guinness world records.

Concept note-4: -Guinness World Records 2022 Hardcover – 15 November 2021.
