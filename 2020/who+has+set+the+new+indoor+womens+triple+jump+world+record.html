Who has set the new indoor women’s triple jump world record?

(A) Tatyana Lebedeva

(B) Brittney Reese

(C) Hrysopiyi Devetzi

(D) ** Yulimar Rojas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current female world record holder is Yulimar Rojas of Venezuela, with a jump of 15.74 m ( 51 ft 71⁄2 in).

Concept note-2: -Yulimar Rojas Rodríguez (Venezuelan Spanish pronunciation: [ʝuliˈmaɾ ˈroxah]; also known as Yolimar Rojas; born 21 October 1995) is a Venezuelan athlete who holds the world record for women’s triple jump, at 15.74 m ( 51 ft 71⁄2 in).

Concept note-3: -The world record for the triple jump is 18.29 meters, achieved by Jonathan Edwards at IAAF World Athletics Championships Gothenburg 1995, Sweden.
