Who has topped the IIFL wealth Hurun India rich list 2020?

(A) Shiv Nadar

(B) ** Mukesh Ambani

(C) Smita V Crishna

(D) Kiran Mazumdar Shaw

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -14 PROFESSIONAL MANAGERS MADE IT TO THE LIST.
