Who has won ICC Spirit of Cricket Award of the Decade?

(A) Aaron Finch

(B) Lasith Malinga

(C) ** M S Dhoni

(D) Chris Gayle

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mitchell becomes the fourth New Zealand player to win the award, following the footsteps of Daniel Vettori, Brendon McCullum and Kane Williamson. Mitchell wins the award for refusing to take a single in the high-pressure T20 World Cup semi-final against England in Abu Dhabi on November 10, 2021.

Concept note-2: -Aasif thus becomes the first player from Nepal to win the ICC Spirit of Cricket Award. Nepal and Ireland faced off in the sixth match of the Quadrangular series in Oman on 14 February 2022. Ireland were put in to bat first but Nepal had the scoring rate on a leash by picking up regular wickets.

Concept note-3: -Gerhard Erasmus of Namibia wins this honour. In 2022, he scored 956 runs in ODIs at an astounding average of 56.23, while his 306 T20I runs came at 38.25.
