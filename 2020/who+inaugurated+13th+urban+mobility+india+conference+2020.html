Who inaugurated 13th Urban Mobility India Conference 2020?

(A) Ramesh Pokhriyal

(B) Nitin Gadkari

(C) ** Hardeep Singh Puri

(D) Piyush Gupta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Puri inaugurates 13th Urban Mobility India Conference 2020. The 13th Urban Mobility India (UMI) Conference was organized by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs. It was inaugurated by Hardeep S. Puri, Minister of State (IC) Housing and Urban Affairs.

Concept note-2: -From November 4-6, 2022, the 15th edition of Urban Mobility India (UMI) Conference & Expo 2022 held at Hotel Grand Hyatt, Kochi, Kerala which was jointly inaugurated by the Union Minister Hardeep Singh Puri, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA); and Chief Minister (CM) of Kerala, Pinarayi Vijayan.

Concept note-3: -In Kerala, the 15th edition of the Urban Mobility India Conference & Expo opens in Kochi on 4th of November.

Concept note-4: -Urban Mobility India (UMI) Conference & Expo 2022 to be held in Kochi from 4th to 6th November, 2022.

Concept note-5: -The theme of 14th Urban Mobility India (UMI) Conference: “Mobility for All” which focuses on providing equitable access and promoting inclusive urban transportation.
