Who inaugurated a first-of-its-kind giant air purifier in Delhi (Gandhi Nagar)?

(A) Arvind Kejriwal

(B) Manish Sisodia

(C) Adesh Gupta

(D) ** Gautam Gambhir

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gautam Gambhir inaugurates first-of-its-kind giant air purifier in Delhi’s Gandhi Nagar market.

Concept note-2: -Following this, the Delhi cabinet approved the smog tower project in October last year. A two-year pilot study will ascertain the effectiveness of the smog tower.

Concept note-3: -India’s Tallest Air Purifier: Chandigarh.

Concept note-4: -India’s tallest air purification tower has been inaugurated in the Union Territory of Chandigarh. The tower has been installed at the initiative of the Chandigarh Pollution Control Committee (CPCC) at Transport Chowk, Sector 26, by Pious Air Pvt Limited.

Concept note-5: -Designed by the University of Minnesota, the tower was modified by IIT-Bombay and IIT-Delhi to fit the Indian weather.
