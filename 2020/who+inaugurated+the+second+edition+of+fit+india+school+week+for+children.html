Who inaugurated the second edition of Fit India School Week for children?

(A) M.Venkaiah Naidu

(B) ** Kiren Rijiju

(C) Narendra Modi

(D) Hardeep Singh Puri

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Sports Minister Kiren Rijiju on Wednesday launched the second edition of the India School Week’ program.

Concept note-2: -In the 2nd edition, Fit India school week was conducted from December 2020 to January 2021. More than 4.3 lakh schools celebrated the Fit India school week across the nation. Fitness activities such as yoga, free hand exercises, painting, debates, symposiums, etc., were part of this edition.

Concept note-3: -Hon’ble Prime Minister of India has launched the Fit India Movement on 29 Aug 2019 with a view to make Physical Fitness a way of life. Fit India Movement aims at behavioural changes – from sedentary lifestyle to physically active way of day-to-day living.

Concept note-4: -FIT INDIA Movement was launched on 29th August, 2019 by Honourable Prime Minister with a view to make fitness an integral part of our daily lives.

Concept note-5: -The learners attended the inauguration ceremony of the new initiative by the government of India, “FIT India” at Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium, on 29th August 2019, which was addressed by the honorable Prime Minister of India, Shri. Narendra Modi.
