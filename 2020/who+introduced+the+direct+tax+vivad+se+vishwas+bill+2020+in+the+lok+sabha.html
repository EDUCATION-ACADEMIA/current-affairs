Who introduced ‘The Direct Tax Vivad se Vishwas Bill, 2020’ in the Lok Sabha?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) Rahul Gandhi

(C) ** Nirmala Sitharaman

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By India Today Web Desk: The Direct Tax Vivad se Vishwas Bill, 2020 was introduced in Lok Sabha by the Minister of Finance, Ms. Nirmala Sitharaman, on February 5, 2020. The Bill provides a mechanism for resolution of pending tax disputes related to income tax and corporation tax.

Concept note-2: -“No Dispute but Trust Scheme” or “Vivad se Vishwas Scheme” was announced by FM Nirmala Sitharaman in Union Budget 2020 to reduce the number of ongoing legal disputes in the domain of direct taxation.

Concept note-3: -India Code: Direct Tax Vivad se Vishwas Act, 2020. Long Title: An Act to provide for resolution of disputed tax and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.

Concept note-4: -Known as The Direct Tax ‘Vivad se Vishwas Bill, 2020’ for dispute resolution to direct taxes, it proposes the idea of levying less or discounted payment from a taxpayer, if he/she settles all disputes before the end of this particular financial year, i.e. FY 2019-20, under this scheme.

Concept note-5: -The Vivad Se Vishwas scheme was announced under Union Budget 2020 to reduce ongoing legal disputes under direct taxation.
