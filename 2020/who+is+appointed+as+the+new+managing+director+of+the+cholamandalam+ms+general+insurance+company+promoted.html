Who is appointed as the New Managing Director of the Cholamandalam MS General Insurance Company promoted?

(A) Padmaja Chunduru

(B) ** V Suryanarayanan

(C) Pallav Mohapatra

(D) Shyam Srinivasan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -V. Suryanarayanan has been appointed as Managing Director & CEO of Cholamandalam MS General Insurance Company Limited (Chola MS), a joint venture between the Murugappa Group and Mitsui Sumitomo Insurance Group.

Concept note-2: -Chola (English: Shadow of Water) is a 2019 Indian Malayalam-language psychological drama film directed by Sanal Kumar Sasidharan and produced by Joju George, and co-produced by Shaji Mathew and Aruna Mathew.

Concept note-3: -MobiKwik has collaborated with Cholamandalam MS General Insurance Company Ltd and SBI General Insurance on 31 March 2022. On its platform, it will offer third party and comprehensive two-wheeler insurance plans.

Concept note-4: -Cholamandalam MS General Insurance Company India Limited (Chola MS) is a joint venture between the Murugappa Group and Mitsui Sumitomo Insurance Company Limited, Japan.
