Who is the brand Ambassador of CEAT tyres?

(A) Shah Rukh Khan

(B) Salman Khan

(C) ** Aamir Khan

(D) Ayushman Khurrana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CEAT Tyres has signed Tollywood superstar Karthik Sivakumar popularly known as (Karthi) as its brand ambassador to promote SecuraDrive Range of premium car tyres.

Concept note-2: -Aamir Khan in the CEAT TVC CEAT Tyres has launched its new campaign featuring Aamir Khan showcasing the 5-star BEE (Bureau of Energy Efficiency) rating received for its premium tyres - SecuraDrive, SportDrive and CrossDrive AT.

Concept note-3: -Currently MRF is endorsed by Virat Kohli. MRF has also sponsored Indian batsmen Rohit Sharma, Gautam Gambhir, Sanju Samson and Shikhar Dhawan. and Mignon du Preez.

Concept note-4: -CEAT tyres would be the best choice if you want a high-performance tyre for highway and city use at an affordable price. MRF tyres would be the best choice for those people who wanted an excellent gripping and traction performance on both on-road and off-road.

Concept note-5: -CEAT Limited (formerly, Cavi Elettrici e Affini Torino) is an Indian multinational tyre manufacturing company owned by the RPG Group. It was established in 1924 in Turin, Italy. It has a presence in global markets.
