Who is the deputy to the Chief Minister of Bihar?

(A) Tarkishore Prasad

(B) Renu Devi

(C) Nitish Kumar

(D) ** Both A and B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Renu Devi (born 1 November 1958) is an Indian politician who served as the 5th Deputy Chief Minister of Bihar from 16 November to 9 August 2022. She became the fifth female Deputy Chief Minister of India from Bihar in 2020.

Concept note-2: -The current Deputy Chief of Bihar is Tejashwi Yadav, serving in office since 10 August 2022.
