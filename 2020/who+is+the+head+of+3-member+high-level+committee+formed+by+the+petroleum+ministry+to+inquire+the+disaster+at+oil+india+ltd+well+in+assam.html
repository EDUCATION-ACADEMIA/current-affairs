(A) K C Bora

(B) ** SCL Das

(C) T K Sengupta

(D) BC Bora

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The order said that the three-member high-level committee comprises the Director General of Hydrocarbons S C L Das, former ONGC Chairman BC Bora and former ONGC Director T K Sengupta.
