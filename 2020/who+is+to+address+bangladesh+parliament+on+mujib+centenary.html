Who is to address Bangladesh Parliament on Mujib Centenary?

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) Venkaiah Naidu

(C) Ram Nath Kovind

(D) ** Pranab Mukherjee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -parliament of Bangladesh, called the Jatiya Sangsad (House of the Nation), is a unicameral entity consisting of some 350 seats, most of which are filled through direct election. The remaining seats are reserved for women; these members are elected by the parliament itself. Legislators serve five-year terms.

Concept note-2: -The Constitution specifies that Parliament consists of 300 directly-elected members from general seats elected by use of first past the post who represent single-constituencies, while 50 seats are reserved exclusively for women and are allocated on a proportional basis.

Concept note-3: -The legislature of Bangladesh is unicameral. Called the Jatiya Sangsad in Bengali, it is the parliament of Bangladesh. The Speaker presides over meetings of the Jatiya Sangsad and conducts its business in an orderly fashion.
