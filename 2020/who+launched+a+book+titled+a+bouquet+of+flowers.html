Who launched a book titled ‘A bouquet of flowers’?

(A) ** Rajnath Singh

(B) Amit Shah

(C) Smriti Irani

(D) Nitin Gadkari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Defence Minister Rajnath Singh has launched a book titled ‘A bouquet of flowers’ written by Dr Krishna Saksena. Dr Krishna Saksena is the first lady to get a PhD, in 1955 from Lucknow in Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Dr Krishna Sakshena unveils her 9th book – ‘A bouquet of flowers’ Speaking about the book, Dr. Saksena says, “The book has been designed to allow the readers to travel their own journey and arrive at their own very personal realization and be inspired by it. I hope the readers enjoy the book and relate with it.”

Concept note-3: -A bouquet is a bunch of flowers which is attractively arranged.

Concept note-4: -Example Sentences The bride carried a bouquet of white and red roses.
