Who launched an Innovation Hub for Financial Inclusion, recently?

(A) State Bank of India

(B) Ministry of Finance

(C) RBL Bank

(D) ** Reserve Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Reserve Bank Innovation Hub (RBIH) is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) set up to promote and facilitate an environment that accelerates innovation across the financial sector.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains Notes: The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has recently announced that it will establish an innovation hub to assist start-ups to tackle the challenges of financial inclusion.

Concept note-3: -Press Releases. The Reserve Bank of India had constructed a composite Financial Inclusion Index (FI-Index) to capture the extent of financial inclusion across the country, in consultation with the concerned stakeholders including the Government and published it in August 2021 for the FY ending March 2021.

Concept note-4: -Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana. Jeevan Suraksha Bandhan Yojana. Credit Enhancement Guarantee Scheme (CEGS) for Scheduled Castes (SCs)

Concept note-5: -Gopal Natarajan. Mr. Gopal Natarajan is the Chief Administrative Officer (CAO) of the Reserve Bank Innovation Hub.
