Who launched AP Police Seva app?

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) Amit Shah

(C) ** Jagan Mohan Reddy

(D) Ramesh Pokhriyal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The AP Police Seva App was launched by Chief Minister Y S Jagan Mohan Reddy today, which would enable registration of complaints online and monitor the follow-up action.

Concept note-2: -AP Police Seva app comes with 87 services enabling citizens to file complaint and get FIR on smartphone.

Concept note-3: -Rajendranath Reddy assumes charge as new DGP of Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-4: -Andhra Pradesh tops country in highest number of police stations sans vehicles. VIJAYAWADA: As many as 135 out of 1, 021 police stations in Andhra Pradesh do not have transport vehicles according to the latest report of the Bureau of Police Research and Development (BPR&D).

Concept note-5: -’8’ APSP Bns are presently funcutioning in the state of Andhra Pradesh after bifurcation. ROLE OF ANDHRA PRADESH SPECIAL POLICE: The Andhra Pradesh Special Police is a quasi–military force within the frame work of the AP Police.
