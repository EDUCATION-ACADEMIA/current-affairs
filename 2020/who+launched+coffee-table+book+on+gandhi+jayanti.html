Who launched coffee-table book on Gandhi Jayanti?

(A) Arvind Kejriwal

(B) ** Manish Sisodia

(C) Amit Shah

(D) Yogi Adityanath

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 2nd October 2020, on the occasion of the 151st Birth Anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi, Manish Sisodia, the Deputy Chief minister of Delhi launched the coffee-table book titled “Babu – The Unforgettable” over a virtual event organized by the Delhi Archives & Department of Art, Culture and Language, Government of Delhi.

Concept note-2: -He was inspired by Leo Tolstoy’s work, ‘The Kingdom of God is within you’, John Ruskin’s work, ‘Unto this last’ and Henry Thoreau’s work, ‘Civil Disobedience’.

Concept note-3: -Manish Sisodia launches book Bapu The unforgettable on Gandhi Jayanti-ANI - BW Businessworld.

Concept note-4: -Gandhi Jayanti is an event celebrated in India to mark the birthday of Mahatma Gandhi. It is celebrated annually on 2 October, and is one of the three national holidays of India.
