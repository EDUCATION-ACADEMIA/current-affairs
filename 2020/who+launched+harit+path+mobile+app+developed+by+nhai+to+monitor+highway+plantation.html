Who launched ‘Harit Path’, Mobile App developed by NHAI to monitor highway plantation?

(A) Smriti Irani

(B) ** Nitin Gadkari

(C) Rajnath Singh

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Minister for Road Transport and Highways, Sh. Nitin Gadkari launched a mobile app named ‘Harit Path’ to conduct monitoring operation of the plantation along the national highways through Geo-tagging and web-based GIS-enabled tools.

Concept note-2: -National Highways Authority of India launched ‘Harit Path’, a mobile app, in 2020. The application is to monitor the location, growth, species details, maintenance activities, targets and achievements of each of its field units for each and every plant under all plantation projects.

Concept note-3: -Notes: Union Minister of Road Transport and Highways Nitin Gadkari has launched an application named “Harit Path”.

Concept note-4: -NEW DELHI: The 27-year-old National Highways Authority of India (NHAI), the country’s main highway development agency, has got the 20th chairman with the government appointing a senior UP cadre IAS as its new chief.

Concept note-5: -Santosh Kumar Yadav, the senior bureaucrat, was appointed as the Chairman of the National Highway Authority of India (NHAI). Santosh Kumar Yadav is a 1995-batch Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer of the Uttar Pradesh cadre.
