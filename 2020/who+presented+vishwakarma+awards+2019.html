Who presented Vishwakarma Awards 2019?

(A) Prakash Javadekar

(B) ** Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank

(C) Harsh Vardhan

(D) Arjun Munda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Awards are presented by the Hon’ble Minister for Labour and Employment .

Concept note-2: -The award presentation Ceremony for Vishwakarma Rashtriya Puraskar (VRP) & National Safety Awards (NSA) is held every year on the occasion of Vishwakarma day on 17th September. The Awards are presented by the Hon’ble Minister for Labour and Employment.

Concept note-3: -Theme for this year’s event was Water & Sanitation.It received 120+ applications from student teams from science and engineering colleges across the country. 35 teams were selected to work on a prototype of their solution.

Concept note-4: -CIDC Vishwakarma Awards: The Vishwakarma awards are inspired by the spirit of construction and creation patronized by Lord Vishwakarma the ruling deity of the Construction practices in India. At the Annual CIDC Vishwakarma Awards, the work and achievements of individuals and organizations is recognized and applauded.
