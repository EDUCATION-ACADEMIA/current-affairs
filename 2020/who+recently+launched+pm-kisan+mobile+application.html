Who recently launched PM-KISAN mobile application?

(A) Ravi Shankar Prasad

(B) Ram Vilas Paswan

(C) ** Narendra Singh Tomar

(D) Thawar Chand Gehlot

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To broaden the reach further, the PM-KISAN mobile app designed and developed by National Informatics Centre (NIC), Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India is being launched.

Concept note-2: -Dr. Khushboo Mirza, Senior Scientist, Regional Remote Sensing Centre (RRSC), National Remote Sensing Centre (NRSC), Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO), New Delhi has developed the ‘KISAN’ app under the guidance of Dr. C S Jha, Outstanding Scientist and Chief General Manager at RRSC, NRSC, ISRO, Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: -PM Kisan Mobile App is the Official Mobile App launched by the Government of India to help farmers manage their PM Kisan Scheme Account. PM Kisan Mobile App is the Official Mobile App launched by the Government of India to help farmers manage their PM Kisan Scheme Account.

Concept note-4: -Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare has awarded the top-performing states, and districts under PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana. It is based on the criteria such as correction of data, addressing farmer grievances, and timely physical verification exercise.

Concept note-5: -Farmers are welcome to visit the official website at pmkisan.gov.in.
