Who set the new record for Longest Space flight by a Woman?

(A) Anne McClain

(B) ** Christina Koch

(C) Jessica Meir

(D) Peggy Whitson

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nasa astronaut Christina Koch has completed the longest-ever single spaceflight by a woman. The Russian Soyuz spacecraft carrying Koch parachuted down to the grasslands of Kazakhstan at around 09:12 GMT.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Christina Koch. The longest-ever single spaceflight by a woman was accomplished by NASA astronaut Christina Koch. At about 09:12 GMT, the Russian Soyuz spacecraft carrying Koch parachuted down to the grasslands of Kazakhstan.
