Who won gold in men’s 57 kg freestyle at the Asian Wrestling Championship?

(A) Hikmatullo Vohidov

(B) Bajrang Punia

(C) Rustam Iskandari

(D) ** Ravi Dahiya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian wrestler Ravi Kumar Dahiya won the gold medal while Bajrang Punia and Gourav Baliyan settled for silvers at the Asian Wrestling Championships 2022 in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia on Saturday.

Concept note-2: -Indian wrestler Ravi Kumar Dahiya won the gold medal in the men’s 57kg freestyle wrestling category at Birmingham 2022.

Concept note-3: -Ravi Kumar Dahiya won the 57kg men’s freestyle wrestling silver medal at the Tokyo Olympics. The Indian is determined to win gold at Paris 2024.
