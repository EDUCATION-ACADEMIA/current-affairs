Who won Spanish Super Cup football title?

(A) ** Real Madrid

(B) Barcelona

(C) Athletico Madrid

(D) Valencia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Barcelona win their first title since former player Xavi took over as the team’s coach in 2021. Barcelona have won the Spanish Super Cup for the first time since the competition was revamped and moved to Saudi Arabia with a 3-1 victory over Real Madrid.

Concept note-2: -The 2022–23 Supercopa de España was the 39th edition of the Supercopa de España, an annual football competition for clubs in the Spanish football league system that were successful in its major competitions in the preceding season. Barcelona won the tournament for their fourteenth Supercopa de España title.

Concept note-3: -No team has won the Spanish Super Cup more times than Barcelona (14) with Real Madrid the second most successful team in the competition with 12 titles.

Concept note-4: -Real Madrid have won the trophy 13 times, in 1988, 1989, 1990, 1993, 1997, 2001, 2003, 2008, 2012, 2017, 2020 and 2022, while Barcelona have now won it 15 times.

Concept note-5: -Barcelona beat Real Madrid 3-0 in the final of the 2022-23 Supercopa de Espana, claiming their first trophy with Xavi in charge of the club.
