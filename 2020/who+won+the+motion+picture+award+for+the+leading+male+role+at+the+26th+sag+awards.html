Who won the Motion Picture Award for the leading male role at the 26th SAG Awards?

(A) Brad Pitt

(B) Leonardo Di Caprio

(C) ** Joaquin Phoenix

(D) Peter Dinklage

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Glenn Close with Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Leading Role. Tom Hanks with Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Leading Role.

Concept note-2: -The Screen Actors Guild Awards have named the year’s best performances in film and TV.

Concept note-3: -No host has been announced for this year’s ceremony. There wasn’t an official host last year for the 2021 or 2022 awards, either. In 2020, Eugene Levy and Dan Levy opened and closed that year’s SAGs, but they made a point of announcing that they weren’t technically the hosts multiple times.
