Who won the Styrian Grand Prix 2020 at the Red Bull Ring in Spielberg, Austria?

(A) Max Verstappen

(B) Valtteri Bottas

(C) ** Lewis Hamilton

(D) None of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the race, Lewis Hamilton won from pole position, ahead of his teammate Valtteri Bottas and Max Verstappen. The result saw Hamilton close to within 6 points of the championship leader, Bottas, and Mercedes extend their lead in the Constructors’ Championship to 41 points.

Concept note-2: -Here are the complete results from the Austrian Grand Prix, won by Ferrari’s Charles Leclerc ahead of Red Bull’s Max Verstappen.

Concept note-3: -Created in 2020 to maximize the number of Grands Prix during seasons affected by the COVID-19 pandemic, the Styrian event has been held as a double-header with the Austrian Grand Prix at the same circuit on consecutive weekends.
