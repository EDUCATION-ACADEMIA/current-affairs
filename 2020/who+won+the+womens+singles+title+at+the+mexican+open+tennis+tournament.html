Who won the women’s singles title at the Mexican Open tennis tournament?

(A) Simona Halep

(B) ** Heather Watson

(C) Leylah Fernandez

(D) Courtney Duffus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rafael Nadal defeated Cameron Norrie in the final, 6–4, 6–4 to win the singles title at the 2022 Mexican Open. Nadal did not drop a set during a tournament for a record-extending 30th time.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Rafael Nadal. Australian Open champion Rafael Nadal captured his fourth title in Acapulco on 26 Feb 2022. He won with a 6-4 6-4 after defeating Cameron Norrie in the final of the ATP 500 event.

Concept note-3: -The 2022 Mexican Open (also known as the Abierto Mexicano Telcel presentado por HSBC for sponsorship reasons) was a professional tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts.
