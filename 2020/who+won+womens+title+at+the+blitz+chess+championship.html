Who won women’s title at the Blitz Chess Championship?

(A) ** Kateryna Lagno

(B) Lei Tingjie

(C) Alisa Galliamova

(D) Koneru Humpy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Magnus Carlsen and Bibisara Assaubayeva win the World Blitz Chess Championships. The World Blitz Chess Championship ended in Almaty (Kazakhstan) with the victory of Magnus Carlsen in the Open section and Bibisara Assaubayeva in the Women’s.

Concept note-2: -1996: Judit Polgár became the first woman to be ranked in the top ten of all chess players. 1998: Judit Polgár became the first woman to win the U.S. Open Chess Championship. She shared the tournament victory with Boris Gulko as each scored 8–1. 2002: FIDE introduced the Woman Candidate Master title.

Concept note-3: -Ten months after becoming the youngest National women’s chess champion, Divya Deshmukh now holds the distinction of being the youngest to retain the premier title.
