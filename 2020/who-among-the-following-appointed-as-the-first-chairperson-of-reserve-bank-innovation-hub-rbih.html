Who among the following appointed as the first chairperson of Reserve Bank Innovation Hub (RBIH)?

(A) Gopal Srinivasan

(B) Ashok Jhunjhunwala

(C) ** Senapathy Gopalakrishnan

(D) H. Krishnamurthy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The Reserve Bank has appointed Senapathy (Kris) Gopalakrishnan, co-founder and former co-Chairman, of Infosys, as the first Chairperson of the RBIH. Gopalakrishnan is currently the Chief Mentor of Start-up Village, an incubation hub for start-ups.

Concept note-2: -Inauguration of the Reserve Bank Innovation Hub The Hub has an independent Board with Shri Senapathy (Kris) Gopalakrishnan as the Chairman and other eminent persons from industry and academia as members.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has appointed Senapathy (Kris) Gopalakrishnan, co-founder and former co-Chairman, Infosys, as the first Chairperson of the Reserve Bank Innovation Hub (RBIH).

Concept note-4: -Amit Saxena will be based out of Bangalore and will be responsible for conceptualizing and operating an innovative ecosystem to uplift the country’s banking & financial sectors aided by emerging tech solutions.
