Who assumed charge as Secretary, Ministry of Women and Child Development (WCD) in New Delhi on 1 May 2020?

(A) Ajay Panwar

(B) ** Ajay Tirkey

(C) Rabindra Panwar

(D) Rabindra Tirkey

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ajay Tirkey assumed charge as Secretary, Ministry of Women and Child Development (WCD) in New Delhi on Friday.

Concept note-2: -Munjapara Mahendrabhai and Shri Indevar Pandey is the Secretary of the Ministry of Women and Child Development. The activities of the Ministry are undertaken through seven bureaux.

Concept note-3: -History. The Department of Women and Child Development was set up in the year 1985 as a part of the Ministry of Human Resource Development to give the much needed impetus to the holistic development of women and children. With effect from 30 January 2006, the Department has been upgraded to a Ministry.
