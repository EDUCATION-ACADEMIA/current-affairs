Who became the first cricketer to play 100 matches in all three formats of the game-Test, ODI & T20?

(A) Virat Kohli

(B) ** Ross Taylor

(C) Ajinkya Rahane

(D) Kane Williamson

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Who is the first cricketer to play 100 matches in all three formats of the game? Notes: Veteran Cricketer of the New Zealand Team, Ross Taylor recently created history by becoming the first cricketer to play 100 matches in all three formats of the game- Test, ODI, T20 I.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Rohit Sharma. Rohit Sharma is the first Indian male cricketer to play 100 matches of T-20 format.

Concept note-3: -Cricket Legend Virat Kohli has become the first Indian and just the second player overall in international cricket history to have played 100 matches each in all three formats of the game.

Concept note-4: -Explanation: New Zealand cricketer Ross Taylor is the first-ever cricketer to play 100 matches in all three formats- Test, ODI and T20.
