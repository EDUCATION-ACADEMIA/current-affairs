Concept note-1: -Mohana Jitarwal, Avani Chaturvedi and Bhawana Kanth received the award as they made the nation proud by becoming the first three Indian women pilots to take a solo flight. Nilza Wangmo from Ladakh received the Nari Shakti Puraskar as she represented the traditional Ladakhi cuisines and recipes across India.

Concept note-2: -Nari Shakti Puraskar History The first awards were presented to five women on 4th January 2001 by Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee at the Vigyan Bhavan in New Delhi.
