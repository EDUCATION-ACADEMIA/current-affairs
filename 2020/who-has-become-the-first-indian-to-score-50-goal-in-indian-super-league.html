Who has become the first Indian to score 50 goal in Indian Super League?

(A) Anirudh Thapa

(B) Sahal Abdul Samad

(C) Ashique Kuruniyan

(D) ** Sunil Chhetri

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bartholomew Ogbeche, who has been a part of ISL since 2018-19, also holds the all-time record for the most goals in ISL with 59 strikes. Ogbeche is closely followed by Indian talisman and Bengaluru FC captain Sunil Chhetri, who has 52 goals to his name.

Concept note-2: -Former Kerala Blasters striker Alvaro Vazquez holds the record for scoring the longest goal in ISL history when he found the back of the net from 59 metres against NorthEast United FC.

Concept note-3: -Sunil Chhetri is the most-capped player in international football for India and is also the record goal-scorer for India. Chhetri scored his first goal for the country against Pakistan on on June 12, 2005. Since then, he has made 131 official appearances in international football and has 84 goals to his name.
