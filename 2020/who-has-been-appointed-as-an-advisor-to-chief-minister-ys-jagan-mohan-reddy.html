Who has been appointed as an advisor to chief minister YS Jagan Mohan Reddy?

(A) Ajay Narayan Jha

(B) Bimal Jalan

(C) Hasmukh Adhia

(D) ** Subhash Chandra Garg

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sajjala Ramakrishna Reddy, a former journalist, who worked as an editorial director of a media house known to follow the YSRC party line to the hilt, was appointed as the adviser of Govt. public affairs, and he also holds an additional charge of political secretary to the CM of Andhra Pradesh YS Jagan Mohan Reddy.

Concept note-2: -Chowdary also serves as ex-officio Special Chief Secretary (Rank of) to the Government in the Chief Minister’s Office. His role includes advising and assisting the government in bringing in investments, generating employment, and encouraging innovation and start-up activities.

Concept note-3: -Sri Y.S Jagan Mohan Reddy, Hon’ble Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh has come up with an elaborate model of governance comprised of welfare schemes which are based on Navaratnalu.
