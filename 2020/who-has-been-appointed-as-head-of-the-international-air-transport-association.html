Who has been appointed as Head of the International Air Transport Association?

(A) Srinivasan Swamy

(B) Ramesh Narayan

(C) ** Willie Walsh

(D) RakeshAsthana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is option 2, i.e. Ajay Singh. SpiceJet’s Chairman and MD Ajay Singh were elected to the board of the International Air transport Association (IATA).

Concept note-2: -IATA’s Director General and CEO is Willie Walsh.

Concept note-3: -The first IATA’s Director General, Sir William Hildred was instrumental in the establishment of commercial standards to support post-World War Two aviation.

Concept note-4: -Willie Walsh, Director General Prior to joining IATA, Walsh spent his entire career in the airline industry.

Concept note-5: -IATA supports airline activity and helps formulate industry policy and standards. It is headquartered in Montreal, Canada with executive offices in Geneva, Switzerland.
