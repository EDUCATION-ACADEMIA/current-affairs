Who has been appointed as MD and CEO of Canara Bank?

(A) Nanda Kishor Pun

(B) ** L V Prabhakar

(C) Prasad Sharma

(D) T. N. Manoharan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Central government has announced the appointment of K Satyanarayana Raju as Managing Director and CEO of Canara Bank. His appointment is effective from February 7. Earlier, Raju served Canara Bank as executive director from March 2021. He replaced L V Prabhakar who demitted office on December 31, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Chennai: State-owned lender Canara Bank on Tuesday said that the union government has appointed K Satyanarayana Raju as the Managing Director and CEO of the bank with immediate effect. Raju replaces L V Prabhakar who demitted office on December 31, 2022, a statement from the bank said.

Concept note-3: -Canara Bank Founder: Ammembal Subba Rao Pai; Canara Bank Founded: 1 July 1906.
