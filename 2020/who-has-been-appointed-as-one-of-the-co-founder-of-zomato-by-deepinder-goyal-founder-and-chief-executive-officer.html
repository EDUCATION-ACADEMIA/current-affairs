(A) Tapan Singhel

(B) Dipankar Datta

(C) ** Mohit Gupta

(D) Leo Puri

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Akriti Chopra is an Indian Entrepreneur and co-founder of Zomato, an online food aggregator service in India. She was appointed as the co-founder of Zomato in June 2021 ahead of IPO.

Concept note-2: -It was a hectic day for food delivery apps such as Zomato and its founder and CEO Deepinder Goyal also doubled up as a delivery agent for the day.

Concept note-3: -Deepinder Goyal, the CEO of Zomato has himself turned into a food delivery boy and helped his staff in the rush hour.

Concept note-4: -Gunjan Patidar was the Chief Technology Officer at Zomato. New Delhi: Online food delivery platform Zomato Ltd said its Co-founder and Chief Technology Officer Gunjan Patidar resigned from the post on Monday.

Concept note-5: -Before Gupta, Rahul Ganjoo, head of new initiatives, had put in his papers, followed by Siddharth Jhawar, head of intercity legends, the company’s inter-state delivery service. Patidar’s exit is different from the others as he, along with Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah, were the original founders of the company.
