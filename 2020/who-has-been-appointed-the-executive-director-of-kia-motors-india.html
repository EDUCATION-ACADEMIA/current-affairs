Who has been appointed the executive director of Kia Motors India?

(A) Kenichiro Yoshida

(B) ** Tae-Jin Park

(C) Kookhyun Shim

(D) Yong S. Kim

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tae-Jin Park has over 30 years of experience in the automotive industry. Prior to his new role, Park served as the Head of Sales, Kia Motors Mexico.

Concept note-2: -Kia India elevates Tae-Jin Park as Managing Director and CEO.

Concept note-3: -Since our inception, we have been achieving new milestones in the Indian market, and this momentum will only be heightened with Sohn taking charge, ” Kia India Managing Director and CEO Tae-Jin Park said. Kia India on Friday said it has appointed MyungSik Sohn as Chief Sales Officer with immediate effect.

Concept note-4: -Till date, Kia India has launched five vehicles for the Indian market – the Seltos, the Carnival, the Sonet, the Carens, and the EV6.

Concept note-5: -Hyundai Motor Group decided to purchase the auto company in 1998 to keep it afloat. Kia and Hyundai Motor Group operate independently, but Hyundai is the parent company of Kia Motors.
