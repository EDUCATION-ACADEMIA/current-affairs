Who has been designated as a new Engineer-in-Chief of Indian Army?

(A) Manoj Mukund Naravane

(B) ** Harpal Singh

(C) Anant Prasad Singh

(D) Rajeev Sabherwal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lieutenant General Arvind Walia assumed the appointment of Engineer-in-Chief at IHQ of MoD (Army), New Delhi from 01 Jan 2023.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi [India], December 26 (ANI): Lieutenant General Arvind Walia has been appointed as the next Engineer-in-Chief of the Indian Army, said Indian Army officials. He would be succeeding Lt Gen Harpal Singh who is superannuating on December 31.

Concept note-3: -Who is currently the Indian Army Chief? Ans. General Manoj Pande is the current Chief of Army Staff and he took over the office on April 30, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Lt Gen MV Suchindra Kumar, AVSM, YSM**, VSM, DCOAS (Strat) Lieutenant General MV Suchindra Kumar, Ati Vishisht Seva Medal, Yudh Seva Medal**, Vishisht Seva Medal assumed the appointment of Deputy Chief of Army Staff (Strategy), Integrated Headquarters of Ministry of Defence (Army) on 01 July 2022.
