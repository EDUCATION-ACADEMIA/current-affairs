Who has been elected as the Speaker of the British Columbia Legislative Assembly?

(A) ** Raj Chouhan

(B) Sir Lindsay Hoyle

(C) Ameenah Gurib-Fakim

(D) Pravind Jugnauth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Elected by the members of assembly, this post is held by two politicians for two identical roles such as “speaker” and “deputy speaker” for assembly session proceedings.

Concept note-2: -Located in Victoria, British Columbia and officially opened in 1898 with a 150-metre-long facade (500 ft), central dome, two end pavilions, and a gold-covered statue of Captain George Vancouver, the British Columbia Parliament Buildings is home to the Legislative Assembly of British Columbia.

Concept note-3: -The speaker of the House of Commons (French: président de la Chambre des communes) is the presiding officer of the lower house of the Parliament of Canada. A member of Parliament (MP), they are elected at the beginning of each new parliament by fellow MPs.
