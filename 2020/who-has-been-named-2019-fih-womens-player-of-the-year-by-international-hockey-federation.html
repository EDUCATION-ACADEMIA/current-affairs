Who has been named 2019 FIH Women’s Player of the Year by International Hockey Federation?

(A) ** Eva de Goede

(B) Carla Rebecchi

(C) Janne Muller-Wieland

(D) Florencia Habif

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Felice Albers of Netherlands is women’s Player of the Year In women’s category, Felice Albers of Netherlands was named the FIH Player of the Year.

Concept note-2: -The Netherlands’ Felice Albers and India’s Harmanpreet Singh have been named the International Hockey Federation (FIH) Player of the Year for the 2021-2022 season in the women’s and men’s competitions respectively.

Concept note-3: -FIH Player of the Year 2021-22: India’s Harmanpreet Singh wins men’s award. The Indian hockey star also won the award last year. Harmanpreet Singh holds the record for the most goals scored in an FIH Pro League season.

Concept note-4: -Lausanne [Switzerland], October 7 (ANI): Indian hockey team star Harmanpreet Singh was awarded the men’s International Hockey Federation (FIH) Player of the Year 2021-22, making him just the fourth player to receive the honour in consecutive years for the men’s division on Friday.
