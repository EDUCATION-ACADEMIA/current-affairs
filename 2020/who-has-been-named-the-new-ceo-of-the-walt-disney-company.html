Who has been named the new CEO of the Walt Disney Company?

(A) Bob Iger

(B) Kathleen Kennedy

(C) ** Bob Chapek

(D) Michael Eisner

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bob Iger named Disney CEO again, replacing Bob Chapek and shocking Hollywood | CNN Business.

Concept note-2: -Bob Iger’s return as the CEO of the Walt Disney Company ends a tumultuous two-year period for Iger’s once-successor, Bob Chapek. Chapek, then the head of Disney’s parks division, took over the CEO position from Iger in 2020.

Concept note-3: -Robert A. Iger returned to The Walt Disney Company as Chief Executive Officer on November 20, 2022. Mr. Iger previously served as CEO and Chairman of The Walt Disney Company from 2005 to 2020, then Executive Chairman and Chairman of the Board through 2021.

Concept note-4: -LOS ANGELES, Nov 21 (Reuters) - Bob Iger is returning to Walt Disney Co (DIS. N) as chief executive less than a year after he retired, a surprise comeback that coincides with the entertainment company’s attempt to boost investor confidence and profits at its streaming media unit.
