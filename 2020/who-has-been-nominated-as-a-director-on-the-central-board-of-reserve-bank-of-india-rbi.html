Who has been nominated as a director on the Central Board of Reserve Bank of India (RBI)?

(A) ** Tarun Bajaj

(B) Arun Kumar Singh

(C) Harsh Vardhan Shringla

(D) Taranjit Singh Sandhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The nomination has come into effect from November 15, 2022, until further orders. The central government has nominated Vivek Joshi, Secretary, Department of Financial Services, Ministry of Finance, Government of India as a Director on the Central Board of Reserve Bank of India vice Sanjay Malhotra.

Concept note-2: -Joshi who is the secretary of the Department of Financial Services, will hold the position of Director at RBI. The nomination has come into effect from November 15, 2022, until further orders.

Concept note-3: -The government has appointed Financial Services Secretary Vivek Joshi as a director on the central board of the Reserve Bank of India. “The nomination of Shri Vivek Joshi is effective from November 15, 2022 and until further orders, ” the RBI said in a release.

Concept note-4: -RBI central board: Government reappoints four independent directors on RBI central board - The Economic Times.

Concept note-5: -Shri Shaktikanta Das is the present Governor of the RBI.
