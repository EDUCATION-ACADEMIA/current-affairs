Who has been picked as captain of ESPN Cricinfo’s ODI and Twenty20 teams of the decade?

(A) Steven Smith

(B) Virat Kohli

(C) Aaron Finch

(D) ** MS Dhoni

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Former India skipper Mahendra Singh Dhoni was picked as captain of ESPN Cricinfo’s ODI and Twenty20 teams of the decade.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. MS Dhoni was picked as the captain of ESPN Cricinfo’s ODI and T20 teams of the decade, while Virat Kohli was named the leader in Tests, on 1 Jan 2020.

Concept note-3: -MS Dhoni remains India’s most successful T20I captain of all time. He captained India in 72 games, out of which the team won 41, lost 28, tied one and two produced no result. His win percentage in the format is 59.28.

Concept note-4: -ESPNcricinfo Awards 2021 Captain of the year - Kane Williamson.

Concept note-5: -ICC’s T20I Team of the Decade: Rohit Sharma, Chris Gayle, Aaron Finch, Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, Glenn Maxwell, MS Dhoni (c), Kieron Pollard, Rashid Khan, Jasprit Bumrah, and Lasith Malinga. The ICC’s ODI team of the decade includes Rohit and Virat, other than captain Dhoni.
