Who has been unanimously elected to the board of global dairy body International Dairy Federation (IDF)?

(A) ** Dilip Rath

(B) Ramesh Pokhriyal

(C) Rajiv Mishra

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chairman, NDDB unanimously elected to Board of International Dairy Federation. 3 November 2020, Anand: Shri Dilip Rath, Chairman, National Dairy Development Board (NDDB) has been unanimously elected to the Board of International Dairy Federation (IDF) on 2nd November 2020 during the General Assembly of IDF.

Concept note-2: -Dr R S Sodhi Elected to IDF Board a representative elected by the General Assembly on 1 June 2021. Managing director - Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation Ltd or GCMMF-Amul branded products in India.

Concept note-3: -Piercristiano Brazzale (pictured), has today (2 November) been elected President of the International Dairy Federation (IDF).

Concept note-4: -IDF President Piercristiano Brazzale flags inflation; moots digitalisation of dairy sector. India is the future of the global dairy industry and it has a huge potential to increase milk output, said Piercristiano Brazzale, President, International Dairy Federation (IDF).

Concept note-5: -Indian National Committee of the IDF The INC IDF has 17 members representing Government, National Institutions, Academia and Industry.
