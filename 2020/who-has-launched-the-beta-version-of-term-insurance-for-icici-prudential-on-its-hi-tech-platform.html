Who has launched the beta version of Term Insurance for ICICI Prudential on its Hi-Tech Platform?

(A) CSE Ebix

(B) NSE Ebix

(C) ** BSE Ebix

(D) IIE Ebix

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BSE Ebix Launches Beta Version of Term Insurance for ICICI Prudential on its On-Demand Hi-Tech Platform.

Concept note-2: -The initiative will allow customers to buy insurance policies and make premium payments digitally, from their homes. With this tie-up, ICICI Prudential Life has become the first such life insurance company to provide the UPI AUTOPAY facility to its customers to simplify the payments processes.

Concept note-3: -ICICI Prudential Life began its operations in the fiscal year 2001. On a retail weighted received premium basis (RWRP), it has consistently been amongst the top companies in the Indian life insurance sector. Our Assets Under Management (AUM) at December 31, 2022 were ‘2, 518.84 billion.

Concept note-4: -Mr. N.S. Kannan is the Managing Director & CEO of ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company since June 19, 2018. Kannan joined ICICI Limited in 1991 and has been with the ICICI group for over 29 years.
