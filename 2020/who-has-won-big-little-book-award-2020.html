Who has won Big little book award 2020?

(A) Subhadra Sen Gupta

(B) Rajiv Eipe

(C) ** Both A and B

(D) Nagesh Hegdeblba

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -I’m very honoured to receive the Big Little Book Award 2020 for Illustrator. I would like to dedicate it to all the wonderful illustrators who are doing such amazing, inspiring work in children’s books in India currently.

Concept note-2: -Winner from the author category, Prof. S. Sivadas based out of Kottayam, has over two hundred books to his name.

Concept note-3: -wins the Waterstones Children’s Book Prize 2020. Nathan Bryon and Dapo Adeola’s “utterly joyful” picture book Look Up! (Puffin) has been crowned overall winner of the Waterstones Children’s Book Prize 2020.

Concept note-4: -It is our great pleasure to confirm the winners of the 2022 British Book Awards – or Nibbies – with former Waterstones Book of the Year nominee You are a Champion by Marcus Rashford and Carl Anka triumphing as both Overall Winner and Children’s Non-fiction Book of the Year.

Concept note-5: -Celebrated Odia and English writer Manoj Das will receive the Mystic Kalinga Literary Award (Indian and Global Languages).
