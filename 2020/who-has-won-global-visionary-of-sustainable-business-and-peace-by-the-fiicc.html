Who has won “Global Visionary of Sustainable Business and Peace” by the FIICC?

(A) Mukesh Ambani

(B) Roshani Nadar

(C) Anil Ambani

(D) ** Ratan Tata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sir Ratan Tata is undoubtedly the most respected global visionary and sustainable business leader who has always stood for peaceful co-existence of people and planet much before the 2030 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals came into existence in 2015, ” he said.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Ratan Tata. Veteran industrialist Ratan Tata has been conferred the title of “Global Visionary of Sustainable Business and Peace” by the Federation of Indo-Israel Chambers of Commerce (FIICC). He has been honoured for epitomising “unity, peace and sustainability".

Concept note-3: -He was instrumental in the acquisition of Tetley, Jaguar Land Rover and Corus, which turned the Tata Group from a major India-centric company to a global brand name. Under his leadership, the organisation achieved new heights and generated significant revenues from overseas operations.

Concept note-4: -Ratan Tata received the Padma Bhushan in 2000 and Padma Vibhushan in 2008, the third and second highest civilian honours awarded by the Government of India.
