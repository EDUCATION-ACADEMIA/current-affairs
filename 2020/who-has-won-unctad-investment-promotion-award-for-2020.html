Who has won UNCTAD Investment Promotion Award for 2020?

(A) Vocal for Local

(B) Make in India

(C) ** Invest India

(D) Made In India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United Nations (UNCTAD) has declared Invest India- the National Investment Promotion Agency of India- as a winner of the 2020 United Nations Investment Promotion Award. The award ceremony took place on 7th December 2020 at UNCTAD Headquarters in Geneva.
