Who inaugurated Rythu Vedika?

(A) ** K. Chandrashekar Rao

(B) Rajnath Singh

(C) Aayush Goyal

(D) Ramesh Pokhriyal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CM inaugurated Rythu Vedika - Sri K. Chandrashekar Rao.

Concept note-2: -At the Kodakandla Mandal headquarters town in the Jangaon district, the Telangana state chief minister launched the Rythu Vedika Scheme on October 31, 2020.

Concept note-3: -The Government of Telangana is building a platform which is known as Rythu Vedika which will be helpful in promising higher returns to the farmers for their farm produce.

Concept note-4: -The scheme was announced by the Chief Minister of Telangana, K. Chandrashekhar Rao at Farmers Coordiantion Committee(Rythu Samanvaya Samithi) conference at Jayashankar Agriculture University on 25 February 2018.An allocation of ₹12, 000 crores was made in 2018-19 state budget.

Concept note-5: -The total plinth area of the rythu vedika will be 2, 046 sq. ft. There will also be facilities for electricity and tap water connection in the rythu vedika.
