Who is appointed as a member of Expert Appraisal Committee (EAC) that deals with violation of environment rules?

(A) Sanjay Aggarwal

(B) Subodh Prasad

(C) ** Anil Kumar Jha

(D) Ramesh Sinha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -K. Deenabandu, former IAS officer, has been made chairman of theState-level Expert Appraisal Committee(SEAC).

Concept note-2: -The EACs’ primary role in the environmental clearance (EC) process is to give recommendations to the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC) on project proposals after considering the potential impacts of the project.

Concept note-3: -The role of EAC is integral to the process of granting environmental clearance to development projects. The Expert Appraisal Committees (EACs) opine on whether a proposed project beyond a certain size ought to be commissioned and recommend ways to mitigate the potential environmental impact.

Concept note-4: -EXPERT APPRAISAL COMMITTEE (EAC)
