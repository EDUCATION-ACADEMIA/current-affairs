Who is appointed as the additional and independent director of Great Eastern Shipping Company Ltd?

(A) ** Urjit Patel

(B) K.M Sheth

(C) S jaishankar

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is option 2 i.e. Urjit Patel . Great Eastern Shipping Company Ltd has appointed former RBI Governor Urjit Patel as an additional and independent director for a term of five years.

Concept note-2: -Mr. Bharat K Sheth is Deputy Chairman & Managing Director of The Great Eastern Shipping Company Limited, India’s premier shipping company.

Concept note-3: -Urjit Patel has been appointed as Additional Director of Britannia Industries with effect from 31st March 2021 for a tenure of 5 years.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Urjit Patel. Former Reserve Bank of India (RBI) Governor Urjit Patel has resigned from the post of non-executive and independent Director of Britannia Industries Limited. His appointment was for a period of five years, which would have ended in 2026.
