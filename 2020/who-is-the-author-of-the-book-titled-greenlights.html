Who is the author of the book titled ‘Greenlights’?

(A) Andrew Sean Greer

(B) Katharine Hepburn

(C) ** Matthew McConaughey

(D) Bong Joon-ho

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Greenlights is a 2020 book by American actor Matthew McConaughey. It was published on October 20, 2020, by the Crown imprint of Crown Publishing Group.

Concept note-2: -Looking back through old journals, McConaughey found eight common categories, and in deconstructing them, he came up with the idea of greenlights. ” He believes that “a greenlight is being kind to our future self. It’s things in our life that affirm our way, they say ‘go, proceed, more, please carry on.

Concept note-3: -1-Sentence-Summary: Greenlights is the fascinating autobiography of Matthew McConaughey in which he takes us on a wild ride of his journey through a childhood of tough love, rising to fame and success in Hollywood, shifting his career, and more, guided by the green lights he saw that led him forward at each step.

Concept note-4: -Greenlights is not a memoir, though it tells true stories from his life in chronological order. Nor is it “an advice book”. It is “an approach book”, bringing together McConaughey’s insights from 35 years of writing journals, and more of collecting bumper stickers.
