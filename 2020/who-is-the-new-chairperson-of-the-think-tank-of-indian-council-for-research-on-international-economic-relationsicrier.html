Who is the new Chairperson of the think tank of Indian Council for Research on International Economic Relations(ICRIER)?

(A) Isher Judge Ahluwalia

(B) ** Pramod Bhasin

(C) Dr. Rajat Kathuria

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr. Pramod Bhasin is ICRIER’s chairperson and Dr. Deepak Mishra is Director & Chief Executive.

Concept note-2: -ICRIER Chair Pramod Bhasin on ADBI’s 25th Anniversary | Asian Development Bank.

Concept note-3: -Dr. Isher Ahluwalia is ICRIER’s chairperson. Dr. Rajat Kathuria is Director and Chief Executive.

Concept note-4: -Founder Chairman K B Lall was succeeded by R N Malhotra, Governor, Reserve Bank of India, who laid solid institutional foundations for ICRIER during the period 1992-96.

Concept note-5: -ICRIER, one of India’s leading think tanks, was established in August 1981 as a not-for-profit research organisation to provide a strong economic basis for policy making.
