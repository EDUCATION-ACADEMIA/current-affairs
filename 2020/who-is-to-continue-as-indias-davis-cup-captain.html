Who is to continue as India’s Davis Cup captain?

(A) ** Rohit Rajpal

(B) Leander Paes

(C) Zeeshan Ali

(D) Mahesh Bhupati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rohit Rajpal to remain India Davis Cup captain.

Concept note-2: -India refused to travel to South Africa for the final due to their government’s opposition to South Africa’s apartheid policies: therefore, the final was scratched and South Africa were awarded the Davis Cup.

Concept note-3: -’India Ousts Soviet Union In Davis Cup’, the headline read, reporting how a team led by the Amritraj brothers advanced to the 1974 Davis Cup final by defeating the Soviet Union.

Concept note-4: -Davis Cup 2023: Denmark defeat India 3-2 in Group I play-offs. The loss pulled India into Davis Cup Group II for the first time since the new format was introduced in 2019.
