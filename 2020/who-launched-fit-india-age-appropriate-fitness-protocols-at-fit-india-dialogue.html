Who launched ‘Fit India Age Appropriate Fitness Protocols’ at Fit India dialogue?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) ** Narendra Modi

(C) Ramnath Kovind

(D) Nitin Gadkari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Speaking on the occasion, the Prime Minister said the Fit India Dialogue focuses on the fitness interests of every age group and brings into play different dimensions of fitness.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports under the aegis of the Fit India Movement has developed Age Appropriate Fitness Protocols named as G.O.A.L.S. (Goals for Active Life Style) for different age groups categorized as 05-18 years, 18-65 years & 65+ years.

Concept note-3: -FIT INDIA Movement was launched on 29th August, 2019 by Hon’ble Prime Minister with a view to make fitness an integral part of our daily lives. The mission of the Movement is to bring about behavioural changes and move towards a more physically active lifestyle.

Concept note-4: -It was launched at Indira Gandhi International Stadium in New Delhi. It is a nationwide movement to motivate Indians to incorporate simple and easy ways to staying fit in their lives to lead the country towards a healthy future.

Concept note-5: -Kashmiri athlete Danish Manzoor ‘honoured and proud’ to be FIT India Movement ambassador | More sports News - Times of India.
