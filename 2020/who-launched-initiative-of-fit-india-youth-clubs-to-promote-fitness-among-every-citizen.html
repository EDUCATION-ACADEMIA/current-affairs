Who launched initiative of Fit India Youth Clubs to promote fitness among every citizen?

(A) Rajnath Singh

(B) Nitin Gadkari

(C) ** Kiren Rijiju

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -FIT INDIA Movement was launched on 29th August, 2019 by Hon’ble Prime Minister with a view to make fitness an integral part of our daily lives. The mission of the Movement is to bring about behavioural changes and move towards a more physically active lifestyle.

Concept note-2: -Background. Hon’ble Prime Minister of India has launched the Fit India Movement on 29 Aug 2019 with a view to make Physical Fitness a way of life. Fit India Movement aims at behavioural changes – from sedentary lifestyle to physically active way of day-to-day living.

Concept note-3: -It was launched at Indira Gandhi International Stadium in New Delhi. It is a nationwide movement to motivate Indians to incorporate simple and easy ways to staying fit in their lives to lead the country towards a healthy future.

Concept note-4: -Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports’ flagship programme, Fit India Movement to start special online series titled ‘Fit India-Sunday Talks’

Concept note-5: -Kashmiri athlete Danish Manzoor ‘honoured and proud’ to be FIT India Movement ambassador | More sports News - Times of India.
