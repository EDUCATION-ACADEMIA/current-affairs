Who launched the 1st edition of “Matsya Sampada”-newsletter fisheries and aquaculture published by the Department of Fisheries?

(A) ** Giriraj Singh

(B) Narendra Singh Tomar

(C) Harsimrat Kaur Badal

(D) Ramvilas Paswan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The scheme was announced by the Finance Minister, Nirmala Sitharaman during her speech in the parliament of India while presenting the Union budget for 2019–20 on 5 July 2019.

Concept note-2: -The Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) was launched in September 2020 with an aim to double the income of fish farmers and fishers in the country. It focuses on sustainable development of India’s fisheries sector and is a part of the Atmanirbhar Bharat scheme.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana | Department of Fisheries, MoCIT, GoI.

Concept note-4: -PMMSY or Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana aims to build a ‘Blue revolution’. The Government launched this scheme to support fisheries and improve the production, infrastructure, marketing and technology of this sector.
