Who launched welfare scheme for women in Goa?

(A) Vishwajit Rane

(B) Nirmala Sitharaman

(C) Pramod Sawant

(D) ** Smriti Irani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union minister for women and child development Smriti Irani launched 3 welfare schemes for women in Goa.

Concept note-2: -A GIRL CHILD (MAMTA): (maximum 02 deliveries) in the registered medical institution. Under the Scheme an amount of Rs. 10000/- shall be paid to all the mothers who delivers a girl child in the registered medical institution.

Concept note-3: -BETI BACHAO BETI PADHAO (BBBP): The Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme (BBBP) is a Central Sector Scheme implemented through State Governments/UTs to empower the girl child and enable her education. This scheme is intended to improve Child sex ratio across the country.

Concept note-4: -SHRI SUBHASH FALDESSAI, HON’BLE MINISTER FOR SOCIAL WELFARE CALLED ON HON’BLE GOVERNOR AT RAJ BHAVAN. RAJ BHAVAN.

Concept note-5: -JananiSurakshaYojana (JSY) is a safe motherhood intervention under the National Health Mission. It is being implemented with the objective of reducing maternal and neonatal mortality by promoting institutional delivery among poor pregnant women.
