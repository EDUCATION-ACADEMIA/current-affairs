Who launches National Digital Health Mission, where all Indians will get Health IDs?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) ** Narendra Modi

(C) Arvind Kejriwal

(D) Smriti Irani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Prime Minister said Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission, will now connect the digital health solutions of hospitals across the country with each other.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched the National Digital Health Mission on 15th August 2020. The mission aims to create an integrated healthcare system linking practitioners with patients digitally by giving them access to real-time health records.

Concept note-3: -What is Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission? It was launched in September 2021 by the Prime Minister through a video conference. It aims to provide digital health IDs for all Indian citizens to help hospitals, insurance firms, and citizens access health records electronically when required.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi: The Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) was launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi to bring a change to the country’s healthcare system. This initiative includes a unique health ID for every citizen and the digital health solutions of hospitals across India making the process of hospitals simplified.
