Who received the first ‘Muppavarapu National Award for Social Service’?

(A) Medha Patkar

(B) ** Gutta Muniratnam

(C) Kiran Bedi

(D) Sunitha Krishnan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eminent Agricultural Scientist Dr M S Swaminathan and well-known social worker, Dr. Gutta Muniratnam were respectively chosen as the first recipients of ‘Muppavarapu Venkaiah Naidu National Award for Excellence’ and ‘Muppavarapu National Award for Social Service”

Concept note-2: -Q. Who has been recently chosen for “Muppavarapu Venkaiah Naidu National Award for Social Service”? Notes: Eminent social worker, Dr. Gutta Muniratnam was chosen as the first recipient of ‘Muppavarapu National Award for Social Service”.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution The correct answer is MS Swaminathan. M S Swaminathan and Dr. Gutta Muniratnam were chosen as the first recipients of the Muppavarapu Venkaiah Naidu National Award for Excellence and the Muppavarapu National Award for Social Service.

Concept note-4: -Swaminathan is awarded for his contributions towards the field of agriculture. This award was presented during the 10th Anniversary Celebrations of Muppavarapu Foundation in Hyderabad by the Vice President of India, Venkiah Naidu.

Concept note-5: -EVERY year, the government of India announces the Padma Awards, the nation’s highest civilian honour for distinguished service in public life, social work, music and art, sports and other fields. India’s highest civilian awards are the Bharat Ratna, Padma Vibhushan, Padma Bhushan and the Padma Shri.
