Who received the Humanitarian Award at 20th edition of annual BET Awards 2020, for her long standing philanthropic work, including recent COVID-19 relief efforts?

(A) Billie Eilish

(B) Rihanna

(C) Lady Gaga

(D) ** Beyonce

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Beyonce, 38, is being honoured for her longstanding philanthropic work, including her recent COVID-19 relief efforts, a statement posted on the official website of the awards read.

Concept note-2: -Danish Manzoor Bhat. Global Humanitarian Award. JaipurFoot USA’s 1st Global Humanitarian Award.

Concept note-3: -Some of the artists performing included 2 Chainz, Big Sean, Burna Boy and Chris Martin, City Girls, Gucci Mane, Jhené Aiko, Quavo, Lil Baby, Big Latto, Tobe Nwigwe and Ty Dolla $ign.

Concept note-4: -The Broncos won the 2022 Sports Humanitarian Team of the Year Award, ESPN announced Tuesday. The annual award is given to a sports franchise that demonstrates how teamwork can create a measurable impact on a community or cause. The 49ers won the award in 2017, the only other time an NFL team has won it.
