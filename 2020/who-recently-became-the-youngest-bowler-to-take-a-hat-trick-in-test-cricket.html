Who recently became the youngest bowler to take a hat-trick in Test cricket?

(A) ** Naseem Shah

(B) Abu Jayed

(C) Saif Hassan

(D) Mohammad Abbas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At 16, Pakistan’s Naseem Shah recently became the youngest man to take a Test hat-trick.

Concept note-2: -Meanwhile, 16-year-old Naseem Shah is the youngest bowler to achieve the feat in the Test cricket.

Concept note-3: -Pele is the youngest player to score a World Cup hat-trick. The Brazilian achieved the feat in the 1958 World Cup against France in the semifinals at the age of 17 years and 244 days.

Concept note-4: -Naseem Abbas Shah (Urdu, Pashto: ; born 15 February 2003) is a Pakistani international cricketer. He is a fast bowler and is in playing-eleven in T20 Format. In October 2019, at the age of 16, he was called up to the Pakistan cricket team for their Test series against Australia.

Concept note-5: -The most recent hat-trick was taken by South Africa’s spin bowler Keshav Maharaj against the West Indies in June 2021 at Daren Sammy Cricket Ground in Gros Islet, Saint Lucia.
