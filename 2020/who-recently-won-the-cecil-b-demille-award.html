Who recently won the “Cecil B DeMille Award”?

(A) ** Tom Hanks

(B) Johnny Depp

(C) Brad Pitt

(D) Michael Clarke Duncan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jane Fonda became the 68th recipient in 2021. She is an actress best known for “Barbarella” (1968), “They Shoot Horses, Don’t They?” (1969), “Klute” (1971) “Coming Home” (1978), “Julia” (1977), “The China Syndrome” (1979), “On Golden Pond” (1981) and “The Morning After” (1986).

Concept note-2: -Robin Williams won the Cecil B. DeMille Awards in 2005. The actor had 10 Globes nominations and five wins, including a special award for his vocal work on Aladdin in 1993.

Concept note-3: -On the film side, “The Banshees of Inisherin” took home the award for best picture, musical or comedy. It was the most nominated film at the Golden Globes, with eight nods.

Concept note-4: -Julia Garner won best supporting actress in a musical, comedy or drama award at the 2023 Golden Globes on Tuesday, for her role in ‘Ozark’.
