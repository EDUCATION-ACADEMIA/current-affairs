Who surpassed Dhoni’s record to take the top position in the list of wicket keepers with most dismissals in T20i?

(A) Rishabh pant

(B) ** Alyssa Healy

(C) KL Rahul

(D) Sara Taylor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Alyssa Healy Breaks MS Dhoni’s Record Of Most Dismissals By Wicket-Keeper In T20Is.

Concept note-2: -MS Dhoni holds the record most dismissals, stumpings and catches in T20Is as a wicket-keeper.

Concept note-3: -Scott Edwards of team Netherlands occupies the #1 spot as the top wicketkeeper in the T20 World Cup 2022 with the most number of dismissals. Joss Butler of England and Quinton de Kock of South Africa occupy the second and third spots respectively.

Concept note-4: -1.MS Dhoni (32) Having led India to their maiden T20 World Cup triumph in the first season in 2007, Mahendra Singh Dhoni holds the record for the most wicketkeeping dismissals in the tournament’s history. In 32 innings, Dhoni has as many dismissals to his name in the competition.
