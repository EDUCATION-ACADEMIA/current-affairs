Who topped Edelgive Hurun India Philanthropy List 2020?

(A) Shiv Nadar

(B) Mukesh Ambani

(C) ** Azim Premji

(D) Rohini Nilkeni

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The list was topped by Azim Premji, the founder chairman of Wipro, with a donation of Rs 9, 713 crore during fiscal 2020-21 which is around Rs 27 crore a day.

Concept note-2: -With total donations estimated at US$102.4bn, Mumbai-based Jamsetji Tata (1839-1904), was the world’s biggest philanthropist of the last century in the 2021 EdelGive Hurun Philanthropists of the Century, a ranking of the world’s most generous individuals from the past century.”

Concept note-3: -Shiv Nadar and family came in first place among the top 10 in the EdelGive Hurun India Philanthropy List 2022 with a donation of ₹1, 161 crore.

Concept note-4: -Industrialist and founder of HCL Technologies Shiv Nadar has reclaimed ‘India’s most generous’ title with an annual donation of Rs 1, 161 crore, the EdelGive Hurun India Philanthropy List for 2022 said.
