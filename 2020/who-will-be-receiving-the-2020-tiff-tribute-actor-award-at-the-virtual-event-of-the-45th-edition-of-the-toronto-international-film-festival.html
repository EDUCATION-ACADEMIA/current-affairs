Who will be receiving the 2020 TIFF Tribute Actor Award at the virtual event of the 45th edition of The Toronto International Film Festival?

(A) Jennifer Lawrence

(B) Angelina Jolie

(C) ** Kate Winslet

(D) Margot Robbie

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Winslet will receive the TIFF Tribute Actor Award on Sept. 15 in a virtual event, replacing the physical gala to ensure safety during the COVID-19 pandemic. Kate Winslet will be honoured by this year’s Toronto International Film Festival.

Concept note-2: -Anthony Shim. The Platform jury provided this statement: “The 2022 TIFF Platform Jury announces the unanimous choice for the Platform Prize - Riceboy Sleeps, written and directed by Anthony Shim for its deeply moving story and precisely-observed characters as they navigate racism, dislocation, family, and love.

Concept note-3: -Taylor Swift, Weird Al Yankovic film, Squid Game star’s directorial debut join TIFF 2022 lineup. See this year’s slate for the Toronto International Film Festival, which includes new titles from Jennifer Lawrence, Brendan Fraser, Tyler Perry, Olivia Colman, Hugh Jackman, Zac Efron, Daniel Radcliffe, and more.

Concept note-4: -The winner this year is Chloé Zhao’s Nomadland, a film that was almost universally beloved but is a mildly surprising win given that it already took top laurels at Venice last week, and the two prizes have never overlapped.
