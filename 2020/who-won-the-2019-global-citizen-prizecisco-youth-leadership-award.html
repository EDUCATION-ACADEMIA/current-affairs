Who won the 2019 Global Citizen Prize:Cisco Youth Leadership Award?

(A) NoorinShereef

(B) ** Priya Prakash

(C) Shaan Rahman

(D) Abhishek Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Priya Prakash is a 29-year-old health care entrepreneur working to ensure every child across India can grow up healthy.

Concept note-2: -The Global Citizen Prize: Cisco Youth Leadership Award honors an individual aged 18-30 who has contributed meaningfully towards the goal of ending extreme poverty. The award includes a US$250, 000 prize paid to the organization to which the individual contributes.

Concept note-3: -The winner of the 2023 Global Citizen Prize: Cisco Youth Leadership Award will be someone who can demonstrate measurable impact of their work over the last three to five years, and who has advanced one or more of the UN’s Global Goals.

Concept note-4: -The 2022 Global Citizen Awards: Honoring the bridge-builders and peacemakers in a time of war. The Atlantic Council’s annual gala honored three world leaders, an Academy Award-winning artist, Google’s CEO, and the strength of the transatlantic relationship.
