Who won the BBC Indian Sportswoman of the Year 2019?

(A) Saina Nehwal

(B) Sania mirza

(C) ** P V Sindhu

(D) Marry Kom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This was the third edition of the BBC Indian Sportswoman of the Year awards. PV Sindhu won it in 2019 while chess grandmaster Koneru Humpy got it in 2020.

Concept note-2: -NEW DELHI: Olympic silver medallist weightlifter Mirabai Chanu on Monday won the BBC Indian Sportswoman Of The Year awardChanu created history last year when she became the first Indian weightlifter to clinch a silver medal at the Summer Olympics.

Concept note-3: -Nikhat Zareen, Boxer: After winning the Junior World Boxing Championship in 2011, she won a gold medal at the IBA Women’s World Boxing Championships in 2022.

Concept note-4: -India’s Olympic medallists PV Sindhu, Mirabai Chanu and Sakshi Malik were named on Monday among the five nominees for the BBC Indian Sportswoman of the Year 2022 award. Two-time wrestling world championships medallist Vinesh Phogat and reigning boxing world champion Nikhat Zareen are also in the fray for the honour.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Koneru Humpy.
