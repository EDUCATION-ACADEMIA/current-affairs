Who won the F1 Tuscan Grand Prix 2020?

(A) Alexander Albon

(B) Valtteri Bottas

(C) ** Lewis Hamilton

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lewis Hamilton won the race, his 90th career win. Valtteri Bottas came in second and Alex Albon finished third; his first career podium. By finishing in ninth place, Kimi Räikkönen scored his first points of the season.

Concept note-2: -Max Verstappen of Red Bull Racing won the Italian Grand Prix, on his debut on the podium at Monza while Charles Leclerc finished second after starting from the pole. Max Verstappen won the Formula One Italian Grand Prix on Sunday to leave him in touching distance of his second straight Formula One title.

Concept note-3: -Winner: Lewis Hamilton It was ruthless. The victory was the 90th of his career, one away from equalling Michael Schumacher’s all-time record.

Concept note-4: -Max Verstappen won his first World Championship (and the first for a Dutch driver), driving for Red Bull Racing-Honda. Lewis Hamilton, the defending champion, finished runner-up, driving for Mercedes. Mercedes won their eighth consecutive Constructors’ Championship.
