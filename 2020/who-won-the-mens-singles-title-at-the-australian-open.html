Who won the men’s singles title at the Australian Open?

(A) Rafael Nadal

(B) Alexander Zverev

(C) ** Novak Djokovic

(D) Dominic Thiem

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Currently, Novak Djokovic is the most successful Australian Open player in history with 10 titles (2008, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2015, 2016, 2019, 2020, 2021 and 2023) followed by Roger Federer with six (2004, 2006, 2007, 2010, 2017 and 2018).

Concept note-2: -Novak Djokovic Comes Full Circle at the Australian Open Deported a year ago and unable to play in 2022’s first Grand Slam tournament, Djokovic deeply felt this major title, his 22nd, calling it “a huge relief.”

Concept note-3: -List of Australian Open Winners - 2023 Winners & Runners. The 2023 Australian Open Winner in the Men’s Singles category is Novak Djokovic.

Concept note-4: -American Serena Williams is a seven-time champion, which is an Open Era record. Naomi Osaka is a two-time champion. Ashleigh Barty won the title in 2022, becoming the first Australian woman to win the title in 44 years.
