Who won the men’s singles title at the Dubai Tennis Championships?

(A) Rafael Nadal

(B) Roger Federer

(C) Stefanos Tsitsipas

(D) ** Novak Djokovic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jelena Ostapenko won her fifth career title, and third at WTA 500 level or above, at the 2022 Dubai Duty Free Tennis Championships. The Latvian defeated four consecutive Grand Slam champions, came from a set down in three straight matches, saved one match point, won two deciding tiebreaks and dished out two bagel sets.

Concept note-2: -2022 US Open Champions Spain’s Carlos Alcaraz, 19, defeated Casper Ruud in the 2022 US Open men’s singles final to earn his first Grand Slam title. Iga Swiatek came to New York as the consensus world No. 1 and beat Ons Jabeur in straight sets in the women’s final.
