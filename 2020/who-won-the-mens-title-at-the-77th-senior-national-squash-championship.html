Who won the men’s title at the 77th Senior National Squash Championship?

(A) Ramit Tandon

(B) Mahesh Mangaonkar

(C) ** Saurav Ghosal

(D) Harinder Pal Sandhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Sourav Ghosal. Saurav Ghosal won the men’s title in the 77th Senior National Squash Championship. The championship was held in February 2020. The Squash Rackets Federation of India (SRFI)-77th Senior National squash championships was held at Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-2: -“Winning the Nationals was a dream for me and winning it in my first final is great. It is certainly my biggest win and very special, ” said Abhay. K. P. Karthikeyan, Member Secretary, SDAT, gave away the prizes.

Concept note-3: -India’s squash ace Joshna Chinappa has clinched 77th Senior National Championship in woman’s category at the ISA courts in Chennai on 15 Feb 2020. With the win, she claimed her 18th title in the career. Saurav Ghosal won in Men’s category bagging his 13th title.

Concept note-4: -Dipika Pallikal Karthik and Saurav Ghosal won the 73rd Senior National Squash Championship in womens and mens single categories respectively.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Saurav Ghosal. Saurav Ghosal is currently ranked 15th in the world (PSA Men’s World Rankings). Saurav has won 5 titles, besides one gold and one silver at the 2014 Asian Games and three bronze medals before that. He is the first Indian Squash player to win the Malaysian Open Championships 2021.
