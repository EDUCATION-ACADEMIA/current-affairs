Who won the men’s title at the Tata Mumbai Marathon?

(A) Geoffery Mutai

(B) Martin Lel

(C) Douglas Wakiihuri

(D) ** Derara Hurisa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MUMBAI: In the end, there was no Asian Games qualification nor was there an expected Sudha Singh victory in the Indian section of the Tata Mumbai Marathon on Sunday. Gopi Thonakkal walked away with victory in 2:16.

Concept note-2: -MUMBAI: Chhagan Bombale of Maharashtra and Andhra Pradesh’s Kavitha Reddy clinched the men’s and women’s titles in the Mumbai Half Marathon 2022 at the Bandra Kurla Complex here on Sunday.

Concept note-3: -In 2020, Derara Hurisa broke the men’s marathon record, completing the race in 2:08:15. In 2023, 2 new course records were set. For the men’s, Hayle Lemi set a new record of 2:07:32 as the marathon’s winner. For the women’s, Anchialem Haymanot set a new record of 2:24:15 as the marathon’s winner.

Concept note-4: -About tata mumbai marathon winner prize money ₹ 50, 000.

Concept note-5: -AFTER A break of two years due to the Covid-19 pandemic, Tata Mumbai Marathon returned on Sunday, with 55, 212 participants across seven categories of races.
