Who won the US Open Women’s tennis tournament?

(A) ** Naomi Osaka

(B) Victoria Azarenka

(C) Serena Williams

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Emma Raducanu defeated Leylah Fernandez in the final, 6–4, 6–3 to win the women’s singles tennis title at the 2021 US Open.

Concept note-2: -Carlos Alcaraz and Iga Świątek won the men’s and women’s singles titles, respectively. They became the first players, both born in the 2000s, to win the tournament’s singles titles.

Concept note-3: -Carlos Alcaraz defeated Casper Ruud in the final, 6–4, 2–6, 7–6, 6–3 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2022 US Open. It was his first major title, and he claimed the world No. 1 singles ranking with the win.

Concept note-4: -Elena Rybakina defeated Ons Jabeur in the final, 3–6, 6–2, 6–2 to win the ladies’ singles tennis title at the 2022 Wimbledon Championships. It was her first major singles title.
