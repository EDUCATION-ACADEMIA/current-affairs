Wings India 2020, an International conference and exhibition on the Civil Aviation sector, has begun in which city?

(A) Leh

(B) New Delhi

(C) Chandigarh

(D) ** Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Asia’s largest event in aviation, Wings India begins in Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which city is set to host ‘WINGS INDIA 2022’, the exhibition on civil aviation? Notes: Hyderabad is scheduled to host the ‘WINGS INDIA 2022’, the flagship biennial exhibition on civil aviation. It is organised from March 24 - 27, 2022 at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. International exhibition and conference on the civil aviation sector, ‘Wings India 2020’ was held at Begumpet Airport in Hyderabad. This event was organized by the Ministry of Civil Aviation along with the Airports Authority of India (AAI) and FICCI.

Concept note-4: -Wings India 2020, the biennial civil aviation and aerospace event was launched today at Begumpet Airport in Hyderabad. The event is being organised by Ministry of Civil Aviation along with Airports Authority of India and FICCI (Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry).

Concept note-5: -The event is scheduled from 18th to 21st, January 2024, at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad, India.
