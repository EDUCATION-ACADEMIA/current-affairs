With whom Amex join hands to leverage scale, differentiated offerings for premium customers?

(A) ** SBI Cards

(B) Master Cards

(C) Rupay Card

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: SBI Card has joined hands with American Express (Amex) to leverage scale and differentiated offerings for its premium customers. Under this partnership, SBI Card’s top-end premium products – SBI Card ELITE and SBI Card PRIME – will be available on American Express’ global network.
