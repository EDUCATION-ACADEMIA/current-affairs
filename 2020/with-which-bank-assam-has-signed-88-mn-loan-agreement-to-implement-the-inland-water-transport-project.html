With which bank Assam has signed $88 mn loan agreement to implement the Inland Water Transport Project?

(A) Asian Development Bank

(B) ** World Bank

(C) Bank of China

(D) Standard Chartered

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is World Bank. The Inland Water Transport (IWT) Department has signed an MoU with the World Bank for a Rs 770 crore loan to improve river transportation in Assam.

Concept note-2: -For the implementation of Assam Inland Water Transport Project, the Government of India (GoI), the Government of Assam and the World Bank have signed a $88 million loan agreement. The loan agreement seeks to modernize the Assam’s passenger ferry sector by improving the infrastructure and its services.

Concept note-3: -“This operation will allow the state to invest in Kolkata’s economic productivity by making its waterways and ferry services part of an efficient and safe urban mobility strategy, ” said Junaid Ahmad, World Bank Country Director in India.
