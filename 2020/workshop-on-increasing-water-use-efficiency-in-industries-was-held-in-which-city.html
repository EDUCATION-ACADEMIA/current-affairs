Workshop on “Increasing Water Use Efficiency in Industries” was held in which city?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Vishakhapatnam

(C) Bengaluru

(D) Kochi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The workshop on “Increasing Water Use Efficiency in Industries” was held in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Water use efficiency (WUE) is defined as the amount of carbon assimilated as biomass or grain produced per unit of water used by the crop.

Concept note-3: -India has low water use efficiency compared to the developed countries. The overall irrigation project efficiency in developed countries is 50 – 60% as compared to only 38% in India.

Concept note-4: -Efficient water use can have major environmental, public health, and economic benefits by helping to improve water quality, maintain aquatic ecosystems, and protect drinking water resources.

Concept note-5: -Water distribution efficiency indicates uniformity in distribution of water over the entire root zone. Water Use Efficiency (WUE): Water use efficiency is defined as yield of marketable crop produced per unit of water used in evapotranspiration.
