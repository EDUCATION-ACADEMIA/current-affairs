World Bank has granted loan of how many Rs. to the West Bengal for tackling coronavirus situation and developmental work?

(A) Rs. 1, 450 crore

(B) Rs. 1, 650 crore

(C) Rs. 1, 350 crore

(D) ** Rs. 1, 950 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI, March 10, 2022 – The Government of India, the Government of West Bengal, and the World Bank have signed a $125 million loan to support efforts to help poor and vulnerable groups access social protection services in the state of West Bengal.

Concept note-2: -The State government had earmarked a total borrowing of Rs 73.268 crore for 2022-23. The previous Marxist government had left a total loan of Rs 1.72 lakh crore in their three decades of rule. The Opposition BJP and Left have held the Chief Minister responsible for gradually leading Bengal towards a debt trap.

Concept note-3: -The press release added, “The World Bank has approves a loan of $125 million (Around Rs 1, 000 crore) to West Bengal for inclusive social protection – A huge recognition of the honourable Chief Minister’s Schemes of social pension to the vulnerable groups of the society.”

Concept note-4: -WASHINGTON, DC, September, 21, 2022 - The World Bank’s Executive Board of Directors today approved a $350 million loan to the western Indian state of Gujarat to improve the quality and access of its public healthcare services, with special focus on adolescent girls and disease surveillance.
