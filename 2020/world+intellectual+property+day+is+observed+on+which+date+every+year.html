World Intellectual Property Day is observed on which date every year?

(A) 22 April

(B) 24 April

(C) 25 April

(D) ** 26 April

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Intellectual Property Day is celebrated every year on April 26.

Concept note-2: -The theme of World Intellectual Property Day 2022 is ‘IP and Youth Innovating for a Better Future’. Events organized to mark the Day seek to inform about the role of intellectual property rights in encouraging innovation and creativity. For 2022, the theme will explore how youth are driving positive change.

Concept note-3: -26 April - World Intellectual Property Day This day is celebrated every year on 26 April and was established by the World Intellectual Property Organisation (WIPO) in 2000 to raise awareness of how patents, copyright, trademarks, and designs impact daily life.

Concept note-4: -World Intellectual Property Day 2022 is an opportunity for young people to find out how IP rights can support their goals, help transform their ideas into reality, generate income, create jobs and make a positive impact on the world around them.
