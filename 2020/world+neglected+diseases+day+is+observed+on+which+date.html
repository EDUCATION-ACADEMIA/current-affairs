World Neglected Diseases day is observed on which date?

(A) 28th January

(B) 29thJanuary

(C) ** 30thJanuary

(D) 31thJanuary

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Neglected Tropical Diseases Day is observed every year on 30 January. This date marks the launch of the first WHO road map and the launch of the London declaration on neglected tropical diseases (NTDs), both in 2012.

Concept note-2: -In 2022, Uniting to Combat NTDs launched the 100% Committed movement on 27 January, just ahead of World NTD Day. 100% Committed exists to begin securing political and financial commitments behind the Kigali Declaration on NTDs.

Concept note-3: -On 31 May 2021, the World Health Assembly (WHA) recognized 30 January as World Neglected Tropical Disease (NTD) Day through decision WHA74(18). This decision formalized 30 January as a day to create better awareness on the devastating impact of NTDs on the poorest populations around the world.

Concept note-4: -Neglected Tropical Diseases Day 2022: A call for equitable health for all – Message from Dr Tedros, Director General, WHO.

Concept note-5: -NTDs are found in several countries in Africa, Asia, and Latin America. NTDs are especially common in tropical areas where people do not have access to clean water or safe ways to dispose of human waste.
