World Immunization Week is celebrated on which dates?

(A) 18-24 April

(B) 12-18 April

(C) 6-12 April

(D) ** 24-30 April

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Immunization Week, celebrated in the last week of April, aims to highlight the collective action needed and to promote the use of vaccines to protect people of all ages against disease.

Concept note-2: -World Immunization Week 2022: #Long Life For All.

Concept note-3: -The world’s first “World Immunization Week” was observed in 2012, which witnessed the simultaneous participation of more than 180 countries and territories worldwide. The basis for vaccination began in the year 1796.

Concept note-4: -National Vaccination Day. The Government of India observes 16 March as National Vaccination Day to acknowledge and appreciate the hard work of frontline health care workers to ensure the vaccination of every child.

Concept note-5: -The theme for the year 2022 is “vaccines work for all".
