World Migratory Bird Day is observed on which date every year?

(A) ** May 9

(B) May 7

(C) May 6

(D) May 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Migratory Bird Day is officially celebrated on the second Saturday of May in Canada and the US (May 13th in 2023), and the second Saturday of October in Mexico, Central and South America, and the Caribbean (October 14th in 2023).

Concept note-2: -The very first World Migratory Bird Day was launched by AEWA and CMS on the weekend of 8-9 April 2006 on Ms. Kuki Gallmann’s famous wildlife reserve ‘Ole Ari Nyiro’ in Laikipia, Kenya.

Concept note-3: -It is celebrated on the second Saturday in May and then in October. This year it was celebrated on 14 May and 8 October 2022.

Concept note-4: -World Migratory Bird Day (WMBD) is an awareness-raising campaign highlighting the need for the conservation of migratory birds and their habitats. It aims to draw attention to the threats faced by migratory birds, their ecological importance, and the need for international cooperation to conserve them.

Concept note-5: -World Migratory Bird Day - celebrated on the second Saturday in May and the second Saturday in October - is an annual global campaign dedicated to raising awareness of migratory birds and the need for international cooperation to conserve them.
