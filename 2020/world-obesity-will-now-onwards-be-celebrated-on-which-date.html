World Obesity will now onwards be celebrated on which date?

(A) 1 January

(B) 25 February

(C) ** 4 March

(D) 29 October

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Obesity Day is celebrated every year on March 4. However, it was previously celebrated on October 11 but the date got changed to March 4.

Concept note-2: -World Obesity Day is observed every year on March 04. It is observed to spread awareness about obesity and encourage action towards its elimination. The day is organised by the World Obesity Federation, a non-profit body that is in official relations with the World Health Organisation.

Concept note-3: -World Obesity Day will return on 4 March 2022 with the theme: “Everybody Needs to Act”. The 2022 campaign is the third unified World Obesity Day, when World Obesity members and allies come together to call for change.

Concept note-4: -This condition puts people at a higher risk for serious diseases such as cancer, diabetes and heart disease. World Obesity Day, observed annually on March 4, highlights the environmental and medical factors that contribute to high obesity rates.

Concept note-5: -World obesity day encourages practical solutions to help people achieve and maintain a healthy weight, undertake proper treatment, and reverse the obesity crisis.
