World Sports Journalists Day is observed annually on which day?

(A) July 1

(B) ** July 2

(C) July 4

(D) July 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Sports Journalist Day [2nd July] World Sports Journalist Day is observed on 2nd July every year to acknowledge the work of sports journalists worldwide.

Concept note-2: -National Press Day - the 16th of November – is symbolic of a free and responsible press in India.

Concept note-3: -SJFI was born in Calcutta. It was on 27 February 1976 that the Sports Journalists Federation of India (SJFI) was formed at the Eden Gardens, Calcutta, during the inaugural year of the J.K. Bose Cricket Tournament.

Concept note-4: -Sports journalism is a form of writing that reports on matters pertaining to sporting topics and competitions.
