World wind day is observed on which day every year?

(A) 13 June

(B) 12 June

(C) ** 15 June

(D) 14 June

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global Wind Day is a worldwide event that occurs annually on 15 June. It is a day for discovering wind energy, its power and the possibilities it holds to reshape our energy systems, decarbonise our economies and boost jobs and growth. Wind. Are You In?

Concept note-2: -Global Wind Day - 15 June Global Wind Day is a worldwide event that occurs annually on 15 June. It is a day for discovering wind energy, its power and the possibilities it holds to reshape our energy systems, decarbonise our economies and boost jobs and growth. Wind. Are You In?

Concept note-3: -Every year, the Global Wind Day is celebrated on June 15 to acknowledge the prospects of wind power. Wind is among the most sustainable renewable energy sources. The Wind Day brings to fore its strength, and the possibilities it brings to reshape energy systems around the world.

Concept note-4: -GLOBAL WIND DAY HISTORY The European Wind Energy Association (EWEA) organized the first Wind Day in 2007. In 2009 the EWEA joined forces with the Global Wind Energy Council (GWEC) and made it a worldwide event. In recent years, WindEurope and the GWEC have organized the day together.

Concept note-5: -The theme for World Wind Day 2022 is the celebration and appreciation of wind energy and its benefits.
