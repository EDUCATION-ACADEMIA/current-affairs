World’s largest meditation centre has been inaugurated in which state?

(A) Ladakh

(B) Assam

(C) ** Telangana

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Heartfulness Institute has its international headquarters at the pristine 1400-acre campus at Kanha Shanti Vanam, located near Chegur Village in Ranga Reddy district, 50 kilometers from Hyderabad in India.

Concept note-2: -Kanha Shanti Vanam. Kanha Shanti Vanam is a spiritual centre with integrated township located 25 kms from Rajiv Gandhi International Airport in Chegur Village, Ranga Reddy District, Hyderabad, spanning 1, 200 acres. It supports extended spiritual training and mentorship programmes.

Concept note-3: -On a campus spread across 1, 400 acres, the Heartfulness meditation centre occupies 30 acres, making it what Shantivanam authorities claim the “world’s largest meditation centre”.

Concept note-4: -Kanha Shanti Vanam is situated near Hyderabad. Apart from Kanha, there are 5000 Heartfulness Centres, known as HeartSpots around the world.

Concept note-5: -Directors of Kanha Shanti Vanam are Shyamsunder Mallick Vadlamani, Anantha Kumar Dommety and Ulhas Rao Kumble.
