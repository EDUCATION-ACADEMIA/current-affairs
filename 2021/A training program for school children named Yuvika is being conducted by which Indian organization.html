A training program for school children named ‘Yuvika’ is being conducted by which Indian organization?

(A) Defence Research and Development Organisation

(B) Bhabha Atomic Research Centre

(C) *** Indian Space Research Organization

(D) Council of Scientific and Industrial Research

Explanations:

Indian Space Research Organisation is organising a special programme for SchoolChildren called “Young Scientist Programme” “YUva VIgyani KAryakram” ,YUVIKA, to impart basic knowledge on Space Technology, Space Scienceand Space Applications to the younger students with a preference to ruralareas.
