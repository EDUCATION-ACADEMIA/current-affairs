As per Eurostat data, which country was the biggest trading partner of the European Union (EU) in 2020?

(A) *** China

(B) Japan

(C) United States

(D) UK

Explanations:

In 2020, the United Kingdom and the United States were the largest destinationfor EU exports of services, with trade valued at EUR 184 billion and EUR 183billion respectively, each representing 20 % of all exports to non-membercountries.
