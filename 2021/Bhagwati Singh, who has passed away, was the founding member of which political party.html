Bhagwati Singh, who has passed away, was the founding member of which political party?

(A) Indian National Congress

(B) *** Samajwadi Party

(C) BJP

(D) Indian National Congress

Explanations:

Bhagwati Singh (1932/3 – 4 April 2021) was a politician and founder of theSamajwadi Party and a member of the Parliament of India representing UttarPradesh in the Rajya Sabha from 2004 to 2010 and a member of the Uttar PradeshLegislative Assembly from 1977 until 2003.
