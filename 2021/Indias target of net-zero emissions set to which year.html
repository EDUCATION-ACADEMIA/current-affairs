India’s target of net-zero emissions set to which year?

(A) *** 2070

(B) 2060

(C) 2080

(D) 2050

Explanations:

Target year – India aims to reach net zero by 2070. India’s Long Term LowEmissions Development Strategy (LTS), submitted to the UNFCCC in November2022, details India’s vision and plan of action for achieving its target to become a netzero emitter by 2070 (Government of India, 2022a).
