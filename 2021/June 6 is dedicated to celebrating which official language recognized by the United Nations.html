June 6 is dedicated to celebrating which official language recognized by the United Nations?

(A) *** Russian

(B) Chinese

(C) English

(D) French

Explanations:

UN Russian Language Day is observed annually on June 6. The event wasestablished by the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization(UNESCO) in 2010. UN Russian Language Day coincides with the birthday ofAlexander Pushkin, a Russian poet who is considered the father of modern Russianlanguage.
