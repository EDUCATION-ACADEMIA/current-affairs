The Festival Mahashivratri is locally known as Herath in which state/UT?

(A) Gujarat

(B) *** Jammu & Kashmir

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Delhi

Explanations:

Herath or the ‘Night of Hara (Shiva)’, generally known as Maha Shivratri, isthe main festival celebrated by the Kashmiri Pandits across Jammu andKashmir (J&K). The festival marks the marriage anniversary of Lord Shiva andGoddess Uma (Parvathi). 2022 Herath Festival was celebrated on 28th February2022.
