The World Press Freedom Index is published annually by which organization?

(A) *** Reporters Without Borders

(B) Transparency International

(C) World Economic Forum

(D) Economist Intelligence Unit

Explanations:

The Paris based Reporters Without Borders (RSF) publishes annually aWorld Press Freedom Index (WPFI) purporting to evaluate the level offreedom available to the media in 180 countries. From a rank of 80 in theinaugural WPFI report, 2002, India’s rank fell to 122 in 2010 and 131 in2012.
