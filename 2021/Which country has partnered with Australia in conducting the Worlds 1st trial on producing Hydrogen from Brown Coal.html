(A) India

(B) USA

Explanations:

The HydrogenEnergy Supply Chain (HESC) Pilot Project is being developed by the consortiumcomprising Kawasaki Heavy Industries, J-POWER, Iwatani, Marubeni, AGL andSumitomo.
