Which day of the year is observed as National Doctors’ Day?

(A) Fourth Wednesday of June

(B) 30 June

(C) First Thursday of July

(D) *** 01 July

Explanations:

Doctors are saviours; hence, many consider them as next to God for their noblework. And to acknowledge their contribution to the society, July 1 is being celebratedas Doctor’s Day in India. The first Doctor’s Day was celebrated by the IndianMedical Association (IMA) on July 1, 1991.
