Which of the following Shatabdi Express has become the first IMS certified train?

(A) Madhya Pradesh Shatabdi Express

(B) Bihar Shatabdi Express

(C) Vivek Shatabdi Express

(D) *** Chennai-Mysore-Chennai Shatabdi Express

Explanations:

Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Mysore- Chennai Shatabdi Express.Mysore- Chennai Shatabdi Express has become the first Integrated ManagementSystems (IMS) certified train of Southern Railway. It has also become thefirst Shatabdi of Indian Railways and second mail/express train on IndianRailways.
