Who has bagged the Kuvempu Rashtriya Puraskar for the year 2020?

(A) Sitakanta Mohapatra

(B) *** Rajendra Kishore Panda

(C) Brajanath Rath

(D) Balarama Dasa

Explanations:

The Kuvempu Rashtriya Puraskar, the national award instituted in memory of latepoet laureate Kuvempu, has been awarded to renowned Odia poet Dr. RajendraKishore Pandafor the year 2020. The prestigious award carries a cash award of Rs 5lakh, a silver medal, and a citation.
