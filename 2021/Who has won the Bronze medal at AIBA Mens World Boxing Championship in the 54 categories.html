(A) *** Akash Kumar

(B) Rohit Mor

(C) Shiva Thapa

(D) None of the above

Explanations:

The first male Indian boxer to win a World Championships medal wasVijender Singh, who won bronze in the middleweight division at the 2009 WorldChampionships. No male Indian boxer has won gold as yet at the WorldChampionships, with the best performance being Amit Panghal’s flyweight (52kg)silver in the 2019 edition.
