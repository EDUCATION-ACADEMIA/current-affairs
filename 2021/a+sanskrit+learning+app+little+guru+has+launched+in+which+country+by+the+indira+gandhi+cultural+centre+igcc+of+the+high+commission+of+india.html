A Sanskrit learning app ‘Little Guru’ has launched in which country by the Indira Gandhi Cultural Centre (IGCC) of the High Commission of India?

(A) ** Bangladesh

(B) Bhutan

(C) Nepal

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bangladesh. A Sanskrit learning app ‘Little Guru’ will be launched in Bangladesh by the Indira Gandhi Cultural Centre (IGCC) of the High Commission of India on 12 April 2021.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR), has celebrated its 71st Foundation Day recently. At the celebrations in Swami Vivekanand Cultural Centre at Indian Embassy in Beijing, the foundation day celebrations were accompanied by the launch of ‘Little Guru’ app.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Little Guru. Sanskrit learning assembles easily with the world’s greatest gamified Sanskrit learning App. The app developed by Gamapp Sportswizz Tech and encouraged by The Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR) has been named Little Guru.

Concept note-4: -Well, no, Sanskrit is not currently on Duolingo and there aren’t any plans to add it to their vast library of courses. It might be that Duolingo’s simple approach to vocabulary and grammar might not work so well for Sanskrit’s complex form, but there are plenty of difficult and technical languages on the platform.
