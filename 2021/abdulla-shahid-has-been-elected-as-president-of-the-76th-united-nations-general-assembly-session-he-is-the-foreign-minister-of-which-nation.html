Abdulla Shahid has been elected as President of the 76th United Nations General Assembly session. He is the foreign minister of which nation?

(A) Sri Lanka

(B) Mauritius

(C) South Africa

(D) ** Maldives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abdulla Shahid (Dhivehi: ; born 26 May 1962) is Maldivian politician who served as President of the 76th United Nations General Assembly between 2021 and 2022. Shahid is the first Maldivian politician to hold that post. He has been serving as Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maldives since 2018.

Concept note-2: -Update: The election of the President of the 76th session of the General Assembly took place in the General Assembly Hall at United Nations Headquarters in New York on 7 June 2021. Honourable Abdulla Shahid was elected as President of the 76th session of the General Assembly.

Concept note-3: -Foreign Minister Abdulla Shahid of the Maldives pledged a focus on pandemic relief and climate change during his yearlong term as UNGA president.

Concept note-4: -His Excellency Volkan Bozkir of Turkey was the President of the 75th session of the General Assembly.

Concept note-5: -Honourable Abdulla Shahid is widely recognised as an exceptional politician and a seasoned diplomat, with a long and distinguished career serving in both the Government and Legislature of the Maldives.
