According to a recent NCRB report, how many children are committing suicide every day in the country?

(A) 39

(B) ** 31

(C) 45

(D) 52

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to NCRB Data, in 2020, 12, 526 students died by suicide, while in 2021, the number rose to 13, 089.

Concept note-2: -The latest statistics from the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) note that the burden of deaths by suicide has increased in India - by 7.2 per cent from 2020 - with a total of 1, 64, 033 people dying by suicide in 2021.

Concept note-3: -The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) released data on suicidal deaths in India in August, 2022 and the figures were startling. A total of 1, 64, 033 suicides were reported in the country in 2021 which is an increase of 7.2% in comparison to the previous year in terms of total numbers.

Concept note-4: -Every hour one student commits suicide in India, with about 28 such suicides reported every day, according to data compiled by the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB).
