According to the TomTom Traffic Index, which city was the most congested city in the world in 2020?

(A) ** Moscow

(B) Bogota

(C) Mumbai

(D) Paris

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the 2020 TomTom Traffic Index, Moscow leads the world in terms of traffic congestion with 54 per cent, displacing Bengaluru – which had taken the top spot in 2019 and is now in sixth place with 51 per cent.

Concept note-2: -Istanbul, Turkey, with an average congestion level of 62%, was the most congested city in 2021.

Concept note-3: -1. London. The British capital was found to be the most congested city in the world for the second year in a row, with drivers spending an average of 156 hours stuck in traffic in 2022, up 5% since before Covid.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Mumbai. Mumbai has been ranked as the fifth most congested city in the world in 2021, as per TomTom’s 2021 Traffic Index released on February 9, 2022.

Concept note-5: -Top 5 most congested cities in the world: London (156 hours), Chicago (155 hours), Paris (138 hours), Boston (134 hours) and New York (117 hours).
