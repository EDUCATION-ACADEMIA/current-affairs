An initiative called “HAUSLA” launched by the Jammu and Kashmir government for women entrepreneurs of UT is in partnership with which company?

(A) PhonePe

(B) DMart

(C) Paytm

(D) ** Flipkart

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tejaswini scheme provides financial assistance to the young women of 18 to 35 years of age’. “Under the scheme, female entrepreneurs are facilitated to avail financial assistance under Mudra from J&K Bank to the extent of Rs 5 lakh.

Concept note-2: -Dena Shakti Scheme is a scheme that provides loans for women entrepreneurs in the following sectors: Partnership firm business. Retail stores.

Concept note-3: -“Hausla” is an event which paves a way to give wings to women entrepreneurs of J&K to fly higher and make their own identity in the field of business and with the objective of exploiting their potential and enable them for wider outreach and visibility of their products and services.

Concept note-4: -In a historic effort aimed at harnessing the energy, enthusiasm, and exuberant dreams of the Women of Jammu & Kashmir, Lieutenant Governor Manoj Sinha today launched the “HAUSLA- inspiring her growth”, a comprehensive programme for Catalysing Women Entrepreneurship in the UT, here at SKICC Srinagar.
