(A) ** Istvan Szabo

(B) Andorai Peter

(C) Klaus Maria Brandauer

(D) Lajos Koltai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Satyajit Ray Lifetime Achievement Award was conferred upon two living legends of modern cinema - Hollywood director Martin Scorsese and Hungarian auteur Istvan Szabo - at the inaugural event of the 52nd International Film Festival of India (IFFI) here on Saturday.

Concept note-2: -Celebrated Spanish film Director Carlos Saura has been honoured with the prestigious Satyajit Ray Lifetime Achievement award at the gala opening ceremony of 53rd edition of the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) in Goa today, 20 November, 2022.

Concept note-3: -IFFI 2022: Satyajit Ray Lifetime Achievement Award given to Spanish film icon Carlos Saura. Awarded the Satyajit Ray Lifetime Achievement Award at the 53rd International Film Festival of India (IFFI) opening event held here on Sunday was renowned Spanish filmmaker Carlos Saura .
