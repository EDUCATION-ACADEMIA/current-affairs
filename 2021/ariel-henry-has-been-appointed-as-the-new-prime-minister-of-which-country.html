Ariel Henry has been appointed as the new Prime Minister of which country?

(A) ** Haiti

(B) South Africa

(C) West Indies

(D) Sweden

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ariel Henry (French pronunciation: [aʁjɛl ɑ̃ʁi]; born 6 November 1949) is a Haitian neurosurgeon and politician who has served as the acting prime minister of Haiti and the acting president of Haiti since 20 July 2021.

Concept note-2: -The office was created under the 1987 Constitution; previously, all executive power was held by the president or head of state, who appointed and chaired the Council of Ministers. The office is currently vacant, with Ariel Henry having been sworn in as acting prime minister on 20 July 2021.

Concept note-3: -The National Palace (French: Palais National; Haitian Creole: Palè nasyonal) was the official residence of the President of Haiti, located in Port-au-Prince, facing Place L’Ouverture near the Champs de Mars. It was severely damaged during a devastating earthquake in 2010.
