As per the health and family welfare department, which state stands in first place in establishing Health & Wellness Centres?

(A) West Bengal

(B) ** Karnataka

(C) Kerala

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1 state in establishing Health & Wellness Centres: Health dept.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which state has been ranked first in setting up Health and Wellness Centres under Ayushman Bharat Program in 2020-21? Notes: Karnataka has been ranked first in establishing Health and Wellness Centres under Ayushman Bharat Program in 2020-21.

Concept note-3: -Karnataka stands first in establishing health and wellness centres (HWC) under the Ayushman Bharat programme to provide comprehensive primary health care in rural areas.

Concept note-4: -Karnataka has ranked number one in the registration of health professionals and is in the second position in the registration of health facilities in the entire country under the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM). Health and Medical Education Minister K.
