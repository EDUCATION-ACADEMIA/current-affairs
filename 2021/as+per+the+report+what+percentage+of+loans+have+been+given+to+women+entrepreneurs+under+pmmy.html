As per the report, What percentage of loans have been given to Women Entrepreneurs under PMMY?

(A) 66%

(B) 50%

(C) ** 68%

(D) 70%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana is a Government of India scheme, which enables a small borrower to borrow from banks, MFIs, NBFCs for loans upto 10 lakh for non farm income generating activities. Generally, loans upto ‘ 10 lakh issued by banks under Micro Small Enterprises is given without collaterals.

Concept note-2: -Mudra. Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY) provides loans upto 10 lakhs to the non-corporate, non-farm small/micro enterprises. Watch some of the achievers from all over India tell their stories, in the language of your choice…

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY) Shishu : covering loans upto 50, 000/- Kishor : covering loans above 50, 000/- and upto 5 lakh. Tarun : covering loans above 5 lakh and upto 10 lakh.
