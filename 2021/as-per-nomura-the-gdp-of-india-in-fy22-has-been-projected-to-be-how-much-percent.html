As per Nomura, the GDP of India in FY22 has been projected to be how much percent?

(A) 12.5%

(B) 11.5%

(C) 10.5%

(D) ** 13.5%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -GDP growth for 2022-23 is projected at 6.6 per cent.

Concept note-2: -The International Monetary Fund (IMF) has also lowered India’s gross domestic product (GDP) growth projection for 2022-23 to 6.8% from the earlier 7.4%, citing the impact of a slowing global economy, stubborn inflation, rising interest rates and the Ukraine war.

Concept note-3: -Real GDP growth is estimated at 7.0 per cent in 2022-23, according to the first advance estimate of the National Statistical Office (NSO).
