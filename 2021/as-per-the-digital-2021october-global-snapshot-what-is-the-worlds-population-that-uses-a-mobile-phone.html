As per the ‘Digital 2021:October Global Snapshot”, what is the world’s population that uses a mobile phone?

(A) 45%

(B) ** 65%

(C) 50%

(D) 52%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Click here to subscribe for free.) Global mobile phone users reached 5.29 billion in October, the firm noted in a report titled ‘Digital 2021: October Global Snapshot”.

Concept note-2: -Globally, 40% of the world’s population owned a smartphone by 2021.

Concept note-3: -Social media users increased by more than 400 million (+9.9 percent) over the past 12 months to reach 4.55 billion in October 2021.

Concept note-4: -Latest figures show that an estimated 5.3 billion people of the earth’s 8 billion are using the Internet in 2022, or roughly 66 per cent of the world’s population. At the same time, three quarters of the population aged 10 years and over own a mobile phone.

Concept note-5: -But out of the world population numbering nearly 7.87 billion as of August 2021 how many people are online? Our estimations show that (as of August 2021) there are nearly 4.82 billion people online around the world and this figure is expected to reach about 5.14 billion by January 2023.
