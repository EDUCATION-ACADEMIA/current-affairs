Assam Govt. & Survey India has signed a Mou for Implementation of which scheme?

(A) ** Svmitva Scheme

(B) Boneej Scheme

(C) Kalpataru Scheme

(D) Svayem Scheme

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Assam. The Assam government has signed an MoU with the Survey of India for implementing the Svamitva scheme in the state.

Concept note-2: -Chief Minister Dr Himanta Biswa Sarma on January 13, 2022 launched Swanirbhar Naari, an ambitious scheme aimed at making the weavers of Assam self-reliant.

Concept note-3: -Under its new tribal land policy, the Assam government would allow a tribal land owner to hold 50 bighas of land, up from the existing eight bighas.
