Autistic Pride Day is observed annually all over the world on which day?

(A) ** 18 June

(B) 17 June

(C) 19 June

(D) 16 June

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Autistic Pride Day is a pride celebration for autistic people held on 18 June each year. Autistic pride recognises the importance of pride for autistic people and its role in bringing about positive changes in the broader society.

Concept note-2: -Autistic Pride Day is celebrated on June 18 every year across the world to raise awareness about Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) and to move society away from discrimination and stigma associated with the condition. A significant aspect of Autistic Pride Day is that it is run by people with autism.

Concept note-3: -To raise awareness about Autism, every year June 18 is observed as Autism Pride Day.

Concept note-4: -AUTISTIC PRIDE DAY - June 18 - National Day Calendar.

Concept note-5: -This 2022 year, World Autistic Pride Day theme is “Inclusion in the Workplace: Challenges and Opportunities in a Post-Pandemic World, ".
