BC Patnaik has taken over as the Managing Director of which company?

(A) IRDA

(B) New Indian Insurance

(C) Tata

(D) ** Life Insurance Corporation of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -B C Patnaik has taken charge as the Managing Director (MD) of Life Insurance Corporation (LIC).

Concept note-2: -Managing Director of LIC of India Shri B.C. Patnaik has taken charge as Managing Director on 1st October 2021. Shri B.C. Patnaik is a postgraduate in Political Science and a Fellow of the Insurance Institute of India. He joined LIC in 1986 as a Direct Recruit Officer.

Concept note-3: -1956: 245 Indian and foreign insurers and provident societies are taken over by the central government and nationalised. LIC formed by an Act of Parliament, viz. LIC Act, 1956, with a capital contribution of Rs. 5 crore from the Government of India.
