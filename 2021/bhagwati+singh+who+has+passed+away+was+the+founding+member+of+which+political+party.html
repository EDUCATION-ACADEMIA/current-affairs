Bhagwati Singh, who has passed away, was the founding member of which political party?

(A) Indian National Congress

(B) ** Samajwadi Party

(C) BJP

(D) Indian National Congress

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bhagwati Singh (1932/3 – 4 April 2021) was a politician and founder of the Samajwadi Party and a member of the Parliament of India representing Uttar Pradesh in the Rajya Sabha from 2004 to 2010 and a member of the Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly from 1977 until 2003.

Concept note-2: -The Samajwadi Party is now led by former Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh Akhilesh Yadav after he was chosen the President by the National Convention held on 1 January, 2017.

Concept note-3: -Though PSP president Shivpal Singh Yadav has announced to fight the 2022 assembly election on the bicycle, the symbol of Samajwadi Party.

Concept note-4: -He is the son of Late Mulayam Singh Yadav, a veteran Indian politician and the founder of Samajwadi Party who served as Minister of Defence, Government of India and three term Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh.
