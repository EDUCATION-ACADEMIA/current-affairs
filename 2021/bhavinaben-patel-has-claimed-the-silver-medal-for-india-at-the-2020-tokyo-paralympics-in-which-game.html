Bhavinaben Patel has claimed the silver medal for India at the 2020 Tokyo Paralympics in which game?

(A) High Jump

(B) Badminton

(C) ** Table Tennis

(D) Shooting

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Congratulations to #BhavinaPatel for creating history by winning India’s first silver medal in women’s singles class 4 table tennis event at the ongoing #TokyoParalympics .

Concept note-2: -Bhavinaben became the first Indian table tennis player to win a Paralympic medal – silver – as she went down to Zhang Miao of China in the summit clash.

Concept note-3: -India’s Nishad Kumar equalled his own personal best, also an Asian record, with a 2.06m jump and claimed the silver medal in the men’s high jump T47 event at the Tokyo Paralympics.

Concept note-4: -Bhavina Hasmukhbhai Patel is an Indian parathlete and table tennis player from Mehsana, Gujarat.

Concept note-5: -Tokyo Paralympics: Avani Lekhara becomes first Indian woman to win 2 medals at single Games edition - India Today.
