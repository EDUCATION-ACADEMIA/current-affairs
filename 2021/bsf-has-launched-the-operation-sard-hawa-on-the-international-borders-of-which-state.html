BSF has launched the Operation Sard Hawa on the international borders of which state?

(A) Himachal Pradesh

(B) Sikkim

(C) Punjab

(D) ** Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BSF (Border Security Force) had launched Operation Sard Hawa along Rajasthan’s western border. It is a week-long operation and was launched on 21 January 2021 and continued till 27 January 2021.

Concept note-2: -India’s first line of defence ‘Border Security Force’ has planned to bolster its surveillance along the Pakistan border in Rajasthan under its “Operation Sard Hawa” from January 23 to 28.

Concept note-3: -The Border Security Force, BSF launches Operation Sard Hawa. Under the operation, the BSF increases the surveillance in the Pakistan border. It is usually launched in the Rajasthan border, especially in the Jaisalmer region.

Concept note-4: -The Border Security Force will increase surveillance along the Pakistan border in Rajasthan under its “Operation Sard Hawa” from January 23 to 28.

Concept note-5: -Therefore, the India’s border with Pakistan is protected by the Border Security Forces. Q. The is a Border Guarding Force of India that guards India’s borders with Pakistan and Bangladesh.
