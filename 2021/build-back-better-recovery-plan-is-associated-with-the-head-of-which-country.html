Build Back Better recovery plan is associated with the Head of which country?

(A) New Zealand

(B) ** USA

(C) Japan

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Build Back Better Plan or Build Back Better agenda was a legislative framework proposed by U.S. president Joe Biden between 2020 and 2021.

Concept note-2: -The framework includes a separate $100 billion investment in immigration reform that is consistent with the Senate’s reconciliation rules, as well as enhancements to reduce backlogs, expand legal representation, and make the asylum system and border processing more efficient and humane.

Concept note-3: -Citation: Act of April 3, 1948, European Recovery Act [Marshall Plan]; Enrolled Acts and Resolutions of Congress, 1789-1996; General Records of the United States Government; Record Group 11; National Archives. On April 3, 1948, President Truman signed the Economic Recovery Act of 1948.
