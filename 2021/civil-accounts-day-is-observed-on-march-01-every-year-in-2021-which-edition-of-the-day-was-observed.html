Civil Accounts Day is observed on March 01 every year. In 2021, which edition of the day was observed?

(A) 43rd

(B) ** 45th

(C) 41st

(D) 47th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 45th Civil Accounts Day was celebrated in New Delhi on 1 March 2021. Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman was the Chief Guest of the event.

Concept note-2: -The 46th Civil Accounts Day will be celebrated on 2nd March 2022 at Dr. Ambedkar International Centre, Janpath, New Delhi. Minister for Finance & Corporate Affairs Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman will be the Chief Guest.

Concept note-3: -Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: The 46th Civil Accounts Day has celebrated on March 02, 2022, at Dr Ambedkar International Centre, Janpath, New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -The Civil Accounts Day is annually celebrated across India by the Indian Civil Accounts Service (ICAS), one of the Civil Services of India under the Department of Expenditure.
