‘Clean India Programme’ has been inaugurated from which state?

(A) Bihar

(B) West Bengal

(C) ** Uttar Pradesh

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is 2nd October 2014. About the Clean India Mission : It is also known as Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. The Swachh Bharat Abhiyan was launched on 2nd October 2014 at Rajghat in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -If India will be clean, it will go green. Green India to keep its environment clean. Give your clean choice a loud voice to keep India clean. Keep India clean to let it always green.

Concept note-3: -Clean India and Green India are national programmes aimed at improving the country. In 2014, a campaign called “Clean India” was started-Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan or the Clean India Movement were the other names for this campaign. The programme was launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi by cleaning the streets.

Concept note-4: -On 2nd October 2014, Swachh Bharat Mission was launched throughout length and breadth of the country as a national movement. The campaign aims to achieve the vision of a ‘Clean India’ by 2nd October 2019. The Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is the most significant cleanliness campaign by the Government of India.

Concept note-5: -Tips for keeping India clean; ● Waste or garbage should not be thrown anywhere, but only in trash bins. Controlling the use of plastic bags. Use recycled and eco-friendly products like paper or jute bags. Don’t litter public places.
