Coal Miners Day is observed every year on which day in India?

(A) May 2

(B) May 5

(C) ** May 4

(D) May 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Miners Day was established in 2009 to honor miners. The date was chosen to acknowledge the Monongah mining disaster, the worst single loss-of-life event due to mining in American history. On December 6, 1907, at least 362 West Virginia coal miners were killed in a catastrophic explosion.

Concept note-2: -Miners’ Day was established to celebrate and recognize those who take part in this dangerous profession, to help our lives be comfortable and full of conveniences.

Concept note-3: -COAL MINERS’ DAY 2022: The dependence on coal for energy needs is rising across the planet.
