DBT-NIBMG, has created the world’s first database of genomic variants named “dbGENVOC”, in which disease?

(A) ** Oral Cancer

(B) Tuberculosis

(C) Covid 19

(D) Malaria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -dbGENVOC is a browsable online database of GENomic Variants of Oral Cancer and is a free resource.

Concept note-2: -dbGENVOC, a comprehensive, flexible database framework, developed with an aim to allow potential users to access, query, browse and download clinically relevant somatic and germline variation data from Indian oral cancer patients.

Concept note-3: -The Database of Genomic Variants archive (DGVa) is a repository that provides archiving, accessioning and distribution of publicly available genomic structural variants, in all species.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is DBT-National Institute of Biomedical Genomics.

Concept note-5: -NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF BIOMEDICAL GENOMICS (NIBMG)
