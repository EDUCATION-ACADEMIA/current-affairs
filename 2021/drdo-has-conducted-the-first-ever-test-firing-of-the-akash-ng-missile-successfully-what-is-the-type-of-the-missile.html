DRDO has conducted the first-ever test firing of the Akash-NG missile successfully. What is the type of the missile?

(A) Surface-to-Surface

(B) Air-to-Surface

(C) ** Surface-to-Air

(D) Air-to-Air

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Surface-to-Air . Akash Missile (Akash-NG): It is a surface-to-air Missile.

Concept note-2: -The Akash missile system can target aircraft up to 50 km (31 mi) away. It has the capability to neutralise aerial targets like fighter jets, cruise missiles and air-to-surface missiles as well as ballistic missiles. It is in operational service with the Indian Army and the Indian Air Force.

Concept note-3: -Akash - New generation (IAST: Ākāśa “Sky") abbreviated as Akash-NG is a medium-range mobile surface-to-air missile defense system developed by the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and produced by Bharat Dynamics Limited and Bharat Electronics.

Concept note-4: -Akash Prime: The next version after Akash-1s was Akash Prime whose test launch was done on 27 September 2021. This missile intercepted and destroyed an unmanned mimicking aircraft aerially.

Concept note-5: -The Indian armed forces successfully test fired the helicopter-launched Nag Missile (HELINA), now named Dhruvastra anti-tank guided missile in direct and top attack mode that is upgraded with new features.
