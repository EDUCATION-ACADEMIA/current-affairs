For the first time, ISRO tested satellites made by two Indian startups. One of the satellites was developed by Pixxel the other satellite was developed by which firm?

(A) Dhruva Space

(B) Skyroot Aerospace

(C) ** SpaceKidz India

(D) Earth2Orbit (E2O)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One of the satellites was developed by Pixxel the other satellite was developed by which firm? Explanation: The ISRO has tested satellites made by two Indian startups. One of the satellites was developed by the Pixxel the other satellite was developed by SpaceKidz India.

Concept note-2: -India has three active operational launch vehicles: Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV), Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle (GSLV), Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle Mk-III (LVM3).

Concept note-3: -Aryabhat was India’s first satellite, named after the famous Indian astronomer. It was launched on 19 April 1975 from Kapustin Yar, a Soviet rocket launch and development site in Astrakhan Oblast using a Kosmos-3M launch vehicle.

Concept note-4: -Aryabhata launched on 19.04. 1975 was the first Indian satellite. Bhaskara-I launched on 07.06. 1979 was the first experimental remote sensing satellite.

Concept note-5: -Aryabhata, first unmanned Earth satellite built by India. It was named for a prominent Indian astronomer and mathematician of the 5th century ce. The satellite was assembled at Peenya, near Bangalore, but was launched from within the Soviet Union by a Russian-made rocket on April 19, 1975.
