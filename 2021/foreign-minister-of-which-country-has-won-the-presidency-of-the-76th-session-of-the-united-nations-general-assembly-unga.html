Foreign Minister of which country has won the Presidency of the 76th Session of the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA)?

(A) Nepal

(B) Belgium

(C) ** Maldives

(D) Chile

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 7 June 2021, the United Nations General Assembly elected Abdulla Shahid of Maldives as President of its seventy-sixth session, which runs from September 2021 until September 2022. (See Press Release GA/12332.) Mr.

Concept note-2: -The 76th session of the UN General Assembly (UNGA 76) opened on Tuesday, 14 September 2021. On that day, Abdulla Shahid of the Maldives, was sworn in as the General Assembly President and he opened the 76th session.

Concept note-3: -Abdulla Shahid (Dhivehi: ; born 26 May 1962) is Maldivian politician who served as President of the 76th United Nations General Assembly between 2021 and 2022. Shahid is the first Maldivian politician to hold that post. He has been serving as Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maldives since 2018.

Concept note-4: -President of the 77th Session of United Nations General Assembly H.E. Mr. Csaba Korosi calls on PM. The President of the 77th Session of the United Nations General Assembly (PGA), H.E. Mr. Csaba Korosi called on Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi today.
