Geeta Jayanti is being celebrated on which date?

(A) December 12

(B) ** December 14

(C) December 15

(D) December 13

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This time according to Geeta jayanti 2022 date, Geeta Jayanti will be celebrated on 3 December 2022 and Mokshada Ekadashi will also be celebrated.

Concept note-2: -Gita Jayanti is the day when Lord Shri Krishna explained the Gita Gyan to Arjuna. It is observed on Ekadashi tithi (11th day) of Shukla Paksha in the month of Margashirsha.

Concept note-3: -Followers of Lord Krishna also observe fast on this day. Gita Jayanti 2022 Date: December 3, Saturday. Ekadasi Tithi Begins: December 03, 2022 5:39 AM. Ekadasi Tithi Ends: December 04, 2022 5:34 AM.

Concept note-4: -Bhagavad-gita literally means the song of the Supreme Lord. It is the most widely known Vedic literature in the world. This year we celebrated Gita Jayanti on December 03, 2022.
