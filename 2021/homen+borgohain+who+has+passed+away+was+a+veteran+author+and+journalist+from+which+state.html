[SOURCES]

Homen Borgohain, who has passed away, was a veteran author and journalist from which state?

(A) ** Assam

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Jammu & Kashmir

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He is married to Nirupama Borgohain, one of the most celebrated writers and an exponent of early feminist writings in Assam. They have two sons Aditya and Pradipta Borgohain. Nirupama has also won the Sahitya Akademi Award for her novel “Abhijatri”.

Concept note-2: -The novel Astarag is authored by ? Explanation: Homen Borgohain is an Assamese writer and journalist.

Concept note-3: -Although Harakanta Sarmah Mazindra Boruah’s Autobiography is the first among all other written Assamese Autobiography, but Hem Chandra Boruah’s “Atmajeen Sarit” is considered to be the first among all published Autobiographies.

Concept note-4: -A Sahitya Akademi winner for the novel Pita Putra, Borgohain, who, along with his wife Nirupama, brought out in 1971 the first Assamese jointly-written novel, is also an esteemed journalist and a keen observer of the Assamese society.
