How many Indian institutions has made it to top 100 in 2021 QS World University Rankings by Subject?

(A) ** 12

(B) 15

(C) 17

(D) 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thirty-five Indian programmes have achieved top-100 positions in the Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) World University Rankings By Subject 2022 -ten more than in 2021’s edition.

Concept note-2: -QS World Ranking: No Indian Institution in top 100, Only Three amongst 200.

Concept note-3: -In a big achievement, around 19 Indian universities featured in the top 200 list of QS Asia Universities Ranking 2023 released on Tuesday.

Concept note-4: -The QS rankings cover a total of 51 disciplines, grouped into five broad subject areas: Arts and Humanities. Engineering and Technology. Life sciences and Medicine.
