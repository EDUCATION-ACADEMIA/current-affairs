How many persons are part of the crew under the Shenzhou-13 mission of China?

(A) 5

(B) ** 3

(C) 4

(D) 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 9 December 2021 at 3:55 p.m. Beijing time (7:55 a.m. UTC), the three crew members held a lesson and conducted the scientific experiments from space, as they did in 2013 at Shenzhou 10, to educate, motivate and inspire Chinese younger generation in science and technology for the long term development of the nation.

Concept note-2: -Shenzhou 13 was the second crewed mission to Tianhe, which launched to low Earth orbit in April 2021. The first was Shenzhou 12, which lifted off in June 2021 and landed that October.

Concept note-3: -Tiangong, which means heavenly palace, is smaller than the International Space Station but similar in its modular design.
