How much amount of financial support was given by the NABARD to West Bengal for the development of agriculture and allied sectors?

(A) 9235 cr

(B) ** 9162 cr

(C) 9355 cr

(D) 9547 cr

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NABARD is a Development Bank with a mandate for providing and regulating credit for the development of agriculture, small-scale industries, cottage and village industries, handicrafts and other allied economic activities in rural areas to promote prosperity of rural areas.

Concept note-2: -NABARD provides refinance assistance under Automatic Refinance Facility (ARF) to eligible banks for a wide spectrum of manufacturing, processing and service sector activities under RNFS (Investment Credit).
