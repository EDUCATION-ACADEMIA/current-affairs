How much amount was approved by governing body of Ladakh “Yountan Society” for Samagra Shiksha in UT?

(A) 170.25 cr

(B) 181 cr

(C) ** 167.25 cr

(D) 185.0 cr

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Education Budget: Outlay for SSA In August 2021, the union cabinet approved the continuation of Samagra Shiksha scheme for five more years, till FY 2025- 26. The total financial outlay of the scheme was set as Rs 2, 94, 283 crore, of which GoI’s share is Rs 1, 85, 398 crore.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Human Resource Development is implementing the Scheme of Vocationalisation of School Education under the umbrella of Samagra Shiksha.

Concept note-3: -The literacy rate of the district records as 65.30% as per 2001 census.
