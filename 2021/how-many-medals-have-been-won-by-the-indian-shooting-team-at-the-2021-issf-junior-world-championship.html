How many medals have been won by the Indian Shooting team at the 2021 ISSF Junior World Championship?

(A) 31

(B) 52

(C) 42

(D) ** 43

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India finished the tournament with eight medals including three golds, four silvers and one bronze and was way ahead of hosts Korea and Serbia in the medal tally.

Concept note-2: -Indian junior shooters were on rampage claiming as many as four more gold medals at the ISSF World Championship here on Tuesday. The four medals swelled India’s tally to 20 medals including nine gold, three silver and eight bronze medals.

Concept note-3: -Indian shooters concluded their campaign on top with a total of 33 medals at the ISSF Junior World Cup 2022 in Suhl, Germany this week. India’s tally of 33 medals - 13 golds, 15 silvers and five bronzes - was the most by any country at the 2022 edition.

Concept note-4: -Olympic silver medallist Vijay Kumar (581) and Anish Bhanwala (575) placed 13th and 28th respectively. India is second in the medals table with 12 gold, nine silver and 13 bronze medals while China sits on top with 25 gold, 16 silver and 14 bronze medals.
