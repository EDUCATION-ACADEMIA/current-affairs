How many states have completed the ONORC system reform?

(A) ** 11

(B) 12

(C) 8

(D) 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Till date, 32 states and Union Territories have joined the ONORC, covering about 69 crore NFSA beneficiaries. Four states are yet to join the scheme - Assam, Chhattisgarh, Delhi and West Bengal.

Concept note-2: -The One Nation One Ration Card (ONORC) plan, for nation-wide portability of the National Food Security Act 2013 (NFSA) beneficiaries is presently enabled in all the 36 States/UTs across the country, covering entire NFSA population (around 80 Crore NFSA beneficiaries) in the country.

Concept note-3: -With this, the ONORC plan is successfully implemented in all the 36 states/UTs, making food security portable throughout the country.

Concept note-4: -With this, the ONORC plan is successfully implemented in all 36 states, and Union Territories, making food security portable throughout the country.

Concept note-5: -Govt Ambitious: One Nation One Ration Card(ONORC) Completes 3 years. The scheme has now been implemented all over the country with Assam being the latest state to join the initiative in June 2022.
