How much amount has been approved by the World Bank to improve management capacity and quality of health services in Mizoram?

(A) ** USD 32 million

(B) USD 78 million

(C) USD 54 million

(D) USD 96 million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Government of India signs $32 million loan with World Bank for improving healthcare services in Mizoram.

Concept note-2: -WASHINGTON, DC, September, 21, 2022 - The World Bank’s Executive Board of Directors today approved a $350 million loan to the western Indian state of Gujarat to improve the quality and access of its public healthcare services, with special focus on adolescent girls and disease surveillance.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI, October 28, 2021 – The Government of India, the Government of Meghalaya and the World Bank today signed a $40 million health project for the north-east state of Meghalaya.

Concept note-4: -World Bank Approves $750 Million Loan to India to Catalyze Private Financing and Support Economic Growth.
