How much was India’s retail inflation in June 2021?

(A) 6.24 percent

(B) 6.25 percent

(C) ** 6.26 percent

(D) 6.27 percent

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Retail inflation eases to 6.71% in July; IIP grows 12.3% in June - Times of India.

Concept note-2: -"Year-on-year inflation for the month stood at 6.16 per cent compared to 6.97 per cent for the previous month (May 2022) and 5.57 per cent during the corresponding month (June 2021) a year before, ” a labour ministry statement said.

Concept note-3: -India’s retail inflation has been above RBI’s upper tolerance limit of 6% for eight consecutive months. India’s retail inflation inched up to 7% in August from 6.71% in July mainly due to higher food prices, government data showed on Monday.

Concept note-4: -RPI inflation was 3.9% in June 2021 (Index: 304.0), up from 3.3% in the year to May 2021.
