IBM Corporation has launched a client innovation centre in which city?

(A) Aurangabad

(B) ** Mysuru

(C) Dehradun

(D) Bhopal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IBM Consulting will now operate from eleven CIC locations in India, including Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Mumbai, National Capital Region, Pune, Mysuru, Kochi and Coimbatore. IBM is a leading provider of global hybrid cloud and AI, and consulting expertise.

Concept note-2: -The IBM Client Innovation Center (CIC) is an innovative and exciting part of IBM, working as IBM Global Business Services’ technical delivery partner. We continually innovate for our customers, employees and shareholders.

Concept note-3: -IBM Consulting, the consulting arm of IBM Corp., on Thursday launched client innovation centres (CICs) in Kochi and Coimbatore, India.
