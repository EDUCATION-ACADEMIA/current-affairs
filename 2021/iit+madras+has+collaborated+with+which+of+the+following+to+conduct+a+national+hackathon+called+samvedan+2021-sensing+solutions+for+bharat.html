[SOURCES]

IIT Madras has collaborated with which of the following to conduct a National hackathon called ‘SAMVEDAN 2021-Sensing Solutions for Bharat’?

(A) Samsung

(B) ** Sony

(C) Vivo

(D) Facebook

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The IIT Madras Pravartak Technologies Foundation (IITM-PTF) is collaborating with Sony India Software Centre Pvt. Ltd. to conduct a National hackathon called ‘SAMVEDAN 2021 – Sensing Solutions for Bharat’ to inspire citizens to solve India-specific problems of societal interest using IoT sensor board.

Concept note-2: -A National hackathon called ‘SAMVEDAN 2021 - Sensing Solutions for Bharat’ is being organized jointly by IIT Madras Pravartak Technologies Foundation (IITM-PTF), along with Sony India Software Centre, and is based on the Sony Semiconductor Solutions Corporation’s SPRESENSE™ Board.

Concept note-3: -IIT Madras Pravartak Technologies Foundation (IITM-PTF) and Sony India Software Centre Pvt Ltd have joined hands to organise a national-level hackathon named ‘SAMVEDAN 2021 – Sensing Solutions for Bharat’.
