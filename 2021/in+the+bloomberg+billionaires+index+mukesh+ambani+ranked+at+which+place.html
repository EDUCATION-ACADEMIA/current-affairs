(A) ** 12th

(B) 10th

(C) 8th

(D) 6th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After that comes the number of America, under The World billionaires 2023, there are about 696 billionaires in America and the third number comes after America, according to which Mukesh Ambani is the richest person in India under The World billionaires 2023, but Mukesh Above Ambani is Gautam Adani.
