In which city, 7th edition of the India International Science Festival was held?

(A) Bangalore

(B) Noida

(C) ** Panaji

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Union Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES) launched the 7th edition of the India International Science Festival (IISF). This year, IISF will be held from December 10-13 in Panaji, Goa.

Concept note-2: -Madhya Pradesh Council of Science and Technology (MPCOST), Bhopal is the nodal agency for this science festival. More than 8, 000 delegates from across the country will participate in this science festival from January 21 to 24.

Concept note-3: -Curtains xame down on 4 day India International Science Festival, IISF-2022 in Bhopal with a call for making India one of the leading Scientific and Technological Powers of the World.

Concept note-4: -The Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh Shivraj Singh Chauhan and Union Minister for Science & Technology, Dr. Jitendra Singh today jointly inaugurated “India International Science Festival, IISF-2022” in Bhopal.

Concept note-5: -The 8th edition of the India International Science Festival (IISF) was inaugurated at the Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT), Bhopal, on January 21, 2023.
