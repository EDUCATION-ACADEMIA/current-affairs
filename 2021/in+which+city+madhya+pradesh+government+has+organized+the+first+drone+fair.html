In which city, Madhya Pradesh government has organized the first drone fair?

(A) Indore

(B) ** Gwalior

(C) Neemuch

(D) Jabalpur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first drone fair in the country was organised last year in the month of December in Gwalior city of Madhya Pradesh that witnessed the biggest congregation of drone manufacturers, service providers, drone enthusiasts and user communities, especially students, farmers and common man of the city.

Concept note-2: -The Drone Fair was organised jointly by the Ministry of Civil Aviation, the Government of Madhya Pradesh and industry body the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI).

Concept note-3: -Madhya Pradesh government organises first drone fair at Gwalior.

Concept note-4: -Varuna is the product of Pune-based Sagar Defence Engineering. (Image credit: Screengrab from video tweeted by ANI) India’s first passenger drone, built by Pune-based Sagar Defence Engineering, will soon be inducted into the Indian Navy.

Concept note-5: -Key Points. On this occasion, Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan, while addressing the youth and farmers present at ‘Drone Mela’ in Gwalior, said that Madhya Pradesh will be made a leading state by using drone technology in the field of development and welfare.
