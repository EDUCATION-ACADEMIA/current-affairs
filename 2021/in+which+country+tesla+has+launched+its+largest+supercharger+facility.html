In which country, Tesla has launched its largest supercharger facility?

(A) India

(B) USA

(C) ** China

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tesla has installed its 10, 000th supercharger for electric vehicle charging in China at Shanghai yesterday.

Concept note-2: -Currently, the largest charging station in the US is a 70-space Tesla Supercharger hub at The Outlets at Tejon, an open-air retail center in the San Joaquin Valley in central California.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 72. Tesla launched the world’s largest Supercharger station in China’s Shanghai with 72 charging stalls in January 2021.

Concept note-4: -Tesla Opens 10, 000th Supercharger In China At The Most Iconic Location In Shanghai. With the launch of supercharger V4 and the expansion of the charging network to none Tesla EVs, 2022 has been a great year for Tesla’s supercharger network.

Concept note-5: -Tesla is rolling out another 420 electric vehicle Supercharger units across its home state of California as part of four new charging facilities, including what could be the world’s largest so far, with a total of 164 stalls.
