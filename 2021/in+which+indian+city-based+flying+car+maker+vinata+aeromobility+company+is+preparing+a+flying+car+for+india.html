In which Indian city-based flying car maker Vinata Aeromobility Company is preparing a flying car for India?

(A) ** Chennai

(B) Delhi

(C) Kolkata

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the other hand, Chennai-based Vinata Aeromobility is developing a fully autonomous flying car, which will be Asia’s first hybrid flying car, and one that will use biofuels.

Concept note-2: -Alef flying car revealed with 177 km flying range: Expected to be launched in 2025.

Concept note-3: -The prototype of the flying car was displayed by Pal-V a few years ago in 2012. Since 2015 it had been going through aviation certification with European Aviation Safety Agency. The certification is expected by 2022 only.

Concept note-4: -VTOL flying car classification: The flying car has fixed wings on four sides and it can hover, take off, and land vertically. Its co-axial quad-rotor system draws energy from eight BLDC motors that come with eight fixed pitch propellers.

Concept note-5: -The car is capable of achieving a flight time of 60 minutes and can go as high as 3, 000 feet. The company Vinata Aeromobility has said that their Hybrid Powered flying car is sustainable and uses Bio Fuel.
