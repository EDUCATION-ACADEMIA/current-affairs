In which the state of India, the world’s first-ever genetically modified rubber plant has been planted?

(A) Bihar

(B) ** Assam

(C) Kerala

(D) Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -First-ever genetically modified rubber planted in Assam.

Concept note-2: -Recently, the world’s first Genetically Modified (GM) rubber plant developed by Rubber Research Institute was planted in Assam.

Concept note-3: -First genetically modified plant commercially released in India is Bt cotton. This has been developed by MAHYCO (Maharashtra Hybrid Seeds Company) in collaboration with American company, Monsanto.

Concept note-4: -The first genetically modified plant (GMP) was a tobacco resistant to antibiotics in 1983. In 1996, the first genetically altered crop, a delayed-ripening tomato was commercially released.

Concept note-5: -Rubber Board has started field trial of world’s first GM (genetically modified) rubber in the state of Assam.
