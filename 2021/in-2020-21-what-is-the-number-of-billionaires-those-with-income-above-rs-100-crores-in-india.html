In 2020-21, what is the Number of billionaires (those with income above Rs. 100 crores) in India?

(A) 133

(B) 150

(C) ** 136

(D) 141

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The pandemic period – March 2020 to November 2022, has been particularly profitable for Indian big business with the number of billionaires in India increasing from 102 in 2020 to 166 in 2022.

Concept note-2: -Oxfam India’s latest report which was released on the opening day of the World Economic Forum in Davos, Switzerland reveals that the total number of billionaires in India increased from 102 in 2020 to 166 billionaires in 2022.
