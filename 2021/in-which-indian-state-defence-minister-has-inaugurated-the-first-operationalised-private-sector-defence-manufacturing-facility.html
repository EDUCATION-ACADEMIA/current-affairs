In which Indian State, Defence Minister has inaugurated the first operationalised private sector defence manufacturing facility?

(A) ** Uttar Pradesh

(B) Manipur

(C) Goa

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh inaugurated the first operationalised private sector defence manufacturing facility in Uttar Pradesh Defence Industrial Corridor (UPDIC) in Lucknow on November 13, 2021.

Concept note-2: -In addition to these major defence facilities, Maharashtra is also home to several important defence manufacturing units. The state has a significant presence of defence public sector units (DPSUs), including Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) and Bharat Dynamics Limited (BDL), which are both headquartered in Mumbai.

Concept note-3: -Uttar Pradesh got its first operationalised private sector defence manufacturing facility on 14 November 2021. It was inaugurated in Lucknow by Union Defence Minister Rajnath Singh.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi: Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone of C-295 transport aircraft manufacturing facility - the country’s first in the private sector - in Vadodara, Gujarat on October 30, 2022.
