In which state Dr. Rameshwar Oraon has presented the state budget for the year 2021-22?

(A) Gujarat

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Bihar

(D) ** Jharkhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Finance Minister of Jharkhand, Dr. Rameshwar Oraon, presented the Budget for the state for the financial year 2021-22 on March 3, 2021.

Concept note-2: -The Union Minister for Finance & Corporate Affairs, Smt Nirmala Sitharaman presented the Union Budget 2021-22 in Parliament today, which is the first budget of this new decade and also a digital one in the backdrop of unprecedented COVID-19 crisis.

Concept note-3: -The Uttar Pradesh government spent ₹2, 06, 754.34 lakh crore during the same six-month period. Its expenditure is 33.54% of the annual budget (about ₹6.15 lakh crore) for 2022-2023. It had spent ₹1, 77, 918.13 lakh crore during the same six-month period in 2021-22.

Concept note-4: -The Finance Minister of Bihar Mr. Tarakishore Prasad presented the Budget for Bihar for the financial year 2022-23. The 2022-23 budget amount is Rs 2, 37, 691.19 crore (more than Rs. 19, 388.19 crores of FY 2021-22) which makes it the biggest budget in the history of Bihar.
