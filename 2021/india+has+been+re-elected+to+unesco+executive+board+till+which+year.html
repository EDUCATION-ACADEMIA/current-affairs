India has been re-elected to UNESCO executive board till which year?

(A) 2024

(B) 2022

(C) ** 2025

(D) 2023

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On November 17, 2021, India was re-elected to the executive board of UNESCO (United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization) for the term of 2021-2025.

Concept note-2: -India has been elected as a member of the Intergovernmental Committee of UNESCO’s 2003 Convention for the Safeguarding of the Intangible Cultural Heritage for the 2022-2026 cycle.

Concept note-3: -What is the Executive Board? The Executive Board is one of the three constitutional organs of UNESCO (the others being the General Conference and the Secretariat) and it is elected by the General Conference.

Concept note-4: -India and France have been re-elected as President and Co-President of the International Solar Alliance (ISA).

Concept note-5: -India gets elected for the Intergovernmental Committee of #UNESCO’s 2003 Convention for the Safeguarding of the Intangible Cultural Heritage (ICH) for the 2022-2026 cycle. India has previously served as a member of the ICH Committee twice - from 2006 to 2010 & from 2014 to 2018.
