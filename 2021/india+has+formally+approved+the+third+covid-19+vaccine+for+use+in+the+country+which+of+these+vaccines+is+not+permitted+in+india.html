India has formally approved the third Covid-19 vaccine for use in the country. Which of these vaccines is not permitted in India?

(A) Covishield

(B) Sputnik V

(C) Covaxin

(D) ** Sinovac

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Central Government has approved two COVID-19 vaccinations, Covaxin (manufactured by Bharat Biotech Limited) and Covishield (Oxford-AstraZeneca’s vaccine manufactured by Serum Institute of India) for emergency use.

Concept note-2: -BBV154 COVID Vaccine is the first intranasal vaccine approved by DCGI for primary immunization against COVID-19 in the 18+ age group for restricted use in emergency situation being developed in the country under Mission COVID Suraksha and adds to our repertoire of COVID-19 vaccine.
