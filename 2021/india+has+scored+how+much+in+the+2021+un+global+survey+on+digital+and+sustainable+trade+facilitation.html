[SOURCES]

India has scored how much in the 2021 UN Global Survey on Digital and Sustainable Trade Facilitation?

(A) 90 percent

(B) 98 precent

(C) ** 90.32 percent

(D) 23 percent

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has scored 90.32% in United Nation’s Economic and Social Commission for Asia Pacific’s (UNESCAP ) latest Global Survey on Digital and Sustainable Trade Facilitation.

Concept note-2: -This report reviews the progress of trade facilitation reforms across 144 countries, based on the fourth UN Global Survey on Digital and Sustainable Trade Facilitation.

Concept note-3: -Global Survey on Digital and Sustainable Trade Facilitation - a survey carried out every two years since 2015 jointly by the five United Nations Regional Commissions (UNRECs) - ECA, ECE, ECLAC, ESCAP and ESCWA. The fifth Survey will be launched in later January 2023.

Concept note-4: -The fifth UN Global Survey on Digital and Sustainable Trade Facilitation was initiated in January 2023. Data will be collected, validated and verified from January to June 2023, and the results are planned to be published in July 2023.
