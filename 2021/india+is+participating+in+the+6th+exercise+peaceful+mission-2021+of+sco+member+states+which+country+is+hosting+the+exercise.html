CURRENT AFFAIRS-2021
[SOURCES]

India is participating in the 6th Exercise PEACEFUL MISSION-2021, of SCO member states Which country is hosting the exercise?

(A) ** Russia

(B) Australia

(C) India

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Russia. The 6th edition of Exercise Peaceful Mission is being hosted by Russia in the Orenburg Region of southwest Russia from 13-25 Sept 2021.

Concept note-2: -The 6th edition of exercise Peaceful Mission is being hosted by Russia in the Orenburg Region of South-West Russia from September 13 to 25. India has participated in a multi-nation counter-terror exercise in Russia’s Orenburg region that has been organised under the aegis of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO).

Concept note-3: -The Exercise Peaceful Mission: 2021 is based on joint counter terrorism operations at operational and tactical level in an urban environment in which Armies and Air Forces of all SCO member states are participating.

Concept note-4: -UPSC Mains Q. Which country hosted the 6th Edition of SCO Exercise Peaceful Mission 2021? Notes: The 6th Edition of SCO Exercise Peaceful Mission 2021 hosted by Russia started at Orenburg Region of South West Russia.

Concept note-5: -The Indian military contingent comprising of an all arms combined force of 200 personnel to include 38 personnel from the Indian Air Force is participating in the Exercise PEACEFUL MISSION -2021. The Indian contingent was inducted to the exercise area by two IL-76 aircrafts.
