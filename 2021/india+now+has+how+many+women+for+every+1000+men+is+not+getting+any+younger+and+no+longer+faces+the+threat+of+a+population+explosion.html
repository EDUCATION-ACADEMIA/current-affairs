India now has how many women for every 1000 men, is not getting any younger, and no longer faces the threat of a population explosion?

(A) ** 1, 020

(B) 1, 050

(C) 1, 030

(D) 1, 010

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India now has 1, 020 women for every 1000 men, is not getting any younger, and no longer faces the threat of a population explosion.

Concept note-2: -In the Population Census of 2011 it was revealed that the population ratio in India 2011 is 940 females per 1000 of males.

Concept note-3: -According to National Family Health Survey, 2020-21 (NFHS-5) India’s Sex ratio in 2022 is 1020 females per 1000 males. In the rural area, the Sex ratio is 1037, and in the urban areas, it’s 985 females to 1000 males.

Concept note-4: -Demographics of India 2022 Due to external migration, the population declined by 577, 298. The sex ratio of the total population was 1.068 (1, 068 males per 1, 000 females) which is higher than global sex ratio. The global sex ratio in the world was approximately 1, 016 males to 1, 000 females as of 2022.
