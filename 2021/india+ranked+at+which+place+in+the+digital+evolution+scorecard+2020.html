India ranked at which place in the Digital Evolution Scorecard 2020?

(A) 1st

(B) 3rd

(C) ** 4th

(D) 11th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Digital Evolution Scorecard 2020: India ranked 4th “Break Out Economies"; China Tops.

Concept note-2: -DIGITAL EVOLUTION SCORECARD 2020: INDIA RANKS 4TH IN ‘BREAK OUT ECONOMIES’ India has been ranked at the 4th position in the third edition of the “Break Out Economies” report. The result was released in the Digital Evolution Scorecard developed by Mastercard in partnership with Tufts University’s Fletcher School.

Concept note-3: -Rapidly digitalising India was ranked 4th in “Break Out Economies” in the third edition of the Digital Evolution Scorecard developed by Tufts University’s Fletcher School in partnership with Mastercard.

Concept note-4: -India, which is driving the global digital economy, has already clocked about 70 billion digital payment transactions in 2022-the highest in the world. This is a sharp increase from the corresponding figure of 44 billion in 2021, according to data collation portal Statista.
