[SOURCES]

India will be conducting the annual Malabar naval exercises with which other countries?

(A) Hungary, Japan, USA

(B) USA, Saudi Arabia, Japan

(C) USA, UAE, UK

(D) ** USA, Japan, Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Malabar series of exercises began in 1992 as a bilateral exercise between the navies of India and US and gained further prominence with joining of the navies of Australia and Japan.

Concept note-2: -Exercise Malabar is a naval exercise involving the United States, Japan and India as permanent partners.

Concept note-3: -India-U.S. Army exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas’ to take place end October in Uttarakhand. Japan will host the 2022 edition of the Malabar naval exercise consisting of India, Australia, Japan and the U.S. in the second week of November, according to official sources.

Concept note-4: -This Edition MILAN 22 will witness its largest ever participation, with more than 40 countries sending their warships/ high level delegations.
