India has developed its first indigenously App Store. What is the name of the app store?

(A) Seva Apps

(B) ** Mobile Seva

(C) Seva Mobile

(D) Indias Seva

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mobile Seva AppStore which is India’s first indigenously developed Appstore came into existence in year 2013 with the vision of serving government departments by being a one stop platform for hosting all government apps.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Mobile Seva Appstore. Mobile Seva Appstore is India’s first indigenously developed app store. Mobile Seva Appstore is a government-owned app store developed by the Department of Electronics and Information Technology (DeitY).

Concept note-3: -This programme was launched in 2011, by Department of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India. This programme includes a Mobile Applications Store that by December, 2013, contained 240 applications available free.

Concept note-4: -Mobile Seva is an innovative initiative launched by MeitY and implemented by CDAC to enable all Government departments and agencies at the Centre, State and Local levels in the country to deliver their services through various channels of mobile such as SMS, IVRS, USSD, Mobile Application and AppStore.
