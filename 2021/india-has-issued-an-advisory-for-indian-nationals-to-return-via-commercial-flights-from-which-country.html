India has issued an advisory for Indian nationals to return via commercial flights from which country?

(A) Pakistan

(B) Syria

(C) China

(D) ** Afghanistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA) facility became operational for citizens of India in 2014, with about 40 eligible countries. The list of eligible countries was next revised and expanded to 113 in 2015.

Concept note-2: -Scheduled international flight services have resumed from Sunday, March 27, 2022 after being suspended for two years due to COVID-19.

Concept note-3: -Air India, controlled by the multinational conglomerate Tata, is pushing forwards with its intentions to increase its domestic and international destinations. The airline announced that it will commence direct flights between Mumbai, New York, Paris, and Frankfurt in February 2023.

Concept note-4: -India is maintaining a ban on all international scheduled passenger flights for now. A number of domestic flights resumed from 25 May between cities.
