India has joined the ‘G20 Troika’ with Indonesia and Italy. India will host the G-20 leaders’ summit in which year?

(A) 2024

(B) ** 2023

(C) 2025

(D) 2022

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1st Tourism Working Group Meeting under G20 to start tomorrow at Rann of Kutch in Gujarat. The breathtaking white sands of Rann of Kutch in Gujarat will witness the congregation of representatives of Tourism Sector of G20 countries during 7-9 February 2023.

Concept note-2: -The 2023 G20 Delhi summit (Hindi: 2023 G20 ) is the upcoming eighteenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), a summit scheduled to take place in Pragati Maidan, New Delhi in 2023.

Concept note-3: -In a significant milestone, India assumed presidency of the G20 on 1st December, 2022. India is currently part of the G20 Troika comprising Indonesia, India and Brazil, marking the first time that the troika is consisting of three developing and emerging economies.

Concept note-4: -India holds the Presidency of the G20 from December 1, 2022 to November 30, 2023. The 43 Heads of Delegations- the largest ever in G20-will be participating in the final New Delhi Summit in September next year.
