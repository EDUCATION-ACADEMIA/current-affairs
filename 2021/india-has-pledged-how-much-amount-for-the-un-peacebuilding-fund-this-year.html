India has pledged how much amount for the UN Peacebuilding fund this year?

(A) ** USD 150, 000

(B) USD 100, 000

(C) USD 175, 000

(D) USD 200, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has contributed US$ 150, 000 to UN Democracy Fund (UNDEF) for 2022.

Concept note-2: -Strengthening its steadfast commitment to democratic ideals and values, India handed over a voluntary contribution of US $150, 000 to the UN Democracy Fund for the year 2022–23.

Concept note-3: -So far India has taken part in 43 Peacekeeping missions with a total contribution exceeding. India has contributed the most personnel to UN 2, 53, 000 troops and a significant number of police personnel having been deployed.

Concept note-4: -The Peacebuilding Commission (PBC) is an intergovernmental advisory body that supports peace efforts in conflict-affected countries and is a key addition to the capacity of the International Community in the broad peace agenda.
