India has qualified for the ICC World Test Championship Final. India will face which country’s cricket team?

(A) Australia

(B) ** New Zealand

(C) England

(D) West Indies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Here’s how India can qualify for the finals of WTC – India is currently placed second on the WTC points table with a PCT of 58.93 and if they beat Australia 3-1 or better, they will qualify for the finals regardless of the results from other series.

Concept note-2: -India also have their fate in their own hands, but have far less margin for error, with a defeat in South Africa, a drawn series in England, and a draw in a home Test against New Zealand counting against them. If they win 3-1 or better, they will be assured of a place in the final.
