India has signed an agreement with which country for cooperation in India’s first human space mission, Gaganyaan?

(A) Russia

(B) ** France

(C) United States

(D) Israel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By Press Trust of India: Space agencies of India and France on Thursday signed an agreement for cooperation for the former’s first human space mission, Gaganyaan, a move that will enable Indian flight physicians to train at French facilities.

Concept note-2: -Space agencies of India and France signed an agreement for cooperation for the India’s first human space mission, Gaganyaan. France has a well-established mechanism for space medicine. It also has the MEDES space clinic, a subsidiary of CNES, where space surgeons undergo training.

Concept note-3: -The Gaganyaan project envisages demonstration of human spaceflight capability by launching a crew of three members to an orbit of 400 km for a three day mission and bringing them back safely to earth, by landing in Indian sea waters.

Concept note-4: -The agreement provides for CNES to support the implementation of a scientific experiment plan on validation missions, exchange information on food packaging and the nutrition programme, and above all, the use of French equipment, consumables and medical instruments by Indian astronauts.
