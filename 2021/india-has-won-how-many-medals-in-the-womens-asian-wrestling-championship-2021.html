India has won how many medals in the Women’s Asian Wrestling Championship 2021?

(A) 5

(B) ** 7

(C) 9

(D) 11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is 7. Indian wrestlers Vinesh Phogat and Anshu Malik clinched their Asian Championship titles on 16 April 2021 at Almaty, Kazakhstan as India ended with seven medals from the women’s event with four gold.

Concept note-2: -India Clinched 14 medals including 5 gold, 3 silver, and 6 bronze at the Senior Asian Wrestling Championship 2021 at Almaty, Kazakhstan. The event was the 34th edition of the Asian Wrestling Championships. India won 14 medals to stand in the third spot on the medal table.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Q. How many medals did India win in the Asian Wrestling Championships 2022? Notes: The Indian team ended its campaign at Asian Wrestling Championships 2022 with a total of 17 medals- one gold, five silvers and 11 bronze.

Concept note-4: -Indian wrestler Ravi Kumar Dahiya wins gold medal in 57kg category while Wrestler Bajrang Punia wins silver at the Asian Championships.

Concept note-5: -Asian Wrestling Championships: Ravi Kumar Dahiya wins gold; Bajrang Punia and Gourav Balian grab silver.
