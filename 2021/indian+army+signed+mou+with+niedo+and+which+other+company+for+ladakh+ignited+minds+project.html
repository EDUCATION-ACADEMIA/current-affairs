Indian Army signed MoU with NIEDO and which other company for Ladakh Ignited Minds Project?

(A) BPCL

(B) ONGC

(C) ** HPCL

(D) TERI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In Ladakh, Indian Army’s Fire and Fury Corps extended ‘Ignited Minds’, a project to train the students for Medical and Engineering entrance exams, to Kargil. The project ‘Ladakh Ignited Minds’, an Indian Army initiative to provide holistic educational training for Ladakhi youth.

Concept note-2: -The objectives of the project include inculcating a sense of competition and competitiveness amongst the students, instilling a sense of responsibility towards society, making them confident and determined to face future challenges and instilling patriotic values.
