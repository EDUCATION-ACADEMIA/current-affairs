Indian Council of Agriculture Research celebrated its which of the following Foundation day on 16 July 2021?

(A) ** 93

(B) 91

(C) 100

(D) 50

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Council for Agricultural Research celebrated its 93rd Foundation Day on July 16th 2021. Shri Tomar addressed the Virtual celebration of 93rd Foundation Day & Awards Ceremony of ICAR held at Krishi Bhawan, New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -The 94th ICAR Foundation Day and Award Ceremony is being organised on 16 July 2022 at A P Shinde Symposium Hall, NASC, PUSA, New Delhi. Shri Narendra Singh Tomar, Union Minister of Agriculture & Farmers’ Welfare will be inaugurating the event.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) was registered as an organization under the Societies Registration Act of 1860 on July 16, 1929.

Concept note-4: -It was established on 16 July 1929 as a registered society under the Societies Registration Act, 1860.

Concept note-5: -The ICAR Institutes pan India also celebrated the World Soil Day - 2022 on the Theme - ‘Soils: Where food begins’. Shri Shripad Yesso Naik, Union Minister of State for Tourism and Ports, Shipping and Waterways graced the occasion as Chief Guest.
