India’s First Food Museum has been launched in which state?

(A) Kerala

(B) Telangana

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) ** Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In November 2021, Union Minister Piyush Goyal virtually launched India’s first digital food museum in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-2: -Q. Where is India’s first Food Museum launched? Notes: The Union Minister for Commerce, Industry, Food and Public Distribution Piyush Goyal has virtually inaugurated the India’s first Food Museum at Thanjavur / Tanjore, Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-3: -The Food Corporation of India (FCI) has set up the country’s first food museum in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu, in association with the Visvesvaraya Industrial and Technological Museum (VITM), Bengaluru.

Concept note-4: -Piyush Goyal launched India’s first digital food museum in Tamil Nadu in November 2021. It is also known as India’s first Food Security Museum. It is located at Thanjavur (birthplace of the FCI), Tamil Nadu, where FCI was inaugurated 56 years ago on 14 January 1965.

Concept note-5: -The Museum has been set up at Thanjavur in Tamil Nadu, which is also the cultural capital of the state. The FCI Food Musem has been setup to digitally exhibit the foodgrain production scenario in India and across the globe also to also highlight the challenges with regards to Food Storage.
