India’s 1st Emergency Landing Facility has been inaugurated on National Highway 925A stretch in which state?

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) ** Rajasthan

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An Emergency Landing Facility (ELF) for the Indian Air Force (IAF) at Satta-Gandhav stretch on NH-925A near Barmer, Rajasthan was inaugurated today.

Concept note-2: -Defence minister Rajnath Singh and Gadkari last year inaugurated the country’s first emergency landing strip on a national highway at Gandhav-Bhakasar section on NH-925 in Barmer, Rajasthan.

Concept note-3: -A cargo plane along with a number of fighter jets simulated the emergency situation landing on the national highway. This 4.1 km emergency landing facility (ELF) has been constructed at Picchikalagudipadu village on National Highway 16 in Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-4: -Speaking at the inauguration ceremony, Rajnath Singh hailed the “great feat” and said that the NHAI is preparing emergency landing fields at 20 locations and also constructing helipads in many other regions.

Concept note-5: -IAF identifies 28 emergency landing strips on highways.
