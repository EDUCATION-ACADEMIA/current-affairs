India’s first FASTag-based metro parking facility has been launched in which city?

(A) Lucknow

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Bengaluru

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Paytm launches India’s first FASTag-based facility at Delhi Metro parking | Business Standard News.

Concept note-2: -Paytm Payments Bank Ltd (PPBL) has launched India’s first FASTag-based metro parking facility, at the Kashmere Gate metro station in partnership with Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC).

Concept note-3: -Delhi Metro Rail Corporation launches its first FASTag or UPI-based cashless parking and a multi-model integration facility, at Kashmere Gate metro station, in New Delhi on Tuesday.

Concept note-4: -Delhi launches Station Access and Mobility Program to improve multimodal integration.

Concept note-5: -Park+ FASTag is a single RFID tag for multiple needs and helps you de-clutter your car windshield. Park+ FASTag helps you with no stopping drive-thru at NHAI Toll plazas, HPCL petrol pumps, society & corporate gates, and select parking lots across the country. Using Park+ you can buy and recharge FASTag online.
