India’s first Igloo Cafe has been Built in which state/UT?

(A) Himachal Pradesh

(B) Uttarakhand

(C) ** Jammu & Kashmir

(D) Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This glass-wall restaurant was built a few days ago in the middle of snow by the Kolahoi Green Heights hotel of Gulmarg in north Kashmir’s Baramulla district. In 2021, the same hotel built a snow igloo in Gulmarg which was the biggest snow igloo in Asia that also attracted tourists.

Concept note-2: -Waseem Shah first got this idea of building an igloo cafe, when he built one at his home in Srinagar. On witnessing the excitement around it, he decided to build an igloo cafe outside his hotel in Gulmarg.

Concept note-3: -An igloo cafe has opened in Jammu and Kashmir’s Gulmarg, which has now become a new tourist attraction in the union territory. More importantly, it is said to have created the record of being the world’s largest igloo cafe! The newly opened cafe has a height of 37.5 ft and a diameter of 44.5 ft.

Concept note-4: -’World’s largest igloo cafe’ in India’s Jammu and Kashmir attracts new tourists - see pictures. A cafe named ‘Snowglu’ opened in Jammu and Kashmir’s Gulmarg. It has become a new tourist attraction in the Indian union territory.

Concept note-5: -Gulmarg has become home to India’s first and Asia’s biggest Igloo Café, started by the Kolahoi Green Group of Hotels and Resorts in Gulmarg. Reportedly, Hotelier Waseem Shah conceived the idea to build this igloo to give a new experience to the locals and tourists.
