India’s largest Hockey Stadium will be named after which freedom fighter?

(A) Tilak Manjhi

(B) ** Birsa Munda

(C) Shibu Soren

(D) B Pradhan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Birsa Munda Hockey Stadium has a seating capacity of 20, 846 spectators and is one of the largest hockey stadiums in India. In fact, it the largest seated hockey stadium in the world.

Concept note-2: -Rourkela SHINES - The new hockey stadium today named after Shri Birsa Munda and it will be known as Birsa Munda Hockey stadium Rourkela Shines | Facebook.

Concept note-3: -Odisha is all decked up to host the men’s hockey World Cup 2023. This time, some of the matches would be held at the newly-inaugurated Birsa Munda Hockey Stadium in Rourkela, which is claimed to be the world’s largest stadium. The stadium was built during the pandemic, especially for the hockey world cup.

Concept note-4: -The 200-crore stadium has been built on an area of 35 acres inside the 120-acre campus of the Biju Patnaik University of Technology at the outskirts of Rourkela.
