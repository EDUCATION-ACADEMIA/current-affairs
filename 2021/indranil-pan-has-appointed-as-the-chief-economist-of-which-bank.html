Indranil Pan has appointed as the chief economist of which bank?

(A) IDBI Bank

(B) ** Yes Bank

(C) HDFC Bank

(D) RBL Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indranil Pan - Chief Economist - YES BANK | LinkedIn.

Concept note-2: -Indermit Gill is Chief Economist of the World Bank Group and Senior Vice President for Development Economics. He brings to the role a broad combination of leadership, expertise, and practical experience working with governments on macroeconomic imbalances, growth, poverty, institutions, conflict, and climate change.

Concept note-3: -Mr. Madan Sabnavis is Chief Economist at Bank of Baroda. He is a post graduate in Economics from Delhi School of Economics and graduated from St Stephen’s College in Economics (Honours).

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Kaushik Basu. World Bank Group President Jim Yong Kim announced the appointment of Kaushik Basu as the institution’s new Chief Economist and Senior Vice President on 5th September 2012. He becomes the first Indian appointed as the chief economist of the World Bank.
