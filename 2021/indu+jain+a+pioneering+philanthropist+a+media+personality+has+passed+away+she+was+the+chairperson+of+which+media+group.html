Indu Jain, a pioneering philanthropist, a media personality, has passed away. She was the Chairperson of which media group?

(A) ** Times Group

(B) Jagran Prakashan Ltd.

(C) Indian Express

(D) Ramoji Group

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indu Jain (8 September 1936 – 13 May 2021) was an Indian media executive and philanthropist. She belonged to the Sahu Jain family and was the chairperson of India’s largest media group, popularly known as The Times Group.

Concept note-2: -Born on 5 August, 1935 Indu Jain emerged as a strong presence on the Hindi literary scene in the 1970s. Her first collection of poems Chaunsath Kavitayein( Sixty four Poems) was published in 1964.

Concept note-3: -Born into an industrial family in India, she realized her dream of becoming a successful entrepreneur at an early stage. After taking over the chairmanship of the media giant BCCL (Bennett Coleman & CO. Ltd), the company grew over $4 billion, and the workforce expanded up to 11, 000 with her continuous hard work.
