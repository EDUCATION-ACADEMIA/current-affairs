Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) has announced to launch which mobile application for students?

(A) ** ICAI-BOS

(B) ICAI-COS

(C) ICAI-FOS

(D) ICAI-DOS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Last Year, the Board of Studies (A), ICAI had launched its mobile application “ICAI BOS” on 1st July 2021 which is a one-stop solution to get all learning material with a single click.

Concept note-2: -Ans: This “ICAI-BOS” is course specific mobile app. In other words, Foundation course students can only view the lectures, recorded lectures, educational contents, mock test, etc.

Concept note-3: -After the expiry of free period students can also subscribe to the Journal by paying a demand draft of Rs. 200 in favour of “The Secretary, The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India". The DD along with the Subscription form is to be sent to The Director, Board of Studies, A-29, Sector-62, Noida-201 309.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Accounting Standards (Ind AS), as notified under section 133 of the Companies Act 2013, have been formulated keeping the Indian economic & legal environment in view and with a view to converge with IFRS Standards, as issued by and copyright of which is held by the IFRS Foundation.
