Intel in collaboration with which one has announced the launch of the AI For All initiative?

(A) DRDO

(B) ** CBSE

(C) ICSE

(D) ICMR

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Intel launches ‘AI For All’ initiative in collaboration with CBSE, Ministry of Education . The self-learning program will create AI awareness and appreciation across India.

Concept note-2: -The AI For All initiative based on Intel’s AI For Citizens program aims to make India AI-ready by building awareness and appreciation of AI among everyone.

Concept note-3: -Which company collaborated with CBSE and Ministry of Education for “AI For All” initiative? Notes: Intel, CBSE and Ministry of Education announced the launch of the “AI For All” initiative on July 29, 2021.

Concept note-4: -Chipmaker Intel on Thursday said it has collaborated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) to launch the ‘AI For All’ initiative, aimed at creating a basic understanding of artificial intelligence (AI) for people in India.

Concept note-5: -India will take over the Chair of the Global Partnership on Artificial Intelligence, GPAI from France today, November 21. Minister of State for Electronics and Information Technology Rajeev Chandrasekhar will represent the country at the GPAI meeting in Tokyo for the symbolic takeover from France.
