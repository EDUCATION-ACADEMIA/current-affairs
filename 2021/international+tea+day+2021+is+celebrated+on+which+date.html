International Tea Day 2021 is celebrated on which date?

(A) December 14

(B) ** December 15

(C) December 12

(D) December 13

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -December 15 is celebrated as International Tea Day in several countries worldwide. This day not only celebrates tea as a special beverage but also promotes sustainable tea manufacture, business, and consumption.

Concept note-2: -International Tea Day is significant because it allows the tea industry to continue with its critical role in combating hunger, reducing poverty, and safeguarding livelihoods and natural resources. The theme for International Tea Day 2022 is not known yet. But last year, the theme was Tea and Fair Trade.

Concept note-3: -International Tea Day is observed every year on 21 May. It was first celebrated in the year 2020. The theme for the year 2021 is “Tea and Fair Trade”.

Concept note-4: -Step aside, coffee and Coca Cola, for International Tea Day on December 15, we drink the most popular beverage in the world. The day mainly seeks to raise awareness on the impact the tea trade has on farmers and workers but is also celebrated by tea lovers worldwide.
