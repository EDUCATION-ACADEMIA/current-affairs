International Day of Friendship is observed globally on which date?

(A) July 23

(B) ** July 30

(C) July 28

(D) July 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Friendship Day is celebrated every year on July 30. The World Friendship Crusade, an international civil entity working to promote a culture of peace through friendship, had proposed International Friendship Day.

Concept note-2: -Though in India, Friendship Day is celebrated on the first Sunday of August, International Friendship Day is celebrated across the world on July 30.

Concept note-3: -Friendship Day is not a public holiday. It falls on Sunday, August 6, 2023 and most businesses follow regular Sunday opening hours in India.

Concept note-4: -In April 2011, the United Nations officially recognized 30th July as International Friendship Day; although most countries celebrate on the first Sunday of August!

Concept note-5: -Friendship day is dedicated to friends with whom you share the precious moments of life and this day is being celebrated on the first Sunday in the month of August i.e., today, 7th August, 2022. Friendship day is celebrated across the world by those who share special bond with their friends.
