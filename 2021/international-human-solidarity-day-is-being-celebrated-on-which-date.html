International Human Solidarity Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** December 20

(B) December 19

(C) December 21

(D) December 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Therefore, the UN General Assembly, convinced that the promotion of the culture of solidarity and the spirit of sharing is important for combating poverty, proclaimed 20 of December as International Human Solidarity Day.

Concept note-2: -The General Assembly, on 22 December 2005, by resolution 60/209 identified solidarity as one of the fundamental and universal values that should underlie relations between peoples in the twenty-first century, and in that regard decided to proclaim 20 December of each year International Human Solidarity Day.

Concept note-3: -International Human Solidarity Day 2022: The day is commemorated to raise public awareness of the value of solidarity. International Human Solidarity Day 2022: International Human Solidarity Day is observed on 20th December annually to celebrate unity in diversity and eradicate poverty.

Concept note-4: -International Human Solidarity Day 2022: Theme The basic purpose of observing the day is to promote the habit of cooperation, equality, and social justice among people. Everyone should celebrate this day and promote awareness among others by educating them about solidarity.

Concept note-5: -On December 20, 2002, the UN General Assembly introduced a World Solidarity Fund to help counter global poverty. This got included in the United Nation Development Programme (UNDP) trust fund in February 2003. To commemorate the above day, the UN declared December 20 as International Human Solidarity Day.
