ISA Conferred the very First ISA Solar Awards at whbeinassembly?

(A) 2nd

(B) ** 3rd

(C) 4th

(D) 8th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Government of Karnataka, India institutionalised the ISA Karnataka Visvesvaraya Solar Award to recognize countries for installed floating solar energy in the ISA regions. The award is dedicated to innovations and achievements in the floating solar technology projects in the ISA member countries.

Concept note-2: -The International Solar Alliance (ISA) is an initiative proposed by our Prime Minister Narendra Modi and was launched by him at the UN Climate Change Conference in Paris along with the President of France. It was launched on 30th November 2015.

Concept note-3: -As a full member of ISA the country can now benefit from multilateral initiatives in several areas such as funding, incentive packages, public policies, and research and development.
