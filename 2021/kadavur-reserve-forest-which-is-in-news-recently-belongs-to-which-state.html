Kadavur Reserve Forest, which is in news recently belongs to which state?

(A) Kerala

(B) ** Tamil Nadu

(C) Karnataka

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Key facts. Tamil Nadu is set to host India’s first ever Slender Loris Sanctuary. Named Kadavur Slender Loris Sanctuary, it will span across 11, 806 hectares in Karur and Dindigul districts, covering areas of Kadavur taluk in Karur and, Dindigul East, Vedasandur and Natham taluks in Dindigul.

Concept note-2: -The Kadavur slender loris sanctuary is to cover 11, 806 hectares in Karur and Dindigul districts.

Concept note-3: -Tamil Nadu notifies India’s first Slender Loris sanctuary. In News: The Tamil Nadu government recently notified the Kadavur Slender Loris sanctuary in the districts of Karur and Dindigul, a first in the country.

Concept note-4: -Kadavur Slender Loris Sanctuary The Tamil Nadu government has notified 11806 hectares of forest area in Dindigul and Karur districts, as a sanctuary for the endangered “Slender Loris”. The Kadavur Slender Loris Sanctuary will be the first such facility for the animal in the country.
