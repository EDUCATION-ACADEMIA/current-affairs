Kareem Abdul-Jabbar Social Justice Champion Award is a newly created award by which sports league?

(A) NHL

(B) ** NBA

(C) UFC

(D) La Liga

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -KAREEM ABDUL-JABBAR SOCIAL JUSTICE CHAMPION AWARD The annual award recognizes a current NBA player for pursuing social justice and upholding the NBA’s values of equality, respect and inclusion.

Concept note-2: -NBA creates Kareem Abdul-Jabbar Social Justice Award The new annual honor that will recognize a current NBA player for pursuing social justice efforts.

Concept note-3: -The NBA will introduce a new Kia Performance Award at the end of the 2022-23 season, the Kia NBA Clutch Player of the Year, with its recipient receiving The Jerry West Trophy.

Concept note-4: -The awards list features the Michael Jordan Trophy, given to the league’s MVP. The NBA added the Clutch Player of the Year award, named the Jerry West Trophy. The NBA renamed the Rookie of the Year award to the Wilt Chamberlain Trophy. The George Mikan Trophy will be awarded to the Most Improved Player of the Year.
