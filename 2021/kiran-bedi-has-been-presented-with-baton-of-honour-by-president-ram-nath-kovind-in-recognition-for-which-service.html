Kiran Bedi has been presented with Baton of Honour by President Ram Nath Kovind in recognition for which service?

(A) ** Union Territory of Puducherry

(B) United Nations Police

(C) Indian Police Service

(D) Bharatiya Janata Party

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Khurana was a prisoner in Tihar during the Emergency, and appreciated her work for prisoners. In 1994, Bedi was awarded the Ramon Magsaysay Award and the Nehru Fellowship. The Magsaysay Foundation recognized her leadership and innovations in crime control, drug rehabilitation, and humane prison reform.

Concept note-2: -Answer- Kiran Bedi is known for her bravery, honesty, integrity, perseverance, and resilience. If you wish to attain these qualities, then reading all about her life and achievements can surely inspire you to inculcate them.

Concept note-3: -At a young age, Kiran Bedi took a liking to sports, especially tennis, and won the Asian Women’s Lawn Tennis Championship. Even though the Indian society was not favourable towards women, Kiran Bedi did not lose hope. She struggled to be on par with men and at times, she even surpassed them in sports and education.
