Kuldiep Singh has been appointed as the new Director-General of which body?

(A) ** CRPF

(B) NSG

(C) CISF

(D) ITBP

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Kuldiep Singh. Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) DG Kuldiep Singh has been given the additional charge of the Director-General of the National Investigation Agency (NIA).

Concept note-2: -Madhya Pradesh cadre IPS officer and CRPF Director General (DG) Sujoy Lal Thaosen now holds additional charge as Border Security Force (BSF) DG.

Concept note-3: -Anish Dayal Singh is the 32nd Chief of the elite mountain-trained Central Armed Police Force- ITBP was raised in 1962 and primarily deployed to guard the India-China Borders. The Force is going to complete 60 years of its foundation on 24 October, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Kuldiep Singh (IPS) is new Director CRPF & MA Ganapathy (IPS) is new DG NSG. New Delhi: The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the proposal of the Ministry of Home Affairs for the appointment of Kuldiep Singh (IPS) as Director-General of the Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF).
