Kushinagar International Airport will be the third international airport of which state?

(A) ** Uttar Pradesh

(B) Arunachal Pradesh

(C) Himachal Pradesh

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kushinagar International Airport is an international airport serving Kushinagar, Uttar Pradesh, India. In August 2021, the airport was licensed to operate as a customs notified airport, facilitating the movement of international passengers and Buddhist pilgrims.

Concept note-2: -the only state in the country to have five international airports. The Kushinagar International Airport is the third international airport apart from operational airports in Lucknow and Varanasi.

Concept note-3: -At present, two international airports - Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport at Lucknow and Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport at Varanasi – are operational in the state while another one is coming up at Jewar in Gautam Budh Nagar. Now Kushinagar became the Third International Airport of Uttar Pradesh.
