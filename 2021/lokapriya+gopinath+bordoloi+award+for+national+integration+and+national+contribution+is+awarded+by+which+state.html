“Lokapriya Gopinath Bordoloi Award for National Integration and National Contribution” is awarded by which state?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Odisha

(D) ** Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This award is one of the biggest civilian awards in Assam. It carries a citation along with a cash prize of Rs 5 lakh. Award is conferred in the name of first chief minister of Assam, Lokapriya Gopinath Bordoloi. He was a multi-dimensional figure with outstanding accomplishments.

Concept note-2: -He was a follower of the Gandhian principle of non-violence as a political tool. Due to his unselfish dedication towards Assam and its people, the then Governor of Assam Jayram Das Doulatram conferred him with the title “Lokapriya” (loved by all).

Concept note-3: -Venkaiah Naidu has presented Lokapriya Gopinath Bordoloi Award for National Integration and National Contribution. This award has been conferred to eminent writer Nirode Kumar Barooah, Assam Branch of the Kasturba Gandhi National Memorial Trust and Shillong Chamber Choir at a special function in Guwahati.
