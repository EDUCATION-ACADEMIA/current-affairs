Mark Selby has become the world champion for the fourth time. He is associated with which sports event?

(A) ** Snooker

(B) Tennis

(C) Badminton

(D) Chess

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The English professional snooker player Mark Selby has won the World Championship for the fourth time. This time he won the title defeating Shaun Murphy. Mark Selby Mark Selby has won the World Championship in 2014, 2016, 2017 and 2021.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Mark Selby. In Snooker, Mark Selby became the World Champion for the fourth time. Selby won the Championship defeating Shaun Murphy by 18-15 victory at Crucible Theatre, Sheffield.
