Ministry of Defence has ordered how many Main Battle Tanks, MBTs Arjun Mk-1A for the Indian Army?

(A) 120

(B) ** 118

(C) 150

(D) 200

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Defence (MoD) placed an order with Heavy Vehicles Factory (HVF), Avadi, Chennai for supply of 118 Main Battle Tanks (MBTs) Arjun Mk-1A for the Indian Army on September 23, 2021.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Defence will procure 118 Main Battle Tanks, MBTs Arjun Mk-1A for the Indian Army. The order was worth Rs 7, 523 crore with Heavy Vehicles Factory, Avadi to sharpen the army’s combat edge.
