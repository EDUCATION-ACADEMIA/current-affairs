miniTV is a newly launched free video streaming service. The service has been launched by which entity?

(A) Zee Entertainment

(B) ** Amazon India

(C) Star India

(D) Reliance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -miniTV is a free, ad-supported video streaming service within the Amazon Shopping app. miniTV is available on Android or iOS phones and on the Amazon website via mobile or desktop web browsers. Amazon customers don’t need a separate application to access and enjoy miniTV content.

Concept note-2: -Amazon’s Freevee app was launched earlier in 2022 and replaces Amazon’s former free streaming service, IMDb TV.

Concept note-3: -Amazon’s miniTV will feature content produced by TVF, Pocket Aces and other production majors. In addition, it will provide content from comedians including - Ashish Chanchlani, Amit Bhadana, Round2Hell, Harsh Beniwal, Shruti Arjun Anand, Elvish Yadav, Prajakta Koli, Swagger Sharma, Aakash Gupta and Nishant Tanwar.

Concept note-4: -Amazon has launched a new video streaming service, called miniTV.
