Mission Youth in Which UT will support more than 2500 students annually to prepare to compete at National Level in various exams?

(A) ** Jammu and Kashmir

(B) Delhi

(C) Chandigarh

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Scheme envisages giving financial assistance upto Rs 5 lakhs to young women for setting up gainful self-employment ventures, suited to their skills, training, aptitude and local conditions. The financial assistance is given to women between the age of 18 to 35 years having a qualification of 10th standard or above.

Concept note-2: -Vision India is a reputed agency which connects various corporate, national and multi-national, with youth having skill and employability. A dedicated portal of job opportunities for youth and J&K has been provisioned to facilitate them in getting the best job opportunities.

Concept note-3: -Under this scheme youth in the age group of 18 to 40 years shall be given financial assistance between 2.0 Lakh to 20 Lakh for creation or expansion of existing business venture within J&K with special focus on high growth entrepreneurship through business development plan competitions.
