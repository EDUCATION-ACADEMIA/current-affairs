Mobile Based E-Voting System will be held in Telangana on which date?

(A) October 22

(B) October 23

(C) October 24

(D) ** October 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About this object This electronic voting box was first put in use with the advent of electronic voting in 1973. The House held its first electronic vote on January 23, 1973.

Concept note-2: -The EVMs were used first time in the general election (entire state) to the assembly of Goa in 1999.

Concept note-3: -Electronic voting (also known as e-voting) is voting that uses electronic means to either aid or take care of casting and counting ballots. Depending on the particular implementation, e-voting may use standalone electronic voting machines (also called EVM) or computers connected to the Internet (online voting).

Concept note-4: -Every adult citizen of India can vote only in their constituency. Candidates who win the State Legislative Assemblies elections are called ‘Member of Legislative Assembly’ (MLA) and hold their seats for five years or until the body is dissolved by the Governor.
