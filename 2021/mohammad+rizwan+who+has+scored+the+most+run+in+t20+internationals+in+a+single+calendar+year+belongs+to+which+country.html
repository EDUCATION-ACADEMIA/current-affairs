Mohammad Rizwan, who has scored the most run in T20 internationals in a single calendar year, belongs to which country?

(A) India

(B) ** Pakistan

(C) Afghanistan

(D) Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rizwan is the only batter to score 2000 runs in a calendar year in T20s. He is a right-handed batter and wicket-keeper and has scored centuries in all three international formats: Tests, One Day Internationals and Twenty20 Internationals. He is the vice-captain of the Pakistan cricket team in Test cricket.

Concept note-2: -Pakistan cricketer Mohammad Rizwan currently holds the record for scoring the most runs in a calendar year in T20 internationals. The star wicket-keeper batter amassed 1, 326 runs in 29 matches in 2021 at an impressive average of 73.66 and a strike rate of 134.89.

Concept note-3: -Suryakumar Yadav becomes India’s leading T20I run-scorer in a calendar year. He reached this landmark during India’s first T20i of the three-match series against South Africa.

Concept note-4: -Most runs in 2022 in international cricket: Babar Azam tops list, Shreyas Iyer first among Indians - Sportstar.

Concept note-5: -Highest individual score in T20 cricket: Chris Gayle leads high-profile list. Chris Gayle’s 175 for Royal Challengers Bangalore in IPL 2013 is the highest T20 score by any batsman in an innings.
