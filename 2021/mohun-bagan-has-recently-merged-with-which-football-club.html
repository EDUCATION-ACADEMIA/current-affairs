Mohun Bagan has recently merged with which football club?

(A) ** ATK FC

(B) Kerala Blasters FC

(C) Chennaiyin FC

(D) NorthEast United FC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Super League (ISL) club ATK Mohun Bagan announced the signing of midfielders Federico Gallego and Puitea on Saturday. Both players have ample experience in the ISL and will add solidity to the midfield for the Mariners.

Concept note-2: -But since KGSPL continued push the “merger” narrative through heavily funded advertising campaigns, PR platforms (press releases, websites, social media) and other visible means, Mohun Bagan fans decided to launch the ‘Remove ATK’ movement in an effort to safeguard their club’s football team’s identity and history.

Concept note-3: -Kolkata (West Bengal) [India], August 21 (ANI): ATK Mohun Bagan who are all set to get their 2022-23 campaign underway on Saturday against Rajasthan United in the Durand Cup have named four players – Pritam Kotal, Florentin Pogba, Joni Kauko and Subhasish Bose as the team’s captains for the upcoming season.

Concept note-4: -KOLKATA: Defender Sandesh Jhingan ends his journey with ATK Mohun Bagan after spending half a season with the Mariners.
