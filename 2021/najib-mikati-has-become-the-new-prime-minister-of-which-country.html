Najib Mikati has become the new Prime Minister of which country?

(A) Haiti

(B) Germany

(C) ** Lebanon

(D) Cango

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Najib Azmi Mikati (Arabic: ; born 24 November 1955) is a Lebanese politician and businessman, and three-time Prime Minister of Lebanon whose current term began in September 2021.

Concept note-2: -On the evening of December 9, 2022 local time, President Xi Jinping met with Lebanese Prime Minister Najib Mikati in Riyadh.

Concept note-3: -Incumbent The current prime minister is Najib Mikati, having taken office on 10 September 2021.

Concept note-4: -The 2022–2023 Lebanese presidential election is an ongoing indirect election to elect the president of Lebanon following the expiration of term-limited incumbent Michel Aoun’s mandate on 31 October 2022. The outgoing president has served since 31 October 2016, following the end of the 2-year presidential crisis.
