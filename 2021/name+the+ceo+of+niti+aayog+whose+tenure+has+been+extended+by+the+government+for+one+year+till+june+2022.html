Name the CEO of NITI Aayog, whose tenure has been extended by the Government for one year till June 2022?

(A) Rajiv Kumar

(B) Arvind Panagariya

(C) Nripendra Misra

(D) ** Amitabh Kant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr Parameswaran Iyer is the Chief Executive Officer of NITI Aayog.

Concept note-2: -NITI Aayog welcomes Shri Parameswaran Iyer as NITI Aayog’s CEO. With over 25 years of experience in the water and sanitation sector, Mr Iyer spearheaded the implementation of the India’s flagship $20-billion Swachh Bharat Mission, which successfully delivered access to safe sanitation to 550 million people.

Concept note-3: -as Chief Executive Officer, NITI Aayog vice Shri Amitabh Kant upon completion of his tenure on 30.06. 2022, for a period of two years or until further orders, whichever is earlier, on the same terms and conditions as were applicable in respect of Shri Amitabh Kant, ” read the official notification from the government.

Concept note-4: -Parameswaran Iyer, who had spearheaded the implementation of the flagship USD 20 billion Swachh Bharat Mission, on Monday took charge as the CEO of NITI Aayog.
