Name the new Prime Minister’s scheme launched by the government for mentoring Young Authors’?

(A) UNNATI

(B) ** YUVA

(C) GURU

(D) LAKSHYA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Education has launched YUVA- Prime Minister’s Scheme For Mentoring Young Authors. What does YUVA stand for?

Concept note-2: -The scheme for Young Upcoming Versatile Authors abbreviates into YUVA. It is a PM Scheme for Mentoring Young Authors in the country. Going along with the Prime Minister’s vision to establish India as a Vishwa Guru, the YUVA scheme, is a great platform for young writers to be International leaders of tomorrow.

Concept note-3: -The Schedule of YUVA 2.0 (Young, Upcoming and Versatile Authors) is as follows: Announcement of the Scheme 2 October 2022. A total of 75 authors will be selected through an All India Contest to be conducted through https://www.mygov.in/ from 2 October 2022 - 30 November 2022.

Concept note-4: -Under this scheme, aspiring young entrepreneurs will be educated and trained about entrepreneurship for a period of 5 years. Launched on 9th November, 2016, this scheme will run for the next 5 years that is from 2016-2017 to 2020-2021.

Concept note-5: -PM YUVA 2.0 Scheme aims to mentor and provide training to young and budding authors below the age of 30 years. PM Mentoring Yuva Scheme 2023 aims to promote reading, writing and book culture in the country and project India and Indian writings globally.
