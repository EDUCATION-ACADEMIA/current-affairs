Name the female IAF officer who has become the first woman flight test engineer of India?

(A) Sophia Qureshi

(B) Padmavathy Bandopadhyay

(C) Mitali Madhumita

(D) ** Aashritha V Olety

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An officer of the Indian Air Force (IAF) has graduated from the Air Force Test Pilot School as the country’s first woman flight test engineer. Squadron Leader Aashritha V Olety is the first and only woman in the IAF qualified for the role.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. Flight Lieutenant Hina Jaiswal became the first woman flight engineer. She was commissioned in January 2015 to the IAF’s engineering branch.

Concept note-3: -Squadron Leader Aashritha V Olety, Indian Air Force, becomes the 1st Female Flight Test Engineer of the Nation. She has graduated from the prestigious Air Force Test Pilot School, one of the seven only universities in the entire world.
