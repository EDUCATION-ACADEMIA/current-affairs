Name the Indian bureaucrat who has recently been appointed as the external auditor of the Organisation for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons (OPCW)?

(A) K. K. Venugopal

(B) Rajiv Mehrishi

(C) ** GC Murmu

(D) Manoj Sinha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -G C Murmu, who is India’s Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG), has been appointed as external auditor of the Organisation for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons (OPCW) at The Hague.

Concept note-2: -As the Organization’s external auditor, CAG will conduct Financial, Compliance and Value for Money audits of OPCW.

Concept note-3: -The OPCW was established to oversee the implementation of the Chemical Weapons Convention - the full global ban on these kind of weapons. The Nobel Committee stated: “The conventions and the work of the OPCW have defined the use of chemical weapons as a taboo under international law.

Concept note-4: -India is a signatory and party to the Chemical Weapons Convention (CWC), of the Organization for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons (OPCW) with Head Quarters at The Hague, Netherlands.
