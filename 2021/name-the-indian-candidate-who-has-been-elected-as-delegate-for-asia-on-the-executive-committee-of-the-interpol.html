Name the Indian candidate who has been elected as ‘Delegate for Asia’ on the executive committee of the INTERPOL?

(A) Anand Mahto

(B) ** Praveen Sinha

(C) Ramani Bharadwaj

(D) Srinivasan Doraiswamy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s candidate, Praveen Sinha, Special Director of Central Bureau of Investigation was elected today as Delegate for Asia on the Executive Committee of the International Criminal Police Organization (INTERPOL).

Concept note-2: -NEW DELHI: Interpol president Ahmed Naser Al-Raisi on Tuesday reiterated his commitment to drive for a modern Interpol, to enhance transparency and to support collaboration between police organisations of member countries.

Concept note-3: -Leadership of Interpol Central Bureau of Investigation(CBI) special director Praveen Sinha is Interpol’s delegate for Asia. He was elected in the 89th General Assembly of Interpol in Turkey, Istanbul in 2021.

Concept note-4: -CBI special director Sinha elected to Interpol executive panel for Asia.

Concept note-5: -He has been associated with many major investigations during his two stints in CBI. He has been instrumental in drafting of latest manual of CVC as well as CBI. He has worked as Addl Secretary in CVC. He has been elected as a delegate for Asia on the executive Committee of the Interpol.
