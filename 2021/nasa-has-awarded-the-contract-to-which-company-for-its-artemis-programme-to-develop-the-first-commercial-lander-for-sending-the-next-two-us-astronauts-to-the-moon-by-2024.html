NASA has awarded the contract to which company for its Artemis programme, to develop the first commercial lander for sending the next two US astronauts to the moon by 2024?

(A) General Dynamics

(B) ** SpaceX

(C) Lockheed Martin

(D) Blue Origin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The initial commercial contract was awarded to SpaceX for two Starship HLS missions, one uncrewed and one crewed as part of Artemis 3. These two missions each require one HLS launch and multiple fueling launches, all on SpaceX Starship launchers.

Concept note-2: -NASA awards SpaceX $1.15 billion contract for second Artemis lander mission.

Concept note-3: -Northrop Grumman Corp. is behind the solid-rocket boosters that look like missiles and are strapped to the main stage to help get the huge vehicle off the ground. Engine maker Aerojet Rocketdyne Holdings Inc. handled the main engines. Lockheed Martin Corp. is behind the Orion spacecraft.

Concept note-4: -Artemis 2 will fly astronauts around the Moon, no earlier than 2024. And Artemis 3 will land a crew near the Moon’s south pole; this mission, scheduled for 2025 or later, will include the first woman to set foot on the lunar surface.

Concept note-5: -Artemis 1’s flight to the moon. On Nov. 16, 2022, two huge solid rocket boosters and a core stage filled with 733, 000 gallons (2.8 million liters) of propellant powered Orion through Earth’s atmosphere to orbit.
