NASA selects which space agency for the Europa clipper mission?

(A) ISRO

(B) ** SpaceX

(C) DRDO

(D) ESA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The spacecraft arrived at JPL to prep for icy moon mission. NASA’s Europa Clipper mission is one step closer to starting its journey to Jupiter’s icy moon.

Concept note-2: -Europa Clipper will launch in October 2024 and will follow a Mars-Earth Gravity Assist (MEGA) trajectory. The spacecraft will travel for five and half years and arrive at Jupiter in April 2030.

Concept note-3: -NASA’s Europa Clipper will conduct detailed reconnaissance of Jupiter’s moon Europa and investigate whether the icy moon could have conditions suitable for life.

Concept note-4: -The Europa Clipper mission will launch in October 2024 and will arrive in 2030. The mission is currently planned to conduct four years of science observations at Europa. Why Europa? Jupiter’s moon Europa shows strong evidence of an ocean of liquid water beneath its icy crust.
