National Bones and Joint Day is observed on which date?

(A) ** August 4

(B) August 5

(C) July 29

(D) August 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Orthopedic Association is celebrating Bone and Joint day on 4th August since 2012.

Concept note-2: -What is World Pediatric Bone and Joint Day? World Pediatric Bone and Joint Day occurs every October 19. According to the American Academy of Orthopedic Surgeons, more than 126 million Americans are impacted by musculoskeletal conditions. Many cases are brought on by obesity developed during childhood.

Concept note-3: -For 2022, Indian Orthopaedic association has assigned, “EachONE Save ONE” as the theme. This year is focus is on deaths occurring due to trauma and accidents in India.

Concept note-4: -The aim of celebrating this day is to create awareness about bone health among the Indian public and help in promoting cost-effective prevention and treatment of bone and joint conditions.

Concept note-5: -National Chocolate Chip Cookie Day and National Coast Guard Day are celebrated on August 4th.
