Nirmala Sitharaman has launched Tejasvini & Hausala schemes and Shikhar & Shikara Schemes in which state/UT?

(A) ** Jammu & Kashmir

(B) Daman & Diu

(C) Manipur

(D) Arunachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Minister of Finance & Corporate Affairs nsitharaman launches various projects & schemes of banks, SIDBI & NABARD like TEJASWANI, HAUSLA, SHIKHAR & SHIKARA schemes at Jammu. FM also laid e-foundation stone of RSETI Building at Shopian & Baramulla.

Concept note-2: -Nirmala Sitharaman launches Single Nodal Agency (SNA) Dashboard as a part of the Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav (AKAM) celebrations by Ministry of Finance in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Jammu & Kashmir Trade Promotion Organization (JKTPO) under aegis of Department of Industries of Commerce moves to the next step on their initiative called “Hausla”- a programme aimed to help women entrepreneurs of J&K to overcome financial, legal, social and marketing challenges in their business ventures.

Concept note-4: -Srinagar, June 29: In a historic effort aimed at harnessing the energy, enthusiasm, and exuberant dreams of the Women of Jammu & Kashmir, Lieutenant Governor Manoj Sinha today launched the “HAUSLA- inspiring her growth”, a comprehensive programme for Catalysing Women Entrepreneurship in the UT, here at SKICC Srinagar.

Concept note-5: -Full form is Tejaswini Socio-Economic Empowerment of Adolescent Girls and Young Women (AGYW) Project. It seeks to empower adolescent girls with basic life skills to acquire market driven skill training or completion of secondary education, depending on the inclination of the beneficiary.
