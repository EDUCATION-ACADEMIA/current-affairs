Nobel Laureate FW de Klerk, who died at 85 due to cancer, belonged to which country?

(A) New Zealand

(B) ** South Africa

(C) China

(D) England

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Frederik Willem de Klerk (/də ˈklɜːrk, də ˈklɛərk/, Afrikaans: [ˈfriədərək ˈvələm də ˈklɛrk], 18 March 1936 – 11 November 2021) was a South African politician who served as state president of South Africa from 1989 to 1994 and as deputy president from 1994 to 1996.

Concept note-2: -From Apartheid to Majority Rule Following the release, the two politicians worked together to bring an end to the policy of racial segregation. It was for his participation in this peace process that de Klerk was awarded the Peace Prize in 1993.

Concept note-3: -Mandela and de Klerk were jointly awarded the Nobel Prize for Peace in 1993 for their efforts.
