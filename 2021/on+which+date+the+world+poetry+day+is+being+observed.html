On which date the World Poetry Day is being observed?

(A) ** 21 March

(B) 19 March

(C) 22 March

(D) 20 March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Held every year on 21 March, World Poetry Day celebrates one of humanity’s most treasured forms of cultural and linguistic expression and identity.

Concept note-2: -National Poetry Day is the annual mass celebration on the first Thursday of October that encourages everyone to make, experience and share poetry with family and friends.

Concept note-3: -Held every year on 21 March, World Poetry Day celebrates one of humanity’s most treasured forms of cultural and linguistic expression and identity.

Concept note-4: -This year’s National Poetry Day is on Thursday 6 October 2022. The theme for National Poetry Day 2022 is The Environment.

Concept note-5: -World Poetry Day is held each year on March 21 to celebrate “the unique ability of poetry to capture the creative spirit of the human mind.” It was founded in 1999 by UNESCO in the hopes of promoting poetry as a way to communicate across borders and cultural differences.
