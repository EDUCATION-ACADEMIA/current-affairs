On which date the National 7-Eleven Day is being celebrated?

(A) 8 July

(B) 9 July

(C) 10 July

(D) ** 11 July

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Synopsis. Every year, July 11 is celebrated as National Seven-eleven Day in the US.

Concept note-2: -Everyone gets free Slurpees at 7-Eleven on 7-11 Every summer, thousands of 7-Eleven stores around the country celebrate the franchise’s unofficial birthday on July 11 (7-11) by hosting 7/11 Day, otherwise known as Free Slurpee Day.

Concept note-3: -The retailer is giving customers 11 days in which to redeem their free small Slurpee drink, from July 1 through July 11, via the 7Rewards loyalty program.

Concept note-4: -National Free Slurpee Day timeline 7-11 celebrates 75 years by giving everyone a free Slurpee.

Concept note-5: -Every annual year, on July 11, the National 7-eleven day is observed. The National 7-eleven Day celebration is done on a massive scale in the United States of America each year. On this day, Slurpee drinks are disturbed amongst the American citizens completely free of cost.
