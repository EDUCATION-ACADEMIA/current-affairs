Padma Shri award-winning renowned actor Vivek has passed away. He was associated with which film industry?

(A) Bengali

(B) Marathi

(C) Gujarati

(D) ** Tamil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vivekanandan (19 November 1961), known professionally as Vivek, was an Indian actor, comedian, television personality, playback singer and social activist who worked in the Tamil film industry. Introduced in films by director K.

Concept note-2: -Balalchander, Vivek made his debut with Manathil Uruthi Vendum (1987) and in his illustrious career spanning more than 25 years, he has appeared in more than 100 films and along the way won several awards including three Filmfare Awards for Best Comedian.

Concept note-3: -Vivek (19 November 1961 – 17 April 2021) was an Indian actor and comedian who worked mainly in Tamil cinema. He won three Filmfare Best Comedian Awards for his performances in Run, Saamy and Perazhagan. In April 2009, Vivek received the Padma Shri award by the Government of India for his contribution to the arts.
