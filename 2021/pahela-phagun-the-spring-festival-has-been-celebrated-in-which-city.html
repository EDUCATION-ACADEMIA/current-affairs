Pahela Phagun, the spring festival has been celebrated in which city?

(A) Mumbai

(B) ** Dhaka

(C) Delhi

(D) Kathmandu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Dhaka. The first day of the spring called ‘Pahela Phagun’ was celebrated in Dhaka on 14 February 2021.

Concept note-2: -Pohela Falgun (Bengali: , Pôhela Falgun or , Pôela Falgun), also known as the first day of Spring of the Bengali month Falgun, is a festival celebrated in Bangladesh. The celebration was started in 1991 by students of Dhaka University’s Faculty of Fine Arts.

Concept note-3: -After the dryness of winter, new leaves start coming out again and nature adorns the branches with new colorful flowers such as Shimul, Polash and Marigold. Falgun brings joys and colours both in nature and life. Everything in nature gives an impression of youthfulness or freshness, as if nature takes a new birth.

Concept note-4: -Pohela Falgun Celebration The day of P0hela Falgun, Bakultola of the Faculty of Fine Arts of Dhaka University was the center point of the festival. There are many young men and women gathered in the morning and celebrated the day by reciting poems, singing songs and dancing.

Concept note-5: -Pohela Boishakh (Bengali: ) is the first day of the Bengali calendar which is also the official calendar of Bangladesh. This festival is celebrated on 14 April in Bangladesh and 15 April in the Indian states of West Bengal, Tripura, and Assam (Barak Valley) by Bengalis regardless of religious faith.
