Parambikulam Tiger Conservation Foundation, which has won the Earth Guardian Award, is located in which state?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Karanataka

(C) ** Kerala

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Parambikulam Tiger Reserve, which also includes the erstwhile Parambikulam Wildlife Sanctuary, is a 643.66 square kilometres (248.5 sq mi) protected area lying in Palakkad district and Thrissur district of Kerala state, South India.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Parambikulam Tiger Conservation Foundation. Parambikulam Tiger Conservation Foundation has won the Earth Guardian Award for its efforts in supporting the conservation of tigers and biodiversity. The award was instituted by NatWest Group India.

Concept note-3: -Parambikulam Tiger Conservation Foundation wins Earth Guardian Award - All you need to read for UPSC current affairs exam! - GKToday.

Concept note-4: -This award recognises an individual (journalist/filmmaker/artist/conservationist) or institution that has influenced thought or inspired action on wildlife conservation, natural resource management, and environmental protection through his/her creative expression.

Concept note-5: -Parambikulam Tiger Reserve is situated in Chittur taluk of Palakkad district and is about 100 km away from Palakkad.
