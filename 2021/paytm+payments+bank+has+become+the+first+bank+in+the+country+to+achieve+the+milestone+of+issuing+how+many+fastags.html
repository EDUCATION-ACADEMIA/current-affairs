Paytm Payments Bank has become the first bank in the country to achieve the milestone of issuing how many FASTags?

(A) 10 crore

(B) ** 1 crore

(C) 4 crore

(D) 20 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which bank has become the first bank in the country to achieve the milestone of issuing 1 crore FASTags? Notes: Paytm Payments Bank Ltd (PPBL) has become the first bank in the country to achieve the milestone of issuing 1 crore FASTags.

Concept note-2: -Paytm Payments Bank has announced that it has become the largest FASTag issuer in India.

Concept note-3: -Only one FASTag can be issued against a particular vehicle at any given point of time.

Concept note-4: -Paytm Payments Bank Ltd has 3 branches in following state(s) of India.

Concept note-5: -Paytm Payments Bank Ltd (PPBL) claims to have led the market in the FASTag segment with 28.2 per cent market share. The firm said it issued 4, 51, 463 FASTags in December 2021 as against 4, 05, 541 in November, cementing its leadership position in the digital toll payments in the country.
