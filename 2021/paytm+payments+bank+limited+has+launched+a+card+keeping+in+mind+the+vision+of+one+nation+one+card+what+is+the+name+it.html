Paytm Payments Bank Limited has launched a card, keeping in mind the vision of one nation, one card, what is the name it?

(A) Paytm Trans Card

(B) Paytm Time Card

(C) Paytm Travel Card

(D) ** Paytm Transit Card

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Paytm Payments Bank Limited Monday announced the launch of the Paytm Transit Card, keeping in mind the vision of one nation, one card.

Concept note-2: -Paytm Vision Statement Our goal is to perue digital excellence, increase earning growth and to perdure cross selling opportunities.

Concept note-3: -One nation one card can be defined as an open loop EMV-based payment card for giving a hassle-free experience to customers at various transport modes and payment systems across the country. This initiative was conceived by the Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs (MoHUA), Government of India.

Concept note-4: -Paytm denotes ‘Pay through Mobile’, representing the digital transactional services of the company. The company’s vision tagline is ‘Paytm Karo’. The company is on a mission to bring half a billion Indians into the mainstream economy through payments, commerce, banking, investments, and financial services.

Concept note-5: -Paytm’s parent company, One97 Communications, got listed on the Indian stock exchanges on 18 November 2021 after an initial public offering, which was the largest in India at the time.
