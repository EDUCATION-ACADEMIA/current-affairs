PM Narendra Modi has inaugurated the International Cooperation and Convention Centre-Rudraksh in which city?

(A) Ahmedabad

(B) ** Varanasi

(C) Kolkata

(D) Dehradun

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PM Modi inaugurates International Cooperation & Convention Centre ‘Rudraksh’ in Varanasi.

Concept note-2: -Rudraksh International Convention Centre, built at a cost of INR 186 Cr, , is built in the shape of Shivalinga and is a unique Convention Centre in itself, showing the confluence of both Japanese and Indian architecture. This convention centre has a seating capacity of 1200 people in the main hall.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which country has financially assisted the International Cooperation and Convention Centre – Rudraksh, opened in Varanasi? Notes: Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the International Cooperation and Convention Centre – Rudraksh in Varanasi. It has been constructed with the financial assistance from Japan.
