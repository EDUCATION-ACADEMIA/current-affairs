Rahi Sarnobat has claimed a gold medal for India in which event?

(A) ** Shooting

(B) Archery

(C) Boxing

(D) Badminton

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rahi Sarnobat is a record-breaking 25m pistol shooter from India. The two-time Olympian was the first Indian pistol shooter to win an ISSF World Cup gold and the first Indian woman shooter to win gold at the Asian Games.

Concept note-2: -LPU student and Wrestler Bajrang Punia won Gold; 8 of its students have together grabbed Silver medal through their collective participation in the Indian men’s hockey team; and two of its women boxer & wrestler- Jasmine Lamboria and Pooja Gehlot, respectively, have bagged Bronze medals individually.

The Indian field hockey team continued their dominance by winning a sixth straight title by defeating Pakistan in the final of 1956 Summer Olympics. The six straight title wins by the Indian team was an Olympic record at that time in a team event.

Concept note-4: -Sarnobat became India’s first pistol shooter to win a gold medal in the World Cup when she won the 25 metre pistol event in the ISSF World Cup in Changwon. In the 2014 Commonwealth Games at Glasgow, she won gold in the women’s 25 metre pistol event.
