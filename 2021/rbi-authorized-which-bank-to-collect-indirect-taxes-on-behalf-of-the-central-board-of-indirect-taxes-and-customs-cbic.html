RBI Authorized which bank to collect indirect taxes on behalf of the Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs (CBIC)?

(A) CSB Bank

(B) ** RBL Bank

(C) Karnataka Bank

(D) City Union Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thrissur: South Indian Bank has been authorised by Reserve Bank of India, for the collection of Direct Taxes and Indirect Taxes, on behalf of CBDT and CBIC.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank of India has authorised South Indian Bankto collect direct and indirect taxes on behalf of the Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) and the Central Board of Indirect Taxes & Customs (CBIC). The Kerala based bank signed an MoU with the CBDT and CBIC for collection of various taxes.
