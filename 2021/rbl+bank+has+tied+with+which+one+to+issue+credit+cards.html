RBL bank has tied with which one to issue credit cards?

(A) ** Visa

(B) Mastercard

(C) Maestro card

(D) Rupay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -RBL Bank currently issues credit cards on the Mastercard network only. As of date, RBL Bank has 3 million credit card customers and is the 5th largest credit card issuer in the country with roughly five per cent market share.

Concept note-2: -The name of the bank has changed from Ratnakar Bank to its current name RBL Bank according to a new memorandum of association issued by the RoC on 24 November 2014.

Concept note-3: -2. Does RBL offer different types of credit cards? A: Yes, RBL offers different credit cards, such as the RBL Platinum Maxima Credit Card, RBL Platinum Delight Credit Card, and the RBL Titanium Delight Card. Other than that, you can also opt for the RBL Bank Insignia Credit Card or the RBL Bank ICON Credit Card.

Concept note-4: -Bajaj Finserv RBL Bank SuperCard, co-branded by Bajaj Finserv and RBL Bank, is a credit instrument that lets you make purchases and helps meet emergency cash requirements. It comes with various industry-first features that make it stand out among all other cards.
