Recently launched ‘Operation Blue Freedom’ is being taken in which area?

(A) ** Siachen

(B) Leh

(C) Ladakh

(D) Kashmir

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. Ambedkar International Centre has supported this world record expedition of people with disabilities to Siachen Glacier as part of its commitment towards the cause and betterment of Divyanjans of our country.

Concept note-2: -Launch of global participation campaign for Operation Blue Freedom - Water World Record at Lakshadweep Islands.

Concept note-3: -C.L.A.W - Conquer Land Air Water.

Concept note-4: -Each participant would need to fill in the medical and waiver form upon sign-up on site. Kindly note that participants with respiratory and cardiology health conditions (eg. asthma or heart issues) should inform the members of the Operation Blue Freedom prior to sign-up.

Concept note-5: -Background. The Turkish Armed Forces have been conducting cross-border military operations against the Kurdistan Workers’ Party (PKK) in Northern Iraq since the 1980s. Since 2019, Turkey has begun conducting operations codenamed Claw, including Claw Eagle and Tiger in 2020 and Claw-Lighting and Thunderbolt in 2021.
