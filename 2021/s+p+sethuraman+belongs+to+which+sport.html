S. P. Sethuraman belongs to which sport?

(A) ** Chess

(B) Cricket

(C) Hockey

(D) Badminton

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sethuraman Panayappan Sethuraman (born 25 February 1993 in Chennai) is an Indian chess grandmaster .

Concept note-2: -Viswanathan Anand won the World Junior Chess Championship when he was 14. He became India’s first chess grandmaster in 1988. Q. Which Grandmaster and former world junior champion won the Commonwealth Chess Championship in July, 2015?

Concept note-3: -Subbaraman Vijayalakshmi (born 25 March 1979) is an Indian chess player who holds the FIDE titles of International Master (IM) and Woman Grandmaster (WGM), the first female player in her country to achieve these titles.

Concept note-4: -Amongst Indian chess players there are 76 Grandmasters (GM); 124 International Masters (IM); 18 Woman Grandmasters (WGM), including some who also hold the higher IM title; and 42 Woman International Masters (WIM) as of January 2021, according to FIDE, the International Chess Federation.
