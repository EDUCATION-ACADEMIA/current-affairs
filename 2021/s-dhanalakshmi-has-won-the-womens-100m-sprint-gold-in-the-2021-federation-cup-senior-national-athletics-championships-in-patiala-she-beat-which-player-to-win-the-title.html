S Dhanalakshmi has won the women’s 100m sprint gold in the 2021 Federation Cup Senior National Athletics Championships in Patiala. She beat which player to win the title?

(A) Hima Das

(B) Tintu Luka

(C) Manjeet Kaur

(D) ** Dutee Chand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Up against Dutee Chand and Hima Das in the final, Dhanalakshmi ran her course in 11.39 seconds to beat second-placed Dutee (11.58 seconds) to the gold. It was her first national-level title. It was Dutee’s first loss at a domestic 100m event since 2015.
