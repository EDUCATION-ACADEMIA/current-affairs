SIDBI along with which country has launched a challenge fund to allocate funds based on competition?

(A) USA

(B) Netherlands

(C) ** UK

(D) UAE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) in partnership with Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office, UK (FCDO UK) has launched ‘Swavalamban Challenge Fund’ (SCF) to lend a helping hand to the entrepreneurs.

Concept note-2: -SIDBI launches Swavalamban Challenge Fund to promote entrepreneurship culture in India - Small Industries Development Bank of India.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which institution has launched the ‘Swavalamban Challenge Fund (SCF)’? Notes: Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) has launched the second window of Swavalamban Challenge Fund (SCF). The theme of the fund is ‘Green Bharat’.

Concept note-4: -SIDBI offers Working Capital Assistance, Term Loan Assistance, Foreign Currency Loan, Support against Receivables, equity support, Energy Saving scheme for the MSME sector, etc.

Concept note-5: -According to a press release issued by Sidbi, “The focus is on innovative projects addressing the green, clean, or efficient climate change. Other themes are sustainable livelihood, financial inclusion, and access to financial services and promoting the culture of entrepreneurship."
