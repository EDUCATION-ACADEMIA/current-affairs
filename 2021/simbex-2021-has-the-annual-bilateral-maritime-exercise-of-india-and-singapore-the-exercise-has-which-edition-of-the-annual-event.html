SIMBEX 2021 has the annual bilateral maritime exercise of India and Singapore. The exercise has which edition of the annual event?

(A) 25th

(B) ** 28th

(C) 27th

(D) 30th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It was the 28th edition of the bilateral naval exercise between India & Singapore. INS Ranvijay (Guided Missile Destroyer), INS Kiltan (Anti-submarine warfare corvette), INS Kora (Guided Missile Corvette) and P8I Long Range Maritime Patrol Aircraft represented Indian Navy.

Concept note-2: -It also highlights the commitment and contribution of the two nations towards enhancing maritime security in the Indian Ocean Region. The Indian Navy is hosting the 29th edition of the Singapore-India Maritime Bilateral Exercise (SIMBEX) from 26 to 30 October, 2022 at Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-3: -The Singapore Army’s Singapore Light Weight Howitzers (SLWH) firing off during a joint live-firing with the Indian Army. The Singapore Army and the Indian Army successfully concluded Exercise Agni Warrior (XAW) 2022, in Devlali, India, from 13 November to 30 November 2022.

Concept note-4: -India’s five-day joint naval war exercise with Singapore began Wednesday at Visakhapatnam. The 29th edition of the Singapore-India Maritime Bilateral Exercise (SIMBEX) is being conducted in two phases – Harbour Phase at Visakhapatnam till Thursday followed by the Sea Phase in Bay of Bengal from Friday.

Concept note-5: -Indian Navy hosted 29th edition of Singapore-India Maritime Bilateral Exercise (SIMBEX) from 26th to 30th October, 2022 at Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. It was conducted in two phases: Harbour Phase at Visakhapatnam from 26 to 27 October, 2022 followed by Sea Phase in Bay of Bengal from 28 to 30 October, 2022.
