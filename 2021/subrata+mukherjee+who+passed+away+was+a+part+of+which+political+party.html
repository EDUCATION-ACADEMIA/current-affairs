Subrata Mukherjee, who passed away, was a part of which political party?

(A) RJD

(B) ** TMC

(C) BJP

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Subrata Mukherjee (14 June 1946 – 4 November 2021) was an Indian politician who was cabinet minister of the government of West Bengal and sitting member of West Bengal Legislative Assembly. He was a member of All India Trinamool Congress.

Concept note-2: -After landing in Tokyo, on 8 November 1960, Mukerjee was having a meal in a restaurant with a friend, an officer in the Indian Navy. A piece of food became lodged in his windpipe, causing him to choke. Before a doctor was called for and could attend, Mukerjee had died.
