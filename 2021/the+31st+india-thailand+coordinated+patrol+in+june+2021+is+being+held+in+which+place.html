The 31st India-Thailand Coordinated Patrol in June 2021 is being held in which place?

(A) Timor Sea

(B) ** Andaman Sea

(C) Red Sea

(D) Arabian Sea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -INS Saryu and HTMS Krabi participate in the 31st Indo-Thai CORPAT, on June 9. The navies of India and Thailand kicked off their three-day, bi-annually India-Thailand Coordinated Patrol (Indo-Thai CORPAT) in the Andaman Sea on June 9.

Concept note-2: -The 31st edition of the India-Thailand Coordinated Patrol (Indo-Thai CORPAT) kicked off on June 09, 2021, in the Andaman Sea. The three-day coordinated patrol between the Indian Navy and the Royal Thai Navy is being conducted from 09 to 11 June 2021.

Concept note-3: -Why in News. Recently, the 31st edition of India-Thailand Coordinated Patrol (Indo-Thai CORPAT) between the Indian Navy and the Royal Thai Navy was conducted in the Andaman sea close to the Strait of Malacca.

Concept note-4: -Ex CORPAT 2022: IND-INDO CORPAT 2022 is being conducted in the Andaman Sea and Straits of Malacca. Military exercises are come under Internal Security of UPSC GS Paper 3 and Bilateral International Relations of UPSC GS Paper 2.

Concept note-5: -What is Indo – Thai CORPAT? The Indo – Thai CORPA is being conducted along the International Maritime Boundary Line since 2005. The maritime boundary is the division of earth water surface area.
