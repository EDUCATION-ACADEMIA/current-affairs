The 6th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of AII has been organised by AIIB in partnership with which country?

(A) ** UAE

(B) China

(C) India

(D) South Korea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United Arab Emirates (UAE) will host the Sixth Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) with the theme “Investing Today, Transforming Tomorrow” virtually on Oct. 26-28, 2021.

Concept note-2: -Union Minister of Finance & Corporate Affairs Nirmala Sitharaman attended the 7th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) via video conference from New Delhi on 26 October 2022.

Concept note-3: -They become members after finishing their domestic procedures. As of 8 August 2022, the total number of countries approved for membership of AIIB is 106 (Regional Members: 47, Non-Regional Members: 45, Prospective Members: 14).

Concept note-4: -The Board of Governors of the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) announced that its eighth Annual Meeting will be held in Sharm el-Sheikh, Egypt, on Sept. 25-26, 2023.

Concept note-5: -India also has the largest project portfolio within AIIB. The theme of this year’s Annual Meeting was “Sustainable Infrastructure Toward a Connected World”. The Finance Minister shared her thoughts in the Governor’s Roundtable Discussion on the theme “Financing Infrastructure in a Crisis-Prone World”.
