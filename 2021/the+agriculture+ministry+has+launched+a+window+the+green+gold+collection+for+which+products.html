The Agriculture Ministry has launched a window. The Green Gold Collection for which products?

(A) ** Bamboo Products

(B) Dairy Products

(C) Corn Products

(D) Hogs Products

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Bamboo Market Page titled “The Green Gold Collection” was launched by the Union Minister of State for Agriculture Shri Parshottam Rupala during a virtual event on 3rd June 2021.

Concept note-2: -It aims to provide bamboo artisans, weavers and entrepreneurs in rural areas with market access to Government buyers. It seeks to promote the adoption and use of bamboo products among Government buyers and usher a sustainable rural economy for an Atmanirbhar Bharat.

Concept note-3: -According to the Union Ministry of Agriculture and Farmer Welfare, India’s annual bamboo production is estimated at 3.23 million tonnes. Termed as the “Green Gold”, the sector has been fueling several other industries too, namely, the agarbatti industry, textiles, handicrafts, agriculture sector, etc.

Concept note-4: -"Bamboo” is called as green gold. It is a “marvel plant” that is changed over from a vagrant plant to green gold. Bamboo is the tallest grass. Bamboos are a diverse group of evergreen perennial flowering plants in the subfamily Bambusoideae of the grass family Poaceae.
