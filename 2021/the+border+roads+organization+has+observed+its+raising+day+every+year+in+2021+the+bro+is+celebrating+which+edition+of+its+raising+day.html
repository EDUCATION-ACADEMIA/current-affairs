The Border Roads Organization has observed its Raising Day every year. In 2021, the BRO is celebrating which edition of its raising day?

(A) 55

(B) ** 61

(C) 72

(D) 36

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Border Roads Organisation. Border Roads Organisation, BRO celebrated its 61st Raising Day on 7 May 2021. BRO is a leading road construction agency under the Ministry of Defence with a primary role of providing road connectivity in the border areas.

Concept note-2: -BRO is the leading road construction organisation in India under the Ministry of Defence which also provides support to the Indian Armed Forces. 7th May 2022 marks the observance of the 63rd Raising Day of BRO.

Concept note-3: -The Border Roads Organisation (BRO) was set up in 1960. The main motive of BRO was to construct motorable roads in border areas. These roads help the armed forces keep vigil in the remote parts of north and northeast India.

Concept note-4: -The BRO Raising Day is observed on May 7 every year. The Border RoadsOrganisation was formed on May 7, 1960.

Concept note-5: -The significance of Border roads is that they are important roads as they improve accessibility in areas of difficult terrain. These roads have also helped in the economic development of border areas. BRO is also entrusted to construct Bridges, Tunnels, Causeways, Helipads, and Airfields.
