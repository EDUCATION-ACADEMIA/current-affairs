The Central Government approved eight hydropower projects of 144 MW in which UT?

(A) Puducherry

(B) ** Ladakh

(C) Andaman and Nicobar Islands

(D) Daman and Diu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India approves development of eight hydropower projects on Indus River.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi: The government has cleared eight hydropower projects of 144 MW on the Indus river and its tributaries in Ladakh, the highest so far, sources in the Jal Shakti Ministry said on Thursday.

Concept note-3: -To harness the hydroelectric potential of River Chenab state government started construction of 900 MW Baglihar Hydro electric Power Project at Chanderkote. Ist phase of 450 MWs has been commissioned with a cost of 5827 Crore.
