The central government has appointed Vice Admiral R Hari Kumar as the next chief of which defense force?

(A) ** Indian Navy

(B) Army & Navy

(C) Air Force

(D) Army

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hari Kumar as the next Chief of the Indian Navy. Presently the Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief of the Indian Navy ’s sword arm Western Command, Vice Admiral Hari Kumar will succeed the outgoing Navy Chief Admiral Karambir Singh to four-star rank on November 30.

Concept note-2: -The Vice Chief is the deputy of the Chief of the Naval Staff and is usually the second highest ranking officer of the Indian Navy. The current VCNS is Vice Admiral Satish Namdeo Ghormade who took over from Vice Admiral G. Ashok Kumar on 31 July 2021.

Concept note-3: -The current Chief of the Naval Staff (CNS) and only full admiral in the Indian Armed Forces is Admiral R. Hari Kumar.

Concept note-4: -Admiral Radhakrishnan Hari Kumar PVSM, AVSM, VSM, ADC (born 12 April 1962) is a serving flag officer in the Indian Navy. He is the 25th and current Chief of the Naval Staff (CNS). Previously, he served as the Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Western Naval Command.
