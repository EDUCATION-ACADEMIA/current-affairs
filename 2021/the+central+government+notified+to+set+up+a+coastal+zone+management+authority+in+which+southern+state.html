The Central Government notified to set up a Coastal Zone Management Authority in which southern state?

(A) Kerala

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) ** Karnataka

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Coastal zone management involves managing coastal areas to balance environmental, economic, human health, and human activities. At the core of the Coastal Zone Management Act are two programs: the National Coastal Zone Management Program and the National Estuarine Research Reserve System (NERRS).

Concept note-2: -As per the notification, the coastal land up to 500m from the High Tide Line (HTL) and a stage of 100m along banks of creeks, estuaries, backwater and rivers subject to tidal fluctuations, is called the Coastal Regulation Zone(CRZ). CRZ along the country has been placed in four categories.

Concept note-3: -With the objective of conservation and protection of the coastal environment, the Ministry of Environment and Forest and Climate Change notified the Coastal Regulation Zone Notification in 1991, which was subsequently revised in 2011.

Concept note-4: -COASTAL REGULATION ZONE NOTIFICATION OF 1991 Notification of 1991 is applicable to the coastal stretches of seas, bays, estuaries, creeks, rivers and backwaters, influenced by the tidal action up to 500 meters from High Tide Line (HTL) and land between Low Tide Line (LTL).
