The Centre for Research and Innovation in Artificial Intelligence has been set up at which IIT?

(A) IIT-Roorkee

(B) IIT-Delhi

(C) ** IIT-Hyderabad

(D) IIT-Raurkela

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. In which city, the Centre for Research and Innovation in Artificial Intelligence has been inaugurated? Notes: Union Education Minister Dharmendra Pradhan has virtually inaugurated the Centre for Research and Innovation in Artificial Intelligence set up at the Indian Institute of Technology-Hyderabad (IIT-H).

Concept note-2: -Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna recently launched a BTech programme in AI and Data Science that will equip students to handle large volumes of data and develop AI-focused algorithms. The BTech AI and data science programme is also offered by IIT Guwahati and IIT Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: -The AI department at IIT-Hyderabad has been recognized for pioneering efforts in AI pedagogy and research, including its BTech, MTech and PhD programs in AI, as well as interdisciplinary AI research.

Concept note-4: -Here we have discussed the top five IITs that are currently offering courses on Artificial Intelligence. Around the world, investment in AI is on a rise. Artificial intelligence (AI) is opening up a world of possibilities for technological advancements across industry verticals.
