The e-Tendering Portal PRANIT has been launched by which of these organizations?

(A) NTPC

(B) NHPC

(C) ** Power Grid Corporation of India

(D) ONGC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: In order to make the tendering process more transparent, Power Grid, a Maharatna Power PSU under the Ministry of Power, has established an e-tendering portal – ‘PRANIT.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains Notes: The Union Power Ministry’s Maharatna PSU – Power Grid has launched an e-tendering portal – “PRANIT”.

Concept note-3: -Pranit portal is a certified e-tendering portal that was recently launched by the POWERGRID (Power Grid Corporation of India Limited)- a public sector unit under the Ministry of Power. It will help reduce paperwork while increasing operational ease and transparency in the tendering process.
