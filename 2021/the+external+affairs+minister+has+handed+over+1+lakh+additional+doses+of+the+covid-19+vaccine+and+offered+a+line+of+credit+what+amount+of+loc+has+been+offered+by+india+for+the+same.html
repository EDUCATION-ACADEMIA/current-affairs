(A) ** $40-million

(B) $20-million

(C) $10-million

(D) $80-million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of External Affairs is the Indian government’s agency responsible for the foreign relations of India.

Concept note-2: -Only employees of Work Place aged 45 years or more will be eligible for vaccination at Work Place, no outsiders including eligible family members will be allowed for vaccination at “CVC at Work Place”. b. Beneficiaries must be registered in Co-WIN portal prior to vaccination.
