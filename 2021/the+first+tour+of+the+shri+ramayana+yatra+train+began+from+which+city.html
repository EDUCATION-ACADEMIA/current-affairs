The first tour of the Shri Ramayana Yatra Train began from which city?

(A) Varanasi

(B) Lucknow

(C) Ayodhya

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It started from Delhi Safdarjung Railway Station. The first halt is at Ayodhya. Here, the tourists will visit Shri Ram Janmbhumi temple, Bharat Mandir and Hanuman temple. The second visit is Sitamarhi in Bihar.

Concept note-2: -In November 2021, the train covered its first journey from Delhi’s Safdarjung Railway Station to Tamil Nadu’s Rameshwaram via Uttar Pradesh’s Ayodhya and Varanasi.

Concept note-3: -From Janakpur, the train will cross into India to reach Sitamarhi in Bihar, from where it would go to Varanasi, where people will visit various temples in the city and will go to Shringverpur, the place where Ram, Sita, and Laxman crossed Ganga, and Chitrakoot by road.

Concept note-4: -Duration of the Holy Ramayana Yatra train tour package: The Holy Ramayana Yatra train tour package is 8 nights and 9 days long and includes places like Ayodhya, Buxar, Chitrakoot, Janakpur, Prayagraj, SitaMarhi, and Varanasi.

Concept note-5: -Ramayan Yatra train price, package 73, 500/- per person, the IRCTC tourist train will be 20 days all-inclusive tour package.
