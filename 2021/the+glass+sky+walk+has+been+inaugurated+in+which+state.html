The Glass Sky Walk has been inaugurated in which state?

(A) Punjab

(B) Kerala

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Uttar Pradesh will get its first glass skywalk at the Tulsi waterfall in Chitrakoot.

Concept note-2: -India has 2 glass skywalks and the first one, was built-in Pelling, Sikkim in 2018, while the second one which is in Rajgir, Bihar was built in 2020. To witness this marvel, people come from all across the country and go back with a plethora of pictures and cherishable memories.

Concept note-3: -India has been gifted her second glass skywalk in Bihar and the magnificence is simply to cherish! When we speak of glass skywalks, China’s Hangzhou and the towering ones of Switzerland come floating to our mind.

Concept note-4: -India’s second Skywalk in Rajgir brings people up at. 250 feet and gives a breathtaking view of the. peaceful greenery. Tourismbihargov.

Concept note-5: -The Sikkim Skywalk is the first glass skywalk in India. It has been built so as to give a wonderful view of the Chenrezig statue and the stairs that lead up to it, with golden prayer wheels on each side. This beast of a walk allows visitors to look down several thousand feet in the air.
