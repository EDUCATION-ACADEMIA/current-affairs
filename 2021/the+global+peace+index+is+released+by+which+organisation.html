The Global Peace Index is released by which organisation?

(A) WEF

(B) ** Institute for Economics and Peace

(C) Transparency International

(D) Greenpeace

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Global Peace Index (GPI) is published by the Institute for Economics and Peace (IEP). This index is developed in partnership Economist Intelligence Unit, which collates data collected from various think tanks and experts from peace institutes.

Concept note-2: -Produced by the Institute for Economics and Peace (IEP), the Global Peace Index (GPI) is the world’s leading measure of global peacefulness. This report presents the most comprehensive data-driven analysis to-date on trends in peace, its economic value, and how to develop peaceful societies.

Concept note-3: -Global Peace Index (GPI) is a report produced by the Institute for Economics & Peace (IEP) which measures the relative position of nations’ and regions’ peacefulness.

Concept note-4: -India was ranked 135th among 163 countries on the Global Peace Index in 2022. It indicated that India needs to further develop itself to achieve higher levels of peace in the country. GPI was first launched in May 2009. The report of the Global Peace Index is prepared by the Institute for Economics and Peace (IEP).

Concept note-5: -India secured 135th rank in Global Peace Index (GPI) in 2021. In that year, India had improved its ranking by two places and became the 135th most peaceful country out of the 163rd countries that were on the list.
