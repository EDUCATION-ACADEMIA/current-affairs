[SOURCES]

The Government of India has accorded which of these statuses to the Power Finance Corporation Ltd (PFC)?

(A) Bharatratna

(B) ** Maharatna

(C) Navratna

(D) Miniratna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Govt. of India accorded ‘Maharatna’ status to the state-owned Power Finance Corporation Ltd (PFC), thus giving PFC greater operational and financial autonomy.

Concept note-2: -PFC is under the administrative control of the Ministry of Power. PFC was conferred the title of a ‘Maharatna CPSE’ in Oct., 2021, and was classified as an Infrastructure Finance Company by the RBI on 28th July, 2010. PFC plays a crucial role in the rise of India as a global player.

Concept note-3: -Recently, the government has accorded the ‘Maharatna’ status to state-owned Power Finance Corporation (PFC). An order to this effect was issued by the Department of Public Enterprises, under the Ministry of Finance.

Concept note-4: -Maharatna Status to Power Finance Corporation Central Government has given “Maharatna” status to Power Finance Corporation (PFC). PFC has become the 11th state-owned entity in India to enter the Maharatna category.
