The Government of India recently formed a committee to protect language, land and the culture of Ladakh. Who is the head of the committee?

(A) G Satish Reddy

(B) ** G Kishan Reddy

(C) Ajay Gupta

(D) Deepak Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Committee will be headed by the Minister of State for Home G Kishan Reddy and will include elected representatives from Ladakh, Ladakh Autonomous Hill Development Council, central government, and the Ladakh administration.

Concept note-2: -The Centre has constituted a High Powered Committee, HPC, to discuss measures to protect Ladakh’s unique culture, language and employment. AIR correspondent reports that a 17 member committee has been constituted under the Chairmanship of Minister of State for Home Affairs Nityanand Rai.

Concept note-3: -Ladakh does not have an elected legislative assembly. The two districts of Ladakh both elect their own autonomous district council-the Leh Autonomous Hill development council and the Kargil Autonomous Hill development Council, which have competence over a range of domestic affairs.
