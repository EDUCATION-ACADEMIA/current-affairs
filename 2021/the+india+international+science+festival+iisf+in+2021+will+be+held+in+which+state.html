The India International Science Festival (IISF) in 2021 will be held in which state?

(A) West Bengal

(B) Kerala

(C) ** Goa

(D) Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first IISF was celebrated in New Delhi in 2015. The festival theme of 2021 was “Celebrating Creativity in Science, Technology and Innovation for Prosperous India", which was held between 11 December 2021 and 13 December 2021 at Panaji, Goa, India.

Concept note-2: -The 7th edition of the four-day India International Science Festival (IISF) is being held in Panaji, Goa, from December 10 to 13, 2021. The Goa-based National Centre for Polar and Ocean Research (NCPOR), under the Ministry of Earth Sciences, is the nodal agency to organize IISF 2021.

Concept note-3: -Madhya Pradesh Council of Science and Technology (MPCOST), Bhopal is the nodal agency for this science festival. More than 8, 000 delegates from across the country will participate in this science festival from January 21 to 24.

Concept note-4: -The eighth India International Science Festival is to be held in Bhopal. The Union Minister Jitendra Singh inaugurated the festival. According to the Government of India, the festival is a step in moving towards “AMRIT KAAL”.

Concept note-5: -Curtains xame down on 4 day India International Science Festival, IISF-2022 in Bhopal with a call for making India one of the leading Scientific and Technological Powers of the World.
