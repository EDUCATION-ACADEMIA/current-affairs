The Indian Air Force is participating in the Air Show of Sri Lanka Air Force (SLAF) in 2021 to mark which anniversary celebration of SLAF?

(A) 80

(B) ** 70

(C) 50

(D) 100

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Air Force will be participating in the 70th Anniversary Celebrations of the Sri Lankan Air Force. The IAF’s aerobatic display teams, the rotary wing ‘Sarang’, fixed-wing ‘Suryakirans’ and Light Combat Aircraft Tejas arrived at Sri Lanka’s capital city, Colombo on February 27, 2021.

Concept note-2: -Indian Air Force Day 2022: Indian Air Force Day is observed on October 8, every year. The Indian Air Force was officially established on October 8, 1932 and this year marked as the 90th-anniversary celebration of the IAF.

Concept note-3: -Indian Air Force Day 2022: Major announcements made during 90th anniversary celebrations. The Indian Air Force on Saturday (September 8) celebrated its 90th year anniversary. The theme for this year’s anniversary celebrations is IAF: Transforming for the Future .

Concept note-4: -The Air Show is a part of the 70th-anniversary celebrations of the Sri Lanka Air Force (SLAF). Earlier, the SKAT- Suryakiran Aerobatic team participated in the 50th anniversary of SLAF in 2001, in Sri Lanka.
