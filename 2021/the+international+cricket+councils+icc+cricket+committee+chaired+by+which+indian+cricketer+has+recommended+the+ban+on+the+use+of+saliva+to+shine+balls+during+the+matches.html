The International Cricket Council’s (ICC) Cricket Committee chaired by which indian cricketer has recommended the ban on the use of saliva to shine balls during the matches?

(A) Kapil Dev

(B) Sunil Gavaskar

(C) Pragyan Ojha

(D) ** Anil Kumble

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The recommendations were made by the ICC Cricket Committee led by BCCI President Sourav Ganguly.

Concept note-2: -Dominic Sibley of England becomes 1st cricketer to break the ‘no saliva on ball’ rule.

Concept note-3: -The ban on the use of saliva to shine the ball has been made permanent by the International Cricket Council. Cricket’s governing body made the decision at a meeting of its chief executives’ committee.

Concept note-4: -The prohibition has been in place for over two years in international cricket as a Covid-related temporary measure. The International Cricket Council (ICC) has decided to permanently ban the use of saliva to polish the ball.
