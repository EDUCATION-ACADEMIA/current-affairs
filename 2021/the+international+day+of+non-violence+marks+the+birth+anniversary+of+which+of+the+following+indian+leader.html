The International Day of Non-Violence marks the birth anniversary of which of the following Indian leader?

(A) Vallabhbhai Patel

(B) Subhas Chandra Bose

(C) Jawaharlal Nehru

(D) ** Mahatma Gandhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Day of Non-Violence is observed on 2 October, the birthday of Mahatma Gandhi, leader of the Indian independence movement and pioneer of the philosophy and strategy of non-violence.

Concept note-2: -The General Assembly, in a resolution adopted on 5 June 2007, established the commemoration, the International Day as an occasion to disseminate the message of non-violence, including through education and public awareness.

Concept note-3: -On June 15, 2007, the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) passed a resolution to commemorate October 2 as the International Day of Non-Violence, with the core objective to “disseminate the message of non-violence, including through education and public awareness.”

Concept note-4: -International Day of Non-Violence 2022:Theme There is no special theme for this day. The event of the International Day of Non-Violence is “disseminate the message of non-violence through education and public awareness and reaffirm the desire for a culture of peace, tolerance, understanding and non-violence”.

Concept note-5: -Gandhi Jayanti is being observed today, on October 2, 2022. Mahatma Gandhi was born on October 2, 1869 in Porbandar city of Gujrat.
