The management control of the Mumbai International Airport has been taken over by which company?

(A) ** Adani Airport Holdings Limited

(B) Airport Authority of India

(C) GVK Airport Holdings Limited

(D) GMR Airports Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Adani Airport Holdings Limited (AAHL) takes management control of Mumbai International Airport from the GVK Group .

Concept note-2: -Read more news on AAHL now owns 74% of MIAL. The culmination of the deal makes the Adani Group India’s biggest private airport operator.

Concept note-3: -Adani Enterprises manages its airport operations through Adani Airport Holdings, which has a mandate to operate six airports – Ahmedabad, Lucknow, Mangaluru, Jaipur, Guwahati and Thiruvananthapuram – as well as Mumbai and greenfield Navi Mumbai International airports.

Concept note-4: -Adani Group took over the management of the Mumbai airport from the GVK Group in July 2021. In an interview to television channel NDTV on Tuesday, Sanjay Reddy said, “There was absolutely no pressure from the Adani group or anyone else for us to sell the Mumbai airport."

Concept note-5: -Adani Enterprises Ltd. has taken control of Mumbai’s international airport from GVK Group, in a bold bet by billionaire Gautam Adani that travel will stage a rapid recovery after being crushed by the pandemic.
