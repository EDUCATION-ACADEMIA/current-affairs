The Pacific Air Chiefs Symposium 2021 (PACS-21) was held at which place?

(A) ** Hawaii

(B) Georgia

(C) Ohio

(D) Florida

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Air Chief Marshal RKS Bhadauria PVSM AVSM VM ADC, Chief of the Air Staff (CAS) attended the Pacific Air Chiefs Symposium 2021 (PACS-21) at Joint Base Pearl Harbor-Hickam, Hawaii from 30 Aug to 02 Sep 21.

Concept note-2: -JOINT BASE PEARL HARBOR-HICKAM, Hawaii (AFNS) – Air chiefs and senior enlisted leaders from the Indo-Pacific region gathered for the Pacific Air Chiefs Symposium 2021 and the first-ever Senior Enlisted Leadership Summit at Headquarters Pacific Air Forces, Aug. 31-Sept. 2.

Concept note-3: -Overall, PACS-21 enables the U.S. to collaborate alongside allies and partners throughout the Indo-Pacific region to address enduring challenges.

Concept note-4: -Air Chief Marshal RKS Bhadauria attended a three-day-long Pacific Air Chiefs Symposium 2021 at Joint Base Pearl Harbor-Hickam in Hawaii.
