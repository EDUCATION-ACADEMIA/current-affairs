The partial lunar eclipse will occur in India on which date?

(A) ** November 19

(B) November 20

(C) November 21

(D) November 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Alternatively you can view the old animation by clicking here. The animation shows what the eclipse approximately looks like in New Delhi. Stages and times of the eclipse are outlined below. All times are local time (IST) for New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -The next lunar eclipse which will be visible from India is on 28th October, 2023 and the same is a partial eclipse. Last lunar eclipse which was visible from India was on 19 November, 2021 and it was a partial eclipse.

Concept note-3: -Lunar Eclipse 2022 in India date and time, Chandra Grahan 2022. As per timeanddate.com website, Lunar Eclipse will occur on November 8, Tuesday. The lunar eclipse will start on November 8 at 5:32 PM and end at 6:18 PM.

Concept note-4: -Chandra Grahan Lunar Eclipse 2022 is on Tuesday, 08 November 2022. It will start on November 08 at 17:32 PM in the evening and will end at 18.18 PM in the evening. The longest duration of totality would be 1 Hour 24 Minutes and 28 Seconds.

Concept note-5: -Lunar eclipse November 2022 India: Date, time, list of cities you can see and live stream. A partial Chandra Grahan 2022 will be seen in India on Tuesday, November 8, 2022, at 2:39 pm. There will also be a full eclipse that will be seen around 3:46 pm. The maximum lunar eclipse will appear at 4:29 pm.
