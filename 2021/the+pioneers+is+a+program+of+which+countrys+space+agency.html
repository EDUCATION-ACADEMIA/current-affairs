The Pioneers is a program of which country’s space agency?

(A) India

(B) UK

(C) ** USA

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Pioneer programs were two series of United States lunar and planetary space probes exploration.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The Pioneers Program was started by US space agency NASA in 2020, which aims to research on astrophysics science using smaller hardware.

Concept note-3: -The Pioneers or Astrophysics Pioneers Program is a NASA program started in 2020 intended to use small-size hardware. The missions are expected to include SmallSats, Balloon payloads, and payloads attached to the ISS, with a $20M cost cap.

Concept note-4: -Pioneer 1 (also known as Able 2) was an American space probe, the first under the auspices of NASA, which was launched by a Thor-Able rocket on 11 October 1958.
