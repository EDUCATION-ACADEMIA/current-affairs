The Project BOLD has been launched from which state in India?

(A) ** Rajasthan

(B) Gujarat

(C) Haryana

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Rajasthan. Project BOLD was launched on 4th July 2021. It was launched from a tribal village Nichla Mandwa in the Udaipur district in Rajasthan. Project BOLD stands for Bamboo Oasis on Lands in Drought.

Concept note-2: -1. When was Project BOLD inaugurated? On July 4, Project BOLD was launched in Nichla Mandwa, a tribal village in the Udaipur district of Rajasthan. On July 27, 2021, KVIC used BSF to plant 1000 bamboo saplings as part of Project BOLD.

Concept note-3: -It functions under the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises.

Concept note-4: -Project BOLD full form is Bamboo Oasis on Lands in Drought. It is the first of its kind exercise in India to provide livelihood, protect land from degradation and boost the economy.

Concept note-5: -BOLD stands for Bamboo Oasis on Lands in Drought. Launched by Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC).
