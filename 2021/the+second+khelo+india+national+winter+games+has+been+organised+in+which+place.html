The second Khelo India National Winter Games has been organised in which place?

(A) Dehradun

(B) ** Gulmarg

(C) Gangtok

(D) Dalhousie

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi in February 2021 virtually inaugurated the second edition of the “Khelo India Winter Games” held at Gulmarg.

Concept note-2: -The second edition of the Games will see a participation of over 1200 people, including 600 athletes across 27 States and Union Territories. The participation list of athletes also includes the Indian Army and the Jawahar Institute of Mountaineering and Winter Sports.

Concept note-3: -The 1st edition of the Khelo India Winter Games took place in 2020 and Jammu and Kashmir have come on top in both editions of the games so far.

Concept note-4: -The second edition of Khelo India Youth Games was inaugurated in Pune on 9 January 2019. Students from colleges and Universities will be allowed to compete in the games. Tennis and table tennis will also be a part of the games. The 1st edition of Khelo India games was held in Delhi in January 2018.

Concept note-5: -The ski resort of Gulmarg in Jammu and Kashmir’s Baramulla district will host the third edition of Khelo India National Winter Games next month, officials said on Sunday. The third edition of the games will start from February 10 and culminate on February 14, they said.
