The State Bank of India (SBI) recently inaugurated an ATM on a Houseboat in which city?

(A) Shillong

(B) Guwahati

(C) ** Srinagar

(D) Kohima

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The floating ATM was inaugurated on August 16 by SBI Chairman Dinesh Khare. The bank said that the ATM, besides fulfilling a long-standing need, will add attraction to the charm of Srinagar. “SBI opened an ATM on a Houseboat at #DalLake, Srinagar for the convenience of locals and tourists.

Concept note-2: -SBI had launched the first floating ATM in Kerala in 2004. This floating ATM of SBI was installed in Jhankar yacht owned by Kerala Shipping and Inland Navigation Corporation (KSINC).

Concept note-3: -Q. Recently, which bank has opened an ATM on a Houseboat at Dal Lake, Srinagar? Notes: State Bank of India (SBI) has opened an ATM on a Houseboat at Dal Lake, Srinagar for the convenience of locals and tourists.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is State Bank Of India. State Bank of India opened a floating ATM on a Houseboat at Dal Lake in Jammu and Kashmir’s Srinagar for the convenience of locals and tourists. The floating ATM was inaugurated by SBI Chairman Dinesh Khare.

Concept note-5: -Dal Lake in Jammu and Kashmir’s Srinagar got its first floating ATM. This ATM has been introduced for the convenience of the people living on the lake as well as the tourists staying in houseboats.
