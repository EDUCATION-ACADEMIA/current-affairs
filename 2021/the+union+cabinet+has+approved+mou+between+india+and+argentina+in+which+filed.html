The Union Cabinet has approved MoU between India and Argentina in which filed?

(A) Oceanography

(B) Space Exploration

(C) ** Mineral Resources

(D) AI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-3: -The MoU seeks for cooperation in the field of geology and mineral resources between Geological Survey of India (Ministry of Mines) and Geological Survey of Finland.
