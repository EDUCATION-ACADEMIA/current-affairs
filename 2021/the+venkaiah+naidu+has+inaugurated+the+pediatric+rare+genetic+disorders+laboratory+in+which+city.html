The Venkaiah Naidu has inaugurated the Pediatric Rare Genetic Disorders Laboratory in which city?

(A) Pune

(B) Chennai

(C) ** Hyderabad

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Venkaiah Naidu having a look at the facilities in the Centre for DNA Fingerprinting and Diagnostics in Hyderabad on Saturday. Vice-President M Venkaiah Naidu on Saturday inaugurated the ‘Paediatric Rare Genetic Disorders’ lab at the Centre for DNA Fingerprinting and Diagnostics here.

Concept note-2: -HYDERABAD: The Hyderabad-based Centre for DNA Fingerprinting and Diagnostics (CDFD) has established a first-of-its-kind Paediatric Rare Genetic Disorders Laboratory.

Concept note-3: -Some of the common genetic disorders in India are: Down Syndrome; Thalassemia; Sickle Cell Anemia; Cystic Fibrosis, and Tay-Sachs. Down Syndrome, also, known as Trisomy 21, is a chromosomal disorder caused because of an abnormal cell division resulting in an additional copy of chromosome 21.
