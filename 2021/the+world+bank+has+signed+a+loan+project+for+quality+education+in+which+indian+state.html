The World Bank has signed a loan project for Quality Education in which Indian state?

(A) Goa

(B) Assam

(C) Manipur

(D) ** Nagaland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Government of India and World Bank Sign Project to Improve Quality of India’s Education in Nagaland.

Concept note-2: -Washington, DC, June 21, 2022 - The World Bank Board of Executive Directors today approved additional financing of $250 million for Outcomes for Accelerated Learning (GOAL), a program which aims to improve education results for children across the state of Gujarat.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 6. STARS project was launched by the Government of India and the World Bank in 2020. The project seeks to cover 6 states- Himachal Pradesh, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Kerala, and Odisha.

Concept note-4: -WASHINGTON, DC, September, 21, 2022 - The World Bank’s Executive Board of Directors today approved a $350 million loan to the western Indian state of Gujarat to improve the quality and access of its public healthcare services, with special focus on adolescent girls and disease surveillance.
