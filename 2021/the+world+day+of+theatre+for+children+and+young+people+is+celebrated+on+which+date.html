The World Day of Theatre for Children and Young People is celebrated on which date?

(A) March 18

(B) March 19

(C) ** March 20

(D) March 21

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Day of Theatre for Children and Young People – March 20, 2023. United States. The World Day of Theatre for Children and Young People is an annual event that takes place on March 20 across the World.

Concept note-2: -March 20, 2022 - Sunday It is a campaign led by the International Association of Theatre for Children and Young People, promoting the message “Take a child to the theatre today".

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Theatre and a Culture of Peace. World Theatre Day is celebrated on 27 March every year. The theme for World Theatre Day 2022 is “Theatre and a Culture of Peace.” World Theatre Day was first celebrated by the International Theatre Institute (ITI) in the year 1961, on 27 March.

Concept note-4: -Ever since, each year on the 27th March (date of the opening of the 1962 “Theatre of Nations” season in Paris), World Theatre Day has been celebrated in many and varied ways by ITI Centres - of which there are now more than 90 throughout the world.

Concept note-5: -WORLD THEATRE DAY 2022: SIGNIFICANCE The significance of this day is to raise awareness regarding the importance of theatre and to know the importance of theatre around the world. The day also provides opportunities for theatrical communities to put forward their work on a large scale.
