(A) May

(B) April

(C) ** March

(D) June

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -History. The first edition of Pariksha pe Charcha was held on 16 February 2018; the second edition was held on 29 January 2019, in New Delhi; the third was held in 2020 and the fourth one was held on 7 April 2021 through an online meeting and the latest edition was held on 27 january 2023.

Concept note-2: -Pariksha Pe Charcha 2022 with Hon’ble Prime Minister Narendra Modi to be held on 1st April. Leave your stress and nervousness behind and get ready to set those butterflies in your stomach free!

Concept note-3: -Pariksha Pe Charcha Live Watch Here Pariksha Pe Charcha 2023 event will begin soon. Prime Minister Narendra Modi reaches Talkatora Indoor stadium. Pariksha pe Charcha registration will close down today, January 27, 2023.

Concept note-4: -The 5th edition of PPC2022 is going to be held on 1st April, 2022 at Talkatora Stadium, New Delhi. Hon’ble PM Shri Narendra Modi will interact with students and share his insights on how to beat exam stress.

Concept note-5: -Dr Jitendra Singh says, ‘Pariksha Pe Charcha’ is a movement driven by Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s vision to bring together Students, Parents, Teachers and the Society on a single platform.
