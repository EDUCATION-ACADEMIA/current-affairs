The 74th World Health Assembly endorsed a decision recognizing which day as World Neglected Tropical Diseases Day?

(A) ** 30 January

(B) l0 June

(C) 13 February

(D) 15 March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -74th World Health Assembly endorsed a decision recognizing 30 January as World Neglected Tropical Diseases Day (’World NTD Day’). World NTD Day commemorates the simultaneous launch of the first NTD road map and the London Declaration on NTDs on 30 January 2012.

Concept note-2: -World Neglected Tropical Diseases Day (World NTD Day) is observed on January 30 every year.

Concept note-3: -World Neglected Tropical Diseases Day is observed every year on 30 January. This date marks the launch of the first WHO road map and the launch of the London declaration on neglected tropical diseases (NTDs), both in 2012.

Concept note-4: -Neglected tropical diseases (NTDs) Buruli Ulcer. Chagas Disease. Cysticercosis.
