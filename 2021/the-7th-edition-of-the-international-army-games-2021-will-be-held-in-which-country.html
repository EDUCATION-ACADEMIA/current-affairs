The 7th edition of the International Army Games, 2021, will be held in which country?

(A) USA

(B) Japan

(C) ** Russia

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year, the Chinese military sent nine teams abroad to participate in competitions in Russia, Iran, Kazakhstan, Algeria, and Uzbekistan. The International Army Games, initiated by the Russian Defense Ministry, have served as an international platform for militaries to exchange experience and strengthen cooperation.

Concept note-2: -The opening ceremony for the International Army Games 2022 (Army Games 2022) took place solemly at Patriot Park on the outskirts of Moscow, Russia on August 15. This year event drew the participation of 264 teams from 34 nations to compete in 34 events.

Concept note-3: -The event saw the presence of a total 260 teams from 45 countries participating in 34 categories, which was held in 11 countries. ii. Russia, with a total of 34 medals was ranked 1st in the event. Note – India ranked 1st in the 2019 Army Scouts Master Competition that was held in Jaisalmer, Rajasthan.

Concept note-4: -The 7th edition of the International Army Games, 2021, will be held from 22 August to 04 September 2021 in Russia. The competition will be hosted in eleven countries in the 2021 Games. Over 280 teams from 42 countries will compete in the game to show their combat skills, professionalism and determination to win.
