The Annual Convention of Association of Domestic Tour Operators of India 2021 has been organized at which place?

(A) Sabarmati Riverfront

(B) Sardar Sarovar Dam

(C) Gir National Park

(D) ** Statue of Unity

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A three-day Annual Convention of Association of Domestic Tour Operators of India starts at Kevadia in Gujarat today.

Concept note-2: -The Indian Association of Tour Operators (IATO) is the National body of the tourism industry. It has over 1400 members covering all segments of Tourism Industry. Established in 1982, IATO today has international acceptance, and linkages.

Concept note-3: -ADTOI- Association of Domestic Tour Operators Of India.

Concept note-4: -According to the India Tourism Statistic 2021 report by India’s Ministry of Tourism report, there were 902 domestic tour operators in total in 2021.
