The annual meeting of the Defence Ministers of Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) member states is in which country?

(A) ** Tajikistan

(B) Kazakhstan

(C) Algeria

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is located in Beijing. Each Member State has a Permanent Representative to the SCO. The Regional Anti-Terrorist Structure (RATS), headquartered in Tashkent, Uzbekistan, is a permanent organ of the SCO which serves to promote cooperation of member states against terrorism, separatism and extremism.

Concept note-2: -Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh is visiting Tashkent, Uzbekistan from 23rd to 25th August, 2022 to attend the Annual Meeting of the Defence Ministers’ of Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Member States.

Concept note-3: -SCO or the Shanghai Cooperation Organization has a total of eight members. China, India, Tajikistan, Russia, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Pakistan, and Uzbekistan are all members of the SCO.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi [India], November 18 (ANI): India has launched the official website of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) as it will host the next SCO summit as a chairman of organisation in 2023. The website highlights the events that will be undertaken under the chairmanship of India next year.
