The book titled ‘Stories I Must Tell:The Emotional Journey of an Actor’ is a memoir of which actor?

(A) Shatrughan Sinha

(B) ** Kabir Bedi

(C) Dilip Kumar

(D) Amitabh Bachchan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Monk, the maverick Kabir Bedi, who became an actor in India, Italy and Hollywood has penned a wonderful tale of his extraordinary life in “Stories I Must Tell .. The Emotional Life of an Actor “ HB Westland Publications, 311 Pages, Price Rs.

Concept note-2: -About the author Kabir Bedi now a debut author with his best-selling autobiography, “Stories I Must Tell: The Emotional Life of an Actor” described by The Week as a ‘must read’, is an actor with a career that spans three continents in three mediums: film, television and theatre.
