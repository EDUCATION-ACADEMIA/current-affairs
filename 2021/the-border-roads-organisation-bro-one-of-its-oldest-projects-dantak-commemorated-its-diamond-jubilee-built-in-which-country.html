The Border Roads Organisation (BRO) one of its oldest projects DANTAK commemorated its Diamond Jubilee built in which country?

(A) ** Bhutan

(B) Pakistan

(C) Myanmar

(D) SriLanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: Recently, Project DANTAK’s diamond Jubilee was commemorated which is located in Bhutan. It was established in 1961 to construct the motorable roads in the Kingdom.

Concept note-2: -Project DANTAK completes 60 years in Bhutan Project DANTAK is commemorating its Diamond Jubilee in Bhutan.

Concept note-3: -At the end of the study, the CSG identified a network of 73 roads, called the India-China Border Roads (ICBR), to be developed along the Indo-China border. The Cabinet Committee on Security (CCS) in 1999 approved the construction of these roads by the Border Roads Organisation (BRO) under the Ministry of Defence.

Concept note-4: -The Organisation was formed on May 7, 1960, to maintain and develop the Indian remote areas of north and northeast situated near the borders.

Concept note-5: -The BRDB was set up in March 1960 as an inter-ministerial body, under the chairmanship of the Prime Minister, to oversee the development of road communications in the border states of the north and north-east. The BRDB was re-constituted in 1985 with the Defence Minister as the chairman.
