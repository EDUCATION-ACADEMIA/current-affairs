The CRPF observed which anniversary on 19 March 2021?

(A) ** 82

(B) 61

(C) 75

(D) 94

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Central Reserve Police Force is celebrating its 83rd Raising Day today. It was on this very day in the year 1939, that the Force was raised in Neemuch with a strength of mere 1 Battalion.

Concept note-2: -CRPF came into existence as the ‘Crown Representative’s Police’ on 27 July 1939. After Independence, it became the Central Reserve Police Force on the enactment of the ‘CRPF Act’ on 28 December 1949, which constituted CRPF as an armed force of the Union.

Concept note-3: -The Central Reserve Police Force came into existence as Crown Representative’s Police on 27th July 1939. It became the Central Reserve Police Force on enactment of the CRPF Act on 28th December 1949. It has completed 83 years of glorious history.

Concept note-4: -On 27th July 1939, a native police force was raised by the Britishers to suppress the rebellions and maintain law and order situation. This force was named as “Crown Representative Police” and it was headquarter at Nimach.
