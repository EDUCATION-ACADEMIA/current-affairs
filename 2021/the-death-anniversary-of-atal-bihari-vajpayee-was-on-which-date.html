The death anniversary of Atal Bihari Vajpayee was on which date?

(A) August 15

(B) August 14

(C) ** August 16

(D) August 17

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After the 1996 general election, the BJP emerged as the single largest party in the Lok Sabha, the lower house of Parliament. Vajpayee was invited by President Shankar Dayal Sharma to form a government, but after 13 days in office, proved unable to muster a governing majority and resigned.

Concept note-2: -Atal Bihari Vajpayee (25 December 1924 – 16 August 2018) was an Indian politician. He served as the 10th Prime Minister of India for six years from 1998 through 2004. He was also serving as Prime Minister from May 1996 through June 1996. He founded the Bharatiya Janata Party.
