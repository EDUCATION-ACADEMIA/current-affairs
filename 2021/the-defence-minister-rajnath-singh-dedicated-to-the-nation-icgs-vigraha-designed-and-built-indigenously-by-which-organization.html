The Defence Minister Rajnath Singh dedicated to the nation ICGS Vigraha, designed and built indigenously by which organization?

(A) Goa Shipyard Limited

(B) Mazagon Dock Limited, Mumbai

(C) ** Larsen & Toubro Ship Building Limited

(D) Garden Reach Shipbuilders and Engineers, Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ship has been designed and built indigenously by Larsen & Toubro Ship Building Limited.

Concept note-2: -ICGS Samarth: The Indian Coast Guard’s latest and largest Offshore Patrol Vessel (OPV). Samarth is first in the series of six 105m offshore patrol vessels and has been built by Goa Shipyard Limited.

Concept note-3: -Indian Coast Guard Ship (ICGS) Saksham is the 5th and last Offshore Patrol Vessel designed and developed by Goa Shipyard Limited (GSL) for Coast Guard.

Concept note-4: -Defence Minister Rajnath Singh today commissioned the state of the art Indian Coast Guard Ship Vigraha in Chennai Harbour.

Concept note-5: -The Indian Coast Guard thus came into being on 01st Feb 1977. with a fleet strength of just seven ships for surveillance in the Indian waters and the Exclusive Economic Zone, The Indian Coast Guard was formally inaugrated by the then Prime Minister Shri Morarji Desai on 19th Aug 1978.
