The Delhi cabinet has approved “Mukhyamantri Vigyan Pratibha Pariksha”. How much amount will be provided to students of Class 9 in schools of Delhi as a science scholarship?

(A) Rs 1, 000

(B) Rs 3, 000

(C) ** Rs 5, 000

(D) Rs 7, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is 5, 000. The Delhi cabinet has approved ‘Mukhyamantri Vigyan Pratibha Pariksha’. Under this, Rs 5, 000 will be provided to 1, 000 meritorious students of Class 9 in schools of Delhi as a science scholarship. The scholarship will provide a boost to science education in secondary classes.

Concept note-2: -5, 000/- per to class 9th and class10th students and Rs. 10, 000/- per year to class 11th and class 12th students. ST/SC/OBC Students of class 9th to 12th who scored minimum 75% in previous class, for them no family income limit is set to apply for scheme.

Concept note-3: -Delhi Mukhyamantri Vigyan Pratibha Pariksha Yojana 2023 Under the ‘Delhi Mukhyamantri Vigyan Pratibha Pariksha’ scheme around 1, 000 students who are studying in class 9th in any school will getae scholarship amount of ₹5, 000 from the state government.

Concept note-4: -Benefits of MVPP Earn scholarship of Rs 5000/-. Provides opportunity to understand about the competitive exams.
