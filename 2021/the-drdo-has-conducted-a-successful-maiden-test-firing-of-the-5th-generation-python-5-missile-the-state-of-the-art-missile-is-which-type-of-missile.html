The DRDO has conducted a successful maiden test firing of the 5th generation Python-5 missile. The state of the art missile is which type of missile?

(A) Air-to-Surface Missile

(B) ** Air-to-Air Missile

(C) Surface-to-Air Missile

(D) Surface-to-Surface Missile

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Air-to-Air Missile. The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), successfully test-fired the 5th generation Python-5 missile from Tejas aircraft, at Goa. The Python-5 missile is an Air-to-Air missile (AAM).

Concept note-2: -Tejas, India’s indigenous Light Combat Aircraft, added the 5th generation Python-5 Air-to-Air Missile (AAM) in its air-to-air weapons capability on April 27, 2021.

Concept note-3: -Python-5 is a fifth-generation air-to-air missile (AAM) manufactured by Rafael Advanced Defense Systems. It is the newest member in the range of Python AAMs. The missile can engage enemy aircraft from very short ranges and near beyond visual range.

Concept note-4: -Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO) conducted a successful maiden flight-test of Phase-II Ballistic Missile Defence (BMD) interceptor AD-1 missile with large kill altitude bracket from APJ Abdul Kalam Island off the coast of Odisha on November 02, 2022.

Concept note-5: -India successfully test-fires Brahmos missile.
