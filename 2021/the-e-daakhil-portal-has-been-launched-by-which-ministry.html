The E-Daakhil portal has been launched by which Ministry?

(A) Ministry of Commerce and Industry

(B) Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

(C) ** Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution

(D) Ministry of Defence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On successful completion of 2 years of e-daakhil portal, since its inception on 7th September 2020, Secretary Department of Consumer Affairs, Shri Rohit Kumar Singh along with Additional Secretary and Chief Commissioner of Central Consumer Protection Authority (CCPA) Mrs.

Concept note-2: -A K Sai J Saravanan Kumar, Hon’ble Minister of Civil Supplies and Consumer Affairs (CS & CA) launched e-daakhil on 01-09-2022, an online portal to receive and dispose consumer complaints.

Concept note-3: -NIC developed eDaakhil portal facilitating e-filing of consumer complaints in State & District Consumer Commissions has been launched in Rajasthan.

Concept note-4: -The e-daakhil portal empowers the consumer and their advocates to file the consumer complaints along with payment of requisite fees online from anywhere for the redressal of their complaints.

Concept note-5: -Going through restrictions on consumers due to COVID-19, the e-daakhil portal was introduced as inexpensive, speedy and hassle-free mechanism for filing consumer complaints.
