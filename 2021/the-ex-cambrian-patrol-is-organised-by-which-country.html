The Ex Cambrian Patrol is organised by which country?

(A) Australia

(B) ** United Kindom

(C) India

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ex Cambrian Patrol organised by the UK Army is considered the ultimate test of human endurance, team spirit and is sometimes referred as the Olympics of Military Patrolling among militaries in the world.

Concept note-2: -Exercise Cambrian Patrol is the premier patrolling event of the British Army which is held in Wales and hosted by Headquarters 160th (Welsh) Brigade.

Concept note-3: -Soldiers from 1st Battalion Royal New Zealand Infantry Regiment will have enjoyed their 11, 450-mile trip back home from Mid Wales clutching gold medals.

Concept note-4: -Indian Army team of 8 GARHWAL wins Gold Medal in the prestigious Cambrian Patrol held at United Kingdom from 17 – 26 Oct 2014. Out of more than 140 participating teams from all over the world the Indian Army’s team won the Gold Medal in the prestigious Exercise Cambrian Patrol 2014.

Concept note-5: -KazInd 2022: India and Kazakhstan carry out joint military drill in Meghalaya. To strengthen positive military relations between India and Kazakhstan, the nations conducted their 6th edition of Army Joint Exercise KazInd 2022 in Meghalaya’s Umroi.
