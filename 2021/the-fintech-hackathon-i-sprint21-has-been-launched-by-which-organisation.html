The FinTech Hackathon, ‘I-Sprint’21’ has been launched by which organisation?

(A) ** IFSCA

(B) NASSCOM

(C) NPCI

(D) RBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. I-Sprint’21 is a FinTech Hackathon Series launched by? Notes: The International Financial Services Centres Authority (IFSCA) along with GIFT city has launched the FinTech Hackathon Series named “I-Sprint’21”. The first series of the Hackathon is “Sprint01: BankTech” with special focus on Banking.

Concept note-2: -International Financial Services Centres Authority (IFSCA), in its endeavour to support a World Class FinTech Hub at GIFT IFSC, had launched I- Sprint’21, the global FinTech Hackathon as part of InFinity Forum 2021 (https://www.infinityforum.in/).

Concept note-3: -What is a Fintech Open Hackathon? The Hackathon will provide an opportunity for innovators, digital creators and developers from all over India to think, ideate and code. Winning teams stand to win exciting cash prizes worth Rs 5 lakhs.
