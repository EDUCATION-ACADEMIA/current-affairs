The first “Vishwakarma Vatika” at “Hunar Haat” has been set up in which place?

(A) ** Rampur

(B) Ranchi

(C) Kota

(D) Patna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first such “Vishwakarma Vatika” has been set up in “Hunar Haat” at Rampur to protect, preserve and promote India’s glorious legacy of centuries-old skills and craftsmanship.

Concept note-2: -The first “Hunar Haat” was organized in the year 2016 at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi during India International Trade Fair (IITF).

Concept note-3: -Hunar Haat has been conceptualized to protect and promote the country’s ancestral legacy of arts and crafts in the current global competition and to support the traditional artisans and craftsmen.

Concept note-4: -41st Hunar Haat in news Recently, Union Minister for Minority Affairs will formally inaugurate 41st “Hunar Haat”, the “Kumbh of Kaushal Kuber” on May19, 2022.

Concept note-5: -Speaking about Hunar Haat which is an initiative of Ministry of Minority Affairs, Union Minister Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi said that one will get to witness ‘Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat’ and experience the essence of ‘Unity in Diversity’ at the Hunar Haat in Mumbai.
