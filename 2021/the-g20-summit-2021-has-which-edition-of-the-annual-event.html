The G20 Summit 2021 has which edition of the annual event?

(A) 18th

(B) 20th

(C) ** 16th

(D) 14th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The G20 Urban 20 Summit 2021 will take place ahead of the G20 Leaders Summit 2021, which is to be hosted by Italy in October 2021. Urban 20 (U20) is one of the G20 Engagement Groups. The 2021 G20, under the Italian Presidency, will focus on three broad, interconnected pillars of action: People, Planet, Prosperity.

Concept note-2: -G20 Summit in Bali, Indonesia, 15 - 16 November 2022 The 17th G20 Heads of State and Government Summit took place on 15-16 November 2022 in Bali. The Summit was the pinnacle of the G20 process and intense work was carried out within the Ministerial Meetings, Working Groups, and Engagement Groups throughout the year.

Concept note-3: -The 2022 G20 Bali summit (Indonesian: Konferensi Tingkat Tinggi G20 Bali 2022) was the seventeenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), which was held in Nusa Dua, Bali, Indonesia on 15–16 November 2022. Indonesia’s presidency began on 1 December 2021, leading up to the summit in the fourth quarter of 2022.

Concept note-4: -The 2023 G20 Delhi summit (Hindi: 2023 G20 ) is the upcoming eighteenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), a summit scheduled to take place in Pragati Maidan, New Delhi in 2023. India’s presidency began on 1 December 2022, leading up to the summit in the fourth quarter of 2023.

Concept note-5: -G20 Summit 2023: India will host the G20 Summit 2023 in New Delhi, as India has the chair. Let us have a look at list of host nations of various G20 meetings.
