The IITF 2021 will be held in November 2021. The event will be which of the following edition of the annual show?

(A) ** 40th

(B) 50th

(C) 35th

(D) 45th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 41st edition of this annual event will take place in 2022. The IITF will take place at Pragati Maidan in New Delhi from November 14 to November 27.

Concept note-2: -The 41st edition of the India International Trade Fair (IITF) has started at Pragati Maidan in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -“IITF offers an ideal platform to showcase Indian products under the ‘Vocal for Local’ campaign, and infuse renewed confidence and vigour in the Indian economy, ” it said. Overseas participation is from 12 countries, including Afghanistan, Bangladesh, Bahrain, Belarus, Iran, Nepal, Thailand, Turkey, UAE, and the UK.

Concept note-4: -The theme of the IITF 2022 is “Vocal for Local, Local to Global” and was held from November 14 – 27, 2022. It was organized by the India Trade Promotion Organization (ITPO), a division of MoCI.

Concept note-5: -The 21 st edition of India International Mega Trade Fair commenced on December 16 showcasing Kolkata as the preferred destination for trade and commerce and as a retail hub.
