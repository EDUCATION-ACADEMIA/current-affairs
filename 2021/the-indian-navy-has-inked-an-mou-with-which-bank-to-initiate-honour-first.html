The Indian Navy has inked an MoU with which bank to initiate ‘Honour FIRST’?

(A) HDFC

(B) ** IDFC

(C) SBI

(D) PNB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is IDFC FIRST Bank. The Indian Navy has inked a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Infrastructure Development Finance Company (IDFC) FIRST Bank to initiate ‘Honour FIRST’. It is a premium banking solution for serving personnel and veterans of the Indian Navy.

Concept note-2: -IDFC FIRST Bank signs up with Indian Navy to offer Honour FIRST banking solutions.

Concept note-3: -HDFC Bank Ltd., signed an MoU with the Indian Navy to offer a salary account to Navy personnel.

Concept note-4: -Personnel serving the Indian army, navy, air force, paramilitary, or its Veterans can open Honour FIRST Defence Account.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution: The correct answer is Indian Bank. Indian Bank has signed an MoU with the Paralympic Committee of India (PCI) as one of the banking partners of the Paralympic Games. The games are scheduled to commence on August 24, 2021, in Tokyo, Japan.
