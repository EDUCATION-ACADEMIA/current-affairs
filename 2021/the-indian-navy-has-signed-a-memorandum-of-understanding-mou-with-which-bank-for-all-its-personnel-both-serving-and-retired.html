The Indian Navy has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with which bank for all its personnel-both serving and retired?

(A) SBI

(B) ** Kotak Mahindra Bank

(C) HDFC

(D) ICICI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai, 31 January 2023: Union Bank of India signed Memorandum of Understanding with Indian Navy for Salary Accounts of their employees’ including Agniveers on 30 January 2023.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is State Bank of India.

Concept note-3: -Axis Bank signed an MoU with the Indian Navy to offer a defence service salary package under ‘’Power Salute” at New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -This MoU is a part of the bank’s ‘Power Salute’ initiative. India’s third largest private lender Axis Bank on Wednesday signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Indian Coast Guard to roll out a defence service salary package with added benefits and features.

Concept note-5: -Overview. IDBI Bank is proud to be associated with the naval branch of the Armed forces of India ‘Indian Navy’.
