The International Asteroid Day is observed on which day?

(A) 29 June

(B) 28 June

(C) 27 June

(D) ** 30 June

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -June 30 is observed as the International Asteroid Day around the globe to educate people about asteroids. Every year, events are held on this day in which astronauts and experts talk about the opportunities and risks associated with asteroids.

Concept note-2: -On June 30, International Asteroid Day will have everyone looking toward the skies. The holiday was founded after the 2014 release of the film 51 Degrees North, which explores what would happen if an asteroid were to strike London.

Concept note-3: -International Asteroid Day 2022: Theme This year, the theme for International Asteroid Day is “small is beautiful". This means that asteroids are indeed small but to the eyes of those who study them, the boulder-strewn surfaces are beautiful to see close-up.
