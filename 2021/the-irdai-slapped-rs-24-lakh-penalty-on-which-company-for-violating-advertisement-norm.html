The IRDAI Slapped Rs 24 Lakh Penalty on which company for Violating Advertisement Norm?

(A) ICICI

(B) Paisabazaar

(C) ** Policybazaar

(D) HDFC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IRDAI Imposes Rs24 Lakh Penalty on Policybazaar for Flouting Advertising Norms.

Concept note-2: -Insurance sector regulator IRDAI has slapped a fine of Rs 24 lakh on policy aggregator Policybazaar for violating advertisement norms with regard to SMS sent to customers last year about the increase in term insurance policy premiums.

Concept note-3: -The Authority acts as the regulator of the insurance industry in India and oversees the functioning of the Life Insurance and General Insurance companies operating in the country. The main objective of the IRDA is to protect the interests of the policyholder and regulate the insurance industry.

Concept note-4: -IRDA or Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India is the apex body that supervises and regulates the insurance sector in India. The primary purpose of IRDA is to safeguard the interest of the policyholders and ensure the growth of insurance in the country.
