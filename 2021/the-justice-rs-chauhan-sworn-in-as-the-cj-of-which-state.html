The Justice RS Chauhan sworn in as the CJ of which state?

(A) Assam HC

(B) ** Uttarakhand HC

(C) Maharashtra HC

(D) Karnataka HC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Justice Dhananjaya Yashwant Chandrachud was sworn in as the 50th Chief Justice of India on November 9, 2022. He will serve as the CJI for two years.

Concept note-2: -The oath to the Judge of a high court is administered by the governor of that concerned state.

Concept note-3: -MUMBAI: The President of India appointed Bombay high court Chief Justice Dipankar Datta as judge of the Supreme CourtHis appointment takes the SC Judge strength to 28.

Concept note-4: -Hon’ble Mr. Justice Ravi Malimath was administered the oath of office of the Chief Justice of High Court of Madhya Pradesh on 14-10-2021, on being transferred from the High Court of Himachal Pradesh.
