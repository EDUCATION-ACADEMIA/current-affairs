The MandateHQ payment interface has been launched which company in partnership with Mastercard?

(A) Instamojo

(B) Paytm

(C) BillDesk

(D) ** Razorpay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To help banks comply with the RBI directive and provide convenience to banks’ customers, Razorpay & Mastercard today partnered to launch MandateHQ, a new recurring payment interface, in accordance with RBI’s circular.

Concept note-2: -Razorpay partners with Mastercard to launch ‘MandateHQ’ for recurring payments.

Concept note-3: -MandateHQ is a unified platform that will help banks with end-to-end mandate lifecycle management including creating, viewing, updating, cancelling, and pausing mandates and processing debits for valid mandates.
