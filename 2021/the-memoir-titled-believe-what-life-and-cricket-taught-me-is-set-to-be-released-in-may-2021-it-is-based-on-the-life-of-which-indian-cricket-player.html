The memoir titled ‘Believe-What Life and Cricket Taught Me’ is set to be released in May 2021. It is based on the life of which Indian cricket player?

(A) Ajinkya Rahane

(B) Yuvraj Singh

(C) ** Suresh Raina

(D) Rohit Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Suresh Raina . Cricketer Suresh Raina has come up with his autobiography ‘Believe- What Life Taught me’. The cricketer has shared a glimpse of his cricket career which included success, failure, injuries, setbacks, and how he came out on top of it, in the book.

Concept note-2: -Cricketer Suresh Raina on autobiography ‘Believe’ and his most-cherished moments so far - The Hindu.

Concept note-3: -Success builds self-confidence. Be Humble - celebrate the peaks but there will be plenty of troughs. Take it One Ball at a time - don’t get ahead of yourself in cricket or life. Sport and exercise help build fitness that can help with mental health.

Concept note-4: -In this book, Suresh Raina takes us through the challenges he faced as a young cricketer. He was bullied in school and at cricket camps, but he always punched above his weight, overcoming every adversity life threw at him and never giving up. This is the story of the lessons he learnt and the friendships he built.
