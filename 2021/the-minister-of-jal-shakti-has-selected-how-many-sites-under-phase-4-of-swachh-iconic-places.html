The Minister of Jal Shakti has selected how many sites under phase-4 of Swachh Iconic Places?

(A) 9

(B) 10

(C) 11

(D) ** 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -JAISALMER: The central government under the Swachh Bharat mission has selected four locations in Rajasthan as iconic places, including the Jaisalmer fort and Ramdevra in the district along with Ajmer dargah and Kumbhalgarh Fort in Rajsamand district.

Concept note-2: -The decision was taken at a national consultation on Swachh Bharat Mission in Delhi.
