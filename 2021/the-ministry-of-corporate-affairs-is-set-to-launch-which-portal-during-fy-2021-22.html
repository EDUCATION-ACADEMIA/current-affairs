The Ministry of Corporate Affairs is set to launch which portal during FY 2021-22?

(A) ** MCA21 Version 3.0

(B) ‘SPICe+’ Web Form

(C) Incredible India

(D) Bhuvan Panchayat V3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) is set to launch the portal ‘MCA21 Version 3.0’ during the Fiscal Year 2021-22. The portal will be the 1st Mission Mode e-Governance project of Government of India (GoI).

Concept note-2: -MCA21 is designed to fully automate all processes relating to the proactive enforcement and compliance of the legal requirements under the Companies Act, 1956.

Concept note-3: -What is V2 Portal of MCA ? V2 portal is Version 2 of the MCA portal which was used to file various forms relating to Companies and LLP’s . LLP Services have been discontinued on V2 portal and have been migrated to V3 portal.

Concept note-4: -MCA 21 is the online portal of the MCA, which has made all company-related information accessible to stakeholders and the general public. It was initially launched in 2006.

Concept note-5: -MCA21 version-3.0 is a technology-driven forward-looking project, envisioned to strengthen enforcement, promote Ease of Doing Business and enhance user experience. MCA21 version-3.0 rollout has been planned in phases to ensure minimum disruption in regulatory filings.
