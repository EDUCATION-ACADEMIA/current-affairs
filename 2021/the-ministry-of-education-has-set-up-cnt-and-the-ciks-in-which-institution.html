The Ministry of Education has set up CNT and the CIKS in which institution?

(A) IIT Kanpur

(B) ** IIT Guwahati

(C) IIT Delhi

(D) IIT Bombay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Education Minister inaugurated the state-of-the-art Centre for Nanotechnology (CNT) and Centre for Indian Knowledge System (CIKS) at IIT Guwahati.

Concept note-2: -GUWAHATI: Union education minister Dharmendra Pradhan on Sunday inaugurated the Centre for Nanotechnology (CNT) and the Centre for Indian Knowledge System at IIT, Guwahati.

Concept note-3: -Dharmendra Pradhan, Minister of Education, Skill Development and Entrepreneurship visited IIT Guwahati and inaugurated the state-of-the-art Centre for Nanotechnology (CNT) and Centre for Indian Knowledge System (CIKS) as well as two hostels at the institute.
