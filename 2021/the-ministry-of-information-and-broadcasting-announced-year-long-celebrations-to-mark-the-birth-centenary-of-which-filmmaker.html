The Ministry of Information and Broadcasting announced year-long celebrations to mark the birth centenary of which filmmaker?

(A) Rajesh Khanna

(B) Priyadarshan

(C) Satyam Kushwaha

(D) ** Satyajit Ray

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Satyajit Ray was among the best known filmmakers in India.

Concept note-2: -Satyajit Ray, (born May 2, 1921, Calcutta [now Kolkata], India-died April 23, 1992, Calcutta), Bengali motion-picture director, writer, and illustrator who brought the Indian cinema to world recognition with Pather Panchali (1955; The Song of the Road) and its two sequels, known as the Apu Trilogy.

Concept note-3: -Aparajito (pronounced [ɔpɔradʒito]), also known as Aparajito – The Undefeated, is a 2022 Indian Bengali-language biographical film directed and co-written by Anik Dutta.

Concept note-4: -The Correct Answer is Film Direction. Satyajit Ray was a film director, writer, and composer of music in India. For films such as The Apu Trilogy (1955–59), The Music Room (1958), The Big City (1963) and Charulata (1964), he is generally considered to have been one of the greatest filmmakers of all time.

Concept note-5: -Celebrated Spanish film Director Carlos Saura has been honoured with the prestigious Satyajit Ray Lifetime Achievement award at the gala opening ceremony of 53rd edition of the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) in Goa today, 20 November, 2022.
