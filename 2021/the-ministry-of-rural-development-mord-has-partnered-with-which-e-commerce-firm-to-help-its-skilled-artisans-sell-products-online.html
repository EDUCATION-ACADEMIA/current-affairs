The Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) has partnered with which e-commerce firm to help its skilled artisans sell products online?

(A) ** Flipkart

(B) Amazon

(C) Ajio

(D) Paytm

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The move will help artisans reach out 10 crore of Flipkart’s existing customers, thus substantially scaling their outreach.

Concept note-2: -MoU is a part of the Flipkart Samarth program. It was signed with the aim of providing skilled & underserved communities of weavers, craftsmen, and artisans with national market access using the Flipkart marketplace.

Concept note-3: -Flipkart signs an MoU with Rural Development Ministry for the DAY-NRLM program.

Concept note-4: - Ministry of Rural Development GOVERNMENT OF INDIA.

Concept note-5: -Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) for construction of quality roads, National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) for social pension, Shyama Prasad Mukherjee RURBAN Mission, Integrated Watershed Management Programme (IWMP) for improving the productivity of the land.
