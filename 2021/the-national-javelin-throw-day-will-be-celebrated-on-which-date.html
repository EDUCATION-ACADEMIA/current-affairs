The National javelin throw day will be celebrated on which date?

(A) August 5

(B) August 8

(C) August 9

(D) ** August 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The decision to celebrate August 7 as the National Javelin Day was made by AFI upon Neeraj Chopra’s return from Tokyo.

Concept note-2: -AFI to celebrate August 7 as National Javelin Day to honour Neeraj Chopra’s Olympic gold | More sports News - Times of India.

Concept note-3: -In 2015, the Government of India decided to designate the 7th August every year, as the National Handloom Day.

Concept note-4: -It’s National Lighthouse Day, Aged Care Employee Day, Professional Speakers Day, Particularly Preposterous Packaging Day, Afternoon Tea Week… and much more!

Concept note-5: -Neeraj Chopra’s best at the World Athletics Championships came during the qualification round at Oregon 2022, where he threw an 88.39m. His effort in the final measured 88.13m, which earned India an historic silver. Neeraj Chopra won the gold medal at the IAAF World U20 Championships 2016 in Bydgoszcz, Poland.
