The National Youth Awards 2017-18 and 2018-19 will be conferred in which city?

(A) ** Delhi

(B) Shimla

(C) Jaipur

(D) Gandhi Nagar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Youth Affairs and Sports Minister Anurag Singh Thakur conferred the National Youth Awards 2017-18 and 2018-19 in New Delhi on 12 August 2021. Total 22 National Youth awards were given in individual and organizations categories.

Concept note-2: -Mallika will receive the award on January 16 at Hubballi in Karnataka. She is one of the 19 youngsters, who have been selected for the award from across the country. The award is being presented to these youngsters for their outstanding services to the community and achieving great heights in their respective fields.

Concept note-3: -Chhattisgarh’s Ankita Pandey honoured with National Youth Award 2023 for social work. An assistant professor from Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, has been awarded the National Youth Award 2023 by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports.

Concept note-4: -The National Youth Icon award was first introduced in 2016 by the International Youth Committee (IYC), which is an organization accredited by the United Nations.
