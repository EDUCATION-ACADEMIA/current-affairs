The NPCI has partnered with which company to launch RuPay SoftPoS solution for Indian merchants?

(A) Stripe

(B) ** PayCore

(C) GoCardless

(D) Checkout

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Praveena Rai, COO, NPCI said, “We are delighted to partner with SBI to launch the RuPay SoftPoS solution aimed at creating an innovative payment solution for Indian MSMEs which forms the backbone of our economy.

Concept note-2: -Cashfree Payments, a leading payments and API banking solutions company, today announced the launch of ‘softPOS’, a point of sale (POS) solution for businesses including grocery stores, retail shops, food delivery apps, automobile industry, and NBFCs.

Concept note-3: -Pragnyat Lalwani, Director, Seshaasai said, “We are honored to be the Technology Solution Partner for this initiative by NPCI & YES BANK.

Concept note-4: -National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) on Friday partnered with SBI Payments to announce the launch of ‘RuPay SoftPoS’ for millions of Indian merchants.

Concept note-5: -Soft POS, or Software Point of Sale, is a ground-breaking new technology that allows businesses to accept card payments directly on their phone or device without the use of any additional software.
