The Prime Minister Abiy Ahmed won parliamentary elections of which country again?

(A) Cuba

(B) Peru

(C) ** Ethiopia

(D) Kazakhstan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The election was a landslide victory for the Prosperity Party. On 30 September 2021, voting took place in 47 constituencies of the Harari, SNNPR, and Somali regions. The House of Peoples’ Representatives confirmed incumbent Abiy Ahmed as prime minister for a five-year term on 4 October 2021.

Concept note-2: -The Prime Minister is elected from the members of the House of Peoples’ Representatives and presents a government platform.

Concept note-3: -The peace initiatives that Abiy Ahmed introduced and for which he received the Nobel Peace Prize sprang from his contribution to the peace agreement with Eritrea and his broad political initiatives for democracy and expanded civil rights.

Concept note-4: -On 2 April 2018, Abiy Ahmed was elected as Prime Minister of Ethiopia by the House of Representatives and sworn in.
