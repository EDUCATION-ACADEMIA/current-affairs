The Prime Minister transferred how much amount into bank accounts of farmers under the PM-KISAN?

(A) 18, 000 crores

(B) ** 19, 000 crores

(C) 21, 000 crores

(D) 20, 000 crores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PM KISAN Scheme is a Central Sector Scheme to supplement financial needs of land holding farmers. Financial benefit of Rs. 6000 per year is transferred into the bank accounts of farmer families through direct benefit transfer .

Concept note-2: -Under the scheme, eligible farmer families are provided a benefit of Rs 6, 000 per year in three equal instalments of Rs 2, 000 each.

Concept note-3: -Under the scheme, eligible farmer families are provided a benefit of Rs. 6000/- per year in three equal instalments of Rs. 2000/- each, every four months.

Concept note-4: -As part of this scheme, income support of Rs 6, 000 per year in three equal instalments is provided to all land-holding farmer families.

Concept note-5: -Under the scheme, eligible farmers will receive ₹6, 000 per year, in three equal instalments, directly into their bank accounts. Farmers who have not received the first two instalments of the Prime Minister’s Samman Nidhi Scheme can check their eligibility and status on the scheme’s official website.
