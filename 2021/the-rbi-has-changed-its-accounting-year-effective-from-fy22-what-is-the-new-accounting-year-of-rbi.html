The RBI has changed its accounting year effective from FY22. What is the new accounting year of RBI?

(A) August-July

(B) July-June

(C) ** April-March

(D) June-May

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The accounting year for the Reserve Bank of India was changed to April-March (earlier July-June) from the financial year 2020-21 onwards. Where available, this chapter has been updated beyond March 2022.

Concept note-2: -The accounting year for the Reserve Bank was changed to April-March (earlier July-June) from the financial year 2020-21 onwards.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Financial Year In India, the fiscal year starts on April 1 and ends on March 31. AY 2023-24 will be the review year for FY 2022-23. In certain circumstances, the financial year may vary from the previous year.

Concept note-4: -(a) Accounting year: The accounting year of the Reserve Bank has been changed from ‘July - June’ to ‘April - March’.
