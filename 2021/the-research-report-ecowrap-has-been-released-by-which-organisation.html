The Research report ‘Ecowrap’ has been released by which organisation?

(A) Moody’s

(B) CBDT

(C) WHO

(D) ** SBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. ‘Ecowrap’ is the flagship report released by which institution? Notes: ‘Ecowrap’ is the report released by the Research Team of the State Bank of India. As per the latest edition of the report, India’s real GDP is expected to grow at around 9.5 per cent in 2021-22.

Concept note-2: -SBI’s research report Ecowrap, in its latest edition, projected India’s GDP growth for FY22 to be at 8.2-8.5%

Concept note-3: -Q. Ecowrap, that was recently in news, is the research report of which institution? Notes: Ecowrap is the research report of India’s largest lender State Bank of India. Recently, SBI released its latest Ecowrap report.

Concept note-4: -According to an SBI’s research report- Ecowrap, India’s GDP is likely to grow at how much per cent in the third quarter of fiscal 2022?

Concept note-5: -The State Bank of India published its Ecowrap report on Income Inequality in India. Findings of the report. Ratio of per capita income of states. Gap between GDP and GNI.
