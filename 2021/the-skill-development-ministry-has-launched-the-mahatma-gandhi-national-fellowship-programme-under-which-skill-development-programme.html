The Skill Development Ministry has launched the Mahatma Gandhi National Fellowship programme under which skill development programme?

(A) Pre Departure Orientation Training

(B) ** SANKALP

(C) Rozgar Mela

(D) Capacity Building Scheme

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MGNF is a Certificate Programme in Public Policy and Management offered by IIMs. It has been designed at the initiative of the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India (GoI), and implemented in collaboration with State Skill Development Missions (SSDMs).

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Union Ministry launched the Mahatma Gandhi National Fellowship (MGNF)? Notes: The Union Minister for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Dharmendra Pradhan launched phase II of the Mahatma Gandhi National Fellowship (MGNF).

Concept note-3: -MGNF is a two-year blended program that consists of sessions at IIMs classes and an opportunity to promote skill planning and development in district economies. This year the Fellowship is hosted by IIM Bangalore. At least a graduate in any discipline (Engineering, Law, Medicine, Social Sciences, etc.)

Concept note-4: -Skills development ministry launches Mahatma Gandhi National Fellowship in partnership with IIMs.

Concept note-5: -It is a two-year long fellowship programme conceived to create opportunities for young, dynamic individuals to contribute to enhancing skill development at the grassroots.
