The SUPACE is an AI-based portal launched for which organization?

(A) SBI

(B) RBI

(C) Indian Railways

(D) ** Supreme Court of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Supreme Court. Artificial Intelligence (AI) based portal ‘SUPACE’ was launched by the Supreme Court of India. ‘SUPACE’ stands for Supreme Court Portal for Assistance in Courts Efficiency.

Concept note-2: -On April 6, 2021, the Supreme Court of India launched the first AI driven research portal, Supreme Court Portal for Assistance in Court’s Efficiency (SUPACE). It was launched by Justice SA Bobde in presence of Justice Nageswara Rao.

Concept note-3: -Supreme Court Portal for Assistance in Court’s Efficiency (SUPACE) is an AI driven portal. The supreme court of India has launched this portal.

Concept note-4: -2022 the Supreme Court of India has launched RTI Online portal for filing of applications, appeals and payment of fees, and other charges under the Right to Information Act, 2005 (RTI Act).

Concept note-5: -In April 2021, the Supreme Court launched its first AI portal Supreme Court Portal for Assistance in Courts Efficiency (SUPACE ), to leverage machine learning in dealing with huge chunks of case data.
