The US President Donald Trump has declared the emergency in which city?

(A) New York

(B) Los Angeles

(C) ** Washington DC

(D) Las Vegas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(a) With respect to Acts of Congress authorizing the exercise, during the period of a national emergency, of any special or extraordinary power, the President is authorized to declare such national emergency.

Concept note-2: -National emergency under Article 352 The President can declare such an emergency only on the basis of a written request by the Cabinet headed by the Prime Minister.

Concept note-3: -A state of emergency is a situation in which a government is empowered to be able to put through policies that it would normally not be permitted to do, for the safety and protection of its citizens.

Concept note-4: -Officially issued by President Fakhruddin Ali Ahmed under Article 352 of the Constitution because of prevailing “internal disturbance", the Emergency was in effect from 25 June 1975 to its withdrawal on 21 March 1977.
