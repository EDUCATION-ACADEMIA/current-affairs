The World Art Day is marked annually on which day?

(A) 13 April

(B) ** 15 April

(C) 14 April

(D) 12 April

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Art Day has been celebrated every year since 2012 on April 15. It is also the birthdate of Leonardo da Vinci.

Concept note-2: -Each year, on 15 April, World Art Day celebrations help reinforce the links between artistic creations and society, encourage greater awareness of the diversity of artistic expressions and highlight the contribution of artists to sustainable development.

Concept note-3: -In order to honour Leonardo da Vinci’s birthday, World Art Day is celebrated. As per IAA, Leonardo da Vinci was considered the symbol of world peace, freedom of expression, tolerance, and brotherhood. World Art Day aims to nurture an artist’s creativity and innovation.

Concept note-4: -By India Today Web Desk: World Art Day is celebrated every year on April 15, to promote the development, diffusion, and enjoyment of art. The day was adopted by the International Association of Art, a partner of UNESCO, to create awareness of creative activity worldwide.

Concept note-5: -This association is an NGO partner of the United Nations Educational, Scientific, and Cultural Organization (UNESCO). Every year, the city of Los Angeles hosts a variety of festivities to celebrate this day.
