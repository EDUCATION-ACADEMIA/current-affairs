The World Cup-winning midfielder Sami Khedira has announced his retirement. He represents which country?

(A) ** Germany

(B) France

(C) UK

(D) Brazil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Germany’s World Cup-winning midfielder Sami Khedira said he will retire following his club Hertha Berlin’s final match of the Bundesliga season against Hoffenheim on Saturday.

Concept note-2: -Enter Sami Khedira and Mesut Özil, young teammates on soccer powerhouse Real Madrid and key ingredients in Germany’s 2010 World Cup run to the semifinals. Khedira (23) is the German born son of a Tunisian father, while Özil (22) is a third generation Turkish-German who recites the Qur’an before every game.

Concept note-3: -Just under two months after clinching the Champions League, Khedira lifted the 2014 World Cup in Brazil with his country. After departing Real Madrid, he continued his career at Juventus and Hertha Berlin, until 19 May 2021, when he announced his retirement from football.

Concept note-4: -Sami Khedira is a Juventus midfielder who hasn’t been involved in any of the 16 Serie A games that his club have played so far in 2022/2023. The last league match that Khedira played came on May 22, 2021 for Hertha BSC versus Hoffenheim; a game that he played 75 minutes in during a 2-1 defeat.
