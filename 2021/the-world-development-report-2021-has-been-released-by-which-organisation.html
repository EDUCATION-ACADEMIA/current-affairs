The World Development Report 2021 has been released by which organisation?

(A) ** World Bank

(B) WEF

(C) UNCTAD

(D) IMF

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Bank’s World Development Report, published annually since 1978, is an invaluable guide to the economic, social, and environmental state of the world today.

Concept note-2: -The latest edition of the World Development Report from the World Bank provides a blueprint on how to harness the power of data for development, to ensure no one is left behind.

Concept note-3: -World Development Report 2021: Data for Better Lives explores the tremendous potential of the changing data landscape to improve the lives of poor people, while also acknowledging its potential to open back doors that can harm individuals, businesses, and societies.

Concept note-4: -World Development Report 2022: Finance for an Equitable Recovery examines the central role of finance in the economic recovery from the pandemic.

Concept note-5: -HDI Rank of India 2021-22 According to the Human Development Report 2021-22 HDI rank of India is 132nd, among 191 countries.
