To further strengthen the bilateral relations with India, Mette Frederiksen is on a 3-day visit to India. She is the Prime Minister of which country?

(A) Norway

(B) ** Denmark

(C) Sweden

(D) Singapore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The diplomatic relations between India and Denmark, established in September 1949, are marked by regular high-level exchanges; and are based on historical links, common democratic traditions, and a shared desire for regional, as well as international peace and stability.

Concept note-2: -Additionally, only four coalition governments since World War II have enjoyed a majority in the Folketing, so the coalitions (and the prime minister) must also gain loose support from other minor parties. The current prime minister of Denmark is Mette Frederiksen, since 27 June 2019.
