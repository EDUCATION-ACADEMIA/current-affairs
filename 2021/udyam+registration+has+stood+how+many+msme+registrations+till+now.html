Udyam Registration has stood how many MSME registrations till now?

(A) 40 lakh

(B) ** 50 lakh

(C) 20 lakh

(D) 34 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian MSME Sector It is estimated that there are 633.9 lakh MSMEs in India.

Concept note-2: -How many days does it take to obtain Udyam registration? Udyam registration application is submitted online on the Udyam registration portal. After submission of the application, it generally takes four or five days to issue a registration certificate.

Concept note-3: -Udyam (MSME) Registration is Free of Cost and paperless and no online or offline agency, institution or consultant is authorised to charge any fee or money.
