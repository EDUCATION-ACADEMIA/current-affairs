UNICEF India with which social media platform has launched a one-year joint initiative on ending violence against children with a special focus on online safety?

(A) WhatsApp

(B) ** Facebook

(C) Telegram

(D) Google

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi, 09 August 2021 – UNICEF India and Facebook today launched a one-year joint initiative on ending violence against children with a special focus on online safety. The partnership seeks to create a safe environment for children online and offline.

Concept note-2: -UNICEF and its India partners are working together to ensure that children are protected from work and exploitation, which is harmful to their development. They are working to ensure that children remain in economically stable family homes and get the opportunity to go to school and be educated.

Concept note-3: -UNICEF India relies on the support and donations from businesses and individuals to sustain and expand health, nutrition, water and sanitation, education and child protection programmes for all girls and boys in India. Support us today to help every child survive and thrive!
