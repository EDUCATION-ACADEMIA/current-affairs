Union Ministry of Tribal Affairs has launched a National Migration Support Portal. What is the name of the portal?

(A) ShramVikas

(B) ** ShramShakti

(C) VikasShakti

(D) ShramYog

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shram Shakti is the name of the National Tribal Migration Support Portal that was launched by the Union Minister of Tribal Affairs, Shri Arjun Munda, in January 2021. Shram Shakti is a National Migration Support Portal.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which Ministry has launched a National Migration Support Portal named “ShramShakti”? Notes: The Union Ministry of Tribal Affairs has launched a dedicated portal that would help in formulation of state and national-level programme for migrant workers.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Shram Shakti. The Shram shakti portal was launched by The Union Minister of Tribal Affairs Arjun Munda virtually on 22nd January 2021. Shram shakti is a National Migration Support Portal.

Concept note-4: -Launch of Shram Shakti Portal This National Migration Support Portal has been launched at a programme held at Panjim, Goa today. It would effectively help in the smooth formulation of state and national level programs for migrant workers.

Concept note-5: -Ministry of Tribal Affairs Virtually Launches “Shramshakti” Digital Data Solution for Migrant Workers.
