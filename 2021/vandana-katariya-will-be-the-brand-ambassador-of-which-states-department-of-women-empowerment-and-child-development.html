Vandana Katariya will be the brand ambassador of which state’s Department of Women Empowerment and Child Development?

(A) Bihar

(B) Haryana

(C) ** Uttarakhand

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Secretary, Women and Child Development Shri D. K. Sikri also thanked Aamir Khan and his creative team for contributing in social causes and social development of the country and the children in particular.

Concept note-2: -Actor Akshay Kumar has been appointed as the brand ambassador of Uttarakhand on 7 February 2022. CM Dhami presented Akshay with a traditional cap of the state, a shawl, and a painting of all the gates of the four pilgrimage sites – Badrinath, Kedarnath, Gangotri, and Yamunotri.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Vandana Katariya.

Concept note-4: -Uttarakhand Government appoints Prasoon Joshi as its Brand Ambassador. CEO & CCO of McCann Worldgroup India, Prasoon Joshi has been appointed by Uttarakhand Government as the state’s brand ambassador.
