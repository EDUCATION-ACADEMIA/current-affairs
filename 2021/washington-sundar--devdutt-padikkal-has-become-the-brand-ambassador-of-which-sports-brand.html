Washington Sundar & Devdutt Padikkal has become the brand ambassador of which sports brand?

(A) Adidas

(B) Nike

(C) Reebok

(D) ** Puma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global sports brand, Puma has signed a long-term partnership deal with cricketers Washington Sundar and Devdutt Padikkal.

Concept note-2: -Washington Sundar & Devdutt Padikkal as Brand Ambassadors of Puma.

Concept note-3: -In a major step towards its commitment to back women in the sports ecosystem, sports brand PUMA India today announced Indian women’s cricket team captain Harmanpreet Kaur as its latest brand ambassador.

Concept note-4: -Adidas has rolled out its film for the upcoming FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022. The film features adidas brand ambassadors Lionel Messi, Karim Benzema, Achraf Hakimi, Son Heung-Min, Jude Bellingham, Pedri, Serge Gnabry and Stormzy.
