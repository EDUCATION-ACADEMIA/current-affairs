(B) Rs 11, 964 crores

(C) Rs 12, 753 crores

(D) ** Rs 10, 683 crores

Concept note-1: -The Economic Survey has estimated the scheme will result in fresh investment of ₹19, 000 crore in the textiles sector over the next five years.
