What amount of penalty has been imposed by RBI on SBI for non-compliance of RBI directives related to Frauds classification and reporting by commercial banks and select FIs?

(A) Rs 50 lakh

(B) Rs 75 lakh

(C) Rs 2 crore

(D) ** Rs 1 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Press Releases. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has, by an order dated November 16, 2021, imposed a monetary penalty of ₹1.00 Crore (Rupees One Crore only) on State Bank of India (the bank) for contravention of sub-section (2) of section 19 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (the Act).

Concept note-2: -RBI imposes monetary penalty on Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited This penalty has been imposed in exercise of powers vested in RBI under the provisions of section 47 A (1) (c) read with sections 46 (4) (i) of the Act.
