What is the deadline to link Pan with Aadhaar?

(A) ** 30 September 2021

(B) 30 June 2021

(C) 31 August 2021

(D) 31 July 2021

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Income Tax department has recently said that it is mandatory to link your permanent account number (PAN) with your Aadhaar card number by March 31, 2023.

Concept note-2: -The last date to link the PAN with Aadhaar was earlier extended from 31st March 2022 to 31st March 2023.

Concept note-3: -Last date 31/03/2023, ” the income tax department said. You will not be able to use your PAN card if it becomes inoperative. The deadline to link PAN with Aadhaar has been extended several times in the past. The latest deadline for PAN-Aadhaar linking is March 31, 2023.

Concept note-4: -Last date to link PAN with Aadhaar is extended till March 31, 2023. People are requested to link their PAN-Aadhaar by deadline or the PAN number will become inoperative.

Concept note-5: -If the PAN Card and the Aadhaar Card are not linked, you may be subject to a penalty of up to Rs. 10, 000 according to the Income Tax Act. In addition, if the two cards are not linked by March 31, 2023, your PAN Card may become inoperable.
