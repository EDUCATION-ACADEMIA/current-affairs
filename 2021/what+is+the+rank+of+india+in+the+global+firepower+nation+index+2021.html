What is the rank of India in the Global Firepower Nation Index 2021?

(A) 3rd

(B) ** 4th

(C) 1st

(D) 9th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India maintained its No. 4 position. Pakistan slipped into the top 15 in 2021.

Concept note-2: -India’s military is placed fourth on the GPI out of 140 countries.

Concept note-3: -The GFP index denotes India as a Top 5 world power. For 2023, India is ranked 4 of 145 out of the countries considered for the annual GFP review. The nation holds a PwrIndx* score of 0.1025 (a score of 0.0000 is considered ‘perfect’). This entry last reviewed on 01/09/2023.

Concept note-4: -According to the official website of Global Firepower Index, “For 2022, India is ranked 4 of 145 out of the countries considered for the annual GFP review. PwrIndx* score of India is 0.1025. A score of 0.0000 is considered ‘perfect’.

Concept note-5: -Pakistan is ranked 7 of 145 out of the countries considered for the annual Global Firepower review.
