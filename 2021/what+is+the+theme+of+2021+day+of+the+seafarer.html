What is the theme of 2021 Day of the Seafarer?

(A) ** Seafarers:at the core of shipping’s future

(B) Seafarers’ Wellbeing

(C) Seafarers Matter

(D) Seafarers Are Key Workers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year the campaign for day of Seafarer calls for a “fair future for seafarers.” The campaign aims to discuss “issues that will still be relevant to seafarers after the pandemic, such as fair treatment of seafarers, fair working conditions, fair training, fair safety, etc."

Concept note-3: -It is celebrated on the 25 of June each year to pay respect to Seafarers and sailors who help the entire world function by conducting sea transport. The day was proposed by International Maritime Organization in 2010 to celebrate the contribution of the seafarer to the world economy and civil society.

Concept note-4: -You can define a seafarer as literally being someone who is employed to serve aboard any type of marine vessel. This usually refers to active seafaring workers, but can be used to describe a person with a long history of serving within the profession.

Concept note-5: -Get Educated About Seafarers Learning more about the work and plight of sailors and seafarers is a good way to observe International Day of the Seafarer. Get more information by visiting the International Maritime Organization’s website (supported by the United Nations).
