What is the theme of the 2021 World Accreditation Day?

(A) Accreditation:Delivering a safer world

(B) Accreditation Improves Food Safety

(C) Accreditation adds value to supply chains

(D) ** Accreditation:Supporting the Implementation of the Sustainable Development Goals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -June 9th 2021 marks World Accreditation Day (#WAD2021), a global initiative established by ILAC and IAF to promote the value of accreditation. This year’s theme focuses on the use of accreditation in supporting the implementation of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Concept note-2: -World Accreditation Day 2022 Theme The theme for World Accreditation Day 2022 is Sustainability in Economic Growth and the Environment. It puts focus on how accreditation supports the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 6, 7, 8, 9, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15.

Concept note-3: -9 June 2022 marks World Accreditation Day (#WAD2022), a global initiative established by IAF and ILAC to promote the value of accreditation. This year’s theme is Accreditation: Sustainability in Economic Growth and the Environment.

Concept note-4: -Significance Of World Accreditation Day The significance of WAD is to promote the role of accreditation in trade and the economy. The day is celebrated to raise awareness of the importance of accreditation activities. WAD focuses on supporting United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
