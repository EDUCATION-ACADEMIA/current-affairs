What is the theme of the 2021 World Migratory Bird Day?

(A) Protect Birds:Be the Solution to Plastic Pollution!

(B) Unifying Our Voices for Bird Conservation

(C) ** Sing, Fly, Soar-Like a Bird!

(D) Birds Connect Our World

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Overview. “Sing, Fly, Soar – Like a Bird!” is the theme of this year’s World Migratory Bird Day, an annual global campaign dedicated to raising awareness of migratory birds and the need for international cooperation to conserve them.

Concept note-2: -Light pollution and its impact on migratory birds is the focus of World Migratory Bird Day 2022, a global campaign that aims to raise awareness of migratory birds and the need for international cooperation to conserve them. The slogan for the 2022 campaign is “Dim the Lights for Birds at Night!”

Concept note-3: -WMBD 2022 is addressing the growing issue of light pollution on these birds and actions on a global scale to help migrate these birds safely.

Concept note-4: -World Migratory Bird Day (WMBD) is an awareness-raising campaign highlighting the need for the conservation of migratory birds and their habitats. It aims to draw attention to the threats faced by migratory birds, their ecological importance, and the need for international cooperation to conserve them.

Concept note-5: -Since its creation in 2006, World Migratory Bird Day has been commemorated twice a year. It is supposed to be celebrated on the second Saturday of May and the second Saturday of October. Since its creation in 2006, World Migratory Bird Day has been commemorated twice a year.
