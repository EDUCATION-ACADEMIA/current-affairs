What is the theme of the India Pavilion at the Dubai Expo 2020?

(A) ** Openness, Opportunity and Growth

(B) Leisure in the Age of Technology

(C) Progress and Harmony for Mankind

(D) Man, Nature, Technology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Share this. The Expo 2020 Dubai is a global stage for renowned architects from all over the world to represent countries whilst showcasing design finesse. With an overarching theme of ‘Connecting Minds, Creating the Future’, it is a platform promoting partnerships for innovation, inclusion and understanding.

Concept note-2: -India Expo 2020 Pavilion is an area located within Al Forsan District, Expo City Dubai, Dubai South, Dubai. The project is owned by NBCC India Ltd. The project was completed by October 2021.
