[SOURCES]

What is the theme of the National Safety Day 2021?

(A) positive behavior at the workplace

(B) ** Sadak Suraksha (Road Safety)

(C) Cultivate and Sustain a Safety Culture

(D) Health & Safety Performance by Use

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Nurture young minds- Develop safety culture. National Safety Day is observed on 4 March. The main objective of this day is to promote the Safety, Health, and Environment (SHE) movement in the country. The day was established by the National Safety Council.

Concept note-2: -National Safety Day theme of 2020, 2021 & 2022 is “Sadak Suraksha”. National Safety Day theme of 2019 was “Reinforce positive behavior at the workplace to achieve safety and health goals”.

Concept note-3: -Last year, the National Safety Council marked the 50th observance of National Safety Day. Every year, a theme is given for this special day to highlight the importance of public work safety. According to a report, the theme for the National Safety Day 2022 is ‘Nurture young minds - Develop safety culture’.

Concept note-4: -For this year, the theme of National Safety Day is – “Nurture young minds - develop a safety culture”. The theme, last year, was “sadak suraksha” [road safety]. The National Safety Council was formed on March 4, 1966, to generate, develop and sustain a voluntary movement on safety, health and environment (SHE).

Concept note-5: -The ongoing COVID-19 pandemic has significantly exacerbated the risk of medication errors and associated medication-related harm. It is in this context that ‘Medication safety’ has been selected as the theme for World Patient Safety Day 2022, with the slogan ‘Medication without harm’.
