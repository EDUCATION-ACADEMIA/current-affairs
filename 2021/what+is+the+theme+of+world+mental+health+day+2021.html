What is the theme of World Mental Health Day 2021?

(A) Young people and mental health in a changing world

(B) ** Mental health in an unequal world

(C) Move for mental health:Increased investment in mental health

(D) Mental Health Promotion and Suicide Prevention

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On this day and throughout the month of October, the Mental Health Strategy Team and system-wide partners will carry out a series of events and activities focused on this year’s theme, “Mental Health in an Unequal World’.

Concept note-2: -Stigma and discrimination continue to be a barrier to social inclusion and access to the right care. The 2022 World Mental Health Day theme ‘Make Mental Health & Well-Being for All a Global Priority’ gives an opportunity to rejuvenate the efforts in making the world a better place.

Concept note-3: -Wear a green ribbon to show colleagues, loved ones or simply those you walk past that you care about their mental health.
