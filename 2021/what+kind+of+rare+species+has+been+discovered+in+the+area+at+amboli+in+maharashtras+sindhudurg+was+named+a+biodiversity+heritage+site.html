What kind of rare species has been discovered in the area at Amboli in Maharashtra’s Sindhudurg was named a biodiversity heritage site?

(A) Frog

(B) ** Freshwater fish

(C) Crocodile

(D) Diatoms

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Schistura Hiranyakeshi is a rare sub-species of Schistura, a freshwater loach.

Concept note-2: -Amboli- biodiversity heritage site The fish was named after the Hiranyakeshi river near Amboli village.

Concept note-3: -It has over 35 species of mammals, a growing list of over 200 species of birds, approximately 150 species of butterflies, and over 45 species of reptiles and amphibians (the list constantly updated), some endemic to Amboli and others to the larger Western Ghats.

Concept note-4: -The Maharashtra government has recently issued a notification declaring a temple at Amboli a biodiversity heritage site in Sindhudurg under the Biodiversity Act, 2002.
