What was the theme of the 14th edition of the Urban Mobility India (UMI) Conference?

(A) ** Mobility for All

(B) Smart Cities

(C) Green Urban Mobility

(D) Accessible and Liveable Cities

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for UMI 14 was Sustainable Transport for Sustainable Cities.

Concept note-2: -Puri, Minister of Housing and Urban Affairs delivered the inaugural and valedictory address. The main theme of the conference was “Mobility for All” which focuses on providing equitable access and promoting inclusive urban transportation.

Concept note-3: -This year, the Urban Mobility India (UMI) Conference & Expo is to focus on the theme of “Azadi75 – Sustainable AatmaNirbhar Urban Mobility”. It will emphasize on designing and implementing efficient, high quality and sustainable transport system in the cities.

Concept note-4: -Theme Of The Conference: The meet is focus on the theme, ‘Azadi 75 - Sustainable Aatmanirbhar Urban Mobility’.

Concept note-5: -From November 4-6, 2022, the 15th edition of Urban Mobility India (UMI) Conference & Expo 2022 held at Hotel Grand Hyatt, Kochi, Kerala which was jointly inaugurated by the Union Minister Hardeep Singh Puri, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA); and Chief Minister (CM) of Kerala, Pinarayi Vijayan.
