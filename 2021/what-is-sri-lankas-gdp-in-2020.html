What is Sri Lanka’s GDP in 2020?

(A) ** 3.6 percent

(B) 4.0 percent

(C) 3.5 percent

(D) 3.0 percen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The GDP figure in 2021 was $88, 979 million, Sri Lanka is number 70 in the ranking of GDP of the 196 countries that we publish. The absolute value of GDP in Sri Lanka rose $3, 630 million with respect to 2020. The GDP per capita of Sri Lanka in 2021 was $4, 016, $122 less than in 2020, when it was $3, 894.

Concept note-2: -Gross domestic product (GDP) in Sri Lanka 2022 The gross domestic product in current prices in Sri Lanka decreased to 73.74 billion U.S. dollars since the previous year. This means a decline of 15.2 billion U.S. dollars (-17.08 percent) in 2022.

Concept note-3: -GDP in Sri Lanka averaged 23.62 USD Billion from 1960 until 2021, reaching an all time high of 94.49 USD Billion in 2018 and a record low of 1.24 USD Billion in 1963.
