What is the name given to the project launched by IGSTC to promote women researchers in joint R & D projects through lateral entry?

(A) Assure

(B) Resilient

(C) She

(D) ** Wiser

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Women Involvement in Science and Engineering Research (WISER)

Concept note-2: -About: WISER is one of-its-kind programme, launched to promote women in the field of research and development by lateral entry. Parent Organizations: WISER Program was launched by Indo-German Science & Technology Centre (IGSTC) to encourage women researchers in R&D projects.

Concept note-3: -The ten (10) women researchers from India and one (1) from Germany holding regular/long-term research positions in academia, research institutions, or industry selected under the WISER-2022 programme will receive financial assistance to participate and collaborate in ongoing R&D & industry projects.
