What is the name given to the world’s largest aircraft by American company ‘Stratoluanch’?

(A) Pamola

(B) Sirin

(C) ** Roc

(D) Ziz

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Scaled Composites Model 351 Stratolaunch or Roc is an aircraft built by Scaled Composites for Stratolaunch Systems to carry air-launch-to-orbit (ALTO) rockets. It was announced in December 2011, rolled out in May 2017, and flew the first time on April 13, 2019.

Concept note-2: -The Antonov An-225 Mriya (Ukrainian: -225 , lit. ‘dream’ or ‘inspiration’; NATO reporting name: Cossack) was a strategic airlift cargo aircraft designed and produced by the Antonov Design Bureau in the Soviet Union.

Concept note-3: -Boeing has built over 1, 500 Boeing 747s, and the planes have served as the largest passenger and cargo aircraft in the world, a military command center in the sky, Air Force One, and a space shuttle lifter and infrared telescope flier for NASA.
