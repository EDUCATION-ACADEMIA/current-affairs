What is the name of Cyclone which has developed into a super cyclone in the Bay of Bengal on 19 May?

(A) UMPHAN

(B) ** AMPHAN

(C) IMPHAN

(D) OMPHAN

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The system quickly developed and was designated as Depression ARB 03 by IMD. The storm unexpectedly rapidly developed and the JTWC designated it as Tropical Cyclone 07A, peaking with winds of 60 mph (95 km/h). After ARB 03, Depression BOB 10 formed and meandered in the Bay of Bengal before dissipating.

Concept note-2: -Cyclone Mandous The cyclone in the Bay of Bengal has already turned into a depression.

Concept note-3: -These include the names Nisarga, Gati, Nivar, Burevi, Tauktae, Yaas, Gulab, Shaheen, Jawad, Asani and Sitrang. And now, Mandous - a name suggested by the United Arab Emirates - is the next in line. The previous cyclone, dubbed Sitrang, had impacted India’s eastern coast during the 2022 Diwali week late in October.

Concept note-4: -Severe cyclone Asani raging in Bay of Bengal; expected to weaken without landfall.
