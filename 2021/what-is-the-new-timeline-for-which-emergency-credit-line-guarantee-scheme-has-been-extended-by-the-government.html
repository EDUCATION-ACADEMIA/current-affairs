What is the new timeline for which Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme has been extended by the government?

(A) May 31

(B) ** June 30

(C) July 31

(D) April 30

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Scope of Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) enhanced and validity extended till 31.3. 2023.

Concept note-2: -4.5 Lakh crore to Rs. 5 Lakh crore for these sectors which will valid till 31st March, 2023.

Concept note-3: -In addition to this, 100% guarantee for additional funding up to Rs 4.5 lakh crore for Covid-affected MSMEs was sanctioned. Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman as a part of the Union Budget 2022 announced that the Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) will be extended to March 2023.

Concept note-4: -Last date of disbursement under fund based facility and utilization of LC under non-fund facility shall be June 30, 2022. The last date of disbursement for fund based facility under the scheme shall be June 30, 2023.
