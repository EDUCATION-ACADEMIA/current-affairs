What is the new wholly-owned subsidiary set up by Punjab National Bank to manage its credit card business?

(A) PNB Credit Cards Freedom Ltd

(B) PNB Card Elite

(C) ** PNB Cards & Services Ltd

(D) PNB Cards Ltd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -## PNB Cards and Services Ltd. has been incorporated on 16.03. 2021 as wholly owned Credit Card Subsidiary of PNB.

Concept note-2: -All nation-wide branches of United Bank of India and Oriental Bank of Commerce will now function as Punjab National bank (PNB) branches while all customers, including depositors, will be treated as PNB customers.

Concept note-3: -In 1993, PNB acquired New Bank of India, which the GOI had nationalised in 1980.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Punjab National Bank. Punjab National Bank (PNB) has launched the ‘6S Campaign’ under customer outreach program to extend financial services at a concessional rate.
