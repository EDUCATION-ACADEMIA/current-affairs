What is the rank of India in the 2021 Global Energy Transition Index (ETI)?

(A) 95

(B) ** 87

(C) 52

(D) 76

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The report was released by the World Economic Forum (WEF), prepared in collaboration with Accenture. Why is Global Energy Transition Index in News? In the recently published report, India ranked 87th out of 115 countries in the Energy Transition Index(ETI).

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Sweden. Sweden is the top-ranking country in the World Economic Forum’s 74th Global Energy Transition Index.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is 87th. India has been ranked at the 87th position among 115 countries in the Energy Transition Index (ETI) that tracks nations on the current performance of their energy systems across various aspects, according to a World Economic Forum (WEF) report released.
