What is the rank of India in the 2021 Renewable Energy Country Attractiveness Index (RECAI)?

(A) 5th

(B) 4th

(C) 2nd

(D) ** 3rd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has retained the third position in the 58th Renewable Energy Country Attractiveness Index (RECAI) released by the consultancy firm Ernst & Young (EY).

Concept note-2: -RECAI ranks the world’s top 40 markets (nations) on the attractiveness of their renewable energy investment and deployment opportunities. The US and China are at the first and second positions respectively.

Concept note-3: -Global renewable energy attractiveness score by country 2021 The United States is the most attractive country for renewable energy investments worldwide, with a RECAI score of 65.8. Mainland China ranked second, with a score of 70.7.
