What is the rank of India in the UN World Happiness Report 2021 among 149 countries?

(A) ** 139

(B) 118

(C) 106

(D) 121

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has ranked 139 in the World Happiness Rankings 2021 published in the 9th World Happiness Report. It has been released in March 2021. India’s rank in 2020 was 144.

Concept note-2: -Finland was deemed the happiest country. India ranked 136th on the World Happiness Report compiled and released by the United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network on Saturday. It has ranked a total of 150 countries.

Concept note-3: -UN World Happiness Report 2022: India Ranks 136th.

Concept note-4: -Finland has been ranked as the happiest country in the world. The Nordic nation is followed by Iceland, Denmark, Switzerland, The Netherlands, Sweden, Germany and Norway. Pakistan is on 105th, Bangladesh on 101st and China on 84th, according to the report.
