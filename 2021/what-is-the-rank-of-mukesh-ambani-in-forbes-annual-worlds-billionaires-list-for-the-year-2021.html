What is the rank of Mukesh Ambani in Forbes’ annual world’s billionaires list for the year 2021?

(A) 12th

(B) 15th

(C) 8th

(D) ** 10th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are 2, 668 billionaires in the 36th-annual ranking of the planet’s richest people. New Delhi: Reliance Industries chairman Mukesh Ambani is the 10th richest person in the world, followed by Infrastructure tycoon Gautam Adani and his family, which claimed the 11th spot on Forbes’ annual world’s billionaires list.

Concept note-2: -Forbes Billionaires 2022: Ambani 10th Richest, Adani 11th; Musk Tops List.

Concept note-3: -Forbes top 100 richest Indian billionaires: Gautam Adani tops the list, Mukesh Ambani at second spot.

Concept note-4: -2. Mukesh Ambani. Scoring tenth place among the richest persons in the world in 2022, Mukesh Ambani has $91 billion in net worth. The founder and chairman of Reliance Industries has his interests across petrochemicals, oil and gas, retail and telecom.

Concept note-5: -The pecking order of the top three is a repeat of 2021. Despite a modest 7% boost to his wealth, Mukesh Ambani, whose Reliance Industries is India’s most valuable company, retained his position as Asia’s richest person-and the tenth richest person in the world-with a net worth of $90.7 billion.
