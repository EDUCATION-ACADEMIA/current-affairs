What is the theme of 2021 World Intellectual Property Day?

(A) Reach for Gold:IP and Sports

(B) ** Intellectual property and small businesses:Taking big ideas to market

(C) Innovate for a Green Future

(D) Powering change:Women in innovation and creativity

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World IP Day 2021 highlights the key role IP rights play in helping SMEs build stronger, more competitive businesses, under the theme ‘Taking your ideas to the market’.

Concept note-2: -The theme of World Intellectual Property Day 2022 is ‘IP and Youth Innovating for a Better Future’. Events organized to mark the Day seek to inform about the role of intellectual property rights in encouraging innovation and creativity.

Concept note-3: -In 2023, the theme for World Intellectual Property Day is “Women and IP: Accelerating Innovation and Creativity”.

Concept note-4: -India ranked at the 40th position in the Global Intellectual Property Index 2021.

Concept note-5: -“Create the future today by encouraging creativity and innovation on this day.” “The World Intellectual Property Day is all about celebrating innovation and promoting respect for intellectual property.
