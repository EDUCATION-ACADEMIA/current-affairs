What is the theme of Bengaluru Tech Summit 2020?

(A) ** Next is Now

(B) Next is Today

(C) Start New

(D) Next is New

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A case in point is the Bengaluru Tech Summit (BTS). With the aim to facilitate the growth of the tech and startup ecosystem and honour the ones taking the country’s growth story forward, the state government has been organising the BTS since 1998, and will be hosting its silver jubilee edition this month.

Concept note-2: -The 25th edition of Asia’s largest technology event, the Bengaluru Tech Summit 2022 (BTS 2022), is all set to start on Wednesday. Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the event virtually.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi will address the inaugural event on 16 November. The flagship event is being organised by the Department of Electronics, IT, Bt, S&T, Government of Karnataka along with Software Technology Parks of India (STPI).

Concept note-4: -Chennai, Dec 9 (KNN) Asia’s largest summit on technology and innovation ‘Umagine Chennai 2023’ is all set to take place in the state from March 23 to 25.
