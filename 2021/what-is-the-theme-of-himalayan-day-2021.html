What is the theme of Himalayan Day 2021?

(A) Himalaya:Science and Knowledge

(B) Sustaining Dynamic Himalaya

(C) The Himalaya and Nature

(D) ** Contribution of Himalayas and our responsibilities

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Mission For Clean Ganga & Naula Foundation Celebrate Himalayan Day 2021 With The Theme ‘Contribution Of Himalayas And Our Responsibilities’. The National Mission for Clean Ganga organised Himalayan Diwas in association with Naula Foundation.

Concept note-2: -Himalaya Day 2022 was observed under the theme ‘Himalayas will be safe only when the interests of its residents are protected’.

Concept note-3: -Himalayan Diwas: History In 2015, September 9 was officially declared as Himalaya Day by the then Chief Minister of Uttarakhand. The Himalayas play an important role in saving and maintaining nature and protecting the country from adverse weather conditions.

Concept note-4: -’Himalaya Diwas’ or ‘Himalaya Day’ is celebrated on September 9 in Uttarakhand to raise awareness about the conservation of the rich flora and fauna of the state. In 2014, the then CM Harish Rawat declared September 9 as ‘Himalaya Diwas’ to propagate the message of conservation of the Himalayan ecosystems.

Concept note-5: -In Uttarakhand, Himalaya Day is observed on September 9 each year.
