What is the theme of the 2021 International Day for Mine Awareness and Assistance in Mine Action?

(A) ** Perseverance, Partnership, and Progress

(B) Safe Ground

(C) Mine Action is Humanitarian Action

(D) Advancing Protection, Peace and Development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Safe Ground, Safe Steps, Safe Home.

Concept note-2: -The day aims to raise awareness about landmines and progress toward their eradication. “Mine action” refers to a range of efforts to clear landmines and explosive remnants of war and to mark and fence off dangerous areas.

Concept note-3: -Significance. The world needs to be free from the dangers of landmines and explosives. So, the theme is highlighting “Safe Ground", concentrating on clearing Earth of landmines and other explosives to make it safe for development.

Concept note-4: -The International Day for Mine Awareness and Assistance in Mine Action reminds us how far we have come in clearing the world of explosive remnants of war – and how far we still have to go. Exactly thirty years ago, civil society activists came together to launch the International Campaign to Ban Landmines.

Concept note-5: -Every year, International Day of Mine Awareness and Assistance in Mine Action is observed on April 04. It is observed for the establishment and development of people affected by the harmful effects of explosive mine action across the world.
