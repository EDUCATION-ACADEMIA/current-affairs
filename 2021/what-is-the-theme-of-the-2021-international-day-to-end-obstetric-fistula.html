What is the theme of the 2021 International Day to End Obstetric Fistula?

(A) Leaving no one behind:Let us commit to ending fisula now!

(B) End gender inequality! End health inequities!

(C) Hope, healing, and dignity for all

(D) ** Women’s rights are human rights! End fistula now!

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Women’s rights are human rights! End fistula now!

Concept note-2: -There are different types of obstetric fistula. Rectovaginal fistula: Hole between birth canal and rectum. Urethrovaginal fistula: Hole between birth canal and urethra (carries urine from bladder out of the body) Ureterovaginal fistula: Hole between birth canal ureters (carry urine to bladder) and birth canal.

Concept note-3: -To that end, the theme of this year’s observance is “End Fistula Now: Invest in Quality Healthcare, Empower Communities!” Obstetric fistula is a development and public health issue, but it’s also a human rights issue, one that grants everyone the right to health and a life of dignity.

Concept note-4: -Obstetric fistula is a hole in the birth canal caused by obstructed labour.

Concept note-5: -Rectovaginal fistula represented 1% to 8% of cases; vesicovaginal fistula made up 79% to 100% of cases, and combined vesico and rectovaginal fistula represented 1% to 23% of cases (Table 2).
