What is the theme of the G7 summit 2021?

(A) Investing in growth that works for everyone

(B) Preparing for jobs of the future

(C) ** Build Back Better

(D) Building a more peaceful and secure world

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The UK government has announced that the focus for this year’s G7 summit will be on global trade, strengthening global response for any future pandemics and tackling climate change.

Concept note-2: -27 June 2022 "Investing in a Better Future” on climate, energy and health with G7 partner countries and international organizations: UK announced £25 million of UK aid backing for a new fund to ensure the world is better prepared to defeat future pandemics.

Concept note-3: -The G7 Summit was held in Carbis Bay, Cornwall on 11-13 June 2021. Prime Minister Boris Johnson brought the world’s leading democracies together to reach major new agreements to help the world fight, and then build back better from coronavirus and create a greener, more prosperous future.

Concept note-4: -“Progress towards an equitable world” – The slogan chosen by the German government for its Presidency of the Group of 7 ( G7Group of 7, seven major industrial countries ) this year clearly indicates the route it has mapped out.
