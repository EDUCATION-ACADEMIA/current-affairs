What is the theme of the Janaushadhi Week 2021?

(A) ** Seva Bhi, Rozgar Bhi

(B) Jan Seva

(C) Generic Medicines

(D) Generic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of 4th Janaushadhi Diwas: “Jan Aushadhi-Jan Upyogi”. The Government has set a target to increase the number of Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) to 10, 500 by the end of March 2025.

Concept note-2: -Q. What is the theme of the Janaushadhi Diwas 2022? Notes: Pharmaceuticals & Medical Devices Bureau of India (PMBI), under the aegis of Department of Pharmaceuticals is set to celebrate the Jan Aushadhi Diwas Week, with the main event on March 7. The theme of 4th Janaushadhi Diwas is “Jan Aushadhi-Jan Upyogi”.

Concept note-3: -The scheme is truly doing justice to its tagline “Jan Aushadhi - Seva bhi, Rozgar bhi”.

Concept note-4: -Share: New Delhi: Prime Minister Narendra Modi interacted with the beneficiaries of the Jan Aushadhi Yojana today on the occasion of Jan Aushadhi Diwas via video conferencing. The theme of the event was ‘Jan Aushadhi-Jan Upyogi’.
