What is the theme of World Pulses Day 2021?

(A) Nitrogen-Fixing Legumes for a Sustainable Future

(B) ** Nutritious Seeds for a Sustainable Future

(C) Plant Proteins for a Sustainable Future

(D) Love Pulses

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Pulses Day 2021 is the celebration of the contribution of pulses to sustainable food systems, healthy growth, and environmental benefits.

Concept note-2: -World Pulses Day 2022 is under the theme “Pulses to empower youth in achieving sustainable agrifood systems”. Pulses are important for a healthy diet. Take this day as an opportunity to raise awareness and recognize the contribution of pulses to sustainable food systems and the healthy food cycles.

Concept note-3: -#LovePulses for a healthy diet and planet! This year’s theme is: - .

Concept note-4: -EVENTS: Based on the benefits that pulses provide to agrifood systems and the environment, the FAO Steering Committee selected “Pulses for a Sustainable Future” as the theme for the 2023 World Pulses Day celebration. Join us online on 10 February 2023 from 14:00–15:30 CET.

Concept note-5: -The theme of World Pulses Day 2022 is “Pulses to empower youth in achieving sustainable agrifood systems". The theme focuses on generating livelihood opportunities and equity, which are also significant for sustainable agrifood systems.
