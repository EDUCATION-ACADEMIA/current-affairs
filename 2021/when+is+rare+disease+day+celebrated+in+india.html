When is Rare Disease Day celebrated in India?

(A) 28 February

(B) ** Last day of February

(C) Last Sunday of February

(D) 27 February

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Social feed Thank you raredisorders for joining the #LightUpForRare movement and raising awareness on #RareDiseaseDay! on 28 February 2023? Did you know that 1 in 5 cancers is rare?

Concept note-2: -February was officially declared Rare Disease Month in 2008 with February having the “rarest” number of days. Thirteen years later, we continue to be rare but stronger than ever!

Concept note-3: -The themes for Rare Disease Day 2023 are #ShowYourStripes and #ShareYourColours, giving the community a visible way to come together on February 28.

Concept note-4: -28th February TOFS support Rare Disease Day – a globally-coordinated movement on rare diseases, working towards equity in social opportunity, healthcare, and access to diagnosis and therapies for people living with a rare disease.
