When is World Laughter Day observed?

(A) 02 May

(B) ** First Sunday of May

(C) 03 May

(D) First Saturday of May

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Laughter Day is celebrated every year on the first Sunday of May to raise awareness about laughter and its many healing benefits.

Concept note-2: -World Laughter Day is celebrated on the first Sunday in May every year to raise awareness of laughter and its many healing benefits.

Concept note-3: -World Laughter Day is celebrated to raise awareness about laughter and its benefits to the people. World Laughter Day is also celebrated with the aim to manifest world peace and build global consciousness through laughter.

Concept note-4: -Madan Kataria, the founder of the Laughter Yoga movement, established World Laughter Day. The first event was held in Mumbai, India on May 10, 1998. Dr. Kataria is a family doctor in India.
