When was Crop Insurance Week 2021 observed?

(A) ** July 1-July 7

(B) July 3-July 9

(C) July 4-July 10

(D) July 2-July 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fasal Bima Yojana aims to provide security cover to each farmer. From 1 to 7 July 2021, the government is celebrating Crop Insurance Week.

Concept note-2: -The purpose of celebrating Crop Insurance Week is to conduct massive awareness campaign and ensure maximum enrolment of the farmers under the Insurance scheme during Rabi- 2022-23 from 1st to 6th of Dec 2022.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) Latest Update: Assam Government under Agriculture department announced on 15th July to Register 5 lakh Farmers under PM Fasal Bima Yojana by July 31 for Sali paddy.

Concept note-4: -Professor V. M. Dandekar, often referred to as the “Father of Crop Insurance in India”, suggested an alternate “Homogeneous Area approach” for crop insurance in the mid-seventies. Based on this Area approach, the General Insurance Corporation of India (GIC) introduced a Pilot Crop Insurance Scheme (PCIS) from 1979.
