When is Italy hosting the G20 summit 2021 in Rome?

(A) Oct 27

(B) Oct 29

(C) Oct 28

(D) ** Oct 30

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 30 and 31 October 2021, G20 leaders met for a two-day summit hosted by the Italian G20 presidency in Rome.

Concept note-2: -The previous G20 summit, in 2021, was hosted by the Italian G20 presidency in Rome.

Concept note-3: -On October 30 and 31, the G20 Rome Summit was held in Rome, Italy.

Concept note-4: -India’s G -20 Presidency India holds the Presidency of the G20 from December 1, 2022 to November 30, 2023. The 43 Heads of Delegations- the largest ever in G20-will be participating in the final New Delhi Summit in September next year.
