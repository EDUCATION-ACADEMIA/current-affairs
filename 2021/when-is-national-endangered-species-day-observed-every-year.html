When is National Endangered Species Day observed every year?

(A) 1st Friday of May

(B) ** 3rd Friday of May

(C) 2nd Monday of May

(D) 3rd Monday of May

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Every year on the third Friday in May, National Endangered Species Day is observed, and this year it will be observed on May 20.

Concept note-2: -The National Endangered Species Day theme 2022 is “Recovering key species for ecosystem restoration”.

Concept note-3: -The International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) in March 2022 listed the long-tailed macaques as endangered due to the rapid population decline and the prognosis of decline giving the continuation of current trends of exploitation and habitat destruction.

Concept note-4: -Endangered Species Day is observed each year on the third Friday of May as a way to both raise awareness of the continued plight of endangered species and celebrate those that have recovered because of conservation efforts.

Concept note-5: -The National Endangered Species Day is observed across the globe annually on every third Friday. This year the day is being observed on 20th May 2022.
