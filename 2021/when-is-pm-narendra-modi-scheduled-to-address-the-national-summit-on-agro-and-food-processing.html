When is PM Narendra Modi scheduled to address the National Summit on Agro and Food Processing?

(A) December 14

(B) December 15

(C) ** December 16

(D) December 17

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi will address the valedictory session of the National Summit on Agro and Food Processing on 16th December 2021 from 11 AM in which detail contours of Natural Farming shall be presented.

Concept note-2: -This summit is being organised as part of “Vibrant Gujarat Summit” which is scheduled to be held in Anand, Gujarat from December 14 to December 16.

Concept note-3: -PM Kisan New Farmer Registration 2023 Direct Link: One of the most beneficial yojana of government of India for farmers is Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana. Under this scheme, the government transfers Rs 6000 every year to the bank accounts of farmers.

Concept note-4: -Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN), a flagship scheme of Government of India, is an outcome of the continued commitment of Prime Minister of India, Sri Narendra Modi to initiate policy actions and implement public programs for an inclusive and productive agricultural sector.

Concept note-5: -Union Minister for Agriculture and Farmers Welfare Shri Narendra Singh Tomar Inaugurated Krishi Mahotsav 2022 at Borimol sports Complex Quepem, Goa on 4th January.
