When is the International Children’s Book Day (ICBD) observed?

(A) 1st April

(B) ** 2nd April

(C) 3rd April

(D) 5th April

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICBD 2022 sponsored by IBBY Canada.

Concept note-2: -Activities around the world on this day include library scavenger hunts, writing competitions, announcements of book awards, and events with authors of children’s literature.

Concept note-3: -World Book Day History It is celebrated all over the world on 23rd April but in the UK it always runs on the first Thursday in March.
