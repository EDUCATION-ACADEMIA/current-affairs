When is Wimbledon scheduled to begin this year?

(A) July 31st

(B) August 8th

(C) ** June 28th

(D) September 11th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When are the draws for the Wimbledon 2022? The draws for the Wimbledon 2022 will take place on Friday, June 24 at 2:30 PM IST.

Concept note-2: -Wimbledon men’s singles final time and how to watch on TV The contest is set to start at 2pm on Centre Court and will be aired live on BBC One, with coverage starting from 1pm.

Concept note-3: -Wimbledon 2023 will begin on Monday 3rd July 2023 and run until the men’s singles final on Sunday 16th July 2023. As ever, the classic two-week format will return, but the tournament will start one week later than usual due to a schedule change from the All England Club.
