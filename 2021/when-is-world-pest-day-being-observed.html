When is World Pest Day being observed?

(A) 5 June

(B) 4 June

(C) ** 6 June

(D) 3 June

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Pest Day, also referred to as World Pest Awareness Day, is celebrated every year on June 6.

Concept note-2: -The day will look at “One World – Protecting Global Public Health through Professional Pest Management.” You can mark World Pest Day by calling in a professional pest control treatment for your home at regular intervals.

Concept note-3: -World Pest Day is celebrated every year on June 6. This day is dedicated to spread awareness about pest management and its benefits. The day also aims to educate people to keep the crops, trees and plants free from pests.

Concept note-4: -World Pest Day was initiated by the Chinese Pest Control Association, and co-sponsored by the Federation of Asian and Oceania Pest Managers’ Association (FAOPMA), the National Pest Management Association (NPMA), and the Confederation of European Pest Management Associations (CEPA).
