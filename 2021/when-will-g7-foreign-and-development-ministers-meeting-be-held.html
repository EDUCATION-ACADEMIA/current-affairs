When will G7 Foreign and Development Minister’s meeting be held?

(A) 1-3 May 2021

(B) ** 3-6 May 2021

(C) 3-8 May 2021

(D) 4-6 May 2021

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -G7 Foreign and Development Ministers met in Liverpool on 11-12 December 2021. We discussed our shared interests and values, and our collaboration as economic and security partners.

Concept note-2: -Following their meeting in Münster, G7 foreign ministers gave a joint statement on a range of international issues including Russia’s war against Ukraine. Foreign Secretary James Cleverly with G7 foreign ministers in Münster, Germany.

Concept note-3: -G7 – Participation of the Minister for Europe and Foreign Affairs in the G7 Foreign Ministers’ Meeting (Weissenhaus, 12-14 May 2022) Minister for Europe and Foreign Affairs Jean-Yves Le Drian will take part in the G7 Foreign Ministers’ Meeting in Weissenhaus, Germany, from May 12 to 14.

Concept note-4: -As the G7 Presidency in 2023, Japan has decided to host the G7 Trade Ministers’ Meeting in Osaka-Sakai, on 28 and 29 October 2023 after hosting the first online meeting on 4 April 2023. In October, the meeting and related events will be held in the host cities of Osaka and Sakai.
