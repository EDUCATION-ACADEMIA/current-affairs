When will PM launch Pradhan Mantri Gati Shakthi?

(A) 11th October

(B) 12th October

(C) ** 13th October

(D) 14th October

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hon’ble Prime Minister launched the PM GatiShakti National Master Plan for providing multimodal connectivity infrastructure to various economic zones on 13th October 2021.

Concept note-2: -Gati Shakti: Launch date On October 13, 2021, PM Modi launched the Pradhan Mantri Gati Shakti (PMGS) scheme, aimed at breaking inter-ministerial silos and to integrate the planning of infrastructure projects.

Concept note-3: -Rs 5, 000 crore of the Rs 1 lakh crore earmarked under the ‘Scheme for Special Assistance to States for Capital Investment for 2022-23’ to the states for undertaking infrastructure projects under the PM Gati Shakti Mission.

Concept note-4: -Under the Pradhan Mantri Gati Shakti (PMGS), one of the more ambitious programmes of Prime Minister Narendra Modi, a total investment of more than Rs 1 lakh crore has been approved for 22 major infrastructure projects, sources have said.
