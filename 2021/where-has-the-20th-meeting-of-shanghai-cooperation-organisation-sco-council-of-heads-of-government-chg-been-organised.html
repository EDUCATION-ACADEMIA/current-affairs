Where has the 20th Meeting of Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Council of Heads of Government (CHG) been organised?

(A) Bishkek

(B) Ashgabat

(C) ** Nur-Sultan

(D) Baku

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -20th Meeting of Prosecutors General of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Member States organized at Astana, Kazakhstan.

Concept note-2: -India has taken over as Chairperson of the SCO for 2023, and will host leaders of all SCO countries at a summit in Delhi expected in mid-2023. Earlier, the SCO summit 2022 was held recently in Samarkand, Uzbekistan.

Concept note-3: -The 20th Meeting of Prosecutors General of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Member States was held on 23rd September 2022 in Astana, Kazakhstan.

Concept note-4: -External Affairs Minister Dr. S. Jaishankar will represent India in the meeting. The SCO CHG meeting held annually focuses on the trade and economic agenda of the Organization and approves its annual budget.

Concept note-5: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi attended the 20th Meeting of Council of Heads of State (CHS) of SCO hosted by Russia in Video Conference Format on November 10, 2020. This was the first SCO Summit held in Virtual Format.
