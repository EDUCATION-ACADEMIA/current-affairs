Where is the World’s tallest swing ride Bollywood Skyflyer located?

(A) Mumbai

(B) London

(C) Paris

(D) ** Dubai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Skyflyer at Bollywood Parks Dubai is the tallest swing ride in the world. It is taller than the Great Pyramid of Giza.

Concept note-2: -Dubai, one of the most-visited countries in the world has once again surprised people by launching the tallest swing ride at the Bollywood Park. The swing has been named as ‘Bollywood Skyflyer’ and this 460 feet tall swing is almost the same height as the Pyramid of Giza.

Concept note-3: -Bollywood Sky flyer is the world record breaking ride and is the tallest swing ride in the world. It is a unique attraction that appeals to both young and old, the adventurous and not so adventurous. At 460ft Bollywood Sky flyer gives you the experience of hang gliding and swinging – all at the same time.

Concept note-4: -Location. The Starflyer is located in ICON Park, an outdoor entertainment complex on International Drive in Orlando. The Starflyer is at 8375 International Drive, part of “The Wheelhouse” cluster of rides and shops. The Starflyer is the centerpiece of this cluster.

Concept note-5: -Bollywood SkyFlyer is a Star Flyer manufactured by Funtime currently located at Bollywood Parks Dubai in Dubai, Dubai, UAE. Upon opening in 2021, the ride was marketed as the world’s tallest swing ride.
