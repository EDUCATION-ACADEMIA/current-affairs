Where will the joint military exercise PANEX-21 with BIMSTEC countries be held?

(A) Delhi

(B) Assam

(C) Sikkim

(D) ** Pune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PANEX-21, a multi-national - multi-agency exercise is being organised at Pune from 20-22 Dec 2021 with a focused aim to foster common and develop capabilities in Disaster Management aspects for the BIMSTEC nations.

Concept note-2: -Tri-services Humanitarian Aid and Disaster Relief Exercise: PANEX-21 for BIMSTEC Member States is currently underway from 20-22 December 2021 in Pune, organised by the Southern Command of the Indian Army.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Pune. Defence Minister Rajnath Singh attended a Multi-Agency Exercise (MAE) and inaugurated an equipment display on the second day of PANEX-21 in Pune 21 December 2021.

Concept note-4: -About: PANEX-21 is a multi-national Humanitarian Assistance and Disaster Relief exercise held among BIMSTEC Countries. Objective: PANEX-21 aims to build regional cooperation among BIMSTEC Member countries in responding to various natural disasters.

Concept note-5: -India, Kazakhstan hold joint military exercise in Meghalaya | North East India News, The Indian Express.
