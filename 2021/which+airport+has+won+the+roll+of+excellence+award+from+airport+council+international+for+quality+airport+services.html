Which airport has won the ‘Roll of Excellence’ award from ‘Airport Council International’ for quality airport services?

(A) Rajiv Gandhi International Airport

(B) Indira Gandhi International Airport

(C) ** Cochin International Airport

(D) Bengaluru International Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Cochin International Airport : The Cochin International Airport Ltd (CIAL) won the Airport Council International’s (ACI) Director General’s Roll of Excellence honour for Airport Service Quality.

Concept note-2: -The Cochin International Airport Limited (CIAL) has been awarded the Airport Service Quality (ASQ) award 2022 by the Airport Council International (ACI). The award is considered to be the highest honour in the global aviation sector.

Concept note-3: -The Cochin International Airport Limited (CIAL) has been awarded the Airport Service Quality (ASQ) award 2022 by the Airport Council International (ACI). The award is considered to be the highest honour in the global aviation sector.

Concept note-4: -The Roll of Excellence recognition is given to those airports which, in the opinion of the passengers, have consistently delivered excellent services in the Airport Service Quality Survey.
