Which among the following has acquired a 40% stake in designer Manish Malhotra’s MM Styles?

(A) Hindustan Unilever Ltd

(B) ** Reliance Industries Ltd

(C) Infiniti Retail Ltd

(D) Trent limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliance Brands acquires 40% stake in designer Manish Malhotra’s MM Styles.

Concept note-4: -RBL has signed a definitive agreement to invest in founder and creative director, Manish Malhotra’s eponymous brand for a 40 per cent minority stake.
