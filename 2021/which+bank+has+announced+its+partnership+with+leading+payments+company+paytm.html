(A) SBI

(B) ** HDFC Bank

(C) ICICI

(D) RBI

Concept note-1: -This partnership will enable Jana Small Finance Bank bank to extend our All-in-one EDC machines to existing and potential customers, providing a one-stop solution for all their digital payment needs.
