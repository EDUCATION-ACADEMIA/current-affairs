Which bank has launched a new loan product named ‘Kavach Personal Loan?

(A) Canara Bank

(B) HDFC Bank

(C) ** State Bank of India

(D) ICICI Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SBI Kavach is a personal loan scheme launched by the State Bank of India which you can avail if you or any member of your family have been diagnosed with Covid-19 after 1 April 2021. You can apply for this SBI Personal loan scheme and use the amount to meet the cost of the treatment.

Concept note-2: -2021. You can get a minimum of Rs. 25, 000 and a maximum of Rs. 5 Lakhs loan amount as per your eligibility.
