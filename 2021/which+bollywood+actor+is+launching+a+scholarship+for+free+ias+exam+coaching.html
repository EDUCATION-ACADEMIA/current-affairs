Which Bollywood actor is launching a scholarship for free IAS exam coaching?

(A) Salman Khan

(B) Amir Khan

(C) ** Sonu Sood

(D) Shahid Kapoor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sonu Sood IAS Free Coaching Scholarship 2023: Sonu Sood Scholarship is a kind initiative by the Bollywood actor, Sonu Sood to provide free coaching to the deserving aspirants of the UPSC Civil Services Exam. The official notification for 2023 will be available soon.

Concept note-2: -SAMBHAVAM: Scholarship Program For UPSC Coaching SAMBHAVAM is an initiative of the Sood Charity Foundation and Divine India Youth Association (DIYA) to provide scholarships for UPSC preparation. It was launched in September 2022. The scholarship schemes provide mentorship and personality development training.

Concept note-3: -Go to the official website portal of the Sonu Sood Foundation. Landing on the home page of the portal, applicants will have to tap on the scholarship campaign. Under the IAS scholarship program, applicants will see a link to register on the portal. Clicking on the option, an application form will open up.

Concept note-4: -Actor and philanthropist Sonu Sood has started a free online coaching programme for those interested in the IAS for the academic year 2022-2023. Sonu Sood became well-known in the nation not only for his roles as villains in Bollywood movies but also for his humanitarian work during the Covid-19 pandemic.

Concept note-5: -This free scholarship test for the UPSC coaching 2023 test includes general knowledge and aptitude questions and the student will get up to 50 percent of the scholarship in the annual coaching fee. To enroll in the free IAS scholarship program, you will have to contact the expert of Youth Destination.
