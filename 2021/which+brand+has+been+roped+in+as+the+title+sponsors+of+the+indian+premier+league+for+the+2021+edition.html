Which brand has been roped in as the title sponsors of the Indian Premier League for the 2021 edition?

(A) Unacademy

(B) Byju’s

(C) ** VIVO

(D) Dream11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2021 Indian Premier League, also known as IPL 14, or for sponsorship reasons, Vivo IPL 2021, was the fourteenth season of the Indian Premier League (IPL), a professional Twenty20 cricket league established by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007.

Concept note-2: -Vivo pulled out as the title sponsor of the tournament on 11 January 2022. Vivo, having previously pulled out as sponsors in 2020, had signed contract as title sponsors until 2023. The Tata Group was named as the title sponsor for the remainder of Vivo’s contract.

Concept note-3: -Indian Premier League Governing Council chairman Brijesh Patel revealed at the IPL 2021 Auction that VIVO is back as the title sponsor of the league.
