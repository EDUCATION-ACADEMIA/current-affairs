Which city has been ranked top in the fastest growing technology center in the world?

(A) Pune

(B) ** Bengaluru

(C) Chennai

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bengaluru has emerged as the world’s fastest growing tech ecosystem in the world since 2016.

Concept note-2: -Some of the fastest-growing tech industries in the world in 2022 are revealed here, and they include FinTech (Financial Technology), Workplace Technologies, Cybersecurity, and Healthcare Technology. Additionally, Virtual Reality (VR), Cloud Computing, and Machine Learning are also rapidly growing tech industries.

Concept note-3: -Tokyo. A world leader in innovation for decades, Tokyo has often been cited as the world’s most advanced city for technology. In fact, Tokyo is home to many recognizable names in consumer and professional-grade electronics.

Concept note-4: -The tech industry in India is one of the fastest-growing tech hubs in the world, reporting a growth rate of 12.2%. The southern Indian tech hub of Bangalore is also home to the most significant number of high-growth companies in India.

Concept note-5: -With the state’s startups raising $2.5 Bn in funding between 2014 and H1 2022, Telangana is at the top of the list. One of India’s youngest states, Telangana is home to one of India’s fastest-growing emerging startup hubs in Hyderabad.
