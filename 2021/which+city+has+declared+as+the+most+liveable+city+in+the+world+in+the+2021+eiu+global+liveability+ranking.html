Which city has declared as the most liveable city in the world in the 2021 EIU Global Liveability Ranking?

(A) Tokyo

(B) ** Auckland

(C) Adelaide

(D) Wellington

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Auckland (New Zealand) has topped the Economist Intelligence Unit’s (EIU) Global Liveability Index of 140 cities around the world. Top 3 Liveable Cities: Auckland (New Zealand), Osaka (Japan), Adelaide (Australia).

Concept note-2: -EIU ranks 173 global cities based on their urban quality of life by accessing healthcare, stability, environment, culture, education and infrastructure. In year 2022, Vienna was ranked the most liveable city. Vienna has been ranked 1st in 2018 and 2019 as well. In 2021, Auckland was ranked as most liveable city.

Concept note-3: -Auckland was ranked the most liveable city in 2021. Melbourne, Australia, had been ranked by the EIU as the world’s most liveable city for seven years in a row, from 2011 to 2017.

Concept note-4: -For the third time in the past five years, Vienna captured the first spot in the 2022 Global Liveability Index, the annual report published by The Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU). The top 10 was dominated by western European cities, which make up half of those on the list.
