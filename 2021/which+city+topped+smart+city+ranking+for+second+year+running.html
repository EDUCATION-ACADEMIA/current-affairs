Which city topped smart city ranking for second year running?

(A) Delhi

(B) ** Singapore

(C) Texas

(D) Indore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indore. This city in Madhya Pradesh ranks high as a smart city in India.

Concept note-2: -Smart city digital capability ranking worldwide 2022 In 2022, the leading global digital city on the index ranking shown here was Copenhagen with a score of 80.3. Seoul, Beijing, Amsterdam, and Singapore rounded out the top 5 for the best digital cities.

Concept note-3: -Bhubaneswar topped the list of the top 20, followed by Pune and Jaipur.

Concept note-4: -1 | Bhopal The smart city Bhopal was placed first in the recent 2022 ranking of the union ministry of housing and urban affairs. It is the capital of the state Madhya Pradesh and has a population of approximately 2.5 million people. Furthermore, it is also considered one of the greenest cities in India.
