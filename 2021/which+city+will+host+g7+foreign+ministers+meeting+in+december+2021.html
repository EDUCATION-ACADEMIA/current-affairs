Which city will host G7 Foreign Ministers’ Meeting in December 2021?

(A) Glasgow

(B) ** Liverpool

(C) Beijing

(D) Berlin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -G7 Foreign and Development Ministers met in Liverpool on 11-12 December 2021.
