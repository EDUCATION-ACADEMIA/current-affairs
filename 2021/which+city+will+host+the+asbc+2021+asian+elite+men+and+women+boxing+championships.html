Which city will host the ASBC 2021 Asian Elite Men and Women Boxing Championships?

(A) ** Dubai

(B) New Delhi

(C) Manali

(D) Jakarta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 31st edition of the Asian Amateur Boxing Championships was held from 24 to 31 May 2021 in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

Concept note-2: -India won 12 medals, including four golds. India’s Shiva Thapa had to settle for a silver medal in the men’s 63.5kg category at Asian Boxing Championships 2022 in Amman, Jordan on Saturday.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is New Delhi. International Boxing Association (AIBA) has announced that the Asian Boxing Championships 2021 will be hosted by New Delhi, India from May 21-31, 2021. It will be the first boxing tournament in Asia to be held in 2021.

Concept note-4: -Silver medallist Saweety Boora from the World Championships, and bronze medallist Parveen from the 2022 Olympics, took home gold, while Minakshi had to settle for silver.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is India. In a historic first for Indian boxing, India will host the men’s World Championship for the first time in 2021 besides next year’s women’s World Championship. AIBA made the announcement at the end of its two-day executive committee meeting in Moscow.
