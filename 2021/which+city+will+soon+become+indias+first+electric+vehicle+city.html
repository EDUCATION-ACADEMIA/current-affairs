Which city will soon become India’s first Electric vehicle city?

(A) ** Kevadia

(B) Kanpur

(C) Patna

(D) Lucknow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ETO Motors deploys 50 3W EVs in Kevadia ; India’s first Electric Vehicle City.

Concept note-2: -Kevadia in Gujarat will soon transform into the country’s first electric-vehicle-only city. This was recently announced by Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Concept note-3: -Bengaluru topped the demand across the country for electric cars, followed by Delhi and Mumbai.

Concept note-4: -Gujarat’s Kevadia Could Soon Become India’s First Electric-Vehicle-Only City.

Concept note-5: -The top 3 states with biggest EV subsidies are Delhi, Haryana and Maharashtra. India may have over 50 lakh electric vehicles on road by 2030.
