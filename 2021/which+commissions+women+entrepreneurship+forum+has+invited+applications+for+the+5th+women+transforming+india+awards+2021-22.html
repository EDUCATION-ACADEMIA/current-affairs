Which Commission’s Women Entrepreneurship Forum has invited applications for the 5th Women Transforming India Awards 2021-22?

(A) ** NITI Aayog

(B) Cultural Commission

(C) Planning Commission

(D) Education Commission

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NITI Aayog’s Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP) is organizing the fifth edition of the Women Transforming India Awards (WTI) on 21 March 2022.

Concept note-2: -NITI Aayog’s Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP) organized the fifth edition of the Women Transforming India Awards (WTI). The WTI Awards 2021 were conferred upon 75 women achievers to celebrate their contribution towards a ‘Sashakt Aur Samarth Bharat’.

Concept note-3: -And so, this year Women Entrepreneurship Platform in the fifth edition of the Women Transforming India (WTI) Awards is saluting 75 strong women of our country who are building ‘Sashakt aur Samarth Bharat’ – via self- sufficient businesses and/or overcoming challenges through unique business solutions.

Concept note-4: -75 Winners Felicitated as Part of Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav. NITI Aayog organized the fifth edition of the Women Transforming India (WTI) Awards on 21 March 2022.

Concept note-5: -Aparna Hegde Wins NITI Aayog’s Women Transforming India Award. Dr. Aparna Hegde was recognised by the Women Entrepreneurship Platform and NITI Aayog for her exemplary work in maternal and child health in the 5th edition of the Women Transforming India Awards, 2021.
