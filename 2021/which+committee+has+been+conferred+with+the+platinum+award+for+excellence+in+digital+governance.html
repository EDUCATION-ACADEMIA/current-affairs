Which Committee has been conferred with the Platinum award for Excellence in Digital Governance?

(A) ** SC eCommittee

(B) Abhijit Sen Committee

(C) Ajit Kumar Committee

(D) Basel Committee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -e-Committee has been awarded the Platinum Award for Excellence in Digital e-Governance | Official Website of e-Committee, Supreme Court of India | India.

Concept note-2: -CBSE has won gold at Digital India Awards 2022. The award was conferred by President of India, Droupadi Murmu. Central Board of Secondary Education has won Digital India Awards 2022- Gold.

Concept note-3: -The composition of the E-COmmittee will be as under: (i) Chairman: Dr. Justice G.C Bharuka, former Judge of Kamataka High Court. (ii) The Committee will also comprise of three Members plus the supporting staff.

Concept note-4: -The Bihar government has won the Digital India Awards, instituted by the central govt, for innovative steps in e-governance.
