Which company has collaborated with NSIC for the digital transformation of MSMEs?

(A) ** Airtel

(B) Jio

(C) Vi

(D) BSNL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NSIC and Airtel join forces to accelerate Digital Transformation of Indian MSMEs.

Concept note-2: -Digitalization for MSMEs The process of digitalization makes use of digital technologies to transform a business model and create value-producing opportunities for growth and progress. Amidst a digital revolution, India is headed towards being an Internet-first nation.

Concept note-3: -While Ministry of MSME caters to numerous businesses needs of all small-scale enterprises, NSIC is majorly focused on commercial growth of micro and small enterprises in the country.

Concept note-4: -NSIC CIN No.U74140DL1955GOI002481. National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC), is an ISO 9001:2015 certified Government of India Enterprise under Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME).
