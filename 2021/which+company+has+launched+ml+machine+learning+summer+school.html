Which company has launched ML (Machine Learning) Summer School?

(A) Google

(B) Walmart

(C) ** Amazon

(D) Facebook

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2021, Amazon launched a summer school in India that gave university students the opportunity to learn from leaders in the machine learning (ML) industry. Over three days, students learned the fundamentals of ML and how to apply those concepts to real-world situations.

Concept note-2: -Eligibility and Selection Process The top 3000 students will be ranked based on their performance in the entrance teaching.

Concept note-3: -The program is free, and there is no nee to pay any fee to attend the program.

Concept note-4: -What is machine learning? Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence (AI) and computer science which focuses on the use of data and algorithms to imitate the way that humans learn, gradually improving its accuracy.
