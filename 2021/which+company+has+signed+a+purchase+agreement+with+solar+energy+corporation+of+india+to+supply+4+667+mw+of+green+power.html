Which company has signed a purchase agreement with Solar Energy Corporation of India to supply 4, 667 MW of green power?

(A) Reliance Power

(B) Tata Power SED

(C) ** Adani Green Energy

(D) Rockwell Collins

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As the world’s largest solar power developer, Adani Green Energy will supply 4, 667 MW.

Concept note-2: -Adani Green Energy to Supply 4, 667 MW AGEL has signed PPAs for close to 6000 MW of the 8, 000 MW awarded. The balance 2000 MW PPAs to be closed in the next two to three months.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI : ReNew Power on Thursday announced that it has signed five solar PPAs with Solar Energy Corporation of India (SECI) and Punjab State Power Corporation Limited (PSPCL), as well as multiple long-term purchase agreements with corporate buyers together totalling around 2 GW.

Concept note-4: -Adani Green Energy Ltd (AGEL), the renewable energy arm of diversified Adani Group, has signed an agreement with the Solar Energy Corporation of India (SECI) to supply 4, 667 MW of green power.

Concept note-5: -Adani Green Energy on Tuesday announced that it received the Letter of Award (LoA) to set-up 150 MW solar power project from Punjab State Power Corporation.
