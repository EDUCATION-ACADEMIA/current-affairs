[SOURCES]

Which countries were invited by the Council of European Union to participate in the bloc’s PESCO defense initiative?

(A) Canada

(B) USA

(C) Norway

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Permanent Structured Cooperation (PESCO) is the part of the European Union’s (EU) security and defence policy (CSDP) in which 25 of the 27 national armed forces pursue structural integration (the exceptions being Denmark and Malta).

Concept note-2: -Ten European countries with the military capabilities and political will to play a role on the international stage have joined this initiative (Belgium, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, the Netherlands, Portugal, Spain and the United Kingdom).

Concept note-3: -The European Union recently approved the requests of Norway, Canada and United States to participate in Permanent Structured Cooperation (PESCO) defence initiative.
