Which country declared all transactions involving cryptocurrencies illegal?

(A) India

(B) ** China

(C) Japan

(D) Netherlands

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -China’s central bank has announced that all transactions of crypto-currencies are illegal, effectively banning digital tokens such as Bitcoin.

Concept note-2: -In September 2021, China declared all cryptocurrency illegal.

Concept note-3: -On 22 April 2022 parliament of the Central African Republic voted for the cryptocurrency law which was promulgated on 27 April officially making Bitcoin a legal tender in the country.

Concept note-4: -When it banned crypto last year, China did so in different phases. First the country prohibited financial institutions from engaging in any crypto transactions in May. Then it banned all domestic crypto mining in June, and finally outlawed cryptocurrencies outright in September.

Concept note-5: -The European Union has placed a blanket ban on all crypto services to Russian entities as part of its eighth round of sanctions against the country.
