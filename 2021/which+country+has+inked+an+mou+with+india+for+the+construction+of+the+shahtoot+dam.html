Which country has inked an MoU with India for the construction of the Shahtoot Dam?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) ** Afghanistan

(C) Bhutan

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One of the most recent trade relations between India and Afghanistan is the inking of the pact for the Shahtoot Dam, also known as the Lalandar Dam.

Concept note-2: -Signing ceremony of MoU for the construction of the Lalandar “Shatoot” Dam in Afghanistan.

Concept note-3: -Shahtoot Dam in Afghanistan, on Maidan river a tributary of the Kabul River. The initiative, costing nearly $300 million, is part of India and Afghanistan’s New Development Partnership.

Concept note-4: -Salma Dam is a landmark infrastructure project undertaken by Government of India on River Harirud at Chist-e-Sharif, in Herat province, Afghanistan.
