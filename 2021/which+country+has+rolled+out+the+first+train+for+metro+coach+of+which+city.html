Which country has rolled out the first train for Metro Coach of which city?

(A) ** Pune

(B) Jaipur

(C) Nashik

(D) Bhopal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Titagarh Wagons Ltd, its plant in Italy has rolled out first train for Pune Metro coach.

Concept note-2: -The first aluminium-body metro train in the country, to be deployed by Pune Metro, was flagged off at an event held at Kolkata. The first aluminium-body metro train in the country, to be deployed by Pune Metro, was flagged off at an event held at Uttarpara, Kolkata on Saturday.

Concept note-3: -After bagging the Pune Metro order, Titagarh Wagons upgraded its manufacturing facility in Uttarpara near Kolkata to make modern metro coaches incorporating the latest features, highest safety standards and comfort running at 90 kmph.

Concept note-4: -ICF is said to be the first railway production facility to manufacture coaches for a state-run metro corporation.
