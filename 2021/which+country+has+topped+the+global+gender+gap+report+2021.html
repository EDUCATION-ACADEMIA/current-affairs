Which country has topped the Global Gender Gap Report 2021?

(A) Finland

(B) ** Iceland

(C) Sweden

(D) Norway

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Iceland has retained its position as the world’s most gender-equal country, among 146 nations on the index. Finland, Norway, New Zealand and Sweden are the top five countries on the list respectively. Afghanistan is the worst-performing country in the report.

Concept note-2: -Global Gender Gap Index 2021 Rankings. Which is the most gender-equal country? Iceland, for the 12th time, has topped the index.

Concept note-3: -Iceland, Finland, and Norway bagged the top three positions with a score of 90.8 percent, 86 percent, and 84.5 percent respectively. The COVID-19 pandemic has had a lasting impact on gender equality.

Concept note-4: -Gender Inequality Index: India has been ranked 122 on the Gender Inequality Index.
