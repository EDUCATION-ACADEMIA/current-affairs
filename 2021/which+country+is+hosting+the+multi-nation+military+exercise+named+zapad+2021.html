Which country is hosting the multi-nation military exercise named ‘ZAPAD 2021’?

(A) Singapore

(B) ** Russia

(C) France

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The multinational military exercise, ZAPAD 2021 is scheduled for September 2021 and will be held in Russia. It is one of the theatre-level exercises of the Russian armed forces and will focus primarily on operations against terrorists.

Concept note-2: -Zapad 2021 (Russian: -2021) was a joint strategic exercise between the armed forces of the Russian Federation and Belarus, which took place from 10 to 15 September 2021.

Concept note-3: -A 200 personnel contingent of the Indian Army is participating in Zapad 2021, a multi-nation military exercise in Russia where China and Pakistan are taking part as observers. Zapad 2021 is one of the theatre-level exercises of the Russian armed forces with the focus primarily on operations against terrorists.

Concept note-4: -The 16th ‘Yudh Abhyas, ’ a joint military exercise between India and the United States, was held from February 8th to February 21st, 2021.

Concept note-5: -Indian Army Contingent Participates in Exercise Vostok- 2022 at Sergeyevsky, East Military District Russia. A multilateral strategic and command Exercise Vostok - 2022 has commenced today at the training grounds of the Eastern Military District of Russia from 01 to 07 September 2022.
