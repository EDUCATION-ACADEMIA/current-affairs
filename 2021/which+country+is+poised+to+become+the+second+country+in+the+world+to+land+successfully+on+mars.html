Which country is poised to become the second country in the world to land successfully on Mars?

(A) UAE

(B) India

(C) Japan

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -China landed the spacecraft carrying the rover on Mars on May 15 in a technically challenging feat that is more difficult than a moon landing. Zhurong, named after the Chinese god of fire, is the second rover to land on another planet, after the U.S rover Perseverance in February.

Concept note-2: -In 1971, the Soviet Union finally reached the Red Planet. Its Mars 2 orbiter, which launched May 19, 1971, arrived on Nov.

Concept note-3: -China becomes only second nation in history to land a rover on Mars.

Concept note-4: -The first successful flyby of Mars was on 14–15 July 1965, by NASA’s Mariner 4. On November 14, 1971, Mariner 9 became the first space probe to orbit another planet when it entered into orbit around Mars.

Concept note-5: -The United States and the Soviet Union are the only two countries to land a spacecraft on Mars.
