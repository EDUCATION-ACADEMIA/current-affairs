Which country is the chair for the G20 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors (FMCBG) meeting of 2021?

(A) Germany

(B) India

(C) Russia

(D) ** Italy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 1st Finance Ministers and Central Banks Governors Meeting is one of the ministerial meetings organized as part of the G20 Leaders’ Summit 2021, which will be hosted by Italy in October 2021.

Concept note-2: -This is the 3rd FMCBG meeting of Indonesia’s G20 Presidency in the Finance Track after previous meetings held in February 2022 (Jakarta) and April 2022 (Washington, D.C.).

Concept note-3: -The First G20 Finance and Central Bank Deputies Meeting, under the Indian Presidency, was held in Bengaluru, Karnataka, during December 13-14, 2022, under the co-chairship of Secretary, Economic Affairs, Shri Ajay Seth and Deputy Governor, Reserve Bank of India, Dr. Michael Patra.

Concept note-4: -India hosts the First G20 Finance and Central Bank Deputies Meeting under the G20 India Presidency in Bengaluru, Karnataka.
