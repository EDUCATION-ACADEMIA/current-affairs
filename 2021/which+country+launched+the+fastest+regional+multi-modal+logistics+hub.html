Which country launched the fastest regional multi-modal logistics hub?

(A) UAE

(B) ** Bahrain

(C) Jordan

(D) Congo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In December 2021, Bahrain launched its Global Sea-to-Air Hub, a multimodal logistics hub connecting Bahrain International Airport and Khalifa Bin Salman Port (KBSP), a multipurpose cargo and cruise facility, which is operated by the Bahrain arm of APM Terminals .

Concept note-2: -Bahrain launched the fastest regional multi-modal logistics hub in the region with only a 2 hours turnaround time for all containers - meaning products can be with customers in half the time and at 40% of the cost.

Concept note-3: -Integrated Multi-Modal Logistics Hub (IMLH) at Nangal Chaudhary in Haryana.

Concept note-4: -Reliance Industries Ltd (RIL) has been awarded the project to build India’s first multimodal logistics park (MMLP) in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. A crucial cog in the Centre’s logistics overhaul, the MMLP will be spread over 184 acres and cost Rs 1, 424 crore, the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH) said.
