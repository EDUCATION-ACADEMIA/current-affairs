Which country will provide 6 P-81 patrol Aircraft to India for the use of the Indian Navy?

(A) UK

(B) ** USA

(C) Japan

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In May 2021, the US State Department approved the proposed sale of six P-8I patrol aircraft and related equipment, a deal estimated to cost 2.42 billion dollars. Boeing has delivered the 12th P-8I maritime patrol aircraft to the Indian Navy on Wednesday.

Concept note-2: -US aerospace major Boeing on Thursday said it has delivered the 12th Poseidon-8I maritime patrol aircraft to India. The fleet is being operated by the Indian Navy primarily to carry out surveillance in the Indian Ocean region.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which firm supplied P-8I long range maritime patrol aircraft to Indian Navy? Notes: The Indian Navy received its 11th P-8I long range maritime patrol aircraft from Boeing. The Navy has signed a contract of 12 P-8Is from Boeing in two batches.

Concept note-4: -Indian Navy’s fleet of P-8I has surpassed 29, 000 flight-hours, since their induction in 2013. The Indian Navy has received delivery of twelve P-8I aircraft. Discover more here.

Concept note-5: -Navy gets full complement of P-8I maritime aircraft “Boeing delivered the 12th P-8I maritime patrol aircraft to India on February 23, ” a company statement said. “The P-8I is an integral part of the Indian Navy’s fleet and has surpassed 35, 000 flight hours since it was inducted in 2013.”
