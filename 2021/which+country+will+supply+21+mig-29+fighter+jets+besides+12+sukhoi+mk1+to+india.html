Which country will supply 21 MiG-29 fighter jets besides 12 Sukhoi MK1 to India?

(A) USA

(B) UK

(C) ** Russia

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government of India is set to procure 21 MiG-29 and 12 Sukhoi-30MKI fighter aircraft from Russia. Apart from this, the Centre will also purchase the upgrades of the existing fleets of aircraft from Russian state-run defence export arm Rosoboronexport.

Concept note-2: -Russian Su-30MKI is India’s most popular fighter jet and serves as the backbone of the Indian Air Force (IAF) which operates around 270 of them.

Concept note-3: -New Delhi and Moscow have been in talks with each other about an upgrade for the Su-30 MKI, which India first ordered in 1996. The upgraded aircraft, if the proposal is approved, will be known as the Super Sukhoi.
