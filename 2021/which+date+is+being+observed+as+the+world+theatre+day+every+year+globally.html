Which date is being observed as the World Theatre Day every year globally?

(A) ** 27 March

(B) 25 March

(C) 26 March

(D) 24 March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Since 1962 World Theatre Day has been celebrated by ITI Centres, ITI Cooperating Members, theatre professionals, theatre organizations, theatre universities and theatre lovers all over the world on the 27th of March.

Concept note-2: -World Theatre Day is observed annually on March 27th.

Concept note-3: -Ever since, each year on the 27th March (date of the opening of the 1962 “Theatre of Nations” season in Paris), World Theatre Day has been celebrated in many and varied ways by ITI Centres - of which there are now more than 90 throughout the world.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Theatre and a Culture of Peace. World Theatre Day is celebrated on 27 March every year. The theme for World Theatre Day 2022 is “Theatre and a Culture of Peace.” World Theatre Day was first celebrated by the International Theatre Institute (ITI) in the year 1961, on 27 March.

Concept note-5: -National Cinema Day was celebrated on September 23 to commemorate the successful reopening of movie halls.
