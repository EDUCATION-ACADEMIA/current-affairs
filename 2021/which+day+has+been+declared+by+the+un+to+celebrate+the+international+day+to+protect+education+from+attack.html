Which day has been declared by the UN to celebrate the International Day to Protect Education from Attack?

(A) September 7

(B) September 6

(C) September 8

(D) ** September 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Day to Protect Education from Attack 2023 will be observed on September 9, 2023. It is a day of annual observance that aims to draw attention towards attacks on students, teachers and education institutions and the use of schools for military purposesthat happens during armed conflict.

Concept note-2: -On 9 September, the world observed the first International Day to Protect Education from Attack that was proclaimed by the United Nations General Assembly last May.

Concept note-3: -Every year on January 24, International Education Day is celebrated around the world. The day is observed to remind people of the importance of education. On December 3, 2018, the General Assembly of the United Nations voted to declare January 24 as the International Day of Education.

Concept note-4: -The United Nations General Assembly adopted a resolution on December 3, 2018, which proclaimed January 24 as the International Day of Education. The aim of the UNGA was to recognise and celebrate the role of learning for peace and development.

Concept note-5: -International Day of Education 2023: It is said that Education is a human right, a public good, and a public responsibility. International Day of Education is celebrated on 24 January to raise awareness about the importance, the role of education for peace and development.
