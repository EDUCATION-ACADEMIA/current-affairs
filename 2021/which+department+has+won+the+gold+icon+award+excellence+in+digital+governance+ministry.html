Which department has won the gold icon award, excellence in digital governance ministry?

(A) ** Department of Posts

(B) Department of Civil Aviation

(C) Department of Agriculture

(D) Department of Consumer Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The e-Committee of the Supreme Court of India and Department of Justice was awarded the prestigious National Gold Award for Excellence in Government Process Re-engineering for Digital transformation with a cash prize of Rs.

Concept note-2: -CBSE has won gold at Digital India Awards 2022. The award was conferred by President of India, Droupadi Murmu. Central Board of Secondary Education has won Digital India Awards 2022- Gold.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bihar.

Concept note-4: -Excellence in Digital Governance - STATE/UT The Award acknowledges the State/UT of India that displays exemplary initiative in establishing comprehensive digital presence in sectors like health, labour, finance, social justice and environment leading to the accomplishment of sustainable development goals.

Concept note-5: -The Digital India Awards, instituted by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), recognize and honor innovative digital solutions and exemplary initiatives by various government entities in the realm of digital governance.
