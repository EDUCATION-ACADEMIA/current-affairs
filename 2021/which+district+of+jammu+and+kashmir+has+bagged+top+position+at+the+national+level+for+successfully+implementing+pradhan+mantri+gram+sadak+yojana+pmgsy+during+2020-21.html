Which district of Jammu and Kashmir has bagged top position at the National level for successfully implementing Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during 2020-21?

(A) Reasi

(B) Kathua

(C) ** Udhampur

(D) Mirpur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Udhampur.

Concept note-2: -Himachal Pradesh’s Mandi district has bagged the top position in successful implementation of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY).

Concept note-3: -SRINAGAR: Jammu and Kashmir has secured the third spot in developing its rural road network under the central government’s road connectivity programme Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) after Madhya Pradesh and Rajasthan, according to official figures from the Union rural development ministry.

Concept note-4: -Implementation of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana | Ministry of Rural Development | Government of India.

Concept note-5: -Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY) was formally launched in 18 B.B village in Sri Ganganagar district of Rajasthan on 23 July 2010.
