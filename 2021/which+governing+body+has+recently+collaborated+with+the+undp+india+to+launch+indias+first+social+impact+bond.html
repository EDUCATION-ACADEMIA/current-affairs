Which governing body has recently collaborated with the UNDP India to launch India’s first Social Impact Bond?

(A) BMC

(B) ** PCMC

(C) RMC

(D) CMC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation (PCMC) signed an MoU with UNDP India to co-create India’s first Social Impact Bond (SIB).

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which state’s Municipal Corporation partnered with UNDP India to launch India’s first Social Impact Bond (SIB)? Notes: The Pimpri-Chinchwad Municipal Corporation has signed an MoU with UNDP India to create India’s first Social Impact Bond (SIB).

Concept note-3: -The first social impact bond was issued in 2010 by Social Finance Ltd. 2 So far, social impact bonds have only been issued by the public sector, but in theory, private sector organizations can also issue them.

Concept note-4: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which institution launched the first ‘Impact Bond’ for skilling in India? Notes: The National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) launched the first and the largest ‘Impact Bond’ for skilling in India.

Concept note-5: -Impact Bonds (“IBs”) are innovative financial instruments that pull private investment to finance high-impact social programs. The implementation of impact bonds aids in financing early childhood education, global health, and workforce development, including recidivism and employment.
