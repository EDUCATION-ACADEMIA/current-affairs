Which health insurance re-branded itself as Care Health Insurance?

(A) Reliance

(B) ** Religare

(C) Bajaj

(D) Birla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The rebranding has been aimed to create opportunities to offer the best possible services to its customers.

Concept note-2: -Care Health Insurance (Formerly Known As Religare Health Insurance) Care Health Insurance (Formerly Known As Religare Health Insurance) is a specialized health insurance company in India. It is a direct subsidiary of Religare Enterprises Limited.

Concept note-3: -All aspects vis–vis customer policies such as the policy number, health card, cashless network, customer care details as well as claims and renewal processes, are unchanged, Anuj Gulati, Founding Managing Director & CEO of Care Health Insurance said.

Concept note-4: -REL is listed on National Stock Exchange of India and Bombay Stock Exchange. It is an holding company for the subsidiaries conducting diversified financial services businesses of the group. Dr Rashmi Saluja is the executive chairperson of the company. Religare Enterprises Limited.

Concept note-5: -Care Health Insurance Limited is a 15 years 9 months old Public Company incorporated on 02 Apr 2007. Its registered office is in South Delhi, Delhi, india. The Company’s status is Active, and it has filed its Annual Returns and Financial Statements up to 31 Mar 2021 (FY 2020-2021).
