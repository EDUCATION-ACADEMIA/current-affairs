Which IIT stands among the top 22 per cent in the 2022 QS Graduate Employability Rankings?

(A) IIT Roorkee

(B) ** IIT Bombay

(C) IIT Bangalore

(D) IIT Bhopal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -While four of IIT Delhi’s academic programmes have been named among the top 100, the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay has topped the ranking on a national level. The subject-wise rankings of institutes throughout the world are a compilation of several lists.

Concept note-2: -Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) secured the first position in India and 40th rank this year in the QS Asia University Rankings 2023. The results were released on November 8, 2022, by QS, a British company. IIT Bombay secured an overall score of 68.7 out of 100.

Concept note-3: -Rankings & ratings Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IITB) is one of the top public universities in Mumbai, India. It is ranked #=172 in QS World University Rankings 2023.
