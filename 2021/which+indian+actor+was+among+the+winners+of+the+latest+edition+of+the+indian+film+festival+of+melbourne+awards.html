Which Indian Actor was among the winners of the latest edition of the Indian Film Festival of Melbourne Awards?

(A) Varun Dhawan

(B) Vickey Kaushal

(C) ** Manoj Bajpayee

(D) Nana Patekar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ranveer Singh and Shefali Shah won the Best Actor and Best Actress Award, respectively, at the event.

Concept note-2: -Indian Film Festival of Melbourne (IFFM) Awards 2022: Ranveer Singh and Shefali Shah took home the major acting honours. The 13th edition of the IFFM began on Friday, August 12 and will conclude on August 30. The in-person event ends on August 20, beyond which the festival continues virtually for another ten days.

Concept note-3: -Missy Higgins wins the $60, 000 Melbourne Prize for Music 2022. Annie Hui-Hsin Hsieh wins the $20, 000 Beleura Emerging Composers Award 2022. Jess Hitchcock wins the $10, 000 Professional Development Award 2022 (+$1, 000 Qantas voucher). Liza Lim wins the $2, 000 Civic Choice Award 2022.
