Which Indian Boxer has been appointed as the Chairperson of AIBA’s champions and veterans committee?

(A) ** Mary Kom

(B) Vikas Krishnan

(C) Vijender Singh

(D) Amit Panghal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Mary Kom . The 37-year-old was voted to the post by AIBA’s board of directors on March 03, 2021. The committee was created in December 2020 by the world body as part of its reforms.

Concept note-2: -About Mary Kom She has created many histories for India in field of Boxing. She is the only female boxer who have become the “World Boxing Champion” for a record of six times.

Concept note-3: -Six-time world champion M C Mary Kom has been picked as the chairperson of the International Boxing Association’s (AIBA) champions and veterans committee, a panel created last year by the world body as part of its reforms.

Concept note-4: -Six-time world champion M C Mary Kom has been picked as the chairperson of the International Boxing Association’s (AIBA) champions and veterans committee. The panel was created in 2020 by the world body as part of its reforms.

Concept note-5: -MC Mary Kom is the first Indian woman to win a boxing gold at the Asian Games. Mary Kom has also managed to bag five gold medals in the Asian women’s boxing championships. The last one in 2017 in Ho Chi Minh City.
