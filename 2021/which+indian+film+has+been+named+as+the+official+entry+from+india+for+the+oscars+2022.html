Which Indian film has been named as the official entry from India for the Oscars 2022?

(A) Mandela

(B) Nayattu

(C) ** Koozhangal

(D) Sardar Udham

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s 2022 Oscar-entry Chhello Show releases in the theatres today. A still from Chhello Show. Chhello Show (Last Film Show), India’s official entry for the 2023 Academy Awards, has arrived in the theatres today. The film is an authentic and heartfelt tribute to the magic and power of cinema.

Concept note-2: -Directed by Pan Nalin and produced by Siddharth Roy Kapur and Dheer Momaya, ‘Chhello Show’ (titled ‘Last Film Show’ in English) is India’s official entry to the Oscars. It is a coming-of-age story of a young boy’s love affair with cinema in a Saurashtra village.

Concept note-3: -Three Indian movies have made it to various lists in the Oscar nominations announced Tuesday evening - All That Breathes in the Documentary Feature Film category, The Elephant Whisperers in the Best Documentary Short Film category, and ‘Naatu Naatu’ from RRR in the Original Song category.

Concept note-4: -Directed by Pan Nalin and produced by Siddharth Roy Kapur and Dheer Momaya, “Chhello Show” (titled “Last Film Show” in English) is India’s official entry to the Oscars.

Concept note-5: -New Delhi: The nominations for the 95th Academy Awards have been announced and, no surprises, RRR has been nominated for an Oscar (one of three nods for Indian cinema) - Best Original Song for Naatu Naatu which also won the Golden Globe in the same category.
