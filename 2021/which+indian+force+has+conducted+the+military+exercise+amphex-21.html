Which Indian Force has conducted the Military Exercise AMPHEX-21?

(A) Air Force

(B) ** Navy

(C) Army

(D) All of above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is the Indian Navy, Army & Air Force. The AMPHEX-21 was a tri-service exercise of the Indian Navy, Army & Air Force.

Concept note-2: -VINBAX 2022 -Vietnam-India Bilateral Army Exercise The third edition of Ex VINBAX 2022 was held between 1st August and 20th August, 2022 in Chandimandir.

Concept note-3: -India and Japan conducted a joint military exercise titled ‘Dharma Guardian 2022’ at Maratha Light Infantry Regimental Centre in Belgaum, Karnataka. It was held from 27th February to 10th March 2022. It covers platoon-level joint training on operations in the jungle and semi-urban and urban terrain.

Concept note-4: -The exercise AMPHEX – 21 was aimed at validating India’s capabilities to safeguard the territorial integrity of its island territories, enhance operational synergy and joint warfighting capabilities amongst the three Services.
