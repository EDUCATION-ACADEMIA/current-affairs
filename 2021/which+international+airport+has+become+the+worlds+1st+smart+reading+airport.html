Which International Airport has become the Worlds 1st Smart reading Airport?

(A) Kempegowda

(B) Bangalore

(C) ** Dubai

(D) Dumdum

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dubai International becomes world’s first smart reading airport.

Concept note-2: -Emirates’ hub is Dubai International Airport, so while it may be the third largest airport in the world, it is home to the world’s largest fleet of the world’s largest passenger aircraft.

Concept note-3: -Our Mission Achieving leadership in creating innovative opportunities to maintain security, safety and enhance the infrastructure of the civil aviation sector by attracting investment for the aviation industry in the Emirate of Dubai.

Concept note-4: -Operating under the airport code (DWC) Dubai World Central, this is Dubai’s second airport. It is being expanded from one to five runways. Al Maktoum International Airport is an area located within Dubai South, Dubai.

Concept note-5: -Dubai is slightly different. DUB is already taken by Dublin in Ireland. DBA is Dalbandin in Pakistan. The letter X is commonly used in airport codes as a filler letter and so, DXB was chosen.
