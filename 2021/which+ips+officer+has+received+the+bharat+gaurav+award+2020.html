Which IPS officer has received the ‘Bharat Gaurav Award 2020’?

(A) Kailash Mohanty

(B) ** Karuna Sagar

(C) Jeevan Kumar

(D) Souhard Goswami

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Karuna Sagar, a 1991 batch IPS officer of the TN cadre, has bagged the prestigious Bharat Gaurav Award for the year 2020 under the public service category.

Concept note-2: -Bharat Gaurav 2022 awardee Mrs Nivedita Saha who is artist based in Dubai & working with a vision & mission of women empowerment.

Concept note-3: -Talking about the accolade in an exclusive conversation with ETimes, Paramita Bhattacharyya shared, “The award ‘Bharat Gaurav Samman’ is given to someone who has worked tirelessly to make a significant contribution to their field of work, benefited society, and worked for the nation throughout their service.”
