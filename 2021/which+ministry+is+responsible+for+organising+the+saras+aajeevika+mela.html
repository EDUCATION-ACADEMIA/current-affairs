Which ministry is responsible for organising the Saras Aajeevika Mela?

(A) Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment

(B) Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

(C) Ministry of Women and Child Development

(D) ** Ministry of Rural Development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Speaking after inaugurating the “SARAS AAJEEVIKA MELA, 2022” at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi, Shri Singh said, Ministry of Rural Development has received over 60, 000 applications from 3 states for start-up ventures in niche products and crafts areas.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains Q. The Saras Aajeevika Mela is organised at Noida by which Ministry? Notes: In order to bring rural women SHGs under a single platform, the Union Ministry of Rural Development has organised Saras Aajeevika Mela at Noida Haat.

Concept note-3: -Organizer: It is an initiative by the Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM) under the Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) organized by the Council for Advancement of People’s Action and Rural Technology (CAPART).

Concept note-4: -Union Minister for Rural Development and Panchayati Raj Shri Giriraj inaugurated the “SARAS AAJEEVIKA MELA, 2022” at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi.

Concept note-5: -Rural Development Minister Giriraj Singh inaugurates SARAS AAJEEVIKA MELA, 2022 at India International Trade Fair in New Delhi.
