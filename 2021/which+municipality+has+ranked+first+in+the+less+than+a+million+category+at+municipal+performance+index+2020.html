Which municipality has ranked first in the less than a million category at Municipal Performance Index 2020?

(A) Dehradun

(B) Surat

(C) ** Delhi

(D) Indore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the Million+ category, Indore has emerged as the highest ranked municipality, followed by Surat and Bhopal. In the Less than Million category, New Delhi Municipal Council has emerged as the leader, followed by Tirupati and Gandhinagar.

Concept note-2: -Indore has emerged as the highest ranked municipality, followed by Surat and Bhopal.

Concept note-3: -Indore bags cleanest city award for sixth year, M.P ranks first among States.

Concept note-4: -As per the index released by Union Housing and Urban Affairs Minister Hardeep Singh Puri on Thursday, March 4, Bengaluru, which is the centre of the country’s high-tech industry, bagged the top spot on the list of Indian cities with a population of over a million.
