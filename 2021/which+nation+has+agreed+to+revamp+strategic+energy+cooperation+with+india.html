Which nation has agreed to revamp strategic energy cooperation with India?

(A) France

(B) Germany

(C) ** US

(D) UK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India, U.S agree to revamp strategic partnership to focus on clean energy.

Concept note-2: -As climate and clean energy leaders, the United States and India share a common vision to deploy clean energy at scale during this critical decade to reduce emissions and achieve climate change mitigation goals, taking into account different national circumstances.

Concept note-3: -In Washington DC, Hon’ble Minister will co-chair the Ministerial dialogue of US-India Strategic Clean Energy Partnership (USISCEP), to be held on 7th October 2022with HE Ms. Jennifer Granholm, Secretary of Energy, USA.

Concept note-4: -1 | U.S.-India Partnership to Advance Clean Energy (PACE) The United States is committed to enhancing energy cooperation with India in light of the central role that energy plays in a wide range of priority issues – from climate change and energy security to energy access, economic growth, trade and investment.
