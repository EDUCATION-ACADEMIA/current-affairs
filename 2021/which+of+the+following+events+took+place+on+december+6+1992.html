Which of the following events took place on December 6, 1992?

(A) ** Babri Masjid demolition

(B) Dissolution of Soviet Union

(C) Mumbai Blasts

(D) Persian Gulf War

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The demolition of the Babri Masjid was illegally carried out on 6 December 1992 by a large group of activists of the Vishva Hindu Parishad and allied organisations.

Concept note-2: -“Babri Masjid was not just a mosque, it had history engraved on it. And on December 6, 1992, its demolition by Hindu Kar Sevaks raised a question about India’s future as a secular nation.

Concept note-3: -On this day (6 Dec), Soviet leader Joseph Stalin and Indian cricketers Ravindra Jadeja & Jasprit Bumrah were born, founder of Indian constitution Dr. B.R. Ambedkar passed away, and Babri Masjid was demolished.

Concept note-4: -1992 – The Babri Masjid in Ayodhya, India, is demolished, leading to widespread riots causing the death of over 1, 500 people.

Concept note-5: -On December 6, 1992, the Babri Masjid was demolished by a mob of ‘kar sevaks’ in culmination of BJP’s Rath Yatra. Several BJP leaders were accused in the demolition case which led to violent Hindu-Muslim clashes across the country.
