Which of the following films have jointly won the Best Film Award at the 6th BRICS Film Festival?

(A) A Little Red Flower From China

(B) The Silence Of The Rain

(C) ** Barakat and The Sun above Me Never Sets

(D) On Wheels and Asuran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Panaji, 28 November, 2021 South African Film Barakat by Director Amy Jephta and Russian Film The Sun above Me Never Sets by Director Lyubov Borisova has shared the Best film Award in the Sixth edition BRICS Film Festival.

Concept note-2: -Dhanush wins Best Actor award for Asuran at 6th BRICS Film Festival.

Concept note-3: -KOLKATA: ‘Aparajito’, the Anik Dutta film that is inspired by the story behind the making of Satyajit Ray’s ‘Pather Panchali’, has won the special jury award at the 2022 BRICS Film Festival.
