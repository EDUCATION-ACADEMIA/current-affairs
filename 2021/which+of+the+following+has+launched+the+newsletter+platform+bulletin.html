Which of the following has launched the newsletter platform “Bulletin”?

(A) Twitter

(B) Microsoft

(C) Amazon

(D) ** Facebook

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Facebook Launches newsletter platform “Bulletin”

Concept note-2: -Bulletin is an online newsletter platform launched by Facebook on July 6, 2021, that allows notable writers to make announcements directly to their subscribers.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. With an aim to promote independent writers, Facebook has announced a set of publishing and subscription tools – Bulletin – to support these creators in the US. Bulletin will include support focused on content creation, monetisation and audience growth.

Concept note-4: -Here’s how we define “Bulletin” and “Newsletter". The former is internal, to keep members informed. The latter is external, intended to reach non-members, supporters, sponsors, your local community, event attendees, news media and so on.

Concept note-5: -A weekly newsletter bringing you the stories of immigrant communities in metro Atlanta. A View from the Center. Antonio Mora.
