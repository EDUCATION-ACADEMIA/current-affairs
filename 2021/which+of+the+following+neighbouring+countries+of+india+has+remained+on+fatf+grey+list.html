Which of the following neighbouring countries of India has remained on FATF ‘Grey List’?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) Nepal

(C) Bhutan

(D) ** Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pakistan has been on the FATF grey list for more than four years.

Concept note-2: -The following countries had their progress reviewed by the FATF since June 2022: Albania, Barbados, Burkina Faso, Cambodia, Cayman Islands, Haiti, Jamaica, Jordan, Mali, Morocco, Myanmar, Nicaragua, Pakistan, Panama, Philippines, Senegal, South Sudan, Türkiye, UAE, and Uganda.

Concept note-3: -Pakistan removed from FATF grey list on terror financing - Times of India.
