Which of the following Shatabdi Express has become the first IMS certified train?

(A) Madhya Pradesh Shatabdi Express

(B) Bihar Shatabdi Express

(C) Vivek Shatabdi Express

(D) ** Chennai-Mysore-Chennai Shatabdi Express

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Mysore- Chennai Shatabdi Express. Mysore- Chennai Shatabdi Express has become the first Integrated Management Systems (IMS) certified train of Southern Railway . It has also become the first Shatabdi of Indian Railways and second mail/express train on Indian Railways.

Concept note-2: -Chennai – Mysore- Chennai Shatabdi Express becomes the first IMS certified train.

Concept note-3: -The first Shatabdi Express train was announced on 10 July 1988 by then Railways Minister Madhavrao Scindia to commemorate the birth centenary of Jawaharlal Nehru, the first Prime Minister of India and plyed between New Delhi and Jhansi Junction.

Concept note-4: -Bhopal Express is India’s first ISO 9000-2001 certified train.

Concept note-5: -Integrated management systems (IMS) certification is a holistic approach that combines multiple aspects of an organisation’s performance in order to meet the requirements of several management system standards.
