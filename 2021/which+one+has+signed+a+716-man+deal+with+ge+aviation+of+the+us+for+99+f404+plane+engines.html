Which one has signed a $716-man deal with GE Aviation of the U.S. for 99 F404 plane engines?

(A) ** HAL

(B) IAF

(C) ISRO

(D) DRDO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian aerospace company, Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL ), has signed a $716 million contract for 99 General Electric F404 engines. “This is largest ever deal and the purchase order placed by HAL for LCA, ” said R.

Concept note-2: -One of the major differences between the F404 and the F414 is the fan section. The F414 fan is larger than that of the F404, but smaller than the F412 fan. The larger fan increases the engine airflow by 16% and is 5 inches (13 cm) longer.

Concept note-3: -The General Electric F404 and F412 are a family of afterburning turbofan engines in the 10, 500–19, 000 lbf (47–85 kN) class (static thrust).
