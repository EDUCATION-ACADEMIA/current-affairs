Which one is cooperating with NITI Aayog to ensure the successful implementation of the Van Dhan Yojana?

(A) FCI

(B) HAFED

(C) ** TRIFED

(D) NABARD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tribal Cooperative Marketing Development Federation of India (TRIFED) in partnerhsip with NITI Aayog will implement the Van Dhan Yojana in the districts that have been identified as aspirational districts by NITI Aayog.

Concept note-2: -Who launched the Van Dhan scheme? The Ministry of Tribal Affairs and TRIFED launched the Van Dhan scheme in 2018.

Concept note-3: -The Van Dhan Scheme is an initiative of the Ministry of Tribal Affairs and TRIFED. It was launched on 14th April, 2018 and seeks to improve tribal incomes through value addition of tribal products.

Concept note-4: -Pradhan Mantri Van Dhan Yojna is an initiative targeting livelihood generation for tribals by harnessing the wealth of forest i.e. Van Dhan.
