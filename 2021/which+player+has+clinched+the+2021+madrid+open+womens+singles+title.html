Which player has clinched the 2021 Madrid Open women’s singles title?

(A) ** Aryna Sabalenka

(B) Simona Halep

(C) Mari Osaka

(D) Ashleigh Barty

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aryna Sabalenka defeated Ashleigh Barty in the final, 6–0, 3–6, 6–4, to win the women’s singles tennis title at the 2021 Madrid Open.

Concept note-2: -Alexander Zverev defeated Matteo Berrettini in the final, 6–7, 6–4, 6–3 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2021 Madrid Open. It was Zverev’s fourth Masters 1000 title, his first since Madrid 2018, and his 15th career ATP Tour singles title overall.

Concept note-3: -Carlos Alcaraz defeated the defending champion Alexander Zverev in the final, 6–3, 6–1 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2022 Madrid Open.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Carlos Alcaraz. Carlos Alcaraz clinched the Madrid Open title, beating defending champion Alexander Zverev on 8 May 2022. He defeated him by 6-3, 6-1 in 62 minutes in the men’s singles final.
