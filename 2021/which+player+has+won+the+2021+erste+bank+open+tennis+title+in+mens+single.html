Which player has won the 2021 Erste Bank Open Tennis title in men’s single?

(A) ** Alexander Zverev

(B) Daniil Medvedev

(C) Juan Sebastian Cabal

(D) Novak Djokovic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It was held at the Wiener Stadthalle in Vienna, Austria, from 25 until 31 October 2021. Second-seeded Alexander Zverev won the singles title.

Concept note-2: -Vienna: The Centre Of Austrian Life, Austrian Tennis The Erste Bank Open, played each year in late October, is regularly frequented by the world’s Top 10 players.

Concept note-3: -The 2022 Erste Bank Open was a men’s tennis tournament to be played on indoor hard courts. It was the 48th edition of the event, and part of the ATP Tour 500 Series of the 2022 ATP Tour.

Concept note-4: -At the Erste Bank Open 2022 in the Wiener Stadthalle, absolute top people are once again at the start: The field is led by Daniil Medvedev and Stefanos Tsitsipas, followed by the top ten colleagues Andrey Rublev and Taylor Fritz, followed by Hubert Hurkacz, Jannik Sinner, Cameron Norrie and Matteo Berrettini.
