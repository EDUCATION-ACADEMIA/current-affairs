Which Small Finance Bank awarded with ‘Great place to work’ certification?

(A) Fincare Small Finance Bank

(B) Equitas Small Finance Bank

(C) ** ESAF Small Finance Bank

(D) Suryoday Small Finance Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Equitas Small Finance Bank Ltd is a Great Place to Work -Certified™ organization. Great Place to Work® Certification is recognized world over by employees and employers alike and is considered the ‘Gold Standard’ in identifying and recognizing Great Workplace Cultures.

Concept note-2: -Ujjivan Small Finance Bank Limited is recognized among India’s Best Companies to Work For 2021 - Rank 11.
