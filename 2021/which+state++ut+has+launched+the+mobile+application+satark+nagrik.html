Which state / UT has launched the mobile application Satark Nagrik?

(A) ** Jammu & Kashmir

(B) Puducherry

(C) Daman and Diu

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -JAMMU, Jan 7: Lieutenant Governor, Manoj Sinha today launched Mobile Application ‘Satark Nagrik’ and Departmental Vigilance Officers Portal of J&K Anti Corruption Bureau at the Civil Secretariat.

Concept note-2: -Odisha Civil Services Application Link Active on 17th January 2023.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which Indian state/UT launched a mobile application named ‘Satark Nagrik’? Notes: The Lieutenant General of the UT of Jammu and Kashmir, Manoj Sinha launched a mobile application named ‘Satark Nagrik’.

Concept note-4: -Satark Nagrik Sangathan (SNS) is a citizens’ group working to promote transparency and accountability in government functioning and to encourage active participation of citizens in governance in India. SNS is an independent non-governmental organization and is not affiliated to, or supported by, any political party.
