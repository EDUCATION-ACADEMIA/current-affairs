Which state government has launched the e-Nirman portal & mobile app for unorganized sector work?

(A) ** Gujarat

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Bihar

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Gujarat government has launched a web portal and a mobile application to facilitate online registration of workers from the unorganized sector to give them smart cards for availing of various welfare schemes. The ‘e-Nirman’ portal and its mobile App were launched by CM Vijay Rupani.

Concept note-2: -The government of Odisha has launched “Nirman Kusuma” programme for providing financial assistance to the children of construction workers for their technical education in the state. It was launched by CM of Odisha Shri Naveen Patnaik in Bhubaneswar.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of Labour & Employment has developed eSHRAM portal for creating a National Database of Unorganized Workers (NDUW) for optimum realization of their employability and extend the benefits of the social security schemes to them.

Concept note-4: -Notes: Gujarat Chief Minister Vijay Rupani has launched eNagar mobile application and portal. The eNagar covers 10 modules with 52 services including property tax, professional tax, water & drainage, Complaints and grievance redressal, building permission, fire and emergency services.

Concept note-5: -e-SHRAM Portal:- Ministry of Labour and Employment has developed e-SHRAM portal (www.eshram.gov.in) for creating a National Data Base of Unorganised Workers (NDUW), which will be seeded with Aadhaar.
