[SOURCES]

Which state governments is planning to introduce Model Tenancy Act in the state?

(A) ** Karnataka

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Centre In Talks With West Bengal Government On New Tenancy Act. To make the rent laws seamless between the landlords and tenants, the central government has finally initiated the Model Tenancy Act (MTA) in 2021.

Concept note-2: -Why in News? According to Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), Model Tenancy Act has been rectified by only four states, i.e., Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Uttar Pradesh, and Assam.

Concept note-3: -Assam has become the first state to adopt the Model Tenancy Act. With this Assam Urban Areas Rent Control Act, 1972 gets repealed.

Concept note-4: -The Bengal Tenancy Act was passed by Warren Hastings in 1822 by which the cultivators had hereditary right of possession of the land as long as they paid the rent.

Concept note-5: -Karnataka Rent Control Act 1961 or as many call it Model Tenancy Act 2021 Karnataka, was passed to ensure the regulation of rent and eviction of tenants and other connected matters..
