Which state govt hiked the financial assistance to Rs.51000 for the beneficiaries of the Ashirwad Scheme?

(A) Assam

(B) Bihar

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Punjab state government has launched the scheme named as Punjab Ashirwad 2022 through this scheme punjab government will provide financial help for female marriages. Through this program a girl who comes from a lower income family in the punjab state will receive economic help.

Concept note-2: -The Shagun scheme is an initiative by the Punjab Government to extend financial support for a daughter’s marriage who belongs to a family earning a lower income.

Concept note-3: -Highlights of Aashirwad Scheme Punjab To avail the benefit, the annual income of the family from all sources should not exceed Rs. 32, 790. The parents/guardians of the girl should be domiciled in Punjab and the financial assistance under this scheme is limited to two girls only from one family.

Concept note-4: -Documents required for Shagun Scheme in Punjab: Age proof. Caste certificate. BPL card. Verified form by Gazette officer or Sarpanch.
