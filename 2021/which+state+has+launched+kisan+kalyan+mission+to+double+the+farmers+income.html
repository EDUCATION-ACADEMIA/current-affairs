Which state has launched Kisan Kalyan Mission to double the farmers’ income?

(A) Bihar

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Kerala

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -The Kisan Kalyan Mission is aimed at doubling the income of the farmers in Uttar Pradesh by 2022.

Concept note-3: -The Kisan Kalyan Abhiyan was launched in 2018 by the Ministry of Agriculture and farmers welfare.
