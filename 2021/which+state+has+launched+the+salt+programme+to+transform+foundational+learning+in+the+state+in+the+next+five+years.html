[SOURCES]

Which state has launched the SALT programme to transform foundational learning in the state in the next five years?

(A) Karnataka

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) ** Andhra Pradesh

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SALT project aims to improve the quality of learning for more than 50 lakh students in the state of Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Rajasekhar has said that the World Bank (WB) has extended an unconditional loan of $250 million to the Supporting Andhra’s Learning Transformation (SALT) project in appreciation of the path-breaking reforms implemented by the State government.

Concept note-3: -About: Supporting Andhra’s Learning Transformation (SALT) is a project undertaken by the Government of Andhra Pradesh with support from the World Bank.

Concept note-4: -SALT programme stands for “Supporting Andhra’s Learning Transformation” programme. This programme was launched with the aim of improving learning outcomes, quality of teaching practices and school management in basic education.

Concept note-5: -The S.A.L.T. Programme is an innovative programme, specially developed for the Irish and UK School Curriculum and is aligned to the key strands of the SPHE (Primary) Programme: Myself, Myself and Others, Relating to Others, Myself and the World.
