Which state has made GPS and Panic Button mandatory in public passenger vehicles?

(A) Jharkhand

(B) Karnataka

(C) ** Chhattisgarh

(D) Meghalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Karnataka makes tracking and emergency panic button devices mandatory for all transport vehicles. The move is expected to contribute significantly to safety on the highways as the GPS devices will alert the authorities of any overspeeding vehicles.

Concept note-2: -The ministry of road transport and highways has mandated installation of VLT device and emergency button/panic button for new public service vehicles except two-wheelers, E-rickshaw, three wheelers and any transport vehicles for which permit is not required under the Motor Vehicle Act, 1988.

Concept note-3: -GPS tracking devices can monitor the whereabouts of a worker, and a panic button allows them to call for help in the event of an emergency. Best GPS trackers and panic buttons are designed to alert designated individuals or authorities to the location of the emergency.

Concept note-4: -Panic buttons make it possible for your drivers to trigger an alarm in an emergency situation, which can give them, not to mention you, peace of mind. It’s a very simple procedure that can prove to be invaluable in a distress situation.
