Which state has organized a Banana Festival?

(A) Assam

(B) Kerala

(C) Jharkhand

(D) ** Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which state is hosting the National Banana Festival (NBF) 2018? Notes: The National Banana Festival (NBF-2018) has started at Kalliyoor, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala from February 17 to 21.

Concept note-2: -We are glad to announce the National Banana Festival (NBF) 2018 from 17 to 21 February at Kalliyoor, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. The festival is organised by Centre for Innovation in Science and Social action (CISSA) in partnership with Kalliyoor Grama Panchayat and a host of National and State organisations.

Concept note-3: -Banana Festival is celebrated as thanksgiving for the abundance of banana in the Municipality of Baco. The celebration includes the traditional “lupakan, ” “harana” (serenade), and “pandangguhan” (dance with lights).

Concept note-4: -Banana Festival of Baco, Oriental Mindoro The Banana Festival is a two-day affair that is held annually between 18th and 19th March in Baco.
