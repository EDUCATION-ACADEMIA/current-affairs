Which team has won the Asian Champions Trophy men’s hockey tournament held in Dhaka, Bangladesh?

(A) India

(B) ** Korea

(C) Japan

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2016 Men’s Asian Champions Trophy was the fourth edition of the Men’s Asian Champions Trophy. The tournament was held in Kuantan, Pahang, Malaysia from 20 to 30 October 2016. India defeated the defending champions Pakistan 3–2 in the final to win the trophy for the second time.

Concept note-2: -South Korea won their fifth title after a finals win over Malaysia.
