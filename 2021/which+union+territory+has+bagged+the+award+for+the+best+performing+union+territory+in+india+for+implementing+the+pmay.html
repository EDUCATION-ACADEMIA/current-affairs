Which Union Territory has bagged the award for the ‘Best Performing Union Territory in India for Implementing the PMAY?

(A) Andaman and Nicobar

(B) Jammu and Kashmir

(C) Port Blair

(D) ** Puducherry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -i. Madhya Pradesh (MP) has won the PMAY -U Awards 2021 under 3 special categories and Jammu and Kashmir has won the award under “Best Performing Union Territory(UT)” alongside Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu. ii.

Concept note-2: -Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Tamil Nadu bag top three positions in PMAY-U awards; poll-bound Gujarat gets 5 ‘Special Category’ awards; J&K adjudged ‘Best performing UT’

Concept note-3: -The specific objectives of the PMAY-U award are as follows: To recognize the achievements by States/UTs in the Mission. To encourage healthy competition among States/UTs/ULBs for achieving the goal of ‘Housing for All’ within stipulated time. To disseminate knowledge on effective methods of design and implementation.
