Which university has released the report on Ageing Dams in the world?

(A) Stanford University

(B) ** UN University

(C) Massachusetts Institute of Technology

(D) University of Oxford

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the UN report In India, over 1, 115 large dams will be at roughly 50 years mark by 2025. The report also finds that India’s current dam construction rate is among the world’s highest.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which organisation released a report titled ‘Ageing water infrastructure: An emerging global risk’? Notes: The report, titled ‘Ageing water infrastructure: An emerging global risk’ was compiled by United Nations University’s Institute for Water, Environment and Health.

Concept note-3: -According to a United Nations (UN) report “Ageing water infrastructure: An emerging global risk", over 1, 000 large dams in India will be roughly 50 years old in 2025 and such ageing embankments across the world pose a growing threat.

Concept note-4: -Large dams have led to the extinction of many fish and other aquatic species, the disappearance of birds in floodplains, huge losses of forest, wetland and farmland, erosion of coastal deltas, and many other unmitigable impacts.
