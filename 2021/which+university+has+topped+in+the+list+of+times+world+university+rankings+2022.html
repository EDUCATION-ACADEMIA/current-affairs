Which university has topped in the list of Times World University Rankings 2022?

(A) Stanford University

(B) Harvard University

(C) ** University of Oxford

(D) California Institute of Technology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The University of Oxford tops the ranking for the sixth consecutive year, while mainland China has two institutions in the top 20 for the first time: Peking University and Tsinghua University share 16th place.

Concept note-2: -View the World University Rankings 2023 methodology The University of Oxford tops the ranking for the seventh consecutive year. Harvard University remains in second place, but the University of Cambridge jumps from joint fifth last year to joint third.

Concept note-3: -THE Rankings 2022: Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bengaluru, at 301-350 position, is the only Indian institute in the top 350, followed by IIT Ropar, JS Academy of Higher Education and Research, IIT Indore, Alagappa University and Thapar University.
