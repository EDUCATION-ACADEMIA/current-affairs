Which women team has won the SAFF U 19 Women’s Championship by defeating India in the finals?

(A) Myanmar

(B) Sri Lanka

(C) Pakistan

(D) ** Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bangladesh. Bangladesh Under-19 Women’s team defeated India by 1-0 to lift the SAFF U-19 Women’s Football Championship in Dhaka, in December 2021.

Concept note-2: -The tournament were played from 6–19 September 2022 in Kathmandu, Nepal. In the final, Bangladesh played Nepal on 19 September 2022 at the Dasharath Rangasala Stadium in Kathmandu. Bangladesh won the final match 3–1, claiming their first SAFF title.

Concept note-3: -India are the reigning champions, having won the 2022 edition. Hosts Bangladesh, meanwhile, have won the first two editions and are the most successful team in the competition.
