Which among the following has been named Assam’s sixth national park?

(A) Dihing Patkai

(B) Sonai Rupai

(C) Pohugarh

(D) ** Raimona

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Raimona National Park: The 6th National Park of Assam.

Concept note-2: -In June this year, the Assam government declared the state’s sixth national park, Raimona National Park, spreading over an area of 422 sq. km. in the Kokrajhar district under the Bodoland Territorial Region (BTR) combining parts of Ripu-Chirang reserve forests.

Concept note-3: -The present Raimona National Park was originally recommended as Ripu-Chirang Wildlife Sanctuary being part of Ripu reserved forest and Chirang reserved forest being adjacent a part of it was also mooted to be within.
