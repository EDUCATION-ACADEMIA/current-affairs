Which bank announced the launch of the “I choose my number” feature for all its customers across India?

(A) ** Jana Small Finance Bank

(B) Fincare Payments Bank

(C) Paytm Payments Bank

(D) ICICI Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jana Small Finance Bank Founded: 24 July 2006; Jana Small Finance Bank Headquarters Location: Bengaluru.

Concept note-2: -About Jana Small Finance Bank Jana Small Finance Bank has served over 8.00 million customers since 2008, including approximately 3.05 million active customers.

Concept note-3: -Capital Small Finance Bank Limited started operations as India’s 1st Small Finance Bank on April 24, 2016 after conversion from Capital Local Area Bank. Prior to conversion to a Small Finance Bank, Capital Local Area Bank was operating as India’s largest local area bank since January 14, 2000.

Concept note-4: -Capital Small Finance Bank (erstwhile Capital Local Area Bank) is the India’s first small finance bank founded in April 2016 as a microfinance lender, with its headquarters in Jalandhar, India.
