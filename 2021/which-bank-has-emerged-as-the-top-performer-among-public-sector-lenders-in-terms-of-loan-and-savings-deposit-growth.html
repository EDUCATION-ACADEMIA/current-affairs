Which bank has emerged as the top performer among public sector lenders in terms of loan and savings deposit growth?

(A) SBI

(B) ** Bank of Maharashtra

(C) PNB

(D) HDFC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank of Maharashtra has emerged as the top performer among public sector lenders in terms of loan growth in percentage terms during second quarter of 2022-23.

Concept note-2: -Bank of Maharashtra (BoM) has emerged as the top performer among the public sector lenders in terms of the loan and deposit growth in percentage terms during the first quarter of 2022-23.

Concept note-3: -First on our list is the Union Bank of India.

Concept note-4: -Motilal Oswal tracks top 7 PSU banks - State Bank of India, Punjab National Bank, Bank of India, Bank of Baroda, Central Bank, Union Bank and Indian Bank and from the list its top picks are SBI, BoB and Central Bank.

Concept note-5: -State Bank of India(SBI) is the largest public sector bank in India and Asia and one of the largest in the world.
