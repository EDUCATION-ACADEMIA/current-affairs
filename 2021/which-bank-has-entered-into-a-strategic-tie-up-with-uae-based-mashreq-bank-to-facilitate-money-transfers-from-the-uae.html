Which bank has entered into a strategic tie-up with UAE-based Mashreq Bank to facilitate money transfers from the UAE?

(A) ICICI Bank

(B) RBL Bank

(C) HDFC Bank

(D) ** Federal Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Federal Bank has entered into a strategic tie-up with Mashreq Bank of UAE, to facilitate money transfers from the UAE to India.

Concept note-2: -Mashreq Neo in partnership with Federal Bank brings you convenience to open an NRI account instantly!

Concept note-3: -Mashreqbank is one of UAE’s leading financial institutions. Established in 1967 by the Al-Ghurair Group, it is the largest private sector bank in the UAE.

Concept note-4: -Mashreq began life as the Bank of Oman, based in Dubai, in 1967. Celebrating its 40th birthday in 2007, Mashreq has grown to its current position as one of the leading financial institutions in the United Arab Emirates (UAE).
