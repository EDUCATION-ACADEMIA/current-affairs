Which bank has launched CASA mobilization campaign?

(A) HDFC Bank

(B) DBS Bank

(C) State Bank

(D) ** Karnataka Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’CASA Mobilisation Campaign’-HDFC Bank.

Concept note-2: -CASA stands for Current Account Saving Account. This is a unique feature which banks offer to their customers to make them keep their money in their banks. The account combines the benefits of savings account and checking accounts.

Concept note-3: -Enjoy benefits of both Savings and Current Account with ICICI Bank CASA Family. With ICICI Bank CASA Family, all your banking needs can now be catered to by a single ‘Family’ bank.

Concept note-4: -In September 2003, the bank shifted its head office from Kodialbail to Kankanady. In 2021, Karnataka Bank Limited operationalized its wholly owned non-financial subsidiary KBL Services Limited with its registered and head office at Bangalore.

Concept note-5: -Karnataka Bank Limited. Kirloskar Brothers Limited, a leading Pump manufacturer of India.
