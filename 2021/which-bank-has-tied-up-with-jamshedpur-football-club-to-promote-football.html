Which bank has tied up with Jamshedpur Football Club to promote football?

(A) ** State Bank of India

(B) Kotak Mahindra Bank

(C) Punjab National Bank

(D) HDFC bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The State Bank of India has signed a strategic agreement with Jamshedpur Football Club, a fully owned subsidiary of Tata Steel, to promote and support football in India.

Concept note-2: -TATA and TATA Steel are the principal sponsors for the ongoing season of the competition for the Jamshedpur-based team. Kit Sponsors: The Men of Steel have also announced a collaboration with a sportswear brand, Nivia, as their official kit sponsor.

Concept note-3: -State Bank of India (SBI) has entered into a strategic partnership with Jamshedpur Football Club (JFC), a fully owned Tata Steel subsidiary, to promote football in the country. With this partnership, SBI will be one of the principal sponsors of the JFC. The JFC match jersey will now feature the SBI logo.

Concept note-4: -2022–23 season On 10 July 2022, Aidy Boothroyd was appointed as head coach of Jamshedpur. He said: Jamshedpur FC are the Champions of India currently and the city has a tremendous football legacy. We want to continue this upward trajectory and make the club reach places and win honours that our fans are dreaming of.
