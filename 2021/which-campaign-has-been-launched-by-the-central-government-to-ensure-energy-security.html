Which campaign has been launched by the central government to ensure energy security?

(A) Go Eco

(B) ** Go Electric

(C) Go Friendly

(D) Go Green

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is a significant cleanliness campaign started by the Government of India.

Concept note-2: -What is the name of the campaign launched by Government of India to boost the adoption of electric vehicles and appliances? Notes: The Government of India has recently launched a campaign named “Go Electric campaign”.

Concept note-3: -India’s 4-plank energy security strategy is based on diversifying supplies, increasing E&P, alternate energy sources and energy transition through a gas-based economy, Green Hydrogen etc. India will contribute 25% of global demand by 2040 and achieve 20% ethanol blending in petrol by 2025: Hardeep Singh Puri.

Concept note-4: -Energy security is defined as the uninterrupted availability of energy sources at an affordable price. Long-term energy security deals with timely investments to supply energy in line with economic developments and environmental needs.
