Which city has become the first in the world to go 100 percent paperless?

(A) Abu Dhabi

(B) Tokyo

(C) ** Dubai

(D) Toronto

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dubai has become the first government in the world to turn 100 per cent paperless. This was announced by Emirate’s Crown Prince Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum.

Concept note-2: -Dubai has become the first nation in the world to be 100 percent paperless: How is it significant? Dubai’s paperless strategy has five significant phases. Countries like USA, Canada and UK also plan to go paper-free soon.

Concept note-3: -The Dubai Paperless Strategy was implemented in five consecutive phases, each of which enlisted a different group of the Dubai Government’s entities.

Concept note-4: -Q. Which city has become the world’s first government to turn completely paperless? Notes: Dubai has become the world’s first government to turn completely paperless, the Emirate’s Crown Prince, Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum.

Concept note-5: -Dubai is the first government globally to turn completely paperless, according to the Emirate’s Crown Prince Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid Al Makhtoum.
