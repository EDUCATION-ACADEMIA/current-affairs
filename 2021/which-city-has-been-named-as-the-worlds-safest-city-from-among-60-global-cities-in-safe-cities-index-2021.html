Which city has been named as the world’s safest city from among 60 global cities, in Safe Cities Index 2021?

(A) ** Copenhagen

(B) London

(C) Rome

(D) Budapest

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The fourth iteration of the index looks at 60 cities across 76 indicators, covering digital, health, infrastructure, personal, and - new this year - environmental security. The top 5 cities were Copenhagen, Toronto, Singapore, Sydney, Tokyo, in that order.

Concept note-2: -Copenhagen has been named as the world’s safest city from among 60 global cities in the Safe Cities Index 2021.

Concept note-3: -In 2021, as per the total combined stat, Abu Dhabi (88.46) was the safest city in the world, followed by Doha (87.96). Sharjah (83.6) and Dubai (83.59) were ranked sixth and seventh for the complete year.

Concept note-4: -A new report by the Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU) found that Copenhagen is now the world’s safest city, surpassing previous frontrunners Tokyo, Singapore, and Osaka.

Concept note-5: -Copenhagen tops the index, followed by Toronto and Singapore The Safe Cities Index 2021 was recently published by The Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU), part of The Economist Group.
