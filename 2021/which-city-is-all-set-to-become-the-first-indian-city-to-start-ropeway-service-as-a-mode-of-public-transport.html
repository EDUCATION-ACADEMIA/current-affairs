Which city is all set to become the first Indian city to start ropeway service as a mode of public transport?

(A) Noida

(B) Kanpur

(C) ** Varanasi

(D) Lucknow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Varanasi All Set To Become India’s First City To Have Ropeway As Public Transport. The ropeway project will connect the area from Varanasi Cantt to Godaulia Chowk.

Concept note-2: -Varanasi of Uttar Pradesh will become the first city in India to use ropeway services in public transportation. Overall, Varanasi will be the third city in the world after Bolivia and Mexico City to use ropeway in public transportation.

Concept note-3: -1- India will be the third country in the world after Mexico City and Bolivia to have a ropeway for public transportation.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s constituency Varanasi is set to become the first city in India to have a ropeway service as a mode of public transport.
