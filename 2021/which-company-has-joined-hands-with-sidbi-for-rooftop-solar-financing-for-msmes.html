Which company has joined hands with SIDBI for rooftop solar financing for MSMEs?

(A) HHV Solar Technologies

(B) Kotak Urja

(C) ** Tata Power

(D) Icomm Tele

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tata Power partners with SIDBI to launch affordable & collateral-free financing for Rooftop Solar for MSMEs Consumers.

Concept note-2: -Tata Power and SIDBI partner to launch affordable financing for MSMEs in rooftop solar segment.

Concept note-3: -Role of SIDBI SIDBI is mandated to serve as the Principal Financial Institution for executing the triple agenda of promotion, financing and development of the MSME sector and coordination of the functions of the various Institutions engaged in similar activities.

Concept note-4: -SIDBI offers Working Capital Assistance, Term Loan Assistance, Foreign Currency Loan, Support against Receivables, equity support, Energy Saving scheme for the MSME sector, etc. under its various direct finance loan schemes.

Concept note-5: -Interest rate of 6% p.a. (5.50% p.a. for women run / owned enterprises). Repayment upto 5 years (3 years for WCTL).
