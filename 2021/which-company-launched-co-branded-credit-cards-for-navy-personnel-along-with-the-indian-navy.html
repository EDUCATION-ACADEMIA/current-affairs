Which Company launched co-branded credit cards for navy personnel along with the Indian Navy?

(A) HDFC Financial Services

(B) Geojit Financial Services

(C) ** BOB Financial Solutions

(D) Fedbank Financial Services

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: The Non-Banking Financial Company, and wholly-owned by Bank of Baroda, BOB Financial Solutions Limited launched a co-branded credit card for navy personnel along with the Indian Navy. The credit card is featured as contactless and will be offered on the RuPay platform.

Concept note-2: -Co-branded credit cards are the product of a mutual partnership between a particular merchant and a credit card issuer. Together, they create a credit card that bears the merchant’s logo and provide merchant-specific benefits to brand-loyal consumers.

Concept note-3: -Bank of Baroda subsidiary launches co-branded RuPay contactless credit card for Indian Army.

Concept note-4: -Central Bank of India was the first Bank to introduce credit cards in India.
