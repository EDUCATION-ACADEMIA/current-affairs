Which countries have now been added to ICC as its new members?

(A) Mongolia

(B) Tajikistan

(C) Switzerland

(D) ** All of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A trio of nations have joined the ICC as Associate Members. Accepted and signed on during the ICC’s Annual General Meeting in Birmingham, Uzbekistan, Cambodia and Côte d’Ivoire became the game’s youngest Associates.

Concept note-2: -The three ICC members include Cote D’Ivoire from Africa and Cambodia and Uzbekistan from Asia. The addition of the three new member countries have taken the total number of ICC members to 108 countries including 96 associates.

Concept note-3: -The ICC has 108 member nations currently: 12 Full Members that play Test matches, and 96 Associate Members.

Concept note-4: -There are 104 Member countries of the International Cricket Council (ICC). This includes 12 Full Members and 92 Associate Members.
