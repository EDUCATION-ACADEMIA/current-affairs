Which country allowed to Import Onions from India?

(A) Pakistan

(B) China

(C) ** Bangladesh

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The top 3 importers of Onion are Sri Lanka with 117, 766 shipments followed by United States with 97, 436 and Malaysia at the 3rd spot with 91, 797 shipments.

Concept note-2: -So the major onion importing partners of India are the USA, UK, Saudi Arabia, Malaysia, Germany, Canada, UAE, Turkey, Spain, China, etc.

Concept note-3: -The varieties of onion in India that exports are Podisu onion, Red Onion, White Onion, Krishnapuram rose onion, Bangalore rose onion etc.
