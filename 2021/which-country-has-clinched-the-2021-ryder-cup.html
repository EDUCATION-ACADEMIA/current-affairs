Which country has clinched the 2021 Ryder Cup?

(A) ** USA

(B) Australia

(C) Japan

(D) Netherlands

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United States, fielding its youngest team in its history, regained the Ryder Cup in 2021 with a record 19–9 victory. This was the largest margin of victory in a Ryder Cup since 1967.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is United States. The United States reclaimed the Ryder Cup in September 2021, defeating Europe 19-9. It’s the first time since 1979 and 1983 that Team USA won back-to-back home Ryder Cups after winning in 2016 at Hazeltine.

Concept note-3: -The United States has won two of the last three editions overall, including a blowout of Team Europe 19-9 at Whistling Straits in 2021.

Concept note-4: -Ryder Cup: Luke Donald named as Team Europe captain.
