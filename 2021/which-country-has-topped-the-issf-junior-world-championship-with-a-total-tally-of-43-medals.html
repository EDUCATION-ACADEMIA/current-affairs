Which country has topped the ISSF Junior World Championship with a total tally of 43 medals?

(A) United States

(B) Italy

(C) France

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Simranpreet Kaur Brar and Vijayveer Sidhu, meanwhile, won the mixed team gold in the same event after beating fellow Indians Anish Bhanwala and Tejaswani 17-9 in the final. At the last edition of the ISSF Junior World Cup in 2021, India had 43 medals - 17 golds, 16 silvers and 10 bronze - to top the medals table.

Concept note-2: -Indian shooters concluded their campaign on top with a total of 33 medals at the ISSF Junior World Cup 2022 in Suhl, Germany this week. India’s tally of 33 medals - 13 golds, 15 silvers and five bronzes - was the most by any country at the 2022 edition.

Concept note-3: -China topped the ISSF Rifle/Pistol World Championships 2022 medals tally with 58 medals, 27 hold, 16 silver and 15 bronze.

Concept note-4: -Olympic silver medallist Vijay Kumar (581) and Anish Bhanwala (575) placed 13th and 28th respectively. India is second in the medals table with 12 gold, nine silver and 13 bronze medals while China sits on top with 25 gold, 16 silver and 14 bronze medals.
