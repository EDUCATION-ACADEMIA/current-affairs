Which country ranked last in the Global Energy Transition Index (ETI) 2021?

(A) Zambia

(B) Botswana

(C) South Africa

(D) ** Zimbabwe

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The report is published by the World Economic Forum (WEF), prepared in collaboration with Accenture to track nations on the current performance of their energy systems across various aspects. Zimbabwe (115) is the last ranked country in the index.

Concept note-2: -In the recently published report, India ranked 87th out of 115 countries in the Energy Transition Index(ETI).

Concept note-3: -Sweden has topped the Index for the third consecutive year and is followed by Switzerland and Finland in the top three. France (8th) and the UK (7th) are the only G20 countries in the top ten.

Concept note-4: -India has been ranked at the 87th position among 115 countries in the Energy Transition Index (ETI) that tracks nations on the current performance of their energy systems across various aspects, according to a World Economic Forum (WEF) report released.

Concept note-5: -What is the News? The World Economic Forum(WEF) has released the Energy Transition Index, 2021.
