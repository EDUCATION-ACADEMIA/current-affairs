Which country was the largest source of FDI in India in FY21?

(A) ** Singapore

(B) China

(C) United States

(D) Thailand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Singapore (27.01%) and USA (17.94%) have emerged as top 2 sourcing nations in FDI equity flows into India in FY2021-22 followed by Mauritius (15.98%), Netherland (7.86%) and Switzerland (7.31%).

Concept note-2: -Singapore is ranked one FDI provider to India. The small island nation has invested $15.9 billion in FY22 which is accounted for 27 per cent of India’s total FDI received. USA is India’s second biggest investor with FDI of $10.5 billion, with 18 per cent of total FDI.

Concept note-3: -Sector-wise FDI Equity Inflows during April-September 2022 During the first half of this fiscal, Singapore emerged as the top investor. It was followed by Mauritius, the U.A.E., the U.S.A., the Netherlands and Japan.
