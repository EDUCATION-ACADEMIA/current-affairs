Which country will host the Conference of Parties (COP-26) on climate change?

(A) France

(B) ** UK

(C) Canada

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It will be held at the Scottish Event Campus (SEC) in Glasgow, United Kingdom, from 31 October to 12 November 2021. COP 26 will be presided over by the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.

Concept note-2: -The UN Climate Change Conference in Glasgow (COP26) brought together 120 world leaders and over 40, 000 registered participants, including 22, 274 party delegates, 14.124 observers and 3.886 media representatives.

Concept note-3: -The COP 26 UN Climate Change Conference, hosted by the UK in partnership with Italy, took place from 31 October to 12 November 2021 in the Scottish Event Campus (SEC) in Glasgow, UK.

Concept note-4: -The conference took place from 6-20 November 2022 in Sharm el-Sheikh, Egypt. Heads of State and Government attended the Sharm el-Sheikh Climate Implementation Summit on 7 and 8 November.
