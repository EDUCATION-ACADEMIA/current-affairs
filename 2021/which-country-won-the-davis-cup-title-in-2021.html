Which country won the Davis Cup Title in 2021?

(A) Croatia

(B) Spain

(C) ** Russia

(D) Serbia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Russian Tennis Federation won the title, defeating Croatia in the final. Andrey Rublev was named the Most Valuable Player of the tournament after going 6–1 in both singles and doubles.

Concept note-2: -Canada won the title, defeating Australia in the final. It was Canada’s first ever Davis Cup title.

Concept note-3: -The Davis Cup by Rakuten Finals will take place across three cities on 25 November - 5 December in 2021. Madrid and Turin will each host two of the six groups, with Madrid staging two quarter-finals, and Innsbruck and Turin one quarter-final each. The Spanish capital will also host the semi-finals and final.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Russia. Russia’s 15-year wait for a Davis Cup title ended in December 2021. Daniil Medvedev beat Marin Cilic 7-6 (7), 6-2 in the second singles match to give Russia a 2-0 lead over Croatia in the final. It is Russia’s third Davis Cup title, and first since 2006.
