Which cricket player has become the youngest bowler to take 400 T20 wickets during his team’s crucial Super 12 encounter against New Zealand in Dubai?

(A) Mehidy Hasan Miraz

(B) ** Rashid Khan

(C) Aftab Baloch

(D) PP Shaw

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Rashid Khan . Afghanistan’s leg-spinner Rashid Khan picked up his 400th wicket in competitive T20 cricket during the team’s T20 World Cup match against New Zealand on 7 Nov’21. He achieved the feat in his 289th T20 match.

Concept note-2: -Rashid achieved the feat in his 289th T20 match. Only three other bowlers have crossed the 400-wicket mark in T20 cricket. Dwayne Bravo, the first to reach the feat in 364 matches, also became the first bowler to cross 500 T20 wickets.
