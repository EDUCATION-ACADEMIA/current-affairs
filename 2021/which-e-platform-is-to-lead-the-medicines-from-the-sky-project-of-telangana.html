Which E-platform is to lead the ‘Medicines from the Sky’ project of Telangana?

(A) ** Flipkart

(B) Amazon

(C) Zomato

(D) Swiggy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Flipkart is to lead the Medicines from the Sky project of Telangana.

Concept note-2: -Approved vaccines and medicines will now be delivered using drones within Beyond Visual Line of Sight (BVLOS) for one year.

Concept note-3: -Seppa, East Kameng District, Arunachal Pradesh, India, 15 August 2022 – Medicine from the Sky, a flagship initiative by the World Economic Forum, is to be launched in Arunachal Pradesh, north-east India, on Independence Day, 15 August.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Flipkart Internet Pvt.

Concept note-5: -It was important to demonstrate the sustained capability of drones in real-time scenarios. Given the state’s vibrant start-up ecosystem and its desire to use Fourth Industrial Revolution technologies for social impact, Telangana was an ideal candidate for an exploratory programme on drone-based medical deliveries.
