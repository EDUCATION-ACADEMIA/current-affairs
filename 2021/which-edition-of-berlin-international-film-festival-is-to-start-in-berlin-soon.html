Which edition of Berlin International Film Festival is to start in Berlin soon?

(A) 68th

(B) ** 70th

(C) 72nd

(D) 74th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Berlin International Film Festival has confirmed its dates for next year, with the event set to run from February 16-26, 2023. The dates are slightly later than this year’s edition, which opened on February 10 and closed this Sunday, February 20.

Concept note-2: -More than 400 films will be shown during the 73rd Berlinale, which will take place in Berlin from February 16 to February 26, 2023.

Concept note-3: -The Berlinale began as a summer festival in 1951, but switched to February in 1978. First celebrated in 1951, the Berlin International Film Festival has evolved over the years to become one of the most important international film festivals.

Concept note-4: -While feature-length films need a minimum running time of 60 min, Berlinale Shorts accepts films up to 30 min, short films for Generation will be accepted up to 20 min. Films for Perspektive Deutsches Kino (German productions only) need a minimum length of 30 min. Documentary forms can be submitted to all sections.
