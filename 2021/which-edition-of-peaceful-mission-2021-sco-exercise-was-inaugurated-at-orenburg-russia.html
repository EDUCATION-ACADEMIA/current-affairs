Which edition of “Peaceful mission 2021”, SCO exercise was inaugurated at Orenburg, Russia?

(A) 5th

(B) ** 6th

(C) 10th

(D) 20th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 6th Edition of SCO Exercise Peaceful Mission : 2021 hosted by Russia started at Orenburg Region of South West Russia today. The aim of the exercise is to foster close relations between SCO Member States and to enhance abilities of the military leaders to command multinational military contingents.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Russia. The 6th edition of Exercise Peaceful Mission is being hosted by Russia in the Orenburg Region of southwest Russia from 13-25 Sept 2021.

Concept note-3: -The sixth edition of the Indo-French Shakti 2021 (bilateral joint training exercise), will be conducted in November in Frejus, France. From the Indian side, a platoon strength of a Gorkha Rifles Infantry Battalion will participate in the exercise.

Concept note-4: -The multilateral joint anti-terror exercise “Manesar Anti-Terror 2022, ” hosted by India under the framework of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO), concluded on Thursday. The multilateral Joint anti-terror exercise hosted by India under the SCO concluded.

Concept note-5: -India, US successfully conclude 18th edition of joint military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas’
