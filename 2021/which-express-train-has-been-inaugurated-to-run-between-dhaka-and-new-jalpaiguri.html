Which express train has been inaugurated to run between Dhaka and New Jalpaiguri?

(A) Bangla Express

(B) ** Mitali Express

(C) Maestro Express

(D) PW Express

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Mitali express train covers the distance between the New Jalpaiguri railway station in Siliguri, West Bengal, and the Cantonment railway station in Dhaka, Bangladesh. The train was inaugurated in March 2021 and its commercial run started in June 2022. It is the third passenger train between India and Bangladesh.

Concept note-2: -The Mitali Express train is an international express rail service that runs between Dhaka (Bangladesh) and New Jalpaiguri (India) every week. It is the 3rd international passenger train between India and Bangladesh. A valid visa and passport are required before purchasing a ticket and booking the Mitali Express train.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Dhaka-New Jalpaiguri. It is a passenger train that was inaugurated by PM Modi & Bangladesh PM Sheikh Hasina. The train will ply between New Jalpaiguri of West Bengal to Dhaka Cantonment in Bangladesh.

Concept note-4: -The new bi-weekly Mitali Express will connect New Jalpaiguri in North Bengal with Dhaka in Bangladesh through Haldibari in India and then Chilahati in Northern Bangladesh.

Concept note-5: -Agartala: The much expected new railway line between India and Bangladesh along Tripura would be operational in June next year, officials said here on Monday quoting Bangladesh Railway Minister Md. Nurul Islam Sujan.
