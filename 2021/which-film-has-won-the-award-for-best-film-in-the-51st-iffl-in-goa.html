Which film has won the award for Best Film in the 51st IFFl in Goa?

(A) Terminator 3

(B) ** Into the darkness

(C) The dark night

(D) Avenger

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Here are the winners of the film festival: I Have Electric Dreams - Best Film. No End - Nader Saeivar, Best Director. Best Male Actor - Vahid Mobasheri, No End.

Concept note-2: -The 51st International Film Festival of India has conferred Biswajit Chatterjee, veteran actor, producer of Hindi and Bengali cinema, with the Indian Personality of the Year Award. This award will be presented to him, when the National Film Awards are presented in March 2021.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Into the Darkness. The 51st edition of the International Film Festival of India, IFFI concluded on 24 January 2021. ‘Into the Darkness’, a film from Denmark won the prestigious Golden Peacock award.
