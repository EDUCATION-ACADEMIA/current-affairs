Which Financial body has approved USD 125 Million Support Program for the ‘Resilient Kerala Program’?

(A) UNDP

(B) ADB

(C) IMF

(D) ** World Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Bank Approves $125 Million Program to Support a Green and Resilient Kerala.

Concept note-2: -PROJECT SIGNING: West Bengal Gets $125 Million to Help Citizens Access Social Protection Services.

Concept note-3: -The Program objective is to enhance the State of Kerala’s resilience against the impacts of climate change and natural disasters, including disease outbreaks and pandemics.

Concept note-4: -The Government of India, the Government of Kerala and the World Bank have signed a Loan Agreement of USD 250 million for the First Resilient Kerala Program to enhance the State’s resilience against the impacts of natural disasters and climate change.
