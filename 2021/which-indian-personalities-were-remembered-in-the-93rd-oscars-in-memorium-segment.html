Which Indian personalities were remembered in the 93rd Oscars In Memorium Segment?

(A) Rishi Kapoor and Sushant Singh Rajput

(B) Rishi Kapoor and Irrfan Khan

(C) ** Irrfan Khan and Bhanu Athaiya

(D) Sushant Singh Rajput and Irrfan Khan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Other artists such as Chadwick Boseman, Cicely Tyson, Sean Connery, among others, were also remembered. The 93rd Academy Awards honoured Irrfan and Bhanu Athaiya in In Memoriam segment.

Concept note-2: -During Sunday’s 94th Oscars, The Academy paid tribute to the artists and filmmakers who left their indelible mark on cinema before passing on, including Sidney Poitier, Betty White, Ivan Reitman, Stephen Sondheim and many more.

Concept note-3: -Bob Saget left out of Oscars In Memoriam segment joyful than usual.

Concept note-4: -Malayalam film Jallikattu, directed by Lijo Jose Pellissery, has been selected as India’s official entry for the Best International Feature Film category for the 93rd Academy Awards.
