Which Indian short film is among the top 10 shortlisted films in Live-Action Category for the 93rd Academy Awards?

(A) Rastaa

(B) That Day After Everyday

(C) Bypass

(D) ** Bittu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bittu has made it to the Live Action Short Film shortlist for the 93rd Academy Awards. We have listed everything that you need to know about the film. Bittu is directed by Karishma Dev Dube.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Shameless. Writer-director Keith Gomes’ short film ‘Shameless’ has been selected as India’s official entry at the 93rd Oscars in the ‘Live Action Short Film’ category.

Concept note-3: -02/8RRR. SS Rajamouli’s ‘RRR’ featured on the Academy’s list of productions eligible for Oscar nominations at the 95th Academy Awards.

Concept note-4: -The Meghan Mylan directed movie is a documentary of a small girl with cleft lips. It won the 2009 Academy Award for Best Short Documentary. ‘Smile Pinki’ is a short documentary about Pinki Sonkar, a six-year-old girl living in one of India’s poorest areas with a severe cleft lip.

Concept note-5: -India’s history with the Oscar entries for the last few years were - Koozhangal, Jallikattu, Gully Boy, Village Rockstars, Newton, Visaranani, all of which have failed to make the Oscar shortlist. The only Indian films to have been nominated for an Oscar so far have been Mother India, Salaam Bombay and Lagaan.
