Which Indian state has been topped in the India Smart Cities Awards Contest 2020?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Gujarat

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -These awards were earlier announced through virtual conference in 2021. However, the award distribution ceremony was deferred due to the prevailing COVID-19 situation at that time. Surat and Indore were given the Best city award, while Uttar Pradesh was awarded the Best State.

Concept note-2: -The smart town Bhopal was given the top spot in the most recent assessment for 2022 by the Union Ministry of Housing and Concrete Affairs. It is the nation of Madhya Pradesh’s capital, and it is home to around 2.5 million people.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Surat. Surat has been awarded for showcasing “great momentum” in the implementation of projects under the Smart Cities Mission, the Housing and Urban Affairs (HUA) Ministry.

Concept note-4: -Frequently Asked Questions on Smart Cities Award India Ans. Indore and Surat were jointly announced as the smartest cities in India for 2020.

Concept note-5: -List of Cities for Smart City Mission in India. A total of 100 cities are selected as of date.
