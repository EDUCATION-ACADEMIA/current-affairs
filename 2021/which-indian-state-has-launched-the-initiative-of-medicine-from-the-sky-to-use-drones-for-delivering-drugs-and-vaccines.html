Which Indian state has launched the initiative of ‘Medicine from the Sky’ to use drones for delivering drugs and vaccines?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) ** Telangana

(D) Uttrakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In a bid to ease the transportation of essential medicines to remote areas, vaccines were delivered in Arunachal Pradesh through drones for the first time in the world.

Concept note-2: -After Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh too has taken a sky route through drones to deliver medicines and vaccines in its inaccessible remote terrains and in emergency situations.

Concept note-3: -Meghalaya becomes first NE state in country to deliver medicines via drone services.

Concept note-4: -World Economic Forum’s ‘Medicine from the Sky‘ Drone Delivery Programme Set for Take-off in Indian State of Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-5: -On the occasion of celebrating India’s independence on August 15, Arunachal Pradesh launched its first drone service, ‘Medicine from the sky’, from Seppa to Chayang Tajo.
