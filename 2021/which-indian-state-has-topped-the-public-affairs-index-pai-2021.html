Which Indian state has topped the Public Affairs Index (PAI 2021)?

(A) Telangana

(B) ** Kerala

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The seventh edition of PAI brought out by the Bengaluru-based Public Affairs Centre (PAC) on Governance in the States in India was released on Friday. It said Haryana, with a score of 0.6948, topped the list in three kinds of justices, followed by Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Chhattisgarh, Punjab, and Karnataka.

Concept note-2: -Haryana has bagged the top position in the category of big states in the Public Affairs Index-2022. The state has emerged at the forefront in social, economic and political justice themes.

Concept note-3: -While Karnataka was fourth last year, this year it is behind Kerala (1.618), Tamil Nadu (0.897), Telangana (0.891), Chhattisgarh (0.0872), Gujarat (0.782) and Punjab (0.643), with an index score of 0.121.

Concept note-4: -The Public Affairs Index (PAI) is a scientifically rigorous, data-based framework that measures the quality of governance at the subnational level and ranks the states and Union Territories (UTs) of India on a Composite Index (CI).
