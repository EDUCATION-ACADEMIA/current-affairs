Which Indian wrestler has won the Olympic wrestling bronze medal in 2021?

(A) Vinesh Phougat

(B) ** Bajrang Punia

(C) Ravi Dahiya

(D) Yogeshwar Dutt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This is Vinesh Phogat’s second bronze medal at the world championships after her podium finish at the 2019 edition at Nur-Sultan, Kazakhstan. Vinesh Phogat’s win was also India’s first medal of the 2022 edition.

Concept note-2: -Ace grappler Bajrang Punia became the only Indian to claim four medals at the World Wrestling Championships by clinching a bronze in the current edition, in Belgrade, Serbia, on Sunday.

Concept note-3: -World Wrestling Championships 2022: India’s Bajrang Punia wins bronze; India finish with two medals.

Concept note-4: -Who won medals in Tokyo Olympics 2021 in India? The Indian hockey team, Mirabai Chanu, and PV Sindhu round up the list of medal winners, which also includes Ravi Kumar Dahiya, Bajrang Punia, Lovlina Borgohain, and Neeraj Chopra.
