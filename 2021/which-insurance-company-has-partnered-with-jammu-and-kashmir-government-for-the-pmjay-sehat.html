Which Insurance Company has partnered with Jammu and Kashmir Government for the PMJAY SEHAT?

(A) ** Bajaj Allianz General Insurance

(B) Bharti Axa Life Insurance

(C) Fino Life Insurance

(D) Life Insurance Corporation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The main aim behind the scheme is to extend health insurance coverage to all residents of Jammu and Kashmir. It provides free of cost insurance cover to all residents of Jammu and Kashmir up to Rs. 5 Lakh per family on a floater basis to all residents.

Concept note-2: -Benefits: – It provides financial cover up to Rs. 5 Lakh per family on a floater basis to all residents of the UT of J&K and an operational extension of PM-JAY to 15 lakh (approximately) additional families.

Concept note-3: -Edelweiss General Insurance becomes the first Insurer to partner with Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) Digital insurer Edelweiss General Insurance (EGI) is the first insurer to partner with the Ministry of Health, Government of India, to help create digital health IDs for all the people of India.

Concept note-4: -Is there a difference between Ayushman Bharat Yojana and PM-JAY? No, these are names of the same scheme.
