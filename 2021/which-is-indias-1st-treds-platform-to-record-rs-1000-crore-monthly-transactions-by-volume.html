Which is India’s 1st TReDS platform to record Rs. 1000 crore monthly transactions by volume?

(A) e-PG Pathshala

(B) M1 Exchange

(C) ** Receivables Exchange of India Ltd

(D) PayKun

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -RXIL becomes first TReDS platform to cross monthly throughput of Rs 1, 000 cr. The government with the support of RBI, Ministry of Finance and Ministry of MSME has been promoting TReDS as an effective tool to address the working capital woes of MSMEs.

Concept note-2: -C2FO, India’s largest Early Pay platform, hosts over 50, 000 MSME suppliers and enterprises on its platform.

Concept note-3: -Credit and finance for MSMEs: The invoice volume uploaded on TReDS jumped from 8.61 lakh invoices involving Rs 19, 669.84 crore in FY21 to 17.33 lakh involving Rs 44, 111.80 crore uploaded in FY22.

Concept note-4: -With this approval, C2FO becomes only the 4th platform to get the license. Currently, there are three operating TReDS platforms in India, namely Invoicemart, M1xchange and RXIL.

Concept note-5: -TReDS is a platform that facilitates the financing / discounting of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) trade receivables through multiple financiers. These receivables may be owed by corporates as well as other buyers, such as government departments and public sector undertakings (PSUs).
