Which is the first city in India to use ropeway services in public transportation?

(A) Coimbatore

(B) Shimla

(C) Dehradun

(D) ** Varanasi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Varanasi became 1st Indian city to start Ropeway Service.

Concept note-2: -The ropeway service will cover a distance of 4.2 km aerially in the city of Varanasi. Nearly, 220 cable cars will run on the ropeway. Varanasi, commonly known as the Gateway of Eastern India, will cover the pilot project of the ropeway service worth Rs 424 crore.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s constituency Varanasi is set to become the first city in India to have a ropeway service as a mode of public transport. The ropeway will connect Cantt Railway Station (Varanasi Junction) and Church Square (Godauliya) to reduce traffic congestion.

Concept note-4: -1- India will be the third country in the world after Mexico City and Bolivia to have a ropeway for public transportation.
