Which Ministry has launched Central Athlete Injury Management System?

(A) Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

(B) ** Ministry of Sports

(C) None of these

(D) Ministry of AYUSH

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is a first-of-its-kind initiative by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports .

Concept note-2: -Chalmeda Anand Rao Institute of Medical Sciences - [CAIMS], Karimnagar.

Concept note-3: -Sport Rehabilitators treat injuries, alleviate pain and work on preventing re-injury. As well as treating sports injuries, Sport Rehabilitators treat everyday and occupational injuries, pain and musculoskeletal conditions affecting the joints, bones and muscles.

Concept note-4: -It is a process designed to minimize the loss associated with acute injury or chronic disease, to promote recovery, and to maximize functional capacity, fitness and performance.
