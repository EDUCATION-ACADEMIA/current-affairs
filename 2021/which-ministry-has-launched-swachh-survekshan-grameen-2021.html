Which ministry has launched Swachh Survekshan Grameen 2021?

(A) ** Ministry of Jal Shakti

(B) Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs

(C) Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs

(D) Ministry of Minority Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Urban Development, Government of India takes up the Swachh Survekshan in urban areas and the Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation in rural areas. The Quality Council of India (QCI) has been commissioned the responsibility of carrying out the assessment.

Concept note-2: -The Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation (DDWS) had started the “Swachh Survekshan Grameen (SSG)” in 2018, as an annual exercise through an independent survey agency to assess status of implementation of Swachh Bharat Mission Grameen [SBM(G)] on sanitation (swachhata) parameters for rural India.

Concept note-3: -Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs.

Concept note-4: -Q 3. Who conducts the Swachh Survekshan Grameen Survey? Ans. Jal Shakti Ministry conducts Swachh Survekshan Grameen Survey.

Concept note-5: -Earlier, the Jal Shakti Ministry launched Swachh Survekshan Grameen 2021 or rural cleanliness survey 2021 under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) Phase-II.
