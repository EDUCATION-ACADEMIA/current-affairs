Which nation has delivered the largest and most advanced warship to Pakistan?

(A) Russia

(B) ** China

(C) France

(D) US

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -China has delivered to Pakistan the largest and most advanced warship that Beijing has ever exported, Chinese state media reported.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which country has gifted the largest and the most advanced warship ‘Tughril’ to the Pakistan Navy? Notes: The Government of China has gifted its largest and the most advanced warship to Pakistan’s Navy to boost its naval powers. The war vessel is named as PNS Tughril by Pakistan.

Concept note-3: -The PNS Tughril is the first hull of four Type 054 frigates being constructed for the Pakistan Navy.

Concept note-4: -On 14th November the US Navy’s new aircraft carrier, USS Gerald R. Ford arrived in UK waters for her first visit to Europe. The 100, 000-ton ship is the largest, most expensive and arguably the most powerful warship ever built.

Concept note-5: -Recently, China handed over a warship to Pakistan. The report said Type 054A is the best frigate in service with the PLA Navy. Military sources said the ship has a fully loaded displacement of about 4, 000 metric tons and is equipped with advanced radars and missiles.
