Which nation has sought two million doses of Covishield vaccine from India?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) Nepal

(C) ** Brazil

(D) Maldives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MUMBAI: Two million doses of Covishield vaccine were flown to Brazil on Emirates Sky Cargo and another two million doses to Morocco on Royal Air Maroc from the Mumbai airport in the early hours of Friday, an airport spokesperson said.

Concept note-2: -No. There is no difference between the 1st and 2nd dose. Each dose has the same content of viral particles.

Concept note-3: -The vaccine is administered in two doses that are given three weeks apart. In December 2020, the UK became the first country in the world to approve this vaccine and began rolling out an initial 800, 000 doses at the start of the month, and is now in use worldwide.

Concept note-4: -China’s Sinovac Biotech nearly quadrupled sales in 2021, edging out Moderna to rank among the world’s top three vaccine makers by revenue.
