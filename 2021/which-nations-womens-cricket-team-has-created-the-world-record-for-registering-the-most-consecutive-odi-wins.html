Which nation’s women’s cricket team has created the world record for registering the most consecutive ODI wins?

(A) England

(B) New Zealand

(C) ** Australia

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Longest winning streak in ODI cricket: Australia women leading the charts.

Concept note-2: -England hold the Most Consecutive Home ODI Series Win record with 9 between 2016-2020.

Concept note-3: -Australia’s women’s team made history when they defeated New Zealand in the first ODI on Sunday. The six-wicket in the opening match of the three-match series, played at Mount Maunganui, was the 22nd consecutive win in the ODI format for the Australian women’s team.

Concept note-4: -The most consecutive cricket test match victories is 16 and was achieved by Australia twice, firstly from 14 October 1999 to 27 February 2001 and most recently from 26 December 2005 to 2 January 2008.

Concept note-5: -In that very game, Deepti Sharma scored 188 off 160 balls and it remains to be the highest individual score by an Indian woman in ODIs. India also registered their highest WODIs score, surpassing the 281/3 that they scored in Derby during the 2017 World Cup.
