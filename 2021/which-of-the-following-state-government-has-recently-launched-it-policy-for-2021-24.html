Which of the following state government has recently launched IT policy for 2021-24?

(A) Delhi

(B) Kerala

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Andhra Pradesh government has launched its new ‘AP Information Technology Policy 2021-24’ on June 30, 2021 to generate how many jobs over three years?

Concept note-2: -ITE&C Department - IT Promotion– Andhra Pradesh Electronics Policy 2021-24 – Orders – Issued. ORDER: The Indian market for electronics goods stood at USD 118.2 Billion in the year 2019-20 and is expected to quadruple to USD 400 Billion by 2025, driven by growth in income levels and higher disposable incomes.

Concept note-3: -Gujarat government has announced a new IT/ITeS policy for the next five years. The new policy was announced by Chief Minister Bhupendra Patel in Gandhinagar today. The new IT/ITeS policy aims to provide financial incentives up to 200 crore rupees to the entities willing to incur capital expenditures.
