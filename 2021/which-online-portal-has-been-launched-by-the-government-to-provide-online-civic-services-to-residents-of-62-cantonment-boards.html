Which online portal has been launched by the Government to provide online civic services to residents of 62 Cantonment Boards?

(A) e-Samuh

(B) ** e-Chhawani

(C) e-Basti

(D) e-Padaav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Defence Minister Rajnath Singh on Tuesday launched the E-Chhawani portal and mobile app that provides online civic services to over 20 lakh residents of 62 Cantonment Boards across the country.

Concept note-2: -eChhawani is an unified portal for 62 Cantonment Boards using which citizens have ready access to information regarding their cantonment board and are aware of the day to day activities of their cantonment board. Using eChhawani portal citizens can avail all civic services offered by respective Cantonment Board.

Concept note-3: -Raksha Mantri launches E-Chhawani portal & mobile app that provides online civic services to residents of 62 Cantonment Boards.

Concept note-4: -The Southern part of India contains 4 cantonments (Karnataka, Kerala, Telangana and Tamil Nadu each contain 1 cantonment).

Concept note-5: -A cantonment board is a civic administration body in India under control of the Ministry of Defence. The board comprises elected members besides ex-officio and nominated members as per the Cantonments Act, 2006. The term of office of a member of a board is five years.
