Which organisation has approved Rs 770 crore loan for Assam to improve river transportation in the state?

(A) ** World Bank

(B) New Development Bank

(C) IMF

(D) Asian Development Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is World Bank. The Inland Water Transport (IWT) Department has signed an MoU with the World Bank for a Rs 770 crore loan to improve river transportation in Assam. The central government would refund 80 per cent of the sanctioned amount and the state government would bear the remaining 20 per cent.

Concept note-2: -For the implementation of Assam Inland Water Transport Project, the Government of India (GoI), the Government of Assam and the World Bank have signed a $88 million loan agreement. The loan agreement seeks to modernize the Assam’s passenger ferry sector by improving the infrastructure and its services.

Concept note-3: -Assam Inland Water Transport was established in the year 1958.
