Which organisation has tested the Smart Anti-Airfield Weapon from Hawk-I aircraft?

(A) Ordnance Factory Board, Kolkata

(B) HAL

(C) BHEL

(D) ** DRDO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) Successfully Test Fires Smart Anti Airfield Weapon (SAAW) From Hawk-i Aircraft.

Concept note-2: -Description. The SAAW has been developed by the Research Centre Imarat (RCI), and other DRDO laboratories in collaboration with the Indian Air Force. It is a lightweight, high precision guided bomb designed to destroy ground targets, such as runways, bunkers, aircraft hangars and other reinforced structures.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which organisation has launched a smart anti-airfield weapon, along with Indian Air Force (IAF)? Notes: The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Air Force (IAF) have jointly carried out two flight tests of the indigenously-developed smart anti-airfield weapon.

Concept note-4: -Smart Anti - Airfield Weapon (SAAW) is long range, standoff, precision air-to-surface weapon.

Concept note-5: -The Hawk is a tandem-seat Aircraft for ground attack, flying training and weapon training. It has a low wing and an all-metal structure and is powered by an Adour Mk 871 turbofan engine.
