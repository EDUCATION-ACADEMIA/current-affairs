Which organization has launched a new mobile application for psycho-social wellness of students and parents?

(A) UGC

(B) ** CBSE

(C) Niti Aayog

(D) ICSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is CBSE Dost for Life. Central Board of Secondary Education has launched a new mobile application for psycho social wellness of students and parents.

Concept note-2: -CBSE launched ‘Dost For Life’ mobile app for psycho-social wellness of students!

Concept note-3: -The app goes beyond the annual exam and post result counselling route, with features such as counselling via chat box, tips on mental health and well-being, CBSE rap songs, audio-visuals messages, Covid protocols and a compendium of courses after +2.

Concept note-4: -Central Board of Secondary Education, CBSE has launched a new mo app for students and parents. The new app ‘Dost for Life’ is an exclusive psychological counselling app for students and parents of CBSE-affiliated schools.

Concept note-5: -UPSC Mains Q. Which institution released the ‘Dost For Life’ mobile application for mental well-being? Notes: The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has recently launched a new application for psycho-social wellness of students.
