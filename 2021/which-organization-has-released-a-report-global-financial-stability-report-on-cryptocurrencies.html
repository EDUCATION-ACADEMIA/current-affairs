Which organization has released a report Global Financial Stability Report on cryptocurrencies?

(A) Bank for International Settlements

(B) International Labour Organization

(C) ** International Monetary Fund

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Global Financial Stability Report (GFSR) is a semiannual report by the International Monetary Fund (IMF) that assesses the stability of global financial markets and emerging-market financing. It is released twice per year, in April and October.

Concept note-2: -Ans: The Global Financial Stability Report is released by the International Monetary Fund (IMF) twice in a year, in the months of June and October.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is International Monetary Fund. The Global Financial Stability Report provides an assessment of the global financial system and markets and addresses emerging market financing in a global context. It is a semiannual report by the International Monetary Fund (IMF).

Concept note-4: -The 26th edition of the Financial Stability Report was released by the Reserve Bank of India on December 29. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) publishes a financial stability report (FSR) twice a year.

Concept note-5: -Reserve Bank of India - Financial Stability Report.
