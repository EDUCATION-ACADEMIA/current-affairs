Which player has won the 2021 French Grand Prix?

(A) Sergio Perez

(B) Lewis Hamilton

(C) ** Max Verstappen

(D) Sebastian Vettel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Max Verstappen won the race for Red Bull Racing, beginning a streak of three consecutive race wins for him, and forming the third of five consecutive wins for Red Bull.

Concept note-2: -Following a Safety Car period at the Yas Marina Circuit, Red Bull’s Max Verstappen overtakes Lewis Hamilton on the final lap of the 2021 Formula 1 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix to take the race victory and win the 2021 drivers’ world championship.

Concept note-3: -Red Bull’s Max Verstappen won the French Grand Prix at Paul Ricard on Sunday, after his title rival Charles Leclerc crashed his Ferrari from the lead of the race.
