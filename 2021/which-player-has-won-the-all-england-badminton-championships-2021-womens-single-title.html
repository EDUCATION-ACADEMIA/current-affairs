Which player has won the All England Badminton Championships 2021 women’s single title?

(A) ** Nozomi Okuhara

(B) Akane Yamaguchi

(C) Yuta Watanabe

(D) Arisa Higashino

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Who won the women’s singles title at the recently concluded All England Badminton Championships? Answer. The answer is Akane Yamaguchi.

Concept note-2: -It’s the 2021 world champion’s 18th career title, a ninth tour title and a third Super 1000 title with so much more yet to come. Akane Yamaguchi is the Women’s Singles YONEX All England Champion!
