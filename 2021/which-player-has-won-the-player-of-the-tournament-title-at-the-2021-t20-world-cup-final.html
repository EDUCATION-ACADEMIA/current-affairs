Which player has won the Player of the Tournament title at the 2021 T20 World Cup final?

(A) Mitchell Marsh

(B) ** David Warner

(C) Kane Williamson

(D) Aaron Finch

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Marsh was acknowledged as the player of the match, while Warner won the Player of the Tournament.

Concept note-2: -In the final, Australia beat New Zealand by eight wickets to win their first T20 World Cup. Mitchell Marsh was named the Player of the Match, with David Warner being named the Player of the Tournament.
