Which road is to be widened in Kerala?

(A) Bodimettu-Kundannoor, Ernakulam

(B) ** S.N. Jn.-Poothotta

(C) Mumbai-Pune Highway

(D) Walayar-Thrissur-Edappally

Concept note-1: -The National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has set April 2025 as the deadline to complete widening of the Edappally-Ramanattukara stretch of NH 66 that connects Kochi and Kozhikode as a four or six-lane corridor. The widened NH will enable motorists to save over an hour in commuting time.
