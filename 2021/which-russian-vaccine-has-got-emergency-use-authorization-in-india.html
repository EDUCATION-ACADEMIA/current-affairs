Which Russian Vaccine has got emergency use authorization in India?

(A) AstraZeneca

(B) Pfizer-BioNTech

(C) Sinovac

(D) ** Sputnik V

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Moscow, August 25, 2021 – The Russian Direct Investment Fund (RDIF, Russia’s sovereign wealth fund) announces the approval of the Russian Sputnik V vaccine against coronavirus in the Republic of Indonesia.

Concept note-2: -Sputnik Light will add to India’s national vaccine portfolio and join Sputnik V, which was approved in the country in April 2021. India is the leading production hub for Sputnik V.

Concept note-3: -GEMCOVAC-19 is the very first mRNA vaccine developed in India and only third mRNA vaccine to be approved for COVID-19 in the world.
