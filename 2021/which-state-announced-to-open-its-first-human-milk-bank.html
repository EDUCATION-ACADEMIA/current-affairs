Which state announced to open its first human milk bank?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Kerala

(C) Goa

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In September 2013, “Yashoda” Human Milk Bank was established in D. Y. Patil Medical College, Hospital and Research Centre, Pimpri, Pune in Maharashtra. Established with the partnership of Rotary Club of Nariman Point, Mumbai, it is the first human milk bank established in a private medical college in India.

Concept note-2: -Odisha gets first human milk bank – ThePrint – PTI Feed.

Concept note-3: -This is a state-of-art facility that will be opened at the Ernakulam general hospital by the Health Minister of Kerala, KK Shailaja.

Concept note-4: -BENGALURU: Amrutha Dhare, the first state government-run human breast milk bank for newborns, is now up and running at Vani Vilas Hospital in the city. The facility will cater to newborns who are ill and in the neonatal ICU of the hospital. The milk bank was launched on Tuesday.
