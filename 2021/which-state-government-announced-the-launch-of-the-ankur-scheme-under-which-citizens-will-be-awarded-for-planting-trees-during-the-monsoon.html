Which state government announced the launch of the Ankur scheme under which citizens will be awarded for planting trees during the monsoon?

(A) Bihar

(B) ** Madhya Pradesh

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Madhya Pradesh government has announced the launch of ‘Ankur’, a scheme under which citizens will be awarded for planting trees during monsoon.

Concept note-2: -In Madhya Pradesh, Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan government has launched a scheme named ‘Ankur’ under which citizens will be awarded for planting trees during monsoon.

Concept note-3: -Madhya Pradesh Government Devaranya Scheme to promote Ayurveda - Madhya Pradesh Civil Services MPPSC.

Concept note-4: -MP LaunchPad Scheme 2023 is being invited by the Government of Madhya Pradesh. In this scheme, the state government will provide financial assistance to the youth to start their own business to make them self-reliant. The Department of Women and Child Development (WCD) will implement MP Launchpad Scheme.

Concept note-5: -Farmers are provided seedilings of the trees in government nursery at the rate of Rs 10 / seedling. If farmers maintain 50 percent of the plants purchased from the forest department in their lands for 3 years, then for this, the farmers will be given an incentive of Rs 60 per plant.
