Which state government has announced a government job to a family member of farmers killed in the farmers protest?

(A) Uttarakhand

(B) Delhi

(C) Haryana

(D) ** Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of 21 March 2021, according to Haryana Police, there are around 40, 000 committed protestors sitting at Singhu and Tikri at the Delhi border. The SKM has planned that 200 farmers will protest outside the Parliament every day during the monsoon session starting from 22 July 2021.

Concept note-2: -"We will hold a review meeting on 15 January. If the government doesn’t fulfil its promises, we may resume the protest, ” farmer leader Gurnam Singh Charuni told local media.

Concept note-3: -’Farmers Protest In Delhi’ - 545 News Result(s) Scores of farmers affiliated to the All India Kisan Congress on Friday gathered at the Jantar Mantar in Delhi to demand legal guarantee for MSP and compensation for the relatives of farmers killed in last year’s agitation.

Concept note-4: -The Samyukta Kisan Morcha (SKM) on Saturday sent a list of 702 farmers who are said to have “died” during protest against three contentious farm laws, to the government. Confirming the development, farmers’ leader Kishan Pal told IANS: “We have sent a list of 702 farmers who were martyred during the agitation."
