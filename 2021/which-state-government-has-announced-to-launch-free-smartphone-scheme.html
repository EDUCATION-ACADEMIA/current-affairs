Which state government has announced to launch ‘Free Smartphone Scheme’?

(A) Uttarakhand

(B) Jharkhand

(C) Chhattisgarh

(D) ** Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under this scheme UP Free Smartphone and Tablet Yojana, the UP government will distribute free smartphones and tablets to 68 lakh youth of the state. UP Free Smartphone Yojana 2021 is a total plan of 3000 thousand crores. Which can avail by all the youth belonging to rural and urban areas.

Concept note-2: -The UP government launched the ‘Free Smartphone Yojana on December 25, 2021. Under the scheme the state government will distribute smartphones and tablets to final year students of graduation and above. In phase I of the scheme around one lakh smartphones and tablets will be given to final year students of B.

Concept note-3: -In Rajasthan, the distribution of mobiles to 1.35 crore women with Chiranjeevi card will start after Diwali. Phones will be distributed after November 15th 2022, by setting up camps in each gram panchayat. These phones will be from Samsung, Nokia and Jio, which will be given with 3 years of data backup.
