Which state government has launched an Ayurvedic telemedicine facility for Corona patients?

(A) Odisha

(B) Bihar

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) ** Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Examples of telemedicine such as Medlife, Practo, Pharmeasy, Netmeds, and 1MG provide a wide range of healthcare services while pretending to be solely technological service providers to avoid health regulatory supervision.

Concept note-2: -The real need for telemedicine is in the remote hilly and tribal settlements.” “People in rural parts of India have to travel far even to visit primary health centres. However, these primary health centres are now getting connected to us through telemedicine.

Concept note-3: -The telemedicine service at AIMS is the first telemedicine centre in Kerala which was started in September 2002. This center was set up with the help of Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO), which selected AIMS for its pilot telemedicine project in the state of Kerala.
