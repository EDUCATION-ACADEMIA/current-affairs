Which state government has mandated a negative RT-PCR report for entry into the state?

(A) Kerala

(B) Karnataka

(C) Maharashtra

(D) ** Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maharashtra Passengers will either have to be fully vaccinated or compulsorily carry RTPCR Test certificate showing negative result within 72 hours before boarding for arrival into Maharashtra. Children below 5 yrs exempted from RTPCR test.

Concept note-2: -A COVID test is not required when arriving in India. Arrivals from China, Japan, Singapore, Hong Kong, South Korea, and Thailand must present a negative RT-PCR test no older than 72 hours. They must fill it out on this app.

Concept note-3: -They are neither required to produce RTPCR negative test report, nor would be quarantined, be it Home or Institutional.

Concept note-4: -All passengers traveling to the state of Gujarat from other states or UT mandatorily need a negative RT-PCR report, not older than 72 hours, from an ICMR approved laboratory.
