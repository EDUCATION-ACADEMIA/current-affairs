Which state has approved a scheme titled EBC Nestham?

(A) ** Andhra Pradesh

(B) Odisha

(C) Tealangana

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The chief minister of Andhra Pradesh YS Jagan Mohan Reddy, introduced the YSR EBC Nestham Scheme. Through this scheme, women from the upper class who are economically disadvantaged and are between the ages of 45 and 60 would receive financial help in the amount of Rs 45, 000.

Concept note-2: -YSR EBC Nestham Scheme was introduced by Andhra pradesh government on 25 January 2023. This programme will offer cash support of ₹45000. Women between 45-60 years of age and with less economic strength will benefit. There will be three phases to the delivery of this financial aid.

Concept note-3: -The upper-caste women candidates who belong to the community of Hindus and are economically unstable will be held through the scheme of YSR EBC Nestham 2023. These women will be receiving monetary benefits to support their living due to economical backwardness.
