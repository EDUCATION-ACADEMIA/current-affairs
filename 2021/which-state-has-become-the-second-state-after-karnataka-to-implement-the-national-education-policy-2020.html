Which state has become the second state after Karnataka to implement the National Education Policy 2020?

(A) ** Madhya Pradesh

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Haryana

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Madhya Pradesh becomes the second state after Karnataka to implement the National Education Policy 2020.

Concept note-2: -The Telangana State government has decided to implement the newly announced National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020) in the State. Maharashtra CM Uddhav Thackeray directs to appoint experts’ committee for implementation of new education policy.

Concept note-3: -Key Points. MP became the second state in India to implement NEP-2020 following Karnataka. This new education policy will break all the shackles and help students in exploring outside their boundaries.
