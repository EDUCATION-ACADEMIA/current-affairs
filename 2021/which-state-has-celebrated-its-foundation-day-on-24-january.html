Which state has celebrated its foundation day on 24 January?

(A) ** Uttar Pradesh

(B) West Bengal

(C) Kerala

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Uttar Pradesh is the center of Indian politics and is a major part of the Indian Independence Movement in modern Indian history. The state celebrates its foundation day on the 24th of January every year. This day is also known as Uttar Pradesh Day or Uttar Pradesh Diwas.

Concept note-2: -Uttar Pradesh is celebrating its foundation day today. The foundation day of state on January 24 is being celebrated for three days since 2018 with the participation of all government departments. The main theme of the ‘Uttar Pradesh Day’ 2023 is ‘Investment and Employment’ this year.

Concept note-3: -The state was formed on 30 March 1949 when Rajputana – the name adopted by the British Raj for its dependencies in the region – was merged into the Dominion of India. Its capital and largest city is Jaipur.
