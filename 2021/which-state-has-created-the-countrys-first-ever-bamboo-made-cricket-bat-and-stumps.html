Which state has created the country’s first-ever bamboo-made cricket bat and stumps?

(A) Assam

(B) Meghalaya

(C) ** Tripura

(D) Nagaland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tripura Develops India’s ‘First-Ever’ Bamboo Made Cricket Bats And Wickets - YouTube.

Concept note-2: -In collaboration with BCDI, Agartala, NECTAR carried out a pilot project on development of indigenous Bamboo Cricket bats and Stumps.

Concept note-3: -The product was designed in Tripura’s Bamboo and Cane Development Institute using locally available materials. The hard “Boom (local Name)” bamboo has been used to develop the bats.

Concept note-4: -Was it at Lord’s? Some historians believe that the first mention of a cricket bat through any form was made in 1624. Its original design was very much like a hockey stick.
