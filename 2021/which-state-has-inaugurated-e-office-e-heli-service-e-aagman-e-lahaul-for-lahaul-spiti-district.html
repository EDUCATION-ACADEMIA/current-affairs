Which state has inaugurated e-Office, e-Heli Service, e-Aagman, e-Lahaul for Lahaul Spiti district?

(A) Assam

(B) ** Himachal Pradesh

(C) Karnataka

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Himachal Pradesh Chief Minister Jai Ram Thakur virtually launched from Shimla e- Office, e-Heli Service, e-Aagman and e-Lahaul for the tribal Lahaul-Spiti district on Tuesday.

Concept note-2: -After the formation of Lahaul & Spiti into a district in 1960, Spiti was formed into a sub-division with its headquarter at Kaza. Lahaul and Spiti district was merged with Himachal Pradesh on 1 November 1966 on enactment of the Punjab Reorganisation Act.

Concept note-3: -In 1840, Maharaja Ranjit Singh took over Lahaul along with Kulu and ruled over it till 1846 when the area came under the sway of the British. From 1846 to 1940, Lahaul formed part of the Kulu sub-division of Kangra district and was administered through the local jagirdars/thakurs.

Concept note-4: -The Lahaul and Spiti district in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh consists of the two formerly separate districts of Lahaul and Spiti. The present administrative centre is Keylong in Lahaul. Before the two districts were merged, Kardang was the capital of Lahaul, and Dhankar the capital of Spiti.
