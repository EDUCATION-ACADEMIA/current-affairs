Which state has launched a mobile application named PROOF, to bring more transparency and accountability to the governance system?

(A) Haryana

(B) Bihar

(C) ** Jammu and Kashmir

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Manoj Sinha. Jammu and Kashmir Lieutenant Governor Manoj Sinha has launched PROOF ‘Photographic Record of On-site Facility’ app. It will bring more transparency and accountability to the governance system.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which State/UT has launched PROOF (Photographic Record of On-site Facility) app to monitor timely completion of projects? Notes: Jammu & Kashmir LG Manoj Sinha has launched PROOF (Photographic Record of On-site Facility) app to monitor timely completion of projects.

Concept note-3: -In a bid to bring more transparency and accountability to the governance system, the Lieutenant Governor of Jammu & Kashmir, Manoj Sinha launched the ‘PROOF’ app on 12 August 2021. PROOF is short for ‘Photographic Record of On-site Facility’. The app will help in monitoring and timely completion of projects.

Concept note-4: -Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: The Department of Rural Development and Panchayati raj, Jammu and Kashmir, under the e-governance initiative has launched an app ‘Jan Nigrani’.

Concept note-5: -By clicking assign button ddo can map that user to selected work. Selected user now able to use PROOF Mobile app and upload images and add comments of Work Progress. Click on Send for Approval to Send Data to DDo for Approval . this option will redirect you data from your mobile to PROOF website for DDO Approval .
