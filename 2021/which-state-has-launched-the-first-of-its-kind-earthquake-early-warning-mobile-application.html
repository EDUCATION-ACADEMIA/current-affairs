Which state has launched the first-of-its-kind earthquake early warning mobile application?

(A) Assam

(B) Haryana

(C) ** Uttarakhand

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. The state government of Uttarakhand has launched an earthquake alert app which is named ‘Uttarakhand Bhookamp Alert. ‘ This app has been developed by the Indian Institute of Technology-Roorkee and the Uttarakhand State Disaster Management Authority.

Concept note-2: -This app has been developed by Uttarakhand State Disaster Management Authority and Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee. This application uses Earthquake Early Warning (EEW), a real-time earthquake information system, which can detect the onset of an earthquake.
