Which state has topped in the Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana list with the highest number of bank accounts?

(A) Bihar

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Odisha

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Uttar Pradesh | Govt: At 7.93 crore, state has highest number of Jan Dhan accounts in country | Cities News, The Indian Express.

Concept note-2: -The world’s largest financial inclusion programme is primarily driven by public sector banks (PSBs) which account for 79% of total PMJDY accounts and 77.5% of total deposits, said the officials who asked not to be named.

Concept note-3: -The total balance in basic bank accounts under Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) reached ₹1.80-lakh crore. As on December 30, 2022, the total balance stood at ₹1, 80, 857 crore with 47.84 crore beneficiaries. Out of this, 26.54 crore are women.

Concept note-4: -Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal have 29% of the total deposits under the scheme, whereas Kerala and Goa became the first states in the country to have at least one basic bank account in every household.

Concept note-5: -Thus, Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana not only serves as an important example of Governance in Mission Mode but also demonstrates what a Government can achieve if it is committed to the welfare of the people.
