Which state has topped the IPF Smart Policing Index 2021 released by the Indian Police Foundation (IPF)?

(A) Gujarat

(B) Rajasthan

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) ** Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Andhra Pradesh Police has topped the ‘IPF Smart Policing’ Index 2021, among 29 states and Union Territories. Key points: Delhi Police has been ranked at seventh spot, among Indian states and union territories (UT).

Concept note-2: -About Smart Policing Index– The IPF Citizen Satisfaction Survey on SMART Policing, 2021, is a pioneering effort by the Indian Police Foundation (IPF) to gauge public perceptions about the quality of policing in India and the level of public trust in the police.

Concept note-3: -It measures public perceptions and citizen satisfaction in the survey. The level of satisfaction with the quality of policing was highest in Andhra Pradesh (8.11), Telangana (8.10), Assam (7.89). They are followed by Kerala, Sikkim and Mizoram.

Concept note-4: -According to a survey by Indian Police Foundation (IPF), Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Assam, Kerala and Sikkim are on top in the IPF Smart Policing Index 2021, having the highest levels of citizen satisfaction and trust.
