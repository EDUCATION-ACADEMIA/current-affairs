Which state is setting up a tea Park at Chhaygaon in Kamrup District?

(A) ** Assam

(B) Nagaland

(C) Manipur

(D) Mizoram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Assam is setting up a tea Park at Chayygaon in Kamrup District.

Concept note-2: -1 Where will the Assam government set up a Tea Park in the state? Ans. 1 The Assam state government will be setting up a Tea Park at Chayygaon in the Kamrup district of the state.

Concept note-3: -Monabarie Tea Estate at Biswanath District of Assam is Asia’s Largest Tea Estate. The tea estate is owned by the McLeod Russel India Limited, a part of Williamson Magor Group.

Concept note-4: -Dibrugarh district in Assam provides an opportunity to experience life in tea estates. It is remarkable as it has an incredible tea estate named Manohari Tea Estate. It is famous as the tea city of Assam as it is the portal to the three tea-producing districts that include Tinsukia, Dibrugarh, and Sivasagar.
