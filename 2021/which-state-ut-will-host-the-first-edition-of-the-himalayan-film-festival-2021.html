Which state/ UT will host the first edition of The Himalayan Film Festival 2021?

(A) ** Ladakh

(B) Haryana

(C) Jharkhand

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -20: The first edition of ‘The Himalayan Film Festival-2021’ (THFF) will begin from 24 to 28 September at Leh. The film festival is being organised by the Administration of Union Territory of Ladakh in collaboration with the Directorate of Film Festivals, Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, Government of India.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Ladakh. The Union Territory of Ladakh would be hosting the first edition of The Himalayan Film Festival 2021 (THFF) from September 24 to 28, 2021, in Leh capital city.

Concept note-3: -Agencies The festival is a part of the celebration of ‘Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav’ commemorating 75 years of India’s Independence. LEH: The first edition of the Himalayan Film Festival will begin in the Leh, capital city of Ladakh from September 24, officials said.

Concept note-4: -The 51st International Film Festival of India was held from 16 to 24 January 2021 in Goa.

Concept note-5: -It was later held in different cities of the country. Since 2004, starting from the 35th edition, Goa has become the permanent venue of the International Film Festival of India (IFFI). It is held in the month of November -December every year.
