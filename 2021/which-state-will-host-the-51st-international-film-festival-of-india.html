Which state will host the 51st International Film Festival of India?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Karnataka

(C) Kerala

(D) ** Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 51st International Film Festival of India was held from 16 to 24 January 2021 in Goa.

Concept note-2: -The 51st edition of the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) was held in Goa.

Concept note-3: -Bangladesh was the country in focus at the 51st International Film Festival of India held in Goa in January 2021. 51st International Film Festival of India (IFF) has announced a line-up of programs that will be showcased on the Over-the-top (OTT) platform. The festival was held in Goa from 16th to 24th January 2021.

Concept note-4: -The Jury will comprise Pablo Cesar (Argentina) as Chairman, Prasanna Vithanage (Sri Lanka), Abu Bakr Shawky (Austria), Priyadarshan (India) and Ms. Rubaiyat Hossain (Bangladesh).
