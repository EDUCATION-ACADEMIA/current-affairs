Which state/UT has launched the Lab on Wheels program for poor students?

(A) ** Delhi

(B) Ladakh

(C) Assam

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Delhi Technological University . With an aim to provide quality education to government school students, the Delhi govt has launched a ‘Lab on Wheels’.

Concept note-2: -’Lab Built on Wheels’ launched in Bengaluru.

Concept note-3: -The lab will impart science education to students in Delhi government schools and under privileged children. The Minister also inaugurated DTU’s new classroom complex Pragya Bhawan and a new centre for the Department of Design.

Concept note-4: -Lab-on-Wheels is a mobile setup that brings science experiments and interactive models to children from government schools or learning centers throughout the academic year. Mode of transport (either a van or a two-wheeler) is decided based on the operation location and number of schools serviced.
