Which team has won the domestic cricket tournament Vijay Hazare Trophy 2020-21?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Railways

(D) Baroda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai won the tournament, to take their fourth title, beating Uttar Pradesh by six wickets in the final.

Concept note-2: -Himachal Pradesh became the latest winner of the Vijay Hazare Trophy winners list after defeating Uttar Pradesh in the 2021 season.

Concept note-3: -The 2022–23 Vijay Hazare Trophy was the 21st season of the Vijay Hazare Trophy, a List A cricket tournament in India. It was played from 12 November to 2 December 2022. Himachal Pradesh were the defending champions. Saurashtra won the final match against Maharashtra by 5 wickets to clinch their second domestic title.

Concept note-4: -Tamil Nadu is the most successful team having won the trophy five times.

Concept note-5: -Saurashtra won their maiden Ranji Trophy title on Friday on the back of their first innings lead against Bengal in the final played at the Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium. It is their first-ever win in the history of India’s premier domestic competition.
