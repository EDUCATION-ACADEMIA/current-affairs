Which telecom company has partnered with Qualcomm to launch the 5G service in India?

(A) ** Airtel

(B) Live

(C) VI

(D) Jio

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliance Jio, the nation’s largest telecom operator, has partnered with Qualcomm to develop 5G solutions for India, Mukesh Ambani said on Monday.

Concept note-2: -Jio started providing the 5G network from October, 2022 in four cities - Mumbai, Delhi, Varanasi, and Kolkata.

Concept note-3: -Global chip design company, Qualcomm Inc, is working closely with Reliance Jio to provide its chipset platforms for the latter’s tryst with the Open RAN 5G network, say sources. Reliance, which is building the network indigenously, has already built its 5G stacks, including the core and the radios.

Concept note-4: -After collaborating with world’s leading technology companies like Meta, Google, Microsoft, Ericsson and others, Reliance has now announced its latest partnership with Qualcomm. This partnership between Qualcomm and Jio will develop 5G solutions for India, Mukesh Ambani said.
