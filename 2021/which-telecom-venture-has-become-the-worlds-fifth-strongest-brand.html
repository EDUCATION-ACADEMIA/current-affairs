Which telecom venture has become the world’s fifth strongest brand?

(A) ** Reliance Jio

(B) Vodafone

(C) Airtel

(D) BSNL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Reliance-Jio. According to the Brand Finance Global 500 Ranking 2021, Reliance-Jio is the world’s 5th strongest brand.

Concept note-2: -With a Brand Strength Index of 90.2, Jio is the only brand of India among the world’s strongest 25 brands, according to the Brand Finance listing.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Indian company has emerged as the 5th strongest brand, as per the Brand Finance’s Global 500 ranking? Notes: As per the Brand Finance’s Global 500 ranking, India’s Reliance Jio owned by Mukesh Ambani has emerged as the fifth strongest brand globally.

Concept note-4: -jio: Jio is India’s strongest brand, ranked ninth globally: Report - The Economic Times.
