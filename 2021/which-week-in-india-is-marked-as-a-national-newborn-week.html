Which Week in India is marked as a National Newborn Week?

(A) November 14 to 20

(B) ** November 15 to 21

(C) November 07 to 13

(D) November 01 to 07

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Newborn Week is being observed by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (MoHFW), Government of India from 15th through 21st November to reinforces the importance of newborn health as a key priority area and reiterates its commitment steered at the highest level.

Concept note-2: -National Newborn Week 2022: Theme The theme of this week is ‘Safety, Quality and Nurturing Care – Every Newborn’s Birth Right’.

Concept note-3: -A neonate is a baby who is 4 weeks old or younger.

Concept note-4: -In India, National Newborn Week is celebrated every year from 15 to 21 November. The theme of this week is ‘Safety, Quality and Nurturing Care - Every Newborn’s Birth Right’.
