(A) ** Neena Gupta

(B) Anurag Thakur

(C) NS Tomar

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Neena Gupta’s autobiography Sach Kahun Toh gave a deep insight into the roller coaster life of the actor, who is enjoying the second innings of her career.

Concept note-2: -Many, many thoughts on Neena Gupta’s autobiography ‘Sach Kahun Toh’ published by Penguin India. She’s led an eventful life, but her candidness is not devoid of self-awareness.
