Who has announced the delivery of the first batch of Light Bullet Proof Vehicles to the IAF?

(A) Maruti Suzuki

(B) Tata Motors

(C) Bajaj Auto

(D) ** Ashok Leyland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ashok Leyland Ltd has announced the delivery of the first batch of light bullet proof vehicles (LBPV) to the Indian Air Force (IAF).

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which company has delivered the first lot of light bulletproof vehicles to the Indian Air Force, in collaboration with Lockheed Martin? Notes: Ashok Leyland, which is a flagship firm of the Hinduja group, has delivered the first lot of light bulletproof vehicles to the Indian Air Force.

Concept note-3: -Check out India’s first bullet proof Toyota Fortuner Legender. The requirement for making a car bullet proof is quite common, especially among the political ranks. One can consider it as an occupational hazard. For self-defence, many politicians and other VIPs convert their vehicles into a wall of armour.
