Who has applied for Team India’s head coach position?

(A) ** Rahul Dravid

(B) Sachin Tendulkar

(C) MS Dhoni

(D) VVS Laxman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On October 26, former India captain Rahul Dravid had applied for the position of head coach of the Men in Blue senior team.

Concept note-2: -Dravid had applied for the position the same day. BCCI President Sourav Ganguly welcomed Rahul Dravid as the Head Coach of India’s senior men team.

Concept note-3: -Laxman, 48, has filled in as the head coach of the Indian team in Dravid‘s absence. He was with the team for the bilateral series against Ireland in June 2022, as well as for the first T20I against England and the short tour of Zimbabwe.

Concept note-4: -India’s bowling coach Paras Mhambrey also suggested R Ashwin will be India’s main spinner more often than Yuzvendra Chahal because of his batting.
