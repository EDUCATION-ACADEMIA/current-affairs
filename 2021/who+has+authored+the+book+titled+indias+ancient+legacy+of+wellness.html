Who has authored the book titled “India’s Ancient Legacy of Wellness”?

(A) Amitav Ghosh

(B) ** Rekha Chaudhari

(C) Subhadra Sen Gupta

(D) Avtar Singh Bhasin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Rekha Chaudhari. Dr Rekha Chaudhari’s book ‘India’s Ancient Legacy of Wellness’ was launched on 15 December 2021. The book reveals how important it is for human beings to be close to nature which helps to revive and recharge to do productive work.

Concept note-2: -10.12. 2021: Governor of Maharashtra Bhagat Singh Koshyari released a book India’s Ancient Legacy of wellness- Tribal treasures of pure knowledge written by Dr. Rekha Chaudhari at Raj Bhavan, Mumbai.

Concept note-3: -The Vedas are considered as the oldest surviving literature of Indian subcontinent. The Vedas were believed to be from the pre-vedic period.
