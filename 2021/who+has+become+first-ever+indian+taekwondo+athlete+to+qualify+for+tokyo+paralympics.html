Who has become First-ever Indian Taekwondo Athlete to Qualify for Tokyo Paralympics?

(A) ** Aruna Tanwar

(B) Tarun Dhillon

(C) Pramod Bhagat

(D) Krishna Nagar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chandigarh University’s Aruna Tanwar becomes India’s first ever Taekwondo athlete to qualify for Paralympics.

Concept note-2: -Following in the footsteps of her elder sisters from Para TT would be Aruna Tanwar, the 21-year-old sole Para Taekwondo representative at the Paralympic Games from India. Haryana-based Aruna will participate in the Women’s under 49kg K44 category.

Concept note-3: -Aman Kumar Kadyan He competes in -54kg and currently holds -18 Rank in the world.
