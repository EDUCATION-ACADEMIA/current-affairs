Who has become India’s youngest female Pilot?

(A) ** Ayesha Aziz

(B) Ritu Rathee Taneja

(C) Akshita Arora

(D) Meghna Arora

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maitri Patel from Surat has created history by becoming the youngest commercial pilot in India. Touching the skies at the young age of 19, this young pilot turned every stone to make her dreams come true.

Concept note-2: -Maitri Patel: Meet The Youngest Commercial Pilot of India.
