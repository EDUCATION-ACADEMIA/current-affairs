Who has become the first woman fighter pilot in the Indian Air Force (IAF) from Rajouri district, J & K.?

(A) Shivangi Sharma

(B) ** Mawya Sudan

(C) Preeti Singh

(D) Manya Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mawya Sudan was commissioned as a fighter pilot at the Airforce Academy in Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -Meet Mawya Sudan, The First IAF Woman Fighter Pilot From Jammu & Kashmir.

Concept note-3: -Squadron Leader Avani Chaturvedi (born 27 October 1993) is an Indian pilot from Rewa district, Madhya Pradesh. She was declared as the first woman combat pilot along with two of her cohorts, Mohana Singh Jitarwal, and Bhawana Kanth. The trio was inducted into the Indian Air Force fighter squadron in June 2016.

Concept note-4: -Christine Mau became the first female F-35 pilot when she completed a flight in the Lightning II.
