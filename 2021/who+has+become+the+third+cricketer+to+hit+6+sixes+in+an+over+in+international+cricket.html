Who has become the third cricketer to hit 6 sixes in an over in international cricket?

(A) ** Kieron Pollard

(B) Chris Gayle

(C) Joe Root

(D) Steve Smith

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pollard became the first player to hit six sixes in an international over since Singh in 2007, while he was also just the third player to do it in international cricket following Yuvraj and Herschelle Gibbs.

Concept note-2: -Yuvraj Singh became the second batter, after Herschelle Gibbs, to hit six sixes in an over in international cricket.

Concept note-3: -Kieron Pollard hits 6 sixes in an over, only 3rd batsman to do so in international cricket.

Concept note-4: -Prithvi Shaw and Ajinkya Rahane are the only two Indian cricketers to hold the 6 balls 6 fours record in IPL. The fast-paced Indian Premier League pit batsmen against the best of talents from India’s domestic circuit as well as the world circuit. Hence, hitting six boundaries in consecutive balls is no mean feat.
