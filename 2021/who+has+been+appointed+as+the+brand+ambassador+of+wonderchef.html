Who has been appointed as the brand ambassador of Wonderchef?

(A) Akshay Kumar

(B) Alia Bhatt

(C) ** Kriti Sanon

(D) Virat Kohali

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Our campaign enhances this positioning where Kriti involves her friend in co-creating delicious recipes while using Wonderchef appliances.

Concept note-2: -NEW DELHI: Skechers South Asia Pvt. Ltd, a subsidiary of Skechers USA, has appointed actor Kriti Sanon as the new brand ambassador for Skechers India. Sanon will be the face of the fashion and lifestyle categories for the brand and appear in its upcoming campaign for a line of streetwear sneakers.

Concept note-3: -Wonderchef is a leader in cookware and kitchen appliances that enable our customers to cook healthy, tasty food with convenience. German standards of quality, Italian designs, & constant innovation are the hallmarks of Wonderchef.

Concept note-4: -Is Wonderchef Indian Brand? Yes. Wonderchef is based out of Mumbai, India.

Concept note-5: -Driven by the passion for making Kitchen the center of wellness and goodness in homes, Wonderchef was created by two enthusiasts, Mr. Ravi Saxena and Chef Sanjeev Kapoor in 2009. They believe the kitchen is the ‘heart of the home’, as it enables them every day to create lasting memories with the food.
