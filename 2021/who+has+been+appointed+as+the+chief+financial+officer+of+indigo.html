Who has been appointed as the chief Financial Officer of IndiGo?

(A) Abhinav Sawant

(B) Pranav Mishra

(C) ** Jiten Chopra

(D) Karan Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -InterGlobe Aviation Ltd, which operates India’s biggest carrier IndiGo has appoined Gaurav Negi as Chief Financial Officer effective April 01, 2022. Negi is IndiGo’s fourth finance chief in six years.

Concept note-2: -IndiGo appoints Gaurav Negi as CFO replacing Jiten Chopra - BusinessToday.

Concept note-3: -Abhijit DasGupta. Mr. Abhijit DasGupta is an experienced veteran in the aviation industry with professional experience of over 20 years. He has functional expertise in Long Term Network and Fleet Strategy, Network Planning and Analysis, Schedule Management, Airline Partnerships and Strategic Regulatory Affairs.

Concept note-4: -NEW DELHI : InterGlobe Aviation Limited-operated IndiGo has appointed Gaurav Negi, currently a senior vice president with the company, as its chief financial officer (CFO) following the resignation of Jiten Chopra, the country’s largest domestic airline said in a statement on Tuesday.

Concept note-5: -Between 2016 and 2022, IndiGo, which commands a domestic market share of over 50 percent, has seen four people, including Negi, take up the position of CFO. Murmurs about what such frequent changes meant for the company’s financial health have been heard for a while.
