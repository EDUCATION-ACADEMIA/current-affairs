Who has been appointed as the new Secretary-General of the United Nations Conference on Trade and Development?

(A) ** Rebeca Grynspan

(B) Maria Eugenia Casar

(C) Arnoldo Lopez Echandi

(D) Enrique Iglesias

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rebeca Grynspan, of Costa Rica, was appointed the eighth Secretary-General of United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD) on 13 September 2021. She is the first woman to hold this position in the history of the organization.

Concept note-2: -United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD) The General Assembly confirmed the nomination on 11 June 2021. Ms. Grynspan will succeed Mukhisa Kituyi of Kenya to whom the Secretary-General is deeply grateful for his commitment and dedicated service in leading UNCTAD.

Concept note-3: -Last Updated: Oct 19, 2022 Views: 423910. António Guterres is the current Secretary-General of the United Nations. He is the ninth Secretary-General, his term began 1 January 2017.
