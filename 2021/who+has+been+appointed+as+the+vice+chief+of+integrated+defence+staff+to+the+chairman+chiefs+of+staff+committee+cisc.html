Who has been appointed as the Vice Chief of Integrated Defence Staff to the Chairman Chiefs of Staff Committee (CISC)?

(A) ** Atul Kumar Jain

(B) Ajit Kumar P

(C) Arun Prakash

(D) Anil Kumar Chawla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chief of Integrated Defence Staff to the Chairman, Chiefs of Staff Committee (CISC) Air Marshal BR Krishna paying homage to the fallen heroes at the National War Memorial in New Delhi on the occasion of 7th Armed Forces Veterans Day on January 14, 2023.

Concept note-2: -VICE ADMIRAL SANJAY J SINGH, AVSM, NM.

Concept note-3: -AIR MARSHAL BR KRISHNA PVSM AVSM SC ADC CHIEF OF INTEGRATED DEFENCE STAFF TO THE CHAIRMAN, COSC.

Concept note-4: -Following the death of the late CDS Bipin Rawat in a helicopter crash in December 2021, the government reverted to the previous system as a stop-gap arrangement and appointed General Manoj Mukund Naravane as Chairman COSC.
