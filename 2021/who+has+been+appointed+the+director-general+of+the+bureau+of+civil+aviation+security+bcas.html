Who has been appointed the Director-General of the Bureau of Civil Aviation Security (BCAS)?

(A) ** Nasir Kamal

(B) Avinash Sinha

(C) Yogesh Kataria

(D) Shubam Anand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Zulfiqar Hasan, a 1988 batch IPS officer of West Bengal cadre, has been appointed as the Director General of Bureau of Civil Aviation Security (BCAS) in the Ministry of Civil Aviation. BCAS is a wing of the Civil Aviation Ministry looking after civil aviation security.

Concept note-2: -Usha Padhee, joint secretary in the Ministry of Civil Aviation, has been given additional charge as Director-General, Bureau of Civil Aviation Security (BCAS). She is the first woman and third IAS officer to have been appointed to the post.

Concept note-3: -The Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) is the regulatory body in the field of Civil Aviation, primarily dealing with safety issues. It is responsible for regulation of air transport services to/from/within India and for enforcement of civil air regulations, air safety, and airworthiness standards.
