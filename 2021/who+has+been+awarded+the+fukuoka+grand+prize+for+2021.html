Who has been awarded the Fukuoka Grand Prize for 2021?

(A) AR Rahman

(B) Romila Thapar

(C) ** Palagummi Sainath

(D) Ramchandra Guha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Palagummi Sainath. The Fukuoka Grand Prize for 2021 has been given to journalist Palagummi Sainath. He is a dedicated journalist who has persisted in looking into India’s poor farming villages and documenting the reality of the people who live there.
