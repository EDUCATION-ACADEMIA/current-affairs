Who has been elected as the President of Central Republic of Africa?

(A) Donald Kaberuka

(B) Ange Felix

(C) ** Faustin Touadera

(D) Andre Kolingba

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current President of the Central African Republic is Faustin-Archange Touadéra, since 30 March 2016.

Concept note-2: -David Dacko (French pronunciation: [david dako]; 1927 – 21 November 2003) was a Central African politician who served as the first president of the Central African Republic from 14 August 1960 to 1 January 1966, and 3rd President from 21 September 1979 to 1 September 1981.

Concept note-3: -Faustin-Archange Touadéra (French: [fostɛ̃ aʁkɑ̃ʒ twadeʁa]; born 21 April 1957) is a Central African politician and academic who has been President of the Central African Republic since March 2016.

Concept note-4: -The people of the Central African Republic range from the hunting-and-gathering forest Pygmy peoples, the Aka, to state-forming groups such as the Zande and Nzakara.
