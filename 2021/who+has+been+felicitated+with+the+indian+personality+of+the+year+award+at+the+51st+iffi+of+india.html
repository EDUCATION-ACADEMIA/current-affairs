Who has been felicitated with the Indian Personality of the Year Award at the 51st IFFI of India?

(A) ** Biswajit Chatterjee

(B) Kamin Kalev

(C) Tarun Majumdar

(D) Mala Sinha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is March 2021. The 51st International Film Festival of India has conferred Biswajit Chatterjee, veteran actor, producer of Hindi and Bengali cinema, with the Indian Personality of the Year Award.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Biswajit Chatterjee. Biswajit Chatterjee won the ‘Indian Personality of the Year Award’ during the 51st International Film Festival. He is a veteran actor, producer, director and singer of Hindi and Bengali cinema.
