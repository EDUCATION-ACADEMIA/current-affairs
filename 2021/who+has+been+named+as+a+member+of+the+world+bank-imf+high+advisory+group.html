Who has been named as a member of the World Bank-IMF High Advisory Group?

(A) ** Montek Singh Ahluwalia

(B) Jayant Kumar Dash

(C) Shashi V Vempatti

(D) Ram Vinay Sahi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The group will also include Gita Gopinath, Economic Counsellor and Director of the Research Department at the IMF, among others.

Concept note-2: -Who funds the IMF? IMF funds come from three sources: member quotas, multilateral and bilateral borrowing agreements.

Concept note-3: -He currently holds the position of Distinguished Fellow at the Centre for Social and Economic Progress - a New Delhi based think tank.

Concept note-4: -The International Monetary Fund (IMF) and the World Bank are institutions in the United Nations system. They share the same goal of raising living standards in their member countries.

Concept note-5: -International Monetary Fund is a separate institution and not a part of the World Bank Group.
