Who has been named chef de cabinet by the incoming UN general assembly president, Maldivian foreign minister Abdulla Shahid?

(A) ** K Nagaraj Naidu

(B) Sanjay Tripathi

(C) Rohit Negi

(D) Kuldeep Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abdulla Shahid (Dhivehi: ; born 26 May 1962) is Maldivian politician who served as President of the 76th United Nations General Assembly between 2021 and 2022. Shahid is the first Maldivian politician to hold that post. He has been serving as Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maldives since 2018.

Concept note-2: -Abdulla Shahid, President of the 76th session of the United Nations General Assembly, addresses the closing of the session.

Concept note-3: -President of the 77th Session of the United Nations General Assembly, Csaba Korosi arrived in New Delhi today on a three-day visit to India. This is his first bilateral visit to any country since he assumed UNGA Presidency in September last year.

Concept note-4: -Event: 76th Session of the UN General Assembly (UNGA 76) | SDG Knowledge Hub | IISD. The 76th session of the UN General Assembly (UNGA 76) opened on Tuesday, 14 September 2021. On that day, Abdulla Shahid of the Maldives, was sworn in as the General Assembly President and he opened the 76th session.
