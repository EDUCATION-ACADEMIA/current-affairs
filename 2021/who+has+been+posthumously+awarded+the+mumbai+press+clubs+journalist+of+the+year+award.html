Who has been posthumously awarded the Mumbai Press Club’s ‘Journalist of the Year’ award?

(A) ** Danish Siddiqui

(B) Shujaat Bukhari

(C) Isravel Moses

(D) Rakesh Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Danish Siddiqui’s wife Frederike Siddiqui received the award. “He was a man with a magical eye and was rightly regarded as one of the foremost photojournalists of this era.

Concept note-2: -TNIE-Kerala chief photographer T P Sooraj wins Redink Award for ‘The Big Picture’ Instituted by the Mumbai Press Club, the Redink Awards honour excellence in Indian journalism.

Concept note-3: -Thayil Jacob Sony George, a Padma Bhushan awardee, won the lifetime achievement award and Dainik Bhaskar Group’s national editor Om Gaur won the journalist of the year award.

Concept note-4: -The winning entries in each category will be awarded a total cash prize of Rs. 1 Lakh, a trophy, and a citation.
