Who has received knighthood at Windsor Castle, London?

(A) Max Verstappen

(B) Yuki Tsunoda

(C) Carlos Sainz Jr.

(D) ** Lewis Hamilton

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sir Lewis Hamilton receives knighthood at Windsor Castle.

Concept note-2: -The Clive Llyod knighthood investiture was performed in January 2022 by the Duke of Cambridge at Windsor Castle. Overall Clive Llyod played in 110 Tests and 87 One-Day Internationals and is one of the most successful captains of all time. He led West Indies to win the World Cup in 1975 and 1979.

Concept note-3: -The honour is awarded to members who have made major contributions to any activity, usually at national level. Per tradition, knighthoods and damehoods are presented with a touch of a sword by the King or Queen.

Concept note-4: -Max Verstappen is now an Officer in the Order of Orange-Nassau. Max Verstappen has been given the Dutch equivalent to a knighthood, being made an Officer in the Order of Orange-Nassau by the Netherlands.
