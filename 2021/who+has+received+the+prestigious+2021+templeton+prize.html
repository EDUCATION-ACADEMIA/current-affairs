Who has received the prestigious 2021 Templeton Prize?

(A) Stuart James

(B) Beckam Franklin

(C) David Lily

(D) ** Jane GoodAll

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Jane Goodall. Jane Goodall, the world-renowned primatologist and conservationist, has won the 2021 Templeton Prize. She is only the fourth woman to win the award, established by the late investor and philanthropist Sir John Templeton.

Concept note-2: -Frank Wilczek, a Nobel Prize-winning theoretical physicist and author, and professor at four universities among them Stockholm University, is awarded the 2022 Templeton Prize.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Frank Wilczek. Dr. Frank Wilczek, a Nobel Prize-winner theoretical physicist and author, receives the 2022 Templeton Prize. The Templeton Prize, valued at more than $1.3 million, is one of the world’s largest annual individual awards.

Concept note-4: -In Memoriam: Today, we remember our inaugural Templeton Prize laureate, Mother Teresa, who founded India’s Missionaries of Charity and gained recognition for her extraordinary efforts to help the homeless and neglected children of Kolkata.
