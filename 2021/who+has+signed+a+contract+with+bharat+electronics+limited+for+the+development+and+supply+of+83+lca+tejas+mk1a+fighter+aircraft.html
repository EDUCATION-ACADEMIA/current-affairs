Who has signed a contract with Bharat Electronics Limited for the development and supply of 83 LCA Tejas Mk1A fighter aircraft?

(A) Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited

(B) ** Hindustan Aeronautics Limited

(C) Defence Research and Development Laboratory

(D) Bharat Earth Movers Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s state-owned Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL ) has signed a contract with Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) for the development and supply of 20 types systems for the Tejas light combat aircraft (LCA) Mk1A programme.

Concept note-2: -BENGALURU: Defence PSU Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) on Thursday signed a Rs 2, 400 crore contract with Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) for development and supply of 20 types systems for the LCA Tejas Mk1A programme.

Concept note-3: -This leaves just four countries in total – Malaysia, Egypt, Argentina and the United States – that could currently be considered potential customers of the Tejas aircraft.

Concept note-4: -The government had informed the Lok Sabha on Friday that Malaysia is procuring 18 Tejas fighter jets while the US, Argentina, Australia, Egypt, Indonesia and Philippines too have shown interest to procure the light combat aircraft (LCA).

Concept note-5: -: The Indian Air Force (IAF) and the Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) on Thursday concluded a ₹6, 800 crore contract for 70 HTT-40 indigenous Basic Trainer Aircraft (BTA).
