Who has taken additional charge as the CRPF DG?

(A) Iqroop Singh

(B) Manoj Pande

(C) ** Kuldiep Singh

(D) Raj Shukla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CRPF chief SL Thaosen takes additional charge of Border Security Force DG.

Concept note-2: -Thaosen, who has served in Special Protection Group, was appointed CRPF chief in October. CRPF Director-General Sujoy Lal Thaosen took the “additional” charge of the Border Security Force (BSF) on Saturday, following the retirement of incumbent Pankaj Kumar Singh.

Concept note-3: -SL Thaosen took over charge of BSF DG from Pankaj Kumar Singh, a 1988-batch IPS officer. SL Thaosen from Assam will hold the additional charge of BSF DG till further orders.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi: The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the proposal of the Ministry of Home Affairs for the appointment of Kuldiep Singh (IPS) as Director-General of the Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF).

Concept note-5: -NEW DELHI: Senior Indian Police Service (IPS) officers Sujoy Lal Thaosen and Anish Dayal Singh have been appointed as the new directors general of the CRPF and ITBP respectively, a government order said Saturday.
