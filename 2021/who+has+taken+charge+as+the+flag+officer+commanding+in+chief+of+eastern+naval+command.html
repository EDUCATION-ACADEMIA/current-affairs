Who has taken charge as the Flag Officer Commanding in Chief of Eastern Naval Command?

(A) Robin K. Dhowan

(B) ** Ajendra Bahadur Singh

(C) Deepak Singh

(D) Atul Kumar Jain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vice Admiral Biswajit Dasgupta assumed charge as the Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Eastern Naval Command (ENC) on 01 December 2021.

Concept note-2: -The Eastern Fleet was constituted on 1 November 1971. The Fleet is commanded by a Rear Admiral with the title Flag Officer Commanding Eastern Fleet (FOCEF). Rear Admiral Gurcharan Singh is the current FOCEF, who took over on 30 November 2022.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Biswajit Dasgupta. Vice Admiral Biswajit Dasgupta assumed charge as the Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief (FOC-in-C), Eastern Naval Command (ENC) on 1 December 2021. He is a recipient of the Ati Vishisht Seva Medal and Vishisht Seva Medal for distinguished service.

Concept note-4: -Vice Admiral M.A. Hampiholi assumed charge as the 29th Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief of the Southern Naval Command, Kochi, on 30 November 2021. Hampiholi was the commandant of the Indian Naval Academy, Ezhimala, prior to taking over this appointment. Hampiholi took over from Vice Admiral Anil Kumar Chawla.
