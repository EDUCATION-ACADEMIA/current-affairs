Who has virtually inaugurated the Central Scrutiny Centre and Investor Education and Protection Fund Authority’s Mobile App?

(A) Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’

(B) ** Nirmala Sitharaman

(C) Raj Nath Singh

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nirmala Sitharaman. These are the Central Scrutiny Centre (CSC) and Investor Education and Protection Fund Authority’s (IEPFA) Mobile App.

Concept note-2: -Bharat Vidya is a first-of-its-kind online platform, which will offer both free and paid courses covering various aspects of Indology.

Concept note-3: -The Union Budget app is free to download on Google Play and Apple App Store. The Union Budget app gets updated after the Finance Minister completes her presentation. The app was launched in 2021 for hassle-free access to 14 budget-related documents.

Concept note-4: -Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman will continue her tradition of presenting a paperless budget for the third year in a row. Since 2021, the Modi government unveils budget on ‘Union Budget Mobile App’ that allows Members of Parliament and the general public to access the annual financial statement.

Concept note-5: -Investor Education and Protection Fund Authority(IEPFA)
