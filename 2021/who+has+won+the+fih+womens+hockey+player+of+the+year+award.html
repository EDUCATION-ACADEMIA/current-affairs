Who has won the FIH women’s Hockey Player of the Year award?

(A) Savita Punia

(B) Rani Rampal

(C) Navjot Kaur

(D) ** Gurjit Kaur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Felice Albers of Netherlands is women’s Player of the Year.

Concept note-2: -The Netherlands’ Felice Albers and India’s Harmanpreet Singh have been named the International Hockey Federation (FIH) Player of the Year for the 2021-2022 season in the women’s and men’s competitions respectively.

Concept note-3: -FIH Player of the Year 2021-22: India’s Harmanpreet Singh wins men’s award. The Indian hockey star also won the award last year. Harmanpreet Singh holds the record for the most goals scored in an FIH Pro League season.

Concept note-4: -Mumtaz Khan was India’s top scorer in the 2022 women’s junior hockey World Cup and at the FIH Hockey5s Lausanne. Teenager Mumtaz Khan was named the FIH Rising Star of the Year 2021-22 in the women’s category, the international hockey federation (FIH) announced on Tuesday.

Concept note-5: -GENEVA, NY – Kristiina Castagnola of Rowan has been chosen as the 2022 National Field Hockey Coaches Association Division III Player of the Year, putting a finishing touch on her long list of accomplishments that solidifies her status as one of the most decorated student-athletes in Prof history.
