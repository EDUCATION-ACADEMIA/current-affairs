Who has won the gold medal at the South Asian Wushu Championship?

(A) Pooja Kadian

(B) ** Aniyan Midhun

(C) Poonam Khatri

(D) Narender Grewal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aniyan Midhun, popularly known as “Son of the Arabian Sea” (born 18 June 1992), is an Indian Wushu practitioner. He holds the World record for being the first South Indian to represent India in International Wushu fighting and win Gold.

Concept note-2: -Aniyan Midhun of Nattika won the gold at the South Asian Wushu Championship that concluded in Nepal on March 31. The 28-year-old youth represented the country in the 70-kg category in the championship.

Concept note-3: -Story highlights. World Wushu Championships gold medallist Sadia Tariq from Srinagar dreams of meeting Indian PM Narendra Modi.

Concept note-4: -Aniyan Midhun, a youth from Kerala, has won gold at the World Pro Wushu Sanda Fight 2022 held recently in Thailand. Midhun, hailing from Nattika in Thrissur, had earlier won the South Asian Wushu Championship held in Nepal in 2021. He is the first player from southern India to make it to the Indian Wushu team.

Concept note-5: -India finished with a record eight medals, including three gold, in the 8th Junior Wushu World Championship in Tangerang, Indonesia.
