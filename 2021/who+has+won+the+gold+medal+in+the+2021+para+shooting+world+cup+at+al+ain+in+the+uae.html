Who has won the gold medal in the 2021 Para Shooting World Cup at Al Ain in the UAE?

(A) Rajinder Singh Rahelu

(B) Girisha Nagarajegowda

(C) Mariyappan Thangavelu

(D) ** Singhraj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: India won two medals - a gold and a bronze - in the Para Shooting World Championship at Al Ain, UAE on Monday. India’s Rahul Jakhar, Singhraj Adhana and Nihal Singh claimed the gold in P3 team event – 25m pistol mixed SH1.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Singhraj. Indian para-athlete Singhraj won a gold medal in the 2021 Para Shooting World Cup at Al Ain in the UAE on 21 March 2021.

Concept note-3: -Shooting Para Sport will be returning to Al Ain for the fifth time, this time as one of the final events prior to the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games.

Concept note-4: -Indian shooters have so far won 13 gold medals, nine silver medals and three bronze medals at the Para Shooting World Cup 2022, which took place in three locations - Chatreauroux, Paris, Munich, Germany and Changwon, South Korea over the year.
