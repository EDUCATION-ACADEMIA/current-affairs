Who has won the inaugural ICC Player of the Month award?

(A) Pat Cummins

(B) Steve Smith

(C) Virat Kohli

(D) ** Rishabh Pant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Virat Kohli scored an unbeaten 53-ball 82* to help India secure an opening victory against Pakistan in their ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022 encounter. Virat Kohli’s brilliant form with the bat en-route to the semi-finals of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup earned him the monthly award.

Concept note-2: -Rishabh Pant voted inaugural ICC Player of the Month.

Concept note-3: -It was a double delight for Pakistan skipper Babar Azam as he was picked for both the ‘ODI Cricketer of the Year’ and the ‘Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for the ICC Men’s Cricketer of 2022’ on January 26, 2023.

Concept note-4: -January 2022 - Heather Knight (England)
