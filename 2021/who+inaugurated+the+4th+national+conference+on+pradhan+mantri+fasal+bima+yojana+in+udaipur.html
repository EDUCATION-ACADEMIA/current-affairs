Who inaugurated the 4th National Conference on Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana in Udaipur?

(A) Motilal Sikka

(B) ** Sanjay Agarwal

(C) Rajeev Bhatia

(D) Deepak Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojna was launched on 18th February 2016 by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi.

Concept note-2: -Sh. Ritesh Chauhan, Joint Secretary, CEO PMFBY spoke for the financial security of farmers.

Concept note-3: -• PMFBY will provide a comprehensive insurance cover against failure of the. crop thus helping in stabilising the income of the farmers and encourage them for adoption of innovative practices. • The Scheme can cover all Food & Oilseeds crops and Annual.

Concept note-4: -Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) scheme was launched in India by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers welfare, New Delhi from Kharif 2016 season onwards.
