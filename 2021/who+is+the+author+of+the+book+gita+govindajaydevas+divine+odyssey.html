Who is the author of the book “Gita Govinda:Jaydeva’s Divine Odyssey”?

(A) Rohinton Mistry

(B) Nayantara Sahgal

(C) ** Utpal K. Banerjee

(D) Chitra Banerjee Divakaruni

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gita Govinda is originally written by 12th century poet Jayadeya.

Concept note-2: -Union Minister of Culture Shri Kishan Reddy Gangapuram launched the book “Gita Govinda :Jaydeva’s Divine Odyssey” by Dr. Utpal K.

Concept note-3: -Jayadeva who wrote “Gita Govinda", was in the Sena court. The Rajput rulers were themselves scholars and they patronised poets. Q.

Concept note-4: -The Gītagovinda is the earliest-known poem dealing with the theme of the divine lovers, Rādhā and Krishna, and in it Rādhā is treated as the mistress, not as the wife, of Krishna.

Concept note-5: -The writers of these imitations included no less than the Gajapati king who wrote Abhinaba Geeta govinda also.
