Who is the author of the book titled ‘Artificial Intelligence and the Future of Power’?

(A) Akshay Kumar

(B) ** Rajiv Malhotra

(C) Sundar Pichai

(D) Bhuvan Bam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -RAJIV MALHOTRA is an internationally acclaimed author and public intellectual. He combines his background in physics and computer science with his quarter century of path-breaking research on India’s historical and future place in the world.

Concept note-2: -John McCarthy was one of the most influential people in the field. He is known as the “father of artificial intelligence” because of his fantastic work in Computer Science and AI. McCarthy coined the term “artificial intelligence” in the 1950s.

Concept note-3: -AI enables an unprecedented ability to analyze enormous data sets and computationally discover complex relationships and patterns. AI, augmenting human intelligence, is primed to transform the scientific research process, unleashing a new golden age of scientific discovery in the coming years.

Concept note-4: -Artificial Intelligence and The Future of Power is a wakeup call to action, compelling public intellectuals to be better informed and more engaged. It educates the social segments most at risk and wants them to demand a seat at the table where policies on Artificial Intelligence are being formulated.
