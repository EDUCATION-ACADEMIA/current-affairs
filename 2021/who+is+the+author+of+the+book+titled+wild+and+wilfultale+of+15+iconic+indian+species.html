Who is the author of the book titled “Wild and Wilful:Tale of 15 Iconic Indian Species”?

(A) Vandana Shiva

(B) ** Neha Sinha

(C) Maneka Gandhi

(D) Sunita Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Neha Sinha is an award-winning wildlife conservationist. She has studied biodiversity conservation at Oxford University, after winning an INLAKS scholarship, and works with the Bombay Natural History Society at present. She is also a noted columnist and has taught environmental politics at Delhi University.
