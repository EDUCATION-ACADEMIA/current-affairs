Who launched the Online Course Mobile App “Matsya Setu”?

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) ** Giriraj Singh

(C) Amit Shah

(D) Nirmala Sitharaman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Giriraj Singh . Union Minister for Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying Giriraj Singh launched the online course mobile app Matsya Setu, on 6 July 2021. The app has been developed by the ICAR-Central Institute of Freshwater Aquaculture, Bhubaneswar.

Concept note-2: -Shri Parshottam Rupala, Union Minister for Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying, yesterday launched the Online Market Place feature “Aqua Bazar” in the “MatsyaSetu” mobile app during the 9th general body meeting of the National Fisheries Development Board.

Concept note-3: -Shri Parshottam Rupala, Union Minister for Fisheries, Animal Husbandry, and Dairy, introduced the “Aqua Bazar” online market place feature in the “MatsyaSetu” mobile app.

Concept note-4: -It is developed by the ICAR-Central Institute of Freshwater Aquaculture (ICAR-CIFA), Bhubaneswar. The National Fisheries Development Board (NFDB), Hyderabad is providing funding support. Source: EPIC july, 2021.

Concept note-5: -NPFB conducts 9th governing body meeting, Parshottam Rupala launches ‘Aqua Bazar’ app. National Fisheries Development Board (NPFB) today conducted its 9th governing body meeting in New Delhi.
