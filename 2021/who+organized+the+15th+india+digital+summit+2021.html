Who organized the 15th India Digital Summit 2021?

(A) Hyperlink InfoSystem

(B) IANA

(C) ** IAMAI

(D) Niti Aayog

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Industry body Internet and Mobile Association of India (IAMAI) hosted its flagship two-day annual summit- the India Digital Summit 2021, virtually on January 19-20, 2021. ‘Atmanirbhar Bharat - Start of a New Decade’ was its central theme.

Concept note-2: -The two-days India Digital Summit 2021, one of the biggest conference for the digital industry, was organised on January 19 and 20, 2021, through virtual platform. The 2021 marked the 15th edition of the annual Summit.

Concept note-3: -Answer: [A] Internet and Mobile Association of India (IAMAI) Notes: The 16th edition of India Digital Summit 2022 was organised by the Internet and Mobile Association of India (IAMAI). The report, titled ‘Creating 10 million Digitally Enabled Micro-entrepreneurs’ was also released during the summit.

Concept note-4: -The theme for the latest edition will be “Enabling A Revolution". The summit hosted virtually will be a 12-episode series, scheduled to be telecast from January 15, 2022, every Saturday at 11:30 am on TIMES NOW, TIMES NOW WORLD, 8:30 pm ET NOW and on the Network’s digital news destination, timenownews.com.

Concept note-5: -Times Network announced the 7th Edition of the Digital India Summit (DIS) on Friday with the aim of accelerating India’s digital transformation by defining an action plan for progress.
