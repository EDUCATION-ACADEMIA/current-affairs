Who took over the additional charge as ONGC CMD?

(A) Rohit kumar

(B) ** Subhash Kumar

(C) Pankaj Kumar

(D) Subhash Guar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: Director (Exploration) of Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited (ONGC) Rajesh Kumar Srivastava has taken over additional charge of the Chairman and Managing Director (CMD) of the Energy Maharatna on Thursday, after Dr Alka Mittal superannuated as ONGC CMD on 31 August 2022.

Concept note-2: -Government appoints Shri Arun Kumar Singh as Chairman, ONGC; approves Board level reforms. Government of India had set up a Search-cum-Selection Committee (SCSC) in February, 2022 for filling up the post of CMD, ONGC.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI: Rajarshi Gupta has taken over as managing director of ONGC Videsh Limited (OVL), effective 19 July. In a statement, the ONGC subsidiary said Gupta was recommended for the position by public enterprise selection board (PESB).

Concept note-4: -Director (Finance) Mr Subhash Kumar has assumed the additional charge of the post of Chairman and Managing Director (CMD) of Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited (ONGC) on 1 April 2021 at New Delhi vide order dated 30 March 2021 issued by the Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas.
