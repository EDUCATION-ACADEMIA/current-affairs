Who will be appointed as IMF’s First Deputy Managing Director?

(A) Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw

(B) Soumya Swaminathan

(C) Suchitra Ella

(D) ** Gita Gopinath

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gopinath is the first deputy managing director of the IMF.

Concept note-2: -Breaking the trend, Indian Origin Gita Gopinath, the Deputy Managing Director of the International Monetary Fund (IMF), became the first woman to feature on IMF’s ‘Wall Of Former Chief Economists’. Gopinath took to twitter to share her excitement via a tweet on Thursday.

Concept note-3: -Gopinath’s research has been published in many top economics journals. Prior to her appointment as IMF Chief Economist, she was the John Zwaanstra Professor of International Studies and Economics in the economics department of Harvard University. Gopinath is the first deputy managing director of the IMF.
