Who will be enrolled as the first member of the NCC Alumni Association?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) ** Narendra Modi

(C) Rajnath Singh

(D) Nirmala Sitharaman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NCC Alumni Association: PM Modi to be enrolled as 1st member, Know charges, membership criteria & how to apply? NCC Alumni Association: PM Modi will launch the NCC Alumni Association by enrolling himself as the first member of the Association.

Concept note-2: -Despite enrolling himself as the first member of the ‘NCC Alumni Association’ at historic Jhansi Fort, the Prime Minister also launched the association which is expected to further the aims of the National Cadet Corps (NCC) and assist in nation-building.

Concept note-3: -NCCAA Life Members – All the Ex- Candidates of NCC can Make NCC Alumni Association Registration 2022 under Life Membership. Cadets who are expelled on the disciplinary ground are not eligible for this.

Concept note-4: -Welcome to NCC Alumni Association We Develop Character, Comradeship, Discipline, Leadership, Secular Outlook, Spirit of Adventure, and Ideals of Selfless Service amongst the Youth of the Country.

Concept note-5: -Gopal Gurunath Bewoor was the first director of NCC.
