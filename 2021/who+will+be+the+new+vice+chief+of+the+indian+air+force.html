Who will be the new Vice Chief of the Indian Air Force?

(A) Ravneet Singh

(B) ** Vivek Ram Chaudhari

(C) Rajesh Pendharkar

(D) Samant Goel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current VCAS is Air Marshal Amar Preet Singh. He succeeded Air Chief Marshal Sandeep Singh to the post on 1 October 2021. As Vice Chief, Air Marshal Singh will directly be in charge of IAF’s ongoing capability build-up and visualizing its future technology requirements.

Concept note-2: -Air Marshal AP Singh succeeded Air Marshal Sandeep Singh, who retired on January 31 after more than 39 years of service. By Manjeet Negi: Air Marshal AP Singh became the new Vice Chief of the Indian Air Force on Wednesday.

Concept note-3: -Air Marshal A P Singh has been appointed as the new Vice Chief of the Indian Air Force, officials said on January 30, 2023. He will succeed Air Marshal Sandeep Singh, who will retire from service on January 31, 2023.

Concept note-4: -Air Marshal AP Singh PVSM AVSM took over as the Vice Chief of the Air Staff (VCAS) on 01 February 2023.

Concept note-5: -Air Vice Marshal Harjinder Singh - Indian Air Force: Touch The Sky With Glory.
