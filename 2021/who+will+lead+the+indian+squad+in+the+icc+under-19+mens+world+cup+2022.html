Who will lead the Indian squad in the ICC Under-19 Men’s World Cup 2022?

(A) Dinesh Bana

(B) Siddarth Yadav

(C) ** Yash Dhull

(D) Nishant Sindhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICC U19 World Cup 2022: Yash Dhull named captain of Team of the Tournament, Raj Bawa finds place - India Today.

Concept note-2: -Rohit Sharma as captain for India from T20 World Cup KL Rahul, Virat Kohli, Deepak Hooda, Suryakumar Yadav, Dinesh Karthik, Rishabh Pant, Hardik Pandya, Axar Patel, Yuzvendra Chahal, Ravichandran Ashwin, Bhuvneshwar Kumar, Harshal Patel and Arshdeep Singh will play for Indian cricket team .

Concept note-3: -India. Yash Dhull (c), Harnoor Singh, Angkrish Raghuvanshi, SK Rasheed (vc), Nishant Sindhu, Siddarth Yadav, Aneeshwar Gautam, Dinesh Bana, Raj Angad Bawa, Manav Parakh, Kaushal Tambe, RS Hangargekar, Vicky Ostwal, Ravikumar, Garv Sangwan.

Concept note-4: -In 2000, they won it under the captaincy of Mohammad Kaif, in 2008, they won it under Virat Kohli’s leadership, in 2012 under Unmukt Chand, in 2018 under Prithvi Shaw, and in 2022 under Yash Dhull. The team is currently captained by Yash Dhull and coached by former India cricketer Hrishikesh Kanitkar.
