Who won British Grand Prix?

(A) ** Lewis Hamilton

(B) Max Verstappen

(C) Pankaj Advani

(D) L. Norris

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It was an action-packed 2022 British Grand Prix at Silverstone, as Ferrari’s Carlos Sainz raced to his first victory in Formula One. A late safety car played to his advantage, and he was able to pass his team-mate Charles Leclerc before holding off Sergio Perez and Lewis Hamilton for a famous victory on Sunday.

Concept note-2: -Carlos Sainz cruised to British Grand Prix victory, earning his first career Formula 1 win in 150 races. Red Bull’s Sergio Pérez crossed the line seconds ahead of Mercedes’ Lewis Hamilton to round out the podium.

Concept note-3: -Carlos Sainz on his first ever GP win: “I don’t know what to say – it’s amazing. My first race win, 150 races later, with Ferrari and in Silverstone. Thanks everyone.

Concept note-4: -2022 FIA Formula One World Championship Mercedes were the only other team to win a race, but it was George Russell who took a celebrated maiden victory, as team mate Lewis Hamilton failed to top a podium for the first time in his illustrious F1 career.
