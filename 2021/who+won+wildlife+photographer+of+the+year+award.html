Who won Wildlife Photographer of the Year Award?

(A) ** Aishwarya Sridhar

(B) Rathika Ramaswamy

(C) Michael Nicholas

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sascha Fonseca’s spectacular camera trap image of a snow leopard at sunset, perfectly posed against the mountains of Ladakh in northern India, has been voted as the winner of Wildlife Photographer of the Year People’s Choice Award.

Concept note-2: -Creatures under the sea award goes to Spain’s photographer Arturo Telle Thiemann for his photograph ‘Say cheeeese’.

Concept note-3: -Amos nachoum, USA - Gold WINNer and grand prize of WORLD NATURE PHOTOGRAPHER OF THE YEAR.

Concept note-4: -The London Natural History Museum’s 58th Wildlife Photographer of the Year winners have been announced, with US photographer Karine Aigne winning the top prize for an image of a ball of cactus bees. The Big Buzz, Wildlife Photographer of the Year overall winning picture.
