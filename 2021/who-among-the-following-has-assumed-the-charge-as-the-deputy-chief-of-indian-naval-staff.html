Who among the following has assumed the charge as the Deputy Chief of Indian Naval Staff?

(A) G Ashok Kumar

(B) ** Ravneet Singh

(C) Dinesh K Tripathi

(D) S R Sarma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The office is held by a Three Star Officer in the rank of Vice Admiral. The current DCNS is Vice Admiral Sanjay Mahindru who took over Vice Admiral Ravneet Singh on 1 April 2022.

Concept note-2: -The Vice Chief is the deputy of the Chief of the Naval Staff and is usually the second highest ranking officer of the Indian Navy. The current VCNS is Vice Admiral Satish Namdeo Ghormade who took over from Vice Admiral G. Ashok Kumar on 31 July 2021.

Concept note-3: -Air Marshal Pankaj M Sinha takes over as chief of IAF’s Western Command | Business Standard News.

Concept note-4: -Vice Admiral SN Ghormade, PVSM, AVSM, NM, ADC He is a graduate of the National Defence Academy (NDA), Khadakwasla, Naval Staff College at the United States Naval War College, Newport, Rhode Island, and the Naval War College, Mumbai.

Concept note-5: -Vice-Admiral Ram Dass Katari was the first Indian who served on the post of Chief of the Naval Staff of India.
