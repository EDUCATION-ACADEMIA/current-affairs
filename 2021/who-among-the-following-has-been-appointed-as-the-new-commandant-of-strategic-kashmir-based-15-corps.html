Who among the following has been appointed as the new commandant of strategic Kashmir-based 15 Corps?

(A) Anand Raj

(B) ** DP Pandey

(C) Nitin Verma

(D) RajniKumari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Amardeep Singh Aujla.

Concept note-2: -Lieutanant General Amardeep Singh Aujla is its current Corps Commander since 9 May 2022 taking over from Lieutanant General Devendra Pratap Pandey.

Concept note-3: -Lieutenant General Amardeep Singh Aujla (born 3 December 1966) YSM, SM, VSM is an Indian Army General serving as the current General Officer-Commanding (GOC) of the Srinagar-based Chinar Corps(XV).

Concept note-4: -Its current General Officer Commanding is Lieutenant General Majinder Singh. Lt Gen Harsha Gupta assuming command of White Knight Corps (16 Corps) from Lt Gen Paramjit Singh Sangha, on 12 Oct 2019.

Concept note-5: -Srinagar, Dec 20: Major General Mohit Seth Tuesday took over as the General Officer Commanding (GOC) of Army’s Kilo Force in the Kashmir Valley.
