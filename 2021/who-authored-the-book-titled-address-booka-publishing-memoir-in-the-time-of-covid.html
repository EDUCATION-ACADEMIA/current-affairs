Who authored the book titled ‘Address Book:A Publishing Memoir in the time of covid’?

(A) ** Ritu Menon

(B) Harish Patel

(C) Giri Prasad

(D) Neha Uppal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Ritu Menon. A book titled ‘Address Book: A Publishing Memoir in the time of COVID’ has been written by writer Ritu Menon. Menon co-founded Kali for Women, India’s first feminist press in 1983.

Concept note-2: -A book titled has ‘Address Book: A Publishing Memoir in the time of COVID’ by Ritu Menon. Menon, who co-founded Kali for Women, India’s first feminist press in 1983, is founder-director of Women Unlimited, an associate of KfW. Weeks after the March 2020 lockdown began, Menon started to write a diary.

Concept note-3: -Title Page: The title page is the page that contains the title of the book, the author (or authors) and the publisher.

Concept note-4: -Book / eBook Copyright Page The page immediately following the title page is often called the copyright page. This is where you find the remaining copyright information for the book. Here is where the publication or copyright date is typically printed.
