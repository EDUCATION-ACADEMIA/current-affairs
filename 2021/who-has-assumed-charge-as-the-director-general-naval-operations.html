Who has assumed charge as the Director-General Naval Operations?

(A) K P Krishnan

(B) Ravneet Singh

(C) ** Rajesh Pendharkar

(D) Pradeep Chandran Nair

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vice Admiral Rajesh Pendharkar, AVSM, VSM.

Concept note-2: -Vice Admiral Rajesh Pendharkar, AVSM, VSM has assumed charge as Director General Naval Operations today, on 07 Jun 21. An alumnus of the National Defense Academy, Khadakwasla, Pune, he was commissioned into the Indian Navy in Jan 1987.

Concept note-3: -Vice Admiral Rajesh Pendharkar, AVSM, VSM.

Concept note-4: -The current CNS is Admiral R. Hari Kumar. The 25th Navy Chief, he took over from Karambir Singh, who retired on 30 November 2021 after four decades of service from the navy.

Concept note-5: -Air Marshal Pankaj M Sinha takes over as chief of IAF’s Western Command | Business Standard News.
