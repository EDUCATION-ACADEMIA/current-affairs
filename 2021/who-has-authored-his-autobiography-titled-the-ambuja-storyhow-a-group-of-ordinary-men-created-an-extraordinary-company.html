Who has authored his autobiography titled ‘The Ambuja Story:How a Group of Ordinary Men Created an Extraordinary Company’?

(A) Pullela Gopichand

(B) Rakeysh Omprakash Mehra

(C) Puneet Dalmia

(D) ** Narotam Sekhsaria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About the Author Narotam Sekhsaria is the chairman of ACC Limited, Ambuja Cements Ltd, and Ambuja Cements Foundation, which runs one of India’s most extensive CSR programmes. He also manages the Narotam Sekhsaria Foundation, a leading philanthropic funding agency.

Concept note-2: -The Ambuja Story (English, Hardcover, Sekhsaria Narotam) WINNER OF THE 2022 TATA LITERATURE LIVE! BUSINESS BOOK AWARD ‘I had never seen a cement plant in my life.

Concept note-3: – Gujarat Ambuja Cement Ltd. (GACL) was set up in 1981 as a joint sector company, promoted by Narottam Sekhsaria and Gujarat Industrial Investment Company (GIIC). Its cement plant which was commissioned in 1985 was set up in technical collaboration with Krupp Polysius, Germany, Bakau Wolf and Fuller KCP.
