Who has become the 1st batsman to complete 6000 runs in the IPL?

(A) Rohit Sharma

(B) ** Virat Kohli

(C) David Warner

(D) Shikhar Dhawan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Virat Kohli becomes first batsman to complete 6, 000 runs in IPL.

Concept note-2: -Shikhar Dhawan completes 6000 runs in IPL, becomes 2nd player after Virat Kohli to achieve the milestone.

Concept note-3: -In Short. By India Today Web Desk: Shikhar Dhawan became the second batter after Virat Kohli to score 6000 runs in the Indian Premier League. Dhawan, who was Delhi Capitals for 3 years till 2021, was bought by Punjab Kings in the 2022 player auction. Virat Kohli is still the leading run-scorer in the IPL.

Concept note-4: -KL Rahul reached the mark in 166 innings, 18 lesser than what Virat Kohli took to achieve the milestone. Lucknow Super Giants skipper KL Rahul has become the fastest Indian to score 6000 runs in the Twenty20 format during a game against Royal Challengers Bangalore at the DY Patil Stadium in Navi Mumbai.

Concept note-5: -1. Hashim Amla (South Africa) – 123 innings. Former South African captain Hashim Amla is the fastest batsman in ODI history to reach 2000, 3000, 4000, 5000, 6000, and 7000 runs. That shows the consistency of the man over the years.
