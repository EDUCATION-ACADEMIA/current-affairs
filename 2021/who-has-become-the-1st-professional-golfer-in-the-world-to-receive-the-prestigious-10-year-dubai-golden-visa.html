Who has become the 1st professional golfer in the world to receive the prestigious 10-year Dubai Golden Visa?

(A) Jordan Spieth

(B) Rory McIlroy

(C) ** Jeev Milkha Singh

(D) Tiger Woods

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Four-time winner on the European Tour, Jeev Milkha Singh has become the first professional golfer in the world to get the prestigious 10-year Dubai Golden Visa in recognition of his outstanding achievements in the sport.

Concept note-2: -Shah Rukh Khan The ‘Badshah’ of the Indian film industry who ruled Bollywood for over 30 years was the first Indian actor to get the UAE Golden visa.

Concept note-3: -Singh lives in Chandigarh with wife Kudrat and their son.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Golf. Jeev Milkha Singh is the golfer. Jeev Milkha Singh received the Arjuna Award in 1999 and 2004 respectively.

Concept note-5: -About Jeev Born on 15 December 1971 in Chandigarh India, Jeev is the first Indian Golf professional to join the European Tour in 1998. He has won four events on the European Tour, becoming the most successful Indian on tour.
