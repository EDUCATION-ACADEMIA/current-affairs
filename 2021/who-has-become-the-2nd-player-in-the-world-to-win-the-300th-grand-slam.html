Who has become the 2nd player in the world to win the 300th Grand Slam?

(A) ** Novak Djokovic

(B) Roger Federer

(C) Dominic Thiem

(D) Rafael Nadal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With his 6-3, 6-1, 6-4 victory, Nadal became just the third man to reach the 300-wins mark at the Grand Slams, behind only Roger Federer’s 369 wins and Novak Djokovic’s 324.

Concept note-2: -Novak Djokovic, the first man in the Open Era to win all 4 Grand Slam titles at least twice.

Concept note-3: -As of January 2023, Rafael Nadal and Novak Djokovic held the record for the most men’s tennis Grand Slam titles, with a total of 22 each. Nadal lifted his 22nd Grand Slam trophy at the French Open in 2022, while Djokovic’s 22nd title came during the 2023 Australian Open.

Concept note-4: -Calendar Year Grand Slam (four majors in one calendar year) Note: Laver is the only player ever to achieve this twice.
