Who has become the fastest player to score 2500 runs in T20Is?

(A) Aaron Finch

(B) Joe Root

(C) ** Babar Azam

(D) Rohit Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pakistan’s Babar Azam is the fastest batsman to score 2500 runs in T20 international cricket. He took 67 matches and 62 innings to reach this milestone.

Concept note-2: -Meanwhile, Pakistan’s skipper Babar Azam is the fastest to score 2500 T20I runs in 62 innings.

Concept note-3: -Virat Kohli, the former India skipper, is the fastest to 3000 runs in T20I cricket while Babar Azam equalled his feat in September 2022. Both the players reached the milestone in 81 innings.

Concept note-4: -In terms of innings, Pakistan’s Babar Azam is the fastest (52 innings) to reach the 2, 000 run mark, surpassing India’s Virat Kohli, who achieved the feat in 56 innings. Currently the list of highest run-scorer is topped with Virat Kohli, Rohit Sharma, and Martin Guptill.
