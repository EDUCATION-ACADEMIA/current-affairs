Who has become the first Indian swimmer to qualify for the upcoming Tokyo Olympics?

(A) ** Sajan Prakash

(B) Prasanta Karmakar

(C) Rohit Havaldar

(D) Suyash Jadhav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sajan Prakash becomes first Indian swimmer to qualify for Tokyo Olympics.

Concept note-2: -Mirabai Chanu’s Silver in Weightlifting in the 49kg category, India’s first medal at the Tokyo Olympics, was the first Silver medal for an Indian in Weightlifting in the Olympics. It was also the country’s second medal in Weightlifting.

Concept note-3: -Both Prakash and Nataraj achieved direct qualification for the Tokyo Olympics, a feat never achieved by any Indian swimmer before.
