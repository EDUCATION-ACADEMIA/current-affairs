(B) Bhawana Kanth

(D) ** Shivani Meena

Concept note-1: -Q. Who has become the first woman excavation engineer to work in an open cast mine at Central Coalfields Ltd? Notes: Shivani Meena, an IIT Jodhpur alumna, has become the first woman excavation engineer to work in an open cast mine at Coal India arm CCL (Central Coalfields Ltd).

Concept note-2: -Biography of First Female Mining Engineer of India: Dr. Chandrani Prasad Verma. Chandrani grew up in the Western Coalfields Limited colony in the Chandrapur district of Maharashtra State, India. She began her adventure after completing her high school education and earning a diploma in mining and mine surveying.
