Who has been appointed as president of the International Automobile Federation?

(A) Max Mosley

(B) ** Mohammed bin Sulayem

(C) Jean Todt

(D) Christian Wolff

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Satish Parekh has been appointed as the President of International Road Federation India president.

Concept note-2: -The FIA president Mohammed Ben Sulayem has stepped back from the day-to-day running of Formula 1 ahead of the new season. Ben Sulayem was elected the president of F1’s governing body in December 2021, and quickly outlined a plan to restructure the FIA’s F1 operations.

Concept note-3: -FIA PRESIDENT MOHAMMED BEN SULAYEM’S SPEECH AT THE 2022 FIA ANNUAL GENERAL ASSEMBLY. Today at the 2022 FIA Annual General Assembly in Bologna, FIA President Mohammed Ben Sulayem delivered his report to Members after the first year of his Presidency.

Concept note-4: -The combined presidency is elected to a four-year term by the General Assembly.

Concept note-5: -The FIA last month unveiled an overhaul of its department responsible for single-seater racing under the leadership of Nikolas Tombazis, who is now in charge of its day-to-day control.
