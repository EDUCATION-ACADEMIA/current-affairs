Who has been appointed as the chairman of the board in Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services?

(A) Vijay Arora

(B) Shankar Bansal

(C) ** Anish Shah

(D) Raunak Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The board of directors at Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services Ltd. have appointed Raul Rebello as managing director and chief executive officer designate. Rebello will assume the position after Ramesh Iyer, the current vice chairman and managing director, retires on April 29, 2023, according to the exchange filing.

Concept note-2: -Mahindra Rural Housing Finance, a subsidiary of Mahindra and Mahindra Financial Services Ltd, announced the appointment of Shantanu Rege as Managing Director and CEO.

Concept note-3: -The Board of Directors of Mahindra and Mahindra Limited has announced that Anish Shah, presently the Deputy Managing Director and Group Chief Financial Officer, would take over as the Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer, effective April 2, 2021.

Concept note-4: -Rajesh Vasudevan - Chief Compliance Officer - MAHINDRA AND MAHINDRA FINANCIAL SERVICES LIMITED | LinkedIn.
