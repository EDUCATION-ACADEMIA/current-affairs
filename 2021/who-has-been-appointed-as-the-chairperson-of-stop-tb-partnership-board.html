Who has been appointed as the Chairperson of Stop TB Partnership Board?

(A) Harsh Vardhan

(B) ** Mansukh Mandaviya

(C) Kiren Rijiju

(D) Dharmendra Pradhan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Harsh Vardhan appointed as Chairman of ‘Stop TB Partnership Board’

Concept note-2: -Union Health Minister Shri Mansukh Mandaviya takes charge as Chairperson of Stop TB Partnership Board.

Concept note-3: -The WHO’s End TB strategy was adopted by the World Health Assembly in 2014 as part of the newly established Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). These goals aim for a 90% reduction in deaths caused by tuberculosis deaths by 2030, compared with 2015 levels.

Concept note-4: -Through the Strategic Initiative, the Global Fund, Stop TB Partnership, WHO, countries and partners seek to ensure that all people suffering from TB are identified and receive adequate treatment.

Concept note-5: -The National Strategic Plan for TB Elimination was launched to achieve the target of ending TB by 2025 in a mission mode. It is a multi-pronged approach which aims to detect all TB patients with an emphasis on reaching TB patients seeking care from private providers and undiagnosed TB in high-risk populations.
