(A) Varun Dhawan

(B) ** Rajkummar Rao

(C) Salman khan

(D) Alia Bhatt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Puneet Arora, Director-Marketing (Brand & Content), Cashify, has said that “Working with the ever talented Rajkumar Rao as our first brand ambassador motivated us to further increase our connect with India’s youth.

Concept note-2: -Rajkumar Rao officially brand ambassador of Cashify.
