Who has been appointed as the head of the committee formed by the RBI to suggest steps for strengthening and consolidating Primary Co-operative Banks?

(A) Hasmukh Adhia

(B) Harsh Kumar Bhanwala

(C) Karnam Sekar

(D) ** N. S. Vishwanathan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shaktikanta Das (born 26 February 1957) is serving as the current & 25th governor of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). He was earlier a member of the Fifteenth Finance Commission and India’s Sherpa to the G20. Das is a retired 1980 batch Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer of Tamil Nadu cadre.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank of India was set up on the basis of the recommendations of the Hilton Young Commission. The Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 (II of 1934) provides the statutory basis of the functioning of the Bank, which commenced operations on April 1, 1935.

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank pursued a liberal licensing policy, especially pursuant to the recommendations of the Marathe Committee, which had suggested dispensing the ‘one-district, one-bank’ approach. As a result, the number of UCBs had increased from 1311 in 1993 to 1926 by March 2004.
