Who has been appointed as the Lieutenant-Governor of Puducherry?

(A) Ganeshi Lal

(B) Biswabhusan Harichandan

(C) ** Tamilisai Soundararajan

(D) Bhagat Singh Koshyari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. Tamilisai Soundararajan was born on 2nd June 1961, in Nagercoil, Kanya Kumari District, Tamilnadu. Dr. Tamilisai Soundararajan took charge as Lieutenant Governor of Puducherry on 18th February 2021.

Concept note-2: -The Lt. Governor is appointed by the President for a term of five years. The executive and legislative powers lie with the Chief Minister and his council of ministers, who are appointed by the President.

Concept note-3: -(Smt.) Tamilisai Soundararajan took charge as Governor of Telangana on 8th September 2019 and happens to be the 1st woman to hold this Office. She assumes the office of the Lt. Governor of Puducherry on 18th February 2021.
