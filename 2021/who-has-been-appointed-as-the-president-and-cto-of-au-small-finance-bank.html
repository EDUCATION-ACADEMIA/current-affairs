Who has been appointed as the President and CTO of AU Small Finance Bank?

(A) ** Sharad Goklani

(B) Sikandar Mustaq

(C) Arun Kumar

(D) Deepak Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ByStaff Reporter. AU Small Finance Bank has named Sharad Goklani as its new President & CTO. Previously, Goklani was EVP & CTO at Equitas Small Finance Bank. He has close to 25 years of professional experience and has worked with companies like Bharti Airtel and NIIT Limited in the past.

Concept note-2: -Mumbai/ Jaipur, 28 December 2021: AU Small Finance Bank, India’s largest Small Finance Bank announced the appointment of Shri Harun Rashid Khan as Non-Executive Independent Director (Additional Director) on the Board, for a period of three years, effective 28th December 2021, subject to the approval of Shareholders.

Concept note-3: -Ajay Singh - Senior Vice President, Regional Head MP - AU SMALL FINANCE BANK | LinkedIn.

Concept note-4: -Mr. Vimal Jain is the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) of the Bank. Prior to this, he was Chief of Finance, Accounts & Budgeting and has been associated with Company since 2010.
