Who has been appointed as the World Bank Education Advisor?

(A) Beula Gabriel

(B) ** Ranjitsinh Disale

(C) Kiran Bir Sethi

(D) Omprakash Mishra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -I’ve been honored as the 2020 Global Teacher of the Year and in 2021, was appointed as the Education Advisor to the World Bank.

Concept note-2: -RWAs are a network of peer volunteers in World Bank Group country offices and some work units in Washington. RWAs are nominated by staff members in their offices (following the RWA Nomination Guidelines), and serve a four-year term.

Concept note-3: -RELATED NEWS. New Delhi: Ranjitsinh Disale, a primary teacher from Maharashtra’s Solapur, who won the Global Teacher Prize 2020, has been appointed as the World Bank Education Advisor for the period June 2021 to June 2024.

Concept note-4: -Ranjitsinh Disale is an Indian teacher from Maharashtra. He is the winner of $1 million annual Global Teacher Prize 2020 which was announced on 3 December 2020.

Concept note-5: -Ranjitsinh Disale - Global Teacher Prize Winner from India Ranjitsinh Disale won price money of $1 million, which he decided to share with his fellow finalists.
