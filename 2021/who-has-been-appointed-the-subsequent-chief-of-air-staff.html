Who has been appointed the subsequent Chief of Air Staff?

(A) Ravi Dubey

(B) Sirish Patel

(C) D Suhag

(D) ** V R Chaudhari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Air Marshal A P Singh is currently serving as the Air Officer Commanding-in-Chief of the Central Air Command. He will take charge as the Vice Chief on 01st Feb 2023. He was commissioned into the fighter stream of the IAF on December 21, 1984.

Concept note-2: -Ans. Air Chief Marshal Vivek Ram Chaudhari, PVSM AVSM VM ADC is an Indian Air Force officer. He is currently the Chief of the Air Staff (CAS). On September 30, 2021, he took over as the 27th CAS, succeeding Air Chief Marshal Rakesh Kumar Singh Bhadauria.

Concept note-3: -The current VCAS is Air Marshal Amar Preet Singh. He succeeded Air Chief Marshal Sandeep Singh to the post on 1 October 2021. As Vice Chief, Air Marshal Singh will directly be in charge of IAF’s ongoing capability build-up and visualizing its future technology requirements.
