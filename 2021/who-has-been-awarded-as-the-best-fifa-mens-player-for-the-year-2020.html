Who has been awarded as the Best FIFA Men’s Player for the year 2020?

(A) Luka Modric

(B) Cristiano Ronaldo

(C) ** Robert Lewandowski

(D) Lionel Messi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The three finalists were revealed on 11 December 2020. Robert Lewandowski won the award with 52 rank points. The selection criteria for the men’s players of the year was: respective achievements during the period from 20 July 2019 to 7 October 2020.

Concept note-2: -Bayern Munich’s Robert Lewandowski has been named the Best FIFA Men’s Player of the Year for the second time in a row.

Concept note-3: -Lionel Messi (Paris Saint-Germain, Argentina)

Concept note-4: -Real Madrid’s Karim Benzema, who won the 2022 Ballon d’Or award, is also in the shortlist.
