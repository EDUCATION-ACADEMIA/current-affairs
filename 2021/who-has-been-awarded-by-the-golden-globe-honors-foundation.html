Who has been awarded by the Golden Globe Honors Foundation?

(A) Ranveer Kapoor

(B) Ranveer Singh

(C) ** Akshay Kumar

(D) Amir khan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At the 66th Golden Globe Awards, Indian composer A. R. Rahman won the Best Original Score for Slumdog Millionaire.

Concept note-2: -Oscar-winning music composer AR Rahman, who became the first Indian to win a Golden Globe in 2009 with his original score for Slumdog Millionaire, said the win marked a “paradigm shift”.

Concept note-3: -The Golden Globe Awards are accolades bestowed by the Hollywood Foreign Press Association starting in January 1944, recognizing excellence in both American and international film and television.

Concept note-4: -The youngest person to win a Golden Globe award is Ricky Schroeder, who was 9 years old when he won New Star of the Year in a Motion Picture for “The Champ” in 1990. The oldest person to win one of the HFPA Globes is Jessica Tandy.
