Who has been awarded the BBC Sports Personality of the Year 2021 award?

(A) Sania Mirza

(B) Simona Halep

(C) ** Emma Raducanu

(D) Serena Williams

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Beth Mead has been crowned the BBC Sports Personality of the Year winner. The member of the successful England women’s European Championship side also won the Golden Boot and was Player of the Tournament. Ben Stokes finished second while Eve Muirhead was third.

Concept note-2: -The recipient must either be British or reside and play a significant amount of their sport in the United Kingdom. The winner is selected from a pre-determined shortlist. The most recent award-winner is footballer Beth Mead, who won the 2022 award.

Concept note-3: -Indian hockey team captain Rani Rampal, wrestler Vinesh Phogat, shooter Manu Bhaker, chess player Koneru Humpy, and sprinter Dutee Chand, were the final nominees who proceeded for public voting. In a press release on 8 March, BBC named Koneru Humpy as Indian Sportswoman of the Year 2021.

Concept note-4: -Beth Mead has been awarded the BBC Sports Personality of the Year for 2022 as she was the player of the tournament and top scorer at Euro 2022.
