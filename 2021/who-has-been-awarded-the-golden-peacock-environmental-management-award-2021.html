Who has been awarded the Golden Peacock Environmental Management Award 2021?

(A) Defense Research and Development Organization

(B) Small Industries Development Bank of India

(C) Indian Space Research Organization

(D) ** Steel Authority of India Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Steel Authority of India Ltd . (SAIL), under the Ministry of Steel has been awarded with the prestigious Golden Peacock Environment Management Award for the year 2021 in the Steel Sector by the Institute of Directors. SAIL has been the winner of this award for successive three years.

Concept note-2: -Isadore, GM & Head (HR), BHEL, received the award during the 23rd World Congress on Environment Management and Climate Change at Bengaluru.

Concept note-3: -The Golden Peacock award went to the Spanish coming-of-age movie I Have Electric Dreams, while top acting honors went to Daniela Marin Navarro and Vahid Mobasheri.
