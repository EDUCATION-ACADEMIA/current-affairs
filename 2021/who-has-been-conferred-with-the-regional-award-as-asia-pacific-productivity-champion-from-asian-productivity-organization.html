Who has been conferred with the regional award as Asia Pacific Productivity Champion from Asian Productivity Organization?

(A) Saurabh Joshi

(B) Pinky Tiwari

(C) ** R.S. Sodhi

(D) Divya Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Who has been conferred with the regional award as Asia Pacific Productivity Champion from Asian Productivity Organization? Note : The MD of Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation R.S. Sodhi has been conferred with the regional award as Asia Pacific Productivity Champion.

Concept note-2: -In recognition of enhanced productivity and efficient milk supply chain, R S Sodhi, managing director, Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation (GCMMF), was conferred with the regional award as Asia Pacific Productivity Champion from Asian Productivity Organization (APO), Tokyo, Japan.

Concept note-3: -Shining a light on individuals who have championed the cause of productivity and advanced the productivity movement in the Asia-Pacific, the APO conferred a total of 20 APO Awards comprising three categories: Regional, Meritorious and Distinguished, and National.
