Who has been crowned Miss India USA 2021 at the beauty pageant?

(A) ** Vaidehi Dongre

(B) Mira Kasari

(C) Arshi Lalani

(D) Neha Uppal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current titleholder of Miss India USA is Vaidehi Dongre from Michigan.

Concept note-2: -In an exclusive conversation with Firstpost, Miss India USA 2021 Vaidehi Dongre opens up about how winning the prestigious title changed her life and more.

Concept note-3: -Meet Aarya Walvekar, the Indian-American teen who has been crowned Miss India USA 2022.

Concept note-4: -The First Miss India 2021 title was bagged by Pearl Agarwal with Lekh Utaiah as 1st runner up and Shreya Gupta as the 2nd runner up. Rayna Sikri was declared as the First Miss India Teen 2021 with Shubhi Dhaneta as 1st runner up.

Concept note-5: -Indian American teenager Aarya Walvekar from Virginia has been crowned Miss India USA 2022 at the annual pageant held in New Jersey. After winning the pageant, the 18-year-old shared that she aspires to be an actor.
