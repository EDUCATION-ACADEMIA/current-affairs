Who has been elected as Chairman of the Indian Society of Advertisers (ISA)?

(A) P Suresh

(B) Shri Bhagwan Dev

(C) Devendra Singh

(D) ** Sunil Kataria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Sunil Kataria.

Concept note-2: -The decision was taken after the newly elected Executive Council of the ISA met on September 29, 2022. Kataria has been leading the society during the past six years, receiving support from members of ISA, fellow executive council members, as well as other industry bodies.

Concept note-3: -Sunil Kataria, Chief Executive Officer of Lifestyle Business at Raymond, has been re-elected as Chairman of the ISA (Indian Society of Advertisers). Other members of the Executive Council are: Atul Agrawal, Senior Advisor, Corporate Brand and Marketing, Tata Services.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Society of Advertisers (ISA) has been the peak national body for advertisers for 60 years and represents the interests of organisations involved in Indian advertising, marketing and media industry.

Concept note-5: -THE Indian Society OF Advertisers is a Guarantee and Association company, incorporated on 09 Jul, 1952.
