Who has been given the additional charge of the Director-General of the National Investigation Agency?

(A) YC Modi

(B) Taraknath Banerjee

(C) ** Kuldip Singh

(D) Subodh Kumar Jaiswal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Currently Dinkar Gupta serves as its Director-General since 28 June 2022.

Concept note-2: -CRPF Chief Kuldiep Singh has been given the additional charge of the Director General of the National Investigation Agency (NIA) to replace Y C Modi.

Concept note-3: -Yogesh Chander Modi (Y.C. Modi) (YC Modi) is the former Director-General/chief of the National Investigation Agency (NIA) of India.

Concept note-4: -Kuldiep Singh (IPS) is new Director CRPF & MA Ganapathy (IPS) is new DG NSG. New Delhi: The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the proposal of the Ministry of Home Affairs for the appointment of Kuldiep Singh (IPS) as Director-General of the Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF).

Concept note-5: -The current Director General is Dr. Sujoy Lal Thaosen, in office since 1 October 2022.
