Who has been honoured with the 51st Dadasaheb Phalke Award?

(A) Mohanlal

(B) Kamal Hassan

(C) Mammootty

(D) ** Rajnikanth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dadasaheb Phalke, the Father of Indian Cinema, produced the first Indian feature film, Raja Harishchandra, in 1913. In his honor, the Indian government established the Dadasaheb Phalke Award in 1969. The iconic actor Rajnikanth received the 51st Dadasaheb Phalke award.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Rajinikanth. Rajinikanth has been conferred with the 51st Dadasaheb Phalke Award.

Concept note-3: -Asha Parekh won the Outstanding Contribution to the Film Industry award at the Dadasaheb Phalke International Film Festival Awards 2022. Pushpa: The Rise won the award for Film of the Year, Shershaah won the award for Best Film, Ranveer Singh won the award for Best Actor, and Kriti Sanon won the award for Best Actress.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Rajini Kanth. Rajinikanth was honored with the prestigious 51st Dada Saheb Phalke Award at 67th National Film Awards Ceremony. It is India’s highest award in cinema.

Concept note-5: -Rajinikanth won the Dadasaheb Phalke Award in 2019 and got awarded in 2021, whereas Asha Parekh won this award in 2020 but will receive it in 2022 due to the COVID pandemic.
