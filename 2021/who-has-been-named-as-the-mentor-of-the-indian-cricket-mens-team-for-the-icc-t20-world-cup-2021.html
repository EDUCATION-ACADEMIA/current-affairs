Who has been named as the mentor of the Indian cricket Men’s team for the ICC T20 World Cup 2021?

(A) Virat Kohli

(B) Sunil Gavaskar

(C) Rahul Dravid

(D) ** M.S.Dhoni

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The iconic Mahendra Singh Dhoni on Sunday joined the Indian team camp as its mentor for the T20 World Cup, two days after leading Chennai Super Kings to their fourth IPL title.

Concept note-2: -MS Dhoni was appointed the team mentor to the Indian squad for the T20 World Cup and will be helping out Virat Kohli, Ravi Shastri with his inputs during the showpiece event in the UAE.

Concept note-3: -T20 World Cup: Former India captain MS Dhoni will join the Virat Kohli-led side in the UAE as he was named the mentor of the 15-member squad that was announced on Wednesday, September 8.

Concept note-4: -Men’s T20 World Cup 2022 final - Eng vs Pak - Mentor Matthew Hayden has had a big impact on this Pakistan team | ESPNcricinfo.
