Who has been named for the Lifetime Achievement Award for 2021 by the Badminton World Federation?

(A) ** Prakash Padukone

(B) Jwala Gutta

(C) Nandu Natekar

(D) Pullela Gopichand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Prakash Padukone. Prakash Padukone will receive the prestigious Lifetime Achievement Award 2021 from the Badminton World Federation (BWF). In 2018, he was also honored with the Badminton Association of India’s (BAI ) Lifetime Achievement Award.

Concept note-2: -Patrick Whitney: 2021 Winner | DFA Lifetime Achievement Award.
