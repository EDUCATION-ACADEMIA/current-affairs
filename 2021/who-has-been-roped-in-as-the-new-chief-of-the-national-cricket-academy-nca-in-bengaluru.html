Who has been roped in as the new chief of the National Cricket Academy (NCA) in Bengaluru?

(A) Virender Sehwag

(B) ** VVS Laxman

(C) Anil Kumble

(D) Gautam Gambhir

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rahul Dravid Appointed As Head Of National Cricket Academy In Bengaluru.

Concept note-2: -Former Indian cricketer VVS Laxman is the current director of the NCA.

Concept note-3: -The Current Head of the National Cricket Academy in Bangalore is none other than Rahul Dravid. Players like Chahal, Kuldeep Yadav, Sanju Samson are all from the coaching background with Rahul Dravid.

Concept note-4: -"Ramesh Powar, former Head Coach of the senior women’s team will join VVS Laxman, Head Cricket, National Cricket Academy and will switch to men’s cricket as part of the restructuring module of the BCCI, ” the BCCI stated in a release.
