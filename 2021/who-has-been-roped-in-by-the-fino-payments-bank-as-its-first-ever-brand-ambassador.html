Who has been roped in by the Fino Payments Bank as its first-ever brand ambassador?

(A) Neeraj Chopra

(B) ** Pankaj Tripathi

(C) Pankaj Kapur

(D) Amitabh Bachchan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fino Payments Bank (FPBL) has appointed Indian actor Pankaj Tripathi as its first brand ambassador, for a period of two-year.

Concept note-2: -A still from the ad Fino Payments Bank has launched a new campaign with their brand ambassador Pankaj Tripathi. It aims to reinforce the trust factor in the backdrop of a shift in the way people can do banking.

Concept note-3: -The approval will enable the customers of Fino Payments Bank to receive money sent from foreign countries. The Bank plans to implement the service in its mobile applications as well and it also plans to partner with more leading money transfer operators (MTOs) to improve its cross-border remittance.
