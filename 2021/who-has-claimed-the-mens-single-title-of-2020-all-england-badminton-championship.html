Who has claimed the men’s single title of 2020 All England Badminton Championship?

(A) ** Viktor Axelsen

(B) Kento Momota

(C) Shi Yuqi

(D) Chen Long

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Viktor Axelsen won the All England Open Badminton Championship Men’s Singles 2020 title. The championship was held in Birmingham, England.

Concept note-2: -Viktor Axelsen downs Lakshya Sen to win the men’s singles final. Olympic champion Viktor Axelsen has overpowered India’s Lakshya Sen to win the All England Open men’s singles title 21-10, 21-15 in 53 minutes.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Prakash Padukone. On March 23, 1980, Prakash Padukone became the first Indian to win the All England Badminton Championships. Padukone clashed with Indonesia’s Liem Swie King in the summit clash at the Wembley Stadium. Padukone defeated his opponent 15-3, 15-10.
