WHO has collaborated with which company to distribute Covid vaccines under the Covax alliance?

(A) ** Pfizer

(B) Merck

(C) Roche

(D) Johnson & Johnson

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The collaboration between Gavi, SII, and the Gates Foundation supports the efforts of the ACT Accelerator’s vaccines pillar, also known as COVAX, co-led by Gavi, CEPI and the World Health Organization (WHO), to accelerate the development of COVID-19 vaccines and ensure rapid, global access to them.

Concept note-2: -COVAX is co-led by the Coalition for Epidemic Preparedness Innovations (CEPI), Gavi and the World Health Organization (WHO), alongside key delivery partner UNICEF. Its aim is to accelerate the development and manufacture of COVID-19 vaccines, and to guarantee fair and equitable access for every country in the world.

Concept note-3: -Pfizer and COVAX Pfizer is a proud partner to the COVAX Facility, an international initiative led by the World Health Organization (WHO), Gavi, the Vaccine Alliance and Coalition for Epidemic Preparedness Innovations (CEPI) focused on equitable access to COVID-19 vaccines around the world.

Concept note-4: -Both COVISHIELD™ (manufactured by Serum Institute of India Pvt Ltd) and COVID-19 Vaccine AstraZeneca (manufactured by AstraZeneca) are ChAdOx1 nCoV- 19 Corona Virus Vaccines (Recombinant).
