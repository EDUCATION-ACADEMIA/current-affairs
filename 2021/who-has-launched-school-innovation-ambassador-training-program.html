Who has launched School Innovation Ambassador Training Program?

(A) ** Dharmendra Pradhan

(B) Narendra Modi

(C) Amit Shah

(D) Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Education’s Innovation Cell and AICTE has launched School Innovation Ambassador Training Program in collaboration with CBSE and EMRS of Ministry of Tribal Affairs. Under this program, the teachers shall be trained on the following five modules.

Concept note-2: -Under this unique program Ministry of Education aims at training 50, 000 school teachers on Innovation, Entrepreneurship, IPR, Design Thinking, Product development, Idea generation etc.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of Education’s Innovation Cell (MIC), All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) jointly launched the School Innovation Council (SIC) and recognised over 12, 800 school teachers across India as Innovation Ambassadors.

Concept note-4: -“Ministry of Education has launched its new initiative in the field of innovation in collaboration with Tribal Affairs Ministry which will benefit the large number of schools for tribal children across the country.

Concept note-5: -Innovation Ambassadors will be given activities at regular intervals to create awareness, inculcate entrepreneurial mindset, and bring cognizance about start-ups, IPR, and the importance of Innovation ecosystem enablers and facilitators among students and fellow faculty members.
