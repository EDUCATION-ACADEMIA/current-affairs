Who has topped the Forbes India Rich List 2021 for the 14th consecutive year?

(A) Cyrus Poonawalla

(B) ** Mukesh Ambani

(C) Gautam Adani

(D) Lakshmi Mittal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliance Industries chairman Mukesh Ambani retained the top spot in the Forbes list of richest Indians in 2021, by adding $4 billion to his net worth amounting to $92.7 billion this year. With this, the 64-year-old industrialist has been named the wealthiest in the country for 14th year in a row since 2008.

Concept note-2: -Gautam Adani (net worth $150 billion) Adani Group Chairman Gautam Adani has a net worth of Rs 1, 211, 460.11 crore. According to Forbes, Adani had tripled his wealth last year and this year he has become India’s richest person for the first time.

Concept note-3: -With a total wealth of $92 billion, Mukesh Ambani, Chairman of Reliance Industries, topped the rich list for the 14th year in a row – adding $4 billion to his net worth in 2021, as per Forbes Rich List.

Concept note-4: -India’s richest wealth has increased by 50% amid pandemic, as per Forbes with Mukesh Ambani remaining the wealthiest Indian for the 14th year in a row, since 2008.

Concept note-5: -The Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) Chairman Mukesh Ambani has topped the Forbes India Rich list for 2021, released.
