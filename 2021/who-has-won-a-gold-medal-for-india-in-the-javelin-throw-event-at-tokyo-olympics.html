Who has won a Gold medal for India in the javelin throw event at Tokyo Olympics?

(A) Bajrang Punia

(B) Saina Nehwal

(C) ** Neeraj Chopra

(D) P V Sindhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On this day in 2021, Neeraj Chopra won the men’s javelin throw gold medal at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics. It was India’s first-ever Olympic medal in athletics and the realisation of a long-standing dream - one which legends like Milkha Singh and PT Usha had also chased but fallen short of by agonisingly narrow margins.

Concept note-2: -Indian javelin thrower DP Manu won the gold medal in the men’s javelin throw at the National Open Athletics Championships 2022 in Bengaluru on Tuesday. Former national champion Rohit Yadav won the silver medal with a best effort of 79.80m and Kishore Kumar Jena took bronze with a best of 78.05m.
