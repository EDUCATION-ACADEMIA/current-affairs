Who has won the F1 Russian Grand Prix 2021 and claimed his 100th Grand Prix win?

(A) ** Lewis Hamilton

(B) Max Verstappen

(C) Valtteri Bottas

(D) Juan Manuel Fangio

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lewis Hamilton wins the Russian Grand Prix, his 100th in total! He regains the lead in the driver standings too! Verstappen is second, Sainz third, Ricciardo fourth and Bottas fifth.

Concept note-2: -It was the 15th round of the 2021 Formula One World Championship. It was the tenth running of the Russian Grand Prix, and the eighth and final held in Sochi. It was won by Mercedes’ Lewis Hamilton, who became the first driver to win 100 Grands Prix and regained the championship lead over Max Verstappen.

Concept note-3: -F1: Hamilton reaches 100 wins with success at Russian Grand Prix – as it happened!

Concept note-4: -The 100th Grand Prix winner was Heikki Kovalainen at the 2008 Hungarian Grand Prix.

Concept note-5: -Following a Safety Car period at the Yas Marina Circuit, Red Bull’s Max Verstappen overtakes Lewis Hamilton on the final lap of the 2021 Formula 1 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix to take the race victory and win the 2021 drivers’ world championship.
