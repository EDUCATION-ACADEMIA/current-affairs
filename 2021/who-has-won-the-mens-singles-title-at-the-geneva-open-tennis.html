Who has won the Men’s Singles title at the Geneva Open tennis?

(A) ** Casper Ruud

(B) Stefanos Tsitsipas

(C) Denis Shapovalov

(D) Cameron Norrie

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Defending champion Casper Ruud defeated João Sousa in the final, 7–6, 4–6, 7–6 to win the singles tennis title at the 2022 Geneva Open.

Concept note-2: -He has won nine ATP Tour singles titles, eight of which were on clay courts. Ruud is the first Norwegian man to win an ATP singles title, to reach major finals (at the 2022 French Open and US Open finals), to reach a Masters 1000 final, and to enter the top 20 in the ATP rankings.
