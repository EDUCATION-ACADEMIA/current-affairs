Who has won the National Florence Nightingale Award 2021 for service amid COVID-19?

(A) Aswathy Kumar

(B) Bhagyalakshmi Rao

(C) ** Bhanumati Gheewala

(D) Clara Barton

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shashikala Pandey, posted at BD Pandey Hospital in Nainital, has been given this honor on her behalf for her dedication to patients and selfless service. Shashikala Pandey, who received the National Florence Nightingale Award for outstanding work in the field of nursing, is the patron of BD Pandey Hospital.

Concept note-2: -On 7 November, 2022, the President of India presented the National Florence Nightingale Awards for the year 2021 to the Nursing professionals. The award was conferred by the President to two nurses Shashikala Pandey and Ganga Joshi from Kumaon, Uttarakhand.

Concept note-3: -Meirs, Martha M. Russell, Mary E. Gladwin, and Alma E. Foerster, and three German nurses including Elsbeth von Keudell.

Concept note-4: -The President of India, Smt Droupadi Murmu presented the National Florence Nightingale Awards (NFNA) for the year 2021 to the Nursing professionals at Rashtrapati Bhavan.
