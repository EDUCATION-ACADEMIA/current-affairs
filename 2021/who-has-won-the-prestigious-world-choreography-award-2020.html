Who has won the prestigious ‘World Choreography Award 2020’?

(A) Remo D’Souza

(B) Terence Lewis

(C) Dharmesh Yelande

(D) ** Suresh Mukund

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Emmy Award-nominated choreographer Suresh Mukund has won the title of 10th Annual World Choreography Awards 2020 for the show World of Dance. He has become the first Indian to win this title. Earlier, Suresh’s team had won NBC’s World of Dance Season 3.

Concept note-2: -Emmy Award nominated choreographer Suresh Mukund, recently became the first Indian to win the 10th Annual World Choreography Award 2020.

Concept note-3: -In the Motion Picture category, the choreographers include Tony Award winner Justin Peck (West Side Story), Ashley Wallen (Cinderella) and Chris Scott (In The Heights).

Concept note-4: -Suresh Mukund won the award in the ‘TV REALITY SHOW/COMPETITION’ category, for his work on hit American TV reality show ‘World of Dance’.

Concept note-5: -The choreographer who has won most Rajat Kamal (Silver Lotus) for Best Choreography is Saroj Khan with 3 wins followed by Prabhu Deva, Raju Sundaram and Ganesh Acharya with two wins. Sundaram and Prabhu Deva, Raju Sundaram are the father and son trio who are honored by this award.
