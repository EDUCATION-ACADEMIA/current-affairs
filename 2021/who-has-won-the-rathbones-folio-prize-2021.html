Who has won the Rathbones Folio prize 2021?

(A) ** Carmen Maria Machado

(B) Val Howlett

(C) Karla Cornejo Villavicencio

(D) Kristen Arnett

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Prize was judged by Roger Robinson (Chair), Sinead Gleeson and Jon McGregor. Carmen Maria Machado’s In The Dream House: A Memoir wins the Rathbones Folio Prize 2021!

Concept note-2: -We were delighted to announce Carmen Maria Machado as the winner of the 2021 Rathbones Folio Prize for her breathtakingly inventive, unflinching memoir In the Dream House.

Concept note-3: -In 2023, the judges will create three distinct shortlists from these 80 titles in Fiction, Non-Fiction and Poetry. Each category winner, selected from a shortlist of four, will receive a £2, 000 prize.

Concept note-4: -The Rathbones Folio Prize, previously known as the Folio Prize and The Literature Prize, is a literary award that was sponsored by the London-based publisher The Folio Society for its first two years, 2014–2015. Starting in 2017 the sponsor is Rathbone Investment Management.

Concept note-5: -The Folio Book Illustration Award 2022 offers the opportunity for aspiring and established illustrators to provide one piece of artwork in response to the short story The Masque of the Red Death by Edgar Allan Poe.
