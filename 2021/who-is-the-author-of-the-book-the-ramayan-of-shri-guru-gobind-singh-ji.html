Who is the author of the book ‘The Ramayan of Shri Guru Gobind Singh Ji’?

(A) Rohit Baban Deo

(B) Sanjay Kishan Kaur

(C) Pushpa Virendra Ganediwala

(D) ** Baljit Kaur Tulsi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Baljit Kaur Tulsi Ji. The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has received the first copy of the book, ‘The Ramayana of Shri Guru Gobind Singh Ji’ penned by Late Mrs. Baljit Kaur Tulsi Ji, who is the mother of noted lawyer Shri KTS Tulsi Ji.

Concept note-2: -Mata Jeeto, Mata Sundri, and Mata Sahib Kaur. Guru Gobind Singh Ji (Gurmukhi: ) (January 5, 16671 - 21 October, 1708), born “Gobind Rai” at Patna Sahib, Bihar, India, was the tenth and last of the human form Gurus of Sikhism.

Concept note-3: -Guru Gobind Singh was a man of great intellectual attainments. He was a linguist familiar with Persian, Arabic, and Sanskrit as well as his native Punjabi. He further codified Sikh law, wrote martial poetry and music, and was the reputed author of the Sikh work called the Dasam Granth (“Tenth Volume”).
