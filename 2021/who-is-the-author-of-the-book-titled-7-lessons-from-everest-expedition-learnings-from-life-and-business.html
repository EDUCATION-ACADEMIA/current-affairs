Who is the author of the book titled “7 Lessons from Everest Expedition Learnings from Life and Business”?

(A) ** Aditya Gupta

(B) Pankaj Mathur

(C) Amit Prakash

(D) Sundar Patel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aditya Gupta, author, 7 Lessons from Everest; Expedition Learnings for Life and Business(Aditya Gupta)

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Aditya Gupta. A Delhi-based mountaineer, Aditya Gupta has authored a new book titled ‘7 Lessons from Everest’. He decided to raise Rs 1 crore through his upcoming book for Covid relief.

Concept note-3: -The way the author Aditya Gupta vividly describes each picture ensures you live through the experience and the beautifully articulated & thought provoking lessons take you on an introspection journey, making you feel positive & spirited to handle the challenges life throws at us.

Concept note-4: -Ascent of Everest by John Hunt | Waterstones.

Concept note-5: -Aditya Gupta: Using the power of focus while expecting to deal with the unexpected, and moving one step at a time while being friends with fear can help you achieve anything. Everest is just a metaphor for anything challenging that one decides to achieve.
