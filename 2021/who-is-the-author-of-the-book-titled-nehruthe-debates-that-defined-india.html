Who is the author of the book titled “Nehru:The Debates that Defined India”?

(A) ** Tripurdaman Singh and Adeel Hussain

(B) Upinder Singh and Shivam Shankar Singh

(C) Rudrangshu Mukherjee and Saumya Roy

(D) Manreet Sodhi Someshwar and Amartya Sen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Adeel Hussain is an assistant professor at Leiden University and a senior research affiliate at the Max Planck Institute for Comparative Public Law and International Law in Heidelberg.

Concept note-2: -Jawaharlal Nehru, India’s first Prime Minister, wrote the book titled, ‘The Discovery of India’. In the book, he spoke about diversity in India and the unity among all the citizens.

Concept note-3: -An Autobiography, also known as Toward Freedom (1936), is an autobiographical book written by Jawaharlal Nehru while he was in prison between June 1934 and February 1935, and before he became the first Prime Minister of India.

Concept note-4: -Description: This book was written by Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru during his incarceration at the Ahmednagar Fort (1942-1946), and provides a broad view of Indian history, philosophy and culture, from the prerspective of a freedom fighter.
