Who is the author of the book titled ‘Raj Kapoor:The Master at Work’, released by Vice President M Venkaiah Naidu?

(A) Subhadra Sen Gupta

(B) Amit Ranjan

(C) Pradeep Magazine

(D) ** Rahul Rawail

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Rahul Rawail. Vice President M. Venkaiah Naidu released the memoir ‘Raj Kapoor: The Master at Work’ on 14 December 2021. Published by Bloomsbury India, the book is written by filmmaker Rahul Rawail, who served as an assistant director to Raj Kapoor on films like ‘’Bobby”.

Concept note-2: -About the Author Rahul Rawail is an Indian film director who worked as an assistant director to Raj Kapoor and was closely associated with him for several years. He is known for directing films like Love Story (1981), Betaab (1983), Arjun (1985), Dacait (1987), Anjaam (1994) and Arjun Pandit (1999).

Concept note-3: -Former Vice President M Venkaiah Naidu today released a book titled New India - a compilation of selected articles of former Union Minister and Padma Vibhushan Arun Jaitley on his death anniversary today in New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -The cover of ‘Raj Kapoor: The Master At Work’, published by Bloomsbury. Vice President M Venkaiah Naidu on Tuesday released the book “Raj Kapoor: The Master At Work” and remembered the late showman for his invaluable contribution to making Indian cinema “a vehicle of cultural diplomacy” across the world.

Concept note-5: -Venkaiah Naidu virtually released a book titled “Future of Higher Education – Nine Mega Trends”, during an event hosted by ICT Academy. The book has been authored by CA V Pattabhi Ram, Author, Public Speaker, and Teacher.
