Who is the author of the book titled-The Sage with Two Horns book?

(A) Sudha Goyal

(B) ** Sudha Murty

(C) Sudha Murli

(D) Sudha Rai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sudha Murty was born in 1950 in Shiggaon, north Karnataka. A prolific writer in English and Kannada, she has written novels, technical books, travelogues, collections of short stories and non-fiction pieces, and bestselling books for children.

Concept note-2: -“The Sage with Two Horns” has stories ranging from quarrels among gods and the follies of great sages to the benevolence of kings and the virtues of ordinary mortals. Murty says there is a powerful force in the universe that can be called by any name.

Concept note-3: -The chairperson of the Infosys Foundation and a renowned author, Sudha Murty has published her new book titled “The Sage with Two Horns: Unusual Tales from Mythology”, the 5th and last book of the “Unusual Tales from Mythology” series.
