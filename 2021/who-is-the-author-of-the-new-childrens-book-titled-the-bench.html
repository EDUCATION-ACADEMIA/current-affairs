Who is the author of the new children’s book titled ‘The Bench’?

(A) ** Meghan Markle

(B) Melania Trump

(C) Ivanka Trump

(D) Prince Harry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Bench is a 2021 picture book for children written by Meghan, Duchess of Sussex. It was published on June 8, 2021, by Random House Children’s Books. Meghan has also narrated the audiobook of the story. The book is illustrated by Christian Robinson; it features Robinson’s first illustrations in watercolour.

Concept note-2: -In The Bench, Meghan, The Duchess of Sussex, touchingly captures the evolving and expanding relationship between father and son and reminds us of the many ways that love can take shape and be expressed in a modern family.

Concept note-3: -’ One source claimed to the outlet that Meghan ‘is contemplating getting entirely candid about her time in the Royal limelight… and leaving no stone unturned. ‘ This is not the case in reality, Entertainment Tonight reported yesterday. The outlet wrote that it had learned reports of Meghan writing a memoir are untrue.

Concept note-4: -“The Bench” - which was released on June 8 - sold 3, 212 copies in its first week, the Daily Mail reported Wednesday, compared to No. 1 book “The Thursday Murder Club” by Richard Osman, which sold 28, 383 copies in the same time period.

Concept note-5: -The Duchess of Sussex’s illustrated book The Bench has officially reached No. 1 on The New York Times Bestseller list for children’s picture books since its release last week.
