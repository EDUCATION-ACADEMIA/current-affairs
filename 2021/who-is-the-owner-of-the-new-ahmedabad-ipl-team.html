Who is the owner of the new Ahmedabad IPL team?

(A) Adani Group

(B) ** CVC Capital Partners

(C) Glazers

(D) RPSG

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ahmedabad IPL Team Owner Details and Price: The Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise based in Ahmedabad city is won by CVC Capitals Partners by shelling out a mammoth sum of INR 5625 Crores. They were the second-highest bidder after RPSG Goenka Group and hence got the ownership of the franchise.

Concept note-2: -Gujarat Titans Team Players 2022 Once the franchise was allowed to commence its operations, they confirmed Ashish Nehra as the head coach and Vikram Solanki as the Director of Cricket.

Concept note-3: -The new Ahmedabad-based IPL team will be called Gujarat Titans. The franchise revealed the name on Wednesday, ahead of the IPL auction on the weekend. Titans, among two new entrants to the IPL along with Lucknow Super Giants, was bought by CVC Capital Partners (Irelia Company Pte Ltd) last October.
