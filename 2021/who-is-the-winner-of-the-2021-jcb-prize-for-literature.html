Concept note-1: -Mukundan Khalid Jawed won the prize for the year 2021 for his novel Delhi: A Soliloquy, originally written in Malayalam and translated into English by Fathima EV and Nandakumar K.

Concept note-2: -2022 JCB Prize for Literature: Urdu writer Khalid Jawed wins prestigious prize.
