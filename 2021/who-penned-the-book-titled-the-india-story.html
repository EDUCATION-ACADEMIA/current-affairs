Who penned the book titled “The India Story”?

(A) Raghuram Rajan

(B) ** Bimal Jalan

(C) Girish Patel

(D) Bisha Roy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bimal Jalan. In July 2021, a book titled ‘The India Story’ was published which has been written by Bimal Jalan. Dr Bimal Jalan was the Governor of the Reserve Bank of India from November 22, 1997, to September 6, 2003.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is I Do What I Do.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Ruskin Bond. Author Ruskin Bond has come up with his book, ‘’A Little Book of India: Celebrating 75 years of Independence”. It pays homage to the country that has been his home for 84 years.

Concept note-4: -Notes: The Union Minister of State (I/C) Power R. K. Singh has launched a book named “The India Story”. The book enlists the initiatives and schemes launched by the Government of India to accelerate energy transition.
