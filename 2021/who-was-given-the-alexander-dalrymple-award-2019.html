Who was given the Alexander Dalrymple Award 2019?

(A) ** Vinay Badhwar

(B) Shekhar Das

(C) Vikram Batra

(D) Ian Cordozo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Alexander Dalrymple Award presented to Vice Admiral Vinay Badhwar. The 2019 Alexander Dalrymple Award has been presented to Vice Adm Vinay Badhwar, National Hydrographer to the Government of India, in recognition of his outstanding contribution to Indian hydrography and across the wider Indian Ocean region.

Concept note-2: -Vice Admiral Vinay Badhwar has been awarded the Alexander Dalrymple Award 2019. He is India’s Chief Hydrographer. Hydrography is the science that measures and describes the physical features of the navigable portion of the Earth’s surface and adjoining coastal areas.

Concept note-3: -The UK Hydrographic Office (UKHO) has presented the 2021 Alexander Dalrymple Award to the US Office of Coast Survey (OCS) in recognition of its outstanding contribution to global hydrography.

Concept note-4: -Vice Admiral Adhir Arora is married to Mrs Tanvi Arora who is a homemaker and their son is a Chemical Engineer.

Concept note-5: -Who is Vice Admiral Vinay Badhwar? Vinay Badhwar had joined the National Defence Academy in 1978 and was commissioned in the Indian Navy in July 1982. After he completed his watchkeeping on board a Destroyer, Badhwar was appointed as the Navigating Officer of a Patrol Board.
