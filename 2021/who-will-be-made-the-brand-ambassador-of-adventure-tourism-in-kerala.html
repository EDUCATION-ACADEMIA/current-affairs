Who will be made the brand ambassador of adventure tourism in Kerala?

(A) ** P.R. Sreejesh

(B) Virat Kohli

(C) P V Sindhu

(D) Sania Nehwal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sreejesh will be made brand ambassador of adventure tourism in Kerala. The feasibility of operating a solar boat in Muziris would be probed. He also visited the ancestral home of social reformer Sahodaran Ayyapan.

Concept note-2: -Why In News. Olympian Parattu Ravindran Sreejesh (PR Sreejesh), goalkeeper and former captain of the Indian national hockey team as the brand ambassador of adventure tourism.

Concept note-3: -Olympian Parattu Raveendran Sreejesh (PR Sreejesh), goalkeeper and former captain of the Indian National Hockey team is set to be named as the brand ambassador of adventure tourism in Kerala. Sreejesh was a part of the Indian Hockey team that won the bronze medal at the Tokyo 2020 Olympic games.

Concept note-4: -PV Sindhu is the precise personification of the ideology of the brand. Just as the brand firmly believes that “Every good thing takes time – Fursat Se” similarly PV Sindhu has proven this to be true with her years of dedication & hard work towards the sport.
