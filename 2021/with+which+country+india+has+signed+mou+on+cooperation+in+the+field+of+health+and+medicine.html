With which country, India has signed MoU on Cooperation in the field of Health and Medicine?

(A) ** Denmark

(B) UK

(C) USA

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This MoU will further strengthen relations between India and the USA within the framework of international scientific and technological cooperation in fields of mutual interest.

Concept note-2: -The Union Cabinet chaired by the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has approved the signing of Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) in the field of Judicial Cooperation between India and Judicial Service Commission of the Republic of Maldives.

Concept note-3: -The MoU has been signed in December, 2021. MoU would reinforce bilateral cooperation between the two countries in the area of urban water security.

Concept note-4: -As a follow up of Letter of Intent, an MoU between DoWR, RD & GR, Government of India and Ministry of Environment. Government of Denmark was signed on 12.09. 2022 during visit of Hon’ble Minister of Jal Shakti to Denmark.

Concept note-5: -Singapore-India sign MoU for cooperation in science, technology and innovation.
