With which port CNB Logitech has signed an MoU to organize the movement of containers in the port?

(A) ** Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust

(B) Cochin Port Trust

(C) Mormugao Port Trust

(D) New Mangalore Port Trust

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jawaharlal Nehru Port. Jawaharlal Nehru Port, abbreviated as JNPT, also known as Nhava Sheva, is the largest container port in India.

Concept note-2: -Jawaharlal Nehru Port (JNPT port code) or JLN Port, also known as Nhava Sheva Port code. Kattupalli Port Sea (inkat1) port is located in India with the INKAT1 port code name.

Concept note-3: -The Jawaharlal Nehru Port Authority (JNPA) at Navi Mumbai is the premier container handling port in India, accounting for around 50% of the total containerized cargo volume, across the major ports of India.

Concept note-4: -The Port of Nhava Sheva was commissioned in 1989, as a satellite port to relieve the Port of Mumbai from heavy congestion. As a small port in its early years, it only had single terminals for bulk cargos and containers. But now as India’s busiest container port, it also handles liquid and dry bulk cargo.
