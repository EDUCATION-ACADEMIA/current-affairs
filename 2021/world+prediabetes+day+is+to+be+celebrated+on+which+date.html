World Prediabetes Day is to be celebrated on which date?

(A) August 12

(B) August 15

(C) ** August 14

(D) August 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -14 November is World Diabetes Day Established in 1991 by the International Diabetes Federation with support from WHO in response to growing concerns about the health and economic threat posed by diabetes, World Diabetes Day became an official UN day in 2006.

Concept note-2: -It is marked every year on 14 November, the birthday of Sir Frederick Banting, who co-discovered insulin along with Charles Best in 1922. WDD is the world’s largest diabetes awareness campaign reaching a global audience of over 1 billion people in more than 160 countries.

Concept note-3: -Every November 14, World Diabetes Day is commemorated, which is an opportunity to raise awareness about the impact of diabetes on the health of people.

Concept note-4: -This year’s theme, ‘access to diabetes education’, underpins the larger multi-year theme of ‘access to care’. In the lead-up to and on 14 November, WHO will highlight not only the challenges, but more importantly the solutions, to scaling-up access to diabetes medicines and care.
