World Toilet Day (WTD) is celebrated every year on which date?

(A) November 16

(B) November 18

(C) November 17

(D) ** November 19

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In a bid to help break taboos around toilets and make sanitation for all a global development priority, the United Nations designated 19 November as World Toilet Day.

Concept note-2: -World Toilet Day is held every year on 19 November. It has been an annual United Nations Observance since 2013.

Concept note-3: -World Toilet Day 2022 focuses on the impact of the sanitation crisis on groundwater. This observance, held annually since 2013, celebrates toilets and raises awareness of the 3.6 billion people living without access to safely managed sanitation.

Concept note-4: -World Toilet Day 2022 observed on 19 November.

Concept note-5: -The theme of World Toilet Day 2022 is ‘Sanitation and Groundwater’ and the campaign title is ‘Making the invisible visible’, same as World Water Day 2022.
