World Toilet Day (WTD) is observed on which date?

(A) November 18

(B) November 17

(C) November 20

(D) ** November 19

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The link between sanitation and groundwater cannot be overlooked. This World Toilet Day, let’s make the invisible visible. World Toilet Day is held every year on 19 November. It has been an annual United Nations Observance since 2013.

Concept note-2: -World Toilet Day 2022 focuses on the impact of the sanitation crisis on groundwater. This observance, held annually since 2013, celebrates toilets and raises awareness of the 3.6 billion people living without access to safely managed sanitation.

Concept note-3: -The 2022 World Toilet Day is being observed on the theme of ‘Making the Invisible Visible’. In this context, discuss various government’s programs to improve sanitation. Every year on November 19, the UN observes World Toilet Day.

Concept note-4: -World. November 19 was designated as World Toilet Day by the United Nations General Assembly, and is coordinated every year by a partnership between UN-Water and governments around the world.
