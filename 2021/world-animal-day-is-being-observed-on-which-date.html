World Animal Day is being observed on which date?

(A) October 05

(B) ** October 04

(C) October 06

(D) October 03

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is World Animal Day all about? World Animal Day is an annual event on the 4th of October, celebrating animal rights and welfare across the globe. It’s an important day and has been marked since 1925, with a growing international community striving to end the needless suffering of all animals.

Concept note-2: -What is World Animal Day all about? World Animal Day is an annual event on the 4th of October, celebrating animal rights and welfare across the globe. It’s an important day and has been marked since 1925, with a growing international community striving to end the needless suffering of all animals.

Concept note-3: -World Animal Day is celebrated every year on October 4 to raise awareness about improving the welfare standards of animals across the globe.

Concept note-4: -Theme this year The theme for World Animal Day 2022 is a “shared planet". It means that the world belongs to every living creature and not just humans.

Concept note-5: -World Animal Welfare Day is celebrated on 4 October to raise awareness among people about taking actions worldwide for the rights of animals as well as welfare.
