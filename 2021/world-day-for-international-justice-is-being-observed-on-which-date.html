World Day for International Justice is being observed on which date?

(A) July 12

(B) August 17

(C) ** July 17

(D) September 13

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -July 17 is observed as the World Day for International Justice all around the world. The day aims to raise awareness about the crimes that put peace, security and well-being of the world at risk. The day also promotes victims’ rights.

Concept note-2: -July 17 is observed as the World Day for International Justice all around the world. The day aims to raise awareness about the crimes that put peace, security and well-being of the world at risk. The day also promotes victims’ rights.

Concept note-3: -The theme of World Day for International Justice 2022 is “Achieving Social Justice through Formal Employment”.

Concept note-4: -World Day for International Justice is observed on July 17 to commemorate the organisations that work to bring justice to the victims of international criminal acts.

Concept note-5: -World Day of International Justice: This day is celebrated annually to mark the anniversary of signing the Rome Statue Treaty in 1998 that to the establishment of International Criminal Court. World Day of International Justice is celebrated every year on July, 17.
