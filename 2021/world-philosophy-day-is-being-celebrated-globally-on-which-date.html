World Philosophy Day is being celebrated globally on which date?

(A) November 15

(B) ** November 18

(C) November 16

(D) November 17

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By celebrating World Philosophy Day each year, on the third Thursday of November, UNESCO underlines the enduring value of philosophy for the development of human thought, for each culture and for each individual.

Concept note-2: -World Philosophy Day is an international day proclaimed by UNESCO to be celebrated every 3rd Thursday of November. It was first celebrated on 21 November 2002.

Concept note-3: -In a hyper-technologized world, the very concept of humanity is in constant evolution.

Concept note-4: -World Philosophy Day was first celebrated on November 15, 2002. The day was created by UNESCO as a way to encourage critical thinking and philosophical debate around the world. The date was chosen to coincide with the anniversary of the death of René Descartes, one of the most famous philosophers in history.

Concept note-5: -World Philosophy Day was first celebrated on November 21, 2002. UNESCO aimed to raise public awareness about philosophy and how vital it is, not only to connect the world today but to the past and present, as well as for a better understanding of the future.
