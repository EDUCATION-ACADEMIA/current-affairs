World Pneumonia Day is observed on which date?

(A) November 11

(B) ** November 12

(C) November 13

(D) November 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Pneumonia Day is a global event observed every year on 12 November to spread awareness and educate people to combat Pneumonia disease. What is Pneumonia?

Concept note-2: -WORLD PNEUMONIA DAY 2022: HISTORY AND SIGNIFICANCE The day first came into effect on November 12, 2009, under the initiative named ‘Stop Pneumonia’. This was a global coalition against child pneumonia. Later, WHO and UNICEF introduced an integrated plan to prevent and control the disease globally.

Concept note-3: -India alone accounts for 23 per cent of the global pneumonia burden. World Pneumonia Day is observed on November 12 every year. The day aims to raise awareness about pneumonia and highlights its symptoms, prevention and treatment.

Concept note-4: -The specific theme of World Pneumonia Day 2022 is ‘Pneumonia Affects Everyone. ‘ How important is it to improve the visibility of this disease and the people it affects?
