Yoweri Museveni has won the sixth term as the President of which country?

(A) USA

(B) Guyana

(C) Palestine

(D) ** Uganda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Museveni won with 75.5 percent of the vote from a turnout of 72.6 percent of eligible voters.

Concept note-2: -The president leads the executive branch of the government of Uganda and is the commander-in-chief of the Uganda People’s Defence Force. The incumbent Yoweri Museveni came to power in 1986 and is the longest serving president of Uganda, ahead of Idi Amin who ruled from 1971 to 1979.

Concept note-3: -Idi Amin Dada Oumee (/ˈiːdi ɑːˈmiːn, ˈɪdi -/, UK also /- æˈmiːn/; c. 1925 – 16 August 2003) was a Ugandan military officer and politician who served as the third president of Uganda from 1971 to 1979.
