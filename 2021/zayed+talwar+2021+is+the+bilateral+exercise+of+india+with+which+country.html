‘Zayed Talwar 2021’ is the bilateral exercise of India with which country?

(A) USA

(B) ** UAE

(C) Japan

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is UAE. Indian Navy undertook bilateral exercise ‘Zayed Talwar 2021’ with UAE Navy off the coast of Abu Dhabi. INS Kochi, with two integral Sea King MK 42B helicopters, deployed in the Persian Gulf, participated in the exercise.

Concept note-2: -The Indian Navy took part in a bilateral exercise with the United Arab Emirates (UAE) Navy off the coast of Abu Dhabi on 7th August 2021. The bilateral naval exercise - ‘Zayed Talwar 2021’- was designed to enhance interoperability and synergy between the two navies.

Concept note-3: -The United Arab Emirates (UAE) took part in the Varuna maritime exercise for the first time. Since 1993, the Indian and French navies have conducted bilateral maritime exercises. These exercises have been known as ‘VARUNA’ since 2001.

Concept note-4: -Joint Military Exercises with the UAE: With the UAE, India has In-UAE BILAT (bilateral naval exercise) as well as Desert Eagle-II (bilateral air force exercise).

Concept note-5: -Konkan Shakti 2021 is the first bilateral tri-service exercise between the armed forces of India and the UK.
