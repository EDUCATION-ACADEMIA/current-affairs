A document published by the Zoological Survey of India, India added how many species to the faunal database in 2021?

(A) 444

(B) 642

(C) ** 540

(D) 642

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India added 540 species to its faunal database in 2021 takingthe total number of animal species to 1,03,258. The country also added 315 taxa tothe Indian flora during 2021, taking the number of floral taxa in the country to55,048.

Concept note-2: Scientists from the Zoological Survey of India(ZSI) have found a new mammal species in the country - the White CheekedMacaque.

Concept note-3: Other ancient creature discoveries in 2021 includedspiders trapped in amber, a plant-eating crocodile relative and a “Jurassic mouse”that once ran between the feet of dinosaurs 166 million years ago in whatis now Scotland.

Concept note-4: It accounts for 7-8% of all recordedspecies, and is home to 49,000 plant species and 91,000 different animalspecies.

Concept note-5: Initially, the ZSI had eight regional centers across India.Currently, there are 16 regional centers spread across the country. The headquartersis in Kolkata.
