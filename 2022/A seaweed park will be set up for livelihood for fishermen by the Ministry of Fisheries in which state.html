A seaweed park will be set up for livelihood for fishermen by the Ministry of Fisheries in which state?

(A) ** Tamil Nadu

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Kerala

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Union Minister of State for Information and Broadcasting.Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying Dr L Murugan today said for the firsttime in the country, a seaweed park will be set up in Tamil Nadu to improve thelivelihood of fishermen.

Concept note-2: Budget 2021 had proposed amulti-purpose seaweed park to be set up in Tamil Nadu as part of promoting seaweedcultivation.

Concept note-3: The Seaweed park in Tamil Nadu , with aninvestment of Rs. 100 crores, will be developed as a hub to serve as a One Stop Parkfor the entire seaweed value chain linking all the activities from pre- and post-harvestinfrastructure, logistics, marketing, export promotion, innovation, technologyincubation etc.

Concept note-4: Among the seaweed species are Gelidiellaacerosa, Gracilaria edulis, Gracilaria crassa, Gracilaria verrucosa, Sargassum spp.,and Turbinaria spp.

Concept note-5: It is the practice of cultivating andharvesting seaweed. In its simplest form, it consists of the management of naturallyfound batches. In its most advanced form, it consists of fully controlling the life cycleof the algae.
