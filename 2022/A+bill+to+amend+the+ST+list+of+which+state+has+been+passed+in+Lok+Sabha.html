A bill to amend the ST list of which state has been passed in Lok Sabha?

(A) Bihar

(B) Maharashtra

(C) ** Chhattisgarh

(D) Jharkhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Three essential Constitution (ST) Order Amendment Bills have been passed in the Parliament during the Winter Session. In relation to the state of Tamil Nadu, the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Second Amendment) Bill, 2022, was passed unanimously in Rajya Sabha on 22.12. 2022.

Concept note-2: -Lok Sabha today passed the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Fifth Amendment) Bill, 2022. The Bill will amend the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order, 1950, for the inclusion of the thirteen communities, in the list of Scheduled Tribes in relation to the State of Chhattisgarh.

Concept note-3: -The Wild Life (Protection) Amendment Bill, 2022 seeks to increase the species protected under the law, and implement the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora.

Concept note-4: -Article 335 “The claims of the members of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes shall be taken into consideration, consistently with the maintenane of efficiency of administration, in the making of appointments to services and posts in connection with • the affairs of the Union or of a State."
