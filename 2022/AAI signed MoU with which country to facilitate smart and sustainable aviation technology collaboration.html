AAI signed MoU with which country to facilitate smart and sustainable aviation technology collaboration?

(A) Belgium

(B) ** Sweden

(C) Germany

(D) Turkey

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: AAI signs MoU with Sweden to facilitate smart andsustainable aviation technology collaboration. Airports Authority of India (AAI) andLFV Air Navigation Services of Sweden (LUFTFARTSVERKET) signed aMemorandum of Understanding today at the Corporate Headquarters of AAI inNew Delhi.

Concept note-2: AAI has been constituted under an Act ofParliament, namely, the Airports Authority of India Act, 1994 and has beenconstituted as a Statutory Body.

Concept note-3: AAI manages a total of137 airports which include 24 International airports (3 Civil Enclaves), 10Custom Airports (4 Civil Enclaves) and 103 Domestic airports (23 CivilEnclaves).

Concept note-4: The Airports Authority of India, or AAI, is a publicsector enterprise under the ownership of the Ministry of Civil Aviation, Governmentof India.

Concept note-5: AAI: Airport Authority of IndiaAAI stands for AirportAuthority of India. It works under the Ministry of Civil Aviation. It is responsiblefor creating, maintaining and upgrading the civil aviation infrastructure inIndia.
