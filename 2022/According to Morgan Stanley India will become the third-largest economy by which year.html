According to Morgan Stanley, India will become the third-largest economy by which year?

(A) ** 2027

(B) 2026

(C) 2029

(D) 2025

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India to be third largest economy by 2030: S&P Global, Morgan Stanley.

Concept note-2: -India will become third largest economy by 2035 : CEBR.

Concept note-3: -India is on track to become the world’s third largest economy by 2027, surpassing Japan and Germany, and have the third largest stock market by 2030, thanks to global trends and key investments the country has made in technology and energy.

Concept note-4: -India will surpass Germany and Japan by 2027 to become the third-largest economy: Gajendra Shekhawat. Union Jal Shakti Minister Gajendra Singh Shekhawat on December 16 asserted that India will become the third largest economy by 2027.
