(A) Falguni Nayar

(B) ** Soma Mondal

(C) Suchi Mukherjee

(D) Roshni Nadar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Recently, Forbes Asia revealed its annual list of Asia’s PowerBusinesswomen featuring twenty women from the Asia-Pacific region. Ghazal Alagh,Namita Thapar and Soma Mandal are the three Indian women entrepreneurswhohave been named in the 2022 Forbes list of powerful businessmen in the Asia-Pacificregion.

Concept note-2: Kiran Mazumdar-ShawKiran Mazumdar-Shaw is anIndian billionaire entrepreneur. She is the chairperson and the managing director ofBiocon Limited based in Bangalore. She is also the former chairperson of IndianInstitute of Management, Bangalore.
