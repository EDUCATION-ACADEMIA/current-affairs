Advanced light Helicopter MK-III commissioned in which city?

(A) Pune

(B) Mumbai

(C) Delhi

(D) ** Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Indian Coast Guard Advanced Light Helicopter Mk-IIIsquadron, 840 Sqn (CG), commissioned in Chennai.

Concept note-2: UPSCMains. Notes: An Indian Coast Guard Advanced Light Helicopter (ALH) Mk-IIIsquadron- 840 Sqn (CG), was commissioned by DG VS Pathania at ICG Air Station,Chennai. The ALH Mk-III helicopters are indigenously manufactured by HindustanAeronautics Limited (HAL).

Concept note-3: Advanced Light Helicopter (ALH-DHRUV) The indigenously designed and developed Advanced Light Helicopter(ALH-DHRUV) is a twin engine, multi-role, multi-mission new generation helicopterin the 5.5 ton weight class. The basic Helicopter is produced in skid version andwheeled version.
