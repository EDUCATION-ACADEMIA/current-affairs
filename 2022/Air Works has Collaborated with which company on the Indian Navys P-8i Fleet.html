Air Works has Collaborated with which company on the Indian Navy’s P-8i Fleet?

(A) Embraer

(B) Airbus

(C) Lockheed Martin

(D) ** Boeing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Air Works and Boeing signed a strategic collaborationlast year for the MRO of two critical Boeing defence platforms in India -the P-8I operated by the Indian Navy (IN) and the VIP transport fleet(BBJ) operated by the Indian Air Force (IAF).

Concept note-2: AirWorks, an Indian MRO company, is partnering with the Boeing Company toconduct heavy maintenance checks on three Indian Navy P-8i maritime patrolaircraft.

Concept note-3: Q. Which firm supplied P-8I long range maritimepatrol aircraft to Indian Navy? Notes: The Indian Navy received its 11th P-8Ilong range maritime patrol aircraft from Boeing. The Navy has signed acontract of 12 P-8Is from Boeing in two batches.

Concept note-4: BoeingIndia has collaborated with Air Works Group to deliver maintenance, repairand overhaul (MRO) services for the Indian Navy’s P-8I maritime patrolaircraft fleet.

Concept note-5: Navy gets full complement of P-8I maritimeaircraft“Boeing delivered the 12th P-8I maritime patrol aircraft to India on February23,” a company statement said. “The P-8I is an integral part of the IndianNavy’s fleet and has surpassed 35,000 flight hours since it was inducted in2013.”
