All 100 smart cities will get integrated command and control centres by which date?

(A) ** August 15

(B) May 30

(C) July 31

(D) December 31

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"Out of the 100 smart cities, 80 already have their Command and Control Centre. We have to complete the work in the remaining 20 cities by August 15, 2022,” he said.

Concept note-2: -’Smart City Mission’ projects across 100 smart cities inch towards completion ahead of June 2023 deadline, Government News, ET Government.

Concept note-3: -The smart city mission launch date is June 25, 2015, and Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched the initiative. The Union Ministry of Urban Development is responsible for implementing the mission.

Concept note-4: -With the selection of Shillong, the total proposed investment in the finally selected 100 cities under the Smart Cities Mission would be Rs. 2,05,018 crores.

Concept note-5: -Shillong selected as the 100th Smart City.
