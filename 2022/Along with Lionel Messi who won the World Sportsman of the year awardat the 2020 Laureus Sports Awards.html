Along with Lionel Messi, who won the World Sportsman of the year awardat the 2020 Laureus Sports Awards?

(A) Marc Marquez

(B) Rafael Nadal

(C) Tiger Woods

(D) ** Lewis Hamilton

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In a moment of sporting history, the greatest Formula One driver of his generation Lewis Hamilton and Lionel Messi, the world’s greatest footballer, were declared joint winners of the prestigious Laureus World Sportsman of the Year Award, in front of a glittering international audience in Berlin.

Concept note-2: -In the 2020 ceremony, Argentine Lionel Messi became the first footballer to win the Laureus World Sportsman of the Year award. He is also the first athlete to win it coming from a team sport.

Concept note-3: -With the incredible backing of Indian cricket fans, who united in support of their hero, the Laureus Sporting Moment Award (2000-2020) public fan vote was won by Sachin Tendulkar.

Concept note-4: -The 2022 award was won by Dutch racing driver Max Verstappen.
