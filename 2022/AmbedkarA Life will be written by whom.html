Ambedkar:A Life’ will be written by whom?

(A) ** Shashi Tharoor

(B) Naresh Patel

(C) Jayesh Gautam

(D) Anil Tembhre

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Ambedkar: A Life is a book about B. R. Ambedkar written bythe senior leader of the Indian National Congress, Shashi Tharoor. The book isdivided into two parts, first part containing the initial phases of his life and Secondpart explains his life in Indian politics.

Concept note-2: The Buddha andHis Dhamma was B.R. Ambedkar’s last work. Published posthumously, itpresented a radical reorientation of Buddhist thought and literature, aptly callednavayana . .

Concept note-3: An Undelivered Speech: Annihilation of Caste:Annihilation of Caste, and Castes in India: Their Mechanism, Genesis andDevelopment.

Concept note-4: In 1920, Ambedkar entered into the world ofnewspapers. He started his first newspaper, Mooknayak, on January 31, 1920. It ranfor three years before being closed. Later, he went on to found three more newspapers– Bahishkrut Bharat (1927-1929), Janata (1930-56), and Prabuddha Bharat(1956).
