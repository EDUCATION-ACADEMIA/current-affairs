Anti-Tobacco Day or World No Tobacco Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** May 31

(B) May 28

(C) May 29

(D) May 27

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Every year, on 31 May, the World Health Organization (WHO) and global partners celebrate World No Tobacco Day (WNTD).

Concept note-2: -31 May is World No Tobacco Day In 1987, the World Health Assembly passed Resolution WHA40. 38, calling for 7 April 1988 to be a “a world no-smoking day.” In 1988, Resolution WHA42. 19 was passed, calling for the celebration of World No Tobacco Day, every year on 31 May.

Concept note-3: -World No Tobacco Day - 31 May 2022.

Concept note-4: -World No Tobacco Day 2022 Webinar WHO in the Western Pacific will host a World No Tobacco Day webinar: “The Devastating Environmental Impact of Tobacco” on 26 May 2022, 10:00 - 11:30 (GMT+8 Manila time).

Concept note-5: -This year, the WHO has selected Jharkhand for the World No Tobacco Day (WNTD) Award-2022. India runs a National Tobacco Control Programme to educate people about the health implications of tobacco consumption. It also helps formulate policies and initiatives that could help the country achieve its goal.
