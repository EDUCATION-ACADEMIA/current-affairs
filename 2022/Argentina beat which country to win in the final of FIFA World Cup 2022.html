Argentina beat which country to win in the final of FIFA World Cup 2022?

(A) Fiji

(B) ** France

(C) Finland

(D) Estonia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Lionel Messi scores twice, Argentina beat France in 2022 WorldCup final in penalty drama.

Concept note-2: WHO DID ARGENTINA BEATIN THE FINAL? * Argentina beat defending champions France 4-2 on penalties,following a dramatic 3-3 draw when striker Kylian Mbappe completed a hat-trick inthe 118th minute to cancel out two goals by Messi and a strike by Angel DiMaria.

Concept note-3: After a tense penalty shootout, Argentina emergedvictorious. Argentina’s goalkeeper, Emiliano Martínez, blocked France’ssecond attempt, and French winger Kingsley Coman missed his penalty.The match ended with a final score of 3-3 and Argentina leading 4-2 inpenalties.

Concept note-4: Argentina has won the FIFA World Cup 2022 bydefeating France in the Penalty shootout by 4-2, after the match was drawn at 3-3 inthe regular time and extra time.
