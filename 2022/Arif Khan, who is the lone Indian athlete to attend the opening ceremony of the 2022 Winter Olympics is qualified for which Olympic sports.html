Arif Khan, who is the lone Indian athlete to attend the opening ceremony of the 2022 Winter Olympics is qualified for which Olympic sports?

(A) Skating

(B) Fencing

(C) ** Skiing

(D) Hockey

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: He is India’s lone entrant to the 2022 Winter Olympics and madehistory when he became the first Indian man to qualify for two events – slalomandgiant slalom – in a single Winter Olympics. Qualifying for his first Olympics tookplace on the slopes of Dubai’s artificial snow.

Concept note-2: Winter Olympics:Arif Khan shouldering India’s hopes in Beijing 2022 – watch live. The Indian Alpineskier will be India’s sole representative at the Beijing 2022 Games but will participatein two events – slalom and giant slalom.

Concept note-3: How many Indiansparticipated in 2022 Winter Olympics? Ans. Just 1 – Arif Mohammed Khan(slalom, giant slalom).

Concept note-4: Know more about skier Arif Khanwho led a small four-strong Indian contingent during the Winter Olympicsopening ceremony in Beijing amid a diplomatic boycott of the event by thecountry.
