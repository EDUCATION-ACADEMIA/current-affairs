Arun Bali, who was suffering from a disease called Myasthenia Gravis, was related to which field?

(A) Attorney

(B) ** Actor

(C) Architect

(D) Author

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Veteran actor Arun Bali, popular for his role in Kumkum, died on Friday after suffering from myasthenia gravis. It is a neuromuscular disorder that causes weakness in the skeletal muscles.

Concept note-2: -Causes of myasthenia gravis Antibodies-Myasthenia gravis is caused by an error in how nerve signals are transmitted to muscles. It occurs when communication between the nerve and muscle is interrupted at the neuromuscular junction-the place where nerve cells connect with the muscles they control.

Concept note-3: -Amitabh bachchan: Although he needs no introduction and is unquestionably a household name in the United States, many people are unaware that he has been battling myasthenia gravis, a serious and fatal disease, for quite some time.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Myasthenia gravis is a treatable disease. Cholinesterase inhibitors are useful, in particular, early in the disease. Pyridostigmine (Mestinon) is the main drug, which is effective to reduce the symptoms of myasthenia gravis, but it does not change the natural course of the disease.
