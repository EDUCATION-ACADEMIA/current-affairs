[SOURCES]

As per NASSCOM, the Assets under management for Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) Funds was recorded how much in 2021?

(A) USD 500 million

(B) USD 450 million

(C) ** USD 650 million

(D) USD 600 million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Assets under management for environmental, social andgovernance (ESG) funds increased 2.5 times to USD 650 million in India in financialyear 2021 on an annual basis, a top official of IT industry body Nasscom said onFriday.

Concept note-2: In our base case scenario, the share of ESG assets overtotal AuM would increase from 14.4% in 2021 to 21.5% in 2026, comprising morethan one-fifth of all assets (see ‘Growth in ESG investments will outpace the industryas a whole’).

Concept note-3: ESG Theme Outlook in India. According to aresearch report, ESG theme mutual fund assets grew 4.7 times betweenNovember 2019 and November 2021. As of November 2021, the total AUMwas INR 12,320 crore across ten funds.

Concept note-4: The reportidentified $8.4 trillion in U.S. investment assets at the beginning of 2022held by firms that factor environmental, social and governance into theirdecisions.

Concept note-5: Alternative Investment Funds: In 2022, the US SIFFoundation identified 383 alternative investment vehicles, including private equityand venture capital funds, hedge funds, and real estate investment trusts (REITs) orother property funds, managing $762 billion in AUM that considered ESGcriteria.
