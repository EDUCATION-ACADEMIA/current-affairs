As per Standards for Charging Infrastructure for Electric Vehicles (EV), who can set up a Public Charging Station?

(A) State Governments

(B) DISCOMs

(C) Ministry of Power

(D) ** Individuals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: 6. Database of Public EV Charging Stations: CentralElectricity Authority (CEA) shall create and maintain a national online database ofall the Public Charging Stations through DISCOMs. Appropriate protocols shall benotified by DISCOMs for this purpose which shall be mandatorily complied by thePCS/BCS.

Concept note-2: The Indian government has concluded that nolicense is required to build charging infrastructure as long as it meets the ‘Ministry ofPower’ standards. Reuters According to experts, the typical cost of installing an EVcharging point ranges between Rs 1 lakh to Rs 50 lakh, depending on the type ofchargers installed.

Concept note-3: Location of public charging stationsFurther,one charging station will be set up at every 25 km on both sides of the highways.There will be at least one fast-charging station with charging infrastructure at every100 km, one on each side of the highways for long-range and heavy-dutyEVs.
