As per the new COVID guidelines, COVID-19 patients have to undergo tests for which disease if cough persists for over 2-3 weeks?

(A) HIV

(B) Cancer

(C) ** Tuberculosis

(D) Diabetes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Usually TB affects the lungs. The symptoms of active TB inthe lungs include: Bad cough that lasts 3 weeks or longer. Coughing up bloodor sputum (mucus from deep inside the lungs)

Concept note-2: Signsand symptoms of active TB include: Coughing for three or more weeks.Coughing up blood or mucus. Chest pain, or pain with breathing orcoughing.

Concept note-3: Tuberculosis treatment response may bemeaningfully assessed by objectively monitoring the time spent coughing.This measure demonstrated that cough was increased in patients with TBrecurrence or co-morbid diabetes, but not because of drug resistance or HIVco-infection.

Concept note-4: In the case of COVID-19, this cough could lastfor as long as six months after the viral infection, especially if the patientcontracted Omicron because it is more airway dependent than the originalstrain.
