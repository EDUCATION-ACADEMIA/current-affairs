As of 2022, which country is the largest bilateral lender to Sri Lanka?

(A) China

(B) ** India

(C) Australia

(D) USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Regional rivals China and India are the biggest bilateral lenders to Sri Lanka, a country of 22 million people that is facing its worst economic crisis in seven decades.

Concept note-2: -India has become Sri Lanka’s biggest lender in 2022, providing the island nation with loans of USD 377 million as it battled an unprecedented political crisis and economic unrest, as per the Verite Research think tank. India is followed by the Asian Development Bank (ADB) with loans totalling USD 360 million.

Concept note-3: -India’s top lender SBI aims to achieve double-digit deposit growth.
