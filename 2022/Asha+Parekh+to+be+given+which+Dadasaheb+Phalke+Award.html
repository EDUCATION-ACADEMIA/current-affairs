Asha Parekh to be given which Dadasaheb Phalke Award?

(A) 23RD

(B) 31ST

(C) 53RD

(D) ** 52ND

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dadasaheb Phalke Award for the year 2020 was accorded to Ms Asha Parekh for her exemplary lifetime contribution to Indian Cinema.

Concept note-2: -At the Dada Saheb Phalke Film Festival in 2022, Farhan Akhtar won Best Actor for the film Toofan. Asha Parekh, a veteran actress, who arrived wearing a blue saree, was awarded the Outstanding Contribution To The Film Industry.

Concept note-3: -Rajinikanth won the Dadasaheb Phalke Award in 2019 and got awarded in 2021, whereas Asha Parekh won this award in 2020 but will receive it in 2022 due to the COVID pandemic.
