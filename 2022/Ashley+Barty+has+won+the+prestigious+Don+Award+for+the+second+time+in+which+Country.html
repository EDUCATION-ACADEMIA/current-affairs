Ashley Barty has won the prestigious ‘Don Award’ for the second time in which Country?

(A) America

(B) Haiti

(C) ** Australia

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ashleigh Barty wins Australia’s most prestigious Don Award for second time - Sportstar.

Concept note-2: -Barty is a three-time Grand Slam singles champion, and the last Australian to win a Grand Slam title, winning at the 2022 Australian Open. She is also a major doubles champion, having won the 2018 US Open with CoCo Vandeweghe. Barty won 15 singles titles and 12 doubles titles on the WTA Tour.

Concept note-3: -Ashleigh Barty, Former Tennis Star, Wins Don Award 2022- Australian Sport’s Highest Honour.

Concept note-4: -Barty is the first home Australian Open singles champion in 44 years. Find out how the proud Indigenous athlete won the hearts of the nation. Ash Barty is Australian Open tennis champion for the first time after a 6-3, 7-6 win over American Danielle Collins.

Concept note-5: -Nominees also include Nick Kyrgios, Alex de Minaur, Matthew Ebden, Storm Hunter, Max Purcell, and Ajla Tomljanovic, who’ve all performed quite well this year. Barty has won the award every year since 2017, though she shared the honors with de Minaur in 2018 and Dylan Alcott in 2021.
