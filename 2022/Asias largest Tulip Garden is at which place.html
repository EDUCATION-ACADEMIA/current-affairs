Asia’s largest Tulip Garden is at which place?

(A) Delhi

(B) Ladakh

(C) ** Srinagar

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: With 20 lakh tulips of 46 varieties in its lap, Asia’s largestTulip garden overlooking the world famous Dal Lake in Srinagar. It was thrown opento visitors and is spread over an area of about 15 hectares, the garden is situatedon the foothills of Zabarwan range.

Concept note-2: Keukenhof, theNetherlandsAlso known as the Garden of Europe, Keukenhof is among the largestflower gardens in the world, situated in Lisse, the Netherlands. The garden hsts anincredible collection of tulips. This garden is spread over an area of 79 acres andremains open to tourists from March till May.

Concept note-3: The largestbotanical garden of Asia is Acharya Jagadish Chandra Bose Indian Botanic Gardenlocated at Shibpur, Howrah India.

Concept note-4: Nishat Bagh is a terracedMughal garden built on the eastern side of the Dal Lake, close to Srinagar in the UTof Jammu and Kashmir, India. It is the second largest Mughal garden in the KashmirValley. The largest in size is the Shalimar Bagh, which is also located on the bank ofthe Dal Lake.
