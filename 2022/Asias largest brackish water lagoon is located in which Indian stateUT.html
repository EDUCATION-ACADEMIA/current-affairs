Asia’s largest brackish water lagoon is located in which Indian state/UT?

(A) Jammu and Kashmir

(B) Sikkim

(C) ** Odisha

(D) Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The largest brackish water lagoon chilika lake situated in Odisha.It spread across the Puri, Khurda and Ganjam districts of the state of Odisha on theeast coast of India, at the mouth of the River Daya, which flows into the Bay ofBengal, covering an area of more than 1,100 km.

Concept note-2: Chilika Lake isthe largest brackish water lake with estuarine character that sprawls along theeast coast of India.

Concept note-3: The vast and dream like ChilikaLagoon is situated on the east-coast of India. It is the largest brackish WaterLagoon with estuarine character that sprawls along the east coast. It is thelargest wintering ground for migratory waterfowl found anywhere on theIndian sub-continent.

Concept note-4: Separated from the Bay of Bengalby a narrow spit, Chilika is one of India’s largest brackish water coastallakes. Located south of the mouth of the Mahanadi River in unspoilt Orissa,this lake was once a bay of the ocean until it was silted up by monsoontides.

Concept note-5: Chilka is the largest brackish water lake in India. It islocated at the south of mouth of Mahanadi River in Orissa. It was a part of Bay ofBengal in ancient times. Then it was silted by monsoon tides and separated from theBay of Bengal.
