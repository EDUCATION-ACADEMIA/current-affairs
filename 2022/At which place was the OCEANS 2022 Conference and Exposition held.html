At which place was the OCEANS 2022 Conference and Exposition held?

(A) Bengaluru

(B) Lucknow

(C) ** Chennai

(D) Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -During the 3 days at OCEANS 2022 Chennai, about 84 sessions (51 Virtual and 33 on-site/ hybrid) were held that consists of 349 scheduled presentations.

Concept note-2: -Recently, the UN (United Nations) Ocean Conference 2022 was held to ensure global cooperation towards protection and sustenance of the Ocean ecosystem of the world. The conference was co-hosted by the Governments of Kenya and Portugal. Minister of Earth Sciences led the Indian delegation at UN Ocean Conference.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is IIT Madras and National Institute of Ocean Technology, Chennai. IIT Madras and the National Institute of Ocean Technology, Chennai, conducted OCEANS 2022 Conference and Exposition in February 2022.
