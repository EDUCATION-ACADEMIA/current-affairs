Atal Tunnel has received the ‘Best Infrastructure Project’ award. Atal Tunnel is located in which state/UT?

(A) Uttrakhand

(B) Ladakh

(C) Jammu & Kashmir

(D) ** Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Atal Tunnel (also known as Rohtang Tunnel), named afterformer Prime Minister of India, Atal Bihari Vajpayeeis a highway tunnel built underthe Rohtang Pass in the eastern Pir Panjal range of the Himalayas on the Leh-ManaliHighway in Himachal Pradesh, India.

Concept note-2: Notes: The Border RoadsOrganization (BRO) engineering marvel, Atal Tunnel, built-in Rohtang in HimachalPradesh, received the Indian Building Congress (IBC) ‘Best Infrastructure Project’award in New Delhi. More than thirty state-of-the-art infrastructure projects werenominated for the award.

Concept note-3: 03 June 2022: Manali-Atal tunnelhighway is open for all the vehicles (including tourists). Tourists need not to bookany permit to travel/traverse the tunnel or go further to Keylong, Jispa,Sissu, Udaipur, Pangi, Ladakh, Koksar or Spiti etc. 26 May 2022: Tunnel isopen to general traffic movement.

Concept note-4: The Border RoadsOrganisation (BRO) Engineering Marvel Atal Tunnel built in Rohtang ofHimachal Pradesh received Indian Building Congress (IBC) ‘Best InfrastructureProject’ award on April 28, 2022 in New Delhi.

Concept note-5: Sometimeyou will not allowed to reach Atal Tunnel if there is snow on the road androad is slippery. Open time: 24x7. and from 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM in theevening.
