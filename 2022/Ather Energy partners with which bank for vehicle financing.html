Ather Energy partners with which bank for vehicle financing?

(A) PNB

(B) BOB

(C) ** SBI

(D) RBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Ather Energy, a smart electric scooter manufacturer hasannounced its partnership with IDFC Bank to offer a first-in-the-industry financingoption to customers. Consumers can upgrade and own a smarter and faster Ather450X scooter by incurring the same monthly expense as that of a conventional petrolscooter.

Concept note-2: The partnership with SBI, the country’s largest publicsector lender, is aimed at leveraging its reach and penetration in accelerating theadoption of electric vehicles (EVs) in the domestic market, Ather Energysaid.

Concept note-3: Bengaluru-based electric two-wheeler manufacturer AtherEnergy has raised ₹400.6 (around $ 50 million) in a fresh funding round led byexisting investor Caladium Investment and participation from Herald SquareVentures.

Concept note-4: Tata Motors has joined hands with SBI to providefinance solutions to authorised passenger EV dealers.

Concept note-5: HeroMotoCorp, Ather’s biggest investor, unveiled its first electric scooter on October7.
