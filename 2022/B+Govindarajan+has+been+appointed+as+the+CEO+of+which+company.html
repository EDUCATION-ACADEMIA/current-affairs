B Govindarajan has been appointed as the CEO of which company?

(A) Harley-Davidson India

(B) TATA Motors

(C) ** Royal Enfield

(D) KIA India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Royal Enfield has elevated B Govindarajan as the new chief executive officer (CEO). The announcement was made by Eicher Motors , the parent company of Enfield.

Concept note-2: -Eicher Motors Limited Announces Appointment of B Govindarajan as CEO of Royal Enfield. Mr. Govindarajan has spent more than 23 years across Royal Enfield and Eicher Motors. He has been the Executive Director of Royal Enfield since August 2021, and was Chief Operating Officer of the company since 2013.

Concept note-3: -Eicher Motors has appointed B Govindarajan as the Chief Executive Officer of Royal Enfield. He will also serve as the Wholetime Director of the Board of Eicher Motors Limited.

Concept note-4: -Siddhartha Lal is the Managing Director and CEO of Eicher Motors Ltd., the flagship company of Eicher Group in India. Siddhartha has also been the Chief Executive Officer of Royal Enfield and the Chairman of VE Commercial Vehicles (VECV); a joint venture between Volvo group and Eicher Motors Ltd. (EML).

Concept note-5: -B. Govindarajan is the Chief Executive Officer at Royal Enfield. He also serves as Wholetime Director on the Board of Eicher Motors Ltd. Govindarajan has spent over 23 years at Eicher Motors Ltd.
