(A) ** Bangladesh

(B) Nepal

(C) China

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Q. Bandhan Express and Maitree Express, which were seen inthe news, run between India and which country? Notes: Bandhan Express, whichruns between India and Bangladesh, recently resumed service after two years, whichwas earlier stopped due to the Covid pandemic.

Concept note-2: The MaitreeExpress train (Bengali: ), pronounced Moitree Express is the first modern day, fullyair conditioned, international, express train service connecting Dhaka in Bangladeshto Kolkata in the Indian state of West Bengal.

Concept note-3: The BandhanExpress train (Bengali: ) is an international express rail service which runsbetween the Indian city Kolkata and the Bangladeshi city Khulna everyweek.

Concept note-4: The New Jalpaiguri-Dhaka Cantonment “Mitali Express,”which connects India and Bangladesh, was officially inaugurated in June 2022 byUnion Railways Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw and his Bangladeshi oppositeMohammad Nurul Islam Sujon. The third passenger train to run between Bangladeshand India is this one.

Concept note-5: The Bandhan Express between Kolkataand Khulna runs two days a week, Maitree Express connecting Kolkata with Dhakawill have a five-day service.
