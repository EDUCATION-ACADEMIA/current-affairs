Bharti Airtel has acquired how much stake in Navi Mumbai based Cnergee Technologies, under the Airtel Startup Accelerator Program?

(A) 8%

(B) 6%

(C) 9%

(D) ** 7%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The stake purchase in Cnergee will allow Bharti Airtel tosharpen its NaaS proposition for SMeEs looking for cloud-based solutions.Bharti Airtel Ltd. has acquired about 7% stake in Cnergee Technologies, acloud-based networking solutions provider, under the Airtel Startup AcceleratorProgram.

Concept note-2: New Delhi, December 20, 2022: Bharti Airtel(“Airtel”), India’s premier communications solutions provider today announced thatit has acquired a strategic stake in Lemnisk (Immensitas Private Limited) underAirtel’s Start Up Accelerator Program. The deal is subject to all applicable statutoryapprovals.

Concept note-3: After the completion of the stake sale, expected in 90days, BTL will own 38.75% in Airtel, up from the current 35.4% level. The Mittalsand Singtel will continue to own 50.56% and 49.44%, respectively, in BTL, whichwon’t change after the deal.

Concept note-4: The company did not divulge intothe financial details of the deal. Tech start-up Waybeo, focused on deep artificialintelligence-based analytics for cloud telephony, is fifth to join the Airtel StartupAccelerator Program.
