Bhukya and which player has won National U-13 badminton titles?

(A) Lakshay Sharma

(B) Ayush Shetty

(C) Ansh Gupta

(D) ** Patri

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Telangana’s Nishanth Bhukya and Odisha’s Tanvi Patri emerged champions in the boys and girls singles respectively at the 34th Under-13 National Badminton Championships , notching contrasting wins at the UP Badminton Academy here on Wednesday.

Concept note-2: -P.V. Sindhu became the first Indian to win Badminton World Championships gold by defeating Nozomi Okuhara.

Concept note-3: -Men’s singles player Lakshya Sen and the men’s doubles pair of Chirag Shetty and Satwiksairaj Ranklireddy entered the tournament as the reigning champions, having won their respective events in the 2022 edition.

Concept note-4: -Thomas Cup, trophy signifying world supremacy in the sport of badminton. The cup was donated in 1939 by Sir George Thomas for a series of men’s international team competitions to be managed by the International Badminton Federation (IBF), of which Thomas was then president.

Concept note-5: -Men’s Team: Ibrahim Rahimtoola Cup. Women’s Team: Gulab Rai Chadha Cup.
