Bihar Diwas is being celebrated on which date?

(A) March 25

(B) March 24

(C) March 23

(D) ** March 22

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Bihar Diwas is observed annually on the same day withcultural and other activities to mark the occasion when Bihar became a state. Bihardiwas date 2021 is 22nd March.

Concept note-2: The correct answer isTo commemorate Bihar’s separation from the Bengal Presidency in 1912.March 22 marks the formation day of the state of Bihar and is called ‘BiharDiwas’.

Concept note-3: The day the state of Bihar was created by the Bengalpresidency in 1912 is celebrated as Bihar day. It is an annual celebration and a publicholiday in Bihar. Though the History of Bihar Diwas dates back to 1912,the day’s official celebration with numerous events and programs started in2011.

Concept note-4: The event, conceptualised around the theme ‘Jaljeevan Hariyali’, was organised from 22nd to 24th March in Gandhi Maidan,Patna.

Concept note-5: Bihar Diwas 2022 marks the 110th anniversary of thestate’s founding. The annual Bihar Diwas is no longer limited to festivities hosted bythe state government. Bihar Diwas 2022 marks the 110th anniversary of the state’sfounding.
