Bihar’s Mithila Makhana received GI Tag status from the government; how long is the tag valid after being granted?

(A) 5

(B) 7

(C) 3

(D) ** 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This tag is valid for a period of 10 years following which it can be renewed.

Concept note-2: -Bihar’s Mithila Makhana has been awarded the Geographical Indication (GI) tag by the Central government recently. This will help growers get a maximum price and boost the trade of the product.

Concept note-3: -(1) The registration of a geographical indication shall be for a period of ten years, but may be renewed from time to time in accordance with the provisions of this section.

Concept note-4: -It was the first craft item from the state to receive a geographical indication tag in 2005.

Concept note-5: -A registered GI shall be valid for 10 years and can be renewed on payment of renewal fee.
