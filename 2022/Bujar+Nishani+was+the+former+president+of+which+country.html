Bujar Nishani, was the former president of which country?

(A) Montenegro

(B) Kosovo

(C) Macedonia

(D) ** Albania

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bujar Nishani (Albanian pronunciation: [bujaɾ ni’ʃani]; 29 September 1966 – 28 May 2022) was an Albanian politician. He served as President of Albania from 24 July 2012 to 24 July 2017.
