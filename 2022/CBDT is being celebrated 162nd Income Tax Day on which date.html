CBDT is being celebrated 162nd Income Tax Day on which date?

(A) July 25

(B) July 23

(C) July 22

(D) ** July 24

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Income Tax department will be celebrating the 162ndIncome Tax Day or Aaykar Diwas on 24 July 2022. This day has been celebrated inIndia since 2010.

Concept note-2: Q. When is ‘Aaykar Diwas (IncomeTax Day)’ celebrated in India? Notes: Aaykar Diwas or Income Tax Day iscelebrated on July 24 by the Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) and all fieldoffices.

Concept note-3: An e-Calendar, 2022 was launched by Chairman,CBDT on the theme of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav. It gives a glimpse ofactivities organized by the Department as a part of #AzadiKaAmritMahotsavcelebrations.

Concept note-4: (1) This Act may be called the Income-tax Act,1961. (2) It extends to the whole of India. (3) Save as otherwise providedin this Act, it shall come into force on the 1st day of April, 1962. *Dated13-9-1961.

Concept note-5: Income Tax Day 2022: On July 24, 1860, income taxwas introduced in India. The first Income Tax Day was celebrated on July 24,2010.
