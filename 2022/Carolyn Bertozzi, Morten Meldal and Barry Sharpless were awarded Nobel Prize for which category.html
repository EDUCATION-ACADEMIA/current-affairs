Carolyn Bertozzi, Morten Meldal and Barry Sharpless were awarded Nobel Prize for which category?

(A) Literature

(B) ** Chemistry

(C) Mathematics

(D) Physics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Nobel Prize in Chemistry 2022 was awarded to Carolyn R.Bertozzi, Morten Meldal, and K. Barry Sharpless “for the development of clickchemistry and bioorthogonal chemistry,” which involve simple, quick chemicalreactions that can occur within living organisms without disrupting normal biologicalfunctions.

Concept note-2: Since 1901, the Nobel Prize has been awarded in thefields of physics, chemistry, physiology or medicine, literature and peace, while amemorial prize in economic sciences was added in 1968.

Concept note-3: In2002, Morten Meldal and Barry Sharpless, independently of each other,developed an elegant and efficient chemical reaction: the copper catalysedazide-alkyne cycloaddition. This is now in widespread use and is utilised inthe development of pharmaceuticals, for mapping DNA and creating newmaterials.

Concept note-4: As an open lesbian in academia and science, Bertozzihas been a role model for students and colleagues. Bertozzi was awarded the2022 Nobel Prize in Chemistry, jointly with Morten P. Meldal and KarlBarry Sharpless, “for the development of click chemistry and bioorthogonalchemistry".

Concept note-5: In 2022, the Nobel prize for Chemistrywas awarded to three laureates for the development of click chemistry andbioorthogonal chemistry, to Carolyn R. Bertozzi, Morten P. Medal, and K. BarrySharpless.
