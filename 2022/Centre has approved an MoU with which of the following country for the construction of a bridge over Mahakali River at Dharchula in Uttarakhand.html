Centre has approved an MoU with which of the following country for the construction of a bridge over Mahakali River at Dharchula in Uttarakhand?

(A) Sri Lanka

(B) Malaysia

(C) ** Nepal

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India and Nepal on Tuesday signed a Memorandum ofUnderstanding (MoU) for the construction of a motorable bridge over the MahakaliRiver connecting Dharchula in Uttarakhand with Darchula in Nepal with Indiangrant assistance.

Concept note-2: Kathmandu: India and Nepal on Tuesdaysigned a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for construction of a motorablebridge with the Indian grant assistance over the Mahakali River connectingDharchula in Uttarakhand with Darchula in Nepal.

Concept note-3: IndianAmbassador to Nepal Vinay Mohan Kwatra and Nepal’s Secretary, Ministry ofPhysical Infrastructure and Transport Rabindra Nath Shrestha signed the MoU inpresence of Transport Minister Renu Kumari Yadav, according to a statement issuedby the Indian Embassy here.

Concept note-4: India and Nepal signed anMemorandum of Understanding (MoU) for the construction of a motorableBridge over the Mahakali River connecting Dharchula (India) with Darchula(Nepal), under Indian grant assistance.

Concept note-5: The MoU wouldhelp in promoting cooperation between the Parties in the field of forests,wildlife, environment, biodiversity conservation and climate change, includingrestoration of corridors and interlinking areas and sharing knowledge and bestpractices.
