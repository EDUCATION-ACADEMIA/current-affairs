China claimed a domestic record for launching 22 satellites into space by using which rocket?

(A) Long March 3C rocket

(B) ** Long March 8 rocket

(C) Long March 2E rocket

(D) Long March 4A rocket

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -China’s new Long March-8 rocket placed 22 satellites in space on Sunday, setting a domestic record for the most spacecraft launched by a single rocket.

Concept note-2: -The Long March-8 rocket carrying 22 satellites blasts off from the Wenchang Spacecraft Launch Site in south China’s Hainan Province, February 27, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The Long March 5B rocket has so far been used to launch modules for China’s now-completed Tiangong space station, as well as a test of a new generation crew spacecraft.

Concept note-4: -China’s commercial spaceflight has also seen a number of strong developments in the past year. In February, the Long March-8 Y2 carrier rocket sent 22 satellites into their preset orbits.

Concept note-5: -The mission marks the fifth launch of the Ceres-1 rocket - a small solid fuel orbital rocket designed by the company, Galactic Energy said. So far, it has successfully put 19 commercial satellites into space, setting a record for a private Chinese firm.
