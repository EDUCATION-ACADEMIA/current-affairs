China claimed a domestic record for launching 22 satellites into space by using which rocket?

(A) Long March 3C rocket

(B) ** Long March 8 rocket

(C) Long March 2E rocket

(D) Long March 4A rocket

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: A total of 22 satellites were blasted off into spaceusing a modified version of the Long March-8 rocket that took off from theWenchang Spacecraft Launch site in China’s southern Hainan province, state-runXinhua News Agency reported.

Concept note-2: The Long March-8 rocketcarrying 22 satellites blasts off from the Wenchang Spacecraft Launch Site insouth China’s Hainan Province, February 27, 2022.

Concept note-3: TheLong March 5B rocket has so far been used to launch modules for China’snow-completed Tiangong space station, as well as a test of a new generation crewspacecraft.

Concept note-4: China’s commercial spaceflight has also seen anumber of strong developments in the past year. In February, the Long March-8 Y2carrier rocket sent 22 satellites into their preset orbits.

Concept note-5: Themission marks the fifth launch of the Ceres-1 rocket - a small solid fuel orbital rocketdesigned by the company, Galactic Energy said. So far, it has successfully put19 commercial satellites into space, setting a record for a private Chinesefirm.
