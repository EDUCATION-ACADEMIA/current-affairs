Chinese Language Day is observed on which date?

(A) 15 April

(B) 18 April

(C) ** 20 April

(D) 25 April

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Why April 20? The date for the Chinese day was selected fromGuyu ("Rain of Millet"), which is the 6th of 24 solar terms in the traditional EastAsian calendars, to pay tribute to Cangjie.

Concept note-2: Q. Whatis the theme of the ‘2022 United Nations Chinese Language Day’? Notes:The 2022 United Nations Chinese Language Day and the second OverseasChinese Language Video Festival has been held successfully. “China Chic”was chosen as the theme of UN Chinese Language Day celebrations thisyear.

Concept note-3: UN Chinese Language Day is observed annually on April20.

Concept note-4: Public Life. English Language Day is a global observanceand not a public holiday.
