Climber Elnaz Rekabi, who was reported missing, belongs to which country?

(A) Qatar

(B) UAE

(C) Afghanistan

(D) ** Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Elnaz Rekabi is a 33-year-old Iranian woman who was awardedthe bronze medal in the women’s combined event at the IFSC Climbing WorldChampionships in 2021.

Concept note-2: Rekabi was reported missing followingthe final day of the competition in South Korea on October 16. Concerns for hersafety mounted when, three days later, she returned to Tehran’s Imam Khomeiniairport apologizing to state reporters. Reports emerged that she was then placedunder house arrest.

Concept note-3: Iranian climber Elnaz Rekabi competedwithout a headscarf at a contest in South Korea in Oct. She was forcedto apologise.

Concept note-4: Elnaz Rekabi, the Iranian climber whodisappeared after competing without a hijab, resurfaces in Tehran. There hadbeen no news of the champion climber since Monday after she appearedwithout a hijab at the final of the Asian climbing championships in Seoul onSunday.

Concept note-5: DUBAI, United Arab Emirates - Iranian climber ElnazRekabi returned to Tehran early Wednesday after competing in South Koreawithout wearing a headscarf, an act widely seen as support for anti-governmentdemonstrators amid weeks of protests over the Islamic Republic’s mandatoryhijab.
