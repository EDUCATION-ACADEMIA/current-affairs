Coal Miners Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** May 04

(B) May 01

(C) May 02

(D) May 03

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Coal Miners Day is observed on 4th May every year to honour the coal miners. The day is celebrated to highlight the contributions of coal miners in meeting our energy demands. Below, we share a bit of the history of coal mining, the current energy scenario, and the role of coal miners in India.

Concept note-2: -Coal Miners’ Day 2022: Themes The main theme of this day is to raise awareness about the importance of coal mining.

Concept note-3: -Miners’ Day was established to celebrate and recognize those who take part in this dangerous profession, to help our lives be comfortable and full of conveniences.

Concept note-4: -COAL MINING IN INDIA: THE PAST India has a long history of commercial coal mining covering nearly 220 years starting from 1774 by M/s Sumner and Heatly of East India Company in the Raniganj Coalfield along the Western bank of river Damodar.
