Commerce Minister Shri Piyush Goyal, India has created history by exporting goods worth how many billion dollars in 1 year?

(A) 300 billion dollars

(B) 100 billion dollars

(C) 200 billion dollars

(D) ** 400 billion dollars

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: piyush goyal: India’s merchandise export set to cross $400 billionin current fiscal: Piyush Goyal - The Economic Times.

Concept note-2: India’soverall export (Merchandise and Services combined) of USD 61.82 Billion inDecember 2022. The exports exhibited a negative growth of (-) 5.26 per cent over thesame period last year.
