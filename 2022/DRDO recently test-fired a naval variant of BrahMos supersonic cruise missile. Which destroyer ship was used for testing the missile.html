DRDO recently test-fired a naval variant of BrahMos supersonic cruise missile. Which destroyer ship was used for testing the missile?

(A) INS Rajput

(B) INS Kolkata

(C) INS Chennai

(D) ** INS Visakhapatnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Indian Navy’s warship INS Visakhapatnam carriedout a test firing of BrahMos supersonic cruise missile off the westernseaboard.

Concept note-2: On March 5, the Indian Navy successfullytested an advanced version of the Brahmos supersonic cruise missile from astealth destroyer in the Indian Ocean. The missile was fired from the stealthdestroyer INS Chennai.

Concept note-3: On April 19, the Indian Air Force(IAF) successfully test-fired the BrahMos missile from a Sukhoi fighter jeton the Eastern seaboard.

Concept note-4: Indian Navy’s warship INSChennai carried out the test-firing of BrahMos supersonic cruise missile onSaturday.

Concept note-5: India’s Defence Research and DevelopmentOrganisation (DRDO) successfully test-fired the naval variant of the supersonicBrahMos cruise missile.
