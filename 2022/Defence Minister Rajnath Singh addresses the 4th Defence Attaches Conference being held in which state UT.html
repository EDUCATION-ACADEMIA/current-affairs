Defence Minister Rajnath Singh addresses the 4th Defence Attaches Conference being held in which state/ UT?

(A) Haryana

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Rajasthan

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Singh became the Defence minister of India on 31 May 2019. Singh was given Ministry of Defence (India) after Amit Shah was given the Ministry of Home Affairs (India).

Concept note-2: -Glimpses of Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh addressing the convocation ceremony of Manipal Academy of Higher Education in Udupi, Karnataka on 18 November 2022.

Concept note-3: -Defence Minister and senior BJP leader Rajnath Singh interacted with the teachers of Bihar via video conferencing from New Delhi. During the interaction Rajnath Singh said that the role of teachers in society is like that of an artist, adding no one can be a better architect of society than a teacher.
