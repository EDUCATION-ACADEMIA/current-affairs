Defence Ministry signed how many crore deal for indigenous Astra Beyond Visual Range missiles?

(A) Rs 3, 971 crore

(B) Rs 5, 971 crore

(C) Rs 4, 971 crore

(D) ** Rs 2, 971 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Defence Ministry signs ₹2,971-crore deal for indigenous AstraBeyond Visual Range missiles.

Concept note-2: Astra (Sanskrit: “weapon") is anIndian family of all weather beyond-visual-range air-to-air missile, developed by theDefence Research and Development Organisation. The missile is capable of engagingtargets at varying ranges from a distance of 500 m (0.31 mi) up to 110 km (68mi).

Concept note-3: Q. The Ministry of Defence signed a contract with whichcompany for supply of ASTRA MK-I Air to Air Missile (AAM)? Notes:The Ministry of Defence signed a contract with Bharat Dynamics Limited(BDL) for the supply of ASTRA MK-I Beyond Visual Range (BVR) Air toAir Missile (AAM).

Concept note-4: Astra (Sanskrit: “weapon") is anall-weather beyond-visual-range air-to-air missile developed by the DefenceResearch and Development Organisation. It is the first air-to-air missiledeveloped by India. It features mid-course inertial guidance with terminal activeradar homing.

Concept note-5: The Defense Research and DevelopmentOrganisation (DRDO) has designed and built the ASTRA MK-I BVR AAMindigenously.
