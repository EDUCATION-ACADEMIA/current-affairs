Defense Ministry approves establishment of ‘BRO Cafes’ at how many locations?

(A) ** 75

(B) 100

(C) 50

(D) 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Border Roads Organisation Cafes: Defence Ministry approvesestablishment of ‘BRO Cafes’ on 75 border roads in 12 States/UTs - The EconomicTimes.

Concept note-2: NEW DELHI: Ministry of Defence has approved settingup of wayside amenities under the brand of ‘BRO Cafes’ at 75 locations on differentsections of roads with Border Roads Organisation across 12 states and Unionterritories.

Concept note-3: Since the inaccessibility and remoteness of theseroads preclude adequate commercial infrastructures, BRO, being co existing, isassisting in opening of Cafe at remote tourist places in order to boost tourism,adequate economic activity and generate revenue. The 75 BRO Cafés will beestablished.

Concept note-4: Initially, BRO was functional under the Ministry ofRoad Transport and Highways. But since 2015, it is being managed and is functionalunder the Ministry of Defence. BRO is operational in 21 Indian states and 1 unionterritory.
