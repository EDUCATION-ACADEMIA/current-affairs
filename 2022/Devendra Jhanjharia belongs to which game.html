Devendra Jhanjharia belongs to which game?

(A) Athlete

(B) Shooting

(C) Boxing

(D) ** Javelin Throw

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Paralympic gold medalist Devendra Jhajharia belongs to thesports javelin throw. He is an Indian Paralympic javelin thrower. He is the first IndianParalympic player to win two gold medals in Paralympics.

Concept note-2: SumitAntil made his Paralympic debut at Tokyo 2020 and came away with a gold medal.Sumit Antil is a rising star in javelin throwing. At the Tokyo 2020 ParalympicGames, he extended the world record three times in the final of the men’s F64 eventto win the gold medal in his Games debut.

Concept note-3: Jhajharia isa multiple-time Paralympics medal winner, having won his maiden goldduring the 2004 Paralympics in Athens, his second gold medal during the RioGames in 2016, and a silver medal during the 2020 Tokyo edition last year.President Kovind presents Padma Bhushan to Shri Devendra Jhajharia forSports.

Concept note-4: Devendra Jhajharia is the first Indian to win twoParalympic Gold Medals.

Concept note-5: For 36 years since Team India’stricolor has been in participation at every edition. India’s first-ever medal atthe Paralympics came in Heidelberg when Murlikant Petkar won Gold byswimming the 50-meter Freestyle in a then World Record time of 37.331seconds.
