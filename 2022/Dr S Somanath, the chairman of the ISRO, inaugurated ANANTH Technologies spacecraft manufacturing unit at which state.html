Dr S Somanath, the chairman of the ISRO, inaugurated ANANTH Technologies’ spacecraft manufacturing unit at which state?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Kerala

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) ** Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Dr S Somanath, the chairman of Indian Space ResearchOrganisation (ISRO), Wednesday inaugurated ANANTH Technologies’spacecraft manufacturing unit at Karnataka Industrial Area DevelopmentBoard (KIADB) Aerospace Park.

Concept note-2: On November 18,Hyderabad-based Skyroot Aerospace became India’s first private firm tolaunch its own rocket.

Concept note-3: Shri S. Somanath assumed thecharge of Secretary, Department of Space and Chairman, Space Commissionon 14 January 2022. In his previous assignment he was Director, VikramSarabhai Space Centre (VSSC), the lead Centre responsible for Launch vehicletechnology development.

Concept note-4: Founded in 2018 by formerISRO scientists Pawan Kumar Chandana and Naga Bharath Daka, Skyrootsuccessfully test-fired India’s privately made solid-rocket stage in December 2020.It was also the country’s first startup in 2021 to sign a memorandum ofunderstanding with the ISRO to launch its rockets.

Concept note-5: Cities.ISRO Chairman S Somanath hopes Gaganyaan will be successful by 2023end.
