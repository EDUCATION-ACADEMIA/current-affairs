Eastern Economic Form is being hosted by which country every year?

(A) ** Russia

(B) Sweden

(C) Germany

(D) Poland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Detailed Solution: Eastern Economic Forum is an internationalforum that is held every year in Vladivostok, Russia, for the purpose of encouragingforeign investment in the Russian far east. The forum is held each year since 2015 inSeptember.

Concept note-2: Eastern Economic Forum (UPSC Notes) TheEastern Economic Forum (EEF) is an annual international forum that takesplace in Vladivostok, Russia. It was established in 2015 for the purpose ofencouraging investment in the Russian Far East.

Concept note-3: EasternEconomic Forum is the forum where all economic, political as well as culturalrelations between Asia Pacific and Russia have been discussed. The EasternEconomic Forum members are regions of Asia Pacific as well as ASEAN andthe Eastern Economic Forum members are; Afghanistan, India, Russia andIran.

Concept note-4: India’s Envoy To Lead Eastern Economic Forum InRussia’s Vladivostok. PM Narendra Modi delivered a video address during theplenary session of the 6th EEF held on September 3, 2021 in Vladivostok,where he was the chief guest for the 5th EEF in 2019-the first by an IndianPM.
