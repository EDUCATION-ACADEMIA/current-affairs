Electoral bonds of how much worth has been sold during the 23rd phase in November?

(A) Rs 500 crore

(B) Rs 1000 crore

(C) Rs 106 crore

(D) ** Rs 676 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Electoral bonds worth Rs. 676 crore were sold during the 23rdphase in November.

Concept note-2: Electoral bonds are instruments/securitiesthat are used to donate funds to political parties. Electoral Bonds may be purchasedby a person who is a citizen of India or incorporated or established in India. Aperson being an individual can buy Electoral Bonds, either singly or jointlywith other individuals.

Concept note-3: For instance, the 22nd trancheof bond sales took place from October 1-10, 2022 while 21st tranche wasfrom July 1-10, 2022. Sale of the first batch of electoral bonds happenedfrom March 1-10, 2018. SBI is the only authorised bank to issue electoralbonds.
