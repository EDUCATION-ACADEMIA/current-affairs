Elisabeth Borne has (May 2022) appointed as the new prime minister of which country?

(A) Switzerland

(B) Netherlands

(C) Canada

(D) ** France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Élisabeth Borne (French pronunciation: [elizabɛt bɔʁn]; born18 April 1961) is a French politician who has served as Prime Minister of Francesince May 2022. She is a member of President Emmanuel Macron’s partyRenaissance.

Concept note-2: Present. Élisabeth Borne has served as PrimeMinister since 16 May 2022.

Concept note-3: Executive power is exercised by thePresident of the Republic and the Government. The Government consists of thePrime Minister and ministers. The Prime Minister is appointed by the President, andis responsible to Parliament.

Concept note-4: Emmanuel Macron (French:[emanɥɛl makʁɔ̃]; born 21 December 1977) is a French politician who has served asPresident of France since 2017.
