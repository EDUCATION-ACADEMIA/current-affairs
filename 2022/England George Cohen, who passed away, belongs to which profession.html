England George Cohen, who passed away, belongs to which profession?

(A) ** Player

(B) Singer

(C) Doctor

(D) Teachers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: George Reginald Cohen MBE (22 October 1939 –23 December 2022) was an English professional footballer who played as aright-back.

Concept note-2: One of Hammersmith & Fulham’s honorary freemen,the World Cup winner and Fulham footballing legend George Cohen, has died at theage of 83.

Concept note-3: Two members of England’s World Cup winningteam from 1966 are still alive. They are Sir Bobby Charlton and Sir GeoffHurst.

Concept note-4: The England team became known as the “winglesswonders", on account of their then-unconventional narrow attacking formation,described at the time as a 4–4–2.

Concept note-5: George Cohen is Ben Cohen’suncle.
