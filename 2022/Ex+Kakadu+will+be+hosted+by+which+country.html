Ex Kakadu will be hosted by which country?

(A) China

(B) ** Australia

(C) NATO

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Australia’s naval exercise Kakadu 2022: India INS Satpura showcases its strength. Indigenously designed INS Satpura showcased its prowess during the ongoing multinational Exercise Kakadu-2022 in Darwin, Australia.

Concept note-2: -A P8I Long Range Maritime Patrol Aircraft of the Indian Navy participated in the Multinational Maritime Exercise Kakadu 2022 hosted by Royal Australian Navy in Darwin from 12 to 25 Sep 22, along with INS Satpura. The exercise had the participation of 34 aircraft from over 20 nations.

Concept note-3: -“Exercise Kakadu is the Royal Australian Navy’s most significant international engagement activity and is vital for building relationships between participating countries.

Concept note-4: -This was part of Australia’s Indo-Pacific Endeavour 2022 (IPE 22).

Concept note-5: -New Delhi: INS Satpura and a maritime patrol aircraft of the Indian Navy have reached Darwin in Australia to take part in multinational Exercise Kakadu-2022, hosted by the Royal Australian Navy, the defence ministry said on Tuesday.
