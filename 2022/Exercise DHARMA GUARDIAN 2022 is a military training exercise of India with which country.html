Exercise DHARMA GUARDIAN 2022 is a military training exercise of India with which country?

(A) USA

(B) ** Japan

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) Nepal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is Japan. A joint military exercise, EXDHARMA GUARDIAN-2022, between India and Japan was conducted at ForeignTraining Node, Karnataka from 27 Feb-10 March 2022.

Concept note-2: UPSCMainsQ. ‘Dharma Guardian’ is a Defence Exercise conducted between the ArmedForces of which countries? Notes: ‘Dharma Guardian 2022’ is an annual militarytraining exercise being conducted in India since 2018 between Indian and JapaneseArmy.

Concept note-3: Recently, a Joint Military Exercise, “Exercise DharmaGuardian-2022”, between India and Japan will be conducted at foreign trainingnode, Belagavi (Belgaum, Karnataka) from 27th February to 10th March2022.
