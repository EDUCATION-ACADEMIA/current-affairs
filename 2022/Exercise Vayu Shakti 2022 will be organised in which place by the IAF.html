Exercise Vayu Shakti 2022 will be organised in which place by the IAF?

(A) Nainital

(B) ** Jaisalmer

(C) Agra

(D) Pithoragarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: Indian Air Force (IAF) will conduct the Exercise Vayu Shakti that will take place at the Pokharan range in Jaisalmer.

Concept note-2: -The exercise was scheduled in the Pokhran ranges in Rajasthan.

Concept note-3: -Indian Air force (IAF) is set to conduct the Vayushakti-2022 military drill at Pokhran field firing range in Rajasthan next month.

Concept note-4: -’Vayu Shakti’ is IAF’s biggest exercise and is held every three years to showcase India’s air power. As many as 148 aircraft of the Indian Air Force will take part in the 2022 edition of ‘Vayu Shakti’ that will held at Rajasthan’s Pokhran test facility on March 7, senior officials of the force said on Wednesday.

Concept note-5: -Over 140 aircraft of the Indian Air Force (IAF) will take part in the 2022 edition of Vayu Shakti that will be held at Rajasthan’s Pokhran test facility on March 7.
