FAO and World Food Program warned about acute food insecurity in which neighbouring country of India?

(A) Myanmar

(B) Afghanistan

(C) ** Sri Lanka

(D) Nepal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: FAO and World Food Program warned about acute foodinsecurity in which neighbouring country of India? Explanation: The Food andAgriculture Organization (FAO) and the World Food Programme (WFP) warned ina new report that around 6.3 million people in Sri Lanka are facing moderate tosevere acute food insecurity.

Concept note-2: Attachments. The Food andAgriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO) and the World FoodProgramme (WFP) warn that acute food insecurity is likely to deteriorate further in19 countries or situations – called hunger hotspots – during the outlook period fromOctober 2022 to January 2023.

Concept note-3: Sub-Saharan Africa has thehighest share of people who are food insecure at 51 percent, reflecting the effects offood price inflation for low-income populations across the region. Food insecurity inthe Latin America and the Caribbean region is estimated at 29 percent and in Asiaat 27 percent.

Concept note-4: The correct answer is Odisha. The UnitedNations World Food Programme (WFP) and the Odisha government have joinedhands. They will improve the food security of small and marginal farmers bystrengthening their resilience to climate change.

Concept note-5: Largest numberof food insecure people are in the states of West Bengal, Jharkhand, parts ofMaharashtra and Madhya Pradesh, Bihar, South eastern and eastern parts of UttarPradesh.
