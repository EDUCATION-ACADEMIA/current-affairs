[SOURCES]

FSIB has recommended whom for the post of NABARD Chairman in August 2022?

(A) Amarjeet Yadav

(B) ** Mohammad Mustafa

(C) Manish Sharma

(D) Tribhuwan Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“After interfacing with 15 candidates on August 16-17, 2022, the bureau recommends Mohammad Mustafa for the position of Chairman in National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD),” FSIB said in a statement.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The Financial Services Institutions Bureau (FSIB) recommended Mohammad Mustafa as the Chairman of National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD).

Concept note-3: -FSIB is headed by former secretary, Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT), Bhanu Pratap Sharma.

Concept note-4: -Shri Shaji K V assumed charge as Chairman of National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) on 7th December 2022.

Concept note-5: -Shaji K V has become the Chairman of National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (Nabard) w.e.f 07 December 2022. He was earlier Deputy Managing Director (DMD) of NABARD from 21 May 2020.
