Fifth Khelo India Youth Games-2022 will be held in which state?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) Gujarat

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) ** Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Madhya Pradesh is hosting the fifth edition of Khelo IndiaYouth Games-2022. In this grand sports event to be held from January 30 toFebruary 11, 6 thousand players will excel in 23 game venues of 8 different cities for13 days.

Concept note-2: As we have told you in this article that the 5th KheloIndia Games 2023 is going to start from 30 January 2023 to 11 February2023 by Madhya Pradesh CM Shivraj Singh Chauhan, which is going to behosted by Madhya Pradesh for these 13 days.

Concept note-3: Over 5,000athletes will compete in 27 sports for 1,936 medals. Watch live. The KheloIndia Youth Games 2023 will be held in Madhya Pradesh from January 30to February 11, 2023.

Concept note-4: The Government of India hasgiven in-principle consent to host the Khelo India Youth Games 2022-23 inMadhya Pradesh.

Concept note-5: Athletics, wrestling, boxing, shooting,kayaking-canoeing, rowing, volleyball, judo and swimming are to be held in Bhopal inthe Khelo India Youth Games.
