First ASEAN-India Start-Up Festival has been inaugurated in which country to mark the 30th anniversary of ASEAN and India’s relationship?

(A) ** Indonesia

(B) Cambodia

(C) Brunei

(D) Laos

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Dr. Srivari Chandrasekhar, Secretary, Department of Scienceand Technology, inaugurated the 1st ASEAN-India Start-up Festival (AISF) on 27thOctober 2022 in Bogor, Indonesia, in the presence of Mr. Satvinder Singh,Deputy Secretary-General for ASEAN Economic Community and AmbassadorMr.

Concept note-2: JAKARTA, 27 October 2022 – ASEAN and Indiacelebrated 30 years of relation with the launch of the 1st ASEAN-India Start-UpFestival today.

Concept note-3: India’s relationship with ASEAN has emerged asa key cornerstone of our foreign policy. The relationship has evolved from the ‘LookEast Policy’ enunciated in early 1990s which led India to become a Sectoral Partnerof ASEAN in 1992, a Dialogue Partner in 1996 and a Summit-level Partner in2002.

Concept note-4: SIIC, IIT Kanpur to be lead coordinator from India atthe ASEAN – India Startup Festival to be held from 27-30 Oct, 2022 in Indonesia.The ASEAN-India Startup Festival is being organized in conjunction withthe Indonesia Research and Innovation Expo (INA-RIE) by the NationalResearch and Innovation Agency of Indonesia.

Concept note-5: Theup-gradation of the relationship into a Strategic Partnership in 2012 was a naturalprogression to the ground covered since India became a Sectoral Partner of theASEAN in 1992, Dialogue Partner in 1996 and Summit Level Partner in2002.
