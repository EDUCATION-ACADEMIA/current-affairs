‘Fit India Cyclothon’ was recently organized in which city?

(A) ** Panaji

(B) Pune

(C) Hyderabad

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This event is organized with the aim to get the people involved in outdoor activities and starting a cycling culture in the state. The Cyclothon event witnessed participation by over 500 cycling enthusiasts who came together to cycle through a five-kilometre stretch within the Panaji city.

Concept note-2: -FIT INDIA Movement was launched on 29th August, 2019 by Hon’ble Prime Minister with a view to make fitness an integral part of our daily lives. The mission of the Movement is to bring about behavioural changes and move towards a more physically active lifestyle.

Concept note-3: -It was launched at Indira Gandhi International Stadium in New Delhi. It is a nationwide movement to motivate Indians to incorporate simple and easy ways to staying fit in their lives to lead the country towards a healthy future.

Concept note-4: -The Fit India Movement is a campaign launched by Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi to encourage Indians to become more active and healthy. The campaign focuses on promoting fitness and healthy living habits and encourages people to get involved.

Concept note-5: -’Cyclothon,’ a 24-km cycling marathon, will be held for the cause of preserving the environment.
