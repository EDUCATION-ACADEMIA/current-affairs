For which language has ICCR signed an MoU with Google for easy search?

(A) ** Sanskrit

(B) Hindi

(C) English

(D) Assamese

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: ICCR- Google event at ICCR headquarters where the MOU tojointly promote Sanskrit globally was exchanged in the gracious presence ofPresident, ICCR, Dr.

Concept note-2: ICCR and Google are collaborating toimprove the Sanskrit translation experience. This will help Google improve artificialintelligence or machine learning technology for translating Sanskrit text to variousother languages and vice versa, Sahasrabuddhe said.

Concept note-3: For thesame, Google has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with theGovernment of Karnataka to support their vision to promote innovation and spur thegrowth of start-ups across the state. The Indian start-up ecosystem is the world’sthird largest such ecosystem, with Karnataka being the start-up hub ofIndia.

Concept note-4: 24 new languages have been added to Google Translate,including Assamese, Bhojpuri, Dogri, Konkani, Maithili, Meiteilon (Manipuri), Mizoand Sanskrit.

Concept note-5: At Google I/O 2022, the company announcedthat Google Translate now supports 24 more languages including Indian languagessuch as Sanskrit, Bhojpuri, Dogri and others. The translation tool fromtech giant now supports a total of 133 languages that are used around theglobe.
