For How many days Yoga Mahotsav 2022 campaign has been started?

(A) 150 days

(B) 200 days

(C) ** 100 days

(D) 50 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Also, to widen the horizons the Ministry of Ayush under the aegis of Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga is also starting a campaign: 100 days, 100 cities and 100 organisations between 13th March, 2022 to 21st June, 2022 across the globe.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Saudi Arabia. Saudi Arabia’s first yoga festival took place on 29 January 2022 in the commercial center of Jeddah.

Concept note-3: -The theme of this year of IDY was “Yoga for Humanity”. The main event of the IDY- 2022 demonstration was held at Mysuru, Karnataka led by the Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi Ji. Get More !

Concept note-4: -IDY- 2015. Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated the first International Day of Yoga on June 21st, 2015, on the theme ‘Yoga for Harmony and Peace.

Concept note-5: -The International Day of Yoga 2022 campaign will focus on the theme 100 days, 100 cities and 100 organisations till June 21 across the globe.
