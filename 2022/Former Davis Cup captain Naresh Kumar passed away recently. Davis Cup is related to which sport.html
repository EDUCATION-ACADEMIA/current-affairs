(A) Badminton

(B) Boxing

(C) Football

(D) ** Tennis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Naresh Kumar, the former tennis player who had reached thefourth round of Wimbledon in 1955, has died at the age of 93. Two years ago, he hadbeen conferred the Dronacharya award.

First played in 1900, it was originallycalled the International Lawn Tennis Trophy.
