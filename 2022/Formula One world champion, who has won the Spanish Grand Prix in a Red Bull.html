Formula One world champion, who has won the Spanish Grand Prix in a Red Bull?

(A) Charles Leclerc

(B) Sergio Perez

(C) George Russell

(D) ** Max Verstappen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Formula One world champion Max Verstappen won theSpanish Grand Prix in a Red Bull one-two on Sunday to take over at the top fromFerrari’s luckless Charles Leclerc, who retired with an engine failure whileleading.

Concept note-2: It was the 62nd edition of the Spanish Grand Prix andthe sixth round of the 2022 Formula One World Championship. Max Verstappen wonthe race ahead of teammate Sergio Pérez, while George Russell completed thepodium. Charles Leclerc, the pole-sitter and championship leader, was forced to retiredue to engine issues.

Concept note-4: Max celebrated his first win on his first start for RedBull Racing. It happened on May 15, 2016 at the Spanish Grand Prix inBarcelona, and MV#33 was 18 years and 228 days young. His success has alsoearned him the titles of Youngest Rider on the Podium and Youngest GPLeader.
