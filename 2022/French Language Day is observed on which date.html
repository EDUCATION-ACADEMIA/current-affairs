French Language Day is observed on which date?

(A) ** March 20

(B) March 21

(C) March 22

(D) March 19

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UN French Language Day is observed annually on20 March. The event was established by UN’s Department of PublicInformation in 2010 “to celebrate multilingualism and cultural diversityas well as to promote equal use of all six official languages throughout theOrganization".

Concept note-2: International Francophonie Day (French:Journée internationale de la Francophonie) is observed within the InternationalOrganization of La Francophonie’s 77 member states every March 20 to celebrate theFrench language and Francophone culture. There are over 369 million Frenchspeakers on Earth.

Concept note-3: French Language Day is on March 20 andwe’re more than excited to celebrate with some French culture, language, and historyas we ask… parlez vous Francais?

Concept note-4: French Language Day isobserved every year on March 20 to celebrate multilingualism and cultural diversity.French Language Day was established by the United Nations (UN) as an opportunityto make the international community aware of the history, culture and useof the French language.

Concept note-5: The theme of InternationalFrancophonie Day this year will be “The French language, connecting foraction.”
