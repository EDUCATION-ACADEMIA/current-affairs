G7 leaders unveiled a USD how many billion plan to rival China’s Belt and Road Initiative in developing nations?

(A) 800

(B) 400

(C) 200

(D) ** 600

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Through the partnership, the G7 nations of Canada,France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the United Kingdom and the United Statesaim to mobilize $600 billion in public and private funding for infrastructureby 2027.

Concept note-2: We are concerned by recent and announcedthreatening actions by the People’s Republic of China (PRC), particularly live-fireexercises and economic coercion, which risk unnecessary escalation. There is nojustification to use a visit as pretext for aggressive military activity in theTaiwan Strait.

Concept note-3: The G7 is an informal grouping of sevenof the world’s advanced economies, including Canada, France, Germany,Italy, Japan, the United Kingdom, and the United States, as well as theEuropean Union.

Concept note-4: As the G7 Presidency in 2023, Japan hasdecided to host the G7 Trade Ministers’ Meeting in Osaka-Sakai, on 28 and 29October 2023 after hosting the first online meeting on 4 April 2023. In October,the meeting and related events will be held in the host cities of Osaka andSakai.
