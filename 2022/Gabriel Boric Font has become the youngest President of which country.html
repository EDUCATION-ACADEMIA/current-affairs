Gabriel Boric Font has become the youngest President of which country?

(A) Syria

(B) ** Chile

(C) Netherlands

(D) Norway

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Gabriel Boric Font (Spanish pronunciation: [ɡaˈɾjel ˈoɾitʃˈfont]; born 11 February 1986) is a Chilean politician who is the current president ofChile, serving since 11 March 2022.

Concept note-2: IncumbentChile’s firstfemale president, Michelle Bachelet, was in office from 2006 to 2010 and againfrom 2014 to 2018. Since 2022, Gabriel Boric is the current president. Hewon the 2021 Chilean general election and assumed the office on 11 March2022.

Concept note-3: Gabriel Borić Font was born in Punta Arenas in 1986. Hehas two brothers, Simón and Tomás. He moved to Santiago to study at University ofChile’s law school in 2004. He failed his law exams and never graduated with a lawdegree.
