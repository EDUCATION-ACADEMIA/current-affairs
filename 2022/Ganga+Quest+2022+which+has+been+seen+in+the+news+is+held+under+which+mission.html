‘Ganga Quest 2022’, which has been seen in the news, is held under which mission?

(A) National Mission for Sustaining the Himalayan Ecosystem

(B) ** National Mission for Clean Ganga

(C) Swachh Bharat Mission

(D) Green India Mission

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Guided by Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi’s vision of transforming Namami Gange Programme into a mass movement, with a special focus on students, National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG) along with Tree Craze Foundation, began Ganga Quest, an online quiz in 2019.

Concept note-2: -Ganga Quest is a bilingual international quiz competition designed with an aim to sensitize people about the national River Ganga, its significance, its cultural and historical values, its diversity and its ecosystem.

Concept note-3: -Ganga Quest is a competitive knowledge-building platform, an awareness initiative and scientific baseline determination exercise to assess the knowledge gaps.

Concept note-4: -The fourth edition of the Ganga Quest for the age group above 10 years is scheduled to commence from 07.04. 2022 (World Health Day) and conclude on 22.05. 2022 (International Day for Biological Diversity).

Concept note-5: -Contestants can participate in Ganga Quest and must register on the website along with an Aadhar Card as well as a valid ID card. If participants do not have an Aadhar Card, they can participate with the help of valid ID cards such as passport, birth certificate and school ID.
