Global escrow banking solution provider Castler has partnered with which bank to offer digital escrow services for the bank’s customers?

(A) HDFC Bank

(B) State Bank of India

(C) ** Yes Bank

(D) Axis Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Global escrow banking solution provider Castler has partneredYes Bank to offer digital escrow services for the bank’s customers. The company saidthe tie-up will further strengthen its digital leadership position and is part of aneffort to make banking more inclusive and accessible to its growing consumerbase.

Concept note-2: Escrow is a legal concept describing a financial agreementwhereby an asset or money is held by a third party on behalf of two other partiesthat are in the process of completing a transaction. Escrow accounts are managed bythe escrow agent.

Concept note-3: Electrum Fintech Solutions provides anintegrated Payments and Spend Management platform- “ZRO” in associationwith Yes Bank. This is an innovative solution for issuance and managementof cards along with a Web Portal for corporates and mobile app for theusers.
