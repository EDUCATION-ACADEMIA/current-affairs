Good Governance Day is being celebrated on the birth anniversary of which former Prime Minister?

(A) Manmohan Singh

(B) Indira Gandhi

(C) Narendra Modi

(D) ** Atal Bihari Vajpayee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Good Governance Day is being celebrated on the birthanniversary of former Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayeetoday. 25th December iscelebrated as Good Governance Day every year to create awareness of accountabilityin government among the people.

Concept note-2: Good Governance Day(Hindi: , IAST: Suśāsan Divas) is observed in India annually on the twenty-fifth dayof December, the birth anniversary of former-Prime Minister Atal BihariVajpayee.

Concept note-3: The World Bank introduced the concept in its 1992report entitled “Governance and Development". According to the document, goodgovernance is an essential complement to sound economic policies and is central tocreating and sustaining an environment which fosters strong and equitabledevelopment.

Concept note-4: The purpose of Good Governance Day is toincrease public access to various government programmes and services via goodgovernance. It was established with the slogan “Good Governance throughe-Governance."
