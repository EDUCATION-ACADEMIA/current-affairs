Government of India has launched MBBS Course books in which language for the very first time?

(A) Bhojpuri

(B) ** Hindi

(C) Punjabi

(D) Tamil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Home Minister Amit Shah launches first Hindi version of MBBS course books in Bhopal. On 16th October, Sunday , Union Home and Cooperation Minister Amit Shah launched an MBBS course in Hindi for the first time in the country in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -As per the revised schedule, the academic calendar for the first phase of MBBS will conclude on December 15, 2022. NMC has allotted 13 months time for this phase. Out of which, 15 weeks will be exhausted in university exam, vacation and holidays. There will be a total of 1638 hours of teaching in the Phase-I of MBBS.
