“Greg Barkle” was re-elected the President of ICC for how many years?

(A) Three

(B) Five

(C) ** Two

(D) One

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Greg Barclay has been unanimously re-elected as chairman of the International Cricket Council (ICC) for a second two-year term on 13 November, 2022. Barkley has been re-elected as independent president for a second two-year term. Barkley was elected unopposed after Zimbabwe’s Tawengwa Mukuhlani withdrew.

Concept note-2: -New Zealand’s Greg Barclay was on Saturday, November 12, 2022, unanimously re-elected as chairman of the International Cricket Council (ICC) for a second two-year term.

Concept note-3: -Greg Barclay has been re-elected as ICC chairman for another two-year term after his rival, Zimbabwe Cricket (ZC) chairman Tavengwa Mukuhlani, pulled out of the contest late on Friday. Barclay, who served his first stint as ICC chair between 2022 and this November, will now be in the position until 2024.

Concept note-4: -Besides Barclay’s re-election, BCCI secretary Jay Shah was elected as the head of ICC’s all powerful Finance and Commercial Affairs (F&CA) committee at the board meeting. Shah will have the responsibility of heading ICC’s most important committee.
