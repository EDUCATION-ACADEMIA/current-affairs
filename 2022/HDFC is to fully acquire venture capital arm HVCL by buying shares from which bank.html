HDFC is to fully acquire venture capital arm HVCL by buying shares from which bank?

(A) ICICI Bank

(B) RBI

(C) Axis Bank

(D) ** SBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Abhishek Mishra. Answer: Option D Explanation: According toHDFC Bank, it will acquire its venture capital subsidiary HVCL by buying out theState Bank of India’s 19.5 percent stake in the company.

Concept note-2: HDFCBank merged with Times Bank in February 2000. This was the first merger of twoprivate banks in the New Generation private sector banks category. Times Bank wasestablished by Bennett, Coleman and Co. Ltd., commonly known as The TimesGroup, India’s largest media conglomerate.

Concept note-3: State Bank of Indiahas a venture capital scheme operated through its subsidiary SBI Caps. ICICI flaggedoff a new venture capital company called Technology Development and InformationCompany of India with the objective of encouraging new technocrats in the privatesector in high-risk areas.

Concept note-4: HDB Financial Services Limited is asubsidiary company of HDFC Bank.
