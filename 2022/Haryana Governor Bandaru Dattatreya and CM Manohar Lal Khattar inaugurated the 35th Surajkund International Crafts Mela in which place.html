Haryana Governor Bandaru Dattatreya and CM Manohar Lal Khattar inaugurated the 35th Surajkund International Crafts Mela in which place?

(A) ** Faridabad

(B) Jhajjar

(C) Sonipat

(D) Gurugram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: On March 19, 2022 Haryana Governor Bandaru Dattatreyaand Chief Minister Manohar Lal Khattar officially inaugurated the world famousSurajkund Crafts Mela after a long gap of two years.

Concept note-2: Where isthe Surajkund crafts Mela held? The Surajkund crafts Mela 2023 is being held atSurajkund in Faridabad, Haryana.

Concept note-3: Jammu & Kashmir is the‘Theme State’ of the 35th Surajkund International Crafts Mela 2022, which isshowcasing its unique culture and rich heritage through various art forms andhandicrafts from the state. Hundreds of artists from Jammu & Kashmir will beperforming different folk arts and dances.

Concept note-4: Detailed Solution.The 35th Surajkund International Crafts Mela will be held in Faridabad from 19thMarch to 4th April 2022. Jammu and Kashmir is the theme State for it. The fairwill showcase the richness and diversity of handicrafts, handloom, and thecultural heritage of India.

Concept note-5: Inauguration of CRAFT MELA“Coalescence"- Craft-Culture-Community-Climate on 23 February 2022 at11 am Bikaner House, New Delhi. The Event Inauguration by Minister ofState of External Affairs Meenakshi Lekhi by President ICCR VinaySahasrabuddhe.
