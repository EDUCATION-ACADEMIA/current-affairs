Haryana state government has partnered with which state to revive the Saraswati river?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Punjab

(C) ** Himachal Pradesh

(D) Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The governments of Himachal and Haryana today signed amemorandum of understanding (MoU) for the construction of a dam on 77 acres inHimachal near the Adi Badri area of Yamunanagar for the revival of the Saraswatiriver.

Concept note-2: The governments of Haryana and Himachal Pradesh haveentered into an agreement to build a dam at Adi Badri in Yamunanagar district,which, among others, will rejuvenate the mythical Saraswati river. Adi Badri,situated in Haryana near the Himachal Pradesh border, is believed to be theriver’s origin point.

Concept note-3: The water stored by constructionof Adi Badri Dam is planned primarily for revival of River Saraswati anddevelopment of Saraswati heritage. Further, the project envisages providing 61.88hectare-metre water per annum for drinking and irrigation in the State ofHimachal Pradesh.
