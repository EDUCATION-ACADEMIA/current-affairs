Hassan Sheikh Mohamud has been selected as the next President of which country?

(A) Argentina

(B) France

(C) South Africa

(D) ** Somalia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hassan Sheikh Mohamud (somali; xassan shiikh maxamuud, Arabic: ; born 29 November 1955) is a Somali politician who has served as the president of Somalia since May 2022. He is the founder and current chairman of the Union for Peace and Development Party.

Concept note-2: -Hassan Sheikh Mohamud was declared president in a peaceful transition of power after incumbent President Mohamed conceded defeat and congratulated the victor.

Concept note-3: -Hamza Abdi Barre The acting prime minister is not numbered. For example, Osman Jama Ali was acting prime minister between Ali Khalif Galaydh, the eighth, and Hassan Abshir Farah, the ninth, he didn’t get a number. In June 2022, Hamza Abdi Barre succeeded the 20th Mohamed Hussein Roble as the 21st Prime Minister.

Concept note-4: -Currently, the president of Somalia is indirectly elected, chosen by the Federal Parliament of Somalia. The office of President of Somalia was established with the proclamation of the Republic of Somalia on 1 July 1960. The first president of Somalia was Aden Abdullah Osman Daar.
