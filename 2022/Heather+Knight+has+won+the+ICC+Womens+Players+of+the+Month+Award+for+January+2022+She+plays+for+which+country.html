Heather Knight has won the ICC Women’s Players of the Month Award for January 2022. She plays for which country?

(A) Netherlands

(B) New Zealand

(C) South Africa

(D) ** England

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At 169/8, Knight stitched a 100-run stand with Sophie Ecclestone for the ninth wicket and pushed England to 297, just 40 runs behind Australia.

Concept note-2: -January 2022 - Keegan Petersen (South Africa) He replicated his heroics in Newlands with a superb 82 as South Africa completed a seven-wicket victory. He finished as the leading scorer in the series with 276 runs and was adjudged the Player of the Series.

Concept note-3: -It was a double delight for Pakistan skipper Babar Azam as he was picked for both the ‘ODI Cricketer of the Year’ and the ‘Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for the ICC Men’s Cricketer of 2022’ on January 26, 2023.

Concept note-4: -Ben Stokes wins the ICC Men’s Test Player of the Year 2022.
