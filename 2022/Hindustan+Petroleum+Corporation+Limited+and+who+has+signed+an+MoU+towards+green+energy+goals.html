Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited and who has signed an MoU towards green energy goals?

(A) Bank of Baroda

(B) ** Solar Energy Corporation of India Limited

(C) Coal India

(D) SEBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NTPC Green Energy Ltd (NGEL) signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd (HPCL) on 03-01-2023 in New Delhi for Development of Renewable Energy based Power Projects to tap Business opportunities in RE and supply of 400 MW round the clock renewable power for requirements of HPCL.

Concept note-2: -In partnership with National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI), BOB Financial and Hindustan Petroleum have launched the HPCL BoB co-branded contactless RuPay Credit Card.

Concept note-3: -HPCL signed MoU with Solar Energy Corporation of India.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Ltd. Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Ltd (ONGC) has signed an MoU with Solar Energy Corporation of India (SECI).
