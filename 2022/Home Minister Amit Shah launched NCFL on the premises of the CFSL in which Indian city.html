Home Minister Amit Shah launched NCFL on the premises of the CFSL in which Indian city?

(A) Sonipat

(B) Shimla

(C) Chennai

(D) ** Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Union Home and Co-operation Minister, Amit Shah hasformally inaugurated the National Cyber Forensic Laboratory (NCFL) in Hyderabadtoday.

Concept note-2: UPSC Mains. Q. ‘National Cyber Forensic Laboratory’has been inaugurated in which city? Notes: Union Home Minister Amit Shahinaugurated the National Cyber Forensic Laboratory (NCFL) at Central ForensicScience Laboratory (CFSL) campus in Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: Union HomeMinister Amit Shah on Saturday inaugurated the ‘National Cyber ForensicLaboratory’ (NCFL) at Central Forensic Science Laboratory (CFSL) campus inHyderabad.

Concept note-4: The CFSL, CBI is located at New Delhi and hasone working unit each at Chennai and Mumbai.

Concept note-5: NCFL-E(National Cyber Forensic Laboratory for Evidenciary purpose) at CFSL, Hyderabadhas been created by upgrading the existing Digital Forensic Facility under the‘Centre for Cybercrime Prevention against women and children’ (CCPWC) scheme ofMHA, GoI.
