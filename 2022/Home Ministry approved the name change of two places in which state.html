Home Ministry approved the name change of two places in which state?

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Uttarakhand

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Home Ministry approves the name change of two places inUttar Pradesh.

Concept note-2: Rajahmahendravaram (Telugu: ), fromRajahmundry in 2015. Gurugram (Hindi: ) from Gurgaon in 2016. Prayagraj (Hindi:), from Allahabad, renamed in 2018. Atal Nagar (Hindi: ), from New Raipur in2018.

Concept note-3: Although initially named Ilahabad the name later becameAllahabad in an anglicized version in Roman script. In 2018 the name ofthe city was changed to Prayagraj by the State government ruled by YogiAdityanath.

Concept note-4: The total cost can go from an estimated sum of Rs200 to Rs. 500 crores or even more, depending on the size and global recognition ofthe city or state. The money is spent on updating the road signage system, highwaymarks, maps, official stationery of the state and civic authorities to conform to a newname.
