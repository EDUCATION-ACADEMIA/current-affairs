Hope mission has been launched by which country to mars?

(A) ** UAE

(B) USA

(C) Hong Kong

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: About: The UAE’s Mars Mission called ‘Hope’ was announcedin 2015 with the aim of creating mankind’s first integrated model of theRed planet’s (Mars) atmosphere.

Concept note-2: The Hope probe waslaunched from Japan by a Japanese H-IIA launch vehicle, built and operated byMitsubishi Heavy Industries (MHI) on 19 July 2020 and arrived at Mars on 9February 2021. It became the first mission to Mars by any West Asian, Arab orMuslim-majority country.

Concept note-3: It was India’s first interplanetarymission and it made ISRO the fourth space agency to achieve Mars orbit, afterRoscosmos, NASA, and the European Space Agency.
