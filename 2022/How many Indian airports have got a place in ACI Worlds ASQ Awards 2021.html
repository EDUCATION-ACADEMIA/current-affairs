How many Indian airports have got a place in ACI World’s ASQ Awards 2021?

(A) ** 6

(B) 3

(C) 4

(D) 2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: From India, six airports have found a place among the ‘BestAirport by Size and Region’, by the Airports Council International (ACI), in itsAirport Service Quality (ASQ) survey for the year 2021.

Concept note-2: In2021, from over 370,000 surveys, 111 awards have been won by 88 individual airportsaround the world. The award categories include Best Airports by Size (passengers peryear) and Region, as well as Best Hygiene Measures by Region, of which there are 46winners.

Concept note-3: There are 34 operational International Airports inIndia. Indira Gandhi International Airport is the largest International airportconstructed in 5495 acres. It is also the busiest airport in India followedby the Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport.

Concept note-4: Asof January 2022, ACI serves 717 members, operating 1950 airports in 185countries.
