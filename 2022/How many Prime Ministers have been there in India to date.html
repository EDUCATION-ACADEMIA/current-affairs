How many Prime Ministers have been there in India to date?

(A) 16

(B) 11

(C) ** 14

(D) 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Since 1947, India has had 14 prime ministers.

Concept note-2: Modiis the 14th and current prime minister of India, serving since 26 May2014.

Concept note-3: Of the 56 past prime ministers, nine served more than10 years while eight served less than a year. Robert Walpole is the onlyperson to have served as prime minister for more than two decades. LizTruss is the shortest-serving former prime minister, resigning after sevenweeks.

Concept note-4: Manmohan Singh was sworn in as Prime Minister on22nd May after the 2004 general elections and took the oath of office for a secondterm on 22nd May 2009. Dr. Singh and his wife Mrs. Gursharan Kaur have threedaughters.
