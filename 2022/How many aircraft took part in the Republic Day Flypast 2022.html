How many aircraft took part in the Republic Day Flypast 2022?

(A) 80

(B) ** 75

(C) 66

(D) 90

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: As many as forty-five aircraft of the Indian Air Force (IAF),four helicopters of the Indian Army, and an aircraft from the Indian Navy,participated in the air show.

Concept note-2: Indian Air Force Day 2022: IndianAir Force (IAF) is the fourth largest air force in the world. It consists of about139,576 active personnel, about 140,000 reserve personnel, and over 1,850aircraft.

Concept note-3: Indian Air Force has said that nine Rafale fighteraircrafts will participate in the fly-past over the Kartvyapath during Republic Dayparade on 26th January. The Republic Day flypast will comprise 45 IAF aircrafts -Indian Air Force.

Concept note-4: 75 IAF aircraft showcased formations like‘Netra’, ‘Vinaash’, ‘Tiranga’, ‘Trishul’ and ‘Varuna’, among others, comprising ofthe Rafale, Sukhoi, Jaguar, Mi-17, Sarang, Apache and Dakota aircraft ofthe Indian Airforce. Watch!

Concept note-5: Nine tableaux of CentralMinistries/Departments had participated in the parade. The tableaux of Ministry ofHousing & Urban Affairs (CPWD), based on the theme ‘Subhash 125’ andthe ‘Vande Bharatam’ dance group have been selected for the special prizecategory.
