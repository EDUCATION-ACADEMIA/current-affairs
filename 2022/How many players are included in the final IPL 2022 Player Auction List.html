How many players are included in the final IPL 2022 Player Auction List?

(A) 1090

(B) 750

(C) ** 590

(D) 630

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: the addition of two new franchises in the tournament; LucknowSuper Giants and Gujarat Titans. A total of 590 players (370 from India, 220overseas) will go under the hammer as franchises enter into a bidding war to rebuildtheir squads for the new season.

Concept note-2: The IPL Player Registrationclosed on 30th Nov 2022.
