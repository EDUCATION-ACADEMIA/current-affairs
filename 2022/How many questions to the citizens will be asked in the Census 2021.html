How many questions to the citizens will be asked in the Census 2021?

(A) 26

(B) ** 31

(C) 37

(D) 42

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Centre has finalised a set of 31 questions to be asked frompeople during the collection of data for Census 2021. The data will be collectedbetween April 1 to September 31 this year.

Concept note-2: Highlights TheCensus 2021 is to ask 31 questions to the citizens.

Concept note-3: Censusofficials to ask 31 questions, will seek information on what you eat, phones youown.

Concept note-4: The 2021 census is divided into 51 questions – althoughsome are voluntary – and must be completed on or as soon as possible after 21March. The census has been taking place in the UK every 10 years since the early19th century, and the 2021 edition is fast approaching.

Concept note-5: The2021 Census of India, also the 16th Indian Census, has been postponed till October2023, at least.
