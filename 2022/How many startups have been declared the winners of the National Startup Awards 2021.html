How many startups have been declared the winners of the National Startup Awards 2021?

(A) 59

(B) 60

(C) ** 46

(D) 50

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: During the ceremony, the Results of the National StartupAwards (NSA) 2021 were declared. A total of 46 Startups have been recognizedas winners of National Startup Awards 2021 along with 1 incubator and 1accelerator.

Concept note-2: The National Startup Awards had 192 finalists and36 winners.

Concept note-3: Karnataka has bagged 14, the most by any state, ofthe 46 national startup awards that were announced and given away by the centralgovernment on Saturday.
