How many teams will compete in the league stage of Ranji Trophy 2022?

(A) 40

(B) ** 38

(C) 25

(D) 36

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Ranji Trophy 2022/23 Squads: Full Squad List For All 38Teams.

Concept note-2: Ranji Trophy 2022/23: FormatA total of 39 teams willparticipate in the upcoming Ranji Trophy, with those sides being divided intofive groups. There are four Elite groups, with eight teams in each of thosegroups.

Concept note-3: This time, the Ranji Trophy is back in its full form,spanning 135 matches from December 13, 2022 to February 20, 2023. The top 32teams have been split into four groups, with eight teams each. Two teams from eachgroup will proceed to the knockouts after the teams play in a round robin leagueformat.

Concept note-4: But what is the format? The top two from each Elitegroup, consisting of eight teams each, make the quarter-finals after an intensiveleague phase of equal home and away matches. In Plate, which is a six-teampool, each side plays the other five after which the top four make it to thesemi-finals.
