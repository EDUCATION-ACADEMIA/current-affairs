How many tigers died in the last three years in India?

(A) 529

(B) ** 329

(C) 29

(D) 129

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to a government report, 329 tigers in India perished in the past three years as a result of poaching, natural disasters, and other factors.

Concept note-2: -India lost 329 tigers in the last three years due to poaching, natural and unnatural causes, the government has said.

Concept note-3: -Tiger deaths in India in recent years While more than 100 tiger deaths in a year are a matter of concern, it should be noted that the fatalities came down this year compared to 2021. In 2021, the official figures said the country had lost 127 tigers, the highest ever recorded.

Concept note-4: -According to government data published in 2019, between 40 and 50 people are killed by tigers each year.
