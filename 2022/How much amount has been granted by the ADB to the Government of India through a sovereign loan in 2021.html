How much amount has been granted by the ADB to the Government of India through a sovereign loan in 2021?

(A) USD 2.9 billion

(B) USD 5.1 billion

(C) USD 3.7 billion

(D) ** USD 4.6 billion

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: In 2021, ADB committed a record $4.6 billion in sovereignloans to India. ADB also committed $36.51 million in technical assistance and $3million in grants under the sovereign portfolio.

Concept note-2: DetailedSolution. The correct answer is The Asian Development Bank. The AsianDevelopment Bank (ADB) provided a record USD 4.6 billion loans to India in 2021,including USD 1.8 billion towards coronavirus response.

Concept note-3: It alsoprovides financing to certain private sector projects as well as public-privatepartnerships through grants, loans, technical assistance, and equity investments topromote development. The ADB is controlled by member countries, with the U.S.and Japan having the largest stake.
