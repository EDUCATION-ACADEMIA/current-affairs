How many Indian billionaires are on the 16th edition of Forbes Asia Heroes of Philanthropy List?

(A) Two

(B) Five

(C) ** Three

(D) Four

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gautam Adani and 2 other Indian billionaires on Forbes Asia Heroes of Philanthropy list. Gautam Adani, HCL Technologies’s Shiv Nadar, and Happiest Minds Technologies’ Ashok Soota are the three Indians who have been named in the 16th edition of Forbes Asia’s Heroes of Philanthropy list.

Concept note-2: -Gautam Adani, India’s richest person, pledged 600 billion rupees ($7.7 billion) when he turned 60 in June, making him one of India’s most generous philanthropists.

Concept note-3: -India’s Gautam Adani overtook Mukesh Ambani to become the richest person in Asia in February 2022.

Concept note-4: -As of Jan 2023, India has 166 billionaires which put the country third in the world, after the United States and China. Mukesh Ambani the chairman and largest shareholder of Reliance Industries, has been the richest Indian for 13 consecutive years.
