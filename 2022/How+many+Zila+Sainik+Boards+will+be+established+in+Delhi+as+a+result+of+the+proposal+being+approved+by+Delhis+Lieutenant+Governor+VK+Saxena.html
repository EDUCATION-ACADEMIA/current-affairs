How many Zila Sainik Boards will be established in Delhi as a result of the proposal being approved by Delhi’s Lieutenant Governor VK Saxena?

(A) 2

(B) ** 4

(C) 5

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"LG V K Saxena, who is also the president of the Rajya Sainik Board, has approved the setting up of four Zila Sainik Boards (ZSBs) in the NCT of Delhi. Districts where these boards will be set up include South-West, East/Shahdara, North-West and Central/New Delhi. Each ZSB will have a team of 10 officials.

Concept note-2: -The four ZSBs in Delhi will be set up in the South-West, East/Shahdara, North-West and Central/New Delhi districts with 10 officials each.

Concept note-3: -To assist the Central Government in this regard, there are 34 Rajya Sainik Boards and 409 Zila Sainik Boards in the country.

Concept note-4: -The Chief Minister of the State is the Chairman of the Rajya Sainik Board in the State and Collector is the Chairman of the Zila Sainik Board.
