How many airports have been awarded as among the ‘Best Airport by Size and Region’, by the ACI, in its ASQ survey for the year 2021?

(A) 4

(B) ** 6

(C) 2

(D) 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -From India, six airports have found a place among the ‘Best Airport by Size and Region’, by the Airports Council International (ACI), in its Airport Service Quality (ASQ) survey for the year 2021. The airports have been adjudged among the Asia Pacific region in different categories based on annual passenger traffic.

Concept note-2: -Mumbai, Delhi among 6 Indian airports awarded as ‘best airport by size’.

Concept note-3: -In 2021, from over 370,000 surveys, 111 awards have been won by 88 individual airports around the world. The award categories include Best Airports by Size (passengers per year) and Region, as well as Best Hygiene Measures by Region, of which there are 46 winners.

Concept note-4: -The Cochin International Airport Limited (CIAL) has been awarded the Airport Service Quality (ASQ) award 2022 by the Airport Council International (ACI). The Cochin International Airport Limited (CIAL) has been awarded the Airport Service Quality (ASQ) award 2022 by the Airport Council International (ACI).
