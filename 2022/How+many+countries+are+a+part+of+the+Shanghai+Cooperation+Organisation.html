How many countries are a part of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation?

(A) 5

(B) 10

(C) 12

(D) ** 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Islamic Republic of Iran becomes 9th member of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation.

Concept note-2: -The Shanghai Five consists of China, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Russia and Tajikistan, and emerged from a series of border demarcation and demilitarization talks which the four former Soviet republics held with China.

Concept note-3: -India invites Pakistan, China to 2023 SCO summit to be held in Goa.

Concept note-4: -India and Pakistan signed the memorandum of obligations in June 2016 in Tashkent, Uzbekistan, thereby starting the formal process of joining the SCO as full members. On 9 June 2017, at the historic summit in Astana, India and Pakistan officially joined SCO as full- members.
