Human Rights Day is being observed on which date?

(A) December 09

(B) December 08

(C) ** December 10

(D) December 07

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Human Rights day is celebrated every 10 December to createawareness and mobilize political will to promote respect for the rights and freedomsenshrined in the Universal Declaration of Human Rights adopted by the UnitedNations General Assembly in 1948.

Concept note-2: Human Rights Day 2022 –Video message from Dr TedrosHuman Rights day is celebrated every 10 December tocreate awareness and mobilize political will to promote respect for the rights andfreedoms enshrined in the Universal Declaration of Human Rights adopted by theUnited Nations General Assembly in 1948.

Concept note-3: The theme forInternational Human Rights Day 2022 has been set as ‘Dignity, Freedom, andJustice for All,’ in celebration of the 75th anniversary of the adoption of theUDHR on December 10, 2023. The year-long campaign will focus on theuniversality of the UDHR and how activism associated with it can helppeople.

Concept note-4: The 2022 theme of Human Rights Day is dignity,freedom, and justice for all.

Concept note-5: 10 December - Human RightsDayHuman Rights Day is celebrated on 10 December. The Universal Declaration ofHuman Rights was adopted in 1948 by the United Nations General Assembly. Thisday is observed to protect the fundamental human rights of all people and their basichuman freedom.
