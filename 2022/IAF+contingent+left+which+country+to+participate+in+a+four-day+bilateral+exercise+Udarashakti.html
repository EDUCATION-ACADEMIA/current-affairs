[SOURCES]

IAF contingent left which country to participate in a four-day bilateral exercise ‘Udarashakti’?

(A) ** Malaysia

(B) Nepal

(C) France

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A contingent of the Indian Air Force left for Malaysia today to participate in a bilateral exercise named ‘Udarashakti’.

Concept note-2: -Udarashakti is a bilateral exercise between Indian Air Force (IAF) and Royal Malaysian Air Force (RMAF). The four-day bilateral exercises recently started in Malaysia.

Concept note-3: -This joint military exercise is unique as it is for the first time that the Armed Forces of Malaysia and India held all three joint exercises in a year. In May 2022, Joint Naval Exercise Samdura Laksamana was conducted, and in August 2022, Joint Air Exercise Udara Shakti and now Joint Military Exercise.

Concept note-4: -What is Exercise Udarashakti? It is a bilateral air force exercise between India and Malaysia. Purpose: The exercise will give a chance to both countries to share and learn best practices and an opportunity for both to discuss mutual combat capabilities.

Concept note-5: -India - Malaysia joint military Exercise “Harimau Shakti -2022” commenced at Pulai, Kluang, Malaysia on 28th November and will culminate on 12th December 22. Exercise HARIMAU SHAKTI is an annual training event between the Indian and Malaysian Army which is being conducted since 2012.
