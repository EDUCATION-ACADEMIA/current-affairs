IISc in which city is to be the G20 science working group’s secretariat?

(A) Agra

(B) Vadodara

(C) ** Bengaluru

(D) Nashik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -G20 India | IISc Bengaluru to be the G20 Science working group’s secretariat.

Concept note-2: -The Indian Institute of Science (IISc) is a public, deemed, research university for higher education and research in science, engineering, design, and management. It is located in Bengaluru, in the Indian state of Karnataka.

Concept note-3: -Institutions | Government of India, Ministry of Education.

Concept note-4: -The Inception Meeting of Science20 (S20), the science engagement vertical of G20, was held at Puducherry from 30-31 January 2023.

Concept note-5: -The 2023 G20 Delhi summit (Hindi: 2023 G20 ) is the upcoming eighteenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), a summit scheduled to take place in Pragati Maidan, New Delhi in 2023.
