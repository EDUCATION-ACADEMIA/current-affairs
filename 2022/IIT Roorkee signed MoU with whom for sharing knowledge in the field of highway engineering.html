IIT Roorkee signed MoU with whom for sharing knowledge in the field of highway engineering?

(A) NHPC

(B) NHAI

(C) WAPCOS

(D) ** NHIDCL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: An MoU was signed on 8 June 2022 between NationalHighways Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (NHIDCL) andIIT-Roorkee for sharing knowledge on innovative ideas and technologies in the fieldof highway engineering.

Concept note-2: Since its establishment, theInstitute has played a vital role in providing the technical manpower andknow-how to the country and in pursuit of research. The Institute ranksamongst the best technological institutions in the world and has contributed toall sectors of technological development.

Concept note-3: Rankings.Internationally, IIT Roorkee was ranked 369 in the QS World University Rankingsof 2023 and 114 in Asia. It was ranked 701–800 in the Academic Rankingof World Universities of 2022. In India, it ranked sixth among engineeringcolleges by the National Institutional Ranking Framework in 2020 and ninthoverall.

Concept note-4: Established in 1847, its mission is to enable a creativeand dynamic learning environment, build industry-academic collaboration, anddevelop sustainable research solutions for the benefit of the nation. The Institute isdependent on Central Government funding to the extent of over 75% of itsneeds.
