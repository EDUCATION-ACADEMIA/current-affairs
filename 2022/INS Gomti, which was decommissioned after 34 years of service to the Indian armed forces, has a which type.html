Concept note-1: Q. INS Gomti, which was recently decommissioned after 34years of service to Indian armed forces, was a ? Notes: INS Gomti (a guided missilefrigate) was decommissioned at sunset on 28 May 2022 at the Naval Dockyard inMumbai after 34 years of service.
