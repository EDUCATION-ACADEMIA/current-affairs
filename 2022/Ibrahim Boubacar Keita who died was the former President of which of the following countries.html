Ibrahim Boubacar Keita, who died was the former President of which of the following countries?

(A) ** Mali

(B) Senegal

(C) Zambia

(D) Nigeria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Keïta founded the centre-left political party Rally for Mali (RPM) in 2001. After a number of unsuccessful campaigns, he was elected president in the 2013 presidential election and reelected in 2018.

Concept note-2: -The 2021 Malian coup d’état began on the night of 24 May 2021 when the Malian Army led by Vice President Assimi Goïta captured President Bah N’daw, Prime Minister Moctar Ouane and Minister of Defence Souleymane Doucouré.

Concept note-3: -Modibo Keïta (4 June 1915 – 16 May 1977) was a Malian politician who served as the first President of Mali from 1960 to 1968. He espoused a form of African socialism.
