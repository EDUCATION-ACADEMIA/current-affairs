In 2022, which edition of Soil Health Card Day is being celebrated?

(A) ** 7

(B) 5

(C) 10

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Every year India observes the Soil Health Card Day on19 February to commemorate the launch of the Soil Health Card (SHC)Scheme.

Concept note-2: The Soil Health Card Day commemorates the day SoilHealth Card Scheme was launched by the Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi onFebruary 19, 2015 at Suratgarh, Rajasthan.

Concept note-3: The theme ofWorld Soil Day for the year 2022 is ‘Soils: Where food begins’. Union Minister ofState for Ports, Shipping and Waterways and Tourism Shri. Shripad Naik attendedand addressed the programme. Soil Health Cards were distributed to thefarmers on this occasion.

Concept note-4: "Swasth Dharaa. Khet Haraa.” -Healthy Earth.

Concept note-5: More than 23 crore soil health cards havebeen distributed to farmers till date. Soil health card provides informationto farmers on nutrient status of their soil along with recommendation onappropriate dosage of nutrients to be applied for improving soil health and itsfertility.
