In Best Student Cities Ranking 2023, which city spotted at 103 ranks in India?

(A) Bangalore

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Hyderabad

(D) Lucknow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India’s highest-ranked student city is Mumbai at 103 spot. It isfollowed by Bengaluru at 114th rank.

Concept note-2: The highest rankedstudent city of India is Mumbai. Globally it is ranked 103rd. Bengaluru is at 114thposition after Mumbai. Chennai and Delhi have made their own entries in the listand are ranked 125th and 129th respectively.

Concept note-3: Manynotable bodies have ranked Mumbai as the best city for university education.Being the most popular city in India, Mumbai has an average literacy rateof 90%.

Concept note-4: QS Best Student Cities 2023: Check top 10cities; Mumbai ranks 1st in India | Education Gallery News,The IndianExpress.

Concept note-5: The latest edition of the QS Best Student Cities 2023uses the opinions of current students to rate university locations on factorsincluding affordability and desirability. This year’s top ten cities includeSydney, Tokyo, Seoul and Zurich – but once again, the top spot goes toLondon.
