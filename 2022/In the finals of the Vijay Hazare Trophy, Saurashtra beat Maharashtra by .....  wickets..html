In the finals of the Vijay Hazare Trophy, Saurashtra beat Maharashtra by ____ wickets.

(A) ** 5

(B) 3

(C) 4

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Vijay Hazare Trophy Final: Saurashtra beat Maharashtraby 5 wickets in Ahmedabad. Saurashtra beat Maharashtra by 5 wickets inthe final to win the Vijay Hazare Trophy at the Narendra Modi StadiuminAhmedabad on Friday. Batting first, Maharashtra scored 248 runs for theloss of 9 wickets in 50 overs.

Concept note-2: Tamil Nadu is the mostsuccessful team having won the trophy five times. Saurashtra cricket teamare the current champions, defeating Maharashtra in the (2022-23 VijayHazare Trophy) to win their second title.

Concept note-3: Saurashtracaptain Jaydev Unadkat, after winning the Vijay Hazare Trophy 2022 said, “Ifthere is one player in the side who deserves to play at the highest level,it’s Chirag Jani”.

Concept note-4: Tamil Nadu has won the trophy 5times which is the most by any team and is followed by Karnataka andMumbai with 4 titles each Karnataka and Tamil Nadu are the only twoteams in the history of the tournament to win the trophy for two consecutiveyears.

Concept note-5: Himachal Pradesh became the latest winner of theVijay Hazare Trophy winners list after defeating Uttar Pradesh in the 2021season.
