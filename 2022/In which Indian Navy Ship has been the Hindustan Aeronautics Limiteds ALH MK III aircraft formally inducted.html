In which Indian Navy Ship has been the Hindustan Aeronautics Limiteds ALH MK III aircraft formally inducted?

(A) INS Dhruv

(B) INS Chennai

(C) INS Brahmaputra

(D) ** INS Utkrosh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Indian Naval Air Squadron (INAS) 325, operating theindigenously built ALH MK III aircraft, was commissioned into the Indian Navy inan impressive ceremony held at INS Utkrosh, Port Blair, Andaman & NicobarCommand (ANC) on 31 May 22.

Concept note-2: Indian Naval Air Squadron324 was commissioned into the Indian Navy in the presence of Vice Admiral BiswajitDasgupta, Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Eastern Naval Command at INSDega, Visakhapatnam, on Monday.

Concept note-3: In a major boostto further strengthening the Coast Guard Region East, 840 Sqn (CG), anIndian Coast Guard Advanced Light Helicopter (ALH) Mk-III squadron, wascommissioned by DG Shri VS Pathania at ICG Air Station, Chennai onNovember 30, 2022.

Concept note-4: In Short. An Indian Coast GuardAdvanced Light Helicopter Mk-III squadron was commissioned in Chennai. Thecommissioning of 840 Sqn (CG) is indicative of the leap towards self-reliance in thefield of helicopter manufacturing.

Concept note-5: By Manjeet Negi: Anadvanced light helicopter squadron was commissioned into the Indian Navyin Visakhapatnam on Monday to further bolster its maritime surveillanceapparatus. It is the first naval squadron on the eastern seaboard operatingthe indigenously designed and built advanced light helicopter (ALH) MKIII.
