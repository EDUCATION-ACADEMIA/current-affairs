In which city a Special Health Care Abhiyan ‘Anchal’ has been launched in the Karauli district for pregnant women?

(A) ** Rajasthan

(B) Gujarat

(C) Punjab

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: In which city a Special Health Care Abhiyan ‘Anchal’ has beenlaunched in the Karauli district for pregnant women? Explanation: In Rajasthan, aSpecial Health Care Abhiyan ‘Anchal’ has been launched in the Karaulidistrict for pregnant women.

Concept note-2: UPSC Mains. Q. Whichstate launched a special Health Care scheme called ‘Anchal’ for pregnantwomen? Notes: Rajasthan launched a special Health Care scheme ‘Anchal’ inthe Karauli district for pregnant women.

Concept note-3: This uniquecampaign was started on the initiative of District Collector Ankit KumarSingh to ensure all necessary medical aid to pregnant women at the preand post-natal stage so that maternal and infant mortality rates could bereduced.
