In which country has the 8th Global Conference of Young Parliamentarians been organized?

(A) India

(B) ** Egypt

(C) Denmark

(D) Britain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Egypt hosts Eighth Global Conference of Young Parliamentariansin Sharm El Sheikh. Egypt is hosting the Eighth Global Conference of YoungParliamentarians in Sharm El Sheikh today.

Concept note-2: The Eighth GlobalConference of Young Parliamentarians will bring together young parliamentariansfrom around the world to coordinate parliamentary action to mitigate the impact ofclimate change and ensure that global warming does not exceed 1.5°C, in line withthe Paris Agreement.

Concept note-3: On 15 and 16 June, some 200young parliamentarians from 60 countries convened in Sharm-el-Sheikh,Egypt for the Eight IPU Global Conference of Young Parliamentarians.The conference was jointly organized by the IPU and the Parliament ofEgypt.

Concept note-4: Which country hosted the ‘Global Conference of YoungParliamentarians’? Notes: Egypt has hosted the Eighth Global Conferenceof Young Parliamentarians in Sharm El Sheikh. The conference is jointlyorganized by the House of Representatives and the Inter-Parliamentary Union(IPU).
