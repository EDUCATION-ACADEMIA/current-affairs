In which place France has conducted the 20th edition of bilateral naval exercise VARUNA 2022?

(A) Red Sea

(B) Caspian Sea

(C) Aral Sea

(D) ** Arabian Sea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The 20th edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise betweenIndian and French Navies – ‘VARUNA’ is being conducted in the Arabian Seafrom 30 Mar – 03 Apr 22.

Concept note-2: In April 2022, the Frenchand Indian navies held the five-day 20th edition of bilateral naval exercise,Varuna 2022 in the Arabian Sea. It was held from 30 March to 3 April2022.

Concept note-3: Varuna 2023: India-France conclude joint naval exercise inGoa.

Concept note-4: The 21st Edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise betweenIndia and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the Western Seaboard today, 16Jan 23.

Concept note-5: This year marks the 26th iteration of the Malabar seriesof exercises, which began in 1992 between the United States and India. Theexercise has evolved in scope and partnerships and now includes Japan andAustralia.
