In which state, PM inaugurated Vanijya Bhawan the new premises of the Commerce & Industry Ministry?

(A) Bihar

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated ‘Vanijya Bhawan’ and launched the NIRYAT portal in Delhi today.

Concept note-2: -Vanijya Bhawan, the new premises of Ministry of Commerce and Industry will be unveiled by Prime Minister Tomorrow. Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the new premises of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry Vanijya Bhawan on Thursday at 10:30 AM.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Thursday inaugurated the new premises of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry - ‘Vanijya Bhawan’. Besides, Modi also launched a new portal called NIRYAT– a one-stop place for all info needed for India’s foreign trade.
