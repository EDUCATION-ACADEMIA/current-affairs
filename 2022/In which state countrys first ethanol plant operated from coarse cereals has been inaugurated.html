In which state country’s first ethanol plant operated from coarse cereals has been inaugurated?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) ** Bihar

(C) Haryana

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In Bihar country’s first ethanol plant was inaugurated in Purnea district yesterday.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. India’s first green-field grain-based ethanol plant has been inaugurated in which state/UT? Notes: Bihar Chief Minister Nitish Kumar inaugurated the country’s first greenfield grain-based ethanol plant in Purnea.

Concept note-3: -PATNA: Bihar has the distinction of being the first state in the country to set up a greenfield grain-based ethanol production plant. Chief Minister Nitish Kumar inaugurated the state’s first ethanol production plant in Purnia district on Saturday.

Concept note-4: -Bihar Chief Minister Nitish Kumar on Saturday opened the country’s first greenfield grain-based ethanol plant in Purnea and said ethanol production will help reduce the cost of petrol and generate employment.

Concept note-5: -Nitish Kumar launches the country’s first greenfield grain-based ethanol plant, in Purnia.
