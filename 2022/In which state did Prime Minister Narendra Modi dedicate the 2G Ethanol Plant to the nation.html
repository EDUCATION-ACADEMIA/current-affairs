In which state did Prime Minister Narendra Modi dedicate the 2G Ethanol Plant to the nation?

(A) Uttarakhand

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) ** Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: On the occasion of World Biofuel Day, Prime Minister ShriNarendra Modi dedicated the 2nd generation (2G) Ethanol Plant in Panipat,Haryana to the nation today via video conferencing.

Concept note-2: PrimeMinister Narendra Modi on August 10 launched a second generation (2G)ethanol plant built at an estimated cost of Rs 900 crore in Haryana’sPanipat.

Concept note-3: With an objective to augment ethanol supplies, theGovernment allowed procurement of ethanol produced from other non-food feedstockbesides molasses, like cellulosic and lignocellulosic materials including petrochemicalroute.

Concept note-4: The 2G ethanol plant has been built at an estimated costof over Rs 900 crore by the Indian Oil Corporation Ltd (IOCL) and is located closeto the Panipat Refinery, said an official statement.

Concept note-5: New Delhi[India], August 10 (ANI): Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Wednesdayinaugurated the second generation (2G) Ethanol Plant in Panipat and said itwill help reduce pollution in Delhi, National Capital Region (NCR) andHaryana.
