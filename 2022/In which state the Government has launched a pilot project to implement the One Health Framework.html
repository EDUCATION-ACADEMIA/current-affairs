In which state the Government has launched a pilot project to implement the One Health Framework?

(A) Punjab

(B) ** Uttarakhand

(C) Telangana

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. The ‘One Health’ framework has been launched as a pilot project in which state? Notes: The Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying (DAHD) has launched a pilot project in the state of Uttarakhand to implement the One Health Framework by One Health Support Unit.

Concept note-2: -DAHD in collaboration with the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation (BMGF) and The Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) as implementation partner is implementing the One-Health Framework undertaking project in the states of Karnataka and Uttarakhand.

Concept note-3: -The “One Health” pilot project aimed at improving national and State-level resource allocation and policy ecosystem on early prediction, detection, and diagnosis of zoonotic diseases through increased quality, availability, and utility of data evidence, was launched in Bengaluru on Tuesday.

Concept note-4: -February 19-22, 2012, the first One Health Summit was held in Davos, Switzerland. The Summit presented the One Health concept as a way to manage health threats, focusing on food safety and security.

Concept note-5: -The Mission is intended to develop a unified framework for addressing priority ‘One Health diseases’ (diseases of zoonotic nature, transboundary animal diseases as well as diseases of epidemic/pandemic potential) through cross-ministerial coordination.
