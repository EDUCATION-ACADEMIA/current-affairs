In 2022, which anniversary of Siachen day was being celebrated?

(A) 27st

(B) 31st

(C) 41st

(D) ** 38th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -38th Siachen Day celebrates on 13 April 2022.

Concept note-2: -On this day in 1984, Indian troops first unfurled the tri colour at Bilafond La pass launching Operation Meghdoot. This was the codename for the Indian Armed Forces’ operation to seize control of the Siachen Glacier in Kashmir.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is 36th. The Indian Army observed the 36th Siachen Day on 13th April. On April 13, 1984, the Indian Army launched ‘’Operation Meghdoot” to secure Bilafond La and other passes on the Saltoro Ridgeline from Pakistani aggression.

Concept note-4: -Siachen Glacier is strategically important to India, Pakistan, and China since it serves as a hub for the Shaksgam Valley, Karakoram Pass, and Aksai Chin. As a result, keeping Siachen is critical for India in order to prevent incursion from Pakistan and China.

Concept note-5: -The Army said the team of Sappers led by Capt Shiva Chouhan will be responsible for numerous combat engineering tasks and will be deployed at the post for a duration of three months.
