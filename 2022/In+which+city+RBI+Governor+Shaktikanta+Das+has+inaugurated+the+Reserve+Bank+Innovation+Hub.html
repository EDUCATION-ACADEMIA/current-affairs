In which city RBI Governor Shaktikanta Das has inaugurated the Reserve Bank Innovation Hub?

(A) Hyderabad

(B) Mumbai

(C) ** Bengaluru

(D) Visakhapatnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Governor, Reserve Bank of India (RBI), Shri Shaktikanta Das inaugurated the Reserve Bank Innovation Hub (RBIH) on March 24, 2022 in Bengaluru, Karnataka.

Concept note-2: -Inception of RBIH The Reserve Bank Innovation Hub (RBIH) was launched on March 24 in Bengaluru by the governor himself.

Concept note-3: -Shaktikanta Das is the present RBI Governor, appointed for a period of three years.

Concept note-4: -Dr Urjit R. Patel served as the 24th governor of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) between September 2016 and December 2018. Born on October 28, 1963, Patel’s tenure had seen the demonetisation, among other things.

Concept note-5: -Gopal Natarajan. Mr. Gopal Natarajan is the Chief Administrative Officer (CAO) of the Reserve Bank Innovation Hub.
