In which city, the 47th meeting of the GST council is going to be held in June 2022?

(A) Chennai

(B) ** Srinagar

(C) Lucknow

(D) Bhubaneswar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The same week also witnessed the 47th GST Council meeting scheduled at Chandigarh for two days- the 28th and 29th of June 2022. Smt Nirmala Sitharaman chaired the meeting in the presence of the Minister of State for Finance and state ministers, including the MoF (state) Shri Pankaj Chaudary.

Concept note-2: -Since the 48th GST Council meeting was scheduled after a gap of 5-6 months, it had many items on its agenda. The 47th GST Council meeting happened in June 2022. Read on to learn about the highlights, live updates, and key expectations from the 48 GST Council meeting.

Concept note-3: -a. The 47th GST Council meeting held on 28th –29th June 2022 has discussed the reports tabled by various GoMs on GST rate rationalisation, fixation of GST rate on online gaming, and IT reforms. Key recommendations of the GST Council were pruning exemptions and providing a slew of measures for trade facilitation.
