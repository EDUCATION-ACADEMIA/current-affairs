In which city the first Incredible India International Cruise Summit-2022 has been held?

(A) Chennai

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Delhi

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Minister of Ports, Shipping and Waterways and AYUSH, Sarbananda Sonowal on Thursday announced that the 1st Incredible India International Cruise Conference-2022 will be held in Mumbai from 14th to 15th of May.

Concept note-2: -May 14-15, 2022, Hotel Trident, Nariman Point, Mumbai, India.

Concept note-3: -Option [C] Mumbai : Union Tourism Minister, G. Kishan Reddy inaugurated the first Incredible India International Cruise Conference (IIICC) in Mumbai. He also invited domestic and international tourists to ‘Come, Cruise in India.

Concept note-4: -Mumbai will host India’s first International Cruise Conference – Mumbai will host the 1st Incredible India International Cruise Conference-2022, which will focus on Developing India as a Cruise Hub. said Sarbananda Sonowal, Union Minister for Ports, Shipping and Waterways, and AYUSH.
