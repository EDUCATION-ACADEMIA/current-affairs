In which country, the NATO military exercise ‘Cold Response 2022’ has been started?

(A) ** Norway

(B) Denmark

(C) Somalia

(D) South Sudan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -More than 30,000 soldiers from NATO and partner nations took part in exercise Cold Response in Norway in the winter of 2022.

Concept note-2: -Exercise Cold Response 2022 was certainly living up to its name. Cold Response is a bi-annual drill organised and hosted by the Norwegian Armed Forces since 2006 – with the exception of 2020, when the exercise was cancelled because of the COVID-19 pandemic, making this year’s edition even more important.

Concept note-3: -Training in Norway allows NATO Allies and partners to practise their skills operating in extreme and rugged surroundings, from frozen fjords to shivering seas to ice-encrusted mountains. There’s a reason that Norway hosts the NATO Centre of Excellence for Cold Weather Operations!

Concept note-4: -The North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) has organised the massive military drill ‘Cold Response 2022’ in Norway from March 14, 2022, and will continue till April 01, 2022. The exercise is held in Norway every second year, for NATO Allies and partners.
