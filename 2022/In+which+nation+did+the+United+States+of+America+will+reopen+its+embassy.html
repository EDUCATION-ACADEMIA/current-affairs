In which nation did, the United States of America will reopen its embassy?

(A) Afghanistan

(B) ** Solomon Islands

(C) Syria

(D) Iraq

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Department informed the government of Solomon Islands that the opening of the embassy became official as of January 27, 2023, and we thanked them for their support to advance our bilateral relationship in this way.

Concept note-2: -From the closure of the U.S. embassy in Honiara in 1993 until 2023, the United States did not have an embassy in the Solomon Islands. The U.S. embassy reopened on the Solomon Islands on 2 February 2023.

Concept note-3: -Embassy of the People’s Republic of China in Solomon Islands.
