India International Science Festival 2022 celebrated in which city?

(A) ** Bhopal

(B) Dehradun

(C) Indore

(D) Patna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Madhya Pradesh Council of Science and Technology(MPCOST), Bhopal is the nodal agency for this science festival. More than 8,000delegates from across the country will participate in this science festivalfrom January 21 to 24.

Concept note-2: The eighth India InternationalScience Festival is to be held in Bhopal. The Union Minister Jitendra Singhinaugurated the festival.

Concept note-3: The 8th edition of the IndiaInternational Science Festival (IISF) was inaugurated at the Maulana AzadNational Institute of Technology (MANIT), Bhopal, on January 21, 2023.The theme of the four-day festival is Marching towards Amrit Kaal withScience, Technology, and Innovation.

Concept note-4: Celebrating ScienceIn The City Of Lakes: India International Science Festival To Be Held InBhopal In January. India International Science Festival 2022 will be held inBhopal in January 2023.

Concept note-5: Notes: Union Ministry of EarthSciences (MoES) launched the 7th edition of the India International ScienceFestival (IISF). This year, IISF will be held from December 10-13 in Panaji,Goa.
