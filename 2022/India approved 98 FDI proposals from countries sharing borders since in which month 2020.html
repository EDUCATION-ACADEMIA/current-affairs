India approved 98 FDI proposals from countries sharing borders since in which month 2020?

(A) May

(B) February

(C) ** April

(D) January

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The government has approved 98 foreign direct investment(FDI) proposals from countries sharing land border with India since April 2020 out of423 received from sectors including trading and electronics, Parliament was informedon Wednesday.

Concept note-2: Trend on Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) inIndia. Singapore (27.01%) and USA (17.94%) have emerged as top 2 sourcing nationsin FDI equity flows into India in FY2021-22 followed by Mauritius (15.98%),Netherland (7.86%) and Switzerland (7.31%).

Concept note-3: Singapore isranked one FDI provider to India. The small island nation has invested $15.9 billionin FY22 which is accounted for 27 per cent of India’s total FDI received. USA isIndia’s second biggest investor with FDI of $10.5 billion, with 18 per cent of totalFDI.

Concept note-4: Gujarat is the top recipient state during the FY 2020-21with a 37 percent share of the total FDI equity inflows, followed by Maharashtra (27percent) and Karnataka (13 percent).
