India beat which country to win the 2022 SAFF U20 Championship title?

(A) Japan

(B) ** Bangladesh

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India clinched the title defeating Bangladesh by 5–2 in the finalon 5 August 2022.

Concept note-2: India beat Bangladesh 5-2 to liftSAFF Under-20 football championship trophy.

Concept note-3: TheSAFF U-18/U-19/U-20 Championship is a biennial football championship formale footballers under the age of 20 and is organized by the South AsianFootball Federation (SAFF). The championship was held for the first time inAugust 2015 in Nepal.

Concept note-4: India set to host SAFF U-18Women’s, SAFF U-19 Championships in 2022.
