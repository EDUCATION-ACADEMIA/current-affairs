India has collaborated with which country to create 150 ‘Villages of Excellence’ in 12 States of India?

(A) ** Israel

(B) Germany

(C) Japan

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: New Delhi, Jan 28 India and Israel will work together to create150 ‘Villages of Excellence’ that will help farmers to adopt latest technology in thefarm sector.

Concept note-2: The Israel government has established 29 Centre ofExcellence (CoEs) in 12 States. The Centre on Friday said it has decided to convertas many as 150 villages into ‘Villages of Excellence’ in 12 States with technicalassistance from the Israel government.
