[SOURCES]

India has decided to establish the ‘Education and Skills Development Working Group’ with which country?

(A) Germany

(B) ** USA

(C) France

(D) Finland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India and the US have decided to establish a new education group to further strengthen cooperation in the field of education and skill development through joint collaborations and promote student and scholar mobility to build people-to-people linkages.

Concept note-2: -UK has entered into a partnership with India under skill India programme.

Concept note-3: -Rajasthan has become the first state to incorporate skill development programmes in higher education. The Programme was launched in 2017 in Jaipur. The programme was launched by the state’s Higher Education Minister Kiran Maheshwari and Vice-Chancellor of IGNOU Prof Ravindra Kumar.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Kanpur. Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone of the country’s first Indian Institute of Skills (IIS) in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh to make India the Skill Capital of the World.
