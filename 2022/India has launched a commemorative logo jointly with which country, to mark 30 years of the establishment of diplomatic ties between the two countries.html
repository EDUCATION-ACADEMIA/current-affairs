India has launched a commemorative logo jointly with which country, to mark 30 years of the establishment of diplomatic ties between the two countries?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) Russia

(C) Mauritius

(D) ** Israel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: It also depicts the growing strategic partnership between thetwo sides. Speaking on the occasion, Ambassador Sanjeev Singla said, India is proudto mark the 30th anniversary of its bilateral relations with Israel and lookforward to using the special logo throughout the year to celebrate this specialmilestone.

Concept note-2: It also depicts the growing strategic partnershipbetween the two sides. India and Israel on Monday launched a commemorative logoto mark the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic ties between the twocountries.

Concept note-3: The logo features the Star of David andthe AshokaChakra- the two symbols that adorn the national flags of bothcountries- and forms the numeral 30 depicting the 30th anniversary of bilateralrelations.

Concept note-4: The correct answer is the Netherlands. A speciallogo was unveiled to commemorate 75 years of diplomatic relations betweenIndia and the Netherlands, in March 2022. The logo features a lotus and atulip – the national flowers of both countries. The chakra in the middle ofthe logo depicts the bond of friendship.

Concept note-5: The winningdesign created by an Indian student, Nikhil Kumar Rai, was chosen through ajoint decision of the Embassies and Consulates from both countries. It wasselected as the official logo for the 30th anniversary of Israel- India diplomaticrelations.
