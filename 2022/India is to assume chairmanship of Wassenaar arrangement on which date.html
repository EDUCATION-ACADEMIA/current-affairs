India is to assume chairmanship of Wassenaar arrangement on which date?

(A) 2 February 2023

(B) ** 1 January 2023

(C) 6 January 2023

(D) 5 January 2023

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: What Is Wassenaar Arrangement?: On 01-01-2023, Indiaassumes the chairmanship of the plenary of the Wassenaar Arrangement forone year.

Concept note-2: India joined the Wassenaar Arrangement inDecember 2017 as its 42nd participating state.

Concept note-3: TheMinistry of External Affairs (MEA) has announced that India will assumeChairmanship of Wassenaar Arrangement (WA) on 01 January 2023 fora period of one year.

Concept note-4: India assumed Chairmanshipof Wassenaar Arrangement (WA) Plenary starting from January 1, 2023.India will hold the Chairmanship for one year.

Concept note-5: On 3-4December, the 19th Plenary meeting of the Wassenaar Arrangement washeld in Vienna and chaired by Ambassador Liselotte Plesner of Denmark.Participating States agreed to continue addressing new challenges to the Agreement,including keeping pace with new advancements in technology, innovation, andresearch.
