India reached up to which position in the Miss World 2021 pageant?

(A) 7

(B) 6

(C) 5

(D) ** 11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Miss India World 2021 Manasa Varanasi made India proud by making it to the Top 13 list at Miss World 2021. Her finale gown was spectacular and was designed by Falguni and Shane Peacock.

Concept note-2: -Manasa Varanasi is an Indian model, engineer and beauty pageant title holder who was crowned Femina Miss India 2020. She represented India at the Miss World 2021 pageant in San Juan, Puerto Rico and finished as a Top 13 semifinalist.

Concept note-3: -At the end of the event, Toni-Ann Singh of Jamaica crowned Karolina Bielawska of Poland as Miss World 2021.
