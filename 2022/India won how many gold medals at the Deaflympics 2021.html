India won how many gold medals at the Deaflympics 2021?

(A) ** 8

(B) 15

(C) 17

(D) 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India rank 9th in the event which includes Sports shooterDhanush Srikanth won the first medal as well as first gold medal for India during theevent as he clinched a gold medal in the men’s 10m air rifle shooting eventwith a new deaf world record by registering a score of 247.5 points in thefinal.

Concept note-2: Detailed Solution. The correct answer is ninthposition. India’s largest contingent ever at a Deaflympics – 65 athletes, returnedwith a total of 16 medals from the event. These include 8 Gold, 1 silver,and 7 bronze.

Concept note-3: In the best-ever performance, Indianteam won 16 medals including 8 Gold medals in the Deaflympics, held inBrazil.

Concept note-4: How many medals did India win in Olympics 2021 now?India broke their previous best of six medals in a single Olympics edition by finishingthe Tokyo Games with a record total of seven medals (1 gold, 2 silver, and 4bronze) (London 2012). With this, India earned 48th place in the total medalstandings.

Concept note-5: On this day in 2021, Neeraj Chopra won the men’sjavelin throw gold medal at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics. It was India’s first-everOlympic medal in athletics and the realisation of a long-standing dream - one whichlegends like Milkha Singh and PT Usha had also chased but fallen short of byagonisingly narrow margins.
