India defeated Australia to win which Sultan of Johor Cup?

(A) 2

(B) 1

(C) ** 3

(D) 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India beat Australia 5-4 in shootout, claim 3rd Sultan of Johor Cup crown.

Concept note-2: -India edged the shootout 5-4 and won their third Sultan of Johor Cup title.

Concept note-3: -Sultan Johor Cup 2022 hockey: India beat Australia in final to win record-equalling third title. Tied 1-1 after full-time, the Indian men’s hockey team beat Australia 5-4 in the shootout to equal England as the most successful team in the competition.

Concept note-4: -The last time India beat Australia outrightly in regulation time, as per International Hockey Federation’s data, was on November 29, 2016 – a 3-2 win in a test match in Australia.
