India has been ranked at which position on the WEF Travel and Tourism Development Index 2021?

(A) 67th

(B) 47th

(C) 58th

(D) ** 54th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -WEF’s Travel and Tourism Competitiveness Index 2021: India Ranks 54. The World Economic Forum’s (WEF) ranked India 54th position (down from 46th in 2019) with a score of 4.1 in its Travel and Tourism Development Index 2021.

Concept note-2: -In a detailed analysis on India’s ranking and performance on the index based on 17 different parameters, ETTravelWorld tries to decode reasons which led to its drop in ranking by 8 points in comparison to 2019-rankings, when the country was placed at the 46th spot. In 2021, India has been ranked 54th.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which country has topped the Travel and Tourism Development Index 2021? Notes: Japan has topped the Travel and Tourism Development Index 2021, followed by the United States, Spain, France and Germany. India is ranked 54th with a score of 4.2 which is eight less than 2019.

Concept note-4: -India 54th On Global Travel Development Index, Top-Ranked In South Asia.

Concept note-5: -GUWAHATI: According to the World Economic Forum’s (WEF) latest study, India ranks 54th place in the global Travel and Tourism Development Index. The study was titled - Travel and Tourism Index 2021 and assessed a total of 117 countries, with India grabbing the 54th position in the overall rankings.
