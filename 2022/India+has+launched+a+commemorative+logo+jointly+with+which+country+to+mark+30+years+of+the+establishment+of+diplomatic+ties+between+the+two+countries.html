India has launched a commemorative logo jointly with which country, to mark 30 years of the establishment of diplomatic ties between the two countries?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) Russia

(C) Mauritius

(D) ** Israel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Speaking on the occasion, Ambassador Sanjeev Singla said, India is proud to mark the 30th anniversary of its bilateral relations with Israel and look forward to using the special logo throughout the year to celebrate this special milestone.

Concept note-2: -It also depicts the growing strategic partnership between the two sides. India and Israel on Monday launched a commemorative logo to mark the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic ties between the two countries.

Concept note-3: -The logo features the Star of David and the AshokaChakra- the two symbols that adorn the national flags of both countries- and forms the numeral 30 depicting the 30th anniversary of bilateral relations.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is the Netherlands. A special logo was unveiled to commemorate 75 years of diplomatic relations between India and the Netherlands, in March 2022. The logo features a lotus and a tulip – the national flowers of both countries. The chakra in the middle of the logo depicts the bond of friendship.
