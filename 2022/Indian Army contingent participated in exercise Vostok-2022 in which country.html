Indian Army contingent participated in exercise Vostok-2022 in which country?

(A) Canada

(B) ** Russia

(C) Germany

(D) Ukraine

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Indian Army Contingent Participates in Exercise Vostok-2022 at Sergeyevsky, East Military District Russia. A multilateral strategicand command Exercise Vostok - 2022 has commenced today at the traininggrounds of the Eastern Military District of Russia from 01 to 07 September2022.

Concept note-2: This follows Russia’s quadrennial military exercises knownas Vostok (East), which took place from 1–7 September. This year, theyfeatured units from China, India, Laos, Mongolia, Nicaragua and Syria,and around 50,000 personnel.

Concept note-3: India has suffered fromIslamist terrorism in the past-therefore, it is important for India to engage withthese nations for its national security interests. Fourth, Russia is criticallyimportant for India’s energy security.

Concept note-4: The concept of theVostok-2022 strategic command post exercise stipulates various operationsinvolving inter-branch and coalition forces in the interests of maintaining thesecurity of the Russian Federation in the Eastern Military District’s zone ofresponsibility.
