Indian Army has decided to shift the Army Day Parade of 2023 to which area?

(A) Eastern Command

(B) Northern Command

(C) ** Southern Command

(D) Central Command

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Indian Army has decided to shift its annual ArmyDay parade out of Delhi and has decided to hold it in 2023 in the SouthernCommand area.

Concept note-2: Republic Day Parade 2023: Indian Army toshowcase ‘Made in India’ weaponry

Concept note-3: The 75th Army DayParade was held on Sunday at the MEG and Centre in Bengaluru with theChief of the Army Staff, General Manoj Pande reviewing the parade andpresenting gallantry awards.

Concept note-4: Today, the Southern Commandcomprises of two Corps with their Headquarters, located at Jodhpur andBhopal. Amongst the static formations are the Maharashtra, Gujarat and GoaArea, with its HQ at Mumbai and the Dakshin Bharat Area with its HQ atChennai.

Concept note-5: In 2022, the Indian Army’s theme for the eventwas “In Stride with the Future”. It was seen as an acknowledgement of the“increasingly critical role played by niche and disruptive technologies in modernwarfare”.
