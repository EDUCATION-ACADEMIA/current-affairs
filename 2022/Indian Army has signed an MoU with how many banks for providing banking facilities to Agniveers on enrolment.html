Indian Army has signed an MoU with how many banks for providing banking facilities to Agniveers on enrolment?

(A) 5

(B) ** 11

(C) 10

(D) 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Indian Army has signed historic MoU with 11 Banks vizState Bank of India, Punjab National Bank, Bank of Baroda, IDBI Bank, ICICIBank, HDFC Bank, Axis Bank, Yes Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank, IDFC FirstBank and Bandhan Bank for providing banking facilities to Agniveers onenrollment.

Concept note-2: Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bank ofBaroda. Under the MOU bank would offer customized services along withfacilities to the account holders.

Concept note-3: The correct answer isAxis Bank. Axis Bank has signed an MoU with the Indian Navy, offering adefence service salary package. Through the exclusive defence service salarypackage–Power Salute–Axis Bank will offer numerous benefits to all ranksof the Indian Navy, veterans and cadets.

Concept note-4: The MoUwas signed by Lt. Gen. Ravin Khosla (AVSM, SM, VSM), DG (MP & PS),Indian Army, and Chandra Shekhar Ghosh, Managing Director & CEO,Bandhan Bank, in New Delhi.

Concept note-5: Total Amount to be given asSeva Nidhi Package after 4 years service (one time): Rs. 11.71 Lakh. 25% ofAgniveer candidates will later on become the part of regular cadre of Armedforces.
