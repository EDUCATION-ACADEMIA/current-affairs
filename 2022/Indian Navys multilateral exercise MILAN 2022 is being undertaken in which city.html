Indian Navy’s multilateral exercise MILAN 2022 is being undertaken in which city?

(A) Mumbai

(B) ** Visakhapatnam

(C) Hyderabad

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The latest edition of Indian Navy’s multilateral exercise MILAN 2022 is scheduled to commence from 25 Feb 22 in the ‘City of Destiny’, Visakhapatnam. MILAN 22 is being conducted over a duration of 9 days in two phases with the harbour phase scheduled from 25 to 28 February and Sea Phase from 01 to 04 March.

Concept note-2: -Indian Navy’s multilateral exercise Milan-2022 was organized from February 25, 2022, in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. It was conducted over a duration of nine days in two phases with the harbour phase scheduled from February 25 and the Sea Phase from March 1 to March 4.

Concept note-3: -2022. The 11th edition of Milan was held from 25 February to 4 March 2022 in Visakhapatnam. It was the largest edition of the exercise, and was the first time it was held in Visakhapatnam under the Eastern Naval Command.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Navy’s multilateral Exercise MILAN 2022 is scheduled to commence from 25th Feb 2022 in the ‘City of Destiny’, Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 46. India has invited a total of 46 nations to participate in the multinational naval exercise ‘Milan’ in Visakhapatnam, scheduled from February 25, 2022. The theme of this edition of exercise Milan is camaraderie, cohesion and collaboration.
