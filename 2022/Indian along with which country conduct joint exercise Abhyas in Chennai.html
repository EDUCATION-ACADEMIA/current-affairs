Indian along with which country conduct joint exercise ‘Abhyas’ in Chennai?

(A) Japan

(B) North Korea

(C) ** USA

(D) UK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Exercise Yudh Abhyas, conducted annually between India andUSA with the aim of exchanging best practices, tactics, techniques and proceduresbetween the Armies of the two nations.

Concept note-2: The exercise ‘VeerGuardian-2023’ involving the Indian Air Force (IAF) and Japan Air Self DefenceForce (JASDF) will be held at Japan’s Hyakuri air base. The IAF said on Saturdaythat its deployment at the exercise will include four Su-30 MKI jets, two C-17aircraft and one IL-78 plane.

Concept note-3: 21st EDITION OF INDIAFRANCE BILATERAL NAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition ofthe Bilateral Naval Exercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commencedon the Western Seaboard today, 16 Jan 23.

Concept note-4: Malabar Exercise isa multilateral naval war-gaming exercise that began in 1992. It began as a bilateralexercise between India’s and the United States’ navies.

Concept note-5: YudhAbhyas 2021 is the 17th edition of this bilateral joint military exercise between Indiaand the USA. The exercise is hosted alternately by the two countries since2004.
