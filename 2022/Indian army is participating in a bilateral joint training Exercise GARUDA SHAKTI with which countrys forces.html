Indian army is participating in a bilateral joint training Exercise GARUDA SHAKTI with which country’s forces?

(A) Australia

(B) ** Indonesia

(C) Australia

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Indian Army Special Forces and Indonesian SpecialForces are participating in the eighth edition of the bilateral military exercise‘Garuda Shakti’ at Karawang, Indonesia that commenced on 21 November2022.

Concept note-2: Garuda Shakti is a bilateral exercise between specialforces of armies of India and Indonesia.

Concept note-3: India, Kazakhstan holdjoint military exercise in Meghalaya | North East India News,The IndianExpress.

Concept note-4: The correct answer is Indonesian Army. Garuda Shaktiis a bilateral exercise between special forces of armies of India(Para Special Force)and Indonesia. It had started off in 2012, in India. It is conducted as part of militarydiplomacy between the Indian and Indonesian Army alternatively in India andIndonesia.

Concept note-5: Vajra Prahar’ is a Special Forces joint trainingexercise conducted alternately in India and the US to share the best practicesand experiences in areas such as joint mission planning and operationaltactics as also to improve interoperability between the Special Forces of bothnations.
