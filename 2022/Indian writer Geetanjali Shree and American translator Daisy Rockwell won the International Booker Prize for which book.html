Indian writer Geetanjali Shree and American translator Daisy Rockwell won the International Booker Prize for “which book”?

(A) ** Tomb of Sand

(B) Khali Jagah

(C) Reta-samadhi

(D) Tirohit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: ’Tomb of Sand’ is the first book in any Indian language to winthe prestigious annual award. Writer Geetanjali Shree and translator Daisy Rockwellmet in person for the first time a few days before the announcement of theInternational Booker Prize in London.

Concept note-2: Author Geetanjali Shree(left) and translator Daisy Rockwell have won the International Booker Prize 2022for ‘Tomb of Sand’.

Concept note-3: Hindi novelist Geetanjali Shree createdhistory by winning the International Booker Prize 2022 for her novel ‘Tomb of Sand’.Her’s is the first novel written in an Indian language, and translated into English,which has won the prestigious award.

Concept note-4: The International BookerPrize - its 2022 edition won by Geetanjali Shree for her Hindi novel, Tomb of Sand,the first Indian-language work to win the prize - is awarded to a work of translationpublished in the same territories.

Concept note-5: The correct answer is Tomb ofSand. Author Geetanjali Shree’s novel ‘Tomb of Sand’ has become the first Hindilanguage work of fiction to be shortlisted for the International Booker Prize. Thebook, translated into English by Daisy Rockwell will compete with five other titlesfrom around the world.
