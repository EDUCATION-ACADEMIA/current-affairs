Indian Army Chief Manoj Pande has been conferred with the honorary rank of Army General by which country?

(A) Japan

(B) ** Nepal

(C) Bhutan

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Army Chief Manoj Pande conferred honorary rank of Nepal Army General. Indian Army chief General Manoj Pande was conferred the title of Honorary General of the Nepali Army by Nepal President Bidya Devi Bhandari in Kathmandu on 5 September.

Concept note-2: -India also confers the honorary rank of “General of Indian Army” to the Nepal Army Chief.

Concept note-3: -Indian Army Chief Manoj Pande conferred honorary rank of Nepal Army General.

Concept note-4: -Captain Manoj Kumar Pandey, PVC (25 June 1975 – 3 July 1999) was an officer of the Indian Army who was posthumously awarded India’s highest military honour, the Param Vir Chakra, for his audacious courage and leadership during the Kargil War in 1999.
