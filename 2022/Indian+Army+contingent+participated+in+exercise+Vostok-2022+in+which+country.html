Indian Army contingent participated in exercise Vostok-2022 in which country?

(A) Canada

(B) ** Russia

(C) Germany

(D) Ukraine

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Army Contingent Participates in Exercise Vostok- 2022 at Sergeyevsky, East Military District Russia. A multilateral strategic and command Exercise Vostok - 2022 has commenced today at the training grounds of the Eastern Military District of Russia from 01 to 07 September 2022.

Concept note-2: -This follows Russia’s quadrennial military exercises known as Vostok (East), which took place from 1–7 September. This year, they featured units from China, India, Laos, Mongolia, Nicaragua and Syria, and around 50,000 personnel.

Concept note-3: -India has suffered from Islamist terrorism in the past-therefore, it is important for India to engage with these nations for its national security interests. Fourth, Russia is critically important for India’s energy security.

Concept note-4: -The concept of the Vostok-2022 strategic command post exercise stipulates various operations involving inter-branch and coalition forces in the interests of maintaining the security of the Russian Federation in the Eastern Military District’s zone of responsibility.
