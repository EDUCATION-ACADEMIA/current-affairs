Indian Women’s Hockey Team won which position in the Asia Cup 2022?

(A) 2nd

(B) ** 3rd

(C) 1st

(D) 4th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gurjit Kaur’s solitary goal in the first quarter was enough to earn the Indian hockey team a 1-0 win in the title decider. The Indian hockey team beat Spain 1-0 in the Women’s FIH Nations Cup 2022 final on Saturday and earned themselves a place in the 2023-24 FIH Pro League.

Concept note-2: -The Indian women’s team won the Asia Cup 2022 defeating Sri Lanka by eight wickets in the final. Opener Smriti Mandhana struck a brilliant 25 ball-51 not out as India clinched the T20 Women’s Asia Cup, routing Sri Lanka by eight wickets in the final on Saturday.

Concept note-3: -Asia Cup hockey: Rajkumar Pal’s solitary goal secures bronze medal for India. Rajkumar Pal’s strike in the first quarter helped India beat Japan 1-0 for the third-place finish. The Indian men’s hockey team beat Japan and settled for the bronze medal at the Asia Cup 2022 in Jakarta, Indonesia on Wednesday.
