[SOURCES]

India’s employable talent rises; women workforce increases according to The Wheebox India Skills Report for which year?

(A) 2022

(B) ** 2023

(C) 2020

(D) 2021

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s employable talent has risen in the past one year, according to The Wheebox India Skills Report 2023.

Concept note-2: -The great majority of women workers in India are engaged either in agriculture and traditional rural industries or in service occupations.

Concept note-3: -The female labor force participation rate in India increased by 0.6 percentage points in 2021 in comparison to the previous year. In total, the rate amounted to 19.23 percent in 2021. Female labor force participation is the share of women over 15 years who are economically active.

Concept note-4: -According to the latest World Bank figures, from 2021, fewer than 1 in 5 Indian women work – at least formally. (Though most work in India is informal – agricultural or domestic work – which often doesn’t get counted.)

Concept note-5: -1)women have been challenged by inequality in workforce. 2)until modern times,legal and cultural practices combined with the inertia of long standing religion. 3) women lack acess to higher education. 4)women in the workforce earning wages and salaries.
