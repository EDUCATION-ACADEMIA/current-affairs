India’s first Chief of Defence Staff Bipin Rawat will be posthumously honoured with which among the following awards?

(A) ** Padma Vibhushan

(B) Bharat Ratna

(C) Padma Bhushan

(D) None of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: He was also awarded the COAS Commendation on twooccasions and the Army Commander’s Commendation. While serving with theUnited Nations, he was awarded the Force Commander’s commendation twice. Hewas posthumously honoured with the Padma Vibhushan in the 2022 Republic Dayhonours list.

Concept note-2: On March 21, 2022, President Ram Nath Kovindposthumously presented the Padma Vibhushan to the country’s first Chief of DefenseStaff (CDS) General Bipin Rawat at a civilian decoration ceremony held atRashtrapati Bhavan.

Concept note-3: India’s first Chief of Defence Staff (CDS)General Bipin Rawat, who died in a helicopter crash in December last year, wasposthumously conferred Padma Vibhushan. His daughters Kritika and Tarinireceived the award on the CDS’s behalf.
