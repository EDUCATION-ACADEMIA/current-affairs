India’s first water taxi service has been launched by Union Minister of Ports, Shipping and Waterways Sarbananda Sonowal in which place?

(A) ** Mumbai

(B) Varanasi

(C) Surat

(D) Patna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is Mumbai. Water taxi services connectingthe twin cities, Mumbai and Navi Mumbai, the first of its kind in India, was flaggedoff by Union Shipping Minister Sarbananda Sonowal (As of Feb 2022), on 17February 2022.

Concept note-2: Recently, India accomplished a significantbreakthrough in this industry by creating the first-ever water taxi service in Mumbai,Maharashtra. The project, which would cost Rs 8.37 crore and currentlycover three routes, is being sponsored equally by the union and the stategovernments.

Concept note-3: India’s first water taxi service was inaugurated inMaharashtra on Thursday which will connect the Navi Mumbai area to mainlandMumbai. Chief Minister Uddhav Thackeray inaugurated the Belapur jettywhile Union Minister for Ports, Shipping and Waterways Sarbanand Sonowalflagged off the water taxi.

Concept note-4: India’s first air taxi servicehas been launched in Chandigarh. It is the very first time when a smallaircraft is used as an air taxi in the country. The air taxi services started on14 January 2021. The first air taxi service was launched by Haryana ChiefMinister Manohar Lal Khatta in Chandigarh.

Concept note-5: Context:India’s first water taxi service was inaugurated in Maharashtra which connectsthe Navi Mumbai area to mainland Mumbai. Maharashtra’s Chief Ministerinaugurated the Belapur jetty while Union Minister for Ports, Shipping andWaterways.
