India’s first-of-its-kind project of mixing hydrogen into the natural gas system has been launched by GAIL in which state of the country?

(A) West Bengal

(B) ** Madhya Pradesh

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Q. Which company has started India’s first project of mixingHydrogen into Natural Gas System? Notes: Gas Authority of India Limited (GAIL)has commenced India’s first-of-its-kind project of mixing Hydrogen into Natural gassystem at Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-2: Gas Authority of IndiaLimited has commenced India’s first-of-it’s-kind project of mixing hydrogen intonatural gas system at Indore, Madhya Pradesh. The hydrogen blended natural gaswill be supplied to Avantika Gas Limited, one of GAIL’s Joint Venture (JV)Company with HPCL, operating in Indore.

Concept note-3: Gujarat: First phaseof Green Hydrogen plant commissioned at L&T’s Hazira complex | Cities News,TheIndian Express.

Concept note-4: State gas utility GAIL (India) Ltd on Mondaysaid it has commenced India’s first-of-its-kind project of mixing hydrogen into thenatural gas system at Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-5: NTPC Ltdcommissions India’s first green hydrogen blending project. The green hydrogenblending has been started in the piped natural gas (PNG) network of NTPC Kawastownship, Surat. The project is a joint effort of NTPC and Gujarat Gas Limited(GGL).
