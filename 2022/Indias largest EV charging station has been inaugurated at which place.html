India’s largest EV charging station has been inaugurated at which place?

(A) Mumbai

(B) Gandhinagar

(C) Hyderabad

(D) ** Gurugram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Gurugram: India’s largest electric vehicle (EV) charging stationwith a capacity of 121 charging points for 4 wheelers, has been opened here in Sector86.

Concept note-2: India’s largest EV charging station has been set up inGurugram.

Concept note-3: Q. Where is India’s largest EV charging stationestablished recently? Notes: India’s largest electric vehicle charging station has beenestablished in Gurugram (Haryana) with 72 AC slow chargers and 24 DCfast chargers (i.e) a total of 96 charging points.

Concept note-4: As ofSeptember 2022, Teld had the largest EV fast charging network in China. TheChinese firm also is one of the largest EV charging station companies in theworld. TELD has over 316,000 EV charging station units and around 20,000stations.

Concept note-5: Developed and installed by GPS Renewables withsupport from Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC) andAeroCare Clean Energy, the EV charging station located at Haji Ali in Mumbai isIndia’s first to be powered by biogasIt works in tandem with a biomethane plantpositioned alongside it.
