India’s retail inflation, as measured by the consumer price index (CPI), rose to ____ % in March 2022?

(A) 5.79%

(B) 5.99%

(C) 5.51%

(D) ** 6.95%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: CPI Inflation Rate March, IIP Growth Rate February2022: The retail inflation, measured by the Consumer Price Index (CPI),rose to 6.95 per cent in March. Separately, the factory output, measuredin terms of Index of Industrial Production (IIP), grew by 1.7 per cent inFebruary.

Concept note-2: India’s retail inflation jumped to a 17-month high of6.95 per cent in March 2022, according to data released by the Ministry of Statisticsand Programme Implementation.

Concept note-3: The country’s retail inflation,which is measured by the consumer price index (CPI), dropped to a 1-year low of5.72% in Dec. 2022 from 5.88% in Nov. 2022.

Concept note-4: The All-IndiaCPI-IW for November, 2022 remained stationary at 132.5 (one hundred thirtytwo point five).

Concept note-5: By current projections, inflation isforecast to average 6.7 per cent in 2022-23 and 5.2 per cent in the first half of2023-24.
