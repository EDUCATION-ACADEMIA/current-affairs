India’s GDP is likely to grow by how much percent in the third quarter of FY 2022, as per SBI?

(A) 6.8

(B) 7.5

(C) ** 5.8

(D) 9.2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The country’s GDP is likely to grow at 5.8 percent in the third quarter of fiscal 2022, according to an SBI’s research report- Ecowrap.

Concept note-2: -Economic growth waned in the third quarter, with GDP expanding 6.3% on an annual basis (Q2: +13.5% year on year), marginally beating market expectations.

Concept note-3: -Real gross domestic product (GDP) increased at an annual rate of 2.9 percent in the fourth quarter of 2022, after increasing 3.2 percent in the third quarter.

Concept note-4: -With nominal GDP of 10.6%-11%, India’s gross tax collection growth rate is likely to be around 8% in 2023/24, compared with 14.5% in the current year, due to base effect, said Gaura Sengupta, an economist at IDFC First Bank.

Concept note-5: -India’s output will expand 7% in fiscal year 2022-2023 and 6% in next fiscal year,” S&P Global Ratings Asia-Pacific chief economist Louis Kuijs said. Meanwhile, SBI Research expects FY23 economic growth at 6.8% as against the RBI’s projection of 7%. In addition, it estimated Q2FY23 growth at 5.8%.
