India’s Rupay debit card is to be launched in which country?

(A) Kuwait

(B) UAE

(C) ** Oman

(D) Syria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) and Central Financial institution Of Oman signed a historic MoU to launch the Rupay debit card in Oman, paving the best way for a brand new period of monetary connectivity.

Concept note-2: -RuPay (portmanteau of Rupee and Payment) is an Indian multinational financial services and payment service system, conceived and launched by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) on 26 March 2012.

Concept note-3: -RuPay card scheme was introduced in 2012 as the RBI’s vision to have a domestic, open and multilateral system of payments.

Concept note-4: -NIPL (NPCI International Payments Limited), which deploys the RuPay and UPI payment systems outside India, is forging partnerships with countries like Singapore, UAE, Malaysia, France, BENELUX markets – Belgium, the Netherlands and Luxembourg – and Switzerland, among others, to use India’s payment systems.
