India’s first commercial space situational awareness observatory is to be established in which state/UT?

(A) Arunachal Pradesh

(B) Sikkim

(C) ** Uttarakhand

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ot Staff. Ensuring that nothing goes out of sight, Digantara, a space industry start-up is launching India’s first-ever commercial Space Situational Awareness (SSA) observatory. It is put up in the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand.

Concept note-2: -Astrosat is India’s first dedicated multi-wavelength space telescope.

Concept note-3: -India’s first private space mission christened Prarambh-by Skyroot Aerospace based in Hyderabad-is set for a 11:30 am launch today from Indian Space Research Organization’s (Isro’s) Satish Dhawan Launch Centre at Sriharikota. Mint explains the significance of the mission.

Concept note-4: -Digantara, a start-up in the space industry, will build India’s first commercial Space Situational Awareness (SSA) observatory in the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand to monitor objects as small as 10 cm in size orbiting the planet.

Concept note-5: -India’s first commercial space situational awareness observatory, to track objects as small as 10 cm in size orbiting the earth, will be set up in the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand by Digantara, a space sector start-up.
