India’s first unique ‘Rock’ museum has been inaugurated in which city?

(A) ** Hyderabad

(B) Lucknow

(C) Bengaluru

(D) Pune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. India’s first Open Rock Museum is located in which city? Notes: Union Minister of State for Science and Technology Dr Jitendra Singh inaugurated India’s first Open Rock Museum in Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Hyderabad. Union Minister of State Science and Technology Dr. Jitendra Singh inaugurated India’s first unique Rock Museum in Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: -Union Minister of State (MoS) Science & Technology (Independent Charge); MoS (Independent Charge) Earth Sciences; MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances, Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh inaugurated the country’s first open rock museum in Hyderabad, on Thursday.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Piyush Goyal. In November 2021, Union Minister Piyush Goyal virtually launched India’s first digital food museum in Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu. Thanjavur is the birthplace of the FCI that’s why Thanjavur was chosen as the site for India’s first digital food Museum.

Concept note-5: -Hyderabad, Jan 6: Union Minister of State for Science & Technology and Earth Sciences, Jitendra Singh, inaugurated India’s first Open Rock Museum, here on Thursday.
