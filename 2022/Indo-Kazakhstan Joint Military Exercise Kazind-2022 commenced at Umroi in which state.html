Indo-Kazakhstan Joint Military Exercise ‘Kazind-2022’ commenced at Umroi in which state?

(A) Mizoram

(B) ** Meghalaya

(C) Tripura

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: KazInd 2022: India and Kazakhstan carry out joint militarydrill in Meghalaya. To strengthen positive military relations between India andKazakhstan, the nations conducted their 6th edition of Army Joint ExerciseKazInd 2022 in Meghalaya’s Umroi.

Concept note-2: Exercise KazInd2022 is the 6th edition of the Indo-Kazakhstan Joint exercise and is aimedat building trust and understanding of each other in the field of nationalsecurity.

Concept note-3: Indo-Kazakhstan Joint Military Exercise “Kazind –2022” to Commence at Umroi (Meghalaya) The 6th Edition of Indo - Kazakhstanjoint training exercise “KAZIND-22” is scheduled to be conducted at Umroi(Meghalaya) from 15 to 28 December 2022.

Concept note-4: The exercise waslater upgraded to a company-level exercise, renamed Ex Kazind in 2018 andinstituted in 2016. Various missions, ranging from joint planning, joint tactical drills,basics of special arms skills, and HADR to raiding a hostile target, were conductedduring the training.

Concept note-5: Surya Kiran is a bilateral Joint MilitaryExercise between India and Nepal. Indian Army and Nepal Army participate in theSurya Kiran exercise. The 15th edition of Indo–Nepal Joint Military TrainingExercise Surya Kiran commenced on September 20, 2021, at Pithoragarh inUttarakhand.
