International Day for Tolerance is observed every year on which date?

(A) November 20

(B) November 18

(C) ** November 16

(D) November 17

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: International Day for Tolerance: 16November.

Concept note-2: International Day for Tolerance 2022: TheInternational Day for Tolerance is observed annually on November 16 to promoteawareness of the value of tolerance in society. The purpose of the day is toraise awareness of the negative effects of an intolerant culture and celebratereceptivity.

Concept note-3: On November 16, the International Day ofTolerance is observed to raise awareness about the importance of tolerance byencouraging mutual understanding among cultures and peoples. By resolution 51/95,the UN General Assembly invited UN Member States to observe the InternationalDay of Tolerance on November 16, 1966.

Concept note-4: International Day forTolerance: HistoryThe UN General Assembly in 1996 by resolution 51/95 invited theUN Member States to observe the International Day for Tolerance on 16 November.Various activities were organised that are directed towards both educationalestablishments and the public on a larger scale.
