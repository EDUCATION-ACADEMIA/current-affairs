International Day of Family Remittances is being celebrated on which date?

(A) June 17

(B) June 18

(C) ** June 16

(D) June 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Day of Family Remittances (IDFR) was adopted by the United Nations General Assembly and is observed on 16 June.

Concept note-2: -The International Day of Family Remittances (IDFR) was adopted by the United Nations General Assembly and is observed on 16 June. The IDFR recognizes more than 200 million migrant workers, women and men, who send money home to over 800 million family members.

Concept note-4: -In its resolution 44/82 of 9 December 1989, The General Assembly proclaimed The International Year of the Family. In 1993, the General Assembly decided in a resolution (A/RES/47/237) that 15 May of every year should be observed as The International Day of Families.
