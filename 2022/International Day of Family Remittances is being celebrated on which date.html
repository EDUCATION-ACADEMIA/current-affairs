International Day of Family Remittances is being celebrated on which date?

(A) June 17

(B) June 18

(C) ** June 16

(D) June 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The International Day of Family Remittances (IDFR) wasadopted by the United Nations General Assembly and is observed on 16June.

Concept note-2: The International Day of Family Remittances (IDFR)was adopted by the United Nations General Assembly and is observed on 16 June.The IDFR recognizes more than 200 million migrant workers, women and men, whosend money home to over 800 million family members.
