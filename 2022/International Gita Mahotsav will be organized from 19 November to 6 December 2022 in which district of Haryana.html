International Gita Mahotsav will be organized from 19 November to 6 December 2022 in which district of Haryana?

(A) ** Kurukshetra

(B) Faridabad

(C) Ambala

(D) Gurgaon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: International Gita Mahotsav will be held from November 19 toDecember 6, 2022 in Kurukshetra, Haryana. President Draupadi Murmu will formallyinaugurate the main programs at the Geeta Yagya at Brahma Sarovar inKurukshetra on November 29.

Concept note-2: International Gita Mahotsav,2022 | District Faridabad, Government of Haryana | India.

Concept note-3: Thefestival is very sacred to Hindus and is celebrated with immense religious fervor anddedication. The festival is celebrated mainly in Kurukshetra, Haryana. The venue ofthe festival adds to the sacredness of the event.

Concept note-4: During theKurukshetra Festival in Haryana the pilgrims all gather to take holy dip in theBrahma Sarovar and the Sannehit Sarovar the waters of which are consideredsacred. The Kurukshetra Festival in Haryana takes place in the month ofNovember/December .

Concept note-5: : President Droupadi Murmu will attendthe International Gita Mahotsav-2022 in Kurukshetra on Tuesday. The President willformally inaugurate the main programmes to be held during the Gita Mahotsav atBrahma Sarovar .
