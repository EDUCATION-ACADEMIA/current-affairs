International Day of Action for Women’s Health 2022 is being celebrated on which date?

(A) May 25

(B) May 27

(C) May 29

(D) ** May 28

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On May 28, the International Day of Action for Women’s Health, activists around the world will take action, mobilize, and highlight demands towards the fulfillment of women’s right to health.

Concept note-2: -International Women’s Health Day 2022 Theme This year, the International Day of Action for Women’s Health 2022 theme was “Resist and Persist.” It aims to encourage women around the world to not give up and fight for their health rights.

Concept note-3: -This day has been celebrated year after year for women, girls, advocates and allies to act and stand up for the sexual and reproductive rights of females which are an indivisible and inalienable part of our human rights.

Concept note-4: -May 28 – International Day of Action for Women’s Health.

Concept note-5: -It began in New York City on March 8, 1857, when female textile workers marched in protest of unfair working conditions and unequal rights for women. It was one of the first organized strikes by working women, during which they called for a shorter work day and decent wages.
