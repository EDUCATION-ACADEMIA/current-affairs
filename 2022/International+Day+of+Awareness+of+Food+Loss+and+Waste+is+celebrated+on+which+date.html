International Day of Awareness of Food Loss and Waste is celebrated on which date?

(A) September 22

(B) ** September 29

(C) September 24

(D) September 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 29 September 2022, the International Day of Awareness of Food Loss and Waste is observed globally. On 29 September 2022, the International Day of Awareness of Food Loss and Waste is observed globally.

Concept note-2: -Event: International Day of Awareness of Food Loss and Waste 2022 | SDG Knowledge Hub | IISD. The UN General Assembly, on 19 December 2019, designated 29 September as the International Day of Awareness of Food Loss and Waste (A/RES/74/209).

Concept note-3: -An estimated 14 per cent of the world’s food is lost between harvest and retail, and an estimated 17 per cent is wasted in retail and at the consumption level. This comes at a time when 811 million people go hungry.

Concept note-4: -China and India produce more household food waste than any other country worldwide at an estimated 92 million and 69 million metric tons every year, respectively.
