International Friendship Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** July 30

(B) July 31

(C) July 28

(D) July 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Friendship day is dedicated to the . friends with whom you share the precious moments of life and this day is being celebrated on the first Sunday in the month of August i.e., today, 7th August, 2022. Friendship day is celebrated across the world by crores of people those who share special bond with their friends.

Concept note-2: -Though in India, Friendship Day is celebrated on the first Sunday of August, International Friendship Day is celebrated across the world on July 30.

Concept note-3: -On the first Sunday in August, National Friendship Day encourages people across the country and world to connect with friends.

Concept note-4: -Friendship Day 2022: The day dedicated to friends and all the beautiful friendships all over the world is slated to be celebrated on August 7.
