International Plastic Bag free day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) July 02

(B) ** July 03

(C) July 01

(D) July 04

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Plastic Bag Free Day is held on July 3 around the world to spread awareness that a plastic bag free world is possible and that sound environmental alternatives to single use plastic bags are available. 2022 is the 13th edition of the celebrations.

Concept note-2: -Reduce, reuse, and recycle today!

Concept note-3: -It’s International Plastic Bag Free Day, National Stay Out of the Sun Day, National Compliment Your Mirror Day, National Chocolate Wafer Day, Disobedience Day… and much more!
