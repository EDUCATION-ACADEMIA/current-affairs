Intersolar Europe conference will take place in which country?

(A) Brazil

(B) ** Germany

(C) Russia

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Intersolar Europe is the world’s leading exhibition for thesolar industry and takes place annually at the Messe München exhibitioncenter. Intersolar 2022 will take place in Munich, Germany from 11-13May.

Concept note-2: Munich, GermanyIntersolar Europe takes place as part ofThe smarter E Europe parallel to the three energy exhibitions ees Europe,Power2Drive Europe and EM-Power Europe at Messe München from May 11–13,2022.

Concept note-3: China- 340 GWChina’s solar prowess is staggering. Witha whopping 340 GW, the country is the largest producer of solar energy in theworld. In the first six months of 2022, the nation has deployed more than30.88 GW of Solar PV systems.

Concept note-4: Intersolar India 2022is going to be held from 07- 09 Dec 2022 at Helipad Ground ExhibitionCentre Gandhinagar, India.

Concept note-5: Around 72,000 visitors fromaround 150 nations visited Intersolar Europe at the New Munich Trade FairCenter.
