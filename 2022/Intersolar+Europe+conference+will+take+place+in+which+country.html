Intersolar Europe conference will take place in which country?

(A) Brazil

(B) ** Germany

(C) Russia

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Intersolar 2022 will take place in Munich, Germany from 11-13 May. The event’s exhibition and conference both focus on the areas of photovoltaics, solar thermal technologies, solar plants, as well as grid infrastructure and solutions for the integration of renewable energy.

Concept note-2: -Munich, Germany Intersolar Europe takes place as part of The smarter E Europe parallel to the three energy exhibitions ees Europe, Power2Drive Europe and EM-Power Europe at Messe München from May 11–13, 2022.

Concept note-3: -Intersolar India 2022 is going to be held from 07- 09 Dec 2022 at Helipad Ground Exhibition Centre Gandhinagar, India.

Concept note-4: -Around 72,000 visitors from around 150 nations visited Intersolar Europe at the New Munich Trade Fair Center.

Concept note-5: -Iberdrola starts up ‘Francisco Pizarro’, the largest photovoltaic plant in Europe. The Francisco Pizarro photovoltaic plant will supply more than 334,000 homes and avoid the emission of 150,000 tons of CO2 per year.
