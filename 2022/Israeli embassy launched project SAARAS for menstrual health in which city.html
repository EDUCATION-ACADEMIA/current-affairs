Israeli embassy launched project SAARAS for menstrual health in which city?

(A) New Delhi

(B) Hyderabad

(C) ** Ghaziabad

(D) Patna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Israeli embassy launches project ‘Saaras’ for menstrual healthin Ghaziabad.
