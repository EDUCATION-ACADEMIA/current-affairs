(A) Income Tax

(B) Crypto currency

(C) ** Consumer Awareness

(D) Climate Change

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. ‘Jagriti’, which was seen in the news recently, is a mascot related to which field? Notes: The Department of Consumer Affairs has launched “Jagriti”, a mascot for empowering consumers and generating awareness of their rights.

Concept note-2: -The Department of Consumer Affairs (DoCA) has launched ‘Jagriti’ mascot. It is a mascot for empowering consumers and generating awareness of their rights. It will be projected as an empowered consumer who is spreading awareness about consumer rights & addressing solutions to the problems faced by the consumers.

Concept note-3: -Aiming to reinforce a young, empowered, and knowledgeable consumer as a top-of-mind consumer rights awareness recall brand, DoCA is integrating the Jagriti Mascot to its consumer awareness campaign. In all of its media campaigns, the Jagriti mascot and slogan Jago Grahak Jago must be displayed.

Concept note-4: -The Department of Consumer Affairs (DoCA) has launched “Jagriti”, a mascot for empowering consumers and generating awareness of their rights.

Concept note-5: -It came into effect on December 24, 1986.
