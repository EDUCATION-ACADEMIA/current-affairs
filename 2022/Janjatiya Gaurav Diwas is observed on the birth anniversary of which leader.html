‘Janjatiya Gaurav Diwas’ is observed on the birth anniversary of which leader?

(A) Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

(B) ** Birsa Munda

(C) B R Ambedkar

(D) Ayyan Kaali

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: President Droupadi Murmu said on Tuesday, joining in thecelebration of the second Janjatiya Gaurav Divas, which marks the birth anniversaryof tribal icon and freedom fighter Birsa Munda.

Concept note-2: 15th Novemberalso marks the birth anniversary of Birsa Munda who is revered as God by tribalcommunities across India.

Concept note-3: Few leaders among them were TilkaMajhi, Tikendrajit Singh, Veer Surendra Sai, Telanga Kharia, Veer Narayan Singh,Sidhu, Kanu Murmu, Rupchand Konwar and Laxman Naik. Born on 15th November1875, in Ulihatu village of a present-day Jharkhand, Birsa spent his childhood inabject poverty in a tribal Munda family.

Concept note-4: Shri ArjunMunda also stated that Government of India, under the leadership of ShriPrime Minister has declared 15th November as Janjatiya Gaurav Divas tocommemorate the contributions of tribal communities in the nation’s history andculture.
