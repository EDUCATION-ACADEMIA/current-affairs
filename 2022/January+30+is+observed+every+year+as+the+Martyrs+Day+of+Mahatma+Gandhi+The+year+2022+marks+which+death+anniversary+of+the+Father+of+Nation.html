[SOURCES]

January 30 is observed every year as the Martyrs’ Day of Mahatma Gandhi. The year 2022 marks which death anniversary of the Father of Nation?

(A) 75

(B) ** 74

(C) 79

(D) 78

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -January 30 is one of the dates on which Shaheed Diwas is observed every year at the national level. The date was chosen as it marks the assassination of Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi in 1948, by Nathuram Godse .

Concept note-2: -January 30th is also celebrated as Martyrs’ Day to honour Mahatma Gandhi’s contribution towards the freedom of the country. Prime Minister Narendra Modi paid tribute to the ‘Father of the Nation’ and said that his sacrifices will never be forgotten.

Concept note-3: -Martyrs’ Day is commemorated on January 30 to mark the death anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi. Martyrs’ Day or Shaheed Divas is also commemorated on March 23 to remember the sacrifice of three extraordinary revolutionaries of India – Bhagat Singh, Shivaram Rajguru, Sukhdev Thapar.

Concept note-4: -Martyrs’ Day, also known as Shaheed Diwas, is observed in India on January 30 to commemorate the assassination of Mahatma Gandhi in 1948. On this day, the entire country pays tribute to all the heroes of the Indian freedom struggle, who sacrificed their lives for the nation.

Concept note-5: -The Theme of Shaheed Diwas 2022 is “Tribute to Revolutionaries.” This day is celebrated in honor of all the freedom fighters who fought for the independence of the nation.
