(B) Iraq

Concept note-2: Several countries haveannounced the development of a sixth-generation aircraft program, including theUnited States, Russia and China, while Japan, Italy, the United Kingdom, France,Germany, Spain, and Sweden have joined together in collaborative multinationalprograms in an effort to spread development costs.

Concept note-3: Thethree countries announced in December plans to work together on a nextgeneration warplane in a bid to bring a new fighter into service by 2035.
