Jitendra Singh inaugurated North India’s first Industrial Biotech Park constructed in which of the following states?

(A) Himachal Pradesh

(B) Delhi

(C) Uttarakhand

(D) ** Jammu & Kashmir

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UPSC Mains. Q. Jitendra Singh inaugurated which of thefollowing states’ first Industrial Biotech Park? Notes: Jammu and Kashmir (J&K)Lieutenant Governor, Manoj Sinha and Union minister Jitendra Singh inauguratedthe north India’s first Industrial Biotech Park constructed at Ghatti near Kathua,Jammu & Kashmir.

Concept note-2: Jitendra Singh today said that Kathua is,because of its resource richness with diversity as well as its geographical location,destined to be North India’s StartUp destination. The Union Minister said this afterinaugurating North India’s first Industrial Biotech Park Ghatti, Kathua, Jammualong with Shri.

Concept note-3: NEW DELHI: Union science and technologyminister Jitendra Singh inaugurated north India’s first industrial biotech park atKathua in Jammu on Saturday.

Concept note-4: Jammu and Kashmir (J&K)Lieutenant Governor, Manoj Sinha and Union minister Jitendra Singh on Saturdayinaugurated the north India’s first Industrial Biotech Park constructed atGhatti near Kathua.

Concept note-5: Biotech Park at Lucknow in UttarPradesh is the first technology incubator. This has been set up jointly as apartnership between Central and State governments i.e. the Department ofBiotechnology, Ministry of Science and Technology and Government of UttarPradesh.
