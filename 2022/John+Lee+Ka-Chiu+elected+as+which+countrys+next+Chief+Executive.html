John Lee Ka-Chiu elected as which country’s next Chief Executive?

(A) South Korea

(B) Singapore

(C) Mauritius

(D) ** Hong Kong

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -John Lee Ka-chiu GBM SBS PDSM PMSM (Chinese: ; born 7 December 1957) is a Hong Kong politician and former police officer who is the 5th and current Chief Executive of Hong Kong.

Concept note-2: -John Lee, the newly appointed sixth-term chief executive of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region (HKSAR), has pledged to start a new chapter for Hong Kong by building consensus and delivering results.

Concept note-3: -Tung Chee-hwa was elected in 1996 by 400-member Selection Committee as the first Chief Executive of Hong Kong.

Concept note-4: -Mr Darryl Chan was appointed as Deputy Chief Executive from 1 November 2022, and is responsible for overseeing the work of External, Monetary Management and Research departments.
