Joint military exercise Lamitye 2022 has been started between India and which country?

(A) Maldives

(B) Singapore

(C) ** Seychelles

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Q. Lamitiye 2022’ is the Joint Military Exercise betweenIndia and which country? Notes: Lamitiye 2022 is the ninth edition of theJoint Military Exercise between the Indian Army and Seychelles DefenceForces (SDF).

Concept note-2: Detailed Solution. The correct answer isSeychelles. The 9th Joint Military Exercise LAMITIYE-2022 between the IndianArmy and Seychelles Defence Forces (SDF) is being conducted from 22-31March 2022. It is being conducted at Seychelles Defence Academy (SDA),Seychelles.

Concept note-3: India-Seychelles Joint Military ExerciseThe Lamityemilitary alliance was organised a couple of times between the Indian army andSeychelles army with the key motive of improving military collaboration andinteroperability among the two nations. “Lamitye” is commonly definedas friendship.

Concept note-4: Ex Dharma Guardian – 2022 is a jointmilitary exercise between India and Japan, is being conducted at the ForeignTraining Node in Belagavi.

Concept note-5: The correct answer isThailand.
