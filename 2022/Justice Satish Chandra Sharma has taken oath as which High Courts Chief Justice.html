Justice Satish Chandra Sharma has taken oath as which High Court’s Chief Justice?

(A) Allahabad High Court

(B) Bombay High Court

(C) ** Delhi High Court

(D) Madras High Court

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Justice Satish Chandra Sharma on Tuesday took oath as theChief Justice of the Delhi High Court. Lieutenant Governor Vinai Kumar Saxenaadministered the oath to the 60-year-old Justice Sharma at a ceremony in the RajNiwas, the LG’s secretariat.

Concept note-2: A judge of high court appointed bythe President of India with the consultation of the chief justice of India and governorof the state. The oath to the Judge of a high court is administered by thegovernor of that concerned state.

Concept note-3: New Delhi: JusticeSatish Chandra Sharma took oath as the chief justice of Delhi High Court onTuesday.

Concept note-4: HON’BLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE SHRI HEMANTGUPTA,Born on 17, Oct. 1957.

Concept note-5: Satish Chandra Sharma (born30 November 1961) is an Indian judge. Presently, he is Chief Justice of Delhi HighCourt. He is former Chief Justice of Telangana High Court. He has served as ActingChief Justice of Karnataka High Court and Judge of Karnataka High Court andMadhya Pradesh High Court.
