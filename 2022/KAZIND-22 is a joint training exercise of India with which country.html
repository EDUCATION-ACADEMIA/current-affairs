‘KAZIND-22’ is a joint training exercise of India with which country?

(A) Kenya

(B) ** Kazakhstan

(C) Israel

(D) Jordan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Indo-Kazakhstan Joint Military Exercise “Kazind – 2022” toCommence at Umroi (Meghalaya) The 6th Edition of Indo - Kazakhstanjoint training exercise “KAZIND-22” is scheduled to be conducted at Umroi(Meghalaya) from 15 to 28 December 2022.

Concept note-2: Exercise KazInd2022 is the 6th edition of the Indo-Kazakhstan Joint exercise and is aimedat building trust and understanding of each other in the field of nationalsecurity.

Concept note-3: KazInd 2022: India and Kazakhstan carry out jointmilitary drill in Meghalaya. To strengthen positive military relations betweenIndia and Kazakhstan, the nations conducted their 6th edition of ArmyJoint Exercise KazInd 2022 in Meghalaya’s Umroi.

Concept note-4: Theexercise ‘Veer Guardian-2023’ involving the Indian Air Force (IAF) andJapan Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) will be held at Japan’s Hyakuri airbase.

Concept note-5: What is the Garuda exercise? Garuda is a joint exercisebetween India and France Air Force that will provide a platform for both countries toenhance operational capability and interoperability, while also sharing bestpractices.
