CURRENT AFFAIRS-2022
[SOURCES]

K.N. Raghavan has been appointed as the Chairman of which international body?

(A) International Automotive Task Force

(B) ** International Rubber Study Group

(C) International Dairy Federation

(D) International Steel Group

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In a significant achievement, K.N. Raghavan, Executive Director, Indian Rubber Board, has been elected the new Chairman of the International Rubber Study Group (IRSG), an intergovernmental organisation of natural and synthetic rubber producing and consuming countries.

Concept note-2: -The new Chairman of the IRSG, Dr. K. N. Raghavan will preside over the meeting of the Heads of Delegation scheduled on March 31, 2022 in Singapore.

Concept note-3: -Singapore – The IRSG Secretariat announced the election of Dr K.N. Raghavan, Executive Director, Rubber Board of India as the new Chairperson of the IRSG Heads of Delegation. Dr Raghavan has been elected unanimously by the IRSG Member Governments’ representatives and will stay in the position for two terms (2022-2024).

Concept note-4: -The IRSG Secretariat is based in Singapore.
