KPAC Lalitha, who has passed away, was a veteran actress of which Indian film industry?

(A) ** Malayalam

(B) Bengali

(C) Marathi

(D) Kannada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Veteran actress Maheshwari Amma, better known byher stage name of KPAC Lalitha who starred in over 500 films primarilyin Malayalam cinema, passed away in Kochi late Tuesday night. She was74.

Concept note-2: Maheshwari Amma, better known by her stagename K. P. A. C. Lalitha, was an Indian film and stage actress who workedprimarily in Malyalam. She started her acting career with K. P. A. C., atheatre collective in Kayamkulam, Kerala.

Concept note-3: PopularMalayalam actress and two time National Award winner KPAC Lalitha passedaway on February 22, 2022 at the age of 74.

Concept note-4: KOCHI:Noted Malayalam actor KPAC Lalitha passed away late on Tuesday at herresidence in Tripunithura near here, film industry sources said. She was 74.Lalitha is survived by her actor-director son Sidharth Bharathan and daughterSreekutty. She was married to the legendary Malayalam film director lateBharathan.
