Kalpana Chawla centre for research in space science & technology has been inaugurated in which university?

(A) Allahabad university

(B) Delhi university

(C) ** Chandigarh university

(D) Madras university

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh inaugurated KalpanaChawla Centre for Research in Space Science & Technology (KCCRSST) atChandigarh University on January 03, 2022. He also launched a ScholarshipScheme, worth Rs 10 crore, for the wards of Defence Personnel of the threeServices.

Concept note-2: The Government informed that Kalpana ChawlaCentre for Research in Space Science and Technology (KCCRSST) was inauguratedat Chandigarh University in January 2022.

Concept note-3: KalpanaChawla Government Medical College (KCGMC) is a Medical college locatedin Karnal, Haryana, India named after Chawla. Kalpana was born inKarnal.

Concept note-4: Kalpana always dreams of travelling to space. Thus,merely graduating from an engineering college will not do for her. She goes tothe United States to get a master’s degree even though her father did notagree.

Concept note-5: Kalpana Chawla completed her earlier schooling atTagore Baal Niketan Sr. Sec. School, Karnal. She completed Bachelor of Engineeringdegree in Aeronautical Engineering at Punjab Engineering College at Chandigarh in1982.
