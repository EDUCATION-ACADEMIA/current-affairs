Kameng Hydro Power Station has been inaugurated in which state?

(A) Assam

(B) Manipur

(C) ** Arunachal Pradesh

(D) Meghalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -North Eastern Electric Power Corporation Limited’s (NEEPCO)600 MW Kameng hydro power station located in Arunachal Pradesh has been inaugurated. The project has been developed at a cost of more than Rs 82 billion.

Concept note-2: -Arunachal Pradesh Turns Power Surplus As PM Modi Inaugurated 600 MW Kameng Hydro Power Project. Border state of Arunachal Pradesh has turned power surplus after Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated 600 MW Kameng Hydro Power Station on Saturday (19 November).

Concept note-3: -PALLIVASAL HYDRO ELECTRIC PROJECT Pallivasal is the first hydro electric power station in Kerala.

Concept note-4: -Kameng Hydro Power Station is a run-of-the river scheme to harness the hydro power of Bichom and Tenga Rivers (both tributaries of the River Kameng) with the Power House located at Kimi, West Kameng District, Arunachal Pradesh. The Power Station has two Dam, one at Bichom & the other at Tenga.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Khopoli. Khopoli Hydroelectric Project is in the Raigad district, Maharashtra. The project was commissioned in 1915.
