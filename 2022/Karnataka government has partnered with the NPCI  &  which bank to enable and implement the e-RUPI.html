Karnataka government has partnered with the NPCI & which bank to enable and implement the ‘e-RUPI’?

(A) Bank of Baroda

(B) ** State Bank of India

(C) IndusInd Bank

(D) Bandhan Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Karnataka govt partnered with NPCI & SBI to implement‘e-RUPI’

Concept note-2: National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) inassociation with Department of Financial Services (DFS), National Health Authority(NHA), Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (MoHFW), and partner banks, haslaunched an innovative digital solution – ‘e-RUPI’.

Concept note-3: e-RUPI is adigital voucher that is received by the beneficiary on his mobile device in the form ofan SMS or QR code. The voucher is in the form of a pre-paid voucher which canbe redeemed at the centers that accept it.

Concept note-4: NationalPayments Corporation of India (NPCI) - Enabling digital payments inIndia.
