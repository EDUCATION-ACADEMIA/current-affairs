Karolina Bielawska has won Miss World 2021 beauty pageant. She belongs to which country?

(A) USA

(B) UAE

(C) ** Poland

(D) Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Poland’s Karolina Bielawska has been crowned Miss World for the year 2021, besting contestants from 96 other countries, including Miss India Manasa Varanasi , who stood 11th at the international beauty pageant.

Concept note-2: -At the end of the event, Toni-Ann Singh of Jamaica crowned Karolina Bielawska of Poland as Miss World 2021. It is the second victory of Poland in the history of the pageant after their victory in 1989.

Concept note-3: -First Miss World from India The First Indian to win the Miss World crown was Reita Faria in 1966.

Concept note-4: -Karolina Bielawska (born 11 April 1999) is a Polish model, television presenter, and beauty queen who was crowned Miss World 2022. She became the second titleholder from Poland, the first one being Aneta Kręglicka who won the pageant in 1989.
