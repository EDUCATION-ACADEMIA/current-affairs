Kwar Hydro-electric project, which has been seen in the news, is located in which state/UT?

(A) Sikkim

(B) ** Jammu and Kashmir

(C) Gujarat

(D) Arunachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kwar Hydro-electric project, which was seen in the news, is located in which state/UT? Notes: The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs approved the investment of Rs 4,526.12 crore for the 540 MW Kwar Hydro-electric project. It is located on Chenab River in Jammu & Kashmir’s Kishtwar district.

Concept note-2: -The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs chaired by the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today has approved the investment of Rs. 4526.12 crore for 540 Megawatt (MW) Kwar Hydro Electric Project located on river Chenab, in Kishtwar district of Union Territory of Jammy and Kashmir.

Concept note-3: -Kiru Hydro Electric Project (624 MW) is proposed on River Chenab, located in Kishtwar district of the Jammu & Kashmir. The project is envisaged as a Run of River Scheme. Government sanctioned accorded on 07.03. 2019.

Concept note-4: -The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA) has approved the 540-megawatt Kwar hydroelectric project on the Chenab in Kishtwar district of Jammu and Kashmir.

Concept note-5: -Pakal Dul HE Project (1000MW) is under active construction after river diversion was carried out. The project will generate 3230 MWs annually and expected to be completed by July 2025, the spokesman said.
