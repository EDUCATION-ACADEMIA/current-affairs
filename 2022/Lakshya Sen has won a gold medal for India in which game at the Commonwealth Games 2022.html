Lakshya Sen has won a gold medal for India in which game at the Commonwealth Games 2022?

(A) Hockey

(B) ** Badminton

(C) Tennis

(D) Squash

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India’s Lakshya Sen won the men’s singles badminton goldmedal at the Commonwealth Games 2022 in Birmingham, UK on Monday. LakshyaSen, in his Commonwealth Games debut, came from a game down to beatMalaysia’s Ng Tze Yong 19-21, 21-9, 21-16 in the final at the National ExhibitionCentre.

Concept note-2: India’s young ace shuttler Lakshya Sen, onMonday, won gold medal in the final of men’s single at the BirminghamCommonwealth Games.

Concept note-3: He won the bronze medal at the2021 World Championships and was runner-up at the 2022 All EnglandOpen. Sen was also a part of the Indian team which won the Thomas Cup2022.
