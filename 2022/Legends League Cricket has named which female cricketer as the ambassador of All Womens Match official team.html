Legends League Cricket has named which female cricketer as the ambassador of All Women’s Match official team?

(A) Harmanpreet Kaur

(B) remembering

(C) Mithali Raj

(D) ** Jhulan Goswami

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is Jhulan Goswami. To promote women’sempowerment, Legends League Cricket (LLC) has appointed cricketer JhulanGoswami as an ambassador. LLC also announced an ‘All Women MatchOfficial Team’ for the League that includes ICC empanelled female umpiresand match referees from all across the world.

Concept note-2: JhulanGoswami: LLC named JG as Ambassador of All Women Match OfficialTeam.

Concept note-3: Who is Shubhda Bhosle Gaikwad, the Indian femaleumpire officiating in Legends League Cricket? Along with Shubhda, Lauren Agenbagfrom South Africa, Humaira Farah from Pakistan and Renee Montgomery fromHongkong will officiate the matches.

Concept note-4: India, undisputedly thesport’s powerhouse, had been slow to develop women’s cricket - well behindtrendsetters Australia and England - before making steps in 2018 by launching athree-team Women’s T20 Challenge staged alongside the men’s money-spinningIPL.
