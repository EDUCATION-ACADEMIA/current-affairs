Linthoi Chanambam, which was in news, is associated with which sport?

(A) Weightlifting

(B) Wrestling

(C) Boxing

(D) ** Judo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Linthoi Chanambam may not be a household figure inIndia yet but the 16-year-old Manipuri from the outskirts of Imphal etchedher name in the history books. She became the first Indian to win the goldmedal at the World Cadet Judo Championship in Sarajevo, Bosnia in lateAugust.

Concept note-2: A young prodigy from Manipur who possesses greatstrength and power, Linthoi has already shown what she can do, with a goldmedal at the Sub-junior National Championship.

Concept note-3: Linthoiis just 16 and she has already scripted history - she beat Brazil’s BiancaReis to clinch the gold medal (57 kg category) at the World Cadet JudoChampionship in Sarajevo, Bosnia, becoming the first from the country to win amedal in the tournament across any age group.

Concept note-4: Imphal:Manipur’s Linthoi Chanambam, who became the first Indian to clinch gold atthe World Judo championship, received a rousing homecoming welcomedupon her arrival at Imphal airport by family, friends and well-wishers onTuesday.

Concept note-5: Meet Linthoi Chanambam: India’s first Judo WorldChampion.
