MUSE and HelioSwarm, which were seen in the news, are associated with which space agency?

(A) ** NASA

(B) ISRO

(C) JAXA

(D) ESA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UPSC Mains. Q. MUSE and HelioSwarm, which were seen inthe news recently, are associated with which space agency? Notes: NASA hasannounced two new science missions namely Multi-slit Solar Explorer (MUSE) andHelioSwarm.

Concept note-2: The primary goal of the MUSE mission is toinvestigate the causes of coronal heating and instability, such as flares andcoronal mass ejections, and gain insight into the basic plasma properties of thecorona.

Concept note-3: NASA has selected two science missions – the Multi-slitSolar Explorer (MUSE) and HelioSwarm – to help improve our understanding ofthe dynamics of the Sun, the Sun-Earth connection, and the constantlychanging space environment.

Concept note-4: Detailed Solution. Thecorrect answer is SpaceX.

Concept note-5: Notes: The United States’space agency National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) hasannounced that it has given approval to two Sun exploration heliophysicsmissions.
