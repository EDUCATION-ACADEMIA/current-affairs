Maha Metro created a world record for building Asia’s longest multilayer viaduct with whom?

(A) NHIDCL

(B) ** NHAI

(C) IRCTC

(D) NTPC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Heartiest Congratulations to Team NHAI and Maha Metro onachieving the Guinness Book of World Record in Nagpur by constructing thelongestDouble Decker Viaduct (3.14 KM) with Highway Flyover & Metro Rail Supported onsingle column."

Concept note-2: Recently, the Nagpur Metro-Rail Project inMaharashtra has set a new record for building Asia’s longest double-decker viaduct.The Maha Metro was able to plan and design this multilayer structure with a metroon top and elevated national highway in the middle, supported on commonpillars.

Concept note-3: This 3.1 Km long double-decker viaduct was builtjointly on Nagpur’s Wadha road by Maha Metro and NHAI. This double-deckerviaduct made possible a three-layered transport corridor with the first layeroccupied by pre-existing highway, the second layer by a flyover highway, and thethird layer by Nagpur metro rail.

Concept note-4: Nagpur Metro inMaharashtra has entered Guinness Book of World Records for creating the longestdouble-decker viaduct metro measuring 3.14 km on Wardha Road. Nagpur Metroin Maharashtra has entered Guinness Book of World Records for creatingthe longest double-decker viaduct metro measuring 3.14 km on WardhaRoad.

Concept note-5: There are currently 16 operational rapid transit(popularly known as ‘metro’) systems in fifteen cities across India, with DelhiMetro being the largest. As of December 2022, India has 822.038 km ofoperational metro lines and 16 systems. A further 568.15 km of lines are underconstruction.
