Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 will be awarded to whom?

(A) Neha Aggarwal

(B) Manika Batra

(C) Soumyajit Ghosh

(D) ** Achanta Sharath Kamal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Table tennis legend Achanta Sharath Kamal will receive theMajor Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award, the country’s highest sporting honour, fromPresident Droupadi Murmu on November 30 at the Rashtrapati Bhavan in NewDelhi.

Concept note-2: While Eldhose became the first Indian to win a CWGgold in the men’s triple jump, Avinash Sable won a silver in the men’s 3000msteeplechase and broke the Kenyan monopoly on the podium in the sport. Indiacricket team captain Rohit Sharma’s coach Dinesh Lad received the DronacharyaAward in the lifetime category.

Concept note-3: Ans. No one received the BharatRatna, India’s highest civilian award, for 2022. The Padma Awards, however, weregiven to 128 winners in total.
