Manghar village located in which state become the first honey village in the country?

(A) ** Maharashtra

(B) Goa

(C) Karnataka

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Maharashtra: Manghar to become first ‘Village of Honey’ on16th May; will also provide job opportunities to rural sectors. Come May 16 andManghar (), a village in Maharashtra’s Mahabaleshwar will head towards becoming aVillage of Honey, the first such in the country.

Concept note-2: Punjab isthe highest honey-producing state in India. It has about 35,000 beekeepersthat deliver the highest amounts of honey compared to any other Indianstate.

Concept note-3: North East Region of India and Maharashtra arethe key areas for natural honey production.

Concept note-4: In 2021,mainland China was the leading producer of natural honey worldwide byfar. China produced over 472,000 metric tons of honey, almost more thanfive times the amount produced in Turkey, the second top producer thatyear. Iran, Argentina, and Ukraine rounded out the top five producers in2021.
