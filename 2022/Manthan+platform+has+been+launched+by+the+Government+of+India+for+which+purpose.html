Manthan platform has been launched by the Government of India for which purpose?

(A) ** R & D Collaboration

(B) Quality Assurance

(C) Supply Chain Management

(D) Artificial Intelligence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Manthan is aimed at promoting collaboration between industry and the scientific research and development ecosystem.

Concept note-2: -The Manthan platform promotes collaboration at scale between industry and the scientific research and development ecosystem to help meet India’s national targets and United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Concept note-3: -It is a digital platform that aims to build and nurture relationships between industry and academia so that more challenges can be solved faster and better, to meet India’s national targets and the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Concept note-4: -Bureau of Police Research and Development (BPR&D) and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) jointly launched MANTHAN-2021 on 26 August 2021.

Concept note-5: -About Manthan. The Government of India established the Office of the Principal Scientific Adviser (PSA) in November 1999. The PSA’s office aims to advice to the Prime Minister and the cabinet in matters of Science and Technology.
