MeitY Startup Hub along with which company will launch a programme to support and accelerate XR technology startups across India?

(A) Google

(B) Amazon

(C) Infosys

(D) ** Meta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: XR Startup Program is a collaboration between Meta andMeitY Startup Hub (MSH). The program aims to accelerate India’s contributiontowards building the foundations of the metaverse and nurturing the development ofExtended Reality (XR) technologies in India.

Concept note-2: As announced inSeptember 2022, XR Startup Program is a collaboration between MSH and Meta todiscover, nurture, and accelerate Extended Reality (XR) technology startups andinnovators across India.

Concept note-3: Detailed Solution: The Ministry ofElectronics & Information Technology startup hub in collaboration withMeta will launch a programme to support and accelerate the XR Technologystartups across India. The collaboration is part of the Government’s efforts forskilling in emerging and future technologies.

Concept note-4: MeitY hadinitiated TIDE 2.0 to promote tech entrepreneurship through financial andtechnical support to incubators engaged in supporting startups primarilyengaged in using emerging technologies such as IoT, AI, Block-chain, Roboticsetc.
