Millennium Challenge Corporation is an initiative of which country?

(A) Sri Lanka

(B) ** Nepal

(C) Russia

(D) Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Millennium Challenge Corporation (MCC) is aninnovative and independent U.S. foreign assistance agency that is helping lead thefight against global poverty.

Concept note-2: As of September 2017, MCC hasformed partnership in 46 countries around the world including Nepal. Nepal is theonly country in South Asia that MCC provided fund. Sri Lanka is in the pipelineafter Nepal.

Concept note-3: MCC’s mandate is to assist the world’s poorestcountries in reducing poverty through economic growth while strengthening goodgovernance, economic freedom, and investments in people in those countries selectedto receive its assistance.

Concept note-4: Created in 2004, MCC providestime-limited grants and assistance to poor countries that meet rigorous standards forgood governance, from fighting corruption to respecting democratic rights. Thiscooperation does not involve funding and is independent of any MCC compact orthreshold program partnerships.
