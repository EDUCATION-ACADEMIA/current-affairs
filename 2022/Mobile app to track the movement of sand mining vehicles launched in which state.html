Mobile app to track the movement of sand mining vehicles launched in which state?

(A) ** Haryana

(B) Gujarat

(C) West Bengal

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Notes: Haryana Chief Minister Manohar Lal Khattar launcheda Vehicle Movement Tracking System (VMTS) mobile app fortracking vehiclescarrying sand and other mining material.

Concept note-2: Haryana ChiefMinister Manohar Lal Khattar has launched a Vehicle Movement Tracking System(VMTS) mobile app to track the vehicles carrying sand and other miningmaterial.

Concept note-3: Chief Minister, Haryana Launched Vehicle MovementTracking System Mobile App on 04/05/2022. Hon’ble Chief Minister Haryana, ShriManohar Lal launched the Vehicle Movement Tracking System (VMTS) Mobile Appon 04/05/2022. This App has been designed and developed in-house by NationalInformatics Centre, Haryana.

Concept note-4: A vehicle tracking system is asoftware system used by transport companies to monitor the location of trucks,trailers and other vehicles. The software is connected to the vehicles viaGPS, giving companies real-time insight into the geographic position of theirvehicles.

Concept note-5: Telematics and GPS TrackingAt the center of atelematics system is a tracking device that gathers real-time data about the vehicle’slocation and status. A telematics solution receives GPS data and sends it to a centralserver.
