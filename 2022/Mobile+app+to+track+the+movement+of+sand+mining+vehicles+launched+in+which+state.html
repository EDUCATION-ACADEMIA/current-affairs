Mobile app to track the movement of sand mining vehicles launched in which state?

(A) ** Haryana

(B) Gujarat

(C) West Bengal

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Haryana. Haryana Chief Minister Manohar Lal Khattar launched a Vehicle Movement Tracking System (VMTS) mobile app in May 2022. It will help in tracking vehicles carrying sand and other mining material.

Concept note-2: -Haryana Chief Minister Manohar Lal Khattar has launched a Vehicle Movement Tracking System (VMTS) mobile app to track the vehicles carrying sand and other mining material.

Concept note-3: -Chandigarh May 4- Haryana Chief Minister, Sh. Manohar Lal launched the Vehicle Movement Tracking System (VMTS) Mobile App on May 4, 2022. This App has been designed and developed in-house by National Informatics Centre, Haryana.

Concept note-4: -A vehicle tracking system is a software system used by transport companies to monitor the location of trucks, trailers and other vehicles. The software is connected to the vehicles via GPS, giving companies real-time insight into the geographic position of their vehicles.

Concept note-5: -Telematics and GPS Tracking At the center of a telematics system is a tracking device that gathers real-time data about the vehicle’s location and status. A telematics solution receives GPS data and sends it to a central server.
