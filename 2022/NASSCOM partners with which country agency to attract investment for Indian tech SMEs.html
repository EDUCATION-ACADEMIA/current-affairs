NASSCOM partners with which country agency to attract investment for Indian tech SMEs?

(A) ** Australia

(B) Russia

(C) Brazil

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: NASSCOM partners with Australian govt agency to attractinvestment for Indian tech SMEs.

Concept note-2: As a part of theshared goal with Government of India, NASSCOM is focussing heavily oncreating suitable platforms to explore opportunities in “high potential butunder-penetrated markets”.

Concept note-3: UNICEF is proud to partner withNASSCOM Foundation to develop child rights online. The ongoing focus of thepartnership will be on supporting online safety and skilling adolescents byengaging key technology businesses through the Foundation’s active network ofcompanies.

Concept note-4: Our mission is to Reskill and Upskill India’sworkforce to ensure talent is Future Ready in terms of New Age Skills and Jobs andto make India a global hub for Digital Talent. SSC NASSCOM catalyzing theIT-ITeS Skills development ecosystem, enabling India to be the global hub for Digitaltalent.

Concept note-5: NASSCOM is the premier trade body and chamber ofcommerce of the Tech industry in India and comprises over 3000 member companiesincluding both Indian and multinational organisations that have a presence inIndia.
