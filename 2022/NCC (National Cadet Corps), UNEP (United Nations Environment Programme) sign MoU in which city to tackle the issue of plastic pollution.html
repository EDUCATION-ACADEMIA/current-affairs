NCC (National Cadet Corps), UNEP (United Nations Environment Programme) sign MoU in which city to tackle the issue of plastic pollution?

(A) Patna

(B) Gurugram

(C) Faridabad

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The aim of the MoU between NCC and UNEP is tosynergise and collate efforts towards engaging youth for promoting clean waterbodies.

Concept note-2: Few cadets along with other PI staffs and CO sirhimself cleaned the campus, planted new saplings, bought new dustbins etc. The DGNCC appreciated for the clean up work. It sets an example for the cleanenvironment. CO sir gave a speech to keep up the environment clean in every possibleway.

Concept note-3: Headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya, UNEP works throughits divisions as well as regional, liaison and out-posted offices and a growingnetwork of collaborating centres of excellence.

Concept note-4: Keeping thesurroundings and environment clean and hygienic is the responsibility of everycitizen, both individually as also collectively. This was the message that theCadets raised by planting trees and conducting rallies in their respectivecities.
