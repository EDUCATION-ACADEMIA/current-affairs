NHAI and Maharashtra Metro achieved a world record for constructing the longest double-decker viaduct of length how many in Nagpur?

(A) 3.34 km

(B) ** 3.14 km

(C) 3.04 km

(D) 3.24 km

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: A 3.14 km double-decker viaduct constructed by MaharashtraMetro Rail Corporation Ltd (Maha Metro) in Nagpur has made it to the GuinnessBook of World Records.

Concept note-2: This 3.1 Km long double-deckerviaduct was built jointly on Nagpur’s Wadha road by Maha Metro and NHAI. Thisdouble-decker viaduct made possible a three-layered transport corridor with the firstlayer occupied by pre-existing highway, the second layer by a flyover highway, and thethird layer by Nagpur metro rail.

Concept note-3: Nagpur Metro hassuccessfully created a Guinness World Record by constructing the longestdouble-decker viaduct (metro) is 3,140 meters and was achieved by the WardhaRoad in Nagpur. The double-decker viaduct of 3.14 km on Wardha Road hasthree metro stations – Chhatrapati Nagar, Jai Prakash Nagar and UjjwalNagar.

Concept note-4: Maha Metro Nagpur has added yet another feather toits cap as the Wardha Road Double Decker Viaduct has been identifiedby Guinness World Records as the Longest Double Decker in the Metrocategory across the world.

Concept note-5: Nagpur Metro has created aGuinness World Record by constructing the longest double-decker viaductfor a metro. The double-decker viaduct on Wardha Road is 3.14 km longand has three stations – Chhatrapati Nagar, Jai Prakash Nagar and UjjwalNagar.
