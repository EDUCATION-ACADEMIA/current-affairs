NPCI has announced to observe which month as the ‘UPI Safety and Awareness Month’?

(A) April

(B) May

(C) ** February

(D) March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: NPCI and the ecosystem comprising of leading banks andfintechs will observe February 1-7 as ‘UPI Safety and Awareness Week’ and the wholeof February as ‘UPI Safety and Awareness Month’.

Concept note-2: With theabove context in mind, NPCI conducted a pilot launch with 21 member banks. Thepilot launch was on 11th April 2016 by Dr. Raghuram G Rajan, Governor, RBI atMumbai. Banks have started to upload their UPI enabled Apps on Google Playstore from 25th August, 2016 onwards.

Concept note-3: The NationalPayments Corporation of India (NPCI) may implement its proposed 31December 2022 deadline to limit the volume cap for the Third-Party UPI AppProviders (TPAP). If the NPCI implements its proposal, people cannotmake unlimited transactions through Google Pay, PhonePe or other UPIapplications.

Concept note-4: The theme selected for current year FLW is “GoDigital, Go Secure” which will be observed between February 14-18, 2022. Thistheme is one of the strategic objectives of the National Strategy for FinancialEducation 2020-2025.

Concept note-5: The service was officially launched on 20September 2022. On 7 December 2022, RBI announced that UPI will upgrade fromsingle-block-single to single-block-multiple debit for recurring transactions andinvestments in securities. The feature will help the user block funds for specificpurpose and release it when needed.
