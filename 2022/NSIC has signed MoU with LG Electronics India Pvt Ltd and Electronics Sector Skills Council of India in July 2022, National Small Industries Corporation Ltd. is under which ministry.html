NSIC has signed MoU with LG Electronics India Pvt Ltd and Electronics Sector Skills Council of India in July 2022, National Small Industries Corporation Ltd. is under which ministry?

(A) Ministry of Commerce and Industry

(B) Ministry of Labour

(C) Ministry of Finance

(D) ** Ministry of MSME

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: NSIC signs MoU with LG Electronics India Pvt Ltd andElectronics Sector Skills Council of India on 22/07/22 in the presence of ShriNarayan Rane, Honorable Cabinet Minister, Shri Bhanu Pratap Singh Verma,Honorable Minister of State, Shri B.

Concept note-2: National SmallIndustries Corporation (NSIC), is an ISO 9001:2015 certified Government ofIndia Enterprise under Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises(MSME).

Concept note-3: National Small Industries Corporation Ltd.(NSIC) is an ISO 9001-2008 certified Government of India Enterprise underMinistry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) established in1955.
