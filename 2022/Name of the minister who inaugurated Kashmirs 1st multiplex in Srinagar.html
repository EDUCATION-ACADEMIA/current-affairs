Name of the minister who inaugurated Kashmir’s 1st multiplex in Srinagar?

(A) Sh R K Mathur

(B) Shri Deepak Hoodda

(C) ** Lt. Governor Manoj Sinha

(D) Shri Vinai Kumar Saxena

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The multiplex was inaugurated by Lieutenant Governor ofJammu and Kashmir Manoj Sinha.

Concept note-2: The Inox multiplex, ownedby a Kashmiri Pandit business family from Srinagar, took five years for completionand has a total seating capacity of 520. Consisting of three movie theatres, ithas been built with state-of-the- art technology at the high security zone,Shivpora, near Badami Bagh Cantonment.

Concept note-3: The filmindustry in Kashmir is probably the oldest in the country-it was in 1932that the first cinema, Kashmir Talkies [later renamed Palladium], was setup in Srinagar.

Concept note-4: Soon people will be able to watch 3Dmovies as well on the screen, said Vikas Dhar, owner of the multiplex. Themultiplex is the collaborative project of the INOX and Dhar family. Thecinemas across Kashmir were shut after an armed insurgency erupted in1989.

Concept note-5: People in Kashmir will get to enjoy the experience ofwatching a movie in the theatres after decades. The valley’s first multiplex is allset to open in Srinagar today. Designed by INOX, the multiplex will havethree movie theatres and a food court to promote regional cuisine, reportsPTI.
