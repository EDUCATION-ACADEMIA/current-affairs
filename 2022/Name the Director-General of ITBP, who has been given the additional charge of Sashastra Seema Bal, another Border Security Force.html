Name the Director-General of ITBP, who has been given the additional charge of Sashastra Seema Bal, another Border Security Force?

(A) Sandeep Masha

(B) Vijay Singh

(C) ** Sanjay Arora

(D) Sanjay Mehta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Government of India has given the Central Reserve PoliceForce(CRPF) Director General Sujoy Lal Thaosen the additional charge as DirectorGeneral Border Security Force (BSF). The incumbent BSF Director General PankajKumar Singh will retire on 31 December 2022.

Concept note-2: TheMadhya Pradesh cadre Indian Police Service (IPS) officer has been serving asthe SSB DG with the additional charge of ITBP. Senior IPS officer AnishDayal Singh on Monday took charge as the new director general (DG) of theIndo-Tibetan Border Police that guards the 3,488-km Line of Actual Control withChina.

Concept note-3: Many posts for some, none for othersFor instance,Surjeet Singh Deswal, a 1984-batch officer of the Haryana cadre, servedstints as the DG of five central forces in a span of two-odd years. He wasappointed as the DG of the ITBP in 2018 and also headed the SSB as anadditional charge.

Concept note-4: Detailed Solution. The correct answer isSujoy Lal Thaosen. A 1988-batch IPS officer, Sujoy Lal Thaosen, on 1 June2022, took charge as the director-general (DG) of the Sashastra Seema Bal(SSB).
