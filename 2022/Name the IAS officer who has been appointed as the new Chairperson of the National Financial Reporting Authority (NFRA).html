Name the IAS officer who has been appointed as the new Chairperson of the National Financial Reporting Authority (NFRA)?

(A) Sanjeev Sanyal

(B) ** Ajay Bhushan Pandey

(C) Hasmukh Adhia

(D) Amitabh Kant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Ajay BhushanPandey.

Concept note-2: National Financial Reporting Authority (NFRA) is anindependent regulator set up to oversee the auditing profession and theIndian Accounting Standards under the Companies Act 2013. It came intoexistence in October 2018. The chairperson since March 2022 is Ajay BhushanPandey.

Concept note-3: The government has appointed former finance secretaryAjay Bhushan Pandey as chairman of the National Financial Reporting Authority(NFRA) for a period of three years.

Concept note-4: The AppointmentsCommittee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the appointment of former financesecretary Ajay Bhushan Pandey as the chairperson of the National FinancialReporting Authority (NFRA).

Concept note-5: The National FinancialReporting Authority (NFRA) was constituted on 01st October,2018 by theGovernment of India under Sub Section (1) of section 132 of the Companies Act,2013.
