Name the Indian actress, who will grace the Cannes Film Festival as a member of the jury, this year?

(A) ** Deepika Padukone

(B) Vidya Balan

(C) Aishwarya Rai Bachchan

(D) Sharmila Tagore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: One of Bollywood’s most bankable actresses Deepika Padukonerecently made headlines for being the only Indian on the 75th Cannes Film Festivaljury.

Concept note-2: Deepika PadukoneIf there’s anything that got moreattention than the news of Padukone being a jury member at Cannes Film Festival2022, it was her beauty and fashion choices.

Concept note-3: Aishwarya RaiBachchanOnly one year later, she was serving on the jury, becoming the firstIndian actress to do so.

Concept note-4: From Cannes regulars DeepikaPadukone and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan to actors like Aditi Rao Hydari,Tamannaah Bhatia, Pooja Hegde and Nawazuddin Siddiqui, from composers likeRicky Kej to folk singer Mame Khan, desi representation was high at Cannes2022.
