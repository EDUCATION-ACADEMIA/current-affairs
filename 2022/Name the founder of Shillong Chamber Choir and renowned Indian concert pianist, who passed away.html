(A) Mahendra Prasad

(B) R L Jalappa

(D) ** Neil Nongkynrih

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Founder of Shillong Chamber Choir (SCC) and renownedIndian concert pianist Neil Nongkynrih passed away. In 2010, SCC performed forformer US President Barack Obama & Michelle Obama during their visit toIndia.
