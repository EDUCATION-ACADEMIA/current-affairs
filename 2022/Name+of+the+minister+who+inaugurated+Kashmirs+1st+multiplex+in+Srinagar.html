Name of the minister who inaugurated Kashmir’s 1st multiplex in Srinagar?

(A) Sh R K Mathur

(B) Shri Deepak Hoodda

(C) ** Lt. Governor Manoj Sinha

(D) Shri Vinai Kumar Saxena

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The multiplex was inaugurated by Lieutenant Governor of Jammu and Kashmir Manoj Sinha.

Concept note-2: -The Inox multiplex, owned by a Kashmiri Pandit business family from Srinagar, took five years for completion and has a total seating capacity of 520. Consisting of three movie theatres, it has been built with state-of-the- art technology at the high security zone, Shivpora, near Badami Bagh Cantonment.

Concept note-3: -Soon people will be able to watch 3D movies as well on the screen, said Vikas Dhar, owner of the multiplex. The multiplex is the collaborative project of the INOX and Dhar family. The cinemas across Kashmir were shut after an armed insurgency erupted in 1989.

Concept note-4: -The film industry in Kashmir is probably the oldest in the country-it was in 1932 that the first cinema, Kashmir Talkies [later renamed Palladium], was set up in Srinagar. Bombay Talkies started in 1934.

Concept note-5: -My father used to run Broadway Cinema,” says Vikas Dhar, owner of the INOX multiplex.
