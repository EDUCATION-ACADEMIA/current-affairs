Name the super app launched by Union Bank of India, similar to SBI’s Yono or HDFC Bank’s PayZapp?

(A) UnionMPay

(B) UnionConnect

(C) UnionGo

(D) ** UnionNXT

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Bank of India has launched its super-app named UnionNXT and digital transformation project SMBHAV.

Concept note-2: -Union Vyom App, the super app of the Bank, is a one stop solution for all financial products. The Vyom app is empowered to deliver a unique experience of banking to the customers where customer can do transactions beyond traditional banking.

Concept note-3: -Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: Union Bank of India has launched UnionNXT and Digital Transformation Project SAMBHAV with an investment outlay of around Rs 1,000 crore for the current financial year 2022-23.

Concept note-4: -The state-owned bank is planning to position YONO as a super app for an entire range of financial services.

Concept note-5: -Put simply, a financial super-app is an ecosystem of features and services that are personalised around the user’s needs. The foundational piece to this puzzle is user experience and interconnectedness. Enabling the user to access all available features easily and efficiently.
