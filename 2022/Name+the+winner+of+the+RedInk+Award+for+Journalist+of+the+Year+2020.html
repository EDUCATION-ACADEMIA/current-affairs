Name the winner of the RedInk Award for ‘Journalist of the Year’ 2020?

(A) Rana Ayyub

(B) Rohit Sardana

(C) ** Danish Siddiqui

(D) Arfa Khanum Sherwani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Danish Siddiqui was posthumously awarded ‘the Journalist of the Year’ for 2020. .

Concept note-2: -T.J.S. George, the first Editor to be charged with sedition in free India-whose column ‘Point of View’ ran non-stop for 25 years and 1,300 episodes in ‘The New Indian Express’-bags the RedInk Award for Lifetime Achievement. #RedInkAwards2022 On a day tech journalists were banned by Twitter.

Concept note-3: -Thayil Jacob Sony George, a Padma Bhushan awardee, won the lifetime achievement award and Dainik Bhaskar Group’s national editor Om Gaur won the journalist of the year award.

Concept note-4: -Sukanya Shantha’s report on the struggles of transgender persons who are incarcerated and Siddharth Varadarajan’s Pegasus Project curtain raiser have won in their respective categories.

Concept note-5: -Alum Jodi Lai wins AJAC’s 2022 Journalist of the Year Award.
