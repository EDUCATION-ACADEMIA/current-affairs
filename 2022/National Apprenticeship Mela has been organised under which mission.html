‘National Apprenticeship Mela’ has been organised under which mission?

(A) Start up India

(B) Make in India

(C) PM Rozgar Yojana

(D) ** Skill India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Skill India Mission. Apprenticeship fair to be held in 242districts on January 9: Government.

Concept note-2: What is NationalApprenticeship Promotion Scheme? National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme(NAPS) is a new scheme of Government of India to provide financial support toestablishments undertaking the apprenticeship training. It was launched on19thAugust 2016.

Concept note-3: Initially, the Act covered the apprenticeshiptraining for the trade Apprentices. Subsequently, the Act was amended in 1973, 1986and 2014 to bring the Graduates, Technician, Technician (Vocational) and OptionalTrade Apprentices respectively under its purview and Apprenticeship Rules, 1992were revised in year 2015.

Concept note-4: Ministry of Skill Development andEntrepreneurship is the administrative ministry responsible for implementation of theAct. apprenticeship as one of the key program for creating skilled manpower inIndia.
