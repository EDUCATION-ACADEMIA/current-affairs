National Institute of Urban Affairs has inked an MoU with which organisation to implement the Sustainable Cities India program?

(A) UNDP

(B) ** WEF

(C) World Bank

(D) IMF

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The NIUA (National Institute of Urban Affairs) and the WEF(World Economic Forum) has reportedly inked an MoU (Memorandum ofUnderstanding) to work together on Sustainable Cities India. It is a crucialstep in realizing India’s vision of expediting the transition towards cleanenergy.

Concept note-2: The ‘Sustainable Cities India’ initiative aims to helpcities decarbonize in a systematic and sustainable manner, reducing emissions whiledelivering resilient and equitable urban ecosystems. This initiative reinforces India’scommitment at COP26 to achieve net-zero emissions by 2070 as a climate mitigationresponse.

Concept note-3: City Sprint Process: The City Sprint process is aseries of multi-sectoral, multi-stakeholder workshops involving business, government,and civil society leaders to enable decarbonization, especially through cleanelectrification and circularity.

Concept note-4: What Initiatives are Taken byGovernment of India’s for Urban Development? Smart Cities Mission. Atal Missionfor Urban Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT). Pradhan MantriAwas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U).
