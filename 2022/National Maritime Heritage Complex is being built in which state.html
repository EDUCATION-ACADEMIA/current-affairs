National Maritime Heritage Complex is being built in which state?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Maharashtra

(C) ** Gujarat

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Rs. 3500 crore project is being built at the historic IndusValley Civilization region of Lothalin Gujarat under the Sagarmala Scheme of theMinistry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways.

Concept note-2: NationalMaritime Heritage Complex (NMHC) at Lothal is being developed as aone-of-its-kind project to not only display India’s rich and diverse maritimeheritage and also help Lothal to emerge as a world-class international touristdestination.

Concept note-3: The correct answer is Lothal. The government isplanning to establish India’s First ‘National Maritime Heritage Complex (NMHC)’ atLothal, Gujarat. The museum will be set up in Lothal, Gujarat by the Ministry ofCulture in collaboration with the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways(MoPSW).

Concept note-4: NEW DELHI: Ministry of Ports, Shipping andWaterways plans to build a National Maritime Heritage Complex (NMHC) at thesite of the Indus Valley civilisation in Lothal, Gujarat. A first of its kindcomplex in India, this centre will showcase India’s rich and diverse maritimeheritage.

Concept note-5: Dating back over 4600 years, the dockyardin Lothal is the oldest man-made dockyard, built of burnt bricks, roughly240 m long, 37 m wide and 4 m deep, with a warehouse located next toit.
