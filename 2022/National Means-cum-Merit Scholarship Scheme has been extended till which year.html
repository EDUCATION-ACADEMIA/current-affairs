National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship Scheme has been extended till which year?

(A) 2029-30

(B) 2023-24

(C) 2028-30

(D) ** 2025-26

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: New Delhi: The last date for submission of applications forNational Means cum Merit Scholarship Scheme (NMMSS) for the year 2022-23 hasbeen extended to 30 November, 2022, the Ministry of Educationsaid in astatement.

Concept note-2: Last date to submit applications (fresh/renewal) forNational Means cum Merit Scholarship Scheme (NMMSS) is 31st October, 2022 onNational Scholarship Portal (NSP) The last date for submission of applications forNMMSS for the year 2022-23 is 31st October, 2022.

Concept note-3: Under theNational Scholarship Scheme, up to ₹ 60,000 will be given by the government to thestudents and girls to study. All the students and girls studying in class VIIIcan participate in NMMS Scholarship.

Concept note-4: Through NSPscholarship, candidates can get education who are from backward classes orcan’t pursue their education due to financial crisis. Candidate must fill theapplication form before the NSP Scholarship 2022-23 last date i.e. is 31 Dec2022.
