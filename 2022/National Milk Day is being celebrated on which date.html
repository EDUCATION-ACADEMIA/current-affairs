National Milk Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) November 25

(B) November 24

(C) ** November 26

(D) November 27

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Dr. Verghese Kurien’s birthday is commemorated onNovember 26th with the National Milk Day. He is credited with bringingIndia’s “white revolution.” The white revolution was started by Dr. VergheseKurien, who also made India’s largest self-sustaining industry the dairyfarm.

Concept note-2: Therefore, the first National Milk Day was observed on 26November 2014.

Concept note-3: National Milk Day is celebrated eachyear on January 11th in the United States.

Concept note-4: The daywas started by the Indian Diary Association (IDA). The story lies in theyear 1970 when the National Diary Development Board (NDDB) begana rural development program called Operation Flood. This program wasconsidered one of the largest and aimed to develop a milk grid throughout thecountry.
