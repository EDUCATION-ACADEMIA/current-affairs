National Museum, India has signed an MoU with which country for Silver Exhibition in March 2023?

(A) Russia

(B) France

(C) ** Denmark

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The exhibition will showcase the finest of the silver collectionfrom the Museum Kolding and the National Museum in New Delhi in March2023. The collaboration and exhibition is under the recently agreed culturalexchange programme between India and Denmark for the years 2022 to2026.

Concept note-2: The National Museum, New Delhi, as we see it today,has an interesting beginning. The blueprint for establishing the NationalMuseum in Delhi was prepared by the Maurice Gwyer Committee in May1946.

Concept note-3: The National Museum in New Delhi, also known as theNational Museum of India, is one of the largest museums in India. Established in1949, it holds a variety of articles ranging from pre-historic era to modern works ofart.

Concept note-4: On August 15, 1949, the National Museum, New Delhi,was inaugurated in the Rashtrapati Bhawan by Shri R.C. Rajagopalachari,the Governor-General of India. The foundation of the present building waslaid by Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru, the Prime Minister of India, on May 12,1955.
