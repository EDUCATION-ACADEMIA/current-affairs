National Panchayati Raj Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) April 26

(B) ** April 24

(C) April 25

(D) April 27

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: on 24th April, commemorates the 73rd Amendment Act, 1992of the Constitution coming into effect in the year 1993.

Concept note-2: Thetheme for the 2022 National Panchayat Raj DayThere is no theme for this year’scelebrations This year’s awards were given to recognize the best work done by thepanchayats in India. The awards were given in the category of: Nanaji DeshmukhRashtriya Gaurav Gram Pushkar.

Concept note-3: The first National PanchayatiRaj Day also called Panchayati Raj Diwas was celebrated in 2010 declared bythe former Prime Minister Manmohan Singh. The annual celebration forNPRD is done to commemorate the day on which the 73rd ConstitutionalAmendment was passed in 1992.

Concept note-4: The then Prime Minister ofIndia Manmohan Singh declared the first National Panchayati Raj Day on24 April 2010. He mentioned that if Panchayati Raj institutions (PRIs)functioned properly and locals participated in the development process, theMaoist threat could be countered.

Concept note-5: Th e system latercame to be known as Panchayati Raj, which was inaugurated by the thenPrime Minister Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru on 2 October 1959 at Nagour inRajasthan.
