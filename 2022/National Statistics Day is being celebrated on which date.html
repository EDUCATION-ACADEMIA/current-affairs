National Statistics Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) June 28

(B) June 26

(C) ** June 29

(D) June 27

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India observes National Statistics Day on 29th June everyyear to honour the work and contribution of late Professor and ScientistPrasanta Chandra Mahalanobis in the field of Statistics and economicplanning.

Concept note-2: India observes June 29 as National Statistics Dayevery year. The day is observed to honour Late Professor Prasanta ChandraMahalanobis’s work in the field of Statistics and his contribution as a member ofIndia’s first planning commission.

Concept note-3: The theme of this year is‘Data for Sustainable Development’.

Concept note-4: World Statistics Day isheld every five years on October 20th. The day celebrates the achievements of theFundamental Principles of Official Statistics. Statistics involves the collection,analyzing, and interpreting large amounts of numerical data. Statistics arevital.

Concept note-5: The next time the day was observed was in 2015,with the topic ‘Better data, better lives’. The same year, the United NationsGeneral Assembly declared that World Statistics Day would thereafter becelebrated once every 5 years. It was a resolution that was agreed upon on 3 June2015.
