‘National Apprenticeship Mela’ has been organised under which mission?

(A) Start up India

(B) Make in India

(C) PM Rozgar Yojana

(D) ** Skill India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme? National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) is a new scheme of Government of India to provide financial support to establishments undertaking the apprenticeship training. It was launched on19th August 2016.

Concept note-2: -Initially, the Act covered the apprenticeship training for the trade Apprentices. Subsequently, the Act was amended in 1973, 1986 and 2014 to bring the Graduates, Technician, Technician (Vocational) and Optional Trade Apprentices respectively under its purview and Apprenticeship Rules, 1992 were revised in year 2015.

Concept note-3: -Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is the administrative ministry responsible for implementation of the Act. apprenticeship as one of the key program for creating skilled manpower in India.

Concept note-4: -: a position as an apprentice : an arrangement in which someone learns an art, trade, or job under another.
