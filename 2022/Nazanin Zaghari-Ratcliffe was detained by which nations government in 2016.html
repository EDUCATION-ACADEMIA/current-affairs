Nazanin Zaghari-Ratcliffe was detained by which nation’s government in 2016?

(A) Sudan

(B) Israel

(C) ** Iran

(D) Iraq

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nazanin Zaghari-Ratcliffe (née Zaghari; Persian: ; born 26 December 1978) is an Iranian-British dual citizen who was detained in Iran from 3 April 2016 as part of a long running dispute between Britain and Iran.

Concept note-2: -What was she accused of doing? Iranian authorities alleged Ms Zaghari-Ratcliffe was plotting to topple the government in Tehran, but no official charges were ever made public. Iran’s Revolutionary Guards said she was leading a “foreign-linked hostile network", and sentenced her to five years in prison.
