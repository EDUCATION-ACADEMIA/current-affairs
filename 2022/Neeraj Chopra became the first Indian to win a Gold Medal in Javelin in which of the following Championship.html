Neeraj Chopra became the first Indian to win a Gold Medal in Javelin in which of the following Championship?

(A) ** Diamond League Championship

(B) World Athletics Championship

(C) Asian Games

(D) European Athletics Championship

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: He is the first Asian athlete to win an Olympic gold medal inMen’s Javelin throw. A Junior Commissioned Officer (JCO) in the Indian Army,Chopra is the first track and field athlete to win a gold medal for India at theOlympics.

Concept note-2: KD Jadhav: The scrawny lad who stood tall in thesport of giants. Khashaba Jadhav may have been lean and stood at only 5’5” but theIndian wrestler, also referred to as the Pocket Dynamo, dwarfs the far bigger athletesin his sport.

Concept note-3: Who can forget August 7, 2021, when NeerajChopra became the first track and field athlete to win any medal at theGames and just the second individual gold medalist from India. At the TokyoOlympics 2020, Neeraj accomplished it with a memorable throw of 87.58meters.

Concept note-4: From an overweight 13-year-old kid who tookup sports to gain confidence to becoming the first Indian track and fieldathlete to win an Olympic medal, javelin thrower Neeraj Chopra has come along way.

Concept note-5: Javelin thrower Neeraj Chopra wins silvermedal to become only the 2nd Indian to win a medal at World AthleticsChampionships.
