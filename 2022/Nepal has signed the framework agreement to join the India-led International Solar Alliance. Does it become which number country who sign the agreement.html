Nepal has signed the framework agreement to join the India-led International Solar Alliance. Does it become which number country who sign the agreement?

(A) 107th

(B) ** 105th

(C) 117th

(D) 112th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Prime Minister Modi welcomed Nepal’s decision tojoin the International Solar Alliance (ISA), with Nepal, becoming the105th country to become a signatory to the Framework Agreement of theISA.

Concept note-2: Denmark has signed the framework agreement of theInternational Solar Alliance Framework Agreement (ISA FA) and Instrument ofRatification with India.

Concept note-3: Nepal has become the 105th membercountry to sign on to the Framework Agreement of International Solar Alliance(ISA).

Concept note-4: India and France launched the International SolarAlliance (ISA) to boost solar energy in developing countries. It was launched at theUnited Nations Climate Change Conference in Paris in November 2015 by the IndianPrime Minister and French President.
