Nobel Prize Day is being observed every year on which date?

(A) December 11

(B) December 09

(C) ** December 10

(D) December 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Since 1901, the Nobel Prizes have been presented to Nobel Prize laureates at ceremonies on 10 December, the anniversary of Alfred Nobel’s death.

Concept note-2: -As stipulated in the will of the Swedish-born inventor and international industrialist Alfred Nobel, which was opened after his death in 1896, the Nobel Prizes in physics, chemistry, physiology or medicine and literature are awarded in Stockholm, Sweden, while the Nobel Peace Prize is awarded in Oslo, Norway.

Concept note-3: -On December 10th, Nobel Prize Day recognizes the annual award established by Alfred Nobel. Alfred Nobel signed his third and last will at the Swedish-Norwegian Club in Paris on November 27, 1895.

Concept note-4: -The Nobel Peace Prize award ceremony takes place in Oslo City Hall on 10 December – the anniversary of Alfred Nobel’s death. At the ceremony, the Nobel Peace Prize is awarded to the Nobel Peace Prize laureates. The ceremony contains artistic performances and the speech of Chair of the Nobel Committee.

Concept note-5: -Announcement of the 2022 Nobel Peace Prize by Berit Reiss-Andersen, Chair of the Norwegian Nobel Committee, on 7 October 2022.
