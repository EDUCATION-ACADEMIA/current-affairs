OCI, based in which state has launched ‘OCI dedicated region’ for the India market?

(A) Vietnam

(B) ** USA

(C) South Korea

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -US-based technology major Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI), Oracle’s cloud services platform, has introduced the ‘OCI dedicated region’ for the Indian market.

Concept note-2: -Dedicated regions are public regions assigned to a single organization. Region specific details, such as region ID and region key are not available in public documentation, check with your Oracle contact for this information for your dedicated region.

Concept note-3: -Now there are a total of 40 regions.

Concept note-4: -Oracle Dedicated Region Cloud at Customer OCI Dedicated Region combines the elasticity and utility of the public cloud with the granular control, security, and predictability of on-premises infrastructure to deliver high-performance and cost-effective infrastructure services.

Concept note-5: -US-based technology major Oracle’s cloud services platform, Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) has launched ‘OCI dedicated region’ for the India market to offer public cloud services to customers on their premises.
