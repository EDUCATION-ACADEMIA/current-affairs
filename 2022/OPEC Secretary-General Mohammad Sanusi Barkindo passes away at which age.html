(A) ** 63

(B) 92

(C) 88

(D) 69

Concept note-1: Mohammad Barkindo, Nigerian politician and secretary-general ofoil producer group OPEC, has died at the age of 63, just days before he was set tofinish his term at the organization.

Concept note-2: OPEC Secretary GeneralMohammad Barkindo died 5 July in his home country of Nigeria. He was 63. Thehead of Nigeria’s National Petroleum Corporation (NNPC), Mele Kyari,announced the news in a tweet on 6 July, which was later confirmed byOPEC.
