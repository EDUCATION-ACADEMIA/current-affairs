On which date the ‘Himachal Diwas’ is being celebrated in India?

(A) April 16

(B) ** April 15

(C) April 12

(D) April 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is always celebrated on April 15th. It was on this day in 1948 that Himachal Pradesh was created as a province of India.

Concept note-2: -Himachal Pradesh was declared as the province of India on 15th April, 1948 which is why it is celebrated as Himachal Day.

Concept note-3: -Himachal Day is observed every year on the 15th of April in Himachal Pradesh. It came into existence in 1948 when, post-Independence, 28 minor princely states were integrated into one province. Himachal Day is declared an official holiday in the state of Himachal Pradesh.

Concept note-4: -The government of India passed the State of Himachal Pradesh Act in 1970 to make it a state. And Himachal Pradesh was made a state on January 25, 1971.

Concept note-5: -Formerly a union territory, Himachal Pradesh became a state of India on January 25, 1971.
