On which date the World Athletics Day is celebrated?

(A) May 06

(B) May 05

(C) May 04

(D) ** May 07

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World athletics day is celebrated on the 7th of May every year.

Concept note-2: -In 2021, World Athletics Day was celebrated on May 7.

Concept note-3: -WORLD ATHLETICS DAY 2022: In a bid to encourage sports activities throughout the world, May 7 is celebrated annually as World Athletics Day. Introduced in 1996, the day aims to spread awareness among the youth about athletics.

Concept note-4: -World Athletics Day is celebrated every year globally on the 7th of May to raise awareness about the importance of fitness and encourage them to play sports, especially athletics. The basic objective of World Athletics Day is to boost the participation of youths in athletics.

Concept note-5: -World Athletics Day 2022: Objectives Making sports, especially athletics popular among children and youth. To establish a firm link between the youth, sport, and environmental conservation.
