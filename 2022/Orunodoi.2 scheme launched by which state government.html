Orunodoi.2 scheme launched by which state government?

(A) Sikkim

(B) Karnataka

(C) ** Assam

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma launched thesecond version of ‘Orunodoi’, the flagship scheme of the state government aimed atproviding financial security to disadvantaged families.

Concept note-2: AboutThe Orunodoi Scheme :The existing beneficiaries of the Deen Dayal DivyanganPension Yojana and Indira Miri Widow Pension Scheme would be subsumed underOrunodoi 2.0. Divyangans and transgenders would also be included. Under the‘Orunodoi 2.0’ scheme the amount has been enhanced from 830/- to Rs. 1250/Women.

Concept note-3: Assam Orunodoi Scheme was drafted for thepurpose of providing financial aid to economically vulnerable families. Theamount will be transferred into the registered woman’s family account. It waslaunched on 2nd Oct 2020. The families receive financial aid of Rs. 1000 permonth.

Concept note-4: Under Assam, Orunodoi Scheme Rs 830 per month willbe given to the beneficiaries to buy fundamental items like medicines, pulses, sugar,etc. Rs 400 will be given to purchase medicines, Rs 200 will be providedto buy 4 kilograms of pulses, Rs 80 to buy sugar, and Rs 150 to buy fruitorigin.

Concept note-5: The state government of Assam has launched a new ZeroInterest Crop Loan Scheme 2023 for the farmers in budget. In this scheme, the stategovt. will provide 100% interest subsidy on agricultural loans for short termcrops. Now farmers can avail a crop loan of up to Rs. 2 Lakh at 0% interestrates.
