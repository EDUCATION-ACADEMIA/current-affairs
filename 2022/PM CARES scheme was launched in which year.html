[SOURCES]

PM CARES scheme was launched in which year?

(A) 2022

(B) 2015

(C) 2018

(D) ** 2021

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Fund was established for the purpose of redressing theCovid-19 pandemic in India, in 2020.

Concept note-2: PM CARES Fund hasbeen registered as a Public Charitable Trust. The trust deed of PM CARES Fundhas been registered under the Registration Act, 1908 at New Delhi on 27thMarch, 2020.

Concept note-3: The Prime Minister has announced PMCARES for Children scheme to support children who have lost both parents orsurviving parent or legal guardian or adoptive parents due to COVID-19pandemic.

Concept note-4: The objective of the Scheme is to ensurecomprehensive care and protection of Children in a sustained manner, and enabletheir well being through health insurance, empower them through education andequip them for self-sufficient existence with financial support on reaching 23years of age.

Concept note-5: The PM CARES for Children Scheme waslaunched by The Hon’ble Prime Minister of India on 29th May 2021 for theChildren.
