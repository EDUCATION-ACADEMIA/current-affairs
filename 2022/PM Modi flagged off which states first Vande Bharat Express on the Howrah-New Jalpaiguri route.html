PM Modi flagged off which state’s first Vande Bharat Express on the Howrah-New Jalpaiguri route?

(A) Himachal Pradesh

(B) Haryana

(C) Goa

(D) ** West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Connecting Howrah to New Jalpaiguri: PM Modi flags offBengal’s first Vande Bharat train.

Concept note-2: The first Vande BharatExpress train was inaugurated on February 18, 2019. The very first route isconnecting the capital city of Delhi to the holy city of Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh),making stops at Thyagraj and Kanpur. This train runs five days a week, exceptMonday and Thursday.

Concept note-3: During its run from Howrah to NewJalpaiguri, Vade Bharat Express will stop at three stations – Bolpur (Shantiniketan);Malda Town and Barsoi.

Concept note-4: Currently, eight Vande BharatExpress trains are running across the country. These include Varanasi-NewDelhi, Katra-New Delhi, Mumbai Central-Gandhinagar, New Delhi-AmbAndaura, Chennai-Mysuru, Bilaspur-Nagpur and Howrah-New JalpaiguriVande Bharat trains.

Concept note-5: The Vande Bharat Express, whichdebuted on November 10, 2022, was the fifth train. Two stops are made bythe high-speed train at Katpadi and KRS Bengaluru. This train travels479 KM in total. It operates five days a week and is not operational onWednesday.
