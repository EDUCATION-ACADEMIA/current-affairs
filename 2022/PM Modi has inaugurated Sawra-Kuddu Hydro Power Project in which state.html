PM Modi has inaugurated Sawra-Kuddu Hydro Power Project, in which state?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated and laid the foundation of the hydropower projects worth Rs 11000 crore in Mandi, Himachal Pradesh. He inaugurated the Sawra-Kuddu Hydro Power Project, a 111 MW Project which has been built at a cost of around Rs 2,080 crore.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Monday inaugurated and laid the foundation stone of hydropower projects worth over Rs 11,000 crore in Himachala Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -This will be the first hydropower project of Hamirpur district. The 66 MW project will be built at a cost of over Rs 680 crore. It will lead to generation of over 300 million units of electricity per year. Prime Minister will inaugurate the Sawra-Kuddu Hydro Power Project.
