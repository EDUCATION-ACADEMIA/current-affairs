[SOURCES]

PM Modi participates in celebrations of which edition of Parkash Purab of Sri Guru Tegh Bahadur Ji in New Delhi?

(A) 350th

(B) ** 400th

(C) 450th

(D) 300th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: PM Modi also released a commemorative coin and postagestamp on the occasion of the 400th Parkash Purab celebrations. “In front ofAurangzeb’s tyrannical thinking, Guru Tegh Bahadur ji, becoming ‘Hind di Chadar’,stood like a rock,” Modi said.

Concept note-2: Guru Tegh Bahadur is the 9thGuru of the Sikh community. He gave his life to protect human rights, and his deathanniversary is celebrated on November 24 as Shaheedi Diwas. Guru TeghBahadur was born on April 21, 1621, in Amritsar. Tyaga Mal was his originalname.

Concept note-3: Guru Tegh Bahadur’s martyrdom dayHe lost his lifebecause of orders given by Mughal King Aurangzeb. Every year on November24th, Shaheed Diwas, or Shaheedi Diwas, is observed to commemorate GuruTegh Bahadur’s martyrdom.

Concept note-4: Every year November 24is observed as the day of the Martyrdom of Guru Tegh Bahadur. On thisday in 1675, he was publicly Executed by the Mughal Emperor Aurangzebin Delhi for refusing to convert to Islam, as it was forcibly done at thattime.

Concept note-5: Guru Tegh Bahadur Martyrdom Day 2022, Guru TeghBahadur Shaheedi Diwas 2022: Guru Tegh Bahadur Martyrdom Day is observedevery year on November 24, commemorating Shaheedi Diwas of Guru TeghBahadur.
