(A) Rs.20, 000/-Cr

(B) Rs.17, 000/-Cr

(C) Rs.19, 000/-Cr

(D) ** Rs.21, 000/-Cr

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated Phase 1 of Modi Shaikshanik Sankul, an educational complex for needy students in Ahmedabad, Gujarat today. The project will help provide facilities to students for holistic development. The Prime Minister cut the ribbon to mark the inauguration of the Bhavan.

Concept note-2: -The foundation stone was laid on 11 October 2015 by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Concept note-3: -Government releases Rs 22 crore per year for the upkeep of Rashtrapati Bhavan and other expenditures. He gets Rs 50,000 as basic pay, Rs 3,000 as expenditure-related allowance, Rs 2 thousand per day that amounts to Rs 60,000 per month as other allowances and Rs 45,000 per month as parliamentary constituency allowance.

The launch of 5G services follows years of intense preparation.
