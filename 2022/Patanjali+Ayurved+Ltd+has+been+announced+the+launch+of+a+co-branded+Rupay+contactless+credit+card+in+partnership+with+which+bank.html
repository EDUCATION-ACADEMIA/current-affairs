Patanjali Ayurved Ltd has been announced the launch of a co-branded Rupay contactless credit card in partnership with which bank?

(A) HDFC Bank

(B) ** Punjab National Bank

(C) Bank of Baroda

(D) State Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Praveena Rai, COO, NPCI said, “We are pleased to associate with Punjab National Bank and Patanjali Ayurved for the launch of co-branded RuPay contactless credit cards.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi: Patanjali Ayurved Limited (PAL) and Punjab National Bank (PNB) on Wednesday launched co-branded credit cards in partnership with the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). The co-branded credit cards are offered on NPCI’s RuPay Platform.

Concept note-3: -Central Bank of India was the first Bank to introduce credit cards in India.

Concept note-4: -Patanjali PNB Co-Branded RuPay Select Credit Card: Minimum joining fees - Rs. 500/- Annual fee - NIL ( If card is used once in a quarter ) 300+ reward points on 1st usage. 2X rewards points on retail merchandise.

Concept note-5: -In 1966, BankAmericard went national to become the nation’s first licensed general-purpose credit card. It would be renamed Visa a decade later to acknowledge its growing international presence.
