Pegatron of which country has become the third Apple vendor to set up a manufacturing unit in Chennai, India?

(A) USA

(B) Russia

(C) China

(D) ** Taiwan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Taiwanese electronics major Pegatron on Friday inauguratedits new facility in Chennai to manufacture iPhone devices. The company isinvesting ₹1,100 crore investment with employment generation for 14,000people.

Concept note-2: Pegatron, the third contract manufacturer of Apple Incto set up shop in India after Wistron and Foxconn, is currently assembling iPhone 12at the new facility. ETB Sivapriyan, DHNS, Chennai, Nov 04 2022, 15:12 ist.updated: Nov 05 2022, 09:12 ist.

Concept note-3: Taiwan’s Pegatron on Fridaybecame the third Apple vendor to set up a manufacturing unit in India,inaugurating a facility at Mahindra World City in Chennai. It will investaround Rs 1,100 crore in the unit, potentially generating around 14,000jobs.

Concept note-4: Foxconn already assembles Apple devices in its Chennaiplant and Apple has widened its production capability through Foxconn andWistron. According to a report from Counterpoint Research, locally manufacturediPhones in India grew 196 per cent YoY in 2021.

Concept note-5: Foxconn,Wistron and Pegatron are the contract manufacturers for Apple’s iPhonesin India. The component suppliers include Sunwoda, Avary, Foxlink andSalcomp.
