Pomila Jaspal has been appointed as the Director (Finance) and CFO of which company?

(A) ** ONGC

(B) HPCL

(C) HCL

(D) IOCL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Pomila Jaspal joins ONGC as new CFO and Director ofFinance.

Concept note-2: Director (Finance) Pomila Jaspal has taken over asDirector (Finance) of ONGC on 19 April 2022. Prior to this, Ms Jaspalserved as Director (Finance) in Mangalore Refinery and PetrochemicalsLtd (MRPL) - Schedule ‘A’ CPSE and subsidiary of ONGC, since October2019.

Concept note-3: Pomila Jaspal | ONGC CFO: ONGC appoints PomilaJaspal as the Director (Finance) and CFO.

Concept note-4: Governmentappoints Shri Arun Kumar Singh as Chairman, ONGC; approves Board levelreforms. Government of India had set up a Search-cum-Selection Committee (SCSC)in February, 2022 for filling up the post of CMD, ONGC.
