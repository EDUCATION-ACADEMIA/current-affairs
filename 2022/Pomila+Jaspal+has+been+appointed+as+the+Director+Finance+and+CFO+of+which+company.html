Pomila Jaspal has been appointed as the Director (Finance) and CFO of which company?

(A) ** ONGC

(B) HPCL

(C) HCL

(D) IOCL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pomila Jaspal joins ONGC as new CFO and Director of Finance.

Concept note-2: -Pomila Jaspal | ONGC CFO: ONGC appoints Pomila Jaspal as the Director (Finance) and CFO.

Concept note-3: -Director (Finance) Pomila Jaspal has taken over as Director (Finance) of ONGC on 19 April 2022. Prior to this, Ms Jaspal served as Director (Finance) in Mangalore Refinery and Petrochemicals Ltd (MRPL) - Schedule ‘A’ CPSE and subsidiary of ONGC, since October 2019.

Concept note-4: -Government appoints Shri Arun Kumar Singh as Chairman, ONGC; approves Board level reforms. Government of India had set up a Search-cum-Selection Committee (SCSC) in February, 2022 for filling up the post of CMD, ONGC.

Concept note-5: -Asset Publisher. Mr Ravi took over the charge of the Director General of ONGC Energy Centre on 18 July 2022.
