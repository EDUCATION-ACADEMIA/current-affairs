(A) ** Tokyo

(B) New Delhi

(C) Paris

(D) Berlin

Concept note-1: India’s Pramod Bhagat defended his men’s singles SL3 worldtitle once again, defeating compatriot Nitesh Kumar in straight games on Sundayin Tokyo at the BWF Para Badminton World Championships 2022. Alsowinning the gold medal was Manisha Ramadass in women’s singles SU5category.
