Prasar Bharati has signed an MoU on cooperation and collaboration in Broadcasting with ORTM in which country?

(A) ** Madagascar

(B) Ethiopia

(C) Tanzania

(D) Mozambique

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Taking to the micro blogging site, Prasar Bharati said, “An MoU on cooperation and collaboration in Broadcasting was signed between Prasar Bharati and ORTM (Office de la Radio et de la Television) Madagascar in Antananarivo by Ambassador Abhay Kumar and Jean Yves Belalahy, Director General of ORTM.

Concept note-2: -I&B Minister Anurag Thakur says Prasar Bharati has signed MoU with IIT, Kanpur to develop Next Generation Broadcast solution.

Concept note-3: -India and Egypt today signed an MOU to facilitate content exchange, capacity building, and Co-Productions between Prasar Bharati and National Media Authority of Egypt.

Concept note-4: -Indian Ambassador to Thailand Suchitra Durai represented Prasar Bharati in signing the MoU, one of the two documents on Thai-Indian cooperation signed during the ceremony.

Concept note-5: -A Memorandum of Understanding was signed between the Sushma Swaraj Institute of Foreign Service, Ministry of External Affairs, Republic of India and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Republic of Madagascar for training of diplomats between India and Madagascar.
