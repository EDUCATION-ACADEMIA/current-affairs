President Draupadi Murmu has launched ‘PARAM KAMRUPA’ Supercomputer facility in which IIT?

(A) ** IIT Guwahati

(B) IIT Bombay

(C) IIT Delhi

(D) IIT BHU

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: President Droupadi Murmu inaugurates ‘PARAM KAMRUPA’Supercomputer facility at IIT Guwahati.

Concept note-2: Deployed at IITGuwahati, the supercomputer facility has been named after Kamrupa, the firstknown historical kingdom of Assam. It replaced Param-Ishan as the northeastregion’s fastest and most powerful supercomputer. Param-Ishan was inaugurated inIIT Guwahati in 2016.

Concept note-3: Param-Kamrupa has 838 teraflops. Ateraflop is a measure of a computer’s speed and can be expressed as a trillion floatingpoint operations per second. An average gaming PC has a processing power ofaround 3-4 teraflops.

Concept note-4: One of the fastest supercomputer in Indiais PARAM Ananta which was launched on May 30, 2022, at IIT, Gandhinagar. Thissupercomputer was developed under the NSM by C-DAC and IIT Gandhinagar. It isat 102 positions among the world’s top 500 supercomputers, with a peak performanceof 3.3 Petaflops.
