President Droupadi Murmu has announced how many Gallantry awards for Armed Forces and CAPF personnel?

(A) 13

(B) ** 107

(C) 94

(D) 81

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: President of India, Droupadi Murmu has approved 412Gallantry awards and other Defence decorations to Armed Forces personnel andothers. These include six Kirti Chakras, including four posthumous, and 15 ShauryaChakras including two posthumous.

Concept note-2: 107 Gallantry awardsannounced for Armed Forces and CAPF personnel.

Concept note-3: PresidentMurmu approves 412 Gallantry Awards to Armed Forces personnel | Full listhere - India Today.

Concept note-4: It includes one Bar to Sena Medal(gallantry), 92 Sena Medals, including four posthumous, one Nao Sena Medal(gallantry), seven Vayu Sena Medals (gallantry) and 29 Param Vishisht SevaMedals.

Concept note-5: President Droupadi Murmu has approved 107 gallantryawards to armed forces and CAPF personnel. The awards include three Kirti Chakra,13 Shaurya Chakras, two bar to Sena Medals (gallantry), 81 Sena Medals(gallantry), one Nao Sena Medal (gallantry) and seven Vayu Sena Medals(gallantry).
