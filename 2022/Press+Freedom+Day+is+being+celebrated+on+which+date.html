Press Freedom Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) May 02

(B) ** May 03

(C) May 01

(D) May 04

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India celebrates 16th November as National Press Day every year and the day is observed in honor of the Press Council of India. The day is meant to mark the presence of the free and responsible press in India.

Concept note-2: -The theme of National Press Day 2022 is “The Media’s Role is Nation Building”.

Concept note-3: -National Press Day - the 16th of November – is symbolic of a free and responsible press in India.

Concept note-4: -According to the World Press Freedom Index, released in May, India’s ranking dropped to 150th position from last year’s 142nd rank among 180 nations.
