Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated India’s first earthquake memorial in which state?

(A) Uttarakhand

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) ** Gujarat

(D) Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Smritivan Earthquake Memorial and Museum is a memorial park dedicated to the victims of 2001 Gujarat earthquake and museum on Bhujiyo Hill in Bhuj, Kutch district, Gujarat, India. The Museum has been designed and curated by Noida based firm Design Factory India.

Concept note-2: -NEW DELHI : Prime minister Narendra Modi on Sunday inaugurated and laid the foundation stone of projects worth around ₹4,400 crore in Bhuj. He inaugurated Smriti Van Memorial in Bhuj district and the Kachchh Branch Canal of Sardar Sarovar Project.
