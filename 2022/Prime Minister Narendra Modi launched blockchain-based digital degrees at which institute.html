Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched blockchain-based digital degrees at which institute?

(A) IIT Roorkee

(B) IIT Bombay

(C) IIT Delhi

(D) ** IIT Kanpur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India’s leading technical and research institution, IIT Kanpur,recently adopted a blockchain based system to award digital degrees to its studentsunder the National Blockchain Project. The system was launched by Narendra Modi,Prime Minister of India.

Concept note-2: IIT-Kanpur, in 2021, became the firstinstitute in India to launch a system to award blockchain-based educational degreesin digital form to students.

Concept note-3: Introducing Our New BlockchainCertification Program in Partnership With IIT Kanpur.

Concept note-4: Asopposed to the lengthy process of emailing the university to authenticate a degree,with blockchain technology, a One Time Password (OTP) is sent to the mobilenumber and email of the concerned student. Once the OTP is shared with theemployer, they can access the original degree digitally.

Concept note-5: DetailedSolution. The correct answer is IIT Kanpur. Prime Minister Narendra Modi launcheda blockchain-powered technology-based digital degree developed by IIT Kanpurunder the National Blockchain Project in December 2021 on the occasion of the 54thConvocation Ceremony of IIT Kanpur.
