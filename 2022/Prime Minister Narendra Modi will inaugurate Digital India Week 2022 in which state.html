Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate Digital India Week 2022 in which state?

(A) ** Gujarat

(B) Karnataka

(C) Odisha

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thousands visit Digital Mela at Gandhinagar, Gujarat to explore tech enabled innovations showcased at the Digital Mela. Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated the Digital India Week, 2022 on July 4.

Concept note-2: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated the Digital India Week 2022, whose theme is ‘Catalyzing New India’s Techade’ in Gandhinagar today.

Concept note-3: -The Digital India Week 2022 with the theme ‘Catalyzing New India’s Techade’ kicked off on 4th July at Gandhinagar, Gujarat. The July 4th to 9th weeklong event commemorated the path-breaking journey of the flagship initiative of the Digital India Programme that was inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.
