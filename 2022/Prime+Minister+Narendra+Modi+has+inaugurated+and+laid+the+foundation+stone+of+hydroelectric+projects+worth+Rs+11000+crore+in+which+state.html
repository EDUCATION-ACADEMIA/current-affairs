Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated and laid the foundation stone of hydroelectric projects worth Rs 11000 crore in which state?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Himachal Pradesh

(C) Kerala

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated and laid the foundation of the hydropower projects worth Rs 11000 crore in Mandi, Himachal Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone of two hydropower projects and launched Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)-III in Chamba, Himachal Pradesh today.

Concept note-3: -Which is the largest hydroelectric power plant in India? The Koyna Hydroelectric Project is the largest hydroelectric power plant in India. It is a complex project with four dams including the largest dam on the Koyna River, Maharashtra hence the name Koyna Hydroelectric Project.

Concept note-4: -Commissioned in 1932, the Shanan hydroelectric plant was constructed under a 99-year lease deed executed between Jogendra Sen, the then Mandi ruler, and Col. B.C. Batty, the then Chief Engineer of the Punjab government, in 1925.

Concept note-5: -Shivanasamudra is a small town in the Mandya District of the state of Karnataka, situated on the bank of the river kaveri. It was the first hydro-electric power station in Asia which was set up in the year 1902.
