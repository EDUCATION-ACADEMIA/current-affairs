Professor Wilfried Brutsaert has been awarded the Stockholm Water Prize 2022 by which of the institutions?

(A) World Department of the Environment

(B) Serum Institute of India

(C) ** Stockholm International Water Institute

(D) World bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2022 Stockholm Water Prize has been awarded to Wilfriend Brutsaert, a professor at Cornell University and a leading authority on terrestrial evaporation.

Concept note-2: -Professor Emeritus Wilfried Brutsaert is named Stockholm Water Prize Laureate 2022 for groundbreaking work to quantify environmental evaporation.

Concept note-3: -2022: Wilfried Brutsaert, for his groundbreaking work to quantify environmental evaporation, helping to make accurate predictions of the impact that climate change has on local rainfall patterns and water sources.

Concept note-4: -UGent alumnus Wilfried Brutsaert awarded the Stockholm Water Prize 2022 Wilfried Brutsaert, em. prof. at Cornell University, renowned hydrologist and alumnus of the Faculty of Bioscience Engineering is awarded the prestigious Stockholm Water Prize for his innovative work on evaporation and groundwater storage.

Concept note-5: -The Prize is awarded by SIWI in cooperation with the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences and presented by H.M King Carl XVI Gustaf of Sweden, who is the official patron of the Prize. The winner of the Stockholm Water Prize is announced every year usually in March in conjunction with World Water Day.
