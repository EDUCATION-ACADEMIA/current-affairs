Qimingxing-50 is the name of the first fully solar-powered UAV of which country?

(A) South Korea

(B) ** China

(C) Japan

(D) Israel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Qimingxing-50: China’s First Fully Solar-poweredUnmanned Aerial Vehicle(UAV) China has successfully tested its first fullysolar-powered unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) which can fly for months and canfunction even as a satellite if required.

Concept note-2: The maiden flightof Qimingxing-50 has been achieved, making it the first large-sized UAVpowered only by solar energy, a Chinese government official informed in atweet.

Concept note-3: The Wing Loong-3 UAV (unmanned aerial vehicle),which also features a stronger strike capability, made its debut at the 14th ChinaInternational Aviation and Aerospace Exhibition in Zhuhai in Guangdong provinceon Tuesday.

Concept note-4: ’QIMINGXING 50’ is China’s first fullysolar-powered unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) that has successfully completedits maiden test flight. It is the first large-sized UAV powered only by solarenergy.

Concept note-5: Solar-powered unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) is definedas drones that use solar cells powered directly from the sun to power its operations.In solar-powered unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), the solar cells are installed on theUAVs.
