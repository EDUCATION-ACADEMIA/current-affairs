RBI has imposed a penalty of Rs 59 lakh on KEB Hana Bank for non-compliance with the certain norms related to what?

(A) Capital Adequacy Ratio

(B) ** Interest rate on deposits

(C) KYC norms

(D) Priority sector lending

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: RBI on Friday said it has imposed a penalty of Rs 59 lakh onKEB Hana Bank for non-compliance with the certain norms related to ‘interest rateon deposits’.

Concept note-2: Reserve Bank of India has imposed a penalty of Rs59 lakh on the Korean bank, KEB Hana Bank.

Concept note-3: Press Releases.The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has, by an order dated September 07,2022, imposed a monetary penalty of ₹36 lakh (Rupees Thirty-six lakh only)on Industrial Bank of Korea (the bank) for non-compliance with certainprovisions of the ‘Reserve Bank of India – (Know Your Customer (KYC))Direction, 2016’.

Concept note-4: This penalty has been imposed inexercise of powers vested in RBI under the provisions of section 47 A (1)(c) read with sections 46 (4) (i) of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (theAct).

Concept note-5: RBI imposes monetary penalty on Indian BankThisaction is based on the deficiencies in regulatory compliance and is not intended topronounce upon the validity of any transaction or agreement entered into by thebank with its customers.
