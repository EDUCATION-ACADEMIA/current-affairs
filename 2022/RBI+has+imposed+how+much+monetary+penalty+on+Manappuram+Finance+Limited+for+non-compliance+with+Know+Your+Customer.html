RBI has imposed how much monetary penalty on Manappuram Finance Limited for non-compliance with Know Your Customer?

(A) ** Rs 17.63 lakh

(B) Rs 10.50 lakh

(C) Rs 90 lakh

(D) Rs 52.56 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -rbi: RBI imposes Rs 17 lakh fine on Manappuram Finance for non-compliance of KYC, PPI rules - The Economic Times.

Concept note-2: -Press Releases. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has, by an order dated November 16, 2021, imposed a monetary penalty of ₹1.00 Crore (Rupees One Crore only) on State Bank of India (the bank) for contravention of sub-section (2) of section 19 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (the Act).

Concept note-3: -RBI imposes monetary penalty on Indian Bank This action is based on the deficiencies in regulatory compliance and is not intended to pronounce upon the validity of any transaction or agreement entered into by the bank with its customers.

Concept note-4: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has, by an order dated October 17, 2022, imposed a monetary penalty of ₹6 lakh (Rupees Six lakh only) on The Pratap Co-operative Bank Ltd., Mumbai (the bank) for non-compliance with certain provisions of the ‘Reserve Bank of India – (Know Your Customer (KYC)) Direction, 2016’.
