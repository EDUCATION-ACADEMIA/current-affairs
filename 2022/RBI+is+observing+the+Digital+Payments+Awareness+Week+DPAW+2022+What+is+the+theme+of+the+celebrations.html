RBI is observing the Digital Payments Awareness Week (DPAW) 2022. What is the theme of the celebrations?

(A) Unified Payments Interface

(B) Smarter Digital Payments

(C) ** Outreach and Availability of Digital Payments

(D) Go Digital, Go Secure

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: The theme of Digital Payments Awareness Week (DPAW) 2022 is: “Outreach and Availability of Digital Payments".

Concept note-2: -"Here comes the sun-monetary policy."

Concept note-3: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has been publishing a composite Reserve Bank of India – Digital Payments Index (RBI-DPI) since January 1, 2021 with March 2018 as base to capture the extent of digitisation of payments across the country. The index for September 2022 stands at 377.46 as against 349.30 for March 2022.

Concept note-4: -The Reserve Bank of India’s composite Digital Payments Index (RBI-DPI) has risen to 349.30 in March 2022 against 270.59 in March 2021, indicating rapid adoption and deepening of digital payments across the country.

Concept note-5: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has come out with “Payments Vision 2025” with an objective to provide every user with safe, secure, fast, convenient, accessible and affordable e-payment options.
