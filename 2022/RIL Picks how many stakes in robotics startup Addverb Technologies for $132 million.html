RIL Picks how many stakes in robotics startup Addverb Technologies for $132 million?

(A) 65%

(B) 50%

(C) ** 54%

(D) 45%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Reliance Retail Ventures Ltd, the retail unit of RelianceIndustries Ltd, has acquired 54% stake in Addverb Technologies for $132million ( ₹983 crore approximately).

Concept note-2: Founded in 2016 bySangeet Kumar, Prateek Jain, Bir Singh, Satish Kumar Shukla and AmitKumar, Addverb was recently in news after Reliance acquired 54% stake inthe company for $132 Mn. Addverb’s proprietary warehouse robotics andautomation products are being used by retail and ecommerce giants around theworld.

Concept note-3: Addverb designs and builds robotics and warehouseautomation solutions for clients and the sale of such products constitutes 90% of itsoperating revenue. This income surged 63.5% to Rs 282 crore in FY22 from Rs 172.5crore in FY21.

Concept note-4: How much does Addverb Technologies pay peryear? The average Addverb Technologies salary ranges from approximately₹1.2 Lakhs per year for a Electrician to ₹32.6 Lakhs per year for a ChiefManager.

Concept note-5: NEW DELHI: Reliance Retail Ventures Ltd-backedAddverb Technologies, a manufacturer of autonomous robots for intralogisticsoperations, is aiming to become a billion dollar company in revenues in next fiveyears and to achieve the same it is opening a new manufacturing facility to expandcapacity.
