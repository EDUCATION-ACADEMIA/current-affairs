RRR won the Best international movie:Saturn Awards, Japan. It is directed by ____ .

(A) Sanjay Leela Bansali

(B) Karan Johar

(C) ** SS Rajamouli

(D) Zoya Akhtar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: SS Rajamouli directed blockbuster RRR won the BestInternational Film award at the 50th Saturn Awards in Los Angeles onTuesday.

Concept note-2: RRR is Rajamouli’s second film to receive the SaturnAward. His magnum opus, Baahubali 2: The Conclusion, was the first. SSRajamouli’s period epic RRR was awarded the prestigious Saturn Award 2022 in theBest International Film category.

Concept note-3: In the late 1970s, the awardwas changed to be a representation of the planet Saturn, with its ring(s) composed offilm. The Saturn Awards are voted upon by members of the presenting Academy.The Academy is a non-profit organization with membership open to thepublic.

Concept note-4: It was produced by D. V. V. Danayya of DVVEntertainment. The film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr., Ram Charan, Ajay Devgn, AliaBhatt, Shriya Saran, Samuthirakani, Ray Stevenson, Alison Doody, and OliviaMorris.

Concept note-5: On January 17, the Seattle Film Critics associationannounced the awards for 2022. SS Rajamouli’s RRR was adjudged as the winner ofSeattle Critics Award for Best Action Choreography. Their post read, “TheSeattleCritics 2022 award for BEST ACTION CHOREOGRAPHY: ** RRR **(sic)."
