RRR won the Best international movie:Saturn Awards, Japan. It is directed by ____ .

(A) Sanjay Leela Bansali

(B) Karan Johar

(C) ** SS Rajamouli

(D) Zoya Akhtar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SS Rajamouli, who iss currently promoting the movie in Japan, received his award virtually. In a video message, the famed director thanked the jury and said the entire team was “super elated” at the honour. RRR is Rajamouli’s second movie to receive the Saturn Award; the first was Baahubali 2: The Conclusion .

Concept note-2: -In the late 1970s, the award was changed to be a representation of the planet Saturn, with its ring(s) composed of film. The Saturn Awards are voted upon by members of the presenting Academy. The Academy is a non-profit organization with membership open to the public.

Concept note-3: -It was produced by D. V. V. Danayya of DVV Entertainment. The film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr., Ram Charan, Ajay Devgn, Alia Bhatt, Shriya Saran, Samuthirakani, Ray Stevenson, Alison Doody, and Olivia Morris.

Concept note-4: -Days after its historic Golden Globe win, the Telugu movie RRR has won two more awards at a prominent awards show. The film won best foreign language film and Naatu Naatu won best song at the 28th edition of the Critics’ Choice Awards in Los Angeles.

Concept note-5: -On January 17, the Seattle Film Critics association announced the awards for 2022. SS Rajamouli’s RRR was adjudged as the winner of Seattle Critics Award for Best Action Choreography. Their post read, “The SeattleCritics 2022 award for BEST ACTION CHOREOGRAPHY: ** RRR ** (sic)."
