‘Raisina Dialogue’ is a Conference in which field?

(A) Information and Communication Technology

(B) ** Foreign policy

(C) Agriculture

(D) Climate Change

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Raisina Dialogue is a multilateral conference held annually in New Delhi, India. Since its inception in 2016, the conference has emerged as India’s flagship conference on geopolitics and geo-economics.

Concept note-2: -The three-day Dialogue is based on the theme - Terranova, impassioned, impatient, imperilled. There are six broad thematic pillars of the Dialogue including Rethinking Democracy, End of Multilateralism, Water Caucuses, Achieving Green Transitions.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the seventh edition of Raisina Dialogue on 25th of April. President of the European Union Ursula Von Der Leyen will be the chief guest at the Dialogue. Several former Prime Ministers of different countries and other dignitaries will also participate in this virtual event.

Concept note-4: -The term “Raisina Hill” was coined following acquisition of land from 300 families from local villages was acquired under the “1894 Land Acquisition Act” to begin the construction of the Viceroy’s House.

Concept note-5: -It was Sir Lutyens who conceptualized the H molded structure, covering a territory of 5 sections of land on a 330 section of land home.
