Recently who has been honoured with the prestigious ‘Friends of Liberation War’ by Bangladesh Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina?

(A) Jenny F. Bailey

(B) ** Edward M Kennedy

(C) Michael Phillips

(D) Thomas Miller

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Bangladesh PM Sheikh Hasina confers ‘Friends of LiberationWar’ honour on Edward M Kennedy. Bangladesh Prime Minister Sheikh Hasinaconferred the prestigious ‘Friends of Liberation War’ honour on former US SenatorEdward M Kennedy posthumously on Monday in Dhaka for his contribution to theliberation of Bangladesh.

Concept note-2: The war led to a substantial numberof refugees (estimated at the time to be about 10 million) flooding into the easternprovinces of India. Facing a mounting humanitarian and economic crisis, Indiastarted actively aiding and organising the Bangladeshi resistance army known as theMukti Bahini.

Concept note-3: She previously served as prime minister fromJune 1996 to July 2001. She is the longest serving prime minister in the history ofBangladesh, having served for a combined total of over 19 years. As of 26January 2023, she is the world’s longest-serving female Head of Government inhistory.
