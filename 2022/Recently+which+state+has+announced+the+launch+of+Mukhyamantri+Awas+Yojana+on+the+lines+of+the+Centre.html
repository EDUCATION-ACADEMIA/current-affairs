Recently which state has announced the launch of Mukhyamantri Awas Yojana on the lines of the Centre?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Haryana

(C) Odisha

(D) Sikkim

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) Mission launched on 25th June 2015 which intends to provide housing for all in urban areas by year 2022.

Concept note-2: -"Indira Awas Yojana” (IAY) was launched by Rajiv Gandhi, the then Prime Minister of India in 1985 and was restructured as “Pradhan Mantri Gramin Awas Yojana” (PMGAY) in 2015.
