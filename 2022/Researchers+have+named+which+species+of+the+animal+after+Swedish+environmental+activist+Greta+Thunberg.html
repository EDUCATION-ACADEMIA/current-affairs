Researchers have named which species of the animal after Swedish environmental activist Greta Thunberg?

(A) Monarch butterfly

(B) Indian cobra

(C) Whale shark

(D) ** Rain frog

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A new species of rainfrog , discovered in the Panama jungle, has been named after Swedish environmental activist Greta Thunberg. The species has been named as Pristimantis gretathunbergae, or popularly known as the Greta Thunberg Rainfrog.

Concept note-2: -Pristimantis gretathunbergae, also known as Greta Thunberg’s rainfrog, is a species of frog in the family Strabomantidae, native to Panama. It is named in honor of climate activist Greta Thunberg.

Concept note-3: -New Species of Rainfrog Discovered in Panama and Named in Honor of Global Environmental Activist Greta Thunberg.

Concept note-4: -Greta Tintin Eleonora Ernman Thunberg FRSGS (Swedish: [ˈɡrêːta ˈtʉ̂ːnbærj] ( listen); born 3 January 2003) is a Swedish environmental activist who is known for challenging world leaders to take immediate action for climate change mitigation.

Concept note-5: -She has inspired many school children to pay attention to climate and environmental issues and the climate strike movement shows the power that lies in each and every one of us when we relentlessly dedicate ourselves to positive change.
