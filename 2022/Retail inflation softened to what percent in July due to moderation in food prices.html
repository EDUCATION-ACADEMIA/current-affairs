Retail inflation softened to what percent in July due to moderation in food prices?

(A) ** 6.71%

(B) 7.37%

(C) 8.02%

(D) 9.14%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Retail inflation softened to 6.71% in July due to moderation infood prices but remained above the Reserve Bank’s comfort level of 6% for theseventh consecutive month.

Concept note-2: The number has remained aboveRBI’s tolerance band of 2-6 percent for the eighth consecutive month. Sequentially,the headline inflation grew by 0.52 percent from 0.46 percent in July. Foodinflation in August 2022 came in at 7.62 percent as against 6.75 percent in thepreceding month.

Concept note-3: CPI Inflation Rate August, IIP GrowthRate July 2022: Retail inflation spikes to 7% in August, IIP grows 2.4% inJuly.

Concept note-4: NEW DELHI: India’s retail inflation based on consumerprice index (CPI) eased to 6.71% in July, data released by the government showed onFriday. The inflation numbers in July is significantly lower than the 8-year high of7.79% touched in April. This is the 3nd straight month when inflation figures havecooled.

Concept note-5: India’s retail inflation eases to within RBI target butindustrial activity contracts. The govt has mandated the RBI to maintain retailinflation at 4% with a margin of 2% on either side for a five-year period endingMarch 2026.
