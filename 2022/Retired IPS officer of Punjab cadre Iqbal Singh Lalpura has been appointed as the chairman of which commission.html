Retired IPS officer of Punjab cadre Iqbal Singh Lalpura has been appointed as the chairman of which commission?

(A) Education Commission

(B) ** National Commission for Minorities

(C) NITI Aayog

(D) Planning Commission

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Former IPS officer Iqbal Singh Lalpura, who joined BJP in2012 and was chosen as chairman of National Commission for Minorities, has beenfacing criticism with Harjinder Singh Dhami, who was re-elected as the chief ofShiromani Gurdwara Parbandhak Committee (SGPC), launching an offensive againsthim.

Concept note-2: NCM diposes 317 petitions ; holds hearing for 13 casesduring the period from 30th June to 20th September, 2022. The monthly meeting ofthe National Commission for Minorities was held today under the chairmanship ofShri Iqbal Singh Lalpura, Chairman.

Concept note-3: Iqbal Singh Lalpura hasassumed charge as Chairperson of the National Commission for Minorities, NCM, inNew Delhi today after being re-nominated as Chairperson vide Ministry of MinorityAffairs Notification dated 12.4. 2022.

Concept note-4: Iqbal Singh Kingra (1May 1926 – 29 January 2022) was an Indian socio-spiritual leader of the Sikhcommunity. He was the Founder President of The Kalgidhar Trust, The KalgidharSociety and Baru Sahib. He was considered to be one of the most influential Sikhs inthe world.
