S & P cuts India’s growth forecast to how much percent for FY23?

(A) 6%

(B) ** 7%

(C) 8%

(D) 9%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: S&P cuts India’s economic growth to 7% for FY23 | BusinessNews,The Indian Express.

Concept note-2: Pegging India’s gross domesticproduct (GDP) growth at seven per cent for 2022 and six per cent for 2023, globalcredit rating agency S&P Global Ratings said the forecast has been lowered by 0.5per cent for next two fiscal years.

Concept note-3: The consequent depreciationof other currencies has been widening the CAD and increasing inflationary pressuresin the net importing economies. India to witness GDP growth of 6.0 per cent to 6.8per cent in 2023-24, depending on the trajectory of economic and politicaldevelopments globally.

Concept note-4: India’s GDP to grow at 6.9%in FY23 & will remain one of the fastest-growing major economies, saysWorld Bank.

Concept note-5: S&P Global Ratings has revised India’sreal GDP growth for next two fiscal years by 0.5 percentage points, citingslowdown in global demand. GDP for 2022 and 2023 is projected at sevenand six per cent, respectively. As per S&P, regional growth remains healthyoverall.
