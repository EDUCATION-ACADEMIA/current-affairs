SBI has partnered with which company for financing solar power projects through its dedicated centralized processing cell named ‘Surya Shakti Cell’?

(A) Jackson Group

(B) Moser Baer Solar

(C) Photon Energy Systems

(D) ** Tata Power Solar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With an aim to strengthen the existing financing arrangement for solar power projects, the country’s largest lender State Bank of India (SBI) has launched a dedicated centralized processing cell - ‘Surya Shakti Cell’. The Bank has entered into an Agreement with Tata Power Solar Systems Ltd.

Concept note-2: -State Bank of India has launched a dedicated centralized processing cell - Surya Shakti Cell in collaboration with Tata Power Solar Systems.

Concept note-3: -One of India’s oldest and largest public sector banks, Union Bank of India (UBI) has partnered with Tata Power Solar Systems Limited (TPSSL) to support the MSME (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises) sector’s transition to solar energy.

Concept note-4: -The Surya Shakti Cell will process all the loan applications for Solar Projects (capacity up to 1 MW) sourced from across India, for installation by business entities as well as households.

Concept note-5: -Tata Power and SIDBI partner to launch affordable financing for MSMEs in rooftop solar segment - Small Industries Development Bank of India.
