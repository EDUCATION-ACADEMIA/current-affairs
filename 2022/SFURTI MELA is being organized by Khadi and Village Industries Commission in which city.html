SFURTI MELA is being organized by Khadi and Village Industries Commission in which city?

(A) Patna

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Jaipur

(D) Sonipat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Union Minister of State for Ministry of MSME, Shri BhanuMinistry of MSME and KVIC organized a SFURTI Mela at Dilli Haat, INA, NewDelhi from 1st October to 15th October, 2022 to promote the traditional productsmade by artisan across the country. Pratap Singh Verma, visited the SFURTI Melaat INA Dilli Haat on 04.10.

Concept note-2: The Khadii and Village IndustriesCommission (KVIC) is a statutory body formed in April 1957 by the Government ofIndia, under the Act of Parliament, ‘Khadi and Village Industries Commission Act of1956’.

Concept note-3: Khadi and Village Industries Commission is anon-constitutional body which is incorporated under the Parliament Act, passed bythe Government of India. KVIC aims to plan, promote, facilitate, organize, and assistin the establishment and development of Khadi and Village industries in the ruralareas.

Concept note-4: The Revamped SFURTI scheme was startedon 1st August 2014 with an outlay of Rs. 149.44 crore for developing 71clusters (including coir) with coverage of 44500 artisans (approx.) in the firstphase.

Concept note-5: KVIC (Khadi Village Industries Commission)was set up during the second five year plan in 1956. It was formed underthe KVIC Act of 1956, this is a statutory body formed by the Governmentof India. It seeks to promote, plan, protect and develop khadi and VillageIndustries.
