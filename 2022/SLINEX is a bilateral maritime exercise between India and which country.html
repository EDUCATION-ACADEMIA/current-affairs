‘SLINEX’ is a bilateral maritime exercise between India and which country?

(A) ** Sri Lanka

(B) Singapore

(C) Oman

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SLINEX (Sri Lanka India Naval Exercise) are a series of naval exercises between the Indian Navy and the Sri Lanka Navy.

Concept note-2: -SLINEX (Sri Lanka–India Naval Exercise) is being conducted in two phases.

Concept note-3: -The Ninth Edition of India - Sri Lanka Bilateral Maritime Exercise SLINEX (Sri Lanka–India Naval Exercise) is being conducted at Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-4: -The Singapore Army’s Singapore Light Weight Howitzers (SLWH) firing off during a joint live-firing with the Indian Army. The Singapore Army and the Indian Army successfully concluded Exercise Agni Warrior (XAW) 2022, in Devlali, India, from 13 November to 30 November 2022.

Concept note-5: -The 9th edition of India – Sri Lanka Bilateral Maritime Exercise named SLINEX (Sri Lanka–India Naval Exercise) is held at Visakhapatnam.
