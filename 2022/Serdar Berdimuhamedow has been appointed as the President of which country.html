Serdar Berdimuhamedow has been appointed as the President of which country?

(A) Armenia

(B) Azerbaijan

(C) ** Turkmenistan

(D) Kyrgyzstan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Serdar Gurbangulyýewiç Berdimuhamedow (Turkmenpronunciation: [ɛɾdɑɾ ɢʊɾbɑnʁʊˈlɯjɛɪtʃ bɛɾdɯmʊxɑmɛˈdo]; born 22 September1981) is the third and current president of Turkmenistan, serving since 19 March2022.

Concept note-2: Results. The Central Election Committee reported on 15March that Serdar Berdimuhamedow won the election with 72.97% of thevote.

Concept note-3: Saparmurat Atayevich Niyazov (19 February 1940 – 21December 2006), also known as Turkmen: Türkmenbaşy, Russian: , meaning “head ofthe Turkmen", was a Turkmen politician who ruled Turkmenistan from 1985 untilhis death in 2006 as a dictator.

Concept note-4: Government. Afterover a century of being a part of the Russian Empire and then the SovietUnion (including 67 years as a union republic), Turkmenistan declared itsindependence on 27 October 1991, following the dissolution of the SovietUnion.
