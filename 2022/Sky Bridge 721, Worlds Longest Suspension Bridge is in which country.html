‘Sky Bridge 721’, World’s Longest Suspension Bridge is in which country?

(A) Poland

(B) ** Czechia

(C) Austria

(D) Slovakia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Built in the municipality of Dolní Morava, Czech Republic, thebridge is 721 metres long and is at a height of up to 95 metres off the ground. It firstopened to the general public on 13 May 2022.

Concept note-2: The 1915Çanakkale Bridge of Turkey, opened in 2022, has the longest central span (2,023 m)of any suspension bridge.

Concept note-3: The correct answer is CzechRepublic. The world’s longest suspension bridge named “Sky Bridge 721” hasbeen opened for tourists in the Czech Republic. The bridge connects twomountain ridges and hangs 95 metres (312 feet) above a valley and can beaccessed by cable car.

Concept note-4: After two years of effort, SkyBridge 721 opens an unprecedented path between heaven and earth. With alength of 721 meters, it takes the lead and becomes the longest suspensionfootbridge in the world. It offers visitors enchanting views surrounded by purenature in the Králský Sněžník mountains.

Concept note-5: (CNN) - Theworld’s longest timber-towered suspension bridge is now open to the publicin Boyne Falls, Michigan. At roughly 1,200 feet long, SkyBridge Michiganat Boyne Mountain provides more than 1,000 feet of suspended walkingsurface.
