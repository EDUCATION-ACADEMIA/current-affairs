South Africa is to chair which BRICS summit in 2023?

(A) 12th

(B) 13th

(C) 14th

(D) ** 15th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The presidency rotates annually in accordance withthe position of the name of each country in the acronym: Russia held thechair in 2020 after Brazil, while India has assumed the presidency for thisyear and will hand it over to China in 2022, followed by South Africa in2023.

Concept note-2: South Africa to host 15th BRICS summit at Durban inlate August 2023 | Business Standard News.

Concept note-3: South Africa,which is presiding over Brics this year, will host the summit for the bloc duringthe second half of the year, the Department of International Relations andCooperation has confirmed.

Concept note-4: South Africa’s Durban is setto host the 15th BRICS Summit in August this year. South Africa tookover the presidency of the five-nation bloc from China on January 1 thisyear.
