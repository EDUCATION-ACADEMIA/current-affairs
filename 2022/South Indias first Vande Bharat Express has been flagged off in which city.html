South India’s first Vande Bharat Express has been flagged off in which city?

(A) ** Bangalore

(B) Chennai

(C) Mysore

(D) Vellore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Darshan Devaiah B.P. Prime Minister Narendra Modi flaggedoff two much-awaited trains from Krantiveera Sangolli Rayanna Railway Station inBengaluru on November 11 - south India’s first Vande Bharat Express betweenMysuru and Chennai via Bengaluru, and Bharat Gaurav Kashi Darshantrain.

Concept note-2: The nation’s seventh Vande Bharat express train betweenHowrah-New Jalpaiguri (NJP) has been flagged off through video conferencing by thePrime Minister minutes after the last rite of his mother Hiraben Modi. Moditook part in the flag-off from Gujarat as the train left Howrah station inBengal.

Concept note-3: Indian Railways launched south India’s first VandeBharat Express train on the Chennai-Bengaluru-Musuru route in November last year.The Secunderabad-Vizag Vande Bharat Express, the second Vande Bharat ofSouth India, was launched on January 14 this year.

Concept note-4: Therailways launched south India’s first Vande Bharat Express train on theChennai-Bengaluru-Mysure route in November this year. Prime Minister NarendraModi flagged off the fifth rake of Vande Bharat Express at the Krantiveera SangolliRailway station in Bengaluru.

Concept note-5: Mumbai gets its first VandeBharat on September 30, 2022. The train operates between Gandhinagar Capital andMumbai Central.
