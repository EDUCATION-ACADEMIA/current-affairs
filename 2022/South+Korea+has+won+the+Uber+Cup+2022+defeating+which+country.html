South Korea has won the Uber Cup 2022 defeating which country?

(A) Singapore

(B) Japan

(C) Indonesia

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Badminton BWF Uber Cup Final 2022 - South Korea beat China 3-2, re-live all the action as it happened. South Korea regain the Uber Cup after 12 years with an historic comeback to defeat the defending champions China at the Impact Arena in Bangkok, Thailand. Re-live all the action as it happened.

Concept note-2: -The men’s event finals was between Indonesia and India. India won 3–0 for their first title. The women’s event finals was between China and South Korea in which the South Korean team won 3–2 for their second title.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which country won the Uber Cup title in 2022? Notes: South Korea defeated the defending champions China to win the prestigious Uber Cup championship, held in Thailand Capital Bangkok. South Korean women team ended their 12-year wait for an Uber Cup title while stopping China to clinch their 16th title.

Concept note-4: -South Korea are the current champions, having won its second title after beating title holders China in the 2022 edition.

Concept note-5: -46 Sim Yu Jin survived a tense decider against world No. 15 Wang Zhi Yi to seal the win for South Korea. This is Korea’s second world women’s team title. The last time a Uber Cup final had gone to the fifth match was in 1994, and in the final too, China had ended runners-up against Indonesia.
