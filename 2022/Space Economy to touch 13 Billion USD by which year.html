Space Economy to touch 13 Billion USD by which year?

(A) ** 2025

(B) 2023

(C) 2022

(D) 2035

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Indian space economy is set to reach $13 billion by 2025,according to a joint report prepared by EY and the Indian Space Association(ISpA), an apex industry association of space and satellite companies in thecountry.

Concept note-2: 18 January 2023: EY, the leading professional servicesorganization, today announced its growth projection for India that estimates Indianeconomy will reach GDP size of US$26 trillion (in market exchange terms) by 2047,the 100th year of the country’s independence.

Concept note-3: The Indian SpaceAssociation (ISpA) and Ernst & Young released a report estimating that India’sspace industry will be valued roughly at US$ 13 billion by 2025, with the satellitelaunch services market expected to develop at the quickest rate due to risingprivate engagement.

Concept note-4: The Space Economy report. Credit:Euroconsult. According to the annual report by Euroconsult on the global spaceeconomy, the value reached an all time high of US$424 billion, a 14.1% increaseover the previous year.

Concept note-5: India contributed 2.1% to theglobal space industry economy in 2020, amounting to US$ 9.6 billion, with acontribution of 0.4% to the country’s gross domestic product (GDP). They areresponsible for launching rockets and spacecraft that study the Earth and theuniverse.
