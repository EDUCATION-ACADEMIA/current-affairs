State Bank of India (SBI) will set up an Innovation, Incubation and Acceleration Centre (IIAC) at which place?

(A) Mumbai

(B) ** Hyderabad

(C) Bengaluru

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abhishek Mishra. Answer: Option B Explanation: The State Bank of India (SBI) will set up an Innovation, Incubation and Acceleration Centre (IIAC) at Hyderabad, Telangana which will be operational in six to nine months of on-boarding a consultant.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Hyderabad. State Bank of India (SBI) has planned set up an Innovation, Incubation and Acceleration Centre (IIAC) in Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: -State Bank of India (SBI) plans to set up an Innovation, Incubation and Acceleration Centre (IIAC) at Hyderabad in a bid to build in-house capability to bolster its current performance and drive higher top-line growth through innovation. The centre will be operational in six to nine months of on-boarding a consultant.
