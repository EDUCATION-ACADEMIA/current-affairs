Successful Flight Tests of Very Short-Range Air Defence System (VSHORADS) Missile was done by which organisation?

(A) ISRO

(B) ** DRDO

(C) CSIR

(D) BARC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Successful Flight Tests of Very Short Range Air Defence System (VSHORADS) Missile by DRDO | Ministry of Defence.

Concept note-2: -DRDO conducted two successful test flight of Very Short Range Air Defence System (VSHORADS) missile on 27 Sep 2022 from a ground based portable launcher at the Integrated Test Range, Chandipur, off the coast of Odisha.

Concept note-3: -Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO) conducted a successful maiden flight-test of Phase-II Ballistic Missile Defence (BMD) interceptor AD-1 missile with large kill altitude bracket from APJ Abdul Kalam Island off the coast of Odisha on November 02, 2022.

Concept note-4: -VSHORADS is a Indian man-portable air-defense system (MANPADS) designed by the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO). It designed for anti-aircraft warfare and neutralizing low altitude aerial threats at short ranges.

Concept note-5: -They have a range of 2400 meters.
