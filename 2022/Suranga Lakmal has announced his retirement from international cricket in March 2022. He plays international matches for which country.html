Suranga Lakmal has announced his retirement from international cricket in March 2022. He plays international matches for which country?

(A) South Africa

(B) ** Sri Lanka

(C) Zimbabwe

(D) West Indies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is Sri Lanka. Former Sri LankaTest captain Suranga Lakmal has announced retirement from internationalcricket.

Concept note-2: On 2 February 2022, Lakmal announced that he wouldretire from all forms of international cricket following Sri Lanka’s tour ofIndia.

Concept note-3: The Event Technical Committee of the ICC Men’s T20World Cup 2022 has approved Binura Fernando as a replacement for left arm fastbowler Dilshan Madushanka in the Sri Lanka squad. Fernando who has playednine T20Is, was named as a replacement after Madushanka was ruled outdue to a torn quad muscle.
