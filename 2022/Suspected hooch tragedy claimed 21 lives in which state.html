Suspected hooch tragedy claimed 21 lives in which state?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Uttarakhand

(C) ** Bihar

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Meanwhile death toll in hooch tragedy in Saran district ofBihar has risen to 40 as six more people have died.

Concept note-2: Over 20killed in Bihar: Why can hooch be so deadly? Over 30 people have died in a hoochtragedy in the Saran district of Bihar, where alcohol is prohibited. The incident hascreated a political furore, with Chief Minister Nitish Kumar drawing charges ofinsensitivity over his reaction to the tragedy.

Concept note-3: According to datapublished by the National Crime Records Bureau, deaths due consumption of illicitliquor declined from 1,510 in 2017 to 947 in 2020. In 2018 and 2019, they were seenat 1,365 and 1,296, respectively.

Concept note-4: The 2016 Bihar alcoholpoisonings killed 16 people on 16 August 2016 in Gopalganj town of Bihar, India.The victims had consumed hooch (spurious liquor) and complained of nausea andstomach ache.

Concept note-5: Six villagers have died in a suspected hoochtragedy in Saran district in dry Bihar, the district administration said on Wednesday.However, villagers told mediapersons that at least 20 people had died in severalvillages.
