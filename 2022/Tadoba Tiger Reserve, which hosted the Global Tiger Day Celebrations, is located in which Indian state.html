Tadoba Tiger Reserve, which hosted the Global Tiger Day Celebrations, is located in which Indian state?

(A) Andhra Pradesh

(B) Gujarat

(C) Sikkim

(D) ** Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Tadoba Tiger Reserve, Maharashtra plays host for NationalGlobal Tiger Day Celebrations 2022.

Concept note-2: Tadoba-Andhari TigerReserve is a tiger reserve in Chandrapur District, Maharashtra, India. It includesTadoba National Park and Andhari Wildlife Sanctuary. Tadoba Andhari TigerReserve is Maharashtra’s oldest National Park created in 1955, . It is also one ofIndia’s 28 Project Tiger Reserve.

Concept note-3: The Tadoba Andhari TigerReserve is a wildlife sanctuary in Chandrapur district of Maharashtra state inIndia. It is Maharashtra’s oldest and largest national park. Created in 1955,the reserve includes the Tadoba National Park and the Andhari WildlifeSanctuary.

Concept note-4: Ans. The combined area of Guru Ghasidas NationalPark (Sanjay National Park) and Tamor Pingla Wildlife Sanctuary of Chhattisgarhhave been designated as India’s 53rd Tiger Reserve by the National TigerConservation Authority.
