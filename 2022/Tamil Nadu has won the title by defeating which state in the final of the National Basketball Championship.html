Tamil Nadu has won the title by defeating which state in the final of the National Basketball Championship?

(A) Haryana

(B) Rajasthan

(C) ** Punjab

(D) Uttrakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which team won the men’s’ title in the 71st Senior National Basketball Championship? Notes: Tamil Nadu defeated Punjab in the men’s final of the 71st Senior National Basketball Championship. While the Indian Railways team won the women’s title, beating Telangana.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: Tamil Nadu defeated defending champions Punjab 87-69 to clinch the men’s title in the final of the 71st Senior National Basketball Championship here on Sunday.

Concept note-3: -Punjab dethroned defending champions Tamil Nadu to lift the winners’ trophy in the men’s section in the 72nd Senior National Basketball Championship, held between November 27 and December 4 at Udaipur in Rajasthan.

Concept note-4: -Men: Final: Tamil Nadu 87 (A Aravind 26, M Arvind Kumar 21, P Jeevanantham 14, Muin Bek 12) beat Punjab 69 (Amjyot Singh 33, Amritpal Singh 12). 3rd-4th place: Karnataka 96 (Abhishek Gowda 29, Shashank J Rai 28, Anil Kumar 25) beat Indian Railways 79 (Sahil 15, Kamran Khan 15).
