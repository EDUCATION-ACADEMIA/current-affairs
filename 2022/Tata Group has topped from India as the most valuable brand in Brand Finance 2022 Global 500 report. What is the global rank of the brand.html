Tata Group has topped from India as the most valuable brand in Brand Finance 2022 Global 500 report. What is the global rank of the brand?

(A) 65

(B) 81

(C) ** 78

(D) 97

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Top 100 Most Valuable Brands in 2022: Annual Global500, 2022, has come up with its annual global list comprising of the mostvaluable brands across the globe. Only Tata Group managed to feature inthis illustrious list as the only Indian company to be ranked at 77thposition.

Concept note-2: Tech downturn slashes billions from value of world’smost valuable brands. Amazon has retaken top spot as the world’s mostvaluable brand despite its brand value falling 15% this year from US$350.3billion to US$299.3 billion, according to a new report from leading brandvaluation consultancy, Brand Finance.

Concept note-3: Brand India hasshown its strength in Brand Finance’s Global Soft Power Index 2022 studyretaining its 7th rank.

Concept note-4: With an increase of 35% sincelast year’s report, Apple retains its top spot on the ranking as the world’smost valuable brand, with a total brand value of $335.1 billion. This is thehighest brand value ever recorded in the history of the Global 500 report,which has been published each year since 2007.

Concept note-5: TheIndian multinational conglomerate Tata had a brand value worth over 23billion U.S. dollars in 2022, securing the first position as India’s most valuablebrand.
