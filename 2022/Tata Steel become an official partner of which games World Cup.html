Tata Steel become an official partner of which game’s World Cup?

(A) Football

(B) Badminton

(C) ** Hockey

(D) Cricket

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Tata Steel becomes an Official Partner of the FIH OdishaHockey Men’s World Cup 2023 Bhubaneswar - Rourkela. Tata Steel Limited signed aMemorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Hockey India on December 13, 2022 tobecome an Official Partner of the FIH Odisha Hockey Men’s World Cup 2023Bhubaneswar - Rourkela.

Concept note-2: The International Hockey Federation(FIH) has formed a partnership with The JSW Group for the upcoming FIH OdishaHockey Men’s World Cup 2023 Bhubaneswar-Rourkela.

Concept note-3: TheInternational Hockey Federation (FIH) has signed a domestic broadcast partnershipwith digital sports platform FanCode for the 2023 men’s FIH Hockey World Cup inIndia.

Concept note-4: The 15th edition of the Hockey Men’s World Cup willtake place in Bhubaneswar-Rourkela from January 13 to January 29, 2023. TataSteel Limited has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) withHockey India to become an Official Partner of the FIH Hockey Men’s WorldCup 2023 in Bhubaneswar-Rourkela.

Concept note-5: Bhubaneswar,India, April 18, 2022: Hon’ble Chief Minister of Odisha Shri Naveen Patnaikunveiled the official logo of the FIH Odisha Hockey Men’s World Cup 2023Bhubaneswar - Rourkela at the Kalinga Hockey Stadium on Thursday, April14.
