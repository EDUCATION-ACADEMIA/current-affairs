Telangana Day commonly known as Telangana Formation Day is being celebrated on which day?

(A) June 03

(B) May 31

(C) June 01

(D) ** June 02

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The state of Telangana was officially formed on 2 June2014.

Concept note-2: Telangana Day or Telangana Formation Day is celebratedon 2 June every year. This day commemorates the formation of the state ofTelangana. On this day, the people of Telangana get a state public holiday. The stateof Telangana was officially established on 2 June 2014 from the state of AndhraPradesh.

Concept note-3: Telangana Integration Day: Holiday declared for alleducational & govt institutions. September 17 this year will mark the completion of74 years since Hyderabad state’s annexation to India, for which the stategovernment has planned year-long celebrations.

Concept note-4: India is acountry in Southern Asia. It is a federation made up of 28 states and 8 unionterritories. The foundation day is celebrated to mark the formation of astate/union territory. Some of these states and union territories observe theirfoundation day as the official state holiday.

Concept note-5: Telangana Daycommonly known as Telangana Formation Day is a state public holiday in theIndian state of Telangana, commemorating the formation of the state ofTelangana.
