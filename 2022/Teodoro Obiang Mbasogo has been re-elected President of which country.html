Teodoro Obiang Mbasogo has been re-elected President of which country?

(A) Sudan

(B) Egypt

(C) Namibia

(D) ** Equatorial Guinea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Teodoro Obiang Nguema Mbasogo (Spanish pronunciation:[teoˈðoɾo oˈjaŋɡ eŋˈɡema embaˈsoɣo]; born 5 June 1942) is an Equatoguineanpolitician and former military officer who has served as the second president ofEquatorial Guinea since August 1979.

Concept note-2: The President ofEquatorial Guinea is the highest authority of the Republic of Equatorial Guinea.This article lists the presidents of Equatorial Guinea, a country in the Gulf of Guineaand on the western equatorial coast of Central Africa, since the establishment of theoffice of president in 1968.
