The 46th GST Council meeting was called to discuss the GST rate on which sector?

(A) ** Textile

(B) Oil

(C) Health

(D) Electronics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The 46th GST Council meeting was called under emergencyprovisions. Finance Minister informed that It was a very brief meeting withthe GST hike on textiles.

Concept note-2: The 46th GST Councilmeeting was held on Friday, 31st December 2021, at Vigyan Bhavan in NewDelhi.

Concept note-3: The 47th GST Council Meeting was held on 28th and29th June 2022 in Chandigarh.

Concept note-4: The tax authority clarified thatGST compensation cess at the rate of 22% is applicable on motor vehicles whichsatisfy all four specifications it has outlined–these vehicles are popularly known asSUVs, the engine capacity exceeds 1,500 cc, the length exceeds 4,000 mm and theground clearance is 170 mm and above.
