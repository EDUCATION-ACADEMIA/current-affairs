The 5th Assembly of International Solar Alliance (ISA) is being held under the presidency of which country?

(A) ** India

(B) Netherlands

(C) France

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Shri R.K Singh , Union Minister for Power and New andRenewable Energy today unvieled the curtain raiser to the Fifth Assembly of theInternational Solar Alliance along with side events to be held in New Delhi from17th - 20th October, 2022. India holds the office of the President of the ISAAssembly.

Concept note-2: About the 5th Assembly of ISAThe Assembly isthe apex decision-making body of ISA, in which each Member Country isrepresented. This body makes decisions concerning the implementation ofthe ISA’s Framework Agreement and coordinated actions to be taken toachieve its objective.

Concept note-3: The correct answer is Germany.Germany has become the 5th country to sign the International Solar AllianceFramework Agreement after amendments to it entered into force on 8thJanuary 2021.

Concept note-4: The fifth Assembly of the InternationalSolar Alliance (ISA) will be held in New Delhi from 17th to 20th October,2022, which will delineate three global reports on Investments, Technology &Markets in the solar sector.

Concept note-5: India and France have beenre-elected as President and Co-President of the International Solar Alliance(ISA).
