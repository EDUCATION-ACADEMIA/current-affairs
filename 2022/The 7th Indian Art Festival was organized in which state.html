The 7th Indian Art Festival was organized in which state?

(A) Goa

(B) Mumbai

(C) ** New Delhi

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: If you are an art aficionado and in Delhi now, then it is time tohead to the Indian Art Festival, which is being held in the national capital untilOctober 16. In its 7th edition, the exhibition is showcasing artworks of over 450artists.

Concept note-2: India Art Fair is owned by Angus Montgomery Arts, adivision of Angus Montgomery Ltd.

Concept note-3: Starting from the 4th edition,the venue was shifted to NSIC grounds, Okhla.

Concept note-4: Presented byBMW India from 28 April to 1 May 2022 at the NSIC Exhibition Grounds inNew Delhi, the fair presented a strong contingent of 77 exhibitors, including63 galleries and 14 institutional participants from 16 cities in India andbeyond.

Concept note-5: First held in 2008, it is India’s largest art fair. The artfair includes several pavilions of exhibits by galleries and solo projects by severalartists.
