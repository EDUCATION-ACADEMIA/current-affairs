The 7th anniversary of the Skill India Mission is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** July 15

(B) July 14

(C) July 17

(D) July 16

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Skill Mission launched by the Prime Minister on15 July 2015, has gathered tremendous steam under the guidance of ShriDharmendra Pradhan, Minister for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship andShri Rajeev Chandrasekhar, Minister of State for Skill Development andEntrepreneurship.

Concept note-2: Skill India is an initiative launched by thegovernment in 2015 to train over 40 crore Indians in different industry-related jobs.The vision is to create an empowered workforce by 2022 with the help of variousschemes and training courses.

Concept note-3: Launched by Hon’ble PrimeMinister Shri Narendra Modi on 15 July 2015, SKILL INDIA MISSION aims to trainover 40 crore people in India in different skills by 2022. The mission aims atvocational training and certification of Indian youth for a better livelihood andrespect in the society.
