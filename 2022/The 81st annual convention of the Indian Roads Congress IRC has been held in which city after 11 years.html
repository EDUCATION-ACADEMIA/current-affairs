The 81st annual convention of the Indian Roads Congress (IRC) has been held in which city after 11 years?

(A) Mumbai

(B) Ranchi

(C) ** Lucknow

(D) Indore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Roads Congress (IRC) is the Apex Body of Highway Engineers in the country. The IRC was set up in December, 1934 on the recommendations of the Indian Road Development Committee best known as Jayakar Committee set up by the Govt. of India with the objective of Road Development in India.

Concept note-2: -The Indian Road Congress was formed as the apex body for Engineering in Highway construction in India in the year 1934 with 73 members with the main objective for development of roads and helping to provides a stage for exchanging expertise and latest research developments relating to it are shared.

Concept note-3: -Its headquarter is at New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -1.2. The Indian Roads Congress Standard on Dimensions and Weights of Road Design Vehicles was first published in January, 1954.
