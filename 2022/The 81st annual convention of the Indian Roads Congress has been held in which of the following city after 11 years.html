The 81st annual convention of the Indian Roads Congress has been held in which of the following city after 11 years?

(A) Chennai

(B) ** Lucknow

(C) Bengaluru

(D) Ahmedabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Indian Roads Congress (IRC) is the Apex Body ofHighway Engineers in the country. The IRC was set up in December, 1934 on therecommendations of the Indian Road Development Committee best known asJayakar Committee set up by the Govt. of India with the objective of RoadDevelopment in India.

Concept note-2: Mission. -To be the lead National Forumfor sharing of knowledge and to pool their experiences and expertise collectively onthe entire range of subjects related with road sector including the connected policyissues on road construction and road transportation.

Concept note-3: Ministry ofRoad Transport and Highways.

Concept note-4: The Congress provides aNational forum for sharing of knowledge and pooling of experience on the entirerange of subjects dealing with the construction & maintenance of roads and bridges,including technology, equipment, research, planning, finance, taxation, organisationand all connected policy issues.
