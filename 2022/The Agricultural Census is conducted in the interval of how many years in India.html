The Agricultural Census is conducted in the interval of how many years in India?

(A) 10

(B) ** 5

(C) 2

(D) 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Agriculture Census in India is conducted at the interval ofFive Years.

Concept note-2: Agriculture Census in India is conductedat an interval of five years following broad guidelines of decennial WorldCensus of Agriculture (WCA) evolved by Food and Agriculture Organization(FAO) of the United Nations.

Concept note-3: Recently, the Ministryof Agriculture & Farmers’ Welfare launched “The Eleventh AgriculturalCensus (2021-22).” This computation will bring huge benefits in a vast andagricultural country like India.

Concept note-4: The USDA’s NationalAgricultural Statistics Service publishes a procedural history of the census ofagriculture, the most recent being the 2007 Census of Agriculture: History [PDF29.6MB].

Concept note-5: Agriculture Census in India forms part of WorldAgriculture Census programme of FAO. The first Agriculture Census in India wasconducted in 1970-71.
