The Antyodaya Diwas is observed every year on which date?

(A) ** September 25

(B) September 24

(C) September 22

(D) September 23

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Every year, Antyodaya Diwas is celebrated in India onSeptember 25 to mark the birth anniversary of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya andremember his life and legacy. In 2014, Prime Minister Narendra Modi dedicated theday in honour of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya.

Concept note-2: In India, theAntyodaya Diwas is observed every year on September 25, to mark the birthanniversary of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya. Antyodaya means “uplifting thepoorest of the poor” or “rise of the last person”.

Concept note-3: Yes, 25September is celebrated as Antyodaya Diwas in India. This is one of theimportant days in September because it commemorates the birth anniversaryof Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya.

Concept note-4: Antyodaya Diwas2022: ThemeThe importance of this day lies in “reaching out to the lastperson”.

Concept note-5: Antyodaya Diwas is celebrated annually on 25thSeptember in India. It marks the birth anniversary of Indian leader PanditDeendayal Upadhyaya and is celebrated in his honour to remember his life andlegacy.
