‘The Blue Book:A Writer’s Journal’ is written by which author?

(A) ** Amitava Kumar

(B) Shekhar Gupta

(C) Raja Mohan

(D) Boria Majumdar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Amitava Kumar is the author of several books ofnon-fiction and three novels. His work has appeared in Granta, the New YorkTimes, Harper’s, and several other publications. He has been awarded aGuggenheim fellowship and residencies from Yaddo, MacDowell and the LannanFoundation.

Concept note-2: Indian writer and journalist, Amitava Kumarhas come out with a new book titled ‘The Blue Book: A Writer’s Journal’.The book has been published by HarperCollins India. The Blue Book is theoutcome of the diary-keeping by the author during the time of lockdown due topandemics.

Concept note-3: An author is someone whose written work has beenpublished. In addition to producing published work, people who write are consideredauthors when they originate the ideas and content of their written work. For thisreason, most authors are writers, but not all writers are considered to beauthors.

Concept note-4: “Persons who participate in and contribute to theresearch.” “The ones who develop and will take responsibility for the publishedpaper.” “Anyone can be the authors of journal articles depending on the qualityof their writing.”

Concept note-5: A writer’s notebook is a record ofimpressions, observations, and ideas that may eventually serve as the basis for moreformal writings, such as essays, articles, stories, or poems. As one of thediscovery strategies, a writer’s notebook is sometimes called a writer’s diary orjournal.
