The CM of Sikkim, has declared whom as “State Butterfly of Sikkim”?

(A) ** Blue Duke

(B) Sumalia

(C) Phaedyma

(D) Auzakia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Sikkim Chief Minister Prem Singh Tamangon the occasionof World Environment Day on 5 June 2022 declared Blue Duke the statebutterfly. Blue Duke, which goes by the scientific name Bassarona durga, isunique to Sikkim and the eastern Himalayas. It was discovered in the state in1858.

Concept note-2: During the celebration of World Environment Day2022; the Chief Minister of Sikkim, P.S. Golay, declared Blue Duke as “StateButterfly of Sikkim”.
