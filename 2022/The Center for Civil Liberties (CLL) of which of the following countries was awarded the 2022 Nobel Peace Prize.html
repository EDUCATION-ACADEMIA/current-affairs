The Center for Civil Liberties (CLL) of which of the following countries was awarded the 2022 Nobel Peace Prize?

(A) Belarusian activist Ales Bialiatski

(B) Ukrainian NGO Center for Civil Liberties (CLL)

(C) Russian human rights group Memorial

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The 2022 Peace Prize is awarded to human rightsadvocate Ales Bialiatski from Belarus, the Russian human rights organisationMemorial and the Ukrainian human rights organisation Center for CivilLiberties.

Concept note-2: Who got the Nobel Peace Prize in 2022? AlesBialiatski, a Belarusian human rights activist, Memorial, a Russian human rightsorganisation, and Center for Civil Liberties, a Ukrainian human rights organisation,share the 2022 Nobel Peace Prize.

Concept note-3: Two Indians, Pratik Sinhaand Mohammad Zubair, have been shortlisted for the Nobel Peace Prize2022.

Concept note-4: Center for Civil Liberties, Ukrainian organization thatpromotes democracy and civil society. The organization won (with Ales Bialiatskiand Memorial) the Nobel Prize for Peace in 2022.

Concept note-5: As stipulatedin Nobel’s will, the Nobel Prizes in Physics, Chemistry, Physiology or Medicine andLiterature are awarded in Stockholm, Sweden, while the Nobel Peace Prize isawarded in Oslo, Norway.
