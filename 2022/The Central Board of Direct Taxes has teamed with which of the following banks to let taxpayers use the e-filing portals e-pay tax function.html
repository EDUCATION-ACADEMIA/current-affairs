The Central Board of Direct Taxes has teamed with which of the following banks to let taxpayers use the e-filing portal’s e-pay tax function?

(A) Bank of Baroda

(B) ** Federal Bank

(C) Punjab National Bank

(D) ICICI Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Federal Bank and the Central Board of Direct Taxes haveteamed to let taxpayers use the e-filing portal’s e-pay tax function. Taxes cannow be paid promptly by anyone. Federal Bank and the Central Board ofDirect Taxes have teamed to let taxpayers use the e-filing portal’s e-pay taxfunction.

Concept note-2: Federal Bank has partnered with the Central Board ofDirect Taxes to assist taxpayers pay through the e-pay tax facility in the e-filingportal. Anyone can now pay taxes instantly through modes such as Debit/CreditCard, UPI, Net banking, cash, NEFT/RTGS etc.

Concept note-3: Federal Bankhas been authorized by Reserve Bank of India, basis recommendation ofController General of Accounts, Ministry of Finance, for collection of Direct andIndirect Taxes, on behalf of CBDT and CBIC.

Concept note-4: TheReserve Bank of India has authorised South Indian Bankto collect direct andindirect taxes on behalf of the Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) and theCentral Board of Indirect Taxes & Customs (CBIC).

Concept note-5: TheCentral Board of Direct Taxes is a statutory authority functioning underthe Central Board of Revenue Act, 1963. The officials of the Board in theirex-officio capacity also function as a Division of the Ministry dealing withmatters relating to levy and collection of direct taxes.
