[SOURCES]

The GST Council has decided to defer the increase in GST Rate on which product?

(A) Automobile

(B) ** Textile

(C) Mobile Phones

(D) Cotton

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. The GST Council has decided to defer the increase in GST Rate on which product? Notes: The GST Council decided to defer the increase in the rate for textiles to 12% from 5%. It has referred the matter to a panel of Ministers, to review Rate Rationalisation.

Concept note-2: -The GST rates on clothes has been raised from 5% to 12% from January 2022. The purpose of increasing the GST rate on clothing is to address the issues related to inverted duty structures, which impact a portion of the textile value chain.

Concept note-3: -In a major relief to MSMEs in the textile and apparel sector, The GST Council on Friday has decided to defer the rate hike on textiles from 5% to 12%.

Concept note-4: -29th November 2021 SIHMA has sent a letter to FM India about the new GST rate on women’s garments and footwear. The new rate is 12% and with effect from January 2022.
