The Gandhi Mandiram (Gandhi temple) and Smrithi Vanam dedicated to freedom fighters have been unveiled in which state?

(A) ** Andhra Pradesh

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Odisha

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is Srikakulam. In Andhra Pradesh, socialactivistshave built a Gandhi Mandiram and also a Smruthi Vanam at Srikakulam.The aim is to inculcate patriotism among the younger generation by rememberingthe sacrifices of freedom fighters and national leaders.

Concept note-2: GandhiTemple built in 1974 is situated at Bhatara in Sambalpur district of the state ofOdisha on the eastern coast of India. This temple is dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi.This is the first temple of India, dedicated to Gandhiji.

Concept note-3: Who IsAlluri Sitaram Raju, Andhra Pradesh’s ‘Unsung’ Tribal Hero Whose Statue PMModi Unveiled? Paying rich tributes to Alluri Sitarama Raju, PM Modisaid he involved himself in the country’s fight for independence early,dedicating himself for Adivasi welfare and the country, and was ‘martyred’at a young age.

Concept note-4: Gandhi Gudi (temple) was built in2014 on a four-and-a-half acre plot of land at Pedakaparthi in Telangana’sNalgonda district, about 75 km from Hyderabad.

Concept note-5: Greatleaders like Gadicherla Harisarvotham Rao, Pattabhi Sitaramayya, TangututriPrakasam Pantulu, Duvvuru Subbamma, Durgabhai Deshmukh, SangamLakshmi Bai and others are well known. Stalwarts like Yellapragada Sitkumari,Ammanna Raja and Arutla Kamala Devi have entered into the pages ofhistory.
