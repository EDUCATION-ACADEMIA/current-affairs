The Government of India has extended the Scheme of Assistance to National Sports Federations (NSFs) for five years till 2025-26. How much amount has been allocated to the scheme over the extended period?

(A) Rs. 1777 crore

(B) Rs. 1272 crore

(C) Rs. 1474 crore

(D) ** Rs. 1575 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Strengthening its support for sports in India, the UnionGovernment has approved continuation of Scheme of Assistance to National SportsFederations (NSFs) with an outlay of Rs. 1575 crore for XV Finance CommissionCycle (2021-22 to 2025-26).

Concept note-2: 20,000/- per athlete once a year forNational campers from earlier Rs. 1000 per athlete. For encouraging NSFs to hostinternational tournaments in the country, quantum of assistance for conduct ofinternational tournaments have been enhanced to Rs. 1.00 Crore from the earlierRs.

Concept note-3: The guidelines for Assistance to National SportsFederations (NSFS) were formulated in 2001 vide Order No. 6-6/94-SP. III,dated 14.8. 2001 with a view to providing financial assistance to the NSFs,recognized by the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports.

Concept note-4: TheNational Sports Code also lays down the roles and responsibilities of agenciessuch as the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports (MYAS), National SportsFederations (NSF) and the Sports Authority of India (SAI). The MYASdetermines the eligibility conditions for recognition of NSFs among otherduties.

Concept note-5: 56 National Sports Federations in India are Recognisedby the Govt of India.
