The Governor of Arunachal Pradesh, B.D. Mishra assumed additional charge as Governor of which state?

(A) New Delhi

(B) Assam

(C) Manipur

(D) ** Meghalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: B. D. Mishra is a former brigadierof the Indian Army. He is thepresent governor of Arunachal Pradesh. and also has additional charge of Governor ofMeghalaya.

Concept note-2: (Dr.) Mishra (Retd.) took oath as the Governor ofArunachal Pradesh on 3rd October 2017 and was also given an additional charged asthe Governor of Meghalaya and took oath as the Governor of Meghalaya on the 04thOctober, 2022.

Concept note-3: Brigadier B. D. Mishra (born 20 July 1939) is anIndian politician and former brigadier of the Indian Army. In October 2017,Governor of Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-4: ITANAGAR- The Governor ofArunachal Pradesh Brig. (Dr.) B.D. Mishra (Retd.) launched the latest English andHindi editions of ‘Exam Warriors’ book written by Prime Minister Narendra Modi,in the General Bipin Rawat Hall, Raj Bhavan, Itanagar on 19th January2023.
