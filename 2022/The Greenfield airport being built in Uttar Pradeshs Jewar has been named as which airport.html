The Greenfield airport being built in Uttar Pradesh’s Jewar has been named as which airport?

(A) Biju Patnaik International Airport

(B) Sri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport

(C) ** Noida International Airport

(D) Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Noida International Airport, also known as JewarInternational Airport, is an international airport being constructed nearJewar in Gautam Buddha Nagar District, Uttar Pradesh in India, whichwill serve the National Capital Region (NCR).

Concept note-2: PMinaugurates first greenfield airport ‘Donyi Polo Airport, Itanagar’ in ArunachalPradesh.

Concept note-3: Centre has accorded an ‘In-principle approval’ for thesetting up of 21 Greenfield Airports across the country. Union Minister of State forCivil Aviation Lt Gen (Retd) VK Singh said that the latest is the ManoharInternational Airport in Goa which was inaugurated on December 11 thisyear.

Concept note-4: Jewar International Airport: Project detailsPrime MinisterNarendra Modi laid the foundation stone of the Noida International Airport onNovember 25, 2021. The first phase of the project is expected to be completedby 2024.

Concept note-5: The Noida International Greenfield AirportProjectThe project spread across an area of 5,000 acres, is being developed byZurich Airport International AG (ZAIA) at an estimated cost of Rs 29,560crore.
