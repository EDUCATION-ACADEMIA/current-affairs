The ‘India Water Pitch-Pilot-Scale Start-up Challenge’ has been launched under which scheme?

(A) PM SVANidhi

(B) ** AMRUT 2.0

(C) Saubhagya Scheme

(D) Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban 2.0

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The start-ups have been shortlisted by the Ministry through a challenge process launched in March, 2022 under AMRUT 2.0 Mission.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry selected the start-ups through a challenge procedure that was initiated in March 2022 as part of the AMRUT 2.0 Mission.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Hardeep Singh Puri. Union Minister of Housing and Urban Affairs Hardeep Singh Puri launched the ‘India WaterPitch-Pilot-Scale Start-up Challenge’ on 12 March 2022. It was launched under Ministry’s Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) 2.0.

Concept note-4: -On 12th March 2022, a national level ‘India Water Pitch-Pilot-Scale Start-up Conclave’ will be organized by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs in New Delhi.
