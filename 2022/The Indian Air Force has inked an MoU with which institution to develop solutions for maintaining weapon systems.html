The Indian Air Force has inked an MoU with which institution to develop solutions for maintaining weapon systems?

(A) IIT Kanpur

(B) IIT Delhi

(C) ** IIT Madras

(D) IIT Ropar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: The Indian Air Force (IAF) hassigned a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with the Indian Institute ofTechnology (IIT) Madras for technology development and finding indigenoussolutions for the sustenance of various weapons systems.

Concept note-2: UPSCMains. Q. Which Indian Armed Force signed a MoU with IIT Madras for technologydevelopment? Notes: The Indian Air Force (IAF) and Indian Institute ofTechnology (IIT) Madras signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU)for technology development.

Concept note-3: IAF Ties Up with IITMadras to Develop Solutions to Maintain Weapon Systems. The Indian AirForce (IAF) and Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras have signed amemorandum of understanding for technology development and finding indigenoussolutions.

Concept note-4: The Indian Air Force Ensign follows the patternof the RAF Ensign, with the Indian national flag and air force roundel inplace of their British equivalents. The first IAF rank flags were also copiedfrom the RAF, with green and saffron (orange) in place of dark blue andred.

Concept note-5: The Primary objective of IAF is to defend the nation andits airspace against Air threats in coordination with Army and Navy. Thesecondary purpose is to assist civil power during natural calamities and internaldisturbances.
