[SOURCES]

The Indian Air Force will participate in the multinational ‘Exercise Cobra Warrior 22’, with LCA Tejas. The annual exercise is organised by the Air Force of which country?

(A) ** United Kingdom

(B) France

(C) United States

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is the United Kingdom. Indian Air Forcewill participate in a multi-nation air exercise named ‘Ex Cobra Warrior 22’at Waddington, UK from 6-27 March 2022. IAF Light Combat Aircraft(LCA) Tejas will participate in the exercise.

Concept note-2: The latestiteration of the three-week exercise is being conducted between 3 and 23September. Forces from the NATO, Germany, Italy and the US have commencedthe UK Royal Air Force’s (Raf) capstone tactical training exercise, CobraWarrior 2022. The three-week exercise is being conducted between 3 and 23September.

Concept note-3: Indian Air Force will participate in a multi-nationair exercise named ‘Exercise Cobra Warrior 22’ at Waddington, in the UnitedKingdom from March 06 to 27, 2022. IAF Light Combat Aircraft (LCA) Tejas willparticipate in the exercise along with fighter aircraft of the UK and other leading AirForces.

Concept note-4: The RAF, the UK, conducts the Cobra warrior exercise,which is a challenging exercise for the aircrew. Through this exercise, the aircrewcan qualify as Qualified Weapons Intelligence ( QWI ), Qualified weaponsinstructors, Qualified Space instructors, and Qualified Multi-engine tacticsinstructors.

Concept note-5: UPSC Mains. Q. Which is the venue of themulti-nation air exercise ‘Ex Cobra Warrior 22’? Notes: India’s indigenously builtfighter aircraft, Light Combat Aircraft (LCA) Tejas, is set to take part in themulti-nation air exercise ‘Ex Cobra Warrior 22’ at Waddington, United Kingdomfrom March 6-27.
