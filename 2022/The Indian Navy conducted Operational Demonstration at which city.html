The Indian Navy conducted ‘Operational Demonstration’ at which city?

(A) Pune

(B) Kochi

(C) ** Visakhapatnam

(D) Ernakulam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India celebrates December 04 as Navy Day, every year, toacknowledge the role of the Indian Navy and commemorate its achievements in‘Operation Trident’ during the 1971 Indo-Pak War.

Concept note-2: The IndianNavy’s Western Fleet is based at Mumbai and the Eastern Fleet is basedat Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-3: In 1934 the Royal Indian Marinechanged its name, with the enactment of the Indian Navy (Discipline) Actof 1934. The Royal Indian Navy was formally inaugurated on 2 October1934, at Bombay.

Concept note-4: The first engagement in action of theIndian Navy was against the Portuguese Navy during the liberation of Goa in1961.

Concept note-5: The Southern Command is the Training Command. Thecutting edge of the Indian Navy are its two Fleets, namely the Western Fleet, basedat Mumbai and the Eastern Fleet, based at Visakhapatnam.
