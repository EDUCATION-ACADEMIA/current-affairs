The Indian Navy has hosted which edition of the Singapore-India Maritime Bilateral Exercise (SIMBEX) at Visakhapatnam?

(A) 25

(B) 10

(C) 36

(D) ** 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: It also highlights the commitment and contribution of the twonations towards enhancing maritime security in the Indian Ocean Region. The IndianNavy is hosting the 29th edition of the Singapore-India Maritime Bilateral Exercise(SIMBEX) from 26 to 30 October, 2022 at Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-2: Itwas the 28th edition of the bilateral naval exercise between India & Singapore. INSRanvijay (Guided Missile Destroyer), INS Kiltan (Anti-submarine warfare corvette),INS Kora (Guided Missile Corvette) and P8I Long Range Maritime Patrol Aircraftrepresented Indian Navy.

Concept note-3: 12th edition of Agni Warrior exercisebetween Indian and Singapore Concludes. The 12th Edition of Exercise AgniWarrior, a bilateral exercise between the Singapore & Indian Army, whichhad commenced on 13 November 2022 concluded at Field Firing Ranges,Devlali (Maharashtra).
