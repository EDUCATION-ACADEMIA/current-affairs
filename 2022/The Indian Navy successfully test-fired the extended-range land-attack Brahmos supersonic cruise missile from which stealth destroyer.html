The Indian Navy successfully test-fired the extended-range land-attack Brahmos supersonic cruise missile from which stealth destroyer?

(A) ** INS Chennai

(B) INS Vikramaditya

(C) INS Shakti

(D) INS Arihant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Incidentally, the Indian Navy had, on March 5, successfullylaunched an extended range land attack BrahMos missile from INS Chennai, theNavy’s indigenously built stealth destroyer.

Concept note-2: The Indian AirForce on Thursday successfully fired the extended range version of the BrahMos AirLaunch missile. “Carrying out a precision strike against a Ship target from a Su-30MKI aircraft in the Bay of Bengal region, the missile achieved the desired missionobjectives”, the IAF tweeted.

Concept note-3: Army successfully tests BrahMossupersonic cruise missile: All you need to know | Latest News India - HindustanTimes.

Concept note-4: On March 5, the Indian Navy successfully tested anadvanced version of the Brahmos supersonic cruise missile from a stealth destroyerin the Indian Ocean. The missile was fired from the stealth destroyer INSChennai.

Concept note-5: India on Wednesday successfully test-firedsurface-to-surface BrahMos supersonic cruise missile at Andaman and NicobarIslands. According to defence officials, the extended range missile hit its target withpinpoint accuracy.
