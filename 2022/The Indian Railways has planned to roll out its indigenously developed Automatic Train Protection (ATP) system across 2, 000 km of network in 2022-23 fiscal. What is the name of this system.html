The Indian Railways has planned to roll out its indigenously developed Automatic Train Protection (ATP) system across 2, 000 km of network in 2022-23 fiscal. What is the name of this system?

(A) Dhaal

(B) Shastra

(C) ** Kavach

(D) Suraksha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: ’Kavach’, the indigenously developed Automatic TrainProtection (ATP) system, will be deployed across the entire rail networkbeginning with 2,000 km in the 2022-23 fiscal and will be rolled out in 4,000 to5,000 km every subsequent year, Railway MinisterAshwini Vaishnaw said onFriday.

Concept note-2: It manages the fourth largest national railway system inthe world by size, with a total route length of 68,103 km (42,317 mi) as of 31 March2022.

Concept note-3: According to Indian Railways, this time the budgetallocation has been the highest in the history. In 2013-14, Rs 28,174 crore came inthe share of Railways. After this, Rs 1,59,100 crore has been received in2022-23 and now Rs 2,40,000 crore has been received in this financial year2023-24.

Concept note-4: Kavach ( lit. ‘Armour’) is an automatic trainprotection (ATP) system indigenously developed by Indian Railways throughResearch Designs & Standards Organisation (RDSO). Initial development ofKavach started in 2012 under the name Train Collision Avoidance System(TCAS).

Concept note-5: KAVACH is meant to provide protection bypreventing trains to pass the Signal Passing at Danger, (SPAD) (Red) and avoidcollision. It activates the train breaking system automatically if the driver fails tocontrol the train as per the speed restrictions.
