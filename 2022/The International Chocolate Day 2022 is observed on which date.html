The International Chocolate Day 2022 is observed on which date?

(A) ** September 13

(B) September 12

(C) September 11

(D) September 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: International Chocolate Day is celebrated every year onSeptember 13.

Concept note-2: Chocolate Day - SignificanceOver the years,chocolates and Valentine’s Day have become synonymous with each other. Every yearon February 9, couples around the world celebrate Chocolate Day. This is separatefrom World Chocolate Day, celebrated on July 7 to commemorate the introduction ofchocolate to Europe in 1550.

Concept note-3: International Chocolate Day is onSeptember 13. Celebrate with us as we give you some cool facts and fun ideas tocelebrate this well-loved treat.

Concept note-4: WORLD CHOCOLATE DAYHISTORYIt’s unclear who came up with this yummy food day. However, the day iscelebrated on July 7th because it was on this date in 1550 that chocolate wasfirst brought to Europe. The first World Chocolate Day was celebrated in2009.
