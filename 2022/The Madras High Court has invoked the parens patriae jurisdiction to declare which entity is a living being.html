The Madras High Court has invoked the “parens patriae jurisdiction” to declare which entity is a ‘living being’?

(A) Agricultural Lands

(B) ** Mother Nature

(C) Tamil Language

(D) Food Grains

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Q. The Madras High Court has invoked the “parens patriaejurisdiction” to declare which entity as a ‘living being’? Notes: The Madras HighCourt has invoked the “parens patriae jurisdiction” to declare Mother Nature as aLiving Being with all corresponding rights, duties and liabilities of a livingperson.

Concept note-2: Parens patriae | Madras High Court retains224-year-old jurisdiction to hear guardianship, child custody cases - TheHindu.

Concept note-3: The Madras High Court exercises Original Jurisdictionover the City of Madras and Appellate Jurisdiction over the entire State as well asextra- ordinary Original Jurisdiction, Civil and Criminal, under the Letters Patentand Special Original Jurisdiction for the issue of writs under the Constitution ofIndia.

Concept note-4: What is Parens patriae jurisdiction? Parens patriae hasits origin from Latin, which means “parent of the nation”. In case of minor childrenor physically/ mentally/ differently abled people whose rights were denied byirresponsible/abusive/careless parents, the state can invoke parens patriaejurisdiction.

Concept note-5: The Madurai Bench of the Madras High Courtrecently declared “Mother Nature” as a “living being” with the status of a legalperson, with all corresponding rights, duties and liabilities of a living person andurged to preserve and conserve them [A Periyakaruppan v The PrincipalSecretary].
