The Minister of State for External Affairs V Muraleedharan has announced that the 12th World Hindi Conference is to be held in which country?

(A) Vanuatu

(B) Tonga

(C) ** Fiji

(D) Samoa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Minister of State for External Affairs V Muraleedharanhas announced at an event in New Delhi on 27 October 2022 that the 12thWorld Hindi Conference will be held in Nadi, Fijifrom 15-17 February2023.

Concept note-2: The conference is to be held at the Denarau IslandConvention Center in Nadi, Fiji, an ANI report read. A post on the WorldHindi Conference’s official website reads,” Several exhibitions related tothe development of the Hindi language will be organised at the conferencevenue."

Concept note-3: The first World Hindi Conference was inaugurated on10 January 1975 by the then Prime Minister of India Indira Gandhi. It was held from10 to 12 January 1975 in Nagpur.

Concept note-4: World Hindi Day 2023: Thetheme this year for World Hindi Conference is “Hindi – Traditional Knowledge toArtificial Intelligence". World Hindi Day is celebrated on January 10 every year topromote awareness about the language across the world.

Concept note-5: Thecorrect answer is Mauritius. World Hindi Conference: The 11th World HindiConference was held in Mauritius. Inaugurated by Prime Minister of MauritiusPraveen Kumar Jagannath.
