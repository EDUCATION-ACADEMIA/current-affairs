The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy has set up a Project Development Cell under whom to attract investment in clean energy, especially in the solar sector?

(A) Pankaj Patel

(B) Balesh Sharma

(C) ** Amitesh Kumar Sinha

(D) Varun Berry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Grid Connected Solar Rooftop Programme To achieve a cumulative installed capacity of 40,000 MW from Grid Connected Rooftop Solar (RTS) projects.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) has launched the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evem Utthan Mahabhiyan (PM KUSUM) Scheme for farmers for installation of solar pumps and grid connected solar and other renewable power plants in the country.

Concept note-3: -Financial Incentives-such as grants, loans, rebates, and tax credits-are provided in some states to encourage renewable energy development. The Database of State Incentives for Renewables and Efficiency tracks the availability of incentives offered by state.
