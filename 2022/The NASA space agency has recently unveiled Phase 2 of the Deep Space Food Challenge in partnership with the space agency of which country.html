The NASA space agency has recently unveiled Phase 2 of the ‘Deep Space Food Challenge’ in partnership with the space agency of which country?

(A) ** Canada

(B) Russia

(C) Japan

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: NASA and the Canadian Space Agency aspire to providefuture deep space explorers the technology to produce nutritious and tasty meals forlong-duration space missions.

Concept note-2: Description of Challenge: TheDeep Space Food Challenge seeks to create novel food production technologies orsystems that require minimal inputs and maximize safe, nutritious, and palatablefood outputs for long-duration space missions, and which has a potentialto benefit people on Earth.

Concept note-3: NASA is a United Statesgovernment agency that is responsible for science and technology related to airand space. The Space Age started in 1957 with the launch of the Sovietsatellite Sputnik. NASA was created in 1958.

Concept note-4: Teams mustsubmit all required documentation by 5p CDT February 28, 2022 and meetthe eligibility requirements in order to participate in the Challenge. Theregistration process will be administered by NASA’s Allied Organization,Methuselah Foundation.

Concept note-5: The National Aeronautics and SpaceAdministration (NASA) is not owned by any individual or ownership group but isrun by the federal government.
