The National Safe Motherhood Day is being celebrated in India, on the birth anniversary of which leader?

(A) Annie Besant

(B) Rani Lakshmi Bai

(C) Vijaya Lakshmi Pandit

(D) ** Kasturba Gandhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: In the year of 2003, the Indian government recognised April11th as National Safe Motherhood Day at the proposal of WRAI, an association of1800 organisations. Kasturba Gandhi’s birthday is also celebrated on thisday.

Concept note-2: However, are you aware of National Safe Motherhood Daycelebrated in India on April 11 every year to mark the birth anniversaryof Kasturba Gandhi, the political advocate of India and wife of MahatmaGandhi.

Concept note-3: In 2003, upon the initiative of the White RibbonAlliance, the Government of India declared the national safe motherhood day to beheld on April 11. 2022 marks the 19th anniversary of the day. The first observationwas held in 2003.

Concept note-4: The day is observed on 11th April everyyear. What is the theme of National Safe Motherhood Day? The theme ofNational Safe Motherhood Day is “Remain at home amid corona virus, protectmother and infant safe from corona virus”.

Concept note-5: National SafeMotherhood Day is observed every year on April 11. It is aimed at raisingawareness about the care that a pregnant woman needs before, during, and afterchildbirth.
