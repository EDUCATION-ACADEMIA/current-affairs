The No Smoking Day 2022 is being celebrated on which date?

(A) March 07

(B) March 10

(C) March 11

(D) ** March 09

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: WHO in the Western Pacific will host a World No TobaccoDay webinar: “The Devastating Environmental Impact of Tobacco” on 26May 2022, 10:00 - 11:30 (GMT+8 Manila time).

Concept note-2: NoSmoking Day is observed on the second Wednesday of March every year.This year it falls on March 9.

Concept note-3: Quit tobacco to save ourplanet.

Concept note-4: WORLD NO TOBACCO DAY 2022: THEMEThe 2022global campaign aims to raise public awareness about the environmental impact ofthe entire tobacco cycle, from its cultivation, production, and distribution to thetoxic waste it generates. This year, the theme focuses on the impact of tobacco onthe environment.
