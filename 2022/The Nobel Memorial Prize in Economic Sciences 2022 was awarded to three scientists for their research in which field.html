The Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences 2022 was awarded to three scientists for their research in which field?

(A) Global Poverty

(B) ** Banks and Financial Crises

(C) Behavioural Economics

(D) Quantitative Methods

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Buck (2004), Françoise Barré-Sinoussi (2008), Elizabeth H.Blackburn (2009), Carol W. Greider (2009), May-Britt Moser (2014) and Tu Youyou(2015). There have been nine years in which the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicinewas not awarded (1915–1918, 1921, 1925, 1940–1942).

Concept note-2: The2022 Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences went to Ben Bernankeand Douglas Diamond and Philip Dybvig, largely in honour of papers theypublished in 1983.

Concept note-3: Amartya Sen, (born November 3, 1933,Santiniketan, India), Indian economist who was awarded the 1998 NobelPrize in Economic Sciences for his contributions to welfare economics andsocial choice theory and for his interest in the problems of society’s poorestmembers.
