The Punjab and Maharashtra Co-operative Bank Ltd. has been amalgamated with which bank?

(A) Ujjivan Small Finance Bank

(B) Shivalik Small Finance Bank

(C) Fincare Small Finance Bank

(D) ** Unity Small Finance Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Press Releases. The Government of India has todaysanctioned and notified the Scheme for the amalgamation of the Punjab andMaharashtra Co-operative Bank Ltd. (PMC Bank) with Unity Small FinanceBankLtd.

Concept note-2: PMC Bank acquisition by Centrum-Bharatpe wassmartest corporate move in history, claims Ashneer Grover.

Concept note-3: Allthe branches of PMC Bank started functioning as branches of Unity SFB with effectfrom January 25, 2022.

Concept note-4: In August 2021, RBI had declined thejoint merger proposal of the Maharashtra State Co-op Bank (MStC) and RupeeBank.

Concept note-5: Unity Small Finance Bank Limited is promoted byCentrum Financial Services Ltd with Resilient Innovations Pvt Ltd as a jointinvestor.
