The ‘Quit Tobacco App’ has been launched by which organization to support people in quitting the use of tobacco in all forms?

(A) NPCI

(B) ** World Health Organization

(C) Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, GOI

(D) Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The World Health Organization today launched ‘Quit TobaccoApp’ to help people kick the cigarette butt and give up use of tobacco in all forms -including smokeless and other newer products.

Concept note-2: The FDACenter for Tobacco Products (CTP) is responsible for carrying out the FamilySmoking Prevention and Tobacco Control Act, which Congress passed in2009. This law-commonly called the Tobacco Control Act-gives us broadauthority to regulate the manufacturing, distribution, and marketing of tobaccoproducts.

Concept note-3: National Tobacco Control Programme (NTCP) |Ministry of Health and Family Welfare | GOI.
