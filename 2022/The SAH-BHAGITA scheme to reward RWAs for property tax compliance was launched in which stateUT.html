The SAH-BHAGITA scheme to reward RWAs for property tax compliance was launched in which state/UT?

(A) ** Delhi

(B) Assam

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Puducherry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: NEW DELHI: Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD) onFriday launched an online module that allows RWAs to register under the Sahbhagitascheme and claim 10% incentive on 90% tax compliance and an additional 5%incentive on waste segregation and processing.

Concept note-2: The MunicipalCorporation of Delhi signed an MoU with the United Residents Joint Action (Urja)for the successful implementation of ‘Sahbhagita’, a scheme that envisages activeparticipation of resident welfare associations to enhance tax collection and improvewaste management.

Concept note-3: Notes: New Delhi Lieutenant Governorannounced significant changes in the property tax policy of the MunicipalCorporation of Delhi (MCD) after its unification. He also launched ‘SAH-BHAGITAscheme’ to reward Residential Welfare Association (RWAs) for property taxcollection and waste management systems.

Concept note-4: The L-G alsolaunched a SAH-BHAGITA scheme to increase the involvement of the ResidentialWelfare Association (RWAs) in optimal tax collection and waste managementsystems. Under this scheme, the RWAs will be incentivised for their efforts toimprove efficiency and compliance in tax collection.
