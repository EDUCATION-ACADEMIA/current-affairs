The Sea Cadet Corps unit of Visakhapatnam recently celebrated its ____ anniversary?

(A) 25th

(B) ** 50th

(C) 75th

(D) 100th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Sea Cadet Corps, Visakhapatnam unit, was commissionedon January 18, 1970, by Admiral A.K.

Concept note-2: 1929 First SeaCadetsThe first open Sea Cadet Corps was formed in Christchurch, by theCanterbury Navy League. Units formed in the four main centres and were controllednationally by the Canterbury branch of the League. The Sea Cadet motto is ‘ReadyAye Ready’.

Concept note-3: The first Sea Cadet unit was established in 1854 atWhitstable in Kent, created by communities wanting to give young people instructionon a naval theme. Traditionally old seafarers provided training while localbusinessmen funded the Unit Headquarters.

Concept note-4: Sea Cadet units aredivided into three types: DIVISIONS – NSCC Divisions train primarily in the field ofseamanship. SQUADRONS – NSCC Squadrons train primarily in the field ofAviation. BATTALIONS – NSCC Battalions train primarily in the field of NavalConstruction.

Concept note-5: The Sea Cadet Corps is a voluntary youthorganisation that gives training to young school-going boys and girls in the age groupof 10 to 12 years.
