The Spituk Gustor Festival is an annual festival celebrated in which state/Union Territory of India?

(A) West Bengal

(B) ** Ladakh

(C) Sikkim

(D) Andaman & Nicobar Islands

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Ladakh. SpitukGustor, the annual celebrations of Ladakhi culture and traditional heritage began on30 January 2022. It is held at Spituk Monastery.

Concept note-2: The two dayfestival is to be celebrated on 29th Jan to 30th January 2022 in the SpitukMonastery. Held in the 28th and 29th days of the 11th month of the Tibetancalendar, the festival celebrated since the 11th century symbolizes victory ofgood over evil.

Concept note-3: Ladakh [India], Jan 31 (ANI): SpitukGustor, the annual celebrations of Ladakhi culture and traditional heritagebegan on Sunday as the hostile weather condition prevailed in the UnionTerritory.

Concept note-4: Losar Festival is celebrated in Ladakh on 24thDecember 2022. The Losar Festival or the Ladakhi New year is a majorsocio-religious festival of Ladakh celebrated during winter. The Losar Festival willcontinue for nine days from the new year.

Concept note-5: Festivals inLadakhHemis Festival, Losar Festival, Sindhu Darshan, Phyang Tsedup Festival,Dosmoche Festival, Saka Dawa Festival, Tak Tok Festival, Matho Nagrang Festival,Ladakh Festival and many more.
