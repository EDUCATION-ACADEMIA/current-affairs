The UNESCO-Madanjeet Singh Prize has been given to Franca Ma-ih Sulem Yong from Cameroon for?

(A) Promotion of Organic agricultural policy

(B) ** Promotion of Tolerance and Non-Violence

(C) Promotion of elderly education

(D) Promotion of Forest conservation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The UNESCO-Madanjeet Singh Prize for the Promotion ofTolerance and Non-Violence, for this 2022 Edition, has been given to Franca Ma-ihSulem Yong from Cameroon, President of the NGOs #Afrogiveness and PositiveYouths Africa.

Concept note-2: The objective of this award is to rewardindividuals, institutions and non-governmental organizations that have madeexceptional contributions to the promotion of tolerance and non-violence. The awardis awarded in the scientific, artistic, cultural or communication and informationfields.

Concept note-3: The two laureates in 1998 were the educator and peaceactivist Narayan Desai of India and the Joint Action Committee for Peoples Rights ofPakistan.

Concept note-4: The Prize was established in 1989 by a resolutionsupported by 120 countries and adopted by the General Conference of UNESCO atits 25th session.

Concept note-5: The UNESCO-Madanjeet Singh Prize for thePromotion of Tolerance and Non-Violence is conferred every two years by UNESCO.The award was created based on the ideals of UNESCO’s Constitution, which statesthat “peace if it is not to fail, must be founded on the intellectual and moralsolidarity of mankind”.
