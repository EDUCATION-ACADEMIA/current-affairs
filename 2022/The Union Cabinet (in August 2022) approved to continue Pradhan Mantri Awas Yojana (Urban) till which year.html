[SOURCES]

The Union Cabinet (in August 2022) approved to continue Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) till which year?

(A) ** 2024

(B) 2027

(C) 2023

(D) 2030

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: PMAY UrbanAs against the prior deadline of March 31, 2022,the urban scheme has now been extended till December 31, 2024, because only 61.77lakh houses have been completed so far, as against the total of 12.26 million housessanctioned under the scheme.

Concept note-2: The continuation of the schemebased on the request of States/Union Territories up to December 31, 2024, will helpin the completion of already sanctioned houses under BLC, AHP & ISSRverticals.

Concept note-3: The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Gramin(PMAY-G) has been extended upto the year 2024. The total target of pucca houseshas also been revised to 2.95 crore houses.

Concept note-4: The PMAY schemeis being implemented in three phases, with the first two phases having ended.Currently, the last phase is on; it began on April 1, 2019, and will end on March 31,2022.
