CURRENT AFFAIRS-2021
[SOURCES]

The Union Cabinet approved the categorization of which institution as an ‘International Organization’, ?

(A) ** Coalition for Disaster Resilient Infrastructure

(B) One Sun One World One Grid

(C) BIMSTEC

(D) International Solar Alliance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Coalition forDisaster Resilient Infrastructure. Cabinet approved categorization of theCoalition for Disaster Resilient Infrastructure (CDRI) as an ‘InternationalOrganization’.

Concept note-2: The agreement will enable the status of an‘Independent and International Legal Entity’ for CDRI. The agreement will enablethe institution to pursue functions internationally with all rights, immunities,and privileges, as per the United Nations (Privileges & Immunities) Act,1947.

Concept note-3: Since its inception, 31 countries, 6 internationalorganisations and 2 private sector organisations have joined CDRI asmembers.
