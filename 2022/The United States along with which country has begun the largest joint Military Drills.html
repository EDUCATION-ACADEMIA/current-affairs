The United States along with which country has begun the largest joint Military Drills?

(A) India

(B) Brazil

(C) Russia

(D) ** South Korea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The US and South Korea have begun their biggest jointmilitary drills in years – a show of forcethat is expected to raise tensions with anincreasingly hostile North Korea.

Concept note-2: RIMPAC, the Rim ofthe Pacific Exercise, is the world’s largest international maritime warfareexercise.

Concept note-3: The US and South Korea plan to hold table topexercises in February that will include plans to look at responses to a North Koreanuclear attack. One of the biggest joint drills may come in the coming months in theKey Resolve/Foal Eagle exercises, which had been held in the spring until they werehalted under Trump.

Concept note-4: Combined Forces Command (CFC),United States Forces Korea (USFK), United Nations Command (UNC), andsubordinate component commands under CFC, began the Ulchi Freedom Shieldexercise on Aug, 22, 2022.

Concept note-5: Modern drill is essentially of twotypes: close-order and extended-order, or combat drill. Close-order drill comprises theformal movements and formations used in marching, parades, and ceremonies.Combat drill trains a small unit in the looser, extended formations and movements ofbattle.
