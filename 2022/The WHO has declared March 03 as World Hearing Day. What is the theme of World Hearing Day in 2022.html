The WHO has declared March 03 as World Hearing Day. What is the theme of World Hearing Day in 2022?

(A) Hearing Care for All

(B) Check your hearing

(C) Hearing for Life. Don’t let hearing loss limit you

(D) ** To hear for life, listen with care

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The World Hearing Day 2022 with the theme “To hear for life,listen with care” will focus on the importance and means of hearing lossprevention through safe listening, with the following key messages: It ispossible to have good hearing across the life course through ear and hearingcare

Concept note-2: World Hearing Day is celebrated on March 3 every year.This year, the theme focusses on the importance and means of hearing lossprevention.

Concept note-3: World Hearing Day is held on 3 March eachyear to raise awareness on how to prevent deafness and hearing loss andpromote ear and hearing care across the world.

Concept note-4: TheWorld Health Organization and all stakeholders are eagerly preparing forthe upcoming World Hearing Day 2023. With this year’s theme, Ear andhearing care for all!

Concept note-5: There will be a focus on hearingproblems and ear disorders throughout life this year with the theme of “Screen,Rehabilitate, and Communicate”. This day is set aside to raise awarenessabout the need of receiving prompt medical attention if you have hearingdamage.
