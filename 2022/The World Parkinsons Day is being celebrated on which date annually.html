The World Parkinson’s Day is being celebrated on which date annually?

(A) April 10

(B) April 12

(C) ** April 11

(D) April 09

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: World Parkinson’s Day takes place on 11 April every year toraise awareness of Parkinson’s.

Concept note-2: World Parkinson’s Day isobserved every year on April 11 to spread awareness about the neurodegenerativedisease. The day also marks the anniversary of James Parkinson who wrote thearticle - An Essay on the Shaking Palsy in 1817, recognising the disease for the firsttime.

Concept note-3: The European Parkinson’s Disease Association (EPDA)and the World Health Organization established World Parkinson’s Day on April11th, 1997. April 11th is the birthday of Dr. James Parkinson. He is the firstphysician to recognize Parkinson’s as a medical condition.

Concept note-4: MDSadvocates for Parkinson’s disease throughout the world, while promoting our missionto disseminate knowledge and promote research to favorably affect the care ofpatients. Thank you to everyone who tuned in from around the world on April 11,2022, to help us celebrate World Parkinson’s Day!

Concept note-5: WorldParkinson’s Day is celebrated on April 11. It is observed on this day because itcorrelates with the birthdate of the person who discovered the disease, Dr. JamesParkinson.
