The World Theater Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** March 27

(B) March 25

(C) March 23

(D) March 24

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: World Theatre Day was initiated in 1961 by the InternationalTheatre Institute ITI. It is celebrated annually on the 27th March by ITI Centresand the international theatre community. Various national and international theatreevents are organized to mark this occasion.

Concept note-2: So, to dedicate aday to theatres, March 27 is observed as ‘World Theatre Day’ across theglobe. Theatre is one of the most popular forms of art that depicts culture, atradition that exists around the world. This day preserves dramas and theiressential role in the theatrical arts.

Concept note-3: Ever since, each yearon the 27th March (date of the opening of the 1962 “Theatre of Nations”season in Paris), World Theatre Day has been celebrated in many and variedways by ITI Centres - of which there are now more than 90 throughout theworld.

Concept note-4: The correct answer is Theatre and a Culture ofPeace. World Theatre Day is celebrated on 27 March every year. The themefor World Theatre Day 2022 is “Theatre and a Culture of Peace.” WorldTheatre Day was first celebrated by the International Theatre Institute(ITI) in the year 1961, on 27 March.

Concept note-5: National CinemaDay postponed to September 23; watch movies at theatres for ₹75 on theD-day. Join in the celebration to have a blast on this day at the movies!National Cinema Day, celebrated every year on September 16, has beenofficially postponed to September 23 this year, and this ‘can happen only inIndia’!
