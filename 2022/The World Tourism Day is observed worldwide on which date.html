The World Tourism Day is observed worldwide on which date?

(A) ** September 27

(B) September 24

(C) September 25

(D) September 22

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: World Tourism Day has been held on 27 September each yearsince 1980. The date marks the anniversary of the adoption of the Statutes of theOrganization in 1970, paving the way for the establishment of UNWTO fiveyears later.

Concept note-2: World Tourism Day is celebrated on 27September and in India, National Tourism Day is celebrated on 25 January. Thiscelebration is all about cultivating and creating awareness among society globallyon the significance of tourism, and its social, political, financial, and alsocultural worth and value.

Concept note-3: World Tourism Day: HistoryAspecial assembly was held in Mexico City on 27 September, 1970 by theInternational Union of Official Travel Organisation (IUOTO) and adopted theStatutes of the World Tourism Organisation. Then, UNWTO decided in lateSeptember 1979 to institute World Tourism Day.

Concept note-4: Thetheme “Rethinking Tourism” will reflect this. It aims to inspire the debatearound rethinking tourism for development, including through education andjobs, and tourism’s impact on the planet and opportunities to grow moresustainably.
