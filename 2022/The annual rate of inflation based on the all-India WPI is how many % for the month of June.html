The annual rate of inflation based on the all-India WPI is how many % for the month of June?

(A) ** 15.18%

(B) 14.55%

(C) 15.88%

(D) 15.08%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The rate of inflation based on WPI Food Index decreased from12.41% in June, 2022 to 9.41% in July, 2022.

Concept note-2: WPIInflation Rate in India (June 2022): India’s wholesale inflation eased to 15.18per cent in June, data released by the Ministry of Commerce & Industryshowed.

Concept note-3: The annual wholesale price inflation rate in Indiadeclined to 4.95 percent in December 2022 from 5.85 percent in the previous month,less than the market forecasts of 5.6 percent.

Concept note-4: CPI Inflation RateJuly, IIP Growth Rate June 2022: Retail inflation eases to 5-month low of 6.71% inJuly, IIP grows 12.3% in June.
