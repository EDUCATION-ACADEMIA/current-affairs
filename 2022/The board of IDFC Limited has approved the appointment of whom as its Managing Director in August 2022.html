The board of IDFC Limited has approved the appointment of whom as its Managing Director in August 2022?

(A) ** Mahendra N Shah

(B) Rakesh Sharma

(C) Manoj Sahay

(D) T. N. Manoharan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Mahendra N Shah will be MD with effect from October 1,2022, till September 30, 2023. The Board of IDFC Ltd has approved the appointmentof Mahendra N Shah as Managing Director (MD) - Designate with immediateeffect.

Concept note-2: IDFC Limited appoints Mahendra N Shah as its newmanaging director.

Concept note-3: Mahendra N Shah will be ManagingDirector with effect from October 1, 2022, till September 30, 2023 subject to theapproval of the shareholders of the Company and other applicable statutory/regulatory approvals, IDFC Limited said.
