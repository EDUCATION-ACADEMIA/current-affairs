The central government announced that India has recorded which highest ever annual FDI inflow during FY22?

(A) ** 83.57 bn

(B) 62.47 bn

(C) 80.12 bn

(D) 77.14 bn

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India received the highest-ever FDI inflows of US$ 84.8billion including US$ 7.1 billion FDI equity inflows in the services sector inFY22.

Concept note-2: India has so far received healthy foreign directinvestment (FDI) in 2022. As per the latest figures of the government,India has registered its highest-ever total FDI inflows of $84.84 billion in2021-22.

Concept note-3: Gujarat and Rajasthan Emerge as India’sLeading Investment Destinations. Gujarat and Rajasthan have emergedas two of India’s top states for attracting corporate investments, invitinginvestments worth INR 3.98 trillion and INR 2.91 trillion, respectively, in FY2022.

Concept note-4: United States Is World’s Top Destination for ForeignDirect Investment.

Concept note-5: FDI in India 2022: Karnataka, Maharashtrahighest FDI states in India in FY 2021-22. FDI in India 2022: The highest FDI inIndia was from two countries Singapore and USA in FY 2021-22, with Singaporeaccounting for 27.01 percent of the FDI equity inflows in India and US for 17.94percent.
