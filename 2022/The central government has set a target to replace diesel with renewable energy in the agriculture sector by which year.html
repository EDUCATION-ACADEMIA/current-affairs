The central government has set a target to replace diesel with renewable energy in the agriculture sector by which year?

(A) 2025

(B) ** 2024

(C) 2022

(D) 2023

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has set an ambitious target of replacing diesel with renewable energy (RE) in the agriculture sector so as to make it diesel free by 2024, as part of the government’s commitment to increase the share of non-fossil fuels by 2030 and becoming net zero emitter by 2070, the Power Ministry said on Friday.

Concept note-2: -India will replace use of diesel in farming with renewables by 2024 and, union power and renewable energy minister R K Singh said on Friday. He said the country targets to make the agriculture sector diesel- free in the next two years.

Concept note-3: -The Central government has set the target for replacing diesel with renewable energy in the agricultural sector by 2024.
