The electronic trading platform promoted by Rubber Board went live in which the Ahmedabad-based i-Sourcing Technologies is the technology partner. What is the name of this electronic trading platform?

(A) rRube

(B) iRube

(C) tRube

(D) ** mRube

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Ahmedabad-based i-Sourcing Technologies is thetechnology partner. mRube follows a simple one-time registration. KN Raghavan,Executive Director will launch the beta version of the electronic platform at afunction in the presence of the Rubber Producer Society and other industryrepresentatives.

Concept note-2: The Beta launch of ‘mRube’, the e-tradingplatform for natural rubber, was inaugurated on Wednesday by KN Raghavan,Executive Director, Rubber Board, at the Rubber Research Institute of India,Kottayam.

Concept note-3: Notes: mRube, the electronic trading platformpromoted by Rubber Board will go live from June 8. The Ahmedabad-basedi-Sourcing Technologies is the technology partner. mRube follows a simple one-timeregistration.

Concept note-4: mRube, the electronic market for natural rubber,was rolled out on June 8.
