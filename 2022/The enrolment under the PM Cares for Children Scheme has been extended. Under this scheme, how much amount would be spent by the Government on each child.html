[SOURCES]

The enrolment under the PM Cares for Children Scheme has been extended. Under this scheme, how much amount would be spent by the Government on each child?

(A) Rs 20 lakh

(B) ** Rs 10 lakh

(C) Rs 5 lakh

(D) Rs 5 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The PM CARES for children scheme inter-alia providessupport to these children through convergent approach, gap funding for ensuringeducation, health, monthly stipend from the age of 18 years, and lump sum amountof Rs. 10 lakhs on attaining 23 years of age.

Concept note-2: Children areentitled to receive monthly stipend between the age of 18 and 23 years, byinvesting the corpus of Rs. 10 lakhs into Monthly Income Scheme of PostOffice. They will receive the amount of Rs. 10 lakh on attaining the age of 23years.

Concept note-3: Further, cheque or demand draft drawn in favour of ‘PMCARES Fund’ can be sent to Under Secretary (Funds), PMO, South Block, NewDelhi, Pin -110011 along with Name, Address, Mobile No. and E-mail ID of thedonor.
