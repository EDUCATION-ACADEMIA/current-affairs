The first Centre of Excellence for Khadi will open in which city?

(A) Hyderabad

(B) Pune

(C) ** New Delhi

(D) Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The First Centre of Excellence for Khadi (CoEK) has beenestablished in which state/UT? Notes: Union Minister for Micro Small andMedium Enterprises Narayan Rane inaugurated the First Centre of Excellencefor Khadi (CoEK) at the National Institute of Fashion Technology, NewDelhi.

Concept note-2: The Centre of Excellence for Khadi has set out to makeKhadi a universal, classic and value driven brand. Union Minister for Micro Smalland Medium Enterprises Shri Narayan Rane will inaugurate the first Centre ofExcellence for Khadi on 11th May 2022 in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: TheCentre of Excellence for Khadi has been established at the National Institute ofFashion Technology as a hub in Delhi and spokes in Bangalore, Gandhinagar,Kolkata, and Shillong.

Concept note-4: KVIC (Khadi Village IndustriesCommission) was set up during the second five year plan in 1956. It was formedunder the KVIC Act of 1956, this is a statutory body formed by the Governmentof India. It seeks to promote, plan, protect and develop khadi and VillageIndustries.
