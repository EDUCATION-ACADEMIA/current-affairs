The first Khelo India National Ranking Women Archery Tournament has been held in which city?

(A) Durgapur

(B) Dhanbad

(C) ** Jamshedpur

(D) Rourkela

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: First Khelo India Women National Ranking ArcheryTournament kicks off in Jamshedpur.

Concept note-2: TATA Archery Academy,Jamshedpur (Jharkhand) is set to host the first phase of the tournament on April 12and 13.

Concept note-3: Step 1- Visit the Official Website Ministry of Sports.Step 2- On Homepage, Click on “Play India Youth Games in 2021 RegistrationForm” Link. Step 3- Registration Form page will displayed on screen. Step 4- Nowenter the required details & After this upload your photo and agree to the terms andconditions.

Concept note-4: The cumulative points of the 5 phases shall becalculated to arrive at the ranking of the top 16 archers for the final phase inDecember wherein the top 16 archers shall be given cash awards, amounting to atotal of Rs 37.5 Lakh.
