The first Partial Solar Eclipse will occur on which date?

(A) May 05

(B) ** April 30

(C) April 29

(D) May 01

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: If we talk about the solar eclipse in October 2022 that willhappen in Delhi, then the solar eclipse will start there at 4:29 pm Indian time.Similarly, the solar eclipse in Chennai will start at 5:14 pm. The solar eclipse inKolkata will start at 4:52 pm Indian Standard Time.

Concept note-2: TheSolar Eclipse will occur on Tuesday, October 25, after Diwali. According toDrik Panchang, this eclipse is a partial Solar Eclipse, which would be visiblefrom 04:29 pm. The eclipse will end with the sunset at 05:42 pm, and themaximum eclipse time will be at 05:30 pm.

Concept note-3: The last solareclipse took place on June 21, 2020, and the next eclipse will take place onAugust 2, 2027. Meanwhile, there will also be a lunar eclipse to take placeon October 28, 2023, but it will not be visible.

Concept note-4: Thesolar eclipse will take place between 4:29 pm and 5:43 pm. The eclipse willbe visible globally like in parts of Europe, Northern Africa, and parts ofwestern and central Asia. In India the celestial phenomenon will be sightedacross Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Ujjain, Varanasi, Bengaluru, andMathura.
