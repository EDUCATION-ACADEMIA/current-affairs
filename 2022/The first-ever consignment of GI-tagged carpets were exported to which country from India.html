The first-ever consignment of GI-tagged carpets were exported to which country from India?

(A) Japan

(B) UAE

(C) ** Germany

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Q. Which Indian state/UT exported its first ever consignmentof GI-tagged carpets to Germany? Notes: The first ever consignment of GI-taggedcarpets of Jammu and Kashmir were exported to Germany from NewDelhi.

Concept note-2: It came into force with effect from 15th September 2003.Darjeeling Tea was the first Indian product to get a GI tag.

Concept note-3: TheJ&K government has finally started a Geographical Indication (GI) registry for thefamed Kashmiri carpet with a quick response (QR) code with details of artisans andthe material used. The first-ever consignment of GI-tagged carpets was exported toGermany from New Delhi in March 2022.

Concept note-4: The first everconsignment of GI-tagged carpets were exported to Germany from New Delhi onFriday.
