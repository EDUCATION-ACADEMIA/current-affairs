The first-of-its-kind EV charging station powered by bio-gas was inaugurated in which city?

(A) ** Mumbai

(B) Noida

(C) Bangalore

(D) Indore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India’s first EV charging station powered by bio-gas wasinaugurated in Mumbai, Maharashtra. This station be will expected to generate 220units of electricity from food waste collected from its nearby areas, mostly from bulkgenerators like hotels and offices.

Concept note-2: Developed and installed byGPS Renewables with support from Biotechnology Industry Research AssistanceCouncil (BIRAC) and AeroCare Clean Energy, the EV charging station located atHaji Ali in Mumbai is India’s first to be powered by biogasIt works in tandem with abiomethane plant positioned alongside it.

Concept note-3: India’s first organicwaste-powered Electric Vehicle (EV) charging station has been inaugurated atKeshavrao Khadye Marg, Mumbai, Aditya Thackeray, Maharashtra’s Minister ofEnvironment and Tourism inaugurated this charging station on 9th May2022.

Concept note-4: Aaditya Thackeray tweeted, “Inauguraated India’sfirst EV charging station powered by bio-gas at Keshavrao Khadye Margtoday, which generates 220 units of energy from household waste. Along withpowering street lights, this energy plant will now charge electric vehiclestoo.”

Concept note-5: Q. Which is the first Indian city to operate anorganic waste-powered EV charging station? Notes: The country’s first organicwaste-powered EV charging station was recently inaugurated in the city ofMumbai.
