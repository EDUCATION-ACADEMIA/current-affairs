The largest commercial jet terminal in the country opened at which city’s airport?

(A) Jaipur

(B) Bangalore

(C) Kolkata

(D) ** Kochi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Country’s largest business jet terminal commissioned at CochinInternational Airport. Kerala Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan inaugurated thecountry’s first chartered gateway, and a business jet terminal at the CochinInternational Airport this evening.

Concept note-2: Cochin InternationalAirport (IATA: COK, ICAO: VOCI) serves Kochi in the state of Kerala,India.

Concept note-3: Complete answer: Nedimbacherry InternationalAirport is situated in the territory of Kerala. Cochin International Airport isa global air terminal serving the city of Kochi, in the province of Kerala,India.

Concept note-4: It is the busiest and largest airport in the stateof Kerala and 4th biggest airport in South India. As of 2019, the CochinInternational Airport caters to 61.8% of the total air passenger movementin Kerala.

Concept note-5: With this, Cochin airport has become thefourth airport in the country to operate a private jet terminal. With twomajestic domestic and international travel terminals, Cochin InternationalAirport Limited(CIAL) is now home to the country’s biggest Business JetTerminal.
