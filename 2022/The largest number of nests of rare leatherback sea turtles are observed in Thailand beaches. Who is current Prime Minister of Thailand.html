The largest number of nests of rare leatherback sea turtles are observed in Thailand beaches. Who is current Prime Minister of Thailand?

(A) Abhisit Vejjajiva

(B) Chavarat Charnvirakul

(C) Yingluck Shinawatra

(D) ** Prayut Chan-o-cha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Leatherbacks occur in the Atlantic, Pacific, and Indian Oceans.Nesting beaches are primarily located in tropical latitudes around the world.Globally, the largest remaining nesting aggregations are found in Trinidad andTobago, West-Indies (Northwest Atlantic) and Gabon, Africa (SoutheastAtlantic).

Concept note-2: Leatherbacks have been recorded as far north asAlaska, and as far south as Africa’s Cape of Good Hope. The Pacific may now haveas few as 2,300 adult females.

Concept note-3: Population Estimate*: Between34,000 and 36,000 nesting females.

Concept note-4: The conservancy said thelargest leatherback ever caught was almost 10 feet long from its beak to it tail andweighed almost 2,020 pounds.
