(A) ARVI Trust

(B) Neptune Foundation.

(C) Isha Foundation

(D) ** NIMHANS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Further, MWCD in collaboration with BPR&D will alsoinitiate self defense training programmes through NIPCCD.
