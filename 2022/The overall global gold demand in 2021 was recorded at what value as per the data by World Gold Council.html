The overall global gold demand in 2021 was recorded at what value as per the data by World Gold Council?

(A) ** 4, 021.3 tonnes

(B) 3, 658.8 tonnes

(C) 5, 051.5 tonnes

(D) 3, 749.2 tonnes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The demand for gold across India was about 797.3metric tons in 2021. This represented an increase of some 78.6 percent incomparison to the previous year, and the first growth after four years ofcontinual annual decline.

Concept note-2: World Gold Council – WGC Whyin the News? According to the latest World Gold Council Report titled‘Gold refining and recycling’, India has ranked 4th in global gold recycling in2021.

Concept note-3: India is the second largest gold jewellery consumer inthe world. In 2021, India bought 611 tonnes of gold jewellery, second onlyto China (673 tonnes), but comfortably ahead of all other gold-consumingmarkets, according to the ‘Jewellery Demand and Trade’ report released bythe World Gold Council on January 19, 2023.

Concept note-4: Golddemand globally 2010-2022. Worldwide gold demand amounted to 4,740.8metric tons in 2022, an increase from 4,012.8 metric tons in the previousyear.
