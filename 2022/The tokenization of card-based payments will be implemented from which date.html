The tokenization of card-based payments will be implemented from which date?

(A) ** October 01

(B) October 04

(C) September 03

(D) September 04

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The tokenization of card-based payments will be implementedfrom October 1, 2022. What is tokenization?

Concept note-2: “As per theregulatory guidelines of RBI, with effect from 01st October, 2022, Banksintroduce tokenization facility for all the card users of both Debit and Creditcard holders of RuPay/VISA/Master card to enhance online transactionsecurity.

Concept note-3: How did India decide to carry out tokenisation? InSeptember 2021, the RBI prohibited merchants from storing customer card details ontheir servers with effect from January 1, 2022, and mandated the adoption ofcard-on-file (CoF) tokenisation as an alternative.

Concept note-4: By September30, all credit and debit card information used for online, in-app and point-of-sale(POS) transactions must be replaced with distinct tokens, according to the ReserveBank of India (RBI).
