The ultra-luxury Sir M Visvesvaraya Railway Terminal has been made operational in which of the Indian city?

(A) ** Bengaluru

(B) Hyderabad

(C) Indore

(D) Varanasi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The ultra luxury Sir M Visvesvaraya Railway Terminal inKarnataka’s capital city Bengaluru was made operational. The Ernakulam Tri-WeeklyExpress chugged trough the station to mark this special occasion. According to therailway officials, the air-conditioned SMV Railway Terminal is a Rs 314 croreproject.

Concept note-2: Visvesvaraya Terminal, Bengaluru railway station(station code: SMVB) is an Indian Railways train station located in Baiyyapanahalli,Bangalore in the Indian state of Karnataka.

Concept note-3: #Bengaluru getsIndia’s first ultra-luxury railway terminal with airport-like facilities . The Sir MVisvesvaraya Terminal at Baiyappanahalli is built at the cost of Rs 314 crore and isopen for public use now. The railway terminal is modelled after the KempegowdaInternational Airport in Bengaluru.

Concept note-4: Some of the popularterminal stations in India are Anand Vihar Terminal (ANVT), Chhatrapati ShivajiTerminus (CST), Lokmanya Tilak Terminus (LTT), Bandra Terminus (BDTS), andRajendra Nagar Terminus (RJPB).

Concept note-5: “Sir M Visvesvarayais India’s first centralised AC Terminal, a benchmark for future Railwayterminals.
