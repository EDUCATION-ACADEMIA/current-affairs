The world’s first population estimation of which species has been revealed to be 176 by the CDA?

(A) Indian Dolphin

(B) Olive Ridley Turtle

(C) Crocodile

(D) ** Fishing Cat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Chilika Lakehas 176 fishing cats, a globally threatenedspecies, says the estimation. : The Chilika Lake, Asia’s largest brackish waterlagoon, has 176 fishing cats, according to a census conducted by ChilikaDevelopment Authority (CDA) in collaboration with The Fishing Cat Project(TFCP).

Concept note-2: Chilika: Chilika Development Authority (CDA) incollaboration with The Fishing Cat Project (TFCP) recently conductedworld’s first fishing cat census at Chilika lake, Asia’s largest brackish waterlagoon.

Concept note-3: The fishing cat is listed as Endangered on theIUCN Red List, which means that it faces a high threat of extinction in thewild.

Concept note-4: IUCN Red List: Vulnerable. Despite multiple threats, theFishing Cat was recently downlisted to “Vulnerable” from “Endangered” in the IUCNRed List species assessment. CITES: Appendix II. Indian Wildlife Protection Act,1972: Schedule I.

Concept note-5: The fishing cat, Prionailurus viverrinus, is ahighly elusive wild cat species found primarily in wetland and mangrove habitatswith some populations in Sri Lanka having even been recorded in highly urbanisedlandscapes and montane forests.
