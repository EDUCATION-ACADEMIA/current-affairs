The world’s largest plant has been discovered in which country?

(A) Canada

(B) Germany

(C) ** Australia

(D) Israel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The world’s largest living plant has been identified in theshallow waters off the coast of Western Australia. The sprawling seagrass, a marineflowering plant known as Posidonia australis, stretches for more than 112miles (180 kilometers) in Shark Bay, a wilderness area protected as a WorldHeritage site.

Concept note-2: A form of flowering plant that far exceedsPando as the largest organism on earth in breadth, is the giant marine plant,Posidonia australis, living in Shark Bay, Australia. Its length is about 180 km(112 mi) and it covers an area of 200 km2 (77 sq mi). It is estimated tobe over 4,500 years old.

Concept note-3: The largest known plant onEarth - a seagrass roughly three times the size of Manhattan - has beendiscovered off the coast of Australia. Using genetic testing, scientists havedetermined a large underwater meadow in Western Australia is in fact oneplant.

Concept note-4: Largest Plant: Kwango Giant CycadKwango giant cycads(Encephalartos laurentianus) has stems which grow to around 60 feet long and4 feet thick. They also have the longest leaves of any cycad at around 23feet.
