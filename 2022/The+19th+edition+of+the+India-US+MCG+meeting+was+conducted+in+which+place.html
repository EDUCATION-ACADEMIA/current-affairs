The 19th edition of the India-US MCG meeting was conducted in which place?

(A) Pune, Maharashtra

(B) Pokhran, Rajasthan

(C) New Delhi, Delhi

(D) ** Agra, Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India & US hold 19th Military Cooperation meeting in Agra to strengthen defence cooperation.

Concept note-2: -The 19th edition of the India-US Military Cooperation Group (MCG) meeting was conducted in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. Madhavi Gaur Published On March 4th, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The 16th edition of the Yudh Abhyas exercise took place between February 8 and February 21, 2021, at Mahajan Field Firing Range in Rajasthan.

Concept note-4: -The India-US MCG is a forum established to take forward defence cooperation between the countries through regular talks at the strategic and operational levels between headquarters, Integrated Defence Staff and the US Indo-Pacific Command.

Concept note-5: -India is ranked 4 of 145 out of the countries considered for the annual Global Firepower review.
