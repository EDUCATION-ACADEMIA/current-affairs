The 46th GST Council meeting was called to discuss the GST rate on which sector?

(A) ** Textile

(B) Oil

(C) Health

(D) Electronics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -GST Hike on Textile Deferred: The Goods and Services Tax (GST) Council, a federal indirect tax authority, decided on Friday to put on hold a decision to hike the GST rate on numerous textile and clothing goods in response to several complaints from the textile industry.

Concept note-2: -The 46th GST Council meeting was held on Friday, 31st December 2021, at Vigyan Bhavan in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -The 47th GST Council Meeting was held on 28th and 29th June 2022 in Chandigarh.

Concept note-4: -From January 2022, the GST rate on fabrics has been raised to 12% from 5%, and the GST rate on garments of any value has been raised to 12%, compared to the previous rate of 5% on items priced up to INR 1,000.

Concept note-5: -15/2021 dated 18/11/2021-Central Tax (Rate), where the rate of GST on certain works contract services provided to Government Authority and Government Entity has been increased from 12% to 18% w.e.f., 01-01-2022.
