The CM of Sikkim, has declared whom as “State Butterfly of Sikkim”?

(A) ** Blue Duke

(B) Sumalia

(C) Phaedyma

(D) Auzakia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sikkim Chief Minister Prem Singh Tamang on the occasion of World Environment Day on 5 June 2022 declared Blue Duke the state butterfly. Blue Duke, which goes by the scientific name Bassarona durga, is unique to Sikkim and the eastern Himalayas. It was discovered in the state in 1858.

Concept note-2: -During the celebration of World Environment Day 2022; the Chief Minister of Sikkim, P.S. Golay, declared Blue Duke as “State Butterfly of Sikkim”.
