The Central Government released Postage Stamp in the Honour of which Legendary Singer?

(A) Meena Kumari

(B) M. S. Subbulakshmi

(C) ** Lata Mangeshkar

(D) Vyjayanthimala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union government has decided to issue a postal stamp to honour legendary singer Lata Mangeshkar.

Concept note-2: -Sachin Tendulkar is the eighth Indian to have a postage stamp issued on him during his lifetime. 2. Mother Teresa: She was a Roman Catholic nun. There would be no such person in the Indian subcontinent, who is not aware of the name of Mother Teresa, a rich personality of extraordinary personality.

Concept note-3: -The First Stamp of Independent India was issued on 21 November 1947. It depicts the Indian Flag with the patriots’ slogan, Jai Hind (Long Live India), on the top right hand corner. It was valued at three and one-half annas.

Concept note-4: -The new system of prepaid postage was introduced in the reign of the then Governor-General, Lord Dalhousie.

Concept note-5: -On 15 August 1948, on the occasion of the first anniversary of India’s Independence Day, Gandhi was honored as the first Indian to be depicted on stamps of India.
