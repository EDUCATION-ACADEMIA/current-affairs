The Global Center of Excellence in Affordable and Clean Energy (GCoE-ACE) has been set up at which of these institutions?

(A) ** IIT Dharwad

(B) IIT Kanpur

(C) IIT Delhi

(D) IIT Ropar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Institute of Technology Dharwad has established a Global Center of Excellence in Affordable and Clean Energy (GCoE-ACE) with the help of the Strategic Alliances division of the office of the Principal Scientific Advisor of Government of India.

Concept note-2: -In which institution the Global Center of Excellence in Affordable and Clean Energy (GCoE-ACE) was launched by K. Vijay Raghavan? Explanation: The one of its kind Global Center of Excellence in Affordable and Clean Energy(GCoE-ACE) was launched at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Dharwad(IITDh), Karnataka.

Concept note-3: -The Global Center of Excellence in Affordable and Clean energy was recently launched at IIT Dharwad. The center will enhance the research in affordable and clean energy. The center will create, incubate technologies, physical infrastructure. Also, it will create clean energy solutions.

Concept note-4: -Suzlon Energy Suzlon is one of India’s leading renewable energy companies. Its services span the entire life of wind energy projects. It designs, develops, and manufactures wind turbine generators (WTGs). It also provides allied services, providing it a strong presence across the wind power value chain.

Concept note-5: -Adani Green Energy Ltd 3,38,515 cr., the highest amongst the list of top 5 green energy stocks. The company has a renewable energy portfolio of  20,434 MW. Adani Green Energy Ltd is the only company on this list that has wind, solar, and hybrid power as its product offering.
