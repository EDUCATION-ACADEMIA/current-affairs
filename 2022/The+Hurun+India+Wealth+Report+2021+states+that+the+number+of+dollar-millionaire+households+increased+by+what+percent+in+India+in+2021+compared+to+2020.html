The Hurun India Wealth Report 2021 states that the number of dollar-millionaire households increased by what percent in India in 2021, compared to 2020?

(A) 21%

(B) 15%

(C) ** 11%

(D) 17%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the Hurun India Wealth Report 2021, the number of dollar-millionaire households in India has increased by 11% to 4,58,000 households compared to last year. These households have a net worth of at least INR 7 crore.

Concept note-2: -In 2021, India had 7.96 lakh millionaires, which is expected to grow 105 per cent to 16.32 lakh.

Concept note-3: -India has witnessed a 12 per cent year-on-year (YoY) growth in total household wealth in 2021, leading financial services company Credit Suisse said in a recent report. India’s total household wealth stood at $14.2 trillion at the end of 2021, which was up by $1.5 trillion or 12 per cent from 2020.
