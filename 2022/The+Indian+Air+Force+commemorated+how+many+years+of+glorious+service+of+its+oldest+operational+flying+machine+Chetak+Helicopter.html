The Indian Air Force commemorated how many years of glorious service of its oldest operational flying machine Chetak Helicopter?

(A) 70

(B) 40

(C) 50

(D) ** 60

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Air Force commemorates 60 years of service of Chetak Helicopters.

Concept note-2: -The workhorse of the Indian Air Force and its oldest helicopter, Chetak, is set to complete 60 years in service next month, and the IAF will celebrate the chopper’s diamond jubilee at Hakimpet Air Force Station near Secunderabad on April 2 and 3, officials familiar with matter said on Monday on condition of anonymity.

Concept note-3: -Indian Air Force Day 2022: Indian Air Force Day is observed on October 8, every year. The Indian Air Force was officially established on October 8, 1932 and this year marked as the 90th-anniversary celebration of the IAF.

Concept note-4: -Indian Air Force Day 2022: IAF Marks 90 Years With Celebration In Chandigarh. Indians proudly celebrate October 8 as the day to pay tribute to the air warriors of the Indian Air Force (IAF) who tirelessly defend the nation by securing the Indian airspace and conducting rescue operations during natural calamities.

Concept note-5: -Till date, HAL has produced and sold more than 350 of these versatile Helicopters which are in service both in India and abroad.
