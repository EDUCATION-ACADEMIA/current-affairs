The International Tea Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) May 22

(B) May 20

(C) ** May 21

(D) May 23

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -December 15 is celebrated as International Tea Day in several countries worldwide. This day not only celebrates tea as a special beverage but also promotes sustainable tea manufacture, business, and consumption.

Concept note-2: -The first International Tea Day was celebrated in New Delhi in 2005, with later celebrations organized in Sri Lanka in 2006 and 2008.

Concept note-3: -International Tea Day is significant because it allows the tea industry to continue with its critical role in combating hunger, reducing poverty, and safeguarding livelihoods and natural resources. The theme for International Tea Day 2022 is not known yet. But last year, the theme was Tea and Fair Trade.

Concept note-4: -Theme: International Tea Day 2022 Fair-trade tea will not only help these regions better utilise their resources but will also highlight their valuable goods on the global market.
