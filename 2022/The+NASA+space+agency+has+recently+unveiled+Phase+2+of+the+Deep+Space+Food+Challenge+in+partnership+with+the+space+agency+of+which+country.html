The NASA space agency has recently unveiled Phase 2 of the ‘Deep Space Food Challenge’ in partnership with the space agency of which country?

(A) ** Canada

(B) Russia

(C) Japan

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NASA and the Canadian Space Agency aspire to provide future deep space explorers the technology to produce nutritious and tasty meals for long-duration space missions.

Concept note-2: -Sponsoring/Partner Organizations: NASA coordinated with the Canadian Space Agency (CSA) to develop and launch the Challenge; with CSA providing its own prize purse for Canadian Teams. The Methuselah Foundation is the Allied Organization executing the Challenge for NASA.

Concept note-3: -NASA is a United States government agency that is responsible for science and technology related to air and space. The Space Age started in 1957 with the launch of the Soviet satellite Sputnik. NASA was created in 1958.

Concept note-4: -Teams must submit all required documentation by 5p CDT February 28, 2022 and meet the eligibility requirements in order to participate in the Challenge. The registration process will be administered by NASA’s Allied Organization, Methuselah Foundation.

Concept note-5: -That’s because NASA isn’t a private or publicly-owned company. Instead, it’s an official agency of the U.S. government. That means it’s technically owned by the United States and receives government funding just like the Department of Justice, Department of Education, National Park Service, etc.
