The Times Higher Education released THE Impact Rankings 2022, what is the rank of India?

(A) 1

(B) 2

(C) ** 4

(D) 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Times Higher Education (THE) Impact Rankings 2022: India ranked 4th. The Times Higher Education (THE) has released the 2022 edition of its Impact Rankings.

Concept note-2: -India is the third most-represented country in the Times Higher Education Asia University Rankings 2022, with 71 of its institutes figuring in the list, up from 63 last year.

Concept note-3: -With 75 institutes included in the Times Higher Education (THE) World University Rankings 2023, India is the fifth most represented country on the list.

Concept note-4: -The 2022 Impact Rankings is the fourth edition and the overall ranking includes 1,406 universities from 106 countries/regions. The overall ranking is led by an Australian institution: Western Sydney University. In second place is the US’s Arizona State University and third is Western University in Canada.
