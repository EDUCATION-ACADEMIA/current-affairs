The central government has extended the “Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana” for how long?

(A) June 2022

(B) August 2022

(C) July 2022

(D) ** September 2022

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Centre extends Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana (PMGKAY) for another three months (October 2022-December 2022) | Prime Minister of India.

Concept note-2: -Rs. 80,000 Crore. The scheme has been extended for further period October - December 2022.

Concept note-3: -In Phase V, the free ration scheme was extended for the fifth time till March 2022.

Concept note-4: -The Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana was launched from April, 2020, which is also operating at present. Under this, 05 kg food grains are provided per unit to Antyodaya/eligible household beneficiaries. The said scheme will continue till September 2022.
