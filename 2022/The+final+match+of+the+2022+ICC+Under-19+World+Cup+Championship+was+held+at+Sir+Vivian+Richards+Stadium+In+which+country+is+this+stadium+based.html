The final match of the 2022 ICC Under-19 World Cup Championship was held at Sir Vivian Richards Stadium. In which country is this stadium based?

(A) England

(B) ** Antigua and Barbuda

(C) Barbados

(D) West Indies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -James Rew of England plays the ball through midwicket during the ICC U19 Men’s Cricket World Cup Final match between England and India at Sir Vivian Richards Stadium on February 05, 2022 in Antigua, Antigua and Barbuda.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is India. Shaik Rasheed and Nishant Sindhu cracked the 50s in chase of 190 as India clinched record-extending fifth U19 World Cup title in Antigua.

Concept note-3: -What time does the India Women U19 vs England Women U19 T20 World Cup final start? The India Women U19 vs England Women U19 T20 World Cup final will start at 5:15 PM IST.
