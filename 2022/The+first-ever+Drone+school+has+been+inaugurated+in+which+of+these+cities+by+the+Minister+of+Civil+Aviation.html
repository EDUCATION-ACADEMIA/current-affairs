The first-ever Drone school has been inaugurated in which of these cities by the Minister of Civil Aviation?

(A) Dehradun

(B) Chandigarh

(C) Gurugram

(D) ** Gwalior

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1) In Arunachal Pradesh’s Itanagar, PM Modi inaugurated the Donyi Polo Airport. The name is a reference to age-old indigenous reference to Sun (Donyi) and Moon (Polo) in the northeastern state. Developed at a cost of ₹640 crore, the airport aims to boost connectivity and boost the trade and tourism in the region.

Concept note-2: -Madhya Pradesh was also the first state in the country to organise a Drone Fair in Gwalior in December 2021.
