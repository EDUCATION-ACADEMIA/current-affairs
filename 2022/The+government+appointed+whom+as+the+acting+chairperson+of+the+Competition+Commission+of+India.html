The government appointed whom as the acting chairperson of the Competition Commission of India?

(A) Tanu Dixit

(B) Bhavana Kumari

(C) ** Sangeeta Verma

(D) Soniya Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Competition Commission of India (CCI) is without a full-time Chairperson since the retirement of Ashok Kumar Gupta from the post on October 25, 2022. Verma, who is a member, has been the acting Chairperson since October last year.

Concept note-2: -The government has extended the tenure of Sangeeta Verma as the acting Chairperson of the Competition Commission of India (CCI). Ms. Verma has been the acting Chairperson of the anti-trust regulator since October last year after Ashok Kumar Gupta demitted office as the full-time Chairperson.

Concept note-3: -Govt extends tenure of Sangeeta Verma as CCI’s acting Chairperson.

Concept note-4: -CCI consists of a Chairperson and 6 Members appointed by the Central Government.

Concept note-5: -Sangeeta Verma appointed as the acting chief of Competition Commission of India. The government of India has appointed Sangeeta Verma as the acting chairperson of the Competition Commission of India (CCI) on 25 October 2022.
