The mutual fund mobile app eInvest has been launched by which asset management company for the investor?

(A) Invesco Asset Management

(B) ** Edelweiss Asset Management

(C) ICICI Prudential Asset Management Company

(D) Kotak Mahindra Asset Management

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Edelweiss Asset Management has launched the mutual fund investing mobile application eInvest to help investors perform all financial transactions on the go.

Concept note-2: -If you are an existing investor or a new investor with Edelweiss Mutual Fund, you can make transactions, start an SIP, avail Insta Redemption anytime and from anywhere through the eInvest app.

Concept note-3: -Edelweiss Asset Management has launched eInvest- a mutual fund mobile app for investors for a smooth investing experience. eInvest is an easy-to-use app that will help investors perform all financial transactions on the go.

Concept note-4: -First Phase - 1964-1987 Unit Trust of India (UTI) was established in 1963 by an Act of Parliament.

Concept note-5: -Definition: A new fund offer (NFO) is the first time subscription offer for a new scheme launched by the asset management companies (AMCs). A new fund offer is launched in the market to raise capital from the public in order to buy securities like shares, govt. bonds etc.
