The sea-level trials of India’s first indigenously developed Flying Trainer was successfully completed at Puducherry. What is the name of this trainer aircraft?

(A) ** HANSA-NG

(B) HAWK-NG

(C) HARRIS-NG

(D) HERCULES-NG

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first indigenous Flying Trainer HANSA-NG designed and developed by CSIR-National Aerospace Laboratories, Bangalore under the aegis of Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, has successfully completed the sea level trials at Puducherry from 19th February to 5th March, 2022.

Concept note-2: -India’s first indigenous trainer aircraft, HANSA-NG successfully completed engine relight test in air.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi unveiled HTT-40, an indigenous trainer aircraft designed and developed by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), at the India Pavilion during 12th DefExpo in Gandhinagar, Gujarat on October 19, 2022.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Pudducherry. ‘HANSA-NG’, which is India’s first indigenous flying trainer, has successfully completed the sea-level trials in Pudducherry from February 19 to March 5, 2022. The flying trainer has been developed by CSIR-National Aerospace Laboratories. It is powered by Rotax Digital Control Engine.

Concept note-5: -A trainer is a class of aircraft designed specifically to facilitate flight training of pilots and aircrews.
