There was an explosion aboard which Indian Naval Ship?

(A) ** INS Ranvir

(B) INS Vikrant

(C) INS Vishakhapatnam

(D) INS Vikramaditya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: On 18 January, an explosion occurred in an internalcompartment of the Indian Navy’s Rajput-class destroyer, INS Ranvir (D-54),while the ship was docked at the Naval Dockyard in Mumbai. The IndianNavy destroyer explosion incident was initially reported by local media ANINews.

Concept note-2: New Delhi: Three Navy sailors were killed and 11 injuredin an explosion on board the Indian Naval Ship (INS) Ranvir at the Naval dockyardin Mumbai on at 4.30 pm on Tuesday, January 18. INS Ranvir is a destroyership.

Concept note-3: At present, Vikrant is at the government-owned CochinShipyard in Kerala state - where the ship was built and where the commissioningceremony was held.

Concept note-4: Indian Naval Ship (INS) Mormugao (D67),second warship of the P15B class of stealth guided-missile destroyers, wascommissioned in the august presence of Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh at NavalDockyard, Mumbai on December 18, 2022.

Concept note-5: NEW DELHI: Theblast onboard Indian Navy destroyer INS Ranvir that killed three sailors and left 11injured on Tuesday took place in the ship’s air conditioning compartment and waspossibly linked to Freon gas leak, officials familiar with the matter said onWednesday.
