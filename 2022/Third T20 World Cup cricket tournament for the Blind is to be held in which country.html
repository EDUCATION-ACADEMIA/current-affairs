Third T20 World Cup cricket tournament for the Blind is to be held in which country?

(A) Canada

(B) Australia

(C) ** India

(D) America

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The third T20 World Cup Cricket Tournament for the Blindwill be held from December 5 to 17, 2022 in India. The participating nations forWorld Cup 2022 are Australia, Bangladesh, Nepal, Pakistan, South Africa, Sri Lankaand the host, India.

Concept note-2: The 3rd T20 World Cup for Blind was heldin India from 6 -17 December 2022. India, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Australia, Bangladesh,and South Africa competed in the tournament. The Pakistan team was denied a visaby the Indian government.

Concept note-3: World Blind Cricket Council (WBC)has announced that the third Blind T20 World Cup will be held in India inNovember 2022. It was decided that India will host the upcoming edition of theWorld Cup in the recent meeting of WBC.

Concept note-4: BANGALORE:World Blind Cricket Council (WBCC) on Friday awarded the hosting rights of thenext T20 World Cup 2024 to Pakistan, Pakistan Blind Cricket Council (PBCC)confirmed.

Concept note-5: The Indian Blind Cricket Team creates history yetagain, winning the 3rd T20 World Cup Cricket for the Blind 2022 title for the thirdtime! The media could not be played.
