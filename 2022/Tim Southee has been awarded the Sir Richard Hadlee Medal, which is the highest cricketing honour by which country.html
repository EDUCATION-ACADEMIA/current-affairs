Tim Southee has been awarded the Sir Richard Hadlee Medal, which is the highest cricketing honour by which country?

(A) South Africa

(B) ** New Zealand

(C) England

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Southee is New Zealand’s third-highest wicket-taker in Tests,23 wickets behind Daniel Vettori and 93 short of the record-holder the award isnamed after, Sir Richard Hadlee.

Concept note-2: Tim Southee, a right-armswing bowler, burst upon the international scene in great style. He was still only 19,with only one T20I cap behind him, and fresh from a Player-of-the-Tournamentperformance at the 2008 Under-19 World Cup when he was handed a Test debutagainst England in Napier.
