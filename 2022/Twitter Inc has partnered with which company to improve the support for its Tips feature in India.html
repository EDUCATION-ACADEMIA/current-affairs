Twitter Inc has partnered with which company to improve the support for its ‘Tips’ feature in India?

(A) Instamojo

(B) PayU

(C) Infibeam Avenues

(D) ** Paytm

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Bengaluru: Twitter Inc said on Wednesday that itpartnered with Paytm’s payment gateway to ramp up the support for its ‘Tips’feature in India. Through ‘Tips’, users can send cash payments to theirfavourite content creators on the microblogging platform or small businessowners.

Concept note-2: Twitter on February 16 announced a partnership withdigital payments and financial services firm Paytm to boost its tipping feature inIndia.

Concept note-3: What is the Tips feature of Twitter? Using the Tipsfeature, users can send payments to their favourite content creators on Twitter. Tipsis available since November to all Twitter users above 18 years of age in India. It isavailable in Indian languages including Bengali, Gujarati, Hindi, Kannada, Marathiand Tamil.

Concept note-4: The correct answer is Twitter. Twitter has partneredwith Paytm Payments Services Limited, as a payments partner for Tips in India.Through Tips, users can support their favorite creators or small businessowners on Twitter by using any Paytm Payment Instrument like PaytmUPI.
