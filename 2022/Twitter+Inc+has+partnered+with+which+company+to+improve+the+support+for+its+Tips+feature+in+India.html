Twitter Inc has partnered with which company to improve the support for its ‘Tips’ feature in India?

(A) Instamojo

(B) PayU

(C) Infibeam Avenues

(D) ** Paytm

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Twitter has partnered with Paytm Payments Services Limited, as a payments partner for Tips in India. Through Tips, users can support their favorite creators or small business owners on Twitter by using any Paytm Payment Instrument like Paytm UPI.

Concept note-2: -Twitter on February 16 announced a partnership with digital payments and financial services firm Paytm to boost its tipping feature in India.

Concept note-3: -Bengaluru: Twitter Inc said on Wednesday that it partnered with Paytm’s payment gateway to ramp up the support for its ‘Tips’ feature in India. Through ‘Tips’, users can send cash payments to their favourite content creators on the microblogging platform or small business owners.

Concept note-4: -What is the Tips feature of Twitter? Using the Tips feature, users can send payments to their favourite content creators on Twitter. Tips is available since November to all Twitter users above 18 years of age in India. It is available in Indian languages including Bengali, Gujarati, Hindi, Kannada, Marathi and Tamil.
