Two volcanic eruptions in Kamchatka Peninsula Siberia region erupted in which country?

(A) Canada

(B) ** Russia

(C) USA

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Two volcanoes in the Kamchatka Peninsula erupted recently.They are among the six volcanoes in the northeastern part of Russia that arecurrently showing signs of increased activity.

Concept note-2: KamchatkaPeninsula, also spelled Kamčatka, Russian Poluostrov Kamchatka, peninsula in fareastern Russia, lying between the Sea of Okhotsk on the west and the PacificOcean and Bering Sea on the east.

Concept note-3: From geographicnorth to south, the erupting volcanoes are Shiveluch, Bezymianny, Kizimen,Karymsky, and Zhupanovsky.

Concept note-4: Kamchatka is a mountainouspeninsula in eastern Russia at the northeastern boundary of Asia, lying betweenlatitudes 51°N and 60°N and longitude 160°E, in the north-west Pacific (Fig.1).
