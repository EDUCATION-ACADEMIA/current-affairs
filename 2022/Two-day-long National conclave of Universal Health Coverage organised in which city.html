Two-day-long National conclave of Universal Health Coverage organised in which city?

(A) Chennai

(B) ** Varanasi

(C) Delhi

(D) Jaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: To commemorate Universal Health Coverage (UHC)Day, 2022, the 2-day National Health Ministers’ Conclave was organised inVaranasi.

Concept note-2: Union Ministry of Health & Family Welfare isorganizing a two days conclave commemorating the “Universal CoverageDay (UHC) 2022” on 10th and 11th December 2022 at Varanasi, UttarPradesh.

Concept note-3: “Build the world we want: A healthy future forall”Everyone, everywhere deserves access to quality health services, in times ofcrisis and calm.

Concept note-4: This year’s theme, “Build the world wewant: A healthy future for all,” emphasizes that in order to build stronghealth systems we need equity, trust, healthy environments, investmentsand accountability.

Concept note-5: Ayushman Bharat (PMJAY) waslaunched as a step towards Universal Health Coverage (UHC). UHC entailsensuring all people have access to quality health services – including prevention,promotion, treatment, rehabilitation, and palliation – without incurring financialhardship.
