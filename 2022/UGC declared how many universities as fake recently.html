UGC declared how many universities as fake recently?

(A) 22

(B) 27

(C) ** 21

(D) 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: A total of 21 institutions are declared as fake by theUGC.

Concept note-2: The University Grants Commission (UGC) hasdeclared 21 universities in India as ‘fake’ and are not empowered to confer anydegree.

Concept note-3: Apart from the 21 fake universities, 2 moreinstitutes - Bhartiya Shiksha Parishad in Lucknow and the Indian Institute ofPlanning and Management in New Delhi have been found in violation of theUGC Act, 1956. The matters of these 2 universities are sub-judice in thecourt.
