US Coast Guard Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) August 01

(B) ** August 04

(C) August 02

(D) August 03

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: August 4 is celebrated as Coast Guard Day to honor theestablishment on that day in 1790 of the Revenue Cutter Service, forebear of today’sCoast Guard, by the Treasury Department.

Concept note-2: U.S. Every year,August 4 is celebrated as the U.S. Coast Guard Birthday, commemorating themilitary organization for its valor and discipline. Since 1790, the U.S. CoastGuard has kept the nation’s waterways safe, playing a critical role in nationalsecurity.

Concept note-3: A ceremony was organised to celebrate the momentousoccasion on January 31 and February 1 at Headquarters, Coast Guard EasternSeaboard.

Concept note-4: Coast Guard Day is held every August 4 tocommemorate the founding of the United States Coast Guard as the RevenueMarine on August 4, 1790, by then-Secretary of the Treasury AlexanderHamilton.

Concept note-5: Today we celebrate the 232nd birthday of theU.S. Coast Guard; our theme for 2022 is: “Honoring our Past… Shaping ourFuture."
