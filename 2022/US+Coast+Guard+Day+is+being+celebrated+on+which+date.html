US Coast Guard Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) August 01

(B) ** August 04

(C) August 02

(D) August 03

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -August 4 is celebrated as Coast Guard Day to honor the establishment on that day in 1790 of the Revenue Cutter Service , forebear of today’s Coast Guard, by the Treasury Department.

Concept note-2: -U.S. Every year, August 4 is celebrated as the U.S. Coast Guard Birthday, commemorating the military organization for its valor and discipline. Since 1790, the U.S. Coast Guard has kept the nation’s waterways safe, playing a critical role in national security.

Concept note-3: -By India Today Web Desk: Every year, February 1 is observed as Indian Coast Guard Day. It is the fourth-largest coast guard in the world.

Concept note-4: -The interim Indian Coast Guard was originally established on February 1, 1977, to prevent the sea-borne smuggling of goods that hampered India’s domestic economy. On August 18, 1978, the Indian Coast Guard (ICG) Day was officially established by the Parliament to be celebrated on February 1.

Concept note-5: -Today we celebrate the 232nd birthday of the U.S. Coast Guard; our theme for 2022 is: “Honoring our Past… Shaping our Future."
