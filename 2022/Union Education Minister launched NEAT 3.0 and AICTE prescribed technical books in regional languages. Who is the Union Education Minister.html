Union Education Minister launched NEAT 3.0 and AICTE prescribed technical books in regional languages. Who is the Union Education Minister?

(A) Piyush Goyal

(B) Narendra Tomar

(C) Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank

(D) ** Dharmendra Pradhan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Union Education Minister and Skill Development Minister ShriDharmendra Pradhan launched NEAT 3.0, a single platform to provide thebest-developed ed-tech solutions and courses to students of the country.The Minister also launched AICTE prescribed technical books in regionallanguages.
