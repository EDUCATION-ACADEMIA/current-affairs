Union Minister for Education has inaugurated IInvenTiv at which IIT?

(A) IIT Madras

(B) IIT Guwahati

(C) IIT Kanpur

(D) ** IIT Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: Union Minister for Education and Skill Development, Dharmendra Pradhan inaugurated IInvenTiv, the first ever all-IIT R&D showcase on Friday at the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi.

Concept note-2: -The first Indian Institute of Technology was founded in May 1950 at the site of the Hijli Detention Camp in Kharagpur, West Bengal. The name “Indian Institute of Technology” was adopted before the formal inauguration of the institute on 18 August 1951 by Maulana Abul Kalam Azad.

Concept note-3: -The first Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) was established in 1951 at Kharagpur.
