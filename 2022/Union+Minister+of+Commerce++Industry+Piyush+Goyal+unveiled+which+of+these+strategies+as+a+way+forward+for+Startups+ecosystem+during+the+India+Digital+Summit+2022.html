[SOURCES]

Union Minister of Commerce & Industry Piyush Goyal unveiled which of these strategies as a way forward for Startups ecosystem, during the India Digital Summit, 2022?

(A) SOAR

(B) GRAB

(C) ** LEAP

(D) EASE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Minister unveiled ‘LEAP’ as the way forward for Startups, - “Leverage, Encourage, Access & Promote”. There is always room for improvement & with Sabka Prayas we can strengthen our Startup ecosystem further, he added.

Concept note-2: -Abhishek Mishra. Answer: [A] Internet and Mobile Association of India (IAMAI) Notes: The 16th edition of India Digital Summit 2022 was organised by the Internet and Mobile Association of India (IAMAI). The report, titled ‘Creating 10 million Digitally Enabled Micro-entrepreneurs’ was also released during the summit.

Concept note-3: -Digital start-ups will drive our economy: Investments, solutions, employment, enablement, and empowerment will come from them! Technology and data-related policies should primarily focus on building a robust data and digital economy by keeping Indian start-ups as their primary focus.

Concept note-4: -South India Digital Summit 2022 to be hosted by Fourth Dimension on November 4th in Coimbatore.

Concept note-5: -Union Minister of Commerce & Industry, Piyush Goyal virtually addressed the 16th India Digital Summit, 2022. The two-day virtual event was organised on January 11 and 12, 2022 by the Internet And Mobile Association of India (IAMAI). The theme of the summit was “Supercharging Startups”.
