United Nations celebrates World Chess Day globally on which date?

(A) July 19

(B) July 18

(C) ** July 20

(D) July 21

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Every year, World Chess Day is observed on 20July.

Concept note-2: Under initiative of FIDE, July 20 has beenobserved as International Chess Day by chess players around the world since1966.

Concept note-3: The International Chess Federation (FIDE) is establishedin Paris. International Chess Day is observed from this day to mark the day theInternational Chess Federation (FIDE) was founded.

Concept note-4: The daycelebrates the long history of chess and recognizes the role chess plays in connectingpeople across societies. The game has broken many barriers – class, language, andcultural – simply by two people sitting down to play one of the most mentallychallenging games in the world.

Concept note-5: Chess is a universal game thatencourages fairness, inclusiveness, and respect for others. It is noteworthy inthis regard because it can support a climate of tolerance and internationalunderstanding.
