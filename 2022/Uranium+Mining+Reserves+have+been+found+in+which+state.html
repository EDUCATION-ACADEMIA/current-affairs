Uranium Mining Reserves have been found in which state?

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) ** Rajasthan

(C) Odisha

(D) Chhattisgarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After Jharkhand and Andhra Pradesh, Rajasthan is the third state where uranium - which is considered one of the rare minerals in the world - has been found.

Concept note-2: -Huge reserves of Uranium have been found at Rohil (Khandela Tehsil) in Sikar district of Rajasthan. With this reserve, this state has come on the world map.

Concept note-3: -Currently, the states of Jharkhand, Telangana, and Andhra Pradesh have India’s largest uranium mines.
