Urban-20 (U20) program being organized in which city?

(A) ** Ahmedabad

(B) Hyderabad

(C) Bangalore

(D) Visakhapatnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With the aim of designing a roadmap for complex urban issues, Ahmedabad is set to host the U20 Engagement Summit.

Concept note-2: -India is ready to host the Urban 20 (U20) meet in Ahmedabad, under its G20 Presidency, for this year. The U20, as one of the G20 engagement groups, offers a venue for city governance from the participating nations to address crucial urban development concerns.

Concept note-3: -The 2023 G20 Delhi summit (Hindi: 2023 G20 ) is the upcoming eighteenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), a summit scheduled to take place in Pragati Maidan, New Delhi in 2023.

Concept note-4: -The members of the G-20 are: Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Italy, Japan, Mexico, Russia, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, South Korea, Turkey, the U.K. and the U.S., as well as the European Union, represented by the rotating council presidency and the European Central Bank.
