Uttarakhand CM Pushkar Singh Dhami has won a crucial by-poll from which seat?

(A) Badrinath

(B) Khatima

(C) ** Champawat

(D) Purola

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: He was re-elected for the position of Chief Minister ofUttarakhand on 21 March 2022. On 3 June 2022 he successfully won the ChampawatAssembly bypoll, which was allowed by the resignation of incumbent KailashChandra Gahtori.

Concept note-2: As of March 2022, Pushkar SinghDhami is the current Chief Minister of Uttarakhand and Leader of the Housein the 5th Vidhan Sabha. The Speaker of the Assembly is Ritu KhanduriBhushan.
