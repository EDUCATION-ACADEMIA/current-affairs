Vande-Bharat-based freight service is going to be introduced on which route?

(A) Delhi-Katra (Jammu)

(B) Delhi-Varanasi

(C) Gandhinagar-Mumbai

(D) ** Delhi-Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: If Indian Railways uses the Vande Bharat trains for freighttransportation, the train will be run on the Delhi-Mumbai route first with acomparatively higher speed to reduce transportation time.

Concept note-2: Thetwo new Vande Bharat Express trains will operate on the Mumbai-Solapur andMumbai-Shirdi routes. The new Vande Bharat Express trains will commencefrom the Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CSMT). While one will take theMumbai-Pune-Solapur route, the other will take the Mumbai-Nashik-Sainagar Shirdiroute.

Concept note-3: Indian Railways after introducing the semi-high-speedtrain Vande Bharat on the Mumbai-Ahmedabad route is looking to launch a freightversion of the train.

Concept note-4: As of now, there are a total of eightVande Bharat Express trains running in India, while the Centre has plans toscale up this project by launching 300 to 400 new Vande Bharat routes inthe near future. Three of the eight Vande Bharat routes are to and fromDelhi.
