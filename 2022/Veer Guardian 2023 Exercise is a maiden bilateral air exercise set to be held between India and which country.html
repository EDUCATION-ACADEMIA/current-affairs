Veer Guardian 2023 Exercise is a maiden bilateral air exercise set to be held between India and which country?

(A) Sudan

(B) ** Japan

(C) Canada

(D) Fiji

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UPSC Mains. Q. Veer Guardian 2023 Exercise is a maidenbilateral air exercise set to be held between India and which country? Notes: IndianAir Force (IAF) and Japanese Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) are set to hold theirmaiden bilateral air exercise, Veer Guardian 23.

Concept note-2: Named VeerGuardian-23, the aerial maneuvers were taking place in the airspace around the AirSelf-Defense Force’s Hyakuri and Iruma air bases in Ibaraki Prefecture, running untilJan. 26.

Concept note-3: 21st EDITION OF INDIA FRANCE BILATERALNAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition of the Bilateral NavalExercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the WesternSeaboard today, 16 Jan 23.

Concept note-4: The inaugural edition of thebilateral air exercise ‘Veer Guardian 2023’ between the Indian Air Force(IAF) and Japan Air Self Defence Force (JASDF) concluded in Japan on 26January 2023. The 16-day long exercise was conducted at Hyakuri air base inJapan’s Ibaraki Prefecture.

Concept note-5: Ans. Exercise Cope Indiastarted as a military training drill at Airbase Gwalior in India around 2004. Incontrast to fighter-training activities, the activity now includes subject matterspecialist interactions, air mobility practice, airdropping training, and large-forceoperations.
