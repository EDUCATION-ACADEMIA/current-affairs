“Vida” is a new brand name launched by which company for its upcoming electric vehicles?

(A) Bajaj Auto

(B) ** Hero MotoCorp

(C) Suzuki

(D) Tvs Motor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Vida, the electric vehicle sub-brand of two-wheelermanufacturer Hero Motocorp, has hinted the launch of its first electric scooter fromthe brand.

Concept note-2: Hero MotoCorp has taken the wraps off their newelectric scooter under the sub-brand, Vida. The automaker launched the V1on Friday as the first electric scooter of the brand, as it looks to catch upwith newer firms who have taken the lead in the country’s clean mobilitypush.

Concept note-3: Hero MotoCorp entered the two-wheeler EV space afterintroducing a standalone brand called ‘Vida’ in October last year, launching itsfirst product in the country in the form of V1.

Concept note-4: HeroMotoCorp has finally launched its new electric scooter under the sub-brand,Vida.

Concept note-5: Bengaluru-based Pravaig Dynamics unveiled its firstelectric sport utility vehicle (SUV) Defy in India on Friday.
