Vidya Balan has been appointed as the brand ambassador of which insurance company?

(A) HDFC Life Insurance

(B) Tata AIA Life Insurance

(C) Max Life Insurance

(D) ** Bharti AXA Life Insurance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MUMBAI/NEW DELHI: March 1, 2022: Bharti AXA Life Insurance, a joint venture between Bharti Enterprises, one of India’s leading business groups, and AXA, one of the world’s largest insurance companies, today announced that it has roped in National Award-winning actress Vidya Balan as its brand ambassador.

Concept note-2: -Founded in 1976, by Sunil Bharti Mittal, Bharti has grown from being a manufacturer of bicycle parts to one of the largest and most respected business groups in India.

Concept note-3: -From Lizol to Boroline, all the brands that are found in every rack of an Indian household is represented by the actress. To name a few more that are the ‘masses’ brand, and are endorsed by Vidya are Muthoot Fianance, Bharti AXA, TTk Prestige, Darfur Sanifresh and many more.
