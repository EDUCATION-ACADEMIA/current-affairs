What amount of capital infusion has been announced by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy in the Solar Energy Corporation of India?

(A) Rs 900 crores

(B) Rs 700 crores

(C) Rs 1200 crores

(D) ** Rs 1000 crores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Govt. approves infusion of Rs. 1000 Cr.

Concept note-2: Theinstalled power capacity in the country is around 408.72 GW as of 30thNovember 2022. India achieves 166GW of renewable energy capacity till October2022.

Concept note-3: New Delhi: India’s renewable capacity, exclusing largehydro, is expected to have touched 122 GW by December 2022 against theGovernment of India’s target of 175 GW – a deficit of 30 per cent, consultancyfirm Bridge to India said.

Concept note-4: Union Budget FY 2021-22Energy SectorAccording to the Union Budget 2021 that was announced by theFinance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman on 1 February 2021, Rs. 3.05 lakh crorewill be allocated for the purposes of power distribution over the next fiveyears.

Concept note-5: India needs a lot more solar power as part of its net-zeroemissions plans. Currently solar accounts for around 7% of India’s electricitygeneration.
