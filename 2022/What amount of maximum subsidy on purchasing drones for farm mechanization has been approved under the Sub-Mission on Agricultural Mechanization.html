What amount of maximum subsidy on purchasing drones for farm mechanization has been approved under the Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization?

(A) Rs 5 lakh

(B) ** Rs 10 lakh

(C) Rs 7 lakh

(D) Rs 12 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: (11) The Farmers Producer Organization (FPOs) willbe provided grants for purchase of Kisan Drones and attachments for itsdemonstration on farmers’ fields 75% of the cost of drone up to a maximum of Rs.7.50 lakhs. The assistance will be limited for purchase of one (01) drone perFPO.

Concept note-2: Agriculture Drone Price – Between 3 lakh to 11.5 lakhdepending on its water holding capacity.

Concept note-3: FPOs (FarmersProducers Organizations) are eligible to receive grants of up to 75% of the cost ofagriculture drones for use over farmers’ fields. Along with this, Under “sub-missionon Agricultural Mechanization” (SMAM) provides grants of 100% on agriculturedrones or Rs. 10 lakhs (whichever is less).

Concept note-4: Sub Mission onAgricultural Mechanization (SMAM) will be implemented in accordance withguidelines described hereunder. The scheme will be implemented in all the states, topromote the usage of farm mechanization and increase the ratio of farm power tocultivable unit area up to 2 kW/ha.

Concept note-5: Plastic 10 Litre AgricultureSpraying Drone/Agriculture Drones in India, Capacity: 5L at Rs 350000 inNellore.
