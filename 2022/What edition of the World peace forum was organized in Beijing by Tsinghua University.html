What edition of the World peace forum was organized in Beijing by Tsinghua University?

(A) 20th

(B) 15th

(C) ** 10th

(D) 5th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Speaking at a panel discussion “BRICS Cooperation: Opportunities and Challenges” as part of the 10th World Peace Forum in Beijing organized by Tsinghua University, Amb Rawat tried to dispel the notion that BRICS is trying to replace other institutions and organizations.

Concept note-2: -The 10th World Peace Forum opened in Beijing last Sunday, with more than 300 senior officials, diplomatic envoys and scholars participating online and offline.

Concept note-3: -Beijing, 3 November 2022, Ambassador Djauhari Oratmangun attended the 2nd Ambassadors Forum themed JD National Pavilions – Synchronizing the Good Life of the World, which was held by JD.Com company in Beijing on 2 November 2022.

Concept note-4: -Background. Initiated in 2012, the World Peace Forum is organized by Tsinghua University with assistance from Chinese People’s Institute of Foreign Affairs. It is China’s first non-governmental high-level forum on international security.
