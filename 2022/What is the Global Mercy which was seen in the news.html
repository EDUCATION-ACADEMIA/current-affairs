What is the ‘Global Mercy’ which was seen in the news?

(A) Non-Profit Organisation

(B) Anti-satellite Missile

(C) Covid-19 Vaccine

(D) ** Civilian hospital ship

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. What is the ‘Global Mercy’ which was seen in the news recently? Notes: The Global Mercy, the world’s largest civilian hospital ship , made its debut in Africa in the port of Dakar, the capital city of Senegal.

Concept note-2: -Rotterdam, February 28, 2022 – On February 26, the Global Mercy, the new purpose-built hospital ship built by Mercy Ships, docked in the Port of Rotterdam. Festivities launched the beginning of a two-week visit that offers the public a unique opportunity to come on board.

Concept note-3: -The current position of GLOBAL MERCY is at West Africa reported 12 mins ago by AIS.

Concept note-4: -The construction costs of about USD 200 million could entirely be covered by an array of corporate and private donations. Hence, the new ship would be able to commence debt-free with its lifetime mission in Africa.

Concept note-5: -Mercy Ships and Senegal In 2023, the Global Mercy will serve the people of both Senegal and the Gambia from the port of Dakar, working together with the Ministry of Health to bring hope and healing to those in need.
