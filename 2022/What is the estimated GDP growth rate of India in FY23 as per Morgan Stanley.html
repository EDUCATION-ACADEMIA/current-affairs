What is the estimated GDP growth rate of India in FY23 as per Morgan Stanley?

(A) ** 7.2%

(B) 8.1%

(C) 6.7%

(D) 9.1%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s real GDP is expected to grow by seven per cent in 2022-23 and by 6.4 per cent in 2023-24, as per Morgan Stanley.

Concept note-2: -India’s GDP had grown at 8.7% in the year ending 31 March, 2022. The country’s nominal GDP growth for FY23 is estimated at 15.4% as against 19.5% in FY22, the Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation said in a statement.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI, Jan 31 (Reuters) - India forecast economic growth of 6% to 6.8% in the 2023/24 fiscal year, slowing from the 7% growth projected for the current year ending on March 31, as a global slowdown is likely to hurt exports.

Concept note-4: -“We have revised down our forecast for real gross domestic product growth to 7 per cent for fiscal 2023 from 7.3 per cent, primarily because of the slowdown in global growth that has started to impact our exports and industrial activity.
