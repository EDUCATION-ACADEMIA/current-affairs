What is the estimated global trade growth (in volume) projection made by WTO for 2022?

(A) 4%

(B) 4.7%

(C) ** 3%

(D) 3.5%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: WTO economists now predict global merchandise tradevolumes will grow by 3.5% in 2022-slightly better than the 3.0% forecast in April. For2023, however, they foresee a 1.0% increase-down sharply from the previous estimateof 3.4%.

Concept note-2: Demand for foreign goods proved resilient throughout2022, with trade volumes increasing by 3 per cent.

Concept note-3: MAINPOINTS. World merchandise trade volume is expected to grow 3.5% in 2022 beforeslowing to 1.0% in 2023 (revised down from 3.4%). World GDP at market exchangerates will increase by 2.8% in 2022 and by 2.3% in 2023 (revised down from3.2%).

Concept note-4: As of 2021, world trade volume and value have expanded4% and 6% respectively on average since 1995, when the WTO was firstestablished.
