What is the full form of BBPS?

(A) Bill Bharat Pay System

(B) Bharat Bill Pay System

(C) Bill Bharat Payment System

(D) ** Bharat Bill Payment System

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Bharat Bill Payment System (BBPS) is a RBImandated system which offers integrated and interoperable bill paymentservices to customers across geographies with certainty, reliability and safety oftransactions.

Concept note-2: Bharat Bill Payment System (BPPS), at anytimeanywhere bill payment system.

Concept note-3: Bharat Bill PaymentOperating Unit aka BBPOU is the entity that is authorized by Reserve Bank ofIndia.

Concept note-4: Bharat Bill Payment System (BBPS) is a Reserve Bankof India (RBI) conceptualised system which offers integrated, accessible andinteroperable bill payment services to consumers across geographies with certainty,reliability and safety of transactions.

Concept note-5: 2.25 (Both Digital andPhysical mode) for Electricity & Gas, Rs. 2.5 (Digital)/ Rs. 5 (Physical) for Water &Telecom).
