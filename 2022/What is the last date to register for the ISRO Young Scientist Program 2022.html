What is the last date to register for the ISRO Young Scientist Program 2022?

(A) April 21st

(B) ** April 10th

(C) April 15th

(D) April 1st

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The online registration process is underway and the last dateto apply is April 10 till 4 pm. The announcement of provisional selection list forYUVIKA will be out on April 20, 2022. Interested candidates can register forYUVIKA 2022 at the official website at www.isro.gov.in.

Concept note-2: LastDate of Application: 10 April, 2022Submit the online application form and downloadthe submitted form.

Concept note-3: Important Dates:Closing of Registration:April 10, 2023, 04:00 p.m. Announcement of provisional selection list forYUVIKA – 2023 : April 20, 2023. YUVIKA 2023 programme : May 16 to 28,2023.

Concept note-4: ISRO YUVIKA 2023 Important DatesFor moredetails visit www.isro.gov.in. If selected, the child will have to report to ISROcenters in Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Shillong, and Trivandrum. Those who havecompleted class 8th and are currently enrolled in class 9th are eligible for thisprogram.
