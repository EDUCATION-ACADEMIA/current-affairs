What is the name of India’s first unique blood group?

(A) RHE Negative

(B) AMU Positive

(C) SNE Negative

(D) ** EMM Negative

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: What is EMM negative? The Rajkot man is said to be thefirst person in India and 10th in the world with an EMM blood group. AsEMM level is low in blood, the International Society of Blood Transfusion(ISBT) named it EMM negative. The blood group has been given symbolISBTO42.

Concept note-2: EMM Negative Blood Group is the first distinctblood type in India. Only 9 persons in the entire globe have the rarest blood type asof today, but a 65-year-old man from Rajkot, Gujarat, has been found to haveit.

Concept note-3: A 65-year-old man from Gujarat who is a heart patient hasbeen identified with EMM negative blood group, making it India’s first and world’stenth case of unique blood group which cannot be categorized into the existinggroups of ‘A’, ‘B’, ‘O’ or ‘AB’.

Concept note-4: What’s the rarest blood type?AB negative is the rarest of the eight main blood types - just 1% of our donors haveit. Despite being rare, demand for AB negative blood is low and we don’t struggle tofind donors with AB negative blood.

Concept note-5: Emm has been designatedthe 42nd blood group system. Rare individuals have an Emm- phenotype due tohomozygous loss-of-function mutations in the PIGG gene. These individuals oftenhave naturally occurring anti-Emm antibodies that are identified during routineblood type and crossing.
