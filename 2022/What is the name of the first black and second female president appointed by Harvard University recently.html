What is the name of the first black and second female president appointed by Harvard University recently?

(A) Angela Rooster

(B) ** Claudine Gay

(C) Annie Grey

(D) Christina Levis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Harvard University named Claudine Gay, a dean of the Facultyof Arts and Science, as its new president, the first African American to hold the postat the prestigious university. Gay, 52, is just the second woman to be electedto head the school in Cambridge, Massachusetts.

Concept note-2: Asurgeon, right-to-life activist, and noted speaker, Mildred Fay Jefferson was thefirst African American woman to graduate from Harvard Medical School in1951.

Concept note-3: Harvard University announced Thursday that ClaudineGay will become its 30th president, making her the first Black person and the secondwoman to lead the Ivy League school. Ms. Gay, who is currently a deanat the university and a democracy scholar, will become president on July1.

Concept note-4: Drew Gilpin FaustFaust served as the 28th president ofHarvard University from July 1, 2007 through June 30, 2018. She was Harvard’sfirst female president, and the first Harvard president without a Harvarddegree.

Concept note-5: Harvard University announced its 30th presidentThursday, drawing from within to hire Claudine Gay, the first Black president in theinstitution’s nearly 400-year history.
