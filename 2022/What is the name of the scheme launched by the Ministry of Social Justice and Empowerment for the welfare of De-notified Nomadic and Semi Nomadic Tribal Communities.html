[SOURCES]

What is the name of the scheme launched by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment for the welfare of De-notified, Nomadic and Semi Nomadic Tribal Communities?

(A) ** SEED

(B) DEED

(C) FEED

(D) NEED

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why in News? Recently, the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has said that only 402 online applications are received to avail benefits under SEED (Scheme for Economic Empowerment of Denotified, Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes).

Concept note-2: -NMBA in Border Villages: Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment in convergence with Department of Border Management, MHA will launch Nasha Mukth Bharat Abhiyaan in the 17000 Border Villages in 18 States/UTs during the month of May 2023.

Concept note-3: -The DWBDNC was constituted on 21st February 2019, under the chairmanship of Bhiku Ramji Idate.

Concept note-4: -About the scheme: The scheme was launched in 2022 by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment.
