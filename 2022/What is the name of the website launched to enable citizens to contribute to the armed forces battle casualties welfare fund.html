What is the name of the website launched to enable citizens to contribute to the armed forces battle casualties welfare fund?

(A) Maa Bharati ke bahadur Sapoot

(B) Bharat Maa ke Sapoot

(C) ** Maa Bharati ke Sapoot

(D) Dharti Maa ke Sapoot

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Website namely ‘Maa Bharati Ke Sapoot’ was launched byRaksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh on 14 October 2022 for contribution towardsArmed Forces Battle Casualties Welfare Fund (AFBCWF) for providing financialassistance to the families of soldiers who are injured or died during militaryoperations.

Concept note-2: The National Defence Fund was set up to takecharge of voluntary donations in cash and kind received for promotion of the nationaldefence effort, and to decide on their utilisation. The Fund is used for the welfare ofthe members of the Armed Forces (including Para Military Forces) and theirdependents.

Concept note-3: Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh launched ‘MaaBharati Ke Sapoot’ website (www.maabharatikesapoot.mod.gov.in) for contributionto the Armed Forces Battle Casualties Welfare Fund (AFBCWF) at a functionorganised at the National War Memorial Complex in New Delhi on October 14,2022.

Concept note-4: Defence Minister Rajnath Singh launched the ‘MaaBharati Ke Sapoot’ website. By Manjeet Negi: Defence Minister Rajnath Singh onFriday launched the ‘Maa Bharati Ke Sapoot’ website at a function organised at theNational War Memorial Complex in New Delhi.

Concept note-5: KendriyaSainik Board administratively controls AFFDF, which is utilised for providingfinancial assistance to needy Ex-Servicemen, War widows and their Dependents; andthe Institutions involved in rehabilitation of the Ex-Servicemen, War widows andtheir Dependents.
