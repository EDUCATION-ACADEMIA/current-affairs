What is the old name for PMKSY?

(A) ** SAMPADA

(B) SIDDHI

(C) SAMADHAN

(D) SAUBHAGYA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Launched on 1st July, 2015 with the motto of “Har Khet KoPaani", the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) is being implementedto expand cultivated area with assured irrigation, reduce wastage of water andimprove water use efficiency.

Concept note-2: The overreaching visionof Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) will be to ensureaccess to some means of protective irrigation to all agricultural farms in thecountry, to produce ‘per drop more crop’, thus bringing much desired ruralprosperity.

Concept note-3: Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)is a Central Sponsored Scheme on Micro Irrigation. Further Reading: PradhanMantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana. Government schemes.
