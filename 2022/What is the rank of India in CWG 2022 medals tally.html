What is the rank of India in CWG 2022 medals tally?

(A) 3

(B) 2

(C) ** 4

(D) 1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India’s Commonwealth Games 2022 gold medal count is itsjoint-fourth best in CWG history. India’s best gold-medal hauls in CWGhistory: ⦿ 2010, New Delhi - 38 gold medals. ⦿ 2002, Manchester - 30 goldmedals.

Concept note-2: India won 61 medals in the overall CommonwealthGames 2022 medal table. India finished its CWG 2022 campaign as thefourth-best country on the medals table.

Concept note-3: As the hosts,India delivered their best-ever performance in the Commonwealth Gameshistory to breach the 100-medal mark for the first time and finish 2nd. Indiawon a total of 101 medals at the Games, including a staggering 38 goldmedals.
