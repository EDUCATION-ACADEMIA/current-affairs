What is the rank of India in the US Green Building Council’s annual list of countries for LEED-certified green buildings in 2021?

(A) ** 3

(B) 4

(C) 2

(D) 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is 3. India has ranked third in the world forLEED certified green buildings in 2021 in the US Green Building Council (USGBC)annual list. China and Canada are in the first two positions.

Concept note-2: TheUS Green Building Council (USGBC) has released the 9th annual ranking of top 10countries outside of the United States (US) for Leadership in Energy andEnvironmental Design (LEED).

Concept note-3: India ranks 3rd in US GreenBuilding Council’s 2021 global list for sustainable spaces. India has a total of 146LEED certified buildings and spaces, representing nearly 2.8 million gross area squaremeters (GSM) of space.

Concept note-4: LEED IndiaIt is recognised in Indiaand globally as a point of reference for design, construction and operation of GreenBuildings. It was originally developed by the US Green Building Council(USGBC) for promoting sustainability through the construction of Greenbuildings.
