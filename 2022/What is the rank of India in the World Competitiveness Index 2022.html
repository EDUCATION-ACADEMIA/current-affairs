What is the rank of India in the World Competitiveness Index 2022?

(A) 57th

(B) 27th

(C) 47th

(D) ** 37th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India has come up on 37th rank in 2022 from 43rd in 2021in the World Competitive Index.

Concept note-2: India has seen thesharpest growth among the Asian economies, with a six-position jump from43rd to 37th rank on the annual World Competitiveness Index 2022. TheIndex has been compiled by the Institute for Management Development(IMD).

Concept note-3: What are the Highlights of the Index? Top GlobalPerformers: Europe: Denmark has moved to the top of the 63-nation listfrom the third position last year, while Switzerland slipped from the topranking to the second position and Singapore regained the third spot fromfifth.
