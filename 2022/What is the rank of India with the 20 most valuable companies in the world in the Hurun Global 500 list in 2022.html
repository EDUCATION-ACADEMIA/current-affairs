What is the rank of India with the 20 most valuable companies in the world in the Hurun Global 500 list in 2022?

(A) 4th

(B) 3rd

(C) ** 5th

(D) 7th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India now ranks at Number 5 as 20 Indian companies featurein 500 most valuable ones in the world, as per the 2022 Hurun Global list.India rose from the 9th rank to 5th rank with 20 companies featured in thelist.

Concept note-2: Out of these 20 companies, 11 are based in Mumbai, four inAhmedabad and 1 each in Noida, New Delhi, Bengaluru and Kolkata. The USD 202billion Reliance Industries is the most valuable company in India, followed by TataConsultancy Services, with USD 139 billion valuation and HDFC Bank having $97billion valuation.

Concept note-3: As many as twenty Indian companies are nowamong the 500 most-valued firms in the world, according to the 2022 Hurun Global500 list, with India improving its position to fifth, up four spots from the previousedition.

Concept note-4: Hurun’s list of most valuable companies: MukeshAmbani-led Reliance Industries Ltd is the most valuable listed company amongst thetop 500 companies in India, as per Hurun India’s ‘2022 Burgundy Private HurunIndia 500’.

Concept note-5: There are a total of nine Indian companies in theFortune Global 500 list 2022, including four private sector companies and five publicsector companies.
