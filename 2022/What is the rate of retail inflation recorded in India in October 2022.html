What is the rate of retail inflation recorded in India in October 2022?

(A) 7%

(B) 7.41%

(C) ** 6.77%

(D) 7.79%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India’s consumer price based inflation eased to an 11-monthlow of 5.88 per cent in November on an annual basis from 6.77 per centin October, 2022.

Concept note-2: The consumer price index (CPI)based retail inflation was 6.77 per cent in October 2022, and 4.91 per cent inNovember last year.

Concept note-3: CPI Inflation November, India IIPData October 2022: Retail inflation eases to a 11-month low of 5.88% inNov, IIP contracts 4% in Oct, shows Govt data.

Concept note-4: Thecountry’s retail inflation, which is measured by the consumer price index(CPI), dropped to a 1-year low of 5.72% in Dec. 2022 from 5.88% in Nov.2022.
