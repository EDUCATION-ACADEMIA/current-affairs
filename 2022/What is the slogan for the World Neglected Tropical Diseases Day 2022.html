What is the slogan for the World Neglected Tropical Diseases Day 2022?

(A) Quicker Picker Upper

(B) ** From neglect to care

(C) A Diamond is Forever

(D) There Are Some Things Money Can’t Buy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: 100% Committed. In 2022, Uniting to Combat NTDs launchedthe 100% Committed movement on 27 January, just ahead of World NTDDay.

Concept note-2: Neglected Tropical Diseases Day 2022: A callfor equitable health for all – Message from Dr Tedros, Director General,WHO. Neglected tropical diseases (NTDs) are widespread in the world’spoorest regions, where water safety, sanitation and access to health care aresubstandard.

Concept note-3: Neglected Tropical Diseases Day 2023: Act now,Act together, Invest in neglected tropical diseases – Message from Dr Tedros,Director-General, WHO. World Neglected Tropical Diseases Day is observed everyyear on 30 January.

Concept note-4: January 30, 2023: World Neglected TropicalDiseases Day.

Concept note-5: On 31 May 2021, the World Health Assembly(WHA) recognized 30 January as World Neglected Tropical Disease (NTD) Daythrough decision WHA74(18). This decision formalized 30 January as a day to createbetter awareness on the devastating impact of NTDs on the poorest populationsaround the world.
