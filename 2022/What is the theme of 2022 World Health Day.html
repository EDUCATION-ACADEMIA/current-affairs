What is the theme of 2022 World Health Day?

(A) Support Nurses and Midwives

(B) Universal Health Coverage:Everyone, Everywhere

(C) Building a fairer, healthier world for everyone

(D) ** Our Planet, Our Health

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: This year’s World Health Day will shine a light on nurses andmidwives, the on-the-call, restless workforce that revolutionized the healthcareindustry as we know it today.

Concept note-2: The theme of this year’s HealthAssembly is: Health for peace, peace for health. The COVID-19 pandemic and otherhealth emergencies with international reach have highlighted the leadership andcoordinating role of WHO in responding to such events.

Concept note-3: WorldMental Health Day 2022 ColourThe green ribbon is the international symbol ofmental health awareness.

Concept note-4: The theme for World Health Day 2022is Our Planet, Our Health.
