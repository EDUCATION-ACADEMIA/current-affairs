What is the theme of Financial Literacy week 2022 being observed by RBI in 2022?

(A) ** Go Digital, Go Secure

(B) MSME

(C) Credit Discipline and Credit from Formal Institutions

(D) Farmers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The theme selected for current year FLW is “Go Digital, GoSecure” which will be observed between February 14-18, 2022. This theme isone of the strategic objectives of the National Strategy for FinancialEducation 2020-2025.

Concept note-2: "Here comes the sun-monetarypolicy."

Concept note-3: Some typical financial literacy topics include: Savingmoney (emergency savings, major purchases, retirement) Budgeting and spending(methods, best practices, psychology) Debt management (refinancing, becomingdebt-free, understanding credit products)

Concept note-4: The themefor International Literacy Day 2022 is “Transforming Literacy LearningSpaces”.

Concept note-5: Financial literacy supports the pursuit of financialinclusion by empowering the customers to make informed choices leading to theirfinancial well-being. 2.
