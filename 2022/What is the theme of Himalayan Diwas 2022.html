What is the theme of Himalayan Diwas 2022?

(A) ** Himalayas will be safe only when the interests of its residents are protected

(B) Contribution to the Himalayas And Our Responsibilities

(C) Sab ka Himalaya

(D) The Himalayas and the Nature

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Himalaya Day 2022 was observed under the theme‘Himalayas will be safe only when the interests of its residents areprotected.

Concept note-2: Himalaya Diwas is celebrated every year on 9thSeptember in the state of Uttarakhand. It is celebrated with the aim to conserveHimalayan ecosystem and region. It was officially declared as Himalaya Day in 2015by the then Chief Minister.

Concept note-3: Answers. ‘Himalaya Diwas’ or‘Himalaya Day’ is celebrated on September 9 in Uttarakhand to raise awarenessabout the conservation of the rich flora and fauna of the state. In 2014, the then CMHarish Rawat declared September 9 as ‘Himalaya Diwas’ to propagate the message ofconservation of the Himalayan ecosystems.
