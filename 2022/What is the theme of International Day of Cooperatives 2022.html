What is the theme of International Day of Cooperatives 2022?

(A) ** Cooperatives Build a Better World

(B) Sustainable consumption and production

(C) Cooperatives for building a people-centred and environmentally just recovery

(D) COOPS 4 CLIMATE ACTION

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: 2022 International Day of Cooperatives: CooperativesBuild a Better World. The International Day of Cooperatives 2022 is beingcelebrated under the theme, “Cooperatives Build a Better World.” The themerecognizes that the current predominant economic model will not achieve thesustainable development goals.

Concept note-2: India celebrated the dayunder the theme “Building a Self-Reliant India and a Better World throughCo-operatives”.

Concept note-3: A decade on from the UN International Year ofCooperatives, which showcased the unique contribution of cooperatives to making theworld a better place, this year’s #CoopsDay slogan - “Cooperatives Build a BetterWorld”- echoes the theme of the International Year.

Concept note-4: The themeof 100th International Cooperative Day is “Cooperatives Build a BetterWorld”.

Concept note-5: International Co-operative Day is an annualcelebration of the co-operative movement observed on the first Saturday in July since1923 by the International Co-operative Alliance. International Day of Cooperatives.Also called. CoopsDay.
