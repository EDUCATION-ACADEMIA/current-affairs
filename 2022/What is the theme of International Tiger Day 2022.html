What is the theme of International Tiger Day 2022?

(A) Fresh Ecology For Tigers’ Protection

(B) ** India launches Project Tiger to revive the tiger population

(C) Save the Tiger

(D) Their survival is in our hands

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Every year the day is celebrated under different themes, andthis year the theme for Global Tiger Day is “India launches Project Tiger to revivethe tiger population”.

Concept note-2: International Tiger Day 2022: Historyand SignificanceSince tigers were on the verge of extinction, a number of countriessigned an agreement at the Saint Petersburg Tiger Summit in Russia to preventthe situation from worsening. The day seeks to protect and expand tigerhabitats apart from preserving these species.

Concept note-3: For the tigerwe must unite, so they don’t fade out of sight. Happy International TigerDay!

Concept note-4: Habitat loss, climate change, hunting, and poaching areonly some of the factors that are responsible for the decline in the tiger population.Along with the preservation of these species, the day also aims to protect and expandtheir habitats.

Concept note-5: The project aims at ensuring a viablepopulation of the Bengal tiger in its natural habitats, protecting it fromextinction, and preserving areas of biological importance as a natural heritagethat represent the diversity of ecosystems across the tiger’s range in thecountry.
