What is the theme of National Protein Day 2022?

(A) Protein and Health

(B) Action on Protein

(C) ** Food Futurism

(D) Powering with Plant Protein

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: "Right to Protein, ahead of India’s third annual Protein Daycelebration on 27th February, declares ‘Food Futurism’ as the theme for 2022 as ittakes ahead its mission to drive food and especially protein sufficiency in the countrythrough awareness, advocacy, and action."

Concept note-2: Every year onFebruary 27th, National Protein Day draws public attention and awareness on thehealth benefits of protein. It’s also a day to learn more about different sources ofplant and animal protein.

Concept note-3: National Protein Day 2022:ThemeThe theme is aimed at bringing food scientists, nutrition expertsand biologists to discuss food science and its role in protein sufficiency andfood security in India.

Concept note-4: The day urges people to includethis crucial macronutrient in their diet. The day also helps people to learnmore about different sources of plant and animal protein. Protein Day iscelebrating its third year in 2022. This year’s theme of India Protein Day is ‘FoodFuturism’.

Concept note-5: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India: Right To Protein, anational-level public health initiative, has launched India’s first ‘Protein Day’ to raiseawareness about the health benefits of protein. Many countries recognize 27thFebruary as the Protein Day and from this year, India will also join thismovement.
