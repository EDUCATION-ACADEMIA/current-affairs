What is the theme of World Blood Donor Day 2022?

(A) Give blood and keep the world-beating

(B) Safe Blood For All

(C) ** Donating blood is an act of solidarity. Join the effort and save lives

(D) Safe Blood Saves Lives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: For 2022, the World Blood Donor Day slogan will be“Donating blood is an act of solidarity. Join the effort and save lives” to drawattention to the roles that voluntary blood donations play in saving lives andenhancing solidarity within communities.

Concept note-2: Join the effortand save lives. The theme for Blood Donor Day 2022 is ‘Blood donation isan act of solidarity. Join the effort and save lives’.

Concept note-3: Ablood service that gives patients access to safe blood and blood products insufficient quantity is a key component of an effective health system. Theglobal theme of World Blood Donor Day changes each year in recognitionof the selfless individuals who donate their blood for people unknown tothem.

Concept note-4: World Blood Donor Day is celebrated every year on June14. For 2021, the World Blood Donor Day slogan will be “Give blood and keep theworld beating”.

Concept note-5: The day was created to raise global awarenessof the need for blood products for transfusion, highlighting the critical contributionvoluntary, unpaid blood donors make to national health systems and supportingnational blood transfusion services in expanding their voluntary blood donorprogrammes.
