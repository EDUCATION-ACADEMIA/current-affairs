What is the theme of World Brain Day (WBD) 2022?

(A) ** Brain Health for all

(B) Move Together to End Parkinson’s Disease

(C) Our brain, our future

(D) Stop Multiple Sclerosis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Brain Day (WBD) 2022 was dedicated to the theme “Brain Health for all” as our brains continue to be challenged by pandemics, wars, climate change and the myriad of disorders impacting human existence globally.

Concept note-2: -On World Brain Day, we advocate for health care, education, and research into neurological disorders. Draft Intersectoral global action plan on epilepsy and other neurological disorders 2022–2031. and its forthcoming position paper on brain health, this year’s theme for World Brain Day is “Brain health for all”.

Concept note-3: -On the July 22, 2022, the Neurology Department at Kuala Lumpur Hospital, Malaysia, celebrated “World Brain Day” in conjunction with the World Federation of Neurology’s theme of “ Brain Health for All.” The theme for this year’s celebration is very meaningful, inclusive, and diverse.

Concept note-4: -People around the world celebrate World Brain Day on July 22 of every year. This is one of the most important annual celebrations in the world, as it helps raise awareness on an increasingly significant health issue, especially in our modern age.

Concept note-5: -The World Federation of Neurology was established on July 22 1957.
