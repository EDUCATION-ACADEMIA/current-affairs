What is the theme of World Coconut Day 2022?

(A) Coconut for Good Health, Wealth & Wellness

(B) Coconut Trees on the Way of Integration and Sustainable Development

(C) Coconut For Family Wellness

(D) ** Growing Coconut for a Better Future and Life

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: “Growing Coconut for a Better Future and Life” is the themefor World Coconut Day in 2022. This day is observed with technical seminars andefforts to raise awareness throughout several nations. These workshops boost coconutproduction and spread knowledge about its uses.

Concept note-2: WorldCoconut Day 2022: Theme and HistoryThis year’s theme for “World Coconut Day” is“Growing coconut for a better future and life".

Concept note-3: You can’t makea piña colada or a decent Thai curry without it, its water makes a great recoverydrink, and its fibrous husk, when burned, repels mosquitos - these are just threeof many excellent reasons to celebrate World Coconut Day on September2.

Concept note-4: World Coconut Day is celebrated every year on September 2to make people aware of the benefits of coconuts.
