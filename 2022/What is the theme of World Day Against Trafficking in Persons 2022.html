What is the theme of World Day Against Trafficking in Persons 2022?

(A) Let’s act now to protect and assist trafficking victims

(B) Focus on the first responders to human trafficking

(C) ** Use and abuse of technology

(D) Victims’ Voices Lead the Way

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: This year’s theme “Use and abuse of technology” focuses onthe role of technology as a tool that can both enable and impede humantrafficking.

Concept note-2: In 2013, the UN General Assembly adoptedresolution A/RES/68/192 designated July 30 as the World Day against Trafficking inPersons and declared that such a day was necessary to “raise awareness ofthe situation of victims of human trafficking and for the promotion andprotection of their rights."

Concept note-3: The Blue Heart represents thesadness of those who are trafficked while reminding us of the cold-heartednessof those who buy and sell fellow human beings. The use of the blue UNcolour also demonstrates the commitment of the United Nations to combatingthis crime against human dignity.

Concept note-4: National HumanTrafficking Awareness Day is observed every year in the US on January 11 toraise awareness about human trafficking and aims the prevention of illegalpractices.

Concept note-5: The World Day against Trafficking in Personswas proclaimed by the United Nations General Assembly, in its resolutionA/RES/68/192.
