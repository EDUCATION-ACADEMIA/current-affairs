What is the theme of World Leprosy Day 2022?

(A) The target of zero cases of leprosy-related disabilities in children

(B) Ending the stigma

(C) ** United for Dignity

(D) Leprosy is curable

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The “United for Dignity” campaign calls for unity in honoringthe dignity of people who have experienced leprosy. The campaign honors the livedexperiences of individuals who have experienced leprosy by: sharing their empoweringstories and.

Concept note-2: World Leprosy Day is observed every year on thelast Sunday of January.

Concept note-3: In this context, the Sasakawa Leprosy(Hansen’s Disease) Initiative implemented a 10-month campaign with the message“Don’t forget leprosy.” The campaign schedule gave special consideration to WorldLeprosy Day, which has been observed year on the last Sunday of January since1954.

Concept note-4: “Act Now. End Leprosy.” is this year’s theme for WorldLeprosy Day.

Concept note-5: World leprosy day background is purple in colorwith a modern design style.
