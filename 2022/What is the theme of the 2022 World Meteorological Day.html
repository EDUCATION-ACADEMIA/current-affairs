What is the theme of the 2022 World Meteorological Day?

(A) ** Early Warning and Early Action

(B) The Sun, the Earth and the Weather

(C) Climate and Water

(D) The Ocean, Our Climate and Weather

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: I am therefore very happy that the theme of WorldMeteorological Day 2022 is “Early Warning and Early Action.” It celebrates the greatachievements of national meteorological and hydrological services in improved earlywarning systems.

Concept note-2: The organization announces a sloganfor World Meteorological Day every year. World Meteorological Day 2022bears the slogan “Early Warning and Early Action,” and highlights the vitalimportance of Hydrometeorological and Climate Information for Disaster RiskReduction.

Concept note-3: World Meteorological Day takes place every year on23 March and commemorates the creation of the World Meteorological Organizationand the essential contribution of National Meteorological and Hydrological Servicesto the safety and wellbeing of society The theme for 2022 is “Early Warning andEarly Action".

Concept note-4: The theme for World MeteorologicalDay is ‘Understanding Clouds. ‘

Concept note-5: The theme for WorldMetrology Day 2021 is Measurement for Health. This theme aims to createawareness of the important role measurement plays in health, and wellbeing ofeveryone.
