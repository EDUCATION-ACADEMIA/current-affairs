What is the theme of the International Global Day of Parents 2022?

(A) Family Awareness

(B) Families and Urbanization

(C) ** Appreciate all parents throughout the world

(D) Honor Your Parents!

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Global Day of Parents is commemorated on June 1 every year.‘Appreciate all parents throughout the world’ is the theme for Global Day of Parents,2022.

Concept note-2: What is the meaning of Global Day of Parents?Global Day of Parents is a United Nations observance that is celebratedon June 1 each year to honor parents and their commitment to childrenworldwide.

Concept note-3: Internationally, Parents’ Day is observed onJune 1st on the same day as International Children’s Day, according tothe United Nations proclamation. In the United States, Parents’ Day isobserved on the fourth Sunday of July.

Concept note-4: The essay on‘Parents Day’ emphasises why we must celebrate the day with great joy.Nobody can indeed replace our parents. From being our first teachers toproviding us with all the necessary things and supporting us financially,mentally and emotionally, we cannot underestimate the role played by ourparents.

Concept note-5: The Global Day of Parents is observed on the 1st ofJune every year. The Day honors parents throughout the world. The day provides anopportunity to appreciate all parents in all parts of the world for their selflesscommitment to children and their lifelong sacrifice towards nurturing thisrelationship.
