What is the theme of the Janaushadhi Diwas 2022?

(A) ** Jan Aushadhi-Jan Upyogi

(B) Ayushman Bharat, Swastha Samaj

(C) Seva Bhi-Rozgar bhi

(D) Achi Dawa, Sasti Dawa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Q. What is the theme of the Janaushadhi Diwas 2022? Notes:Pharmaceuticals & Medical Devices Bureau of India (PMBI), under the aegis ofDepartment of Pharmaceuticals is set to celebrate the Jan Aushadhi Diwas Week,with the main event on March 7. The theme of 4th Janaushadhi Diwas is “JanAushadhi-Jan Upyogi”.

Concept note-2: The event is an initiative by theDepartment of Pharmaceuticals which functions under the Ministry of Chemicals andFertilizers. The theme for 2022 is ‘Jan Aushadhi-Jan Upyogi’ and the theme ofthe third Jan Aushadhi Diwas celebrations (2021) was ‘Seva bhi – Rozgarbhi’.

Concept note-3: The 4th Janaushadhi Diwas 2022 celebrations commencedtoday. It will be a weeklong celebration starting today ie from 1st March to 7thMarch 2022. Jan Aushadhi kendras today conducted Jan Aushadhi Sankalp Padyatraat 75 locations, across the country.

Concept note-4: The Jan Aushadhi initiativewill make available quality drugs at affordable prices through dedicated stores sellinggeneric medicines which are available at lesser prices but are equivalent in qualityand efficacy as expensive branded drugs. Promote greater awareness about costeffective drugs and their prescription.

Concept note-5: Ministry of Chemicalsand Fertilizers will organize the Janaushadhi Diwas from 1st March to 7th March2022. Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers will organize the Janaushadhi Diwas from1st March to 7th March 2022.
