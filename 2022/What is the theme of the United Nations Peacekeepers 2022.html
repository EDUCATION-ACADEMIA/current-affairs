What is the theme of the ‘United Nations Peacekeepers 2022’?

(A) Planet Peace Partnerships

(B) Peace for All

(C) Tribute to Peacekeepers

(D) ** People Peace Progress:The Power of Partnerships

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2022 Theme: End racism. Build peace. Each year the International Day of Peace is observed around the world on 21 September. The UN General Assembly has declared this as a day devoted to strengthening the ideals of peace, through observing 24 hours of non-violence and cease-fire.

Concept note-2: -Peace, dignity and equality on a healthy planet.

Concept note-3: -UN Peacekeeping is made up of over 90,000 total field personnel, with 125 countries contributing troops, police, and civilian personnel including several dozen peacekeepers from the U.S.

Concept note-4: -Peacekeeping by the United Nations is a role held by the Department of Peace Operations as an “instrument developed by the organization as a way to help countries torn by conflict to create the conditions for lasting peace".
