What is the theme of the first quiz under the government’s Sabka Vikas Mahaquiz?

(A) National Pension Scheme

(B) Pradhan Mantri SVANidhi Scheme

(C) Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

(D) ** Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Sabka Vikas Mahaquiz: Govt launches quiz contestto promote citizen awareness on schemes and initiatives. In this context,MyGov has invited the people to participate and test their knowledge of ‘NewIndia’.

Concept note-2: The country’s biggest ever Quiz Contest, which aims tobuild awareness amongst citizens about Government’s various developmental andwelfare schemes and good governance, was recently launched with the inaugural quizon PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana. It was launched with the objective of‘Aatmanirbhar Bharat’.

Concept note-3: This series aims to sensitise theparticipants about the various government schemes and initiatives. The governmentunder the leadership of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi is committed to theideals of “Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas, Sabka Vishwas, Sabka Prayaas” to build anAatmanirbhar Bharat.

Concept note-4: Answer: MyGov, which is theGovernment of India’s citizen engagement platform has launched “Sabka VikasMahaQuiz” series commencing from 1st March 2022.

Concept note-5: InSeptember 2019, the Central Government planned to launch the People’s PlanCampaign, popularly known as “Sabki Yojana Sabka Vikas.” It intends to createGram Panchayat Development Plans (GPDP) around the country and post them ona website where anybody can view the status of the many government flagshipinitiatives.
