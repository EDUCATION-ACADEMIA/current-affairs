What is the total export of finished steel from India in FY 22?

(A) ** Rs 13.5 million tonnes

(B) Rs 20.5 million tonnes

(C) Rs 15 million tonnes

(D) Rs 10 million tonnes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -During April-January, FY22 exports at 111.42 LMT and imports at 39.07 LMT have increased by 26.2% and 3.1% respectively over CPLY. ii. India was net exporter of finished steel recording a net trade surplus of 3.64 LMT inJanuary’22 and 72.35 LMT during April-January, FY22, respectively. iii.
