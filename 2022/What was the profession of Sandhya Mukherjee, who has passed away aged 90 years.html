What was the profession of Sandhya Mukherjee, who has passed away aged 90 years?

(A) Sportswomen

(B) Politician

(C) Actress

(D) ** Singer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Geetashree Sandhya Mukherjee (also spelled Mukhopadhyay; 4October 1931 – 15 February 2022) was an Indian playback singer and musician,specialising in Bengali music. She received Banga Bibhushan, the highest civilianhonour of the Indian state of West Bengal in 2011.

Concept note-2: She attendedDhakuria Balika Vidyalaya, West Bengal and Binodini Girls’ High School, WestBengal.

Concept note-3: KOLKATA: Veteran Bengali singer Sandhya Mukherjeedied following a massive cardiac attack at a private hospital in Kolkata onTuesday evening, doctors said. She was 90 and is survived by her daughter andson-in-law.
