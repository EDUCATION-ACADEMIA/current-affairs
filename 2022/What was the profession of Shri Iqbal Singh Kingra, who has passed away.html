What was the profession of Shri Iqbal Singh Kingra, who has passed away?

(A) Environmentalist

(B) Economist

(C) Econometricians

(D) ** Educationalist

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Iqbal Singh Kingra (1 May 1926 – 29 January 2022)was an Indian socio-spiritual leader of the Sikh community. He was theFounder President of The Kalgidhar Trust, The Kalgidhar Society and BaruSahib.

Concept note-2: The correct answer is Social worker. Social worker BabaIqbal Singh passed away in January 2022. He was awarded the Padma Shri for hiscontributions in the field of social work in 2022. He was the founder of AkalAcademy.

Concept note-3: In 1964 he married Gertrude Egli, the personalsecretary to a Swiss criminologist.

Concept note-4: Baba Iqbal Singh had workedrelentlessly in only one direction – imparting value-based education in rural India.Founder of Akal Academies and Padma Shri awardee Baba Iqbal Singh died inHimachal Pradesh’s Sirmaur district on Saturday, a district official said. He was96.
