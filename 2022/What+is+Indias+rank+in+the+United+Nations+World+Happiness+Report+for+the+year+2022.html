What is India’s rank in the United Nations’ World Happiness Report for the year 2022?

(A) 134

(B) ** 136

(C) 139

(D) 137

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is India’s rank in World Happiness Report 2020, 2021 and 2022? India ranked 136th in the World Happiness Report 2022. 139th in 2021 and 144th in World Happiness Report 2020.

Concept note-2: -Finland is in the top position in the world happiness report in 2022. Followed by Denmark and Iceland in second and third place. Switzerland, Netherlands, Luxembourg, Sweden, Norway, Israel and New Zealand, were among the top 10 ‘happiest’ countries in the world [1].

Concept note-3: -The 2022 World Happiness Report included a section looking at possible genetic effects on individual happiness. Finland has been named the world’s happiest country for the fifth year running followed by Denmark, Iceland, Switzerland, and the Netherlands.

Concept note-4: -With an average life evaluation of 3.819, India now holds the 136th place among the nations in the World Happiness Index 2023, which will be released in 2023.

Concept note-5: -High on Happiness index and banking on its “Responsible tourism policy”, Mizoram has chalked out grand plans to draw tourists to its lush green hills and forests.
