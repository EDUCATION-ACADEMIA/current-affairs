What is the name of the Ideathon launched by SEBI to promote innovations in the securities market?

(A) Rachna

(B) Naveen

(C) ** Manthan

(D) Chintan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SEBI | “Manthan” – SEBI Ideathon to promote innovation in the Securities Market.

Concept note-2: -SEBI has launched the ideathon ‘Manthan’. It is a six-week-long ideathon being organized by SEBI in association with BSE, NSE, and more. It will facilitate the creation of a pool of ideas and innovative solutions revolving around the securities market.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which regulator body launched the ideathon named ‘Manthan’? Notes: SEBI Chairperson Madhabi Puri Buch launched the ‘Manthan’, a six-week long ideathon, in association with various institutions including BSE, NSE, NSDL, CDSL, KFintech, CAMS, LinkInTime and MCX.

Concept note-4: -“Everything begins with an idea!” SEBI in association with BSE, NSE, NSDL, CDSL, KFintech, CAMS, Link Intime, MCX and NCDEX is conducting a 6-week Ideathon titled ‘Manthan’, to promote innovation in the securities market.

Concept note-5: -It ensures that the three most important financial market players are taken care of. Securities issuers: These entities get listed on the stock exchanges and raise funds by issuing shares. The SEBI makes sure that the initial public offering and post-public offer take place transparently.
