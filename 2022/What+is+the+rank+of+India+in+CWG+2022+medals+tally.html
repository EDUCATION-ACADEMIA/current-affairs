What is the rank of India in CWG 2022 medals tally?

(A) 3

(B) 2

(C) ** 4

(D) 1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At CWG 2022, Indian athletes won 61 medals, 22 golds, 16 silvers and 23 bronze, at the Commonwealth Games 2022.

Concept note-2: -As the hosts, India delivered their best-ever performance in the Commonwealth Games history to breach the 100-medal mark for the first time and finish 2nd. India won a total of 101 medals at the Games, including a staggering 38 gold medals.

Concept note-3: -India finished its CWG 2022 campaign as the fourth-best country on the medals table.
