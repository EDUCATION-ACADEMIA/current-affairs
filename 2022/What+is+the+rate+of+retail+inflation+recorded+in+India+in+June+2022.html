What is the rate of retail inflation recorded in India in June 2022?

(A) ** 7.01 per cent

(B) 8.01 per cent

(C) 6.01 per cent

(D) 5.01 per cent

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CPI Inflation India Rate June 2022: Retail inflation eases to 7.01% in June; IIP grows 19.6% in May, shows govt data.

Concept note-2: -The wholesale price index (WPI) inflation has softened to 10.7 per cent in September 2022 from its peak of 16.2 per cent in June 2022, Saket Dalmia, president, PHD Chamber of Commerce and Industry, said.

Concept note-3: -India CPI Inflation Rate July, IIP Growth Rate June 2022: India’s retail inflation, which is measured by the Consumer Price Index (CPI), eased to a 5-month low 6.71 per cent in the month of July, down from 7.01 per cent in June.

Concept note-4: -The country’s retail inflation, which is measured by the consumer price index (CPI), dropped to a 1-year low of 5.72% in Dec. 2022 from 5.88% in Nov. 2022.

Concept note-5: -India’s retail inflation surged to 7 percent on an annual basis in the month of August from 6.71 percent in July 2022.
