What is the theme for World Water Day 2022?

(A) Water and Climate Change

(B) Leaving No One Behind

(C) ** Groundwater, Making the Invisible Visible

(D) Valuing Water

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Groundwater: Making the Invisible Visible” the theme of World Water Day 2022.

Concept note-2: -In 2022, World Water Day is celebrated under the theme “Groundwater: Making the invisible visible”, and UNESCO is the lead UN agency.

Concept note-3: -The World Water Day theme 2022 is “Groundwater, Making the Invisible Visible”.

Concept note-4: -ACCELERATING CHANGE. This World Water Day is about accelerating change to solve the water and sanitation crisis. And because water affects us all, we need everyone to take action. That means you!
