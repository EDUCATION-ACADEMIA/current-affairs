What is the theme of World Alzheimer’s Day 2022?

(A) Can we reduce the risk?

(B) Remember Me

(C) Let’s talk about dementia

(D) ** Know Dementia, Know Alzheimer’s

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for World Alzheimer’s Month in September 2022 is Know dementia, know Alzheimer’s.

Concept note-2: -World Alzheimer’s Day 2022: Theme This year’s theme for World Alzheimer’s Month is ‘Know Dementia, know Alzheimer’s’. It is in continuation with the last year’s campaign which focused on warning signs and diagnosis of dementia and also the effect of the COVID-19 pandemic on the dementia community around the world.

Concept note-3: -September 21, 2022 September 21st every year is World Alzheimer’s Day around the world. This is an international campaign aimed at raising awareness and challenge the common stigma that surrounds Alzheimer related dementia. Alzheimer’s is a progressive disease that impairs memory and other mental functions.

Concept note-4: -World Alzheimer’s Day is observed on September 21st yearly to raise awareness about Alzheimer’s disease (AD). The theme of World Alzheimer’s day 2022 is Know dementia, Know Alzheimer’s. This year’s theme is the same as 2021, but it has a special focus on post diagnosis support.

Concept note-5: -Go Purple to Raise Awareness for Alzheimer’s Purple is the official color of the Alzheimer’s movement.
