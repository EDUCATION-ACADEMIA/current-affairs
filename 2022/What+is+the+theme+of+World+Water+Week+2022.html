What is the theme of World Water Week 2022?

(A) Water and Climate Change:Accelerating Action

(B) Water for society-Including all

(C) Building Resilience Faster

(D) ** Seeing the unseen:The value of water

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Water Week 2022 will be held on the theme ‘Seeing the Unseen: The Value of Water,” and is built on a global need to not only see, but to understand, and – more importantly – value water.

Concept note-2: -The theme for IWW-2022 is “Water Security for Sustainable Development with Equity” focusing on various aspects of water security and management and related challenges for equitable development.

Concept note-3: -World Water Week 2023: 20 –24 August World Water Week 2023 is focused on innovation at a time of unprecedented challenges. The theme Seeds of Change: Innovative Solutions for a Water-Wise World invites us to rethink how we manage water.

Concept note-4: -The theme for World Water Week 2022 is “Seeing the Unseen: The Value of Water.” The theme will look at three main areas: the value of water among people and for development, the value of water with respect to nature and climate change, and the economic and financial value of water.

Concept note-5: -World Water Week 2022 takes place from 23 August to 1 September. The World Water Week is an annual event organized by Stockholm International Water Institute (SIWI) since 1991 to address the global water issues and related concerns of international development.
