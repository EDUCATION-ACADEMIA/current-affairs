What will be the subsidy sharing pattern between Centre and the North Eastern States for PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana?

(A) 25:75

(B) 10:90

(C) 50:50

(D) ** 90:10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The PM-KISAN was launched in February 2019 but implemented with effect from December 2018. Under the scheme, eligible farmers are provided with a financial benefit of Rs 6,000 per year in three equal instalments of Rs 2,000 each every four months.

Concept note-2: -The 12th installment of Prime Minister Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana has been declared on 31 May 2023 as Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi has shifted this installment to the receiver of the PM Kisan 12th Installment on Tuesday.

Concept note-3: -More than 8 crore framers received the 12th installment amount of Rs.2,000 through the bank account linked to the PM-Kisan Yojana. However, the beneficiary farmer must have completed their PM-Kisan eKYC process by 31 August 2022 to receive the 12th installment amount.
