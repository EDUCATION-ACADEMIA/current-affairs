When did the World Photography Day 2020 celebrated?

(A) 9 August

(B) 12 August

(C) 15 August

(D) ** 19 August

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: In 2020, the World Photography Day theme was ‘PandemicLockdown through the lens’.

Concept note-2: The day is held August 19th as it’sthe date in 1839 that the government in France purchased the patent for theDaguerreotype process. The French government called the invention of theDaguerreotype process a free gift to the world. There are over 1,500 national days.Don’t miss a single one.

Concept note-3: The theme of World Photography Day2022This year on the eve of World Photography Day, the theme that went viral in2022 is “Pandemic Lockdown through the lens".

Concept note-4: On August 19,1839, the government of France purchased the patent for the invention and gave it asa gift, “free to the world.” Hence, this day was marked as World Photography Daylater on.
