When has the Ministry of Education announced the celebration of ‘Tribal Pride Day’?

(A) ** November 15

(B) November 14

(C) November 10

(D) November 11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Ministry of Education announced that this year India willcelebrate ‘Janjatiya Gaurav Diwas’ in a grand manner in schools, and highereducation institutions on 15 November 2022.

Concept note-2: Date:15-Nov-2022. Gangtok, 15th November 2022 (IPR): Janjatiya Gaurav Diwas orTribal Pride Day, was celebrated today at Manan Kendra to honour the tribalfreedom fighter, Birsa Munda, on his birth anniversary.

Concept note-3: Tocelebrate JanJatiya Gaurav Divas 2022 in the spirit of Jan Bhagidari, anonline ‘Best Essay’ Competition on the theme of ‘My Inspiration from BirsaMunda’ is being conducted by Ministry of Tribal Affairs, in coordination withMyGov.

Concept note-4: 15th November- Birsa Munda JayantiBirsa Munda wasa significant figure in the Indian freedom movement.

Concept note-5: Notes:Tribal Pride Day Mahasammelan is scheduled to be held on November 15 atJamboori Maidan Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. Mahasammelan is being organised onthe birth anniversary of Lord Birsa Munda. This year, the Prime Minister NarendraModi will address the Tribal Pride Day Mahasammelan.
