When is National Vaccination Day 2022 is being celebrated in India?

(A) ** March 16

(B) March 14

(C) March 17

(D) March 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: National Vaccination Day. The Government of India observes16 March as National Vaccination Day to acknowledge and appreciate thehard work of frontline health care workers to ensure the vaccination of everychild.

Concept note-2: November 10 is observed as World Immunization Dayevery year, which aims to spread the incredible health benefits of getting vaccinatedand how it can prevent many diseases and disorders. Vaccination has become theneed of the hour in today’s world.

Concept note-3: World Immunization Week2022: #Long Life For All.

Concept note-4: On 16 March every year, NationalVaccination Day is observed in India which is also known as National ImmunisationDay (IMD). It was first observed on 16 March 1995 when the first dose of Oral PolioVaccine was given.
