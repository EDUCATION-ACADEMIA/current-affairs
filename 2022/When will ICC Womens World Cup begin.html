When will ICC Women’s World Cup begin?

(A) February 15

(B) ** March 04

(C) March 01

(D) April 03

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Momentum builds up for ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2023in South Africa.

Concept note-2: Women’s T20 World Cup 2023Theeighth edition of the tournament will be played at three venues in SouthAfrica.

Concept note-3: ICC Women’s World Cup 2022 began on 4 March 2022in New Zealand. The 8 teams who are part of it are India, Pakistan, Australia,Bangladesh, New Zealand, South Africa, England, and West Indies. The ICCWomen’s World Cup 2022 final is scheduled to be played on 3rd April atHagley Oval in Christchurch.
