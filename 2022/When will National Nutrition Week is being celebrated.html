When will National Nutrition Week is being celebrated?

(A) ** 1st to 7th September

(B) 3rd to 9th September

(C) 4th to 10th September

(D) 2nd to 8th September

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In India, the first week of September every year is celebrated as National Nutrition Week. In India, the first week of September every year is celebrated as National Nutrition Week. The week is observed every year from September 1-7.

Concept note-2: -National Nutrition Week is observed every year from September 1 to September 7 in a bid to raise awareness about the importance of nutrition for the human body and how we can take adequate and timely measures to eradicate hunger and malnourishment.

Concept note-3: -National Nutrition Week (NNW) is observed from September 1-7 every year to educate the populace about the significance of nutrition.

Concept note-4: -To increase awareness about healthy eating, Bharat Nutrition Week is celebrated in India from September 1st to 7th. Bharat Nutrition Week is also known as National Nutrition Week. The theme of National Nutrition Week 2022 is ‘Celebrate a World of Flavours. ‘

Concept note-5: -This year the theme of Poshan Maah 2022 is Maahila aur Swasthya and Bacha aur Shiksha’. Through the fifth Rashtriya Poshan Maah, the ministry plans to rope in the Gram Panchayats as Poshan Panchayats to carry out various activities focusing on the health of the women and the children.
