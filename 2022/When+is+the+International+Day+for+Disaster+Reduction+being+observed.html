When is the International Day for Disaster Reduction being observed?

(A) October 10

(B) October 11

(C) October 12

(D) ** October 13

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Held every 13 October, the day celebrates how people and communities around the world are reducing their exposure to disasters and raising awareness about the importance of reining in the risks that they face.

Concept note-2: -International Day for Disaster Reduction began in 1989, after a call by the United Nations General Assembly for a day to promote a global culture of risk-awareness and disaster reduction.
