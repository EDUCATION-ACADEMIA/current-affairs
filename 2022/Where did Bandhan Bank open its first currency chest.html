Where did Bandhan Bank open its first currency chest?

(A) ** Patna

(B) Indore

(C) Kolkata

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The currency chest has been set up at Vinimay CommercialComplex, Deedarganj Road, Patna. Regional Director of the RBI, SanjivDayal, MD & CEO of Bandhan Bank, Chandra Shekhar Ghosh, along withother senior officials were present at the inauguration of the Patna currencychest.

Concept note-2: Bandhan Bank opened its first currency chestin Deedargunj, Patna.

Concept note-3: Bandhan Bank has opened acurrency chest in Deedargunj, Patna. This is the bank’s first ever currencychest and will help in cash management for the branches and ATMs in thecity.

Concept note-4: The depository of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) isknown as the currency chest. It consists of the cash meant for the banksand ATMs. The chests are located on-premises of selected banks all acrossIndia.
