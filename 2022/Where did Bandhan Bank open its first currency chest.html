Where did Bandhan Bank open its first currency chest?

(A) ** Patna

(B) Indore

(C) Kolkata

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The currency chest has been set up at Vinimay Commercial Complex, Deedarganj Road, Patna. Regional Director of the RBI, Sanjiv Dayal, MD & CEO of Bandhan Bank, Chandra Shekhar Ghosh , along with other senior officials were present at the inauguration of the Patna currency chest.

Concept note-2: -Bandhan Bank has opened a currency chest in Deedargunj, Patna. This is the bank’s first ever currency chest and will help in cash management for the branches and ATMs in the city.

Concept note-3: -The depository of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) is known as the currency chest. It consists of the cash meant for the banks and ATMs. The chests are located on-premises of selected banks all across India.

Concept note-4: -On August 23, 2015, Bandhan Bank was born with a bouquet of products for customers in all segments. In its journey from an NGO to an NBFC and finally a universal bank, the objective has remained reaching out to the unbanked and the under-banked and providing last-mile banking for all, big or small.
