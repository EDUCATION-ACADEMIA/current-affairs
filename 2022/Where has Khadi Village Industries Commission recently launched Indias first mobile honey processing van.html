Where has Khadi Village Industries Commission recently launched India’s first mobile honey processing van?

(A) Lucknow

(B) Bhopal

(C) ** Ghaziabad

(D) Jaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India’s first Mobile Honey Processing Van was launchedat Village Sirora in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. It was inaugurated by theChairman of KVIC, Vinai Kumar Saxena. The Van has been designed by KVICat its Multi-disciplinary Training Centre, Panjokehra, at a cost of INR 15lakh.

Concept note-2: Chairman KVIC Shri Vinai Kumar Saxena launched thecountry’s first Mobile Honey Processing Van at Village Sirora in Ghaziabad, UPtoday.

Concept note-3: KVIC has designed the innovative Mobile HoneyProcessing Van that will process beekeepers’ honey at their doorsteps and thus savethem the hassle and the cost of taking the honey to processing plants in far-off citiesfor processing.
