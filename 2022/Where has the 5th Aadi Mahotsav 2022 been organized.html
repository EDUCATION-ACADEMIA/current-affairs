Where has the 5th Aadi Mahotsav 2022 been organized?

(A) ** Mandla, Madhya Pradesh

(B) Jaipur, Rajasthan

(C) Ahmedabad, Gujarat

(D) Gurugram, Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Kevadia. Aadi Bazaars–’ A Celebration of the spirit of Tribal culture and cuisine’ was inauguratedon 26th March 2022 at Kevadia, Statue of Unity in Narmada district ofGujarat.

Concept note-2: Aadi Mahotsav 2022- a 5 day celebration for AadivasiArt and Culture at TRTI, Pune Aadi Mahotsav 2022- a 5 day celebration forAadivasi Art and Culture at TRTI, Pune. 28th March 2022, Mumbai: Tribalculture and traditions remind us of our centuries-old traditions and culturalheritage.

Concept note-3: The most recent Aadi Mahotsav, organised inNovember 2021, in New Delhi registered a record footfall and was a grand success.More than 500+ artisans attended the festival and transacted business worth over Rs20 Crores in the two-week-long festival.

Concept note-4: The Aadi Mahotsav,organised by TRIFED and the Ministry of Tribal Affairs not only provides anopportunity to expand tribal commerce but also ensures that tribal craft, cuisine andother traditions are maintained.

Concept note-5: Venkaiah Naidu, VicePresident of India inaugurated the tribal festival Aadi Mahotsav in on Monday. Onthe occasion, Shri Naidu had called for a model of development that preserves thespecial identity of tribals.
