Where has the ‘Semicon India Conference-2022’ been organized?

(A) Kolkata

(B) Chennai

(C) Mumbai

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Semicon India 2022 Conference will be organised by Ministryof Electronics & Information Technology from April 29 – 01 May 2022 at ITCGardenia, Bengaluru around theme of - Design and Manufacture in India, for theWorld: Making India a “Semiconductor Nation”.

Concept note-2: Q. Which isthe venue of the ‘Semicon India Conference-2022’? Notes: The Prime MinisterNarendra Modi inaugurated Semicon India Conference-2022 in Bengaluru. Theconsumption of semiconductors is expected to cross USD 110 billion by 2030 andIndia has the world’s fastest growing start-up ecosystem.

Concept note-3: TheConference was inaugurated by Hon’ble Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modivia Video Conference on 29th April, 2022 at 11:00 AM. The Conferencebrings together global leaders from Industry, Academia and ResearchInstitutions.

Concept note-4: The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modiinaugurated Semicon India Conference 2022 and shared his remarks via videoconferencing at the inaugural session today.

Concept note-5: The Indiansemiconductor market was valued at USD 27.2 billion in 2021 and according to theIndia Electronics and Semiconductor Association, India’s semiconductor marketis expected to more than double between 2021 to 2026 and reach US$ 64billion.
