Where has the annual joint military training between IAF and RSAF started?

(A) Goa

(B) Maharashtra

(C) ** West Bengal

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Republic of Singapore Air Force (RSAF) and the IndianAir Force (IAF) are conducting the Joint Military Training (JMT) at Kalaikunda AirForce Station in India from 3 November to 14 December 2022, after a two-year hiatusdue to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Concept note-2: The Indian Army also talkedabout the greater synergy between both the armies as a result of the exercise.Exercise Yudh Abhyas, conducted annually between India and USA with the aim ofexchanging best practices, tactics, techniques and procedures between the Armies ofthe two nations.

Concept note-3: The 12th Edition of Exercise AgniWarrior, a bilateral exercise between the Singapore & Indian Army, which hadcommenced on 13 November 2022 concluded at Field Firing Ranges, Devlali(Maharashtra) on 30 November 2022.

Concept note-4: As of 2021, the RSAFuses four air bases domestically, most notably at Paya Lebar and Tengah, aswell as at civil airports such as Changi and Seletar. It also has overseasdetachments in various countries, most notably in Australia, the United States andFrance.

Concept note-5: DURATION OF TRAINING AND FITNESSa) Theduration of training will be 74 weeks for flying and technical branch and 52 weeks incase of Non-Tech branches. However, this period may be subject to variations due toservice exigencies.
