Where was the Indian Air Force’s Air Fest 2022 organized?

(A) Chandigarh

(B) ** Nagpur

(C) Kanpur

(D) Lucknow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Air Fest 2022 showcased maneuvers of planes &helicopters in the arsenal of the Indian Air Force (IAF) in an annual eventorganized at Head Quarters Maintenance Command, Vayusena Nagar inNagpur.

Concept note-2: Ans. It has been reported that the exam forthe Indian Air Force Agniveer Vayu batch 1/2022 will start on July 24th,2022.

Concept note-3: Indian Air Force held Air Fest 2022 showcasingmanoeuvres of planes & helicopters on November 19. The annual air showwas organised at Headquarters Maintenance Command, Vayusena Nagar inNagpur.

Concept note-4: Aspirants require to visit the official website i.e.agnipathvayu.cdac.in.

Concept note-5: Indian Air Force Group X Age Limit2022The maximum Age Limit to become Eligible to apply for the post of Air ForceGroup X is 21 years. The candidates who have surpassed the age of 21 years cannotapply for this post. However, there is no minimum age given for the candidates toapply.
