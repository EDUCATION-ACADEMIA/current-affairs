Where has the 2022 NATO Madrid Summit been held?

(A) ** Madrid, Spain

(B) Washington DC, USA

(C) London, United Kingdom

(D) Geneva, Switzerland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Issued by NATO Heads of State and Government participating in the meeting of the North Atlantic Council in Madrid 29 June 2022. We, the Heads of State and Government of the North Atlantic Alliance, have gathered in Madrid as war has returned to the European continent.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 Madrid summit was a meeting of the heads of state and heads of government of NATO member and partner countries held in Madrid, Spain, on 28–30 June 2022. Spain previously hosted a NATO Summit in 1997.

Concept note-3: -I am grateful to Lithuania for hosting our Summit. Lithuania is a vital NATO Ally. As host to a multinational battlegroup, Lithuania helps to provide strong deterrence and defence for the whole Alliance. I look forward to a successful Summit in Vilnius in July 2023.

Concept note-4: -In Madrid, Leaders will endorse a new Strategic Concept, which will assess our changed security environment and reaffirm NATO’s values, purpose and tasks. It will set out NATO’s joint positions, including on Russia and emerging challenges, and for the first time, it will also address China.
