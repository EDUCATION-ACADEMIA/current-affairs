Where has the EX-DUSTLIK exercise between the armies of India and Uzbekistan started?

(A) Jaisalmer, Rajasthan

(B) ** Yangiarik, Uzbekistan

(C) Tashkent, Uzbekistan

(D) Ranikhet, Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 3rd edition of joint training exercise between Indian and Uzbekistan armies, EX-DUSTLIK is being conducted at Yangiarik, Uzbekistan from 22nd to 31st March 2022.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Uzbekistan.

Concept note-3: -KazInd 2022: India and Kazakhstan carry out joint military drill in Meghalaya. To strengthen positive military relations between India and Kazakhstan, the nations conducted their 6th edition of Army Joint Exercise KazInd 2022 in Meghalaya’s Umroi.

Concept note-4: -It is one of the important joint military exercises that India conducts with other nations. The 8th edition of India-Sri Lanka bilateral joint exercise Mitra Shakti began on October 5, 2021, in the Combat Training School in Ampara, Sri Lanka.

Concept note-5: -The Indian Army also talked about the greater synergy between both the armies as a result of the exercise.
