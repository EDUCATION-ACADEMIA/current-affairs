Which Bangladesh film won Prasad Di Award in the 16th film Bazaar at IIFI in Goa?

(A) Baby

(B) ** Agantuk

(C) Airlift

(D) Drishyam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Film Bazaar concludes at IFFI in Goa; Bangladesh film ‘Agantuk’wins Prasad DI award. The 5-day long Film Bazaar concluded at the InternationalFilm Festival of India (IFFI) in Goa with Bangladesh feature film ‘Agantuk’ beingnamed the winner of the Prasad DI award.

Concept note-2: Detailed Solution.The correct answer is Koozhangal. ‘Koozhangal’ from India won the best film awardin the Asian Film Competition section at the 20th Dhaka International Film Festivalwhich concluded on 23 Jan 2022 in Dhaka.

Concept note-3: Here are the winnersof the film festival:No End - Nader Saeivar, Best Director. Best Male Actor - VahidMobasheri, No End. Best Female Actress - Daniela Marin Navarro, I Have ElectricDreams. Behind the Haystacks by Asimina Proedrou, is the Best Debut Feature Filmby a new Director.

Concept note-4: The Film Bazaar is a five-day event fromNov 20th to Nov 24, 2022, in the 53rd edition of the International FilmFestival of India (IFFI) that is to be held in Goa on November 20-28. Thebazaar will provide a platform for connecting all the selected film projectswith the delegates from the industry.

Concept note-5: Golden PeacockAward winners (Best Feature Film)The award carries a cash prize of ₹40lakh (US$50,000) shared equally between the director and producer. Thedirector will receive the ‘Golden Peacock’ and a certificate in addition tothe cash prize. The Producer will receive a certificate in addition to thecash.
