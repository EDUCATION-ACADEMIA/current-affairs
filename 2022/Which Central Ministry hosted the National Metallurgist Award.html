Which Central Ministry hosted the National Metallurgist Award?

(A) ** Ministry of Steel

(B) Ministry of Home Affairs

(C) Ministry of New & Renewable Energy

(D) Ministry of Power

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Background of the Scheme: National Metallurgists DayAwards, was instituted in 1962 by the then Ministry of Steel & Mines to recognise &honour the outstanding contributions of Metallurgists in the metallurgical fieldcovering operation, research, design, education, waste management and energyconservation.

Concept note-2: The Ministry of Steel has instituted the NationalMetallurgist Award after extensive deliberations with Ministry of Home Affairs(MHA) regarding rationalisation of the awards in line with the vision of Hon’blePrime Minister.

Concept note-3: Udyog Ratan Award, Manav Ratna Award,NDMC Gold Medal, Shram Yashaswi Puraskar and SMS-DEMAG ExcellenceAward-Gold Medal are some of the other awards that were bagged by SAILemployees in their individual capacities.

Concept note-4: Each year, 14thNovember, which is the birthday of India’s first Prime Minister Pt. JawaharLal Nehru, is a notable day in the calendar of the metallurgical industry ofIndia.
