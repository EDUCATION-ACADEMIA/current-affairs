Which Company has launched M15 Petrol, with a 15 percent blend of methanol with petrol?

(A) Bharat Petroleum

(B) ** Indian Oil Corp

(C) Chennai Petroleum Corp Ltd

(D) Hindustan Petroleum

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Indian Oil Corp (IOC) rolled out M15 petrol – 15per cent blend of methanol with petrol – on a pilot basis in Assam’sTinsukia district.

Concept note-2: Checks by PREMIUM TIMES showedthat the global average for methanol content in petrol is between 2% to3%.

Concept note-3: Blending of 15% methanol in gasoline can result in at least15% reduction in the import of gasoline/crude oil. In addition, this would bring downGHG emissions by 20% in terms of particulate matter, NOx, and SOx, therebyimproving the urban air quality.

Concept note-4: M-15 is a blend of 15 %methanol and 85 % Gasoline. Use of blended fuel M-15 in BS-IV cars can result inlowering down greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions by about 5 to 10 percent therebyimproving air quality.

Concept note-5: INTRODUCTION. Methanol isa clean burning, high octane blending component made from alternativenon-petroleum energy sources such as natural gas, coal and biomass. It hasbeen commercially blended into gasoline at various times and locations since1980.
