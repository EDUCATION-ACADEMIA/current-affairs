Which General Insurance Company has launched a campaign titled ‘#BahaneChhodoTaxBachao’?

(A) ** SBI General Insurance Company

(B) Oriental Insurance Company

(C) Bharti AXA General Insurance

(D) Religare Insurance Company

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Detailed Solution. The correct answer is SBI GeneralInsurance. SBI General Insurance has announced the launch of a campaign toincrease awareness of Indians around the need to buy health insurance to save tax on05 January 2022.

Concept note-2: SBI General Insurance launches BahaneChhodo Tax Bachao.

Concept note-3: ICICI Lombard General InsuranceCompanyIn its network, you have over 7800 cashless garages where you can avail ofcashless claim settlement services. This is the highest among all car insurers inIndia.
