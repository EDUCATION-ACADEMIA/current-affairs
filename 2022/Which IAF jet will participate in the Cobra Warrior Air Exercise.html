Which IAF jet will participate in the Cobra Warrior Air Exercise?

(A) Su-30MKI

(B) Rafale

(C) ** LCA Tejas

(D) MiG-21

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: IAF C-17 aircraft will provide the necessary transport supportfor induction and de-induction. Indian Air Force will participate in a multination air exercise named ‘Ex Cobra Warrior 22’ at Waddington, UK from 06to 27 March 2022.

Concept note-2: Forces from the NATO, Germany,Italy and the US have commenced the UK Royal Air Force’s (Raf) capstonetactical training exercise, Cobra Warrior 2022.

Concept note-3: DetailedSolution. The correct answer is the United Kingdom. Indian Air Force willparticipate in a multi-nation air exercise named ‘Ex Cobra Warrior 22’ atWaddington, UK from 6-27 March 2022. IAF Light Combat Aircraft (LCA)Tejas will participate in the exercise.

Concept note-4: All Four existingMig-21 Bis squadrons will be retired by 2025 and all will be re-equipped withLCA-Tejas Mk1A aircraft that will start induction from 2024 onwards till2029.
