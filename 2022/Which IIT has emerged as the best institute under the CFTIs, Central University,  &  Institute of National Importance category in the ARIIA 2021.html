Which IIT has emerged as the best institute under the “CFTIs, Central University, & Institute of National Importance” category in the ARIIA 2021?

(A) ** IIT Madras

(B) IIT Guwahati

(C) IIT Delhi

(D) IIT Roorkee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Explanation: IIT Madras has again emerged as thebest institute under the “CFTIs, Central University, & Institute of NationalImportance” category in the ARIIA 2021 list, followed by IIT Bombay and IITDelhi.

Concept note-2: Q. Which is the most innovative educational institute ofIndia in the technical category, as per the ARIIA 2021 ranking? Notes: In the AtalRanking of Institutions on Innovation Achievement 2021 award list released, IITMadras was named the most innovative educational institute of India in the technicalcategory.

Concept note-3: In the technical ranking, the Indian Institute ofTechnology Madras (IIT Madras) has been recognised as the Most InnovativeEducational Institute in India. This is the third time that IIT Madras has securedthe first rank. IIT Bombay and IIT Delhi were placed at the second and third ranksrespectively.

Concept note-4: Top IITs in India – The Ministry of HumanResource Development, MHRD, has now announced NIRF (National InstitutionalRanking Framework) Ranking for top Engineering colleges in the country. IITMadras has won the first position. IIT Delhi and IIT Bombay are in second and thirdpositions, respectively.

Concept note-5: IIT Madras has managed to retain itsrank to be No. 1 IIT in India 2021.
