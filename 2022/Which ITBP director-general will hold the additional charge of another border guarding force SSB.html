Which ITBP director-general will hold the additional charge of another border guarding force SSB?

(A) Umesh Agrawal

(B) Rohit Thakur

(C) Vipin Verma

(D) ** Sanjay Arora

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Director General of Sashastra Seema Bal, SSB Dr. Sujoy LalThaosen today took over the additional charge of Director General of Indo-TibetanBorder Police in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: The Madhya Pradesh cadre IndianPolice Service (IPS) officer has been serving as the SSB DG with the additionalcharge of ITBP. Senior IPS officer Anish Dayal Singh on Monday took charge as thenew director general (DG) of the Indo-Tibetan Border Police that guards the3,488-km Line of Actual Control with China.

Concept note-3: Anish Dayal Singhtakes charge as ITBP DG.

Concept note-4: Many posts for some, none forothersFor instance, Surjeet Singh Deswal, a 1984-batch officer of the Haryana cadre,served stints as the DG of five central forces in a span of two-odd years. He wasappointed as the DG of the ITBP in 2018 and also headed the SSB as an additionalcharge.

Concept note-5: The post of Director, SSB got re-designated asAdditional DG, SSB.
