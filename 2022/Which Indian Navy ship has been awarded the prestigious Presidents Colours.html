Which Indian Navy ship has been awarded the prestigious President’s Colours?

(A) INS Sindhurakshak

(B) INS Vikrant

(C) INS Hansa

(D) ** INS Valsura

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: President Ram Nath Kovindon Friday presented thePresident’s colour to INS Valsura, the Navy’s premier technological trainingestablishment. The President’s Colour is bestowed on a military unit inrecognition of the exceptional service rendered to the nation, both in peace and inwar.

Concept note-2: Shri Ram Nath Kovind, the Hon’ble President of Indiapresented the prestigious President’s Colour to INS Valsura on 25 March2022. The ‘Nishan Adhikari’ Lt Arun Singh Sambyal received the President’sColour.

Concept note-3: President Ram Nath Kovind awarded the prestigiousPresident’s Colour to Indian Naval Ship- INS) Valsura in Jamnagar- Gujarat thismorning.

Concept note-4: The President’s Colour Award is the highesthonour that can be bestowed upon any military unit of India. It is also knownas ′Nishaan′ which is an emblem that is worn by all unit officers on theleft-hand sleeve of their uniform. Highest Military Honour, awarded to militaryunit.

Concept note-5: Indian Naval Ship (INS) Mormugao (D67), second warshipof the P15B class of stealth guided-missile destroyers, was commissioned in theaugust presence of Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh at Naval Dockyard, Mumbaion December 18, 2022.
