Which Indian State/UT has launched the first AI-powered digital Lok Adalat?

(A) Odisha

(B) ** Rajasthan

(C) Kerala

(D) Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UPSC Mains. Q. Which Indian state/UT has launched firstAI-powered digital Lok Adalat? Notes: National Legal Services AuthorityChairman Uday Umesh Lalit launched the country’s first AI-powered digitalLok Adalat during the 18th All India Legal Services Authorities’ meet inRajasthan.

Concept note-2: Jupitice’s Digital Lok Adalat will be used byMaharashtra and Rajasthan to dispose of pending disputes/disputes at thepre-litigation stages quickly and efficiently, said Raman Aggarwal, Founder and CEO,Justice Technologies.

Concept note-3: India’s first e-court was opened at HighCourt of Judicature at Hyderabad, which is the common high court for the states ofTelangana and Andhra Pradesh. Supreme Court judge Justice Madan B. LokurInaugurated the e-court.

Concept note-4: With the first-ever full digital LokAdalat held in Rajasthan, the country’s justice system etched a history, said DineshGupta, Member Secretary of RSLSA. While Maharashtra also observed its first-everdigital Lok Adalat, the cases are restricted to traffic challans, he said inJaipur.

Concept note-5: Notes: The state of Chhattisgarh has the become thefirst in the country, to organise an state-wide e-Lok Adalat, through videoconferencing.
