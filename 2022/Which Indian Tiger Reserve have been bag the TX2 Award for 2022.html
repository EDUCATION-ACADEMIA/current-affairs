Which Indian Tiger Reserve have been bag the TX2 Award for 2022?

(A) Similipal National Park

(B) Orang Tiger Reserve

(C) Mudumalai National Park

(D) ** Sathyamangalam Tiger Reserve

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Sathyamangalam Tiger Reserve in India and Bardia NationalPark in Nepal, have won the prestigious 2022 TX2 Award for doubling their wildtiger population. The awards also celebrate the upcoming launch of the 2022 LunarYear of the Tiger.

Concept note-2: While Pench (MP) increased its tigerpopulation from 33 (2006) to 87 (2018), STR increased its numbers from 13 in2010 to 48 in 2021. In 2022, the Orang National Park and Tiger Reserve inAssam and the Sathyamangalam Tiger Reserve in Tamil Nadu received theTX2 Award.

Concept note-3: Detailed Solution. The correct answer isTamil Nadu. The Sathyamangalam Tiger Reserve in Tamil Nadu has beenawarded the international TX2 Award for doubling its tiger population since2010. It is located in Erode district, and now houses 80 tigers in its 1408sq.

Concept note-4: Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Pilibhit TigerReserve. In 2020, Pilibhit tiger reserves won the ‘TX2 Award’ for its efforts toincrease the tiger population.

Concept note-5: Two categories of awards - theTX2 Award and Tiger Conservation Excellence Award - will celebrate these sites.These awards are open to submissions from any site in a tiger range country that hasachieved remarkable measurable progress since 2010. Award winners will beannounced in January 2023.
