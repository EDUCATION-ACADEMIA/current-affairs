Which Indian automotive company has acquired a 75 percent stake in the Swiss e-Mobility Group (SEMG) for $100 million?

(A) Eicher Motors

(B) Bajaj Auto Limited

(C) Honda Motor Company

(D) ** TVS Motor Company

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: TVS Motor Company on January 27 announcedthat it has acquired a 75 percent stake in Swiss E Mobility Group AG(SEMG).

Concept note-2: TVS Motor Company, an Indian automotivemanufacturer, announced that it has acquired a 75% stake in the Swiss E-MobilityGroup (SEMG).

Concept note-3: TVS acquired EGO Mobility, a Swisse-mobility solutions company and Norton Motorcycles, a British performancemotorcycle brand in 2021 and 2020 respectively. SEMG is the DACH (Switzerland,Austria, and Germany) region’s leading e-bike manufacturer with 20% market sharein Germany & Switzerland.
