Which Indian cities have been recognised as the ‘2021 Tree City of the World’ by FAO?

(A) Indore and Hyderabad

(B) Mumbai and Indore

(C) Kolkata and Mumbai

(D) ** Mumbai and Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai and Hyderabad were awarded the tag of ‘2021 Tree City of the World’ recently by the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (UN-FAO) and Arbor Day Foundation.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Mumbai. Mumbai has been recognized as the “2021 Tree City of the World” by the Food and Agriculture Organisation of the United Nations (UN) jointly with Arbor Day Foundation.

Concept note-3: -After Hyderabad, Mumbai Recognised As A ‘Tree City Of The World’

Concept note-4: -MUMBAI: United Nations (UN) Food and Agriculture Organisation (FAO) has recognised Mumbai as the ‘2021 Tree City of the World’.

Concept note-5: -Hyderabad, Telangana In 2021, Hyderabad was announced as ‘2020 Tree City of the World’ by the United Nations Food and Agriculture Organisation (FAO) and Arbor Day Foundation. Again this year, Hyderabad and Mumbai share the title ‘2021 Tree City of the World’.
