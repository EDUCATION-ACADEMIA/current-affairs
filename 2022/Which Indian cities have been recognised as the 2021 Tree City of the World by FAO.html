Which Indian cities have been recognised as the ‘2021 Tree City of the World’ by FAO?

(A) Indore and Hyderabad

(B) Mumbai and Indore

(C) Kolkata and Mumbai

(D) ** Mumbai and Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Mumbai and Hyderabad were awarded the tag of ‘2021 TreeCity of the World’ recently by the Food and Agriculture Organization of the UnitedNations (UN-FAO) and Arbor Day Foundation.

Concept note-2: The correctanswer is Mumbai. Mumbai has been recognized as the “2021 Tree City of theWorld” by the Food and Agriculture Organisation of the United Nations (UN) jointlywith Arbor Day Foundation.

Concept note-3: After Hyderabad, MumbaiRecognised As A ‘Tree City Of The World’

Concept note-4: Q. Which Indiancity has been recognised as one of the ‘Tree Cities of the World’ in 2022? Notes: Forthe second consecutive year, Hyderabad has been recognised as one of the ‘TreeCities of the World’. It is a tag presented by Arbor Day Foundation and Food andAgriculture Organization of United Nations.

Concept note-5: MUMBAI: UnitedNations (UN) Food and Agriculture Organisation (FAO) has recognised Mumbai asthe ‘2021 Tree City of the World’.
