Which Indian city has got Chairmanship of Urban-20 events under the G20 Summit?

(A) Palwal

(B) Udupi

(C) Panchkula

(D) ** Ahmadabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under the G20 presidency of India, Ahmedabad, a UNESCO World Heritage city, will host the U20 cycle.

Concept note-2: -The G20 Summit 2023 will be held in India this year. It will be held in New Delhi at Pragati Maidan.

Concept note-3: -The 2023 G20 Delhi summit (Hindi: 2023 G20 ) is the upcoming eighteenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), a summit scheduled to take place in Pragati Maidan, New Delhi in 2023. India’s presidency began on 1 December 2022, leading up to the summit in the fourth quarter of 2023.

Concept note-4: -India is ready to host the Urban 20 (U20) meet in Ahmedabad, under its G20 Presidency, for this year. The U20, as one of the G20 engagement groups, offers a venue for city governance from the participating nations to address crucial urban development concerns.

Concept note-5: -The Group of Twenty (G20) will assemble for the eighteenth time in 2023, and the summit is expected to take place at New Delhi’s Pragati Maidan. India assumed the presidency on December 1, 2022, and held it until the summit in the third quarter of 2023.
