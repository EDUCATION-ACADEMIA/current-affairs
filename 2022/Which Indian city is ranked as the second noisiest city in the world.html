Which Indian city is ranked as the second noisiest city in the world?

(A) Mumbai

(B) Pune

(C) New Delhi

(D) ** Moradabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The city of Moradabad in Uttar Pradesh is the second-mostnoise polluted city globally, according to a recent report by the United NationsEnvironment Programme (UNEP).

Concept note-2: Which are the noisiest citiesin India? Uttar Pradesh’s Moradabad, two West Bengal cities– Kolkata and Asansol,National Capital Delhi, and Rajasthan’s capital Jaipur are the top five noisiest citiesin India.

Concept note-3: Dhaka is the loudest city in the world; Moradabadcomes a close second. Which are the other Indian cities on the list? The UnitedNations report titled ‘Frontiers 2022: Noise, Blazes and Mismatches’, released lastmonth, measured noise levels in 61 cities of the world.

Concept note-4: UP’sMoradabad world’s 2nd noisiest city on UN list, 4 more Indian cities exceed noiselevels.

Concept note-5: According to the ‘Frontiers 2022: Noise, Blazes andMismatches’ report that mentions a total of 61 cities, the highest noise pollution of119 db has been recorded in Bangladesh’s capital Dhaka, while Pakistan’s capitalIslamabad is in third place, where the noise pollution level has been recorded at 105db.
