Which Indian company has joined hands with the ‘Africa50’ investment platform to develop the ‘Kenya Transmission Project’?

(A) ** Power Grid Corporation

(B) ONGC

(C) Power Finance Corporation

(D) Rural Electrification Corporation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Q. Which Indian company has joined hands with ‘Africa50’investment platform to develop ‘Kenya Transmission Project’? Notes: Power GridCorporation of India Ltdhas signed a joint development agreement with Africa50, tocontinue to develop the Kenya Transmission Project on a public-privatepartnership basis.

Concept note-2: The correct answer is Africa50. Power GridCorporation of India Ltd has signed a joint development agreement withAfrica50, a pan-African infrastructure investment platform. It has been signed todevelop the Kenya Transmission Project on a public-private partnershipbasis.

Concept note-3: Africa50-Project FinanceThe Kenya Transmission PPPproject entails the development, financing, construction, and operation of the 400kVLessos – Loosuk and 220kV Kisumu – Musaga transmission lines under apublic-private partnership (PPP) framework.
