Which Indian conglomerate has acquired a franchise in the upcoming UAE’s T20 League?

(A) Reliance Industries

(B) Aditya Birla Group

(C) ** Adani Group

(D) Tata Group

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: "We are excited to be part of the UAE T20 league,” PranavAdani, the managing director of Adani Enterprises Limited, said. “The UAE isan amazing amalgamation of several cricket loving nations. It provides anexcellent platform for enhancing the visibility of cricket as the sport goesincreasingly global."

Concept note-2: With this, GMR has made its thirdinvestment in franchise cricket and the Adani group has made its secondinvestment. Both the conglomerates recently acquired teams at the UAE T20league.

Concept note-3: Emirati multinational logistics company DPWorld has acquired the five-year title sponsorship rights for the six-teamfranchise-based cricket league International League T20, the organisers have said in astatement.

Concept note-4: The correct answer is GMR Group. Delhi-basedinfrastructure company GMR Group has acquired the rights to own and operate aDubai franchise in the T20 League in UAE. The six-team league that will be playedin 2022 has been sanctioned by the Emirates Cricket Board and will be heldannually.

Concept note-5: The league will comprise six teams and 34matches.
