Which Indian economist has been appointed the chief economist of the World Bank?

(A) Viral Acharya

(B) Urjit Patel

(C) Raghuram Rajan

(D) ** Indermit Gill

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Kaushik Basu. WorldBank Group President Jim Yong Kim announced the appointment of Kaushik Basuas the institution’s new Chief Economist and Senior Vice President on 5th September2012. He becomes the first Indian appointed as the chief economist of theWorld Bank.

Concept note-2: Kaushik Basu (born 9 January 1952) is anIndian economist who was Chief Economist of the World Bank from 2012 to2016.

Concept note-3: WASHINGTON, July 21, 2022-World Bank PresidentDavid Malpass today announced the selection of Indermit Gill as the ChiefEconomist of the World Bank Group and Senior Vice President for DevelopmentEconomics.

Concept note-4: Gill will be only the second Indian to serve as chiefeconomist at the World Bank. Kaushik Basu was the first, having served in the jobfrom 2012-2016.
