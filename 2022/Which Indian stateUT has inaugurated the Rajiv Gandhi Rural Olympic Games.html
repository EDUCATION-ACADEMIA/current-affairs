Which Indian state/UT has inaugurated the ‘Rajiv Gandhi Rural Olympic Games’?

(A) Chhattisgarh

(B) ** Rajasthan

(C) Telangana

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Gehlot inaugurated the block-level Rajiv Gandhi RuralOlympic Games at Rajgarh in Churu district on Monday.

Concept note-2: InRajasthan, Rajiv Gandhi Rural Olympic Games have started in more than 11,000panchayats this morning. Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot inaugurated the games at thestate-level function organised at Pal Panchayat of Luni block in Jodhpurdistrict.

Concept note-3: On October 20, 2022, Rajasthan Chief Minister AshokGehlot concluded the Rajiv Gandhi Rural Olympic Games, which started fromLuni Panchayat Samiti of Jodhpur in the state from August 29, at SawaiMansingh Stadium in Jaipur.

Concept note-4: UPSC Mains. Q. Which stateannounced ‘Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award’ and ‘Rajiv Gandhi RuralOlympics’? Notes: Rajasthan Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot announced thatthe Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award will be introduced in the state forsportspersons.

Concept note-5: Qila Raipur Sports Festival, popularly known asthe Rural Olympics, is held annually in Qila Raipur (near Ludhiana), in Punjab,India.
