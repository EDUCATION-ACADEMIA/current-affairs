Which Indian woman cricketer became the fastest to score 3000 runs in ODIs?

(A) Mithali Raj

(B) Harmanpreet Kaur

(C) Jhulan Goshwami

(D) ** Smriti Mandhana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Star batter Smriti Mandhana has completed 3000 runs inone-day internationals, becoming the quickest India woman player, and thethird quickest Indian - after Shikhar Dhawanand Virat Kohli - to cross thelandmark.

Concept note-2: 22 women players in the world have over 3000 ODIruns, but only two have reached to the landmark faster than Mandhana -Belinda Clark (62 innings) and Meg Lanning (64 innings). Since her debut inODIs, only three women batters have made more scores of fifty or more inODIs.

Concept note-3: Who was the fastest to 3000 runs in ODIs? Hashim Amlawas the fastest to complete 3000 runs in ODI cricket. He completed the milestone in57 innings in the ODIs.

Concept note-4: Detailed Solution. The correctanswer is Harmanpreet Kaur. Harmanpreet Kaur is the first Indian womancricketer to hit a century in T20 International cricket by scoring 103 runs in 51balls.
