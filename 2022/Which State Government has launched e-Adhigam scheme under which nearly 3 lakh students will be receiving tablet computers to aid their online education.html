Which State Government has launched ‘e-Adhigam’ scheme under which nearly 3 lakh students will be receiving tablet computers to aid their online education?

(A) Assam

(B) Bihar

(C) ** Haryana

(D) Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: The Haryana State Government launches the ‘e-Adhigam’ scheme under which nearly 3 lakh students will be receiving tablet computers to aid their online education. The state government has planned to provide the gadget to five lakh students.

Concept note-2: -The vision was set by the state’s CM, whose support helped the Department of Education navigate difficult choices. The essence of the Haryana tablets model is that each student should have a separate tablet, for learning at her own pace, and the tablet can be used at home or at school in the presence of the teacher.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which state government launched ‘e-Adhigam’ scheme to provide tablets to five lakh students? Notes: Under the Haryana Government, around five lakh school students of classes 10th and 12th will receive tablets.

Concept note-4: -“Tablets and data are tools for students which will help them acquire skills of the 21st century and open new opportunities. Through e-learning, students of Haryana will become global students,” he said.

Concept note-5: -Objective Of e-Adhigam Scheme 3 lakh students will be provided free tablets in order to help them get online education and these devices will have preloaded content and personalization according to the adapted learning schedule provided by the Haryana Government. The students will also be provided 2GB of free data.
