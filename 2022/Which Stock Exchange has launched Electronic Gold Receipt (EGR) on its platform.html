Which Stock Exchange has launched Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) on its platform?

(A) Frankfurt Stock Exchange

(B) Calcutta Stock Exchange

(C) Madras Stock Exchange

(D) ** Bombay Stock Exchange

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: BSE, or the Bombay Stock Exchange, launched ElectronicGold Receipt (EGR) on its platform on 24 October 2022. BSE, or the Bombay StockExchange, launched Electronic Gold Receipt (EGR) on its platform on 24 October2022.

Concept note-2: The Bombay Stock Exchange launched EGRtrading on its platform. It introduced two new products of 995 and 999purity during the Muhurat trading on Diwali.

Concept note-3: TheInstruments representing gold in electronic form is termed as Electronic GoldReceipts (EGRs) and is notified as securities, with trading, clearing andsettlement features similar to other securities that are currently available inIndia. BSE is the first exchange in India to launch Electronic Gold Receipts(EGR).

Concept note-4: Electronic Gold Receipts can be defined as depositorygold receipts that can be traded on the stock exchanges. They are similar to stocksand are traded and held in Demat accounts, just like them. Under this form of gold,the trading exchange holds the underlying value of the receipt in physical gold in avault.

Concept note-5: Gold will first have to be converted into a Demat accountsimilar to share trading and then with the receipt investors can trade ingold. This Gold will be in vaults and vault manager will be provided byERG.
