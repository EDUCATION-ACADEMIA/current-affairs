Which UT has become the first one to be integrated with the National Single Window System?

(A) Ladakh

(B) New Delhi

(C) Puducherry

(D) ** Jammu and Kashmir

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: In a historic achievement, Jammu & Kashmir became the firstUnion Territory to be onboarded the National Single Window System (NSWS).This marks a major leap in Ease of Doing Business (EoDB) in the UnionTerritory.

Concept note-2: The Single Window Clearance System was formed asa consequence to Jammu and Kashmir Single Window (Industrial Investments andBusiness Facilitation) Act, 2018 (Act No. 10 of 2018) to provide necessary timebound license, permissions and sanctions for the establishment of industry in the UTof Jammu and Kashmir.

Concept note-3: At the same time, a reorganisation actwas also passed, which would reconstitute the state into two union territories, Jammuand Kashmir and Ladakh. The reorganisation took effect from 31 October2019.

Concept note-4: The National Single Window System (NSWS) is a digitalplatform to guide you in identifying and applying for approvals according to yourbusiness requirements. The Know Your Approvals (KYA) module includes guidancefor 32 Central Departments and 31 States.

Concept note-5: NationalSingle Window System for Investors and Businesses Launched by Shri PiyushGoyal.
