Which Union Ministry implemented the ‘Saksham Anganwadi and Poshan 2.0’ scheme?

(A) Ministry of Tribal Affairs

(B) Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare

(C) ** Ministry of Women and Child Development

(D) Ministry of Health

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Ministry of Women and Child Developmenthas issuedGuidelines for the implementation of ‘Saksham Anganwadi and Poshan2.0′.

Concept note-2: Muskan Anand. B [Ministry of Women and ChildDevelopment] :Ministry of Women and Child Development has issued OperationalGuidelines.

Concept note-3: The name of the scheme was changed toPM-POSHAN (Pradhan Mantri Poshan Shakti Nirman) Scheme, in September2021, by the Ministry of Education, which is the nodal ministry for thescheme.

Concept note-4: Saksham Anganwadi and Poshan 2.0 is anIntegrated Nutrition Support Programme. The scheme aims to address thechallenges of malnutrition among pregnant women & lactating mothers, children,adolescent girls, by bringing in a strategic shift in nutrition content anddelivery.

Concept note-5: Mission POSHAN 2.0It seeks to address the challengesof malnutrition in children, adolescent girls, pregnant women and lactating mothersthrough a strategic shift in nutrition content and delivery and by creation of aconvergent eco-system to develop and promote practices that nurture health, wellnessand immunity.
