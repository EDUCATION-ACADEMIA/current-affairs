Which Union Ministry launched the ‘Bhasha Certificate Selfie’ Campaign?

(A) Ministry of Culture

(B) Ministry of Tourism

(C) ** Ministry of Education

(D) Ministry of External Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Education launched a campaign ‘Bhasha Certificate Selfie to encourage cultural diversity and promote mulitlinguism and to foster the spirit of Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat.

Concept note-2: -Link for Download Bhasha Sangam Mobile App launched by Hon’ble Education Minister under Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat Programme - ,

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bhasha Sangam. Bhasha Sangam is an initiative of the Ministry of Education under Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat. This app consists of daily used conversations in various languages.

Concept note-4: -"Bhasha Sangam” marks the unique symphony of languages of our country and is an expression of our shared dreams, hopes and aspirations for one India. Under Bhasha Sangam initiative it was planned to provide multilingual exposure to students in Indian languages listed in the VIII schedule of the Constitution of India.
