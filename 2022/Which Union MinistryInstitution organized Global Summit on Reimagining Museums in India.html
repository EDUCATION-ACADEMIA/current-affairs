Which Union Ministry/Institution organized Global Summit on ‘Reimagining Museums in India’?

(A) NITI Aayog

(B) Ministry of Tourism

(C) ** Ministry of Culture

(D) UNESCO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Q. Which Union Ministry/Institution organized GlobalSummit on ‘Reimagining Museums in India’? Notes: Ministry of Culture organised atwo-day Global Summit on ‘Reimagining Museums in India’ in the monthof February 2022. The summit was organized under the aegis of Azadi kaAmrit Mahotsav.

Concept note-2: The correct answer is Hyderabad.The Ministry of Culture is organizing a first-of-its-kind, a global summit inHyderabad on ‘Reimagining Museums in India’, on 15-16 February 2022. Itwill be inaugurated by Minister of Culture, Tourism and Development ofNortheastern Region, G. Kishan Reddy.

Concept note-3: Ministry of Culture,Government of India will organize a first of its kind, 2-Day Global Summit on‘Reimagining Museums in India’, on 15-16 February 2022.

Concept note-4: TheReimagining Museums Global Summit 2022 will focus on best practices andstrategies to develop India’s museums. It will be organised as a part of Azadi kaAmrit Mahotsav, a flagship program to mark the 75th anniversary of India’sindependence.

Concept note-5: The Ministry of Culture is hosting thefirst-ever global summit on ‘Reimagining Museums in India’, scheduled forinauguration tomorrow, February 15, 2022. The Summit will be held inHyderabad and the Union Culture Minister, G Kishan Reddy will inaugurate theevent.
