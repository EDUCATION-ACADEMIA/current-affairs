Which airport in India has been awarded the ‘Covid champion’ award at Wings India 2022?

(A) Goa International Airport

(B) Surat International Airport

(C) ** Cochin International Airport

(D) Chennai International Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Kochi: The Cochin International Airport Ltd (Cial) has wonthe Covid Champion award at Wings India 2022 - Asia’s largest civil aviation eventjointly organized by the ministry of civil aviation (MoCA) and Federation ofIndian Chamber of Commerce and Industry (FICCI) at Begumpet airport,Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: Q. Which city is set to host ‘WINGS INDIA 2022’,the exhibition on civil aviation? Notes: Hyderabad is scheduled to host the ‘WINGSINDIA 2022’, the flagship biennial exhibition on civil aviation. It is organised fromMarch 24 - 27, 2022 at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: TheAirport Authority has won recognition in the 5-15 million passenger category ofairports operating in the Asia-Pacific region. The ACI award is recognised as thehighest honour in the global aviation sector owing to its extensive surveymethodology practices.

Concept note-4: Bengaluru Airport awarded ‘BestAirport’ at Wings India Awards 2022.
