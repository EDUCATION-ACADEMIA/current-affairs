Which app has become the first app to go live on the Open Network for Digital Commerce in Bengaluru?

(A) Microsoft To Do

(B) Any.do

(C) Todoist

(D) ** Paytm Mall

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Paytm Mall Becomes The First App To Go Live On ONDC’sSoft Launch. Paytm E-commerce Pvt Ltd (PEPL), which is the parent entity ofPaytm Mall, has gone live on the ONDC network’s beta testing launch in Bengaluru,Karnataka.

Concept note-2: Paytm Mall becomes the first app to be on theOpen Network of Digital Commerce, It came on the network while its beta testingon 16 Pincode in Bangalore. Paytm mall has gone live today on the OpenNetwork Of Digital Commerce (ONDC) network.

Concept note-3: Logisticsservice provider Delhivery has announced that it is going live on the OpenNetwork for Digital Commerce (ONDC), the government-backed e-commercenetwork.

Concept note-4: Open Network for Digital Commerce, abbreviatedONDC, is a private non-profit Section 8 company established by the Department forPromotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) of Government of India todevelop open e-commerce.

Concept note-5: Prior to Meesho, e-commerceplatforms, including Paytm Mall and CoutLoot, had also joined ONDC in abid to democratize online shopping. Start-ups like Dunzo, Loadshare, andShiprocket are shouldering the logistics and delivery responsibilities on the ONDCplatform.
