Which bank has allowed invoicing and payments for international trade in Indian Rupee in August 2022?

(A) State Bank of India

(B) Securities & Exchange Board of India

(C) Life Insurance Corporation

(D) ** Reserve Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has allowed invoicing andpayments for international trade in Indian Rupee vide A.P (DIR Series) Circular No.10 RBI/2022-2023/90 dated 11.07. 2022 on “International Trade Settlement in IndianRupees (INR)”.

Concept note-2: Banknotes in India are currently beingissued in the denomination of ₹10, ₹20, ₹50, ₹100 ₹200, ₹500, and ₹2000.These notes are called banknotes as they are issued by the Reserve Bank ofIndia.

Concept note-3: Kolkata-based UCO Bank was the first Indian bank toreceive the RBI’s approval to open a special Vostro account with Russia’sGazprombank, which was set up by the world’s largest gas producer and exporter,Gazprom.

Concept note-4: India currently has 6 Payment Banks namely, AirtelPayment Bank, India Post Payment Bank, Fino, Paytm Payment Bank, NSDLPayment Bank and Jio Payment Bank.

Concept note-5: The Reserve Bankof India (RBI), vide press release dated February 03, 2022, had cautionedthe public not to undertake forex transactions on unauthorised electronictrading platforms (ETPs) or remit/deposit money for unauthorised forextransactions.
