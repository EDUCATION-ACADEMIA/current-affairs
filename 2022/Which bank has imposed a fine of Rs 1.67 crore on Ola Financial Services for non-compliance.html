Which bank has imposed a fine of Rs 1.67 crore on Ola Financial Services for non-compliance?

(A) Union Bank of India

(B) ** Reserve Bank of India

(C) Central Bank of India

(D) State Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has imposed a monetarypenalty of ₹1.67 crore on Ola Financial Services Private Limited for non-compliancewith certain provisions related to pre-paid payment instruments and Know YourCustomer norms.

Concept note-2: The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has, by anorder dated October 17, 2022, imposed a monetary penalty of ₹6 lakh (Rupees Sixlakh only) on The Pratap Co-operative Bank Ltd., Mumbai (the bank) fornon-compliance with certain provisions of the ‘Reserve Bank of India – (Know YourCustomer (KYC)) Direction, 2016’.

Concept note-3: Ambedkar NagarikSahakari Bank Maryadit, Gwalior (M.P) (the bank) for contravention of/non-compliance with the directions issued by the RBI to Urban Co-operative Bankson Exposure Norms & Statutory/ Other Restrictions and Know Your Customer(KYC).

Concept note-4: RBI imposes ₹1.68-crore penalty on Ola FinancialServices - The Hindu BusinessLine.
