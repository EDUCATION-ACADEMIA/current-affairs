Which bank has launched Green Deposit Program?

(A) State Bank of India

(B) Kotak Mahindra Bank

(C) ** DSB Bank India

(D) Punjab National Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is DBS Bank India. DBS Bank India hasannounced the launch of its Green Deposit Programme for corporate clientsproviding a simple way for companies to support environmentally friendly projectsor avenues.

Concept note-2: Green Deposits is a fixed deposit for aspecific tenure, where the amount deposited by you will be used for lending torenewable energy projects, green building projects and projects in smart agri,water or waste management projects etc.

Concept note-3: Mumbai,28 December, 2021: IndusInd Bank has announced the launch of ‘GreenFixed Deposits’ where the deposit proceeds will be used to finance projectsand firms supporting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals(SDGs).
