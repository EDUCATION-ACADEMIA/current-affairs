Which bank has launched its KCC Digital Renewal scheme, enabling eligible customers to renew their Kisan Credit Card?

(A) ICICI Bank

(B) HDFC Bank

(C) Axis Bank

(D) ** Indian Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abhishek Mishra. Answer: Option D Explanation: Indian Bank Unveiled KCC Digital Renewal Scheme enabling customers to renew their Kisan Credit Card accounts through the digital platform.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Option A Explanation: Indian Bank launched its KCC Digital Renewal scheme, enabling eligible customers to renew their Kisan Credit Card accounts via digital modes.

Concept note-3: -NABARD IN NEWS The model of KCC scheme was prepared by NABARD (National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development) that met the recommendations of RV Gupta Committee.

Concept note-4: -Annual Renewal: Every Kisan credit card account has to be renewed every year before the due date to get the assigned credit limit. This renewal has to be done for the entire period of five years.

Concept note-5: -To meet the short term credit requirement of rearing of animals, Birds, and fisheries etc. Farmers individual or joint Liability Groups or Self Help Groups including tenant farmers engaged in Dairy, Poultry, Inland Fisheries and Aquaculture, Marine Fisheries are eligible for availing loan under this scheme.
