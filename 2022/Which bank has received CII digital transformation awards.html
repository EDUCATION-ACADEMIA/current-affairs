Which bank has received CII digital transformation awards?

(A) State Bank of Mysore

(B) State Bank of Indore

(C) ** Karnataka Bank

(D) State Bank of Saurashtra

Concept note-1: HDFC Bank wins award for Most Innovative Practicein Customer Experience at CII DX Digital Transformation Awards2022.
