Which bank has set up a “RACE” goal as part of its plan to be top 3 PSBs in India?

(A) ** Union Bank

(B) Punjab National Bank

(C) State Bank of India

(D) Central Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Answer: Option A Explanation: The fifth largest Public SectorBank in India, the Union Bank has set “RACE” - grow RAM loans, improveAsset quality, increase CASA and increase Earnings - as its goal for theyear.

Concept note-2: State Bank of India was the leading Indian publicsector bank based on market capitalization with nearly 3.7 trillion Indianrupees as of October 2022. Bank of Baroda followed, with Canara Bankranking third that year.

Concept note-3: Motilal Oswal tracks top 7 PSUbanks - State Bank of India, Punjab National Bank, Bank of India, Bank ofBaroda, Central Bank, Union Bank and Indian Bank and from the list itstop picks are SBI, BoB and Central Bank.

Concept note-4: Bank ofBaroda is a multinational Indian bank owned by Government of India andcountry’s third largest lender. The bank merge with Vijaya Bank and DenaBank and become third largest bank in India after SBI bank and ICICIbank.
