Which bank has won the digital transformation award ‘DX 2021 Awards’ by the CII?

(A) ** Karnataka Bank

(B) Axis

(C) ICICI

(D) HDFC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Karnataka Bankwins CII award for best practice indigital transformation.

Concept note-2: Karnataka Bank is awardedwith the prestigious Digital Transformation Award, ‘DX 2021 Awards’ inrecognition of the “Innovative” Best Practice ‘KBL VIKAAS’, conferredby Confederation of Indian Industry [CII] for Best Practice in DigitalTransformation.

Concept note-3: Karnataka Bank has bagged National DigitalTransformation Awards - DX 2022 Awards - instituted by the Confederationof Indian Industry for best practice in digital transformation under BFSIsegment.

Concept note-4: Mumbai, December 24, 2021: HDFC Bank’s efforts infurthering financial inclusion through partnership with Government of India’sCommon Service Centres (CSC) have been adjudged ‘Most Innovative BestPractice’ for financial inclusion at the coveted CII Digital Transformation Award2021.

Concept note-5: Karnataka Bank wins DX 2022 Awards - 3 Awards inDigital Transformation.
