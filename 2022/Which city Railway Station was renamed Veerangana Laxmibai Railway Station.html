Which city Railway Station was renamed Veerangana Laxmibai Railway Station?

(A) Kanpur

(B) Lucknow

(C) ** Jhansi

(D) Agra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: JHANSI: After waking up on the misty first day of 2022,people of Jhansi found the new name ‘Virangana Lakshmibai’ railway stationwritten on the board in place of Jhansi railway station as well as on the mainentrance of the station.

Concept note-2: The correct answer is JhansiRailway Station. Uttar Pradesh’s Jhansi railway station will now be known a‘Veerangana Lakshmibai Railway Station’.

Concept note-3: VirangnaLakshmibai Railway Station is located in the heart of Jhansi City, UttarPradesh.
