Which city became the first city in South Asia to top CDP’s climate action list?

(A) Pune

(B) Delhi

(C) ** Mumbai

(D) Surat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Mumbai Becomes the First City in South Asia to Top CDP’sClimate Action List.

Concept note-2: The CDP “A List” comprises companiesrecognized as global leaders who demonstrate excellence in their actions, strategies,and results in responding to climate change. CDP evaluated over 10,000companies worldwide this year, and the climate change 2022 “A List” includes283 companies, of which 74 are Japanese.

Concept note-3: MumbaiBecomes First City In The Country To Launch Climate Action Plan: Know AllDetails.

Concept note-4: Mumbai becomes the first major city in India to launcha Climate Action Plan.

Concept note-5: UPSC MainsQ. Which Indian cityreleased a ‘Climate Action Plan’ and laid down 30-year Roadmap to tackle challengesof Climate Change? Notes: The Mumbai Climate Action Plan (MCAP) releasedrecently has laid down a 30-year roadmap for the city to tackle the challenges ofclimate change.
