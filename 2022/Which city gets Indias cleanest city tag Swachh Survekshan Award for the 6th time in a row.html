Which city gets India’s cleanest city tag ‘Swachh Survekshan Award’ for the 6th time in a row?

(A) Pune

(B) Hyderabad

(C) ** Indore

(D) Lucknow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Recently, the President awarded Indore as the cleanest city forthe sixth consecutive year as part of the Azadi75 Swachh Survekshan 2022, hosted aspart of the Swachh Bharat Mission- Urban 2.0.

Concept note-2: Indore baggedthe title of India’s cleanest city for the sixth time in a row as the results of theCentral government’s annual cleanliness survey ‘Swachh Survekshan Awards 2022’were announced.

Concept note-3: As per the States, Madhya Pradesh hastopped the chart in the category of best-performing states in the Swach SurvekshanAwards 2022. This survey is the seventh edition of the Swachh Survekshan 2022initiative.

Concept note-4: Shivamogga in Karnataka received the fast mover cityaward. Indore further cemented its position by emerging as India’s first 7-starGarbage Free city, while Surat, Bhopal, Mysuru, Navi Mumbai, Vishakhapatnam,and Tirupati earned 5-star Garbage Free certifications. The State awards saw a fewsurprises.

Concept note-5: President Droupadi Murmu presents awards as partof Azadi75 Swachh Survekshan 2022. Indore has been adjudged the cleanest city ofIndia for the sixth year in a row, while Madhya Pradesh is the cleanest state in thecountry.
