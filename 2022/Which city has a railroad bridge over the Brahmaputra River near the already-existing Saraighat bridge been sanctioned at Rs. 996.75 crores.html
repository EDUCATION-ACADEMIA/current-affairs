Which city has a railroad bridge over the Brahmaputra River near the already-existing Saraighat bridge been sanctioned at Rs. 996.75 crores?

(A) Shillong

(B) Kohima

(C) Dispur

(D) ** Guwahati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Rail-cum-Road Bridge across Brahmaputra river nearthe existing Saraighat bridge at Guwahati approved at cost of Rs. 996.75Crore.

Concept note-2: The New Saraighat Bridge, also referred to as the 2ndSaraighat Bridge is a beam bridge in India, connecting the North bank of Assam toSouth bank of Assam. The bridge spans the Brahmaputra River besides OldSaraighat Bridge, from Pandu, Guwahati in the south to Amingaon to thenorth.

Concept note-3: The roadway opened in March 1963. Prime MinisterJawaharlal Nehru dedicated the bridge and formally named it after the Battle ofSaraighat on 7 June 1963. Total cost of construction was around Rs 10.65 crore andthe total length of it is 4258 feet.

Concept note-4: Saraighat Bridge is the firstrail-cum-road bridge constructed over the Brahmaputra River in Guwahati.It was opened to traffic in April 1962 by then Prime Minister JawaharlalNehru.
