Which city has been named 1st cultural and tourism capital of SCO?

(A) ** Varanasi

(B) Haridwar

(C) Mathura

(D) Ayodhya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The city of Varanasi has been nominated as the first-ever SCOTourism and Cultural Capital during the period 2022-2023 at the 22nd Meeting ofShanghai Cooperation Organization (SCO) Council of Heads of State in Samarkand,Uzbekistan on September 16, 2022.

Concept note-2: Varanasi nominated asfirst-ever SCO Tourism and Cultural Capital for 2022-2023. Varanasi city has beennominated as the first cultural and tourism capital during 2022-2023 at the 22ndmeeting of the SCO council in Samarkand.

Concept note-3: SCO is aneight-member organisation which looks after the economic and security welfare ofcountries like China, Russia, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan,Pakistan and India. The organisation aims to promote tourism and human contactamong the masses across different countries.

Concept note-4: The ShanghaiCooperation Organization (SCO) is an intergovernmental organization founded inShanghai on 15 June 2001.

Concept note-5: The SCP Foundation originated inthe “paranormal” /x/ forum of 4chan in 2007, where the very first SCP file,SCP-173, was posted by an anonymous user (later identified as Moto42),accompanied by an image of the sculpture “Untitled 2004” by Japanese artist IzumiKato.
