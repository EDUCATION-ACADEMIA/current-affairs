Which city hosted the ‘UN session to save Biodiversity’?

(A) Rome

(B) ** New York

(C) Tokyo

(D) Paris

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: 7-19 December 2022 in Montreal, Canada, governments fromaround the world came together to agree on a new set of goals to guide global actionthrough 2030 to halt and reverse nature loss.

Concept note-2: Notes: The ‘UNsession to save Biodiversity’ was held at the United Nations headquarters in NewYork.

Concept note-3: The General Assembly selected New York City as the sitefor the headquarters of the UN, construction began on 14 September 1948 and thefacility was completed on 9 October 1952.

Concept note-4: United NationsDivision for Sustainable Development- Sustainable Development Issues - Biodiversity.Conservation of biological diversity is the subject of Chapter 15 of Agenda 21, whichwas adopted by the United Nations Conference on Environment and Development, in1992, in Rio de Janeiro.
