Which city is hosting India’s biggest Drone Festival-Bharat Drone Mahotsav 2022?

(A) Hyderabad

(B) Bengaluru

(C) ** New Delhi

(D) Pune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi will inaugurate India’sbiggest Drone Festival - Bharat Drone Mahotsav 2022 - on 27 May 2022 at 10 AM atPragati Maidan, New Delhi.

Concept note-2: ’Bharat Drone Mahotsav 2022’ is atwo-day event and being held on May 27 and 28. Modi will inaugurate India’s biggestDrone Festival - ‘Bharat Drone Mahotsav 2022’ - on May 27 at 10 am atPragati Maidan, New Delhi, the Prime Minister’s Office (PMO) said in astatement.

Concept note-3: He will also witness open-air drone demonstrations.Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate Bharat Drone Mahotsav 2022, India’sbiggest drone festival, today at 10am at Delhi’s Pragati Maidan. The prime ministerwill interact with Kisan Drone pilots and startups in the drone exhibitioncentre.

Concept note-4: Recently, India’s biggest Drone Festival - Bharat DroneMahotsav 2022 was inaugurated in New Delhi by the Prime Minister. A virtualaward of drone pilot certificates, panel discussions, product launches, display of a‘Made in India’ Drone Taxi prototype, flying demonstrations, among others were thekey events.

Concept note-5: BHARAT DRONE MAHOTSAV 2022Morethan 70 exhibitors will display various use cases of drones at the exhibitioncentre.
