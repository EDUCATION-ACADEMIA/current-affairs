Which city is to host the first G20 Sherpa Meeting in India from 04 to 07 December?

(A) ** Udaipur

(B) Varanasi

(C) Srinagar

(D) Thane

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The 1st Sherpa Meeting of India’s G20 Presidency commencedtoday, on 04 December 2022, in Udaipur Rajasthan.

Concept note-2: The 1stSherpa Meeting of India’s G20 Presidency, chaired by G20 Sherpa Shri AmitabhKant, was commencing in the historic city of Udaipur, a city located in RajasthanState.

Concept note-3: India hosted a meeting of G20 finance ministers andcentral bank governors in 2002. G20 was raised to the Summit level in 2008 toaddress the global financial and economic crisis of 2008. The G-20 operates without apermanent secretariat or staff.

Concept note-4: India holds the Presidency of theG20 from December 1, 2022 to November 30, 2023. The 43 Heads of Delegations- thelargest ever in G20-will be participating in the final New Delhi Summit in Septembernext year.
