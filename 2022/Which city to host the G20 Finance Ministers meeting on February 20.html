Which city to host the G20 Finance Ministers meeting on February 20?

(A) Delhi

(B) Hyderabad

(C) Jaipur

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Bengaluru to host G20 Finance Ministers meeting in Februarynext year.

Concept note-2: The first G20 Infrastructure Working Group (IWG)meeting under G20 India Presidency will take place in Pune, Maharashtra on16th-17th January 2023.

Concept note-3: The First G20 Finance and Central BankDeputies Meeting, under the Indian Presidency, was held in Bengaluru, Karnataka,during December 13-14, 2022, under the co-chairship of Secretary, Economic Affairs,Shri Ajay Seth and Deputy Governor, Reserve Bank of India, Dr. MichaelPatra.

Concept note-4: India holds the Presidency of the G20 from December 1,2022 to November 30, 2023. The 43 Heads of Delegations- the largest ever inG20-will be participating in the final New Delhi Summit in September nextyear.
