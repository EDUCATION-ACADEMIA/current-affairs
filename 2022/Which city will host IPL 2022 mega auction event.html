Which city will host IPL 2022 mega auction event?

(A) Pune

(B) New Delhi

(C) Kochi

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The IPL 2022 mega auction will be a two-day event that willbe held in Bengaluru.

Concept note-2: The IPL 2022 player auction is scheduledto be held in Bengaluru on February 12 and 13.

Concept note-3: In this auction,each team will have an additional ₹5 crore to spend at this auction, making theoverall purse ₹95 crore (approx. $11.5 million).

Concept note-4: IPLauction 2023 will take place in Kochi town of Kerala. The event will behosted at Grand Hyatt. This is the first time that Kochi will host the IPLauction.
