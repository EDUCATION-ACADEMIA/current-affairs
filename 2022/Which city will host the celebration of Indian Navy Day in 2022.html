Which city will host the celebration of Indian Navy Day in 2022?

(A) Delhi

(B) Chennai

(C) Mumbai

(D) ** Visakhapatnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: This year, as India ushers in the ‘Amrit Kaal’ with thecommencement of the celebrations of 75 years of her independence, the Indian Navyis all set to demonstrate India’s combat prowess and capability through an‘Operational Demonstration’ at Visakhapatnam on Sunday, December 04,2022.

Concept note-2: It is notable that on the auspicious occasion of IndianNavy Day 2022, Her Excellency the President of our country Mrs. Draupadi Murmuwill visit Andhra Pradesh on 4th December, where she is going to participate in theIndian Navy Day 2022 to be held in Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-3: Indiacelebrates December 04 as Navy Day, every year, to acknowledge the role of theIndian Navy and commemorate its achievements in ‘Operation Trident’ during the1971 Indo-Pak War.

Concept note-4: The theme of Indian Navy Day 2022 is“Swarnim Vijay Varsh”. Navy Week is observed from 1 to 7 December. On thisday, The Indian Navy demonstrated India’s combat prowess and capabilitythrough an ‘Operational Demonstration’ at Visakhapatnam on December 4,2022.

Concept note-5: President Droupadi Murmu will be the guest ofhonour at the event hosted by Admiral R Hari Kumar, Chief of the NavalStaff. Dignitaries from the central and state governments will also attend theceremony.
