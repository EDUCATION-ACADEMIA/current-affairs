Which company has bagged the contract to build the upcoming Noida International Airport at Jewar, UP?

(A) Hindustan Construction Company

(B) Adani Group

(C) GMR Group

(D) ** Tata Projects

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tata Group’s infrastructure and construction arm, Tata Projects, has bagged the contract to construct the upcoming Noida International Airport at Jewar, in UP.

Concept note-2: -In October 2021, Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) began preparing a Detailed Project Report (DPR) for a new metro line linking Knowledge Park II and Jewar Airport. In July 2022, APCO Infratech was declared as the lowest bidder to construct a 30 km 6-lane expressway from Noida Airport to the Delhi Mumbai Expressway.

Concept note-3: -Tata Projects Limited has been selected by Yamuna International Airport Private Limited (YIAPL) to build Noida International Airport (NIA). Tata Projects will construct the terminal, runway, airside infrastructure, roads, utilities, landside facilities and other ancillary buildings at the airport.

Concept note-4: -Tata Projects wins EPC contract for Noida International Airport.
