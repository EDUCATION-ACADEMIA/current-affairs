Which company has become the first Indian firm to post earnings call on metaverse?

(A) Infosys Technologies

(B) Hindustan Unilever Limited

(C) ** Reliance Industries Limited

(D) Tata Advanced Systems

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) posted the proceedingsof its second quarter earnings call on the metaverse. The announcementmade Reliance the first Indian firm to use the medium to engage with itsstakeholders.

Concept note-2: Reliance Industries Limited (RIL) posted theproceedings of its Q2 FY 2022-23 earnings call on the metaverse. This is the firsttime in the history of corporate India that a company has used metaverseto engage with its stakeholders.

Concept note-3: Mukesh Ambani-ledRIL’s net profit surged from Rs 3,280 crore in FY2001-02 to Rs 67,845 crorein FY2021-22. Chairman and Managing Director of the textile-to-telecomconglomerate Reliance Industries, Mukesh Ambani has completed 20 years at thehelm.
