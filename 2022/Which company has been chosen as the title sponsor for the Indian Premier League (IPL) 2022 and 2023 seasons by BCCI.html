Which company has been chosen as the title sponsor for the Indian Premier League (IPL) 2022 and 2023 seasons by BCCI?

(A) ** Tata Group

(B) Amazon

(C) Vivo

(D) Paytm

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Tata Group has replaced Chinese mobile manufacturer Vivo asthe title sponsor of the Indian Premier League (IPL) for 2022 and 2023 seasons. Tatawill remain the main sponsor as Vivo still has two years in its IPL title sponsorshipcontract.

Concept note-2: Tata Group, the multinational conglomerate, replacedChinese phone maker Vivo as the title sponsor for the Indian Premier League for2022 and 2023.

Concept note-3: The Saudi Tourism Authority (STA) hasbecome one of the official partners of the Indian Premier League for the 2023season, replacing edtech company Unacademy, an ad industry executivesaid.

Concept note-4: Principal Sponsors: DP World, Exide and Jio.Associate Partners: Puma, MPL, PhonePe, boAT, Max Life Insurance andNippon Paint. Official Partners: Kingfisher, Myntra, socios.com, JGI, Milo,BKT Ture, Herbalife Nutrition, Kotak, Sosyo, iB Cricket, EUME, Fever10.4FM, MX TataTak and DNA Networks.

Concept note-5: The IndianPremier League (IPL), also known as TATA IPL for sponsorship reasons,is a men’s T20 franchise cricket league of India. It is annually contestedby ten teams based out of seven Indian cities and three Indian states. Theleague was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in2007.
