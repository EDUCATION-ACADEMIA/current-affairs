Which company has bought a stake worth USD 264 million in Flipkart from its co-founder Binny Bansal?

(A) Sony

(B) IBM

(C) Microsoft

(D) ** Tencent

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chinese technology conglomerate Tencent has bought stake worth USD 264 million (about Rs 2,060 crore) in Flipkart from its co-founder Binny Bansal through its European subsidiary, according to official documents.
