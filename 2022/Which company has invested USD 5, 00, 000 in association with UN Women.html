Which company has invested USD 5, 00, 000 in association with UN Women?

(A) Amazon

(B) Netflix

(C) Google

(D) ** LinkedIn

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: LinkedIn is investing USD 5,00,000 (Rs 3.88 crore) ina three-year regional partnership with UN Women, according to a pressstatement. The collaboration will digitally upskill women giving themgreater access to jobs and equipping them to fully participate in the formaleconomy.

Concept note-2: Professional network LinkedIn has announced that itwill be investing $500,000 (Rs 3.88 crore) in a three-year, regional partnership withUN Women – the United Nations entity dedicated to gender equality – to advancewomen’s economic empowerment.

Concept note-3: Q. Which company haspartnered with UN Women to invest USD 5,00,000 (Rs 3.88 crore) in the LINKWomen project to create job opportunities for women? Notes: LinkedInCorporation has invested USD 5,00,000 (? 3.88 crore) in association with UNWomen for the LINK Women project to create employment opportunities forwomen.

Concept note-4: From January to April 2022, UNIDO made significantcontributions to promoting a more conducive entrepreneurial eco-system for womenentrepreneuership in Egypt through the delivery of an institutional capacityenhancement programme.

Concept note-5: India has contributed USD 500,000 tothe UN Women, the United Nations agency for gender equality and womenempowerment for their core budget.
