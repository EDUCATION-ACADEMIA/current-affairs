Which company has partnered with UNESCO to promote and safeguard the ‘Intangible Cultural Heritage of India?

(A) Ashoka Leyland

(B) Tata Motors

(C) ** Royal Enfield

(D) Mahindra & Mahindra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Royal Enfield has partnered with UNESCO (the UnitedNations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organisation) to promote andsafeguard the ‘Intangible Cultural Heritage of India, beginning with theHimalayas.

Concept note-2: IHCL partners with UNESCO to safeguard theintangible cultural heritage in India | UNESCO.
