Which company has partnered with badminton players to promote digital payments in India?

(A) DigiCash

(B) ** MasterCard

(C) PhonePe

(D) Amazon Pay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Mastercard teams up with badminton stars to promote digitalpayments in India. Financial services company Mastercard has announced badmintonplayers Lakshya Sen, Kidambi Srikanth, Satwiksairaj Rankireddy and Chirag Shettyas brand ambassadors in India.

Concept note-2: Globally, Mastercard is deeplyinvested in sports from tennis to cricket to soccer to e-sports, with badminton nowadded to the list.

Concept note-3: The American multinational financial servicesfirm, Mastercard has announced a collaboration with Indian badminton players,Lakshya Sen, Kidambi Srikanth, Satwiksairaj Rankireddy and Chirag Shetty asbrand ambassadors in India.

Concept note-4: Similar to the key role played byits brand ambassador MS Dhoni, the badminton stars will be a part of its ‘TeamCashless India’ campaign, which works with merchants and cardholders to createawareness of the safety, security of digital payments.

Concept note-5: NikhilSahni, division president, South Asia, Mastercard, said, “The company is delightedto team up with Indian badminton stars Sen, Srikanth, Rankireddy andShetty, who serve as an inspiration to millions of sports enthusiasts in thecountry.”
