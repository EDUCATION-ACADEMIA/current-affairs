Which company has partnered with the MSDC and CBC for capacity building of civil servants of India?

(A) ** Microsoft

(B) Wipro

(C) HCL technology

(D) Google

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Microsoft has partnered with the Ministry of Skill Developmentand Entrepreneurship (MSDE) and the Capacity Building Commission (CBC) to equipIndian civil servants with skills for the future.

Concept note-2: Notes: Microsoftand the Indian government are set to collaborate for a programme to train 2.5 millioncivil servants in using Microsoft’s suite of digital tools.

Concept note-3: Thepartnership under the project- Capacity Building by MSDE in Microsoft DigitalProductivity Skills aims to enhance the functional computer literacy of nearly 2.5million civil servants of Government of India (GoI).

Concept note-4: Which ITcompany and the Indian government will collaborate on a programme to educate itsdigital toolkit? Explanation: Microsoft and the Indian government will worktogether on a programme to teach its digital toolkit to almost 2.5 million civilservants.
