Which company has received India’s first Micro Category Drone Certification?

(A) Throttle Aerospace Systems Pvt Ltd

(B) Shivayu Aerospace

(C) ** Asteria Aerospace Limited

(D) Aadyah Aerospace Pvt Ltd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Asteria Aerospace Limited on Thursday announced thatit has received India’s first micro category drone type certification for itsindigenously designed A200 drone from the Directorate General of CivilAviation (DGCA).

Concept note-2: India’s first micro category drone typecertification was received by Asteria Aerospace Limited for its indigenouslydesigned A200 drone from the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) onThursday.

Concept note-3: Drone manufacturer Garuda Aerospace Private Ltd.said it has received Type Certification and Remote Pilot Training Organisation(RPTO) approval from the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) for itsindigenously-designed Kisan drones.

Concept note-4: The Type certification wasintroduced by the government of India in August 2021 under Drone Rules and thehistoric dual DGCA approval validates the Chennai-based startup’s tag of beingIndia’s most valuable Drone startup.

Concept note-5: The DGCA certification isprovided on the basis of quality checks and is issued after a rigorous testing processof unmanned aerial vehicles in various NABL-accredited testing labs. This makesUAVs go through a series of material, environmental, and operational tests for safe,secure and reliable operations.
