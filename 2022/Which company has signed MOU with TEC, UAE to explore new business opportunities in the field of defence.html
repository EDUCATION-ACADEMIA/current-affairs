Which company has signed MOU with TEC, UAE to explore new business opportunities in the field of defence?

(A) ISRO

(B) DRDO

(C) ** Bharat Dynamics Ltd

(D) HAL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Explanation: Bharat Dynamicson Thursday announced that ithas signed a Memorandum of Understanding with Tawazun Economic Council(TEC), United Arab Emirates based company for exploring new businessopportunities in the field of defence.

Concept note-2: BDL is capable ofintegrating and supplying Anti - Submarine Warfare Suite (ASW Suite) equippedwith systems like Torpedoes, Torpedo Defence System including fire controlsystem.

Concept note-3: Headquartered in Hyderabad, Bharat DynamicsLimited (BDL), was incorporated on 16 July, 1970 as a Public Sector Undertakingunder the Ministry of Defence, Government of India to be the manufacturingbase for guided missile systems and allied equipment for the Indian ArmedForces.

Concept note-4: BDL was incorporated in JULY 1970 as a CentralPublic Sector Undertaking under the Ministry of Defence (Dept. of DefenceProduction and Supplies), with the primary objective to establish a production basefor guided missiles in India and to make her self-reliant in hi-tech weaponsystems.
