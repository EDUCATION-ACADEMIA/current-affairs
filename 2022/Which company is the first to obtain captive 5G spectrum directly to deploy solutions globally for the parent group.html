Which company is the first to obtain captive 5G spectrum directly to deploy solutions globally for the parent group?

(A) ** L & T Tech

(B) Wipro

(C) Infosys

(D) Tech Mahindra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: L&T Technology Services has become the first technologycompany to publicly express its interest in the government’s direct allocation ofspectrum for captive 5G networks.

Concept note-2: Notes: L&T Tech first toobtain captive 5G spectrum directly, deploy solutions globally for parent group. Tobuild use cases on the technology, it will obtain spectrum to set up a 5G non-publicnetwork.

Concept note-3: Airtel becomes the first company to launch 5Gservices: Names of cities, 5G pricing and other details. Bharti Airtel’s 5G service willbe available in select cities from Saturday (October 1), the company’s chairmanSunil Bharti Mittal said at the sixth edition of India Mobile Congress (IMC2022).

Concept note-4: Airtel is the first company to offer 5G to users in Indiaas the company’s Chairman Suni Mittal announced that 5G mobile servicesare being rolled out to select cities.

Concept note-5: South Korea isthe country which deployed the first 5G network and is expected to stay inthe lead as far as penetration of the technology goes, By 2025, almost 60percent of mobile subscriptions in South Korea are expected to be for 5Gnetworks.
