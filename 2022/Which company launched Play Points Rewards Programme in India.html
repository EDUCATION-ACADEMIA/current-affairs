Which company launched Play Points Rewards Programme in India?

(A) TCS

(B) Wipro

(C) ** Google

(D) Amazon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Google has launched ‘Google Play Points’ in India. Being rolledout later this week, Google Play Points is a global rewards program thathelps users earn points and rewards for the many ways they use GooglePlay.

Concept note-2: NEW DELHI: After expanding third-party billing to appdevelopers in India last month, Google has announced that it’s launching its globalrewards programme, called Play Points, in India.

Concept note-3: You start toearn 1 point for every ₹5 INR you spend with Google Play using Google Play’s billingsystem. For the first week after you join, you’ll receive a welcome offer of3x bonus points for purchases you make.

Concept note-4: Bronze Tier(0 to 149 points)One point per dollar spent. Weekly events for up to 4xmultiplier on in-game purchases.

Concept note-5: In September 2018,Google launched Google Play Points, a program that rewards Google PlayStore customers with points that can be put toward purchase and rentalcredit.
