Which countries are the members of the formed ‘I2U2 Grouping’?

(A) ** India, Israel, USA and UAE

(B) India, Iraq, USA and UAE

(C) India, Israel, USA and UK

(D) India, Iraq, USA and UK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The I2U2 Group is a grouping of India, Israel, the United ArabEmirates, and the United States.

Concept note-2: Joint Statement of theLeaders of India, Israel, United Arab Emirates, and the United States (I2U2) OnJuly 14, 2022, we, the Heads of Government of India, Israel, the UnitedArab Emirates (UAE), and the United States, convened for the first leaders’meeting of the “I2U2” Group.

Concept note-3: The I2U2 is a four-nationgrouping, where “I” stands for India and Israel, and “U” for the US and theUAE.

Concept note-4: I2U2 (India-Israel-UAE-USA) Grouping wasconceptualized during the meeting of the Foreign Ministers of the four countries heldon 18 October 2021. Each country also has Sherpa-level interactions regularly todiscuss the possible areas of cooperation.

Concept note-5: Known as the I2U2,the countries’ foreign ministers formed the bloc in the fall of 2021 to deepentechnological and private sector collaboration in the region and tackle transnationalchallenges in six focus areas: water, energy, transportation, space, health and foodsecurity.
