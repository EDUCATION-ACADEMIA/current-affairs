Which country awarded the ‘Meritorious Service Medal’ to Lamba, Former Navy Chief of India?

(A) Malaysia

(B) ** Singapore

(C) Cambodia

(D) Indonesia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: HE Madam Halimah Yacob, the President of Republic ofSingapore, conferred the prestigious Pingat Jasa Gemilang (Tentera) [MeritoriousService Medal (Military)] on Admiral Sunil Lanba (Retd), the former Chief of NavalStaff, Indian Navy.

Concept note-2: The Meritorious Service Medal (MSM) is aBritish medal awarded to sergeants and warrant officers of the British armed forcesfor long and meritorious service.

Concept note-3: Admiral Sunil Lanba, PVSM,AVSM, PJG, ADC (born 17 July 1957) is a retired Indian naval officer who served asthe 23rd Chief of the Naval Staff of the Indian Navy. He assumed the office on 31May 2016 after Admiral Robin K. Dhowan and demitted office three years later on 31May 2019.

Concept note-4: Associate Professor Kenneth Mak Seck Wai,Mr Pang Kin Keong and Mr Tan Chong Meng will receive the MeritoriousService Medal (COVID-19), the highest honour under the National Awards(COVID-19).

Concept note-5: Hari Kumar. The 25th Navy Chief, he took overfrom Karambir Singh, who retired on 30 November 2021 after four decades of servicefrom the navy.
