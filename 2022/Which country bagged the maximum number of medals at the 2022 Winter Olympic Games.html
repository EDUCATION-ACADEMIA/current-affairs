Which country bagged the maximum number of medals at the 2022 Winter Olympic Games?

(A) South Korea

(B) China

(C) Germany

(D) ** Norway

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Norway is the most successful nation of all time at the WinterOlympic Games, having amassed a total of 368 medals since the first WinterOlympics in 1924 – this tally includes 132 gold medals, 125 silver, and 111bronze.

Concept note-2: With all 109 medal events decided, competition at the2022 Winter Olympics in Beijing is officially complete. After three weeks ofdominance, Norway stands alone atop the chart with the most total medals (37) andthe most gold medals (16).
