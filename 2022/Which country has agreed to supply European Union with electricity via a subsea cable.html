Which country has agreed to supply European Union with electricity via a subsea cable?

(A) India

(B) Ukraine

(C) Poland

(D) ** Azerbaijan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The EU-Azerbaijan Partnership Priorities reflect thevalues and principles of the European Neighbourhood Policy, and include:strengthening institutions and good governance. economic development andmarket opportunities. connectivity, energy efficiency, environment and climateaction.

Concept note-2: Meanwhile Germany, the largest electricity producer inEurope, had an average per capita consumption of 6.7 megawatt hours in2021.

Concept note-3: Russia is the main EU supplier of crude oil, natural gasand solid fossil fuels. In 2020, almost three quarters of the extra-EU crude oil importscame from Russia (29 %), the United States (9 %), Norway (8 %), Saudi Arabia andthe United Kingdom (both 7 %) as well as Kazakhstan and Nigeria (both 6%).

Concept note-4: With more than half of energy from renewable sources in itsgross final consumption of energy, Sweden (62.6 %) had by far the highest shareamong the EU Member States in 2021, ahead of Finland (43.1 %) and Latvia (42.1%).
