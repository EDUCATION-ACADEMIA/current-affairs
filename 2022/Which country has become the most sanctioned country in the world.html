Which country has become the most sanctioned country in the world?

(A) Pakistan

(B) Iraq

(C) Iran

(D) ** Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The United States applies sanctions more frequently than anyother country and does so by a wide margin. According to American Studiesacademic Manu Karuka, the United States has imposed two-thirds of the world’ssanctions since the 1990s.

Concept note-2: Iran was the most sanctionedcountry in the world until it was surpassed by Russia following its invasion ofneighboring Ukraine in February 2022. The first sanctions were imposed by theUnited States in November 1979 after a group of radical students seized theAmerican Embassy in Tehran and took hostages.

Concept note-3: Sanctionsimposed by IndiaIndia was the first country to sanction South Africa for theapartheid. Following deterioration of diplomatic ties India imposed a tradeembargo.
