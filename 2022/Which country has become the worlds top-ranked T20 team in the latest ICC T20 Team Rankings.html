Which country has become the world’s top-ranked T20 team in the latest ICC T20 Team Rankings?

(A) Australia

(B) ** India

(C) South Africa

(D) New Zealand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India currently leads the ICC Men’s T20I Team Rankings as of 01 February 2023.

Concept note-2: -India have retained their number 1 spot in the International Cricket Council (ICC) T20I rankings despite a semifinal exit from the recently concluded T20 World Cup 2022.

Concept note-3: -According to the ICC Test Rankings in Hindi 2022, Australia is in first place with 2439 points on 128th ratings.

Concept note-4: -Leading T20 teams 2023 Currently at the top of the list for men’s T20 nations is India with a rating of 268.

Concept note-5: -ICC Test Ranking India with 115 rating is on the second spot. England with 107 rating occupies the third place. South Africa is on fourth, New Zealand on fifth, Pakistan on sixth, Sri Lanka on seventh, West Indies on eight , Bangladesh on ninth and Zimbabwe on the tenth place.
