Which country has been chosen as the “Country of Focus” in the 17th Mumbai International Film Festival?

(A) Nepal

(B) India

(C) China

(D) ** Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bangladesh. 17th edition of Mumbai International Film festival (MIFF) is being held from 29 May-4 June 2022 at Nehru Centre Auditorium, Worli, Mumbai.

Concept note-2: -In commemoration of Bangladesh’s 50 years of Independence the country has been chosen as the ‘Country of Focus’ this year.

Concept note-3: -Bangladesh was the country in focus at the 51st International Film Festival of India held in Goa in January 2021.

Concept note-4: -Notes: The 17th edition of the Mumbai International Film Festival for Documentary, Short Fiction and Animation Films (MIFF-2022) is being held from May 29 to June 4, 2022 at Films Division Complex, Mumbai.

Concept note-5: -#IFFIWood, 24 November 2022 This year at IFFI 53, France has been chosen as the festival’s Country of Focus, in reciprocity of India being named the Country of Honour at the Marche du Cinema of the Cannes Film Festival earlier this year on the occasion of the 75th anniversary of the country’s Independence.
