Which country has been chosen as the sponsor for International Children’s Book Day 2022?

(A) Germany

(B) Singapore

(C) United States

(D) ** Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: IBBY Canada was the sponsor for International Children’sBook Day 2022.

Concept note-2: The theme of 2022 ICBD is “Stories arewings that help you soar every day”. The author of the ICBD 2022 messagewas Richard Van Camp and the 2022 poster was designed by Julie Flett.IBBY Canada is the international sponsor of the celebration of the 2022ICBD.

Concept note-3: International Children’s Book Day (ICBD) is a yearlyevent sponsored by the International Board on Books for Young People (IBBY), aninternational non-profit organization. Founded in 1967, the day is observed on oraround Hans Christian Andersen’s birthday, April 2.

Concept note-4: In 2022,we are celebrating the 25th anniversary of World Book Day on 3 March – with themessage for all children ‘you are a reader’! It will be filled with lots of exciting,celebratory ways to promote reading for pleasure on World Book Day, and all-yearround.

Concept note-5: The John Newbery Medal was the first award forchildren’s book in the world. Awarded annually by the American LibraryAssociation (ALA), these prize winning books bear the stamp of recognition forthe most distinguished American children’s book published the previousyear.
