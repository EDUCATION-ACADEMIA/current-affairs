Which country has created a new Guinness world record by simultaneously waving 78, 220 national flags?

(A) Japan

(B) China

(C) ** India

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: On 23 April 2022, India achieved this feat by waving 78thousand 220 tricolours together in the Veer Kunwar Singh Vijayotsava program atDulour Maidan, Jagdishpur in Bhojpur district of Bihar.

Concept note-2: Indiahas entered the Guinness Book of World Records for waving the maximum number ofnational flags simultaneously. 78,220 tricolours were waved at the Veer Kunwar SinghVijayotsav programme organized at Duler Ground in Jagdishpur, Bihar in thepresence of Home Minister Amit Shah, in April 2022.

Concept note-3: Nepalcreates new Guinness World Record for organizing highest altitude FashionShow on earth. The Mount Everest Fashion Runway was held at 5340 meters(17515 feet) elevation at Kala Patthar, near Everest base camp on Sunday.18 super models from 10 different countries walked the ramp to create thehistory.

Concept note-4: Breaking the Guinness World Record, the ‘LargestHuman Image of a Waving National Flag’ was created by Chandigarh University andthe NID Foundation, a non-governmental organisation, on Saturday. A GuinnessWorld Records (GWR) official confirmed the record which was accomplished with theparticipation of 5,885 persons.

Concept note-5: 5,885 students form world‘slargest human Image of a waving national flag. Along with the 5,885 youngboys and girl students, Banwarilal Purohit, Meenakshi Lekhi, S. SatnamSingh Sandhu, Dharam Pal and other dignitaries were also a part of the flagformation.
