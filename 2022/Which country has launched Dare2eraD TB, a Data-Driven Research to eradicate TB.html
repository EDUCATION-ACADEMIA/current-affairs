Which country has launched “Dare2eraD TB”, a Data-Driven Research to eradicate TB?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) China

(C) Nepal

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Jitendra Singh announced the launch Dare2eraD-TB on Marchthe 24th. The initiative is data-driven research to eradicate TB by the Department ofBiotechnology, Government of India.

Concept note-2: Abhishek Mishra. Answer:Option D Explanation: “Dare2eraD TB” will be the umbrella TB programcomprising initiatives like the Indian Tuberculosis Genomic Surveillance Consortium,Indian TB Knowledge Hub- Webinar Series and Host Directed therapiesagainst TB and Evidence-based procedures for treating extra-pulmonaryTB.

Concept note-3: The National Tuberculosis Elimination Programme(NTEP), previously known as Revised National Tuberculosis Control Programme(RNTCP), aims to strategically reduce TB burden in India by 2025, five years aheadof the Sustainable Development Goals.

Concept note-4: On the WorldTuberculosis Day on Thursday, Health Minister Mansukh Mandaviya reaffirmed thegovernment’s commitment to making India tuberculosis–free by 2025 and said thiswill be achieved by ensuring access to quality healthcare and advanced treatment.“Tuberculosis is a preventable and treatable disease.

Concept note-5: Tribal TBinitiative, a unique partnership between the Ministry of Health and Family Welfareand Ministry of Tribal Affairs to improve the cascade of TB care and support servicesamong Tribal Populations in India. The technical assistance for this initiate will beprovided by USAID.
