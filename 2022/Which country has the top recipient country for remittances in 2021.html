Which country has the top recipient country for remittances in 2021?

(A) Indonesia

(B) ** India

(C) Mexico

(D) Egypt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: In 2021, India was the country with the largest inflow ofremittances worldwide. That year, Indians abroad sent the equivalent of 89.4 billionU.S. dollars in remittances. Mexico followed, with remittances amounting to atotal of 54.1 billion U.S. dollars.

Concept note-2: India will retain itsposition as the world’s top recipient of remittances. Data from the RBIRemittances Survey, 2021 shows that the four leading countries in sendingremittances to India are the US, the United Arab Emirates (UAE), theUK and Singapore.

Concept note-3: Over the last several years, manyIndians have moved to high-skilled and well paid jobs in high-income countries,such as the US, UK and Singapore. Notably in 2021, India had received$89.4 billion in remittances, according to the World Bank report, whichthen made India the top recipient globally.

Concept note-4: The USA isIndia’s biggest source of remittance accounting for over 20% of total inwardremittance.

Concept note-5: The top five recipient countries for remittances in2022 are expected to be India, establishing a benchmark of $100 billion in the year,followed by Mexico, with a tally of $60 billion (which replaced China in secondposition during 2021), and China, the Philippines, and the Arab Republic ofEgypt.
