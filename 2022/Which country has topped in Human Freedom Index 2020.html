Which country has topped in Human Freedom Index 2020?

(A) ** New Zealand

(B) Switzerland

(C) Sudan

(D) Hong Kong

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India is ahead of China and Bangladesh, which ranked 129 and139 on the 2020 index respectively. New Zealand, Switzerland and HongKong bagged the first three spots.

Concept note-2: The jurisdictionsthat took the top 10 places, in order, were New Zealand, Switzerland, HongKong, Denmark, Australia, Canada, Ireland, Estonia, and Germany andSweden (tied in 9th place).

Concept note-3: As per the Index, personalfreedom has decreased globally since 2008. In the Human Freedom Index for2020, India came in at number 111. India was rated 94th in the world in2019.

Concept note-4: According to the World Press Freedom Index, released inMay, India’s ranking dropped to 150th position from last year’s 142nd rank among180 nations.

Concept note-5: In the 2022 index, New Zealand is ranked mostfree overall, while North Korea is last. Hong Kong was ranked most free ineconomic liberty, while Norway was ranked most free in the social libertycategory.
