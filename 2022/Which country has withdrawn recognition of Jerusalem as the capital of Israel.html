Which country has withdrawn recognition of Jerusalem as the capital of Israel?

(A) Canada

(B) Japan

(C) ** Australia

(D) Afghanistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: However, Morrison also announced that Australia would notrelocate its embassy to West Jerusalem until after the final status of Jerusalem wasresolved. On 17 October 2022, Australia’s foreign minister confirmed itsreversal of the previous governments recognition of West Jerusalem as Israel’scapital.

Concept note-2: Today the Government has reaffirmed Australia’sprevious and longstanding position that Jerusalem is a final status issue that shouldbe resolved as part of any peace negotiations between Israel and the Palestinianpeople. This reverses the Morrison Government’s recognition of West Jerusalem asthe capital of Israel.

Concept note-3: Tel Aviv was the temporary governmentcenter of the State of Israel until the government moved to Jerusalem in December1949. Due to the international dispute over the status of Jerusalem, most embassiesremained in or near Tel Aviv.

Concept note-4: Drawing from the deep well ofJewish connection to Jerusalem and the centrality of the city to the life ofthe newly established State of Israel, it was only natural that the Knesset,Israel’s parliament, declared Jerusalem as the capital of Israel on 23 January1950.
