Which country inaugurated the Ijmuiden Sea Lock, the world’s largest sea lock?

(A) Australia

(B) ** Netherlands

(C) USA

(D) UAE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The IJmuiden sea lock(Dutch: zeesluis IJmuiden) is the secondlargest lock in the world. The lock, situated in the municipality of Velsen, wasopened on 26 January 2022 in the presence of King Willem-Alexander of theNetherlands.

Concept note-2: Dutch cities are famous for their canals,particularly Amsterdam, which has 165 canals. A wide variety of the city’s old canalsare listed on the UNESCO world heritage list. But the city of Ijmuiden recentlybecame home to the world’s largest canal lock.

Concept note-3: TheKieldrecht Lock (Dutch: Kieldrechtsluis), referred to as the Deurgank Dock Lock(Dutch: Deurgankdoksluis) during construction, is the largest lock in theworld.

Concept note-4: The world’s largest canal lock has been inaugurated atIjmuiden, a small port city, in the Port of Amsterdam, The Netherlands. The sea lockwas inaugurated by Dutch King Willen-Alexander. The Ijmuiden sea lock is500-meter (1,640-feet) long and 70-meter wide.
