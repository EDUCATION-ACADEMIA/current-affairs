Which country is hosting Badminton Asia Team Championships 2022?

(A) Japan

(B) Indonesia

(C) India

(D) ** Malaysia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2022 Badminton Asia Team Championships (also known as the 2022 Selangor Badminton Asia Team Championship) was held at the Setia City Convention Centre in Shah Alam, Selangor, Malaysia, from 15 to 20 February 2022.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 Badminton Asia Championships was a badminton tournament held at the Muntinlupa Sports Complex, Muntinlupa in the Philippines from 26 April to 1 May 2022.

Concept note-3: -Sri Lanka won the Asia Cup 2022 title.

Concept note-4: -The season-ending Badminton World Tour Finals tournament was shifted out of Guangzhou to Bangkok due to the COVID-19 situation in the Chinese city last December.
