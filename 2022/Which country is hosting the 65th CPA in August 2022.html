Which country is hosting the 65th CPA in August 2022?

(A) ** Canada

(B) France

(C) Maldives

(D) Thailand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: This document contains the Conference Concluding Statementfor the 65th Commonwealth Parliamentary Conference (CPC) which took place inHalifax, Nova Scotia, Canada from 20 to 26 August 2022, hosted by the CPA CanadaRegion.

Concept note-2: This opens in a new window. One of the largest annualgatherings of Commonwealth Parliamentarians will take place at the 65thCommonwealth Parliamentary Conference (CPC) hosted by the CPA Canada Regionin Halifax, Canada from August 20 to 26, 2022.

Concept note-3: Ahead ofthe 65th Commonwealth Parliamentary Conference (CPC) hosted by theCPA Canada Region in Halifax, Nova Scotia from 20 to 26 August 2022, theCPA have published a supplementary magazine to the latest issue of TheParliamentarian.

Concept note-4: The next Commonwealth ParliamentaryConference will be held in 2023 in Ghana.

Concept note-5: The 65thCommonwealth Parliamentary Conference (CPC), will be held in Halifax, Canada,from August 22 to 26, 2022.
