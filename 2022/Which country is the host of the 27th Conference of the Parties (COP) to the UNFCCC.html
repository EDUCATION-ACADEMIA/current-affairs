Which country is the host of the 27th Conference of the Parties (COP) to the UNFCCC?

(A) ** Egypt

(B) UAE

(C) Australia

(D) Brazil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Background. In November 2022, the Government of the ArabRepublic of Egypt will host the 27th Conference of the Parties of the UNFCCC(COP 27), with a view to building on previous successes and paving theway for future ambition to effectively tackle the global challenge of climatechange.

Concept note-2: During COP26 (Conference of the Parties), the UNFramework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC) named UAE thesecond Middle Eastern nation to host the UN Climate Change Conference.The 2023 event will be held at Expo City Dubai from 30th November to12th December 2023.

COP15 wasconcerned with biodiversity while COP27 – short for the 27th Conference of theParties of the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change –focused on solving the issue of climate change.
