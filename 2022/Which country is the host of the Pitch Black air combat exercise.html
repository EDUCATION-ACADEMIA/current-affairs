Which country is the host of the ‘Pitch Black’ air combat exercise?

(A) Japan

(B) ** Australia

(C) France

(D) USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Exercise Pitch Black is a biennial warfare exercise hosted bythe Royal Australian Air Force (RAAF). The exercise is normally held in NorthernAustralia, primarily at RAAF Bases Darwin and Tindal.

Concept note-2: ExercisePitch Black 22 was hosted by the Royal Australian Air Force at its Darwin Air Base.Spanning over a duration of three weeks, the exercise saw the participation of 17 AirForces and over 2500 military personnel.

Concept note-3: What is Pitch Black?Exercise Pitch Black 2022 is the latest iteration of Exercise Pitch Black, a war gameor warfare exercise that first started in Australia’s Northern Territories in the 1980sbetween different RAAF units.

Concept note-4: Participating nations includedCanada, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Japan, Malaysia, Netherlands, NewZealand, Philippines, Republic of Korea, Singapore, Thailand, UAE, UK and theUSA.

Concept note-5: Exercise Austra Hind will be a yearly event that will beconducted alternatively in India and Australia,” said a press statement from theIndian Army.
