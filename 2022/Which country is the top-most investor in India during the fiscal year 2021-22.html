Which country is the top-most investor in India during the fiscal year 2021-22?

(A) ** Singapore

(B) UAE

(C) USA

(D) Netherlands

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Singapore (27.01%) and USA (17.94%) have emergedas top 2 sourcing nations in FDI equity flows into India in FY2021-22followed by Mauritius (15.98%), Netherland (7.86%) and Switzerland(7.31%).

Concept note-2: Sector-wise FDI Equity Inflows duringApril-September 2022During the first half of this fiscal, Singapore emerged as the topinvestor. It was followed by Mauritius, the U.A.E., the U.S.A., the Netherlandsand Japan.

Concept note-3: Singapore is ranked one FDI provider toIndia. The small island nation has invested $15.9 billion in FY22 whichis accounted for 27 per cent of India’s total FDI received. USA is India’ssecond biggest investor with FDI of $10.5 billion, with 18 per cent of totalFDI.

Concept note-4: FDI equity inflow in manufacturing sectors hasincreased by 76 per cent in 2021-22. Singapore was the topmost investorin India last fiscal year, accounting for 27% of the FDI, followed by theUS at 18% and Mauritius at 16%.

Concept note-5: The year 2021-22recorded the highest ever FDI at $83.6 billion. This FDI has come from101 countries, and invested across 31 UTs and States and 57 sectors in thecountry.
