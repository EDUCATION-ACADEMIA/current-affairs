Which country is to ban Russians from entry with Schengen tourist visas from September 30?

(A) Sweden

(B) USA

(C) UAE

(D) ** Finland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Schengen visa. The European Union does not apply a generalban on the issuance of Schengen visas to Russian nationals. Furthermore,the vast majority of European Union Member States have not closed theirborders to Russian travellers and continue to issue visas, including for tourismpurposes.

Concept note-2: Poland, Lithuania, Latvia, Estonia and Finlandannounced in September that they would bar entry to Russians holding SchengenArea tourist visas, with exemptions for those requiring humanitarian assistance orvisiting family.

Concept note-3: The list of the EU countries that have bannedRussians and have decided not to issue Schengen visas for them includes Estonia,Latvia, Lithuania, Poland, the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Belgium, and theNetherlands, among others, SchengenVisaInfo.com reports.

Concept note-4: HowDo Russians Get To Europe? In order to travel to the EU, Russians need to apply fora short-stay visa.
