Which country ranked 1st in the Passport Index 2022 published by Arton Capital?

(A) ** UAE

(B) USA

(C) UK

(D) Ukraine

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: According to Arton Capital’s “Passport Index 2022,” India isplaced 66(as of 16th December 2022) in the world’s strongest passport list, whilethe United Arab Emirates (UAE) is ranked 1st in the passport ranking for2022.

Concept note-2: After taking all of that into account, the United ArabEmirates passport is currently the most powerful passport in the world, with visa-freeor visa-upon-arrival access to 181 destinations.

Concept note-3: India ranked 87in the world’s strongest passport list, while UAE ranked first in the ratingof passports for 2022.

Concept note-4: At the top of Arton’s powerranking, holders of a United Arab Emirates passport can travel visa-free orobtain visas on arrival in 181 countries and territories. U.S. passport holdersrank a bit lower, with access to 173 countries.

Concept note-5: Whatare the world’s most powerful passports? Japan has sneaked back ahead ofSingapore after the two countries jointly held the title in 2022. Japan now grantseasy access to 193 destinations, compared to Singapore’s 192. Singapore isjoined by South Korea in joint second with Germany and Spain in jointfourth.
