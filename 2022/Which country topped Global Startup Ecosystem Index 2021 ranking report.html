Which country topped Global Startup Ecosystem Index 2021 ranking report?

(A) China

(B) ** The United States

(C) Israel

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is United States. India has jumped three spots up in the global start-up ranking for 2021. India stands at the 20th spot among the top 100 countries that have been ranked in the Global Startup Ecosystem Index 2021 by Startup Blink.

Concept note-2: -Silicon Valley is #1, followed by New York City and London tied at #2, Boston at #4, and Beijing at #5. Seoul entered the global top 10 ecosystems for the first time, up six places from #16 in 2021 and #20 in 2020.

Concept note-3: -India ranks globally 3rd in Start-Up ecosystem and also in terms of number of Unicorns. As per the latest data, there are currently 105 unicorns, out of which 44 were born in 2021 and 19 in 2022.

Concept note-4: -According to “Startup Blink Global Ecosystem Report 2022,” the US maintains its status as the highest ranked country. The USA remains in first place on the list for three years in a row, 2020, 2021, and 2022. The US accumulated a total score gap almost four times that of the second-ranked country, the UK.
