Which country will hold the presidency of the G20 from 1st December 2022 to 30th November 2023?

(A) Australia

(B) Mexico

(C) ** India

(D) Argentina

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India holds the Presidency of the G20 from December 1, 2022to November 30, 2023. The 43 Heads of Delegations- the largest ever inG20-will be participating in the final New Delhi Summit in September nextyear.

Concept note-2: The 2023 G20 Delhi summit (Hindi: 2023 G20 ) is theupcoming eighteenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), a summit scheduled totake place in Pragati Maidan, New Delhi in 2023. India’s presidency beganon 1 December 2022, leading up to the summit in the fourth quarter of2023.

Concept note-3: The Indian G20 Presidency’s theme of ‘One Earth, OneFamily, One Future’ sets the tone for the 2023 Infrastructure Agenda under the 2023Indian G-20 Presidency.

Concept note-4: India intends to play an active role,representing the ‘Global South’ in negotiations for a multilateral Global HealthPlatform. Chandigarh: The Union Territory is all set to host one of the two G20meetings, which will be held on January 30th-31st. The second set of meetings will beheld in March.
