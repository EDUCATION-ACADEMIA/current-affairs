[SOURCES]

Which country’s central bank has launched a five-year plan for financial standardisation’?

(A) Japan

(B) USA

(C) India

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: BEIJING - China’s financial regulators have issued a plan toadvance standardization of the financial sector over the 14th Five-Year Plan period(2021-2025).

Concept note-2: Notes: China’s four government agencies includingits central bank published a five-year plan for financial standardisation The plan is tobe implemented during the 2021-2025 period.

Concept note-3: In the FifthFive-Year Plan, for the first time, the financial sector was included as an integral partof the Plan.

Concept note-4: China’s regulators issued the “14th Five-YearPlan for Financial Standardization.” The plan sets quantitative targets onthe number of financial standards issued by 2025 in a broad set of areas,including fintech, green finance, securities and futures, and internationalfinance.
