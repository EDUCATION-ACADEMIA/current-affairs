Which country’s first private launchpad has been set up by AgniKul Cosmos at Sriharikota?

(A) Canada

(B) Indonesia

(C) ** India

(D) Brazil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Chennai headquartered space-tech start-up Agnikul, has set upIndia’s first-ever launchpad that will be operated by a private player, atSriharikota. The facility was inaugurated by S. Somanath, chairman, IndianSpace Research Organisation (ISRO) and Secretary, Department of Space,recently.

Concept note-2: India’s first launchpad and mission controlcentre designed and operated by a private player has been inaugurated at theSatish Dhawan Space Center in Sriharikota, space startup Agnikul Cosmosannounced on Monday.

Concept note-3: The Centre has two launch padsfrom where the rocket launching operations of PSLV and GSLV are carriedout.

Concept note-4: Geographical location: Close To EquatorSriharikota ispositioned close to the equator, which is favourable for launches to geostationaryorbit. As it can take advantage of the Earth’s rotational motion, launchinga satellite near the equator helps it to achieve geostationary orbit morequickly.

Concept note-5: New Delhi: India’s first launchpad and mission controlcentre designed and operated by a private player has been inaugurated at the SatishDhawan Space Center in Sriharikota, space startup Agnikul Cosmos announced onMonday.
