Which country’s start-up ‘KHEYTI’ received the prestigious Earthshot award-2022?

(A) Australia

(B) Angola

(C) ** India

(D) Bahamas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: NEW YORK: Indian startup Kheyti, which providesground-breaking yet simple farming solutions, has been named as one of the fivewinners of this year’s prestigious Earthshot Prize, an initiative of Prince William thatrewards each winner with 1 million pounds.

Concept note-2: Takachar won the£1 million prize for its cheap technology innovation that converts crop residues intosellable bio-products in the “clean our air” category.

Concept note-3: TheEarthshot Prize is awarded to five winners each year for their contributions toenvironmentalism. It was first awarded in 2021 and is planned to run annually until2030.

Concept note-4: The deadline for submitting nominations to theGrantham Research Institute at LSE for The Earthshot Prize 2023 has nowpassed.
