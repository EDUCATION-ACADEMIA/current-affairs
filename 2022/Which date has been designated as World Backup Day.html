Which date has been designated as World Backup Day?

(A) March 29

(B) ** March 31

(C) March 30

(D) March 28

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Every year on March 31, many companies tweet and have podcasts about the importance of backing up data to prevent data loss. On the website WorldBackupDay.com people can make a pledge in 10 languages on various social media channels about the importance of backing up their data.

Concept note-2: -Pat Doherty, Chief Revenue Officer for Flexential, “In 2022, investment in Disaster Recovery-as-a-Service (DRaaS) will be a major theme for businesses of all sizes to ensure long-term business success and survival -no matter the disruption.” March 31 is World Backup Day.

Concept note-3: -World Backup Day celebrated annually on March 31, is about - the importance of data protection. An expert provides the best practices to prevent data loss Millions of laptops and smartphones are lost every year due to accidents or malice.

Concept note-4: -We support the initiative of World Backup Day, especially once you consider some of its stats. Reportedly, 30% of you have never backed up anything, one in every ten computers is infected with a virus each month, and 113 phones are lost or stolen every minute.
