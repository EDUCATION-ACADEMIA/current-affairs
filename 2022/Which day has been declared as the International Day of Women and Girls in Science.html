Which day has been declared as the International Day of Women and Girls in Science?

(A) February 12

(B) February 09

(C) ** February 11

(D) February 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Royal Academy of Science International Trust (RASIT)celebrates the 7th International Day of Women and Girls in Science Assembly, takingplace on 11 February 2022 at UN Headquarters in New York in a virtualformat.

Concept note-2: The International Day of Women and Girls in Sciencewas adopted by the United Nations General Assembly in order to promote full andequal access and participation for women and girls in science. 11 February is anopportunity to celebrate the essential role that women and girls play in science andtechnology.

Concept note-3: In order to achieve full and equal access to andparticipation in science for women and girls, and further achieve gender equality andthe empowerment of women and girls, the UN General Assembly adopted resolutionA/RES/70/212 declaring 11 February as the International Day of Women and Girlsin Science.

Concept note-4: In alignment with the United Nations SustainableDevelopment Goal 6, to ensure access to water and sanitation for all, this year’stheme is “Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion: Water Unites Us”. The Day,on February 11th, aims to amplify the critical role that women and girlscan and do play in science and technology.

Concept note-5: Use thehashtag #WomenInScience with messages that defy gender stereotypes andspread the word on the need to include more women and girls in STEMfields!
