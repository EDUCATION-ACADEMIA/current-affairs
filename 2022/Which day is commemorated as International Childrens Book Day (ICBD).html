Which day is commemorated as International Children’s Book Day (ICBD)?

(A) April 03

(B) April 01

(C) ** April 02

(D) March 31

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Since 1967, on or around Hans Christian Andersen’s birthday,2 April, International Children’s Book Day (ICBD) is celebrated to inspire a love ofreading and to call attention to children’s books.

Concept note-2: InternationalChildren’s Book Day (ICBD) is a yearly event sponsored by the International Boardon Books for Young People (IBBY), an international non-profit organization.Founded in 1967, the day is observed on or around Hans Christian Andersen’sbirthday, April 2.

Concept note-3: The theme of 2022 ICBD is “Stories arewings that help you soar every day”. The author of the ICBD 2022 messagewas Richard Van Camp and the 2022 poster was designed by Julie Flett.IBBY Canada is the international sponsor of the celebration of the 2022ICBD.

Concept note-4: Activities around the world on this day include libraryscavenger hunts, writing competitions, announcements of book awards, and eventswith authors of children’s literature.
