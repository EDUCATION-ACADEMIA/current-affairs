Which day is marked as World Hindi Day globally every year?

(A) January 09

(B) ** January 10

(C) January 06

(D) January 08

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: NEW DELHI: Every year on January 10, World Hindi Day,also known as Vishwa Hindi Diwas, is commemorated. Vishwa Hindi Diwaswas formed to mark the day when Hindi was first spoken in the UNGA in1949.

Concept note-2: World Hindi Day is celebrated on January 10th everyyear, whereas, Hindi Diwas is celebrated on September 14th every year to mark theadoption of Hindi written in the Devanagari script as the official language ofIndia.

Concept note-3: World Hindi Day 2023: World Hindi Day, alsoknown as Vishwa Hindi Diwas, is observed on January 10 every year. The dayaims to create awareness as well as promote the Hindi language across theworld.

Concept note-4: The day was first celebrated in 2006 by the Indiangovernment to mark the adoption of Hindi, written in the Devanagari script, as oneof the official languages of India, along with English, by the Constituent Assembly onJanuary 14th, 1950.

Concept note-5: The date of January 10 was chosen asHindi was spoken for the first time in the United Nations General Assembly onJanuary 10, 1949.
