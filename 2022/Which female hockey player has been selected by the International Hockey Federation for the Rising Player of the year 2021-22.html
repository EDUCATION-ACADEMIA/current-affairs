Which female hockey player has been selected by the International Hockey Federation for the Rising Player of the year 2021-22?

(A) Vandana Rampal

(B) Vandana Katariya

(C) ** Mumtaz Khan

(D) Rani Rampal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Netherlands’ Felice Albers and India’s HarmanpreetSinghhave been named the International Hockey Federation (FIH) Player ofthe Year for the 2021-2022 season in the women’s and men’s competitionsrespectively.

Concept note-2: Mumtaz Khan was India’s top scorer in the 2022women’s junior hockey World Cup and at the FIH Hockey5s Lausanne. TeenagerMumtaz Khan was named the FIH Rising Star of the Year 2021-22 in thewomen’s category, the international hockey federation (FIH) announced onTuesday.

Concept note-3: Indian hockey team star Harmanpreet Singhhas been named the men’s FIH Player of the Year 2021-22 becoming justthe fourth player to win the award in the men’s category in consecutiveyears.

Concept note-4: At 22 years of age, Felice Albers is the youngest winnerof the Player of the Year award since 1999, when Germany’s Natascha Kellerwon the title.
