Which festival in Goa, do people wear crowns of fresh fruit and wildflowers, traditionally called ‘Kopel’ to celebrate the occasion?

(A) Goa Sunburn Festival

(B) ** Sao Joao

(C) Samvatsar Padvo

(D) Grape Escapade

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The festivities also include revellers wearing the Kopel, thecrown of fruits, flowers and leaves, parading in villages and often well hopping fromone place to another as they chant, “Sao Joao!

Concept note-2: The feast of St.John the Baptist or São João is celebrated every year on 24th June. It’s a feastwith the added excitement of jumping into wells and other water bodies forfun.

Concept note-3: The people of Goa has celebrated the Sao Joao festival, thefeast of St John the Baptist with traditional enthusiasm after a two-yearcoronavirus-induced gap. The festival is dedicated to Saint John the Baptist, whobaptised Lord Jesus on the river of Jordan and is celebrated at the onset ofmonsoon.

Concept note-4: Goa Carnival is the most popular festivalof Goa. The colourful streets and floats resonate the joy of people in thecarnival.

Concept note-5: Detailed Solution. During the Sao Joao festivities,Goans present fruits to each other. The Sao Joao festival is celebrated in Goa to paytribute to St. John the Baptist.
