Which film has won the Best Film award in the International Indian Film Academy Awards held in Abu Dhabi?

(A) ** Shershaah

(B) 83

(C) Mimi

(D) Sardar Udham

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: This year the IIFA 2022 Awards were hosted by Salman Khan,Maniesh Paul and Riteish Deshmukh. The film Shershah has won awards in themaximum five categories this year.

Concept note-2: The correct answer isVicky Kaushal. The 22nd International Indian Film Academy Awards, alsoknown as the IIFA, was held on 3–4 June 2022.

Concept note-3: TheIIFA Award for Best Actor recognizes leading male actor who has deliveredan outstanding performance in a leading role. The recipient is chosen byviewers and the winner is announced at the ceremony. With 4 awards each,Hrithik Roshan and Shahrukh Khan share the record for most wins in thiscategory.
