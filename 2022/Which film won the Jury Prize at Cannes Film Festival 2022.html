Which film won the Jury Prize at Cannes Film Festival 2022?

(A) Stars at Noon

(B) ** Joyland

(C) Close

(D) Triangle of Sadness

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pakistani film ‘Joyland’ which is written and directed by debutant, Saim Sadiq, won the Jury prize in the Un Certain Regard section of the 75th Cannes Film Festival on May 28. It became the first ever film from the subcontinent to bag the award.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 Cannes Film Festival concluded on a high note for India. Film-maker Shaunak Sen’s ‘All That Breathes’ won the prestigious - 2022 L’Oeil d’Or (Golden Eye) for the best documentary at the 75th Cannes Film Festival.

Concept note-3: -The 75th edition of the Cannes Film Festival takes place from May 17 to 28; Here’s all the information you need.

Concept note-4: -At the 1st Cannes Film Festival held in 1946, Ray Milland was the first winner of this award for his performance in The Lost Weekend, and Song Kang-ho is the most recent winner in this category for his role in Broker at the 75th Cannes Film Festival in 2022.
