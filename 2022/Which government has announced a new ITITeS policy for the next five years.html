Which government has announced a new IT/ITeS policy for the next five years?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Maharashtra

(D) ** Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Gujarat government announces new IT/ITeS policy, aims togenerate 1 lakh employment in next five years. Gujarat government has announced anew IT/ITeS policy for the next five years.

Concept note-2: The new GujaratIT/ITeS policy was unveiled on 8 February 2022 at GIFT City, Gandhinagar byChief Minister Bhupendra Patel. The objective of the policy is to strengthen theInformation technology ecosystem of Gujarat and to generate 1 lakh direct jobs inthe IT/ITeS sector.

Concept note-3: Overview. The Government of Maharashtraannounced its 1st LoI / Registrations to IT/ITES Parks and Units Policyin 1998.

Concept note-4: MOC,Shri Ashwini Vaishnaw. Minister forCommunications, Electronics & Information Technology and Railways, Governmentof India.
