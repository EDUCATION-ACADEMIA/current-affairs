Which group has launched a plan named ‘Global Shield’ at the 27th UN Climate Change Conference?

(A) ** G7

(B) G20

(C) NATO

(D) QUAD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Detailed Solution: The G7 nations have launched a plan named‘Globa Shield’ at the 27th UN Climate Change Conference.

Concept note-2: Lossand damage protection V20The Vulnerable Twenty Group and G7Group of 7, sevenmajor industrial countries jointly launch Global Shield against Climate Risks atCOP27.

Concept note-3: At the Sharm el-Sheikh Climate Change Conference(COP 27), countries came together to take action towards achieving theworld’s collective climate goals as agreed under the Paris Agreement and theConvention. The conference took place from 6-20 November 2022 in Sharmel-Sheikh, Egypt.

Concept note-4: The final decision text, known as theSharm el-Sheikh Implementation Plan was published on 20 November afternegotiations overran the conference. The text reaffirms the commitment tolimit global temperature rise to 1.5 degrees Celsius above pre-industriallevels.
