Which institution announced the Infrastructure Resilience Accelerator Fund (IRAF)?

(A) ISA

(B) ** CDRI

(C) WEF

(D) BIMSTEC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: CDRI announces Infrastructure Resilience Accelerator Fund atCOP27. New Delhi: The Coalition for Disaster Resilient Infrastructure (CDRI) onWednesday announced Infrastructure Resilience Accelerator Fund (IRAF), aCDRI Multi-Partner Trust Fund, at the India Pavilion, COP27, Sharm ElSheikh, Egypt.

Concept note-2: Q. Which institution announced theInfrastructure Resilience Accelerator Fund (IRAF)? Notes: The Coalition for DisasterResilient Infrastructure (CDRI) announced Infrastructure Resilience AcceleratorFund (IRAF), a CDRI Multi-Partner Trust Fund.

Concept note-3: Asyou all know, IRIS is a flagship strategic initiative that has been designedas an instrument to achieve and deliver resilience and climate adaptationsolutions to the SIDS, which are among the most vulnerable and exposedcountries.

Concept note-4: Amit Prothi, Director General, CDRI, said, “Webelieve that IRAF will enable CDRI to achieve its mandate of resilience throughrisk-informed investments and infrastructure development, resulting in reducedvulnerability of populations and reduced impact of extreme events and disasters oninfrastructure systems."

Concept note-5: Resilient infrastructure means adequateengineering design built to robust metrics to withstand storms and other naturaldisasters of a predetermined magnitude. In addition, the designs should be simplisticyet complex enough to allow modifications to be made over time as necessary ascurrent conditions change.
