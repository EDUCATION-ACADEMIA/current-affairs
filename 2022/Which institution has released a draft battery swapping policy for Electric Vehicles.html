Which institution has released a draft battery swapping policy for Electric Vehicles?

(A) ** NITI Aayog

(B) Ministry of New and Renewable Energy

(C) Bureau of Energy Efficiency

(D) Ministry of Power

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UPSC Mains. Q. Which institution has released a draft batteryswapping policy for Electric Vehicles? Notes: Government think-tank NitiAayog has prepared a draft battery swapping policy, to support the adoptionof battery-swapping, mainly in electric scooters and three-wheeler electricrickshaw.

Concept note-2: The government’s think tank Niti Aayog has releaseda draft policy for implementing battery swapping stations in an effort to encouragebuyers to opt for electric vehicles.

Concept note-3: The correct answer is NitiAayog. Niti Aayog has released a draft battery swapping policy. It is aimed atcatalyzing large-scale adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) by making themmore accessible, affordable, and efficient.

Concept note-4: Sun MobilityA50:50 joint venture between Maini Group and SUN Group, and the mostprominent player in India’s battery swapping space for electric 3Ws ande-buses.
