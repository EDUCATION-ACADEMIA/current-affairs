Which institution issued a working paper on the decline of India’s rankings on global indices?

(A) ** Economic Advisory Council to the Prime Minister

(B) NITI Aayog

(C) Department of Economic Affairs

(D) Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Economic Advisory Council to the Prime Minister,EAC-PM has issued a working paper that says that the decline in India’s rankings ona number of global opinion-based indices are due to the “serious problems” with themethodology used in these perception-based indices.

Concept note-2: India isplaced in the category of “Flawed Democracy” and its rank deteriorated sharply from27 in 2014 to 53 in 2020 and then improved a bit to 46 in 2021. The decline in rankhas been on account of decline in scores primarily in the categories- Civil Libertiesand Political Culture.

Concept note-3: India’s Rank in Various Index 2022PDFIndia is ranked 40th in the index. India was ranked 46th in the GlobalInnovation Index for 2021, a leap of 6 positions.

Concept note-4: 2022 India’srank: Out of 180 countries, India is at the least 5 with a grade of 18.9. The countryhas also performed poorly on various measures such as control of corruption, the ruleof law, and government effectiveness.

Concept note-5: The correct answer is theWorld Bank.
