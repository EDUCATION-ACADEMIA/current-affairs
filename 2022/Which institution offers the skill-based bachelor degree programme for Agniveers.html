Which institution offers the skill-based bachelor degree programme for ‘Agniveers’?

(A) AICTE

(B) IIT Madras

(C) Jawaharlal Nehru University

(D) ** IGNOU

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UPSC Mains. Q. Which institution offers the skill basedbachelor degree programme for ‘Agniveers’? Notes: The Ministry of Education(MoE)announced the launch of a special three-year skill based bachelor degree programmefor ‘Agniveers’.

Concept note-2: In a bid to help the Agniveers – thebeneficiaries under the Union Government’s Agnipath scheme – build a good careerand with the prime motive of making them job-ready, the Indira GandhiNational Open University (IGNOU) will launch a skill-based bachelor’s degreeprogramme.

Concept note-3: The Indira Gandhi National Open University willlaunch a skill-based bachelor’s degree program for the “Agnipath” Schemebeneficiaries called Agniveers. This program will be BA-level degree courses forthe enhancement of their skills and related knowledge to serve the defenseforces.

Concept note-4: The scheme envisages recruitment of youth aged between17.5 and 21 years as soldiers in the armed forces for a period of four years.Class 10 or Class 12 is the minimum eligibility criterion, according to a pressstatement issued by the Education Ministry Thursday.

Concept note-5: TheAgnipath scheme will provide a special 3 year skill-based bachelor degreeprogramme for serving defence personnel, in order to improve the Agniveers’future career prospects and equip them for various job roles in the civiliansector.
