Which institution released the Adaptation Gap Report 2022?

(A) NITI Aayog

(B) ** UNEP

(C) WEF

(D) UNFCCC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) willlaunch the 2022 edition of the Adaptation Gap Report in a virtual pressconference in the lead up to the 2022 United Nations Climate Change Conference(COP27).

Concept note-2: Alexandre Magnan contributed to 2 chapters of the2022 issue of the United Nations Environment Programme’s Adaptation GapReport.

Concept note-3: According to the United Nations EnvironmentProgramme’s (UNEP) Adaptation Gap Report, 2022, global efforts in adaptationplanning, financing and implementation are not enough to prepare vulnerablecommunities around the world to adapt to the rising risks from the impacts ofclimate change.

Concept note-4: The global mean temperature in 2022 iscurrently estimated to be about 1.15 [1.02 to 1.28] °C above the 1850-1900pre-industrial average. A rare triple-dip cooling La Niña means that 2022 is likely to“only” be fifth or sixth warmest.

Concept note-5: It was established in 2015 tomeet the cost of adaptation to climate change for the State and Union Territoriesof India that are particularly vulnerable to the adverse effects of climatechange.
