Which institution released the ‘Living Planet Report 2022’?

(A) FAO

(B) ** World Wide Fund

(C) UNEP

(D) UNESCO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Living Planet Report, WWF’s flagship publicationreleased every two years, is a comprehensive study of trends in global biodiversityand the health of the planet.

Concept note-2: The World Wildlife Fund forNature (WWF) published the Living Planet Report in collaboration with theZoological Society of London (ZSL.) It is a biennial report (published every twoyears.) ZSL manages the living planet index (LPI) that too is released along with thereport.

Concept note-3: The 2022 global Living Planet Index shows an average69% decrease in monitored wildlife populations between 1970 and 2018.Latin America shows the greatest regional decline in average populationabundance (94%). Population trends for monitored freshwater species are alsofalling steeply (83%).

Concept note-4: Published: Thursday 13 October2022There has been a 69 per cent decline in the wildlife populations of mammals,birds, amphibians, reptiles and fish, across the globe in the last 50 years,according to the latest Living Planet Report by World Wide Fund for Nature(WWF).

Concept note-5: The Living Planet Index (LPI) is an indicator of thestate of global biological diversity, based on trends in vertebrate populations ofspecies from around the world. The Zoological Society of London (ZSL)manages the index in cooperation with the World Wide Fund for Nature(WWF).
