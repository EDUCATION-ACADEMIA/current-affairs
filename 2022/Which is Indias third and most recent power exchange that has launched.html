Which is India’s third and most recent power exchange that has launched?

(A) Bharat Power Exchange

(B) PTC Power Exchange

(C) ** Hindustan Power Exchange

(D) Amrit Power Exchange

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The country’s third power exchange -Hindustan PowerExchange Ltd. (HPX)– promoted by PTC India Ltd, Bombay Stock Exchange andICICI Bank commenced operations on Wednesday upon receiving approval fromCentral Electricity Regulatory Commission.

Concept note-2: Hindustan PowerExchange (HPX), formerly known as Pranurja Solutions Ltd, is a recentlylaunched electricity exchange in India. It has commenced the operations from6th July 2022.

Concept note-3: New Delhi: India got its third powerexchange - Hindustan Power Exchange Ltd (HPX) - running on Wednesdayupon getting approval from Central Electricity Regulatory Commission. Theexchange is promoted by PTC India Ltd, Bombay Stock Exchange and ICICIBank.
