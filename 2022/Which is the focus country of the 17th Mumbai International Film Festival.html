Which is the focus country of the 17th Mumbai International Film Festival?

(A) Nepal

(B) ** Bangladesh

(C) Bhutan

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Bangladesh was the ‘Country of Focus’ this year incommemoration of its 50 years of Independence and 11 films from the country werepresented during MIFF 2022.

Concept note-2: Detailed SolutionThe correctanswer is Bangladesh. 17th edition of Mumbai International Film festival (MIFF) isbeing held from 29 May-4 June 2022 at Nehru Centre Auditorium, Worli,Mumbai.

Concept note-3: Mumbai, 29 May 2022In commemoration ofBangladesh’s 50 years of Independence the country has been chosen as the‘Country of Focus’ this year. A special package of 11 films from Bangladeshincluding the critically acclaimed film ‘Hasina- A Daughter’s Tale’ will bepresented at MIFF 2022.

Concept note-4: Mumbai (Maharashtra) [India],June 7 (ANI/BusinessWire India): Organized by the Films Division and theMinistry of Information and Broadcasting of the Government of India, thecurtains fell on the 17th Edition of the week-long Mumbai International FilmFestival (MIFF) at a gala ceremony.

Concept note-5: Bangladesh was thecountry in focus at the 51st International Film Festival of India held inGoa in January 2021. 51st International Film Festival of India (IFF) hasannounced a line-up of programs that will be showcased on the Over-the-top(OTT) platform. The festival was held in Goa from 16th to 24th January2021.
