Which is the headquarters of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA)?

(A) Geneva

(B) New York

(C) Moscow

(D) ** Vienna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The IAEA Secretariat is headquartered at the ViennaInternational Centre in Vienna, Austria. Operational liaison and regionaloffices are located in Geneva, Switzerland, New York, USA, Toronto, Canadaand Tokyo, Japan.

Concept note-2: The International Atomic EnergyAgency (IAEA) serves as the world’s foremost intergovernmental forumfor scientific and technical cooperation in the peaceful use of nuclearenergy.

Concept note-3: The International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) is theinternational center for nuclear cooperation, safeguards, and peaceful nucleartechnology.

Concept note-4: The IAEA is the world’s centre for cooperation inthe nuclear field and seeks to promote the safe, secure and peaceful use of nucleartechnologies.

Concept note-5: Director GeneralMr Grossi is a diplomatwith more than 35 years of experience in the fields of non-proliferation anddisarmament. In 2013, he was appointed Ambassador of Argentina to Austria andArgentine Representative to the IAEA and other Vienna-based InternationalOrganizations.
