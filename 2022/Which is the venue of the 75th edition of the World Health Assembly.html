Which is the venue of the 75th edition of the World Health Assembly?

(A) Rome

(B) New York

(C) ** Geneva

(D) Paris

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Seventy-fifth World Health Assembly is being held inGeneva, Switzerland, on 22-28 May 2022. It is the first in-person Health Assemblysince the start of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Concept note-2: The WHA takesplace each May at the United Nations in Geneva, Switzerland, and is attendedby delegations from all 194 Member States.

Concept note-3: The 75thWorld Health Assembly is being organised in Geneva, Switzerland from22 to 28 May 2022. This is the first individual health gathering since thestart of the COVID-19 pandemic. On behalf of India, the Union Minister ofHealth and Family Welfare, Dr. Mansukh Mandaviya addressed this 75thsession.

Concept note-4: First World Health Assembly, Geneva 24 June to 24July 1948: plenary meetings: verbatim records: main committees: summary ofresolutions and decisions.

Concept note-5: The World Health Assembly is thedecision-making body of WHO. It is attended by delegations from all WHO MemberStates and focuses on a specific health agenda prepared by the ExecutiveBoard.
