Which is the venue of the ‘SCO Defence Ministers’ Meeting’ held in 2022?

(A) Nursultan

(B) ** Tashkent

(C) Shanghai

(D) Beijing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Raksha MantriShri Rajnath Singh is visiting Tashkent,Uzbekistan from 23rd to 25th August, 2022 to attend the Annual Meeting of theDefence Ministers’ of Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) MemberStates.

Concept note-2: Defence Minister Rajnath Singh will attend theShanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Defence Ministers’ Meeting, which is setto take place on 24 August 2022 in Tashkent, Uzbekistan.

Concept note-3: TheSCO summit was held two years later in Samarkand, Uzbekistan on 15 and 16September. The next meeting of the SCO Council of Heads of State will be held in2023 in India.

Concept note-4: India invites Pakistan, China to 2023 SCOsummit to be held in Goa.
