Which ministry has launched the ‘Temple 360’ website?

(A) Ministry of Rural Development

(B) ** Ministry of Culture

(C) Ministry of Defense

(D) Ministry of Finance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Minister of State for Culture& External Affairs, Smt.Meenakshi Lekhi launched the website ‘Temple 360’ in an event organized byMinistry of Culture at IGNCA Ampitheatre, Indira Gandhi National Centre of Arts,New Delhi under the aegis of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav.

Concept note-2: Aboutthe Temple 360:Temple 360 is a digital platform where anyone can visit or dodarshan of 12 Jyotirlinga and Char Dham from any location, making everyone’s lifeconvenient while also keeping people connected.

Concept note-3: Tamil Nadu isrightfully known as “The Land of Temples in India".

Concept note-4: TheSrirangam Temple is often listed as the largest functioning Hindu templein the world. The temple, located in Tamil Nadu, occupies an area of 156acres (631,000 m²) with a perimeter of 4,116m (10,710 feet), making it thelargest temple in India and one of the largest religious complexes in theworld.

Concept note-5: The Utsav Portal website, a digital initiative launched bythe Ministry of Tourism, aims to showcase all the events, festivals and live darshansacross India to promote different regions of the country as popular touristdestinations worldwide.
