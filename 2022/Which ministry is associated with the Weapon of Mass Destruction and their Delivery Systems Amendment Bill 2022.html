Which ministry is associated with the Weapon of Mass Destruction and their Delivery Systems Amendment Bill 2022?

(A) Ministry of Home Affairs

(B) ** Ministry of External Affairs

(C) Ministry of Tribal Affairs

(D) Ministry of Defence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Weapons of Mass Destruction and their Delivery Systems(Prohibition of Unlawful Activities) Amendment Bill, 2022 amends a 17-yr-old law,enacted in 2005. Introducing the Bill, the External Affairs minister said it is in ournational interest.

Concept note-2: [6th August, 2022.] An Act to amend theWeapons of Mass Destruction and their Delivery Systems (Prohibition ofUnlawful Activities) Act, 2005. 1. (1) This Act may be called the Weaponsof Mass Destruction and their Delivery Systems (Prohibition of UnlawfulActivities) Amendment Act, 2022.

Concept note-3: Notes: ‘Weapons of MassDestruction and their Delivery Systems (Prohibition of Unlawful Activities)Amendment Bill, 2022’ was introduced by the External Affairs Ministerin Lok Sabha. The Bill, which was passed by the Lok Sabha amends theoriginal act of 2005.

Concept note-4: Weapons of mass destruction arebiological, chemical or nuclear weapons. The Bill bars persons from financing anyprohibited activity related to weapons of mass destruction and their deliverysystems.

Concept note-5: The Central Universities (Amendment) Bill, 2022 wasintroduced in Lok Sabha on August 1, 2022. The Bill amends the Central UniversitiesAct, 2009, which provides for establishing central universities in variousstates.
