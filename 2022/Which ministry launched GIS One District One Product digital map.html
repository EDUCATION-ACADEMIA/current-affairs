Which ministry launched GIS One District One Product digital map?

(A) Ministry of Corporate Affairs

(B) Ministry of Rural Development

(C) ** Food Processing Ministry

(D) Ministry of Textiles

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: By The Ministry of Food Processing. The digital ODOP mapprovides detailed information about ODOP products to all states and facilitates thestakeholders. The digital map also has indicators for tribal, SC, ST, and aspirationaldistricts.

Concept note-2: Central Government has initiated One District OneProduct (ODOP) in all States/UTs of the country, as a transformational steptowards realizing the true potential of a district, fueling economic growth, generatingemployment and rural entrepreneurship, taking us to the goal of AatmanirbharBharat.

Concept note-3: It was launched in January 2018, by the UttarPradesh Government, and due to its success, was later adopted by the CentralGovernment.

Concept note-4: The scheme adopts the One District One Product(ODOP) approach to reap the benefit of scale in terms of procurement of inputs,availing common services and marketing of products. ODOP for the scheme willprovide the framework for value chain development and alignment of supportinfrastructure.
