Which nation has launched the “Ziyuan-1 02E” or “five-meter optical satellite 02”?

(A) Mongolia

(B) South Korea

(C) ** China

(D) Indonesia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Recently, China has launched a new satellite with a cameracapable of taking photos of the ground with a resolution of five meters called“Ziyuan-1 02E” or “Five Meter 02 Optical Satellite”.

Concept note-2: China on26 Dec’21 launched a new satellite with a camera that can take pictures of theground with a resolution of 5 m. The satellite, called “Ziyuan-1 02E” or “five-meteroptical satellite 02,” was launched by a Long March-4C rocket from north China’sShanxi province.

Concept note-3: China’s missions in spaceLaunch of Ziyuan-102E satellite marks China’s 53rd space launch.

Concept note-4: The missioncarried eight satellites developed by commercial remote sensing firm ChangguangSatellite Technology, designated Jilin-1 Gaofen 03D 44-50 remote sensing satelliteand the Jilin-1 Pingtai 01A01 satellites.
