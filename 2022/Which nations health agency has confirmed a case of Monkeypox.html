Which nation’s health agency has confirmed a case of Monkeypox?

(A) ** UK

(B) South Africa

(C) India

(D) USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UKHSA has confirmed that an individual has been diagnosedwith monkeypox linked to recent travel to West Africa.

Concept note-2: Updateson EU/EEA countries, the Western Balkans and TürkiyeThe five countries reportingmost cases since the start of the outbreak are: Spain (7 514), France (4 114), Germany (3676), the Netherlands (1 260), and Italy (959).

Concept note-3: Monkeypoxendemic countries are: Benin, Cameroon, the Central African Republic, theDemocratic Republic of the Congo, Gabon, Ghana (identified in animals only),Ivory Coast, Liberia, Nigeria, the Republic of the Congo, Sierra Leone, andSouth Sudan.

Concept note-4: On July 23, World Health OrganizationDirector-General Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus declared monkeypox a publichealth emergency of international concern and recommended governmentstake certain steps to strengthen and coordinate the global response to theoutbreak.
