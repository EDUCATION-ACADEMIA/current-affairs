Which of the following Indian classical dance is associated with the late Milena Salvini of France?

(A) ** Kathakali

(B) Sattriya

(C) Odissi

(D) Bharatanatyam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Milena Salvini (23 April 1933 – 25 January 2022) was anItalian-born French exponent and teacher of Indian classical dance. Especially knownfor her services to Kathakali, in 2019 she was awarded India’s fourth highest civilianaward, the Padma Shri.

Concept note-2: The correct answer is KathakaliDancer. Noted Kathakali dancer Milena Salvini of France passed away in January2022. She was conferred with Padma Shri in 2019 by the Government of India for hercontribution to the field of performing arts. She was an Italian-born French exponentand teacher of Indian classical dance.

Concept note-3: Kathak (NorthernIndia)Kathak is the principal dance of northern India, and is widely practised inUttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Delhi, Madhya Pradesh, and even parts of western andeastern India today.

Concept note-4: Bharatanatyam is the oldest classicaldance tradition in India. Bharatanatyam is the state dance form of TamilNadu.
