Which of the following countries has sent the military to Kazakhstan?

(A) USA

(B) France

(C) ** Russia

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: It is bounded on the northwest and north by Russia,on the east by China, and on the south by Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, theAral Sea, and Turkmenistan; the Caspian Sea bounds Kazakhstan to thesouthwest.

Concept note-2: At the request of Tokayev government, Russiaparticipated in the CSTO Peacekeeping Force effort to quell the anti-governmentprotests on 6 January 2022. The Russian forces included units of the Airborne Troopsand the air transport of the Russian Aerospace Forces.

Concept note-3: Russianswere able to seize Kazakh territory because the khanates were preoccupied byKalmyk invaders of Mongol origin, who in the late sixteenth century had begun tomove into Kazakh territory from the east.

Concept note-4: DestinationKazakhstan, a republic in Central Asia, south of Russia, that extends east from theCaspian Sea to the Altai Mountains and China in west. Until 1991 Kazakhstan wasone of the fifteen Soviet republics.
