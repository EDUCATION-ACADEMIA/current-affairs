Which of the following edition of the Army day is being commemorated?

(A) 72

(B) 75

(C) ** 74

(D) 78

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 15 January 2023, India celebrated its 75th Indian Army Day in Bengaluru. Army Day marks a day to salute the valiant soldiers who sacrificed their lives to protect the country and its citizens.

Concept note-2: -In 2022, the Indian Army’s theme for the event was “In Stride with the Future”. It was seen as an acknowledgement of the “increasingly critical role played by niche and disruptive technologies in modern warfare”.

Concept note-3: -On 15 January 1949, Field Marshal Kodandera M. Cariappa, who was then a Lieutenant General, took over as the first Indian Commander-in-Chief of the Indian Army from General Sir Francis Butcher, the last British person to hold that post. This day is celebrated as Army Day in India annually.

Concept note-4: -Republic Day Parade 2023: Indian Army to showcase ‘Made in India’ weaponry
