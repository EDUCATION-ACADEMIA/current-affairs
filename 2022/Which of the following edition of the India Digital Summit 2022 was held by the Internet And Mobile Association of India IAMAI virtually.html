Which of the following edition of the India Digital Summit 2022 was held by the Internet And Mobile Association of India (IAMAI) virtually?

(A) ** 16

(B) 15

(C) 20

(D) 19

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abhishek Mishra. Answer: [A] Internet and Mobile Association of India (IAMAI) Notes: The 16th edition of India Digital Summit 2022 was organised by the Internet and Mobile Association of India (IAMAI). The report, titled ‘Creating 10 million Digitally Enabled Micro-entrepreneurs’ was also released during the summit.

Concept note-2: -South India Digital Summit 2022 to be hosted by Fourth Dimension on November 4th in Coimbatore.

Concept note-3: -Digital start-ups will drive our economy: Investments, solutions, employment, enablement, and empowerment will come from them! Technology and data-related policies should primarily focus on building a robust data and digital economy by keeping Indian start-ups as their primary focus.

Concept note-4: -Union Minister of Commerce & Industry, Piyush Goyal virtually addressed the 16th India Digital Summit, 2022. The two-day virtual event was organised on January 11 and 12, 2022 by the Internet And Mobile Association of India (IAMAI). The theme of the summit was “Supercharging Startups”.

Concept note-5: -Digital Summit 2022 The aim is to accelerate and promote digitisation processes and to exploit their potential to develop prosperity, freedom, social participation, and sustainability. In this context, the Digital Summit remains the central platform for shaping the digital transformation with all parties involved.
