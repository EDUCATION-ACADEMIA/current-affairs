Which of the following has agreed to provide USD 4.5 billion loan for Bangladesh to combat the economic crisis?

(A) AIIB

(B) Asian Development Bank

(C) ** IMF

(D) New Development Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: IMFagrees $4.5 billion loan for Bangladesh to combateconomic crisis.

Concept note-2: DHAKA – Bangladesh received a doseof economic relief this week as the International Monetary Fund’s boardapproved a $4.7 billion loan program, but experts warn that the South Asiancountry’s problems will not be solved so easily.

Concept note-3: TheIMF has readily agreed to extend to Bangladesh the asked-for loan worthUS$4.5 billion. According to the IMF, the credit will be used to preservethe country’s ‘macroeconomic stability and support strong, inclusive, andgreen growth’. Protecting the vulnerable is also part of the objective of theIMF loan.

Concept note-4: The IMF provides financial assistance andworks with governments to ensure responsible spending. The IMF offersvarious types of loans that are tailored to countries’ different needs andspecific circumstances. Loans to low-income countries carry a zero interestrate.
