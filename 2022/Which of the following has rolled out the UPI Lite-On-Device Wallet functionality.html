Which of the following has rolled out the “UPI Lite-On-Device Wallet” functionality?

(A) ** National Payments Corporation of India

(B) NITI Aayog

(C) Reserve Bank of India

(D) Ministry of Finance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: In September last year, Reserve Bank of India governorShaktikanta Das launched UPI Lite to make low-value UPI payments fasterand simpler. UPI Lite is an on-device wallet feature that will allow users tomake real-time small-value payments of up to Rs 200 without using a UPIPIN.

Concept note-2: Explanation: National Payments Corporation of India hasrolled out the “UPI Lite - On-Device Wallet” functionality for UPI users for smallamounts transactions.

Concept note-3: Founded in 2010, Paytm is anotherleading digital wallets and upi apps in India. Paytm app facilitates users a bankto bank transfer via BHIM UPI and also enables them to instantly pay atonline & offline stores.

Concept note-4: Maximum transaction limit andWallet BalanceA UPI Lite transaction can have a maximum value of Rs200. The total UPI Lite balance limit for an on-device wallet will be Rs2,000.

Concept note-5: What is UPI Lite? The Reserve Bank of India (RBI)launched UPI Lite last week. It is an ‘on-device wallet’ feature that will allow usersto make real-time small-value payments without using a UPI PIN. First, you have toadd funds to or load money into the wallet on the app, from your bankaccount.
