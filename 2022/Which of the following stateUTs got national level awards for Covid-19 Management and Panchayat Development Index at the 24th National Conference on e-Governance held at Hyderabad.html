Which of the following state/UTs got national level awards for Covid-19 Management and Panchayat Development Index at the 24th National Conference on e-Governance held at Hyderabad?

(A) ** Jammu and Kashmir

(B) Gujarat

(C) Chandigarh

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: JAMMU: In a significant achievement, the Union Territory ofJammu and Kashmir got national level awards for Covid-19 Management andPanchayat Development Index at the 24th National Conference on e-Governance heldat Hyderabad today.

Concept note-2: MUMBAI: A team of researchers at IITBombay was recently awarded the 25th National E-Governance Gold Award 2022 inJammu and Kashmir, by union minister of state for science and technologyJitendra Singh.

Concept note-3: Award was presented by Hon’ble UnionMinster Dr Jitendra Singh on 7th Jan 2022 during 2 days 24th NationalConference on eGovernance held at Hyderabad International Convention Centre,Hyderabad.

Concept note-4: The theme of this Conference is “India’s Techade:Digital Governance in a Post Pandemic World”.
