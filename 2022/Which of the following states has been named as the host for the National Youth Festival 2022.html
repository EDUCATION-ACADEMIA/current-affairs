Which of the following states has been named as the host for the National Youth Festival 2022?

(A) Jammu & Kashmir

(B) Kerala

(C) ** Puducherry

(D) Chhattisgarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the 25thNational Youth Festival on 12th January 2022 in Puducherry. As part ofthe festival, a National Youth Summit will be organized on January 13,2022.

Concept note-2: Speaking at the occasion, Shri Anurag Thakur said thatPuducherry as a venue for the National Youth Festival 2022 was handpicked by thePrime Minister , Shri Narendra Modi.

Concept note-3: Key Highlights: The 26thNational Youth Festival is being organized at Hubballi- Dharwad, Karnataka byUnion Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports in collaboration with Government ofKarnataka from 12th-16th January. Theme of the festival this year is ‘Viksit YuvaViksit Bharat’.

Concept note-4: The Festival is being held in Hubballi-Dharwadin Karnataka from 12th to 16th January, with the theme - Viksit Yuva - ViksitBharat.
