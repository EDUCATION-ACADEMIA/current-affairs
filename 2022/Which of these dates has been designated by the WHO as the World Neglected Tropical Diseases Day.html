Which of these dates has been designated by the WHO as the World Neglected Tropical Diseases Day?

(A) ** January 30

(B) January 31

(C) January 29

(D) January 28

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: This decision formalized 30 January as a day to create betterawareness on the devastating impact of NTDs on the poorest populations around theworld.

Concept note-2: World Neglected Tropical Diseases Day (WorldNTD Day) is observed on January 30 every year.

Concept note-3: WorldNeglected Tropical Diseases Day is observed every year on 30 January.This date marks the launch of the first WHO road map and the launchof the London declaration on neglected tropical diseases (NTDs), both in2012.

Concept note-4: Cysticercosis. Dengue Fever. Dracunculiasis (GuineaWorm Disease) Echinococcosis.

Concept note-5: Neglected tropical disease day isobserved every year on 30th January. It was declared in the 74th World HealthAssembly (2021).
