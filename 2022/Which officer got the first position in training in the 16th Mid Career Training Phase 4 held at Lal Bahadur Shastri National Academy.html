Which officer got the first position in training in the 16th Mid Career Training Phase 4 held at Lal Bahadur Shastri National Academy?

(A) Aman Gupta

(B) Alok Ranjan

(C) Amanbir Singh Bains

(D) ** Dr. Manish Ranjan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Explanation: Manish Ranjan, an Indian Administrative Serviceofficer of Jharkhand 2002 cadre, got the first position all over India in the 16thCareer Training Career Phase 4 held at Lal Bahadur Shastri AdministrativeAcademy Mussoorie, and got a gold medal for it.

Concept note-2: However,the training for the Indian Administrative Service (IAS) Officers is held atLBSNAA, and it involves Bharat Darshan (a tour of India) and Districttraining.

Concept note-3: The total duration of the IAS trainingperiod is of 2 years. It includes a four-month long foundation course, IAStraining Phase-I, District Training, IAS training Phase-II, and AssistantSecretary-ship.

Concept note-4: Posting at State Secretariats at Director/JointSecretary level. Deputation to the Central Government can be opted forduring any stage of service after serving for a few years. Principal/AdditionalSecretary in the State Secretariat/Central Secretariat. Chief Secretary of astate.
