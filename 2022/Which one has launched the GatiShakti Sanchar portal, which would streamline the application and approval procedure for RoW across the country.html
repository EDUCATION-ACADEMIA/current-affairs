Which one has launched the GatiShakti Sanchar portal, which would streamline the application and approval procedure for RoW across the country?

(A) NITI Aayog

(B) TRAI

(C) Department of Investment & Public asset

(D) ** Department of Telecommunications

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Department of Telecommunication Launches “GatiShakti Sanchar”Portal for Centralised Right of Way (RoW) approvals.

Concept note-2: UPSCMains. Q. Which Union Ministry launched the ‘GatiShakti Sanchar portal’? Notes:Ministry of Electronics and IT launched the ‘GatiShakti Sanchar’ portal, which aimsto accelerate approvals for laying of fibre and tower installations, ahead ofthe 5G roll-out in the country.

Concept note-3: “GatiShakti SancharPortal” is a collaborative institutional mechanism between all stakeholdersincluding Central and State/UT Government(s), Local bodies, and ServiceProviders to facilitate the Right of Way (RoW) Application Process througha single interface.
