Which one has signed the bancassurance pact with Howrah-based-Paschim Banga Gramin Bank?

(A) ICICI Prudential Life

(B) ** SBI Life

(C) LIC

(D) HDFC Life

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Answer: Option B Explanation: SBI Lifesigns bancassurancepact with Paschim Banga Gramin Bank.

Concept note-2: Answer: Option DExplanation: A bancassurance agreement has been reached between the PaschimBanga Gramin Bank and SBI Life Insurance.

Concept note-3: Detailed Solution.Indian Bank signed a bancassurance agreement with Max Bupa to provide MaxBupa’s suite of health insurance products to its customers.

Concept note-4: Thisbank is sponsored by UCO Bank and owned by the government of India, thegovernment of West Bengal and UCO Bank. The shareholders of the bank are theGovernment of India (50%), UCO Bank (35%) and the Government of WestBengal (15%). The bank’s head office is located at Tikiapara, Howrah, WestBengal.

Concept note-5: PBGB profilePaschim Banga Gramin Bank is one of theleading Regional Rural Banks formed by amalgamation of three erstwhile GraminBanks viz. Howrah Gramin Bank, Bardhaman Gramin Bank and MayurakshiGramin Bank as per Govt of India Notification No: 1/4/2006-RRB dated 26.02.2007.
