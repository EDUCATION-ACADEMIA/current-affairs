Which organisation unfurled the Indian Flag 30 kilometres above the Earth on Independence Day 2022?

(A) Periyar Science and Technology Centre

(B) Space Zone India

(C) Spectra Academy

(D) ** Space Kidz India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: That’s right! To mark the momentous occasion, Space KidzIndia – an organisation creating “Young scientists” for the country, made itpossible to unfurl the Indian flag and sent it to an altitude of 1,06,000 feetabove the planet on a balloon that unfurled it.

Concept note-2: ThePresident of India unfurls the Indian flag on Republic Day (January 26)at Kartavya Path (formerly called Rajpath) in New Delhi. Whereas, thePrime Minister of India hoists the Indian flag on Independence Day (August15) at the Red Fort in New Delhi every year.

Concept note-3: Everyyear on Independence Day, the tricolour is hoisted by the Prime Minister ofIndia at the Red Fort. The national flag is tied at the lower part of theflagpole and it is raised by the Prime Minister.

Concept note-4: Thecorrect answer is Bhikaji Cama. Bhikaji Cama was one of the pioneers ofIndia’s struggle for independence. On August 21, 1907, an InternationalSocialist Conference was being held in Stuttgart, a city in Germany. On thisoccasion, Bhikaji Rustam Cama unfurled the first version of the Indian NationalFlag.

Concept note-5: Every year, the Prime Minister hoists the NationalFlag at Delhi’s Red Fort and addresses the nation, which is followed bya military parade. Q. The Andaman and Nicobar Islands administrationhas celebrated the 74th anniversary of First Flag Hoisting Day on whichdate?
