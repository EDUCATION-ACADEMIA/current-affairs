Which organization has approved a USD 47 million financial support to the Government of India for the Mission Karmayogi, a national program to build civil service capacity?

(A) Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank

(B) New Development Bank

(C) ** World bank

(D) Asian Development Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is World Bank. The World Bank hasapproved a USD 47 million project to support the Government of India’sMission Karmayogi. Mission Karmayogi is a national program to build civilservice capacity.

Concept note-2: In September 2020, Government of Indiahas launched Mission Karmayogi a National Programme for Civil ServicesCapacity Building (NPCSCP). The programme aims at building a future-readycivil service with the right attitude, skills and knowledge, aligned to thevision of New India.

Concept note-3: It is an Integrated GovernmentOnline Training digital platform under the Ministry of Human Resources andDevelopment (MHRD).

Concept note-4: Mission Karmayogi was launchedon September 20, 2020 by the Prime Minister Narendra Modi-led UnionCabinet.
