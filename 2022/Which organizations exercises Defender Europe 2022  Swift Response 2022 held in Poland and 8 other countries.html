Which organization’s exercises Defender Europe 2022 & Swift Response 2022 held in Poland and 8 other countries?

(A) South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation

(B) Association of Southeast Asian Nations

(C) ** North Atlantic Treaty Organization

(D) Shanghai Cooperation Organization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DEFENDER-Europe 22 is a multinational, regular joint and combined exercise organized by the United States Armed Forces to build preparedness and interoperability between Allies and partners of the USA and NATO.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: North Atlantic Treaty Organisation (NATO) exercise, Defender Europe 2022 (DE22) and Swift Response 2022 (SR22) began aimed at building preparedness and interoperability between Allies and partners of the United States (US) and NATO.

Concept note-3: -More than 30,000 soldiers from NATO and partner nations took part in exercise Cold Response in Norway in the winter of 2022.

Concept note-4: -Air forces from across NATO will exercise nuclear deterrence capabilities involving dozens of aircraft over north-western Europe starting on Monday (17 October 2022). The exercise, which runs until 30 October, is a routine, recurring training activity and it is not linked to any current world events.
