Which pharma company has completed trials for its intranasal Covid vaccine candidate?

(A) Biocon

(B) Serum Institute

(C) ** Bharat Biotech

(D) Dr Reddy’s

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: India grants approval for Bharat Biotech’s intranasal Covid-19vaccine. The vaccine for intranasal delivery via nasal drops was assessed in Phase I, IIand III clinical trials with successful data.

Concept note-2: The iNCOVACC isan adenoviral vector-based SARS CoV-2 vaccine for nasal administration only and itsvaccination course consists of two separate doses of 0.5 ml of 8 drops with 4 dropsindicated for each nostril.

Concept note-3: iNCOVACC - Intranasal Vaccine ForCovid-19 | Bharat Biotech.

Concept note-4: It’s a public unlisted company andis classified as’company limited by shares’.
