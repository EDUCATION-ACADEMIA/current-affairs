Which player has been chosen by the ICC as the ICC Men’s Player of the Month for July 2022?

(A) Rohit Sharma

(B) Ramesh Mendis

(C) Virat Kohli

(D) ** Prabath Jayasuriya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Sri Lankanspinner Prabath Jayasuriya’s sensationalstart to international cricket has been capped off by winning the ICC Men’sPlayer of the Month award for July 2022.

Concept note-2: July 2022 -Prabath Jayasuriya (Sri Lanka)Sri Lanka’s newest spin sensation PrabathJayasuriya was crowned ahead of rival contenders England’s Jonny Bairstow -who won the Men’s award back in June - and French youngster GustavMcKeon.

Concept note-3: On 11 January, the International Cricket Council(ICC) announced the ICC Player of the Month Awards for December 2022,with England’s rising star batsman Harry Brook and Australia’s AshleyGardner winning the ICC Men’s and Women’s Player of the Month awardsrespectively.

Concept note-4: The International Cricket Council (ICC) todayannounced the winners of the ICC Player of the Month awards for June 2022 asEngland’s all-conquering Jonny Bairstow and South Africa’s batting force MarizanneKapp.
