Which renowned Footballer has announced retirement from the game?

(A) Sergio Busquets Burgos

(B) Jordi Alma Ramos

(C) ** Gerard Pique

(D) Marcos Alonso

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Belgiumcaptain Eden Hazardhas announced his retirementfrom international football after Belgium’s early exit from the FIFA World Cup2022.

Concept note-2: Once retired, they then find a role at a club as a coach ormanager. It might be expected that the better the level of football a player hasplayed at, the better the coach/manager they will be.

Concept note-3: Theaverage retirement age of a football player is 35-years-old. Taking 35 as a rough ageand working backwards and forwards from that depending on the positionthe player plays in and the level they play to is, therefore, a good startingblock.

Concept note-4: Notably, the most common reason for the retirementof football players are injuries. Severe injuries unfortunately interruptedthe careers of many players. One of the most serious injuries of footballplayers, in addition to a broken leg or arm, is an injury to the anterior cruciateligaments.
