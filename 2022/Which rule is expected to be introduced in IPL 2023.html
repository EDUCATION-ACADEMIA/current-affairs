Which rule is expected to be introduced in IPL 2023?

(A) Set off Rule

(B) Effect Play Rule

(C) ** Impact Player Rule

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The ‘Impact Player Rule’ allows a team to have four suchplayers named in a squad of 15 before the match and they can be substitute any timeduring a team’s innings by the 14th over. However, that player has to be an Indian incase the playing XI has all four overseas players.

Concept note-2: IPL 2023RetentionIPL 2023 auction will be held in December tentatively and the IPLgoverning council has finalized the retention rules for the franchises. As per the newIPL 2023 auction retention rules, each franchise will be allowed to retain a maximumof three players and two Right to Match or RTM cards.

Concept note-3: IPL2023 auction rulesThere won’t be a Right to Match (RTM) card option in theIPL auction, unlike prior years. Chennai Super Kings – Rs. 20.45 crore; 9slots.

Concept note-4: The IPL 2023 will be streamed live on the Jio CinemaApp as Viacom18-Reliance has picked up the digital broadcasting rights of the IPLfrom the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI).

Concept note-5: Whereto Watch IPL 2023 in India? Star Sports Network will be broadcasting all the IPL2023 matches on their TV channels in the country. As for online live streaming, JioPlatforms subsidiary Viacom18 has required digital rights. Jio has announced that itwill stream IPL 2023 free on Jio Cinema App.
