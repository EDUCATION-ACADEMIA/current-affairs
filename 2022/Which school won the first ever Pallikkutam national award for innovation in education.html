Which school won the first ever Pallikkutam national award for innovation in education?

(A) St. Cecilia

(B) St. Xaviers

(C) ** St. Christopher School

(D) KVS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Tuensang, August 24 (MExN): The first-ever PallikkutamNational Award for Innovation in Education, instituted by Rajagiri Media,has been won by St Christopher School, Chessore village under Tuensangdistrict.

Concept note-2: Bharat Shiksha Puraskar is highest nationalrecognition in the field of education. The purpose of this award is to celebrate theachievements of finest institutions and teachers of our country who havenot only improved the quality of education but also enriched the lives ofstudents.

Concept note-3: All regular teachers are eligible and no minimum yearsof service is required. This enabled meritorious young teachers to apply. The numbersof awards have been rationalized to 45+2, as against the earlier 378, therebyrestoring the prestige of the awards.
