Which social media platform has launched an ‘alert’ feature to help find missing children?

(A) Whatsapp

(B) Meta

(C) Snapchat

(D) ** Instagram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So if you get an alert on your Instagram feed, do note that an active search is on for a missing child nearby. Instagram uses a variety of signals, including the city you list on your profile, your IP address and location services (if you have it turned on), to show you the AMBER alerts.

Concept note-2: -The app, named “ReUnite” that works on the technology of facial recognition software will help to report, search and identify abandoned children on the streets. The app was launched by child rights activist and Noble Peace Laureate Mr. Kailash Satyarthi and Union Minister for Commerce and Industry Mr Suresh Prabhu.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which company recently announced to launch ‘AMBER alerts’ to find missing children? Notes: Meta recently announced that AMBER alerts will be launched in Instagram in 25 countries.

Concept note-4: -Instagram has launched an ‘alert’ feature to help find missing children.
