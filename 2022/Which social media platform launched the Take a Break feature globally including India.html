Which social media platform launched the Take a Break feature globally including India?

(A) Whatsapp

(B) Facebook

(C) ** Instagram

(D) Twitter

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Instagram expands ‘Take a Break’ feature globally, includingIndia.

Concept note-2: Facebook had the highest traffic at around 58 percentamong social media platforms across India in July 2022. The social media companyhas consistently secured a high page traffic share in India since March 2019 when itwas around 90 percent.

Concept note-3: A social media break, or social mediadetox, is a period of time during which a person stops using social media tovarying degrees. The individual defines how long the break will last and whichsocial media platforms it includes.

Concept note-4: You can turn onreminders to take regular breaks while you are using Instagram. These breakreminders will show up after you spend a certain amount of time in onesession on Instagram. If you choose to turn this setting on, you can decidehow often you want to see the reminder.

Concept note-5: Instagram isnow telling users when to take a break. The social media app rolled out thenew feature ahead of the CEO testifying in front of Congress Wednesday.Instagram is now telling young users to take a break from the photo-sharingplatform.
