Which sportsperson has been appointed as DSP by the Assam government?

(A) PV Sindhu

(B) Avani Lakhera

(C) Mira Bai Chanu

(D) ** Lovlina Borgohain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -GUWAHATI: Champion boxer and Tokyo Olympics bronze medallist Lovlina Borgohain was on Wednesday appointed by the Assam government as a deputy superintendent (DSP) in the state police force.

Concept note-2: -Sprinter Hima Das inducted as DSP in Assam Police, says will continue her athletics career.

Concept note-3: -Indian boxer Lovlina Borgohain was formally appointed as a Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP) by Assam on Wednesday. The 24-year-old Lovlina Borgohain won bronze in the women’s 69kg on her Olympic debut at Tokyo last year.

Concept note-4: -OLYMPIC ATHLETE Born on January 9, 2000, to rice farmers in Dhing village of Assam’s Nagaon district, Das won five back-to-back gold medals in about a month in Czech Republic in July 2019. Hima Das was interested in sports since childhood.
