Which state CM has unveiled the logo of the 2023 FIH Men’s Hockey World Cup?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) ** Odisha

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Odisha Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik yesterday unveiled logoof 2023 FIH Men’s Hockey World Cup at the Kalinga stadium in the capitalBhubaneswar. To be held in the twin cities of Bhubaneswar and Rourkela,the prestigious quadrennial tournament is scheduled from January 13 to29.

Concept note-2: Bhubaneswar, India, April 18, 2022: Hon’ble ChiefMinister of Odisha Shri Naveen Patnaik unveiled the official logo of theFIH Odisha Hockey Men’s World Cup 2023 Bhubaneswar - Rourkela at theKalinga Hockey Stadium on Thursday, April 14.

Concept note-3: Itwas held at the Kalinga Stadium in Bhubaneswar and at the 20,000 seatBirsa Munda International Hockey Stadium in Rourkela, India from 13 to29 January 2023.

Concept note-4: Krishan Bahadur Pathak and PRSreejesh are the goalkeepers. Manpreet Singh, Hardik Singh, Nilakanta Sharma,Shamsher Singh, Vivek Sagar Prasad, and Akashdeep Singh are all midfielders.Mandeep Singh, Lalit Kumar Upadhyay, Abhishek, and Sukhjeet Singh are theforwards.
