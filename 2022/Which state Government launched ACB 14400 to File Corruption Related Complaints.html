Which state Government launched ‘ACB 14400’ to File Corruption Related Complaints?

(A) Kerala

(B) Karnataka

(C) ** Andhra Pradesh

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An app named “ACB 14400” has been launched by the Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, YS Jagan Mohan Reddy. This app was launched to curb corruption at government offices across the State. Which organization has developed this app?

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which state/UT has developed a mobile application named “ACB 14400” for lodging complaints against corruption? Notes: Andhra Pradesh government’s Anti-Corruption Bureau has developed a mobile application named “ACB 14400” for lodging complaints against corruption.

Concept note-3: -Andhra Pradesh CM YS Jagan Mohan Reddy launched an app named ‘ACB 14400’ to curb corruption in the state.

Concept note-4: -After launching the toll free number 14400 and ACB-14400 app, the ACB has given widespread publicity in the form of wall posters, hoardings, posters on buses and bus shelters in order to create awareness among the public.
