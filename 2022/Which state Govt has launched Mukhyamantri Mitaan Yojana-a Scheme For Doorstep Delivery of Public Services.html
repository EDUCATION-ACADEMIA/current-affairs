Which state Govt has launched ‘Mukhyamantri Mitaan Yojana’-a Scheme For Doorstep Delivery of Public Services?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Nagaland

(C) Punjab

(D) ** Chhattisgarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: To provide benefits of government services at people’sdoorsteps in Chhattisgarh, a Mukhyamantri Mitan Yojana has been started at theinitiative of Chief Minister Bhupesh Baghel. Begun on May 1, it has won goodresponse and become popular among the citizens.

Concept note-2: TheMukhyamantri Mitan Yojana, an initiative of the Chhattisgarh government, providesgovernment services to urban citizens at their doorstep. More than 41,000 peoplehave benefitted from it so far. Information related to the documents is given tocitizens on toll-free number 14545.

Concept note-3: Notes: Goa government hasformally launched a state-sponsored pilgrimage scheme for people named‘Mukhyamantri Devdarshan Yatra Yojana’.
