Which state Menar bird village has been declared as a wetland area?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Manipur

(C) ** Rajasthan

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Recognised as the “bird village” following community-drivenconservation efforts, Menar in Udaipur district is set to be notified as Rajasthan’snew wetland.

Concept note-2: Tamil Nadu has maximum no. of Ramsar sites (14nos), followed by UP which has 10 nos. of Ramsar sites.

Concept note-3: Thefive new Ramsar sites are Karikili Bird Sanctuary, Pallikaranai Marsh Reserve Forestand Pichavaram Mangrove in Tamil Nadu, Sakhya Sagar in Madhya Pradesh andPala Wetlands in Mizoram.

Concept note-4: Menar village in Udaipur district,recognized as “Bird Village” after various conservation efforts, has been decided tobe notified as the new wetland of Rajasthan.

Concept note-5: 8.1.8RajasthanTotal wetland area estimated is 782314 ha that is around 2.29 per cent ofthe geographic area (Table 29). River/Stream contributed 39.95% to thetotal wetland area. The reservoir/barrage with 190600 ha (24.36% area) isthe second major wetland category. Area under tank/pond is 151027 ha(19.31%).
