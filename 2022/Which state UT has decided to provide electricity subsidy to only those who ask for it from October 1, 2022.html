Which state/ UT has decided to provide electricity subsidy to only those who ask for it from October 1, 2022?

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) ** Delhi

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: From October 1, Delhi government will provide electricity subsidyto only those who would want the same.

Concept note-2: Synopsis. The Delhigovernment has extended the last date to apply for getting electricity at subsidisedrates in the national capital till November 15, 2022.

Concept note-3: Now, thestate government has announced a new voluntary subsidy scheme (VSS)where it is mandatory for the people of Delhi to opt-in for the subsidy tocontinue getting electricity at subsidised rates beginning from October 1,2022.

Concept note-4: At present, consumers with monthly consumption of lessthan 200 units do not have to pay any charges while those with consumption up to400 units get 50 per cent subsidy. A power consumer who has not applied for subsidycan get it from the next bill cycle after submitting his application, the officialsadded.

Concept note-5: There are certain rules that come into play. You canavail electricity subsidy when: Your consumption of electricity is within 200 unitsduring the billing period. You can get a subsidy of Rs 800 for consuming 201 to 400units in a month.
