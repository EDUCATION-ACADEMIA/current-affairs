Which state bagged the International Travel Award 2023 for Best Destination for Culture?

(A) Assam

(B) ** West Bengal

(C) Telangana

(D) Tripura

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: West Bengal has been accredited the International TravelAward 2023 for Best Destination for Culture by the Pacific Area Travel WritersAssociation (PATWA), an affiliate member of the United Nations World TourismOrganization (UNWTO).

Concept note-2: West Bengal CM Mamata Banerjeeon Monday announced that the state has won the International Travel Award 2023for ‘Best Destination for Culture’.

Concept note-3: West Bengal has bagged theInternational Tourism award for ‘best destination for culture’, chief minister saidduring a programme in Kolkata on Monday. “Bengal has made its mark on the globalcultural map,” said Banerjee.

Concept note-4: Bengal Takes Home PrestigiousInternational Tourism Award For Best Destination For Culture. Adjudged as the‘Best Destination for Culture’ by a global organisation of travel writers Pacific AreaTravel Writers Association (PATWA), Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee announcedthe news on September 5.
