Which state government & NABARD has signed a pact to increase fish production?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) Bihar

(C) ** Odisha

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: In a bid to help the State create fisheries infrastructurefacilities both in marine and inland sectors and augment fish production,NABARD has inked a pact with the State government. Principal Secretaryof Finance department Vishal Kumar Dev and CGM (Odisha region) CUdayabhaskar signed the MoU on Friday.

Concept note-2: NABARD alsoprovides RIDF assistance for strengthening seed production infrastructureto state govts. Nursery rearing facility for marine and brackish water finfishes and mud crab seed production needs to be promoted under privatesector.

Concept note-3: NABARD partakes in development of institutionswhich help the rural economy. NABARD also keeps a check on its clientinstitutes. It regulates the institutions which provide financial help to therural economy. It provides training facilities to the institutions working inthe field of rural upliftment.

Concept note-4: Current Context: Thegovernment of Odisha has signed an agreement with the National Bankfor Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD). The agreement wassigned to create fish infrastructure in order to enhance fish production in thestate.

Concept note-5: Subsidy 10% with a maximum ceiling of Rs 1.50 lakh toall categories of fish farmers.
