Which state government has announced launching drones in healthcare in 2022?

(A) Himachal Pradesh

(B) Rajasthan

(C) Haryana

(D) ** Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Arunachal Pradesh government, in partnership withBengaluru-based Redwing Labs, which manufactures and operates drone logisticssystems, also launched a fully functional health care drone network in East Kamenglast August.

Concept note-2: Health officials in Meghalaya said the drone stationwould run routine and emergency deliveries of vaccines, medicines, diagnosticsamples, blood units and other medical supplies to and from the remote healthcarefacilities for an initial period of a year.

Concept note-3: With this newdrone policy, Himachal Pradesh has become the first state in the country toformally acknowledge the public use of drones.

Concept note-4: Thecommencement of project began with the inauguration of Asia’s First DroneDelivery Hub built at the Jengjal Sub-Divisional Hospital, on 5th Dec 2022by James P.K. Sangma the Hon’ble Minister, Health and Family Welfare,Meghalaya.

Concept note-5: The Drone Policy also seeks to enable thegovernment to utilize the cutting edge technologies for improving servicedelivery for societal benefit. Accordingly, this Drone Policy seeks to incentivizeDrone and Drone enabled Technology, Design, Services and ManufacturingIndustry.
