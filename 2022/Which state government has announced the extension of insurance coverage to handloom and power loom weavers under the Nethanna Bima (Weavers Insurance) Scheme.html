Which state government has announced the extension of insurance coverage to handloom and power loom weavers under the ‘Nethanna Bima’ (Weaver’s Insurance) Scheme?

(A) ** Telangana

(B) Odisha

(C) Assam

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Hyderabad: For the first time in the country, TelanganaGovernment has launched ‘Nethanna Ku Bima’ Scheme, which extends insurancecoverage to handloom and powerloom weavers in the State.

Concept note-2: Underthe scheme, the Telangana government has joined hands with Life InsuranceCorporation (LIC) of India for the ‘Nethanna Beema’ scheme and termedthe state department of handlooms and textiles as the nodal agency for itsimplementation.

Concept note-3: Hyderabad: Handloom and powerloom workersin Telangana came under a Rs five lakh insurance coverage on Sunday whenthe State government launched the ‘Nethanna Ku Bima,’ a first-of-its-kindscheme in the country.

Concept note-4: For the first time in the country,Telangana Government has launched the ‘Nethanna Ku Bima’ Scheme, whichextends insurance coverage to handloom and power loom weavers in the State.Handlooms Minister KT Rama Rao virtually launched the scheme on NationalHandloom Day.

Concept note-5: On National Handloom Day 7 August2022, the Chief Minister Of Telangana Mr. K Chandrashekhar Rao startedNethanna Bima Scheme. This scheme has been launched for the welfare ofweavers.
