Which state government has announced to set up of ‘Gramin Aajeevika Parks’ (rural industrial parks)?

(A) West Bengal

(B) Karnataka

(C) Odisha

(D) ** Chhattisgarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Which state government has announced to set up graminaajeevika parks (rural industrial parks)? Notes: Chhattisgarh government announced toset up rural industrial parks in the state. Chief Minister Bhupesh Baghel announcedthat the project will be launched on Gandhi Jayanti.

Concept note-2: Thecorrect answer is Chhattisgarh. Chhattisgarh govt has announced to set up 300 ruralindustrial parks in the state to boost and strengthen the village economy. Theproject will be launched on the occasion of Gandhi Jayanti on October 2,2022.

Concept note-3: “We are going to start ‘gramin aajeevika parks’,to be known as ‘rural industrial parks’, to develop gauthans as livelihoodcentres.”

Concept note-4: The industrial park will be developed as arural production and service center to meet local, government and otherneeds. In order to promote livelihood and employment in rural areas, privateentrepreneurs will be encouraged to set up enterprises in these industrialparks.
