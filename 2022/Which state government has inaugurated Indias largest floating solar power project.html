Which state government has inaugurated India’s largest floating solar power project?

(A) Kerala

(B) Tripura

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) ** Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indi’s largest floating Solar Power Project is now fully operational. NTPC declared Commercial Operation of the final part capacity of 20 MW out of 100 MW Ramagundam Floating Solar PV Project at Ramagundam, Telangana with effect from 00:00 hours of July 01, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Banwari Lal Purohit inaugurates North India’s largest floating Solar project in Chandigarh. Union Territory Administrator, Banwari Lal Purohit has inaugurated northern India’s largest floating solar power project of 2000kWp worth Rs 11.70 crore at waterworks, Sector 39.

Concept note-3: -Hence, the largest solar power plant in India is located at Kamuthi in Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Madhya Pradesh. The world’s largest floating solar energy project of 600 MW is started in Khandwa district of Madhya Pradesh. Will begin power generation by the year 2022-23.
