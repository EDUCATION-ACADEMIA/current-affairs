Which state government has launched “Student Startup and Innovation Policy (SSIP) 2.0”?

(A) Punjab

(B) Bihar

(C) Odisha

(D) ** Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Government of Gujarat has developed a policy for providingassistance to Startups/ Innovation.

Concept note-2: SSIP 2.0 rules providessupport to any person aged up to 35 years, who is a school student; or a vocational,diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate & doctoral student; or an alumna, alumnus orany dropout from institute, university or school.

Concept note-3: StudentStart-up and Innovation Policy. 1) Outreach and sensitize 5 million students forinnovation and entrepreneurship, covering at least 1000 Higher EducationalInstitutions (HEIs) and 10,000 Schools of the State. 2) Support for 10,000student-led Proof-of-Concepts (PoCs/Prototype). 3) Assistance to 5000 IPfilings.
