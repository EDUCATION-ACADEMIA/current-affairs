Which state government has launched an e-governance portal-“Samarth”?

(A) ** Uttarakhand

(B) Odisha

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Q. Which state’s educationdepartment has launched ane-governance portal named ‘Samarth’? Notes: The Uttarakhand educationdepartment recently launched an e-governance portal named ‘Samarth’, to make theeducation system more transparent in the state.

Concept note-2: The correctanswer is Karnataka. Karnataka government has unveiled the Farmer Registrationand Unified beneficiary Information System (FRUITS). FRUITS is an e-governanceportal to create a repository of farmland information and farm loan details on asingle platform.

Concept note-3: E-governance, meaning ‘electronic governance’is using information and communication technologies (ICTs) (such as WideArea Networks, the Internet, and mobile computing) at various levels of thegovernment and the public sector and beyond, for the purpose of enhancinggovernance.

Concept note-4: The Samarth E-Gov Suite is a purpose builtplatform for Universities and HEIs to deploy a digital framework of goodgovernance. Samarth increases the efficiency of Institutions by enabling themto : (1) leverage digital technologies to define and govern administrativeprocesses.

Concept note-5: SAMARTH is an e-Governance platform, developedunder National Mission of Education in Information and CommunicationTechnology Scheme (NMEICT) of M/o HRD. SAMARTH toenhance productivitythrough better information management in the institute by seamless access toinformation.
