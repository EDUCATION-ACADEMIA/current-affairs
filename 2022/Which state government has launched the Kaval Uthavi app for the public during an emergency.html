Which state government has launched the “Kaval Uthavi” app for the public during an emergency?

(A) Government of Punjab

(B) Gujarat government

(C) ** Government of Tamil Nadu

(D) Government of Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is Tamil Nadu. Tamil Nadu Chief Minister MK Stalin on 4 April 2022 launched ‘Kaaval Uthavi’ app. It contains 60 features that willhelp seek police assistance during any emergency.

Concept note-2: CHENNAI:Tamil Nadu DGP C Sylendra Babu on Friday launched a mobile app called TracKDthat will help cops monitor criminals and rowdies.

Concept note-3: KaavalUthavi app is used to trigger & send ‘Emergency’ alert to control roomin order to get immediate assistance by general public during any kind ofdistress/emergency.

Concept note-4: Police commissioner Shankar Jiwallaunched roadEase on Thursday in association with Lepton, which developed theapp.
