Which state government has launched the ‘Nan Mudhalvan’ scheme for students?

(A) ** Tamil Nadu

(B) Karnataka

(C) Jharkhand

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Trichy: District collector M Pradeep Kumar launchedthe ‘Tamil Nadu Naan Mudhalvan Scheme’ at Bharathidasan University inTrichy on Monday. Speaking on the occasion, he said the programme willhelp students to identify their skills and give them training to help themfind a better carrier.

Concept note-2: The extensive Tamil Nadu NaanMudhalvan Scheme was unveiled on March 1st, 2022 by MK Stalin, thestate’s chief minister.

Concept note-3: These online live sessions has beenstarted on 18th April 2022 and will end on 23rd April 2022. The scheme hasbeen started with the objective of improving the educational schemes ofgovernment school students in Tamilnadu State.

Concept note-4: All collegestudents can apply for this Scheme. All educational documents should be orshould be original, only then they will be able to take advantage of NaanMudhalvan Scheme. It is imperative to have a genuine certificate document thatproves that you are the one you are.

Concept note-5: Courses in theseareas have been included in the curriculum for Anna University and itsaffiliated colleges as mandatory courses. L&T EduTech courses have beenmade mandatory for current semester civil engineering students in about 400colleges across Tamil Nadu. The programme was launched on 29 August,2022.
