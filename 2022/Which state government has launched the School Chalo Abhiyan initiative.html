Which state government has launched the ‘School Chalo Abhiyan’ initiative?

(A) Goa

(B) Assam

(C) Kerala

(D) ** Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath has launched ‘School Chalo Abhiyan’ to ensure 100% enrollment in primary and upper primary schools of Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Uttar Pradesh. UP CM Yogi Adityanath launched the ‘School Chalo Abhiyan’ in Shravasti district on 4 April 2022 to ensure 100 percent enrolment in primary and upper primary schools in the state. Shravasti has the lowest literacy rate in the state.

Concept note-3: -SCHOOL CHALO ABHIYAN is a campaign to enroll children into regular schooling and it is a regular feature of the organization from the past many years.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution The School Chalo Abhiyan was started by the State Government in July 2000 for the elementary education of all children of 6-11 and 11-14 in 2000.
