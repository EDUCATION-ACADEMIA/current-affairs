Which state government has partnered with NPCI and SBI to implement “E-RUPI”?

(A) Gujarat government

(B) Government of Punjab

(C) ** Government of Karnataka

(D) Government of Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Notes: Recently ,The government of Karnataka has partneredwith the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) & the State Bank of India(SBI). They have partnered to launch an ‘e-RUPI’ payment solution. The‘e-RUPI’ payment solution will be launched for students under its scholarshipprogram.

Concept note-2: The retail digital rupee is an electronic tokenissued by the Reserve Bank of India. At present, four banks including StateBank of India (SBI), ICICI Bank, YES Bank, and IDFC First Bank havebegun the pilot launch of retail digital rupee in four cities - Mumbai, NewDelhi, Bengaluru, and Bhubaneswar.

Concept note-3: National PaymentsCorporation of India (NPCI) in association with Department of FinancialServices (DFS), National Health Authority (NHA), Ministry of Health andFamily Welfare (MoHFW), and partner banks, has launched an innovativedigital solution – ‘e-RUPI’.

Concept note-4: “Digital Rupee” or “e₹” is alegal tender, similar to sovereign paper currency, and is issued in digitalform by the Reserve Bank of India.

Concept note-5: The Karnatakagovernment’s e-Governance department has partnered with the NationalPayments Corporation of India (NPCI) and State Bank of India (SBI) forimplementing a digital payment platform through which the government candirectly pay fees to colleges on behalf of the beneficiaries of its scholarshipprogrammes.
