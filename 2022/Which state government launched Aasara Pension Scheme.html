Which state government launched Aasara Pension Scheme?

(A) Haryana

(B) ** Telangana

(C) Kerala

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Telangana Government introduced “ Aasara ” – a newPension scheme – enhancing the monthly pension from Rs. 200 to Rs. 1000 for theold aged, widows, weavers, toddy tappers and AIDS patients and Rs. 500 toRs.

Concept note-2: The pension scheme was started on 8 November 2014 byChief Minister of Telangana, Kalvakuntla Chandrashekhar Rao in Kothur inMahboobnagar district. The government is spending ₹5,500 crore every year on thescheme.

Concept note-3: Aasara pension is a pension scheme by the Governmentof Telangana. It is a welfare scheme of pensions to old people, widows, Goudcommunity, elephantiasis patients, AIDS afflicted people, physically disabled andbeedi workers.
