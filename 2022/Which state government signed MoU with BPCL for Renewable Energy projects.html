Which state government signed MoU with BPCL for Renewable Energy projects?

(A) Karnataka

(B) ** Uttrakhand

(C) Haryana

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: of Energy, Government of Rajasthan and Shri Shelly Abraham,Head Renewable Energy, BPCL, signed the Memorandum of Understanding to set upa 1 GW Renewable Energy Power plant. The MoU signing ceremony waspresided over by Shri Ashok Gehlot, Hon’ble Chief Minister of Rajasthan,Smt.

Concept note-2: In a statement, BPCL said it has signed a memorandumof understanding (MoU) with the government of Rajasthan for setting up therenewable energy power plant.

Concept note-3: It plans to expand itsrenewable portfolio to reach 1 GW of generating capacity by 2025 and 10 GW by2040.

Concept note-4: with BPCL, vide Order dated 18.8. 2006 from Ministry ofCompany Affairs, the total paid up share capital of BPCL had increased to36,15,42,124 equity shares of Rs. 10 each and the percentage of shareholding of theGOI has reduced from 66.20% to 54.93%.
