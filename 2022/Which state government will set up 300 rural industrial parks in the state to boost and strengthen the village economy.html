Which state government will set up 300 rural industrial parks in the state to boost and strengthen the village economy?

(A) Assam

(B) Tripura

(C) West Bengal

(D) ** Chhattisgarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Chhattisgarh Govt To Set Up 300 Rural Industrial Park Units,Aims To Make Villagers Financially Self-Dependent.

Concept note-2: Thecorrect answer is Chhattisgarh. Chhattisgarh govt has announced to set up 300 ruralindustrial parks in the state to boost and strengthen the village economy. Theproject will be launched on the occasion of Gandhi Jayanti on October 2,2022.

Concept note-3: Which state government has announced to setup gramin aajeevika parks (rural industrial parks)? Notes: Chhattisgarhgovernment announced to set up rural industrial parks in the state. Chief MinisterBhupesh Baghel announced that the project will be launched on GandhiJayanti.

Concept note-4: The industrial park will be developed as a ruralproduction and service center to meet local, government and other needs. In order topromote livelihood and employment in rural areas, private entrepreneurs will beencouraged to set up enterprises in these industrial parks.

Concept note-5: RuralIndustrial Parks (RIPA) | Vibrant Rural Industrial Parks are envisaged in eachGothans where products and services which are in demand locally and globally wouldbe produced and marketed.
