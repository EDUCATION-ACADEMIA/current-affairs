Which state government’s “e-Proposal System” initiative of the Planning Department has won the WSIS Forum Award 2022?

(A) Government of Kerala

(B) Gujarat government

(C) ** Government of Meghalaya

(D) Government of Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Meghalaya government key initiative of e-proposal system, part of Meghalaya Enterprise Architect has won a coveted UN Award- World Summit on the Information Society Forum (WSIS) prize at Geneva in Switzerland.

Concept note-2: -The important effort of Meghalaya’s Planning Department, which is part of Meghalaya Enterprise Architecture, has been awarded the prestigious UN Award.

Concept note-3: -As part of MeghEA, the state government rolled out the ‘e-Proposal System’, which claims to have eliminated 75 percent of the government’s internal paperwork. The government has claimed that the system automates sanctions and administrative approvals for proposals submitted by all departments and directorates.

Concept note-4: -Planning Department’s key initiative of e-Proposal System, part of Meghalaya Enterprise Architecture (MeghEA) wins coveted UN Award – World Summit on the Information Society Forum (WSIS) Prizes 2022.

Concept note-5: -“Meghalaya was awarded for Best Practice in Advocacy, Communication and Social Mobilization in Tuberculosis control at the National Workshop on ACSM to end TB in India,” he stated.
