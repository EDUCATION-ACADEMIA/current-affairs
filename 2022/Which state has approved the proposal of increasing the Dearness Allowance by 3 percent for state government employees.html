Which state has approved the proposal of increasing the Dearness Allowance by 3 percent for state government employees?

(A) Kerala

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) ** Odisha

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Bringing cheers for the government employees, the Odishagovernment last month hiked Dearness Allowance (DA) of employees by3 per cent with retrospective effect from January 1, 2022. The proposalwas approved by Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik.

Concept note-2: TheTelangana State Government has increased the Dearness Allowance (DA) forits employees and pensioners from 17.29% of basic pay to 20.02% of basicpay, a 2.73% hike. This change will take effect retroactively from 1 July2021 and will benefit around 4.4 lakh government employees and 2.88 lakhpensioners.

Concept note-3: Tripura Chief Minister Manik Saha on Tuesdaydeclared a 12% hike in Dearness Allowance (DA) and Dearness Relief (DR) forstate government employees and pensioners with effect from December 1.With this, the state government employees’ DA has gone up from 8% to20%.

Concept note-4: DA hike news: 12% salary increase for governmentemployees of THIS state | New Year 2023 Gift. DA hike news: With this, thestate government employees’ DA has gone up from 8 per cent to 20 percent.

Concept note-5: The ‘basic pay’ does not include any other type ofpay like special pay, etc. DA for central government employees and DR forcentral government pensioners have been increased from 34 per cent to 38per cent of the basic pay. The revised rate will be applicable from July 1,2022.
