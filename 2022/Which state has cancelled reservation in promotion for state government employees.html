Which state has cancelled reservation in promotion for state government employees?

(A) ** Uttarakhand

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Now Uttarakhand government has implemented government order which bans reservation in promotion and removed the restriction in promotions in all state government departments.

Concept note-2: -Reservation in promotion by non-selection method is available to SCs and the STs in all groups of services viz. A. B, C and D at the rate of 15% and 7.5% respectively. In case of promotion by selection method reservation is available upto the lowest rung of Group ‘A’ at the same rates.

Concept note-3: -Article 16 (4A): Provides that the State can make any provision for reservation in matters of promotion in favour of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes if they are not adequately represented in the services under the State.

Concept note-4: -The Supreme Court on Friday lifted the the stay imposed by the Uttarakhand High Court on the government order (GO) which provides 30 per cent reservation in State services for women domiciled in the State [State of Uttarakhand v Pavitra Chauhan]. Want to read the full story?
