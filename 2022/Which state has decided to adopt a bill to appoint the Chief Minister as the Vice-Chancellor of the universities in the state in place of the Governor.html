[SOURCES]

Which state has decided to adopt a bill to appoint the Chief Minister as the Vice-Chancellor of the universities in the state in place of the Governor?

(A) Punjab

(B) ** West Bengal

(C) Manipur

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hon’ble Governor is ex-officio Chancellor of the State Universities. As per the provisions of the Act of the concerned University, the Governor appoints the Vice Chancellor on the advice/ in consultation with the State Government.

Concept note-2: -Governance. The Governor of West Bengal is the ex-officio Chancellor of the university while the Vice-chancellor of the university is the chief executive officer of the university. In 2021 Mahua Das was appointed as Vice-chancellor of the university.

Concept note-3: -The Vice-President of India is the Chancellor of all the Central Universities in India.
