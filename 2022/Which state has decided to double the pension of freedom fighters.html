Which state has decided to double the pension of freedom fighters?

(A) Tamilnadu

(B) ** Maharashtra

(C) Telangana

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Maharashtra Government has decided to double the pension of freedom fighters from ten thousand to twenty thousand rupees per month. Accordingly, freedom fighters associated with India’s freedom struggle, Marathwada Mukti Sangram and Goa Liberation Movement will benefit from this decision.

Concept note-2: -Freedom Fighters receiving State Freedom Fighter’s Pension (Rs. 16,000 + Rs. 500/- Medical Allowance per month).

Concept note-3: -Punjab people contributed the most.

Concept note-4: -Karnataka pays pension to 4,475 freedom fighters, but not many alive.

Concept note-5: -Kittur Chennamma was born on 23 October 1778, in Kakati, a small village in the present Belagavi District of Karnataka, India. She belonged to the Lingayat community and received training in horse riding, sword fighting and archery from a young age. She married Raja Mallasarja of the Desai family at the age of 15.
