Which state has declared a public holiday on March 23rd?

(A) ** Punjab

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Goa

(D) Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Answer: Option A Explanation: Punjab CM Bhagwant Mannhas declared a public holiday on March 23rd on the occasion of ShaheedDiwas.

Concept note-2: Punjab Government declares public holiday on 23March.

Concept note-3: “March 23, 2022 (Wednesday) will be observed asPublic Holiday in all the Government Offices/ Boards/ Corporations/ Institutions,including Industrial Establishments under the Chandigarh Administration, onaccount of Shaheedi Diwas of Bhagat Singh,” the UT administration said in anorder.

Concept note-4: Shaheed Diwas is observed on March 23 to pay tribute tofreedom fighters Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev Thapar and Shivaram Rajguru.The three young freedom fighters were hanged at the Lahore Central Jail inLahore, now in Pakistan, on March 23, 1931.

Concept note-5: Martyr’sDay or Shaheed Diwas 2023: It is observed in India on several dates. March23 is remembered as the day when three brave freedom fighters, namelyBhagat Singh, Shivaram Rajguru, and Sukhdev Thapar, were hanged by theBritish.
