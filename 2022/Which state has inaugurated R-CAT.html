Which state has inaugurated R-CAT?

(A) ** Rajasthan

(B) Sikkim

(C) Chhattisgarh

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot inaugurated Rajiv Gandhi Centrefor Advanced Technologies (RCAT) in Jaipur on 20 August, 2022. RCAT isan initiative taken by the Government of Rajasthanwith an objective ofimbibing and developing IT skills among the students & mid- careerprofessionals.

Concept note-2: Detailed Solution. The correct answer isRajasthan. With an aim to make tech graduates more employable, Rajasthangovernment is set to inaugurate Rajiv Gandhi Centre of Advance Technology orR-CAT. It will be inaugurated by Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot on 20 August 2022during a two-day DigiFest-2022.

Concept note-3: What are the course durationand fee? As of August 2022, three companies – SAS, Oracle and VMware havelaunched courses. The course duration ranges from 4 days to 259 days and the feeranges from Rs 4,800 to Rs 240,000.
