Which state has issued the guidelines to prevent the dismissal of employees without enquiry?

(A) Kerala

(B) Tripura

(C) ** Haryana

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Haryana Government issues orders which prohibitsdismissing an employee without a proper enquiry, Hindustan Timesreported.

Concept note-2: "The employer is not allowed to hire andfire even if the employee, maybe ad hoc or probationer, and the servicescannot be given a go-bye by one stroke of pen on the ground of misconductby casting stigma, without holding a regular inquiry in accordance withthe principles of natural justice."

Concept note-3: An employee can beremoved or dismissed from service, on the ground of misconduct which hasled to his conviction on a criminal charge.

Concept note-4: Dischargeis the separation on mutual understanding between employee & employerwhich is in line with the procedures of the organisation. Dismissal is however,temporary removal of the person form the organisation as a punishment for hisdoing.
