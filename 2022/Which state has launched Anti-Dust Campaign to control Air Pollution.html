Which state has launched Anti-Dust Campaign to control Air Pollution?

(A) Haryana

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Punjab

(D) ** Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Delhi govt launches anti-dust campaign to curb air pollution,586 teams to conduct surprise checks at construction sites, GovernmentNews, ET Government.

Concept note-2: Delhi Chief Minister ArvindKejriwal announced a 15-point Winter Action Plan and said that the planwill serve as a blueprint for the Delhi government to fight pollution thiswinter season.

Concept note-3: WATER SPRINKLERS. Till now, 581water sprinklers, 80 mechanized road sweeping machines, 233 anti-smog gunsat construction sites, 150 vehicles mounted anti-smog guns as well as ninesprinkling facilities on high-rise buildings are operational in the nationalcapital.

Concept note-4: BreatheLife is a global campaign that mobilizes citiesand individuals to take action on air pollution to protect our health and theplanet.
