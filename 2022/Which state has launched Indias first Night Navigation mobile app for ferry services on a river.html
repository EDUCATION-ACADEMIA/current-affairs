Which state has launched India’s first Night Navigation mobile app for ferry services on a river?

(A) Gujarat

(B) West Bengal

(C) ** Assam

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UPSC Mains. Q. Which state government is the first in Indiato launch a night navigation mobile app for ferry services? Notes: Chief Minister ofAssam, Himanta Biswa Sarma has launched India’s first Night Navigationmobile application for ferry services on the Brahmaputra River in Guwahati,Assam.

Concept note-2: The correct answer is Assam. The first-night navigationmobile application in rivers in the country was launched for ferries on theBrahmaputra river in Assam, in Feb 2022.

Concept note-3: The nightnavigation mobile application was developed by the transport department inassociation with K Raju, the principal scientist of IIT Madras and will survey thedraft of the rivers, the spokesperson said.

Concept note-4: HimachalPradesh has become the first state in the country to connect all registeredcommercial vehicles equipped with Vehicle Location Tracking Device (VLTD) withEmergency Response Support System (ERSS). Now these vehicles can be trackedanywhere in the country through VLTD.
