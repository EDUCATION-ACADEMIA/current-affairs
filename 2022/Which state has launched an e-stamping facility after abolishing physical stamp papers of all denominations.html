Which state has launched an e-stamping facility after abolishing physical stamp papers of all denominations?

(A) Assam

(B) ** Punjab

(C) Gujarat

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To bring efficiency and check the pilferage of state revenue, the Punjab government on Wednesday abolished the physical stamp papers.

Concept note-2: -The Punjab government on Wednesday decided to abolish the physical stamp papers in an attempt to bring efficiency and check the pilferage of State revenue.

Concept note-3: -Delhi has become the first state in the country to issue e-Stamp papers in all denominations.

Concept note-4: -The High Court of Rajasthan has recently launched the e-stamp facility in its Jodhpur and Jaipur benches simultaneously.

Concept note-5: -When was the e stamp paper launched in India? The Indian Government launched the e-stamping facility in July 2013. This initiative aimed to minimize fraud and mistakes during the payment of stamp duty.
