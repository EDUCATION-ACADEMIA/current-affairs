Which state has launched the ‘14400 app’ to report the corrupt officials with proof?

(A) Meghalaya

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) ** Andhra Pradesh

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UPSC Mains. Q. Which state/UT has developed a mobileapplication named “ACB 14400” for lodging complaints against corruption?Notes: Andhra Pradesh government’s Anti-Corruption Bureau has developeda mobile application named “ACB 14400” for lodging complaints againstcorruption.

Concept note-2: An app named “ACB 14400” has been launched bythe Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, YS Jagan Mohan Reddy. This app waslaunched to curb corruption at government offices across the State. Whichorganization has developed this app?

Concept note-3: This app has beendeveloped by Anti-Corruption Bureau (ACB). This app is customized for the peopleto register corruption-related complaints against the officials in the state. This appalso aims to ensure fool-proof evidence to present before the court. A complaint canbe filed through a toll-free number 14400.

Concept note-4: Toll FreeNumber: In order to prefer complaints or furnish information by the generalpublic/citizens about corruption a Toll-Free No. 14400 was launched by theGovernment.
