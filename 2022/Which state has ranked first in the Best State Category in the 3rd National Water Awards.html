Which state has ranked first in the ‘Best State Category’ in the 3rd National Water Awards?

(A) ** Uttar Pradesh

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Telangana

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Abhishek Mishra. Answer: Option A Explanation: UttarPradesh secured the first position in the ‘Best State Category’ in the 3rd NationalWater Awards, followed by Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-2: OnMarch 29, 2022, President Ram Nath Kovind presented the first prize to UttarPradesh in the ‘Best State’ category for its best performance towards waterconservation at the 3rd National Water Awards distribution ceremony at VigyanBhawan, New Delhi.

Concept note-3: The correct answer is Uttar Pradesh.Uttar Pradesh has been awarded the first prize in the 3rd National WaterAwards in the best state category, followed by Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu.The first National Water Award was launched by the Jal Shakti Ministry in2018.

Concept note-4: New Delhi [India], March 29 (ANI): Shri Mata VaishnoDevi Shrine Board won the first prize at the 3rd National Water Award on Tuesday.The board won the award under the ‘Institution/RWA/Religious Organisationcategory.
