Which state has received the first prize for the best adventure tourism destination and all-around?

(A) ** Uttarakhand

(B) Kerala

(C) Telangana

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the occasion of World Tourism Day, the state of Uttarakhand has received the first prize in two categories, for the best adventure tourism destination and all-around development of tourism from the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India .

Concept note-2: -Notes: Telangana won the first prize under the Large States category under Swachh Survekshan Gramin (SSG) 2022.

Concept note-3: -It encompasses Land based, Air based and Water based adventerous activities. Land based Adventure Tourism (15) comprises, All Terrain Vehicle Tours (ATV), Bungee Jumping, Cycling Tours, Camel Safaris, Horse Safaris, Jeep Safaris, Motorcycle.

Concept note-4: -Goa tourism won awards for being the best civic management of a tourist destination in India - Calangute village, category C - and national tourism hall of fame, category union territory: comprehensive development of tourism.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Uttarakhand.
