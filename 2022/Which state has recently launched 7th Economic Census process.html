Which state has recently launched 7th Economic Census process?

(A) Haryana

(B) Odisha

(C) Maharashtra

(D) ** Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 7th economic census was being rolled out at that stage, and Mospi claimed this would be an authentic geo-tagged database of all firms in the country. Mospi said it would create a ‘national statistical business register’ based on this database.

Concept note-2: -It is 100% centrally sponsored scheme of the Ministry of Statistics and Program Implementation, Government of India. Five Economic Census have been conducted earlier throughout the country during the year 1977,1980,1990, 1998 and 2005.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is 1977. In 1976, the Government of India launched a planning scheme called Economic Census and Surveys. In 1977, Central Statistical Organization conducted the first economic census in collaboration with the Directorate of Economics & Statistics (DES) in the States/Union Territories.
