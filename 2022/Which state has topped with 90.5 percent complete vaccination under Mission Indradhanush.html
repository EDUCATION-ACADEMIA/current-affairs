Which state has topped with 90.5 percent complete vaccination under Mission Indradhanush?

(A) Tripura

(B) Punjab

(C) ** Odisha

(D) Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: According to the National Family Health Survey (NFHS)-5,Odisha became the top state in the list of full immunizations in India with 90.5%coverage under Mission Indradhanush.

Concept note-2: Abhishek Mishra.Explanation: The Odisha state has secured the first position in MissionIndradhanush.

Concept note-3: The programme provides vaccination againsteight life-threatening diseases (diphtheria, whooping cough, Haemophilus influenzaetype B (Hib) causing pneumonia and meningitis, tetanus, polio, tuberculosis, measlesand hepatitis B) in the entire country.

Concept note-4: Hepatitis B.In additionto all these diseases, vaccines for Japanese encephalitis and Haemophilus influenzaetype B are also being provided in selected states.
