Which state has won the title of Khelo India Youth Games 2021 by securing the top position?

(A) ** Haryana

(B) Punjab

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The winner of the fourth edition of KIYG was Haryana who edged past defending champions Maharashtra who finished 2nd. Karnataka for the first time finished in the 3rd position.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which state has clinched the top position in the Khelo India Youth Games 2021? Notes: The fourth edition of Khelo India Youth Games 2021 concluded in Panchkula and the hosts Haryana grabbed the top position in the medal tally with 52 gold, 39 silver and 46 bronze medals.

Concept note-3: -Madhya Pradesh currently top the KIYG 2022 medal table at the end of Day 3 with four gold medals. Maharashtra is second with one gold. Get full Khelo India Youth Games medal tally.

Concept note-4: -Andhra Pradesh wins five medals including one gold in Khelo India Youth Games.

Concept note-5: -Maharashtra topped the medal tally with 83 medals including 28 gold, 30 silver and 25 bronze medals. Haryana and Madhya Pradesh are at the second and third positions with 23-23 gold medals respectively.
