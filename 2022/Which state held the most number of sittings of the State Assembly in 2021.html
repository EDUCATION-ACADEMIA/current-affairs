Which state held the most number of sittings of the State Assembly in 2021?

(A) ** Kerala

(B) Odisha

(C) Karnataka

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Q. Which state held the most number of sittings of the StateAssembly in 2021? Notes: As per the recent study of the think tank PRS LegislativeResearch (PRS), Kerala held the most number of sittings of the State Assembly in2021 at 61 days.

Concept note-2: The biggest state, Uttar Pradesh, has 403members in its Assembly. States which have small populations and are small in sizehave a provision for having an even smaller number of members in the LegislativeAssembly.

Concept note-3: The longest was the first (76th Congress), whichlasted 58 days, meeting (usually every third day) between November 7, 1940(the day after election day), and January 3, 1941.

Concept note-4: TheConstitution of India states that a State Legislative Assembly must have noless than 60 and no more than 500 members however an exception may begranted via an Act of Parliament as is the case in the states of Goa, Sikkim,Mizoram and the union territory of Puducherry which have fewer than 60members.
