Which state is to Get India’s First Buddhist Varsity?

(A) Haryana

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) ** Tripura

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Tripura To Get India’s First Buddhist Varsity; Foundation StoneTo Be Laid On November 29.

Concept note-2: The Dhamma Dipa InternationalBuddhist University is expected to become the first Buddhist-run university in Indiato offer Buddhist education along with courses in other disciplines of moderneducation.

Concept note-3: Maharashtra has the highest number of Buddhists inIndia, with 5.81% of the total population. Almost 90 per cent of Navayana orNeo-Buddhists live in the state.

Concept note-4: Maharastra is a top state witha Buddhist population. Sikkim (27.39%), Arunachal Pradesh (11.77%), and Mizoram(8.5 %) have emerged as the top three states in terms of having the maximumpercentage of Buddhist population.

Concept note-5: Agartala, November 29,2022: In a first-of-its-kind in India, the foundation stone of the Dhamma DipaInternational Buddhist University (DDIBU) was unveiled near Sabroom Helipad inSouth Tripura district on Tuesday.
