Which state is to disburse Rs 7600 crore to farmers under the ‘Rythu Bandhu’ scheme?

(A) Jharkhand

(B) Bihar

(C) ** Telangana

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: By Abdul Basheer: Telangana government announced that atotal of Rs 7,600 crore would be deposited into the accounts of farmers in thestate.

Concept note-2: The Telangana State government launched the“Agriculture Investment Support Scheme” (Rythu Bandhu), the first cash transferprogramme for farmers, in 2018–19.

Concept note-3: The Rythu Bandhu schemeis a cash transfer programme for farmers. Under this scheme, the state governmentprovides investment support for agriculture and horticulture crops by way of a grantof Rs 5,000 per acre to farmers each season for the purchase of seeds, fertilizers andpesticides.

Concept note-4: YSR Rythu Bharosa is a program launched by theGovernment of Andhra Pradesh to financially assist farmers by depositing an amountof ₹13,500 per annum in three installments, in association with Pradhan MantriKisan Samman Nidhi with the state government contributing ₹7500 and the center₹6000.

Concept note-5: Incentives Under Rythu Bandhu 2023In the RythuBandhu Scheme, an incentive of rupees 4000 per acre of land will be given to all ofthe farmers of the state.
