Which state launched the ‘Vidya Rath-School on Wheels’ project?

(A) Arunachal Pradesh

(B) ** Assam

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Vidya Rath: School on Wheels project launched foreconomically weak children in Assam. Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarmaattended the launch of the Vidya Rath- School on Wheels project on theoccasion of India’s 76th Independence day at a function held at the premises ofGauhati High Court.

Concept note-2: Sarma along with Chief Justice ofGauhati High Court Justice RM Chhaya launched Vidya Rath- School onWheels, a project aimed at imparting elementary education to economicallychallenged children of the society, at a function held at the premises of GauhatiHigh Court here.

Concept note-3: Assam Chief Minister Himanta BiswaSarma on Monday inaugurated the ‘Vidyarath’ mission aimed at providingeducation by motile means at Gauhati High Court.

Concept note-4: Sarmainaugurated the schools at Ghagra, Rupajuli, Sonajuli, Thakurbari and Fulbari teagardens in Sonitpur district.

Concept note-5: Sri Himanta Biswa Sarmais the 15th Chief Minister of Assam. On 10 May 2021, Sarma has swornin as the Chief Minister of Assam, succeeding his colleague SarbanandaSonowal.
