Which state ranked first in Asia in Affordable Talent in GSER?

(A) ** Kerala

(B) Bihar

(C) Karnataka

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Muskan Anand. Option [B] Kerala : Kerala has been rankedfirst in Asia in Affordable Talent in Global Startup Ecosystem Report (GSER).
