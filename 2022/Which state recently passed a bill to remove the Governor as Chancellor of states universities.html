Which state recently passed a bill to remove the Governor as Chancellor of state’s universities?

(A) ** Kerala

(B) Tripura

(C) Manipur

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Hon’ble Governor is ex-officio Chancellor of the StateUniversities. As per the provisions of the Act of the concerned University, theGovernor appoints the Vice Chancellor on the advice/ in consultation with the StateGovernment.

Concept note-2: The Governor of Rajasthan, is the ex-officio Chancellor of the State Universities. He shall, when present, presideover the Convocations of the State Universities.

Concept note-3: TheVice-President of India is the Chancellor of all the Central Universities inIndia.
