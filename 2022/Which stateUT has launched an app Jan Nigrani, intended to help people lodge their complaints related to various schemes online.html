Which state/UT has launched an app ‘Jan Nigrani’, intended to help people lodge their complaints related to various schemes online?

(A) ** Jammu & Kashmir

(B) Ladakh

(C) Manipur

(D) Daman & Diu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Explanation: The Department of Rural Development andPanchayati raj, Jammu and Kashmir, under the e-governance initiative has launchedan app ‘Jan Nigrani’.

Concept note-2: The correct answer is Jammu andKashmir. The Rural Development and Panchayati Raj Department of Jammu andKashmir administration have launched a mobile application ‘Jan Nigrani’. It is aninternet-based solution for reporting and resolving grievances by the residents ofJ&K against various schemes of the department.

Concept note-3: The‘jan nigrani’ app is an internet based solution for reporting and resolvinggrievances by the residents of the Jammu and Kashmir against various schemes ofthe department, they said. The app has been developed as an e-governanceinitiative to provide a 24x7 platform to the people for redressal of theirgrievances.
