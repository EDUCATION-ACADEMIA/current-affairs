Which state/UT has topped the India Press Freedom Report 2021 released by the Rights and Risks Analysis Group (RRAG)?

(A) ** Jammu and Kashmir

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Tripura

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The correct answer is Jammu and Kashmir. Jammu andKashmir, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh and Tripura topped the list of States andUnion Territories where journalists and media houses were targeted in 2021. This wasstated in India Press Freedom Report 2021 by the Rights and Risks Analysis Group(RRAG).

Concept note-2: Hyderabad: India’s ranking fell from 133 in 2016to 142 in 2021, of 180 countries, on the World Press Freedom Index thathighlights the degree of freedom that journalists, news organisations andnetizens have in each country, and the government’s efforts to respect suchfreedom.

Concept note-3: Notes: Rights and Risks Analysis Group (RRAG),a New Delhi-based rights group released the India Press Freedom Report2021.

Concept note-4: According to the World Press Freedom Index, released inMay, India’s ranking dropped to 150th position from last year’s 142nd rank among180 nations.
