Which state/UT launched India’s first District Good Governance Index?

(A) ** Jammu & Kashmir

(B) Kerala

(C) Goa

(D) Sikkim

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UPSC Mains. Q. Which state/UT launched India’s firstDistrict Good Governance Index (DGGI)? Notes: India’s first ever District GoodGovernance Index (DGGI) was released in the Union Territory of Jammu andKashmir.

Concept note-2: District Good Governance Index (DGGI) waslaunched in Jammu and Kashmir, virtually in January 2022. It has been launched for20 districts of J&K. This is India’s very first District Good GovernanceIndex.

Concept note-3: Jammu and Kashmir’s Pulwama district hasgrabbed the top slot in district good governance index (DGGI) 2021-22 in theunion territory, officials said on Saturday.

Concept note-4: Rajasthan hastopped the Other States (Group B) category in Judiciary and Public Safety,Environment & Citizen Centric Governance.

Concept note-5: The DistrictGood Governance Index (DGGI) is a framework document comprising ofperformance under ten governance sectors having 58 indicators with 116 datapoints.
