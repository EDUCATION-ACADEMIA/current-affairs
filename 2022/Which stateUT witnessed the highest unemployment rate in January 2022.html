Which state/UT witnessed the highest unemployment rate in January 2022

(A) Jammu & Kashmir

(B) Tripura

(C) Rajasthan

(D) ** Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Haryana is the top region by unemployment rate inIndia.

Concept note-2: The figures on the CMIE website indicated that while theurban unemployment rate rose to 10.09% in December from 8.96% the previousmonth, the rural jobless rate decreased to 7.44% from 7.55%. The highestunemployment rate in India State Wise 2022 last month was 37.4% in Haryana, whilethe lowest was 0.9% in Odisha.

Concept note-3: In December 2022,Illinois and Nevada had the highest unemployment rates of any state in theUnited States. The unemployment rate in Nevada was 5.2, and 4.7 in Illinois.The District of Columbia also had an unemployment rate of 4.7 percentduring the same time period.

Concept note-4: Unemployment rate inIndia rose to 8.30 per cent in December 2022, the highest in 16 months,according to data from Centre for Monitoring Indian Economy (CMIE). Itwas 8 per cent in the month of November.

Concept note-5: Published:Monday 02 January 2023This is a marked increase from January 2022 whenthe unemployment rate was 6.56 per cent and is also higher than the levelrecorded in November 2022 (8 per cent). The overall unemployment rateis the worst in 16 months, an analysis of data from the CMIE dashboardshowed.
