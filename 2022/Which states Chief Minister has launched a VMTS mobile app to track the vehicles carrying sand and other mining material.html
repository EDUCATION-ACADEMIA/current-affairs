Which state’s Chief Minister has launched a VMTS mobile app to track the vehicles carrying sand and other mining material?

(A) West Bengal

(B) ** Haryana

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: UPSC Mains. Q. Which Indian state launched VehicleMovement Tracking System (VMTS) mobile app? Notes: Haryana ChiefMinister Manohar Lal Khattar launched a Vehicle Movement Tracking System(VMTS) mobile app fortracking vehicles carrying sand and other miningmaterial.

Concept note-2: Hon’ble Chief Minister Haryana, Shri Manohar Lallaunched the Vehicle Movement Tracking System (VMTS) Mobile App on04/05/2022. This App has been designed and developed in-house by NationalInformatics Centre, Haryana.

Concept note-3: Haryana Chief MinisterManohar Lal Khattar has launched a Vehicle Movement Tracking System(VMTS) mobile app to track the vehicles carrying sand and other miningmaterial.

Concept note-4: A vehicle tracking system is a software system used bytransport companies to monitor the location of trucks, trailers and other vehicles.The software is connected to the vehicles via GPS, giving companies real-time insightinto the geographic position of their vehicles.
