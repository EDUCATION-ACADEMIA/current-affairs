Which state’s first Vande Bharat Express is to be flagged off on December 30?

(A) Telangana

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) ** West Bengal

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Prime Minister Narendra Modi will flag off West Bengal’s firstVande Bharat Express on the busy Howrah-New Jalpaiguri route on December 30, asenior Eastern Railway official said on Sunday.

Concept note-2: "Prime MinisterNarendra Modi will flag off the Vande Bharat Express service from Howrah to NewJalpaiguri on December 30,” Eastern Railway spokesperson Ekalabya Chakrabortytold PTI. It will be the first Vande Bharat Express to operate in West Bengal as wellas on the Eastern parts of the country.

Concept note-3: The first Vande BharatExpress train was inaugurated on February 18, 2019. The very first route isconnecting the capital city of Delhi to the holy city of Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh),making stops at Thyagraj and Kanpur. This train runs five days a week, exceptMonday and Thursday.

Concept note-4: There are eight operational VandeBharat Express trains at the moment. These routes are: New Delhi to Varanasi, NewDelhi to Katra, Mumbai Central to Gandhinagar, New Delhi to Amb Andaura,Chennai to Mysuru, Bilaspur to Nagpur, Howrah to New Jalpaiguri, andVisakhapatnam to Secunderabad.

Concept note-5: West Bengal has got its firstVande Bharat Express train on the busy Howrah-New Jalpaiguri route today, Friday,December 30. Prime Minister Narendra Modi flagged off the train through videoconference as he had to rush to Ahmedabad to attend the last rites of his motherHeeraben who died this morning.
