Which team has won the 2021 FIFA Club World Cup Championship?

(A) Palmeiras

(B) ** Chelsea

(C) Liverpool

(D) Manchester City

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The current world champions are Chelsea, whodefeated Palmeiras 2–1 after extra time in the 2021 FIFA Club World CupFinal.

Concept note-2: Real Madrid has won the tournament in 2014, 2016,2017, and 2018, making them the most successful club in the competition. Chelseaare the current defending champions.

Concept note-3: The 32 finalsmatches were played in six venues, which were also used for the 2022 FIFAWorld Cup. Host nation Qatar beat Egypt in the third-place match. In thefinal, Algeria played Tunisia on 18 December at the Al Bayt Stadium in AlKhor. Algeria won the match 2–0 after extra time to claim their first ArabCup.
