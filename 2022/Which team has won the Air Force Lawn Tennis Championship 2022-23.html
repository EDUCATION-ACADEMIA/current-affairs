Which team has won the Air Force Lawn Tennis Championship 2022-23?

(A) Eastern Air Command

(B) ** Western Air Command

(C) Training Command

(D) Central Air Command

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Western Air Command, Indian Air Force (IAF) won the Air Force Lawn Tennis Championship 2022-23 held at Head Quarter MC, Vayusena Nagar, Nagpur. The final match of team Championship was played between Western Air Command and Training Command in which Western Air Command emerged as the winner.

Concept note-2: -Air Marshal Pankaj Mohan Sinha assumed command of the Indian Air Force’s Western Air Command on 01 January 2023. The Air Marshal is a graduate of National Defence Academy, Pune and was commissioned into the IAF as a fighter pilot in Jun 1985.

Concept note-3: -IAF stands for Indian Air Force. It is the air arm of Indian armed forces.

Concept note-4: -The AFCAT exam is the most preferred competitive exam to join the India Air force as an officer. AFCAT is conducted twice every year. The AFCAT 2023 application form is released on the IAF official website. The AFCAT selection process consists of a written test, AFSB interview and medical test.
