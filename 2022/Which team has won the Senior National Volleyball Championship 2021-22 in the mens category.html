Which team has won the Senior National Volleyball Championship 2021-22 in the men’s category?

(A) Indian Railways

(B) Kerala

(C) Indian Army

(D) ** Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Haryana team defeated the Indian Railway 3-0, to win the Men’stitle in Senior National Volleyball Championship2021-22.

Concept note-2: OnDecember 22, 2022, the 25th National Volleyball Championship was held atTalaiya Field Ground in Panna district of Madhya Pradesh, in which Gujaratin the men’s category and West Bengal in the women’s category becamechampions.

Concept note-3: The 42nd edition of the tournament began onDecember 1, 2022 in various college campuses across the country, locationdeterminations were chosen based on participating teams seedings. The tournamentconcluded with the championship game at CHI Health Center in Omaha onDecember 17, when Texas defeated Louisville 3–0.

Concept note-4: MaharajaRanjit Singh AwardInstituted in 1978, the honor carries a trophy of MaharajaRanjit Singh and cash prize.

Concept note-5: Gurinder Singh is thecaptain of the Indian men’s volleyball team. In Pro Volleyball league, he playsfor Ahmedabad team. The team is managed by Volleyball Federation ofIndia.
