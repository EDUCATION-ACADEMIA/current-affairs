Which tourism state has bagged the prestigious Responsible Tourism Global award at the World Travel Mart held in London?

(A) Uttarakhand Tourism

(B) ** Kerala Tourism

(C) Himachal Tourism

(D) J & K Tourism

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Kerala Tourism has bagged the prestigious ResponsibleTourism Global award at the World Travel Mart held in London. State TourismMinister PA Mohammed Riyas received the award on behalf of the department atLondon.

Concept note-2: Responsible Tourism’s Most Prestigious AwardsProgrammeThe WTM World Responsible Tourism Awards cover the globe.Championing the very best work in Responsible Tourism. Free to enter, the 2022Awards are split into four regions, with the winner of each region going forward tocompete in the Global Awards.

Concept note-3: Tourism NewsKeralaTourism will launch a project named STREET in select locations in sevendistricts with an aim of taking tourism deep into the state’s interiors and ruralhinterlands.

Concept note-4: Kerala Tourism bagged the Responsible TourismGlobal award at the World Travel Mart held in London. The award wasgiven for the STREET project implemented by the Responsible TourismMission.

Concept note-5: Mr P.B. Nooh has been appointed the new KeralaTourism Director, replacing Mr V. R. Krishna Teja Mylavarapu. Mr Nooh, a2018-batch IAS officer, will hold the additional charge of Chief Executive Officer,LIFE Mission.
