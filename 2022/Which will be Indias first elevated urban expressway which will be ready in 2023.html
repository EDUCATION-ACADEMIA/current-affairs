Which will be India’s first elevated urban expressway which will be ready in 2023?

(A) Somnath Expressway

(B) ** Dwarka Expressway

(C) Mathura Expressway

(D) Badrinath Expressway

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Nitin Gadkari, the Union Minister for Road Transport andHighways on Monday said that the Dwarka Expressway, which will be India’s first elevatedurban expressway, will be operational by 2023.

Concept note-2: GURUGRAM:The Dwarka expressway will open to commuters in stages, and the 19km section inHaryana will be fully open by early 2023, not by the year-end as announced earlier,according to senior officials with the National Highways Authority of India(NHAI).

Concept note-3: Mumbai–Pune Expressway, India’s first everexpressway and is 6 lanes wide.

Concept note-4: Dwarka Expressway is expectedto be completed and opened in June 2023 which is beyond its current deadline ofDecember 2022. The Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) has planned a metroline along Dwarka Expressway, but the project – along with many others – is pendingapproval from the Haryana Government.
