Which word has been selected as the ‘Word of the Year 2022 by Collins dictionary’?

(A) ** Permacrisis

(B) Pandemic

(C) Lock Down

(D) Non-Fungible Token

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: ’Goblin mode’ by Oxford, ‘permacrisis’ by Collins, ‘gaslighting’by Merriam-Webster and ‘homer’ by Cambridge are the words of the year for2022.

Concept note-2: Goblin modeThe OED defines it as “a type of behaviourwhich is unapologetically self-indulgent, lazy, slovenly, or greedy, typically in a waythat rejects social norms or expectations”. The term was first coined on Twitter in2009, but entered popular culture when the term went viral on social media inFebruary 2022.
