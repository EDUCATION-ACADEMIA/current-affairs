Which zone won the title of Duleep Trophy 2021-22?

(A) ** West

(B) North

(C) East

(D) South

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: West Zone thrashed South Zone by 294 runs to win the 2022Duleep Trophy here on Sunday, completing the formalities before lunch onthe fifth and last day’s play.

Concept note-2: RESULT: WEST ZONEBEATS SOUTH ZONE BY 294 RUNS, WINS THE DULEEP TROPHY 2022TITLE.

Since2016–17 it has been played by teams chosen by BCCI selectors. West Zone are thecurrent champions.
