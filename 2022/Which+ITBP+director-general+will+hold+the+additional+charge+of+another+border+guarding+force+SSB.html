Which ITBP director-general will hold the additional charge of another border guarding force SSB?

(A) Umesh Agrawal

(B) Rohit Thakur

(C) Vipin Verma

(D) ** Sanjay Arora

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Director General of Sashastra Seema Bal, SSB Dr. Sujoy Lal Thaosen today took over the additional charge of Director General of Indo-Tibetan Border Police in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -The Madhya Pradesh cadre Indian Police Service (IPS) officer has been serving as the SSB DG with the additional charge of ITBP. Senior IPS officer Anish Dayal Singh on Monday took charge as the new director general (DG) of the Indo-Tibetan Border Police that guards the 3,488-km Line of Actual Control with China.

Concept note-3: -Anish Dayal Singh takes charge as ITBP DG.

Concept note-4: -The post of Director, SSB got re-designated as Additional DG, SSB.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sanjay Arora. Indo-Tibetan Border Police (ITBP) Director-General Sanjay Arora will hold the additional charge of another border guarding force SSB. He will replace the current chief of Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) Kumar Rajesh Chandra who is set to retire on December 31, 2021.
