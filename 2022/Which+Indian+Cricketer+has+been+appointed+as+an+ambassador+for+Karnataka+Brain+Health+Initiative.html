Which Indian Cricketer has been appointed as an ambassador for Karnataka Brain Health Initiative?

(A) ** Robin Uthappa

(B) Ruturaj Gaikwad

(C) Devdutt Padikkal

(D) Ambati Rayudu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: The Karnataka Health Department has appointed Indian Cricket Player Robin Uthappa as the brand ambassador for Karnataka Brain Health Initiative (KA-BHI) (Launched in January 2022).

Concept note-2: -Real11 has announced the appointment of Indian Cricketer, Kuldeep Yadav as its brand ambassador. The company said the association with the cricketer will help it reach a wider audience in the nation.

Concept note-3: -BENGALURU: The Karnataka government has appointed Kannada star Sudeep as the brand ambassador for its ‘’Punyakoti Dattu Yojana’, cattle adoption scheme.
