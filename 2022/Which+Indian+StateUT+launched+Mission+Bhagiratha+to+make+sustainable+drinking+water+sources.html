Which Indian State/UT launched Mission Bhagiratha to make sustainable drinking water sources?

(A) ** Telangana

(B) Jharkhand

(C) Bihar

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which Indian state/UT launched Mission Bhagiratha to make sustainable drinking water sources? Notes: Telangana launched Mission Bhagiratha in 2015 to make drinking water sources safe and sustainable. Several districts of the state are hard-rock terrains with minimum rainfall.
