Which Insurance Company has launched India’s first dental health insurance plan?

(A) SBI General Insurance Company

(B) Bharti AXA General Insurance

(C) Religare Insurance Company

(D) ** PNB MetLife India Insurance Company

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On Monday, PNB MetLife India Insurance Co. Ltd. launched India’s first dental health insurance plan. This plan covers specific outpatient expenses (OPD) & accidental dental procedures and provides financial aid related to overall dental care.

Concept note-2: -PNB MetLife India Insurance Company has launched India’s first dental health insurance plan. This is the first insurance plan in India that covers fixed-benefit outpatient expenses and provides financial assistance with costs related to overall dental health.

Concept note-3: -Milestone. General Insurance Corporation launched India’s first mediclaim policy in 1986 to standardise the terms and conditions under health insurance.

Concept note-4: -Yes, dental treatment can be covered under mediclaim or health insurance policies.
