Which Union Ministry is associated with ‘One Nation, One Grid, One Frequency and One National Load Dispatch Centre (NLDC)?

(A) ** Ministry of Power

(B) Ministry of Electronics and IT

(C) Ministry of Communications

(D) Ministry of Science and Technology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’ It is managed by the Ministry of Power’s Public Sector Enterprise, the Power Grid Corporation of India (PGCIL). The Indian government designated PGCIL as a Maharatna Public Sector Undertaking in 2019.

Concept note-2: -On 31st December 2013, the southern Region was connected to the Central Grid. Thereby achieving ‘One Nation, One Grid, One Frequency’. All possible measures are taken to ensure that the grid frequency always remains within the 49.90-50.05 Hz (hertz) band.

Concept note-3: -The National Grid is the high-voltage electricity transmission network in India, connecting power stations and major substations and ensuring that electricity generated anywhere in India can be used to satisfy demand elsewhere.
