Which University has received an A grade after obtaining 3.85 points in the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) grading?

(A) I P University

(B) Maharishi Dayanand University

(C) Delhi University

(D) ** The Guru Nanak Dev University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Guru Nanak Dev University ,Amritsar, Punjab has become the first University in India to get A grade by scoring 3.85 in National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) grading.

Concept note-2: -The Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar has got A grade by scoring 3.85 points in National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) grading, thereby becoming the only University in India to get this score.

Concept note-3: -MUMBAI: A deemed university in Bhubaneswar has pipped India’s top-ranked university of 2022, the Institute of Science, in its NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) score. The university, ranked outside the top 15, has scored 3.88, the highest ever awarded in the country, compared to IISc’s 3.67.

Concept note-4: -Institutions are graded for each Key Aspect under four categories, viz. A, B, C and D, denoting Very good, Good, Satisfactory and Unsatisfactory levels respectively.
