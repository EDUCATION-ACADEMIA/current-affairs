Which actor has been appointed the new brand ambassador for the Hero MotoCrop

(A) Varun Dhawan

(B) Salman Khan

(C) Akshay Kumar

(D) ** Ram Charan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Hero MotoCrop has appointed actor Ram Charan as the new brand ambassador.

Concept note-2: -Hero MotoCorp has appointed Indian movie star Ram Charan as its new brand ambassador. The energized high-decibel campaign of Glamour XTEC - Gazab Style, Gazab Technology features Ram Charan riding stunning Glamour in Nexus Blue Colour.

Concept note-3: -Motorcycle and scooter manufacturer Hero MotoCorp has announced the appointment of actor Ram Charan as the new brand ambassador. The high-decibel campaign of Glamour XTEC – ‘Gazab Style, Gazab Technology’ features Ram Charan riding Glamour in Nexus Blue colour.

Concept note-4: -Over a video call, Chandrasekar Radhakrishnan, head of business growth (emerging mobility), Hero MotoCorp, mentions that the intent behind the tagline (’Make Way’) was to drive change and create an inclusive brand.

Concept note-5: -Hero MotoCorp Limited (formerly Hero Honda) is Indian multinational motorcycle and scooter manufacturer headquartered in New Delhi.
