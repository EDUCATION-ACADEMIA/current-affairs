Which airport has won the ‘Covid Champion’ award at Wings India 2022?

(A) Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport, Kolkata

(B) ** Cochin International Airport Limited

(C) Kempegowda International Airport Bangalore

(D) Chennai International Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kochi: The Cochin International Airport Ltd (Cial) has won the Covid Champion award at Wings India 2022 - Asia’s largest civil aviation event jointly organized by the ministry of civil aviation (MoCA) and Federation of Indian Chamber of Commerce and Industry (FICCI) at Begumpet airport, Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which city is set to host ‘WINGS INDIA 2022’, the exhibition on civil aviation? Notes: Hyderabad is scheduled to host the ‘WINGS INDIA 2022’, the flagship biennial exhibition on civil aviation. It is organised from March 24 - 27, 2022 at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: -Bengaluru Airport awarded ‘Best Airport’ at Wings India Awards 2022.
