Which band has won the most Billboard Music Awards in history?

(A) Bruno Mars

(B) Jonas Brothers

(C) ** BTS

(D) Destiny’s Child

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The record for most Billboard Music Awards won by a group is held by BTS who have won 12 awards.

Concept note-2: -Made Group History at the Billboard Music Awards With three trophies at the 2022 Billboard Music Awards, BTS scored more than any other group or duo this year. Plus, with their third win as top duo/group, BTS tied with One Direction for the most wins ever in the category.

Concept note-3: -U2 holds the record for most Grammy Awards won by a group. They have won 22 awards.

Concept note-4: -BTS has won 442 awards, out of 636 nominations, from their debut in 2013 until the writing of this post in April 2022. This K-pop group has come a long way since their debut. Here is a complete collection of all BTS awards, ranging from music show wins to Melon Music Awards, Daesangs and more.

Concept note-5: -A Grammy Award (originally called Gramophone Award), or Grammy, is an accolade by the National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences of the United States to recognise outstanding achievement in the music industry.
