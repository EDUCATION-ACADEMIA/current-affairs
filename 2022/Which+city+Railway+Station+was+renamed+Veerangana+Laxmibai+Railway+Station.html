Which city Railway Station was renamed Veerangana Laxmibai Railway Station?

(A) Kanpur

(B) Lucknow

(C) ** Jhansi

(D) Agra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UP’s Jhansi Railway Station Renamed As ‘Veerangana Laxmibai Railway Station’

Concept note-2: -Jhansi: Recently, Uttar Pradesh chief minister Yogi Adityanath announced the state government’s decision to rename Jhansi railway station – in the largest city of the Bundelkhand region and one of the busiest railway stations in the country – as Virangana Laxmibai railway station.

Concept note-3: -The station would, henceforth, be known as Veerangana Laxmi Bai Jhansi railway station. The dropping of the word Jhansi in December 2021 had stirred protests and BJP MP Anurag Sharma had written to the railway minister for reinstatement of the word.

Concept note-4: -Which city is served by the newly named Virangana Laxmibai railway station? The correct answer is Jhansi.
