Which city became first in India to get ISO certification for FSSM service?

(A) Kolkata

(B) Jaipur

(C) ** Bhubaneswar

(D) Ahmadabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Note – Jenaidah is the first city in the world to get ISO certification for FSSM. Bhubaneswar is 2nd in the world and 1st city in India in this regard. Key Points: i.

Concept note-2: -Bhubaneswar is the second city in the world and first in India to get ISO 9001:2015 certification.

Concept note-3: -Bhubaneswar becomes 1st Indian city to get ISO 9001:2015 certification - What is FSSM services?

Concept note-4: -National standardization activity started in India in 1947 with the establishment of the Indian Standards Institution (ISI) as a society under the Societies Registration Act 1860, to prepare and promote the adoption of national standards.

Concept note-5: -The very first ISO standard, called “ISO/R 1:1951” – was first published in 1951 to set a standard reference temperature for industrial length measurements. Today, that standard still exists (after many updates) as ISO 1:2002.
