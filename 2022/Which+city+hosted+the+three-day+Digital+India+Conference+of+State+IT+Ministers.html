Which city hosted the three-day Digital India Conference of State IT Ministers?

(A) Patna

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Jaipur

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lucknow: The state capital will host a three-day conference of G20’s digital economy working group from February 13 to 15 that will be attended by foreign delegates, local experts and academicians.

Concept note-2: -It is centred on three key areas – digital infrastructure as a utility to every citizen, governance and services on demand, and digital empowerment of citizens.

Concept note-3: -The award was conferred by President of India, Droupadi Murmu. Central Board of Secondary Education has won Digital India Awards 2022- Gold. The award was conferred by President of India, Droupadi Murmu to the Board. CBSE won the award in the Data Sharing and Use for Socio Economic Development Category.
