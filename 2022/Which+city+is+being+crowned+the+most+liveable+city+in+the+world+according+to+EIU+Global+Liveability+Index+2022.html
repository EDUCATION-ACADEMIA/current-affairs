Which city is being crowned the “most liveable city” in the world according to EIU Global Liveability Index 2022?

(A) ** Vienna

(B) Valletta

(C) Sydney

(D) Canberra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Austria’s capital, Vienna, was ranked the most liveable city in 2022 among the 172 cities surveyed by The Economist Intelligence Unit, having previously won in 2018 and 2019. Auckland was ranked the most liveable city in 2021.

Concept note-2: -For the third time in the past five years, Vienna captured the first spot in the 2022 Global Liveability Index, the annual report published by The Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU).

Concept note-3: -Topping the annual list of liveable cities for 2022 is Vienna, Austria, followed by Copenhagen, Denmark. The Swiss city of Zurich claims the third spot and Canada’s Calgary comes in fourth.

Concept note-4: -The Global Liveability Index 2022 was announced by the European Intelligence Unit (EIU) on June 24, 2022. It assessed 173 cities based on their living conditions and, in general, citizen living standards.
