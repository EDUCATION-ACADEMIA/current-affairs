Which city will host India’s first International Cruise Conference?

(A) Delhi

(B) Kolkata

(C) Chennai

(D) ** Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -May 14-15, 2022, Hotel Trident, Nariman Point, Mumbai, India.

Concept note-2: -Objectives: Incredible India International Cruise Conference aims to- Showcase India as a desired destination for cruise passengers, Highlight the regional connectivity, Promote building of new destinations and attractions like lighthouses

Concept note-3: -Mumbai (aka Bombay) is a major Indian Ocean port and capital city of India’s Maharashtra state (country’s second-most populous and the world’s 9th most populous).
