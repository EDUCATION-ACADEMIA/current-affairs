(A) Agra

(B) Lucknow

(C) ** Ayodhya

(D) Kanpur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ealrlier, Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath inaugurated an intersection in Ayodhya named after Lata Mangeshkar on the occasion of her 93rd birth anniversary.
