Which company is the first to obtain captive 5G spectrum directly to deploy solutions globally for the parent group?

(A) ** L & T Tech

(B) Wipro

(C) Infosys

(D) Tech Mahindra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -L&T Technology Services has become the first technology company to publicly express its interest in the government’s direct allocation of spectrum for captive 5G networks.

Concept note-2: -Notes: L&T Tech first to obtain captive 5G spectrum directly, deploy solutions globally for parent group. To build use cases on the technology, it will obtain spectrum to set up a 5G non-public network.

Concept note-3: -L&T Tech: First company to directly obtain and implement 5G spectrum.

Concept note-4: -Airtel is the first company to offer 5G to users in India as the company’s Chairman Suni Mittal announced that 5G mobile services are being rolled out to select cities.

Concept note-5: -Only six businesses have been assigned 31 percent of 5G patents. Samsung occupies a large revenue share of the market, with over 600 patents, and is followed by Intel, Nokia, Huawei, Ericsson, and ZTE. The top 10 companies leading the 5G network market are: Samsung.
