Which company signed an MoU with a Belarusian Company and its subsidiary for the supply of ADS for helicopters of the IAF?

(A) ** Bharat Electronics Limited

(B) Engineers India Limited

(C) Bharat Dynamics

(D) Hindustan Aeronautics Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BEL today signed an MoU with Defense Initiatives, Belarus, & its subsidiary Defense Initiatives Aero Pvt Ltd, India. The MoU aims at co-operation for supply of Airborne Defense Suite for IAF helicopters.

Concept note-2: -Notes: Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) signed an MoU with a Belarusian Company and its subsidiary for supply of Airborne Defence Suite (ADS) for helicopters of the Indian Air Force (IAF).

Concept note-3: -Bharat Electronics Limited on Friday signed an MoU with a Belarusian company and its subsidiary for supply of Airborne Defense Suite (ADS) for helicopters of the Indian Air Force (IAF).

Concept note-4: -Bharat Electronics signs MoU with Belarus firm for the supply of Airborne Defense Suite - The Hindu BusinessLine.

Concept note-5: -Defence public sector undertaking Bharat Electronics Ltd (BEL) has signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with Aerosense Technologies Pvt Ltd for cooperation in the development and marketing of drones and soft kill aerial anti-drone.
