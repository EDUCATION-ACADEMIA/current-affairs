Which country backed India for Permanent UNSC Membership again?

(A) Israel

(B) Japan

(C) Libya

(D) ** Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The UK, France, and UAE have extended their support for India’s permanent membership at the United Nations Security Council (UNSC). India is holding the presidency of United Nations Security Council for the month of December 2022.

Concept note-2: -Among the five permanent members of the 15-nation Council, US, UK, France and Russia have supported a permanent seat for India in the UN body.

Concept note-3: -India’s Bid For Permanent Seat At UN Security Council Receives Support From France, UK. Among the five permanent members of the 15-nation Council, the US, UK, France and Russia have supported a permanent seat for India in the UN body.

Concept note-4: -Russia supports India becoming UNSC permanent member: Ambassador. Russia also appreciates the ideas on the consensus-based response to the challenges with regard to emerging energy and food crises as well as through sustainable development needs.

Concept note-5: -In the past, India’s was offered to join the UNSC by both the superpowers, the US and the then Soviet Union in 1950 and in 1955 respectively, However, India denied the offer due to Cold war politics in that era.
