Which country has become the first in the Pacific to host World Hindi Conference?

(A) The Marshall Islands

(B) ** Fiji

(C) New Zealand

(D) Kiribati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indeed Fiji feels blessed and privileged to be the first country in the Pacific to host this prestigious event,” said Fijian Secretary Anjeela Jokhan. “We will do our best to make this conference a grand success. Fiji Hindi is in fact enshrined in the Fiji Constitution as one of our official languages.

Concept note-2: -Dubbed the “World Hindi Conference,” it was first organised in 1975 in Nagpur. A total of 122 representatives from 30 nations attended the conference.

Concept note-3: -The Minister of State for External Affairs V Muraleedharan has announced at an event in New Delhi on 27 October 2022 that the 12th World Hindi Conference will be held in Nadi, Fiji from 15-17 February 2023.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi: The 12th World Hindi Conference is being organized by the Ministry of External Affairs in collaboration with the Government of Fiji from 15 to 17 February 2023 in Fiji.

Concept note-5: -In 1973, Rashtra bhasha Prachar Samiti, Wardha, had the idea for the inaugural World Hindi Conference. On January 10, 1975, Nagpur hosted the event. The goal was to spread the language throughout the world. Total of 122 representatives attended the conference from 30 nations.
