Which country has decided to expand the testing capacity by distributing rapid COVID-19 tests?

(A) ** USA

(B) UAE

(C) China

(D) Nepal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Irene Bosch developed a quick, inexpensive COVID-19 test in early 2020. The Harvard-trained scientist already had a factory set up.

Concept note-2: -When is it Recommended to Use a Rapid Antigen Test? Rapid antigen tests are recommended for people with symptoms. If you are symptomatic: A positive rapid antigen test is highly indicative that you have COVID-19, and you are required to self-isolate, in accordance with guidance.

Concept note-3: -Can I get a rapid test? Rapid tests are acceptable if they are a viral test that meet the requirements under the Order.

Concept note-4: -The test correctly identified 98.1% (102 out of 104) SARS-CoV-2 positive nasal samples with a confidence interval of 93.2% to 99.8% (known as test sensitivity).
