Which country has launched a new batch of 3 Yagon-35 remote sensing satellites?

(A) Vietnam

(B) Japan

(C) South Korea

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -China launches third group of Yaogan-35 remote sensing satellites - CGTN.

Concept note-2: -Notes: China successfully launched three new remote sensing satellites, belonging to the Yaogan-35 family.

Concept note-3: -Answers. China launched a remote sensing VRSS-2 satellite for Venezuela from the Jiuquan Satellite Launch Center in Gobi desert. VRSS -2 Satellite was launched by China’s Long March-2D carrier rocket.

Concept note-4: -China sent a new remote sensing satellite of the Yaogan-34 series into space atop a Long March-4C carrier rocket from Jiuquan Satellite Launch Center in northwest China at 09:38 a.m. on Tuesday. The Yaogan-34 03 satellite has successfully entered its planned orbit, according to China Media Group (CMG).
