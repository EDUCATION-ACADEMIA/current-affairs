Which country is designated as the Guest of Honour Country at the Paris Book Festival 2022?

(A) USA

(B) France

(C) China

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. India has been designated as the Guest of Honour at Paris Livre (Paris Book Fair ) in 2020. France will be the Guest Country at the New Delhi World Book Fair 2022.

Concept note-2: -After being cancelled for two years in a row owing to the Covid pandemic, the Paris Book Festival, France’s main literary event, was held in Paris – and welcomed India as its guest of honour country.

Concept note-3: -Visit the India Pavilion at the Grand Palais Ephémère, Paris, France from Thursday, April 21 to Sunday, April 24, 2022 from 10:00am to 8:00pm.

Concept note-4: -The International Kolkata Book Fair is the world’s largest book fair by footfall, and over 25 lakhs footfalls have been recorded in the book fair in the past. Last year, despite the pandemic, the book fair noted a footfall of 20 lakhs.
