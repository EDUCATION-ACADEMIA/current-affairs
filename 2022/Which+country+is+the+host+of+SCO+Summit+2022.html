Which country is the host of SCO Summit 2022?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) India

(C) Kazakhstan

(D) ** Uzbekistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shanghai Cooperation Organisation Summit 2022: key takeaways. Last week, the 22nd Meeting of the Council of Heads of State of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) took place in Samarkand, Uzbekistan against the backdrop of geopolitical and economic uncertainty.

Concept note-2: -Samarkand, Uzbekistan. The 2022 annual summit of the Shangai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Heads of State Council will be held on September 15-16 in Samarkand.

Concept note-3: -Visit the website for the latest information on India as SCO Chair, calendar of events and other updates!” India received the SCO rotating presidency yesterday in Samarkand, Uzbekistan. India will hold the presidency of the grouping for a year until September 2023.

Concept note-4: -India has taken over as Chairperson of the SCO for 2023, and will host leaders of all SCO countries at a summit in Delhi expected in mid-2023. Earlier, the SCO summit 2022 was held recently in Samarkand, Uzbekistan.

Concept note-5: -Samarkand: Uzbekistan on Friday handed over the rotating presidency of the eight-member Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) to India in this historic city of Samarkand. Uzbek President Shavkat Mirziyoyev chaired the 22nd SCO summit in Samarkand which was attended by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.
