Which country is the host of the 19th Conference of the Parties to CITES?

(A) Japan

(B) ** Panama

(C) Maldives

(D) Papua New Guinea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 19th Meeting of the Conference of the Parties (CoP 19) to the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora (CITES) is being held at Panama City from 14th November to 25th November 2022.

Concept note-2: -The 19th meeting of the Conference of the Parties to the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora (CITES CoP19) is expected to take place in Panama in 2022.

Concept note-3: -All member states of the United Nations are party to the treaty, with the exception of Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, Federated States of Micronesia, Haiti, Kiribati, Marshall Islands, Nauru, South Sudan, East Timor, Turkmenistan, and Tuvalu. UN observer the Holy See is also not a member.

Concept note-4: -Currently there are 184 Parties. List of Parties in alphabetical or chronological order.
