Which country will host the 21st WCOA 2022?

(A) Brazil

(B) China

(C) Russia

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICAI is delighted to host the 21st World Congress of Accountants scheduled to be held from November 18-21, 2022 at Jio World Centre, Mumbai, India, in Hybrid mode.

Concept note-2: -The Institute of Chartered Accountant of India (ICAI) is hosting the 21st World Congress of Accountants 2022(WCOA) at the Jio World Centre, Mumbai, from November 18-21, 2022 in a Hybrid mode. WCOA 2022 is being held in hybrid mode for the first time ever in the Congress’s history.

Concept note-3: -Excitement is building and registration is open for hybrid World Congress of Accountants 2022, hosted by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India in Mumbai, India, November 18-21, 2022.

Concept note-4: -The 21st World Congress of Accountants will be held in Mumbai, India, in November 2022. The host organization is the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India.

Concept note-5: -The theme of the 21st World Congress of Accountants (WCOA) 2022 is: Building Trust Enabling Sustainability”.
