Which film bagged “Best Film award” at the IFFM 2022?

(A) Sardar Udham

(B) Gangubai Kathiawadi

(C) ** 83

(D) Jai Bhim

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kabir Khan was named the best director for 83, which also won the award for the best film. Ranveer thanked the IFFM jury members for bestowing on him the honour for 83, which he described as “one of the most loved films in my career”. “It’ll always be one of the most cherished films in my filmography.

Concept note-2: -In the series section, Nikkhil Advani’s Mumbai Diaries 26/11 was named the best series along with lead star Mohit Raina winning the title of best actor in a series.

Concept note-3: -Here are the winners of the film festival: No End - Nader Saeivar, Best Director. Best Male Actor - Vahid Mobasheri, No End. Best Female Actress - Daniela Marin Navarro, I Have Electric Dreams. Behind the Haystacks by Asimina Proedrou, is the Best Debut Feature Film by a new Director.

Concept note-4: -Suriya’s superhit Tamil film ‘Soorarai Pottru’ also received the Best Feature Film award and the Best Actress award was taken by Aparna Murali. ‘Soorarai Pottru’ was the big winner of this year’s National Awards.
