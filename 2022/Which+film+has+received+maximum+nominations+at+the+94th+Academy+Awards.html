Which film has received maximum nominations at the 94th Academy Awards?

(A) Belfast

(B) ** The Power Of The Dog

(C) West Side Story

(D) Dune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The nominees for the 94th Academy Awards were announced on February 8, 2022, by actors Leslie Jordan and Tracee Ellis Ross. The Power of the Dog led all nominees with twelve nominations; Dune came in second with ten.

Concept note-2: -In 2022, “The Power of the Dog” had the most nominations for the Academy Awards: a total of 12. It ended up winning in the Best Achievement in Directing category. It was the second time filmmaker Jane Campion received an Academy Award, and the only Oscar win for a Netflix movie that year.

Concept note-3: -What did make it to the final list was Indian documentary feature Writing With Fire, which bagged a nomination at the 94th edition of the Academy Awards. Directed by Rintu Thomas and Sushmit Ghosh, Writing With Fire was the first Indian feature documentary to be nominated for the Oscars.
