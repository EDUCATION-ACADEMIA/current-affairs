Which film has won the Best Picture award at the 94th Academy Awards?

(A) King Richard

(B) ** CODA

(C) Belfast

(D) The Power of the Dog

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is CODA. CODA movie won the award for Best Picture at the 94th Academy Awards. The movie is directed by Director Sian Heder.

Concept note-2: -02/8RRR. SS Rajamouli’s ‘RRR’ featured on the Academy’s list of productions eligible for Oscar nominations at the 95th Academy Awards.
