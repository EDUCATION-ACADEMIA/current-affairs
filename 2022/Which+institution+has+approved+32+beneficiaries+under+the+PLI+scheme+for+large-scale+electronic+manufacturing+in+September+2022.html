Which institution has approved 32 beneficiaries under the PLI scheme for large-scale electronic manufacturing in September 2022?

(A) Ministry of Commerce

(B) CERC

(C) CEA

(D) ** NITI Aayog

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NITI Aayog led high-level panel approves 32 beneficiaries under PLI for large-scale electronics manufacturing. The Aayog claimed that the scheme is expected to bring in additional production to the tune of Rs 10,69,432 crore and generate employment for 7,00,000 people.

Concept note-2: -The PLI for Large-Scale Electronics Manufacturing sector has attracted leading global players, including Foxconn, Samsung, Pegatron, Rising Star and Wistron while leading domestic companies, including Lava, Micromax, Optiemus, United Telelinks Neolyncs and Padget Electronics, have also participated in this scheme.

Concept note-3: -Direct Beneficiaries Of PLI ’ Reliance Industries, Dixon Technologies Ltd., Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., Amber Enterprises Ltd. and TVS Motor Ltd.
