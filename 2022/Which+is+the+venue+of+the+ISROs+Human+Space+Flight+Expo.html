Which is the venue of the ISRO’s ‘Human Space Flight Expo’?

(A) Vishakhapatnam

(B) ** Bengaluru

(C) Ernakulam

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Human Space Flight Centre, Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) has organised Human Space Flight Expo, as part of Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav, at Jawaharlal Nehru planetarium in Bengaluru.

Concept note-2: -It is being held at the Jawaharlal Nehru Planetarium in Bengaluru.

Concept note-3: -About Human Space Flight Expo Human Space Flight Centre (HSFC), ISRO is organising “Human Space Flight Expo.” as part of “Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav.” This is a space-themed celebration to commemorate 75 years of Indian Independence.

Concept note-4: -India’s First Human Space Mission Gaganyaan’s Test Flights Scheduled For February 2023; All You Need To Know.

Concept note-5: -Gaganyaan project envisages demonstration of human spaceflight capability by launching crew of 3 members to an orbit of 400 km for a 3 days mission and bring them back safely to earth, by landing in Indian sea waters.
