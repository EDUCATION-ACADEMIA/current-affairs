Which ministry has organized the ‘Yoga Prabha’ program in New Delhi?

(A) Ministry of Minority Affairs

(B) ** Ministry of Civil Aviation

(C) Ministry of Rural Development

(D) Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Civil Aviation said, Yog Prabha will help in creating awareness among the participants about the importance of Yoga and will motivate others to do Yoga on a daily basis.

Concept note-2: -Scindia and Dr. VK Singh led the Mega Yoga Event. The Ministry of Civil Aviation organized “Yog Prabha”, a mega Yoga event today at Safdarjung Airport, New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of AYUSH has been organizing International Day of Yoga (IDY) successfully, which has found great enthusiasm and got worldwide support. It is one of the noble initiatives taken by the Government of India under the dynamic leadership of Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi Ji.

Concept note-4: -Yog Prabha preludes IDY and will help create a wider awareness among the participants about importance of Yoga in daily life.

Concept note-5: -The theme of this year of IDY was “Yoga for Humanity”. The main event of the IDY- 2022 demonstration was held at Mysuru, Karnataka led by the Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi Ji. Get More !
