Which mobile banking app has been launched by Canara Bank?

(A) Canara di1

(B) Canara bi1

(C) Canara ci1

(D) ** Canara ai1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Canara Bank launches “Canara ai1” Mobile Banking Super App with more than 250 features integrated with the futuristic vision of “One Bank, One App”.

Concept note-2: -Kindly visit Play Store/App Store on your smart phone and search Canara Bank Mobile Banking application. Click on mobile app icon & press install. After successful installation open app by clicking new Canara mobile banking icon.

Concept note-3: -Description. With an objective to provide a hassle-free post-sales experience, the mServ app empowers retailers to assist customers effectively. Without the need for a messy paper trail nor the need to call. The app is also equipped to facilitate a transparent trade-in program for smartphones.

Concept note-4: -What is CanDigital? CanDigital, secured by REL-ID, is a next generation secure digital banking app for all your online banking needs. It provides the convenience, security and uniform experience across all devices including desktops, laptops, smart phones and tablets through the apps medium to deliver services.
