Which nation has imposed sanctions on Russian banks and individuals?

(A) France

(B) ** UK

(C) US

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International sanctions have been imposed against Russia and Crimea during the Russo-Ukrainian War by a large number of countries, including the United States, Canada, the European Union, and international organisations following the Russian annexation of Crimea , which began in late February 2014.

Concept note-2: -The Department of State is designating 10 Russian naval entities. This action imposes severe costs on Russia’s defense establishment in response to Putin’s unprovoked war of choice in Ukraine.

Concept note-3: -Prior to 2022, the United States had imposed sanctions on Russia in response to Moscow’s 2014 invasion of Ukraine and other malign activities.

Concept note-4: -More than 30 countries have imposed sanctions against Russia, cutting energy imports, blocking financial transactions and halting shipments of key imports, such as semiconductors and other electronics.
