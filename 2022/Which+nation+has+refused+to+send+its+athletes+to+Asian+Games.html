Which nation has refused to send its athletes to Asian Games?

(A) Japan

(B) ** Australia

(C) Indonesia

(D) South Korea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Forty-six nations have participated in the Games, including Israel, which was excluded from the Games after their last participation in 1974. The most recent games was held in Jakarta and Palembang, Indonesia from 18 August to 2 September 2018. The next games are scheduled to be held in Hangzhou, China.

Concept note-2: -Abhishek Mishra. Australia and New Zealand will not be sending their athletes to the Asian Games that will be held in Hangzhou, China in September this year, the Australian Olympic Committee (AOC), released a statement on Tuesday.

Concept note-3: -Australia will not send athletes to the 2022 Asian Games due to the COVID-19 outbreak, which is a setback for the organising committee. Hangzhou in China will be the host city for the continental competition that takes place from 10 September 2022 to 25 September 2022.

Concept note-4: -Saudi Arabia was the sole NOC to not send female athletes to the Games.
