Which nation’s President will visit Russia, Ukraine next week to mediate the crisis?

(A) ** France

(B) US

(C) Japan

(D) Denmark

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -France and its European partners will continue working to accompany Ukraine on its European path.

Concept note-2: -Putin was in India on a two-day official visit from January 25 with a view to boost- ing business and strategic ties and expanding cooperation in civilian nuclear energy. During his visit, the Russian leader held wideranging discus- sion with Prime Minister Manmohan Singh and called on President A.P.J. Abdul Kalam.

Concept note-3: -The UN said that it aims to increase the number of inter-agency convoys providing aid to parts of Ukraine “close to the frontlines”, in support of work being carried out by local organisations and volunteers. Also, the first UN aid convoy reached the vicinity of the battered eastern Ukrainian city of Soledar.

Concept note-4: -Moscow proclaimed its new objective as occupying all of Donbas, consisting of the oblasts (regions) of Luhansk and Donetsk, some 35% of which had already been occupied by Russian and Russian proxy forces in 2014 and 2015.
