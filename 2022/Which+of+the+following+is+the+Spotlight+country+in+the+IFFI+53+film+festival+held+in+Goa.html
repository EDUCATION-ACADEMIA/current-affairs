Which of the following is the ‘Spotlight’ country in the IFFI 53 film festival held in Goa?

(A) Lebanon

(B) ** France

(C) South Korea

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -France is the ‘Spotlight’ country and 8 films will be screened under Country Focus package. The ‘Homage’ section will include fifteen Indian and five international films.

Concept note-2: -Eight movies were shown as part of the Country Focus package, with France serving as the “spotlight” nation. ii. The opening and closing films of the IFFI 2022 festival were the Austrian film ‘Alma and Oskar’, directed by Dieter Berner, and Krzysztof Zanussi’s ‘Perfect Number’.

Concept note-3: -France is the country of focus in the festival with eight films of the country included in ‘country of focus’ section. On the occasion of the 75th Anniversary of Indian Independence, India was named the ‘Country of Honour’ at the Marche du Cinema of the Cannes Film Festival this year.

Concept note-4: -Bangladesh was the country in focus at the 51st International Film Festival of India held in Goa in January 2021. 51st International Film Festival of India (IFF) has announced a line-up of programs that will be showcased on the Over-the-top (OTT) platform. The festival was held in Goa from 16th to 24th January 2021.
