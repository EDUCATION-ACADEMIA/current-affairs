Which one is the venue of the ‘Bharat Bhagya Vidhata’ festival?

(A) Lucknow

(B) ** Delhi

(C) Sonipat

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which is the venue of the ‘Bharat Bhagya Vidhata’ festival? Notes: The ten-day mega Red Fort Festival titled ‘Bharat Bhagya Vidhata’ commenced recently at Red Fort in New Delhi. Union Women and Child Development Minister Smriti Irani inaugurated the festival.

Concept note-2: -From societies and educational institutions to workplaces, Independence Day will be celebrated with pomp and flair across the country. However, all eyes remain glued to the Red Fort in Delhi, which acts as the setting for Independence Day celebrations each year.

Concept note-3: -“Bharat Bhagya Vidhata” is a phrase from India’s national anthem “Jana Gana Mana” and is about those people who have contributed towards eliminating the fear and other forms of darkness from the people and serve the motherland.

Concept note-4: -The festival aims to commemorate the heritage of the country and culture of every part of India. The festival Bharat Bhagya Vidhata will help everyone to appreciate the diversity of India.
