Which organization has launched the first fully private astronaut mission to the International Space Station?

(A) ISRO

(B) NASA

(C) ** SpaceX

(D) JAXA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which organisation launched the first fully private astronaut mission to the International Space Station? Notes: A SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket launched Ax-1 , a mission from Houston-based Company Axiom Space. It is the first fully private astronaut mission to the International Space Station.

Concept note-2: -The 1st private astronaut mission to International Space Station is back on Earth. The SpaceX crew seated in the Dragon spacecraft earlier this month in Cape Canaveral, Fla. CAPE CANAVERAL, Fla.

Concept note-3: -Space X is the first private space company to send astronauts into space.

Concept note-4: -On Apr. 8, 2022, SpaceX and Houston company Axiom Space made history with the launch of Ax-1, the first all-private mission to the International Space Station. SpaceX launched the four-person crew on a 10-day mission to space, which included an 8-day stay aboard the space station.

Concept note-5: -CAPE CANAVERAL, Fla. (AP) - SpaceX launched four astronauts to the International Space Station for NASA on Wednesday, less than two days after completing a flight chartered by millionaires.
