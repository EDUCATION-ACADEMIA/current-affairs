Which organization has launched the military drill ‘Cold Response 2022’?

(A) International Development Association

(B) ** North Atlantic Treaty Organisation

(C) United Nations Institute for Disarmament Research

(D) Association of South East Nations

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cold Response 2022 (CR 22) was a Norwegian military exercise with invited allies and partner nations. The field exercise started on 14 March and ended on 31 March 2022. The first allied troops arrived in Norway autumn of 2021 and winter of 2022 to train and prepare for the exercise.

Concept note-2: -Cold Response is a bi-annual drill organised and hosted by the Norwegian Armed Forces since 2006 – with the exception of 2020, when the exercise was cancelled because of the COVID-19 pandemic, making this year’s edition even more important.

Concept note-3: -Cold Response 2022 is organised by the Norwegian Armed Forces. Learn more about the exercise on their website.

Concept note-4: -This follows Russia’s quadrennial military exercises known as Vostok (East), which took place from 1–7 September. This year, they featured units from China, India, Laos, Mongolia, Nicaragua and Syria, and around 50,000 personnel.

Concept note-5: -Ex Dharma Guardian – 2022 is a joint military exercise between India and Japan, is being conducted at the Foreign Training Node in Belagavi.
