Which photographer has won the 2022 World Press Photo of the Year award?

(A) Mads Nissen

(B) ** Amber Bracken

(C) John Moore

(D) Yasuyoshi Chiba

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Photographer Senthil Kumaran’s telling images from 10 years of covering the human-tiger conflict in India, have won him the prestigious World Press Photo 2022 award in the Long Term Story Category (Asia Region)

Concept note-2: -Congratulations to Adam Ferguson (Australia) for being awarded this year’s Photographer of the Year title for his series Migrantes.

Concept note-3: -On Tuesday, American photographer Karine Aigner was announced as this year’s winner of the grand title for her image called “The Big Buzz". Ms Aigner became only the fifth woman to win the top prize in the 58-year-old history of the competition.

Concept note-4: -The winner and Photographer of the Year 2022 is Marcus Yam (USA) with his image “Afghanistan’s air force is a rare U.S.-backed success story.
