Which player has won the Sportstar of the Year (Female) award at the 2022 Sportstar Aces Awards?

(A) Lovlina Borgohain

(B) ** Mirabai Chanu

(C) Savita

(D) Avani Lekhara

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mirabai Chanu was adjudged the Sportstar of the Year (Female) at the 2022 Sportstar Aces Awards at the Taj Mahal Hotel in Mumbai on Saturday. She also won Sportswoman of the Year (Individual Sports) award, which she shared with Lovlina Borgohain, 2020 Tokyo boxing bronze medallist.

Concept note-2: -Beth Mead guided England to Women’s Euro victory in the summer and has won the BBC Sports Personality of the Year 2022 award. The Arsenal attacker and Lionesses star scored six goals and earned the Golden Boot during the successful campaign.

Concept note-3: -The way Nikhat has bossed the year, going undefeated in every major event and winning all three titles via unanimous verdicts makes her ESPN India’s female Athlete of the Year (2022).

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Neeraj Chopra. Neeraj Chopra claimed the ‘Sportstar of the Year (Male)’ award at the 2022 Sportstar Aces Awards in March 2022. Mirabai Chanu, who claimed the silver medal at the Tokyo Olympics, received the ‘Sportstar of the Year (Female)’ award.
