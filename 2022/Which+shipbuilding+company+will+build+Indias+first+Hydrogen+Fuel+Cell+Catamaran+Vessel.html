Which shipbuilding company will build India’s first Hydrogen Fuel Cell Catamaran Vessel?

(A) ** Cochin Shipyard

(B) Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders

(C) Hindustan Shipyard

(D) Garden Reach Shipbuilders

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cochin Shipyard has signed an MoU with Inland Waterways Authority of India to build the country’s first hydrogen fuel cell catamaran vessel for Varanasi in Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -LARSEN & TOUBRO LTD (L&T) Engineering major L&T commissioned a green hydrogen plant at its AM Naik Heavy Engineering Complex in Hazira, Gujarat in August 2022. The plant produces 45 Kg of green hydrogen on a daily basis, which is used for captive consumption in the company’s Hazira manufacturing complex.

Concept note-3: -Within the Green Hydrogen sector, the top gainers were ADANI ENTERPRISES (up 14.5%) and OIL INDIA (up 4.5%). On the other hand, BPCL (down 0.5%) and NTPC (down 0.4%) were among the top losers.

Concept note-4: -KOCHI: The Cochin Shipyard Ltd (CSL) will build the country’s first indigenous hydrogen fuel cell catamaran vessel for the spiritual city of Varanasi.

Concept note-5: -INS Vikrant Cochin Shipyard built India’s first indigenous aircraft carrier. INS Vikrant (formerly, the Project 71 “Air Defence Ship") is the first aircraft carrier of the Indian Navy to be designed and built in India. The carrier will be the largest warship built by CSL.
