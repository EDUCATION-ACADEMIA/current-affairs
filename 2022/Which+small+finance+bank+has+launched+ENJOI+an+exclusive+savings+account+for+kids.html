Which small finance bank has launched ENJOI an exclusive savings account for kids?

(A) Capital small finance bank

(B) ** Equitas small finance bank

(C) Janlakshmi small finance bank

(D) Utkarsh small finance bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Equitas Small Finance Bank has launched a special savings account named ‘ENJOI’ with an aim to introduce “young children” to the financial world. It was launched on June 19 on the occasion of Father’s Day. ENJOI will allow 0-18 year olds to open savings accounts under the supervision of their parents.

Concept note-2: -Equitas Small Finance Bank on Thursday announced that it is all set to launch an exclusive savings account for kids named ‘ENJOI’. The savings account is aimed at introducing young kids to the financial world and encourage them to develop the saving habit early.

Concept note-3: -Features of ENJOI saving Accounts: ENJOI will allow kids of 0-18 years to open savings accounts under the supervision of their parents. Minors aged 10 and above will also get the option for a personalised debit card.
