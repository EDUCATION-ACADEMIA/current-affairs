Which state CM has launched a new health & wealth app AAYU?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Jharkhand

(D) ** Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains Q. Which state launched a new health and wellness app ‘AAYU’? Notes: Karnataka Chief Minister Basavaraj Bommai launched a new health and wellness app AAYU to address and heal chronic diseases and lifestyle disorders through yoga and meditation.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Karnataka. Karnataka Chief Minister Basavaraj Bommai on 29 May 2022 launched a new health and wellness app AAYU to address and help heal chronic diseases and lifestyle disorders through yoga and meditation.
