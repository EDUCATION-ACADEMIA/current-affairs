[SOURCES]

Which state Cabinet approved the bill to create Safai Karamchari Commission?

(A) Gujarat

(B) Rajasthan

(C) Karnataka

(D) ** Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ACT NO. 64 OF 1993 [4th September, 1993.] An Act to constitute a National Commission for Safai Karamcharis and to provide for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.

Concept note-2: -The National Commission for Safai Karamcharis (NCSK) was constituted on 12th August, 1994 as a statutory body by an Act of Parliament viz. ‘National Commission for Safai Karamcharis Act, 1993’, for a period of three years i.e. up to 31st March, 1997.

Concept note-3: -The National Commission for Safai Karamcharis is currently a non-statutory body of the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment. Earlier it was a statutory body which was established by ‘National Commission for Safai Karamcharis Act 1993’ on 12th August 1994.

Concept note-4: -Shri M. Venkatesan, Chairman for National Commission for Safai Karamcharis called on Hon’ble Governor at Raj Bhavan | RAJ BHAVAN.
