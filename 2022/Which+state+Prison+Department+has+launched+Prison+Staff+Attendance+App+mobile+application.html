Which state Prison Department has launched Prison Staff Attendance App mobile application?

(A) Assam

(B) ** Nagaland

(C) Manipur

(D) Meghalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nagaland Prison Department goes digital by introducing the mobile attendance application for Prison staff.

Concept note-2: -The Prison Staff Attendance is a mobile app along with an admin portal that aims to provide a comprehensive attendance management system that can make processes hassle-free and provide a seamlessly integrated system for monitoring and tracking the employee’s attendance.

Concept note-3: -Presently there are Forty(40) Jails functional in the Province including One(1) High Security Prison, Nine(9) Central Jails, Twenty Five(25) District Jails, Two(2) Borstal Institutions & Juvenile Jails, One(1) Women Jail and Two(2) Sub Jails.
