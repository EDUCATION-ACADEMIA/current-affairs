Which state government has launched ‘Fruits’ software?

(A) Punjab

(B) ** Karnataka

(C) Kerala

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Karnataka. The Karnataka government has launched software for Aadhar-based, single-window registration for the schemes. It is called the “Farmer Registration and Unified Beneficiary Information System” or FRUITS software.

Concept note-2: -DPAR e Governance department in association with NIC has developed a software application called Farmer Registration &Unified Beneficiary Information System – FRUITS.

Concept note-3: -The Karnataka government has launched ‘The Farmer Registration & Unified Beneficiary Information System’ or FRUITS software for Aadhar-based, single-window registration for the schemes.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Karnataka. Karnataka government has unveiled the Farmer Registration and Unified beneficiary Information System (FRUITS). FRUITS is an e-governance portal to create a repository of farmland information and farm loan details on a single platform.

Concept note-5: -Notes: Karnataka Vikas Grameena Bank (KVGB), in association with the Karnataka government, has launched ‘Farmer Registration and Unified Beneficiary Information System’ (FRUITS) portal.
