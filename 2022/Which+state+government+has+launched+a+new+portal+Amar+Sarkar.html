Which state government has launched a new portal ‘Amar Sarkar’?

(A) Bihar

(B) Assam

(C) ** Tripura

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The issues registered can be viewed by the users of Line Departments based on their jurisdiction.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains Notes: The Tripura Chief Minister Dr Manik Saha launched the ‘Amar Sarkar’ web portal in the capital city Agartala.

Concept note-3: -Amar completed master’s degrees in psychology at the University of Oxford (Brasenose College) and neuroscience at the University of Cambridge (Trinity College).

Concept note-4: -Q. Which state has launched the portal named “Akanksha”? Notes: The Chief Minister of Karnataka B S Yediyurappa has launched a portal named Akanksha, which would provide details on all the Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) activities in Karnataka.

Concept note-5: -Maneka Gandhi, the union minister of Women and Child Development (WCD), inaugurated an online portal NARI in New Delhi for the empowerment of women.
