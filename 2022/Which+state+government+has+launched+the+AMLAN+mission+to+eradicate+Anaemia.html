Which state government has launched the AMLAN mission to eradicate Anaemia?

(A) Karnataka

(B) Kerala

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In a bid to ensure complete eradication of anaemia problem among women and children in Odisha, Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik has launched AMLAN- ‘Anaemia Mukta Lakhya Abhiyan’ in the state.

Concept note-2: -The Anemia Mukt Bharat strategy will be implemented in all villages, blocks, and districts of all the States/UTs of India through existing delivery platforms as envisaged in the National Iron Plus Initiative (NIPI) and Weekly Iron Folic Acid Supplementation (WIFS) programme.

Concept note-3: -BHUBANESWAR: The Odisha government on Wednesday launched Anaemia Mukta Lakhya Abhiyan (Amlan), a multi-departmental campaign to reduce anaemia or haemoglobin deficiency in the state.

Concept note-4: -Around half of WRA in Odisha are anemic (51.0%). The prevalence of anemia among women is high across subgroups: those who are breastfeeding (54.8%), pregnant (47.6%), and WRA who are neither breastfeeding nor pregnant (50.3%).
