Which state government has partnered with NPCI and SBI to implement “E-RUPI”?

(A) Gujarat government

(B) Government of Punjab

(C) ** Government of Karnataka

(D) Government of Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which state collaborated with NPCI and SBI to launch the ‘e-RUPI’ payment mechanism for students? Notes: Recently ,The government of Karnataka has partnered with the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) & the State Bank of India (SBI). They have partnered to launch an ‘e-RUPI’ payment solution.

Concept note-2: -Karnataka partners with NPCI, SBI to launch e-RUPI digital platform, pay scholarship fees.

Concept note-3: -National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) in association with Department of Financial Services (DFS), National Health Authority (NHA), Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (MoHFW), and partner banks, has launched an innovative digital solution – ‘e-RUPI’.

Concept note-4: -On 2nd August 2021, Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi launched the digital payment solution e-RUPI - a cashless and contactless instrument for digital payment.
