Which state has won the first prize in the National MSME Awards For Outstanding Development of the MSME Sector?

(A) Telangana

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains Notes: The Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) Department, Government of Odisha has been awarded first prize in the category “National MSME Award 2022 to States/UTs for outstanding contribution in the promotion and Development of MSME Sector”. Bihar and Haryana were second and third respectively.

Concept note-2: -The Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) Department, Odisha has been awarded first prize in the category “National MSME Award 2022 to States/UTs for outstanding contribution in the promotion.

Concept note-3: -The ET MSME Awards 2022, a flagship programme of the Economic Times Digital is arguably India’s most unbiased initiative to celebrate and recognise Indian top micro, small, and medium enterprises for their exceptional achievement in business performance and contributions to the country’s socio-economic growth.
