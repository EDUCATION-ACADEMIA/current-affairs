Which state hosted the ‘Conclave of State S & T Ministers’?

(A) Bihar

(B) Maharashtra

(C) ** Gujarat

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The two-day Gujarat Science Conclave of State Science & Technology (S&T) Ministers, to be inaugurated by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, at Science City Ahmedabad will explore State specific technologies and innovations for adoption and scaling up through integrated approach with Centre’s collaboration.

Concept note-2: -PM inaugurates Centre-State Science Conclave in Ahmedabad via video conferencing. The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated the ‘Centre-State Science Conclave’ in Ahmedabad via video conferencing.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the ‘Centre-State Science Conclave’, in Ahmedabad. Prime Minister Narendra Modi addressed the event via video conferencing. Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the ‘Centre-State Science Conclave’, in Ahmedabad.

Concept note-4: -Dr Jitendra Singh, has taken over as the Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Science and Technology and Earth Sciences as the Ministries were included under the charge of Office of the Prime Minister.
