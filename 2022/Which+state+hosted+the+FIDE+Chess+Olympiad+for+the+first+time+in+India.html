Which state hosted the FIDE Chess Olympiad for the first time in India?

(A) Odisha

(B) Maharashtra

(C) ** Tamil Nadu

(D) Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 44th Chess Olympiad (also known as the Indian Chess Olympiad), was organised by the Fédération Internationale des Échecs (FIDE) in Chennai, the state capital of Tamil Nadu, India from 28 July to 10 August 2022.

Concept note-2: -The 1st Chess Olympiad, organized by the FIDE and comprising an open and women’s tournament, as well as several events designed to promote the game of chess, took place between 18 and 30 July, 1927 at the Westminster Central Hall, London, United Kingdom. It was officially known by its current name from 1952.

Concept note-3: -Reigning world champion Magnus Carlsen will represent Norway while Viswanathan Anand will mentor the 30-member Indian team. Watch live! One of the biggest chess events in the world, the FIDE Chess Olympiad 2022 is set to start on July 28 in Chennai and will run till August 10.

Concept note-4: -The 44th edition of the FIDE Chess Olympiad 2022 is currently being hosted by India for the first time.
