Which state launched the ‘Vidya Rath-School on Wheels’ project?

(A) Arunachal Pradesh

(B) ** Assam

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vidya Rath: School on Wheels project launched for economically weak children in Assam. Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma attended the launch of the Vidya Rath- School on Wheels project on the occasion of India’s 76th Independence day at a function held at the premises of Gauhati High Court.

Concept note-2: -Guwahati: Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma launched Vidya Rath-School on Wheels, a project aimed at imparting elementary education to economically challenged children of the society.

Concept note-3: -Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma on Monday inaugurated the ‘Vidyarath’ mission aimed at providing education by motile means at Gauhati High Court.

Concept note-4: -Sarma inaugurated the schools at Ghagra, Rupajuli, Sonajuli, Thakurbari and Fulbari tea gardens in Sonitpur district.
