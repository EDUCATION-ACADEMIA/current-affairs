Which team has won the 71st Men’s Senior National Basketball Championship?

(A) ** Tamil Nadu

(B) Punjab

(C) Gujarat

(D) Indian Railways

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tamil Nadu defeated defending champion Punjab 87-69 in the final to win the men’s title in the 71st Senior National Basketball Championship on Sunday. The formidable Indian Railways team won the women’s title, beating Telangana 131-82 in convincing fashion, riding on Poonam Chaturvedi’s 26 points.

Concept note-2: -The Punjab team avenged the defeat it had suffered against Tamil Nadu in the 71st edition of the championship at Chennai, in which the team had lost 69-87 in the finals and had to be contented with the runners-up trophy.

Concept note-3: -Punjab Men and Indian Railways Women win 72nd Senior National Basketball Championship held at Udaipur. Indian Railway retained the title while Punjab regain the trophy from Tamil Nadu .

Concept note-4: -Women’s: Indian Railways team won the title after defeating Telangana.

Concept note-5: -71st Senior National Basketball Championship will be from 3rd to 10th April 2022 at Nehru Stadium, Chennai. 71st Senior National Basketball Championship will be from 3rd to 10th April 2022 at Nehru Stadium, Chennai.
