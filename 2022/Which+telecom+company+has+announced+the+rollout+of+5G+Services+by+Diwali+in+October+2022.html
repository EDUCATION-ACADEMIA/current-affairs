Which telecom company has announced the rollout of 5G Services by Diwali in October 2022?

(A) Vodafone Group

(B) Adani Data Networks

(C) ** Reliance Jio

(D) Bharti Airtel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Must Read. Reliance Jio has confirmed that 5G will first be available in four cities, including Kolkata, Mumbai, Delhi, and Varanasi by Diwali.

Concept note-2: -Airtel kicks off 5G services, Jio sets launch for Diwali - Times of India.

Concept note-3: -5G– the next generation of mobile broadband– was officially launched in India on October 1 this year, after years of waiting. It has been nearly two months since launch, and slowly 5G connectivity is being made available in many cities in the country by Airtel and Jio.

Concept note-4: -So far, Bharti Airtel and Reliance Jio have launched the 5G services in India in select cities. The 5G technology is expected to deliver ten times better download speed than that of 4G and up to three times greater spectrum efficiency.

Concept note-5: -Airtel 5G Launch Date Airtel speaks the 5G network will be accessible in major cities by December 2023. By March 2024, Airtel will be allowed to cover every town and key local region with 5G.
