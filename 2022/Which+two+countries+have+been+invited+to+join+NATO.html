Which two countries have been invited to join NATO?

(A) ** Finland and Sweden

(B) Israel and Egypt

(C) Australia and New Zealand

(D) South Korea and Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ratification process for Sweden and Finland began on 5 July 2022. As of 15 October 2022, all NATO member states except for Hungary and Turkey have approved the accession of the two countries and deposited their instruments of accession with the Government of the US.

Concept note-2: -Finland and Sweden, two long-neutral countries, were formally invited to join NATO in 2022.

Concept note-3: -How many countries comes in NATO? Ans. NATO is an organization which comprises 30 member countries.

Concept note-4: -Since then, 18 more European powers have joined: Albania, Bulgaria, Croatia, the Czech Republic, Estonia, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Latvia, Lithuania, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Poland, Turkey, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia and Spain.

Concept note-5: -Any prospective member must share NATO’s core values and have the capacity and willingness to contribute to security in the Euro-Atlantic area. Today, NATO has 30 members, who are stronger and safer together.
