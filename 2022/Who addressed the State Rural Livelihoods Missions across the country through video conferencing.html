Who addressed the State Rural Livelihoods Missions across the country through video conferencing?

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) ** Narendra Singh Tomar

(C) Smriti Irani

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -| Aajeevika - National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM) was launched by the Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD), Government of India in June 2011.

Concept note-2: -Deen Dayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM) The DAY-NRLM is essentially a poverty relief programme of the Central government. It was launched as ‘Aajeevika – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM)’ by the GOI’s Ministry of Rural Development in the year 2011.

Concept note-3: -The Department of Rural Development in the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India (GoI) has the overall responsibility of policy formulation, monitoring and evaluation of the programme and for release of funds.
