(A) K. Menon

(B) Udupi Ramachandra Rao

(C) G.Madhavan Nair

(D) ** K Sivan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: K Sivan, among 67 to receive Rajyotsava awards by Karnatakaawards. Former ISRO Chairman K Sivan, actors Dattanna, Avinash and Sihi KahiChandru are among 67 personalities who will be awarded Rajyotsava Award thisyear, by the Karnataka government.

Concept note-2: Ex-ISRO chief K Sivanamong 67 to get Rajyotsava Award.
