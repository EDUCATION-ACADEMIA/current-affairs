Who among the following singers will not get Lata Mangeshkar Award?

(A) Kumar Sanu

(B) Shailendra Singh

(C) Anand-Milind

(D) ** Shreya Ghoshal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Noted playback singers Kumar Sanu and Shailendra Singhand music-composer duo Anand-Milind were conferred with the NationalLata Mangeshkar Award.

Concept note-2: The correct answer is PMNarendra Modi. PM Narendra Modi on 24 April 2022 received the first LataDeenanath Mangeshkar Award. He was awarded for his selfless service to thecountry and society at the 80th annual Master Deenanath Mangeshkar Awardsceremony held in Mumbai.

Concept note-3: Noted music director andviolinist Uttam Singh has won the 2016 Lata Mangeshkar Award for LifetimeAchievement.

Concept note-4: Instituted in 1984, the National Lata MangeshkarAward is given to promote excellence in the area of light music annually by theMadhya Pradesh government’s culture department. It carries a cash prize of Rs 2lakh and a citation, officials said.
