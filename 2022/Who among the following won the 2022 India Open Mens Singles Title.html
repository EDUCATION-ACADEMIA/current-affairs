Who among the following won the 2022 India Open Men’s Singles Title?

(A) Loh Kean Yew

(B) Chirag Shetty

(C) Satwiksairaj Rankireddy

(D) ** Lakshya Sen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Men’s singles player Lakshya Sen and the men’s doubles pair of Chirag Shetty and Satwiksairaj Ranklireddy entered the tournament as the reigning champions, having won their respective events in the 2022 edition.

Concept note-2: -India Open Badminton: Korean sensation An Seyoung wins Women’s singles final.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Lakshya Sen. Lakshya Sen clinched his first Super 500 title in the final of the men’s singles final at the Yonex-Sunrise India Open, in New Delhi on 16 January 2022.
