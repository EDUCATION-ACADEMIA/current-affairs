Who approved USD 1 billion loan for Delhi-Meerut Regional Rapid Transit System, a 1st of its kind in India?

(A) ** ADB

(B) World Bank

(C) IBRD

(D) IDA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: ADB Approves $1 Billion Package for Rapid Transit System inIndia. The Asian Development Bank (ADB) has approved a $1 billion loan tosupport construction of the modern, high-speed Delhi–Meerut Regional RapidTransit System, a first of its kind in India.

Concept note-2: Notes: India’s firstRegional Rapid Transit System (RRTS) between Delhi and Meerut, is beingdesigned by global mobility provider Alstom in Hyderabad and manufacturedin Savli, Gujarat.

Concept note-3: Delhi-Meerut RRTS is India’s firstRRTS corridor and aims to reduce the commuting time between Delhi andMeerut to 50 minutes. The project entails an outlay of Rs 30,270 crore.Once operational, the RRTS train will have a designated speed of 180 kmphand an operational speed of 160 kmph.

Concept note-4: New Delhi:The first train set of India’s first Regional Rapid Transit System reachedDuhai Depot on Monday. This train set was brought by road on a trailerfrom the manufacturing plant located in Savli, Gujarat.
