Who became the National Ambassador of Ni-Kshay Mitra and the TB-free India campaign?

(A) Seema Devi

(B) Neetu Gupta

(C) ** Deepa Malik

(D) Mamta Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Deepa Malikas Nikshay Mitra ambassador. UnionHealth Ministry designated the President of the Paralympic Committee ofIndia Dr. Deepa Malik as Nikshay Mitra ambassador in New Delhi today,November 26. It is an initiative under Pradhan Mantri TB Mukt BharatAbhiyaan.

Concept note-2: (h.c) Deepa Malik becomes Ni-Kshay Mitra andnational ambassador for TB Mukt Bharat Campaign.

Concept note-3: NewDelhi: Union Health Ministry designated Padma Shri, Khel Ratna Arjuna Awardeeand the President of the Paralympic Committee of India Deepa Malik as NikshayMitra ambassador in New Delhi today, November 26.

Concept note-4: India’sfirst woman paralympic medalist Deepa Malik has pledged her support to a campaignto eradicate tuberculosis by becoming the national ambassador of its ‘Ni-KshayMitra’ initiative, the Union Health Ministry said on Saturday.
