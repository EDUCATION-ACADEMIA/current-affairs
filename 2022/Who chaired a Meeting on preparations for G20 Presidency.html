Who chaired a ‘Meeting on preparations for G20 Presidency’?

(A) Manmohan Singh

(B) ** Narendra Modi

(C) Nirmala Sitharaman

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: G20 Presidency belongs to the entire nation! An All Partymeeting was held on 5th December to discuss aspects relating to India’sG20 Presidency. The meeting, which was chaired by Prime Minister ShriNarendra Modi, witnessed active participation from political leaders across thelength and breadth of India.

Concept note-2: Dharmendra Pradhanchairs high-level meeting on preparations for G20 Summit going to be heldin India in 2023.

Concept note-3: The G20 Presidency steers the G20agenda for one year and hosts the Summit. The G20 consists of two paralleltracks: the Finance Track and the Sherpa Track. Finance Ministers andCentral Bank Governors lead the Finance Track while Sherpas lead the SherpaTrack.

Concept note-4: Since 1999, the meetings or summits of the G20 areorganized a G20 Leaders’ Summit annually. The last G20 Summit was held inNovember 2022 in Indonesia. Presently, the Prime of India, Shri Narendra Modi, isthe chairman of G20.
