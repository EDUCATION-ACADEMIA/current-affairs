Who has authored a children’s book titled ‘The Little Book of Joy’?

(A) Desmond Tutu

(B) Dalai Lama

(C) Penpa Tsering

(D) ** Both A and B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Little Book of Joy by Dalai Lama, Desmond Tutu: 9780593484234 | PenguinRandomHouse.com: Books.

Concept note-2: -About the Author Dalai Lama (Author) His Holiness the Dalai Lama is the spiritual and political leader of Tibet.

Concept note-3: -The Book of Joy: Lasting Happiness in a Changing World : Lama, Dalai, Tutu, Desmond, Abrams, Douglas Carlton: Amazon.in: Books.

Concept note-4: -This is the story of two friends: the late Archbishop Desmond Tutu and His Holiness the Dalai Lama. Once two lonely boys on opposite sides of the world, they grew up to discover the joy in everyday life and spread their joy with the people around them. Their message for every child is that joy is never far away.

Concept note-5: -About the Author His Holiness the 14th Dalai Lama is the spiritual leader of Tibetan people. In 1989, he was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize for his non-violent struggle for the liberation of Tibet.
