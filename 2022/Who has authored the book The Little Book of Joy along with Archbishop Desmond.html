Concept note-1: The Book of Joy: Lasting Happiness in a Changing World is abook by the Nobel Peace Prize Laureates Tenzin Gyatso, the 14th DalaiLama, and Archbishop Desmond Tutu published in 2016 by CornerstonePublishers.

Concept note-2: The Little Book of Joy by Dalai Lama, DesmondTutu: 9780593484234 | PenguinRandomHouse.com: Books.

Concept note-3: "TheLittle Book of Joy” is a wonderful book for both children and adults. This beautifullywritten story from His Holiness the Dalai Lama and Archbishop Desmond Tutuspeaks to the importance of finding joy and happiness in the little things in life. It’san important reminder of what really matters in life.

Concept note-4: This is thestory of two friends: the late Archbishop Desmond Tutu and His Holinessthe Dalai Lama. Once two lonely boys on opposite sides of the world, theygrew up to discover the joy in everyday life and spread their joy with thepeople around them. Their message for every child is that joy is never faraway.
