Who has become BYJU’s first global brand ambassador for its social impact arm-Education for All?

(A) ** Lionel Messi

(B) Shah Rukh Khan

(C) Roger Federer

(D) Virat Kohli

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lionel Messi in Byju’s Education For All campaign. India’s most valuable edtech company Byju’s has appointed football superstar Lionel Messi as the global brand ambassador of its social impact arm, Education For All.

Concept note-2: -Lionel “Leo” Messi has been named the first global brand ambassador of BYJU’S social impact arm, Education For All. Messi, the captain of Argentina’s football team and a player for Paris Saint-Germain, has signed an agreement with BYJU’S to promote the cause of equitable education.

Concept note-3: -Argentina and Paris Saint-Germain star Lionel Messi on Tuesday posted on Instagram with folded hands as he became the global brand ambassador of ed-tech platform BYJU’s Education For All (EFA) foundation. In an Instagram post, the World Cup winning footballer wrote: Children are our future.

Concept note-4: -Edtech major BYJU’s on Friday announced that the company has roped in football star Lionel “Leo” Messi as the first global brand ambassador of its social impact arm ‘Education For All’, according to the news agency PTI.

Concept note-5: -The edtech company faced a lot of backlash when it announced the onboarding of Lionel Messi as the brand ambassador of its social impact arm in November, just a month after announcing layoffs.
