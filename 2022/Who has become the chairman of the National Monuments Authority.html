Who has become the chairman of the National Monuments Authority?

(A) Ella Deloria

(B) ** Kishor Kumar Basa

(C) Ruth Benedict

(D) Franz Boas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Professor Kishor Kumar Basa has been appointed as the chairman of the National Monument Authority (NMA).

Concept note-2: -The Authority shall consist of a Chairperson, on a whole-time basis, to be appointed by the President, having proven experience and expertise in the field of archaeology, country and town planning architecture, heritage, conservation architecture or law,” Ramesh said.

Concept note-3: -National Monuments Authority (NMA) under the Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India has been setup as per provisions of The Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains AMASR (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2010 which was enacted in March, 2010.

Concept note-4: -BHUBANESWAR: Kishor Kumar Basa, vice-chancellor of Maharaja Sriram Chandra Bhanja Deo University Baripada, has been appointed as new chairman of the National Monuments Authority (NMA). He will remain in the post for three years.
