Who has become the first Australian woman to win Australian Open in 44 years?

(A) Maddison Inglis

(B) Daria Saville

(C) Samantha Stosur

(D) ** Ashleigh Barty

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Ashleigh Barty won the title in 2022, becoming thefirst Australian woman to win the title in 44 years.

Concept note-2: Afew points later, Barty threaded a forehand passing shot winner to seal afamous 6-3 7-6(2) triumph. It earned her a third major singles title andsaw her become the first Australian player to win the AO singles title in 44years. It turned out that forehand winner would be the last she would everstrike.

Concept note-3: Ashleigh Barty wins Australian Open, first Aussie in 44years to win women’s singles title at home. For the first time in 44 years,the Australian Open has a home-grown women’s singles champion and hername is Ashleigh Barty. The 25-year-old Queenslander and consensus worldNo.
