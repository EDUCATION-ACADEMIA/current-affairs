Who has become the first Indian footballer to play at the UEFA Women’s Champions League 2022?

(A) Pyari Xaxa

(B) Aditi Chauhan

(C) Manisha Panna

(D) ** Manisha Kalyan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: Manisha Kalyanbecomes 1st Indian to play in UEFA Women’s Champions League. Manisha Kalyanon Thursday replaced Cyprus’ Merilena Georgiou in the 60th minute at the MakareioStadium, as Apollon Ladies FC beat Latvian top flight club SFK Riga 3-0 in theirUWCL opener.
