Who has become the first Indian to win Gold at World Cadet Judo Championships 2022?

(A) Poonam Chopra

(B) Thoudam Kalpana Devi

(C) Avtar Singh

(D) ** Linthoi Chanambam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Indian judoka Linthoi Chanambam made history as she wonthe gold medal at the world cadet judo championships 2022 in the women’s -57kgcategory in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina on Friday. The teenager fromManipur is the first Indian to win gold in the world judo championships in anycategory.

Concept note-2: Linthoi Chanambam, the first Indian to win a gold atthe World Cadet Judo Championship, was accorded a hero’s welcome as she landedfrom Sarajevo, Bosnia, on Tuesday.

Concept note-3: At the World Cadet JudoChampionship, Linthoi Chanambam created history by becoming the firstIndian to win a gold medal by defeating Brazil’s Bianca Reis in Women’s 57Kg category.

Concept note-4: DAIS wins ISSO Judo Championship2022.

Concept note-5: Linthoi Chanambam created history on Friday as the16-year-old from Manipur became the first ever judo world champion fromIndia.
