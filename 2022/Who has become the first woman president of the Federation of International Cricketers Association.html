Who has become the first woman president of the Federation of International Cricketers Association?

(A) Kimi Paul

(B) Swati Dahiya

(C) ** Lisa Sthalekar

(D) Priyanka Dhankar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Lisa Sthalekar, who played for Australiafrom 2001 to2013, replaces Vikram Solanki as president of the Federation of InternationalCricketers’ Associations. Former Australia international Lisa Sthalekar is the firstwoman appointed president of the Federation of International Cricketers’Associations.

Concept note-2: The Federation of International Cricketers’Associations (FICA) is an organisation that co-ordinates the activities of all the nationalplayers’ associations that represent professional cricketers.

Concept note-3: LisaSthalekar Appointed FICA PresidentLisa Sthalekar has been confirmed as FICA’sPresident at the FICA Executive Committee meeting held in Nyon, Switzerlandthis week.
