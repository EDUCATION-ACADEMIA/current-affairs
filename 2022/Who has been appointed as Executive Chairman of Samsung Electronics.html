Who has been appointed as Executive Chairman of Samsung Electronics?

(A) ** Jay Y Lee

(B) Chung Yong-jin

(C) Hong Ra-hee

(D) Lee Boo-Jin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Jay Y. Lee Appointed Samsung Electronics ExecutiveChairman – Samsung Global Newsroom.

Concept note-2: The Board ofDirectors of Samsung Electronics today approved the appointment of Jay Y. Lee asExecutive Chairman of the company. The Board approved the appointment,which was recommended by Independent Director and Board ChairmanHan-Jo Kim.

Concept note-3: Jongbum Park, popularly known as “JB”Park, who served as executive vice president and head of mobile business inIndia, has taken over as CEO and MD of Samsung India and South WestAsia.

Concept note-4: Samsung India chief executive officer Ken Kang has beenreplaced by the company’s mobile phone business head JB Park who took charge lastweekend. Park was recently promoted as the executive vice president inSamsung.
