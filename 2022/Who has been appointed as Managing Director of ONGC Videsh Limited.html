Who has been appointed as Managing Director of ONGC Videsh Limited?

(A) ** Rajarshi Gupta

(B) Raj Shukla

(C) R K Gupta

(D) Rajiv Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Rajarshi Gupta has been appointed as Managing Director ofONGC Videsh.

Concept note-2: Rajarshi Gupta takes over as ManagingDirector of ONGC Videsh.

Concept note-3: NEW DELHI : Arun Kumar Singh,former chairman of oil refining and marketing company BPCL, is appointedchairman and managing director of ONGC by the Search-cum-SelectionCommittee (SCSC) of the Government of India.

Concept note-4: NEWDELHI: Rajarshi Gupta has taken over as managing director of ONGC VideshLimited (OVL), effective 19 July. In a statement, the ONGC subsidiary saidGupta was recommended for the position by public enterprise selection board(PESB).

Concept note-5: Government appoints Shri Arun Kumar Singh asChairman, ONGC; approves Board level reforms. Government of India had set up aSearch-cum-Selection Committee (SCSC) in February, 2022 for filling up the post ofCMD, ONGC.
