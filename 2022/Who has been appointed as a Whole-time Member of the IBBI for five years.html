Who has been appointed as a Whole-time Member of the IBBI for five years?

(A) ** Jayanti Prasad

(B) Preeti Tiwari

(C) Priyanka Agrawal

(D) Roshi Dixit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Jayanti Prasad took charge as Whole Time Member, Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India on 5th July 2022.
