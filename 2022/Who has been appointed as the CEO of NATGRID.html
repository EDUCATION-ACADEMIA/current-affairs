Who has been appointed as the CEO of NATGRID?

(A) Adarsh Chauhan

(B) Anurag Thakur

(C) Rakesh Mishra

(D) ** Piyush Goyal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: IAS officer of the Nagaland cadre, Piyush Goyalhas beenappointed by the Central Government as the new CEO of NATGRID (NationalIntelligence Grid).

Concept note-2: The Appointments Committee ofthe Cabinet (ACC) led by Prime Minister Narendra Modi has cleared theappointment of senior Nagaland cadre IAS officer Piyush Goyal as NationalIntelligence Grid (NATGRID) CEO.

Concept note-3: NATGRID or theNational Intelligence Grid is a part of the radical revamp of the intelligenceand security framework of India initiated by the former Home Minister P.Chidambaram.

Concept note-4: Biography. Raghu Raman has possibly one ofthe most unique career profiles spanning over 25 years. He has spent eleven years asan officer in the Indian Armed Forces , followed by another eleven years in thecorporate sector before joining the Government as CEO of the National IntelligenceGrid.

Concept note-5: National Intelligence Grid (NATGRID) project will beginby December 31st 2020. It was announced in Lok Sabha on 20 November 2019. TheNATGRID is a counter-terrorism programme. It was first proposed in 2008 after theMumbai terror attacks.
