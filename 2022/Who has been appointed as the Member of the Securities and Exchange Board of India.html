Who has been appointed as the Member of the Securities and Exchange Board of India?

(A) Sandeep Kumar Bist

(B) Arun Kumar Sharma

(C) ** Ashwini Bhatia

(D) Ashok Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Currently, the SEBI board consists of Ajay Tyagi (Chairman); G Mahalingam and Madhabi Puri Buch (both Whole-time members); besides Subhash Chandra Garg, Injeti Srinivas, NS Vishwanathan and Arun P Sathe as part-time members.

Concept note-2: -Securities and Exchange Board of India: Founded: 12 April 1992. Headquarters: Mumbai. Agency Executive: Ajay Tyagi (As of Feb 2022).

Concept note-3: -Sources said the Appointments Committee of Cabinet, headed by Prime Minister Narendra Modi, has approved Ms. Buch’s appointment for an initial period of three years.

Concept note-4: -Mumbai: The government named financial industry veteran Madhabi Puri Buch as the next chairperson of the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Sebi) on Monday, becoming the first woman to head the regulator.

Concept note-5: -The Sebi board has nine members, including the Chairperson. There are two government nominees and one RBI nominee (usually a Deputy Governor).
