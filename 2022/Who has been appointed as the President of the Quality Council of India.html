Who has been appointed as the President of the Quality Council of India?

(A) Deepak Shah

(B) ** Jaxay Shah

(C) Arun Bansal

(D) Partha Satpathy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Jaxay Shah nominated as the new Chairman of Quality Council of India. Shri Jaxay Shah, former Chairman of CREDAI and founder Chairman of Savvy Group has been appointed as the Chairman of Quality Council of India (QCI) for a period of three years effective from 21st October 2022.

Concept note-2: -QCI is governed by a Council comprising of 38 members including the Chairman and Secretary General. The Council has an equal representation of Government, Industry and other Stakeholders. The Chairman of QCI is appointed by the Hon’ble Prime Minister of India.

Concept note-3: -The Governing Council of QCI was initially constituted by the government as a part of the Cabinet decision in 1996. There were initially 31 constituent members, with equal representation from the Government, Industry and other stakeholders. Presently, the Governing Council comprises 38 members.
