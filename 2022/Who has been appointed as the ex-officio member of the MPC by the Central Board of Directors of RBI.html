Who has been appointed as the ex-officio member of the MPC by the Central Board of Directors of RBI?

(A) Mridul Saggar

(B) Ashima Goyal

(C) Shashanka Bhide

(D) ** Rajiv Ranjan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: They need to observe a “silent period” seven days before andafter the rate decision for “utmost confidentiality". The governor of the ReserveBank of India is the chairperson ex officio of the committee. Decisions aretaken by majority with the governor having the casting vote in case of atie.

Concept note-2: The central board of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI)approved the appointment of executive director Rajiv Ranjan as an ex-officiomember of the Monetary Policy Committee (MPC).

Concept note-3: RBIGovernor is the ex-officio chairperson of the Central Board of Directors of theRBI.

Concept note-4: The Governor of the Reserve Bank of India is theex-officio chairperson of the RBI. Q. Who among the following is the Chairperson ofthe 13th Finance Commission?

Concept note-5: “The Board approved thenomination of Dr. Rajiv Ranjan, Executive Director as an ex-officio member of theMonetary Policy Committee,” RBI said after the board met. Ranjan was an advisorin-charge in the monetary policy department, before he was elevated as an executivedirector.
