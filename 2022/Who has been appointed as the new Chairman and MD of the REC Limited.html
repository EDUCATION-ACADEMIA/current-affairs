Who has been appointed as the new Chairman and MD of the REC Limited?

(A) Pushp Kumar Joshi

(B) ** Ravinder Singh Dhillon

(C) Puneet Chawla

(D) Alkesh Kumar Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: Vivek Kumar Dewangan, an Additional Secretary in the Ministry Power , has been appointed as the CMD of state-owned REC Limited, according to an official statement.

Concept note-2: -Shri Sanjay Malhotra, IAS, assumed the charge of Chairman and Managing Director of REC Limited w.e.f. November 9, 2020. Shri Sanjay Malhotra is a 1990-batch Indian Administrative Service officer of Rajasthan cadre.

Concept note-3: -Shri Vivek Kumar Dewangan, a 1993 batch Indian Administrative Service Officer of Manipur Cadre took charge as Chairman & Managing Director of REC Limited on 17th May, 2022.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi: Sanjay Malhotra (IAS) is new Chairman & Managing Director (CMD) of REC Limited, as he assumed charge of the post on Monday. Prior to this assignment, Malhotra was serving as Additional Secretary in the Ministry of Power.
