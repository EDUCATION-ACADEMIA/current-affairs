Who has been appointed as the new Director-General of the Geological Survey of India?

(A) ** Dr. S Raju

(B) Gargi Bhattacharya

(C) H Rajaram

(D) Pralay Mukherjee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Brief History of Geological Survey of India1851 ThomasOldham arrived in Calcutta on 4th March and took charge of office on 5thMarch, 1851, which marks the establishment of the Geological Survey ofIndia.

Concept note-2: S Raju takes charge as DG of Geological Survey of India.Dr S Raju has taken over as the Director-General of the Geological Survey of India(GSI) with effect from April 01, 2022.

Concept note-3: Geological Survey ofIndia (GSI) Established in 1851, started its voyage to investigate for andassess coal and other mineral resources of the country with regional levelexploration.

Concept note-4: GSI, headquartered in Kolkata, has six regionaloffices located in Lucknow, Jaipur, Nagpur, Hyderabad, Shillong and Kolkata andstate unit offices in almost all states of the country.
