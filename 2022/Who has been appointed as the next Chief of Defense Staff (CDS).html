Who has been appointed as the next Chief of Defense Staff (CDS)?

(A) Nehal Malik

(B) Bhupendra Nath

(C) ** Anil Chauhan

(D) Vipin Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Government on Wednesday, September 28, 2022,appointed former Eastern Army Commander Lt. General Anil Chauhanas the nextChief of Defence Staff(CDS).

Concept note-2: I will try to fulfill the expectationsfrom the three defence forces as the Chief of Defence Staff. We will tackle allchallenges & difficulties together.” Sixty-one-year-old Chauhan will also function assecretary in the department of military affairs.

Concept note-3: General AnilChauhan, PVSM, UYSM, AVSM, SM, VSM assumed office as the Chief of DefenceStaff (CDS) on September 30, 2022. General Chauhan will also be the PrincipalMilitary Advisor to the Raksha Mantri on all Tri-Service matters as well as headDepartment of Military Affairs as Secretary.
