Who has been appointed in Mercedes-Benz India as MD & CEO from January 2023?

(A) Raju Tripathi

(B) Sandeep Bakshi

(C) Vivek Singh

(D) ** Santosh Iyer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: German luxury carmaker Mercedes-Benz on Monday namedSantosh Iyer as the Managing Director & CEO of the Indian operationswith effect from January 1, 2023. Santosh Iyer, 46, is currently working asVice President, Sales & Marketing and would be the first Indian to leadMercedes-Benz India operations.

Concept note-2: Mercedes-Benz, a Germanautomobile manufacturer, on Monday named Santosh Iyer the ManagingDirector and CEO of the Indian business, effective January 1, 2023. Iyer,46, would be the first Indian to serve as the head of Mercedes-Benz Indiaoperations.

Concept note-3: Santosh Iyer - Managing Director & CEO -Mercedes-Benz India Pvt. Ltd.

Concept note-4: India was the fastest-growingglobal market for Mercedes-Benz in 2022, chief executive and chairman Ola Källeniussaid, in a year when its global sales shrank 1% following the slowdown in China, theworld’s largest car market.

Concept note-5: Mercedes-benz India Private Limitedis majorly in Manufacturing (Machinery & Equipments) business from last 29 yearsand currently, company operations are active. Current board members & directorsare PIYUSH SHAILENDRA ARORA, ROLAND SEBASTIAN FOLGER, AMITATRE GOVIND, MANFRED AUSWEGER and STEPHAN ANDREAS KROEL.
