Who has been appointed the director general of the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation?

(A) Rajni Mishra

(B) Parul Dahiya

(C) Manjeet Kataria

(D) ** Rajendra Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Rajendra Kumar takes over the charge of Director General,ESIC.

Concept note-2: Rajendra Kumar, a 1992 batch IAS officer from theTamil Nadu cadre, has taken charge as Director-General, Employees’ State InsuranceCorporation (ESIC) on October 4.

Concept note-3: Employees’ State InsuranceCorporation (ESIC), established by ESI Act, is an autonomous corporation underMinistry of Labour and Employment, Government of India.

Concept note-4: ANILKUMAR SAHU - Additional Commissioner Regional Director, ESIC, Karnataka &Pondicherry - Ministry of Labour & Employment, Government of India |LinkedIn.
