Who has been awarded 31st Bihari Puraskar 2021 for her 2018 novel ‘Hum Yahan The’?

(A) ** Madhu Kankariya

(B) Tabish Khair

(C) Amitava Kumar

(D) Gunjesh Bond

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: It carries a citation, a plaque, and prize money of Rs. 2.5lakh. Madhu Kankariya won 2021 Bihari Puraskar for her book ‘Hum YahanThe’.

Concept note-2: Author Madhu Kankariya has been chosen for the 31stBihari Puraskar for her 2018 novel ‘Hum Yahan The’. The award carries a cash prizeof ₹2.5 lakh, a plaque and a citation.

Concept note-3: The 32nd Bihari Awardfor the year 2022 will be given to Dr. Madhav Harda by the Birla Foundation for hisliterary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chol Pahar Sakhi Ri’. K.K. Birla Foundationmade this announcement in New Delhi.

Concept note-4: Kankariya, whowas awarded Bihari Puraskar 2021, has written several prose, poems andbooks. Her novel, ‘Hum Yahan The’, is based on the struggles of the tribals inJharkhand.

Concept note-5: Literary critic and academic Madhav Hada wasawarded the Bihari Puraskar 2022 for his extensive work in literature, media, cultureand history. He has also been a member of the General Council of Sahitya Akademiand the Hindi Advisory Board.
