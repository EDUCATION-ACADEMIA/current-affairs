Who has been awarded the Sangita Kalanidhi award for the year 2021 by Music Academy?

(A) ** Tiruvarur Bhakthavathsalam

(B) Vijayalakshmi

(C) Neyveli R Santanagopalan

(D) Lalgudi GJR Krishnan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Santhanagopalan has been selected for the award for2020, mridhangam maestro Tiruvarur Baktavatsalam for 2021 and violinistsLalgudi GJR Krishnan and his sister GJR Vijayalakshmi for 2022. The MusicAcademy on Sunday announced Sangita Kalanidhi award winners for the years2020, 2021 and 2022.

Concept note-2: For the Sangita Kala Acharyaawards, the Academy has chosen nagaswaram exponent Kivalur N G Ganesan(2020), vocalist, musicologist, and guru Dr Ritha Rajan (2021), and vainikaand musicologist Dr R S Jayalakshmi (2022).

Concept note-3: Whoreceived the famous ‘Sangita Kalanithi’ award of the Music Academy, recently?Notes: The leading Carnatic vocalist S. Sowmya was selected for the ‘SangitaKalanithi’ award instituted by the Music Academy and recently receivedthe award.

Concept note-4: This award was instituted in 1993 and isgiven to those who have contributed by bringing several disciples to theconcert platform. Two senior musicians/preceptors are given this award eachyear.

Concept note-5: Kanyakumari - first woman violinist to get SangitaKalanidhi award.
