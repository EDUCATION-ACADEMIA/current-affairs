Who has been chosen as the male goalkeeper by International Hockey Federation?

(A) Subhashish Roy

(B) Gurpreet Singh Sandhu

(C) ** PR Sreejesh

(D) Peter Thangaraj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -FIH Goalkeepers of 2021-22: India’s Savita Punia, PR Sreejesh take the honours. Both players have won the award for the second consecutive year.

Concept note-2: -All set to play in his fourth FIH men’s hockey World Cup and the third on home soil, veteran India goalkeeper P R Sreejesh feels his team can do better than the quarterfinal exit in the last edition, and even finish on top of the podium this time.

Concept note-3: -Mr. Tayyab IKRAM He has been an FIH Executive Board member since 2016. The former Pakistan and China Coach believes hockey is pioneering in its inclusivity and universality. On 5 November 2022, Tayyab Ikram was elected as the 13th President of the International Hockey Federation.

Concept note-4: -Krishan Bahadur Pathak and PR Sreejesh are the goalkeepers. Manpreet Singh, Hardik Singh, Nilakanta Sharma, Shamsher Singh, Vivek Sagar Prasad, and Akashdeep Singh are all midfielders. Mandeep Singh, Lalit Kumar Upadhyay, Abhishek, and Sukhjeet Singh are the forwards.

Concept note-5: -The former Indian captain and Olympian was elected unopposed. Tirkey’s position has been accepted by the international hockey federation.
