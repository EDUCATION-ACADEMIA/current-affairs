Who has been conferred Singapore’s prestigious military award, the Pingat Jasa Gemilang (Tentera) or Meritorious Service Medal (Military) (MSM(M))?

(A) Vice-Admiral Ram Dass Katari

(B) Admiral Adhar Kumar Chatterji

(C) ** Admiral Sunil Lanba

(D) Vice-Admiral Bhaskar Sadashiv Soman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Former India Chief of Naval Staff, Admiral Sunil Lanba hasbeen conferred Singapore’s prestigious military award, the Pingat Jasa Gemilang(Tentera) or Meritorious Service Medal (Military) (MSM(M)), by President HalimahYacob.

Concept note-2: Former India Chief of Naval Staff, Admiral Sunil Lanbawas conferred Singapore’s prestigious military award, the Pingat Jasa Gemilang(Tentera) or Meritorious Service Medal (Military). He was awarded by PresidentHalimah Yacob in Sept’22.

Concept note-3: HE Madam Halimah Yacob, thePresident of Republic of Singapore, conferred the prestigious Pingat Jasa Gemilang(Tentera) [Meritorious Service Medal (Military)] on Admiral Sunil Lanba (Retd), theformer Chief of Naval Staff, Indian Navy.

Concept note-4: Associate ProfessorKenneth Mak Seck Wai, Mr Pang Kin Keong and Mr Tan Chong Mengwill receive the Meritorious Service Medal (COVID-19), the highest honourunder the National Awards (COVID-19).

Concept note-5: Former IndiaChief of Naval Staff, Admiral Sunil Lanba was conferred the award for hisoutstanding contributions in enhancing the strong and long-standing bilateraldefence relationship between the Indian Navy and the Republic of SingaporeNavy.
