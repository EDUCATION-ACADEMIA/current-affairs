Who has been conferred with Karnataka Ratna Award posthumously?

(A) KV Raju

(B) Sanchari Vijay

(C) Jayanthi

(D) ** Puneeth Rajkumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Puneeth Rajkumar conferred ‘Karnataka Ratna’ posthumously.

Concept note-2: -BENGALURU: Karnataka’s highest civilian award the ‘Karnataka Ratna’ was conferred posthumously on late actor Puneeth Rajkumar on Tuesday, on the occasion of the 67th Kannada Rajyotsava (the state formation day).

Concept note-3: -The Karnataka Ratna award consisting a full silver plaque and a 50 gram gold medal, was received by the late actor’s wife Ashwini Puneeth Rajkumar. PTI, Bengaluru, Nov 01 2022, 17:52 ist.

Concept note-4: -From 1992 till date including Puneet Rajkumar total ten people have been honoured with Karnataka Ratna Award. Interestingly Puneet Rajkumar’s father late legendary Kannada Actor Dr. Rajkumar was the first recipient of this award along with Notable Poet Kuvempu.
