Who has been elected as UNICEF’s YuWaah board co-chair?

(A) Ayush Yardi

(B) ** Ashwin Yardi

(C) Arvind Yardi

(D) Vivek Yardi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: UNICEF’s YuWaah’ - theIndian chapter of Generation Unlimited has announced the CEO of Capgemini inIndia Ashwin Yardi as its co-chair and will be a co-chair along with YasumasaKimura, the UNICEF Representative.

Concept note-2: UNICEF’s YuWaah’ -the Indian chapter of Generation Unlimited - has announced Ashwin Yardi, the CEOof Capgemini in India, to be its co-chair.

Concept note-3: YuWaah (GenerationUnlimited/GenU) is a UNICEF-initiated multi-stakeholder global platform, that aimsto prepare young people to transition from education and learning to productivework and active citizenship.

Concept note-4: About. Ashwin is CEOof Capgemini Technology Services India and also a member of CapgeminiGroup Executive Committee. Ashwin Yardi is an accomplished leader withmore than twenty five years of experience in consulting and informationtechnology.

Concept note-5: As Co-Chair, Ashwin Yardi, CEO of CapgeminiIndia, joins the UNICEF YuWaah Board.
