Who has been elected as a member of the International Committee for Weights and Measures?

(A) ** Venu Gopal Achanta

(B) Subroto Bagchi

(C) Anil Agarwal

(D) Arati Kousalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: CIPM is a supreme international committee, which isresponsible for Development and Implementation of Weight and Measures globally.Prof. Venu Gopal Achanta is among 18 members worldwide from differentcountries.

Concept note-2: The General Conference on Weights and Measures(CGPM), with diplomatic representatives of some 40 countries, meets everysix years to consider reform. The conference selects 18 scientists who formthe International Committee for Weights and Measures that governs thebureau.

Concept note-3: The SI was established in 1960 by the 11th GeneralConference on Weights and Measures (CGPM, Conférence Générale des Poids etMesures). The CGPM is the international authority that ensures wide disseminationof the SI and modifies the SI as necessary to reflect the latest advances in science andtechnology.

Concept note-4: An International Committee of Weights andMeasures, composed of 18 scientists elected by the conference, meets annually tomonitor worldwide uniformity in units of measure.

Concept note-5: Theprincipal task of the CIPM is to promote world-wide uniformity in units ofmeasurement and it does this by direct action or by submitting draft resolutions tothe General Conference.
