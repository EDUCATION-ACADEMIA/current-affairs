Who has been elected as the Prime Minister of Israel?

(A) Benny Gantz

(B) Yair Lapid

(C) Aryeh Deri

(D) ** Benjamin Netanyahu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The current prime minister is Benjamin Netanyahuof Likud,the ninth person to hold the position (excluding caretakers). Following an election,the president nominates a member of the Knesset to become prime minister afterasking party leaders whom they support for the position.

Concept note-2: Underthe coalition agreement establishing the thirty-sixth government of Israel, NaftaliBennett’s tenure as Prime Minister concluded at the end of 30 June 2022 and YairLapid took office at the beginning of 1 July 2022.

Concept note-3: Benjamin“Bibi” Netanyahu (/ˌnɛtɑːnˈjɑːhuː/; Hebrew: Bīnyāmīn Nētanyāhū (help. info);born 21 October 1949) is an Israeli politician who has been serving as the primeminister of Israel since December 2022, having previously held the office from 1996 to1999 and again from 2009 to 2021.
