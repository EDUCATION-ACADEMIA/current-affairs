Who has been elected as the first Dalit Female mayor in London Council?

(A) Maninder Singh

(B) ** Mohinder K Midha

(C) Gurpreet Bedi

(D) Abhilasha Bhakar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dorothy Mary Donaldson, Baroness Donaldson of Lymington, GBE, DStJ (née Warwick, 29 August 1921 – 4 October 2003), previously known as Dame Mary Donaldson, was the first female Lord Mayor of London (1983–84).

Concept note-2: -Almost 700 people have served as lord mayor. Dame Mary Donaldson GBE, elected in 1983, as well as Dame Fiona Woolf DBE, elected in 2013, are the only women to have held the office.

Concept note-3: -Gwenllian Morgan was the first woman to serve as mayor in Wales.

Concept note-4: -The Mayor of Ealing for the municipal years 2022/2023 is Councillor Mohinder Midha. The Deputy Mayor is Councillor Hitesh Tailor.
