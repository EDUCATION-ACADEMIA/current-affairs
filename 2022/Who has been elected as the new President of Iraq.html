Who has been elected as the new President of Iraq?

(A) Ali Allawi

(B) JumaInad

(C) ** Abdul Latif Rashid

(D) Fuad Hussein

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: On the 2022 Iraqi presidential election held on 13 October2022, the Iraqi parliament voted Abdul Latif Rashid as the new president of Iraq,replacing Barham Salih.

Concept note-2: The prime minister of Iraq is the headof government of Iraq. On 27 October 2022, Mohammed Shia’ Al Sudani became theincumbent prime minister.

Concept note-3: Abdul Latif Rashid (Arabic: ; born10 August 1944), also known as Latif Rashid (Kurdish: ) is an Iraqi Kurdishpolitician and the ninth president of Iraq, following the 2022 Iraqi presidentialelection. He was previously the Minister of Water Resources under the government ofNouri al-Maliki.

Concept note-4: The current Prime Minister of Iraqis Mohammed Shia al-Sudani, who holds most of the executive authorityand appointed the Council of Ministers, which acts as a cabinet and/orgovernment.
