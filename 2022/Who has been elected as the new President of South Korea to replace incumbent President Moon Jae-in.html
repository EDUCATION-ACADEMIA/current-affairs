Who has been elected as the new President of South Korea to replace incumbent President Moon Jae-in?

(A) Lee Jae-myung

(B) Kim Kun-hee

(C) Hong Joon-pyo

(D) ** Yoon Suk-yeol

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The current president, Yoon Suk-yeol, a former prosecutorgeneral and member of the conservative People Power Party, assumed office on 10May 2022, after defeating the Democratic Party’s nominee Lee Jae-myungwith a narrow 48.5% plurality in the 2022 South Korean presidentialelection.

Concept note-2: Yoon Suk-yeol (Korean: ; born 18 December 1960) is aSouth Korean politician, former public prosecutor and lawyer who has been servingas the 13th and current president of South Korea since 2022. Prior to hispresidency, he served as the prosecutor general of South Korea between 2019 and2021.
