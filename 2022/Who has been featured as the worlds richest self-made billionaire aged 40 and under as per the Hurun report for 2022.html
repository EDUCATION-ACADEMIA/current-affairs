Who has been featured as the world’s richest self-made billionaire aged 40 and under as per the Hurun report for 2022?

(A) ** Mark Zuckerberg

(B) Zhang Yiming

(C) Sam Bankman-Fried

(D) Dustin Moskovitz

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Nikhil Kamath, cofounder of Zerodha, topped the ‘IIFLWealth Hurun India 40 & Under Self-Made Rich List 2022’ with a net worthof Rs 17,500 crore. Bhavish Aggarwal, the founder of Ola, came in secondplace (Rs 11,700 crore), and Divyank Turakhia of Media.net in third place(Rs 11,200 crore).

Concept note-2: The Hurun India rich list has 53entrepreneurs under 40 years of age. With a wealth of Rs 17,500 crore, Zerodha’sfounder Nikhil Kamath, 36, tops a self-made rich list of individuals under 40years.

Concept note-3: Gautam Adani has surged ahead to the top spotbeating Mukesh Ambani with wealth more than doubling (116%) in the lastyear. He is ahead of the second-ranking Ambani by more than INR 3,00,000crore.

Concept note-4: Byju’s founder Divya Gokulnath (36) and Zerodha’sfounder Nikhil Kamath (35) are the youngest billionaires in India, according to theForbes India Rich List 2022.
