Who has been featured as the world’s richest self-made billionaire aged 40 and under as per the Hurun report for 2022?

(A) ** Mark Zuckerberg

(B) Zhang Yiming

(C) Sam Bankman-Fried

(D) Dustin Moskovitz

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nikhil Kamath, cofounder of Zerodha, topped the ‘IIFL Wealth Hurun India 40 & Under Self-Made Rich List 2022’ with a net worth of Rs 17,500 crore. Bhavish Aggarwal, the founder of Ola, came in second place (Rs 11,700 crore), and Divyank Turakhia of Media.net in third place (Rs 11,200 crore).

Concept note-2: -Who is the youngest self-made billionaire? Alexandr Wang is the youngest self-made billionaire as of November 2022, according to Forbes. At the age of 25, Wang is estimated to have a net worth of $1 billion.

Concept note-3: -With a wealth of Rs 17,500 crore, Zerodha’s founder Nikhil Kamath, 36, tops a self-made rich list of individuals under 40 years.

Concept note-4: -The AIIFL Wealth Hurun India Rich List 2022 has just been released. The latest list of richest Indians, which includes several promoters of startups, also features Kaivalya Vohra. Vohra, who founded Zepto, is the youngest in the list aged 19. According to the list, Vohra has net worth of Rs 1000 crore.

Concept note-5: -Gautam Adani has surged ahead to the top spot beating Mukesh Ambani with wealth more than doubling (116%) in the last year. He is ahead of the second-ranking Ambani by more than INR 3,00,000 crore.
