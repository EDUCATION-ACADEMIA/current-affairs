Who has been made the new Chief Scientist of the World Health Organisation?

(A) Dr. Pooja Singh

(B) Dr. Shivraj Joshi

(C) ** Jeremy Farrar

(D) Sumit Arora

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: World Health Organization names Sir Jeremy Farrar as ChiefScientist, Dr Amelia Latu Afuhaamango Tuipulotu as Chief Nursing Officer. WHOannounced that Dr Jeremy Farrar will become its new Chief Scientist. Currently,Director of the Wellcome Trust, Dr Farrar will join WHO in the second quarter of2023.

Concept note-2: Dr John Reeder. Dr John Reeder is Chief Scientist andHead of the Science Division ad interim. During leadership transition, he isguiding the Science Division’s mission of bringing the best of science intohealth.

Concept note-3: The World Health Organization said on Tuesday thatDr. Jeremy Farrar will become its new chief scientist as it prepares for apost-pandemic future. Dr. Farrar will join the WHO in the second quarter of 2023,replacing Soumya Swaminathan.

Concept note-4: Soumya Swaminathan (born 2May 1959) is an Indian paediatrician and clinical scientist known for her research ontuberculosis and HIV. From 2019 to 2022, she served as the chief scientist at theWorld Health Organization under the leadership of Director General TedrosAdhanom Ghebreyesus.

Concept note-5: Dr Zsuzsanna Jakab, DeputyDirector-General, World Health Organization.
