Who has been named FIH Women’s Rising Player of the Year 2021-22?

(A) Mamta Kharab

(B) Neha Malik

(C) Sonia Dahiya

(D) ** Mumtaz Khan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Netherlands’ Felice Albers and India’s HarmanpreetSinghhave been named the International Hockey Federation (FIH) Player ofthe Year for the 2021-2022 season in the women’s and men’s competitionsrespectively.

Concept note-2: Young India Hockey player Mumtaz Khanwas named the FIH Women’s Rising Star of the Year 2021-22 on 4 October2022.

Concept note-3: Harmanpreet Singh holds the record for the most goalsscored in an FIH Pro League season. Indian hockey team star Harmanpreet Singhhas been named the men’s FIH Player of the Year 2021-22 becoming justthe fourth player to win the award in the men’s category in consecutiveyears.

Concept note-4: Harmanpreet Singh becomes just the fourth player to winthe Player of the Year award (men’s category) in consecutive years. India defenderHarmanpreet Singh was on Friday named the FIH Player of the Year in the men’scategory for the second successive time.

Concept note-5: Argentina trumpedsix-time champions Germany in the final to win gold at the FIH Men’s Junior HockeyWorld Cup 2021 held in Bhubaneshwar, Odisha, while France and the Indian hockeyteam finished third and fourth, respectively.
