Who has been named as the new Defence Secretary by the Government of India?

(A) Ajay Bhatt

(B) Amarendra Tiwari

(C) ** Aramane Giridhar

(D) Radha Sridharan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current Defence Secretary is Shri. Giridhar Aramane, I.A.S.

Concept note-2: -Austin III. Lloyd James Austin III was sworn in as the 28th Secretary of Defense on January 22, 2021.

Concept note-3: -Lloyd J. Austin III is the 28th secretary of defense, sworn in on Jan. 22, 2021. A graduate of the U.S. Military Academy at West Point, N.Y., Austin served 41 years in uniform, retiring as a four-star Army general after three years as commander of U.S. Central Command.

Concept note-4: -They are often assisted by a Minister of State for Defence and less-commonly, the lower-ranked Deputy Minister of Defence. The first defence minister of independent India was Baldev Singh Chokkar, who served in Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru’s cabinet during 1947–52.
