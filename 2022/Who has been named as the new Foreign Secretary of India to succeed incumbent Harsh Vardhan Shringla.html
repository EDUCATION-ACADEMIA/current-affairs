Who has been named as the new Foreign Secretary of India to succeed incumbent Harsh Vardhan Shringla?

(A) ** Vinay Mohan Kwatra

(B) Hemant H. Kotalwar

(C) Sanjay Kumar Panda

(D) Ram Karan Verma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Foreign SecretaryOn April 4, 2022, it was announced that Mr.Kwatra will succeed Harsh Vardhan Shringla to assume the post of the ForeignSecretary of India upon the latter’s superannuation of the post on 30 April 2022. On1 May, he became the 34th Foreign Secretary of India.
