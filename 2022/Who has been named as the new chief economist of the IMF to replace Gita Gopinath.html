Who has been named as the new chief economist of the IMF to replace Gita Gopinath?

(A) Piotr Trabinski

(B) Pablo Moreno Garcia

(C) Paul Hilbers

(D) ** Pierre-Olivier Gourinchas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The International Monetary Fund said on Monday it hasappointed French-born University of California-Berkeley economist Pierre-OlivierGourinchas as the Fund’s next chief economist, replacing Gita Gopinath, who is joiningthe IMF management team this month.

Concept note-2: Pierre-Olivier Gourinchasto succeed Gita Gopinath as IMF’s Chief Economist.

Concept note-3: IANSInternational Monetary Fund’s chief economist Pierre-Olivier Gourinchas, secondfrom left, and Daniel Leigh, the head of the World Economic Studies Division, right,at the news conference to release the World Economic Outlook report in Washingtonon Tuesday, October 11, 2022.

Concept note-4: The first Indian to achieve thehonour was Raghuram Rajan who was Chief Economist and Director of Research ofIMF between 2003 and 2006.
