Who has been nominated as executive chairman of the National Legal Services Authority?

(A) Justice U U Lalit

(B) Justice Ranjan Gogoi

(C) Justice N V Ramana

(D) ** Justice D Y Chandrachud

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Hon’ble Dr. Justice D. Y. Chandrachud,Judge TheChief Justice of India is the Patron-in-Chief. NALSA is housed at SupremeCourt of India, Tilak Marg, New Delhi, Delhi(110001). The NALSA Centrefor Citizen Services is set up at Jaisalmer House,Man Singh Road, NewDelhi-110011.

Concept note-2: The prime objective of NALSA is speedy disposalof cases and reducing the burden of judiciary. The current Executive-Chairman ofNALSA is Justice Sanjay Kishan Kaul.

Concept note-3: Who has beennominated as executive chairman of the National Legal Services Authority? Notes:Justice D Y Chandrachud, the second senior-most judge in the Supreme Court,has been nominated as executive chairman of the National Legal ServicesAuthority.

Concept note-4: His Lordship Hon. Mr. Justice B.N. Kirpal, Judge,Supreme Court of India and Chairman, Supreme Court Legal Services Committeehas a very long association with legal aid movement.
