Who has been sworn in as the Controller General of Accounts in place of Dipak Dash?

(A) Anil Yadav

(B) ** Sonali Singh

(C) Dinesh Kumar Gupta

(D) Ashok Kumar Pal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: In February 2022, the Centre had given the charge theController General of Accounts, Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance toSonali Singh, Additional Controller General of Accounts, after superannuation ofDipak Dash.

Concept note-2: Ms Bharati Das, a 1988-batch Indian CivilAccounts Service (ICAS) officer, is appointed by the Government of India as theController General of Accounts (CGA), Department of Expenditure, Ministry ofFinance, with effect from October 18, 2021.

Concept note-3: The correctanswer is Sonali Singh. The centre has given the charge of the ControllerGeneral of Accounts (CGA), Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Financeto Sonali Singh, after the superannuation of Dipak Dash. She is presentlythe Additional Controller General of Accounts.

Concept note-4: VivekSinghGovernment of India has appointed Smt. Sonali Singh, 1987-batch IndianCivil Accounts Service (ICAS) Officer as the Controller General of Accounts(CGA), Ministry of Finance, Department of Expenditure. Smt. Sonali Singh isthe 26th ICAS officer to hold the coveted position of Controller General ofAccounts.
