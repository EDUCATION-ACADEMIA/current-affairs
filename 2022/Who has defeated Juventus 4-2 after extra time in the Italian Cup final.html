Who has defeated Juventus 4-2 after extra time in the Italian Cup final?

(A) ** Inter Milan

(B) Roma

(C) Cagliari Calcio

(D) Fiorentina

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Seven finals have been decided by a penalty shoot-out,the most recent being between Napoliand Juventus in 2020. The currentchampions are Inter Milan, who beat Juventus 4–2 after extra time in the 2022final.

Concept note-2: Inter Milan defeated Juventus 4-2 after extra time in theItalian Cup final.

Concept note-3: It was played on 11 May 2022 betweenJuventus and Internazionale. The Stadio Olimpico in Rome hosted the final.Internazionale won the match 4–2 after extra time for their eighth Coppa Italiatitle.

Concept note-4: As of 2022, Juventus had won the most Coppa Italia titlesin Italy, winning the competition 14 times in total. The team from Turinis followed by AS Roma, who have won on nine occasions. Inter last wonthe cup in the 2021/22 season.

Concept note-5: Italian soccer clubs bySerie A titles 1897-2022. With 36 Serie A trophies in total, Turin-basedJuventus FC held the record for the most Serie A championships won inItaly.
