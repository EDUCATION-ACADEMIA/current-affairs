Who has inaugurated Rs 11, 420 Crore Pune Metro Rail Project?

(A) ** PM Modi

(B) Piuysh Goyal

(C) Amit Shah

(D) Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated PuneMetro Rail Project today. He also laid the foundation stone and inaugurated multipledevelopmental projects in Pune.

Concept note-2: This trial run marks the firstmetro train run between the twin cities of Pimpri Chinchwad and Pune. Earlier, asuccessful trial run was conducted from Garware College to Civil Courtstation. Around 10 stations have been operational since the inauguration inMarch 2022.

Concept note-3: PM Narendra Modi launching phase-1 ofAhmedabad Metro rail project.

Concept note-4: On 6 March 2022, IndianPrime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the metro system in the city ofPune.

Concept note-5: 10% of the project’s finance will come from thefederal government, 15% from the state, and the other 60% from the civicorganisations in Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad. While the PCMC-Nigdi extension linewill cost Rs 9.11 billion, the 5.464-km Swargate-Katraj line will cost Rs 36bn.
