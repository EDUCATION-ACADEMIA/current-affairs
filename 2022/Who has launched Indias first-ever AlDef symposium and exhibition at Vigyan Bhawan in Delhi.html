Who has launched India’s first-ever AlDef symposium and exhibition at Vigyan Bhawan in Delhi?

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) ** Rajnath Singh

(C) Piyush Goyal

(D) Nitin Gadkari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: 3. Who has launched India’s first-ever AlDef symposium andexhibition at Vigyan Bhawan in Delhi? Explanation: Defence Minister Rajnath Singhlaunched the first-ever ‘Artificial Intelligence in Defence’ (AIDef) symposium andexhibition organised.

Concept note-2: On July 11, Defence MinisterRajnath Singh launched 75 newly-developed artificial intelligence (AI) productsduring the first-ever symposium and exhibition on Artificial Intelligence inDefence (AIDef) in New Delhi. The event was a part of the ‘Azadi Ka AmritMahotsav’ celebrations.

Concept note-3: UPSC Mains. Q. Where isthe ‘Artificial Intelligence in Defence’ (AIDef) symposium and exhibitionorganised? Notes: Defence Minister Rajnath Singh inaugurated the first ever‘Artificial Intelligence in Defence’ (AIDef) symposium and exhibition in NewDelhi.

Concept note-4: The Department of Defense Production, Ministry ofDefense, will host the first-ever Artificial Intelligence in Defence symposium andexhibition in New Delhi.

Concept note-5: The introduction of autonomy inweapon systems, in ISR (Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance), datamanagement, can be a huge asset in stopping terrorism, installing counter-terrorismmeasures, protecting soldiers. In fact, AI in defence can change combat and conflictat the deepest levels.
