Who has launched One station One Product?

(A) North-Eastern Railway

(B) Northern Railway

(C) Eastern Railway

(D) ** South-Central Railway

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: South Western Railway : One Station One Product byIndian Railway. KSR Bengaluru Karnataka : In the spirit of the “Vocal forLocal” campaign, a “One Station, One Product” stall promoting regional craftthrough jute products has been opened recent at KSR Bengaluru Stationin Karnataka.

Concept note-2: The correct answer is South-CentralRailway. The South-Central Railway (SCR) has launched the “One station OneProduct” initiative at 6 major stations in all its six divisions. The stallshave also been opened at Secunderabad, Kacheguda, Vijayawada, Guntur,and Aurangabad stations.

Concept note-3: One Station One Productconcept aims to encourage indigenous and specialized products and craftsof India by providing display and sale outlets on railway stations acrossthe country.

Concept note-4: Indian Railways’ Bhopal Railway Stationhas been awarded a 4- star ‘Eat Right Station’ certification for providinghigh-quality, nutritious food to passengers. This certification is granted byFSSAI to railway stations adhering to standard food storage and hygienepractices.
