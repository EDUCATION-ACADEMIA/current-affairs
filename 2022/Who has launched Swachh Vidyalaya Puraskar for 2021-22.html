Who has launched Swachh Vidyalaya Puraskar for 2021-22?

(A) Smriti Zubin Irani

(B) ** Subhas Sarkar

(C) Arjun Munda

(D) Kiren Rijiju

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Puraskar is given at District, State and National level.Swachh Vidyalaya Puraskar (SVP), 2021-22 was successfully launched by Dr SubhasSarkar on 12th January 2022, which was the birthday of Swami Vivekanand and iscelebrated as National Youth Day.

Concept note-2: The Ministry ofEducation (earlier Human Resource Development), Government of Indialaunched ‘Swachh Bharat Swachh Vidyalaya’ (SBSV) initiative in 2014 to ensurethat all schools in India have access to separate functional toilets for boysand girls.

Concept note-3: About The Swachh Vidyalaya Puraskar:TheSwachh Vidyalaya Puraskar has been instituted by Ministry Of Education torecognize, inspire and celebrate excellence in sanitation and hygiene practice inschools.

Concept note-4: If your school is ensuring a safe, hygienic environment,it can participate in Swachh Vidyalaya Puraskar 2021-22 before April 15,2022.

Concept note-5: The Swachh Vidyalaya Puraskar honors the school thathas taken exemplary work in the field of water, sanitation, and hygiene. Italso provides a benchmark and roadmap for the schools to make furtherimprovements.
