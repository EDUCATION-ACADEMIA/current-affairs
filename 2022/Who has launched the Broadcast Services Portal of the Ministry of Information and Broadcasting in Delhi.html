Who has launched the Broadcast Services Portal of the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting in Delhi?

(A) Piyush Goyal

(B) Rajnath Singh

(C) ** Anurag Thakur

(D) Nirmala Sitharaman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Minister of information and broadcasting Anurag Thakur.NEW DELHI: The ministry of information and broadcasting launched theBroadcast Seva Portal, a platform aimed at improving ease of doing businessin the broadcast industry.

Concept note-2: The Iron Man of IndiaSardar Vallabhbhai Patel served as the first Minister for Information andBroadcasting.

Concept note-3: The ‘Broadcast Seva Portal’ will help satelliteTV channels, teleport operators, MSOs, community radio stations (CRS) and privateFM channels. It is also used for tracking applications, calculating fees and makingpayments.
