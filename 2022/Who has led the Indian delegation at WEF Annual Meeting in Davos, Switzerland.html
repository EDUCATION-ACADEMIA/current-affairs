Who has led the Indian delegation at WEF Annual Meeting in Davos, Switzerland?

(A) ** Piyush Goyal

(B) Harsh Vardhan Shringla

(C) V M Kwatra

(D) K Rajaraman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW
