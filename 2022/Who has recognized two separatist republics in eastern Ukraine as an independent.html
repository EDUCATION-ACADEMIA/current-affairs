Who has recognized two separatist republics in eastern Ukraine as an independent?

(A) Olaf Scholz

(B) ** Vladimir Putin

(C) Joe Biden

(D) Emmanuel Macron

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The Donetsk People’s Militia and Luhansk People’s Militia(formerly also called Russian separatist forces in Donbas) are pro-Russianparamilitaries in the Donbas region of eastern Ukraine, which have been fighting theArmed Forces of Ukraine in the Russo-Ukrainian War.

Concept note-2: Shortlythereafter, Russia annexed Crimea from Ukraine, while the armed separatistsseized government buildings and proclaimed the Donetsk People’s Republic(DPR) and Luhansk People’s Republic (LPR) as independent states, whichreceived no international recognition from United Nations member states before2022.

Concept note-3: The Luhansk People’s Republic (LPR) initially soughtrecognition as a sovereign state following its declaration of independence in April2014.
