Who has secured a spot at the 2024 Paris Paralympics after winning the gold medal in the Para Shooting World Cup in women’s 10m air rifle?

(A) Anahat Singh

(B) ** Avani Lekhara

(C) Priyanka Mohite

(D) Jahnavi Dangeti

Concept note-1: Tokyo Paralympics champion Avani Lekhara won gold at thePara Shooting World Cup with a world record score of 250.6 in women’s 10m air riflestanding SH1 in Chateauroux, France on Tuesday. The 20-year-old shooterbroke her own world record of 249.6 to secure her a spot at the 2024 ParisParalympics.
