Who has taken additional charge as CMD of Navratna Defence PSU BEL?

(A) Ajit Kumar Saxena

(B) ** Dinesh Kumar Batra

(C) Pankaj Gupta

(D) R Subramaniakumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -September 1.2022: Mr Dinesh Kumar Batra, Director (Finance) & CFO, has taken additional charge as Chairman & Managing Director (CMD) of Navratna Defence PSU Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) with effect from September 1, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Bhanu Prakash Srivastava joined BEL in August 1986 and in a career spanning 36 years in three Units of BEL. New Delhi: Mr Bhanu Prakash Srivastava, who is Director (Other Units) of BEL since April 20, 2022, assumed Additional Charge as Chairman & Managing Director and Director (Marketing) today (November 1, 2022).

Concept note-3: -Bengaluru: Mr Damodar Bhattad S has taken charge as the Director (Finance) of Navratna Defence PSU Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) with effect from January 11, 2023.
