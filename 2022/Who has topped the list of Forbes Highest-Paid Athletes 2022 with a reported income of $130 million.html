Who has topped the list of Forbes Highest-Paid Athletes 2022 with a reported income of $130 million?

(A) ** Lionel Messi

(B) Neymar

(C) Cristiano Ronaldo

(D) LeBron James

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Messi earned $130 million, a figure which includes$55 million of endorsements, during the 12-month period ending May 1,2022, to sit atop the list of the 10 highest-paid athletes a year after finishingsecond to Irish MMA fighter Conor McGregor.

Concept note-2: LionelMessi is ranked first among the highest-paid athletes in Forbes’ ranking.130 million US dollars are his annual earnings.
