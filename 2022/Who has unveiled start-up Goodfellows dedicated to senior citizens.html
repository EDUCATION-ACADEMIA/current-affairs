Who has unveiled start-up Goodfellows dedicated to senior citizens?

(A) Mukesh Ambani

(B) Saurab Gupta

(C) Gautam Adani

Concept note-1: Ratan Tataunveils start-up Goodfellows dedicated to seniorcitizens. Ratan Tata funded start-up “Goodfellows”, which provides companionshipto seniors through young, educated graduates who are vetted intensely for empathyand emotional intelligence, was officially unveiled on Tuesday in the presence ofMr.

Concept note-2: Since its inception, the company has been working withabout 20 elders. Shantanu Naidu, founder of senior citizenship company Goodfellows,shared in an emotional post what inspired him to begin something of his own, astart-up, in other words.
