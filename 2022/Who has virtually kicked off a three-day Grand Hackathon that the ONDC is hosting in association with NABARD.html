Who has virtually kicked off a three-day Grand Hackathon that the ONDC is hosting in association with NABARD?

(A) Ashwini Vaishnaw

(B) ** Piyush Goyal

(C) Narendra Modi

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Union Minister Piyush Goyal has virtually kicked off athree-day Grand Hackathon that the Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC)is hosting in association with NABARD.

Concept note-2: A three-day“Grand Hackathon” organized by the Open Network for Digital Commerce(ONDC) in collaboration with NABARD, was launched by Union MinisterPiyush Goyal on 1 July 2022.

Concept note-3: NABARD in associationwith ONDC organized a grand hackathon, to bring solutions for providingecommerce for produce of the Producer Organisations, products throughONDC platform. Around 700 Startups registered, in which more than 300Startups participated.

Concept note-4: CM launches Hackathon 2022, hailsIndia’s ‘centuries-old’ ties with Africa.

Concept note-5: Notably, ONDC isa Government of India initiative that aims to build an open network forbuyers and sellers to democratise the current e-commerce ecosystem in thecountry. It is also very crucial in democratizing the agri-value chain andensuring the digital transformation of agri sector, as was stated by ShriGoyal.
