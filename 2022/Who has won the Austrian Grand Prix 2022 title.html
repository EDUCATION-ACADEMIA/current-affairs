Who has won the Austrian Grand Prix 2022 title?

(A) Lewis Hamilton

(B) Mick Schumacher

(C) ** Charles Leclerc

(D) Max Verstappen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Charles Leclerc won the race in spite of late issues with his car,retaking second place in the standings from Sergio Pérez. This race marked Leclerc’sthird and final win of the 2022 season and Ferrari’s first win in Austria since MichaelSchumacher won there for the team in 2003.

Concept note-2: With 103 raceswon throughout his career, Lewis Hamilton topped the ranking of Formula 1 driversas of October 2022. The Briton surpassed German legend Michael Schumacher by 12race wins.

Concept note-3: Race results from the Austrian MotoGP at the RedBull Ring, round 13 (of 20) in the 2022 world championship. Francesco Bagnaia hastaken his third victory in a row at the 2022 Austrian MotoGP, while world championFabio Quartararo fought his way past the other Ducatis for a hard-earned secondplace.
