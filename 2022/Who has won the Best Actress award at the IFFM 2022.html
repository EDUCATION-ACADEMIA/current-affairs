(A) Vaani Kapoor

(B) ** Shefali Shah

(C) Kareena Kapoor

(D) Sakshi Tanwar

Concept note-1: -IFFM Awards 2022 full list: Ranveer Singh, Shefali Shah, Shoojit Sircar bag awards. Ranveer Singh and Shefali Shah bagged Best Actor and Best Actress awards in the prestigious Indian Film Festival of Melbourne (IFFM) awards this year.

Concept note-2: -Iffm Awards: Ranveer Singh Gets Best Actor, Shefali Shah The Best Actress Award; Check Full Winner List Here.
