Who has won the Women’s Prize for Fiction this year for her novel ‘The Book of Form and Emptiness?

(A) ** Ruth Ozeki

(B) Lisa Allen-Agostini

(C) Meg Mason

(D) Louise Erdrich

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ruth Ozeki’s fourth novel, The Book of Form and Emptiness, has won the Women’s prize for fiction.
