Who has won the last gold medal for India in Commonwealth Games 2022?

(A) Sudhir

(B) Vinesh Phogat

(C) ** Sharath Kamal

(D) Bajrang Punia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: Team India finished fourth onthe medals tally, behind Australia (178 medals), England (176 medals) andCanada (92 medals). Overall, Indian athletes won 22 gold, 16 silver and 23bronze medals, for a total of 61 medals.
