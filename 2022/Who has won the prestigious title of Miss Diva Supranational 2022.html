Who has won the prestigious title of Miss Diva Supranational 2022?

(A) ** Pragnya Ayyagari

(B) Pallavi Singh

(C) Aarya Walvekar

(D) Divita Rai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Pragnya Ayyagari of Telangana was declared LIVA Miss DivaSupranational 2022. She was crowned by Miss Supranational Asia 2022 RitikaKhatnani. Born in Mangalore, Karnataka, Divita Rai went onto to live in manycities across the country, thanks to her father’s job.

Concept note-2: A23-year-old model Divita Rai, who is also an architect by profession, wonMiss Diva Universe 2022 title on Sunday. She was crowned by Miss Universe2021 Harnaaz Sandhu at the 10th anniversary of the Miss Diva Universepageant.

Concept note-3: Pragnya Ayyagari, who has won our hearts by winningthe LIVA Miss Diva Supranational 2022 crown, has finally arrived in Hyderabad. Shereceived a grand and warm reception from fans and family at the Hyderabadairport.
