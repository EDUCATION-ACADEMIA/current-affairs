Who has won the prestigious title of Miss Diva Universe 2022?

(A) ** Divita Rai

(B) Pallavi Singh

(C) Dia Mirza

(D) Aarya Walvekar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: By Srimoyee Chowdhury: 23-year-old Divita Rai fromKarnataka won the prestigious title of Miss Diva Universe 2022. Rai was crowned bythe reigning Miss Universe2021, Harnaaz Sandhu, at a lavish ceremony thatcelebrated the 10th anniversary of the coveted pageant.

Concept note-2: MissDiva 2022 was the 10th anniversary edition of the Miss Diva beauty pageantheld on 30th October 2022. At the end of the event, Harnaaz Sandhucrowned Divita Rai as her successor. She will represent India in the MissUniverse 2022 pageant. Ritika Khatnani crowned Pragnya Ayyagari as hersuccessor.

Concept note-3: Pragnya Ayyagari of Telangana was declared LIVAMiss Diva Supranational 2022.

Concept note-4: Harnaaz Sandhu of Indiacrowned R’Bonney Gabriel of the United States as Miss Universe 2022. It is theUnited States’ first victory in 10 years, and the ninth entrant from the United Statesto win the title, as well as the oldest entrant to be crowned, surpassing Andrea Mezaof Mexico in 2020.
