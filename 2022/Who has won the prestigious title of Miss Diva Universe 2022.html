Who has won the prestigious title of Miss Diva Universe 2022?

(A) ** Divita Rai

(B) Pallavi Singh

(C) Dia Mirza

(D) Aarya Walvekar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By Srimoyee Chowdhury: 23-year-old Divita Rai from Karnataka won the prestigious title of Miss Diva Universe 2022. Rai was crowned by the reigning Miss Universe 2021, Harnaaz Sandhu , at a lavish ceremony that celebrated the 10th anniversary of the coveted pageant.

Concept note-2: -Miss Diva 2022 was the 10th anniversary edition of the Miss Diva beauty pageant held on 30th October 2022. At the end of the event, Harnaaz Sandhu crowned Divita Rai as her successor. She will represent India in the Miss Universe 2022 pageant. Ritika Khatnani crowned Pragnya Ayyagari as her successor.

Concept note-3: -Pragnya Ayyagari of Telangana was declared LIVA Miss Diva Supranational 2022.

Concept note-4: -Divita Rai Karnataka’s Divita Rai has been crowned LIVA Miss Diva Universe 2022.
