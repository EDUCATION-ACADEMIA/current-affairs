Who has won the winner of the Indian Medical Device Innovation of the Year?

(A) Poly Medicure Ltd

(B) ** Meril Lifesciences Pvt Ltd

(C) Glenmark Pharmaceuticals Ltd

(D) Zydus Lifesciences Ltd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: 1. Medtronic $31.7bn +8% Medtronic once again tops the listas the largest medical device company in the world. With a workforce of over90,000, operating in 150 countries, Medtronic is at the forefront of medicaltechnology.

Concept note-2: The Indian Pharma Leader of the Year award wasbestowed to Cipla Ltd, while the award for India Pharma Innovation of the Year wasconferred upon Glenmark Pharmaceuticals Ltd. The Indian Pharma (Formulation)Award winner is Micro Labs Ltd.

Concept note-3: Revenue in the MedicalDevices segment in India is projected to reach US$5.99bn in 2023. The market’slargest segment is Cardiology Devices with a projected market volume ofUS$0.85bn in 2023. Revenue is expected to show an annual growth rate(CAGR 2023-2027) of 8.35%, resulting in a market volume of US$8.26bn by2027.
