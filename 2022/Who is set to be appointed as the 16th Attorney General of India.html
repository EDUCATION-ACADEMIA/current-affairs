Who is set to be appointed as the 16th Attorney General of India?

(A) Harish Salve

(B) ** R. Venkataramani

(C) Tushar Mehta

(D) AB Rohtagi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: NEW DELHI: The Union government is all set toappoint senior advocate and ex-attorney general Mukul Rohatgi as 16thAG for India from October 1 as the present AG K K Venugopal expressedunwillingness to continue in the top law officer’s post beyond September30.

Concept note-2: R. Venkataramani is the incumbent Attorney-General forIndia. He succeeded to the office as the 16th Attorney-General on 1 October2022.

Concept note-3: Ans. The Indian President appoints the Attorney Generalof India He is in office as long as the President so chooses. He has to belongto one of the two Houses of Parliament. The Parliament has the power toimpeach the Attorney General of India.

Concept note-4: Senior advocate R.Venkataramani has been appointed as the new Attorney General (AG) of India byPresident Droupadi Murmu for a period of three years, the Law Ministryannounced on Wednesday.

Concept note-5: “The President is pleased toappoint R Venkataramani, Senior Advocate as Attorney General for India fora period of three years from the date he enters upon his office,” read thenotification.
