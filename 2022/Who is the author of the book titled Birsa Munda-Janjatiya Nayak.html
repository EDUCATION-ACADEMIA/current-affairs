Who is the author of the book titled ‘Birsa Munda-Janjatiya Nayak’?

(A) Hiren Doshi

(B) ** Prof. Alok Chakrawal

(C) Karuna Shankar Misra

(D) Devinder Banwet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Union Education and Skill Development Minister ShriDharmendra Pradhan today released a book “Birsa Munda – Janjatiya Nayak”written by Prof. Alok Chakrawal, Vice Chancellor, Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya,Bilaspur.

Concept note-2: Birsa Munda gave the slogan ‘Abua Raj EteJana, Maharani Raj Tundu Jana,’ which means “Let the queen’s kingdomend and our kingdom be established.” The British police arrested him onMarch 3, 1900. On June 9 of the same year, at the age of 25, he died incustody at the Ranchi Jail.

Concept note-3: Birsa Munda was a tribalfreedom fighter, religious leader, and folk hero who belonged to the Mundatribe. He spearheaded a tribal religious millenarian movement that arose inthe Bengal Presidency (now Jharkhand) in the late 19th century, duringthe British Raj.

Concept note-4: Not only does it happen to be thefoundation day of the State, but also the birth anniversary of the great freedomfighter, Birsa Munda, a figure who is worshipped like a God and is referred toas Bhagwan Birsa Munda. Bhagwan Birsa Munda is an illustrious hero ofJharkhand.

Concept note-5: Birsa Munda is related to Jharkhand. Hewas an Indian tribal freedom fighter, religious leader. He belongs to theMunda tribe of Jharkhand. Ranchi Airport (Birsa Munda) is named afterhim.
