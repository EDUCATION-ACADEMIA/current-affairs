Who is the author of the graphic novel Atharva-The Origin, in which Cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni has been portrayed as the superhero Atharva?

(A) Shamik Dasgupta

(B) ** Ramesh Thamilmani

(C) Sarnath Banerjee

(D) Ashok Banker

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Atharv, The Origin is a graphic novel with the storyby Ramesh Thamilmani and illustrations by Virzu Studios, and the entirething was quite unique to my taste. And yes, the face of the protagonistis M.S. Dhoni, and this idea is brilliant (to see from the marketing pointof view.)

Concept note-2: What is Atharva: The Origin? The novel istouted as India’s first three-dimensional graphic novel with a realistic visualinterpretation of about 150 scenes. Former Indian cricket captain MS Dhoni as amythological superhero on the cover of the novel.

Concept note-3: Theco-collaborators on the project Virzu Studios and MIDAS Deals Pvt ltdalso released an official trailer on their YouTube Channel on 24 February2022. Atharva is a story of a warrior leader portrayed by Captain Cool MSDhoni.

Concept note-4: The comic is developed in vertical reading format whichmakes it convenient for scrolling and reading on Pratilipi Comics’ app. MS Dhonicommented, “It is great to see all the hard work come to life in ‘Atharva;The Origin’.
