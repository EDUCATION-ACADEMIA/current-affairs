Who is the chairman of the restructured advisory committee on mutual funds by SEBI?

(A) Neeraj Choksi

(B) Dharmishta Narendraprasad Raval

(C) Sunil Gulati

(D) ** Usha Thorat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Markets regulator Sebi has restructured its advisory committee on mutual funds. The 25-member advisory committee will be chaired by former Deputy Governor of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) Usha Thorat„ according to the latest update with the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Sebi).

Concept note-2: -S Sahoo, Professor at National Law University, Delhi, and former chairperson of the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India (IBBI) will chair the advisory committee.

Concept note-3: -Sebi has restructured its advisory committee on market data that recommends policy measures pertaining to securities market data access and privacy. The market data advisory committee will now have 20 members, the latest information on the Securities and Exchange Board of India’s (Sebi) website showed.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI). Markets regulator Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has restructured its advisory committee on mutual funds.
