Who is the head of the Committee constituted by the Finance Ministry to address issues faced by VC and PE?

(A) Subash Chandra Garg

(B) ** M Damodaran

(C) Ajay Tyagi

(D) Mahalingam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Notes: The Finance Ministry constituted an expert committeeheaded by former SEBI chief M. Damodaran to examine and suggest measures toaddress regulatory issues to scale up investments by venture capital (VC) and privateequity (PE).

Concept note-2: The committee will be headed by former SEBIchairman Meleveetil Damodaran.

Concept note-3: The six-member panel will beheaded by former SEBI chairman M Damodaran.

Concept note-4: The FinanceMinistry on Tuesday constituted an expert committee under the chairmanshipof former SEBI chairman Meleveetil Damodaran with a view to identifymeasures to unravel regulatory hurdles for venture capital and private equityinvestors.

Concept note-5: The government has constituted an expert panel tosuggest ways for scaling up of VC/PE investments, according to a circular releasedon September 13. The six-member panel will be headed by former Securities andExchange Board of India (SEBI) chairman M Damodaran.
