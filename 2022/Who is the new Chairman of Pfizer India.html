Who is the new Chairman of Pfizer India?

(A) ** Pradip Shah

(B) Samir Kazi

(C) Uday Khanna

(D) S. Sridhar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Pfizer India on Monday appointed Pradip Shah as thechairman of its board following the resignation of R A Shah on February3.

Concept note-2: Chairman & Independent DirectorMr. Pradip Shah holds anMBA from the Harvard Business School. He is also a Chartered Accountant and aCost Accountant and ranked first in India in the Chartered AccountancyExamination.

Concept note-3: Pradip Shah is the ex-managing director and afounding member of Crisil. Prior to founding Crisil, he assisted in founding HDFC in1977. He has also served as a consultant to USAID, the World Bank, and the AsianDevelopment Bank.
