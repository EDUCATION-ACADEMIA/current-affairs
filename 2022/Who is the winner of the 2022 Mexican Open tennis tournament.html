Who is the winner of the 2022 Mexican Open tennis tournament?

(A) Novak Djokovic

(B) Stefanos Tsitsipas

(C) Daniil Medvedev

(D) ** Rafael Nadal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which Tennis player won the Mexican Open Title 2022? Notes: Rafael Nadal defeated Cameron Norris 6-4, 6-4 to win the Mexican Open 2022. He also extended his best-ever start of his career at 15-0 as he won his 91st ATP title.

Concept note-2: -Nadal became the sole holder of the men’s singles record after edging past Roger Federer and Novak Djokovic with his 2022 Australian Open triumph. He then extended his lead at the French Open, before Djokovic pulled back within one at Wimbledon.

Concept note-3: -Rafael Nadal wins 2022 French Open, extending men’s Grand Slam titles record to 22. Rafael Nadal defeated Casper Ruud 6-3, 6-3, 6-0 in the men’s 2022 French Open singles final Sunday, extending his record to 22 Grand Slam titles.
