Who launched the logo of Purple Fest at a function held in Manohar Parrikar Memorial Hall of Sanjay Centre for Education, Porvorim?

(A) ** Pramod Sawant

(B) Narendra Modi

(C) Amit Shah

(D) P.S. Sreedharan Pillai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Hon’ble CM Pramod Sawant along with Subhash Phaldessai,Hon’ble Minister for Social Welfare, Hema Sardesai, playback singer, GuruprasadPawaskar, the State Commissioner for PwDs and others at the Purple Fest logolaunch event.

Concept note-2: Virendra Kumar said, Goa was earlier known forits New Year celebrations but now with the introduction of Purple Fest, Goa willestablish a milestone in Divyangjan empowerment.

Concept note-3: India’sfirst-of-its-kind inclusivity festival, “Purple Fest: Celebrating Diversity,” kicked offwith a grand ceremony on January 6, 2023 in Goa. The festival aims to bring peopletogether and create a welcoming and inclusive world for all.

Concept note-4: Thefestival is India’s first inclusive event celebrating persons with disabilities (PwDs).Sawant said the reservation quota in Goa is the highest in the nation, which has beenincreased from 3% to 4%.
