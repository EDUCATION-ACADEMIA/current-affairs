Who received the Sahitya Academy Awards 2021 in the English language?

(A) ** Namita Gokhale

(B) Anuradha Sarma

(C) Niranjan Hansda

(D) Sanjiv Verenkar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Author Namita Gokhale won the Sahitya Akademi Award 2021in English for her novel ‘Things to Leave Behind’; the award was given to19 other authors in various languages.

Concept note-2: Mumbai: The‘Sahitya Akademi Award’ for 2022 will be given to Anuradha Roy for EnglishLiterature, Badri Narayan for Hindi Literature and Praveen Bandekar for MarathiLiterature.

Concept note-3: Sahitya Akademi confers Awards and Fellowships,publishes books and organizes literary programmes in all these 24 recognizedlanguages.
