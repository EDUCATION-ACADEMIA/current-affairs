Who topped ‘Arton Capital’s list of the world’s strongest passports?

(A) Cuba

(B) Togo

(C) Canada

(D) ** UAE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Answer: According to Henley Passport Index 2022, Japan isthe best passport to have, while the Arton Capital Passport Index names the UAE asthe most powerful passport. Question: Is the UAE’s passport the best? Answer: UAEpassport is the best according to the Arton Capital as it has an access to over 121countries.

Concept note-2: At the top of Arton’s power ranking, holders of aUnited Arab Emirates passport can travel visa-free or obtain visas on arrival in 181countries and territories. U.S. passport holders rank a bit lower, with access to173 countries.

Concept note-3: According to Arton Capital’s “PassportIndex 2022,” India is placed 66(as of 16th December 2022) in the world’sstrongest passport list, while the United Arab Emirates (UAE) is ranked 1stin the passport ranking for 2022.

Concept note-4: Japan has toppedthe list of the world’s most powerful passports for the fifth year in a row.Japanese citizens enjoy visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to 193 countries – or85% of the world – making the Japanese passport the most powerful in theworld.
