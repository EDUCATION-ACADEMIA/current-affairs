Who was appointed as chairman of the National Commission for Backward?

(A) ** Hansraj Gangaram Ahir

(B) Anil kumar Dube

(C) Satish Sing

(D) Devendr Bhardwaaj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri. Hansraj Gangaram Ahir assumed charge as Chairperson, National Commission for Backward Classes here today.

Concept note-2: -(3) The Chairperson, Vice-Chairperson and other Members of the Commission shall be appointed by the President by warrant under his hand and seal.

Concept note-3: -Shri Hansraj Gangaram Ahir assumed charge as Chairperson, National Commission for Backward Classes on 02-12-2022.

Concept note-4: -The Government of India had appointed the Second Backward Classes Commission in 1979. It was headed by B.P. Mandal.
