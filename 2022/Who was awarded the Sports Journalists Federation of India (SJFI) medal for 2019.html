Who was awarded the Sports Journalists’ Federation of India (SJFI) medal for 2019?

(A) ** Prakash Padukone

(B) Vijay Amritraj

(C) Mary Kom

(D) Mithali Raj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Badminton legend Prakash Padukone was awarded the SportsJournalists’ Federation of India (SJFI) medal for 2019 at a warm ceremony at theKarnataka State Cricket Association here on Friday. The medal was instituted in2018, with Vijay Amritraj the first winner.

Concept note-2: The correctanswer is Sunil Gavaskar. The Sports Journalists Federation of India (SJFI)has decided to confer the prestigious ‘SJFI Medal’ to legendary cricketerSunil Gavaskar. Neeraj Chopra was adjudged the SJFI Sportsman of theYear while weightlifter Mirabai Chanu was voted as the Sportswoman of theYear.

Concept note-3: Madhavi Gaur Published On October 16th, 2022. PrakashPadukone awarded with SJFI medal: The Sports Journalists’ Federation of Indiamedal (SJFI medal) for 2019 was presented to badminton legendary starPrakash Padukone during a cordial ceremony at the Karnataka State CricketAssociation.
