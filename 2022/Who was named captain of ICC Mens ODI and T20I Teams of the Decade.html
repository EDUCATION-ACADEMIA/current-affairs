Who was named captain of ICC Men’s ODI and T20I Teams of the Decade?

(A) V Sehwag

(B) G Gambhir

(C) ** MS Dhoni

(D) R Dravid

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The ICC announced the Men’s T20I and ODI teams of thedecade on Sunday, naming former India captain MS Dhoni as the leader of bothsides. Virat Kohli announced captain of Test side.

Concept note-2: ICCAwards of the Decade: MS Dhoni named skipper of the ODI team of thedecade as other Indian cricketers in the XI include Virat Kohli and RohitSharma.

Concept note-3: UPSC MainsNotes: Former Indian cricket teamcaptain, Mahendra Singh Dhoni was selected as the captain of both theESPNCricinfo’s ODI and T20 teams of the decade.

Concept note-4: After the2021 T20 World Cup, Rohit took over as T20I captain, beginning with the homeseries against New Zealand. He took over Virat Kohli as ODI captain in December2021.
