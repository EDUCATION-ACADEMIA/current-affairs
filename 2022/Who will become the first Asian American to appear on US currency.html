Who will become the first Asian American to appear on US currency?

(A) Kamala Harris

(B) Hiram Fong

(C) David Chiu

(D) ** Anna May Wong

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Anna May Wong will become the first Asian Americanfeatured on U.S. currency The U.S. Mint will begin shipping quarters featuringactress Anna May Wong on Monday. Wong championed the need formore representation and less stereotypical roles for Asian Americans onscreen.

Concept note-2: Wong, who got her first leading role in 1922, was a‘courageous advocate’ who fought for representation in Hollywood. WhenAnna May Wong first entered Hollywood in the 1920s, US entertainmentwas dominated by white filmmakers and actors.

Concept note-3: AnnaMay Wong, largely considered the first Chinese American movie star, hasmade history as the first Asian American to appear on U.S. currency. She’llappear on quarters, as part of the U.S. Mint’s new American Women QuartersProgram.

Concept note-4: Consumers will soon see a new face peering upat them from the United States quarter: Anna May Wong, the glamorousChinese-American movie star. When the U.S. Mint begins shipping the new coins onMonday, Wong will become the first Asian American to appear on the country’scurrency.
