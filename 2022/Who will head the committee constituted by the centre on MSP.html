Who will head the committee constituted by the centre on MSP?

(A) Kaushik Basu

(B) C Rangarajan

(C) ** Sanjay Agarwal

(D) Arvind Subramanian

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The committee, headed by former agriculture secretary SanjayAgrawal, discussed ways to “promote zero budget based farming", to “change” croppatterns keeping in mind the changing needs of the country, and to make MSP more“effective and transparent".

Concept note-2: The Cabinet Committee onEconomic Affairs chaired by Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi hasapproved the increase in the Minimum Support Prices (MSP) for all mandated RabiCrops for Marketing Season 2023-24.

Concept note-3: Ans. Based onthe recommendations of the Commission for Agricultural Costs and Prices,the Department of Agriculture and Co-operation, Government of India,declares Minimum Support Prices (MSP) for 22 crops before their sowingseasons.
