Who will host the Ministerial Conference on the Financing of Terrorism?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) Sri Lanka

(C) China

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: New Delhi, India/18 November 2022: Hosted and chaired bythe Government of India, the third ‘No Money for Terror’ Ministerial Conference onCounter-Terrorism Financing was held in New Delhi, attended by 450 seniorgovernment delegates from 70 nations and opened by the Prime Minister of India,Narendra Modi.

Concept note-2: India Is Hosting the ‘No Money for Terror’Conference.

Concept note-3: Amit Shah, the Home Minister of India, noted thatthe use of cryptocurrencies for terror funding has gone up in the recent past. Whilespeaking at the third ‘No Money for Terror’ Ministerial Conference on Friday, theHome Minister said, “We have a new challenge in the form of Virtual Assets beforeus.

Concept note-4: Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India is organisingthe 3rd Ministerial ‘No Money for Terror’ Conference on 18th and 19th ofNovember in New Delhi.

Concept note-5: Sources of terrorist fundinginclude, but are not limited to, low-level fraud, kidnapping for ransom, themisuse of non-profit organisations, the illicit trade in commodities (suchas oil, charcoal, diamonds, gold and the narcotic “captagon”), and digitalcurrencies.
