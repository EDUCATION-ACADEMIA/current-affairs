Who will inaugurate the National Conference of Rural Cooperative Banks in New Delhi?

(A) ** Amit Shah

(B) Narendra Modi

(C) Rajnath Singh

(D) Nitin Gadkari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Report of the Committee on Urban Cooperative Banks, 1978 (Madhava Das Committee) provided a well-documented study of urban banking sector in India and set standards of viability for sustained growth of urban banks.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank regulates the banking functions of StCBs/DCCBs/UCBs under the provisions of Sections 22 and 23 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (As Applicable to Cooperative Societies (AACS).

Concept note-3: -The Indian banking system consists of 12 public sector banks, 22 private sector banks, 44 foreign banks, 56 regional rural banks, 1,485 urban cooperative banks, and 96,000 rural cooperative banks in addition to cooperative credit institutions.

Concept note-4: -Union Minister of Home and Cooperation, Shri Amit Shah addressed the ‘Cooperative Beneficiaries Conference’ in Bengaluru, Karnataka today.
