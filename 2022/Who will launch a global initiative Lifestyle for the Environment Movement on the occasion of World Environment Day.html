Who will launch a global initiative ‘Lifestyle for the Environment Movement, on the occasion of World Environment Day?

(A) ** Narendra Modi

(B) Yogi Adithyanath

(C) Ram Nath Kovind

(D) Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi launched a global initiative ‘Lifestyle for the Environment – LiFE Movement’ today via video conference.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Thursday launched Mission LiFE movement (Lifestyle for the Environment) at the Statue of Unity in Gujarat.

Concept note-3: -Mr Aniruddha Dasgupta, CEO and President of World Resources Institute also thanked the Prime Minister for a much needed global movement and conversation on how we live, how we consume and how we take care of the planet.

Concept note-4: -The Government of India launched National Action Plan on Climate Change (NAPCC) on 30thJune, 2008 outlining eight National Missions on climate change. These include: National Solar Mission. National Mission for Enhanced Energy Efficiency.
