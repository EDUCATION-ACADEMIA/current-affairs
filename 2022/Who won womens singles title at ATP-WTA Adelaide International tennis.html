Who won women’s singles title at ATP-WTA Adelaide International tennis?

(A) Elina Svitolina

(B) Marta Kostyuk

(C) ** Ashleigh Barty

(D) Dayana Yastremska

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Belinda Bencic captured a title Down Under for the first timein her career, dropping only two games in an emphatic victory over Daria Kasatkinain the Adelaide International 2 final.

Concept note-2: Belinda Bencic defeatedDaria Kasatkina in the final, 6–0, 6–2 to win the women’s singles tennis title at the2023 Adelaide International 2. Madison Keys was the reigning champion from 2022,when the event was a WTA 250 tournament, but withdrew after competing at theUnited Cup.

Concept note-3: Thanasi Kokkinakis defeated Arthur Rinderknechin the final, 6–7, 7–6, 6–3 to win the men’s singles title at the 2022 AdelaideInternational 2. It was his maiden ATP Tour title, and he won it in his hometown ofAdelaide.

Concept note-4: Ashleigh Barty and Gaël Monfils were crowned thewomen’s and men’s singles champions, respectively.

Concept note-5: ThanasiKokkinakis’s brilliant run at the Adelaide International has come to an end after apulsating semifinal clash with Spain’s Roberto Bautista Agut. The world No. 26 heldhis nerve in front of the Australian’s home crowd to record a 7-6(4) 3-6 6-3victory.
