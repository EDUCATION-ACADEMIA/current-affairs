Who among the following has been nominated as the CEO-designate of Deloitte India?

(A) Charu Sehgal

(B) ** Romal Shetty

(C) Nandita Shyamsunder Pai

(D) Krishna Rangnath Chaturvedi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shetty will succeed N Venkatram, who led Deloitte India to its highest growth years in the firm’s history.

Concept note-2: -Romal Shetty, CEO-Designate, Deloitte India, talks about the merits of digitalisation in India and how it is India’s turn to be the front office of the world and no longer the back office.

Concept note-3: -Senior consultant Romal Shetty has been nominated CEO-designate of Deloitte India after a months-long selection process, according to an email sent to the firm’s partners late on Tuesday.

Concept note-4: -Romal Shetty is President, Consulting at Deloitte India and is based out of the Bengaluru office. He has 20 years of experience in advisory services across large scale transformation programs covering the business and technology domain and has worked in over 30+ countries across risk and consulting domain.
