Who has become the first Indian to be named for the Boltzmann Medal in 2022 for contribution in the field of statistical physics?

(A) Meghnad Saha

(B) Ashoken Sen

(C) ** Deepak Dhar

(D) Piara Gill

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Deepak Dhar, physicist, from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Pune, has been selected for the Boltzmann medal, awarded by the Commission on Statistical Physics (C3) of the International Union of Pure and Applied Physics. Concept note-2: -Recently, the prestigious Boltzmann Medal for the year 2022 has been awarded to Professor Deepak Dhar. He became the first Indian to win this award and shared the award with American scientist John Hopfield.

Concept note-3: -It was initiated in 1975, with Nobel laureate (1982) K.G. Wilson being the first recipient.
