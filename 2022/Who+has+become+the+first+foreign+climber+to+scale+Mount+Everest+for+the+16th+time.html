Who has become the first foreign climber to scale Mount Everest for the 16th time?

(A) Karina Oliani

(B) Adele Pennington

(C) Carlos Hatuka

(D) ** Kenton Cool

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kenton Edward Cool (born 30 July 1973) is an English mountaineer and mountain guide. He is one of Britain’s leading alpine and high altitude climbers and has reached the summit of Mount Everest sixteen times, including leading Sir Ranulph Fiennes’ 2008 and 2009 Expeditions.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Kenton Cool. A British climber scaled the world’s highest mountain for the 16th time in May 2022 becoming the first foreign climber to make the most Mt Everest summits.
