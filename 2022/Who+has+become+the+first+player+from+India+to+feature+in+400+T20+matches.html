Who has become the first player from India to feature in 400 T20 matches?

(A) Suresh Raina

(B) Hardik Pandya

(C) ** Rohit Sharma

(D) Virat Kohli

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India vs South Africa : India captain Rohit Sharma became the first man from the country to complete 400 T20 matches. Rohit already holds the record for the most number of T20I appearances in men’s cricket.

Concept note-2: -Team India captain Rohit Sharma has become the first-ever Indian cricketer to feature in 400 matches in T20 cricket history. India’s second T20I against South Africa in Guwahati marks Rohit’s 400th T20 match. West Indian all-rounder Kieron Pollard holds the record of playing the most number of T20 matches (614).

Concept note-3: -Windies’ all-rounder Kieron Pollard has become the first cricketer to play 400 T20 matches, achieving the feat while playing for Melbourne Renegades against Adelaide Strikers in the BBL on Monday. Apart from Melbourne Renegades, Pollard has represented 11 other domestic teams including IPL franchise Mumbai Indians.

Concept note-4: -Rohit Sharma becomes the first Indian to play 400 matches in T20 history.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Rohit Sharma. Rohit Sharma is the first Indian male cricketer to play 100 matches of T-20 format. Stand-in captain Rohit Sharma became the first Indian cricketer and second in world cricket to complete 100 T20 Internationals.
