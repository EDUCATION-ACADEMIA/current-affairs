Who has been appointed as Director General of the Indian Ordnance Factory Service (IOFS)?

(A) Neha Uppal

(B) Harish Dubey

(C) ** Sanjeev Kishore

(D) M D Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Sanjeev Kishore, a 1985 batch officer of the Indian Ordnance Factory Service (IOFS), has taken over as the Director General Ordnance (C&S) with effect from 01-10-2022 upon the superannuation of Shri M K Grag.

Concept note-2: -Shri Kishore has held many senior positions, including that of the first CMD of Armoured Vehicles Nigam Ltd (AVNL), one of the seven new DPSUs formed by the Government of India in 2021.

Concept note-3: -DIRECTOR GENERAL ORDNANCE SERVICES & COLONEL COMMANDANT Lt Gen Suresh Chandra Tandi, Vishisht Seva Medal, the 27th Director General Ordnance Services assumed office on 07 Dec 2022.
