Who has been appointed as the new Director of the NCERT?

(A) Denesh Prasad Saklani

(B) Rajesh Prasad Saklani

(C) Rahul Prasad Saklani

(D) ** Dinesh Prasad Saklani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Professor Saklani is also a life member in the Indian History Congress; Uttarakhand History and Culture Association and of Book Club IIAS Shimla.

Concept note-2: -Prof. Dinesh Prasad Saklani has assumed the charge of the post of Director, NCERT Con14 February, 2022 (F/N).

Concept note-3: -Logo of NCERT The motto of NCERT is written in Sanskrit meaning “life eternal through learning”.

Concept note-4: -The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) is an autonomous organisation set up in 1961 by the Government of India to assist and advise the Central and State Governments on policies and programmes for qualitative improvement in school education.
