Who has been appointed as the new captain of the West Indies ODI and T20I Team?

(A) Shai Hope

(B) Jermaine Blackwood

(C) Shimron Hetmyer

(D) ** Nicholas Pooran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pooran will take over the captaincy for the West Indies ODI and T20I teams after being Pollard’s deputy over the last year. The appointment will include the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup in 2022 and the ICC Men’s Cricket World Cup in October 2023. Shai Hope has been recommended to stand as vice-captain of the ODI team.

Concept note-2: -Cricket West Indies, the board that handles the international team had appointed Pooran as the captain for the 2022 edition of the T20 World Cup, as well as the 2023 edition of the ODI World Cup. At the time Shae Hope was appointed as the deputy.

Concept note-3: -As of 1 Sep. 2022, West Indies Cricket Team is captained by Kraig Brathwaite in Tests and by Nicholas Pooran in ODIs and T20Is. Phil Simmons is the head coach.

Concept note-4: -The 15-member West Indies squad for T20 World Cup 2022 has been announced – here is the complete team list for the T20 event, including injury news, reserve players and replacement updates for West Indies at the tournament. The team will be led by Nicholas Pooran, while Rovman Powell has been named vice-captain.
