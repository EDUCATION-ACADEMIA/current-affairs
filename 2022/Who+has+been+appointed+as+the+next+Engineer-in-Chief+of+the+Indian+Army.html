Who has been appointed as the next Engineer-in-Chief of the Indian Army?

(A) General K.S. Thimayya

(B) ** General Arvind Walia

(C) General Sir Roy Bucher

(D) General J.N. Choudhary

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lieutenant General Arvind Walia assumed the appointment of Engineer-in-Chief at IHQ of MoD (Army), New Delhi from 01 Jan 2023.

Concept note-2: -Lieutenant General Arvind Walia has been appointed as the next Engineer-in-Chief of the Indian Army. He would be succeeding Lt Gen Harpal Singh who is superannuating on December 31.

Concept note-3: -Lt Gen Rana Pratap Kalita, UYSM, AVSM, SM, VSM and alumnus of Sainik School Goalpara, National Defence Academy and Indian Military Academy. He was commissioned in 9 KUMAON on 09 June 1984.

Concept note-4: -General Manoj Pande is the current Chief of Army Staff and he took over the office on April 30, 2022.

Concept note-5: -LT GEN AMARDEEP SINGH BHINDER, AVSM, VSM, GOC-IN-C, SOUTH WESTERN COMMAND. Lieutenant General Amardeep Singh Bhinder, AVSM, VSM took over the command of South Western Command on 1st Apr 2021.
