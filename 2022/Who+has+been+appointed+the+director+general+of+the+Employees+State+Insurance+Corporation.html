Who has been appointed the director general of the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation?

(A) Rajni Mishra

(B) Parul Dahiya

(C) Manjeet Kataria

(D) ** Rajendra Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajendra Kumar takes over the charge of Director General, ESIC.

Concept note-2: -The 189th Meeting of Employees’ State Insurance Corporation was held today at ESIC Headquarters under the Chairmanship of Shri Bhupender Yadav, Union Minister for Labour and Employment, and Environment, Forest and Climate Change.

Concept note-3: -Rajendra Kumar, a 1992 batch IAS officer from the Tamil Nadu cadre, has taken charge as Director-General, Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) on October 4.

Concept note-4: -ESI Scheme Chairman : Union Minister of Labour, Govt. of India.
