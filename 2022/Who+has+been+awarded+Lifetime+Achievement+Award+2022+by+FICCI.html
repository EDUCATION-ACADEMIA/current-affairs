Who has been awarded Lifetime Achievement Award 2022 by FICCI?

(A) Jayanti Prasad

(B) ** Rajendra Pawar

(C) Rajiv Kumar

(D) Rajarshi Gupta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi, 27 November 2022: At the TURF2022 & India Sports Awards of FICCI, former Ranji cricketer Sarkar Talwar was honoured with the Lifetime Achievement of the Year award on Saturday.

Concept note-2: -Held at the historic Wilshire Ebell Theater in Los Angeles, the ceremony honored Bobby McFerrin, Nirvana, Ma Rainey, Nile Rodgers, Slick Rick, The Supremes and Ann Wilson and Nancy Wilson of Heart with the Recording Academy’s 2023 lifetime achievement awards.

Concept note-5: -Houston, Dec 8 (PTI) US President Joe Biden has recognised Indian-American and a longtime Houstonian, Krishna Vavilala, with the Presidential Lifetime Achievement (PLA) Award, the nation’s highest honour for his contributions to his community and the country at large.
