Who has been declared the winner of the Miss India Worldwide 2022, the longest-running Indian pageant outside of India?

(A) Shrutika Mane

(B) Roshani Razack

(C) ** Khushi Patel

(D) Vaidehi Dongre

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Khushi Patel. Khushi Patel, a biomedical student from the United Kingdom, has been declared the winner of the Miss India Worldwide 2022. Vaidehi Dongre from the US was declared the first runner up while Shrutika Mane was adjudged the second runner-up.

Concept note-2: -Khushi Patel, a biomedical science major-cum-clothing store owner from UK, wins Miss India Worldwide 2022. Patel said that she is delighted to have won the Miss India Worldwide 2022 pageant.

Concept note-3: -Khushi Patel from United Kingdom wins Miss India Worldwide title.

Concept note-4: -Miss World 1st runner-up Shree Saini was happy meeting Miss Universe Harnaaz Sandhu at an event. Shree shared a note on the similarities she shares with Harnaaz.

Concept note-5: -Belonging from Karnataka, Sini Shetty won the title of Miss India 2022 in the Femina Miss India beauty pageant. Manasa Varanasi passed on the ground to her in a glamorous and dazzling ceremony yesterday.
