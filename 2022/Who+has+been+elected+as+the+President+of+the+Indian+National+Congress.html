Who has been elected as the President of the Indian National Congress?

(A) Priyanka Gandhi

(B) ** Mallikarjun Kharge

(C) Sonia Gandhi

(D) Shashi Tharoor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sonia Gandhi is the longest-serving president of the party, having held office for over twenty years from 1998 to 2017 and again from 2019 till 2022. Mallikarjun Kharge is the current serving President. The district party is the smallest functional unit of Congress.

Concept note-2: -Mallikarjun Kharge won the election amassing 7,897 votes compared to 1,072 of Shashi Tharoor. The election was triggered by resignation of Rahul Gandhi as party leader on 10 August 2019.

Concept note-3: -Sonia Gandhi is the longest serving president of the party, having held the office for over twenty years from 1998 to 2017 and from 2019 to 2022.
