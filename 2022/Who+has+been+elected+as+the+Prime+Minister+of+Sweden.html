Who has been elected as the Prime Minister of Sweden?

(A) Ebba Busch

(B) ** Ulf Kristersson

(C) Jimmie Akesson

(D) Johan Pehrson

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of 2022, the prime minister of Sweden is Ulf Kristersson, leader of the Moderate Party .

Concept note-2: -The prime minister is appointed by the monarch, through the exercise of the royal prerogative. In the past, the monarch has used personal choice to dismiss or appoint a prime minister (the last time being in 1834), but it is now the case that they should not be drawn into party politics.

Concept note-3: -Ulf Hjalmar Ed Kristersson (born 29 December 1963) is a Swedish politician who has been serving as Prime Minister of Sweden since October 2022.
