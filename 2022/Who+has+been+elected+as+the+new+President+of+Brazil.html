Who has been elected as the new President of Brazil?

(A) ** Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva

(B) Antonio Brito

(C) Jair Bolsonaro

(D) Geraldo Alckmin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva is the 39th and current president. He was sworn in on 1 January 2023 following the 2022 Brazilian general election.

Concept note-2: -General elections were held in Brazil on 2 October 2022 to elect the president, vice president, the National Congress, the governors, vice governors, and legislative assemblies of all federative units, and the district council of Fernando de Noronha.

Concept note-3: -Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva (Brazilian Portuguese: [luˈiz iˈnasju ˈlulɐ dɐ ˈsiwvɐ] ( listen); born Luiz Inácio da Silva; 27 October 1945), known mononymously as Lula, is a Brazilian politician and trade unionist who has served as the 39th president of Brazil since 1 January 2023.

Concept note-4: -The presidency of Jair Bolsonaro started on January 1, 2019, when he was inaugurated as the 38th president of Brazil, and ended on December 31, 2022, with the inauguration of the cabinet of Lula da Silva III on January 1, 2023.
