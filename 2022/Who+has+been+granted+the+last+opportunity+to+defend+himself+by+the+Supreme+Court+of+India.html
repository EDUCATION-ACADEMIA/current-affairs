Who has been granted the last opportunity to defend himself by the Supreme Court of India?

(A) Lalit Modi

(B) Mehul Choksi

(C) Nirav Modi

(D) ** Vijay Mallya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Contempt Case: SC Gives Last Opportunity To Vijay Mallya To Defend Himself.

Concept note-2: -Vijay Mallya fled to the UK in 2016 following the charges of bank loan default to the tune of over ₹9,000 crore associated with the collapse of his Kingfisher Airlines in 2012.

Concept note-3: -Vijay Mallya has been accused of conspiring to defraud the members of the company and his employees along with various banks in the country.

Concept note-4: -Mallya is accused of money laundering, misappropriation, and other crimes. He has been hiding in the UK where his extradition was cleared by the UK high court in April 2020.
