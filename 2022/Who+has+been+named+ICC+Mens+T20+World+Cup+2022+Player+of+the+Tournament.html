Who has been named ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022 Player of the Tournament?

(A) ** Sam Curran

(B) Virat Kohli

(C) Suryakumar Yadav

(D) Jos Buttler

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the final, England beat Pakistan by five wickets to win their second ICC Men’s T20 World Cup title. Sam Curran was named the Player of the match and also the Player of the tournament.

Concept note-2: -The T20I Cricketers of the Year both find themselves at the summit of the MRF Tyres ICC T20I Player Rankings for batters – India’s Suryakumar Yadav claiming the ICC Men’s T20I Cricketer of the Year award, and Australia’s Tahlia McGrath named the ICC Women’s T20I Cricketer of the Year.

Concept note-3: -England captain Jos Buttler claimed the ICC Men’s Player of the Month award for November after being at his remarkable best with the bat in his side’s charge to their T20 World Cup triumph. Buttler’s month started with a Player of the Match performance in the dramatic 20-run victory over New Zealand in Brisbane.
