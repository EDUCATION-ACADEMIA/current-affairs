Who has been named as the new CEO of the Data Security Council of India?

(A) Bipin Rawat

(B) Mukul Bhandarkar

(C) Bepisha Yadav

(D) ** Vinayak Godse

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Godse will be taking over as the CEO from October 1. The final decision was taken by a selection committee of the DSCI Board Chaired by former DSCI chairman Dr. N Balakrishnan, current chairman Rajendra S Pawar, NASSCOM President, and other board members.

Concept note-2: -Vinayak Godse, CEO Vinayak Godse is the CEO of Data Security Council of India. He has been associated with DSCI since its inception in 2008.

Concept note-3: -Data Security Council of India New CEO: Data Security Council of India (DSCI), a leading industry organisation founded by NASSCOM, promoted senior vice president Vinayak Godse.
