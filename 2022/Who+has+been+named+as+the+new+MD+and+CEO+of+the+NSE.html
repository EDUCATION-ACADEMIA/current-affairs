Who has been named as the new MD and CEO of the NSE?

(A) K V Kamath

(B) Urjit Patel

(C) Arundhati Bhatacharya

(D) ** Ashish Chauhan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Meet Ashish Chauhan - The New Md And Ceo Of National Stock Exchange.

Concept note-2: -Chauhan took over the helm of NSE from Vikram Limaye, whose five-year term ended on July 16. Leading bourse National Stock Exchange (NSE) on Sunday said that shareholders have approved the appointment of Ashishkumar Chauhan as its managing director and chief executive.

Concept note-3: -His appointment at the top post of the stock exchange was cleared by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) on July 18.
