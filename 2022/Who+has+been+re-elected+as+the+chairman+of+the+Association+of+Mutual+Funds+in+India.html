Who has been re-elected as the chairman of the Association of Mutual Funds in India?

(A) ** A Balasubramanian

(B) Aryama Sundaram

(C) Abhishek Singhvi

(D) R Venkataramani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mutual funds industry body Amfi on Friday said Aditya Birla Sun Life AMC managing director and chief executive A Balasubramanian has been re-elected as its chairman.

Concept note-2: -A Balasubramanian, MD & CEO of Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund, has been re-elected as the Chairperson of Association of Mutual Funds in India (AMFI), at the recent Board Meeting of AMFI post the 27th AGM held in September 2022.

Concept note-3: -A Balasubramanian re-elected Amfi chairman, Radhika Gupta vice-chairperson.

Concept note-4: -The Association of Mutual Funds in India (AMFI) is dedicated to developing the Indian Mutual Fund Industry on professional, healthy and ethical lines and to enhance and maintain standards in all areas with a view to protecting and promoting the interests of mutual funds and their unit holders.

Concept note-5: -As far as mutual funds are concerned, SEBI formulates policies, regulates and supervises mutual funds to protect the interest of the investors. SEBI notified regulations for mutual funds in 1993.
