Who has been signed as the Brand ambassador of Gulf Oil India?

(A) M S Dhoni

(B) Jhulan Goswami

(C) ** Smriti Mandhana

(D) All of these

Concept note-2: -Smriti Mandhana as the brand ambassador of Gulf Oil Gulf Oil Lubricants, a lubricant manufacturing and marketing company, has roped in Indian cricketer Smriti Mandhana as its new brand ambassador. Mandhana will join cricketers and current brand ambassadors Mahendra Singh Dhoni and Hardik Pandya.
