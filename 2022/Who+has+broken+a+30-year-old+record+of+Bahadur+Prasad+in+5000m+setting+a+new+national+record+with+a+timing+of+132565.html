Who has broken a 30-year-old record of Bahadur Prasad in 5000m, setting a new national record with a timing of 13:25.65?

(A) Serpa Sanjay Thapa

(B) Bahadur Prasad

(C) ** Avinash Sable

(D) Veer Singh Rana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s Avinash Sable broke a 30-year-old record of Bahadur Prasad in 5000m, setting a new national record with a timing of 13:25.65 at the Sound Running Track meet in San Juan Capistrano, USA. The 27-year-old from Maharashtra broke the old record of 13:29.70 set by Bahadur Prasad in 1992.
