Who has defeated Juventus 4-2 after extra time in the Italian Cup final?

(A) ** Inter Milan

(B) Roma

(C) Cagliari Calcio

(D) Fiorentina

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Seven finals have been decided by a penalty shoot-out, the most recent being between Napoli and Juventus in 2020. The current champions are Inter Milan, who beat Juventus 4–2 after extra time in the 2022 final.

Concept note-2: -Inter Milan defeated Juventus 4-2 after extra time in the Italian Cup final.

Concept note-3: -As of 2022, Juventus had won the most Coppa Italia titles in Italy, winning the competition 14 times in total. The team from Turin is followed by AS Roma, who have won on nine occasions. Inter last won the cup in the 2021/22 season.

Concept note-4: -It was played on 11 May 2022 between Juventus and Internazionale. The Stadio Olimpico in Rome hosted the final. Internazionale won the match 4–2 after extra time for their eighth Coppa Italia title.

Concept note-5: -With 36 Serie A trophies in total, Turin-based Juventus FC held the record for the most Serie A championships won in Italy.
