Who has given in the Times Business Award 2022 for Eastern India’s Leading READY-TO-EAT brand?

(A) Vineta Suyal

(B) Roshni Dixit

(C) ** Rashmi Sahoo

(D) Deepika Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mrs Rashmi Sahoo, founder FROZIT was honoured with MOST PROMISING BRAND IN READY-TO-EAT in eastern India. The award was given by Bollywood actor Sonu Sood at Times Business Award 2022, at Welcom Hotel, ITC, Bhubaneswar.

Concept note-2: -Sahoo won the coveted Best Woman Entrepreneur Award 2022 this year for her leadership. Share: The director of the ready-to-eat food company FARRMB from Odisha, as well as the creator of FROZIT Rashmi Sahoo, sparkled at the recent International Women’s Entrepreneurial Challenge-2022 conference in Madrid, Spain.

Concept note-3: -1 spices company, Rashmi Sahoo has been presented the Times Business Award 2022.

Concept note-4: -The Institute of Directors (IoD) awards are a celebration of leadership talent, success and achievement. You can nominate yourself or nominate someone you know who represents excellence as a business leader and who you feel deserves recognition.

Concept note-5: -The National Entrepreneurship Awards have been instituted by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MoSDE) in 2016 to encourage a culture of entrepreneurship across the country.
