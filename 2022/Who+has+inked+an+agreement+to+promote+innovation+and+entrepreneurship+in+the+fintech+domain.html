Who has inked an agreement to promote innovation and entrepreneurship in the fintech domain?

(A) Gift City

(B) Fintech Association of Japan

(C) Bajaj Finserv

(D) ** Both A and B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -16. Who has inked an agreement to promote innovation and entrepreneurship in the fintech domain? Explanation: The Gujarat International Finance Tec-City (Gift City) has inked an agreement with the Fintech Association of Japan (FAJ) to promote innovation and entrepreneurship in the fintech domain.

Concept note-2: -Hamdard Pakistan (HP) and Excellence Delivered (ExD) inked an agreement to promote innovation and automation of enterprise functions through the effective utilization of SAP S/4 Hana.

Concept note-3: -Lead aggregation - Lead aggregation that involves compiling data from a number of marketing and allied activities and then trying to tap into prospective customers is not Fintech.
