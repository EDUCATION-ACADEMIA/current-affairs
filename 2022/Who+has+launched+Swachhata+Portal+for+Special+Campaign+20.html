Who has launched Swachhata Portal for Special Campaign 2.0?

(A) ** Jitender Singh

(B) Amit Shah

(C) Smriti Irani

(D) Narender Modi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Science & Technology, Minister of State (Independent Charge) Earth Sciences; MoS PMO, Personnel, Public Grievances, Pensions, Atomic Energy and Space, Dr Jitendra Singh today launched exclusive “Swachhata” Portal for the Special Campaign 2.0, scheduled to begin on October 2.

Concept note-2: -Special Campaign 2.0 is an ongoing endeavour undertaken by Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology (MeitY) from 02.10. 2022 to 31.010. 2022 to undertake Swachhta activities within office premises of the Ministry and all its organizations across the country.

Concept note-3: -Campaign aimed to achieve space management and enhancement of workplace experience in offices. The Department of Justice has participated in the Special Campaign 2.0 Campaign on Swachhta across its premises at Jaisalmer House, New Delhi since its commencement from October 2, 2022.

Concept note-4: -The main aim of the programme is to raise awareness among the masses on single-use plastic and waste management, The campaign was started on October 1st and will continue till october 31st 2022.

Concept note-5: -The Swachh Bharat Mission – Gramin (SBM-G) programme, under the Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation, is a flagship programme of the Government of India. The e-Governance application used for its monitoring has been developed in-house by NIC under the guidance of the SBM-G programme division.
