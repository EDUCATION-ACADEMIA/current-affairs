Who has scripted history as he became the first batter in history to reach 4000 runs in T20 Internationals?

(A) Paul Stirling

(B) Rohit Sharma

(C) ** Virat Kohli

(D) Martin Guptill

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Senior India batter Virat Kohli scripted history in the T20 World Cup semi-final against England on Thursday as he became the first batter to complete 4,000 runs in T20Is.

Concept note-2: -India’s premier batsman Virat Kohl became the first batter in history to score 4000 runs in T20 Internationals. Kohli reached the milestone against England in the second semi-final of the T20 World Cup 2022 in Adelaide on Thursday.

Concept note-3: -As of January 2023, Aaron Finch of Australia was the individual with the highest score in Twenty20 international cricket.

Concept note-4: -Most centuries in T20 Internationals 2023 Rohit Sharma, current Indian team captain, has scored the most centuries in T20I cricket with the highest score of 118.
