Who has signed MoU with the Chamba district of Himachal Pradesh to develop a green Hydrogen project?

(A) NTPC

(B) ONGC

(C) SJVN

(D) ** NHPC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: State-owned NHPC has signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with the district administration of Chamba, Himachal Pradesh for development of green hydrogen technologies to produce hydrogen. This project will be implemented by NHPC Renewable Energy Limited (NREL), a wholly-owned subsidiary of NHPC.

Concept note-2: -The National Hydroelectric Power Corporation (NHPC) will set up a green hydrogen fuel generation plant in Chamba district soon. It would be the first hydrogen fuel plant in Himachal Pradesh. The National Hydroelectric Power Corporation (NHPC) will set up a green hydrogen fuel generation plant in Chamba district soon.

Concept note-3: -"Under the project, a 300 kilowatt solar plant will be set up and its energy will be utilised for electrolysis of water to generate green hydrogen. Further hydrogen will be stored in cylinders in compressed form which will be used to run vehicles,” A K Pathak, CEO of NREL told PTI over phone.

Concept note-4: -2.8 Government of India will accordingly implement the Mission through a comprehensive and integrated approach through various Central and State Government agencies. The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy will be responsible for the overall coordination for implementation of the Mission.

Concept note-5: -Prime Minister laid the foundation stone of the Green Hydrogen Mobility Project in Leh which is a pilot project and aims for five Fuel Cell Buses to be run in and around Leh. This pilot project would be the first deployment of Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles for public use in India.
