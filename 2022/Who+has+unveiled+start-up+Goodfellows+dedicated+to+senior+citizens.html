Who has unveiled start-up Goodfellows dedicated to senior citizens?

(A) Mukesh Ambani

(B) Saurab Gupta

(C) Gautam Adani

(D) ** Ratan Tata

Concept note-1: -Ratan Tata unveils start-up Goodfellows dedicated to senior citizens. Concept note-2: -Since its inception, the company has been working with about 20 elders. Shantanu Naidu, founder of senior citizenship company Goodfellows, shared in an emotional post what inspired him to begin something of his own, a start-up, in other words.

Concept note-3: -WHAT DOES GOODFELLOWS DO? Goodfellows hires youngsters who have the skills of empathy and emotional intelligence to work as the companions of senior citizens. They are also required to ease their day or help them with any task.

Concept note-4: -Industrialist Ratan Tata launched India’s companionship startup for senior citizens, Goodfellows, in a bid to encourage intergenerational friendships. Shantanu Naidu, an MBA from Cornell University, founded the Goodfellows startup.

Concept note-5: -This startup will offer support and friendship to the elderly.
