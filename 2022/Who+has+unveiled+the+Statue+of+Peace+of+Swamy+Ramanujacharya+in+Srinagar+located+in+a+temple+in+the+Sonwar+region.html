Who has unveiled the ‘Statue of Peace’ of Swamy Ramanujacharya in Srinagar, located in a temple in the Sonwar region?

(A) ** Amit Shah

(B) Ram Nath Kovind

(C) Narendra Modi

(D) Manoj Sinha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union home minister Amit Shah today virtually unveiled the ‘Statue of Peace’ of Swamy Ramanujacharya in Srinagar, located in a temple in the Sonwar region. Union home minister Amit Shah today virtually unveiled the ‘Statue of Peace’ of Swamy Ramanujacharya in Srinagar, located in a temple in the Sonwar region.

Concept note-2: -Home Minister Amit Shah unveiled the Statue of Peace of Swami Ramanujacharya in Srinagar through video conferencing. Lieutenant Governor Manoj Sinha, along with saints and followers of Swami Ramanujacharya from across the country attended the unveiling ceremony at Shuryar Mandir in Srinagar yesterday.

Concept note-3: -Today, the establishment of this peace statue in Kashmir is a very auspicious sign for the whole country and especially for Jammu and Kashmir. Union Home and Cooperation Minister Shri Amit Shah today unveiled the Statue of Peace of Swami Ramanujacharya in Srinagar in Jammu and Kashmir through video conferencing.

Concept note-4: -The statue has been installed at Vijay Vallabh Sadhana Kendra in Rajasthan.

Concept note-5: -PM to unveil ‘Statue of Peace’ to mark 151st Birth Anniversary celebrations of Jainacharya Shree Vijay Vallabh Surishwer Ji Maharaj on 16th November.
