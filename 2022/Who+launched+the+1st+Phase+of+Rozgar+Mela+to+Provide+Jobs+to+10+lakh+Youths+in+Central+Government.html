Who launched the 1st Phase of “Rozgar Mela” to Provide Jobs to 10 lakh Youths in Central Government?

(A) Chief Minister

(B) Governor

(C) President

(D) ** Prime Minister

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi today launched the first phase of “Rozgar Mela”, a recruitment drive for 10 lakh central government jobs through video conferencing in 50 centers across the country. Appointment letters were handed over to more than 75,000 newly appointed persons during the function.

Concept note-2: -PM Modi on 22 October launched the ‘Rozgar Mela’, a recruitment drive for 10 lakh personnel, through video conferencing today at 11 am. During the ceremony, letters of appointment will be handed over to 75,000 newly inducted appointees, a statement from Prime Minister’s Office (PMO) said.

Concept note-3: -PM Modi launches Rozgar Mela, releases 71,000 job-offer letters to freshers | Business Standard News.

Concept note-4: -A NSDC Rozgar Mela is a 1/2-day event where several employers and job seekers come together for the purpose of applying and interviewing for jobs. Defined more precisely, a Rozgar Mela is an employment strategy to fast-track the meeting of job seekers and employers.

Concept note-5: -Rozgar Mela is a significant step forward towards fulfilling the continuous commitment of the Central Government to provide job opportunities for the youth. The new recruits, selected from across the country, will join various Ministries/ Departments of Government of India.
