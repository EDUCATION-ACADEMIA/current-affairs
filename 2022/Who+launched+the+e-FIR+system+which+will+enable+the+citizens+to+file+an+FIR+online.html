Who launched the e-FIR system which will enable the citizens to file an FIR online?

(A) Piyush Goyal

(B) Nitin Gadkari

(C) ** Amit Shah

(D) Narendra Modi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Union Minister Home Minister Amit Shah launched the e-FIR system of Gujarat Police in Gandhinagar. The system will enable the citizens to file an FIR online, without visiting the police stations.

Concept note-2: -In simple words, it is a complaint lodged with the police by the victim of a cognizable offence or by someone on his or her behalf, but anyone can make such a report either orally or in writing to the police. People can also lodge an FIR through online medium.

Concept note-3: -A complaint is a written or verbal statement made by a person to a police officer or other authority, alleging that a crime has been committed. A First Information Report (FIR) is a formal document prepared by the police when they receive information about the commission of a cognizable offence.
