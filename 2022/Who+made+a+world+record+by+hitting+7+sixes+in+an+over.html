Who made a world record by hitting 7 sixes in an over?

(A) Joe Root

(B) ** Rituraj Gaikwad

(C) Virat kohli

(D) MS Dhoni

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India batsman Ruturaj Gaikwad has created a huge List A cricket record by slamming 7 sixes in an over.

Concept note-2: -The latest addition to the list is Indian youngster Ruturaj Gaikwad who scored 42 runs in an over for his state team Maharashtra. A no-ball from Shiva, the bowler on the receiving end, helped Ruturaj Gaikwad hit 7 sixes in an over. With this, he made a world record for most runs in a List A over by a single batter.

Concept note-3: -Ruturaj Gaikwad hit a world record seven sixes in the 49th over of Maharashtra’s Vijay Hazare Trophy quarter-final against Uttar Pradesh in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Rituraj Gaikwad, playing for Maharashtra, became the first batsman in List-A cricket to hit 7 sixes in an over in the quarter-finals.

A total of 43 runs came in this over. Out of these 42 runs were from Rituraj’s bat and one run was of no ball.
