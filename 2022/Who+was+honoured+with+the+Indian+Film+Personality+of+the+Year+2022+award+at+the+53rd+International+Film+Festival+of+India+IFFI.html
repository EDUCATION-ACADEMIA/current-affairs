Who was honoured with the Indian Film Personality of the Year 2022 award at the 53rd International Film Festival of India (IFFI)?

(A) ** Chiranjeevi

(B) Amitabh Bhachchan

(C) Kamal Hassan

(D) Mohanlal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mega Star of Tollywood, Padma Bhushan awardee Shree Konidela Siva Sankara Vara Prasad popularly known as Chiranjeevi has been presented with Indian Film Personality of the Year Award for 2022, at the closing ceremony of 53rd edition of International Film Festival of India (IFFI) in Goa today.

Concept note-2: -Chiranjeevi honoured with the Indian Film Personality of the Year 2022 award at 53rd IFFI. Chiranjeevi at 53rd IFFI in Goa. Veteran actor Chiranjeevi was honoured with the Indian Film Personality of the Year 2022 award at the closing ceremony of the 53rd International Film Festival of India (IFFI) in Goa on Monday.

Concept note-3: -The 53rd edition of International Film Festival of India (IFFI) has concluded with Spanish film ‘I Have Electric Dreams’ directed by Valentina Maurel winning the prestigious ‘Golden Peacock’ for the best film of the festival.

Concept note-4: -The 53rd edition of International Film Festival of India (IFFI) held its coveted award ceremony on the closing day of the festival on Monday in Goa. While Spanish coming-of-age drama I Have Electric Dreams won the Golden Peacock award, Vahid Mobasheri and Daniela Marin Navarro took home the top acting honours.

Concept note-5: -Here are the winners of the film festival: No End - Nader Saeivar, Best Director. Best Male Actor - Vahid Mobasheri, No End. Best Female Actress - Daniela Marin Navarro, I Have Electric Dreams. Behind the Haystacks by Asimina Proedrou, is the Best Debut Feature Film by a new Director.
