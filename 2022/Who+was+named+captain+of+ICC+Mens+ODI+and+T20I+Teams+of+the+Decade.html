Who was named captain of ICC Men’s ODI and T20I Teams of the Decade?

(A) V Sehwag

(B) G Gambhir

(C) ** MS Dhoni

(D) R Dravid

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MS Dhoni named captain of ICC Men’s ODI and T20I Teams of the Decade, Kohli leads Test side | Cricket - Hindustan Times.

Concept note-2: -ICC Awards of the Decade: MS Dhoni named skipper of the ODI team of the decade as other Indian cricketers in the XI include Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma.

Concept note-3: -The ICC’s ODI team of the decade includes Rohit and Virat, other than captain Dhoni. While David Warner and Rohit have been picked as openers, former South Africa cricketer AB De Villiers is selected as a middle-order batsman.

Concept note-4: -UPSC Mains Notes: Former Indian cricket team captain, Mahendra Singh Dhoni was selected as the captain of both the ESPNCricinfo’s ODI and T20 teams of the decade.
