Who won gold in wrestling in the 2022 National Games?

(A) Vinesh Phogat

(B) Bajrang Punia

(C) Neha Malik

(D) ** Antim Panghal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -U-20 world champion Antim Panghal wins gold in wrestling in National Games 2022.

Concept note-2: -The 16-year-old Aman Sehrawat beat junior European silver-medallist Ahmet Duman of Turkey 12-4 in the final to win the gold. Olympic medallists Bajrang Punia and Ravi Kumar Dahiya had made the final in their categories in previous editions but could only win the silver medal.

Concept note-3: -National Games 2022 medal tally: Know the final standing and winners. Services won the National Games 2022 with 128 medals, including 61 golds, 35 silvers and 32 bronze. The National Games 2022 was held across six cities, Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Surat, Vadodara, Rajkot and Bhavnagar, in Gujarat.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Five. India has so far won 5 medals in total in wrestling.

Concept note-5: -On Saturday night, Aman Sehrawat overwhelmed Turkey’s Ahmet Duman 12-4 to become the first Indian to win gold at the U-23 World Championships.
