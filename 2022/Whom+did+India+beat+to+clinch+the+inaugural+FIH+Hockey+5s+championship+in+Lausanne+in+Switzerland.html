Whom did India beat to clinch the inaugural FIH Hockey 5s championship in Lausanne in Switzerland?

(A) Canada

(B) Netherlands

(C) Australia

(D) ** Poland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India beat Poland 6-4 in final to clinch the inaugural FIH Hockey 5s championship in Lausanne in Switzerland on Sunday. India, ended their campaign with an unbeaten record.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. India beat Poland 6-4 in the final to clinch the inaugural FIH Hockey 5s championship in Lausanne in Switzerland on 5 June 22. India, who had topped the 5-team league standings with three wins and one draw towards the final, ended their campaign with an unbeaten record.

Concept note-3: -The competition marked the first time the International Hockey Federation hosted a senior international tournament in the Hockey5s format. The tournament was held simultaneously with a women’s event. India won the tournament, defeating Poland 6–4 in the final.
