Wings India 2022 has been organized by the Ministry of Civil Aviation and FICCI in which city?

(A) Kolkata

(B) Pune

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Asia’s largest event on Civil Aviation, Wings India 2022 haskicked off at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad. Led by the Ministry of Civil Aviation ofIndia (MoCA) and the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry(FICCI) jointly, the theme of the event this year is ‘India75: New Horizon forAviation Industry’.

Concept note-2: Q. Which city is set to host ‘WINGS INDIA2022’, the exhibition on civil aviation? Notes: Hyderabad is scheduled tohost the ‘WINGS INDIA 2022’, the flagship biennial exhibition on civilaviation. It is organised from March 24 - 27, 2022 at Begumpet Airport,Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: One of Asia’s biggest civil aviation shows, WingsIndia 2022, jointly organised by the Ministry of Civil Aviation (MOCA)and Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FICCI) washeld at the Begumpet airport recently.

Concept note-4: The event isscheduled from 18th to 21st, January 2024, at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad,India.
