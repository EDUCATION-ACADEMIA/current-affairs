Wings India 2022 has been organized by the Ministry of Civil Aviation and FICCI in which city?

(A) Kolkata

(B) Pune

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why in the News? The Ministry of Civil Aviation, Airports Authority of India (AAI) and Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FICCI) is jointly organising Wings India 2022 from 24th – 27th March 2022, Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad, India.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which city is set to host ‘WINGS INDIA 2022’, the exhibition on civil aviation? Notes: Hyderabad is scheduled to host the ‘WINGS INDIA 2022’, the flagship biennial exhibition on civil aviation. It is organised from March 24 - 27, 2022 at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: -WINGS INDIA AWARDS will also be conferred during this event. The event focuses on new business acquisition, investments, policy formation, and regional connectivity. It is being held from 24th to 27th March 2022, at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad. The theme of the event: India75: New Horizon for Aviation Industry.

Concept note-4: -The event is scheduled from 18th to 21st, January 2024, at Begumpet Airport, Hyderabad, India.
