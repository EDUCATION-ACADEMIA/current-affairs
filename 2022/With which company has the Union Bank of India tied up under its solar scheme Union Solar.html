With which company has the Union Bank of India tied up under its solar scheme ‘Union Solar’?

(A) IBM

(B) TCSL

(C) WIPRO

(D) ** TPSSL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Mumbai, 16th November 2022: Union Bank of India has tiedup with Tata Power Solar Systems Limited (TPSSL), a hundred percent subsidiary ofTata Power Renewable Energy Limited (TPREL) to help MSME (Micro, Small andMedium Enterprises) sector switch to solar solutions.

Concept note-2: Adanisolar is one of the largest solar panels installers in India. They provide a wide rangeof solar products for retail and commercial users.

Concept note-3: TataPower Solar is committed to enabling solar everywhere and aims to provideenergy access to millions of people across the country via its integrated solarsolutions.
