With which country India has signed MoUs in the field of culture, wildlife, and health?

(A) America

(B) Singapore

(C) France

(D) ** Cambodia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India and Cambodia today signed four MoUs in the areas of culture, wildlife and health. The MoUs were inked following bilateral talks between Vice President Jagdeep Dhankhar and Cambodian Prime Minister Hun Sen on the sidelines of the ongoing ASEAN Summit at Phnom Penh.
