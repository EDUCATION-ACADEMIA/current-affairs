With the help of the Ministry of Education and which organization has launched the “Kanya Shiksha Pravesh Utsav” campaign?

(A) UNESCO

(B) SEBI

(C) ** UNICEF

(D) world bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Women And Child Development In Partnership With The Ministry Of Education and UNICEF Launches Campaign ‘Kanya Shikhsa Pravesh Utsav’To Bring Back Out of School Adolescent Girls To The Formal Education.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of Women and Child Development (MoWCD), in collaboration with the Ministry of Education and UNICEF, has established the Kanya Shiksha Pravesh Utsav Abhiyan. The campaign’s purpose is to reintegrate four lakh out-of-school adolescent girls between the ages of 11 and 14 into the educational system.

Concept note-3: -A special campaign namely Kanya Shiksha Pravesh Utsav was launched on 7th March, 2022 to re-enroll out of school adolescent girls (OoS AG) in the age group of 11-14 years. The main components of the scheme are Social mobilization & Awareness and Incentivization of AWWs for Counselling and Referrals.

Concept note-4: -On the eve of International Women’s Day (8th March), the Kanya Shiksha Pravesh Utsav Campaign was launched by the Ministry of Women And Child Development in partnership with the Ministry of Education and UNICEF.

Concept note-5: -Recently, the government launched a nation-wide campaign called Kanya Shiksha Pravesh Utsav on the eve of the International Women’s Day (8th March). The aim of the campaign is to bring back four lakh out-of-school adolescent girls in the 11-14 years age group into the education system.
