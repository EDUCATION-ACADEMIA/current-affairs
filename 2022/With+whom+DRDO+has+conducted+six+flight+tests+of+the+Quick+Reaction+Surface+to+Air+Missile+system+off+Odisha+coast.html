With whom DRDO has conducted six flight tests of the Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile system off Odisha coast?

(A) Indian navy

(B) ISRO

(C) ** Indian army

(D) Indian airforce

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Army have successfully completed six flight-tests of Quick Reaction Surface to Air Missile (QRSAM ) system from Integrated Test Range (ITR) Chandipur off the Odisha coast. The flight tests were conducted as part of evaluation trials by the Indian Army.

Concept note-2: -India successfully test fires Agni Prime ballistic missile off Odisha coast.

Concept note-3: -It is a short-range surface-to-air missile (SAM) system, primarily designed and developed by DRDO to provide a protective shield to moving armoured columns of the Army from enemy aerial attacks.

Concept note-4: -The flight-test was carried out with participation of all BMD weapon system elements located at different geographical locations. The AD-1 is a long-range interceptor missile designed for both low exo-atmospheric and endo-atmospheric interception of long-range ballistic missiles as well as aircraft.

Concept note-5: -QRSAM is a short-range Surface to Air Missile (SAM) system, primarily designed and developed by the DRDO to provide a protective shield to the moving armoured columns of the Army from aerial attacks.
