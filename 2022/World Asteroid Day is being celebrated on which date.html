World Asteroid Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** June 30

(B) June 27

(C) June 28

(D) June 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: June 30 is observed as the International Asteroid Day aroundthe globe to educate people about asteroids. Every year, events are held on this dayin which astronauts and experts talk about the opportunities and risks associatedwith asteroids.

Concept note-2: Asteroid Day is observed annually on 30 June tomark the date of Earth’s largest asteroid impact in recorded history, the SiberiaTunguska event.

Concept note-3: On June 30, International Asteroid Day willhave everyone looking toward the skies. The holiday was founded after the 2014release of the film 51 Degrees North, which explores what would happen if anasteroid were to strike London.
