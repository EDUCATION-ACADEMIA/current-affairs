World Bamboo Day is being observed on which date?

(A) ** September 18

(B) September 17

(C) September 19

(D) September 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: It is September 18, which commemorates World Bamboo dayglobally. Mostly, the day is observed for promoting the benefits related to bambooand its large cultivation associated with it. Natural resources like bamboo need to beensured with sustainable utilization for various purposes across industriesglobally.

Concept note-2: World Bamboo Day 2022 is observed on September 18in order to raise awareness about the conservation of this extremely usefulplant.

Concept note-3: September 18 is World Bamboo Day!World Bamboo Dayis a day of celebration to increase the awareness of bamboo globally. Where bamboogrows naturally, bamboo has been a daily element, but its utilization has not alwaysbeen sustainable due to exploitation.

Concept note-4: This year the themeof World Bamboo Day 2022 is- Bamboo for Green Life and SustainableDevelopment.
