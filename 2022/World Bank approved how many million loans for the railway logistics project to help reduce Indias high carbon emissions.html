World Bank approved how many million loans for the railway logistics project to help reduce India’s high carbon emissions?

(A) 327

(B) 215

(C) ** 245

(D) 175

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The World Bank has approved a loan of USD 245 million tosupport the Railway Logistics Project. The loan will support India’s efforts tomodernise rail freight and logistics infrastructure. Railways are losing market share totrucks over time.

Concept note-2: World Bank Provides $245 Million to HelpIndian Railways Carry More Freight, Reduce GHG Emissions. WASHINGTON, June10, 2022 – The World Bank’s Board of Executive Directors today approved a $245million loan to support India’s efforts to modernize rail freight and logisticsinfrastructure.

Concept note-3: Yet, 71% of India’s freight is transported by roadand only 17% by rail.

Concept note-4: Detailed Solution. The correct answer isCoal.

Concept note-5: “The target of 40-45 per cent modal share for railways isnecessary from the perspective of sustainability and also from the nationalcommitments made globally for reducing emission levels,” stated the EconomicSurvey.
