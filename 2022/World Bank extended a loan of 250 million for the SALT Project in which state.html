World Bank extended a loan of $250 million for the SALT Project in which state?

(A) Karnataka

(B) ** Andhra Pradesh

(C) Telangana

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajasekhar has said that the World Bank (WB) has extended an unconditional loan of $250 million to the Supporting Andhra’s Learning Transformation (SALT) project in appreciation of the path-breaking reforms implemented by the State government.

Concept note-2: -The SALT Project is the first project in the school education sector to be funded by the World Bank without any precondition. Approximately ₹53,000 crores has been spent on school education in the last three years. In the academic year 2022-2023, more than 40,31,239 children were studying in government schools.

Concept note-3: -WASHINGTON, DC, September, 21, 2022 - The World Bank’s Executive Board of Directors today approved a $350 million loan to the western Indian state of Gujarat to improve the quality and access of its public healthcare services, with special focus on adolescent girls and disease surveillance.
