World Bank has approved a loan of $150 million (nearly Rs 1, 200 crores) to which state?

(A) Haryana

(B) ** Punjab

(C) Kerala

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: "The World Bank is pleased to be a partner of the state ofPunjab in the state’s effort to deliver timely, cost-effective and good-quality publicservices, which is critical for inclusive development,” said Auguste Tano Kouame, theWorld Bank’s Country Director in India.

Concept note-2: The funds-starvedcoffers of Punjab government has got a loan of $150 million (approximately Rs 1,200crore) from the World Bank to help the state better manage its financial resourcesand improve access to public services.

Concept note-3: WASHINGTON, DC,September, 21, 2022 - The World Bank’s Executive Board of Directors todayapproved a $350 million loan to the western Indian state of Gujarat to improve thequality and access of its public healthcare services, with special focus on adolescentgirls and disease surveillance.

Concept note-4: The World Bank on Fridaysaid its board of executive directors has approved a $150 million (about Rs1,112 crore)program to support Tamil Nadu’s vision of making Chennaiinto a world-class city that is more green, livable, and resilient to climatechange.
