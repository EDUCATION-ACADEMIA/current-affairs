World Book and Copyright Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) April 21

(B) April 20

(C) April 22

(D) ** April 23

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: World Book and Copyright Day is a celebration to promote theenjoyment of books and reading. Each year, on 23 April, celebrations take place allover the world to recognize the scope of books - a link between the past and thefuture, a bridge between generations and across cultures.

Concept note-2: Thetheme for World Book and Copyright Day 2022 is “Read, so you never feel low”. Themotive is to enhance the scope of reading.

Concept note-3: World Book andCopyright Day is a celebration to promote the enjoyment of books and reading. Eachyear, on 23 April, celebrations take place all over the world to recognize the scope ofbooks - a link between the past and the future, a bridge between generations andacross cultures.

Concept note-4: History of World Book and CopyrightDayAlthough World Book and Copyright Day was officially inaugurated in 1995, itsconception is traceable to Spanish writer, Vicente Clavel Andres, who conceived theidea in 1922. He developed the holiday to pay tribute to the notable author, Miguelde Cervantes.

Concept note-5: 23 April - World Book and Copyright DayEveryyear on 23rd April this day is celebrated to promote the enjoyment of books andreading.
