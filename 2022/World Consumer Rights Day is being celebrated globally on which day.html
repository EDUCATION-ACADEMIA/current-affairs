World Consumer Rights Day is being celebrated globally on which day?

(A) March 13

(B) March 14

(C) March 12

(D) ** March 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Every year 15th March is celebrated as the World ConsumerRights Day. World Consumer Rights Day was inspired by President John F Kennedy,who sent a special message to the US Congress on 15th March 1962, in which heformally addressed the issue of consumer rights.

Concept note-2: The theme for2021 was “Consumer- Know Your Rights.” The Consumer Rights Day theme 2022was “Fair Digital Finance.”

Concept note-3: World Consumer Rights Day iscelebrated on 15 March of every year. This day was inspired by a speech John F.Kennedy (former US President) gave on consumer rights on 15 March 1962 whilespeaking at the US Congress.

Concept note-4: Every Year 24th December isobserved as National Consumer Day with a specific theme in India. On this day theConsumer Protection Act, 1986 had received the assent of the president. Theenactment of this Act is considered as a historic milestone in the consumer movementin the country.
