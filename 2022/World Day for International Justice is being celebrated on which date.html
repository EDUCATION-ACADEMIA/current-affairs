World Day for International Justice is being celebrated on which date?

(A) July 16

(B) July 15

(C) ** July 17

(D) July 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The theme of World Day for International Justice 2022 is“Achieving Social Justice through Formal Employment”.

Concept note-2: July17 is observed as the World Day for International Justice all around the world. Theday aims to raise awareness about the crimes that put peace, security and well-beingof the world at risk. The day also promotes victims’ rights.

Concept note-3: OnJuly 17, 1998, 120 countries came together to sign a treaty titled the Rome Statute ofthe International Criminal Court.

Concept note-4: World Day of InternationalJustice: This day is celebrated annually to mark the anniversary of signing the RomeStatue Treaty in 1998 that to the establishment of International CriminalCourt. World Day of International Justice is celebrated every year on July,17.
