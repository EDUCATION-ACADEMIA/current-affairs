World Hepatitis Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) July 29

(B) July 27

(C) ** July 28

(D) July 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: World Hepatitis Day (WHD) is recognized annually onJuly 28th, the birthday of Dr. Baruch Blumberg (1925–2011). Dr. Blumbergdiscovered the hepatitis B virus in 1967, and 2 years later he developed thefirst hepatitis B vaccine.

Concept note-2: World Hepatitis Day takesplaces every year on 28 July to raise awareness and promote global action onviral hepatitis.

Concept note-3: In 2022 the theme is ‘I Can’t Wait’. OnWorld Hepatitis Day, 28 July, we call on people from across the world to takeaction and raise awareness of hepatitis because Hepatitis Can’t Wait. WHD isa day for the world’s hepatitis community to unite and make our voicesheard.

Concept note-4: The date of 28 July was chosen because it is thebirthday of Nobel-prize winning scientist Dr Baruch Blumberg, who discoveredhepatitis B virus (HBV) and developed a diagnostic test and vaccine for thevirus.

Concept note-5: World Hepatitis Day is observed each year on 28th Julyto enhance awareness of viral hepatitis. The theme for the year 2022 is “Bringinghepatitis care closer to you”.
