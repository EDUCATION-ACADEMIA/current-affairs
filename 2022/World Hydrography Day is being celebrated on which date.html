World Hydrography Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) June 19

(B) ** June 21

(C) June 18

(D) June 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: The U.N. Charters the International HydrographicOrganizationto collect data on hydrography. According to a resolution adopted bythe U.N., June 21 is designated as World Hydrography Day to appreciate the effortsof hydrographers.

Concept note-2: World Hydrography Day, 21 June,was adopted by the International Hydrographic Organization as an annualcelebration to publicise the work of hydrographers and the importance ofhydrography.

Concept note-3: The theme for 2022 is “Hydrography -contributing to the United Nations Ocean Decade". The goal of this year’scommemoration is to highlight how hydrography as an applied science supports thesustainable use of the oceans.

Concept note-4: The Work and Contribution ofHydrographersOrganizations involved in hydrography may wish to use this day tohighlight the relevance of their activities and to raise awareness of the importance ofthe oceans and waterways to our lives.

Concept note-5: The origins ofhydrography lay in the making of charts to aid navigation, by individual mariners asthey navigated into new waters. These were usually the private property, evenclosely held secrets, of individuals who used them for commercial or militaryadvantage.
