World Hypertension Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) May 16

(B) ** May 17

(C) May 15

(D) May 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: In observance of World Hypertension Day 2022 -conmemorated every May 17th, the Pan American Health Organization (PAHO),through the HEARTS in the Americas Initative in collaboration with TheLancet Regional Health-Americas, kindly invites you to attend the webinar onFriday 13 May, 2022.

Concept note-2: World Hypertension Day – May17.

Concept note-3: The objective of WHD is to communicate to the public theimportance of hypertension and its serious medical complications, and to provideinformation on prevention, detection and treatment. The WHL launched its firstWHD on May 14, 2005. Since 2006, the WHL has been dedicating May 17 of everyyear as WHD.

Concept note-4: World Hypertension Day: “Measure your bloodpressure, control it, live longer”
