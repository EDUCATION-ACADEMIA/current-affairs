World Music day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) June 18

(B) June 23

(C) ** June 21

(D) June 19

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Music Day is celebrated on 21st June every year. The day originated in France in 1982, and since then, it has been celebrated all over the world with music enthusiasm. World Music Day is about coming together and rejoicing in the joy of making or listening to music.

Concept note-2: -Fleuret, who was a composer, music journalist, radio producer and festival organiser, played a crucial role in initiating a day for celebrating music. The concert took place on June 21, 1982, in several public locations in Paris. It celebrated professional and amateur musicians from the remotest corners of France.

Concept note-3: -The International Music Day 2022 theme is “Music on the intersections.” The theme implies that music is at the crossroads of several identity intersections as a joining force.

Concept note-4: -Initiated in 1973 by the 15th General Assembly of the International Music Council, the first International Music Day was celebrated in 1975. The intention behind the day was to promote musical art among all sectors of society as well as applying the UNESCO ideas of peace and friendship among people.
