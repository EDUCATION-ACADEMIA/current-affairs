World Portuguese language Day is being celebrated every year on which date?

(A) May 04

(B) May 03

(C) ** May 05

(D) May 02

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Portuguese Language Day, celebrated on May 5 every year, is dedicated to highlighting the Portuguese language. It plays a role in bringing the Portuguese people together and in preserving their culture and identity.

Concept note-2: -Portuguese is a Romance language that was developed over 2,000 years ago when the Romans arrived on the Iberian Peninsula, which is modern-day Portugal and Spain. Roman soldiers arrived speaking Vulgar Latin, a form of spoken Latin, which is the root of all Romance languages.

Concept note-3: -Portuguese (Português) is a Romance language, spread worldwide in the 15th and 16th centuries as Portugal created the first and the longest lived modern-world colonial and commercial empire (1415-1975).

Concept note-4: -The History of Portuguese The Portuguese language originated from Latin in the Western Iberian Peninsula. Roman soldiers and colonists introduced Latin in 216 BCE. The language extended to other regions by Roman soldiers, settlers, and merchants.
