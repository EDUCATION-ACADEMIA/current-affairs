World Rivers Day is celebrated every year on the Fourth Sunday of which month?

(A) August

(B) ** September

(C) July

(D) January

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: ON THE 4th SUNDAY OF EVERY SEPTEMBER. WorldRivers Day is a celebration of the world’s waterways. It highlights the many values ofrivers and strives to increase public awareness and encourages the improvedstewardship of rivers around the world.

Concept note-2: With World Rivers Daytaking place on the fourth Sunday of September, it’s time for us to reflect on thesignificance of rivers.

Concept note-3: A brief historyIn 2005, the United Nationslaunched the Water for Life Decade to help create a greater awareness of the need tobetter care for our water resources. Following this, the establishment ofWorld Rivers Day was in response to a proposal initiated by internationallyrenowned river advocate, Mark Angelo.

Concept note-4: In response toAngelo’s proposal, the United Nations established World Rivers Day, to beannually celebrated on the 4th Sunday of every September. The first event in2005 was marked by a huge success as millions of people joined hands tohelp conserve rivers around the world.

Concept note-5: World RiversDay is celebrated on the fourth Sunday of September every year to increasepublic awareness about rivers and to encourage their conservation. The daycelebrates Earth’s waterways, in which over 60 countries participate everyyear.
