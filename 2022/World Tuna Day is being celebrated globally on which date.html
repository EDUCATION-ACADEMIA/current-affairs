World Tuna Day is being celebrated globally on which date?

(A) May 01

(B) April 30

(C) April 29

(D) ** May 02

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 02 May. On May2 every year, World Tuna Day is celebrated to spread awareness amongpeople about Tuna fish regarding sustainable fishing practices. The day wasestablished by the UN in 2016 by the UN General Assembly to preserve the Tunafish.

Concept note-2: World Tuna Day is observed every year on May 2. Thisday is established by the United Nations General Assembly to raise awareness aboutthe importance of Tuna Day.

Concept note-3: Tuna is rich in Omega-3 and italso contains minerals, proteins, and vitamin B12, among other advantages. That iswhy in December 2016, the United Nations General Assembly voted to officiallyobserve World Tuna Day.

Concept note-4: There are 7 tuna species and 4 speciesof mackerel reported from the Bangladesh continental shelf (Khan, 1992). They areeither not exploited or exploited only as by catch or as incidental catches of the driftgillnet fisheries. These groups of fishes are not popular for food to the localpeople.

Concept note-5: The United Nations (U.N.) is responsible for settingoverall policies and guidelines for the management of fisheries – including tunafisheries – around the world. Adopted in 1982 and put into force in 1994, the U.N.Convention on the Law of the Seas (UNCLOS) is considered a “constitution for theoceans”.
