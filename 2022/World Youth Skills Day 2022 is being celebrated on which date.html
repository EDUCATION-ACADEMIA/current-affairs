World Youth Skills Day 2022 is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** July 15

(B) July 17

(C) July 16

(D) July 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Why is World Youth Skills Day important? In 2014, the UnitedNations General Assembly declared 15 July as World Youth Skills Day, to celebratethe strategic importance of equipping young people with skills for employment,decent work and entrepreneurship.

Concept note-2: The celebrations of WorldYouth Skills Day 2022 highlighted the ongoing focus on the Transforming EducationSummit (September 2022), and contributed to the work being done under itsThematic Action Track “Learning and skills for life, work, and sustainabledevelopment”.
