World Antimicrobial Awareness Week (WAAW) runs from which date every year?

(A) ** 18 to 24 November

(B) 17 to 23 November

(C) 16 to 22 November

(D) 19 to 25 November

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Antimicrobial Awareness Week (WAAW) is celebrated from 18-24 November every year.

Concept note-2: -WAAW is celebrated from 18 to 24 November every year.

Concept note-3: -World Antimicrobial Awareness Week | 18-24 November 2022. The theme of WAAW 2022 is “Preventing Antimicrobial Resistance together". AMR is a threat to humans, animals, plants and the environment. It affects us all.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is 16 November to 22 November 2015. The First World Antibiotic Awareness Week was observed from 16 November to 22 November 2015. It aims to increase awareness of antibiotic resistance.

Concept note-5: -The slogan of World Antimicrobial Awareness Week remains ‘Antimicrobials: Handle with Care’.
