World Bamboo Day is being observed on which date?

(A) ** September 18

(B) September 17

(C) September 19

(D) September 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is September 18, which commemorates World Bamboo day globally. Mostly, the day is observed for promoting the benefits related to bamboo and its large cultivation associated with it. Natural resources like bamboo need to be ensured with sustainable utilization for various purposes across industries globally.

Concept note-2: -World Bamboo Day 2022 is observed on September 18 in order to raise awareness about the conservation of this extremely useful plant.

Concept note-3: -This year the theme of World Bamboo Day 2022 is- Bamboo for Green Life and Sustainable Development.
