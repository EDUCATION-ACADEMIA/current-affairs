World Brain Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** July 22

(B) July 20

(C) July 24

(D) July 23

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Brain Day is observed on July 22 every year to create awareness on brain health.

Concept note-2: -Each year, the World Federation of Neurology-a non-governmental organization-celebrates World Brain Day on July 22.

Concept note-3: -People around the world celebrate World Brain Day on July 22 of every year. This is one of the most important annual celebrations in the world, as it helps raise awareness on an increasingly significant health issue, especially in our modern age.

Concept note-4: -More about World Brain Day from the World Federation of Neurology : “World Brain Day (WBD) 2022 is dedicated to the theme “Brain Health for all” as our brains continue to be challenged by pandemics, wars, climate change and the myriad of disorders impacting human existence globally.
