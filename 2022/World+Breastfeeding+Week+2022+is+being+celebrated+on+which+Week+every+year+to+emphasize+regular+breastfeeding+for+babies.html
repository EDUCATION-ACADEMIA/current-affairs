World Breastfeeding Week 2022 is being celebrated on which Week every year to emphasize regular breastfeeding for babies?

(A) August 02-08

(B) August 05-11

(C) August 03-09

(D) ** August 01-07

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The idea of celebrating it on a single day was later expanded to a week. It became known as World Breastfeeding Week (WBW), which is observed from August 1-7 to commemorate the Innocent Declaration. 1992 marked the beginning of World Breastfeeding Week. It is now observed in more than a hundred countries.

Concept note-2: -1-7 AUGUST 2022 This year, the theme for World Breastfeeding Week (WBW 2022) is Step up for Breastfeeding - Educate and Support. Since 2016, WBW has been aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Concept note-3: -The Annual Campaign WABA coordinates the global World Breastfeeding Week (WBW) campaign that aims to inform, anchor, engage and galvanise action on breastfeeding and related issues. WBW was started in 1992 to generate public awareness and support for breastfeeding. WBW is officially celebrated on 1-7 August every year.

Concept note-4: -It commemorates the Innocenti Declaration signed in August 1990 by government policymakers, WHO, UNICEF and other organizations to protect, promote and support breastfeeding.

Concept note-5: -Four to six weeks This period is the most critical time for building baby’s immune system. As your little one grows, your breast milk adapts to their needs, providing nutrients that are perfect for their developmental state.
