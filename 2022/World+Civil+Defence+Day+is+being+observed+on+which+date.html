World Civil Defence Day is being observed on which date?

(A) ** March 01

(B) March 02

(C) February 28

(D) February 27

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for International Civil Aviation Day 2022 is, “Advancing Innovation for Global Aviation Development”. The theme will remain the same until the year 2023.

Concept note-2: -The International Civil Defense Organization (ICDO) General Assembly instituted World Civil Defence Day in 1990.

Concept note-3: -World Civil Defence Day is celebrated on 1 March.

Concept note-4: -Every year, World Civil Defence Day is celebrated on March 1. The day was brought into existence by the International Civil Defence Organisation or the ICDO in 1990. We observe this day to honour the people who work for civil defence and make sacrifices to protect their nation.
