World Hearing Day is being celebrated every year on which date?

(A) ** March 03

(B) March 05

(C) March 04

(D) March 06

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first event was held in 2007. Before 2016 it was known as International Ear Care Day. Each year, the WHO selects a theme, develops educational materials, and makes these freely available in several languages. It also coordinates and reports on events around the globe.

Concept note-2: -World Hearing Day is held on 3 March each year to raise awareness on how to prevent deafness and hearing loss and promote ear and hearing care across the world.

Concept note-3: -The World Hearing Day 2022 with the theme “To hear for life, listen with care” will focus on the importance and means of hearing loss prevention through safe listening, with the following key messages: It is possible to have good hearing across the life course through ear and hearing care

Concept note-4: -The World Health Organization and all stakeholders are eagerly preparing for the upcoming World Hearing Day 2023. With this year’s theme, Ear and hearing care for all!
