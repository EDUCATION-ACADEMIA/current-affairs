World Malala day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) July 11

(B) July 13

(C) July 09

(D) ** July 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -July 12 is observed as International Malala Day to salute the Nobel Laureate Malala Yousafzai , who works for women’s right to education across the globe. Born in Swat Valley, Malala was never permitted to enter school as the Taliban had banned girls from going to school in Pakistan.

Concept note-2: -14 Nov The Fight Goes On: The International Malala Day One month after the 15-year-old Pakistani education activist, Malala Yousafzai was shot in the head by the Taliban, the United Nations called upon the international community to observe November 10 as the International Malala Day.

Concept note-3: -Malala Day 2022 provides an opportunity to world leaders to ensure compulsory and free education for every child. Malala Day 2022 is also a reminder of the courageous Malala Yousafzai who at a young age stood up against Taliban forces.
