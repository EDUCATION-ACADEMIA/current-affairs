World Milk Day is being celebrated on which day?

(A) ** June 01

(B) May 31

(C) May 30

(D) May 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -June 1 was chosen as the date because many countries were already celebrating a milk day during that time of year. The Day provides an opportunity to focus attention on milk and raise awareness of dairy’s part in healthy diets, responsible food production, and supporting livelihoods and communities.

Concept note-2: -Dr. Verghese Kurien’s birthday is commemorated on November 26th with the National Milk Day. He is credited with bringing India’s “white revolution.” The white revolution was started by Dr. Verghese Kurien, who also made India’s largest self-sustaining industry the dairy farm.

Concept note-3: -Join us in celebrating #WorldMilkDay June 1, 2023! In 2001, World Milk Day was established by the Food and Agriculture Organisation of the United Nations to recognize the importance of milk as a global food, and to celebrate the dairy sector.

Concept note-4: -There is no specific theme for National Milk Day 2022. Unlike World Milk Day, the National Dairy Development Board and Indian Dairy Association do not set any theme for Milk Day in India.
