World Standards Day is being observed on which date?

(A) October 25

(B) October 12

(C) October 19

(D) ** October 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Standards Day (or International Standards Day) is an international day celebrated internationally each year on 14 October.

Concept note-2: -World Standards Day 2022 History World Standards Day was first created in 1946 on October 14 in the presence of known delegates and experts from around 25 countries in London.

Concept note-3: -2022 Theme: Shared Vision for a Better World The objective is that by 2030, international standards are recognized as an integral part of the success of the SDGs.

Concept note-4: -National days on Sat Oct 14th, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s National Dessert Day, Universal Music Day, National Chess Day, I Love Yarn Day, Adventure Day… and much more!
