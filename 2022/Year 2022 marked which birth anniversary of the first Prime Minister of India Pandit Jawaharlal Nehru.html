Year 2022 marked which birth anniversary of the first Prime Minister of India Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru?

(A) 136th

(B) ** 133rd

(C) 135th

(D) 134th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Jawaharlal Nehru birth anniversary, Children’s Day 2022:Jawaharlal Nehru who served as the first Prime Minister of India was born onNovember 14, 1889 in Allahabad, now Prayagraj. Today is his 133rd birthanniversary.

Concept note-2: Veer Bal Diwas is a day to commemorate andhonour the martyrdom of sons of Guru Gobind Singh – Zorawar Singh and FatehSingh. On January 9 this year, the birth anniversary of Guru Gobind Singh, Modihad announced that December 26 will be observed as ‘Veer Bal Diwas’ tomark the martyrdom of the Sikh guru’s sons.

Concept note-3: Whatare the Slogans given by Jawaharlal Nehru? Laziness is harmful to oneself(Aaram haraam hai) is the famous slogan of Jawaharlal Nehru. Pandit Nehrugraduated from law school in 1912 and served as India’s first independent primeminister.

Concept note-4: November 14, 2023Jawaharlal Nehru was born onNovember 14, 1889, and was the Prime Minister of India for 16 years. He was aprominent figure in the middle of the 20th century, pioneering the Indian nationalistmovement.
