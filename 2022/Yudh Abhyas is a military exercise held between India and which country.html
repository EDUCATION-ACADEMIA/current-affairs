Yudh Abhyas is a military exercise held between India and which country?

(A) Brazil

(B) China

(C) ** US

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: “Exercise Yudh Abhyas is conducted annually between Indiaand USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, Tactics, Techniques andProcedures between the Armies of the two nations,” the Ministry of Defence had saidbefore the beginning of the exercise.

Concept note-2: Exercise Yudh Abhyas,conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging bestpractices, tactics, techniques and procedures between the Armies of the two nations.The previous edition of the exercise was conducted at Joint Base ElmendorfRichardson, Alaska (USA) in October 2021.

Concept note-3: Notably, theIndo-US military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas 2022’ commenced in Uttarakhand’s Auli onNovember 19.

Concept note-4: Vajra Prahar’ is a Special Forces joint trainingexercise conducted alternately in India and the US to share the best practicesand experiences in areas such as joint mission planning and operationaltactics as also to improve interoperability between the Special Forces of bothnations.

Concept note-5: Exercise “SURYA KIRAN” is conducted annuallybetween India and Nepal with the aim to enhance interoperability in jungle warfare& counter terrorism operations in mountainous terrain and HADR under UNmandate.
