As per the ‘Portrait of Immigration to Canada’ Report, which is the leading country of birth for recent immigration?

(A) China

(B) Indonesia

(C) ** India

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Almost one in five recent immigrants (18.6%) were born in India, making it the leading country of birth for recent immigration to Canada.

Concept note-2: -According to the report “Portrait of Immigration to Canada”, about 23 per cent of the population in the country is or has been landed immigrant or permanent resident. This is the largest share of immigrants since the country was founded in 1867. As of 2021, the country hosted 8.3 million immigrants.

Concept note-3: -Some 62% of the recent immigrant arrivals were born in Asia, with 18.6% coming from India alone, the leading source country of Canada’s newcomers. The Philippines accounts for 11.4% of newcomers, followed by China at 8.9%. This is the first time India took the top spot of Canada’s newcomers.

Concept note-4: -Almost a third of the new immigrants who arrived in Canada in 2021 were from India - by far the highest of any country. Eight percent of them were from China.

Concept note-5: -As reported earlier by TOI, Canada had admitted 4.05 lakh new permanent residents during 2021, of 1.28 lakh (32%) were Indian nationals. The country-wise break up for admissions in 2022 is not yet available, but India has been the largest source country for many years.
