Bird flu was reported from two villages in which state where a Rapid response team formed?

(A) Karnataka

(B) Manipur

(C) ** Kerala

(D) Tripura

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After confirmation of avian influenza (bird flu) positive samples in poultry (two poultry farms) of Panchkula district of Haryana, the state government has deployed nine Rapid Response team, and containment operation is underway in both the epicentres.

Concept note-2: -A bird flu outbreak has been reported in a state-run poultry farm in Kozhikode district of Kerala as around 1,800 chickens have died of the infection, official sources said.

Concept note-3: -Kerala declares Bird flu as state disaster.
