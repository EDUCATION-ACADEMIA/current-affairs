‘CAPSTONE’, which has been seen in the news, is a satellite launched by which space agency?

(A) ESA

(B) Rocosmos

(C) ** NASA

(D) SpaceX

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NASA’s CAPSTONE mission is a demonstration of a unique lunar orbit and a cutting-edge navigation system that can support the space agency’s Artemis Moon missions.

Concept note-2: -The OCEANSAT 3 satellite launched by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) carrying French Space Agency CNES’ payload was placed in orbit on Saturday November 26, 2022, by a PSLV launcher from the Satish-Dhawan space centre in Sriharikota, Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -On June 28, 2022 Rocket Lab launched the Cislunar Autonomous Positioning System Technology Operations and Navigation Experiment (CAPSTONE) spacecraft for NASA and Advanced Space.

Concept note-4: -13, 2022. On Nov. 13, CAPSTONE will reach the Moon and become the first spacecraft to enter into a unique, elongated orbit that will support NASA’s Artemis missions. This microwave oven-sized satellite, weighing just 55 pounds, will be the first CubeSat to fly to and operate at the Moon.

Concept note-5: -CAPSTONE – short for Cislunar Autonomous Positioning System Technology Operations and Navigation Experiment – is a technology demonstration, designed to prove the reliability of new capabilities so that they can be used in future missions.
