Climber Elnaz Rekabi, who was reported missing, belongs to which country?

(A) Qatar

(B) UAE

(C) Afghanistan

(D) ** Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Iranian competitive climber Elnaz Rekabi, who was believed to have gone missing, arrived back in Tehran today. Media reports say large crowds of supporters gathered at the Imam Khomeini International Airport outside of Tehran today morning to welcome her back.

Concept note-2: -Rekabi was reported missing following the final day of the competition in South Korea on October 16.

Concept note-3: -She is on her flight back with others and her brother is also OK at home in Zanjan. Nothing has happened to anyone yet and we hope that she will be safe while arriving in Iran.” Climbing reached out to the International Federation of Sport Climbing for comment.

Concept note-4: -Iranian climber Elnaz Rekabi competed without a headscarf at a contest in South Korea in Oct. She was forced to apologise.

Concept note-5: -Elnaz Rekabi, the Iranian climber who disappeared after competing without a hijab, resurfaces in Tehran. There had been no news of the champion climber since Monday after she appeared without a hijab at the final of the Asian climbing championships in Seoul on Sunday.
