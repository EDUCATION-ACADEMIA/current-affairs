CRPF, is being celebrated which edition of its Rising Day on 27 July 2022?

(A) 82nd

(B) 81st

(C) ** 84th

(D) 83rd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -I congratulate the CRPF personnel on the 83rd Raising Day and salute their service and dedication to the nation,” Amit Shah said. BJP national president JP Nadda extended his greetings to the CRPF on the 83rd Raising Day.

Concept note-2: -Western Sector CRPF celebrated 83rd CRPF day with 102 Bn RAF at Taloja, Navi Mumbai on 19 Mar 2022 and the 83rd CRPF day of the C.R.P.F. was also celebrated in various ways by the institutions and units under the Western Sector, CRPF, Navi Mumbai.

Concept note-3: -October 21st is celebrated every year as the Police Commemoration Day. The day marks the sacrifice of 10 CRPF men who lost their lives to a Chinese attack in 1959.

Concept note-4: -After Indian independence, it became the Central Reserve Police Force on enactment of the CRPF Act on 28 December 1949. Besides law and order and counter-insurgency duties, the CRPF has played an increasingly large role in India’s general elections.
