d Internet Exchange is to be set up in which city?

(A) Varanasi

(B) Agra

(C) Kota

(D) ** Visakhapatnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: Centre to establish internet exchange point in Visakhapatnamto propel IT sector growth: Rajeev Chandrasekhar. A total of 42 internet exchangesof National Internet Exchange of India(NIXI) have been installed and operational ason date.

Concept note-2: Explanation: 43rd Internet Exchange is to be set up inVisakhapatnam.

Concept note-3: Internet exchanges (IXs) facilitate theexchange of internet traffic reducing latency and cost of bandwidth. They are acrucial component of the Digital India campaign.

Concept note-4: An InternetExchange lets Internet service providers (ISPs), content delivery networks (CDNs),and other network providers exchange Internet traffic with one another, typically ona cost-neutral basis.

Concept note-5: National Internet Exchange ofIndia(NIXI)NIXI is managed and operated on a Neutral basis, in line with the bestpractices for such initiatives globally.
