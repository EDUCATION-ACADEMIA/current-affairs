Danish Siddiqui posthumously got which city’s Press Club’s RedInk Award?

(A) Chennai

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Delhi

(D) Puna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Mumbai Press Club instituted The RedInk Awards a decade ago to recognise good investigative and feature writing and raise the bar of journalism in the country. Apart from Siddiqui and Jha, variour other journalists were awarded in 12 categories as part of the 10th edition of the award event.

Concept note-2: -TNIE-Kerala chief photographer T P Sooraj wins Redink Award for ‘The Big Picture’ Instituted by the Mumbai Press Club, the Redink Awards honour excellence in Indian journalism.

Concept note-3: -T.J.S. George, the first Editor to be charged with sedition in free India-whose column ‘Point of View’ ran non-stop for 25 years and 1,300 episodes in ‘The New Indian Express’-bags the RedInk Award for Lifetime Achievement. #RedInkAwards2022 On a day tech journalists were banned by Twitter.

Concept note-4: -The winning entries in each category will be awarded a total cash prize of Rs. 1 Lakh, a trophy, and a citation.
