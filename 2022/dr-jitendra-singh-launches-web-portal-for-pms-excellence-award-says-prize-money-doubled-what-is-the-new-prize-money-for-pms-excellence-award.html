Dr. Jitendra Singh launches Web Portal for PM’s Excellence Award, says prize money doubled. What is the new Prize money for PM’s Excellence Award?

(A) 10 Lakh

(B) 5 Lakh

(C) 50 Lakh

(D) ** 20 Lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -10 lakh to Rs. 20 lakh to the awarded District/Organization which is to be utilized for implementation of project/programme or bridging resource gaps in any area of public welfare. The Minister informed that it is now mandatory for all District Collectors to apply for the Award.

Concept note-2: -Prize money for PM’s Excellence Awards will be Rs. 20 lakh in 2022.

Concept note-3: -The PM’s Awards portal was launched by Dr. Jitendra Singh, MOS(PP) on 3rd October, 2022. The registration on the PM’s Awards web portal - https://pmawards.gov.in/login commenced on 3rd October,2022. The portal for registration and submission nomination was operational from 3rd October, 2022 to 28th November, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister’s Awards for Excellence in Public Administration is awarded to acknowledge, recognize and reward the extraordinary and innovative work done by the Indian Civil Servants.
