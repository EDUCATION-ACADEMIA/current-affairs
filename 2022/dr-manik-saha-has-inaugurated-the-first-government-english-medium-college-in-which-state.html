Dr Manik Saha has inaugurated the first Government English Medium College in which state?

(A) Kerala

(B) ** Tripura

(C) Assam

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chief Minister of Tripura Dr. Manik Saha along with Education Minister Ratan Lal Nath inaugurated the first English medium general degree college of the state in the city having 100 intake capacity.

Concept note-2: -Manik Saha on Tuesday inaugurated the state’s first government-run English Medium College. The new college- Sri Aurobindo General Degree College has been set up at the old DIET building in Kunjaban of Agartala.

Concept note-3: -Manik Saha after inaugurating - State’s first government English medium college - Sri Aurobindo General Degree College at the old DIET building, Kunjaban, Agartala on Tuesday (Oct 18). The Chief Minister said Sri Aurobindo College is the first government English medium College of Tripura.

Concept note-4: -Manik Saha (born 8 January 1953) is an Indian politician serving as the current and the 11th Chief Minister of Tripura. Govt. Dental College, Patna, Bihar (B.D.S.) King George’s Medical College, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh (M.D.S.)
