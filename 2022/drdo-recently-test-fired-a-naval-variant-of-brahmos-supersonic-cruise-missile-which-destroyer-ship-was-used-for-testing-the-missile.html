DRDO recently test-fired a naval variant of BrahMos supersonic cruise missile. Which destroyer ship was used for testing the missile?

(A) INS Rajput

(B) INS Kolkata

(C) INS Chennai

(D) ** INS Visakhapatnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India on Friday successfully test-fired a naval variant of the advanced supersonic BrahMos cruise missile from a stealth guided-missile destroyer of the Indian Navy. The Indian Navy’s warship INS Visakhapatnam carried out a test firing of BrahMos supersonic cruise missile off the western seaboard.

Concept note-2: -On March 5, the Indian Navy successfully tested an advanced version of the Brahmos supersonic cruise missile from a stealth destroyer in the Indian Ocean. The missile was fired from the stealth destroyer INS Chennai.

Concept note-3: -On April 19, the Indian Air Force (IAF) successfully test-fired the BrahMos missile from a Sukhoi fighter jet on the Eastern seaboard.

Concept note-4: -Indian Navy’s warship INS Chennai carried out the test-firing of BrahMos supersonic cruise missile on Saturday.

Concept note-5: -The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) has successfully test-fired an advanced sea-to-sea variant of the BrahMos supersonic cruise missile.
