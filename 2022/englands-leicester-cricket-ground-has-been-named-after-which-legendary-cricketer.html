England’s Leicester Cricket Ground has been named after which legendary cricketer?

(A) Rahul Dravid

(B) ** Sunil Gavaskar

(C) VVS Laxman

(D) Sachin Tendulkar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In England , the Leicester Cricket Ground has been named after India’s legendary cricketer, Sunil Gavaskar. The former Indian cricket team captain Gavaskar is often considered one of the most skilled batters ever. He was the first cricketer to score 10,000 runs and once held the record for most number (34) of centuries.

Concept note-2: -Leicester Cricket Ground Named After Sunil Gavaskar, Legend Feels “Blessed". See Pic | Cricket News.

Concept note-3: -The Leicester Cricket Ground, the ownership of which lies with Bharat Sports and Cricket Club, has decided to name the ground after Gavaskar to acknowledge his immense contribution to uplifting Indian Cricket to a certain height.

Concept note-4: -Grace Road, known for sponsorship reasons as the Uptonsteel County Ground, Grace Road, is a cricket ground in Leicester, England.

Concept note-5: -The venue has attracted some of the world’s biggest names in music, whilst also hosting, smaller concert and outdoor events. With a capacity of up to 19,999 and over 400 Hospitality seats, the venue can be tailored to a variety of events.
