FSSAI has awarded Eat Right Station certification with a 4-star rating to which railway station?

(A) Lokmanya Tilak Railway Station

(B) Nagpur Railway Station

(C) CST Mumbai

(D) ** Bhopal Railway Station

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The other railway stations with Star certification include Anand Vihar Terminal Railway Station (Delhi); Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (Mumbai); Mumbai Central Railway Station, (Mumbai); Vadodara Railway Station, Chandigarh Railway Station and Bhopal Railway Station.

Concept note-2: -As such, the Bhopal Junction Railway Station of West Central Railway has been certified as ‘Eat Right Station’ with a 4-star rating by FSSAI India, the Ministry added.

Concept note-3: -Bhopal Railway Station awarded ‘Eat Right Station’ certification with a 4-star rating by FSSAI.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The Guwahati Railway Station has become the first ever railway station in the Indian Railways to get an ISO certification from the National Green Tribunal (NGT) for providing passenger amenities in a clean and green environment.
