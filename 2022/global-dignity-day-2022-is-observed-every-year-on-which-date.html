Global Dignity Day 2022 is observed every year on which date?

(A) ** October 19

(B) October 20

(C) October 21

(D) October 22

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global Dignity Day is observed every year on the 3rd Wednesday of October. This year Global Dignity Day is observed on the 19th of October. The day is an initiative to educate and inspire young people and help them to understand their self-worth and goals.

Concept note-2: -On the third Wednesday in October, we celebrate all that unites us during Global Dignity Day. Join us on October 18, 2023!

Concept note-3: -The right of every human being to live a dignified life.

Concept note-4: -Global Dignity teaches the concept of dignity-every person’s equal and inherent value-to promote inclusion, equality and a greater sense of our shared humanity.

Concept note-5: -On this Day, voluntary facilitators conduct events by visiting schools and tell students about their lives and experiences with dignity. The voluntary facilitators can be of any nationality and any profession. The celebration Day is all about the attitude of the speaker’s life and is less about the profession.
