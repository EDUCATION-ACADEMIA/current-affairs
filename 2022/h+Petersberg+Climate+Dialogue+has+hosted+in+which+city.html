h Petersberg Climate Dialogue has hosted in which city?

(A) Tokyo

(B) ** Berlin

(C) New Delhi

(D) Paris

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 13th Petersberg Climate Dialogue began in Berlin, Germany.

Concept note-2: -The central goal of the Petersberg Climate Dialogue which takes place from 17 to 19 July in Berlin is to strengthen trust both in multilateral climate negotiations and between states.

Concept note-3: -Speeches were given by the Federal Minister for the Environment, Nature Conservation, and Nuclear Safety in Germany, Svenja Schulze and by Alok Sharma (president of COP26) and António Guterres, Secretary-General of the United Nations.

Concept note-4: -Since then, it has been held in Berlin. From 2011 to 2021, the Petersberg Climate Dialogue was organised by the Environment Ministry. Following its assumption of responsibility for climate diplomacy, the Federal Foreign Office is hosting the conference for the first time in 2022.

Concept note-5: -The 13th Petersburg Climate Dialogue’ 2022 will open way for a successful global climate conference, which is scheduled to be held in Egypt in November 2022. Petersburg Dialogue was started in 2010 by former German Chancellor Angela Merkel.
