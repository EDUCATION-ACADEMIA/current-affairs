Hardik Pandya led Team India against which country for the T20I series?

(A) Australia

(B) Kenya

(C) ** Sri Lanka

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Suryakumar Yadav named vice-captain for the T20 squad while Pandya was elevated as deputy for Rohit in ODIs. The transition phase in Indian cricket kick-started on December 27, 2022 with premier all-rounder Hardik Pandya being named captain of the T20 side for the upcoming series against Sri Lanka starting January 3.

Concept note-2: -Indian Squad. Hardik Pandya (C), Surya Kumar Yadav (VC), Ruturaj Gaikwad, Shubman Gill, Ishan Kishan, Sanju Samson, Yuzvendra Chahal, Harshal Patel, Umran Malik, Mukesh Kumar, Rahul Tripathi, Deepak Hooda, Washington Sundar, Axar Patel, Shivam Mavi and Arshdeep Singh.

Concept note-3: -India’s squad for Sri Lanka T20Is: Hardik Pandya (Captain), Ishan Kishan (wk), Ruturaj Gaikwad, Shubman Gill, Suryakumar Yadav (VC), Deepak Hooda, Rahul Tripathi, Sanju Samson, Washington Sundar, Yuzvendra Chahal, Axar Patel, Arshdeep Singh, Harshal Patel, Umran Malik, Shivam Mavi, Mukesh Kumar.

Concept note-4: -India Squad New Zealand T20 Series As Rohit Sharma and Virat Kohli continue to be absent from the India Squad New Zealand Series , Hardik Pandya continues to captain the Indian team in the shortest format of the game.

Concept note-5: -For India, the T20 Captain is Hardik Pandya and for Sri Lanka, it is Dasun Shanaka.
