Haryana Governor Bandaru Dattatreya and CM Manohar Lal Khattar inaugurated the 35th Surajkund International Crafts Mela in which place?

(A) ** Faridabad

(B) Jhajjar

(C) Sonipat

(D) Gurugram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On March 19, 2022 Haryana Governor Bandaru Dattatreya and Chief Minister Manohar Lal Khattar officially inaugurated the world famous Surajkund Crafts Mela after a long gap of two years.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The 35th Surajkund International Crafts Mela will be held in Faridabad from 19th March to 4th April 2022. Jammu and Kashmir is the theme State for it. The fair will showcase the richness and diversity of handicrafts, handloom, and the cultural heritage of India.

Concept note-3: -A splash of colours, rhythm of drum beats and joy de-vivre merge at Surajkund in Faridabad, Haryana during the first fortnight of each February. The curtains go up on the acclaimed annual Surajkund International Crafts Mela from 1st February onwards, a celebration of Indian folk traditions and cultural heritage.

Concept note-4: -Surajkund International Craft Mela of India is held during the spring season, every year in February month. It is the largest crafts fair in the world. This world-famous fair was first started in 1987.

Concept note-5: -Inauguration of CRAFT MELA “Coalescence"- Craft-Culture-Community-Climate on 23 February 2022 at 11 am Bikaner House, New Delhi. The Event Inauguration by Minister of State of External Affairs Meenakshi Lekhi by President ICCR Vinay Sahasrabuddhe.
