How many medals are won by the Indian wrestling team at U-20 World Championships?

(A) ** Sixteen

(B) Fourteen

(C) Twenty

(D) Fifteen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"Our wrestlers make us proud again! Congratulations to our team on winning 16 medals (7 each in Men’s and Women’s freestyle and 2 in Greco-Roman) at the U20 World Championships. This is India’s best-ever performance. It also shows the future of Indian wrestling is in safe hands!"

Concept note-2: -U-20 world champion Antim Panghal wins gold in wrestling in National Games 2022.

Concept note-3: -Aman Sehrawat becomes first Indian wrestler to win Gold medal at U-23 World Wrestling Championships in Spain. In wrestling, teenager Aman Sehrawat made history as he became the first Indian wrestler to win a gold medal at the U-23 World Wrestling Championships in Pontevedra, Spain.

Concept note-4: -The World U20 Wrestling Championships 2022 was held in Sofia, Bulgaria between 15–21 August 2022.
