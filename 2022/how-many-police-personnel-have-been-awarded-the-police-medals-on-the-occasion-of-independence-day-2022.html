How many Police personnel have been awarded the Police Medals on the occasion of Independence Day, 2022?

(A) 1075

(B) 1062

(C) ** 1082

(D) 1072

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1,082 Police personnel have been awarded Medals on the occasion of Independence Day, 2022.

Concept note-2: -The Central Reserve Force (CRPF) with 48, bagged the most gallantry medals. Thirty-one gallantry awards are from Maharashtra, 25 are from Jammu and Kashmir Police, nine are from Jharkhand, seven are each from Delhi, Chhattisgarh Police and the BSF, and the remaining are from the other States/UTs and CAPFs.

Concept note-3: -On August 15, 2022, on the occasion of 75th anniversary of Independence, Uttarakhand Chief Minister Pushkar Singh Dhami awarded Chief Minister Meritorious Service Medal for distinguished work to police officers and employees.

Concept note-4: -Independence Day 2022: Here’s the full list of 107 gallantry awards announced | Latest News India - Hindustan Times.
