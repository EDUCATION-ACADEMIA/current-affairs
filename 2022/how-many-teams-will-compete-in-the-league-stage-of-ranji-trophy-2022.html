How many teams will compete in the league stage of Ranji Trophy 2022?

(A) 40

(B) ** 38

(C) 25

(D) 36

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s First-class domestic competition, the Ranji Trophy 2022/23, will kick off on Tuesday December 13 and last for ten weeks with the final set to start on Thursday February 16 – here are the full squad lists for the Ranji Trophy 2022/23.

Concept note-2: -Ranji Trophy 2022/23: Format A total of 39 teams will participate in the upcoming Ranji Trophy, with those sides being divided into five groups. There are four Elite groups, with eight teams in each of those groups.

Concept note-3: -This time, the Ranji Trophy is back in its full form, spanning 135 matches from December 13, 2022 to February 20, 2023. The top 32 teams have been split into four groups, with eight teams each. Two teams from each group will proceed to the knockouts after the teams play in a round robin league format.

Concept note-4: -But what is the format? The top two from each Elite group, consisting of eight teams each, make the quarter-finals after an intensive league phase of equal home and away matches. In Plate, which is a six-team pool, each side plays the other five after which the top four make it to the semi-finals.
