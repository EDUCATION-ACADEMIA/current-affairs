How many years of diplomatic relations have been completed between India and Belgium?

(A) ** 75 years

(B) 85 years

(C) 55 years

(D) 65 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On February 25, 2022, a special logo was unveiled to commemorate 75 years of diplomatic relations between India and Belgium.

Concept note-2: -Belgium was among the first European countries to establish diplomatic relations with independent India in September 1947. Our close bilateral relationship is based on shared commonalities and commitment to the rule of law, federalism and pluralism.

Concept note-3: -India and the Netherlands established diplomatic relations in 1947. Year 2022 marks the 75 years of establishment of diplomatic relations. Today, India and the Netherlands enjoy strong political, economic & commercial relations.

Concept note-4: -Year 2022 marks the celebration of the 75th anniversary of diplomatic relations between the Netherlands and India. The relations between India and the Netherlands date back to the early 17th century.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is the Netherlands. A special logo was unveiled to commemorate 75 years of diplomatic relations between India and the Netherlands, in March 2022. The logo features a lotus and a tulip – the national flowers of both countries. The chakra in the middle of the logo depicts the bond of friendship.
