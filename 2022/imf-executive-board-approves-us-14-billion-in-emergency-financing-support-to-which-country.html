IMF Executive Board Approves US$ 1.4 Billion in Emergency Financing Support to which country?

(A) Sri Lanka

(B) Pakistan

(C) India

(D) ** Ukraine

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As part of this engagement, the Fund quickly disbursed US$1.4 billion in emergency financing to Ukraine in early March-making the IMF the first International Financial Institution to provide financing since the start of the war.

Concept note-2: -March 13, 2022 The IMF approved $1.4 billion U.S. in emergency funding for Ukraine.

Concept note-3: -B… Argentina is the biggest debtor to the IMF, with a total outstanding debt of $42.2bn. The country has had a long and troubled relationship with the IMF, with a history of equally spectacular fall-outs and bail-outs. At the turn of the century, the IMF made $88.3bn available to bail out the country’s ailing economy.

Concept note-4: -IMF Grants Ukraine USD 1.4 Billion in Emergency Financing.

Concept note-5: -India’s quota in the IMF, which determines voting shares in the multi-lateral lending agency, is 2.75 per cent.
