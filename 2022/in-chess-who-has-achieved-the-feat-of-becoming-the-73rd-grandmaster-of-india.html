(C) ** Bharath Subramaniyam

Concept note-1: -Iniyan and become a GM at the age of 14 years, 2 months and 23 days. If you take out 16 months of waiting time, you realize that Bharath could have achieved the feat before he hit 13 years of age! India’s 73rd GM at the age of 14 years, 2 months and 23 days!
