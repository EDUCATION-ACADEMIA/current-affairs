In May 2022, INS Gomati has decommissioned at which naval dockyard?

(A) Naval Dockyard, Chennai

(B) Naval Dockyard, Kochi

(C) Naval Dockyard, Kolkata

(D) ** Naval Dockyard, Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Having served the nation and the Indian Navy with great distinction for 34 eventful years, INS Gomati was decommissioned at sunset at the Naval Dockyard in Mumbai on 28 May 2022, in an elegant, solemn and poignant ceremony.

Concept note-2: -INS Gomati was decommissioned at the Naval Dockyard in Mumbai on 28 May 2022. INS Gomati derives her name from the river Gomti and was commissioned on 16 April 1988. It was also the oldest warrior of the Western Fleet when decommissioned.

Concept note-3: -INS Ajay was decommissioned on 19 September 2022 after rendering 32 years of glorious service to the nation.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Navy on Saturday decommissioned INS Gomati, a Godavari class guided-missile frigate, which had been in service for 34 years, an official said. The ship, which had been deployed in Operations Cactus, Parakram and Rainbow, was decommissioned at sunset at the Naval Dockyard here, the official said.

Concept note-5: -The INS Ajay was in active naval service for more than 32 years during her illustrious journey.
