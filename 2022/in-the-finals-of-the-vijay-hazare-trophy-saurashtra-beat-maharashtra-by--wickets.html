In the finals of the Vijay Hazare Trophy, Saurashtra beat Maharashtra by ____ wickets.

(A) ** 5

(B) 3

(C) 4

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vijay Hazare Trophy Final: Saurashtra beat Maharashtra by 5 wickets. Saurashtra beat Maharashtra by 5 wickets in the final to win the Vijay Hazare Trophy at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad, Gujarat.

Concept note-2: -Saurashtra captain Jaydev Unadkat, after winning the Vijay Hazare Trophy 2022 said, “If there is one player in the side who deserves to play at the highest level, it’s Chirag Jani”.

Concept note-3: -Tamil Nadu has won the trophy 5 times which is the most by any team and is followed by Karnataka and Mumbai with 4 titles each Karnataka and Tamil Nadu are the only two teams in the history of the tournament to win the trophy for two consecutive years.

Concept note-4: -Himachal Pradesh became the latest winner of the Vijay Hazare Trophy winners list after defeating Uttar Pradesh in the 2021 season.

Concept note-5: -Ruturaj Gaikwad, Maharashtra Matches: 5, Runs: 660, HS: 220*, 100s/50s: 4/0, Average: 220, Strike Rate: 113.59. A wrist injury during the league stage meant that the Maharashtra captain was forced to skip five of his 10 games in the tournament. But he made the most of his opportunities.
