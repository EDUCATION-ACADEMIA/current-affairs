In the second T20 match of Asia Cup 2022, India won the match against Pakistan by how many wickets?

(A) 7

(B) ** 5

(C) 4

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Asia Cup 2022: India defeats Pakistan by five wickets in a nail-biting contest - The Hindu.

Concept note-2: -# India have won nine and lost five (NR 1) out of 15 Asia Cup matches played against Pakistan.

Concept note-3: -India has so far won 27 of their 36 T20 matches in 2022, the year when they lost two matches in the Asia Cup, 1 in T20 World Cup, 2 in home series vs SA, 1 vs Australia at home, 1 each vs West Indies and England away.

Concept note-4: -Head-to-head Record of India vs Pakistan T20 Matches India and Pakistan have faced each other in 12 matches in T20 internationals. India have won 9 while Pakistan have won 3 times.
