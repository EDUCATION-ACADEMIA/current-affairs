In the World Air Power Index 2022, which country has topped?

(A) ** Chinese Aviation based armed forces (PLAAF)

(B) Israeli Aviation based armed forces

(C) United States Air Force (USAF)

(D) Japan Air Self-Preservation Power (JASDF)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Global Air Powers Ranking (2022) report has given the highest TvR score to the United States Air Force (USAF). It comprises a broad mix of aircraft types and many products are locally sourced from the massive industrial base of the country.

Concept note-2: -Top 10 Nations with the Most Military Aircraft (All Branches Combined) - Flight International 2022: United States - 13,247 (5,217 Air Force, 4,409 Army, 2,464 Navy, 1,157 Marines) Russia - 4,173 (3,863 Air Force, 310 Navy) China - 3,285 (1,991 Air Force, 857 Army, 437 Navy)

Concept note-3: -The Indian Air Force (IAF) has been placed at the third position on the World Air Power Index in terms of the total fighting strength of the various air services of different nations of the world. The World Directory of Modern Military Aircraft (WDMMA) published the Global Air Powers Ranking of 2022.

Concept note-4: -The World Directory of Modern Military Aircraft (WDMMA) has ranked the Indian Air Force (IAF) third in the Global Air Powers Ranking for 2022 after examining the air strength of various countries around the world.
