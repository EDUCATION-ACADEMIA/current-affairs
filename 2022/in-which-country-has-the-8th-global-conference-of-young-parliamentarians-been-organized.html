In which country has the 8th Global Conference of Young Parliamentarians been organized?

(A) India

(B) ** Egypt

(C) Denmark

(D) Britain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Egypt hosts Eighth Global Conference of Young Parliamentarians in Sharm El Sheikh . Egypt is hosting the Eighth Global Conference of Young Parliamentarians in Sharm El Sheikh today.

Concept note-2: -On 15 and 16 June, some 200 young parliamentarians from 60 countries convened in Sharm-el-Sheikh, Egypt for the Eight IPU Global Conference of Young Parliamentarians. The conference was jointly organized by the IPU and the Parliament of Egypt.

Concept note-3: -The Eighth Global Conference of Young Parliamentarians will bring together young parliamentarians from around the world to coordinate parliamentary action to mitigate the impact of climate change and ensure that global warming does not exceed 1.5°C, in line with the Paris Agreement.

Concept note-4: -Which country hosted the ‘Global Conference of Young Parliamentarians’? Notes: Egypt has hosted the Eighth Global Conference of Young Parliamentarians in Sharm El Sheikh. The conference is jointly organized by the House of Representatives and the Inter-Parliamentary Union (IPU).
