In Which state Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated the National Defense University and dedicated it to the nation?

(A) Haryana

(B) Bihar

(C) ** Gujarat

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Narendra Modi, Former Chief Minister of Gujarat inaugurated Raksha Shakti University .

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Gandhinagar. PM Narendra Modi, on 12 March 2022, dedicated the National Defense University to the nation and addressed its first convocation at Gandhinagar, Gujarat. National Defense University provides students with different academic courses related to policing and internal security.

Concept note-3: -It is located at Binola village in Gurugram district of Haryana state in India that is being built.

Concept note-4: -Notes: Indian National Defence University set up in Gurgaon in Haryana.

Concept note-5: -The government set up a national police university named Rashtriya Raksha University by upgrading the Raksha Shakti University which was established by the Government of Gujarat in 2010. The University, which is an Institution of National Importance, commenced its operations from 1st October 2020.
