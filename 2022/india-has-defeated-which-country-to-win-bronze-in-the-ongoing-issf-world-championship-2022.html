India has defeated which country to win Bronze in the ongoing ISSF World Championship 2022?

(A) Indonesia

(B) Egypt

(C) China

(D) ** Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian shooters Naamya Kapoor, Vibhuti Bhatia and Esha Singh won the bronze medal in the 25m pistol junior women’s team event at the ISSF Rifle/Pistol World Championship 2022 in Cairo, Egypt on Thursday. The Indian trio beat Germany’s Nina Adels, Vanessa Seeger and Lydia Vetter 17-1 in the bronze medal match.

Concept note-2: -China topped the ISSF Rifle/Pistol World Championships 2022 medals tally with 58 medals, 27 hold, 16 silver and 15 bronze.

Concept note-3: -India is second in the medals table with 12 gold, nine silver and 13 bronze medals while China sits on top with 25 gold, 16 silver and 14 bronze medals.

Concept note-4: -Which country topped the medal tally in the ISSF World Cup 2022? Notes: India topped the medal tally in the ISSF World Cup 2022 in Cairo, Egypt with a total of seven, four gold, two silver and one bronze, medals. Norway was at the second place India with six medals.

Concept note-5: -The 1st ISSF Rifle/Pistol World Shooting Championships were held in Cairo, Egypt from 12 to 27 October 2022 in 78 events. This also served as qualification event for 2024 Summer Olympics.
