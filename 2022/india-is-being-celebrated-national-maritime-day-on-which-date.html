India is being celebrated National Maritime Day on which date?

(A) April 04

(B) ** April 05

(C) April 01

(D) April 03

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Maritime Day is celebrated on 05th April to commemorate the maiden voyage of the first Indian commercial vessel SS Loyalty that set sail from Bombay (Mumbai) to London, on 05th April 1919.

Concept note-2: -World Maritime Day 2022 It was held on 28 and 29 September 2022.

Concept note-3: -Since 1964, National Maritime Day has been celebrated in India on April 5. Indian National Maritime Day is celebrated on April 5 because, on this date in 1919, SS Loyalty, the first Indian flag merchant ship, sailed from Mumbai to London. Scindia Steam Navigation Company Ltd was the owner of SS Loyalty.

Concept note-4: -The International Maritime Organization observed World Maritime Day on the last Thursday of September. This year, it will be observed on September 29. The day focuses on drawing public attention to maritime safety and the marine environment.
