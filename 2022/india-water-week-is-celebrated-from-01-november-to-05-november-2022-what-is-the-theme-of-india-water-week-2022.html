India Water Week is celebrated from 01 November to 05 November 2022. What is the theme of India Water Week 2022?

(A) Water, Energy and Food Security

(B) Leaving no one behind

(C) Groundwater, making the invisible visible

(D) ** Water Security for Sustainable Development and Equity

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for IWW-2022 is “Water Security for Sustainable Development with Equity” focusing on various aspects of water security and management and related challenges for equitable development.

Concept note-2: -World Water Week 2023: 20 –24 August World Water Week 2023 is focused on innovation at a time of unprecedented challenges. The theme Seeds of Change: Innovative Solutions for a Water-Wise World invites us to rethink how we manage water.

Concept note-3: -World Water Week 2023 has as its theme Seeds of Change: Innovative Solutions for a Water-Wise World.

Concept note-4: -The 2022 theme, Seeing the Unseen: The Value of Water, helps us view water in new and fascinating ways. We focus on the value of water, from many different perspectives. Expect interesting discussions on topics like pricing of water, indigenous knowledge, human rights, and much more.
