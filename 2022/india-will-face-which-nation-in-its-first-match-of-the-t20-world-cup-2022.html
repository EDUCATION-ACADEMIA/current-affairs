India will face which nation in its first match of the T20 World Cup 2022?

(A) Sri Lanka

(B) ** Pakistan

(C) Australia

(D) England

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India will play its first match in ICC T20 World Cup against arch-rival Pakistan on October 23 at the Melbourne Cricket Ground, Melbourne.

Concept note-2: -The first match of the super 12 will be played between New Zealand vs Australia at Sydney Cricket Ground, Sydney on 22nd October 2022. India will play their first match in T20 World Cup 2022 on 23rd October (Sunday) against Pakistan.

Concept note-3: -India suffered an embarrassing exit from the ICC T20 World Cup 2022 as they were trounced by England by 10 wickets in the semi-final at the Adelaide Oval Thursday.

Concept note-4: -The Group A of the T20 World Cup consists of England, Australia, New Zealand, Afghanistan, Netherlands, and Sri Lanka. Meanwhile, Group B has India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, South Africa, Ireland, Zimbabwe.

Concept note-5: -ICC T20 World Cup Semi-Final, India vs England: Team India advanced to the semi-finals of ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022 after defeating Zimbabwe by 71 runs in their last group stage match on Sunday. As a result, Team India will face England on November 10 at Adelaide Oval, Australia.
