India won how many gold medals at the Deaflympics 2021?

(A) ** 8

(B) 15

(C) 17

(D) 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India rank 9th in the event which includes Sports shooter Dhanush Srikanth won the first medal as well as first gold medal for India during the event as he clinched a gold medal in the men’s 10m air rifle shooting event with a new deaf world record by registering a score of 247.5 points in the final.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is ninth position. India’s largest contingent ever at a Deaflympics – 65 athletes, returned with a total of 16 medals from the event. These include 8 Gold, 1 silver, and 7 bronze.

Concept note-3: -This was India’s richest medal haul at the Deaflympics. A total of 16 medals were won over the fortnight-long quadrennial event, including eight gold, one silver and seven bronze.

Concept note-4: -How many medals did India win in Olympics 2021 now? India broke their previous best of six medals in a single Olympics edition by finishing the Tokyo Games with a record total of seven medals (1 gold, 2 silver, and 4 bronze) (London 2012). With this, India earned 48th place in the total medal standings.

Concept note-5: -On this day in 2021, Neeraj Chopra won the men’s javelin throw gold medal at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics. It was India’s first-ever Olympic medal in athletics and the realisation of a long-standing dream - one which legends like Milkha Singh and PT Usha had also chased but fallen short of by agonisingly narrow margins.
