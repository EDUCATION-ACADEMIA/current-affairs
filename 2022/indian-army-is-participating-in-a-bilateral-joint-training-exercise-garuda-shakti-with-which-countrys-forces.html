Indian army is participating in a bilateral joint training Exercise GARUDA SHAKTI with which country’s forces?

(A) Australia

(B) ** Indonesia

(C) Australia

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Army Special Forces and Indonesian Special Forces are participating in the eighth edition of the bilateral military exercise ‘Garuda Shakti’ at Karawang, Indonesia that commenced on 21 November 2022.

Concept note-2: -Garuda Shakti is a bilateral exercise between special forces of armies of India and Indonesia. This joint military exercise is a part of India and Indonesia defence cooperation.

Concept note-3: -India, Kazakhstan hold joint military exercise in Meghalaya | North East India News,The Indian Express.

Concept note-4: -INDIAN ARMY SPECIAL FORCES COMMENCE JOINT EXERCISE GARUDA SHAKTI WITH INDONESIAN SPECIAL FORCES AT SANGGA BUANA TRAINING AREA, KARAWANG, INDONESIA.

Concept note-5: -21st EDITION OF INDIA FRANCE BILATERAL NAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the Western Seaboard today, 16 Jan 23.
