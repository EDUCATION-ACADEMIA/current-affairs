Indian Navy conducted an exercise in the Atlantic Ocean along with which country?

(A) Germany

(B) ** France

(C) Australia

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India Navy and France Navy conducted exercise in the Atlantic Ocean.

Concept note-2: -SIMBEX-2022 is being conducted in two phases – Harbour Phase at Visakhapatnam from 26 to 27 October 2022 followed by the Sea Phase in Bay of Bengal from 28 to 30 October 2022.

Concept note-3: -21st EDITION OF INDIA FRANCE BILATERAL NAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the Western Seaboard today, 16 Jan 23.

Concept note-4: -Malabar Exercise is a multilateral naval war-gaming exercise that began in 1992. It began as a bilateral exercise between India’s and the United States’ navies.

Concept note-5: -About VARUNA Exercise : The Bilateral Naval Exercises between the two Navies were initiated in 1983. The exercise was christened as ‘VARUNA’ in 2001 and has become a vital part of India–France strategic bilateral relationship.
