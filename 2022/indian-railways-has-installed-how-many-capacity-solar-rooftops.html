Indian Railways has installed how many capacity solar rooftops?

(A) 5 MW

(B) 10 MW

(C) ** 4 MW

(D) 8 MW

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -26. Indian Railways has installed how many capacity solar rooftops? Explanation: Indian Railways has installed 142 MW of solar rooftop capacity and 4 MW of Wind energy till August.

Concept note-2: -2022, cumulatively over 1.52 lakh stand-alone solar pumps have been installed under the Component-B out of which 79,418 nos. of pumps have been installed during 2022 and 73.45 MW cumulative capacity solar power plants installed under Component-A out of which 48.2 MW has been installed during 2022.

Concept note-3: -In 9M 2022, India added over 12 GW of total power capacity, with 76% of the new capacity coming from solar power, according to the report. India’s cumulative installed solar capacity now stands at 60 GW. The country has a utility-scale project pipeline of over 58 GW.

Concept note-4: -Which state in India is the largest producer of solar energy? Karnataka, the southwestern state heads India’s list of states producing solar energy. With a total installed solar power capacity of about 7,100MW.
