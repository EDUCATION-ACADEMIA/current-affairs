Indian Railways is setting up the country’s biggest and world-class wrestling academy to support wrestling as a sport. In which city will the academy be built?

(A) Pune

(B) ** Delhi

(C) Mumbai

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Ministry of Railways. The Railway Ministry will set up a world-class wrestling academy. Work has been sanctioned to establish the same at Kishanganj, Delhi at an estimated cost of Rs 30.76 crores.

Concept note-2: -Railways to provide a World Class Wrestling academy at Kishanganj, Delhi at an approximate cost of Rs. 30.76 crores.

Concept note-3: -National Academy of Indian Railways - Wikipedia.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Railways will provide a world class wrestling academy at Delhi’s Kishanganj at an approximate cost of ₹30.76 crore, ministry informed on Friday. The railway minister had committed Indian Railways wrestlers to provide the state-of-art wrestling academy at the earliest.

Concept note-5: -NEW DELHI: The Indian Railways will soon set up the country’s biggest world-class academy, exclusively for wrestlers in railways, with all the best sports facilities at Kishanganj locality in Delhi. An estimated cost of Rs 30.76 crore for its establishment has already been sanctioned by the railway.
