India’s first ‘Amrit Sarovar’ has been established in Rampur of which state?

(A) Haryana

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Kerala

(D) Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct solution is Uttar Pradesh. Union Minister of Minority Affairs Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi inaugurated India’s first “Amrit Sarovar” in Patwai, Rampur, Uttar Pradesh. on 24th April 2022, mission Amrit Sarovar was launched by PM Modi.

Concept note-2: -India’s first ‘Amrit Sarovar’ was inaugurated on May 13, 2022 by Union Minister for Minority Affairs Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi and Uttar Pradesh’s Jal Shakti Minister Swatantra Dev Singh at Rampur in Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -No separate financial allocation for Mission Amrit Sarovar launched on 24th April 2022. The Mission Amrit Sarovar had initially planned a target to construct or rejuvenate 50,000 Amrit Sarovar ponds across India till 15th August 2023. Now, additional 50,000 Amrit Sarovar ponds will be constructed by 15th August 2023.

Concept note-4: -Uttar Pradesh has constructed largest number of lakes under the Indian Government’s ambitious Mission Amrit Sarovar.

Concept note-5: -India’s first ‘Amrit Sarovar’ comes up in Uttar Pradesh’s Rampur, to be inaugurated by Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi today | Mint.
