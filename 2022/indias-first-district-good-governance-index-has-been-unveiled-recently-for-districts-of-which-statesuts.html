India’s First “District Good Governance Index” has been unveiled recently for districts of which states/UTs?

(A) Uttarakhand

(B) ** Jammu and Kashmir

(C) Himachal Pradesh

(D) Ladakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jammu & Kashmir will become the first Union Territory in the country to have Good Governance Index.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which state/UT launched India’s first District Good Governance Index (DGGI)? Notes: India’s first ever District Good Governance Index (DGGI) was released in the Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir.

Concept note-3: -Jammu and Kashmir’s Pulwama district has grabbed the top slot in district good governance index (DGGI) 2021-22 in the union territory, officials said on Saturday.

Concept note-4: -Rajasthan has topped the Other States (Group B) category in Judiciary and Public Safety, Environment & Citizen Centric Governance.

Concept note-5: -India ranks overall 52, that is, exactly at midpoint of 104 countries ranked.
