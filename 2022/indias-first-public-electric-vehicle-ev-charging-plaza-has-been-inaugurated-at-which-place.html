India’s first public Electric Vehicle (EV) charging plaza has been inaugurated at which place?

(A) Jaipur

(B) Guwahati

(C) ** New Delhi

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -EESL in collaboration with NDMC has established India’s first of its kind public EV Charging Plaza in Central Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Developed and installed by GPS Renewables with support from Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC) and AeroCare Clean Energy, the EV charging station located at Haji Ali in Mumbai is India’s first to be powered by biogasIt works in tandem with a biomethane plant positioned alongside it.

Concept note-3: -In a bid to promote a sustainable environment, the world’s highest electric vehicle charging station was inaugurated in Kaza of Lahaul and Spiti district in Himachal Pradesh on Thursday.

Concept note-4: -Ladakh gets world’s highest EV charging stations at a height of 14000 ft.
