India’s largest EV charging station has been inaugurated at which place?

(A) Mumbai

(B) Gandhinagar

(C) Hyderabad

(D) ** Gurugram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gurugram: India’s largest electric vehicle (EV) charging station with a capacity of 121 charging points for 4 wheelers, has been opened here in Sector 86.

Concept note-2: -Q. Where is India’s largest EV charging station established recently? Notes: India’s largest electric vehicle charging station has been established in Gurugram (Haryana) with 72 AC slow chargers and 24 DC fast chargers (i.e) a total of 96 charging points.

Concept note-3: -India’s largest EV charging station has been set up in Gurugram.

Concept note-4: -As of September 2022, Teld had the largest EV fast charging network in China. The Chinese firm also is one of the largest EV charging station companies in the world. TELD has over 316,000 EV charging station units and around 20,000 stations.

Concept note-5: -Developed and installed by GPS Renewables with support from Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC) and AeroCare Clean Energy, the EV charging station located at Haji Ali in Mumbai is India’s first to be powered by biogasIt works in tandem with a biomethane plant positioned alongside it.
