India’s presidency of the Asia-Pacific institute for broadcasting development extended for how many years?

(A) 2 Years

(B) 3 Years

(C) ** 1 Year

(D) 5 Year

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s Presidency of the prestigious Asia-pacific Institute of Broadcasting Development (AIBD) has been extended for one more year. This was unanimously decided by the AIBD member countries at the two day General Conference of the Institute held in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which country is the President of the Asia-pacific Institute of Broadcasting Development as of 2022? Notes: India’s Presidency of the prestigious Asia-pacific Institute of Broadcasting Development, AIBD has been extended for one more year.

Concept note-3: -It is hosted by the Government of Malaysia and the secretariat is located in Kuala Lumpur.

Concept note-4: -Currently, Chief Executive Officer of Prasar Bharati and Director General of Doordarshan Mayank Kumar Agrawal is the President of AIBD.

Concept note-5: -* India’s Presidency of Asia-pacific Institute of Broadcasting Development (AIBD) has been extended for one more year. * Currently, CEO of Prasar Bharati and DG of Doordarshan, Mayank Kumar Agrawal is the President of AIBD.
