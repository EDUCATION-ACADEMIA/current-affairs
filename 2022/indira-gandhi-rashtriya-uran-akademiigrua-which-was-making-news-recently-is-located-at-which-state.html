Indira Gandhi Rashtriya Uran Akademi(IGRUA), which was making news recently, is located at which state?

(A) West Bengal

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Bihar

(D) Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indira Gandhi Rashtriya Uran Akademi (IGRUA) located at Fursatganj District Amethii Uttar Pradesh, is an autonomous body under the control of the Ministry of Civil Aviation.

Concept note-2: -Indira Gandhi Rashtriya Uran Akademi (IGRUA) is a public pilot training institute located at Fursatganj Airfield in Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established in 1985. It is an autonomous institution and comes under the Ministry of Civil Aviation, Government of India.

Concept note-3: -The fee charged is INR 45 Lacs . There is no other subsidy or relaxation in training fee available to any candidate including SC,ST, OBC and EWS. IGRUA provides scholarship to deserving EWS Candidates for the CPL Course.
