Indo-Kazakhstan Joint Military Exercise ‘Kazind-2022’ commenced at Umroi in which state?

(A) Mizoram

(B) ** Meghalaya

(C) Tripura

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -KazInd 2022: India and Kazakhstan carry out joint military drill in Meghalaya. To strengthen positive military relations between India and Kazakhstan, the nations conducted their 6th edition of Army Joint Exercise KazInd 2022 in Meghalaya’s Umroi.

Concept note-2: -Exercise KazInd 2022 is the 6th edition of the Indo-Kazakhstan Joint exercise and is aimed at building trust and understanding of each other in the field of national security.

Concept note-3: -Indo-Kazakhstan Joint Military Exercise “Kazind – 2022” to Commence at Umroi (Meghalaya) The 6th Edition of Indo - Kazakhstan joint training exercise “KAZIND-22” is scheduled to be conducted at Umroi (Meghalaya) from 15 to 28 December 2022.

Concept note-4: -The exercise was later upgraded to a company-level exercise, renamed Ex Kazind in 2018 and instituted in 2016. Various missions, ranging from joint planning, joint tactical drills, basics of special arms skills, and HADR to raiding a hostile target, were conducted during the training.
