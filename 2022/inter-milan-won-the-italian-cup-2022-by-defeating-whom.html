Inter Milan won the ‘Italian Cup’ 2022 by defeating whom?

(A) SS Lazio

(B) Parma FC

(C) ACF Fiorentina

(D) ** Juventus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current champions are Inter Milan, who beat Juventus 4–2 after extra time in the 2022 final.

Concept note-2: -It was played on 11 May 2022 between Juventus and Internazionale. The Stadio Olimpico in Rome hosted the final. Internazionale won the match 4–2 after extra time for their eighth Coppa Italia title.

Concept note-3: -Champions (10): 1964, 1966, 1969, 1989, 2002, 2007, 2012, 2017, 2018, 2022.

Concept note-4: -With 19 league titles, Milan is tied as the second most successful club in Serie A with local rivals Inter Milan (also with 19 league titles), behind Juventus (36 league titles).
