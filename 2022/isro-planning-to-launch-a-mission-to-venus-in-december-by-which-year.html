ISRO planning to launch a mission to Venus in December by which year?

(A) 2023

(B) 2021

(C) 2022

(D) ** 2024

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISRO plans mission to Venus, eyes December 2024 launch window | IBEF.

Concept note-2: -Shukrayaan is the Indian space mission to Venus by ISRO. Read here to know more about the mission. The Chairman of the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) has announced that it is expecting to launch the Venus mission Shukrayaan by December 2024.

Concept note-3: -Shukrayaan-1 ( transl. Venus craft) is a planned orbiter to Venus by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) to study the surface and atmosphere of Venus.

Concept note-4: -In 2020, the Indian Space Agency (ISRO) first announced plans to collaborate with France for the Venus mission, set to launch in 2025.

Concept note-5: -According to Union Minister of State for Space Jitendra Singh, India’s first human space flight mission under the ‘Gaganyaan program’ is scheduled to launch in the fourth quarter of 2024. (21 December). Gaganyaan, India’s first human space mission, is set to launch in 2024.
