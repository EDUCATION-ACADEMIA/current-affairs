(A) ** NATO

(B) WTO

(C) WEF

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jens Stoltenberg (born 16 March 1959) is a Norwegian politician who has been serving as the 13th secretary general of NATO since 2014.

Concept note-2: -Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg outlined the key decisions taken by NATO Foreign Ministers at their first meeting in Bucharest on Tuesday (29 November 2022), underlining that the meeting sent “a strong message of NATO unity, and of sustained support to Ukraine".

Concept note-3: -Lord Hastings Lionel Ismay was NATO’s first Secretary General, a position he was initially reluctant to accept.
