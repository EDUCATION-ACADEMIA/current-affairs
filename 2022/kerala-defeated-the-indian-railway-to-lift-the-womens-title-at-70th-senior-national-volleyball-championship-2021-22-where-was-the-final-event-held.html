Kerala defeated the Indian Railway to lift the women’s title at 70th Senior National Volleyball Championship 2021-22. Where was the final event held?

(A) ** Biju Patnaik Indoor Stadium

(B) Gachibowli Indoor Stadium

(C) Dr Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Indoor Stadium

(D) Talkatora Stadium

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Similarly, in the women’s category, the Kerala team defeated the Indian Railway 3-1, to lift the trophy. The 70th Senior National Volleyball (Men & Women) Championship 2021-22 was held at the Biju Patnaik Indoor Stadium, KIIT Deemed to be University, Bhubaneswar.

Concept note-2: -Haryana defeated the Indian Railway 3-0, to win Men’s title in Senior National Volleyball Championship 2021-22. Similarly, in women’s category, Kerala defeated the Indian Railway 3-1, to lift the trophy.

Concept note-3: -Texas wins the 2022 NCAA volleyball national championship

Concept note-4: -Senior National Volleyball Tournament From February 2 To 9 In Guwahati. GUWAHATI: The 71st Senior National Volleyball tournament will be held from February 2 to 9 in Guwahati.

Concept note-5: -Bhubaneswar: Haryana Men’s Team and Kerala Women’s Team became champions of 70th Senior National Volleyball Championship held at the Biju Patnaik Indoor Stadium, KIIT Deemed to be University by following COVID guidelines. The Haryana Men’s Team became the champion after defeating the Indian Railway 3-0.
