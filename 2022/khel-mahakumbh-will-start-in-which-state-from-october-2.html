Khel Mahakumbh will start in which state from October 2?

(A) ** Uttarakhand

(B) Bihar

(C) Odisha

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On September 22, 2022, Uttarakhand Sports Minister Rekha Arya told the media in the auditorium of the Assembly that the Khel Mahakumbh will start from October 2 in 662 Nyay panchayats of 13 districts of the state.

Concept note-2: -Khel Mahakumbh 2022: Indian Premier Narendra Modi, Gujarat Chief Minister Shri Bhupendra Patel, as well as Gujarat Governor Acharya Devvrat inaugurated the “Khel Mahakumbh” 11th Edition in Ahmedabad on March 12, 2022.

Concept note-3: -Khel Mahakumbh 2023: On 12th March 2022, Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Gujarat Chief Minister Shri Bhupendra Patel, and Gujarat Governor Acharya Devvrat opened the 11th edition of the ‘Khel Mahakumbh’ in Ahmedabad.

Concept note-4: -Khel Mahakumbh Prizes 2022 The applicant who will get the title 1st rank in his / her district will get the prize money of ₹1.5 Lakhs, 2nd rank will get the prize money of ₹1 Lakh and 3rd will get the prize money of ₹75,000.
