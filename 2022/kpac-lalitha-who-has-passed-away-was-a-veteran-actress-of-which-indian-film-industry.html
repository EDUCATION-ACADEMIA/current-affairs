KPAC Lalitha, who has passed away, was a veteran actress of which Indian film industry?

(A) ** Malayalam

(B) Bengali

(C) Marathi

(D) Kannada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Veteran actress Maheshwari Amma, better known by her stage name of KPAC Lalitha who starred in over 500 films primarily in Malayalam cinema , passed away in Kochi late Tuesday night. She was 74.

Concept note-2: -Maheshwari Amma, better known by her stage name K. P. A. C. Lalitha, was an Indian film and stage actress who worked primarily in Malyalam. She started her acting career with K. P. A. C., a theatre collective in Kayamkulam, Kerala.

Concept note-3: -Popular Malayalam actress and two time National Award winner KPAC Lalitha passed away on February 22, 2022 at the age of 74.

Concept note-4: -KOCHI: Noted Malayalam actor KPAC Lalitha passed away late on Tuesday at her residence in Tripunithura near here, film industry sources said. She was 74. Lalitha is survived by her actor-director son Sidharth Bharathan and daughter Sreekutty. She was married to the legendary Malayalam film director late Bharathan.

Concept note-5: -Veteran Malayalam actor Kochu Preman passes away at 68 - India Today.
