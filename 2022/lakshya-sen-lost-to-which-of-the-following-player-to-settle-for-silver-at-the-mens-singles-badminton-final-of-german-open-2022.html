Lakshya Sen lost to which of the following player to settle for silver at the men’s singles badminton final of German Open 2022?

(A) Viktor Axelsen

(B) ** Kunlavut Vitidsarn

(C) Lee Zii Jia

(D) Thomas Rouxel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s Lakshya Sen won a silver medal at the German Open 2022 on 13 March 2022. He lost the men’s singles final to Thailand’s Kunlavut Vitidsarn.

Concept note-2: -Hence the correct answer is Lakshya Sen. He lost the men’s singles final to Thailand’s Kunlavut Vitidsarn. He lost 18-21, 15-21 in the final. Lakshya Sen has become the 1st Indian to clinch a silver medal at the Badminton World Federation BWF Superseries 300 German Open 2022.

Concept note-3: -India’s Lakshya Sen won the men’s singles badminton gold medal at the Commonwealth Games 2022 in Birmingham, UK on Monday. Lakshya Sen, in his Commonwealth Games debut, came from a game down to beat Malaysia’s Ng Tze Yong 19-21, 21-9, 21-16 in the final at the National Exhibition Centre.
