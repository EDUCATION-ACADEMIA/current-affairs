Legendary Indian player Surajit Sengupta has passed away. He had played at the national level for the Indian team in which sports?

(A) Hockey

(B) Cricket

(C) ** Football

(D) Tennis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Surajit Sengupta (30 August 1951 – 17 February 2022) was an Indian footballer who played as a winger. He played for the India national team, representing the country in the 1974 Asian Games in Tehran and the 1978 Asian Games in Bangkok.

Concept note-2: -He scouted Bhaichung Bhutia in the early 1990s as a coach of East Bengal. Shyamal Ghosh, who played as a defender in the Indian football team in the 1970s, died on Tuesday after a brief bout with illness. He was 71 and breathed his last in a city hospital in Kolkata.

Concept note-3: -Sengupta was married to Joya Sengupta. Together they had one son, Showmon Sengupta.
