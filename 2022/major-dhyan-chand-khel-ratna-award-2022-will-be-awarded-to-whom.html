Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 will be awarded to whom?

(A) Neha Aggarwal

(B) Manika Batra

(C) Soumyajit Ghosh

(D) ** Achanta Sharath Kamal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Sharath Kamal Achanta has won the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 in the Table Tennis discipline. The Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 ( 2022) is given by the Indian government and the Department of Sport to those sportspersons who have excelled in their sports.

Concept note-2: -Table tennis legend Achanta Sharath Kamal will receive the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award, the country’s highest sporting honour, from President Droupadi Murmu on November 30 at the Rashtrapati Bhavan in New Delhi. Achanta Sharath Kamal.

Concept note-3: -While Eldhose became the first Indian to win a CWG gold in the men’s triple jump, Avinash Sable won a silver in the men’s 3000m steeplechase and broke the Kenyan monopoly on the podium in the sport. India cricket team captain Rohit Sharma’s coach Dinesh Lad received the Dronacharya Award in the lifetime category.
