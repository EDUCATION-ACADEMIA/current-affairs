March 4th is being celebrated as which date?

(A) World Post Day

(B) World Service Day

(C) ** World Obesity Day

(D) World Diet Food Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -March 4th also marks National Grammar Day and National Hug a G.I. Day.

Concept note-2: -National Safety Day is celebrated in India on 4th March by the National Safety Council of India. This day is celebrated to make people get safe from several issues like financial loss, health problems, and also any other problems that people are facing in their life. Employee Appreciation Day is observed on 4 March.
