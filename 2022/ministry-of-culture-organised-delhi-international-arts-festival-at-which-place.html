Ministry of culture organised Delhi International Arts Festival at which place?

(A) ** India Gate

(B) Shanti Van

(C) Red Fort

(D) Jama Masjid

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Culture in collaboration with Prasiddha Foundation inaugurated Delhi International Arts Festival at Kartavya Path with the tagline - ‘Where Bharat Meets India’ today. The festival is being organised from 16th December to 30th December.

Concept note-2: -Art and Craft in Delhi Delhi is famous for its fine Meenakari work and golden thread zarkozi embroidery. Pottery from Delhi is also famous for its clay toys, idols and decorations apart from those summer season water pots. These figures bear a tinch of that Mughal culture that survived here long before.

Concept note-3: -ACF, an initiative by an NGO Good Cause, encourages and promotes the arts in the country. We are a forum that disseminates information on the latest happenings and news in the world of art and culture through our website.
