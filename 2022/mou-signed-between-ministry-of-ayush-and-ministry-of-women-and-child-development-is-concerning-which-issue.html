MoU signed between Ministry of AYUSH and Ministry of women and child development is concerning which issue?

(A) Infant mortality

(B) Education

(C) Covid-19

(D) ** Malnutrition

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Addressing the holistic well-being of women and children at the grass root level, an MoU has been signed between the Ministry of AYUSH and Ministry of Women and Child Development. MoU seeks to address the issue of malnutrition amongst women and children.

Concept note-2: -Highlights. According to the Memorandum of understanding, the Women and Child Development Ministry will undertake scientifically proven and user based solutions to control malnutrition in the country. Also, medicinal Gardens and nutri Gardens are to be established in every Anganwadi in the near future.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Scheme.

Concept note-4: -As a nodal Ministry for the advancement of women and children, the Ministry formulates plans, policies and programmes; enacts/ amends legislation, guides and coordinates the efforts of both governmental and non-governmental organisations working in the field of Women and Child Development.

Concept note-5: -The Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, came into existence as a separate Ministry with effect from 30 January, 2006.
