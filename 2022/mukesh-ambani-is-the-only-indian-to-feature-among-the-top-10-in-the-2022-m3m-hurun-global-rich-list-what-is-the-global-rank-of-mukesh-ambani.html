Mukesh Ambani is the only Indian to feature among the top 10 in the 2022 M3M Hurun Global Rich List. What is the global rank of Mukesh Ambani?

(A) ** 9th

(B) 7th

(C) 8th

(D) 10th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The report, titled ‘The 2022 M3M Hurun Global Rich List’, said that Ambani occupied the ninth rank globally with a net worth of $103 billion.

Concept note-2: -2. Mukesh Ambani. Scoring tenth place among the richest persons in the world in 2022, Mukesh Ambani has $91 billion in net worth. The founder and chairman of Reliance Industries has his interests across petrochemicals, oil and gas, retail and telecom.

Concept note-3: -Ambani was India’s richest person for 14 years until he was overtaken by Gautam Adani in February 2022. He ranked among the world’s top 10 richest in the world on Forbes’ global rankings between 2008 and 2011 and again starting in 2021.

Concept note-4: -Mukesh Ambani, who controls Reliance Industries, the oil and gas to telecom giant, is at No. 2 with $88 billion, down 5% from last year. Between them, Adani and Ambani now account for 30% of the total wealth of India’s 100 richest.

Concept note-5: -2 globally and No. 1 among Indians in Brand Guardianship Index 2023.
