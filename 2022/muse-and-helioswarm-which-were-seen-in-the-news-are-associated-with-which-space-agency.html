MUSE and HelioSwarm, which were seen in the news, are associated with which space agency?

(A) ** NASA

(B) ISRO

(C) JAXA

(D) ESA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. MUSE and HelioSwarm, which were seen in the news recently, are associated with which space agency? Notes: NASA has announced two new science missions namely Multi-slit Solar Explorer (MUSE) and HelioSwarm.

Concept note-2: -The primary goal of the MUSE mission is to investigate the causes of coronal heating and instability, such as flares and coronal mass ejections, and gain insight into the basic plasma properties of the corona.

Concept note-3: -NASA has selected two science missions – the Multi-slit Solar Explorer (MUSE) and HelioSwarm – to help improve our understanding of the dynamics of the Sun, the Sun-Earth connection, and the constantly changing space environment.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is SpaceX.

Concept note-5: -Notes: The United States’ space agency National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) has announced that it has given approval to two Sun exploration heliophysics missions.
