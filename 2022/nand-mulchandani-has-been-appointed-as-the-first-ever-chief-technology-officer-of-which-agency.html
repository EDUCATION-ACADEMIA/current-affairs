Nand Mulchandani has been appointed as the first-ever Chief Technology Officer of which agency?

(A) IB

(B) NIA

(C) FBI

(D) ** CIA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nand Mulchandani, an Indian-origin man, has been appointed as the first-ever Chief Technology Officer of Central Intelligence Agency (CIA).

Concept note-2: -Washington: The US Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) has appointed Nand Mulchandani, an Indian-origin man, as its first-ever Chief Technology Officer (CTO).

Concept note-3: -Nand Mulchandani, an Indian-origin Silicon Valley IT expert and acting director of Joint Artificial Intelligence Center under US Department of Defense, has been appointed as the first-ever Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of the Central Intelligence Agency.

Concept note-4: -With more than 25 years of experience, Mr. Mulchandani will ensure the Agency is leveraging cutting-edge innovations to further CIA’s mission.
