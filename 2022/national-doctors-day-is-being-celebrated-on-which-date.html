National Doctors’ Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) July 02

(B) ** July 01

(C) June 29

(D) June 30

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In India, it marks the birth and death anniversary of the legendary physician and second Chief Minister of West Bengal, Dr. Bidhan Chandra Roy. It was his belief that only a healthy person who is strong in mind and body can ever dream of achieving India’s freedom i.e. Swaraj.

Concept note-2: -Every country celebrates this day on different dates, but to honour Dr BC Roy’s contribution to the medical fraternity, IMA decided to observe July 1 as India’s National doctors day.

Concept note-3: -The special day is observed on July 1 in India. The first time this day was established was in 1991 and ever since annually, National Doctor’s Day is celebrated across the country.
