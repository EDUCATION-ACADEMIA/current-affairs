National Monument Authority declaring Mangarh hillock as a monument of National Importance. Mangarh hillock is located in which state?

(A) ** Rajasthan

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Gujarat

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mangarh, the hillock in Banswara District, Rajasthan was visited by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi when he was Chief Minister of Gujarat and the local Bhil tribals had been demanding the Mangarh hills to be declared as National Monument.

Concept note-2: -Mangarh Hillock is located in Banswara District, Rajasthan. It is situated in the Aravali mountains on the Rajasthan-Gujarat border. It is a site of a tribal uprising where a massacre of over 1500 Bhil tribal freedom fighters took place in 1913.

Concept note-3: -PM Modi mentioned that India’s past, present, and future are not complete without the tribal community. Mangarh Dham is a symbol of tenacity and sacrifices of tribals and added that this is the common heritage of Rajasthan, Gujarat, Maharashtra, and Madhya Pradesh.
