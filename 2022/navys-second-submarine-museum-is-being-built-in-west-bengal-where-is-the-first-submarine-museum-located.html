Navy’s second submarine museum is being built in West Bengal, where is the first submarine museum located?

(A) Mumbai

(B) ** Vishakhapatnam

(C) Ahmedabad

(D) Pune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: Navy’s second submarine museum is being built in West Bengal. The first one is in Vishakhapatnam.

Concept note-2: -The Kurusura Submarine Museum was dedicated to the Nation on 9th August 2002 by Sri N. Chandrababu Naidu, Hon’ble Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh and opened to public on 14-8-2002 by VMRDA.

Concept note-3: -The first naval communication museum in India was setup in Visakhapatnam in the year 2001. The museum is named after the city as the Visakhapatnam Naval Museum. It is located on the premises of the Eastern Naval Command of the Indian Navy.

Concept note-4: -Ticket Price Only 30/- Rupees Aircraft Museum Kolkata Entry Fee.
