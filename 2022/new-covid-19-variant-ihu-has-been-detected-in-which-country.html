New Covid-19 variant IHU has been detected in which country?

(A) ** France

(B) Spain

(C) Germany

(D) Denmark

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The variant, B. 1.640. 2, was discovered in a male patient at the University Hospital Institute (IHU) Mediterranee Infection in Southern France around the same time Omicron was discovered in South Africa, according to Bloombergopens in a new tab or window. Thus, it’s been dubbed the “IHU” variant.

Concept note-2: -The original Omicron strain (BA.1) was first identified in Botswana and South Africa in late November 2021, and cases quickly began to surface and multiply in other countries.

Concept note-3: -Following the second COVID wave in India, the government announced the presence of yet another deadly variant, known as Delta Plus variant.

Concept note-4: -Currently, the dominant variant nationwide is XBB.1.5, with 66% of cases, followed by BQ.1.1, with 20% of cases.
