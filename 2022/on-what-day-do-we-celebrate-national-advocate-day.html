On what day do we celebrate National Advocate Day?

(A) ** December 03

(B) December 04

(C) December 06

(D) December 02

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Advocates’ day is celebrated in India by the lawyer community on the 3rd of December to mark the birth anniversary of Dr. Rajendra Prasad , the First President of India and a very eminent lawyer himself.

Concept note-2: -Day. But we are observing Lawyer’s day on 28 April of each year in the memory of our jewel/ Builder of our state, UTKALA GOURV Barrister Madhusudan Das. Dr. Rajendra Prasad was our first president and had contributed his life for the country, so happy Lawyer’s day.

Concept note-3: -Ans. An advocate is a qualified individual representing the client in a court of law, whereas a lawyer is used to designate anyone in the legal profession.

Concept note-4: -“Today is a very special day for you, for you are the one who represents law to all. Wishing you a day full of celebrations on Advocates Day.” “May this occasion of Advocates Day be full of celebrations and inspirations for you. Warm wishes on Advocates Day to a wonderful lawyer.”
