PetrFiala has been sworn in as the new Prime Minister of which country?

(A) Austria

(B) Latvia

(C) Serbia

(D) ** Czech Republic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -His appointment took effect upon his Cabinet being sworn in, on 17 December 2021. Fiala’s government won a confidence vote in the Chamber of Deputies of the Czech Republic on 13 January 2022 by 106–86.

Concept note-2: -Petr Fiala is the current Czech Prime Minister from 17 December 2021.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry is led by the Minister of Foreign Affairs, who is nominated by the Prime Minister of the Czech Republic. The current Minister of Foreign Affairs is Jan Lipavský, in office since 17 December 2021.

Concept note-4: -Andrej Babiš (Czech pronunciation: [ˈandrɛj ˈbabɪʃ]; born 2 September 1954) is a Slovak-born Czech politician and businessman who served as the Prime Minister of the Czech Republic from 2017 to 2021.
