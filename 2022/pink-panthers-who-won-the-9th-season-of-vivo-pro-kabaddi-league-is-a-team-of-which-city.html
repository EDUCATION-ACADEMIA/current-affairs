Pink Panthers, who won the 9th season of VIVO Pro Kabaddi League, is a team of which city?

(A) ** Jaipur

(B) Bhopal

(C) Indore

(D) Patna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jaipur Pink Panthers (JPP) is a professional kabaddi team which belongs to Pink City of India Jaipur, competes in Pro Kabaddi League which is a professional Kabaddi league in India.

Concept note-2: -The Panthers topped the league standings, thereby directly making it to the semifinals. Two clinical performances – against Bengaluru Bulls and Puneri Paltan – handed them the trophy once again. “Before the season began, I asked the boys how many of them had a PKL title.

Concept note-3: -Pro Kabaddi League Season 9 Final: Jaipur Pink Panthers won the Pro Kabaddi League Season 9 Final against a hard-fighting Puneri Paltan by a score of 33-29 to win their second PKL championship. After Patna, Jaipur Pink Panthers, the league’s first-ever winners, are currently only the second team to win multiple titles.

Concept note-4: -The sky was pink for Jaipur Pink Panthers as they clinched the Season 9 trophy. The Panthers were on top of their game in the 9th edition of vivo Pro Kabaddi. The team brought their A-game on the mat under the leadership of Sunil Kumar to lift the trophy.
