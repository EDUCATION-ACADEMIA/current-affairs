PM inaugurated the 356 Km long Bina-Panki Multiproduct Pipeline Project which connects the Bina refinery in MP to Panki in which city?

(A) ** Kanpur

(B) Puna

(C) Varanasi

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Extending from Bina refinery in Madhya Pradesh to Panki in Kanpur, the project has been built at a cost of over Rs. 1,500 crore. The multiproduct pipeline project will help the region access petroleum products from the Bina refinery. Modi will also be the chief guest of the 54th Convocation of IIT Kanpur.

Concept note-2: -356 Km long project has a capacity of around 3.45 million metric tonne per annum. This Project also includes augmentation of Tankage capacity and construction of Rail Loading Gantry at Panki POL Terminal. Total cost of the project is Rs. 1524 crore (Rs.

Concept note-3: -20 May 2011 | intralink. It was a very special day for the employees of Bharat Oman Refineries Limited (BORL) when the Hon’ble Prime Minister Dr. Manmohan Singh visited Bina, Madhya Pradesh on 20th May to dedicate the new Refinery to the nation.

Concept note-4: -The 356-km Bina-Panki multi-product pipeline, to be operated by Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL), has been set up at the cost of ₹1,524 crore and will cater to the demand of petroleum products of three northern states (HT photo)
