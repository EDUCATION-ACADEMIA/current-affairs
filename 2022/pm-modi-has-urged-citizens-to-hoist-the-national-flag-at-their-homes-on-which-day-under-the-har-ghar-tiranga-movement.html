PM Modi has urged citizens to hoist the national flag at their homes on which day under the Har Ghar Tiranga movement?

(A) ** August 13-15

(B) August 12-16

(C) August 11-20

(D) August 14-15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Har Ghar Tiranga: Hoist Tricolour from Aug 13 to 15 - PM Modi’s call to citizens on 75 years of Independence.

Concept note-2: -On Independence Day, the hoisting of the flag signifies the rise of a new nation, free from colonial domination. On Republic Day, the country celebrates the day when the Constitution was adopted.

Concept note-3: -’Har Ghar Tiranga’ is a campaign under the aegis of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav to encourage people to bring the Tiranga home from 13th -15th August 2022.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi, on July 22, launched the Har Ghar Tiranga Campaign to hoist the national flag, the Tricolour, to mark the 75th Independence Day. Modi, on Saturday, urged people to change the display pictures (DPs) of their social media accounts to the Tricolour during his Mann ki Baat address.

Concept note-5: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi launched “Har Ghar Tiranga” campaign to encourage people to bring the Tiranga home and hoist it to celebrate 75 years of India’s Independence.
