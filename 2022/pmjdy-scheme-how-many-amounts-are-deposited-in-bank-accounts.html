PMJDY scheme, How many amounts are deposited in bank accounts?

(A) ** Rs 1.5 lakh crore

(B) Rs 3.5 lakh crore

(C) Rs 2.5 lakh crore

(D) Rs 4.5 lakh crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The total number of Jan-Dhan accounts in Bihar is 53 million , West Bengal 46 million, Madhya Pradesh 38 million, Rajasthan 32 million and Maharashtra 32 million.

Concept note-2: -While the withdrawal limit from an account under PMJDY is Rs. 10,000, the account holder can deposit up to Rs. 1 lakh in this account.

Concept note-3: -The total balance in basic bank accounts under Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) reached ₹1.80-lakh crore. As on December 30, 2022, the total balance stood at ₹1,80,857 crore with 47.84 crore beneficiaries. Out of this, 26.54 crore are women.

Concept note-4: -The average balance in an account hovers around ₹3,000, and the average cost of maintaining these accounts for a bank is almost the same. When balances grow, banks tend to benefit from PMJDY.
