President of which country will be the chief guest at Republic Day celebrations in India 2023?

(A) Japan

(B) China

(C) ** Egypt

(D) Oman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s Republic Day chief guest is Egypt’s President Abdel Fattah El-Sisi .

Concept note-2: -Republic Day 2023 Chief Guest This year, Egyptian President Abdel-Fattah el-Sisi is the chief guest of Republic Day celebrations.

Concept note-3: -Republic Day parade 2023: Egyptian President to be the chief guest. On the 74th Republic Day celebration, Egyptian President Abdel Fattah El-Sisi will be the chief guest on Thursday. It is for the first time that an Egyptian leader has been invited on India’s Republic Day.

Concept note-4: -1. President Sukarno of Indonesia was the Guest of Honour during India’s first Republic Day event in 1950.

Concept note-5: -President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi is on a State visit to India from 24-26 January 2023 at the invitation of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi. President Sisi, who is on his second State Visit to India, will also be the Chief Guest on India’s 74th Republic Day.
