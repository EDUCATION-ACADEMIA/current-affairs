Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated Digital India Week 2022. What is the theme of Digital India Week 2022?

(A) Digital India a New Era

(B) Catalyzing New India’s Technology

(C) Make the Digital

(D) ** Catalyzing New India’s Techade

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Digital India Week 2022 with the theme ‘Catalyzing New India’s Techade’ kicked off on 4th July at Gandhinagar, Gujarat. The July 4th to 9th weeklong event commemorated the path-breaking journey of the flagship initiative of the Digital India Programme that was inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated Digital India Week 2022 at Mahatma Mandir in Gandhinagar today. The theme of this Digital India Week is ‘Catalyzing New India’s Techade’ to transform the nation into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy.

Concept note-3: -Recently, the Prime Minister has inaugurated the Digital India Week 2022 under the Digital India Programme, with the aim to strengthen Ease of Doing Business and Ease of Living. Theme: ‘Catalyzing New India’s Techade’. To transform the nation into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy.

Concept note-4: -Digital India is a campaign launched by the Government of India in order to ensure that the Government’s services are made available to citizens electronically by improved online infrastructure and by increasing Internet connectivity or making the country digitally empowered in the field of technology.

Concept note-5: -To celebrate the unprecedented digital transformation of India, the Government of India organized Digital India Week 2022 from July 4-10, 2022 at Gandhinagar Gujarat.
