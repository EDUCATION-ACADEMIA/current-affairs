Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone of the world’s first CNG terminal in which state?

(A) Assam

(B) Haryana

(C) Kerala

(D) ** Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone for the “world’s first CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) terminal” at Bhavnagar in Gujarat on Thursday (September 29). This is a three-year-old project that is expected to infuse Rs 4,000 crore in developing the existing port infrastructure at Bhavnagar.

Concept note-2: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated and laid the foundation stone of projects worth over ₹5200 crores in Bhavnagar. The Prime Minister laid the foundation stone of the World’s First CNG Terminal and of brownfield port at Bhavnagar.

Concept note-3: -Bharat Biogas Plant - Biggest Biogas (CNG) Plant Of Gujarat.

Concept note-4: -The primary objective of the CNG terminal will be to develop CNG receiving infrastructure facilities for India through Gujarat coast. The state-run GMB will continue to operate the berths on the south side of the port.
