Security Printing and Minting Corporation of India Limited has set up two new banknote printing lines. Where are these two located?

(A) Mysore and Salboni

(B) Dewas and Mysore

(C) ** Nashik and Dewas

(D) Salboni and Nashik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Security Printing and Minting Corporation of India Limited (SPMCIL) under its modernization initiatives has set up New Bank Note Printing Lines each at Currency Note Press, Nashik and Bank Note Press, Dewas .

Concept note-2: -There are two Security printing presses of SPMCIL, namely the India Security Press (ISP) at Nashik and Security Printing Press (SPP) at Hyderabad.

Concept note-3: -(BRBNML). The government owned presses are at Nasik (Western India) and Dewas (Central India). The other two presses are at Mysore (Southern India) and Salboni (Eastern India). Coins are minted in four mints owned by the Government of India.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Both Option 1 and 2. Security Printing and Minting Corporation of India Ltd (SPMCIL) has set up New Bank Note Printing Lines each at Currency Note Press, Nashik, and Bank Note Press, Dewas.
