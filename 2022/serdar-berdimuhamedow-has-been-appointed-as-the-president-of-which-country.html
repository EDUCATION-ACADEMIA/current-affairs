Serdar Berdimuhamedow has been appointed as the President of which country?

(A) Armenia

(B) Azerbaijan

(C) ** Turkmenistan

(D) Kyrgyzstan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Serdar Gurbangulyýewiç Berdimuhamedow (Turkmen pronunciation: [ɛɾdɑɾ ɢʊɾbɑnʁʊˈlɯjɛɪtʃ bɛɾdɯmʊxɑmɛˈdo]; born 22 September 1981) is the third and current president of Turkmenistan, serving since 19 March 2022.

Concept note-2: -Results. The Central Election Committee reported on 15 March that Serdar Berdimuhamedow won the election with 72.97% of the vote.

Concept note-3: -Government. After over a century of being a part of the Russian Empire and then the Soviet Union (including 67 years as a union republic), Turkmenistan declared its independence on 27 October 1991, following the dissolution of the Soviet Union.

Concept note-4: -A virtual guide to Turkmenistan, a country in Central Asia, east of the Caspian Sea, south of Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan, and north of Iran and Afghanistan. Turkmenistan occupies an area of 488,100 km², compared it is slightly smaller than Spain, or slightly larger than the US state of California.
