Shubman Gill and which sportsperson have been appointed as the new brand ambassadors for the My11Circle fantasy gaming platform?

(A) Virat Kohli

(B) ** Ruturaj Gaikwad

(C) Rohit Sharma

(D) Shreyas Iyer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Online skill gaming company, Games24x7, has appointed cricketers, Shubman Gill and Ruturaj Gaikwad as the new brand ambassadors for fantasy sports platform, My11Circle.

Concept note-2: -Shubman Gill endorses brands like Nike, JBL, CEAT, Cinthol, VIVO and Gillette.

Concept note-3: -In March 2022, Dream11 announced actors Kartik Aaryan and Samantha Ruth Prabhu as their brand ambassadors. This was the first time that the company roped in celebrities outside of sports as their ambassadors.
