South India’s first Vande Bharat Express has been flagged off in which city?

(A) ** Bangalore

(B) Chennai

(C) Mysore

(D) Vellore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Darshan Devaiah B.P. Prime Minister Narendra Modi flagged off two much-awaited trains from Krantiveera Sangolli Rayanna Railway Station in Bengaluru on November 11 - south India’s first Vande Bharat Express between Mysuru and Chennai via Bengaluru, and Bharat Gaurav Kashi Darshan train.

Concept note-2: -Indian Railways launched south India’s first Vande Bharat Express train on the Chennai-Bengaluru-Musuru route in November last year. The Secunderabad-Vizag Vande Bharat Express, the second Vande Bharat of South India, was launched on January 14 this year.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday flagged off Secunderabad-Visakhapatnam Vande Bharat. Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday flagged off the 8th Vande Bharat Express of the country that will connect Telangana’s Secunderabad and Andhra Pradesh’s Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-4: -The railways launched south India’s first Vande Bharat Express train on the Chennai-Bengaluru-Mysure route in November this year. Prime Minister Narendra Modi flagged off the fifth rake of Vande Bharat Express at the Krantiveera Sangolli Railway station in Bengaluru.
