(A) Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change

(B) ** Ministry of Jal Shakti

(C) Ministry of Earth Sciences

(D) Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Minister of Jal Shakti, Shri Gajendra Singh Shekhawat launched the Sujalam 2.0 campaign for greywater management at a virtual event hosted by the Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation (DDWS), Ministry of Jal Shakti to mark the World Water Day, 2022 yesterday.

Concept note-2: -On 23rd March 2022, the Sujalam 2.0 campaign was launched by Gajendra Singh Shekhawat, the Union Minister of Jal Shakti. This campaign has been launched for greywater management.
