T-Hub, which was seen in the news, is a start-up incubator based in which state?

(A) Odisha

(B) ** Telangana

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HYDERABAD: T-Hub, which leads the country’s pioneering innovation ecosystem, has won the ‘best incubator in India’ award at the National Startup Awards 2022 commemorating National Startup Day. efforts to bolster the startup ecosystem in Telangana.

Concept note-2: -T-Hub (Technology Hub) is an innovation intermediary and business incubator based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India.

Concept note-3: -Technology Hub (T-HUB) T-Hub enables and empowers an ecosystem hungry for innovation. Based out of Hyderabad, India, it leads India’s pioneering innovation ecosystem that powers next-generation products and new business models.

Concept note-4: -Their Innovation Campus, which is the world’s largest, has a total built-up area of 5,85,000 sq ft. A state-of-the-art destination, T-Hub has been created to enhance the innovation experience. Its design is unrivalled, drawing inspiration from Hyderabad’s iconic structure, Charminar.

Concept note-5: -Promoted by the Telangana Government, phase 1 of T-Hubstarted operations in 2015 at the temporary location situated at the International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT-H) at Gachibowli.
