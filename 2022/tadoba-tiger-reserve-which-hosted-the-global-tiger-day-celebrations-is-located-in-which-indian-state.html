Tadoba Tiger Reserve, which hosted the Global Tiger Day Celebrations, is located in which Indian state?

(A) Andhra Pradesh

(B) Gujarat

(C) Sikkim

(D) ** Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tadoba Tiger Reserve, Maharashtra plays host for National Global Tiger Day Celebrations 2022.

Concept note-2: -Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve is a tiger reserve in Chandrapur District, Maharashtra, India. It includes Tadoba National Park and Andhari Wildlife Sanctuary. Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve is Maharashtra’s oldest National Park created in 1955, . It is also one of India’s 28 Project Tiger Reserve.

Concept note-3: -The Guru Ghasidas National Park and Tamor Pingla Wildlife Sanctuary have been united to form India’s newest tiger reserve, which is located in the state of Chhattisgarh. This will be the fourth tiger reserve in Chhattisgarh and the 53rd tiger reserve in all of India.

Concept note-4: -The Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve is a wildlife sanctuary in Chandrapur district of Maharashtra state in India. It is Maharashtra’s oldest and largest national park. Created in 1955, the reserve includes the Tadoba National Park and the Andhari Wildlife Sanctuary.
