Tamil Nadu Chief Minister MK Stalin flagged off which luxury cruise liner from Chennai port?

(A) King

(B) ** Empress

(C) Prince

(D) Titanic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Tamil Nadu Chief Minister MK Stalin recently flagged off the luxury cruise liner “Empress” from Chennai port. This is regarded as the first luxury cruise liner to be operated from the state.

Concept note-2: -Operated by Cordelia Cruises, The Empress set sail from the Chennai Port. The vessel embarked on its journey with 1,800 guests and 600 crew members. It’d be an additional attraction for those in Chennai.

Concept note-3: -Navy launches indigenously-built ship ‘Arnala’ in Chennai | Cities News,The Indian Express.
