The 14th Asian U-18 Championship is held in which city?

(A) ** Tehran

(B) Paris

(C) Tokyo

(D) Berlin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The tournament will be held in Nakhon Pathom, Thailand, from 6 to 13 June 2022.

Concept note-2: -The current champion is Japan, which won its third title at the 2022 tournament.

Concept note-3: -Asian Volleyball Tournament: The first Asian volleyball tournament was held in Tokyo in 1955.

Concept note-4: -Indian men’s volleyball team wins bronze medal in 14th Asian U-18 Championship. Indian men’s volleyball team won bronze medal by defeating Korea 3-2 in the 14th Asian U-18 Championship in Tehran yesterday. In a five-set thriller, India defeated South Korea 25-20, 25-21, 26-28, 19-25, 15-12 in the third-place playoff.

Concept note-5: -The current champion is Iran, which won its fourth title at the 2021 tournament.
