The 1st Edition of Women Indian Premier League will be starting by which year?

(A) 2022

(B) 2025

(C) 2024

(D) ** 2023

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Women’s Premier League (WPL) is an upcoming women’s Twenty20 cricket franchise league in India. It is owned and operated by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). The first season will be played in 2023 in Mumbai and Navi Mumbai with five franchises taking part.

Concept note-2: -Women’s IPL Schedule 2023 Teams List & Auction Date: After the success of men’s IPL, The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) is all set to organize the first ever women’s IPL in the year 2023. And a total of 5 teams will participate in the Women’s IPL 2023, which have been selected recently.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Premier League began in 2008, a year after the Indian cricket team won the first edition of the World T 20 against Pakistan in a thrilling final.

Concept note-4: -IPL Schedule 2023 has started once again, according to information received from official sources, IPL will start from 20 March 2023 and according to IPL Schedule 2023 the final match will be played on 1 June 2023.

Concept note-5: -The last week of March 2023 will mark the start of the 16th season of the Indian Premier League, or IPL 2023, which will last through May 2023. This season will feature ten teams, 74 matches, and 14 league matches for each club.
