The 20th Meeting of NTC has held at Pakke Tiger Reserve. In which state is the reserve located?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Haryana

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) ** Arunachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Arunachal Pradesh Hosts ‘20th Meet Of National Tiger Conservation Authority’ At Pakke Tiger Reserve.

Concept note-2: -The 20th Meeting of National Tiger Conservation Authority (NTCA) was held on April 09, 2022 at Pakke Tiger Reserve in Arunachal Pradesh. The meeting was chaired by Union Minister for Environment, Forest & Climate Change Shri Bhupender Yadav.

Concept note-3: -Karnataka is the second position with 524 Royal Bengal Tiger population. Uttrakhand is third with 442 Tigers Population.

Concept note-4: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Indian state has adopted the ‘Pakke Tiger Reserve 2047 Declaration’? Notes: The Arunachal Pradesh Cabinet adopted the ‘Pakke Tiger Reserve 2047 Declaration on Climate Change Resilient and Responsive Arunachal Pradesh’.

Concept note-5: -Pakke Tiger Reserve is located in the East Kameng district of Arunachal Pradesh. It is surrounded by the Tenga Reserve Forest to the North, Doimara Reserve Forest on the West, Nameri National Park and Tiger Reserve (Assam) on the South and some agricultural land as well as Papum Reserve Forest on the East.
