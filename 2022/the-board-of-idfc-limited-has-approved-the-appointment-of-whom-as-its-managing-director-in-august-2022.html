The board of IDFC Limited has approved the appointment of whom as its Managing Director in August 2022?

(A) ** Mahendra N Shah

(B) Rakesh Sharma

(C) Manoj Sahay

(D) T. N. Manoharan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mahendra N Shah will be MD with effect from October 1, 2022, till September 30, 2023. The Board of IDFC Ltd has approved the appointment of Mahendra N Shah as Managing Director (MD) - Designate with immediate effect.

Concept note-2: -IDFC Limited appoints Mahendra N Shah as its new managing director.

Concept note-3: -Mahendra N Shah will be Managing Director with effect from October 1, 2022, till September 30, 2023 subject to the approval of the shareholders of the Company and other applicable statutory/ regulatory approvals, IDFC Limited said. Shah is the Company Secretary & Compliance Officer of IDFC Limited since May 24, 2019.

Concept note-4: -Mr. V. Vaidyanathan - Managing Director & CEO of IDFC FIRST Bank | IDFC FIRST Bank.

Concept note-5: -Addressing the 10th season of ‘Leaders of Tomorrow Awards 2022’, on Wednesday, V Vaidyanathan, MD & CEO, IDFC First Bank, exhorted small business owners and entrepreneurs to leverage technologies to their advantage.
