The central government announced that India has recorded which highest ever annual FDI inflow during FY22?

(A) ** 83.57 bn

(B) 62.47 bn

(C) 80.12 bn

(D) 77.14 bn

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Commerce and Industry ministry revealed that India has recorded the highest ever annual FDI inflow of $83.57 billion during FY22.

Concept note-2: -India has so far received healthy foreign direct investment (FDI) in 2022. As per the latest figures of the government, India has registered its highest-ever total FDI inflows of $84.84 billion in 2021-22.

Concept note-3: -The year 2021-22 recorded the highest ever FDI at $83.6 billion. This FDI has come from 101 countries, and invested across 31 UTs and States and 57 sectors in the country.

Concept note-4: -FDI in India 2022: Karnataka, Maharashtra highest FDI states in India in FY 2021-22. FDI in India 2022: The highest FDI in India was from two countries Singapore and USA in FY 2021-22, with Singapore accounting for 27.01 percent of the FDI equity inflows in India and US for 17.94 percent.
