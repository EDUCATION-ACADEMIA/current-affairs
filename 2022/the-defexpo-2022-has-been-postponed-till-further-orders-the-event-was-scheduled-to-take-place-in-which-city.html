The DefExpo 2022 has been postponed till further orders. The event was scheduled to take place in which city?

(A) ** Gandhinagar

(B) Ranchi

(C) Bengaluru

(D) Lucknow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -12th DefExpo to be held in Gandhinagar, Gujarat between October 18-22, 2022. The 12th edition of DefExpo - India’s flagship exhibition on Land, Naval and Homeland Security systems - will be held in Gandhinagar, Gujarat between October 18-22, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Why in News? The 12th Edition of DefExpo 2022 is being held in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. The 11th edition of DefExpo was held at Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh) in 2020.

Concept note-3: -In order to boost the defence sector, Prime Minister (PM) Narendra Modi inaugurated the DefExpo 2022 (12th edition), India’s “biggest ever” defence exhibition, in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, from October 18 – 22, 2022.

Concept note-4: -12th Defense Expo begins in Gujarat; India underlines commitment to further defence engagements with African countries. The 12th edition of the biennial Defence Expo began in Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar, Gujarat today.

Concept note-5: -The upcoming ‘DefExpo 2022’, slated to be held in Gujarat’s Gandhinagar, is set to be the first-ever edition exclusively for Indian companies, the Ministry of Defence said on Saturday.
