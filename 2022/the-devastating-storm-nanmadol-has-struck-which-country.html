The devastating storm Nanmadol has struck which country?

(A) India

(B) Vietnam

(C) Laos

(D) ** Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Authorities warned millions of citizens to take shelter as Typhoon Nanmadol made landfall in southwestern Japan on Sunday night. The massive storm was packing fierce gusts and torrential rain.

Concept note-2: -Later that same day, upon attaining tropical storm strength, it was named Nanmadol by the Japan Meteorological Agency (JMA).

Concept note-3: -China. The fact that China’s typhoon season lasts the entire year makes it the country that experiences the most hurricanes.

Concept note-4: -Nan Madol is an archaeological site adjacent to the eastern shore of the island of Pohnpei, now part of the Madolenihmw district of Pohnpei state in the Federated States of Micronesia in the western Pacific Ocean.

Concept note-5: -Nanmadol passed by Ibusuki, Kagoshima Prefecture, around 17:30 local time on Sunday, 18 September. Nanmadol’s center made first landfall near the city of Kagoshima in Kagoshima Prefecture, around 19:00 local time on Sunday, 18 September.
