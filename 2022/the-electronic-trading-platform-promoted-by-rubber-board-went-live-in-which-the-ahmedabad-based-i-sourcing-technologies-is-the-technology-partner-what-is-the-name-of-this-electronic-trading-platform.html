The electronic trading platform promoted by Rubber Board went live in which the Ahmedabad-based i-Sourcing Technologies is the technology partner. What is the name of this electronic trading platform?

(A) rRube

(B) iRube

(C) tRube

(D) ** mRube

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ahmedabad-based i-Sourcing Technologies is the technology partner. mRube follows a simple one-time registration. KN Raghavan, Executive Director will launch the beta version of the electronic platform at a function in the presence of the Rubber Producer Society and other industry representatives.

Concept note-2: -The Beta launch of ‘mRube’, the e-trading platform for natural rubber, was inaugurated on Wednesday by KN Raghavan, Executive Director, Rubber Board, at the Rubber Research Institute of India, Kottayam.

Concept note-3: -Notes: mRube, the electronic trading platform promoted by Rubber Board will go live from June 8. The Ahmedabad-based i-Sourcing Technologies is the technology partner. mRube follows a simple one-time registration.

Concept note-4: -mRube, the electronic market for natural rubber, was rolled out on June 8.
