The first-ever pure Green Hydrogen plant in India has been unveiled in which state?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) Sikkim

(C) Telangana

(D) ** Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Oil India Limited (OIL) has taken the first significant step towards Green Hydrogen Economy in India with the commissioning of India’s First 99.999% pure Green Hydrogen pilot plant, with an installed capacity of 10 kg per day at its Jorhat Pump Station in Assam today.

Concept note-2: -Gujarat: First phase of Green Hydrogen plant commissioned at L&T’s Hazira complex | Cities News,The Indian Express.

Concept note-3: -NTPC Ltd commissions India’s first green hydrogen blending project. The green hydrogen blending has been started in the piped natural gas (PNG) network of NTPC Kawas township, Surat. The project is a joint effort of NTPC and Gujarat Gas Limited (GGL).

Concept note-4: -PM Narendra Modi lays foundation stone of 1st Green Hydrogen Mobility Project of India , Leh Ladakh | The Administration of Union Territory of Ladakh | India. S.O.

Concept note-5: -UPSC Mains. Q. India’s first Green Hydrogen Micro-grid Project is to come up in which state? Notes: National Thermal Power Corporation Limited (NTPC) has awarded a fuel-cell based Green Hydrogen Micro-grid Project at Simhadri in Andhra Pradesh.
