The first Partial Solar Eclipse will occur on which date?

(A) May 05

(B) ** April 30

(C) April 29

(D) May 01

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the information received, the solar eclipse that is Surya Grahan October 2022 date and time will start in India from 11:30 in the day and after about 7 hours at 5:24 in the evening, the sun will get salvation.

Concept note-2: -The last solar eclipse took place on June 21, 2020, and the next eclipse will take place on August 2, 2027. Meanwhile, there will also be a lunar eclipse to take place on October 28, 2023, but it will not be visible.

Concept note-3: -The solar eclipse will take place between 4:29 pm and 5:43 pm. The eclipse will be visible globally like in parts of Europe, Northern Africa, and parts of western and central Asia. In India the celestial phenomenon will be sighted across Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Ujjain, Varanasi, Bengaluru, and Mathura.

Concept note-4: -In India, the Solar Eclipse will visible from 04:29 pm and will end with the sunset at 05:42 pm. The maximum eclipse time will be at 05:30 pm.
