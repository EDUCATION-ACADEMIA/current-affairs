The Government of India has organized the annual Polio National Immunization Day 2022 on which day?

(A) February 24

(B) February 25

(C) February 26

(D) ** February 27

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first Sub-National Immunization Day for 2022 for administering Polio vaccine drops is being conducted from 19th June 2022 in 11 states/ UTs of the country viz. Bihar, Chandigarh, Delhi, Gujarat, Haryana, Jharkhand, Maharashtra, Punjab, Rajasthan, Uttarakhand, and West Bengal.

Concept note-2: -The election bound states of Uttar Pradesh and Manipur plan to organize the Polio NID on 20th March and 24th March 2022 respectively.

Concept note-3: -KABUL, 17 January 2022 - The first national polio immunization campaign for 2022 begins this week, 17-20 January 2022 targeting 9.9 million children aged 0–59 months across the country.

Concept note-4: -World Polio Day 2022 and Beyond: A healthier future for mothers and children.

Concept note-5: -National Immunization Day (NID) commonly known as Pulse Polio Immunization programme was launched in India in 1995, and is conducted twice in early part of each year. Additionally multiple rounds (at least two) of sub national immunization day (SNID) have been conducted over the years in high risk states/areas.
