The India-Sri Lanka bilateral maritime exercise SLINEX 2022 will be held in which place?

(A) ** Visakhapatnam

(B) Kochi

(C) Chennai

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ninth Edition of India - Sri Lanka Bilateral Maritime Exercise SLINEX (Sri Lanka–India Naval Exercise) is scheduled at Visakhapatnam from 07 March to 10 March 2022.

Concept note-2: -The 9th edition of India – Sri Lanka Bilateral Maritime Exercise named SLINEX (Sri Lanka–India Naval Exercise) is held at Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-3: -Mitra Shakti Exercise is a bilateral joint military exercise between the armies of India and Sri Lanka. It is also called the India-Sri Lanka Joint Training Exercise. It is one of the important joint military exercises that India conducts with other nations.

Concept note-4: -THE NINTH edition of the India-Sri Lanka bilateral maritime exercise SLINEX is underway at Visakhapatnam with an aim to enhance interoperability and improve mutual understanding between the navies of the two neighbouring countries in the strategically significant Indian Ocean Region.
