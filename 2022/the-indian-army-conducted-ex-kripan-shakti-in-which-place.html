The Indian Army conducted Ex Kripan Shakti in which place?

(A) Leh

(B) Ahmednagar

(C) Jabalpur

(D) ** Siliguri

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. ‘Kripan Shakti’ Integrated Fire Power exercise was held in which state? Notes: The Trishakti Corps conducted Exercises Kripan Shakti, an Integrated Fire Power exercise at Teesta Field Firing Ranges (TFFR) near Siliguri in West Bengal.

Concept note-2: -India–Malaysia joint military exercise “Harimau Shakti 2022” commenced at Pulai, Kluang, Malaysia on 28th November 2022. The annual training exercise between the Indian and Malaysian Army will culminate on 12th December 2022. The military exercise will be conducted for the next two weeks in Malaysia.

Concept note-3: -It is one of the important joint military exercises that India conducts with other nations. The 8th edition of India-Sri Lanka bilateral joint exercise Mitra Shakti began on October 5, 2021, in the Combat Training School in Ampara, Sri Lanka.

Concept note-4: -Mitra Shakti exercise is a bilateral joint military exercise between armies of India and Sri Lanka. It is also called the India-Sri Lanka Joint Training exercise. The latest edition of the Mitra Shakti exercise is called Mitra Shakti-VIII and took place in October 2021 (4th Oct – 16th Oct) in Sri Lanka.

Concept note-5: -Frequently Asked Questions about Maitree Exercise It is an annual event between the troops of the armies of both countries. It is also called Indo-Thailand Joint Exercise.
