The Indian Navy has hosted which edition of the Singapore-India Maritime Bilateral Exercise (SIMBEX) at Visakhapatnam?

(A) 25

(B) 10

(C) 36

(D) ** 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It also highlights the commitment and contribution of the two nations towards enhancing maritime security in the Indian Ocean Region. The Indian Navy is hosting the 29th edition of the Singapore-India Maritime Bilateral Exercise (SIMBEX) from 26 to 30 October, 2022 at Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-2: -It was the 28th edition of the bilateral naval exercise between India & Singapore. INS Ranvijay (Guided Missile Destroyer), INS Kiltan (Anti-submarine warfare corvette), INS Kora (Guided Missile Corvette) and P8I Long Range Maritime Patrol Aircraft represented Indian Navy.

Concept note-3: -12th edition of Agni Warrior exercise between Indian and Singapore Concludes. The 12th Edition of Exercise Agni Warrior, a bilateral exercise between the Singapore & Indian Army, which had commenced on 13 November 2022 concluded at Field Firing Ranges, Devlali (Maharashtra).

Concept note-4: -India Navy conducts maritime exercise with Singapore at Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-5: -Singapore India Maritime Bilateral Exercise (SIMBEX) is an annual bilateral naval exercise conducted by the Indian Navy and the Republic of Singapore Navy (RSN).
