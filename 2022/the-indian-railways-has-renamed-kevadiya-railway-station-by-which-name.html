The Indian Railways has renamed Kevadiya railway station by which name?

(A) Biswas Nagar railway station

(B) ** Ekta Nagar railway station

(C) Patel Nagar railway station

(D) Gandhi Nagar railway station

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ekta Nagar railway station (station code: EKNR) earlier known as Kevadiya railway station (station code: KDCY) is located in Kevadiya, Narmada district, Gujarat, India. It consists of 3 platforms.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of Railways has approved the renaming of the Kevadiya railway station in the Narmada district of Gujarat, as Ekta Nagar railway station. The Statue of Unity’s Kevadiya railway station falls under the Vadodara division.

Concept note-3: -Kevadiya Railway Station (KDCY) Railway Station, Book Trains, Check Online Reservation, Timetable & Schedule - Goibibo.

Concept note-4: -A few days ago, the Kevadiya railway station was renamed Ekta Nagar railway station. The Railway Ministry had approved the renaming of the Statue of Unity’s Kevadiya railway station.

Concept note-5: -CM Yogi Adityanath is known for changing the name of railway stations, districts, roads etc.
