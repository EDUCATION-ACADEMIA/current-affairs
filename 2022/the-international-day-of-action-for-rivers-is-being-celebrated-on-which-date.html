The International Day of Action for Rivers is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** March 14

(B) March 11

(C) March 12

(D) March 13

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International Day of Action For Rivers, observed on March 14 every year, is a day that is dedicated to saving, celebrating, and creating awareness about the importance of rivers. Did you know that two million tons of sewage, industrial and agricultural waste are discharged into the world’s water every day?
