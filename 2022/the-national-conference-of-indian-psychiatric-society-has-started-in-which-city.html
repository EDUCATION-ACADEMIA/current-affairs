The National Conference of Indian Psychiatric Society has started in which city?

(A) ** Visakhapatnam

(B) Mumbai

(C) Hyderabad

(D) Jaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -73rd Annual National Conference of Indian Psychiatric Society (ANCIPS2022) is organized by Medisquire Global Education Resources (MGER) and will be held from Jan 20 - 23, 2022 at Vizag Conventions, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India.

Concept note-2: -It is time for us to look forward to congregating once again at Annual National Conference of IPS (ANCIPS) that is being held at Bhubaneswar, Odisha in 2023.

Concept note-3: -Postal Address C-827, Indira Nagar, (Near H.A.L. Main gate), Lucknow-226016, U.P., India.

Concept note-4: -The organisation holds its origins in 1929, when Berkeley Hill founded the Indian Association for Mental Hygiene. In 1935, the Indian division of the Royal Medico Psychological Association was formed due to the efforts of Banarasi Das.
