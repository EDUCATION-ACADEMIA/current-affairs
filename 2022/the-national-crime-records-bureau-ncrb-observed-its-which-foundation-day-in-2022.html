The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) observed it’s which Foundation Day in 2022?

(A) 31st

(B) 39th

(C) ** 37th

(D) 35th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -37th Foundation of National Crime Records Bureau | 14 Mar 2022.

Concept note-2: -NCRB was set-up in 1986 to function as a repository of information on crime and criminals so as to assist the investigators in linking crime to the perpetrators based on the recommendations of the Tandon Committee, National Police Commission (1977-1981) and the MHA’s Task force (1985).

Concept note-3: -New Delhi: Northern Coalfields Limited (NCL), a Miniratna PSU and subsidiary company of Coal India Limited (CIL) celebrated its 36th Foundation on Saturday.

Concept note-4: -National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) – Formation NCRB was set-up in 1986. NCRB was formed by merging the Inter-State Criminals Data Branch, Directorate of Coordination and Police Computer (DCPC), and Central Finger Print Bureau of CBI.

Concept note-5: -According to the NCRB report, the number of crimes against women grew from 56.5% in 2020 to 64.5% in 2021, (incidents per 1 lakh population).
