The No Smoking Day 2022 is being celebrated on which date?

(A) March 07

(B) March 10

(C) March 11

(D) ** March 09

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 1987, the World Health Assembly passed Resolution WHA40. 38, calling for 7 April 1988 to be a “a world no-smoking day.” In 1988, Resolution WHA42. 19 was passed, calling for the celebration of World No Tobacco Day, every year on 31 May.

Concept note-2: -World No Tobacco Day 2022 Webinar WHO in the Western Pacific will host a World No Tobacco Day webinar: “The Devastating Environmental Impact of Tobacco” on 26 May 2022, 10:00 - 11:30 (GMT+8 Manila time).

Concept note-3: -The important message is to assist smokers in quitting their harmful habit. No Smoking Day: Quit smoking today! Every year on the second Wednesday of March, No Smoking Day is observed. It falls on March 9 this year.

Concept note-4: -Quit tobacco to save our planet.
