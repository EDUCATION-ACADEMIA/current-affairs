The PM will dedicate how many National Institutes of AYUSH to the country?

(A) Two

(B) Four

(C) ** Three

(D) Five

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi to dedicate three National Ayush Institutes to the nation on 11 December 2022.

Concept note-2: -National Institute of Ayurveda (NIA), Jaipur 2. National Institute of Homeopathy (NIH), Kolkata 3. National Institute of Unani Medicine (NIUM), Bengaluru 4. National Institute of Siddha (NIS), Chennai 5.

Concept note-3: -AYUSH is an acronym for Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homeopathy and are the six Indian systems of medicine prevalent and practiced in India and some of the neighboring Asian countries with very few exceptions in some of the developed countries.
