(A) CII and FICCI

(B) ** FICCI and CIPET

(C) CIPET and ASSOCHAM

(D) FICCI and ASSOCHAM

Concept note-1: -Dr. Mansukh Mandaviya, Minister of Health and Family Welfare and Minister of Chemicals and Fertilizers.
