The Spices Board of India has launched India’s first portal for spice exports. What is the name of the portal?

(A) Spice Export India

(B) ** Spice Xchange India

(C) Spice Trade India

(D) Spice Chat India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Launched by the Spices Board, spicexchangeindia.com is a 3D virtual platform aimed at connecting India’s spice exporters with buyers from around the world. The portal uses artificial intelligence (AI)-based technology to connect spice buyers with exporters in India.

Concept note-2: -The platform was launched by Som Parkash, Minister of State for Commerce & Industry, Government of India, in Kochi.

Concept note-3: -Spice Xchange India is an innovative virtual B2B platform dedicated to the spice trade that focuses on a 3D virtual portal that uses the most up-to-date AI-based technology to connect more than 50,000+ global buyers with Indian Spices exporters.

Concept note-4: -Address: Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Govt. of India, Sugandha Bhavan, N.H. By Pass, Palarivattom P.O., Cochin - 682025, Kerala, India.

Concept note-5: -The platform was launched by Som Parkash, the Union Minister of State for Commerce and Industry, at a hybrid event held in Kochi and online. India’s first virtual platform for spice exports ‘Spice Xchange India.
