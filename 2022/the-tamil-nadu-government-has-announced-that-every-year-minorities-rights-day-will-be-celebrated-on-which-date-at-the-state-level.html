The Tamil Nadu government has announced that every year Minorities Rights Day will be celebrated on which date at the state level?

(A) December 22

(B) December 21

(C) ** December 18

(D) December 19

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Minority Rights Day which is observed on December 18th every year was celebrated at the National Commission for Minorities on December 16, 2022. The United Nations on December 18, 1992 adopted and broadcast the Statement on the individual’s Rights belonging to Religious or Linguistic National or Ethnic Minorities.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The Tamil Nadu government will observe December 18 as Minorities Rights Day at the state level , every year. As per the State Ministry for Minorities Welfare and Non-Resident Tamils Welfare, Rs 2.50 lakh will be earmarked for the purpose.

Concept note-3: -The theme for 2022 is centred around “All in 4 Minority Rights” as part of the commemoration of the 30-year anniversary of the UN Declaration being adopted. Evert tear December 18 is marked as Minorities Rights Day.

Concept note-4: -Minorities Welfare State Schemes i) Award of Prizes for State & District levels ii) Urdu Award iii) Maintenance of Minorities School / College Hostels iv) Muslim Women Aid Societies v) Jerusalem Pilgrimage vi) Ulema & Other Employees Welfare Board vii) Rehabilitation Assistance to the victims of communal riots.
