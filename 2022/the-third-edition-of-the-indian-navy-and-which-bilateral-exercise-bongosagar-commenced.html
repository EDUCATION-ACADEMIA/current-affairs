The third edition of the Indian Navy and which Bilateral Exercise ‘Bongosagar’ commenced?

(A) Sri Lanka Navy

(B) Russian Navy

(C) ** Bangladesh Navy

(D) US Navy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The third edition of the Indian Navy and Bangladesh Navy bilateral exercise ‘Bongosagar’ commenced at Port Mongla, Bangladesh on 24 May 22. The Harbour Phase of exercise is scheduled from 24-25 May which will be followed by a Sea Phase in the Northern Bay of Bengal from 26-27 May.

Concept note-2: -Recently, the third edition of Exercise Bongosagar, bilateral naval exercise between India and Bangladesh, commenced at Port Mongla, Bangladesh.

Concept note-3: -Exercise Bongosagar is aimed at developing a high degree of interoperability and joint operational skills through the conduct of a wide spectrum of maritime exercises and operations between the two navies.

Concept note-4: -The third edition of Indian Navy and Bangladesh Navy Bilateral Exercise Bongosagar commenced at Port Mongla, Bangladesh.

Concept note-5: -The Special Forces of India and Indonesia commenced the joint military exercise Garuda Shakti.
