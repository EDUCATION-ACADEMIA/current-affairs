The World Green Economy Summit (WGES) has been organized in which city?

(A) Beijing

(B) Jaipur

(C) ** Dubai

(D) Astana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -479. The World Green Economy Summit (WGES) has been organized in which city? Explanation: The World Green Economy Summit (WGES) has been organized from 28 to 29 September 2022 in Dubai.

Concept note-2: -Global Alliance on Green Economy launched during World Green Economy Summit in Dubai | Business Wire.

Concept note-3: -The World Green Economy Summit (WGES) is one of the leading global forums on the green economy. It brings together world-class experts in critical sectors from around the world to directly focus on advancing the global green economy and sustainability agenda, achieving the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Concept note-4: -Forget digital transformation: in 2022 and beyond, sustainable transformation will drive a global surge in demand for green infrastructure that will reshape finance, shake up natural resources markets and redefine the technology sector.

Concept note-5: -The term green economy was first coined in a pioneering 1989 report for the Government of the United Kingdom by a group of leading environmental economists, entitled Blueprint for a Green Economy (Pearce, Markandya and Barbier, 1989).
