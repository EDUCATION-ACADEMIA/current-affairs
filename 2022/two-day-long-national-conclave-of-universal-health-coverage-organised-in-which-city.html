Two-day-long National conclave of Universal Health Coverage organised in which city?

(A) Chennai

(B) ** Varanasi

(C) Delhi

(D) Jaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To commemorate Universal Health Coverage (UHC) Day, 2022, the 2-day National Health Ministers’ Conclave was organised in Varanasi.

Concept note-2: -Union Ministry of Health & Family Welfare is organizing a two days conclave commemorating the “Universal Coverage Day (UHC) 2022” on 10th and 11th December 2022 at Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -“Build the world we want: A healthy future for all” Everyone, everywhere deserves access to quality health services, in times of crisis and calm.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is World Health Organization.

Concept note-5: -This year’s theme, “Build the world we want: A healthy future for all,” emphasizes that in order to build strong health systems we need equity, trust, healthy environments, investments and accountability.
