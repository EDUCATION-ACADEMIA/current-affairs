Ukraine will face which country in the FIFA World Cup 2022 European Playoff decider on June 5th?

(A) Poland

(B) Sweden

(C) ** Wales

(D) North Macedonia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Wales face Ukraine in European playoff final for FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar - How to watch. The last remaining qualifying spot on offer to European nations for the biggest tournament in men’s international football will be decided this weekend.

Concept note-2: -Despite having not qualified for the 2022 showpiece, Ukraine have still been in the World Cup headlines ahead of Qatar 2022, after it was confirmed they have joined a bid to host the 2030 tournament.

Concept note-3: -The six seeded teams are Portugal, Scotland, Italy, Russia, Sweden and Wales. The unseeded teams are Turkey, Poland, North Macedonia and Ukraine, as well as Austria and Czech Republic via the Nations League path.

Concept note-4: -Ukraine confirmed to be playing in the FIFA World Cup Play-Off against Scotland in Glasgow on June 1, 2022 - AIPS Media. CARDIFF, April 14, 2022 - FIFA has confirmed the date for Wales’ World Cup Play-Off Final in Cardiff against Ukraine or Scotland. The match will take place on Sunday, 5 June 2022.
