Union Minister Nitin Gadkari has approved the Draft GSR Notification to introduce Bharat NCAP. What does ‘A’ stand for in NCAP?

(A) Access

(B) ** Assessment

(C) Attention

(D) Applied

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -3.1 Bharat New Car Assessment Program (Bharat NCAP): For the purpose of this standard Bharat NCAP is an assessment programme for safety rating of new vehicle models sold or being sold in India.

Concept note-2: -Union minister for Road Transport and Highways Shri Nitin Gadkari has approved the Draft GSR Notification to introduce Bharat NCAP (New Car Assessment Program), wherein automobiles in India shall be accorded Star Ratings based upon their performance in Crash Tests.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains Bharat-NCAP is the initiative of which union ministry? Notes: A draft notification was approved by the Union Minister for Road Transport and Highways recently for the introduction of Bharat New Car Assessment Program (Bharat-NCAP).

Concept note-4: -New Car Assessment Programs (NCAP) test new cars and gives them a safety rating from zero to five stars.
