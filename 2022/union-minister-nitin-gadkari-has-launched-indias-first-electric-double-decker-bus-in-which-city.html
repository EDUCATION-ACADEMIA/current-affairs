Union Minister Nitin Gadkari has launched India’s first electric double-decker bus in which city?

(A) Pune

(B) Bangalore

(C) Lucknow

(D) ** Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Minister for Road Transport & Highways, Nitin Gadkari launches Ashok Leyland Electric Double Decker Bus in Mumbai.

Concept note-2: -Union Transport Minister, Nitin Gadkari has launched India’s first electric double-decker bus at YB Centre in south Mumbai. The name of the bus is “Switch EiV 22”, the double-decker bus will be run by the Mumbai civic transport body from September.

Concept note-3: -India’s first Double Decker AC electric bus unveiled in Mumbai - ‘Switch EiV 22’ | The Economic Times.

Concept note-4: -In August, Ashok Leyland’s electric vehicle arm, Switch Mobility, unveiled India’s first and unique electric double-decker air-conditioned bus ‘Switch EiV 22. ‘

Concept note-5: -Bangalore Metropolitan Transport Corporation (BMTC) has introduced India’s first electric bus for passenger service in Bangalore. Bangalore Metropolitan Transport Corporation (BMTC) has introduced India’s first electric bus for passenger service in Bangalore.
