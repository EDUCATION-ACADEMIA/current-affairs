vIndian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) is to conduct how many month trial on the Bacillus Calmette-Guerin (BCG) vaccine?

(A) ** 10-month trial

(B) 12-month trial

(C) 8-month trial

(D) 5-month trial

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: When is the best time for my child to have the BCG vaccine?It is best for your child to have the vaccine within a few days of being born and up tosix months old, but they can be vaccinated any time up to five years of age. If yourchild is older than six months, he or she will be tested to see if they haveTB.

Concept note-2: BCG vaccination given to babies and young childrenprovides consistent protection (up to 80%) against severe forms of childhoodTB, such as TB meningitis. It can be less effective against TB affecting thelungs in adults. The protection from the BCG vaccine can last up to 15years.

Concept note-3: Children aged between 1 and 16 years who should havethe BCG vaccine. BCG vaccination is recommended for all older children and adultsat risk of TB, including: children with an increased risk of TB who were notvaccinated against TB when they were babies.

Concept note-4: In countrieswhere tuberculosis or leprosy is common, one dose of the BCG vaccine isrecommended to new born babies as soon as they are born. BCG vaccineis a live attenuated vaccine designed to help people become immune fromTB.
