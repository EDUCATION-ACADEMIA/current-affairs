Vipula Gunatilleka has been appointed as the new CFO of which airlines?

(A) Air India

(B) Vistara

(C) Indigo

(D) ** Jet Airways

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He played a pivotal role to turnaround the loss-making national carrier of Angola. Former Sri Lankan Airlines CEO Vipula Gunatilleka has been named as the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) of Jet Airways by the new promoters Jalan Kalrock Consortium.

Concept note-2: -Vipula Gunatilleka is the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) of Jet Airways. He joined the Indian aviation company on March 1, 2022. He is considered a turnaround specialist and expert in the civil aviation industry.

Concept note-3: -SriLankan Airlines, the National Carrier of Sri Lanka and a member of the oneworld alliance, has appointed Joshua Bustos as its Chief Commercial Officer and Shaminda Samaraweera as its Chief Financial Officer with effect from April this year.

Concept note-4: -Nuttall joined SriLankan Airlines as the Chief Commercial Officer (CCO) in November 2021 and was promoted to the position of Chief Executive Officer (CEO) in April 2022.

Concept note-5: -SriLankan Airlines (formerly known as Air Lanka) is the flag carrier of Sri Lanka and a member airline of the Oneworld airline alliance.
