What amount of maximum subsidy on purchasing drones for farm mechanization has been approved under the Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization?

(A) Rs 5 lakh

(B) ** Rs 10 lakh

(C) Rs 7 lakh

(D) Rs 12 lakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(11) The Farmers Producer Organization (FPOs) will be provided grants for purchase of Kisan Drones and attachments for its demonstration on farmers’ fields 75% of the cost of drone up to a maximum of Rs. 7.50 lakhs. The assistance will be limited for purchase of one (01) drone per FPO.

Concept note-2: -Agriculture Drone Price – Between 3 lakh to 11.5 lakh depending on its water holding capacity.

Concept note-3: -FPOs (Farmers Producers Organizations) are eligible to receive grants of up to 75% of the cost of agriculture drones for use over farmers’ fields. Along with this, Under “sub-mission on Agricultural Mechanization” (SMAM) provides grants of 100% on agriculture drones or Rs. 10 lakhs (whichever is less).

Concept note-4: -Sub Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM) will be implemented in accordance with guidelines described hereunder. The scheme will be implemented in all the states, to promote the usage of farm mechanization and increase the ratio of farm power to cultivable unit area up to 2 kW/ha.

Concept note-5: -Plastic 10 Litre Agriculture Spraying Drone/Agriculture Drones in India, Capacity: 5L at Rs 350000 in Nellore.
