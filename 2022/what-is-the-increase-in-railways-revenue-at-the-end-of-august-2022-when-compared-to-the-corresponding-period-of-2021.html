What is the increase in Railways Revenue at the end of August 2022 when compared to the corresponding period of 2021?

(A) ** 38%

(B) 32%

(C) 37%

(D) 31%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Railways’ overall revenue grew by 38% till August 2022 over the corresponding period of last year.

Concept note-2: -26271.29 cr (38%) over the corresponding period of last year. The revenue from passenger traffic was Rs. 25,276.54cr with an increase of ₹13,574.44 cr (116%) over the corresponding period of last year, according to official notes movers released by ministry of railways.

Concept note-3: -The revenue made from the unreserved passenger sector from 1 April to 31 December 2022 is ₹10430 crores, which is a 381% growth over ₹2169 crores made during the same period of the previous year.

Concept note-4: -Sharing started in 2014 in the ratio of 80:20 between IRCTC and the Indian Railways.
