What is the last date to register for the ISRO Young Scientist Program 2022?

(A) April 21st

(B) ** April 10th

(C) April 15th

(D) April 1st

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The online registration process is underway and the last date to apply is April 10 till 4 pm. The announcement of provisional selection list for YUVIKA will be out on April 20, 2022. Interested candidates can register for YUVIKA 2022 at the official website at www.isro.gov.in.

Concept note-2: -Last Date of Application: 10 April, 2022 Submit the online application form and download the submitted form.

Concept note-3: -YUva VIgyani KAryakram or “Young Scientist Programme” is a sponsored programme organised by ISRO for School Children to impart basic knowledge on Space Technology, Space Science and Space Applications to the younger students with a preference to rural areas.

Concept note-4: -Important Dates: Closing of Registration: April 10, 2023, 04:00 p.m. Announcement of provisional selection list for YUVIKA – 2023 : April 20, 2023. YUVIKA 2023 programme : May 16 to 28, 2023.
