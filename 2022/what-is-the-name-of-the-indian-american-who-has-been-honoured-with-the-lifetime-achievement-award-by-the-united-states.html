(A) Ashish Modi

(B) Nahar Gupta

(C) ** Vivek Lall

(D) Ganesh Patel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -US President Joe Biden has recognised Indian-American and a longtime Houstonian, Krishna Vavilala, with the Presidential Lifetime Achievement (PLA) Award, the nation’s highest honour for his contributions to his community and the country at large.

Concept note-2: -Indian-American Krishna Vavilala receives US Presidential Lifetime Achievement Award. The United States of America President Joe Biden has conferred the Presidential Lifetime Achievement (PLA) Award on the Indian American Krishna Vavilala.
