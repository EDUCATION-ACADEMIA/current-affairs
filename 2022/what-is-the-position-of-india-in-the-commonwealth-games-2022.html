What is the position of India in the Commonwealth Games 2022?

(A) 5th

(B) 7th

(C) ** 4th

(D) 9th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Commonwealth Games 2022 Medal Tally The 22nd edition of the Commonwealth Games concluded on 8 August with a closing ceremony with stars line Ozzy Osborne in Birmingham. India ended their CWG 2022 campaign as they ended with a historic 22 gold, 16 silver, and 23 bronze at the CWG 2022, bringing the total tally to 61.

Concept note-2: -India finished at the 4th position in the medal tally in the 2022 Commonwealth Games by winning 22 Gold medals (61 medals in total – 16 Silver & 23 Bronze medals). This is one of the best performances of India in the Commonwealth Games.

Concept note-3: -India finished at the fourth position at the Commonwealth Games with a total of 22 gold medals in its kitty. India won 61 medals at the Commonwealth Games 2022 and clinched the number four position in the medal tally. India’s medal tally consists of 22 gold, 16 silver and 23 bronze medals.
