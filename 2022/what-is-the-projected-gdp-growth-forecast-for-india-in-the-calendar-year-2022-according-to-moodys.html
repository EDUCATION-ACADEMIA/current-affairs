What is the projected GDP growth forecast for India in the Calendar Year 2022, according to Moody’s?

(A) 9%

(B) 8.5%

(C) 7%

(D) ** 9.5%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"For India, the 2022 real GDP growth projections have been lowered to 7 per cent from 7.7 per cent.

Concept note-2: -Moody’s said it now expects India’s GDP growth to slow to 7% in 2022 – versus its previous estimate of 7.7% – and then decelerate to 4.8% in 2023, before recovering to around 6.4% in 2024. The Reserve Bank of India expects 2022-23 growth of 7%.

Concept note-3: -The growth in real GDP during 2022-23 is estimated at 7 per cent as compared to 8.7 per cent in 2021-22, it stated.

Concept note-4: -The World Bank has revised its 2022-23 GDP forecast upward to 6.9 percent from 6.5 percent (in October 2022), considering a strong outturn in India in the second quarter (July-September) of the 2022-23 financial year.
