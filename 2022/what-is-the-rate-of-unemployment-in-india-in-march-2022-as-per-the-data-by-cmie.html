What is the rate of unemployment in India in March 2022, as per the data by CMIE?

(A) ** 7.6 per cent

(B) 8.4 per cent

(C) 7.8 per cent

(D) 8.1 per cent

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The labour market conditions seem skewed against higher education. This is evident from the unemployment rate at different levels of educational qualification. During September-December 2022, the unemployment rate in India was 7.5 per cent.

Concept note-2: -Press Trust of India The unemployment rate for persons aged 15 years and above in urban areas dipped to 8.2 per cent in January-March 2022 from 9.3 per cent in the year-ago quarter, showed a periodic labour force survey by the National Statistical Office (NSO).

Concept note-3: -The overall unemployment rate increased to 8.3%, up from 8% in November, according to data from the Centre for Monitoring Indian Economy Pvt. Ltd. That’s the highest reading since August 2021.
