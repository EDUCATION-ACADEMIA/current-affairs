What is the theme of the 2022 World Meteorological Day?

(A) ** Early Warning and Early Action

(B) The Sun, the Earth and the Weather

(C) Climate and Water

(D) The Ocean, Our Climate and Weather

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -I am therefore very happy that the theme of World Meteorological Day 2022 is “Early Warning and Early Action.” It celebrates the great achievements of national meteorological and hydrological services in improved early warning systems.

Concept note-2: -The organization announces a slogan for World Meteorological Day every year. World Meteorological Day 2022 bears the slogan “Early Warning and Early Action,” and highlights the vital importance of Hydrometeorological and Climate Information for Disaster Risk Reduction.

Concept note-3: -World Meteorological Day takes place every year on 23 March and commemorates the creation of the World Meteorological Organization and the essential contribution of National Meteorological and Hydrological Services to the safety and wellbeing of society The theme for 2022 is “Early Warning and Early Action".

Concept note-4: -The theme for World Meteorological Day is ‘Understanding Clouds. ‘

Concept note-5: -The theme for World Metrology Day 2021 is Measurement for Health. This theme aims to create awareness of the important role measurement plays in health, and wellbeing of everyone.
