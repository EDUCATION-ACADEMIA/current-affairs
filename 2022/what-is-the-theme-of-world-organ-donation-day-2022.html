What is the theme of World Organ Donation Day 2022?

(A) Every blood donor is a hero

(B) Donating an Organ Is Like Gifting A Life

(C) Removing the taboo around organ donation

(D) ** let’s pledge to donate organs and save lives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is the theme of world organ donation Day? The theme of World Organ Donation Day is “let’s pledge to donate organs and save lives”. The day is observed to create awareness about organ donation and thank donors for their life-saving contributions.

Concept note-2: -World Organ Donation Day 2022: Theme World Organ Donation Day 2022 is celebrated under the theme “let’s pledge to donate organs and save lives”.

Concept note-3: -Complete answer: 1) Let’s share our life with others. 2) Come ahead and take part in saving lives. 3) No caste – No bar – save lives – donate organs. 4) Giving a second change of life is in your hands.

Concept note-4: -World Organ Donation Day is observed every year on August 13 across the globe. The purpose of observing this day is to spread awareness about the importance of organ donation and to encourage people to do the same. Organ failure was one of the major reasons that led to a large number of deaths during the pandemic.

Concept note-5: -By India Today Web Desk: Every year, August 13 is celebrated as World Organ Donation Day to create awareness about organ donation. The day marks its importance for encouraging people to donate organs. Organ failure is one of the major issues faced by people across the world.
