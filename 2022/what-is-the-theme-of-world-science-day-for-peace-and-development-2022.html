What is the theme of World Science Day for Peace and Development 2022?

(A) ** Basic Sciences for Sustainable Development

(B) Future of STI:Impact on Education Skills and Work

(C) Science and Technology for a sustainable future

(D) Women in Science

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Participate! The theme of World Science Day on 10 November 2022 is Basic Sciences for Sustainable Development.

Concept note-2: -The theme for National Science Day 2022 was ‘Integrated Approach in S&T for Sustainable Future’. If you’re wondering where the event will take place, look no further. The response is that, due to the pandemic scenario, the gathering will be conducted throughout the country.

Concept note-3: -National Science Day 2022: Theme This year, the theme of the National Science Day is said to be ‘Integrated Approach in Science and Technology for a Sustainable Future’.

Concept note-4: -World Science Day for Peace and Development – November 10, 2023. Science Day for Peace and Development is an annual tradition, commemorated on November 10, to celebrate how science promotes global peace and development.

Concept note-5: -Union Minister Dr Jitendra Singh unveils the theme for National Science Day 2023, titled Global Science for Global Wellbeing.
