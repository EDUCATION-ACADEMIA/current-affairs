What percent of stake has been acquired by NTPC Vidyut Vyapar Nigam Ltd. (NVVN) in Power Exchange of India Ltd?

(A) 11%

(B) 8%

(C) 7%

(D) ** 5%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(NVVN), a wholly owned subsidiary of NTPC Ltd. has acquired 5% equity stake in Power Exchange of India Ltd (PXIL). NVVN has purchased these shares from NSE Investment Limited, a co-promoter shareholder of PXIL today.

Concept note-2: -As per industry data, short-term trading is around 14-15 per cent of total power supply in India and most of that is bilateral PPAs (power purchase agreements) while the exchange trading is 5 per cent. The government intends to increase this 5 per cent to 25 per cent by 2023-24.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is NTPC Vidyut Vyapar Nigam Ltd. NTPC Vidyut Vyapar Nigam Ltd (NVVN), a wholly-owned subsidiary of NTPC, has acquired a 5% equity stake in the Power Exchange of India (PXIL).

Concept note-4: -NEW DELHI : NTPC Vidyut Vyapar Nigam Ltd (NVVN), a wholly-owned subsidiary of state-run power major NTPC, has acquired a 5% equity stake in Power Exchange of India (PXIL).
