When is CISF raising day is being celebrated?

(A) March 11

(B) March 08

(C) March 09

(D) ** March 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year, CISF is celebrating its 53rd Raising Day on 05th March 2022 to celebrate their glorious 52 years of dedicated & selfless service to the nation. The saga of CISF Contingent in CPCL started from 15th Feb 1973 with modest strength and over the decades it has increased along with the Refinery’s growth to 260.

Concept note-2: -CISF was set up under an Act of the Parliament of India on 10 March 1969, to provide security cover to industrial units, government infrastructure projects and facilities and establishments located all over India.

Concept note-3: -BSF Raising Day is an important occasion observed every 1st December to honor the Border Security Force (BSF) in India. Formed in 1965, the primary role of the BSF lies in securing the borders of India shared with Bangladesh and Pakistan. BSF Raising Day was first celebrated on 1st December 1965.

Concept note-4: -Subodh Kumar Jaiswal (IPS) appointed as Director General of CISF.

Concept note-5: -53rd Raising Day ceremony of CISF: Amit Shah lauds CISF for its services during Pandemic and evacuation of Indians from Ukraine. Ghaziabad (UP), Mar 06 (ANI): Union Home Minister Amit Shah on March 06 attended the 53rd Raising Day ceremony of Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh.
