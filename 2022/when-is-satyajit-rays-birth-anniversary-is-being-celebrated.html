When is Satyajit Ray’s birth anniversary is being celebrated?

(A) ** May 02

(B) April 28

(C) May 01

(D) April 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Satyajit Ray was born on May 2, 1921.

Concept note-2: -Satyajit Ray was born on May 2, 1921, in Kolkata. The prolific filmmaker went to Rabindranath Tagore’s university in Shantiniketan to study art. Ray debuted in direction with Pather Panchali in 1955. One of the finest works by Ray was the Apu Trilogy and aided in driving Indian cinema into the global scenario.

Concept note-3: -The year 2021 was Satyajit Ray’s Birth Centenary year. Ray is one of the famous filmmakers not only of India, but of the world. He made notable contribution by taking Indian cinema to the world and catering to the global cinema and its language.
