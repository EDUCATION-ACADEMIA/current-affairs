When was National War Memorial dedicated to the nation?

(A) ** February 25, 2019

(B) February 25, 2018

(C) August 15, 2020

(D) August 15, 2015

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The monument was dedicated to the Armed Forces on behalf of the nation by the Prime Minister Narendra Modi on 25 February 2019.

Concept note-2: -Introduction. The National War Memorial was inaugurated and dedicated to the nation by the Hon’ble Prime Minister on 25 Feb 2019. The memorial complex is in harmony with the existing layout and symmetry of the majestic Rajpath and Central Vista.

Concept note-3: -India Gate, official name Delhi Memorial, originally called All-India War Memorial, monumental sandstone arch in New Delhi, dedicated to the troops of British India who died in wars fought between 1914 and 1919.

Concept note-4: -National War Memorial, New Delhi The only official war memorial in the country, AD India spoke with Lieutenant General Satish Dua, who served as Chief of Integrated Defence Staff and steered the construction of the war memorial from the design to the execution stage.

Concept note-5: -National War Museum is located in Princess Park, near India Gate, New Delhi. It is built in memory of all Indian soldiers who have made the supreme sacrifice for the country post-Independence. Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the National War Memorial in Delhi on 25 February 2019.
