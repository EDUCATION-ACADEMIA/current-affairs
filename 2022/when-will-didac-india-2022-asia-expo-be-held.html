When will DIDAC India 2022 Asia Expo be held?

(A) September 21st to 22rd

(B) September 21st to 24rd

(C) ** September 21st to 23rd

(D) September 22st to 23rd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Asian Summit on Education & Skills 2022 (ASES) and DIDAC lndia 2022 Exhibition and Conference to be held in September 2022 in Bengaluru.

Concept note-2: -12th edition of DIDAC INDIA 2022 shall be back on a bigger and grandeur scale from 21 – 23 September, at Bangalore International Exhibition Centre, Bengaluru, India.

Concept note-3: -DIDAC INDIA 2023 shall be back on a bigger and grander scale from 17 – 19 October, at Bangalore International Exhibition Centre, Bengaluru, India.

Concept note-4: -The 7th Edition of BSX 2022, organised in association with ISRO, Indian National Space Promotion and Authorisation Centre (IN-SPACE) and NewSpace India Limited (NSIL) is scheduled from 5 - 7 September 2022 at BIEC in Bengaluru, India.

Concept note-5: -"Global Destination for Higher Education: Advantage INDIA" The 17th edition of FICCI Higher Education Summit and Exhibition 2022 is being organized on November 17-19, 2022 at Indira Gandhi Stadium Complex (IG Stadium), New Delhi.
