When will the first total Lunar Eclipse of 2022 be visible?

(A) ** May 15-16

(B) May 25-26

(C) May 13-14

(D) May 10-11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Total Eclipse of the Moon on 08th, 2022, Tuesday, 17 Kartika, 1944 Saka Era. A total lunar eclipse will occur on 8 November, 2022 (17 Kartika, 1944 Saka Era). The eclipse is visible from all places of India at the time of Moonrise.

Concept note-2: -In India, the total Lunar Eclipse will be visible only in the eastern parts, including Kohima, Agartala, Guwahati, Kolkata, Bhubaneswar, Siliguri, Patna and Ranchi.

Concept note-3: -Chandra Grahan 2022 In India Date and Time, Total Lunar Eclipse 2022 Time In India: The lunar eclipse 2022 will start on November 08 at 17:32 PM in the evening and will end at 18.18 PM in the evening.

Concept note-4: -The last total lunar eclipse that occurred on November 8, 2022, thrilled skywatchers worldwide, the next total lunar eclipse - also known as a blood moon lunar eclipse - won’t happen until March 13/14, 2025. Daisy is Space.com’s reference writer since February 2022.
