Where has Union Minister Dr Jitendra Singh inaugurated the Seismological Observatory?

(A) Raipur, Chhattisgarh

(B) Dehradun, Uttarakhand

(C) ** Udhampur, Jammu & Kashmir

(D) Jaipur, Rajasthan

Concept note-1: -Dr. Jitendra Singh said this while inaugurating a Seismological Observatory at Udhampur in J&K, an initiative of the National Centre of Seismology, Union Ministry of Earth Sciences. Secretary, Ministry of Earth Sciences Dr. M Ravichandran was also present during the inauguration.
