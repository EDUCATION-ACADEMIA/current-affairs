Where will the 53rd International Film Festival of India be held?

(A) Mumbai

(B) ** Goa

(C) Assam

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 53rd International Film Festival of India (IFFI) came to an end at a ceremony held at the Dr Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Stadium near Panaji on 28 November 2022.

Concept note-2: -The 53rd International Film Festival of India (IFFI) awards were revealed on Monday during the ending ceremony. The Golden Peacock award went to the Spanish coming-of-age movie I Have Electric Dreams, while top acting honors went to Daniela Marin Navarro and Vahid Mobasheri.

Concept note-3: -The Film Bazaar is a five-day event from Nov 20th to Nov 24, 2022, in the 53rd edition of the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) that is to be held in Goa on November 20-28. The bazaar will provide a platform for connecting all the selected film projects with the delegates from the industry.
