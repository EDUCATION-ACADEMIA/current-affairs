(B) New Zealand

(C) Australia

(D) ** Both B and C

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Penguin Tazuni has been unveiled as the official mascot of next year’s FIFA Women’s World Cup in Australia and New Zealand.
