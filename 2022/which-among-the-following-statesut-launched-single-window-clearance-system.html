Which among the following States/UT launched Single Window Clearance System?

(A) Ladakh

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Puducherry

(D) ** Jammu & Kashmir

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jammu & Kashmir has become the first Union Territory to be onboarded on the National Single Window System (NSWS), a digital platform that helps investors get government approvals for projects.

Concept note-2: -16 States/UTs onboarded namely Andhra Pradesh, Bihar, Goa, Gujarat, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Nagaland, Odisha, Punjab, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Uttar Pradesh, and Uttarakhand.

Concept note-3: -In a historic achievement, Jammu & Kashmir became the first Union Territory to be onboarded the National Single Window System (NSWS). This marks a major leap in Ease of Doing Business (EoDB) in the Union Territory.

Concept note-4: -Jammu & Kashmir on Tuesday became the first union territory to start the National Single Window Clearance System (NSWS).

Concept note-5: -Single Window Clearance System is the innovative endeavour of the Ministry of coal to create a platform to obtain various clearances for the operationalization of coal mines in the country.
