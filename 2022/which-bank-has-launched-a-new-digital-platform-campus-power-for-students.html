Which bank has launched a new digital platform ‘Campus Power’ for students?

(A) State Bank of India

(B) Punjab National Bank

(C) HDFC bank

(D) ** ICICI Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICICI Bank | ICICI Bank launches ‘Campus Power’, a digital platform for the student ecosystem.

Concept note-2: -ICICI Bank has launched a new digital-lending platform called ‘iLens’, which is developed by Tata Consultancy Services (TCS).

Concept note-3: -ICICI Bank on Thursday launched a digital platform called ‘Campus Power’ to help students aspiring to pursue higher education in India and abroad. The platform caters to the varied needs of the entire student ecosystem comprising students, parents, and institutes.

Concept note-4: -ICICI Bank Launches digital Platform for the Student Ecosystem – ‘Campus Power’ On 23rd June 2022 ICICI Bank launched a digital platform, ‘Campus Power’, first-of-its-kind to address the needs of the students aspiring to pursue higher education in India and abroad. It is also available for other bank customers also.
