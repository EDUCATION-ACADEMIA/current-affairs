Which bank has partnered with Retailio to launch a new range of co-branded credit cards primarily targeted at chemists and pharmacies in the merchant segment?

(A) Yes Bank

(B) ICICI Bank

(C) ** HDFC Bank

(D) Kotak Mahindra Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is HDFC Bank. India’s largest private sector bank, HDFC Bank has partnered with Retailio to launch a new range of co-branded credit cards. These credit cards are primarily targeted at chemists and pharmacies in the merchant segment. Retailio is the country’s largest B2B pharma marketplace.

Concept note-2: -HDFC Bank partners with B2B pharma marketplace Retailio to launch co-branded credit cards.

Concept note-3: -Samsung India, Axis Bank launch co-branded Credit Card.

Concept note-4: -ICICI Bank HPCL Coral Credit Card is a co-branded credit card that lets you save on your fuel expenses. With this card, you can get up to 2.5% cashback on your fuel spends along with a fuel surcharge waiver.

Concept note-5: -Other co-branded cards include Maruti Suzuki NEXA HDFC Bank AllMiles (car-related privileges), Platinum Times Card (discounts on entertainment), and Snapdeal HDFC Bank Card (online benefits).
