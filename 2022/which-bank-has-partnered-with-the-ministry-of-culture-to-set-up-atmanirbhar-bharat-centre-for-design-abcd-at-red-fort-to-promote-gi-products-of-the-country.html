Which bank has partnered with the Ministry of Culture to set up Atmanirbhar Bharat Centre for Design (ABCD) at Red Fort to promote GI products of the country?

(A) Bank of Baroda

(B) ** State Bank of India

(C) Bank of Maharashtra

(D) Punjab National Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SBI-NCF-IGNCA sign MoU for Development of Atmanirbhar Bharat Centre for Design at Red Fort, Delhi.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains Q. Which Indian bank sponsors the ‘Atmanirbhar Bharat Centre of Design (ABCD)’? Notes: A tripartite MoU has been signed between State Bank of India, Indira Gandhi Centre for the Arts and National Culture Fund for developing ‘Atmanirbhar Bharat Centre for Design’ at Red Fort, Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is State Bank Of India. A tripartite MoU has been signed between SBI, Indira Gandhi Centre for the Arts, and National Culture Fund for the development of Atmanirbhar Bharat Centre for Design (ABCD) at L1 Barrack, Red Fort, Delhi.

Concept note-4: -These schemes and programmes inter alia include Prime Minister’s Employment Generation programme (PMEGP), Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI), Credit Guarantee Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprises, Micro and Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), etc.

Concept note-5: -Recently, an agreement was signed between the State Bank of India(SBI), Indira Gandhi Centre for the Arts(IGNCA) and National Culture Fund (NCF) for the development of Atmanirbhar Bharat Centre for Design(ABCD) at Red Fort, Delhi.
