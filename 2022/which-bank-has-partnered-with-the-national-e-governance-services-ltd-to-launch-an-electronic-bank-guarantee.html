Which bank has partnered with the National E-Governance Services Ltd to launch an electronic bank guarantee?

(A) SBI

(B) ** Canara Bank

(C) BOI

(D) HDFC Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The country’s largest lender State Bank of India (SBI), has launched e-Bank Guarantee (e-BG) facility by joining hands with National e-Governance Services Limited (NeSL).

Concept note-2: -State Bank of India (SBI) has launched e-Bank Guarantee (e-BG) facility in association with National e-Governance Services Ltd (NeSL).
