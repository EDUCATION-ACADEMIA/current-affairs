Which bank has signed MoU with the BSF under the CAPSP Scheme to offer financial security to the serving and retired personnel?

(A) ** State Bank of India

(B) HDFC Bank

(C) ICICI Bank

(D) Punjab National Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: The State Bank of India (SBI) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Border Security Force (BSF) to provide solutions for financial security to BSF personnel through the Central Armed Police Salary Package (CAPSP) Scheme.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi, November 19, 2022: Bank of Baroda (Bank), one of India’s leading public sector banks, today announced the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Border Security Force to extend the Baroda Central Forces Salary Package offerings to its serving personnel and pensioners.

Concept note-3: -Banque Saudi Fransi has a branch network of 120 branches. In June 2012 Fitch Ratings has affirmed Banque Saudi Fransi’s (BSF) Long-term Issuer Default Rating (IDR) at ‘A’ with a Stable Outlook and Short-term IDR at ‘F1’.

Concept note-4: -Personnel of the following Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) can avail of Salary Accounts under Central Armed Police Salary Package. Retired Personnels can also open / convert their accounts in CAPSP – Pension and will be eligible for all benefits mentioned below except Insurance, Overdraft and Xpress Credit.
