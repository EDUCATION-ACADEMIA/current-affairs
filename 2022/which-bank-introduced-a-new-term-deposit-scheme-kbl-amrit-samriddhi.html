Which bank introduced a new Term Deposit Scheme, “KBL Amrit Samriddhi”?

(A) ICICI Bank

(B) Kota Bank

(C) ** Karnataka Bank

(D) Kotak Mahindra Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Karnataka Bank has introduced a new term deposit scheme, KBL Amrit Samriddhi, under ACC and fixed deposit for a tenure of 75 weeks (525 days). The interest rate for this deposit scheme is 6.10% per annum. The scheme has been introduced on the occasion of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav.

Concept note-2: -“I also wish to inform our esteemed shareholders that, as a precursor to the Centenary year 2023-24, the Bank has launched a Term Deposit Scheme, “KBL Centenary Deposit Scheme", with an attractive rate of interest of 7.20% for the general public and 7.60% p.a. for Senior Citizens.

Concept note-3: -Baroda Tiranga Deposit Scheme is a special term deposit product scheme that is offering now an interest up to 7.50% along with 0.50% extra for senior citizens and 0.25 % for no callable deposits. 3/5. Baroda Tiranga Deposit Scheme tenors. The scheme is of two tenors – 444 days and 555 days.
