Which bank raises Benchmark Lending Rate by 0.7 per cent?

(A) ADB

(B) PNB

(C) RBI

(D) ** SBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The bank has also raised the base rate by similar basis points to 8.7 per cent, effective Thursday.

Concept note-2: -The increase in the benchmark lending rates comes weeks ahead of the Reserve Bank of India’s (RBI) monetary policy meeting, which is again expected to hike rates to tame inflation.

Concept note-3: -Benchmark Prime Lending Rate (BPLR) revised as 14.15% p.a. w.e.f. 15.12. 2022.

Concept note-4: -For Men: 1.15% above 1 year MCLR i.e. 9.70% p.a. Men: 2.25% above 1 year MCLR i.e. 10.80% p.a. For Women: 2.20% above 1 year MCLR i.e. 10.75% p.a. From 11.05% to 12.05% (CIC Based rates are applicable).
