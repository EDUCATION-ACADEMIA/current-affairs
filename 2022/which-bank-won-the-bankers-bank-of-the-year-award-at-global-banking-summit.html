(B) ICICI

(C) ** Canara Bank

(D) PNB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Public sector lender Canara Bank announced on December 15 that it has won “the Banker’s Bank of the Year Award 2022” for the India segment at the Global Banking Summit, which was held in London, from November 29 to December 01.
