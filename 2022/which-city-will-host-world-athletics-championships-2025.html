Which city will host World Athletics Championships 2025?

(A) Kuala Lumpur

(B) London

(C) Beijing

(D) ** Tokyo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2025 World Athletics Championships will be the twentieth edition of the World Athletics Championships, and are scheduled to be held in 13-21 september 2025 in Tokyo, Japan. The championships will use the National Stadium, rebuilt for the Tokyo 2020 Olympic and Paralympic Games.
