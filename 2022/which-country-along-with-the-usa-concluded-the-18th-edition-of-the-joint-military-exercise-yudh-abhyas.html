Which country along with the USA concluded the 18th edition of the joint military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas’?

(A) Australia

(B) ** India

(C) Hong Kong

(D) America

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India and the US have successfully concluded the 18th edition of joint military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas’ in the Auli district of Uttarakhand on Saturday.

Concept note-2: -Exercise Yudh Abhyas is conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, Tactics, Techniques and Procedures between the Armies of the two nations. The previous edition of the exercise was conducted at Joint Base Elmendorf Richardson, Alaska (USA) in October 2021.

Concept note-3: -Notably, the Indo-US military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas 2022’ commenced in Uttarakhand’s Auli on November 19. The joint military exercise is conducted annually between India and US with the aim of exchanging best practices.

Concept note-4: -Yudh Abhyas 20: India-US joint military exercise commenced in Rajasthan. As part of defence cooperation, soldiers from India and the United States participated in a joint military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas 20.

Concept note-5: -India, Kazakhstan hold joint military exercise in Meghalaya | North East India News,The Indian Express.
