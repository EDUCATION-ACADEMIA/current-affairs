Which country has launched a military satellite named “Noor-2”?

(A) India

(B) ** Iran

(C) Iraq

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Nour satellite (also spelled Noor, Persian: , lit. ‘Light’) callsign “NOUR 02” or “NOOR 02", is an Iranian military satellite, which was launched to orbit on 8 March 2022.

Concept note-2: -On October 4, 1957, the Soviet Union launched the earth’s first artificial satellite, Sputnik I. The successful launch came as a shock to experts and citizens in the United States, who had hoped that the United States would accomplish this scientific advancement first.

Concept note-3: -China sends second Shiyan-10 test satellite sent into orbit with its final launch of 2022.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Iran. Iran has successfully launched the country’s first military satellite into orbit. The satellite “Noor”, or “Light”, was orbiting 425 km (264 miles) above the earth’s surface. Three Stage Qased or “Messenger” carrier was used to launch Noor.
