Which country has successfully test-fired the Zircon Hypersonic missile?

(A) ** Russia

(B) China

(C) Ukraine

(D) North Korea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Russia. Russia successfully test-fired a hypersonic Zircon cruise missile over a distance of about 1,000 km (625 miles), the defence ministry said on Saturday. The Tsirkon hypersonic system was salvo-launched on December 24, 2021, and again launched on February 19, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Russia test-fires Zircon hypersonic missile: How far can it travel, all you need to know. The Russian navy on Saturday successfully test-fired a hypersonic Zircon cruise missile amid the ongoing war with Ukraine.

Concept note-3: -It is one of several hypersonic missiles that Russia has developed. Putin has hailed Zircon as a potent weapon capable of penetrating any existing anti-missile defenses by flying nine times faster than the speed of sound at a range of more than 1,000 kilometers.

Concept note-4: -Russia conducts test-launch of Tsirkon hypersonic cruise missile.

Concept note-5: -Recently, Russia has carried out a successful test of its Zircon hypersonic cruise missile. Key highlights: Zircon is a Hypersonic Cruise Missile developed by Russia. The missile was launched from the Admiral Gorshkov warship towards a test target set in Arctic waters of the White Sea in northern Russia.
