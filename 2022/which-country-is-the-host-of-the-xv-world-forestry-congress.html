Which country is the host of the XV World Forestry Congress?

(A) Argentina

(B) Japan

(C) ** South Korea

(D) USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -XV WFC will convene in Seoul, Republic of Korea, 2–6 May 2022, with the central theme of “Building a Green, Healthy and Resilient Future with Forests.” The Congress is organized by the Food and Agricultural Organization of the UN (FAO) and hosted by the Republic of Korea.

Concept note-2: -THE first World Forestry Congress was held in Rome in 1926 and the second in Budapest in 1936.

Concept note-3: -The XV WFC, which takes place for the first time in the Republic of Korea, is hosted by the Korean Forest Service in partnership with the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations.

Concept note-4: -5th World Forestry Congress: Seattle, United States, 1960 Forest Protection.

Concept note-5: -The XV World Forestry Congress will take place from 2 to 6 May 2022 in Seoul under the theme “Building a Green, Healthy and Resilient Future with Forests” to further develop the outcomes of major global events scheduled before the Congress, including the Rio Conventions’ Conferences of the Parties.
