Which country is the top-most investor in India during the fiscal year 2021-22?

(A) ** Singapore

(B) UAE

(C) USA

(D) Netherlands

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Singapore (27.01%) and USA (17.94%) have emerged as top 2 sourcing nations in FDI equity flows into India in FY2021-22 followed by Mauritius (15.98%), Netherland (7.86%) and Switzerland (7.31%).

Concept note-2: -Sector-wise FDI Equity Inflows during April-September 2022 During the first half of this fiscal, Singapore emerged as the top investor. It was followed by Mauritius, the U.A.E., the U.S.A., the Netherlands and Japan.

Concept note-3: -FDI equity inflow in manufacturing sectors has increased by 76 per cent in 2021-22. Singapore was the topmost investor in India last fiscal year, accounting for 27% of the FDI, followed by the US at 18% and Mauritius at 16%.

Concept note-4: -The global market for inbound foreign direct investment (FDI) rose by 18% in 2021 when compared with 2020 figures, according to the Global FDI Annual Report 2022. The top three destination countries for FDI globally – Germany, the US and the UK – welcomed more than 26% of total FDI projects in 2021.
