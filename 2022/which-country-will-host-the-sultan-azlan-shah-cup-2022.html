Which country will host the Sultan Azlan Shah Cup 2022?

(A) India

(B) Indonesia

(C) ** Malaysia

(D) South Korea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Malaysia. The Sultan Azlan Shah Cup is an annual invitational international men’s field hockey tournament held in Malaysia. It began in 1983 as a biennial contest.

Concept note-2: -Hockey- Malaysia beats South Korea 3-2 lift the maiden Sultan Azlan Shah Cup 2022 trophy, scores, results. The 29th edition of Sultan Azlan Shah Hockey Cup 2022 event was played in its traditional Venue in Ipoh, from November 1st to 10th Nov.

Concept note-3: -World champions Australia have lifted the coveted 27th edition of the Sultan Azlan Shah cup 2018 in Ipoh, Malaysia on March 10,2018. In the final, they beat defending champions England by a narrow 2−1 margin. With this, Australia has now won the prestigious tournament for the 10th time.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is option 1 i.e Australia. Australia has won the 27th Sultan Azlan Shah Cup.
