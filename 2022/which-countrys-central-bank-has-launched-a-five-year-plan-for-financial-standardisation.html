Which country’s central bank has launched a five-year plan for financial standardisation’?

(A) Japan

(B) USA

(C) India

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BEIJING - China’s financial regulators have issued a plan to advance standardization of the financial sector over the 14th Five-Year Plan period (2021-2025).

Concept note-2: -Notes: China’s four government agencies including its central bank published a five-year plan for financial standardisation The plan is to be implemented during the 2021-2025 period.

Concept note-3: -In the Fifth Five-Year Plan, for the first time, the financial sector was included as an integral part of the Plan.

Concept note-4: -China’s regulators issued the “14th Five-Year Plan for Financial Standardization.” The plan sets quantitative targets on the number of financial standards issued by 2025 in a broad set of areas, including fintech, green finance, securities and futures, and international finance.
