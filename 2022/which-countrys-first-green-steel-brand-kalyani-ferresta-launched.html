Which country’s first Green Steel Brand “KALYANI FeRRESTA” launched?

(A) ** India

(B) Australia

(C) Fiji

(D) Haiti

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India-based Kalyani Group has launched India’s first “green” steel brand called Kalyani Ferresta.

Concept note-2: -Government launches country’s first Green Steel Brand “KALYANI FeRRESTA.

Concept note-3: -What is Green Steel? Green Steel is the manufacturing of steel using renewable or low-carbon energy sources such as hydrogen, coal gasification, or electricity instead of the traditional carbon-intensive manufacturing route of coal-fired plants.

Concept note-4: -Essentially, green steel is the manufacturing of steel without the use of fossil fuels. So-called “green hydrogen” is one solution that could help reduce the steel industry’s carbon footprint. “When burned, hydrogen emits only water.
