Which defence organization will display two tableaux during the forthcoming Republic Day Parade?

(A) HAL

(B) IAF

(C) ** DRDO

(D) BRO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The tableaux are ‘Suite of Indigenously Developed Sensors, Weapons and Electronic Warfare Systems for LCA Tejas ’ and ‘Air Independent Propulsion System (AIP)’ developed for the submarines of Indian Navy.

Concept note-2: -A total of 23 tableaux - 17 from States and Union Territories and six from various Ministries and departments depicting India’s vibrant cultural heritage, and economic and social progress were a part of the parade on the Kartavya Path.

Concept note-3: -West Bengal, Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Tripura, Jammu & Kashmir will be among the 17 states and union territories to display their tableaux at Kartavya Path for the Republic Day parade, the Ministry of Defence said Sunday.

Concept note-4: -Every year, around September, the Defence Ministry, which is responsible for the Republic Day parade and the celebrations, initiates the process to decide the tableaux by asking all the states, the Union Territories, Central Government departments, and a few constitutional authorities to send their tableau proposals.

Concept note-5: -An expert committee directed by the Defence Ministry is in charge of the tableaux selection process. The tableaux proposals received from various states and departments are evaluated by the panel comprising of people from various disciplines such as art, culture, painting, sculpture, music, architecture etc.
