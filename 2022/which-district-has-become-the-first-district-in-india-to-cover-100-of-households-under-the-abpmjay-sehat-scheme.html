Which district has become the first district in India to cover 100% of households under the ABPMJAY-SEHAT scheme?

(A) ** Samba district, Jammu

(B) Reasi district, Jammu & Kashmir

(C) Jamtara district, Jharkhand

(D) Ukhrul district, Manipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Samba district has a total of 62,641 families with 3,04,510 people eligible for ABPM-JAY SEHAT golden cards.

Concept note-2: -In the Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir, the Samba district of the Jammu division has become the first district in India to cover 100% of households under the Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (ABPMJAY)- SEHAT scheme.

Concept note-3: -Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: The Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir, Samba district of Jammu division has become the first district in India to cover 100% households under Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana.

Concept note-4: -Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) SEHAT scheme was launched in the year 2020. The main aim behind the scheme is to extend health insurance coverage to all residents of Jammu and Kashmir UT.
