Which Ecommerce joint has unveiled the design of its delivery drone MK30 drone?

(A) ** Amazon

(B) Flipkart

(C) Jio Mart

(D) Dunzo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -E-commerce giant Amazon has unveiled the design of its new Prime Air delivery drone, MK30 drone which will handle high temperatures and light rain.

Concept note-2: -Having gone through various prototypes since its inception, Amazon Prime Air has recently unveiled its latest delivery drone, the MK30. Set for a 2024 release, the MK30 is the successor- and a lighter and smaller version-of the MK27-2.

Concept note-3: -Image Credits: Amazon Following this morning’s debut of the Sparrow bin-picking robot, Amazon just unveiled MK30, the latest iteration of its delivery drone. The system is the successor to the MK27-2, which is set to debut limited deliveries to residents in Lockeford, California and College Station, Texas.

Concept note-4: -Amazon is now delivering orders by drones in California and Texas with the aim to ultimately fly out packages to customers’ homes within an hour, Ars Technica reports.

Concept note-5: -This marks the ‘careful first steps’ in the company’s future plans.
