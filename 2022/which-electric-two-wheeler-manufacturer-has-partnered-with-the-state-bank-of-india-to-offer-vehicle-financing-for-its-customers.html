Which electric two-wheeler manufacturer has partnered with the State Bank of India to offer vehicle financing for its customers?

(A) ** Ather Energy

(B) Hero Electric

(C) Ola

(D) Uber

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Electric two-wheeler maker Ather Energy on Wednesday said it has partnered with the State Bank of India (SBI) for vehicle financing for its customers.

Concept note-2: -Tata Motors has joined hands with SBI to provide finance solutions to authorised passenger EV dealers.

Concept note-3: -Ather Energy, a smart electric scooter manufacturer has announced its partnership with IDFC Bank to offer a first-in-the-industry financing option to customers.

Concept note-4: -The partnership with the country’s largest public sector lender is aimed at leveraging its reach and penetration in accelerating the adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) in the domestic market, Ather Energy said.

Concept note-5: -Atul Auto, Ather Energy, Hero Electric, Lohia Auto, Mahindra Electric, Okinawa, and Ola Electric are among the leading electric vehicle manufacturers, with other new entrants like EeVe, Matter, and PURE EV.
