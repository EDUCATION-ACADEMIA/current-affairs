Which foundation day has been celebrated by the Border Roads Organization?

(A) 60th

(B) 68th

(C) 65th

(D) ** 62nd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ans. The BRO Raising Day is observed on May 7 every year. The Border RoadsOrganisation was formed on May 7, 1960.

Concept note-2: -Border roads are those roads that are built in the border area for the defence of the country. They are built by the Border Roads Organisation or BRO. These roads have aided the economic development of these border areas and helped armed forces keep vigil in remote areas in north and north-eastern parts of the country.

Concept note-3: -The BRO was formed on 7 May 1960 to secure India’s borders and develop infrastructure in remote areas of the north and north-east states of the country.

Concept note-4: -BRO is the leading road construction organisation in India under the Ministry of Defence which also provides support to the Indian Armed Forces. 7th May 2022 marks the observance of the 63rd Raising Day of BRO.

Concept note-5: -The Border Road Organisation (BRO) was set up in 1960 for strengthening defence preparedness and accelerating economic development through improvement of roads along the northern and north-eastern boundary of the country.
