Which government has announced a new IT/ITeS policy for the next five years?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Maharashtra

(D) ** Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gujarat government announces new IT/ITeS policy, aims to generate 1 lakh employment in next five years. Gujarat government has announced a new IT/ITeS policy for the next five years.

Concept note-2: -The new Gujarat IT/ITeS policy was unveiled on 8 February 2022 at GIFT City, Gandhinagar by Chief Minister Bhupendra Patel. The objective of the policy is to strengthen the Information technology ecosystem of Gujarat and to generate 1 lakh direct jobs in the IT/ITeS sector.

Concept note-3: -Overview. The Government of Maharashtra announced its 1st LoI / Registrations to IT/ITES Parks and Units Policy in 1998.

Concept note-4: -Minister for Communications, Electronics & Information Technology and Railways, Government of India.
