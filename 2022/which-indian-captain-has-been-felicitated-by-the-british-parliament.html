Which Indian captain has been felicitated by the British Parliament?

(A) MS Dhoni

(B) ** Sourav Ganguly

(C) Kapil Dev

(D) Virat Kohli

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It was a memorable day for BCCI President Sourav Ganguly as the former India captain was felicitated by the British Parliament on Wednesday.

Concept note-2: -Ganguly was awarded the Padma Shri in 2004. He was awarded with the Banga Bibhushan Award from the Government of West Bengal in 2013.

Concept note-3: -Roger Binny is currently the BCCI president after he took over the post from former India cricket team captain Sourav Ganguly. Ganguly was the BCCI Chairman from 2019 to 2022. Binny became the 36th president of the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) just before the T20 World Cup.
