Which Indian female wrestler was nominated for United World Wrestling Rising Star of the Year honour?

(A) Nirmala Devi

(B) ** Antim Panghal

(C) Geeta Phogat

(D) Sakshi Malik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian female wrestler Antim Panghal has been nominated for the honour of United World Wrestling Rising Star of the Year. Antim Panghal made history by winning India’s first-ever gold medal in the World U20 Wrestling Championships 2022 in August.

Concept note-2: -Indian female wrestler Antim Panghal nominated for United World Wrestling Rising Star of the Year honour.

Concept note-3: -Antim Panghal created history this year by winning India’s first-ever gold medal at the World U20 Wrestling Championships 2022 in August. The 18-year-old freestyle wrestler from Haryana is also the reigning Asian champion in the same age category.

Concept note-4: -Antim Panghal nominated for the United World Wrestling 2022 Rising Star of the Year award. Indian women’s wrestler Antim Panghal has been nominated for United World Wrestling 2022 Rising Star of the Year award.

Concept note-5: -Geeta Phogat is often considered the best female wrestler of India due to the adversities she overcame and her achievements. Phogat won India’s first women’s wrestling gold medal at the 2010 New Delhi Commonwealth Games. However, Alka Tomar won the gold at the same event as well.
