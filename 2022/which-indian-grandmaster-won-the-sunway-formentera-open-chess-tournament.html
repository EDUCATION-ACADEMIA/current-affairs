Which Indian Grandmaster won the Sunway Formentera Open chess tournament?

(A) Raja Rithvik

(B) Harshit Raja

(C) Sankalp Gupta

(D) ** D Gukesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is D Gukesh.

Concept note-2: -Divya Deshmukh wins second consecutive National Chess Championship, youngest to defend crown - Sportstar.

Concept note-3: -Gukesh D is the 3rd youngest person in history to qualify for the title of Grandmaster, which FIDE awarded him in March 2019. He became the youngest player ever to defeat current world champion Magnus Carlsen, when he beat Carlsen in a game at the Aimchess Rapid Tournament.

Concept note-4: -In 1988, Viswanathan Anand became the first Grandmaster of India, by winning the International Chess tournament held in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. This achievement won him one of India’s most prestigious civilian awards - the Padma Shri.

Concept note-5: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Indian chess player won the ‘La Roda International Open Chess tournament’? Notes: Young Indian Grandmaster D Gukesh clinched victory in the 48th La Roda International Open Chess tournament, scoring eight points from nine rounds.
