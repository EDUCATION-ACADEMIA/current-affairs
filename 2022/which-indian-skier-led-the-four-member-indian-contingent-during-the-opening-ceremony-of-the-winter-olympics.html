Which Indian skier led the four-member Indian contingent during the opening ceremony of the Winter Olympics?

(A) Sanjay Verma

(B) ** Mohammad Arif Khan

(C) Sanjit Mehta

(D) Sandeep Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Know more about skier Arif Khan who led a small four-strong Indian contingent during the Winter Olympics opening ceremony in Beijing amid a diplomatic boycott of the event by the country.

Concept note-2: -Skier Arif Khan led a small four-strong Indian contingent during the Winter Olympics opening ceremony on Friday amid a diplomatic boycott of the event by the country.

Concept note-3: -India competed at the 2022 Winter Olympics in Beijing, China, from 4 to 20 February 2022. India’s team consisted of one male alpine skier. Arif Khan was the country’s flagbearer during the opening ceremony. Meanwhile a volunteer was the flagbearer during the closing ceremony.

Concept note-4: -Arif Khan (born 9 March 1990) is an Indian alpine skier, who competes in the slalom. In 2011, he won two gold medals: in the slalom and giant slalom Alpine Skiing at the South Asian Winter Games. He represented India at the 2022 Winter Olympics in the slalom and giant slalom events.
