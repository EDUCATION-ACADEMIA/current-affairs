Which institute topped 4th time in a row?

(A) IISc Bangalore

(B) ** IIT Madras

(C) IIT Delhi

(D) IIT Bombay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The list of top 100 ranking colleges and Universities in Engineering, Management, Medical, Dental, Law and Architecture fields is available on the official website- nirfindia.org. As per the NIRF report, under the overall category, IIT Madras has bagged the top position for the fourth time in a row.

Concept note-2: -Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM) In fourth place we have the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM).

Concept note-3: -(NIRF Rank 1) One of the best institutes for advanced scientific and technological research and education in India, Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore has topped the NIRF Ranking 2021 with a whopping total score of 83.57.

Concept note-4: -The top five position as per NIRF 2022 Ranking has been grabbed by Miranda House of Delhi University, Hindu College, Presidency College, Loyola College and Lady Shri Ram College for Women.

Concept note-5: -IIT Madras holds 1st rank among top Engineering colleges in India. Admissions to all these courses are done on the basis of relevant entrance exams. Candidates have to qualify JEE Advanced exam to get admission into the most popular B. Tech Program of IITs.
