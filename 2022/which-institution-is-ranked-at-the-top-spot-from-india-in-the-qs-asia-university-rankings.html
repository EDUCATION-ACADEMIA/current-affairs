Which institution is ranked at the top spot from India in the QS Asia University rankings?

(A) ** IIT Bombay

(B) IIT Delhi

(C) IIT Delhi

(D) IISc Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IITB is also India’s top institute as per the list. Seven Indian institutions of higher, technical education have been named among Asia’s top 100 in the 2023 edition of Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) Asia University Rankings.

Concept note-2: -Key takeaways from the QS World University Rankings: Asia 2023. With an improvement on the previous year, Peking University sits in first place with a flawless score of 100. After standing in first place for four consecutive years, the National University of Singapore (NUS) are now ranked second with a score of 97.4.

Concept note-3: -QS Asia University Rankings 2023: The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay is the top educational institute in South Asia, as per the recently released QS Asia University Rankings 2023.

Concept note-4: -While China bags the second place with 97 ranked institutions, India follows as the third most represented country, with 71 of its universities making it to the rankings. In terms of the top 10 institutions, China leads the way with several entries along with smaller geos such as South Korea, Hong Kong, etc.
