Which international airport has been declared the world’s best airport for 2022?

(A) Dubai International Airport Garhoud, Dubai

(B) Kempegowda International Airport, Bangalore

(C) Madrid Barajas Airport, Barajas, Madrid

(D) ** Hamad International Airport, Qatar Doha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hamad International Airport (HIA ) has been ranked and named the “Best Airport in the World” for the second year in a row by the prestigious SKYTRAX World Airport Awards 2022 at the global event Passenger Terminal Expo in Paris, France.

Concept note-2: -Hamad International Airport Named World’s Best Airport 2022.

Concept note-3: -Delhi : The Indira Gandhi International Airport (IGI) or the Delhi airport has been chosen as the best airport in South Asia and India for the year 2022 in the Skytrax World Airport Awards. Meanwhile, the Bangalore, Mumbai, and Hyderabad airports have secured second, third, and fourth positions, respectively.

Concept note-4: -Atlanta continues to be the world’s busiest airport with 4.7 million seats, a 1% increase compared to last month. Dubai and Tokyo Haneda airports remain in 2nd and 3rd places with Dubai increasing capacity between November and December 2022 by 8% and Tokyo Haneda by 7%.
