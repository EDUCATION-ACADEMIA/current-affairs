Which International airport has been named after Shaheed Bhagat Singh in August 2022?

(A) Indira Gandhi International Airport

(B) Chennai International Airport

(C) ** Chandigarh International Airport

(D) Thiruvananthapuram International Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shaheed Bhagat Singh International Airport (IATA: IXC, ICAO: VICG) is a civil enclave customs airport serving the city of Chandigarh. The airport is located in the village of Jhiurheri, Mohali, Punjab.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated Mopa International Airport in Goa, which has been named after former Chief Minister Manohar Parrikar. The new airport, which is about 35 km from capital city Panaji, can handle 44 lakh passengers annually.

Concept note-3: -Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman today named the Chandigarh International Airport as Shaheed Bhagat Singh International Airport on the occasion of 115th birth anniversary of Shaheed e Azam Bhagat Singh.

Concept note-4: -Chandigarh (IXC) International Airport.
