Which is the “Best Performing UT” in the PMAY-U Awards 2021?

(A) ** Jammu and Kashmir

(B) Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu

(C) Puducherry

(D) Andaman and Nicobar Islands

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jammu and Kashmir and Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu were adjudged as the ‘Best Performing UT’.

Concept note-2: -In the awards, Uttar Pradesh bagged the first position followed by Madhya Pradesh and Tamil Nadu at second and third place, respectively. Poll-bound Gujarat saw five special category awards - for performance related to Affordable Rental Housing Complexes and ‘Convergence with other Missions’ - coming its way.

Concept note-3: -Uttar Pradesh(UP) has won the top honour at the Pradhan Mantri Awas yojana-Urban(PMAY-U) Awards 2021 under the “Best Performing State” category.

Concept note-4: -The category-wise selection of urban local bodies for Atal Nirmal Nagar Puraskar 2022-23 was done based on their performance in Swachh Survekshan-2022. Under the municipal corporation categories, Dehradun stood first, Roorkee second and Rishikesh third.
