Which is the first company in the world to achieve a three trillion dollar market cap?

(A) ** Apple

(B) Amazon

(C) Microsoft

(D) Tesla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple becomes first company to hit $3 trillion market value, then slips - The Hindu.

Concept note-2: -Apple Inc. on Monday became the first company to hit a market capitalisation of $3 trillion, before ending the day a hair below that milestone, as investors bet on the iPhone maker’s ability to keep launching best-selling products and enter new segments such as automated cars and virtual reality.

Concept note-3: -Just as Apple’s market capitalization hits the $3 trillion milestone, its share price as a percentage of the Nasdaq 100 index’s value is bumping up against a key technical level.

Concept note-4: -On the first trading day of 2022, Apple marked a new milestone: it became the first U.S. company to reach a market value of $3 trillion. Apple’s shares briefly touched a record $182.88 per share in Monday’s trading. At that price, the iPhone maker’s value topped $3 trillion.
