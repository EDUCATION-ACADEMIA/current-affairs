Which is the mascot of the FIFA Women’s World Cup Australia & New Zealand 2023?

(A) ** Tazuni

(B) Naranjito

(C) Gauchito

(D) Juanito

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sydney: Tazuni, a fun, football-loving penguin was unveiled on Tuesday as the Official Mascot of the FIFA Women’s World Cup Australia & New Zealand 2023, capturing the spirit of an event that will go Beyond Greatness.

Concept note-2: -"Tazuni stands for everything which makes the Women’s World Cup unique, and her story will resonate with millions of young fans around the world,” FIFA Secretary General Fatma Samoura said.

Concept note-3: -According to FIFA, the new mascot is a 15-year-old midfielder, who fell in love with football after playing the game on the beach one day. Her name is the combination of the ‘Tasman Sea’, which separates Australia and New Zealand, and ‘unity’.

Concept note-4: -La’eeb is an Arabic word meaning super-skilled player.
