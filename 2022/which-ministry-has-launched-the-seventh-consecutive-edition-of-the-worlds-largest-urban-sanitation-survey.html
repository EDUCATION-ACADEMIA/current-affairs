Which ministry has launched the seventh consecutive edition of the world’s largest urban sanitation survey?

(A) Ministry of Women

(B) Ministry of Education

(C) Ministry of Tribals

(D) ** The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -District ranking introduced for the first time. The field assessment for seventh consecutive edition of world’s largest urban cleanliness survey, Swachh Survekshan (SS), was launched on 1st March 2022 by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA).

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs has launched the 7th consecutive edition of Swachh Survekshan (SS) with ‘People First’ as its driving philosophy. Swachh Survekshan is the world’s largest urban cleanliness survey and is conducted by Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U).

Concept note-3: -SBM-U 2.0, announced in Budget 2021-22, is the continuation of SBM-U first phase. The government is trying to tap safe containment, transportation, disposal of faecal sludge, and septage from toilets.

Concept note-4: -The Swachh Bharat Mission – Gramin (SBM-G) programme, under the Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation, is a flagship programme of the Government of India. The e-Governance application used for its monitoring has been developed in-house by NIC under the guidance of the SBM-G programme division.

Concept note-5: -Swachh Bharat Mission: Daljit Singh appointed brand ambassador for Sahnewal.
