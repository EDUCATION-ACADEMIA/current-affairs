Which ministry has organized the all India program ‘Jharokha’?

(A) Labour Ministry

(B) ** Ministry of Culture and Ministry of Textiles

(C) home Ministry

(D) Finance Ministry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As part of the Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav Ministry of Culture and Ministry of Textiles are organizing “Jharokha-Compendium of Indian handicraft/ handloom, art and culture”.

Concept note-2: -Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: The Ministry of Culture and Textiles is organizing an event titled “Jharokha-Indian Handicrafts/Handlooms, Collection of Art and Culture” to celebrate traditional Indian handicrafts, handlooms and art and culture.

Concept note-3: -Ministry of Culture and Ministry of Textiles are organising a programme called “Jharokha-Compendium of Indian handicraft/ handloom, art and culture” to celebrate the traditional Indian handicrafts.

Concept note-4: -Jharokha is a celebration of the traditional Indian handicrafts, handlooms, and art & culture. It is scheduled to be held across the country at 16 locations in 13 states and UTs. The first event under this celebration is being organized in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-5: -The Union ministry of Textile in partnership with the Union Ministry of Culture is organizing “Jharokha-Compendium of Indian handicraft/ handloom, art and culture” at Delhi from 2nd December 2022 to 15th December 2022. Jharokha is a celebration of the traditional Indian handicrafts, handlooms, and art & culture.
