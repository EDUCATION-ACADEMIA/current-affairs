Which ministry is associated with the Weapon of Mass Destruction and their Delivery Systems Amendment Bill 2022?

(A) Ministry of Home Affairs

(B) ** Ministry of External Affairs

(C) Ministry of Tribal Affairs

(D) Ministry of Defence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Weapons of Mass Destruction and their Delivery Systems (Prohibition of Unlawful Activities) Amendment Bill, 2022 amends a 17-yr-old law, enacted in 2005. Introducing the Bill, the External Affairs minister said it is in our national interest.

Concept note-2: -[6th August, 2022.] An Act to amend the Weapons of Mass Destruction and their Delivery Systems (Prohibition of Unlawful Activities) Act, 2005. 1. (1) This Act may be called the Weapons of Mass Destruction and their Delivery Systems (Prohibition of Unlawful Activities) Amendment Act, 2022.

Concept note-3: -Notes: ‘Weapons of Mass Destruction and their Delivery Systems (Prohibition of Unlawful Activities) Amendment Bill, 2022’ was introduced by the External Affairs Minister in Lok Sabha. The Bill, which was passed by the Lok Sabha amends the original act of 2005.

Concept note-4: -9. Scientific analysis support and participation in the acquisition and evaluation proceedings of all weapon systems and related technologies proposed to be acquired by the Ministry of Defence.

Concept note-5: –No person shall export any material, equipment or technology knowing that such material, equipment or technology is intended to be used in the design or manufacture of a biological weapon, chemical weapon, nuclear weapon or other nuclear explosive device, or in their missile delivery systems.
