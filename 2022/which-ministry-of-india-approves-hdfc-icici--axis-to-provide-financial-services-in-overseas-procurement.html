Which ministry of India approves HDFC, ICICI, & Axis to provide financial services in overseas procurement?

(A) ** Ministry of Defence

(B) Ministry of Women and Child Development

(C) Ministry of External Affairs

(D) Ministry of Home Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -However, Axis Bank provides a better risk-reward opportunity in terms of valuation, as it is trading at 2.5 times its book value while ICICI Bank and HDFC Bank are trading at 3.4 and 3.64 times their book value, respectively."

Concept note-2: -State Bank of India, ICICI Bank, and HDFC Bank continue to be identified as Domestic Systemically Important Banks (D-SIBs), under the same bucketing structure as in the 2021 list of D-SIBs.

Concept note-3: -The scheduled banks are those banks that are listed in the second schedule of the RBI Act 1934. But a bank needs to have a paid-up capital and raised funds of at least Rs. 5 lakh to be eligible as a scheduled bank.

Concept note-4: -ICICI Bank Limited is an Indian Private bank. It is headquartered at Mumbai.
