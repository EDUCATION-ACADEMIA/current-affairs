Which multilateral agency has become the first to open an office in the Gujarat International Finance Tech City?

(A) International Monetary Fund

(B) World Bank

(C) Asian Development Bank

(D) ** New Development Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which is the first multilateral agency to open an office in the Gujarat International Finance Tech City (GIFT)? Notes: New Development Bank (NDB) is set to become the first multilateral agency to open an office in the Gujarat International Finance Tech City (GIFT).

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is NDB. The India office will focus on expanding its footprint in the country.

Concept note-3: -“We welcome Deutsche Bank, one of the leading European banks to launch its offshore banking operations at GIFT IFSC. This will serve as a primer for renowned banks from other geographies to consider GIFT City a viable destination for international financial services.

Concept note-4: -BRICS stands for Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa. India is chair of the BRICS grouping for 2021. BRICS Development Bank was founded in the year 2014 at the 6th BRICS Summit in Fortaleza, Brazil.

Concept note-5: -BRICS bank or the New Development Bank was developed by an agreement signed during the sixth BRICS Summit in Fortaleza, Brazil (2014). The headquarters of the BRICS Bank is in Shanghai.
