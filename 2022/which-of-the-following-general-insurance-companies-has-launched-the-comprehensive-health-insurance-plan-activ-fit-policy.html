Which of the following General Insurance companies has launched the comprehensive health insurance plan, ‘Activ Fit policy’?

(A) HDFC Life Insurance Company

(B) Future Generali India Life Insurance

(C) IndiaFirst Life Insurance Company

(D) ** Aditya Birla Health Insurance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aditya Birla Health Insurance launches Activ Fit a health policy for young and healthy adults.

Concept note-2: -Aditya Birla General Insurance Company Limited Overview. Aditya Birla Insurance Brokers Limited (ABIBL), incorporated in 2001, is one of India’s leading composite insurance brokers, which is licensed by the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) in the year 2003.

Concept note-3: -Some popular top-up and super top-up health insurance plans available in the market are: Health Recharge Policy by Niva Bupa Health Insurance. Lifeline Supreme Super Top-Up Policy by Royal Sundaram Health Insurance. Extra Care Plus Policy by Bajaj Allianz General Insurance.

Concept note-4: -Mediclaim is a type of health cover which offers coverage for expenses you incur during a medical emergency including hospitalisation expenses due to illness, surgery, or accident.

Concept note-5: -Health insurance encompasses two types - Indemnity Plans and Definite Benefit Plans. The indemnity plans are traditional health covers that cover hospitalization costs from the sum assured. Definite benefit plans offer lump-sum payments on the detection of illness.
