Which of the following state/UTs got national level awards for Covid-19 Management and Panchayat Development Index at the 24th National Conference on e-Governance held at Hyderabad?

(A) ** Jammu and Kashmir

(B) Gujarat

(C) Chandigarh

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Award was presented by Hon’ble Union Minster Dr Jitendra Singh on 7th Jan 2022 during 2 days 24th National Conference on eGovernance held at Hyderabad International Convention Centre, Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -MUMBAI: A team of researchers at IIT Bombay was recently awarded the 25th National E-Governance Gold Award 2022 in Jammu and Kashmir, by union minister of state for science and technology Jitendra Singh.

Concept note-3: -The theme of this Conference is “India’s Techade: Digital Governance in a Post Pandemic World”.
