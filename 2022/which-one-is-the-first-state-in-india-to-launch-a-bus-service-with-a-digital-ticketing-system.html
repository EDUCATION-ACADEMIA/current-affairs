(A) Andhra Pradesh

(B) UP

(C) ** Maharashtra

(D) Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which Indian state recently launched a bus service with a completely digital ticketing system? Notes: Maharashtra State Environment Minister Aditya Thackeray inaugurated the tap-in tap-out service on the Gateway of India to Churchgate route in an effort to make commuting across Mumbai easier.
