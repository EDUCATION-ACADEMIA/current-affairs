Which online mobile application has been launched by Jammu & Kashmir Police?

(A) JK Dcop

(B) JK Kcop

(C) JK Scop

(D) ** JK Ecop

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In Jammu and Kashmir, the J&K Police has launched online Mobile application “JK Ecop", an easy way to use interface to facilitate common people. AIR Jammu Correspondent reports that the Mobile App enables the citizens to use a host of services ranging from registering a complaint to downloading a copy of FIR.

Concept note-2: -On this page, jkpolice.gov.in, candidates can submit an online application. State police in Jammu and Kashmir releasing a single announcement to fill up to 800 Sub-Inspector positions and 2700 open positions for CT Constables in diverse positions.

Concept note-3: -Answer: Candidates who completed any graduation degree and age limit between 18 to 28 years (UR) can apply for JK Police SI Recruitment 2021.

Concept note-4: -National police helpline number is 112. National women helpline number is 181 and Cyber Crime Helpline is 1930.
