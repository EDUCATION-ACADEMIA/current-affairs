Which organization has announced the promotion of gender equality in collaboration with UNICEF?

(A) International Solar Alliance

(B) Asian Cricket Council

(C) ** International Cricket Council

(D) Indian National Cricket Team

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UNICEF India works closely with other UN agencies to support gender equality, especially with United Nations Population Fund and UN Women. Civil society organizations, including gender experts and activities are also key partners.

Concept note-2: -The International Cricket Council (ICC) and UNICEF have launched a global partnership to empower women and girls and promote inclusion and gender equality through cricket.

Concept note-3: -Gender equality means that girls and boys enjoy the same rights, resources, opportunities and protections. In 2021, UNICEF ushered in a new gender policy (2021–2030) articulating our vision for gender equality in our programmes, as well as our workplaces and practices, around the globe.

Concept note-4: -Gender equality in education Gender-equitable education systems empower girls and boys and promote the development of life skills – like self-management, communication, negotiation and critical thinking – that young people need to succeed.

Concept note-5: -Gender Equality was made part of international human rights law by the Universal Declaration of Human Rights, which was adopted by the UN General Assembly on 10 December 1948.
