Which platform has launched the SMB Saathi Utsav initiative to support small businesses through digital mediums?

(A) Facebook

(B) ** WhatsApp

(C) SnapChat

(D) Instagram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Muskan Anand. Option B [WhatsApp] :WhatsApp India announced an initiative named SMBSaathi Utsav that aims to support small businesses by helping them adopt digital mediums such as the WhatsApp Business App.

Concept note-2: -WhatsApp has launched the programme, SMBSaathi Utsav, on a pilot basis in Jaipur’s Johri Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar where more than 500 small businesses are being trained on various aspects of running their business online, the Meta-owned platform said.

Concept note-3: -Whatsapp launched SMBSaathi Utsav initiative which aims to support small businesses by helping them to adopt digital mediums like Whatsapp Business App.

Concept note-4: -WhatsApp SMB Saathi Utsav is an initiative, launched with the aim of supporting small businesses. Initiative will help them in adopting digital mediums like WhatsApp Business App to run their business.

Concept note-5: -Technology for MSMEs: Meta-owned messaging platform WhatsApp, which has India as its biggest market, on Tuesday launched SMBSaathi Utsav initiative to help small businesses get onto its WhatsApp Business app for growth.
