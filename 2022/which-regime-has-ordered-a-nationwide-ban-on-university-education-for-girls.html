Which regime has ordered a nationwide ban on university education for girls?

(A) ** Taliban

(B) Saudi Arab

(C) Kuwait

(D) European Union

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Women are banned from private and public universities in Afghanistan with immediate effect and until further notice, a Taliban government spokesman said Tuesday, the latest edict cracking down on their rights and freedoms. The decision was announced after a meeting of the Taliban government.

Concept note-2: -In a televised news conference on Thursday, the Taliban’s higher education minister said it had banned women from universities for not observing Islamic dress rules and other “Islamic values,” citing female students traveling without a male guardian.

Concept note-3: -The Undersecretary of Education Mawlawi Sa’id Shahidkhayl explained in 1998, that education for women was in need of a fatwa regarding its limits. On 20 December 2022, the Ministry of Higher Education informed the country’s public and private universities that women were suspended from university education.
