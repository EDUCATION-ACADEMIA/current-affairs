Which regulator body launched the ideathon named ‘Manthan’?

(A) NABARD

(B) ** SEBI

(C) RBI

(D) IRDAI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: SEBI Chairperson Madhabi Puri Buch launched the ‘Manthan’, a six-week-long ideathon, in association with various institutions including BSE, NSE, NSDL, CDSL, KFintech, CAMS, LinkInTime and MCX.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is SEBI. SEBI has launched the ideathon ‘Manthan’. It is a six-week-long ideathon being organized by SEBI in association with BSE, NSE, and more. It will facilitate the creation of a pool of ideas and innovative solutions revolving around the securities market.

Concept note-3: -Manthan is a 6-week long Ideathon by SEBI in association with BSE, NSE, NSDL, CDSL, KFintech, CAMS, LinkInTime & MCX to promote innovation in the securities market. Manthan will facilitate the creation of a pool of ideas &innovative solutions revolving the securities market.

Concept note-4: -SEBI | “Manthan” – SEBI Ideathon to promote innovation in the Securities Market.
