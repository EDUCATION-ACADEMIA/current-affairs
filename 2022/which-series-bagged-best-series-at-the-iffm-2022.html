Which series bagged “Best Series” at the IFFM 2022?

(A) Mirzapur

(B) Jalsa

(C) ** Mumbai Diaries 26/11

(D) Asur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pakistani film Joyland, debutant Saim Sadiq’s Jury Prize winner at the 2022 Cannes Film Festival’s Un Certain Regard section, won the best film from subcontinent award at the IFFM. Payal Kapadia’s acclaimed title A Night of Knowing Nothing emerged as the winner in the best documentary segment.

Concept note-2: -Here are the winners of the film festival: No End - Nader Saeivar, Best Director. Best Male Actor - Vahid Mobasheri, No End. Best Female Actress - Daniela Marin Navarro, I Have Electric Dreams. Behind the Haystacks by Asimina Proedrou, is the Best Debut Feature Film by a new Director.

Concept note-3: -Mega Star of Tollywood, Padma Bhushan awardee Shree Konidela Siva Sankara Vara Prasad popularly known as Chiranjeevi has been presented with Indian Film Personality of the Year Award for 2022, at the closing ceremony of 53rd edition of International Film Festival of India (IFFI) in Goa today.
