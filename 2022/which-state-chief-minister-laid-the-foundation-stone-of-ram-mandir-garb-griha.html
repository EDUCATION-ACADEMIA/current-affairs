Which state chief minister laid the foundation stone of Ram Mandir ‘Garb Griha’?

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) Uttarakhand

(C) Haryana

(D) ** Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -AYODHYA: Uttar Pradesh chief minister Yogi Adityanath on Wednesday laid the foundation stone of Ram Temple’s ‘Garbha Griha’ (sanctum sanctorum). The chief minister also participated in a ‘Shila Pujan’ ceremony for the construction of the sanctum sanctorum.

Concept note-2: -The Ram Mandir in Ayodhya will be India’s national temple, Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath stated on Wednesday as he laid the foundation stone of the garba griha (sanctum sanctorum) of the temple with a traditional Hindu ‘ shila pujan’.

Concept note-3: -However, on 9 November 1989, a group of VHP leaders and Sadhus laid the foundation stone by digging a 200-litre (7-cubic-foot) pit adjacent to the disputed land. The singhdwar ( transl. main entrance) of the sanctum was laid here.
