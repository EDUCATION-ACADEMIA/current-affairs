Which state CM has unveiled the logo of the 2023 FIH Men’s Hockey World Cup?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) ** Odisha

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Odisha Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik yesterday unveiled logo of 2023 FIH Men’s Hockey World Cup at the Kalinga stadium in the capital Bhubaneswar. To be held in the twin cities of Bhubaneswar and Rourkela, the prestigious quadrennial tournament is scheduled from January 13 to 29.

Concept note-2: -Bhubaneswar, India, April 18, 2022: Hon’ble Chief Minister of Odisha Shri Naveen Patnaik unveiled the official logo of the FIH Odisha Hockey Men’s World Cup 2023 Bhubaneswar - Rourkela at the Kalinga Hockey Stadium on Thursday, April 14.

Concept note-3: -The FIH Men’s Hockey World Cup 2023 will be held in Bhubaneswar and Rourkela, two cities in India’s Odisha state, from January 13 to 29.

Concept note-4: -On 29 January 2023 at the Kalinga Stadium in Bhubaneswar, the capital of Odisha and the Birsa Munda Stadium in Rourkela. This time India will present the hockey world cup 2023 schedule. The matches will be played in the Indian cities of Bhubaneswar and Rourkela 2023.
