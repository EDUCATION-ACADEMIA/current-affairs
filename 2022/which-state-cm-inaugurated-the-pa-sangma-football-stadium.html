Which state CM inaugurated the PA Sangma football stadium?

(A) Mizoram

(B) ** Meghalaya

(C) Assam

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium locally known as Polo Ground is a football stadium in Shillong, Meghalaya, India. It is used mainly for football and has most notably hosted the home matches of Shillong Lajong FC in the I-League.

Concept note-2: -Conrad Kongkal Sangma (born 27 January 1978) is an Indian politician who is the 12th and current Chief Minister of Meghalaya.

Concept note-3: -The PA Sangma sports complex in Tura was inaugurated by Meghalaya CM Conrad Sangma in the presence of state health minister James Sangma.
