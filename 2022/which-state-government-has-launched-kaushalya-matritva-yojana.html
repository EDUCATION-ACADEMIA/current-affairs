Which state government has launched ‘Kaushalya Matritva Yojana’?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) ** Chhattisgarh

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -[Apply] CG Kaushalya Matritva Yojana ( ) Online Application / Registration Form: Chhattisgarh government has launched a new CG Kaushalya Maternity Scheme in state Budget 2023. In this Kaushalya Matritva Yojana, govt. will incentivize mothers on the birth of second girl child.

Concept note-2: -Raipur, Mar 7 (PTI) Chhattisgarh Chief Minister Bhupesh Baghel launched the ‘Kaushalya Matritva Yojana’ and gave away cheques of Rs 5,000 each to five women beneficiaries for safe motherhood at a state-level conference held here on Monday, a day before International Women’s Day, an official said.

Concept note-3: -On March 7, 2022, Chief Minister Bhupesh Baghel launched the ‘Kaushalya Matritva Yojana’ by providing checks of Rs 5000 each to five beneficiaries for safe motherhood in a state-level women’s conference held at BTI Ground in the capital Raipur.

Concept note-4: -Kishori Shakti Yojana was launched by the Chhattisgarh government for the benefit of adolescent girls. The program is run under the auspices of the Ministry of Women & Child Development, Government of India.

Concept note-5: -Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) is a Centrally Sponsored DBT scheme with the cash incentive of ₹ 5000/- (in three instalments) being provided directly in the bank/post office account of Pregnant Women and Lactating Mothers.
