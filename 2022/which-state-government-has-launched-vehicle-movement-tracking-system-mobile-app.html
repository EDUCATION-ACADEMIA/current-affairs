Which state government has launched ‘Vehicle Movement Tracking System’ mobile app?

(A) Kerala

(B) ** Haryana

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Indian state launched Vehicle Movement Tracking System (VMTS) mobile app? Notes: Haryana Chief Minister Manohar Lal Khattar launched a Vehicle Movement Tracking System (VMTS) mobile app fortracking vehicles carrying sand and other mining material.

Concept note-2: -Chief Minister, Haryana Launched Vehicle Movement Tracking System Mobile App on 04/05/2022. Hon’ble Chief Minister Haryana, Shri Manohar Lal launched the Vehicle Movement Tracking System (VMTS) Mobile App on 04/05/2022. This App has been designed and developed in-house by National Informatics Centre, Haryana.

Concept note-3: -About the app: If the vehicle is to move between source and destination checkpoints, then officials can mark the vehicle exit from the originating check post as an interim exit and at the destination checkpoint it will be marked as a final exit.

Concept note-4: -A vehicle tracking system is a software system used by transport companies to monitor the location of trucks, trailers and other vehicles. The software is connected to the vehicles via GPS, giving companies real-time insight into the geographic position of their vehicles.
