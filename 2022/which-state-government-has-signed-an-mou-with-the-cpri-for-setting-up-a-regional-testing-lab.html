(A) Karnataka

(B) Gujarat

(C) ** Chhattisgarh

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Central Power Research Institute (CPRI) has signed an MoU with the Chhattisgarh govt for setting up a regional testing laboratory in Raipur district.

Concept note-2: -The Bengaluru-based Central Power Research Institute (CPRI) on Wednesday signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with the Chhattisgarh government for setting up a regional testing laboratory in Raipur district, an official said.

Concept note-3: -Central Power Research Institute (CPRI) was established by the Government of India in 1960. CPRI is an autonomous Society under the Ministry of Power, Government of India.
