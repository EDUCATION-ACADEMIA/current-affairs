Which state government will launch India’s first state-owned OTT platform named ‘CSpace’?

(A) Karnataka

(B) Odisha

(C) ** Kerala

(D) Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kerala will become the first state in India to launch a state-owned over-the-top (OTT) platform, ‘CSpace’, on November 1.

Concept note-2: -The first dependent Indian OTT platform was BIGFlix, launched by Reliance Entertainment in 2008. In 2010 Digivive launched India’s first OTT mobile app called nexGTv, which provides access to both live TV and on–demand content.

Concept note-3: -India’s first state-owned OTT platform, CSPACE, will be launched by the Kerala government on November 1. CPSACE is an initiative of Kerala State Film Development Corporation (KSFDC) on behalf of the state government.

Concept note-4: -Q. Which state/UT has announced to launch a state-owned over-the-top (OTT) platform, CSpace? Notes: Kerala will launch a state-owned over-the-top (OTT) platform, CSpace to offer movies, short films and documentaries.

Concept note-5: -This OTT platform named C Space is going to officially debut on November 1, and by launching it, Kerala will become the first-ever state to launch an OTT Platform under State Control.
