Which state government will provide free sewer connections under the “Mukhyamantri Muft Sewer Connection Yojana”?

(A) ** Delhi

(B) Rajasthan

(C) Assam

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The free sewer connections will be provided by the Delhi government in 12 colonies in Mustafabad and Karawal Nagar.

Concept note-2: -Sewerage Connection is one of the methods that allows the citizen to connect their household sewerage with the government’s sewerage system which later gets treated and is disposed efficiently.

Concept note-3: -An applicant may download the application form from www.delhijalboard.nic.in or obtain the application form from any ZRO / ZE Office. Before submitting the field application form Delhi, Water and Sewer (Tariff and Metering) regulation’s 2012 may be referred to avoid any inconvenience.

Concept note-4: -Development Charge Water (in case of Unauthorized Regularized Colonies) Sewer (in case of Unauthorized Regularized Colonies) Rs. 440/- per sqm.
