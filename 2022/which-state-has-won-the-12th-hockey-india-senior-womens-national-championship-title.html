Which state has won the 12th Hockey India Senior Women’s National Championship title?

(A) Haryana

(B) Punjab

(C) ** Odisha

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BHOPAL: Odisha notched a thrilling 2-0 victory over Karnataka in a closely-fought final to be crowned champions of the 12th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship here on Tuesday.

Concept note-2: -Hockey Association Of Odisha crowned as Champions of 12th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship 2022 in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh - Hockey India.

Concept note-3: -In the final, Haryana beat Tamil Nadu 3-1 via penalties to win the title. Haryana won the trophy for the first time since 2011.

Concept note-4: -Kovilpatti, Tamil Nadu, May 29, 2022: Uttar Pradesh Hockey successfully defended their Crown with a win over Hockey Chandigarh in the Final of the 12th Hockey India Junior Men National Championship 2022 in Kovilpatti, Tamil Nadu on Sunday.
