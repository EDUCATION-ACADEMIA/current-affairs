Which state has won the Khelo India Youth Games 2021 title with 52 gold medals on the final day?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Gujarat

(C) Karnataka

(D) ** Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hosts Haryana won the Khelo India Youth Games (KIYG) 2021 title with 52 gold medals, of which, 10 came in boxing on the final day on Monday. The late surge helped Haryana leapfrog the 2020 champions Maharashtra on the KIYG medals table.

Concept note-2: -Hockey Madhya Pradesh clinches Khelo India Youth Games 2022 Men’s U-18 title. In Hockey, Madhya Pradesh defeated Odisha 6-5 to clinch Khelo India Youth Games 2022 Men’s Under-18 Qualifiers title in Bhubaneswar yesterday, December 30.

Concept note-3: -Hosts Haryana topped the KIYG 2021 medals tally with with 52 gold medals. Last edition’s champions Maharashtra came second with 45. Get full medal tally. The Khelo India Youth Games 2021 officially concluded at Panchkula, Haryana on June 13.

Concept note-4: -Khelo India Youth Games 2023 medal table. Haryana lead the KIYG 2023 medal table with 21 gold medals, 12 silvers and 11 bronze after Day 6. Maharashtra are second with 20 golds, 25 silvers and 19 bronze.
