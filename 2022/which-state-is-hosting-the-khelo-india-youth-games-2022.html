Which state is hosting the Khelo India Youth Games 2022?

(A) ** Haryana

(B) Telangana

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Madhya Pradesh is hosting the fifth edition of Khelo India Youth Games -2022. In this grand sports event to be held from January 30 to February 11, 6 thousand players will excel in 23 game venues of 8 different cities for 13 days.

Concept note-2: -Khelo India Games 2023 will be held in Bhopal, the capital of Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -Bhopal and Maheshwar (Khargone) will be playing host to disciplines like canoeing, kayaking and rowing, which will be held at the MP Water Sports Academy. Water Sports is set to make its debut at the Khelo India Youth Games 2022, which will be held across Madhya Pradesh from January 30 to February 11.

Concept note-4: -The Khelo India Youth Games 2022 is all set to kick off on January 30, 2023, with a Grand Opening ceremony at the TT Nagar Stadium in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.
