Which state is the champion of the 12th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship 2022?

(A) West Bengal

(B) ** Odisha

(C) Jharkhand

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Odisha was crowned as the Champions of the 12th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship 2022 in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -The 12th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship- 2022 will begin on Friday in Bhopal. A total of 27 teams including the defending champions Madhya Pradesh will participate in the tournament.

Concept note-3: -In the final, Haryana beat Tamil Nadu 3-1 via penalties to win the title. Haryana won the trophy for the first time since 2011.

Concept note-4: -Preview: 12th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2022 gets underway in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh from tomorrow - Hockey India.

Concept note-5: -Notes: Hockey Association of Odisha became the champion of the 12th Hockey India Senior Women’s National Championship 2022. The team won after a 2-0 victory over Hockey Karnataka.
