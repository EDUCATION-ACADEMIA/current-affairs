Which state presented the children’s budget in its budget for the financial year 2022-23?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Haryana

(C) Delhi

(D) ** Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abhishek Mishra. Explanation: The Madhya Pradesh state government for the first time presented a ‘child budget’ as part of its annual financial plan.

Concept note-2: -Children - The budgetary allocation for children as a percentage of the total expenditure has been declining from 3.30% in 2017-18 to 2.35% in the Budget Estimate of 2022-23.

Concept note-3: -Total Expenditure: The government is estimated to spend Rs 39,44,909 crore during 2022-23. This is an increase of 4.6% over the revised estimate of 2021-22.

Concept note-4: -While the size of the Budget grew by 119.78%; from Rs 1,794,892 crore in 2014-15 to Rs 3,944,909 crore in 2022-23, the budget for children grew by only 14.38%, from Rs 81,075 crore in 2014-15 to Rs 92,736.5 crore in 2022-23.

Concept note-5: -10,116.11 crore for the financial year 2022-23 on the third day of the Sixth Session of the Tenth Assembly (Budget Session 2022-23) of the SLA today.
