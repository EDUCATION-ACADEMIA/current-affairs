Which state/ UT government is planning to launch Urban Farming Policy?

(A) Gujarat

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Rajasthan

(D) ** Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The “Draft Citizen’s Policy for Urban Agriculture in Delhi”, submitted to the Delhi government in September 2022, aims to provide a holistic framework for urban farming.

Concept note-2: -Urban agriculture can reflect varying levels of economic and social development. It may be a social movement for sustainable communities, where organic growers, “foodies", and “locavores” form social networks founded on a shared ethos of nature and community holism.

Concept note-3: -An urban farming technology that involves the large-scale agricultural production in the urban surroundings is the vertical farming (VF) or high-rise farming technology.

Concept note-4: -All types of urban agriculture come into one of four different categories: Commercial, Community-Based, Public, and Institutional.
