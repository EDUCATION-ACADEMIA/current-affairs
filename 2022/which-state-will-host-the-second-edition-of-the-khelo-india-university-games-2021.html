Which state will host the second edition of the Khelo India University Games 2021?

(A) Assam

(B) ** Karnataka

(C) Uttarakhand

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The chief minister of Karnataka, BS Yeddyurappa, announced that Karnataka will be hosting the second Khelo India University Games (KIUG) in the year 2021. Key Points The Khelo India University Games will be inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bengaluru. Vice President of India M. Venkaiah Naidu declared the second Khelo India University Games 2021 open at the Sree Kanteerava Indoor Stadium, Bengaluru on 24 April 2022.

Concept note-3: -Uttar Pradesh to host 2023 Khelo India University Games - Sportstar.

Concept note-4: -The mascot, Veera, is an elephant. The official Khelo India mobile app was launched at the ceremony. KIUG 2021, which is the second edition of the meet, will be hosted by Jain University in Bengaluru from April 24 to May 3. It was postponed last year due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Concept note-5: -The Fifth Khelo India Youth Games -2022 will be held in Madhya Pradesh. The games will be held from 31st January to 11th February next year. This was announced by Union Minister of Youth Affairs and Sports Anurag Singh Thakur at an event in New Delhi in presence of Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan.
