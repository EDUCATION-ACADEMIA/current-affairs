Which state’s high court has banned animal slaughtering in public places?

(A) Meghalaya

(B) Assam

(C) ** Tripura

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There is a lack of uniformity among State laws governing cattle slaughter.
