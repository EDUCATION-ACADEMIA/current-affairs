Which state/UT released the ‘SAMRIDDHI’, a one-time property tax amnesty scheme?

(A) Gujarat

(B) Jammu and Kashmir

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Summary. The scheme was launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on 22 January 2015 in Panipat, Haryana.

Concept note-2: -Under this scheme, property owners of authorised and regularised colonies can settle all property tax related liabilities by paying property tax of the residential properties for the current year and previous five years.

Concept note-3: -LG of Delhi launches MCD property tax one-time amnesty scheme - Samriddhi. The scheme commenced on October 26, 2022, and will end on March 31, 2023. The Lieutenant Governor of Delhi, Vinay Kumar Saxena, in October 2022 launched the one-time amnesty scheme for Delhi property taxpayers known as SAMRIDDHi 2022-23.
