Which team has been ranked at the top of World Test Championship Table 2021-23?

(A) ** Sri Lanka

(B) South Africa

(C) New Zealand

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Australia leads the Test Championship points table with a win percentage of 75.56 (with 10 wins, 4 draws and 1 loss in 14 Test matches). India is second with a win percentage of 58.93, ahead of Sri Lanka (53.33) and South Africa (48.72).

Concept note-2: -ICC Test Team Ranking 2023 Australia is at the top of the rating table this year with 3,668 points in only 29 matches. India conquered second place with 3,690 points in 32 matches.

Concept note-3: -According to the ICC Test Rankings in Hindi 2022, Australia is in first place with 2439 points on 128th ratings.
