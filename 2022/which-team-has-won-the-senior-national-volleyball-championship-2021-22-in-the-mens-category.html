Which team has won the Senior National Volleyball Championship 2021-22 in the men’s category?

(A) Indian Railways

(B) Kerala

(C) Indian Army

(D) ** Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Haryana team defeated the Indian Railway 3-0, to win the Men’s title in Senior National Volleyball Championship 2021-22.

Concept note-2: -On December 22, 2022, the 25th National Volleyball Championship was held at Talaiya Field Ground in Panna district of Madhya Pradesh, in which Gujarat in the men’s category and West Bengal in the women’s category became champions.

Concept note-3: -The 42nd edition of the tournament began on December 1, 2022 in various college campuses across the country, location determinations were chosen based on participating teams seedings. The tournament concluded with the championship game at CHI Health Center in Omaha on December 17, when Texas defeated Louisville 3–0.

Concept note-4: -Although they have never qualified for the Olympics, they have appeared in the World Championship twice. Despite having little to no presence at the world stage in its history, the team has enjoyed some success at the continental level by clinching a silver medal and two bronze medals at the Asian Games.
