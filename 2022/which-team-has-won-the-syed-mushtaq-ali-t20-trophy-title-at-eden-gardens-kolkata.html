Which team has won the Syed Mushtaq Ali T20 Trophy title at Eden Gardens, Kolkata?

(A) ** Mumbai

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Himachal Pradesh

(D) Baroda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sarfaraz Khan’s fine unbeaten 36 and Tanush Kotian’s all-round showing propelled Mumbai to its maiden title win in the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy T20 tournament at the Eden Gardens.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-3: -The tournament is named after a former Indian test cricketer Syed Mushtaq Ali. The first match was held in 200607 and the trophy was won by Tamil Nadu under the captaincy of Dinesh Karthick and also Rohit Sharma scored the first ever T20 century in the match.
