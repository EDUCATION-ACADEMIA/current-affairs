Which tech giant company has announced the launch of the ‘Play Pass’ subscription service in India that will provide Android device users access?

(A) Amazon

(B) Meta

(C) Netflix

(D) ** Google

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Google on Monday announced the launch of ‘Play Pass’ subscription service in India that will provide Android device users access to over 1,000 applications and games without ads, in-app purchases, and upfront payments.

Concept note-2: -Introducing Google Play Pass Your pass to hundreds of awesome games and apps, completely free of ads and in-app purchases. All for one low monthly price. Explore a curated catalog spanning games that help you unwind to apps that power productivity-plus a great selection of family-friendly titles.

Concept note-3: -Google Play Pass is now available to Android users in India. The subscription service provides access to multiple apps and games without ads, in-app purchases, and upfront payments.

Concept note-4: -Using Google Play’s billing system, you can offer in-app products that charge users for content or services on a recurring basis, known as subscriptions. Subscriptions can include items like a collection of apps, games, or other content for a recurring fee within your app on Google Play.

Concept note-5: -Google Play Pass is an app subscription service by Google for Android devices. It was launched on September 23, 2019 in the United States. In July 2020, the service launched as planned in other Western countries, including Germany, Australia, and Canada.
