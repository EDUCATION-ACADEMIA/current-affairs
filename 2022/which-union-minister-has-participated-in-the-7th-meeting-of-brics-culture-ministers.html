Which Union Minister has participated in the 7th meeting of BRICS Culture Ministers?

(A) Smriti Irani

(B) Nirmala Sitharaman

(C) Rajnath Singh

(D) ** Meenakshi Lekhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Meenakshi Lekhi , participated in the 7th BRICS Culture Ministers’ Meeting.

Concept note-2: -She was speaking at the 7th BRICS Culture Ministers’ Meeting yesterday hosted by China through a video conference. Ms Lekhi said India believes in diversity of cultural heritage and intercultural dialogue.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Prahlad Singh Patel.

Concept note-4: -Meenakshi Lekhi, Minister of State for Culture and External Affairs, attended the 7th BRICS Culture Ministers’ Meeting via video conference, which was hosted by the People’s Republic of China and attended by all BRICS Member Nations.
