Which Union Ministry implements the RGSA?

(A) Ministry of Women and Child Development

(B) ** Ministry of Panchayati Raj

(C) Ministry of Home Affairs

(D) Ministry of Rural Development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: The Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi launched the ‘Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA), the ‘Transformation of Aspirational Districts’ program, under the Ministry of Panchayati Raj.

Concept note-2: -RGSA - Ministry of Panchayati Raj.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan at Ramnagar in tribal-dominated district of Madhya Pradesh on the occasion of National Panchayati Raj Day that seeks to strengthen the country’s Panchayati Raj system and address critical gaps that hinder its success.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer Slogan of the scheme is ‘Aapni Yojana Aapno Vikas’. Key PointsRashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyaan: Launched in 2018. It is a centrally sponsored scheme.

Concept note-5: -RGSA is proposed to be implemented as a core Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) for four years viz., from 2018-19 to 2021-22 with State and Central shares. The sharing ratio for the State components will be in the ratio of 60:40 except NE and Hilly States, where the Central and State Ratio will be 90:10.
