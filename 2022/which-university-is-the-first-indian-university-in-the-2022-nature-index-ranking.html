Which university is the first Indian University in the 2022 Nature Index ranking?

(A) Anna University

(B) Osmania University

(C) ** University of Hyderabad

(D) Pondicherry University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the index, University of Hyderabad has secured first position among Indian Universities while has been placed at 16th position among all institutions in academic sector. Hyderabad was ranked first with 72 research papers and a share of 19.46 in academic sector.

Concept note-2: -(NIRF Rank 1) One of the best institutes for advanced scientific and technological research and education in India, Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore has topped the NIRF Ranking 2021 with a whopping total score of 83.57.

Concept note-3: -According to the ranking report, the three top universities in India are IISc Bangalore, Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), and Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI).
