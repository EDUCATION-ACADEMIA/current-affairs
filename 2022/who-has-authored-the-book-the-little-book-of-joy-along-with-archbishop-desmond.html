Who has authored the book “The Little Book of Joy” along with Archbishop Desmond?

(A) ** Tenzin Gyatso

(B) Azim Premji

(C) Ratan Tata

(D) Mukesh Ambani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Book of Joy: Lasting Happiness in a Changing World is a book by the Nobel Peace Prize Laureates Tenzin Gyatso , the 14th Dalai Lama, and Archbishop Desmond Tutu published in 2016 by Cornerstone Publishers.

Concept note-2: -The Little Book of Joy by Dalai Lama, Desmond Tutu: 9780593484234 | PenguinRandomHouse.com: Books.

Concept note-3: -"The Little Book of Joy” is a wonderful book for both children and adults. This beautifully written story from His Holiness the Dalai Lama and Archbishop Desmond Tutu speaks to the importance of finding joy and happiness in the little things in life. It’s an important reminder of what really matters in life.

Concept note-4: -This is the story of two friends: the late Archbishop Desmond Tutu and His Holiness the Dalai Lama. Once two lonely boys on opposite sides of the world, they grew up to discover the joy in everyday life and spread their joy with the people around them. Their message for every child is that joy is never far away.
