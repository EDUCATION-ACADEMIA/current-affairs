Who has become the first Indian woman cricketer to win ICC Player of the Month?

(A) ** Harmanpreet Kaur

(B) Pooja Vastrakar

(C) Smriti Mandhana

(D) Sneh Rana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Harmanpreet Kaur Becomes First Indian Woman To Be Named ICC Player Of The Month. Harmanpreet had a brilliant September wherein she lead India to a memorable 3-0 ODI series sweep over England . This was India’s first series win in England since 1999.

Concept note-2: -Harmanpreet Kaur becomes India’s first winner of the ICC Women’s Player of the Month thanks to her leading role in her team’s first ODI series victory in England since 1999.

Concept note-3: -It was no surprise that Gardner was named Player of the Series for her exploits in India and she made it a double celebration by collecting the monthly ICC award as a result.
