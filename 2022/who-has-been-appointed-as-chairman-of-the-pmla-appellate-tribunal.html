Who has been appointed as Chairman of the PMLA appellate tribunal?

(A) Sharad Arvind Bobde

(B) Ranjan Gogoi

(C) ** Munishwar Nath Bhandari

(D) NV Ramana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Madras High Court Chief Justice Munishwar Nath Bhandari was appointed as the Chairperson of the Appellate Tribunal established under the Smugglers and Foreign Exchange Manipulators Act (SAFEMA).

Concept note-2: -Justice M N Bhandari appointed as chairman of the PMLA Appellate Tribunal. 6.

Concept note-3: -Section 6 of PMLA provides for appointment of adjudicating authority consisting of a Chairperson and two other members by the Central Government provided that one member each shall be a person having experience in the field of Law, Administration, Finance or Accountancy.

Concept note-4: -Hon’ble Mr. Justice Munishwar Nath Bhandari, Chief Justice Born on 13/09/1960. Appointed as Judge of the High Court of Rajasthan High Court on Jul 5, 2007. Transferred to Allahabad High Court and took oath as Judge on March 15, 2019.
