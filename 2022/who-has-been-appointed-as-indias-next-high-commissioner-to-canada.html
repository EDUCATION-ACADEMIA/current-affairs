Who has been appointed as India’s next high commissioner to Canada?

(A) Pratibha Parkar

(B) ** Sanjay Kumar Verma

(C) Gaurav Ahluwalia

(D) Kishan Dan Dewal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -High Commissioner Shri Sanjay Kumar Verma called on Premier of Ontario Honorable Doug Ford. Gandhi Jayanti 2022: Acting High Commissioner Mr.

Concept note-2: -Verma, 57, succeeds Acting High Commissioner Anshuman Gaur. Among his other postings, Verma has served in Indian missions in Hong Kong, China, Vietnam and Turkey.
