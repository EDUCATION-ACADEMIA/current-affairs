(A) Barkha Bisht

(B) ** Kriti Sanon

(C) Amisha Patel

Concept note-1: -Skechers India appoints Kriti Sanon as brand ambassador . NEW DELHI: Skechers South Asia Pvt. Ltd, a subsidiary of Skechers USA, has appointed actor Kriti Sanon as the new brand ambassador for Skechers India.

Concept note-2: -Footwear and lifestyle brand Skechers has appointed acttor Kriti Sanon as its new brand ambassador for the Indian market.
