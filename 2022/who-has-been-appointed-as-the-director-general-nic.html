Who has been appointed as the Director-General, NIC?

(A) Ramesh Singh

(B) Vinod Tiwari

(C) ** Rajesh Gera

(D) Soniya Kumari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Rajesh Gera. Senior scientist Rajesh Gera was appointed as the Director General, National Informatics Centre (NIC) on 31 May, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Shri Rajesh Gera has joined as the Director General, National Informatics Centre (NIC), Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology (MeitY) on 31st May 2022.

Concept note-3: -Padma Bhushan N. Seshagiri, founder director-general of NIC, dies at 73 - India Today.

Concept note-4: -Taj Hassan, IPS Director General of Fire Service, Civil Defence and Home Guards 09th July 2021 Till Date.

Concept note-5: -Dr. Neeta Verma is the former Director General of National Informatics Centre, a premier Technology organization of the GoI. With a career spanning over three & a half decades, she has been spearheading the Digital Transformation initiatives through development of Digital Platforms for Govt.
