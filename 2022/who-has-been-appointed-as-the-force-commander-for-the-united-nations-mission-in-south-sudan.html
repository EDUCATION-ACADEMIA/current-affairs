Who has been appointed as the Force Commander for the United Nations Mission in South Sudan?

(A) Lt Gen B. S. Raju

(B) Lt Gen YK Joshi

(C) ** Lt Gen M. Subramanian

(D) Lt Gen Ajai Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -United Nations Secretary-General António Guterres announced on 5 July 2022 the appointment of Lieutenant General Mohan Subramanian of India as his new Force Commander of the United Nations Mission in South Sudan (UNMISS).

Concept note-2: -Sunny skies in South Sudan welcomed the new UNMISS Force Commander, Lieutenant-General Mohan Subramanian from India, as he began his tour of duty in the world’s largest peacekeeping mission.

Concept note-3: -UN chief Guterres appoints Lt Gen Subramanian as Force Commander of UN Mission in South Sudan - Times of India.

Concept note-4: -United Nations Secretary-General António Guterres announced on 16 November 2021 the appointment of Lieutenant General Cornelis Johannes Matthijssen of the Netherlands as Force Commander of the United Nations Multidimensional Integrated Stabilization Mission in Mali (MINUSMA).
