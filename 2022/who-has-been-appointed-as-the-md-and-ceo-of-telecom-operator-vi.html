Who has been appointed as the MD and CEO of telecom operator Vi?

(A) Anoop Kumar

(B) Arvind Kumar

(C) ** Ravindra Takkar

(D) Raj Kalpana Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ravinder Takkar, the managing director (MD) and chief executive officer (CEO) of telecom operator Vodafone Idea Limited (Vi), will replace Himanshu Kapania as the telco’s new chairman.

Concept note-2: -Shareholders of Vodafone Idea have approved elevation of company’s chief financial officer Akshaya Moondra as new chief executive officer, according to voting result of the annual general meeting released on Tuesday.

Concept note-3: -Average Annual Salary (Estimated) CEO salary in Vodafone Idea ranges between ₹ 3.2 Lakhs to ₹ 254.0 Lakhs per year. This is an estimate based on salaries received from employees of Vodafone Idea.

Concept note-4: -Mr. Kapania joined Idea, earlier known as Birla AT&T, in 1997. During his tenure with Idea Cellular Limited, he served as the Chief Operating Officer (2006-2011) and subsequently as Managing Director of Idea from March 2011 to September 2018.
