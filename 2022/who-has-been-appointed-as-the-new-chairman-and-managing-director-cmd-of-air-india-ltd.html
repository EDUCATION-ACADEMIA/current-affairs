Who has been appointed as the new chairman and managing director (CMD) of Air India Ltd?

(A) V.A.Patwardhan

(B) R S Sandhu

(C) Amrita Sharan

(D) ** Vikram Dev Dutt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vikram Dev Dutt Appointed As Chairman And Managing Director Of Air India.
