Who has been appointed as the new chief executive officer (CEO) of Adani Power Limited (APL)?

(A) ** Shersingh B Khyalia

(B) Praveer Sinha

(C) Kesava Menon Chandrasekhar

(D) Banmali Agrawala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shersingh B Khyalia is a Chartered Accountant and brings with him over 32 years of experience in managing complex business in the power industry, including generation, transmission and distribution.

Concept note-2: -Adani Power has appointed Shersingh Khyalia as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) with effect from 11 January 2022. Earlier, Khyalia has worked as Managing Director in Gujarat Power Corporation.

Concept note-3: -Mr. Karan Adani is the Chief Executive Officer of Adani Ports and SEZ Limited (APSEZ).

Concept note-4: -Adani Airport Holdings has again rejigged its top management, naming Ericsson veteran Arun Bansal its chief executive officer.
