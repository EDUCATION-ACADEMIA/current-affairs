Who has been appointed as the new Private Secretary to Prime Minister Narendra Modi?

(A) Kiran Deshmukh

(B) ** Vivek Kumar

(C) Jagdish Mukhi

(D) Ashok Bhandari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hardik Satishchandra Shah is an Indian civil servant as a member of Indian Administrative Service of Gujarat Cadre and is currently serving as the Private Secretary to the Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi.

Concept note-2: -The PMO is headed by the Principal Secretary, currently Pramod Kumar Misra. The PMO was originally called the Prime Minister’s Secretariat until 1977, when it was renamed during the Morarji Desai ministry.

Concept note-3: -Vivek Kumar (born 8 December 1994) is an Indian cricketer. He made his first-class debut for Bihar in the 2018–19 Ranji Trophy on 20 November 2018.

Concept note-4: -The Principal Secretary to PM is the head of the Prime Minister’s Office. He/she holds the rank and status of Cabinet Secretary to the Government of India.
