Who has been appointed as the new US Ambassador to India?

(A) ** Elizabeth Jones

(B) Patricia A. Lacina

(C) Kenneth Juster

(D) Atul Keshap

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Since October 24, 2022, A. Elizabeth Jones has been Chargée d’affaires ad interim. Ambassador Kenneth Juster , who served from 2017 to 2021 under President Donald Trump, was the last non-interim appointee.

Concept note-2: -Ambassador Taranjit Singh Sandhu is one of the most experienced Indian diplomats on US affairs, having served in the Indian Mission in Washington DC twice earlier.
