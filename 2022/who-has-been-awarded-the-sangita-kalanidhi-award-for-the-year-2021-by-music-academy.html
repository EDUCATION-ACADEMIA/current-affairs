Who has been awarded the Sangita Kalanidhi award for the year 2021 by Music Academy?

(A) ** Tiruvarur Bhakthavathsalam

(B) Vijayalakshmi

(C) Neyveli R Santanagopalan

(D) Lalgudi GJR Krishnan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Santhanagopalan has been selected for the award for 2020, mridhangam maestro Tiruvarur Baktavatsalam for 2021 and violinists Lalgudi GJR Krishnan and his sister GJR Vijayalakshmi for 2022. The Music Academy on Sunday announced Sangita Kalanidhi award winners for the years 2020, 2021 and 2022.

Concept note-2: -For the Sangita Kala Acharya awards, the Academy has chosen nagaswaram exponent Kivalur N G Ganesan (2020), vocalist, musicologist, and guru Dr Ritha Rajan (2021), and vainika and musicologist Dr R S Jayalakshmi (2022).

Concept note-3: -Who received the famous ‘Sangita Kalanithi’ award of the Music Academy, recently? Notes: The leading Carnatic vocalist S. Sowmya was selected for the ‘Sangita Kalanithi’ award instituted by the Music Academy and recently received the award.

Concept note-4: -This award was instituted in 1993 and is given to those who have contributed by bringing several disciples to the concert platform. Two senior musicians/preceptors are given this award each year.

Concept note-5: -Kanyakumari - first woman violinist to get Sangita Kalanidhi award.
