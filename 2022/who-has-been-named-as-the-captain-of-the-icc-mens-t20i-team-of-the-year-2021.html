Who has been named as the captain of the ICC Men’s T20I Team of the Year 2021?

(A) Virat Kohli

(B) Kane Williamson

(C) Joe Root

(D) ** Babar Azam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Babar Azam has been named captain of the ICC’s men’s T20I team of the year for 2021, with two other Pakistan players making the cut. Babar was the leading run scorer in the men’s T20 World Cup in 2021, with many disagreeing with the decision not to make him Player of the Tournament at that event.

Concept note-2: -ESPNcricinfo Awards 2021 Captain of the year - Kane Williamson.

Concept note-3: -The batting line-up is along expected lines with captain Rohit Sharma, KL Rahul, Virat Kohli, Suryakumar Yadav, Rishabh Pant and Dinesh Karthik all included.

Concept note-4: -Pakistan captain Babar Azam once again showed his prowess in the 50-over format, displaying once again why he has ruled the roost at the top of the MRF Tyres ICC Men’s ODI Player Rankings since July 2021.
