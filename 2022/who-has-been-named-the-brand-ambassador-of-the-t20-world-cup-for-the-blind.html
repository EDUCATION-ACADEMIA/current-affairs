Who has been named the brand ambassador of the T20 World Cup for the Blind?

(A) ** Yuvraj Singh

(B) Virender Sehwag

(C) MS Dhoni

(D) Dinesh Karthik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Yuvraj Singh named brand ambassador of T20 World Cup for the Blind. The Cricket Association for the Blind in India (CABI) on Friday announced former India cricketer Yuvraj Singh as their brand ambassador for the 3rd T20 World Cup for the Blind to be held in India in December.

Concept note-2: -Bengaluru, January 6th 2023: Indian Women’s cricket team captain Harmanpreet Kaur is the Brand Ambassador for the Women’s Blind Cricket.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Blind Cricket Team creates history yet again, winning the 3rd T20 World Cup Cricket for the Blind 2022 title for the third time!

Concept note-4: -Former cricketer Yuvraj Singh named brand ambassador of T20 World Cup for the Blind.

Concept note-5: -The third T20 World Cup Cricket Tournament for the Blind will be held from December 5 to 17, 2022 in India. The participating nations for World Cup 2022 are Australia, Bangladesh, Nepal, Pakistan, South Africa, Sri Lanka and the host, India.
