Who has been selected for the post of Chairman & Managing Director, RailTel Corporation of India Ltd in July 2022?

(A) Rajendra Prasad

(B) Gurdeep Singh

(C) ** Sanjai Kumar

(D) Debasish Nanda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Sanjai Kumar took over charge of Chairman & Managing Director wef 23.09. 2022 .

Concept note-2: -RailTel Corporation of India on Firday (23 September 2022), announced that its board has approved the appointment of Sanjai Kumar as chairman & managing director of the company. He is an officer of the Indian Railway Service of Signal Engineers (IRSSE1).

Concept note-3: -Sanjai Kumar has taken charge as the Chairman & Managing Director of RailTel. Before taking over this post, he was shouldering the responsibilities of Director (Network Planning & Marketing/NPM) with additional charge of Director (Project, Operations & Maintenance/ POM) in RailTel.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Aruna Singh.
