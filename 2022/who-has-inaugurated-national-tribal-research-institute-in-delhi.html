Who has inaugurated National Tribal Research Institute in Delhi?

(A) Kiren Rijiju

(B) Nitin Gadkari

(C) Giriraj Singh

(D) ** Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Amit Shah Minister inaugurates the National Tribal Research Institute in New Delhi today. As a part of the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav celebrations by the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, the Minister of Home Affairs and Cooperation, Shri Amit Shah, inaugurated the National Tribal Research Institute today in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Tribal Research and Cultural Institute was established under Tribal Welfare Department in the year 1970 as per the decision of Government of India.

Concept note-3: -Tribal Research Institute (TRI) is the research body of the Ministry of Tribal affairs at state level .

Concept note-4: -Indian Institute of Public Administration (IIPA) has been entrusted with the task to strengthen training and active research in various aspects of tribal welfare, by Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA), Government of India.
