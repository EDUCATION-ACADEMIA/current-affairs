Who has inaugurated the silver jubilee edition of the Bengaluru Tech Summit?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) ** Narendra Modi

(C) Rajnath Singh

(D) Anurag Thakur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 25th edition of Asia’s largest technology event, the Bengaluru Tech Summit 2022 (BTS 2022), is all set to start on Wednesday. Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the event virtually.

Concept note-2: -About the Global Technology Summit 2022 It was co-hosted by the Ministry of External Affairs and Carnegie India. The theme for the 2022 edition of GTS is “Geopolitics of Technology”.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi will address the inaugural event on 16 November. The flagship event is being organised by the Department of Electronics, IT, Bt, S&T, Government of Karnataka along with Software Technology Parks of India (STPI).

Concept note-4: -As India’s biggest technology summit, the event will deliver another fulfilling, enriching, and inspiring event with participants from IT, bio 1tech, Deeptech and other future unravelling technologies.
