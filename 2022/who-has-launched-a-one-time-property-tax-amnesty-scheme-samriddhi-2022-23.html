Who has launched a one-time property tax amnesty scheme “SAMRIDDHI 2022-23”?

(A) ** Vinai Kumar Saxena

(B) Arvind Kejriwal

(C) Jitendra Singh

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Delhi Lieutenant Governor Vinai Kumar Saxena on October 25 launched a one-time property tax amnesty scheme “SAMRIDDHI 2022-23", saying it will provide a major relief to lakhs of residential and commercial property owners in the city.

Concept note-2: -LG of Delhi launches MCD property tax one-time amnesty scheme - Samriddhi. The scheme commenced on October 26, 2022, and will end on March 31, 2023.
