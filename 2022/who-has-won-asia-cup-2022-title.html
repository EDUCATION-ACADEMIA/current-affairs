Who has won Asia Cup 2022 title?

(A) ** Sri Lanka

(B) Bangladesh

(C) Pakistan

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The winner of the Asia Cup 2022 was Sri Lanka in the tournament held in UAE. This tournament is held biennially (every two years). After defeating Pakistan by 23 runs in the final match, Sri Lanka was added to the prestigious Asia Cup Winners List.

Concept note-2: -IND vs SL Women’s Asia Cup 2022 Final Match Highlights: India beat Sri Lanka by eight wickets to win their seventh Asia Cup title.

Concept note-3: -Asia Cup Winners List (1984 – 2022) On 11 September 2022, Sri Lanka team won the Asia Cup 2022 in Dubai by defeating Pakistan by 23 runs. Our Asia Cup Winners List includes the names of the winning team and runner up team of the tournament starting from the 1984.

Concept note-4: -Defending champion India heads into the 2022 Asia Cup as the most successful team, having won the title seven times, including the last time the tournament was played in the T20 format in 2016.

Concept note-5: -Talking about Asia Cup’s history, the very first edition of the tournament was played in 1984 in UAE with India and Sri Lanka facing each other in the final match. The Men in Blue were crowned as the first Asia Cup winners.
