Who has won the 57th Jnanpith Award 2022?

(A) Shankha Ghosh

(B) Bhalchandra Nemade

(C) Kedarnath Singh

(D) ** Damodar Mauzo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Damodar Mauzo. Key Points Damodar Mauzo: He is a Goa-based poet and recipient of 57th Jnapith Award. .

Concept note-2: -Assamese poet Nilmani Phookan and Konkani writer Damodar Mauzo has been conferred with the 56th and 57th Jnanpith Award respectively.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Nilamani Phookan. On 11 April 2022, Eminent Assamese poet Nilamani Phookan has conferred the 56th Jnanpith Award for the year 2021. The ceremony was held for the first time in Assam.

Concept note-4: -Key Facts Assamese Poet Nilmani Phookan Jr. and Konkani Novelist Damodar Mauzo have emerged as the winners of 56th and 57th Award respectively. They were selected for their contribution to Indian literature.
