Who has won the Belgian Formula 1 Grand Prix 2022?

(A) ** Max Verstappen

(B) Sebastian Vettel

(C) Sergio Perez

(D) Lewis Hamilton

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Max Verstappen made light work of his grid penalties to win the 2022 Belgian Grand Prix from P14 on the grid in a Red Bull one-two backed up by Sergio Perez , while pole-sitter Carlos Sainz finished P3 for Ferrari.

Concept note-2: -Once the race resumed Verstappen easily worked his way up the field, taking the lead by lap 12, relinquishing it only briefly after his first pit stop. Verstappen took the win, his ninth of the season, ahead of his teammate Pérez. Sainz finished third after being unable to match the pace of the Red Bulls.

Concept note-3: -Max Verstappen wins Belgian Grand Prix as Hamilton crashes out.

Concept note-4: -Formula-1 Racing: Red Bull’s Max Verstappen wins Abu Dhabi F1 Grand Prix.
