(A) Poly Medicure Ltd

(B) ** Meril Lifesciences Pvt Ltd

(C) Glenmark Pharmaceuticals Ltd

(D) Zydus Lifesciences Ltd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1:- Johnson & Johnson: Johnson & Johnson, located in the United States, is India’s top medical equipment maker. As a result, they have a stronghold on the Indian medical equipment market. Johnson & Johnson is the world’s largest and most diverse healthcare firm.

Concept note-2: -1. Medtronic $31.7bn +8% Medtronic once again tops the list as the largest medical device company in the world. With a workforce of over 90,000, operating in 150 countries, Medtronic is at the forefront of medical technology.
