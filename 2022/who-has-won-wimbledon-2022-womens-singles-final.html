Who has won Wimbledon 2022 Women’s Singles Final?

(A) Tatjana Maria

(B) ** Elena Rybakina

(C) Ons Jabeur

(D) Simona Halep

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Elena Rybakina defeated Ons Jabeur in the final, 3–6, 6–2, 6–2 to win the ladies’ singles tennis title at the 2022 Wimbledon Championships. It was her first major singles title.

Concept note-2: -Wimbledon, also known as The Championships, is one of the four biggest tennis tournaments, the Grand Slam tournaments. The winner of the 2022 edition was Novak Djokovic.

Concept note-3: -Three-time defending champion Novak Djokovic defeated Nick Kyrgios in the final, 4–6, 6–3, 6–4, 7–6 to win the gentlemen’s singles tennis title at the 2022 Wimbledon Championships.

Concept note-4: -Nearly two hours of big swinging and plenty of sprinting later, she had won the championship at the All England Club with a 3-6, 6-2, 6-2 victory over Ons Jabeur - the first singles trophy at a major tournament for her adopted country of Kazakhstan.
