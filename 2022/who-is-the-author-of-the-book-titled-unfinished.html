Who is the author of the book titled-Unfinished?

(A) Salman Rushdie

(B) ** Priyanka Chopra

(C) Anuja Chauhan

(D) Amitav Ghosh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Unfinished is a 2021 memoir by Indian actress Priyanka Chopra.

Concept note-2: -The memoir titled ‘Unfinished’ is written by Bollywood actress Priyanka Chopra Jonas. With the release of her first novel, “Unfinished: A Memoir,” which she described as “honest, raw and vulnerable,” actor-producer Priyanka Chopra Jonas officially became an author.

Concept note-3: -Her journey is definitely unfinished. Great read! This book tells the interesting life of Chopra from childhood to now. It describes her background, family and growth into the person she is now.
