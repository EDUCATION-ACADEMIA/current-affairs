Who is the richest Indian-American on the list of America’s richest self-made women in the 8th annual Forbes?

(A) Anjali Sud

(B) Leena Nair

(C) Neha Narkhede

(D) ** Jayshree Ulla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jayshree Ullal is the richest Indian-American on the list, thanks in part to the stock she owns in Arista, the computer networking firm of which she has been president and CEO since 2008. The 61-year-old owns 5% of Arista’s stock, some of which has been set aside for her two children, niece and nephew.

Concept note-2: -Indian-Americans Vinod Khosla, Romesh Wadhwani and Rakesh Gangwal made it to the Forbes 2022 list of 400 wealthiest Americans with Zscaler CEO Jay Chaudhry leading the pack with a net worth of $8.2 billion.

Concept note-3: -Forbes India has released the India Rich List for this year. While Adani Group’s chairman Gautam Adani has topped Forbes India’s 100 richest list in 2022, Jindal Group Chairperson, Savitri Jindal was listed as the richest woman in India in the Forbes Billionaire’s list 2022.

Concept note-4: -Nykaa Founder Falguni Nayar Now India’s Richest Self-Made Woman.
