Who took charge as the new director general (DG) of ITBP?

(A) Avinash Singh

(B) ** Anish Dayal Singh

(C) Bayeish Sharma

(D) Ravi Dubey

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Senior IPS officer Anish Dayal Singh on Monday took charge as the new director general (DG) of the Indo-Tibetan Border Police that guards the 3,488-km Line of Actual Control with China.

Concept note-2: -Synopsis. The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) headed by the prime minister had on October 1 issued the order for the appointment of Singh who has service till December 2024.

Concept note-3: -Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) in a memorandum issued on Monday directed Singh to take charge of SSB chief as his batch mate S. L. Thaosen was transferred from the post to hold the responsibility of Director General of Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF).

Concept note-4: -Anish Dayal Singh, IPS took over as the Director General of Sashastra Seema Bal on 02 Oct, 2022.
