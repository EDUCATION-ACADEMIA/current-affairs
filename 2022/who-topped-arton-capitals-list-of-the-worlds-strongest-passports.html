Who topped ‘Arton Capital’s list of the world’s strongest passports?

(A) Cuba

(B) Togo

(C) Canada

(D) ** UAE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: According to Henley Passport Index 2022, Japan is the best passport to have, while the Arton Capital Passport Index names the UAE as the most powerful passport.

Concept note-2: -At the top of Arton’s power ranking, holders of a United Arab Emirates passport can travel visa-free or obtain visas on arrival in 181 countries and territories. U.S. passport holders rank a bit lower, with access to 173 countries.

Concept note-3: -According to Arton Capital’s “Passport Index 2022,” India is placed 66(as of 16th December 2022) in the world’s strongest passport list, while the United Arab Emirates (UAE) is ranked 1st in the passport ranking for 2022.

Concept note-4: -Japan has topped the list of the world’s most powerful passports for the fifth year in a row. Japanese citizens enjoy visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to 193 countries – or 85% of the world – making the Japanese passport the most powerful in the world.
