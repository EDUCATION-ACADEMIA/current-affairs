Who was elected as the first chairman of the Asian Palm Oil Alliance?

(A) Neha Salil

(B) Sahil Singh

(C) ** Atul Chaturvedi

(D) Manjeet Kataria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Atul Chaturvedi elected as first chairman of Asian Palm Oil Alliance - The Economic Times.

Concept note-2: -Shatadru Chattopadhayay sur LinkedIn : Atul Chaturvedi elected as first chairman of Asian Palm Oil Alliance | 11 commentaires.

Concept note-3: -“The APOA secretariat would be initially managed by The Solvent Extractors’ Association (SEA) of India. In the first General Body Meeting of the APOA, Atul Chaturvedi, Director of Adani Wilmar Ltd and the President of SEA, was elected as the first Chairman of the APOA,” said APOA in a release.

Concept note-4: -India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh and Nepal formed APOA; Atul Chaturvedi elected as its Chairman. On September 21, 2022, the 25th Globoil Summit 2022, organized by Tefla’s, was held in Agra, Uttar Pradesh (UP) which saw the participation of 1,500 attendees from 50 countries.

Concept note-5: -The apex edible oil industry associations from five major palm oil importing countries of Asia – India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh and Nepal – have come together to form the Asian Palm Oil Alliance (APOA).
