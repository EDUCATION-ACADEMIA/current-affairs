With whom did the Ministry of Railways sign an agreement to upgrade railway telecommunications?

(A) ** Center for Development of Telematics

(B) Defence Research and Development Organisation

(C) Small Industries Development Bank of India

(D) Indian Space Research Institute

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The collaboration between C-DOT and the Ministry of Railways will lower the total cost of ownership of indigenous telecom equipment and services for train operations, public safety, and security applications, boosting the Government of India’s Make in India initiative and bridging the digital divide in the country.

Concept note-2: -Centre for Development of Telematics (C-DOT) is the premier R&D centre of the Department of Telecommunications, Ministry of Communications, Government of India.

Concept note-3: -India and Belgium signed Agreement for the Modernisation of Indian Railways.

Concept note-4: -The MoU between the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, India and the Federal Ministry of Transport, Innovation and Technology, Austria was approved by the Cabinet.

Concept note-5: -With this MoU, CDoT and Ministry of Railways will work together for modernization of Telecommunication in Indian Railways for Public Safety and Security Services. The MoU signing ceremony was attended by Shri Rajkumar Upadhyay, Executive Director of C-DOT and Smt.
