Women’s Indian Premier League (IPL) is set to take place in which year?

(A) ** 2023

(B) 2022

(C) 2026

(D) 2024

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After the huge success of 15 seasons of all-male seasons of IPL, BCCI is all set to roll out Women’s Indian Premier League (WIPL) in 2023 with top talents in India and overseas competing in the tournament . The WIPL 2023 will begin in the first week of March 2023. BCCI has started setting the rules of the league.

Concept note-2: -The Women’s Premier League 2023 is the upcoming inaugural season of the Women’s Primer Leauge, a women’s franchise T20 cricket league founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). The tournament will featured 5 teams and likely to held in March 2023, entirely in Mumbai of Maharashtra.

Concept note-3: -The inaugural edition of the Women’s Premier League is set to be played in Mumbai from 4th – 26th March, 2023. A total of 22 matches will be played with the Brabourne Stadium & D.Y. Patil Stadium playing hosts to the marquee tournament.

Concept note-4: -The new upcoming 16th season of Tata IPL will be played at Mumbai’s Wankhede Stadium on 1 April 2023.

Concept note-5: -BCCI governing council is premiering the Women’s IPL in March 2023 in India Just after the Women’s cricket world cup in 2023. The format of the games and rules will be the same as the Men’s IPL. The proposed plan of BCCI for WIPL has 5 teams and a complete of 22 matches in the tournament.
