World Gratitude Day is being observed on which date?

(A) September 18

(B) September 19

(C) September 20

(D) ** September 21

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Gratitude Day is celebrated on September 21 annually. This day aims to remind us of the importance of expressing gestures of thanks and appreciation.

Concept note-2: -World Gratitude Day is celebrated on September 21 annually. This day aims to remind us of the importance of expressing gestures of thanks and appreciation.

Concept note-3: -The World Gratitude Day 2022 theme remains what it is every year – to tap into the innate human nature of self-reflection and self-introspection and bring internal thankfulness to the surface to express gratitude overtly.

Concept note-4: -How you celebrate the day is limited only by your imagination. Some people write thank you notes to those they appreciate. Some make a point of having “gratitude dinners” with family members. And others start a gratitude journal in which they record what they’re grateful for.

Concept note-5: -World Gratitude Day is observed on September 21 every year. This day gives all individuals, societies, organizations, and countries a chance to dedicatedly celebrate the occasion of gratitude and give thanks to each other.
