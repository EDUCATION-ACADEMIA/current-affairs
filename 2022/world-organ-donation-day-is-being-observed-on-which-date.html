World Organ Donation Day is being observed on which date?

(A) August 11

(B) August 12

(C) ** August 13

(D) August 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Organ Donation Day is observed every year on August 13 across the globe. The purpose of observing this day is to spread awareness about the importance of organ donation and to encourage people to do the same.

Concept note-2: -World Organ Donation Day is celebrated on 13 August every year to raise awareness about the significance of organ donation.

Concept note-3: -World Organ Donation Day 2022: Theme This year’s theme for World Organ Donation Day 2022 is “let’s pledge to donate organs and save lives”. The Day is celebrated to raise awareness of the need for donating organs and to thank donors for their life-saving contributions.

Concept note-4: -Organ Donation Day was celebrated first in 1954. Ronald Lee Herrick donated his kidney to his twin brother in the US to have their lives.

Concept note-5: -India’s first liver transplant happened on November 6, 1998 by Dr. A.S. Soin and Dr. Rajashekar on 42-year-old Bharat Bhushan at Delhi’s Apollo Hospital. Bhushan was terminally ill and a corporate executive was declared brain-dead, whose liver was donated.
