year-old girl Rhythm Mamania from which state has climbed Everest Base Camp?

(A) Karnataka

(B) ** Maharashtra

(C) Kerala

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Everest Base Camp, located at an altitude of 5,364 metres, recently bore witness to one of the youngest climbers to conquer its terrain. Rhythm Mamania, from Mumbai, took 11 days to complete her expedition to the tumultuous destination along with her parents.

Concept note-2: -10-year-old girl Rhythm Mamania from Maharashtra summits Everest base camp. A little 10-year-old champion skater from Worli, Rhythm Mamania, has become one of the youngest Indian mountaineers to summit the Everest Base Camp.

Concept note-3: -Three-year-old Heyansh Kumar of Babra Bakipur village in Gurugram district has become the youngest person to reach the Mount Everest base camp, which is situated at 5,364m above sea level. It may be noted that Heyansh was 3 years, 7 months and 27 days old when he achieved the milestone.

Concept note-4: -Bachendri Pal, (born May 24, 1954, Nakuri, India), Indian mountaineer who in 1984 became the first Indian woman to reach the summit of Mount Everest.

Concept note-5: -Anshu Jamsenpa is the first woman to climb Mount Everest twice in five days. Santosh Yadav was the first woman to summit twice in 1992 and 1993.
