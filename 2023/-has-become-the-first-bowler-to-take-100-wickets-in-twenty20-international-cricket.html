____ has become the first bowler to take 100 wickets in Twenty20 International cricket.

(A) Adil Rashid

(B) ** Lasith Malinga

(C) Jasprit Bumrah

(D) Trent Bouit

(E) Kagiso Rabada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Records. First bowler to take 100 T20I wickets. First bowler to take 100 wickets in across all three formats of international cricket (Tests, ODIs and T20Is). The first and, to date, only bowler to take three hat-tricks in One-Day International cricket.

Concept note-2: -Deepti Sharma has become the first Indian bowler to complete 100 wickets in T20Is. The 25-year-old reached the landmark in the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup match against West Indies. She took 3 wickets and conceded just 15 runs in four overs, with Women in Blue restricting the rivals to 118/6 in 20 overs.
