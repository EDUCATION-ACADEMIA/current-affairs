____ Which state was awarded the first place for excellence in implementation of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojna (PMAY) Mission?

(A) Gujarat

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: The Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi announced the annual awards for excellence in implementation of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) Mission. In the best performing states category, Uttar Pradesh bagged the first place, followed by Madhya Pradesh and Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Madhya Pradesh ranks first in the whole country in the implementation of PM Self Fund Scheme. Loans have been distributed to 6 lakh 87 thousand 192 street vendors under the scheme.

Concept note-3: -Narendra Modi, the Prime Minister of India, launched the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY), on 1 June 2015. PMAY Scheme, an initiative of the Indian government, aims at providing affordable housing solutions to the urban poor.

Concept note-4: -Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U), a flagship Mission of Government of India being implemented by Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), was launched on 25th June 2015.
