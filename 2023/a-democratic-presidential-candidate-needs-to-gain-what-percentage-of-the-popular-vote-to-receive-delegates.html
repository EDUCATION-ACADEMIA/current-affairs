A democratic presidential candidate needs to gain what percentage of the popular vote to receive delegates?

(A) 5%

(B) 10%

(C) ** 15%

(D) 20%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A total of 538 electors form the Electoral College. Each elector casts one vote following the general election. The candidate who gets 270 votes or more wins. The newly elected President and Vice President are then inaugurated on January 20th.

Concept note-2: -Today, in 48 states, individuals participate in primaries or caucuses to elect delegates who support their presidential candidate of choice. At national party conventions, the presidential contender with the most state delegate votes wins the party nomination.
