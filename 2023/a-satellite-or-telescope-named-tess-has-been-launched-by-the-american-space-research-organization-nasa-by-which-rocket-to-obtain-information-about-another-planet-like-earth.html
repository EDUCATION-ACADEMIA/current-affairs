A satellite (or telescope) named TESS has been launched by the American space research organization NASA by which rocket to obtain information about another planet like Earth?

(A) Space X-11

(B) ** Falcon-9

(C) Kepler-3

(D) Falcon-5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -TESS launched April 18, 2018 aboard a SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket.

Concept note-2: -Newly discovered Earth-size planet TOI 700 e orbits within the habitable zone of its star in this illustration. Its Earth-size sibling, TOI 700 d, can be seen in the distance.

Concept note-3: -It found 2, 100 planetary candidates and 66 confirmed exoplanets.

Concept note-4: -Like Kepler, TESS finds planets by staring at stars and looking for a dip in brightness as a planet passes in front, blocking some of the star’s light in a so-called transit.
