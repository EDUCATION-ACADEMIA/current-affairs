A year and a half after traveling, they made it to their mom in Washington, DC in this month and year?

(A) December; 2018

(B) December; 2011

(C) ** December; 2012

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Today, not until the age of 45 have 8 in 10 people married. In 1960, 87 percent of children lived in a household with two married parents. Today, that number is 62 percent. Today 26 percent of children live with one parent, 15 percent live with parents in a remarriage and 7 percent live with unmarried parents.

Concept note-2: -Ultimately, honor what’s best for you and your family-and note that these circumstances are highly individual and may change over time. Pew Research Center. Stay at home moms and dads account for about 1-in-5 U.S. parents.

Concept note-3: -“By divine design, fathers are to preside over their families in love and righteousness and are responsible to provide the necessities of life and protection for their families. Mothers are primarily responsible for the nurture of their children.

Concept note-4: -Today’s father is no longer always the traditional married breadwinner and disciplinarian in the family. He can be single or married; externally employed or stay-at home; gay or straight; an adoptive or step-parent; and a more than capable caregiver to children facing physical or psychological challenges.
