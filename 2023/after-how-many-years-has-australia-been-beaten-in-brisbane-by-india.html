After how many years has Australia been beaten in Brisbane by India?

(A) 45 years

(B) 30 years

(C) ** 32 years

(D) 15 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The importance of the series is also fuelled by Australia’s desire to beat India in a Test match series in India, which, until their victory in the 2004–05 series, they had not done so since 1969–70.

Concept note-2: -Australia has a formidable test match record at the ground. In the 55 matches played at the ground, Australia has won 33, drawn 13, tied 1 and lost 9. The last loss came on 19 January 2021 against India in the 4th and final test of 2020-21 Border-Gavaskar trophy.

Concept note-3: -India and Australia have faced each other in 143 ODIs, with the latter dominating the rubber with 80 wins. Both sides first squared off in a One-day international at the MCG in December 1980, which India won by 66 runs.
