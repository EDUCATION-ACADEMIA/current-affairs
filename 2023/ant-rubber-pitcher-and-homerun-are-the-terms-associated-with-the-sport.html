Ant rubber, Pitcher, and Homerun are the terms associated with the sport?

(A) Polo

(B) Billiards

(C) Golf

(D) ** Baseball

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bunting, Diamond, and Ant rubber are the terms associated with baseball.

Concept note-2: -In baseball, the pitcher is the player who throws ("pitches") the baseball from the pitcher’s mound toward the catcher to begin each play, with the goal of retiring a batter, who attempts to either make contact with the pitched ball or draw a walk.

Concept note-3: -Ant rubber and pitcher are associated with baseball game The Baseball game start when throwing ball by one team player gets hit by opposite team.

Concept note-4: -Some other terms associated with Boxing are Hook, Punch, Knock-out, Uppercut, Kidney Punch.
