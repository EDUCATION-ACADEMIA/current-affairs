Arogya Sri Scheme, which is an initiative to provide free medical treatment to the poor, is the flagship scheme of which state?

(A) Telengana

(B) Kerala

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) ** Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. YSR Aarogyasri (previously known as Aarogyasri) is an upgradation of Andhra Pradesh’s government healthcare scheme Aarogyasri which was launched by the late chief minister Y. S. Rajasekhara Reddy in 2007.

Concept note-2: -Eligibility For YSR Aarogyasri Scheme: Families that are native to Andhra Pradesh and Telangana are allowed to enrol under the scheme. The annual income of a family should be below Rs. 5 Lakh. They should also be white ration cardholders.

Concept note-3: -Aarogyasri means Rajiv Aarogyasri Scheme (RAS) of the Govt. of the State of Telangana, under the Aarogyasri Health Care Trust.
