As of 3/4/2020 Joe Biden had won all of the following states in the caucuses and primaries EXCEPT ____

(A) Alabama

(B) Texas

(C) Oklahoma

(D) ** Vermont

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vermont was won by Biden with 66.1% of the vote, making this his best performance in a state (if D.C. is not counted). Prior to the election, all 14 news organizations projected Vermont as a strongly Democratic state, or a safe blue state. The Green Mountain State gave Biden a victory margin of 35.4% over Trump.

Concept note-2: -Senator Bernie Sanders won the primary with 25.6% of the vote, edging out former mayor Pete Buttigieg after his narrow win in Iowa, who came in second place with 24.3% of the vote.

Concept note-3: -With the addition of Michael Bloomberg on November 24, 2019, the number of major Democratic presidential candidates in the 2020 Democratic primaries totaled 29.
