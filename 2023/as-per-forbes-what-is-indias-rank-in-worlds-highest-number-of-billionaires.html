As per Forbes, what is India’s rank in world’s highest number of billionaires?

(A) 4th

(B) 1st

(C) 2nd

(D) ** 3rd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of Jan 2023, India has 166 billionaires which put the country third in the world, after the United States and China. Mukesh Ambani the chairman and largest shareholder of Reliance Industries, has been the richest Indian for 13 consecutive years.

Concept note-2: -According to the recently released Wealth Report 2022 by Knight Frank India has the third highest number of billionaire population( 145 billionaires) globally in 2021 after the US (748 billionaires), and China ( 554 billionaires).

Concept note-3: -Amazon founder Jeff Bezos on Tuesday replaced Gautam Adani becoming the World’s third richest man. Adani Group founder with a total net worth of USD 120 billion ranked fourth place on the Bloomberg Billionaires Index as his net worth fell by USD 872 million in the last 24 hours.
