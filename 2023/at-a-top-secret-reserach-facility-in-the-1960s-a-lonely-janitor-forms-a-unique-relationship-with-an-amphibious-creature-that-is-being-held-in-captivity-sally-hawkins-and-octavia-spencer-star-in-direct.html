At a top secret reserach facility in the 1960’s, a lonely janitor forms a unique relationship with an amphibious creature that is being held in captivity. Sally Hawkins and Octavia Spencer star in director Guillermo del Toro’s fable. What is the title of the film?

(A) The Florida project

(B) The Darkest Hour

(C) ** The Shape of Water

(D) Red Sparrow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At a top secret research facility in the 1960s, a lonely janitor forms a unique relationship with an amphibious creature that is being held in captivity.

Concept note-2: -’The Shape of Water’, directed by Guillermo del Toro, stars Sally Hawkins as a mute cleaner at a government facility who encounters a strange creature brought in by a colonel performed by Michael Shannon. This is easily del Toro’s best-looking film on a cinematography perspective.

Concept note-3: -Movie Info Elisa is a mute, isolated woman who works as a cleaning lady in a hidden, high-security government laboratory in 1962 Baltimore. Her life changes forever when she discovers the lab’s classified secret–a mysterious, scaled creature from South America that lives in a water tank.

Concept note-4: -The Incredible Mr. Limpet is a 1964 American live-action/animated comedy film produced by Warner Bros. and based on the 1942 novel Mr. Limpet by Theodore Pratt. It is about a man named Henry Limpet who turns into a talking fish and helps the U.S. Navy locate and destroy Nazi submarines.

Concept note-5: -The theory is that Elisa either is part fish-creature like the amphibian man, or perhaps at one point was fully a fish creature herself, and somehow transformed.
