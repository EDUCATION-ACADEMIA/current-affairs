Berlin is the Capital of ____

(A) China

(B) Russia

(C) United kingdom

(D) ** Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Berlin, the capital city of Germany, is renowned for its exceptional range of landmarks, vibrant cultural scene and way of life that’s somehow all go yet relaxed.

Concept note-2: -Although Berlin is officially the capital of the Federal Republic of Germany, 8, 000 out of the 18, 000 total officials employed at the federal bureaucracy still work in Bonn, about 600 km (370 mi) away from Berlin.

Concept note-3: -Berlin is the capital and chief urban center of Germany. Berlin was the capital of Prussia and then, from 1871, of a unified Germany. Though partitioned into East and West Berlin after World War II, the reunification of East and West Germany led to Berlin’s reinstatement as the all-German capital in 1990.

Concept note-4: -It would be hard to find a more charming town than Quedlinburg! The narrow, cobblestone streets are lined with over 1300 half-timbered houses.
