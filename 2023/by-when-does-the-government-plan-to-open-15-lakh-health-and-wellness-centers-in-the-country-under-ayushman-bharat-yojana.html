By when does the government plan to open 1.5 lakh health and wellness centers in the country under Ayushman Bharat Yojana?

(A) ** 2022

(B) 2021

(C) 2019

(D) 2025

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under the Ayushman Bharat, the government had set a target of transforming existing 150, 000 lakh Sub-Health Centres (SHCs) and rural and urban Primary Health Centres (PHCs) across the country as AB-HWCs by December 2022 to bring health care closer to the community.

Concept note-2: -In February 2018, the Government of India’s announced the creation of 1, 50, 000 Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) by transforming existing Sub Centres and Primary Health Centres as the base pillar of Ayushman Bharat.

Concept note-3: -The nationwide rollout of this pilot project was announced by Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri. Narendra Modi on 27th September 2021 with the name “Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission” (ABDM).

Concept note-4: -Ayushman Bharat –Pradhan Mantri Jan AarogyaYojana (AB-PMJAY) to be launched by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi in Ranchi, Jharkahnd on September 23, 2018.

Concept note-5: -Caste certificate. PAN and Aadhaar Card as age and identity proof. Income certificate (maximum annual income up to Rs. 5 lakhs)
