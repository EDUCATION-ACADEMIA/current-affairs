Car companies like Mercedes Benz, BMW and Volkwagen are all German

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Most Popular German Car Companies. These are the most popular car manufacturers from Germany, that include BMW, Volkswagen, Mercedes-Benz, Audi and Porsche. As you can see, most of these manufacturers are luxury car makes, and have greatly increased the standard of automobiles worldwide.

Concept note-2: -Germany is often considered the birthplace of the automobile. Home to many car manufacturing companies, Germany is also the birthplace for Porsche, Mercedes Benz and BMW. All three of these luxury brands are associated with ultra-high-performance vehicles.

Concept note-3: -But many of the cars and crossover manufactured by Teutonic firms aren’t actually built in the Fatherland. German companies have factories all around the world, from emerging markets like China and India to Eastern Europe, South Africa and even the good old U.S. of A.

Concept note-4: -Bayerische Motoren Werke has always been based in the German state of Bavaria, but if you’re wondering what country makes BMW, you should know that your favorite BMW cars and Sport Activity Vehicles® are manufactured in five different countries, all around the globe.

Concept note-5: -Mercedes-Benz was originally founded in Germany but has since built facilities all over the world! Read on to learn more about where the luxury brand originated and where Mercedes-Benz vehicles are made.
