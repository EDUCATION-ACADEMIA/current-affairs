Chandrayaan-3 to be launched during the third quarter of 2022.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Chandrayaan-3 mission will tentatively be launched between June and July 2023. The mission will only be carrying a Lunar lander and rover, unlike its predecessor which carried an orbiter, lander and rover.

Concept note-2: -Chandrayaan-3 is a follow-on mission to Chandrayaan-2 to demonstrate end-to-end capability in safe landing and roving on the lunar surface. It consists of Lander and Rover configuration. It will be launched by GSLV MkIII from SDSC, Sriharikota.

Concept note-3: -ISRO to launch Chandrayaan-3, its third mission to moon, in June 2023 | Mint.

Concept note-4: -The engineering is significantly different.” The Chandrayaan-3 mission is expected to be launched in August 2023, S. Somnath, Chairman, Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), said here on the sidelines of a conference.
