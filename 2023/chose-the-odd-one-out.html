Chose the odd one out?

(A) 4

(B) 9

(C) 16

(D) 25

(E) ** 39

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -a person or thing that is different from or kept apart from others that form a group or set: Guess which number of the following sequence is the odd one out. She was always the odd one out at school-she didn’t have many friends.

Concept note-2: -To tell whether a number is even or odd, look at the number in the ones place. That single number will tell you whether the entire number is odd or even. An even number ends in 0, 2, 4, 6, or 8. An odd number ends in 1, 3, 5, 7, or 9.

Concept note-3: -In the given question The alphabets A B C D are given. Among them we can say that B, C, D are consonants and A is a Vowel. so the odd one is letter A.
