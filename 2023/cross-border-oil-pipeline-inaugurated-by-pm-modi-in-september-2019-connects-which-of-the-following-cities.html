Cross border oil Pipeline inaugurated by P.M. Modi in September 2019 connects which of the following cities?

(A) ** Motihari and Amlekhganj

(B) Darbhanga and Amlekhganj

(C) Motihari and Kathmandu

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Correct Answer is Mothihari and Amlekhganj. Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Nepal PM KP Sharma Oli inaugurated South Asia’s first cross-border petroleum products pipeline from Motihari in India to Amlekhgunj in Nepal.

Concept note-2: -This pipeline transports petroleum from Indian Oil Corporation (IOC) to Nepal Oil Corporation (NOC). It is also the first ever trans-national pipeline in the Indian subcontinent. It has an annual capacity of two million metric tonn.

Concept note-3: -The Motihari-Amlekhgunj oil pipeline, the first cross-border petroleum products pipeline in South Asia, was inaugurated by Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi and his Nepalese counterpart K.P.
