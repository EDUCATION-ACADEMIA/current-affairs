Deepak is the brother of Ravi. Rekha is the sister of Atul. Ravi is the son of Rekha. Then, how is Deepak related to Rekha?

(A) ** Son

(B) Brother

(C) Nephew

(D) None of the options.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Deepak is the son of Ravi, who is the son of Rekha. Thus, Deepak is the son of Rekha.

Concept note-2: -Ravi is son of Rekha. We can say Deepak is related to Rekha as the son. Hence, the correct answer is “Son".

Concept note-3: -Solution : Deepak is the brother of Ravi, who is the son of Reena. Thus Deepak is the son of Reena.

Concept note-4: -Anil is brother of grandson of Sunil ∴ Anil is grandson of Sunil.
