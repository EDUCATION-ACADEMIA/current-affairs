(A) Table tennis

(B) ** Archery

(C) High Jump

(D) Weightlifting

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Deepika Kumari India won 3 gold medals in the Archery’s World Cup Stage 3 tournament in Paris 2021.

Concept note-2: -Archery World Cup, Stage 3: Abhishek Verma, Jyothi Surekha Vennam Win Compound Mixed Team Gold For India.

Concept note-3: -The Indians managed just one 10 in the second end as the French duo reduced the deficit to one-point. The third was a tie, while in the deciding fourth end, Abhishek and Jyothi held their nerves to edge out their opponents by two points to annexe the title.

Concept note-4: -Jayanta Talukdar He earned bronze medals in the Asian Games in 2006 and 2010. He was the World’s No. 1 player in 2009. He became India’s first gold medalist in archery at the 2006 World Cup in Merida.

Concept note-5: -Jayanta Talukdar He then went on to win bronze medals in the 2006 and 2010 Asian Games. He held the World Number one ranking in 2009. He is the first Indian archer to be a gold medallist in the 2006 World Cup in Merida. He debuted in 2003 and won his first medal at the World Championships in 2005.
