Estonia has made its citizenship rules in such a way that people belonging to Russian minority find it difficult to get ____

(A) The economic rights

(B) ** The right to vote

(C) Right to women’s vote

(D) All

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Estonia has made its citizenship rules in such a way that people belonging to Russian minority find it difficult to get the right to vote. Explanation: The feature that is missing here is “one man one vote"

Concept note-2: -Around 25% of the population in Estonia are Russians and the citizenship rules for them are framed in such a way that they do not get their right to vote easily. These Russians are the minority in Estonia.

Concept note-3: -Under the law, residents without citizenship may not elect the Riigikogu (the national parliament) nor the European Parliament, but are eligible to vote in the municipal elections.

Concept note-4: -Estonia do the people belonging to the Russian minority find it difficult to get the right to vote. Estonia has made its rules for citizenship in a manner that people who belong to the Russian minority community find it hard to get the basic political right to vote. Estonia is a country in Northern Europe.
