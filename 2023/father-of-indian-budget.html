Father of Indian Budget.

(A) Bal Gangadhar Tilak

(B) ** Mahalanobis

(C) James Wilson

(D) Shanmugham Chetty

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mahalanobis devised a measure of comparison between two data sets that is now known as the Mahalanobis distance. He introduced innovative techniques for conducting large-scale sample surveys and calculated acreages and crop yields by using the method of random sampling.

Concept note-2: -He was a famous Indian Scientist and Mathematician. He is famous for the creation of the Mahalanobis distance. He was also a member of the planning commission of India post its independence. He is also famous for drafting India’s second five-year plan.

Concept note-3: -Prasanta Chandra Mahalanobis is also known as the father of Indian Statistics. He was a physicist by training, a statistician by instinct and a planner by conviction.

Concept note-4: -Brilliant and ambitious, Professor Mahalanobis moved to England to further his education. There he met the eminent Indian mathematical genius Srinivasa Ramanujan and was deeply influenced by him.
