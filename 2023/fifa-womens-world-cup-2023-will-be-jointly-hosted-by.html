FIFA Womens World Cup 2023 will be jointly hosted by

(A) INDIA, PAKISTAN

(B) INDIA, SRILANKA

(C) ** AUSTRALIA, NEWZealand

(D) USA CANADA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Eden Park, Auckland will host New Zealand’s opening match, and Stadium Australia, Sydney will host Australia’s opening match (both group stages). Lang Park, Brisbane will host the 3rd place match on 19 August; and Stadium Australia, Sydney will host the final which will be held on 20 August.

Concept note-2: -The FIFA Women’s World Cup 2023™ is jointly hosted by two countries, Australia and Aotearoa New Zealand, and held for the first time in the Southern Hemisphere.

Concept note-3: -NZ Football-FIFA Women’s World Cup 2023™ This year we will play co-host to the biggest women’s sporting event in the world. The FIFA Women’s World Cup Australia-New Zealand 2023™ will be a historic event on multiple levels.

Concept note-4: -Stadium Australia will host the Final of the FIFA Women’s World Cup 2023 following the official announcement of the 9 Host Cities and 10 stadiums in Australia and New Zealand that will host matches during the tournament.
