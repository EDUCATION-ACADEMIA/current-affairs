Freedom of expression

(A) Nobody has any right to hurt us or to torture us.

(B) ** We all have the right to make up our own minds, to think what we like, to say what we think, and to share our ideas with other people

(C) We can all ask for the law to help us when we are not treated fairly

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Everyone has the right to freedom of expression. This right shall include freedom to hold opinions and to receive and impart information and ideas without interference by public authority and regardless of frontiers.

Concept note-2: -Article 13 indicates that freedom of thought and expression “ includes freedom to seek, receive, and impart information and ideas of all kinds?.

Concept note-3: -Everyone has the right to freedom of opinion and expression; this right includes freedom to hold opinions without interference and to seek, receive and impart information and ideas through any media and regardless of frontiers.

Concept note-4: -Freedom of expression enables dialogue, builds understanding, and increases public knowledge. When we can freely exchange ideas and information, our knowledge improves, which benefits our communities and societies. Freedom of expression also enables us to question our governments, which helps to keep them accountable.
