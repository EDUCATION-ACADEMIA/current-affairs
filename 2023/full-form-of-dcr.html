Full form of DCR

(A) Democratic Republic Congo

(B) ** Democratic Republic of the Congo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) is a country in Central Africa bordered to the west by the South Atlantic Ocean. By land area, the DRC is the second-largest country in Africa and the 11th-largest in the world.

Concept note-2: -Democratic Republic of Congo Overview: Development news, research, data | World Bank. Overview.

Concept note-3: -Democratic Republic of the Congo.

Concept note-4: -Officially known as the Republic of the Congo, the country is often called Congo (Brazzaville), with its capital added parenthetically, to distinguish it from neighbouring Democratic Republic of the Congo, which is often referred to by its acronym, the DRC, or called Congo (Kinshasa).

Concept note-5: -The Democratic Republic of the Congo hosts the United Nations’ largest peacekeeping mission in the world, with over 21, 000 soldiers from approximately 50 different countries.
