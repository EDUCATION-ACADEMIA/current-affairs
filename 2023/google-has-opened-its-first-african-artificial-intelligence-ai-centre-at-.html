(B) Durban

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The center will host researchers and engineers working in AI-related projects. Google has opened its first Artificial Intelligence (AI) center in Africa in Ghana’s capital city, Accra.

Concept note-2: -Google opens an artificial intelligence (AI) research center in Accra, Ghana, its first on the African continent. We look forward to the amazing talents and breakthrough innovations of Google’s AI Research Centre right here in the heart of Accra, Ghana.

Concept note-3: -Since 2015, when Google introduced its first AI into search named RankBrain, Google has continued to deploy AI systems to better understand language and thus improve the search results Google presents to its searches.

Concept note-4: -Multinational tech giant, Google, has unveiled a new office in Accra, Ghana, to support the impressive growth of the company’s AI research team in the count.
