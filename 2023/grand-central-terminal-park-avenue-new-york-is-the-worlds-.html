(B) LONGEST RAILWAY STATION

(C) ** LARGEST RAILWAY STATION

(D) NONEN OF THE ABOV

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Built by and named for the New York Central Railroad in the heyday of American long-distance passenger rail travel, it covers 48 acres (19 ha) and has 44 platforms, more than any other railroad station in the world.

Concept note-2: -Hubli Railway Station in Karnataka is the world’s longest railway station. Also known as Shree Siddharoodha Swamiji – Hubballi Junction, it is operated by the South Western Railways under the Hubli division. Platform 1 of the Hubbali station, with a length of 1, 505 metres, is the longest railway platform in the world.

Concept note-3: -Opened to the public on February 2, 1913, Grand Central is a world-famous landmark and transportation hub in Midtown Manhattan. Its rich history is a story of immense wealth and great engineering, but also of survival and rebirth.

Concept note-4: -Solution(By Examveda Team) Grand Central Terminal, Park Avenue, New York is the world’s largest railway station.

Concept note-5: -Grand Central Terminal-New York City’s most famous train station.
