How many bills were passed in Rajya Sabha in the winter session of Parliament?

(A) 12

(B) 10

(C) ** 15

(D) 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -During this Session, a total of 13 Bills (12 in Lok Sabha and 01 in Rajya Sabha) were introduced. 13 Bills were passed by Lok Sabha and 11 Bills were passed by Rajya Sabha. Total number of Bills passed by both the Houses of Parliament were 11.

Concept note-2: -2022. Earlier, the Constitution (Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes) Order (Second Amendment) Bill, 2022 was passed unanimously by the Rajya Sabha during the ongoing Winter Session of the Parliament on 14.12.

Concept note-3: -2022. Following this, in relation to the state of Karnataka, the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Fourth Amendment) Bill, 2022, was also passed unanimously in Rajya Sabha on 22.12.

Concept note-4: -The Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Amendment) Bill, 2021.
