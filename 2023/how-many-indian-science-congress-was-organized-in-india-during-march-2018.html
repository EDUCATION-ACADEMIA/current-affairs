How many Indian Science Congress was organized in India during March 2018?

(A) 101st

(B) 103rd

(C) ** 105th

(D) 108th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 7th Women Science Congress was inaugurated as part of the 105th Indian Science Congress, 2018, at the Centenary Hall of Manipur University, Imphal.

Concept note-2: -The 105th edition of the Indian Science Congress was held at the Manipur University campus in Imphal over March 16-20th with the theme of “Reaching the Unreached through Science and Technology”.

Concept note-3: -The focal theme for 105th Indian Science Congress is “Reaching the Unreached through Science and Technology”.

Concept note-4: -The largest gathering of Scientific luminaries from Countries across the world, with focus on “Future India : Science & Technology", the 106th Session of Indian Science Congress, will be held from January 3 to 7, 2019 at Lovely Professional University (LPU) campus in Phagwara, Jalandhar, Punjab.

Concept note-5: -Option 1 is correct, i.e. Bengaluru. The 107th edition of the Indian Science Congress was held in Bengaluru. It was inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Theme: “Science and Technology: Rural Development”.
