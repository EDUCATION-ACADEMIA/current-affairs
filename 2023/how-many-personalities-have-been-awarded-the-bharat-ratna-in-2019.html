How many personalities have been awarded the Bharat Ratna in 2019?

(A) 2

(B) 5

(C) ** 3

(D) 1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A maximum of 3 people can be awarded the Bharat Ratna. In 2019, this award was given to three eminent personalities namely: Nanaji Deshmukh, Pranab Mukherjee, and Bhupen Hazarika.

Concept note-2: -2019 Bharat Ratna Award: Former President Pranab Mukherjee, Assamese singer-composer Bhupen Hazarika and social activist Nanaji Deshmukh will be awarded the countrys highest civilian award. Bharat Ratna Winners 2019: Pranab Mukherjee, Bhupen Hazarika and Nanaji Deshmukh to get awards.

Concept note-3: -It is awarded by the President of India. Till now, 48 eminent persons have been awarded Bharat Ratna Award. It is also presented to people posthumously and as of now, 14 eminent persons have been awarded posthumously.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Shri Anilkumar Manibhai Naik.
