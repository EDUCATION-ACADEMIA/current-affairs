How much SINOVAC doses did the PH’s government received on Friday, July 23?

(A) 10, 000

(B) 100, 000

(C) ** 1, 000, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: In case that 2 doses of Sinovac or Sinopharm COVID-19 vaccine were given: Moderna COVID-19 vaccine should be administered 1-3 months apart from the second dose of Sinovac or Sinopharm COVID-19 vaccine.

Concept note-2: -The WHO recommends that you receive the Sinovac-CoronaVac COVID-19 vaccine for both of your doses. The following people should not receive the second dose of vaccine: people who have had a severe allergic reaction to the first dose or any of the ingredients in the vaccine.

Concept note-3: -Sinovac: CoronaVac – COVID19 Vaccine Tracker.

Concept note-4: -The Sinovac vaccine is a vaccine that uses an inactivated form of the COVID-19 virus, instead of the mRNA technology that Pfizer and Moderna use.
