If you are in South Africa and you started travelling in east direction, which continent would you get to first?

(A) South America

(B) ** Asia

(C) Australia

(D) Europe

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solutions for If you are in South Africa and you started travelling in east direction, which continent would you get to first? a)South Americab)Asiac)Australiad)EuropeCorrect answer is option ‘B’.

Concept note-2: -The first continent to the east of South Africa is South America.

Concept note-3: -Your flight direction from Asia to South Africa is Southwest (-127 degrees from North).

Concept note-4: -South Africa lies between the Indian and Atlantic Oceans and is under the influence from the southern oceans around Antarctica.

Concept note-5: -The Isthmus of Suez in eastern Egypt connects the continents of Africa and Asia, and separates the Mediterranean and Red Seas.
