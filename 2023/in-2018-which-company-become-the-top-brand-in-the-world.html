In 2018, which company become the top brand in the world?

(A) . Facebook

(B) . Google

(C) ** . Apple

(D) . Microsoft

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple pipped Google to become world’s top brand in the world in 2018, as no brand from India finds mention in the list, an annual report said on Thursday.

Concept note-2: -Brand Finance Global 500 2018 The annual report on the world’s 500 most valuable brands. Amazon is the world’s most valuable brand ahead of Apple and Google in the Brand Finance Global 500. The e-commerce giant’s brand value increased by 42% year on year to a whopping US$150.8 billion.

Concept note-3: -Billionaire Jeff Bezos’s e-commerce company Amazon has become the most valued brand, overtaking Apple, last year’s topper. Amazon bagged the top spot despite its brand value falling 15% this year from $350.3 billion to $299.3 billion.
