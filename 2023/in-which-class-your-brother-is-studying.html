EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For a normal course, 1 hour per subject will be a good strategy, whereas preparation for any competitive exam demands proper management of time and schedule for each day. One should be regular and stick to the schedule made for each day. For competitive exams at least 6-7 hours study is mandatory.

Concept note-2: -Sleep is crucial to physical health, brain function, and learning. Eight hours of sleep a night is ideal, but some students need closer to nine.

Concept note-3: -5–6 hours can gain you a solid rank, and if you have 8–10 hours to spare, you’ll almost surely be in the top 50–100.
