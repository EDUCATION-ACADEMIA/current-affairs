In which state green ramayan park is built-up

(A) Meghalaya

(B) Kerela

(C) Madhya pardesh

(D) ** Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Uttarakhand, known as ‘Dev Bhumi’ has developed a ‘Green Ramayana Park’ with a motive to introduce and preserve ancient species of plants that are related to Lord Ram’s life. Forest division of the state had come up with this unique idea which is an amalgamation of our tradition and environment.

Concept note-2: -Flora is part of Uttarakhand’s Ramayana celebration. The state forest department is celebrating the flora mentioned in Valmiki’s Ramayana. The first of its kind in the state, the Green Ramayana Park in Haldwani includes plants mentioned in Valmiki’s Ramayana, over the journey of Rama through the forests.

Concept note-3: -The Uttara Kanda is the seventh and last book of what we call the Valmiki Ramayana. The word Uttara has many meanings, among them, “after”, “epilogue”, “ultimate” and “answer”.
