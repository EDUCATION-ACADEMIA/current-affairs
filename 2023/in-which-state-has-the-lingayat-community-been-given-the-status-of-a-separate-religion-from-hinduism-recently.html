In which state has the Lingayat community been given the status of a separate religion from Hinduism recently?

(A) Kerala

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) ** Karnataka

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lingayatism is generally considered a Hindu sect because their beliefs include many Hindu elements. Worship is centered on Shiva as the universal god in the iconographic form of Ishtalinga.

Concept note-2: -In Karnataka, Basaveshwar spread the lingayat stream of thought.

Concept note-3: -It was founded by the philosopher Basava in the 12th century and spread by his followers, who are known as ‘Sharanas’. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-4: -At present, Hindu-Lingayats are considered backward class in Karnataka and come under Category 3B with a 5% reservation. Their inclusion as OBC in the Central list will mean 27% reservation in Central government services and Central educational institutions.
