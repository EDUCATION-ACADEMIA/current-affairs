India banned how many more Chinese apps on July 24, 2020?

(A) ** 47

(B) 56

(C) 35

(D) 45

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In an order issued on February 5, the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) directed the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) to ban and block as many as 232 apps that either originate or have links to China. These included 138 betting apps and 94 online lending apps.

Concept note-2: -In this article, we will tell you about the List of Chinese Apps Banned by Indian government. Ministry of Information & Technology had banned 59 apps on 29 June 2020, 47 clone apps on 28 July 2020, 118 apps on 2 September 2020, 43 apps on 24 November 2020 and finally 54 apps on 14 February 2022.

Concept note-3: -The 54 Chinese apps include Beauty Camera: Sweet Selfie HD, Beauty Camera-Selfie Camera, Equalizer & Bass Booster, CamCard for SalesForce Ent, Isoland 2: Ashes of Time Lite, Viva Video Editor, Tencent Xriver, Onmyoji Chess, Onmyoji Arena, AppLock, Dual Space Lite.
