India builds one kilometer long World’s first electrified rail tunnel in which state?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Maharashtra

(C) ** Haryana

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World’s first electrified double-stack container tunnel is 1 km long and is situated at the Western Dedicated Freight Corridor near Sohna, Haryana.

Concept note-2: -India’s longest electrified rail tunnel has been constructed in Andhra Pradesh and it is shaped like a horseshoe. Situated between Cherlopalli and Rapuru stations in Andhra Pradesh, the tunnel is 6.6 kilometres long and has been built at a cost of Rs 460 crore.

Concept note-3: -Pir Panjal Railway Tunnel, the 11.2 km long railway tunnel, passes through the Pir Panjal Range of middle Himalayas in Jammu and Kashmir. It is a part of its Udhampur – Srinagar – Baramulla rail link project, India’s longest railway tunnel and reduced the distance between Quazigund and Banihal.
