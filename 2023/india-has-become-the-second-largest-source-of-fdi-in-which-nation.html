India has become the second-largest source of FDI in which nation?

(A) US

(B) Japan

(C) Canada

(D) ** UK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has retained its position as the second largest source of foreign direct investment (FDI) for the United Kingdom. Indian companies have invested in at least 99 projects in the UK and created over 4, 800 jobs.

Concept note-2: -India has remained the second largest investor in the UK for another year as revealed in the UK’s Department for International Trade Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) 2021/22 figures.

Concept note-3: -India is the 2nd largest FDI contributor in the UK. Over 900 Indian companies operate in the UK which has generated a total turnover of USD 68.04 Bn, employing 141, 005 people in 2022. India’s trade in goods and services with the UK increased to USD 31.34 billion in 2022 from USD 19.51 billion in 2015.

Concept note-4: -USA is India’s second biggest investor with FDI of $10.5 billion, with 18 per cent of total FDI.
