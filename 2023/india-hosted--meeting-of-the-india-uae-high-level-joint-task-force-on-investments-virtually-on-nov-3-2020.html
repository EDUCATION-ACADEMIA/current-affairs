India Hosted ____ meeting of the India-UAE High Level Joint Task Force on Investments virtually on Nov 3 2020?

(A) 15th

(B) 6th

(C) ** 8th

(D) 10th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (CEPA) between the Government of the Republic of India and the Government of the United Arab Emirates (UAE)-Mcommerce.

Concept note-2: -UAE’s exports to India stood at US$ 43.04 billion while India’s exports to UAE stood at US$ 25.4 billion. In 2022 India & UAE signed a Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (CEPA) with an aim of taking bilateral trade to US$ 100 billion within 5 years.

Concept note-3: -The two nations share historical ties and have maintained regular cultural exchanges both at official and popular levels. India and UAE signed a Cultural Agreement in 1975. The ICCR had opened a Culture Centre in Abu Dhabi in 2009 but it was closed in June 2014.
