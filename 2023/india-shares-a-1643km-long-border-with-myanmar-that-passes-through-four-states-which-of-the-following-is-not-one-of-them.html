India shares a 1643km long border with Myanmar that passes through four states. Which of the following is not one of them?

(A) Nagaland.

(B) Arunachal Pradesh

(C) ** Tripura

(D) Mizoram SCERT Class 6-Basic Science-chapter 5-Food for health

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tripura, Assam, Meghalaya doesn’t share a border with Myanmar. So, the correct answer is Option B. Note: There are only four states in India that share a border with Myanmar which are Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Manipur, and Mizoram.

Concept note-2: -Four North-Eastern States viz. Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Manipur and Mizoram share international boundary with Myanmar.

Concept note-3: -Four Northeast Indian states share the border with Myanmar: Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Mizoram, and Manipur. Tripura does not share border with Myanmar.

Concept note-4: -Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Manipur, and Mizoram share an international boundary with Myanmar.
