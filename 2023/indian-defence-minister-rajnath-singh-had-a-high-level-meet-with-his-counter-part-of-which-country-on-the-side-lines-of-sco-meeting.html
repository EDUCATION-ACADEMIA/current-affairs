Indian Defence Minister Rajnath Singh had a high-level meet with his counter part of which country, on the side-lines of SCO meeting?

(A) Iran

(B) United States

(C) ** China

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh to attend Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Defence Ministers ’ Meeting in Tashkent, Uzbekistan.

Concept note-2: -India invites Pakistan, China to 2023 SCO summit to be held in Goa.

Concept note-3: -India has taken over as Chairperson of the SCO for 2023, and will host leaders of all SCO countries at a summit in Delhi expected in mid-2023. Earlier, the SCO summit 2022 was held recently in Samarkand, Uzbekistan.
