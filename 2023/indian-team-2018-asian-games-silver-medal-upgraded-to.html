indian team 2018 ASIAN GAMES silver medal upgraded to

(A) none

(B) ** gold

(C) bronze

(D) silver

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Poovamma, Hima Das and Arokia Rajiv, who represented India at the 4x400m mixed relay event at the 2018 Asian Games, had its silver medal upgraded to gold on Thursday, the Athletics Federation of India (AFI) confirmed.

Concept note-2: -Sachin Nag was the first Indian to win a gold medal at the Asian Games held in Delhi in 1951. The swimmer won the medal in the 100m freestyle event. Sachin competed twice for India at the Olympics in the 1948 and 1952 editions competing in the water polo event.

Concept note-3: -India won 51 medals – 15 gold, 16 silver and 20 bronze – at Asian Games 1951 to finish second behind Japan (60 medals). It remains India’s best finish at the continental Games.
