India’s first coronavirus airport testing facility was launched at

(A) mumbai airport

(B) ** delhi airport

(C) karnataka airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Delhi airport: 110 RT-PCR tests on day one; 500 tests daily expected at current 2% rate.

Concept note-2: -There are currently 21 CTCs in Delhi which are open from 8 AM to 4 PM.

Concept note-3: -As per the latest government advisory, effective 1st January 2023 (1000 hrs IST), all passengers arriving from countries identified as ‘High Risk’ will have to undergo a mandatory RT-PCR test (to be conducted 72 hrs before undertaking the journey).

Concept note-4: -The test results will be available within 4-6 hours. All Passengers are suggested to book the slot 1 hour after the time of arrival and a minimum of 4-6 hours before the departure flight (the post sample collection) or book a domestic flight after test results are available.
