Inspire Institute of Sports (IIS) Bellary have partnered with which of the following to develop a software for boxing analytics called ‘Smartboxer’?

(A) ** IIT, Madras

(B) IIT, Delhi

(C) IIT, Guwahati

(D) IIT, Bombay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IIT Madras joins Inspire Institute of Sports for innovative boxing analytics software. IIT Madras joins Inspire Institute of Sports: IIT Madras researchers and Inspire Institute of Sports in Karnataka have joined forces to create the analytics platform “Smartboxer."

Concept note-2: -’Smartboxer’, a cost-effective analytic platform, will use wearable sensors and video cameras to provide assessments of boxers on the four key traits for scoring in boxing. At the recently concluded Commonwealth Games 2022, India’s best performance was in boxing and in badminton with three gold medals each.

Concept note-3: -Smartboxer, being developed as a multi-version software by the Centre of Excellence for Sports Science and Analytics at IIT-M will provide boxers and their trainers feedback and performance assessments using Internet of Things (IoT) enabled wearable sensors and video cameras.
