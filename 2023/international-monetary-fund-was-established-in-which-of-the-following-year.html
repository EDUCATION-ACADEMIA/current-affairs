International Monetary Fund was established in which of the following year?

(A) ** 1945

(B) 1956

(C) 1943

(D) 1929

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The IMF was established in 1944 in the aftermath of the Great Depression of the 1930s. 44 founding member countries sought to build a framework for international economic cooperation. Today, its membership embraces 190 countries, with staff drawn from 150 nations.

Concept note-2: -The International Monetary Fund (IMF) was created in 1945. The International Monetary Fund (IMF) is an organization of 189 countries.

Concept note-3: -The IMF formally came into existence on 27 December 1945, when the first 29 countries ratified its Articles of Agreement. By the end of 1946 the IMF had grown to 39 members.

Concept note-4: -The formation of the IMF was initiated in 1944 at the Bretton Woods Conference. IMF came into operation on 27th December 1945 and is today an international organization that consists of 189 member countries.

Concept note-5: -The IMF and the World Bank were created in July 1944 at an international conference in the United States (in Bretton Woods, New Hampshire) that established a framework for economic cooperation aimed at creating a more stable and prosperous global economy.
