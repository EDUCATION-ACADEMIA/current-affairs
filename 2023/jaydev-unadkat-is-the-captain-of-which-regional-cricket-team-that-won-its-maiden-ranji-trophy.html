Jaydev Unadkat is the captain of which regional cricket team that won its maiden Ranji trophy?

(A) Mumbai cricket team

(B) Bengal cricket team

(C) Karnataka cricket team

(D) ** Saurashtra cricket team

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Saurashtra skipper and India pacer Jaydev Unadkat on Sunday dedicated the team’s Ranji Trophy triumph to India batter Cheteshwar Pujara, calling him one of the region’s favourite sons.

Concept note-2: -The Mumbai cricket team is the most successful team of the tournament by winning it record 41 times. Saurashtra cricket team is present title holder by winning 2022-23 Ranji Trophy.

Concept note-3: -The final match of the 83rd edition of the Ranji Trophy was concluded at Holkar Stadium (Indore), Madhya Pradesh. Gujarat became the winner of the Ranji Trophy, 2016-17. This is Gujarat’s first Ranji title in the history of 83 years. The captain of Gujarat was Parthiv Patel.

Concept note-4: -Jaydev Unadkat has not played a Test since his debut in 2010. In February 2022, he was bought by the Mumbai Indians in the auction for the 2022 Indian Premier League tournament.

Concept note-5: -The 2017–18 Ranji Trophy was the 84th season of the Ranji Trophy, the premier first-class cricket tournament that took place in India between October 2017 and January 2018. Vidarbha won the tournament, beating Delhi by 9 wickets in the final, to win their first Ranji Trophy title.
