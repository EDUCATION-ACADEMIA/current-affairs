july is celebrated as which day on the whole country?

(A) Uri Victory day

(B) ** Kargil Victory day

(C) LOC Victory day

(D) Kashmir Victory day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kargil Vijay Diwas is celebrated on 26 July every year in honour of the Kargil War’s Heroes. This day is celebrated all over India and in the national capital, New Delhi, where the Prime Minister of India pays homage to the soldiers at Amar Jawan Jyoti at the India Gate every year.

Concept note-2: -26 July Special Day in India is celebrated as Kargil Vijay Diwas every year to mark the successful victory of Operation Vijay which lasted for 3 months along the Line of Control. Nearly, 500 Indian army officials, soldiers & Jawans were martyred during the battle.

Concept note-3: -Kargil Vijay Diwas is celebrated every year on 26th July to rekindle the pride and valour of the soldiers who took part in Operation Vijay. The day marks the victory of Indian soldiers in capturing the mountain heights that were occupied by the Pakistani Army on Jul 26, 1999 known as the Kargil War.

Concept note-4: -Kargil Vijay Diwas: Kargil Vijay Diwas is observed on July 26 every year in India. The day is dedicated to soldiers who lost their lives for the country during the Kargil war in 1999.

Concept note-5: -NHCE ignited the spark in the young leaders of tomorrow by celebrating Kargil Vijay Diwas on the 26th of July, 2022. India’s victory on this day 23 years ago was proudly declared.
