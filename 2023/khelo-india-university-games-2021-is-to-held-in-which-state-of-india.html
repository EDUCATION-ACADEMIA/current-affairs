Khelo India University Games 2021 is to held in which State of India?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Bihar

(C) ** Karnataka

(D) Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The chief minister of Karnataka, BS Yeddyurappa, announced that Karnataka will be hosting the second Khelo India University Games (KIUG) in the year 2021. Key Points The Khelo India University Games will be inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Concept note-2: -KIIT University in Bhubaneswar, which is located in the state of Odisha, is slated to host the first Khelo India University Games in 2022.

Concept note-3: -The first Edition of Khelo India was held at Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT), Bhubaneswar, Odisha in 2020. The second edition of the Khelo India University games, which were postponed due to Covid in 2021, were finally hosted by Bangalore’s Jain University in 2022.

Concept note-4: -Khelo India University Games 2022: Jain University Clinches KIUG Crown; Swimmer Siva Sridhar Reigns Supreme. Lovely Professional University finished second while defending champions Punjab University had to be satisfied with the third spot.

Concept note-5: -Khelo India University Games 2022: From venues in Bengaluru, to sports to participants and former winners, here’s the lowdown on the event. The Khelo India University Games (KIUG) are all set to return in April. The event which was to be held in 2021 was delayed due to Covid-19 and will finally be held in Bengaluru.
