Kilauea Volcano, which was seen in the news, is located in which country?

(A) Indonesia

(B) Philippines

(C) ** Hawaii (USA)

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Kilauea Volcano, located in the Hawaii state of the USA is considered as one of the most active volcanoes on the planet. The summit of Kilauea is located inside Hawaii Volcanoes National Park. The Kilauea volcano has started erupting after a brief pause.

Concept note-2: -Kīlauea Volcano is a shield volcano located on the eastern slope of Mauna Loa Volcano on the Island of Hawai’i. The volcano is considered to be in the shield-building stage of Hawaiian volcanism.

Concept note-3: -Kīlauea is an unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) in Kauaʻi County, Hawaii, United States. As of the 2020 census it had a population of 3, 014. Kīlauea shares the name of the active volcano Kīlauea on the island of Hawaii.

Concept note-4: -Activity Summary: Mauna Loa Volcano is not erupting. Observations: Webcams show no signs of activity on Mauna Loa’s Northeast Rift Zone, site of the recent eruption in November–December 2022. There is no active lava within Moku’āweoweo caldera nor along either rift zone.

Concept note-5: -Kīlauea volcano is erupting. At approximately 4:34 p.m. HST on January 5, 2023, the USGS Hawaiian Volcano Observatory detected glow in Kīlauea summit webcam images indicating that the eruption has resumed within Halemaʻumaʻu crater in Kīlauea’s summit caldera, within Hawai’i Volcanoes National Park.
