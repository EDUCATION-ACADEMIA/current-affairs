Lt Gen ____ has been appointed as the new Army Vice-Chief.

(A) ** Chandy Prasad Mohanty

(B) SK Saini

(C) Manoj Mukund Naravane

(D) Bipin Rawat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lieutenant General Chandi Prasad Mohanty PVSM AVSM SM VSM is a retired General Officer in the Indian Army. He was the 42nd Vice Chief of the Army Staff and assumed office on February 1, 2021, following the retirement of Satinder Kumar Saini.

Concept note-2: -The sources said Lt Gen Kumar too has been promoted as the Army Commander and appointed as the new Vice Chief of Army Staff.

Concept note-3: -By Abhishek Bhalla : Lt Gen Manoj Pande took over as the new Vice Chief of Indian Army from Lt Gen Lieutenant General Chandi Prasad Mohanty who superannuated after four decades.

Concept note-4: -By Manjeet Negi: General Manoj Pande took charge as the 29th Chief of the Army Staff on Saturday after incumbent Gen MM Naravane retired from service. Gen Pande is the first officer from the Corps of Engineers to head the 1.3 million strong force.

Concept note-5: -Lt Gen Manoj Pande is an alumnus of the National Defence Academy.
