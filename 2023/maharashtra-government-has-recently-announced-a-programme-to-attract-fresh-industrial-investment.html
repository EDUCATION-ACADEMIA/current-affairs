Maharashtra government has recently announced a programme to attract fresh Industrial Investment.

(A) ** Maha Parwana

(B) Maha Gharana

(C) Maha Karkhana

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under the scheme, the state will offer several incentives and offer a single-window clearance system for project execution. The state cabinet approved that the companies investing Rs 50 crore or more, are not required to obtain clearances from several departments.

Concept note-2: -MAHA-PARVANA-For all the proposals of foreign direct investment and. investment proposals of Rs. 50 Crore and above, the Government Letter of Assurance shall be given for commencing the construction and production without availing the permissions related to pre-set up as mentioned in the Schedule-I, .
