Name of the project that provides facility to consult doctor online

(A) ehealthOPD

(B) ** eSanjeevaniOPD

(C) ehealthOPD

(D) earogyaOPD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -eSanjeevaniOPD-National Teleconsultation Service.

Concept note-2: -eSanjeevani-a doctor to doctor telemedicine system is being deployed nationally for the Miinistry of Health & Family Welfare at 155, 000 Health and Wellness Centres under Ayushman Bharat Scheme of Government of India.

Concept note-3: -Using eSanjeevaniOPD is a simple 4 step process to help patients get the consultation they need. wait for patients to call you for a consultation 3. Acceot & join the call and help the patients with a consultation 4. Complete the consultation and issue eprescription online.

Concept note-4: -Difference between eSanjeevani and eSanjeevani OPD eSanjeevani was launched in 2019. The first state to launch this service was Andhra Pradesh. eSanjeevani OPD: This is based on the eSanjeevani model and offers OPD consultation services to people in the comfort of their homes.
