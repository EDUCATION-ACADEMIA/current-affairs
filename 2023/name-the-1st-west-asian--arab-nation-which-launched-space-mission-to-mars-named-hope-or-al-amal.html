Name the 1st West Asian & Arab Nation which launched space mission to mars (named ‘Hope’ or ‘Al-Amal’)?

(A) Bahrain

(B) ** U.A.E.

(C) Saudi Arabia

(D) Comoros

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2020, the UAE’s space agency launched its first Mars mission, less than a decade after it was created.

Concept note-2: -About: The UAE’s Mars Mission called ‘Hope’ was announced in 2015 with the aim of creating mankind’s first integrated model of the Red planet’s (Mars) atmosphere.

Concept note-3: -In 1971, the Soviet Union finally reached the Red Planet. Its Mars 2 orbiter, which launched May 19, 1971, arrived on Nov. 2.
