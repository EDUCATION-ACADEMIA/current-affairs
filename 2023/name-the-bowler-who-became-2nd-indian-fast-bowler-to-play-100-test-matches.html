Name the bowler who became 2nd Indian fast bowler to play 100 Test matches?

(A) ** Ishant Sharma

(B) Umesh Yadav

(C) R Ashwin

(D) Jasprit Bumrah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Former skipper Anil Kumble (132), Kapil (131), and spinner Harbhajan Singh (103) are the only bowlers other than Ishant to feature in 100 Tests for India.

Concept note-2: -Ishant Sharma has become the second Indian fast bowler to play 100 Test matches when he took the field against England in Ahmedabad. The only other pacer from India to have played 100 Tests is the great Kapil Dev (131). In the second Test at Chennai, he claimed his 300th Test wicket.

Concept note-3: -From India, there are 13 players on the list who made it to century of Test matches.

Concept note-4: -Hit For Six! Only three bowlers in the history of Indian cricket have played 100 Test matches.
