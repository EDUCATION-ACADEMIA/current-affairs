(A) Grand Chess tour

(B) London Chess Classic

(C) ** Banter Blitz Cup

(D) Shenzhen Master

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Player Of The Year: Hikaru Nakamura The “streamer-only” genius came out of over-the-board “retirement” to win the FIDE Grand Prix and play in the Candidates Tournament. Online, aside from his routine Titled Tuesday wins, Nakamura beat none other than Carlsen to win the 2022 Speed Chess Championship.

Concept note-2: -Carlsen again successfully defended his title, defeating Ian Nepomniachtchi in the World Chess Championship 2021.

Concept note-3: -The current rapid world champion is grandmaster Magnus Carlsen. Tan Zhongyi from China is the current women’s rapid world champion.

Concept note-4: -Carlsen lost twice in the space of a week in the Netherlands, the same number of defeats he suffered in 2018 (from 62 games), 2020 (from 79 games) and 2021 (24 games). In 2022 he lost just once in 52 games and in 2019 he was unbeaten in 76.
