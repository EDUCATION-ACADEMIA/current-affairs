Name the winner of 2020 ONV literary award?

(A) ** M.Leelavathy

(B) Sachidanandan

(C) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -21 March 2022, 07:42 AM IST The award will be handed over to Dr M Leelavathy on Thakazhi Sivasankara Pillai’s birthday which falls on April 17 at a function held in Sankaramangalam.

Concept note-2: -The Sahitya Akademi Award 2022: Accordingly, the central government has announced the Sahitya Akademi Award for literature on December 22. Writer M. Rajendran from Tamil Nadu has received this award for his novel ‘Kala Pani’.

Concept note-3: -Payal Sharma (Faculty/Staff Award) UNLV Libraries & Greenspun College of Urban Affairs We Need To Talk Series-Su Kim Chung, Claytee White, Afsha Bawany, Sean Kennedy, Jennifer Ream, and the entire We Need to Talk team (Faculty/Staff Award) 1st Generation Club (Student Award)

Concept note-4: -The award carries a monetary award of INR 30000, a plaque, and a citation. Balakavi Raman won the first 1968 Odakkuzhal Award for his work ‘Narayaneeyam’ (Tamil translation) in 1969. N Prabhakaran has won 2019 Odakkuzhal Award for his work ‘Mayamanushyar’ in 2020.
