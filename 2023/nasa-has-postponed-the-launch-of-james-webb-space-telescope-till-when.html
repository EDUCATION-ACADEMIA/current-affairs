NASA has postponed the launch of James Webb Space Telescope till when?

(A) December 2021

(B) September 2021

(C) ** October 2021

(D) July 2021

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NASA delays launch of flagship James Webb Space Telescope to Oct. 31, 2021 | Space.

Concept note-2: -James Webb Space Telescope Launch Delayed to Christmas Eve-or Later. The launch of NASA’s James Webb Space Telescope has been delayed yet again. NASA’s long-awaited space observatory is now scheduled to lift off from French Guiana no earlier than Dec. 24, two days later than previously planned.

Concept note-3: -The agency said that a “communication issue between the observatory and the launch vehicle system” caused the postponement but didn’t disclose any additional details.

Concept note-4: -In June 2018, NASA delayed the launch by an additional 10 months to March 2021, based on the assessment of the independent review board convened after the failed March 2018 test deployment.

Concept note-5: -The delay is due to a communication issue between the observatory and the launch vehicle system. The delay came just days after the telescope was placed on top of the Ariane 5 rocket that will launch it to space from Europe’s Spaceport in French Guiana.
