National Tribal Dance Festival will be held in which of the following cities?

(A) Indore

(B) ** Raipur

(C) Ranchi

(D) Kohima

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chhattisgarh celebrates its 23rd State Foundation Day on 1st November 2022 and as a part of the celebrations, Raipur will host the 3rd National Tribal Dance Festival.

Concept note-2: -Sikkim won the top prize in the ‘dance on traditional occasions and rituals’, while Odisha and Jharkhand teams won the second and third prizes respectively.

Concept note-3: -The National Tribal Dance Festival will commence in Chhattisgarh’s capital city Raipur from November 1 and over 1, 500 local and international artistes will take part in it. Organised by the Chhattisgarh Tourism Board, the three-day event will be held at the Science College Ground.
