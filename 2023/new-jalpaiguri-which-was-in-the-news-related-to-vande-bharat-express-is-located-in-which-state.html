New Jalpaiguri, which was in the news related to Vande Bharat Express, is located in which state?

(A) Assam

(B) Gujarat

(C) ** West Bengal

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 22301/22302 Howrah–New Jalpaiguri Vande Bharat Express is India’s 7th Vande Bharat Express train, connecting West Bengal’s capital Kolkata with Siliguri, the largest city in northern part of West Bengal.

Concept note-2: -The new Vande Bharat Express from Howrah to New Jalpaiguri is expected to save three hours of travel time compared to other trains on the route. According to officials, there are 14 trains on this route. They all cover the 564 km distance at an average travel time of around 10:45 hours.

Concept note-3: -It is the headquarters of the Jalpaiguri district as well as of the Jalpaiguri division of West Bengal, covering the jurisdiction of the five districts of North Bengal.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi is also set to launch two new Vande Bharat trains-Mumbai-Solapur and Mumbai-Shirdi routes-on February 10. The first Vande Bharat train was flagged off in 2019 on the New Delhi-Kanpur-Allahabad-Varanasi route.
