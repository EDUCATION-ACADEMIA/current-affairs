Nidhi Apke Nikat’ is the outreach program conducted by which institution?

(A) IRDAI

(B) PFRDA

(C) ** EPFO

(D) RBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MUMBAI: The Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) on Friday launched the revamped Nidhi Aapke Nikat 2.0 outreach programme across the country. The programme was formally launched by labour secretary Arti Ahuja and Central Provident Fund commi-ssioner Neelam Shami Rao virtually from New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -The Nidhi Aapke Nikat is a programme where EPFO stakeholders come to the EPFO field offices for grievance redressal, under the Nidhi Aapke Nikat 2.0, EPFO will reach out to the stakeholders, thereby increasing accessibility and visibility of the organisation, in all the districts of the country.

Concept note-3: -https://www.epfindia.gov.in IS THE ONLY OFFICIAL WEBSITE OF EPFO.

Concept note-4: -Missed call and SMS service-Members who have activated their UAN can access their PF balance, previous contribution, the status of KYC, etc., by sending an SMS (Format: EPFOHO UAN) to 7738299899 or by giving a missed call to 011-22901406. Employers will also receive an SMS for non-payment of EPF.
