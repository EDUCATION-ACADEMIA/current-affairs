Nigeria is to Abuja as Kenya is to

(A) Accra

(B) ** Nairobi

(C) Kuwait

(D) Apapa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The travel duration between these cities in a non-stop flight is usually around 05h 15m.

Concept note-2: -The flight time between Nairobi (NBO) and Lagos (LOS) is around 7h 12m and covers a distance of around 3832 km. This includes an average layover time of around 1h 13m. Services are operated by Kenya Airways, Rwandair Express and Ethiopian Airlines.

Concept note-3: -The average price for connecting flights from Lagos to Nairobi is NGN364, 542. The average price for direct flights from Lagos to Nairobi is NGN519, 657.

Concept note-4: -The average price for direct flights from Abuja to Nairobi is NGN349, 649.
