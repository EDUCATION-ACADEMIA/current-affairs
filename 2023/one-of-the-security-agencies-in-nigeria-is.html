One of the security agencies in Nigeria is?

(A) Criminal

(B) teacher

(C) accountant

(D) ** Police

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Well, they’re responsible for law enforcement and maintaining public safety across the country. They’re also in charge of criminal investigations, and you can depend on them to protect you and your property.

Concept note-2: -Checks by Daily Trust show that the major security agencies in Nigeria are 10; namely, State Security Services (SSS), National Intelligence Agency (NIA), Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA), the armed forces (comprising the Nigerian Army, Navy and Air force) as well as the Nigeria Police Force (NPF).

Concept note-3: -The Nigeria Police Force (NPF) is designated by Section 194 of the 1979 constitution as the national police with exclusive jurisdiction throughout the country.

Concept note-4: -The National Intelligence Agency (NIA) is a Nigerian government division tasked with overseeing foreign intelligence and counterintelligence operations.

Concept note-5: -Nigeria Security and Civil Defence Corps (NSCDC) is a paramilitary institution in Nigeria that was first introduced in May 2003 by the Nigerian Government, as the Lagos Civil Defence. The act was amended in 2007 to enhance the statutory duties of the corp.
