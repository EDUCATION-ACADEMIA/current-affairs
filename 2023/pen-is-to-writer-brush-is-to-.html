Concept note-1: -Painter uses Brush to paint. Similarly, Author uses Pen to write. Hence, pen is the correct answer.

Concept note-3: -A writer is a type of artist. They just have different tools and skills than others who are typically considered to be artists-painters, sculptorers, etc.

Concept note-4: -calligraphy, the art of beautiful handwriting. The term may derive from the Greek words for “beauty” (kallos) and “to write” (graphein).

Concept note-5: -calligraphy, Art of beautiful, stylized, or elegant handwriting or lettering with pen or brush and ink. It involves the correct formation of characters, the ordering of the various parts, and the harmony of proportions. In the Islamic and Chinese cultures, calligraphy is as highly revered as painting.
