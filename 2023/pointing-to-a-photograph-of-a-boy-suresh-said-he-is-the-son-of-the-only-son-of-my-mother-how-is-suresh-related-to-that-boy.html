Pointing to a photograph of a boy Suresh said, “He is the son of the only son of my mother.” How is Suresh related to that boy?

(A) Brother

(B) Uncle

(C) Cousin

(D) ** Father

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The boy in the photograph is the only son of the son of Suresh’s mother i.e., the son of Suresh. Hence, Suresh is the father of boy.

Concept note-2: -Pointing to a photograph of a boy Suresh said, ‘’He is the son of the only son (Suresh) of my (Suresh’s) mother”. The below given diagram shows the relationship. Suresh is the father of the boy in the photograph. Hence, Father is correct.

Concept note-3: -Expert-Verified Answer The father of my mother will be my maternal grandfather. His son will be the brother of my mother.
