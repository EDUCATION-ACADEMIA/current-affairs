Prevention of anti social activities act (PASA) which was in the news recently, is said to be introduced in which Indian UT?

(A) Andaman and nicobar and islands

(B) ** Lakswadeep

(C) Chandigarh

(D) Puducherry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prevention of Anti-Social Activities Act (PASA) According to it, person can be detained without any public disclosure for a period of up to one year.

Concept note-2: -An Act to provide for preventive detention of bootleggers, dangerous persons, drug offenders, immoral traffic offenders and property grabbers for preventing their anti-social and dangerous activities prejudicial to the maintenance of public order. 1.

Concept note-3: -As per Section 3 of the PASA Act, the administrator may issue an order directing to detain a person if convinced that the person will act in a way that will be harmful to the maintenance of public order and intends to prevent him from doing so.

Concept note-4: -EXTRACT FROM THE GUJARAT PREVENTION OF ANTI-SOCIAL ACTIVITIES ACT, 1985. An Act to provide for preventive detention of boot-leggers, dangerous persons, drug offenders, immoral traffic offenders and property grabbers preventing their anti-social and dangerous activities prejudicial to the maintenance of public order.
