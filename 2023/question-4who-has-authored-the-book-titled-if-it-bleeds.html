Question 4Who has authored the book titled “If It Bleeds”?

(A) ** Stephen King

(B) VS Naipaul

(C) Ruskin Bond

(D) Alice Munro

(E) Salman Rushdie

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Stephen King is the author of more than sixty books, all of them worldwide bestsellers. His recent work includes Fairy Tale, Billy Summers, If It Bleeds, The Institute, Elevation, The Outsider, Sleeping Beauties (cowritten with his son Owen King), and the Bill Hodges trilogy: End of Watch, Finders Keepers, and Mr.

Concept note-2: -Holly Gibney of the Finders Keepers detective agency sees footage of a horrific school bombing on TV. She suspects that there is something odd about the reporter Ondowsky who was first on the scene and that he may be a supernatural ‘outsider’.

Concept note-3: -If it Bleeds is the 75th book published by Stephen King, his eleventh collection of stories, released in April 21, 2020 by Scribner. The book is part of The Hodges/Gibney metaseries.

Concept note-4: -In April 1974, Stephen King published his first novel, Carrie, the story of a young girl with telekinetic powers and a crazy religious mother.
