Rajya Sabha member Sanjay Singh submitted his resignation from the Parliament’s upper house today. Singh belonged to which party?

(A) BJP

(B) ** INC

(C) BSP

(D) SP

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sanjay Singh (born 22 March 1972) is an Indian politician who has served as a member of the Rajya Sabha from Delhi since 2018. He is the national spokesperson for the Aam Aadmi Party and the state-in-charge for Uttar Pradesh, Odisha and Rajasthan.

Concept note-2: -The Rajya Sabha, constitutionally the Council of States, is the upper house of the bicameral Parliament of India.

Concept note-3: -Membership is limited to 250 members, and the present Rajya Sabha has 275 members. 23 members are elected by the Vidhan Sabha members and 12 are nominated by the President for their contributions to art, literature, science, and social services.
