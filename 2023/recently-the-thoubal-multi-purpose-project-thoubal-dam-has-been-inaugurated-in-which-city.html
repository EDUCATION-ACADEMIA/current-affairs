Recently the Thoubal Multi Purpose Project (Thoubal Dam) has been inaugurated in which city?

(A) ** Imphal

(B) agartala

(C) itnanagar

(D) none

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union Home Minister Shri Amit Shah inaugurated several development projects in Manipur today. Shri Amit Shah inaugurated the e-office and Thoubal Multipurpose Project (Thoubal Dam) in Imphal through virtual mode.

Concept note-2: -The Thoubal Multipurpose Project on river Thoubal, a tributary of Manipur river, is located near the village Phayang at 35 km from Imphal town in Manipur. The Thoubal Multipurpose Project estimated to cost Rs. 47.25 Crores was sanctioned for implementation by the Planning Commission during May’1980.

Concept note-3: -Mapithel-Mapithel dam, initially known as Thoubal Multipurpose Project, aimed to produce 7.5MW of power. Irrigation and providing drinking water were also the objectives.
