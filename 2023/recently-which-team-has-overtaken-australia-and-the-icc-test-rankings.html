Recently, which team has overtaken Australia and the ICC Test rankings?

(A) ** New Zealand

(B) b. South Africa

(C) c. India

(D) d. None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On February 15, India overtook Pat Cummins’ Australia to take the top spot in the ICC Test rankings, making them the top-ranked team across all three formats of the game. India, who had already established themselves as the best T20 team, surpassed New Zealand 3-0 in January to take the top spot in ODIs.

Concept note-2: -ICC Men’s Test Team of the Year: Australia enjoyed another year of Test success, consolidating their position atop the ICC World Test Championship standings.

Concept note-3: -Australia is currently the highest-ranked team in the rankings, as of an update published in January 2022.
