(A) Zomato

(B) ** Oyo Hotels

(C) Swiggy

(D) Byjus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ritesh Agarwal is an Indian entrepreneur who is a founder and CEO of OYO Rooms.
