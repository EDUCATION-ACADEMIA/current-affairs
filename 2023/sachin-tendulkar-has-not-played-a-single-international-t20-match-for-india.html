Sachin Tendulkar has not played a single International T20 match for India.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Yes, Sachin has played just 1 T20I for India.

Concept note-2: -Among these are records for most ODI and Test appearances-Tendulkar played just one T20 international for India and that was before the 2007 T20 World Cup itself. He sits atop the ODI list with 463 appearances and is the only player to have played 200 Test matches.

Concept note-3: -That remains his only T20I appearance as he opted out of the format in 2007 along with others as they felt this new format is for the youngsters to make their entry into international cricket while they focused on the other two formats.

Concept note-4: -The aforementioned game marked Sachin’s debut and final T20 international outing for India. He made the decision to forego the 2007 World T20 and subsequent T20I matches in order to allow younger players to participate in the said format.
