Sadbhavana Diwas or Harmony Day is celebrated on which date?

(A) .August 18

(B) .August 12

(C) ** .August 20

(D) .August 24

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is ‘Sadbhavana Divas. ‘ 20th August is the birth anniversary of former Prime Minister ‘Rajiv Gandhi’. Sadbhavana Diwas is celebrated on 20th August to appreciate his efforts in Indian development.

Concept note-2: -It is given on 20 August, the birth anniversary of Rajiv Gandhi, which is celebrated as Sadbhavna Diwas (Harmony Day).

Concept note-3: -Our nation will celebrate Sadbhavana Diwas 2023 to commemorate Rajiv Gandhi’s 78th birth anniversary this year. As a mark of respect to the late former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi, India observes Sadbhavana Diwas on August 20 every year.

Concept note-4: -The theme of Sadbhavana is to promote National Integration and Communal Harmony among people of all religions, languages and regions. The idea behind observance of ‘Sadbhavana Diwas’ is to eschew violence and to promote goodwill among the people. ceremony may be observed on 19th August, 2022.
