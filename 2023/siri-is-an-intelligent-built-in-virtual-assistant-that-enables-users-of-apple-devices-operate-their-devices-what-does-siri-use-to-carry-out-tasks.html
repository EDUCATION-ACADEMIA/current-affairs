Siri is an intelligent built-in virtual assistant that enables users of Apple devices operate their devices. What does Siri use to carry out tasks?

(A) Initiative

(B) ** Voice commands

(C) Internet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It uses voice queries, gesture based control, focus-tracking and a natural-language user interface to answer questions, make recommendations, and perform actions by delegating requests to a set of Internet services.

Concept note-2: -The “Hey Siri” detector uses a Deep Neural Network (DNN) to convert the acoustic pattern of your voice at each instant into a probability distribution over speech sounds.

Concept note-3: -Weak AI, also known as narrow AI, is an AI system that is designed and trained to complete a specific task. Industrial robots and virtual personal assistants, such as Apple’s Siri, use weak AI.

Concept note-4: -Siri is Apple’s virtual assistant for iOS, macOS, tvOS and watchOS devices that uses voice recognition and is powered by artificial intelligence (AI).

Concept note-5: -Siri was coded in Python. It was originally built by SRI, hence the name, and its knowledge base was called SPARK.
