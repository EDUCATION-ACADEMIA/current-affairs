STIP is associated with which field?

(A) Epidemiology

(B) ** Science and Technology

(C) Statistics and Economics

(D) None of The Above-It is Arts and Culture

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: The Department of Science and Technology (DST), Union Ministry of Science and Technology has released the draft Science, Technology and Innovation Policy (STIP). It includes nudge for self-reliance in technology, enhanced funding for science, technology and innovation via state budget.

Concept note-2: -Investment in science, technology and innovation (STI) is essential for economic development and social progress. Research and development (R&D) can foster sustainable development by building greener, more inclusive societies.

Concept note-3: -The policy proposes creating a National STI Observatory that will act as a central repository for all kinds of data related to and generated from the STI ecosystem. Further, an STI Development Bank will be set up to facilitate a corpus fund for investing in direct long term investments in select strategic areas.

Concept note-4: -Creating, collecting, and sharing STI through a robust, collaborative, and innovative Program forged by all Departmental elements to facilitate access to STI and allow maximum use of the information resulting from DOE research and other technological activities.

Concept note-5: -Science, Technology and Innovation Policy (STIP 2020)
