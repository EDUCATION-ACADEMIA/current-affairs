Technology platform Champion was launched by ____

(A) The Governor

(B) The President

(C) ** PM Mr. Narendra Modi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PM Modi Launches CHAMPIONS: Technology Platform to empower MSMEs.

Concept note-2: -You are aware that Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi launched the technology platform CHAMPIONS ( https://champions.gov.in ) which stands for Creation and Harmonious Application of Modern Processes for Increasing the Output and National Strength.

Concept note-3: -MSME-Champions is a technology-driven centralized control room, grievance management, and information platform launched by the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) under Government of India. The Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi launched the Champions portal on 9 May 2020.

Concept note-4: -The Government of India’s Ministry of Micro Small and Medium Enterprises has introduced the CHAMPIONS portal, www.Champions.gov.in, a technology driven control room cum management information system, as part of a significant effort.
