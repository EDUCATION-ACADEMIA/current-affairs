(A) ** New York Times, Wall Street Journal

(B) New York Times, Chicago Tribune

(C) Houston Chronicle, Chron

(D) Wall Street Journal, Houston Chronicle

(E) Los Angeles Times, New York Post

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The NY Times is focused on New York City. The Wall Street Journal is a national paper. Was this worth your time?

Concept note-2: -The most ambitious people in the world read The Wall Street Journal. Winner of 38 Pulitzer Prizes for outstanding journalism, the Journal includes coverage of U.S. and world news, politics, arts, culture, lifestyle, sports, health and more.

Concept note-3: -The New York Times has won 132 Pulitzer Prizes. It won its first award in 1918, and has since won more Pulitzer prizes than any other organization.

Concept note-4: -The Journal is regarded as a newspaper of record, particularly in terms of business and financial news. The newspaper has won 38 Pulitzer Prizes, the most recent in 2019. New York, New York 10036, U.S.
