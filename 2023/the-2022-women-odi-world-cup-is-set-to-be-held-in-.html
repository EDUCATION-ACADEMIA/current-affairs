The 2022 Women ODI World Cup is set to be held in ____

(A) ** New Zealand

(B) South Africa

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 15 December 2021, the International Cricket Council (ICC) announced that the tournament would start on 4 March 2022, with the final scheduled for 3 April 2022. New Zealand qualified automatically as hosts, with all other qualification places determined by the ICC ODI Rankings.

Concept note-2: -Where is Australia vs. Pakistan women’s ODI series being held? Allan Border Field in Brisbane will host the opening two matches of the January ODI series. Named after the legendary Australian cricketer, it is located just north of the Queensland capital’s CBD and has an approximate capacity of 6, 500.

Concept note-3: -ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2023: Latest results and fixtures from tournament in South Africa. The latest results and upcoming fixtures from the 2023 Women’s T20 World Cup in South Africa, with all matches live on Sky Sports.

Concept note-4: -In August 2022, the ICC announced that 3 venues in three cities would host matches. The venues were Newlands Cricket Ground, St George’s Park and Boland Park.
