The 2023 Men’s Boxing World Championship to be held in which city?

(A) Beijing

(B) Kathmandu

(C) ** Tashkent

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The marquee event, which will be held at the Uzbek capital from May 1 to 14 next year, will see the prize money double from the previous edition. “For the first time in history, we will have a record prize money fund, USD 5.2 million.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. correct answer is Uzbekistan. The 2023 Men’s Boxing World Championships will be held in Uzbekistan capital Tashkent. International Boxing Association (AIBA) President Umar Kremlev has confirmed Tashkent as the host city.

Concept note-3: -President of the International Boxing Association Mr. Umar Kremlev during his visit to Uzbekistan officially announced that AIBA Men’s World Boxing Championships will take place in Tashkent in 2023.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is India.
