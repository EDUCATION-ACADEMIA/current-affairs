The Airports Authority of India (AAI) signed an MoU) with which public sector undertaking to collaborate and support the emerging global opportunities in the airport business?

(A) ** Bharat Electronics Ltd

(B) Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited

(C) NTPC Limited

(D) Engineers India Limited

(E) Hindustan Aeronautics Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -AAI has been constituted under an Act of Parliament, namely, the Airports Authority of India Act, 1994 and has been constituted as a Statutory Body.

Concept note-2: -The Airports Authority of India, or AAI, is a public sector enterprise under the ownership of the Ministry of Civil Aviation, Government of India.

Concept note-3: -Airports Authority of India (AAI) is responsible for creation development and management of Air Cargo Terminals for processing of international air cargo (export, import, transshipment and courier consignment) at international and domestic airports in India.
