The Best strategy to use while teaching Current Affairs is

(A) Conduct Quiz Competition.

(B) Round Table Discussion.

(C) Use Bulletin board

(D) ** All the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Student Centred Inquiry This strategy is a great way to generate curiosity among young learners and engage an inactive class. Through such an approach, children develop skills of researching, co-relating, and reflecting on information through independent exploration and engagement with the content.

Concept note-2: -According to the results of this study, the best teaching approach is the mixed method (student-centered together with teacher-centered) plus educational planning and previous readiness.
