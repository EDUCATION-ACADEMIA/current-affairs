The birth anniversary of which freedom fighter is to be celebrated as “Parakram Diwas” every year?

(A) Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

(B) ** Subhash Chandra Bose

(C) VD Savarkar

(D) Bal Gangadhar Tilak

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A grateful nation remembers the great freedom fighter Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose on his birth anniversary today, January 23. It was on this day in 1897, that Neta Ji was born in Cuttack, Odisha. The day is celebrated as Parakram Diwas.

Concept note-2: -Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose Jayanti or Netaji Jayanti is a national event celebrated in India as Parakram Diwas on January 23 to mark the birth of prominent Indian freedom fighter Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose. This year nation celebrates 126th birth anniversary of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose.

Concept note-3: -"Parakram Diwas” is celebrated with various events and programs across the country. A national level function is organised in Kolkata, the birthplace of Bose, on this day every year. The move to celebrate Bose’s birth anniversary as “Parakram Diwas” has been widely welcomed by the people of India.

Concept note-4: -Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Biography : Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose was born in Cuttack on January 23, 1897, which is Subhash Chandra Bose’s birthday.
