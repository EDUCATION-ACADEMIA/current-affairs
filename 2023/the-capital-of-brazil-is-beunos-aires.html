The capital of Brazil is Beunos Aires

(A) true

(B) ** false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is situated on the south east coast of the south America continent. Buenos Aires is not the capital of Brazil. This is an incorrect option.

Concept note-2: -The current capital of Brazil, since its construction in 1960, is Brasília.

Concept note-3: -Brasília, city, federal capital of Brazil. It is located in the Federal District (Distrito Federal) carved out of Goiás state on the central plateau of Brazil. At an elevation of some 3, 500 feet (1, 100 metres), it lies between the headwaters of the Tocantins, Paraná, and São Francisco rivers.

Concept note-4: -Buenos Aires is a city located in the state of Pernambuco, Brazil. Located 78 km from the state capital of Recife, it has an estimated (IBGE 2020) population of 13, 190 people.

Concept note-5: -History. One of the country’s oldest cities, Salvador was founded in 1549 as the capital of the Portuguese colony of Brazil by Tomé de Sousa, the first governor-general.
