The COMELEC extends voter registration on this date.

(A) October 8-11

(B) October 8-30

(C) October 11-15

(D) ** October 11-30

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pursuant to provisions of COMELEC Resolutions No. 10755 and No. 10743, the AFP, PNP, and PCG shall provide security to polling places, including election materials that will be used in connection with the elections.

Concept note-2: -10724, to prevent disenfranchisement of voters in the country, Project of Precincts (POPs) are established across regions, with which the purpose is to provide correct names and addresses of voting centers so that registered voters can freely, conveniently and safely exercise their right to vote on election day.

Concept note-3: -The number of voters in the Philippines was posted at 43, 331, 229 persons or 56.64 percent of the total population.

Concept note-4: -The Commission on Elections (COMELEC) is the premier guardian of the ballot. Its principal role is to enforce and administer all laws and regulations relative to the conduct of elections, plebiscites, initiatives, referendums and recalls.
