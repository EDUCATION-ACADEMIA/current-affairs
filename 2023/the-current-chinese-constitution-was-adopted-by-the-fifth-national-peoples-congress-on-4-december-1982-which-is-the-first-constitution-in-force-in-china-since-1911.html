The current Chinese constitution was adopted by the Fifth National People’s Congress on 4 December 1982, which is the first constitution in force in China since 1911?

(A) 9 th

(B) 10 m

(C) 11 m

(D) ** 12 m

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Article 1 of the Constitution describes China as “a socialist state under the people’s democratic dictatorship” meaning that the system is based on an alliance of the working classes-in communist terminology, the workers and peasants-and is led by the Chinese Communist Party (CCP), the vanguard of the working class.

Concept note-2: -The current PRC Constitution was enacted in 1982. Prior to the 2018 amendment, the 1982 Constitution was amended four times-in 1988, 1993, 1999, and 2004.

Concept note-3: -Article 85 The State Council of the People’s Republic of China, namely, the Central People’s Government, is the executive organ of the highest state organ of power; it is the highest state administrative organ. a secretary general. The State Council shall practice a premier responsibility system.

Concept note-4: -The Constitution of the Republic of China is the fifth and current constitution of the Republic of China (ROC), ratified by the Kuomintang during the Constituent National Assembly session on 25 December 1946, in Nanjing, and adopted on 25 December 1947.
