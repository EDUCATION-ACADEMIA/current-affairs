The first Sainik school to admit girls is in which states?

(A) Assam

(B) ** Mizoram

(C) sikkim

(D) mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Malsawmtluangi, 15, is one of the first six girls ever to join Sainik Schools as cadets. Conceived in 1961 by VK Krishna Menon, then union defence minister, Sainik Schools were exclusively for boys until 2018 when the six joined the new Sainik School Chhingchhhip, Mizoram, as “pilot project”.

Concept note-2: -All 33 Sainik Schools have started admitting girls w.e.f. academic session 2021-22.

Concept note-3: -Gender-Girls and boys, both are eligible for class 6 admission at Sainik Schools. Age Limit-The age of a student should be between 10-12 years, as on March 31, 2023.

Concept note-4: -The Rashtriya Military schools are located at Chail (Himachal Pradesh), Ajmer (Rajasthan), Belgaum (Karnataka), Bengaluru (Karnataka) & Dholpur (Rajasthan). Around 30% of the available seats are also earmarked for wards of civilians.

Concept note-5: -Total No. of Sainik School In India Are 34. All Sainik School take 10 Admission for girls in Each School So Total vacancy of Girls In All Sainik School Are 340.
