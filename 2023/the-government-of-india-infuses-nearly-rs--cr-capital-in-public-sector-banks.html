The government of India infuses nearly Rs ____ cr capital in public sector banks.

(A) Rs. 5, 000

(B) Rs. 9, 000

(C) ** Rs. 11, 000

(D) Rs. 17, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Centre has announced to provide capital support to weak public sector lenders like the Central Bank of India and Punjab & Sind Bank. Such banks will get a big chunk of the ₹15, 000 crore earmarked for capital infusion in state-owned banks for the current fiscal.

Concept note-2: -Capital infusion. Often refers to the cross-subsidization of divisions within a firm. When one division is not doing well, it might benefit from an infusion of new funds from the more successful divisions.

Concept note-3: -The minimum net worth of such small finance banks shall be Rs. 100 crore from the date of commencement of business.
