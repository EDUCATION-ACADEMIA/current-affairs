The IDANRE HILLS are located in ____

(A) Osun State

(B) Ogun State

(C) ** Ondo State

(D) Oyo State

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Idanre hill is one of the most awesome and beautiful natural landscapes in Ondo State and Nigeria.

Concept note-2: -The ancient settlement of Idanre has existed on the hill since antiquity, however Western civilization was introduced to the ancient city when a team of missionaries led by Rev. Gilbert Carter arrived in the year 1894.

Concept note-3: -Idanre is a local government area in Ondo state with headquarters in Owena. The town which is located at the foot of the scenic Idanre hill which is of unique cultural and its environmental significance attracts many tourists.

Concept note-4: -Ondo is Nigeria’s chief cocoa-producing state. Other crops include rice, yams, corn (maize), coffee, taro, cassava (manioc), vegetables, and fruits. Traditional industries include pottery making, cloth weaving, tailoring, carpentry, and blacksmithing.
