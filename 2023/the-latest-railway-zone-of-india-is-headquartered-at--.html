The latest Railway zone of India is Headquartered at ____ ?

(A) Jalandhar

(B) ** Visakhapatnam

(C) Guwahati

(D) Lucknow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The headquarters for the South Coast Railway (SCoR) Zone will be built at the scenic Mudasarlova area in Visakhapatnam, say railway officials.

Concept note-2: -The headquarters of Indian Railway is situated in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -South Coast Railway Zone (SCoR) has been announced as the newest railway zone of the Indian Railways and is headquartered at Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-4: -The South Coast Railway zone is the 18th railway zone of the Indian railway which was introduced in the year 2019 by Piyush Goyal, the railway minister during the period. The zone has Guntur, Guntakal, Vijayawada, and Waltair divisions under it and is headquartered in Visakhapatnam.
