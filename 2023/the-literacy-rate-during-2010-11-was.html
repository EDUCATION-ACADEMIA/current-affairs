The literacy rate during 2010-11 was

(A) 64%

(B) ** 74%

(C) 70%

(D) 80%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As per the census of 2011, the literacy rate of India is 74.04%. Literacy rate among the males is 82.14% and among females is 65.46%.

Concept note-2: -As per the census of 2011, the literacy rate in the country is 74.04 per cent. The literacy rates based on gender is 82.14 percent for males and 65.46 percent for females.

Concept note-3: -Census of India pegged the average literacy rate to be 73% in 2011 while National Statistical Commission surveyed literacy to be 77.7% in 2017–18. Literacy rate in urban areas was 87.7%, higher than rural areas with 73.5%.

Concept note-4: -The literacy rate went up from 64.83 per cent in 2001 to 74.04 per cent in 2011 showing an increase of 9.21 per cent. Interestingly, females literacy level saw a significant jump as compared to males between 2001-2011.

Concept note-5: -Literacy rate is the literate population of the age group 7 years and above divided by the total population of the same age group.
