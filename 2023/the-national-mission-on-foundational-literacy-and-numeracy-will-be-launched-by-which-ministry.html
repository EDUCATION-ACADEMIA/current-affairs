The National mission on foundational literacy and numeracy will be launched by which ministry?

(A) Ministry of Home Affairs

(B) ** Ministry of Human Resource Development

(C) Ministry of Tribal Affairs

(D) Ministry of Commerce and Industry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Education has launched NIPUN (National Initiative for Proficiency in Reading with Understanding and Numeracy) Bharat Scheme.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Education has launched a National Initiative for Proficiency in Reading with Understanding and Numeracy (NIPUN Bharat), for ensuring that every child in the country necessarily attains foundational literacy and numeracy (FLN) by the end of Grade 3, by 2026-27.

Concept note-3: -Flickr. Union Minister for Education and Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Dharmendra Pradhan launched the new Ramakrishna Mission’s ‘Awakening’ Programme for students studying in classes 1 to 5.

Concept note-4: -The vision of the Mission is to create an enabling environment to ensure universal acquisition of foundational literacy and numeracy, so that by 2026-27 every child achieves the desired learning competencies in reading, writing and numeracy at the end of Grade III and not later than Grade V.
