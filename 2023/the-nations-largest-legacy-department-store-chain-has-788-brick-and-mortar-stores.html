The nation’s largest legacy department store chain has 788 brick-and-mortar stores.

(A) JCPenney

(B) ** Macy’s

(C) Kohl’s

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World’s Largest Store: Macy’s Herald Square In addition to shoes and clothing, Macy’s has a wide variety of housewares, gifts and furniture in select stores.

Concept note-2: -Dollar General, Target and Bath & Body Works are the largest department stores in the United States in 2023 based on the number of locations. Dollar General has the most number of locations with 18, 904 locations across 47 states and territories. Target has 1, 948 and Bath & Body Works has 1, 694 locations in the US.

Concept note-3: -Macy’s Herald Square is one of the largest department stores in the world. The flagship store covers almost an entire New York City block, features about 1.1 million square feet of retail space, includes additional space for offices and storage, and serves as the endpoint for the Thanksgiving Day parade.

Concept note-4: -Not only is Macy’s Herald Square the largest department store in New York, but it’s one of the largest stores in the entire world. It takes up an entire city block, and people come from all over the world to visit it. Read on to learn more about this gigantic store.
