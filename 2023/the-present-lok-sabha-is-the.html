The present Lok Sabha is the

(A) 13th Lok Sabha

(B) 14th Lok Sabha

(C) 15th Lok Sabha

(D) ** 16th Lok Sabha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Bharatiya Janata Party (of the NDA) achieved an absolute majority with 282 seats out of 543, 166 seats more than in the previous 15th Lok Sabha. Its PM candidate Narendra Modi took office on 26 May 2014 as the 14th prime minister of India. The first session was convened from 4 to 11 June 2014.

Concept note-2: -The 17th Lok Sabha was formed by the members elected in the 2019 Indian general election.

Concept note-3: -This is a List of members of the 17th Lok Sabha (17 June 2019 – 16 June 2024) arranged by state-wise & union territory-wise representation in Lok Sabha.

Concept note-4: -General elections are expected to be held in India by May 2024 to elect the members of the 18th Lok Sabha.

Concept note-5: -Currently, the house has 543 seats which are made up by the election of up to 543 elected members and at a maximum.
