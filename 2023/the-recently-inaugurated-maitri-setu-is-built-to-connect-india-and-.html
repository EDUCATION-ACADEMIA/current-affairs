The recently inaugurated Maitri Setu is built to connect India and ____

(A) Nepal

(B) Bhutan

(C) ** Bangladesh

(D) Myanmar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The bridge was inaugurated on 9 March 2021 by the Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Bangladesh Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina via video conference. The bridge was named “Maitri Setu” symbolizing the growing friendship and bilateral ties between India and Bangladesh.

Concept note-2: -The name ‘Maitri Setu’ symbolizes growing bilateral relations and friendly ties between India and Bangladesh. The construction was taken up by the National Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Ltd at a project cost of Rs. 133 Crores.

Concept note-3: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated ‘Maitri Setu’ between India and Bangladesh today through a video conference. He also inaugurated and laid the foundation stone of multiple infrastructure projects in Tripura. The Governor and the Chief Minister of Tripura were present.

Concept note-4: -1.9 km long Maitri Setu connecting Tripura and Bangladesh, over river Feni, has been built. Choose the correct statement. The bridge is between Sabroom in Tripura and Ramgarh in Bangladesh. The bridge is between Ramgarh in Tripura and Sabroom in Bangladesh.

Concept note-5: -1) Subhas Bridge: It is situated at Agartala. 2) Chandramohan Setu: This bridge is situated at Pratagarh, Agartala. 3) Sudhanya Debbarman Bridge: The bridge is siutatued at Jogendranagar, Agartala. 4) Jawahar Bridge (Oldest Bridge): It is situated at Agartala.
