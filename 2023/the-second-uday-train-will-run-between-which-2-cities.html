The second UDAY train will run between which 2 cities?

(A) Mumbai to Thane

(B) ** Vishakapatnam to Vijayawada

(C) Delhi to Chandigarh

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Railways’ second double-decker Uday Express will run between Visakhapatnam and Vijayawada to cater to the busy route in the East Coast Railway zone, officials said on Monday.

Concept note-2: -Visakhapatnam to Vijayawada distance is 350 km by train. The average travel duration on this route is 4 hr 53 mins.

Concept note-3: -There are two Uday Express trains operating in India: 22665/22666 Bangalore City – Coimbatore Uday Express. 22701/22702 Visakhapatnam–Vijayawada Uday Express.

Concept note-4: -The UDAY (Utkrisht Double Decker Air-Conditioned Yatri) Express is equipped with a host of new features such as infotainment system, with wi-fi, GPS-based passenger information system, food vending machines and an exclusive dining area for passengers.

Concept note-5: -The Howrah–Dhanbad Double Decker Express was a Superfast train of Indian Railways, which was the first of its type to be introduced. It was accommodated with latest stainless steel LHB coach, manufactured at Kapurthala Coach Factory.
