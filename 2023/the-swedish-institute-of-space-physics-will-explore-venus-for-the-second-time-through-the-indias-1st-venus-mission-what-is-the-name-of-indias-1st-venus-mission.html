The Swedish Institute of Space Physics will explore Venus for the second time through the India’s 1st Venus Mission. What is the name of India’s 1st Venus Mission?

(A) ** Shukrayaan-1

(B) Venus-1

(C) Shukra-1

(D) Venusyaan-1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With a scientific instrument to examine the planet, Sweden is joining India’s Venus orbiter mission, “Shukrayaan.”

Concept note-2: -ISRO’s Venus mission, called Shukrayaan I, was expected to be launched in December 2024.

Concept note-3: -NASA’s Mariner 2 was the first spacecraft to visit any planet beyond Earth when it flew past Venus on Dec. 14, 1962. Data gathered in a 42-minute scan forever changed how we see Earth’s closest neighbor.

Concept note-4: -Shukrayaan I, the ISRO Venus mission, was scheduled to launch in December 2024. The concept was conceived in 2012; five years later, after the Department of Space got a 23% increase in the 2017–2018 budget, ISRO started preliminary investigations.
