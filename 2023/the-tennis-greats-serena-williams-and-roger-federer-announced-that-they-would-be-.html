The tennis greats Serena Williams and Roger Federer announced that they would be ____

(A) ** retiring from the sport

(B) starring in a new rom-com, “Tennis, Anyone?”

(C) the new celebrity representatives for Chanel

(D) writing a self-help book together

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Crucial Quote. Williams said she “didn’t even think” about retirement when starting her company and just “jumped right” into it. “I still haven’t really thought about it, ” she added.

Concept note-2: -Roger Federer announces retirement from professional tennis – DW – 09/15/2022.

Concept note-3: -“I am not retired, ” Williams said at a conference in San Francisco while promoting her investment company, Serena Ventures.

Concept note-4: -As a testament to his career longevity and durability, Federer amazingly never retired during 1, 526 singles matches and 223 doubles matches. (He conceded three singles walkovers, all due to back injury).
