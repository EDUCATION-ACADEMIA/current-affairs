The World Day of War Orphans was observed every year on ____

(A) October 6

(B) ** January 6

(C) August 22

(D) July 13

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Day of War Orphans is observed on January 6 every year with an aim to raise awareness about children orphaned in wars. These children are subjected to hardships that are more than just physical neglect after losing their caregivers.

Concept note-2: -Every year on January 6th, the World Day of War Orphans is commemorated.

Concept note-3: -World Day of War Orphans, Jan 6, started by French organisation SOS Enfants en Détresses, aims to create awareness about children affected by conflict.

Concept note-4: -The topic of the day in 2022 was “Care for children in a horrible situation is an obligation, especially in the face of a pandemic.” The theme for the Year 2021 focused on the need to increase awareness of children who had become orphaned as a result of the conflict.

Concept note-5: -World Day of War Orphans is a day dedicated to acknowledging the hardships that children who lost their parents to wars face. In 2015, there were 140 million orphaned children who lost their parents to wars.
