The world’s smallest gold coin, which features Albert Einstein, is recently released by which country?

(A) USA

(B) ** Switzerland

(C) Germany

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. The world’s smallest gold coin, which features Albert Einstein, is recently released by which country? Notes: Switzerland has recently minted the world’s smallest gold coin, which bears the famous picture of the great Scientist Albert Einstein with his tongue out.

Concept note-2: -Switzerland has minted the 0.12-inch gold coin, which is the smallest in the world.

Concept note-3: -To proudly commemorate this currency change, the Croatian Mint decided to create the world’s smallest coin. And it succeeded. The coin has a diameter of 1.99 mm and a weight of 0.05 g. The theme fits: the 1-kuna coin is dedicated to Hum, which is referred to as the world’s smallest city.”

Concept note-4: -Hum, known as the smallest city in the world, is represented on the smallest gold coin in the world, issued by the Croatian National Bank and minted by the Croatian Mint. The 2022 . 9999 fine gold 1-kuna coin measures 1.99 millimeters in diameter and weighs 0.05 gram.

Concept note-5: -Based on landmass, Vatican City is the smallest country in the world, measuring just 0.2 square miles, almost 120 times smaller than the island of Manhattan.
