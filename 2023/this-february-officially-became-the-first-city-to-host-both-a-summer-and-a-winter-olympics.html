(A) Tokyo

(B) Milan

(C) ** Beijing

Concept note-1: -Beijing the first city in the world to host both Winter and Summer Olympics. With the inauguration of the 24th Winter Olympics 2022 in Beijing by the Chinese President Xi Jingping on 4 February, Beijing became the only city in the world to host Summer and Winter Olympics . It hosted the 29th Summer Olympics 2008.

Concept note-2: -In 2022, Beijing became the first city that has held both the summer and the winter Olympic Games.

Concept note-3: -Beijing made history in 2022 when it hosted both the summer and winter Olympic Games. There are 10 Olympics Host Cities, which have hosted the Olympics twice so far. Those ten Olympics Hosted Cities are Athens, Paris, London, St. Moritz, Lake Placid, Los Angeles, Cortina d’Ampezzo, Innsbruck, Tokyo and Beijing.

Concept note-4: -Beijing is the first city to host both the Summer and Winter Olympics. WRAL meteorologist Elizabeth Gardner says its all in the coordinates.
