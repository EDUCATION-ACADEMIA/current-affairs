Under which act, government has formed Inter-Ministerial Co-ordination Committee (IMCC) headed by Mr Ajay Bhushan Pandey to check money laundering activities?

(A) Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA)2005

(B) Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA) 2006

(C) Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA) 2003

(D) ** Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA), 2002

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The government has set up the IMCC using its powers conferred under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002.

Concept note-2: -Secretaries of Department of Economic Affairs, Department of Financial Services, Ministry of Corporate Affairs, and Ministry of External Affairs. Chairman SEBI.

Concept note-3: -In other words, Money Laundering refers to converting illegal earned money into legitimate money. The main objectives of the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002 is to prevent money laundering as well as to provide for confiscation of property either derived from or involved in, money-laundering.
