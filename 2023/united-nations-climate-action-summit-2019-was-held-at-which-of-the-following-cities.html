United Nations Climate Action Summit 2019 was held at which of the following cities?

(A) Paris

(B) Geneva

(C) Doha

(D) ** New York

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2019 UN Climate Action Summit was held at the headquarters of the United Nations in New York City on 23 September 2019. The UN 2019 Climate Summit convened on the theme, “Climate Action Summit 2019: A Race We Can Win.

Concept note-2: -The 2019 United Nations Climate Change Conference, also known as COP25, was the 25th United Nations Climate Change conference. It was held in Madrid, Spain, from 2 to 13 December 2019 under the presidency of the Chilean government.

Concept note-3: -The 2021 United Nations Climate Change Conference, also known as COP26, is the 26th United Nations Climate Change conference. It is scheduled to be held in the city of Glasgow, Scotland, between 31 October and 12 November 2021, under the co-presidency of the United Kingdom and Italy.

Concept note-4: -United Nations Conference on Environment and Development.
