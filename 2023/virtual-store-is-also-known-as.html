Virtual store is also known as

(A) ** online store

(B) internet market

(C) conventional store

(D) electronic market

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An online store may also be called an e-web-store, e-shop, e-store, Internet shop, web-shop, web-store, online store, online storefront and virtual store. Mobile commerce (or m-commerce) describes purchasing from an online retailer’s mobile device-optimized website or software application ("app").

Concept note-2: -A virtual store is a new type of digital shopping experience that provides a much higher level of engagement and immersion for the customer. At the simplest level, virtual stores are 3D, 360 full-page visual experiences that live on a brand’s e-commerce site.

Concept note-3: -The term virtual shopping refers to an e-commerce service that allows customers to browse and purchase items online as they normally would in-store. In simple terms, it’s all about using technology to bring the human element to e-commerce.

Concept note-4: -‍An online store is a website where customers may visit and purchase the goods and services offered on that website as per their preference. Customers can search for the products they want and place orders by making payments for the same.
