Vistara inked code share agreement with which of the following company?

(A) Alliance Air

(B) GoAir

(C) SpiceJet

(D) ** Lufthansa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vistara inked code share agreement with which of the following company? Explanation: Full-service airline Vistara on December 8 said it has entered into a codeshare pact with Lufthansa. The two carriers already have an interline partnership.

Concept note-2: -Another Tata group airline, FSC Vistara has six codeshare agreements with Singapore Airlines, United Airlines, Lufthansa, British Airways, Air Canada and Japan Air Lines.

Concept note-3: -Some of the flights may be operated by Lufthansa’s codeshare carriers, including Lufthansa CityLine, Eurowings, Air Dolomiti, Austrian Airlines, Helvetic Airways, Air Nostrum, Air Baltic, City Jet and Eurowings Discover. Services that differ from those of Lufthansa operating flights may apply.
