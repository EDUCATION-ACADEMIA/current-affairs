What country’s Prime Minister resigned early in the month, and another was elected by the end of the month?

(A) France

(B) Germany

(C) ** Great Britain

(D) Ireland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In addition, a prime minister can resign from office; Morarji Desai was the first prime minister to resign while in office.

Concept note-2: -Rishi Sunak became Prime Minister on 25 October 2022. He was previously appointed Chancellor of the Exchequer from 13 February 2020 to 5 July 2022.

Concept note-3: -Margaret Hilda Thatcher, Baroness Thatcher, LG, OM, DStJ, PC, FRS, HonFRSC (née Roberts; 13 October 1925 – 8 April 2013) was a British politician and stateswoman who served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 1979 to 1990 and Leader of the Conservative Party from 1975 to 1990.

Concept note-4: -Notable lengths. Of the 56 past prime ministers, nine served more than 10 years while eight served less than a year.
