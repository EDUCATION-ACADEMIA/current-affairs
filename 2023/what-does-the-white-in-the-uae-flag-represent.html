What does the white in the UAE flag represent

(A) Unity

(B) Strength

(C) ** Peace and honesty

(D) Freedom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -White. The white strip represents peace and honesty. Some people interpret it as a symbol of cleanliness and purity.

Concept note-2: -Through history, white has stood for purity. It can represent peace and honesty, but also signify cleanliness. Historians tend to believe that the white in the UAE flag signifies peace and neutrality, serving as a counterpoint to the more militant red and black.

Concept note-3: -Colors and Symbolism of the United Arab Emirates Flag The red stripe represents energy and the sacrifices made by the people for their nation, the green stands for growth and prosperity, the black stands for dignity, and the white stripe represents peace and purity.

Concept note-4: -Colours in UAE Flag Green refers to goodness and agriculture, abundant throughout the country. White refers to peace. Black refers to courage and power. Red refers to to power and sovereignty.

Concept note-5: -The four colours used reflect the unity of the Arab nations. The Arabic poem of Safiul Din Al Holi describes the themes of benevolence, goodness, and kindness. Al Holi wrote about hard-fought historical battles of the land.
