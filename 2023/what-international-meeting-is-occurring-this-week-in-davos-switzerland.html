What international meeting is occurring this week in Davos, Switzerland?

(A) European Union Congress

(B) ** World Economic Forum

(C) International Monetary Fund Convention

(D) World Bank Summit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This week marks the World Economic Forum’s annual meeting in Davos, Switzerland. The event brings some of the planet’s most powerful people together to discuss global issues-but it’s an event that recently attracted criticism for being supposedly out of touch and irrelevant.

Concept note-2: -The 53rd edition of the annual World Economic Forum summit was held in Davos, Switzerland. Theme: Cooperation in a Fragmented World.

Concept note-3: -Event: World Economic Forum Annual Meeting 2022 | SDG Knowledge Hub | IISD. The 52nd World Economic Forum Annual Meeting will convene on the theme, ‘Working Together, Restoring Trust, ’ with the aim to address economic, environmental, political, and social fault-lines exacerbated by the COVID-19 pandemic.
