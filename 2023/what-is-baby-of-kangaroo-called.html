What is baby of kangaroo called?

(A) Kitten

(B) Colt

(C) ** Joey

(D) Cub

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The baby kangaroo, called a joey, spends about 235 days in the mother’s pouch. Australia has about 120 species of marsupials, New Guinea has 53 species of marsupials, South and Central America have 90 species of marsupials, and North America has only two species of marsupials.

Concept note-2: -Question: Why is a baby kangaroo called a joey? Answer: Originates in the aborigine language and joey means ‘small animal’. Joey is the collective norm for any small animals.

Concept note-3: -Female kangaroos sport a pouch on their belly, made by a fold in the skin, to cradle baby kangaroos called joeys. Newborn joeys are just one inch long (2.5 centimeters) at birth, or about the size of a grape.
