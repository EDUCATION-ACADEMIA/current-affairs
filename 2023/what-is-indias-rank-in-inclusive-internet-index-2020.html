What is India’s rank in Inclusive Internet Index 2020?

(A) 40

(B) ** 46

(C) 51

(D) 60

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India was ranked at 52nd on Inclusive Internet Index, 2020.

Concept note-2: -India ranks 49th in Inclusive Internet Index 2021 by Facebook-EIU.

Concept note-3: -The Inclusive Internet Index has been updated for 2022, with revised rankings and scores. The 100 countries in this year’s index represent 97% of global GDP and 99% of global population. The overall Index score based on the scores of the Availability, Affordability, Relevance and Readiness categories.

Concept note-4: -The index seeks to measure the extent to which the internet is not only accessible and affordable, but also relevant to all, allowing usage that enables positive social and economic outcomes.
