What is the full meaning of FRSC?

(A) ** Federal road safety crops

(B) Federal road settlement country

(C) Federal road safety corps

(D) Federal right safety crops

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -FRSC is the abbreviation of Federal Road Safety Commission Nigeria or Federal Road Safety Corps, responsible for the administration of road transport safety throughout the country. FRSC operates in all states and is assigned the lead agency to administer and manage Nigerian road safety.

Concept note-2: -President Muhammadu Buhari has approved the appointment of Deputy Corps Marshal Dauda Ali Biu as the substantive Corps Marshal of the Federal Road Safety Corps (FRSC). Until this appointment, Dauda Ali Biu was the acting corps marshal of the Commission, a position he held since July, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The meaning of FRSC is Federal Road Safety Corps.

Concept note-4: -The commission has a sole responsibility of making sure that drivers and motorists and the members of the public are educated property on the use of Nigerian highways. This is because, careless use of roads can lead to some sort of problems on the highways, such as road accidents.
