What is the largest religion in the world in terms of membership?

(A) Judaism

(B) ** Christianity

(C) Islam

(D) Hinduism

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Of the world’s major religions, Christianity is the largest, with more than two billion followers. Christianity is based on the life and teachings of Jesus Christ and is approximately 2, 000 years old.

Concept note-2: -The United States has the largest Christian population in the world, followed by Brazil, Mexico, Russia, and the Philippines.

Concept note-3: -Globally, Muslims make up the second largest religious group, with 1.8 billion people, or 24% of the world’s population, followed by religious “nones” (16%), Hindus (15%) and Buddhists (7%). Adherents of folk religions, Jews and members of other religions make up smaller shares of the world’s people.
