What is the name of famous mathematician from Bihar who passed away recently?

(A) Basant Sagar

(B) ** Vashishtha Narayan Singh

(C) Harish Chandra

(D) Rahul Sachdeva

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Who was Vashishtha Narayan Singh? He was born on April 2, 1942, in a rural village Basantpur, Bihar. Professor John Kelly of the University of California recognized his talent. He moved to America in 1965.

Concept note-2: -Vashishtha Narayan Singh [was] a man from Bihar who challenged Einstein theory E = mc2 and also theory of Gauss. Many other sites make the same claim about Vashishth Narayan Singh.

Concept note-3: -Vashishtha Narayan Singh. Image credit: Twitter. After amassing a number of degrees, Singh’s started working as an assistant professor at the University of Washington. He also worked at NASA, during the Apollo missions in 1969 and it is said that he helped out with the mathematical calculations during the mission.
