What is the name of the application of the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) for FASTag users?

(A) India FASTag

(B) ** My FASTag

(C) Fast FASTag

(D) Naya FASTag

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Two mobile apps, FASTag Partner and MyFASTag have been launched by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI). Both these mobile applications will help users users to avail themselves of a FASTag for Electronic Toll Collection.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has recently updated its mobile application “My FASTag” with a new feature to check balance status.

Concept note-3: -Harit Path application The National Highways authority of India launched “Harit Path", a mobile application to monitor plantations along National Highways.

Concept note-4: -UPSC Mains Notes: Union Minister of Road Transport and Highways Nitin Gadkari has launched an application named “Harit Path”.
