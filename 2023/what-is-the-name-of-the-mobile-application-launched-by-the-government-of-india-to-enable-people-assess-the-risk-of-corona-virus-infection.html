What is the name of the mobile application launched by the Government of India, to enable people assess the risk of corona virus infection?

(A) India Fights Back

(B) Corona Fights

(C) ** Arogya Setu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The App, called ‘AarogyaSetu’ joins Digital India for the health and well-being of every Indian. It will enable people to assess themselves the risk of their catching the Corona Virus infection.

Concept note-2: -Aarogya Setu App : COVID-19 Tracker launched to alert you and keep you safe. Download now! The Government of India has launched Aarogya Setu, a mobile application aimed to connect health services and we the people of India in our combined fight against COVID-19.

Concept note-3: -Aarogya Setu application was launched by the Government of Indi on 2-April-2020, to aid the CO a VID-19 efforts of the Government. based contact tracing platform in the world with more than 19 Crore Users.

Concept note-4: -The App uses Bluetooth based contact tracing to identify potential users who may have come in close proximity (during the past couple of weeks) with a person who has tested positive for COVID-19.

Concept note-5: -It is a tracking app which uses the smartphone’s GPS and Bluetooth features to track COVID-19 cases.
