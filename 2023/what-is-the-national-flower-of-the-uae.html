What is the national flower of the UAE?

(A) ** Tribulus Omanese

(B) Palm tree

(C) Arrojadoa

(D) Acanthocalycium Ferrarii

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tribulus Omanense flower is a flower of the desert which is why it is a national flower of UAE; however, it is also found in colder environments at times. Tribulus consists of five petals. It has a beautiful yellow tone, blended in with green, adds to its beauty and elegance.

Concept note-2: -Meaning and Significance of Tribulus Omanense Tribulus flower is a countrywide flower of UAE and its vibrant shade every so often combined with green supply a splash of elation, ecstasy, bliss, and joy. It is a subtropical herb in North America with a yellow flower, it flourishes a prickly or spiny fruit.

Concept note-3: -The national emblem of UAE or the UAE Coat of Arms is represented by a falcon with a UAE flag at the heart of it. The Falcon represents the strength and heritage while the flag at the centre with the seven stars around it represents the unity of the seven emirates.

Concept note-4: -Tribulus omanense is the national flower of Dubai.

Concept note-5: -Tribulus terrestris (family Zygophyllaceae), commonly known as Gokshur or Gokharu or puncture vine, has been used for a long time in both the Indian and Chinese systems of medicine for treatment of various kinds of diseases.
