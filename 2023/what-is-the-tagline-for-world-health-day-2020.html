What is the tagline for World Health Day 2020?

(A) Support Health Care

(B) Contribute to Health Care

(C) Support Doctors and Health professionals

(D) ** Support nurses and midwives

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The tagline for World Health Day is: Support nurses and midwives. On World Health Day, we are launching the first ever State of the World’s Nursing Report 2020.

Concept note-2: -World Health Day 2022-Our planet, our health.

Concept note-3: -The theme for World Health Day 2020 was to support nurses and midwives. WHO chose the year 2020 as the “Year of the Nurse and Midwife” to honour their contribution in making the world a healthier place. Nurses and midwives play a key role in caring for people everywhere.

Concept note-4: -Each year for this date, a theme is chosen that highlights an area of priority concern for WHO. In the face of the current pandemic, a polluted planet, and an increasing incidence of diseases, the theme for World Health Day 2022 is Our Planet, Our Health.

Concept note-5: -WHO EMRO | International Year of the Nurse and Midwife | World Health Day 2020 | World Health Days.
