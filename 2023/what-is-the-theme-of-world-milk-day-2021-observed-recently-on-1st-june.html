What is the theme of World Milk Day 2021, observed recently on 1st June?

(A) Leaving No one Behind

(B) Milk For All

(C) ** Sustainability in the dairy sector

(D) Milk and SDG

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Milk Day is celebrated every year on 1 June. The theme of World Milk Day 2021 is “Sustainability in the dairy sector with messages on nutrition."

Concept note-2: -This year’s theme highlighted the work already being done to accelerate climate action and help reduce the dairy sector’s impact on the planet. Using the World Milk Day platform, we aimed to raise awareness of the messaging and action toward Dairy Net Zero.

Concept note-3: -World Milk Day is celebrated with different themes each year. In 2022, this day will follow the theme of achieving “Dairy Net Zero”. This means that the day aims to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by the dairy industry over the next 30 years.

Concept note-4: -June 1 was chosen as the date because many countries were already celebrating a milk day during that time of year. The Day provides an opportunity to focus attention on milk and raise awareness of dairy’s part in healthy diets, responsible food production, and supporting livelihoods and communities.

Concept note-5: -Slogan on World Milk Day 2022 Milk makes a complete diet. Make sure you include it in your life. Saying no to milk is saying no to nutrition. Enjoy it as butter or cheese or relish it as ice-cream or yogurt.
