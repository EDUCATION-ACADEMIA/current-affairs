what is your mother

(A) ** teacher

(B) driver

(C) conductor

(D) nurse

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1 & 2. It means in one class there are two teachers one Mother teacher and one Assistant teacher. Mother teacher teaches all subjects in the class.

Concept note-2: -Mother Teacher Approach is about a single teacher, sometimes aided by an assistant teacher or a support staff, who teaches all subjects for a specific grade level.

Concept note-3: -Mother is the first teacher of a child.

Concept note-4: -In short, a master teacher is one who has mastered the basics of teaching, one who goes above and beyond to ensure a positive learning experience for each student, and one who shares his or her knowledge with the broader learning community.

Concept note-5: -A teacher, also called a schoolteacher or formally an educator, is a person who helps students to acquire knowledge, competence, or virtue, via the practice of teaching.
