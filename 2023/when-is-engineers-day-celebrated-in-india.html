When is Engineer’s Day celebrated in India?

(A) 13 September

(B) 14 September

(C) ** 15 September

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Engineer’s Day is observed every year on September 15 on the birth anniversary of the legendary engineer Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya . The day is observed every year to pay tribute to Visvesvaraya and also to commemorate the contributions of the engineers to the nation.

Concept note-2: -National Engineers Day 2022: National Engineer’s Day is observed every year on September 15. The day is celebrated on the birth anniversary of engineer statesman Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya, a diwan of the former Mysore kingdom who is credited with pioneering engineering achievements.

Concept note-3: -Engineers Day in India The nation observes Engineers Day each year on 15th September. This day is celebrated to commemorate the achievements of Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya (Sir MV) on his birth anniversary. Sir MV was a civil engineer, statesman, and administrator. He was made the Diwan of Mysore from 1912 to 1918.

Concept note-4: -15th September: Engineer’s Day (India) In India, Engineer’s Day is honored annually on September 15. One of the important September days, this day celebrates the birth anniversary of Bharat Ratna Awardee Mokshagundam Vishweshvaraya.

Concept note-5: -Engineer’s Day in India-15 September Engineer’s Day is celebrated annually on 15 September. This day is observed every year to commemorate the birth anniversary of engineering pioneer and Bharat Ratna Awardee, sir Mokshagundam Vishweshvaraya.
