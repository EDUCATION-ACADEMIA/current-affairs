(A) 25th November

(B) 27th November

(C) ** 29th November

(D) 28th November

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The annual event, organized by the UN Department of Global Communication within the framework of its media program on the question of Palestine, aims to educate the public on the question of Palestine and support a peaceful settlement of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.
