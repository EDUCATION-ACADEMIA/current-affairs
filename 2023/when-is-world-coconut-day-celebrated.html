When is world coconut day celebrated?

(A) 1st September

(B) ** 2nd September

(C) 3rd september

(D) 4th september

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2009, the APCC decided to mark September 2 as World Coconut Day to promote awareness about the coconut fruit. Every year, September 2 is celebrated as World Coconut Day.

Concept note-2: -WORLD COCONUT DAY 2022: THEME Across several countries, this day is celebrated by raising awareness campaigns and technical sessions. These seminars are conducted to increase the production of coconuts and raise awareness regarding its use.

Concept note-3: -In the United States, National Coconut Day is observed on June 28th.

Concept note-4: -This special day was Initiated by the Asian and Pacific Coconut Community, headquartered in Jakarta, Jakarta. This day aims to raise awareness about the use and benefits of coconuts across various industries. The day is about coconut as a nutritious fruit, a crucial raw material and a significant crop.
