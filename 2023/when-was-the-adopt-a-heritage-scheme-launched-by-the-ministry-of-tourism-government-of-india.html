When was the Adopt a Heritage Scheme launched by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India?

(A) ** 27 September, 2017

(B) November 27, 2017

(C) March 27, 2017

(D) March 27, 2018

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is Adopt a Heritage Scheme? The President of India launched the scheme on World Tourism Day, i.e., 27 September 2017.

Concept note-2: -When was the ‘Adopt a Heritage’ project launched? The project Adopt a Heritage: ‘Apni Dharohar Apni Pehchaan’ was launched by the Hon’ble President of India on 27th September 2017 on the occasion of World Tourism Day.

Concept note-3: -Why was Adopt a Heritage Scheme launched? Ans. The Ministry of Tourism, Government of India launched the “Adopt a Heritage: Apni Dharohar, Apni Pehchaan” project to develop tourism amenities at heritage/ natural/ tourist sites spread across India for making them tourist-friendly.

Concept note-4: -Heritage tourism involves visiting historical or industrial sites that may include old canals, railways, battlegrounds, military sites, etc. The overall purpose is to gain an appreciation of the past.
