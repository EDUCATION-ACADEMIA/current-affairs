When will Rafale will arrive in India?

(A) 5 August 2020

(B) ** 29 July 2020

(C) 31 July

(D) 3 August 2020

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first batch of five Rafale jets flew out of France and will arrive in India on Wednesday. The fighter jets will officially be inducted and join the Indian Air Force fleet in Haryana’s Ambala on July 29th.

Concept note-2: -The 36th aircraft was provided to the Indian side by France with all its spares and other parts replaced as it was being used for developmental activities. The first batch of Rafale jets arrived in India on July 29, 2020.

Concept note-3: -The last Rafale jet from France, that was part of the 36-aircraft deal between the two countries, will arrive in India by December 15. By Manjeet Negi: India will receive the last Rafale fighter jet from France by December 15, completing the delivery of all the 36 aircraft as part of the deal between the two countries.

Concept note-4: -India has received all 36 Rafale aircraft from France, with the last aircraft landing in the country today. According to the Indian Air Force, the aircraft arrived after a quick en-route mid-air refuelling from a UAE Air Force tanker aircraft, after taking off from France.

Concept note-5: -Final batch of 36 Rafale aircrafts land in India.
