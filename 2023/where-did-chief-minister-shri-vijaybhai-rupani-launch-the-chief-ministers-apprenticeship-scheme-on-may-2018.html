Where did Chief Minister Shri Vijaybhai Rupani launch the ’Chief Minister’s Apprenticeship Scheme’ on May 2018?

(A) Ahmedabad

(B) Gandhinagar

(C) ** Bharuch

(D) Surat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mukhyamantri Apprenticeship Scheme by Government of Gujarat. To achieve this target, Government of India, through National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) has incentivised employers employing apprentices.

Concept note-2: -Launch details Nitin Patel is the Deputy CM of Gujarat, and he announced that the state government was all set to implement the Mukhyamantri Apprenticeship Scheme. It was done on the 20th of February, 2018.

Concept note-3: -Rupani also digitally launched ‘Anubandham’ portal and Mobile Application to fulfil huge opportunity to provide jobs to youth at 50-odd Job Fairs digitally launched from Surat at municipal corporations and districts in the state.
