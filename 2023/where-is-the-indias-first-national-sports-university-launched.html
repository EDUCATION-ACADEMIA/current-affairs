Where is the India’s first National Sports University launched?

(A) ** Manipur

(B) Assam

(C) Meghalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. India’s first national sports university will come up in which state? Notes: The Union Cabinet, chaired by PM Narendra Modi, has approved an ordinance to set up the India’s first national sports university in Imphal, Manipur.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. National Sports University is a sports university in Manipur in India. Prime Minister Narendra Modi has laid the foundation stone for National Sports University on 16 March 2018. It is the first, and as of 2019 the only, central university in India focusing on sports education.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution: National Sports Universityis asports universityin Manipur in India. Prime Minister Narendra Modi has laid the foundation stone forNational Sports Universityon 16 March 2018. It is thefirst, and as of 2019 the only, central universityin India focusing onsportseducation.

Concept note-4: -The National Sports University is presently located in the Khuman Lampak Sports Complex, Imphal in an area of 168 acres. The Khuman Lampak Sports Complex is situated in the heart of Imphal and it was the main venue of the Vth National Games held in February, 1999.
