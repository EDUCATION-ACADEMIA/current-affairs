Where is the Indo-South Korean Friendship Park built?

(A) Chennai

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Beijing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Where is the Indo-South Korean friendship park constructed? Notes: The Indo-South Korean friendship park has been constructed in New Delhi – Cantonment. It has been built to commemorate the contribution of Indian peacekeeping force during the Korean war of 1950-53.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Delhi.
