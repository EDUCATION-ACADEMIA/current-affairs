Where is WHO headquarter situated

(A) Rome

(B) Newyork

(C) ** Geneva

(D) Washington

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Headquarters. WHO’s global office is located in Geneva, Switzerland.

Concept note-2: -Headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland, it has six regional offices and 150 field offices worldwide.

Concept note-3: -The WHO Country Office for India is headquartered in Delhi with country-wide presence.

Concept note-4: -A range of UN entities are headquartered in Geneva, such as the World Health Organization, UNHCR, the International Labour Organization, the International Organization for Migration and many more.
