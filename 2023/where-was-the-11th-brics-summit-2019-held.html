Where was the 11th BRICS Summit 2019 held?

(A) ** Brazil

(B) China

(C) South Africa

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The latest summit of the BRICS is held at Brasília (Brazil) on 13–14 November 2019. Its meeting is chaired by the Brazilian President Jair Bolsonaro. The theme of the 11th BRICS summit 2019 is ‘BRICS: Economic Growth for an Innovative Future’.

Concept note-2: -The 11th BRICS Summit will convene in Brasília, Brazil. The 2019 Brazilian Presidency focuses on the theme, ‘BRICS: Economic Growth for an Innovative Future’.

Concept note-3: -The latest summit of the BRICS is held at Brasília (Brazil) on 13–14 November 2019. Its meeting is chaired by the Brazilian President Jair Bolsonaro. The theme of the 11th BRICS summit 2019 is ‘BRICS: Economic Growth for an Innovative Future’.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. Prime Minister Narendra Modi will be on a two-day visit to Brazil from the 13th of November to attend the 11th BRICS Summit in Brasilia. The theme of the Summit this year is “Economic Growth for an Innovative Future”.
