Where was the 5th World Congress on Disaster Management held?

(A) Mumbai

(B) ** Delhi

(C) Chennai

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 5th edition of the World Congress on Disaster Management was held at IIT Delhi from 24 to 27 November by Disaster Management Initiatives and Convergence Society (DMICS), Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -The Fifth World Conference on Disaster Management (WCDM-2021) was held on November 24-27 in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Notes: The Fifth World Congress on Disaster Management (WCDM-2021) was organised in New Delhi from 24 to 27 November 2021.

Concept note-4: -A P J Abdul Kalam, former President of India inaugurated the Congress while Y S Rajasekhara Reddy former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh presided over the inaugural function.
