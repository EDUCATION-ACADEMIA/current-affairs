Where was the Asian Marathon Championship 2017 held?

(A) India

(B) ** China

(C) Brazil

(D) South Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2017 Asian Athletics Championships was the 22nd edition of the Asian Athletics Championships. It was held from 6 to 9 July 2017 at the Kalinga Stadium in Bhubaneswar, India. Bhubaneswar was the third Indian city to host Asian Championships. Around 560 athletes from 41 countries attended the event.

Concept note-2: -Govindan Lakshmanan is an Indian distance runner, belongs to Tamil Nadu, India. His track events are 5000 meters, 10, 000 meters. He won the 5, 000m at the Asian Athletic Championships (AAC) in Bhubaneswar at Kalinga Stadium. India won five gold medals on the final day of the Asian Athletics Championships in 2017.

Concept note-3: -The 21st Asian Athletics Championships was held at the Wuhan Sports Center in Wuhan, China from 3–7 June 2015.
