Which Airlines launched six new flights in October at New York, Frankfurt, London, Bangkok and Jaipur?

(A) . Vistara

(B) ** . Air India

(C) . Emirates

(D) . Jet Airways

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The world’s first regularly scheduled heavier-than-air airline took off from the Municipal Pier in St. Petersburg on New Year’s Day 1914. The airline was known as the St. Petersburg–Tampa Airboat Line.

Concept note-2: -Vistara brings together Tata’s and SIA’s legendary hospitality and renowned service excellence to offer the finest full-service flying experience in India.

Concept note-3: -Around 80 international airlines operate regular services to India. Air India (incl. Air India Express, a low-cost regional carrier), India’s privatised national air carrier, serves destinations in Asia, Australia, Europe and North America.

Concept note-4: -The first commercial airline in present-day India was Handley Page Indo-Burmese Transport. On 15 October 1932, J.R.D. Tata flew a consignment of mail from Karachi to Juhu Airport. His airline later became Air India.
