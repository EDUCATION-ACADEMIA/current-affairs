Concept note-1: -Gold and food manufacturing are currently the leading trade sectors between India and the UAE, Mr Lootah said.

Concept note-2: -The agreement, negotiated in a record 88 days, was signed on 18 February. This is the first major bilateral free trade pact signed by the Narendra Modi-led government and is likely to benefit $26 billion worth of Indian goods that were subjected to a 5% import duty by the UAE.
