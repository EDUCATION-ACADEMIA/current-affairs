Which among the following currency note is not made up of polymer?

(A) 5dhs

(B) 10dhs

(C) ** 20dhs

(D) 50dhs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -E-15, 20 Rs Full Bundle That Consists of 100 Notes Signed by Dr.

Concept note-2: -What are polymer banknotes made from? Polymer banknotes are made from synthetic polymer substrates (polypropylene) that consist of chains of joined individual molecules or monomers. The reason to use polymer is that it is strong, printable and suitable for enhanced security features.

Concept note-3: -The note is printed on polymer, which is a thin and flexible plastic material. On the front of the note, you can feel raised print. For example, on the words ‘Bank of England’ and in the bottom right corner, over the smaller window.

Concept note-4: -Polymer currency is made using special synthetic polymer material that can result in 3-4 times longer note life as compared to conventional paper currency. Such notes are made using a more energy-efficient manufacturing process resulting in a lower environmental impact.
