Which bank becomes the first leader to impose charges UPI transactions, namely, Paytm, Google Pay, PhonePe?

(A) Lakshmi Vilas Bank

(B) City Union Bank

(C) ** Kotak Mahindra Bank

(D) ICICI Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kotak Mahindra Bank Becomes the First Lender To impose charges on UPI transactions, namely, Paytm, Google Pay, PhonePe.

Concept note-2: -Nil charges up to 1 transaction per month and subsequent transactions will be charged Rs. 10/-per transaction.

Concept note-3: -Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is an instant real-time payment system developed by National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). The interface facilitates inter-bank peer-to-peer (P2P) and person-to-merchant (P2M) transactions. It is used on mobile devices to instantly transfer funds between two bank accounts.

Concept note-4: -Pioneered and developed by National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI), BHIM has been conceived and launched by the Hon’ble Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi on 30th December 2016 to bring in Financial Inclusion to the nation and a digitally empowered society.
