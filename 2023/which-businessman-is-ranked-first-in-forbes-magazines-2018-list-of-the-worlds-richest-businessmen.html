Which businessman is ranked first in Forbes magazine’s 2018 list of the world’s richest businessmen?

(A) ** Jeff Bezos

(B) Mukesh Ambani

(C) Mark Zuckerberg

(D) Bill Gates

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 2018, Amazon founder Jeff Bezos was ranked at the top for the first time and became the first centibillionaire included in the ranking, surpassing Microsoft founder Bill Gates, who had topped the list 18 of the previous 24 years. In 2022, after topping the list for four years, Bezos was surpassed by Elon Musk.

Concept note-2: -Jeff Bezos Jeff Bezos is the founder of Amazon and he holds an estimated net worth of $136 billion.
