Which campaign by Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal was re-launched on 18 October 2021, after it was successful last year in Delhi in reducing pollution?

(A) Switch Delhi

(B) 10 Hafte-10 Baje-10 Minute

(C) ** Red Light ON Gaadi OFF

(D) Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is ‘Red Light On, Gaadi Off’? As the name suggests, the campaign is meant to persuade people to switch off their vehicles’ engines as they wait for the traffic light to turn green. The campaign has roped in civil defence volunteers who will egg on commuters to turn their vehicles off.

Concept note-2: -The Delhi government first launched the ‘Red Light On, Gaadi Off’ campaign on October 16, 2020.

Concept note-3: -The month-long ‘Red light on, Gaadi off’ campaign aims at encouraging drivers to turn off the ignition of their vehicles while waiting at traffic signals. Delhi Lieutenant V.K.

Concept note-4: -The state government started spraying PUSA bio-decomposer, free of cost, in the farmlands of Delhi to combat pollution. Nearly 3, 200 acres have already been covered.
