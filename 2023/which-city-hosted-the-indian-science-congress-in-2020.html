Which city hosted the Indian Science Congress in 2020?

(A) Chennai

(B) ** Bengaluru

(C) Mumbai

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Bengaluru. The Indian Science Congress 2020 was held in Bengaluru, Karnataka. It was held at the University of Agricultural Sciences, GKVK Campus, Bangalore, Karnataka.

Concept note-2: -Through video conferencing, Indian Science Congress’s 108th edition was officially inaugrated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University is hosting the five-day 108th session of ISC as it commemorates its centennial.

Concept note-3: -Recently, the 108th session of the India Science Congress (ISC) was inaugurated by the Prime Minister. The event’s theme is ‘Science and Technology for Sustainable Development with Women’s Empowerment’.

Concept note-4: -The 107 Indian Science Congress 2022 was held at the University of Agricultural Sciences, GKVK Campus, Bangalore, Karnataka in January 2022. Theme of the 108th Indian Science Congress 2023: Science and Technology for Sustainable Development with Women Empowerment”.

Concept note-5: -The 108th Indian Science Congress being inaugurated at Nagpur tomorrow by Prime Minister Narendra Modi will focus on sustainable development with inclusive involvement of all sections of society including women.
