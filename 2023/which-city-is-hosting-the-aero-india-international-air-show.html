WHICH CITY IS HOSTING THE AERO INDIA INTERNATIONAL AIR SHOW?

(A) PUNE

(B) ** BANGLURU

(C) DELHI

(D) HYDRABAD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aero India is a biennial air show and aviation exhibition held in Bengaluru, India at the Yelahanka Air Force Station. It is organised by the Defence Exhibition Organisation, Ministry of Defence. The Aero India is the largest air show in Asia. The theme of Aero India 2023 is “The Runway to a Billion Opportunities".

Concept note-2: -"Aero India 2023” will be launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi which is the annual exhibition of India’s domestic defense capabilities on Monday. The event is being held by the Indian Department of Defense Production from February 13–17 at the Yelahanka Air Force Station near Bangalore, Karnataka.

Concept note-3: -Bangalore Air Show 2023 is a highly anticipated event in the aerospace and defense industry, bringing together global leaders and experts to showcase the latest advancements and technologies. The event will take place from 13th February to February 17th, 2023 at the Yelahanka Air Force Station in Bengaluru, India.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Monday (February 13) inaugurated the Aero India 2023 airshow at Yelahanka air base in Bengaluru.

Concept note-5: -Aero India show 2023 in Bengaluru Theme PM Modi inaugurated the five-day event on the theme ‘The runway to a billion opportunities’.
