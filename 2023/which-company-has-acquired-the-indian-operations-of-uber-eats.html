(A) ** Zomato

(B) Swiggy

(C) Foodpanda

(D) McDonald

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Zomato has acquired the Indian operations of Uber Eats, the food delivery biz run by Uber, for around $350 million.

Concept note-2: -Zomato has acquired the Indian operations of Uber Eats, the food delivery business run by Uber, for around $350 million (Rs 2, 485 crore).

Concept note-3: -On January 21 Zomatoo acquired UberEats in the all-stock acquisition, with UberEats gaining 9.99 per cent stake in Zomato. Zomato is an Indian restaurant and food delivery startup founded in the year 2008 by Deepinder Goyal. Zomato offers its services in 24 countries and in 10, 000 cities.

Concept note-4: -On 29 June 2021, the firm acquired a 9.3% stake in Grofers for nearly US$120 million. On 24 June 2022, Zomato acquired Blinkit (formerly Grofers) for US$568 million in an all-stock deal.
