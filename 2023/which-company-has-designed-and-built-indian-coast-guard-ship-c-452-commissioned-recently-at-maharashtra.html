Which company has designed and built Indian coast guard ship ‘C-452 ‘ commissioned recently at Maharashtra?

(A) Goa shipyard Ltd

(B) Hindustan shipyard Ltd

(C) ** Larsen & Turbo

(D) Mazagon Dock Ltd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Coast Guard Ship (ICGS) Saksham is the 5th and last Offshore Patrol Vessel designed and developed by Goa Shipyard Limited (GSL) for Coast Guard.

Concept note-2: -Indian Coast Guard ship Kamla Devi, the last in series of five fast-patrol vessels, was commissioned in Kolkata on Thursday.

Concept note-3: -Indian Coast Guard Ship (ICG) C-453, the 17th of the 18 Interceptor Boats being indigenously built by Larsen & Toubro Ltd., was commissioned in Chennai on Friday.

Concept note-4: -ICGS Samarth: The Indian Coast Guard’s latest and largest Offshore Patrol Vessel (OPV). Samarth is first in the series of six 105m offshore patrol vessels and has been built by Goa Shipyard Limited.
