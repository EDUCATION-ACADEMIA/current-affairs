Which company is set to establish first-ever India House at Paris Olympics 2024?

(A) ** Reliance Industries

(B) Tata Group

(C) Adani Industries

(D) Mahindra Industries

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which company is set to establish first-ever India House at Paris Olympics 2024? Notes: Reliance Industries (RIL) and Indian Olympic Association (IOA) are set to together establish the first-ever India House at Paris Olympics 2024.

Concept note-2: -The International Olympic Committee has announced a deal with media group Viacom18 for exclusive rights across the Indian subcontinent to the 2024 Olympic Games in Paris. The deal, which marks Viacom18’s first rights acquisition to a major global multi-sport event, was exclusively reported by SportBusiness in October.

Concept note-3: -International Olympic Committee President Thomas Bach has officially revealed the slogan for the 2024 Paris Games. For those who prefer English, the official slogan is “Games Wide Open.” For French-speakers, the slogan is “Ouvrons Grand les Jeux.”

Concept note-4: -In partnership with IOA, RIL will support Indian athletes in major multi-sport events including the Commonwealth Games, Asian Games, and the Olympics Games.

Concept note-5: -The Look of the Games The visual identity of the Paris 2024 Games has been created around four main themes: celebration, transfer, rationalisation and personalisation. Venues will be decorated in bright colours to represent the worldwide celebration of sports.
