Which continent has the highest population density?

(A) ** Asia

(B) Africa

(C) North America

(D) Europe

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of 2021, Asia was the most densely populated region of the world with nearly 150 inhabitants per square kilometer, whereas Oceania’s population density was just over five inhabitants per square kilometer. Worldwide, the population density was 60 people per square kilometer.

Concept note-2: -Asia is the most densely populated continent.

Concept note-3: -The dramatic growth in the population of Asia is the result of an increase in the number of people surviving the reproductive age. Population growth accompanies changes in fertility rates due to better education about birth control.

Concept note-4: -Most Tightly Packed Continent South America-61.3 people per square mile. Europe-187.7 people per square mile. Asia-257.8 people per square mile.
