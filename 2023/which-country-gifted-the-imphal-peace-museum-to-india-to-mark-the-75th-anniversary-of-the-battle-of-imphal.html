Which country gifted the Imphal Peace Museum to India to mark the 75th anniversary of the Battle of Imphal?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) China

(C) ** Japan

(D) Vietnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Japan gifted Manipur, the Imphal Peace Museum. It is built on the memories of the Battle of Imphal (during 2nd World War). The inauguration marked the 75th anniversary of the Battle of Imphal. The Imphal Peace Museum was inaugurated at Red Hill.

Concept note-2: -The Second World War reached India with the Japanese bombing of Imphal, the capital of Manipur on 10th May 1942. This changed the life of the denizens of Imphal and also that of the people living in the far flung region of Manipur. The people of Imphal immediately fled their home after the bombing.

Concept note-3: -Fought between 8 March and 18 July 1944, these were the turning point of one of the most gruelling campaigns of the Second World War (1939-45). The decisive Japanese defeat in north-east India became the springboard for the Fourteenth Army’s subsequent re-conquest of Burma.

Concept note-4: -Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park (, Hiroshima Heiwa Kinen Kōen) is a memorial park in the center of Hiroshima, Japan.
