Which country has built first of its kind Underwater Military Museum?

(A) India

(B) China

(C) Germany

(D) ** Jordan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Underwater military museum dive site, Aqaba, Jordan. Off the Gulf of Aqaba in the Red Sea, you can explore the first Underwater Military Museum. It represented by 21 unique objects of battles. The military machines placed along the coral reefs.

Concept note-2: -The world’s first underwater military museum has been created just off the shores of Aqaba, Jordan.

Concept note-3: -The wreck known simply as ‘The Tank’ is in actual fact an M42 ‘duster’ anti-aircraft vehicle, sunk in 1999 by the Jordanian Royal Ecological Diving Society as another artificial reef project.
