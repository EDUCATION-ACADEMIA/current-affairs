Which country has the largest number of migrants living abroad, as per World Migration Report 2020?

(A) Japan

(B) Mexico

(C) China

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The report found that two thirds of all international migrants live in just 20 countries. The United States of America remained the largest destination, hosting 51 million international migrants in 2020, equal to 18 per cent of the world’s total.

Concept note-2: -India remains the top origin country for the world’s migrants. India has been a large source of international migrants for more than a century. In 2020, 17.9 million international migrants traced their origins back to India, followed by Mexico with about 11.2 million and Russia with about 10.8 million.

Concept note-3: -Although the United States is outside the top 20, it still has by far the most immigrants of any other country (50 million vs. 16 million in second-place Germany).

Concept note-4: -The all-India migration rate was 28.9 per cent for July 2020-June 2021, with 26.5 per cent migration rate in rural areas and 34.9 per cent in urban areas. Females recorded a higher share of migration rate of 47.9 per cent, with 48 per cent in rural areas and 47.8 per cent in urban areas.

Concept note-5: -According to the latest available estimates, there were 280.6 million global migrants in 2020-representing close to 4 percent of the world’s 7.8 billion people.
