Which country has won the 2019 Rugby World Cup in Japan?

(A) Japan

(B) England

(C) ** South Africa

(D) Singapore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -YOKOHAMA, Japan-South Africa captured its third Rugby World Cup championship on Saturday, defeating a favored England team, 32-12, and extending the dominance of teams from the Southern Hemisphere in the tournament.

Concept note-2: -South Africa South Africa’s rugby team, the Springboks, won the Rugby World Cup in 1995, 2007, and 2019.

Concept note-3: -Following the end of apartheid, they hosted the 1995 Rugby World Cup and won the tournament, they were champions again at the 2007 tournament in France. The Springboks then made history when they beat England in the Rugby World Cup 2019 in Japan despite losing a pool stage match.

Concept note-4: -Four tries in the second half see USA get their first victory in Pool B. USA turned on the style after the break to defeat Japan 30-17 at the Northland Events Centre in Whangārei in a cracking Rugby World Cup 2021 match.
