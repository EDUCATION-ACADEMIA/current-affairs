Which country hosted the 8th BIMSTEC Agriculture Experts Meeting?

(A) Srilanka

(B) Nepal

(C) Myanmar

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is India. India hosted the 8th Meeting of Agriculture Experts of the Bay of Bengal Initiative for Multi-Sectoral Technical and Economic Cooperation (BIMSTEC) Countries virtually on 31 August 2021.

Concept note-2: -India hosted the Second Agriculture Ministerial-level meeting of the Bay of Bengal Initiative for Multi-Sectoral Technical and Economic Cooperation (BIMSTEC) here today under the chairmanship of the Union Minister for Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Shri Narendra Singh Tomar.

Concept note-3: -Which country was the host of the BIMSTEC summit in 2022? In 2022, Sri Lanka served as the host country for the Bay of Bengal Initiative for Multi-Sectoral Technical and Economic Cooperation (BIMSTEC) Summit.

Concept note-4: -Cultural Cooperation (Bhutan) India hosted the 1st Expert Group Meeting BCIC&O in 2006 in New Delhi. The first BIMSTEC Ministerial meeting on Culture was held in Paro, Bhutan in May 2006.
