Which country is going to host the 13th edition of Indian Premier League (IPL) 2020?

(A) ** United Arab Emirates

(B) South Africa

(C) Australia

(D) New Zealand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 2 August 2020, it was announced that the tournament would be played between 19 September and 10 November 2020 in the United Arab Emirates. The tagline was Ab Khel bolega: (Now game will speak.)

Concept note-2: -Gujarat Titans won IPL 2022 by 7 wickets. Gujarat Titans and Rajasthan Royals were in the finals, and the finals were held on 29th May 2022, in Ahmedabad. Under the Captaincy of Hardik Pandya, Gujarat Titans have created history in their debut season.

Concept note-3: -The first match will scheduled on 26 March and will played between CSK Vs KKR in Wankhede Stadium, Mumbai .

Concept note-4: -As per unofficial news the 16th season of the Indian Premier League, IPL 2023, will start on 31 March 2023, and end on 28 May 2023.

Concept note-5: -A total of 74 matches will be played including 4 playoff matches. As of before, the 8 teams will play 60 matches in total where each team will play 14 league matches each, they will face each team twice in the league match.
