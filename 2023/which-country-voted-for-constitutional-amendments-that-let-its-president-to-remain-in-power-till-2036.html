(C) ** 3.Russia

(D) 4.New Zealand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The president is elected directly through a popular vote to a six-year term. Previously, the Constitution established term limit for the presidency restricting the officeholder to serve no more than two terms.

Concept note-2: -It also extended presidential term limits, enabling Putin, if reelected, to hold presidential office until 2036. The previous version of the constitution would have required him to step down in 2024; this move has been labelled a “power grab” by some commentators.
