Which cricketer has been named as the most RELIABLE captain at ICC?

(A) Steven Smith

(B) Virat Kohli

(C) ** Kane Williamson

(D) Eoin Morgan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Statistically, Williamson is the best Test captain in New Zealand’s history, winning 22 of 40 matches at the helm, as well as eight draws and just 10 defeats.

Concept note-2: -All Formats Captaincy Record of Kane Williamson Kane Williamson led the New Zealand team in 193 total matches, winning 99 and losing 79, with a win rate of 51 percent across all formats.

Concept note-3: -Williamson, who led New Zealand to victory in the inaugural World Test Championship final against India at the Ageas Bowl in 2021, will continue as ODI and T20I captain and also reiterated his desire to keep playing all three formats.
