Which decade is announced as the International Decade for People of African Descent?

(A) 2012-2021

(B) ** 2015-2024

(C) 2017-2026

(D) 2020-2029

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In December 2014, the UN General Assembly, in its Resolution 68/237, proclaimed the International Decade for People of African Descent (2015-2024).

Concept note-2: -In December 2013, the UN General Assembly adopted resolution 68/237, by which it proclaimed 2015 to 2024 to be the International Decade for People of African Descent, with the theme “People of African descent: recognition, justice and development”.

Concept note-3: -Last year, the United Nations marked the first-ever International Day for People of African Descent on 31 August 2021.

Concept note-4: -International Day for People of African Descent – August 31, 2023. International Day for People of African Descent is observed on August 31 each year. This holiday was created by the United Nations to celebrate the diverse heritage and several contributions of people of African descent.
