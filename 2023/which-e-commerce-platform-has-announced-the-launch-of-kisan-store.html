Which e-commerce platform has announced the launch of ‘Kisan’ store?

(A) Flipkart

(B) ** Amazon

(C) JioMart

(D) Big Basket

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Amazon India. Amazon India has announced the launch of Kisan Store.

Concept note-2: -Amazon India announced the launch of Kisan Store. The store was launched by Union Minister for Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Narendra Singh Tomar on September 2, 2021.

Concept note-3: -Addressing at the launch event, Shri Narendra Singh Tomar, Hon’ble Minister of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare said, “It gives me immense pleasure to launch Amazon Kisan Store.

Concept note-4: -To ensure that as many farmers as possible are able to access the benefits, the Kisan Store has five Indian languages including Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, Tamil, and Malayalam.
