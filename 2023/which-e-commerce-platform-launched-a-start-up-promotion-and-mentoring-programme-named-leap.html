Which E-commerce platform launched a Start-up promotion and mentoring programme named ‘Leap’?

(A) Amazon

(B) ** Flipkart

(C) Snapdeal

(D) Shopclues

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Flipkart. Flipkart announced the evolution of its flagship startup accelerator programme, Flipkart Leap.

Concept note-2: -Flipkart Leap is a startup accelerator program by India’s leading e-commerce giant Flipkart, aiming to create an ecosystem of strong startups in the Technology and Consumer Internet space focused on the India Market.

Concept note-3: -Two new programs – Flipkart Leap Ahead (FLA) and Flipkart Leap Innovation Network (FLIN) have also been launched under Flipkart Leap.

Concept note-4: -This program is an initiative under Flipkart’s flagship startup accelerator program, Flipkart Leap. The program aims to identify relevant startups working towards various problem statements that can accelerate the adoption of e-commerce for the next 500 million customers.

Concept note-5: -Through the accelerator program, Flipkart aims to provide mentorship and guidance to early-stage startups to grow, scale, and build innovations for the country. As a part of the program, the selected startups will undergo a 16-week mentorship program that has been designed by Bain & Company.
