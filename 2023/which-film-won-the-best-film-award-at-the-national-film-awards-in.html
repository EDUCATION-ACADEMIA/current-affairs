Which film won the best film award at the National Film Awards in?

(A) d

(B) Mom

(C) Baahubali 2: The Conclusion

(D) ** Village Rockstar (Assamese)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 65th National Film Awards for 2017 were announced at PIB Conference Room, Shastri Bhawan in New Delhi on April 13, 2018. In it, Assamese Film ’Village Rockstar’ bagged the best film award, while ‘Newton’ has been selected as the Best Hindi Film.

Concept note-2: -’Bridge’ won the Best Assamese Film award in the Feature Films category. The film is produced by Sabita Devi, and directed by Kripal Kalita. The film received the Rajat Kamal medal and a cash prize of Rs. 1, 00, 000 (each).

Concept note-3: -Dikchow Banat Palaax, an Assamese feature film received the Nargis Dutt Award for Best Feature Film on National Integration at the 64th National Film Awards. This film was written and directed by National Award-winning filmmaker Sanjib Sabhapandit.
