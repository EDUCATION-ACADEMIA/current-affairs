Which film won the Golden Peacock Award at IFFI 2019?

(A) ** Particles

(B) Jallikattu

(C) Marighella

(D) Balloon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Particles. The French-Swiss co-produced drama film Particles directed by Blaise Harrison and produced by Estelle Fialon won the Golden Peacock Award at India’s 50th International Film Festival (IFFI). It is an ambitious yet modest film about the mysteries of being a teenager.

Concept note-2: -PANAJI: The Costa Rican film ‘I have electric dreams’ won the prestigious Golden Peacock award at the 53rd International Film Festival of India.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Into the Darkness. The 51st edition of the International Film Festival of India, IFFI concluded on 24 January 2021. ‘Into the Darkness’, a film from Denmark won the prestigious Golden Peacock award.
