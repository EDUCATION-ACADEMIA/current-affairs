Which framework was agreed upon by the G20 countries during the G20 Ministerial Meeting on Energy Transitions and Global Environment for Sustainable Growth 2019 held in Nagano, Japan?

(A) Anti-Corruption Plan

(B) Convention on Climate Change

(C) Strengthen Economic Resilience

(D) ** Marine Plastic Pollution Deal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -G20 members also agreed to the G20 Karuizawa Innovation Action Plan on Energy Transitions and Global Environment for Sustainable Growth, which specifies concrete actions for realizing this concept. G20 members also delivered a unified message on climate change issues.

Concept note-2: -Key environmental issues are being discussed as part of G20 working groups. They include climate finance under the sustainable finance working group, climate resilience as part of the disaster risk reduction working group, lifestyles and consumption under the development working group.

Concept note-3: -The G20 recognises the importance of collective action in tackling environmental challenges and climate change while promoting transitions towards more flexible, transparent and cleaner energy systems.
