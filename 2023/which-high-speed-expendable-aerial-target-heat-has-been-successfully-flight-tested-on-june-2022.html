Concept note-2: -The correct answer is ‘ABHYAS’. Key Points India on 29 June 2022 successfully tested the indigenously-designed ABHYAS-a High-speed Expendable Aerial Target (HEAT). The test was carried out by the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) from the Integrated Test Range in Odisha’s Chandipur.
