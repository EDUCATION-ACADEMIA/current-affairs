Which Indian institution has launched the world’s first online Bachelor of Science Degree in Data Science and programming?

(A) ** 1.Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras

(B) 2.Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay

(C) 3.Indian Institute of Science (IISc)

(D) 4.Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which Indian institution has launched the world’s first online Bachelor of Science Degree in Data Science and programming? Notes: The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras has launched the world’s first online Diploma and Bachelor of Science Degree in Data Science and programming.

Concept note-2: -Congratulating the IIT Madras team on the launch of the program, Shri. Sanjay Dhotre, Hon’ble Minister of State (HRD), said, “In the world today, education is a continuous process.

Concept note-3: -IITM’s Experience in Online Education IITM is well-equipped to provide an online degree course having initiated the NPTEL program, the country’s first online content portal as an inter-IIT consortium project in 2001.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras is offering a four-year Bachelor of Science (BS) Degree in Data Science and Applications. This is an online programme. Those who have cleared Class 12 examination can apply for the programme. Those who have cleared Class 11, irrespective of their group, can also apply.

Concept note-5: -It helps you build on your social skills and career goals while enjoying a great social life. IIT Madras started this unique BS program to provide access to IIT quality education to the learners across the country. Data Science is a growing field and the demand for skilled resources in the market is very high.
