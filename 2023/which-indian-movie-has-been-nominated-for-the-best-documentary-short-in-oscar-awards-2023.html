Which Indian movie has been nominated for the Best Documentary Short in Oscar Awards 2023?

(A) All that Breathe

(B) ** The Elephant Whisperers

(C) RRR

(D) Tell it like a women The Oscar nominations were announced on January 24, 2023, and India received three nominations for the 95th Academy Awards. The Elephant Whisperers, a 41-minute short by Kartiki Gonsalves, was also nominated for Best Documentary Short. SS Rajamouli’s RRR received only one nomination in the Best Original Song category for “Naatu Naatu, “ despite the fact that it has a big fan base in the United States. The film did not receive nominations for Best Picture or Best Director. All That Breathes by Shaunak Sen was nominated for Best Documentary Feature Film.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian-made documentaries All That Breathes and The Elephant Whisperers have been nominated for Oscars. All that Breathes will compete for Best Documentary Feature Film at the 95th Academy Awards. The Elephant Whisperers is among the nominees for Best Documentary Short Film.

Concept note-2: -Oscars 2023 Nominations Highlights: Rrr’S Naatu Naatu, All That Breathes, Elephant Whisperers Nominated.

Concept note-3: -RRR, Chhello Show, All That Breathes, and The Elephant Whisperers are the four Indian films nominated for Academy Awards in 2023. The Oscar nominations announcement will be live-streamed on YouTube, Oscar.com, Oscars.org, and other Academy social media platforms.

Concept note-4: -India has bagged not one, but three nominations at the Academy Awards. SS Rajamouli’s epic period drama ‘RRR’ bagged an Oscar nomination for Best Original Song ‘Naatu Naatu’.

Concept note-5: -Kartiki Gonsalves’s The Elephant Whisperers is nominated in the Best Documentary Short Film category at the 95th Oscars. For her passion project, she picked cinematographers that had previously worked with animals and weren’t scared of being in the forest.
