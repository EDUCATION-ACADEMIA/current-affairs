Which Indian space craft has completed 9000 orbits around the moon.

(A) Chandrayan 1

(B) ** Chandrayan 2

(C) Mangalyan

(D) Voyager

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chandrayaan-2 spacecraft of India has completed more than 9, 000 orbits around the Moon.

Concept note-2: -Chandrayaan-2 spacecraft completes over 9, 000 orbits around moon: ISRO | Mint.

Concept note-3: -Chandrayaan-2 completes 9, 000 orbits around Moon: Science results ‘encouraging’ says Isro chief.

Concept note-4: -NEW DELHI: In a significant discovery, a scientific instrument on board Chandrayaan-2’s orbiter has made first-of-its-kind observation of the global distribution of Argon-40, an inert, colourless and odorless element, in the tenuous lunar exosphere of the Moon.

Concept note-5: -The Chandrayaan-2 mission consisted of three main modules: Vikram lander (named after Vikram Sarabhai, the late father of India’s space program) Lunar rover named Pragyan. All of the above parts were developed in India.
