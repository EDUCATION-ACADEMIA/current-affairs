Which Indian state has signed a MoU with the UK India Business Council, to promote business relations between UK and India?

(A) Gujarat

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) ** Maharashtra

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To realise new momentum for Maharashtra’s economy, the UKIBC signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Govt of Maharashtra. This MoU promotes increasing the connections between UK businesses and Maharashtra that deliver investment, jobs, and economic growth for both.

Concept note-2: -UK India Business Council signs MoU with WBIDC UK businesses such as BT, Diageo, Chivas Brothers, PwC, HSBC and Standard Chartered are already significant investors in West Bengal.

Concept note-3: -’Doing Business in India’ Report 2022 was released by the UK India Business Council (UKIBC). Its findings are based on a survey of 111 UK businesses and higher education institutions.
