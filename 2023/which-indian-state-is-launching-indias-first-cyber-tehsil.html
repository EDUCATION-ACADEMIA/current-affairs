Which Indian state is launching India’s first cyber tehsil?

(A) Kerala

(B) Bihar

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) ** Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first police station for cybercrimes inaugurated at Bengaluru in Karnataka.

Concept note-2: -Madhya Pradesh Cabinet has approved the proposal to create cyber tehsils in the state of Madhya Pradesh. After this, MP will become the first state in the country to have a cyber tehsil. Cyber Tehsil will make the mutation process easy and people from anywhere in the state can avail of its benefit.

Concept note-3: -The Revenue and Transport Minister said that Madhya Pradesh is the only state in the country to implement cyber tehsil, which has achieved unexpected success in resolving pending revenue cases through this innovative experiment.
