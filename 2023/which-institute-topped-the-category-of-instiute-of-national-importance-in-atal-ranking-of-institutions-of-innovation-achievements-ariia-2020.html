WHICH INSTITUTE TOPPED THE CATEGORY OF INSTIUTE OF NATIONAL IMPORTANCE IN ATAL RANKING OF INSTITUTIONS OF INNOVATION ACHIEVEMENTS (ARIIA) 2020??

(A) IIT-BOMBAY

(B) ** IIT MADRAS

(C) IIT-ROPAR

(D) IIT-PATNA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The top position has been achieved by IIT Madrasas the most innovative educational institute of India in the technical category. IIT Madras has won this recognition for the third time in a row.

Concept note-2: -The top rank has been bagged by IIT Madras followed by IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Kanpur and IIT Roorkee.
