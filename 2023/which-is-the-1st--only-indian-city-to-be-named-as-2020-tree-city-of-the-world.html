Which is the 1st & only Indian City to be named as ‘2020 Tree City of the World’?

(A) New Delhi

(B) Bangalore

(C) Mysore

(D) ** Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hyderabad was recognized as a ‘Tree City of the World’ for the second consecutive year for planting 3.5 crore trees in 500 volunteer hours under the state government’s ‘Haritha Haram’ programme and Urban Forest Parks initiative.

Concept note-2: -Mumbai has been recognized as the “2021 Tree City of the World” by the Food and Agriculture Organisation of the United Nations (UN) jointly with Arbor Day Foundation.

Concept note-3: -Only Indian city other than Mumbai to be on the list. Hyderabad is among the two cities in India, along with Mumbai, to be listed as ‘Tree Cities of the World-2021’ by Arbor Day Foundation and FAO of UN. Hyderabad finds a place on the list for the second year in a row.

Concept note-4: -Due to this pride, the city of Mumbai, which is known as ‘Dream City’, has now got a new identity as ‘Tree City’.

Concept note-5: -Mumbai became the second city to receive the recognition of the “2021 Tree City of the World” by the Food and Agriculture Organisation of the United Nations (UN) in association with Arbor Day Foundation. Hyderabad was the first city to receive the honour.
