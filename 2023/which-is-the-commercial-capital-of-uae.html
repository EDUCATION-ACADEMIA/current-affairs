Which is the commercial capital of UAE?

(A) Abu Dhabi

(B) ** Dubai

(C) Sharjah

(D) Ajman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abu Dhabi, Arabic Abū Ẓaby, city and capital of Abu Dhabi emirate, one of the United Arab Emirates (formerly Trucial States, or Trucial Oman), and the national capital of that federation.

Concept note-2: -Dubai is the commercial capital of the oil-rich United Arab Emirates in the Persian Gulf.

Concept note-3: -Dubai is emerging as an international destination for the commercial community to set up in. In 1994, Dubai was declared as the first IT-free zone and strategically positioned itself at the centre of global trade routes for logistics and finance.

Concept note-4: -Dubai was the residence of the British political agent for the former Trucial States from 1954 until 1971. Known as the Venice of the Gulf, Dubai is the chief port and commercial centre of the United Arab Emirates.

Concept note-5: -The UAE capital Abu Dhabi is a great place to do business, invest, live and work. Abu Dhabi is the capital of the UAE and the largest emirate – by size and GDP contribution.
