Which is the first direct flight between Srinagar and Sharjah?

(A) Air Asia

(B) ** Go first

(C) SpiceJet

(D) AirIndia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Operated by an Airbus A320neo, the maiden GO FIRST flight G8 1595 departed Sheikh ul-Alam International Airport in Srinagar at 18:00 hours and arrived in Sharjah at 21:00 hours. GO FIRST will operate four flights every week between Srinagar and Sharjah.

Concept note-2: -Which is the first airline to launch direct flights from Srinagar to Sharjah? Notes: India’s low-cost airline Go First became the first airline to launch direct flights from Srinagar to Sharjah.

Concept note-3: -Srinagar Airport clocks record 100 plus daily flights.

Concept note-4: -Sharjah International Airport is home base of the low-cost carrier Air Arabia. The headquarters of Air Arabia is in the Sharjah Freight Center, on the property of the airport in Sharjah, UAE.

Concept note-5: -The UAE has opened up international travel from India and flights are operating between the two nations and Indians travelling to the UAE can now book their seats.
