Which is the second tallest building in the world?

(A) Royal Clock Tower-Mecca

(B) ** Shanghai Tower-Shanghai

(C) One World Trade Center-New York

(D) The Torch-Dubai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2. Shanghai Tower. Height: 2, 073 ft. Standing at 2, 073 ft, the Shanghai Tower, located in China, is the second tallest building in the world.

Concept note-2: -’Shanghai Center Building’) is a 128-story, 632-meter-tall (2, 073 ft) megatall skyscraper in Lujiazui, Pudong, Shanghai. It is the tallest building in China and the world’s third-tallest building by height to architectural top. It is the tallest and largest LEED Platinum certified building in the world since 2015.

Concept note-3: -Shanghai Tower, Shanghai World Financial Center, and Jin Mao Tower form the cluster.
