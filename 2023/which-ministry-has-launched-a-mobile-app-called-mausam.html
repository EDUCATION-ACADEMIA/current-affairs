Which Ministry has launched a mobile app called ‘Mausam’?

(A) Environment Ministry

(B) ** Earth science Ministry

(C) Home Ministry

(D) Health Ministray

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MAUSAM is a Mobile App of the India Meteorological Department (IMD), Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES), Government of India to provide seamless and user-friendly access to weather products available on https://mausam.imd.gov.in/.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Earth Sciences launches Mobile App “Mausam”

Concept note-3: -MAUSAM App by IMD: Created by the India Meteorological Department (IMD) and the Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES), the MAUSAM app is one of the most accurate weather apps available in the country.

Concept note-4: -AccuWeather can provide you with everything from a fast read on current temperatures and weather conditions to a complete precipitation study. This AccuWeather apps for Android and iOS contains over 3 million weather predictions. MinuteCast is one of the app’s most outstanding features.
