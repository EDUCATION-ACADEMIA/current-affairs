Which ministry sponsored for CSIR innovation award?

(A) Ministry of Defence

(B) Ministry of Finance

(C) Ministry of Education

(D) ** Ministry of Science and Technology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: - Council of Scientific & Industrial Research ( , ) Ministry of Science & Technology, Govt. of India.

Concept note-2: -About CSIR Innovation Award for School Children 2023 The Council of Scientific and Industrial Research invites the application for the CSIR Innovation Award for School Children. The council has been providing the innovation award to the students since 2002 on World Intellectual Property Day.

Concept note-3: -CIASC (CSIR Innovation Award for School Children) is an annual national competition to harness the creative and innovative spirit of school children. The competition has been started in the year 2002 on the occasion of the World Intellectual Property Day which is celebrated throughout the world on 26th April.

Concept note-4: -Gujar Mal Modi Award for Science & Technology This award is to acknowledge and reward excellence in research and development and outstanding contribution to Innovative Science & Technology in India. The award consists of a cash prize of Rs 2.01 Lakh, a scroll of honour and a silver shield.
