Which Mobile Network company recently achieved a total speed of 5 GBPS in testing of its 5G network?

(A) Jio

(B) ** VI

(C) Airtel

(D) BSNL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vodafone Idea (Vi) and Ericsson today achieved a peak download speed of 5.92 Gbps during the ongoing 5G trials.

Concept note-2: -What is the speed of VI 5G? Vi tested peak data rates of 3.7 Gbps on the mmWave spectrum in a further experiment. Vi also achieved speeds of up to 1.5 Gbps in an artificial 5G network during testing in Gandhinagar using the 3.5 GHz band.

Concept note-3: -Ericsson (NASDAQ: ERIC) has successfully achieved a peak data rate of more than 1Gbps for a single user device in a recent 5G Standalone (SA) field trial.

Concept note-4: -5G speed in India is the highest in Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai and Varanasi. In Delhi, Jio has fared better than Airtel with a download speed of 598.58Mbps, while Airtel has only 197.98Mbps. Ookla report says that Jio’s 5G performance is superior thanks to the spectrum band it uses.
