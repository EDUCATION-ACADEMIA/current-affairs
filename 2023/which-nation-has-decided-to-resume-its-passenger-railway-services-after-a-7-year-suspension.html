Which nation has decided to resume its passenger railway services after a 7-year suspension?

(A) Myanmar

(B) ** Nepal

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nepal to resume railway services after 7-years suspension.

Concept note-2: -The East-West Railway Line (Nepali: - ) or the Mechi-Mahakali Railway is an upcoming project in Nepal. The railway will be the longest in Nepal, stretching 945.244 km (587 mi) expanding from the Eastern Indo-Nepal border of Kakarbhitta to the Western Indo-Nepal border of Kanchanpur.

Concept note-3: -After a successful trial, the railway network was inaugurated on April 2, 2022, for general train operations. Jaynagar-Kurtha Railway line is 68.7 km long in which a total of 34.9 km long line falls under the Indian Railways part. The rest of the line is handed over to Nepal Railway Company Ltd.

Concept note-4: -In a historic move to boost connectivity, Prime Minister Narendra Modi and his Nepalese counterpart Sher Bahadur Deuba on Saturday inaugurated the first broad-gauge passenger railway service connecting Bihar’s Jayanagar with Nepal’s Kurtha region.

Concept note-5: -India–Nepal Jaynagar–Bardibas railway line is a semi-operational line between India and Nepal. DEMU passenger service is operation on this route.
