Which of the following Banks has launched a special business vertical dedicated for gold loans to meet the financial needs of its customers due to the COVID-19 pandemic?

(A) UCO Bank

(B) ** Canara Bank

(C) Indian Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Canara Bank on Monday said that it has launched a special business vertical dedicated to gold loans.

Concept note-2: -Maximum Amount-Rs. 35.00 Lakh. Against the pledge of gold ornaments / Jewellery. Loan shall be against Ornaments, specially minted Gold Coins sold by Banks and not against Gold Coin, Bullion etc.

Concept note-3: -You can apply for a gold loan from Canara Bank where the lender charges interest rates at their MCLR rates at 7.25% p.a. with maximum loan amount up to Rs. 35 lakh. The maximum repayment tenure is up to 2 years and 0.5% of the loan amount subject to a minimum of Rs.

Concept note-4: -But the most significant push for gold loan comes from its benefits to the borrower. One can monetise idle gold jewellery for small or larger amounts, unlike a personal loan that comes with both lower and upward limits. Moreover, gold loans entail the lowest interest rates to the borrower.
