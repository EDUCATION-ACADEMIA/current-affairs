Which of the following country has got the first approval for coronavirus self-test kit?

(A) India

(B) China

(C) ** US

(D) UK

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are two types of COVID-19 diagnostic tests, molecular and antigen, which detect different parts of the virus. Most authorized at-home OTC COVID-19 tests are antigen tests, and there are also a small number of authorized at-home OTC COVID-19 molecular tests.

Concept note-2: -This is the first locally made PCR based COVID-19 test kit approved by the FDA which was developed in collaboration with the University of the Philippines-National Institute of Health (UP-NIH), funded by the Department of Science and Technology (DOST).

Concept note-3: -Viral tests look for a current infection with SARS-CoV-2, the virus that causes COVID-19, by testing specimens from your nose or mouth. There are two main types of viral tests: nucleic acid amplification tests (NAATs) and antigen tests. In certain circumstances, one test type may be recommended over the other.

Concept note-4: -Coviself By Mylab-India’s First Rapid Antigen Self Test Kit.
