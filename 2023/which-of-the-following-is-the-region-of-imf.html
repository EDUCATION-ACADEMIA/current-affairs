Concept note-1: -IMF headquarters has two buildings: HQ1 visitors’ entrance is at 720 19th Street, NW and HQ2 visitors’ entrance is at the corner of 19th St and Pennsylvania Avenue. For more information about the Washington, DC metro, see Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority.

Concept note-2: -The International Monetary Fund (IMF) is an organization of 190 countries, working to foster global monetary cooperation, secure financial stability, facilitate international trade, promote high employment and sustainable economic growth, and reduce poverty around the world.

Concept note-3: -We Are A Global Organization The IMF is governed by and accountable to those 190 countries that make up its near-global membership.

Concept note-4: -Out of the total 196 countries of the world, 189 countries are members of the International Monetary Fund or IMF. The countries that are not a part of the IMF are Cuba, North Korea, Monaco, Taiwan, Vatican City, and East Timor Liechtenstein.
