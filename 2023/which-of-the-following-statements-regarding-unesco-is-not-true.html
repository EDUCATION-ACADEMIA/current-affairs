Which of the following statements regarding UNESCO is not true?

(A) Its full name is United Nations Educational Scientific and Cultural Organization.

(B) It was established on November 4, 1946.

(C) Its headquarters are located at the Place de Fontenoy in Paris, France.

(D) ** All three above are true.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution: The correct answer is Gateway of India. United Nations Educational, Scientific, and Cultural Organisation is the full form of UNESCO.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Jantar Mantar, New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Matheran Hill Railway.
