Which of the following states is not located in the North?

(A) ** Jharkhand

(B) Jammu and Kashmir

(C) Himachal Pradesh

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jammu and Kashmir C. Himachal Pradesh D. Haryana? JHARKHAND IS NOT LOCATED IN NORTH INDIA.

Concept note-2: -Latitude-based definition Indian states that are entirely above the Tropic of Cancer are Himachal Pradesh, Punjab, Haryana, Delhi, Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh and Bihar and most of North East Indian states.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: Jharkhand state is not located in the North. It is located in the northeastern part of the country. It is bordered by the states of Bihar to the north, West Bengal to the east, Odisha to the south, Chhattisgarh to the west, and Uttar Pradesh to the northwest.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Sikkim. The seven sister states of India from north to south are Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Manipur, Tripura, and Mizoram.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Hemis National Park. Hemis National Park is NOT located in the Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir.
