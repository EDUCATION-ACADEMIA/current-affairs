Which of these is not an international airport in Kerala?

(A) Kannur International Airport

(B) Kochi International Airport

(C) Kozhikode International Airport

(D) ** Kottayam International Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Trivandrum International Airport in Shankumugham in state capital of Thiruvananthapuram district. Cochin International Airport in Nedumbassery in Ernakulam district. Calicut International Airport in Karipur in border of Malappuram and Kozhikode districts. Kannur International Airport in Mattanur in Kannur district.

Concept note-2: -Hence, the correct solution for the above question is option (c), i.e. Palam airport. Note:1.

Concept note-3: -At present, there are four operational international airports in Kerala – Thiruvananthapuram, Kochi, Kozhikode, and Kannur.
