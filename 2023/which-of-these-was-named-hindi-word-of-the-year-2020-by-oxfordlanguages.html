Which of these was named Hindi word of the year 2020 by OxfordLanguages?

(A) Nari Shakti

(B) Aadhaar

(C) ** Aatmanirbharta

(D) Samvidhaan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Aatmanirbharta’ implying self-reliance has been named by Oxford Languages as its Hindi word of the year 2020 as it “validated the day-to-day achievements of the countless Indians who dealt with and survived the perils of a pandemic”.

Concept note-2: -The Oxford Hindi Word of the Year is a word or expression that is chosen to reflect the ethos, mood, or preoccupations of the passing year, and have lasting potential as a term of cultural significance. The Oxford Hindi Word of the Year 2020 is… Aatmanirbharta or Self-Reliance.

Concept note-3: -Explained: How was ‘Aatmanirbharta’ chosen as the Hindi Word of the Year by Oxford Languages?

Concept note-4: -Goblin mode: Oxford Word of the Year 2022-Times of India.

Concept note-5: -Besides these words, a few other words that were picked by the Oxford dictionary are-Bas, Jhuggi, Funda, Nivas, Gully, Natak, Sevak, Sevika, Chup, Surya Namaskar, Desh, Diya, Bada Din among many others.
