Which organisation has won the UNEP’s Asia Environmental Enforcement Award-2020 underInnovation category?

(A) Central Zoo Authority

(B) ** Wildlife Crime Control Bureau (WCCB)

(C) National Ganga River Basin Authority (NGRBA)

(D) National Tiger Conservation Authority (NTCA)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Wildlife Crime Control Bureau. The Wildlife Crime Control Bureau (WCCB) has received the Asia Environmental Enforcement Award-2020 awarded by the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP).

Concept note-2: -UNEP Award WCCB has been conferred this award in the Innovation category. WCCB has adopted innovative enforcement techniques that have dramatically increased enforcement of trans-boundary environmental crimes in India.

Concept note-3: -MS. NENENG KURNIASIH, INVESTIGATOR, DIRECTORATE-GENERAL FOR LAW ENFORCEMENT, MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT AND FORESTRY (INDONESIA)

Concept note-4: -The Asia Environmental Enforcement Awards (hereafter the ‘Awards’) publicly recognize and celebrate excellence in enforcement by government officials and institutions or teams combating transboundary environmental crime.

Concept note-5: -NEW DELHI: Recognizing commendable work done by senior IPS officer Tilotama Varma in the wildlife sector, she was announced as one of the winners of the 2022 Asia Environmental Enforcement Awards (AEEA) in the category of Gender Leadership and Women’s Empowerment.
