Which player is nominated by BCCI for the Rajiv Gandhi Khel ratna Award 2020?

(A) K.l.Rahul

(B) ** Rohit Sharma

(C) Virat Kohli

(D) Shikhar Dhawan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) on Saturday nominated India opener Rohit Sharma for the prestigious Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award 2020 while pacer Ishant Sharma, Shikhar Dhawan and India women’s cricketer Deepti Sharma have been nominated for the Arjuna Awards.

Concept note-2: -He is only the fourth Indian cricketer to receive this award. We are proud of you, Hitman!

Concept note-3: -Batsman Rohit Sharma is among the five athletes who will receive the country’s highest sporting honour, the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award, for 2020. Rohit has become just the fourth cricketer who will be conferred the prestigious award after Sachin Tendulkar (1997-98), MS Dhoni (2007) and Virat Kohli (2018).
