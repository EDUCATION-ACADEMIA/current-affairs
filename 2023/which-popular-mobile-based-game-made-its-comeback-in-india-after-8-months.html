Which popular mobile based game made its comeback in India after 8 months?

(A) Call of Duty

(B) Free Fire

(C) ** PUBG

(D) Fortnite

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In Short. South Korean developer Krafton’s BGMI was banned by the Indian government over safety concerns. In November 2022, Krafton issued a press release in which it said that the company will continue trying to bring the game back.

Concept note-2: -According to the possibilities that are coming out regarding BGMI 2.4 Beta Update, the target has been set from January 11, 2023 to January 15, 2023.

Concept note-3: -First released at the start of 2017, PUBG: Battlegrounds (previously known as PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds) is one of the most popular games on Steam and had over 425.49 thousand peak concurrent players in the last recorded month, November 2022.
