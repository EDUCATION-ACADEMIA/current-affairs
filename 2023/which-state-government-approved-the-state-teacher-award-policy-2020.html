Which state government approved the State Teacher Award Policy 2020?

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) ** Haryana Haryana

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CHANDIGARH: The Haryana government has approved the ‘State Teacher Award Policy-2020’. This policy will be deemed applicable from August 29, 2020 in place of the policy formulated in the year 2016.

Concept note-2: -As an award, each awardee teacher will be given a cash prize of Rs. 1 lakh, a silver medal, a certificate, a shawl and two advance increments including dearness allowance for future service.

Concept note-3: -How much does a Teacher at Government of Haryana make? The typical Government of Haryana Teacher salary is ₹58, 930. Teacher salaries at Government of Haryana can range from ₹36, 425-₹1, 00, 000.

Concept note-4: -The number of awards at present stands at 378. The break up of which is as under: (I) 355 Awards States/UTs, Kendriya Vidyalayas, Navodaya Vidyalayas, Atomic Energy Education Society, Sainik Schools, Sanskrit And Arabic/Persian teachers.
