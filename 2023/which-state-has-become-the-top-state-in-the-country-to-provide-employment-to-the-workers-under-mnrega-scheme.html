Which state has become the top state in the country to provide employment to the workers under MNREGA scheme?

(A) Gujarat

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Rajasthan

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Not only this, in the financial year 2022-23, 2, 77, 878 families have been provided 100 days of full employment and Uttar Pradesh ranks first in the country in providing 100 days of full employment.

Concept note-2: -Uttar Pradesh has become the top state in the country to provide employment to the workers under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme MGNREGA.

Concept note-3: -Colorado ranks first in the nation for employment. Utah – No. 1 in the economy category overall – places second in this subcategory, followed by Massachusetts, Idaho and Virginia. Learn more about the Best States for employment below.

Concept note-4: -The mandate of the MGNREGA is to provide at least 100 days of guaranteed wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.

Concept note-5: -MGNREGA aims to enhance livelihood security in rural areas by providing at least 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.
