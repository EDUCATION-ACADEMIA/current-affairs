Which state has recently issued orders to limit the retail price of bottled drinking water?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Karnataka

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) ** Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -The Kerala High Court has stayed the order from the state government that reduced the price of packaged drinking water from Rs 20 to Rs 13. By Rickson Oommen: The Kerala High Court has stayed the state government order reducing the maximum retail price (MRP) of packaged drinking water from Rs 20 to Rs 13.
