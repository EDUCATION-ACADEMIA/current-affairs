Which state has recently sanctioned Rs 686 crore for education scheme?

(A) Punjab

(B) ** Andhra Pradesh

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Jharkhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jagananna Vidya Deevena is a program launched by the Government of Andhra Pradesh to encourage students hailing from poor economic backgrounds to pursue higher education by reimbursing full fee which includes the tuition, special and examination fees.

Concept note-2: -Fee repayment Under Jagananna Vidya Deewana Scheme YS Jagan Mohan Reddy, the Andhra Pradesh’s CM, released cash for the Scheme on 16 March 2023, and deposited them straight into the bank accounts of the students’ moms. The recipients’ bank accounts have been credited with a sum of ₹709 crore.

Concept note-3: -AP Vidya Deevena Scheme 2023 The Vidya Deevena scheme is for students who want to pursue their higher education like polytechnic, graduation, engineering, and PG courses, but are unable to pay their fees. These students can now avail the facility of fee reimbursement through this scheme.
