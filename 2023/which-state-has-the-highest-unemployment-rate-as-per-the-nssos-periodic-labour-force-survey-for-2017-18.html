(A) Goa

(B) Bihar

(C) Manipur

(D) ** Nagaland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Haryana has the highest unemployment rate, whereas, Odisha has the lowest unemployment rate in India. Which State Has The Highest Employment Rate In India? Concept note-2: -The figures on the CMIE website indicated that while the urban unemployment rate rose to 10.09% in December from 8.96% the previous month, the rural jobless rate decreased to 7.44% from 7.55%.
