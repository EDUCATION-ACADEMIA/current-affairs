Which state is also known as the “Fruit Bowl” of India?

(A) Jammu and Kashmir

(B) ** Himachal Pradesh

(C) Assam

(D) Meghalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This is the reason why Himachal is also called the “Fruit and vegetable bowl of India”. Manufacturing, agriculture, and allied activities together accounted for 55 per cent of Himachal Pradesh’s GDP in 2020-21.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution Himachal Pradesh has covered 1.7% of india with apricot pear, apple, orange. So Himachal Pradesh is also called as fruit bowl of India.

Concept note-3: -Himachal Pradesh is also known as Dev Bhoomi, meaning ‘Land of Gods’ and Veer Bhoomi which means ‘Land of the Brave’. The predominantly mountainous region comprising the present-day Himachal Pradesh has been inhabited since pre-historic times, having witnessed multiple waves of human migrations from other areas.

Concept note-4: -Hence, the correct answer is Himachal Pradesh. Himachal Pradesh covers India’s area 1.7%, it is the 18th state in Terms of Country.
