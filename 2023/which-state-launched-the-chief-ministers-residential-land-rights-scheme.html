Which state launched the ‘Chief Minister’s Residential Land Rights scheme’?

(A) Gujarat

(B) ** Madhya Pradesh

(C) Assam

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In Madhya Pradesh, Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan today launched the Chief Minister’s Residential Land Rights scheme (Mukhyamantri Awasiya Bhu Adhikar Yojana) in a programme organised at the Bagaj Mata temple complex in Tikamgarh district.

Concept note-2: -An Act to provide for the conferring of leasehold rights on landless persons in respect of sites for dwelling house in urban areas in the State of Madhya Pradesh. Madhya Pradesh Nagariya Kshetro ke bhoomihin Vyakti (Pattadhruti Adhikaron Ka Pradan kiya Jana) Adhiniyam, 1984.

Concept note-3: -The first Governor of Madhya Pradesh Shri B. P. Sitaramaiya was administered the oath of office at the Vidhan Sabha. The first Chief Minister of M.P. Shri Ravi Shankar Shukla was also sworn in at the Vidhan Sabha.

Concept note-4: -Shivraj Singh Chouhan of the Bharatiya Janata Party is the longest-serving and current incumbent.

Concept note-5: -Shri Shivraj Singh Chauhan Hon’ble Chief Minister | Law and Legislative Affairs Department, Government of Madhya Pradesh.
