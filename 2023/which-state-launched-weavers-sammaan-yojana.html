Which state launched Weavers’ Sammaan Yojana?

(A) Telangana

(B) ** Karnataka

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Karnataka Nekar Samman Yojana Kar Weaver Samman Scheme Apply Online, Application Form 2023 at sevasindhu.karnataka.gov.in. Karnataka Nekar Samman Yojana Apply Online, Application Form – The Nekar Samman Yojana for handloom weavers has been launched by Karnataka Chief Minister on 6 July 2020.

Concept note-2: -What is Karnataka Nekar Samman Yojana 2023? The Nekar Samman Scheme was launched on 6th July 2023 by the Karnataka Chief Minister BS Yeddyurappa for the weaver’s community. In the pandemic of COVID-19, to help the handloom weavers, the CM announced this scheme to support them by providing one-time financial aid.

Concept note-3: -The beneficiaries under the scheme are provided yearly assistance of Rs. 2000. the amount is transferred to their registered bank accounts through DBT mode.

Concept note-4: -On July 6, 2020, the state Government of Karnataka launched the “Weavers Samman Yojana”. Under the scheme, 19, 744 handloom weavers will be assisted financially.

Concept note-5: -Accordingly, fill the Nekar Samman Yojana online registration form and submit it to avail the scheme benefits. Applicants can visit the official website of Department of Handlooms and Textiles of Karnataka Govt. at http://www.karnatakadht.org or Contact the Helpline number at 080 235 61628.
