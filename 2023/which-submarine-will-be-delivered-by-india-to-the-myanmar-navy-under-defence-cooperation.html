Which submarine will be delivered by India to the Myanmar Navy under defence cooperation?

(A) INS Arihant

(B) ** INS Sindhuveer

(C) INS Virat

(D) INS Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"India will be delivering a kilo class submarine INS Sindhuvir to the Myanmar Navy. This will be the first submarine in the Myanmar navy. This is in accordance with our vision of SAGAR-Security and Growth for All in the Region, ” Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) spokesperson Anurag Shrivastava said.

Concept note-2: -The submarine was renamed UMS Minye Theinkhathu in honor of a Myanmar historical hero, and it was then displayed at a navy fleet drill. Myanmar was chastised by China’s Global Times for receiving the Indian Navy’s Sindhuvir offer.

Concept note-3: -In a largely unexpected development, the Myanmar Navy commissioned its second submarine – a Chinese-origin Type 035 Ming class – as UMS Minye Kyaw Htin on its 74th Navy Day event on 24 December 2021.

Concept note-4: -Myanmar officially inducted the Kilo Class Submarine, ‘INS Sindhuvir’ into its Navy under the name ‘UMS Minye Theinkhathu’.
