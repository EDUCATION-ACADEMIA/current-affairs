Which TV series won the 72nd Emmy award in the Outstanding Drama Series category?

(A) Ozark

(B) Stranger Things

(C) ** Succession

(D) The Crown

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Succession and Watchmen each won four awards, including Outstanding Drama Series and Outstanding Limited Series, respectively.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Game of Thrones. Game of Thrones won the award in outstanding drama series category.

Concept note-3: -Mike White, the show’s creator and director, won a pair of Emmys for best directing and writing. And performers from the show, Murray Bartlett and Jennifer Coolidge, both received acting Emmys.

Concept note-4: -“Succession, ” which had the most nominations, with 25, took home awards for best drama and writing for a drama series, and Matthew Macfadyen took home the Emmy for best supporting actor in a drama.

Concept note-5: -Succession wins Outstanding drama series. Ted Lasso wins Outstanding comedy series.
