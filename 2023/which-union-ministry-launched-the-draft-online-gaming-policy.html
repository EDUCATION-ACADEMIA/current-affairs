Which Union Ministry launched the ‘Draft Online Gaming Policy’?

(A) ** Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology

(B) Ministry of Science and Technology

(C) Ministry of Home Affairs

(D) Ministry of Information and Broadcasting

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) released the draft rules on January 2 and had asked stakeholders to submit their views by January 17.

Concept note-2: -In December last year, the Centre appointed the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) as the nodal ministry for online gaming. Last month, the MeitY released draft rules to regulate the content of online gaming while proposing a ban on betting and wagering.

Concept note-3: -Online Gaming Law in India Therefore, as of now the subject of gaming in India is governed by the respective state governments as per the respective state legislations enacted by them. In India, online gaming is not prohibited. Online gaming with stakes or real money is allowed in most of the states of India.
