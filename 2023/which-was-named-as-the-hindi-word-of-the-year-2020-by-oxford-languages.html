WHICH WAS NAMED AS THE HINDI WORD OF THE YEAR 2020 BY OXFORD LANGUAGES?

(A) AADHAR

(B) ** AATMANIRBHAR

(C) NARI SHAKTI

(D) KRISHI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW
