Who accepted the Republican nomination for President of the United States?

(A) Ted Cruz

(B) Marco Rubio

(C) ** Donald Trump

(D) John Kasich

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Trump has officially run as a candidate for president four times, in 2000, 2016, 2020, and 2024; he also “unofficially” campaigned in 2012 and mulled a run in 2004.

Concept note-2: -On March 4, 2008, John McCain became the Republican presumptive presidential nominee when he obtained the 1, 191 delegates necessary to receive the party’s nomination.

Concept note-3: -Biden earned enough delegates on Super Tuesday 2020 to pull ahead of Sanders. On April 8, after Sanders suspended his campaign, Biden became the presumptive Democratic presidential nominee.
