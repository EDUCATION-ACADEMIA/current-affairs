Who among the following has been appointed as the first Chief Justice of the newly-constituted Andhra Pradesh High Court?

(A) ** Vikram Nath

(B) N. V. Ramana

(C) Uday U. Lalit

(D) S. Abdul Nazeer

(E) Indu Malhotra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Justice Vikram Nath was recommended for first Chief Justice of new Andhra Pradesh HC, following the bifurcation of a combined High Court for Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, but the Centre disapproved the recommendations.

Concept note-2: -Hon’ble Justice Koka Subba Rao was the first Chief Justice of erstwhile High Court of Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -Venkata Jyothirmai and V. Gopalakrishna Rao were sworn in as additional judges of the Andhra Pradesh High Court (HC) by Chief Justice Prashant Kumar Mishra on Friday.

Concept note-4: -Sri Justice B. Krishna Mohan Hon’ble Justice B. Krishna Mohan was born on 05-02-1965 at Guntur. His Lordship’s father late Sri B.S. R. Anjaneyulu was a District and Sessions Judge in Andhra Pradesh State Higher Judicial Service.

Concept note-5: -Justice Harilal Jekisundas Kania was the first (Indian) Chief Justice of India.
