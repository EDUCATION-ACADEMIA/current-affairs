Who among the following players has been rested for India’s tour of West Indies in August 2019?

(A) Virat Kohli

(B) Rohit Sharma

(C) KL Rahul

(D) ** MS Dhoni

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For India, Shikhar Dhawan, who was ruled out of the World Cup after sustaining an injury to his left hand, has made his way to the limited-overs squads while Rishabh Pant has been named as the wicket-keeper across all three formats as veteran wicket-keeper batsman MS Dhoni has been rested.

Concept note-2: -India T20I Squad: Virat Kohli (captain), Rohit Sharma (vice-captain), Shikhar Dhawan (replaced by Sanju Samson), KL Rahul, Shreyas Iyer, Manish Pandey, Rishabh Pant (wicket-keeper), Shivam Dube, Washington Sundar, Ravindra Jadeja, Yuzvendra Chahal, Kuldeep Yadav, Deepak Chahar, Mohammed Shami, Bhuvneshwar Kumar.

Concept note-3: -Rohan Kanhai, West Indies The former West Indies cricketer was born in Guyana but has his family origins from India. Kanhai played in 79 Test matches and scored 6, 227 runs with 15 centuries and 28 fifties.

Concept note-4: -Shikhar Dhawan named captain of India’s ODI squad for West Indies tour, Rohit Sharma and Virat Kohli rested.

Concept note-5: -Star West Indies spin bowler of the 1950s Sonny Ramadhin died on Sunday aged 92. He was the first player of Indian origin to play for the West Indies.
