(A) Srinivasan Jain

(B) ** Ritika Chopra

(C) Maryam Alavi

(D) Lakshmi Subramaniam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ritika Chopra, National Education Editor at The Indian Express, won the award for the year 2020, for her report on differences within the Election Commission of India on how to deal with violations of the model code of conduct.

Concept note-3: -The India chapter of the International Press Institute, IPI-India, on Friday conferred the Award for Excellence in Journalism 2022 jointly to The Print and NDTV correspondent Saurabh Shukla.

Concept note-4: -The logo of the International Press Institute | Twitter globalfreemedia. New Delhi: ThePrint has been awarded with the prestigious International Press Institute (IPI) India Award for Excellence in Journalism 2022 for its coverage of the Covid-19 pandemic.
