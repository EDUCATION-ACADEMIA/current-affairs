Who are the beneficiaries of the ‘KCR Kit Scheme’ of Telangana?

(A) ** Pregnant women

(B) Farmers

(C) MSMEs

(D) Doctors

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The items provided in the Rs. 15, 000 worth KCR Kit would be useful for the new born for up to 3 months. ‘KCR Kits’ would be distributed to pregnant woman from 4 June. The state government under the scheme would also provide a financial assistance of Rs. 12000 to working pregnant women to cover the wage loss.

Concept note-2: -This scheme provides financial incentives to the women who gave birth to the child in the government hospitals/PHC Centre’s as well as KCR Kit consisting of 16 items necessary to keep newborn babies warm and hygienic.

Concept note-3: -About Arogya Lakshmi Scheme 2022 The Government of Telangana has launched Arogya Lakshmi Scheme. Through this scheme, pregnant and lactating women are provided one full meal at the Anganwadi centre along with iron and folic acid tablets. Spot feeding of the meal is ensured through this scheme.

Concept note-4: -The Telangana government has launched a scheme named Arogya Lakshmi Scheme. The scheme will provide one full meal to the pregnant and lactating women of the state. The meal will be provided at the Anganwadi centres.
