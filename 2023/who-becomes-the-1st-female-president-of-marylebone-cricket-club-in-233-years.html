Who becomes the 1st female president of Marylebone Cricket Club in 233 years?

(A) ** Clare Connor

(B) Meg Lanning

(C) Marizanne Kapp

(D) Smriti Mandhana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Clare Connor, the former England captain, will become the first woman president of Marylebone Cricket Club next year.

Concept note-2: -Former England captain Clare Connor on Friday took charge as the MCC (Marylebone Cricket Club) president, becoming the first woman to hold the post in the club’s 234-year-old history.

Concept note-3: -Former England captain Clare Connor is set to become the first-ever female president of the Marylebone Cricket Club in its 233-year history.

Concept note-4: -Former England skipper Clare Connor takes office as MCC’s first female President.

Concept note-5: -VVS Laxman, the former batsman, has been awarded the honorary life membership of Marylebone Cricket Club. Anjum Chopra is the first Indian woman cricketer to be awarded an honorary life membership of the Marylebone Cricket Club.
