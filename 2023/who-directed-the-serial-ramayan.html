Who directed the Serial RAMAYAN?

(A) ** Rama Nand Sagar

(B) Rahi Masoom Raza

(C) B C Chopra

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ramayan (also known as Ramanand Sagar’s Ramayan) is an Indian Hindi-language epic television series based on ancient Indian Sanskrit Epic Ramayana. The show was created, written, and directed by Ramanand Sagar.

Concept note-2: -Chandramauli Chopra (/tʃhɑːndrɑːmɑːuli ˈtʃhoʊprɑː/ Chan-druh-mao-li Ch-aupra; 29 December 1917 – 12 December 2005) known professionally as Ramanand Sagar, was an Indian filmmaker, editor, playwright, poet and an author. He is best known for making the television show Ramayan (1987-1988).

Concept note-3: -Arun Govil (/ɑːrun ɡoʊvɪl/ GO-will; born 12 January 1958) is an Indian actor who works in Hindi films and television. He is best known for portraying the role of Lord Rama in Ramanand Sagar’s epic Television Series Ramayan and King Vikramāditya in Vikram Aur Betaal.
