Who has become No.1 all-rounder in latest ICC test ranking (July 2020)?

(A) Hardik pandya

(B) Ravindra Jadeja

(C) ** Ben stokes

(D) Jason holder

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ravindra Jadeja has progressed seven places to take the ninth spot in the ICC Test bowling rankings after his 10-wicket match haul against Australia in the second Test in Delhi. This is the first time Jadeja, the no. 1 Test all-rounder, has breached the top-10 in the bowlers’ rankings since September 2019.

Concept note-2: -2022, Shakib Al Hasan is the best all rounder in the world in ODI cricket. He has 372 rating points in the ODI all rounder rankings.
