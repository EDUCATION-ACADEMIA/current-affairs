Who has become the second-highest active international goal scorer?

(A) ** Sunil Chhetri

(B) Ali Mabkhout

(C) Neymar

(D) Luis Suarez

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cristiano Ronaldo, Lionel Messi, along with India’s Sunil Chhetri, are the top three active international goal scorers in men’s football.

Concept note-2: -Sunil Chhetri is currently the highest active international goal-scorer in Asia. He is also the fourth-highest goal-scorer in history and the third-highest active goal-scorer in international football.

Concept note-3: -Sunil Chhetri | 84 Goals The 38-year-old is 3rd on the list of active footballers with the most international goals (84) to their name. FIFA recently honoured him for his achievements with a 3-episode series on his life and career.
