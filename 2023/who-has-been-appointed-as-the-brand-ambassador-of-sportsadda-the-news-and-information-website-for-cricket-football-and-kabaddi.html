Who has been appointed as the brand ambassador of SportsAdda, the news and information website for Cricket, Football and Kabaddi?

(A) Sachin Tendulkar

(B) Mahendra Singh Dhoni

(C) Rickey Ponting

(D) Michael Clarke

(E) ** Brett Lee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Option 2 is correct i.e. Brett Lee.

Concept note-4: -The Uttarakhand government has named Prasoon Joshi, the CEO and CCO of McCann Worldgroup India, the state’s brand ambassador.

Concept note-5: -Deepika Padukone is the brand ambassador of Adidas in India.
