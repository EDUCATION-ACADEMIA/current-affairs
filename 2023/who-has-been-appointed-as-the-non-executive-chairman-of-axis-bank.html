Who has been appointed as the non-executive chairman of Axis bank?

(A) Sandeep Bakshi

(B) Raunak Gill

(C) Hemant Bhargav

(D) ** Rakesh Makhija

(E) Pradeep Mishra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -RBI approves Rakesh Makhija’s reappointment as Axis Bank’s Non-Executive Chairman.

Concept note-2: -The central government vide its notification dated November 7, 2022 has appointed Varadarajan as part-time non-official director as well as non-executive chairman of the bank for a period of three years or until further orders, whichever is earlier, the bank said in a regulatory filing.

Concept note-3: -Sumit Bali is the Group Executive and Head – Retail Lending & Payments Axis Bank. Sumit is a veteran in the banking industry with almost 3 decades of rich experience in Retail Banking. He was associated with Kotak Mahindra Bank for last 24 years, wherein he worked in multiple roles across Retail Lending.
