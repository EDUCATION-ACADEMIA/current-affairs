Who has been nominated the Rajya sabha member recently?

(A) Sachin Tendulkar

(B) ** Ranjan Gogoi

(C) L K Advani

(D) Amtratya Sen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Supreme Court Reinstates Staffer Who Accused Ex CJI Ranjan Gogoi of Sexual Harassment. After alleging sexual harassment by Gogoi, the woman was first transferred and then removed from service.

Concept note-2: -Ranjan Gogoi was the Chief Justice of India from October 3, 2018 to November 17, 2019. He was sworn in as a member of the Rajya Sabha on March 19, 2020. Gogoi is the son of former Assam Chief Minister Keshab Chandra Gogoi. He was elevated as the Chief Justice of Punjab & Haryana High Court on February 12, 2011.
