Who has been removed as the Deputy Chief Minister of Rajasthan?

(A) Vasundhara Raje

(B) Ashok Gehlot

(C) ** Sachin Pilot

(D) Milind Deora

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sachin Pilot removed as Deputy CM; Govind Singh Dotasara appointed new Rajasthan Cong Chief. The Congress party has dismissed Sachin Pilot from Rajasthan State Cabinet and also from the post of party’s state president. Cabinet Minister Vishvendra Singh and Ramesh Meena were also dismissed from the Rajasthan cabinet.

Concept note-2: -An aide of Pilot’s indicated that he would not be joining the Bharatiya Janata Party. On 14 July 2020, the Indian National Congress sacked Pilot as the Deputy Chief Minister and Rajasthan Congress President for his revolt against the party and its leadership.

Concept note-3: -Shri Manish Sisodia ( Deputy Chief Minister, Delhi ) | Official Website of Government of National Capital Territory of Delhi, India.
