Who has been selected as the first woman fighter pilot to fly Rafale aircraft?

(A) ** Shivangi Singh

(B) Shweta Sharma

(C) Nalini Vishwanathan

(D) Prerna Chaudhary

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first woman Rafale fighter jet pilot Shivangi Singh part of IAF tableau. The country’s first woman Rafale fighter jet pilot Shivangi Singh was part of the Indian Air Force tableau at the Republic Day parade on Wednesday.

Concept note-2: -NEW DELHI: Flight Lieutenant Shivangi Singh, the country’s first Rafale pilot, was a part of the Indian Air Force (IAF) tableau that featured in the Republic Day parade on Wednesday. She joined the IAF’s fighter stream in 2017 and flew the MiG-21 Bison aircraft before being posted to a Rafale squadron based in Ambala.

Concept note-3: -The first woman fighter pilot on the #Rafale combat aircraft, Flight Lieutenant Shivangi Singh at the Indian Air Force (IAF) tableau today.
