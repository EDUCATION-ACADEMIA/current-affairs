Who has taken the most wickets in IPL

(A) Sunil Narine

(B) Harbhajan Singh

(C) Amit Mishra

(D) ** Lasith Malinga

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He has taken 170 wickets in 122 IPL matches at an average of 19.80.

Concept note-2: -In IPL, Malinga has so far played 122 matches, in which he has taken 170 wickets at an average of 19.80 and the best bowling figure of 5 for 13.

Concept note-3: -Dwayne Bravo is the highest wicket-taker in IPL history with the total number of wickets taken is 183.

Concept note-4: -Royal Challengers Banglore pacer Harshal Patel tops the list of Most wickets in IPL season. The right-arm pacer was at his best during the IPL 2021 and took a total of 32 wickets in the season. He achieved that feat in 15 games at an impressive strike rate of 10.56.
