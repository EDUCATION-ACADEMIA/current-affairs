WHO IS APPOINTED AS THE NEW CHAIIRMAN OF SSC?

(A) JAI RAJ SINHA

(B) SURAJ SINHA

(C) ** BRAJ RAJ SHARMA

(D) RAKESH ASTHANA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Former Chief Secretary Braj Raj Sharma, has been appointed Chairman of the Staff Selection Commission (SSC). BR Sharma was presently posted as Secretary, Border Management, in the Ministry of Home Affairs.

Concept note-2: -The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved re-employment of Kishore as chairman, Staff Selection Commission in the rank and pay of Secretary to the government of India on contract basis for a period of one year beyond the date of his superannuation (31.01.

Concept note-3: -Staff Selection Commission 2022: Senior bureaucrat S. Kishore appointed as new SSC Chairman. Senior bureaucrat S. Kishore has been appointed as Chairman, Staff Selection Commission (SSC).

Concept note-4: -Chief Conservator of Forests, PGLI.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is S. Kishore.
