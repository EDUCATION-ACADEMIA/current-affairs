Who is currently playing Batman on the big screen?

(A) Ben Affleck

(B) Joe Biden

(C) ** Robert Pattinson

(D) Donald Trump

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For almost 80 years, Batman has been on the big screen. The character made his theatrical debut in 1943 with the Batman serial. Now, in 2022, Robert Pattinson will put on the cape and cowl for the first time in Matt Reeves’ The Batman.

Concept note-2: -The newest film iteration of the Caped Crusader is directed by Matt Reeves and stars Robert Pattinson as Batman/Bruce Wayne. The movie is already the highest-grossing film of 2022, but surprisingly, it’s the first stand-alone Batman movie in a decade.

Concept note-3: -’This is entirely untrue’: James Gunn Debunks Reports of Robert Pattinson’s Batman Replacing Ben Affleck in DCU.

Concept note-4: -The film was directed by Matt Reeves, who wrote the screenplay with Peter Craig. It stars Robert Pattinson as Bruce Wayne / Batman alongside Zoë Kravitz, Paul Dano, Jeffrey Wright, John Turturro, Peter Sarsgaard, Andy Serkis, and Colin Farrell.
