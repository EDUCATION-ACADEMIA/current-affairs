Who is known as the Iron Man of Bollywood?

(A) ** Millind Soman

(B) Shah Rukh Khan

(C) Dharam Deol (Dharmendra)

(D) Rajesh Khanna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Soman represented India in swimming in the inaugural South Asian Games (then known as South Asian Federation Games) in 1984 held at Kathmandu where he won a Silver medal. In 2015, Milind completed the Ironman challenge in 15 hours and 19 minutes, in his first try.

Concept note-2: -Shah Rukh Khan As Iron Man Talking about Tony Stark’s intellect, his quirky one-liners, and his overall charisma, Shah Rukh is the one Indian actor who comes to mind. Moreover, it was actually Shah Rukh who actually kicked the superhero mania in India by starring in Ra. One.

Concept note-3: -A few years back Milind completed the IRONMAN Triathlon which is one of the most difficult fitness challenges. The triathlon was held in Switzerland and because of this he won the title, Iron Man.

Concept note-4: -Milind ruled the hearts of kids with his role as superhero “Captain Vyom” in the science fiction TV show ‘Captain Vyom’ (1998).

Concept note-5: -To win the title of ‘Ironman, ’ participants are required to complete a 3.8 kilometre swim, a 180.2 kilometre-long bicycle ride, and a 42.2 kilometre run race in this exact order within 16 hours, so it goes without saying that he’s worthy of respect.
