Who is the author of the book titled ‘OVERDRAFT:SAVING THE INDIAN SAVER’?

(A) 1.Amartya Sen

(B) 2.Raghuram Rajan

(C) ** 3.Urjit Patel

(D) 4.ShaktiKanta Das

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Option 3 is correct: Former RBI governor, Urjit Patel has written a book titled ‘Overdraft: Saving the Indian Saver’.

Concept note-2: -Overdraft: Saving the Indian Saver by Urjit Patel | Goodreads.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Overdraft: Saving the Indian Saver. Urjit Patel has authored a Book named ‘Overdraft: Saving the Indian Saver’. This book focuses on the issue of Non-Performing Assets that have affected Indian banking in some past years and its effects. The book has been published by HarperCollins India.

Concept note-4: -C Rangarajan authored a book titled “Forks in the Road: My Days at RBI and Beyond”. It was published by the Penguin Business (Penguin Group). The book is the memoir of Dr C. Rangarajan, an Indian economist, a former Member of Parliament, and the 19th Governor of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI).

Concept note-5: -Dr Urjit R. Patel served as the 24th governor of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) between September 2016 and December 2018. Born on October 28, 1963, Patel’s tenure had seen the demonetisation, among other things.
