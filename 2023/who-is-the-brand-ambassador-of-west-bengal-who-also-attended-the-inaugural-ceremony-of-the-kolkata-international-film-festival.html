Who is the brand ambassador of West Bengal, who also attended the inaugural ceremony of the Kolkata International Film Festival?

(A) Salman Khan

(B) ** Shah Rukh Khan

(C) Anil Kapoor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: The 26th edition of the Kolkata International Film Festival (KIFF) was inaugurated in the state of West Bengal, by the state Chief Minister. The inaugural ceremony was also virtually attended by Bollywood star Shah Rukh Khan, who is also the brand ambassador of West Bengal.

Concept note-2: -Shah Rukh Khan is the brand ambassador for West Bengal and has been attending the Kolkata International Film Festival’s inauguration ceremonies for the past few years.

Concept note-3: -As per industry estimates, Khan charges over Rs 5-10 crore per day for brand endorsements.
