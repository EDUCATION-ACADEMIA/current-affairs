Who is the current chairman of Tata Consumer Products Limited (TCPL)?

(A) Ratan Tata

(B) Rakesh Sony

(C) ** N Chandrasekaran

(D) Saket Kanoria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr. Chandrasekaran is the Chairman of the Board at Tata Sons, the holding company and promoter of all Tata Group companies. His appointment as Chairman followed a 30-year business career at TCS, which he joined from university.

Concept note-2: -To streamline business operations, Tata Group in March 2022 announced a merger between TCL and TCPL. Following the effectiveness of the scheme, the shareholders of TCL (other than TCPL) as on record date will receive three equity shares of TCPL for every 10 equity shares held in TCL.

Concept note-3: -Pawan Satyawali-Global Chief Information Officer-Tata Consumer Products | LinkedIn.

Concept note-4: -Tata Trusts Names Siddharth Sharma As New CEO, Aparna Uppaluri As First COO.
