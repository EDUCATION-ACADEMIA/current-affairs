Who is the Current Chief Justice Of TamilNadu?

(A) ** (a) Justice Amreshwar Pratap Sahi

(B) (b) Justice Indira Banerjee

(C) (c) Justice Huluvadi G Ramesh

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -His lordship was elevated as Chief Justice of Patna High Court from 17.11. 2018. His Lordship was transferred to Madras High Court and sworn in as Chief Justice on 11.11. 2019.

Concept note-2: -Hon’ble Mr. Justice Munishwar Nath Bhandari, Chief Justice Born on 13/09/1960.

Concept note-3: -The new Chief Justice of India is Justice D.Y. Chandarchud who took his oath in presence of the President of India, Droupadi Murmu on 8th November 2022.

Concept note-4: -Amreshwar Pratap Sahi (born 1 January 1959) is an Indian Judge. He is former Chief Justice of Madras High Court, and Patna High Court and Judge of Allahabad High Court. Hon’ble Chief Justice Retd.

Concept note-5: -It was also the first instance that nine judges to the Supreme Court took oath at once. 7 of the 33 judges (excluding chief justice) are expected to eventually become Chief Justices of India. Justice Dhananjaya Y. Chandrachud is the 50th and current Chief Justice of India.
