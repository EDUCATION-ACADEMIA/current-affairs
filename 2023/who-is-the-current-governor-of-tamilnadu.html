Who is the Current governor Of TamilNadu

(A) J. Jayalalithaa

(B) ** Banwarilal Purohit

(C) K. Palaniswami

(D) None of These

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Banwarilal Purohit (born 16 April 1939) is an Indian politician. He is the current and 21st Governor of Tamil Nadu since October 2017. He was appointed as Governor of Assam State by President of India on 17 August 2016.

Concept note-2: -Honorable Thiru R.N. Ravi, Governor of Tamil Nadu is the Constitutional Head of the state. Hon’ble Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu Thiru M.K.Stalin is the Head of the elected Government and heads the Council of Ministers.

Concept note-3: -His father’s name is Bhagwandas Purohit, he is married to Pushpa Devi Purohit, and he has three children. Two sons Rajendra Purohit and Rakesh Purohit, daughter Meena Purohit Joshi. He has six grandchildren and a great grandson.
