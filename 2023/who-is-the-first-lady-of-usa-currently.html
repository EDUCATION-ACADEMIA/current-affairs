Who is the First Lady of USA currently?

(A) Jane Biden

(B) ** Jill Biden

(C) Mary Biden

(D) Julia Biden

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jill Tracy Biden (née Jacobs) (born June 3, 1951) is an American educator and the current first lady of the United States since 2021, as the wife of President Joe Biden.

Concept note-2: -Jill Biden, Ed.D., is the First Lady of the United States, a community college educator, a military mother, a grandmother, and bestselling author.

Concept note-3: -First Lady of the United States. First Lady Melania Trump is the wife of the 45th President, Donald J. Trump and the mother to their son, Barron Trump. She is the second First Lady born outside of the United States, and she is the only First Lady to become a naturalized United States citizen.

Concept note-4: -The Office of the First Lady is an entity of the White House Office, part of the Executive Office of the President. It is located in the East Wing.
