(A) Dr. Surjit S Bhalla

(B) G Rohini

(C) ** U K Sinha

(D) Rajiv Gauba

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Key Recommendations of the Committee: The committee also suggested forming a government-sponsored Fund of Funds of ₹10, 000 crore to support venture capital and private equity firms investing in MSMEs. The RBI should increase the limit for non-collateralized loans to 20 lakhs.

Concept note-2: -The U.K.Sinha Committee on Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises has made recommendations with regard to amendments in MSME Development Act, strengthening of financial delivery mechanism, improving marketing support, encouraging technology adoption and strengthening of cluster development support for MSMEs etc.
