Who is the Indian physicist responsible for the discovery of a subatomic particle?

(A) Jagdish Chandra Bose

(B) ** Satyendra Nath Bose

(C) Arya Bhatt

(D) Chandra Shekhar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the 1920s, Indian scientist Satyendra Nath Bose discovered one of the classes of particles, which came to be known as the boson . Bose worked with Albert Einstein to define the two classes of subatomic particles: fermions and bosons.

Concept note-2: -Satyendra Nath Bose FRS, MP (/ˈboʊs/; 1 January 1894 – 4 February 1974) was an Indian mathematician and physicist specializing in theoretical physics. He is best known for his work on quantum mechanics in the early 1920s, in developing the foundation for Bose statistics and the theory of the Bose condensate.
