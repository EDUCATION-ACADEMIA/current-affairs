Who is the only Indian whose birth anniversary has been celebrated by the United Nations?

(A) Mahatma Gandhiji

(B) Swami Vivekananda

(C) Jawaharlal Nehru

(D) ** Babasaheb Ambedkar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Babasaheb (Devanagari: , IAST: Bābāsāhēb) is an honorary title and given name. “Babasaheb” is a Marathi phrase which means “Respected Father” (Baba = father and Saheb = sir). This epithet is commonly applied to B. R. Ambedkar.

Concept note-2: -There are 195 countries in the world today. This total comprises 193 countries that are member states of the United Nations and 2 countries that are non-member observer states: the Holy See and the State of Palestine.

Concept note-3: -Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar (14 April 1891 – 6 December 1956), popularly known as Babasaheb, was an Indian jurist, economist, politician and social reformer who Chaired the Drafting Committee of the Constituent Assembly and was India’s First Minister for Law and Justice.

Concept note-4: -Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar was born on April 14, 1891 and to pay homage to his contribution to India, every year 14th April is celebrated as Ambedkar Jayanti. Ambedkar Jayanti or Bhim Jayanti is celebrated annually on April 14th in a bid to pay homage to the father of the Constitution of India Dr B.R.
