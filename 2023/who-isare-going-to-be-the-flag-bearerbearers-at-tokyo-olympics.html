Who is/are going to be the flag bearer/bearers at Tokyo Olympics?

(A) Manpreet Singh

(B) Mary Kom

(C) ** Both A and B

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For the first time in the Olympics, India will have two flag-bearers at the opening ceremony of the Summer Games at Tokyo 2020: Mary Kom and Manpreet Singh.

Concept note-2: -Tokyo Olympics medallists-weightlifter Mirabai Chanu and boxer Lovlina Borgohain-are the other two athletes, who were also considered before IOA decided to go for Sindhu. “It’s with great pleasure that we announce P. V. Sindhu as the flagbearer for Team India, ” acting IOA president Anil Khanna said in a release.

Concept note-3: -Usually at the Olympics, each country will select one athlete to carry the flag during the Games’ opening ceremony, though on occasion, more than one has been chosen before.
