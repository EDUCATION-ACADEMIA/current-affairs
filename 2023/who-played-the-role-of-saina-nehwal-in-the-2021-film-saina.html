Who played the role of Saina Nehwal in the 2021 film Saina?

(A) ** Parineeti Chopra

(B) Shraddha Kapoor

(C) Alia Bhatt

(D) Anushka Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian badminton player Saina Nehwal’s biopic, titled Saina, was released on March 26, 2021. With Parineeti Chopra in the lead role, the Bollywood movie–celebrating the Indian badminton star’s journey–completed shooting in 2020.

Concept note-2: -Naishaa Kaur Bhatoye – that’s the name of the girl who is playing the role of little Saina in the film. Not only is Naishaa a brilliant actor, as one can judge from the glimpses of the trailer, but she is also one of India’s brightest young prospects in the sport of badminton.

Concept note-3: -At least Manav Kaul (who plays Saina’s fictional coach, Rajan) does a fairly neat job, striking a balance between what is required of his character and what is mentioned in point C.

Concept note-4: -Saina Nehwal, still playing, still fighting-Sportstar.
