Who took charged as MD of LIC?

(A) Isha Mandal

(B) ** Mini Ipe

(C) Ila Kumari

(D) Neha Sejwal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ms. Mini Ipe is a post graduate in Commerce from Andhra University and has joined LIC in 1986 as a Direct Recruit Officer. She has rich and diverse experience in LIC having worked in various capacities. Prior to taking charge as Managing Director, she was Executive Director, Legal Department, LIC of India.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is B C Patnaik. B C Patnaik has taken charge as the Managing Director (MD) of Life Insurance Corporation (LIC).

Concept note-3: -T S Ramakrishnan is the MD of LIC 2022.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Siddhartha Mohanty. Siddhartha Mohanty: The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the appointment of Siddhartha Mohanty as the new Managing Director of the Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) of India. Hence, Option 1 is correct.
