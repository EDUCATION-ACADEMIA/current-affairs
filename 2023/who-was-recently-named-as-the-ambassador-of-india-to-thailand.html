Who was recently named as the Ambassador of India to Thailand?

(A) ** Nagesh Singh

(B) Mukesh Ambani

(C) Abhimanyu Verma

(D) Sanchit Arora

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nagesh Singh was Chief of Protocol of the Ministry of External Affairs from July 2018 to October 2022.

Concept note-2: -Q. Who was recently appointed as the Ambassador of India to the United States? Notes: Veteran diplomat Taranjit Singh Sandhu has been recently appointed as the next Indian Ambassador to the United States. He replaces Harsh Vardhan Shringla, who was the previous Indian envoy to the US.

Concept note-3: -IFS Nagesh Singh has been appointed as India’s next ambassador to Thailand.

Concept note-4: -Naveen Srivastava assumed charge as the Ambassador of India to Nepal on 25 June 2022. Ambassador Srivastava joined the Indian Foreign Service in 1993, and began his diplomatic career in Hong Kong.

Concept note-5: -Reenat Sandhu is the Ambassador of India to the Kingdom of the Netherlands and is also responsible for India’s relations with OPCW, ICJ, PCA and other legal institutions in The Hague.
