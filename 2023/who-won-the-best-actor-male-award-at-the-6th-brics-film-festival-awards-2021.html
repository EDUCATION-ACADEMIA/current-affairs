Who won the Best Actor (Male) Award at the 6th BRICS Film Festival Awards 2021?

(A) Pankaj Tripathi

(B) Surya Sivakumar

(C) Manoj Bajpayee

(D) ** Dhanush

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian actor Dhanush won the Best Actor (Male) award for his role in Asuran, a film on a farmer father-son duo (both played by Dhanush) who fights against the archaic systems of the society.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Who has been awarded as the “Best Actor Male” at the BRICS Film Festival? Notes: Indian actor Dhanush has been awarded with the Best actor (Male) at the BRICS Film Festival for his role in Tamil movie ‘Asuran’, which was directed by Vetrimaaran.

Concept note-3: -The film also won three National Awards. Dhanush won Best Actor at BRICS film festival.
