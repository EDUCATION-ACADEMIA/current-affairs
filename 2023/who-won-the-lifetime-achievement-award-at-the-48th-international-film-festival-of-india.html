Who won the Lifetime Achievement Award at the 48th International Film Festival of India?

(A) Amitabh Bachchan

(B) Vivian Qu

(C) ** Atom Egoyan

(D) Nahuel Perez Biscayart

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Smriti Irani presenting the Life Time Achievement Award to the Canadian film maker, Atom Egoyan, at the closing ceremony of the 48th International Film Festival of India (IFFI-2017), in Panaji, Goa on November 28, 2017.

Concept note-2: -“The 53rd International Film Festival of India (IFFI), held in Goa between 20th to 28th November 2022, will be honouring Spanish film director Carlos Saura with the Satyajit Ray Lifetime Achievement award.

Concept note-3: -IFFI 2022 full list of winners: I have Electric Dreams wins Best Film | Bollywood-Hindustan Times.

Concept note-4: -The Golden Peacock award went to the Spanish coming-of-age movie I Have Electric Dreams, while top acting honors went to Daniela Marin Navarro and Vahid Mobasheri.

Concept note-5: -The 1st edition of IFFI was organized by the Films Division, Government of India, with the patronage of the first Prime Minister of India. Held in Mumbai from 24 January to 1 February 1952, the Festival was subsequently taken to Madras, Delhi, Calcutta and Trivandrum.
