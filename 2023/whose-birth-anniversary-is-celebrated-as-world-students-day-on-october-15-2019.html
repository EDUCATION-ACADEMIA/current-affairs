Whose birth anniversary is celebrated as World Student’s Day on October 15, 2019?

(A) Swami Vivekananda

(B) Vallabhbhai Patel

(C) ** APJ, Abdul Kalam

(D) B. R. Ambedkar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Students’ Day 2022: World Students’ Day is widely celebrated on October 15 to mark the birth anniversary of former President APJ Abdul Kalam.

Concept note-2: -October 15 this year marks the 91st birth anniversary of former President of India Dr APJ Abdul Kalam.

Concept note-3: -Since 2010, World Student’s Day has been celebrated on October 15, on the birth anniversary of Indian scientist Dr APJ Abdul Kalam.

Concept note-4: -Abdul Kalam, former President of India, on his birth anniversary at Rashtrapati Bhavan today (October 15, 2022).

Concept note-5: -World Students’ Day is observed annually on October 15. The day commemorates the birthday of former Indian president Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam. A.P.J.
