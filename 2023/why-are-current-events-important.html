Why are current events important?

(A) Because we are going to war

(B) Because North Korea wants to rule us

(C) The U.S is shutting down

(D) ** Encourages you to explore and learn more about the news

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Keeping up with current events lets you know what is going on around the world, and therefore lets you learn about different cultures. News stories teach you what is acceptable in some cultures and what is not. They also teach you how different daily life in other cultures can be.

Concept note-2: -Current Affairs is one of the most important sections to read while preparing for any competitive exam. Bank Exams, UPSC, SSC, RRB, etc. demand candidates to be updated with everything happening around the world. It forms an integral part of the general awareness section.
