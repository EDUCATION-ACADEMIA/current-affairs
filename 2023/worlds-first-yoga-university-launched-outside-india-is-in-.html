World’s first Yoga University launched outside India is in ____

(A) Chicago

(B) ** Los Angeles

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Los Angeles. Dr. H R Nagendra will be the first chancellor of this University.

Concept note-2: -Vivekananda Yoga University (VaYU), USA is the 1st yoga university outside India focused on graduate yoga education and research. VaYU is located in Los Angeles, California.

Concept note-3: -SVYASA was founded in the year 2002, it is the first Yoga University of India and also of the World.

Concept note-4: -The Vivekananda Yoga University (VaYU) was jointly launched on Tuesday by Minister of State for External Affairs V Muraleedharan and Chairperson of the Standing Committee on External Affairs P P Chaudhary during a virtual event held at the Consulate General of India in New York.

Concept note-5: -It is not a secret anymore that India’s ancient tradition of Yoga has taken the whole world by storm. Now, Gujarat is ready to flaunt a full-fledged Yoga University in Ahmedabad.
