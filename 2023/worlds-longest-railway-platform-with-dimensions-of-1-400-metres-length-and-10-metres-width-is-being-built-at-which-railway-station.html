World’s longest railway platform with dimensions of 1, 400 metres length and 10 metres width is being built at which Railway station?

(A) ** Huballi

(B) Howrah

(C) Vijaywada

(D) Cuttack

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hubli Railway Station in Karnataka is the world’s longest railway station. Also known as Shree Siddharoodha Swamiji – Hubballi Junction, it is operated by the South Western Railways under the Hubli division. Platform 1 of the Hubbali station, with a length of 1, 505 metres, is the longest railway platform in the world.

Concept note-2: -The World’s Longest Railway Platform at Shri Siddharooda Swamiji (SSS) Hubballi junction railway station will be inaugurated by January 2023. Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the World’s Longest Railway Platform built at Hubballi Railway Station.

Concept note-3: -The platform number 1 of Hubli Junction has a length of 1, 505 metres, making it the longest railway platform in the world as of March 2021.

Concept note-4: -The longest Railway Platform in India is Hubli Railway Station in Karnataka.
