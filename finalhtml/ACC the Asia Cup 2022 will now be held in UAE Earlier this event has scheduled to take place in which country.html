CURRENT AFFAIRS-2022
[SOURCES]

ACC, the Asia Cup 2022 will now be held in UAE. Earlier this event has scheduled to take place in which country?

(A) India

(B) ** Sri Lanka

(C) Bangladesh

(D) Afghanistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Asian Cricket Council has confirmed that the Asia Cup will be held in the United Arab Emirates with Sri Lanka , the original hosts, retaining hosting rights.

Concept note-2: -On 27 July 2022, the ACC confirmed that the tournament would be played in the United Arab Emirates, with Sri Lanka Cricket serving as the tournament’s hosts. The fixtures for the tournament were announced on 2 August 2022.

Concept note-3: -Asia Cup 2023 Schedule will be held on 9 September 2023 between Pakistan vs Qualifying Team. Super 4 matches will start from 10 September 2023, under which the first match will start on 10 September itself under B1 vs B2. Similarly, on 11 September 2023, the second match of Super Four will be played between A 1 vs A 2.

Concept note-4: -Since Pakistan was awarded the hosting rights for the 2023 Asia Cup, there has been speculation that India will withdraw its name from the tournament.
