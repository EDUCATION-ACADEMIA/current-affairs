According to the Forbes 2022 Asia’s Power Businesswomen list, honouring 20 outstanding women in business across the Asia-Pacific region. Who among the following was named from India?

(A) Falguni Nayar

(B) ** Soma Mondal

(C) Suchi Mukherjee

(D) Roshni Nadar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Recently, Forbes Asia revealed its annual list of Asia’s Power Businesswomen featuring twenty women from the Asia-Pacific region. Ghazal Alagh, Namita Thapar and Soma Mandal are the three Indian women entrepreneurs who have been named in the 2022 Forbes list of powerful businessmen in the Asia-Pacific region.

Concept note-2: -Soma Mondal, Ghazal Alagh and Namita Thapar are the three women who have featured in Forbes Asia’s Power Businesswomen 2022 list.

Concept note-3: -Falguni Nayar, Roshni Nadar, Nirmala Sitharaman in Forbes’ World’s 100 Most Powerful Women list. Maintaining her position for the fifth time in a row, Finance Minister, Nirmala Sitharaman, was featured in the Forbes’ annual list of the World’s 100 Most Powerful Women.

Concept note-4: -Soma Mandal, Ghazal Alagh & Namita Thapar Entrepreneurs - Soma Mandal, Ghazal Alagh and Namita Thapar - made India proud after they got featured in the Forbes issue listing of 20 Asian businesswomen. Soman Mandal is the Chairperson of the Steel Authority of India Ltd.
