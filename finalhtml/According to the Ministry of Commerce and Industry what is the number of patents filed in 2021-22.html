According to the Ministry of Commerce and Industry, what is the number of patents filed in 2021-22?

(A) 19796

(B) ** 66, 440

(C) 30, 074

(D) 42, 763

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Filing of patents have increased from 42763 in 2014-15 to 66440 in 2021-22, more that 50% increase in a span of 7 years.

Concept note-2: -A total of 31,261 patent applications were granted in 2022 marking an increase of roughly 2.72% from 2021. The maximum grants came from the Delhi Patent Office which issued as many as 11,563 patents.

Concept note-3: -India received 61,573 patent applications in 2021, up from 56,771 in 2020. Almost 43% of the Indian patent applications were local, and filed by resident applicants.

Concept note-4: -The number of patent filings increased from 42,763 in 2014-15 to 66,440 in 2021-22 due to steps taken by the government to strengthen intellectual property rights regime of the country.
