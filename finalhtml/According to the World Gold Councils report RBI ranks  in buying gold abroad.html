According to the World Gold Council’s report, RBI ranks ____ in buying gold abroad?

(A) 4th

(B) ** 6th

(C) 8th

(D) 10th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With 132.34 metric tonnes of gold purchase, RBI emerged as the largest buyer of the yellow metal among central banks between April 2020 and September 2022. Gold has a very unusual lover.

Concept note-2: -The majority of the central bank buying in 2022 came from emerging markets, with the Central Bank of Turkey the largest buyer at a record 542 tons. China, India, Egypt, Qatar, Iraq, the UAE and Oman all significantly boosted their gold reserves over the year.

Concept note-3: -World Gold Council Report: India ranked 4th in global gold recycling.

Concept note-4: -The central bank of Qatar, for instance, made history by buying 15 MT of Gold in a single month of Q3 2022, breaking its old record of a similar buying spree way back in 1967. Turkey bought 94.87 MT of gold in the first nine months of 2022.
