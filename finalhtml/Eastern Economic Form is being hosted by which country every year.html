Eastern Economic Form is being hosted by which country every year?

(A) ** Russia

(B) Sweden

(C) Germany

(D) Poland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution: Eastern Economic Forum is an international forum that is held every year in Vladivostok, Russia, for the purpose of encouraging foreign investment in the Russian far east . The forum is held each year since 2015 in September.

Concept note-2: -Eastern Economic Forum (UPSC Notes) The Eastern Economic Forum (EEF) is an annual international forum that takes place in Vladivostok, Russia. It was established in 2015 for the purpose of encouraging investment in the Russian Far East.

Concept note-3: -Eastern Economic Forum is the forum where all economic, political as well as cultural relations between Asia Pacific and Russia have been discussed. The Eastern Economic Forum members are regions of Asia Pacific as well as ASEAN and the Eastern Economic Forum members are; Afghanistan, India, Russia and Iran.

Concept note-4: -Addressing the Plenary Session of 7th Eastern Economic Forum being held in Vladivostok through video conferencing, Mr Modi said, there is immense scope for cooperation in the field of energy.
