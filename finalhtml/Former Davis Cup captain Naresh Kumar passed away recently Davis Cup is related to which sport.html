Former Davis Cup captain Naresh Kumar passed away recently. Davis Cup is related to which sport?

(A) Badminton

(B) Boxing

(C) Football

(D) ** Tennis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Naresh Kumar (Hindi: ; Punjabi: , ) (22 December 1928 – 14 September 2022) was an Indian tennis player. He was the captain of the India Davis Cup team from 1989 to 1993.

Concept note-2: -Recent News. Davis Cup, trophy awarded to the winner of an annual international lawn-tennis tournament originally for amateur men’s teams. The official name is the International Lawn Tennis Challenge Trophy. The trophy was donated in 1900 by American Dwight F.

Concept note-3: -The Davis Cup is the World Cup of tennis. It is the largest annual international team competition in world sport and was founded in 1900 by Dwight Davis. Great Britain are the only nation to have competed in all editions of the tournament and have lifted the trophy ten times, most recently in 2015.

Concept note-4: -On September 23, 1974, The New York Times published an article on one of the biggest moments in Indian sporting history from Poona. ‘India Ousts Soviet Union In Davis Cup’, the headline read, reporting how a team led by the Amritraj brothers advanced to the 1974 Davis Cup final by defeating the Soviet Union.
