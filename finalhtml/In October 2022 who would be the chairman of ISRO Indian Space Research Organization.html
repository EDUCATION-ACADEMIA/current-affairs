In October 2022, who would be the chairman of ISRO (Indian Space Research Organization)?

(A) G. Mahalaxmi

(B) ** S. Somanath

(C) R. N. Subraminamam

(D) S. Chandra Rajashekhar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri S. Somanath assumed the charge of Secretary, Department of Space and Chairman, Space Commission on 14 January 2022. In his previous assignment he was Director, Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre (VSSC), the lead Centre responsible for Launch vehicle technology development.

Concept note-2: -’Isro Chairman’ - 181 News Result(s) According to ISRO Chairman S Somanath, the agency is monitoring weather conditions ahead of the test of the reusable launch vehicle technology.

Concept note-3: -H.E. Dr. Claudio Ansorena Montero, Ambassador of Costa Rica to India called on Shri S. Somanath, Chairman, Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)/ Secretary, Department of Space at ISRO Headquarters on January 09, 2023.
