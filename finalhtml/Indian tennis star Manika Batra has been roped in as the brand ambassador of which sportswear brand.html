Indian tennis star Manika Batra has been roped in as the brand ambassador of which sportswear brand?

(A) ** Adidas

(B) Puma

(C) Reebok

(D) Nike

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Manika joins Adidas’ rich roster of women athletes, consisting of names like Mirabai Chanu, Lovlina Borgohain, Hima Das, and Nikhat Zareen. Sportswear brand Adidas has brought on board India’s top table tennis talent, Manika Batra.

Concept note-2: -India’s top table tennis player, Manika Batra has been roped in as the brand ambassador of the sportswear brand Adidas.

Concept note-3: -She is India’s top-ranked female table tennis player as of November 2020. Manika Batra is the brand ambassador of Adidas’ sportswear.

Concept note-4: -Adidas appoints Palak Kohli as brand ambassador.
