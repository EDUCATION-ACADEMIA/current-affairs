India’s first heli-hub has recently been inaugurated in which place, having all aviation facilities?

(A) Chennai

(B) Agra

(C) Nagpur

(D) ** Gurugram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first heli-hub has recently been inaugurated in which place, having all aviation facilities? Explanation: India’s first heli-hub with all aviation facilities is proposed to be built at Gurugram in Haryana. This was announced by Haryana Deputy Chief Minister Dushyant Chautala.

Concept note-2: -The heli hub has been proposed to be set up in the upcoming Global City being developed around sectors 36, 36B, 37 and 37B close to the Dwarka Expressway and NH-48.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Gurugram. Haryana Deputy Chief Minister Dushyant Chautala has announced that Gurugram will get India’s first heli-hub with all aviation facilities. It will be the first of its kind in the country to have all facilities at one place for helicopters.

Concept note-4: -By India Today Web Desk: Haryana Deputy Chief Minister Dushyant Chautala announced that Gurugram will get India’s first heli-hub with all aviation facilities.
