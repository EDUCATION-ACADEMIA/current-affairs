Lok Sabha cleared the bill to add which state’s Hattee community to the ST list?

(A) Karnataka

(B) ** Himachal Pradesh

(C) Assam

(D) Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lok Sabha passes Bill to include Himachal Pradesh’s Hattee community in the list of Scheduled Tribes. The Lok Sabha on Friday passed the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Third Amendment) Bill, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Earlier, the Constitution (Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes) Order (Second Amendment) Bill, 2022 was passed unanimously by the Rajya Sabha during the ongoing Winter Session of the Parliament on 14.12. 2022 wrt state of Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -Madhya Pradesh. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-4: -Some seats are reserved in Lok Sabha for the members of the Schedule Castes and Scheduled Tribes. As per the order issued by the Delimitation Commission in 2008 , 412 are general, 84 seats are reserved for Scheduled Castes and 47 seats for the Scheduled Tribes .

Concept note-5: -d Madhya Pradesh is the state where the largest number of seats are reserved for scheduled tribe in the Lok Sabha.
