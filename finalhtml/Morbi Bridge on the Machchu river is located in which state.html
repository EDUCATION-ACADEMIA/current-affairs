Morbi Bridge on the Machchu river is located in which state?

(A) Arunachal Pradesh

(B) Maharashtra

(C) ** Gujarat

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 30 October 2022 a pedestrian suspension bridge over the Machchhu River in the city of Morbi in Gujarat, India, collapsed, causing the deaths of at least 135 people and injuries to more than 180 others.

Concept note-2: -Located 64 km from Rajkot is the town of Morbi with its quaint cobbled alleyways and buildings reminiscent of 19th century Europe.

Concept note-3: -About: The Machchhu river originates from Madla hills in the Surendranagar district of Gujarat and ends 141.75 km downstream in the Rann of Kutch.
