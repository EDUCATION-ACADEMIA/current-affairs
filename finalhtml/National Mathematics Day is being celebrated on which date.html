National Mathematics Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) December 14

(B) December 16

(C) December 10

(D) ** December 22

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Mathematics Day is celebrated on 22 December every year to mark the birth anniversary of legendary Indian mathematician, Srinivasa Ramanujan .

Concept note-2: -National Mathematics Day 2022: It is observed on 22 December every year to mark the birth anniversary of legendary Indian Mathematician, Srinivasa Ramanujan. The day recognizes and celebrates his works.

Concept note-3: -The first National Mathematics Day was observed in India on 22 December 2012. The former Prime Minister of India, Dr. Manmohan Singh announced this day. Since then, it has been celebrated every year on 22nd December.

Concept note-4: -National Mathematics Day is celebrated on December 22 across the nation every year. The National Mathematics Day is marked to recognise and celebrate the works of Srinivasa Ramanujan. Srinivasa Ramanujan, the Indian mathematical genius, was born on this day in 1887.

Concept note-5: -The Indian government declared 22 December to be National Mathematics Day. It was introduced by Prime Minister Manmohan Singh on 26 December 2011 at Madras University, to mark the 125th birth anniversary of the Indian mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan.
