Nepal has signed the framework agreement to join the India-led International Solar Alliance. Does it become which number country who sign the agreement?

(A) 107th

(B) ** 105th

(C) 117th

(D) 112th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nepal also became the 105th member country to sign on to the Framework Agreement of International Solar Alliance (ISA).

Concept note-2: -Denmark has signed the framework agreement of the International Solar Alliance Framework Agreement (ISA FA) and Instrument of Ratification with India.

Concept note-3: -India and France launched the International Solar Alliance (ISA) to boost solar energy in developing countries. It was launched at the United Nations Climate Change Conference in Paris in November 2015 by the Indian Prime Minister and French President.

Concept note-4: -International Solar Alliance. The following 112 countries have signed the ISA Framework Agreement: Commonwealth of Australia.
