PM Modi has inaugurated a new campus of Chittaranjan National Cancer Institute (CNCI) in which city?

(A) Hyderabad

(B) ** Kolkata

(C) Patna

(D) Lucknow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Located in Kolkata’s Rajarhat New Town area, CNCI’s new campus will have a 460-bed comprehensive cancer unit equipped with state-of-the-art facilities for cancer diagnosis, staging, treatment and care. The campus will also serve as an advanced cancer research facility.

Concept note-2: -CNCI Rajarhat or CHITTARANJAN NATIONAL CANCER INSTITUTE is a popularly known government cancer Hospital in West Bengal.

Concept note-3: -The economic policies of Modi’s government focused on privatization and liberalization of the economy, based on a neoliberal framework. Modi liberalized India’s foreign direct investment policies, allowing more foreign investment in several industries, including in defense and the railways.
