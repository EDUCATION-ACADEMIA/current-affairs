Seventh edition of the Global Technology Summit to be held in New Delhi from which date?

(A) ** November 29

(B) November 27

(C) November 24

(D) November 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To discuss these issues and many other questions, we hosted the 7th iteration of the Global Technology Summit, themed Geopolitics of Technology from November 29 to December 1.

Concept note-2: -The 7th edition of the Global Technology Summit (GTS) was held from November 29 to December 1 in New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -2022 Global Technology Summit The seventh iteration of the Global Technology Summit explored the present climate in the shapeshifting world of international alliances, emerging technologies, and geopolitics.
