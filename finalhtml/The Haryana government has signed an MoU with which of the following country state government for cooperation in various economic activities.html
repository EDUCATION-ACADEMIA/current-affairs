The Haryana government has signed an MoU with which of the following country state government for cooperation in various economic activities?

(A) ** Dubai

(B) Berlin

(C) Tokyo

(D) Paris

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The head of state of Haryana is the Governor, appointed by the President of India on the advice of the central government. His or her post is largely ceremonial. The Chief Minister is the head of government and is vested with most of the executive powers to run the 22 districts of Haryana across its six divisions.

Concept note-2: -Since 1966, ten people have served as the Chief Minister of Haryana. The first was B. D. Sharma of the Indian National Congress party.
