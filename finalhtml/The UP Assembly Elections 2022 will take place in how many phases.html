The UP Assembly Elections 2022 will take place in how many phases?

(A) ** 7

(B) 5

(C) 8

(D) 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Legislative Assembly elections were held in Uttar Pradesh from 10 February to 7 March 2022 in seven phases to elect all 403 members for the 18th Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly. The votes were counted and the results were declared on 10 March 2022.

Concept note-2: -The election to the Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly was held in seven phases from 8 February through 3 March 2012.

Concept note-3: -The election to the 17th Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly was held from 11 February to 8 March 2017 in 7 phases. This election saw a voter turnout of 61.11% compared to 59.40% in the previous election.

Concept note-4: -The first Legislative Assembly was constituted on 8th March, 1952. Since then, it has been constituted Eighteen times. The present, Eighteenth Legislative Assembly was constituted on 11th March, 2022.
