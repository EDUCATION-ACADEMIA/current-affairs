Upgraded Inner Line Permit System portal launched in which state?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Manipur

(C) Mizoram

(D) Nagaland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In Manipur, an upgraded Inner Line Permit (ILP) System portal was launched by Chief Minister N. Biren Singh today. The upgraded portal has been launched considering the importance of saving indigenous population of the State and also for the convenience of visitors from outside the State for applying ILP.

Concept note-2: -The Inner Line Permit was implemented as part of the Bengal Eastern Frontier Regulation of 1873. It was enacted to protect the commercial interests of the British Empire by preventing its subjects (at the time, Indians) from trading in these regions.

Concept note-3: -There are four North-Eastern states that have ILP: Arunachal Pradesh. Nagaland. Mizoram.

Concept note-4: -Four states in the North-Eastern belt have an inner line permit. They are Nagaland, Manipur, Arunachal Pradesh, Mizoram, and Lakshadweep. In addition, the inner line permit is required to enter the islands of Lakshadweep.
