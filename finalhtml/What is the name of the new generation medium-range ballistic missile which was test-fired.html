What is the name of the new generation medium-range ballistic missile, which was test-fired?

(A) Naran Prime

(B) Akash Prime

(C) ** Agni Prime

(D) Gagan Prime

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. What is the name of the new generation medium-range ballistic missile, which was recently test-fired? Notes: India test-fired indigenously developed new generation medium-range ballistic missile ‘Agni Prime’ (Agni-P) from the Odisha coast.

Concept note-2: -Agni-V is a solid fueled intercontinental ballistic missile (ICBM) developed by Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) of India. It will greatly expand India’s reach to strike targets more than 7,000 km away.

Concept note-3: -India’s successful Agni-5 tests a warning to China? Here’s all about the ballistic missile. The Defence Ministry successfully tested Agni-5 yesterday from Abdul Kalam Island, off the coast of Odisha.

Concept note-4: -In June, India successfully carried out a night launch of the nuclear-capable Agni-4 ballistic missile, in a boost to India’s military capabilities. Following the test, the defence ministry had said that it reaffirmed India’s policy of having a ‘credible minimum deterrence capability.”

Concept note-5: -Agni-5 is an ingeniously built advanced surface-to-surface ballistic missile developed under the Integrated Guided Missile Development Programme (IGMDP).
