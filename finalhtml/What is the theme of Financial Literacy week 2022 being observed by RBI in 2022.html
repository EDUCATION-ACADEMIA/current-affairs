What is the theme of Financial Literacy week 2022 being observed by RBI in 2022?

(A) ** Go Digital, Go Secure

(B) MSME

(C) Credit Discipline and Credit from Formal Institutions

(D) Farmers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme selected for current year FLW is “Go Digital, Go Secure” which will be observed between February 14-18, 2022. This theme is one of the strategic objectives of the National Strategy for Financial Education 2020-2025.

Concept note-2: -"Here comes the sun-monetary policy."

Concept note-3: -Some typical financial literacy topics include: Saving money (emergency savings, major purchases, retirement) Budgeting and spending (methods, best practices, psychology) Debt management (refinancing, becoming debt-free, understanding credit products)

Concept note-4: -Celebrations in 2022 This year’s International Literacy Day will be celebrated worldwide under the theme, Transforming Literacy Learning Spaces and will be an opportunity to rethink the fundamental importance of literacy learning spaces to build resilience and ensure quality, equitable, and inclusive education for all.

Concept note-5: -The Reserve Bank had announced opening of the Fourth Cohort under Regulatory Sandbox vide Press release dated June 06, 2022 for theme ‘Prevention and Mitigation of Financial Frauds’.
