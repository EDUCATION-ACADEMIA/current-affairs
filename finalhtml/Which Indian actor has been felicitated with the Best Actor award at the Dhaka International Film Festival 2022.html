Which Indian actor has been felicitated with the Best Actor award at the Dhaka International Film Festival 2022?

(A) Dhanush

(B) ** Jayasurya

(C) Ram Charan

(D) Prabhas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Koozhangal’ from India won the best film award in the Asian Film Competition section at the 20th Dhaka International Film Festival which concluded on 23 Jan 2022 in Dhaka. It has been directed by PS Vinothraj. The best actor award was given to Jayasurya for the film Sunny.

Concept note-2: -The special audience award was given to Aimee Baruah Directed film Semkhor. India won 4 out of 17 awards.

Concept note-3: -Ganesan was the first Indian film actor to win a “Best Actor” award in an International film festival, the Afro-Asian Film Festival held in Cairo, Egypt in 1960.
