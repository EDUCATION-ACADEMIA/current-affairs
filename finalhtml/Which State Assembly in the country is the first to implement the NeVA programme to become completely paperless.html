Which State Assembly in the country is the first to implement the NeVA programme to become completely paperless?

(A) Mizoram

(B) Manipur

(C) ** Nagaland

(D) Meghalaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nagaland Legislative Assembly (NLA) has become the first in the country to have operationalised the National e-Vidhan Application (NeVA) programme.

Concept note-2: -In 2021, Uttar Pradesh became the first Indian state to present a paperless budget. It was the first time since India’s independence that the Union budget 2021 was delivered in a paperless format. Ipads were provided to all the State Legislature members to view the highlights of the budget.

Concept note-3: -National eVidhan Application (NeVA)

Concept note-4: -The Ministry of Parliamentary Affairs, Government of India is the Nodal Department for e-Vidhan MMP.
