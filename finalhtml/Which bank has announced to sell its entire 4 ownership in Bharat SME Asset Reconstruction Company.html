Which bank has announced to sell its entire 4% ownership in Bharat SME Asset Reconstruction Company?

(A) Kashmir Bank

(B) Yes Bank

(C) Gujarat Bank

(D) ** Bank of Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bank of Maharashtra announced that it will sell its entire 4% ownership in India SME Asset Reconstruction Company for almost Rs 4 crore.

Concept note-2: -Bank of Maharashtra will divest its entire stake of 4 per cent in India SME Asset Reconstruction Company for nearly Rs 4 crore.

Concept note-3: -The bank will divest its entire stake in ISARC for a consideration of ₹3.92 crore. The transaction is expected to close by December 2022-end. A floor price of ₹9.80 per equity share has been proposed for the divestment. The bank holds 40 lakh equity shares or 4% at a face value of ₹10 each in ISARC.

Concept note-4: -Ans. ARCIL was the first Asset Reconstruction Company in India.

Concept note-5: -Pridhvi Asset Reconstruction And Securitisation Company Ltd.
