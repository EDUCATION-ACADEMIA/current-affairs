Which city is the host of the fourth India-US 2+2 Ministerial Dialogue?

(A) Gandhi Nagar

(B) New Delhi

(C) New York

(D) ** Washington

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In March it was announced that India and the U.S. will hold the 2+2 meeting in Washington, D.C. on April 11 between External Affairs Minister S Jaishankar, Defence Minister Rajnath Singh, Secretary of State Antony Blinken, and Secretary of Defense Lloyd Austin. Singh was expected to be in the U.S. from April 10-13.

Concept note-2: -Jaishankar to Washington, D.C., on April 11, 2022, for the fourth U.S.-India 2+2 Ministerial Dialogue. The Dialogue was preceded by a virtual meeting between Prime Minister Narendra Modi and President Joseph Biden.

Concept note-3: - Ministry Of DEFENCE Glimpses of Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh addressing the convocation ceremony of Manipal Academy of Higher Education in Udupi, Karnataka on 18 November 2022.
