Which company has become the first to be traded on the Indian Gas Exchange?

(A) ** Oil And Natural Gas Corporation Limited

(B) Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited

(C) Bharat Oman Refineries Limited

(D) Indian Oil Corporation Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(ONGC) has become the first Exploration and Production (E&P) company in India to trade domestic gas on Indian Gas Exchange.

Concept note-2: -"ONGC has become the first exploration and production (E&P) company in India to trade domestic gas on Indian Gas Exchange. The first online trade was made on May 23, 2022 by ONGC Director (Onshore) & In-charge Marketing Anurag Sharma on India’s first automated national level Gas Exchange, IGX,” it said.

Concept note-3: -On Monday, the state-owned Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) announced it was the first gas producer to trade domestic gas on the Indian Gas Exchange, exchanging unidentified amounts from its eastern offshore KG-DWN-98/2 block.

Concept note-4: -ONGC has made history by becoming India’s first exploration and production (E&P) company to trade domestic gas on the Indian Gas Exchange. Key Points: ONGC Director (Onshore) & In-charge Marketing Anurag Sharma made the first online trade on India’s first automated national level Gas Exchange, IGX.

Concept note-5: -Indian Gas Exchange Limited (IGX) is India’s first natural gas exchange, offering all players a national level market for gas trade. Numerous buyers and sellers can deal with the actual delivery of natural gas on IGX, which allows for automated trading.
