Which is the theme of World Paper Bag Day 2022?

(A) Living Sustainably in Harmony with Nature

(B) It’s never too late to switch from plastic to paper

(C) ** If You’re ‘fantastic’, Do Something ‘dramatic’ To Cut The ‘Plastic’, Use ‘Paper Bags’

(D) Only One Earth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for Paper Bag Day this year is, “If You’re ‘fantastic’, Do Something ‘dramatic’ To Cut The ‘Plastic’, Use ‘Paper Bags’.”

Concept note-2: -"Avoid using plastic bags. Keep my earth pollution-free by using paper bags!” “Make a pledge and do your part to protect the environment. Make the switch to paper bags and avoid plastics!"

Concept note-3: -American inventor, Francis Wolle, received credit for his patent of the first paper bag machine in 1852. Another paper bag inventor, William Goodale, received his patent on July 12th, 1859. His was designed to cut the paper such that it was ready for folding.

Concept note-4: -A paper bag is a bag made of paper, usually kraft paper. Paper bags can be made either with virgin or recycled fibres to meet customers’ demands. Paper bags are commonly used as shopping carrier bags and for packaging of some consumer goods.

Concept note-5: -Paper Bag Day (also known as World Paper Bag Day) occurs on July 12 each year and celebrates the humble paper bag, which does so much more than carrying our groceries.
