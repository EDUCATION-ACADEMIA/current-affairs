Which of the following has been declared a National Monument?

(A) ** Mangarh Dham

(B) Lascar War Memorial

(C) Balidan Stambh

(D) Jharkhand War Memorial

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is India Gate. The India Gate is considered as National Monument of India.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi declared Mangarh Dham a National Monument in the Banswara District, Rajasthan. Mangarh Dham is a symbol of tenacity and sacrifices of tribals.

Concept note-3: -National monuments are nationally significant lands and waters set aside for permanent protection. These public lands are similar to national parks in many ways, with some key differences.

Concept note-4: -There are 116 Indian monuments that can be visited, across 19 states. Out of them, 17 monuments of India are located in Uttar Pradesh. Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World is located in Agra, in this state.

Concept note-5: -Mangarh Dham, situated near the Rajasthan-Gujarat border, is known for massacre of tribals by the British Indian Army in 1913.
