Which one has become India’s 100th unicorn?

(A) Bigbasket

(B) ** Open

(C) zomato

(D) Paytm Mall

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mabel Chacko is the co-founder of neo-banking platform Open, which became India’s 100th unicorn earlier this year.

Concept note-2: -Open. Bengaluru-based neobanking startup Open becomes the country’s 100th unicorn after raising $50 Mn from IIFL.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which start-up recently became the 100th unicorn of India? Notes: Open, a neo-banking fin-tech portal recently raised capital to push up its value over a billlion dollars and became India’s 100th unicorn.

Concept note-4: -The First Unicorn Startup In India InMobi is the first Indian startup that attained unicorn status almost 11 years ago. It started its journey as a mobile advertisement optimization agency and has become the first unicorn startup in India.

Concept note-5: -Noida-based edtech platform PhysicsWallah, also known as PW, has become India’s 101st unicorn, and the first unicorn in almost a month as it raised $100 Mn in a Series A funding round from Westbridge and GSV Ventures.
