Which state government has launched a rainwater harvesting scheme named CHHATA?

(A) Gujarat

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) ** Odisha

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -of Odisha, intends to launch the Scheme, “CHHATA” a State Sector Scheme for rooftop rain water harvesting and ground water recharge for the period from FY-2022-23 to FY-2026-27 (five years) in 52 water stressed blocks and 27 water scarcity ULBs.

Concept note-2: -Tamil Nadu is the first and the only state in India, which has made rooftop rainwater harvesting compulsory. Amendments made to Section 215 (a) of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 and Building Rules 1973, have made it mandatory to provide Rain Water Harvesting structures in all new buildings.

Concept note-3: -Odisha government launches Rs 270 crore rainwater harvesting scheme to improve groundwater table. Under the scheme, a target has been set to harvest 373.52 crore litres of water in five years.

Concept note-4: -Odisha Government has launched a new health centric scheme named as Nirmal Scheme 2023. The state govt. has proposed Rs. 370 crore to maintain quality of sanitation, laundry, security, gardening and lift services in public health facilities.

Concept note-5: -’Jal Shakti Abhiyan – Catch the Rain-2022’ was launched by Hon’ble President of India on 29.03. 2022. & GR, inter-alia advocates rainwater harvesting and conservation of water and highlights the need for augmenting the availability of water through direct use of rainfall.
