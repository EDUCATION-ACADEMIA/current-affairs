Which state government has signed an agreement with the Indian Council of Cultural Relations (ICCR) to help artists from the state, handicrafts & several other ethnic arts expected to get international exposure?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Bihar government has signed an MoU with the ICCR to help artists from the state, handicrafts and several other ethnic arts to get international exposure, an official said. The Art and Culture Department of the state government signed an MoU with the Indian Council of Cultural Relations (ICCR).

Concept note-2: -The agreement between the Chhattisgarh government and the Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR) will open up new possibilities in the field of culture and tourism in the state.

Concept note-3: -Notes: The Bihar government signed an MoU with the Indian Council of Cultural Relations (ICCR) to help artists, handicrafts and ethnic arts to get international exposure. The MoU will help Bihar to send group of artists to other countries as part of cultural exchange initiative by the Central and the state government.

Concept note-4: -Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR), Ministry of External Affairs is responsible to promote cultural exchanges with other countries and people.

Concept note-5: -Since January 2018, Dr. Sahasrabuddhe is the President of Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR). He has been the Member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha (Upper House of Indian Parliament) from July 2016 to July 2022.
