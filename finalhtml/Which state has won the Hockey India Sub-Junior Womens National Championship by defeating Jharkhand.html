Which state has won the Hockey India Sub-Junior Women’s National Championship by defeating Jharkhand?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Kerala

(C) ** Haryana

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Expressing his delight after the Title win Hockey Haryana Coach Azad Singh Malik said, “Hockey Jharkhand are a very strong side, we knew it was going to be a challenging game.

Concept note-2: -Hockey Haryana emerged winners of the Hockey India sub-junior women’s national championship 2022 after defeating Hockey Jharkhand 2-0 in the final in Imphal yesterday.

Concept note-3: -Bhopal, 17 April 2022: Hockey Haryana were crowned as the Champions of the 12th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2022 in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh with a thrilling 1-1 (3-1 SO) victory over Hockey Unit Of Tamil Nadu in the Final of the competition.

Concept note-4: -Odisha was crowned as the Champions of the 12th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship 2022 in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. The final match was a closely contested affair between Karnataka and Odisha but Odisha defeated Karnataka by 2-0.

Concept note-5: -India have won the hockey World Cup just once. The men’s team lifted the trophy in 1975. The women’s team’s best finish at the FIH Hockey World Cup is fourth. Since the 1970s, the Hockey World Cup has been the premier standalone tournament for the sport.
