Which state is set to roll out the comprehensive health insurance scheme, ‘MEDISEP’?

(A) ** Kerala

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) Bihar

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which state is set to roll out the comprehensive health insurance scheme, ‘MEDISEP’? Notes: Kerala government is set to launch MEDISEP scheme from July 1, 2022. It is a medical insurance scheme for state government employees, pensioners and their eligible family members.

Concept note-2: -MEDISEP Scheme 2023: Kerala’s Health Insurance Scheme MEDICSEP Health Insurance Scheme is a state scheme only for the State of Kerala and only for government employees and retired pensioners. During its three-year lifespan, this program will assist 30 million people in the state. This scheme covers Rs 3 Lakh annually.

Concept note-3: -Notes: Kerala state government has issued orders regarding the implementation of a medical insurance “MEDISEP” scheme for government employees and pensioners and the deduction of premium from the salary of June 2022 and pension of July 2022.

Concept note-4: -The scheme was announced by the Chief Minister of Rajasthan, Shri Ashok Gehlot, during the state budget 2021-22. Starting from May 1, 2021, the benefits under the scheme were made available to all the families of Rajasthan if they fulfil the eligibility criteria.
