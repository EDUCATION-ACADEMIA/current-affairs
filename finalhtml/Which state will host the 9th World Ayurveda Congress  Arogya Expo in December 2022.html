Which state will host the 9th World Ayurveda Congress & Arogya Expo in December 2022?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Rajasthan

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sowa-rigpa participates in 9th World Ayurveda Congress and Arogya Expo 2022 inaugurated in Goa. Leh, Dec 09: The National Institute of Sowa-Rigpa, Leh participates in the 9th World Ayurveda Congress & Arogya Expo 2022 is organized at Panaji, Goa from 8 to 11 December 2022.

Concept note-2: -9th World Ayurveda Congress (WAC) was inaugurated today at Panaji, Goa in the presence of Union Minister of State for Tourism and Shipping, Ports and Waterways Shripad Naik, Chief Minister Dr Pramod Sawant and Secretary, Ministry of Ayush, Vaidya Rajesh Kotecha.

Concept note-3: -9th World Ayurveda Congress & Arogya Expo 2022 is scheduled to be conducted at Kala Academy, Panjim, Goa, India from 8th to 11th December, 2022.

Concept note-4: -This year’s Ayurveda Day is being celebrated with the theme “Har Din Har Ghar Ayurveda” so as to propagate benefits of Ayurveda to larger and grass root community.

Concept note-5: -Where is the Global Ayurveda Festival always held? The Global Ayurveda Summit is held annually in Kerala.
