Who has been named as the ICC Men’s Player of the Month for April?

(A) ** Keshav Maharaj

(B) Rashid Khan

(C) Umran Malik

(D) Rishabh Pant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maharaj registered seven-wicket hauls on two separate occasions, both during the second innings of each Test where he ended with figures of 7/32 and 7/40.

Concept note-2: -The International Cricket Council (ICC) today announced that Australia’s star of the ICC Women’s Cricket World Cup Final Alyssa Healy, and South Africa’s spin wizard Keshav Maharaj have been voted the ICC Women’s and Men’s Players of the Month for April 2022.

Concept note-3: -On 11 January, the International Cricket Council (ICC) announced the ICC Player of the Month Awards for December 2022, with England’s rising star batsman Harry Brook and Australia’s Ashley Gardner winning the ICC Men’s and Women’s Player of the Month awards respectively.

Concept note-4: -Hit For Six! England star Jos Buttler has been crowned the ICC Men’s Player of the Month for November 2022 after leading his side to the T20 World Cup title.
