Who has inaugurated the Bina (MP)-Panki (UP) multiproduct pipeline project?

(A) Hardeep Singh Puri

(B) Yogi Adityanath

(C) ** Narendra Modi

(D) Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the Bina-Panki Multiproduct Pipeline Project on 28th December, 2021 at Kanpur. The 356 Km long project has a capacity of around 3.45 million metric ton per annum.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated the 356 Km long Bina Refinery (Madhya Pradesh)- POL Terminal at Panki (Kanpur, UP) Multiproduct pipeline project ( capacity of 45 million metric tonnes per annum) with an outlay of Rs 1524 crore (Rs. 1227 crores in UP and Rs. 297 crores in MP).

Concept note-3: -It was a very special day for the employees of Bharat Oman Refineries Limited (BORL) when the Hon’ble Prime Minister Dr. Manmohan Singh visited Bina, Madhya Pradesh on 20th May to dedicate the new Refinery to the nation.

Concept note-4: -to the nation. • The 356 km pipeline will cater to demand of petroleum products from northern states. including UP, Bihar and Southern Uttarakhand. • Established at the cost of Rs 1,524 crore, the pipeline will be operated by BPCL.

Concept note-5: -356 Km long project has a capacity of around 3.45 million metric tonne per annum.
