Who has launched the ‘Stree Manoraksha Project’?

(A) ** Smriti Irani

(B) Anupriya Patel

(C) Meenakshi Lekhi

(D) Nirmala Sitharaman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Union Ministry launched the ‘Stree Manoraksha Project’? Notes: The Ministry of Women and Child Development (MoWCD) launched the ‘Stree Manoraksha Project’ to improve the mental health of women in India.

Concept note-2: -The MoWCD and NIMHANS Bengaluru launched the ‘Stree Manoraksha Project’ on Wednesday with the goal of improving women’s mental health in India. Madhavi Gaur Published On March 4th, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The ‘Stree Manoraksha Project’ has been launched by the Ministry of Women and Child Development (MoWCD)in collaboration with NIMHANS Bengaluru. This was launched with the aim to improve women’s mental health across India.
