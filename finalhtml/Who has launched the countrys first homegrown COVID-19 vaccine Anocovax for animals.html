Who has launched the country’s first homegrown COVID-19 vaccine “Anocovax” for animals?

(A) Mansukh Mandaviya

(B) Parshottam Rupala

(C) ** Narendra Singh Tomar

(D) Mahendra Nath Pandey

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -COVAXIN®, India’s indigenous COVID-19 vaccine by Bharat Biotech is developed in collaboration with the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) - National Institute of Virology (NIV).

Concept note-2: -In December 2020, the UK became the first country in the world to approve this vaccine and began rolling out an initial 800,000 doses at the start of the month, and is now in use worldwide.

Concept note-3: -Nanocovax is a Vietnamese COVID-19 vaccine candidate developed by Nanogen Pharmaceutical Biotechnology JSC. It is a subunit vaccine (SARS‑CoV‑2 recombinant spike protein with aluminum adjuvant).
